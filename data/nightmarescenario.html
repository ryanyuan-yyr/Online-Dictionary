nightmare scenario
ˈnaɪtˌmɛə sɪˈnɑːrɪəʊ
Word forms: plural nightmare scenarios
N-COUNT [usually singular] 噩梦般的镜头;梦魇般的场景 If you describe a situation or event as a nightmare scenario, you mean that it is the worst possible thing that could happen. 噩梦般的镜头;梦魇般的场景

  • Discovering your child takes drugs is a nightmare scenario for most parents.

    对大多数父母来说，发现自己的孩子吸毒简直就是场噩梦。
