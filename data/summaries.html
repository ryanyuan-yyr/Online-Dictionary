summary
sʌməri ˈsʌməri
Word forms: plural summaries
1 N-COUNT 总结;摘要;概括 A summary of something is a short account of it, which gives the main points but not the details. 总结;摘要;概括

  • What follows is a brief summary of the process. [+ of]

    接下来是对此流程的一个简短概括。

  • Here's a summary of the day's news. [+ of]

    下面是今日新闻简报。

  • Milligan gives a fair summary of his subject within a relatively short space.

    米利根用比较小的篇幅对他的课题作了一个清晰简要的概述。

You use in summary to indicate that what you are about to say is a summary of what has just been said.总的来说;归纳起来

  • In summary, it is my opinion that this complete treatment process was very successful.

    总的来说，我认为整个治疗过程非常成功。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 从速的;即决的;草草的 Summary actions are done without delay, often when something else should have been done first or done instead. 从速的;即决的;草草的 [formal]

  • It says torture and summary execution are common.

    那上面称酷刑和草草处决很常见。

  • There is no doubt that some considered that a beating was no more than summary justice.

    毫无疑问，有人认为拷打比草菅人命好不到哪去。

  • The four men were killed after a summary trial.

    草草审讯后这四个人即被处死。

SYN hasty, cursory, perfunctory, arbitrary  
summarily

  • Several detainees had been summarily executed.

    几个在押犯被草草处决。
