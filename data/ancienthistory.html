ancient history
1 N-UNCOUNT （尤指希腊和罗马）古代史 Ancient history is the history of ancient civilizations, especially Greece and Rome. （尤指希腊和罗马）古代史
2 N-UNCOUNT （发生在过去，与现在无关的）旧闻，陈年老账 If you describe something as ancient history, you mean that it happened in the past and is no longer relevant to the present. （发生在过去，与现在无关的）旧闻，陈年老账

  • It does not bother me now at all. It is all ancient history.

    现在它丝毫不让我感到烦恼。它只不过是陈年老账。
