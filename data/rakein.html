rake in
PHRASAL VERB 轻易赚取，大捞（一笔钱） If you say that someone is raking in money, you mean that they are making a lot of money very easily, more easily than you think they should. 轻易赚取，大捞（一笔钱） [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The privatisation allowed companies to rake in huge profits. [VERB PREP. noun]

    私有化使许多公司大赚了一笔。

SYN earn, raise, net, acquire
