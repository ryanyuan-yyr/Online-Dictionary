follow
fɒloʊ ˈfɑloʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense follows, present participle following, past tense , past participle followed
1 VERB 跟随;跟着 If you follow someone who is going somewhere, you move along behind them because you want to go to the same place. 跟随;跟着 [Also V + after]

  • We followed him up the steps into a large hall. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    我们跟着他上楼来到一个很大的大厅。

  • Please follow me, madam. [VERB noun]

    请跟我来，夫人。

  • They took him into a small room and I followed. [VERB]

    他们把他带到一个小房间，我跟了进去。

SYN accompany, attend, escort, come after  
2 VERB 尾随;跟踪 If you follow someone who is going somewhere, you move along behind them without their knowledge, in order to catch them or find out where they are going. 尾随;跟踪

  • She realized that the Mercedes was following her. [VERB noun]

    她意识到那辆奔驰在跟踪她。

  • I think we're being followed. [be VERB-ed]

    我想我们被跟踪了。

SYN pursue, track, dog, hunt  
3 VERB 追随 If you follow someone to a place where they have recently gone and where they are now, you go to join them there. 追随

  • He followed Janice to New York, where she was preparing an exhibition. [VERB noun + to]

    他追随贾尼丝到了纽约，她正在那里准备一个展览。

4 VERB 接着…发生;在…后发生 An event, activity, or period of time that follows a particular thing happens or comes after that thing, at a later time. 接着…发生;在…后发生

  • ...the rioting and looting that followed the verdict. [VERB noun]

    判决宣布之后发生的暴乱和抢劫

  • I remember nothing else about the days following Daddy's death. [VERB noun]

    父亲去世之后那几天我不记得有什么其他事了。

  • He was arrested in the confusion which followed. [VERB]

    他在之后的混乱中被捕。

  • Other problems may follow. [VERB]

    其他的问题可能会接踵而至。

  • Eyewitnesses spoke of a noise followed by a huge red light. [VERB-ed]

    目击者说响声过后出现了一道耀眼的红光。

SYN come after, go after, come next  
5 VERB 在…后做(或说) If you follow one thing with another, you do or say the second thing after you have done or said the first thing. 在…后做(或说)

  • Her first major role was in Martin Scorsese's 'Goodfellas' and she followed this with a part in Spike Lee's 'Jungle Fever'. [VERB noun + with]

    她主演的第一部影片是马丁·斯科塞斯的《好家伙》，之后接拍了斯派克·李的《丛林热》。

Follow up means the same as follow .follow up 同 follow

  • The book proved such a success that the authors followed it up with 'The Messianic Legacy'.

    这本书取得了巨大的成功，作者于是又推出一部《弥赛亚的遗产》。

6 VERB 可以推断出;由此得出 If it follows that a particular thing is the case, that thing is a logical result of something else being true or being the case. 可以推断出;由此得出

  • Just because a bird does not breed one year, it does not follow that it will fail the next. [VERB that]

    不能因为一只鸟某一年没有繁殖就断定它第二年也不会繁殖。

  • If the explanation is right, two things follow. [VERB]

    如果解释是正确的，可以推断出两件事。

  • It is easy to see the conclusions described in the text follow from this equation. [VERB + from]

    很容易可以看出文中所述的结论是由这个方程式推出的。

SYN result, issue, develop, spring  
7 VERB (文章或讲演)接下来是,后面的是，下面是 If you refer to the words that follow or followed, you are referring to the words that come next or came next in a piece of writing or speech. (文章或讲演)接下来是,后面的是，下面是

  • What follows is an eyewitness account.

    接下来的是目击者的陈述。

  • There followed a list of places where Hans intended to visit. [there V n]

    下面列出的是汉斯打算游览的地点。

  • General analysis is followed by five case studies. [be VERB-ed + by]

    综合分析之后是5个个案研究。

8 VERB 循着，沿着(道路、标志等) If you follow a path, route, or set of signs, you go somewhere using the path, route, or signs to direct you. 循着，沿着(道路、标志等)

  • If they followed the road, they would be certain to reach a village. [VERB noun]

    如果他们沿着这条路走，就一定会到达一个村庄。

  • All we had to do was follow the map. [VERB noun]

    我们所要做的就是按照地图走。

  • I followed the signs to Metrocity. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    我循着指示标志找到了地铁城购物中心。

9 VERB (小径、河流等)沿着，经过 If something such as a path or river follows a particular route or line, it goes along that route or line. (小径、河流等)沿着，经过

  • Our route follows the Pacific coast through densely populated neighbourhoods. [VERB noun]

    我们的路线沿着太平洋海岸，途经人口稠密的街区。

  • The Lot river follows a winding and tortuous course. [VERB noun]

    洛特河的河道蜿蜒曲折。

10 VERB 注视(运动物体);(眼睛)跟随着 If you follow something with your eyes, or if your eyes follow it, you watch it as it moves or you look along its route or course. 注视(运动物体);(眼睛)跟随着

  • Ann's eyes followed a police car as it drove slowly past. [VERB noun]

    安注视着一辆缓缓驶过的警车。

11 VERB 按照…方式发展;以…方式发生 Something that follows a particular course of development happens or develops in that way. 按照…方式发展;以…方式发生

  • His release turned out to follow the pattern set by that of the other six hostages. [VERB noun]

    最终他的获释方式也和其他 6名人质的获释如出一辙。

12 VERB 接受，遵循(建议、指示等);按(菜谱)进行 If you follow advice, an instruction, or a recipe, you act or do something in the way that it indicates. 接受，遵循(建议、指示等);按(菜谱)进行

  • Take care to follow the instructions carefully. [VERB noun]

    注意严格按照说明进行。

  • No two chefs follow the same recipe. [VERB noun]

    每个厨师都有不同的烹饪方式。

SYN obey, observe, comply with, adhere to  
13 VERB 仿效;效仿 If you follow what someone else has done, you do it too because you think it is a good thing or because you want to copy them. 仿效;效仿

  • His admiration for the athlete did not extend to the point where he would follow his example in taking drugs. [VERB noun]

    他对于这个运动员的崇拜还没有达到要学他吸毒的程度。

  • Where eastern Germany goes the rest will surely follow. [VERB]

    东德怎样做，其他地方一定会效仿。

SYN copy, imitate, emulate, mimic  
14 VERB 沿袭;继承 If you follow someone in what you do, you do the same thing or job as they did previously. 沿袭;继承

  • He followed his father and became a surgeon. [VERB noun]

    他子承父业，成为了一名外科医生。

  • Anni-Frid's son has followed her into the music business. [VERB noun + into]

    安妮-弗瑞德的儿子子承母业，也进入了音乐圈。

SYN succeed, replace, come after, take over from  
15 VERB 理解，跟得上(解释或故事情节) If you are able to follow something such as an explanation or the story of a film, you understand it as it continues and develops. 理解，跟得上(解释或故事情节)

  • Can you follow the plot so far?. [VERB noun]

    目前为止剧情你能看得懂吗？

  • I'm afraid I don't follow. [VERB]

    恐怕我没明白。

SYN understand, get, see, catch  
16 VERB 追踪;对…感兴趣;关注 If you follow something, you take an interest in it and keep informed about what happens. 追踪;对…感兴趣;关注

  • ...the millions of people who follow football because they genuinely love it. [VERB noun]

    数以百万计的人关注足球，因为他们发自内心地热爱足球

  • She was following Laura's progress closely. [VERB noun]

    她密切关注着劳拉的进展。

SYN keep up with, support, be interested in, cultivate  
17 VERB 以…为原型;讲述…的故事 A story, film, or television programme that follows someone or something is about their experiences over a particular period of time. 以…为原型;讲述…的故事

  • The film follows the fortunes of two women. [VERB noun]

    这部电影讲述了两个女人的命运。

18 VERB (欣赏节目的同时)对照看(脚本或乐谱) If you follow a score or written copy of a play, you read it while you listen to it being performed. (欣赏节目的同时)对照看(脚本或乐谱)

  • ...an annotated version of Mozart's opera that allows the listener to follow the score. [VERB noun]

    使听者能够一边欣赏一边对照着看的莫扎特歌剧的注释乐谱

19 VERB 信奉;追随;支持;拥护 If you follow a particular religion or political belief, you have that religion or belief. 信奉;追随;支持;拥护

  • 'Do you follow any particular religion?' — 'Yes, we're all Hindus.' [VERB noun]

    “你们有自己的宗教信仰吗？”—“是的，我们都是印度教徒。”

20See also:following 
21 PHRASE 如下;分别是 You use as follows in writing or speech to introduce something such as a list, description, or explanation. 如下;分别是

  • The winners are as follows: E. Walker; R. Foster; R. Gates; A. Mackintosh.

    优胜者分别是：E.沃克、R.福斯特、R.盖茨和A.麦金托什。

  • This can be done if you proceed as follows.

    按照如下操作即可完成。

22 PHRASE 紧随其后;其次是 You use followed by to say what comes after something else in a list or ordered set of things. 紧随其后;其次是

  • Potatoes are still the most popular food, followed by white bread.

    马铃薯仍然是最受欢迎的食物，其次是白面包。

23 PHRASE 下一道菜 After mentioning one course of a meal, you can mention the next course by saying what you will have to follow or what there will be to follow . 下一道菜

  • He decided on roast chicken and vegetables, with apple pie to follow.

    他决定点烤鸡配蔬菜，然后是苹果派。

27 PHRASAL VERB 探究;钻研;深入研究 If you follow through an action, plan, or idea or follow through with it, you continue doing or thinking about it until you have done everything possible. 探究;钻研;深入研究

  • The leadership has been unwilling to follow through the implications of these ideas.

    领导层不愿意对这些想法的含意进行深入探究。

  • I was trained to be an actress but I didn't follow it through.

    我接受过演员培训，但是我没有坚持下去。

  • He decided to follow through with his original plan.

    他决定把最初的计划执行到底。

28 PHRASAL VERB 追查;将…深入下去;对…采取进一步行动 If you follow up something that has been said, suggested, or discovered, you try to find out more about it or take action about it. 追查;将…深入下去;对…采取进一步行动

  • State security police are following up several leads.

    国家安全警察正在追查几条线索。

  • An officer took a statement from me, but no one's bothered to follow it up.

    一位警官收集了我的供述，但却没有人愿意追查下去。
