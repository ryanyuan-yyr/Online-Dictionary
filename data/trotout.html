trot out
PHRASAL VERB 反复提出，反复说（陈旧的观点或过时的信息） If you say that a person trots out old ideas or information, you are criticizing him or her for repeating them in a way that is not new or interesting. 反复提出，反复说（陈旧的观点或过时的信息） [informal, disapproval] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Was it really necessary to trot out the same old stereotypes about Ireland? [VERB PREP. noun]

    真的有必要反复提关于爱尔兰的那套老看法吗？

SYN repeat, relate, exhibit, bring up
