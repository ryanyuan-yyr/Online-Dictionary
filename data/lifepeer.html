life peer
Word forms: plural life peers
N-COUNT （英国爵位不能世袭的）终身贵族 In Britain, a life peer is a person who is given a title such as 'Lord' or 'Lady' which they can use for the rest of their life but which they cannot pass on when they die. （英国爵位不能世袭的）终身贵族 [Also + of]

  • He was made a life peer in 1991.

    他于1991年被封为终身贵族。
