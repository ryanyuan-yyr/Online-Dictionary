spender
spendəʳ ˈspɛndər
Word forms: plural spenders
N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 花钱者；花钱…的人 If a person or organization is a big spender or a compulsive spender, for example, they spend a lot of money or are unable to stop themselves spending money. 花钱者；花钱…的人

  • The Swiss are Europe's biggest spenders on food.

    瑞士人是欧洲人中在食品上开销最大的人群。

  • Once the compulsive spender stops at the mall, she will be unable to control her spending.

    患强迫性购物症的人一旦来到购物中心，就无法控制自己的开支。
