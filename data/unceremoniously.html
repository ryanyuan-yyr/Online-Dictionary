unceremoniously
ʌnserɪmoʊniəsli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 随便地；突然地；唐突地 If someone or something is removed, left, or put somewhere unceremoniously, this is done in a sudden or rude way that shows they are not thought to be important. 随便地；突然地；唐突地

  • She was unceremoniously dumped to be replaced by a leader who could win the election.

    她被人踢掉了，取而代之的是一个能够赢得选举的领导人物。

  • He had to be bundled unceremoniously out of the way.

    他被人粗鲁地推搡到了一边。

SYN rudely, hastily, hurriedly, summarily
