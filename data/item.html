item
aɪtəm ˈaɪtəm
Word forms: plural items
1 N-COUNT (系列物品或清单中的)一件 An item is one of a collection or list of objects. (系列物品或清单中的)一件

  • The most valuable item on show will be a Picasso drawing.

    展览会上最贵重的展品是毕加索的一幅素描。

  • The menu includes the occasional off-beat item.

    菜单中包括了偶尔会有的非传统菜肴。

SYN article, thing, object, piece  
2See also:collector's item 
3 N-COUNT 项目;条目;条款 An item is one of a list of things for someone to do, deal with, or talk about. 项目;条目;条款

  • The other item on the agenda is the tour.

    日程中的另一项是旅游。

SYN matter, point, issue, case  
4 N-COUNT 一篇报道(或文章);(新闻等的)一条,一则 An item is a report or article in a newspaper or magazine, or on television or radio. 一篇报道(或文章);(新闻等的)一条,一则

  • There was an item in the paper about him.

    报纸上有一条关于他的报道。

SYN report, story, piece, account  
5 N-SING (关系亲密的)一对 If you say that two people are an item, you mean that they are having a romantic or sexual relationship. (关系亲密的)一对 [informal]

  • She and Gino were an item.

    她和吉诺曾是一对。
