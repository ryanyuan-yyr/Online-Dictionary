glower
glaʊəʳ ˈglaʊər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense glowers, present participle glowering, past tense , past participle glowered
VERB 怒视；瞪眼看 If you glower at someone or something, you look at them angrily. 怒视；瞪眼看 [Also V adv/prep]

  • He glowered at me but said nothing. [VERB + at]

    他怒视着我，却一言不发。

  • He glowered and glared, but she steadfastly refused to look his way. [VERB]

    他怒目而视，但是她铁了心不肯朝他这边看。

SYN scowl, glare, frown, look daggers
