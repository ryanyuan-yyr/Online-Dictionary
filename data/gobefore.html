go before
1 PHRASAL VERB （时间）居先；先前发生 Something that has gone before has happened or been discussed at an earlier time. （时间）居先；先前发生

  • This is a rejection of most of what has gone before. [VERB PREP.]

    这是对早先讨论过的很多事情的否定。

2 PHRASAL VERB 被提交给（法官、法庭等） To go before a judge, tribunal, or court of law means to be present there as part of an official or legal process. 被提交给（法官、法庭等）

  • The case went before Mr Justice Henry on December 23 and was adjourned. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该案件于12月23日被提交给高等法院法官亨利先生审理，后被延期。
