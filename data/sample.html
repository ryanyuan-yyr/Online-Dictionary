sample
sɑːmpəl sæm- ˈsæmpəl ˈsɑmpəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense samples, present participle sampling, past tense , past participle sampled
1 N-COUNT 样品;货样 A sample of a substance or product is a small quantity of it that shows you what it is like. 样品;货样

  • You'll receive samples of paint, curtains and upholstery. [+ of]

    你将收到涂料、窗帘、家具垫衬套的样品。

  • We're giving away 2000 free samples.

    我们正免费发放 2,000 份样品。

  • They asked me to do some sample drawings.

    他们请我画些样品图。

SYN specimen, example, trial, model  
2 N-COUNT (用于化验的)取样,样本 A sample of a substance is a small amount of it that is examined and analysed scientifically. (用于化验的)取样,样本

  • They took samples of my blood. [+ of]

    他们取了我的血样。

  • ...urine samples.

    尿样

3 N-COUNT (抽查的)样本;抽选人员;抽样 A sample of people or things is a number of them chosen out of a larger group and then used in tests or used to provide information about the whole group. (抽查的)样本;抽选人员;抽样

  • We based our analysis on a random sample of more than 200 males. [+ of]

    我们随机抽查了 200 多名男性，并在此基础上进行分析。

SYN cross section, test, sampling  
4 VERB 品尝;试尝 If you sample food or drink, you taste a small amount of it in order to find out if you like it. 品尝;试尝

  • We sampled a selection of different bottled waters. [VERB noun]

    我们品尝了一系列不同品牌的瓶装水。

SYN test, try, check out [informal] , experience  
5 VERB 体验;尝试 If you sample a place or situation, you experience it for a short time in order to find out about it. 体验;尝试

  • ...the chance to sample a different way of life. [VERB noun]

    体验不同生活方式的机会

6 VERB [usually passive] 节录,选录(乐曲) When musicians or pieces of their music are sampled, parts of their music are used by other musicians in their own work. 节录,选录(乐曲)

  • I don't actually mind being sampled as long as people give credit where it's due. [be VERB-ed]

    只要人们在适当处标明出处，我其实并不在乎自己的音乐被人节录。

SYN borrow from, rip off
