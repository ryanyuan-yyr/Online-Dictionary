cloverleaf
kloʊvərlif ˈkləʊvəˌliːf ˈkloʊvərˌlif
Word forms: cloverleafs, cloverleaves
N-COUNT (道路的)四叶式立体交叉A cloverleaf is an arrangement of curved roads, resembling a four-leaf clover, that joins two main roads. (道路的)四叶式立体交叉 [US]

  • The 405-167 motorway interchange is a classic cloverleaf, built in the 1960s.

    建于60年代的405-167高速公路交流道是一条经典的四叶式立体交叉道。
