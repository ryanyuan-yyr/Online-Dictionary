colourist
kʌlərɪst ˈkʌlərɪst
Word forms: plural colourists
in AM, use 美国英语用 colorist
1 N-COUNT 色彩画家;善于运用色彩的时装设计师 A colourist is someone such as an artist or a fashion designer who uses colours in an interesting and original way. 色彩画家;善于运用色彩的时装设计师
2 N-COUNT 染发师 A colourist is a hairdresser who specializes in colouring people's hair. 染发师
