concealment
kənsiːlmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT 掩盖;隐藏 Concealment is the state of being hidden or the act of hiding something. 掩盖;隐藏

  • The criminals vainly sought concealment from the searchlight.

    罪犯们徒劳地躲闪着探照灯光。

  • ...the concealment of weapons. [+ of]

    藏匿武器

SYN cover, hiding, camouflage, hiding place  
2 N-UNCOUNT 隐瞒;掩饰 The concealment of information or a feeling involves keeping it secret. 隐瞒;掩饰

  • His concealment of his true motives was masterly. [+ of]

    他隐瞒自己真实动机的手段十分高明。

  • I think there was deliberate concealment of relevant documents. [+ of]

    我认为有人故意隐瞒相关文件。

SYN cover-up, disguise, keeping secret
