micro
maɪkroʊ ˈmaɪkrəʊ ˈmaɪkroʊ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 微观的；具体的 You use micro to indicate that something relates to a specific area, rather than a general one 微观的；具体的 [mainly technical]

  • The vital task was to allow the economy to operate freely at a micro level.

    关键任务是要实现经济在微观层面的自由运转。

micro- maɪkroʊ- ˈmaɪkroʊ ˈmaɪkrə
PREFIX (用以构成名词)微，小 Micro- is used to form nouns that refer to something that is a very small example or fraction of a particular type of thing. (用以构成名词)微，小

  • These are the cells that directly attack and kill micro-organisms.

    这些细胞可以直接攻击和杀死微生物。

  • The pulse is usually timed in microseconds.

    脉冲通常以微秒计。
