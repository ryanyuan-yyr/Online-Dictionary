director
daɪrektəʳ dɪr- dɪˈrɛktə dɪˈrɛktər daɪˈrɛktər
Word forms: plural directors
1 N-COUNT （戏剧、电影、电视等的）导演 The director of a play, film, or television programme is the person who decides how it will appear on stage or screen, and who tells the actors and technical staff what to do. （戏剧、电影、电视等的）导演
2 N-COUNT 主任;主管;署长;局长 In some organizations and public authorities, the person in charge is referred to as the director . 主任;主管;署长;局长

  • ...the director of the intensive care unit at Guy's Hospital. [+ of]

    盖伊医院重症护理病房的主任

  • ...the Director of Public Prosecutions.

    (英格兰以及威尔士的)检察官

  • She has just been appointed artistic director of Queensland Theatre Company.

    她刚刚被任命为昆士兰剧团的艺术总监。

SYN controller, head, leader, manager  
3 N-COUNT (公司的)经理，董事 The directors of a company are its most senior managers, who meet regularly to make important decisions about how it will be run. (公司的)经理，董事 [business]

  • He served on the board of directors of a local bank.

    他是当地一家银行的董事会成员之一。

  • ...Karl Uggerholt, the financial director of Braun UK.

    卡尔·乌格洛尔特，贝朗英国分公司的财务经理

4 N-COUNT (管弦乐队或合唱团的)指挥 The director of an orchestra or choir is the person who is conducting it. (管弦乐队或合唱团的)指挥 [US]
