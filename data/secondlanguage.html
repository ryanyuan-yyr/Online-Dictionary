second language
Word forms: plural second languages
N-COUNT 第二语言 Someone's second language is a language which is not their native language but which they use at work or at school. 第二语言

  • Lucy teaches English as a second language.

    露西从事作为第二语言的英语教学工作。

  • French remained her second language for the rest of her life.

    在她的余生中，法语一直是她的第二语言。
