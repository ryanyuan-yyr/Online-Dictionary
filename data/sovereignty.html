sovereignty
sɒvrɪnti ˈsɑvrənti ˈsɑvərɪnti ˈsʌvrənti ˈsʌvərɪnti
N-UNCOUNT (国家的)主权，自主统治权 Sovereignty is the power that a country has to govern itself or another country or state. (国家的)主权，自主统治权

  • Britain's concern to protect national sovereignty is far from new.

    英国对捍卫国家主权的关切早已不是什么新鲜事了。

SYN supreme power, domination, supremacy, primacy
