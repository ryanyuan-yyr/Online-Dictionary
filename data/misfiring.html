misfire
mɪsfaɪəʳ mɪsˈfaɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misfires, present participle misfiring, past tense , past participle misfired
1 VERB 未奏效;未获得预期效果;失败 If a plan misfires, it goes wrong and does not have the results that you intend it to have. 未奏效;未获得预期效果;失败

  • Some of their policies had misfired. [VERB]

    他们的一些政策已经失败了。

SYN fail, go wrong, fall through, miscarry  
2 VERB (引擎)不发火，不启动 If an engine misfires, the fuel fails to start burning when it should. (引擎)不发火，不启动

  • The boat's engine misfired after he tried to start it up. [VERB]

    他试着启动船的引擎，但没发动起来。

3 VERB (枪)不能开火，射不出子弹 If a gun misfires, the bullet is not sent out as it should be when the gun is fired. (枪)不能开火，射不出子弹

  • The gun misfired after one shot and jammed. [VERB]

    开过一枪后，枪哑火卡住了。
