indulgent
ɪndʌldʒənt ɪnˈdʌldʒənt
ADJ-GRADED 纵容的;宽厚的;溺爱的 If you are indulgent, you treat a person with special kindness, often in a way that is not good for them. 纵容的;宽厚的;溺爱的

  • His indulgent mother was willing to let him do anything he wanted.

    他母亲很溺爱他，任由他想干什么就干什么。

SYN lenient, liberal, kind, kindly  
indulgently

  • Ned smiled at him indulgently.

    内德宽容地对他笑了笑。
