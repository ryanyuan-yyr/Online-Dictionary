resemble
rɪzembəl rɪˈzɛmbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense resembles, present participle resembling, past tense , past participle resembled
VERB [no cont] 类似;与…相似;与…相像 If one thing or person resembles another, they are similar to each other. 类似;与…相似;与…相像

  • Some of the commercially produced venison resembles beef in flavour. [VERB noun]

    有些商业化养殖的鹿肉味道和牛肉很相似。

  • She so resembles her mother. [VERB noun]

    她很像她母亲。

SYN be like, look like, favour [informal] , mirror
