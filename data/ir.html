ir-ɪr- ɪr- ɪr
Usually pronounced /'ɪr-/ before an unstressed syllable, and /ɪr-/ before a stressed syllable. 通常在非重读音节前重读,在重读音节前弱读。
PREFIX (加在以字母r开头的单词前构成反义词)表示“不”，“非” Ir- is added to words that begin with the letter 'r' to form words with the opposite meaning. (加在以字母r开头的单词前构成反义词)表示“不”，“非”

  • His behaviour was becoming increasingly irrational.

    他的行为变得越来越不理智了。

  • ...its mixture of satirical wit, irreverence and spontaneity.

    它融讽刺、机智、不敬、率真于一体
