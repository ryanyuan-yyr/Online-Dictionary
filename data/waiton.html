wait on
PHRASAL VERB 等待（某事发生） If you are waiting on something, you are waiting for it to happen, for example before you do or decide anything. 等待（某事发生） [US]

  • Since then I've been waiting on events. [VERB PREP. noun]

    自那以后，我就一直在观望事情的发展。

  • We cannot wait on the government to make changes at its own pace. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们不可能等待政府按照它自己的步调作出改变。
