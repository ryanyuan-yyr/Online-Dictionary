tumbler
tʌmbləʳ ˈtʌmblər
Word forms: plural tumblers
1 N-COUNT (平底)玻璃杯 A tumbler is a drinking glass with straight sides. (平底)玻璃杯

  • He took a tumbler from a cupboard.

    他从碗橱里拿出一只平底玻璃杯。

The contents of a tumbler can be referred to as a tumbler of something.一平底玻璃杯之量

  • Add a few drops to half a tumbler of water.

    在半玻璃杯水里面加上几滴。

2 N-COUNT （在地上表演的）杂技演员;翻筋斗杂技演员 A tumbler is an acrobat who performs on the ground, often with other members of a group. （在地上表演的）杂技演员;翻筋斗杂技演员
