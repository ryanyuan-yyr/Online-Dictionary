roam
roʊm roʊm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense roams, present participle roaming, past tense , past participle roamed
VERB 漫步;漫游;游荡;闲逛 If you roam an area or roam around it, you wander or travel around it without having a particular purpose. 漫步;漫游;游荡;闲逛

  • Barefoot children roamed the streets. [VERB noun]

    光脚丫的孩子们在街上游荡。

  • They're roaming around the country shooting at anything that moves. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他们在乡下到处转悠，看到活动的东西就开枪。

  • Farmers were encouraged to keep their livestock in pens rather than letting them roam freely. [VERB]

    鼓励农民们把家畜关进栏圈，而不是让它们到处乱跑。

SYN wander, walk, range, travel
