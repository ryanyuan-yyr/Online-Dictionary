lambast

Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lambasts, present participle lambasting, past tense , past participle lambasted
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 lambaste /læm'beɪst/
VERB （通常指公开地）猛烈批评，痛斥，炮轰 If you lambast someone, you criticize them severely, usually in public. （通常指公开地）猛烈批评，痛斥，炮轰 [formal]

    格雷不放过任何一个炮轰汤普森及其组织的机会。

SYN reprimand, carpet [informal] , flame [informal] , censure
