clarity
klærɪti ˈklærəti
1 N-UNCOUNT (书、论点等的)清楚，清晰易懂，明晰 The clarity of something such as a book or argument is its quality of being well explained and easy to understand. (书、论点等的)清楚，清晰易懂，明晰

  • ...the ease and clarity with which the author explains difficult technical and scientific subjects.

    作者解释复杂的科技主题时的游刃有余、清晰透彻

SYN clearness, precision, simplicity, transparency  
2 N-UNCOUNT 思路清晰;有条理 Clarity is the ability to think clearly. 思路清晰;有条理

  • In business circles he is noted for his flair and clarity of vision. [+ of]

    在商界，他的天赋和远见卓识是出了名的。

3 N-UNCOUNT (轮廓、声音的)清楚，清晰 Clarity is the quality of being clear in outline or sound. (轮廓、声音的)清楚，清晰

  • This remarkable technology provides far greater clarity than conventional x-rays.

    这项卓越技术的清晰度比常规的X光高得多。

4 N-UNCOUNT (液体、玻璃或空气的)清澈度，透明度 The clarity of a liquid, of glass, or of the air is the degree to which it is clear. (液体、玻璃或空气的)清澈度，透明度

  • The first thing to strike me was the amazing clarity of the water.

    首先吸引我的是水的无比清澈。

SYN transparency, translucency, translucence, clearness
