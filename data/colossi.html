colossus
kəlɒsəs kəˈlɑsəs
Word forms: plural colossi/kəlɒsaɪ/
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 巨人;伟人;庞然大物;极重要的事情 If you describe someone or something as a colossus, you think that they are extremely important and great in ability or size. 巨人;伟人;庞然大物;极重要的事情 [journalism, emphasis]

  • He was a colossus, a legend.

    他是一个伟人，一个传奇人物。

  • ...saxophone colossus Sonny Rollins.

    萨克斯演奏大师桑尼·罗利斯

  • He became a colossus of the labour movement. [+ of]

    他成为了劳工运动中举足轻重的人物。

2 N-COUNT 巨型雕塑 A colossus is an extremely large statue. 巨型雕塑
