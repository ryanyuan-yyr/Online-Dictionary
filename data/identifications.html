identification
aɪdentɪfɪkeɪʃən aɪˌdɛntəfɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural identifications
1 N-VAR 识别;鉴定;确认 The identification of something is the recognition that it exists, is important, or is true. 识别;鉴定;确认

  • Early identification of a disease can prevent death and illness. [+ of]

    病症的及早诊断可避免死亡与病痛。

  • Their work includes the identification of genes which govern the growth rate and fertility.

    他们的工作包括找出那些控制生长率和繁殖能力的基因。

  • There should be some identification of goals, and how far these have been achieved.

    应该对目标加以明确，并确认完成情况。

SYN discovery, recognition, determining, establishment  
2 N-VAR 识别;验明;认出 Your identification of a particular person or thing is your ability to name them because you know them or recognize them. 识别;验明;认出

  • Officials are awaiting positive identification before charging the men with war crimes.

    在以战争罪起诉这些人以前，官员们一直在等待他们身份的核实。

  • He's made a formal identification of the body. [+ of]

    他正式确认了死者身份。

  • ...the Hamlyn identification guide to wild flowers of Britain.

    哈姆林对英国野生花草的鉴定指导

SYN recognition, naming, labelling, distinguishing  
3 N-UNCOUNT 身份证明;身份证件 If someone asks you for some identification, they want to see something such as a driving licence, which proves who you are. 身份证明;身份证件

  • The woman who was on passport control asked me if I had any further identification.

    检查护照的女士问我是否有进一步的身份证明。

4 N-VAR 密切关联;相互关联 The identification of one person or thing with another is the close association of one with the other. 密切关联;相互关联

  • Throughout the Balkans, there is a close identification of nationhood with language.

    整个巴尔干半岛上，国家地位与语言有着非常密切的联系。

  • ...the identification of Spain with Catholicism. [+ of/with]

    西班牙与天主教的密切联系

SYN connection, relationship, link, association  
5 N-UNCOUNT 认同;同情;支持 Identification with someone or something is the feeling of sympathy and support for them. 认同;同情;支持

  • I need your full, emotional identification with the problem and with me.

    我需要你对我、对此问题都有充分的情感认同与支持。

  • Marilyn had an intense identification with animals. [+ with]

    玛丽莲非常怜爱动物。

SYN understanding, relationship, involvement, unity
