password
pɑːswɜːʳd pæs- ˈpæsˌwɜrd
Word forms: plural passwords
N-COUNT 口令；密码 A password is a secret word or phrase that you must know in order to be allowed to enter a place such as a military base, or to be allowed to use a computer system. 口令；密码

  • Advance and give the password.

    上前说出口令。

  • No-one could use the computer unless they had a password.

    除非知道密码，否则谁也用不了那台计算机。

SYN watchword, key word, magic word [informal] , open sesame
