artificial respiration
N-UNCOUNT 人工呼吸 Artificial respiration is the forcing of air into the lungs of someone who has stopped breathing, usually by blowing through their mouth or nose, in order to keep them alive and to help them to start breathing again. 人工呼吸

  • She was given artificial respiration and cardiac massage.

    有人给她做了人工呼吸和心脏按压。
