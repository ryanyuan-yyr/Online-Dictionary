recede
rɪsiːd ˌriˈsid rɪˈsid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recedes, present participle receding, past tense , past participle receded
1 VERB 退回;后退;远离 If something recedes from you, it moves away. 退回;后退;远离

  • Luke's footsteps receded into the night. [VERB prep.]

    卢克在夜色中渐行渐远。

  • As she receded he waved goodbye. [VERB]

    她离开的时候他挥手告别。

  • ...the receding lights of the car. [VERB-ing]

    越来越远的汽车灯光

SYN fall back, withdraw, retreat, draw back  
2 VERB （质量）下降;（问题或疾病等）逐渐减弱，好转 When something such as a quality, problem, or illness recedes, it becomes weaker, smaller, or less intense. （质量）下降;（问题或疾病等）逐渐减弱，好转 [Also VERB preposition]

  • Just as I started to think that I was never going to get well, the illness began to recede. [VERB]

    正当我开始觉得自己永远都好不了了的时候，我的病开始好转了。

  • Dealers grew concerned over the sliding dollar and receding prospects for economic recovery. [VERB-ing]

    交易者对美元走软和经济复苏前景渐趋暗淡渐渐担忧起来。

3 VERB （前额的头发）脱落 If a man's hair starts to recede, it no longer grows on the front of his head. （前额的头发）脱落 [Also V at/from n]

  • ...a youngish man with dark hair just beginning to recede. [VERB]

    前额黑发刚开始脱落的年轻人

  • The bartender had a florid face and a receding hairline. [VERB-ing]

    酒吧男招待面色红润，前额有点脱发。

SYN thin  
4 VERB （常指牙龈因为感染）萎缩 If your gums start to recede, they begin to cover less of your teeth, usually as the result of an infection. （常指牙龈因为感染）萎缩

  • If untreated, the gums recede, become swollen and bleed. [VERB]

    如果不进行治疗，牙龈会开始萎缩、红肿和出血。

  • Receding gums can be the result of disease or simply incorrect brushing. [VERB-ing]

    牙龈萎缩可能是疾病导致的，也可能仅仅是错误的刷牙方式引起的。
