profiteer
prɒfɪtɪəʳ ˌprɑfɪˈtɪr
Word forms: plural profiteers
N-COUNT [usually plural] 奸商；投机商；牟取暴利的人 If you describe someone as a profiteer, you are critical of them because they make large profits by charging high prices for goods that are hard to get. 奸商；投机商；牟取暴利的人 [disapproval]

  • ...a new social class composed largely of war profiteers and gangsters.

    主要由战争投机商和强盗组成的新的社会阶层

SYN racketeer, exploiter, extortionist, bloodsucker [informal]
