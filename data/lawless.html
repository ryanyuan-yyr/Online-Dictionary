lawless
lɔːləs ˈlɔlɪs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （尤指疯狂暴力的行为）违法的，非法的，不法的 Lawless actions break the law, especially in a wild and violent way. （尤指疯狂暴力的行为）违法的，非法的，不法的

  • The government recognised there were problems in urban areas but these could never be an excuse for lawless behaviour.

    政府承认市区存在问题，但决不可拿这些作为不法行为的借口。

SYN disorderly, wild, unruly, rebellious  
lawlessness

  • Lawlessness is a major problem.

    法治败坏是一个重大问题。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （某一地方或时期）目无法纪的，法网不及的 A lawless place or time is one where people do not respect the law. （某一地方或时期）目无法纪的，法网不及的

  • ...lawless inner-city streets plagued by muggings, thefts, assaults and even murder.

    不断有抢劫、盗窃、伤人乃至谋杀案件发生的无法无天的市中心区街道

  • ...people struggling to restore moral values in an increasingly lawless and godless age.

    在一个越来越罔顾法纪、缺失信仰的时代力图重树道德观的人们
