recovery room
rɪˈkʌvərɪ rʊm
Word forms: recovery rooms
N-COUNT (手术后)观察病房; 恢复室A recovery room is a room in a hospital where patients are placed after they have had an operation under anesthetia, so that they can be monitored while they recover. (手术后)观察病房; 恢复室

  • He was monitored in the recovery room for approximately 30 minutes following the anaesthesia.

    麻醉后，他在观察病房中待了大约30分钟。
