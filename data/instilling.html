instil
ɪnstɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense instils, present participle instilling, past tense , past participle instilled
in AM, use 美国英语用 instill
VERB 逐渐灌输 If you instil an idea or feeling in someone, especially over a period of time, you make them think it or feel it. 逐渐灌输

  • They hope that their work will instil a sense of responsibility in children. [V n + in/into]

    他们希望自己的工作能够逐步培养起孩子们的一种责任感。

  • The motive of the executions would be to instil fear. [VERB noun]

    执行死刑的动机是要灌输恐惧。

SYN introduce, implant, engender, infuse
