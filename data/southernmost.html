southernmost
sʌðəʳnmoʊst ˈsʌðənˌməʊst ˈsʌðərnˌmoʊst
ADJ-SUPERL [usually ADJ n] 最南的;极南的 The southernmost part of an area or the southernmost place is the one that is farthest towards the south. 最南的;极南的

  • The ancient province of Satsuma lies in the southernmost part of the Japanese island of Kyushu.

    古老的萨摩省位于日本九州岛最南端。

  • ...Aswan, Egypt's southernmost city.

    阿斯旺—埃及最南端的城市
