casserole
kæsəroʊl ˈkæsəˌroʊl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural casseroles, present participle casseroling, past tense , past participle casseroled
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (以肉、蔬菜为原料做成的)炖菜，砂锅菜 A casserole is a dish made of meat and vegetables that have been cooked slowly in a liquid. (以肉、蔬菜为原料做成的)炖菜，砂锅菜

  • ...a huge beef casserole, full of herbs, vegetables and wine.

    一大份加满了香料、蔬菜和葡萄酒的牛腩煲

2 N-COUNT 砂锅;炖锅 A casserole or a casserole dish is a large heavy container with a lid. You cook casseroles and other dishes in it. 砂锅;炖锅

  • Place all the chopped vegetables into a casserole dish.

    把所有切好的蔬菜放入砂锅。

  • ...a flameproof casserole.

    耐火砂锅

3 VERB 用文火烹煮;炖 If you casserole meat and vegetables, you cook them slowly in a liquid. 用文火烹煮;炖

  • If you casserole chicken pieces, take the skin off first. [VERB noun]

    要是炖鸡块的话，先去掉鸡皮。

  • ...casseroled chicken. [VERB-ed]

    鸡煲
