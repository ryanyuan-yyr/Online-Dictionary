china
tʃaɪnə ˈtʃaɪnə
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 瓷;瓷料 China is a hard white substance made from clay. It is used to make things such as cups, bowls, plates, and ornaments. 瓷;瓷料

  • ...a small boat made of china.

    瓷制小船

  • ...china cups.

    瓷杯

2See also:bone china 
3 N-UNCOUNT 瓷器;瓷制品 Cups, bowls, plates, and ornaments made of china are referred to as china . 瓷器;瓷制品

  • Judy collects blue and white china.

    朱迪收集青花瓷。

SYN pottery, ceramics, ware, porcelain  
4 a bull in a china shop→see: bull
