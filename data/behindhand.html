behindhand
bɪhaɪndhænd bɪˈhaɪndˌhænd bɪˈhaɪndˌhænd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （进度）落后的，拖延的 If someone is behindhand, they have been delayed or have made less progress in their work than they or other people think they should. （进度）落后的，拖延的

  • Poor Dr Pegler got terribly behindhand with his appointments.

    可怜的佩格勒博士大大落后于当初的约定。

SYN behind schedule, late, slow, delayed
