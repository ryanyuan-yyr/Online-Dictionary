demeaning
dɪmiːnɪŋ dɪˈmiːnɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 降低身份的;贬低的;贬损的 Something that is demeaning makes people have less respect for the person who is treated in that way, or who does that thing. 降低身份的;贬低的;贬损的 [Also + to]

  • ...demeaning sexist comments.

    侮辱性的性别歧视言论

  • Aid, however it is obtained, is demeaning to the recipients.

    不管救助是如何取得的，对受益人来说都不是很光彩的事。

SYN humiliating, degrading, disgraceful, shameful
