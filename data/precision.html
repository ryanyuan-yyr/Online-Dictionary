precision
prɪsɪʒən priˈsɪʒən prɪˈsɪʒən
N-UNCOUNT [oft with NOUN] 精确；精密 If you do something with precision, you do it exactly as it should be done. 精确；精密

  • The choir sang with precision.

    唱诗班的演唱准确无误。

  • The interior is planned with a precision the military would be proud of.

    内部设计一丝不苟，足以让军方引以为豪。

SYN exactness, care, accuracy, fidelity
