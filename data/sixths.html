sixth
sɪksθ sɪksθ
Word forms: plural sixths
1 ORD 第六 The sixth item in a series is the one that you count as number six. 第六

  • ...the sixth round of the World Cup.

    世界杯第六轮的比赛

  • ...the sixth of December.

    12 月 6 日

2 FRACTION 六分之一 A sixth is one of six equal parts of something. 六分之一

  • The company yesterday shed a sixth of its workforce.

    这家公司昨天裁掉了 1/6 的员工。

  • ...five-sixths of a mile.

    5/6 英里
