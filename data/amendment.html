amendment
əmendmənt əˈmɛndmənt
Word forms: plural amendments
1 N-VAR （法令或法规的）修正条款，修正案 An amendment is a section that is added to a law or rule in order to change it. （法令或法规的）修正条款，修正案

  • ...an amendment to the defense bill.

    防御法案的修正案

  • ...a constitutional amendment.

    宪法修正条款

  • Parliament gained certain rights of amendment.

    议会得到一定的修正权利。

SYN addition, change, adjustment, attachment  
2 N-COUNT 修改；修订 An amendment is a change that is made to a piece of writing. 修改；修订
