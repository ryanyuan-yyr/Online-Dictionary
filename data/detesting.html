detest
dɪtest diˈtɛst dɪˈtɛst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense detests, present participle detesting, past tense , past participle detested
VERB 厌恶;憎恶 If you detest someone or something, you dislike them very much. 厌恶;憎恶

  • My mother detested him. [VERB noun]

    我母亲对他很是厌恶。

  • Jean detested being photographed. [VERB verb-ing]

    琼非常讨厌拍照。

SYN hate, loathe, despise, abhor
