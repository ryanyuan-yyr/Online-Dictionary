nipple
nɪpəl ˈnɪpəl
Word forms: plural nipples

  • Sore nipples can inhibit the milk supply.

    乳头发炎会抑制出奶。

2 N-COUNT (橡皮或塑料)奶嘴 A nipple is a piece of rubber or plastic which is fitted to the top of a baby's bottle. (橡皮或塑料)奶嘴

  • ...a white plastic bottle with a rubber nipple.

    有橡皮奶嘴的白色塑料奶瓶
