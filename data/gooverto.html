go over to
1 PHRASAL VERB 转而采取（其他办法） If someone or something goes over to a different way of doing things, they change to it. 转而采取（其他办法）

  • The Armed Forces could do away with conscription and go over to a volunteer system. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    军队可以废除强制服兵役制度而采取自愿入伍的形式。

2 PHRASAL VERB 投奔，转投（对立组织或政党） If you go over to a group or political party, you join them after previously belonging to an opposing group or party. 投奔，转投（对立组织或政党）

  • Only a small number of tanks and paratroops have gone over to his side. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    只有少量坦克兵和伞兵投奔到他旗下。
