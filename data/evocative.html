evocative
ɪvɒkətɪv ɪˈvɒkətərɪ iˈvɑkətɪv ɪˈvɑkətɪv
ADJ-GRADED 唤起回忆的;引起感情共鸣的 If you describe something as evocative, you mean that it is good or interesting because it produces pleasant memories, ideas, emotions, and responses in people. 唤起回忆的;引起感情共鸣的 [formal]

  • Her story is sharply evocative of Italian provincial life. [+ of]

    她的故事让人清晰地回忆起意大利的乡村生活。

  • ...the evocative power of cinema.

    电影引起共鸣的力量

SYN expressive, moving, striking, revealing  
evocatively

  • ...the collection of islands evocatively known as the South Seas.

    一个让人充满回忆的名为南海的群岛
