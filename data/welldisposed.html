well disposed wɛl dɪˈspəʊzd
(also well-disposed)
ADJ-GRADED 对…友好的;支持…的 If you are well disposed to a person, plan, or activity, you are likely to agree with them or support them. 对…友好的;支持…的

  • They are likely to be well disposed to an offer of a separate peace deal. [+ to]

    他们很可能会赞同单独签订和平协议的提案。

  • He felt well disposed towards her. [+ towards]

    他对她很有好感。
