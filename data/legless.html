legless
legləs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 无腿的 A legless person or animal has no legs. 无腿的

  • ...Douglas Bader, the legless wartime fighter pilot.

    道格拉斯·巴德—昔日战场上的无腿战斗机飞行员

  • The slow-worm is in fact not a snake but a legless lizard.

    蛇蜥其实不是蛇，而是无脚的蜥蜴。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 烂醉如泥的 If you say that someone is legless, you mean that they are extremely drunk. 烂醉如泥的 [British, informal]

  • They found the locals getting legless on tequila.

    他们发现当地人喝龙舌兰喝得酩酊大醉。
