consuming
kənsjuːmɪŋ -suː-
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 令人沉迷的;极其强烈的;令人忘乎一切的 A consuming passion or interest is more important to you than anything else. 令人沉迷的;极其强烈的;令人忘乎一切的

  • He has developed a consuming passion for chess.

    他对下棋产生了极为浓厚的兴趣。

SYN overwhelming, gripping, absorbing, compelling
