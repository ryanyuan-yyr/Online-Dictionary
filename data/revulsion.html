revulsion
rɪvʌlʃən rɪˈvʌlʃən
N-UNCOUNT 深恶痛绝;强烈反对;嫌恶 Someone's revulsion at something is the strong feeling of disgust or disapproval they have towards it. 深恶痛绝;强烈反对;嫌恶

  • ...their revulsion at the act of desecration.

    他们对亵渎神灵的行为的憎恶

  • His voice was filled with horror and revulsion.

    他的声音里充满了恐惧和反感。

SYN disgust, loathing, distaste, aversion
