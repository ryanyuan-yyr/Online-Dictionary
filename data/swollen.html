swollen
swoʊlən ˈswoʊlən
1 ADJ-GRADED (身体部位)肿胀的,肿起来的 If a part of your body is swollen, it is larger and rounder than normal, usually as a result of injury or illness. (身体部位)肿胀的,肿起来的

  • My eyes were so swollen I could hardly see.

    我的眼睛肿得很厉害，几乎看不到东西。

SYN enlarged, bloated, puffy, inflamed  
2 ADJ-GRADED (河水)上涨的,湍流的 A swollen river has more water in it and flows faster than normal, usually because of heavy rain. (河水)上涨的,湍流的
3 Swollen is the past participle of swell.
