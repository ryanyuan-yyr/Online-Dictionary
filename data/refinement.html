refinement
rɪfaɪnmənt rɪˈfaɪnmənt
Word forms: plural refinements
1 N-VAR 完善;修正;改进 Refinements are small changes or additions that you make to something in order to improve it. Refinement is the process of making refinements. 完善;修正;改进

  • Older cars inevitably lack the latest safety refinements.

    老款的汽车难免缺少经过改进的最新安全保护装置。

  • ...development and refinement of the game.

    比赛的发展和改进

2 N-UNCOUNT 彬彬有礼;有教养 Refinement is politeness and good manners. 彬彬有礼;有教养

  • ...a girl who possessed both dignity and refinement.

    既有尊严又有教养的女孩

SYN sophistication, finish, style, culture
