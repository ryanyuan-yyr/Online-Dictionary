self-effacing
ADJ-GRADED 自我谦避的;不求闻达的 Someone who is self-effacing does not like talking about themselves or drawing attention to themselves. 自我谦避的;不求闻达的

  • As women we tend to be self-effacing and make light of what we have achieved.

    作为女性我们往往不喜夸耀，对自己获得的成功轻描淡写。

  • ...the slightly self-effacing manner adopted by many diplomats.

    许多外交官所持的那种稍稍自我谦避的态度
