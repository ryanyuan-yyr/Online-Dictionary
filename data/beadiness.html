beady
biːdi ˈbidi
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （眼睛）珠子般的，圆而亮的 Beady eyes are small, round, and bright. （眼睛）珠子般的，圆而亮的

  • Meg felt the old woman's beady eyes on her.

    梅格觉得老太太那双圆溜溜的小眼睛正盯着她。

SYN bright, powerful, concentrated, sharp  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （目光）仔细而怀疑的 If someone keeps a beady eye on a person or organization, they watch them carefully and suspiciously. （目光）仔细而怀疑的

  • The chairman keeps a beady eye on things.

    主席小心地看管着这些东西。
