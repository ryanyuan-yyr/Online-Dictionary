rub in
1 PHRASAL VERB 按摩使…吸收;搽 If you rub a substance in, you press it into something by continuously moving it over its surface. 按摩使…吸收;搽 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • When hair is dry, rub in a little oil to make it smooth and glossy. [VERB PREP. noun]

    等头发干了以后，搽上一点油，使其顺滑而有光泽。

2 PHRASAL VERB 不断提及（某人不愿记住的事） If someone keeps reminding you of something you would rather forget you can say that they are rubbing it in . 不断提及（某人不愿记住的事）

  • Officials couldn't resist rubbing it in. [VERB noun PREP.]

    官员们忍不住反复讲这件事。

  • The home side rubbed in their superiority with a further goal. [VERB PREP. noun]

    主队再进一球从而奠定了优势。

SYN make an issue of, stress, highlight, emphasize
