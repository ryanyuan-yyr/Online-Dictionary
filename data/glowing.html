glowing
gloʊɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 热烈赞扬的；热情洋溢的 A glowing description or opinion about someone or something praises them highly or supports them strongly. 热烈赞扬的；热情洋溢的

  • The media has been speaking in glowing terms of the relationship between the two countries.

    媒体一直盛赞两国关系。

  • The premieres of his plays brought in glowing reviews.

    他首演的几部戏都获得了如潮好评。

SYN complimentary, enthusiastic, rave [informal] , ecstatic  
2See also:glow 
glowingly

  • Wallis spoke glowingly about players like Chapman.

    沃利斯对查普曼这样的选手大加赞赏。
