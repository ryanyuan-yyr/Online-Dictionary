red herring
Word forms: plural red herrings
N-COUNT 遮眼法；转移注意力之物 If you say that something is a red herring, you mean that it is not important and it takes your attention away from the main subject or problem you are considering. 遮眼法；转移注意力之物

  • As Dr Smith left he said that the inquiry was something of a red herring.

    史密斯博士离开时说这次调查只是为掩人耳目。
