nothingness
nʌθɪŋnəs ˈnʌθɪŋnɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT 不存在;无 Nothingness is the fact of not existing. 不存在;无

  • There might be something beyond the grave, you know, and not nothingness.

    坟墓之外也许还有些什么，你知道，并非万事皆空。

SYN oblivion, nullity, nonexistence, nonbeing  
2 N-UNCOUNT 虚无;空虚 Nothingness means complete emptiness. 虚无;空虚

  • Her eyes, glazed with the drug, stared with half closed lids at nothingness.

    她因吸食毒品而目光呆滞，双眼半睁半闭，茫然呆视。
