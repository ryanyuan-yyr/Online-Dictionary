cruelty
kruːəlti ˈkruːəltɪ ˈkruəlti
Word forms: plural cruelties
N-VAR 残暴;残忍;残酷 Cruelty is behaviour that deliberately causes pain or distress to people or animals. 残暴;残忍;残酷

  • Britain had laws against cruelty to animals but none to protect children. [+ to]

    英国有禁止虐待动物的法律，但却没有保护儿童的法律。

  • He had been unable to escape the cruelties of war. [+ of]

    他没能逃脱战争的残酷。

SYN brutality, spite, severity, savagery
