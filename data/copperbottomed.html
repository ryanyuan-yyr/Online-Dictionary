copper-bottomed
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 定会成功的;稳妥保险的 If you describe something as copper-bottomed, you believe that it is certain to be successful. 定会成功的;稳妥保险的 [British]

  • The combination of sex and treachery proved a copper-bottomed circulation booster.

    事实证明，性和背叛的话题结合起来无疑大大增加了发行量。

  • Their copper-bottomed scheme went badly wrong.

    他们认为自己的计划万无一失，结果一败涂地。
