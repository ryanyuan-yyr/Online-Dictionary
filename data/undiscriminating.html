undiscriminating
ʌndɪskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ ˌʌndɪsˈkrɪmɪˌneɪtɪŋ
1 ADJ 无鉴别力的Someone who is undiscriminating is not able to judge which things are of good quality and therefore does not make good choices. 无鉴别力的 [disapproval]

  • These are shows aimed at the undiscriminating viewer.

    这些表演是针对没有鉴赏力的观众的。

2 ADJ 无区别的If something such as an action or policy is described as undiscriminating, it does not make careful distinctions between people or things. 无区别的 [disapproval]

  • Her parents were determined to do something to help other victims of this undiscriminating disease.

    她父母决心要做些什么来帮助这种所有人都容易感染的疾病的受害者。
