giveaway
gɪvəweɪ ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ ˈgɪvəˌweɪ
(also give-away)Word forms: plural giveaways
1 N-SING 暴露真相的事物 A giveaway is something that makes you realize the truth about a particular person or situation. 暴露真相的事物

  • The only giveaway was the look of amusement in her eyes.

    唯独她俏皮的眼神使事情露馅儿了。

SYN sign, suggestion, hint, indication  
2 N-COUNT （通常为吸引顾客而派发的）赠品 A giveaway is something that a company or organization gives to someone, usually in order to encourage people to buy a particular product. （通常为吸引顾客而派发的）赠品

  • Next week TODAY is celebrating with a great giveaway of FREE garden seeds.

    下周，《今日》杂志将举行庆祝活动，免费赠送大量花种。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 低廉的；廉价的 When you talk about giveaway prices, you are emphasizing that the prices are very low. 低廉的；廉价的 [emphasis]

  • Wine and food of superlative quality are available everywhere at giveaway prices.

    随处都能买到价格低廉而质量上乘的酒和食品。
