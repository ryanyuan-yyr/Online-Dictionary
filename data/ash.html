ash
æʃ æʃ
Word forms: plural ashes
1 N-UNCOUNT 灰；灰烬 Ash is the grey or black powdery substance that is left after something is burnt. You can also refer to this substance as ashes . 灰；灰烬

  • A cloud of volcanic ash is spreading across wide areas of the Philippines.

    火山灰云层正在菲律宾大范围扩散。

  • He brushed the cigarette ash from his sleeve.

    他掸掉了袖子上的烟灰。

  • He ordered their villages burned to ashes.

    他下令将他们的村庄烧成灰烬。

2 N-PLURAL [usually poss NOUN] 骨灰 A dead person's ashes are their remains after their body has been cremated. 骨灰
3 N-VAR 白蜡树; An ash is a tree that has smooth grey bark and loses its leaves in winter. 白蜡树;

  • ...a high forest of oak and ash.

    生长着橡树和白蜡树的高山林

Ash is the wood from this tree.木

  • The rafters are made from ash.

    椽是用木做的。
