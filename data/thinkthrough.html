think through
PHRASAL VERB 充分考虑,全盘考虑，想透(所有可能的影响或结果) If you think a situation through, you consider it thoroughly, together with all its possible effects or consequences. 充分考虑,全盘考虑，想透(所有可能的影响或结果)

  • I didn't think through the consequences of promotion. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我没有把晋升带来的所有后果都考虑清楚。

  • The administration has not really thought through what it plans to do once the fighting stops. [VERB PREP. wh]

    政府还没有认真考虑过一旦战斗结束，它打算怎么办。

  • It was the first time she'd had a chance to think it through. [VERB noun PREP.]

    那是她第一次有机会把事情好好考虑一下。
