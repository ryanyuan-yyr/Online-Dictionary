curtailment
kɜːʳteɪlmənt
N-SING 减缩;缩短;限制 The curtailment of something is the act of reducing or limiting it. 减缩;缩短;限制 [formal]

  • ...a considerable curtailment of military spending.

    军费开支的大幅减缩

  • ...the curtailment of presidential power. [+ of]

    对总统权力的限制

SYN cutting, reduction, docking, lessening
