dependable
dɪpendəbəl diˈpɛndəbəl dɪˈpɛndəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可靠的;可信赖的 If you say that someone or something is dependable, you approve of them because you feel that you can be sure that they will always act consistently or sensibly, or do what you need them to do. 可靠的;可信赖的 [approval]

  • He was a good friend, a dependable companion.

    他是一个很好的朋友，一个可以信赖的伙伴。

SYN reliable, sure, responsible, steady
