prefixed
priːfɪkst ˈpriːfɪkst
V-PASSIVE 有前置代码的；有前置符号的 A word or code number that is prefixed by one or more letters or numbers has them as its prefix. 有前置代码的；有前置符号的

  • Sulphur-containing compounds are often prefixed by the term 'thio'. [be VERB-ed + by]

    含硫化合物的名称前通常有前缀 thio。

  • Calls to Dublin should now be prefixed with 010 3531. [be VERB-ed + with]

    现在往都柏林打电话前面要加拨010 3531。
