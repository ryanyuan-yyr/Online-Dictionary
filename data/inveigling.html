inveigle
ɪnveɪgəl ɪnˈveɪgəl ɪnˈvigəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inveigles, present participle inveigling, past tense , past participle inveigled
VERB 诱骗;引诱 If you inveigle someone into doing something, you cleverly persuade them to do it when they do not really want to. 诱骗;引诱 [formal]

  • She inveigles Paco into a plot to swindle Tania out of her savings. [VERB noun + into]

    她引诱帕科一起合谋来骗取塔妮娅的积蓄。
