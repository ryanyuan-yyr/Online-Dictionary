dreamer
driːməʳ ˈdrimər
Word forms: plural dreamers
N-COUNT 梦想家;空想家;不切实际的人 If you describe someone as a dreamer, you mean that they spend a lot of time thinking about and planning for things that they would like to happen but which are improbable or impractical. 梦想家;空想家;不切实际的人

  • Far from being a dreamer, she's a level-headed pragmatist.

    她是个头脑冷静的务实派,绝不是一个不切实际的人。

SYN idealist, visionary, daydreamer, utopian
