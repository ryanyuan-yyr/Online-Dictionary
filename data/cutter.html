cutter
kʌtəʳ ˈkʌtər
Word forms: plural cutters
1 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 切割工具;裁剪工具 A cutter is a tool that you use for cutting through something. 切割工具;裁剪工具

  • ...a pastry cutter.

    面团分切机

  • ...wire cutters.

    钢丝钳

2 N-COUNT 切割工;剪裁工;减少…的人 A cutter is a person who cuts or reduces something. 切割工;剪裁工;减少…的人

  • ...a glass cutter.

    玻璃切割工

  • He has been using every opportunity to boost his credibility as a budget cutter.

    他一有机会就吹嘘自己削减预算的能力。

3 N-COUNT 小艇;接应船 A cutter is a type of boat. 小艇;接应船
