tatty
tæti ˈtæti
ADJ-GRADED 不整洁的;邋遢的;破烂的 If you describe something as tatty, you think it is untidy, rather dirty, and looks as if it has not been cared for. 不整洁的;邋遢的;破烂的 [mainly British]

  • There were a lot of guest houses which were very tatty.

    有许多小旅馆都又脏又乱。

  • ...a very tatty old bathrobe.

    破旧的浴衣

SYN shabby, seedy, scruffy, worn
