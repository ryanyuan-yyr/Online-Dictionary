consumer price index kənˈsjuːmə praɪs ˈɪndɛks
N-SING [usu the N] 消费者物价指数; 缩写成CPIThe consumer price index is an official measure of the rate of inflation within a country's economy. The abbreviation is also used. 消费者物价指数; 缩写成CPI

  • In May the consumer price index fell by 1.1 per cent.

    五月份的消费者物价指数下降了1.1个百分点。
