raffle
ræfəl ˈræfəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense raffles, present participle raffling, past tense , past participle raffled
1 N-COUNT 抽奖;抽彩 A raffle is a competition in which you buy tickets with numbers on them. Afterwards some numbers are chosen, and if your ticket has one of these numbers on it, you win a prize. 抽奖;抽彩

  • Any more raffle tickets? Twenty-five pence each or five for a pound.

    还有要买奖券的吗？每张25便士，1英镑5张。

SYN draw, lottery, ballot, sweepstake  
2 VERB 抽奖赠送（货品） If someone raffles something, they give it as a prize in a raffle. 抽奖赠送（货品）

  • During each show we will be raffling a fabulous prize. [VERB noun]

    每场表演期间，我们将以抽彩方法送出大奖。

SYN draw
