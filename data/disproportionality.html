disproportion
dɪsprəpɔːʳʃən ˌdɪsprəˈpɔrʃən ˈdɪsprəˈpɔrʃən
Word forms: plural disproportions
N-VAR 不成比例;不相称 A disproportion is a state in which two things are unequal. 不成比例;不相称 [formal]

  • ...a disproportion in the legal resources available to the two sides.

    双方可获得的法律资源不成比例

SYN inequality, imbalance, disparity, discrepancy
