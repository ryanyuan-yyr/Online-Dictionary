sensationalism
senseɪʃənəlɪzəm sɛnˈseɪʃənəˌlɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT (报道的)耸人听闻，哗众取宠 Sensationalism is the presenting of facts or stories in a way that is intended to produce strong feelings of shock, anger, or excitement. (报道的)耸人听闻，哗众取宠 [disapproval]

  • The report criticises the newspaper for sensationalism.

    这篇报道批评了报纸哗众取宠的作风。
