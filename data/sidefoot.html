side-foot
(also sidefoot)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense side-foots, present participle side-footing, past tense , past participle side-footed
VERB 用脚侧踢(球) In football, if a player side-foots the ball, they kick it with the side of their foot. 用脚侧踢(球) [British, journalism] [Also VERB]

  • Currie sidefooted his first goal of the season. [VERB noun]

    柯里用脚侧踢进了他本赛季的第一个进球。

Side-foot is also a noun.

  • Anthony scored with a simple side-foot.

    安东尼用脚侧踢了下球，球便进了。
