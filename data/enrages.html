enrage
ɪnreɪdʒ ɛnˈreɪdʒ ɪnˈreɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enrages, present participle enraging, past tense , past participle enraged
VERB 激怒;使非常愤怒 If you are enraged by something, it makes you extremely angry. 激怒;使非常愤怒

  • He was enraged by news of plans to demolish the pub. [be VERB-ed + by]

    看到计划拆除酒馆的新闻，他非常愤怒。

  • He enraged the government by renouncing the agreement. [VERB noun]

    他否认那项协议，从而激怒了政府。

SYN anger, provoke, irritate, infuriate  
enraged

  • I began getting more and more enraged at my father.

    我开始变得越来越生父亲的气了。

  • The enraged crowd stoned the car, then set it on fire.

    愤怒的人群向那辆车扔石头，然后放火烧它。
