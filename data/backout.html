back out
PHRASAL VERB 食言；违约；退出 If you back out, you decide not to do something that you previously agreed to do. 食言；违约；退出

  • Madonna backed out of the project after much wrangling. [VERB PREP. + of]

    多番争吵之后，麦当娜退出了这个项目。

  • Wells was supposed to put up half the money, but later backed out. [VERB PREP.]

    韦尔斯本应当出一半的钱，但后来他食言了。

SYN withdraw, retire, give up, pull out
