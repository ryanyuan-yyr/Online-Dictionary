oatmeal
oʊtmiːl ˈoʊtˌmil
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 燕麦粉 Oatmeal is a kind of flour made by crushing oats. 燕麦粉

  • ...oatmeal biscuits.

    燕麦饼干

2 N-UNCOUNT （尤指早餐吃的）燕麦片粥,燕麦糊 Oatmeal is a thick sticky food made from oats cooked in water or milk and eaten hot, especially for breakfast. （尤指早餐吃的）燕麦片粥,燕麦糊 [mainly US]
in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 porridge
3 COLOUR 米灰色；浅棕色；燕麦黄 Something that is oatmeal is a pale creamy brown colour. 米灰色；浅棕色；燕麦黄

  • ...an oatmeal tweed jacket.

    米灰色的粗花呢夹克
