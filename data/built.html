built
bɪlt bɪlt
1 Built is the past tense and past participle of build .
2 ADJ [adverb ADJ] 体形…的 If you say that someone is built in a particular way, you are describing the kind of body they have. 体形…的

  • All the Trollope boys were heavily built and quite tall.

    特罗洛普家的所有男孩都是体格强壮、身材高大。

  • ...a strong, powerfully-built man of 60.

    一位60岁、强壮结实的男子

  • He was a huge man, built like an oak tree.

    他是个大块头，结实得像棵橡树。
