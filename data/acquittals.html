acquittal
əkwɪtəl əˈkwɪtəl əˈkwɪtəl
Word forms: plural acquittals
N-VAR 宣告无罪；无罪的判决 Acquittal is a formal declaration in a court of law that someone who has been accused of a crime is innocent. 宣告无罪；无罪的判决

  • ...the acquittal of six police officers charged with beating a suspect. [+ of]

    对6名被控殴打嫌犯的警官作出的无罪判决

  • The jury voted 8-to-4 in favor of acquittal.

    陪审团以8比4的投票结果赞成无罪判决。

  • The trial resulted in acquittals on all but one count.

    审判结果是除一项罪名之外其他罪名都不成立。

SYN clearance, freeing, release, relief
