family doctor
Word forms: plural family doctors
N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 家庭医生 A family doctor is a doctor who does not specialize in any particular area of medicine, but who has a medical practice in which he or she treats all types of illness. 家庭医生 [British]

  • Only your family doctor can refer you to a surgeon.

    只有你的家庭医生才能把你介绍给外科医生。
