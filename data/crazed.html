crazed
kreɪzd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 疯狂的;发狂的 Crazed people are wild and uncontrolled, and perhaps insane. 疯狂的;发狂的 [written]

  • A crazed gunman slaughtered five people last night.

    昨晚一名疯狂的持枪歹徒连杀5人。

  • ...a crazed act of revenge.

    疯狂的复仇计划

SYN mad, crazy, raving, insane  
-crazed -kreɪzd
COMB in ADJ (用于名词后构成形容词)表示“因…发狂的”，“因…失控的” -crazed combines with nouns to form adjectives that describe people whose behaviour is wild and uncontrolled because of the thing the noun refers to. (用于名词后构成形容词)表示“因…发狂的”，“因…失控的”

  • ...a drug-crazed killer.

    因吸毒而发狂的杀手

  • ...a power-crazed TV executive.

    醉心于权力的电视台总监
