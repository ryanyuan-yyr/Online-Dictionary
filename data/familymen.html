family man
Word forms: plural family men
1 N-COUNT 恋家的男人;居家型男人 A family man is a man who is very fond of his wife and children and likes to spend a lot of time with them. 恋家的男人;居家型男人

  • I'm very much a family man and need to be close to those I love.

    我是个非常恋家的男人，必须生活在所爱的人身旁。

2 N-COUNT 有家室的男人;有妻儿的男人 A family man is a man who has a wife and children. 有家室的男人;有妻儿的男人

  • I am a family man with a mortgage.

    我是个有家室的男人，还有笔按揭款要偿付。
