come up
1 PHRASAL VERB 走近;走到跟前 If someone comes up or comes up to you, they approach you until they are standing close to you. 走近;走到跟前

  • Her cat came up and rubbed itself against their legs. [VERB PREP.]

    她的猫跑上前来，在他们腿上蹭来蹭去。

  • He came up to me and said: 'Come on, John.' [VERB PREP. + to]

    他走到我跟前说：“快点，约翰。”

2 PHRASAL VERB 被提及;被讨论 If something comes up in a conversation or meeting, it is mentioned or discussed. 被提及;被讨论

  • The subject came up during a pre-dinner drink with our guests.

    在与客人喝餐前酒的时候，有人提到了这个话题。

  • Jeane Kirkpatrick's name has come up a lot. [VERB PREP.]

    珍妮·柯克帕特里克的名字被多次提到。

3 PHRASAL VERB 即将发生;即将到来 If something is coming up, it is about to happen or take place. 即将发生;即将到来

  • Plan your activities so that you are rested and refreshed when something important is coming up. [VERB PREP.]

    做好日常规划，这样一旦发生大事，才能够从容不迫。

  • We do have elections coming up. [VERB PREP.]

    我们的确将要进行选举。

4 PHRASAL VERB 突然发生 If something comes up, it happens unexpectedly. 突然发生

  • I was delayed — something came up at home. [VERB PREP.]

    我耽搁了些时候—家里出了点事。

  • Other projects came up and the emphasis of my work altered. [VERB PREP.]

    突然来了一些其他的项目，我的工作重点便转移了。

SYN happen, occur, arise, turn up  
5 PHRASAL VERB (工作)出现空缺;(商品)上市 If a job comes up or if something comes up for sale, it becomes available. (工作)出现空缺;(商品)上市

  • A research fellowship came up at Girton and I applied for it and got it. [VERB PREP.]

    格顿学院设立了一项研究奖学金，我提出申请并且成功获得。

  • The house came up for sale and the couple realised they could just about afford it. [VERB PREP. + for]

    这幢房子放盘，两口子才意识到他们的钱差不多刚够买下它。

6 PHRASAL VERB (太阳、月亮)升起 When the sun or moon comes up, it rises. (太阳、月亮)升起

  • It will be so great watching the sun come up. [VERB PREP.]

    看着太阳冉冉升起将是十分美妙的事情。

7 PHRASAL VERB (案件)开庭审理 In law, when a case comes up, it is heard in a court of law. (案件)开庭审理

  • He is one of the reservists who will plead not guilty when their cases come up. [VERB PREP.]

    案件开庭审理的时候，这些预备役军人不会认罪，他就是其中一个。
