palette
pælɪt ˈpælɪt
Word forms: plural palettes
1 N-COUNT 调色板 A palette is a flat piece of wood or plastic on which an artist mixes paints. 调色板
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （画家使用的）一组颜色，色彩范围 You can refer to the range of colours that are used by a particular artist or group of artists as their palette. （画家使用的）一组颜色，色彩范围

  • David Fincher paints from a palette consisting almost exclusively of grey and mud brown.

    戴维·芬彻几乎只用灰色和土褐色作画。
