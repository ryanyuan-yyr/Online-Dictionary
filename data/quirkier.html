quirky
kwɜːʳki
Word forms: comparative quirkier, superlative quirkiest
ADJ-GRADED (事物或人)奇特的，离奇的，古怪的 Something or someone that is quirky is rather odd or unpredictable in their appearance, character, or behaviour. (事物或人)奇特的，离奇的，古怪的

  • We've developed a reputation for being quite quirky and original.

    我们因为风格奇特又独具创意而名声在外。

SYN odd, unusual, eccentric, idiosyncratic  
quirkiness

  • You will probably notice an element of quirkiness in his behaviour.

    你可能会发现他的行为有点怪异。
