fifth
fɪfθ fɪfθ
Word forms: plural fifths
1 ORD 第五 The fifth item in a series is the one that you count as number five. 第五

  • Joe has recently returned from his fifth trip to Australia.

    乔最近结束了第五次澳大利亚之行归来。

2 FRACTION 五分之一 A fifth is one of five equal parts of something. 五分之一

  • India spends over a fifth of its budget on defence.

    印度的国防开支占了其财政预算的 1/5 强。
