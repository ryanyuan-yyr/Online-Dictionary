proximity
prɒksɪmɪti prɑkˈsɪməti
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft in NOUN] 接近；临近；邻近 Proximity to a place or person is nearness to that place or person. 接近；临近；邻近 [formal] [Also + of]

  • Part of the attraction is Darwin's proximity to Asia. [+ to]

    达尔文市的魅力一部分在于它靠近亚洲。

  • He became aware of the proximity of the Afghans.

    他意识到阿富汗人近在咫尺。

  • Families are no longer in close proximity to each other. [+ to]

    各家不再像以前一样比邻而居。
