museum piece
Word forms: plural museum pieces
N-COUNT 古老而不寻常的东西;历史文物;珍贵文物 If you describe an object or building as a museum piece, you mean that it is old and unusual. 古老而不寻常的东西;历史文物;珍贵文物

  • One day these are multi-million dollar war machines and the next they are museum pieces.

    终有一天，今天这些还是价值不菲的战争武器，明天它们就会进入历史的博物馆。
