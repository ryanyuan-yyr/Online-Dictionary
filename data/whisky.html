whisky
hwɪski ˈwɪskɪ
Word forms: plural whiskies
N-MASS (尤指产于苏格兰、由大麦或黑麦酿制的)威士忌酒 Whisky is a strong alcoholic drink made, especially in Scotland, from grain such as barley or rye. (尤指产于苏格兰、由大麦或黑麦酿制的)威士忌酒

  • ...a bottle of whisky.

    一瓶威士忌

  • ...expensive whiskies and brandies.

    昂贵的威士忌和白兰地

A whisky is a glass of whisky.一杯威士忌

  • She handed him a whisky.

    她递给他一杯威士忌。
