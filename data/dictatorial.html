dictatorial
dɪktətɔːriəl ˌdɪktəˈtɔriəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 独裁的;专政的 Dictatorial means controlled or used by a dictator. 独裁的;专政的

  • He suspended the constitution and assumed dictatorial powers.

    他搁置宪法，实行独裁统治。

SYN absolute, unlimited, totalitarian, autocratic  
2 ADJ-GRADED 专横的;霸道的;喜欢发号施令的 If you describe someone's behaviour as dictatorial, you do not like the fact that they tell people what to do in a forceful and unfair way. 专横的;霸道的;喜欢发号施令的 [disapproval]

  • If you are too strict with them, your children will see you as dictatorial.

    如果对孩子要求太严，他们会觉得你独断专行。

  • ...his dictatorial management style.

    他专横霸道的管理风格

SYN domineering, authoritarian, oppressive, bossy [informal]
