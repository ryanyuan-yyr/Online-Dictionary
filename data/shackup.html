shack up
PHR-V-RECIP (与…)同居 If you say that someone has shacked up with someone else or that two people have shacked up together, you disapprove of the fact that they have started living together as lovers. (与…)同居 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...the deserters who had shacked up with local women. [VERB PREP. + with]

    与当地妇女同居的逃兵

  • The Government was keen for people to get married rather than shack up. [VERB PREP.]

    政府非常希望人们能正常结婚而不是未婚同居。

  • It turned out she was shacked up with a lawyer in New York. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    原来她在纽约和一名律师同居了。
