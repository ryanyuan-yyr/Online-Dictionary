emanate
eməneɪt ˈɛməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense emanates, present participle emanating, past tense , past participle emanated
1 V-ERG 表现出;散发;流露 If a quality emanates from you, or if you emanate a quality, you give people a strong sense that you have that quality. 表现出;散发;流露 [formal]

  • Intelligence and cunning emanated from him. [VERB + from]

    他透着一种智慧和狡黠。

  • He emanates sympathy. [VERB noun]

    他流露出同情。

2 VERB 来自(于);(从…)散发出 If something emanates from somewhere, it comes from there. 来自(于);(从…)散发出 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • The heady aroma of wood fires emanated from the stove. [VERB from noun]

    炉子里散发出木柴燃烧时的浓烈香味。

  • ...reports emanating from America. [VERB + from]

    来自美国的报道

SYN flow, emerge, spring, proceed
