non-linear
(also nonlinear)
ADJ 不按逻辑规律的;非线性的 If you describe something as non-linear, you mean that it does not progress or develop smoothly from one stage to the next in a logical way. Instead, it makes sudden changes, or seems to develop in different directions at the same time. 不按逻辑规律的;非线性的

  • ...a non-linear narrative structure.

    非线性叙事结构

  • ...a computer program that could help detect non-linear trends in currency markets.

    有助于发现货币市场非线性变化趋势的计算机程序
