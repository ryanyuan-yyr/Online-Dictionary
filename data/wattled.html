wattle
wɒtəl ˈwɑtəl
N-UNCOUNT (用以编筑篱笆及围墙的)枝条构架 Wattle is a framework made by weaving thin sticks through thick sticks which is used for making fences and walls. (用以编筑篱笆及围墙的)枝条构架 [British]

  • ...the native huts of mud and wattle.

    当地用泥巴和枝条盖起的小屋

  • ...wattle fencing.

    篱笆围栏
