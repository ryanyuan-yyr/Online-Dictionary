fancy dress
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 化装舞会服装 Fancy dress is clothing that you wear for a party at which everyone tries to look like a famous person or a person from a story, from history, or from a particular profession. 化装舞会服装

  • Guests were told to come in fancy dress.

    客人们被告知要身穿化装舞会服装前来。

  • He turned up at the fancy dress party as a footballer.

    他扮成足球运动员出现在化装舞会上。
