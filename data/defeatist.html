defeatist
dɪfiːtɪst diˈfitɪst dɪˈfitɪst
Word forms: plural defeatists
N-COUNT 失败主义者 A defeatist is someone who thinks or talks in a way that suggests that they expect to be unsuccessful. 失败主义者
Defeatist is also an adjective.

  • There is no point going out there with a defeatist attitude.

    怀着一种失败主义的态度上阵根本没有任何意义。
