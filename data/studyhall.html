study hall
ˈstʌdɪ hɔːl
Word forms: study halls
N-VAR 自习室; 自习课A study hall is a room where students can study during free time between classes, or a period of time during which such study takes place. 自习室; 自习课 [US]

  • Children are hard at work in the study hall.

    孩子们在自习室里刻苦学习。

  • She'd go to the library during ninth period, when she had study hall.

    第九节课是自习时间，她会去图书馆学习。
