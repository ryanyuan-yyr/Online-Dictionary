toneless
toʊnləs
ADJ-GRADED 缺乏声调的;平板的;单调的 A toneless voice is dull and does not express any feeling. 缺乏声调的;平板的;单调的 [written]

  • 'What shall we do?' Milton said again in a toneless voice.

    “我们该怎么办？”米尔顿又用平板而单调的声音问道。

tonelessly

  • 'That's most kind of him,' Eleanor said tonelessly.

    “他可真是太好心了，”埃莉诺声调呆板地说道。
