accelerate
ækseləreɪt ækˈsɛlərˌeɪt ækˈsɛləˌreɪt əkˈsɛlərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense accelerates, present participle accelerating, past tense , past participle accelerated
1 V-ERG （使）加快；（使）增速 If the process or rate of something accelerates or if something accelerates it, it gets faster and faster. （使）加快；（使）增速

  • Growth will accelerate to 2.9 per cent next year. [VERB]

    明年的增长会加快到2.9%。

  • The government is to accelerate its privatisation programme. [VERB noun]

    政府将加快其私有化计划的进程。

SYN expedite, press, forward, promote  
2 VERB （运动中的车辆）加速 When a moving vehicle accelerates, it goes faster and faster. （运动中的车辆）加速

  • Suddenly the car accelerated. [VERB]

    汽车突然加速。

  • She accelerated away from the kerb. [VERB prep./adv.]

    她加速驶离了马路牙子。

SYN speed up, speed, advance, quicken
