instigate
ɪnstɪgeɪt ˈɪnstəˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense instigates, present participle instigating, past tense , past participle instigated
VERB 发起;促成;鼓动;唆使 Someone who instigates an event causes it to happen. 发起;促成;鼓动;唆使

  • Jenkinson instigated a refurbishment of the old gallery. [VERB noun]

    詹金森促成了老美术馆的重新整修。

  • The violence over the last forty-eight hours was instigated by ex-members of the secret police. [VERB noun]

    过去 48 小时的暴力事件是在前秘密警察的煽动下爆发的。

SYN provoke, start, encourage, move  
instigation

  • The talks are taking place at the instigation of Germany.

    德国推动了会谈的开启。

  • At Kukhar's instigation he joined the restaurant as an apprentice chef.

    在库哈里的鼓动下,他到餐馆做了厨师学徒。
