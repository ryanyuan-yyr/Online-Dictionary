underwrite
ʌndəʳraɪt ˈʊndəˌraɪt ˈʌndərˈraɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense underwrites, present participle underwriting, past tense underwrote, past participle underwritten
VERB (机构或公司)为…保险，负担…的费用，承销 If an institution or company underwrites an activity or underwrites the cost of it, they agree to provide any money that is needed to cover losses or buy special equipment, often for an agreed fee. (机构或公司)为…保险，负担…的费用，承销 [business]

  • ...58,500 dollars to underwrite a new home. [VERB noun]

    58,500美元承保一个新居

  • The government will have to create a special agency to underwrite small business loans. [VERB noun]

    政府需要设立专门机构来为小企业贷款作担保。

  • Projects included a pilot scheme, underwritten by the trade department. [VERB noun]

    项目中包括一个由贸易部出资的试点方案。

SYN finance, back, fund, guarantee
