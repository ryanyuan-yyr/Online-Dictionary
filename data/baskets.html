basket
bɑːskɪt bæs- ˈbæskɪt ˈbɑskɪt
Word forms: plural baskets
1 N-COUNT 篮子；篓子；筐子 A basket is a stiff container that is used for carrying or storing objects. Baskets are made from thin strips of materials such as straw, plastic, or wire woven together. 篮子；篓子；筐子

  • ...big wicker picnic baskets filled with sandwiches.

    装满三明治的柳编野餐篮

  • ...a laundry basket.

    洗衣筐

SYN wickerwork box, box, pannier, punnet  
A basket of things is a number of things contained in a basket.一篮（或篓、筐）之量

  • ...a small basket of fruit and snacks.

    一小筐水果和点心

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （用于比较不同货币或商品价值的）一篮子，一组（货币或商品） In economics, a basket of currencies or goods is the average or total value of a number of different currencies or goods. （用于比较不同货币或商品价值的）一篮子，一组（货币或商品） [business]

  • The pound's value against a basket of currencies hit a new low of 76.9. [+ of]

    英镑对一篮子货币的比价创下76.9的新低。

  • ...an inflation measure that gauges the price of a fixed basket of goods and services.

    通过衡量固定的一组商品和服务的价格来测算通货膨胀的方法

3 N-COUNT （篮球运动的）篮 In basketball, the basket is a net hanging from a ring through which players try to throw the ball in order to score points. （篮球运动的）篮
