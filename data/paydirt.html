paydirt peɪdɜːʳt
(also pay dirt)
PHRASE 突然成功；暴富 If you say that someone has struck paydirt or has hit paydirt, you mean that they have achieved sudden success or gained a lot of money very quickly. 突然成功；暴富 [mainly US, informal]

  • Howard Hawks hit paydirt with 'Rio Bravo'.

    霍华德·霍克斯凭借影片《赤胆屠龙》一夜成名。
