chaplain
tʃæplɪn ˈtʃæplən
Word forms: plural chaplains
N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (医院、学校、监狱、军队等的)牧师，神父 A chaplain is a member of the Christian clergy who does religious work in a place such as a hospital, school, prison, or in the armed forces. (医院、学校、监狱、军队等的)牧师，神父

  • He joined the 40th Division as an army chaplain.

    他以随军牧师的身份加入第40师。
