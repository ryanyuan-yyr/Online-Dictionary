workday
wɜːʳkdeɪ ˈwɜrkˌdeɪ
(also work day)Word forms: plural workdays
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 一天的工作时间 A workday is the amount of time during a day which you spend doing your job. 一天的工作时间 [mainly US]

  • His workday starts at 3.30 a.m. and lasts 12 hours.

    他凌晨3点半开始工作，一直持续12小时。

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 working day
2 N-COUNT 工作日 A workday is a day on which people go to work. 工作日

  • What's he doing home on a workday?

    该上班的日子，他在家干什么？
