comatose
koʊmətoʊs ˈkoʊməˌtoʊs ˈkɑməˌtoʊs
1 ADJ 昏迷的 A person who is comatose is in a coma. 昏迷的 [medicine]

  • The right side of my brain had been so severely bruised that I was comatose for a month.

    由于右脑遭受重创，我昏迷了一个月。

SYN unconscious, in a coma, out cold, insensible  
2 ADJ [oft ADJ after v] (通常指因劳累或饮酒过量而)酣睡的 A person who is comatose is in a deep sleep, usually because they are tired or have drunk too much alcohol. (通常指因劳累或饮酒过量而)酣睡的 [informal]

  • Grandpa lies comatose on the sofa.

    爷爷躺在沙发上，睡得很沉。

SYN inert, stupefied, out cold, somnolent
