long-distance
ˈlɔŋˈdɪstəns
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 长距离的；长途的；远途的 Long-distance is used to describe travel between places that are far apart. 长距离的；长途的；远途的

  • Trains are reliable, cheap and best for long-distance journeys.

    火车安全可靠、费用低廉，最适宜长途旅行。

  • ...the first long-distance sea voyages made by Portuguese navigators at the end of the 15th century.

    15世纪末葡萄牙航海家进行的首次远航

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （通讯）长途的 Long-distance is used to describe communication that takes place between people who are far apart. （通讯）长途的

  • He received a long-distance phone call from his girlfriend in Colorado.

    他接到女朋友从科罗拉多打来的长途电话。

Long-distance is also an adverb.

  • I phoned Nicola long distance to suggest it.

    我给妮古拉打了长途电话，向她作此提议。
