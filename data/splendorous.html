splendour
splendəʳ ˈsplɛndər
Word forms: plural splendours
in AM, use 美国英语用 splendor
1 N-UNCOUNT 壮丽辉煌;光彩夺目 The splendour of something is its beautiful and impressive appearance. 壮丽辉煌;光彩夺目

  • The foreign ministers are meeting in the splendour of Oktyabrskaya Hotel in central Moscow.

    外长们正在莫斯科市中心富丽堂皇的十月酒店里开会。

2 N-PLURAL 华丽;豪华;奢华 The splendours of a place or way of life are its beautiful and impressive features. 华丽;豪华;奢华

  • Montagu was extremely impressed by the splendours of the French court. [+ of]

    法国宫廷的豪华给蒙塔古留下了很深的印象。
