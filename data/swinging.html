swinging
swɪŋɪŋ ˈswɪŋɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 活跃的;活泼的;时髦的;新潮的 If you describe something or someone as swinging, you mean that they are lively and fashionable. 活跃的;活泼的;时髦的;新潮的 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • The stuffy '50s gave way to the swinging '60s.

    古板乏味的 50 年代被多姿多彩的60年代所取代。

  • ...a young student in mid-1960s 'swinging London'.

    20 世纪 60 年代中期“摇摆伦敦”时期的年轻学生

SYN trendy, happening [informal] , with it [informal] , hip [slang]
