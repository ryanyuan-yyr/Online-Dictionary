tranquillize
træŋkwɪlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tranquillizes, present participle tranquillizing, past tense , past participle tranquillized
The spellings tranquilize in American English, and tranquillise in British English, are also used. 美国英语亦拼作 tranquilize，英国英语亦拼作 tranquillise。
VERB (用镇静剂)使安定，使镇静，使昏睡 To tranquillize a person or an animal means to make them become calm, sleepy, or unconscious by means of a drug. (用镇静剂)使安定，使镇静，使昏睡

  • This powerful drug is used to tranquilize patients undergoing surgery. [VERB noun]

    这种强效药剂被用来麻醉手术病人。

SYN calm, sedate, knock out, lull
