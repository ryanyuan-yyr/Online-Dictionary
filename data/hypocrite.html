hypocrite
hɪpəkrɪt ˈhɪpəˌkrɪt
Word forms: plural hypocrites
N-COUNT 伪君子;伪善者;假道学 If you accuse someone of being a hypocrite, you mean that they pretend to have qualities, beliefs, or feelings that they do not really have. 伪君子;伪善者;假道学 [disapproval]

  • The magazine wrongly suggested he was a liar and a hypocrite.

    该杂志诬枉地暗示他撒谎，是伪君子。

SYN fraud [informal] , deceiver, pretender, charlatan
