spear
spɪəʳ spɪr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense spears, present participle spearing, past tense , past participle speared
1 N-COUNT 矛;投枪;梭镖 A spear is a weapon consisting of a long pole with a sharp metal point attached to the end. 矛;投枪;梭镖
2 VERB 用矛(投)刺;叉住 If you spear something, you push or throw a pointed object into it. 用矛(投)刺;叉住

  • Spear a piece of fish with a carving fork and dip it in the batter. [VERB noun]

    用切肉叉叉一片鱼肉，在面糊里蘸一下。

  • A police officer was speared to death. [be V-ed to n]

    一名警官被刺死了。

SYN gore, pierce, lance, bayonet  
3 N-COUNT (芦笋、花椰菜等的)嫩茎 Asparagus or broccoli spears are individual stalks of asparagus or broccoli. (芦笋、花椰菜等的)嫩茎
