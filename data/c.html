c
siː si
1 N-VAR 英语字母表的第3个字母 C is the third letter of the English alphabet. 英语字母表的第3个字母
2 N-VAR C大调音阶中的第一音;C音 In music, C is the first note in the scale of C major. C大调音阶中的第一音;C音
3 N-VAR （作业、考试等）丙等，第三等，C级 If you get a C as a mark for a piece of work or in an exam, your work is average. （作业、考试等）丙等，第三等，C级
4 (用于日期或数字前)约,大约 c. is written in front of a date or number to indicate that it is approximate. c. is an abbreviation for (缩略 =) 'circa'. (用于日期或数字前)约,大约

  • ...the museum's recreation of a New York dining-room (c. 1825-35).

    博物馆内对一家纽约餐厅(约1825-1835年)的再现

5 (用于首字母为C的一些单词的缩写,如 copyright, Celsius 等) C or c is used as an abbreviation for words beginning with c, such as 'copyright' or 'Celsius'. (用于首字母为C的一些单词的缩写,如 copyright, Celsius 等)

  • Heat the oven to 180˚C.

    把炉子加热到摄氏180度。
