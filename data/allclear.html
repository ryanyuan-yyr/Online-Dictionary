all clear
1 N-SING 险情解除的信号；警报解除的信号 The all clear is a signal that a dangerous situation, for example an air raid, has ended. 险情解除的信号；警报解除的信号

  • The all clear was sounded about 10 minutes after the alert was given.

    警报发出10分钟后又响起解除的信号。

All clear is also a convention.

  • 'All clear,' Misha growled.

    “警报解除，”米莎吼道。

2 N-SING 准许；许可 If someone in authority gives you the all clear, they give you permission to continue with a plan or activity, usually after a problem has been sorted out. 准许；许可

  • I was given the all clear by the doctor to resume playing.

    医生准许我重新开始训练。
