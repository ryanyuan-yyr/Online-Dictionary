inchoate
ɪnkoʊɪt ɪnˈkoʊɪt ɪnˈkoʊˌeɪt
ADJ 才开始的;刚起步的;未发展的 If something is inchoate, it is recent or new, and vague or not yet properly developed. 才开始的;刚起步的;未发展的 [formal]

  • His dreams were senseless and inchoate.

    他的梦想根本行不通,很不成熟。

  • ...the inchoate mood of dissatisfaction with all politicians.

    对所有政客都心存不满的幼稚心态
