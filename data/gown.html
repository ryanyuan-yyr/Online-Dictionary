gown
gaʊn gaʊn
Word forms: plural gowns
1 N-COUNT 女裙；女礼服；女长服 A gown is a dress, usually a long dress, which women wear on formal occasions. 女裙；女礼服；女长服

  • The new ball gown was a great success.

    这种新款舞会礼服很畅销。

  • ...wedding gowns.

    结婚礼服

2 N-COUNT （律师、学者等在正式场合穿的）礼袍,长袍 A gown is a loose black garment worn on formal occasions by people such as lawyers and academics. （律师、学者等在正式场合穿的）礼袍,长袍

  • ...an old headmaster in a flowing black gown.

    身着宽松黑礼袍的老校长
