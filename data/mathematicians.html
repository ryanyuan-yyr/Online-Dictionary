mathematician
mæθəmətɪʃən ˌmæθəməˈtɪʃən ˌ mæθməˈtɪʃən
Word forms: plural mathematicians
1 N-COUNT 数学家 A mathematician is a person who is trained in the study of numbers and calculations. 数学家

  • The risks can be so complex that banks hire mathematicians to puzzle them out.

    由于风险过高，银行便聘请数学家来解决这些问题。

2 N-COUNT 善于计算的人;有数学头脑的人 A mathematician is a person who is good at doing calculations and using numbers. 善于计算的人;有数学头脑的人

  • I'm not a very good mathematician.

    我不太擅长计算。
