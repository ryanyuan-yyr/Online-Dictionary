commemorate
kəmeməreɪt kəˈmɛməˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense commemorates, present participle commemorating, past tense , past participle commemorated
1 VERB 纪念 To commemorate an important event or person means to remember them by means of a special action, ceremony, or specially created object. 纪念

  • One room contained a gallery of paintings commemorating great moments in baseball history. [VERB noun]

    有一个房间里陈列着很多纪念棒球史上伟大时刻的画作。

  • The 200th anniversary of Mozart's death is being commemorated around the world with concerts featuring his work. [VERB noun]

    世界各地将举办以莫扎特的作品为主题的音乐会，以纪念他逝世200周年。
