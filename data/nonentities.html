nonentity
nɒnentɪti ˌnɒnˈɛntɪtɪ nɑnˈɛntəti
Word forms: plural nonentities
N-COUNT 无足轻重的人 If you refer to someone as a nonentity, you mean that they are not special or important in any way. 无足轻重的人 [disapproval]

  • Amidst the current bunch of nonentities, he is a towering figure.

    在当前这帮无足轻重的人里面，他算是鹤立鸡群。

  • She was written off then as a political nonentity.

    她当时被认定是成不了气候的政坛小人物。

SYN nobody, lightweight [informal] , mediocrity, cipher
