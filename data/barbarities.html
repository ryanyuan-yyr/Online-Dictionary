barbarity
bɑːʳbærɪti bɑrˈbɛrəti
Word forms: plural barbarities
N-VAR 残忍的行为；暴行 If you refer to someone's behaviour as barbarity, you strongly disapprove of it because you think that it is extremely cruel. 残忍的行为；暴行 [disapproval]

  • Rebellions were put down with appalling barbarity.

    叛变被残酷地镇压了下去。

  • ...the barbarity of war.

    战事的残酷

SYN viciousness, horror, cruelty, brutality
