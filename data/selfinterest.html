self-interest
ˈsɛlfˈɪntrɪst ˈsɛlfˈɪntərɪst
N-UNCOUNT 自私自利;利己主义 If you accuse someone of self-interest, you disapprove of them because they always want to do what is best for themselves rather than for anyone else. 自私自利;利己主义 [disapproval]

  • Their current protests are motivated purely by self-interest.

    他们当前的抗议完全是受一己之利驱使的。

SYN selfishness, egotism, self-centredness, greed
