keep back
1 PHRASAL VERB 留存，保留（…的一部分） If you keep back part of something, you do not use or give away all of it, so that you still have some to use at a later time. 留存，保留（…的一部分） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Roughly chop the vegetables, and keep back a little to chop finely and serve as a garnish. [VERB PREP. noun]

    大致切一下蔬菜，留下一点切细用作饰菜。

SYN hold back, hold, save, set aside  
2 PHRASAL VERB 隐瞒；保密 If you keep some information back, you do not tell all that you know about something. 隐瞒；保密 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Neither of them is telling the whole truth. Invariably, they keep something back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他俩谁也没把全部实情都说出来。他们总是要隐瞒些什么。

SYN suppress, hide, reserve, conceal
