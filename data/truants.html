truant
truːənt ˈtruːənt ˈtruənt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense truants, present participle truanting, past tense , past participle truanted
1 N-COUNT 逃学者;旷课者 A truant is a pupil who stays away from school without permission. 逃学者;旷课者
SYN absentee, skiver [British , slang] , shirker, dodger  
2 VERB 逃学;旷课 If a pupil truants, he or she stays away from school without permission. 逃学;旷课

  • In his fourth year he was truanting regularly. [VERB]

    上四年级时，他频频旷课。

SYN absent yourself, play truant, skive [British , slang] , bunk off [slang]  
truanting

  • Truanting is a small but growing problem in primary schools.

    逃学是小学里一个虽小却日趋严重的问题。

3 PHRASE 逃学;旷课 If a pupil plays truant, he or she stays away from school without permission. 逃学;旷课

  • She was getting into trouble over playing truant from school. [+ from]

    她因逃课而惹了麻烦。
