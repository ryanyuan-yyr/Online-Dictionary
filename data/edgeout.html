edge out
PHRASAL VERB 险胜;勉强击败 If someone edges out someone else, they just manage to beat them or get in front of them in a game, race, or contest. 险胜;勉强击败 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • France edged out the British team by less than a second. [VERB PREP. noun]

    法国队以不到1秒的优势险胜英国队。

  • McGregor's effort was enough to edge Johnson out of the top spot. [VERB noun PREP. + of]

    麦格雷戈奋力一搏，成功地将约翰逊从第一的位置上挤了下来。
