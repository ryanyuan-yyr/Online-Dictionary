dead end
Word forms: plural dead ends
1 N-COUNT 死路;死胡同 If a street is a dead end, there is no way out at one end of it. 死路;死胡同
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 绝境;僵局 A dead end job or course of action is one that you think is bad because it does not lead to further developments or progress. 绝境;僵局

  • Waitressing was a dead-end job.

    做女服务生是一份没有前途的工作。
