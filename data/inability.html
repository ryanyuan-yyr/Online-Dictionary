inability
ɪnəbɪlɪti ˌɪnəˈbɪlɪti
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN to-infinitive, usually with poss] 无能;无力;不能 If you refer to someone's inability to do something, you are referring to the fact that they are unable to do it. 无能;无力;不能

  • Her inability to concentrate could cause an accident.

    她无法集中精力可能会导致事故。

  • ...the government's inability to provide basic services.

    政府无力提供基本的公共服务

SYN incapability, incompetence, inadequacy, impotence
