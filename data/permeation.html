permeate
pɜːʳmieɪt ˈpɜrmiˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense permeates, present participle permeating, past tense , past participle permeated
1 VERB （思想、感情、态度）感染，传播，扩散，影响 If an idea, feeling, or attitude permeates a system or permeates society, it affects every part of it or is present throughout it. （思想、感情、态度）感染，传播，扩散，影响

  • Bias against women permeates every level of the judicial system. [VERB noun]

    司法体制中各个层面都有歧视女性的现象。

  • An obvious change of attitude at the top will permeate through the system. [VERB + through]

    上级态度的明显改变将影响到整个体制。

SYN infiltrate, fill, pass through, pervade  
2 VERB 渗透；弥漫；扩散 If something permeates a place, it spreads throughout it. 渗透；弥漫；扩散

  • The smell of roast beef permeated the air. [VERB noun]

    空气中弥漫着烤牛肉的香味。

  • Eventually, the water will permeate through the surrounding concrete. [VERB + through]

    最终,水会渗透进周围的混凝土中。

SYN pervade, saturate, charge, fill
