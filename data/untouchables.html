untouchable
ʌntʌtʃəbəl ʌnˈtʌtʃəbəl
Word forms: plural untouchables
1 ADJ-GRADED 不可触动的；不受影响的；碰不得的 If you say that someone is untouchable, you mean that they cannot be affected or punished in any way. 不可触动的；不受影响的；碰不得的

  • I want to make it clear, however, that no one is untouchable in this investigation.

    但我想说明白一点，这次调查谁都逃不了。

An untouchable is someone who is untouchable.不可触动的人；不受影响的人

  • ...an anti-corruption squad nicknamed the 'Untouchables'.

    绰号为“铁面”的反贪小组

2 ADJ 高人一等的；无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 If you describe someone, especially a sports player or entertainer, as untouchable, you are emphasizing that they are better than anyone else in what they do. 高人一等的；无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 [emphasis]

  • A lot of the players began to feel they were untouchable.

    许多球手开始觉得自己天下无敌。

3 N-COUNT 不可接触者，贱民（指印度社会最低阶层的人） Some people refer to Hindus of the lowest social rank as untouchables . 不可接触者，贱民（指印度社会最低阶层的人） [offensive]

  • He was born an untouchable in a very poor village in south India.

    他出身贱民，生于印度南部一个非常贫困的村子。
