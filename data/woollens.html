woollen
wʊlən
Word forms: plural woollens
in AM, use 美国英语用 woolen
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 毛料的；羊毛制的;羊毛混纺的 Woollen clothes or materials are made from wool or from a mixture of wool and artificial fibres. 毛料的；羊毛制的;羊毛混纺的

  • ...thick woollen socks.

    厚厚的羊毛袜

2 N-PLURAL 毛料衣服;(尤指)毛衣,羊毛衫 Woollens are clothes, especially sweaters, that are made of wool. 毛料衣服;(尤指)毛衣,羊毛衫

  • ...winter woollens.

    冬天穿的毛衣
