humanize
hjuːmənaɪz ˈhjuməˌnaɪz ˈjuməˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense humanizes, present participle humanizing, past tense , past participle humanized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 humanise
VERB 使人性化;使舒适化;使更人道 If you humanize a situation or condition, you improve it by changing it in a way which makes it more suitable and pleasant for people. 使人性化;使舒适化;使更人道

  • Jo Robinson began by humanizing the waiting time at the health centre with tea-making and toys for children. [VERB noun]

    乔·鲁宾逊最早是为在医疗中心候诊的人们提供茶水和儿童玩具，使候诊过程变得人性化。

SYN civilize, improve, refine, polish
