turnstile
tɜːʳnstaɪl ˈtɜrnˌstaɪl
Word forms: plural turnstiles
N-COUNT (博物馆、足球场等入口处的)旋转栅门，十字转门 A turnstile is a mechanical barrier at the entrance to a place such as a museum or a football ground. Turnstiles have metal arms that you push round as you go through them and enter the building or area. (博物馆、足球场等入口处的)旋转栅门，十字转门
