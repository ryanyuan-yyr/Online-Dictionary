vicar
vɪkəʳ ˈvɪkər
Word forms: plural vicars
1 N-COUNT; N-VOC （英国国教的）教区牧师，教区教堂主持 A vicar is an Anglican priest who is in charge of a church and the area it is in, which is called a parish. （英国国教的）教区牧师，教区教堂主持 [mainly British]
2 N-COUNT; N-VOC （美国新教圣公会的）教堂主持,教区牧师 A vicar is a priest who is in charge of a chapel that is associated with a parish church in the Episcopal Church in the United States. （美国新教圣公会的）教堂主持,教区牧师 [US]
