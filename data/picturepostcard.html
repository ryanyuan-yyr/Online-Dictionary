picture postcard ˈpɪktʃə ˈpəʊstˌkɑːd
Word forms: plural picture postcards
The spelling picture-postcard is also used for meaning 2. 拼写picture-postcard亦用于义项2。
1 N-COUNT 风景明信片 A picture postcard is a postcard with a photograph of a place on it. People often buy picture postcards of places they visit when on holiday. 风景明信片
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 风景明信片般的；风光如画的 You can use picture postcard to describe a place that is very attractive. 风景明信片般的；风光如画的

  • ...picture-postcard Normandy villages.

    美丽如画的诺曼底村庄
