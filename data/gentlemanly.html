gentlemanly
dʒentəlmənli ˈdʒɛntəlmənli
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 彬彬有礼的；有绅士风度的；有教养的 If you describe a man's behaviour as gentlemanly, you approve of him because he has good manners. 彬彬有礼的；有绅士风度的；有教养的 [approval]

  • He was respected by all who knew him for his kind and gentlemanly consideration.

    他为人谦和而且有绅士风度，认识他的人都很尊重他。

SYN chivalrous, mannerly, obliging, refined  
Quotations
Anyone can be heroic from time to time, but a gentleman is something you have to be all the timeLuigi Pirandello The Pleasure of Honesty
