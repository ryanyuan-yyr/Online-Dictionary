unbeatable
ʌnbiːtəbəl ʌnˈbitəbəl
1 ADJ 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 If you describe something as unbeatable, you mean that it is the best thing of its kind. 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 [emphasis]

  • These resorts, like Magaluf and Arenal, remain unbeatable in terms of price.

    像马加拉夫和阿雷纳尔这样的度假胜地，在价格方面仍具有绝对的优势。

  • ...unbeatable Italian cars.

    无与伦比的意大利汽车

SYN unsurpassed, matchless, unsurpassable  
2 ADJ 无法战胜的；无敌的 In a game or competition, if you describe a person or team as unbeatable, you mean that they win so often, or perform so well that they are unlikely to be beaten by anyone. 无法战胜的；无敌的

  • The opposition was unbeatable.

    对手实力超群。

  • With two more days of competition to go China is in an unbeatable position.

    还有两个比赛日，中国队的位置已经不可撼动。

SYN invincible, unstoppable, indomitable, unconquerable
