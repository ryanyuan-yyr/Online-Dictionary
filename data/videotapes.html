videotape
vɪdioʊteɪp ˈvɪdɪəʊˌteɪp ˈvɪdioʊˌteɪp
(also video tape)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense videotapes, present participle videotaping, past tense , past participle videotaped
1 N-UNCOUNT 录像带;录影带 Videotape is magnetic tape that is used to record moving pictures and sounds to be shown on television. 录像带;录影带
2 N-COUNT 同video cassette A videotape is the same as a video cassette . 同video cassette
3 VERB 把…录在录像带上 If you videotape a television programme or event, you record it on tape using a video recorder or video camera, so that you can watch it later. 把…录在录像带上 [mainly US]

  • She videotaped the entire trip. [VERB noun]

    她录下了整个行程。
