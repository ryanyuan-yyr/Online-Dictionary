sage
seɪdʒ seɪdʒ
Word forms: plural sages
1 N-COUNT 哲人;智者;圣人 A sage is a person who is regarded as being very wise. 哲人;智者;圣人 [literary]

  • ...ancient Chinese sages.

    中国先哲

SYN wise man, philosopher, guru, authority  
2 ADJ-GRADED (尤指因经验丰富而)贤明的，睿智的,博学多识的 Sage means wise and knowledgeable, especially as the result of a lot of experience. (尤指因经验丰富而)贤明的，睿智的,博学多识的 [literary]

  • He was famous for his sage advice to younger painters.

    他因向后起的年轻画家提供睿智的忠告而闻名。

SYN wise, learned, intelligent, sensible  
sagely

  • Susan nodded sagely as if what I had said was profoundly significant.

    苏珊洞悉一切似的点点头，好像我所说的话意义深刻。

  • The family sagely married into American money many years ago.

    该家族在很多年前就作出了与美国财团联姻的明智之举。

3 N-UNCOUNT (用于烹调的)鼠尾草叶 Sage is a herb used in cooking. (用于烹调的)鼠尾草叶
4 N-VAR 鼠尾草;洋苏草 Sage is a plant with grey-green leaves and purple, blue, or white flowers. 鼠尾草;洋苏草
