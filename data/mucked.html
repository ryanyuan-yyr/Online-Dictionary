muck
mʌk mʌk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mucks, present participle mucking, past tense , past participle mucked
1 N-UNCOUNT 污物;污秽 Muck is dirt or some other unpleasant substance. 污物;污秽 [informal]

  • This congealed muck was interfering with the filter.

    这团凝固的污物堵塞了过滤器。

SYN dirt, mud, filth, sewage  
2 N-UNCOUNT (动物的)粪便，排泄物 Muck is animal faeces. (动物的)粪便，排泄物 [informal]

  • He could smell muck and clean fresh hay.

    他能闻到粪臭和清新的干草味。

SYN manure, dung, ordure  
3 N-UNCOUNT 无用的东西;质量低劣的东西 If you refer to something as muck, you are emphasizing that you think it is of very bad quality. 无用的东西;质量低劣的东西 [informal, disapproval]

  • He hasn't eaten anything for days. And are you surprised, when this muck is all I have to give him?.

    他已有好几天没吃东西了，可我只能给他这种垃圾一样的东西，你奇怪吗？

  • The script is utterly banal. It is incredible that human minds can put such muck on to paper.

    这个剧本是十足的平庸之作，难以想象有人会写出这样的垃圾。
