seize on
PHRASAL VERB 对…大为关注;抓住(有用的事物) If you seize on something or seize upon it, you show great interest in it, often because it is useful to you. 对…大为关注;抓住(有用的事物)

  • Newspapers seized on the results as proof that global warming wasn't really happening. [VERB PREP. noun]

    各报纸纷纷以此结果为证据来证明全球变暖并没有真正发生。

  • The main fear was that both sides may seize upon a ceasefire and free food aid to rearm. [VERB PREP. noun]

    最担心的是双方可能利用停火和无偿食品援助的机会重整军备。

SYN take advantage of, use, exploit, cash in on [informal]
