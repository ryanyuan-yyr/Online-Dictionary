catchword
kætʃwɜrd ˈkætʃˌwɜːd ˈkætʃˌwɜrd
Word forms: catchwords
N-COUNT 标语; 口号 (常用在政治运动中)A catchword is a word or phrase that becomes popular or well-known, for example, because it is associated with a political campaign. 标语; 口号 (常用在政治运动中)

  • The catchword he and his supporters have been using is "consolidation."

    他和他的支持者们一直在用的口号是“统一”。
