reed
riːd riːd rid
Word forms: plural reeds
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 芦苇 Reeds are tall plants that grow in large groups in shallow water or on ground that is always wet and soft. They have strong, hollow stems that can be used for making things such as mats or baskets. 芦苇
2 N-COUNT （木管乐器中的）簧片 A reed is a small piece of cane or metal inserted into the mouthpiece of a woodwind instrument. The reed vibrates when you blow through it and makes a sound. （木管乐器中的）簧片
