paramedic
pærəmedɪk -medɪk ˌpærəˈmɛdɪk ˈpærəˌmɛdɪk ˌ pærəˈmɛdɪk
Word forms: plural paramedics
N-COUNT 急救医士；医务辅助人员 A paramedic is a person whose training is similar to that of a nurse and who helps to do medical work. 急救医士；医务辅助人员

  • We intend to have a paramedic on every ambulance within the next three years.

    我们计划在未来3年内为每辆救护车配备一名急救医士。
