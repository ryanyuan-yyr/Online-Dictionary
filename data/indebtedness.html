indebted
ɪndetɪd ɪnˈdɛtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 感激的;感恩的 If you say that you are indebted to someone for something, you mean that you are very grateful to them for something. 感激的;感恩的

  • I am deeply indebted to him for his help. [+ to]

    我非常感激他对我的帮助。

SYN grateful, obliged, in debt, obligated  
indebtedness

  • Mortimer recounted his indebtedness to her in his autobiography.

    莫蒂默在他的自传里讲述了对她的感激。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 负债的 Indebted countries, organizations, or people are ones that owe money to other countries, organizations, or people. 负债的

  • America's treasury secretary identified the most heavily indebted countries.

    美国财政部长指出了负债最多的那些国家。

indebtedness

  • The company has reduced its indebtedness to just $15 million.

    公司把负债降低到仅仅 1,500 万美元。
