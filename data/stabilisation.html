stabilize
steɪbɪlaɪz ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stabilizes, present participle stabilizing, past tense , past participle stabilized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 stabilise
V-ERG (使)稳定;(使)安定 If something stabilizes, or is stabilized, it becomes stable. (使)稳定;(使)安定

  • Although her illness is serious, her condition is beginning to stabilize. [VERB]

    虽然她病得很重，但病情正开始趋于稳定。

  • Officials hope the move will stabilize exchange rates. [VERB noun]

    官员们希望此举能使汇率稳定下来。

  • I believe he would have had a stabilising effect on the team. [VERB-ing]

    我相信他原本可以让全组人稳定下来的。

SYN make stable, support, balance, root  
stabilization

  • ...the stabilisation of property prices.

    房地产价格的稳定
