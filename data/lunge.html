lunge
lʌndʒ lʌndʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense lunges, present participle lunging, past tense , past participle lunged
VERB 猛冲；扑 If you lunge in a particular direction, you move in that direction suddenly and clumsily. 猛冲；扑

  • He lunged at me, grabbing me violently. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他向我扑过来，使劲把我抓住。

  • I lunged forward to try to hit him. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我猛冲向前要打他。

SYN pounce, charge, bound, dive  
Lunge is also a noun.

  • The attacker knocked on their door and made a lunge for Wendy when she answered.

    歹徒敲了他们的门，温迪一开门便向她扑去。
