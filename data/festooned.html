festoon
festuːn fɛˈstuːn fɛsˈtun
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense festoons, present participle festooning, past tense , past participle festooned
VERB [usually passive] 给…饰花彩;结彩于 If something is festooned with, for example, lights, balloons, or flowers, large numbers of these things are hung from it or wrapped around it, especially in order to decorate it. 给…饰花彩;结彩于

  • The temples are festooned with lights. [be V-ed + with/in]

    灯彩装饰着庙宇。

  • ...a lamppost festooned in political stickers. [VERB-ed]

    贴满政治招贴画的电线杆

SYN decorate, deck, array, drape  
Festoon is also a noun.

  • ...a grand amphitheater, whose huge columns were wreathed with festoons of laurel and of magnolia.

    壮观的环形剧场，巨大的柱子上缠绕着月桂和木兰花做的花彩
