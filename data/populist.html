populist
pɒpʊlɪst
Word forms: plural populists
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 平民主义的；民粹主义的 If you describe a politician or an artist as populist, you mean that they claim to care about the interests and opinions of ordinary people rather than those of a small group. 平民主义的；民粹主义的 [formal]

  • ...Jose Sarney, the current populist president.

    若泽·萨尔内，现任平民主义总统

  • The city of Memphis is promoting a populist approach to culture.

    孟菲斯市在推行文化的民粹主义道路。

A populist is someone who expresses populist views.平民主义者；民粹主义者
