near-death experience ˈnɪrˌdɛθ
Word forms: plural near-death experiences
N-COUNT 濒死体验;濒死经历 A near-death experience is a strange experience that some people who have nearly died say they had when they were unconscious. 濒死体验;濒死经历

  • A recent Gallup poll revealed that 20 million Americans claimed to have had near-death experiences.

    最近的一次盖洛普民意测验披露，2,000 万美国人声称他们有过濒死体验。
