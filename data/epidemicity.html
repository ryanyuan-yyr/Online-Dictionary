epidemic
epɪdemɪk ˌɛpəˈdɛmɪk
Word forms: plural epidemics
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] （疾病的）流行，传播 If there is an epidemic of a particular disease somewhere, it affects a very large number of people there and spreads quickly to other areas. （疾病的）流行，传播

  • A flu epidemic is sweeping through Moscow.

    一场流感正席卷莫斯科。

  • ...a killer epidemic of yellow fever. [+ of]

    致命的黄热病的流行

SYN outbreak, plague, growth, spread  
2 N-COUNT （坏事的）盛行，泛滥，猖獗 If an activity that you disapprove of is increasing or spreading rapidly, you can refer to this as an epidemic of that activity. （坏事的）盛行，泛滥，猖獗 [disapproval]

  • ...an epidemic of serial killings. [+ of]

    连环杀人现象的猖獗

  • Drug experts say it could spell the end of the crack epidemic.

    禁毒专家说它可能会结束强效可卡因吸食泛滥的局面。
