summit
sʌmɪt ˈsʌmɪt
Word forms: plural summits
1 N-COUNT (政府间的)峰会，首脑会议 A summit is a meeting at which the leaders of two or more countries discuss important matters. (政府间的)峰会，首脑会议

  • ...next week's Washington summit.

    下周的华盛顿峰会 

  • ...the NATO summit meeting in Rome.

    在罗马举行的北约峰会

2 N-COUNT 山顶;顶峰 The summit of a mountain is the top of it. 山顶;顶峰

  • Snow gleamed on the summit of Mount Simon.

    西蒙山山顶白雪皑皑。

SYN peak, top, tip, pinnacle
