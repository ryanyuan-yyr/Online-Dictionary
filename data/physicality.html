physicality
fɪzɪkælɪti ˌfɪzɪˈkæləti
N-UNCOUNT （艺术家、音乐家等作品中包含的）活力，热情，激情 If you refer to the physicality of something such as an artist's or a musician's work, you mean that their energy and enthusiasm is obvious in the work they produce. （艺术家、音乐家等作品中包含的）活力，热情，激情
