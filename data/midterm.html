midterm
mɪdtɜrm ˈmɪdˈtɜːm ˈmɪdˌtɜrm
Word forms: midterms
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 中期的A midterm election is an election that takes place approximately halfway through a president's or a government's term of office. 中期的

  • Bush's Republican Party faces a tough challenge in midterm congressional elections in November.

    布什的共和党在11月中期国会选举中面临艰难的挑战。

2 N-COUNT 期中A midterm or a midterm exam is a test which a student takes in the middle of a school or college term. 期中

  • She was about to walk into a midterm exam for a subject she knew very little about.

    她马上就要参加一个她知之甚少的科目的期中考试了。
