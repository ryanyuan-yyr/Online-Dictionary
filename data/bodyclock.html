body clock
ˈbɒdɪ klɒk
Word forms: plural body clocks
N-COUNT [usually singular] （人体）生物钟，生理时钟 Your body clock is the internal biological mechanism which causes your body to automatically behave in particular ways at particular times of the day. （人体）生物钟，生理时钟

  • Jet lag is caused because the body clock does not readjust immediately to the time change.

    由于人体生物钟不能迅速对时差作出调整，因而产生了飞机时差反应。
