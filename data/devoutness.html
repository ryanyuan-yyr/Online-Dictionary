devout
dɪvaʊt dɪˈvaʊt
1 ADJ-GRADED 虔诚的;虔敬的 A devout person has deep religious beliefs. 虔诚的;虔敬的

  • She was a devout Christian.

    她是一个虔诚的基督徒。

  • His devout Catholicism appeals to ordinary people.

    他对天主教的虔诚信仰感染了普通民众。

SYN religious, godly, pious, pure  
The devout are people who are devout.

  • ...priests instructing the devout.

    向虔诚的信徒讲授教义的牧师

2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 衷心的;坚定的;坚决的 If you describe someone as a devout supporter or a devout opponent of something, you mean that they support it enthusiastically or oppose it strongly. 衷心的;坚定的;坚决的

  • ...devout Marxists.

    坚定的马克思主义者
