momentary
moʊməntəri -teri ˈmoʊmənˌtɛri
ADJ 瞬息的;片刻的;短暂的 Something that is momentary lasts for a very short period of time, for example for a few seconds or less. 瞬息的;片刻的;短暂的

  • ...a momentary lapse of concentration.

    片刻的走神儿

  • His hesitation was only momentary.

    他只迟疑了一下。

SYN short-lived, short, brief, temporary
