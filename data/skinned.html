skinned
skɪnd
COMB in ADJ-GRADED (用于 dark、clear 等形容词之后构成形容词)表示“有…皮肤的” -skinned is used after adjectives such as 'dark' and 'clear' to form adjectives that indicate what kind of skin someone has. (用于 dark、clear 等形容词之后构成形容词)表示“有…皮肤的”

  • Dark-skinned people rarely develop skin cancer.

    黑皮肤的人很少得皮肤癌。

  • She was smooth-skinned and pretty.

    她皮肤光滑，非常漂亮。
