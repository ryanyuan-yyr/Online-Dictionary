pacific
pəsɪfɪk pəˈsɪfɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 爱好和平的；以和平为目的的 A pacific person, country, or course of action is peaceful or has the aim of bringing about peace. 爱好和平的；以和平为目的的 [formal]

  • The Liberals were traditionally seen as the more pacific party.

    传统上，自由党人被认为更加爱好和平。

Pacific
pəˈsɪfɪk
1 N-PROPER 太平洋 The Pacific or the Pacific Ocean is a very large sea to the west of North and South America, and to the east of Asia and Australia. 太平洋

  • ...an island in the Pacific.

    太平洋岛屿

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 太平洋里的；与太平洋有关的 Pacific is used to describe things that are in or that relate to the Pacific Ocean. 太平洋里的；与太平洋有关的

  • ...the tiny Pacific island of Pohnpei.

    太平洋小岛波纳佩
