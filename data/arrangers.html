arranger
əreɪndʒəʳ
Word forms: plural arrangers
1 N-COUNT （乐曲）改编者 An arranger is a musician who arranges music by other composers, either for particular instruments or voices, or for a particular performance. （乐曲）改编者
2 N-COUNT 安排者；筹备者 An arranger is a person who arranges things for other people. 安排者；筹备者

  • ...a loan arranger.

    贷款协调人
