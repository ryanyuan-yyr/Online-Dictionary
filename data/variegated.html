variegated
veəriəgeɪtɪd ˈvɛərɪˌɡeɪtɪd ˈvɛriəˌgeɪtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （树叶或植物）杂色的，斑驳的 A variegated leaf or plant has different colours on it. （树叶或植物）杂色的，斑驳的 [technical]

  • The leaves are a variegated red.

    这些树叶呈斑驳的红色。

2 ADJ-GRADED 多种多样的;形形色色的 Something that is variegated consists of many different parts or types. 多种多样的;形形色色的 [formal]

  • ...our variegated dialects.

    我们形形色色的方言
