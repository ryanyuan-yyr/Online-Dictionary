brogue
broʊg broʊg
Word forms: plural brogues
1 N-SING （尤指爱尔兰人或苏格兰人说英语时带有的）土腔，方言口音 If someone has a brogue, they speak English with a strong accent, especially Irish or Scots. （尤指爱尔兰人或苏格兰人说英语时带有的）土腔，方言口音

  • Gill speaks in a quiet Irish brogue.

    吉尔说话带有轻微的爱尔兰口音。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （带有花纹的）粗皮鞋 Brogues are thick leather shoes which have an elaborate pattern punched into the leather. （带有花纹的）粗皮鞋
