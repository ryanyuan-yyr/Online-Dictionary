animation
ænɪmeɪʃən ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən
Word forms: plural animations
1 N-UNCOUNT 动画片绘制；卡通片制作 Animation is the process of making films in which drawings or puppets appear to move. 动画片绘制；卡通片制作

  • The films are a mix of animation and full-length features.

    这些电影将动画制作和长篇故事片融为一体。

  • ...computer animation.

    电脑动画制作

2 N-COUNT 动画片 An animation is a film in which drawings or puppets appear to move. 动画片

  • This film is the first British animation sold to an American network.

    这是第一部出售给美国电视网的英国动画片。

3 N-UNCOUNT 生气；活泼；热烈 Someone with animation shows liveliness in the way that they speak, look, or behave. 生气；活泼；热烈

  • They both spoke with animation.

    他们兴致勃勃地说着。

SYN liveliness, life, action, activity  
4 See also: suspended animation
