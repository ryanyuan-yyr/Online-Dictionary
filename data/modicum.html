modicum
mɒdɪkəm ˈmɑdɪkəm
QUANT （尤指好事或值得拥有的事物）少量，一点点 A modicum of something, especially something that is good or desirable, is a reasonable but not large amount of it. （尤指好事或值得拥有的事物）少量，一点点 [formal]

  • I'd like to think I've had a modicum of success. [+ of]

    我倒觉得我取得了一点成绩。

  • ...a modicum of privacy. [+ of]

    一点点隐私
