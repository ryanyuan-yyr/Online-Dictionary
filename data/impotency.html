impotence
ɪmpətəns ˈɪmpətəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 无能力;不起作用 Impotence is a lack of power to influence people or events. 无能力;不起作用

  • ...a sense of impotence in the face of deplorable events.

    面对悲惨事件无能为力的感觉

SYN powerlessness, inability, helplessness, weakness  
2 N-UNCOUNT 阳痿;性无能 Impotence is a man's sexual problem in which his penis fails to get hard or stay hard. 阳痿;性无能

  • Impotence affects 10 million men in the US alone.

    仅仅在美国,就有 1,000 万男子患有阳痿。
