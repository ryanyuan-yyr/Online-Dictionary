dour
dʊəʳ daʊəʳ dʊə ˈdaʊə dʊr daʊr
ADJ-GRADED 严厉的;冷冰冰的 If you describe someone as dour, you mean that they are very serious and unfriendly. 严厉的;冷冰冰的

  • ...a dour, taciturn man.

    冷冰冰、不苟言笑的男子

  • No wonder he looked so dour.

    难怪他看上去如此严厉。

SYN gloomy, forbidding, grim, sour  
dourly

  • The old man stared dourly at them.

    老人严厉地看着他们。
