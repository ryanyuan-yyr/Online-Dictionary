double-check
ˈdʌbəlˌtʃɛk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense double-checks, present participle double-checking, past tense , past participle double-checked
VERB 复核;复查 If you double-check something, you examine or test it a second time to make sure that it is completely correct or safe. 复核;复查 [Also VERB]

  • Check and double-check spelling and punctuation. [VERB noun]

    要反复检查拼写和标点符号。

  • Double-check that the ladder is secure. [VERB that]

    再检查一下那梯子是否牢固。

  • Don't believe what you are told; double-check with an independent source. [VERB + with]

    别相信别人告诉你的话，用独立的信息来源再核实一遍。
