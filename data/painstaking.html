painstaking
peɪnsteɪkɪŋ ˈpeɪnzˌteɪkɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （研究、检查、调查）十分小心的，极其仔细的，缜密的 A painstaking search, examination, or investigation is done extremely carefully and thoroughly. （研究、检查、调查）十分小心的，极其仔细的，缜密的

  • Forensic experts carried out a painstaking search of the debris.

    法医专家对残骸进行了极其仔细的搜索。

SYN thorough, careful, meticulous, earnest  
painstakingly

  • Broken bones were painstakingly pieced together and reshaped.

    断骨被十分小心地接合复位。
