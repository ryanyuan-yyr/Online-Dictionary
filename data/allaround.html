all around
PHRASE 全面地，在各方面You say all around to indicate that something affects all parts of a situation or all members of a group. 全面地，在各方面

  • He compared the achievements of the British and the French during 1916 and concluded that the latter were better all around.

    他比较了英国和法国1916年的成就, 得出结论后者总体更胜一筹。
