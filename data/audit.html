audit
ɔːdɪt ˈɔdɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense audits, present participle auditing, past tense , past participle audited
VERB 查（账）；审计 When an accountant audits an organization's accounts, he or she examines the accounts officially in order to make sure that they have been done correctly. 查（账）；审计

  • Each year they audit our accounts and certify them as being true and fair. [VERB noun]

    他们每年对我们进行账务审核，以确保其真实无误。

SYN inspect, check, review, balance  
Audit is also a noun.

  • The bank first learned of the problem when it carried out an internal audit.

    银行是在进行内部审计的时候首次发现了这个问题。
