jackboot
dʒækbuːt ˈdʒækˌbut
Word forms: plural jackboots
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 长筒靴；长筒军靴 Jackboots are heavy boots that come up to the knee, such as the ones worn by some soldiers. 长筒靴；长筒军靴
2 PHRASE 处于暴政下；处于专制统治下 If a country or group of people is under the jackboot, they are suffering because the government is cruel and undemocratic. 处于暴政下；处于专制统治下 [disapproval]
