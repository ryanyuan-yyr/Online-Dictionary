puzzled
pʌzəld ˈpʌzəld
ADJ-GRADED 迷惑的；困惑的；无法理解的 Someone who is puzzled is confused because they do not understand something. 迷惑的；困惑的；无法理解的 [Also + about/at]

  • Critics remain puzzled by the British election results. [+ by]

    批评家仍然对英国大选的结果困惑不解。

  • Norman looked puzzled.

    诺曼看上去很困惑。

SYN perplexed, beaten, confused, baffled
