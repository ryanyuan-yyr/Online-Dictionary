shakedown
ʃeɪkdaʊn ˈʃeɪkdaʊn ˈʃeɪkˌdaʊn
Word forms: plural shakedowns
1 N-COUNT 整顿;调整;重组 If an organization or system is given a shakedown, it is thoroughly reorganized in order to make it more efficient. 整顿;调整;重组
2 N-COUNT (船只、飞机、汽车的)试用，试航，试飞 A shakedown of a boat, plane, or car is its final test before it starts to be used. (船只、飞机、汽车的)试用，试航，试飞
