graceful
greɪsfʊl ˈgreɪsfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 优美的;优雅的 Someone or something that is graceful moves in a smooth and controlled way which is attractive to watch. 优美的;优雅的

  • His movements were so graceful they seemed effortless.

    他的动作优美自如，看起来毫不费劲。

  • ...graceful ballerinas.

    优雅的芭蕾舞女演员

SYN elegant, easy, flowing, smooth  
gracefully

  • She stepped gracefully onto the stage.

    她步态优雅地走上舞台。

2 ADJ-GRADED 优美的；动人的；雅致的 Something that is graceful is attractive because it has a pleasing shape or style. 优美的；动人的；雅致的

  • A graceful medieval cathedral.

    典雅的中世纪大教堂

  • His handwriting, from earliest young manhood, was flowing and graceful.

    他的字从小就写得流畅优美。

gracefully

  • She loved the gracefully high ceiling, with its white-painted cornice.

    她喜欢这里高高的天花板，它造型优美，还带有一圈漆成白色的飞檐。

3 ADJ-GRADED 优雅的；得体的；有风度的 If a person's behaviour is graceful, it is polite, kind, and pleasant, especially in a difficult situation. 优雅的；得体的；有风度的

  • Aubrey could think of no graceful way to escape Corbet's company.

    奥布里想不出有什么得体的办法能摆脱科比特。

  • He was charming, cheerful, and graceful under pressure.

    他在重压之下依然开朗迷人，风度优雅。

SYN polite, mannerly, charming, gracious  
gracefully

  • We managed to decline gracefully.

    我们设法婉拒了。
