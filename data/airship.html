airship
eəʳʃɪp ˈɛəˌʃɪp ˈɛrˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural airships
N-COUNT 飞艇；飞船 An airship is an aircraft that consists of a large balloon which is filled with gas and is powered by an engine. It has a section underneath for passengers. 飞艇；飞船
SYN zeppelin, blimp, dirigible
