symptomatic
sɪmptəmætɪk ˌsɪmptəˈmætɪk
ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 作为症状的;作为征候的;表明…的 If something is symptomatic of something else, especially something bad, it is a sign of it. 作为症状的;作为征候的;表明…的 [formal]

  • The city's problems are symptomatic of the crisis that is spreading throughout the country. [+ of]

    该市的问题是正在全国蔓延的危机的征兆显现。

SYN indicative, representative, typical, characteristic
