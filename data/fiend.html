fiend
fiːnd find
Word forms: plural fiends
1 N-COUNT 恶魔般的人;极端凶残的人 If you describe someone as a fiend, you mean that they are extremely wicked or cruel. 恶魔般的人;极端凶残的人 [written]

  • A tearful husband repeated calls for help in catching the fiend who battered his wife.

    泪流满面的丈夫不断呼喊求助，想抓到摧残他妻子的恶魔。

2 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 对…着魔的人;对…痴狂的人;…迷 Fiend can be used after a noun to refer to a person who is very interested in the thing mentioned, and enjoys having a lot of it or doing it often. 对…着魔的人;对…痴狂的人;…迷

  • ...if you're a heavy coffee drinker or strong-tea fiend.

    如果你喝浓咖啡，或是喝浓茶成瘾
