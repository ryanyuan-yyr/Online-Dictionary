ploy
plɔɪ plɔɪ
Word forms: plural ploys
N-COUNT [oft adjective NOUN, NOUN to-infinitive] 计策；计谋；手段 A ploy is a way of behaving that someone plans carefully and secretly in order to gain an advantage for themselves. 计策；计谋；手段 [Also + of]
