silver-tongued
ˈsɪlvərˌtʌŋd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 伶牙俐齿的;能说会道的;有辩才的 A silver-tongued person is very skilful at persuading people to believe what they say or to do what they want them to do. 伶牙俐齿的;能说会道的;有辩才的

  • ...a silver-tongued lawyer.

    一名巧舌如簧的律师

  • ...the luckless lady who fell for his silver-tongued charm.

    被他的滔滔口才所迷惑的不幸女士
