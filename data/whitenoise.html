white noise
N-UNCOUNT 白噪音(尤指持续而无规律的杂音) White noise is sound, especially of a loud, continuous, or unpleasant kind, that seems to have no pattern or rhythm. 白噪音(尤指持续而无规律的杂音)

  • They were made to listen to white noise, such as static of the sort you might pick up between radio stations.

    他们被迫听白噪音，类似于你在调电台时听到的那种静电噪音。
