eerie
ɪəri ˈɪri ˈiri
Word forms: comparative eerier, superlative eeriest
ADJ-GRADED 怪异的;可怕的 If you describe something as eerie, you mean that it seems strange and frightening, and makes you feel nervous. 怪异的;可怕的

  • I walked down the eerie dark path.

    我走在那条漆黑恐怖的小路上。

  • ...an eerie calm.

    静得瘆人

SYN uncanny, strange, frightening, ghostly  
eerily

  • Monrovia after the fighting is eerily quiet.

    战斗过后的蒙罗维亚静得让人害怕。
