field goal
Word forms: field goals
1 N-COUNT 射门得分In American football, a field goal is a score of three points that is gained by kicking the ball through the opponent's goalposts above the crossbar. 射门得分

  • Stoyanovich kicked a 52-yard field goal, placing it squarely between Miami's goalposts.

    斯托扬诺维奇在52码处射门，从迈阿密球门正中射入。

2 N-COUNT 投篮得分In basketball, a field goal is a goal that is scored by throwing the ball through the basket during normal play. 投篮得分
