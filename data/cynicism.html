cynicism
sɪnɪsɪzəm ˈsɪnəˌsɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 认为世人皆自私的观念;愤世嫉俗 Cynicism is the belief that people always act selfishly. 认为世人皆自私的观念;愤世嫉俗

  • I found Ben's cynicism wearing at times.

    我有时觉得本的愤世嫉俗令人厌烦。

SYN scepticism, pessimism, sarcasm, misanthropy  
2 N-UNCOUNT (对成功或人的真善的）怀疑 Cynicism about something is the belief that it cannot be successful or that the people involved are not honourable. (对成功或人的真善的）怀疑

  • This talk betrays a certain cynicism about free trade. [+ about]

    这番讲话流露出一种对自由贸易不看好的态度。

SYN disbelief, doubt, scepticism, mistrust  
Quotations
Cynicism is intellectual dandyism without the coxcomb's feathersGeorge Meredith The Egoist
