peanut
piːnʌt ˈpiˌnʌt ˈpinət
Word forms: plural peanuts
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft NOUN n] 花生 Peanuts are small nuts that grow under the ground. Peanuts are often eaten as a snack, especially roasted and salted. 花生

  • ...a packet of peanuts.

    一包花生

  • Add 2 tablespoons of peanut oil.

    加两汤匙花生油。

2 N-PLURAL 少得可怜的钱；小钱 If you say that a sum of money is peanuts, you mean that it is very small. 少得可怜的钱；小钱 [informal, disapproval]

  • The cost was peanuts compared to a new kitchen.

    与建新厨房相比，那笔费用微不足道。

  • The jobs they offer pay peanuts.

    他们提供的工作报酬很低。
