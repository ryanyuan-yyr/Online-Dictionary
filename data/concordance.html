concordance
kənkɔːʳdəns kənˈkɔrdəns
Word forms: plural concordances
1 N-VAR 相似;一致;协调 If there is concordance between two things, they are similar to each other or consistent with each other. 相似;一致;协调 [formal]

  • ...a partial concordance between theoretical expectations and empirical evidence. [+ between]

    理论预期与实验证据之间的部分一致

2 N-COUNT 语汇索引 A concordance is a list of the words in a text or group of texts, with information about where in the text each word occurs and how often it occurs. The sentences each word occurs in are often given. 语汇索引
