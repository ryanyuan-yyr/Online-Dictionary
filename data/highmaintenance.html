high-maintenance
(also high maintenance)
耗费精力的;耗神的 If you describe something or someone as high-maintenance, you mean that they require a lot of attention, time, money, or effort. 耗费精力的;耗神的

  • Small gardens can be high maintenance.

    小花园可能需要耗费很多精神打理。

  • She was a high-maintenance girl who needed lots of attention.

    她是个很缠人的女孩，需要很多的关注。
