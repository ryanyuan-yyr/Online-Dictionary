mannequin
mænɪkɪn ˈmænəkɪn
Word forms: plural mannequins
1 N-COUNT （尤指商店橱窗中用以展示服装的）人体模型 A mannequin is a life-sized model of a person which is used to display clothes, especially in shop windows. （尤指商店橱窗中用以展示服装的）人体模型 [old-fashioned]
2 N-COUNT （尤指T台或平面）时装模特 A mannequin is a person who displays clothes, hats, or shoes by wearing them, especially in fashion shows or in fashion photographs. （尤指T台或平面）时装模特 [old-fashioned]
