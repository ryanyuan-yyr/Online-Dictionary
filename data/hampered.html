hamper
hæmpəʳ ˈhæmpər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hampers, present participle hampering, past tense , past participle hampered
1 VERB 妨碍;阻碍;牵制;束缚 If someone or something hampers you, they make it difficult for you to do what you are trying to do. 妨碍;阻碍;牵制;束缚

  • The bad weather hampered rescue operations. [VERB noun]

    恶劣的天气阻碍了救援工作的进行。

  • I was hampered by a lack of information. [VERB noun]

    我受到了信息不足的牵累。

SYN hinder, handicap, hold up, prevent  
2 N-COUNT （装有各种食品的）礼物篮 A hamper is a basket containing food of various kinds that is given to people as a present. （装有各种食品的）礼物篮

  • ...a luxury food hamper.

    豪华食品礼物篮

SYN basket, case, package, flax kit [New Zealand]  
3 N-COUNT （尤指装食品用的）带盖大篮 A hamper is a large basket with a lid, used especially for carrying food in. （尤指装食品用的）带盖大篮

  • ...a picnic hamper.

    野餐篮子
