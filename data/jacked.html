jack
dʒæk dʒæk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural jacks, present participle jacking, past tense , past participle jacked
1 N-COUNT 千斤顶；起重器 A jack is a device for lifting a heavy object off the ground, for example a car. 千斤顶；起重器
2 N-COUNT （纸牌中的）J牌，杰克 A jack is a playing card whose value is between a ten and a queen. A jack is usually represented by a picture of a young man. （纸牌中的）J牌，杰克
