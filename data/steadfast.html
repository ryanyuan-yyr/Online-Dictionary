steadfast
stedfɑːst -fæst ˈstɛdˌfæst ˈstɛdˌfɑst ˈstɛdfəst
ADJ-GRADED 坚定的;坚信的;坚贞不渝的 If someone is steadfast in something that they are doing, they are convinced that what they are doing is right and they refuse to change it or to give up. 坚定的;坚信的;坚贞不渝的

  • He remained steadfast in his belief that he had done the right thing. [+ in]

    他一直坚信自己做得对。

SYN resolute, firm, fast, fixed  
steadfastly

  • She steadfastly refused to look his way.

    她坚决拒绝朝他那边看。

steadfastness

  • By now the government was well aware of the steadfastness and strength of his resistance.

    此刻政府已经清楚地意识到他的反抗是何等的坚定有力。
