damnable
dæmnəbəl ˈdæmnəbəl
ADJ [ADJ n] 该死的;可恶的;讨厌的;糟糕的 You use damnable to emphasize that you dislike or disapprove of something a great deal. 该死的;可恶的;讨厌的;糟糕的 [old-fashioned, rude, emphasis]

  • What a damnable climate we have!

    我们这的气候真是糟糕透顶！

  • The charge of rape was a damnable lie.

    这项强奸指控完全是卑劣的谎言。

damnably

  • It was damnably unfair that he should suffer so much.

    他遭受了这么多的痛苦，真是太不公平了。
