whippersnapper
hwɪpəʳsnæpəʳ ˈhwɪpərˌsnæpər ˈwɪpərˌsnæpər
Word forms: plural whippersnappers
N-COUNT 自以为是的人;妄自尊大的人 If you refer to a young person as a whippersnapper, you disapprove of them because you think that they are behaving more confidently than they should. 自以为是的人;妄自尊大的人 [informal, old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • ...this bunch of irresponsible young whippersnappers.

    这群不负责任自以为是的年轻人
