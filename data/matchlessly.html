matchless
mætʃləs ˈmætʃlɪs
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无与伦比的;举世无双的 You can use matchless to emphasize that you think something is extremely good. 无与伦比的;举世无双的 [emphasis]

  • A timeless comic actor—his simplicity and his apparent ease are matchless.

    作为喜剧界的常青树—他的简单淳朴与自然大方无人能及。

  • The Savoy provides a matchless hotel experience.

    萨伏伊酒店为您提供无与伦比的下榻享受。

SYN unequalled, unique, unparalleled, unrivalled
