come out
1 PHRASAL VERB (书或光盘)出版，发行 When a new product such as a book or CD comes out, it becomes available to the public. (书或光盘)出版，发行

  • The book comes out this week. [VERB PREP.]

    这本书本周出版。

  • Christian Slater has a new movie coming out next month in which he plays a vigilante. [VERB PREP.]

    克里斯汀·史莱特有一部新片下月发行，他在片中扮演一个治安维持会成员。

SYN be published, appear, be released, be issued  
2 PHRASAL VERB (事实)暴露，披露，显露 If a fact comes out, it becomes known to people. (事实)暴露，披露，显露

  • The truth is beginning to come out about what happened. [VERB PREP.]

    真相开始逐渐浮出水面。

  • It will come out that she has covertly donated considerable sums to the IRA. [VERB PREP. that]

    她暗中给爱尔兰共和军提供了大笔资助的事情即将曝光。

SYN be revealed, emerge, be reported, be announced  
3 PHRASAL VERB 公开同性恋身份 When a gay person comes out, they let people know that they are gay. come out of the closet→see: closet  公开同性恋身份

  • ...the few gay men there who dare to come out. [VERB PREP.]

    那里少数几位有勇气公开宣布自己是同性恋的男子

  • I came out as a lesbian when I was still in my teens. [VERB PREP. + as]

    我才十几岁的时候，就公开了自己的同性恋身份。

4 PHRASAL VERB 结果是;到头来 To come out in a particular way means to be in the position or state described at the end of a process or event. 结果是;到头来

  • In this grim little episode of recent American history, few people come out well. [VERB PREP. adv./prep.]

    在美国近代史上这一小段阴暗时期，很少有人能有好的结果。

  • So what makes a good marriage? Faithfulness comes out top of the list. [V P adj]

    那么，是什么造就了美满的婚姻呢？结果表明忠诚是第一位的。

  • Julian ought to have resigned, then he'd have come out of it with some credit. [VERB PREP. + of]

    朱利安本应该辞职，这样他总归还能算是有些功劳。

5 PHRASAL VERB 宣布赞成/反对 If you come out for something, you declare that you support it. If you come out against something, you declare that you do not support it. 宣布赞成/反对

  • The German Chancellor and the French President have come out in favour of direct financial aid. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    德国总理和法国总统已经宣布支持直接的财政援助。

  • Its members had come out virtually unanimously against the tests. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    会员们几乎一致表示反对这些测试。

6 PHRASAL VERB 举行(罢工) When a group of workers comes out on strike, they go on strike. 举行(罢工) [British]

  • On September 18 the dockers again came out on strike. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    9月18日，码头工人再次举行罢工。

in AM, use 美国英语用 go out on strike
7 PHRASAL VERB (照片)冲洗(或打印)清楚 If a photograph does not come out, it does not appear or is unclear when it is developed and printed. (照片)冲洗(或打印)清楚

  • None of her snaps came out. [VERB PREP.]

    她的快照一张都没有洗出来。

8 PHRASAL VERB (太阳、月亮或星星)出现 When the sun, moon, or stars come out, they appear in the sky. (太阳、月亮或星星)出现

  • Oh, look. The sun's come out. [VERB PREP.]

    喂，瞧，太阳出来了。
