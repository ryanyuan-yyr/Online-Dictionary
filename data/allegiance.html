allegiance
əliːdʒəns əˈlidʒəns
Word forms: plural allegiances
N-VAR （对组织、人物、信仰的）忠诚，拥护 Your allegiance is your support for and loyalty to a particular group, person, or belief. （对组织、人物、信仰的）忠诚，拥护

  • My allegiance to Kendall and his company ran deep. [+ to]

    我对肯德尔及其公司的忠诚加深了。

  • ...a community driven by strong ties and allegiances.

    紧密团结、精诚合作的团体

SYN loyalty, duty, obligation, devotion
