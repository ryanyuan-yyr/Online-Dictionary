come by
PHRASAL VERB 弄到;得到 To come by something means to obtain it or find it. 弄到;得到

  • How did you come by that cheque?. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你是怎么弄到那张支票的？

  • In rural France, English language magazines are rather hard to come by. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在法国乡村，很难找到英语杂志。

SYN get, win, land [informal] , score [slang]
