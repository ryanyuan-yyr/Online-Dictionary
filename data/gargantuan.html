gargantuan
gɑːʳgæntʃuən gɑrˈgæntʃuən
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 巨大的；庞大的 If you say that something is gargantuan, you are emphasizing that it is very large. 巨大的；庞大的 [written, emphasis]

  • ...a marketing event of gargantuan proportions.

    大规模的促销活动

  • ...a gargantuan corruption scandal.

    骇人听闻的腐败丑闻

SYN huge, big, large, giant
