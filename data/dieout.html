die out
1 PHRASAL VERB 逐渐消亡;灭绝;绝种 If something dies out, it becomes less and less common and eventually disappears completely. 逐渐消亡;灭绝;绝种

  • How did the dinosaurs die out?. [VERB PREP.]

    恐龙是如何灭绝的？

  • I want to teach Irish so that it won't die out.

    我想教爱尔兰语，这样它就不会彻底消失。

SYN vanish, disappear, fade (away), perish  
2 PHRASAL VERB （火）逐渐熄灭;（风等）逐渐停止 If something such as a fire or wind dies out, it gradually stops burning or blowing. （火）逐渐熄灭;（风等）逐渐停止 [US]

  • Once the fire has died out, the salvage team will move in. [VERB PREP.]

    一旦大火熄灭，救援小组就会马上进入。
