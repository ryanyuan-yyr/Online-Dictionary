death trap
(also death-trap)Word forms: plural death traps
N-COUNT 死亡陷阱;危险场所（或车辆） If you say that a place or vehicle is a death trap, you mean it is in such bad condition that it might cause someone's death. 死亡陷阱;危险场所（或车辆） [informal]

  • Badly-built kit cars can be death traps.

    质量低劣的组装车可能会有安全隐患。
