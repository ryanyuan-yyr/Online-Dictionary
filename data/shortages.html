shortage
ʃɔːʳtɪdʒ ʃɔrtɪdʒ
Word forms: plural shortages
N-VAR [n NOUN] 不足;缺少;短缺 If there is a shortage of something, there is not enough of it. 不足;缺少;短缺

  • A shortage of funds is preventing the UN from monitoring relief.

    资金短缺使联合国无法督导救援工作。

  • Vietnam is suffering from food shortage.

    越南正遭受食物短缺之苦。

  • There's no shortage of ideas when it comes to improving the education of children.

    谈到改善孩子教育问题，大家都想法多多。

SYN deficiency, want, lack, failure
