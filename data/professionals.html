professional
prəfeʃənəl prəˈfɛʃənəl proʊˈfɛʃənəl prəˈfɛʃənəl
Word forms: plural professionals
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 职业的；专业的 Professional means relating to a person's work, especially work that requires special training. 职业的；专业的

  • His professional career started at Liverpool University.

    他的职业生涯是从利物浦大学开始的。

professionally

  • ...a professionally-qualified architect.

    一个具有专业从业资格的建筑师

  • The opening months of 1987 were difficult, personally and professionally.

    无论从个人生活还是从工作上来说，1987年初的几个月都非常艰难。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （人）职业的，专业的 Professional people have jobs that require advanced education or training. （人）职业的，专业的

  • ...highly qualified professional people like doctors and engineers.

    像医生和工程师这样需要高技能的专业人员

SYN qualified, trained, skilled, white-collar  
Professional is also a noun.

  • My father wanted me to become a professional and have more stability.

    我父亲想让我成为职业人士，这样能更稳定一些。

3 ADJ 专门从事的；非业余的 You use professional to describe people who do a particular thing to earn money rather than as a hobby. 专门从事的；非业余的

  • This has been my worst time for injuries since I started as a professional footballer.

    这是我当职业足球运动员以来受伤来得最不是时候的一次。

  • Jack Nicklaus has played in every Major Championship since he turned professional in 1961.

    杰克·尼克劳斯自从1961年转为职业选手以来参加了四大赛的所有比赛。

Professional is also a noun.

  • He had been a professional since March 1985.

    他从1985年3月开始就成了职业选手。

professionally

  • By age 16 he was playing professionally with bands in Greenwich Village.

    16岁的时候，他就为格林尼治村的乐队进行专业演奏。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] （运动）职业性质的 Professional sports are played for money rather than as a hobby. （运动）职业性质的

  • ...an art student who had played professional football for a short time.

    踢过短时间职业足球的艺术系学生

5 ADJ-GRADED 专业的；内行的；高水平的 If you say that something that someone does or produces is professional, you approve of it because you think that it is of a very high standard. 专业的；内行的；高水平的 [approval]

  • They run it with a truly professional but personal touch.

    他们的经营既有真正的专业水准又不乏个人特色。

Professional is also a noun.

  • ...a dedicated professional who worked harmoniously with the cast and crew.

    一位非常敬业的、同所有演职人员合作融洽的专业人士

professionally

  • These tickets have been produced very professionally.

    这些标签制作得非常专业。

6 See also: semi-professional
