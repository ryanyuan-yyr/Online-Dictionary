nickname
nɪkneɪm ˈnɪkˌneɪm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense nicknames, present participle nicknaming, past tense , past participle nicknamed
1 N-COUNT 绰号;诨号;外号 A nickname is an informal name for someone or something. 绰号;诨号;外号

  • Red got his nickname for his red hair.

    “红毛”的绰号源于他的红头发。

SYN pet name, label, diminutive, epithet  
2 VERB 给…起绰号;把…俗称为 If you nickname someone or something, you give them an informal name. 给…起绰号;把…俗称为

  • When he got older I nicknamed him Little Alf. [VERB noun noun]

    当他大一点时，我给他起了个“小阿尔夫”的绰号。

  • Which newspaper was once nicknamed The Thunderer? [be VERB-ed noun]

    哪家报纸一度被称为“怒吼者”？
