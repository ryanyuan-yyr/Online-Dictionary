coveted
kʌvɪtɪd ˈkʌvɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 人人向往的 You use coveted to describe something that very many people would like to have. 人人向往的

  • Allan Little from Radio 4 took the coveted title of reporter of the year.

    第四广播台的艾伦·利特尔获得了人人梦寐以求的年度记者称号。

  • ...one of sport's most coveted trophies.

    体育界最令人垂涎的一个奖杯

  • ...a supply of highly coveted hard currency.

    大受追捧的强势货币的供应
