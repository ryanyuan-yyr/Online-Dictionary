potpourri
poʊpʊəri -pʊriː ˌpoʊpʊˈri ˈpoʊpʊˌri
(also pot-pourri,pot pourri)Word forms: plural potpourris
1 N-MASS 百花香（各种不同的干花瓣和叶子的混合物，可使室内空气芬芳） Potpourri is a mixture of dried petals and leaves from different flowers. Potpourri is used to make rooms smell pleasant. 百花香（各种不同的干花瓣和叶子的混合物，可使室内空气芬芳）
2 N-SING 混杂物；杂烩；集锦 A potpourri of things is a collection of various different items which were not originally intended to form a group. 混杂物；杂烩；集锦

  • ...a potpourri of architectural styles from all over the world. [+ of]

    世界各地建筑风格的荟萃

SYN mixture, collection, combination, patchwork
