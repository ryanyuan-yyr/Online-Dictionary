crease
kriːs kris
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense creases, present participle creasing, past tense , past participle creased
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (衣服或纸的)褶痕，皱痕 Creases are lines that are made in cloth or paper when it is crushed or folded. (衣服或纸的)褶痕，皱痕

  • She stood up, frowning at the creases in her silk dress. [+ in]

    她站起身来，看到丝裙上的褶痕皱起了眉头。

  • Papa flattened the creases of the map with his broad hands. [+ of]

    爸爸用他的一双大手捋平了地图上的折痕。

  • ...cream coloured trousers with sharp creases.

    带有来去缝的米黄色长裤

SYN fold, ruck, line, tuck  
2 V-ERG [usually plural] (使)起皱;(使)起褶 If cloth or paper creases or if you crease it, lines form in it when it is crushed or folded. (使)起皱;(使)起褶

  • Most outfits crease a bit when you are travelling. [VERB]

    旅行时大多数服装都会略微起褶。

  • Liz sat down on the bed, lowering herself carefully so as not to crease her skirt. [VERB noun]

    莉兹小心翼翼地坐在床上，生怕把裙子弄皱。

SYN fold, ruck, line, tuck  
creased

  • His clothes were creased, as if he had slept in them.

    他的衣服皱巴巴的，好像他是穿着衣服睡的。

3 V-ERG (皮肤因为皱眉或微笑而)起皱纹;使(皮肤)起皱纹 If your face creases or if an expression creases it, lines appear on it because you are frowning or smiling. (皮肤因为皱眉或微笑而)起皱纹;使(皮肤)起皱纹 [written]

  • His ruddy face still routinely creases with mirth. [VERB]

    他红润的脸庞仍旧时常笑得堆起褶子。

  • For just the second time a look of emotion creases his face. [VERB noun]

    他的脸上，第二次流露出了感情。

4 N-COUNT (皮肤上的)皱纹 Creases in someone's skin are lines which form where their skin folds when they move. (皮肤上的)皱纹

  • ...the tiny creases at the corners of his eyes.

    他眼角的细纹

  • When Crevecoeur smiled, the creases in his face deepened.

    克雷弗克一笑，脸上的皱纹就加深了。

SYN wrinkle, line, crow's-foot  
creased

  • ...Jock's creased drunken face.

    乔克醉醺醺的脸上满是皱纹

5 N-SING (板球场上击球手的)位置线，击球线 In cricket, the crease is a line on the playing surface where the batsman stands. (板球场上击球手的)位置线，击球线

  • Haynes was still at the crease, unbeaten on 84.

    海恩斯还在击球线上，他已经得了84分了。
