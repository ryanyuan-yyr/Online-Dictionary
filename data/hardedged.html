hard-edged
ˈhɑːdˌɛdʒd
ADJ-GRADED （风格、戏剧、文章等）精辟有力的，犀利的，客观理智的 If you describe something such as a style, play, or article as hard-edged, you mean you admire it because it is powerful, critical, or unsentimental. （风格、戏剧、文章等）精辟有力的，犀利的，客观理智的 [approval]

    针砭时弊的戏剧
