concussion
kənkʌʃən kənˈkʌʃən
Word forms: plural concussions
1 N-VAR 脑震荡 If you suffer concussion after a blow to your head, you lose consciousness or feel sick or confused. 脑震荡

  • Nicky was rushed to hospital with concussion.

    尼基因脑震荡被火速送往医院。

  • She fell off a horse and suffered a concussion.

    她从马上摔下来，造成了脑震荡。

SYN shock, brain injury
