basket case
Word forms: plural basket cases
1 N-COUNT （经济或金融）困境 If someone describes a country or organization as a basket case, they mean that its economy or finances are in a seriously bad state. （经济或金融）困境 [informal]

  • The country is an economic basket case with chronic unemployment and rampant crime.

    这个国家长期失业严重、犯罪猖獗，经济几近瘫痪。

2 N-COUNT 精神失常者 If you describe someone as a basket case, you think that they are insane. 精神失常者 [informal, disapproval]

  • You're going to think I'm a basket case when I tell you this.

    如果我告诉了你这件事，你会认为我脑子进水了。
