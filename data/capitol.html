capitol
kæpɪtəl
(also Capitol,capitols)
1 N-COUNT 州议会大厦A capitol is a government building in which a state legislature meets. 州议会大厦 [US]

  • Thousands of striking teachers in Washington state will protest in front of the state capitol today.

    成千上万的华盛顿州罢工教师今天将在州议会大厦前举行抗议。

2 N-PROPER [the N] 美国国会大厦The Capitol is the government building in Washington, D.C., in which the U.S. Congress meets. 美国国会大厦 [US]

  • Thousands of demonstrators rallied in front of the Capitol.

    成千上万的示威者在美国国会大厦前集会。
