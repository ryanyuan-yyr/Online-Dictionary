overuse oʊvəʳjuːz oʊvəʳjuːs ˈoʊvərˌjus ˌ oʊvərˈjus ˌ oʊvərˈjuz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overuses, present participle overusing, past tense , past participle overused
The verb is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'juːz/. The noun is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'juːs/. 动词读作 /ˌəʊvə'juːz/。名词读作 /ˌəʊvə'juːs/。
1 VERB 过多（或过度）使用；滥用 If someone overuses something, they use more of it than necessary, or use it more often than necessary. 过多（或过度）使用；滥用

  • A nasal spray or drops would ease your symptoms, but don't overuse them or further congestion could result. [VERB noun]

    鼻腔喷剂或滴剂能减轻症状，但请勿使用过频，否则鼻塞可能会加重。

  • Don't overuse heated appliances on you hair.

    不要过多地使用电热器具弄头发。

Overuse is also a noun.

  • Supplies are under increasing threat from overuse and pollution.

    由于滥用和污染，供应正受到日益严重的威胁。

2 VERB 滥用（词语等）；使成为陈词滥调 If you say that people overuse a word or idea, you mean that they use it so often that it no longer has any real meaning or effect. 滥用（词语等）；使成为陈词滥调

  • Which words or phrases do you most overuse? [VERB noun]

    你最为常用的词或词组有哪些？

overused

  • 'Just Do It' has become one of the most overused catch phrases in recent memory.

    Just Do it (想做就做)成了近来记忆中用得最滥的流行语之一。
