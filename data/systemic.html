systemic
sɪstiːmɪk sɪsˈtɛmɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 系统的;影响全面的;全身的 Systemic means affecting the whole of something. 系统的;影响全面的;全身的 [formal]

  • The economy is locked in a systemic crisis.

    经济陷入全面危机。

2 ADJ (化学品或药品)内吸的 Systemic chemicals or drugs are absorbed into the whole of an organism such as a plant or person, rather than being applied to one area. (化学品或药品)内吸的 [technical]
