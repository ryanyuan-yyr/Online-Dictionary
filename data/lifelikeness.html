lifelike
laɪflaɪk ˈlaɪfˌlaɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 像有生命的 Something that is lifelike has the appearance of being alive. 像有生命的

  • ...a lifelike doll.

    像真人的玩偶

SYN realistic, faithful, authentic, natural  
2 ADJ-GRADED （艺术品）逼真的，栩栩如生的，生动真切的 A lifelike work of art is so skilfully done that it appears very like the person or thing that it is supposed to represent. （艺术品）逼真的，栩栩如生的，生动真切的

  • The painting depicted a hall so lifelike that a casual observer might believe himself to be looking through an open door.

    这幅画描绘的厅堂如此逼真，若不细看，还真以为是从一扇开着的门往里看呢。
