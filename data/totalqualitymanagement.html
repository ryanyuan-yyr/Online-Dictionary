total quality management
N-UNCOUNT 全面质量管理(缩略形式为TQM) Total quality management is a set of management principles aimed at improving performance throughout a company, especially by involving employees in decision-making. The abbreviation TQM is also used. 全面质量管理(缩略形式为TQM) [business]
