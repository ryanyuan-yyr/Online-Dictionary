unpack
ʌnpæk ʌnˈpæk ʌnˈpæk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unpacks, present participle unpacking, past tense , past participle unpacked
1 VERB 打开（箱、盒等）；（从箱、盒等中）取出 When you unpack a suitcase, box, or similar container, or you unpack the things inside it, you take the things out of the container. 打开（箱、盒等）；（从箱、盒等中）取出 [Also VERB]

  • He unpacked his bag. [VERB noun]

    他打开了包。

  • Our guide unpacked a picnic of ham sandwiches and offered us tea. [VERB noun]

    我们的导游打开装着火腿三明治的野餐盒，并给我们倒了些茶水。

2 VERB 分析；剖析；仔细考虑 If you unpack an idea or problem, you analyse it and consider it in detail. 分析；剖析；仔细考虑

  • A lot of ground has been covered in unpacking the issues central to achieving this market-led strategic change. [VERB noun]

    在分析实现这个以市场为导向的战略转变的关键问题时，涉及了诸多领域。
