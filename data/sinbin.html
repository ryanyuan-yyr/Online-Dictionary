sin-bin
ˈsɪnbɪn
(also sin bin)
N-SING (冰球或橄榄球运动中的)受罚席，被罚下场球员的座席 In the sports of ice hockey and rugby league, if a player is sent to the sin-bin, they are ordered to leave the playing area for a short period of time because they have done something that is against the rules. (冰球或橄榄球运动中的)受罚席，被罚下场球员的座席
