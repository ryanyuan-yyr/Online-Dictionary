obliging
əblaɪdʒɪŋ əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ oʊˈblaɪdʒɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 乐于助人的;与人方便的；热情的 If you describe someone as obliging, you think that they are willing and eager to be helpful. 乐于助人的;与人方便的；热情的 [old-fashioned, or written, approval]

  • He is an extremely pleasant and obliging man.

    他是个非常友善、古道热肠的人。

SYN accommodating, kind, helpful, willing  
obligingly

  • He swung round and strode towards the door. Benedict obligingly held it open.

    他突然转身，大步朝门口走去。本尼迪克特主动上前将门打开。
