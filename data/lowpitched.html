low-pitched
ˈloʊˈpɪtʃt
1 ADJ-GRADED （声音）低沉的，沉闷的 A sound that is low-pitched is deep. （声音）低沉的，沉闷的

  • With a low-pitched rumbling noise, the propeller began to rotate.

    伴随着隆隆的低沉噪声，螺旋桨开始旋转起来。

2 ADJ-GRADED （嗓音）轻柔的，轻细的 A voice that is low-pitched is very soft and quiet. （嗓音）轻柔的，轻细的

  • He kept his voice low-pitched in case someone was listening.

    他把声音放低以防被人听见。
