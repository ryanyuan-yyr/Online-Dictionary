squeaky clean
(also squeaky-clean)
ADJ-GRADED 品行端正的;品德高尚的 If you say that someone is squeaky clean, you mean that they live a very moral life and have never done anything wrong. 品行端正的;品德高尚的 [informal]

  • Maybe this guy isn't so squeaky clean after all.

    也许这个人说到底品行并非那么完美。

  • ...his desire to foster a squeaky-clean image.

    他对树立一个品德高尚的形象的渴望
