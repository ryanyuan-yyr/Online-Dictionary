atmosphere
ætməsfɪəʳ ˈætməsˌfɪr
Word forms: plural atmospheres
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 大气;大气层;大气圈 A planet's atmosphere is the layer of air or other gases around it. 大气;大气层;大气圈

  • ...dangerous levels of pollution in the Earth's atmosphere.

    地球大气层污染程度严重

  • The Partial Test-Ban Treaty bans nuclear testing in the atmosphere.

    《部分禁止核试验条约》禁止在大气层中进行核试验。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （呼吸的）空气 The atmosphere of a place is the air that you breathe there. （呼吸的）空气

  • These gases pollute the atmosphere of towns and cities. [+ of]

    这些气体污染了城镇的空气。

SYN air, sky, heavens, aerosphere  
3 N-SING 氛围；环境 The atmosphere of a place is the general impression that you get of it. 氛围；环境

  • Pale wooden floors and plenty of natural light add to the relaxed atmosphere.

    淡色木地板和充足的自然光平添了几分轻松的氛围。

  • There's still an atmosphere of great hostility and tension in the city. [+ of]

    强烈的敌对和紧张气氛依然笼罩着这座城市。

4 N-UNCOUNT （地方或事件的）情调，魅力 If a place or an event has atmosphere, it is interesting. （地方或事件的）情调，魅力

  • The old harbour is still full of atmosphere and well worth visiting.

    这座古老的港口依然充满了情趣，颇值得一游。
