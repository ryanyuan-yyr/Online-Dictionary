-toned -toʊnd təʊnd
COMB in ADJ (与形容词构成复合词)表示“具有…音调(或色调)” -toned combines with adjectives to indicate that something has a particular kind of tone. (与形容词构成复合词)表示“具有…音调(或色调)”

  • …a beautiful silver-toned voice ideal for Mozart.

    最适于演绎莫扎特乐曲的银铃般美妙的嗓音

  • ...soft, pastel-toned drawings.

    色调柔和淡雅的图画
