roaring
rɔːrɪŋ ˈrɔrɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 熊熊燃烧的;炽烈的 A roaring fire has large flames and is sending out a lot of heat. 熊熊燃烧的;炽烈的
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (成功)巨大的 If something is a roaring success, it is very successful indeed. (成功)巨大的

  • The government's first effort to privatize a company has been a roaring success.

    政府首次实行公司私有化的努力获得了巨大的成功。

3 See also: roar 
4 PHRASE 做成一笔大买卖;售出大批货物;生意兴隆 If someone does a roaring trade in a type of goods, they sell a lot of them. 做成一笔大买卖;售出大批货物;生意兴隆

  • Salesmen of unofficial souvenirs have also been doing a roaring trade.

    出售非官方纪念品的推销员的生意也很红火。
