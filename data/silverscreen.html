silver screen
N-SING 银幕电影 People sometimes refer to the films that are shown in cinemas as the silver screen . 银幕电影

  • Marlon Brando, Steve McQueen, and James Dean are now legends of the silver screen.

    马龙·白兰度、史蒂夫·麦奎因和詹姆斯·迪恩现在成了银幕上的的传奇人物。

  • We don't see much of dear old Peter O'Toole on the silver screen these days.

    最近我们不常见到亲爱的彼得·奥图尔活跃在银幕上了。
