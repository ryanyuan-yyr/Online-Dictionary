defame
dɪfeɪm diˈfeɪm dɪˈfeɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense defames, present participle defaming, past tense , past participle defamed
VERB 诽谤;中伤;损害…的名誉 If someone defames another person or thing, they say bad and untrue things about them. 诽谤;中伤;损害…的名誉 [formal]

  • Sgt Norwood complained that the article defamed him. [VERB noun]

    诺伍德中士申诉说这篇文章对他进行了诽谤。

SYN slander, smear, libel, discredit
