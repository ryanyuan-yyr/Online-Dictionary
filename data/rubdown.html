rub down
1 PHRASAL VERB (用砂纸等)打磨平滑 If you rub down a rough surface, you make it smooth by rubbing it with something such as sandpaper. (用砂纸等)打磨平滑 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They were settling to their work, rubbing down the woodwork with sandpaper. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们开始工作，用砂纸把木制品打磨平滑。

2 PHRASAL VERB （用布、毛巾等）擦干，按摩 If you rub someone down, you dry them or massage them with something such as a towel or cloth. （用布、毛巾等）擦干，按摩

  • He set him on the bed and rubbed him down with a coarse towel. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他让他躺到床上，用一条粗糙的毛巾给他擦干了全身。

  • After a bath or shower rub down the whole body with a loofah. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    在洗澡或淋浴后，用丝瓜络按摩身体。
