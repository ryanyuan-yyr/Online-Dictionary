horrific
hərɪfɪk hɔːr- hɔˈrɪfɪk həˈrɪfɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED （攻击、事故、伤势等）令人震惊的，极其可怕的，十分严重的 If you describe a physical attack, accident, or injury as horrific, you mean that it is very bad, so that people are shocked when they see it or think about it. （攻击、事故、伤势等）令人震惊的，极其可怕的，十分严重的

  • I have never seen such horrific injuries.

    我从来没有见过如此严重的伤势。

  • The slaughter was horrific.

    屠杀令人震惊。

SYN horrifying, shocking [informal] , appalling, frightening  
horrifically

  • He had been horrifically assaulted before he died.

    他死前曾遭到惨无人道的殴打。

2 ADJ-GRADED 大得令人厌恶的 If you describe something as horrific, you mean that it is so big that it is extremely unpleasant. 大得令人厌恶的

  • ...piling up horrific extra amounts of money on top of your original debt.

    在你原来债务的基础上又高筑债台

horrifically

  • Opera productions are horrifically expensive.

    歌剧演出耗资惊人。
