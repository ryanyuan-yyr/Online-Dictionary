castigate
kæstɪgeɪt ˈkæstɪˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense castigates, present participle castigating, past tense , past participle castigated
VERB 怒声责骂;斥责 If you castigate someone or something, you speak to them angrily or criticize them severely. 怒声责骂;斥责 [formal]

  • Marx never lost an opportunity to castigate colonialism. [VERB noun]

    马克思抓住每一个机会严厉谴责殖民主义。

  • She castigated him for having no intellectual interests. [VERB noun + for]

    她斥责他没有智识兴趣。

SYN reprimand, blast, carpet [informal] , flame [informal]
