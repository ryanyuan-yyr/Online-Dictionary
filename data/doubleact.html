double act
ˈdʌbəl ækt
(also double-act)Word forms: plural double acts
N-COUNT 双人戏;双簧 Two comedians or entertainers who perform together are referred to as a double act. Their performance can also be called a double act . 双人戏;双簧

  • ...a famous comedy double act.

    著名的喜剧双人戏

  • I met a pal of mine later and he suggested that we do a double act.

    后来我遇到一个朋友，他建议我们演双簧。
