hew
hjuː hju
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hews, present participle hewing, past tense hewed, past participle hewed, past participle hewn
1 VERB 砍;劈 If you hew stone or wood, you cut it, for example with an axe. 砍;劈 [old-fashioned]

  • He felled, peeled and hewed his own timber. [VERB noun]

    他自己砍树、剥皮、劈成木材。

SYN cut, chop, axe, hack  
2 VERB [usually passive] （在岩石或木材上）砍成，劈出 If something is hewn from stone or wood, it is cut from stone or wood. （在岩石或木材上）砍成，劈出 [literary, old-fashioned]

  • ...the rock from which the lower chambers and subterranean passageways have been hewn. [be V-ed + from/out of]

    开凿出下闸室和地下通道的岩石

  • ...medieval monasteries hewn out of the rockface. [VERB-ed]

    在岩壁上凿出的中世纪修道院

SYN carve, make, form, fashion
