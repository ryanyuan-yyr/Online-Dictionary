suicidal
suːɪsaɪdəl ˌsuəˈsaɪdəl ˈsuəˌsaɪdəl
1 ADJ 有自杀倾向的;想自杀的 People who are suicidal want to kill themselves. 有自杀倾向的;想自杀的

  • I was suicidal and just couldn't stop crying.

    我当时想自杀，一直哭个不停。

  • Her suicidal tendencies continued for several more weeks.

    她的自杀倾向又持续了几周。

2 ADJ 自取灭亡的;有致命危险的 If you describe an action or behaviour as suicidal, you mean that it is very dangerous. 自取灭亡的;有致命危险的

  • They realized it would be suicidal to resist in the face of overwhelming military superiority.

    他们意识到面对力量相差如此悬殊的军队，抵抗无异于自取灭亡。

  • ...the suicidal bicycle rickshaws of New Delhi.

    新德里非常危险的人力车
