undersized
ʌndəʳsaɪzd ˌʌndəˈsaɪzd ˌʌndəˈsaɪz ˈʌndərˌsaɪzd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 小于一般尺寸的；偏小的 Undersized people or things are smaller than usual, or smaller than they should be. 小于一般尺寸的；偏小的

  • ...undersized and underweight babies.

    偏小偏轻的婴儿

  • They squashed into an undersized reception room.

    他们挤到了一个较小的接待室里。

  • He was undersized, as were all the local children I was to meet.

    他个头偏矮，跟我见到的所有当地孩子一样。

SYN stunted, underdeveloped, small, tiny
