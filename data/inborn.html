inborn
ɪnbɔːʳn ˈɪnˈbɔːn ˈɪnˌbɔrn
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 天生的;生来就有的 Inborn qualities are natural ones which you are born with. 天生的;生来就有的

  • He had an inborn talent for languages.

    他有语言天分。

  • It is clear that the ability to smile is inborn.

    显然，笑的能力是与生俱来的。

SYN natural, inherited, inherent, hereditary
