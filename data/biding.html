bide
baɪd baɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bides, present participle biding, past tense , past participle bided
PHRASE 等待时机；等待良机 If you bide your time, you wait for a good opportunity before doing something. 等待时机；等待良机

  • He was content to bide his time patiently, waiting for the opportunity to approach her.

    他乐于耐心等待接近她的良机。
