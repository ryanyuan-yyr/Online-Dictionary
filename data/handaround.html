hand around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 hand round
PHRASAL VERB 递送，传递（食物等） If you hand around or hand round something such as food, you pass it from one person to another in a group. 递送，传递（食物等）

  • John handed round the plate of sandwiches. [VERB PREP. noun]

    约翰把三明治盘子递给每一个人。

  • ...the free Jamaican cigars that were always handed around at official functions. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    总是在正式场合递送的免费牙买加雪茄

  • Dean produced another bottle and handed it round. [VERB noun PREP.]

    迪安又拿出一瓶，挨个儿传给大家。
