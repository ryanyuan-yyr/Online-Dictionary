come in for
PHRASAL VERB 遭到(批评);受到(指责) If someone or something comes in for criticism or blame, they receive it. 遭到(批评);受到(指责)

  • The plans have already come in for fierce criticism in many quarters of the country. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    这些计划已经遭到该国多方面人士的严厉批评。

SYN receive, get, suffer, endure
