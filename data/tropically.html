tropical
trɒpɪkəl ˈtrɑpɪkəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 热带的;有热带特征的 Tropical means belonging to or typical of the tropics. 热带的;有热带特征的

  • ...tropical diseases.

    热带疾病

  • ...a plan to preserve the world's tropical forests.

    保护世界热带雨林的计划

2 ADJ-GRADED 湿热的;典型热带气候的 Tropical weather is hot and damp weather that people believe to be typical of the tropics. 湿热的;典型热带气候的
