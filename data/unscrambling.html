unscramble
ʌnskræmbəl ʌnˈskræmbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unscrambles, present participle unscrambling, past tense , past participle unscrambled
VERB 整理；使有条理 To unscramble things that are in a state of confusion or disorder means to arrange them so that they can be understood or seen clearly. 整理；使有条理

  • All you have to do to win is unscramble the words here to find four names of birds. [VERB noun]

    只要你能从这些单词中找到四种鸟的名称你就赢了。

  • ...electronic circuits which can be programmed to allow the user to unscramble transmitted signals. [VERB noun]

    可以编程让用户来调试出传输信号的电路
