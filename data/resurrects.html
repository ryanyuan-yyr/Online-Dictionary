resurrect
rezərekt ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense resurrects, present participle resurrecting, past tense , past participle resurrected
VERB 使复兴;恢复;使重新出现 If you resurrect something, you cause it to exist again after it had disappeared or ended. 使复兴;恢复;使重新出现

  • Attempts to resurrect the ceasefire have already failed once. [VERB noun]

    为再次停火作出的努力已经失败了一次。

  • Sam Torrance is the man I have to thank for resurrecting my career. [VERB noun]

    多亏了萨姆·托兰斯，我才重新开始我的职业。

SYN revive, renew, bring back, kick-start [informal]  
resurrection

  • This is a resurrection of an old story from the mid-70s.
