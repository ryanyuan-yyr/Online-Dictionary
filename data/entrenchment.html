entrenchment
ɪntrentʃmənt
Word forms: plural entrenchments
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 堑壕;战壕 Entrenchments are a series of long deep holes called trenches which are dug for defence by soldiers in war. 堑壕;战壕
2 N-UNCOUNT 根深蒂固;牢固 Entrenchment means the firm establishment of a system or your own position in a situation. 根深蒂固;牢固

  • ...the entrenchment of democratic norms. [+ of]

    民主规范的牢固确立
