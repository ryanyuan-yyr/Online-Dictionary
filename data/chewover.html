chew over
PHRASAL VERB 反复思考;不断考虑 If you chew something over, you keep thinking about it. 反复思考;不断考虑

  • He tends to chew things over too much in his mind. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他这个人就爱在心里反复琢磨事儿。

  • Goldstone chewed over the idea further. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    戈德斯通进一步考虑了这个问题。

SYN consider, weigh up, ponder, mull (over)
