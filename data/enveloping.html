envelop
ɪnveləp ɛnˈvɛləp ɪnˈvɛləp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense envelops, present participle enveloping, past tense , past participle enveloped
VERB 包住;盖住;围绕 If one thing envelops another, it covers or surrounds it completely. 包住;盖住;围绕

  • That lovely, rich fragrant smell of the forest enveloped us. [VERB noun]

    森林里那种馥郁芬芳的迷人气味围绕着我们。

  • ...an enveloping sense of well-being. [VERB-ing]

    萦绕的幸福感

SYN enclose, cover, hide, surround
