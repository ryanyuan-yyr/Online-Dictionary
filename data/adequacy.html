adequacy
ædɪkwəsi ˈædɪkwəsi
N-UNCOUNT 适当;恰当 Adequacy is the quality of being good enough or great enough in amount to be acceptable. 适当;恰当

  • I am very critical of the adequacy of Britain's race laws.

    我对英国种族法律的适当性颇有微词。

  • Several studies point to a real cause for concern over the adequacy of the diet eaten by British children.

    针对英国儿童膳食质量的担忧，数份研究将矛头指向了同一根源。

SYN sufficiency, capability, competence, suitability
