critique

Word forms: plural critiques
N-COUNT 评论;评论文章 A critique is a written examination and judgment of a situation or of a person's work or ideas. 评论;评论文章 [formal]

  • She had brought a book, a feminist critique of Victorian lady novelists. [+ of]

    她带来了一本书，这本书从女性主义视角对维多利亚时代女性小说家进行了评论。

  • ...the Marxist critique of capitalism.

    马克思主义者对资本主义的批判

SYN essay, review, analysis, assessment
