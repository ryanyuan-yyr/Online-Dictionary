defamatory
dɪfæmətri -tɔːri diˈfæməˌtɔri dɪˌfæməˌtɔri
ADJ-GRADED 诬蔑的;诽谤的;中伤的 Speech or writing that is defamatory is likely to damage someone's good reputation by saying something bad and untrue about them. 诬蔑的;诽谤的;中伤的 [formal]

  • The article was highly defamatory.

    这篇文章极具诽谤性。

SYN slanderous, insulting, abusive, denigrating
