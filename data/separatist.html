separatist
sepərətɪst ˈsɛpərətɪst ˈsɛpəˌreɪtɪst
Word forms: plural separatists
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 分裂主义的;独立主义的 Separatist organizations and activities within a country involve members of a group of people who want to establish their own separate government or are trying to do so. 分裂主义的;独立主义的

  • ...the Basque separatist movement.

    巴斯克分裂主义运动

  • Nearly 2,500 have died in separatist violence this year.

    近 2,500 人在今年的分裂主义暴乱中丧生。

2 N-COUNT 分裂主义者;独立主义者 Separatists are people who want their own separate government or are involved in separatist activities. 分裂主义者;独立主义者

  • The army has come under attack by separatists.

    军队一直遭到分裂主义者的袭击。
