defined dɪfaɪnd
ADJ-GRADED [usually adverb ADJ] 轮廓分明的;外形清晰的 If something is clearly defined or strongly defined, its outline is clear or strong. 轮廓分明的;外形清晰的

  • A clearly defined track now leads down to the valley.

    眼前有一条清晰可辨的小径通向山谷。

  • Here the path is less defined.

    小路在这里就不那么清楚好认了。

  • She had a strongly defined chin, a high forehead, and light grey eyes.

    她有着轮廓分明的下巴、高高的额头和一双浅灰色的眼睛。

SYN marked, clear, obvious, prominent
