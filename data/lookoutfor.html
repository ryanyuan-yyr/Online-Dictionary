look out for
1 PHRASAL VERB 留心；注意；留意 If you look out for something, you pay attention to things so that you notice it if or when it occurs. 留心；注意；留意

  • Look out for special deals. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    留意特价。

  • What are the symptoms to look out for? [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    有哪些症状需要注意？

SYN be careful of, beware, watch out for, pay attention to  
2 PHRASAL VERB 考虑…的利益；关照 If you look out for someone, you make sure that they have all the advantages that they can. 考虑…的利益；关照

  • I'm just trying to look out for you. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我只是想要为你着想。

  • I felt that I had to look out for myself, because I didn't see that anyone else was going to. [V P P pron-refl]

    我觉得必须为自己考虑一下了，因为我看不出还有谁会替我考虑。
