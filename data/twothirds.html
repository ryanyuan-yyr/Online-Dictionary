two-thirds
ˌtuːˈθɜːdz
(also two thirds)
QUANT 三分之二 Two-thirds of something is an amount that is two out of three equal parts of it. 三分之二

  • Two-thirds of householders in this country live in a mortgaged home. [+ of]

    该国 2/3 的屋主住在抵押贷款买来的房子里。

  • Stir the chopped chocolate, fruit and nuts into two-thirds of the cheese mixture.

    把切碎的巧克力、水果和坚果按 2 比 1 的比例与奶酪拌成混合物。

Two-thirds is also a pronoun.

  • The United States and Russia hope to conclude a treaty to cut their nuclear arsenals by two-thirds.

    美国与俄罗斯希望达成一项将各自核武器削减 2/3 的条约。

Two-thirds is also an adverb.

  • Do not fill the container more than two-thirds full.

    加入的量不要满过容器总容量的 2/3。

  • A second book has already been commissioned and is two-thirds finished.

    已经委托作者撰写第二本书，现在完成了 2/3。

Two-thirds is also an adjective.

  • ...the two thirds majority in parliament needed to make constitutional changes.

    修改宪法需占议会2/3的多数议员同意
