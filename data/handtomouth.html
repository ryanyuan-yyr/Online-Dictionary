hand-to-mouth ˈhæntuˈmaʊθ
(also hand to mouth)
ADJ-GRADED （生活）勉强糊口的 A hand-to-mouth existence is a way of life in which you have hardly enough food or money to live on. （生活）勉强糊口的

  • The worst-paid live a hand-to-mouth existence without medical or other benefits.

    收入最低者过着只能勉强糊口的日子，没有医疗补助或其他福利。

SYN insecure, uncertain, unsettled, unsure  
Hand-to-mouth is also an adverb.

  • ...penniless students living hand-to-mouth.

    身无分文、勉强度日的学生
