trading card
Word forms: trading cards
N-COUNT 收藏卡A trading card is one of a set of thin pieces of cardboard with a picture relating to a particular theme, such as baseball or football, printed on it. Collectors buy the cards or trade them with other collectors. 收藏卡 [mainly US]

  • Now collectors can choose trading cards from a variety of sports.

    现在藏家们可以从一系列体育项目中选择收藏卡。
