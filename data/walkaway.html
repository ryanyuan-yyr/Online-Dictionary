walk away
PHRASAL VERB 脱身;一走了之 If you walk away from a problem or a difficult situation, you do nothing about it or do not face any bad consequences from it. 脱身;一走了之

  • The most appropriate strategy may simply be to walk away from the problem. [VERB PREP. + from]

    最好的对策可能就是回避这个问题。

  • No one knows you're a part of this. You can just walk away. [VERB PREP.]

    没人知道这件事与你有关。你可以置身事外。
