switch off
1 PHRASAL VERB 关掉，关上(电灯等) If you switch off a light or other electrical device, you stop it working by operating a switch. 关掉，关上(电灯等) [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • She switched off the coffee-machine. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她关掉了咖啡机。

  • Glass parked the car and switched the engine off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    格拉斯停好车，熄了火。

SYN turn off, shut off, deactivate, cut  
2 PHRASAL VERB 失去兴趣;不考虑;不再想着;不再担忧 If you switch off, you stop paying attention or stop thinking or worrying about something. 失去兴趣;不考虑;不再想着;不再担忧 [informal]

  • Thankfully, I've learned to switch off and let it go over my head. [VERB PREP.]

    谢天谢地，我已经学会了释然，随它去吧。

  • You may find you've got so many things to think about that it's difficult to switch off. [VERB PREP.]

    你会发觉需要考虑的事情太多了，想不操心都难。

SYN turn off
