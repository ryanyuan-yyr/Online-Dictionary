deplete
dɪpliːt diˈplit dɪˈplit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense depletes, present participle depleting, past tense , past participle depleted
1 VERB （大量）减少，消耗 To deplete a stock or amount of something means to reduce it. （大量）减少，消耗 [formal]

  • ...substances that deplete the ozone layer. [VERB noun]

    大量消耗臭氧层的物质

  • They fired in long bursts, which depleted their ammunition. [VERB noun]

    他们长时间开火，耗尽了弹药。

  • Most native mammal species have been severely depleted. [VERB noun]

    大多数本地哺乳动物的数量都已经大大减少了。
