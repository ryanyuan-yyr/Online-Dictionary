presentiment
prɪzentɪmənt priˈzɛntəmənt prɪˈzɛntəmənt
Word forms: plural presentiments
N-COUNT [usu N that] 预感；预知 A presentiment is a feeling that a particular event, for example someone's death, will soon take place. 预感；预知 [formal]

  • I had a presentiment that he represented a danger to me.

    我预感他会给我带来危险。

  • He had a presentiment of disaster. [+ of]

    他预感会有灾难降临。

SYN premonition, feeling, expectation, anticipation
