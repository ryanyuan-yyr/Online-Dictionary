ground level
N-UNCOUNT [oft preposition NOUN] 地面（相对于高处或地下而言） If something is at ground level, it is at the same level as the ground, as opposed to being higher up or below the surface. 地面（相对于高处或地下而言）

  • The hotel is set on three floors. There's a bar and cafe at ground level.

    这家饭店有3层，在一楼有间咖啡吧。

  • The remaining block of woodland is cut down to ground level.

    剩下的那片树林被砍得精光。
