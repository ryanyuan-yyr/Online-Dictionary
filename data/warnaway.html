warn away
PHRASAL VERB 劝告(…)离开;告诫(…)停止(做…) If you warn someone away, you tell them to go away or to stop doing something because of possible danger or punishment. 劝告(…)离开;告诫(…)停止(做…)

  • Soon an official appeared to warn them away. [VERB noun PREP.]

    很快一位官员出来劝告他们离开。

  • Analysts warn us away from drawing any conclusions. [V n P from n/-ing]

    分析家劝告我们不要妄下结论。
