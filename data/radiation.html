radiation
reɪdieɪʃən ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 辐射物；放射物 Radiation consists of very small particles of a radioactive substance. Large amounts of radiation can cause illness and death. 辐射物；放射物

  • They suffer from health problems and fear the long term effects of radiation.

    他们遭受着健康问题的困扰并且害怕辐射的长期影响。

  • If the cancer returns, radiation therapy is successful in 90 per cent of cases.

    如果癌症复发，放射性疗法有90%的治愈率。

2 N-UNCOUNT 辐射能;放射能;（尤指）热能 Radiation is energy, especially heat, that comes from a particular source. 辐射能;放射能;（尤指）热能

  • The satellite will study energy radiation from stars.

    这颗人造卫星将用来研究恒星的能量辐射。
