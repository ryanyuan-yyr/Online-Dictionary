riff
rɪf rɪf
Word forms: plural riffs
1 N-COUNT (爵士乐和摇滚乐中的)重复乐段 In jazz and rock music, a riff is a short repeated tune. (爵士乐和摇滚乐中的)重复乐段
2 N-COUNT (一小段表达一定主题或思想的)讲话，文章 A riff is a short piece of speech or writing that develops a particular theme or idea. (一小段表达一定主题或思想的)讲话，文章
