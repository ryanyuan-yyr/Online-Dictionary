happily
hæpɪli
1 ADV-GRADED 幸运地;幸运的是 You can add happily to a statement to indicate that you are glad that something happened or is true. 幸运地;幸运的是

  • Happily, his neck injuries were not serious.

    很幸运，他脖子上的伤并不严重。

  • Happily, Lisa Martineau takes an opposite approach.

    幸运的是，莉萨·马蒂诺采取了截然相反的方式。

SYN luckily, fortunately, providentially, favourably
