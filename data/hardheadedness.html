hard-headed
ADJ-GRADED （人）讲究实际的，头脑清晰的，不感情用事的 You use hard-headed to describe someone who is practical and determined to get what they want or need, and who does not allow emotions to affect their actions. （人）讲究实际的，头脑清晰的，不感情用事的
