home stretch ˈhəʊmˌstrɛtʃ
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 home straight
1 N-SING （赛跑的）最后阶段，冲刺阶段 The home stretch or the home straight is the last part of a race. （赛跑的）最后阶段，冲刺阶段

  • Holmes matched Boulmerka stride for stride down the home straight to finish second.

    霍姆斯在最后冲刺阶段和布尔梅卡齐头并进，最后获得了第二名。

2 N-SING （尤指困难或乏味的长时间活动的）最后部分 You can refer to the last part of any activity that lasts for a long time as the home stretch or the home straight, especially if the activity is difficult or boring. （尤指困难或乏味的长时间活动的）最后部分

  • ...as his two hours of banter, quips and anecdotes goes into the home straight.

    随着他长达两小时的逗乐说笑、闲谈轶事进入尾声
