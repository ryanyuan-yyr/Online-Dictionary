safekeeping
seɪfkiːpɪŋ ˈseɪfˈkiːpɪŋ seɪfˈkipɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT [usu for N] 安全照看;妥善保管 If something is given to you for safekeeping, it is given to you so that you will make sure that it is not harmed or stolen. 安全照看;妥善保管

  • Hampton had been given the bills for safekeeping by a business partner.

    一个生意伙伴曾将账单交给汉普顿代为保管。

SYN protection, keeping, care, charge
