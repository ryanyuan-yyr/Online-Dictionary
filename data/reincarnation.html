reincarnation
riːɪŋkɑːʳneɪʃən ˌriːɪnkɑːˈneɪʃən ˌriɪnkɑrˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural reincarnations
1 N-UNCOUNT 转世（说）；再生（说） If you believe in reincarnation, you believe that you will be reincarnated after you die. 转世（说）；再生（说）

  • Many African tribes believe in reincarnation.

    许多非洲的部落都相信人会转世。

SYN rebirth, metempsychosis, transmigration of souls  
2 N-COUNT (人或动物灵魂的)化身，转世 A reincarnation is a person or animal whose body is believed to contain the spirit of a dead person. (人或动物灵魂的)化身，转世
