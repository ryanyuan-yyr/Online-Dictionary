vacuity
vækjuːɪti væˈkjuəti
N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] (思想的)贫乏,空洞,空虚 If you refer to the vacuity of something or someone, you are critical of them because they lack intelligent thought or ideas. (思想的)贫乏,空洞,空虚 [formal, disapproval]

  • His vacuity was a handicap in these debates.

    在这些辩论中，思想贫乏是他的一个弱点。

  • ...a campaign notable for its intellectual vacuity and personal nastiness.

    以思想空洞和人身攻击闻名的竞选活动
