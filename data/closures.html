closure
kloʊʒəʳ ˈkloʊʒər
Word forms: plural closures
1 N-VAR (企业、工厂等的)永久性关闭，停业 The closure of a place such as a business or factory is the permanent ending of the work or activity there. (企业、工厂等的)永久性关闭，停业

  • ...the closure of the Ravenscraig steelworks. [+ of]

    雷文斯克拉格钢铁厂的关闭

  • ...British Coal's proposed pit closures.

    英国煤炭公司建议关闭的矿井

  • Almost three in four clinics say they face closure by the end of the year.

    几乎有3/4的诊所说年底前要面临歇业。

SYN closing, end, finish, conclusion  
2 N-COUNT (道路或边界的)封闭，封锁 The closure of a road or border is the blocking of it in order to prevent people from using it. (道路或边界的)封闭，封锁
3 N-UNCOUNT 解脱 If someone achieves closure, they succeed in accepting something bad that has happened to them. 解脱 [mainly US]

  • I asked McKeown if the reunion was meant to achieve closure.

    我问麦基翁重聚是不是为了寻求解脱。
