balance of payments
Word forms: plural balances of payments
N-COUNT [usually singular] （一国的）国际收支差额 A country's balance of payments is the difference, over a period of time, between the payments it makes to other countries for imports and the payments it receives from other countries for exports. （一国的）国际收支差额 [business]

  • Britain's balance of payments deficit has improved slightly.

    英国的国际收支逆差已略有改善。
