pleasurable
pleʒərəbəl ˈplɛʒərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 令人愉快的；使人高兴的 Pleasurable experiences or sensations are pleasant and enjoyable. 令人愉快的；使人高兴的

  • The most pleasurable experience of the evening was the wonderful fireworks display.

    这个晚上最让人高兴的就是精彩的焰火表演。

  • He found sailing more pleasurable than skiing.

    他觉得驾驶帆船比滑雪更好玩。

SYN enjoyable, pleasant, diverting, good  
pleasurably

  • They spent six weeks pleasurably together.

    他们愉快地共度了6个星期。
