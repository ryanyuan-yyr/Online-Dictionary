sunk
sʌŋk sʌŋk
1 Sunk is the past participle of sink .
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 玩完的;完蛋的;没救的 If you say that someone is sunk, you mean that they have no hope of avoiding trouble or failure. 玩完的;完蛋的;没救的 [informal]

  • Without him we'd be well and truly sunk.

    没有他我们肯定彻底玩完了。

SYN ruined, lost, finished, done for [informal]
