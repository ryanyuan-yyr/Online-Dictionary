annoyance
ənɔɪəns əˈnɔɪəns
Word forms: plural annoyances
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 气恼；烦恼 Annoyance is the feeling that you get when someone makes you feel fairly angry or impatient. 气恼；烦恼

  • To her annoyance the stranger did not go away.

    让她恼火的是，那个陌生人并没有走。

  • He denied there was any annoyance with the British among other EC members.

    他否认其他欧盟委员会委员对英国人有不满。

SYN irritation, trouble, anger, bother  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 令人烦恼的事物；令人厌烦的事物 An annoyance is something that makes you feel angry or impatient. 令人烦恼的事物；令人厌烦的事物

  • Inconsiderate neighbours can be more than an annoyance.

    不为他人着想的邻居会非常惹人厌。

SYN nuisance, bother, pain [informal] , bind [informal]
