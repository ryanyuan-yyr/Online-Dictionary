retrogressive
retrəgresɪv
ADJ 倒退的;后退的;退化的 If you describe an action or idea as retrogressive, you disapprove of it because it returns to old ideas or beliefs and does not take advantage of recent progress. 倒退的;后退的;退化的 [formal, disapproval]

  • ...the retrogressive policies of the National parties.

    国家党派的倒退政策
