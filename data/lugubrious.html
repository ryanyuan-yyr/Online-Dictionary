lugubrious
luːguːbriəs lʊˈɡuːbrɪəs ləˈgubriəs ləˈgjubriəs
ADJ-GRADED 悲戚的；悲伤的 If you say that someone or something is lugubrious, you mean that they are sad rather than lively or cheerful. 悲戚的；悲伤的 [literary]

  • ...a tall, thin man with a long and lugubrious face.

    一张长脸、面色悲戚的瘦高男子

  • He plays some passages so slowly that they become lugubrious.

    有些段落他演奏得异常徐缓,听上去有些忧伤。

SYN gloomy, serious, sad, dismal  
lugubriously

  • The dog gazed at us lugubriously for a few minutes.

    那只狗阴郁地盯着我们看了几分钟。
