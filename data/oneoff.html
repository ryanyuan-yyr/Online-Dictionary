one-off ˈwʌnˈɔf ˈwʌnˈɑf
Word forms: plural one-offs
1 N-COUNT 一次性事物 You can refer to something as a one-off when it is made or happens only once. 一次性事物 [mainly British]

  • Our survey revealed that these allergies were mainly one-offs.

    我们的调查显示这些过敏多为一次性反应。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 一次性的 A one-off thing is made or happens only once. 一次性的 [mainly British]

  • ...one-off cash benefits.

    一次性的现金补助
