immerse
ɪmɜːʳs ɪˈmɜrs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense immerses, present participle immersing, past tense , past participle immersed
1 VERB 使沉浸(于);使专心(于);使深陷(于) If you immerse yourself in something that you are doing, you become completely involved in it. 使沉浸(于);使专心(于);使深陷(于)

  • Since then I've lived alone and immersed myself in my career. [V pron-refl + in]

    自那以后,我一直一个人生活,全身心投入我的事业当中。

immersed

  • He's really becoming immersed in his work.

    他真的开始专心埋头于工作。

2 VERB [usually passive] 使浸没(于液体中) If something is immersed in a liquid, someone puts it into the liquid so that it is completely covered. 使浸没(于液体中)

  • The electrodes are immersed in liquid. [be VERB-ed + in]

    电极浸没在液体中。

SYN plunge, dip, submerge, sink
