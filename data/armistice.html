armistice
ɑːʳmɪstɪs ˈɑrməstɪs
N-SING 停战，休战（协定） An armistice is an agreement between countries who are at war with one another to stop fighting and to discuss ways of making peace. 停战，休战（协定）

  • Finally, the Bolsheviks signed an armistice with Germany.

    最终布尔什维克和德国签署了停战协议。

SYN truce, peace, ceasefire, suspension of hostilities
