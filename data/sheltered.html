sheltered
ʃeltəʳd
1 ADJ-GRADED 不受风雨侵袭的;有遮蔽物的 A sheltered place is protected from wind and rain. 不受风雨侵袭的;有遮蔽物的

  • ...a shallow-sloping beach next to a sheltered bay.

    有天然屏障的海湾旁的一个缓坡海滩

SYN screened, covered, protected, shielded  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 受庇护的;处处受保护的 If you say that someone has led a sheltered life, you mean that they have been protected from difficult or unpleasant experiences. 受庇护的;处处受保护的

  • Perhaps I've just led a really sheltered life.

    也许我一直过的就是一种呵护备至的生活。

  • My mother, who had a sheltered upbringing, has fantastic memories of childhood.

    我妈妈在呵护下长大，对童年有着非常美好的回忆。

3 ADJ (住所或工作)适于老人(或残疾人)的，福利性的 Sheltered accommodation or work is designed for old or disabled people. It allows them to be independent but also allows them to get help when they need it. (住所或工作)适于老人(或残疾人)的，福利性的

  • For the last few years I have been living in sheltered accommodation.

    最近几年我一直住在福利院里。

  • ...sheltered employment for people with severe disabilities.

    为身体严重残疾的人提供的福利性工作
