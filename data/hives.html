hive
haɪv haɪv
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hives, present participle hiving, past tense , past participle hived
1 N-COUNT 蜂房;蜂箱 A hive is a structure in which bees are kept, which is designed so that the beekeeper can collect the honey that they produce. 蜂房;蜂箱
2 N-COUNT 熙熙攘攘的场所;人群忙碌的地方 If you describe a place as a hive of activity, you approve of the fact that there is a lot of activity there or that people are busy working there. 熙熙攘攘的场所;人群忙碌的地方 [approval]

  • In the morning the house was a hive of activity. [+ of]

    早上，房子里一片繁忙景象。

  • Stuart Tannahill's shed is a veritable hive of photographic creativity.

    斯图尔特·坦纳希尔的小屋是个名副其实的摄影创意中心。

SYN centre, hub, powerhouse [slang]  
3 N-UNCOUNT 荨麻疹 Hives is a condition in which patches of your skin become red and very uncomfortable and itchy. 荨麻疹
Phrasal verbs:
hive off
