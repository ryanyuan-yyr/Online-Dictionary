sheeting
ʃiːtɪŋ ˈʃitɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft n NOUN] (金属、塑料等的)薄片，压片，片材，薄膜 Sheeting is metal, plastic, or other material that is made in the form of sheets. (金属、塑料等的)薄片，压片，片材，薄膜

  • They put plastic sheeting on the insides of our windows.

    他们在我们的窗子里面贴了层塑料薄膜。

2 N-UNCOUNT 被单布;床单布 Sheeting is cloth that is used for making sheets. 被单布;床单布

  • ...six yards of white sheeting.

    六码长的白色床单布
