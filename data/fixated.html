fixated
fɪkseɪtɪd fɪkseɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 过分沉溺的;过分迷恋的 If you accuse someone of being fixated on a particular thing, you mean that they think about it to an extreme and excessive degree. 过分沉溺的;过分迷恋的

  • But by then the administration wasn't paying attention, for top officials were fixated on Kuwait. [+ on/with/by]

    但在那之前，当局并没有特别注意，因为高层官员们都一门心思扑在科威特上。

Fixated is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • ...a pop-fixated music journalist.

    痴迷于流行乐的音乐记者
