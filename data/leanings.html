leaning
liːnɪŋ ˈlinɪŋ
Word forms: plural leanings
N-COUNT [usually plural] 倾向；偏向；爱好 Your particular leanings are the beliefs, ideas, or aims you hold or a tendency you have towards them. 倾向；偏向；爱好

  • During the war his pacifist leanings were not helpful.

    战时他的和平主义思想派不上用场。

  • I always had a leaning towards sport. [+ towards]

    我一向喜欢运动。

SYN tendency, liking for, bias, inclination
