rendezvous
rɒndeɪvuː rɒndeɪvuːz ˈrɑndeɪˌvu ˈrɑndiˌvu ˈrɑndəˌvu
Word forms: present participle rendezvousing, past tense , past participle rendezvoused
The form rendezvous is pronounced /'rɒndeɪvuːz/ when it is the plural of the noun or the third person singular of the verb. rendezvous用作名词复数或动词第三人称单数时读作/'rɒndeɪvuːz/。
1 N-COUNT (常指秘密的)约会，会面，会合 A rendezvous is a meeting, often a secret one, that you have arranged with someone for a particular time and place. (常指秘密的)约会，会面，会合

  • I had almost decided to keep my rendezvous with Tony. [+ with]

    我差不多已经决定如约和托尼见面了。

SYN appointment, meeting, date, engagement  
2 N-COUNT 约会地点;见面地点 A rendezvous is the place where you have arranged to meet someone, often secretly. 约会地点;见面地点

  • Their rendezvous would be the Penta Hotel at Heathrow Airport.

    他们将在希思罗机场的五角饭店碰面。

SYN meeting place, venue, gathering point, place of assignation  
3 V-RECIP 与…约会;和…会面 If you rendezvous with someone or if the two of you rendezvous, you meet them at a time and place that you have arranged. 与…约会;和…会面

  • The plan was to rendezvous with him on Sunday afternoon. [VERB + with]

    计划在周日下午和他会面。

  • She wondered where they were going to rendezvous afterwards. [VERB]

    她想知道之后他们要去哪里约会。

SYN meet, assemble, get together, come together
