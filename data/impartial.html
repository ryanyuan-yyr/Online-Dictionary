impartial
ɪmpɑːʳʃəl ɪmˈpɑrʃəl
ADJ-GRADED 不偏不倚的;公正的;无偏见的 Someone who is impartial is not directly involved in a particular situation, and is therefore able to give a fair opinion or decision about it. 不偏不倚的;公正的;无偏见的

  • As an impartial observer my analysis is supposed to be objective.

    作为中立的观察员,我的分析应该客观。

  • Careers officers offer impartial advice to all pupils.

    就业指导员向所有学生提供公正无私的建议。

SYN neutral, objective, detached, just  
impartiality

  • ...a justice system lacking impartiality by democratic standards.

    依民主标准衡量，缺乏公正性的司法体系

impartially

  • He has vowed to oversee the elections impartially.

    他已宣誓公平公正地监督竞选。
