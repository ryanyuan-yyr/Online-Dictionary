wring out
PHRASAL VERB 把…拧干 When you wring out a wet cloth or a wet piece of clothing, you squeeze the water out of it by twisting it strongly. 把…拧干

  • He turned away to wring out the wet shirt. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他走开去把湿衬衫拧干。

  • Soak a small towel in the liquid, wring it out, then apply to the abdomen. [VERB noun PREP.]

    将一块小毛巾浸在液体中，拧干后敷在腹部。
