match up to
PHRASAL VERB 与…相当;符合…标准;比得上 If someone or something does not match up to what was expected, they are smaller, less impressive, or of poorer quality. 与…相当;符合…标准;比得上

  • Her career never quite matched up to its promise. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    她的事业从来都没有预期的那样好。

  • ...a father's inability to match up to the expectations of his son. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    父亲无法达到儿子的期望

  • The other stories don't quite match up to the high standard of the first. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    其他的报道都达不到第一篇报道那么高的水平。

SYN live up to, satisfy, fulfil, come up to
