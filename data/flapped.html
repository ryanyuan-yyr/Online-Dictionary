flap
flæp flæp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense flaps, present participle flapping, past tense , past participle flapped
1 V-ERG (使)飘动;摆动;抖搂 If something such as a piece of cloth or paper flaps or if you flap it, it moves quickly up and down or from side to side. (使)飘动;摆动;抖搂

  • Grey sheets flapped on the clothes line. [VERB]

    灰色的床单在晾衣绳上飘来摆去的。

  • They would flap bath towels from their balconies as they chatted. [VERB noun]

    他们聊天时会在阳台上抖搂浴巾。

SYN flutter, wave, swing, swish  
2 V-ERG 振(翅);挥(翅);(翅膀)拍打，抖动 If a bird or insect flaps its wings or if its wings flap, the wings move quickly up and down. 振(翅);挥(翅);(翅膀)拍打，抖动

  • The bird flapped its wings furiously. [VERB noun]

    鸟儿使劲地扑打着翅膀。

  • A pigeon emerges, wings flapping noisily, from the tower. [VERB]

    一只鸽子从塔里钻出来，扑扑地拍打着翅膀。

SYN beat, wave, thrash, flutter  
3 VERB 挥动，舞动(手臂) If you flap your arms, you move them quickly up and down as if they were the wings of a bird. 挥动，舞动(手臂)

  • ...a kid running and flapping her arms. [VERB noun]

    边跑边挥舞着手臂的小孩子

4 N-COUNT (布、皮等)扁平下垂物,垂下的部分 A flap of cloth or skin, for example, is a flat piece of it that can move freely up and down or from side to side because it is held or attached by only one edge. (布、皮等)扁平下垂物,垂下的部分

  • He drew back the tent flap and strode out into the blizzard.

    他拉开帐篷的门帘，大步走进暴风雪中。

  • ...a loose flap of skin.

    耷拉着快要掉下的一块皮

SYN cover, covering, tail, fold  
5 N-COUNT (飞机的)襟翼，副翼，阻力板 A flap on the wing of an aircraft is an area along the edge of the wing that can be raised or lowered to control the movement of the aircraft. (飞机的)襟翼，副翼，阻力板

  • ...the sudden slowing as the flaps were lowered.

    襟翼放下时的突然减速

6 N-COUNT (鸟翼、布条、纸片的)拍打声，拍动，拍打 A flap is a sudden noise or movement made by a bird's wing or by a piece of paper or cloth when it flaps. (鸟翼、布条、纸片的)拍打声，拍动，拍打

  • Nothing to be heard but the soft flap of a silk banner.

    万籁俱寂，只有一面绸制旗子轻轻飘动的响声。

7 N-SING [a NOUN, oft in a N] 激动;焦虑;惊慌 Someone who is in a flap is in a state of great excitement, worry, or panic. 激动;焦虑;惊慌 [informal]

  • Why did people get in a flap over nuclear energy?.

    为什么人们对核能惶恐不安呢？

  • Wherever he goes there's always a flap.

    无论他到哪里，都会引起一阵骚动。

SYN panic, state [informal] , agitation, commotion
