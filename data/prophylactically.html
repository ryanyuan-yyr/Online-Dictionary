prophylactic
prɒfɪlæktɪk ˌproʊfəˈlæktɪk
Word forms: plural prophylactics
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 预防（性）的 Prophylactic means concerned with preventing disease. 预防（性）的 [medicine]

  • Vaccination and other prophylactic measures can be carried out.

    可以采取疫苗接种和其他预防措施。

SYN preventative, protective, preventive, precautionary  
2 N-COUNT 预防剂；预防器 A prophylactic is a substance or device used for preventing disease. 预防剂；预防器 [medicine]

  • The region began to use quinine successfully as a prophylactic.

    该地区开始成功地用奎宁作为预防剂。

SYN preventative measure, safeguard, precaution, safety measure  
3 N-COUNT 避孕套 A prophylactic is a condom. 避孕套 [formal]

  • The prospect of teachers passing out prophylactics to their pupils has infuriated some parents.

    想到老师将分发避孕套给学生使一些家长愤慨不已。
