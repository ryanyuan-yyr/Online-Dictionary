acoustic
əkuːstɪk əˈkustɪk
Word forms: plural acoustics
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （吉他等乐器）原声的，自然声的，不用电传音的 An acoustic guitar or other instrument is one whose sound is produced without any electrical equipment. （吉他等乐器）原声的，自然声的，不用电传音的
acoustically

  • ...acoustically based music.

    以原声为基调的音乐

2 N-COUNT （空间的）传声效果，音响效果 If you refer to the acoustics or the acoustic of a space, you are referring to the structural features which determine how well you can hear music or speech in it. （空间的）传声效果，音响效果

  • In this performance, Rattle had the acoustic of the Symphony Hall on his side.

    这场演出中，拉特尔有交响乐大厅出色的音响效果做他的后盾。

acoustically

  • The church is acoustically perfect.

    这座教堂收音效果极佳。

3 N-UNCOUNT 声学 Acoustics is the scientific study of sound. 声学
4 ADJ 声音的;听觉的 Acoustic means relating to sound or hearing. 声音的;听觉的

  • ...acoustic signals.

    声音信号
