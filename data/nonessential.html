non-essential
Word forms: plural non-essentials
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 nonessential
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 非必要的;不重要的 Non-essential means not absolutely necessary. 非必要的;不重要的

  • The crisis has led to the closure of a number of non-essential government services.

    危机已导致了许多可有可无的政府机构的关闭。

  • ...non-essential goods.

    非必要商品

SYN unnecessary, peripheral, unimportant, superfluous  
2 N-PLURAL 不重要的东西;可有可无的事物 Non-essentials are things that are not absolutely necessary. 不重要的东西;可有可无的事物

  • In a recession, consumers could be expected to cut down on non-essentials like toys.

    经济不景气时，消费者可能会减少在类似玩具等非必需品上的支出。
