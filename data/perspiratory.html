perspiration
pɜːʳspɪreɪʃən ˌpɜrspəˈreɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 汗；汗珠 Perspiration is the liquid which comes out on the surface of your skin when you are hot or frightened. 汗；汗珠 [formal]

  • His hands were wet with perspiration.

    他双手被汗水浸湿了。

  • ...night sweats and excessive perspiration.

    夜间盗汗和出汗过多

SYN sweat, moisture, wetness, exudation
