convalesce
kɒnvəles ˌkɑnvəˈlɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense convalesces, present participle convalescing, past tense , past participle convalesced
VERB 逐渐康复;病后疗养 If you are convalescing, you are resting and getting your health back after an illness or operation. 逐渐康复;病后疗养 [formal]

  • After two weeks, I was allowed home, where I convalesced for three months. [VERB]

    两周之后，我获准回家，休养了3个月之后逐渐康复。

  • ...those convalescing from illness or surgery. [VERB + from]

    那些从疾病和手术中康复的人

SYN recover, rest, rally, rehabilitate
