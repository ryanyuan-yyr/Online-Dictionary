habeas corpus
heɪbiəs kɔːʳpəs ˈheɪbɪəs ˈkɔːpəs ˌheɪbiəs ˈkɔrpəs
N-UNCOUNT 人身保护令（据此令当事人可请求先由法庭裁决其受拘禁是否合法） Habeas corpus is a law that states that a person cannot be kept in prison unless they have first been brought before a court of law, which decides whether it is legal for them to be kept in prison. 人身保护令（据此令当事人可请求先由法庭裁决其受拘禁是否合法）
