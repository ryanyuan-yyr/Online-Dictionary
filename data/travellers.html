traveller
trævələʳ
Word forms: plural travellers
in AM, use 美国英语用 traveler
1 N-COUNT 旅行者;旅客 A traveller is a person who is making a journey or a person who travels a lot. 旅行者;旅客

  • Many air travellers suffer puffy ankles and feet during long flights.

    很多坐飞机出行的旅客在长途飞行中脚踝和脚会浮肿。

SYN voyager, tourist, passenger, journeyer  
2 N-COUNT 流浪者;漂泊者;居无定所者 A traveller is a person who travels from place to place, often living in a van or other vehicle, rather than living in one place. 流浪者;漂泊者;居无定所者 [British]
