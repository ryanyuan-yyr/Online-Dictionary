despise
dɪspaɪz dɪˈspaɪz dɪˈspaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense despises, present participle despising, past tense , past participle despised
VERB 鄙视;藐视;看不起 If you despise something or someone, you dislike them and have a very low opinion of them. 鄙视;藐视;看不起

  • I can never, ever forgive him. I despise him. [VERB noun]

    我永远不会原谅他。我鄙视他。

  • She secretly despises his work. [VERB noun]

    她暗地里瞧不起他的工作。

  • How I despised myself for my cowardice! [V pron-refl for n/-ing]

    我是多么鄙视自己的怯懦啊！

SYN look down on, loathe, scorn, disdain
