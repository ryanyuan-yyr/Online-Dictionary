invective
ɪnvektɪv ɪnˈvɛktɪv
N-UNCOUNT 咒骂;痛骂 Invective is rude and unpleasant things that people shout at people they hate or are angry with. 咒骂;痛骂 [formal]

  • A woman had hurled racist invective at the family.

    一个女人对那家人大骂有种族歧视的脏话。

  • Crowley maintained a stream of invective and abuse against Waite.

    克劳利连珠炮似的对韦特骂个不停。

SYN abuse, censure, tirade, reproach
