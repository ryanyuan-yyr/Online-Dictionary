call back
PHRASAL VERB 给…回电话 If you call someone back, you telephone them again or in return for a telephone call that they have made to you. 给…回电话 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • If we're not around she'll take a message and we'll call you back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果我们不在,她会带个话儿,我们给您打过去。

  • If you want further advice, you can call back and speak to the same adviser. [VERB PREP.]

    您若需要更多的建议,可以回拨电话向同一位顾问请教。
