high-profile ˌhaɪˈprəʊfaɪl ˈhaɪˈproʊˌfaɪl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （人或事件）引人注目的，备受关注的 A high-profile person or a high-profile event attracts a lot of attention or publicity. （人或事件）引人注目的，备受关注的

  • ...one of football's high profile chairmen.

    备受关注的足球俱乐部主席之一

    阿拉法特先生所受到的隆重接待

SYN famous, prominent, eminent, celebrated
