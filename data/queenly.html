queenly
kwiːnli ˈkwinli
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 女王似的;王后似的;高贵的 You use queenly to describe a woman's appearance or behaviour if she looks very dignified or behaves as if she is very important. 女王似的;王后似的;高贵的

  • She was a queenly, organizing type.

    她属于那种像女王般把一切都组织得井然有序的类型。

SYN majestic, royal, grand, imperial
