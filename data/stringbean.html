string bean
Word forms: plural string beans
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 青刀豆;四季豆 String beans are long, very narrow green vegetables consisting of the cases that contain the seeds of a climbing plant. 青刀豆;四季豆 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 French beans
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 红花菜豆 String beans are vegetables similar to French beans, but thicker. 红花菜豆 [British]
