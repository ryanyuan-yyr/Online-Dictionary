unappetizing
ʌnæpɪtaɪzɪŋ
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 unappetising
ADJ-GRADED 引不起食欲的；倒胃口的 If you describe food as unappetizing, you think it will be unpleasant to eat because of its appearance. 引不起食欲的；倒胃口的

  • ...cold and unappetizing chicken.

    让人没有一点食欲的冷冰冰的鸡肉

SYN unpalatable, disgusting, unsavoury, unpleasant
