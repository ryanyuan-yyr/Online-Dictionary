impairment
ɪmpeəʳmənt
Word forms: plural impairments
N-VAR 损害；损伤；障碍 If someone has an impairment, they have a condition which prevents their eyes, ears, or brain from working properly. 损害；损伤；障碍

  • He has a visual impairment in the right eye.

    他右眼视力有损伤。

SYN disability, disorder, defect, complaint
