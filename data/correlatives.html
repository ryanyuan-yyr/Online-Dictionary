correlative
kɒrelətɪv kəˈrɛlətɪv
Word forms: plural correlatives
N-COUNT 密切相关;相互关联 If one thing is a correlative of another, the first thing is caused by the second thing, or occurs together with it. 密切相关;相互关联 [formal]

  • Man has rights only in so far as they are a correlative of duty. [+ of]

    只有承担义务才能享受权利。
