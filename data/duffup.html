duff up
PHRASAL VERB 痛击;猛打 duff someone up means to hit them many times and injure them. 痛击;猛打 [British, informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She began telling us about some fight on a bus in which our kids had duffed up the bus conductor. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    她开始给我们讲在公共汽车上我们的孩子们把售票员痛扁一顿。

SYN beat (up), attack, assault, batter
