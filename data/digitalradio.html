digital radio
Word forms: plural digital radios
1 N-UNCOUNT 数字无线电 Digital radio is radio in which the signals are transmitted in digital form and decoded by the radio receiver. 数字无线电
2 N-COUNT 数字收音机 A digital radio is a radio that can receive digital signals. 数字收音机

  • Manufacturers are working on a new generation of cheaper digital radios.

    制造商正在研制价格更便宜的新一代数字收音机。
