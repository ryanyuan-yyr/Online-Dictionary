asthmatic
æsmætɪk æsˈmætɪk æz- æzˈmætɪk
Word forms: plural asthmatics
1 N-COUNT 气喘病患者；哮喘病患者 People who suffer from asthma are sometimes referred to as asthmatics . 气喘病患者；哮喘病患者

  • I have been an asthmatic from childhood and was never able to play any sports.

    我从小就患有气喘病，参加不了任何体育活动。

Asthmatic is also an adjective.患气喘病的

  • One child in ten is asthmatic.

    每十个孩子里就有一人患有气喘病。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 哮喘（性）的;气喘引起的 Asthmatic means relating to asthma. 哮喘（性）的;气喘引起的

  • ...asthmatic breathing.

    气喘
