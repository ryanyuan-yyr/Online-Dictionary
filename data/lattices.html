lattice
lætɪs ˈlætɪs
Word forms: plural lattices
N-COUNT [usually singular] 格子木架；斜条格构 A lattice is a pattern or structure made of strips of wood or another material which cross over each other diagonally leaving holes in between. 格子木架；斜条格构

  • We were crawling along the narrow steel lattice of the bridge. [+ of]

    我们沿着桥上狭窄的钢制格架往前爬。

SYN grid, network, web, grating
