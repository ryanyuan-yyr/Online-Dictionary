tauten
tɔːtən ˈtɔtən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tautens, present participle tautening, past tense , past participle tautened
V-ERG 绷紧;拉紧;(使)(身体某部位)变紧 If a part of your body tautens or if you tauten it, it becomes stiff or firm. 绷紧;拉紧;(使)(身体某部位)变紧

  • Her whole body tautened violently. [VERB]

    她全身紧绷得厉害。
