tenancy
tenənsi ˈtɛnənsi
Word forms: plural tenancies
N-VAR (土地、房屋的)租赁，租用 Tenancy is the use that you have of land or property belonging to someone else, for which you pay rent. (土地、房屋的)租赁，租用

  • His father took over the tenancy of the farm 40 years ago. [+ of]

    他父亲 40 年前租下了这个农场。

  • Tenants should check their tenancy agreements closely.

    租户应该认真核对租赁合同。
