1 N-PLURAL 统计数字(或资料) Stats are facts which are obtained from analysing information expressed in numbers. Stats is an abbreviation for 'statistics'. 统计数字(或资料) [informal]

  • ...a fall in April's retail sales stats.

    4月零售总额的下降

2 N-UNCOUNT 统计学 Stats is a branch of mathematics concerned with the study of information that is expressed in numbers. 统计学 [informal]
