incorruptible
ɪnkərʌptɪbəl ˌɪnkəˈrʌptəbəl
ADJ 不动摇的;不受腐蚀的 If you describe someone as incorruptible, you approve of the fact that they cannot be persuaded or paid to do things that they should not do. 不动摇的;不受腐蚀的 [approval]

  • He was a sound businessman, totally reliable and incorruptible.

    他是个正直的商人,完全可靠,不会被收买。

SYN honest, straight, upright, honourable
