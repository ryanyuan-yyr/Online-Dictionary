carer
keərəʳ ˈkɛərə
Word forms: plural carers
N-COUNT (照料残疾人、病人、年幼者等的)护理人,照管人 A carer is someone who is responsible for looking after another person, for example, a person who is disabled, ill, or very young. (照料残疾人、病人、年幼者等的)护理人,照管人

  • We are looking for foster carers who can care for a child for a few days.

    我们在找寄养家庭,帮忙照看一个孩子几天。

  • Women are more likely than men to be carers of elderly dependent relatives. [+ of]

    和男人相比,女人更有可能照看无法独立生活的年老亲戚。
