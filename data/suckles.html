suckle
sʌkəl ˈsʌkəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense suckles, present participle suckling, past tense , past participle suckled
1 VERB 给(婴儿)喂奶;哺乳 When a mother suckles her baby, she feeds it by letting it suck milk from her breast. 给(婴儿)喂奶;哺乳 [old-fashioned]

  • A young woman suckling a baby is one of life's most natural and delightful scenes. [VERB noun]

    年轻女性哺育婴儿是生命中最自然、最令人愉悦的一幕。

2 VERB (婴儿)吃奶，吸奶 When a baby suckles, it sucks milk from its mother's breast. (婴儿)吃奶，吸奶 [formal]

  • As the baby suckles, a further supply of milk is generated. [VERB]

    婴儿吮吸时，会有更多的奶水流出来。
