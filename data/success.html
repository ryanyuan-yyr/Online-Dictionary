success
səkses səkˈsɛs
Word forms: plural successes
1 N-UNCOUNT 成功;胜利;成就 Success is the achievement of something that you have been trying to do. 成功;胜利;成就

  • It's important for the long-term success of any diet that you vary your meals.

    如果想要成功做到长期节食，很重要的一点是要膳食多样化。

  • ...the success of European business in building a stronger partnership between management and workers. [+ of]

    欧洲企业在劳资双方之间建立更牢固的伙伴关系上所取得的成功

SYN victory, triumph, positive result, favourable outcome  
2 N-UNCOUNT (在某一领域的)成功，成名 Success is the achievement of a high position in a particular field, for example in business or politics. (在某一领域的)成功，成名

  • Nearly all of the young people interviewed believed that work was the key to success.

    几乎所有被采访的年轻人都认为工作是成功的关键。

SYN prosperity, riches, fortune, luck  
3 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] (某事所取得的)成功，良好表现 The success of something is the fact that it works in a satisfactory way or has the result that is intended. (某事所取得的)成功，良好表现

  • Most of the cast was amazed by the play's success.

    大多数演员都为该剧大获成功而感到惊奇。

  • Enthused by the success of the first exhibition, its organisers are hoping to repeat the experience.

    由于首展告捷，组织者大受鼓舞，希望能再创佳绩。

4 N-COUNT 成功的人(或事物) Someone or something that is a success achieves a high position, makes a lot of money, or is admired a great deal. 成功的人(或事物)

  • The jewellery was a great success.

    这些珠宝大获赞誉。

  • We hope it will be a commercial success.

    我们希望它能取得商业上的成功。

SYN hit [informal] , winner, smash [informal] , triumph
