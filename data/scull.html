scull
skʌl skʌl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural sculls, present participle sculling, past tense , past participle sculled
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (单人划的)短桨 Sculls are small oars which are held by one person and used to move a boat through water. (单人划的)短桨
2 N-COUNT 单人双桨赛艇 A scull is a small light racing boat which is rowed with two sculls. 单人双桨赛艇
3 VERB 用短桨划(船) To scull a boat means to row it using sculls. 用短桨划(船) [Also VERB]
