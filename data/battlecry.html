battle cry
(also battle-cry)Word forms: plural battle cries
1 N-COUNT （用于鼓励人们支持某项事业或运动的）口号，战斗口号 A battle cry is a phrase that is used to encourage people to support a particular cause or campaign. （用于鼓励人们支持某项事业或运动的）口号，战斗口号

  • Their battle-cry will be: 'Sign this petition before they sign away your country.'

    他们的口号将是“如果不想看到自己的国家被签字出卖，就请在这份请愿书上签字吧。”

2 N-COUNT （战斗时的）呐喊，喊杀声 A battle cry is a shout that soldiers give as they go into battle. （战斗时的）呐喊，喊杀声
