horror-struck
ˈhɔrˌər strʌk
ADJ 惊恐万分的;无比震惊的 If you describe someone as horror-struck or horror-stricken, you mean that they feel very great horror at something that has happened. 惊恐万分的;无比震惊的

  • 'What is the matter with Signora Anna?' he whispered, horror-struck at her vacant face.

    “安娜夫人怎么啦？”他惊恐万分地看着她那失神的脸小声问道。

SYN horrified, shocked, appalled, petrified
