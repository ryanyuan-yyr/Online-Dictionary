conception
kənsepʃən kənˈsɛpʃən
Word forms: plural conceptions
1 N-VAR 概念;观念;想法 A conception of something is an idea that you have of it in your mind. 概念;观念;想法

  • My conception of a garden was based on gardens I had visited in England. [+ of]

    我对花园的概念是基于我在英格兰观赏过的花园。

  • I see him as someone with not the slightest conception of teamwork. [+ of]

    我认为他丝毫没有团队意识。

2 N-UNCOUNT 构想;构思;设想 Conception is the forming of an idea for something in your mind. 构想;构思;设想

  • The symphony is admirable in conception.

    这支交响乐的构思好极了。

  • The other fundamental consideration in the conception of a plan is function.

    在构思设计图时要考虑的另一个基本因素是功能。

SYN idea, plan, design, image  
3 N-VAR 受孕;怀孕 Conception is the process in which the egg in a woman is fertilized and she becomes pregnant. 受孕;怀孕

  • Six weeks after conception your baby is the size of your little fingernail.

    怀孕6个星期后，胎儿的大小相当于小拇指的指甲。

  • Teenage conceptions have risen steadily in the last ten years.

    过去10年中，少女怀孕的现象不断增多。

SYN impregnation, insemination, fertilization, germination
