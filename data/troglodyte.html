troglodyte
trɒglədaɪt ˈtrɑgləˌdaɪt
Word forms: plural troglodytes
1 N-COUNT 穴居人 A troglodyte is someone who lives in a cave. 穴居人 [formal]
2 N-COUNT 井底之蛙;见识短浅的人 If you refer to someone as a troglodyte, you mean that they are unsophisticated and do not know very much about anything. 井底之蛙;见识短浅的人 [disapproval]

  • He dismissed advocates of a completely free market as economic troglodytes with no concern for the social consequences.

    他认为那些鼓吹完全自由市场经济的人对经济只是一知半解，完全没有顾及到可能产生的社会后果。
