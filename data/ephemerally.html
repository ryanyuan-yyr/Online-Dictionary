ephemeral
ɪfemərəl ɛˈfɛmərəl ɪˈfɛmərəl iˈfɛmərəl
ADJ-GRADED 短暂的;瞬间的 If you describe something as ephemeral, you mean that it lasts only for a very short time. 短暂的;瞬间的 [formal]

  • He talked about the country's ephemeral unity being shattered by the defeat.

    他谈到战败使得该国短暂的统一彻底破灭。

  • These paintings are in some ways a reminder that earthly pleasures are ephemeral.

    这些画在某种意义上提醒人们世俗的享乐是短暂的。

SYN transient, short, passing, brief
