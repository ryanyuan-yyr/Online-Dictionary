chip shop
tʃɪp ʃɒp
Word forms: plural chip shops
N-COUNT 薯条店，油炸食品外卖店(出售炸鱼薯条、炸鸡、香肠、肉饼等热食) A chip shop is a shop which sells hot food such as fish and chips, fried chicken, sausages, and meat pies. The food is cooked in the shop and people take it away to eat at home or in the street. 薯条店，油炸食品外卖店(出售炸鱼薯条、炸鸡、香肠、肉饼等热食) [British]
