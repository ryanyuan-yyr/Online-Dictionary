diplomacy
dɪploʊməsi dəˈploʊməsi
1 N-UNCOUNT 外交 Diplomacy is the activity or profession of managing relations between the governments of different countries. 外交

  • Today's Security Council resolution will be a significant success for American diplomacy.

    今天的安理会决议将是美国外交取得的一个重大成功。

  • The talks have now gone into a stage of quiet diplomacy.

    会谈现在已经进入了“温和外交”阶段。

SYN statesmanship, foreign affairs, international relations, statecraft  
2See also:shuttle diplomacy 
3 N-UNCOUNT (处理人际关系等的)手腕，策略，手段 Diplomacy is the skill of being careful to say or do things which will not offend people. (处理人际关系等的)手腕，策略，手段

  • He stormed off in a fury, and it took all Minnelli's powers of diplomacy to get him to return.

    他愤然离去，米内利使出了浑身解数才把他劝了回来。

SYN tact, skill, sensitivity, craft
