aphorism
æfərɪzəm ˈæfəˌrɪzəm
Word forms: plural aphorisms
N-COUNT 格言；警句 An aphorism is a short witty sentence which expresses a general truth or comment. 格言；警句 [formal]

  • 'What if they gave a war and nobody came?' was one of his generation's favored aphorisms.

    “如果他们宣战却无人应战该怎么办？”是他那一代人钟爱的格言。

SYN saying, maxim, gnome, adage
