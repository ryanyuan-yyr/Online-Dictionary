megalomaniac
megələmeɪniæk
Word forms: plural megalomaniacs
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 有自大狂的人;妄自尊大的人 If you describe someone as a megalomaniac, you are criticizing them because they enjoy being powerful, or because they believe that they are more powerful or important than they really are. 有自大狂的人;妄自尊大的人 [disapproval]
