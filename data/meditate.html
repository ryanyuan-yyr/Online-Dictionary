meditate
medɪteɪt ˈmɛdəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense meditates, present participle meditating, past tense , past participle meditated
1 VERB 沉思;深思;仔细考虑 If you meditate on something, you think about it very carefully and deeply for a long time. 沉思;深思;仔细考虑

  • He meditated on the problem. [VERB on noun]

    他仔细斟酌着这个问题。

  • On the day her son began school, she meditated on the uncertainties of his future. [VERB + on]

    儿子开始上学的那一天，她就在考虑他未来的种种不确定因素了。

2 VERB （宗教中）冥想，默想，打坐 If you meditate you remain in a silent and calm state for a period of time, as part of a religious training or so that you are more able to deal with the problems and difficulties of everyday life. （宗教中）冥想，默想，打坐

  • I was meditating, and reached a higher state of consciousness. [VERB]

    我在冥想，并进入了一个更高的意识境界。

SYN reflect, think, consider, contemplate
