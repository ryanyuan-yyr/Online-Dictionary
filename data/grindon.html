grind on
PHRASAL VERB 令人厌烦地继续下去 If you say that something grinds on, you disapprove of the fact that it continues to happen in the same way for a long time. 令人厌烦地继续下去 [disapproval]

  • Civil war in the Sudan has been grinding on for nine years. [VERB PREP.]

    苏丹内战已经持续了9年之久。

SYN drag on, last, persist, keep going
