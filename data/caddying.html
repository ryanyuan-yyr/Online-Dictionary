caddie
kædi ˈkædi
(also caddy)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense caddies, present participle caddying, past tense , past participle caddied
1 N-COUNT (为高尔夫球手背球棒等器具的)球童 In golf, a caddie is a person who carries golf clubs and other equipment for a player. (为高尔夫球手背球棒等器具的)球童
2 VERB (为高尔夫球手)当球童 If you caddie for a golfer, you act as their caddie. (为高尔夫球手)当球童 [Also VERB]

  • Lili caddied for her son.

    莉莉给儿子当球童。
