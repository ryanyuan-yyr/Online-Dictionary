agitation
ædʒɪteɪʃən ˌædʒəˈteɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 焦虑;烦恼;不安 If someone is in a state of agitation, they are very worried or upset, and show this in their behaviour, movements, or voice. 焦虑;烦恼;不安

  • Danny returned to Father's house in a state of intense agitation.

    丹尼回到神父的房子时显得极为焦虑不安。

  • Diane lit a cigarette, trying to mask her agitation.

    黛安娜点燃一根香烟，试图掩饰她的焦躁不安。
