elevation
elɪveɪʃən ˌɛləˈveɪʃən
Word forms: plural elevations
1 N-COUNT (建筑物)立面，立面图，立视图 In architecture, an elevation is the front, back, or side of a building, or a drawing of one of these. (建筑物)立面，立面图，立视图 [technical]

  • ...the addition of two-storey wings on the north and south elevations.

    在南北向的立视图上各增加两层配楼

SYN side, back, face, front  
2 N-COUNT 海拔 The elevation of a place is its height above sea level. 海拔

  • We're probably at an elevation of about 13,000 feet above sea level. [+ of]

    我们可能在大约海拔13,000英尺的高度。

SYN altitude, height  
3 N-COUNT 高地；高处 An elevation is a piece of ground that is higher than the area around it. 高地；高处
4 See also: elevate
