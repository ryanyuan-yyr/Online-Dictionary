playhouse
pleɪhaʊs ˈpleɪˌhaʊs
Word forms: plural playhouses
1 N-COUNT 剧场；戏院 A playhouse is a theatre. 剧场；戏院

  • The Theatre Royal is one of the oldest playhouses in Britain.

    皇家剧院是英国最古老的剧场之一。

2 N-COUNT 儿童游戏房；玩具房 A playhouse is a small house made for children to play in. 儿童游戏房；玩具房

  • My father built me a playhouse.

    父亲给我建了一个儿童游戏房。
