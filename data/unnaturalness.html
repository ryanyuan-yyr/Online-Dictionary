unnatural
ʌnnætʃərəl ʌnˈnætʃərəl ʌnˈnætʃərəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 不自然的；诡异的；反常的 If you describe something as unnatural, you mean that it is strange and often frightening, because it is different from what you normally expect. 不自然的；诡异的；反常的

  • The aircraft rose with unnatural speed on take-off.

    那架飞机以反常的速度起飞升空。

  • The altered landscape looks unnatural and weird.

    改造后的景观看起来很不自然，极其怪异。

SYN abnormal, odd, strange, unusual  
unnaturally

  • The house was unnaturally silent.

    房子里异常安静。

  • ...unnaturally cold conditions.

    异常寒冷的环境

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不自然的；做作的 Behaviour that is unnatural seems artificial and not normal or genuine. 不自然的；做作的

  • She gave him a bright, determined smile which seemed unnatural.

    她对他开朗、坚定地一笑，但笑得有点不自然。

SYN false, forced, artificial, studied  
unnaturally

  • Try to avoid shouting or speaking unnaturally.

    尽量不要高声喧哗或是不自然地讲话。
