alphabet

Word forms: plural alphabets
N-COUNT （一种语言的）全部字母，字母表 An alphabet is a set of letters usually presented in a fixed order which is used for writing the words of a particular language or group of languages. （一种语言的）全部字母，字母表

  • The modern Russian alphabet has 31 letters.

    现代俄语字母表有31个字母。

    两岁半时他已认识全部字母。

SYN letters, script, writing system, syllabary
