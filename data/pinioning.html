pinion
pɪnjən ˈpɪnjən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pinions, present participle pinioning, past tense , past participle pinioned
VERB （尤指通过抓住或绑住双臂）使动弹不得 If you are pinioned, someone prevents you from moving or escaping, especially by holding or tying your arms. （尤指通过抓住或绑住双臂）使动弹不得 [Also VERB n]

  • At nine the next morning Bentley was pinioned, hooded and hanged. [be VERB-ed]

    次日上午9点，本特里被捆住双臂、戴上头罩，然后绞死了。

SYN immobilize, tie, bind, chain
