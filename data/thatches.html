thatch
θætʃ θætʃ
Word forms: plural thatches
1 N-COUNT 茅草屋顶;稻草屋顶;芦苇屋顶 A thatch or a thatch roof is a roof made from straw or reeds. 茅草屋顶;稻草屋顶;芦苇屋顶

  • They would live in a small house with a green door and a new thatch.

    他们将住在一所新苫顶的绿门小房子里。

2 N-UNCOUNT (用来盖屋顶的)茅草,稻草，芦苇 Thatch is straw or reeds used to make a roof. (用来盖屋顶的)茅草,稻草，芦苇

  • Thatch is naturally warm in winter and cool in summer.

    茅草料天生冬暖夏凉。

3 N-SING (尤指浓密蓬乱的)头发 You can refer to someone's hair as their thatch of hair, especially when it is very thick and untidy. (尤指浓密蓬乱的)头发

  • Teddy ran thick fingers through his unruly thatch of hair. [+ of]

    特迪用自己的粗手指头捋了一下浓密蓬乱的头发。
