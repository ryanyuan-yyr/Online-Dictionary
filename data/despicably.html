despicable
dɪspɪkəbəl despɪk- dɪˈspɪkəbəl ˈdɛspɪkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可鄙的;卑鄙的 If you say that a person or action is despicable, you are emphasizing that they are extremely nasty, cruel, or evil. 可鄙的;卑鄙的 [emphasis]

  • The Minister said the bombing was a despicable crime.

    部长说这次爆炸是一起卑劣的罪行。

SYN contemptible, mean, low, base
