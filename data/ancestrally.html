ancestral
ænsestrəl ænˈsɛstrəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 祖先的；祖传的 You use ancestral to refer to a person's family in former times, especially when the family is important and has property or land which they have had for a long time. 祖先的；祖传的

  • ...the family's ancestral home in southern Germany.

    这个家族在德国南部的祖居

  • ...the ancestral portraits in the hallway.

    走廊里祖先的画像

SYN inherited, hereditary, patriarchal, antecedent
