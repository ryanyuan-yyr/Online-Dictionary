nosh
nɒʃ nɑʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense noshes, present participle noshing, past tense , past participle noshed
1 N-UNCOUNT 食品;食物 Food can be referred to as nosh . 食品;食物 [British, informal]

  • Fancy some nosh?

    来点儿吃的？

2 N-SING 小吃;点心 A nosh is a snack or light meal. 小吃;点心 [US, informal]
3 VERB 吃;进餐 If you nosh, you eat. 吃;进餐 [informal]

  • She sprinkled pepper on my grub, watching me nosh. [VERB]

    她在我的食物上撒了点胡椒粉，看着我吃。

  • ...a big-bellied bird noshing some heather. [VERB noun]

    正在吃石南花的、肚子圆鼓鼓的小鸟

SYN eat, consume, scoff [slang] , devour
