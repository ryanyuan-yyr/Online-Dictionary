phalanx
fælæŋks ˈfeɪˌlæŋks ˈfælˌæŋks
Word forms: plural phalanxes, plural phalanges/fəlændʒiːz/
1 N-COUNT 方阵 A phalanx is a group of soldiers or police who are standing or marching close together ready to fight. 方阵 [formal]
2 N-COUNT 密集的人群 A phalanx of people is a large group who are brought together for a particular purpose. 密集的人群 [formal]

  • ...a phalanx of waiters. [+ of]
