incontrovertible
ɪnkɒntrəvɜːʳtɪbəl ɪnˌkɑntrəˈvɜrtəbəl
ADJ 无可辩驳的;不容置疑的;无可否认的 Incontrovertible evidence or facts are absolutely certain and cannot be shown to be wrong. 无可辩驳的;不容置疑的;无可否认的

  • We have incontrovertible evidence of what took place.

    我们有确凿无疑的证据证明到底发生了什么。

SYN indisputable, sure, certain, established  
incontrovertibly

  • No solution is incontrovertibly right.

    没有任何解决方案是百分之百正确的。
