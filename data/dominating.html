dominating
dɒmɪneɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 个性强势的;左右他人的 A dominating person has a very strong personality and influences the people around them. 个性强势的;左右他人的

  • She certainly was a dominating figure in politics.

    她在政坛上无疑是一个强势人物。

  • The boy was brought up by his mother, who was a dominating influence in his life.

    那个男孩被他的母亲带大，母亲对他一生起着决定性的影响。
