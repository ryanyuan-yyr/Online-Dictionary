freshen
freʃən ˈfrɛʃən ˈfrɛʃən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense freshens, present participle freshening, past tense , past participle freshened
1 VERB (风)增强，变冷 If the wind freshens, it becomes stronger and colder. (风)增强，变冷

  • The wind had freshened. [VERB]

    风增强变冷了。

SYN cool off, become stronger, become colder, become chilly  
2 PHRASAL VERB 使洁净;使亮丽;使清新 If you freshen something up, you make it clean and pleasant in appearance or smell. 使洁净;使亮丽;使清新

  • A thorough brushing helps to freshen up your mouth.

    彻底刷牙有助于口气清新。

  • My room needed a lick of paint to freshen it up.

    我的房间只要刷一点油漆就能焕然一新。

3 PHRASAL VERB 梳洗 If you freshen up, you wash your hands and face and make yourself look neat and tidy. 梳洗

  • After Martine had freshened up, they went for a long walk.

    马蒂娜梳洗完毕后，他们出去散了一个长步。
