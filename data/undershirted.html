undershirt
ʌndəʳʃɜːʳt ˈʌndəˌʃɜːt ˈʌndərˌʃɜrt
Word forms: plural undershirts
N-COUNT 贴身内衣；汗衫；背心 An undershirt is a piece of clothing that you wear on the top half of your body next to your skin in order to keep warm. 贴身内衣；汗衫；背心 [US]

  • He put on a pair of short pants and an undershirt.

    他穿上了一条短裤和一件汗衫。
