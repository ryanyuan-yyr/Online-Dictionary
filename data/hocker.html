hock
hɒk hɒk hɑk
Word forms: plural hocks
1 N-MASS 霍克酒（德国产的一种干白葡萄酒） Hock is a type of dry white wine from Germany. 霍克酒（德国产的一种干白葡萄酒） [mainly British]

  • ...a glass of hock.

    一杯霍克酒

2 N-COUNT （尤指马后腿上的）跗关节 The hock of an animal, especially a horse, is the joint in its back leg that points backwards. （尤指马后腿上的）跗关节
3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] （尤指猪的）腿肉 A hock is a piece of meat from above the foot of an animal, especially a pig. （尤指猪的）腿肉

  • ...ham hocks with garlic and mustard sauce.

    配蒜蓉芥末酱汁的蹄膀

4 PHRASE 负债的;欠债的 If someone is in hock, they are in debt. 负债的;欠债的

  • Even company directors on £100,000 a year can be deeply in hock to the banks.

    即便是年薪10万英镑的公司董事也可能欠银行很多债。

5 PHRASE 欠…的债（或人情） If you are in hock to someone, you feel you have to do things for them because they have given you money or support. 欠…的债（或人情）

  • It is almost impossible for the prime minister to stand above the factions. He always seems in hock to one or another.

    总理想要不受各派系的影响几乎是不可能的。他好像总是为某一派所牵制。
