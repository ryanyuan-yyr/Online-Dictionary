cocky
kɒki ˈkɑki
Word forms: comparative cockier, superlative cockiest
ADJ-GRADED 骄傲自大的;自以为是的 Someone who is cocky is so confident and sure of their abilities that they annoy other people. 骄傲自大的;自以为是的 [informal, disapproval]

  • He was a little bit cocky when he was about 11 because he was winning everything.

    11岁左右的时候，他就有点自以为是，因为他那时从未失败过。

SYN overconfident, arrogant, brash, swaggering  
cockiness

  • The pair of them were both blinded by their own cockiness.

    他们两个人都因为自己的狂妄而变得盲目。
