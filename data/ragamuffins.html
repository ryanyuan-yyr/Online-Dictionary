ragamuffin
rægəmʌfɪn ˈrægəˌmʌfɪn
Word forms: plural ragamuffins
N-COUNT 衣衫褴褛的人（尤指儿童） A ragamuffin is someone, especially a child, who is dirty and has torn clothes. 衣衫褴褛的人（尤指儿童） [old-fashioned]

  • They looked like little ragamuffins.

    他们看上去像小叫花子。

SYN urchin [old-fashioned] , gamin, guttersnipe, scarecrow [informal]
