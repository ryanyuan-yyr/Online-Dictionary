pipe down
PHRASAL VERB [no cont, usually imper] 安静下来；停止讲话 If you tell someone who is talking a lot or talking too loudly to pipe down, you are telling them to stop talking. 安静下来；停止讲话 [informal]

  • Just pipe down and I'll tell you what I want. [VERB PREP.]

    别说了，我来告诉你我想要什么。

SYN be quiet, shut up [informal] , hush, stop talking
