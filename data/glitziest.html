glitzy
glɪtsi ˈglɪtsi
Word forms: comparative glitzier, superlative glitziest
ADJ-GRADED 浮华的；华丽的；光彩夺目的 Something that is glitzy is exciting and attractive in a showy way. 浮华的；华丽的；光彩夺目的

  • ...Aspen, Colorado, one of the glitziest ski resorts in the world.

    位于科罗拉多州的全球最奢华的滑雪胜地之一阿斯彭

SYN splendid, exciting, impressive, magnificent
