rattle off
PHRASAL VERB 不假思索地说出；脱口而出 If you rattle off something, you say it or do it very quickly and without much effort. 不假思索地说出；脱口而出 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Asked what English he knew, Mr Semko rattled off 'One, two, three'. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    问他懂哪些英文词，塞姆科先生不假思索地回答：“一，二，三。”

  • Hendry, playing an afternoon match, rattled off a 6-1 win over the Englishman. [VERB PREP. noun]

    亨德里在一场下午的比赛中以6比1的比分轻松地打败了英国选手。

SYN recite, list, run through, rehearse
