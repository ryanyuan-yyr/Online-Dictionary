unitary
juːnɪtri -teri ˈjunəˌtɛri
ADJ [ADJ n] 统一的；集中的；中央集权制的 A unitary country or organization is one in which two or more areas or groups have joined together, have the same aims, and are controlled by a single government. 统一的；集中的；中央集权制的

  • ...a call for the creation of a single unitary state.

    建立单一中央集权制国家的呼声
