cannibalize
kænɪbəlaɪz ˈkænəbəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cannibalizes, present participle cannibalizing, past tense , past participle cannibalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 cannibalise
1 VERB 拆用，拆卸利用（零件） If you cannibalize something, you take it to pieces and use it to make something else. 拆用，拆卸利用（零件）

  • They cannibalized damaged planes for the parts. [VERB noun]

    他们从毁坏的飞机上拆零件用。

2 VERB (公司中一种产品)损害，冲击（其他产品的销量） If one of a company's products cannibalizes the company's sales, people buy it instead of any of the company's other products. (公司中一种产品)损害，冲击（其他产品的销量） [business]

  • A website need not cannibalise existing sales. [VERB noun]

    网站并不妨碍目前的销售。
