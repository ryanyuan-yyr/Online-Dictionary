examine
ɪgzæmɪn ɪɡˈzæmɪn ɛgˈzæmən ɪgˈzæmən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense examines, present participle examining, past tense , past participle examined
1 VERB 仔细检查;细查 If you examine something, you look at it carefully. 仔细检查;细查

  • He examined her passport and stamped it. [VERB noun]

    他仔细检查了她的护照，然后在上面盖了章。

  • Forensic scientists are examining what police believe to have been the bombers' car. [VERB noun]

    法医学家正在对一辆警方怀疑属于投弹者的汽车进行仔细检查。

SYN inspect, test, consider, study  
examination

  • The Navy is to carry out an examination of the wreck tomorrow.

    海军明天将对失事船只进行细查。

  • They have also searched offices in Sheffield and taken away documents for examination.

    他们还搜查了位于设菲尔德的办公室，并带走了一些文件进行仔细检查。

2 VERB （医生）诊察，检查 If a doctor examines you, he or she looks at your body, feels it, or does simple tests in order to check how healthy you are. （医生）诊察，检查

  • Another doctor examined her and could still find nothing wrong. [VERB noun]

    另外一名医生对她进行了诊察，仍旧没有发现任何问题。

  • He was examined again and then prescribed a different herbal medicine. [VERB noun]

    医生又对他诊察了一遍，然后开了另外一种草药。

SYN check, analyse, check over  
examination

  • He was later discharged after an examination at Westminster Hospital.

    在威斯敏斯特医院进行了一次诊察后他就出院了。

  • Further examination is needed to exclude the chance of disease.

    需要进一步检查以排除患病的可能。

3 VERB 仔细研究;审查;剖析 If an idea, proposal, or plan is examined, it is considered very carefully. 仔细研究;审查;剖析

  • I have given the matter much thought, examining all the possible alternatives. [VERB noun]

    这个问题我已经考虑了很多，分析了各种可能的选择。

  • The plans will be examined by EU environment ministers.

    欧盟各国环境部部长将仔细研究这些计划。

examination

  • The proposal requires careful examination and consideration.

    那项提议需要经过仔细研究和考虑。

4 VERB [usually passive] 测验;考核 If you are examined, you are given a formal test in order to show your knowledge of a subject. 测验;考核

  • ...learning to cope with the pressures of being judged and examined by our teachers. [being V-ed]

    学会应对老师评判和测验时的压力

SYN test, question, assess, quiz
