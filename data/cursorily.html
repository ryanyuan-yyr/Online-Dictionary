cursory
kɜːʳsəri ˈkɜrsəri
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 仓促的;匆匆的;粗略的 A cursory glance or examination is a brief one in which you do not pay much attention to detail. 仓促的;匆匆的;粗略的

  • Burke cast a cursory glance at the menu, then flapped it shut.

    伯克匆匆地瞥了一眼菜单，然后啪地合上了。

  • I gave the letter a fairly cursory reading.

    我大略地读了一下那封信。

SYN brief, passing, rapid, casual
