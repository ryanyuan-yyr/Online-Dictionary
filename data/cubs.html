cub
kʌb kʌb
Word forms: plural cubs
The spelling Cub is also used for meanings 2, 3, and 4. 拼写 Cub 亦用于义项 2，3，4。
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (狮、狼、熊等的)幼兽，崽 A cub is a young wild animal such as a lion, wolf, or bear. (狮、狼、熊等的)幼兽，崽

  • ...three five-week-old lion cubs.

    3只5周大的幼狮

2 N-PROPER-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 幼童军(8至10岁的男孩组成的一支童子军) The Cubs or the Cub Scouts is a version of the Scouts for boys between the ages of eight and ten. 幼童军(8至10岁的男孩组成的一支童子军)
3 N-COUNT 幼童军成员 A cub or a cub scout is a boy who is a member of the Cubs. 幼童军成员
4 N-UNCOUNT [usually preposition NOUN] 幼童军会议 Cubs is a meeting of the Cubs. 幼童军会议

  • He was on his way to Cubs.

    他在去参加幼童军会议的路上。
