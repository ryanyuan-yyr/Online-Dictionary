peruse
pəʳuːz pəˈruz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense peruses, present participle perusing, past tense , past participle perused
VERB 阅读；读 If you peruse something such as a letter, article, or document, you read it. 阅读；读 [formal]

  • We perused the company's financial statements for the past five years. [VERB noun]

    我们翻阅了公司过去5年来的财务报表。

  • She found the information while she was perusing a copy of Life magazine. [VERB noun]

    她在读《生活》杂志的时候看到了这个消息。

SYN read, study, scan, check
