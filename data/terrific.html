  • What a terrific idea!.

    多么好的主意！

  • Everybody there was having a terrific time.

    在场的每个人都玩得开心极了。

  • You look terrific, Ann. You really do.

    安，你看起来太迷人了。真的。

SYN excellent, great [informal] , wonderful, mean [slang]  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 很多的;很大的;很强的 Terrific means very great in amount, degree, or intensity. 很多的;很大的;很强的 [emphasis]

  • He did a terrific amount of fundraising.

    他做了大量的筹款工作。

  • All of a sudden there was a terrific bang and a flash of smoke.

    突然传来惊天动地一声响，还冒出一股烟。

SYN great, huge, vast, enormous
