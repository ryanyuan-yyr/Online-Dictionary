tempest
tempɪst ˈtɛmpɪst
Word forms: plural tempests
1 N-COUNT 暴风雨 A tempest is a very violent storm. 暴风雨 [literary]

  • Torrential rain and a howling tempest cut a swathe of destruction across the country.

    狂风暴雨横扫全国，所到之处，一片狼藉。

2 N-COUNT 风波;风暴 You can refer to a situation in which people are very angry or excited as a tempest . a tempest in a teapot→see: teapot  风波;风暴

  • I hadn't foreseen the tempest my request would cause.

    我没有料到我的请求会掀起这么大一场风波。

  • The takeover provoked a tempest of criticism.

    这次收购引发了潮水般的批评。
