culmination
kʌlmɪneɪʃən ˌkʌlməˈneɪʃən
N-SING 终点;高潮;顶点 Something, especially something important, that is the culmination of an activity, process, or series of events happens at the end of it. 终点;高潮;顶点

  • Their arrest was the culmination of an operation in which 120 other people were detained. [+ of]

    他们的被捕标志着一场另有120人被拘留的行动的结束。
