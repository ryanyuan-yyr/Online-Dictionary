palatable
pælətəbəl ˈpælɪtəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 美味的；可口的 If you describe food or drink as palatable, you mean that it tastes pleasant. 美味的；可口的 [formal]

  • ...flavourings and preservatives, designed to make the food look more palatable.

    用于使食物看起来更加可口的调味料和防腐剂

SYN delicious, tasty, luscious, savoury  
2 ADJ-GRADED （想法、方法等）合意的，可接受的 If you describe something such as an idea or method as palatable, you mean that people are willing to accept it. （想法、方法等）合意的，可接受的

  • ...a palatable way of sacking staff.

    可以接受的裁员方式

  • That option is not very palatable.

    那个选择不太可以接受。
