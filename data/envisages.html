envisage
ɪnvɪzɪdʒ ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒ ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense envisages, present participle envisaging, past tense , past participle envisaged
VERB 设想;想象;预想 If you envisage something, you imagine that it is true, real, or likely to happen. 设想;想象;预想 [Also VERB that]

  • He envisages the possibility of establishing direct diplomatic relations in the future. [VERB noun]

    他设想在未来可以建立直接的外交关系。

  • He had never envisaged spending the whole of his working life in that particular job. [VERB verb-ing]

    他从没有想象过自己整个职业生涯都要从事那个工作。

  • Personally, I envisage them staying together. [VERB noun verb-ing]

    就我看来，我估计他们会呆在一起。
