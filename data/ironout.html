iron out
PHRASAL VERB 解决;消除 If you iron out difficulties, you resolve them and bring them to an end. 解决;消除 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • It was in the beginning, when we were still ironing out problems. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那是在刚开始,我们还在着力解决问题。

  • The various groups had now managed to iron out their differences. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    各团体现在已努力消除了彼此间的分歧。

SYN settle, resolve, sort out, eliminate
