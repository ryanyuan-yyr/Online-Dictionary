hamstring
hæmstrɪŋ ˈhæmˌstrɪŋ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hamstrings, present participle hamstringing, past tense , past participle hamstrung
1 N-COUNT 绳肌腱;腿筋 A hamstring is a length of tissue or tendon behind your knee which joins the muscles of your thigh to the bones of your lower leg. 绳肌腱;腿筋

  • Webster has not played since suffering a hamstring injury in the opening game.

    韦伯斯特自从在首场比赛中伤了绳肌腱后就再也没有参赛了。

2 VERB 使无能为力;使陷入瘫痪 If you hamstring someone, you make it very difficult for them to take any action. 使无能为力;使陷入瘫痪

  • If he becomes the major opposition leader, he could hamstring a conservative-led coalition. [VERB noun]

    如果他成为主要反对党的领袖，可能会导致保守党领导的联合政府陷于瘫痪。

SYN thwart, stop, block, prevent
