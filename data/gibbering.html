gibber
dʒɪbəʳ ˈɡɪbə ˈdʒɪbər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gibbers, present participle gibbering, past tense , past participle gibbered
VERB 急促不清地说；语无伦次地说 If you say that someone is gibbering, you mean that they are talking very fast and in a confused manner. 急促不清地说；语无伦次地说 [informal]

  • Everyone is gibbering insanely, nerves frayed as showtime approaches. [VERB]

    随着开演时间逼近，大家都紧张起来，慌里慌张地念着台词。

  • I was a gibbering wreck by this stage. [VERB-ing]

    我到这时候说话已颠三倒四，神经高度紧张。

SYN gabble, chatter, babble, waffle [informal , mainly British]
