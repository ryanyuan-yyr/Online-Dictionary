prosperous
prɒspərəs ˈprɑspərəs
ADJ-GRADED 富足的；成功的；兴旺的；繁荣的 Prosperous people, places, and economies are rich and successful. 富足的；成功的；兴旺的；繁荣的 [formal]

  • ...the youngest son of a relatively prosperous British family.

    一个相对富裕的英国家族中最小的儿子

  • The place looks more prosperous than ever.

    这地方显得空前繁荣。

SYN wealthy, rich, affluent, well-off
