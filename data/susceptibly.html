susceptible
səseptɪbəl səˈsɛptəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 易受…影响的;易为…左右的 If you are susceptible to something or someone, you are very likely to be influenced by them. 易受…影响的;易为…左右的

  • Young people are the most susceptible to advertisements. [+ to]

    年轻人最容易受广告的影响。

  • James was extremely susceptible to flattery. [+ to]

    詹姆斯听几句奉承话就忘乎所以。

  • He was, she believes, unusually susceptible to women.

    她认为他对女人毫无招架之力。

SYN responsive, sensitive, receptive, alive to  
2 ADJ-GRADED 易受(伤)的;易患(病)的;体质过敏的 If you are susceptible to a disease or injury, you are very likely to be affected by it. 易受(伤)的;易患(病)的;体质过敏的

  • Walking with weights makes the shoulders very susceptible to injury. [+ to]

    负重行走时肩膀很容易受伤。

  • Diesel exhaust is particularly aggravating to many susceptible individuals.

    许多体质敏感的人尤其反感柴油废气。

SYN liable, inclined, prone, given  
3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 感情丰富的;善变的 A susceptible person is very easily influenced emotionally. 感情丰富的;善变的

  • Hers was a susceptible nature.

    她很情绪化。
