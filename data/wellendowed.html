well-endowed
ˈwɛlɛnˈdaʊd
1 ADJ-GRADED (婉指女性或男性性别特征明显)天赋异禀的，硕大的 If someone says that a woman is well-endowed, they mean that she has large breasts. If someone says that a man is well-endowed, they mean that he has a large penis. People often use this expression if they are trying to be polite. (婉指女性或男性性别特征明显)天赋异禀的，硕大的

  • I spotted a well-endowed girl in the audience wearing a tight white T-shirt.

    我在观众席上看到一个穿着紧身白色T恤的巨乳女孩。

  • ...the chalk figure of a well-endowed warrior.

    用白垩画成的一个生殖器硕大的勇士形象

2 ADJ-GRADED 资金充足的 A well-endowed organization has a lot of money or resources. 资金充足的

  • In a large, well-endowed school, the opportunities for laboratory work are likely to be greater.

    在一所规模庞大且资金充足的学校，可能会有更多机会参与实验室的研究工作。
