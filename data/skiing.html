ski
skiː ʃi ski
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense skis, present participle skiing, past tense , past participle skied
1 N-COUNT 滑雪板;滑水板 Skis are long, flat, narrow pieces of wood, metal, or plastic that are fastened to boots so that you can move easily on snow or water. 滑雪板;滑水板

  • ...a pair of skis.

    一副滑雪板

2 VERB 滑雪;滑水 When people ski, they move over snow or water on skis. 滑雪;滑水

  • They surf, ski and ride. [VERB]

    他们冲浪，滑雪，还骑马。

  • The whole party then skied off. [VERB adv./prep.]

    然后整群人都滑着雪离去。

skier

  • He is an enthusiastic skier.

    他是个滑雪爱好者。

skiing

  • My hobbies were skiing and scuba diving.

    我爱好滑雪和潜水。

  • ...a skiing holiday.

    滑雪假日

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 滑雪用的;滑雪的;滑水的 You use ski to refer to things that are concerned with skiing. 滑雪用的;滑雪的;滑水的

  • ...the Swiss ski resort of Klosters.

    瑞士克洛斯特斯滑雪胜地

  • ...a private ski instructor.

    私人滑雪教练

  • ...artificial ski slopes.

    人造滑雪坡
