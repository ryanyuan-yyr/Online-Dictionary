stressed
strest strɛst
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 焦虑不安的;心力交瘁的 If you are stressed, you feel tense and anxious because of difficulties in your life. 焦虑不安的;心力交瘁的

  • Work out what situations or people make you feel stressed and avoid them.

    弄清是什么状况或什么人让你焦虑不安，然后避开他们。

SYN anxious, worried, tense, upset  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 受重压的;受应力的 A stressed object is affected by strong physical pressure which has been applied to it. 受重压的;受应力的 [technical]

  • ...stressed metal.

    受压金属

3 ADJ 重读的 If a word or part of a word is stressed, it is pronounced with emphasis. 重读的
