customer relations
ˈkʌstəmə rɪˈleɪʃənz
1 N-PLURAL 客户关系 Customer relations are the relationships that a business has with its customers and the way in which it treats them. 客户关系 [business]

  • Good customer relations require courtesy, professionalism and effective response.

    良好的客户关系需要做到礼貌、专业，并提供有效的回复。

2 N-UNCOUNT 客户关系部 Customer relations is the department within a company that deals with complaints from customers. 客户关系部 [business]

  • ...Tucson Electric's customer-relations department.

    图森电力公司的客户关系部
