troika
trɔɪkə ˈtrɔɪkə
Word forms: plural troikas
N-COUNT [usually singular] 三人领导小组;三头政治;三国集团 Journalists sometimes refer to a group of three powerful politicians or states as a troika . 三人领导小组;三头政治;三国集团

  • ...leader of the troika of past, present and future presidents. [+ of]

    前总统、现任总统与继任总统组成的三驾马车

  • The press regard her as merely one of a ruling troika.

    新闻界仅把她视为执政三巨头之一。
