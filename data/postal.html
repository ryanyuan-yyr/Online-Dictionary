postal
poʊstəl ˈpoʊstəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 邮政的；邮政业务的 Postal is used to describe things or people connected with the public service of carrying letters and packages from one place to another. 邮政的；邮政业务的

  • Compensation for lost or damaged mail will be handled by the postal service.

    对丢失或毁坏邮件的赔偿将由邮政部门处理。

  • Include your full postal address.

    写上完整的邮政地址。

  • ...postal workers.

    邮政人员

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 邮寄的；邮递的 Postal is used to describe activities that involve sending things by post. 邮寄的；邮递的

  • Unions would elect their leadership by secret postal ballot.

    工会将通过秘密邮寄选票的方式选举领导人。

  • ...a postal course in freelance photography.

    自由摄影的函授课程
