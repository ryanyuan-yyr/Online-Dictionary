pushed
pʊʃt pʊʃt
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] （时间或金钱）紧缺的，短缺的，拮据的 If you are pushed for something such as time or money, you do not have enough of it. （时间或金钱）紧缺的，短缺的，拮据的 [British, informal]

  • He's going to be a bit pushed for money. [+ for]

    他手头会有点儿紧。

SYN short of, pressed, rushed, tight  
in AM, use 美国英语用 pressed for
2 PHRASE 难于（做某事） If you are hard pushed to do something, you find it very difficult to do it. 难于（做某事） [British]

  • I'd be hard pushed to teach him anything.

    我发觉很难教他什么东西。
