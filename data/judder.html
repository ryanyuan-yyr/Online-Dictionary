judder
dʒʌdəʳ ˈdʒʌdər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense judders, present participle juddering, past tense , past participle juddered
VERB 剧烈震动；震颤 If something judders, it shakes or vibrates violently. 剧烈震动；震颤 [British]

  • The lift started off, juddered, and went out of action. [VERB]

    电梯开动了，接着剧烈震颤起来，然后就毫无反应了。

  • The car was juddering and vibrating as if it would explode. [VERB]

    汽车剧烈地摇晃着、颤动着，就像要爆炸似的。
