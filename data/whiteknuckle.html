white-knuckle
ˈhwaɪtˌnʌkəl ˈwaɪtˌnʌkəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （乘坐游乐装置）令人兴奋又紧张的 In a fairground, a white-knuckle ride is any large machine that people ride on which is very exciting but also frightening. （乘坐游乐装置）令人兴奋又紧张的

  • ...white-knuckle rides such as the rollercoaster.

    类似过山车这样的惊险娱乐项目

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （经历等）惊险刺激的 A white-knuckle experience is something that you find very exciting but also very frightening. （经历等）惊险刺激的

  • ...a hellish white-knuckle ride through the heavy London traffic.

    在伦敦拥堵的车流中惊心动魄的穿行
