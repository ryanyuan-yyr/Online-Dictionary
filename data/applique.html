applique
əpliːkeɪ əˈpliːkeɪ æplɪkeɪ
(also appliqué)
N-UNCOUNT 缝饰；嵌花；贴花 Applique is the craft of sewing fabric shapes onto larger pieces of cloth. You can also use applique to refer to things you make using this craft. 缝饰；嵌花；贴花

  • Bead-work, applique and embroidery add a uniquely feminine touch.

    珠饰、嵌花和刺绣可增添一种独特的女人味。
