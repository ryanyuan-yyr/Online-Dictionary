stance
stæns stæns
Word forms: plural stances
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 态度;立场 Your stance on a particular matter is your attitude to it. 态度;立场

  • The Congress had agreed to reconsider its stance on the armed struggle.

    国会已同意重新考虑其对武装斗争的态度。

  • They have maintained a consistently neutral stance.

    他们一直持中立态度。

  • His stance towards the story is quite similar to ours.

    他对该报道的态度和我们很相像。

SYN attitude, stand, position, viewpoint  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 站姿 Your stance is the way that you are standing. 站姿 [formal]

  • Take a comfortably wide stance and flex your knees a little.

    双腿适度分开站立，双膝稍稍弯曲。

  • The woman detective shifted her stance from one foot to another.

    女侦探改变了站姿，把重心从一只脚移到另一只脚。

SYN posture, carriage, bearing, deportment
