læmp læmp
Word forms: plural lamps
1 N-COUNT 灯 A lamp is a light that works by using electricity or by burning oil or gas. 灯

  • She switched on the bedside lamp.

    她打开床头灯。

  • In the evenings we eat by the light of an oil lamp.

    晚上我们便在一盏油灯下吃饭。

2 N-COUNT （尤指治疗或美容用的）发热灯，照射灯 A lamp is an electrical device which produces a special type of light or heat, used especially in medical or beauty treatment. （尤指治疗或美容用的）发热灯，照射灯

  • ...a sun lamp.

    太阳灯

  • ...the use of infra-red lamps.

    红外线灯的使用
