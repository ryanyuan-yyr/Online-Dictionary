absent-minded
ˈæbsəntˈmaɪndɪd ˈæbsəntˌmaɪndɪd
(also absentminded)
ADJ-GRADED 漫不经心的;心不在焉的;健忘的 Someone who is absent-minded forgets things or does not pay attention to what they are doing, often because they are thinking about something else. 漫不经心的;心不在焉的;健忘的

  • In his later life he became even more absent-minded.

    他晚年变得愈加健忘了。

SYN forgetful, absorbed, abstracted, vague  
absent-mindedly

  • Elizabeth absent-mindedly picked a thread from his lapel.

    伊丽莎白随手从他的翻领上揪下一根线头。

absent-mindedness

  • You will have to put up with Grace's absent-mindedness.

    你须得忍受格蕾丝心不在焉的作风。
