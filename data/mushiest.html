mushy
mʌʃi ˈmʌʃi
Word forms: comparative mushier, superlative mushiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (蔬菜和水果)软塌塌的 Vegetables and fruit that are mushy are soft and have lost most of their shape. (蔬菜和水果)软塌塌的

  • When the fruit is mushy and cooked, remove from the heat.

    当水果变软煮好后，就从火上端下来。

SYN soft, squidgy [informal] , slushy, squashy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 感伤的;多愁善感的;多情的 If you describe someone or something as mushy, you mean that they are very sentimental. 感伤的;多愁善感的;多情的 [disapproval]

  • Don't go getting all mushy and sentimental.

    不要变得过于多愁善感。

SYN sentimental, emotional, wet [British , informal] , sloppy [informal]
