obsessed
əbsest əbˈsɛst
ADJ-GRADED （对…）着迷的;（受…）困扰的；（为…）心神不宁的 If someone is obsessed with a person or thing, they keep thinking about them and find it difficult to think about anything else. （对…）着迷的;（受…）困扰的；（为…）心神不宁的

  • He was obsessed with American gangster movies. [+ with]

    他迷上了美国黑帮片。

  • She wasn't in love with Steve, she was obsessed by him physically. [+ by]

    她并没有爱上史蒂夫，只是被他的外表迷住了。

  • ...letters from all these really obsessed people.

    所有这些痴迷者的来信

SYN absorbed, dominated, gripped, caught up
