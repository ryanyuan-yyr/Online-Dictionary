court of inquiry
Word forms: plural courts of inquiry
N-COUNT 调查法庭 A court of inquiry is a group of people who are officially appointed to investigate a serious accident or incident, or an official investigation into a serious accident or incident. 调查法庭 [mainly British]

  • The government has instituted a court of inquiry to look into the allegations.

    政府已成立了调查法庭来调查这些指控。

  • Isherwood was at the court of inquiry to hear the evidence.

    伊舍伍德在调查法庭听取证据。
