miasma
miæzmə maɪˈæzmə miˈæzmə
Word forms: plural miasmas
N-VAR 难闻的气体;瘴气 You can describe something bad or confused that seems to be in the air all around you as a miasma . 难闻的气体;瘴气 [literary]

  • As time went on, his ambition to be part of the US Supreme Court faded in a miasma of alcohol and despair.

    随着时间的推移，他跻身美国最高法院的雄心壮志在弥漫着酒精和绝望气息的乌烟瘴气中消失殆尽。

SYN unwholesomeness, smell, pollution, odour
