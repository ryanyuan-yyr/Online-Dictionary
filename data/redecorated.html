redecorate
riːdekəreɪt riːˈdɛkəˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redecorates, present participle redecorating, past tense , past participle redecorated
VERB 重新装饰;再装修;重新粉刷 If you redecorate a room or a building, you put new paint or wallpaper on it. 重新装饰;再装修;重新粉刷

  • Americans redecorate their houses and offices every few years. [VERB noun]

    美国人每隔几年就会把家里和办公室重新粉刷一下。

  • Our children have left home, and we now want to redecorate. [VERB]

    我们的孩子们都离开了家，现在我们想要重新粉刷一下房屋。

SYN refurbish, paint, renovate, do up [informal]
