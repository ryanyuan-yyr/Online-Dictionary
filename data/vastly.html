vastly
vɑːstli væst-
ADV 很大程度上;极大地 Vastly means to an extremely great degree or extent. 很大程度上;极大地

  • The jury has heard two vastly different accounts of what happened.

    陪审团听到了对所发生事件的两种截然不同的陈述。

  • ...cars that are vastly more competitive.

    更富竞争力、优势极为明显的汽车

SYN hugely, enormously, immensely, massively
