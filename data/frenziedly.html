frenzied
frenzid
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 狂乱的;狂暴的;疯狂似的 Frenzied activities or actions are wild, excited, and uncontrolled. 狂乱的;狂暴的;疯狂似的

  • ...the frenzied activity of the general election.

    大选中异常激烈的活动

  • The man was stabbed to death in a frenzied attack.

    这名男子在一次疯狂的袭击中被刺死。

SYN uncontrolled, wild, excited, mad
