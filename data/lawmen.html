lawman
lɔːmæn ˈlɔːmən ˈlɔmən
Word forms: plural lawmen
1 N-COUNT （警察等）执法者；（律师等）法律工作者 Lawmen are men such as policemen or lawyers, whose work involves the law. （警察等）执法者；（律师等）法律工作者 [journalism]

  • ...the 61-year-old lawman who headed the enquiry.

    那位主持调查的61岁的执法官

2 N-COUNT （昔日北美西部的）治安官 In former times in western North America, a lawman was a sheriff or deputy sheriff. （昔日北美西部的）治安官 [US]
