put forward
PHRASAL VERB 提出（计划、建议）；推荐 If you put forward a plan, proposal, or name, you suggest that it should be considered for a particular purpose or job. 提出（计划、建议）；推荐 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He has put forward new peace proposals. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他提出了新的和平建议。

  • I rang the Colonel and asked him to put my name forward for the vacancy in Zurich. [V n P + for]

    我给上校打电话请他推荐我去填补苏黎世的那个职位空缺。

SYN recommend, present, suggest, introduce
