palatial
pəleɪʃəl pəˈleɪʃəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 宫殿般的；富丽堂皇的 A palatial house, hotel, or office building is very large and impressive. 宫殿般的；富丽堂皇的

  • ...a palatial Hollywood mansion.

    宫殿般的好莱坞住宅

  • ...the academy's palatial headquarters in Moscow.

    该学会位于莫斯科的富丽堂皇的总部

SYN magnificent, grand, imposing, splendid
