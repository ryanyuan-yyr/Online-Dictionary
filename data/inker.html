ink
ɪŋk ɪŋk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural inks, present participle inking, past tense , past participle inked
1 N-MASS 墨水;油墨 Ink is the coloured liquid used for writing or printing. 墨水;油墨

  • The letter was handwritten in black ink.

    信是用黑色墨水写的。

2 VERB 使沾上墨水(或油墨等) If you ink something, you put ink on it. 使沾上墨水(或油墨等)

  • Ritter took his left hand and inked the fingertips. [VERB noun]

    里特伸出左手,在指尖上沾上印油。
