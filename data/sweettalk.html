sweet talk swiːt tɔːk
(also sweet-talk)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sweet talks, present participle sweet talking, past tense , past participle sweet talked
VERB 对…甜言蜜语;给…灌迷魂汤;哄骗 If you sweet talk someone, you talk to them very nicely so that they will do what you want. 对…甜言蜜语;给…灌迷魂汤;哄骗

  • She could always sweet-talk Pamela into letting her stay up late. [VERB noun + into]

    她总能哄得帕梅拉同意她晚睡一会。

  • He even tried to sweet-talk the policewoman who arrested him. [VERB noun]

    他甚至想和逮住他的女警官套近乎。
