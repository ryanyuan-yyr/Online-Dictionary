skitter
skɪtəʳ ˈskɪtər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense skitters, present participle skittering, past tense , past participle skittered
VERB 滑行;疾行;轻轻掠过 If something skitters, it moves about very lightly and quickly. 滑行;疾行;轻轻掠过

  • The rats skittered around them in the drains and under the floorboards. [VERB adv./prep.]

    无论是下水道里，还是地板下面，都有许多耗子绕着它们蹿来蹿去。

  • Pieces of paper were skittering along the sidewalk. [VERB adv./prep.]

    人行道上飞舞着一张张纸片。
