two-dimensional ˈtudəˈmɛnʃənəl
(also two dimensional)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 二维的;平面的 A two-dimensional object or figure is flat rather than solid so that only its length and width can be measured. 二维的;平面的

  • ...new software, which generates both two-dimensional drawings and three-dimensional images.

    可以制作出二维图画与三维图像的新软件

2 ADJ-GRADED (虚构人物形象)无深度的，平面化的,不真实的 If you describe fictional characters as two-dimensional, you are critical of them because they are very simple and not realistic enough to be taken seriously. (虚构人物形象)无深度的，平面化的,不真实的 [disapproval]

  • I found the characters very two-dimensional, not to say dull.

    我觉得那些人物形象即使不说是乏味无聊，至少也是毫无深度。
