ruffled
rʌfəld ˈrʌfəld
1 ADJ-GRADED 不平的;起伏的;不整齐的 Something that is ruffled is no longer smooth or neat. 不平的;起伏的;不整齐的

  • Her short hair was oddly ruffled and then flattened around her head.

    她的短发顶心乱糟糟的,周围一圈却很平整，十分怪异。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （服饰）有褶边的 Ruffled clothes are decorated with small folds of material. （服饰）有褶边的

  • She was wearing a white ruffled blouse and a blue velvet skirt.

    她穿着一件有花边的白色上衣和一条蓝色天鹅绒裙子。
