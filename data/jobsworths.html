jobsworth
dʒɒbzwɜːʳθ
Word forms: plural jobsworths
N-COUNT 工作刻板机械的人 If you refer to someone as a jobsworth, you are criticizing them for using the rules connected to their job as an excuse not to be helpful. 工作刻板机械的人 [British, disapproval]

  • A surly jobsworth alerted security.

    一个态度粗暴、工作死板的人叫了警卫。
