dewy-eyed ˈduˌaɪd ˈdjuˌaɪd
ADJ-GRADED 天真的;易信的;不切实际的 If you say that someone is dewy-eyed, you are criticizing them because you think that they are unrealistic and think events and situations are better than they really are. 天真的;易信的;不切实际的 [disapproval]

  • I can never understand why people become dewy-eyed and sentimental about the past.

    我无论如何也不能理解为什么一说起过去人们就会那么容易动情和伤感。
