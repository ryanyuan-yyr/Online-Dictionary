low-maintenance
(also low maintenance)
ADJ 要求低的; 易维护的If you describe something or someone as low-maintenance, you mean that they require very little time, money, or effort to look after them. 要求低的; 易维护的

  • ...a secluded, low-maintenance garden.

    ...一座隐静的、易维护的花园。

  • I'm probably the most low-maintenance, easygoing person ever.

    我可能是史上要求最低、最好相处的人了。
