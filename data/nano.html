nano- nænoʊ ˈnænoʊ ˈnænə
PREFIX 纳”，表示毫微Nano- is used to form nouns and adjectives that refer to something that is so small that it can only be seen with a powerful microscope. “ 纳”，表示毫微

  • The fabrics are coated with nano-sized particles that repel stains.

    这种织物表面覆有一层抗着色污染的纳米粒子。
