sporting
spɔːʳtɪŋ ˈspɔrtɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 与体育运动有关的;体育运动用的 Sporting means relating to sports or used for sports. 与体育运动有关的;体育运动用的

  • ...major sporting events, such as Wimbledon and the World Cup finals.

    主要的体育赛事，如温布尔登网球赛及世界杯决赛

  • ...a huge sporting goods store.

    大型体育用品商店

SYN fair, sportsmanlike, game [informal] , gentlemanly  
2 PHRASE 极大的可能 If you have a sporting chance of doing something, it is quite likely that you will do that thing. 极大的可能

  • There was a sporting chance they would meet, but not necessarily at the party.

    他们很有可能会见面，但不一定在聚会上。

SYN probability, good chance, fair chance, fighting chance
