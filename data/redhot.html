red-hot ˈrɛdˈhɑt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （金属或岩石）炽热的，火红的 Red-hot metal or rock has been heated to such a high temperature that it has turned red. （金属或岩石）炽热的，火红的

  • ...red-hot iron.

    烧红了的烙铁

SYN very hot, burning, heated, steaming  
2 ADJ 温度极高的;烫手的 A red-hot object is too hot to be touched safely. 温度极高的;烫手的

  • In the main rooms red-hot radiators were left exposed.

    在主室里，炽热的散热器暴露在外。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 炙手可热的;流行的 Red-hot is used to describe a person or thing that is very popular, especially someone who is very good at what they do or something that is new and exciting. 炙手可热的;流行的 [journalism]

  • Some traders are already stacking the red-hot book on their shelves.

    一些经销商已经把这本火暴的书摆上架了。

  • ...red-hot guitarist David Grissom.

    炙手可热的吉他手戴维·格里索姆

4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 激情澎湃的；热力四射的；激烈的 If someone or something is described as red-hot, they show a lot of passion, enthusiasm, or excitement. 激情澎湃的；热力四射的；激烈的 [informal]

  • ...a red-hot sex-life.

    充满激情的性生活

  • ...a red-hot fury she had never realized she possessed.

    她从来没有意识到自己会发那么大的脾气

SYN passionate, thrilling, sexy, arousing  
5 ADJ [ADJ n] 夺冠热门的 The red-hot favourite in a race or contest is a person or team that is strongly expected to win. 夺冠热门的 [informal, emphasis]

  • His team are red-hot favourites.

    他的队伍是夺冠大热门。

SYN clear
