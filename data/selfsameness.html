self-same
(also selfsame)
ADJ [ADJ n] (用于强调)同一的，完全相同的 You use self-same when you want to emphasize that the person or thing mentioned is exactly the same as the one mentioned previously. (用于强调)同一的，完全相同的 [emphasis]

  • You find yourself worshipped by the self-same people who beat you up at school.

    你发现原来在学校里痛扁你的同一群人现在成了你的崇拜者。
