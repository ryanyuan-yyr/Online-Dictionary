pic
pɪk pɪk
Word forms: plural pics
1 N-COUNT 影片；电影 A pic is a cinema film. 影片；电影 [informal]

  • 'Angels with Dirty Faces' is a Cagney gangster pic.

    《一世之雄》是一部由卡格尼主演的黑帮片。

2 N-COUNT 照片；图片 A pic is a photograph. 照片；图片 [informal]

  • Photographer Weegee shot to fame with his shocking pics of New York crime in the 30s. [+ of]

    摄影师维吉以拍摄30年代纽约罪案的骇人照片而声名鹊起。
