lock away
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…锁起妥藏 If you lock something away in a place or container, you put or hide it there and fasten the lock. 把…锁起妥藏

  • She meticulously cleaned the gun and locked it away in its case. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她小心翼翼地把枪擦拭干净，放进枪盒里锁好。

  • He had even locked away all the videos of his previous exploits. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他甚至将记录他以前伟绩的录像带通通锁起来了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 把…关入监狱（或精神病院） To lock someone away means to put them in prison or a secure mental hospital. 把…关入监狱（或精神病院） [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Locking them away is not sufficient, you have to give them treatment. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把他们关起来是不够的，还要对他们进行治疗才行。

3 PHRASAL VERB （自我）隔绝， 封闭 If you lock yourself away, you go somewhere where you can be alone, and do not come out or see anyone for some time. （自我）隔绝， 封闭

  • I locked myself away with books and magazines. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    我自行与外界隔绝，仅以书和杂志为伴。
