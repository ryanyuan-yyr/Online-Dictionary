involvement
ɪnvɒlvmənt
Word forms: plural involvements
1 N-UNCOUNT 参与;介入;卷入 Your involvement in something is the fact that you are taking part in it. 参与;介入;卷入

  • You have no proof of my involvement in anything.

    你没有证据证明我参与了任何活动。

  • She disliked his involvement with the group and disliked his friends. [+ with]

    她不喜欢他与那帮人交往,也不喜欢他的朋友。

SYN collaboration, implication, participation, hand  
2 N-UNCOUNT (投入的)感情;(倾注的)热情 Involvement is the enthusiasm that you feel when you care deeply about something. (投入的)感情;(倾注的)热情

  • Ben has always felt a deep involvement with animals. [+ with]

    本对动物总是有着深厚的感情。

SYN connection, interest, relationship, concern  
3 N-VAR 暧昧关系;恋爱关系 An involvement is a close relationship between two people, especially if they are not married to each other. 暧昧关系;恋爱关系

  • They were very good friends but there was no romantic involvement.

    他们是非常要好的朋友，却没有任何暧昧关系。

  • Mahler was also known for his passionate involvements outside marriage.

    马勒也因为其轰轰烈烈的婚外情而出名。

SYN intimacy, attachment, closeness, familiarity
