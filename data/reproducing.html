reproduce
riːprədjuːs -duːs ˌriprəˈdus ˌriprəˈdjus
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reproduces, present participle reproducing, past tense , past participle reproduced
1 VERB 复制;模拟;再现 If you try to reproduce something, you try to copy it. 复制;模拟;再现

  • I shall not try to reproduce the policemen's English. [VERB noun]

    我不会试图去模仿那个警察讲的英语。

  • The effect has proved hard to reproduce. [VERB noun]

    事实证明，这种效果很难再现。

SYN copy, recreate, replicate, duplicate  
2 VERB 复制;临摹;翻印 If you reproduce a picture, speech, or a piece of writing, you make a photograph or printed copy of it. 复制;临摹;翻印

  • We are grateful to you for permission to reproduce this article. [VERB noun]

    非常感谢您允许我们复印这篇文章。

SYN print, copy, duplicate, photocopy  
3 VERB 重复 If you reproduce an action or an achievement, you repeat it. 重复

  • If we can reproduce the form we have shown in the last couple of months we will be successful. [VERB noun]

    如果我们能再采用前两个月的形式，我们就会成功。

4 VERB 繁殖;生殖 When people, animals, or plants reproduce, they produce young. 繁殖;生殖

  • ...a society where women are defined by their ability to reproduce. [VERB]

    用生育能力衡量女性价值的社会

  • We are reproducing ourselves at such a rate that our numbers threaten the ecology of the planet. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    我们的繁衍速度太快，以至于人口数量对地球的生态系统造成了威胁。

SYN breed, produce young, bear young, procreate [formal]
