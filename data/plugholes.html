plughole
plʌghoʊl
Word forms: plural plugholes
1 N-COUNT （浴缸或水槽的）排水孔，出水口 A plughole is a small hole in a bath or sink which allows the water to flow away and into which you can put a plug. （浴缸或水槽的）排水孔，出水口 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 drain
2 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 失败；丢失；白白浪费 If you say that something has gone down the plughole, you mean that it has failed or has been lost or wasted. 失败；丢失；白白浪费 [British, informal]

  • Millions of pounds have gone down the plughole.

    数百万英镑付诸东流。

  • Germans worry that their economy is going down the plughole.

    德国人担心他们的经济会滑入深渊。

in AM, use 美国英语用 down the drain
