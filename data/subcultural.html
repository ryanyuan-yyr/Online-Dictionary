subculture
sʌbkʌltʃəʳ ˈsʌbˌkʌltʃər
(also sub-culture)Word forms: plural subcultures
N-COUNT 亚文化(模式) A subculture is the ideas, art, and way of life of a group of people within a society, which are different from the ideas, art, and way of life of the rest of the society. 亚文化(模式)

  • ...the latest American subculture.

    最近的美国亚文化

  • ...the violent subculture of London youth gangs.

    伦敦年轻帮派的暴力亚文化
