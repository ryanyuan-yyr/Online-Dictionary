labyrinth
læbɪrɪnθ ˈlæbəˌrɪnθ
Word forms: plural labyrinths
1 N-COUNT 迷宫；迷阵 If you describe a place as a labyrinth, you mean that it is made up of a complicated series of paths or passages, through which it is difficult to find your way. 迷宫；迷阵 [literary]

  • ...the labyrinth of corridors. [+ of]

    迷宫般的走廊

2 N-COUNT 错综复杂的事物；千头万绪的局面；盘根错节的情况 If you describe a situation, process, or area of knowledge as a labyrinth, you mean that it is very complicated. 错综复杂的事物；千头万绪的局面；盘根错节的情况 [formal]

  • ...a labyrinth of conflicting political and sociological interpretations. [+ of]

    错综复杂、相互矛盾的政治学与社会学诠释

SYN intricacy, puzzle, complexity, riddle
