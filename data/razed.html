raze
reɪz reɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense razes, present participle razing, past tense , past participle razed
VERB [usually passive] 把（建筑或城镇）夷为平地；彻底摧毁 If buildings, villages or towns are razed or razed to the ground, they are completely destroyed. 把（建筑或城镇）夷为平地；彻底摧毁

  • Dozens of villages have been razed. [be VERB-ed]

    几十个村庄被夷为平地。

  • Towns such as Mittelwihr and Bennwihr were virtually razed to the ground. [be VERB-ed + to]

    如米特维尔和本维尔这样的城镇基本上被夷为平地。

SYN destroy, level, remove, ruin
