assumed name
əˈsjuːmd neɪm
Word forms: plural assumed names
N-COUNT [usu under N] 化名;假名 If you do something under an assumed name, you do it using a name that is not your real name. 化名;假名

  • The articles were published in San Francisco newspapers under the assumed name of Dorothy Dodge.

    这些发表在旧金山报纸上的文章都出自一个化名为多萝西·道奇的人之手。

  • In Arnham, she and her mother lived under assumed names and took care to speak Flemish rather than their more usual French and English.

    在阿纳姆，她和母亲隐姓埋名，而且刻意说着一口佛兰芒语而不是她们更常用的法语和英语。
