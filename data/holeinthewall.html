hole-in-the-wall
N-SING [usually NOUN n] （设在墙内的）自动取款机 A hole-in-the-wall machine is a machine built into the wall of a bank or other building, which allows people to take out money from their bank account by using a special card. （设在墙内的）自动取款机 [British, informal]

  • Hole-in-the-wall cash machines were introduced in the 1970s.
