town hall
ˌtaʊnˈhɔːl
(also Town Hall)Word forms: plural town halls
1 N-COUNT (英国的)市(或镇)政会大楼，市(或镇)政会 In Britain, a town hall in a town is a large building owned and used by the town council, often as its main office. You can also use town hall to refer to the town council that uses this building. (英国的)市(或镇)政会大楼，市(或镇)政会
2 N-COUNT (美国，尤指新英格兰的)市政厅，镇公所 In the United States, especially in New England, a town hall is a building or hall used for local government business. (美国，尤指新英格兰的)市政厅，镇公所
