swap
swɒp swɑp swɔp
(also swop)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense swaps, present participle swapping, past tense , past participle swapped
1 V-RECIP 交换 If you swap something with someone, you give it to them and receive a different thing in exchange. 交换

  • Next week they will swap places and will repeat the switch weekly. [VERB noun]

    下周他们将交换座位，并且以后每周换一次。

  • I'd gladly swap places with mummy any day. [VERB noun + with]

    什么时候让我和妈妈交换角色我都愿意。

  • I know a sculptor who swaps her pieces for drawings by a well-known artist. [V n + for/with]

    我认识一个雕刻家，她用自己的作品去换一位著名画家的画作。

  • Some hostages were swapped for convicted prisoners. [be V-ed + for/with]

    用几名人质交换了已经定罪的囚犯。

SYN exchange, trade, switch, traffic  
Swap is also a noun.

  • If she ever fancies a job swap, I could be interested.

    如果她想和别人互换工作，我会很感兴趣的。

2 VERB 用…替换;把…换成;停下(手头的事)改做 If you swap one thing for another, you remove the first thing and replace it with the second, or you stop doing the first thing and start doing the second. 用…替换;把…换成;停下(手头的事)改做

  • Despite the heat, he'd swapped his overalls for a suit and tie. [VERB noun + for]

    尽管很热，他还是脱掉工作服换上了西装和领带。

  • He has swapped his hectic rock star's lifestyle for that of a country gentleman. [VERB noun for noun]

    他一改摇滚歌星紧张忙碌的生活方式，开始过起了乡村绅士的生活。

  • Both sides swapped their goalies in the 30th minute. [VERB noun]

    比赛进行到第 30 分钟时双方都更换了守门员。

3 VERB 交流，交换(意见);互相讲述(故事) If you swap stories or opinions with someone, you tell each other stories or give each other your opinions. 交流，交换(意见);互相讲述(故事)

  • They all sat together at table, laughing and swapping stories. [V pl-n]

    他们都坐在桌子旁，有说有笑地讲述各自的故事。

SYN trade, exchange, reciprocate, bandy
