faucet
fɔːsɪt ˈfɔːsɪt ˈfɔsɪt ˈfɑsɪt
Word forms: plural faucets
N-COUNT 龙头;开关 A faucet is a device that controls the flow of a liquid or gas from a pipe or container. Sinks and baths have faucets attached to them. 龙头;开关 [US]

  • She turned off the faucet and dried her hands.

    她关掉水龙头，把手擦干。

SYN tap, spout, spigot, stopcock
