roller-skate
ˈrəʊləˌskeɪt
(also roller skate)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense roller-skates, present participle roller-skating, past tense , past participle roller-skated
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 滚轴溜冰鞋;（四轮）旱冰鞋 Roller-skates are shoes with four small wheels on the bottom. 滚轴溜冰鞋;（四轮）旱冰鞋

  • A boy of about ten came up on roller-skates.

    一个10岁左右、脚蹬旱冰鞋的男孩滑了过来。

2 VERB 溜旱冰;滑旱冰 If you roller-skate, you move over a flat surface wearing roller-skates. 溜旱冰;滑旱冰

  • On the day of the accident, my son Gary was roller-skating outside our house. [VERB]

    事故发生的那天，我儿子加里正在我们的房子外面滑旱冰。
