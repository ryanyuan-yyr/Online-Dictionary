praise
preɪz preɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense praises, present participle praising, past tense , past participle praised
1 VERB 称赞；赞扬；表扬 If you praise someone or something, you express approval for their achievements or qualities. 称赞；赞扬；表扬

  • The American president praised Turkey for its courage. [VERB noun + for]

    美国总统赞扬了土耳其的勇气。

  • Many others praised Sanford for taking a strong stand. [V n for n/-ing]

    其他很多人称赞桑福德立场坚定。

  • He praised the excellent work of the UN weapons inspectors.

    他赞扬了联合国武器核查人员的出色工作。

SYN acclaim, approve of, honour, cheer  
2 N-UNCOUNT 称赞；赞美；赞扬 Praise is what you say or write about someone when you are praising them. 称赞；赞美；赞扬

  • All the ladies are full of praise for the staff and service they received. [+ for]

    所有女士都高度赞扬了工作人员和他们所提供的服务。

  • I have nothing but praise for the police.

    我对警方唯有称赞。

  • That is high praise indeed.

    那真是高度的赞美。

SYN approval, acclaim, applause, cheering  
3 VERB 称颂，赞美，感谢（上帝） If you praise God, you express your respect, honour, and thanks to God. 称颂，赞美，感谢（上帝）

  • She asked the church to praise God. [VERB noun]

    她请求教会赞美上帝。

SYN give thanks to, bless, worship, adore  
4 N-UNCOUNT （对上帝的）赞美，敬拜，感谢 Praise is the expression of respect, honour, and thanks to God. （对上帝的）赞美，敬拜，感谢

  • Hindus were singing hymns in praise of the god Rama. [+ of]

    印度教教徒在唱圣歌赞美罗摩神。

SYN thanks, glory, worship, devotion  
5 PHRASE 用冷漠的赞许贬低；明褒实贬 If someone damns something with faint praise, they say something about it which sounds quite nice but is not enthusiastic, and shows that they do not have a high opinion of it. 用冷漠的赞许贬低；明褒实贬

  • ...the belief that the US had signalled a policy shift by damning with faint praise the mediation efforts.

    认为美国对调停努力明褒实贬是表明其政策已发生转变的观点

6 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 高度赞扬；热情表扬 If you sing someone's praises, you praise them in an enthusiastic way. 高度赞扬；热情表扬

  • Ottershaw's been singing your praises for years.

    奥特肖多年来对你一直高度赞赏。

  • Andy Sinton is not a man given to singing his own praises.

    安迪·辛顿不是一个惯于吹嘘自我的人。
