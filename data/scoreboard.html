scoreboard
skɔːʳbɔːʳd ˈskɔrˌbɔrd
Word forms: plural scoreboards
N-COUNT 记分牌 A scoreboard is a large board, for example at a sports ground or stadium, which shows the score in a match or competition. 记分牌

  • The figures flash up on the scoreboard.

    分数在记分牌上亮起。
