militaristic
mɪlɪtərɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 军国主义的;好战的;黩武的 Militaristic is used to describe groups, ideas, or policies which support the strengthening of the armed forces of their country in order to make it more powerful. 军国主义的;好战的;黩武的 [disapproval]

    带有侵略性的军国主义政府

SYN war-mongering, martial, aggressive, belligerent
