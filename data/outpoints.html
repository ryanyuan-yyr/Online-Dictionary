outpoint
aʊtpɔɪnt ˈaʊtˈpɔɪnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outpoints, present participle outpointing, past tense , past participle outpointed
VERB （在拳击比赛中）以点数战胜 In boxing, if one boxer outpoints another, they win the match by getting more points than their opponent. （在拳击比赛中）以点数战胜

  • Kane won the world title in 1938 when he outpointed Jackie Durich. [VERB noun]

    1938年，凯恩以点数击败杰基·杜里克，获得了世界拳王称号。
