post office
Word forms: plural post offices
1 N-SING 邮政部 The Post Office is the national organization that is responsible for postal services. 邮政部

  • The Post Office has confirmed that up to fifteen thousand jobs could be lost.

    邮政部已确认可能会流失掉多达1.5万份工作。

2 N-COUNT 邮局；邮政所 A post office is a building where you can buy stamps, post letters and packages, and use other services provided by the national postal service. 邮局；邮政所
