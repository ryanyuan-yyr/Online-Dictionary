left-handed
ˈlɛftˈhændɪd
ADJ 惯用左手的；左撇子的 Someone who is left-handed uses their left hand rather than their right hand for activities such as writing and sports and for picking things up. 惯用左手的；左撇子的

  • I noticed she was left-handed.

    我注意到她是个左撇子。

  • There is a place in London that supplies practically everything for left-handed people.

    伦敦有一个地方卖左撇子用品，几乎什么都有。

Left-handed is also an adverb.

  • My father thought that I'd be at a disadvantage if I wrote left-handed.

    我爸觉得我如果用左手写字，将来会吃亏的。
