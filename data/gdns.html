gdn
Word forms: plural gdns
（用于地址、待售的房屋广告等） gdn is a written abbreviation for (书面缩略=) garden, for example in addresses, or in advertisements for houses that are for sale. （用于地址、待售的房屋广告等）

    西中央区2区，科芬园露天广场

  • ...flat, private gdn, close to station.

    公寓，专享私家花园，靠近车站
