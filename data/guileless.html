guileless
gaɪlləs ˈgaɪllɪs
ADJ-GRADED 不狡诈的；老实的；厚道的 If you describe someone as guileless, you mean that they behave openly and truthfully and do not try to deceive people. 不狡诈的；老实的；厚道的 [written, approval]

  • Daphne was so guileless that Claire had no option but to believe her.

    达夫妮如此忠厚老实，克莱尔对她的话也只有信以为真了。

SYN artless, open, genuine, simple
