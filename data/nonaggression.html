non-aggression
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 nonaggression
N-UNCOUNT 不侵略;不侵犯 If a country adopts a policy of non-aggression, it declares that it will not attack or try to harm a particular country in any way. 不侵略;不侵犯

  • The agreement includes a pledge of non-aggression.

    协议包括不发动侵略的保证。

  • A non-aggression pact will be signed between the two countries.

    两国将签署互不侵犯条约。
