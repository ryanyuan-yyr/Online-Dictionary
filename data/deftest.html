deft
deft dɛft
Word forms: comparative defter, superlative deftest
ADJ-GRADED 灵巧的;熟练的;机敏的 A deft action is skilful and often quick. 灵巧的;熟练的;机敏的 [written]

  • With a deft flick of his foot, Mr Worth tripped one of the raiders up.

    沃思先生机敏地把脚一伸，把其中一个袭击者给绊倒了。

SYN skilful, able, expert, clever  
deftly

  • One of the waiting servants deftly caught him as he fell.

    他快要跌倒时，其中一个在旁服侍的仆人眼疾手快地扶住了他。

deftness

  • ...Dr Holly's surgical deftness and experience.

    霍利医生在手术方面的驾轻就熟和经验老道
