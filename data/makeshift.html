makeshift
meɪkʃɪft ˈmeɪkˌʃɪft
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 权宜的;临时凑合的 Makeshift things are temporary and usually of poor quality, but they are used because there is nothing better available. 权宜的;临时凑合的

  • ...the cardboard boxes and makeshift shelters of the homeless.

    无家可归者的纸箱与临时的栖身处

  • ...a makeshift coffee table.

    临时代用咖啡桌

SYN temporary, provisional, make-do, substitute
