perfunctory
pəʳfʌŋktəri -tɔːri pərˈfʌŋktəri
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 敷衍的；潦草的；马马虎虎的 A perfunctory action is done quickly and carelessly, and shows a lack of interest in what you are doing. 敷衍的；潦草的；马马虎虎的

  • She gave the list only a perfunctory glance.

    她马马虎虎地扫了一眼清单。

  • ...a perfunctory handshake.

    漫不经心的握手

  • Our interest was purely perfunctory.

    我们的兴趣只有三分热度。

SYN offhand, routine, wooden, automatic  
perfunctorily

  • Melina was perfunctorily introduced to the men.

    梅利娜被草草地介绍给了男士们。
