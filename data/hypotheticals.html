hypothetical
haɪpəθetɪkəl ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl
Word forms: plural hypotheticals
1 ADJ （基于）假设的;假定的 If something is hypothetical, it is based on possible ideas or situations rather than actual ones. （基于）假设的;假定的

  • Let's look at a hypothetical situation in which Carol, a recovering cocaine addict, gets invited to a party.

    让我们假设这样一种情景，卡萝尔，一位正在康复的可卡因成瘾者，应邀去参加一个聚会。

  • ...a purely hypothetical question.

    纯属假设的问题

SYN theoretical, supposed, academic, assumed  
Hypothetical is also a noun.

  • Well, at present we won't speculate on hypotheticals.

    嗯，目前我们不想去推测那些假想的东西。

hypothetically

  • He was invariably willing to discuss the possibilities hypothetically.

    他总是愿意谈论各种假设的可能性。

  • It bases its figures on what it might, hypothetically, be earning on past investment.

    这些假定的可能收益数字是依据过去的投资得出的。
