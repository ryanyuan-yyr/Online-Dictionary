PHRASE 口才,辩才（美国英语中亦作the gift of gab） If someone has the gift of the gab, they are able to speak easily and confidently, and to persuade people. Also the gift of gab, mainly in American English. 口才,辩才（美国英语中亦作the gift of gab） [approval]

  • They are naturally good salesmen with the gift of the gab.

    他们天生就是能说会道的推销能手。

SYN eloquence, fluency, expressiveness, way with words
