tack on
PHRASAL VERB (匆忙或不尽如人意地)增添，附加 If you say that something is tacked on to something else, you think that it is added in a hurry and in an unsatisfactory way. (匆忙或不尽如人意地)增添，附加 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The childcare bill is to be tacked on to the budget plan now being worked out in the Senate. [be V-ed P + to]

    儿童保育款项将被追加到参议院正在审议的预算方案中。
