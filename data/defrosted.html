defrost
diːfrɒst -frɔːst diˈfrɔst dɪˈfrɔst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense defrosts, present participle defrosting, past tense , past participle defrosted
1 V-ERG (使)(冷冻食物)解冻 When you defrost frozen food or when it defrosts, you allow or cause it to become unfrozen so that you can eat it or cook it. (使)(冷冻食物)解冻

  • She has a microwave, but uses it mainly for defrosting bread. [VERB noun]

    她有个微波炉，不过主要用来解冻面包。

  • Once the turkey has defrosted, remove the giblets. [VERB]

    一旦火鸡解冻了之后，就把其内脏清除掉。

SYN thaw, warm, soften, de-ice  
2 V-ERG (给)(冰箱或冰柜)除霜 When you defrost a fridge or freezer, you switch it off or press a special switch so that the ice inside it can melt. You can also say that a fridge or freezer is defrosting . (给)(冰箱或冰柜)除霜 [Also VERB]

  • Defrost the fridge regularly so that it works at maximum efficiency. [VERB noun]

    定期给冰箱除霜，以便它能发挥其最高效率。
