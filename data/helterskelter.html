helter-skelter
heltəʳ skeltəʳ ˈhɛltərˈskɛltər
ADJ [ADJ n] 匆促忙乱的;杂乱无章的 You use helter-skelter to describe something that is hurried and disorganized, especially when things happen very quickly, one after the other. 匆促忙乱的;杂乱无章的

  • He now faces another crisis in his helter-skelter existence.

    他乱糟糟的生活中现在又面临着另一个危机。

SYN haphazard, confused, disordered, random  
Helter-skelter is also an adverb.

  • ...a panic-stricken crowd running helter-skelter to get away from the tear gas.

    争相躲避催泪瓦斯的恐慌人群
