interplay
ɪntəʳpleɪ ˈɪntərˌpleɪ ˌ ɪntərˈpleɪ
N-UNCOUNT 相互作用;相互影响 The interplay between two or more things or people is the way that they have an effect on each another or react to each other. 相互作用;相互影响

  • ...the personal interplay between great entertainers and a live public.

    演艺巨星与现场观众之间的直接互动

  • ...the interplay of political, economic, social and cultural factors. [+ of]

    政治、经济、社会及文化因素之间的相互作用

SYN interaction, give-and-take, reciprocity, reciprocation
