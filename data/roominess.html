roomy
ruːmi ˈruːmɪ ˈrumi
Word forms: comparative roomier, superlative roomiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 宽敞的;空间大的 If you describe a place as roomy, you mean that you like it because it is large inside and you can move around freely and comfortably. 宽敞的;空间大的 [approval]

  • The car is roomy and a good choice for anyone who needs to carry equipment.

    这种车非常宽敞，对于需要携带装备的人来说是不错的选择。

2 ADJ-GRADED (衣服)宽松的 If you describe a piece of clothing as roomy, you mean that you like it because it is large and fits loosely. (衣服)宽松的 [approval]

  • ...roomy jackets.

    宽松的夹克
