diversity
daɪvɜːʳsɪti dɪ- dəˈvɜrsəti daɪˈvɜrsəti
Word forms: plural diversities
1 N-VAR 多样性;多样化;多元性 The diversity of something is the fact that it contains many very different elements. 多样性;多样化;多元性

  • ...the cultural diversity of British society.

    英国社会文化的多元性

  • ...to introduce more choice and diversity into the education system.

    在教育体系中引入更多的选择和多样性

SYN difference, diversification, variety, divergence  
2 N-SING 不同;差异 A diversity of things is a range of things which are very different from each other. 不同;差异

  • His object is to gather as great a diversity of material as possible. [+ of]

    他的目标就是尽可能多地搜集各种材料。

SYN range, variety, sweep, scope
