idiosyncratic
ɪdioʊsɪŋkrætɪk ˌɪdɪəʊsɪŋˈkrætɪk
ADJ-GRADED 乖僻的;怪异的 If you describe someone's actions or characteristics as idiosyncratic, you mean that they are rather unusual. 乖僻的;怪异的

  • ...a highly idiosyncratic personality.

    非常乖僻的个性

  • ...his erratic typing and idiosyncratic spelling.

    他古怪的打字与奇特的拼写

SYN distinctive, special, individual, typical
