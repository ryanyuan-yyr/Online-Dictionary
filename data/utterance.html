utterance
ʌtərəns ˈʌtərəns
Word forms: plural utterances
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 言辞；言语；言论 Someone's utterances are the things that they say. 言辞；言语；言论 [formal]

  • ...the Queen's public utterances.

    女王的公开讲话

  • ...a host of admirers who hung on her every utterance.

    全神贯注地听她每一句话的一群仰慕者

SYN speech, words, statement, comment  
2 N-UNCOUNT 吐露；表达 Utterance is the expression in words of ideas, thoughts, and feelings. 吐露；表达 [formal]

  • She could choose her own partner in matrimony, as long as she gave no utterance to her passions and emotions. [+ to]

    只要她不表露她的激情和情感，她就可以选择自己的婚姻伴侣。
