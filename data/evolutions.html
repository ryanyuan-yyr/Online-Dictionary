evolution
iːvəluːʃən ev- ˌiːvəˈluːʃən ˌɛvəˈluʃən ˌ ivəˈluʃən
Word forms: plural evolutions
1 N-UNCOUNT 进化 Evolution is a process of gradual change that takes place over many generations, during which species of animals, plants, or insects slowly change some of their physical characteristics. 进化

  • ...the evolution of plants and animals. [+ of]

    动植物的进化

  • ...the theory of evolution by natural selection.

    自然选择的进化论

  • ...human evolution.

    人类进化

SYN rise, development, adaptation, natural selection  
2 N-VAR 演变;演化;演进;发展 Evolution is a process of gradual development in a particular situation or thing over a period of time. 演变;演化;演进;发展 [formal]

  • ...a crucial period in the evolution of modern physics. [+ of]

    现代物理学演化过程中的重要时期

  • ...an accurate account of his country's evolution.

    对他的国家演进的准确描述

  • His long life comprised a series of evolutions.

    他漫长的一生包括一系列的发展变化。

SYN development, growth, advance, progress
