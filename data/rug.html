rug
rʌg rʌg
Word forms: plural rugs
1 N-COUNT 小地毯;垫子 A rug is a piece of thick material that you put on a floor. It is like a carpet but covers a smaller area. 小地毯;垫子

  • A Persian rug covered the hardwood floors.

    硬木地板上铺着一张波斯地毯。

SYN mat, runner, carpet, hearthrug  
2 N-COUNT (盖肩膀或膝盖以保暖的)围毯，小毯子 A rug is a small blanket which you use to cover your shoulders or your knees to keep them warm. (盖肩膀或膝盖以保暖的)围毯，小毯子 [mainly British]

  • The old lady was seated in her chair at the window, a rug over her knees.

    老太太坐在窗边的椅子上，膝盖上盖着一条小毯子。

  • ...a travel rug.

    旅行毛毯

SYN blanket, throw, wrap, coverlet  
3 PHRASE 不再支持;停止帮助 If someone pulls the rug from under a person or thing or pulls the rug from under someone's feet, they stop giving their help or support. to sweep something under the rug→see: sweep  不再支持;停止帮助

  • If the banks opt to pull the rug from under the ill-fated project, it will go into liquidation.

    如果这些银行不再对这个倒霉的项目提供支持，它也就破产了。
