far-reaching
ˈfɑrˈritʃɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 影响深远的;意义重大的 If you describe actions, events, or changes as far-reaching, you mean that they have a very great influence and affect a great number of things. 影响深远的;意义重大的

  • The economy is in danger of collapse unless far-reaching reforms are implemented.

    如果不实施影响深远的改革，经济就面临崩溃的危险。

SYN extensive, important, significant, sweeping
