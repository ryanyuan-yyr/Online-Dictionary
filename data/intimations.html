intimation
ɪntɪmeɪʃən ˌɪntəˈmeɪʃən
Word forms: plural intimations
N-COUNT [NOUN that] 暗示;提示 An intimation is an indirect suggestion or sign that something is likely to happen or be true. 暗示;提示 [formal]

  • ...intimations of mortality.

    濒临死亡的征兆

  • I did not have any intimation that he was going to resign.

    我一点儿都不知道他要辞职。

SYN hint, warning, suggestion, indication
