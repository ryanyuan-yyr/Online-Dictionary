veterinary
vetərənəri -neri ˈvɛtərəˌnɛri ˈvɛtrəəˌnɛri
ADJ [ADJ n] 兽医的 Veterinary is used to describe the work of a person whose job is to treat sick or injured animals, or to describe the medical treatment of animals. 兽医的

  • It was decided that our veterinary screening of horses at events should be continued.

    已经决定继续对参赛马匹进行疾病筛查。
