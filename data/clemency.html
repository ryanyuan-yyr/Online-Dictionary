clemency
klemənsi ˈklɛmənsi
N-UNCOUNT 宽大;轻判;宽恕 If someone is granted clemency, they are punished less severely than they could be. 宽大;轻判;宽恕 [formal]

  • Seventeen prisoners held on death row are to be executed after their pleas for clemency were turned down.

    17名死刑犯申请宽大处理遭到驳回后即将被处决。

SYN mercy, pity, humanity, compassion
