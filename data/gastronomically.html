gastronomic
gæstrənɒmɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 烹饪的；美食的 Gastronomic is used to describe things that are concerned with good food. 烹饪的；美食的 [formal]

  • Paris is the gastronomic capital of the world.

    巴黎是世界美食之都。

  • She is sampling gastronomic delights along the Riviera.

    她在里维埃拉一路品尝着美食。
