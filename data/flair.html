flair
fleəʳ fleɪr flɛr
1 N-SING 天资;天赋;天分 If you have a flair for a particular thing, you have a natural ability to do it well. 天资;天赋;天分

  • ...a friend who has a flair for languages. [+ for]

    有语言天赋的朋友

SYN ability, feel, talent, gift  
2 N-UNCOUNT 才华;资质;创意 If you have flair, you do things in an original, interesting, and stylish way. 才华;资质;创意 [approval]

  • Their work has all the usual punch, panache and flair you'd expect.

    他们的作品一如既往地引人入胜、气韵潇洒、新颖别致，不会让你失望。

SYN style, taste, dash, chic
