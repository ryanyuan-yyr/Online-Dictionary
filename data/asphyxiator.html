asphyxiate
æsfɪksieɪt æsˈfɪksiˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense asphyxiates, present participle asphyxiating, past tense , past participle asphyxiated
VERB [usually passive] 使窒息;使昏厥 If someone is asphyxiated, they die or lose consciousness because they are unable to breathe properly. 使窒息;使昏厥

  • Three people were asphyxiated in the crush for last week's train. [be VERB-ed]

    在上周的火车撞车事故中有3人窒息死亡。

  • John Doe died in his bath, asphyxiated by the fumes from a gas water-heater. [VERB-ed]

    一名男子死于浴室中，死因是吸入燃气热水器中排出的气体导致窒息。

SYN suffocate, choke, strangle, stifle  
asphyxiation

  • A post mortem examination found that she died from asphyxiation.

    尸检结果发现她死于窒息。
