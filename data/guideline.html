guideline
gaɪdlaɪn ˈgaɪdˌlaɪn
Word forms: plural guidelines
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 指导方针；指导原则；准则 If an organization issues guidelines on something, it issues official advice about how to do it. 指导方针；指导原则；准则

  • The government should issue clear guidelines on the content of religious education. [+ on]

    政府应当就宗教教育的内容颁布明确的指导方针。

  • The accord also lays down guidelines for the conduct of American drug enforcement agents. [+ for]

    该协议也为美国毒品稽查人员的行为制订了准则。

SYN recommendation, advice, proposal, direction  
2 N-COUNT 参考 A guideline is something that can be used to help you plan your actions or to form an opinion about something. 参考

  • The effects of the sun can be significantly reduced if we follow certain guidelines.

    如果我们遵循某些建议，日晒的影响会大大降低。

  • A written IQ test is merely a guideline.

    书面智商测试仅供参考。
