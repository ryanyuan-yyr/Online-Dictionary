wicketkeeper
wɪkɪtkiːpəʳ ˈwɪkɪtˌkiːpə ˈwɪkɪtˌkipər
(also wicket-keeper)Word forms: plural wicketkeepers
N-COUNT (板球运动中的)三柱门守门员，捕手 A wicketkeeper is the player in a cricket team who stands behind the wicket in order to stop balls that the batsman misses or to catch balls that the batsman hits. (板球运动中的)三柱门守门员，捕手
