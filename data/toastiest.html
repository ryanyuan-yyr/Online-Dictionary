toasty
toʊsti ˈtoʊsti
Word forms: comparative toastier, superlative toastiest
ADJ-GRADED 暖烘烘的;温暖舒适的 If something is toasty, it is comfortably warm. 暖烘烘的;温暖舒适的 [informal]

  • The heating system knows to make the temperature toasty on a cold morning before a guest is out of bed.

    在寒冷的早晨，供暖系统会在客人起床前把温度调得暖和舒适。
