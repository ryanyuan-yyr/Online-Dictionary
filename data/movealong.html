move along
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (尤指被警察告知)走开，别停留;命令…离开 If someone, especially a police officer, tells you to move along, or if they move you along, they tell you to stop standing in a particular place and to go somewhere else. (尤指被警察告知)走开，别停留;命令…离开 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Curious pedestrians were ordered to move along. [VERB PREP.]

    好奇的路人被责令离开。

  • Our officers are moving them along and not allowing them to gather in large groups. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们的警察正让他们离开，不允许他们大批集结。

2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）取得进展;（使）发展 If a process moves along or if something moves it along, it progresses. （使）取得进展;（使）发展

  • Research tends to move along at a slow but orderly pace. [VERB PREP.]

    研究虽进展缓慢，但井然有序。

  • Delay is part of the normal process, but I hope we can move things along. [VERB noun PREP.]

    虽然推迟是常事儿，但我希望我们能继续推进事情的进展。
