ludicrous
luːdɪkrəs ˈludɪkrəs
ADJ-GRADED 愚蠢的；荒唐的；可笑的 If you describe something as ludicrous, you are emphasizing that you think it is foolish, unreasonable, or unsuitable. 愚蠢的；荒唐的；可笑的 [emphasis]

  • It was ludicrous to suggest that the visit could be kept secret.

    认为此次访问能够保密是可笑的。

  • It's a completely ludicrous idea.

    这主意真是荒唐透顶。

SYN ridiculous, crazy [informal] , absurd, preposterous  
ludicrously

  • By Western standards the prices are ludicrously low.

    以西方标准来看，价格低得离谱。
