aftermath
ɑːftəʳmɑːθ æftəʳmæθ ˈɑːftəˌmɑːθ ˈæftərˌmæθ ˈɑftərˌmæθ
N-SING 后果；余波 The aftermath of an important event, especially a harmful one, is the situation that results from it. 后果；余波

  • In the aftermath of the coup, the troops opened fire on the demonstrators. [+ of]

    政变结果是军队向游行示威者开火了。

  • During the Soviet era and its immediate aftermath, the region was officially known as 'Middle Asia and Kazakhstan'.

    在苏联时代和紧随其后的一段时期，该地区的官方名称为“中亚和哈萨克斯坦”。

SYN effects, end, results, wake
