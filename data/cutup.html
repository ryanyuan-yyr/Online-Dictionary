cut up
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 不高兴的;伤心的 If you are cut up about something that has happened, you are very unhappy because of it. 不高兴的;伤心的 [mainly British, informal]

  • Terry was very cut up about Jim's death. [+ about]

    吉姆的死让特丽无比悲伤。

PHRASAL VERB 切碎If you cut something up, you cut it into several pieces. 切碎

  • Halve the tomatoes, then cut them up coarsely. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把西红柿切成两半，然后再把它们粗略切一下。
