doorway
dɔːʳweɪ ˈdɔrˌweɪ
Word forms: plural doorways
1 N-COUNT 出入口;门口;门道 A doorway is a space in a wall where a door opens and closes. 出入口;门口;门道

  • Hannah looked up to see David and another man standing in the doorway.

    汉娜抬头看见戴维和另一个男的站在门口。

  • We were escorted through a low doorway.

    我们在人陪同下穿过一条低矮的门道。

2 N-COUNT 门廊;（有遮顶的）门口处 A doorway is a covered space just outside the door of a building. 门廊;（有遮顶的）门口处

  • ...homeless people sleeping in shop doorways.

    睡在商店门口无家可归的人们
