swingeing
swɪndʒɪŋ ˈswɪndʒɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] (抨击等)尖锐的,猛烈的;(削减)大幅度的，严重的 A swingeing action, such as an attack or cut, is very great or severe. (抨击等)尖锐的,猛烈的;(削减)大幅度的，严重的 [British, mainly journalism]

  • ...the book mounted a swingeing attack on the materialist, growth-oriented economics of the day.

    该书对当今追逐实利、以增长为导向的经济学进行了猛烈的抨击

  • There have been swingeing cuts on government subsidies, on housing, food and fuel.

    政府已经大幅度削减了其在住房、食品和燃料方面的补贴。

SYN severe, heavy, drastic, huge
