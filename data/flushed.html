flushed
flʌʃt flʌʃt
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ with n] 兴奋的;激动的 If you say that someone is flushed with success or pride you mean that they are very excited by their success or pride. 兴奋的;激动的

  • Grace was flushed with the success of the venture.

    格雷斯为冒险成功而感到兴奋。

  • The publishers were flushed with triumph when they secured rights to her novel.

    出版商为争取到她的小说的出版权而兴奋不已。

SYN exhilarated, excited, aroused, elated
