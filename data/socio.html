socio- soʊsioʊ- ˈsoʊsioʊ soʊʃioʊ ˈsoʊsiə ˈsoʊʃiə
PREFIX (用于构成形容词和名词)表示“社会的”，“社会学的” Socio- is used to form adjectives and nouns which describe or refer to things relating to or involving social factors. (用于构成形容词和名词)表示“社会的”，“社会学的”

  • Fernandez studied the socioeconomic backgrounds of new recruits.

    费尔南德斯研究了新成员的社会经济背景。
