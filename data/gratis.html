gratis
grætɪs grɑːt- ˈɡreɪtɪs ˈgrætɪs ˈgrɑtɪs ˈgreɪtɪs
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 免费地；无偿地 If something is done or provided gratis, it does not have to be paid for. 免费地；无偿地

  • David gives the first consultation gratis.

    戴维免费提供初次咨询。

Gratis is also an adjective.

  • What I did for you was free, gratis, you understand?

    我为你所做的一切都是免费的，无偿的，你明白吗？
