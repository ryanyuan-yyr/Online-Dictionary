warmth
wɔːʳmθ wɔrmθ
1 N-UNCOUNT 温度;温暖;热度 The warmth of something is the heat that it has or produces. 温度;温暖;热度

  • She went further into the room, drawn by the warmth of the fire. [+ of]

    暖暖的炉火吸引着她继续向前走进那间屋子。

  • June had brought with it the first of the summer warmth.

    六月带来了夏日的第一缕热气。

SYN heat, snugness, warmness, comfort  
2 N-UNCOUNT 保暖 The warmth of something such as a garment or blanket is the protection that it gives you against the cold. 保暖

  • The blanket will provide additional warmth and comfort in bed.

    毛毯能让你睡得更温暖、更舒适。

3 N-UNCOUNT 热情;亲切；友好 Someone who has warmth is friendly and enthusiastic in their behaviour towards other people. 热情;亲切；友好

  • He greeted us both with warmth and affection.

    他热情且友好地迎接了我们俩。

SYN affection, feeling, love, goodwill
