management
mænɪdʒmənt ˈmænɪdʒmənt
Word forms: plural managements
1 N-UNCOUNT （商务或机构的）管理，经营 Management is the control and organizing of a business or other organization. （商务或机构的）管理，经营

  • The zoo needed better management rather than more money.

    这座动物园需要更好的管理，而不是更多的资金。

  • The dispute is about wages, working conditions and the management of the mining industry. [+ of]

    这场争论围绕着工资、工作条件以及矿业管理展开。

  • ...the responsibility for its day to day management.

    负责日常管理

  • ...having just completed a management studies course.

    刚刚完成一门管理学课程

SYN administration, control, rule, government  
2 N-VAR-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 资方;管理部门;经营者 You can refer to the people who control and organize a business or other organization as the management . 资方;管理部门;经营者 [business]

  • The management is doing its best to improve the situation.

    管理部门正在竭尽全力改善局面。

  • We need to get more women into top management.

    我们需要让更多的女性进入高层管理。

  • A change of management would help.

    管理层方面的变化会有帮助。

SYN directors, board, executive(s), bosses [informal]  
3 N-UNCOUNT 管理;安排;驾驭 Management is the way people control different parts of their lives. 管理;安排;驾驭

  • ...her management of her professional life. [+ of]

    她对自己职业生涯的规划

  • ...intelligent money management, for example paying big bills monthly where possible.

    聪明的理财手法，例如尽量按月支付大宗的账单

  • ...the secret of time management.

    管理时间的诀窍
