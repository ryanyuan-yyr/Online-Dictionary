astringent
əstrɪndʒənt əˈstrɪndʒənt əˈstrɪndʒənt
Word forms: plural astringents
1 N-COUNT 紧肤水;收敛剂;止血剂 An astringent is a liquid that you put on your skin to make it less oily or to make cuts stop bleeding. 紧肤水;收敛剂;止血剂
Astringent is also an adjective.

  • ...an astringent lotion.

    紧肤水

2 ADJ-GRADED 严厉的；尖刻的 Astringent means forceful and critical. 严厉的；尖刻的

  • Sunset Boulevard was originally conceived by Wilder as an astringent satire on Hollywood.

    怀尔德最初是想把《日落大道》拍成一部对好莱坞的辛辣讽刺片。

3 ADJ-GRADED 味道浓烈的;味涩的 If something has an astringent taste, it is sharp or bitter. 味道浓烈的;味涩的 [formal]

  • The fruit has a tart and astringent flavour.

    这种水果又酸又涩。
