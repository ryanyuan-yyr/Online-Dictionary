accused
əkjuːzd
Word forms: plural accused
N-COUNT 被告 You can use the accused to refer to a person or a group of people charged with a crime or on trial for it. 被告 [law]

  • The accused is alleged to be a member of a right-wing gang.

    据称被告是某右翼组织的成员。

  • The fifteen accused, young men from different parts of England, denied the charges.

    被告是15名来自英格兰各地的年轻男子，他们对指控矢口否认。
