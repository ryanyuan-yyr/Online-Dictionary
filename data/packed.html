packed
pækt
1 ADJ-GRADED 非常拥挤的 A place that is packed is very crowded. 非常拥挤的

  • From 3.30 until 7pm, the shop is packed.

    从下午3点30分到7点，那家商店非常拥挤。

  • ...a packed meeting at Westminster.

    在威斯敏斯特召开的与会人数众多的会议

  • The streets were packed with men, women and children. [+ with]

    街上挤满了男男女女和孩子们。

SYN filled, full, crowded, jammed  
2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ with n] 充满…的；富含…的 Something that is packed with things contains a very large number of them. 充满…的；富含…的

  • The Encyclopedia is packed with clear illustrations and over 250 recipes. [+ with]

    那本百科全书有很多清晰的插图和250多个食谱。

  • Fish and chips are packed with protein.

    炸鱼薯条富含蛋白质。
