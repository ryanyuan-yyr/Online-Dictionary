airbrush
eəʳbrʌʃ ˈɛəˌbrʌʃ ˈɛrˌbrʌʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense airbrushes, present participle airbrushing, past tense , past participle airbrushed
1 N-COUNT (绘画等用的）喷枪，气笔 An airbrush is an artist's tool which sprays paint onto a surface. (绘画等用的）喷枪，气笔
2 VERB 用喷枪修饰，用气笔修改（相片等） To airbrush a photograph or other image means to change it using an airbrush, especially to make it more beautiful or perfect. 用喷枪修饰，用气笔修改（相片等） [Also VERB n]

  • ...perfect airbrushed bodies. [VERB-ed]

    用喷枪修饰过的完美躯体

  • ...bits of photographs cut, pasted and then airbrushed to create a convincing whole. [VERB-ed]

    把照片的碎片裁剪好后粘贴在一起，然后用气笔修成一个令人信以为真的整体图片
