killer instinct
ˈkɪləʳ ˈɪnstɪŋkt
Word forms: plural killer instincts
N-VAR （运动员或政客的）争强好胜的本能 If you say that a sports player or politician has the killer instinct, you admire them for their toughness and determination to succeed. （运动员或政客的）争强好胜的本能 [approval]
