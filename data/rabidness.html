rabid
ræbɪd reɪb- ˈræbɪd ˈræbɪd ˈreɪbɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （尤指政治上）狂暴的，偏激的，固执的 You can use rabid to describe someone who has very strong and unreasonable opinions or beliefs about a subject, especially in politics. （尤指政治上）狂暴的，偏激的，固执的 [disapproval]

  • The party has distanced itself from the more rabid nationalist groups in the country.

    此党派与国内较偏激的民族主义团体保持了距离。

rabidly

  • Mead calls the group 'rabidly right-wing'.

    米德把该团体称为“激进右翼团体”。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 患狂犬病的 A rabid dog or other animal has the disease rabies. 患狂犬病的
