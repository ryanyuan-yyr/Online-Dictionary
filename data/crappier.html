crappy
kræpi ˈkræpɪ
Word forms: comparative crappier, superlative crappiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 劣质的,蹩脚的(具冒犯意味) If you describe something as crappy, you think it is of very poor quality. Many people consider this word offensive. 劣质的,蹩脚的(具冒犯意味) [informal, disapproval]

  • ...reading a crappy detective novel.

    读一本蹩脚的侦探小说
