wooden
wʊdən ˈwʊdən
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 木质的;木制的 Wooden objects are made of wood. 木质的;木制的

  • ...the shop's bare brick walls and faded wooden floorboards.

    商店裸露的砖墙和褪色的木地板

SYN made of wood, timber, woody, of wood  
2 ADJ-GRADED (演员)呆板的，死板的，无活力的 If you describe an actor as wooden, you are critical of them because their performance is not at all lively or natural. (演员)呆板的，死板的，无活力的 [disapproval]
