tax break
tæks breɪk
Word forms: plural tax breaks
N-COUNT 税收优惠;减税 If the government gives a tax break to a particular group of people or type of organization, it reduces the amount of tax they have to pay or changes the tax system in a way that benefits them. 税收优惠;减税 [mainly US]

  • Today they'll consider tax breaks for businesses that create jobs in inner cities. [+ for]

    现在，他们会考虑给那些在市中心创造了就业机会的企业提供减税优惠。
