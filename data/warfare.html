warfare
wɔːʳfeəʳ ˈwɔːˌfɛə ˈwɔrˌfɛr
1 N-UNCOUNT 作战;战争 Warfare is the activity of fighting a war. 作战;战争

  • ...the threat of chemical warfare.

    化学战的威胁

2 N-UNCOUNT 打架;冲突;斗争 Warfare is sometimes used to refer to any violent struggle or conflict. 打架;冲突;斗争

  • Much of the violence is related to drugs and gang warfare.

    大多数暴力事件都与吸毒和群殴有关。

  • At times party rivalries have broken out into open warfare.

    政党间的矛盾有时爆发为公开的冲突。
