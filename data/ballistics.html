ballistics
bəlɪstɪks bəˈlɪstɪks
N-UNCOUNT 弹道学；发射学 Ballistics is the study of the movement of objects that are shot or thrown through the air, such as bullets fired from a gun. 弹道学；发射学

  • The weapons are undergoing ballistics tests to see whether they were used in the shootings.

    这些武器正在接受弹道测试，以确定它们是否曾用于枪击案中。
