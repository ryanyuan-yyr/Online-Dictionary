non-specific
(also nonspecific)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (病况、症状等)非特异性的，有多种致病原因的 Non-specific medical conditions or symptoms have more than one possible cause. (病况、症状等)非特异性的，有多种致病原因的

  • ...non-specific headaches.

    非特异性的头痛

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不明确的;非特定的 Something that is non-specific is general rather than precise or exact. 不明确的;非特定的

  • I intend to use these terms in a deliberately non-specific and all-embracing way.

    我打算有意含糊笼统地使用这些词语。
