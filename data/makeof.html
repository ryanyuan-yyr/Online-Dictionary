make of
PHRASAL VERB 理解;看待 If you ask a person what they make of something, you want to know what their impression, opinion, or understanding of it is. 理解;看待

  • Well, what did you make of her?. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那么，你认为她怎么样？

  • Nancy wasn't sure what to make of Mick's apology. [VERB PREP. noun]

    南希不知应该如何理解米克的道歉。
