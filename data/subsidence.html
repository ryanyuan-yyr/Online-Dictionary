subsidence
səbsaɪdəns sʌbsɪdəns səbˈsaɪdəns ˈsʌbsɪdənsɪ səbˈsaɪdənsɪ
N-UNCOUNT 下沉;沉降;下陷 When there is subsidence in a place, the ground there sinks to a lower level. 下沉;沉降;下陷

  • A surveyor said that the problems were caused by subsidence and the house needed to be underpinned.

    一名勘测员说是地面下陷导致了这些问题出现，房子需要加固地基。
