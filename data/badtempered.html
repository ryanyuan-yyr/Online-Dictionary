bad-tempered
ˈbædˌtɛmpərd
ADJ-GRADED 脾气不好的；易怒的 Someone who is bad-tempered is not very cheerful and gets angry easily. →see usage note at: angry 脾气不好的；易怒的

  • When his headaches developed Nick became bad-tempered and even violent.

    尼克出现头痛时脾气就变得很差，甚至还会动粗。

  • They walked in bad-tempered silence.

    他们心情很糟，一言不发地走着。
