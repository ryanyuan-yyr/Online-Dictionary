mode
moʊd moʊd
Word forms: plural modes
1 N-COUNT 方法;做法;方式 A mode of life or behaviour is a particular way of living or behaving. 方法;做法;方式 [formal]

  • ...the capitalist mode of production.

    资本主义生产方式

  • He switched automatically into interview mode.

    他自动切换到访谈模式。

SYN method, way, plan, course  
2 N-COUNT （艺术、着装等的）风格、文体 A mode is a particular style in art, literature, or dress. （艺术、着装等的）风格、文体

  • ...a slightly more elegant and formal mode of dress.

    略显典雅和正式的着装风格

  • Levi is best known for work in a very different mode from what is to be found here.

    利瓦伊最为人所知之处在于他的工作风格同这里的人迥然不同。

3 N-COUNT (照相机、电子设备的)模式，状态 On some cameras or electronic devices, the different modes available are the different programs or settings that you can choose when you use them. (照相机、电子设备的)模式，状态

  • ...when the camera is in manual mode.

    当相机处于手动拍摄模式的时候

SYN function, position, role, operation
