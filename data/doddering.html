doddering
dɒdərɪŋ ˈdɑdərɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 年老体衰的，老态龙钟的(对人不敬的说法) If you refer to someone as a doddering old man or woman, you are saying in a disrespectful way that they are old and not strong. 年老体衰的，老态龙钟的(对人不敬的说法) [disapproval]

  • ...a doddering old man making his will before he's too senile.

    趁尚未老糊涂之前立下遗嘱的年迈老者

SYN tottering, aged, weak, shaky
