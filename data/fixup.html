fix up
1 PHRASAL VERB 修整If you fix something up, you do work that is necessary in order to make it more suitable or attractive. 修整 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I've fixed up Matthew's old room. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我已经把马修的旧房间修缮好了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 为…准备If you fix someone up with something they need, you provide it for them. 为…准备 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We'll fix him up with a tie. [V n P with n]

    我们会为他准备一条领带。

SYN provide, supply, accommodate, bring about  
3 PHRASAL VERB 安排If you fix something up, you arrange it. 安排 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]
SYN arrange, plan, settle, fix
