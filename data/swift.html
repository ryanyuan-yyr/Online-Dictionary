swift
swɪft swɪft
Word forms: comparative swifter, superlative swiftest, plural swifts
1 ADJ-GRADED 快的;迅速的 A swift event or process happens very quickly or without delay. 快的;迅速的

  • Our task is to challenge the UN to make a swift decision.

    我们的任务是敦促联合国快速做出决策。

  • The police were swift to act.

    警方行动迅速。

SYN quick, immediate, prompt, rapid  
swiftly

  • The French have acted swiftly and decisively to protect their industries.

    法国人采取了迅速而果断的行动来保护他们的产业。

swiftness

  • The secrecy and swiftness of the invasion shocked and amazed army officers.

    这次入侵的隐秘和迅速令陆军军官们大为震惊。

2 ADJ-GRADED 快速移动的;速度非常快的 Something that is swift moves very quickly. 快速移动的;速度非常快的

  • With a swift movement, Matthew Jerrold sat upright.

    马修· 杰罗尔德迅速坐直了。

swiftly

  • Lenny moved swiftly and silently across the front lawn.

    伦尼悄无声息地快速穿过房前的草坪。

  • ...a swiftly flowing stream.

    湍急的小溪

swiftness

  • With incredible swiftness she ran down the passage.

    她沿通道飞奔而去。

3 N-COUNT 雨燕 A swift is a small bird with long curved wings. 雨燕
