peacenik
piːsnɪk ˈpiːsnɪk pisnɪk
Word forms: plural peaceniks
N-COUNT 和平主义者；反战分子 If you describe someone as a peacenik, you mean that they are strongly opposed to war. 和平主义者；反战分子 [informal]

  • His campaign attracted the support of feminists, peaceniks and ecologists.

    他发起的运动得到了女权主义者、反战分子和生态学家的支持。
