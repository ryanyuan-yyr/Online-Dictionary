impeccable
ɪmpekəbəl ɪmˈpɛkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 无瑕疵的;无可挑剔的 If you describe something such as someone's behaviour or appearance as impeccable, you are emphasizing that it is perfect and has no faults. 无瑕疵的;无可挑剔的 [emphasis]

  • She had impeccable taste in clothes.

    她的穿衣品位无可挑剔。

  • Her academic credentials are impeccable.

    她的学术背景无可挑剔。

SYN faultless, perfect, pure, exact  
impeccably

  • He was impeccably polite.

    他彬彬有礼,举止无可指摘。
