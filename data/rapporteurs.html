rapporteur
ræpɔːʳtɜːʳ ˌræpɔːˈtɜː ˌræpɔrˈtɜr
Word forms: plural rapporteurs
N-COUNT 汇报人;报告人 A rapporteur is a person who is officially appointed by an organization to investigate a problem or attend a meeting and to report on it. 汇报人;报告人 [formal]

  • ...the United Nations special rapporteur on torture.

    联合国关于刑讯逼供的特别报告员

  • ...UN human rights rapporteurs.

    联合国人权报告员
