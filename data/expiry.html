expiry
ɪkspaɪəri ɪkˈspaɪərɪ ɛkˈspaɪri ˈɛkspəri
N-UNCOUNT [NOUN n] （合同、最终期限、签证等的）期满，到期 The expiry of something such as a contract, deadline, or visa is the time that it comes to an end or stops being valid. （合同、最终期限、签证等的）期满，到期

  • ...the expiry of a fixed term contract. [+ of]

    定期合同的到期

  • Make a note of credit card numbers and check expiry dates.

    记下信用卡卡号，核对有效期限。

SYN expiration, ending, end, conclusion
