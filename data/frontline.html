front line
(also front-line)Word forms: plural front lines
1 N-COUNT (战争中的)前线，前方 The front line is the place where two opposing armies are facing each other and where fighting is going on. (战争中的)前线，前方

  • ...a massive concentration of soldiers on the front line.

    在前线大规模的兵力集结

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (国家)位于战争(或冲突)前线的，与敌对国毗邻的 A front line state shares a border with a country that it is at war with or is in conflict with. (国家)位于战争(或冲突)前线的，与敌对国毗邻的

  • ...the front-line states bordering South Africa.

    和南非接壤的前线国家

3 PHRASE 处于第一线;居于极重要岗位 Someone who is in the front line has to play a very important part in defending or achieving something. 处于第一线;居于极重要岗位

  • Information officers are in the front line of putting across government policies.

    新闻发言官处于传达政府政策的第一线。
