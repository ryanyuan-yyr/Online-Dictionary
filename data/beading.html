beading
biːdɪŋ ˈbidɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （家具或门上的）装饰木条 Beading is a narrow strip of wood that is used for decorating or edging furniture and doors. （家具或门上的）装饰木条
2 N-UNCOUNT （衣服上的）串珠饰物 Beading is an arrangement of beads used for decorating clothes. （衣服上的）串珠饰物

  • ...a black velvet bodice with jet black beading.
