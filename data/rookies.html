rookie
rʊki ˈrʊkɪ ˈrʊki
Word forms: plural rookies
1 N-COUNT 新手;菜鸟;（尤指）新兵，新警员 A rookie is someone who has just started doing a job and does not have much experience, especially someone who has just joined the army or police force. 新手;菜鸟;（尤指）新兵，新警员 [mainly US, informal]

  • I don't want to have another rookie to train.

    我不想再训练新兵了。

  • ...a rookie police officer.

    新上任的警官

2 N-COUNT (第一年参加体育比赛的)新队员 A rookie is a person who has been competing in a professional sport for less than a year. (第一年参加体育比赛的)新队员 [US]
