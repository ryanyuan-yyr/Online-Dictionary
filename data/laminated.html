laminated
læmɪneɪtɪd ˈlæmɪˌneɪtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 由薄片叠成的；层压的；夹层的 Material such as wood or plastic that is laminated consists of several thin sheets or layers that are stuck together. 由薄片叠成的；层压的；夹层的

  • Modern windscreens are made from laminated glass.

    现在的挡风玻璃都由夹层玻璃制成。

SYN layered, stratified, foliated  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 覆有薄片护层的；（尤指）覆有透明（或有色）塑料薄膜的 A product that is laminated is covered with a thin sheet of something, especially clear or coloured plastic, in order to protect it. 覆有薄片护层的；（尤指）覆有透明（或有色）塑料薄膜的

  • The photographs were mounted on laminated cards.

    照片镶在压膜卡片上。

  • ...laminated work surfaces.

    贴了膜的工作台

SYN covered, coated, overlaid, veneered
