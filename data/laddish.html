laddish
lædɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 典型年轻人的；年轻放浪的；年少不羁的 If you describe someone as laddish, you mean that they behave in a way that people think is typical of young men, for example by being rough and noisy, drinking a lot of alcohol, and having a bad attitude towards women. 典型年轻人的；年轻放浪的；年少不羁的 [disapproval]

  • Their manager is unconcerned at the laddish image and the drinking that goes with it.

    经纪人对他们年轻放浪的形象和相伴而来的恣意纵酒的行为毫不担心。

laddishness

  • These young women are not threatened by laddishness. They think it is funny.

    这些年轻姑娘没被小伙子们的粗野吓着，反倒觉得好玩。
