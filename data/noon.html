noon
nuːn nun
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft preposition NOUN] 正午;中午 Noon is twelve o'clock in the middle of the day. 正午;中午

  • The long day of meetings started at noon.

    会议接连不断的漫长一天从中午开始。

  • Our branches are open from 9am to 5pm during the week and until 12 noon on Saturdays.

    我们的分行每周一至周五上午9点到下午5点营业，周六营业到中午12点。

SYN midday, high noon, noonday, noontime  
2See also:high noon 
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 正午的;中午的 Noon means happening or appearing in the middle part of the day. 正午的;中午的

  • The noon sun was fierce.

    正午的阳光火辣辣的。

  • He expected the transfer to go through by today's noon deadline.

    他希望转账在今天中午的最后期限之前完成。

SYN midday, noonday, noontime, noontide
