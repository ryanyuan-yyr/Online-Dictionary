reimbursement
riːɪmbɜːʳsmənt
Word forms: plural reimbursements
N-VAR 赔付;偿付;赔偿 If you receive reimbursement for money that you have spent, you get your money back, for example because the money should have been paid by someone else. 赔付;偿付;赔偿 [formal]

  • She is demanding reimbursement for medical and other expenses. [+ for]

    她正在索赔医药费和其他花费。

  • It can take up to six months before reimbursements are paid.

    最长要等6个月才能得到赔付。

SYN repayment, compensation, refund, recompense
