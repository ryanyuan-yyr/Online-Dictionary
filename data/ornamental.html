ornamental
ɔːʳnəmentəl ˌɔrnəˈmɛntəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （摆在某处）作装饰的 Ornamental things have no practical function but are put in a place because they look attractive. （摆在某处）作装饰的

  • ...ornamental trees.

    观赏树

2 ADJ-GRADED 装饰性的；美观的 Something that is ornamental is attractive and decorative. 装饰性的；美观的

  • ...ornamental plaster mouldings.

    装饰性石膏线脚

SYN decorative, pretty, attractive, fancy
