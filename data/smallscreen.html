small screen
N-SING 电视(相对于电影而言) When people talk about the small screen, they are referring to television, in contrast to films that are made for the cinema. 电视(相对于电影而言)

  • Now he is also to become a star of the small screen.

    现在他也即将成为一个电视明星。

  • Live concerts are never quite the same on the small screen.

    电视转播的音乐会与现场音乐会不可同日而语。
