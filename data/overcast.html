overcast
oʊvəʳkɑːst -kæst ˈoʊvərˌkæst ˌ oʊvərˈkæst
ADJ 多云的；阴沉的；阴天的 If it is overcast, or if the sky or the day is overcast, the sky is completely covered with cloud and there is not much light. 多云的；阴沉的；阴天的

  • For three days it was overcast.

    连续3天都是阴天。

  • The weather forecast is for showers and overcast skies.

    天气预报上说多云并伴有阵雨。

  • It was a cold, windy, overcast afternoon in Washington.

    这是华盛顿一个寒风料峭、天空阴沉的下午。

SYN cloudy, grey, dull, threatening
