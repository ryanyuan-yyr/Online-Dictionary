sappy
sæpi ˈsæpi
1 ADJ-GRADED (茎、叶)多汁的，汁液丰富的 Sappy stems or leaves contain a lot of liquid. (茎、叶)多汁的，汁液丰富的

  • Do not overfeed them, as this will encourage soft sappy growth.

    不要给它们施肥过多，以防其长得柔软多汁。

2 ADJ-GRADED 愚昧的;愚蠢的 If you describe someone or something as sappy, you think they are foolish. 愚昧的;愚蠢的 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • I wrote this sappy love song.

    我写了这首相当花痴的爱情歌曲。
