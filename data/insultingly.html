insulting
ɪnsʌltɪŋ ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 侮辱的;侮慢的;无礼的 Something that is insulting is rude or offensive. 侮辱的;侮慢的;无礼的

  • The article was insulting to the families of British citizens. [+ to]

    那篇文章侮辱了英国公民家庭。

  • One of the apprentices made an insulting remark to a passing officer.

    其中一名学徒对着一名路过的警官说了一句侮辱他的话。

SYN offensive, rude, abusive, slighting  
insultingly

  • Anthony laughed loudly and insultingly.

    安东尼无礼地放声大笑。

  • I have rarely read anything so insultingly sexist as this article.

    我很少读到像这篇文章般带有侮辱性的性别歧视的东西。
