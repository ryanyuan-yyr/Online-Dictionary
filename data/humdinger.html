humdinger
hʌmdɪŋəʳ ˈhʌmˈdɪŋər
Word forms: plural humdingers
N-COUNT [usually singular, oft a N of n] 非常出色的人（或事物）;令人兴奋的人（或事物） If you describe someone or something as a humdinger, you mean that they are very impressive, exciting, or enjoyable. 非常出色的人（或事物）;令人兴奋的人（或事物） [informal, approval]

  • It should be a humdinger of a match. [+ of]

    这应该是一场非常精彩的比赛。

  • His latest novel is a humdinger.

    他最新的一部小说棒极了。
