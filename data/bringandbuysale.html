bring-and-buy sale
Word forms: plural bring-and-buy sales
N-COUNT （为慈善机构或其他组织筹款而举行的）义卖，义捐义卖 A bring-and-buy sale is an informal sale to raise money for a charity or other organization. People who come to the sale bring things to be sold and buy things that other people have brought. （为慈善机构或其他组织筹款而举行的）义卖，义捐义卖 [British]
