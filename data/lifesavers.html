lifesaver laɪfseɪvəʳ ˈlaɪfˌseɪvər
Word forms: plural lifesavers
N-COUNT （常指在健康方面）帮助解除困境之物 If you say that something is a lifesaver, you mean that it helps people in a very important way, often in a way that is important to their health. （常指在健康方面）帮助解除困境之物

  • The cervical smear test is a lifesaver.

    宫颈涂片检查能够帮上大忙。
