unwind
ʌnwaɪnd ʌnˈwaɪnd ʌnˈwaɪnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unwinds, present participle unwinding, past tense , past participle unwound
1 VERB 放松；减压 When you unwind, you relax after you have done something that makes you tense or tired. 放松；减压

  • It helps them to unwind after a busy day at work. [VERB]

    这有助于他们忙碌一天后放松自己。

  • Singing is a nice way of unwinding. [VERB]

    唱歌是一种不错的减压方法。

SYN relax, wind down, take it easy, slow down  
2 V-ERG 解开；展开 If you unwind a length of something that is wrapped round something else or round itself, you loosen it and make it straight. You can also say that it unwinds . 解开；展开

  • One of them unwound a length of rope from around his waist. [VERB noun]

    其中一人把绑在腰间的一条绳子解下来。

  • The thread unwound a little more. [VERB]

    线又松开了一些。

SYN unravel, undo, uncoil, slacken
