outfox
aʊtfɒks ˈaʊtˈfɑks
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outfoxes, present participle outfoxing, past tense , past participle outfoxed
VERB 智胜；谋略上比…更胜一筹 If you outfox someone, you defeat them in some way because you are cleverer than they are. 智胜；谋略上比…更胜一筹

  • There is no greater thrill than to bluff a man, trap him and outfox him. [VERB noun]

    虚张声势吓唬别人，让他钻入圈套，用计将他击败，没有比这更刺激的事情了。
