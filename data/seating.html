seating
siːtɪŋ ˈsitɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT (总称)座椅，座位 You can refer to the seats in a place as the seating . (总称)座椅，座位

  • The stadium has been fitted with seating for over eighty thousand spectators. [+ for]

    这个体育场配备了8万多个观众座席。

SYN accommodation, room, places, seats  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] (公共场所、正式场合的)座位安排(或设置) The seating at a public place or a formal occasion is the arrangement of where people will sit. (公共场所、正式场合的)座位安排(或设置)

  • She checked the seating arrangements before the guests filed into the dining-room.

    在客人们鱼贯进入餐厅之前，她核实了一遍座位安排。
