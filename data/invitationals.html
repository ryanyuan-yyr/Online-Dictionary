invitational
ɪnvɪteɪʃənəl ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃənəl ˌɪnvəˈteɪʃənəl
Word forms: invitationals
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 邀请的An invitational tournament or event is a sports competition in which only players who have been asked to take part can compete. 邀请的

  • After all, The Masters is an invitational tournament.

    毕竟，这项大师赛是一场邀请赛。

2 N-COUNT 邀请赛Invitational is also a noun. 邀请赛

  • ...a 59-team invitational for field and running events.

    ...一场59支队伍参加的田径邀请赛。
