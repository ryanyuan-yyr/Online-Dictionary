drop off
1 →see: drop 9 
2 PHRASAL VERB 打瞌睡;睡着 If you drop off to sleep, you go to sleep. 打瞌睡;睡着 [informal]

  • I must have dropped off to sleep. [V P + to sleep]

    我肯定是睡着了。

  • Just as I was dropping off, a strange thought crossed my mind. [VERB PREP.]

    就在我打瞌睡的时候,一个奇怪的念头闪过我的脑海。

3 PHRASAL VERB 减退;减少 If the level of something drops off, it becomes less. 减退;减少

  • Sales to the British forces are expected to drop off. [VERB PREP.]

    预计对英国军队的销售量会减少。

  • The daily toll of casualties has dropped off sharply. [VERB PREP.]

    每日伤亡人数已经大大减少。

SYN decrease, lower, decline, shrink
