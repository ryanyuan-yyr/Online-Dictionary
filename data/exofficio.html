ex officio

ADJ [ADJ n] 依据职权的;出于职权的;（由于职位而）理所当然的 Ex officio is used to describe something such as a rank or privilege that someone is entitled to because of the job or position they have. 依据职权的;出于职权的;（由于职位而）理所当然的 [formal]

    顾问委员会理所当然的委员

  • ...an ex-officio degree.

    依据职权而拥有的学位
