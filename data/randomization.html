randomize
rændəmaɪz ˈrændəmˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense randomizes, present participle randomizing, past tense , past participle randomized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 randomise
VERB 使随机化;任意排列 If you randomize the events or people in scientific experiments or academic research, you use a method that gives them all an equal chance of happening or being chosen. 使随机化;任意排列 [technical]

  • The wheel is designed with obstacles in the ball's path to randomise its movement. [VERB noun]

    设计轮盘时在小球的行进路线上设置了障碍，以使其运动随机化。

  • Properly randomized studies are only now being completed. [VERB-ed]

    真正的随机研究现在才接近完成。
