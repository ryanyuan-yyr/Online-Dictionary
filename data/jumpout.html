jump out
PHRASAL VERB 极易引起注意 If you say that something jumps out at you, you mean that it is easy to notice it because it is different from other things of its type. 极易引起注意 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • A phrase jumped out at me in a piece about copyright. [VERB PREP. + at]

    在一篇有关版权的文章中，有一个短语引起了我的注意。
