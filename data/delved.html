delve
delv dɛlv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense delves, present participle delving, past tense , past participle delved
1 VERB 探究;钻研 If you delve into something, you try to discover new information about it. 探究;钻研

  • Tormented by her ignorance, Jenny delves into her mother's past. [VERB + into]

    珍妮为自己的一无所知而苦恼，开始探究起母亲的过去。

  • If you're interested in a subject, use the Internet to delve deeper. [VERB adv.]

    如果对某一主题感兴趣，就上网进一步深入研究。

SYN research, investigate, explore, examine  
2 VERB (在壁橱、袋子等中)搜寻，翻找 If you delve inside something such as a cupboard or a bag, you search inside it. (在壁橱、袋子等中)搜寻，翻找 [written]

  • She delved into her rucksack and pulled out a folder. [VERB prep./adv.]

    她在背包里翻找一番后拿出了一个文件夹。

SYN rummage, search, look, burrow
