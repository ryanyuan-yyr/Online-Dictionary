delayed action
ADJ [ADJ n] (机械装置等)延迟的，延时的，定时的 A delayed action mechanism causes a delay on the device it is fitted to, so that it does not work as soon as you switch it on or operate it. (机械装置等)延迟的，延时的，定时的
