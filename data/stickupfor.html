stick up for
PHRASAL VERB 捍卫;维护 If you stick up for a person or a principle, you support or defend them forcefully. 捍卫;维护

  • Dad spoils me. He loves me. He sticks up for me. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    爸爸很宠我，很爱我。他总是护着我。

  • I can stick up for myself. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我能捍卫自己的权利。

  • He has shown a great deal of courage in sticking up for democracy and civil liberties. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他在捍卫民主和公民自由上表现出巨大的勇气。

SYN defend, support, champion, stand by
