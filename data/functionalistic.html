N-UNCOUNT (尤指建筑和家具的)实用主义，功能主义 Functionalism is the idea that the most important aspect of something, especially the design of a building or piece of furniture, is how it is going to be used or its usefulness. (尤指建筑和家具的)实用主义，功能主义 [technical]
