avenge
əvendʒ əˈvɛndʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense avenges, present participle avenging, past tense , past participle avenged
VERB 报…之仇；雪…之耻 If you avenge a wrong or harmful act, you hurt or punish the person who is responsible for it. 报…之仇；雪…之耻

  • He has devoted the past five years to avenging his daughter's death. [VERB noun]

    他过去5年一心报丧女之仇。

  • She had decided to avenge herself and all the other women he had abused. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    她决定为自己和所有其他被他侮辱的女性报仇。

SYN get revenge for, revenge, repay, retaliate for  
avenger

  • He saw himself as a Master of the Universe, an avenger of wrongs.
