ahold
əhoʊld əˈhəʊld əˈhoʊld
1 PHRASE 联系到；找到；得到 If you get ahold of someone or something, you manage to contact, find, or get them. 联系到；找到；得到 [US, informal]

  • I tried again to get ahold of my cousin Joan.

    我再次尝试联系我的表妹琼。

2 PHRASE （受惊吓或遇到困难之后）保持镇静，保持理智 If you get ahold of yourself, you force yourself to become calm and sensible after a shock or in a difficult situation. （受惊吓或遇到困难之后）保持镇静，保持理智 [US, informal]

  • I'm going to have to get ahold of myself.

    我一定要保持头脑冷静。
