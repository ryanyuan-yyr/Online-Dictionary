temperate
tempərɪt ˈtɛmpərɪt ˈtɛmprɪt
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (气候)温和的;(地区)温带的 Temperate is used to describe a climate or a place which is never extremely hot or extremely cold. (气候)温和的;(地区)温带的

  • The Nile Valley keeps a temperate climate throughout the year.

    尼罗河流域一年四季气候温和。

SYN mild, moderate, balmy, fair  
2 ADJ-GRADED (行为)温和的，心平气和的，平心静气的 If a person's behaviour is temperate, it is calm and reasonable, so that they do not get angry or lose their temper easily. (行为)温和的，心平气和的，平心静气的 [formal]

  • His final report to the President was far more temperate and balanced than the earlier memorandum.

    和之前的备忘录相比，他递交给总统的总结报告措辞要更为温和得体。

SYN moderate, dispassionate, self-controlled, calm
