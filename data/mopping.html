mop
mɒp mɑp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense mops, present participle mopping, past tense , past participle mopped
1 N-COUNT 拖把 A mop is a piece of equipment for washing floors. It consists of a sponge or many pieces of string attached to a long handle. 拖把
2 VERB 用拖把擦(地板) If you mop a surface such as a floor, you clean it with a mop. 用拖把擦(地板)

  • There was a woman mopping the stairs. [VERB noun]

    一个女人在拖楼梯。

SYN clean, wash, wipe, sponge  
3 VERB (用布)擦去，揩干(汗水) If you mop sweat from your forehead or mop your forehead, you wipe it with a piece of cloth. (用布)擦去，揩干(汗水)

  • He mopped perspiration from his forehead. [VERB noun + from]

    他擦去了前额的汗水。

  • The Inspector took out a handkerchief and mopped his brow. [VERB noun]

    检察员掏出手帕，擦了擦眉头。

4 N-COUNT 乱蓬蓬的头发;蓬乱不洁的头发 If someone has a mop of hair, they have a lot of hair and it looks rather untidy. 乱蓬蓬的头发;蓬乱不洁的头发

  • He was long-limbed and dark-eyed, with a mop of tight, dark curls. [+ of]

    他四肢修长、眼睛乌黑发亮，留着一头蓬乱浓密的黑色卷发。

SYN mane, shock, mass, tangle
