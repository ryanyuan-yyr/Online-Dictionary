bowling
boʊlɪŋ ˈboʊlɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 保龄球运动 Bowling is a game in which you roll a heavy ball down a narrow track towards a group of wooden objects and try to knock down as many of them as possible. 保龄球运动

  • I go bowling for relaxation.

    我打保龄球是为了消遣。

2 N-UNCOUNT （板球等运动中的）投球 In a sport such as cricket, bowling is the action or activity of bowling the ball towards the batsman. （板球等运动中的）投球
