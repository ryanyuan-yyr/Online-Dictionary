catch on
1 PHRASAL VERB 明白;认识到 If you catch on to something, you understand it, or realize that it is happening. 明白;认识到

  • He got what he could out of me before I caught on to the kind of person he'd turned into. [VERB PREP. + to]

    我还没有意识到他已经变成了那样的人，他就已经从我这儿把能套走的都套走了。

  • Wait a minute! I'm beginning to catch on. [VERB PREP.]

    等一下！我开始有点懂了。

SYN understand, see, find out, grasp  
2 PHRASAL VERB 时兴;流行 If something catches on, it becomes popular. 时兴;流行

  • The idea has been around for ages without catching on. [VERB PREP.]

    这个观点已经提出来有年头了，可是一直没被广泛接受。

SYN become popular, take off, become trendy, come into fashion
