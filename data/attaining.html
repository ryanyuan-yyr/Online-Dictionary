attain
əteɪn əˈteɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense attains, present participle attaining, past tense , past participle attained
1 VERB （通常指经过努力）获得，达到 If you attain something, you gain it or achieve it, often after a lot of effort. （通常指经过努力）获得，达到 [formal]

  • Jim is halfway to attaining his pilot's licence. [VERB noun]

    吉姆就快要拿到飞行员执照了。

SYN obtain, get, win, reach  
2 VERB 达到，进入，实现（某种状态或情况） If you attain a particular state or condition, you may reach it as a result of natural development or work hard to attain this state. 达到，进入，实现（某种状态或情况）

  • ...attaining a state of calmness and confidence. [VERB noun]

    达到镇定自若、信心十足的状态

SYN reach, achieve, realize, acquire
