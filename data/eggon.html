egg on
PHRASAL VERB 怂恿;煽动;唆使 If you egg a person on, you encourage them to do something, especially something dangerous or foolish. 怂恿;煽动;唆使 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • He was lifting up handfuls of leaves and throwing them at her. She was laughing and egging him on. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他抓起一把把叶子扔向她，她大笑着鼓动他。

  • They egged each other on to argue and to fight. [V n P to-inf]

    他们互相拱火，大打大闹。

SYN incite, push, encourage, urge
