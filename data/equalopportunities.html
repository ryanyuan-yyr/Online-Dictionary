equal opportunities
ˈiːkwəl ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪtɪz
N-PLURAL （职业发展的）均等机会，机会均等 Equal opportunities refers to the policy of giving everyone the same opportunities for employment, pay and promotion, without discriminating against particular groups. （职业发展的）均等机会，机会均等 [business]

  • The profession's leaders must take action now to promote equal opportunities for all.

    该行业的领导们现在必须采取措施促使所有人获得均等机会。
