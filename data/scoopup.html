scoop up
PHRASAL VERB (敏捷地)抱起，拿起，捡起 If you scoop something up, you put your hands or arms under it and lift it in a quick movement. (敏捷地)抱起，拿起，捡起

  • Use both hands to scoop up the leaves. [VERB PREP. noun]

    用双手捧起叶子。

  • He began to scoop his things up frantically. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他开始手忙脚乱地捡起他的东西。
