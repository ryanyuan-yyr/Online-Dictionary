cough up
1 PHRASAL VERB (常指勉强地)支付，给予(钱) If you cough up an amount of money, you pay or spend that amount, usually when you would prefer not to. (常指勉强地)支付，给予(钱) [informal] [Also V P n + for]

  • I'll have to cough up $10,000 a year for tuition. [VERB PREP. + for]

    我每年将不得不付1万美元的学费。

  • Will this be enough to persuade Congress to cough up? [VERB PREP.]

    这能说服国会掏钱吗？

SYN fork out, deliver, hand over, surrender
