specialism
speʃəlɪzəm ˈspɛʃəlˌɪzəm
Word forms: plural specialisms
1 N-COUNT 专业(研究);专长;特长 Someone's specialism is a particular subject or skill which they study and know a lot about. 专业(研究);专长;特长

  • ...a teacher with a specialism in mathematics.

    一位数学专业的老师

2 N-UNCOUNT 专业化;专门化;专科化 Specialism is the act of specializing in a particular subject. 专业化;专门化;专科化

  • The needs of children may not be best met by an overemphasis on subject specialism.

    过分强调学科的专业化也许并不能最好地满足儿童的需要。
