misremember
mɪsrɪmembəʳ mɪsrɪˈmɛmbə ˌmɪsriˈmɛmbər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misremembers, present participle misremembering, past tense , past participle misremembered
VERB 记错 If you misremember something, you remember it incorrectly. 记错 [mainly US, formal]

  • He proved overconfident on the witness stand, misremembering a key piece of evidence.

    他在证人席上表现得过于自信，记错了一条重要的证据。
