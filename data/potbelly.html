pot belly
(also potbelly)Word forms: plural pot bellies
N-COUNT 大肚子；罗汉肚 Someone who has a pot belly has a round, fat stomach which sticks out, either because they eat or drink too much, or because they have had very little to eat for some time. 大肚子；罗汉肚
SYN paunch, beer belly or gut [informal] , spread [informal] , corporation [informal]
