recollect
rekəlekt ˌrɛkəˈlɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recollects, present participle recollecting, past tense , past participle recollected
VERB 想起；记起 If you recollect something, you remember it. 想起；记起 [Also VERB]

  • Ramona spoke with warmth when she recollected the doctor who used to be at the county hospital. [VERB noun]

    拉蒙纳回忆起曾在乡村医院工作的那位医生时，言语中透着温情。

  • His efforts, the Duke recollected many years later, were distinctly half-hearted. [VERB that]

    据公爵许多年之后的回忆，他当时明显只是敷衍了事。

SYN remember, mind [dialect] , recall, reminisce
