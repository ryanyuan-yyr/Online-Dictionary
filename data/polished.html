polished
pɒlɪʃt ˈpɑlɪʃt
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 优雅的；有教养的 Someone who is polished shows confidence and knows how to behave socially. 优雅的；有教养的 [approval]

  • He is polished, charming, articulate and an excellent negotiator.

    他文雅、迷人、能言善辩，是一名优秀的谈判人。

SYN elegant, sophisticated, refined, polite  
2 ADJ-GRADED 完美无缺的；炉火纯青的 If you describe a performance, ability, or skill as polished, you mean that it is of a very high standard. 完美无缺的；炉火纯青的 [approval]

  • It was simply a very polished performance.

    这简直就是一场完美的演出。

  • ...polished promotional skills.

    高明的促销技巧

3 See also: polish
