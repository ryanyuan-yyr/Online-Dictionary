duo
djuːoʊ duː- ˈduoʊ ˈdjuoʊ
Word forms: plural duos
1 N-COUNT 一对表演者;表演搭档 A duo is two musicians, singers, or other performers who perform together as a pair. 一对表演者;表演搭档

  • ...a famous dancing and singing duo.

    一对著名的歌舞表演搭档

SYN partnership, couple, pair, duet  
2 N-COUNT (尤指拥有共同特征的)一对,一双 You can refer to two people together as a duo, especially when they have something in common. (尤指拥有共同特征的)一对,一双 [mainly journalism]

  • ...Britain's former golden Olympic duo of Linford Christie and Sally Gunnell.

    英国前奥运会金牌得主二人档林福德·克里斯蒂和萨莉·冈纳尔
