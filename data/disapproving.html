disapproving
dɪsəpruːvɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 不赞成的;表示反对的;不以为然的 A disapproving action or expression shows that you do not approve of something or someone. 不赞成的;表示反对的;不以为然的

    珍妮特不满地看了他一眼。

SYN critical, discouraging, frowning, disparaging
