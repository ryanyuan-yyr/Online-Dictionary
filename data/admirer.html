admirer
ədmaɪərəʳ
Word forms: plural admirers
1 N-COUNT 钦佩者；欣赏者 If you are an admirer of someone, you like and respect them or their work very much. 钦佩者；欣赏者

  • He was an admirer of her grandfather's paintings. [+ of]

    他非常欣赏她祖父的画作。

SYN fan, supporter, follower, enthusiast  
2 N-COUNT （女人的）追求者,爱慕者 A woman's admirers are the men who are attracted to her. （女人的）追求者,爱慕者

  • Johnny was the most persistent and most harmless of her admirers.

    约翰尼是追她的人中最痴心、最没有心机的一个。
