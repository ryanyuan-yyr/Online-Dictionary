unhook
ʌnhʊk ʌnˈhʊk ʌnˈhʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unhooks, present participle unhooking, past tense , past participle unhooked
1 VERB 解开（衣服）的钩扣 If you unhook a piece of clothing that is fastened with hooks, you undo the hooks. 解开（衣服）的钩扣

  • She unhooked her dress. [VERB noun]

    她解开裙子的钩扣。

2 VERB 把…从钩上取下 If you unhook something that is held in place by hooks, you open it or remove it by undoing the hooks. 把…从钩上取下

  • Chris unhooked the shutters and went out on the balcony. [VERB noun]

    克里斯拉开百叶窗，走到了阳台上。
