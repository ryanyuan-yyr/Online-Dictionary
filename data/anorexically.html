anorexic
ænəreksɪk ˌænəˈrɛksɪk
Word forms: plural anorexics
ADJ 食欲缺乏的;厌食的;瘦骨嶙峋的 If someone is anorexic, they are suffering from anorexia and so are very thin. 食欲缺乏的;厌食的;瘦骨嶙峋的

  • Claire had been anorexic for three years.

    克莱尔曾经遭受厌食症3年的折磨。

  • ...an anorexic teenager.

    患有厌食症的少年

An anorexic is someone who is anorexic.患厌食症的人;厌食者

  • Not eating makes an anorexic feel in control.

    禁食让厌食者感到能掌握自我。
