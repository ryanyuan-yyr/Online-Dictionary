escapism
ɪskeɪpɪzəm ɪˈskeɪpɪzəm ɛˈskeɪpˌɪzəm ɪˈskeɪpˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 消遣;帮人逃避现实的事物 If you describe an activity or type of entertainment as escapism, you mean that it makes people think about pleasant things instead of the uninteresting or unpleasant aspects of their life. 消遣;帮人逃避现实的事物

  • Horoscopes are merely harmless escapism from an ever-bleaker world. [+ from]

    占星术只不过是让人逃避越发令人沮丧的世界的无害消遣罢了。

SYN fantasy, dreaming, illusion, delusion
