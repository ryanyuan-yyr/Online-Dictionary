unsupported
ʌnsəpɔːʳtɪd ˌʌnsəˈpɔːtɪd
1 ADJ 尚无证据的；尚未证实的 If a statement or theory is unsupported, there is no evidence which proves that it is true or correct. 尚无证据的；尚未证实的

  • It was a theory unsupported by evidence. [+ by]

    这一理论尚无证据支持。

  • The letters contained unsupported allegations.

    这些信里有一些未经证实的指控。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （人）无资助的，无依靠的 An unsupported person does not have anyone to provide them with money and the things they need. （人）无资助的，无依靠的

  • Unsupported mothers are one of the fastest-growing groups of welfare claimants.

    无生活依靠的单身母亲是福利救济申领者中人数增长最快的群体之一。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无承托的;无撑扶的;无支撑的 An unsupported building or person is not being physically supported or held up by anything. 无承托的;无撑扶的;无支撑的

  • ...the vast unsupported wall of the Ajuda Palace in Lisbon.

    里斯本阿茹达宫那面巨大的、无承托的墙

  • ...the child's first unsupported step.

    小孩在无人扶持的情况下迈出的第一步
