bedecked
bɪdekt bɪˈdɛkt
ADJ [adverb ADJ] 装饰着…的；装点 If a place is bedecked with flags or other ornaments, these things have been hung up to decorate it. 装饰着…的；装点 [literary]

  • The palace was bedecked with flags. [+ with/in]

    王宫到处彩旗飘飘。

Bedecked is also a combining form.亦可用于构词

  • ...a flower-bedecked stage.

    鲜花点缀的舞台
