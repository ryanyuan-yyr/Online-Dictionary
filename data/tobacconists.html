tobacconist
təbækənɪst təˈbækənɪst
Word forms: plural tobacconists
1 N-COUNT 烟草商;烟草店老板 A tobacconist is a shopkeeper who sells things such as tobacco, cigarettes, and cigars. 烟草商;烟草店老板
2 N-COUNT 烟草店 A tobacconist or a tobacconist's is a shop that sells things such as tobacco, cigarettes, and cigars. 烟草店
