secretion
sɪkriːʃən sɪˈkriʃən
Word forms: plural secretions
1 N-UNCOUNT 分泌 Secretion is the process by which certain liquid substances are produced by parts of plants or from the bodies of people or animals. 分泌

  • ...the secretion of adrenaline. [+ of]

    肾上腺素的分泌

  • ...insulin secretion.

    胰岛素分泌

SYN discharge, emission, excretion, exudation  
2 N-PLURAL 分泌物 Secretions are liquid substances produced by parts of plants or bodies. 分泌物

  • ...gastric secretions.

    胃分泌物
