reaffirm
riːəfɜːʳm ˌriːəˈfɜːm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reaffirms, present participle reaffirming, past tense , past participle reaffirmed
VERB 重申；再次肯定 If you reaffirm something, you state it again clearly and firmly. 重申；再次肯定 [formal]

  • He reaffirmed his commitment to the country's economic reform programme. [VERB noun]

    他重申了实施国家经济改革计划的决心。

  • The government has reaffirmed that it will take any steps necessary to maintain law and order. [VERB that]

    政府重申将采取一切必要措施维护法律和秩序。

SYN reiterate [formal] , repeat, restate
