inopportune
ɪnɒpəʳtjuːn -tuːn ɪnˌɑpərˈtun ɪnˌɑpərˈtjun
ADJ-GRADED 不合时宜的;不合适的 If you describe something as inopportune or if you say that it happens at an inopportune time, you mean that it happens at an unfortunate or unsuitable time, and causes trouble or embarrassment because of this. 不合时宜的;不合适的

  • The dismissals came at an inopportune time.

    解雇通知来得不合时宜。

SYN inconvenient, unfortunate, inappropriate, unsuitable
