prescriptive
prɪskrɪptɪv priˈskrɪptɪv prɪˈskrɪptɪv
ADJ-GRADED 规定性的；指定的；规范的 A prescriptive approach to something involves telling people what they should do, rather than simply giving suggestions or describing what is done. 规定性的；指定的；规范的 [formal]

  • ...prescriptive attitudes to language on the part of teachers.

    对教师使用语言的规范性态度

  • The psychologists insist, however, that they are not being prescriptive.

    然而，心理学家坚称他们并非一味灌输。

SYN dictatorial, rigid, authoritarian, legislating
