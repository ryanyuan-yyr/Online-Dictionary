sailing
seɪlɪŋ ˈseɪlɪŋ
Word forms: plural sailings
1 N-UNCOUNT 帆船驾驶;帆船运动 Sailing is the activity or sport of sailing boats. 帆船驾驶;帆船运动

  • There was swimming and sailing down on the lake.

    有人在湖上游泳和扬帆。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 水运航班 Sailings are trips made by a ship carrying passengers. 水运航班

  • Ferry companies are providing extra sailings from Calais.

    渡船公司加开了从加来出发的航班。

  • We'll get the next sailing.

    我们将坐下一班船。

3 PHRASE 轻而易举的;一帆风顺的 If you say that a task was not all plain sailing, you mean that it was not very easy. 轻而易举的;一帆风顺的

  • Pregnancy wasn't all plain sailing and once again there were problems.

    孕期并非一帆风顺，这不，又出现问题了。

  • We know it won't be plain sailing at Wembley because there are no easy games at this level.

    我们知道在温布利不会顺风顺水，因为在这一级别就没有轻松的比赛。
