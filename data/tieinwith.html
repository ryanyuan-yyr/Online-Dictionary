tie in with
(also tie up with)
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (想法或事实)与…一致，与…有关 If something such as an idea or fact ties in with or ties up with something else, it is consistent with it or connected with it. (想法或事实)与…一致，与…有关

  • Our wedding had to tie in with David leaving the army. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我们的婚礼要和戴维退伍的时间吻合。

  • I've got a feeling that the death may be tied up with his visit in some way. [be V-ed P P n]

    我有种感觉，死亡似乎和他的来访有某种关联。

SYN fit in with, coincide with, coordinate with, harmonize with
