anecdotal
ænɪkdoʊtəl ˌænɪkˈdoʊtəl ˌænɛkˈdoʊtəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 传闻的；轶事的 Anecdotal evidence is based on individual accounts, rather than on reliable research or statistics, and so may not be valid. 传闻的；轶事的

  • Anecdotal evidence suggests that sales in Europe have slipped.

    传闻有证据表明欧洲的销售量已经下滑。

  • ...countless anecdotal reports.

    无数的八卦消息

SYN unreliable, untrustworthy, based on rumour  
2 ADJ-GRADED （讲演或写作）含轶事的，多轶闻的 Anecdotal speech or writing is full of anecdotes or is based on anecdotes. （讲演或写作）含轶事的，多轶闻的

  • Gray's book is anecdotal and entertaining.

    格雷的书中尽是些八卦消息，颇具娱乐性。
