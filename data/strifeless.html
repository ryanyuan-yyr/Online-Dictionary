strife
straɪf straɪf
N-UNCOUNT 严重不和;冲突;争斗 Strife is strong disagreement or fighting. 严重不和;冲突;争斗 [formal]

  • Money is a major cause of strife in many marriages.

    金钱是造成很多婚姻不和的一个主要原因。

  • The boardroom strife at the company is far from over.

    公司董事会的明争暗斗还远没有结束。

  • It remains a highly unstable and strife-torn country.

    这仍是一个动荡不安、冲突不断的国家。

SYN conflict, battle, struggle, row
