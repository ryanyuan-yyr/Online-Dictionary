crate
kreɪt kreɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense crates, present participle crating, past tense , past participle crated
1 N-COUNT (运输或储藏物品的)板条箱，大木箱 A crate is a large box used for transporting or storing things. (运输或储藏物品的)板条箱，大木箱

  • ...a pile of wooden crates.

    一堆大木箱

  • A crane was already unloading crates and pallets.

    起重机已经开始卸载木箱和托盘了。

SYN container, case, box, packing case  
2 VERB [usually passive] 把…装进木箱(或板条箱) If something is crated, it is packed in a crate so that it can be transported or stored somewhere safely. 把…装进木箱(或板条箱)

  • The much repaired plane was crated for the return journey. [be VERB-ed]

    经过大修的刨子被装入木箱运回去。

SYN box, pack, enclose, pack up  
3 N-COUNT (运送瓶子用的)塑料分格箱，金属分隔箱 A crate is a plastic or wire box divided into sections which is used for carrying bottles. (运送瓶子用的)塑料分格箱，金属分隔箱
A crate of something is the amount of it that is contained in a crate.一箱(的量)

  • We've also got a bonus quiz with crates of beer as prizes!

    我们还获得了一次参加有奖智力竞赛的机会，奖品是成箱的啤酒！
