crested
krestɪd ˈkrɛstɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 长有羽冠的;长有鸟冠的 A crested bird is a bird that has a crest on its head. 长有羽冠的;长有鸟冠的

  • ...crested hawks.

    有羽冠的鹰

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有饰章的;带纹章的 Crested objects have on them the crest of a noble family, a town, or an organization. 有饰章的;带纹章的

  • ...crested china.

    有饰章的瓷器

  • ...crested writing paper.

    有饰章的信纸
