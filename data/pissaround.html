piss around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 piss about
1 PHRASAL VERB 游手好闲；无所事事 If you say that someone pisses around or pisses about, you mean they waste a lot of time doing unimportant things. 游手好闲；无所事事 [mainly British, informal, rude, disapproval]

  • Now, let's stop pissing about, shall we? [VERB PREP.]

    我们现在别在小事上浪费时间了，可以吗？

2 PHRASAL VERB 胡闹；没正经 If you say that someone pisses around or pisses about, you mean they behave in a silly, childish way. 胡闹；没正经 [British, informal, rude]

  • We just pissed about, laughing. [VERB PREP.]

    我们只是胡闹，大笑。
