mistreat
mɪstriːt mɪsˈtrit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mistreats, present participle mistreating, past tense , past participle mistreated
VERB （尤指在身体上）虐待 If someone mistreats a person or an animal, they treat them badly, especially by making them suffer physically. （尤指在身体上）虐待

  • ...a lad who mistreats a horse. [VERB noun]

    虐待马的马夫

  • She has been mistreated by men in the past. [be VERB-ed]

    以前她受过男人的虐待。

SYN abuse, injure, harm, molest
