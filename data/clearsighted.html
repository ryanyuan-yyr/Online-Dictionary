clear-sighted
ADJ-GRADED 有见识的;有决断力的 If you describe someone as clear-sighted, you admire them because they are able to understand situations well and to make sensible judgments and decisions about them. 有见识的;有决断力的 [approval]

  • He was clear-sighted enough to keep a sense of perspective.

    他很有见识，能保持正确的判断力。

  • Try to keep a clear-sighted view of your objective.

    对目标要有明确的认识。
