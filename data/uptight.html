uptight
ʌptaɪt ˈʌpˈtaɪt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 紧张不安的；忧虑的;易怒的 Someone who is uptight is tense, nervous, or annoyed about something and so is difficult to be with. 紧张不安的；忧虑的;易怒的 [informal]

  • Penny never got uptight about exams. [+ about]

    彭妮从来不会因为考试而紧张。

  • I tend to get very uptight during a match.

    比赛时我总会紧张得不行。

SYN tense, wired [slang] , anxious, neurotic
