freezing
friːzɪŋ
1 ADJ 极冷的;冻得彻骨的 If you say that something is freezing or freezing cold, you are emphasizing that it is very cold. →see usage note at: cold 极冷的;冻得彻骨的

  • The cinema was freezing.

    电影院太冷了。

  • ...a freezing January afternoon.

    1月份的一个滴水成冰的下午

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] (人)感到极冷的，冻得要命的 If you say that you are freezing or freezing cold, you emphasizing that you feel very cold. (人)感到极冷的，冻得要命的 [emphasis]

  • 'You must be freezing,' she said.

    “你一定冻坏了，”她说。

SYN frozen, chilled, numb, chilly  
3 N-UNCOUNT 同 freezing point Freezing means the same as freezing point . 同 freezing point

  • It's 15 degrees below freezing.

    温度为零下 15 度。
