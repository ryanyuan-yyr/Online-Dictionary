utter
ʌtəʳ ˈʌtər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense utters, present participle uttering, past tense , past participle uttered
1 VERB 发出（声音）；说；讲 If someone utters sounds or words, they say them. 发出（声音）；说；讲 [literary]

  • He uttered a snorting laugh. [VERB noun]

    他嗤笑了一声。

  • They departed without uttering a word. [VERB noun]

    他们一言不发地离开了。

SYN say, state, speak, voice  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 完全的；彻底的；十足的 You use utter to emphasize that something is great in extent, degree, or amount. 完全的；彻底的；十足的 [emphasis]

  • This, of course, is utter nonsense.

    这当然是一派胡言。

  • ...this utter lack of responsibility.

    这种完全没有责任感的表现

  • A look of utter confusion swept across his handsome face.

    他英俊的脸上掠过一丝大惑不解的神情。

SYN absolute, complete, total, perfect
