confederacy
kənfedərəsi kənˈfɛdərəsi
Word forms: plural confederacies
N-COUNT 同盟;联盟;联邦 A confederacy is a union of states or people who are trying to achieve the same thing. 同盟;联盟;联邦

  • They've entered this new confederacy because the central government's been unable to control the collapsing economy.

    由于中央政府无力控制逐渐崩溃的经济，所以他们加入了这个新联盟。

SYN union, league, alliance, coalition
