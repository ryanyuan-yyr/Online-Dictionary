poise
pɔɪz pwɑːz pɔɪz
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 镇定；沉着；泰然自若 If someone has poise, they are calm, dignified, and self-controlled. 镇定；沉着；泰然自若

  • What amazed him even more than her appearance was her poise.

    她的容貌让他惊叹，而更让他惊讶的是她的沉着。

  • It took a moment for Mark to recover his poise.

    马克过了一会儿才恢复镇静。

SYN composure, cool [slang] , presence, assurance  
2 N-UNCOUNT （优雅的）姿势，姿态 Poise is a graceful, very controlled way of standing and moving. （优雅的）姿势，姿态

  • Ballet classes are important for poise and grace.

    芭蕾课对培养优雅的姿仪非常重要。

  • Even when he moved he did so without poise.

    他就连走起路来也姿势难看。

SYN grace, balance, equilibrium, elegance
