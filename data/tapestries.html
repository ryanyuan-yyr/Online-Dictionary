tapestry
tæpɪstri ˈtæpəstri
Word forms: plural tapestries
1 N-VAR 挂毯;壁毯;织锦;花毯 A tapestry is a large piece of heavy cloth with a picture sewn on it using coloured threads. 挂毯;壁毯;织锦;花毯

  • The seats of the chairs had been recovered in tapestry.

    椅座上又铺回了花毯。

  • He sat on a tapestry cushion next to the hearth.

    他坐在壁炉边上的一块绣花软垫上。

  • On the opposite wall is a large tapestry.

    对面的墙上挂着一块大壁毯。

2 N-COUNT 各式各样的人(或物) You can refer to something as a tapestry when it is made up of many varied types of people or things. 各式各样的人(或物) [literary]

  • Hedgerows and meadows are thick with a tapestry of wild flowers. [+ of]

    灌木树篱和草地中开满了五彩缤纷的野花。

  • ...Chicago's political tapestry.

    芝加哥政坛的丰富多彩
