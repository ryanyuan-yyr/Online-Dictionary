obtuse
əbtjuːs -tuːs ɑbˈtus əbˈtus ɑbˈtjus əbˈtjus
1 ADJ-GRADED 迟钝的；愚笨的 Someone who is obtuse has difficulty understanding things, or makes no effort to understand them. 迟钝的；愚笨的 [formal]

  • I've really been very obtuse and stupid.

    我实在笨得不可理喻。

  • I'm a limited and obtuse clergyman while you're the expert.

    你是专家，而我是个能力有限的愚笨牧师。

obtuseness

  • Naivety bordering on obtuseness helped sustain his faith.

    近乎鲁钝的天真使他坚守自己的信仰。

2 ADJ （角）钝的；钝角的 An obtuse angle is between 90˚ and 180˚. （角）钝的；钝角的
