groundswell
graʊndswel ˈgraʊndˌswɛl
N-SING （公众情绪、支持度等的）迅速高涨 A sudden growth of public feeling or support for something is often called a groundswell. （公众情绪、支持度等的）迅速高涨 [journalism]

  • There is undoubtedly a groundswell of support for the idea of a strong central authority. [+ of]

    毋庸置疑，拥护强有力的中央集权的呼声越来越高。

  • The groundswell of opinion is in favour of a referendum. [+ of]

    支持全民公决的舆论呼声越来越高。
