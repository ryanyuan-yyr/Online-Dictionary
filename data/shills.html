shill
ʃɪl ʃɪl ʃɪl
Word forms: shills
N-COUNT [oft N for n] 托儿If you refer to someone as a shill, you mean that they are paid to sell something or to participate in an activity in order to persuade others to buy or participate. 托儿 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • He is tarnishing his reputation by being a shill for the tobacco industry.

    他因为烟草工业做托儿而在玷污他的名誉。
