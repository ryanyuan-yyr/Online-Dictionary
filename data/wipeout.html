wipe out
PHRASAL VERB 摧毁;毁灭;使灭绝 To wipe out something such as a place or a group of people or animals means to destroy them completely. 摧毁;毁灭;使灭绝 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The spill could wipe out the Gulf's turtle population. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这次原油泄漏可能会使波斯湾的海龟灭绝。

  • The man is a fanatic who is determined to wipe out any opposition to the way he conducts himself. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这人是个狂热分子，决意扫除任何反对其行事作风的声音。
