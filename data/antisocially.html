anti-social
(also antisocial)
1 ADJ-GRADED 不爱交际的；孤僻的；不合群的 Someone who is anti-social is unwilling to meet and be friendly with other people. 不爱交际的；孤僻的；不合群的

  • ...teenagers who will become aggressive and anti-social.

    将会变得好斗、孤僻的青少年一代

2 ADJ-GRADED （行为）反社会的，危害社会的，令人讨厌的 Anti-social behaviour is annoying or upsetting to other people. （行为）反社会的，危害社会的，令人讨厌的

  • Playing these games can lead to anti-social behaviour.

    玩这类游戏会引发反社会行为。
