straight-faced
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n, ADJ after verb] 一本正经的;绷着脸的 A straight-faced person appears not to be amused in a funny situation. 一本正经的;绷着脸的

  • ...a straight-faced, humourless character.

    不苟言笑、毫无幽默感的人

  • 'Whatever gives you that idea?' she replied straight-faced.

    “你怎么会有那种想法呢?”她绷着脸反问道。
