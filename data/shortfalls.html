shortfall
ʃɔːʳtfɔːl ˈʃɔrtˌfɔl
Word forms: plural shortfalls
N-COUNT 不足;缺口;差额;亏空 If there is a shortfall in something, there is less of it than you need. 不足;缺口;差额;亏空

  • The government has refused to make up a £30,000 shortfall in funding. [+ in]

    政府拒不补上 30,000 英镑的资金缺口。

SYN deficit, shortage, deficiency, loss
