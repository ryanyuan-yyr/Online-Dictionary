inseparable
ɪnseprəbəl ɪnˈsɛpərəbəl ɪnˈsɛprəbəl
1 ADJ 不可分割的;分不开的 If one thing is inseparable from another, the things are so closely connected that they cannot be considered separately. 不可分割的;分不开的

  • He firmly believes liberty is inseparable from social justice. [+ from]

    他坚信自由与社会正义是不可分的。

  • For the ancient Mexicans, life and death were inseparable, two halves of the same whole.

    对古代墨西哥人来说,生与死是同一整体不可分割的两个部分。

SYN indivisible, inalienable, conjoined, indissoluble  
inseparably

  • The doctrine of karma is inseparably linked with reincarnation.

    业的教义与轮回转世说紧密相关。

  • In his mind, religion and politics were inseparably intertwined.

    在他心中,宗教与政治是密不可分地交织在一起的。

2 ADJ-GRADED 形影不离的 If you say that two people are inseparable, you are emphasizing that they are very good friends and spend a great deal of time together. 形影不离的 [emphasis]

  • She and Kristin were inseparable.

    她和克里斯廷形影不离。
