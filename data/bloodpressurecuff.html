blood pressure cuff
Word forms: blood pressure cuffs
N-COUNT 血压袖带A blood pressure cuff is a medical device consisting of a piece of rubber or similar material that is wrapped around a patient's arm and then inflated in order to measure their blood pressure. 血压袖带 [medicine]
