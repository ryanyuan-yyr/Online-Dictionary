exemplar
ɪgzemplɑːʳ ɪɡˈzɛmplə ɛgˈzɛmplər ɛgˈzɛmˌplɑr ɪgˈzɛmplər ɪgˈzɛmplɑr
Word forms: plural exemplars
1 N-COUNT 典范;榜样 An exemplar is someone or something that is considered to be so good that they should be copied or imitated. 典范;榜样 [formal]

  • They viewed their new building as an exemplar of taste. [+ of]

    他们认为他们的新大厦品位独特，可作为典范。

SYN model, example, standard, ideal  
2 N-COUNT 范例;样品 An exemplar is a typical example of a group or class of things. 范例;样品 [formal]

  • One of the wittiest exemplars of the technique was M. C. Escher. [+ of]

    最为巧妙地运用那种技巧的一个典型人物就是M.C.埃舍尔。

SYN example, instance, illustration, type
