stateside
steɪtsaɪd ˈsteɪtˌsaɪd ˈsteɪtˌsaɪd
(also Stateside)
ADJ 在美国的;来自美国的;去美国的 Stateside means in, from, or to the United States. 在美国的;来自美国的;去美国的 [journalism, informal]

  • The band are currently planning a series of Stateside gigs.

    该乐队目前正在策划在美国举办一系列演唱会。

  • ...a well-known Stateside cop show.

    著名的美国警匪剧

Stateside is also an adverb.

  • His debut album was hugely successful Stateside.

    他的首张专辑在美国获得了巨大成功。
