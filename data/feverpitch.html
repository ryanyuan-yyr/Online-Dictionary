fever pitch
N-UNCOUNT [oft at NOUN] 极度兴奋;狂热 If something is at fever pitch, it is in an extremely active or excited state. 极度兴奋;狂热

  • Frances kept talking, her mind at fever pitch.

    弗朗西丝不停地说着，头脑处于极度兴奋状态。

  • Campaigning is reaching fever pitch for elections on November 6.

    为 11 月 6 日选举进行的竞选活动逐渐达到白热化。
