magical
mædʒɪkəl ˈmædʒɪkəl
1 ADJ 施魔法的;产生魔力的 Something that is magical seems to use magic or to be able to produce magic. 施魔法的;产生魔力的

  • ...the story of Sin-Sin, a little boy who has magical powers.

    一个有魔力的小男孩辛辛的故事

SYN supernatural, magic, mystical, mystic  
magically

  • ...the story of a young boy's adventures after he is magically transported through the cinema screen.

    关于一个小男孩神奇地进入了银幕之后的历险故事

2 ADJ-GRADED （地方或物体）奇妙的，神奇的 You can say that a place or object is magical when it has a special mysterious quality that makes it seem wonderful and exciting. （地方或物体）奇妙的，神奇的

  • The beautiful island of Cyprus is a magical place to get married.

    美丽的塞浦路斯岛是缔结良缘的美妙之地。

SYN enchanting, wonderful, beautiful, lovely
