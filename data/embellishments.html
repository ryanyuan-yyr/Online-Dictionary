embellishment
ɪmbelɪʃmənt ɛmˈbɛlɪʃmənt ɪmˈbɛlɪʃmənt
Word forms: plural embellishments
N-VAR 装饰;修饰 An embellishment is a decoration added to something to make it seem more attractive or interesting. 装饰;修饰

  • ...Renaissance embellishments.

    文艺复兴时期的装饰

  • ...public buildings with little bits of decoration and embellishment.

    没有太多装潢的公共建筑

SYN decoration, garnishing, ornament, gilding
