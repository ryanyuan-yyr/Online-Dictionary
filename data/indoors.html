indoors
ɪndɔːʳz ˈɪnˌdɔrz ɪnˈdɔrz
ADV [be ADVERB, ADVERB after verb] 在室内;在屋里 If something happens indoors, it happens inside a building. 在室内;在屋里

  • Since she was indoors, she had not been wearing a coat.

    由于她在室内,就没有穿外套。

  • I think perhaps we should go indoors.

    我觉得也许我们应该进屋里去。

  • Spend an evening indoors watching TV.

    晚上呆在屋里看电视。
