humid
hjuːmɪd ˈhjumɪd ˈjumɪd
ADJ-GRADED （空气、气候）潮湿的，湿热的 You use humid to describe an atmosphere or climate that is very damp, and usually very hot. （空气、气候）潮湿的，湿热的

  • Visitors can expect hot and humid conditions.

    游客会遇到炎热潮湿的天气状况。

  • The day is overcast and humid.

    天气多云潮湿。

SYN damp, sticky, moist, wet
