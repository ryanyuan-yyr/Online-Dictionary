double-park
ˈdʌbəlˌpɑrk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense double-parks, present participle double-parking, past tense , past participle double-parked
V-ERG (使)并排停放;(使)双行停车 If someone double-parks their car or their car double-parks, they park in a road by the side of another parked car. (使)并排停放;(使)双行停车

  • Murray double-parked his car. [VERB noun]

    默里并排停放了他的车。

  • The car pulled in and double-parked in front of the town hall. [VERB]

    车在市政大厅前靠边并排停下了。
