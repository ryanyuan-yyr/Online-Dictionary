rascal
rɑːskəl ræs- ˈræskəl
Word forms: plural rascals
N-COUNT 坏蛋；淘气鬼；家伙 If you call a man or child a rascal, you mean that they behave badly and are rude or dishonest. 坏蛋；淘气鬼；家伙 [old-fashioned]

  • What's that old rascal been telling you?

    那个老家伙一直在跟你说些什么？

SYN rogue, devil, villain, scoundrel [old-fashioned]
