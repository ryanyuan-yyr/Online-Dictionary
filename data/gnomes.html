gnome
noʊm noʊm ˈnoʊmi
Word forms: plural gnomes
N-COUNT 地精，土地神（童话故事中的神灵，英国人有时在花园中摆放小的土地神雕像） In children's stories, a gnome is an imaginary creature that is like a tiny old man with a beard and pointed hat. In Britain people sometimes have small statues of gnomes in their gardens. 地精，土地神（童话故事中的神灵，英国人有时在花园中摆放小的土地神雕像）
