nil
nɪl nɪl
1 NUM 零分(通常用于表示橄榄球、足球等比赛中的得分) Nil means the same as zero. It is usually used to say what the score is in sports such as rugby or football. →see usage note at: nought 零分(通常用于表示橄榄球、足球等比赛中的得分)

  • They beat Argentina one-nil in the final.

    他们在决赛中以 1 比 0 击败了阿根廷队。

2 N-UNCOUNT 零;无;不存在 If you say that something is nil, you mean that it does not exist at all. 零;无;不存在

  • Their legal rights are virtually nil.

    他们实际上毫无法律权利。

  • The heating in winter was almost nil.

    冬季供暖设施几乎毫无用处。
