semi-tropical
(also semitropical)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 副热带的;亚热带的 Semi-tropical places have warm, wet air. 副热带的;亚热带的

  • ...a semi-tropical island.

    一个亚热带的岛屿

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (花草、树木)副热带的，亚热带的 Semi-tropical plants and trees grow in places where the air is warm and wet. (花草、树木)副热带的，亚热带的

  • The inn has a garden of semi-tropical vegetation.

    这家小酒店的花园里种着亚热带植物。
