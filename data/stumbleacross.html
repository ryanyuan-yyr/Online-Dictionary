stumble across
(also stumble on)
PHRASAL VERB 意外发现;偶然看见 If you stumble across something or stumble on it, you find it or discover it unexpectedly. 意外发现;偶然看见

  • I stumbled across an extremely simple but very exact method for understanding where my money went. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我意外发现了一个非常简单却能精确了解我所花钱去向的方法。

  • History relates that they stumbled on a magnificent waterfall. [VERB PREP. noun]

    历史上记载他们曾意外地发现了一处壮观的瀑布。
