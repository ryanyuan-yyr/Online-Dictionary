reprise
rɪpriːz rɪˈpraɪz rəˈpriz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural reprises, present participle reprising, past tense , past participle reprised
1 N-COUNT (乐章的)重复，再现 In music, if there is a reprise, an earlier section of music is repeated. (乐章的)重复，再现
2 VERB 重复（扮演或演唱） If someone reprises a role or a song, they play or sing it again. 重复（扮演或演唱） [written]

  • He then proceeded to play for more than two hours, reprising every one of his hits. [VERB noun]

    他又接着演奏了两个多小时，将他的每一首知名作品都演奏了一遍。
