flunk out
PHRASAL VERB (因成绩太差) 被退学If you flunk out, you are dismissed from a school or college because your grades are not satisfactory. (因成绩太差) 被退学 [mainly US, informal]

  • He flunked out, a school official told CNN.

    一位校方官员告诉美国有线电视新闻网说，他被退学了。

  • If he doesn't find a solution to his problem soon, he'll surely flunk out of college.

    如果他不能很快找到解决问题的办法，他肯定要被学院退学。
