undertake
ʌndəʳteɪk ˌʌndərˈteɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense undertakes, present participle undertaking, past tense undertook, past participle undertaken
1 VERB 着手做;从事;承担 When you undertake a task or job, you start doing it and accept responsibility for it. 着手做;从事;承担

  • She undertook the arduous task of monitoring the elections. [VERB noun]

    她承担了监督选举的艰巨任务。

SYN take on, embark on, set about, commence  
2 VERB 承诺；保证；答应 If you undertake to do something, you promise that you will do it. 承诺；保证；答应

  • He undertook to edit the text himself. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他答应亲自编辑正文的内容。

SYN agree, promise, contract, guarantee
