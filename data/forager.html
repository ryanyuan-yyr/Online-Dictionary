forage
fɒrɪdʒ fɔːr- ˈfɔrɪdʒ ˈfɑrɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense forages, present participle foraging, past tense , past participle foraged
1 VERB 搜查;搜寻 If someone forages for something, they search for it in a busy way. 搜查;搜寻

  • They were forced to forage for clothing and fuel. [VERB + for]

    他们不得不去寻找衣服和燃料。

SYN search, hunt, scavenge, cast about  
2 VERB (动物)觅食 When animals forage, they search for food. (动物)觅食

  • We disturbed a wild boar that had been foraging by the roadside. [VERB]

    我们惊动了一只一直在路边觅食的野猪。

  • The cat forages for food. [VERB + for]

    猫在寻找食物。

3 N-UNCOUNT 饲料;草料 Forage is crops that are grown as food for cattle and horses. 饲料;草料

  • ...the amount of forage needed to feed one cow and its calf.

    喂养一头母牛和小牛犊所需要的饲料

SYN fodder, food, feed, foodstuffs
