bring off
PHRASAL VERB 完成，做完（艰难的事） If you bring off something difficult, you do it successfully. 完成，做完（艰难的事）

  • They were about to bring off an even bigger coup. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们要发动一次更大的政变。

  • He thought his book would change society. But he didn't bring it off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他认为他的书能改变社会，但是他没有完成它。

SYN accomplish, achieve, perform, carry out
