knit
nɪt nɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense knits, present participle knitting, past tense , past participle knitted
The past tense can be either knit or knitted for meaning 4. knit和knitted均可用作义项4的过去式。
1 VERB 编织；针织；机织 If you knit something, especially an article of clothing, you make it from wool or a similar thread by using two knitting needles or a machine. 编织；针织；机织 [Also VERB n + into]

  • I had endless hours to knit and sew. [VERB]

    我整天无休止地编织缝纫。

  • I have already started knitting baby clothes. [VERB noun]

    我已经开始给宝宝织衣服了。

  • She knitted him 10 pairs of socks to take with him. [VERB noun noun]

    她给他织了10双袜子让他带走。

  • During the war, Joan helped her mother knit scarves for soldiers. [VERB noun + for]

    战争期间，琼帮妈妈给战士们织围巾。

  • She pushed up the sleeves of her grey knitted cardigan and got to work. [VERB-ed]

    她撸起身上灰色开襟毛衣的衣袖开始工作。

Knit is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • Ferris wore a heavy knit sweater.

    费里斯身着一件织得厚厚的毛衣。

  • ...a cotton-knit sweater.

    棉针织衫

  • ...hand-knit garments.

    手工编织的衣服

knitter

  • Pattern charts with small print are often difficult for older knitters to use.

    上了年纪的针织工使用小的图样时通常很不方便。

2 VERB 使紧密结合；使严密；使紧凑 If someone or something knits things or people together, they make them fit or work together closely and successfully. 使紧密结合；使严密；使紧凑 [Also VERB n]

  • The best thing about sport is that it knits the whole family close together. [VERB noun with together]

    体育运动最大的益处在于它使整个家庭紧密地团结在一起。

  • Ordinary people have some reservations about their president's drive to knit them so closely to their neighbors. [V n + to/into]

    普通百姓对总统号召人们邻里间亲密相处的动机心存疑虑。

SYN join, unite, link, tie  
Knit is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...a closer-knit family.

    关系更加亲密的家庭

  • ...a tightly knit society.

    紧密团结的社会

3 VERB （骨头）愈合，接合 When broken bones knit, the broken pieces grow together again. （骨头）愈合，接合

  • The bone hasn't knitted together properly. [V + together]

    骨头愈合得不好。

  • ...broken bones that have failed to knit. [VERB]

    没能愈合好的断骨

SYN heal, unite, join, link  
4 PHRASE 皱眉（表示恼怒、担忧） If you knit your brows or knit your eyebrows, you frown because you are angry or worried. 皱眉（表示恼怒、担忧） [literary]

  • They knitted their brows and started to grumble.

    他们皱着眉头抱怨起来。

  • Billy's eyebrows knitted together in a little frown.

    比利眉头微皱。
