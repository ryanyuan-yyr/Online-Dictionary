obstructive
ɒbstrʌktɪv əbˈstrʌktɪv
ADJ-GRADED 故意刁难的；蓄意阻挠的 If you say that someone is being obstructive, you think that they are deliberately causing difficulties for other people. 故意刁难的；蓄意阻挠的

  • Mr Smith was obstructive and refused to follow correct procedure.

    史密斯先生故意刁难，就是不肯照章办事。

SYN unhelpful, difficult, awkward, blocking
