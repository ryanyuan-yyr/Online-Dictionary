rabbit on
PHRASAL VERB [usually cont] 唠叨；喋喋不休 If you describe someone as rabbiting on, you do not like the way they keep talking for a long time about something that is not very interesting. 唠叨；喋喋不休 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • What are you rabbiting on about? [V P + about]

    你在唠叨什么？

SYN go on, gas, rattle, gossip
