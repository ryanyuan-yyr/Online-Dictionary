economic
iːkənɒmɪk ek- ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˌ ikəˈnɑmɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 经济的;经济上的 Economic means concerned with the organization of the money, industry, and trade of a country, region, or society. 经济的;经济上的

  • ...Poland's radical economic reforms.

    波兰彻底的经济改革

  • The pace of economic growth is picking up.

    经济增长的步伐正在加快。

SYN financial, business, trade, industrial  
economically

  • ...an economically depressed area.

    经济萧条地区

  • Small English orchards can hardly compete economically with larger French ones.

    英国的小型果园很难在经济上和法国的大型果园竞争。

  • Economically and politically, this affair couldn't come at a worse time.

    无论从经济上还是政治上来看，这件事来得都太不是时候了。

2 ADJ-GRADED 有利可图的;赚钱的 If something is economic, it produces a profit. 有利可图的;赚钱的

  • The new system may be more economic but will lead to a decline in programme quality.

    新系统可能更合算，但是会降低程序质量。

Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse economic, economical, economics, and economy.

    注意不要混淆 economic, economical, economics 和 economy。
