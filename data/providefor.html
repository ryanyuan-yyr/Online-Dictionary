provide for
1 PHRASAL VERB 赡养；供养 If you provide for someone, you support them financially and make sure that they have the things that they need. 赡养；供养

  • Elaine wouldn't let him provide for her. [VERB PREP. noun]

    伊莱恩不愿让他养活自己。

  • Her father always ensured she was well provided for. [be adv V-ed for]

    她的父亲总是确保她衣食无忧。

2 PHRASAL VERB 为…做准备；为…事先安排妥当 If you provide for something that might happen or that might need to be done, you make arrangements to deal with it. 为…做准备；为…事先安排妥当

  • James had provided for just such an emergency. [VERB PREP. noun]

    詹姆斯已为这种紧急情况做好了准备。

3 PHRASAL VERB （法律、条约）规定，允许 If a law or agreement provides for something, it makes it possible. （法律、条约）规定，允许 [formal]

  • The bill provides for the automatic review of all death sentences. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该法案规定所有死刑判决都要自动接受审核。
