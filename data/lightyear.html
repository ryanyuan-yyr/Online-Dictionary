light year
Word forms: plural light years
1 N-COUNT 光年（指光在1年中所经过的距离） A light year is the distance that light travels in a year. 光年（指光在1年中所经过的距离）

  • ...a star system millions of light years away.

    几百万光年外的一个星系

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 极大的差距；极远的距离；极长的时期 You can say that two things are light years apart to emphasize a very great difference or a very long distance or period of time between them. 极大的差距；极远的距离；极长的时期 [informal, emphasis]

  • She says the French education system is light years ahead of the English one.

    她说法国的教育体制遥遥领先于英国。

  • I had not eaten anything hot since my last dinner with Sean. It seemed light-years away.

    上次和肖恩吃过饭后我就一直没吃过热菜热饭。好像已经过了很久很久似的。
