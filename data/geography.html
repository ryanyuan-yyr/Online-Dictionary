geography
dʒiɒgrəfi dʒiˈɑgrəfi
1 N-UNCOUNT 地理学 Geography is the study of the countries of the world and of such things as the land, seas, climate, towns, and population. 地理学
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 地形；地貌 The geography of a place is the way that features such as rivers, mountains, towns, or streets are arranged within it. 地形；地貌
