analyser
ænəlaɪzəʳ
Word forms: plural analysers
in AM, use 美国英语用 analyzer
1 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 分析器 An analyser is a piece of equipment which is used to analyse the substances that are present in something such as a gas. 分析器

  • ...an oxygen analyser.

    氧气分析器

2 N-COUNT 分析者；分解者 An analyser is someone who analyses information. 分析者；分解者
