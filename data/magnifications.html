magnification
mægnɪfɪkeɪʃən ˌmægnəfɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural magnifications
1 N-UNCOUNT 放大；扩大 Magnification is the act or process of magnifying something. 放大；扩大

  • Pores are visible without magnification.

    毛孔无须放大就可以看见。

  • I find England strange and unique and beautiful. And for me, London is the magnification of all that.

    我觉得英格兰可谓奇、绝、美。在我看来，伦敦在这些方面更为明显。

2 N-VAR 放大率;放大倍数 Magnification is the degree to which a lens, mirror, or other device can magnify an object, or the degree to which the object is magnified. 放大率;放大倍数

  • The electron microscope uses a beam of electrons to produce images at high magnifications.

    电子显微镜利用电子束形成高倍影像。

  • The magnification is 833,333 times the original size.

    放大倍数为实物大小的833,333倍。
