life support laɪf səˈpɔːt
N-UNCOUNT [oft on N] 生命支持系统Life support is a system that is used to keep a person alive when they are very ill and cannot breathe without help. 生命支持系统

  • She was kept on life support through the afternoon so family and friends could say goodbye.

    那个下午给她用了生命支持系统，以便家人和朋友可以和她道别。
