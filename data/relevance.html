relevance
reləvəns
N-UNCOUNT (对…的)重要性，意义，相关性 Something's relevance to a situation or person is its importance or significance in that situation or to that person. (对…的)重要性，意义，相关性

  • Politicians' private lives have no relevance to their public roles. [+ to]

    政治家的私生活与他们的公众角色不相关。

  • There are additional publications of special relevance to new graduates.

    还有对于新毕业生来说特别重要的其他出版物。

SYN pertinence, applicableness, appositeness, appropriateness
