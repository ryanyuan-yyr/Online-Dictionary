rye
raɪ raɪ
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 黑麦;裸麦 Rye is a cereal grown in cold countries. Its grains can be used to make flour, bread, or other foods. 黑麦;裸麦

  • One of the first crops that I grew when we came here was rye.

    我们来到这里时，我种的第一批作物里就有黑麦。

  • ...100g wholemeal or rye flour.

    100克全麦或黑麦面粉

2 N-UNCOUNT [usu on N] 黑麦面包;裸麦面包 Rye is bread made from rye. 黑麦面包;裸麦面包 [US]

  • I was eating ham and Swiss cheese on rye.

    我正吃夹火腿和瑞士干酪的黑麦面包。
