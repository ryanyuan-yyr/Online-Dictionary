authoritarian
ɔːθɒrɪteəriən ɔːˌθɒrɪˈtɛərɪən -θɔːr- əˌθɔrəˈtɛriən əˌθɑrəˈtɛriən
Word forms: plural authoritarians
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 专制的；独裁的 If you describe a person or an organization as authoritarian, you are critical of them controlling everything rather than letting people decide things for themselves. 专制的；独裁的 [disapproval]

  • Senior officers could be considering a coup to restore authoritarian rule.

    高官们可能会策划一场政变来复辟独裁统治。

SYN strict, severe, absolute, harsh  
An authoritarian is someone who is authoritarian.独裁者

  • Don became the overly strict authoritarian he felt his brother needed.

    顿成为了一个他觉得哥哥需要的极度刻薄的独裁者。
