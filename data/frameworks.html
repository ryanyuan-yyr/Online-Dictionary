framework
freɪmwɜːʳk ˈfreɪmˌwɜrk
Word forms: plural frameworks
1 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 体制;体系 A framework is a particular set of rules, ideas, or beliefs which you use in order to deal with problems or to decide what to do. 体制;体系

  • ... within the framework of federal regulations. [+ of]

    在联邦法规的体制内

SYN system, plan, order, scheme  
2 N-COUNT 框架;构架;支架 A framework is a structure that forms a support or frame for something. 框架;构架;支架

  • ...wooden shelves on a steel framework.

    钢支架上的木隔板

SYN structure, body, frame, foundation
