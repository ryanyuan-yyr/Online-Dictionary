perspire
pəʳspaɪəʳ pəˈspaɪə pərˈspaɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense perspires, present participle perspiring, past tense , past participle perspired
VERB 出汗；排汗；发汗 When you perspire, a liquid comes out on the surface of your skin, because you are hot or frightened. 出汗；排汗；发汗 [formal]

  • He began to perspire heavily. [VERB]

    他开始大量出汗。

  • ...mopping their perspiring brows. [VERB-ing]

    擦拭他们一直在出汗的额头

SYN sweat, glow, swelter, drip with sweat
