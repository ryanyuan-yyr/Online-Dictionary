erstwhile
ɜːʳsthwaɪl ˈɜrstˌhwaɪl
ADJ [ADJ n] 以往的;往昔的 You use erstwhile to describe someone that used to be the type of person indicated, but no longer is. 以往的;往昔的 [formal]

  • Erstwhile workers may have become managers.

    过去的工人可能已经成了经理。

  • He fled to America with Phyllis Burton, an erstwhile friend of his wife's.

    他和他妻子以前的一个朋友菲莉丝·伯顿一起逃往美国。

SYN former, old, late, previous
