misleading
mɪsliːdɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 误导性的;迷惑性的；欺骗性的 If you describe something as misleading, you mean that it gives you a wrong idea or impression. 误导性的;迷惑性的；欺骗性的

  • It would be misleading to say that we were friends.

    说我们是朋友会让别人产生误解。

  • The article contains several misleading statements.

    这篇文章有几处误导性说法。

SYN confusing, false, ambiguous, deceptive  
misleadingly

  • The data had been presented misleadingly.

    这些数据的提出容易使人产生误解。
