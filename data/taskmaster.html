taskmaster
tɑːskmɑːstəʳ tæskmæstəʳ ˈtæskˌmæstər
Word forms: plural taskmasters
N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 监工;工头 If you refer to someone as a hard taskmaster, you mean that they expect the people they supervise to work very hard. 监工;工头

  • They're both tough taskmasters, but Stephen is much more supportive.

    他们俩都是严厉的工头，不过斯蒂芬更喜欢帮助他人。
