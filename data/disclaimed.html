disclaim
dɪskleɪm dɪsˈkleɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disclaims, present participle disclaiming, past tense , past participle disclaimed
VERB 否认;拒绝承认 If you disclaim knowledge of something or disclaim responsibility for something, you say that you did not know about it or are not responsible for it. 否认;拒绝承认 [formal]

  • She disclaims any knowledge of her husband's business. [VERB noun]

    她否认对她丈夫的事知情。

  • The government has disclaimed responsibility for the deaths of six people. [VERB noun]

    政府否认对 6 人的死亡负有责任。

SYN deny, decline, reject, disallow
