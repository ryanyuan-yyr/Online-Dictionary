multi-storey
(also multistorey,multi-storeyed)
in AM, use multi-story, multistory or multi-storied are also used. 美国英语用multi-story, 亦用multistory或multi-storyied。
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 多层的;多层(楼)的 A multi-storey building has several floors at different levels above the ground. 多层的;多层(楼)的

  • ...the Moskovski Department Store, a vast multi-story complex near the city's center.

    莫斯可夫斯基百货商场，一座靠近市中心的大型多层综合建筑

  • ...a multi-storey car park.

    多层停车场

  • ...multi-storeyed apartment blocks.

    多层公寓大楼
