anxiety
æŋzaɪɪti æŋˈzaɪɪtɪ æŋˈzaɪəti
Word forms: plural anxieties
N-UNCOUNT 忧虑；焦虑；不安 Anxiety is a feeling of nervousness or worry. 忧虑；焦虑；不安

  • Her voice was full of anxiety.

    她的声音满是焦虑不安。

  • Many editorials express their anxieties about the economic chaos in the country. [+ about]

    许多社论对该国经济的混乱状况表示忧虑。

SYN uneasiness, concern, care, worry
