magnificent
mægnɪfɪsənt mægˈnɪfəsənt
ADJ-GRADED 宏伟的;壮丽的;令人印象深刻的 If you say that something or someone is magnificent, you mean that you think they are extremely good, beautiful, or impressive. 宏伟的;壮丽的;令人印象深刻的

  • ...a magnificent country house in wooded grounds.

    绿树掩映中的一幢乡间豪宅

  • ...magnificent views over the San Fernando Valley.

    圣费尔南多河谷的壮丽美景

  • She is magnificent at making you feel you can talk quite naturally to her.

    她让你觉得能和她畅所欲言，这是她非常了不起的地方。

SYN splendid, striking, grand, impressive  
magnificence

  • ...the magnificence of the Swiss mountains.

    瑞士山峦的壮丽风光

magnificently

  • The team played magnificently throughout the competition.

    球队整场比赛发挥非常出色。

  • ...a magnificently elaborate head-dress.

    华美精致的头饰
