sob story
Word forms: plural sob stories
N-COUNT (意在博取别人同情的)诉苦,伤感故事 You describe what someone tells you about their own or someone else's difficulties as a sob story when you think that they have told you about it in order to get your sympathy. (意在博取别人同情的)诉苦,伤感故事
