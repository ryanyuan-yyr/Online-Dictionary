indentation
ɪndenteɪʃən ˌɪndɛnˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural indentations
1 N-COUNT 行首缩格,行首留空格 An indentation is the space at the beginning of a line of writing when it starts further away from the edge of the paper than all the other lines. 行首缩格,行首留空格
2 N-COUNT 凹口;缺口 An indentation is a shallow hole or cut in the surface or edge of something. 凹口;缺口

  • Using a knife, make slight indentations around the edges of the pastry.

    用刀子绕馅饼边沿刻小锯齿。

SYN notch, cut, nick, depression
