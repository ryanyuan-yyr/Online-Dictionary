pull into
PHRASAL VERB （使）驶入停下 When a vehicle or driver pulls into a place, the vehicle moves into the place and stops there. （使）驶入停下

  • He pulled into the driveway in front of her garage. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他把车停在了她家车库前的车道上。

  • She pulled the car into a tight parking space on a side street. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    她将车停进了小路上一个狭窄的停车位里。
