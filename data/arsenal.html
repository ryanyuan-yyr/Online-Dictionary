arsenal
ɑːʳsənəl ˈɑrsənəl
Word forms: plural arsenals
1 N-COUNT 储藏的武器;军备 An arsenal is a large collection of weapons and military equipment held by a country, group, or person. 储藏的武器;军备

  • Russia and the other republics are committed to destroying most of their nuclear arsenals.

    俄罗斯和其他加盟共和国承诺销毁他们储存的大部分核武器。

  • They possess a formidable arsenal of rifles, machine guns, landmines and teargas.

    他们持有的步枪、机枪、地雷和催泪弹数量惊人。

2 N-COUNT 武器库；军火库 An arsenal is a building where weapons and military equipment are stored. 武器库；军火库

  • The murder weapon was part of a haul stolen in a raid on a South African air-force arsenal in 1990.

    1990年南非空军武器库遭到突袭，这件凶器就是当时被盗走的武器中的一件。

SYN armoury, storehouse, ammunition dump, arms depot  
3 N-COUNT [usually singular] （方法、资源等的）宝库；(储藏工具的)仓库 You can use arsenal to refer to a large number of tools, methods, or resources that someone has available to help them achieve what they want to do. （方法、资源等的）宝库；(储藏工具的)仓库

  • Managers use a full arsenal of motivational techniques to get employees to take risks.

    管理者运用各种激励措施使雇员勇于冒险。

  • He has more punches in his arsenal than other boxers.

    他比其他拳击手出拳花样多。
