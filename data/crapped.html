crap
kræp kræp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense craps, present participle crapping, past tense , past participle crapped
1 ADJ-GRADED 错误的;质量差的;蹩脚的 If you describe something as crap, you think that it is wrong or of very poor quality. 错误的;质量差的;蹩脚的 [informal, rude, disapproval]
Crap is also a noun.

  • It is a tedious, humourless load of crap.

    那是一堆枯燥乏味、毫无幽默感的废话。

2 N-UNCOUNT 粪便;屎 Crap is sometimes used to refer to faeces. 粪便;屎 [informal, rude]
3 VERB 排便;拉屎 To crap means to get rid of faeces from your body. 排便;拉屎 [informal, rude]
4 N-UNCOUNT (主要流行于北美洲的)双骰子赌博游戏 Craps or crap is a gambling game, played mainly in North America, in which you throw two dice and bet what the total will be. (主要流行于北美洲的)双骰子赌博游戏

  • I'll shoot some craps or play some blackjack.

    我要掷几把骰子，玩几把21点纸牌。
