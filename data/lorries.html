lorry
lɒri lɔːri ˈlɔri
Word forms: plural lorries
1 N-COUNT 卡车；货车 A lorry is a large vehicle that is used to transport goods by road. 卡车；货车 [British]

  • ...a seven-ton lorry.

    7吨的卡车

SYN truck, van, juggernaut, HGV  
in AM, use 美国英语用 truck
2 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb] （货物等）来路不明，是老鼠货，是贼赃 If someone says that something has fallen off the back of a lorry, or that they got something off the back of a lorry, they mean that they bought something that they knew was stolen. （货物等）来路不明，是老鼠货，是贼赃 [British, informal]

  • He gets caviare that has fallen off the back of a lorry.

    他能弄到来路不明的鱼子酱。

  • Pete once bought the boys a bicycle cheap off the back of a lorry.

    皮特有一回给孩子们买了一辆便宜的老鼠货自行车。
