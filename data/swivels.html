swivel
swɪvəl ˈswɪvəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense swivels, present participle swivelling, past tense , past participle swivelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 swiveling, swiveled
1 V-ERG (使)旋转;(使)转动 If something swivels or if you swivel it, it turns around a central point so that it is facing in a different direction. (使)旋转;(使)转动 [Also VERB n]

  • She swivelled her chair round and stared out across the back lawn. [VERB noun adv./prep.]

    她把椅子转过来，向外凝视着屋后的草坪。

  • His chairs can swivel, but they can't move up or down. [VERB]

    他的椅子能旋转，但是不能升降。

2 VERB (突然)转身朝向 If you swivel in a particular direction, you turn suddenly in that direction. (突然)转身朝向

  • He swivelled round to face Sarah. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他转过身面对着萨拉。

SYN turn, spin, revolve, rotate  
3 V-ERG (使)(头或眼睛)转动 If your head or your eyes swivel in a particular direction or if you swivel them in a particular direction, you quickly look in that direction. (使)(头或眼睛)转动 [Also VERB]

  • Roger swivelled his head to look at her. [VERB noun]

    罗杰转过头看着她。

  • His eyes swivelled from one girl to the other. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他的目光在两个女孩身上打转。
