log out
(also log off)
PHRASAL VERB 注销；退出，离开（计算机系统） When someone who is using a computer system logs out or logs off, they finish using the system by typing a particular command. 注销；退出，离开（计算机系统）

  • If a computer user fails to log off, the system is accessible to all. [VERB PREP.]

    如果计算机用户没有成功退出，那么谁都可以进入该系统。
