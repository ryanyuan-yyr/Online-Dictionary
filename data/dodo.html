dodo
doʊdoʊ ˈdoʊdoʊ
Word forms: plural dodos, plural dodoes
1 N-COUNT 渡渡鸟(现已灭绝的一种不能飞行的大鸟) A dodo was a very large bird that was unable to fly. Dodos are now extinct. 渡渡鸟(现已灭绝的一种不能飞行的大鸟)
2 N-COUNT 笨人;蠢人 If you refer to someone as a dodo, you think they are foolish or silly. 笨人;蠢人 [informal, disapproval]

  • Any dodo could put this together.

    任何一个笨蛋都能把这个安装好。
