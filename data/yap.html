yap
jæp jæp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense yaps, present participle yapping, past tense , past participle yapped
1 VERB （小狗）狂吠，汪汪乱叫 If a small dog yaps, it makes short loud sounds in an excited way. （小狗）狂吠，汪汪乱叫

  • The little dog yapped frantically. [VERB]

    那只小狗狂吠着。

  • ...two yapping cairn terriers. [VERB-ing]

    两只疯狂吠叫的凯恩犭更狗

SYN yelp, bark, woof, yip [mainly US]  
2 VERB 哇啦哇啦地说个不停;瞎扯 If you say that someone yaps, you mean that they talk continuously in an annoying way. 哇啦哇啦地说个不停;瞎扯 [informal]

  • The guy just loves to yap, and will do so at length. [VERB]

    那家伙就爱胡扯，而且一说起来就没完没了。

  • She keeps yapping at me about Joe. [V at/about n]

    她没完没了地和我唠叨着乔的事。

SYN talk, go on, rabbit (on) [British , informal] , gossip
