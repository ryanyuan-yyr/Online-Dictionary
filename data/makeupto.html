make up to
PHRASAL VERB （尤指）因自己的过失向…弥补，补偿 If you say that you will make it up to someone, you are promising that you will do something good for them after they have been upset or disappointed, especially by you. （尤指）因自己的过失向…弥补，补偿

  • I'll make it up to you, I promise. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    我保证，我会补偿你的。

  • I must make it up to him for the awful intrusion of last night. [V n P P n + for]

    因为昨晚过分的打扰，我必须对他有所补偿。

SYN flirt with, be all over, come on to, chase after
