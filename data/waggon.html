wagon
wægən ˈwægən
Word forms: plural wagons
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 waggon
1 N-COUNT 四轮运货马车 A wagon is a strong vehicle with four wheels, usually pulled by horses or oxen and used for carrying heavy loads. 四轮运货马车
2 N-COUNT (火车的)货车车厢，车皮 A wagon is a large container on wheels which is pulled by a train. (火车的)货车车厢，车皮 [mainly British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 freight car
3 PHRASE 停止喝酒;戒酒 Someone who is on the wagon has stopped drinking alcohol. 停止喝酒;戒酒 [informal]

  • I'm on the wagon for a while. Cleaning out my system.

    我这段时间正在戒酒，对体内进行大扫除。

4 See also: station wagon
