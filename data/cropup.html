crop up
PHRASAL VERB (常指出其不意地)出现，发生 If something crops up, it appears or happens, usually unexpectedly. (常指出其不意地)出现，发生

  • His name has cropped up at every selection meeting this season. [VERB PREP.]

    他的名字突然出现在这一季所有选举会议上。

  • Problems will crop up and hit you before you are ready. [VERB PREP.]

    你还未准备好，问题就会突然出现并打击你。

SYN happen, appear, emerge, occur
