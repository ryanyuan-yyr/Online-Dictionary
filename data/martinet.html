martinet
mɑːʳtɪnet ˌmɑrtənˈɛt ˈmɑrtənˌɛt
Word forms: plural martinets
N-COUNT 严格执行纪律的人;主张服从命令的人 If you say that someone is a martinet, you are criticizing them because they are very strict and demand that people obey their rules and orders. 严格执行纪律的人;主张服从命令的人 [formal, disapproval]

  • He's a retired Lieutenant Colonel and a bit of a martinet.

    他是个退役中校，有点军人作风。

SYN disciplinarian, authoritarian, stickler, taskmaster
