deliberation
dɪlɪbəreɪʃən dɪˌlɪbərˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural deliberations
1 N-UNCOUNT 慎重的考虑;仔细的思考 Deliberation is the long and careful consideration of a subject. 慎重的考虑;仔细的思考

  • After much deliberation, a decision was reached.

    经过深思熟虑后终于作出了一个决定。

  • After five minutes of deliberation, he was found guilty of murdering the president.

    经过 5 分钟的仔细斟酌之后，他被判谋杀总统罪名成立。

2 N-PLURAL 审议;评议;商议 Deliberations are formal discussions where an issue is considered carefully. 审议;评议;商议

  • ...the outcome of the deliberations.

    评议的结果

  • Their deliberations were rather inconclusive.

    他们的审议几乎没有得出什么结论。

SYN discussion, talk, conference, exchange  
3 N-UNCOUNT [usu with N] 从容;不慌不忙;审慎 If you say or do something with deliberation, you do it slowly and carefully. 从容;不慌不忙;审慎

  • Fred spoke with deliberation.

    弗雷德字斟句酌。

  • My mother folded her coat across the back of the chair with careful deliberation.

    母亲小心翼翼地把大衣搭在椅背上。
