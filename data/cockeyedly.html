cockeyed
kɒkaɪd -aɪd ˈkɑkˌaɪd
(also cock-eyed)
1 ADJ-GRADED (想法或计划)不可能成功的 If you say that an idea or scheme is cockeyed, you mean that you think it is very unlikely to succeed. (想法或计划)不可能成功的

  • She has some cockeyed delusions about becoming a pop star.

    她还幻想着自己能成为流行歌星。

SYN absurd, crazy [informal] , ludicrous, preposterous  
2 ADJ-GRADED 歪的;斜的 If something is cockeyed, it looks wrong because it is not in a level or straight position. 歪的;斜的

  • ...dusty photographs hanging at cockeyed angles on the walls.

    歪挂在墙上、布满灰尘的照片

SYN crooked, squint [informal] , awry, lopsided
