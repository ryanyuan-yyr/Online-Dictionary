self-indulgence ˈsɛlfɪnˈdʌldʒəns
Word forms: plural self-indulgences
N-VAR 自我放纵;自我纵容 Self-indulgence is the act of allowing yourself to have or do the things that you enjoy very much. 自我放纵;自我纵容

  • He prayed to be saved from self-indulgence.

    他祈祷自己能从自我放纵中摆脱出来。

  • Going to the movies in the afternoon is one of my big self-indulgences.

    下午去看电影是我尽情放纵自己的一种方式。

SYN extravagance, excess, incontinence, dissipation
