melodrama
melədrɑːmə ˈmɛləˌdrɑːmə ˈmɛloʊˌdrɑmə ˈmɛloʊˌdræmə ˈmɛləˌdrɑmə ˈmɛləˌdræmə
Word forms: plural melodramas
N-VAR (情节离奇而夸张的)惊险故事，传奇剧，情节剧 A melodrama is a story or play in which there are a lot of exciting or sad events and in which people's emotions are very exaggerated. (情节离奇而夸张的)惊险故事，传奇剧，情节剧
