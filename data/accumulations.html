accumulation
əkjuːmjʊleɪʃən əˌkjumjuˈleɪʃən əˌkjumjəˈleɪʃən
Word forms: plural accumulations
1 N-COUNT 堆积物；聚积物 An accumulation of something is a large number of things which have been collected together or acquired over a period of time. 堆积物；聚积物

  • ...an accumulation of experience and knowledge. [+ of]

    经验和知识的积累

  • ...accumulations of dirt.

    积尘

SYN collection, increase, stock, store  
2 N-UNCOUNT 堆积;积累;积聚 Accumulation is the collecting together of things over a period of time. 堆积;积累;积聚

  • ...the accumulation of capital and the distribution of income. [+ of]

    资本的积累和收入的分配

  • The rate of accumulation decreases with time.

    积累的速度随着时间变慢。

SYN growth, collection, gathering, build-up
