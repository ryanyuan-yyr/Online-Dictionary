2 PHRASAL VERB 尽心尽力(去做某事) If you sit down and do something, you spend time and effort doing it in order to try to achieve something. 尽心尽力(去做某事)

  • Have you both sat down and worked out a budget together?. [VERB PREP.]

    你们有没有同心协力制订出一份预算呢？

  • 150 countries sat down to discuss the impact of human activities on the atmosphere. [VERB PREP.]

    150 个国家共同认真讨论了人类活动对大气的影响。

sit-down ˈsɪtdaʊn ˈsɪtˌdaʊn
1 N-SING 坐下休息;小憩 If you have a sit-down, you sit down and rest for a short time. 坐下休息;小憩 [British, informal]

  • All he wanted was a cup of tea and a sit-down.

    他只求一杯清茶、片刻小憩。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (餐饭)由侍者送上餐桌的，坐在桌前吃的 A sit-down meal is served to people sitting at tables. (餐饭)由侍者送上餐桌的，坐在桌前吃的

  • A sit-down dinner was followed by a disco.

    晚宴之后播放了一曲迪斯科舞曲。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (抗议)静坐的 In a sit-down protest, people refuse to leave a place until they get what they want. (抗议)静坐的

  • Teachers staged a sit-down protest in front of the president's office.

    老师们在校长办公室门前举行了一场静坐抗议。
