onshore
ɒnʃɔːʳ ˈɒnˈʃɔː ˈɑnˌʃɔr
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 在陆地上的；在岸上的；近岸的 Onshore means happening on or near land, rather than at sea. 在陆地上的；在岸上的；近岸的

  • ...Western Europe's biggest onshore oilfield.

    西欧最大的陆上油田

Onshore is also an adverb.

  • They missed the ferry and remained onshore.

    他们没有赶上渡轮，还滞留在岸上。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 向岸的；朝陆地的 Onshore means happening or moving towards the land. 向岸的；朝陆地的

  • The onshore wind blew steadily past him.

    向岸海风在他身边徐徐吹过。

Onshore is also an adverb.

  • There was a bit of a wind and it was blowing onshore.

    有一丝向岸微风在轻轻吹拂。

  • ...the gas comes onshore in great quantities.

    大量的天然气从海上输往陆地。
