head up
PHRASAL VERB 领导;主管;负责 The person who heads up a group, organization, or activity is the leader of it. 领导;主管;负责

  • Judge Frederick Lacey headed up the investigation. [VERB PREP. noun]

    弗雷德里克·莱西法官负责该项调查。

  • We asked ourselves what we wanted from our management structure and who we wanted to head it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们问自己，我们想要自己的管理结构做些什么，希望它由谁来领导。

SYN lead, run, head, control
