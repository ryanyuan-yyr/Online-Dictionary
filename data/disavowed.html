disavow
dɪsəvaʊ ˌdɪsəˈvaʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disavows, present participle disavowing, past tense , past participle disavowed
VERB 否认;不承认;拒绝对…承担责任 If you disavow something, you say that you are not connected with it or responsible for it. 否认;不承认;拒绝对…承担责任 [formal]

  • Dr. Samuels immediately disavowed the newspaper story. [VERB noun]

    赛缪尔斯博士当即对新闻报道予以了否认。

SYN deny, reject, contradict, retract
