double-space
ˈdʌbəlˌspeɪs
(also double space)Word forms: double-spaces, double-spacing, double-spaced
1 V-T 隔行书写If you double-space something you are writing or typing, you include a full line of space between each line of writing. 隔行书写

  • Double-space the entire list.

    隔行写整个清单。

2 ADJ double-spaced

  • ...forty pages of double-spaced typescript.

    ...隔行打出的40页文件。

3 N-COUNT 双倍行距A double space is a full line of space between each line of a piece of writing. 双倍行距

  • Leave a double space between entries.

    在条目之间留出双倍行距。
