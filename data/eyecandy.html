eye candy
(also eye-candy)
N-UNCOUNT 虚有其表的人;华而不实之物 Eye candy is used to refer to people or things that are attractive to look at but are not interesting in other ways. 虚有其表的人;华而不实之物 [informal]

  • Back then, women on TV were mostly seen as eye candy.

    那时候，电视中的女性大多都被看作花瓶。

  • Animation has stopped being eye-candy for kids and geeks and become mainstream entertainment.

    动画不再仅仅吸引小孩子和动漫迷的眼球，而成了主流娱乐方式。
