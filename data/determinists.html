determinist
dɪtɜːʳmɪnɪst
Word forms: plural determinists
1 N-COUNT 决定论者 A determinist is someone who believes in determinism. 决定论者 [formal]
2 ADJ (观点等)（基于）决定论的 Determinist ideas are based on determinism. (观点等)（基于）决定论的 [formal]

  • The determinist doctrines in question maintained that certain people were born to be slaves.

    这里所说的决定论信条坚称一些人天生就是奴隶。
