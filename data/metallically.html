metallic
mətælɪk məˈtælɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 金属撞击般的；叮当声的 A metallic sound is like the sound of one piece of metal hitting another. 金属撞击般的；叮当声的

  • There was a metallic click and the gates swung open.

    随着一声金属碰击的咔嗒声，大门打开了。

  • It gave a metallic clang, like a cracked bell.

    它发出了金属碰击般的哐啷声，跟破钟似的。

SYN tinny, jarring, grating, jangling  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 尖厉刺耳的;难听的 A metallic voice has a harsh unpleasant sound. 尖厉刺耳的;难听的

  • ...that creaking metallic voice of hers.

    她那尖厉刺耳的嗓音

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (涂料、颜色)有金属光泽的，金属般闪亮的 Metallic paint or colours shine like metal. (涂料、颜色)有金属光泽的，金属般闪亮的

  • He had painted all the wood with metallic silver paint.

    他给所有的木材都涂上了一层闪耀着金属光泽的银漆。

  • Metallic finishes are seen as upmarket.

    金属般闪亮的罩面漆被认为是高档漆。

4 ADJ-GRADED 金属味的 Something that tastes metallic has a bitter unpleasant taste. 金属味的

  • There was a metallic taste at the back of his throat.

    他喉咙深处有一股金属味儿。

5 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 含金属的；金属制的 Metallic means consisting entirely or partly of metal. 含金属的；金属制的

  • Even the smallest metallic object, whether a nail file or cigarette lighter, is immediately confiscated.

    即便是最小的金属物品，不管是指甲锉刀还是打火机，都会马上被没收。

  • Place the salmon in a nonstick metallic dish.

    将鲑鱼放在不粘底的金属盘内。
