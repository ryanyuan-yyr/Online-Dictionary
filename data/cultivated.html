cultivated
kʌltɪveɪtɪd ˈkʌltəˌveɪtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 有教养的;有修养的;举止文雅的 If you describe someone as cultivated, you mean they are well educated and have good manners. 有教养的;有修养的;举止文雅的 [formal]

  • His mother was an elegant, cultivated woman.

    他的母亲是一位端庄文雅的女性。

SYN refined, cultured, advanced, polished  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 栽培的;培植的 Cultivated plants have been developed for growing on farms or in gardens. 栽培的;培植的

  • ...a mixture of wild and cultivated varieties.

    野生品种和栽培品种的混作
