re-entry riːˈɛntrɪ
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 reentry
1 N-UNCOUNT 再进入 Re-entry is the act of returning to a place, organization, or area of activity that you have left. 再进入

  • The house has been barred and bolted to prevent re-entry.

    房子已经被封了，门被闩上，防止有人入内。

  • The military men are contemplating a re-entry into politics.

    这些军人在考虑重返政界。

2 N-UNCOUNT （航天器的）重返大气层，返回地球 Re-entry is used to refer to the moment when a spacecraft comes back into the earth's atmosphere after being in space. （航天器的）重返大气层，返回地球

  • The station would burn up on re-entry into the earth's atmosphere.

    空间站在重返地球大气层时会被烧毁。
