referral
rɪfɜːrəl rɪˈfɜrəl
Word forms: plural referrals
N-VAR 移交;送交 Referral is the act of officially sending someone to a person or authority that is qualified to deal with them. A referral is an instance of this. 移交;送交

  • Legal Aid can often provide referral to other types of agencies. [+ to]

    法律援助机构通常可以帮助引荐到其他类型的机构去。

  • Ask your doctor for a referral to a clinical psychologist.

    让你的医生给你推荐一位临床心理学家。
