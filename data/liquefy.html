liquefy
lɪkwɪfaɪ ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense liquefies, present participle liquefying, past tense , past participle liquefied
V-ERG （使）液化 When a gas or solid substance liquefies or is liquefied, it changes its form and becomes liquid. （使）液化

  • Heat the jam until it liquefies. [VERB]

    把果酱加热至其熔化。

  • You can liquefy the carbon dioxide to separate it from the other constituents. [VERB noun]

    可以将二氧化碳液化，以便和其他成分分开。

  • ...a truck carrying liquefied petroleum gas. [VERB-ed]

    装着液化石油气的卡车

SYN melt, dissolve, thaw, liquidize
