reality check
Word forms: plural reality checks
N-COUNT [usually singular] 真情实况;现实的提醒 If you say that something is a reality check for someone, you mean that it makes them recognize the truth about a situation, especially about the difficulties involved in something they want to achieve. 真情实况;现实的提醒

  • Jones's wise words are a timely reality check for many at the club who believe the great days are just around the corner. [+ for]

    琼斯的一番高见来得正是时候，对于俱乐部中许多相信好日子已经不远的人来说不啻于当头一棒。
