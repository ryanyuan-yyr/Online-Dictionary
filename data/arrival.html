arrival
əraɪvəl əˈraɪvəl
Word forms: plural arrivals
1 N-VAR [oft with poss, on N] (人或交通工具的）到达，到来 When a person or vehicle arrives at a place, you can refer to their arrival . (人或交通工具的）到达，到来

  • ...the day after his arrival in England.

    他到英格兰的第二天

  • He was dead on arrival at the nearby hospital.

    到达附近的医院时他已经死亡。

  • ...the airport arrivals hall.

    机场到港大厅

  • ...the arrival gate at Penn Station.

    佩恩车站的出站口

SYN appearance, coming, arriving, entrance  
2 N-VAR [oft with poss] 上任；就任 When someone starts a new job, you can refer to their arrival in that job. 上任；就任

  • ...the power vacuum created by the arrival of a new president.

    新主席接任造成的权力真空

  • The company had eight departures and 11 new arrivals on its management board in 1980-1989.

    在1980至1989年间，公司管理委员会离职8人，新吸收11人。

3 N-SING [usually with poss] [usually with poss] 送到；运到；到货；出现 When something is brought to you or becomes available, you can refer to its arrival . 送到；运到；到货；出现

  • I was flicking idly through a newspaper while awaiting the arrival of orange juice and coffee. [+ of]

    我一边无所事事地翻着报纸，一边等着我点的橙汁和咖啡。

  • The coronation broadcast marked the arrival of television.

    加冕典礼的转播标志着电视时代的到来。

SYN birth, delivery, childbirth, nativity  
4 N-SING （某一时间的）到来；（某一事件的）发生 When a particular time comes or a particular event happens, you can refer to its arrival. （某一时间的）到来；（某一事件的）发生

  • He celebrated the arrival of the New Year with a party for his friends. [+ of]

    他为朋友们举行了一个聚会，庆祝新年的到来。

SYN coming, happening, taking place, dawn [literary]  
5 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 初到者；新来的人 You can refer to someone who has just arrived at a place as a new arrival . 初到者；新来的人

  • A high proportion of the new arrivals are skilled professionals.

    新来的人中很大一部分是技术熟练的专业人士。

  • He was the most junior and most recent arrival at the embassy.

    在大使馆里，他是职位最低也是入职最晚的一位。

SYN newcomer, arriver, incomer, visitor  
6 N-SING [usually with poss] （婴儿的）出生，诞生 When a baby is born, you can refer to its arrival. （婴儿的）出生，诞生

  • ...a couple anticipating the arrival of a new child.

    盼望着孩子出生的一对夫妇

SYN birth, delivery, childbirth, nativity  
7 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 新生儿 You can refer to a baby who has just been born as a new arrival . 新生儿

  • Her father was besotted with the new arrival.

    她的父亲完全沉浸在新生儿带来的喜悦中。
