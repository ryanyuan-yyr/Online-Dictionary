obnoxious
ɒbnɒkʃəs əbˈnɑkʃəs ɑbˈnɑkʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 令人讨厌的；使人反感的；可憎的 If you describe someone as obnoxious, you think that they are very unpleasant. 令人讨厌的；使人反感的；可憎的 [disapproval]

  • One of the parents was a most obnoxious character. No-one liked him.

    其中一位家长极为惹人厌，没人喜欢他。

  • The people at my table were so obnoxious I simply had to change my seat.

    我那桌的人如此讨厌，我只好换了座位。

SYN loathsome, offensive, nasty, foul
