submarine
sʌbməriːn ˈsʌbməˌriːn -riːn ˌsʌbməˈrin ˈsʌbməˌrin
Word forms: plural submarines
1 N-COUNT 潜艇(亦使用缩略形式sub) A submarine is a type of ship that can travel both above and below the surface of the sea. The abbreviation sub is also used. 潜艇(亦使用缩略形式sub)

  • ...a nuclear submarine.

    核潜艇

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 水下的;海底的 Submarine means existing below the surface of the sea. 水下的;海底的 [formal]

  • ...submarine caves.

    海底洞穴

  • ...submarine plants.

    海底植物

3 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] 潜艇三明治，长卷三明治(亦使用缩略形式sub) A submarine sandwich is a long soft bread roll filled with a combination of things such as meat, cheese, eggs, and salad. The abbreviation sub is also used. 潜艇三明治，长卷三明治(亦使用缩略形式sub) [US]
