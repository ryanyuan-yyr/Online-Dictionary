incomprehensible
ɪnkɒmprɪhensɪbəl ɪnˌkɑmpriˈhɛnsəbəl ɪnˌkɑmprɪˈhɛnsəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不能理解的;晦涩难懂的;费解的 Something that is incomprehensible is impossible to understand. 不能理解的;晦涩难懂的;费解的

  • Her speech was almost incomprehensible.

    她的演讲几乎无人能懂。

  • ...incomprehensible mathematics puzzles.

    难以破解的数学难题

SYN unintelligible, incoherent, indecipherable, meaningless
