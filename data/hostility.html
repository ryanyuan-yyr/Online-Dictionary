hostility
hɒstɪlɪti hɒˈstɪlɪtɪ hɑsˈtɪləti
1 N-UNCOUNT 敌意;对抗;敌对行为 Hostility is unfriendly or aggressive behaviour towards people or ideas. 敌意;对抗;敌对行为

  • The last decade has witnessed a serious rise in the levels of racism and hostility to Black and ethnic groups. [+ to/towards]

    过去十年里，对黑人及少数族裔的种族歧视和敌对行为变得日益猖獗。

  • Christabel looked at Ron with open hostility.

    克丽丝特布尔带着公然的敌意看着罗恩。

SYN unfriendliness, hatred, animosity, spite  
2 N-UNCOUNT 反对;抵制 Your hostility to something you do not approve of is your opposition to it. 反对;抵制

  • There is hostility among traditionalists to this method of teaching history. [+ to/towards]

    传统主义者反对这种讲授历史的方法。

SYN opposition, resentment, antipathy, aversion
