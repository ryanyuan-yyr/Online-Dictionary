product line
Word forms: plural product lines
N-COUNT 产品线；产品系列 A product line is a group of related products produced by one manufacturer, for example products that are intended to be used for similar purposes or to be sold in similar types of shops. 产品线；产品系列 [business]

  • A well-known UK supermarket launches more than 1,000 new product lines each year.

    一家著名的英国超市每年投放1,000多种新产品。
