uncongenial
ʌnkəndʒiːniəl ˌʌnkənˈdʒiːnjəl
ADJ-GRADED （人）不友好的；（地方）不合意的 If you describe a person or place as uncongenial, you mean that they are unfriendly and unpleasant. （人）不友好的；（地方）不合意的

  • He continued to find the Simpsons uncongenial bores.

    他依然觉得辛普森一家人很讨厌。

  • Hollywood was an uncongenial place to work.

    好莱坞是个不适宜工作的地方。

SYN unfriendly, unpleasant, incompatible, distasteful
