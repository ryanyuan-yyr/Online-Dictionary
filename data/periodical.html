periodical
pɪəriɒdɪkəl ˌpɪriˈɑdɪkəl
Word forms: plural periodicals
1 N-COUNT （尤指学术）期刊 Periodicals are magazines, especially serious or academic ones, that are published at regular intervals. （尤指学术）期刊

  • The walls would be lined with books and periodicals.

    这几面墙会摆放书籍和期刊。

  • ...a monthly periodical.

    月刊

SYN publication, paper, review, magazine  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 间发性的；定期的；周期的 Periodical events or situations happen occasionally, at fairly regular intervals. 间发性的；定期的；周期的

  • She made periodical visits to her dentist.

    她定期去看牙医。

  • ...periodical screening for cancer.

    定期进行肿瘤检查

SYN recurrent, regular, repeated, occasional  
periodically

  • Meetings are held periodically to monitor progress on the case.

    定期举行会议以监督案件的进展。

  • Police were periodically patrolling the area.

    警察定时在这一地区巡逻。
