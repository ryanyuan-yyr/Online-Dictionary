stool
stuːl stul
Word forms: plural stools
1 N-COUNT 凳子 A stool is a seat with legs but no support for your arms or back. 凳子

  • O'Brien sat on a bar stool and leaned his elbows on the counter.

    奥布赖恩坐在一张吧凳上，胳膊肘靠在吧台上。

2 PHRASE 两头落空;鸡飞蛋打 If someone has fallen between two stools, they are unable to decide which of two courses of action to take and as a result they have not done either of them successfully. 两头落空;鸡飞蛋打
3 N-COUNT [usually plural] 大便;粪便 Stools are the pieces of solid waste matter that are passed out of a person's body through their bowels. 大便;粪便 [mainly medicine]
