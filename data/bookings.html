booking
bʊkɪŋ ˈbʊkɪŋ
Word forms: plural bookings
N-COUNT 预约；预订 A booking is the arrangement that you make when you book something such as a hotel room, a table at a restaurant, a theatre seat, or a place on public transport. 预约；预订

  • I suggest you tell him there was a mistake over his late booking.

    我建议你告诉他，他应该及早预订。

SYN reservation, date, appointment, engagement
