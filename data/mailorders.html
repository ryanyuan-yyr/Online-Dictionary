mail order
Word forms: plural mail orders
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft by NOUN, NOUN n] 邮购;函购 Mail order is a system of buying and selling goods. You choose the goods you want from a company by looking at their catalogue, and the company sends them to you by post. 邮购;函购

  • The toys are available by mail order from Opi Toys.

    这些玩具可以从Opi玩具公司邮购。

  • Many of them also offer a mail-order service.

    他们中有许多家也提供邮购服务。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 邮购的商品 Mail orders are goods that have been ordered by mail order. 邮购的商品 [mainly US]

  • I supervise the packing of all mail orders.

    我负责监督所有邮购商品的包装过程。
