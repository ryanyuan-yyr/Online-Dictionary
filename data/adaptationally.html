adaptation
ædæpteɪʃən ˌædəpˈteɪʃən ˌædəpˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural adaptations
1 N-COUNT （电影或电视的）改写本，改编本 An adaptation of a book or play is a film or a television programme that is based on it. （电影或电视的）改写本，改编本

  • ...Branagh's screen adaptation of Shakespeare's Henry the Fifth. [+ of]

    布拉纳改编自莎士比亚的《亨利五世》的影片

2 N-UNCOUNT 适应;作出调整 Adaptation is the act of changing something or changing your behaviour to make it suitable for a new purpose or situation. 适应;作出调整

  • Most living creatures are capable of adaptation when compelled to do so.

    大多数生物都能在迫于压力的情况下适应新环境。

SYN acclimatization, naturalization, habituation, familiarization
