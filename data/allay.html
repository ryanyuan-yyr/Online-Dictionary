allay
əleɪ æˈleɪ əˈleɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense allays, present participle allaying, past tense , past participle allayed
VERB 消除（疑虑、恐惧等） If you allay someone's fears or doubts, you stop them feeling afraid or doubtful. 消除（疑虑、恐惧等） [formal]

  • He did what he could to allay his wife's fears. [VERB noun]

    他竭尽所能来消除妻子的各种恐惧。

SYN reduce, quiet, relax, ease
