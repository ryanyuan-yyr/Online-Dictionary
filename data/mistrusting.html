mistrust
mɪstrʌst ˈmɪsˌtrʌst mɪsˈtrʌst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mistrusts, present participle mistrusting, past tense , past participle mistrusted
1 N-UNCOUNT 不相信;不信任;怀疑 Mistrust is the feeling that you have towards someone who you do not trust. 不相信;不信任;怀疑

  • There was mutual mistrust between the two men.

    两个男人互相猜疑。

  • ...a deep mistrust of state banks. [+ of]

    对国家银行的极端不信任

SYN suspicion, scepticism, distrust, doubt  
2 VERB 不相信;不信任;怀疑 If you mistrust someone or something, you do not trust them. 不相信;不信任;怀疑

  • It frequently appears that Bell mistrusts all journalists. [VERB noun]

    似乎贝尔常常对所有新闻记者都持怀疑态度。

SYN be wary of, suspect, beware, distrust
