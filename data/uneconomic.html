uneconomic
ʌniːkənɒmɪk -ek- ˌʌniːkəˈnɒmɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 获利不多的；效益不好的 If you describe something such as an industry or business as uneconomic, you mean that it does not produce enough profit. 获利不多的；效益不好的 [business]

  • ...the closure of uneconomic factories.

    关闭效益差的工厂

  • The company said the service was uneconomic.

    公司说这项业务不赚钱。

SYN unprofitable, loss-making, non-profit-making, nonpaying  
2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 不合算的；不经济的 If you say that an action or plan is uneconomic, you think it will cost a lot of money and not be successful or not be worth the expense. 不合算的；不经济的

  • It would be uneconomic to try and repair it.

    花钱修理不合算。

  • Sending a replacement jet would have been uneconomic.

    当时派一架喷气式飞机去替换的话，代价太大了。
