bash
bæʃ bæʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bashes, present participle bashing, past tense , past participle bashed
1 N-COUNT 盛会；聚会；庆祝仪式 A bash is a party or celebration, especially a large one held by an official organization or attended by famous people. 盛会；聚会；庆祝仪式 [informal]

  • He threw one of the biggest showbiz bashes of the year as a 36th birthday party for Jerry Hall.

    为了庆祝杰里·霍尔的36岁生日，他举办了当年演艺圈最盛大的庆祝宴会。

SYN party, do [informal] , social, at-home  
2 VERB 痛打；猛揍 If someone bashes you, they attack you by hitting or punching you hard. 痛打；猛揍 [informal]

  • If someone tried to bash my best mate they would have to bash me as well. [VERB noun]

    如果谁想揍我最好的朋友，那我就跟他拼了。

  • I bashed him on the head and dumped him in the cold, cold water. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    我猛击他的头部，然后把他扔进了冰冷冰冷的水里。

  • Two women were hurt and the chef was bashed over the head with a bottle. [be/get V-ed]

    两个女人受伤，厨师头部被人用瓶子击中。

SYN hit, break, beat, strike  
3 VERB 猛敲；猛撞；用力砸 If you bash something, you hit it hard in a rough or careless way. 猛敲；猛撞；用力砸 [informal] [Also VERB n]

  • Too many golfers try to bash the ball out of sand. That spells disaster. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    太多高尔夫球手都想把球从沙子里敲出来，结果酿成了一场灾难。

  • A stand-in drummer bashes on a single snare and a pair of cymbals. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一名临时顶替的鼓手使劲敲着一根响弦和一对铙钹。

4 N-COUNT 重击；猛撞 If you get a bash on a part of your body, someone or something hits you hard, or you bump into something. 重击；猛撞 [informal]
5 VERB （通常指公开地）痛斥，抨击 To bash someone means to criticize them severely, usually in a public way. （通常指公开地）痛斥，抨击 [journalism]

  • The President could continue to bash Democrats as being soft on crime. [VERB noun]

    总统可能会继续抨击民主党人对待犯罪活动心慈手软。

SYN criticize, pan [informal] , condemn, slam [slang]  
6 N-COUNT [usually singular, N at n/-ing] 努力；尝试 If you have a bash at something, you try to do it. 努力；尝试 [British, informal]

  • He's prepared to have a bash at discussing it intelligently.

    他已经准备好尽量巧妙地讨论这个问题。

SYN attempt, go [informal] , try, shot [informal]  
7 See also: -bashing
