indisputable
ɪndɪspjuːtəbəl ˌɪndɪˈspjutəbəl ɪnˈdɪspjutəbəl
ADJ 无可争辩的;不容置疑的 If you say that something is indisputable, you are emphasizing that it is true and cannot be shown to be untrue. 无可争辩的;不容置疑的 [emphasis]

  • It is indisputable that birds in the UK are harbouring this illness.

    无可争辩的是,英国的鸟类携带着这种疾病的病菌。

  • The indisputable fact is that computers carry out logical operations.

    不容置疑的事实是，电脑执行逻辑操作指令。

SYN undeniable, sure, certain, positive  
indisputably

  • She has an indisputably lovely voice.

    毫无疑问，她的声音很好听。
