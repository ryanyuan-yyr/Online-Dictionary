automatic
ɔːtəmætɪk ˌɔtəˈmætɪk
Word forms: plural automatics
1 ADJ 自动的;自动化的 An automatic machine or device is one which has controls that enable it to perform a task without needing to be constantly operated by a person. Automatic methods and processes involve the use of such machines. 自动的;自动化的

  • Modern trains have automatic doors.

    现代火车装有自动门。

SYN mechanical, robot, automated, mechanized  
2 N-COUNT 自动枪 An automatic is a gun that keeps firing shots until you stop pulling the trigger. 自动枪

  • He drew his automatic and began running in the direction of the sounds.

    他拔出自动手枪，向声音传来的方向跑去。

  • The gunmen opened fire with automatic weapons.

    歹徒用自动武器开火了。

3 N-COUNT 自动挡汽车;自动变速汽车 An automatic is a car in which the gears change automatically as the car's speed increases or decreases. 自动挡汽车;自动变速汽车
4 ADJ 无意识的；不假思索的 An automatic action is one that you do without thinking about it. 无意识的；不假思索的

  • All of the automatic body functions, even breathing, are affected.

    身体所有的无意识功能，甚至呼吸，都受到影响。

SYN involuntary, natural, unconscious, mechanical  
automatically

  • Strangely enough, you will automatically wake up after this length of time.

    奇怪的是，过了这样一段时间你就会自己醒来。

  • Automatically Tommy's hand went to his forehead.

    汤米不由自主地用手去摸额头。

5 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （行为、惩罚等）必然的，当然的 If something such as an action or a punishment is automatic, it happens without people needing to think about it because it is the result of a fixed rule or method. （行为、惩罚等）必然的，当然的

  • Those drivers should face an automatic charge of manslaughter.

    那些司机必然会受到过失杀人的指控。

  • Israel offers automatic citizenship to all Jews who want it.

    以色列为所有希望获得公民身份的犹太人敞开大门。

SYN inevitable, certain, necessary, assured  
automatically

  • As an account customer, you are automatically entitled to a variety of benefits.

    作为立有存款账户的客户，您自然可以享受一系列的优惠。

  • Anyone giving in excess of £100 automatically becomes a member of the Trust.

    任何出资超过100英镑者均会自动成为信托基金的成员。
