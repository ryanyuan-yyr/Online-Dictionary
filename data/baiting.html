-baiting -beɪtɪŋ
1 COMB in N-UNCOUNT （用于名词后）表示“攻击”，“嘲笑” You use -baiting after nouns to refer to the activity of attacking a particular group of people or laughing at their beliefs. （用于名词后）表示“攻击”，“嘲笑”
2 COMB in N-UNCOUNT 表示“纵狗斗（獾、熊或牛等）” Badger-baiting, bear-baiting, and bull-baiting involve making these animals fight dogs, while making sure that the animals are unable to defend themselves properly. 表示“纵狗斗（獾、熊或牛等）”
