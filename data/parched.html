parched
pɑːʳtʃt pɑːtʃt
1 ADJ-GRADED （尤指地面或植物）焦干的，焦枯的 If something, especially the ground or a plant, is parched, it is very dry, because there has been no rain. （尤指地面或植物）焦干的，焦枯的

  • The clouds gathered and showers poured down upon the parched earth.

    乌云聚涌，倾盆大雨落在焦干的大地上。

  • ...a hill of parched brown grass.

    满山的褐色枯草

SYN dried out or up, dry, withered, scorched  
2 ADJ-GRADED （嘴、喉咙或嘴唇）干燥的 If your mouth, throat, or lips are parched, they are unpleasantly dry. （嘴、喉咙或嘴唇）干燥的
3 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 干渴的；焦渴的 If you say that you are parched, you mean that you are very thirsty. 干渴的；焦渴的 [informal]
