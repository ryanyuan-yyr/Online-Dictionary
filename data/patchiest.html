patchy
pætʃi ˈpætʃi
Word forms: comparative patchier, superlative patchiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 分布不均衡的；散落的；斑驳的 A patchy substance or colour exists in some places but not in others, or is thick in some places and thin in others. 分布不均衡的；散落的；斑驳的

  • Thick patchy fog and irresponsible driving were to blame.

    局部浓雾和驾驶员不负责任是罪魁祸首。

  • Bottle tans can make your legs, arms and face look a patchy orange colour.

    瓶装美黑剂可能会让你的腿、手臂和脸呈现出深一块、浅一块的橙色。

  • ...the brown, patchy grass.

    疏密不均的棕色草地

SYN uneven, irregular, variegated, spotty  
2 ADJ-GRADED 时好时坏的；不稳定的；差强人意的 If something is patchy, it is not completely reliable or satisfactory because it is not always good. 时好时坏的；不稳定的；差强人意的

  • The evidence is patchy.

    证据不充分。

  • Transport is difficult, communications are patchy.

    交通困难，通信时断时续。

  • The rest of the acting is patchy at best.

    剩下的表演顶多算是差强人意。
