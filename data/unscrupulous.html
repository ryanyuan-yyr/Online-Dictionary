unscrupulous
ʌnskruːpjʊləs ʌnˈskrupjələs
ADJ-GRADED 不诚实的；不道德的；无道德原则的 If you describe a person as unscrupulous, you are critical of the fact that they are prepared to act in a dishonest or immoral way in order to get what they want. 不诚实的；不道德的；无道德原则的 [disapproval]

  • These kids are being exploited by very unscrupulous people.

    这些孩子正在被无良之人利用。

  • ...the unscrupulous use of hostages.

    对人质的无耻利用

SYN unprincipled, corrupt, crooked [informal] , ruthless
