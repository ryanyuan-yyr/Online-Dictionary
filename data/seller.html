seller
seləʳ ˈsɛlə ˈsɛlər
Word forms: plural sellers
1 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 卖者;销售者;销售商 A seller of a type of thing is a person or company that sells that type of thing. 卖者;销售者;销售商

  • ...a flower seller.

    卖花人

  • ...Kraft, the largest seller of cheese in the United States. [+ of]

    卡夫公司，美国最大的奶酪销售商

SYN dealer, merchant, vendor, agent  
2 N-COUNT (交易的)卖方 In a business deal, the seller is the person who is selling something to someone else. (交易的)卖方

  • In theory, the buyer could ask the seller to have a test carried out.

    理论上，买方可以要求卖方进行一次测试。

  • Housing became a seller's market, and prices zoomed up.

    房地产市场成了卖方市场，价格一路飙升。

3 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] (畅销或滞销的)商品 If you describe a product as, for example, a big seller, you mean that large numbers of it are being sold. (畅销或滞销的)商品

  • The gift shop's biggest seller is a photo of Nixon meeting Presley.

    这家礼品店最畅销的商品是尼克松会见猫王普雷斯利的照片。
