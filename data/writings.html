writing
raɪtɪŋ ˈraɪtɪŋ
Word forms: plural writings
1 N-UNCOUNT (写下或印出的)文字 Writing is something that has been written or printed. (写下或印出的)文字

  • 'It's from a notebook,' the sheriff said, 'And there's writing on it.'. [+ on]

    “这是在一个笔记本里找到的，”法官道，“上面还有文字。”

  • If you have a complaint about your holiday, please inform us in writing.

    如果您对您的假日有什么意见，请书面告知我们。

2 N-UNCOUNT (尤指评论语言风格时的)文字作品，文章 You can refer to any piece of written work as writing, especially when you are considering the style of language used in it. (尤指评论语言风格时的)文字作品，文章

  • The writing is brutally tough and savagely humorous.

    该作品带有残酷的强悍和野性的幽默感。

  • It was such a brilliant piece of writing.

    这真是一篇才华横溢的文章。

3 N-UNCOUNT (尤指为赚取稿费而进行的)写作 Writing is the activity of writing, especially of writing books for money. (尤指为赚取稿费而进行的)写作

  • She had begun to be a little bored with novel writing.

    她开始对写小说有些厌倦了。

  • ...activities to help prepare children for writing.

    帮助孩子准备写作的活动

4 N-UNCOUNT [usually poss NOUN] 笔迹;字迹;书写 Your writing is the way that you write with a pen or pencil, which can usually be recognized as belonging to you. 笔迹;字迹;书写

  • It was a little difficult to read your writing.

    你写的字有点不好认。

  • I think it's due to being left handed that he's got terrible writing.

    我想他的书写这么糟糕是因为他是左撇子。

SYN script, hand, print, printing  
5 N-PLURAL [usually with poss] (尤指某一作者有关某一专题的全部)著作，作品 An author's writings are all the things that he or she has written, especially on a particular subject. (尤指某一作者有关某一专题的全部)著作，作品

  • Althusser's writings are focused mainly on France.

    阿尔都塞的著作主要是论述法国的。

  • The pieces he is reading are adapted from the writings of Michael Frayn. [+ of]

    他目前阅读的作品改编自迈克尔·弗雷恩的著作。

SYN document, work, book, letter  
6 PHRASE 凶兆毕现；有大难临头的预兆 If you say that the writing is on the wall, you mean that there are clear signs that a situation is going to become very difficult or unpleasant. 凶兆毕现；有大难临头的预兆

  • The writing is clearly on the wall. If we do nothing about it, we shall only have ourselves to blame.

    凶兆显而易见。如果我们对此毫无行动，就只能怨自己了。
