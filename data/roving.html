roving
roʊvɪŋ ˈrəʊvɪŋ ˈroʊvɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （人）流动的，漂泊的,不固定的 You use roving to describe a person who travels around, rather than staying in a fixed place. （人）流动的，漂泊的,不固定的

  • ...a roving reporter.

    流动记者

  • Left to raise themselves on the streets, these children form roving bands of delinquents.

    这些孩子被迫流浪街头，结成了一些流窜的犯罪团伙。

2 PHRASE [NOUN inflects] (男人)好色，眼神不安分 If you say that a man has a roving eye, you are criticizing him for continually paying attention to different women. (男人)好色，眼神不安分 [disapproval]
