hybridize
haɪbrɪdaɪz ˈhaɪbrɪˌdaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hybridizes, present participle hybridizing, past tense , past participle hybridized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 hybridise
V-RECIP-ERG （使）（动植物）杂交 If one species of plant or animal hybridizes with another, the species reproduce together to make a hybrid. You can also say that you hybridize one species of plant or animal with another. （使）（动植物）杂交 [technical]

  • All sorts of colours will result as these flowers hybridise freely. [VERB]

    这些花自由杂交后会产生各种各样的颜色。

  • Wild boar readily hybridises with the domestic pig. [VERB + with]

    野猪很容易和家猪杂交。

  • Hybridising the two species will reduce the red to orange. [VERB noun]

    将这两个物种杂交后颜色会由红色淡化成橙色。

  • Some people will take the seeds and hybridize the resulting plants with others of their own. [VERB noun + with]

    有些人会拿取种子，然后将培育出来的植株和自己的其他植株进行杂交。
