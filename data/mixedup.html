mixed up
mɪkst ʌp
1 ADJ-GRADED （常指因情感或社会问题而）头脑混乱的，迷惑不解的 If you are mixed up, you are confused, often because of emotional or social problems. （常指因情感或社会问题而）头脑混乱的，迷惑不解的

  • I think he's a rather mixed up kid.

    我认为他是一个思想有障碍的孩子。

  • I get mixed up about times and places.

    我没有时间感和方位感。

2 ADJ 卷入…的;牵连进…的;与…厮混在一起的 To be mixed up in something bad, or with someone you disapprove of, means to be involved in it or with them. 卷入…的;牵连进…的;与…厮混在一起的

  • How could David be mixed up in a murder?.

    戴维怎么会卷进一宗谋杀案呢？

  • Why did I ever get mixed up with you?

    我怎么会曾经跟你混在一起呢？
