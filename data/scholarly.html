scholarly
skɒləʳli ˈskɑlərli
1 ADJ-GRADED 勤奋好学的;有学问的 A scholarly person spends a lot of time studying and knows a lot about academic subjects. 勤奋好学的;有学问的

  • He was an intellectual, scholarly man.

    他才华横溢，知识渊博。

SYN learned, academic, intellectual, lettered  
2 ADJ-GRADED (书籍、文章)学术的，学术性的 A scholarly book or article contains a lot of academic information and is intended for academic readers. (书籍、文章)学术的，学术性的

  • ...the more scholarly academic journals.

    学术性较强的学术期刊

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (问题、活动)学术的，学术性的,学术界的 Scholarly matters and activities involve people who do academic research. (问题、活动)学术的，学术性的,学术界的

  • This has been the subject of intense scholarly debate.

    这一直是学术讨论的热门话题。

  • Faculty members devote most of their time to scholarly research.

    全院教师大部分时间都用来从事学术研究。
