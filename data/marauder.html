marauder
mərɔːdəʳ
Word forms: plural marauders
N-COUNT 强盗;抢劫者;攫食的动物 If you describe a group of people or animals as marauders, you mean they are unpleasant and dangerous, because they wander around looking for opportunities to steal or kill. 强盗;抢劫者;攫食的动物 [literary]

  • Numb with terror, she stared at the departing marauders.

    她吓得呆若木鸡，眼睁睁地看着劫匪们离去。

  • They were raided by roaming bands of marauders.

    他们遭到了流窜作案的抢劫团伙的袭击。

SYN raider, outlaw, bandit, pirate
