lime
laɪm laɪm
Word forms: plural limes
1 N-VAR 酸橙 A lime is a green fruit that tastes like a lemon. Limes grow on trees in tropical countries. 酸橙

  • ...peeled slices of lime.

    剥了皮的酸橙片

  • Oranges, lemons and limes were found to cure scurvy.

    人们发现橙子、柠檬和酸橙能治疗坏血病。

  • Add a few drops of lime juice.

    加几滴酸橙汁。

2 N-UNCOUNT 酸橙味饮料 Lime is a drink that tastes of limes. 酸橙味饮料

  • ...a pint of lager and lime.

    一品脱莱姆淡啤酒

3 N-COUNT 欧椴树 A lime is a large tree with pale green leaves. It is often planted in parks in towns and cities. 欧椴树

  • ...dilapidated avenues of limes.

    破败不堪的欧椴树林阴道

4 N-UNCOUNT 石灰 Lime is a substance containing calcium. It is found in soil and water. 石灰

  • If your soil is very acid, add lime.

    如果土壤酸性太强，就加些石灰。

Lime is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...lime-rich sand.

    石灰含量高的沙子

  • ...old lime-stained baths.

    有石灰渍的旧浴缸
