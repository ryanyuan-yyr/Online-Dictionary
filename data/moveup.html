move up
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤指为靠近某人或给某人让出空间而)挪动，靠拢 If you move up, you change your position, especially in order to be nearer someone or to make room for someone else. (尤指为靠近某人或给某人让出空间而)挪动，靠拢

  • Move up, John, and let the lady sit down. [VERB PREP.]

    约翰，挪一挪，让这位女士坐下。

2 PHRASAL VERB 上涨;升级;升迁 If someone or something moves up, they go to a higher level, grade, or class. 上涨;升级;升迁

  • Share prices moved up. [VERB PREP.]

    股票价格上涨了。

  • Children learn in mixed ability classes and move up a class each year. [VERB PREP. noun]

    孩子们在学生能力参差不齐的班级学习，每年升一级。
