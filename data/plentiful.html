plentiful
plentɪfʊl ˈplɛntɪfəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 丰富的；充足的；多的 Things that are plentiful exist in such large amounts or numbers that there is enough for people's wants or needs. 丰富的；充足的；多的

  • Fish are plentiful in the lake.

    这个湖里鱼很多。

  • ...a plentiful supply of vegetables and salads and fruits.

    蔬菜、色拉和水果的充分供应

plentifully

  • Nettle grows plentifully on any rich waste ground.

    荨麻在任何肥沃的荒地上都能茂盛地生长。
