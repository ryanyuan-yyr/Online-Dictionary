while away
PHRASAL VERB 消磨，打发(时间) If you while away the time in a particular way, you spend time in that way, because you are waiting for something else to happen, or because you have nothing else to do. 消磨，打发(时间) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Miss Bennett whiled away the hours playing old films on her video-recorder. [VERB PREP. noun]

    贝内特小姐用录像机播放老电影来消磨时间。

SYN pass, spend, kill, occupy
