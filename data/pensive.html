pensive
pensɪv ˈpɛnsɪv
ADJ-GRADED 沉思的；忧思的 If you are pensive, you are thinking deeply about something, especially something that worries you slightly. 沉思的；忧思的

  • He looked suddenly sombre, pensive.

    他突然看起来很阴郁，一副忧虑的样子。

SYN thoughtful, serious, sad, blue [informal]  
pensively

  • Angela stared pensively out of the window.

    安杰拉忧郁地望着窗外。
