muff
mʌf mʌf
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense muffs, present participle muffing, past tense , past participle muffed
1 VERB 把…弄糟;做…时出差错 If you muff something, you do it badly or you make a mistake while you are doing it, so that it is not successful. 把…弄糟;做…时出差错 [informal]

  • He muffed his opening speech. [VERB noun]

    他的开幕讲话搞砸了。

  • ...a muffed opportunity. [VERB-ed]

    错失的机会

SYN botch, bungle, fluff [informal] , spoil  
2 N-COUNT (防寒用的)毛皮短筒，手笼 A muff is a piece of fur or thick cloth shaped like a short hollow cylinder. You wear a muff on your hands to keep them warm in cold weather. (防寒用的)毛皮短筒，手笼
3 N-PLURAL muffs 同 earmuffs Muffs are the same as earmuffs . muffs 同 earmuffs
