loom
luːm lum
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense looms, present participle looming, past tense , past participle loomed
1 VERB （庞然大物或形状模糊之物）耸现，出现 If something looms over you, it appears as a large or unclear shape, often in a frightening way. （庞然大物或形状模糊之物）耸现，出现 [Also VERB]

  • Vincent loomed over me, as pale and grey as a tombstone. [VERB prep./adv.]

    文森特赫然耸现在我面前，面色灰白得像一块墓碑。

  • ...the bleak mountains that loomed out of the blackness and towered around us. [VERB prep./adv.]

    黑暗中若隐若现、耸立在我们四周的荒山

SYN appear, emerge, hover, take shape  
2 VERB 迫近；逼近；即将爆发 If a worrying or threatening situation or event is looming, it seems likely to happen soon. 迫近；逼近；即将爆发 [journalism]

  • Another government spending crisis is looming in the United States. [VERB]

    另一场政府开支危机在美国一触即发。

  • The threat of renewed civil war looms ahead. [VERB adv./prep.]

    内战战火重燃的威胁正迫在眉睫。

  • ...the looming threat of recession. [VERB-ing]

    日益迫近的经济衰退的威胁

SYN threaten, be close, menace, be imminent  
3 PHRASE [VERB inflects] （尤指问题或忧虑）挥之不去，萦绕心中，显得突出 If something, especially a problem or worry looms large, it occupies a lot of your thoughts and seems important. （尤指问题或忧虑）挥之不去，萦绕心中，显得突出

  • ...the terrible problem of armed crime now looming large in our society.

    已日渐成为我们当今社会一块心病的严重的持械犯罪问题

  • As such tensions increase, they loom larger in Russia's domestic politics.

    这种紧张关系的不断加剧使其在俄罗斯国内政治中愈发凸显。

4 N-COUNT 织机；织布机 A loom is a machine that is used for weaving thread into cloth. 织机；织布机
Phrasal verbs:
loom up
