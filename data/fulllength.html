full-length
ˈfʊlˈlɛŋkθ ˈfʊlˈlɛŋθ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (书籍、唱片或电影)标准长度的，未经删节的 A full-length book, record, or film is the normal length, rather than being shorter than normal. (书籍、唱片或电影)标准长度的，未经删节的

  • ...his first full-length recording in well over a decade.

    他十好几年以来第一张足长的唱片

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (上衣或裙子)长及小腿(或脚踝)的;(衣袖)长及手腕的 A full-length coat or skirt is long enough to reach the lower part of a person's leg, almost to the ankles. A full-length sleeve reaches a person's wrist. (上衣或裙子)长及小腿(或脚踝)的;(衣袖)长及手腕的
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (窗帘等)长及地面的，落地的 Full-length curtains or other furnishings reach to the floor. (窗帘等)长及地面的，落地的
4 ADJ [ADJ n] (镜子或画像)全身的 A full-length mirror or painting shows the whole of a person. (镜子或画像)全身的
5 ADV [ADVERB after verb] (四肢)伸展开地，摊平地 Someone who is lying full-length, is lying down flat and stretched out. (四肢)伸展开地，摊平地

  • She stretched herself out full-length.

    她摊平了四肢。
