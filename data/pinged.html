ping
pɪŋ pɪŋ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pings, present participle pinging, past tense pinged
VERB （铃或金属）发出当（或砰）的响声 If a bell or a piece of metal pings, it makes a short, high-pitched noise. （铃或金属）发出当（或砰）的响声

  • The lift bell pinged at the fourth floor. [VERB]

    电梯铃在第五层响了。

Ping is also a noun. ...a metallic ping.似金属撞击的砰然一声响
