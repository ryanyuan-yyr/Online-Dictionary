seclusion
sɪkluːʒən sɪˈkluʒən
N-UNCOUNT 隔绝;隐退;隐居 If you are living in seclusion, you are in a quiet place away from other people. 隔绝;隐退;隐居

  • She lived in seclusion with her husband on their farm in Panama.

    她与丈夫隐居在他们在巴拿马的农场。

  • They love the seclusion of their garden. [+ of]

    他们喜欢自家花园的幽静。

SYN privacy, isolation, solitude, hiding
