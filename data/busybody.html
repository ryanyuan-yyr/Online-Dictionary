busybody
bɪzibɒdi ˈbɪziˌbɑdi
Word forms: plural busybodies
N-COUNT 好管闲事的人 If you refer to someone as a busybody, you are criticizing the way they interfere in other people's affairs. 好管闲事的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • This government is full of interfering busybodies.

    这个政府里净是爱管闲事的人。

SYN nosy parker [informal] , gossip, troublemaker, snoop
