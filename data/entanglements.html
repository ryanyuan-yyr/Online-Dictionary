entanglement
ɪntæŋgəlmənt ɪnˈtæŋɡəlmənt
Word forms: plural entanglements
1 N-COUNT 复杂关系（或情况）；纠葛;瓜葛 An entanglement is a complicated or difficult relationship or situation. 复杂关系（或情况）；纠葛;瓜葛

  • ...romantic entangle-ments.

    爱情纠葛

  • ...a military and political entanglement the Government probably doesn't want.

    政府可能并不想看到的军政之间的纠葛

2 N-VAR 纠缠;缠绕;缠住 If things become entangled, you can refer to this as entanglement . 纠缠;缠绕;缠住

  • Many dolphins are accidentally killed through entanglement with fishing equipment. [+ with]

    很多海豚因为被渔具缠住而意外死亡。

SYN becoming entangled, mix-up, becoming enmeshed, becoming ensnared
