half-truth ˈhæfˌtruθ
(also half truth)Word forms: plural half-truths
N-COUNT 半真半假的陈述;真伪混同的欺骗性陈述 If you describe statements as half-truths, you mean that they are only partly based on fact and are intended or likely to deceive people. 半真半假的陈述;真伪混同的欺骗性陈述
