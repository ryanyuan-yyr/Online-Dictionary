don
dɒn dɑn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dons, present participle donning, past tense , past participle donned
1 VERB 穿上;戴上;披上 If you don clothing, you put it on. 穿上;戴上;披上 [written]

  • The crowd threw petrol bombs at the police, who responded by donning riot gear. [VERB noun]

    人群向警察扔汽油弹，警察则穿上了防暴衣。

SYN put on, get into, dress in, pull on  
2 N-COUNT (英国牛津或剑桥大学的)讲师，教师 A don is a lecturer at Oxford or Cambridge University in England. (英国牛津或剑桥大学的)讲师，教师 [British]
Lecturers from any university are sometimes referred to as dons .
