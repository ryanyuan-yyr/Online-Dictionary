emblem
embləm ˈɛmbləm
Word forms: plural emblems
1 N-COUNT （国家或组织的）徽章，纹章 An emblem is a design representing a country or organization. （国家或组织的）徽章，纹章

  • ...the emblem of the Soviet Union. [+ of]

    苏联国徽

  • ...the Red Cross emblem.

    红十字会徽章

SYN crest, mark, design, image  
2 N-COUNT 象征;标志 An emblem is something that represents a quality or idea. 象征;标志

  • The eagle was an emblem of strength and courage. [+ of]

    鹰是力量和勇气的象征。

SYN representation, symbol, mark, sign
