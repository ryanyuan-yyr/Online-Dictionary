per diem
pəʳ diːəm ˈpɜː ˈdaɪɛm ˈdiːɛm pər ˈdiəm ˈdaɪəm
N-SING [oft NOUN n] 日补贴；日津贴 A per diem is an amount of money that someone is given to cover their daily expenses while they are working. 日补贴；日津贴 [mainly US]

  • He received a per diem allowance to cover his travel expenses.

    他拿到了一笔日补贴以弥补他的差旅费。
