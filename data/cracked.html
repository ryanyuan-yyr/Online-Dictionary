cracked
krækt krækt
1 ADJ-GRADED 破裂的;有裂纹的 An object that is cracked has lines on its surface because it is damaged. 破裂的;有裂纹的

  • The ceiling was grey and cracked.

    天花板是灰色的，而且有裂纹。

  • ...a cracked mirror.

    破裂的镜子

SYN broken, damaged, split, chipped  
2 ADJ-GRADED (嗓音)沙哑的，颤抖的;(律音)刺耳的，不齐的 A cracked voice or a cracked musical note sounds rough and unsteady. (嗓音)沙哑的，颤抖的;(律音)刺耳的，不齐的

  • When he spoke, his voice was hoarse and cracked.

    他说话时声音沙哑颤抖。

3 ADJ-GRADED (行为或想法)奇怪的，错乱的 If you say that someone is cracked, you think that their behaviour or ideas are very strange. (行为或想法)奇怪的，错乱的 [informal, disapproval]
