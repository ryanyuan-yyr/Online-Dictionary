motto
mɒtoʊ ˈmɑtoʊ
Word forms: plural mottoes, plural mottos
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 箴言;格言;座右铭 A motto is a short sentence or phrase that expresses a rule for sensible behaviour, especially a way of behaving in a particular situation. 箴言;格言;座右铭

  • Our motto is 'Plan for the worst and hope for the best'.

    我们的格言是“做最坏的打算，抱最大的希望”。

SYN saying, slogan, maxim, rule
