cum laude
kʊm laʊdeɪ kʌm ˈlɔːdɪ kʊm ˈlaʊdeɪ kʊm ˈlaʊdeɪ ˈlaʊdɛ ˈlaʊdə ˈlaʊdi
ADV (第三级)优等地If a college student graduates cum laude, they receive the third highest honour that is possible. The second-highest grade is known as magna cum laude, and the highest grade of all is known as summa cum laude. (第三级)优等地 [US]

  • In 1963, she graduated cum laude from Harvard.

    1963年，她以三级优等成绩从哈佛毕业。

  • He graduated magna cum laude.

    他以二级优等成绩毕业。

  • Jeremy Heyl graduated summa cum laude with a degree in Astrophysics.

    杰里米• 海尔以一等全优成绩获得天文学学位。
