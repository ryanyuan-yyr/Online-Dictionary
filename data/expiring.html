expire
ɪkspaɪəʳ ɪkˈspaɪə ɛkˈspaɪr ɪkˈspaɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense expires, present participle expiring, past tense , past participle expired
1 VERB 到期;期满;失效 When something such as a contract, deadline, or visa expires, it comes to an end or is no longer valid. 到期;期满;失效

  • He had lived illegally in the United States for five years after his visitor's visa expired. [VERB]

    在访问签证到期后，他又在美国非法居住了5年。

SYN become invalid, end, finish, conclude  
2 VERB 气绝;死亡;故去 When someone expires, they die. 气绝;死亡;故去 [literary]

  • He endured excruciating agonies before he finally expired. [VERB]

    在最终离世前他经受了极大的痛苦。
