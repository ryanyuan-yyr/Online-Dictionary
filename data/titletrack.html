title track
ˈtaɪtəl træk
Word forms: plural title tracks
N-COUNT [usually singular] (光碟、唱片、磁带上的)标题歌，标题曲 The title track on a CD, record, or tape is a song or piece of music that has the same title as the CD, record, or tape. (光碟、唱片、磁带上的)标题歌，标题曲

  • They come from Tuam, a place they refer to on the title track of their album, 'All the Way From Tuam'. [+ of]
