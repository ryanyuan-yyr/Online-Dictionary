donkey
dɒŋki ˈdɑŋki ˈdɔŋki ˈdʌŋki
Word forms: plural donkeys
1 N-COUNT 驴 A donkey is an animal which is like a horse but which is smaller and has longer ears. 驴
SYN ass, mule, jackass, neddy  
2 PHRASE 很久;多年 For donkey'syears means for a very long time. 很久;多年

  • I've been a vegetarian for donkey's years.

    多年以来我一直是个素食者。

  • ...old iron mines that haven't been used in donkey's years.

    废弃已久的旧铁矿
