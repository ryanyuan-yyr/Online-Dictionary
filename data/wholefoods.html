wholefood
hoʊlfuːd
Word forms: plural wholefoods
N-MASS (未经过精加工、无人工添加剂的)粗制食物，全天然食物 Wholefoods are foods which have not been processed much and which have not had artificial ingredients added. (未经过精加工、无人工添加剂的)粗制食物，全天然食物 [mainly British]

  • It pays to avoid food additives and eat only wholefoods.

    避免摄入食物添加剂而只吃全天然食物是有好处的。

  • A healthy diet should consist of wholefood.

    健康饮食应由全天然食物构成。
