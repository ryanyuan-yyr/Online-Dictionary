jackass
dʒækæs ˈdʒækˌæs ˈdʒækˌæs
Word forms: jackasses
N-COUNT [oft N n] 笨蛋; 蠢货If you call someone a jackass, you mean they are very stupid or they have done something very stupid. 笨蛋; 蠢货 [informal, vulgar, disapproval]

  • Paul is a tedious, self-absorbed jackass who neglects his girlfriend.

    保罗是个烦人的、自以为是的笨蛋，把他的女朋友忽略了。

  • I hate to talk bad about my government, but it is a jackass government.

    我讨厌说自己政府的坏话，但这就是个笨蛋政府。
