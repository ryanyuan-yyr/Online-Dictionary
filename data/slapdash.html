slapdash
slæpdæʃ slæpˌdæʃ
(also slap-dash)
ADJ-GRADED 马虎的;草率的;鲁莽的;粗心大意的 If you describe someone as slapdash, you mean that they do things carelessly without much thinking or planning. 马虎的;草率的;鲁莽的;粗心大意的 [disapproval]

  • ...a slapdash student.

    粗心大意的学生

  • Malcolm's work methods appear amazingly slapdash.

    马尔科姆的工作方法显得极其粗枝大叶。

SYN careless, sloppy [informal] , hasty, disorderly
