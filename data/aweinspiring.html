awe-inspiring
ADJ-GRADED 令人敬佩的;令人惊叹的;令人望而生畏的 If you describe someone or something as awe-inspiring, you are emphasizing that you think that they are remarkable and amazing, although sometimes rather frightening. 令人敬佩的;令人惊叹的;令人望而生畏的 [emphasis]

  • ...a museum with an awe-inspiring display of jewellery.

    展出了一批令人无比惊叹的珠宝的博物馆

  • The higher we climbed, the more awe-inspiring the scenery became.

    我们爬得越高，风景越是让人叹绝。

SYN impressive, striking, wonderful, amazing
