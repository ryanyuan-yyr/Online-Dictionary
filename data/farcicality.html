farcical
fɑːʳsɪkəl ˈfɑrsɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 胡闹的;滑稽的;荒诞的 If you describe a situation or event as farcical, you mean that it is so silly or extreme that you are unable to take it seriously. 胡闹的;滑稽的;荒诞的 [disapproval]

  • ...a farcical nine months' jail sentence imposed yesterday on a killer.

    昨天判处一个杀人犯 9 个月监禁的荒唐判决

SYN ludicrous, ridiculous, absurd, preposterous
