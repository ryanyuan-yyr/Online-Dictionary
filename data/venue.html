venue
venjuː ˈvɛnˌju
Word forms: plural venues
N-COUNT （事件或活动的）发生地,举办地点;场地 The venue for an event or activity is the place where it will happen. （事件或活动的）发生地,举办地点;场地

  • Birmingham's International Convention Centre is the venue for a three-day arts festival. [+ for]

    为期3天的艺术节在伯明翰的国际会议中心举办。

  • Peace talks failed to take place because of a dispute over the venue.

    由于在谈判地点上存在分歧，和平谈判未能举行。
