gross out
PHRASAL VERB 令…恶心；使…作呕 If something grosses you out, it makes you feel disgusted. 令…恶心；使…作呕 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Ordinarily, Anastasia was grossed out when her father spoke to total strangers in public. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    父亲在公共场所与陌生人说话时，阿纳斯塔西娅通常都会觉得很不舒服。

  • Your eating habits even grossed out my mother. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你的饮食习惯甚至连我妈妈都不能接受。
