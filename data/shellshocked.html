shell-shocked
(also shell shocked)
1 ADJ-GRADED (通常指因为发生某些不好的事而)非常震惊的，极度惊恐的 If you say that someone is shell-shocked, you mean that they are very shocked, usually because something bad has happened. (通常指因为发生某些不好的事而)非常震惊的，极度惊恐的 [informal]

  • We were shell-shocked when Chelsea took the lead.

    切尔西队领先让我们十分震惊。

  • ...shell-shocked investors.

    惊呆了的投资者们

2 ADJ-GRADED 患炮弹休克症的 If someone is shell-shocked, they have a confused or nervous mental condition as a result of a shocking experience such as being in a war or an accident. 患炮弹休克症的

  • ...a shell-shocked war veteran.

    患了炮弹休克症的退伍老兵
