dampness
dæmpnəs
1 N-UNCOUNT 湿气;潮气 Dampness is moisture in the air, or on the surface of something. 湿气;潮气

  • I could see big circles of dampness under each arm.

    我能看到每只胳膊下都湿了一大圈。

  • The tins had to be kept away from dampness.

    罐头应该防止受潮。

2 N-UNCOUNT 潮湿 Dampness is the quality of being damp. 潮湿

  • The dampness of the forest did not agree with him physically.

    他的身体不能适应森林的潮湿。
