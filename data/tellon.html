tell on
PHRASAL VERB 告发;检举;告…的状 If you tell on someone, you give information about them to a person in authority, especially if they have done something wrong. 告发;检举;告…的状 [informal]

  • Never mind, I won't tell on you. [VERB PREP. noun]

    别担心，我不会告发你。

  • I'll tell my mummy on you. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    我要到妈妈那儿告你一状。

SYN inform on, shop [British , informal] , give someone away, denounce
