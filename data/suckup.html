suck up
PHRASAL VERB 奉承;巴结;拍马屁 You say that someone is sucking up to a person in authority when you do not like the fact that they are trying to please the person because of the person's position. 奉承;巴结;拍马屁 [informal, disapproval] [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • She kept sucking up to the teachers, especially Mrs Clements. [VERB PREP. + to]

    她不停地巴结老师，尤其是克莱门特夫人。
