scarlet
skɑːʳlət ˈskɑrlɪt
Word forms: plural scarlets
1 COLOUR 猩红色;绯红色;鲜红色 Something that is scarlet is bright red. 猩红色;绯红色;鲜红色

  • ...her scarlet lipstick.

    她鲜红色的唇膏

2 COLOUR [usu v-link COLOUR] (脸)羞红的,红晕的,涨红的 If someone with pale skin turns or goes scarlet, their face becomes redder than usual because they are very embarrassed or angry. (脸)羞红的,红晕的,涨红的

  • She turned scarlet from embarrassment, once she realized what she had done.

    等她明白自己的所作所为后，尴尬得满脸通红。

  • Her face went bright scarlet.

    她的脸变得通红。
