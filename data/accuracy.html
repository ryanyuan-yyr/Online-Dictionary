accuracy
ækjʊrəsi ˈækjərəsi
1 N-UNCOUNT 精确（性）；准确（性） The accuracy of information or measurements is their quality of being true or correct, even in small details. 精确（性）；准确（性）

  • We cannot guarantee the accuracy of these figures. [+ of]

    我们不能保证这些数据的准确性。

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft with NOUN] 准确无误 If someone or something performs a task, for example hitting a target, with accuracy, they do it in an exact way without making a mistake. 准确无误

  • ...weapons that could fire with accuracy at targets 3,000 yards away.

    能准确无误地击中3,000码以外目标的武器

  • Every bank pays close attention to the speed and accuracy of its staff.

    每家银行都非常关注雇员的工作速度和无差错率。

SYN precision, correctness, exactitude, exactness
