labour market
ˈleɪbə ˈmɑːkɪt
Word forms: plural labour markets
N-COUNT [usually singular] 劳动力市场 When you talk about the labour market, you are referring to all the people who are able to work and want jobs in a country or area, in relation to the number of jobs there are available in that country or area. 劳动力市场 [business]

  • The longer people have been unemployed, the harder it is for them to compete in the labour market.

    失业时间越长，就越难在劳动力市场与别人竞争。
