dissimulate
dɪsɪmjʊleɪt dɪˈsɪmjuˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dissimulates, present participle dissimulating, past tense , past participle dissimulated
VERB 掩盖，掩饰(情感或动机) When people dissimulate, they hide their true feelings, intentions, or nature. 掩盖，掩饰(情感或动机) [formal]

  • This man was too injured to dissimulate well. [VERB]

    这个人受伤严重，无法完全遮掩住。

  • They were decked out in tracksuits, seemingly to dissimulate their true function. [VERB noun]

    他们都穿着田径服，似乎想掩饰他们的真实职能。

SYN pretend, hide, mask, disguise
