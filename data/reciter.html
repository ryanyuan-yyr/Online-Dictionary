recite
rɪsaɪt rɪˈsaɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recites, present participle reciting, past tense , past participle recited
1 VERB 朗诵;背诵;朗读 When someone recites a poem or other piece of writing, they say it aloud after they have learned it. 朗诵;背诵;朗读 [Also VERB]

  • They recited poetry to one another. [VERB noun]

    他们互相朗诵诗歌。

SYN perform, relate, deliver, repeat  
2 VERB （大声）说出，细数，列举 If you recite something such as a list, you say it aloud. （大声）说出，细数，列举

  • All he could do was recite a list of Government failings. [VERB noun]

    他能做的只是列举政府的种种不是。

  • She suddenly realized that Wim was reciting Kirk's telephone number. [VERB noun]

    她突然意识到威姆正在念的是柯克的电话号码。
