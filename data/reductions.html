reduction
rɪdʌkʃən rɪˈdʌkʃən
Word forms: plural reductions
1 N-COUNT 减少；降低 When there is a reduction in something, it is made smaller. 减少；降低

  • ...a future reduction in UK interest rates.

    未来英国利率的下调

  • Many companies have announced dramatic reductions in staff.

    许多公司已经宣布大幅裁员。

SYN decrease, lowering, lessening, minimizing  
2 N-UNCOUNT 减少；降低；缩小 Reduction is the act of making something smaller in size or amount, or less in degree. 减少；降低；缩小

  • ...a new strategic arms reduction agreement.

    新的战略武器削减协议

SYN cut, cutting, trimming, pruning
