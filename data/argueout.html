argue out
PHRASAL VERB （为得出结论或决定而）充分讨论 If two or more people argue something out, they discuss it thoroughly in order to reach a conclusion or decision. （为得出结论或决定而）充分讨论 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • If there's a dispute we argue it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果有了纷争，我们就说清楚。

  • The question of divorce was discussed and argued out in the frankest tones. [VERB noun PREP.]

    离婚问题经过开诚公布的商讨最终得出了结果。
