ordinary
ɔːʳdɪnri ˈɔːdənrɪ -neri ˈɔrdənˌɛri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 平常的；普通的；一般的；平凡的 Ordinary people or things are normal and not special or different in any way. 平常的；普通的；一般的；平凡的

  • I strongly suspect that most ordinary people would agree with me.

    我坚定地认为绝大部分老百姓会赞同我的观点。

  • It has 25 calories less than ordinary ice cream.

    它所含热量比普通的冰激凌少了25卡路里。

  • It was just an ordinary weekend for us.

    对我们来说，这只是一个平常的周末。

SYN usual, standard, normal, common  
2 ADJ-GRADED 平庸的；平淡无奇的 If you describe someone or something as ordinary, you mean they are not special or interesting in any way and may be rather dull. 平庸的；平淡无奇的

  • I'm just a very ordinary, boring normal guy.

    我只是一个颇为平庸无趣的普通人。

  • Your life since then must have seemed very ordinary.

    你的生活从那时起一定非常平淡无奇。

  • ...very ordinary, if very well made, drinking glasses, lamps and tableware.

    制作精良但毫无特色的酒杯、灯具和餐具

3 PHRASE 不寻常的；非凡的；特殊的 Something that is out of the ordinary is unusual or different. 不寻常的；非凡的；特殊的

  • The boy's knowledge was out of the ordinary.

    这个男孩懂得的知识非比寻常。

  • I've noticed nothing out of the ordinary.

    我没注意到什么特别之处。

SYN unusual, different, odd, important
