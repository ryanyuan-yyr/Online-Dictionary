astounded
əstaʊndɪd əˈstaʊndɪd
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ to-infinitive, ADJ that] 大吃一惊的;震惊的 If you are astounded by something, you are very shocked or surprised that it could exist or happen. 大吃一惊的;震惊的

  • I was astounded by its beauty. [+ by]

    它的美丽震撼了我。

  • I am astounded at the comments made by the Chief Superintendent. [+ at]

    主管的评语让我大吃一惊。

SYN astonished, surprised, shocked, stunned
