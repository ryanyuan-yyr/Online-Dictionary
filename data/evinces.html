evince
ɪvɪns iˈvɪns ɪˈvɪns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense evinces, present participle evincing, past tense , past participle evinced
VERB （经常间接地）表明，显示 If someone or something evinces a particular feeling or quality, they show that feeling or quality, often indirectly. （经常间接地）表明，显示 [formal]

  • The entire production evinces authenticity and a real respect for the subject matter. [VERB noun]

    整部作品表现出真实性以及对主题的真正尊重。

  • The new president has so far evinced no such sense of direction. [VERB noun]

    新总统到目前尚未表现出对未来发展的判断力。

SYN show, evidence, reveal, establish
