litigate
lɪtɪgeɪt ˈlɪtɪˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense litigates, present participle litigating, past tense , past participle litigated
VERB 提起诉讼；打官司 To litigate means to take legal action. 提起诉讼；打官司 [law]

  • ...the cost of litigating personal injury claims in the county court. [VERB noun]

    在县级法院打人身伤害索赔官司的费用

  • If we have to litigate, we will. [VERB]

    迫不得已的话，我们会提起诉讼。

SYN sue, prosecute, go to court, press charges
