mogul moʊgəl ˈməʊɡʌl ˈmoʊgəl
Word forms: plural moguls
1 N-COUNT 莫卧儿人(16至18世纪印度的穆斯林统治者) A Mogul was a Muslim ruler in India in the sixteenth to eighteenth centuries. 莫卧儿人(16至18世纪印度的穆斯林统治者)
2 N-COUNT (尤指新闻、影视业的)有权势的富商，大人物，大亨 A mogul is an important, rich, and powerful businessman, especially one in the news, film, or television industry. (尤指新闻、影视业的)有权势的富商，大人物，大亨

  • ...an international media mogul.

    国际传媒大亨

  • ...Hollywood movie moguls.

    好莱坞电影业巨头

SYN tycoon, lord, baron, notable
