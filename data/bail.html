bail
beɪl beɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bails, present participle bailing, past tense , past participle bailed
The spelling bale is also used for meaning 5, and for meanings 1 and 3 of the phrasal verb. 拼写bale亦用于义项5以及短语动词的义项1和3。
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft on NOUN] 保释金 Bail is a sum of money that an arrested person or someone else puts forward as a guarantee that the arrested person will attend their trial in a law court. If the arrested person does not attend it, the money will be lost. 保释金

  • He was freed on bail pending an appeal.

    他被取保候审。

  • The high court set bail at $8,000.

    高等法院将保释金定为8,000美元。

SYN security, bond, guarantee, pledge  
2 N-UNCOUNT 保释 Bail is permission for an arrested person to be released after bail has been paid. 保释

  • He was yesterday given bail by South Yorkshire magistrates.

    昨天南约克郡法院准予他获得保释。

3 VERB [usually passive] 保释 If someone is bailed, they are released while they are waiting for their trial, after paying an amount of money to the court. 保释

  • He was bailed for probation reports. [be VERB-ed]

    他因等待鉴定报告而获得保释。

  • He was bailed to appear before local magistrates on 5 November. [be VERB-ed to-infinitive]

    他获得保释，将于11月5日在地方法庭出庭受审。

4 N-COUNT [usually plural] （板球运动中）三柱门上的横木 In cricket, the bails are the two small pieces of wood that are laid across the top of the stumps to form the wicket. （板球运动中）三柱门上的横木
5 VERB （往外）舀水 If you bail, you use a container to remove water from a boat or from a place which is flooded. （往外）舀水 [Also VERB n]

  • We kept her afloat for a couple of hours by bailing frantically. [VERB]

    我们拼命往外舀水，使它继续漂浮了几个小时。

SYN scoop, empty, dip, ladle  
Bail out means the same as bail .bail out同bail

  • A crew was sent down the shaft to close it off and bail out all the water.

    一组人员被派下矿井将其封闭并把水舀干。

  • The flood waters have receded since then, but residents are still bailing out.

    此后洪水已经开始消退，但居民还在往外舀水。

6 PHRASE （囚犯）在保释期间逃跑，弃保潜逃 If a prisoner jumps bail, he or she does not come back for his or her trial after being released on bail. （囚犯）在保释期间逃跑，弃保潜逃

  • He had jumped bail last year while being tried on drug charges.

    去年他在因涉毒被控告期间弃保潜逃。
