downspout
daʊnspaʊt ˈdaʊnˌspaʊt ˈdaʊnˌspaʊt
Word forms: plural downspouts
N-COUNT (把雨水从屋顶输送到下水道的)雨水管，排水管 A downspout is a pipe attached to the side of a building, through which water flows from the roof into a drain. (把雨水从屋顶输送到下水道的)雨水管，排水管 [US]
