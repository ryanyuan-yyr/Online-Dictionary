thoroughfare
θʌrəfeəʳ ˈθʌrəˌfɛə θɜːroʊ- ˈθɜroʊˌfɛr ˈθɜrəˌfɛr
Word forms: plural thoroughfares
N-COUNT 大道;大街;通衢 A thoroughfare is a main road in a town or city which usually has shops along it and a lot of traffic. 大道;大街;通衢 [formal]

  • ...a busy thoroughfare.

    繁忙的大街

SYN road, way, street, highway
