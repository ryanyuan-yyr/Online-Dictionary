hairdresser
heəʳdresəʳ ˈhɛəˌdrɛsə ˈhɛrˌdrɛsər
Word forms: plural hairdressers
1 N-COUNT 理发师;美发师 A hairdresser is a person who cuts, colours, and arranges people's hair. 理发师;美发师
2 N-COUNT 理发店;美发店 A hairdresser or a hairdresser's is a shop where a hairdresser works. 理发店;美发店

  • I work in this new hairdresser's.

    我在这家新开的理发店工作。
