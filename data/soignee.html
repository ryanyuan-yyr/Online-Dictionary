soignée
swɑːnjeɪ ˈswɑːnjeɪ swaɲe -jeɪ swaˈnjeɪ
LANGUAGE NOTE:

  • The spelling soigné is also used when referring to a man.

    拼写soigné通常用于指代男性。

ADJ 优雅的If you describe a person as soignée, you mean that they are very elegant. 优雅的 [formal]

  • She looked very soignée in black.

    她身着黑衣，看起来非常优雅。
