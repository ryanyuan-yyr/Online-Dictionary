asleep
əsliːp əˈslip
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 睡着的；熟睡的 Someone who is asleep is sleeping. 睡着的；熟睡的

  • My four year-old daughter was asleep on the sofa.

    我4岁的女儿在沙发上睡着了。

SYN sleeping, napping, dormant, crashed out [slang]  
2 PHRASE 入睡；睡着；进入梦乡 When you fall asleep, you start sleeping. 入睡；睡着；进入梦乡

  • Sam snuggled down in his pillow and fell asleep.

    萨姆舒服地枕着枕头进入了梦乡。

SYN drop off [informal] , go to sleep, doze off, nod off [informal]  
3 PHRASE 熟睡的；酣睡的 Someone who is fast asleep or sound asleep is sleeping deeply. 熟睡的；酣睡的

  • They were both fast asleep in their cots.

    他们俩在婴儿床上酣睡着。

  • Turning over, she was soon sound asleep again.

    她翻了个身，很快又沉沉睡去。
