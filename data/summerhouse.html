summer house
ˈsʌmə haʊs
(also summerhouse)Word forms: plural summer houses
1 N-COUNT (花园中的)凉棚，凉亭 A summer house is a small building in a garden. It contains seats, and people can sit there in the summer. (花园中的)凉棚，凉亭
2 N-COUNT (乡村或海边的)避暑别墅 Someone's summer house is a house in the country or by the sea where they spend the summer. (乡村或海边的)避暑别墅

  • He visited relatives at their summer house on the river.

    他去拜访住在河畔避暑别墅的亲戚。
