blindside
blaɪndsaɪd ˈblaɪndˌsaɪd
Word forms: blindsides, blindsiding, blindsided
V-T [usu passive] 意外打击If you say that you were blindsided by something, you mean that it surprised you in a negative way. 意外打击 [mainly US, disapproval]

  • He complained about being blindsided by the decision.

    他抱怨该决定让他感到意外。
