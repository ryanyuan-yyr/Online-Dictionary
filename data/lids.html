lid
lɪd lɪd
Word forms: plural lids
1 N-COUNT （容器的）盖，盖子 A lid is the top of a box or other container which can be removed or raised when you want to open the container. （容器的）盖，盖子
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 眼睑；眼皮 Your lids are the pieces of skin which cover your eyes when you close them. 眼睑；眼皮

  • A dull pain began to throb behind his lids.

    他的眼皮后面开始隐隐作痛地抽动。

3 N-SING 限制；抑制；隐瞒 If you say that someone is keeping the lid on an activity or a piece of information, you mean that they are restricting the activity or are keeping the information secret. 限制；抑制；隐瞒 [informal]

  • The soldiers' presence seemed to keep a lid on the violence. [+ on]

    士兵的存在似乎抑制住了暴力活动。

  • Their finance ministry is still trying to put a lid on the long-simmering securities scandal. [+ on]

    他们的财政部仍在试图遮掩一触即发的证券丑闻。

4 N-SING （对开支的）控制，封顶 If you say that you want to keep a lid on the cost of doing something, you mean that you want to prevent it costing you more than you feel is reasonable. （对开支的）控制，封顶

  • If our industry is to remain competitive it must mean keeping a lid on prices.

    我们的行业要想保持竞争力，就必须对价格加以控制。
