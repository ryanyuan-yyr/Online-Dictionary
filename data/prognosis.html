prognosis
prɒgnoʊsɪs prɑgˈnoʊsɪs
Word forms: plural prognoses/prɒgnoʊsiːz/
N-COUNT （尤指对病人病情的）预后，预断 A prognosis is an estimate of the future of someone or something, especially about whether a patient will recover from an illness. （尤指对病人病情的）预后，预断 [formal]

  • If the cancer is caught early the prognosis is excellent.

    如果癌症在早期发现，预后效果还是非常好的。

  • ...a gloomy prognosis of the Scots' championship prospects.

    对苏格兰队的夺冠前景并不看好

SYN forecast, prediction, diagnosis, expectation
