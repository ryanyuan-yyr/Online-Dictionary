scruple
skruːpəl ˈskrupəl
Word forms: plural scruples
N-VAR [usually plural] (道德上的)顾忌,顾虑;良心不安 Scruples are moral principles or beliefs that make you unwilling to do something that seems wrong. (道德上的)顾忌,顾虑;良心不安

  • ...a man with no moral scruples.

    道德败坏的人

SYN misgiving, hesitation, qualm, doubt
