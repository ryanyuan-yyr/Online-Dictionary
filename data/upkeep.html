upkeep
ʌpkiːp ˈʌpˌkip
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 维修；保养；养护 The upkeep of a building or place is the work of keeping it in good condition. 维修；保养；养护

  • The money will be used for the estate's upkeep.

    这笔钱将用于物业维护。

  • The maintenance department is responsible for the general upkeep of the park. [+ of]

    维护部负责公园的一般养护。

SYN maintenance, running, keep, subsistence  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 赡养；供养；（公共事业的）维持 The upkeep of a group of people or services is the process of providing them with the things that they need. 赡养；供养；（公共事业的）维持

  • He offered to pay £100 a month towards his son's upkeep.

    他愿意每月付100英镑作为儿子的抚养费。

  • ...subsidies for the upkeep of kindergartens and orphanages.

    用于维持幼儿园和孤儿院日常运转的补助

SYN running costs, expenses, overheads, expenditure
