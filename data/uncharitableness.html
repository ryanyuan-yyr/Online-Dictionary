uncharitable
ʌntʃærɪtəbəl ʌnˈtʃærɪtəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不宽厚的；刻薄的；苛刻的 If you describe someone's remarks, thoughts, or behaviour as uncharitable, you think they are being unkind or unfair to someone. 不宽厚的；刻薄的；苛刻的

  • Don't be uncharitable.

    别太苛刻。

  • This was an uncharitable assessment of the reasons for the failure.

    这是对失败原因毫不讲情面的分析评估。

SYN unkind, mean [informal] , cruel, insensitive
