meddlesome
medəlsəm ˈmɛdəlsəm
ADJ-GRADED 好干预的;爱管闲事的 If you describe a person as meddlesome, you are criticizing them because they try to influence or change things that do not concern them. 好干预的;爱管闲事的 [disapproval]

  • ...a meddlesome member of the public.

    一个爱管闲事的公众人士

SYN interfering, meddling, intrusive, intruding
