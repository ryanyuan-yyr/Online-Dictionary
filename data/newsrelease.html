news release
njuːz rɪˈliːs
Word forms: plural news releases
N-COUNT (相关组织提供给报纸的)新闻稿 A news release is a written statement about a matter of public interest which is given to the press by an organization concerned with the matter. (相关组织提供给报纸的)新闻稿 [mainly US]

  • In a news release, the company said it had experienced severe financial problems.

    在新闻稿中，该公司表示自己遭遇了严重的财务问题。
