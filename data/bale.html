bale
beɪl beɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bales, present participle baling, past tense , past participle baled
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 大包；大捆 A bale is a large quantity of something such as hay, cloth, or paper, tied together tightly. 大包；大捆

  • ...bales of hay.

    成捆的干草

2 VERB 把…打成大包；把…扎成大捆 If something such as hay, cloth, or paper is baled, it is tied together tightly. 把…打成大包；把…扎成大捆 [Also VERB n]

  • Once hay has been cut and baled it has to go through some chemical processes. [be VERB-ed]

    干草割下并捆扎之后需要对其进行化学处理。

3 See also: bail
