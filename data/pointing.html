pointing
pɔɪntɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （对建筑物外墙砖、石砌缝的）勾嵌 Pointing is a way of filling in the gaps between the bricks or stones on the outside of a building so that the surface becomes sealed. （对建筑物外墙砖、石砌缝的）勾嵌

  • He did the pointing in the stonework himself.

    他自己完成了建筑石造部分的勾嵌。

2 N-UNCOUNT （勾嵌砖或石墙砌缝的）水泥 Pointing is the cement between the bricks or stones in a wall. （勾嵌砖或石墙砌缝的）水泥

  • Cracks, bulges, crumbling pointing and damp patches mean trouble.

    裂缝、凸起、剥落的勾缝和湿斑都意味着存在问题。
