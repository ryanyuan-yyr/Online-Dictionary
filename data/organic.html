organic
ɔːʳgænɪk ɔrˈgænɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有机的；绿色的；无农害的；不使用化肥的 Organic methods of farming and gardening use only natural animal and plant products to help the plants or animals grow and be healthy, rather than using chemicals. 有机的；绿色的；无农害的；不使用化肥的

  • Organic farming is expanding everywhere.

    有机农业正在各地迅速发展起来。

  • ...organic fruit and vegetables.

    有机水果和蔬菜

SYN pesticide-free, natural, chemical-free, additive-free  
organically

  • ...organically grown vegetables.

    有机种植的蔬菜

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 生物体的；有机体的 Organic substances are of the sort produced by or found in living things. 生物体的；有机体的

  • Incorporating organic material into chalky soils will reduce the alkalinity.

    在白垩质土壤中添加有机物可降低其碱性。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （变化或发展）逐渐的，自然的，演进的 Organic change or development happens gradually and naturally rather than suddenly. （变化或发展）逐渐的，自然的，演进的 [formal]

  • ...to manage the company and supervise its organic growth.

    经营公司并指导其逐步发展

SYN developing, growing, progressing, maturing  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] （社群或结构）有机的，统一的，不可分割的 If a community or structure is an organic whole, each part of it is necessary and fits well with the other parts. （社群或结构）有机的，统一的，不可分割的 [formal]

  • City planning treats the city as a unit, as an organic whole.

    城市规划将城市视为一个单位，一个有机的整体。

SYN systematic, ordered, structured, organized
