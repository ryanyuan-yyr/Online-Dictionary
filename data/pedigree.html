pedigree
pedɪgriː ˈpɛdɪˌgri
Word forms: plural pedigrees
1 N-COUNT （狗、猫等动物的）血统记录，纯种谱系 If a dog, cat, or other animal has a pedigree, its ancestors are known and recorded. An animal is considered to have a good pedigree when all its known ancestors are of the same type. （狗、猫等动物的）血统记录，纯种谱系

  • 60 per cent of dogs and ten per cent of cats have pedigrees.

    60%的狗和10%的猫有血统记录。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有纯种谱系证明的；纯种的 A pedigree animal is descended from animals which have all been of a particular type, and is therefore considered to be of good quality. 有纯种谱系证明的；纯种的

  • ...a pedigree dog.

    纯种狗

  • ...pedigree horses.

    纯种马

3 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 背景；出身；门第；世系 Someone's pedigree is their background or their ancestors. 背景；出身；门第；世系

  • Hammer's business pedigree almost guaranteed him the acquaintance of U.S. presidents.

    哈默的商业背景几乎保证了他能够结识美国总统。

  • She had an impeccable aristocratic pedigree.

    她有纯正的贵族血统。
