ulterior
ʌltɪəriəʳ ʌlˈtɪriər
ADJ [ADJ n] （动机等）隐蔽的，隐秘的，隐藏的 If you say that someone has an ulterior motive for doing something, you believe that they have a hidden reason for doing it. （动机等）隐蔽的，隐秘的，隐藏的

  • Sheila had an ulterior motive for trying to help Stan. [+ for]

    希拉想帮助斯坦是另有所图的。

  • Posing for the camera pleased him more than anything else. No doubt with the ulterior object of catching votes.

    在照相机前摆姿势是最令他高兴的事了，背后的动机无疑是为了拉选票。

SYN hidden, secret, concealed, personal
