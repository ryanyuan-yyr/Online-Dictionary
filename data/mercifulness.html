merciful
mɜːʳsɪfʊl ˈmɜrsɪfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 仁慈的;宽厚的 If you describe God or a person in a position of authority as merciful, you mean that they show kindness and forgiveness to people. 仁慈的;宽厚的

  • ...a merciful God.

    仁慈的上帝

  • We can only hope the court is merciful.

    我们唯有寄希望于法庭的宽大处理。

SYN compassionate, forgiving, sympathetic, kind  
2 ADJ-GRADED （尤指因结束了某人的痛苦或不适）幸运的,有福气的 If you describe an event or situation as merciful, you mean that it is a good thing, especially because it stops someone's suffering or discomfort. （尤指因结束了某人的痛苦或不适）幸运的,有福气的

  • We were told when he was taken to hospital that his injuries were so severe death would be merciful.

    他被送到医院时，医生告诉我们他伤势过重，死亡对他算是一种幸运的解脱。

  • Eventually the session came to a merciful end.

    这次开庭的最终结果令人庆幸。

SYN welcome, desired, blessed
