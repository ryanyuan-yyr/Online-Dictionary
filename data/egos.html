ego
iːgoʊ egoʊ ˈiːɡəʊ ˈɛɡəʊ ˈigoʊ ˈɛgoʊ
Word forms: plural egos
1 N-VAR 自我;自我评价;自尊 Someone's ego is their sense of their own worth. For example, if someone has a large ego, they think they are very important and valuable. 自我;自我评价;自尊

  • He had a massive ego, never would he admit he was wrong.

    他自视甚高，从来不会承认自己错了。

SYN self-esteem, self-confidence, self-respect, self-image  
2See also:alter ego  super-ego
