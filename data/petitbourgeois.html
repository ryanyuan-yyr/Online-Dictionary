petit bourgeois
peti bʊəʳʒwɑː ˈpɛtɪ ˈbʊəʒwɑː pəti burʒwa ˈpɛti pəˈti
(also petty bourgeois)
ADJ （人或事物）小资产阶级的，属于小资产阶级的 Someone or something that is petit bourgeois belongs or relates to the lower middle class. （人或事物）小资产阶级的，属于小资产阶级的 [disapproval]

  • He had a petit bourgeois mentality.

    他有小资产阶级思想。
