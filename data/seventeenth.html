seventeenth
sevəntiːnθ ˈsɛvənˈtiːnθ ˈsɛvənˈtinθ
Word forms: plural seventeenths
1 ORD 第十七 The seventeenth item in a series is the one that you count as number seventeen. 第十七

  • She gave birth to Annabel just after her seventeenth birthday.

    她 17 岁生日刚过便生下了安娜贝尔。

2 FRACTION 十七分之一 A seventeenth is one of seventeen equal parts of something. 十七分之一
