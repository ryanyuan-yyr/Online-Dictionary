right-hand man ˈraɪtˌhænd mæn
Word forms: plural right-hand men
N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 得力助手;左膀右臂 Some-one's right-hand man is the person who acts as their chief assistant and helps and supports them a lot in their work. 得力助手;左膀右臂

  • He is Rupert Murdoch's right-hand man at News International.

    他是鲁珀特·默多克在国际新闻公司的得力助手。
