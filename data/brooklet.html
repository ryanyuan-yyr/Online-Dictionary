brook
brʊk brʊk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense brooks, past tense , past participle brooking, present participle brooked
1 N-COUNT 小溪；小河 A brook is a small stream. 小溪；小河
SYN stream, burn [Scottish , Northern England] , rivulet, gill [dialect]  
2 VERB 容许，忍受（他人的干预或反对） If someone in a position of authority will brook no interference or opposition, they will not accept any interference or opposition from others. 容许，忍受（他人的干预或反对）

  • From childhood on, she'd had a plan of action, one that would brook no interference. [VERB noun]

    从孩提时代起，她就有了一个行动计划，而且是个不容他人干预的计划。

  • The army will brook no weakening of its power. [VERB noun]

    这支部队不会容忍对其实力的削弱。

SYN tolerate, stand, allow, suffer
