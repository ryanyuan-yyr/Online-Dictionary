mercifully
mɜːʳsɪfʊli ˈmɜːsɪfʊlɪ
ADV [ADVERB adjective] [ADVERB with verb] 幸运地;有福气地;幸好 You can use mercifully to show that you are glad that something good has happened, or that something bad has not happened or has stopped. 幸运地;有福气地;幸好 [feelings]

  • Mercifully, a friend came to the rescue.

    幸好一个朋友赶来解了围。

  • Bolivia has been mercifully free of large-scale, drug-related violence.

    幸运的是，玻利维亚没有发生过与毒品有关的大规模暴力事件。

SYN thankfully, happily, luckily, thank goodness
