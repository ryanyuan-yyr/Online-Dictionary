plume
pluːm plum
Word forms: plural plumes
1 N-COUNT （尘、火、水等升起的）（一）股，（一）团 A plume of smoke, dust, fire, or water is a large quantity of it that rises into the air in a column. （尘、火、水等升起的）（一）股，（一）团

  • The rising plume of black smoke could be seen all over Kabul. [+ of]

    整个喀布尔都能看到腾起的黑烟。

  • ...the volcano's towering ash plume.

    火山直冲云霄的烟灰柱

2 N-COUNT 羽毛；大羽 A plume is a large, soft bird's feather. 羽毛；大羽

  • ...broad straw hats decorated with ostrich plumes.

    装饰着鸵鸟羽毛的宽边草帽

SYN feather, crest, quill, pinion  
3 N-COUNT （战士头盔等的）羽饰 A plume is a bunch of long, thin pieces of material which is used to decorate something such as a soldier's helmet. （战士头盔等的）羽饰
