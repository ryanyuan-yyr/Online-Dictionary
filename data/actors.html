actor
æktəʳ ˈæktər
Word forms: plural actors
N-COUNT 演员（单数形式的actor通常指男演员，但有些女演员喜欢被称为actor而不是actress) An actor is someone whose job is acting in plays or films. 'Actor' in the singular usually refers to a man, but some women who act prefer to be called 'actors' rather than 'actresses'. 演员（单数形式的actor通常指男演员，但有些女演员喜欢被称为actor而不是actress)

  • His father was an actor in the Cantonese Opera Company.

    他的父亲是粤剧团的演员。

  • You have to be a very good actor to play that part.

    要想演那个角色，你必须是个非常出色的演员。

SYN performer, player, artiste, leading man or lady
