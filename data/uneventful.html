uneventful
ʌnɪventfʊl ˌʌnɪˈvɛntfʊl ˌʌniˈvɛntfəl
ADJ-GRADED 无惊人（或重大）事件的；平凡的；平静的 If you describe a period of time as uneventful, you mean that nothing interesting, exciting, or important happened during it. 无惊人（或重大）事件的；平凡的；平静的

  • The return journey was uneventful, the car running perfectly.

    回程波澜不惊，车开得很顺。

  • It was rare for her to have an opportunity to discuss her dull, uneventful life.

    她极少有机会谈论自己枯燥平淡的生活。

SYN humdrum, ordinary, routine, quiet  
uneventfully

  • The five years at that school passed fairly uneventfully.

    在那所学校的5年过得颇为平静。
