electric
ɪlektrɪk iˈlɛktrɪk ɪˈlɛktrɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 用电的;电动的 An electric device or machine works by means of electricity, rather than using some other source of power. 用电的;电动的

  • ...her electric guitar.

    她的电吉他

SYN electric-powered, powered, cordless, battery-operated  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 电的;由电产生的 An electric current, voltage, or charge is one that is produced by electricity. 电的;由电产生的
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 通电的;导电的 Electric plugs, sockets, or power lines are designed to carry electricity. 通电的;导电的
4 ADJ [ADJ n] 电的;供电的 Electric is used to refer to the supply of electricity. 电的;供电的 [informal]

  • An average electric bill might go up $2 or $3 per month.

    每月电费可能会平均增加两三美元。

5 ADJ-GRADED 扣人心弦的;紧张刺激的 If you describe the atmosphere of a place or event as electric, you mean that people are in a state of great excitement. 扣人心弦的;紧张刺激的

  • The mood in the hall was electric.

    大厅里的气氛非常紧张。

SYN charged, exciting, stirring, thrilling
