resettle
riːsetəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense resettles, present participle resettling, past tense , past participle resettled
V-ERG (使在别处)重新定居;(使)搬迁 If people are resettled by a government or organization, or if people resettle, they move to a different place to live because they are no longer able or allowed to stay in the area where they used to live. (使在别处)重新定居;(使)搬迁

  • The refugees were put in camps in Italy before being resettled. [be VERB-ed]

    这些难民在重新定居之前被安置在意大利的难民营中。

  • In 1990, 200,000 Soviet Jews resettled on Israeli territory. [VERB]

    在1990年，20万名苏联犹太人在以色列重新定居。
