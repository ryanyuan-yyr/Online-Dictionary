endure
ɪndjʊəʳ -dʊr ɛnˈdʊr ɛnˈdjʊr ɪnˈdʊr ɪnˈdjʊr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense endures, present participle enduring, past tense , past participle endured
1 VERB 忍耐;忍受 If you endure a painful or difficult situation, you experience it and do not avoid it or give up, usually because you cannot. 忍耐;忍受

  • The company endured heavy financial losses. [VERB noun]

    那家公司遭受了严重亏损。

  • ...unbearable pain, which they had to endure in solitude because not even the doctors could get near them. [VERB noun]

    他们不得不独自承受的无法忍受的疼痛，因为甚至连医生都无法靠近他们

2 VERB 持续;持久 If something endures, it continues to exist without any loss in quality or importance. 持续;持久

  • Somehow the language endures and continues to survive. [VERB]

    那种语言以某种方式保存下来，并继续存在下去。

SYN last, live, continue, remain  
enduring

  • ...the start of an enduring friendship.

    长久友谊的开始
