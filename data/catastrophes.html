catastrophe
kətæstrəfi kəˈtæstrəfi
Word forms: plural catastrophes
N-COUNT 重大灾难;灾祸;横祸 A catastrophe is an unexpected event that causes great suffering or damage. 重大灾难;灾祸;横祸

  • From all points of view, war would be a catastrophe.

    不管从哪方面说，战争都将是场灾难。

  • If the world is to avoid environmental catastrophe, advanced economies must undergo a profound transition.

    世界若要想避免环境灾难，经济发达的国家必须进行深刻变革。

SYN disaster, tragedy, calamity, meltdown [informal]
