palm
pɑːm pɑm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense palms, present participle palming, past tense , past participle palmed
1 N-COUNT 棕榈树 A palm or a palm tree is a tree that grows in hot countries. It has long leaves growing at the top, and no branches. 棕榈树
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 手掌 The palm of your hand is the inside part. 手掌

  • Dornberg slapped the table with the palm of his hand. [+ of]

    多恩伯格用手拍着桌子。

  • He wiped his sweaty palm.

    他擦了擦满是汗水的手掌。

SYN hand, hook, paw [informal] , mitt [slang]  
3 PHRASE 在…的手心里；在…的完全掌控中 If you have someone or something in the palm of your hand, you have control over them. 在…的手心里；在…的完全掌控中

  • Johnson thought he had the board of directors in the palm of his hand.

    约翰逊以为自己完全掌控了董事会。

  • They held his fate in the palms of their ancient hands.

    他们这些老人将他的命运掌握在手心里。
