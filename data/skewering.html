skewer
skjuːəʳ ˈskjuər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense skewers, present participle skewering, past tense , past participle skewered
1 N-COUNT 串肉扦;扦子 A skewer is a long metal pin which is used to hold pieces of food together during cooking. 串肉扦;扦子
2 VERB 用扦子串住;将…叉住 If you skewer something, you push a long, thin, pointed object through it. 用扦子串住;将…叉住

  • He skewered his victim through the neck. [VERB noun prep.]

    他用扦子刺穿了受害人的脖子。

  • ...skewered beef with vegetables. [VERB-ed]

    串有蔬菜的烤牛肉串

SYN pierce, gore, impale, spike
