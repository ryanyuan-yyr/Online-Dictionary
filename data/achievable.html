achievable
ətʃiːvəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可达到的；可获得的 If you say that something you are trying to do is achievable, you mean that it is possible for you to succeed in doing it. 可达到的；可获得的

  • A 50% market share is achievable.

    50%的市场份额是可以实现的。

  • It is often a good idea to start with smaller, easily achievable goals.

    一开始把目标定得低一点、容易实现一点，总不失为一个好主意。

SYN attainable, obtainable, winnable, reachable
