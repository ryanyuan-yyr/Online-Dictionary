quiz
kwɪz kwɪz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense quizzes, present participle quizzing, past tense , past participle quizzed
1 N-COUNT 问答竞赛;智力竞赛 A quiz is a game or competition in which someone tests your knowledge by asking you questions. 问答竞赛;智力竞赛

  • We'll have a quiz at the end of the show.

    节目的最后我们要举行一次智力竞赛。

SYN competition, test, quiz show, panel game  
2 VERB 询问;查问;盘问 If you are quizzed by someone about something, they ask you questions about it. 询问;查问;盘问

  • He was quizzed about his income, debts and eligibility for state benefits. [be V-ed + about]

    他被问及收入、债务以及是否有资格享受政府发放的救济金等问题。

  • Sybil quizzed her about life as a working girl. [VERB noun + about]

    西比尔询问了她当女工的生活。

SYN question, ask, interrogate, examine
