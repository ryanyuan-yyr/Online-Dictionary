canteen
kæntiːn kænˈtin
Word forms: plural canteens
1 N-COUNT (工厂、商店、高校的)食堂,餐厅 A canteen is a place in a factory, shop, or college where meals are served to the people who work or study there. (工厂、商店、高校的)食堂,餐厅

  • Rennie had eaten his tea in the canteen.

    伦尼已经在餐厅吃过茶点了。

  • ...a school canteen.

    学校食堂

  • ...canteen food.

    食堂饭菜

2 N-COUNT (士兵等用的)水壶 A canteen is a small plastic bottle for carrying water and other drinks. Canteens are used by soldiers. (士兵等用的)水壶

  • ...a full canteen of water. [+ of]

    满满一水壶水

3 N-COUNT (放置刀、叉、勺子的)餐具盒 A canteen of cutlery is a set of knives, forks, and spoons in a specially designed box. (放置刀、叉、勺子的)餐具盒
