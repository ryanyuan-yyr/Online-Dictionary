crossbeam
krɔsbim ˈkrɒsˌbiːm ˈkrɔsˌbim
Word forms: crossbeams
N-COUNT (房顶的)横梁A crossbeam is a long, thick bar of wood, metal, or concrete that is placed between two walls or other structures, especially in order to support the roof of a building. (房顶的)横梁

  • He hung the light from a steel crossbeam in the roof.

    他在屋顶上的钢横梁上吊上电灯。
