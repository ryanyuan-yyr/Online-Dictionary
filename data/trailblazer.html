trailblazer
treɪlbleɪzəʳ ˈtreɪlbreɪkə ˈtreɪlˌbleɪzər
Word forms: plural trailblazers
N-COUNT 先驱(者);创始人 A trailblazer is a person who is the leader in a particular field, especially who does a particular thing before anyone else does. 先驱(者);创始人

  • He has been the trailblazer and given British sprinters the belief that we are able to take on and beat the world's best.

    他一直是开路先锋，让英国短跑选手相信我们能与世界上最好的运动员一决高下，并且击败他们。
