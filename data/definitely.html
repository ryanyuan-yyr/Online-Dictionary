definitely
defɪnɪtli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB before verb] 清楚地;明显地;肯定地;当然 You use definitely to emphasize that something is the case, or to emphasize the strength of your intention or opinion. 清楚地;明显地;肯定地;当然 [emphasis]

  • I'm definitely going to get in touch with these people.

    我肯定会跟这些人取得联系。

  • Something should definitely be done about that.

    对此一定要采取一些措施。

  • 'I think the earlier ones are a lot better.' —'Mm, definitely.'

    “我觉得早前的那些要好得多。”—“嗯，毫无疑问。”

SYN certainly, clearly, obviously, surely  
2 ADV [ADVERB before verb] 明确地;确定地 If something has been definitely decided, the decision will not be changed. 明确地;确定地

  • He told them that no venue had yet been definitely decided.

    他告诉他们还没有确定下场地。
