canal
kənæl kəˈnæl
Word forms: plural canals
1 N-COUNT 运河;水道;沟渠 A canal is a long, narrow stretch of water that has been made for boats to travel along or to bring water to a particular area. 运河;水道;沟渠

  • ...the Grand Union Canal.

    大联盟运河

  • ...Venetian canals and bridges.

    威尼斯水道和桥梁

2 N-COUNT (人体内输送食物、空气等的)管,道 A canal is a narrow tube inside your body for carrying food, air, or other substances. (人体内输送食物、空气等的)管,道

  • ...delaying the food's progress through the alimentary canal.

    延缓食物通过消化道
