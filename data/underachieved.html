underachieve
ʌndərətʃiːv ˌundərəˈtʃiːv ˌʌndərəˈtʃiv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense underachieves, present participle underachieving, past tense , past participle underachieved
VERB 未展现实力；未发挥水平 If someone underachieves in something such as school work or a job, they do not perform as well as they could. 未展现实力；未发挥水平

  • Some people might think I've underachieved in my job. [VERB]

    有些人可能会认为我的才华在目前的工作中没能充分施展出来。

underachiever

  • He just wanted people to stop calling him disadvantaged, an underachiever.

    他只希望人们不要再叫他后进生、差等生。
