taxation
tækseɪʃən tækˈseɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 征税;课税;税制 Taxation is the system by which a government takes money from people and spends it on things such as education, health, and defence. 征税;课税;税制
2 N-UNCOUNT 税;税额;税款 Taxation is the amount of money that people have to pay in taxes. 税;税额;税款

  • The result will be higher taxation.

    结果将是征收更高的税。
