memento
mɪmentoʊ məˈmɛntoʊ
Word forms: plural mementos, plural mementoes
N-COUNT 纪念物;纪念品 A memento is an object which you keep because it reminds you of a person or a special occasion. 纪念物;纪念品

  • More anglers are taking cameras when they go fishing to provide a memento of catches. [+ of]

    更多的垂钓者去钓鱼的时候带上了照相机，以便钓上鱼后留个纪念。

SYN souvenir, trophy, memorial, token
