skull and crossbones
N-SING 骷髅标志，骷髅画(旧时为海盗旗的标记，现有时会出现在盛放有毒物质的容器上) A skull and crossbones is a picture of a human skull above a pair of crossed bones which warns of death or danger. It used to appear on the flags of pirate ships and is now sometimes found on containers holding poisonous substances. 骷髅标志，骷髅画(旧时为海盗旗的标记，现有时会出现在盛放有毒物质的容器上)

  • Skull and crossbones stickers on the drums aroused the suspicion of the customs officers.
