flummox
flʌməks ˈflʌməks
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense flummoxes, present participle flummoxing, past tense , past participle flummoxed
VERB [usually passive] 使糊涂;使失措 If someone is flummoxed by something, they are confused by it and do not know what to do or say. 使糊涂;使失措

  • The two leaders were flummoxed by the suggestion. [be VERB-ed]

    两个领导被这个建议搞晕了。

SYN baffle, confuse, stump, perplex  
flummoxed

  • No wonder Josef was feeling a bit flummoxed.

    难怪约瑟夫觉得有点糊涂。
