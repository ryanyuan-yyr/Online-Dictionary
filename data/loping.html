lope
loʊp loʊp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lopes, present participle loping, past tense , past participle loped
VERB （轻松而大步地）慢跑，缓跑 If a person or animal lopes somewhere, they run in an easy and relaxed way, taking long steps. （轻松而大步地）慢跑，缓跑 [Also VERB]

  • He was loping across the sand toward Nancy. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他迈着大步穿过沙滩缓缓向南希跑去。

  • Matty saw him go loping off, running low. [VERB prep./adv.]

    马蒂看着他大步慢慢跑远。

SYN stride, spring, bound, gallop  
loping

    她转过身，大步走开。
