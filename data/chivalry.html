chivalry
ʃɪvəlri ˈʃɪvəlri
1 N-UNCOUNT 骑士风度;(尤指对女子的)彬彬有礼，殷勤 Chivalry is polite, kind, and unselfish behaviour, especially by men towards women. 骑士风度;(尤指对女子的)彬彬有礼，殷勤

  • Marie seemed to revel in his old-fashioned chivalry.

    玛丽好像陶醉在他那旧式的骑士风度里。

2 N-UNCOUNT （中世纪的）骑士精神，骑士风范 In the Middle Ages, chivalry was the set of rules and way of behaving which knights were expected to follow. （中世纪的）骑士精神，骑士风范

  • ...the age of chivalry.

    骑士时代
