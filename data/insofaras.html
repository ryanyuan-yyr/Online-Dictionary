insofar as
ɪnsəfɑːr æz
(also in so far as)
PHR-CONJ-SUBORD 在…的范围内;到…的程度;只要 You use insofar as to introduce a statement which explains and adds to something you have just said. 在…的范围内;到…的程度;只要 [formal]

  • Looking back helps insofar as it helps you learn from your mistakes.

    审视过去的作用是能帮你从错误中吸取教训。
