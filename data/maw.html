maw
mɔː mɔ
Word forms: plural maws
N-COUNT [usually singular] (吞噬周围一切的)大嘴,大洞 If you describe something as a maw, you mean that it is like a big open mouth which swallows everything near it. (吞噬周围一切的)大嘴,大洞 [literary]

  • ...helping to chop wood to feed the red maw of the stove.

    帮忙把木头劈开，丢进炉子的大肚子里

SYN mouth, crop, throat, jaws
