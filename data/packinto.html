pack into
1 PHRASAL VERB 往…塞入；将…塞进 If someone packs a lot of something into a limited space or time, they fit a lot into it. 往…塞入；将…塞进

  • ...packing more events or tasks into less time. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    在更短的时间内完成更多的事情或任务

  • I have tried to pack a good deal into a few words. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    我尽量言简意赅。

2 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 挤满；装满 If people or things are packed into a place, so many of them are put in there that the place becomes very full. 挤满；装满

  • Some 700 people were packed into a hotel room. [be VERB-ed PREP. noun]

    约700人挤在一个酒店房间里。
