drum into
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 反复地讲;灌输;鼓吹 If you drum something into someone, you keep saying it to them until they understand it or remember it. 反复地讲;灌输;鼓吹 [Also V P n that]

  • All through school we had it drummed into us that you need a degree to get a job. [have it V-ed P n that]

    读书时我们被反复灌输找工作要有文凭的观点。

  • Standard examples were drummed into students' heads. [be VERB-ed PREP. noun]

    学生脑子里被灌输了各种标准的范例。

  • They drummed it into her that she was not to tell anyone. [V n P n that]

    他们反复告诫她，让她不要告诉任何人。

SYN drive, hammer, instil, din
