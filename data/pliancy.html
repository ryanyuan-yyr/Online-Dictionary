pliant
plaɪənt ˈplaɪənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 易受影响的；柔顺的；顺从的 A pliant person can be easily influenced and controlled by other people. 易受影响的；柔顺的；顺从的

  • She's proud and stubborn, you know, under that pliant exterior.

    你要知道，在温顺的外表下，她既自傲又固执。

SYN impressionable, susceptible, manageable, adaptable  
2 ADJ-GRADED 易弯的；柔韧的 If something is pliant, you can bend it easily without breaking it. 易弯的；柔韧的

  • ...pliant young willows.

    易弯的嫩柳枝

SYN flexible, plastic, supple, lithe
