worn
wɔːʳn wɔrn
1 Worn is the past participle of wear .
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 破旧的；磨损的;陈旧的 Worn is used to describe something that is damaged or thin because it is old and has been used a lot. 破旧的；磨损的;陈旧的

  • Worn rugs increase the danger of tripping.

    磨损的小地毯会使人更容易绊倒。

  • Most of the trek is along worn paths.

    大部分时候都是沿着被人踩得坑坑洼洼的小路跋涉。

3 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 憔悴的；疲惫的;苍老的 If someone looks worn, they look tired and old. 憔悴的；疲惫的;苍老的

  • She was looking very haggard and worn.

    她看上去非常憔悴和疲惫。

4 See also: well-worn
