salvo
sælvoʊ ˈsælvoʊ
Word forms: plural salvoes
1 N-COUNT (枪炮、导弹的)齐射，并发;(礼炮的)齐鸣 A salvo is the firing of several guns or missiles at the same time in a battle or ceremony. (枪炮、导弹的)齐射，并发;(礼炮的)齐鸣

  • They were to fire a salvo of blanks, after the national anthem. [+ of]

    奏完国歌后，他们将同时发射空弹。

2 N-COUNT (书面或口头的)一连串，一顿，一阵(生气的话) A salvo of angry words is a lot of them spoken or written at about the same time. (书面或口头的)一连串，一顿，一阵(生气的话)

  • His testimony, however, was only one in a salvo of new attacks. [+ of]

    然而他的证词只是新一轮唇枪舌剑中的一股火力。

SYN barrage, storm, bombardment, strafe
