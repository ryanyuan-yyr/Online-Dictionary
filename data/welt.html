welt
welt wɛlt
Word forms: plural welts
N-COUNT (撞击或擦伤所致的)红肿,条痕 A welt is a mark which is made on someone's skin, usually by a blow from something such as a whip or sword. (撞击或擦伤所致的)红肿,条痕

  • He had a reddening welt on his temple and his nose was bleeding.

    他太阳穴处有块红肿，鼻子也在流血。

SYN mark, scar, ridge, streak
