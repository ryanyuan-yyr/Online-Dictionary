yuan
juːæn -ɑːn juˈɑn
Word forms: plural yuan
N-COUNT 元（中国货币单位） The yuan is the unit of money used in the People's Republic of China. 元（中国货币单位）

  • For most events, tickets cost one, two or three yuan.

    大多数活动的票价是一元、两元或三元。

The yuan is also used to refer to the Chinese currency system.元，人民币（中国货币体系）

  • The yuan recovered a little; it now hovers around 8.2 to the dollar.

    人民币稍有回升，现在与美元的汇率在8.2比1附近徘徊。
