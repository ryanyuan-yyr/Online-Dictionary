patch together
PHRASAL VERB 拼凑 If you patch something together, you form it from a number of parts in a quick, hurried way. 拼凑 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • ...to avoid an election by patching together a new government. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    拼凑起新政府以避免选举

  • A hasty deal was patched together. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    一份协议被仓促拼凑了出来。

  • ...this patched-together Commonwealth. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    这个拼凑而成的联邦
