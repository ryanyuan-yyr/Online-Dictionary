note down
PHRASAL VERB 作记录;记笔记 If you note down something, you write it down quickly, so that you have a record of it. 作记录;记笔记

  • She had noted down the names and she told me the story simply and factually. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她记下了那些名字，并且简单地给我讲了这件事的真实情况。

  • If you find a name that's on the list I've given you, note it down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果你发现有我给你的那张单子上的名字，就记下来。

  • Please note down what I'm about to say. [VERB PREP. wh]

    请把我下面要说的内容记录下来。
