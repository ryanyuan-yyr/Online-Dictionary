thumb through
PHRASAL VERB 匆匆翻阅，浏览(书籍、杂志) If you thumb through something such as a book or magazine, you turn the pages quickly rather than reading each page carefully. 匆匆翻阅，浏览(书籍、杂志)

  • He thumbed through a couple of pages, feigning just a slight interest. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他随手翻了几页，假装兴趣不大。

  • He had the drawer open and was thumbing through the files. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他打开抽屉，翻看着档案。

SYN flick through, browse through, leaf through, glance at
