final
faɪnəl ˈfaɪnəl
Word forms: plural finals
1 ADJ (出现次序)最后的，末尾的 In a series of events, things, or people, the final one is the last one. (出现次序)最后的，末尾的

  • Astronauts will make a final attempt today to rescue a communications satellite from its useless orbit.

    今天宇航员将会做最后一次尝试,力图把一颗通信卫星从无效的轨道上抢救下来。

  • This is the fifth and probably final day of testimony before the Senate Judiciary Committee.

    今天是在参议院司法委员会作证的第5天，很可能也是最后一天。

  • On the last Saturday in September, I received a final letter from Clive.

    9 月的最后一个星期六，我收到了克莱夫的最后一封信。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (发生时间)最终的，末了的 Final means happening at the end of an event or series of events. (发生时间)最终的，末了的

  • You must have been on stage until the final curtain.

    你肯定在台上一直呆到了演出结束。

  • The countdown to the Notting Hill Carnival is in its final hours.

    诺丁山狂欢节已经进入了最后几个小时的倒计时。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 无比的;极度的 You can use final to emphasize that a situation has a particular quality to a very great or severe degree. 无比的;极度的 [written, emphasis]

  • Only a few go through the final humiliation of meeting the bailiff at the door.

    很少有人经受执达官上门的奇耻大辱。

4 ADJ (决定)不可更改的;(权威)毋庸置疑的 If a decision or someone's authority is final, it cannot be changed or questioned. (决定)不可更改的;(权威)毋庸置疑的

  • The judges' decision is final.

    法官的判决不可更改。

  • The White House has the final say.

    白宫掌握着最后决定权。

  • I'm not going, and that's final.

    我不会去，这我已经决定了。

SYN irrevocable, absolute, decisive, definitive  
5 N-COUNT 决赛 The final is the last game or contest in a series and decides who is the winner. 决赛

  • ...the Scottish Cup Final.

    苏格兰杯的决赛

  • Pakistan's Jansher Khan has won the men's final at the Singapore Open.

    巴基斯坦的詹谢尔汗取得了新加坡公开赛男子决赛的胜利。

6See also:quarter-final  semi-final 
7 N-PLURAL (锦标赛的)决赛阶段比赛 The finals of a sporting tournament consist of a smaller tournament that includes only players or teams that have won earlier games. The finals decide the winner of the whole tournament. (锦标赛的)决赛阶段比赛

  • Poland know they have a chance of qualifying for the World Cup Finals.

    波兰队知道他们有机会获得参加世界杯决赛的资格。

8 N-PLURAL [oft poss NOUN] (大学或学院的)课程终结考试，期终考试 When a student takes his or her finals, he or she takes the last and most important examinations in a university or college course. (大学或学院的)课程终结考试，期终考试

  • Anna sat her finals in the summer.

    安娜在夏天参加了课程终结考试。
