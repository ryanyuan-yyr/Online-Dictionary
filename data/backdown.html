back down
PHRASAL VERB 放弃（主张、要求、承诺等）；打退堂鼓 If you back down, you withdraw a claim, demand, or commitment that you made earlier, because other people are strongly opposed to it. 放弃（主张、要求、承诺等）；打退堂鼓

  • The United States had no intention of backing down in its bitter row over farm subsidies. [VERB PREP.]

    美国无意就激烈争吵的农产品补贴问题作出让步。

  • It's too late to back down now. [VERB PREP.]

    现在打退堂鼓已经太晚了。

  • He had to back down on plans to backdate the tax changes. [V P + on/over]

    他不得不放弃追溯税收调整的计划。

SYN give in, collapse, withdraw, yield
