bobble
bɒbəl ˈbɑbəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural bobbles, present participle bobbling, past tense , past participle bobbled
1 N-COUNT （用来装饰衣物的）小绒球，小羊毛球 A bobble is a small ball of material, usually made of wool, which is used for decorating clothes. （用来装饰衣物的）小绒球，小羊毛球 [British]

  • ...the bobble on his nightcap.

    他睡帽上的小绒球

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 tassel
2 VERB （球）不规则移动，乱动 If a ball bobbles, it moves in an irregular and uncontrolled way. （球）不规则移动，乱动 [British]

  • The ball bobbled into the back of the net. [VERB]

    球蹦到了球网背面。

3 VERB 失掉（球）；漏接（球） If a player bobbles a ball, they drop it or fail to control it. 失掉（球）；漏接（球） [US]

  • The ball was bobbled momentarily, allowing Holloway to race home. [VERB noun]

    球一下子漏了，使得霍洛韦跑垒成功。
