questionnaire
kwestʃəneəʳ kes- ˌkwɛstʃəˈnɛə ˌkwɛstʃəˈnɛr ˌkwɛstjəˈnɛr
Word forms: plural questionnaires
N-COUNT 调查问卷;调查表 A questionnaire is a written list of questions which are answered by a lot of people in order to provide information for a report or a survey. 调查问卷;调查表

  • Headteachers will be asked to fill in a questionnaire.

    校长们将被要求完成一份问卷调查。

SYN set of questions, form, survey form, question sheet
