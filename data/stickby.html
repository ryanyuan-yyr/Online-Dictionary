stick by
1 PHRASAL VERB 继续帮助;继续支持 If you stick by someone, you continue to give them help or support. 继续帮助;继续支持

  • ...friends who stuck by me during the difficult times as Council Leader. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在我任议会主席的困难时期一直支持我的朋友们

  • She'd stuck by Bob through thick and thin. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她和鲍勃患难与共。

2 PHRASAL VERB 遵守，坚持(诺言、协议、决定或原则等) If you stick by a promise, agreement, decision, or principle, you do what you said you would do, or do not change your mind. 遵守，坚持(诺言、协议、决定或原则等)

  • But I made my decision then and stuck by it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    但那时我已作了决定，并坚定不移。
