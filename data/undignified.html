undignified
ʌndɪgnɪfaɪd
ADJ-GRADED 有失尊严的；不像话的；令人尴尬的 If you describe someone's actions as undignified, you mean they are foolish or embarrassing. 有失尊严的；不像话的；令人尴尬的

  • It is sad to see a county confine its activities to undignified public bickering.

    看到整个县都不顾颜面地公开争吵，真让人感到难过。

  • There followed an undignified slamming of doors.

    随后传来了很不像话的摔门声。

  • All this public outpouring is so undignified.

    所有这些当众宣泄都是很失体面的。

SYN unseemly, inappropriate, unsuitable, improper
