purse strings
N-PLURAL 资金的支配；财权 If you say that someone holds or controls the purse strings, you mean that they control the way that money is spent in a particular family, group, or country. 资金的支配；财权

  • Women control the purse-strings of most families.

    大多数家庭由女性掌握财政大权。

  • The bank has been too slow in loosening the purse strings.

    银行一直迟迟不愿放开资金。
