inevitably
ɪnevɪtəbli
ADV [usually ADVERB with verb] [ADVERB adjective] 不可避免地;必然(发生)地 If something will inevitably happen, it is certain to happen and cannot be prevented or avoided. 不可避免地;必然(发生)地

  • Technological changes will inevitably lead to unemployment.

    技术变革必然会导致失业。

  • Inevitably, the proposal is running into difficulties.

    提案不可避免地遭遇了麻烦。

SYN unavoidably, naturally, necessarily, surely
