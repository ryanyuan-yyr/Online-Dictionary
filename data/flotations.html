flotation
floʊteɪʃən floʊˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural flotations
1 N-VAR (公司的)股票发行，上市 The flotation of a company is the selling of shares in it to the public. (公司的)股票发行，上市 [business]
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (舱室)有浮力的，漂浮的 A flotation compartment helps something to float because it is filled with air or gas. (舱室)有浮力的，漂浮的
