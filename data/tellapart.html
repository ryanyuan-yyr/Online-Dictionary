tell apart
PHRASAL VERB 区分;区别;辨别 If you can tell people or things apart, you are able to recognize the differences between them and can therefore identify each of them. 区分;区别;辨别

  • Perhaps it is the almost universal use of flavourings that makes it so hard to tell the products apart. [VERB noun PREP.]

    或许是因为调料的使用几乎千篇一律才使得那些产品难以区分开。

SYN differentiate between, distinguish between, discriminate between, make a distinction between
