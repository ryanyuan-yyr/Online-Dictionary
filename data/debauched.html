debauched
dɪbɔːtʃt dɪˈbɔːtʃt
ADJ-GRADED 道德败坏的;放荡的;淫逸的 If you describe someone as debauched, you mean they behave in a way that you think is socially unacceptable, for example because they drink a lot of alcohol or have sex with a lot of people. 道德败坏的;放荡的;淫逸的 [old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • ...a debt-ridden and debauched lifestyle.

    负债累累、放荡无度的生活方式

SYN corrupt, abandoned, perverted, degraded
