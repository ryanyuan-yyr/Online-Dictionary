secondment
sɪkɒndmənt
Word forms: plural secondments
N-VAR [oft on NOUN] 临时调任;暂调 Someone who is on secondment from their normal employer has been sent somewhere else temporarily in order to do special duties. 临时调任;暂调 [British]

  • We have two full-time secretaries, one of whom is on secondment from the Royal Navy. [+ from/to]

    我们有两个全职秘书，其中一人是临时从皇家海军调来的。
