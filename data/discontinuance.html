discontinue
dɪskəntɪnjuː ˌdɪskənˈtɪnju
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense discontinues, present participle discontinuing, past tense , past participle discontinued
1 VERB 终止;中断 If you discontinue something that you have been doing regularly, you stop doing it. 终止;中断 [formal] [Also V -ing]

  • Do not discontinue the treatment without consulting your doctor. [VERB noun]

    未经医嘱不得中断治疗。

SYN stop, end, finish, drop  
2 VERB [usually passive] 停止，终止(生产) If a product is discontinued, the manufacturer stops making it. 停止，终止(生产)

  • The Leica M2 was discontinued in 1967. [be VERB-ed]

    徕卡 M2 型相机于 1967 年停产。

  • They have a huge selection of discontinued cookers. [VERB-ed]

    他们有大量绝版的精选炉灶。
