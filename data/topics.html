topic
tɒpɪk ˈtɑpɪk
Word forms: plural topics
N-COUNT 题目;话题；论题 A topic is a particular subject that you discuss or write about. 题目;话题；论题

  • The weather is a constant topic of conversation in Britain…

    在英国，天气是交谈中永恒的话题。

  • The main topic for discussion is political union.

    讨论的主题是政治联合。

  • They offer tips on topics such as home safety.

    他们就家居安全等问题提供建议。

SYN subject, point, question, issue
