barbarous
bɑːʳbərəs ˈbɑrbərəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 粗野的；未开化的；野蛮的 If you describe something as barbarous, you strongly disapprove of it because you think that it is rough and uncivilized. 粗野的；未开化的；野蛮的 [disapproval]

  • He thought the poetry of Whitman barbarous.

    他认为惠特曼的诗歌太粗俗。

SYN uncivilized, wild, rough, gross  
2 ADJ-GRADED 残暴的；极其残忍的；凶残的 If you describe something as barbarous, you strongly disapprove of it because you think that it is extremely cruel. 残暴的；极其残忍的；凶残的 [disapproval]

  • ...a barbarous attack.

    凶残的进攻
