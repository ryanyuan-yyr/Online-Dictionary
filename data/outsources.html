outsource
aʊtsɔːʳs ˈaʊtˌsɔrs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outsources, present participle outsourcing, past tense , past participle outsourced
VERB 将…外包；外购 If a company outsources work or things, it pays workers from outside the company to do the work or supply the things. 将…外包；外购 [business] [Also VERB]

  • Increasingly, corporate clients are seeking to outsource the management of their facilities. [VERB noun]

    越来越多的公司客户寻求将设施的管理外包出去。
