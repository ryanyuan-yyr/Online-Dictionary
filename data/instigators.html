instigator
ɪnstɪgeɪtəʳ
Word forms: plural instigators
N-COUNT 发起者;鼓动者;唆使者;怂恿者 The instigator of an event is the person who causes it to happen. 发起者;鼓动者;唆使者;怂恿者

  • He was accused of being the main instigator of the coup. [+ of]

    他被指控为这场政变的主谋。

  • ...the key instigators of reform.

    改革的主要发起者

SYN ringleader, inciter, motivator, leader
