unhygienic
ʌnhaɪdʒiːnɪk ˌʌnhaɪˈdʒiːnɪk -dʒienɪk
ADJ-GRADED 不清洁的；不卫生的 If you describe something as unhygienic, you mean that it is dirty and likely to cause infection or disease. 不清洁的；不卫生的

  • Parts of the shop were very dirty, unhygienic, and an ideal breeding ground for bacteria.

    店里的一些地方十分肮脏不洁，简直就是滋生细菌的理想温床。

  • ...unhygienic conditions.

    不卫生的条件

SYN insanitary, dirty, filthy, unhealthy
