publicize
pʌblɪsaɪz ˈpʌbləˌsaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense publicizes, present participle publicizing, past tense , past participle publicized
in AM, also use 美国英语用 publicise
VERB 宣传；宣扬；推广 If you publicize a fact or event, you make it widely known to the public. 宣传；宣扬；推广

  • The author appeared on television to publicize her latest book. [VERB noun]

    那位作家上电视宣传她的新书。

  • He never publicized his plans. [VERB noun]

    他从未宣传过他的计划。

  • ...his highly publicized trial this summer. [VERB-ed]

    今年夏天他的审讯被广为报道

SYN make known, report, reveal, publish
