terracotta
terəkɒtə ˌtɛrəˈkɒtə
(also terra-cotta)
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 赤陶土；赤土 Terracotta is a brownish-red clay that has been baked and is used for making things such as flower pots, small statues, and tiles. 赤陶土；赤土

  • ...plants in terracotta pots.

    赤土花盆里的植物

2 COLOUR 赤褐色;棕红色 Terracotta is used to describe things that are brownish-red in colour. 赤褐色;棕红色

  • ...the soft tones of blue, cream and terracotta.

    蓝色、乳白色和棕红色相间的柔和色调
