preheat
priːhiːt priːˈhiːt priˈhit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense preheats, present participle preheating, past tense , past participle preheated
VERB 预热；预先加热 If you preheat an oven, you switch it on and allow it to reach a certain temperature before you put food inside it. 预热；预先加热

  • Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. [VERB noun]

    将烤箱预先加热到400度。

  • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes or until golden brown. [VERB-ed]

    在预热过的烤箱里烤25分钟，或烤成金黄色。
