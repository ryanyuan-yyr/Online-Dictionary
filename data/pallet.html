pallet
pælɪt ˈpælɪt
Word forms: plural pallets
1 N-COUNT （用作睡铺的）草垫 A pallet is a narrow mattress filled with straw which is put on the floor for someone to sleep on. （用作睡铺的）草垫
2 N-COUNT 硬板小床 A pallet is a hard, narrow bed. 硬板小床

  • He was given only a wooden pallet with a blanket.

    只给了他一张硬板小床和一条毯子。

3 N-COUNT （叉车搬运货物时用的）货盘，托盘 A pallet is a flat wooden or metal platform on which goods are stored so that they can be lifted and moved using a forklift truck. （叉车搬运货物时用的）货盘，托盘

  • The warehouse will hold more than 90,000 pallets storing 30 million Easter eggs.

    那间仓库将用9万多个货盘装3,000万只复活节彩蛋。
