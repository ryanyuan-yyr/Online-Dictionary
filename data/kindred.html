kindred
kɪndrɪd ˈkɪndrɪd
1 N-UNCOUNT [with poss] （统称）家人，亲属 Your kindred are your family, and all the people who are related to you. （统称）家人，亲属 [dialect, or old-fashioned]

  • The offender made proper restitution to the victim's kindred.

    违法者对受害人亲属给予了适当的补偿。

SYN family, relations, relatives, connections  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 类似的；相似的 Kindred things are similar to each other. 类似的；相似的 [formal]

  • I recall many discussions with her on these and kindred topics.

    我回想起多次同她就这些问题及类似话题进行的讨论。

SYN similar, like, related, allied
