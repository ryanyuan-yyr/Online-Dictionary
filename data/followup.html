follow-up
ˈfɒləʊˌʌp ˈfɑloʊˌʌp
Word forms: plural follow-ups
N-VAR [oft NOUN n] 继续;后续 A follow-up is something that is done to continue or add to something done previously. 继续;后续

  • They are recording a follow-up to their successful 1989 album.

    他们正在灌录他们 1989 年畅销专辑的续篇。

  • One man was arrested during the raid and another during a follow-up operation.

    一人在突袭中被捕，另一人在后续行动中被捕。
