amphibious
æmfɪbiəs æmˈfɪbiəs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （军事行动）两栖作战的 In an amphibious military operation, army and navy forces attack a place from the sea. （军事行动）两栖作战的

  • A third brigade is at sea, ready for an amphibious assault.

    还有一个旅已在海上待命，准备两栖进攻。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （车辆)水陆两用的 An amphibious vehicle is able to move on both land and water. （车辆)水陆两用的

  • ...an amphibious landing craft which can carry helicopters and up to 2,000 troops.

    能运送直升机和多达两千人的部队的水陆两用登陆艇

3 ADJ （动物）水陆两栖的 Amphibious animals are animals such as frogs and toads that can live both on land and in water. （动物）水陆两栖的
