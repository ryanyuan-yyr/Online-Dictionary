lecherous
letʃərəs ˈlɛtʃərəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （男子）好色的，淫荡的 If you describe a man as lecherous, you disapprove of him because he behaves towards women in a way which shows he is only interested in them sexually. （男子）好色的，淫荡的 [disapproval]

  • ...lecherous old men offering sweets at the school gate.

    在学校门口分发糖果的老色鬼们

SYN lustful, randy [informal , mainly British] , raunchy [slang] , lewd
