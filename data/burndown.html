burn down
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 焚毁；（使）烧成平地 If a building burns down or if someone burns it down, it is completely destroyed by fire. 焚毁；（使）烧成平地 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Six months after Bud died, the house burned down. [VERB PREP.]

    巴德死后6个月，这座房子被烧毁。

  • Anarchists burnt down a restaurant. [VERB PREP. noun]

    无政府主义者把一家饭店烧成平地。
