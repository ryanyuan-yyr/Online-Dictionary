zap
zæp zæp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense zaps, present participle zapping, past tense , past participle zapped
1 VERB （用枪或在电脑游戏中等）杀死，摧毁，击中 To zap someone or something means to kill, destroy, or hit them, for example with a gun or in a computer game. （用枪或在电脑游戏中等）杀死，摧毁，击中 [informal]

  • A guard zapped him with the stun gun. [VERB noun]

    一名警卫用眩晕枪击中了他。

  • ...throat spray, which absolutely zaps any remnant of my sore throat. [VERB noun]

    彻底消除我喉咙肿痛的咽喉喷雾剂

SYN hit, shoot, blast, kill  
2 VERB （用遥控器）变换（频道），换（台） If you zap channels while watching television, you change channels using the remote control. （用遥控器）变换（频道），换（台） [informal]

  • Men like to zap the TV channels, something that can drive certain women berserk. [VERB noun]

    男人喜欢不停地换台，这会让一些女人抓狂。

3 VERB 删除，从屏幕上清除（计算机文件） To zap something such as a computer file or document means to to delete it from the computer memory or to clear it from the screen. 删除，从屏幕上清除（计算机文件） [computing, informal]
