accomplishment
əkʌmplɪʃmənt əˈkɒmplɪʃmənt əkɒm- əˈkɑmplɪʃmənt əˈkʌmplɪʃmənt
Word forms: plural accomplishments
1 N-COUNT 成就;成绩 An accomplishment is something remarkable that has been done or achieved. 成就;成绩

  • For a novelist, that's quite an accomplishment.

    对于一位小说家来说，那是相当了不起的成就。

  • By any standards, the accomplishments of the past year are extraordinary.

    不管以哪种标准来衡量，过去一年都成果斐然。

SYN achievement, feat, attainment, act  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft poss NOUN] 才艺；技能；造诣 Your accomplishments are the things that you can do well or the important things that you have done. 才艺；技能；造诣 [formal]
3 N-UNCOUNT 完成；实现 The accomplishment of something is the fact of achieving or finishing it. 完成；实现

  • His function is vital to the accomplishment of the agency's mission.

    要完成该机构的使命，他的作用至关重要。

SYN accomplishing, effecting, finishing, carrying out
