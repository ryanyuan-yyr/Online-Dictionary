natch
nætʃ nætʃ
ADV 当然;自然 Natch is used to indicate that a particular fact or event is what you would expect and not at all surprising. 当然;自然 [mainly journalism, informal]

  • ...a bizarre, dreamy (but sarcastic, natch) ballad.

    怪诞的、梦幻般的(但当然会带些讥讽的)民谣

  • Ina is a bad girl so, natch, ends up in prison.

    艾娜是个坏女孩，后来自然就进了监狱。
