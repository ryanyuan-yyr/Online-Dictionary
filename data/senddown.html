send down
1 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 开除;勒令退学 If a student is sent down from their university or college, they are made to leave because they have behaved very badly. 开除;勒令退学 [British]

  • She wondered if he had been sent down for gambling. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    她寻思他是不是已经因赌博而被学校开除了。

in AM, use 美国英语用 be expelled
2 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 判…入狱 If someone who is on trial is sent down, they are found guilty and sent to prison. 判…入狱 [British]

  • The two rapists were sent down for life in 1983. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    这两名强奸犯在 1983 年被判终身监禁。

SYN imprison, confine, detain, lock up  
in AM, use 美国英语用 send up
