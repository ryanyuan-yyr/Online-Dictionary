murderer
mɜːʳdərəʳ ˈmʌrdərər
Word forms: plural murderers
N-COUNT 谋杀者;杀人犯 A murderer is a person who has murdered someone. 谋杀者;杀人犯

  • One of these men may have been the murderer.

    他们其中有一个人可能是杀人凶手。

  • ...a notorious mass murderer.

    臭名昭著的大屠杀的凶手

SYN killer, assassin, slayer, butcher
