malodorous
mæloʊdərəs mælˈəʊdərəs ˈmælˈoʊdərəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 恶臭的;难闻的 Something that is malodorous has an unpleasant smell. 恶臭的;难闻的 [literary]

  • ...tons of malodorous garbage bags.

    数吨散发着恶臭的袋装垃圾

  • ...living in a malodorous London street.

    住在伦敦一条臭气熏天的街道上

SYN smelly, stinking, reeking, nauseating
