hush-hush
ˈhʌʃˈhʌʃ
ADJ-GRADED 机密的;不宜张扬的 Something that is hush-hush is secret and not to be discussed with other people. 机密的;不宜张扬的 [informal]

  • Apparently there's a very hush-hush project under way up north.

    貌似这里往北的地方有个非常机密的项目正在进行。

  • They were desperate to keep the marriage hush-hush.

    他们拼命想要瞒住这桩婚事。

SYN secret, confidential, classified, top-secret
