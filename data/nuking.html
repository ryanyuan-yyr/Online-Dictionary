nuke
njuːk nuːk nuk njuk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense nukes, present participle nuking, past tense , past participle nuked
1 N-COUNT 核武器 A nuke is a nuclear weapon. 核武器 [informal]

  • They have nukes, and if they're sufficiently pushed, they'll use them.

    他们拥有核武器，如果逼得太紧，他们就有可能会使用。

2 VERB 对…进行核打击 If one country nukes another, it attacks it using nuclear weapons. 对…进行核打击 [informal]

  • He wanted to nuke the area. [VERB noun]

    他想用核弹轰掉那个地区。
