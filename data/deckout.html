deck out
PHRASAL VERB 装饰;打扮 If a person or thing is decked out with or in something, they are decorated with it or wearing it, usually for a special occasion. 装饰;打扮 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • The cab was decked out with multi-coloured lights. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    出租车上装饰了五颜六色的彩灯。

  • She had decked him out from head to foot in expensive clothes. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她用昂贵的服装把他从头到脚打扮一新。
