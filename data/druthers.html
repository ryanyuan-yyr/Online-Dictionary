druthers
drʌðərz ˈdrʌðəz ˈdrʌðərz
PHRASE 自由选择You can say that you would do something if I had my druthers or given my druthers when you mean that you would do it if you were able to choose. 自由选择 [US]

  • If you could have your druthers, what would you be doing?

    如果当时你可以选择的话，你会在做什么？

  • Given my druthers I'd rather write an essay than draw a picture.

    如果我可以选择的话，我会写一篇论文，而不是画一幅画。
