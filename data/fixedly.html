fixedly
fɪksɪdli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb] 目不转睛地;凝视良久地 If you stare fixedly at someone or something, you look at them steadily and continuously for a period of time. 目不转睛地;凝视良久地 [literary]

  • I stared fixedly at the statue.

    我目不转睛地看着那尊雕像。

  • She was aware that he was watching her fixedly.

    她知道他正在盯着她看。
