bodice ripper
Word forms: plural bodice rippers
N-COUNT （以旧时生活为背景的）情色电影，艳情小说 You can refer to a film or novel which is set in the past and which includes a lot of sex scenes as a bodice ripper, especially if you do not think it is very good and is just intended to entertain people. （以旧时生活为背景的）情色电影，艳情小说 [disapproval]
