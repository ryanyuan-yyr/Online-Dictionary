monarchy
mɒnəʳki ˈmɑnərki ˈmɑnɑrki
Word forms: plural monarchies
1 N-VAR 君主政体;君主制 A monarchy is a system in which a country has a monarch. 君主政体;君主制

  • In a few years we may no longer have a monarchy.

    几年后君主政体可能将不复存在。

  • ...a serious debate on the future of the monarchy.

    关于君主政体前途的严肃辩论

SYN sovereignty, despotism, autocracy, kingship  
2 N-COUNT 君主国;君主制国家 A monarchy is a country that has a monarch. 君主国;君主制国家
3 N-COUNT 皇室;王族 The monarchy is used to refer to the monarch and his or her family. 皇室;王族

  • The monarchy has to create a balance between its public and private lives.

    皇室成员不得不在公众生活和私生活之间建立起一种平衡。
