aperture
æpəʳtʃəʳ ˈæpətʃə ˈæpərtʃər
Word forms: plural apertures
1 N-COUNT 孔；缝隙；缺口 An aperture is a narrow hole or gap. 孔；缝隙；缺口 [formal]

  • Through the aperture he could see daylight.

    透过缝隙，他能看见日光。

SYN opening, space, hole, crack  
2 N-COUNT （照相机的）光圈，孔径 In photography, the aperture of a camera is the size of the hole through which light passes to reach the film. （照相机的）光圈，孔径

  • Use a small aperture and position the camera carefully.

    用小光圈，仔细调整相机位置。

  • The camera has a 32mm lens with a maximum aperture of f/4.5 and a fixed shutter speed of 1/100sec.

    这架相机有一个32毫米的镜头，最大孔径为f/4.5，固定快门速度为1/100秒。
