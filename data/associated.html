associated əsoʊsieɪtɪd
1 ADJ (与…）相关的；相联系的 If one thing is associated with another, the two things are connected with each other. (与…）相关的；相联系的

  • These symptoms are particularly associated with migraine headaches.

    这些症状尤其与偏头痛相关。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （用于公司名称）联合的，联营的 Associated is used in the name of a company that is made up of a number of smaller companies which have joined together. （用于公司名称）联合的，联营的

  • ...the Associated Press.

    美联社

SYN connected, united, joined, leagued
