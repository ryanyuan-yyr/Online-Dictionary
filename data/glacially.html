glacial
gleɪʃəl ˈgleɪsiəl ˈgleɪʃəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 冰河的；冰川的；由冰川形成的 Glacial means relating to or produced by glaciers or ice. 冰河的；冰川的；由冰川形成的 [technical]

  • ...a true glacial landscape with U-shaped valleys.

    有U形山谷的名副其实的冰川景观

  • ...rising sea levels at the end of the last glacial period.

    末次冰川期结束时上升的海平面

2 ADJ-GRADED 冷淡的；冷若冰霜的 If you say that a person, action, or atmosphere is glacial, you mean that they are very unfriendly or hostile. 冷淡的；冷若冰霜的 [disapproval]

  • The Duchess's glare was glacial.

    公爵夫人冷漠地看着。

  • Inside the jeep the atmosphere was glacial.

    吉普车里的气氛相当冷淡。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 冰川般运动的；极其缓慢的 If you say that something moves or changes at a glacial pace, you are emphasizing that it moves or changes very slowly. 冰川般运动的；极其缓慢的 [emphasis]

  • Change occurs at a glacial pace.

    变化极其缓慢。

4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （常指美女）冷若冰霜的，冰美人似的 If you describe someone, usually a woman, as glacial, you mean they are very beautiful and elegant, but do not show their feelings. （常指美女）冷若冰霜的，冰美人似的

  • Her glacial beauty is magnetic.

    她冷若冰霜的美令人倾倒。
