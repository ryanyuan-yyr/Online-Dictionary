nasal
neɪzəl ˈneɪzəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 鼻的 Nasal is used to describe things relating to the nose and the functions it performs. 鼻的

  • ...inflamed nasal passages.

    鼻腔发炎

  • ...nasal decongestant sprays.

    减轻鼻充血的喷雾剂

2 ADJ 带鼻音的;有鼻音的 If someone's voice is nasal, it sounds as if air is passing through their nose as well as their mouth while they are speaking. 带鼻音的;有鼻音的

  • Her voice was nasal and penetrating.

    她的声音带点儿鼻音，富有穿透力。

  • She talked in a deep nasal monotone.

    她说话鼻音很重，语调单一。
