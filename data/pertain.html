pertain
pəʳteɪn pəˈteɪn pərˈteɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pertains, present participle pertaining, past tense , past participle pertained
VERB 与…相关；属于；适用（于） If one thing pertains to another, it relates, belongs, or applies to it. 与…相关；属于；适用（于） [formal] [Also VERB]

  • The restrictions he imposed pertained to the type and height of buildings and the activities for which they could be used. [VERB to noun]

    他限制的是建筑物的种类和高度以及它们的用途。

  • I would much rather that you asked Mrs Zuckerman any questions pertaining to herself. [VERB to noun]

    我宁愿你问楚克尔曼夫人的是与她本人相关的问题。

  • ...matters pertaining to naval district defense. [VERB + to]

    有关海防区防御的问题
