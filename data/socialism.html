socialism
soʊʃəlɪzəm ˈsoʊʃəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 社会主义 Socialism is a set of political principles whose general aim is to create a system in which everyone has an equal opportunity to benefit from a country's wealth. Under socialism, the country's main industries are usually owned by the state. 社会主义
SYN Marxism, communism, leftism, social democracy
