commonality
kɒmənælɪti ˌkɑmənˈæləti
Word forms: plural commonalities
N-VAR 共同特征;共性 Commonality is used to refer to a feature or purpose that is shared by two or more people or things. 共同特征;共性 [formal]

  • We don't have the same commonality of interest. [+ of]

    我们没有共同的兴趣。

  • There are an amazing number of commonalities between systems. [+ between]

    体制之间有着多得惊人的共性。
