injudicious
ɪndʒʊdɪʃəs ˌɪndʒuˈdɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 不明智的;缺乏判断力的;考虑不周到的 If you describe a person or something that they have done as injudicious, you are critical of them because they have shown very poor judgment. 不明智的;缺乏判断力的;考虑不周到的 [formal, disapproval]

  • He blamed injudicious comments by bankers for last week's devaluation.

    他将上周的货币贬值归咎于银行家所作的不当评论。

SYN unwise, foolish, rash, hasty
