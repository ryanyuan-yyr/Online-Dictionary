disappear
dɪsəpɪəʳ ˌdɪsəˈpɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disappears, present participle disappearing, past tense , past participle disappeared
1 VERB (通常指因位置改变而)消失,不见 If you say that someone or something disappears, you mean that you can no longer see them, usually because you or they have changed position. (通常指因位置改变而)消失,不见

  • The black car drove away from them and disappeared. [VERB]

    那辆黑色轿车驶离了他们的视线。

  • Clive disappeared into a room by himself. [VERB prep.]

    克莱夫独自一人进了屋。

  • The airliner disappeared off their radar. [VERB prep.]

    那架班机从他们的雷达屏幕上消失了。

SYN vanish, evaporate, recede, drop out of sight  
2 VERB 消失;失踪 If someone or something disappears, they go away or are taken away somewhere where nobody can find them. 消失;失踪

  • ...a Japanese woman who disappeared thirteen years ago. [VERB]

    一个 13 年前失踪的日本女人

  • Janet's husband and sister noticed that small kitchen objects were disappearing with increasing regularity. [VERB]

    珍妮特的丈夫和她的姐姐发现厨房里的小物件经常不翼而飞，而且次数愈发频繁。

3 VERB 不复存在;灭绝;消亡 If something disappears, it stops existing or happening. 不复存在;灭绝;消亡

  • The immediate security threat has disappeared. [VERB]

    直接的安全威胁已不复存在了。

SYN cease, end, fade, vanish
