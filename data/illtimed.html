ill-timed
ˈɪlˈtaɪmd
ADJ-GRADED 不合时宜的;时机不佳的 If you describe something as ill-timed, you mean that it happens or is done at the wrong time, so that it is damaging or rude. 不合时宜的;时机不佳的

  • He argued that the tax cut was ill-timed.

    他认为减税不合时宜。

SYN inopportune, inappropriate, inconvenient, untimely
