hector hektəʳ ˈhɛktər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hectors, present participle hectoring, past tense , past participle hectored
VERB 威吓;欺凌 If you say that someone is hectoring you, you do not like the way they are trying to make you do something by bothering you and talking to you aggressively. 威吓;欺凌 [disapproval]

  • I suppose you'll hector me until I phone him. [VERB noun]

    我想若是我不给他打电话，你就会一直威吓我。

SYN bully, harass, browbeat, worry  
hectoring

  • In a loud, hectoring tone, Alan told us that he wasn't going to waste time discussing nonsense.

    艾伦大声威逼我们说，他不会把时间浪费在讨论没用的事情上。
