bygone
baɪgɒn -gɔːn ˈbaɪˌgɔn
Word forms: plural bygones
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 过去已久的；很久以前的 Bygone means happening or existing a very long time ago. 过去已久的；很久以前的

  • The book recalls other memories of a bygone age.

    这本书追忆了一个久远时代的别样记忆。

  • ...bygone generations.

    过去的几代人

SYN past, former, previous, lost  
2 PHRASE 让过去的事过去；冰释前嫌 If two people let bygones be bygones, they decide to forget about unpleasant things that have happened between them in the past. 让过去的事过去；冰释前嫌
