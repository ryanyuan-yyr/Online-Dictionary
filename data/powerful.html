powerful
paʊəʳfʊl ˈpaʊərfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 有权力的；有势力的；有影响力的 A powerful person or organization is able to control or influence people and events. 有权力的；有势力的；有影响力的

  • You're a powerful man — people will listen to you.

    你是有影响力的人，大家会听你的。

  • ...Russia and India, two large, powerful countries.

    两大强国，俄罗斯和印度

  • ...Hong Kong's powerful business community.

    香港有影响力的商界

SYN influential, dominant, controlling, commanding  
2See also:all-powerful 
3 ADJ-GRADED 强壮的；强健的 You say that someone's body is powerful when it is physically strong. 强壮的；强健的

  • Hans flexed his powerful muscles.

    汉斯活动了一下他那强健的肌肉。

  • It's such a big powerful dog.

    它是一条如此强壮的大狗。

powerfully

  • He is described as a strong, powerfully-built man of 60.

    他被描述成一个强壮的、肌肉发达的60岁老人。

  • You can contract your muscles more powerfully by linking up your breathing to the exercise.

    你可以将呼吸与运动保持一致，使肌肉收缩更有力。

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （机器）大功率的；强效的；高效能的 A powerful machine or substance is effective because it is very strong. （机器）大功率的；强效的；高效能的

  • The more powerful the car the more difficult it is to handle.

    汽车的功率越大，就越难驾驭。

  • ...powerful computer systems.

    功能强大的计算机系统

  • Alcohol is also a powerful and fast-acting drug.

    酒也是一种效力强且迅速给人快感的毒品。

SYN effective, hard, strong  
powerfully

  • Crack is a much cheaper, smokable form of cocaine which is powerfully addictive.

    强效可卡因更便宜，可以吸食，极易使人上瘾。

5 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （气味）强烈的，浓的 A powerful smell is very strong. （气味）强烈的，浓的

  • There was a powerful smell of stale beer.

    有一种呛人的过期啤酒味。

  • ...tiny creamy flowers with a powerful scent.

    散发浓香的淡黄色小花

SYN intoxicating, strong, heady  
powerfully

  • The railway station smelt powerfully of cats and drains.

    火车站上有冲鼻的猫味和臭水沟味。

6 ADJ-GRADED （声音）响亮的，洪亮的 A powerful voice is loud and can be heard from a long way away. （声音）响亮的，洪亮的

  • At that moment Mrs. Jones's powerful voice interrupted them, announcing a visitor.

    就在那一刻，琼斯夫人响亮的声音打断了他们，说有人来访。

7 ADJ-GRADED （文章、演讲或艺术品）极具感染力的，有震撼力的 You describe a piece of writing, speech, or work of art as powerful when it has a strong effect on people's feelings or beliefs. （文章、演讲或艺术品）极具感染力的，有震撼力的

  • ...Bleasdale's powerful 11-part drama about a corrupt city leader.

    布利兹戴尔创作的关于一个腐败市领导的极具感染力的电视剧，共有11集

  • ...one of the world's most powerful and moving operas, Verdi's 'Otello'.

    威尔第创作的《奥泰罗》，世界最具感染力、最动人的歌剧之一

  • ...a powerful new style of dance-theatre.

    有感染力的新型舞蹈剧

SYN persuasive, convincing, effective, telling  
powerfully

  • It's a play — painful, funny and powerfully acted.

    这是一部悲喜交加、表演感人的戏剧。
