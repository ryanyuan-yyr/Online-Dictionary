tip over
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 打翻;翻倒 If you tip something over or if it tips over, it falls over or turns over. 打翻;翻倒

  • He tipped the table over in front of him. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他把面前的桌子打翻了。

  • She tipped over the chair and collapsed into the corner with a splintering crash. [VERB PREP. noun]

    坐在椅子上的她翻倒了，咔嚓一下摔在了角落里。

  • We grabbed it just as it was about to tip over. [VERB PREP.]

    我们在它就要翻倒时把它抓住了。

SYN topple over, overturn, slant, capsize
