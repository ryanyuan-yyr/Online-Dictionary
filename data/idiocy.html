idiocy
ɪdiəsi ˈɪdiəsi
Word forms: plural idiocies
N-VAR 愚蠢行为;蠢事 If you refer to something as idiocy, you mean that you think it is very stupid. 愚蠢行为;蠢事

  • ...the idiocy of continuing government subsidies for environmentally damaging activities. [+ of]

    政府对破坏环境的活动继续提供补助的愚蠢行为

  • ...his gentle, ironic analysis of the idiocies of the previous regime.

    他对前政权的愚蠢政策既温和又语带讽刺的分析

SYN foolishness, insanity, lunacy, tomfoolery
