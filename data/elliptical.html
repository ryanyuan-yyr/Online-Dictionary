elliptical
ɪlɪptɪkəl ɛˈlɪptɪkəl ɪˈlɪptɪkəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 椭圆形的;椭圆的 Something that is elliptical has the shape of an ellipse. 椭圆形的;椭圆的 [formal]

  • ...the moon's elliptical orbit.

    月球的椭圆形轨道

  • The stadium is elliptical in plan.

    在设计图上，体育场呈椭圆形。

SYN oval, egg-shaped, ovoid, ovate  
2 ADJ-GRADED 隐讳的;间接的 Elliptical references to something are indirect rather than clear. 隐讳的;间接的 [formal]

  • ...Kirsty Gunn's austere, elliptical account of a childhood tragedy.

    柯丝蒂·甘对一段童年不幸遭遇的朴实、委婉的记述

  • ...elliptical references to problems best not aired in public.

    对那些不宜当众谈及的问题的隐诲提及

SYN oblique, concentrated, obscure, compact  
elliptically
