directorate
daɪrektərət dɪr- dɪˈrɛktərɪt dɪˈrɛktərɪt daɪˈrɛktərɪt
Word forms: plural directorates
1 N-COUNT (公司或机构的)董事会，理事会 A directorate is a board of directors in a company or organization. (公司或机构的)董事会，理事会 [business]

  • The Bank would be managed by a directorate of professional bankers. [+ of]

    该银行将由一个职业银行家组成的董事会来管理。

2 N-COUNT (政府的)专门委员会 A directorate is a part of a government department which is responsible for one particular thing. (政府的)专门委员会

  • ...the Health and Safety Directorate of the EU.

    欧盟的健康安全理事会
