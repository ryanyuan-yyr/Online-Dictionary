protrude
prətruːd proʊ- proʊˈtrud prətrud
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense protrudes, present participle protruding, past tense , past participle protruded
VERB 伸出；凸出 If something protrudes from somewhere, it sticks out. 伸出；凸出 [formal]

  • ...a huge round mass of smooth rock protruding from the water. [VERB prep.]

    一块光滑的巨型圆石露出水面

  • The tip of her tongue was protruding slightly. [VERB]

    她的舌尖微微伸出。

SYN stick out, start (from), point, project
