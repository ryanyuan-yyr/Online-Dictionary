Prohibition
ˌprəʊɪˈbɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT （美国1919年至1933年间的）禁酒令；禁酒令时期 In the United States, Prohibition was the law that prevented the manufacture, sale, and transporting of alcoholic drinks between 1919 and 1933. Prohibition also refers to the period when this law existed. （美国1919年至1933年间的）禁酒令；禁酒令时期
