party pooper
pɑːʳti puːpəʳ ˈpuːpə ˈpɑrtiˌpupər
Word forms: plural party poopers
N-COUNT 令人扫兴的人；煞风景的人 You describe someone as a party pooper when you think that they spoil other people's fun and their enjoyment of something. 令人扫兴的人；煞风景的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • I hate to be a party pooper, but I am really tired.

    我不想扫大家的兴，不过我真的累了。
