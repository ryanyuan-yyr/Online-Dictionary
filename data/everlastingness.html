everlasting
evəʳlɑːstɪŋ -læst- ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ
1 ADJ 永久的;永恒的;无穷无尽的 Something that is everlasting never comes to an end. 永久的;永恒的;无穷无尽的

  • ...a message of peace and everlasting life.

    和平和永生的预言

2 ADJ 永恒不变的；永不停止的 If you describe something as everlasting, you mean that it seems never to change or end. 永恒不变的；永不停止的 [literary]

  • I have loved you with an everlasting love!.

    我对你的爱从未改变！

  • Sometimes the work can feel unrewarding and everlasting.

    有时候，那个工作让人觉得没有成就、永无止境。
