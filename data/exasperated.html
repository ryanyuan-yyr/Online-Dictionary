exasperated
ɪgzɑːspəreɪtɪd -zæs- ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 恼怒的;恼火的；烦恼的 If you describe a person as exasperated, you mean that they are frustrated or angry because of something that is happening or something that another person is doing. 恼怒的;恼火的；烦恼的

  • Bertha was exasperated at the delay.

    延误让伯莎十分恼怒。

  • The president was clearly exasperated by the whole saga. [+by]

    整个事件显然让总统很恼火。

  • Welland gave an exasperated sigh and turned back.

    韦兰十分烦恼地叹了口气，转过身去。

SYN irritated, cross, annoyed, infuriated
