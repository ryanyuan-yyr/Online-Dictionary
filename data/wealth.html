wealth
welθ wɛlθ
1 N-UNCOUNT 财富;财产 Wealth is the possession of a large amount of money, property, or other valuable things. You can also refer to a particular person's money or property as their wealth . 财富;财产

  • Economic reform has brought relative wealth to peasant farmers.

    经济改革给农民带来了相对的财富。

  • His own wealth grew.

    他的个人财富在增长。

2 N-SING 大量;丰富 If you say that someone or something has a wealth of good qualities or things, you are emphasizing that they have a very large number or amount of them. 大量;丰富 [formal, emphasis]

  • ...such a wealth of creative expertise. [+ of]

    如此丰富的创造性专门技能

  • The city boasts a wealth of beautiful churches.

    这座城市拥有众多美丽的教堂。

SYN abundance, store, plenty, richness
