outrun
aʊtrʌn ˌaʊtˈrʌn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outruns, present participle outrunning, past tense outran
The form outrun is used in the present tense and is also the past participle of the verb. outrun的过去分词与原形相同。
1 VERB 跑得比…快 If you outrun someone, you run faster than they do, and therefore are able to escape from them or to arrive somewhere before they do. 跑得比…快

  • There are not many players who can outrun me. [VERB noun]

    跑得比我快的球员为数不多。

SYN outdistance, beat, escape, leave behind  
2 VERB 超过；超出；发展快于 If one thing outruns another thing, the first thing develops faster than the second thing. 超过；超出；发展快于

  • Spending could outrun the capacity of businesses to produce the goods. [VERB noun]

    消费有可能超出企业的产能。
