smithereens
smɪðəriːnz ˌsmɪðərˈinz
N-PLURAL [usu to N] 小片;碎片 If something is smashed or blown to smithereens, it breaks into very small pieces. 小片;碎片

  • She dropped the vase and smashed it to smithereens.

    她失手将花瓶摔得粉碎。

  • They walked right into a booby-trap and got blown to smithereens.

    他们正好踩上了饵雷，结果被炸得粉身碎骨。
