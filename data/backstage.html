backstage
bæksteɪdʒ ˌbækˈsteɪdʒ ˌbækˌsteɪdʒ
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 在（或往）后台 In a theatre, backstage refers to the areas behind the stage. 在（或往）后台

  • He went backstage and asked for her autograph.

    他跑到后台向她索要签名。

  • He met Jackson backstage at his concert in Leeds.

    他在利兹音乐会的后台见到了杰克逊。

Backstage is also an adjective.

  • ...a backstage pass.

    后台通行证
