talk out of
1 PHRASAL VERB 说服…放弃;劝服…不做 If you talk someone out of doing something they want or intend to do, you persuade them not to do it. 说服…放弃;劝服…不做

  • My mother tried to talk me out of getting a divorce. [V n P P -ing/n]

    我妈妈试图劝我不要离婚。

  • People tried to talk him out of it, but he insisted. [V n P P -ing/n]

    大家都劝他放弃，可他却很坚持。

SYN dissuade someone from, put someone off, discourage someone from, stop someone from  
2 PHRASAL VERB (通过谈话)摆脱，甩掉,避开 If you talk yourself out of a particular situation or state, you get yourself out of it by talking. (通过谈话)摆脱，甩掉,避开

  • I tried to talk myself out of a fight. [V pron-refl P P n]

    我费尽口舌想避免一场打架。
