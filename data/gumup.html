gum up
PHRASAL VERB 使出故障；把…搞糟 To gum something up means to stop it working properly or efficiently. 使出故障；把…搞糟 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Regulators may gum up an efficient system. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    管理者可能会使一个行之有效的制度陷于瘫痪。

  • The house price chain is gummed up. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    房屋买卖价格链已经失控。
