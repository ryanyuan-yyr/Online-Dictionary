bring back
1 PHRASAL VERB 使记起；使想起 Something that brings back a memory makes you think about it. 使记起；使想起

  • Your article brought back sad memories for me. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你的文章使我想起了伤心的往事。

  • Talking about it brought it all back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    谈到这个让人想起了整件事。

SYN bring to mind, evoke, summon up, remind you of  
2 PHRASAL VERB 恢复（惯例）；使（潮流）再度流行 When people bring back a practice or fashion that existed at an earlier time, they introduce it again. 恢复（惯例）；使（潮流）再度流行 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The House of Commons is to debate once again whether to bring back the death penalty. [VERB PREP. noun]

    下议院将再次就是否恢复死刑展开辩论。
