hush
hʌʃ hʌʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hushes, present participle hushing, past tense , past participle hushed
1 CONVENTION （用于要求或命令别人安静）嘘 You say 'Hush!' to someone when you are asking or telling them to be quiet. （用于要求或命令别人安静）嘘

  • Hush, my love, it's all right.

    嘘，宝贝，没事了。

2 V-ERG （使）安静下来;（使）停止说话 If you hush someone or if they hush, they stop speaking or making a noise. （使）安静下来;（使）停止说话

  • She tried to hush her noisy father. [VERB noun]

    她试图让吵吵嚷嚷的父亲安静下来。

  • I had to box Max's ears to get him to hush. [VERB]

    我不得不打了马克斯一耳光让他闭嘴。

SYN quieten, still, silence, suppress  
3 N-SING 寂静;宁静;（突然出现的）安静 You say there is a hush in a place when everything is quiet and peaceful, or suddenly becomes quiet. 寂静;宁静;（突然出现的）安静

  • A hush fell over the crowd and I knew something terrible had happened.

    人群突然一片肃静，我知道一定发生了什么可怕的事。

SYN quiet, silence, calm, still [poetic]  
Phrasal verbs:
hush up
