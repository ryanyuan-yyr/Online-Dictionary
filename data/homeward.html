homeward
hoʊmwəʳd ˈhoʊmwərd
(also homewards)
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 归家的;还乡的;回国的 If you are on a homeward journey, you are on a journey towards your home. 归家的;还乡的;回国的

  • She is ready for her homeward journey.

    她准备好踏上返家之程了。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向家;向家乡;向本国 If you are travelling homeward or homewards, you are travelling towards your home. 向家;向家乡;向本国

  • John drove homeward through the lanes.

    约翰驱车穿过小巷向家驶去。

  • They travelled happily homewards.

    他们高高兴兴地往家赶。
