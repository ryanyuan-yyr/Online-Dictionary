fondue
fɒndjuː ˈfɒndjuː fɔ̃dy -duː fɑnˈdu ˈfɑnˌdu
Word forms: plural fondues
N-VAR 干酪火锅;热油火锅 A fondue is a sauce made from melted cheese into which you dip bread, or a pot of hot oil into which you dip pieces of meat or vegetables. 干酪火锅;热油火锅

  • They stopped at a little cafe and had a fondue.

    他们在一家小餐馆歇脚，吃了顿干酪火锅。
