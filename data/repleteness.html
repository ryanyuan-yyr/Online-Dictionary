replete
rɪpliːt rɪˈplit
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ with n] 充满的;装满的 To be replete with something means to be full of it. 充满的;装满的 [formal]

  • The Harbor was replete with boats.

    港口里满是船只。

  • History is replete with examples of populations out of control.

    历史上人口失控的例子比比皆是。

SYN filled, stuffed, jammed, crammed  
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 饱的;吃饱喝足的 If you are replete, you are pleasantly full of food and drink. 饱的;吃饱喝足的 [formal]

  • Replete, guests can then retire to the modern conservatory for coffee.

    吃饱喝足后，宾客们可以到现代化的暖房里享受一杯咖啡。
