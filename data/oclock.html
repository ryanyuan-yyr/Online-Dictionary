o'clock əklɒk əˈklɑk oʊˈklɑk
1 ADV …点钟 You use o'clock after numbers from one to twelve to say what time it is. For example, if you say that it is 9 o'clock, you mean that it is nine hours after midnight or nine hours after midday. …点钟

  • The trouble began just after ten o'clock last night.

    昨晚刚过10点钟麻烦就开始了。

  • I went to sleep, and at two o'clock in the morning I woke up.

    我睡着了，凌晨两点的时候醒了过来。

+−Usage
Usageo'clock
Use o'clock for times that are exactly on the hour: 'Is it four o'clock yet?' 'Not quite, it's three forty-five.'
