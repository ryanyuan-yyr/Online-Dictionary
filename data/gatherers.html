gatherer
gæðərəʳ
Word forms: plural gatherers
N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 收集者；采集者 A gatherer is someone who collects or gathers a particular thing. 收集者；采集者

  • The brazil nut gatherers were paid only 2 to 3 percent of the New York wholesale price.

    这些巴西果采摘者所卖的钱仅为纽约批发价格的2%到3%。

  • ...professional intelligence gatherers.

    专业情报收集员
