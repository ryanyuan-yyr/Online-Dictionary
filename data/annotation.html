annotation
ænoʊteɪʃən ˌænəˈteɪʃən ˌ ænoʊˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural annotations
1 N-UNCOUNT 注解；评注 Annotation is the activity of annotating something. 注解；评注

  • She retained a number of copies for further annotation.

    她保留了许多副本以便作进一步的注解。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 注释；评注 An annotation is a note that is added to a text or diagram, often in order to explain it. 注释；评注

  • He supplied annotations to nearly 15,000 musical works.

    他给近1.5万部音乐作品作过注解。

SYN note, comment, explanation, observation
