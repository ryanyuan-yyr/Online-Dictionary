vagrant
veɪgrənt ˈveɪgrənt
Word forms: plural vagrants
1 N-COUNT 流浪汉;漂泊者;乞丐 A vagrant is someone who moves a lot from place to place because they have no permanent home or job, and have to ask for or steal things in order to live. 流浪汉;漂泊者;乞丐

  • He lived on the street as a vagrant.

    他以在大街上乞讨为生。

SYN tramp, bum [informal] , drifter, vagabond
