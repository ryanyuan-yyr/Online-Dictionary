sector
sektəʳ ˈsɛktər ˈsɛkˌtɔr
Word forms: plural sectors
1 N-COUNT (经济的)部门，行业，领域 A particular sector of a country's economy is the part connected with that specified type of industry. (经济的)部门，行业，领域

  • ...the nation's manufacturing sector.

    该国的制造业

  • ...the service sector of the Hong Kong economy.

    香港经济中的服务行业

SYN part, division, branch, category  
2See also:public sector  private sector 
3 N-COUNT (大型组织的)部分,小群体 A sector of a large group is a smaller group which is part of it. (大型组织的)部分,小群体

  • Workers who went to the Gulf came from the poorest sectors of Pakistani society. [+ of]

    前往海湾的工人来自巴基斯坦最贫穷的社会阶层。

4 N-COUNT 军事管制区;军事防御区 A sector is an area of a city or country which is controlled by a military force. 军事管制区;军事防御区

  • Officers were going to retake sectors of the city. [+ of]

    军官们打算重新夺回该市的防御区。

SYN area, part, region, district  
5 N-COUNT 扇形 A sector is a part of a circle which is formed when you draw two straight lines from the centre of the circle to the edge. 扇形 [technical]
