supervisor
suːpəʳvaɪzəʳ ˈsupərˌvaɪzər
Word forms: plural supervisors
N-COUNT (尤指工人或学生的)管理者，监督者，指导者 A supervisor is a person who supervises activities or people, especially workers or students. (尤指工人或学生的)管理者，监督者，指导者

  • ...a full-time job as a supervisor at a factory.

    在一家工厂担任主管的全职工作

  • Each student has a supervisor to advise on the writing of the dissertation.

    每个学生都有导师指导他们写论文。

SYN boss [informal] , manager, superintendent, chief
