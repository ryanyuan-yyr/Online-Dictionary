pitch in
PHRASAL VERB 参与；协力 If you pitch in, you join in and help with an activity. 参与；协力 [informal]

  • The agency says international relief agencies also have pitched in. [VERB PREP.]

    该机构说国际救援机构也提供了支援。

  • Cartoonists have pitched in with their favourites from their own and other hands. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    漫画家拿出了他们最喜爱的出自自己和他人之手的作品。

  • The entire company pitched in to help. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    公司上下都协力相助。
