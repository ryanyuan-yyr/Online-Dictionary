caress
kəres kəˈrɛs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense caresses, present participle caressing, past tense , past participle caressed
VERB 爱抚;抚摸 If you caress someone, you stroke them gently and affectionately. 爱抚;抚摸 [written]

  • He was gently caressing her golden hair. [VERB noun]

    他深情抚摸着她的金发。

Caress is also a noun.

  • Margaret took me to one side, holding my arm in a gentle caress.

    玛格丽特将我拉到一边，轻轻地抚摸着我的胳膊。
