rimmed
rɪmd
1 ADJ 镶有…边框的 If something is rimmed with a substance or colour, it has that substance or colour around its border. 镶有…边框的

  • The plates and glassware were rimmed with gold.

    这些盘子和玻璃器皿都镶着金边。

2 See also: rim  -rimmed
