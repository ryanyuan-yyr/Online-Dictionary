well-intentioned ˌwɛlɪnˈtɛnʃənd
(also well intentioned)
ADJ-GRADED (虽常事与愿违但)出于好心的,善意的,本意良好的 If you say that a person or their actions are well-intentioned, you mean that they intend to be helpful or kind but they are unsuccessful or cause problems. (虽常事与愿违但)出于好心的,善意的,本意良好的

  • He is well-intentioned but a poor administrator.

    他是个空有一番好意但能力很差的主管。

  • ...rules that, however well-intentioned, are often hopelessly impractical.

    尽管出发点很好却往往完全不切实际的规定
