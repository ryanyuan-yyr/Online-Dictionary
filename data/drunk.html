drunk
drʌŋk drʌŋk
Word forms: plural drunks
1 ADJ-GRADED 喝醉的 Someone who is drunk has drunk so much alcohol that they cannot speak clearly or behave sensibly. 喝醉的

  • Stewart could not remember exactly why he had done it because he was so drunk.

    斯图尔特记不清他为什么要那样做了,因为他烂醉如泥。

  • I got drunk and had to be carried home.

    我喝醉了，只好让人把我送回家。

  • He was arrested on suspicion of drunk driving.

    他因涉嫌酒后驾车而被捕。

SYN intoxicated, loaded [slang , mainly US , Canadian] , tight [informal] , canned [slang]  
2 N-COUNT 醉汉,酒鬼;酗酒者 A drunk is someone who is drunk or frequently gets drunk. 醉汉,酒鬼;酗酒者

  • A drunk lay in the alley.

    一个酒鬼躺在小胡同里。

SYN drunkard, alcoholic, lush [slang] , boozer [informal]  
3 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 陶醉的;兴奋的;沉醉的 If you are drunk with a strong emotion or an experience, you are in a state of great excitement because of it. 陶醉的;兴奋的;沉醉的

  • They are currently drunk with success. [+ with]

    他们现在正陶醉于成功之中。

  • I felt drunk with the excitement of life.

    当时我沉醉于生活中的精彩刺激。

4 Drunk is the past participle of drink.
