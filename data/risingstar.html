rising star
ˈraɪzɪŋ stɑː
Word forms: plural rising stars
N-COUNT 新星;新秀;后起之秀 A rising star in a particular sport, art, or area of business is someone who is starting to do very well and who people think will soon be very successful. 新星;新秀;后起之秀 [journalism]

  • Anna is a rising star in the world of modelling.

    安娜是模特界的新秀。
