shoestring
ʃuːstrɪŋ ʃuˌstrɪŋ
Word forms: plural shoestrings
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 鞋带 Shoestrings are long, narrow pieces of material like pieces of string that you use to fasten your shoes. 鞋带 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 shoelaces
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 资金极少的;小成本的 A shoestring budget is one where you have very little money to spend. 资金极少的;小成本的

  • The British-produced film was made on a shoestring budget.

    这部英国出品的电影是小成本制作。

3 PHRASE 用很少的钱;以小成本 If you do something or make something on a shoestring, you do it using very little money. 用很少的钱;以小成本

  • The theatre will be run on a shoestring.

    戏院将小本经营。
