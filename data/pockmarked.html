pockmarked
pɒkmɑːʳkt ˈpɒkˌmɑːkd
(also pock-marked)
ADJ-GRADED 有痘痕的；有麻点的；有凹痕的 If the surface of something is pockmarked, it has small hollow marks covering it. 有痘痕的；有麻点的；有凹痕的

  • He had a pockmarked face.

    他的脸上有麻子。

  • The living room is pockmarked with bullet holes. [+ with]

    客厅墙壁上有弹孔。

SYN scarred, spotted, pitted, blemished
