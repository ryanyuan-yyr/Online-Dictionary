stepping stone
(also stepping-stone)Word forms: plural stepping stones
1 N-COUNT (尤指事业的)垫脚石，进身之阶 You can describe a job or event as a stepping stone when it helps you to make progress, especially in your career. (尤指事业的)垫脚石，进身之阶

  • Many students now see university as a stepping stone to a good job. [+ to]

    很多学生现在将上大学看成是找一份好工作的敲门砖。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (小溪或小河中的)垫脚石，踏脚石 Stepping stones are a line of large stones which you can walk on in order to cross a shallow stream or river. (小溪或小河中的)垫脚石，踏脚石
