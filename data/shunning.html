shun
ʃʌn ʃʌn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shuns, present participle shunning, past tense , past participle shunned
VERB 刻意避开;有意回避 If you shun someone or something, you deliberately avoid them or keep away from them. 刻意避开;有意回避

  • From that time forward everybody shunned him. [VERB noun]

    从那时候起，每个人都有意回避他。

  • He has always shunned publicity. [VERB noun]

    他一直有意避开媒体的关注。

  • This extremist organization has shunned conventional politics. [VERB noun]

    这个极端主义组织有意避开了传统政治。

SYN avoid, steer clear of, keep away from, snub
