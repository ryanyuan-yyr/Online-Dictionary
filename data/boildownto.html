boil down to
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 重点是；将…归结为 If you say that a situation or problem boils down to a particular thing or can be boiled down to a particular thing, you mean that this is the most important or the most basic aspect of it. 重点是；将…归结为

  • What they want boils down to just one thing. It is land. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他们说穿了就是想要一样东西，那就是土地。

  • For Malcolm work could always be boiled down to one idea: being good in business. [be V-ed P P n]

    对于马尔科姆来说，工作归根结底就是要取得好的业绩。

SYN amount to, mean, come to, equal
