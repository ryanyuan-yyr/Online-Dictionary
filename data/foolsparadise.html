fool's paradise
N-SING 幻想的幸福;愚人乐园;虚幻的满足 If you say that someone is living in a fool's paradise, you are criticizing them because they are not aware that their present happy situation is likely to change and get worse. 幻想的幸福;愚人乐园;虚幻的满足 [disapproval]

  • ...living in a fool's paradise of false prosperity.

    生活在虚假繁荣的虚幻幸福中
