nob
nɒb nɑb
Word forms: plural nobs
N-COUNT [usually plural] 上层人士;有钱人 If you refer to a group of people as the nobs, you mean they are rich or come from a much higher social class than you do. 上层人士;有钱人 [British, informal, old-fashioned]

  • ...the nobs who live in the Big House.

    住在深宅大院里的有钱人

  • I'm not tellin' you. Not now you're in with the nobs.

    我不打算告诉你。你现在跟上流社会的人来往，我不会说的。

SYN aristocrat, fat cat [slang , mainly US] , toff [British , slang] , bigwig [informal]
