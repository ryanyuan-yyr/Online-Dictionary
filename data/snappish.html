snappish
snæpɪʃ ˈsnæpɪʃ ˈsnæpɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (对人说话的方式)恶狠狠的，疾言厉色的，发急的 If someone is snappish, they speak to people in a sharp, unfriendly manner. (对人说话的方式)恶狠狠的，疾言厉色的，发急的

  • 'That is beautiful, Tony,' Momma said, no longer sounding at all snappish.

    “真美呀，托尼，”妈妈说道，话音里的恶声恶气不见了。

snappishly

  • She said snappishly. 'I'm not pregnant, Brian.'

    她没好气地说：“布赖恩，我没有怀孕。”
