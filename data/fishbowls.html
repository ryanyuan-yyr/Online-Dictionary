fishbowl
fɪʃboʊl ˈfɪʃˌbəʊl ˈfɪʃˌboʊl
(also fish bowl)Word forms: fishbowls
1 N-COUNT 玻璃鱼缸A fishbowl is a glass bowl in which you can keep fish as pets. 玻璃鱼缸

  • ...the round fishbowl in which Samson the goldfish spent his days.

    ...金鱼萨姆森消磨时光的那个圆玻璃鱼缸。

2 N-COUNT [usu sing] 毫无隐秘的场合You can use fishbowl to describe a place or situation that is open to observation. 毫无隐秘的场合

  • The members of the family lived in a fishbowl where their behaviour was constantly scrutinized.

    这个家的成员生活在毫无隐私的状态，他们的行为举止被不间断的仔细检查着。

  • ...in the fishbowl of the White House.

    ...在白宫的无隐私状态下。
