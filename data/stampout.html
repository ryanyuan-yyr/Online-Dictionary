stamp out
PHRASAL VERB 消灭;根除 If you stamp out something bad that is happening, you make it stop. 消灭;根除 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Dr Muffett stressed that he was opposed to bullying in schools and that action would be taken to stamp it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    马费特博士强调说，他反对学校中的恃强凌弱现象，将会采取行动杜绝该现象。

  • ...on-the-spot fines to stamp the problems out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    为根除这些问题采取的当场罚款

SYN eliminate, destroy, eradicate, crush
