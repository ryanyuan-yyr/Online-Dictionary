true-blue
ˈtruˈblu
(also true blue)
1 ADJ (思想观念)忠于右派的，坚决支持保守党的 If you describe someone as true-blue, you mean that they are right-wing in their ideas and opinions. (思想观念)忠于右派的，坚决支持保守党的 [British]

  • Her husband is a true blue Tory.

    她丈夫是坚定的保守党党员。

2 ADJ 忠贞不渝的;坚定不移的 A true-blue supporter of something is someone who is very loyal and reliable. 忠贞不渝的;坚定不移的 [US]
