dozy
doʊzi ˈdoʊzi
Word forms: comparative dozier, superlative doziest
1 ADJ-GRADED 瞌睡的;昏昏欲睡的;困倦的 If you are dozy, you are feeling sleepy and not very alert. 瞌睡的;昏昏欲睡的;困倦的

  • Maybe I eat too much and that's what makes me dozy.

    也许我吃得太多了，所以昏昏欲睡。

SYN drowsy, sleepy, dozing, nodding  
2 ADJ-GRADED 愚笨的;迟钝的;反应慢的 If you describe someone as dozy, you mean they are rather stupid and slow to understand things. 愚笨的;迟钝的;反应慢的 [informal, disapproval]
