commode
kəmoʊd kəˈmoʊd
Word forms: plural commodes
1 N-COUNT (尤指供病人使用的)座椅式便桶 A commode is a movable piece of furniture shaped like a chair, which has a large pot below or inside it. It is used as a toilet, especially by people who are too ill to be able to walk easily. (尤指供病人使用的)座椅式便桶 [mainly British]
2 N-COUNT 马桶 A commode is a toilet. 马桶 [US]
