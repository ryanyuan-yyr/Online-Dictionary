discourteous
dɪskɜːʳtiəs dɪsˈkɜrtiəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不礼貌的;失礼的 If you say that someone is discourteous, you mean that they are rude and have no consideration for the feelings of other people. 不礼貌的;失礼的 [formal]

  • Staff are often discourteous and sometimes downright rude.

    职员常常态度很不礼貌，有时候极其蛮横。

SYN rude, abrupt, curt, disrespectful
