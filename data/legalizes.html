legalize
liːgəlaɪz ˈligəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense legalizes, present participle legalizing, past tense , past participle legalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 legalise
VERB 使合法化；使得到法律认可 If something is legalized, a law is passed that makes it legal. 使合法化；使得到法律认可

  • Divorce was legalized in 1981. [be VERB-ed]

    离婚合法化是在1981年。

  • ...the decision of the Georgian government to legalise multi-party elections. [VERB noun]

    格鲁吉亚政府让多党选举合法化的决定

SYN permit, allow, approve, sanction  
legalization

  • She ruled out the legalisation of drugs.

    她排除了毒品合法化的可能性。
