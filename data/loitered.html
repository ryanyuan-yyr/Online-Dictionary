loiter
lɔɪtəʳ ˈlɔɪtər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense loiters, present participle loitering, past tense , past participle loitered
VERB 闲逛；游荡；徘徊 If you loiter somewhere, you remain there or walk up and down without any real purpose. 闲逛；游荡；徘徊

  • Unemployed young men loiter at the entrance of the factory. [VERB]

    失业的小年轻在工厂门口游荡。

SYN linger, idle, loaf, saunter
