consideration
kənsɪdəreɪʃən kənˌsɪdərˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural considerations
1 N-UNCOUNT 仔细考虑;斟酌 Consideration is careful thought about something. 仔细考虑;斟酌

  • He said there should be careful consideration of the future role of the BBC. [+ of]

    他说应该认真思考英国广播公司将来要扮演的角色。

SYN thought, study, review, attention  
2 N-UNCOUNT [under N] 讨论;考虑 If something is under consideration, it is being discussed. 讨论;考虑

  • Several proposals are under consideration by the state assembly.

    有几个提案州议会正在讨论中。

3 N-UNCOUNT 体贴;体谅;为人着想 If you show consideration, you pay attention to the needs, wishes, or feelings of other people. 体贴;体谅;为人着想

  • Show consideration for other rail travellers. [+ for]

    体谅其他铁路乘客。

  • Really, her tone said, some people have absolutely no consideration.

    她的语气的确是在说有些人从不替他人考虑。

SYN thoughtfulness, concern, respect, kindness  
4 N-COUNT (尤指在计划或决定时)应考虑的因素 A consideration is something that should be thought about, especially when you are planning or deciding something. (尤指在计划或决定时)应考虑的因素

  • They should not allow partisan political considerations or interests to cloud their judgment.

    他们不应该让党派政治因素或者党派利益影响自己的判断。

  • A major consideration when choosing a dog is the size of your house and garden.

    选择狗时，一个重要的考虑因素是房子和花园的大小。

5 PHRASE 考虑到;顾及 If you take something into consideration, you think about it because it is relevant to what you are doing. 考虑到;顾及

  • The whole affair is bound to be taken into consideration when the Indian side is picked.

    如果选的是印度一方，整个事件必定会纳入考虑范围之中。

  • Safe driving is good driving because it takes into consideration the lives of other people.

    安全驾驶是良好的驾驶习惯，因为它考虑到了他人的人身安全。
