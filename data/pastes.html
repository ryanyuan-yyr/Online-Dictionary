paste
peɪst peɪst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pastes, present participle pasting, past tense , past participle pasted
1 N-MASS 糊糊；糨糊 Paste is a soft, wet, sticky mixture of a substance and a liquid, which can be spread easily. Some types of paste are used to stick things together. 糊糊；糨糊

  • He then sticks it back together with flour paste.

    然后他用面糊把它重新粘了起来。

  • Blend a little milk with the custard powder to form a paste.

    往蛋奶沙司粉里加入一点儿牛奶，搅成糊糊。

  • ...wallpaper paste.

    壁纸糨糊

2 N-MASS （肉、水果或蔬菜弄碎做成的）酱 Paste is a soft smooth mixture made of crushed meat, fruit, or vegetables. You can, for example, spread it onto bread or use it in cooking. （肉、水果或蔬菜弄碎做成的）酱

  • ...tomato paste.

    番茄酱

  • ...fish paste sandwiches.

    鱼酱三明治

3 VERB 粘贴；黏合 If you paste something on a surface, you put glue or paste on it and stick it on the surface. 粘贴；黏合 [Also V n with n]

  • ...pasting labels on bottles. [VERB noun prep.]

    把商标贴在瓶子上

  • Activists pasted up posters criticizing the leftist leaders. [VERB noun with adv.]

    激进分子张贴起海报批评左翼领袖。

SYN stick, fix, glue, cement  
4 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] （制造人造宝石的）铅玻璃 Paste is a hard shiny glass that is used for making imitation jewellery. （制造人造宝石的）铅玻璃

  • ...paste emeralds.

    铅玻璃翡翠
