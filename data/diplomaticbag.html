diplomatic bag
Word forms: plural diplomatic bags
N-COUNT 外交邮袋;外交信袋 A diplomatic bag is a bag or container in which mail is sent to and from foreign embassies. Diplomatic bags are protected by law, so that they are not opened by anyone except the official or embassy they are addressed to. 外交邮袋;外交信袋 [British]
