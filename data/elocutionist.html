elocution
eləkjuːʃən ˌɛləˈkjuʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 演说术;演讲技巧 Elocution lessons are lessons in which someone is taught to speak clearly and in an accent that is considered to be standard and acceptable. 演说术;演讲技巧

  • When I was 11 my mother sent me to elocution lessons.

    在我11岁的时候，妈妈让我去上演说技巧课。

SYN diction, speech, delivery, rhetoric
