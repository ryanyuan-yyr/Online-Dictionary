untouched
ʌntʌtʃt ʌnˈtʌtʃt
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 不受影响的 Something that is untouched by something else is not affected by it. 不受影响的

  • Asian airlines remain untouched by the deregulation that has swept America. [+ by]

    亚洲航空公司并未受到席卷美国的撤销管制浪潮的影响。

  • Vested interests were left untouched.

    既得利益未受影响。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 完好无缺的；未受损害的 If something is untouched, it is not damaged in any way, although it has been in a situation where it could easily have been damaged. 完好无缺的；未受损害的

  • Michael pointed out to me that amongst the rubble, there was one building that remained untouched.

    迈克尔指给我看说，瓦砾中有一栋建筑完好无损。

  • The desk had been rifled for money, some banknotes taken but cheque-book and credit cards left untouched.

    为了找到钱，桌子被翻过了，拿走了一些钞票，但支票簿和信用卡原封未动。

SYN unharmed, intact, unscathed, undamaged  
3 ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 世外桃源般的；不染尘嚣的；天然质朴的 An untouched area or place is thought to be beautiful because it is still in its original state and has not been changed or damaged in any way. 世外桃源般的；不染尘嚣的；天然质朴的

  • Ducie is one of the world's last untouched islands, nearly 5,000km from Australia.

    迪西岛是当今世上仅存的几个不染尘嚣的岛屿之一，距澳大利亚近5,000公里。

  • ...a relatively untouched cottage within only an hour or so's drive of London.

    相对而言未受破坏的小屋，距伦敦仅一个小时左右的车程

SYN unspoilt, pristine, virgin, natural  
4 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] 未吃过（或喝过）的；未动过的 If food or drink is untouched, none of it has been eaten or drunk. 未吃过（或喝过）的；未动过的

  • The coffee was untouched, the toast had cooled.

    咖啡没有动过，吐司已经凉了。

  • He murmured something, then, food left untouched, went into the sitting-room.

    他咕哝了些什么，然后食物丝毫未动就进了起居室。

SYN unconsumed, uneaten, undrunk, surplus
