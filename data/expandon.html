expand on
(also expand upon)
PHRASAL VERB 更充分地阐述;详谈 If you expand on or expand upon something, you give more information or details about it when you write or talk about it. 更充分地阐述;详谈

  • The president used today's speech to expand on remarks he made last month. [VERB PREP. noun]

    总统利用今天的演讲更充分地阐述了他上个月所讲的话。

  • ...a view that I will expand upon below. [VERB PREP. noun]

    接下来我将详细论述的观点

SYN go into detail about, embellish, elaborate on, develop
