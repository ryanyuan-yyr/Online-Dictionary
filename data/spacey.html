spacey
speɪsi ˈspeɪsi
(also spacy)Word forms: comparative spacier, superlative spaciest
ADJ-GRADED （尤指音乐）缥缈虚幻的，荒诞无稽的 You can use spacey to describe things, especially music, which seem strange, especially because they are very modern or like things in a dream. （尤指音乐）缥缈虚幻的，荒诞无稽的 [informal]

  • ...brilliant, spacey guitar sounds.

    华丽而缥缈的吉他声
