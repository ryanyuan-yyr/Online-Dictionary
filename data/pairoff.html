pair off
PHRASAL VERB-RECIP-ERG （使）成对儿，（使）成双（常指成为恋人） When people pair off or are paired off, they form a pair, often in order to become girlfriend and boyfriend. （使）成对儿，（使）成双（常指成为恋人） [Also V P + with]

  • I knew she wouldn't be able to resist pairing me off with someone. [VERB noun PREP. + with]

    我知道她忍不住要将我和某人撮合到一块儿。

  • The squad members paired off to find places to eat and sleep. [VERB PREP.]

    小组成员结对儿找地方吃饭睡觉。
