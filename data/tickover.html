tick over
1 PHRASAL VERB (引擎)空转，低速运转 If an engine is ticking over, it is running at a low speed or rate, for example when it is switched on but you are not actually using it. (引擎)空转，低速运转 [British]

  • Very slowly he moved forward, the engine ticking over. [VERB PREP.]

    他慢慢往前开，发动机低速运转。

2 PHRASAL VERB (人、系统、业务)慢吞吞地工作，进展缓慢 If a person, system, or business is ticking over, they are working steadily, but not producing very much or making much progress. (人、系统、业务)慢吞吞地工作，进展缓慢 [British]

  • The market is at least ticking over. [VERB PREP.]

    至少市场还在缓慢发展。

  • It keeps you ticking over, stops you being complacent. [VERB PREP.]

    这使你进展缓慢，得意不起来。
