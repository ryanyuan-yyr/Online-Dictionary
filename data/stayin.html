stay in
PHRASAL VERB (晚上)呆在家里,没外出 If you stay in during the evening, you remain at home and do not go out. (晚上)呆在家里,没外出

  • If I stay in my boyfriend cooks a wonderful lasagne or chicken or steak. [VERB PREP.]

    如果我呆在家里，男友就会做可口的卤汁面条啦、鸡肉啦，或者牛排。

  • Before we had our child the idea of staying in every night would have been horrific. [VERB PREP.]

    在有孩子前，我们认为每天晚上呆在家里的想法是很可怕的。

SYN stay at home, stop in [informal] , not go out
