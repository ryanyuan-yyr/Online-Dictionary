umpire
ʌmpaɪəʳ ˈʌmpaɪr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense umpires, present participle umpiring, past tense , past participle umpired
1 N-COUNT （体育比赛或竞赛中的）裁判，裁判员 An umpire is a person whose job is to make sure that a sports match or contest is played fairly and that the rules are not broken. （体育比赛或竞赛中的）裁判，裁判员

  • The umpire's decision is final.

    裁判的裁定是不可更改的。

SYN referee, judge, ref [informal] , arbiter  
2 VERB 在…中担任裁判 To umpire means to be the umpire in a sports match or contest. 在…中担任裁判

  • He umpired baseball games. [VERB noun]

    他担任棒球比赛的裁判。

  • He umpired for school football matches until he was in his late 50s. [+ for

    他担任校际足球比赛的裁判一直到年近花甲。

SYN referee, judge, adjudicate, arbitrate
