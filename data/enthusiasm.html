enthusiasm
ɪnθjuːziæzəm ɪnˈθjuːzɪˌæzəm -θuː- ɛnˈθuziˌæzəm ɛnˈθjuziˌæzəm ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm ɪnˈθjuziæzəm
Word forms: plural enthusiasms
1 N-VAR 热爱;热情;热心;热忱 Enthusiasm is great eagerness to be involved in a particular activity which you like and enjoy or which you think is important. 热爱;热情;热心;热忱

  • The lack of enthusiasm for unification among most West Germans fills him with disappointment.

    大多数西德人都没有渴望统一的热情，这令他无比失望。

  • Their skill, enthusiasm and running has got them in the team.

    他们的技术、热忱和跑动能力使他们得以加入这支球队。

2 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 感兴趣的活动;热衷的学科 An enthusiasm is an activity or subject that interests you very much and that you spend a lot of time on. 感兴趣的活动;热衷的学科

  • Draw him out about his current enthusiasms and future plans.

    让他畅所欲言地谈谈自己目前热衷的活动和将来的计划。
