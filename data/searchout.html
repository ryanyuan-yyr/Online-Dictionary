search out
PHRASAL VERB 找出;查出;找寻到 If you search something out, you keep looking for it until you find it. 找出;查出;找寻到

  • Traditional Spanish food is delicious and its specialities are worth searching out. [VERB PREP. noun]

    传统的西班牙食品美味可口，其特色菜肴值得寻访。

  • Many people want jobs. They try to search them out every day. [VERB noun PREP.]

    许多人都想有份工作。他们每天努力寻找。
