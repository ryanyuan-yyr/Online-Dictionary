poach
poʊtʃ poʊtʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense poaches, present participle poaching, past tense , past participle poached
1 VERB （侵入他人地界）偷猎，偷捕 If someone poaches fish, animals, or birds, they illegally catch them on someone else's property. （侵入他人地界）偷猎，偷捕 [Also VERB]

  • Many wildlife parks are regularly invaded by people poaching game. [VERB noun]

    很多野生动物园都时常遭到偷猎之徒的侵入。

SYN steal, rob, plunder, hunt or fish illegally  
poacher

  • Security cameras have been installed to guard against poachers.

    已安装了安全摄像头来监视偷猎者。

poaching

  • ...the poaching of elephants for their tusks.

    为获取象牙而偷猎大象

2 VERB 挖（其他机构的成员或顾客） If an organization poaches members or customers from another organization, they secretly or dishonestly persuade them to join them or become their customers. 挖（其他机构的成员或顾客）

  • The company authorised its staff to poach customers from the opposition. [VERB noun]

    公司指使其职员挖对手的客户。

  • ...allegations that it had poached members from other unions. [VERB noun + from]

    它从其他工会挖走了成员的说法

SYN take, steal, appropriate, snatch [informal]  
poaching

  • The union was accused of poaching.

    该工会被指挖人墙脚。

3 VERB 窃取，盗用（他人的想法） If someone poaches an idea, they dishonestly or illegally use the idea. 窃取，盗用（他人的想法）

  • The opposition parties complained that the government had poached their ideas. [VERB noun]

    众反对党抱怨政府盗用它们的想法。

4 VERB 把（鸡蛋）打入沸水中煮 When you poach an egg, you cook it gently in boiling water without its shell. 把（鸡蛋）打入沸水中煮

  • Poach the eggs for 4 minutes. [VERB noun]

    把鸡蛋打入开水中煮4分钟

  • He had a light breakfast of poached eggs and tea. [VERB-ed]

    他早餐就吃了点水煮荷包蛋，喝了点茶。

5 VERB （在煮沸的水、牛奶或其他液体中）用文火煮，煨（鱼等食品） If you poach food such as fish, you cook it gently in boiling water, milk, or other liquid. （在煮沸的水、牛奶或其他液体中）用文火煮，煨（鱼等食品）

  • Poach the chicken until just cooked. [VERB noun]

    用文火把鸡肉煮到刚刚熟。

  • ...a pear poached in red wine. [VERB-ed]

    红酒煨的梨

  • The main course was to be a whole poached salmon. [VERB-ed]

    主菜将是一整条清炖鲑鱼。

poaching

  • You will need a pot of broth for poaching.

    你需要一锅汤来煮。
