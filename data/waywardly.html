wayward
weɪwəʳd ˈweɪwərd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 任性的;不易管教的;难以捉摸的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as wayward, you mean that they behave in a selfish, bad, or unpredictable way, and are difficult to control. 任性的;不易管教的;难以捉摸的

  • ...wayward children with a history of severe emotional problems.

    出现过严重情绪问题的任性的孩子

SYN erratic, unruly, wilful, unmanageable  
waywardness

  • ...the curiosity, caprice and waywardness of children.

    孩子的好奇、善变和任性
