blood-curdling
(also bloodcurdling)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 令人毛骨悚然的；让人深感恐惧的 A blood-curdling sound or story is very frightening and horrible. 令人毛骨悚然的；让人深感恐惧的

  • ...blood-curdling tales.

    令人不寒而栗的故事

  • ... a bloodcurdling battle cry.

    让人胆战心惊的战场上的喊杀声

SYN terrifying, shocking [informal] , frightening, scary [informal]
