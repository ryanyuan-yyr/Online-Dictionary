dinosaur
daɪnəsɔːʳ ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr
Word forms: plural dinosaurs
1 N-COUNT 恐龙 Dinosaurs were large reptiles which lived in prehistoric times. 恐龙
2 N-COUNT 庞大臃肿的机构;过时的庞然大物 If you refer to an organization as a dinosaur, you mean that it is large, inefficient, and out of date. 庞大臃肿的机构;过时的庞然大物 [disapproval]

  • ...industrial dinosaurs.

    庞大陈旧的工业体系
