conundrum
kənʌndrəm kəˈnʌndrəm
Word forms: plural conundrums
N-COUNT 难题;复杂难解的问题 A conundrum is a problem or puzzle which is difficult or impossible to solve. 难题;复杂难解的问题 [formal]

  • ...this theological conundrum of the existence of evil and suffering in a world created by a good God. [+ of]

    在善良的上帝创造的世界里存在着邪恶和苦难这一神学之谜

SYN puzzle, problem, riddle, enigma
