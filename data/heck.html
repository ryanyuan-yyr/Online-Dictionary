heck
hek hɛk
1 EXCLAM （表示稍感恼怒、吃惊等）见鬼，糟糕 People sometimes say 'heck!' when they are slightly irritated or surprised. （表示稍感恼怒、吃惊等）见鬼，糟糕 [informal, feelings]

  • Heck, if you don't like it, don't vote for him.

    如果你不喜欢，不要投他的票不就得了。

  • Oh, heck. What can I write about?

    真见鬼。我能写些什么呢？

2 PHRASE 极大;极多 People use a heck of to emphasize how big something is or how much of it there is. 极大;极多 [informal, emphasis]

  • They're spending a heck of a lot of money.

    他们正大把大把地花钱。

  • The truth is, I'm in one heck of a mess.

    事实上我麻烦大了。

3 PHRASE （尤在迷惑或恼怒时用于疑问句中表示强调）到底，究竟 You use the heck in expressions such as 'what the heck' and 'how the heck' in order to emphasize a question, especially when you are puzzled or annoyed. （尤在迷惑或恼怒时用于疑问句中表示强调）到底，究竟 [informal, emphasis]

  • What the heck's that?.

    那到底是什么鬼东西？

  • The question was, where the heck was he?

    问题是，他究竟人在哪里？

4 PHRASE 管它呢;不管它 You say 'what the heck' to indicate that you do not care about a bad aspect of an action or situation. 管它呢;不管它 [informal, feelings]

  • What the heck, I thought, I'll give it a whirl.

    管它呢，我想，我就试试吧。
