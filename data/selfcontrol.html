self-control
ˈsɛlfkənˈtroʊl
N-UNCOUNT 自制力;自我克制 Self-control is the ability to not show your feelings or not do the things that your feelings make you want to do. 自制力;自我克制

  • His self-control, reserve and aloofness were almost inhuman.

    他自制、矜持和冷漠得几乎有些不近人情。

  • I began to wish I'd shown more self-control.

    我开始想当时若是更自制一些就好了。

SYN willpower, restraint, self-discipline, cool [slang]
