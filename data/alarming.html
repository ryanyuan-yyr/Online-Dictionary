alarming
əlɑːʳmɪŋ əˈlɑrmɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 使人惊恐的；令人忧虑的；让人担心的 Something that is alarming makes you feel afraid or anxious that something unpleasant or dangerous might happen. 使人惊恐的；令人忧虑的；让人担心的

  • The disease has spread at an alarming rate.

    这种病以惊人的速度传播开来。

  • ...the alarming increase in crime.

    令人忧虑的犯罪攀升现象

SYN frightening, shocking, scaring, disturbing  
alarmingly

  • ...the alarmingly high rate of heart disease.

    高得令人担心的心脏病发病率
