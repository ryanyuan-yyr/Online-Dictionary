vendor
vendəʳ ˈvɛndə ˈvɛndər vɛnˈdɔr
Word forms: plural vendors
1 N-COUNT 小贩;摊贩 A vendor is someone who sells things such as newspapers, cigarettes, or food from a small stall or cart. 小贩;摊贩

  • ...ice-cream vendors.

    卖冰激凌的小贩

2 N-COUNT （房地产的）卖主，卖方 The vendor of a house or piece of land is the person who owns it and is selling it. （房地产的）卖主，卖方 [law]

  • Remember, the estate agent is working for the vendor.

    记住，房地产代理商是为房地产卖方服务的。
