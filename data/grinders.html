grinder
graɪndəʳ ˈgraɪndər
Word forms: plural grinders
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 研磨机，碾磨机（厨用电器） In a kitchen, a grinder is a device for crushing food such as coffee or meat into small pieces or into a powder. 研磨机，碾磨机（厨用电器）

  • ...an electric coffee grinder.

    电动咖啡研磨机

2 N-COUNT 磨床；砂轮 A grinder is a machine or tool for sharpening, smoothing, or polishing the surface of something. 磨床；砂轮
