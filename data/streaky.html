streaky
striːki ˈstriki
Word forms: comparative streakier, superlative streakiest
ADJ-GRADED 有条纹(或条痕)的 Something that is streaky is marked with long stripes that are a different colour to the rest of it. 有条纹(或条痕)的

  • She has streaky fair hair and blue eyes.

    她有一双蓝色的眼睛，头上有一缕缕的金发。

  • ...the empty house with its streaky windows.

    带有条纹窗户的空房子
