bottomless
bɒtəmləs ˈbɑtəmlɪs
1 ADJ 无限的；无穷尽的 If you describe a supply of something as bottomless, you mean that it seems so large that it will never run out. 无限的；无穷尽的

  • Princess Anne does not have a bottomless purse.

    安妮公主的钱并非多得取之不尽，用之不竭。

SYN unlimited, endless, infinite, limitless  
2 ADJ 深不见底的；深不可测的 If you describe something as bottomless, you mean that it is so deep that it seems to have no bottom. 深不见底的；深不可测的

  • His eyes were like bottomless brown pools.

    他的双眼深不见底，犹如两湾棕色的深潭。

SYN deep, profound, yawning, boundless  
3 PHRASE 取之不竭的事物；宝藏；无底洞 If you describe something as a bottomless pit, you mean that it seems as if you can take things from it and it will never be empty or put things in it and it will never be full. 取之不竭的事物；宝藏；无底洞

  • A gold mine is not a bottomless pit, the gold runs out.

    金矿并非无底的宝藏，金子终究会被采光。

  • The problem is we don't have a bottomless pit of resources.

    问题是我们没有取之不尽的资源。

  • He's a bottomless pit as far as food is concerned.

    他吃起东西来肚子像个无底洞。
