headlock
hɛdlɒk ˈhɛdˌlɒk ˈhɛdˌlɑk
Word forms: headlocks
N-COUNT (摔跤动作中的)夹头; 锁头A headlock is a hold in wrestling in which a wrestler grips his opponent's head between his elbow and the side of his body. (摔跤动作中的)夹头; 锁头

  • The guard put a youth in a headlock and dragged him away.
