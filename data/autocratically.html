autocratic
ɔːtəkrætɪk ˌɔːtəˈkrætɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 独裁的;专制的;独断专行的 An autocratic person or organization has complete power and makes decisions without asking anyone else's advice. 独裁的;专制的;独断专行的

  • The people have grown intolerant in recent weeks of the King's autocratic ways.

    最近几周国王的专制行径让民众忍无可忍。

SYN dictatorial, absolute, unlimited, all-powerful
