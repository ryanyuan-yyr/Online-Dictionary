morose
məroʊs məˈroʊs
ADJ-GRADED 阴郁的;脾气不好的;孤僻的 Someone who is morose is miserable, bad-tempered, and not willing to talk very much to other people. 阴郁的;脾气不好的;孤僻的

  • She was morose, pale, and reticent.

    她面色苍白、忧郁孤僻，并且沉默寡言。

SYN sullen, miserable, moody, gloomy  
morosely

  • One elderly man sat morosely at the bar.

    一位老者闷闷不乐地坐在酒吧里。
