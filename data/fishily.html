fishy
fɪʃi ˈfɪʃi
1 ADJ-GRADED (味道或气味)鱼腥味的，鱼味的 A fishy taste or smell reminds you of fish. (味道或气味)鱼腥味的，鱼味的
2 ADJ-GRADED 可疑的;有鬼的 If you describe a situation as fishy, you feel that someone is not telling the truth or behaving completely honestly. 可疑的;有鬼的 [informal]

  • There seems to be something fishy going on.

    事情好像有点不对劲。

SYN suspicious, odd, suspect, unlikely
