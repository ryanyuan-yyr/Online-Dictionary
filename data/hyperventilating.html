hyperventilate
haɪpəʳventɪleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hyperventilates, present participle hyperventilating, past tense , past participle hyperventilated
VERB （通常指因恐惧、疲惫或兴奋而）呼吸急促，通气过度 If someone hyperventilates, they begin to breathe very fast in an uncontrollable way, usually because they are very frightened, tired, or excited. （通常指因恐惧、疲惫或兴奋而）呼吸急促，通气过度

  • I hyperventilate when they come near me with the needle. [VERB]

    当他们拿着注射针走近我时，我的呼吸急速加快。
