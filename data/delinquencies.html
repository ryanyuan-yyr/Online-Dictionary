delinquency
dɪlɪŋkwənsi dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi
Word forms: plural delinquencies
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤指青少年的)违法行为，犯罪行为 Delinquency is criminal behaviour, especially that of young people. (尤指青少年的)违法行为，犯罪行为

  • He had no history of delinquency.

    他没有违法记录。

  • ...a whole range of crimes and delinquencies.

    各种违法犯罪行为

SYN crime, misconduct, wrongdoing, fault  
2See also:juvenile delinquency 
3 N-UNCOUNT [also NOUN in plural] 逾期未还债务（或未缴税款） Delinquency is failure to pay a debt or tax. 逾期未还债务（或未缴税款） [US, technical]

  • Not all delinquencies lead to foreclosure.

    并不是所有未按时还债的情形都会导致丧失抵押品的赎回权。

  • ...increases in mortgage delinquency rates.

    抵押贷款拖欠率的上升
