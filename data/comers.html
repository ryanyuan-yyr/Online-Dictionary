comer
kʌməʳ ˈkʌmər
Word forms: plural comers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 到达者;到来的人 You can use comers to refer to people who arrive at a particular place. 到达者;到来的人

  • I arrived at the church at two-thirty p.m. to find some early comers outside the main door.

    我下午两点半到达教堂，发现大门外面有一些人已经到了。

  • The first comer was the Sultan himself.

    第一个来的人是苏丹自己。
