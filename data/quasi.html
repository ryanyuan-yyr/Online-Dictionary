quasi
ˈkweɪzaɪ ˈkwɑːzɪ ˈkweɪˌzaɪ ˈkweɪˌsaɪ ˈkwɑzi ˈkwɑsi
COMB in ADJ (构成形容词和名词)表示“类似的”，“准”，“半” Quasi- is used to form adjectives and nouns that describe something as being in many ways like something else, without actually being that thing. (构成形容词和名词)表示“类似的”，“准”，“半”

  • The flame is a quasi-religious emblem of immortality.

    火焰可以算作不朽的宗教象征。

  • ...a few key quasi-governmental institutions.

    几个重要的准政府机构
