root for
PHRASAL VERB 支持;为…鼓劲 If you are rooting for someone, you are giving them your support while they are doing something difficult or trying to defeat another person. 支持;为…鼓劲 [informal]

  • Good luck, we'll be rooting for you. [VERB PREP. noun]

    祝你好运，我们会支持你的。

  • It's one of those movies in which you're forced to root for the villain. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这是那种让人不得不为坏人叫好的电影。

SYN support, back, encourage, defend
