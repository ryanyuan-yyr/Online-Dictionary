ring round
(also ring around)
PHRASAL VERB (为组织活动或询问信息)打一圈电话，四处打电话 If you ring round or ring around, you phone several people, usually when you are trying to organize something or to find some information. (为组织活动或询问信息)打一圈电话，四处打电话 [mainly British]

  • She'd ring around and get back to me. [VERB PREP.]

    她会打上一圈电话，最后又来找我。

  • She immediately started ringing round her friends and relatives. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她马上开始给亲戚朋友们打电话。
