accuser
əkjuːzəʳ
Word forms: plural accusers
N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 指责者；（尤指）控告者 An accuser is a person who says that another person has done something wrong, especially that he or she has committed a crime. 指责者；（尤指）控告者
