bodily
bɒdɪli ˈbɑdəli
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 身体的；肉体的 Your bodily needs and functions are the needs and functions of your body. 身体的；肉体的

  • ...descriptions of natural bodily functions.

    对人体自然生理功能的描述

  • There's more to eating than just bodily needs.

    饮食不仅仅是出于身体的需要。

SYN physical, material, actual, substantial [formal]  
2See also:grievous bodily harm 
3 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 整个身体地；全身地 You use bodily to indicate that an action involves the whole of someone's body. 整个身体地；全身地

  • I was hurled bodily to the deck.

    我整个人都被摔到了甲板上。

SYN physically, completely, entirely, wholly
