class-conscious
ADJ-GRADED 阶级意识强烈的;有强烈社会阶层意识的 Someone who is class-conscious is very aware of the differences between the various classes of people in society, and often has a strong feeling of belonging to a particular class. 阶级意识强烈的;有强烈社会阶层意识的

  • Nineteenth-century Britain was a class-conscious society.

    19世纪的英国是个阶级意识强烈的社会。

class-consciousness

  • There was very little snobbery or class-consciousness in the wartime navy.

    战时的海军中很少有势利眼，阶级意识也不强。
