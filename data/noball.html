no-ball
Word forms: plural no-balls
N-COUNT (板球运动中的)犯规投球 In cricket, a no-ball is a ball that is bowled in a way that is not allowed by the rules. It results in an extra run being given to the side that is batting. (板球运动中的)犯规投球

  • In the nine matches up to the final, England bowled 48 wides and 29 no-balls.

    在打入决赛前的9场比赛中，英格兰队总计投出48个歪球，29个废球。
