put up for
PHRASAL VERB 提供…供销售（或拍卖等） If you put something up for sale or auction, for example, you make it available to be sold or auctioned. 提供…供销售（或拍卖等）

  • The company should put its claims up for review by an arbitrator. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    公司应该将其要求交由仲裁者审核。

  • The old flower and fruit market has been put up for sale. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    旧的花草水果市场已开始求售了。

  • She put up her daughter for adoption in 1967. [V P n P n]

    1967年她为女儿征领养家庭。
