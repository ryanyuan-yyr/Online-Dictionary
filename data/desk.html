desk
desk dɛsk
Word forms: plural desks
1 N-COUNT 书桌;写字台;办公桌 A desk is a table, often with drawers, which you sit at to write or work. 书桌;写字台;办公桌
2 N-SING [oft n NOUN] (宾馆、医院、机场等的)服务台，问询台 The place in a hotel, hospital, airport, or other building where you check in or obtain information is referred to as a particular desk . (宾馆、医院、机场等的)服务台，问询台

  • I spoke to the girl on the reception desk.

    我跟服务台的女孩说了一下。

  • A map and a bird-watchers' field checklist are available at the front desk.

    前台有地图和观鸟地点一览表可供拿取。

  • ...the main information desk.

    主问询台

3 N-SING [oft n NOUN] (广播公司、报社、杂志社等的)部，室，组 A particular department of a broadcasting company, or of a newspaper or magazine company, can be referred to as a particular desk . (广播公司、报社、杂志社等的)部，室，组

  • Let our news desk know as quickly as possible on 414 3926.

    请尽快拨打电话 4143926 通知我们的新闻部。

  • Over now to Simon Ingram at the sports desk.

    现在交给体育组的西蒙·英格拉姆。
