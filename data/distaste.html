distaste
dɪsteɪst dɪsˈteɪst dɪsˈteɪst ˈdɪsˌteɪst
N-UNCOUNT 不喜欢;反感;厌恶 If you feel distaste for someone or something, you dislike them and consider them to be unpleasant, disgusting, or immoral. 不喜欢;反感;厌恶

  • Roger looked at her with distaste.

    罗杰厌恶地看着她。

  • He professed a distaste for everything related to money.

    他声称厌恶与金钱有关的一切。

SYN dislike, horror, disgust, loathing
