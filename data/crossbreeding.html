cross-breed
(also crossbreed)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cross-breeds, present participle cross-breeding, past tense , past participle cross-bred
1 V-RECIP-ERG (使)杂交;杂交繁育 If one species of animal or plant cross-breeds with another, they reproduce, and new or different animals or plants are produced. You can also say that someone cross-breeds something such as an animal or plant. (使)杂交;杂交繁育 [Also VERB]

  • By cross-breeding with our native red deer, the sika deer have affected the gene pool. [VERB + with]

    通过与我们本地的赤鹿杂交，梅花鹿的基因库已经发生了改变。

  • Unfortunately attempts to crossbreed it with other potatoes have been unsuccessful. [VERB noun + with]

    不幸的是，将它与其他土豆品种杂交的尝试均告失败。

  • Dr Russel is creating an elite herd by cross-breeding goats from around the globe. [VERB noun]

    拉塞尔博士正通过把全球各地的山羊品种进行杂交来培育一个优良种群。

  • ...a cross-bred labrador. [VERB-ed]

    混种拉布拉多猎犬

cross-breeding

  • ...centuries of cross-breeding.

    几个世纪的杂交培育

2 N-COUNT (动物的)杂交品种 A cross-breed is an animal that is the result of cross-breeding. (动物的)杂交品种
