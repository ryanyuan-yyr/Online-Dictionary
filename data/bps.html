bps
biː piːes
比特/秒（计算机数据传输速度的计量单位） bps is a measurement of the speed at which computer data is transferred, for example by a modem. bps is an abbreviation for 'bits per second'. 比特/秒（计算机数据传输速度的计量单位）

  • A minimum 28,800 bps modem is probably the slowest you'll want to put up with.

    传输速度为 28,800 比特/秒的调制解调器很可能是你愿意忍受的最低速度的解调器。
