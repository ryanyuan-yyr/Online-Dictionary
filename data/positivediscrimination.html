positive discrimination
N-UNCOUNT （对女性、少数种族和残疾人等的）积极区别对待，积极差别待遇 Positive discrimination means making sure that people such as women, members of smaller racial groups, and disabled people get a fair share of the opportunities available. （对女性、少数种族和残疾人等的）积极区别对待，积极差别待遇 [British]

  • Mr Singh wanted to reserve places for low-caste Indians within the country's public sector, as a form of positive discrimination.

    辛格先生想在国家公共部门为下层印度人保留职位，这是一种积极的区别对待。
