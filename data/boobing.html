boob
buːb bub
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense boobs, present participle boobing, past tense , past participle boobed
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] （女性的）乳房，奶子 A woman's boobs are her breasts. （女性的）乳房，奶子 [informal, rude]
2 VERB 犯错误 If you boob, you make a mistake. 犯错误 [British, informal]

  • Is their timing right, or have they boobed again? [VERB]

    他们的时间掐得准吗？是不是他们又弄错了？

Boob is also a noun.

  • The government once again has made a big boob.

    政府又一次犯了大错。
