tune out
PHRASAL VERB 走神;开小差 If you tune out, you stop listening or paying attention to what is being said. 走神;开小差 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Whatever you're talking about, children rapidly tune out if you go beyond them… [VERB PREP.]

    不管你在说什么，如果孩子们听不懂，就会很快走神。

  • Rose heard the familiar voice, but tuned out the words. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    罗斯听到了熟悉的声音，但没注意听说的是什么。
