shape up
1 PHRASAL VERB 开始成形;有可能发生 If something is shaping up, it is starting to develop or seems likely to happen. 开始成形;有可能发生 [Also V P + into]

  • There are also indications that a major tank battle may be shaping up for tonight. [VERB PREP.]

    也有迹象表明今晚可能会发生大规模的坦克战。

  • The accident is already shaping up as a significant environmental disaster. [VERB PREP. + as]

    这次事故已经造成严重的环境灾难。

  • It's shaping up to be a terrible winter. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    这将会是一个极其寒冷的冬天。

2 PHRASAL VERB (人)表现(如何);(事物）进展(情况) If you ask how someone or something is shaping up, you want to know how well they are doing in a particular situation or activity. (人)表现(如何);(事物）进展(情况)

  • I did have a few worries about how Hugh and I would shape up as parents. [VERB PREP. + as]

    我对休和我将如何为人父母确实有些忧虑。

  • Girls are being recruited now. I heard they are shaping up very well. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    现在开始招女兵了，我听说她们表现很好。

3 PHRASAL VERB 端正行为;好自为之;好好干 If you tell someone to shape up, you are telling them to start behaving in a sensible and responsible way. 端正行为;好自为之;好好干

  • It is no use simply to tell adolescents to shape up and do something useful. [VERB PREP.]

    仅仅告诉青少年要品行端正，做有意义的事，是没有用的。
