pacifist
pæsɪfɪst
Word forms: plural pacifists
1 N-COUNT 和平主义者；反战主义者 A pacifist is someone who believes that violence is wrong and refuses to take part in wars. 和平主义者；反战主义者
SYN peace lover, dove, conscientious objector, peacenik [informal]  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 和平主义的；反战主义的 If someone has pacifist views, they believe that war and violence are always wrong. 和平主义的；反战主义的
