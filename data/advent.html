advent
ædvent ˈædvɛnt
1 N-UNCOUNT （重大事件、发明或情况的）出现，问世，到来 The advent of an important event, invention, or situation is the fact of it starting or coming into existence. （重大事件、发明或情况的）出现，问世，到来 [formal]

  • ...the leap forward in communication made possible by the advent of the mobile phone. [+ of]

    手机的问世带来了通讯业的迅猛发展

  • The advent of war led to a greater austerity.

    战争的到来使形势更为严峻。

SYN coming, approach, appearance, arrival  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually the NOUN of n] （人物的）到达，到来 The advent of a person at a place is their arrival there. （人物的）到达，到来 [literary]

  • Deptford had come alive with the advent of the new priest at St Paul's.

    圣保罗教堂新牧师的到来使德特福德变得生机勃勃。
