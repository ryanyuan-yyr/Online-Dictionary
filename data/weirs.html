weir
wɪəʳ wɪə wɪr
Word forms: plural weirs
1 N-COUNT 堰;拦河坝 A weir is a low barrier which is built across a river in order to control or direct the flow of water. 堰;拦河坝
2 N-COUNT （置于河中捕鱼的）鱼梁，拦鱼栅 A weir is a wooden fence which is built across a stream in order to create a pool for catching fish. （置于河中捕鱼的）鱼梁，拦鱼栅
