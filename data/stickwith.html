stick with
1 PHRASAL VERB 坚持(做…) If you stick with something, you do not change to something else. 坚持(做…)

  • If you're in a job that keeps you busy, stick with it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果你所做的工作能让你很忙，那要坚持下去。

  • They prefer, in the end, to stick with what they know. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们最终选择了继续做自己熟悉的事。

2 PHRASAL VERB 紧跟;紧随;和…呆在一起 If you stick with someone, you stay close to them. 紧跟;紧随;和…呆在一起

  • Tugging the woman's arm, she pulled her to her side saying: 'You just stick with me, dear.' [VERB PREP. noun]

    她拽着那个女人的胳膊，将她拉到自己身边说：“亲爱的，你就跟我呆在一起。”
