transact
trænzækt trænˈzækt trænˈsækt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense transacts, present participle transacting, past tense , past participle transacted
VERB 办理，处理(生意、交易等) If you transact business, you enter into a deal with someone, for example by buying or selling something. 办理，处理(生意、交易等) [formal]

  • This would free them to transact business across state lines. [VERB noun]

    这将使他们能够跨越州界进行交易。

  • Mr. Harrison reckons half of his business could be transacted by computer. [VERB noun]

    哈里森先生估计他有一半生意可以用计算机处理。

SYN carry out, handle, conduct, do
