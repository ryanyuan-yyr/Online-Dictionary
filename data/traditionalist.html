traditionalist
trədɪʃənəlɪst
Word forms: plural traditionalists
1 N-COUNT 传统主义者 A traditionalist is a person who supports the established customs and beliefs of his or her society or group, and does not want to change them. 传统主义者
2 ADJ 传统主义的;因循守旧的 A traditionalist idea, argument, or organization supports the established customs and beliefs of a society or group, rather than modern ones. 传统主义的;因循守旧的
