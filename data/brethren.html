brethren
breðrɪn ˈbrɛðrən ˈbrɛðərn
N-PLURAL [oft with poss] （某组织或团体的）成员，会员；（尤指宗教团体的）教友 You can refer to the members of a particular organization or group, especially a religious group, as brethren. （某组织或团体的）成员，会员；（尤指宗教团体的）教友 [old-fashioned]

  • We must help our brethren, it is our duty.

    我们必须帮助我们的教友，这是我们的责任。

  • Sri Lankans share a common ancestry with their Indian brethren.

    斯里兰卡人和他们的印度兄弟有共同的祖先。
