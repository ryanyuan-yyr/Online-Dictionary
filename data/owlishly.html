owlish
aʊlɪʃ ˈaʊlɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （尤指戴眼镜的人）猫头鹰似的，看似严肃而又聪慧的 An owlish person looks rather like an owl, especially because they wear glasses, and seems to be very serious and clever. （尤指戴眼镜的人）猫头鹰似的，看似严肃而又聪慧的

  • With his owlish face, it is easy to understand why he was called 'The Professor'.

    看看那张严肃而又睿智的猫头鹰脸，就不难理解他为什么被称为“教授”了。
