strung out
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 一条线地伸展开来的;一字排开的 If things are strung out somewhere, they are spread out in a line. 一条线地伸展开来的;一字排开的

  • Buildings were strung out on the north side of the river.

    河的北岸是一排大楼。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] (吸毒后)神志恍惚的 If someone is strung out on drugs, they are heavily affected by drugs. (吸毒后)神志恍惚的 [informal]

  • He was permanently strung out on heroin. [+ on]

    吸食海洛因令他整日神志不清。
