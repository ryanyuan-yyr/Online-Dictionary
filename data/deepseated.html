deep-seated ˈdipˌsitɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 根深蒂固的;难以改变的 A deep-seated problem, feeling, or belief is difficult to change because its causes have been there for a long time. 根深蒂固的;难以改变的

  • The country is still suffering from deep-seated economic problems.

    该国仍然受到根深蒂固的经济问题的困扰。

  • ...our morbid and deep-seated fear of death.

    我们对死亡近乎病态而且根深蒂固的恐惧
