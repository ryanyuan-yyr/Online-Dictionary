jib
dʒɪb dʒɪb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense jibs, present participle jibbing, past tense , past participle jibbed
1 N-COUNT 艏三角帆；主帆前的小帆 The jib is the small triangular sail that is sometimes used at the front of a sailing boat. 艏三角帆；主帆前的小帆
2 VERB 不愿做；不肯接受 If you jib at something, you are unwilling to do it or to accept it. 不愿做；不肯接受 [old-fashioned] [Also VERB]

  • ...those who jib at the idea of selling their land. [VERB + at]

    那些拒绝出让土地的人
