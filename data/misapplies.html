misapply
mɪsəplaɪ ˌmɪsəˈplaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misapplies, present participle misapplying, past tense , past participle misapplied
VERB [usually passive] 误用;滥用 If something is misapplied, it is used for a purpose for which it is not intended or not suitable. 误用;滥用

  • Many lines from Shakespeare's plays are misquoted and misapplied. [be VERB-ed]

    莎士比亚戏剧中的许多诗句被人误引误用。

  • The law had been misapplied. [be VERB-ed]

    法律遭到了滥用。

SYN misuse, abuse, pervert, misappropriate
