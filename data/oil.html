oil
ɔɪl ɔɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense oils, present participle oiling, past tense , past participle oiled
1 N-MASS 石油；燃料油；润滑油 Oil is a smooth, thick liquid that is used as a fuel and for making the parts of machines move smoothly. Oil is found underground. 石油；燃料油；润滑油

  • The company buys and sells about 600,000 barrels of oil a day.

    该公司每天买卖约60万桶石油。

  • ...the rapid rise in prices for oil and petrol.

    石油与汽油价格的急剧上涨

  • ...a small oil lamp.

    一盏小油灯

2 VERB 给…加润滑油；给…上油 If you oil something, you put oil onto or into it, for example to make it work smoothly or to protect it. 给…加润滑油；给…上油

  • A crew of assistants oiled and adjusted the release mechanism until it worked perfectly. [VERB noun]

    一班助手给排放装置加润滑油并作调试，直到其达至理想工作状态。

  • The leather may need to be oiled every two to three weeks in order to retain its suppleness. [VERB noun]

    为了保持皮革的柔韧性，可能两三周就要上一次油。

SYN lubricate, grease, make slippery  
3 N-MASS [usually n NOUN] 食用油；植物油 Oil is a smooth, thick liquid made from plants and is often used for cooking. 食用油；植物油

  • Combine the beans, chopped mint and olive oil in a large bowl.

    将菜豆、切碎的薄荷和橄榄油放在大碗里搅拌。

  • Drop the slices into the oil and fry until golden brown.

    将切片倒进油里，煎至金棕色。

4 N-MASS 润肤油；沐浴油 Oil is a smooth, thick liquid, often with a pleasant smell, that you rub into your skin or add to your bath. 润肤油；沐浴油

  • Try a hot bath with some relaxing bath oil.

    用舒缓沐浴油洗个热水澡吧。

  • My sister smeared herself with suntan oil and slept by the swimming pool all day.

    我姐姐抹了些防晒油，一整天睡在游泳池边。

5 N-COUNT [usually plural] 同 oil painting Oils are oil paintings . 同 oil painting

  • Her colourful oils and works on paper have a naive, dreamlike quality.

    她那些色彩斑斓的油画与纸上作品给人一种天真、梦幻般的感觉。

6 N-PLURAL 油彩；油画颜料 When an artist paints in oils, he or she uses oil paints. 油彩；油画颜料

  • When she paints in oils she always uses the same range of colours.

    她创作油画时总是使用同一组色彩。

7 See also: castor oil  crude oil  olive oil 
8 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 平息风波；调停争端 If you pour oil on troubled waters, you try to calm down a difficult situation. 平息风波；调停争端
9 PHRASE 使…顺利进行；使…正常运作 If someone or something oils the wheels of a process or system, they help things to run smoothly and successfully. 使…顺利进行；使…正常运作

  • On all such occasions, the king stands in the wings, oiling the wheels of diplomacy.

    在所有这样的场合，国王总是身处幕后确保外交活动的顺利进行。
