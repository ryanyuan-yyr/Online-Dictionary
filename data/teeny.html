teeny
tiːni ˈtini
Word forms: comparative teenier, superlative teeniest
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 很小的;极小的 If you describe something as teeny, you are emphasizing that it is very small. 很小的;极小的 [informal, emphasis]

  • ...little teeny bugs.

    丁点儿大的虫子

  • Sue never carried anything other than the teeniest purse.

    休从来都只带着一只小钱包。

SYN tiny, minute, wee, miniature
