dessertspoon
dɪzɜːʳtspuːn dɪˈzɜrtˌspun
(also dessert spoon)Word forms: plural dessertspoons
1 N-COUNT 点心匙;中型匙 A dessertspoon is a spoon which is midway between the size of a teaspoon and a tablespoon. You use it to eat desserts. 点心匙;中型匙
2 N-COUNT 一点心匙的量;一中型匙的量 You can refer to an amount of food resting on a dessertspoon as a dessertspoon of food. 一点心匙的量;一中型匙的量 [British]

  • ...a rounded dessertspoon of flour. [+ of]
