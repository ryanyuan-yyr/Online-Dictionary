persnickety
pəʳsnɪkɪti pəˈsnɪkɪtɪ pərˈsnɪkəti
ADJ-GRADED 挑剔的；吹毛求疵的 If you describe someone as persnickety, you think that they pay too much attention to small, unimportant details. 挑剔的；吹毛求疵的 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • He is a very rigorous man, very persnickety.

    他是个很严厉的人，非常挑剔。
