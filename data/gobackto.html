go back to
1 PHRASAL VERB 恢复；重新开始；重操（旧业） If you go back to a task or activity, you start doing it again after you have stopped doing it for a period of time. 恢复；重新开始；重操（旧业）

  • I now look forward to going back to work as soon as possible. [V P P n/v-ing]

    我现在渴望尽早回到工作岗位。

  • Amy went back to studying. [V P P n/v-ing]

    埃米重拾学业。

2 PHRASAL VERB 回到（原来的话题等） If you go back to a particular point in a lecture, discussion, or book, you start to discuss it. 回到（原来的话题等）

  • Let me just go back to the point I was making. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    请允许我再回到我刚才所提的观点。
