borderland
bɔːʳdəʳlænd ˈbɔrdərˌlænd
Word forms: plural borderlands
1 N-SING （介乎两种事物之间的）边缘地带，所属不清的状态 The borderland between two things is an area which contains features from both of these things so that it is not possible to say that it belongs to one or the other. （介乎两种事物之间的）边缘地带，所属不清的状态

  • ...on the borderland between sleep and waking.

    处于半睡半醒之间

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 边疆；边境地区 The area of land close to the border between two countries or major areas can be called the borderlands . 边疆；边境地区

  • ...Lebanon's southern borderlands.

    黎巴嫩的南部边境地区
