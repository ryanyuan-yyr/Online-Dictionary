faux pas
foʊpɑː ˌfəʊ ˈpɑː fo pɑ foʊˈpɑ
Word forms: plural faux pas
N-COUNT 失态;有失检点;失礼 A faux pas is a socially embarrassing action or mistake. 失态;有失检点;失礼 [formal]

  • It was not long before I realised the enormity of my faux pas.

    没过多久，我就意识到自己有多么失态。

SYN gaffe, blunder, indiscretion, impropriety
