heritage
herɪtɪdʒ ˈhɛrətɪdʒ
Word forms: plural heritages
N-VAR [oft poss NOUN] （国家的）遗产，传统 A country's heritage is all the qualities, traditions, or features of life there that have continued over many years and have been passed on from one generation to another. （国家的）遗产，传统

  • The historic building is as much part of our heritage as the paintings.

    这座历史建筑和这些画一样，都是留给我们的文化遗产的一部分。

  • ...the rich heritage of Russian folk music.

    俄罗斯民间音乐的丰富遗产

SYN inheritance, legacy, birthright, lot
