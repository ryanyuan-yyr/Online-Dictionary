go about
1 PHRASAL VERB 着手干；做；处理 The way you go about a task or problem is the way you approach it and deal with it. 着手干；做；处理

  • I want him back, but I just don't know how to go about it. [VERB PREP. noun/verb-ing]

    我想让他回来，但就是不知道该怎么做才好。

SYN tackle, begin, approach, undertake  
2 PHRASAL VERB 从事，进行（日常的活动） When you are going about your normal activities, you are doing them. 从事，进行（日常的活动）

  • We were simply going about our business when we were pounced upon by these police officers. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们像往常一样正干得好好的，却遭到这些警察的突然袭击。

SYN engage in, perform, conduct, pursue  
3 PHRASAL VERB （经常）穿…；（经常）表现得… If you go about in a particular way, you behave or dress in that way, often as part of your normal life. （经常）穿…；（经常）表现得…

  • He used to go about in a black cape. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    他以前常穿一件黑披风。

  • He went about looking ill and unhappy. [VERB PREP. verb-ing]

    他看上去总是一副病恹恹、不开心的样子。
