disingenuous
dɪsɪndʒenjuəs ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs
ADJ-GRADED 不诚恳的;不坦率的;狡猾的 Someone who is disingenuous is slightly dishonest and insincere in what they say. 不诚恳的;不坦率的;狡猾的 [formal]

  • It would be disingenuous to claim that this is great art.

    称这是艺术瑰宝是言不由衷。

SYN dishonest, cunning, sly, designing  
disingenuously

  • He disingenuously remarked that he knew nothing about strategy.

    他假惺惺地称自己对战术一窍不通。
