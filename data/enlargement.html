enlargement
ɪnlɑːʳdʒmənt ɛnˈlɑrdʒmənt ɪnˈlɑrdʒmənt
Word forms: plural enlargements
1 N-UNCOUNT 放大;扩大 The enlargement of something is the process or result of making it bigger. 放大;扩大

  • There is insufficient space for enlargement of the buildings. [+ of]

    没有足够的空间扩建这些建筑。

  • ...the Community's enlargement.

    共同体的扩大

SYN expansion, increase, development, growth  
2 N-COUNT 扩印的照片;放大的照片 An enlargement is a photograph that has been made bigger. 扩印的照片;放大的照片
