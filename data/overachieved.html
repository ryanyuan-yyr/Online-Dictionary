overachieve
oʊvərətʃiːv ˌoʊvərəˈtʃiv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overachieves, present participle overachieving, past tense , past participle overachieved
VERB （在学业或工作上）过于强求成功，过于争强好胜 If someone overachieves in something such as school work or a job, they work very hard, especially in a way that makes them tired or unhappy. They want to be successful because it is very important to them to do well and not because they enjoy what they are doing. （在学业或工作上）过于强求成功，过于争强好胜

  • ...emotions such as guilt, compulsion to please or overachieve, or depression. [VERB]

    诸如愧疚、沮丧、总想取悦于人和凡事争强好胜等种种情绪

overachiever

    他来自一个家里人个个一味追求成功的家庭。
