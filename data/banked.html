banked
bæŋkt bæŋkt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （路段）一边高出的 A banked stretch of road is higher on one side than the other. （路段）一边高出的

  • He struggled to hold the bike down on the banked corners.

    拐过斜弯时他竭力将自行车把稳。

  • The car took off from the banked track and went through a fence.

    车子冲出倾斜的赛道，穿过了一道护栏。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 堆积的；高高积起的 If a place is banked with something, it is piled high with that thing. If something is banked up, it is piled high. 堆积的；高高积起的

  • Flowerbeds and tubs are banked with summer bedding plants.

    花坛和花盆里种满了夏令花草。

  • The snow was banked up along the roadside.

    路边堆积着厚厚的雪。
