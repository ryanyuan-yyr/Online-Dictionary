delimit
dɪlɪmɪt diˈlɪmɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense delimits, present participle delimiting, past tense , past participle delimited
VERB 确定…的界限;限定;划定…的边界 If you delimit something, you fix or establish its limits. 确定…的界限;限定;划定…的边界 [formal]

  • This is not meant to delimit what approaches social researchers can adopt. [VERB noun]

    这并不是为了限制社会研究人员所能采用的方法。

SYN define, mark (out), determine, fix
