fastball
fæstbɔl ˈfɑːstˌbɔːl ˈfæstˌbɑl ˈfɑstˌbɑl
Word forms: fastballs
N-COUNT (棒球中的)快球In baseball, a fastball is a ball that is pitched very fast. (棒球中的)快球

  • Soriano hit Peavy's first pitch, a 90-mph fastball, down the left-field line.

    索里亚诺击中皮威的第一投，这个快球时速90英里，落在了左边线上。
