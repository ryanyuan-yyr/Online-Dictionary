brew
bruː bru
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense brews, present participle brewing, past tense , past participle brewed
1 VERB 沏（茶）；冲（咖啡） If you brew tea or coffee, you make it by pouring hot water over tea leaves or ground coffee. 沏（茶）；冲（咖啡）

  • I'll get Venner to brew some tea. [VERB noun]

    我会让文纳沏些茶。

  • He brewed a pot of coffee. [VERB noun]

    他冲了一壶咖啡。

SYN boil, make, soak, steep  
2 N-COUNT （某种）茶，咖啡；一壶茶（或咖啡） A brew is a particular kind of tea or coffee. It can also be a particular pot of tea or coffee. （某种）茶，咖啡；一壶茶（或咖啡）

  • She swallowed a mouthful of the hot strong brew, and wiped her eyes.

    她喝了一口热浓茶，又擦了擦眼睛。

  • ...a mild herbal brew.

    清淡的药草茶

SYN drink, preparation, mixture, blend  
3 VERB 酿（啤酒） If a person or company brews beer, they make it. 酿（啤酒）

  • I brew my own beer. [VERB noun]

    我自己酿制啤酒。

  • The beer is brewed at the Charles Wells Brewery. [VERB noun]

    这种啤酒是查尔斯·威尔斯啤酒厂生产的。

SYN make, ferment, prepare by fermentation  
brewing

  • ...the brewing of home-made alcohol.

    自制酒的酿造

4 N-COUNT [usually with supplement] （某种或某地某一时间生产的）啤酒 A brew is a particular kind of beer or beer that is produced in a particular place at a particular time. （某种或某地某一时间生产的）啤酒

  • Britons still consume more than 29 million pints a day, ranging over 1,000 or more different brews.

    英国人每天仍然要喝掉2,900多万品脱、1,000多种不同牌子的啤酒。

  • ...low-alcohol brews.

    低酒精度啤酒

5See also:home-brew 
6 VERB [usually cont] （风暴）酝酿 If a storm is brewing, large clouds are beginning to form and the sky is becoming dark because there is going to be a storm. （风暴）酝酿

  • We'd seen the storm brewing when we were out in the boat. [VERB]

    我们在外面划船的时候就发现风暴将至。

SYN develop, form, gather, loom  
7 VERB [usually cont] （困境或危机）酝酿，形成 If an unpleasant or difficult situation is brewing, it is starting to develop. （困境或危机）酝酿，形成

  • At home a crisis was brewing. [VERB]

    家中一场危机正在酝酿。

  • There's trouble brewing. [VERB]

    要有麻烦了。

SYN start, develop, gather, loom  
8 N-COUNT [usually singular] 混合物 A brew of several things is a mixture of those things. 混合物

  • Most cities generate a complex brew of pollutants. [+ of]

    多数城市都会产生各种物质混杂的污染物。

  • ...a potent brew of smooth salesmanship and amateur psychiatry. [+ of]

    熟练的推销术与业余攻心术的有效结合

SYN blend, compound, fusion, concoction
