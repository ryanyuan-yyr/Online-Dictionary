relativist
relətɪvɪst
Word forms: plural relativists
ADJ 相对论的;相对主义的 A relativist position or argument is one according to which the truth is not always the same, but varies according to circumstances. 相对论的;相对主义的

  • Bonger advocated a relativist position. In his view, what is considered immoral depends on the social structure.

    邦格提倡一种相对论的观点。在他看来，是不是不道德取决于社会结构。

A relativist is someone with relativist views.相对论者;相对主义者
