lose out
PHRASAL VERB 失败；受损；吃亏 If you lose out, you suffer a loss or disadvantage because you have not succeeded in what you were doing. 失败；受损；吃亏

  • We both lost out. [VERB PREP.]

    我们俩都输了。

  • Laura lost out to Tom. [VERB PREP. + to]

    劳拉输给了汤姆。

  • Women have lost out in this new pay flexibility. [V P + in]

    在这一新的薪资弹性化政策中吃亏的是女性。

  • Egypt has lost out on revenues from the Suez Canal. [VERB PREP. + on]

    埃及在苏伊士运河上的收益上受了损。

SYN miss out on, be defeated, be unsuccessful
