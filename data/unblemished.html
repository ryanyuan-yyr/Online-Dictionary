unblemished
ʌnblemɪʃt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 没有污点的；无瑕疵的；清白的 If you describe something such as someone's record, reputation, or character as unblemished, you mean it has not been harmed or spoiled. 没有污点的；无瑕疵的；清白的

  • Ghana does not have an unblemished record on human rights.

    加纳在人权方面并非毫无瑕疵。

  • ...Lee's unblemished reputation as a man of honor and principle.

    李为人诚实、坚持原则的清白名誉

SYN spotless, immaculate, impeccable, perfect  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无斑痕的；无瑕疵的；完好无损的 If you describe something as unblemished, you mean that it has no marks or holes on its surface. 无斑痕的；无瑕疵的；完好无损的

  • Be sure to select firm, unblemished fruit.

    一定要选坚实、无疤痕的果子。

  • ...an open field of unblemished snow.

    皑皑白雪覆盖着的一片旷野

SYN perfect, flawless, spotless, unstained
