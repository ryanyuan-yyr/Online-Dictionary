thrift
θrɪft θrɪft
Word forms: plural thrifts
1 N-UNCOUNT 节俭;节省;节约 Thrift is the quality and practice of being careful with money and not wasting things. 节俭;节省;节约 [approval]

  • They were rightly praised for their thrift and enterprise.

    他们勤俭节约，积极进取，得到了当之无愧的赞扬。

SYN economy, prudence, frugality, saving  
2 N-COUNT 储蓄银行 A thrift or a thrift institution is a kind of savings bank. 储蓄银行 [US, business]
