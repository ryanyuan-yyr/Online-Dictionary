self-made
ˈsɛlfˈmeɪd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 靠自己奋斗成功的;(尤指)白手起家的 Self-made is used to describe people who have become successful and rich through their own efforts, especially if they started life without money, education, or high social status. 靠自己奋斗成功的;(尤指)白手起家的

  • He is a self-made man.

    他白手起家。

  • ...a self-made millionaire.

    一个靠双手打拼的百万富翁
