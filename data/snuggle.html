snuggle
snʌgəl ˈsnʌgəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense snuggles, present participle snuggling, past tense , past participle snuggled
VERB (尤指靠近另一人)依偎，舒适地蜷卧 If you snuggle somewhere, you settle yourself into a warm, comfortable position, especially by moving closer to another person. (尤指靠近另一人)依偎，舒适地蜷卧

  • Jane snuggled up against his shoulder. [VERB adv./prep.]

    简依偎着他的肩膀。

  • I snuggled down in the big, comfortable seat. [VERB adv./prep.]

    我蜷缩在舒适的大座位里。

SYN nestle, curl up, cuddle up, huddle
