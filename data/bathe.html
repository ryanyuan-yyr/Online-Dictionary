bathe
beɪð beɪð
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bathes, present participle bathing, past tense , past participle bathed
1 VERB （在海、河、湖中）游泳，嬉水 If you bathe in a sea, river, or lake, you swim, play, or wash yourself in it. Birds and animals can also bathe. （在海、河、湖中）游泳，嬉水 [mainly British, formal] [Also VERB]

  • The police have warned the city's inhabitants not to bathe in the polluted river. [VERB prep./adv.]

    警方已经告诫该市居民不要在这条被污染的河里游泳。

  • ...small ponds for the birds to bathe in. [VERB prep./adv.]

    可供禽鸟嬉戏的小池塘

SYN swim, dip, go swimming, take a dip  
Bathe is also a noun.

  • Fifty soldiers were taking an early morning bathe in a nearby lake.

    清晨，50名士兵正在附近的湖里游泳。

bathing

  • Nude bathing is not allowed.

    禁止裸泳。

  • ...Britain's 440 designated bathing beaches.

    英国440处指定可以游泳的海滩

2 VERB （在浴缸中）洗澡，沐浴 When you bathe, you have a bath. （在浴缸中）洗澡，沐浴 [US, also British, formal]

  • At least 60% of us now bathe or shower once a day. [VERB]

    现在我们当中至少60%的人每天淋浴或洗澡一次。

3 VERB 给（小孩）洗澡 If you bathe someone, especially a child, you wash them in a bath. 给（小孩）洗澡 [US, also British, formal]

  • Back home, Shirley plays with, feeds and bathes the baby. [VERB noun]

    回到家里，雪莉逗宝宝玩耍，喂他吃饭，给他洗澡。

SYN wash, clean, bath, soap  
4 VERB （用液体）轻轻地清洗（某身体部位或伤口）；将（某身体部位或伤口）浸泡（在液体中） If you bathe a part of your body or a wound, you wash it gently or soak it in a liquid. （用液体）轻轻地清洗（某身体部位或伤口）；将（某身体部位或伤口）浸泡（在液体中）

  • Bathe the infected area in a salt solution. [VERB noun]

    用盐水清洗感染的部位。

  • She paused long enough to bathe her blistered feet. [VERB noun]

    她把双脚长时间泡在水中，以充分浸泡长满水泡的脚。

SYN cleanse, clean, wash, soak  
5 VERB （光线）洒满，覆盖；使沐浴在（光线中） If a place is bathed in light, it is covered with light, especially a gentle, pleasant light. （光线）洒满，覆盖；使沐浴在（光线中） [Also VERB n]

  • The arena was bathed in warm sunshine. [be VERB-ed + in]

    竞技场沐浴在温暖的阳光中。

  • I was led to a small room bathed in soft red light. [VERB-ed]

    我被带到了一间笼罩在柔和红光中的小屋里。

  • The lamp behind him seems to bathe him in warmth. [VERB noun + in]

    他身后的灯光似乎将他笼罩在一片温暖之中。

SYN cover, flood, steep, engulf
