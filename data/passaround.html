pass around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 pass round
（一群人）传，传递 If a group of people pass something around or pass it round, they each take it and then give it to the next person. （一群人）传，传递

  • Serve the pudding, and pass around a bowl of yogurt to go with it. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    上布丁，把酸奶碗传一下，用酸奶拌着布丁吃。

  • Just pass this round as I'm talking and take a little look at it. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我讲的时候大家把这个传一下，稍微看一眼。

  • A bottle of whisky was passed around. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    传着喝一瓶威士忌。
