burr
bɜːʳ bɜr
Word forms: plural burrs
The spelling bur is also used for meaning 1. 拼写bur亦用于义项1。
1 N-COUNT （某些植物）带芒刺的小果实 A burr is the part of some plants which contains seeds and which has little hooks on the outside so that it sticks to clothes or fur. （某些植物）带芒刺的小果实
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （英语方言中的）颤动小舌的r音，粗喉音 If someone has a burr, they speak English with a regional accent in which 'r' sounds are pronounced more strongly than in the standard British way of speaking. （英语方言中的）颤动小舌的r音，粗喉音

  • ...a warm West Country burr.

    一种热情的英格兰西南部小舌颤音
