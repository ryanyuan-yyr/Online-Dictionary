prowess
praʊɪs ˈpraʊɪs
N-UNCOUNT 深厚的造诣；高超的技艺 Someone's prowess is their great skill at doing something. 深厚的造诣；高超的技艺 [formal]

  • He's always bragging about his prowess as a cricketer.

    他总是吹嘘自己板球水平高超。

  • The best and the brightest pupils competed to demonstrate their intellectual prowess.

    最出色、最聪明的学生们竞相展示他们的才智。

SYN skill, ability, talent, expertise
