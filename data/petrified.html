petrified
petrɪfaɪd ˈpɛtrɪfaɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ that] 非常害怕的；吓呆的 If you are petrified, you are extremely frightened, perhaps so frightened that you cannot think or move. 非常害怕的；吓呆的

  • I've always been petrified of being alone. [+ of]

    我总是害怕孤单一人。

  • Most people seem to be petrified of snakes. [+ of]

    好像很多人都怕蛇。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （动植物）石化的 A petrified plant or animal has died and has gradually turned into stone. （动植物）石化的

  • ...a block of petrified wood.

    一块木化石

SYN fossilized, ossified, rocklike
