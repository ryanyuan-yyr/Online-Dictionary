amenable
əmiːnəbəl əˈminəbəl əˈmɛnəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 顺从的；服从的 If you are amenable to something, you are willing to do it or accept it. 顺从的；服从的

  • The Jordanian leader seemed amenable to attending a conference. [+ to]

    约旦领导人似乎愿意参加会议。

  • I never had a long-term relationship as I wasn't good enough, or interesting or amenable enough.

    因为我不够优秀，不够有趣或者不够亲切，所以恋爱关系从来都维持不长。

SYN receptive, open, susceptible, responsive
