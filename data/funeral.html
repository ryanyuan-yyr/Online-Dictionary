funeral
fjuːnərəl ˈfjunərəl
Word forms: plural funerals
1 N-COUNT 葬礼;丧礼 A funeral is the ceremony that is held when the body of someone who has died is buried or cremated. 葬礼;丧礼

  • His funeral will be on Thursday at Blackburn Cathedral.

    他的葬礼将于星期四在布莱克本大教堂举行。

  • He was given a state funeral.

    为他举行了国葬。

SYN burial, committal, laying to rest, cremation  
2 PHRASE 这是你自作自受;那你可要倒霉了 If someone says to you 'It's your funeral', they think your decision or your actions will have bad consequences for you, but they are unwilling to interfere. 这是你自作自受;那你可要倒霉了 [informal, feelings]

  • None of my business, I guess. It's your funeral.

    我想是不关我什么事儿，你就自寻死路吧。
