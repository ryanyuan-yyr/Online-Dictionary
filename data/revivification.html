revivify
riːvɪvɪfaɪ rɪˈvɪvɪˌfaɪ rɪˈvɪvəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense revivifies, present participle revivifying, past tense , past participle revivified
VERB 使恢复活力;使更加活跃;使更加振奋;使恢复效率 To revivify a situation, event, or activity means to make it more active, lively, or efficient. 使恢复活力;使更加活跃;使更加振奋;使恢复效率 [formal]

  • They've revivified rhythm and blues singing by giving it dance beats. [VERB noun]

    他们为节奏布鲁斯的歌曲中加入了舞曲的节奏，使之变得更加活跃。

SYN revive, restore, renew, refresh
