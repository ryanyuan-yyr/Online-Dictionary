powerless
paʊəʳləs ˈpaʊərlɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED 无权力的；无影响力的 Someone who is powerless is unable to control or influence events. 无权力的；无影响力的

  • If you don't have money, you're powerless.

    如果你没有钱，就寸步难行。

  • ...political and economic systems that keep women poor and powerless.

    致使女性一直贫穷且没有权力的政治经济体系

SYN defenceless, vulnerable, dependent, subject  
powerlessness

  • If we can't bring our problems under control, feelings of powerlessness and despair often ensue.

    如果我们不能解决自己的问题，往往会感到无能为力和绝望。

2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ to-infinitive] 无能为力的；做不到的 If you are powerless to do something, you are completely unable to do it. 无能为力的；做不到的

  • People are being murdered every day and I am powerless to stop it.

    每天都有人被杀，而我却无力阻止。

  • He was sympathetic, but powerless to help.

    他表示同情，但爱莫能助。
