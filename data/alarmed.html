alarmed
əlɑːʳmd əˈlɑːmd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 惊恐的；忧虑的；担心的 If someone is alarmed, they feel afraid or anxious that something unpleasant or dangerous might happen. 惊恐的；忧虑的；担心的

  • They should not be too alarmed by the press reports. [+ by/at]

    对于新闻报道他们不应过于担心。

  • The Americans are alarmed at this prospect.

    美国人对于这样的前景表示忧虑。

SYN frightened, troubled, shocked, scared
