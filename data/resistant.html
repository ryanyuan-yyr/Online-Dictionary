resistant
rɪzɪstənt rɪˈzɪstənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 反对的;抵制的;抗拒的 Someone who is resistant to something is opposed to it and wants to prevent it. 反对的;抵制的;抗拒的

  • Some people are very resistant to the idea of exercise. [+ to]

    有些人十分反对锻炼。

SYN opposed, hostile, dissident, unwilling  
2 ADJ-GRADED 有抵抗力的;不受…损害的 If something is resistant to a particular thing, it is not harmed by it. 有抵抗力的;不受…损害的

  • ...how to improve plants to make them more resistant to disease. [+ to]

    如何改良植物以增强它们对病害的抵抗力

  • The body may be less resistant if it is cold.

    天冷时，身体的抵抗力会下降。

SYN impervious, hard, strong, tough  
-resistant -rɪzɪstənt
COMB in ADJ-GRADED （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“抗…的”，“耐…的”，“防…的” -resistant is added to nouns to form adjectives that describe something as not being harmed or affected by the thing mentioned. （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“抗…的”，“耐…的”，“防…的”

  • Children's suncare products are normally water-resistant.

    儿童防晒产品通常都是防水的。

  • ...heat-resistant glass dishes.

    耐热玻璃器皿

  • ...bullet-resistant glass.

    防弹玻璃
