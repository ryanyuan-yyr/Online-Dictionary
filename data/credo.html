credo
kriːdoʊ kreɪ- ˈkriːdəʊ ˈkriˌdoʊ ˈkreɪˌdoʊ
Word forms: plural credos
N-COUNT 信条;教义 A credo is a set of beliefs, principles, or opinions that strongly influence the way a person lives or works. 信条;教义 [formal]

  • Lord Clarendon's liberal credo was one of the foundations of his political conduct.

    克拉伦登勋爵的自由主义信条是他从事政治活动的一大基础。
