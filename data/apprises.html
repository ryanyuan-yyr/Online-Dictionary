apprise
əpraɪz əˈpraɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense apprises, present participle apprising, past tense apprised
VERB 通知；告知 When you are apprised of something, someone tells you about it. 通知；告知 [formal]

  • Have customers been fully apprised of the advantages?. [be VERB-ed + of]

    顾客们已充分了解这些好处吗？

  • We must apprise them of the dangers that may be involved. [VERB noun + of]

    我们必须告诉他们可能涉及的危险。

SYN make aware, tell, warn, advise
