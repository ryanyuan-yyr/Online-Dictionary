cuddle up
PHRASAL VERB-RECIP 紧靠…而坐(或躺下);依偎 If you cuddle up to someone, you sit or lie as near to them as possible. 紧靠…而坐(或躺下);依偎 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • 'Yes,' he laughs, cuddling up to Debbie. [V P to n]

    “是的，”他笑着紧挨黛比坐下。

  • Then we'd go home and cuddle up together to watch TV. [V P together]

    然后我们就回家，依偎在一起看电视。

SYN snuggle, nestle
