pit stop
Word forms: plural pit stops
1 N-COUNT （汽车赛中的）加油（或维修）停车 In motor racing, if a driver makes a pit stop, he or she stops in a special place at the side of the track to get more fuel and to make repairs. （汽车赛中的）加油（或维修）停车

  • He had to make four pit stops during the race.

    他不得不在比赛过程中4次停车进站。

2 N-COUNT （尤指旅行中的）短暂休息，歇脚 A pit stop is a brief stop for rest and food, especially when you are on a journey. （尤指旅行中的）短暂休息，歇脚 [informal]

  • They went around the world in a week without a pit stop.

    他们用一周时间环绕了世界，中途没有一刻停留。
