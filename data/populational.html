population
pɒpjʊleɪʃən ˌpɑpjəˈleɪʃən
Word forms: plural populations
1 N-COUNT （某一国家或地区的）全体人民，人口 The population of a country or area is all the people who live in it. （某一国家或地区的）全体人民，人口

  • Bangladesh now has a population of about 110 million. [+ of]

    孟加拉国现有大约1.1亿人口。

  • ...the annual rate of population growth.

    人口年增长率

  • ...the local population.

    当地人口

SYN inhabitants, people, community, society  
2 N-COUNT （某一国家或地区某类人或动物的）总数 If you refer to a particular type of population in a country or area, you are referring to all the people or animals of that type there. （某一国家或地区某类人或动物的）总数 [formal]

  • ...75.6 per cent of the male population over sixteen.

    16岁以上男性中的75.6%

  • ...areas with a large black population.

    黑人人口众多的地区

  • ...the elephant populations of Tanzania and Kenya.

    坦桑尼亚和肯尼亚的大象种群
