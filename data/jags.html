jag
dʒæg dʒæɡ dʒæg
Word forms: jags
1 N-COUNT [n N] 一阵(控制不住的大哭或咳嗽)If you have a crying jag or a coughing jag, you have a period of uncontrolled crying or coughing. 一阵(控制不住的大哭或咳嗽) [informal]

  • I don't know how long my crying jag lasted.

    我不知道我一阵大哭持续了多久。

  • Laughing again, she was seized by a sudden coughing jag.

    再次发笑，她突然不受控制地一阵咳嗽。

2 N-COUNT 缺口; 突出端a jagged notch or projection 缺口; 突出端
3 N 醉酒; 药物中毒intoxication from drugs or alcohol 醉酒; 药物中毒
4 N 一次饮酒; 一次吸毒a bout of drinking or drug taking 一次饮酒; 一次吸毒
5 PHRASAL VERB 使···产生缺口to cut unevenly; make jagged 使···产生缺口
