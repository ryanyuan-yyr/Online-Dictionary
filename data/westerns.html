western
westəʳn ˈwɛstərn
(also Western)Word forms: plural westerns
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 西方的;西部的;来自西部的 Western means in or from the west of a region, state, or country. 西方的;西部的;来自西部的

  • ...hand-made rugs from Western and Central Asia.

    来自西亚和中亚的手工小地毯

  • ...Moi University, in western Kenya.

    位于肯尼亚西部的莫恩大学

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (来自)西方国家的 Western is used to describe things, people, ideas, or ways of life that come from or are associated with the United States, Canada, and the countries of Western, Northern, and Southern Europe. (来自)西方国家的

  • Mexico had the support of the big western governments.

    墨西哥拥有西方大国的支持。

  • Those statements have never been reported in the Western media.

    那些言论似乎从来没有被西方媒体报道过。

3 N-COUNT (关于19世纪美国西部生活，尤指牛仔生活的)西部电影(或小说) A western is a book or film about life in the west of America in the nineteenth century, especially the lives of cowboys. (关于19世纪美国西部生活，尤指牛仔生活的)西部电影(或小说)
