yellow card
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural yellow cards, present participle yellow carding, past tense , past participle yellow carded

2 VERB [usually passive] （足球或橄榄球比赛中）对…出示黄牌 In football or rugby, if a player is yellow carded, they are shown the yellow card by the referee. （足球或橄榄球比赛中）对…出示黄牌 [journalism]
