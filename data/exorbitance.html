exorbitant
ɪgzɔːʳbɪtənt ɛgˈzorbɪtənt ɪgˈzorbətənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 过度的;过分的；过高的 If you describe something such as a price or fee as exorbitant, you are emphasizing that it is much greater than it should be. 过度的;过分的；过高的 [emphasis]

  • Exorbitant housing prices have created an acute shortage of affordable housing for the poor.

    过高的房价已经造成了穷人能够买得起的住房严重短缺。

SYN excessive, high, expensive, extreme  
exorbitantly

  • ...exorbitantly high salaries.

    高得离谱的薪水
