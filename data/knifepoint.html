knifepoint naɪfpɔɪnt
(also knife-point)
PHRASE 在刀子威胁下 If you are attacked or robbed at knifepoint, someone threatens you with a knife while they attack or steal from you. 在刀子威胁下 [journalism]

  • A 15-year-old girl was attacked at knifepoint in a subway.

    一名15岁的女孩在地铁里遭持刀袭击。

  • He held her at knifepoint and threatened to kill her.

    他用刀将她挟持并威胁说要杀了她。
