yip
jɪp jɪp jɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense yips, present participle yipping, past tense , past participle yipped
VERB （狗等因害怕、痛苦等）吠叫，尖叫 If a dog or other animal yips, it gives a sudden short cry, often because of fear or pain. （狗等因害怕、痛苦等）吠叫，尖叫 [mainly US]

  • Far up the west rim of the canyon, a coyote yipped twice. [VERB]

    远处峡谷的西缘，一只丛林狼嚎叫了两声。

Yip is also a noun.

  • ...a yip of pain.

    痛苦的吠叫
