region
riːdʒən ˈridʒən
Word forms: plural regions
1 N-COUNT 地区;地带;行政区域 A region is a large area of land that is different from other areas of land, for example because it is one of the different parts of a country with its own customs and characteristics, or because it has a particular geographical feature. 地区;地带;行政区域

  • ...Barcelona, capital of the autonomous region of Catalonia.

    加泰罗尼亚自治区首府巴塞罗那

  • ...a remote mountain region.

    边远山区

SYN area, country, place, part  
2 N-PLURAL （首都以外的）各地区，外省 The regions are the parts of a country that are not the capital city and its surrounding area. （首都以外的）各地区，外省 [British]

  • ...London and the regions.

    伦敦和外省地区

  • Tax incentives would be used to attract firms to the regions, away from the South-East.

    将采取税收优惠政策把公司吸引到东南部以外的各地区。

3 N-COUNT （身体的）部位，区域 You can refer to a part of your body as a region. （身体的）部位，区域

  • ...the pelvic region.

    骨盆区

  • ...the frontal region of the brain.

    大脑的额区

4 PHRASE （指数量）差不多，在…左右，大约 You say in the region of to indicate that an amount that you are stating is approximate. （指数量）差不多，在…左右，大约 [vagueness]

  • The scheme will cost in the region of six million pounds.

    这个计划将耗资大约600万英镑。
