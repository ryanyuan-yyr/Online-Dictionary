leading
liːdɪŋ ˈlɛdɪŋ ˈlidɪŋ ˈlɛdɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 最重要的；卓著的；显赫的 The leading person or thing in a particular area is the one which is most important or successful. 最重要的；卓著的；显赫的

  • ...a leading member of Bristol's Sikh community.

    布里斯托尔锡克教社群一位出类拔萃的成员

  • Britain's future as a leading industrial nation depends on investment.

    作为一个主要工业国英国的未来要倚赖投资。

SYN principal, top, major, main  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 主角的；主演的 The leading role in a play or film is the main role. A leading lady or man is an actor who plays this role. 主角的；主演的
3 ADJ [ADJ n] （在比赛或行列中）首位的，居前的 The leading group, vehicle, or person in a race or procession is the one that is at the front. （在比赛或行列中）首位的，居前的
