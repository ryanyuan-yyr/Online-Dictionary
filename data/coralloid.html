coral
kɒrəl kɔː- ˈkɔrəl ˈkɑrəl
Word forms: plural corals
1 N-VAR 珊瑚 Coral is a hard substance formed from the bones of very small sea animals. It is often used to make jewellery. 珊瑚

  • The women have elaborate necklaces of turquoise and pink coral.

    那些女人戴着由绿松石和粉色珊瑚制成的精美项链。

2 N-COUNT 珊瑚虫 Corals are very small sea animals. 珊瑚虫
3 COLOUR 珊瑚色;珊瑚红;橘红色 Something that is coral is dark orangey-pink in colour. 珊瑚色;珊瑚红;橘红色

  • ...coral lipstick.

    珊瑚红色的唇膏

  • ...the coral-colored flower buds.

    橘红色的花蕾
