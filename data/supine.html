supine
suːpaɪn suˈpaɪn ˈsuˌpaɪn
1 ADJ 仰卧的;平躺着的 If you are supine, you are lying flat on your back. 仰卧的;平躺着的 [formal]

  • ...bedridden persons confined to the supine position.

    那些只能平躺在床上的久病不起的人。

  • ...a statue of a supine dog.

    一只仰卧姿势的狗的雕像

SYN flat on your back, flat, horizontal, recumbent  
Supine is also an adverb.

  • I lay supine on the poolside grass.

    我仰面躺在游泳池边的草地上。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 懒散的;得过且过的;软弱的 If you describe someone as supine, you mean that they let events happen because they are too lazy or afraid to influence them. 懒散的;得过且过的;软弱的 [formal]

  • ...a willing and supine executive.

    一个为人和气、得过且过的主管

SYN lethargic, passive, lazy, idle
