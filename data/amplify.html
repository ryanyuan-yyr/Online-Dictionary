amplify
æmplɪfaɪ ˈæmpləˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense amplifies, present participle amplifying, past tense , past participle amplified
1 VERB （通常用电子设备）放大，扩大（声音） If you amplify a sound, you make it louder, usually by using electronic equipment. （通常用电子设备）放大，扩大（声音）

  • This landscape seemed to trap and amplify sounds. [VERB noun]

    这种地貌好像可以笼住并放大声音。

  • The music was amplified with microphones. [be VERB-ed + with]

    音乐声通过麦克风被放大。

  • 'This is the police,' came the amplified voice from the helicopter. [VERB-ed]

    “我们是警察，”直升机上传来扩音器中的声音。

SYN expand, raise, extend, boost  
amplification

  • ...a voice that needed no amplification.

    不需要使用扩音设备的大嗓门

2 VERB 增强；加强 To amplify something means to increase its strength or intensity. 增强；加强

  • The mist had been replaced by a kind of haze that seemed to amplify the heat. [VERB noun]

    薄雾散了，取而代之的是一种似乎更添酷热的烟霭。

  • Her anxiety about the world was amplifying her personal fears about her future. [VERB noun]

    她对世界的忧虑加剧了她对自己未来的恐惧。
