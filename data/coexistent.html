coexist
koʊɪgzɪst ˌkəʊɪɡˈzɪst ˌkoʊɪgˈzɪst
(also co-exist)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense coexists, present participle coexisting, past tense , past participle coexisted
V-RECIP 同时存在;共存 If one thing coexists with another, they exist together at the same time or in the same place. You can also say that two things coexist . 同时存在;共存

  • Pockets of affluence coexist with poverty. [VERB + with]

    少数富裕地区与贫困地区并存。

  • Bankers and clockmakers have coexisted in the City for hundreds of years. [VERB]

    银行家与钟表匠已经在伦敦城共同存在好几百年了。
