connection
kənekʃən kəˈnɛkʃən
Word forms: plural connections
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 connexion
1 N-VAR 关系;联系 A connection is a relationship between two things, people, or groups. 关系;联系

  • There was no evidence of a connection between BSE and the brain diseases recently confirmed in cats. [+ between]

    没有证据表明疯牛病与最近确诊的猫类脑病有关联。

  • The police say he had no connection with the security forces. [+ with]

    警方说他与安全部队没有关系。

  • He has denied any connection to the bombing.

    他否认与爆炸事件有任何关联。

SYN association, relationship, link, relation  
2 N-COUNT (电线或管道的)连接部分，接头 A connection is a joint where two wires or pipes are joined together. (电线或管道的)连接部分，接头

  • Check all radiators for small leaks, especially round pipework connections.

    检查所有的暖气片，看有没有小裂缝，尤其是管道连接处附近。

SYN link, coupling, junction, fastening  
3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] (公路、铁路或空中的)交通连接，交通线 If a place has good road, rail, or air connections, many places can be directly reached from there by car, train, or plane. (公路、铁路或空中的)交通连接，交通线

  • Fukuoka has excellent air and rail connections to the rest of the country. [+ to]

    福冈与国内其他城市之间有非常便捷的空运和铁路运输系统。

4 N-COUNT [usually singular] 联运;转车;转机 If you get a connection at a station or airport, you catch a train, bus, or plane, after getting off another train, bus, or plane, in order to continue your journey. 联运;转车;转机

  • My flight was late and I missed the connection.

    航班晚点，我没赶上转机。

5 N-PLURAL 关系;人脉 Your connections are the people who you know or are related to, especially when they are in a position to help you. 关系;人脉

  • She used her connections to full advantage.

    她充分利用了自己的人脉。

SYN contact, friend, relation, ally  
6 PREP-PHRASE 关于;与…有关 If you write or talk to someone in connection with something, you write or talk to them about that thing. 关于;与…有关 [formal]

  • I am writing in connection with Michael Shower's letter.

    关于迈克尔·肖尔的来信，现复信如下。

  • 13 men have been questioned in connection with the murder.

    有13名男子因谋杀案受到盘问。

7 PHRASE [PHRASE with cl] 关于这/那一点;在这/那方面 You say in this connection or in that connection to indicate that what you are talking about is related to what you have just mentioned. 关于这/那一点;在这/那方面 [formal]

  • It is the 100th anniversary of his death. We here are having very great celebrations in this connection.

    今天是他逝世100周年纪念日，我们为此在这里举行盛大的纪念活动。
