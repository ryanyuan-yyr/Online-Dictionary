scavenge
skævɪndʒ ˈskævɪndʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense scavenges, present participle scavenging, past tense , past participle scavenged
VERB 捡破烂;拾荒;(从废弃物中)觅食，寻找 If people or animals scavenge for things, they collect them by searching among waste or unwanted objects. 捡破烂;拾荒;(从废弃物中)觅食，寻找 [Also VERB]

  • Many are orphans, their parents killed as they scavenged for food. [VERB + for]

    很多人是孤儿，他们的父母在垃圾中找吃的时丢了性命。

  • Children scavenge through garbage. [VERB prep./adv.]

    孩子们翻捡垃圾。

  • The foxes come and scavenge the bones. [VERB noun]

    狐狸过来寻觅骨头。

  • Cruz had to scavenge information from newspapers and journals. [VERB noun]

    克鲁兹不得不从报纸和杂志中搜寻信息。

SYN search, hunt, forage, rummage  
scavenger
