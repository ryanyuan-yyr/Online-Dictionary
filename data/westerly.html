westerly
westəʳli ˈwɛstərli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 西部的;向西的 A westerly point, area, or direction is to the west or towards the west. 西部的;向西的

  • ...Finisterre, Spain's most westerly point.

    坐落在西班牙最西端的菲尼斯特雷

  • They set out in a westerly direction along the riverbank.

    他们沿着河岸向西出发了。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (风)从西方吹来的 A westerly wind blows from the west. (风)从西方吹来的

  • ...a prevailing westerly wind.

    盛行西风
