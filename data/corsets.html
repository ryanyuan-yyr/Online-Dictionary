corset
kɔːʳsɪt ˈkɔrsɪt
Word forms: plural corsets
N-COUNT (尤指旧时妇女束腰的)紧身内衣 A corset is a stiff piece of underwear worn by some women, especially in the past. It fits tightly around their hips and waist and makes them thinner around the waist when they wear it. (尤指旧时妇女束腰的)紧身内衣
SYN girdle, bodice, foundation garment, panty girdle
