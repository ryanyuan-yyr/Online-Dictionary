revolutionize
revəluːʃənaɪz ˌrɛvəˈluʃəˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense revolutionizes, present participle revolutionizing, past tense , past participle revolutionized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 revolutionise
VERB 使发生巨大变革;给…带来革命性剧变 When something revolutionizes an activity, it causes great changes in the way that it is done. 使发生巨大变革;给…带来革命性剧变

  • Over the past forty years plastics have revolutionised the way we live. [VERB noun]

    在过去的40年中，塑料彻底改变了我们的生活方式。

  • Automation revolutionized the olive industry in the early 1970s. [VERB noun]

    自动化技术在20世纪70年代早期给橄榄油产业带来了一场革命。

SYN transform, reform, restructure, revamp
