slow motion
(also slow-motion)
N-UNCOUNT [usually in NOUN] 慢动作;慢镜头 When film or television pictures are shown in slow motion, they are shown much more slowly than normal. 慢动作;慢镜头

  • It seemed almost as if he were falling in slow motion.

    他看上去几乎是以慢动作倒下的。

  • This can be seen easily with the benefit of slow motion video playback.

    借助于慢镜头的回放，可以很容易地看清楚这个过程。
