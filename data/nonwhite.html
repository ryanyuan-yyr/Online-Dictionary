non-White
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 nonwhite
ADJ 非欧洲血统的;非白种人的 A non-white person is a member of a race of people who are not of European origin. 非欧洲血统的;非白种人的

  • Non-white people are effectively excluded from certain jobs.

    一些特定的工作事实上将非白种人排斥在外。

  • 60 percent of the population is non-white.

    60％的人口属于非欧洲血统。

Non-white is also a noun.

  • Not one non-white has ever been selected to play for the team.

    没有一个非白种人曾入选球队。
