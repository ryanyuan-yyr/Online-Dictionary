unholy
ʌnhoʊli ʌnˈhoʊli
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 极不合理的；极令人厌恶的 You use unholy to emphasize how unreasonable or unpleasant you think something is. 极不合理的；极令人厌恶的 [emphasis]

  • She protested that it wasn't traditional jazz at all, but an unholy row.

    她抗议说那根本不是传统爵士乐，而是令人憎恶的噪音。

  • The economy is still an unholy mess.

    经济依然是一团糟。

SYN shocking [informal] , awful, appalling, dreadful  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （由通常敌对的两方或多方组成的联盟）不同寻常的，诡异的 If you refer to two or more people or groups working together as an unholy alliance, you mean that this arrangement is unusual because the people usually oppose each other. （由通常敌对的两方或多方组成的联盟）不同寻常的，诡异的 [disapproval]

  • If the government does fall it will be because of this unholy alliance between the far right and the left.

    如果政府真的倒台了，那一定是因为极右翼势力和左翼势力的诡异联盟。

  • Westerners charged that the party was run by an unholy coalition between North and South.

    西部人指责说该党被北方和南方不同寻常的联盟所操控。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 邪恶的；罪恶的；亵渎的 If you describe something as unholy, you mean that it is wicked or bad. 邪恶的；罪恶的；亵渎的

  • 'This ought to be fun,' he told Alex, eyes gleaming with an almost unholy relish.

    “这一定会很有趣,”他对亚历克斯说，眼神里几乎透出一丝邪恶的光芒。

  • He screamed unholy things at me.

    他冲我高声咒骂。

SYN evil, vile, wicked, base
