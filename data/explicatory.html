explicate
eksplɪkeɪt ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense explicates, present participle explicating, past tense , past participle explicated
VERB 解释;说明;阐明 To explicate something means to explain it and make it clear. 解释;说明;阐明 [formal]

  • We shall have to explicate its basic assumptions before we can assess its implications. [VERB noun]

    在评估它的影响之前，我们应该解释清楚它的基本假设。
