pitfall
pɪtfɔːl ˈpɪtˌfɔl
Word forms: plural pitfalls
N-COUNT [usually plural] 隐患；陷阱；圈套 The pitfalls involved in a particular activity or situation are the things that may go wrong or may cause problems. 隐患；陷阱；圈套

  • The pitfalls of working abroad are numerous. [+ of]

    到国外工作存在许多陷阱。

  • He also points out that forward planning can help avoid stressful pitfalls.

    他也指出，预先计划可有助于消除令人不安的隐患。

SYN danger, difficulty, peril, catch [informal]
