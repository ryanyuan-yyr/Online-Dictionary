refurbish
riːfɜːʳbɪʃ riːˈfɜːbɪʃ rɪˈfɜrbɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense refurbishes, present participle refurbishing, past tense , past participle refurbished
VERB 翻新，整修（建筑物或房间） To refurbish a building or room means to clean it and decorate it and make it more attractive or better equipped. 翻新，整修（建筑物或房间）

  • We have spent money on refurbishing the offices. [VERB noun]

    我们已经花钱对办公室进行了翻新。

  • This hotel has recently been completely refurbished. [VERB noun]

    这家旅店近来彻底重新装修过。

SYN renovate, restore, repair, clean up
