collocate
kɒləkət kɒləkeɪt ˈkɑləˌkeɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense collocates, present participle collocating, past tense , past participle collocated
The noun is pronounced /'kɒləkət/. The verb is pronounced /'kɒləkeɪt/. 名词读作 /'kɒləkət/，动词读作 /'kɒləkeɪt/。
1 N-COUNT (语言学中)搭配词，组合词 In linguistics, a collocate of a particular word is another word which often occurs with that word. (语言学中)搭配词，组合词 [technical]
2 V-RECIP 搭配;组合 In linguistics, if one word collocates with another, they often occur together. 搭配;组合 [technical] [Also VERB]

  • 'Detached' collocates with 'house'. [VERB + with]

    detached 与 house 搭配使用。
