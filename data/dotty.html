dotty
dɒti ˈdɑti
Word forms: comparative dottier, superlative dottiest
ADJ-GRADED 疯疯癫癫的;半痴的;古怪的 If you say that someone is dotty, you mean that they are slightly mad or likely to do strange things. 疯疯癫癫的;半痴的;古怪的 [mainly British, informal]

  • She was obviously going a bit dotty.

    她显然变得有点疯疯癫癫的了。

SYN crazy, touched, peculiar, eccentric
