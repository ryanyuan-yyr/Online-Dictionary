toy with
1 PHRASAL VERB 随便对待;不很认真地考虑 If you toy with an idea, you consider it casually without making any decisions about it. 随便对待;不很认真地考虑

  • He toyed with the idea of going to China. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他动过去中国的念头，但并没当真。

  • For a time he had toyed with the notion of becoming a doctor. [VERB PREP. noun]

    有一段时间他还不大当真地有过做医生的念头。

SYN play with, consider, trifle with, flirt with  
2 PHRASAL VERB (尤指心不在焉地)玩弄，摆弄 If you toy with an object, you keep moving it around with your fingers, especially while you are thinking about something else. (尤指心不在焉地)玩弄，摆弄

  • He picked up a pencil and toyed with it idly. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他捡起一支铅笔，无所事事地摆弄着。

SYN fiddle with, finger, play with, twiddle  
3 PHRASAL VERB 拨弄(食物);漫不经心地小口啜(饮料) If you toy with food or drink, you do not eat or drink it with any enthusiasm, but only take a bite or a little drink from time to time. 拨弄(食物);漫不经心地小口啜(饮料)

  • She had no appetite, and merely toyed with the bread and cheese. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她没有胃口，只是拨弄着面包和奶酪。
