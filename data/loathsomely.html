loathsome
loʊðsəm ˈloʊðsəm ˈloʊθsəm
ADJ-GRADED 令人憎恶的；让人恶心的；讨厌的 If you describe someone or something as loathsome, you are indicating how much you dislike them or how much they disgust you. 令人憎恶的；让人恶心的；讨厌的

  • ...the loathsome spectacle we were obliged to witness.

    我们不得不目睹的令人恶心的场面。

SYN hateful, offensive, nasty, disgusting
