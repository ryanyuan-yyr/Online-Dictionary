mooring
mʊərɪŋ ˈmʊrɪŋ
Word forms: plural moorings
1 N-COUNT 泊地;停泊区 A mooring is a place where a boat can be tied so that it cannot move away, or the object it is tied to. 泊地;停泊区

  • Free moorings will be available.

    将会有免费停泊区。

  • ...mooring fees.

    停泊费

2 N-PLURAL (绳、链等)系船用具，系泊装置 Moorings are the ropes, chains, and other objects used to moor a boat. (绳、链等)系船用具，系泊装置

  • He cut the engine and grabbed the mooring lines.

    他关掉发动机，抓住系船的绳子。

  • Emergency workers fear that the burning ship could slip its moorings.

    急救人员担心燃烧着的船只可能会脱链滑走。
