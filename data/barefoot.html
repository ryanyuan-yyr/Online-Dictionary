barefoot
beəʳfʊt ˈbɛəˌfʊt ˈbɛrˌfʊt
(also barefooted)
ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] 赤脚的；光着脚的 Someone who is barefoot or barefooted is not wearing anything on their feet. 赤脚的；光着脚的

  • I wore a white dress and was barefoot.

    我穿着一件白色连衣裙，赤着脚。

  • Alan came running barefoot through the house.

    艾伦赤脚跑过房子。

  • ...barefoot little children.

    光着脚的小孩子们
