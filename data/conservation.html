conservation
kɒnsəʳveɪʃən ˌkɑnsərˈveɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT (对环境的)保护 Conservation is saving and protecting the environment. (对环境的)保护

  • ...a four-nation regional meeting on elephant conservation.

    四国保护大象的地区性会议

  • ...tree-planting and other conservation projects.

    植树以及其他环保计划

2 N-UNCOUNT (对文物或艺术品的) 保护 Conservation is saving and protecting historical objects or works of art such as paintings, sculptures, or buildings. (对文物或艺术品的) 保护

  • Then he began his most famous work, the conservation and rebinding of the Book of Kells. [+ of]

    之后他就开始了他最为出名的工作，即保护并重新装订《凯尔斯书》。

3 N-UNCOUNT 节约;防止浪费 The conservation of a supply of something is the careful use of it so that it lasts for a long time. 节约;防止浪费

  • ...projects aimed at promoting energy conservation.

    旨在促进节约能源的计划

  • ...rules concerning the conservation of fishery resources.

    有关保护渔业资源的规定

SYN economy, saving, thrift, husbandry
