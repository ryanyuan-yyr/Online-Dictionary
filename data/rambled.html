ramble
ræmbəl ˈræmbəl ˈræmbəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense rambles, present participle rambling, past tense , past participle rambled
1 N-COUNT (在乡间的）漫游，闲逛 A ramble is a long walk in the countryside. (在乡间的）漫游，闲逛

  • ...an hour's ramble through the woods.

    一个小时的林间漫步

SYN walk, tour, trip, stroll  
2 VERB （在乡间）漫游，闲逛 If you ramble, you go on a long walk in the countryside. （在乡间）漫游，闲逛

  • ...freedom to ramble across the moors. [VERB adv./prep.]

    在荒野里漫步的自由

SYN walk, range, drift, wander  
3 VERB 漫谈；漫笔 If you say that a person rambles in their speech or writing, you mean they do not make much sense because they keep going off the subject in a confused way. 漫谈；漫笔

  • Sometimes she spoke sensibly; sometimes she rambled. [VERB]

    有时她讲话很有条理，有时则讲得漫无边际。

  • It would have been best written in a more concise way as it does tend to ramble. [VERB]

    它的行文确实有些信马由缰，要是简洁一些就好了。

Phrasal verbs:
ramble on
