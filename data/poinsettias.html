poinsettia
pɔɪnsetiə pɔɪnˈsɛtə pɔɪnˈsɛtiə
Word forms: plural poinsettias
N-COUNT （在英美两国的圣诞节时尤受欢迎的）一品红，圣诞花 A poinsettia is a plant with groups of bright red or pink leaves that grows in Central and South America. Poinsettias are very popular in Britain and the United States, especially at Christmas. （在英美两国的圣诞节时尤受欢迎的）一品红，圣诞花
