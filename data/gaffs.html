Word forms: plural gaffs
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] （固定在桅杆上以支撑帆的）斜桁 On a boat, a gaff is a pole which is attached to a mast in order to support a particular kind of sail. （固定在桅杆上以支撑帆的）斜桁
2 N-COUNT （钓大鱼的）手钩，挽钩 A gaff is a pole with a point or hook at one end, which is used for catching large fish. （钓大鱼的）手钩，挽钩
