supplicant sʌplɪkənt ˈsʌpləkənt
Word forms: plural supplicants
N-COUNT (向上帝或重要人物请求的)祈求者，恳求者，哀求者 A supplicant is a person who prays to God or respectfully asks an important person to help them or to give them something that they want very much. (向上帝或重要人物请求的)祈求者，恳求者，哀求者 [formal]

  • He flung himself down in the flat submissive posture of a mere supplicant.

    他以一个卑微的哀求者绝对谦恭的姿态猛地趴在地上。

SYN petitioner, applicant, suitor, suppliant
