rediscover
riːdɪskʌvəʳ ˌriːdɪˈskʌvə
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rediscovers, present participle rediscovering, past tense , past participle rediscovered
VERB 重新发现;再发现 If you rediscover something good or valuable that you had forgotten or lost, you become aware of it again or find it again. 重新发现;再发现

  • ...a one-time rebel who had rediscovered his faith. [VERB noun]

    重拾信仰的昔日叛乱者

  • Some of Naomi's earlier writings have been rediscovered in the 1980s and 1990s. [VERB noun]

    娜奥米的一些早期作品在20世纪80年代和90年代被重新发掘出来。

  • Despite the newly rediscovered friendship, there's no doubt that more difficulties lie ahead. [VERB-ed]

    尽管最近重新恢复了友好关系，但接下来无疑会面临更多的困难。

SYN discover, find, turn up, locate
