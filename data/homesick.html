homesick
hoʊmsɪk ˈhoʊmˌsɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 想家的;思乡的 If you are homesick, you feel unhappy because you are away from home and are missing your family, friends, and home very much. 想家的;思乡的

  • She's feeling a little homesick.

    她有点想家。

homesickness

  • There were inevitable bouts of homesickness.

    难免会涌起一阵阵的思乡情绪。
