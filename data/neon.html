neon

    在城市广场上，霓虹灯依次闪烁。

  • ...neon slogans which light up the city by night.

    夜晚照亮整个城市的霓虹灯广告

2 N-UNCOUNT 氖(一种稀有气体) Neon is a gas which occurs in very small amounts in the atmosphere. 氖(一种稀有气体)

  • ...inert gases like neon and argon.

    像氖和氩这样的惰性气体
