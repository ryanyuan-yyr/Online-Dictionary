alfresco
ælfreskoʊ ælˈfrɛskoʊ ɑlˈfrɛskoʊ
(also al fresco)
ADJ [ADJ n] 露天的；户外的 An alfresco activity, especially a meal, is one that takes place in the open air. 露天的；户外的

  • ... an al fresco breakfast of fresh fruit.

    以新鲜水果为主的露天早餐

Alfresco is also an adverb.

  • He came across the man shaving alfresco.

    他碰见那个男人正在外面刮胡子。
