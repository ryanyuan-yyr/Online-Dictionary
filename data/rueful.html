rueful
ruːfʊl ˈrufəl
ADJ-GRADED 后悔的;懊悔的;遗憾的 If someone is rueful, they feel or express regret or sorrow in a quiet and gentle way. 后悔的;懊悔的;遗憾的 [literary]

  • He shook his head and gave me a rueful smile.

    他摇了摇头，遗憾地对我笑了笑。

  • 'Our marriage was a mistake,' she said, looking rueful.

    “我们的婚姻是个错误，”她说着，看起来懊悔不已。

SYN regretful, sad, dismal, melancholy  
ruefully

  • He grinned at her ruefully.

    他遗憾地对她咧嘴一笑。
