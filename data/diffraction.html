diffraction
dɪfrækʃən dɪˈfrækʃən dɪˈfrækʃən
N-UNCOUNT (声波或光波的)衍射In physics, diffraction is a change in the direction of a sound wave or a light wave caused by the presence of an obstacle in its path. (声波或光波的)衍射

  • ...the diffraction of light that occurs in natural phenomena such as rainbows.

    ...自然现象中产生的光衍射，例如彩虹。
