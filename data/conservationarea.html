conservation area
ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃən ˈɛərɪə
Word forms: plural conservation areas
1 N-COUNT (英国鸟类及其他动物的)保护区 In Britain, a conservation area is an area where birds and animals are protected. (英国鸟类及其他动物的)保护区

  • ...wildlife conservation areas.

    野生动物保护区

2 N-COUNT (英国的)古建保护区 In Britain, a conservation area is an area where old buildings are protected and new building is controlled. (英国的)古建保护区

  • You probably won't need to apply for planning permission unless you live in a listed building or conservation area.

    除非你住在已列入文保名录的建筑或古建保护区之内，否则应该不需要申请规划许可。
