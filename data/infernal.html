infernal
ɪnfɜːʳnəl ɪnˈfɜrnəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 可恨的;坏透的;该死的 Infernal is used to emphasize that something is very annoying or unpleasant. 可恨的;坏透的;该死的 [old-fashioned, emphasis]

  • The post office is shut, which is an infernal bore.

    邮局关门了,真是可恶。

  • They can't work in these infernal conditions.

    他们不能在这样糟糕透顶的条件下工作。

SYN damned [slang] , malevolent, hellish, devilish  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 阴间的;地狱的 Infernal is used to describe things that relate to hell. 阴间的;地狱的 [literary]

  • ...the goddess of the infernal regions.

    阴间冥府的女神

SYN hellish, lower, underworld, nether
