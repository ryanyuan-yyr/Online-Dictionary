clean out
1 PHRASAL VERB 彻底清理(橱柜、房间、容器等);把…打扫干净 If you clean out something such as a cupboard, room, or container, you take everything out of it and clean the inside of it thoroughly. 彻底清理(橱柜、房间、容器等);把…打扫干净

  • Mr. Wall asked if I would help him clean out the bins. [VERB PREP. noun]

    沃尔先生问我能不能帮他把各个箱子彻底清理一遍。

  • If you are using the same pan, clean it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果还用同一个平底锅，要把它清理干净。

2 PHRASAL VERB 把…抢光;把…洗劫一空 If someone cleans you out, they take all the money and valuables you have. If they clean out a place, they take everything of value that is in it. 把…抢光;把…洗劫一空 [informal]

  • I'm sure the burglars waited until my insurance claim was through and came back to clean me out again. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我敢肯定，窃贼们一定是等着我领到了保险赔偿金，然后回来再次把我洗劫一空。

  • When they first captured the port, they virtually cleaned out its warehouses. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们刚占领港口时，把那里的仓库简直抢了个精光。
