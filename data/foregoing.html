foregoing
fɔːʳgoʊɪŋ fɔːʳgoʊ- fɔːˈɡəʊɪŋ ˈfɔrˌgoʊɪŋ fɔrˈgoʊɪŋ
PRON 前述内容 You can refer to what has just been stated or mentioned as the foregoing . 前述内容 [formal]

  • You might think from the foregoing that the French want to phase accents out. Not at all.

    根据前文所述，你们可能以为法国人打算废除各种口音。事实并非如此。

Foregoing is also an adjective.

  • The foregoing paragraphs were written in 1985.

    前面的几个段落写于 1985 年。
