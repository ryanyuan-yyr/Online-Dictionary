author
ɔːθəʳ ˈɔθər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural authors, present participle authoring, past tense , past participle authored
1 N-COUNT 作者;著作者 The author of a piece of writing is the person who wrote it. 作者;著作者

  • ...Jill Phillips, author of the book 'Give Your Child Music'. [+ of]

    吉尔·菲利普斯，《给孩子音乐》一书的作者

SYN writer, composer, novelist, hack  
2 N-COUNT 作家 An author is a person whose job is writing books. 作家

  • Haruki Murakami is Japan's best-selling author.

    村上春树是日本当红的畅销书作家。

3 N-COUNT (计划或建议的）筹划者，制定者 The author of a plan or proposal is the person who thinks of it and works out the details. (计划或建议的）筹划者，制定者

  • The authors of the plan believe they can reach this point within about two years. [+ of]

    该计划的提出者相信他们约在两年内可以达成该目标。

4 VERB 创作；撰写 To author something means to be the author of it. 创作；撰写

  • Then he opened a restaurant, authored a book, and landed his own radio show. [VERB noun]

    后来他开了个餐馆，写了本书，还制作了自己的广播剧。

  • ...a proposal authored by subcommittee chairman Quentin Burdick. [VERB-ed]

    专门小组委员会主席昆廷·伯迪克提出的建议

5 See also: co-author
