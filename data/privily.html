privy
prɪvi ˈprɪvi
Word forms: plural privies
1 ADJ 允许获知的；知情的；了解内情的 If you are privy to something secret, you have been allowed to know about it. 允许获知的；知情的；了解内情的 [formal]

  • Only three people, including a policeman, will be privy to the facts. [+ to]

    只会允许3个人，其中包括一名警察，了解这些内情。

SYN informed of, aware of, in on, wise to [slang]  
2 N-COUNT （尤指户外的）厕所，茅房 A privy is a toilet, especially one that is in a small building outside a house. （尤指户外的）厕所，茅房 [old-fashioned]
SYN lavatory, closet, bog [slang] , latrine
