newsworthy
njuːzwɜːʳði nuːz- ˈnuzˌwɜrði
ADJ-GRADED 有报道价值的;有新闻价值的;值得报道的 An event, fact, or person that is newsworthy is considered to be interesting enough to be reported in newspapers or on the radio or television. 有报道价值的;有新闻价值的;值得报道的

  • The number of deaths makes the story newsworthy.

    死亡人数使这篇报道具有了新闻价值。

  • This situation might develop into an even more newsworthy item if the police were involved.

    如果警方涉入的话，这个局面就会变得更有新闻价值了。

SYN interesting, important, arresting, significant
