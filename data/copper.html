copper
kɒpəʳ ˈkɑpər
Word forms: plural coppers
1 N-UNCOUNT 铜 Copper is reddish brown metal that is used to make things such as coins and electrical wires. 铜

  • Chile is the world's largest producer of copper.

    智利是世界上最大的产铜国。

  • ...a copper mine.

    铜矿

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 铜色的;紫铜色的;红棕色的 Copper is sometimes used to describe things that are reddish-brown in colour. 铜色的;紫铜色的;红棕色的 [literary]

  • His hair has reverted back to its original copper hue.

    他的头发又变回原来的淡红棕色。

3 N-COUNT 铜板;铜币 Coppers are brown metal coins of low value. 铜板;铜币 [mainly British, informal]
4 N-COUNT 警察 A copper is a policeman or a policewoman. 警察 [British, informal]

  • ...your friendly neighbourhood copper.

    你们友善的社区警察
