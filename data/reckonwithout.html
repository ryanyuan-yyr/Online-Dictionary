reckon without
PHRASAL VERB 没有考虑到…；没把…算在内 If you say that you had reckoned without something, you mean that you had not expected it and so were not prepared for it. 没有考虑到…；没把…算在内

  • I thought that it would take only a day of hard driving to reach Chengdu. But I had reckoned without the landslides. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我原以为只要辛苦点儿，驾车一天就可以到达成都。但是我没有考虑到山体滑坡。
