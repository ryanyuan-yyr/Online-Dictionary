kill off
1 →see: kill 3 
2 PHRASAL VERB 被全部杀死；被灭绝 If you say that a group or an amount of something has been killed off, you mean that all of them or all of it have been killed or destroyed. 被全部杀死；被灭绝

  • Their natural predators have been killed off. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    它们的天敌被全部杀死。

  • It is an effective treatment for the bacteria and does kill it off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    这是杀菌的特效药，能将其全部消灭。

  • All blood products are now heat treated to kill off any infection. [VERB PREP. noun]

    目前所有血液制品均经过热处理，以防止各种感染。
