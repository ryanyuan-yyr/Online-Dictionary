cartridge
kɑːʳtrɪdʒ ˈkɑrtrɪdʒ
Word forms: plural cartridges
1 N-COUNT 弹壳;弹药筒 A cartridge is a metal or cardboard tube containing a bullet and an explosive substance. Cartridges are used in guns. 弹壳;弹药筒
2 N-COUNT (机器、设备等可更换使用的)盒，筒 A cartridge is part of a machine or device that can be easily removed and replaced when it is worn out or empty. (机器、设备等可更换使用的)盒，筒

  • Change the filter cartridge as often as instructed by the manufacturer.

    按照生产厂家的使用说明，经常更换过滤筒。

SYN container, case, magazine, cassette
