animus
ænɪməs ˈænəməs
N-UNCOUNT 敌意；仇视；憎恶 If a person has an animus against someone, they have a strong feeling of dislike for them, even when there is no good reason for it. 敌意；仇视；憎恶 [formal]

  • Your animus towards him suggests that you are the wrong man for the job.

    你对他有看法表明你不是这份工作的合适人选。

  • ...Mr Milner's animus towards Mr Stevens.

    米尔纳先生对史蒂文斯先生的敌意
