deceitful
dɪsiːtfʊl diˈsitfəl dɪˈsitfəl
ADJ-GRADED 欺骗的;欺诈的 If you say that someone is deceitful, you mean that they behave in a dishonest way by making other people believe something that is not true. 欺骗的;欺诈的

  • They claimed the government had been deceitful.

    他们声称政府一直存心欺骗。

  • The ambassador called the report deceitful and misleading.

    大使指称那份报告具有欺骗性和误导性。

SYN dishonest, false, deceiving, fraudulent
