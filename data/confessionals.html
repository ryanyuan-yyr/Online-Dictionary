confessional
kənfeʃənəl kənˈfɛʃənəl kənˈfɛʃənəl
Word forms: plural confessionals
1 N-COUNT (尤指天主教教堂的)告解室 A confessional is the small room in a church where Christians, especially Roman Catholics, go to confess their sins. (尤指天主教教堂的)告解室
2 ADJ-GRADED 忏悔的;自白的 A confessional speech or writing contains confessions. 忏悔的;自白的

  • The convictions rest solely on disputed witness and confessional statements.

    仅仅根据有争议的目击者证言和供词就定了罪。

  • Their first album was painfully frank to the point of being confessional.

    他们的第一张专辑是那么的坦率，近乎自白。

3 N-COUNT 忏悔;自白;检讨会；自我批评会 A confessional is a statement or meeting in which people confess things. 忏悔;自白;检讨会；自我批评会

  • In regular group confessionals, the worst performers have to explain themselves to their peers.

    在定期举行的集体检讨会上，表现最差的人必须向同事作出解释。
