extrinsic
ɪkstrɪnzɪk ɛkˈstrɪnsɪk -sɪk ɛkˈstrɪnsɪk ɛksˈtrɪnzɪk ɪkˈstrɪnsɪk ɪkˈstrɪnzɪk
ADJ [ADJ n] 外来的;外在的;外部的 Extrinsic reasons, forces, or factors exist outside the person or situation they affect. 外来的;外在的;外部的 [formal]

  • Nowadays there are fewer extrinsic pressures to get married.

    现在来自外部的结婚压力少了。

SYN external, outside, exterior, foreign
