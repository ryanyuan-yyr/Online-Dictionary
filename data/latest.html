latest
leɪtɪst ˈleɪtɪst
1 （late的最高级） Latest is the superlative of late. （late的最高级）
2 ADJ-SUPERL 最近的；最新的 You use latest to describe something that is the most recent thing of its kind. 最近的；最新的

  • ...her latest book.

    她的新书

  • Latest reports say another five people have been killed.

    最新报道称又有5人被害。

  • The strike is the latest in a series by public employees in protest at worsening economic conditions and government austerity measures.

    这是公务员发起的一系列罢工中的最新一次，以抗议日趋恶化的经济状况和政府的紧缩措施。

SYN up-to-date, current, fresh, newest  
3 ADJ-SUPERL 最新式的；最现代的 You can use latest to describe something that is very new and modern and is better than older things of a similar kind. 最新式的；最现代的 [Also + of]

  • Crooks are using the latest laser photocopiers to produce millions of fake banknotes.

    犯罪分子正利用最新式的激光复印机制造数以百万计的假钞。

  • I got to drive the latest model.

    我开上了最新款的车。

  • Computers have always represented the latest in technology. [+ in]

    计算机向来是最新科技的象征。

4 See also: late 
5 PHRASE 最晚；至迟 You use at the latest in order to indicate that something must happen at or before a particular time and not after that time. 最晚；至迟 [emphasis]

  • She should be back by ten o'clock at the latest.

    她最晚应该在10点前回来。
