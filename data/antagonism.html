antagonism
æntægənɪzəm ænˈtægəˌnɪzəm
Word forms: plural antagonisms
N-UNCOUNT 敌对；对立；对抗 Antagonism between people is hatred or dislike between them. Antagonisms are instances of this. 敌对；对立；对抗

  • There is still much antagonism between trades unions and the oil companies. [+ between]

    工会和石油公司之间仍然存在着相当大的敌意。

  • Old antagonisms resurfaced.

    旧怨重生。

SYN hostility, competition, opposition, conflict
