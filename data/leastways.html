leastways
listweɪz listwaɪz ˈliːstˌweɪz ˈlistˌweɪz
(also leastwise)
ADV 至少You use leastways to indicate that you are correcting or changing something you have just said. 至少 [US, spoken]

  • Well, I don't imagine that will be necessary. Leastways, I hope not.

    我想那是不必要的。至少，我希望不是。

  • "Your granddaddy don't care for 'em, neither," he threw in. "Not that I know of, leastways."

    "你的祖父对他们都不喜欢，" 他插话说。 "至少，据我所知不是。"
