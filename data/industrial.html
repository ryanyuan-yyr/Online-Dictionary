industrial
ɪndʌstriəl ɪnˈdʌstriəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 工业的;产业的 You use industrial to describe things which relate to or are used in industry. 工业的;产业的

  • ...industrial machinery and equipment.

    工业机械与设备

  • ...a link between industrial chemicals and cancer.

    化工制品与癌症的联系

SYN manufacturing, business, commercial  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 工业高度发达的;工业化的 An industrial city or country is one in which industry is important or highly developed. 工业高度发达的;工业化的

  • ...ministers from leading western industrial countries.

    西方工业大国的部长们
