affirmative
əfɜːʳmətɪv əˈfɜrmətɪv
1 ADJ （话语、手势等）肯定的 An affirmative word or gesture indicates that you agree with what someone has said or that the answer to a question is 'yes'. （话语、手势等）肯定的 [formal]

  • Haig was desperately eager for an affirmative answer.

    黑格非常渴望得到一个肯定的回答。

  • Dr Sinclair's affirmative nod seemed a shade reluctant.

    辛克莱博士虽然点头表示了同意，但还显得有点不情愿。

SYN agreeing, confirming, positive, approving  
affirmatively

  • 'Is that clear?' Bob nodded his head affirmatively.

    “清楚了吗？”鲍勃肯定地点点头。

2 PHRASE 以肯定方式；表示赞成 If you reply to a question in the affirmative, you say 'yes' or make a gesture that means 'yes'. 以肯定方式；表示赞成 [formal]

  • He asked me if I was ready. I answered in the affirmative.

    他问我是否准备好了，我给出肯定的回答。

3 ADJ （语法中）肯定的 In grammar, an affirmative clause is positive and does not contain a negative word. （语法中）肯定的
