siege mentality
N-SING 受围心态(感觉别人总想伤害或击败自己，因而一味想要自卫的心理) If a group of people have a siege mentality, they think that other people are constantly trying to harm or defeat them, and so they care only about protecting themselves. 受围心态(感觉别人总想伤害或击败自己，因而一味想要自卫的心理)

  • Police officers had a siege mentality that isolated them from the people they served.

    警察过去有一种受围心态，这将他们与他们服务的民众孤立开来。
