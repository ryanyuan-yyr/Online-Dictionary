gaily
geɪli ˈgeɪli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 快乐地；喜气洋洋地 If you do something gaily, you do it in a lively, happy way. 快乐地；喜气洋洋地

  • Magda laughed gaily.

    玛格达开心地笑了起来。

2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB -ed] 艳丽地；花哨地；华丽地 Something that is gaily coloured or gaily decorated is coloured or decorated in a bright, pretty way. 艳丽地；花哨地；华丽地

  • He put on a gaily coloured shirt.

    他穿上一件花哨的衬衫。

  • ...gaily painted front doors.

    刷有亮丽油漆的前门

SYN colourfully, brightly, vividly, flamboyantly
