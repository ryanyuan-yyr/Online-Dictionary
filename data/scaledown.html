scale down
PHRASAL VERB 缩小;缩减;减弱 If you scale down something, you make it smaller in size, amount, or extent than it used to be. 缩小;缩减;减弱 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • One factory has had to scale down its workforce from six hundred to only six. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一家工厂不得不将员工人数从 600 名裁减至仅剩 6 名。

  • The air rescue operation has now been scaled down. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    空中救援行动的规模现在已经有所缩小。

  • The Romanian government yesterday unveiled a new, scaled-down security force. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    罗马尼亚政府昨天对外公布了精简编制后新组建的安全部队的情况。

SYN reduce, cut, moderate, slow down
