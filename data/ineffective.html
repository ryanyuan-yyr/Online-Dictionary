ineffective
ɪnɪfektɪv ˌɪnɛˈfɛktɪv ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪv
ADJ-GRADED 不起作用的;无效果的;无效率的 If you say that something is ineffective, you mean that it has no effect on a process or situation. 不起作用的;无效果的;无效率的

  • Economic reform will continue to be painful and ineffective.

    经济改革仍将举步维艰,毫无成效。

  • This is an ineffective method of controlling your dog.

    用这种方法控制你的狗不起作用。

SYN unproductive, useless, futile, vain  
ineffectiveness

  • ...the ineffectiveness of some of the police's anti-crime strategies.

    警方某些打击犯罪的策略毫无作用
