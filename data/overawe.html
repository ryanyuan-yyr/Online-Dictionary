overawe
oʊvərɔː ˌoʊvərˈɔ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overawes, present participle overawing, past tense , past participle overawed
VERB [usually passive] 使极为敬畏；慑服 If you are overawed by something or someone, you are very impressed by them and a little afraid of them. 使极为敬畏；慑服

  • Don't be overawed by people in authority, however important they are. [be VERB-ed]

    不管当官儿的职权有多大，都不要被他们吓倒。

  • He's never been overawed or intimidated by big occasions. [be VERB-ed]

    各种大场合都没让他犯过憷。
