intelligible
ɪntelɪdʒɪbəl ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可理解的;明白易懂的 Something that is intelligible can be understood. 可理解的;明白易懂的

  • The language of Darwin was intelligible to experts and non-experts alike. [+ to]

    达尔文的语言无论对专家还是非专业人士而言都明白易懂。

  • The woman moaned faintly but made no intelligible response.

    那个女人发出微弱的呻吟声，却没有作出任何有意义的反应。

SYN understandable, clear, distinct, lucid
