confessor
kənfesəʳ kənˈfɛsər
Word forms: plural confessors
1 N-COUNT 告解神甫；告解司铎 A confessor is a priest who hears a person's confession. 告解神甫；告解司铎
2 N-COUNT 倾诉对象 If you describe someone as your confessor, you mean that they are the person you can talk to about your secrets or problems. 倾诉对象

  • He was their adviser, confidant and father confessor.

    他是他们的顾问、密友和倾听者。
