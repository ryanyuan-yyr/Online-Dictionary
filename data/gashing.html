gash
gæʃ gæʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense gashes, present participle gashing, past tense , past participle gashed
1 N-COUNT 深长的伤口（或切口） A gash is a long, deep cut in your skin or in the surface of something. 深长的伤口（或切口）

  • There was an inch-long gash just above his right eye.

    就在他右眼的上方有一条一英寸长的伤口。

SYN cut, tear, split, wound  
2 VERB 划伤；砍伤 If you gash something, you accidentally make a long and deep cut in it. 划伤；砍伤

  • He gashed his leg while felling trees. [VERB noun]

    他砍树的时候砍伤了腿。

SYN cut, tear, split, wound
