animate
ænɪmət ænɪmeɪt ˈænɪˌmeɪt ˈænəmɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense animates, present participle animating, past tense , past participle animated
The adjective is pronounced /'ænɪmət/. The verb is pronounced /'ænɪmeɪt/. 形容词读作 /'ænɪmət/，动词读作 /'ænɪmeɪt/。
1 ADJ 有生命的；活的 Something that is animate has life, in contrast to things like stones and machines which do not. 有生命的；活的

  • ...all aspects of the material world, animate and inanimate.

    物质世界的方方面面，包括有生命的和无生命的

SYN living, live, moving, alive  
2 VERB 使有生气；使活泼；激励；鼓舞 To animate something means to make it lively or more cheerful. 使有生气；使活泼；激励；鼓舞

  • There was precious little about the cricket to animate the crowd. [VERB noun]

    板球比赛能激起观众热情的地方很少。

  • The girls watched, little teasing smiles animating their faces. [VERB noun]

    女孩们注视着，脸上挂着调皮的微笑，显得愈加活泼。

SYN enliven, encourage, excite, urge
