    我 15 岁了，还没有亲过一个女孩。

  • They were snogging under a bridge. [VERB]

    他们在一座桥下面拥抱接吻。

  • We went in for secret snogging sessions in the toilets at the restaurant. [VERB-ing]

    我们到饭店的卫生间里偷偷地接吻拥抱。
