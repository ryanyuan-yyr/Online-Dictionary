manfully
mænfəli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] （尤指男人）英勇地;果断地 If you say that someone, especially a man, does something manfully, you mean that they do it in a very determined or brave way. （尤指男人）英勇地;果断地

  • They stuck to their task manfully.

    他们恪尽职守。

  • ...simple people who manfully bear the deprivations that are their lot.

    勇于承受贫苦命运的淳朴的人们

SYN bravely, boldly, vigorously, stoutly
