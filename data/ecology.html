ecology
ɪkɒlədʒi iˈkɑlədʒi
Word forms: plural ecologies
1 N-UNCOUNT 生态学 Ecology is the study of the relationships between plants, animals, people, and their environment, and the balances between these relationships. 生态学

  • ...a senior lecturer in ecology.

    生态学高级讲师

2 N-VAR 生态（环境）；生态系统 When you talk about the ecology of a place, you are referring to the pattern and balance of relationships between plants, animals, people, and the environment in that place. 生态（环境）；生态系统

  • ...the ecology of the rocky Negev desert in Israel. [+ of]

    以色列多岩石的内盖夫沙漠生态系统

  • ...the extinction of the marshes' unique ecology.

    湿地独特生态的消亡

  • Global ecological efforts can easily be at odds with local ecologies.

    全球性生态保护工作很可能会和地方生态系统存在矛盾。
