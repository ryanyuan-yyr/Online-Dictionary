spruce
spruːs sprus
Word forms: plural spruce, 3rd person singular present tense spruces, present participle sprucing, past tense , past participle spruced
1 N-VAR 云杉;花旗松 A spruce is a kind of evergreen tree. 云杉;花旗松

  • Trees such as spruce, pine and oak have been planted.

    种植了云杉、松树和橡树等树木。

  • ...a young blue spruce.

    蓝云杉幼株

  • ...80-year-old spruces.

    有着80年树龄的云杉

Spruce is the wood from this tree.云杉木材

  • Early settlers built frames of spruce, maple and pine.

    早期的定居者用云杉木、枫木和松木建造屋架。

2 ADJ-GRADED (人)整洁漂亮的 Someone who is spruce is very neat and smart in appearance. (人)整洁漂亮的

  • Chris was looking spruce in his stiff-collared black shirt and new short hair cut.

    克里斯穿着硬领黑衫，新理了短发，看起来整洁帅气。

SYN smart, trim, neat, elegant
