disembowel
dɪsɪmbaʊəl ˌdɪsɪmˈbaʊəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disembowels, present participle disembowelling, past tense , past participle disembowelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 disemboweling, disemboweled
1 VERB 取出(人或动物)的内脏;将…开膛破肚 To disembowel a person or animal means to remove their internal organs, especially their stomach, intestines, and bowels. 取出(人或动物)的内脏;将…开膛破肚

  • ...a psychopath who hangs and disembowels his prey. [VERB noun]

    绞死受害者并将其开膛破肚的变态狂

  • It shows a fox being disembowelled by a pack of hounds. [VERB noun]

    画面中一只狐狸正被一群猎狗撕咬得肠子外流。

SYN gut, eviscerate, remove the innards from, draw  
2 VERB 取出…内部的东西;把…掏空 To disembowel something means to take out the inside of it, especially in a such a way that it is destroyed or made completely useless. 取出…内部的东西;把…掏空 [literary]

  • Next, she disembowelled a melon with a quiet fury. [VERB noun]

    接着，她闷着头愤愤地掏空瓜瓤。

  • The interiors were disembowelled and rebuilt. [VERB noun]

    内部全拆后又重建了。
