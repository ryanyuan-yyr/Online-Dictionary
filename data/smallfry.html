small fry
Word forms:
N-UNCOUNT 不重要的小角色;小人物;无关紧要的东西 Small fry is used to refer to someone or something that is considered to be unimportant. 不重要的小角色;小人物;无关紧要的东西

  • What they owe to the Inland Revenue is small fry compared to the overall £1.2 million debt.

    与 120 万英镑的总债务相比，他们所欠国内税收署的那点钱实在不算什么。

  • It's the small fry who are usually the last to get paid.

    小人物往往是最后才拿到酬金。
