café kæfeɪ ˈkæfɪ kæfeɪ
(also cafe)Word forms: plural cafés
1 N-COUNT 小餐馆 A café is a place where you can buy drinks, simple meals, and snacks. 小餐馆
2 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 街边小餐馆 A street café or a sidewalk café is a café which has tables and chairs on the sidewalk outside it where people can eat and drink. 街边小餐馆

  • ...an Italian street café.

    …一家意大利街边小餐馆

  • ...sidewalk cafés and boutiques.

    …街边小餐馆和精品店。
