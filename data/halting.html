halting
hɔːltɪŋ ˈhɔltɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED （通常指因不确定而）结结巴巴的，迟疑不决的 If you speak or do something in a halting way, you speak or do it slowly and with a lot of hesitation, usually because you are uncertain about what to say or do next. （通常指因不确定而）结结巴巴的，迟疑不决的

  • In a halting voice she said that she wished to make a statement.

    她结结巴巴地说自己要发表一项声明。

  • The officer replied in halting German.

    这位官员用结结巴巴的德语作答。

  • Russia's efforts to attract investment have been halting and confused.

    俄罗斯在吸引投资上一直踌躇不前，混乱不清。

SYN faltering, stumbling, awkward, hesitant  
haltingly

  • She spoke haltingly of her deep upset and hurt.

    她吞吞吐吐地诉说自己深深的苦恼及伤痛。

  • Kenya is moving haltingly towards democracy.

    肯尼亚正步履蹒跚地走向民主。
