preoccupy
priɒkjʊpaɪ priˈɑkjuˌpaɪ priˈɑkjəˌpaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense preoccupies, present participle preoccupying, past tense , past participle preoccupied
VERB 使全神贯注；使入神；占据…的思想 If something is preoccupying you, you are thinking about it a lot. 使全神贯注；使入神；占据…的思想

  • Crime and the fear of crime preoccupy the community. [VERB noun]

    犯罪和对犯罪的恐惧一直困扰着这个社区。

  • ...the particular issues, problems and theories that preoccupied him. [VERB noun]

    萦绕在他心头的那些议题、问题和理论

SYN absorb, concern, dominate, occupy
