remarry
riːmæri riːˈmærɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense remarries, present participle remarrying, past tense , past participle remarried
VERB (离婚或丧偶后)再婚 If someone remarries, they marry again after they have obtained a divorce from their previous husband or wife, or after their previous husband or wife has died. (离婚或丧偶后)再婚 [Also VERB n]

  • Her mother had never remarried. [VERB]

    她的母亲一直没有再婚。
