widespread
waɪdspred ˈwaɪdˌsprɛd
ADJ-GRADED 普遍的;广泛的 Something that is widespread exists or happens over a large area, or to a great extent. 普遍的;广泛的

  • There is widespread support for the new proposals.

    新提议获得了广泛的支持。

  • Food shortages are widespread.

    食物短缺的情况很普遍。

SYN common, general, popular, sweeping
