scare off
1 PHRASAL VERB 吓跑 If you scare off or scare away a person or animal, you frighten them so that they go away. 吓跑

  • ...an alarm to scare off an attacker. [VERB PREP. noun]

    用来吓跑袭击者的警报器

  • ...the problem of scaring birds away from airport runways. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把鸟轰离机场跑道的难题

2 PHRASAL VERB 吓退;把…吓跑 If you scare someone off, you accidentally make them unwilling to become involved with you. 吓退;把…吓跑

  • I don't think that revealing your past to your boyfriend scared him off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我并不认为是因为你把自己的过去向男朋友坦白才把他吓跑的。

  • The new Democratic Party is not likely to scare off voters. [VERB PREP. noun]

    新成立的民主党不太可能吓跑选民。
