undisclosed
ʌndɪskloʊzd ˌʌndɪsˈkləʊzd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 未公开的；未公布的；未披露的 Undisclosed information is not revealed to the public. 未公开的；未公布的；未披露的

  • The company has been sold for an undisclosed amount.

    那家公司已被卖掉，具体售价未公开。

  • They are now in hiding at an undisclosed address.

    他们现在藏身于一个秘密的地方。

SYN unrevealed, unpublished, unannounced, secret
