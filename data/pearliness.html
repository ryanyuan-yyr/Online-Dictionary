pearly
pɜːʳli ˈpɜrli
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 珍珠般的 Something that is pearly is pale and shines softly, like a pearl. 珍珠般的

  • ...the pearly light of early morning.

    黎明时柔和的阳光

Pearly is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...pearly pink lipstick.

    有着珍珠光泽的粉红色的口红

  • Her skin was pearly white.

    她的皮肤如珍珠般白皙。
