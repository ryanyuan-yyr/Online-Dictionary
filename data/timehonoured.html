time-honoured
ADJ [ADJ n] 古老的;由来已久的;历史悠久的 A time-honoured tradition or way of doing something is one that has been used and respected for a very long time. 古老的;由来已久的;历史悠久的

  • The beer is brewed in the time-honoured way at the Castle Eden Brewery.

    伊登堡酿酒厂以传统酿制方式酿造啤酒。

SYN long-established, traditional, customary, old
