ratification
rætɪfɪkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural ratifications
N-VAR [usually singular] 批准；认可 The ratification of a treaty or written agreement is the process of ratifying it. 批准；认可

  • The EU will now complete ratification of the treaty by June 1.

    欧盟将于6月1日前完成条约的批准。

SYN approval, sanction, acceptance, confirmation
