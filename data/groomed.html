groomed
gruːmd ɡruːmd ɡrʊmd
ADJ [usually adverb ADJ] 打扮得（干净利落或邋遢）的 You use groomed in expressions such as well groomed and badly groomed to say how neat, clean, and smart a person is. 打扮得（干净利落或邋遢）的

  • ...a very well groomed man.

    衣着整洁得体的男子

  • She always appeared perfectly groomed.

    她看起来总是打扮得干净利落。
