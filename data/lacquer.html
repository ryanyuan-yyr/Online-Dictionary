lacquer
lækəʳ ˈlækər
Word forms: plural lacquers
1 N-MASS （涂在木材或金属上的）漆 Lacquer is a special liquid which is painted on wood or metal in order to protect it and to make it shiny. （涂在木材或金属上的）漆

  • We put on the second coating of lacquer.

    我们上了第二道漆。

  • Only the finest lacquers are used for finishes.

    只有上等好漆才能用作罩面漆。

SYN varnish, wax, glaze, enamel  
2 N-MASS 喷发定型剂；发胶 Lacquer is a clear sticky liquid that you spray on your hair in order to hold it in place. 喷发定型剂；发胶 [British]
