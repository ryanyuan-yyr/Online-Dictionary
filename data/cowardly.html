cowardly
kaʊəʳdli ˈkaʊərdli
ADJ-GRADED 胆小的;懦弱的 If you describe someone as cowardly, you disapprove of them because they are easily frightened and avoid doing dangerous and difficult things. 胆小的;懦弱的 [disapproval]

  • I was too cowardly to complain.

    我太胆小，不敢抱怨。

  • ...a cowardly act of violence.

    懦弱的暴力行为

SYN faint-hearted, scared, spineless, gutless [informal]
