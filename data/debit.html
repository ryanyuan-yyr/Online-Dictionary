debit
debɪt ˈdɛbɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense debits, present participle debiting, past tense , past participle debited
1 VERB 记入（账户）的借方 When your bank debits your account, money is taken from it and paid to someone else. 记入（账户）的借方

  • We will always confirm the revised amount to you in writing before debiting your account. [VERB noun]

    在记入您账户的借方之前，我们总是会就发生变动的数额书面通知您。

SYN pay out from  
2 N-COUNT (银行账户的)借记，借入 A debit is a record of the money taken from your bank account, for example when you write a cheque. (银行账户的)借记，借入

  • The total of debits must balance the total of credits.

    借记总额必须与贷记总额相平衡。

SYN payout, debt, payment, commitment  
3 See also: direct debit
