south
saʊθ saʊθ
(also South)
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] 南;南方 The south is the direction which is on your right when you are looking towards the direction where the sun rises. 南;南方

  • The town lies ten miles to the south of here. [+ of]

    那个小镇位于这里以南 10 英里处。

  • All around him, from east to west, north to south, the stars glittered in the heavens.

    在他的四周，从东到西，从北向南，苍穹里到处繁星闪闪。

2 N-SING (某个地方、国家、区域的)南部 The south of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the south. (某个地方、国家、区域的)南部

  • ...holidays in the south of France. [+ of]

    在法国南部度过的假日

3 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向南方;朝南 If you go south, you travel towards the south. 向南方;朝南

  • We did an extremely fast U-turn and shot south up the Boulevard St. Michel.

    我们飞快地来了个 180 度的转弯，然后沿着圣米歇尔大道向南疾驰而去。

  • He went south to climb Taishan, a mountain sacred to the Chinese.

    他南下去爬泰山了，那是中国人心目中一座神圣的山。

4 ADV 位于…以南;在…南边 Something that is south of a place is positioned to the south of it. 位于…以南;在…南边

  • They now own and operate a farm 50 miles south of Rochester. [+ of]

    他们现在拥有并经营着一家位于罗切斯特以南 50 英里处的农场。

  • I was living in a house just south of Market Street. [+ of]

    我就住在市场街南边的一座房子里。

5 ADJ [ADJ n] 南面的;南部的;南方的 The south edge, corner, or part of a place or country is the part which is towards the south. 南面的;南部的;南方的

  • ...the south coast of Alderney.

    奥尔德尼岛的南部海岸

6 ADJ (用于国家、州或地区等的名称中)南部的 'South' is used in the names of some countries, states, and regions in the south of a larger area. (用于国家、州或地区等的名称中)南部的

  • Next week the President will visit five South American countries in six days.

    总统将在下周的 6 天时间内访问南美洲的 5 个国家。

  • ...the states of Mississippi and South Carolina.

    密西西比州与南卡罗来纳州

7 ADJ (风)来自南方的 A south wind is a wind that blows from the south. (风)来自南方的
8 N-SING 南方国家(指世界上较贫穷、欠发达的国家) The South is used to refer to the poorer, less developed countries of the world. 南方国家(指世界上较贫穷、欠发达的国家)

  • The debate will pit the industrial North against developing countries in the South.

    这次辩论会将形成北方工业国家与南方发展中国家针锋相对的局面。
