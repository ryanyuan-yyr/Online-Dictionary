dissident
dɪsɪdənt ˈdɪsədənt
Word forms: plural dissidents
1 N-COUNT 持不同政见者;异议者 Dissidents are people who disagree with and criticize their government, especially because it is undemocratic. 持不同政见者;异议者

  • ...political dissidents.

    持不同政见者

  • ...former Soviet dissident Natan Sharansky.

    前苏联持不同政见者纳坦·夏兰斯基

SYN protester, rebel, dissenter, demonstrator  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 有不同意见的;持不同政见的 Dissident people disagree with or criticize their government or a powerful organization they belong to. 有不同意见的;持不同政见的

  • ...a dissident Russian novelist.

    持不同政见的俄罗斯小说家

  • She was suspected of having links with a dissident group.

    她被怀疑与一个持不同政见的政治团体有关联。

  • ...dissident shareholders.

    持不同意见的股东

SYN dissenting, disagreeing, nonconformist, heterodox
