silent majority
N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 沉默的大多数(不公开表达自己意见的大部分人) If you believe that, in society or in a particular group, the opinions of most people are very different from the opinions that are most often heard in public, you can refer to these people as the silent majority . 沉默的大多数(不公开表达自己意见的大部分人)

  • ...arguing that a silent majority should never again allow extremists to take control of the country.

    坚决主张沉默的大多数民众绝不应该再次允许极端分子掌控国家

  • The silent majority of supportive parents and teachers should make their views known. [+ of]

    表示支持的父母和老师是沉默的大多数，他们应该公开表达他们的观点。
