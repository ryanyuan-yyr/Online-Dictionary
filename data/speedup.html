speed up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 加快(…的)速度 When something speeds up or when you speed it up, it moves or travels faster. 加快(…的)速度 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • You notice that your breathing has speeded up a bit. [VERB PREP.]

    你注意到自己的呼吸加快了一些。

  • He pushed a lever that speeded up the car. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他挂挡加快了速度。

2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)(某一过程或活动)加快 When a process or activity speeds up or when something speeds it up, it happens at a faster rate. (使)(某一过程或活动)加快

  • Job losses are speeding up. [VERB PREP.]

    失业趋势正在不断加快。

  • I had already taken steps to speed up a solution to the problem. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我已经采取措施加快解决该问题。

  • I kept praying that the DJ would speed the music up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我不停地祈望唱片播放师能加快音乐的节奏。
