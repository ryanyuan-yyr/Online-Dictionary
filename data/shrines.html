shrine
ʃraɪn ʃraɪn
Word forms: plural shrines
1 N-COUNT 圣地;圣祠;神庙;神龛 A shrine is a place of worship which is associated with a particular holy person or object. 圣地;圣祠;神庙;神龛

  • ...the holy shrine of Mecca. [+ of]

    麦加圣地

SYN holy place, temple, sanctuary, altar  
2 N-COUNT (缅怀逝者的)纪念之地，圣地 A shrine is a place that people visit and treat with respect because it is connected with a dead person or with dead people that they want to remember. (缅怀逝者的)纪念之地，圣地

  • The monument has been turned into a shrine to the dead and the missing. [+ to]

    这座纪念碑已成为人们纪念那些死者和失踪者的神圣之地。

SYN memorial, tribute, cairn, monument
