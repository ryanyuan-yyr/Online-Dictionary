station house
Word forms: station houses
N-COUNT 派出所; 消防站A station house is a police station or a fire station, or a building that is attached to a police station or a fire station. 派出所; 消防站 [US]

  • They were taken in a police van to the 60th Precinct station house for the lineup.

    他们被警车拉往第60警区派出所以待进行嫌疑犯列队辨认。

  • Both the station house and the fire truck were destroyed.

    消防站和救火车都被摧毁了。
