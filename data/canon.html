canon
kænən ˈkænən
Word forms: plural canons
1 N-COUNT (大教堂的)咏礼司铎 A canon is a member of the clergy who is on the staff of a cathedral. (大教堂的)咏礼司铎
2 N-COUNT 精品集;真作集 A canon of texts is a list of them that is accepted as genuine or important. 精品集;真作集 [formal]

  • He had to read a canon of accepted literary texts. [+ of]

    他不得不阅读一系列公认的经典文学作品。

  • ...the Irish literary canon.

    正宗的爱尔兰文学作品

3 N-COUNT 规范;规则;原则 A canon is a general rule or principle. 规范;规则;原则 [formal]

  • The very first canon of nursing is to keep the air inside as fresh as the air outside.

    护理工作的首要原则是要保持室内空气像室外一样清新。

SYN list, index, catalogue, syllabus
