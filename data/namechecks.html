namecheck
neɪmtʃek ˈneɪmˌtʃɛk
(also name-check)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense namechecks, present participle namechecking, past tense , past participle namechecked
N-COUNT 列出名字;提到名字 If someone gets a namecheck in something such as an article or interview, their name is mentioned in it. 列出名字;提到名字

  • She has had many credits and namechecks in American Vogue.

    美国版《时尚》多次对她进行赞扬或是提到她的名字。

Namecheck is also a verb.

  • Several bands have namechecked Lee Hazelwood in interviews.

    在采访中，几个乐队都提到了李·黑兹尔伍德的名字。
