congestion
kəndʒestʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 堵塞;拥堵 If there is congestion in a place, the place is extremely crowded and blocked with traffic or people. 堵塞;拥堵

  • The problems of traffic congestion will not disappear in a hurry.

    交通拥堵问题不会一下子就消失。

  • Energy consumption, congestion and pollution have increased.

    能源消耗、交通堵塞以及污染状况愈加严重。

SYN overcrowding, crowding, mass, jam  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft adjective NOUN] (身体部位的)堵塞，充血，淤血 Congestion in a part of the body is a medical condition in which the part becomes blocked. (身体部位的)堵塞，充血，淤血 [formal]
