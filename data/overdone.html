overdone
oʊvəʳdʌn ˌəʊvəˈdʌn
1 ADJ-GRADED 煮得过久的；煮得太烂的 If food is overdone, it has been spoiled by being cooked for too long. 煮得过久的；煮得太烂的

  • The meat was overdone and the vegetables disappointing.

    肉煮得太老，蔬菜也让人没有胃口。

SYN overcooked, burnt, spoiled, dried up  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 做得过分的；做得夸张的 If you say that something is overdone, you mean that you think it is excessive or exaggerated. 做得过分的；做得夸张的

  • In fact, the panic is overdone. As the map shows, the drought has been confined to the south and east of Britain.

    事实上，人们有些过于恐慌。如地图所示，干旱仅限于英国的南部和东部地区。

SYN excessive, too much, unfair, unnecessary
