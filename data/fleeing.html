flee
fliː fli
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense flees, present participle fleeing, past tense , past participle fled
VERB [no passive] 逃离;逃避 If you flee from something or someone, or flee a person or thing, you escape from them. 逃离;逃避 [written]

  • He slammed the bedroom door behind him and fled. [VERB]

    他把卧室房门重重地关上，然后逃跑了。

  • He fled to Costa Rica to avoid military service. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他逃到了哥斯达黎加以逃避服兵役。

  • ...refugees who have fled from wars, famine and persecution. [VERB prep./adv.]

    逃避战争、饥荒和迫害的难民

  • ...refugees fleeing persecution or torture. [VERB noun]

    躲避迫害和酷刑的难民

  • Thousands have been compelled to flee the country in makeshift boats. [VERB noun]

    数以千计的人被迫乘坐简易船只逃离了这个国家。

SYN run away, leave, escape, bolt
