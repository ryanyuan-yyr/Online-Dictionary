reuse
riːjuːz riːjuːs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reuses, present participle reusing, past tense , past participle reused
The verb is pronounced /riː'juːz/. The noun is pronounced /riː'juːs/. 动词读作/riː'juːz/。名词读作/riː'juːs/。
VERB 再使用;重复使用;重新利用 When you reuse something, you use it again instead of throwing it away. 再使用;重复使用;重新利用

  • Try where possible to reuse paper. [VERB noun]

    尽可能重复使用纸张。

Reuse is also a noun.

  • Copper, brass and aluminium are separated and remelted for reuse.

    铜、黄铜和铝被分门别类重新熔化以进行再利用。
