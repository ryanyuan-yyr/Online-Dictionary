muck in
PHRASAL VERB 一起干活;一起出力;参加 If someone mucks in, they join in with an activity or help other people with a job and do not consider themselves to be too important to do it. 一起干活;一起出力;参加 [mainly British, informal] [Also V P together]

  • Course residents are expected to muck in and be prepared to share rooms. [VERB PREP.]

    场地附近的居民也将会加入并分享自己的住处。

  • She mucked in with the chores and did her own washing and ironing. [VERB PREP. + with]

    她也一起做些家务杂事，并自己洗熨衣服。

  • Ian was never afraid to take his coat off and muck in with everybody else. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    伊恩从不怕甩开膀子和大家一起干活。
