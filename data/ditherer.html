dither
dɪðəʳ ˈdɪðər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dithers, present participle dithering, past tense , past participle dithered
VERB 犹豫不决;踌躇 When someone dithers, they hesitate because they are unable to make a quick decision about something. 犹豫不决;踌躇 [Also VERB]

  • We're still dithering over whether to marry. [VERB + over]

    我们仍在犹豫要不要结婚。

  • If you have been dithering about buying shares, now could be the time to do it. [VERB + about]

    如果你一直在犹豫是否要买股票，现在或许是买的时候了。

SYN vacillate, hesitate, waver, haver
