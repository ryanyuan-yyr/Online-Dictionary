ricochet
rɪkəʃeɪ ˈrɪkəˌʃɛt ˈrɪkəˌʃɛt -ʃeɪ ˈrɪkəˌʃeɪ ˌ rɪkəˈʃeɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ricochets, present participle ricocheting, past tense , past participle ricocheted
VERB (子弹)反弹，弹回 When a bullet ricochets, it hits a surface and bounces away from it. (子弹)反弹，弹回 [Also VERB]

  • The bullets ricocheted off the bonnet and windscreen. [VERB prep./adv.]

    子弹从汽车引擎盖和挡风玻璃上弹飞了。

Ricochet is also a noun.

  • He was wounded in the shoulder by a ricochet.

    他被一颗跳弹打伤了肩膀。
