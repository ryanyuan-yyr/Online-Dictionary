-ful -fʊl fəl fʊl
Word forms: plural -fuls
SUFFIX (用于构成名词)表示“充满…所需的量” You use -ful to form nouns that refer to the quantity of a substance that an object contains or can contain. For example, a handful of sand is the amount of sand that you can hold in your hand. (用于构成名词)表示“充满…所需的量”

  • ...a spoonful of brown sugar.
