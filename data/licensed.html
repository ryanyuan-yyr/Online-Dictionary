licensed
laɪsənst ˈlaɪsənst
1 ADJ [oft ADJ to-infinitive] 得到许可的；领有执照的 If you are licensed to do something, you have official permission from the government or from the authorities to do it. 得到许可的；领有执照的

  • There were about 250 people on board, about 100 more than the ferry was licensed to carry.

    渡船上大约有250名乘客，超出了核定载客量约100名。

  • ...a licensed doctor.

    领有执照的医生

2 ADJ 获准拥有（或使用）的 If something that you own or use is licensed, you have official permission to own it or use it. 获准拥有（或使用）的

  • While searching the house they discovered an unlicensed shotgun and a licensed rifle.

    搜查房子时，他们找到了一把无牌猎枪和一把有牌步枪。

3 ADJ 领有售酒执照的；特许销售酒类的 If a place such as a restaurant or hotel is licensed, it has been given a licence to sell alcoholic drinks. 领有售酒执照的；特许销售酒类的 [British]

  • ...licensed premises.

    领有售酒执照的经营场所

  • The restaurant is licensed, offering draught beers, wines, spirits and soft drinks.

    这家餐馆有酒类经营执照，提供扎啤、葡萄酒、烈性酒和汽水。
