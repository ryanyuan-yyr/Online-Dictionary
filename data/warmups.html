warm up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 加热;使暖和;变暖和 If you warm something up or if it warms up, it gets hotter. 加热;使暖和;变暖和

  • He blew on his hands to warm them up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他冲着双手哈气，好让手暖和些。

  • All that she would have to do was warm up the pudding. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她所需要做的就是把布丁加热。

  • The weather had warmed up. [VERB PREP.]

    天气已经转暖。

2 PHRASAL VERB (为体育比赛) 热身，做准备活动 If you warm up for an event such as a race, you prepare yourself for it by doing exercises or by practising just before it starts. (为体育比赛) 热身，做准备活动

  • In an hour the drivers will be warming up for the main event. [VERB PREP.]

    一个小时后车手们将做好准备参加这项重要的赛事。

  • Carl slipped a disc in his back while warming up. [VERB PREP.]

    热身时卡尔腰椎间盘不慎错位了。

3See also:warm-up 
4 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)(机器或引擎)预热;(使)启动 When a machine or engine warms up or someone warms it up, it becomes ready for use a little while after being switched on or started. (使)(机器或引擎)预热;(使)启动 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He waited for his car to warm up. [VERB PREP.]

    他等着汽车预热。

  • We spent a frustrating five minutes while the pilot warmed up the engines. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们沮丧地等了5分钟，让飞行员预热引擎。

5 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)活跃起来;(使)兴奋起来;暖场 If a comedian or speaker warms up an audience or the audience warms up, the audience is prepared for the main show or speaker by being told jokes, so that they are in a good mood. (使)活跃起来;(使)兴奋起来;暖场

  • They would always come out and warm up the audience. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们总是会出来调动观众情绪。

  • The crowd began to warm up. [VERB PREP.]

    人群开始活跃起来。

warm-up ˈwɔːmˌʌp ˈwɔrmˌʌp
Word forms: plural warm-ups
N-COUNT [usually singular, NOUN n] 准备活动;热身活动 A warm-up is something that prepares you for an activity or event, usually because it is a short practice or example of what the activity or event will involve. 准备活动;热身活动

  • The exercises can be fun and a good warm-up for the latter part of the programme. [+ for]

    这些练习很有趣，而且为该课程后面的部分做了很好的铺垫。

  • The criticism was merely a warm-up for what is being prepared for the finance minister. [+ for]

    对于财政部长即将面临的困境而言，这些批评只是个小小的开始。

  • In a warm-up game for the World Cup, Uruguay have beaten England.

    在世界杯热身赛中，乌拉圭队打败了英格兰队。
