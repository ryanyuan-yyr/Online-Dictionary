foray
fɒreɪ fɔːreɪ ˈfɔreɪ
Word forms: plural forays
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 初次尝试;涉足 If you make a foray into a new or unfamiliar type of activity, you start to become involved in it. 初次尝试;涉足

  • Emporio Armani, the Italian fashion house, has made a discreet foray into furnishings. [+ into]

    意大利时装品牌安普里奥·阿玛尼谨慎进入家居用品市场。

  • ...her first forays into politics. [+ into]

    她首次涉足政界

SYN raid, sally, incursion, inroad  
2 N-COUNT 短暂旅行;闪电造访 You can refer to a short journey that you make as a foray if it seems to involve excitement or risk, for example because it is to an unfamiliar place or because you are looking for a particular thing. 短暂旅行;闪电造访

  • Most guests make at least one foray into the town. [+ into/to]

    多数客人至少去过一次城里。

  • A foray to your supermarket will supply all the ready-made foods for an excellent picnic.

    去一趟超市就可以将一次完美野餐所需的现成食物置办妥当。

3 N-COUNT 突袭;闪电式袭击 If a group of soldiers make a foray into enemy territory, they make a quick attack there, and then return to their own territory. 突袭;闪电式袭击

  • These base camps were used by the PKK guerrillas to make forays into Turkey. [+ into]

    这些基地训练营被库尔德工人党游击队用于对土耳其实施突袭行动。
