largesse
lɑːʳʒes lɑrˈdʒɛs ˈlɑrdʒɪs
in AM, use 美国英语用 largess
N-UNCOUNT 慷慨赠予（或捐助）；善举 Largesse is a generous gift of money or a generous act of kindness. 慷慨赠予（或捐助）；善举 [formal]

  • ...grateful recipients of their largesse.

    对他们的慷慨解囊心存感激的受捐者

  • ...his most recent act of largesse.

    他最近的一次善举

SYN generosity, charity, bounty, philanthropy
