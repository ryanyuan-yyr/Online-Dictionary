sterile
steraɪl ˈstɛrˌaɪl -rəl ˈstɛrəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 消过毒的;无菌的 Something that is sterile is completely clean and free from germs. 消过毒的;无菌的

  • He always made sure that any cuts were protected by sterile dressings.

    他总是坚持要用无菌纱布包扎伤口。

  • Urine is sterile.

    尿液是无菌的。

SYN germ-free, antiseptic, sterilized, disinfected  
sterility

  • ...the antiseptic sterility of the hospital.

    医院的无菌消毒

2 ADJ 无生殖能力的;不育的 A person or animal that is sterile is unable to have or produce babies. 无生殖能力的;不育的

  • George was sterile.

    乔治没有生育能力。

  • ...a sterile male.

    没有生育能力的男子

SYN barren, infertile, unproductive, childless  
sterility

  • This disease causes sterility in both males and females.

    该病会导致男女不孕不育。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 无生气的;缺乏新意的 A sterile situation is lacking in energy and new ideas. 无生气的;缺乏新意的 [disapproval]

  • Too much time has been wasted in sterile debate.

    在毫无新意的辩论上已经浪费了太多时间。

SYN unproductive, fruitless, unprofitable, empty  
sterility

  • ...the sterility of Dorothea's life in industry.

    多罗西娅在业界毫无生气的生活
