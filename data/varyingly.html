vary
veəri ˈvɛərɪ ˈvɛri ˈværi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense varies, present participle varying, past tense , past participle varied
1 VERB 不同;有差异 If things vary, they are different from each other in size, amount, or degree. 不同;有差异

  • As they're handmade, each one varies slightly. [VERB]

    由于它们是手工制作的，彼此都会有些微小的差异。

  • The text varies from the earlier versions. [VERB + from]

    这一文本与较早的版本有些不同。

  • Different writers will prepare to varying degrees. [VERB-ing]

    不同作者的准备程度也会各不相同。

SYN differ, be different, be dissimilar, disagree  
2 V-ERG （使）变化;改变 If something varies or if you vary it, it becomes different or changed. （使）变化;改变

  • The cost of the alcohol duty varies according to the amount of wine in the bottle. [VERB]

    酒税税额随酒瓶容量的大小而有所不同。

  • You are welcome to vary the diet. [VERB noun]

    我们鼓励不时变换饮食。

3 See also: varied
