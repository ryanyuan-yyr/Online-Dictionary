dilettante
dɪlətænti ˌdɪlɪˈtɑːntɪ -tɑːnt ˈdɪləˌtɑnt ˈdɪləˌtænt ˌ dɪləˈtɑnt ˈdɪləˈtænt ˌ dɪləˈtɑnti ˌdɪləˈtænti
Word forms: plural dilettantes, plural dilettanti
N-COUNT （尤指艺术方面的）半吊子，浅薄涉猎者 You can use dilettante to talk about someone who seems interested in a subject, especially in art, but who does not really know very much about it. （尤指艺术方面的）半吊子，浅薄涉猎者 [formal, disapproval]
SYN amateur, aesthete, dabbler, trifler
