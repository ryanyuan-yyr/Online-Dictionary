prolapse
proʊlæps prəlæps proʊlæps proʊˈlæps ˈproʊˌlæps
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense prolapses, present participle prolapsing, past tense , past participle prolapsed
The verb is also pronounced /prə'læps/. 动词亦读作 /prə'læps/。
1 N-VAR 下垂；脱垂 A prolapse is when one of the organs in the body moves down from its normal position. 下垂；脱垂 [medicine]

  • One complication which can arise is a prolapse.

    可能出现的一种并发症是脱垂。

  • ...the causes and treatment of uterine prolapse.

    子宫下垂的病因和治疗

2 VERB 下垂；脱垂 If an organ in someone's body prolapses, it moves down from its normal position. 下垂；脱垂 [medicine]

  • Sometimes the original abortion was done so badly that the uterus prolapsed. [VERB]

    有时第一次流产手术做得太糟糕，会导致子宫下垂。
