intensify
ɪntensɪfaɪ ɪnˈtɛnsəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense intensifies, present participle intensifying, past tense , past participle intensified
1 V-ERG 加强;强化;加剧 If you intensify something or if it intensifies, it becomes greater in strength, amount, or degree. 加强;强化;加剧

  • Britain is intensifying its efforts to secure the release of the hostages. [VERB noun]

    英国正加紧努力确保人质的获释。

  • The conflict is almost bound to intensify. [VERB]

    冲突几乎注定会加剧。

  • Groups of refugees are on the move following intensified fighting in the region. [VERB-ed]

    该地区战事加剧之后,大批难民开始逃离。

SYN escalate, increase, extend, widen  
intensification
