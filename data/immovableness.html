immovable
ɪmuːvəbəl ɪˈmuvəbəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不可移动的;固定的 An immovable object is fixed and cannot be moved. 不可移动的;固定的
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 坚定不移的;坚决的 If someone is immovable in their attitude to something, they will not change their mind. 坚定不移的;坚决的

  • On one issue, however, she was immovable.

    但是在一个问题上她态度坚决。

SYN inflexible, adamant, resolute, steadfast
