blonde
blɒnd blɑnd
Word forms: plural blondes, comparative blonder, superlative blondest
The form blonde is usually used to refer to women, and blond to refer to men. blonde通常用于指女性，而blond通常用于指男性。
1 COLOUR （头发）金色，浅棕色，浅黄色 A woman who has blonde hair has pale-coloured hair. Blonde hair can be very light brown or light yellow. The form blond is used when describing men. （头发）金色，浅棕色，浅黄色

  • There were two little girls, one Asian and one with blonde hair.

    那里有两个小女孩，一个是亚洲女孩，一个是金发女孩。

  • The baby had blond curls.

    那个婴儿有一头金色卷发。

2 ADJ-GRADED 金发的；浅色头发的 Someone who is blonde has blonde hair. 金发的；浅色头发的

  • She was tall, blonde, and attractive.

    她个子高挑，一头金发，非常迷人。

  • He was blonder than his brother.

    他的头发颜色比他哥哥的浅。

  • ...the striking blond actor.

    英俊的金发男演员

SYN fair-haired, golden-haired, tow-headed  
3 N-COUNT 金发女郎 A blonde is a woman who has blonde hair. 金发女郎
