distressing
dɪstresɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 使人痛苦的;令人苦恼的;使人忧虑的 If something is distressing, it upsets you or worries you. 使人痛苦的;令人苦恼的;使人忧虑的

  • It is very distressing to see your baby attached to tubes and monitors.

    看见自己宝宝身上连着各种管子和监视器，心里会非常痛苦。

  • Tranquillizers help alleviate the distressing symptoms of anxiety.

    镇静剂有助于缓解焦虑所带来的不安症状。

SYN upsetting, worrying, disturbing, painful  
distressingly

  • ...a distressingly large bloodstain.

    惊人的大片血迹
