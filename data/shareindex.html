share index
Word forms: plural share indices, plural share indexes
N-COUNT 股票价格指数;股指 A share index is a number that indicates the state of a stock market. It is based on the combined share prices of a set of companies. 股票价格指数;股指 [business]

  • The FT 30 share index was up 16.4 points to 1,599.6.

    《金融时报》30股指上涨16.4点，收于1,559.6 点。
