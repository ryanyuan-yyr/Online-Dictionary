come out with
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 说出，提出(尤指令人惊讶的话) If you come out with a remark, especially a surprising one, you make it. 说出，提出(尤指令人惊讶的话)

  • Everyone who heard it just burst out laughing when he came out with it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当他说出这句话时，听到的人都大笑起来。

  • What was that marvellous quote that she came out with? [VERB PREP. noun]

    她引用的那句妙语是什么来着？

SYN say, speak, utter, let out
