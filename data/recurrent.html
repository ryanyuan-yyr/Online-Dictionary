recurrent
rɪkʌrənt -kɜːr- rɪˈkɜrənt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 重现的；再次发生的 A recurrent event or feeling happens or is experienced more than once. 重现的；再次发生的

  • Race is a recurrent theme in the work.

    种族问题是该作品里多次出现的主题。

  • ...buildings in which staff suffer recurrent illness.

    里面的员工经常生病的建筑

SYN periodic, continued, regular, repeated
