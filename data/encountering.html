encounter
ɪnkaʊntəʳ ɛnˈkaʊntər ɪnˈkaʊntər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense encounters, present participle encountering, past tense , past participle encountered
1 VERB 遇到，遭遇（问题、困难） If you encounter problems or difficulties, you experience them. 遇到，遭遇（问题、困难）

  • Every day of our lives we encounter stresses of one kind or another. [VERB noun]

    我们在每天的生活中会面临这样或那样的压力。

  • Environmental problems they found in Poland were among the worst they encountered. [VERB noun]

    他们在波兰发现的环境问题是他们遇到过的最严重的。

SYN experience, meet, face, suffer  
2 VERB 偶遇;邂逅 If you encounter someone, you meet them, usually unexpectedly. 偶遇;邂逅 [formal]

  • Did you encounter anyone in the building?. [VERB noun]

    你在大楼里遇到谁了么？

  • Renata wrote him that she had encountered her long-estranged father. [VERB noun]

    雷娜塔写信告诉他说她碰到了分离多年的父亲。

3 N-COUNT 偶遇;邂逅;(重要的)相逢 An encounter with someone is a meeting with them, particularly one that is unexpected or significant. 偶遇;邂逅;(重要的)相逢

  • The author tells of a remarkable encounter with a group of South Vietnamese soldiers. [+ with]

    作者讲述了和一群南越士兵的奇遇。

4 N-COUNT 特殊经历;特殊体验 An encounter is a particular type of experience. 特殊经历;特殊体验

  • ...a sexual encounter.

    性体验

  • ...his first serious encounter with alcohol. [+ with]

    他第一次正儿八经喝酒的经历
