frost
frɒst frɔːst frɔst frɑst
Word forms: plural frosts
1 N-VAR 霜;霜冻;严寒天气 When there is frost or a frost, the temperature outside falls below freezing point and the ground becomes covered in ice crystals. 霜;霜冻;严寒天气

  • There is frost on the ground and snow is forecast.

    地上结霜了，而且预报说会有雪。

  • The wind had veered to north, bringing clear skies and a keen frost.

    风向已经转北，带来了晴朗的天空和严重的霜冻。

SYN hoarfrost, freeze, freeze-up, Jack Frost  
2 PHRASE 冰点以下…度;零下…度 When someone says that there are a particular number of degrees of frost they mean that the temperature is that number of degrees below freezing point. 冰点以下…度;零下…度 [British]

  • We had 11 degrees of frost last night.

    昨天夜里气温降到了零下 11 度。
