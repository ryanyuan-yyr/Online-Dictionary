hoodie
hʊdɪ ˈhʊdɪ ˈhʊdi
1 连帽衫;带帽夹克 A hoodie is a type of casual jacket with a hood. 连帽衫;带帽夹克

  • She wore jeans and a hoodie.

    她穿着牛仔裤和连帽衫。

2 穿着连帽衫的小混混 A hoodie is a young person wearing a hoodie, thought by some people to be badly behaved or possibly criminal. 穿着连帽衫的小混混

  • He said he had felt threatened by a group of hoodies outside a shop.

    他说自己在一家商店外面受到一群小混混的威胁。
