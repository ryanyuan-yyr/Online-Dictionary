N-COUNT [usu sing] (公司或组织的)对外办公室; 也指高级行政人员The front office of a company or other organization is the room or rooms where staff deal with the public. The executives of a company or other organization are sometimes referred to collectively as the front office. (公司或组织的)对外办公室; 也指高级行政人员 [US]
