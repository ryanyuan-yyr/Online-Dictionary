labourer
leɪbərəʳ ˈleɪbərə
Word forms: plural labourers
in AM, use 美国英语用 laborer
N-COUNT 体力劳动者；劳工 A labourer is a person who does a job which involves a lot of hard physical work. 体力劳动者；劳工

  • He has worked as a labourer in factories and on building sites.

    他在工厂和建筑工地干过苦工。

  • Her husband had been a farm labourer.

    她丈夫以前是个农场雇工。

SYN worker, workman, working man, manual worker
