gymnastics
dʒɪmnæstɪks ˈdʒɪmnæstɪks
The form gymnastic is used as a modifier. gymnastic用作修饰语。
1 N-UNCOUNT 体操；体操训练 Gymnastics consists of physical exercises that develop your strength, co-ordination, and ease of movement. 体操；体操训练

  • ...the British Amateur Gymnastics Association.

    英国业余体操协会

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 体操的 Gymnastic is used to describe things relating to gymnastics. 体操的

  • ...gymnastic exercises.

    体操训练

3 N-UNCOUNT [adjective NOUN] 技巧；绝技 You can use gymnastics to refer to activities which require skills such as speed and adaptability. 技巧；绝技

  • Hers is the kind of voice that excels at vocal gymnastics.

    她的嗓音擅长演绎有难度的歌曲。

  • They are the only ones whose brains are supple enough for the mental gymnastics required.

    他们是仅有的那些头脑足够灵活来应对这些需要动脑筋的事的人。
