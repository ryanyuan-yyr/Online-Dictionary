chickenshit
tʃɪkɪnʃɪt ˈtʃɪkɪnʃɪt ˈtʃɪkənˌʃɪt
1 ADJ 没用的If you say that someone or something is chickenshit, you mean that they are worthless. 没用的 [US, informal, vulgar] [US, vulgar]

  • I don't want a bunch of nerds telling me whether to take a limo or not. That's all chickenshit.

    我可不要一群讨厌鬼来告诉我要不要去坐豪华轿车。那些都是废话。

  • You think I'm chickenshit, don't you?

    你认为我分文不值？不是吗？

2 ADJ 胆小的If you say that someone is chickenshit, you mean that they are cowardly. 胆小的 [US, vulgar]

  • ...djs who are too chickenshit to play experimental music.

    ...太过胆小而不敢放实验音乐的流行乐节目主持人们。
