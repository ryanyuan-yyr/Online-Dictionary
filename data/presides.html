preside
prɪzaɪd priˈzaɪd prɪˈzaɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense presides, present participle presiding, past tense , past participle presided
1 VERB 负责；主持 If you preside over a meeting or an event, you are in charge. 负责；主持

  • The PM presided over a meeting of his inner Cabinet. [V + over/at]

    首相主持了一次核心内阁会议。

  • He presided at the trial of the Maguire Seven. [V over/at n]

    他主审马圭尔等七人案。

  • The presiding officer ruled that the motion was out of order. [VERB-ing]

    主管官员裁定这项动议不符合议事规程。

SYN officiate, chair, moderate, be chairperson
