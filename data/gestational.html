gestation
dʒesteɪʃən dʒɛˈsteɪʃən dʒɛsˈteɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 怀孕（期）；妊娠（期） Gestation is the process in which babies grow inside their mother's body before they are born. 怀孕（期）；妊娠（期） [technical]

  • ...the seventeenth week of gestation.

    妊娠第17周

  • The gestation period can be anything between 95 and 150 days.

    妊娠期从95天至150天不等。

SYN incubation, development, growth, pregnancy  
2 N-UNCOUNT （想法等的）构思，酝酿，孕育 Gestation is the process in which an idea or plan develops. （想法等的）构思，酝酿，孕育 [formal]

  • ...the prolonged period of gestation of this design. [+ of]

    这项设计漫长的构思阶段

SYN development, progress, evolution, progression
