bid up
PHRASAL VERB 抬高…的价格 If someone bids up the value of something, they try to increase it, for example by offering to buy it at a higher price than usual. 抬高…的价格

  • ...the British passion for bidding up the price of each other's houses. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    英国人哄抬彼此房价的热情。

  • They agreed to bid the picture up to 4,500 francs. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们同意将这幅画的价格提至4,500法郎。
