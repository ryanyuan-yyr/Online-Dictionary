doublespeak
dʌbəlspiːk ˈdʌbəlˌspik
N-UNCOUNT 含糊其辞的话;欺人之谈 If you refer to what someone says as doublespeak, you are criticizing them for presenting things in a way that is intended to hide the truth or give people the wrong idea. 含糊其辞的话;欺人之谈 [disapproval]

  • ...the doublespeak so fluently used by governments and their press offices.

    被政府及其新闻办公室运用自如的欺人之谈
