anomalous
ənɒmələs əˈnɑmələs
ADJ-GRADED 反常的；异常的；例外的 Something that is anomalous is different from what is usual or expected. 反常的；异常的；例外的 [formal]

  • For years this anomalous behaviour has baffled scientists.

    几年来这种反常行为让科学家们很困惑。

  • His position here is anomalous.

    他在此的身份很不寻常。

SYN unusual, odd, rare, bizarre
