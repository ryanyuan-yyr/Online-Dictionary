greying
greɪɪŋ ˈɡreɪɪŋ
in AM, use 美国英语用 graying
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （头发）灰白的，花白的 If someone has greying hair, there is a lot of grey hair mixed with the person's natural colour. （头发）灰白的，花白的

  • ...a stern woman with greying hair.

    头发花白的严厉女人

  • He was a smallish, greying man, with a wrinkly face.

    他是一个满脸皱纹、头发灰白的小个子男人。
