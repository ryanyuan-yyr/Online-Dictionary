-ity -ɪti əti ɪti
Word forms: plural -ities
SUFFIX (加在形容词后,有时替换 -ious 以构成名词)表示“…状态”，“…特征”，“…行为” -ity is added to adjectives, sometimes in place of '-ious', to form nouns referring to the state, quality, or behaviour described by the adjective. (加在形容词后,有时替换 -ious 以构成名词)表示“…状态”，“…特征”，“…行为”

  • I, for one, admire his audacity.

    比如说，我就很钦佩他的胆识。

  • He enjoyed the tranquillity of village life.

    他非常享受乡村生活的宁静。

  • ...life with all its contradictions and complexities.

    充满矛盾和复杂因素的生活
