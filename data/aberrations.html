aberration
æbəreɪʃən ˌæbəˈreɪʃən ˌæbərˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural aberrations
1 N-VAR 偶发事件;非典型行为 An aberration is an incident or way of behaving that is not typical. 偶发事件;非典型行为 [formal]

  • It became very clear that the incident was not just an aberration, it was not just a single incident.

    事实已经很清楚，这件事不是偶发的，它并不是一起孤立的事件。

SYN anomaly, exception, defect, abnormality  
2 N-VAR 不正常的怪人；异常行为 If someone considers a person or their behaviour to be an aberration, they think that they are strange and not socially acceptable. 不正常的怪人；异常行为 [formal, disapproval]

  • Single people are treated as an aberration and made to pay a supplement.

    独身者被当成异类，而且要付一笔额外的费用。

  • ...sexual aberration.

    性变态

SYN oddity, abnormality, rarity, eccentricity
