edgy
edʒi ˈɛdʒi
Word forms: comparative edgier, superlative edgiest
ADJ-GRADED 紧张的;烦躁不安的;心神不宁的 If someone is edgy, they are nervous and anxious, and seem likely to lose control of themselves. 紧张的;烦躁不安的;心神不宁的 [informal]

  • She was nervous and edgy, still chain-smoking.

    她紧张不安，还在一根接一根地抽着烟。

SYN nervous, wired [slang] , anxious, tense
