complaisant
kəmpleɪzənt kəmˈpleɪzənt kəmpleɪsənt ˈkɑmpləˌzænt
ADJ-GRADED 顺从的;殷勤的 If you are complaisant, you are willing to accept what other people are doing without complaining. 顺从的;殷勤的 [old-fashioned]

  • ...his pretty and complaisant wife.

    他漂亮又温顺的妻子

SYN obliging, accommodating, polite, conciliatory
