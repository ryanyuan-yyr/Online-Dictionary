diphthong
dɪfθɒŋ dɪp- ˈdɪfθɒŋ ˈdɪfθɔŋ ˈdɪpθɔŋ
Word forms: plural diphthongs
N-COUNT 复合元音;双元音 A diphthong is a vowel in which the speaker's tongue changes position while it is being pronounced, so that the vowel sounds like a combination of two other vowels. The vowel sound in 'tail' is a diphthong. 复合元音;双元音
