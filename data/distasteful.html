distasteful
dɪsteɪstfʊl ˈdɪsˈteɪstfəl ˈdɪsˌteɪstfəl
ADJ-GRADED 使人不愉快的;令人反感的;讨厌的 If something is distasteful to you, you think it is unpleasant, disgusting, or immoral. 使人不愉快的;令人反感的;讨厌的

  • He found it distasteful to be offered drinks before witnessing the execution. [+ to]

    他觉得在目睹行刑前还要上酒让人很不舒服。

SYN unpleasant, offensive, obscene, undesirable
