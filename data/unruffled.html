unruffled
ʌnrʌfəld ʌnˈrʌfəld
ADJ-GRADED 沉着的；处乱不惊的 If you describe someone as unruffled, you mean that they are calm and do not seem to be affected by surprising or frightening events. 沉着的；处乱不惊的

  • Anne had remained unruffled, very cool and controlled.

    安妮处乱不惊，十分冷静沉着。

SYN calm, cool, collected, peaceful
