furry
fɜːri ˈfɜri
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (动物)毛皮覆盖的，毛茸茸的 A furry animal is covered with thick, soft hair. (动物)毛皮覆盖的，毛茸茸的

  • ...the coyote's furry tail.

    郊狼毛茸茸的尾巴

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 毛皮般柔软的 If you describe something as furry, you mean that it has a soft rough texture like fur. 毛皮般柔软的

  • ...his herringbone tweed coat with its furry lining.

    他那件带毛绒衬里的人字纹粗花呢外套

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (通常指因生病而)长(舌)苔的 If you have a furry tongue, your tongue is covered with a layer of a soft greyish-white substance, usually because you are unwell. (通常指因生病而)长(舌)苔的
