mummy
mʌmi ˈmʌmi
Word forms: plural mummies
1 N-FAMILY (尤指儿语)妈咪，妈妈 Some people, especially young children, call their mother mummy . (尤指儿语)妈咪，妈妈 [British, informal]

  • I want my mummy.

    我要找妈妈。

  • Mummy, I'm tired!.

    妈妈，我累了。

  • Mummy says I can play out in the garden.

    妈妈说我可以到外面花园里玩。

in AM, use 美国英语用 mommy
2 N-COUNT 木乃伊 A mummy is a dead body which was preserved long ago by being rubbed with special oils and wrapped in cloth. 木乃伊

  • ...an Egyptian mummy.

    埃及木乃伊
