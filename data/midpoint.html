midpoint
mɪdpɔɪnt ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnt
(also mid-point)
1 N-SING 中点;中心位置 The midpoint between two things is the point that is the same distance from both things. 中点;中心位置

  • ...the midpoint between Paris and Warsaw. [+ between/of]

    巴黎和华沙之间的中点位置

  • ...the midpoint of the front of your house.

    房子前院的中心位置

2 N-SING （事件进程的）中间，一半 The midpoint of an event is the time halfway between the beginning and the end of it. （事件进程的）中间，一半

  • She has not yet reached the midpoint of her life. [+ of]

    她的人生还没有走到一半。

  • ...the midpoint in the current fiscal year.

    本财政年度的中期
