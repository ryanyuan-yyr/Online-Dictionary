crony
kroʊni ˈkroʊni
Word forms: plural cronies
N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 好友;密友;(尤指)狐朋狗友 You can refer to friends that someone spends a lot of time with as their cronies, especially when you disapprove of them. 好友;密友;(尤指)狐朋狗友 [informal, disapproval]

  • He returned from a lunchtime drinking session with his business cronies.

    中午跟那帮生意上的朋友喝完酒后，他回来了。

SYN friend, china [British , slang] , colleague, associate
