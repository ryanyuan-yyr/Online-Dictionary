stratum
strɑːtəm streɪtəm ˈstreɪtəm ˈstrætəm
Word forms: plural strata
1 N-COUNT 社会阶层 A stratum of society is a group of people in it who are similar in their education, income, or social status. 社会阶层 [formal]

  • It was an enormous task that affected every stratum of society. [+ of]

    这是一项影响到社会各阶层的重大工作。

  • The rebels came overwhelmingly from the poorest strata of rural society.

    叛乱分子绝大多数来自农村的最贫困阶层。

SYN class, group, level, station  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 岩层 Strata are different layers of rock. 岩层 [technical]

  • Contained within the rock strata is evidence that the region was intensely dry 15,000 years ago.

    岩层中有证据表明，该地区在 15,000 年前曾经非常干旱。

SYN layer, level, seam, table
