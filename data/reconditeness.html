recondite
rɪkɒndaɪt rekən- rɪˈkɒndaɪt ˈrɛkənˌdaɪt rɪˈkɑnˌdaɪt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （知识或学问）深奥的，艰涩的，艰深的 Recondite areas of knowledge or learning are difficult to understand, and not many people know about them. （知识或学问）深奥的，艰涩的，艰深的 [formal]

  • Her poems are modishly experimental in style and recondite in subject-matter.

    她的诗在风格上是时髦的实验派，主题艰深难懂。

SYN obscure, involved, difficult, deep
