onrush
ɒnrʌʃ ˈɑnˌrʌʃ
N-SING 突如其来；猛然袭来 The onrush of something is its sudden development, which happens so quickly and forcefully that you are unable to control it. 突如其来；猛然袭来

  • The onrush of tears took me by surprise. [+ of]

    我没料到自己竟一下子泪如泉涌。

  • She was screwing up her eyes against the onrush of air. [+ of]

    一阵风突然吹来，她眯起了眼睛。

SYN surge, charge, push [informal] , flow
