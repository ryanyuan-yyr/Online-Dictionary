yesterday
jestəʳdeɪ -di ˈjɛstədɪ ˈjɛstərˌdeɪ ˈjɛstərdi
Word forms: plural yesterdays
1 ADV 昨天 You use yesterday to refer to the day before today. 昨天

  • She left yesterday.

    她昨天走了。

  • Yesterday she announced that she is quitting her job.

    昨天她宣布打算辞职。

Yesterday is also a noun.

  • In yesterday's games, Switzerland beat the United States two-one.

    在昨天的比赛中，瑞士队以2比1战胜了美国队。

2 N-UNCOUNT 过去；（尤指）不久前，近日，日前 You can refer to the past, especially the recent past, as yesterday . 过去；（尤指）不久前，近日，日前

  • The worker of today is different from the worker of yesterday.

    如今的工人和以往的不同了。

  • ...a world without yesterdays or tomorrows.

    没有过去和未来的世界
