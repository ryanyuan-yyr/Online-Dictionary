craic
kræk kræk
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 crack
N-SING 好时光;(尤指)友好愉快的交谈 If you are talking about something that you did and you say 'the craic was great', or 'it was a good craic', you mean that you had a really good time, especially because everyone was talking, joking, and laughing. 好时光;(尤指)友好愉快的交谈 [Irish, informal]

  • I have enjoyed every minute. The craic has been great.

    我自始至终都很愉快，玩得很开心。

  • They go to the pubs not for the drink alone, but for the crack.

    他们去酒馆不仅是为了喝酒，也是为了找乐子。
