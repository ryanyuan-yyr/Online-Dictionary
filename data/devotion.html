devotion
dɪvoʊʃən dɪˈvoʊʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 深爱;挚爱;仰慕 Devotion is great love, affection, or admiration for someone. 深爱;挚爱;仰慕

  • At first she was flattered by his devotion.

    刚开始他的爱慕让她感到荣幸。

SYN love, passion, affection, intensity  
2 N-UNCOUNT 献身;奉献 Devotion is commitment to a particular activity. 献身;奉献

  • ...devotion to the cause of the people and to socialism. [+ to]

    献身于人民的事业，献身于社会主义

  • I don't mean to keep criticising his devotion to his job.

    我并不是要一再批评他对工作的专注。

SYN dedication, commitment, loyalty, allegiance  
3 N-UNCOUNT （对宗教的）虔诚 Devotion is religious worship or strong religious feeling. （对宗教的）虔诚

  • He was kneeling by his bed in an attitude of devotion.

    他虔诚地跪在床边。

SYN worship, reverence, spirituality, holiness
