shingle
ʃɪŋgəl ˈʃɪŋgəl
Word forms: plural shingles
1 N-UNCOUNT (海边或河边的)碎石滩 Shingle is a mass of small rough pieces of stone on the shore of a sea or a river. (海边或河边的)碎石滩

  • ...a beach of sand and shingle.

    碎石沙滩

2 N-UNCOUNT 带状疱疹(尤见于腰部) Shingles is a disease in which painful red spots spread in bands over a person's body, especially around their waist. 带状疱疹(尤见于腰部)
3 N-COUNT [usually plural] 木瓦;墙面板;屋顶板 Shingles are thin pieces of wood or another material which are fixed in rows to cover a roof or wall. 木瓦;墙面板;屋顶板

  • The roofs had shingles missing.

    一些屋顶板不见了。

4 N-COUNT (诊所或律师事务所等外面所挂的)招牌 A shingle is a small sign that is hung outside a building, such as the place where a doctor or lawyer works. (诊所或律师事务所等外面所挂的)招牌 [US]
