lip service
N-UNCOUNT 空口的应酬话；口惠 If you say that someone pays lip service to an idea, you are critical of them because they say they are in favour of it, but they do not do anything to support it. 空口的应酬话；口惠 [disapproval]

  • Unhappily, he had done no more than pay lip service to their views. [+ to]

    不幸的是，他对他们观点的支持只是口惠而实不至。
