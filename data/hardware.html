hardware

1 N-UNCOUNT （计算机）硬件 In computer systems, hardware refers to the machines themselves as opposed to the programs which tell the machines what to do. （计算机）硬件
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 重武器;军事装备 Military hardware is the machinery and equipment that is used by the armed forces, such as tanks, aircraft, and missiles. 重武器;军事装备
3 N-UNCOUNT （家庭和园艺用的）五金制品，金属器具 Hardware refers to tools and equipment that are used in the home and garden, for example saucepans, screwdrivers, and lawnmowers. （家庭和园艺用的）五金制品，金属器具
