brick up
PHRASAL VERB 用砖将…堵起来 If you brick up a hole, you close it with a wall of bricks. 用砖将…堵起来

  • We bricked up our windows. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我们用砖把窗户堵起来了。

  • All the doors have been bricked up to deter vandals. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    所有的门都用砖堵了起来，防止破坏分子闯入。

SYN wall up, cover, close up, shut up
