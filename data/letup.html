let up
1 PHRASAL VERB （指持续的令人不快的过程）停止，缓和，减弱 If an unpleasant, continuous process lets up, it stops or becomes less intense. （指持续的令人不快的过程）停止，缓和，减弱

  • The traffic in this city never lets up, even at night. [VERB PREP.]

    这个城市道路上的车辆从来就没少过，甚至晚上也不例外。

  • The rain had let up. [VERB PREP.]

    雨势已经减弱。

SYN stop, diminish, decrease, subside  
2See also:let-up 
let-up ˈlɛtˌʌp
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] （通常指令人不快的事情的）缓和，减弱 If there is no let-up in something, usually something unpleasant, there is no reduction in the intensity of it. （通常指令人不快的事情的）缓和，减弱

  • There was no let-up in the battle on the money markets yesterday. [+ in]

    对金融市场的争夺战昨天依然非常激烈。

  • There were no signs of any let-up in job-shedding.

    无迹象显示裁员势头会减缓。

SYN lessening, break, pause, interval
