abusive
əbjuːsɪv əˈbjuːsɪv əˈbjusɪv əˈbjuzɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 虐待的;凌辱的 Someone who is abusive behaves in a cruel and violent way towards other people. 虐待的;凌辱的

  • He became violent and abusive toward Ben's mother.

    他对本的妈妈动起粗来。

  • One in eight women lives in an abusive relationship.

    每八位女性中就有一位遭受伴侣的虐待。

  • ...her cruel and abusive husband.

    她残酷暴虐的丈夫

2 ADJ-GRADED 侮辱性的；恶言谩骂的 Abusive language is extremely rude and insulting. 侮辱性的；恶言谩骂的

  • He was alleged to have used abusive language.

    他被指称使用过侮辱性语言。

SYN insulting, offensive, rude, degrading
