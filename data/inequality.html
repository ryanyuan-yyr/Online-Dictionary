inequality
ɪnɪkwɒlɪti ˌɪniˈkwɔləti ˌɪniˈkwɑləti ˌɪnɪˈkwâləti ˌɪnɪˈkwɑləti
Word forms: plural inequalities
1 N-VAR (社会地位、财富、机会等)不平等，不均等 Inequality is the difference in social status, wealth, or opportunity between people or groups. (社会地位、财富、机会等)不平等，不均等

  • People are concerned about social inequality.

    人们很关注社会不平等问题。

  • Blacks have been hurt by racial inequalities in housing and education.

    住房与教育方面的种族不平等现象让黑人深受伤害。

SYN disparity, prejudice, difference, bias
