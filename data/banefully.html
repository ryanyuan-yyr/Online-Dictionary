bane
beɪn ben beɪn
N-SING 苦恼之根；烦扰之源 The bane of someone or the bane of someone's life is something that frequently makes them feel unhappy or annoyed. 苦恼之根；烦扰之源

  • Rain is the bane of holiday-makers.

    雨天令度假者头疼。

  • Spots can be the bane of a teenager's life.

    粉刺会给青少年带来无尽的烦恼。

SYN plague [informal] , bête noire, trial, disaster
