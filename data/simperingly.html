simper
sɪmpəʳ ˈsɪmpər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense simpers, present participle simpering, past tense , past participle simpered
VERB 傻笑;痴笑 When someone simpers, they smile in a rather silly way. 傻笑;痴笑

  • The maid lowered her chin and simpered. [VERB]

    侍女低头痴笑着。

SYN smile coyly, smirk, smile self-consciously, smile affectedly  
Simper is also a noun.

  • 'Thank you doctor,' said the nurse with a simper.

    “谢谢你，医生，”护士傻笑着说。
