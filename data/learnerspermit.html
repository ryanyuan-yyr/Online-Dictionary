learner's permit
ˈlɜːnəz ˈpɜːmɪt
Word forms: learner's permits
N-COUNT 临时驾车许可证A learner's permit is a licence that allows you to drive a vehicle before you have passed your driving test. 临时驾车许可证 [US]

  • She took some friends for a ride in the family car when she only had her learner's permit.
