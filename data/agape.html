agape
əgeɪp ˈægəˌpi əˈgeɪp ˈɑgəˌpeɪ əˈgɑˌpeɪ ɑˈgɑˌpeɪ
ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （由于惊愕等）张大嘴的 If you describe someone as having their mouth agape, their mouth is open very wide, often because they are very surprised by something. （由于惊愕等）张大嘴的 [written]

  • She stood looking at Carmen with her mouth agape.

    她站着，张大了嘴看着卡门。

SYN wide open, wide, yawning, gaping
