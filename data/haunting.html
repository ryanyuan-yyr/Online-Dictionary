haunting
hɔːntɪŋ ˈhɔntɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （声音、形象、话语等）令人难忘的，萦绕心头的 Haunting sounds, images, or words remain in your thoughts because they are very beautiful or sad. （声音、形象、话语等）令人难忘的，萦绕心头的

  • ...the haunting calls of wild birds in the mahogany trees.

    红木树林中野鸟令人难忘的鸣叫声

  • ...haunting prose.

    令人难忘的散文

SYN evocative, poignant, unforgettable, indelible  
hauntingly

  • Each one of these ancient towns is hauntingly beautiful.

    这些古镇个个都美得令人难以忘怀。
