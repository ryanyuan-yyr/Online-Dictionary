shanty
ʃænti ˈʃæntɪ ˈʃænti ˈʃɑnti
Word forms: plural shanties
1 N-COUNT (穷人居住的)简陋的小屋，棚屋 A shanty is a small rough hut which poor people live in, built from tin, cardboard, or other materials that are not very strong. (穷人居住的)简陋的小屋，棚屋
2 N-COUNT (过去水手劳动时唱的)船夫曲，水手号子,船歌 A shanty is a song which sailors used to sing while they were doing work on a ship. (过去水手劳动时唱的)船夫曲，水手号子,船歌

  • ...one of my father's favourite sea-shanties.

    我父亲最喜爱的船夫曲之一

SYN song, song, air, tune
