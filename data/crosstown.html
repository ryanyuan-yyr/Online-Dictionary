crosstown krɔstaʊn ˈkrɔsˌtaʊn
(also cross-town)
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 往来于城镇主干线的A crosstown bus or route is one that crosses the main roads or transportation lines of a town or city. 往来于城镇主干线的 [US]

  • ...the crosstown bus that takes me to work.

    ...我上班乘坐的城镇主干线公车。

  • ...the long-awaited completion of a crosstown subway.

    ...期待已久的城市主干线地铁的竣工。

2 ADV 往来于城镇主干线地Crosstown is also an adverb. 往来于城镇主干线地

  • I have trouble these days getting crosstown in Manhattan.

    我近来很难在曼哈顿城中自由往来。
