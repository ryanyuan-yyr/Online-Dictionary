itinerary
aɪtɪnərəri -eri aɪˈtɪnərˌɛri ɪˈtɪnərˌɛri
Word forms: plural itineraries
N-COUNT 路线;旅程 An itinerary is a plan of a journey, including the route and the places that you will visit. 路线;旅程

  • The next place on our itinerary was Silistra.

    我们行程的下一站是锡利斯特拉。

SYN schedule, line, programme, tour
