deaden
dedən ˈdɛdən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deadens, present participle deadening, past tense , past participle deadened
VERB 缓和，减弱（感觉）；降低（声音） If something deadens a feeling or a sound, it makes it less strong or loud. 缓和，减弱（感觉）；降低（声音）

  • He needs morphine to deaden the pain in his chest. [VERB noun]

    他需要吗啡来缓解胸痛。

  • They managed to deaden the sound on TV every time the alleged victim's name was spoken. [VERB noun]

    每当提到据称是受害者的人的姓名时，他们都设法调低电视机的声音。

SYN reduce, dull, diminish, check
