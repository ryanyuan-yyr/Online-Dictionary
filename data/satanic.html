satanic
sətænɪk seɪˈtænɪk səˈtænɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 受撒旦影响的;撒旦引起的 Something that is satanic is considered to be caused by or influenced by Satan. 受撒旦影响的;撒旦引起的

  • ...satanic cults.

    撒旦崇拜

  • ...satanic ritual.

    撒旦仪式

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 极其邪恶的;穷凶极恶的 A person or thing that is satanic is extremely wicked and evil. 极其邪恶的;穷凶极恶的

  • ...satanic mass murderers.

    穷凶极恶的嗜杀者
