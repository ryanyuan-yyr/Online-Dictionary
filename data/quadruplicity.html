quadruple
kwɒdruːpəl ˈkwɒdrʊpəl kwɒˈdruːpəl kwɑˈdrupəl kwəˈdrupəl ˈkwɑdrəpəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense quadruples, present participle quadrupling, past tense , past participle quadrupled
1 V-ERG （使）成四倍;（使）翻两番 If someone quadruples an amount or if it quadruples, it becomes four times bigger. （使）成四倍;（使）翻两番

  • Norway has quadrupled its exports to the EU.

    挪威对欧盟的出口已经增加了3倍。

  • The price has quadrupled in the last few years. [VERB]

    近几年来价格已经翻了两番。

2 PREDET 是…的四倍；四倍于 If one amount is quadruple another amount, it is four times bigger. 是…的四倍；四倍于

  • They could sell their merchandise for quadruple the asking price.

    他们能够以4倍于要价的价钱出售他们的商品。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 由四部分组成的;四次的 You use quadruple to indicate that something has four parts or happens four times. 由四部分组成的;四次的

  • ...a quadruple murder.

    四尸命案
