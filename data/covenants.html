covenant
kʌvənənt ˈkʌvənənt ˈkʌvənənt
Word forms: plural covenants
1 N-COUNT (受法律约束的)协约，契约，合同 A covenant is a formal written agreement between two or more people or groups of people which is recognized in law. (受法律约束的)协约，契约，合同

  • ...the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights.

    《公民权利和政治权利国际公约》

2 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] (定期付款的)书面承诺;(尤指向慈善机构定期捐款的)契约 A covenant is a formal written promise to pay a sum of money each year for a fixed period, especially to a charity. (定期付款的)书面承诺;(尤指向慈善机构定期捐款的)契约 [mainly British]

  • If you make regular gifts through a covenant we can reclaim the income tax which you have already paid on this money.

    如果你签订契约定期捐赠，那么我们可以要求返还你这笔钱上交的所得税。

SYN deed, contract, bond  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 pledge
