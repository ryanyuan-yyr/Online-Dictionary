allusive
əluːsɪv əˈlusɪv æˈlusɪv
ADJ-GRADED 间接提到的；暗指的；影射的；含典故的 Allusive speech, writing, or art is full of indirect references to people or things. 间接提到的；暗指的；影射的；含典故的

  • His new play, Arcadia, is as intricate, elaborate and allusive as anything he has yet written.

    他的新剧《阿卡迪亚》和他写过的任何作品一样，错综复杂、构思精巧并且富含典故。
