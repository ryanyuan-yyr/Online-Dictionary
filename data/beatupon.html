beat up on
1 PHRASAL VERB 殴打；痛打 If someone beats up on a person or beats on them, they hit or kick the person many times. 殴打；痛打 [US] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • He beat up on my brother's kid one time. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他有一次殴打了我哥的孩子。

2 PHRASAL VERB 威胁；恐吓；粗暴对待 If someone beats up on another person, they threaten them or treat them unkindly. 威胁；恐吓；粗暴对待 [US, informal]

  • She had to beat up on every customer just to get the bills paid. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    她不得不威胁每一位顾客，好让他们付款。
