contented
kəntentɪd kənˈtɛntɪd
ADJ-GRADED 满足的;满意的 If you are contented, you are satisfied with your life or the situation you are in. 满足的;满意的

  • Whenever he returns to this place he is happy and contented.

    每次回到这个地方，他都十分快乐，心满意足。

  • She was gazing at him with a soft, contented smile on her face.

    她注视着他，脸上带着温柔而满足的微笑。

SYN satisfied, happy, pleased, content  
contentedly

  • The landlady sighed contentedly.

    房东太太心满意足地叹了口气。
