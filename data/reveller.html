reveller
revələʳ
Word forms: plural revellers
in AM, use 美国英语用 reveler
N-COUNT [usually plural] (常指喝醉后的)狂欢者，寻欢作乐者 Revellers are people who are enjoying themselves in a noisy way, often while they are drunk. (常指喝醉后的)狂欢者，寻欢作乐者 [literary]

  • Many of the revellers are tourists and British day-trippers.

    很多饮酒狂欢的人都是观光客和来一日游的英国旅客。

SYN merrymaker, carouser, pleasure-seeker, partygoer
