confined
kənfaɪnd
1 ADJ 受限制的；被限定的 If something is confined to a particular place, it exists only in that place. If it is confined to a particular group, only members of that group have it. 受限制的；被限定的

  • The problem is not confined to Germany. [+ to]

    问题不仅限于德国。

  • These dangers are not confined to smokers. [+ to]

    这些危险不只是困扰吸烟者。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (空间或面积)有限的，狭小的 A confined space or area is small and enclosed by walls. (空间或面积)有限的，狭小的

  • His long legs bent up in the confined space.

    他的两条长腿蜷在狭小的空间里。

SYN restricted, small, limited, narrow  
3 ADJ (因残疾或生病)受困于…的 If someone is confined to a wheelchair, bed, or house, they have to stay there, because they are disabled or ill. (因残疾或生病)受困于…的

  • He had been confined to a wheelchair since childhood. [+ to]

    他打小就只能坐轮椅。
