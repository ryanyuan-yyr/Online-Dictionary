input
ɪnpʊt ˈɪnˌpʊt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense inputs, present participle inputting
The form input is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. input 的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
1 N-VAR 投入物(指信息、资源等) Input consists of information or resources that a group or project receives. 投入物(指信息、资源等)

  • We listen to our employees and value their input.

    我们听取员工的想法,重视他们的意见和建议。

  • They may need some additional inputs and advice on how to improve the management of their farms.

    他们可能需要听取更多的意见和建议来改善农场的经营。

2 N-UNCOUNT 输入信息 Input is information that is put into a computer. 输入信息 [computing]
3 VERB 输入(信息) If you input information into a computer, you feed it in, for example by typing it on a keyboard. 输入(信息) [computing]

  • All this information had to be input onto the computer. [be V-ed + onto]

    所有这些信息都必须输入计算机。
