curvature
kɜːʳvətʃəʳ ˈkɜrvətʃər
N-UNCOUNT 弯曲;曲度;(尤指)圆弧 The curvature of something is its curved shape, especially when this shape is part of the circumference of a circle. 弯曲;曲度;(尤指)圆弧 [technical]

  • ...the curvature of the earth. [+ of]

    地球的曲度

  • He suffered from curvature of the spine.

    脊柱弯曲使他痛苦不堪。

SYN curving, bend, curve, arching
