sleep around
PHRASAL VERB 到处与人上床;滥交 If you say that someone sleeps around, you disapprove of them because they have sex with a lot of different people. 到处与人上床;滥交 [informal, disapproval]

  • I don't sleep around. [VERB PREP.]

    我不随便跟别人上床。

  • ...a drunken husband who slept around with other women. [VERB PREP. + with]

    到处跟其他女人鬼混的酒鬼丈夫
