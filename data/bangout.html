bang out
1 PHRASAL VERB 大量生产（劣质产品） If a company bangs out a poor quality product, they produce large quantities of it in order to make money. 大量生产（劣质产品） [disapproval]

  • ...factories that banged out the same product year after year. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    年复一年滥造同样产品的工厂

2 PHRASAL VERB （并不出色地用乐器大声）演奏出，敲出，砸出（某一乐曲） If someone bangs out a tune on a musical instrument such as a piano, they play it loudly and not very well. （并不出色地用乐器大声）演奏出，敲出，砸出（某一乐曲）

  • ...the same version of 'Mr Tambourine Man' he's been banging out since the world was young. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他打地球开始转的时候就一直在弹奏的这同一首《铃鼓先生》
