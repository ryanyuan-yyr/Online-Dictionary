ineptitude
ɪneptɪtjuːd -tuːd ɪnˈɛptəˌtud ɪnˈɛptəˌtjud
N-UNCOUNT 无能;笨拙 If you refer to someone's ineptitude, you are criticizing them because they do something with a complete lack of skill. 无能;笨拙 [disapproval]

  • ...the tactical ineptitude of the allied commander. [+ of]

    盟军指挥官战术上的无能

SYN incompetence, inefficiency, inability, incapacity
