radiant
reɪdiənt ˈreɪdiənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 容光焕发的;喜气洋洋的 Someone who is radiant is so happy that their happiness shows in their face. 容光焕发的;喜气洋洋的

  • Kathy smiled at her daughter's radiant face.

    凯茜对着女儿容光焕发的面孔微笑着。

  • On her wedding day the bride looked truly radiant.

    婚礼那天，新娘看上去真是光彩照人。

SYN happy, glowing, ecstatic, joyful  
radiantly

  • He smiled radiantly and embraced her.

    他高兴地笑着拥抱了她。

  • She looks radiantly happy.

    她看上去喜气洋洋。

2 ADJ-GRADED 光辉灿烂的；光芒四射的 Something that is radiant glows brightly. 光辉灿烂的；光芒四射的

  • The evening sun warms the old red brick wall to a radiant glow.

    夕阳为古老的红砖墙罩上了一层暖暖的炫目光辉。

  • Out on the bay the morning is radiant.

    海湾里的早晨阳光灿烂。

SYN bright, brilliant, shining, glorious  
radiantly

  • The sun was still shining radiantly.

    太阳依旧光芒四射。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] （热量、能量等）辐射的 Radiant heat or energy is sent out in the form of rays. （热量、能量等）辐射的

  • The earth would be a frozen ball if it were not for the radiant heat of the sun.

    如果没有太阳辐射的热量，地球将是一个冰封的世界。
