misconception
mɪskənsepʃən
Word forms: plural misconceptions
N-COUNT 错误观念;错误看法;误解 A misconception is an idea that is not correct. 错误观念;错误看法;误解

  • It is a misconception that Peggy was fabulously wealthy.

    人们误以为佩吉相当有钱。

  • There are many fears and misconceptions about cancer.

    人们对癌症非常恐惧，而且存在许多错误看法。

SYN delusion, error, misunderstanding, fallacy
