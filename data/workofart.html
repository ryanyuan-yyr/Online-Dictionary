work of art
Word forms: plural works of art
1 N-COUNT (绘画、雕塑等)艺术品 A work of art is a painting or piece of sculpture which is of high quality. (绘画、雕塑等)艺术品

  • ...a collection of works of art of international significance.

    一批具有国际影响力的艺术品

SYN masterpiece, magnum opus, master work  
2 N-COUNT 精致的东西;精品 A work of art is something which is very complex or which has been skilfully made or produced. 精致的东西;精品

  • The actual nest is a work of art.

    真实的鸟巢十分精致。
