infringement
ɪnfrɪndʒmənt
Word forms: plural infringements
1 N-VAR (对他人权利等的)侵犯,侵害 An infringement is an action or situation that interferes with your rights and the freedom you are entitled to. (对他人权利等的)侵犯,侵害

  • ...infringement of privacy. [+ of]

    对隐私的侵犯

  • They see it as an infringement on their own freedom of action. [+ on]

    他们把它视为对他们行动自由的侵犯。

SYN contravention, breach, violation, trespass  
2 N-VAR (对法律、规则等的)违反,违背 An infringement of a law or rule is the act of breaking it or disobeying it. (对法律、规则等的)违反,违背

  • There might have been an infringement of the rules. [+ of]

    可能存在违反规章制度的现象。

  • Infringement of the regulation is punishable by a fine.

    违反规则应处以罚款。
