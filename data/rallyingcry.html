rallying cry ˈrælɪɪŋ kraɪ
Word forms: plural rallying cries
N-COUNT 战斗口号 A rallying cry or rallying call is something such as a word or phrase, an event, or a belief which encourages people to unite and to act in support of a particular group or idea. 战斗口号

  • ...an issue that is fast becoming a rallying cry for many Democrats: national health care.

    很快成为许多民主党人战斗口号的议题：全国医疗保健
