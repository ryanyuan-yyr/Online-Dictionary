decommission
diːkəmɪʃən ˌdikəˈmɪʃən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense decommissions, present participle decommissioning, past tense , past participle decommissioned
VERB 拆散(核反应堆、大型机械等);使退役 When something such as a nuclear reactor or a large machine is decommissioned, it is taken to pieces because it is no longer going to be used. 拆散(核反应堆、大型机械等);使退役 [Also VERB n]

  • HMS Warspite was decommissioned as part of defence cuts. [be VERB-ed]

    作为削减国防开支的一部分，英国皇家海军舰艇“厌战”号宣布退役。
