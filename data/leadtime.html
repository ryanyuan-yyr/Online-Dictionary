lead time
liːd lid
Word forms: plural lead times
1 N-COUNT 研制时间；开发周期 Lead time is the time between the original design or idea for a particular product and its actual production. 研制时间；开发周期 [business]

  • They aim to cut production lead times to under 18 months.

    他们力求把开发周期缩至18个月以下。

2 N-COUNT （从订货到交货的）交付周期 Lead time is the period of time that it takes for goods to be delivered after someone has ordered them. （从订货到交货的）交付周期 [business]

  • Lead times on new equipment orders can run as long as three years. [+ on]

    新设备订单的交货周期可长达3年。
