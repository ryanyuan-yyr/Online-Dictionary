substratum
sʌbstrɑːtəm sʌbˈstrɑːtəm -streɪt- ˈsʌbˌstreɪtəm ˈsʌbˌstrætəm
Word forms: plural substrata
N-COUNT 下层;底层;深层特征;隐性特质 A substratum of something is a layer that lies under the surface of another layer, or a feature that is less obvious than other features. 下层;底层;深层特征;隐性特质 [formal]

  • ...its deep substratum of chalk. [+ of]

    深厚的白垩地层

  • His recreation of the city is credible, with a substratum of fact to bolster the fiction.

    他对该城历史的再现是可信的，虚构的小说情节隐念着一些事实作为支撑。
