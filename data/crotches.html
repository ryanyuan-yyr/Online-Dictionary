crotch
krɒtʃ krɑtʃ
Word forms: plural crotches
1 N-COUNT (人体的)胯部，两腿分叉处 Your crotch is the part of your body between the tops of your legs. (人体的)胯部，两腿分叉处

  • Glover kicked him hard in the crotch.

    格洛弗狠狠地踢他的胯部。

SYN groin, lap, crutch  
2 N-COUNT (裤子等的)裆部 The crotch of something such as a pair of trousers is the part that covers the area between the tops of your legs. (裤子等的)裆部

  • They were too long in the crotch.

    裤裆太深了。
