raspberry
rɑːzbri ræzberi ˈræzˌbɛri ˈræzbəri
Word forms: plural raspberries
1 N-COUNT 悬钩子;覆盆子;木莓;山莓 Raspberries are small, soft, red fruit that grow on bushes. 悬钩子;覆盆子;木莓;山莓
2 N-COUNT （表示轻蔑的）吐舌声，呸声 If you blow a raspberry, you make a sound by putting your tongue out and blowing, in order to insult someone. （表示轻蔑的）吐舌声，呸声 [informal]

  • He blows a raspberry down the telephone line and hangs up.

    他对着电话呸了一声，然后挂断了。

  • They're all making raspberry noises.

    他们所有人都在吐舌头发出讥笑声。

3 N-COUNT 痛斥;厉声责备 If you blow a raspberry at someone or something, you reject them completely or criticize them severely. 痛斥;厉声责备 [mainly British, journalism]

  • ...a time to blow a modest raspberry at politics.

    对政治稍加指责的时期
