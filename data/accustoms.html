accustom
əkʌstəm əˈkʌstəm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense accustoms, present participle accustoming, past tense , past participle accustomed
1 VERB 使习惯（于）；使适应 If you accustom yourself or another person to something, you make yourself or them become used to it. 使习惯（于）；使适应 [formal]

  • The team has accustomed itself to the pace of first division rugby. [VERB pronoun-reflexive + to]

    球队适应了英式橄榄球甲级联赛的节奏。

  • Shakespeare has accustomed us to a mixture of humor and tragedy in the same play. [VERB noun + to]

    莎士比亚让我们习见了同一场戏目中的悲喜交错。

SYN familiarize, train, coach, exercise
