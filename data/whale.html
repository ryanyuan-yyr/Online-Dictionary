whale
hweɪl hweɪl weɪl
Word forms: plural whales
1 N-COUNT 鲸 Whales are very large mammals that live in the sea. See also: killer whale  sperm whale  鲸
2 PHRASE 非常高兴;非常愉快 If you say that someone is having a whale of a time, you mean that they are enjoying themselves very much. 非常高兴;非常愉快 [informal]

  • I had a whale of a time in Birmingham.

    我在伯明翰度过了一段非常快乐的时光。
