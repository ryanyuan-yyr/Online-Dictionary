succour
sʌkəʳ ˈsʌkə
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense succours, present participle succouring, past tense , past participle succoured
in AM, use 美国英语用 succor
1 N-UNCOUNT 援助;援救;帮助 Succour is help given to people who are suffering or in difficulties. 援助;援救;帮助 [formal]

  • ...a commitment to give succour to populations involved in the conflict.

    要向战争中受难的人们提供援助的承诺

SYN help, support, aid, relief  
2 VERB 援助;援救;帮助 If you succour someone who is suffering or in difficulties, you help them. 援助;援救;帮助 [formal]

  • Helicopters fly in appalling weather to succour shipwrecked mariners. [VERB noun]

    直升机在恶劣的天气条件下起飞前去援救遇难的船员。

SYN help, support, aid, encourage
