sift
sɪft sɪft
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sifts, present participle sifting, past tense , past participle sifted
1 VERB 筛(面粉、沙子等) If you sift a powder such as flour or sand, you put it through a sieve in order to remove large pieces or lumps. 筛(面粉、沙子等)

  • Sift the flour and baking powder into a medium-sized mixing bowl. [VERB noun]

    将面粉和发酵粉筛入中等大小的搅拌碗中。

SYN sieve, filter, strain, separate  
2 VERB 细查;详审 If you sift through something such as evidence, you examine it thoroughly. 细查;详审

  • Police officers have continued to sift through the wreckage following yesterday's bomb attack. [VERB + through]

    警察继续仔细检查昨天炸弹袭击后留下的残骸。

  • Brook has sifted the evidence and summarises it clearly. [VERB noun]

    布鲁克已经仔细审查过证据并将其总结得一清二楚。

SYN examine, investigate, go through, research
