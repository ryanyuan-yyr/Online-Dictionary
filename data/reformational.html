reformation
refəʳmeɪʃən ˌrɛfərˈmeɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 改革；革新 The reformation of something is the act or process of changing and improving it. 改革；革新

  • He devoted his energies to the reformation of science. [+ of]

    他致力于科学的革新。

2 N-PROPER 宗教改革运动（16世纪天主教会的改革运动） The Reformation is the movement to reform the Catholic Church in the sixteenth century, which led to the Protestant church being set up. 宗教改革运动（16世纪天主教会的改革运动）

  • ...a famous statue of the Virgin which was destroyed during the Reformation.

    在宗教改革运动中被毁的著名的圣母马利亚塑像
