forecourt
fɔːʳkɔːʳt ˈfɔrˌkɔrt
Word forms: plural forecourts
N-COUNT [n NOUN] (大楼或加油站的)前院，前场 The forecourt of a large building or petrol station is the open area at the front of it. (大楼或加油站的)前院，前场 [mainly British]

  • I locked the bike in the forecourt of the Kirey Hotel. [+ of]

    我把自行车锁在了吉日旅馆的前院。

  • A police patrol car was parked in the forecourt of the petrol station.

    一辆巡逻警车停在加油站的停车区。
