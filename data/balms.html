balm
bɑːm bɑm bɑlm
Word forms: plural balms
1 N-MASS （用于制作疗伤或镇痛油膏的）香树脂 Balm is a sweet-smelling oil that is obtained from some tropical trees and used to make creams that heal wounds or reduce pain. （用于制作疗伤或镇痛油膏的）香树脂
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 安慰（物）；慰藉（物） If you refer to something as balm, you mean that it makes you feel better. 安慰（物）；慰藉（物） [approval]

  • The place is balm to the soul.

    这个地方能给灵魂带来慰藉。

SYN comfort, support, relief, cheer
