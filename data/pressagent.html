press agent
Word forms: plural press agents
N-COUNT [oft with poss] （受雇于名人的）新闻发言人，宣传员 A press agent is a person who is employed by a famous person to give information about that person to the press. （受雇于名人的）新闻发言人，宣传员

  • The child actor's press agent says: 'He does not talk to the press on such matters.'

    这位童星的新闻发言人说：“他不会就这些事情向媒体发表观点。”
