undeserved
ʌndɪzɜːʳvd ˌʌndɪˈzɜːvd
ADJ 不应得的；不该有的 If you describe something such as a reaction, treatment, or result as undeserved, you mean that the person who experiences it has not earned it and should not really have it. 不应得的；不该有的

  • Douglas has an undeserved reputation for being dull and dry.

    道格拉斯呆板无趣的名声并不副实。

  • Jim's treatment was harsh and undeserved.

    吉姆受到了不应有的苛刻待遇。
