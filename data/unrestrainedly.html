unrestrained
ʌnrɪstreɪnd ˌʌnrɪˈstreɪnd
ADJ 无限制的；极度的 If you describe someone's behaviour as unrestrained, you mean that it is extreme or intense, for example because they are expressing their feelings strongly or loudly. 无限制的；极度的

  • There was unrestrained joy on the faces of the people.

    人们的脸上洋溢着无尽的欢乐。

  • His campaign has been unrestrained and often vulgar.

    他的竞选活动恣意而为而且往往很低俗。

SYN uncontrolled, uninhibited, unbridled, free
