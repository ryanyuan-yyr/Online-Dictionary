timely
taɪmli ˈtaɪmli
ADJ-GRADED 及时的;适时的 A timely event happens at a moment when it is useful, effective, or relevant. 及时的;适时的 [approval]

  • The recent outbreaks of cholera are a timely reminder that this disease is still a serious health hazard.

    最近霍乱的爆发及时提醒了人们该疾病仍然会严重危害健康。

  • The exhibition is timely, since 'self-taught' art is catching on in a big way.

    展会召开得非常及时，因为“自修”艺术正在广泛流行。

SYN opportune [formal] , appropriate, well-timed, prompt
