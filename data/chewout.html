chew out
PHRASAL VERB 叱责;训斥 If you chew someone out, you tell them off in a very angry way. 叱责;训斥 [informal]

  • He chewed out the player, who apologized the next time I saw him. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他训斥了那个球员，我再次看见那个球员时，他道了歉。

  • When Tom got back to Dallas, Perot called him over and chewed him out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    汤姆回到达拉斯时，佩罗把他叫过去，骂了他一顿。
