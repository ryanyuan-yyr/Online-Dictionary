promo
proʊmoʊ ˈproʊmoʊ
Word forms: plural promos
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 商品广告；广告短片 A promo is something such as a short video film which is used to promote a product. 商品广告；广告短片 [journalism, informal]

  • He races his cars, and hires them out for film, TV and promo videos.
