tartan
tɑːʳtən ˈtɑrtən
Word forms: plural tartans
N-VAR [oft NOUN n] (苏格兰)花格图案，花格织物，格呢 Tartan is a design for cloth traditionally associated with Scotland, and which has a number of distinctive types. The design is made up of lines of different widths and colours crossing each other at right angles. Tartan is also used to refer to cloth which has this pattern. (苏格兰)花格图案，花格织物，格呢
