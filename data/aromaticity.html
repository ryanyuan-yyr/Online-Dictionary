aromatic
ærəmætɪk ˌærəˈmætɪk ˌærəˈmætɪk ˌ ɛrəˈmætɪk
ADJ-GRADED （植物或食品）有香味的，芳香的 An aromatic plant or food has a strong, pleasant smell of herbs or spices. （植物或食品）有香味的，芳香的

  • ...an evergreen shrub with deep green, aromatic leaves.

    叶子呈深绿色且带芳香气息的常青灌木丛

  • An aromatic beef stew waited on the stove.

    炉子上放着香味四溢的炖牛肉。

SYN fragrant, perfumed, spicy, savoury
