variance
veəriəns ˈvɛərɪəns ˈvɛriəns ˈværiəns
Word forms: plural variances
1 PHRASE （与…）不一致，相矛盾 If one thing is at variance with another, the two things seem to contradict each other. （与…）不一致，相矛盾 [formal]

  • Many of his statements were at variance with the facts. [+ with]

    他的说法有很多和事实不符。

  • This idealistic concept is at variance with reality.

    这种理想主义的观念和现实相矛盾。

SYN in disagreement, conflicting, at odds, in opposition  
2 N-VAR 差异;不同 The variance between things is the difference between them. 差异;不同 [formal]

  • ...the variances in the stock price. [+ in]

    股票价格的变化

  • ...total revenue variance.

    总收入的差异

SYN difference, contrast, discrepancy, variation
