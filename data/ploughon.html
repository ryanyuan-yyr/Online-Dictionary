plough on
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 奋力前进；奋力从事 If you plough on, you continue moving or trying to complete something, even though it takes a lot of effort to go on. 奋力前进；奋力从事

  • The King, however dispirited, ploughs on. [VERB PREP.]

    国王虽说沮丧，但仍一往无前。

  • The Chancellor has opted to plough on with policies that could run his coalition on to the rocks. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    总理选择了苦苦执行可能使他的联合政府触礁的政策。
