fame
feɪm feɪm
1 N-UNCOUNT 声誉;名望 If you achieve fame, you become very well-known. claim to fame→see: claim  声誉;名望

  • At the height of his fame, his every word was valued.

    在他声名鼎盛之时，他的每句话都受到重视。

  • The film earned him international fame.

    这部电影为他赢得了国际声望。

  • ...her rise to fame and fortune as a dramatist.

    她成为名利双收的剧作家

SYN prominence, glory, celebrity, stardom
