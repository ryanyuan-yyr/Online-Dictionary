browse
braʊz braʊz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense browses, present participle browsing, past tense , past participle browsed
1 VERB （在商店里）随便看 If you browse in a shop, you look at things in a fairly casual way, in the hope that you might find something you like. （在商店里）随便看

  • I stopped in several bookstores to browse. [VERB]

    我曾在几家书店停留，翻看里面的书。

  • She browsed in an up-market antiques shop. [VERB]

    她逛了一家高级古董店。

  • I'm just browsing around. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我就随便看看。

Browse is also a noun.

  • ...a browse around the shops.

    逛商店

2 VERB 浏览；翻阅 If you browse through a book or magazine, you look through it in a fairly casual way. 浏览；翻阅

  • ...sitting on the sofa browsing through the TV pages of the paper. [VERB prep.]

    坐在沙发上翻阅报纸上的电视节目版面

  • There are plenty of biographies for him to browse over. [VERB prep.]

    有大量传记供他浏览。

SYN skim, scan, glance at, survey  
3 VERB （尤指使用电脑在万维网上）浏览信息 If you browse on a computer, you search for information in computer files or on the Internet, especially on the World Wide Web. （尤指使用电脑在万维网上）浏览信息 [computing]

  • Try browsing around in the network bulletin boards. [VERB adv./prep.]

    试着在网络论坛上浏览信息。

4 VERB （动物）吃草 When animals browse, they feed on plants. （动物）吃草

  • ...the three red deer stags browsing 50 yards from my lodge on the fringes of the forest. [VERB]

    在距我的小屋50码远的森林边缘吃草的3只牡赤鹿

SYN graze, eat, feed, crop
