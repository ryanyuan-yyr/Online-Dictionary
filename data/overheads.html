overheads
oʊvəʳhedz ˈəʊvəˌhɛdz
N-PLURAL （企业的）间接费用，营运费用，经常性开支 The overheads of a business are its regular and essential expenses, such as salaries, rent, electricity, and telephone bills. （企业的）间接费用，营运费用，经常性开支 [business]

  • We are having to cut our costs to reduce overheads and remain competitive.

    我们现在必须减少开支以降低营运费用，保持竞争力。
