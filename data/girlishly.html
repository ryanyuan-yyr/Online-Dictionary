girlish
gɜːʳlɪʃ ˈgɜrlɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 像女孩子的；少女似的 If you describe a woman as girlish, you mean she behaves, looks, or sounds like a young girl, for example because she is shy, excited, or lively. 像女孩子的；少女似的

  • She gave a little girlish giggle.

    她像个小女孩一样咯咯地笑了笑。

SYN youthful, feminine, childish
