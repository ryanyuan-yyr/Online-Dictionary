imbalance
ɪmbæləns ɪmˈbæləns
Word forms: plural imbalances
1 N-VAR 不平衡;不均衡;失衡 If there is an imbalance in a situation, the things involved are not the same size, or are not the right size in proportion to each other. 不平衡;不均衡;失衡 [Also + in]

  • ...the imbalance between the two sides in this war. [+ between]

    这场战争中双方力量的悬殊

  • ...an international strategy to reduce trade imbalances.

    降低贸易逆差的国际战略

SYN unevenness, bias, inequality, unfairness
