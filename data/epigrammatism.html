epigram
epɪgræm ˈɛpəˌgræm
Word forms: plural epigrams
N-COUNT 诙谐短诗;警句;隽语 An epigram is a short saying or poem which expresses an idea in a very clever and amusing way. 诙谐短诗;警句;隽语
SYN witticism, quip, aphorism, bon mot  
Quotations
A thing well said will be wit in all languagesJohn Dryden Essay of Dramatic Poesy
