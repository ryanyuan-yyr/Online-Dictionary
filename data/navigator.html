navigator
nævɪgeɪtəʳ ˈnævəˌgeɪtər
Word forms: plural navigators
1 N-COUNT (飞机或船只的)领航员，导航员 The navigator on an aircraft or ship is the person whose job is to work out the direction in which the aircraft or ship should be travelling. (飞机或船只的)领航员，导航员

  • He became an RAF navigator during the war.

    他在战争期间成为皇家空军的领航员。

2 N-COUNT (旧时的)航海家 In the past, a navigator was an explorer who travelled by sea. (旧时的)航海家
