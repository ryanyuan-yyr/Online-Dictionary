hive off
PHRASAL VERB 转让，出售（部分业务） If someone hives off part of a business, they transfer it to new ownership, usually by selling it. 转让，出售（部分业务） [mainly British] [Also V n P, V n P into/to n]

  • Klockner plans to hive off its loss-making steel businesses. [VERB PREP. noun]

    克劳克纳公司计划出售其亏损的钢铁业务。

  • Lufthansa had originally planned to hive off its domestic operations into a subsidiary, Lufthansa Express. [V P n (not pron) into/to n]

    汉莎航空公司本来计划将其国内业务分立出去，成立一个子公司“汉莎运通”。
