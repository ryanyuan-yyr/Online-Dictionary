enlightenment
ɪnlaɪtənmənt ɛnˈlaɪtənmənt ɪnˈlaɪtənmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT 启发;教化;开明;开通 Enlightenment means the act of enlightening or the state of being enlightened. 启发;教化;开明;开通

  • Stella had a moment of enlightenment.

    斯特拉豁然开朗。

2 N-UNCOUNT （佛教的）般若，觉悟，智慧 In Buddhism, enlightenment is a final spiritual state in which everything is understood and there is no more suffering or desire. （佛教的）般若，觉悟，智慧

  • ...a sense of deep peace and spiritual enlightenment.

    深深的平和感和精神觉悟
