anticipatory
æntɪsɪpeɪtəri -pətɔːri ænˈtɪsəpəˌtɔri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 期待的；期望的；预期的 An anticipatory feeling or action is one that you have or do because you are expecting something to happen soon. 期待的；期望的；预期的 [formal]

  • ...an anticipatory smile.

    期许的笑容

SYN expectant, foreseeing, apprehensive, provident
