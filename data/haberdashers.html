haberdasher
hæbəʳdæʃəʳ ˈhæbərˌdæʃər ˈhæbəˌdæʃər
Word forms: plural haberdashers
1 N-COUNT 缝纫用品店 A haberdasher or a haberdasher's is a shop where small articles for sewing are sold. 缝纫用品店 [British]
2 N-COUNT 男装制作销售商;男装裁缝 A haberdasher is a shopkeeper who makes and sells men's clothes. 男装制作销售商;男装裁缝 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 tailor
3 N-COUNT 男装店 A haberdasher or a haberdasher's is a shop where men's clothes are sold. 男装店 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 tailor, tailor's
