left-justify ˌlɛftˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense left-justifies, present participle left-justifying, past tense , past participle left-justified
VERB 使左对齐 If printed text is left-justified, each line begins at the same distance from the left-hand edge of the page or column. 使左对齐

  • The data in the cells should be left-justified. [be VERB-ed]
