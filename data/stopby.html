stop by
PHRASAL VERB 顺便拜访;顺路探望 If you stop by somewhere, you make a short visit to a person or place. 顺便拜访;顺路探望 [informal]

  • Perhaps I'll stop by the hospital. [VERB PREP. noun]

    也许我会顺路去一下医院。

  • I'll stop by to see Leigh before going home. [VERB PREP.]

    我回家前将顺道去看望一下利。

SYN call in, drop in, look in, drop by
