bureaucratic
bjʊərəkrætɪk ˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 官僚式的；官僚主义的 Bureaucratic means involving complicated rules and procedures which can cause long delays. 官僚式的；官僚主义的

  • Diplomats believe that bureaucratic delays are inevitable.

    外交官们认为繁文缛节造成的延迟是在所难免的。

  • The department has become a bureaucratic nightmare.

    该部门的官僚作风非常严重。
