eyrie
ɪəri ˈɪərɪ ˈɛərɪ eri ˈɛri ˈɪri
Word forms: plural eyries
in AM, use 美国英语用 aerie
1 N-COUNT （难以接近的）高处的建筑 If you refer to a place such as a house or a castle as an eyrie, you mean it is built high up and is difficult to reach. （难以接近的）高处的建筑 [literary]

  • ...marooned in my 48th floor eyrie in the sky.

    困在我那位于48层高空的家中

2 N-COUNT （通常筑在高山上的）鹰巢，猛禽巢 An eyrie is the nest of a bird of prey such as an eagle, and is usually built high up in the mountains. （通常筑在高山上的）鹰巢，猛禽巢
