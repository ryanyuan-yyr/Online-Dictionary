aboveboard əbʌvbɔrd əˈbʌvˌbɔrd əˈbʌvˈbɔrd
(also above board,above-board)
ADJ [v-link ADJ] 正大光明的An arrangement or deal that is aboveboard is legal and is being carried out openly and honestly. A person who is aboveboard is open and honest about what they are doing. 正大光明的

  • His business dealings were aboveboard.

    他的商业交易是光明正大的。

  • If there was one thing she prided herself on, it was being open and aboveboard.

    如果她对自己有一事而自豪，那就是她的公开及光明正大。
