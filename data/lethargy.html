lethargy
leθəʳdʒi ˈlɛθərdʒi
N-UNCOUNT 慵懒；倦怠；没精打采 Lethargy is the condition or state of being lethargic. 慵懒；倦怠；没精打采

  • Symptoms include tiredness, paleness, and lethargy.

    症状包括疲倦、脸色苍白、没有精神。

  • ...the lethargy that plagued this project from its outset.

    一开始就困扰着这个项目的人员懒散问题

SYN sluggishness, inertia, inaction, slowness
