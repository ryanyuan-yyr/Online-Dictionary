innermost
ɪnəʳmoʊst ˈɪnərˌmoʊst
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 内心深处的;最隐秘的 Your innermost thoughts and feelings are your most personal and secret ones. 内心深处的;最隐秘的

  • ...expressing your innermost feelings.

    表达内心深处的情感

  • ...revealing a company's innermost secrets.

    揭开一家公司最不为人知的秘密

SYN deep, private, personal, true  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 最接近中心的;最里面的;最深处的 The innermost thing is the one that is nearest to the centre. 最接近中心的;最里面的;最深处的

  • ...the innermost part of the eye.

    眼睛最深处的部分

  • She put the receipt into the innermost pocket of her bag.

    她把收据放在提包最里面的口袋里。

SYN central, middle, interior, inner
