evoke
ɪvoʊk iˈvoʊk ɪˈvoʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense evokes, present participle evoking, past tense , past participle evoked
VERB 唤起;召唤;引起 To evoke a particular memory, idea, emotion, or response means to cause it to occur. 唤起;召唤;引起 [formal]

  • ...the scene evoking memories of those old movies. [VERB noun]

    唤起对那些老电影的记忆的场景

  • A sense of period was evoked by complementing pictures with appropriate furniture. [VERB noun]

    给照片配上一些合适的家具，唤起了一种年代感。
