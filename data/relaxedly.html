relaxed
rɪlækst
1 ADJ-GRADED 放松的;松弛的;安心的;轻松的 If you are relaxed, you are calm and not worried or tense. 放松的;松弛的;安心的;轻松的

  • As soon as I had made the final decision, I felt a lot more relaxed.

    我一作出最后的决定就感到轻松多了。

  • Try to adopt a more relaxed manner.

    试着换一种更加放松的方式。

SYN easy-going, easy, casual, informal  
2 ADJ-GRADED (地方或情形)轻松的，舒适的，使人安心的 If a place or situation is relaxed, it is calm and peaceful. (地方或情形)轻松的，舒适的，使人安心的

  • The atmosphere at lunch was relaxed.

    午餐的气氛很轻松。

SYN comfortable, easy-going, casual, laid-back [informal]
