department
dɪpɑːʳtmənt diˈpɑrtmənt dɪˈpɑrtmənt
Word forms: plural departments
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (政府的)部，司，局，处;(企业的)部门，科;(大学的)系，所;(大型商店的)货品区 A department is one of the sections in an organization such as a government, business, or university. A department is also one of the sections in a large shop. (政府的)部，司，局，处;(企业的)部门，科;(大学的)系，所;(大型商店的)货品区

  • ...the U.S. Department of Health, Education and Welfare. [+ of]

    美国卫生、教育和福利部

  • He moved to the sales department.

    他转到了销售部。

  • ...the geography department of Moscow University.

    莫斯科大学地理系

  • ...the jewelry department.

    珠宝首饰区

2 PHRASE 不是…的职责（或知识）范围;并非…的专长 If you say that a task or area of knowledge is not your department, you mean that you are not responsible for it or do not know much about it. 不是…的职责（或知识）范围;并非…的专长

  • 'I'm afraid the name means nothing to me,' he said. 'That's not my department.'

    “恐怕这个名字对我来说毫无意义，”他说，“我对此并不熟悉。”
