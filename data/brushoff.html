brush off
1 PHRASAL VERB 不理睬；冷落 If someone brushes you off when you speak to them, they refuse to talk to you or be nice to you. 不理睬；冷落 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • When I tried to talk to her about it she just brushed me off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    当我试图和她谈这件事时，她就是不理我。

SYN ignore, cut [informal] , reject, dismiss
