cerebellum
sɛrəbɛləm ˌsɛrɪˈbɛləm ˌsɛrəˈbɛləm
Word forms: cerebellums, cerebella
N-COUNT [oft the N] 小脑The cerebellum is a part of the brain in humans and other mammals that controls the body's movements and balance. 小脑 [medicine]
