fusillade
fjuːzɪleɪd -lɑːd ˈfjusəˌlɑd ˈfjʊsəˌleɪd ˈfjʊzəlɑd ˈfjʊzəleɪd
N-SING (枪炮等的)齐射，连发 A fusillade of shots or objects is a large number of them fired or thrown at the same time. (枪炮等的)齐射，连发 [formal]

  • Both were killed in a fusillade of bullets fired at close range. [+ of]

    两人都是在近距离扫射中丧命的。

SYN barrage, fire, burst, hail
