oddly
ɒdli
1 ADV [ADVERB adjective] 奇怪的是；说来也怪 You use oddly to indicate that what you are saying is true, but that it is not what you expected. 奇怪的是；说来也怪

  • He said no and seemed oddly reluctant to talk about it.

    他说没有，而且奇怪的是似乎不愿谈及此事。

  • Oddly, Emma says she never considered her face was attractive.

    奇怪的是，埃玛说她从来都不认为自己长得好看。

  • There were, oddly, few other Britons living in this northern part.

    说来也怪，很少有其他英国人住在这一北部地区。

SYN strangely, remarkably, curiously, extraordinarily
