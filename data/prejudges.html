prejudge
priːdʒʌdʒ priːˈdʒʌdʒ priˈdʒʌdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense prejudges, present participle prejudging, past tense , past participle prejudged
VERB 预先判断；过早判断 If you prejudge a situation, you form an opinion about it before you know all the facts. 预先判断；过早判断 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • They tried to prejudge the commission's findings. [VERB noun]

    他们试图预先判定考察团的发现结果。

SYN jump to conclusions about, anticipate, presume, presuppose
