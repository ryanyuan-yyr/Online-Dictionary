rig up
PHRASAL VERB 仓促建起;草草做成 If you rig up a device or structure, you make it or fix it in place using any materials that are available. 仓促建起;草草做成 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • When it rained I rigged up a partial shelter with a tarpaulin. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    下雨的时候，我用油布草草搭了一个能简单避雨的棚子。

  • Election officials have rigged up speakers to provide voters with music. [VERB PREP. noun]

    负责选举的官员们已经临时安了几个喇叭，让投票者可以同时享受音乐。

SYN set up, build, construct, put up
