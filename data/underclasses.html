underclass
ʌndəʳklɑːs -klæs ˈʌndərˌklæs
Word forms: plural underclasses
N-COUNT [usually singular] 下层社会；社会底层 A country's underclass consists of those members of its population who are poor, and who have little chance of improving their situation. 下层社会；社会底层

  • The basic problems of the inner-city underclass are inadequate housing and lack of jobs.

    老城区底层人群面临的基本问题是住房紧张和就业不足。

  • Welfare has become identified with the long-term poor, the underclass.

    福利已经与长期贫困的下层社会联系在了一起。
