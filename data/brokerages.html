brokerage
broʊkərɪdʒ ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ
Word forms: plural brokerages
1 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] 经纪公司；代理公司 A brokerage or a brokerage firm is a company of brokers. 经纪公司；代理公司 [business]

  • ...Japan's four biggest brokerages.

    日本4家最大的代理公司

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] （经纪人收取的）佣金，中介费，提成 A brokerage fee or commission is the money charged by a broker for his or her services. （经纪人收取的）佣金，中介费，提成
