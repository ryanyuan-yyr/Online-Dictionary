shop around
PHRASAL VERB 货比三家再买;逐店比较后选购 If you shop around, you go to different shops or companies in order to compare the prices and quality of goods or services before you decide to buy them. 货比三家再买;逐店比较后选购

  • Prices may vary so it's well worth shopping around before you buy. [VERB PREP.]

    价格可能会有高有低，所以在买之前很有必要货比三家。

  • He shopped around for a firm that would be flexible. [VERB PREP. + for]

    他到处寻觅一家可灵活变通的公司。

SYN search, look, hunt, cast around
