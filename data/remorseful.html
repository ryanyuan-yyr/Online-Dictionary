remorseful
rɪmɔːʳsfʊl rɪˈmɔrsfəl
ADJ-GRADED 懊悔的;悔恨的;自责的 If you are remorseful, you feel very guilty and sorry about something wrong that you have done. 懊悔的;悔恨的;自责的

  • He was genuinely remorseful.

    他真心实意地感到懊悔。

SYN regretful, sorry, guilty, sad  
remorsefully

  • 'My poor wife!' he said, remorsefully.

    “我可怜的妻子！”他悔恨地说。
