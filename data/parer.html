pare
peəʳ pɛə pɛr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pares, present participle paring, past tense , past participle pared
1 VERB 削去…的皮；削去；剥去 When you pare something, or pare part of it off or away, you cut off its skin or its outer layer. 削去…的皮；削去；剥去

  • Pare the brown skin from the meat with a very sharp knife. [VERB noun + from]

    用一把非常锋利的刀将褐色的皮从肉上剥去。

  • He took out a slab of cheese, pared off a slice and ate it hastily. [VERB noun with adv.]

    他拿出一大块奶酪，切下一片，匆忙吃了。

  • ...thinly pared lemon rind. [VERB-ed]

    削得很薄的柠檬皮

2See also:paring 
3 VERB 削减；减少 If you pare something down or back, or if you pare it, you reduce it. 削减；减少

  • The number of Ministries has been pared down by a third. [be V-ed adv]

    部委的数量已经减少了1/3。

  • The luxury tax won't really do much to pare down the budget deficit. [VERB noun with adv.]

    奢侈品税其实对削减预算赤字并不会有多少帮助。

  • The United States can pare back its military presence in Asia as well. [VERB noun with adv.]

    美国还可以削减其在亚洲的军事力量。

  • Local authorities must pare their budgets. [VERB noun]

    地方政府必须削减预算。

SYN cut back, cut, reduce, crop
