encroachment
ɪnkroʊtʃmənt
Word forms: plural encroachments
N-VAR 侵占;侵蚀;蚕食 You can describe the action or process of encroaching on something as encroachment . 侵占;侵蚀;蚕食 [formal, disapproval]

  • It's a sign of the encroachment of commercialism in medicine. [+ of]

    这是医药界被逐渐商业化的一个的征兆。

  • The problem was to safeguard sites from encroachment by property development.

    问题是要保护遗址免受房地产开发商的侵占。

SYN intrusion, invasion, violation, infringement
