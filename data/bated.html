bated
beɪtɪd ˈbeɪtɪd
PHRASE 焦急地；屏息地 If you wait for something with bated breath, you wait anxiously to find out what will happen. 焦急地；屏息地 [formal]

  • Every Monday the whole office used to wait with bated breath for his report.

    过去每到周一，整个办公室都会焦急地等待他的报告。

  • We listened with bated breath to Grandma's stories of her travels.

    我们屏住呼吸听祖母讲述她的游历故事。
