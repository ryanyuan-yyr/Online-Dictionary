set up
PHRASAL VERB 创建;建立;安排;组建 If you set something up, you create or arrange it. 创建;建立;安排;组建

  • The two sides agreed to set up a commission to investigate claims. [VERB PREP. noun]

    双方同意组建一个委员会来调查那些索赔要求。

  • ...an organization which sets up meetings about issues of interest to women. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    召开会议讨论妇女关心的问题的组织

  • Tell us when and why you started your business and how you went about setting it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    告诉我们你是何时出于何种原因开办了自己的企业，又是如何着手创建的。

set-up ˈsetʌp
(also setup)Word forms: plural set-ups
1 N-COUNT 体制;组织方式;建制 A particular set-up is a particular system or way of organizing something. 体制;组织方式;建制 [informal]

  • It appears to be an idyllic domestic set-up.

    这似乎是一种田园诗般的家庭组成格局。

  • I gradually got rather disillusioned with the whole setup of the university. [+ of]

    渐渐地，我对大学的整个体制感到相当失望。

2 N-COUNT 陷害;栽赃 If you describe a situation as a set-up, you mean that people have planned it in order to deceive you or to make it look as if you have done something wrong. 陷害;栽赃 [informal]

  • He was asked to pick somebody up and bring them to a party, not realizing it was a setup.

    有人让他开车去接几个人来参加聚会，他并没有意识到这是个陷阱。

3 N-SING (软件或硬件的)安装，设置 The set-up of computer hardware or software is the process of installing it and making it ready to use. (软件或硬件的)安装，设置 [computing]

  • The worst part of the set-up is the poor instruction manual.

    安装时最糟糕就是操作指南讲述不够清楚。

  • ... the setup program.

    安装程序
