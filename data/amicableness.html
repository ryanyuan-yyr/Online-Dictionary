amicable
æmɪkəbəl ˈæmɪkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED （关系）友好的，和睦的 When people have an amicable relationship, they are pleasant to each other and solve their problems without quarrelling. （关系）友好的，和睦的

  • The meeting ended on reasonably amicable terms.

    会议在较为友好的气氛中结束了。

  • Our discussions were amicable and productive.

    我们的讨论气氛非常友好并且富有成果。

SYN friendly, kindly, brotherly, civil
