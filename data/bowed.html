bowed
boʊd baʊd baʊd
Pronounced /'bəʊd/ for meaning 1, and /'baʊd/ for meaning 2. 义项1读作 /'bəʊd/，义项2读作/'baʊd/。
1 ADJ-GRADED 成弓形的；弯曲的 Something that is bowed is curved. 成弓形的；弯曲的

  • ...an old lady with bowed legs.

    罗圈腿的老妇人

2 ADJ-GRADED 弯腰曲背的；佝偻的 If a person's body is bowed, it is bent forward. 弯腰曲背的；佝偻的

  • He walked aimlessly along street after street, head down and shoulders bowed.

    他低着头、垂着肩，漫无目的地走过一条条街道。

SYN bent, lowered, angled, curved
