unanswerable
ʌnɑːnsərəbəl -æns-
1 ADJ 无法回答的；没有答案的 If you describe a question as unanswerable, you mean that it has no possible answer or that a particular person cannot possibly answer it. 无法回答的；没有答案的

  • They would ask their mother unanswerable questions.

    他们会问母亲一些无法回答的问题。

SYN insoluble, unexplainable, unresolvable, unascertainable  
2 ADJ 明摆着的；无可辩驳的 If you describe a case or argument as unanswerable, you think that it is obviously true or correct and that nobody could disagree with it. 明摆着的；无可辩驳的

  • He actually became convinced that the nurses had an unanswerable case.

    他实际上已经相信了护士们的情况确实如此。

  • The argument for recruiting McGregor was unanswerable.

    录用麦格雷戈的主张毋庸置疑。

SYN indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable, conclusive
