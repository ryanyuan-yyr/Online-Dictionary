bottom
bɒtəm ˈbɑtəm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bottoms, present participle bottoming, past tense , past participle bottomed
1 N-COUNT 底；底部 The bottom of something is the lowest or deepest part of it. 底；底部

  • He sat at the bottom of the stairs. [+ of]

    他坐在最下面的一级楼梯上。

  • Answers can be found at the bottom of page 8. [+ of]

    在第8页末尾可以找到答案。

  • ...the bottom of the sea. [+ of]

    海底

SYN lowest part, base, foot, bed  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 最下方的；底部的；底层的 The bottom thing or layer in a series of things or layers is the lowest one. 最下方的；底部的；底层的

  • There's an extra duvet in the bottom drawer of the cupboard.

    橱柜最底层的抽屉里还有一床羽绒被。

SYN lowest, last, base, ground  
3 N-COUNT 底面；内底面；外底面 The bottom of an object is the flat surface at its lowest point. You can also refer to the inside or outside of this surface as the bottom . 底面；内底面；外底面

  • Spread the onion slices on the bottom of the dish.

    把洋葱片铺在盘底。

  • ...the bottom of their shoes.

    他们的鞋底

  • ...a suitcase with a false bottom.

    活底手提箱

SYN underside, sole, underneath, lower side  
4 N-SING （某市场或产业的)最差状况，低谷 If you say that the bottom has dropped or fallen out of a market or industry, you mean that people have stopped buying the products it sells. （某市场或产业的)最差状况，低谷 [business, journalism]

  • The bottom had fallen out of the city's property market.

    该市的房地产市场已陷入极度低迷。

5 N-SING （街道、花园的）尽头，远端 The bottom of a street or garden is the end farthest away from you or from your house. （街道、花园的）尽头，远端 [British]

  • ...the Cathedral at the bottom of the street. [+ of]

    街道尽头的大教堂

SYN end, far end, furthest point, furthest part  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 end
6 N-SING （桌子的）边角；（床）脚，尾 The bottom of a table is the end farthest away from where you are sitting. The bottom of a bed is the end where you usually rest your feet. （桌子的）边角；（床）脚，尾 [British]

  • Malone sat down on the bottom of the bed. [+ of]

    马隆在床脚坐下了。

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 end
7 N-SING （组织机构或职业架构的）最底层，基层 The bottom of an organization or career structure is the lowest level in it, where new employees often start. （组织机构或职业架构的）最底层，基层

  • He had worked in the theatre for many years, starting at the bottom.

    他从最基础的干起，已经从事戏剧工作很多年了。

  • ...a contract researcher at the bottom of the pay scale. [+ of]

    工资级别最低的合同研究人员

SYN lowest level, lowest position, least successful part  
8 N-SING （调查、测试或联赛中的）表现最差者，最后一名 If someone is bottom or at the bottom in a survey, test, or league their performance is worse than that of all the other people involved. （调查、测试或联赛中的）表现最差者，最后一名

  • He was always bottom of the class. [+ of]

    他一直是全班的最后一名。

  • The team is close to bottom of the League. [+ of]

    这支球队快跌至联赛末席了。

9 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 臀部；屁股 Your bottom is the part of your body that you sit on. 臀部；屁股

  • If there was one thing she could change about her body it would be her bottom.

    如果能对自己的身体做一点改变，那她会选择臀部。

10 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft n NOUN] （比基尼泳装的）泳裤；（田径服、睡衣的）裤子 The lower part of a bikini, tracksuit, or pair of pyjamas can be referred to as the bottoms or the bottom. （比基尼泳装的）泳裤；（田径服、睡衣的）裤子

  • She wore blue tracksuit bottoms.

    她穿了一件蓝色田径裤。

  • ...a skimpy bikini bottom.

    极其暴露的比基尼泳裤

11 See also: -bottomed  rock bottom 
12 PHRASE 实质上；实际上 You use at bottom to emphasize that you are stating what you think is the real nature of something or the real truth about a situation. 实质上；实际上 [emphasis]

  • The two systems are, at bottom, conceptual models.

    这两个系统实质上是两种概念模式。

  • At bottom, such an attitude is born not of concern for your welfare, but out of fear of losing you.

    实际上，这种态度并非源自对你幸福的关心，而是出于害怕失去你。

13 PHRASE 是…的真正原因；是…的根源 If something is at the bottom of a problem or unpleasant situation, it is the real cause of it. 是…的真正原因；是…的根源

  • Often I find that anger and resentment are at the bottom of the problem.

    我常常发现愤怒和不满是问题的真正根源。

14 PHRASE 打心眼里；衷心地 You can say that you mean something from the bottom of your heart to emphasize that you mean it very sincerely. 打心眼里；衷心地 [emphasis]

  • I'm happy, and I mean that from the bottom of my heart.

    我很开心，而且是那种发自肺腑的开心。

  • I want to thank everyone from the bottom of my heart.

    我要衷心感谢每一个人。

15 PHRASE 找到…的真正起因；查寻到…的根源 If you want to get to the bottom of a problem, you want to solve it by finding out its real cause. 找到…的真正起因；查寻到…的根源

  • I have to get to the bottom of this mess.

    我必须查清造成这一混乱局面的真正原因。

16 CONVENTION 干杯 Some people say bottoms up to each other just before drinking an alcoholic drink. 干杯 [informal]
