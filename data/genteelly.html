genteel
dʒentiːl dʒɛnˈtil
1 ADJ-GRADED 彬彬有礼的；有教养的；上流社会的；装出绅士派头的 A genteel person is respectable and well-mannered, and comes or seems to come from a high social class. 彬彬有礼的；有教养的；上流社会的；装出绅士派头的

  • It was a place to which genteel families came in search of health and quiet.

    这是上流社会家庭寻求健康和静谧的场所。

  • ...two maiden ladies with genteel manners and voices.

    两位举止文雅、嗓音轻柔的少女

SYN refined, cultured, mannerly, elegant  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （地方）幽静的,古朴单调的 A genteel place or area is quiet and traditional, but may also be old-fashioned and dull. （地方）幽静的,古朴单调的

  • ...the genteel towns of Winchester and Chichester.

    古朴幽静的温彻斯特和奇切斯特
