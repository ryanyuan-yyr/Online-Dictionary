pastiche
pæstiːʃ pæˈstiːʃ pæˈstɪtʃəʊ pæsˈtiʃ pɑsˈtiʃ
Word forms: plural pastiches
N-VAR （文学作品、音乐等的）模仿作品；（融合各种风格的）集成之作 A pastiche is something such as a piece of writing or music in which the style is copied from somewhere else, or which contains a mixture of different styles. （文学作品、音乐等的）模仿作品；（融合各种风格的）集成之作 [formal]

  • Peter Baker's bathroom is a brilliant pastiche of expensive interior design. [+ of]

    彼得·贝克的浴室是高档室内设计的集大成之作。
