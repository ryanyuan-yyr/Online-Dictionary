dowager
daʊədʒəʳ ˈdaʊədʒər
Word forms: plural dowagers
1 ADJ [ADJ n, n ADJ] (已故公爵、君主等的)孀居贵妇 You use dowager to refer to the wife of a dead duke, emperor, or other man of high rank. (已故公爵、君主等的)孀居贵妇

  • ...the Dowager Countess Spencer.

    斯潘塞伯爵遗孀

  • Nobody was allowed to eat in the Empress Dowager's presence.

    在皇帝遗孀面前任何人不得进餐。

Dowager is also a noun.
2 N-COUNT 气度不凡的老妇;老年贵妇 If you describe a woman as a dowager, you mean that she is old and rich or looks important. 气度不凡的老妇;老年贵妇 [literary]

  • ...like stately dowagers on a cruise.

    像游轮上气宇不凡的老年贵妇
