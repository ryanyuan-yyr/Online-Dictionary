tenth
tenθ tɛnθ
Word forms: plural tenths
1 ORD 第十 The tenth item in a series is the one that you count as number ten. 第十
2 FRACTION 十分之一 A tenth is one of ten equal parts of something. 十分之一

  • We received only two tenths of an inch of rain during the entire month of June.

    整个 6 月，我们这里的降水量只有0.2英寸。
