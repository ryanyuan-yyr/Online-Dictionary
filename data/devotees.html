devotee
devətiː ˌdɛvəˈtiː ˌdɛvəˈti ˌdɛvəˈteɪ
Word forms: plural devotees
1 N-COUNT （狂热的）爱好者，热衷者 Someone who is a devotee of a subject or activity is very enthusiastic about it. （狂热的）爱好者，热衷者

  • Mr Carpenter is obviously a devotee of Britten's music. [+ of]

    很显然，卡彭特先生是布里顿音乐的狂热爱好者。

SYN enthusiast, fan, supporter, follower  
2 N-COUNT （某一宗教团体的）教徒 A devotee is a member of a religious group. （某一宗教团体的）教徒

  • Monks shave their heads, as do devotees of the Hare Krishna movement.

    和尚要剃度，克利须那派教徒也一样。

SYN follower, student, supporter, pupil
