secular
sekjʊləʳ ˈsɛkjələr
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 世俗的;现世的;非宗教的 You use secular to describe things that have no connection with religion. 世俗的;现世的;非宗教的

  • He spoke about preserving the country as a secular state.

    他谈及了保持该国世俗化的问题。

  • ...secular and religious education.

    世俗教育和宗教教育

SYN worldly, state, lay, earthly
