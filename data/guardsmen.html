guardsman
gɑːʳdzmən ˈgɑrdzmən
(also Guardsman)Word forms: plural guardsmen
1 N-COUNT （英国）近卫军士兵 In Britain, a guardsman is a soldier who is a member of one of the regiments of Guards. （英国）近卫军士兵
2 N-COUNT （美国）国民警卫队士兵 In the United States, a guardsman is a soldier who is a member of the National Guard. （美国）国民警卫队士兵
