doubter
daʊtəʳ
Word forms: plural doubters
N-COUNT [usually plural] 持怀疑态度的人;(尤指在宗教或政治方面)信仰不坚定的人 If you refer to people as doubters, you mean that they have doubts about something, especially their religious or political system. 持怀疑态度的人;(尤指在宗教或政治方面)信仰不坚定的人

  • Some doubters fear this news may not be as good as it appears.

    有些持怀疑态度的人担心这条新闻并非像报道那般美好。

SYN sceptic, questioner, disbeliever, agnostic
