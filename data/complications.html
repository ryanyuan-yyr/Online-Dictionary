complication
kɒmplɪkeɪʃən ˌkɑmplɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural complications
1 N-COUNT 使复杂化的难题(或困难) A complication is a problem or difficulty that makes a situation harder to deal with. 使复杂化的难题(或困难)

  • The age difference was a complication to the relationship. [+ to]

    年龄差异使得双方交往更加困难。

  • An added complication is the growing concern for the environment.

    还有个问题就是对环境的日益关注。

SYN problem, difficulty, obstacle, drawback  
2 N-COUNT 并发症 A complication is a medical problem that occurs as a result of another illness or disease. 并发症

  • Blindness is a common complication of diabetes. [+ of]

    失明是糖尿病常见的并发症。

  • He died of complications from a heart attack. [+ from]

    他死于心脏病发作引起的并发症。
