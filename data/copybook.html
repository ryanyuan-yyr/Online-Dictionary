copybook
kɒpibʊk ˈkɑpiˌbʊk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 精确的;准确的 A copybook action is done perfectly, according to established rules. 精确的;准确的 [mainly British]

  • Yuri gave a copybook display.

    尤里的表演堪称完美。

2 PHRASE 败坏…的名声 If you blot your copybook, you spoil your good reputation by doing something wrong. 败坏…的名声 [mainly British]

  • Alec blotted his copybook — got sent home for bad behaviour.

    亚历克因为行为恶劣被遣回了家，一下子名声扫地。
