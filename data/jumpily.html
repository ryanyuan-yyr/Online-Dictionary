jumpy
dʒʌmpi ˈdʒʌmpi
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 坐立不安的；胆战心惊的 If you are jumpy, you are nervous or worried about something. 坐立不安的；胆战心惊的 [informal]

  • I told myself not to be so jumpy.

    我告诉自己别这么提心吊胆的。

  • When he spoke his voice was jumpy.

    他说话的时候声音听起来很紧张。

SYN nervous, anxious, tense, shaky
