facilitate
fəsɪlɪteɪt fəˈsɪləˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense facilitates, present participle facilitating, past tense , past participle facilitated
VERB 促进;使便利 To facilitate an action or process, especially one that you would like to happen, means to make it easier or more likely to happen. 促进;使便利

  • The new airport will facilitate the development of tourism. [VERB noun]

    新机场将促进旅游业的发展。

  • He argued that the economic recovery had been facilitated by his tough stance. [be VERB-ed]

    他认为他的强硬立场推动了经济的复苏。

SYN further, help, forward, promote
