immersion
ɪmɜːʳʃən ɪˈmɜrʒən ɪˈmɜrʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 潜心钻研;专心 Someone's immersion in a subject is their complete involvement in it. 潜心钻研;专心

  • ...long-term assignments that allowed them total immersion in their subjects. [+ in]

    可以让他们潜心研究课题的长期任务

SYN involvement, concentration, preoccupation, absorption  
2 N-UNCOUNT 浸没;浸泡 Immersion of something in a liquid means putting it into the liquid so that it is completely covered. 浸没;浸泡

  • The wood had become swollen from prolonged immersion.

    因长时间浸泡，木头发胀了。

SYN dipping, submerging, plunging, ducking
