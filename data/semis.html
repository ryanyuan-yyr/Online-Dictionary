semi
semi semaɪ ˈsɛmˌaɪ
Word forms: plural semis
The pronunciation /'semaɪ/ can also be used for meaning 3. 义项 3 也可读作 /'semaɪ/。
1 N-COUNT 半独立式住宅 A semi is a semi-detached house. 半独立式住宅 [British, informal]
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (体育比赛中的)半决赛 In a sporting competition, the semis are the semi-finals. (体育比赛中的)半决赛 [British, informal]

  • He reached the semis after beating Lendl in the quarterfinal.

    在 1/4 决赛中他击败了伦德尔，闯入半决赛。

3 N-COUNT 半拖车；半挂车 A semi is a semi-trailer . 半拖车；半挂车 [US]
semi- semi- ˈsɛmɪ ˈsɛmi ˈsɛmaɪ ˈsɛmə
