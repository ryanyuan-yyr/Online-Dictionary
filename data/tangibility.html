tangible
tændʒɪbəl ˈtændʒəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 清晰可见的;摸得着的;感觉得到的;明显的 If something is tangible, it is clear enough or definite enough to be easily seen, felt, or noticed. 清晰可见的;摸得着的;感觉得到的;明显的

  • There should be some tangible evidence that the economy is starting to recover.

    应该有明显迹象表明经济开始复苏了。

  • The relief was almost tangible.

    这种解脱几乎可以感觉得到。

SYN definite, real, positive, solid  
tangibly

  • This tangibly demonstrated that the world situation could be improved.

    这清楚地表明世界局势是可以改善的。
