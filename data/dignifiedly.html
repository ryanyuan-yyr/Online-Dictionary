dignified
dɪgnɪfaɪd ˈdɪgnəˌfaɪd
ADJ-GRADED 有尊严的;庄严的;高贵的 If you say that someone or something is dignified, you mean they are calm, impressive and deserve respect. 有尊严的;庄严的;高贵的

  • He seemed a very dignified and charming man.

    他看上去很高贵，富有魅力。

  • Mr Smith is maintaining a dignified silence.

    史密斯先生庄重地保持着沉默。

SYN distinguished, august, reserved, imposing
