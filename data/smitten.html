smitten
smɪtən ˈsmɪtən ˈsmɪtn
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 被迷住的;着了迷的;神魂颠倒的 If you are smitten, you find someone so attractive that you are or seem to be in love with them. 被迷住的;着了迷的;神魂颠倒的

  • They were totally smitten with each other. [+ with/by]

    他们完全被对方迷住了。

SYN infatuated, charmed, captivated, beguiled  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ with/by n] (被…)打动的;醉心(于…)的 If you are smitten by something, you are very impressed by it and enthusiastic about it. (被…)打动的;醉心(于…)的

  • Simon Fraser was smitten by the landscapes he found in the wild southwest of the United States.

    西蒙·弗雷泽深深地陶醉于自己在美国西南部所发现的这片原始美景中。

3 Smitten is the past participle of smite.
