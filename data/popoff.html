pop off
1 一命呜呼；翘辫子 When someone pops off, they die. 一命呜呼；翘辫子

  • None of Olive's relatives looked likely to pop off for the time being, thank God. [VERB PREP.]

    感谢上帝，奥利芙所有的亲戚看上去暂时都没有翘辫子之虞。

2 离开；去别处 If you pop off, you leave and go somewhere else. 离开；去别处

  • I'll make the tea and you pop off for a while. [VERB PREP.]

    我来备茶，你去歇一会儿吧。

  • She should pop off back to Scotland. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    她应该回苏格兰去。

3 哇啦哇啦地乱发牢骚（或议论）；情绪激动地乱写 If someone pops off, they say or write something very angrily or in a very emotional way. 哇啦哇啦地乱发牢骚（或议论）；情绪激动地乱写

  • The others are meeting to see what they can do to shut him up. He's been popping off to the press. [VERB PREP.]

    其他人正在碰头，看怎么样能让他闭嘴。他一直在对报界哇啦哇啦地胡言乱语。
