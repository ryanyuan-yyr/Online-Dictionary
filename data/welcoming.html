welcoming
welkəmɪŋ ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 热情的;友好的;好客的 If someone is welcoming or if they behave in a welcoming way, they are friendly to you when you arrive somewhere, so that you feel happy and accepted. 热情的;友好的;好客的

  • When we arrived at her house Susan was very welcoming.

    当我们到达苏珊家时，她非常热情。

  • Her face spread in a welcoming smile.

    她脸上展露出热情的笑容。

2 ADJ-GRADED （建筑物或房间）舒适的，惬意的 A welcoming building or room looks pleasant and you feel as though you would be happy and comfortable spending time there. （建筑物或房间）舒适的，惬意的 [mainly British]

  • The restaurant is small and green and very welcoming.

    这家餐厅小巧精致，绿意盎然，令人感到非常惬意。
