reel in
PHRASAL VERB 收线;绕线 If you reel in something such as a fish, you pull it towards you by winding around a reel the wire or line that it is attached to. 收线;绕线 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Gleacher reeled in the first fish. [VERB PREP. noun]

    格里奇收线钓起第一条鱼。

  • The crew of the US space shuttle Atlantis were preparing to reel in the craft.

    美国“亚特兰蒂斯”号航天飞机上的宇航员正准备收回该航天器。
