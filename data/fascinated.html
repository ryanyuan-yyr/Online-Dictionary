fascinated
fæsɪneɪtɪd ˈfæsɪneɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 极感兴趣的;入迷的 If you are fascinated by something, you find it very interesting and attractive, and your thoughts tend to concentrate on it. 极感兴趣的;入迷的 [Also + with]

  • I sat on the stairs and watched, fascinated.

    我坐在楼梯上出神地观望。

  • A new generation of scientists became fascinated by dinosaurs. [+ by]

    新一代科学家对恐龙研究着了迷。

SYN entranced, charmed, absorbed, very interested
