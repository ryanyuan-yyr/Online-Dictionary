grub up
(also grub out)
PHRASAL VERB 掘出，挖出（植物） If you grub up trees or plants, or grub them out, you dig them out of the ground, usually because they are no longer wanted. 掘出，挖出（植物） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Farmers are grubbing up ancient varieties of crops. [VERB PREP. noun]

    农民正在掘除自古以来就一直种植的各种作物。

  • One by one, orchards were grubbed up. [VERB PREP. noun]

    果园被一个接一个地铲掉了。

SYN unearth, uncover, pull up, uproot
