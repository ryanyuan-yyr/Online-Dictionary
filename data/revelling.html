revel
revəl ˈrɛvəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , 3rd person singular present tense revels, present participle revelling, past tense , past participle revelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 reveling, reveled
1 VERB 陶醉于;沉湎于 If you revel in a situation or experience, you enjoy it very much. 陶醉于;沉湎于

  • Revelling in her freedom, she took a hotel room and stayed for several days. [VERB + in]

    陶醉在自由中的她在宾馆开了一间房，住了几天。

  • Cats positively revel in heat, whether natural or man-made. [VERB in noun]

    猫确实喜暖，不管是自然的还是人工创造的温暖环境。

SYN celebrate, rave [British , slang] , carouse, live it up [informal]  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 狂欢;作乐;欢闹 Revels are noisy celebrations. 狂欢;作乐;欢闹 [literary]

  • The revels often last until dawn.

    狂欢常常持续到黎明。

SYN merrymaking, party, celebration, rave [British , slang]
