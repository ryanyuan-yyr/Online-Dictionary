PREFIX (用于构成名词或形容词)中间，中部，中期 Mid- is used to form nouns or adjectives that refer to the middle part of a particular period of time, or the middle point of a particular place. (用于构成名词或形容词)中间，中部，中期

    18世纪中叶

    戴维丝35岁左右。
