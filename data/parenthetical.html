parenthetical
pærənθetɪkəl ˌpærənˈθɛtɪkəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 作为插入语的；补充说明的 A parenthetical remark or section is put into something written or spoken but is not essential to it. 作为插入语的；补充说明的

  • Fox was making a long parenthetical remark about his travels on the border of the country.

    福克斯正在插入一大段题外话，讲他在该国边境游历的故事。

SYN interposed, incidental, explanatory, qualifying  
parenthetically

  • Well, parenthetically, I was trying to quit smoking at the time.

    嗯，插一句，我那时正在努力戒烟。

  • And what, we may ask parenthetically, does it mean?

    我们想顺带问一句，这是什么意思？
