militarist
mɪlɪtərɪst ˈmɪlətərɪst
Word forms: plural militarists
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 军国主义者；穷兵黩武者 If you describe someone as a militarist, you mean that they want their country's armed forces to be strengthened in order to make it more powerful. 军国主义者；穷兵黩武者 [disapproval]

  • They became overheated nationalists, militarists, and they were out to conquer.

    他们变成了过分狂热的民族主义分子和军国主义分子，并想要征服别国。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 同 militaristic Militarist means the same as militaristic . 同 militaristic [disapproval]

  • ...militarist policies.

    军国主义政策
