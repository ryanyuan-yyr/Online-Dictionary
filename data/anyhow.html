anyhow
enihaʊ ˈəniˌhaʊ
1 ADV 同anyway Anyhow means the same as anyway . 同anyway
2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 马马虎虎地；杂乱无章地 If you do something anyhow, you do it in a careless or untidy way. 马马虎虎地；杂乱无章地

  • ...her long legs which she displayed all anyhow getting in and out of her car.

    她上下车时不经意露出的长腿

SYN carelessly, haphazardly, heedlessly
