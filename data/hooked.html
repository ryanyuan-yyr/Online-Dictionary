hooked
hʊkt hʊkt
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 钩状的 If you describe something as hooked, you mean that it is shaped like a hook. 钩状的

  • He was thin and tall, with a hooked nose.

    他又瘦又高，长着鹰钩鼻。

  • ...hooked claws.

    钩状爪

SYN bent, curved, beaked, aquiline  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （被…）迷住的;（对…）入迷的 If you are hooked on something, you enjoy it so much that it takes up a lot of your interest and attention. （被…）迷住的;（对…）入迷的 [informal]

  • Many of the leaders have become hooked on power and money. [+ on]

    很多领导人都变得贪恋权力和金钱。

  • Open this book and read a few pages and you will be hooked.

    打开这本书读上几页，你就会沉迷其中。

SYN obsessed, addicted, taken, devoted  
3 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （吸毒）上瘾的 If you are hooked on a drug, you are addicted to it. （吸毒）上瘾的 [informal]

  • He spent a number of years hooked on cocaine, heroin and alcohol. [+ on]

    他有好几年时间吸食可卡因和海洛因成瘾，还整日酗酒。

SYN addicted, dependent, using [informal] , having a habit
