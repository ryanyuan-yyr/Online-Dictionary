Pronounced (-d) after a vowel sound and (-əd) after a consonant sound. 元音后读(-d),辅音后读(-əd)。
1 "had"做助动词的口语形式 -'d is a spoken form of "had," especially when "had" is an auxiliary verb. It is added to the end of the pronoun which is the subject of the verb. For example, "you had" can be shortened to "you'd."
