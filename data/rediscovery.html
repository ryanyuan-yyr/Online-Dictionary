rediscovery
riːdɪskʌvəri ˌriːdɪˈskʌvərɪ
Word forms: plural rediscoveries
N-VAR 重新发现；再发现 The rediscovery of something good that you had forgotten or lost is the fact or process of becoming aware of it again or finding it again. 重新发现；再发现

  • The best part of his expedition had been the rediscovery of his natural passion for making things. [+ of]

    他这次探险的最大收获就是重新发现了自己对动手制作物品与生俱来的热忱。
