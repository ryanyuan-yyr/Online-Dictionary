claustrophobia
klɔːstrəfoʊbiə ˌklɔstrəˈfoʊbiə
1 N-UNCOUNT 幽闭恐惧症 Someone who suffers from claustrophobia feels very uncomfortable or anxious when they are in small or enclosed places. 幽闭恐惧症
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN of n] 幽闭恐惧的感觉;受限制产生的不适感 If you talk about the claustrophobia of a place or situation, you mean it makes you feel uncomfortable or unhappy because you are enclosed or restricted. 幽闭恐惧的感觉;受限制产生的不适感

  • In the claustrophobia of her parents' house she had no stimulus for creativity.

    在父母那所令人感到封闭恐惧的房子里，没有任何东西可以刺激她的创作。
