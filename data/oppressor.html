oppressor
əpresəʳ
Word forms: plural oppressors
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 压迫者；压制者 An oppressor is a person or group of people that is treating another person or group of people cruelly or unfairly. 压迫者；压制者

  • Lacking sovereignty, they could organise no defence against their oppressors.

    没有主权他们就无法组织起来反抗压迫者。

SYN persecutor, tyrant, bully, scourge
