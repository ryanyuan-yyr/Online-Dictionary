exacting
ɪgzæktɪŋ ɛgˈzæktɪŋ ɪgˈzæktɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 费劲的;苛刻的；要求严格的 You use exacting to describe something or someone that demands hard work and a great deal of care. 费劲的;苛刻的；要求严格的

  • The Duke was not well enough to carry out such an exacting task.

    公爵身体不太好，无法执行那么艰巨的任务。

  • Privately they seem to have the same exacting standards.

    私下里，他们似乎都有同样苛刻的标准。

SYN demanding, hard, taxing, difficult
