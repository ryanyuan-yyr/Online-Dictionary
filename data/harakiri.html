hara-kiri
hærə kɪri ˌhærəˈkɪrɪ ˌhærɪˈkɑːrɪ ˈhɑrəˈkɪri ˈhærəˈkɪri ˈhɛriˈkɛri
N-UNCOUNT （旧时日本男子为免受羞辱而进行的）剖腹自尽 In former times, if a Japanese man committed hara-kiri, he killed himself by cutting his own stomach open, in order to avoid dishonour. （旧时日本男子为免受羞辱而进行的）剖腹自尽
SYN ritual suicide, suicide, seppuku
