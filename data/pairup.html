pair up
PHRASAL VERB-RECIP-ERG （尤指为共同做事）（使）成对，（使）配对 If people pair up or are paired up, they form a pair, especially in order to do something together. （尤指为共同做事）（使）成对，（使）配对 [Also V n P + with]

  • They asked us to pair up with the person next to us and form teams. [VERB PREP. + with]

    他们让我们与身边的人配对组成小组。

  • Men and teenage girls pair up to dance. [VERB PREP.]

    男人和少女结伴跳舞。

  • Smokers and nonsmokers are paired up as roommates. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    吸烟者和不吸烟者被配对儿成为室友。
