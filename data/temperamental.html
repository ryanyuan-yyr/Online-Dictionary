temperamental
temprəmentəl ˌtɛmpərəˈmɛntəl ˌtɛmprəˈmɛntəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 爱发脾气的;脾气乖张的;喜怒无常的 If you say that someone is temperamental, you are criticizing them for not being calm or quiet by nature, but having moods that change often and suddenly. 爱发脾气的;脾气乖张的;喜怒无常的 [disapproval]

  • He is very temperamental and critical.

    他好使性子，还爱挑毛病。

  • ...a man given to temperamental outbursts and paranoia.

    喜怒无常、生性多疑的人

SYN moody, emotional, touchy, sensitive  
2 ADJ-GRADED (机器、汽车)老出问题的，易出故障的，性能不稳的 If you describe something such as a machine or car as temperamental, you mean that it often does not work properly. (机器、汽车)老出问题的，易出故障的，性能不稳的

  • I first started cruising in yachts with temperamental petrol engines.

    我刚开始驾驶游艇航行时，艇上的汽油发动机老出毛病。

  • Vickers machine-guns could be temperamental.

    维克斯机枪有时会出故障。

SYN unreliable, unpredictable, undependable, inconsistent
