dastardly
dæstəʳdli ˈdæstərdli
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] (行为)卑鄙的，卑劣的，恶劣的 If you describe an action as dastardly, you mean it is wicked and intended to hurt someone. (行为)卑鄙的，卑劣的，恶劣的 [old-fashioned]

  • He described the killing as a dastardly act.

    他把这起谋杀称为极其卑劣的行为。

  • ...a dastardly attack on the queen.

    对女王的恶意攻击

SYN despicable, mean, low, base  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] (人)卑鄙的，歹毒的，邪恶的 If you describe a person as dastardly, you mean they are wicked. (人)卑鄙的，歹毒的，邪恶的 [old-fashioned]

  • ...the heiress who is badly treated by her dastardly uncle.

    受到歹毒的叔叔虐待的女继承人
