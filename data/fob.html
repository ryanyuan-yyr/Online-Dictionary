fob
fɒb fɑb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fobs, present participle fobbing, past tense , past participle fobbed
1 N-COUNT (旧时的)怀表短链 In former times, a fob was a short chain or piece of cloth which fastened a man's watch to his clothing. (旧时的)怀表短链
2 PHRASAL VERB 敷衍;搪塞 If someone fobs you off, they tell you something just to stop you asking questions. 敷衍;搪塞

  • I've asked her about it but she fobs me off.

    我问过她这件事，但她一味搪塞。

  • Don't be fobbed off with excuses.

    不要被各种借口给蒙骗了。

fob off
