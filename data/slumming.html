slum
slʌm slʌm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural slums, present participle slumming, past tense , past participle slummed
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 贫民区;贫民窟 A slum is an area of a city where living conditions are very bad and where the houses are in bad condition. 贫民区;贫民窟

  • ...a slum area of St Louis.

    圣路易斯的贫民区

  • ...inner-city slums in the old cities of the north and east.

    北部和东部老城市里的市中心贫民区

SYN hovel, ghetto, shanty  
2 VERB [usually cont] 逛贫民窟;体验下层社会的生活 If someone is slumming it or is slumming, they are spending time in a place or in conditions that are at a much lower social level than they are used to. 逛贫民窟;体验下层社会的生活

  • ...rich kids slumming it. [VERB it]

    体验下层社会生活的有钱人的孩子

  • ...aristocratic types who enjoyed slumming around in musty old Scottish castles. [VERB]

    喜欢在发霉的苏格兰古堡里体验苦日子的贵族阶层
