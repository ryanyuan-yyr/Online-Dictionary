bemuse
bɪmjuːz biˈmjuz bɪˈmjuz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bemuses, present participle bemusing, past tense , past participle bemused
VERB 使困惑；使不解；使茫然 If something bemuses you, it puzzles or confuses you. 使困惑；使不解；使茫然

  • The sheer quantity of detail would bemuse even the most clear-headed author. [VERB noun]

    光是这么多的细节就会让哪怕是头脑最清醒的作者感到困惑。

SYN puzzle, stun, confuse, overwhelm
