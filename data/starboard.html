starboard
stɑːʳbəʳd ˈstɑːbəd ˈstɑrbərd ˈstɑrˌbɔrd
ADJ (船舶的)右舷的，右侧的 In sailing, the starboard side of a ship is the right side when you are on it and facing towards the front. (船舶的)右舷的，右侧的 [technical]

  • He detected a ship moving down the starboard side of the submarine.

    他发现有一艘船正在潜艇的右侧航行。

Starboard is also a noun.

  • I could see the fishing boat to starboard.

    我能看到右侧的那艘渔船。
