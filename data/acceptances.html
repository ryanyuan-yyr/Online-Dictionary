acceptance
ækseptəns ækˈsɛptəns əkˈsɛptəns
Word forms: plural acceptances
1 N-VAR [oft poss NOUN] 接受;赞同 Acceptance of an offer or a proposal is the act of saying yes to it or agreeing to it. 接受;赞同

  • The Party is being degraded by its acceptance of secret donations. [+ of]

    该党因私下接受捐赠而蒙羞。

  • I sent them more than 6,000 cartoons before I had my one and only acceptance by them.

    我寄给他们6,000多幅漫画，最后才有1幅得以采用。

  • Several shareholders have withdrawn earlier acceptances of the offer.

    几个股东对于先前同意的报价又反悔了。

  • ...a letter of acceptance.

    中标函

  • ...his acceptance speech for the Nobel Peace Prize.

    他的诺贝尔和平奖获奖感言

SYN accepting, taking, receiving, obtaining  
2 N-UNCOUNT 相信；承认 If there is acceptance of an idea, most people believe or agree that it is true. 相信；承认

  • ...a theory that is steadily gaining acceptance.

    逐步得到承认的理论

  • There was a general acceptance that the defence budget would shrink.

    人们普遍相信国防预算将会缩减。

SYN acknowledgement, agreement, belief, approval  
3 N-UNCOUNT 忍受;容忍;承受 Your acceptance of a situation, especially an unpleasant or difficult one, is an attitude or feeling that you cannot change it and that you must get used to it. 忍受;容忍;承受

  • Popular acceptance of authority had been an early aim of the Nationalists.

    国家主义者的一个早期目标是推动民众对权威的接受。

  • ...his calm acceptance of whatever comes his way. [+ of]

    他遇事泰然处之的态度

4 N-UNCOUNT [usually with supplement] （对新产品的）接受，认可 If there is acceptance of a new product, people start to like it and get used to it. （对新产品的）接受，认可

  • Car manufacturers are already producing small numbers of electric vehicles to test customer acceptance.

    汽车制造商们已经开始生产少量的电动车辆以测试顾客的接受程度。

  • Avant-garde music to this day has not found general public acceptance.

    先锋音乐时至今日都没有得到大众的广泛认可。

SYN approval, following, support, embracing  
5 N-UNCOUNT 接纳;欢迎 Acceptance of someone into a group means beginning to think of them as part of the group and to act in a friendly way towards them. 接纳;欢迎

  • ...an effort to ensure that the disabled achieve real acceptance.

    为确保残疾人真正得到接纳而作出的努力

SYN recognition, appreciation, acknowledgment
