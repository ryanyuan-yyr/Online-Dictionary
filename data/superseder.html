supersede
suːpəʳsiːd ˌsupərˈsid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense supersedes, present participle superseding, past tense , past participle superseded
VERB [usually passive] 取代，替代(陈旧或令人不满意的事物) If something is superseded by something newer, it is replaced because it has become old-fashioned or unacceptable. 取代，替代(陈旧或令人不满意的事物) [Also VERB n]

  • Hand tools are relics of the past that have now been superseded by the machine. [be VERB-ed]

    手工工具是历史的遗物，现在已经被机器所取代。
