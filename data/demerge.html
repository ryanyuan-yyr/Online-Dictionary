demerge
diːmɜːʳdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense demerges, present participle demerging, past tense , past participle demerged
V-ERG 拆分，分立(大公司) If a large company is demerged or demerges, it is broken down into several smaller companies. 拆分，分立(大公司) [British, business] [Also VERB n from n]

  • Zeneca was at last demerged from its parent firm, ICI. [be V-ed from n]

    捷利康公司终于从其母公司帝国化工集团中分离了出来。

  • His ultimate aim is to demerge the group. [VERB noun]

    他的最终目的是要拆分这个集团。

  • Many companies merge and few demerge. [VERB]

    有很多公司合并，而分立的却很少。
