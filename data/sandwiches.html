sandwich
sænwɪdʒ -wɪtʃ ˈsændˌwɪtʃ ˈsænˌwɪtʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense sandwiches, present participle sandwiching, past tense , past participle sandwiched
1 N-COUNT 三明治;夹心面包片 A sandwich usually consists of two slices of bread with a layer of food such as cheese or meat between them. 三明治;夹心面包片

  • ...a ham sandwich.

    火腿三明治

2 VERB 夹裹;在…中夹入;把…夹入 If you sandwich two things together with something else, you put that other thing between them. If you sandwich one thing between two other things, you put it between them. 夹裹;在…中夹入;把…夹入

  • Sandwich the two halves of the sponge together with cream. [V n together]

    在两半儿海绵蛋糕中间夹入奶油。

  • When you write, avoid sandwiching the bad news between an irrelevant, indirect, or overly cushioned beginning and end. [V n between pl-n]

    在写文章时，不要把坏消息夹在毫不相关、拐弯抹角或过度铺垫的开头和结尾中间。
