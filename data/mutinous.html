mutinous
mjuːtɪnəs ˈmjutənəs
ADJ-GRADED 反叛的;有反叛倾向的 If someone is mutinous, they are strongly dissatisfied with a person in authority and are likely to stop obeying them. 反叛的;有反叛倾向的

  • His own army, stung by defeats, is mutinous.

    经历失败的痛楚后，他所率军队出现反叛情绪。

SYN rebellious, revolutionary, turbulent, subversive
