underspend
ʌndəʳspend
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense underspends, present participle underspending, past tense , past participle underspent
VERB 支出不足；花费少于预算；花费低于支付能力 If an organization or country underspends, it spends less money than it plans to or less money than it can afford. 支出不足；花费少于预算；花费低于支付能力 [Also VERB n] [Also + on]

  • ...a country that underspends on health and overspends on statisticians. [VERB + on]

    一个在公共卫生方面支出不足而在统计人员方面支出过高的国家

Underspend is also a noun.

  • There has been an underspend in the department's budget.

    该部门的花费少于其预算。
