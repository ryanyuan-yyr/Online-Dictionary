leggings
legɪŋz
1 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] （女式）紧身裤 Leggings are close-fitting trousers, usually made out of a stretchy fabric, that are worn by women and girls. （女式）紧身裤

  • She is wearing tight, black leggings and a baggy green jersey.

    她身穿黑色紧身裤和一件宽松的绿色针织套衫。

2 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] 裹腿；绑腿；护腿 Leggings are an outer covering of leather or other strong material, often in the form of trousers, that you wear over your normal trousers in order to protect them. 裹腿；绑腿；护腿

  • ...a pair of leggings to slip on over your other clothes.

    套在其他衣服外面的一副裹腿
