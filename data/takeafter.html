take after
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] (在外表、举止、性格方面)像(某家庭成员) If you take after a member of your family, you resemble them in your appearance, your behaviour, or your character. (在外表、举止、性格方面)像(某家庭成员)

  • Ted's always been difficult, Mr Kemp — he takes after his dad. [VERB PREP. noun]

    肯普先生，特德一直很难相处—像他爸。

SYN resemble, be like, be similar to, look like
