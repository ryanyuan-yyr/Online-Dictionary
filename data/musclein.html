muscle in
PHRASAL VERB 强行挤入（以便分享利益）;强行插手 If someone muscles in on something, they force their way into a situation where they have no right to be and where they are not welcome, in order to gain some advantage for themselves. 强行挤入（以便分享利益）;强行插手 [disapproval]

  • Cohen complained that Kravis was muscling in on his deal. [VERB PREP. + on]

    科恩抱怨克拉维斯非要在他的买卖中插上一脚。

  • It would be surprising were the Mafia not to have muscled in. [VERB PREP.]

    如果黑手党不插上一脚，那反倒令人奇怪了。

SYN impose yourself, encroach, butt in, force your way in
