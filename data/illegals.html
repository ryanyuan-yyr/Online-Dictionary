illegal
ɪliːgəl ɪˈligəl
Word forms: plural illegals
1 ADJ 不合法的;非法的;违反规则的 If something is illegal, the law says that it is not allowed. 不合法的;非法的;违反规则的

  • It is illegal to intercept radio messages.

    拦截无线电报是非法的。

  • Birth control was illegal there until 1978.

    1978 年以前那里节育是非法的。

  • He has been charged with membership of an illegal organisation.

    他被指控参与非法组织。

  • ...illegal drugs.

    毒品

  • ...an illegal action.

    非法行动

SYN unlawful, banned, forbidden, prohibited  
illegally

  • They were yesterday convicted of illegally using a handgun.

    他们昨天被判犯有非法使用手枪罪。

  • The previous government had acted illegally.

    上届政府采取的行动不合法。

illegality

  • There is no evidence of illegality.

    没有违法的证据。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (移民)非法入境的;(劳工)非法务工的 Illegal immigrants or workers have travelled into a country or are working without official permission. (移民)非法入境的;(劳工)非法务工的
Illegal immigrants or workers are sometimes referred to as illegals .非法移民;非法务工人员

  • ...a clothing factory where many illegals worked.

    雇用很多非法移民的服装厂
