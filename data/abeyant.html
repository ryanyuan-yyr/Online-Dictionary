abeyance
əbeɪəns əˈbeɪənsɪ əˈbeɪəns
PHRASE 搁置;悬而未决;暂停使用 If something is in abeyance, it is not operating or being used at the present time. 搁置;悬而未决;暂停使用 [formal]

  • The threat is likely to remain in abeyance until next year's meeting in Reykjavik.

    这一威胁很可能会一直存在下去，直至明年的雷克雅未克会议。

  • The matter was left in abeyance until Haig saw French.

    事情被搁置了起来，直到黑格见到法国人。

SYN shelved, pending, on ice [informal] , in cold storage [informal]
