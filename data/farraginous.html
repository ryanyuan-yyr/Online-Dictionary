farrago
fərɑːgoʊ fəˈreɪgoʊ fəˈrɑgoʊ
Word forms: plural farragoes, plural farragos
N-COUNT 大杂烩;杂乱组合 If you describe something as a farrago, you are critical of it because you think it is a confused mixture of different types of things. 大杂烩;杂乱组合 [formal, disapproval]

  • His own books and memoirs are a farrago of half-truth and outright invention. [+ of]

    他自己的那些书和回忆录都是掺杂着半真半假的叙述和彻头彻尾的编造的大杂烩。

SYN hotchpotch, mixture, jumble, medley
