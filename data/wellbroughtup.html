well-brought-up ˌwɛlbrɔːtˈʌp
(also well brought up)
ADJ-GRADED (尤指儿童)有礼貌的，有涵养的，有教养的 If you say that someone, especially a child, is well-brought-up, you mean that they are very polite because they have been taught good manners. (尤指儿童)有礼貌的，有涵养的，有教养的

  • He's just a well-brought-up, middle-class boy.

    他只是个有教养的中产阶级家庭的男孩。
