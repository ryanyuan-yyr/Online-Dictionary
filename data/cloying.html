cloying
klɔɪɪŋ ˈklɔɪɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED （太甜腻或太多愁善感而）使人倒胃口的，让人腻烦的 You use cloying to describe something that you find unpleasant because it is much too sweet, or too sentimental. （太甜腻或太多愁善感而）使人倒胃口的，让人腻烦的

  • Her cheap, cloying scent enveloped him.

    她那廉价香水甜腻熏人的气味一下子包围了他。

  • Most TV kids are so cloying.

    电视上的大部分童星都太让人腻烦。

SYN sickly, nauseating, icky [informal] , treacly  
cloyingly

  • The film is too cloyingly sentimental.

    这部电影太过感伤，看着就倒胃口。
