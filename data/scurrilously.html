scurrilous
skʌrɪləs skɜːr- ˈskɜrələs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 恶语诽谤的;造谣中伤的 Scurrilous accusations or stories are untrue and unfair, and are likely to damage the reputation of the person that they relate to. 恶语诽谤的;造谣中伤的

  • Scurrilous and untrue stories were being invented.

    有人正在捏造虚假诽谤的故事。

  • ...scurrilous rumours.

    恶语诽谤的谣言

SYN slanderous, scandalous, defamatory, low
