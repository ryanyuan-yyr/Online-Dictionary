parliamentarian
pɑːʳləmenteəriən ˌpɑːləmɛnˈtɛərɪən ˌpɑrləmɛnˈtɛriən
Word forms: plural parliamentarians
1 N-COUNT 议员；（尤指）专门委员会议员 Parliamentarians are Members of a Parliament; used especially to refer to a group of Members of Parliament who are dealing with a particular task. 议员；（尤指）专门委员会议员

  • He's been meeting with British parliamentarians and government officials.

    他一直在与英国议员和政府官员会晤。

2 N-COUNT （精通议会议事规则和程序并积极参与辩论的）资深议员 A parliamentarian is a Member of Parliament who is an expert on the rules and procedures of Parliament and takes an active part in debates. （精通议会议事规则和程序并积极参与辩论的）资深议员

  • He is a veteran parliamentarian whose views enjoy widespread respect.

    他是个资深议员，其观点受到广泛尊重。
