diplomatic immunity
N-UNCOUNT 外交豁免（外交官在所驻国内无需承担法律责任和缴税义务的特权） Diplomatic immunity is the freedom from legal action and from paying taxes that a diplomat has in the country in which he or she is working. 外交豁免（外交官在所驻国内无需承担法律责任和缴税义务的特权）

  • The embassy official claimed diplomatic immunity and was later released.

    那名使馆官员申请外交豁免，随后被释放。
