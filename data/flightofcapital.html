flight of capital
N-SING 资本外逃 When people lose confidence in a particular economy or market and withdraw their investment from it, you can refer to a flight of capital from that economy or market. 资本外逃 [business]

  • TI has seen its shares suffer because of a flight of capital from telecom and Internet-related businesses.

    德州仪器的股票因为资金从电信和因特网相关企业外逃而遭受损失。
