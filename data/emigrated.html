emigrate
emɪgreɪt ˈɛmɪˌgreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense emigrates, present participle emigrating, past tense , past participle emigrated
1 VERB 移居国外;移民 If you emigrate, you leave your own country to live in another country. 移居国外;移民

  • He emigrated to Belgium. [VERB + to]

    他移民到比利时。

  • They planned to emigrate. [VERB]

    他们打算移民。

SYN move abroad, move, relocate, migrate  
emigration

  • ...the huge emigration of workers to the West.

    工人向西方国家的大规模移民
