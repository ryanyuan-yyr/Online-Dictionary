pigsty
pɪgstaɪ ˈpɪgˌstaɪ
(also pig sty)Word forms: plural pigsties
1 N-COUNT 猪圈；猪栏 A pigsty is an enclosed place where pigs are kept on a farm. 猪圈；猪栏 [mainly British]
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 pigpen
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （猪圈似的）邋遢房间，肮脏住所 If you describe a room or a house as a pigsty, you are criticizing the fact that it is very dirty and untidy. （猪圈似的）邋遢房间，肮脏住所 [mainly British, informal, disapproval]

  • The office is a pigsty.

    这间办公室像个猪圈。
