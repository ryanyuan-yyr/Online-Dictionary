pleasantry
plezəntri ˈplɛzəntri
Word forms: plural pleasantries
N-COUNT [usually plural] 轻松诙谐的话；寒暄；客套 Pleasantries are casual, friendly remarks which you make in order to be polite. 轻松诙谐的话；寒暄；客套

  • He exchanged pleasantries about his hotel and the weather. [+ about]

    他就宾馆和天气寒暄了几句。

SYN comment, remark, casual remark, polite remark
