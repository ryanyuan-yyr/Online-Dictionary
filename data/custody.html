custody
kʌstədi ˈkʌstədi
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤指父母离异后孩子的)监护权 Custody is the legal right to keep and look after a child, especially the right given to a child's mother or father when they get divorced. (尤指父母离异后孩子的)监护权

  • I'm going to go to court to get custody of the children. [+ of]

    我将起诉，要求获得孩子们的监护权。

  • Child custody is normally granted to the mother.

    孩子的监护权通常判给母亲。

  • ...a bitter custody battle.

    对监护权的激烈争夺战

SYN care, charge, protection, supervision  
2 PHRASE 被拘留;在拘禁中 Someone who is in custody or has been taken into custody has been arrested and is being kept in prison until they can be tried in a court. 被拘留;在拘禁中

  • Three people appeared in court and two of them were remanded in custody.

    有3个人出庭，其中两个被还押候审。

  • She was taken into custody later that day.

    她那天后来被拘留了。

3 N-UNCOUNT (类似监狱的)监禁，关押 If someone is being held in a particular type of custody, they are being kept in a place that is similar to a prison. (类似监狱的)监禁，关押

  • The youngster got nine months' youth custody.

    这个少年被处以9个月的管教。

  • Barrett was taken into protective custody.

    巴雷特被保护性地监禁起来。
