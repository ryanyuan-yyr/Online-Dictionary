quizzical
kwɪzɪkəl ˈkwɪzɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 诧异的；感到好笑的 If you give someone a quizzical look or smile, you look at them in a way that shows that you are surprised or amused by their behaviour. 诧异的；感到好笑的

  • He gave Robin a mildly quizzical glance.

    他有些惊讶地瞥了罗宾一眼。

SYN mocking, questioning, inquiring, curious  
quizzically

  • She looked at him quizzically.

    她狐疑地看着他。
