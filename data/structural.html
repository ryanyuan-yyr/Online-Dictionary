structural
strʌktʃərəl ˈstrʌktʃərəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 结构上的;构造上的 Structural means relating to or affecting the structure of something. 结构上的;构造上的

  • The explosion caused little structural damage to the office towers themselves.

    爆炸对办公大楼本身几乎没有造成结构上的损坏。

SYN constructional, constitutional, configurational, formational  
structurally

  • When we bought the house, it was structurally sound, but I decided to redecorate throughout.

    我们买下这幢房子时，房子的格局不错，但我还是决定全部重新装修。
