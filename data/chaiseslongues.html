chaise longue
ʃeɪz lɒŋ ˈʃeɪz ˈlɒŋ ʃɛz lɔ̃ɡ ˌʃeɪzˈ lɔŋ ˈlaʊndʒ ʃeɪz laʊndʒ
Word forms: plural chaises longues
The singular and the plural are both pronounced in the same way. 单复数发音相同。
N-COUNT (仅在一端有扶手、通常半边附有靠背的)长椅，躺椅 A chaise longue is a kind of sofa with only one arm and usually a back along half its length. (仅在一端有扶手、通常半边附有靠背的)长椅，躺椅
