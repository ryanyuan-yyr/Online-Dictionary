shut in
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…关在(房间)里面;把…关起来 If you shut someone or something in a room, you close the door so that they cannot leave it. 把…关在(房间)里面;把…关起来

  • The door enables us to shut the birds in the shelter in bad weather. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    有了这扇门，天气不好时我们就可以将鸟关进鸟屋。

SYN confine, cage, enclose, imprison  
2 PHRASAL VERB 把自己关起来 If you shut yourself in a room, you stay in there and make sure nobody else can get in. 把自己关起来

  • After one particular bad result, he shut himself in the shower room for an hour. [V pron-refl P n]

    一个特别糟的结果出来后，他将自己关在淋浴间里关了一小时。

  • He shut himself in his office, telling his secretary to hold all calls. [V pron-refl P n]

    他把自己关在办公室里，并告诉秘书所有的电话都不要转给他。
