propensity
prəpensɪti prəˈpɛnsəti
Word forms: plural propensities
N-COUNT [oft NOUN to-infinitive] （性格上的）倾向；习性 A propensity to do something or a propensity for something is a natural tendency that you have to behave in a particular way. （性格上的）倾向；习性 [formal]

  • Mr Bint has a propensity to put off decisions to the last minute. [+ for]

    宾特先生习惯拖到最后时刻才作决定。

  • She hasn't reckoned on his propensity for violence.

    她不曾料到他有暴力倾向。

SYN tendency, leaning, weakness, inclination
