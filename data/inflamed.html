inflamed
ɪnfleɪmd ɪnˈfleɪmd
ADJ-GRADED 发炎的;变红肿的 If part of your body is inflamed, it is red or swollen, usually as a result of an infection, injury, or illness. 发炎的;变红肿的 [formal]

  • Symptoms include red, itchy and inflamed skin.

    症状包括皮肤发红、发痒、发炎。

  • Her eyes were sore and inflamed.

    她的眼睛红肿疼痛。

SYN swollen, sore, red, hot
