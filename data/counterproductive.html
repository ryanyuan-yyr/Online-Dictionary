counter-productive
(also counterproductive)
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 产生相反效果的;事与愿违的;适得其反的 Something that is counter-productive achieves the opposite result from the one that you want to achieve. 产生相反效果的;事与愿违的;适得其反的

  • In practice, however, such an attitude is counter-productive.

    然而，实际上这种态度只会适得其反。

  • It is counter-productive to address an interviewee in patronizing tones.

    以盛气凌人的语气同面试者讲话将会适得其反。
