Word forms: plural chocoholics
N-COUNT 吃巧克力有瘾的人;嗜食巧克力的人 A chocoholic is someone who eats a great deal of chocolate and finds it hard to stop themselves eating it. 吃巧克力有瘾的人;嗜食巧克力的人 [informal]
