perfection
pəʳfekʃən pərˈfɛkʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 最佳；完美；十全十美 Perfection is the quality of being as good as it is possible for something of a particular kind to be. 最佳；完美；十全十美

  • His quest for perfection is relentless.

    他不断追求完美。

  • Physical perfection in a human being is exceedingly rare.

    体貌完美的人极为罕见。

2 N-UNCOUNT 完美；极好 If you say that something is perfection, you mean that you think it is as good as it could possibly be. 完美；极好

  • The house and garden were perfection.

    房屋和花园都非常漂亮。

3 N-UNCOUNT 完善；改善 The perfection of something such as a skill, system, or product involves making it as good as it could possibly be. 完善；改善

  • Madame Clicquot is credited with the perfection of this technique. [+ of]

    这项技术的完善要归功于凯歌夫人。

SYN accomplishment, achieving, achievement, polishing  
4 PHRASE 达到尽善尽美 If something is done to perfection, it is done so well that it could not be done any better. 达到尽善尽美

  • Like the old trouper he is, he timed his entry to perfection.

    作为一名老戏骨，他上场的时间把握得恰到好处。

  • ...fresh fish, cooked to perfection.

    烹饪得无比美味的鲜鱼
