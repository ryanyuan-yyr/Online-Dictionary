philosopher
fɪlɒsəfəʳ fɪˈlɑsəfər
Word forms: plural philosophers
1 N-COUNT 哲学家 A philosopher is a person who studies or writes about philosophy. 哲学家

  • ...the Greek philosopher Plato.

    希腊哲学家柏拉图

SYN thinker, theorist, sage, wise man  
2 N-COUNT 思想家；哲人 If you refer to someone as a philosopher, you mean that they think deeply and seriously about life and other basic matters. 思想家；哲人
