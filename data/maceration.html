macerate
mæsəreɪt ˈmæsərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense macerates, present participle macerating, past tense , past participle macerated
V-ERG 浸泡;浸渍;(使)浸透;(使)浸软 If you macerate food, or if it macerates, you soak it in a liquid for a period of time so that it absorbs the liquid. 浸泡;浸渍;(使)浸透;(使)浸软

  • I like to macerate the food in liqueur for a few minutes before serving. [VERB noun + in]

    我喜欢先把这种食物在利口酒里浸泡几分钟再端上桌。

  • Cognac is also used to macerate and flavour ingredients and casseroles. [VERB noun]

    干邑白兰地也用于浸渍食材，给砂锅炖菜提味。

  • Seal tightly then leave for four to five days to macerate. [VERB]

    密封之后再浸泡4至5天。

SYN soften, soak, steep, squash
