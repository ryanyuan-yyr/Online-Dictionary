repository
rɪpɒzɪtri -tɔːri rɪˈpɑzəˌtɔri
Word forms: plural repositories
1 N-COUNT 贮存处;存放处;仓库 A repository is a place where something is kept safely. 贮存处;存放处;仓库 [formal]

  • A church in Moscow became a repository for police files. [+ for]

    莫斯科的一座教堂成为警方存放档案的地点。

SYN store, archive, storehouse, depository  
2 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] 博学者;无所不知的人 A repository of information is a person or group of people who know a lot of information about a particular place or subject. 博学者;无所不知的人

  • The repository of all important knowledge in a small town was the chief barman of the local pub.

    小镇上最无所不知的人就是当地酒吧里的服务员领班。

SYN storehouse, fund, mine, oracle
