bottleneck
bɒtəlnek ˈbɑtəlˌnɛk
Word forms: plural bottlenecks
1 N-COUNT 瓶颈路段；狭窄路段；交通拥堵地段 A bottleneck is a place where a road becomes narrow or where it meets another road so that the traffic slows down or stops, often causing traffic jams. 瓶颈路段；狭窄路段；交通拥堵地段
2 N-COUNT （阻碍进展的）瓶颈，障碍 A bottleneck is a situation that stops a process or activity from progressing. （阻碍进展的）瓶颈，障碍

  • He pushed everyone full speed ahead until production hit a bottleneck.

    他催促所有人拼命干活，直到生产遭遇瓶颈。
