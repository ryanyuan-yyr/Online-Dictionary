jaundiced
dʒɔːndɪst
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有偏见的；狭隘的；敌视的 If someone has a jaundiced view of something, they can see only the bad aspects of it. 有偏见的；狭隘的；敌视的

  • The financial markets are taking a jaundiced view of the Government's motives.

    金融市场对政府的动机存有偏见。

  • The general watched the proceedings with a jaundiced eye.

    这位将军以带有偏见的眼光关注事态发展。

SYN cynical, bitter, hostile, prejudiced
