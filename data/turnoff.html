turn off
1 PHRASAL VERB 驶离，离开（…路);转弯;拐入旁路 If you turn off the road or path you are going along, you start going along a different road or path which leads away from it. 驶离，离开（…路);转弯;拐入旁路

  • The truck turned off the main road along the gravelly track which led to the farm. [VERB PREP. noun]

    卡车驶离大路，沿着通向农场的碎石小道行进。

  • He turned off only to find he was trapped in a town square with no easy exit. [VERB PREP.]

    他转入岔路，却发现自己困在了一个难觅出口的市镇广场。

SYN branch off, leave, quit, depart from  
2 PHRASAL VERB 关上;关掉；截断，切断（…的供应） When you turn off a piece of equipment or a supply of something, you stop heat, sound, or water being produced by adjusting the controls. 关上;关掉；截断，切断（…的供应）

  • The light's a bit too harsh. You can turn it off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    灯光有点太刺眼了。你可以把灯关掉。

  • I have to get up and turn off the radio. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我必须起床关上收音机。

  • Their water was turned off weeks ago without explanation. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们的水在几周以前就毫无理由地被切断了。

3 PHRASAL VERB 使丧失兴趣 If something turns you off a particular subject or activity, it makes you have no interest in it. 使丧失兴趣 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • What turns teenagers off science and technology?. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    是什么使青少年对科学技术丧失了兴趣？

  • Teaching off a blackboard is boring, and undoubtedly turns people off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    照本宣科枯燥乏味，无疑会使人们丧失学习兴趣。

5 PHRASAL VERB 使没有性欲；使失去性欲 If something or someone turns you off, you do not find them sexually attractive or they stop you feeling sexually excited. 使没有性欲；使失去性欲 [informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Aggressive men turn me off completely. [VERB noun PREP.]

    咄咄逼人的男人让我完全没有欲望。

SYN repel, bore, put someone off, disgust

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 使人扫兴(或倒胃口)的事物;让人丧失性欲的事物 Something that is a turn-off causes you to lose interest or sexual excitement. 使人扫兴(或倒胃口)的事物;让人丧失性欲的事物 [informal]
