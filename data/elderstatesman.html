elder statesman
Word forms: plural elder statesmen
1 N-COUNT 政界元老 An elder statesman is an old and respected politician or former politician who still has influence because of his or her experience. 政界元老
2 N-COUNT 前辈;权威人士;元老 An experienced and respected member of an organization or profession is sometimes referred to as an elder statesman . 前辈;权威人士;元老
