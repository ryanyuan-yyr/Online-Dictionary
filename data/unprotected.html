unprotected
ʌnprətektɪd ˌʌnprəˈtɛktɪd ˌʌnprəˈtɛktɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] （人或地方）未受保护的，未设防的 An unprotected person or place is not looked after or defended, and so they may be harmed or attacked. （人或地方）未受保护的，未设防的

  • The landing beaches would be unprotected.

    登陆的海滩可能没有设防。

SYN vulnerable, helpless, defenceless, unarmed  
2 ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 暴露在外的；易受损的 If something is unprotected, it is not covered or treated with anything, and so it may easily be damaged. 暴露在外的；易受损的

  • Exposure of unprotected skin to the sun carries the risk of developing skin cancer.

    皮肤没有采取任何防晒措施就在太阳下曝晒的话有得皮肤癌的危险。

  • This leaves fertile soil unprotected and prone to erosion.

    这会让肥沃的土壤暴露在外，使其容易受到侵蚀。

SYN exposed, naked, unshielded, open  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 未用避孕套的；无防护的 If two people have unprotected sex, they do not use a condom when they have sex. 未用避孕套的；无防护的
