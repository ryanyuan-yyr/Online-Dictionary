freewheeling
friːhwiːlɪŋ ˌfriːˈwiːlɪŋ ˈfriˈhwilɪŋ friˈwilɪŋ
(also free-wheeling)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 随心所欲的;自由放纵的 If you refer to someone's freewheeling lifestyle or attitudes, you mean that they behave in a casual, relaxed way without feeling restricted by rules or accepted ways of doing things. 随心所欲的;自由放纵的

  • He has given up his freewheeling lifestyle to settle down with his baby daughter.

    他放弃了自由放纵的生活方式，和幼小的女儿安顿下来。

  • ...an update on corporate affairs delivered in Johnson's unique, freewheeling style.

    约翰逊以他独特的、随意漫谈的方式对公司事务所作的最新情况介绍
