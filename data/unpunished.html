unpunished
ʌnpʌnɪʃt ʌnˈpʌnɪʃt
ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ n, ADJ after verb] 未受惩罚的 If a criminal or crime goes unpunished, the criminal is not punished. 未受惩罚的

  • Persistent criminals who have gone unpunished by the courts have been dealt with by local people. [+ by]

    没有受到法庭制裁的惯犯被当地人处治了。

  • I have been amazed at times that cruelty can go unpunished.

    我有时深感震惊，暴行竟可以不受惩罚。
