telepathy
tɪlepəθi təˈlɛpəθi
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft by NOUN] 传心术;读心术;心灵感应 If you refer to telepathy, you mean the direct communication of thoughts and feelings between people's minds, without the need to use speech, writing, or any other normal signals. 传心术;读心术;心灵感应

  • Many of us find it very difficult to state our needs. We expect people to know by telepathy what we are feeling.

    我们许多人感觉很难用语言表达自己的需求，希望别人能通过心灵感应了解我们的感受。
