adorn
ədɔːʳn əˈdɔrn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adorns, present participle adorning, past tense , past participle adorned
VERB 装饰；装扮 If something adorns a place or an object, it makes it look more beautiful. 装饰；装扮

  • His watercolour designs adorn a wide range of books. [VERB noun]

    他的水彩设计使许多图书大为生色。

  • Several magnificent oil paintings adorn the walls. [VERB noun]

    几幅恢宏的油画装点着墙面。

SYN decorate, enhance, deck, trim
