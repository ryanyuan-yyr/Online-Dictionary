feat
fiːt fiːt fit
Word forms: plural feats
N-COUNT 功绩;伟业;壮举 If you refer to an action, or the result of an action, as a feat, you admire it because it is an impressive and difficult achievement. 功绩;伟业;壮举 [approval]

  • A racing car is an extraordinary feat of engineering.

    赛车是工程学上一项相当了不起的成就。

SYN accomplishment, act, performance, achievement
