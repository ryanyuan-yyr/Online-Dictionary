personality disorder
Word forms: personality disorders
N-VAR 人格障碍A personality disorder is a mental disorder in which a person habitually behaves in ways considered likely to cause suffering to himself or others. 人格障碍 [medicine]

  • One forensic psychiatrist described him as having an incurable antisocial personality disorder.

    一位法庭精神病医生将他描述为患有不可治愈反社会人格障碍。
