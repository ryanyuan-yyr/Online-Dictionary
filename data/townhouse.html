town house
Word forms: plural town houses
1 N-COUNT 排房;连栋房屋 A town house is a tall narrow house in a town, usually in a row of similar houses. 排房;连栋房屋
2 N-COUNT [with poss] (富人除乡村住宅外的)城市住宅，城镇住房 The town house of a wealthy person is the house that they own in a town or city, rather than another house that they own in the country. (富人除乡村住宅外的)城市住宅，城镇住房
