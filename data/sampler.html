sampler
sɑːmpləʳ sæm- ˈsæmplər ˈsɑmplər
Word forms: plural samplers
1 N-COUNT 刺绣样本 A sampler is a piece of cloth with words and patterns sewn on it, which is intended to show the skill of the person who made it. 刺绣样本
2 N-COUNT 音乐采样器(用于节录一段音乐以合成新曲的装置) A sampler is a piece of equipment that is used for copying a piece of music and using it to make a new piece of music. 音乐采样器(用于节录一段音乐以合成新曲的装置)
