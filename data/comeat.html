come at
PHRASAL VERB 扑向;向…逼近 If a person or animal comes at you, they move towards you in a threatening way and try to attack you. 扑向;向…逼近 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • He maintained that he was protecting himself from Mr Cox, who came at him with an axe. [V P n + with]

    他坚持说当时考克斯先生拿着斧头向他冲过来，他是在自卫。

SYN attack, charge, rush, go for
