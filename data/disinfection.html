disinfect
dɪsɪnfekt ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disinfects, present participle disinfecting, past tense , past participle disinfected
VERB 为…消毒（或杀菌） If you disinfect something, you clean it using a substance that kills germs. 为…消毒（或杀菌）

  • Chlorine is used to disinfect water. [VERB noun]

    氯用来给水消毒。

  • Make sure the draining board, sink and plug hole are regularly disinfected. [VERB noun]

    滴水板、水槽和塞孔一定要定期消毒。

SYN sterilize, purify, decontaminate, clean
