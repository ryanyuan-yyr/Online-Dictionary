home plate
N-UNCOUNT (棒球)本垒板In baseball, home plate is the piece of rubber or other material that the batter stands beside. It is the last of the four bases that a runner must touch in order to score a run. (棒球)本垒板

  • He severely injured his ankle in a collision at home plate.

    他在一次本垒板碰撞中裸关节严重受伤。
