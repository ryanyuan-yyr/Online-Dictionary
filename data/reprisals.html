reprisal
rɪpraɪzəl rɪˈpraɪzəl
Word forms: plural reprisals
N-VAR 报复（行为） If you do something to a person in reprisal, you hurt or punish them because they have done something violent or unpleasant to you. 报复（行为）

  • There were fears that some of the Western hostages might be killed in reprisal.

    有人担心一些西方人质可能会受到报复性杀害。

  • Witnesses are unwilling to testify through fear of reprisals.

    由于害怕报复，目击者不愿作证。

SYN retaliation, revenge, vengeance, retribution
