disengage
dɪsɪngeɪdʒ ˌdɪsɪnˈgeɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disengages, present participle disengaging, past tense , past participle disengaged
1 V-ERG （使）脱离;（使）松开;解开;拆开 If you disengage something, or if it disengages, it becomes separate from something which it has been attached to. （使）脱离;（使）松开;解开;拆开 [Also VERB n from n]

  • She disengaged the film advance mechanism on the camera. [VERB noun]

    她松开了照相机上的送片装置。

  • John gently disengaged himself from his sister's tearful embrace. [V pron-refl + from]

    约翰轻轻推开抱着他哭泣的妹妹。

  • His front brake cable disengaged. [VERB]

    他的前轮刹车索断了。

SYN release, free, separate, ease  
2 VERB (军队)撤军 If an army disengages from an area, it withdraws from that area. (军队)撤军

  • ...the evident desire of both superpowers to disengage from Afghanistan. [VERB from noun]

    两个超级大国都想从阿富汗地区撤军的明显意愿

  • More vigorous action is needed to force the federal army to disengage. [VERB]

    需要更强有力的行动来迫使联邦军队撤军。

SYN detach, withdraw
