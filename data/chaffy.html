chaff
tʃɑːf tʃæf tʃæf tʃɑf
1 N-UNCOUNT 谷糠;麸皮 Chaff is the outer part of grain such as wheat. It is removed before the grain is used as food. 谷糠;麸皮
2 PHRASE 分清良莠;去芜存菁 If you separate the wheat from the chaff or sort the wheat from the chaff, you decide which people or things in a group are good or important and which are not. 分清良莠;去芜存菁

  • It isn't always easy to separate the wheat from the chaff.

    分清良莠并不总是很容易。
