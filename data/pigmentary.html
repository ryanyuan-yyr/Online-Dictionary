pigment
pɪgmənt ˈpɪgmənt
Word forms: plural pigments
N-MASS 颜料；色料；色素 A pigment is a substance that gives something a particular colour. 颜料；色料；色素 [formal]

  • The Romans used natural pigments on their fabrics and walls.

    古罗马人在织物和墙壁上使用天然颜料。

  • ...the brown pigment in the skin.

    皮肤内的褐色素

SYN colour, colouring, paint, stain
