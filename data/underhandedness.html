underhand
ʌndəʳhænd ˈʌndərˌhænd
(also underhanded)
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 秘密的；狡诈的；欺诈的 If an action is underhand or if it is done in an underhand way, it is done secretly and dishonestly. 秘密的；狡诈的；欺诈的 [disapproval]

  • ...underhand financial deals.

    秘密的金融交易

  • ...a list of the underhanded ways in which their influence operates in the United States.

    他们在美国施展影响的一系列阴险手段

  • Mr Livingstone accused the government of being underhand.

    利文斯通先生指责政府搞欺诈。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （投球等）低手的，手不过肩的 You use underhand or underhanded to describe actions, such as throwing a ball, in which you do not raise your arm above your shoulder. （投球等）低手的，手不过肩的 [US]

  • ...an underhanded pitch.

    低手投球

Underhand is also an adverb.

  • In softball, pitches are tossed underhand.

    垒球运动中采用低手投球。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 underarm
underarm
ʌndərɑːʳm ˈʌndərˌɑrm
Word forms: plural underarms
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 腋下的；腋窝的 Underarm means in or for the areas under your arms, where they are joined to your body. 腋下的；腋窝的

  • ...underarm deodorants.

    腋下除臭剂

Underarm is also a noun.

  • Wash the feet, underarms and body surface using a soap.

    用肥皂洗洗脚、腋下及全身。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （投球等）低手的，手不过肩的 You use underarm to describe actions, such as throwing a ball, in which you do not raise your arm above your shoulder. （投球等）低手的，手不过肩的 [British]

  • ...an underarm throw.

    低手投球

Underarm is also an adverb.

  • Practise throwing a ball underarm.

    练习低手投球。

in AM, use 美国英语用 underhand, underhanded
