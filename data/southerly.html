southerly
sʌðəʳli ˈsʌðərli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 朝南的;向南的 A southerly point, area, or direction is to the south or towards the south. 朝南的;向南的

  • We set off in a southerly direction.

    我们向南出发了。

  • ...the most southerly areas of Zimbabwe and Mozambique.

    津巴布韦和莫桑比克最南边的地区

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (风)从南方吹来的 A southerly wind is a wind that blows from the south. (风)从南方吹来的
