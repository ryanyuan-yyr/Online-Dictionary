supplier
səplaɪəʳ
Word forms: plural suppliers
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 供应者;供应商;供货方 A supplier is a person, company, or organization that sells or supplies something such as goods or equipment to customers. 供应者;供应商;供货方 [business] [Also + of]

  • ...Hillsdown Holdings, one of the UK's biggest food suppliers.

    希尔斯顿控股公司，英国最大的食品供应商之一 

  • Japan is Asia's dominant supplier of imports and technology.

    日本是亚洲进口货物和技术的最大供应国。
