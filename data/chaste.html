chaste
tʃeɪst tʃeɪst
1 ADJ-GRADED 童贞的；贞洁的;禁欲的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as chaste, you mean that they do not have sex with anyone, or they only have sex with their husband or wife. 童贞的；贞洁的;禁欲的 [old-fashioned]

  • He remained chaste.

    他一直保持童贞之身。

  • Abramov did not live a chaste life.

    阿布拉莫夫生活不检点。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 简洁的;朴实的;不重装饰的 Something that is chaste is very simple in style, without very much decoration. 简洁的;朴实的;不重装饰的

  • ...chaste clothes.

    朴素的衣服
