repossess
riːpəzes ˌriːpəˈzɛs ˌripəˈzɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense repossesses, present participle repossessing, past tense , past participle repossessed
VERB [usually passive] (因未付清全款而)收回，重新占有 If your car or house is repossessed, the people who supplied it take it back because they are still owed money for it. (因未付清全款而)收回，重新占有

  • His car was repossessed by the company. [be VERB-ed]

    他的汽车因为欠款被公司收回。

  • The firm's sales of repossessed properties is up by 40 per cent. [VERB-ed]

    这家公司收回房产的销售额上升了40%。

SYN take back
