scribble down
PHRASAL VERB 草草写下;匆匆记下 If you scribble down something, you write it quickly or roughly. 草草写下;匆匆记下

  • I attempted to scribble down the names. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我努力匆匆地写下那些名字。

  • He took my name and address, scribbling it down in his notebook. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他要了我的姓名和地址，匆匆写在他的笔记本上。
