beneficent
bɪnefɪsənt bəˈnɛfəsənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 行善的；慈善的；有益的 A beneficent person or thing helps people or results in something good. 行善的；慈善的；有益的 [formal]

  • ...optimism about the beneficent effects of new technology.

    对新技术会带来有益成效的乐观态度

SYN charitable, benign, benevolent, philanthropic
