right-thinking
ˈraɪtˌθɪŋkɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 思想正直的;有正义感的;明智的 If you think that someone's opinions or beliefs are sensible and you agree with them, you can describe them as a right-thinking person. 思想正直的;有正义感的;明智的 [approval]

  • Every right-thinking American would be proud of them.

    每一个有正义感的美国人都会为他们感到自豪。
