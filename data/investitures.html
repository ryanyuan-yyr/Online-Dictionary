investiture
ɪnvestɪtʃəʳ ɪnˈvɛstətʃər
Word forms: plural investitures
N-COUNT 授职仪式;授衔仪式 An investiture is a ceremony in which someone is given an official title. 授职仪式;授衔仪式

  • ...Edward VIII's investiture as Prince of Wales in 1911.

    爱德华八世 1911 年被封为威尔士亲王的仪式

SYN installation, ordination, inauguration, investment
