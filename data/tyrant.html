tyrant
taɪərənt ˈtaɪrənt
Word forms: plural tyrants
N-COUNT 暴君;专制君主;暴君似的人 You can use tyrant to refer to someone who treats the people they have authority over in a cruel and unfair way. 暴君;专制君主;暴君似的人

  • Since 1804 the country has mostly been ruled by tyrants.

    自 1804 年以来，该国大部分时间都处于暴君的统治之下。

  • ...households where the father was a tyrant.

    父亲残暴专横的家庭

SYN dictator, bully, authoritarian, Big Brother
