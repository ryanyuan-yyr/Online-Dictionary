flavoured

ADJ-GRADED 添加了调料的 If a food is flavoured, various ingredients have been added to it so that it has a distinctive flavour. 添加了调料的

    添加了香草的肉

  • Many of these recipes are highly flavoured.

    这些菜谱中很多都添加了极浓的调味料。
