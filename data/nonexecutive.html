non-executive
Word forms: plural non-executives
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (董事等)非执行的，无决策权的 Someone who has a non-executive position in a company or organization gives advice but is not responsible for making decisions or ensuring that decisions are carried out. (董事等)非执行的，无决策权的 [business]

  • ...non-executive directors.

    非执行董事

  • The issue became whether he should leave altogether or remain as chairman in a non-executive role.

    问题成了他是应该彻底离开，还是继续留下做一个无决策权的主席。

2 N-COUNT 非执行董事;无行政职权者 A non-executive is someone who has a non-executive position in a company or organization. 非执行董事;无行政职权者 [business]
