demographic
deməgræfɪk
Word forms: plural demographics
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 人口（学）的;人口统计（学）的 Demographic means relating to or concerning demography. 人口（学）的;人口统计（学）的
2 N-PLURAL (某个地方或社会的)人口特征，人口统计数据 The demographics of a place or society are the statistics relating to the people who live there. (某个地方或社会的)人口特征，人口统计数据

  • ...the changing demographics of the United States. [+ of]

    美国不断变化的人口特征

3 N-SING (尤指特定年龄段的)人群 In business, a demographic is a group of people in a society, especially people in a particular age group. (尤指特定年龄段的)人群 [business]

  • Most of our listeners are in the 25-39 demographic.

    我们的大多数听众都属于 25至39 岁这个年龄段。
