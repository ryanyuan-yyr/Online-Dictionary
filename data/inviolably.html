inviolable
ɪnvaɪələbəl ɪnˈvaɪələbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 不可触犯的;不可违背的 If a law or principle is inviolable, you must not break it. 不可触犯的;不可违背的 [formal]

  • The game had a single inviolable rule: obstacles were to be overcome, not circumvented.

    该比赛有一个规则不可违背：必须跳过而不能绕过障碍物。

SYN sacrosanct, sacred, hallowed, holy  
2 ADJ 不可更改的;不可侵犯的 If a country says its borders are inviolable, it means they must not be changed or crossed without permission. 不可更改的;不可侵犯的 [formal]

  • Yesterday's resolution says the present Polish border is 'inviolable'.

    昨天的决议称现在的波兰边界是“不可侵犯的”。
