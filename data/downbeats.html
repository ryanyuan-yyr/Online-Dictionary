downbeat
daʊnbiːt ˈdaʊnˌbit
Word forms: plural downbeats
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 冷淡的;无热情的;故作随意的 If people or their opinions are downbeat, they are deliberately casual and not enthusiastic about a situation. 冷淡的;无热情的;故作随意的

  • Its headlines were suitably downbeat and don't scream out.

    其标题平淡无奇而非触目惊心，非常得体。

  • ...a downbeat assessment of 1992's economic prospects.

    对 1992 年经济前景的低调判断

2 ADJ-GRADED 沮丧的;悲观的;绝望的 If you are feeling downbeat, you are feeling depressed and without hope. 沮丧的;悲观的;绝望的

  • They found him in gloomy, downbeat mood.

    他们发现他情绪低落，无精打采。

SYN gloomy, negative, depressed, pessimistic  
3 N-COUNT 下拍(乐队指挥示意强拍的向下手势) In music, the downbeat is the downward movement of the conductor's hand, indicating the first beat of the bar. 下拍(乐队指挥示意强拍的向下手势)
