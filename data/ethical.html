ethical
eθɪkəl ˈɛθɪkəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 道德的;伦理的 Ethical means relating to beliefs about right and wrong. 道德的;伦理的

  • ...the moral and ethical standards in the school.

    学校的道德和伦理标准

  • ...the medical, nursing and ethical issues surrounding terminally-ill people.

    围绕患不治之症的人们的医疗、护理和伦理方面的问题

ethically

  • Attorneys are ethically and legally bound to absolute confidentiality.

    道德和法律都要求律师们绝对保密。

2 ADJ-GRADED 合乎道德的;合乎伦理规范的 If you describe something as ethical, you mean that it is morally right or morally acceptable. 合乎道德的;合乎伦理规范的

  • ...ethical investment schemes.

    合乎道德规范的投资计划

  • Does the party think it is ethical to link tax policy with party fund-raising?

    该政党认为将税收政策和党内筹资挂钩是合乎道德的做法吗？

ethically

  • Mayors want local companies to behave ethically.

    市长们希望地方公司在经营方面要遵守职业道德。
