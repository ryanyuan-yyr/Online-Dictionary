bug
bʌg bʌg
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bugs, present participle bugging, past tense , past participle bugged
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 小虫；臭虫 A bug is an insect or similar small creature. 小虫；臭虫 [informal]

  • We noticed tiny bugs that were all over the walls.

    我们注意到墙上爬满了小虫子。

  • ...a bloodsucking bug which infests poor housing.

    贫民区滋生的一种吸血的虫子

SYN insect, beastie [informal] , creepy-crawly [informal] , gogga [South Africa , informal]  
2 N-COUNT 小病；轻微的传染病 A bug is an illness which is caused by small organisms such as bacteria. 小病；轻微的传染病 [informal]

  • I think I've got a bit of a stomach bug.

    我想我的胃可能出了点儿毛病。

  • There was a bug going around at the club.

    这个俱乐部流行着一种轻微的传染病。

  • ...the killer brain bug meningitis.

    致命的脑部疾病脑膜炎

SYN illness, disease, complaint, virus  
3 N-COUNT （计算机程序中的）漏洞，错误，故障 If there is a bug in a computer program, there is a mistake in it. （计算机程序中的）漏洞，错误，故障 [computing]

  • There is a bug in the software.

    软件有漏洞。

SYN fault, failing, virus, error  
4 N-COUNT （微型）窃听器 A bug is a tiny hidden microphone which transmits what people are saying. （微型）窃听器

  • There was a bug on the phone.

    电话装有窃听器。

SYN bugging device, wire, listening device, phone tap  
5 VERB 在…装窃听器 If someone bugs a place, they hide tiny microphones in it which transmit what people are saying. 在…装窃听器

  • He heard that they were planning to bug his office. [VERB noun]

    他得知他们打算在他办公室安装窃听器。

  • I found out my phone was bugged. [VERB noun]

    我发现我的电话被窃听了。

SYN tap, eavesdrop, listen in on, wiretap  
bugging

  • ...an electronic bugging device.

    电子窃听装置

6 N-SING [oft n NOUN] （对…的）热衷，迷恋 You can say that someone has been bitten by a particular bug when they suddenly become very enthusiastic about something. （对…的）热衷，迷恋 [informal]

  • I've definitely been bitten by the gardening bug.

    我被园艺彻底迷住了。

  • Roundhay Park in Leeds was the place I first got the fishing bug.

    利兹市的朗德海公园是我首次对钓鱼着迷的地方。

SYN mania, passion, rage, obsession  
7 VERB 使烦恼；使厌烦 If someone or something bugs you, they worry or annoy you. 使烦恼；使厌烦 [informal]

  • I only did it to bug my parents. [VERB noun]

    我这么做只是为了让我父母心烦。

SYN annoy, bother, disturb, needle [informal]
