courtship
kɔːʳtʃɪp ˈkɔrtˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural courtships
1 N-VAR 求爱;恋爱;求爱(或恋爱)期 Courtship is the activity of courting or the time during which a man and a woman are courting. 求爱;恋爱;求爱(或恋爱)期 [old-fashioned]

  • They were more interested in courtship and cars than in school.

    比起上学，他们对谈恋爱和汽车更感兴趣。

  • After a short courtship, she accepted his marriage proposal.

    恋爱没多长时间，她就接受了他的求婚。

SYN wooing, courting, suit, romance  
2 N-UNCOUNT (动物的)求偶 The courtship of male and female animals is their behaviour before they have sex. (动物的)求偶

  • Courtship is somewhat vocal with a lot of displaying by the male.

    雄性动物在求偶时会尽量展示自己，并发出一些声响。

  • ...elaborate courtship dances.

    复杂的求偶舞
