subtitled
sʌbtaɪtəld ˈsʌbtaɪtəld
1 V-PASSIVE 给…加副标题 If you say how a book or play is subtitled, you say what its subtitle is. 给…加副标题

  • 'Lorna Doone' is subtitled 'a Romance of Exmoor'. [be V-ed with quote]

    小说《罗娜·杜恩》的副标题是“埃克斯穆尔绝恋”。

2 ADJ (外国电影)配字幕的 If a foreign film is subtitled, a printed translation of the words is shown at the bottom of the picture. (外国电影)配字幕的

  • Much of the film is subtitled.

    电影的大部分都有字幕。

  • ...subtitled films.

    配字幕的电影
