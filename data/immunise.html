immunize
ɪmjʊnaɪz ˈɪmjuˌnaɪz ˈɪmjəˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense immunizes, present participle immunizing, past tense , past participle immunized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 immunise
VERB [usually passive] (常指通过注射疫苗)使免疫 If people or animals are immunized, they are made immune to a particular disease, often by being given an injection. (常指通过注射疫苗)使免疫

  • We should require that every student is immunized against hepatitis B. [be V-ed + against]

    我们应要求每个学生都注射乙肝疫苗。

  • The monkeys had been immunized with a vaccine made from infected cells. [be VERB-ed]

    这些猴子已经注射了由受感染的细胞培养而成的疫苗。

  • All parents should have their children immunized. [have noun VERB-ed]

    所有父母都应给孩子打疫苗。

SYN vaccinate, inoculate, protect, safeguard  
immunization
