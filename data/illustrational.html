illustration
ɪləstreɪʃən ˌɪləˈstreɪʃən ˌɪləˈstreɪʃən
Word forms: plural illustrations
1 N-COUNT 例证;实例 An illustration is an example or a story which is used to make a point clear. 例证;实例

  • ...a perfect illustration of the way Britain absorbs and adapts external influences. [+ of]

    英国吸收借鉴外来影响的绝好例证

2 N-COUNT 插图;图案;图表 An illustration in a book is a picture, design, or diagram. 插图;图案;图表

  • She looked like a princess in a nineteenth-century illustration.

    她看起来像19世纪插图读物中的公主。

SYN picture, drawing, painting, image  
3 See also: illustrate
