silky
sɪlki ˈsɪlki
Word forms: comparative silkier, superlative silkiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 丝绸一样的;柔软光洁的 If something has a silky texture, it is smooth, soft, and shiny, like silk. 丝绸一样的;柔软光洁的

  • ...dresses in seductively silky fabrics.

    迷人的丝绸长裙

  • Claire smoothed Katy's silky hair.

    克莱尔捋了捋凯蒂如丝般的秀发。

  • ...skin that's silky and unblemished.

    丝滑光洁的皮肤

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (声音)轻柔的，柔和的，温和的 If you describe someone's voice as silky, you mean that it sounds confident but gentle and you find it attractive. (声音)轻柔的，柔和的，温和的

  • ...a well-spoken man with a silky voice.

    说话温和而且彬彬有礼的男子

3 ADJ-GRADED (行动)平稳的，流畅的 If you describe the way someone or something moves as silky, you mean that they move very smoothly. (行动)平稳的，流畅的

  • Some people moved in silky, liquid movements, others were jerky, probably drunk.

    一些人动作平稳流畅，其他人则跌跌撞撞，大概是喝醉了。

  • I had the car for two years and much enjoyed its excellent handling, quietness and silky gearchange.

    我的车买了两年了，我非常喜欢它卓越的操控性，安静舒适的驾车环境和平稳流畅的换挡操作。
