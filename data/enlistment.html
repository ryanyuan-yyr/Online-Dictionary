enlistment
ɪnlɪstmənt ɛnˈlɪstmənt ɪnˈlɪstmənt
Word forms: plural enlistments
1 N-UNCOUNT 参军;应征入伍 Enlistment is the act of joining the army, navy, marines, or air force. 参军;应征入伍

  • Canadians seek enlistment in the US Marines because they don't see as much opportunity in the Canadian armed forces.

    加拿大人寻求加入美国海军陆战队，因为他们认为在加拿大军队里机会没那么多。

2 N-VAR 服兵役年限;服役期 Enlistment is the period of time for which someone is a member of one of the armed forces. 服兵役年限;服役期

  • At the end of my term of enlistment I decided to return to civilian life.

    在我退役的时候，我决定复员过平民生活。
