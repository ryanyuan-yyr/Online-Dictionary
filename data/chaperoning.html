chaperone
ʃæpəroʊn
(also chaperon)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense chaperones, present participle chaperoning, past tense , past participle chaperoned
1 N-COUNT 陪护;监护人 A chaperone is someone who accompanies another person somewhere in order to make sure that they do not come to any harm. 陪护;监护人
SYN escort, companion, governess, duenna  
2 VERB [usually passive] 给…做监护人;陪伴 If you are chaperoned by someone, they act as your chaperone. 给…做监护人;陪伴

  • We were chaperoned by our aunt. [be VERB-ed]

    我们由婶子陪着。

SYN escort, protect, attend, accompany
