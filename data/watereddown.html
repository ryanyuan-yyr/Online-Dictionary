watered-down
(also watered down)
1 ADJ-GRADED (提议、言论、声明等）减弱了的，淡化了的，简化了的 If you describe something such as a proposal, speech, or statement as watered-down, you mean that it is weaker or less forceful than its original form. (提议、言论、声明等）减弱了的，淡化了的，简化了的

  • The British government introduced a watered-down version of the proposals.

    英国政府针对这些提议提出了另一较缓和的版本。

2See also:water down
