put up to
PHRASAL VERB 怂恿；鼓动 If you put someone up to something wrong or foolish or something which they would not normally do, you suggest that they do it and you encourage them to do it. 怂恿；鼓动

  • How do you know he asked me out? You put him up to it. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    你怎么知道他约我出去了？是你鼓动他那样做的。

SYN encourage, urge, persuade, prompt
