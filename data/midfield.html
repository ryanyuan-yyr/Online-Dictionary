midfield
mɪdfild ˌmɪdˈfiːld ˈmɪdˌfild
N-UNCOUNT [oft N n] (美国足球)中场位置In football and American football, the midfield is the central area of the playing field between the two goals, or the players whose usual position is in this area. (美国足球)中场位置

  • In the last two years, I played a lot of games in midfield.

    在过去两年，我作为中场队员参加了多次足球比赛。

  • We need to try and get the midfield scoring more goals.

    我们需要努力在中场进更多的球。

  • ...the best midfield player in the world.

    ...世界上最优秀的中场球员。
