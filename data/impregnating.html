impregnate
ɪmpregneɪt ɪmpreg- ɪmˈprɛgˌneɪt ɪmˈprɛgnɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense impregnates, present participle impregnating, past tense , past participle impregnated
1 VERB 使浸透;浸渍 If someone or something impregnates a thing with a substance, they make the substance spread through it and stay in it. 使浸透;浸渍 [Also VERB n]

  • Undercover officers found drug-making equipment used to impregnate paper with LSD. [VERB noun + with]

    卧底警官发现了用于使迷幻药浸透到纸里的毒品制造设备。

  • ...a block of plastic impregnated with a light-absorbing dye. [VERB-ed]

    一块浸透吸光染料的塑料

SYN saturate, soak, steep, fill  
-impregnated

  • ...nicotine-impregnated chewing gum.

    浸有尼古丁的口香糖

2 VERB 使受精;使怀孕 When a man or a male animal impregnates a female, he makes her pregnant. 使受精;使怀孕 [formal]

  • Norman's efforts to impregnate her failed. [VERB noun]

    诺曼想使她怀孕的努力失败了。
