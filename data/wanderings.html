wanderings
wɒndərɪŋz ˈwɒndərɪŋz
N-PLURAL [usually with poss] 漫游;流浪;漂泊 Someone's wanderings are journeys that they make from place to place without staying in one place for a long time. 漫游;流浪;漂泊

  • On his wanderings he's picked up Spanish, Italian, French and a smattering of Russian.

    在流浪途中，他学会了西班牙语、意大利语、法语和一丁点儿俄语。
