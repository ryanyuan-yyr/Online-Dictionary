unopened
ʌnoʊpənd ʌnˈəʊpənd
ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 未被打开的 If something is unopened, it has not been opened yet. 未被打开的

  • ...unopened bottles of olive oil.

    一瓶瓶封着的橄榄油

  • The letter lay unopened in the travel firm's pigeonhole.

    那封信被放在旅游公司的文件架上，一直没有拆封。

  • Catherine put all the envelopes aside unopened.

    凯瑟琳把所有信件放在一旁，全都未启封。
