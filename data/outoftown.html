out-of-town ˌaʊtəvˈtaʊn
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 城外的；市郊的 Out-of-town shops or facilities are situated away from the centre of a town or city. 城外的；市郊的

  • ...shopping at cheaper, out-of-town supermarkets.

    在价格低廉的城郊超市购物

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 外来的；非本地的 Out-of-town is used to describe people who do not live in a particular town or city, but have travelled there for a particular purpose. 外来的；非本地的

  • ...a deluxe hotel for out-of-town visitors.

    供外地来客下榻的豪华旅馆
