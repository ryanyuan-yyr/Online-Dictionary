abundantly
əbʌndəntli
1 ADV [ADVERB adjective] 充分地;十分地 If something is abundantly clear, it is extremely obvious. 充分地;十分地

  • He made it abundantly clear that anybody who disagrees with his policies will not last long.

    他非常明确地表示任何不同意他政策的人都坚持不了多久。

SYN exceptionally, very, highly, greatly  
2 ADV-GRADED [usually ADVERB with verb, oft ADVERB adjective] 大量地;丰富地;充足地 Something that occurs abundantly is present in large quantities. 大量地;丰富地;充足地

  • ...a plant that grows abundantly in the United States.

    在美国大量生长的一种植物

  • All the pages are abundantly illustrated with colour photographs.

    每页都配有丰富的彩色照片。

SYN plentifully, greatly, freely, amply
