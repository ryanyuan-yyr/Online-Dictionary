eucalyptus
juːkəlɪptəs ˈjuːkəˌlɪpt ˌjukəˈlɪptəs
Word forms: plural eucalyptuses, plural eucalyptus
N-VAR [oft NOUN n] 桉树（原产于加拿大） A eucalyptus is an evergreen tree, originally from Australia, that is grown to provide wood, gum, and an oil that is used in medicines. 桉树（原产于加拿大）
