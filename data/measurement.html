measurement
meʒəʳmənt ˈmɛʒərmənt
Word forms: plural measurements
1 N-COUNT （通常以数字形式呈现的）测量结果 A measurement is a result, usually expressed in numbers, that you obtain by measuring something. （通常以数字形式呈现的）测量结果

  • We took lots of measurements.

    我们得到了许多测量结果。

  • The measurements are extraordinarily accurate.

    测量结果极为精确。

2 N-VAR 测量;计量 Measurement of something is the process of measuring it in order to obtain a result expressed in numbers. 测量;计量

  • Measurement of blood pressure can be undertaken by practice nurses. [+ of]

    测量血压可以由实习护士完成。

SYN calculation, assessment, evaluation, estimation  
3 N-VAR （质量、价值或影响的）衡量，评估，估量 The measurement of the quality, value, or effect of something is the activity of deciding how great it is. （质量、价值或影响的）衡量，评估，估量

  • ...the measurement of output in the non-market sector. [+ of]

    评估非市场部门的效益

4 N-PLURAL [with poss] 三围；(身材)尺寸 Your measurements are the size of your waist, chest, hips, and other parts of your body, which you need to know when you are buying clothes. 三围；(身材)尺寸
