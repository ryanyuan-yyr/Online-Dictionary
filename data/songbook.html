songbook
sɔŋbʊk ˈsɒŋˌbʊk ˈsɔŋˌbʊk
Word forms: songbooks
1 N-COUNT 歌集A songwriter's songbook is all the songs that he or she has written. You can also refer to the songs that a singer performs as their songbook. 歌集

  • When you have a songbook as powerful as Dylan's, why not use it.

    当你的歌集像迪伦的那样强大，为什么不用它。

2 N-COUNT 词曲集A songbook is a book containing the words and music of a lot of songs. 词曲集

  • ...a pop songbook.

    ...一本流行歌曲集。
