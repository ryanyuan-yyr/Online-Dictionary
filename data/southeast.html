southeast
saʊθist ˌsaʊθˈiːst ˌsaʊˈiːst ˌsaʊθˈist ˌ saʊist
1 N-UNCOUNT [also the N] 东南;东南方 The south-east is the direction which is halfway between south and east. 东南;东南方

  • The city of Chengdu lies some seven hundred miles to the South-East.

    成都市位于东南方大约 700 英里处。

2 N-SING (地方、国家或地区的)东南部 The south-east of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the south-east. (地方、国家或地区的)东南部

  • ...the regional electricity company serving the South-east of England.

    为英格兰东南部服务的区域电力公司

  • The heaviest snowfalls today are expected in the south east.

    今天最大的降雪预计将出现在东南部地区。

3 ADV 向东南方;朝东南 If you go south-east, you travel towards the south-east. 向东南方;朝东南

  • We turned south-east, making for Portoferraio.

    我们转向东南方向，朝费拉约港前进。

4 ADV [ADV of n] 位于…的东南方;在…的东南 Something that is south-east of a place is positioned to the south-east of it. 位于…的东南方;在…的东南

  • ...the potteries of Iznik, some 120km south-east of Istanbul.

    位于伊斯坦布尔东南大约120公里处的伊兹尼克出产的陶器

5 ADJ 东南部的;东南方的 The south-east part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-east. 东南部的;东南方的

  • ...South-East Asia.

    东南亚

  • ...the south-east quarter of Lincolnshire.

    林肯郡的东南区

  • ...an island just off Shetland's south-east coast.

    设得兰群岛东南海岸附近的一个小岛

6 ADJ (风)来自东南方的 A south-east wind is a wind that blows from the south-east. (风)来自东南方的
