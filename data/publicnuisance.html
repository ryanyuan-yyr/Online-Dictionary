public nuisance
Word forms: plural public nuisances
N-COUNT [usually singular] 妨害公共利益的人（或行为）；社会公害；为公众厌恶的人 If something or someone is, or causes, a public nuisance, they break the law by harming or annoying members of the public. 妨害公共利益的人（或行为）；社会公害；为公众厌恶的人 [law]

  • ...the 45-day jail sentence he received for causing a public nuisance after taking part in a demonstration.

    他在参与游行之后因妨害公共利益被判入狱45天

  • Back in the 1980s drug users were a public nuisance in Zurich.

    在20世纪80年代，吸毒者在苏黎世是被众人所唾弃的。
