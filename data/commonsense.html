common sense
(also commonsense)
N-UNCOUNT 常识;直觉判断力 Your common sense is your natural ability to make good judgments and to behave in a practical and sensible way. 常识;直觉判断力

  • Use your common sense.

    用一用你的常识吧。

  • She always had a lot of common sense.

    她一直都有很强的直觉判断力。

  • ...a common-sense approach.

    符合常理的方法

SYN good sense, sound judgment, level-headedness, practicality
