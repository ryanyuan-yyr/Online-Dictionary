uniformity
juːnɪfɔːʳmɪti ˌjunəˈfɔrməti
1 N-UNCOUNT 统一（性);一致（性）；规则（性） If there is uniformity in something such as a system, organization, or group of countries, the same rules, ideas, or methods are applied in all parts of it. 统一（性);一致（性）；规则（性）

  • Spanish liberals sought to create linguistic as well as administrative uniformity.

    西班牙自由主义者寻求语言及行政上的统一。

  • ...the strength of the ideology is seen in the remarkable uniformity of attitudes and beliefs.

    从态度和信仰上的高度一致可以看出意识形态的力量
