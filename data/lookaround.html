PHRASAL VERB （四处）转转；参观 If you look around or look round a building or place, you walk round it and look at the different parts of it. （四处）转转；参观

  • We went to look round the show homes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们去参观了一下样板间。

  • I'm going to look around and see what I can find. [VERB PREP.]

    我去四下里转转，看能发现些什么。
