sweetness
swiːtnəs
1 PHRASE (关系)友好;(情况)令人愉快，顺利 If you say that a relationship or situation is not all sweetness and light, you mean that it is not as pleasant as it appears to be. (关系)友好;(情况)令人愉快，顺利

  • It has not all been sweetness and light between him and the Prime Minister.

    他和首相的关系并不总是那么愉快融洽。
