tote
toʊt toʊt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense totes, present participle toting, past tense , past participle toted
1 N-SING (赛马的)赌金计算系统 The Tote is a system of betting money on horses in races. (赛马的)赌金计算系统 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 parimutuel
2 VERB （可见地）携带;（尤指）佩（枪） To tote something, especially a gun, means to carry it with you in such a way that people can see it. （可见地）携带;（尤指）佩（枪） [journalism]

  • The demonstrators fled when soldiers toting machine guns advanced on the crowd. [VERB noun]

    当持有机枪的士兵向人群进逼时，示威者逃散了。

-toting

  • They are too frightened to speak out against the gun-toting thugs.

    他们非常害怕，不敢公开抗议持枪暴徒。

  • Durham has much to offer the camera-toting visitor.

    达勒姆景点众多，对携带相机的游客来说大有可拍。
