special school
Word forms: plural special schools
N-COUNT (为身体或智力有缺陷的儿童开设的)特殊学校 A special school is a school for children who have some kind of serious physical or mental problem. (为身体或智力有缺陷的儿童开设的)特殊学校 [British]

  • The 5-year-old has no speech and limited movement, and attends a special school.

    这名 5 岁的儿童不能说话，行动受限，在特殊学校上学。
