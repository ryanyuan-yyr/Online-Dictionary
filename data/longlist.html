long-list ˈlɒŋlɪst
(also longlist)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense long-lists, present participle long-listing, past tense , past participle long-listed
1 N-COUNT 初选名单；初审名单；初赛名单 A long-list for something such as a job or a prize is a large group that has been chosen from all the people who applied for the job, or all the people or things that are competing for the prize. The successful ones from this group are chosen to go on the shortlist . 初选名单；初审名单；初赛名单

  • There are 27 riders on the long-list.

    27位骑手进入初赛。

2 VERB 将…列入初选名单 If someone or something is long-listed for a job or a prize, they are put on a long-list of those to be considered for that job or prize. 将…列入初选名单

  • She was long-listed for the senior team last year. [be VERB-ed + for]

    她去年被列入了一线队初选名单。
