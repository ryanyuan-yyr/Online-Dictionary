forewarn
fɔːʳwɔːʳn fɔrˈwɔrn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense forewarns, present participle forewarning, past tense , past participle forewarned
1 VERB 预先警告;事先告诫 If you forewarn someone about something, you warn them in advance that it is going to happen. 预先警告;事先告诫

  • The Macmillan Guide had forewarned me of what to expect. [V n + of/about]

    《麦克米伦指南》已经事先告诫过我会遇到什么情况。

SYN alert, advise, caution, tip off
