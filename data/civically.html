civic
sɪvɪk ˈsɪvɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 市政的;城镇当局的 You use civic to describe people or things that have an official status in a town or city. 市政的;城镇当局的

  • ...the businessmen and civic leaders of Manchester.

    曼彻斯特的商人和市政官员

  • ...Bromley Civic Centre.

    布罗姆利市政中心

SYN public, community, borough, municipal

  • ...a sense of civic pride.

    公民的自豪感
