acceleration
ækseləreɪʃən ækˌsɛlərˈeɪʃən ækˌsɛləˈreɪʃən əkˌsɛlərˈeɪʃən əkˌsɛləreɪʃɛn
1 N-UNCOUNT 加快；增速 The acceleration of a process or change is the fact that it is getting faster and faster. 加快；增速

  • He has also called for an acceleration of political reforms. [+ of/in]

    他同时呼吁加快政治改革的步伐。

  • ...the sharp acceleration in job losses.

    失业的急速增加

SYN hastening, hurrying, stepping up [informal] , expedition  
2 N-UNCOUNT （汽车等的）加速能力，加速幅度 Acceleration is the rate at which a car or other vehicle can increase its speed, often seen in terms of the time that it takes to reach a particular speed. （汽车等的）加速能力，加速幅度

  • Acceleration to 60 mph takes a mere 5.7 seconds.

    加速到时速60英里只需要5.7秒。

SYN speeding up, gathering speed, opening up, increasing speed  
3 N-UNCOUNT 加速度 Acceleration is the rate at which the speed of an object increases. 加速度 [technical]
