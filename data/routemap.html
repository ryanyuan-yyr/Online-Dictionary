route map
ruːt mæp
Word forms: plural route maps
1 N-COUNT 交通图;线路图 A route map is a map that shows the main roads in a particular area or the main routes used by buses, trains and other forms of transport in a particular area. 交通图;线路图
2 N-COUNT 示范;范例;最佳方式 If you describe one thing as a route map for another thing, you mean that it provides a model showing the best way to achieve or describe it. 示范;范例;最佳方式

  • Nowhere could you find a better route map of the troubles of Northern Ireland than in the articles of The Independent's David McKittrick. [+ of]

    对北爱尔兰问题作出最佳阐述与分析的莫过于《独立报》戴维·麦基特里克的文章了。
