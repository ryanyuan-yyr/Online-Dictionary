blurt out
PHRASAL VERB 脱口而出；说漏嘴 If someone blurts something out, they blurt it. 脱口而出；说漏嘴 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • 'You're mad,' the driver blurted out. [VERB PREP. with quote]

    “你疯了，”司机脱口而出。

  • Over the food, Richard blurted out what was on his mind. [VERB PREP. noun]

    吃东西的时候，理查德把自己的想法一股脑儿全说了出来。

SYN tell, reveal, give away, cry
