grub
grʌb grʌb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense grubs, present participle grubbing, past tense , past participle grubbed
1 N-COUNT （昆虫的）幼虫，蛆 A grub is a young insect which has just come out of an egg and looks like a short fat worm. （昆虫的）幼虫，蛆
2 N-UNCOUNT 食物；吃的 Grub is food. 食物；吃的 [informal]

  • Get yourself some grub and come and sit down.

    你自己去盛些吃的，过来坐下吃。

SYN food, feed, rations, tack [informal]  
3 VERB 翻找；搜寻 If you grub around, you search for something. 翻找；搜寻

  • I simply cannot face grubbing through all this paper. [VERB adv./prep.]

    我简直受不了要翻阅这么多文件。

Phrasal verbs:
grub up
