compunction
kəmpʌŋkʃən kəmˈpʌŋkʃən
N-UNCOUNT 内疚;愧疚 If you say that someone has no compunction about doing something, you mean that they do it without feeling ashamed or guilty. 内疚;愧疚 [disapproval]

  • He has no compunction about relating how he killed his father.

    他在讲述自己杀害父亲的过程时，没有感到丝毫的内疚。

SYN guilt, misgiving, qualm, scruples
