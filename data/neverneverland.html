never-never land ˈnɛvəˈnɛvə lænd
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 理想之地;世外桃源;“永无岛” Never-never land is an imaginary place where everything is perfect and no-one has any problems. 理想之地;世外桃源;“永无岛” [informal]

  • We became suspended in some stately never-never land of pleasure, luxury and idleness. [+ of]

    我们流连忘返大气、奢华、惬意、闲适的世外桃源。
