bully-boy
(also bully boy)Word forms: plural bully-boys
1 N-COUNT 恶棍；流氓 If you describe a man as a bully-boy, you disapprove of him because he is rough and aggressive. 恶棍；流氓 [disapproval]

  • ...bully-boys and murderers.

    恶棍和杀人犯

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 恶棍（行径）；流氓（手段） If you say that someone uses bully-boy tactics, you disapprove of them because they use rough and aggressive methods. 恶棍（行径）；流氓（手段） [journalism, disapproval]

  • Some people accuse the tax inspectors of bully-boy tactics.

    有些人指控税务稽查员使用了流氓手段。
