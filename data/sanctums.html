sanctum
sæŋtəm ˈsæŋktəm
Word forms: plural sanctums
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] (不受干扰的)私室，密室 If you refer to someone's inner sanctum, you mean a room which is private and sometimes secret, where they can be quiet and alone. (不受干扰的)私室，密室

  • His bedroom's his inner sanctum.

    卧室是他的一方净土。

SYN refuge, retreat, den [US] , hideaway  
2 N-COUNT (寺庙、清真寺的)圣所,圣殿 A sanctum is the holiest place inside a holy building such as a temple or mosque. (寺庙、清真寺的)圣所,圣殿
