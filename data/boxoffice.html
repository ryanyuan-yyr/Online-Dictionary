box office
(also box-office)Word forms: plural box offices
1 N-COUNT （剧院、电影院或音乐厅的）售票处 The box office in a theatre, cinema, or concert hall is the place where the tickets are sold. （剧院、电影院或音乐厅的）售票处
2 N-SING [NOUN n] （电影等的）卖座情况，票房收入 When people talk about the box office, they are referring to the degree of success of a film or play in terms of the number of people who go to watch it or the amount of money it makes. （电影等的）卖座情况，票房收入

  • The film has taken £180 million at the box office.

    这部电影票房收入已达1.8亿英镑。

  • The film was a huge box-office success.

    这部影片票房大卖。
