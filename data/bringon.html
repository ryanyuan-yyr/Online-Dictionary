bring on
PHRASAL VERB 引起，导致（疾病、痛苦或某种感觉） If something brings on an illness, pain, or feeling, especially one that you often suffer from, it causes you to have it. 引起，导致（疾病、痛苦或某种感觉） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Severe shock can bring on an attack of acne. [VERB PREP. noun]

    严重的惊吓会造成痤疮突发。

  • Bob died of a heart attack, brought on by his lifestyle. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    鲍勃死于由他的生活方式引起的心脏病突发。

SYN cause, produce, create, effect
