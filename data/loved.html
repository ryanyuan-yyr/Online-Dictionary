-loved -lʌvd
COMB in ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （与副词连用构成形容词）表示“被爱（或受喜爱）程度” -loved combines with adverbs to form adjectives that describe how much someone or something is loved. （与副词连用构成形容词）表示“被爱（或受喜爱）程度”

  • The similarities between the much-loved father and his son are remarkable.

    这位深受爱戴的父亲和他儿子之间惊人地相似。

  • ...two of Mendelssohn's best-loved works.

    门德尔松最受喜爱的其中两个作品
