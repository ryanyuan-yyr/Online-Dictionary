northward
nɔːʳθwəʳd ˈnɔrθwərd ˈnɔrðərd
(also northwards)
ADV [usually ADVERB after verb, oft n ADV] 向北 Northward or northwards means towards the north. 向北

  • Tropical storm Marco is pushing northward up Florida's coast.

    热带风暴“马可”正沿佛罗里达海岸向北推进。

  • ...the flow of immigrants northward.

    向北推进的移民潮

Northward is also an adjective.

  • The northward journey from Jalalabad was no more than 120 miles.

    从贾拉拉巴德向北的旅程不到120英里。
