voyeur
vwaɪɜːʳ vwaɪˈɜː vwajœr vɔɪ- vɔɪˈɜr vwɑˈjɜr
Word forms: plural voyeurs
1 N-COUNT 窥淫癖者 A voyeur is someone who gets sexual pleasure from secretly watching other people having sex or taking their clothes off. 窥淫癖者
2 N-COUNT 好刺探他人隐私者 If you describe someone as a voyeur, you disapprove of them because you think they enjoy watching other people's suffering or problems. 好刺探他人隐私者 [disapproval]

  • The media has made unfeeling voyeurs of all of us.

    媒体把我们所有人都变成了无情刺探他人隐私的人。
