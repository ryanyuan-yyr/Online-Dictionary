unmarked
ʌnmɑːʳkt ʌnˈmɑːkt
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 没有污渍的；没有污点的；没有疤痕的；没有斑点的 Something that is unmarked has no marks on it. 没有污渍的；没有污点的；没有疤痕的；没有斑点的

  • Her shoes are still white and unmarked.

    她的鞋仍然很白，没有任何污渍。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 没有标记的；没有标志的 Something that is unmarked has no marking on it which identifies what it is or whose it is. 没有标记的；没有标志的

  • He had seen them come out and get into the unmarked police car.

    他看见他们走出来，上了一辆没有标志的警车。

  • He lies in an unmarked grave at Elmton.

    他长眠在爱尔莫顿一处无名冢下。

3 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ after v] （球类比赛中）无人防守的，没被注意到的 In a sport such as football, hockey, or basketball, if a player is unmarked, there are no players from the opposing team who are watching them in order to challenge them when they have control of the ball. （球类比赛中）无人防守的，没被注意到的 [British]

  • Sheringham was unmarked as he met Anderton's free kick and headed in after nine minutes.

    9分钟后，谢林汉姆在无人防守的情况下接安德顿开出的任意球头球破门得分。
