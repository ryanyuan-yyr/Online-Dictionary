tax incentive
tæks ɪnˈsɛntɪv
Word forms: tax incentives
N-COUNT 税收激励A tax incentive is a government measure that is intended to encourage individuals and businesses to spend money or to save money by reducing the amount of tax that they have to pay. 税收激励

  • ...a new tax incentive to encourage the importation of manufactured products.

    ...一项旨在鼓励制成品进口的新的税收激励政策。
