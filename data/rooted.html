rooted
ruːtɪd
1 ADJ 起源于…的;根源于…的;由…产生的 If you say that one thing is rooted in another, you mean that it is strongly influenced by it or has developed from it. 起源于…的;根源于…的;由…产生的

  • The crisis is rooted in deep rivalries between the two groups. [+ in]

    这场危机的根源是这两个群体之间积怨已深的敌对情绪。

  • ...powerful songs rooted in traditional African music.

    起源于传统非洲音乐的打动人心的歌曲

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n, usually adverb ADJ] （观点、感受）根深蒂固的，难以改变的 If someone has deeply rooted opinions or feelings, they believe or feel something extremely strongly and are unlikely to change. （观点、感受）根深蒂固的，难以改变的

  • Racism is a deeply rooted prejudice which has existed for thousands of years.

    种族主义是存在了几千年的根深蒂固的偏见。

3See also:deep-rooted 
4 PHRASE （因害怕或吃惊）呆住，不能动弹 If you are rooted to the spot, you are unable to move because you are very frightened or shocked. （因害怕或吃惊）呆住，不能动弹

  • We just stopped there, rooted to the spot.

    我们一下子就停在了那里，呆若木鸡。
