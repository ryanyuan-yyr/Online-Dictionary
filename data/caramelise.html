caramelize
kærəməlaɪz ˈkɑrməˌlaɪz ˈkærəməˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense caramelizes, present participle caramelizing, past tense , past participle caramelized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 caramelise
1 VERB (糖)熬成焦糖 If sugar caramelizes, it turns to caramel as a result of being heated. (糖)熬成焦糖

  • Cook until the sugar starts to caramelize. [VERB]

    将糖熬成焦糖。

2 VERB 给(水果等)炒上一层焦糖 If you caramelize something such as fruit, you cook it with sugar so that it is coated with caramel. 给(水果等)炒上一层焦糖

  • Start by caramelizing some onions. [VERB noun]

    先将一些洋葱外面炒上焦糖。

  • ...caramelised apples and pears. [VERB-ed]

    拔丝苹果和梨
