shadow boxing
1 N-UNCOUNT 空拳攻防练习 Shadow boxing is a form of physical exercise or training in which you move your hands and feet as if you are boxing someone. 空拳攻防练习
2 N-UNCOUNT 佯装的冲突;装模作样的斗争 If you describe what two people or groups are doing as shadow boxing, you mean that they seem to be taking action against each other but in fact are not serious about the dispute. 佯装的冲突;装模作样的斗争

  • ...the tedious shadow boxing that we normally see between bosses and unions in Britain.

    在英国我们通常所见的老板和工会之间令人厌烦的假模假样的斗争
