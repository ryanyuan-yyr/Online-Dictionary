version
vɜːʳʃən -ʒən ˈvɜrʒən ˈvɜrʃən
Word forms: plural versions
1 N-COUNT 变化形式;变体;版本 A version of something is a particular form of it in which some details are different from earlier or later forms. 变化形式;变体;版本

  • ...an updated version of his book. [+ of]

    他的书的最新版本

  • Ludo is a version of an ancient Indian racing game. [+ of]

    鲁多是由一种古印度竞赛游戏演变而来。

  • The second-hand version is a poor copy of the original.

    盗版版本是对原版的低劣复制。

SYN form, variety, variant, sort  
2 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] （某人对某事件区别于他人的）描述，说法 Someone's version of an event is their own description of it, especially when it is different to other people's. （某人对某事件区别于他人的）描述，说法

  • Yesterday afternoon the White House put out a new version of events. [+ of]

    昨天下午，白宫对事件给出了另一种说法。

  • She and her friends wanted to go public with their version of the incident.

    她和朋友们想把他们看到的事情经过公之于众。

  • There have been widely differing versions in the newspapers about the prison siege.

    关于围攻监狱一事，各家报纸众说纷纭，莫衷一是。

SYN account, report, side, description
