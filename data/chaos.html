chaos
keɪɒs ˈkeɪˌɑs
1 N-UNCOUNT 混乱;乱糟糟 Chaos is a state of complete disorder and confusion. 混乱;乱糟糟

  • The world's first transatlantic balloon race ended in chaos last night.

    昨晚世界第一届跨大西洋热气球比赛在一片混乱中收场。

  • It is impossible to establish democracy amid economic chaos.

    经济秩序混乱不堪，是不可能建立民主的。

SYN disorder, confusion, mayhem, havoc [informal]
