inequitable
ɪnekwɪtəbəl ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不公正的;不公平的 If you say that something is inequitable, you are criticizing it because it is unfair or unjust. 不公正的;不公平的 [formal, disapproval]

  • The welfare system is grossly inequitable and inefficient.

    福利制度非常不公平,效率也极低。

SYN unfair, prejudiced, biased, one-sided
