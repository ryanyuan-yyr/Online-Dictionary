saver
seɪvəʳ
Word forms: plural savers
N-COUNT 储蓄者;储户 A saver is a person who regularly saves money by paying it into a bank account or a building society. 储蓄者;储户

  • Low interest rates are bad news for savers.

    低利率对于储蓄者来说是个坏消息。

-saver -seɪvəʳ
Word forms: plural -savers
COMB in N-COUNT (与 time，energy 等词连用)表示“省…的”，“节省…的” -saver combines with words such as 'time' and 'energy' to indicate that something prevents the thing mentioned from being wasted. (与 time，energy 等词连用)表示“省…的”，“节省…的”

  • These potatoes are sold ready sorted and washed, and can prove a great time-saver for the busy cook.

    这些土豆卖时已拣好洗净，对于赶时间做饭的人来说能节省很多时间。

  • These zip-top bags are great space-savers if storage is limited.

    如果可储藏的地方有限，这些拉环式背包能节省很大空间。
