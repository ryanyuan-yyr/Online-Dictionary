thoughtless
θɔːtləs ˈθɔtlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 不顾及他人的;欠考虑的 If you describe someone as thoughtless, you are critical of them because they forget or ignore other people's wants, needs, or feelings. 不顾及他人的;欠考虑的 [disapproval]

  • ...a small minority of thoughtless and inconsiderate people.

    一小群只顾自己、不顾别人的人

  • It was thoughtless of her to mention it. [+ of]

    她提起这事有些欠考虑。

  • It was just a thoughtless remark.

    那不过是一句欠考虑的话。

SYN unthinking, stupid, silly, careless  
thoughtlessly

  • They thoughtlessly planned a picnic without him.

    他们太粗心，安排野餐竟然没有带上他。

  • Nobody is perfect, and everyone acts thoughtlessly on occasion.

    人无完人，谁都难保不偶尔疏忽一次。
