dripping
drɪpɪŋ ˈdrɪpɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT (烤肉时滴下的)油滴 Dripping is the fat which comes out of meat when it is fried or roasted, and which can be used for frying food. (烤肉时滴下的)油滴
2 PHRASE 湿淋淋的;湿透的 If you are dripping wet, you are so wet that water is dripping from you. 湿淋淋的;湿透的

  • We were dripping wet from the spray.

    我们被水花打湿了。

3 See also: drip
