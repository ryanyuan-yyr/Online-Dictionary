unassuming
ʌnəsjuːmɪŋ ˌʌnəˈsjuːmɪŋ -suːm- ˌʌnəˈsumɪŋ ˌʌnəˈsjumɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 低调的；谦逊的；不招摇的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as unassuming, you approve of them because they are quiet and do not try to appear important. 低调的；谦逊的；不招摇的 [approval]

  • He's a man of few words, very polite and unassuming.

    他话不多，很有礼貌，为人谦和。

  • She has a gentle, unassuming manner.

    她很和善，不摆架子。

SYN modest, quiet, humble, meek
