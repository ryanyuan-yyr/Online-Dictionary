sparing
speərɪŋ ˈspɛərɪŋ ˈspɛrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 节约的;俭省的 Someone who is sparing with something uses it or gives it only in very small quantities. 节约的;俭省的

  • I've not been sparing with the garlic.

    我总是用很多大蒜。

  • Her sparing use of make-up only seemed to enhance her classically beautiful features.

    一点淡妆反而增添了她五官的古典韵味。

  • Only a sparing amount is needed.

    只需要很少的一点。

SYN economical, frugal, thrifty, saving  
sparingly

  • Medication is used sparingly.

    用药很节省。

  • Normally she ate sparingly.

    她通常吃得很省。
