transgender
trænzdʒendəʳ trænsˈdʒɛndər trænzˈdʒɛndər
ADJ 跨性别的；变性的 Transgender people, such as transsexuals, do not have a straightforward gender identity. 跨性别的；变性的

  • ...a three-year-project designed to overcome prejudice towards gay, lesbian, bisexual and transgender people.

    为期三年旨在克服对男女同性恋者、双性恋者和跨性别者所持偏见的项目

Transgender is also a noun.

  • Karen said she is a transgender — a man who wants to be a woman and is attracted to women.

    卡伦说她是个跨性别者—一个渴望成为女人并被女人所吸引的男人。
