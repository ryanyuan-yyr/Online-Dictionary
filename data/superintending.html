superintend
suːpərɪntend ˌsupərɪnˈtɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense superintends, present participle superintending, past tense , past participle superintended
VERB 管理;监督;指挥;主管 If you superintend something, you have responsibility for ensuring that it is carried out properly. 管理;监督;指挥;主管 [formal]

  • During the interval, Linton superintended a prize draw. [VERB noun]

    中间休息时，林顿组织了一次抽奖。

SYN supervise, run, oversee, control
