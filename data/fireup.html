fire up
1 PHRASAL VERB 发动 (机器等) If you fire up a machine, you switch it on. 发动 (机器等) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • fire up your engine and head out.

    发动你的引擎，开出去。

2 PHRASAL VERB 激励; 鼓舞 If you fire someone up, you make them feel very enthusiastic or motivated. 激励; 鼓舞 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The president knows his task is to fire up the delegates. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这位主席知道自己的任务就是激励代表们。
