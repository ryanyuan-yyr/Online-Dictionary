drunken
drʌŋkən ˈdrʌŋkən
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 酒醉引起的;与酒醉有关的 Drunken is used to describe events and situations that involve people who are drunk. 酒醉引起的;与酒醉有关的

  • The pain roused him from his drunken stupor.

    他喝得烂醉如泥,而后一阵疼痛把他疼醒了。

  • He hit her with a frying pan during a drunken brawl.

    他酒后耍酒疯用平底锅打了她。

2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 醉酒的;酗酒的 A drunken person is drunk or is frequently drunk. 醉酒的;酗酒的

  • Groups of drunken hooligans smashed shop windows and threw stones.

    一群群醉醺醺的小流氓砸碎商店的橱窗，还投掷石块。

drunkenly

  • One night Bob stormed drunkenly into her house.

    一天晚上鲍勃醉醺醺地闯入她家。

drunkenness

  • He was arrested for drunkenness on his way to the football ground.

    他在去足球场的路上因醉酒闹事而被捕。
