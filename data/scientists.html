scientist
saɪəntɪst ˈsaɪəntɪst
Word forms: plural scientists
1 N-COUNT 科学家;科学工作者 A scientist is someone who has studied science and whose job is to teach or do research in science. 科学家;科学工作者

  • Scientists have collected more data than expected.

    科学家们已经收集到了比预期更多的数据。

SYN researcher, inventor, technologist, boffin [informal]  
2See also:social scientist
