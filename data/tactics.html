tactic
tæktɪk ˈtæktɪk
Word forms: plural tactics
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 战术;策略;招数 Tactics are the methods that you choose to use in order to achieve what you want in a particular situation. 战术;策略;招数

  • What sort of tactics will the President use to rally the people behind him?

    总统会用什么样的策略把人们号召起来支持他呢？

  • He pressed on in the hope that a few others would join him. The tactic paid off.

    他硬着头皮干下去，希望能有几个人加入进来。这一招奏效了。

SYN policy, approach, course, way
