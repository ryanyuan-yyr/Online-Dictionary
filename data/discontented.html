discontented
dɪskəntentɪd ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd ˈdɪskənˈtɛntɪd
ADJ-GRADED 不满的;不满意的 If you are discontented, you are not satisfied with your situation. 不满的;不满意的

  • The government tried to appease discontented workers. [+ with]

    政府试图安抚不满的工人们。

  • ...farmers discontented with low prices for their produce.

    农民们对农产品低廉价格的不满

SYN dissatisfied, complaining, unhappy, miserable
