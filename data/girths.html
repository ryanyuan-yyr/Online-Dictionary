girth
gɜːʳθ gɜrθ
Word forms: plural girths
1 N-VAR [oft poss NOUN] （人、动物等的）围长、腰围 The girth of an object, for example a person's or an animal's body, is its width or thickness, considered as the measurement around its circumference. （人、动物等的）围长、腰围 [formal]

  • A girl he knew had upset him by commenting on his increasing girth.

    他认识的一位女孩对他越来越粗的腰身说三道四，这让他心里很不痛快。

SYN size, measure, proportions, dimensions  
2 N-COUNT （马的）肚带，鞍带 A girth is a leather strap which is fastened firmly around the middle of a horse to keep the saddle or load in the right place. （马的）肚带，鞍带
