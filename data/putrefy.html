putrefy
pjuːtrɪfaɪ ˈpjutrəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense putrefies, present participle putrefying, past tense , past participle putrefied
VERB 腐烂；腐败 When something putrefies, it decays and produces a very unpleasant smell. 腐烂；腐败 [formal]

  • The meat in all of the open flasks putrefied. [VERB]

    所有开口瓶中的肉都腐烂了。

  • ...putrefying corpses. [VERB-ing]

    腐烂的尸体

SYN rot, break down, spoil, corrupt
