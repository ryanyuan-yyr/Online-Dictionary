syrupy
sɪrəpi ˈsɜrəpi ˈsɪrəpi
1 ADJ-GRADED 糖浆般甜腻的;糖浆般黏稠的 Liquid that is syrupy is sweet or thick like syrup. 糖浆般甜腻的;糖浆般黏稠的
2 ADJ-GRADED 缠绵的;过分多情的;煽情的 If you describe something as syrupy, you dislike it because it is too sentimental. 缠绵的;过分多情的;煽情的 [disapproval]

  • ...this syrupy film version of Conroy's novel.

    根据康罗伊小说拍成的这一非常煽情的电影

SYN romantic, emotional, sloppy [informal] , touching
