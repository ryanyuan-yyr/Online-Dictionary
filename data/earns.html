earn
ɜːʳn ɜrn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense earns, present participle earning, past tense , past participle earned
1 VERB 赚取;挣得 If you earn money, you receive money in return for work that you do. 赚取;挣得

  • Charlie was earning eight pounds, I was earning five. [VERB noun]

    查利赚了8英镑，我赚了5英镑。

  • What a lovely way to earn a living. [VERB noun]

    谋生妙道啊！

  • The dancers can earn anything between £20 and £30 for each session. [VERB noun]

    这些舞蹈演员每场演出的收入在20至30英镑之间。

  • She was always out earning. [VERB]

    她总是在外面赚钱。

SYN be paid, make, get, receive  
2 VERB 盈（利）;生（利） If something earns money, it produces money as profit or interest. 盈（利）;生（利）

  • ...a current account which earns little or no interest. [VERB noun]

    利息很少或为零的活期账户

  • We buy everything abroad with the money earned from oil imports. [VERB noun]

    我们用进口石油赚到的钱从国外购买一切商品。

3 VERB 赢得，博得（赞扬等） If you earn something such as praise, you get it because you deserve it. 赢得，博得（赞扬等）

  • Companies must earn a reputation for honesty. [VERB noun]

    公司必须树立诚信的声誉。

  • I think that's earned him very high admiration. [VERB noun noun]

    我认为那一点为他赢得了极高的赞誉。

SYN deserve, win, gain, attain
