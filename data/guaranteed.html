guaranteed
gærəntiːd ˌɡærənˈtiːd
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] [usually ADJ to-infinitive] 有保证的；确定的 If you say that something is guaranteed to happen, you mean that you are certain that it will happen. 有保证的；确定的

  • Reports of this kind are guaranteed to cause anxiety.

    这种报道势必会引发忧虑。

  • It's guaranteed that my colleagues think I'm deranged.

    同事们一定以为我疯了。

  • Success is not guaranteed.

    无法担保肯定能成功。

SYN sure, certain, bound
