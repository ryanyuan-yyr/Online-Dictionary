pow-wow paʊwaʊ
(also powwow)Word forms: plural pow-wows
1 N-COUNT （北美印第安人的）议事会 A pow-wow is a meeting or conference of Native Americans. （北美印第安人的）议事会
2 N-COUNT 会议；聚会；商谈 People sometimes refer to a meeting or discussion as a pow-wow . 会议；聚会；商谈 [informal]

  • Every year my father would call a family powwow to discuss where we were going on vacation.

    每年我父亲都会召开一次家庭会议，讨论我们要去哪儿度假。
