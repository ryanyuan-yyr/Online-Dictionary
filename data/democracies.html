democracy
dɪmɒkrəsi dɪˈmɑkrəsi
Word forms: plural democracies
1 N-UNCOUNT 民主;民主制度;民主政体 Democracy is a system of government in which people choose their rulers by voting for them in elections. 民主;民主制度;民主政体

  • ...the spread of democracy in Eastern Europe.

    民主制度在东欧的传播

  • ...the pro-democracy movement.

    亲民主运动

SYN self-government, republic, commonwealth, autonomy  
2 N-COUNT 民主国家 A democracy is a country in which the people choose their government by voting for it. 民主国家

  • The new democracies face tough challenges.

    这些新兴的民主国家面临着严峻挑战。

3 N-UNCOUNT 民主管理制度;民主作风 Democracy is a system of running organizations, businesses, and groups in which each member is entitled to vote and take part in decisions. 民主管理制度;民主作风

  • ...the union's emphasis on industrial democracy.

    该工会对工业民主的强调
