begging bowl
Word forms: plural begging bowls
N-COUNT 讨饭碗；（国家或机构）乞求经济援助 If a country or organization approaches other countries or organizations with a begging bowl, it asks them for money. 讨饭碗；（国家或机构）乞求经济援助 [mainly British]

  • He said earlier that he is not holding out a begging bowl.

    他早先说他不会伸手去乞求援助。
