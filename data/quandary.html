quandary
kwɒndəri ˈkwɒndrɪ ˈkwɑndəri ˈkwɑndri
Word forms: plural quandaries
N-COUNT [usually singular] 困境；窘境;左右为难的境地 If you are in a quandary, you have to make a decision but cannot decide what to do. 困境；窘境;左右为难的境地

  • The government appears to be in a quandary about what to do with so many people. [+ about]

    政府似乎陷入了左右为难的境地，不知道该拿这么多人怎么办。

SYN difficulty, dilemma, predicament, puzzle
