resettlement
riːsetəlmənt
N-UNCOUNT 重新定居;移居;重新安置 Resettlement is the process of moving people to a different place to live, because they are no longer allowed to stay in the area where they used to live. 重新定居;移居;重新安置 [Also + of]

  • Only refugees are eligible for resettlement abroad.

    只有难民才符合移民国外的条件。

  • ...the forcible resettlement of villagers from the countryside into towns.

    强制村民从农村搬迁到城市

  • ...resettlement agencies.

    移民代理机构
