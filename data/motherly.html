motherly
mʌðəʳli ˈmʌðərli
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 慈母般的 Motherly feelings or actions are like those of a kind mother. 慈母般的

  • It was an incredible display of motherly love and forgiveness.

    那是慈母般关爱与宽容的了不起的表现。

  • ...a plump, motherly woman.

    身材丰满的、慈母般的女人

SYN maternal, loving, kind, caring
