ponce
pɒns pɑns
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ponces, present participle poncing, past tense , past participle ponced
1 N-COUNT 同 pimp A ponce is the same as a pimp . 同 pimp [British, informal, old-fashioned]
2 N-COUNT 娘娘腔的人；脂粉气十足的人 If you call a man a ponce, you are insulting him because you think the way he dresses or behaves is too feminine. 娘娘腔的人；脂粉气十足的人 [British, informal, rude, disapproval]

  • The younger ones as far as I am concerned are a bunch of ponces.

    在我看来，现在年轻的一代就是一群娘娘腔。
