electromagnet
ɪlektroʊmægnɪt ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈmæɡnɪt iˌlɛktroʊˈmægnɪt ɪˈlɛktroʊˌmægnɪt iˈlɛktrəˌmægnɪt ɪˈlɛktrəˌmægnɪt
Word forms: plural electromagnets
N-COUNT 电磁铁;电磁体 An electromagnet is a magnet that consists of a piece of iron or steel surrounded by a coil. The metal becomes magnetic when an electric current is passed through the coil. 电磁铁;电磁体
