arduous
ɑːʳdʒuəs ˈɑrdʒuəs
ADJ-GRADED 困难的；艰巨的；费力的 Something that is arduous is difficult and tiring, and involves a lot of effort. 困难的；艰巨的；费力的

  • ...a long, hot and arduous journey.

    一段漫长、炎热而又艰苦的旅程

  • The task was more arduous than he had calculated.

    这项任务比他所估计的要艰巨得多。

SYN difficult, trying, hard, tough
