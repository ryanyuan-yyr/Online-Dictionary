laid-back
ˈleɪdˈbæk
ADJ-GRADED 悠然自得的；放松随和的 If you describe someone as laid-back, you mean that they behave in a calm relaxed way as if nothing will ever worry them. 悠然自得的；放松随和的 [informal]

  • Nothing worried him, he was really laid back.

    他万事无忧，日子过得十分悠游自在。

  • Everyone here has a really laid-back attitude.

    这里的每个人都是一副优哉游哉的态度。

SYN relaxed, calm, casual, together [slang]
