tic-tac-toe tɪktæktoʊ ˌtɪktækˈtəʊ ˌtɪkˌtækˈtoʊ
(also tick-tack-toe)
N-UNCOUNT 井字棋Tic-tac-toe is a game in which two players take turns in drawing either an "O" or an "X" in one square of a grid consisting of nine squares. The winner is the first player to get three of the same symbols in a row. 井字棋 [US]
