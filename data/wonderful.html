wonderful
wʌndəʳfʊl ˈwʌndərfəl
ADJ-GRADED 精彩的;绝妙的;极好的 If you describe something or someone as wonderful, you think they are extremely good. 精彩的;绝妙的;极好的

  • The cold, misty air felt wonderful on his face.

    雾蒙蒙的寒冷空气使他脸上感觉很舒爽。

  • It's wonderful to see you.

    见到你真好。

  • I've always thought he was a wonderful actor.

    我一直认为他是个出色的演员。

SYN excellent, mean [slang] , great [informal] , topping [British , slang]  
wonderfully

  • It's a system that works wonderfully well.

    该系统运转得十分顺畅。

  • The weather was wonderfully warm.

    天气暖洋洋的。
