pad
pæd pæd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pads, present participle padding, past tense , past participle padded
1 N-COUNT 垫子；衬垫；护垫 A pad is a fairly thick, flat piece of a material such as cloth or rubber. Pads are used, for example, to clean things, to protect things, or to change their shape. 垫子；衬垫；护垫

  • He withdrew the needle and placed a pad of cotton-wool over the spot. [+ of]

    他把针头拔出，在扎针处按了一块药棉。

  • ...a scouring pad.

    百洁布

  • ...a flowered dress with shoulder pads.

    有肩垫的印花裙子

SYN wad, dressing, pack, padding  
2 N-COUNT 拍纸簿；便笺本 A pad of paper is a number of pieces of paper which are fixed together along the top or the side, so that each piece can be torn off when it has been used. 拍纸簿；便笺本

  • She wrote on a pad of paper. [+ of]

    她写在了便笺簿上。

  • Have a pad and pencil ready and jot down some of your thoughts.

    准备好铅笔和便笺簿，把你的一些想法记下来。

  • 'Here's your ticket,' he said, and he tore it off the pad.

    “给你票，”他说着，从票本上把票撕了下来。

SYN notepad, block, tablet, notebook  
3 VERB 轻步快速地走；蹑手蹑脚地走 When someone pads somewhere, they walk there with steps that are fairly quick, light, and quiet. 轻步快速地走；蹑手蹑脚地走

  • Freddy speaks very quietly and pads around in soft velvet slippers. [VERB prep./adv.]

    弗蕾迪说话轻声细气，穿着柔软的天鹅绒拖鞋蹑手蹑脚地四处走动。

  • ...a dog padding through the streets. [VERB prep./adv.]

    轻步快速穿过街道的狗

  • I often bumped into him as he padded the corridors. [VERB noun]

    他在走廊里轻轻地走过时，我常常碰见他。

4 N-COUNT 直升机停机坪；火箭发射台 A pad is a platform or an area of flat, hard ground where helicopters take off and land or rockets are launched. 直升机停机坪；火箭发射台

  • ...a little round helicopter pad.

    小型圆形直升机停机坪

  • ...a landing pad on the back of the ship.

    船尾的停机坪

  • Journalists report seeing a fire on the pad after the launch.

    记者们报道说在发射后看到发射台着火了。

5See also:launch pad 
6 N-COUNT [usually singular, usually poss NOUN] 住处；（尤指）寓所，公寓 People can refer to the place where they live as their pad, especially if it is a flat. 住处；（尤指）寓所，公寓 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • I moved on round the big house to reach my pad.

    我绕过大房子走向我的公寓。

  • It wouldn't have occurred to me to get myself a bachelor pad.

    否则我不会想到给自己找一套单身公寓。

7 N-COUNT [usually plural] 指头肚儿；（动物的）爪垫 The pads of a person's fingers and toes or of an animal's feet are the soft, fleshy parts of them. 指头肚儿；（动物的）爪垫

  • Tap your cheeks all over with the pads of your fingers. [+ of]

    用手指头肚儿轻拍脸颊一遍。

8 VERB 给…加衬垫；垫衬 If you pad something, you put something soft in it or over it in order to make it less hard, to protect it, or to give it a different shape. 给…加衬垫；垫衬

  • Pad the back of a car seat with a pillow. [VERB noun + with]

    用枕头垫一下汽车坐椅靠背。

  • I can tell you I always padded my bras. [VERB noun]

    我可以告诉你我的内衣一直装衬垫的。

SYN pack, line, fill, protect  
padded

  • ...a padded jacket.

    夹袄

  • ...backrests padded with camel's wool.

    用骆驼绒填充的靠垫

9 See also: padding
