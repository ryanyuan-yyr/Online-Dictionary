re-route
(also reroute)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense re-routes, present participle re-routing, past tense , past participle re-routed
VERB 改变线路;改道 If vehicles or planes are re-routed, they are directed along a different route because the usual route cannot be used. 改变线路;改道

  • The heavy traffic was re-routed past my front door. [be VERB-ed]

    大量的车流改道经过我家前门。

  • They rerouted the planes at La Guardia airport. [VERB noun]

    他们改变了拉瓜迪亚机场飞机的航线。
