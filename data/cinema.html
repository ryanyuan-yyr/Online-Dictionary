cinema
sɪnɪmɑː ˈsɪnɪmə ˈsɪnəmə
Word forms: plural cinemas
1 N-COUNT 电影院 A cinema is a place where people go to watch films for entertainment. 电影院 [mainly British]

  • The country has relatively few cinemas.

    相对来讲，这个国家几乎没有几家电影院。

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 movie theater, movie house
2 N-SING （电影院的）电影 You can talk about the cinema when you are talking about seeing a film in a cinema. （电影院的）电影 [mainly British]

  • I can't remember the last time we went to the cinema.

    我记不得上次我们去看电影是什么时候了。

  • They decided to spend an evening at the cinema.

    他们决定晚上去看电影。

SYN pictures, movies, picture-house, flicks [slang]  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 the movies
3 N-UNCOUNT 电影业;电影艺术 Cinema is the business and art of making films. 电影业;电影艺术

  • Contemporary African cinema has much to offer in its vitality and freshness.

    当代非洲电影在活力和新颖性上颇足称道。

  • ...in the early days of cinema.

    电影业的早期

SYN films, pictures, movies, the big screen [informal]
