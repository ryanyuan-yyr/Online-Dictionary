spine
spaɪn spaɪn
Word forms: plural spines
1 N-COUNT 脊椎;脊柱 Your spine is the row of bones down your back. 脊椎;脊柱
2 N-COUNT 书脊 The spine of a book is the narrow stiff part which the pages and covers are attached to. 书脊
3 N-COUNT (动物身体上的)棘状突起;(植物上的)棘，刺，针 Spines are also long, sharp points on an animal's body or on a plant. (动物身体上的)棘状突起;(植物上的)棘，刺，针
