well-nigh
ˈwɛlˈnaɪ
(also well nigh)
ADV [ADVERB adjective] 几乎;差不多 Well-nigh means almost, but not completely or exactly. 几乎;差不多

  • Finding a rug that's just the colour, size and price you want can be well-nigh impossible.

    找到一块颜色、尺寸和价格都符合你要求的毛毯几乎是不可能的。

  • ...a hierarchical structure that was well-nigh unassailable.

    几乎不可撼动的等级制度

SYN almost, nearly, virtually, practically
