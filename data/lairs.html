lair
leəʳ ler lɛə lɛr
Word forms: plural lairs
1 N-COUNT [usually with poss] 兽穴；兽窝；巢穴 A lair is a place where a wild animal lives, usually a place which is underground or well-hidden. 兽穴；兽窝；巢穴

  • ...a fox's lair.

    狐狸窝

SYN nest, den, hole, burrow  
2 N-COUNT [usually with poss] 藏匿之所；隐避之处；（尤指）独居处 Someone's lair is the particular room or hiding place that they go to, especially when they want to get away from other people. 藏匿之所；隐避之处；（尤指）独居处 [informal]

  • Green recounts how he once went to see Bremner in his lair.

    格林讲述他有一次去布雷姆纳隐居之处看他的经历。

  • The village was once a pirates' lair.

    这个村子曾一度是海盗藏匿之处。

SYN hide-out [informal] , retreat, refuge, den [US]
