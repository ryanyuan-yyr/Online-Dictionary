sizzle
sɪzəl ˈsɪzəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sizzles, present participle sizzling, past tense , past participle sizzled
VERB (热油、脂肪等)发出咝咝声 If something such as hot oil or fat sizzles, it makes hissing sounds. (热油、脂肪等)发出咝咝声

  • The sausages and burgers sizzled on the barbecue. [VERB]

    香肠和牛肉饼在烤架上咝咝作响。

  • ...a frying pan of sizzling oil. [VERB-ing]

    油烧得咝咝作响的平底锅

SYN hiss, spit, crackle, sputter
