ululate
juːljʊleɪt ʌl- ˈjuljʊˌleɪt ˈʌljʊˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ululates, present participle ululating, past tense , past participle ululated
VERB 号叫；大呼小叫 If someone ululates, they make quickly repeated loud sounds, often to express sorrow or happiness. 号叫；大呼小叫 [literary]

  • They ululated like Red Indians.

    他们像印第安人一样大呼小叫。

  • He let out this long ululating moan. [VERB-ing]

    他发出这种痛苦的长嚎。

SYN howl, cry, keen, weep
