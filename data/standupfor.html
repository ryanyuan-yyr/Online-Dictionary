stand up for
PHRASAL VERB 坚决维护;捍卫 If you stand up for someone or something, you defend them and make your feelings or opinions very clear. 坚决维护;捍卫 [approval]

  • They stood up for what they believed to be right. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他们坚决捍卫自己认为是对的事。

  • Don't be afraid to stand up for yourself. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    要敢于维护自己的权利。
