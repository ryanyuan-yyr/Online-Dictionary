speed bump
spiːd bʌmp
Word forms: speed bumps
1 N-COUNT 制约(人或事)发展的障碍A speed bump is something that stops a person or thing from progressing. 制约(人或事)发展的障碍

  • It was little more than a speed bump – a minor distraction during my day.

    那只不过是个小挫折—我一天中无足轻重的小烦恼。

2 N-COUNT (横置马路上用于防止车辆行驶过快的)路面减速装置A speed bump is a raised part in a road that is designed to make the traffic travel more slowly. (横置马路上用于防止车辆行驶过快的)路面减速装置
