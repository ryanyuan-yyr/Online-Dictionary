workmanlike
wɜːʳkmənlaɪk ˈwɜːkmənlɪ ˈwɜrkmənˌlaɪk
ADJ-GRADED 出自匠人之手的；技艺纯熟但无创意的 If you describe something as workmanlike, you mean that it has been done quite well and sensibly, but not in a particularly imaginative or original way. 出自匠人之手的；技艺纯熟但无创意的

  • Really it's a workmanlike conference rather than a dramatic one.

    会议的确开得有板有眼，但谈不上激动人心。

  • The script was workmanlike at best.

    这个剧本充其量就是匠人之作。

SYN efficient, professional, skilled, expert
