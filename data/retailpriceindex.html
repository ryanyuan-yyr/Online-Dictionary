retail price index
N-PROPER 零售价格指数 The retail price index is a list of the prices of typical goods which shows how much the cost of living changes from one month to the next. 零售价格指数 [British, business]

  • The retail price index for September is expected to show inflation edging up to about 10.8 per cent.

    预计9月份的零售价格指数将显示通货膨胀率已逼近10.8％。
