apparatus
æpəreɪtəs -ræt- ˌæpəˈreɪtəs ˈæpəˌreɪtəs ˌæpəˈrætəs ˌæpəˈreɪtəs
Word forms: plural apparatuses
1 N-VAR 机构；组织 The apparatus of an organization or system is its structure and method of operation. 机构；组织

  • For many years, the country had been buried under the apparatus of the regime.

    多年来，这个国家一直处于该政权机构的高压统治下。

  • ...a massive bureaucratic apparatus.

    庞大的官僚机构

SYN organization, system, network, structure  
2 N-VAR 器械;器具;仪器;设备 Apparatus is the equipment, such as tools and machines, which is used to do a particular job or activity. 器械;器具;仪器;设备

  • One of the boys had to be rescued by firemen wearing breathing apparatus.

    其中一个男孩须由带着呼吸装置的消防员才能救出。

SYN equipment, machine, tackle, gear
