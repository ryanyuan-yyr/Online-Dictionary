returnee
rɪtɜːʳniː rɪˈtɜːˌniː rɪˌtɜrˈni
Word forms: plural returnees
N-COUNT [usually plural] 回国人员;归国者 A returnee is a person who returns to the country where they were born, usually after they have been away for a long time. 回国人员;归国者

  • The number of returnees could go as high as half a million.

    回国人员的数量可能高达50万。
