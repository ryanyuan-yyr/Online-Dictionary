paragon
pærəgɒn ˈpærəˌgɑn ˈpærəgən
Word forms: plural paragons
N-COUNT 杰出典范；尽善尽美的人；完人 If you refer to someone as a paragon, you mean that they are perfect or have a lot of a good quality. 杰出典范；尽善尽美的人；完人

  • We don't expect candidates to be paragons of virtue. [+ of]

    我们不指望候选人在道德上尽善尽美。

  • ...a paragon of neatness, efficiency and reliability.

    整洁、高效和可靠的典范

  • He was not a paragon. He would never be perfect.

    他不是完人，永远不可能十全十美。

SYN model, standard, pattern, ideal
