chain-smoke
tʃeɪnˌsmoʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense chain-smokes, present participle chain-smoking, past tense , past participle chain-smoked
VERB 一支接一支地抽(烟) Someone who chain-smokes smokes cigarettes or cigars continuously. 一支接一支地抽(烟) [Also VERB n]

  • Melissa had chain-smoked all evening while she waited for a phone call from Tom. [VERB]

    整个晚上，梅利莎都在等汤姆的电话，其间一支接一支地抽着烟。
