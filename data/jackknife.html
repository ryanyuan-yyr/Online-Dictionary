jack-knife
(also jackknife)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense jack-knifes, present participle jack-knifing, past tense , past participle jack-knifed
VERB （铰接卡车失控时）弯成V字形，弯折 If a truck that is in two parts jack-knifes, the back part swings around at a sharp angle to the front part in an uncontrolled way as the truck is moving. （铰接卡车失控时）弯成V字形，弯折

  • His vehicle jack-knifed, and crashed across all three lanes of the opposite carriageway. [VERB]

    他的车弯成V字形，撞进了反方向车道，车身挡住了所有的3个车道。
