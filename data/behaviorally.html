behaviour
bɪheɪvjəʳ biˈheɪvjər bɪˈheɪvjər
Word forms: plural behaviours
in AM, use 美国英语用 behavior
1 N-VAR 行为；举止 People's or animals' behaviour is the way that they behave. You can refer to a typical and repeated way of behaving as a behaviour . 行为；举止

  • Make sure that good behaviour is rewarded.

    确保懿行得到嘉奖。

  • ...human sexual behaviour.

    人类性行为

  • These eating patterns are a learned behavior.

    这些进食模式是习得性行为。

2 N-UNCOUNT [with poss] （科学领域中的）运动方式，活动方式，性能，特点 In science, the behaviour of something is the way that it behaves. （科学领域中的）运动方式，活动方式，性能，特点

  • It will be many years before anyone can predict a hurricane's behavior with much accuracy.

    要能够相当准确地预测出飓风的特性尚需多年。

3 PHRASE 极力表现；尽量做到举止得体 If someone is on their best behaviour, they are trying very hard to behave well. 极力表现；尽量做到举止得体
