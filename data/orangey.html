orangey
ɒrɪndʒi ˈɒrɪndʒɪ ɔːr-
ADJ-GRADED 略带橙色的；浅橙色的 Orangey means slightly orange in colour. 略带橙色的；浅橙色的

  • He used to have orangey coloured hair.

    以前他的头发是浅橙色的。

Orangey is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • The hall is decorated in bright orangey-red with black and gold woodwork.

    整个大厅用鲜艳的橘红色装饰，木建部分漆成黑色和金色。
