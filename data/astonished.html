astonished
əstɒnɪʃt əˈstɒnɪʃt
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ to-infinitive, ADJ that] 感到惊讶的;十分惊奇的 If you are astonished by something, you are very surprised about it. 感到惊讶的;十分惊奇的

  • I was astonished by his stupidity.

    他的愚笨让我大跌眼镜。

  • They were astonished to find the driver was a six-year-old boy.

    令他们吃惊的是，开车的居然是个6岁的小男孩。

SYN amazed, surprised, staggered, bewildered
