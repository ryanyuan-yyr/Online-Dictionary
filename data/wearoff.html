wear off
PHRASAL VERB （感觉等）逐渐消失，消逝 If a sensation or feeling wears off, it disappears slowly until it no longer exists or has any effect. （感觉等）逐渐消失，消逝

  • For many the philosophy was merely a fashion, and the novelty soon wore off. [VERB PREP.]

    对许多人来说哲学只是一种时髦罢了，不久就没新鲜感了。

  • Now that the initial shock was wearing off, he was in considerable pain. [VERB PREP.]

    最初的震惊渐渐过去之后，他感到疼痛难忍。

  • The contraceptive effect wears off in two days. [VERB PREP.]

    避孕效果两天就会消失。
