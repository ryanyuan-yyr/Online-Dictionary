hilt
hɪlt hɪlt
Word forms: plural hilts
1 N-COUNT （刀、剑、匕首等的）柄，把 The hilt of a sword, dagger, or knife is its handle. （刀、剑、匕首等的）柄，把

  • ...the hilt of the small, sharp knife.

    一把锋利小刀的刀柄

SYN handle, grip, haft, handgrip  
2 PHRASE 最大限度地;完全地;彻底地 To the hilt and up to the hilt mean to the maximum extent possible or as fully as possible. 最大限度地;完全地;彻底地 [informal, emphasis]

  • The men who wield the power are certainly backing him to the hilt.

    掌权者肯定在全力支持他。

  • James was overdrawn and mortgaged to the hilt.

    詹姆斯已经透支到最大额度并把能抵押的都抵押上了。

  • She was in this right up to the hilt.

    她已经完全卷入了此事。

SYN fully, completely, totally, entirely
