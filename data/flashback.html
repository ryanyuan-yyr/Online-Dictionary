flashback
flæʃbæk ˈflæʃˌbæk
Word forms: plural flashbacks
1 N-COUNT (电影、小说、戏剧中的)闪回，倒叙 In a film, novel, or play, a flashback is a scene that returns to events in the past. (电影、小说、戏剧中的)闪回，倒叙

  • There is even a flashback to the murder itself. [+ to]

    甚至有一段关于谋杀本身的闪回。

2 N-COUNT (往事的)突然重现，闪回，再现 If you have a flashback to a past experience, you have a sudden and very clear memory of it. (往事的)突然重现，闪回，再现

  • He has recurring flashbacks to the night his friends died. [+ to]

    他反复想起朋友们死去的那个夜晚的情形。
