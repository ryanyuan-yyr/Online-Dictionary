irritating
ɪrɪteɪtɪŋ ˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 恼人的;使人生气的 Something that is irritating keeps annoying you. 恼人的;使人生气的

  • They also have the irritating habit of interrupting.

    他们还有打岔的烦人习惯。

SYN annoying, trying, provoking, infuriating  
irritatingly

  • They can be irritatingly indecisive at times.

    他们有时优柔寡断得令人气恼。

2 ADJ-GRADED 有刺激性的;引起瘙痒的;引发疼痛的 An irritating substance can cause your body to itch or become sore. 有刺激性的;引起瘙痒的;引发疼痛的

  • In heavy concentrations, ozone is irritating to the eyes, nose and throat. [+ to]

    高浓度的臭氧会对眼睛、鼻子和咽喉有刺激作用。
