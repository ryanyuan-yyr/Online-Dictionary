arousal
əraʊzəl
1 N-UNCOUNT （性欲的）激起，激发 Arousal is the state of being sexually excited. （性欲的）激起，激发

  • ...sexual arousal.

    性欲的激起

  • Use this technique to control your level of arousal.

    用这种方法来控制性兴奋程度。

2 N-UNCOUNT 唤起;激起 Arousal is a state in which you feel excited or very alert, for example as a result of fear, stress, or anger. 唤起;激起

  • Thinking angry thoughts can provoke strong physiological arousal.

    想着生气的事情能激起强烈的生理反应。

SYN stimulation, movement, response, reaction
