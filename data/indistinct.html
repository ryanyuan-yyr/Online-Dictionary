indistinct
ɪndɪstɪŋkt ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt
ADJ-GRADED (看、听)不清楚的;模糊的;难以清楚辨认的 Something that is indistinct is unclear and difficult to see, hear, or recognize. (看、听)不清楚的;模糊的;难以清楚辨认的

  • The lettering is fuzzy and indistinct.

    书写的文字模糊难认。

  • ...the indistinct murmur of voices.

    模糊不清的低语声

SYN unclear, confused, obscure, faint  
indistinctly

  • He speaks so indistinctly that many listeners haven't a clue what he is saying.

    他说话含混不清，以至很多听众一点都不知道他在说什么。
