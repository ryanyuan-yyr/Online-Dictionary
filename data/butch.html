butch
bʊtʃ bʊtʃ
1 ADJ-GRADED （女人举止或衣着）像男人的，男性化的 If you describe a woman as butch, you mean that she behaves or dresses in a masculine way. This use could cause offence. （女人举止或衣着）像男人的，男性化的 [informal]
2 ADJ-GRADED 男子气十足的 If you describe a man as butch, you mean that he behaves in an extremely masculine way. 男子气十足的 [informal]
