device
dɪvaɪs dɪˈvaɪs
Word forms: plural devices
1 N-COUNT 装置;设备;器具;仪器 A device is an object that has been invented for a particular purpose, for example for recording or measuring something. 装置;设备;器具;仪器

  • ...an electronic device that protects your vehicle 24 hours a day.

    24小时保护车辆的电子装置

  • ...a device that could measure minute quantities of matter.

    能够测量微量物质的设备

  • We believe that an explosive device had been left inside a container.

    我们认为有一个爆炸装置被留在了一只集装箱里。

SYN gadget, machine, tool, instrument  
2 N-COUNT 手段;策略;方法;技巧 A device is a method of achieving something. 手段;策略;方法;技巧

  • They claim that military spending is used as a device for managing the economy. [+ for]

    他们声称军费开支是用来作为调控经济的手段的。

  • ...the literary device of the metaphor.

    隐喻的文学手法

3 PHRASE 听任…自便;让…自行发展;不去管 If you leave someone to their own devices, you leave them alone to do as they wish. 听任…自便;让…自行发展;不去管

  • Left to his own devices, Osborn is a fluent — and often original — guitarist.

    任其自由发挥时，奥斯本是一个弹奏流畅、常常富有创意的吉他手。
