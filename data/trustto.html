trust to
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 仰仗，依靠(运气、直觉) If you trust to luck or instinct, you hope that it will enable you to achieve what you are trying to do, because you have nothing else to help you. 仰仗，依靠(运气、直觉)

  • I set off for the valley, trusting to luck. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我动身去山谷了，一切全凭运气。

  • Gardiner is simply trusting to instinct and experience. [VERB PREP. noun]

    加德纳完全凭直觉与经验。
