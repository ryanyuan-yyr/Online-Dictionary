unconvincing
ʌnkənvɪnsɪŋ ˌʌnkənˈvɪnsɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED （论点、解释等）难以令人信服的，没有说服力的 If you describe something such as an argument or explanation as unconvincing, you find it difficult to believe because it does not seem real. （论点、解释等）难以令人信服的，没有说服力的

  • Mr Patel phoned the University for an explanation, and he was given the usual unconvincing excuses.

    帕特尔先生致电大学要求对方作出解释，但只得到了一些无关痛痒的惯用托词。

  • To many readers it sounded unconvincing.

    在很多读者看来，这很难令人信服。

SYN implausible, unlikely, lame, suspect  
unconvincingly

  • 'It's not that I don't believe you, Meg,' Jack said, unconvincingly.

    “并不是我不相信你，梅格。”杰克口是心非地说道。

2 ADJ-GRADED （故事或人物）不可信的，不真实的 If you describe a story or a character in a story as unconvincing, you think they do not seem likely or real. （故事或人物）不可信的，不真实的

  • ...an unconvincing love story.

    不太可信的爱情故事
