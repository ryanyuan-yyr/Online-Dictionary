penance
penəns ˈpɛnəns
Word forms: plural penances
N-VAR 补赎；悔罪；忏悔 If you do penance for something wrong that you have done, you do something that you find unpleasant to show that you are sorry. 补赎；悔罪；忏悔

  • ...a time of fasting, penance and pilgrimage.

    斋戒、补赎和朝圣的时代

  • The Koran recommends fasting as a penance before pilgrimages.

    《古兰经》劝告人们在朝圣前斋戒，以作为一种补赎。

SYN atonement, punishment, penalty, reparation
