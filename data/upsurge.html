upsurge
ʌpsɜːʳdʒ ʌpˈsɜrdʒ ˈʌpˌsɜrdʒ
N-SING 飙升；急剧增长 If there is an upsurge in something, there is a sudden, large increase in it. 飙升；急剧增长 [formal]

  • ...the upsurge in oil prices. [+ in]

    油价的暴涨

  • Saudi bankers say there's been an upsurge of business confidence since the end of the war. [+ of]

    沙特的银行家说战后工商业界信心猛增。

SYN increase, rise, growth, boost
