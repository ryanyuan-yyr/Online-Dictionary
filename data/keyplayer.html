key player
kiː ˈpleɪə
Word forms: plural key players
N-COUNT 关键人物；关键因素 The key players in a particular organization, event, or situation are the most important people or things involved in it. 关键人物；关键因素

  • The former chairman was a key player in the deals that pushed the bank to the top. [+ in]
