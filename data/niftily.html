nifty
nɪfti ˈnɪfti
Word forms: comparative niftier, superlative niftiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 整洁的;漂亮的;极好的;绝妙的 If you describe something as nifty, you think it is neat and pleasing or cleverly done. 整洁的;漂亮的;极好的;绝妙的 [informal, approval]

  • Bridgeport was a pretty nifty place.

    布里奇波特是个十分漂亮的地方。

  • It was a nifty arrangement, a perfect partnership.

    这是绝妙的安排，完美的搭档。
