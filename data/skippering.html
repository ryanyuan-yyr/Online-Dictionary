skipper
skɪpəʳ ˈskɪpər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense skippers, present participle skippering, past tense , past participle skippered
1 N-COUNT; N-VOC 船长 You can use skipper to refer to the captain of a ship or boat. 船长

  • ...the skipper of an English fishing boat. [+ of]

    一艘英国渔船的船长

  • Gunfire, skipper!

    开火，船长！

SYN captain, master  
2 N-COUNT (体育运动队的)队长 You can use skipper to refer to the captain of a sports team. (体育运动队的)队长

  • The England skipper is confident.

    英格兰队的队长信心十足。

3 VERB 当…的队长(或船长) To skipper a team or a boat means to be the captain of it. 当…的队长(或船长)

  • He skippered the second Rugby XV. [VERB noun]

    他担任了橄榄球 15 人联队的第二任队长。

  • The yacht was skippered by Pierre Mas. [VERB noun]

    这艘游艇的船长是皮埃尔·马斯。
