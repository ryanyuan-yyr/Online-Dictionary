vaccine
væksiːn væksiːn vækˈsin ˈvæksin
Word forms: plural vaccines
N-MASS 疫苗 A vaccine is a substance containing a harmless form of the germs that cause a particular disease. It is given to people, usually by injection, to prevent them getting that disease. 疫苗

  • Anti-malarial vaccines are now undergoing trials.

    抗疟疾的疫苗正在试验阶段。

  • Seven million doses of vaccine are annually given to British children.

    英国孩子每年要接种7百万剂疫苗。

  • ...the rabies vaccine.

    狂犬病疫苗

SYN inoculation, injection, immunization
