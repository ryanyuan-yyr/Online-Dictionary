squib
skwɪb skwɪb
Word forms: plural squibs
PHRASE 未取得预期效果的事;失败;落空 You can describe something such as an event or a performance as a damp squib when it is expected to be interesting, exciting, or impressive, but fails to be any of these things. 未取得预期效果的事;失败;落空 [British]

  • The all-party meeting was a damp squib.

    全党大会没有取得预期效果。
