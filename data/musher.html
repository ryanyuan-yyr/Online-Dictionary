mush
mʌʃ mʊʃ mʌʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense mushes, present participle mushing, past tense , past participle mushed
1 N-VAR [oft a NOUN] 烂糊状东西;软乎乎的东西 Mush is a thick, soft paste. 烂糊状东西;软乎乎的东西

  • The brown mush in the fridge is some veg soup left over.

    冰箱里的棕色糊状东西是一些剩的蔬菜汤。

2 N-UNCOUNT 感伤的事物;多愁善感的作品 If you describe something such as a film or book as mush, you mean that it is very sentimental. 感伤的事物;多愁善感的作品 [disapproval]

  • Whenever famous actresses get together to make a 'woman's film' you can bet on an overload of sentimental mush.

    无论什么时候，只要知名女演员们聚在一起，去演绎一部“女性题材电影”，那毫无疑问里面准是一堆多愁善感的故事。

3 VERB 把…碾碎;把…弄烂 If you mush something, you make it into a mush. 把…碾碎;把…弄烂

  • ...mushed-up potato and cauliflower. [V-ed up]

    捣成泥的土豆和花菜
