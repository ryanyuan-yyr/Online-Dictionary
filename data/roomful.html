roomful
ruːmfʊl ˈruːmfʊl ˈrumˌfʊl
Word forms: plural roomfuls
N-COUNT 一屋子（人或物） A roomful of things or people is a room that is full of them. You can also refer to the amount or number of things or people that a room can contain as a roomful . 一屋子（人或物）

  • It was like a teacher disciplining a roomful of second-year pupils. [+ of]

    那情景就像一个老师正在管教一屋子的二年级小学生。

  • I accumulated a roomful of documents and tape recordings.

    我搜集了满屋子的文件和磁带录音。
