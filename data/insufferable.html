insufferable
ɪnsʌfrəbəl ɪnˈsʌfərəbəl
ADJ 不能容忍的;难以忍受的 If you say that someone or something is insufferable, you are emphasizing that they are very unpleasant or annoying. 不能容忍的;难以忍受的 [formal, emphasis]

  • He was an insufferable bore.

    他很讨厌，真令人受不了。

SYN unbearable, impossible, intolerable, dreadful  
insufferably

  • His letters are insufferably dull.

    他的信乏味不堪。

  • He found most of them insufferably arrogant.

    他发现他们当中大部分人都傲慢得让人难以忍受。
