barbarism
bɑːʳbərɪzəm ˈbɑrbəˌrɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 野蛮（行为）；凶残（行为） If you refer to someone's behaviour as barbarism, you strongly disapprove of it because you think that it is extremely cruel or uncivilized. 野蛮（行为）；凶残（行为） [disapproval]

  • We do not ask for the death penalty: barbarism must not be met with barbarism.

    我们不赞成死刑：不能以暴制暴。

SYN cruelty, outrage, atrocity, brutality
