chummy
tʃʌmi ˈtʃʌmi
Word forms: comparative chummier, superlative chummiest
ADJ-GRADED (人、社交活动)令人愉快的，友好的 If people or social events are chummy, they are pleasant and friendly. (人、社交活动)令人愉快的，友好的 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • Following the performances there were chummy gatherings in the drawing room.

    表演过后会在客厅举行气氛融洽的聚会。

SYN friendly, close, thick [informal] , pally [informal]
