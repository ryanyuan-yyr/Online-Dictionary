disreputable
dɪsrepjʊtəbəl dɪsˈrɛpjutəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 名声不好的;不名誉的;不光彩的 If you say that someone or something is disreputable, you are critical of them because they are not respectable or cannot be trusted. 名声不好的;不名誉的;不光彩的 [disapproval]

  • He was found to have been enjoying the company of disreputable women.

    他被人发现喜欢与那些不三不四的女人混在一起。

  • ...the noisiest and most disreputable bars.

    最为喧哗、名声最差的酒吧

SYN discreditable, mean, low, base
