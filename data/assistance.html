assistance
əsɪstəns əˈsɪstəns
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 帮助;协助 If you give someone assistance, you help them do a job or task by doing part of the work for them. 帮助;协助

  • Since 1976 he has been operating the shop with the assistance of volunteers.

    从1976年开始，他就在志愿者的帮助下经营这个店。

  • She can still come downstairs with assistance but she's very weak.

    在有人搀扶的情况下她还是能够下楼的，但是很虚弱。

SYN help, backing, service, support  
2 N-UNCOUNT （以提供信息或意见的方式给予的）帮助，援助 If you give someone assistance, you give them information or advice. （以提供信息或意见的方式给予的）帮助，援助

  • Any assistance you could give the police will be greatly appreciated.

    您提供的任何帮助警方都将万分感谢。

  • Employees are being offered assistance in finding new jobs.

    正在帮助雇员另觅新职。

3 N-UNCOUNT 资助；补助 If someone gives a person or country assistance, they help them by giving them money. 资助；补助

  • ...a viable programme of economic assistance.

    切实可行的经济援助方案

  • We shall offer you assistance with legal expenses up to $5,000.

    我们将为您提供最高5,000美元的法律援助金。

4 N-UNCOUNT 辅助;协助 If something is done with the assistance of a particular thing, that thing is helpful or necessary for doing it. 辅助;协助

  • The translations were carried out with the assistance of a medical dictionary.

    这些翻译是借助一本医学词典完成的。

5 PHRASE 有帮助;有用处 Someone or something that is of assistance to you is helpful or useful to you. 有帮助;有用处

  • He was of great assistance to me in researches for my books.

    在我为写书所作的研究中，他提供了很大的帮助。

  • Can I be of any assistance?

    我能帮什么忙吗？

6 PHRASE （采取行动）帮助，援助 If you come to someone's assistance, you take action to help them. （采取行动）帮助，援助

  • They are appealing to the world community to come to Jordan's assistance.

    他们正在呼吁国际社会向约旦伸出援手。

  • Ben suffered a heart attack and Helen rushed to his assistance.

    本心脏病犯了，海伦急忙赶过来帮忙。
