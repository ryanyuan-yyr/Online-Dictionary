segregated
segrɪgeɪtɪd ˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd ˈsɛgrəˌgeɪtɪd
ADJ (因种族、性别、宗教不同而)隔离的，分开的 Segregated buildings or areas are kept for the use of one group of people who are the same race, sex, or religion, and no other group is allowed to use them. (因种族、性别、宗教不同而)隔离的，分开的

  • ...racially segregated schools.

    实行种族隔离制度的学校

  • John grew up in Baltimore when that city was segregated.

    约翰在巴尔的摩长大，当时该市还在实行种族隔离制度。

SYN divided, separate, set apart, isolated
