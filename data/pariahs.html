pariah
pəraɪə pəˈraɪə ˈpæriə pəˈraɪə
Word forms: plural pariahs
N-COUNT 社会弃儿 If you describe someone as a pariah, you mean that other people dislike them so much that they refuse to associate with them. 社会弃儿 [disapproval]

  • His landlady had treated him like a dangerous criminal, a pariah.

    房东太太对待他就像对待危险的罪犯、对待社会弃儿一样。

SYN outcast, exile, outlaw, undesirable
