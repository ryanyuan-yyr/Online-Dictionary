deficient
dɪfɪʃənt diˈfɪʃənt dɪˈfɪʃənt
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 缺乏的;缺少的;不足的 If someone or something is deficient in a particular thing, they do not have the full amount of it that they need in order to function normally or work properly. 缺乏的;缺少的;不足的 [formal]

  • ...a diet deficient in vitamin B. [+ in]

    缺乏维生素 B 的饮食

Deficient is also a combining form.

  • Vegetarians can become iron-deficient.

    素食者可能会缺铁。

2 ADJ-GRADED 有缺点的;有缺陷的;不完善的 Someone or something that is deficient is not good enough for a particular purpose. 有缺点的;有缺陷的;不完善的 [formal]

  • ...deficient landing systems.

    存在缺陷的降落系统

SYN unsatisfactory, poor, inadequate, weak
