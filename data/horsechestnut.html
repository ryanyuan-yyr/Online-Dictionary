horse chestnut
(also horse-chestnut)Word forms: plural horse chestnuts
1 N-COUNT 七叶树 A horse chestnut is a large tree which has leaves with several pointed parts and shiny reddish-brown nuts called conkers that grow in cases with points on them. 七叶树
2 N-COUNT 七叶树果 Horse chestnuts are the nuts of a horse chestnut tree. They are more commonly called conkers . 七叶树果
