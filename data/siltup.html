silt up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)(河流、湖泊)淤塞 If a river or lake silts up or something silts it up, it becomes blocked with silt. (使)(河流、湖泊)淤塞

  • Without huge investment the reservoirs will silt up. [VERB PREP.]

    如果没有巨额投资的话，水库肯定会淤塞。

  • The soil washed from the hills is silting up the hydroelectric dams. [VERB PREP. noun]

    从山上冲刷下来的泥土就要让水电大坝淤塞了。

SYN clog up, block up, choke up, obstruct
