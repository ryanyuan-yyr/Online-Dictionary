hammer away
1 PHRASAL VERB 不懈地致力（于）;埋头（于） If you hammer away at a task or activity, you work at it constantly and with great energy. 不懈地致力（于）;埋头（于）

  • Palmer kept hammering away at his report. [VERB PREP. + at]

    帕尔默一直在埋头写报告。

2 PHRASAL VERB （尤指因不赞成而）反复谈论（某观点或话题） If you hammer away at an idea or subject, you keep talking about it, especially because you disapprove of it. （尤指因不赞成而）反复谈论（某观点或话题）

  • They also hammered away at Labor's plans to raise taxes. [VERB PREP. + at]

    他们还不断谈到工党的增税计划。
