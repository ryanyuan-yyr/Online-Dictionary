court of law kɔːt əv lɔː
Word forms: plural courts of law
N-COUNT (尤指在审判中可以提供证据的)法庭,法院 When you refer to a court of law, you are referring to a legal court, especially when talking about the evidence that might be given in a trial. (尤指在审判中可以提供证据的)法庭,法院

  • We have a witness who would swear to it in a court of law.

    我们有一位证人愿意在法庭上宣誓作证。
