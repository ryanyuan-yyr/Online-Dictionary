flipping
flɪpɪŋ
ADV [ADVERB adjective] (尤用于恼火时加强语气)可恶的，该死的 Some people use flipping to emphasize what they are saying, especially when they are annoyed. (尤用于恼火时加强语气)可恶的，该死的 [British, informal, spoken, emphasis]

  • This is such a flipping horrible picture.

    这张照片真是难看得要死。

Flipping is also an adjective.

  • I even washed the flipping bed sheets yesterday.

    我昨天甚至把那些可恶的床单给洗了。
