homeland

Word forms: plural homelands
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 祖国 Your homeland is your native country. 祖国 [mainly written]

  • Many are planning to return to their homeland.

    很多人正计划回到自己的祖国去。

SYN native land, birthplace, motherland, fatherland  
2 N-COUNT “黑人家园”（指过去南非种族隔离制度下的黑人有一定自治权的地区） The homelands were regions within South Africa in which black South Africans had a limited form of self-government. “黑人家园”（指过去南非种族隔离制度下的黑人有一定自治权的地区）
