bonnet
bɒnɪt ˈbɑnɪt
Word forms: plural bonnets
1 N-COUNT （汽车的）引擎盖 The bonnet of a car is the metal cover over the engine at the front. （汽车的）引擎盖 [British]

  • When I eventually stopped and lifted the bonnet, the noise seemed to be coming from the alternator.

    当我最终停下车打开引擎盖后，发现噪声似乎是从交流发电机中传出来的。

in AM, use 美国英语用 hood
2 N-COUNT （用带子系于颏下的）童帽，旧时女帽 A bonnet is a hat with ribbons that are tied under the chin. Bonnets are now worn by babies. In the past, they were also worn by women. （用带子系于颏下的）童帽，旧时女帽
