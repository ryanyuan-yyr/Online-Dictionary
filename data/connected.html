connected
kənektɪd kəˈnɛktɪd
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 有关的;有联系的 If one thing is connected with another, there is a link or relationship between them. 有关的;有联系的

  • Have you ever had any skin problems connected with exposure to the sun?. [+ with]

    你曾因日晒出现过皮肤问题吗？

  • The dispute is not directly connected to the negotiations. [+ to]

    这一争端与谈判没有直接联系。

  • She was born at Ambala, India, her family being closely connected with the Indian army.

    她出生在印度的安巴拉，她们家族与印度军队关系甚密。

SYN linked, united, joined, coupled
