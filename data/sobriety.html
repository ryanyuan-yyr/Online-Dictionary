sobriety
səbraɪɪti səˈbraɪəti soʊˈbraɪəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 清醒(指未喝醉) Sobriety is the state of being sober rather than drunk. 清醒(指未喝醉) [formal]
2 N-UNCOUNT 严肃;持重 Sobriety is serious and thoughtful behaviour. 严肃;持重 [formal]

  • ...the values society depends upon, such as honesty, sobriety and trust.

    社会赖以维系下去的价值观，例如诚实、持重和信任

SYN seriousness, gravity, steadiness, restraint
