bumpy
bʌmpi ˈbʌmpi
Word forms: comparative bumpier, superlative bumpiest
1 ADJ-GRADED （道路）崎岖不平的 A bumpy road or path has a lot of bumps on it. （道路）崎岖不平的

  • ...bumpy cobbled streets.

    铺了鹅卵石的凹凸不平的街道

SYN uneven, rough, pitted, irregular  
2 ADJ-GRADED （旅途）颠簸的 A bumpy journey is uncomfortable and rough, usually because you are travelling over an uneven surface. （旅途）颠簸的

  • ...a hot and bumpy ride across the desert.

    一次炎热而又颠簸的沙漠之行

  • We had a bumpy flight over the centre of Panama.

    我们的飞机摇摇晃晃地飞过巴拿马中部地区。

SYN jolting, jarring, bouncy, choppy
