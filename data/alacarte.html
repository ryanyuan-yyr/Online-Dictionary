à la carte ɑːlɑː kɑːʳt ɑː lɑː ˈkɑːt æ lə a la kart
ADJ [ADJ n] 每道菜分别计价的; 按照菜单点菜的 An à la carte menu in a restaurant offers you a choice of individually priced dishes for each course. 每道菜分别计价的; 按照菜单点菜的

  • You could choose as much or as little as you wanted from an à la carte menu.

    你可以从单点菜单上点菜，点多点少随你意。

à la carte is also an adverb.

  • A set meal is 35 dollars, or you can eat à la carte.

    套餐的价格是35美元，或者你可以单独点菜吃。
