ungovernable
ʌngʌvəʳnəbəl ʌnˈgʌvərnəbəl
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] （国家或地区）难治理的，难控制的 If you describe a country or region as ungovernable, you mean that it seems impossible to control or govern it effectively, for example because of violence or conflict among the population. （国家或地区）难治理的，难控制的

  • Beset by ethnic strife, the province remains ungovernable.

    该省因受种族冲突困扰而失控。

  • The country has become virtually ungovernable.

    这个国家已经基本上失控了。

SYN unruly, rebellious, unmanageable, refractory  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （感情）难以抑制的 If you describe feelings as ungovernable, you mean that they are so strong that they cannot be controlled. （感情）难以抑制的

  • He was filled with an ungovernable rage.

    他怒不可遏。

  • ...ungovernable passions.

    无法控制的激情

SYN uncontrollable, wild, unrestrainable
