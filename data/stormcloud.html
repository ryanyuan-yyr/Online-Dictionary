storm cloud
(also stormcloud)Word forms: plural storm clouds
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 暴风云 Storm clouds are the dark clouds which are seen before a storm. 暴风云
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 不祥的预兆;凶兆 You can use storm clouds to refer to a sign that something very unpleasant is going to happen. 不祥的预兆;凶兆 [formal]

  • Over the past three weeks, the storm clouds have gathered again over the government.

    在过去 3 周里，政府再次为不祥的阴云所笼罩。
