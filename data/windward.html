windward
wɪndwəʳd ˈwɪndwərd ˈwɪndərd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (尤指船侧)迎风的，向风的，顶风的 Windward is used to describe the side of something, especially a ship, which is facing the wind. (尤指船侧)迎风的，向风的，顶风的

  • ...the windward side of the quarterdeck.

    上层后甲板区的迎风面

  • Gardens on the windward side of a hill receive more rain than those on the lee side.

    山坡向风面的花园比背风面的雨水多。

2 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb] (船等)顶风，迎风 If a ship sails to windward, it sails towards the place from which the wind is blowing. (船等)顶风，迎风 [technical]
