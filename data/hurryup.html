hurry up
1 PHRASAL VERB 赶紧;赶快 If you tell someone to hurry up, you are telling them do something more quickly than they were doing. 赶紧;赶快 [Also V it P]

  • Franklin told Howe to hurry up and take his bath; otherwise, they'd miss their train. [VERB PREP.]

    富兰克林告诉豪赶快去洗澡;否则他们会赶不上火车。

  • Hurry up with that coffee, will you. [VERB PREP. + with]

    请你快点把咖啡端上来。

2 PHRASAL VERB 使加快;使提早 If you hurry something up or hurry it along, you make it happen faster or sooner than it would otherwise have done. 使加快;使提早

  • ...if you want to hurry up the application process. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果想要加快申请

  • Petter saw no reason to hurry the divorce along. [VERB noun PREP.]

    彼得认为没有必要加速离婚进程。
