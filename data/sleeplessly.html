sleepless
sliːpləs ˈsliplɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不睡觉的;不眠的 A sleepless night is one during which you do not sleep. 不睡觉的;不眠的

  • I have sleepless nights worrying about her.

    我好几个晚上都睡不着，一直担心她。

SYN wakeful, disturbed, restless, insomniac  
2 ADJ 睡不着的;失眠的 Someone who is sleepless is unable to sleep. 睡不着的;失眠的

  • A sleepless baby can seem to bring little reward.

    宝宝睡不着觉似乎没有任何好处。

sleeplessness

  • Sleeplessness is sometimes the side effect of certain medications.

    失眠有时是某些药物产生的副作用。
