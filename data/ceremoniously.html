ceremoniously
serɪmoʊniəsli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 极其正规地;隆重地 If someone does something ceremoniously, they do it in an extremely formal way. 极其正规地;隆重地 [written]

  • They ceremoniously cut a piece of ribbon, declaring the exhibition open.

    他们郑重其事地剪彩，宣布展览开幕。

  • He thanked her ceremoniously.

    他对她郑重道谢。
