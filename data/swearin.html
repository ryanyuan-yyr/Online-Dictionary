swear in
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 使宣誓就职 When someone is sworn in, they formally promise to fulfil the duties of a new job or appointment. 使宣誓就职

  • Soon after the New Year, the new Congress will come to Washington to be sworn in. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    新年后不久，新一届国会议员就将前往华盛顿宣誓就职。

SYN install, inaugurate, establish, adopt
