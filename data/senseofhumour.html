sense of humour sɛns əv ˈhjuːmə
in AM, use 美国英语用 sense of humor
N-SING 幽默感 Someone who has a sense of humour often finds things amusing, rather than being serious all the time. 幽默感

  • He had enormous charm and a great sense of humour.

    他极富魅力而且非常有幽默感。

  • ...someone with a very warped sense of humour.

    有着怪异幽默感的人
