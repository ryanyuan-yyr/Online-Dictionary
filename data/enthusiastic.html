ADJ-GRADED 非常感兴趣的;热心的;热情的 If you are enthusiastic about something, you show how much you like or enjoy it by the way that you behave and talk. 非常感兴趣的;热心的;热情的

  • Tom was very enthusiastic about the place. [+ about]

    汤姆对那个地方非常感兴趣。

    鲍勃·多尔似乎对提议的这项行动不太感兴趣。

  • He knew much about pictures and fine furniture, and was an enthusiastic gardener.

    他对电影和高档家具知之甚多，还是个园艺爱好者。
