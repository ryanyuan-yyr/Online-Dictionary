holocaust
hɒləkɔːst ˈhɑləˌkɑst ˈhoʊləˌkɔst ˈhɔləˌkɔst
Word forms: plural holocausts
1 N-VAR （尤指战争引起的）大屠杀，大毁灭，浩劫 A holocaust is an event in which there is a lot of destruction and many people are killed, especially one caused by war. （尤指战争引起的）大屠杀，大毁灭，浩劫

  • A nuclear holocaust seemed a very real possibility in the '50s.

    核灾难在20世纪50年代似乎真的非常可能发生。

SYN devastation, destruction, carnage, genocide  
2 N-SING （第二次世界大战中纳粹对犹太人进行的）大屠杀 The Holocaust is used to refer to the killing by the Nazis of millions of Jews during the Second World War. （第二次世界大战中纳粹对犹太人进行的）大屠杀

  • ...an Israeli-based fund for survivors of the Holocaust and their families.

    设在以色列的为大屠杀幸存者及其家人建立的基金

SYN genocide, massacre, carnage, mass murder
