no-win situation ˌnəʊˈwɪn ˌsɪtʃʊˈeɪʃən
Word forms: no-win situations
N-COUNT 只输无赢的情况If you are in a no-win situation, any action you take will fail to benefit you in any way. 只输无赢的情况

  • It was a no-win situation. Either she pretended she hated Ned and felt awful or admitted she loved him and felt even worse!

    这是一个只输无赢的情形。如果她假装讨厌内德，她会觉得痛苦；而如果她承认喜欢他，她会感觉更难受!
