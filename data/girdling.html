girdle
gɜːʳdəl ˈgɜrdəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense girdles, present participle girdling, past tense , past participle girdled
1 N-COUNT (女子的）紧身褡，束腹紧身衣 A girdle is a piece of women's underwear that fits tightly around the stomach and hips. (女子的）紧身褡，束腹紧身衣
SYN belt, band, fillet, sash  
2 VERB 围绕；环绕 If one thing girdles another, it surrounds it. 围绕；环绕 [literary]

  • Weather satellites have observed a ring of volcanic ash girdling the earth. [VERB noun]

    气象卫星观测到一个环绕地球的火山灰带。

  • The old town centre is girdled by a boulevard lined with trees. [be V-ed by/with n]

    一条林阴大道环绕古城的中心。

SYN surround, ring, bound, enclose
