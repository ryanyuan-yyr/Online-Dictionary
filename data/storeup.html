store up
PHRASAL VERB 储备;囤积 If you store something up, you keep it until you think that the time is right to use it. 储备;囤积 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Investors were storing up a lot of cash in anticipation of disaster. [VERB PREP. noun]

    投资者预料会出现灾难，正在储备大量现金。

  • I got all the emotion out of me which had been stored up. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我把心中压抑的所有情感都发泄了出来。
