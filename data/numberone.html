number one
Word forms: plural number ones
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 头号的;头等的;最重要的 Number one means better, more important, or more popular than anything else of its kind. 头号的;头等的;最重要的 [informal]

  • The economy is the number one issue by far.

    到目前为止，经济是头等大事。

  • By the way, I'm your number-one fan.

    顺便说一句，我是你的头号粉丝。

2 N-COUNT 排行榜冠军(指周销售量最高的流行歌曲CD、歌手或乐队组合) In popular music, the number one is the best selling CD in any one week, or the group or person who has made that CD. 排行榜冠军(指周销售量最高的流行歌曲CD、歌手或乐队组合) [informal]

  • Paula is the only artist to achieve four number ones from a debut album.

    葆拉是唯一的首张唱片4次荣登排行榜榜首的艺人。

3 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 只想到自己的利益;只顾自己 If you are looking out for number one, you are thinking of yourself rather than considering other people. 只想到自己的利益;只顾自己 [informal]

  • My priority is to look after number one — to create a lifestyle I am happy with.

    我首先要顾好自己，追求自己喜欢的生活方式。
