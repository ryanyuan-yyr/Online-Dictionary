compere
kɒmpeəʳ ˈkɒmpɛə
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense comperes, present participle compering, past tense , past participle compered
1 N-COUNT (广播、电视或现场演出的)主持人 A compere is the person who introduces the people taking part in a radio or television show or a live show. (广播、电视或现场演出的)主持人 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 emcee
2 VERB 主持(节目) The person who comperes a show introduces the people who take part in it. 主持(节目) [British]

  • Sarita Sabharwal compered the programme.

    萨里塔·萨巴瓦尔主持了这个节目。

  • They asked Paul to compere. [VERB]

    他们请保罗来主持。

in AM, use 美国英语用 emcee
