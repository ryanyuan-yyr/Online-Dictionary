pawn off
PHRASAL VERB 使 (自己不想要的人或物) 脱手If you pawn off something or someone that you do not want on another person, you persuade the person to accept them. 使 (自己不想要的人或物) 脱手 [disapproval]

  • The factories produce hugely subsidized rubbish they can't pawn off on anybody but the Russians.

    这些工厂生产享受了巨额补贴的垃圾产品，除了兜售给俄国人外，找不到其他买主。

  • Are you trying to pawn me off on somebody?

    你在试图把我硬塞给别人吗？
