immature
ɪmətjʊəʳ -tʊr ˌɪməˈtʊr ˌɪməˈtʃʊr ˌɪməˈtjʊr
1 ADJ 发育未全的;不成熟的 Something or someone that is immature is not yet completely grown or fully developed. 发育未全的;不成熟的

  • The birds were in immature plumage.

    这些鸟儿羽翅未全。

  • She is emotionally immature.

    她情感上不成熟。

SYN young, adolescent, undeveloped, green  
immaturity

  • In spite of some immaturity in the figure drawing and painting, it showed real imagination.

    尽管这幅画的人物勾勒和着色略显稚嫩,但它却表现出了真正的想象力。

2 ADJ-GRADED 冒失的;鲁莽的;冲动的 If you describe someone as immature, you are being critical of them because they do not behave in a sensible or responsible way. 冒失的;鲁莽的;冲动的 [disapproval]

  • ...grossly immature drivers who flout the rules of the road.

    无视交通法规、极端鲁莽的司机

  • She's just being childish and immature.

    她在耍小孩子脾气，真是任性。

SYN childish, juvenile, infantile, puerile  
immaturity

  • ...his immaturity and lack of social skills.

    他冒失鲁莽,缺乏社交技巧
