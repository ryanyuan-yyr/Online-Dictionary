northwest
nɔrθwɛst ˌnɔːθˈwɛst ˌnɔːˈwɛst ˌnɔrθˈwɛst ˌ nɔrˈwɛst
1 N-UNCOUNT [also the N] 西北;西北方 The north-west is the direction which is halfway between north and west. 西北;西北方

  • ...Ushant, five miles out to the north-west.

    西北方向5英里外的阿申特岛

2 N-SING 西北部;西北部地区 The north-west of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west. 西北部;西北部地区

  • Labour took its pre-election campaign to the North-West.

    工党在西北部地区展开了其选举前的竞选活动。

  • ...the extreme north-west of South America.

    南美洲的最西北部

3 ADV 向西北 If you go north-west, you travel towards the north-west. 向西北

  • Take the narrow lane going north-west parallel with the railway line.

    走和铁路线平行的那条西北向的窄巷。

4 ADV 在西北 Something that is north-west of a place is positioned to the north-west of it. 在西北

  • This was situated to the north-west of the town, a short walk from the railway station.

    这里地处镇子的西北方，离火车站仅有一小段步行距离。

5 ADJ-GRADED 在西北的;西北部的 The north-west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the north-west. 在西北的;西北部的

  • ...the North-West Regional Health Authority.

    西北地区卫生局

  • ...the north-west coast of the United States.

    美国的西北海岸

  • ...Sydney's north-west suburbs.

    悉尼的西北郊

6 ADJ (风)从西北方吹来的;西北风的 A north-west wind is a wind that blows from the north-west. (风)从西北方吹来的;西北风的

  • A brisk north-west wind swept across the region.

    寒冷干燥的西北风横扫了这个地区。
