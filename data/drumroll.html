drum roll
(also drumroll)Word forms: plural drum rolls
N-COUNT 连续击鼓声（常用于表明重要人物即将出场） A drum roll is a series of drumbeats that follow each other so quickly that they make a continuous sound. A drum roll is often used to show that someone important is arriving, or to introduce someone. 连续击鼓声（常用于表明重要人物即将出场）

  • A long drum roll introduced the trapeze artists.

    长长的一阵紧锣密鼓声中迎来了高空秋千表演者。
