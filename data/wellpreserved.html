well-preserved
ˌwɛlpriˈzɜrvd
(also well preserved)
1 ADJ-GRADED 不显老的;保养得好的 If you describe a middle-aged or old person as well-preserved, you mean that they look good for their age. 不显老的;保养得好的

  • Annie is a well-preserved 50-year-old.

    安妮50岁，保养得很好。

2 ADJ-GRADED (物件或建筑)保存良好的 A well-preserved object or building does not show any signs of its age. (物件或建筑)保存良好的

  • ...well-preserved fossils.

    保存得很好的化石

  • Although many of the stones have fallen out, the monument remains very well preserved.

    尽管许多石头都已经掉下来了，那个纪念碑仍保存良好。
