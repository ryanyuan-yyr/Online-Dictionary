construe
kənstruː kənˈstru
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense construes, present participle construing, past tense , past participle construed
VERB 理解;领会 If something is construed in a particular way, its nature or meaning is interpreted in that way. 理解;领会 [formal]

  • What may seem helpful behaviour to you can be construed as interference by others. [be VERB-ed + as]

    在你看来似乎是施助的行为在别人眼中可能就会被解读成一种干涉。

  • He may construe the approach as a hostile act. [VERB noun + as]

    他也许会将这种做法理解为敌视行为。

  • We are taught to construe these terms in a particular way. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    我们被教导要以特定的方式理解这些词语。

SYN interpret, take, read, explain
