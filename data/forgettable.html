forgettable
fəʳgetəbəl
ADJ-GRADED (因平淡无奇)容易被遗忘的，不被人记住的 If you describe something or someone as forgettable, you mean that they do not have any qualities that make them special, unusual, or interesting. (因平淡无奇)容易被遗忘的，不被人记住的

  • He has acted in three forgettable action films.

    他曾经在3部不起眼的动作片中出演角色。

  • He was average height, average build, with mousy hair and a forgettable face.

    他中等身高、中等身材、灰褐色头发，长着一张过目即忘的脸。
