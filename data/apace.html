apace
əpeɪs əˈpeɪs
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 高速地；迅速地 If something develops or continues apace, it is developing or continuing quickly. 高速地；迅速地 [formal]

  • Land reclamation continues apace.

    土地开垦继续快速进行。

  • The plan is proceeding apace, with another 13 superstores opened in the first half of this year.

    该计划进展迅速，在今年上半年又增开了13家大型超市。

SYN quickly, rapidly, swiftly, speedily
