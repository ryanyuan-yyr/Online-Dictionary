hot-desk
ˈhɒtˌdɛsk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hot-desks, present participle hot-desking, past tense , past participle hot-desked
VERB 轮用办公桌;无固定办公桌 If employees hot-desk, they are not assigned particular desks and work at any desk that is available. 轮用办公桌;无固定办公桌 [business]

  • Some ministers will have to hot-desk until more accommodation can be found. [VERB]

    一些部长将不得不轮用办公桌，直到能够找到更多的办公场所为止。

hot-desking

  • I think that very few employees prefer hot-desking to having a fixed desk.

    我想大多数雇员都更愿意有一张固定的办公桌而不是轮用办公桌。
