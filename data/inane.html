inane
ɪneɪn ɪnˈeɪn
ADJ-GRADED 愚蠢的;傻的 If you describe someone's behaviour or actions as inane, you think they are very silly or stupid. 愚蠢的;傻的 [disapproval]

  • He always had this inane grin.

    他总是这样傻傻地咧嘴笑。

  • She started asking me inane questions.

    她开始问我愚蠢的问题。

SYN senseless, stupid, silly, empty  
inanely

  • He lurched through the bar, grinning inanely.

    他踉踉跄跄地穿过酒吧间,傻乎乎地笑着。

inanity

  • ...the inanity of the conversation.

    谈话的空虚无聊
