dinner
dɪnəʳ ˈdɪnər
Word forms: plural dinners
1 N-VAR 正餐;主餐;晚餐 Dinner is the main meal of the day, usually served in the early part of the evening. 正餐;主餐;晚餐

  • She invited us to her house for dinner.

    她请我们去她家吃晚餐。

  • Would you like to stay and have dinner?.

    你留下来一起吃饭好吗？

  • Enjoy your dinner.

    请慢慢享用您的晚餐。

  • ...four-course dinners.

    包括四道菜的正餐

SYN meal, evening meal, main meal, spread [informal]  
2See also:TV dinner 
3 N-VAR 午餐;午饭 Any meal you eat in the middle of the day can be referred to as dinner . →see usage note at: meal 午餐;午饭
4 N-COUNT 晚宴;宴会 A dinner is a formal social event at which a meal is served. It is held in the evening. 晚宴;宴会

  • ...a series of official lunches and dinners.

    一系列的正式午餐和晚宴

  • The Professional Cricketers' Association held its annual dinner in London.

    职业板球运动员协会在伦敦举办了自己的年度宴会。

SYN banquet, feast, blowout [slang] , repast
