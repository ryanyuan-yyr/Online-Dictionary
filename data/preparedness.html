preparedness
prɪpeərɪdnəs prɪˈpɛərɪdnɪs priˈpærɪdnɪs prɪˈpærɪdnɪs
N-UNCOUNT （尤指对战争或灾难）有准备，做好准备 Preparedness is the state of being ready for something to happen, especially for war or a disaster. （尤指对战争或灾难）有准备，做好准备 [formal]

  • The situation in the capital forced them to maintain military preparedness.

    首都的形势迫使他们时刻做好军事准备。

  • ...red alert, the maximum state of preparedness.

    红色警戒，一级准备状态

SYN readiness, order, preparation, fitness
