pillion
pɪliən ˈpɪljən
Word forms: plural pillions
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 在（摩托车或自行车的）后座上 If someone rides pillion on a motorcycle or bicycle, they sit behind the person who is controlling it. 在（摩托车或自行车的）后座上

  • She rode pillion on her son's motor bike.

    她坐在儿子摩托车的后座上。

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] （摩托车的）后座 On a motorcycle, the pillion is the seat or part behind the rider. （摩托车的）后座

  • As a learner rider you must not carry a pillion passenger.

    作为摩托车初学者，切勿后座载人。
