unsentimental
ʌnsentɪmentəl
ADJ-GRADED 理智的；不感情用事的 If you describe someone as unsentimental, you mean that they do not allow emotions like pity or affection to interfere with their work or decisions. 理智的；不感情用事的

  • She was a practical, unsentimental woman.

    她是一个务实、理智的女人。

  • They are unsentimental about their impact on employees. [+ about]

    他们理智看待自己给员工所带来的影响。
