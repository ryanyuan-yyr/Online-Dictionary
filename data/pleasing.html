pleasing
pliːzɪŋ ˈplizɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ to-infinitive] 令人愉快的；使人满意的 Something that is pleasing gives you pleasure and satisfaction. 令人愉快的；使人满意的 [Also + to]

  • This area of France has a pleasing climate in August.

    法国的这一地区在8月份气候宜人。

  • Such a view is pleasing.

    这样的景色令人心旷神怡。

  • It's pleasing to listen to.

    它听着令人愉快。

  • It's pleasing to see some criminals have a conscience.

    看到有些罪犯还有良知让人感到欣慰。

pleasingly

  • The interior design is pleasingly simple.

    室内设计简约朴素，令人惬意。

  • He sets the atmosphere and shapes the scenes pleasingly.

    他把气氛和场面调动得轻松愉快。
