untangle
ʌntæŋgəl ʌnˈtæŋgəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense untangles, present participle untangling, past tense , past participle untangled
1 VERB 解开，解松（绳结） If you untangle something that is knotted or has become twisted around something, you undo the knots in it or free it. 解开，解松（绳结）

  • He was found desperately trying to untangle several reels of film. [VERB noun]

    他被撞见拼命想要解开几盘缠在一起的胶卷。

  • ...a light, non-sticky mousse which untangles hair and adds brilliant shine. [VERB noun]

    一种能理顺头发、增加其光泽的轻柔、无黏性的摩丝

SYN disentangle, unravel, sort out, extricate  
2 VERB 排解，解决，处理（混乱或复杂的局面） If you untangle a confused or complicated situation, you make the different things involved clear, or put the situation right. 排解，解决，处理（混乱或复杂的局面）

  • Lawyers and accountants began trying to untangle the complex affairs of the bank. [VERB noun]

    律师和会计师开始着手梳理这家银行复杂的事务。

  • The problem took three hours to untangle. [VERB noun]

    这个问题花了3个小时才解决。

SYN solve, clear up, straighten out, understand
