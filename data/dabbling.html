dabble
dæbəl ˈdæbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dabbles, present participle dabbling, past tense , past participle dabbled
VERB . 涉猎;浅尝 If you dabble in something, you take part in it but not very seriously . 涉猎;浅尝

  • He dabbled in business. [V + in/with/at]

    他搞过一点生意。

  • She dabbled with drugs. [V in/with/at n]

    她沾过毒品。

  • Magicians do not dabble, they work hard. [VERB]

    魔术师可不是随便玩两下的，他们下了苦功钻研。

SYN play (at or with), toy (with), potter, tinker (with)
