pyrotechnics
paɪroʊteknɪks ˌpaɪrəʊˈtɛknɪks ˌpaɪrəˈtɛknɪks
1 N-UNCOUNT 烟花制造；烟花施放 Pyrotechnics is the making or displaying of fireworks. 烟花制造；烟花施放

  • The festival will feature pyrotechnics, live music, and sculptures.

    节日里将有烟花表演、现场音乐会和雕塑展。

2 N-PLURAL 出色的表现；炫示 Impressive and exciting displays of skill are sometimes referred to as pyrotechnics . 出色的表现；炫示

  • ...the soaring pyrotechnics of the singer's voice.

    歌手歌喉的完美展示
