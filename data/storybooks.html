storybook
stɔːribʊk ˈstɔriˌbʊk
Word forms: plural storybooks
1 N-COUNT 儿童故事书;童话书 A storybook is a book of stories for children. 儿童故事书;童话书

  • As a child she learned to draw by tracing pictures out of her mother's old storybooks.

    小时候她通过描摹母亲旧童话书里的插图学会了画画。

  • ...two of her favourite storybook characters.

    她最喜欢的两个童话人物

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 童话般美好的;结局圆满的;皆大欢喜的 A storybook relationship, situation, or life is one that is perfect and ends happily, just as many fairy stories do. 童话般美好的;结局圆满的;皆大欢喜的

  • In many ways it was a storybook life. Summers in Salzburg. Weekends hunting in Connecticut.

    在很多方面，这都是种童话般的生活。夏天在萨尔茨堡度过。周末在康涅狄格打猎。

  • It was a real storybook romance.

    这是发生在现实中的童话般的罗曼史。
