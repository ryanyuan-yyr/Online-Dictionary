inseminate
ɪnsemɪneɪt ɪnˈsɛməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inseminates, present participle inseminating, past tense , past participle inseminated
1 VERB 使受精;使怀孕 To inseminate a woman or female animal means to put a male's sperm into her in order to make her pregnant. 使受精;使怀孕

  • The gadget is used to artificially inseminate cows. [VERB noun]

    这个器具被用来为母牛实施人工授精。
