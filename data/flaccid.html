flaccid
flæsɪd flæksɪd ˈflæsɪd ˈflæksɪd ˈflæksɪd ˈflæsɪd
ADJ-GRADED (人体某部分)松弛的，松垂的，绵软的 You use flaccid to describe a part of someone's body when it is unpleasantly soft and not hard or firm. (人体某部分)松弛的，松垂的，绵软的

  • I picked up her wrist. It was limp and flaccid.

    我抓起她绵软无力的手腕。

SYN limp, soft, weak, loose
