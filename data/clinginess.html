clingy
klɪŋi
1 ADJ-GRADED 过于依赖别人的 If you describe someone as clingy, you mean that they become very attached to people and depend on them too much. 过于依赖别人的 [disapproval]

  • A very clingy child can drive a parent to distraction.

    依赖性太强的孩子会逼得父母心烦意乱。

2 ADJ-GRADED (衣服)紧身的 Clingy clothes fit tightly round your body. (衣服)紧身的

  • ...long clingy skirts.

    紧身长裙
