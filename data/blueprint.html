blueprint
bluːprɪnt ˈbluˌprɪnt
Word forms: plural blueprints
1 N-COUNT 蓝图；计划；设想；构想 A blueprint for something is a plan or set of proposals that shows how it is expected to work. 蓝图；计划；设想；构想

  • The country's president will offer delegates his blueprint for the country's future. [+ for]

    该国总统会向代表们展示他构想的国家未来发展的蓝图。

  • ...the blueprint of a new plan of economic reform. [+ of]

    有关经济改革新计划的设想

SYN scheme, plan, design, system  
2 N-COUNT （建筑、设计的）蓝图 A blueprint of an architect's building plans or a designer's pattern is a photographic print consisting of white lines on a blue background. Blueprints contain all of the information that is needed to build or make something. （建筑、设计的）蓝图

  • ...a blueprint of the whole place, complete with heating ducts and wiring. [+ of]

    整个地方的规划图，包括暖气管和布线

  • The documents contain a blueprint for a nuclear device.

    文件内附有一张核装置的设计蓝图。

SYN plan, scheme, project, pattern  
3 N-COUNT （基因）模型，型板 A genetic blueprint is a pattern which is contained within all living cells. This pattern decides how the organism develops and what it looks like. （基因）模型，型板

  • The offspring contain a mixture of the genetic blueprint of each parent. [+ of]

    子女身上遗传了父母双方的基因型板。

  • DNA is the genetic material inside every cell that carries the blueprints for everything from hair color to the risk of cancer.

    脱氧核糖核酸是存在于每个细胞内的基因物质，它承载从头发颜色到致癌风险等各种遗传型板。
