morass
məræs məˈræs mɔˈræs
Word forms: plural morasses
N-COUNT [usually singular] 复杂混乱的情况;乱糟糟的一堆;困境 If you describe an unpleasant or confused situation as a morass, you mean that it seems impossible to escape from or resolve, because it has become so serious or so complicated. 复杂混乱的情况;乱糟糟的一堆;困境

  • I tried to drag myself out of the morass of despair. [+ of]

    我试图从绝望的困境中走出来。

  • ...the economic morass.

    经济混乱

SYN mess, confusion, chaos, jam [informal]
