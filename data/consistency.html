consistency
kənsɪstənsi kənˈsɪstənsi
1 N-UNCOUNT 连贯性;一致性 Consistency is the quality or condition of being consistent. 连贯性;一致性

  • He scores goals with remarkable consistency.

    他的进球一气呵成。

  • There's always a lack of consistency in matters of foreign policy.

    外交政策总是缺乏连贯性。

SYN agreement, harmony, correspondence, accordance  
2 N-UNCOUNT 稠度;平滑度 The consistency of a substance is how thick or smooth it is. 稠度;平滑度

  • Dilute the paint with water until it is the consistency of milk. [+ of]

    用水把涂料稀释到牛奶般的稠度。

  • I added a little milk to mix the dough to the right consistency.

    我加了一点儿牛奶，好让面团稠度适中。

SYN texture, density, thickness, firmness
