transsexual
trænsekʃuəl trænsˈsɛkʃuəl trænˈsɛkʃuəl
Word forms: plural transsexuals
N-COUNT 易性癖者;变性人 A transsexual is a person who has decided that they want to live as a person of the opposite sex, and so has changed their name and appearance in order to do this. Transsexuals sometimes have an operation to change their sex. 易性癖者;变性人
