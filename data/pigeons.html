pigeon
pɪdʒɪn ˈpɪdʒɪn ˈpɪdʒən
Word forms: plural pigeons
1 N-COUNT 鸽子 A pigeon is a bird, usually grey in colour, which has a fat body. Pigeons often live in towns. 鸽子
2 See also: clay pigeon  homing pigeon
3 PHRASE  闹得鸡犬不宁  to put the cat among the pigeons  闹得鸡犬不宁
SYN responsibility, business, concern, baby [slang]
