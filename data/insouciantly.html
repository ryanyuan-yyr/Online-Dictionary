insouciant
ɪnsuːsiənt ɪnˈsusiənt ɪnˈsuʃənt
ADJ-GRADED 漠不关心的;漫不经心的 An insouciant action or quality shows someone's lack of concern about something which they might be expected to take more seriously. 漠不关心的;漫不经心的 [formal]

  • Programme-makers seem irresponsibly insouciant about churning out violence. [+ about]

    编程者似乎对大批量制造含暴力因素的产品处之漠然，毫无责任心。

SYN nonchalant, casual, carefree, gay
