resit
riːsɪt riːsɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense resits, present participle resitting, past tense , past participle resat
The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'sɪt/. The noun is pronounced /'riːsɪt/. 动词读作/ˌriː'sɪt/。名词读作/'riːsɪt/。
VERB 重考;补考 If someone resits a test or examination, they take it again, usually because they failed the first time. 重考;补考 [British]

  • This year, Jim is resitting the exams he failed. [VERB noun]

    今年，吉姆将重考没有及格的科目。

  • If they fail, they can often resit the next year. [VERB]

    如果没有通过的话，他们常常可以在第二年补考。

Resit is also a noun.

  • He failed his First Year exams and didn't bother about the resits.

    他一年级的考试没有通过，却没有为补考而感到烦恼。
