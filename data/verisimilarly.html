verisimilitude
verɪsɪmɪlɪtjuːd ˌvɛrɪsɪˈmɪlɪtɪ -tuːd ˌvɛrəsɪˈmɪləˌtud ˌvɛrəsɪˈmɪləˌtjud
N-UNCOUNT 真实性;逼真 Verisimilitude is the quality of seeming to be true or real. 真实性;逼真 [formal]

  • At the required level of visual verisimilitude, computer animation is costly.

    要使电脑动画的视觉效果达到要求的逼真程度，花费会十分昂贵。

SYN realism, authenticity, credibility, resemblance
