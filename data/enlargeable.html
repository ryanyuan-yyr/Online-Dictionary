enlarge
ɪnlɑːʳdʒ ɛnˈlɑrdʒ ɪnˈlɑrdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enlarges, present participle enlarging, past tense , past participle enlarged
1 V-ERG 扩大;增大;放大 When you enlarge something or when it enlarges, it becomes bigger. 扩大;增大;放大

  • ...the plan to enlarge Ewood Park into a 30,000 all-seater stadium. [VERB noun]

    将伊伍德公园球场扩建成能容纳3万座席的体育场的计划

  • The glands in the neck may enlarge. [VERB]

    颈部腺体可能增大。

SYN grow, increase, extend, stretch  
enlarged

  • The UN secretary-general yesterday recommended an enlarged peacekeeping force.

    联合国秘书长昨天建议扩充维和部队。

2 VERB 放大（照片） To enlarge a photograph means to develop a bigger print of it. 放大（照片）

  • ...newlyweds wishing to enlarge snaps of their big day.

    希望把他们大喜之日的照片放大的新婚夫妻

3 VERB 进一步补充;详述;详细说明 If you enlarge on something that has been mentioned, you give more details about it. 进一步补充;详述;详细说明 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • He didn't enlarge on the form that the interim government and assembly would take. [VERB + on/upon]

    他没有进一步说明过渡政府和立法机构将采取的形式。

  • I wish to enlarge upon a statement made by Gary Docking. [VERB on/upon noun]

    我想就加里·多金的话再展开说一下。
