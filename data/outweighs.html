outweigh
aʊtweɪ ˌaʊtˈweɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outweighs, present participle outweighing, past tense , past participle outweighed
VERB （在重要性、益处、意义等上）重于，大于，超过 If one thing outweighs another, the first thing is of greater importance, benefit, or significance than the second thing. （在重要性、益处、意义等上）重于，大于，超过 [formal]

  • The medical benefits of x-rays far outweigh the risk of having them. [VERB noun]

    X光在医学上的益处远甚于它带来的风险。

  • The advantages of this deal largely outweigh the disadvantages. [VERB noun]

    这笔交易的利远远大于弊。

SYN override, cancel (out), eclipse, offset
