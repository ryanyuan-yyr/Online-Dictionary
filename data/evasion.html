evasion
ɪveɪʒən iˈveɪʒən ɪˈveɪʒən
Word forms: plural evasions
1 N-VAR [oft n NOUN] 逃避;规避 Evasion means deliberately avoiding something that you are supposed to do or deal with. 逃避;规避

  • Many Koreans were angered at what they saw as an evasion of responsibility. [+ of]

    很多韩国人认为这是在逃避责任，对此非常愤慨。

  • He was arrested for tax evasion.

    他因为逃税而被捕。

2 N-VAR 回避;避而不谈;避而不答 If you accuse someone of evasion when they have been asked a question, you mean that they are deliberately avoiding giving a clear direct answer. 回避;避而不谈;避而不答

  • We want straight answers. No evasions.

    我们要的是正面回答，不是托词。

  • Ted grinned in evasion and cleared his throat.

    特德咧嘴笑了笑，避而不谈，然后清了清嗓子。
