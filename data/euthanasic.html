euthanasia
juːθəneɪziə juːˈθænəsɪ -ʒə ˌjuθəˈneɪʒə ˌjuθəˈneɪʒiə ˌjuθəˈneɪziə
N-UNCOUNT 安乐死 Euthanasia is the practice of killing someone who is very ill and will never get better in order to end their suffering, usually done at their request or with their consent. 安乐死
SYN mercy killing, assisted suicide
