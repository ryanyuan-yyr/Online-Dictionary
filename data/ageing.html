ageing
eɪdʒɪŋ
(also aging)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 变老的；变旧的；变陈的 Someone or something that is ageing is becoming older and less healthy or efficient. 变老的；变旧的；变陈的

  • John lives with his ageing mother.

    约翰和他年迈的母亲一起生活。

  • Ageing aircraft need more frequent safety inspections.

    老化的飞机需要更加频繁地进行安全检查。

SYN growing old or older, declining, maturing, deteriorating  
2 N-UNCOUNT 变老；变陈；变旧 Ageing is the process of becoming old or becoming worn out. 变老；变陈；变旧
SYN growing old, decline, decay, deterioration
