Word forms: plural hundredweights
The plural form is hundredweight after a number. 用在具体数字后复数形式为 hundredweight。
N-COUNT 英担（重量单位，英国等于112磅，美国等于100磅） A hundredweight is a unit of weight that is equal to 112 pounds in Britain and to 100 pounds in the United States. 英担（重量单位，英国等于112磅，美国等于100磅）
