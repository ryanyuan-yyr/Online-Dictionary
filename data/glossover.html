gloss over
PHRASAL VERB 粉饰；掩盖；掩饰 If you gloss over a problem, a mistake, or an embarrassing moment, you try and make it seem unimportant by ignoring it or by dealing with it very quickly. 粉饰；掩盖；掩饰

  • Some foreign governments appear happy to gloss over continued human rights abuses. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一些外国政府似乎很乐于掩盖不断出现的侵犯人权现象。

SYN conceal, hide, mask, disguise
