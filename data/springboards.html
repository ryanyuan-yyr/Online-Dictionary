springboard
sprɪŋbɔːʳd ˈsprɪŋˌbɔrd
Word forms: plural springboards
1 N-COUNT 跳板;推动力 If something is a springboard for something else, it makes it possible for that thing to happen or start. 跳板;推动力

  • The 1981 budget was the springboard for an economic miracle. [+ for/to]

    1981 年的预算促成了一场经济奇迹的出现。

  • It could provide a springboard to success.

    这可能是迈向成功的跳板。

2 N-COUNT (跳水或体操运动用的)跳板 A springboard is a flexible board from which you jump into a swimming pool or onto a piece of gymnastic equipment. (跳水或体操运动用的)跳板
