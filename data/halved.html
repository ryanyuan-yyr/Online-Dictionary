halve
hɑːv hæv hæv hɑv
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense halves, present participle halving, past tense , past participle halved
1 V-ERG 把…减半;减到一半 When you halve something or when it halves, it is reduced to half its previous size or amount. 把…减半;减到一半

  • Dr Lee believes that men who exercise can halve their risk of cancer of the colon. [VERB noun]

    李医生相信，锻炼身体能使患结肠癌的风险降低一半。

  • The work force has been halved in two years. [VERB noun]

    劳动力在两年里减少了一半。

  • Meanwhile, sales of vinyl records halved in 1992 to just 6.7m. [VERB]

    与此同时，黑胶唱片的销售量在1992年下降了一半，只有670万张。

SYN cut in half, reduce by fifty per cent, decrease by fifty per cent, lessen by fifty per cent  
2 VERB 将…对半分;把…二等分 If you halve something, you divide it into two equal parts. 将…对半分;把…二等分

  • Halve the pineapple and scoop out the inside. [VERB noun]

    将菠萝对半切开，挖出里面的果肉。

SYN split in two, cut in half, bisect, divide in two  
3 （half 的复数） Halves is the plural of half. （half 的复数）
