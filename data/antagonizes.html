antagonize
æntægənaɪz ænˈtægəˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense antagonizes, present participle antagonizing, past tense , past participle antagonized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 antagonise
VERB 惹恼；引起…的敌意（或反感） If you antagonize someone, you make them feel angry or hostile towards you. 惹恼；引起…的敌意（或反感）

  • He didn't want to antagonize her. [VERB noun]

    他不想引起她的反感。

SYN annoy, anger, insult, offend
