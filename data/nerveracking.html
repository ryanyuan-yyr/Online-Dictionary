nerve-racking
ˈnɜrvˌrækɪŋ
(also nerve-wracking)
ADJ-GRADED 使人焦虑的;使人十分紧张的 A nerve-racking situation or experience makes you feel very tense and worried. 使人焦虑的;使人十分紧张的

  • The women and children spent a nerve-racking day outside waiting while fighting continued around them.

    女人和孩子们在外面紧张不安地挨过了一天，而战斗仍在她们身边继续。

  • It was more nerve-wracking than taking a World Cup penalty.

    这比在世界杯上罚点球还扣人心弦。

SYN tense, trying, difficult, worrying
