festivity
festɪvɪti fɛsˈtɪvəti
Word forms: plural festivities
1 N-UNCOUNT 庆祝;欢庆 Festivity is the celebration of something in a happy way. 庆祝;欢庆

  • There was a general air of festivity and abandon.

    到处是一派纵情狂欢的气氛。

SYN merrymaking, fun, pleasure, amusement  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 庆祝活动;庆典 Festivities are events that are organized in order to celebrate something. 庆祝活动;庆典

  • The festivities included a huge display of fireworks.

    庆祝活动包括盛大的焰火表演。

SYN celebration, party, festival, entertainment
