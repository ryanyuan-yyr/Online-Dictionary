purport
pəʳpɔːʳt pɜrˈpɔrt ˈpɜrˌpɔrt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense purports, present participle purporting, past tense , past participle purported
VERB 声称；自称；标榜 If you say that someone or something purports to do or be a particular thing, you mean that they claim to do or be that thing, although you may not always believe that claim. 声称；自称；标榜 [formal]

  • ...a book that purports to tell the whole truth. [VERB to-infinitive]

    声称要讲述全部事实的一本书

SYN claim, allege, proclaim, maintain
