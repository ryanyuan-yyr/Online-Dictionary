editor
edɪtəʳ ˈɛdɪtər
Word forms: plural editors
1 N-COUNT （报纸或杂志的）主编 An editor is the person who is in charge of a newspaper or magazine and who decides what will be published in each edition of it. （报纸或杂志的）主编
2 N-COUNT （报纸或杂志专栏的）编辑，主笔 An editor is a journalist who is responsible for a particular section of a newspaper or magazine. （报纸或杂志专栏的）编辑，主笔

  • Cookery Editor Moyra Fraser takes you behind the scenes.

    烹调专栏主笔莫伊拉·弗雷泽会带您了解幕后的故事。

  • ...by Alan Travis, Home Affairs Editor.

    由家庭事务专栏编辑艾伦·特拉维斯执笔

3 N-COUNT 编辑;校订者 An editor is a person who checks and corrects texts before they are published. 编辑;校订者

  • Your role as editor is important, for you can look at a piece of writing objectively.

    你的编辑角色很重要，因为你能够客观地审视作品。

4 N-COUNT （报道某类广播、电视新闻的）记者，编辑 An editor is a radio or television journalist who reports on a particular type of news. （报道某类广播、电视新闻的）记者，编辑

  • ...our economics editor, Dominic Harrod.

    我台经济新闻记者多米尼克·哈罗德

5 N-COUNT （电影、广播或电视节目的）剪辑员，剪辑师 An editor is a person who prepares a film, or a radio or television programme, by selecting some of what has been filmed or recorded and putting it in a particular order. （电影、广播或电视节目的）剪辑员，剪辑师

  • She worked at 20th Century Fox as a film editor.

    她在20世纪福克斯公司担任电影剪辑师。

6 N-COUNT (书籍的)编者，编选人 An editor is a person who collects pieces of writing by different authors and prepares them for publication in a book or a series of books. (书籍的)编者，编选人

  • Michael Rosen is the editor of the anthology. [+ of]

    迈克尔·罗森是这本选集的编者。

7 N-COUNT 编辑程序 An editor is a computer program that enables you to change and correct stored data. 编辑程序 [computing]
