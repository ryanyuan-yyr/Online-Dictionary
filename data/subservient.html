subservient
səbsɜːʳviənt səbˈsɜrviənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 屈从的;恭顺的;顺从的 If you are subservient, you do whatever someone wants you to do. 屈从的;恭顺的;顺从的

  • She is expected to be subservient to her uncle. [+ to]

    她被要求对叔叔恭敬顺从。

SYN servile, submissive, deferential, subject  
subservience

  • ...an austere regime stressing obedience and subservience to authority.

    强调要服从并听命于当局的强权政权

2 ADJ 次要的;从属于…的 If you treat one thing as subservient to another, you treat it as less important than the other thing. 次要的;从属于…的

  • The woman's needs are seen as subservient to the group interest. [+ to]

    妇女的需求被认为要服从团体的利益。

SYN subordinate, subsidiary, accessory, auxiliary
