footloose
fʊtluːs ˈfʊtˌlus
1 ADJ-GRADED 无拘无束的;自由自在的;不受约束的 If you describe someone as footloose, you mean that they have no responsibilities or commitments, and are therefore free to do what they want and go where they want. 无拘无束的;自由自在的;不受约束的

  • People that are single tend to be more footloose.

    单身的人往往更加无拘无束。

2 PHRASE (单身的人)无拘无束的，自由自在的 If you describe someone as footloose and fancy-free, you mean that they are not married or in a similar relationship, and you therefore consider them to have very few responsibilities or commitments. (单身的人)无拘无束的，自由自在的

  • In the eyes of the public, a divorced man is footloose and fancy-free.

    在公众心目中，离婚的男人无牵无挂、自由自在。
