pansy
pænzi ˈpænzi
Word forms: plural pansies
1 N-COUNT 三色堇 A pansy is a small brightly coloured garden flower with large round petals. 三色堇
2 N-COUNT 同性恋男子 If someone describes a man as a pansy, they mean that he is a homosexual. 同性恋男子 [informal, offensive, old-fashioned]
