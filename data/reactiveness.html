reactive
riæktɪv rɪˈæktɪv riˈæktɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 活性的;起化学反应的 Something that is reactive is able to react chemically with a lot of different substances. 活性的;起化学反应的

  • Ozone is a highly reactive form of oxygen gas.

    臭氧是一种非常活跃的氧气形态。

SYN unstable  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 起反应的；回应性的 If someone is reactive, they behave in response to what happens to them, rather than deciding in advance how they want to behave. 起反应的；回应性的

  • I want our organization to be less reactive and more proactive.

    我希望我们的组织少一些被动反应，多一些积极主动。

SYN apathetic, passive, laissez-faire, inactive
