calling
kɔːlɪŋ ˈkɔlɪŋ
Word forms: plural callings
N-COUNT [usually singular] 行业;职业 A calling is a profession or career which someone is strongly attracted to, especially one which involves helping other people. 行业;职业

  • He was a consultant physician, a serious man dedicated to his calling.

    他是个会诊医生,严肃认真,对自己的职业全身心投入。

SYN profession, work, business, line
