maternity
mətɜːʳnɪti məˈtɜrnəti
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 产妇的;孕妇的 Maternity is used to describe things relating to the help and medical care given to a woman when she is pregnant and when she gives birth. 产妇的;孕妇的

  • Your job will be kept open for your return after maternity leave.

    你的工作会一直保留到你休完产假回来。

  • The boy was born at the city's maternity hospital.

    这个男孩出生在市妇产医院。

  • ...maternity clothes.

    孕妇装

2 N-UNCOUNT 母亲身份 Maternity is the state of being a mother. 母亲身份

  • Morisot had experienced maternity herself.

    莫里佐也曾为人母。

SYN motherhood, parenthood, motherliness
