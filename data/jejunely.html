jejune
dʒɪdʒuːn dʒɪˈdʒun
1 ADJ-GRADED 幼稚的；不成熟的；头脑简单的 If you describe something or someone as jejune, you are criticizing them for being very simple and unsophisticated. 幼稚的；不成熟的；头脑简单的 [formal, disapproval]

  • They were of great service in correcting my jejune generalizations.

    他们纠正了我不成熟的泛泛之论，帮了我大忙。

SYN simple, silly, juvenile, naive  
2 ADJ-GRADED 枯燥乏味的；单调的 If you describe something or someone as jejune, you mean they are dull and boring. 枯燥乏味的；单调的 [old-fashioned]

  • We knew we were in for a pretty long, jejune evening.

    我们很清楚我们注定要过一个乏味单调的漫长夜晚了。

SYN dull, dry, banal, prosaic
