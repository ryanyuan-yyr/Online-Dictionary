crusty
krʌsti ˈkrʌsti
Word forms: comparative crustier, superlative crustiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有硬皮的;有脆皮的 Crusty bread has a hard, crisp outside. 有硬皮的;有脆皮的

  • ...crusty French loaves.

    脆皮法式烤面包

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (尤指老头儿)脾气急躁的，易发怒的 If you describe someone, especially an old man, as crusty, you mean they are impatient and easily irritated. (尤指老头儿)脾气急躁的，易发怒的

  • ...a crusty old colonel.

    脾气暴躁的老上校
