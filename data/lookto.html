look to
1 PHRASAL VERB 寄望；指望；依靠 If you look to someone or something for a particular thing that you want, you expect or hope that they will provide it. 寄望；指望；依靠

  • The difficulties women encounter with their doctors partly explain why so many of us are looking to alternative therapies. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们很多人都寄望于替代疗法，个中原因从女性看医生所遇到的困难可知一二。

2 PHRASAL VERB 展望，思考（将来发生的事） If you look to something that will happen in the future, you think about it. 展望，思考（将来发生的事）

  • Looking to the future, though, we asked him what the prospects are for a vaccine to prevent infection in the first place. [VERB PREP. noun]

    不过，展望未来，我们问到他研制出防止感染的疫苗究竟有多大的可能性。
