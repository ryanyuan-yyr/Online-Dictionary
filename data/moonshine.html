moonshine
muːnʃaɪn ˈmunˌʃaɪn
1 N-UNCOUNT (非法私酿的)威士忌酒 Moonshine is whisky that is made illegally. (非法私酿的)威士忌酒 [mainly US]
2 N-UNCOUNT 空谈;空想;空话 If you say that someone's thoughts, ideas, or comments are moonshine, you think they are foolish and not based on reality. 空谈;空想;空话 [disapproval]

  • As Morison remarks, the story is pure moonshine.

    正如莫里森所说，这个故事纯属妄想。
