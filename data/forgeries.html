forgery
fɔːʳdʒəri ˈfɔrdʒəri
Word forms: plural forgeries
1 N-UNCOUNT 伪造罪 Forgery is the crime of forging money, documents, or paintings. 伪造罪

  • He was found guilty of forgery.

    他被判伪造罪名成立。

SYN falsification, faking, pirating, counterfeiting  
2 N-COUNT 伪造品;仿造物;赝品 You can refer to a forged document, banknote, or painting as a forgery . 伪造品;仿造物;赝品

  • The letter was a forgery.

    这封信是伪造的。

SYN fake, imitation, sham, counterfeit
