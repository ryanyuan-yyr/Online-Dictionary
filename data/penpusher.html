pen-pusher
(also penpusher)Word forms: plural pen-pushers
N-COUNT 文员；文书；耍笔杆子的人 If you call someone a pen-pusher, you mean that their work consists of writing or dealing with documents, and does not seem very useful or important. 文员；文书；耍笔杆子的人 [British, disapproval]

  • As a result, industry was overmanned and pen-pushers were everywhere.

    结果是，企业人浮于事，到处都是耍笔杆子的人。
