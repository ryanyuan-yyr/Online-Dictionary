susceptibility
səseptɪbɪlɪti səˌsɛptəˈbɪləti
Word forms: plural susceptibilities
1 N-VAR 易受影响(或损害)的状况;敏感性;过敏性 If you have a susceptibility to something unpleasant, you are likely to be affected by it. 易受影响(或损害)的状况;敏感性;过敏性

  • ...his increased susceptibility to infections. [+ to]

    他越来越容易受感染的体质

SYN vulnerability, weakness, liability, propensity  
2 N-PLURAL 感情脆弱之处;情感的要害 A person's susceptibilities are feelings which can be easily hurt. 感情脆弱之处;情感的要害 [formal]

  • I am well aware that in saying this I shall outrage a few susceptibilities.

    我十分清楚这样说会戳到一些人的痛处。
