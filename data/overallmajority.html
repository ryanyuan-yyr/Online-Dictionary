overall majority ˈəʊvərˌɔːl məˈdʒɒrɪtɪ
Word forms: plural overall majorities
N-COUNT [usually singular] （选举或投票时的）压倒性多数票，绝对多数票 If a political party wins an overall majority in an election or vote, they get more votes than the total number of votes or seats won by all their opponents. （选举或投票时的）压倒性多数票，绝对多数票
