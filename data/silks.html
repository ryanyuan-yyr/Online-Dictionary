silk
sɪlk sɪlk
Word forms: plural silks
1 N-MASS 蚕丝;丝绸;丝线 Silk is a substance which is made into smooth fine cloth and sewing thread. You can also refer to this cloth or thread as silk . 蚕丝;丝绸;丝线

  • They continued to get their silks from China.

    他们继续从中国购买丝绸。

  • Pauline wore a silk dress with a strand of pearls.

    保利娜穿了件丝绸长裙，戴了串珍珠。

  • ...softer-looking shades in silk.

    看上去更柔和的丝绸颜色

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] (蜘蛛等所分泌的)丝 You can refer to the substance produced by some creatures such as spiders as silk . (蜘蛛等所分泌的)丝

  • ...the silk threads of a spider's web.

    蜘蛛网的丝线
