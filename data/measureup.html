measure up
PHRASAL VERB 符合(标准);达到(期望) If you do not measure up to a standard or to someone's expectations, you are not good enough to achieve the standard or fulfil the person's expectations. 符合(标准);达到(期望)

  • It was fatiguing sometimes to try to measure up to her standard of perfection. [VERB PREP. + to]

    有时候，力求达到她尽善尽美的标准让人觉得很累。

  • She's always comparing me to other people, and somehow I never measure up. [VERB PREP.]

    她总是拿我跟别人相比，不知怎的，我总是不如人家。
