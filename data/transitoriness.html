transitory
trænzɪtəri ˈtrænsɪtərɪ -tɔːri ˈtrænsəˌtɔri ˈtrænzəˌtɔri
ADJ-GRADED 短暂的;转瞬即逝的;昙花一现的 If you say that something is transitory, you mean that it lasts only for a short time. 短暂的;转瞬即逝的;昙花一现的

  • Most teenage romances are transitory.

    绝大多数十几岁少年的恋情都转瞬即逝。

  • …a sad reminder of the transitory nature of political success.

    不由得令人悲哀地想到政治成功昙花一现的本质

SYN short-lived, short, passing, brief
