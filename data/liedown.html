lie down
PHRASAL VERB 躺下（休息或睡眠） When you lie down, you move into a horizontal position, usually in order to rest or sleep. 躺下（休息或睡眠）

  • Why don't you go upstairs and lie down for a bit? [VERB PREP.]

    你不为什么不上楼去躺一会儿呢？

lie-down
ˌlaɪˈdaʊn
N-SING （通常指躺在床上的）小睡，小憩 If you have a lie-down, you have a short rest, usually in bed. （通常指躺在床上的）小睡，小憩 [British, informal]

  • She had departed upstairs for a lie-down.

    她去了楼上小睡一会儿。

SYN rest, sleep, nap, doze
