growing season
ˈɡrəʊɪŋ ˈsiːzən
Word forms: plural growing seasons
N-COUNT [usually singular] （植物或作物的）生长季节 The growing season in a particular country or area is the period in each year when the weather and temperature is right for plants and crops to grow. （植物或作物的）生长季节

  • Any farmer or gardener knows that a milder winter means a longer growing season.

    农民和花匠都知道，冬天越暖和，作物的生长季节便越长。
