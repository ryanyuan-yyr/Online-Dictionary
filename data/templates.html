template
templeɪt ˈtɛmplɪt -plɪt ˈtɛmplɪt
Word forms: plural templates
1 N-COUNT (切割木材、纸张、金属等使用的)模板，样板 A template is a thin piece of metal or plastic which is cut into a particular shape. It is used to help you cut wood, paper, metal, or other materials accurately, or to reproduce the same shape many times. (切割木材、纸张、金属等使用的)模板，样板

  • Trace around your template and transfer the design onto a sheet of card.

    沿着你的样板边画一圈，把图案描在一张硬纸板上。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 样板;范例;范式 If one thing is a template for something else, the second thing is based on the first thing. 样板;范例;范式

  • The deal is likely to provide a template for other agreements. [+ for]

    这个协议可能会成为其他协议的范例。
