economize
ɪkɒnəmaɪz ɪˈkɑnəˌmaɪz iˈkɑnəˈmaɪzə
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense economizes, present participle economizing, past tense , past participle economized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 economise
VERB 节约;节省 If you economize, you save money by spending it very carefully. 节约;节省

  • We're going to have to economize from now on. [VERB]

    从现在开始，我们不得不节约开支。

  • Hollywood has been talking about economizing on movie budgets. [VERB + on]

    好莱坞一直在讨论紧缩电影预算。

SYN cut back, save, save money, be sparing
