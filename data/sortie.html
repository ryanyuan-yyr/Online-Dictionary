sortie
sɔːʳti ˈsɔrti sɔrˈti
Word forms: plural sorties
1 N-COUNT 短程旅行(尤指去陌生地方) A sortie is a brief trip away from your home base, especially a trip to an unfamiliar place. 短程旅行(尤指去陌生地方) [formal]

  • From here we plan several sorties into the countryside on foot.

    我们计划了几次从这里出发徒步去乡间的短途旅行。

  • ...little sorties into antique shops.

    去古董店小逛

SYN outing, trip, expedition, excursion  
2 N-COUNT (军队的)出击，袭击 If a military force makes a sortie, it leaves its own position and goes briefly into enemy territory to make an attack. (军队的)出击，袭击 [formal]

  • His men made a sortie to Guazatan and took a prisoner.

    他的士兵袭击了瓜扎坦，擒获了一名俘虏。

  • They flew 2,700 sorties in a day and didn't lose a single plane.

    他们一天出动了 2,700 架次飞机，而且没有损失一架。

SYN raid, operation, mission, flight
