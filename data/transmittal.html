transmit
trænzmɪt trænsˈmɪt trænzˈmɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense transmits, present participle transmitting, past tense , past participle transmitted
1 VERB 传送;传递;播送 When radio and television programmes, computer data, or other electronic messages are transmitted, they are sent from one place to another, using wires, radio waves, or satellites. 传送;传递;播送

  • The game was transmitted live in Spain and Italy. [be VERB-ed]

    这场比赛在西班牙和意大利进行了现场直播。

  • The information is electronically transmitted to schools and colleges… [be VERB-ed]

    信息通过电子方式传送到各所学校和学院。

  • This is currently the most efficient way to transmit certain types of data like electronic mail. [VERB noun]

    这是目前传送电子邮件等某些类型的数据的最高效方式。

  • The device is not designed to transmit to satellites. [VERB + to]

    该设备不是为向卫星发送信号而设计的。

2 VERB 传播，传染(疾病) If one person or animal transmits a disease to another, they have the disease and cause the other person or animal to have it. 传播，传染(疾病) [formal]

  • ...mosquitoes that transmit disease to humans. [VERB noun + to]

    传染疾病给人类的蚊子

  • There was no danger of transmitting the infection through operations. [VERB noun]

    不存在通过手术传播这种传染病的风险。

  • …the spread of sexually transmitted diseases. [VERB-ed]

    性传播疾病的蔓延

SYN pass on, carry, spread, communicate  
3 VERB 传达，传输(思想、感情) If you transmit an idea or feeling to someone else, you make them understand and share the idea or feeling. 传达，传输(思想、感情) [literary]

  • The message they are transmitting to their daughters is very different from that of previous generations… [VERB noun to noun]

    他们向女儿传输的信息与上几代人截然不同。

  • He transmitted his keen enjoyment of singing to the audience. [VERB noun + to]

    他把自己对演唱的激情投入传递给了观众。

4 VERB 传导(声、电等) If an object or substance transmits something such as sound or electrical signals, the sound or signals are able to pass through it. 传导(声、电等)

  • These thin crystals transmit much of the power. [VERB noun]

    这些薄薄的晶体传导了大部分的动力。

  • There was no vibration transmitted to the handles and the machine wasn't noisy either. [VERB-ed]

    没有任何振动传导到手柄，机器也没有任何噪音。
