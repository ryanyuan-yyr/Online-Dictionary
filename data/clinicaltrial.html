clinical trial
ˈklɪnɪkəl ˈtraɪəl
Word forms: plural clinical trials
N-COUNT 临床试验 When a new type of drug or medical treatment undergoes clinical trials, it is tested directly on patients to see if it is effective. 临床试验

  • Two rival laser surgery systems are undergoing clinical trials in the US.

    两个相互竞争的激光手术系统正在美国进行临床试验。
