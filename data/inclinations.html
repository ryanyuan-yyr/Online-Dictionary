inclination
ɪnklɪneɪʃən ˌɪnkləˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural inclinations
N-VAR [oft NOUN to-infinitive] 意向;倾向;爱好 An inclination is a feeling that makes you want to act in a particular way. 意向;倾向;爱好

  • He had neither the time nor the inclination to think of other things.

    他没有时间也不愿意去考虑其他事情。

  • She showed no inclination to go.

    她丝毫没有要走的意思。

  • He set out to follow his artistic inclinations.

    他开始追求自己的艺术爱好。

SYN tendency, liking, taste, turn
