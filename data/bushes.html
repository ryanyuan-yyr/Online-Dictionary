bush
bʊʃ bʊʃ
Word forms: plural bushes
1 N-COUNT 灌木；灌木丛 A bush is a large plant which is smaller than a tree and has a lot of branches. 灌木；灌木丛

  • Trees and bushes grew down to the water's edge.

    树和灌木丛一直长到了水边。

SYN shrub, plant, hedge, undergrowth  
2 N-SING [oft NOUN n] （热带国家未开发的）荒野地带，丛林地带 The wild, uncultivated parts of some hot countries are referred to as the bush. （热带国家未开发的）荒野地带，丛林地带

  • They walked through the dense Mozambican bush for thirty six hours.

    他们花了36个小时穿过茂密的莫桑比克丛林。

  • The jeep was found lying in thick bush.

    人们发现吉普车翻倒在一处茂密的丛林里。

3 PHRASE 拐弯抹角；旁敲侧击 If you tell someone not to beat about the bush, you mean that you want them to tell you something immediately and quickly, rather than in a complicated, indirect way. 拐弯抹角；旁敲侧击

  • Stop beating about the bush. What's he done?

    别再拐弯抹角了，他到底干了什么？
