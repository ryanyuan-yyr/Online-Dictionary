sequestrate
siːkwestreɪt sɪˈkwɛsˌtreɪt ˈsikwəˌstreɪt ˈsɛkwəˌstreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sequestrates, present participle sequestrating, past tense , past participle sequestrated
VERB [usually passive] (通常指按照法庭决议)扣押，查封(债务人资产) When property is sequestrated, it is taken officially from someone who has debts, usually after a decision in a court of law. If the debts are paid off, the property is returned to its owner. (通常指按照法庭决议)扣押，查封(债务人资产) [law]

  • He tried to prevent union money from being sequestrated by the courts. [be VERB-ed]

    他试图阻止法院扣押工会的资产。
