mojo
məʊdʒəʊ ˈməʊdʒəʊ ˈmoʊdʒoʊ
Word forms: plural mojos, plural mojoes
N-SING 魔术; 魔咒; 护身符People use mojo to mean magic, or a magic spell or magic charm. 魔术; 魔咒; 护身符

  • I don't know what mojo is working with this team, but it's not a good one right now.

    我不知道这个团队用的是什么魔咒，但目前肯定不是个良咒。

  • ...the best sign that Congress got its mojo working.

    ...国会魔力乍现的最好迹象。
