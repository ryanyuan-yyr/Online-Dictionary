in situ
ɪn sɪtjuː ɪn ˈsɪtjuː - sɪtuː ɪn ˈsaɪˌtu
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 在原地;在原来的位置 If something remains in situ, especially while something is done to it, it remains where it is. 在原地;在原来的位置 [formal]

  • Major works of painting, sculpture, mosaic and architecture were examined in situ in Venice.

    重要的绘画、雕塑、镶嵌画和建筑作品都在威尼斯原地得到了仔细检查。

In situ is also an adjective.

  • ...technical data derived from laboratory and in-situ experimentation.

    由实验室与就地实验得出的技术数据
