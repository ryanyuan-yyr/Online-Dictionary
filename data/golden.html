golden
goʊldən ˈgoʊldən
1 ADJ 金色的；金黄色的 Something that is golden is bright yellow in colour. 金色的；金黄色的

  • She combed and arranged her golden hair.

    她梳理好自己的金发。

  • ...an endless golden beach.

    一望无际的金色沙滩

SYN yellow, bright, brilliant, blonde  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 金的；金制的 Golden things are made of gold. 金的；金制的

  • ...a golden chain with a golden locket.

    带有金制盒式链坠的金项链

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 极好的；有发展前途的；有益的 If you describe something as golden, you mean it is wonderful because it is likely to be successful and rewarding, or because it is the best of its kind. 极好的；有发展前途的；有益的

  • He says there's a golden opportunity for peace which must be seized.

    他说务必抓住这个谋求和平的绝佳机会。

SYN promising, excellent, valuable, favourable  
4 PHRASE 大受欢迎（或十分成功）的人 If you refer to a man as a golden boy or a woman as a golden girl, you mean that they are especially popular and successful. 大受欢迎（或十分成功）的人

  • When the movie came out the critics went wild, hailing Tarantino as the golden boy of the 1990s.

    这部影片上映后，批评家们反响十分热烈，他们将塔拉蒂诺誉为20世纪90年代的金牌男星。
