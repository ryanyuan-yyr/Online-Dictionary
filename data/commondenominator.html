common denominator
Word forms: plural common denominators
1 N-COUNT (数学中的)公分母 In mathematics, a common denominator is a number which can be divided exactly by all the denominators in a group of fractions. (数学中的)公分母
2 N-COUNT 共同点 A common denominator is a characteristic or attitude that is shared by all members of a group of people. 共同点

  • I think the only common denominator of success is hard work.

    我认为取得成功唯一的共同要素是勤奋。
