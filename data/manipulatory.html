manipulator
mənɪpjʊleɪtəʳ məˈnɪpjuˌleɪtər məˌnɪpjəˌleɪtər
Word forms: plural manipulators
N-COUNT 善于操控者;善于左右时局的人 If you describe someone as a manipulator, you mean that they skilfully control events, situations, or people, often in a way that other people disapprove of. 善于操控者;善于左右时局的人

  • Jean Brodie is a manipulator. She cons everybody.

    琼·布罗迪是个善于摆布他人的高手，欺骗了所有人。

  • ...some of the best PR manipulators in the business.

    这个行业里的几位顶尖公关高手
