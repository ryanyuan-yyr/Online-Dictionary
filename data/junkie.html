junkie
dʒʌŋki ˈdʒʌŋki
Word forms: plural junkies
1 N-COUNT 吸毒成瘾者；瘾君子 A junkie is a drug addict. 吸毒成瘾者；瘾君子 [informal]
2 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 对…上瘾的人；迷恋…的人 You can use junkie to refer to someone who is very interested in a particular activity, especially when they spend a lot of time on it. 对…上瘾的人；迷恋…的人 [informal]

  • ...a computer junkie.

    电脑迷
