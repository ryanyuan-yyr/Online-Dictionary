overblown
oʊvəʳbloʊn ˌoʊvərˈbloʊn
ADJ-GRADED 夸张的；过分的；夸大其词的 Something that is overblown makes something seem larger, more important, or more significant than it really is. 夸张的；过分的；夸大其词的

  • Warnings of disaster may be overblown.

    灾害预警有被夸大的可能。

  • The reporting of the hostage story was fair, if sometimes overblown.

    尽管这篇关于人质的报道有些夸大其词，但还算公正。

  • ...overblown dreams.

    不切实际的梦想

SYN excessive, exaggerated, over the top [slang] , too much
