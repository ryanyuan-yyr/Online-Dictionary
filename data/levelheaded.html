level-headed
ADJ-GRADED 头脑冷静的；稳健的 If you describe a person as level-headed, you mean that they are calm and sensible even in difficult situations. 头脑冷静的；稳健的

  • Simon is level-headed and practical.

    西蒙为人头脑冷静，讲求实际。

  • His level-headed approach suggests he will do what is necessary.

    他这人沉稳冷静，会见机行事的。

SYN calm, balanced, reasonable, composed
