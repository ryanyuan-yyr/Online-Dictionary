supertanker
suːpəʳtæŋkəʳ ˈsupərˌtæŋkər
Word forms: plural supertankers
N-COUNT 超级油轮;巨型油轮 A supertanker is an extremely large ship that is used for transporting oil. 超级油轮;巨型油轮

  • An oil slick caused by a spill from a supertanker has hit several beaches.

    一艘超级油轮的石油泄漏形成的浮油膜已经影响了好几个海滩。
