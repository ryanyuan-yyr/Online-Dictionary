outgrowth
aʊtgroʊθ ˈaʊtˌgroʊθ
Word forms: plural outgrowths
N-COUNT 产物；自然发展的结果 Something that is an outgrowth of another thing has developed naturally as a result of it. 产物；自然发展的结果

  • Her first book is an outgrowth of an art project she began in 1988. [+ of]

    她的第一本书是一个她开始于1988年的艺术项目的产物。

SYN product, result, development, fruit
