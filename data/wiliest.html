wily
waɪli ˈwaɪli
Word forms: comparative wilier, superlative wiliest
ADJ-GRADED 诡计多端的;狡诈的;狡猾的 If you describe someone or their behaviour as wily, you mean that they are clever at achieving what they want, especially by tricking people. 诡计多端的;狡诈的;狡猾的

  • His appointment as prime minister owed much to the wily manoeuvring of the President.

    他之所以被任命为总理，很大程度上是因为总统在阴谋操纵。

SYN cunning, designing, scheming, sharp
