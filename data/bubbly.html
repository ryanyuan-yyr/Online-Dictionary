bubbly
bʌbli ˈbʌbli
1 ADJ-GRADED 兴奋的；活泼的；爱说话的 Someone who is bubbly is very lively and cheerful and talks a lot. 兴奋的；活泼的；爱说话的 [approval]

  • a bubbly girl who loves to laugh

    爱笑的活泼女孩

  • She had a bright and bubbly personality.

    她性格开朗活泼。

SYN lively, happy, excited, animated  
2 N-UNCOUNT 香槟酒 Champagne is sometimes called bubbly . 香槟酒 [informal]

  • Guests were presented with glasses of bubbly on arrival.

    客人来后，都有香槟酒招待。

SYN champagne, sparkling wine, champers [informal] , cava  
3 ADJ-GRADED 多泡沫的 If something is bubbly, it has a lot of bubbles in it. 多泡沫的

  • ...a nice hot bubbly bath.

    舒服的热水泡沫浴

  • Melt the butter over a medium-low heat. When it is melted and bubbly, put in the flour.

    用中低火把黄油加热，等黄油熔化起泡时，加入面粉。

SYN frothy, sparkling, fizzy, effervescent
