professorial
prɒfɪsɔːriəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 像教授的；有学者风范的 If you describe someone as professorial, you mean that they look or behave like a professor. 像教授的；有学者风范的

  • His manner is not so much regal as professorial.

    他的言行举止不太像帝王，倒更像学者一些。

  • I raised my voice to a professorial tone.

    我提高了嗓音，像教授一样说话。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 和教授（或大学教师）工作有关的 Professorial means relating to the work of a professor. 和教授（或大学教师）工作有关的

  • ...the cuts which have led to 36 per cent of professorial posts remaining unfilled.

    裁员导致36%的教授职位空缺
