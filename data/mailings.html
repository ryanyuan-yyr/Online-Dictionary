mailing
meɪlɪŋ ˈmeɪlɪŋ ˈmeɪlɪŋ
Word forms: plural mailings
1 N-UNCOUNT 邮寄;投寄 Mailing is the activity of sending things to people through the postal service. 邮寄;投寄

  • The newsletter was printed towards the end of June in readiness for mailing.

    快到6月底的时候，时事通讯印刷完毕准备投寄。

  • The owners of the store have stepped up customer mailings.

    店主们加大了对顾客的邮寄宣传力度。

2 N-COUNT 邮件 A mailing is something that is sent to people through the postal service. 邮件

  • The seniors organizations sent out mailings to their constituencies.

    老年人组织向各自的会员寄送了邮件。
