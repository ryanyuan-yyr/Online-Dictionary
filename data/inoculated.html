inoculate
ɪnɒkjʊleɪt ɪˈnɑkjəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inoculates, present participle inoculating, past tense , past participle inoculated
VERB 给…免疫接种;给…打预防针 To inoculate a person or animal means to inject a weak form of a disease into their body as a way of protecting them against the disease. 给…免疫接种;给…打预防针 [Also + against]

  • ...a program to inoculate every child in the state. [VERB noun]

    为该州所有儿童预防接种的计划

  • His dogs were inoculated against rabies. [be V-ed + against]

    他的狗注射过狂犬疫苗。
