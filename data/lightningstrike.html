lightning strike
ˈlaɪtnɪŋ straɪk
Word forms: plural lightning strikes
N-COUNT （事先没有警告的）闪电式罢工 A lightning strike is a strike in which workers stop work suddenly and without any warning, in order to protest about something. （事先没有警告的）闪电式罢工 [British]

  • Bank staff are to stage a series of lightning strikes in a dispute over staffing.

    银行职员因雇佣争端将发起一系列的闪电式罢工。
