settle down
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤指结婚或买房后)定居，安顿下来 When someone settles down, they start living a quiet life in one place, especially when they get married or buy a house. (尤指结婚或买房后)定居，安顿下来

  • One day I'll want to settle down and have a family. [VERB PREP.]

    总有一天，我会想安定下来，成个家。

  • Before she settled down in Portugal, she had run her own antiques shop in London. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    在定居葡萄牙之前，她在伦敦经营一家自己的古董店。

SYN put down roots, get married, have a home, stop moving from place to place  
2 PHRASAL VERB (历经磨难或变化后)安静下来，平静下来 If a situation or a person that has been going through a lot of problems or changes settles down, they become calm. (历经磨难或变化后)安静下来，平静下来

  • It'd be fun, after the situation in Europe settles down, to take a trip over to France. [VERB PREP.]

    等欧洲的局势平定下来后，去法国旅行一趟应该不错。

  • We saw the therapist four times, and the children have now settled down. [VERB PREP.]

    我们去看了四次治疗专家，现在孩子们已经稳定下来了。

3 PHRASAL VERB 集中精力做;开始认真做 If you settle down to do something or to something, you prepare to do it and concentrate on it. 集中精力做;开始认真做

  • He got his coffee, came back and settled down to listen. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    他喝了杯咖啡，回来后开始认真听讲。

  • They settled down to some serious work. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他们着手做一些重要的工作。

4 PHRASAL VERB 准备,开始(就寝) If you settle down for the night, you get ready to lie down and sleep. 准备,开始(就寝)

  • They put up their tents and settled down for the night. [VERB PREP.]

    他们搭好帐篷，准备过夜。

SYN go to bed, retire, turn in, go to sleep
