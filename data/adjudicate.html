adjudicate
ədʒuːdɪkeɪt əˈdʒudɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adjudicates, present participle adjudicating, past tense , past participle adjudicated
VERB 判决；裁决 If you adjudicate on a dispute or problem, you make an official judgment or decision about it. 判决；裁决 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • ...a commissioner to adjudicate on legal rights. [VERB prep.]

    裁决合法权益的专员

  • The international court of justice might be a suitable place to adjudicate claims. [VERB noun]

    国际法庭或许是对所有权作出裁决的合适之地。

SYN decide, judge, determine, settle
