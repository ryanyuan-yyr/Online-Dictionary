acclamation
ækləmeɪʃən ˌækləˈmeɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 欢呼;喝彩;赞扬 Acclamation is a noisy or enthusiastic expression of approval for someone or something. 欢呼;喝彩;赞扬 [formal]

  • The news was greeted with considerable popular acclamation.

    消息赢得了相当普遍的欢呼。

2 N-UNCOUNT 口头表决方式 If someone is chosen or elected by acclamation, they are elected without a written vote. 口头表决方式 [formal]

  • At first it looked like I was going to win by acclamation.

    起初看起来我会以口头表决的方式获胜。
