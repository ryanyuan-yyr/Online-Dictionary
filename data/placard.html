placard
plækɑːʳd ˈplækɑrd ˈplækərd pləˈkɑrd
Word forms: plural placards
N-COUNT 标语牌；告示牌；布告；海报 A placard is a large notice that is carried in a march or displayed in a public place. 标语牌；告示牌；布告；海报

  • The protesters sang songs and waved placards.

    抗议者唱着歌，挥舞着标语牌。

SYN notice, bill, advertisement, poster
