restorative
rɪstɔːrətɪv rɪˈstɒrətɪv rɪˈstɔrətɪv
Word forms: plural restoratives
1 ADJ-GRADED 恢复健康（或体力、精神）的 Something that is restorative makes you feel healthier, stronger, or more cheerful after you have been feeling tired, weak, or miserable. 恢复健康（或体力、精神）的

  • She opened the door to her bedroom, thinking how restorative a hot bath would feel tonight.

    她打开了卧室的门，想着今晚洗个热水澡该多么令人惬意。

2 N-COUNT 使人恢复健康（或体力、精神）的事物 If you describe something as a restorative, you mean that it makes you feel healthier, stronger, or more cheerful after you have been feeling tired, weak, or miserable. 使人恢复健康（或体力、精神）的事物

  • Seven days off could be a wonderful restorative.

    休假7天肯定能让人精神焕发。
