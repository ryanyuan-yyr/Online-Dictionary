northerly
nɔːʳðəʳli ˈnɔrðərli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 北的;向北的 A northerly point, area, or direction is to the north or towards the north. 北的;向北的

  • Unst is the most northerly island in the British Isles.

    安斯特岛是不列颠群岛中最北的一个。

  • I wanted to go a more northerly route across Montana.

    我想走一条穿过蒙大拿州的更靠北的路线。

2 ADJ (风)从北方吹来的 A northerly wind is a wind that blows from the north. (风)从北方吹来的
