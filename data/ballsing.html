balls
bɔːlz bɔːlz bɔlz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ballses, present participle ballsing, past tense , past participle ballsed
1 N-UNCOUNT 勇气；胆识 If you say that someone has balls, you mean that they have courage. 勇气；胆识 [informal, rude, approval]

  • To work on this show you've got to have balls.

    要参与这个节目的制作，你得够大胆。

  • I never had the balls to do anything like this.

    我从来没胆量做这种事。

2 EXCLAM; N-UNCOUNT 胡说八道；屁话 You can say 'balls' or say that what someone says is balls when you think that it is stupid or wrong. 胡说八道；屁话 [British, informal, very rude, feelings]

  • What complete and utter balls!

    全是屁话！

Phrasal verbs:
balls up
