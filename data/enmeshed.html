enmeshed
ɪnmeʃt ɪnˈmɛʃt
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 被缠住的;不得脱身的;陷入…之中的 If you are enmeshed in or with something, usually something bad, you are involved in it and cannot easily escape from it. 被缠住的;不得脱身的;陷入…之中的

  • All too often they become enmeshed in deadening routines. [+ in]

    他们时常陷入枯燥的日常事务之中。

  • ...as her life gets enmeshed with Andrew's. [+ with]

    当她的生活和安德鲁的生活纠缠在一起时

SYN entangled, involved, caught, netted
