convinced
kənvɪnst kənˈvɪnst
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ, usu ADJ that] 确信的;深信的;坚信的 If you are convinced that something is true, you feel sure that it is true. 确信的;深信的;坚信的

  • He was convinced that I was part of the problem.

    他确信我就是问题的一部分。

  • He became convinced of the need for cheap editions of good quality writing. [+ of]

    他开始相信大众对优秀作品的普及本有需求。

  • I'm not convinced.

    我还是不很信服。

  • He was a convinced Communist.

    他是个坚定的共产主义者。
