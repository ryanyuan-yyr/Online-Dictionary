profuse
prəfjuːs proʊˈfjus prəˈfjus
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 大量的；丰富的；充沛的 Profuse sweating, bleeding, or vomiting is sweating, bleeding, or vomiting large amounts. 大量的；丰富的；充沛的

  • ...a remedy that produces profuse sweating.

    使人大量出汗的疗法

profusely

  • He was bleeding profusely.

    他正在大出血。

  • Profusely sweating and quivering with chills, he murmured his last words.

    出了很多汗的他打着冷战，含糊不清地说出了他的遗言。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （道歉或感谢）一再的；毫不吝惜的，过多的 If you offer profuse apologies or thanks, you apologize or thank someone a lot. （道歉或感谢）一再的；毫不吝惜的，过多的

  • Then the policeman recognised me, breaking into profuse apologies.

    然后这个警察认出了我，一个劲儿地给我道歉。

profusely

  • They were very grateful to be put right and thanked me profusely.

    他们非常感谢我纠正了他们的错误，一再地向我道谢。
