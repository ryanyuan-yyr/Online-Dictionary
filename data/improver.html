improve
ɪmpruːv ɪmˈpruv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense improves, present participle improving, past tense , past participle improved
1 V-ERG 改进;改善;增进 If something improves or if you improve it, it gets better. 改进;改善;增进

  • Both the texture and condition of your hair should improve. [VERB]

    你头发的发质与健康状况都应改善。

  • The weather is beginning to improve. [VERB]

    天气开始好转了。

  • Time won't improve the situation. [VERB noun]

    形势不会因时间而有所改善。

  • He improved their house. [VERB noun]

    他修缮了他们的房子。

SYN get better, pick up, look up [informal] , develop  
2 V-ERG 提高;(使)变得更熟练 If a skill you have improves or you improve a skill, you get better at it. 提高;(使)变得更熟练

  • Their French has improved enormously. [VERB]

    他们的法语进步很大。

  • He said he was going to improve his football. [VERB noun]

    他说他要提高足球技术。

SYN make better, perfect, polish, strengthen  
3 VERB 康复;健康好转 If you improve after an illness or an injury, your health gets better or you get stronger. 康复;健康好转

  • He had improved so much the doctor had cut his dosage. [VERB]

    他康复很快,医生已减少了用药剂量。

SYN recuperate, recover, rally, mend  
4 VERB 超过;胜过 If you improve on a previous achievement of your own or of someone else, you achieve a better standard or result. 超过;胜过

  • We need to improve on our performance against France. [VERB + on]

    我们需要比对阵法国队时表现得更好。
