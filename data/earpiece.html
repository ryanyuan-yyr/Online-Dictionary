earpiece
ɪəʳpiːs ˈɪrˌpis
Word forms: plural earpieces
1 N-COUNT （电话的）听筒;（助听器等的）耳承;耳塞 The earpiece of a telephone receiver, hearing aid, or other device is the part that you hold up to your ear or put into your ear. （电话的）听筒;（助听器等的）耳承;耳塞
2 N-COUNT 眼镜脚 The earpieces of a pair of glasses are the parts which fit over your ears to keep the glasses on. 眼镜脚
