interest rate
ˈɪntrəst reɪt
Word forms: plural interest rates
N-COUNT 利(息)率 The interest rate is the amount of interest that must be paid. It is expressed as a percentage of the amount that is borrowed or gained as profit. 利(息)率

  • The Finance Minister has renewed his call for lower interest rates.

    财政部长再次呼吁降低利率。

  • ...a forthcoming interest rate rise.

    即将到来的加息
