competent
kɒmpɪtənt ˈkɑmpətənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 能胜任的;称职的 Someone who is competent is efficient and effective. 能胜任的;称职的

  • He was a loyal, distinguished and very competent civil servant.

    他是一位敬业、优秀而且非常称职的公务员。

  • ...a competent performance.

    令人满意的表现

SYN able, skilled, capable, clever  
competently

  • The government performed competently in the face of multiple challenges.

    面临多种挑战，政府表现得非常出色。

2 ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ to-infinitive] 有…能力的;能干的 If you are competent to do something, you have the skills, abilities, or experience necessary to do it well. 有…能力的;能干的

  • Most adults do not feel competent to deal with a medical emergency involving a child.

    大部分成年人不懂得如何对儿童实施急救。

SYN fit, qualified, equal, appropriate
