balls up
PHRASAL VERB 弄砸；把…搞得一团糟 If you balls up a task or activity, you do it very badly, making a lot of mistakes. 弄砸；把…搞得一团糟 [British, informal, rude] [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • You have single-handedly ballsed up the best opportunity we've had!. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你一个人就把我们最好的机会给搞砸了！

  • I have no intention of letting you balls it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我可不想让你把事情弄得一塌糊涂。

balls-up
Word forms: plural balls-ups
N-COUNT 一团糟；烂摊子 If you make a balls-up of something, you do it very badly and make a lot of mistakes. 一团糟；烂摊子 [British, informal, rude]

  • He's made a real balls-up of this.

    他把这个弄得一团糟。
