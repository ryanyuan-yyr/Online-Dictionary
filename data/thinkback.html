think back
PHRASAL VERB 回忆;回想 If you think back, you make an effort to remember things that happened to you in the past. 回忆;回想

  • I thought back to the time in 1975 when my son was desperately ill. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    我回想起 1975 年，那时我儿子病得很重。

  • When you think back on it, do you think that it was the right thing to do?. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    现在回想起来，你觉得当时那样做对吗？

  • Thinking back, I don't know how I had the courage. [VERB PREP.]

    现在回想起来，我都搞不清我当时哪来的那股勇气。
