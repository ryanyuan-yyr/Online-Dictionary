ferret out
PHRASAL VERB 搜寻出;搜索出;找到 If you ferret out some information, you discover it by searching for it very thoroughly. 搜寻出;搜索出;找到 [informal]

  • The team is trying to ferret out missing details. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该队正在试图找到遗失的细节。

  • I leave it to the reader to ferret these out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我留给读者去发现这些。
