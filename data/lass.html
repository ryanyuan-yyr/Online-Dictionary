lass
læs læs
Word forms: plural lasses
N-COUNT; N-VOC 小姑娘；少女；小姐 A lass is a young woman or girl. 小姑娘；少女；小姐 [mainly Scottish, or Northern England]

  • Anne is a Lancashire lass from Longton, near Preston.

    安妮是兰开夏郡姑娘，老家在普雷斯顿附近的朗顿。

  • 'What is it, lass?' Finlay cried.

    “怎么回事，小姑娘？”芬利嚷道。

SYN girl, young woman, miss, bird [slang]
