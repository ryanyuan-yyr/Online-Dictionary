taken
teɪkən ˈteɪkən
1 Taken is the past participle of take .
2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 对…感兴趣的;为…所吸引的 If you are taken with something or someone, you are very interested in them or attracted to them. 对…感兴趣的;为…所吸引的 [informal]

  • She seems very taken with the idea. [+ with]

    她好像对这个想法很感兴趣。

  • I was quite taken with him when I was young.

    我年轻时被他深深吸引住了。

SYN charmed, pleased, delighted, fascinated
