bureaucracy
bjʊrɒkrəsi bjʊˈrɑkrəsi bjuˈrɑkrəsi
Word forms: plural bureaucracies
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 官僚制度 A bureaucracy is an administrative system operated by a large number of officials. 官僚制度

  • State bureaucracies can tend to stifle enterprise and initiative.

    国家的官僚体制往往会扼杀人们的事业心和积极性。

SYN government, officials, authorities, administration  
2 N-UNCOUNT 官僚作风；官僚主义 Bureaucracy refers to all the rules and procedures followed by government departments and similar organizations, especially when you think that these are complicated and cause long delays. 官僚作风；官僚主义 [disapproval]

  • People usually complain about having to deal with too much bureaucracy.

    人们经常抱怨不得不应付太多的繁文缛节。

SYN red tape, regulations, paperwork, officialdom
