get by
PHRASAL VERB 勉强过活；勉强应付 If you can get by with what you have, you can manage to live or do things in a satisfactory way. 勉强过活；勉强应付

  • I'm a survivor. I'll get by. [VERB PREP.]

    我挺过来了，我能应付下去。

  • Melville managed to get by on a small amount of money. [VERB PREP. + on]

    梅尔维尔仅靠一点点钱勉强度日。

SYN manage, survive, cope, fare
