goggle-eyed ˈgɑgəlˌaɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n, ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ] （由于吃惊或感兴趣）瞪大眼睛的 If you say that someone is goggle-eyed, you mean that they are very surprised or interested by something. （由于吃惊或感兴趣）瞪大眼睛的

  • Johnson stared goggle-eyed at Kravis' sumptuous quarters.

    约翰逊目瞪口呆地望着克拉维斯的豪宅。

2 ADJ 常看电视的 If you say that someone is goggle-eyed, you mean they watch television a lot. 常看电视的 [British, informal, disapproval]
