impasse
æmpæs ɪm- ˈɪmˌpæs ɪmˈpæs
N-SING 绝境;僵局;死胡同 If people are in a difficult position in which it is impossible to make any progress, you can refer to the situation as an impasse . 绝境;僵局;死胡同

  • The company says it has reached an impasse in negotiations with the union.

    公司称与工会的谈判已陷入僵局。

SYN deadlock, stalemate, standstill, dead end
