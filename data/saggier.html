saggy
sægi ˈsæɡɪ ˈsægi
Word forms: comparative saggier, superlative saggiest
ADJ-GRADED 下垂的;松垂的;松弛耷拉的 If you describe something as saggy, you mean that it has become less firm over a period of time and become unattractive. 下垂的;松垂的;松弛耷拉的

  • Is the mattress lumpy and saggy?.

    床垫是否高低不平并且下陷？

  • Exercise for just 20 minutes a day to firm up even the saggiest bottom.

    每天只锻炼 20 分钟，即使最松弛的臀部也会变挺翘。
