notwithstanding
nɒtwɪðstændɪŋ ˌnɒtwɪθˈstændɪŋ ˌnɑtwɪðˈstændɪŋ ˌnɑtwɪθˈstændɪŋ
PREP 尽管 If something is true notwithstanding something else, it is true in spite of that other thing. 尽管 [formal]

  • He despised William Pitt, notwithstanding the similar views they both held.

    尽管他和威廉·皮特看法相似，却瞧不起他。

SYN despite, in spite of, regardless of  
Notwithstanding is also an adverb.

  • His relations with colleagues, differences of opinion notwithstanding, were unfailingly friendly.

    尽管有意见分歧，他和同事们一直关系融洽。
