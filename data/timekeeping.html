timekeeping
taɪmkiːpɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT [adjective NOUN] 守时 If you talk about someone's timekeeping, you are talking about how good they are at arriving in time for things. 守时 [British]

  • I am trying to improve my timekeeping.

    我努力使自己变得更加守时。

  • He was penalized for bad timekeeping.

    他因不守时而受到处罚。

2 N-UNCOUNT (比赛的)计时，测定时间 Timekeeping is the process or activity of timing an event or series of events. (比赛的)计时，测定时间

  • Who did the timekeeping?

    谁负责计时？

  • …improvements in timekeeping technology.

    计时技术的提高
