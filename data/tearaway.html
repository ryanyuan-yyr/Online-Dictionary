tearaway
teərəweɪ ˈtɛərəweɪ ˈtɛrəˌweɪ
Word forms: plural tearaways
N-COUNT 小流氓;小痞子;不良青年 If you refer to a young person as a tearaway, you mean that they behave in a wild and uncontrolled way. 小流氓;小痞子;不良青年 [British]

  • He blamed lack of parental control for the young tearaways' behaviour.

    他认为，那些小流氓行为不端是因为缺少家教。

SYN hooligan, delinquent, tough, rough [informal]
