clear away
PHRASAL VERB 将…收走；把…拿开；（尤指）收拾餐具 When you clear things away or clear away, you put away the things that you have been using, especially for eating or cooking. 将…收走；把…拿开；（尤指）收拾餐具 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The waitress had cleared away the plates and brought coffee. [VERB PREP. noun]

    女服务生收拾走盘子，端来了咖啡。

  • Tania cooked, served, and cleared away. [VERB PREP.]

    塔妮娅做饭，上菜，还收拾了餐具。
