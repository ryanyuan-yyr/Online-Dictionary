citation
saɪteɪʃən saɪˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural citations
1 N-COUNT 嘉奖令;表彰 A citation is an official document or speech which praises a person for something brave or special that they have done. 嘉奖令;表彰

  • His citation says he showed outstanding and exemplary courage.

    颁给他的嘉奖令称他表现出非凡的勇气，堪为表率。

SYN commendation, award, mention  
2 N-COUNT (出自书籍等的)引述，引文 A citation from a book or other piece of writing is a passage or phrase from it. (出自书籍等的)引述，引文 [formal]
3 N-COUNT 同 summons A citation is the same as a summons . 同 summons [US]

  • The court could issue a citation and fine Ms. Robbins.

    法庭可能会发传票，对罗宾斯女士处以罚款。
