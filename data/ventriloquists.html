ventriloquist
ventrɪləkwɪst vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst
Word forms: plural ventriloquists
N-COUNT 腹语术表演者;口技表演者 A ventriloquist is someone who can speak without moving their lips and who entertains people by making their words appear to be spoken by a puppet 腹语术表演者;口技表演者

  • He looked like a ventriloquist's dummy.

    他看上去像腹语术表演者用的人偶。
