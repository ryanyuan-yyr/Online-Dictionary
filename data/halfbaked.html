half-baked
ˈhæfˌbeɪkt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （想法、计划等）考虑不周的，愚蠢的，不切实际的 If you describe an idea or plan as half-baked, you mean that it has not been properly thought out, and so is stupid or impractical. （想法、计划等）考虑不周的，愚蠢的，不切实际的 [disapproval]

  • This is another half-baked scheme that isn't going to work.

    这又是一个不靠谱的计划，是不会成功的。

  • I didn't want to make things worse by coming up with half-baked notions.

    我可不想一拍脑门想出些馊主意而把事情搞得更糟。

SYN stupid, impractical, crazy [informal] , silly
