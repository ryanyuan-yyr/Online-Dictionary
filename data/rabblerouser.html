rabble-rouser
ˈræbəlˌraʊzər
Word forms: plural rabble-rousers
N-COUNT 煽动者；蛊惑人心者 A rabble-rouser is a clever speaker who can persuade a group of people to behave violently or aggressively, often for the speaker's own political advantage. 煽动者；蛊惑人心者 [disapproval]
SYN agitator, troublemaker, incendiary, firebrand
