educated
edʒʊkeɪtɪd ˈɛdʒuˌkeɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 受过教育的;有教养的 Someone who is educated has a high standard of learning. 受过教育的;有教养的

  • He is an educated, amiable and decent man.

    他是个有教养、和蔼可亲的正人君子。

SYN cultured, lettered, intellectual, learned  
-educated -edʒʊkeɪtɪd
1 COMB in ADJ （与名词和形容词连用构成形容词）表示“在…受教育的”，“在…读书的” -educated combines with nouns and adjectives to form adjectives indicating where someone was educated. （与名词和形容词连用构成形容词）表示“在…受教育的”，“在…读书的”

  • ...the Oxford-educated son of a Liverpool merchant.

    在牛津受过教育的利物浦商人之子

  • ...an American-educated lawyer.

    在美国接受教育的律师
