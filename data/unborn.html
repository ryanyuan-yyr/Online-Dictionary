unborn
ʌnbɔːʳn ʌnˈbɔːn ʌnˈbɔrn
ADJ 未降生的；未出世的 An unborn child has not yet been born and is still inside its mother's womb. 未降生的；未出世的

  • ...her unborn baby.

    她未出世的婴儿

  • They will affect generations of Britons still unborn.

    他们将会影响还未出世的好几代英国人。

SYN expected, awaited, embryonic, in utero  
The unborn are children who are not born yet.未降生的婴儿；未出世的孩子
