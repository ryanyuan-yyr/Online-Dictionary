repay
rɪpeɪ rɪˈpeɪ rɪˈpeɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense repays, present participle repaying, past tense , past participle repaid
1 VERB 偿还，偿付(贷款或债务) If you repay a loan or a debt, you pay back the money that you owe to the person who you borrowed or took it from. 偿还，偿付(贷款或债务)

  • It will take 30 years to repay the loan. [VERB noun]

    还清贷款需要30年的时间。

  • He advanced funds of his own to his company, which was unable to repay him. [VERB noun]

    他把自己的资金预付给他的公司，而公司却不能把钱偿还给他。

SYN pay back, refund, settle up, return  
2 VERB 报答，回报（恩惠等） If you repay a favour that someone did for you, you do something for them in return. 报答，回报（恩惠等）

  • It was very kind. I don't know how I can ever repay you. [VERB noun]

    真是太好了。我都不知道怎么报答你才好。

  • I owe them a debt that cannot easily be repaid. [VERB noun]

    我欠他们一笔钱，不容易偿还。
