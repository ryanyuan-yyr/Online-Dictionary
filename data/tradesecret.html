trade secret
Word forms: plural trade secrets
1 N-COUNT 行业秘密;商业机密 A trade secret is information that is known, used, and kept secret by a particular firm, for example about a method of production or a chemical process. 行业秘密;商业机密

  • The nature of the polymer is currently a trade secret.

    这一聚合物的性质目前是个商业机密。

2 N-COUNT (尤指怎样做某事的)秘密 A trade secret is a piece of knowledge that you have, especially about how to do something, that you are not willing to tell other people. (尤指怎样做某事的)秘密

  • I'd rather not talk about it too much because I don't like giving trade secrets away.

    我不愿就此多谈，因为我不想泄露秘密。
