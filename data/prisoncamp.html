prison camp
ˈprɪzən kæmp
Word forms: plural prison camps
1 N-COUNT 政治犯集中营；战俘营 A prison camp is a guarded camp where prisoners of war or political prisoners are kept. 政治犯集中营；战俘营

  • He was shot down over Denmark and spent three years in a prison camp.

    他在丹麦上空被击落，在集中营呆了3年。

2 N-COUNT （囚禁一般犯人并允许其外出服劳役的）拘禁营地 A prison camp is a prison where the prisoners are not considered dangerous and are allowed to work outside the prison. （囚禁一般犯人并允许其外出服劳役的）拘禁营地 [US]
