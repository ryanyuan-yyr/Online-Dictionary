unceasing
ʌnsiːsɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不停的；持续的；连续不断的 If you describe something as unceasing, you are emphasizing that it continues without stopping. 不停的；持续的；连续不断的 [emphasis]

  • ...the unceasing quest for more speed.

    不断追求更高的速度

  • ...his unceasing labours.

    他夜以继日的操劳

SYN continual, constant, incessant, continuing  
unceasingly

  • Paul talked unceasingly from dawn to dusk.

    保罗滔滔不绝地从早说到晚。
