poet laureate
poʊɪt lɒriət - lɔːr-
Word forms: plural poet laureates, plural poets laureate
N-COUNT 桂冠诗人（在英国可终身享受政府薪俸，在美国则有一定时限） The poet laureate is the official poet of a country. In Britain the poet laureate is paid by the government for the rest of their life. In the United States they are paid for a fixed period. 桂冠诗人（在英国可终身享受政府薪俸，在美国则有一定时限）
