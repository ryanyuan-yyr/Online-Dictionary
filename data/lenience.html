leniency
liːniənsi
N-UNCOUNT 仁慈；宽大 Leniency is a lenient attitude or lenient behaviour. 仁慈；宽大 [Also + to/towards]

  • The judge rejected pleas for leniency and sentenced him to six months in prison.

    法官驳回了宽大处理的请求，判处他6个月的监禁。

  • He said he would show no leniency towards those who stirred up trouble between Muslims and Christians.

    他说自己绝不轻饶那些在穆斯林和基督徒之间挑起事端的人。

SYN mercy, compassion, clemency, quarter
