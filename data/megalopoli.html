megalopolis
mɛgəlɒpəlɪs ˌmɛɡəˈlɒpəlɪs ˌmɛgəˈlɑpəlɪs
Word forms: megalopolises, megalopoli
N-COUNT [usu sing] 特大城市; 特大城市带A megalopolis is a very large city, or an urban area that consists of several towns and cities. 特大城市; 特大城市带

  • ...the densely populated megalopolis that stretches down the East Coast from Boston to Washington.

    ...沿着从波士顿到华盛顿的东海岸连绵不断、人口稠密的特大城市带。
