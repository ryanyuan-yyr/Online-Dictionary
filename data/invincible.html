invincible
ɪnvɪnsɪbəl ɪnˈvɪnsəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 不可战胜的;所向披靡的 If you describe an army or sports team as invincible, you believe that they cannot be defeated. 不可战胜的;所向披靡的

  • When Sotomayor is on form he is virtually invincible.

    索托马约尔状态好时几乎无人可敌。

SYN unbeatable, unassailable, indomitable, unyielding  
invincibility

  • ...symbols of the invincibility of the Roman army.

    罗马军队不可战胜的象征

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (信念、态度)不可动摇的，不可改变的 If someone has an invincible belief or attitude, it cannot be changed. (信念、态度)不可动摇的，不可改变的

  • He also had an invincible faith in the medicinal virtues of garlic.

    他同时也对大蒜的医学功效深信不疑。
