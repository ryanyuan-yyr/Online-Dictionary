reinforcement
riːɪnfɔːʳsmənt ˌriɪnfɔrsmənt
Word forms: plural reinforcements
1 N-PLURAL 援军;增援部队 Reinforcements are soldiers or policemen who are sent to join an army or group of police in order to make it stronger. 援军;增援部队

  • ...the despatch of police and troop reinforcements.

    派遣警察和军队支援

2 N-VAR 增强;加强;加固 The reinforcement of something is the process of making it stronger. 增强;加强;加固

  • I am sure that this meeting will contribute to the reinforcement of peace and security all over the world. [+ of]

    我相信这次会议将会促进世界范围内的和平与安全。

  • What the teacher now has to do is remove the reinforcement for this bad behaviour.

    现在老师要做的是避免这种不良行为更加严重。

  • There are reinforcement bars on all doors.

    所有的门上都有钢筋条加固。

SYN support, stay, shore, prop
