parking
pɑːʳkɪŋ ˈpɑːkɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 停车；泊车 Parking is the action of moving a vehicle into a place in a car park or by the side of the road where it can be left. 停车；泊车

  • In many towns parking is allowed only on one side of the street.

    很多小镇只允许将车停在街道的一侧。

  • I knew I'd never find a parking space in the Square.

    我知道在广场上肯定找不到停车位。

2 N-UNCOUNT 停车位 Parking is space for parking a vehicle in. 停车位

  • Cars allowed, but parking is limited.

    车辆准许进入，但是车位有限。
