outflow
aʊtfloʊ ˈaʊtˌfloʊ
Word forms: plural outflows
N-COUNT （金钱、人等的）外流，流出 When there is an outflow of money or people, a large amount of money or people move from one place to another. （金钱、人等的）外流，流出

  • There was a net outflow of about £50m in short-term capital. [+ of]

    短期资金净流出额大约为5,000万英镑。

  • ...an increasing outflow of refugees. [+ of]

    越来越多出逃的难民

SYN stream, issue, flow, rush
