malinger
məlɪŋgəʳ məˈlɪŋgər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense malingers, present participle malingering, past tense , past participle malingered
VERB [usually cont] （为逃避工作而）装病 If someone is malingering, they pretend to be ill in order to avoid working. （为逃避工作而）装病 [disapproval]

  • She was told by her doctor that she was malingering. [VERB]

    医生告诉她，她这是在装病。
