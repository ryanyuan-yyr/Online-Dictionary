body search
(also body-search)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense body searches, present participle body searching, past tense body searched
VERB 对…搜身；对…进行搜身检查 If a person is body searched, someone such as a police officer searches them while they remain clothed. 对…搜身；对…进行搜身检查

  • Foreign journalists were body-searched by airport police. [be VERB-ed]

    外国记者被机场警察搜身。

Body search is also a noun.

  • Fans may undergo body searches by security guards.

    球迷可能要接受保安人员的搜身检查。
