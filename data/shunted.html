shunt
ʃʌnt ʃʌnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shunts, present participle shunting, past tense , past participle shunted
1 VERB [usually passive] (通常因为不方便而)将…转移至别处，将…调往别处 If a person or thing is shunted somewhere, they are moved or sent there, usually because someone finds them inconvenient. (通常因为不方便而)将…转移至别处，将…调往别处 [disapproval]

  • He has spent most of his life being shunted between his mother, father and various foster families. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    他一生中的大部分时间都在他母亲、父亲及各种各样的寄养家庭之间辗转度过。

  • Independent thinkers are shunted into minor jobs or refused promotion. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    见解独到的人士要么被调往次要的岗位，要么就不被提升。

2 VERB 使(火车车皮或车厢)转轨 When railway engines shunt wagons or carriages, they push or pull them from one railway line to another. 使(火车车皮或车厢)转轨

  • The GM diesel engine shunted the coaches to Platform 4. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    通用柴油动力火车头将车厢转轨到了 4 号月台。
