shipwreck
ʃɪprek ˈʃɪpˌrɛk
Word forms: plural shipwrecks, past participle shipwrecked
1 N-VAR 船舶失事;船只遇难;海难 If there is a shipwreck, a ship is destroyed in an accident at sea. 船舶失事;船只遇难;海难

  • He was drowned in a shipwreck off the coast of Spain.

    他在西班牙海岸附近的一次海难中溺水身亡。

  • ...the perils of storm and shipwreck.

    发生暴风雨和海难的危险

2 N-COUNT 遇难船只;失事船只;沉船 A shipwreck is a ship which has been destroyed in an accident at sea. 遇难船只;失事船只;沉船
3 V-PASSIVE 使(人)遭遇海难 If someone is shipwrecked, their ship is destroyed in an accident at sea but they survive and manage to reach land. 使(人)遭遇海难

  • He was shipwrecked after visiting the island. [be VERB-ed]

    他在到那个岛上观光后就遭遇了海难。

  • The shipwrecked couple were rescued by two fishermen. [VERB-ed]

    遭遇海难的那对夫妻被两名渔夫救起。
