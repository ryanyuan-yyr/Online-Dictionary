viewpoint
vjuːpɔɪnt vjuˌpɔɪnt
Word forms: plural viewpoints
1 N-COUNT 观点;看法；角度 Someone's viewpoint is the way that they think about things in general, or the way they think about a particular thing. 观点;看法；角度

  • The novel is shown from the girl's viewpoint.

    这部小说是从这个女孩的视角来叙述的。

  • To include as many viewpoints as possible, the editor reserves the right to shorten letters.

    为了能包括尽可能多的观点，编辑保留对信件进行删节的权力。

SYN point of view, perspective, angle, position  
2 N-COUNT 视角;角度；观察点 A viewpoint is a place from which you can get a good view of something. 视角;角度；观察点

  • You have to know where to stand for a good viewpoint.

    你得知道站在哪里观察角度比较理想。
