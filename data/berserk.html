berserk
bəʳzɜːʳk -sɜːʳk bəˈzɜːk bərˈsɜrk bərˈzɜrk bəˈsɜrk
1 ADJ-GRADED 发疯的；失控的 Berserk means crazy and out of control. 发疯的；失控的

  • He tossed back his head in a howl of berserk laughter.

    他仰天狂笑。

SYN crazy, wild, mad [informal] , frantic  
2 PHRASE 暴跳如雷；发狂 If someone or something goes berserk, they lose control of themselves and become very angry or violent. 暴跳如雷；发狂

  • When I saw him I went berserk.

    我一看见他就冒火。
