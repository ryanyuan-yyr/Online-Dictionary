briefing
briːfɪŋ ˈbrifɪŋ
Word forms: plural briefings
1 N-VAR 情况介绍会；任务下达会 A briefing is a meeting at which information or instructions are given to people, especially before they do something. 情况介绍会；任务下达会

  • They're holding a press briefing tomorrow.

    他们明天将召开一个新闻发布会。

  • Security staff did not then receive any briefing before they started each shift.

    保安人员当时开始轮岗前没有收到任何指示。

SYN conference, meeting, priming, question and answer session
