neigh
neɪ neɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense neighs, present participle neighing, past tense , past participle neighed
VERB (马)嘶鸣,嘶 When a horse neighs, it makes a loud sound with its mouth. (马)嘶鸣,嘶

  • The mare neighed once more, turned and disappeared amongst the trees. [VERB]

    母马又嘶鸣了一声，扭头消失在树林里。

Neigh is also a noun.

  • The horse gave a loud neigh.

    这匹马大声嘶叫。
