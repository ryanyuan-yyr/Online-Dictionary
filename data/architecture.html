architecture
ɑːʳkɪtektʃəʳ ˈɑrkəˌtɛktʃər
Word forms: plural architectures
1 N-UNCOUNT 建筑学;建筑术 Architecture is the art of planning, designing, and constructing buildings. 建筑学;建筑术

  • He studied classical architecture and design in Rome.

    他在罗马学习了古典建筑学和设计。

SYN design, planning, building, construction  
2 N-UNCOUNT 建筑式样；建筑风格 The architecture of a building is the style in which it is designed and constructed. 建筑式样；建筑风格

  • ...modern architecture.

    现代建筑式样

  • ...a fine example of Moroccan architecture.

    摩洛哥建筑风格的典范

  • ...the architecture of the city's buildings.

    该城市建筑物的建筑风格

SYN construction, design, style  
3 N-UNCOUNT 结构;架构 The architecture of something is its structure. 结构;架构 [formal]

  • ...the crumbling intellectual architecture of modern society. [+ of]

    现代社会处于崩溃边缘的知识结构

  • ...the architecture of muscle fibres.

    肌肉纤维的结构

SYN structure, design, shape, make-up
