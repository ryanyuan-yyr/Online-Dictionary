ticketless
tɪkɪtlɪs ˈtɪkɪtləs
1 ADJ 没有票的Someone who is ticketless does not have a ticket for a particular event such as a concert or a sports game. 没有票的

  • The band begged ticketless fans to stay away.

    乐队恳求没有票的乐迷们离开。

2 ADJ (销售系统等)无票的A ticketless system is a way of buying something, such as a seat on an aircraft, without being given a ticket. (销售系统等)无票的

  • ...a ticketless reservation system.

    ...无票预订系统。
