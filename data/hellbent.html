hell-bent
(also hellbent)
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 决意的;不顾一切的 If you say that someone is hell-bent on doing something, you are emphasizing that they are determined to do it, even if this causes problems or difficulties for other people. 决意的;不顾一切的 [emphasis]

  • He accused Ford of being hell-bent on achieving its cuts by whatever means. [+ on]

    他指责福特公司不择手段一心要实现削减。

  • ...a side hell-bent on a place in the Premier League.

    决意打入英超联赛的球队
