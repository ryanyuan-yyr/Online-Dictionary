gratin
grætəŋ ɡratɛ̃ ˈgrɑtən ˈgrætən gʀaˈtɛ̃
Word forms: plural gratins
N-VAR 脆皮烙菜（表面覆以奶酪或奶汁） A gratin is a dish containing vegetables or sometimes meat or fish. It is covered with cheese or cheese sauce and baked in the oven. 脆皮烙菜（表面覆以奶酪或奶汁）

  • ...fresh salmon with potato and cheese gratin.
