bemused
bɪmjuːzd
ADJ-GRADED 困惑不解的；茫然的 If you are bemused, you are puzzled or confused. 困惑不解的；茫然的

  • He was rather bemused by children.

    他为孩子们大伤脑筋。

  • Mr. Sebastian was looking at the boys with a bemused expression.

    塞巴斯蒂安先生一脸茫然地看着男孩儿们。

SYN puzzled, stunned, confused, stumped  
bemusedly

  • He was staring bemusedly at the picture of himself.

    他茫然地盯着自己的照片。
