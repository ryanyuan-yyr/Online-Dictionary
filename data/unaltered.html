unaltered
ʌnɔːltəʳd ʌnˈɔːltəd
ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] 未改变的；照旧的 Something that remains unaltered has not changed or been changed. 未改变的；照旧的

  • The rest of the apartment had fortunately remained unaltered since that time.

    幸运的是，从那时起公寓的其余部分就没有变过。

  • These were my opinions, and they continue unaltered.

    这是我以前的看法，现在仍旧没有变。
