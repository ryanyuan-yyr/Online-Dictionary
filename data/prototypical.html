prototypical
proʊtətɪpɪkəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 模范的；典型的 Prototypical is used to indicate that someone or something is a very typical example of a type of person or thing. 模范的；典型的 [formal]

  • Park Ridge is the prototypical American suburb.

    帕克里奇是美国郊区的典型。

  • ...a prototypical socialist.

    典型的社会主义者
