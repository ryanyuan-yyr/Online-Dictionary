natural selection
N-UNCOUNT (生物的)自然选择，物竞天择，适者生存 Natural selection is a process by which species of animals and plants that are best adapted to their environment survive and reproduce, while those that are less well adapted die out. (生物的)自然选择，物竞天择，适者生存

  • Natural selection ensures only the fittest survive to pass their genes on to the next generation.

    自然选择确保只有最适应环境的物种得以存活下来，把基因传给下一代。
