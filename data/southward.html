southward
saʊθwəʳd ˈsaʊθwərd ˈsʌðərd
(also southwards)
ADV [usually ADVERB after verb, oft n ADV] 向南 Southward or southwards means towards the south. 向南

  • They drove southward.

    他们驾车南行。

  • It was a visit that took him to Mogadishu and southwards to Kismayo.

    由于一次访问的缘故，他来到了摩加迪沙，又向南来到基斯马尤。

Southward is also an adjective.

  • Instead of her normal southward course towards Alexandria and home, she headed west.

    她没有像往常一样选择前往亚历山大和回家的南行线路，而是向西走了。
