bead
biːd bid
Word forms: plural beads
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 珠子；小珠 Beads are small pieces of coloured glass, wood, or plastic with a hole through the middle. Beads are often put together on a piece of string or wire to make jewellery. 珠子；小珠

  • ...a string of beads.

    一串珠子

  • ...a multicoloured bead necklace.

    一串彩珠项链

2 N-COUNT （液体的）小珠，小滴 A bead of liquid or moisture is a small drop of it. （液体的）小珠，小滴

  • ...beads of blood. [+ of]

    血滴

  • He wiped away the beads of sweat on his forehead. [+ of]

    他擦去了额头上的汗珠。

SYN drop, tear, bubble, pearl
