bravado
brəvɑːdoʊ brəˈvɑdoʊ
N-UNCOUNT 虚张声势；逞能；逞强 Bravado is an appearance of courage or confidence that someone shows in order to impress other people. 虚张声势；逞能；逞强

  • 'You won't get away with this,' he said with unexpected bravado.

    “这事你休想就这么过去，”他以出人意料的强横语气说道。

SYN swagger, boast, boasting, swaggering
