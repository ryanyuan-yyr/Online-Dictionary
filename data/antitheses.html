antithesis
æntɪθəsɪs ænˈtɪθəsɪs
Word forms: plural antitheses/æntɪθəsiːz/
1 N-COUNT 对立面；形成对立（或对照）的事物 The antithesis of something is its exact opposite. 对立面；形成对立（或对照）的事物 [formal]

  • The little black dress is the anithesis of fussy dressing. [+ of]

    与花哨的打扮形成鲜明对比的是这件小黑裙子。

2 N-COUNT （二者间的）对比，对照 If there is an antithesis between two things, there is a contrast between them. （二者间的）对比，对照 [formal]

  • ...the antithesis between instinct and reason.

    本能和理智的对立

SYN contrast, opposition, contradiction, reversal
