picture rail
ˈpɪktʃə reɪl
(also picture-rail)Word forms: plural picture rails
N-COUNT （嵌在天花板下方墙壁四周供挂画用的）挂镜线，画镜线 A picture rail is a continuous narrow piece of wood which is fixed round a room just below the ceiling. Pictures can be hung from it using string and hooks. （嵌在天花板下方墙壁四周供挂画用的）挂镜线，画镜线 [mainly British]
