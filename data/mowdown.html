mow down
PHRASAL VERB (车辆或炮火)撂倒，扫灭，杀死 If someone is mown down, they are killed violently by a vehicle or gunfire. (车辆或炮火)撂倒，扫灭，杀死 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She was mown down on a pedestrian crossing. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    她在人行横道上被撞死了。

  • Gunmen mowed down 10 people in one attack. [VERB PREP. noun]

    持枪歹徒在一次袭击中射杀了10个人。
