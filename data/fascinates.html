fascinate
fæsɪneɪt ˈfæsəˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fascinates, present participle fascinating, past tense , past participle fascinated
VERB 使着迷;迷住 If something fascinates you, it interests and delights you so much that your thoughts tend to concentrate on it. 使着迷;迷住

  • Politics fascinated Franklin's father. [VERB noun]

    政治让富兰克林的父亲着迷。

  • She fascinated him, both on and off stage. [VERB noun]

    不管是台上还是台下的她，都让他着迷。

SYN entrance, delight, charm, absorb
