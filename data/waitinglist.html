waiting list
Word forms: plural waiting lists
N-COUNT [oft on NOUN] 等候者名单 A waiting list is a list of people who have asked for something which cannot be given to them immediately, for example medical treatment, housing, or training, and who must therefore wait until it is available. 等候者名单

  • There were 20,000 people on the waiting list for a home. [+ for]

    名单上有两万人等着分房。
