lippy
lɪpi ˈlɪpi
Word forms: plural lippies
1 ADJ-GRADED 出言不逊的；冒犯顶撞的 If someone is lippy, they speak to other people in a way that shows no respect. 出言不逊的；冒犯顶撞的 [British, informal]

  • Bruce Willis plays a lippy cop battling it out with a female partner.

    布鲁斯·威利斯扮演一个言辞莽撞、与女搭档唇枪舌剑的警察。

2 N-MASS （lipstick 的缩约形式） Lippy is short for lipstick. （lipstick 的缩约形式） [British, informal]
