lean towards
PHRASAL VERB 倾向于；偏向于 If you lean towards or lean toward a particular idea, belief, or type of behaviour, you have a tendency to think or act in a particular way. 倾向于；偏向于

  • Politically, I lean towards the right. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政治上我属于右倾。

  • Most scientists would probably lean toward this viewpoint. [VERB PREP. noun]

    大多数科学家可能会倾向于这个观点。
