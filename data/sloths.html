sloth
sloʊθ sloʊθ slɔθ slɑθ
Word forms: plural sloths
1 N-UNCOUNT 懒惰，怠惰(尤指工作方面) Sloth is laziness, especially with regard to work. 懒惰，怠惰(尤指工作方面) [formal]

  • He admitted a lack of motivation and a feeling of sloth.

    他承认自己缺乏动力，总想犯懒。

2 N-COUNT 树懒(见于中美洲和南美洲) A sloth is an animal from Central and South America. Sloths live in trees and move very slowly. 树懒(见于中美洲和南美洲)
