effectual
ɪfektʃuəl ɛˈfɛktʃuəl ɪˈfɛktʃuəl iˈfɛktʃuəl əˈfɛktʃuəl
ADJ-GRADED （行动、计划等）有效的，奏效的 If an action or plan is effectual, it succeeds in producing the results that were intended. （行动、计划等）有效的，奏效的 [formal]

  • This is the only effectual way to secure our present and future happiness.

    这是确保我们现在及以后都会幸福的唯一有效途径。
