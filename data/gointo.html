go into
1 PHRASAL VERB 描述；叙述；调查；研究 If you go into something, you describe or examine it fully or in detail. 描述；叙述；调查；研究

  • It was a private conversation and I don't want to go into details about what was said. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那是私人之间的谈话，我不想透露其中的细节。

SYN investigate, consider, study, research  
2 PHRASAL VERB 从事（某工作或职业） If you go into something, you decide to do it as your job or career. 从事（某工作或职业）

  • Mr Pok has now gone into the tourism business. [VERB PREP. noun]

    波克先生现在从事旅游业。

SYN enter, begin, participate in  
3 PHRASAL VERB （时间、精力或钱）被用在 If an amount of time, effort, or money goes into something, it is spent or used to do it, get it, or make it. （时间、精力或钱）被用在

  • Is there a lot of effort and money going into this sort of research? [VERB PREP. noun]

    这种研究目前是否有大量的人力和资金投入？
