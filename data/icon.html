icon
aɪkɒn ˈaɪˌkɑn
Word forms: plural icons
1 N-COUNT 偶像;代表人物 If you describe something or someone as an icon, you mean that they are important as a symbol of a particular thing. 偶像;代表人物

  • Only Marilyn has proved as enduring a fashion icon.

    只有玛丽莲是常青的时尚偶像。

  • ...Picasso and the other icons of modernism.

    毕加索和现代主义的其他代表人物

  • ...the greatest icon of this century.

    本世纪最伟大的偶像人物

2 N-COUNT 圣像(如基督、圣母马利亚等) An icon is a picture of Christ, his mother, or a saint painted on a wooden panel. 圣像(如基督、圣母马利亚等)
3 N-COUNT 图标 An icon is a picture on a computer screen representing a particular computer function. If you want to use it, you move the cursor onto the icon using a mouse. 图标 [computing]
