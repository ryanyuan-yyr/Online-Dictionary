seasoned
siːzənd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 富有经验的;老练的 You can use seasoned to describe a person who has a lot of experience of something. For example, a seasoned traveller is a person who has travelled a lot. 富有经验的;老练的

  • The author is a seasoned academic.

    作者是一位经验丰富的学者。

  • He began acting with the confidence of a seasoned performer.

    作为一名久经历练的表演者，他满怀自信地开始演出。

SYN experienced, veteran, mature, practised
