give out
1 PHRASAL VERB 分发；散发 If you give out a number of things, you distribute them among a group of people. 分发；散发 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • There were people at the entrance giving out leaflets. [VERB PREP. noun]

    有人在入口处散发传单。

SYN distribute, issue, deliver, circulate  
2 PHRASAL VERB 公开，宣布（消息等） If you give out information, you make it known to people. 公开，宣布（消息等）

  • He wouldn't give out any information. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他不愿透露任何消息。

  • How often do you give your phone number out? [VERB noun PREP.]

    你经常把自己的电话告诉别人吗？

SYN make known, announce, publish, broadcast  
3 PHRASAL VERB （设备或身体部位）停止运转，停止运行 If a piece of equipment or part of the body gives out, it stops working. （设备或身体部位）停止运转，停止运行

  • All machines give out eventually. [VERB PREP.]

    最终，所有的机器都停止了运转。

  • One of his lungs gave out entirely. [VERB PREP.]

    他的其中一片肺叶完全不起作用了。

4 PHRASAL VERB 发出(尖叫、叹息等） If you give out something such as a scream or a sigh, you sigh, scream, or make some other sound. 发出(尖叫、叹息等） [written]

  • He gave out a scream of pain. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他疼得大叫了一声。
