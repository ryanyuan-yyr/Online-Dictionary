negation
nɪgeɪʃən nɪˈgeɪʃən
1 N-SING 反面;对立面;否定 The negation of something is its complete opposite or something which destroys it or makes it lose its effect. 反面;对立面;否定 [formal]

  • Unintelligible legislation is the negation of the rule of law and of parliamentary democracy. [+ of]

    晦涩的法律法规有悖于法治和议会民主。

SYN opposite, reverse, contrary, contradiction  
2 N-UNCOUNT 否定;否认;拒绝 Negation is disagreement, refusal, or denial. 否定;否认;拒绝 [formal]

  • The editor grimaced, gesturing in negation.

    编辑一脸苦相，做了个拒绝的手势。

  • Irena shook her head, but in bewilderment, not negation.

    伊连娜摇了摇头，是困惑，而非拒绝。

SYN denial, refusal, rejection, contradiction
