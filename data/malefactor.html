malefactor
mælɪfæktəʳ ˈmæləˌfæktər
Word forms: plural malefactors
N-COUNT 罪犯;坏人 A malefactor is someone who has done something bad or illegal. 罪犯;坏人 [formal]

  • ...a well-known criminal lawyer who had saved many a malefactor from going to jail.

    一位使许多犯罪分子避免锒铛入狱的著名刑事律师

SYN wrongdoer, criminal, convict, offender
