fleshy
fleʃi ˈflɛʃi
1 ADJ-GRADED 多肉的;肥胖的 If you describe someone as fleshy, you mean that they are slightly too fat. 多肉的;肥胖的

  • He was well-built, but too fleshy to be impressive.

    他肌肉发达，但是太胖了，没给人留下好印象。

SYN plump, fat, chubby, obese  
2 ADJ-GRADED 肉厚的;肉质的;多果肉的 Fleshy parts of the body or fleshy plants are thick and soft. 肉厚的;肉质的;多果肉的

  • ...the fleshy part of the thigh.

    大腿肉多的部分

  • ...fleshy fruits like apples, plums, pears, peaches.

    苹果、李子、梨和桃等果肉多的水果
