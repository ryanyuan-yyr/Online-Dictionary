harrowing
hæroʊɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 折磨人的;烦人的;令人无比痛苦的 A harrowing experience is extremely upsetting or disturbing. 折磨人的;烦人的;令人无比痛苦的

  • You've had a harrowing time this past month.

    过去的这个月你过得太痛苦了。

  • ...harrowing pictures of the children who had been murdered.

    被杀儿童的那些令人肝肠寸断的照片

SYN distressing, disturbing, alarming, frightening
