spruce up
PHRASAL VERB 装扮;打扮 If something is spruced up, its appearance is improved. If someone is spruced up, they have made themselves look very smart. 装扮;打扮

  • Many buildings have been spruced up. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    很多大楼都被装扮一新。

  • In the evening we spruced ourselves up a bit and went out for dinner. [VERB noun PREP.]

    到了晚上，我们稍微收拾打扮了一番，然后出去吃晚餐。
