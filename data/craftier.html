crafty
krɑːfti kræfti ˈkræfti ˈkrɑfti
Word forms: comparative craftier, superlative craftiest
ADJ-GRADED 巧妙的;狡诈的;诡计多端的 If you describe someone as crafty, you mean that they achieve what they want in a clever way, often by deceiving people. 巧妙的;狡诈的;诡计多端的

  • ...a crafty, lying character who enjoys plotting against others.

    狡猾奸诈、满嘴谎言而且爱算计人的家伙

  • A crafty look came to his eyes.

    他眼中闪出狡黠的神情。

  • That was my crafty little plan.

    那是我的小妙计。

SYN cunning, scheming, sly, devious  
craftily

  • The government has craftily put up all the hidden taxes.

    政府巧妙地提高了所有隐蔽税。
