ingratitude
ɪngrætɪtjuːd -tuːd ɪnˈgrætɪˌtud ɪnˈgrætɪˌtjud
N-UNCOUNT 忘恩负义 Ingratitude is lack of gratitude for something that has been done for you. 忘恩负义

  • The Government could expect only ingratitude from the electorate. [+ from]

    政府从选民那里得到的只有忘恩负义。

SYN ungratefulness, thanklessness, lack of recognition, unappreciativeness
