launch into
PHRASAL VERB 积极投入；猛烈展开 If you launch into something such as a speech, task, or fight, you enthusiastically start it. 积极投入；猛烈展开

  • Horrigan launched into a speech about the importance of new projects. [VERB PREP. noun]

    霍里根就新项目的重要性慷慨陈词。

  • Geoff has launched himself into fatherhood with great enthusiasm. [V pron-refl P n]

    杰夫满怀热情地做起父亲来。

SYN start enthusiastically, begin, initiate, embark on
