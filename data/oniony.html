onion
ʌnjən ˈʌnjən
Word forms: plural onions
N-VAR 洋葱；洋葱头 An onion is a round vegetable with a brown skin that grows underground. It has many white layers on its inside which have a strong, sharp smell and taste. 洋葱；洋葱头

  • Will you chop an onion up for me?.

    你能帮我把一个洋葱切碎吗？

  • It is made with fresh minced meat, cooked with onion and a rich tomato sauce.

    这道菜用鲜肉沫加洋葱和浓郁的番茄酱做成。

  • ...onion soup.

    洋葱汤
