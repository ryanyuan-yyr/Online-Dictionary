wordless
wɜːʳdləs ˈwɜrdlɪs
1 ADJ (尤指期待某人说话时)无言的，沉默的 You say that someone is wordless when they do not say anything, especially at a time when they are expected to say something. (尤指期待某人说话时)无言的，沉默的 [literary]

  • She stared back, now wordless.

    她凝视着后面，一时无语。

  • Here and there, husbands sit in wordless despair.

    丈夫们各处坐着，默默无言，绝望至极。

wordlessly

  • Gil downed his food wordlessly, his attention far away.

    吉尔一声不吭地吃着，心已在千里之外。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (声音)无言的，不含词语的 If someone makes a wordless sound, they make a sound that does not seem to contain any words. (声音)无言的，不含词语的 [literary]

  • ...a wordless chant.

    没有歌词的咏唱

  • He shrieked a long, wordless cry.

    他只是无语地发出长长的一声尖叫。
