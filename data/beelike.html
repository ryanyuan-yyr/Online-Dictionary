bee
biː bi
Word forms: plural bees
1 N-COUNT 蜜蜂 A bee is an insect with a yellow-and-black striped body that makes a buzzing noise as it flies. Bees make honey, and can sting. 蜜蜂
2 PHRASE 痴迷于…；对…念念不忘 If you have a bee in your bonnet about something, you are so enthusiastic or worried about it that you keep mentioning it or thinking about it. 痴迷于…；对…念念不忘

  • He's got a bee in his bonnet about factory farming. [+ about]

    他满脑子都是工厂化养殖的事儿。

3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] （进行缝纫等比赛或集体活动的）聚会 A bee is a social event where people get together for a competition or to do something such as sew. （进行缝纫等比赛或集体活动的）聚会 [US]

  • That year I won first prize in the spelling bee.

    那年，我在单词拼写比赛中得了第一名。

  • ...a group of friends at a quilting bee.

    大家缝聚会上的一群好友
