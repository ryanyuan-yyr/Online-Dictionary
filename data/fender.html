fender

4 N-COUNT (汽车的)保险杠，缓冲装置 The fender of a car is a bar at the front or back that protects the car if it bumps into something. (汽车的)保险杠，缓冲装置 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 bumper
5 N-COUNT (船只的)护舷 The fenders of a boat are objects which hang against the outside and protect it from damage when it comes next to a harbour wall or another boat. (船只的)护舷
