fashionable
fæʃənəbəl ˈfæʃənəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 时髦的;时兴的;流行的 Something or someone that is fashionable is popular or approved of at a particular time. 时髦的;时兴的;流行的

  • It became fashionable to eat certain kinds of fish.

    吃某些种类的鱼开始流行起来。

  • Chelsea Harbour is renowned for its fashionable restaurants.

    切尔西港以高档餐馆闻名。

SYN popular, in fashion, trendy [British , informal] , cool [slang]
