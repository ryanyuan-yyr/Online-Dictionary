nursery
nɜːʳsəri ˈnɜrsəri ˈnɜrsri
Word forms: plural nurseries
1 N-COUNT [oft at/from/to N] 托儿所 A nursery is a place where children who are not old enough to go to school are looked after. 托儿所

  • This nursery will be able to cater for 29 children.

    这家托儿所能照料29个孩子。

  • Her company ran its own workplace nursery.

    她的公司开办了内部的托儿所。

SYN crèche, kindergarten, playgroup, play-centre [New Zealand]  
2See also:day nursery 
3 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] 幼儿园;幼稚园 Nursery is a school for very young children. 幼儿园;幼稚园

  • An affordable nursery education service is an essential basic amenity.

    人们能负担得起的幼儿园教育是一项基本的社会福利。

  • ...a nursery teacher.

    幼儿园老师

4 N-COUNT 育儿室;儿童房 A nursery is a room in a family home in which the young children of the family sleep or play. 育儿室;儿童房

  • He has painted murals in his children's nursery.

    他在孩子们的儿童房墙壁上画上了画。

5 N-COUNT 苗圃 A nursery is a place where plants are grown in order to be sold. 苗圃

  • The garden, developed over the past 35 years, includes a nursery.

    这个有35年历史的花园里有一个苗圃。
