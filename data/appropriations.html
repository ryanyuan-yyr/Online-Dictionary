appropriation
əproʊprieɪʃən əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural appropriations
1 N-COUNT 拨款 An appropriation is an amount of money that a government or organization reserves for a particular purpose. 拨款 [formal]

  • The government raised defence appropriations by 12 per cent.

    政府将国防拨款提高了12%。

SYN setting aside, assignment, allocation, earmarking  
2 N-UNCOUNT 私占；侵吞；盗用；挪用 Appropriation of something that belongs to someone else is the act of taking it, usually without having the right to do so. 私占；侵吞；盗用；挪用 [formal]

  • Other charges include fraud and illegal appropriation of land. [+ of]

    其他指控包括欺诈罪和非法侵占土地罪。

SYN seizure, taking, takeover, assumption
