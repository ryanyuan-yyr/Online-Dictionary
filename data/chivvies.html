chivvy tʃɪvi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense chivvies, present participle chivvying, past tense , past participle chivvied
VERB 一再要求;不断催促 If you chivvy someone, you keep telling them to do something that they do not want to do. 一再要求;不断催促 [British]

  • Jovial ladies chivvy you into ordering more than you can eat!. [VERB noun + into]

    热情的女招待会一再推荐，最后点的餐多到吃不下!

  • He chivvies the troops along with a few well-directed words. [VERB noun with adv.]

    他通过几句有针对性的话不断督促着队伍。

SYN nag, annoy, plague, hound
