fungus
fʌŋgəs ˈfʌŋgəs
Word forms: plural fungi
N-MASS 真菌 A fungus is a plant that has no flowers, leaves, or green colouring, such as a mushroom or a toadstool. Other types of fungus such as mould are extremely small and look like a fine powder. 真菌
SYN mould, mushroom, decay, mildew
