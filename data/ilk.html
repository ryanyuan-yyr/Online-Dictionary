ilk
ɪlk ˈɪlkə ɪlk
N-SING …之辈;…之类 If you talk about people or things of the same ilk, you mean people or things of the same type as a person or thing that has been mentioned. …之辈;…之类

  • He currently terrorises politicians and their ilk on 'Newsnight'.

    他目前在《新闻之夜》节目中的讲话搞得政客们惶恐不安。

  • Where others of his ilk have battled against drugs, Gabriel's problems have centred on his marriage.

    加布里埃尔的同辈都在与毒品作斗争,而他的问题却集中在他的婚姻上。

SYN type, sort, kind, class
