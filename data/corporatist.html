corporatist
kɔːʳprətɪst ˈkɔrpərəˌtɪst
Word forms: plural corporatists
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 社团主义的 You use corporatist to describe organizations, ideas, or systems which follow the principles of corporatism. 社团主义的 [disapproval]

  • ... a corporatist political system.

    社团主义政治体系

  • They did show decidedly corporatist tendencies.

    他们确实表现出明显的社团主义倾向。

2 N-COUNT 社团主义者 A corporatist is someone who believes in the principles of corporatism. 社团主义者 [disapproval]

  • The defeat of the corporatists is easy to understand.

    社团主义者的落败不难理解。
