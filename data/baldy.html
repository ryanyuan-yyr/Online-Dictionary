baldy
bɔːldi ˈbɔldi
Word forms: plural baldies
N-COUNT; also N-VOC （尤用作友善或幽默的称呼）秃子，光头 People sometimes refer to a bald person as a baldy, especially if they are talking about them or to them in a friendly or humorous way. （尤用作友善或幽默的称呼）秃子，光头

  • The actor Patrick Stewart is a long-time baldy and proud of it.

    演员帕特里克·斯图尔特早就秃头了，为此他还很得意。

  • Get lost, baldy.

    别烦我，秃子。
