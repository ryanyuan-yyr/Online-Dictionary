toytown tɔɪtaʊn
ADJ [ADJ n] 幼稚愚蠢的;毫无价值的 You use toytown to show that you think something is silly, childish, or worthless. 幼稚愚蠢的;毫无价值的 [British, disapproval]

  • He denounced what he called toytown revolutionaries advocating non-payment of taxes.

    他谴责他称为幼稚愚蠢的改革者鼓吹不缴纳税款。

  • Inflation has turned the rouble into a toytown currency.

    通货膨胀使得卢布变成了毫无价值的货币。
