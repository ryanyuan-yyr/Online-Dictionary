unbroken
ʌnbroʊkən ʌnˈbroʊkən
ADJ 连续不断的；未被打断的；完整的；未破损的 If something is unbroken, it is continuous or complete and has not been interrupted or broken. 连续不断的；未被打断的；完整的；未破损的

  • ...an unbroken run of 38 match wins.

    38场连胜

  • We've had ten days of almost unbroken sunshine.

    我们这儿连续10天差不多都是阳光明媚。

  • Labour support remained strong and unbroken by the depression.

    工党的支持率依然很高，并未受到经济萧条的影响。
