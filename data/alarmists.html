alarmist
əlɑːʳmɪst əˈlɑrmɪst
Word forms: plural alarmists
1 ADJ-GRADED 轻事重报的；危言耸听的；杞人忧天的 Someone or something that is alarmist causes unnecessary fear or anxiety that something unpleasant or dangerous is going to happen. 轻事重报的；危言耸听的；杞人忧天的

  • Contrary to the more alarmist reports, he is not going to die.

    那些更加危言耸听的报道说他生命垂危，但事实并非如此。

SYN fatalistic, pessimistic, scaremongering  
2 N-COUNT 轻事重报的人；危言耸听的人；杞人忧天的人 An alarmist is someone who causes unnecessary fear or anxiety that something unpleasant or dangerous might happen. 轻事重报的人；危言耸听的人；杞人忧天的人
