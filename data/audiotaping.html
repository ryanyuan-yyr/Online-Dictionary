audiotape
ɔːdioʊteɪp ˈɔːdɪəʊteɪp ˈɔdioʊˌteɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense audiotapes, present participle audiotaping, past tense , past participle audiotaped
1 N-UNCOUNT 录音磁带 Audiotape is magnetic tape which is used to record sound. 录音磁带
2 N-COUNT 录了音的磁带 An audiotape is a recording of speech, music, or other sounds on magnetic tape. 录了音的磁带 [US]
in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 cassette
3 VERB 用磁带给…录音 If you audiotape speech, music, or other sounds, you record them on magnetic tape. 用磁带给…录音 [US]

    我们总是对这些采访进行磁带录音。
