one-horse
ˈwʌnˈhɔrs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （城镇）极小的，穷乡僻壤的，闭塞落后的 If someone describes a town as a one-horse town, they mean it is very small, dull, and old-fashioned. （城镇）极小的，穷乡僻壤的，闭塞落后的 [disapproval]

  • Would you want to live in a small, one-horse town for your whole life?

    你想在一个闭塞落后的小镇上过一辈子吗？

SYN small, slow, quiet, minor  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （比赛）胜负分明的 If a contest is described as a one-horse race, it is thought that one person or thing will obviously win it. （比赛）胜负分明的

  • He described the referendum as a one-horse race.

    他称这次全民公决是一场胜负分明的比赛。

  • Last season looked to be a one-horse race for much of the time, with the reigning champions coasting to an 11-point lead.

    上一赛季大部分时间都毫无悬念，卫冕冠军轻而易举便取得了11分的领先优势。
