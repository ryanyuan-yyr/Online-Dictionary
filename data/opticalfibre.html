optical fibre
Word forms: plural optical fibres
in AM, use 美国英语用 optical fiber
N-VAR 光导纤维；光纤 An optical fibre is a very thin thread of glass inside a protective coating. Optical fibres are used to carry information in the form of light. 光导纤维；光纤

  • Nearly every long-distance call made in Britain now travels on optical fibre instead of cable.

    现在在英国，几乎所有长途电话都通过光纤而非电缆传输。

  • ...an optical fibre network to which every home and office can be connected.

    可连接所有家庭与办公室的光纤网络
