scallop
skɒləp skæl- ˈskɒləp ˈskɑləp ˈskæləp
Word forms: plural scallops
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 扇贝(可食用的贝类动物) Scallops are large shellfish with two flat fan-shaped shells. Scallops can be eaten. 扇贝(可食用的贝类动物)
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (衣服、桌布或手帕等的)扇形饰边，荷叶边 Scallops are a series of small curves that form an ornamental border on things such as clothes, tablecloths, or handkerchiefs. (衣服、桌布或手帕等的)扇形饰边，荷叶边
