fume
fjuːm fjum
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fumes, present participle fuming, past tense , past participle fumed
1 N-PLURAL (难闻且常为有害的)烟,气体 Fumes are the unpleasant and often unhealthy smoke and gases that are produced by fires or by things such as chemicals, fuel, or cooking. (难闻且常为有害的)烟,气体

  • ...car exhaust fumes.

    汽车尾气

  • They have been protesting about fumes from a chlorine factory.

    他们一直在抗议一家氯工厂排放有毒气体。

SYN smoke, gas, exhaust, pollution  
2 VERB 发怒;生气 If you fume over something, you express annoyance and anger about it. 发怒;生气

  • He was still fuming over the remark. [V + over/at/about]

    他还在为那句话生气。

  • 'It's monstrous!' Jackie fumed. [VERB with quote]

    “真是太过分了！”杰姬气冲冲地说。

  • The old man fumed and threatened. [VERB]

    这个老头大发脾气，口出威胁。

SYN rage, boil, seethe, see red [informal]
