choppy
tʃɒpi ˈtʃɑpi
Word forms: comparative choppier, superlative choppiest
ADJ-GRADED (水)波浪滔滔的，波浪起伏的 When water is choppy, there are a lot of small waves on it because there is a wind blowing. (水)波浪滔滔的，波浪起伏的

  • A gale was blowing and the sea was choppy.

    狂风大作，海面上波浪起伏。

SYN rough, broken, ruffled, tempestuous
