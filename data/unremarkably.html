unremarkable
ʌnrɪmɑːʳkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 一般的；普通的 If you describe someone or something as unremarkable, you mean that they are very ordinary, without many exciting, original, or attractive qualities. 一般的；普通的

  • ...a tall, lean man, with an unremarkable face.

    又高又瘦、长相一般的男人

  • ...a rather unremarkable town in North Wales.

    北威尔士一个极其普通的小镇
