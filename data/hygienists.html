hygienist
haɪdʒiːnɪst ˈhaɪdʒiːnɪst ˈhaɪdʒiːɪst haɪˈdʒinɪst haɪˈdʒɛnɪst ˈhaɪˌdʒinɪst
Word forms: plural hygienists
N-COUNT 口腔保健员;牙科洁治员;牙医助手 A hygienist or a dental hygienist is a person who is trained to clean people's teeth and to give them advice on how to look after their teeth and gums. 口腔保健员;牙科洁治员;牙医助手
