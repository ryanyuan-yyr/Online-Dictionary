kick around
1 PHRASAL VERB 非正式地讨论；随便谈谈 If you kick around ideas or suggestions, you discuss them informally. 非正式地讨论；随便谈谈 [informal]

  • We kicked a few ideas around. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们随便谈了一些想法。

  • They started to kick around the idea of an electric scraper. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们开始讨论弄一个电铲。

SYN discuss, consider, talk about, review  
2 PHRASAL VERB 粗暴地对待；虐待；凌辱 If you say that someone kicks you around, you do not like the way that they treat you because it is unfair. 粗暴地对待；虐待；凌辱 [informal]

  • I don't feel that anyone can kick me around anymore. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我认为没人敢再欺负我了。

SYN mistreat, wrong, abuse, injure
