log-rolling
(also logrolling)
N-UNCOUNT 政客间相互支持或互投赞成票的议案If you accuse politicians of log-rolling, you mean that they make private agreements with each other, so that, for example, they give each other help or vote for each other's bills. 政客间相互支持或互投赞成票的议案 [US, disapproval]

  • Often, such projects are the result of legislative log-rolling.

    一般来说，这样的项目都是立法机关之间相互投赞成票所通过的议案结果。
