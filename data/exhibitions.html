exhibition
eksɪbɪʃən ˌɛksəˈbɪʃən
Word forms: plural exhibitions
1 N-COUNT 展览会；展出 An exhibition is a public event at which pictures, sculptures, or other objects of interest are displayed, for example at a museum or art gallery. 展览会；展出

  • ...an exhibition of expressionist art. [+ of]

    表现派艺术展览会

  • ...an exhibition on the natural history of the area.

    该地区自然历史的展览会

SYN show, display, exhibit, showing  
2 N-SING 展示；显示 An exhibition of a particular skilful activity is a display or example of it that people notice or admire. 展示；显示

  • He responded in champion's style by treating the fans to an exhibition of power and speed. [+ of]

    他向崇拜者展示了他的力量和速度，表现得颇有冠军风范。

SYN display, show, performance [informal] , demonstration  
3 See also: exhibit
