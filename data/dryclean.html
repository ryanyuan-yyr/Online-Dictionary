dry-clean
ˈdraɪˌklin
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dry-cleans, present participle dry-cleaning, past tense , past participle dry-cleaned
VERB [usually passive] 干洗 When things such as clothes are dry-cleaned, they are cleaned with a liquid chemical rather than with water. 干洗

  • Natural-filled duvets must be dry-cleaned by a professional. [be VERB-ed]

    天然填充的羽绒被必须交由专业人士干洗。
