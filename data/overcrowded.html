overcrowded
oʊvəʳkraʊdɪd ˌəʊvəˈkraʊdɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （地方）过度拥挤的 An overcrowded place has too many things or people in it. （地方）过度拥挤的

  • ...one of the most overcrowded prisons in the country.

    该国最拥挤的监狱之一

  • ...a windswept, overcrowded, unattractive beach.

    受大风吹袭、人满为患又毫无景色可言的海滩

  • Morocco's capital, until independence, was badly overcrowded.

    摩洛哥的首都在独立前一直都拥挤不堪。

SYN packed (out), full, crowded, jammed
