eke
iːk ik
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ekes, present participle eking, past tense , past participle eked
PHRASE 勉强维持生计 If you eke a living or eke out an existence, you manage to survive with very little money. 勉强维持生计

  • That forced peasant farmers to try to eke a living off steep hillsides . [+ off]

    那迫使农民们努力在陡峭的山坡上勉强维持生计。

  • He ekes out a living with a market stall.

    他靠摆摊勉强糊口。

  • He was eking out an existence on a few francs a day.

    他每天就靠几法郎勉强度日。

Phrasal verbs:
eke out
