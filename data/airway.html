airway
eəʳweɪ ˈɛəˌweɪ ˈɛrˌweɪ
Word forms: plural airways
1 N-COUNT 气道；呼吸道 A person's airways are the passages from their nose and mouth down to their lungs, through which air enters and leaves their body. 气道；呼吸道

  • ...an inflammation of the airways.

    呼吸道炎症

2 N-PLURAL 航空路线；航线 The airways are all the routes that planes can travel along. 航空路线；航线

  • How does a private pilot get access to the airways?

    私人飞行员如何进入航空路线呢？

3 N-PLURAL 同airwaves Airways means the same as airwaves . 同airwaves

  • The interview went out over the airways.

    采访通过电台和电视台播出。
