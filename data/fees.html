fee
fiː fi
Word forms: plural fees
1 N-COUNT 费;费用 A fee is a sum of money that you pay to be allowed to do something. 费;费用

  • He hadn't paid his television licence fee.

    他尚未缴纳电视机使用许可费。

2 N-COUNT 服务费;报酬;酬金 A fee is the amount of money that a person or organization is paid for a particular job or service that they provide. 服务费;报酬;酬金

  • Find out how much your surveyor's and solicitor's fees will be.

    弄清你要向鉴定员和律师支付多少费用。

SYN charge, pay, price, cost
