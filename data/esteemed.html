esteemed
ɪstiːmd
ADJ-GRADED 受尊敬的;受敬重的;受仰慕的 You use esteemed to describe someone who you greatly admire and respect. 受尊敬的;受敬重的;受仰慕的 [formal]

  • He was esteemed by his neighbours.

    邻居们很敬重他。

  • It is indeed an honour to serve my country in such an esteemed position.

    在如此受人尊敬的岗位上为我的国家服务的确是一种荣誉。
