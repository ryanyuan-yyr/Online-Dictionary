sharp practice
ʃɑːp ˈpræktɪs
N-UNCOUNT (尤指商业或职业领域中的)欺诈性伎俩，狡诈行为，卑鄙手段 You can use sharp practice to refer to an action or a way of behaving, especially in business or professional matters, that you think is clever but dishonest. (尤指商业或职业领域中的)欺诈性伎俩，狡诈行为，卑鄙手段 [disapproval]

  • He accused some solicitors of sharp practice.

    他指责一些律师的卑鄙行为。
