achingly
eɪkɪŋli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective/adverb] （因得不到而）渴望地，痛苦地 You can use achingly for emphasis when you are referring to things that create feelings of wanting something very much, but of not being able to have it. （因得不到而）渴望地，痛苦地 [written, emphasis]
