pour
pɔːʳ pɔr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pours, present participle pouring, past tense , past participle poured
1 VERB 使（液体等）连续流出；倾倒；倒出 If you pour a liquid or other substance, you make it flow steadily out of a container by holding the container at an angle. 使（液体等）连续流出；倾倒；倒出

  • Pour a pool of sauce on two plates and arrange the meat neatly. [VERB noun prep.]

    在两个盘子里倒少许沙司酱，然后把肉摆放整齐。

  • Francis poured a generous measure of the whisky into a fresh glass. [VERB noun prep.]

    弗朗西斯往一个干净的玻璃杯里倒了很多威士忌。

  • Heat the oil in a non-stick frying-pan, then pour in the egg mixture. [VERB noun with adv.]

    在不粘煎锅里将油加热，然后倒入鸡蛋糊。

SYN let flow, spill, splash, dribble  
2 VERB （给…）斟，倒（饮料等） If you pour someone a drink, you put some of the drink in a cup or glass so that they can drink it. （给…）斟，倒（饮料等） [Also VERB n]

  • He got up and poured himself another drink. [VERB noun noun]

    他站起身来，给自己又倒了一杯。

  • She asked Tillie to pour her a cup of coffee. [VERB noun noun]

    她请蒂莉给她倒杯咖啡。

  • Quietly Mark poured and served drinks for all of them. [VERB noun + for]

    马克一声不响地给他们每人倒好饮料并端上来。

3 VERB （液体等）倾泻，涌流，喷发 When a liquid or other substance pours somewhere, for example through a hole, it flows quickly and in large quantities. （液体等）倾泻，涌流，喷发

  • Blood was pouring from his broken nose. [VERB prep./adv.]

    血从他受伤的鼻子里涌出来。

  • There was dense smoke pouring from all four engines. [VERB prep./adv.]

    4个发动机全都在冒浓烟。

  • Tears poured down both our faces. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我俩都泪流满面。

  • The tide poured in from the south. [VERB prep./adv.]

    潮水从南面涌来。

SYN flow, stream, run, course  
4 VERB [usually cont] （雨）倾盆而下，倾泻 When it rains very heavily, you can say that it is pouring. （雨）倾盆而下，倾泻

  • It has been pouring in Delhi almost non stop for the past three days, disrupting normal life. [it VERB]

    德里市在过去的3天里一直在下着倾盆大雨，几乎没有停过，扰乱了人们的正常生活。

  • It has been pouring with rain all week. [VERB]

    一个星期以来一直在下着瓢泼大雨。

  • The rain was pouring down. [V down]

    天空下着倾盆大雨。

  • We drove all the way through pouring rain. [VERB-ing]

    我们在倾盆大雨中一路驱车前行。

SYN rain, sheet, pelt (down), teem  
5 VERB （人）大量涌入（或涌出），蜂拥 If people pour into or out of a place, they go there quickly and in large numbers. （人）大量涌入（或涌出），蜂拥

  • Any day now, the Northern forces may pour across the new border. [VERB prep./adv.]

    北方的军队现在随时都可能大批越过新边界。

  • Holidaymakers continued to pour down to the coast in search of surf and sun. [VERB prep./adv.]

    度假者不断涌至海边冲浪和享受阳光。

  • At six p.m. large groups poured from the numerous offices. [VERB prep./adv.]

    下午6点钟大群大群的人从无数办公楼里蜂拥而出。

6 VERB （信息等）大量涌来，源源而来 If something such as information pours into a place, a lot of it is obtained or given. （信息等）大量涌来，源源而来

  • Martin, 78, died yesterday. Tributes poured in from around the globe. [VERB adv./prep.]

    马丁于昨天去世，享年78岁。世界各地的人们纷纷致以哀悼。

  • The commission has invited interested parties to submit comments, and these are now pouring in. [VERB adv./prep.]

    委员会已邀请各当事方递交意见，现在意见书纷至沓来。

7 PHRASE 向（计划或想法）泼冷水 If someone pours cold water on a plan or idea, they criticize it so much that people lose their enthusiasm for it. 向（计划或想法）泼冷水

  • The education secretary poured cold water on the recommendations of a working party.

    教育大臣对一个工作小组的建议泼冷水。
