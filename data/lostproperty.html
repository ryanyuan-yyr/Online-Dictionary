lost property
lɒst ˈprɒpətɪ
1 N-UNCOUNT （遗失在公共场所的）失物 Lost property consists of things that people have lost or accidentally left in a public place, for example on a train or in a school. （遗失在公共场所的）失物

  • Lost property should be handed to the driver.

    请将失物交给司机。

2 N-UNCOUNT 失物招领处 Lost property is a place where lost property is kept. 失物招领处 [British]

  • I was enquiring in Lost Property at Derby.

    我当时正在德比的失物招领处打听。
