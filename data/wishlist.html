wish list
Word forms: plural wish lists
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 心愿榜，愿望清单(指希望发生或得到的一切) If you refer to someone's wish list, you mean all the things which they would like to happen or be given, although these things may be unlikely. 心愿榜，愿望清单(指希望发生或得到的一切) [informal]

  • ...one special toy that tops the wish list of every child. [+ of]

    每个孩子心目中最希望获得的特殊玩具

  • The resolution that was passed was a wish list that nobody could deliver.

    通过的决议不过是张空头支票而已。
