deform
dɪfɔːʳm diˈfɔrm dɪˈfɔrm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deforms, present participle deforming, past tense , past participle deformed
V-ERG （使）变形;（使）成畸形;损毁…的外形 If something deforms a person's body or something else, it causes it to have an unnatural shape. In technical English, you can also say that the second thing deforms. （使）变形;（使）成畸形;损毁…的外形

  • Bad rheumatoid arthritis deforms limbs. [VERB noun]

    严重的类风湿性关节炎会造成四肢变形。

  • ...the ability of a metal to deform to a new shape without cracking. [VERB]

    金属重新塑形而不断裂的能力

SYN disfigure, twist, injure, cripple  
deformed

    他出生时右腿畸形。
