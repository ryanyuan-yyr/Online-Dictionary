psych out
PHRASAL VERB 使心虚；在心理上压倒 If you psych out your opponent in a contest, you try to make them feel less confident by behaving in a very confident or aggressive way. 使心虚；在心理上压倒 [informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • They are like heavyweight boxers, trying to psych each other out and build themselves up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们像是重量级拳击运动员，竭力想在心理上压倒对方，树立自己的信心。

SYN unsettle, upset, disturb, intimidate
