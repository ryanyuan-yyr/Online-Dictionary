kick down
(also kick in)
PHRASAL VERB 踢破；踢倒 If someone kicks something down or if they kick it in, they hit it violently with their foot so that it breaks or falls over. 踢破；踢倒 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She was forced to kick down the front door. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她只好踢开前门。

  • My neighbour's door had been kicked in. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我邻居的门被踢开了。
