contribution
kɒntrɪbjuːʃən ˌkɑntrəˈbjuʃən
Word forms: plural contributions
1 N-COUNT 贡献 If you make a contribution to something, you do something to help make it successful or to produce it. 贡献

  • American economists have made important contributions to the field of financial and corporate economics.

    美国的经济学家们在金融和企业经济学领域做出了重要的贡献。

  • He was awarded a prize for his contribution to world peace. [+ to]

    他由于为世界和平做出贡献而获奖。

2 N-COUNT 捐款;捐资 A contribution is a sum of money that you give in order to help pay for something. 捐款;捐资

  • This list ranked companies that make charitable contributions of a half million dollars or more. [+ of]

    这张名单列出了慈善捐款额达50万美元以上的公司。

SYN gift, offering, grant, donation  
3 N-COUNT 稿件;投稿 A contribution to a magazine, newspaper, or book is something that you write to be published in it. 稿件;投稿
