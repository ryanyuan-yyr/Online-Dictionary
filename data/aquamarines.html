aquamarine
ækwəməriːn ˌækwəməˈrin ˌ ɑkwəməˈrin
Word forms: plural aquamarines
1 N-VAR 海蓝宝石；水蓝宝石 Aquamarines are clear, greenish-blue stones, sometimes used to make jewellery. 海蓝宝石；水蓝宝石

  • A necklace set with aquamarines.

    项链镶嵌着海蓝宝石。

  • ...a large aquamarine ring.

    大颗海蓝宝石戒指

2 COLOUR 蓝绿色;海蓝色 Aquamarine is used to describe things that are greenish-blue in colour. 蓝绿色;海蓝色

  • ...warm aquamarine seas and white beaches.

    暖洋洋的蓝绿色海面和白色沙滩

  • ...a shimmering suit of aquamarine satin.

    海蓝色缎子做的华贵套装
