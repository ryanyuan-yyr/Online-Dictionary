omnivorous
ɒmnɪvərəs ɑmˈnɪvərəs
1 ADJ 杂食（性）的 An omnivorous person or animal eats all kinds of food, including both meat and plants. 杂食（性）的 [technical, formal]

  • Brown bears are omnivorous, eating anything that they can get their paws on.

    棕熊是杂食性动物，抓到什么吃什么。

2 ADJ （在某一方面）兴趣广泛的，什么都喜欢的 Omnivorous means liking a wide variety of things of a particular type. （在某一方面）兴趣广泛的，什么都喜欢的 [formal]

  • As a child, Coleridge developed omnivorous reading habits.

    柯尔律治从孩提时期便养成了广泛阅读的习惯。
