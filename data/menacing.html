menacing
menɪsɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 威胁的;威吓的;危险的 If someone or something looks menacing, they give you a feeling that they are likely to cause you harm or put you in danger. 威胁的;威吓的;危险的

  • The strong dark eyebrows give his face an oddly menacing look.

    两道浓黑的眉毛使他的脸看起来异常凶狠。

  • He moved his menacing bulk closer to the table.

    他把自己骇人的庞大身躯向桌子挪近了些。

  • The wide river looked less menacing when flanked by the warm yellow houses.

    在两岸暖黄色房子的映衬下，这宽阔的大河看起来就不那么险恶了。

SYN threatening, dangerous, alarming, frightening  
menacingly

  • A group of men suddenly emerged from a doorway and moved menacingly forward to block her way.

    一群男子突然出现在门口，并且向她逼近，堵住了她的去路。
