attempted
ətemptɪd əˈtɛmptɪd
ADJ [ADJ n] （犯罪、不法行为）未遂的，未得手的 An attempted crime or unlawful action is an unsuccessful effort to commit the crime or action. （犯罪、不法行为）未遂的，未得手的

  • ...a case of attempted murder.

    谋杀未遂案

  • The attempted coup took place in January.

    那场未遂政变发生在1月份。

SYN tried, ventured, undertaken, endeavoured
