byway
baɪweɪ ˈbaɪˌweɪ
Word forms: plural byways
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 旁道；偏僻小路 A byway is a small road which is not used by many cars or people. 旁道；偏僻小路

  • ...the highways and byways of America. [+ of]

    美国大大小小的街道

  • ...the narrow city byways.

    狭窄的城中小路

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （学科的）次要领域；冷僻学科 The byways of a subject are the less important or less well known areas of it. （学科的）次要领域；冷僻学科

  • My research focuses on the byways of children's literature. [+ of]

    我的研究集中于儿童文学中的一些冷门领域。
