unbridgeable
ʌnbrɪdʒəbəl ʌnˈbrɪdʒəbəl
ADJ 难以逾越的；无法弥合的 An unbridgeable gap or divide between two sides in an argument is so great that the two sides seem unlikely ever to agree. 难以逾越的；无法弥合的 [journalism]

  • ...the apparently unbridgeable gulf between the SIS and the Security Service.

    秘密情报局和国家安全局之间显然难以逾越的鸿沟

  • The gap between the President and his opponents is unbridgeable.

    总统和反对派之间的嫌隙是不可弥合的。
