harvester
hɑːʳvɪstəʳ ˈhɑrvɪstər
Word forms: plural harvesters
1 N-COUNT 收割机 A harvester is a machine which cuts and often collects crops such as wheat, maize, or vegetables. 收割机
2See also:combine harvester 
3 N-COUNT 收割庄稼的人;收割者 You can refer to a person who cuts, picks, or gathers crops as a harvester . 收割庄稼的人;收割者
