undergo
ʌndəʳgoʊ ˌʌndəˈɡəʊ ˌʌndərˈgoʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense undergoes, present participle undergoing, past tense underwent, past participle undergone
VERB 经历；接受；遭受；忍受 If you undergo something necessary or unpleasant, it happens to you. 经历；接受；遭受；忍受

  • New recruits have been undergoing training in recent weeks. [VERB noun]

    最近几周，新兵正在接受训练。

  • He underwent an agonising 48-hour wait for the results of tests. [VERB noun]

    他苦苦等待了48个小时，化验结果才出来。

SYN experience, go through, be subjected to, stand
