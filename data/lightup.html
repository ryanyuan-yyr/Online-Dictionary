light up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 照亮；发亮 If you light something up or if it lights up, it becomes bright, usually when you shine light on it. 照亮；发亮 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • ...a keypad that lights up when you pick up the handset. [VERB PREP.]

    拿起听筒就会发亮的电话键盘

  • On September 5, at the end of the festival, a massive display of fireworks will light up the sky around Broadlands. [VERB PREP. noun]

    9月5日，也就是庆祝活动的最后，会有大型焰火表演照亮布罗德兰附近的夜空。

SYN shine, flash, beam, blaze  
2 PHRASAL VERB 现出吃惊的神情；露出喜色 If your face or your eyes light up you suddenly look very surprised or happy. 现出吃惊的神情；露出喜色

  • Sue's face lit up with surprise. [VERB PREP. + with]

    她一脸惊诧。

  • You should see his eyes light up when he talks about home. [VERB PREP.]

    你真该看看他谈到家的时候眼睛放光的模样。

SYN cheer, shine, blaze, sparkle  
3 PHRASAL VERB 点烟；开始吸烟 If you light up, you make a cigarette, cigar, or pipe start burning and you start smoking it. 点烟；开始吸烟 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He held a match while she lit up. [VERB PREP.]

    他拿了根火柴为她点烟。

  • He took his time lighting up a cigarette. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他不慌不忙地点了一支烟。
