complacency
kəmpleɪsənsi kəmˈpleɪsənsi
N-UNCOUNT 自满;沾沾自喜 Complacency is being complacent about a situation. 自满;沾沾自喜 [disapproval]

  • ...a worrying level of complacency about the risks of infection from AIDS. [+ about]

    对感染艾滋病的危险毫不在意，实在堪忧

  • She warned that there was no room for complacency on inflation. [+ on]

    她警告说对待通货膨胀不能有一丝的懈怠。

SYN smugness, satisfaction, gratification, contentment
