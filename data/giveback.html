give back
PHRASAL VERB 归还；交还 If you give something back, you return it to the person who gave it to you. 归还；交还

  • I gave the textbook back to him. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    我将课本还给他了。

  • You gave me back the projector. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    你将幻灯机还给我了。

  • I gave it back politely. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我很客气地把它还了回去。
