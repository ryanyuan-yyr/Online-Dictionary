probationer
prəbeɪʃənəʳ proʊ- prəˈbeɪʃənə proʊˈbeɪʃənər
Word forms: plural probationers
1 N-COUNT 缓刑犯 A probationer is someone who has been found guilty of committing a crime but is on probation rather than in prison. 缓刑犯
2 N-COUNT 见习生；试用人员 A probationer is someone who is still being trained to do a job and is on trial. 见习生；试用人员

  • ...a probationer policeman.

    见习警察
