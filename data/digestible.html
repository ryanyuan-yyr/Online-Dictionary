digestible
daɪdʒestɪbəl daɪˈdʒɛstəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [oft adverb ADJ] (食物)易消化的 Digestible food is food that is easy to digest. (食物)易消化的

  • Bananas are easily digestible.

    香蕉很容易消化。

digestibility

  • Processing and refining can alter digestibility.

    通过加工处理和精炼可以改变食物的可消化性。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (食物)可口的，美味的 Food that is digestible is pleasant to eat. (食物)可口的，美味的

  • Fish is inexpensive, easy to cook and very digestible.

    鱼价格便宜，容易烹调，且味美可口。

3 ADJ-GRADED (理论或想法)易理解的，易领会的 If a theory or idea is digestible, it is easy to understand. (理论或想法)易理解的，易领会的

  • The book's aim was to make economic theory more digestible.

    该书的目的是要让经济学理论更加简单易懂。
