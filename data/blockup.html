block up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）封闭；（使）堵住；（使）封死 If you block something up or if it blocks up, it is blocked completely so that nothing can get through it. （使）封闭；（使）堵住；（使）封死

  • 'Any holes in the kitchen where the mice are getting through?' – 'I've blocked them up.'. [VERB noun PREP.]

    “厨房里还有没有老鼠洞？”—“我已经把它们堵死了。”

  • Powdering a sweaty nose will only block up the pores and make the skin more uncomfortable. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    往出汗的鼻子上扑粉只会堵塞毛孔，使皮肤更加不舒服。

  • With this disease the veins in the liver can block up, and all sorts of damage follows. [VERB PREP.]

    这种病会堵塞肝脏中的静脉，并引发多种损伤。

SYN clog (up), jam (up), stop up, bung up
