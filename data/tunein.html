tune in
1 PHRASAL VERB 收听;收看 If you tune in to a particular television or radio station or programme, you watch or listen to it. 收听;收看

  • More than six million youngsters tune in to Blockbusters every day. [VERB PREP. + to]

    每天有 600 多万年轻人收看“热门大片”这档节目。

  • The idea that people plan their radio listening is nonsense; most tune in impulsively. [VERB PREP.]

    认为人们有计划地收听广播的观点完全是无稽之谈，大部分人是随意收听。

2 PHRASAL VERB 开始注意;开始了解 If you tune in to something such as your own or other people's feelings, you become aware of them. 开始注意;开始了解

  • You can start now to tune in to your own physical, social and spiritual needs. [VERB PREP. + to]

    你现在可以开始关注自己的生理、社交与精神需求。
