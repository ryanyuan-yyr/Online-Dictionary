adjacent
ədʒeɪsənt əˈdʒeɪsənt
ADJ 邻近的；毗连的 If one thing is adjacent to another, the two things are next to each other. 邻近的；毗连的

  • He sat in an adjacent room and waited.

    他坐在隔壁房间里等候。

  • The schools were adjacent but there were separate doors.

    这些学校紧挨着，但各自独门独户。

  • ...offices adjacent to the museum. [+ to]

    紧靠着博物馆的办公室

SYN adjoining, neighbouring, nearby, abutting
