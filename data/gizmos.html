gizmo
gɪzmoʊ ˈgɪzmoʊ
Word forms: plural gizmos
N-COUNT 小玩意儿；小装置 A gizmo is a device or small machine which performs a particular task, usually in a new and efficient way. People often use gizmo to refer to a device or machine when they do not know what it is really called. 小玩意儿；小装置 [informal]

  • ...a plastic gizmo for holding a coffee cup on the dashboard.

    汽车仪表盘上放咖啡杯的塑料装置
