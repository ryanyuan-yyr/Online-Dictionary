shelf
ʃelf ʃɛlf
Word forms: plural shelves
1 N-COUNT (固定在墙上或橱柜上的)架子，搁板 A shelf is a flat piece of wood, metal, or glass which is attached to a wall or to the sides of a cupboard. Shelves are used for keeping things on. (固定在墙上或橱柜上的)架子，搁板

  • He took a book from the shelf.

    他从书架上拿了一本书。

  • ...the middle shelf of the oven.

    烤箱的中间搁板

SYN shelving, rack, bookshelf, mantelpiece  
2 N-COUNT 搁板状岩石;突出的岩石;(水下的)暗礁，陆架 A shelf is a section of rock on a cliff or mountain or underwater that sticks out like a shelf. 搁板状岩石;突出的岩石;(水下的)暗礁，陆架

  • The house stands on a shelf of rock among pines. [+ of]

    房子矗立在松树丛中一块突出的岩石上。

3See also:continental shelf 
4 PHRASE 现成的;不用定制的 If you buy something off the shelf, you buy something that is not specially made for you. 现成的;不用定制的 [British]

  • Any car you can buy off the shelf in a pastel pink has got to be saying something.

    现货供应的任何一辆淡粉色汽车一定都有自己的卖点。

  • ...off-the-shelf software.

    现成的软件

5 PHRASE [verb-link PHRASE, PHRASE after verb] 搁置的;闲置的;没人要的 If you say that someone or something is on the shelf, you mean that no one wants them. 搁置的;闲置的;没人要的 [informal]

  • I was afraid of getting left on the shelf.

    我害怕嫁不出去。

  • ...first-rate plans which sit on the shelf.

    不被采用的最佳方案

SYN unmarried, single, unwed, unwedded
