couch potato
Word forms: plural couch potatoes
N-COUNT 老泡在电视机前的人;电视迷 A couch potato is someone who spends most of their time watching television and does not exercise or have any interesting hobbies. 老泡在电视机前的人;电视迷 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...couch potatoes flicking through endless satellite TV channels.

    在无数个卫星电视频道之间来回换台的电视迷
