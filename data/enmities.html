enmity
enmɪti ˈɛnməti
Word forms: plural enmities
N-VAR 敌意;仇恨;愤恨 Enmity is a feeling of hatred towards someone that lasts for a long time. 敌意;仇恨;愤恨

  • I think there is an historic enmity between them. [+ between]

    我觉得他们之间的仇恨由来已久。

  • President Mitterrand arrived in Hanoi yesterday to bury old colonial and cold war enmities.

    密特朗总统昨天到达河内，以期结束长久以来殖民战争和冷战造成的敌意。

SYN hostility, hate, spite, hatred
