rubric
ruːbrɪk ˈrubrɪk
Word forms: plural rubrics
1 N-COUNT （试卷等开头的）说明，规定 A rubric is a set of rules or instructions, for example the rules at the beginning of an examination paper. （试卷等开头的）说明，规定 [formal]

  • There was a firm rubric in the book about what had to be observed when interrogating anyone under seventeen.

    书中严格规定了审讯17岁以下未成年人时应当遵守的事项。

2 N-COUNT 题目;标题;类目 A rubric is a title or heading under which something operates or is studied. 题目;标题;类目 [formal]

  • The aid comes under the rubric of technical co-operation between governments.

    这项援助归于政府间的技术合作这一类别。
