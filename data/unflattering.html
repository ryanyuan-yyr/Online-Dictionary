unflattering
ʌnflætərɪŋ ʌnˈflætərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 贬低的；有损形象的 If you describe something as unflattering, you mean that it makes a person or thing seem less attractive than they really are. 贬低的；有损形象的

  • He depicted the town's respectable families in an unflattering light.

    他在描述镇上体面人家时满是贬抑之词。

  • The knee-length dresses were unflattering and ugly.

    齐膝长裙既不显身材又不好看。

SYN unattractive, plain, unbecoming, unprepossessing
