noontime
nuntaɪm ˈnuːnˌtaɪm ˈnunˌtaɪm
(also noon-time,noon time)
N-UNCOUNT [oft N n] 中午Noontime is the middle part of the day. 中午 [US]

  • There was a demonstration at noontime yesterday at the Chinese Embassy.

    昨天中午在中国大使馆有示威。

  • The animals settled peacefully to their noontime meal.

    这些动物安静下来，吃它们的午餐。
