imaginary
ɪmædʒɪnəri -neri ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 想象中的;假想的;虚构的 An imaginary person, place, or thing exists only in your mind or in a story, and not in real life. 想象中的;假想的;虚构的

  • Lots of children have imaginary friends.

    许多孩子都会凭空想象一些朋友。

  • ...creating an imaginary world.

    创造一个虚构的世界

SYN fictional, made-up, invented, supposed
