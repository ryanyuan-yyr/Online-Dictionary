reunite
riːjuːnaɪt ˌriːjuːˈnaɪt ˌrijuˈnaɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reunites, present participle reuniting, past tense , past participle reunited
1 V-ERG （使）团聚;（使）重聚;（使）重逢 If people are reunited, or if they reunite, they meet each other again after they have been separated for some time. （使）团聚;（使）重聚;（使）重逢

  • She and her youngest son were finally allowed to be reunited with their family. [be VERB-ed + with]

    她和小儿子终于得到允许和家人团聚。

  • She spent the post-war years of her marriage trying to reunite father and son. [VERB noun]

    在战后几年的婚姻生活中，她千方百计想让父子团聚。

  • The band will reunite for this show only. [VERB]

    乐队的重聚仅仅是为了这场演出。

2 V-ERG （使）统一;（使）重新团结 If a divided organization or country is reunited, or if it reunites, it becomes one united organization or country again. （使）统一;（使）重新团结

  • ...a federation under which the divided island would be reunited. [be VERB-ed]

    将使这个分裂的岛屿实现统一的联邦政府

  • His first job will be to reunite the army. [VERB noun]

    他的首要任务将是使军队重新团结一心。

  • ...when East and West Germany reunited. [VERB]

    当东德和西德实现统一的时候
