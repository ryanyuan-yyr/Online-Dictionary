testimonial
testɪmoʊniəl ˌtɛstəˈmoʊniəl
Word forms: plural testimonials
1 N-COUNT (通常由雇主出具的、证明某人品格和能力的)推荐信，证明书 A testimonial is a written statement about a person's character and abilities, often written by their employer. (通常由雇主出具的、证明某人品格和能力的)推荐信，证明书

  • She could hardly expect her employer to provide her with testimonials to her character and ability. [+ to]

    她可不敢指望雇主给她出具关于她品格和能力的证明书。

SYN reference, recommendation, credential, character  
2 N-COUNT (部分票款捐给某运动员或其家人的)纪念赛，义捐比赛 A testimonial is a sports match which is specially arranged so that part of the profit from the tickets sold can be given to a particular player or to a particular player's family. (部分票款捐给某运动员或其家人的)纪念赛，义捐比赛
