nine-to-five ˈnaɪntəˈfaɪv ˈnaɪntəˈfaɪv
1 ADJ 朝九晚五的; 正常工作时间的A nine-to-five job is one that you do during normal office hours, for example a job in a factory or an office. 朝九晚五的; 正常工作时间的

  • She works a nine-to-five job.

    她有一份朝九晚五的工作。

2 ADV 朝九晚五地Nine to five is also an adverb. 朝九晚五地

  • I wish I could go to work in a factory, nine to five.

    我要是能在一家工厂朝九晚五地上班就好了。
