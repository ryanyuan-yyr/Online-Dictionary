density
densɪti ˈdɛnsəti
Word forms: plural densities
1 N-VAR 密度;稠密;密集 Density is the extent to which something is filled or covered with people or things. 密度;稠密;密集

  • ...a law which restricts the density of housing. [+ of]

    限制房屋密集程度的法规

  • The region has a very high population density.

    该地区的人口密度很高。

  • ...areas with high densities of immigrant populations.

    移民人口稠密的地区

SYN tightness, closeness, thickness, compactness  
2 N-VAR [oft with poss] （物质或物体的）密度 In science, the density of a substance or object is the relation of its mass or weight to its volume. （物质或物体的）密度 [technical]

  • Jupiter's moon Io, whose density is 3.5 grams per cubic centimetre, is all rock.

    密度为每立方厘米 3.5 克的木星卫星木卫一上遍布岩石。

SYN mass, body, bulk, consistency
