toy
tɔɪ tɔɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense toys, present participle toying, past tense , past participle toyed
1 N-COUNT 玩具 A toy is an object that children play with, for example a doll or a model car. 玩具

  • He was really too old for children's toys.

    他已经长大了，实在不再适合玩儿童玩具了。

  • ...a toy telephone.

    玩具电话

SYN plaything, game, doll  
2See also:soft toy 
3 N-COUNT 消遣物;小玩意儿 You can refer to objects that adults use for fun rather than for a serious purpose as toys . 消遣物;小玩意儿

  • Computers have become household toys.

    计算机已成了家庭的消遣玩意儿。

SYN trinket, trifle, bauble, gimcrack  
Phrasal verbs:
toy with
