bald
bɔːld bɔld
Word forms: comparative balder, superlative baldest
1 ADJ-GRADED 秃头的；秃顶的 Someone who is bald has little or no hair on the top of their head. 秃头的；秃顶的

  • The man's bald head was beaded with sweat.

    这个男人的光头上满是汗珠。

  • She is going bald.

    她开始谢顶。

SYN hairless, smooth, bare, shorn  
baldness

  • He wears a cap to cover a spot of baldness.

    他戴帽以遮斑秃。

2 ADJ-GRADED （轮胎表面）磨平了的，磨光了的 If a tyre is bald, its surface has worn down and it is no longer safe to use. （轮胎表面）磨平了的，磨光了的
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 不加修饰的；直截了当的；赤裸裸的 A bald statement is in plain language and contains no extra explanation or information. 不加修饰的；直截了当的；赤裸裸的

  • The announcement came in a bald statement from the official news agency.

    官方通讯社发表了直截了当的声明。

  • The bald truth is he's just not happy.

    事实明摆着，他就是不开心。

SYN plain, direct, simple, straight  
baldly

  • 'The leaders are outdated,' he stated baldly. 'They don't relate to young people.'

    “这些领导都落伍了，”他直言不讳地说，“他们不了解年轻人的想法。”
