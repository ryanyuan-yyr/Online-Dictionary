railway
reɪlweɪ ˈreɪlˌweɪ
Word forms: plural railways
1 N-COUNT 铁路;铁道 A railway is a route between two places along which trains travel on steel rails. 铁路;铁道 [mainly British]

  • The road ran beside a railway.

    公路建在铁路旁边。

  • ...a disused railway line.

    废弃的铁路线

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 railroad
2 N-COUNT 铁路公司;铁路部门 A railway is a company or organization that operates railway routes. 铁路公司;铁路部门 [British]

  • ...the state-owned French railway.

    法国国有铁路公司

  • ...the privatisation of the railways.

    铁路私有化

in AM, use 美国英语用 railroad
3 N-COUNT 铁路系统；铁路网 A railway is the system and network of tracks that trains travel on. 铁路系统；铁路网 [mainly US]
