dynamo
daɪnəmoʊ ˈdaɪnəˌmoʊ
Word forms: plural dynamos
1 N-COUNT 发电机 A dynamo is a device that uses the movement of a machine or vehicle to produce electricity. 发电机
2 N-COUNT 精力充沛的人 If you describe someone as a dynamo, you mean that they are very energetic and are always busy and active. 精力充沛的人

  • Myles is a human dynamo.

    迈尔斯是个精力充沛的人。
