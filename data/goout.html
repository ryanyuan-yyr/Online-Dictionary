go out
1 PHRASAL VERB 外出交际；外出娱乐 If you go out, you leave your home in order to do something enjoyable, for example to go to a party, a bar, or the cinema. 外出交际；外出娱乐

  • I'm going out tonight. [VERB PREP.]

    我今晚要出去玩。

  • It will be a marvellous occasion and they should go out and enjoy it.

    这是个好时机，他们应该出去痛快地玩一玩。

2 PHR-V-RECIP 与（异性）交往；与…谈恋爱（或有性关系） If you go out with someone, the two of you spend time together socially, and have a romantic or sexual relationship. 与（异性）交往；与…谈恋爱（或有性关系）

  • I once went out with a French man. [VERB PREP. + with]

    我曾经同一位法国男子交往过一段时间。

  • They've only been going out for six weeks.

    他们相恋才6周时间。

3 PHRASAL VERB 刻意；故意 If you go out to do something, you make a deliberate effort to do it. 刻意；故意

  • You do not go out to injure opponents. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    你并非蓄意伤害对手。

4 PHRASAL VERB （灯）熄灭 If a light goes out, it stops shining. （灯）熄灭

  • The bedroom light went out after a moment. [VERB PREP.]

    卧室里的灯过了一会儿就灭了。

SYN be extinguished, die out, fade out  
5 PHRASAL VERB （燃烧物）熄灭 If something that is burning goes out, it stops burning. （燃烧物）熄灭

  • The fire seemed to be going out. [VERB PREP.]

    火看起来快灭了。

6 PHRASAL VERB 被广播；被发表；被公布 If a message goes out, it is announced, published, or sent out to people. 被广播；被发表；被公布

  • Word went out that a column of tanks was on its way. [VERB PREP.]

    有消息说一队坦克已经出动了。

7 PHRASAL VERB （电视或广播节目）播出 When a television or radio programme goes out, it is broadcast. （电视或广播节目）播出 [British]

  • The series goes out at 10.30pm, Fridays, on Channel 4. [VERB PREP.]

    这部连续剧每周五晚上10点30分在第4频道播出。

8 PHRASAL VERB 不复存在；被替代；过时 If a type of thing goes out, it stops existing or being used, usually because it is replaced by something else. 不复存在；被替代；过时

  • Double-breasted suits went out years ago. [VERB PREP.]

    双排扣的套装好多年前就过时了。

  • The weapons had gone out of use. [V P of n]

    这些武器已经被淘汰了。

9 PHRASAL VERB （潮水）退去 When the tide goes out, the water in the sea gradually moves back to a lower level. （潮水）退去

  • The tide was going out. [VERB PREP.]

    正在退潮。

10 PHRASAL VERB （比赛中）被淘汰，出局 If a person or team goes out of a competition, they are defeated in a game and therefore can no longer take part in the competition. （比赛中）被淘汰，出局

  • Newcastle went out of the competition, losing 2-1 on aggregate. [V P of n]

    纽卡斯尔队以1比2的总比分被淘汰出局。

11 PHRASE 我十分同情他/ 她 You can say 'My heart goes out to him' or 'My sympathy goes out to her' to express the strong sympathy you have for someone in a difficult or unpleasant situation. 我十分同情他/ 她

  • My heart goes out to Mrs Adams and her fatherless children.

    我十分同情亚当斯太太和她那些失去了父亲的孩子们。
