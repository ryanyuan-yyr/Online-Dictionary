stick around
PHRASAL VERB 呆在原处(等待) If you stick around, you stay where you are, often because you are waiting for something. 呆在原处(等待) [informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Stick around a while and see what develops. [VERB PREP.]

    呆上一会儿，看看会发生什么。

  • I didn't stick around long enough to find out. [VERB PREP.]

    我在那儿呆的时间很短，没弄清楚。
