rampart
ræmpɑːʳt ˈræmˌpɑrt ˈræmpərt
Word forms: plural ramparts
N-COUNT [usually plural] （城堡或城市周围的）城墙，壁垒 The ramparts of a castle or city are the earth walls, often with stone walls on them, that were built to protect it. （城堡或城市周围的）城墙，壁垒

  • ...a walk along the ramparts of the Old City.

    沿着老城的城墙漫步

SYN defence, wall, parapet, fortification
