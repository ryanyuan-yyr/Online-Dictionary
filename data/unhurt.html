unhurt
ʌnhɜːʳt ʌnˈhɜːt
ADJ [ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ] 没有受伤的；安然无恙的 If someone who has been attacked, or involved in an accident, is unhurt, they are not injured. 没有受伤的；安然无恙的

  • The lorry driver escaped unhurt, but a pedestrian was injured.

    卡车司机安然脱险，但有一位行人受了伤。

  • The two girls suddenly emerged from among the trees. Both seemed to be calm and unhurt.

    那两个女孩突然从树丛中冒了出来。她们看起来都很镇静也没受伤。

SYN uninjured, safe, intact, unscathed
