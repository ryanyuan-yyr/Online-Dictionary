photo-finish
ˌfəʊtəʊˈfɪnɪʃ
(also photo finish)Word forms: plural photo-finishes
N-COUNT （赛跑中的）摄影定胜负 If the end of a race is a photo-finish, two or more of the competitors cross the finishing line so close together that a photograph of the finish has to be examined to decide who has won. （赛跑中的）摄影定胜负

  • He was just beaten in a photo-finish.

    他以微弱劣势败北。
