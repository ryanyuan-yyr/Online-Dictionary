homily
hɒmɪli ˈhɑməli
Word forms: plural homilies
N-COUNT 责诘;说教;训诫 A homily is a speech or piece of writing in which someone complains about the state of something or tells people how they ought to behave. 责诘;说教;训诫 [formal]

  • ...a receptive audience for his homily on moral values.

    乐于倾听他关于道德价值观的说教的观众

SYN sermon, talk, address, speech
