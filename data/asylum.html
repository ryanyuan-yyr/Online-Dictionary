asylum
əsaɪləm əˈsaɪləm
Word forms: plural asylums
1 N-UNCOUNT （通常指政治）庇护，避难 If a government gives a person from another country asylum, they allow them to stay, usually because they are unable to return home safely for political reasons. （通常指政治）庇护，避难

  • He applied for asylum in 1987 after fleeing the police back home.

    1987年他在本国逃脱警察追捕后，就申请了政治庇护。

SYN refuge, security, haven, safety  
2 N-COUNT 精神病院 An asylum is a psychiatric hospital. 精神病院 [old-fashioned]
