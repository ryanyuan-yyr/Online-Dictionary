watertight
wɔːtəʳtaɪt ˈwɔtərˌtaɪt
(also water-tight)
1 ADJ 不透水的;水密的 Something that is watertight does not allow water to pass through it, for example because it is tightly sealed. 不透水的;水密的

  • The flask is completely watertight, even when laid on its side.

    即使平放，这个真空水杯也是滴水不漏。

  • The batteries are safely enclosed in a watertight compartment.

    电池被安全地置于一个防水的隔间里。

SYN waterproof, hermetically sealed, sealed, water-resistant  
2 ADJ-GRADED (事实、论点或协议)严密的，无懈可击的 A watertight case, argument, or agreement is one that has been so carefully put together that nobody will be able to find a fault in it. (事实、论点或协议)严密的，无懈可击的

  • The police had a watertight case. They even got his fingerprints from that glass cabinet.

    警方找到了确凿的证据。他们甚至在那个玻璃橱柜上提取到了他的指纹。

  • ...a legally watertight agreement.

    严密规范的协议

SYN foolproof, firm, sound, perfect
