moulder
moʊldəʳ ˈmoʊldər ˈməʊldə
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense moulders, present participle mouldering, past tense , past participle mouldered
in AM, use 美国英语用 molder
VERB [usually cont] 腐烂;崩塌;腐朽 If something is mouldering, it is decaying slowly where it has been left. 腐烂;崩塌;腐朽

  • ...one of your scripts that's been mouldering under the bed for ages. [VERB]

    你的一部在床下尘封已久已经开始腐烂的书稿

  • It is clear that such ideas will be left to moulder. [VERB]

    明摆着，这些想法将无人问津。

  • ...the empty, mouldering old house. [VERB-ing]

    空无一人的陈年旧房

SYN decay, waste, break down, crumble
