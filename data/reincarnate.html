reincarnate
riːɪŋkɑːʳneɪt ˌriɪnˈkɑrneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reincarnates, present participle reincarnating, past tense , past participle reincarnated
VERB [usually passive] (使)转世;(使)再生 If people believe that they will be reincarnated when they die, they believe that their spirit will be born again and will live in the body of another person or animal. (使)转世;(使)再生 [Also VERB]

  • ...their belief that human souls were reincarnated in the bodies of turtles. [be VERB-ed]

    他们认为人的灵魂转世投胎到海龟身体里的信仰

  • ...a reincarnated soul. [VERB-ed]

    转世化身的灵魂
