plantation
plɑːnteɪʃən plæn- plænˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural plantations
1 N-COUNT （尤指在热带国家的）种植园，大农场 A plantation is a large piece of land, especially in a tropical country, where crops such as rubber, coffee, tea, or sugar are grown. （尤指在热带国家的）种植园，大农场

  • ...banana plantations in Costa Rica.

    哥斯达黎加的香蕉种植园

2 N-COUNT 人造林 A plantation is a large number of trees that have been planted together. 人造林

  • ...a plantation of almond trees. [+ of]

    人造扁桃树林
