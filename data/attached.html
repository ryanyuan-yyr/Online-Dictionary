attached
ətætʃt
1 ADJ-GRADED 非常喜欢…的，热爱…的 If you are attached to someone or something, you like them very much. 非常喜欢…的，热爱…的

  • She is very attached to her family and friends. [+ to]

    她非常热爱家人和朋友。

2 ADJ （通常只是短时间）从属于…的，为…工作的 If someone is attached to an organization or group of people, they are working with them, often only for a short time. （通常只是短时间）从属于…的，为…工作的

  • Ford was attached to the battalion's first line of transport. [+ to]

    福特曾在该营的第一运输队服役。

3 ADJ 隶属于…的;附属的 If one organization or institution is attached to a larger organization, it is part of that organization and is controlled and run by it. 隶属于…的;附属的

  • At one time the schools were mainly attached to the church. [+ to]

    这些学校曾一度归教会主管。

  • ...a think-tank, attached to the Academy of Sciences.

    为科学院出谋划策的智囊团
