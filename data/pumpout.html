pump out
1 PHRASAL VERB 大量生产（或提供） To pump out something means to produce or supply it continually and in large amounts. 大量生产（或提供） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Japanese companies have been pumping out plenty of innovative products. [VERB PREP. noun]

    日本公司一直在生产大量富有创意的产品。

  • World Service Television is pumping out 24-hour news to 38 countries in Asia. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    英国广播公司世界电视台正24小时不间断地向38个亚洲国家播出新闻。

2 PHRASAL VERB （流行音乐等）响亮地奏出，喧闹地传出 If pop music pumps out, it plays very loudly. （流行音乐等）响亮地奏出，喧闹地传出

  • Teenage disco music pumped out at every station. [VERB PREP.]

    每一站都喧闹地播放着十几岁少年所喜爱的劲爆迪斯科音乐。
