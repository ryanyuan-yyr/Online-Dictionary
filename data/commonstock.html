common stock
1 N-UNCOUNT 普通股 Common stock refers to the shares in a company that are owned by people who have a right to vote at the company's meetings and to receive part of the company's profits after the holders of preferred stock have been paid. 普通股 [US, business]

  • The company priced its offering of 2.7 million shares of common stock at 20 cents a share.

    这家公司把其抛出的270万股普通股的股价定为每股20美分。
