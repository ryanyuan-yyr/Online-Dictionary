quiet down
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)安静;(使)平静下来 If someone or something quiets down or if you quiet them down, they become less noisy or less active. (使)安静;(使)平静下来 [mainly US]

  • Once the vote was taken, things quieted down quickly. [VERB PREP.]

    一旦投完票，事情便很快平息了下来。

  • Try gradually to quiet them down as bedtime approaches. [VERB noun PREP.]

    临近就寝的时候，要尽量让他们渐渐安静下来。

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 quieten down
