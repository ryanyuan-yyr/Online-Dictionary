verifiable
verɪfaɪəbəl ˈvɛrəˌfaɪəbəl
ADJ 可证实的;可核实的 Something that is verifiable can be proved to be true or genuine. 可证实的;可核实的

  • This is not a romantic notion but verifiable fact.

    这不是一个不切实际的念头，而是一个可以证明的事实。

  • It is crucial that all documents presented are authentic and easily verifiable.

    所有提交的文件必须真实且易于查证，这一点很关键。

SYN provable, demonstrable, testable, attestable
