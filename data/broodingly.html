brooding
bruːdɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （气氛或感情）压抑的，令人不安的 Brooding is used to describe an atmosphere or feeling that makes you feel anxious or slightly afraid. （气氛或感情）压抑的，令人不安的 [literary]

  • The same heavy, brooding silence descended on them.

    他们也感觉到了同样沉重压抑的寂静。

SYN gloomy, troubled, depressed, moody  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （尤指对令人不快的事物）沉思的，苦苦思索的 If someone's expression or appearance is brooding, they look as if they are thinking deeply and seriously about something, especially something that is making them unhappy. （尤指对令人不快的事物）沉思的，苦苦思索的 [literary]

  • She kissed him and gazed into his dark, brooding eyes.

    她吻了吻他，久久凝视着他乌黑忧郁的双眼。
