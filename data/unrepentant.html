unrepentant
ʌnrɪpentənt ˌʌnrɪˈpɛntənt
ADJ 不思悔改的；不悔悟的；不知羞耻的 If you are unrepentant, you are not ashamed of your beliefs or actions. 不思悔改的；不悔悟的；不知羞耻的

  • Pamela was unrepentant about her strong language and abrasive remarks. [+ about]

    帕梅拉对自己那些脏话和伤人的言辞一点也不感到羞愧。

  • ...unrepentant defenders of the death penalty.

    顽固不化地赞成死刑的人

SYN impenitent, shameless, incorrigible, abandoned
