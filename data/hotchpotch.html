hotch-potch hɒtʃ pɒtʃ
(also hotchpotch)
N-SING 杂乱无章的一堆东西;大杂烩 A hotch-potch is an untidy mixture of different types of things. 杂乱无章的一堆东西;大杂烩 [British]

  • The palace is a complete hotch-potch of architectural styles. [+ of]

    这座宫殿完全是各种建筑风格的大杂烩。

SYN mixture, mess, jumble, medley
