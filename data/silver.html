silver
sɪlvəʳ ˈsɪlvər
Word forms: plural silvers
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 银 Silver is a valuable pale grey metal that is used for making jewellery and ornaments. 银

  • ...a hand-crafted brooch made from silver.

    一枚手工银制胸针

  • ...amber earrings set in silver.

    镶琥珀的银耳环

  • ...silver teaspoons.

    银茶匙

2 N-UNCOUNT 银币 Silver consists of coins that are made from silver or that look like silver. 银币

  • ...the basement where £150,000 in silver was buried.

    埋有 150,000 英镑银币的地下室

SYN silver coins, coins, loose change, (small) change  
3 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] 银器(尤指餐具) You can use silver to refer to all the things in a house that are made of silver, especially the cutlery and dishes. 银器(尤指餐具)

  • He beat the rugs and polished the silver.

    他拍打去地毯上的灰尘，还擦拭了银器。

SYN silverware, silver plate  
4 COLOUR 银色;银灰色;银白色 Silver is used to describe things that are shiny and pale grey in colour. 银色;银灰色;银白色

  • He had thick silver hair which needed cutting.

    他浓密的银发该理理了。

  • ...a silver sports car.

    一辆银色跑车

  • Using silver tape, they taped all the doors and windows shut.

    他们用银色的胶带将所有的门窗都封起来了。

5 N-VAR 同 silver medal A silver is the same as a silver medal . 同 silver medal

  • Britain went on to take bronze and then followed it up by winning silver in the World Cup.

    英国斩获了铜牌，接下来又赢得了世界杯的银牌。

SYN second prize, silver medal  
6 born with a silver spoon in your mouth→see: spoon
