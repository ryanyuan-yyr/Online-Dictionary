stellar
steləʳ ˈstɛlər
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 星的;星球的 Stellar is used to describe anything connected with stars. 星的;星球的

  • A stellar wind streams outward from the star.

    从那颗恒星上不断吹出恒星风。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 杰出的;优秀的;出色的 A stellar person or thing is considered to be very good. 杰出的;优秀的;出色的

  • ...a stellar education at Eton and Oxford.

    伊顿和牛津的精英教育

  • The French companies are registering stellar profits.

    法国各家公司都获利丰厚。
