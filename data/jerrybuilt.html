jerry-built
dʒeri bɪlt ˈdʒɛrɪˌbɪlt ˈdʒɛriˌbɪlt
ADJ-GRADED 草率建成的；粗制滥造的 If you describe houses or blocks of flats as jerry-built, you are critical of the fact that they have been built very quickly and cheaply, without much care for safety or quality. 草率建成的；粗制滥造的 [disapproval]

  • ...jerry-built equipment.

    偷工减料的设备

  • The place is a bit jerry-built.

    这地方建造得有些不牢靠。

SYN ramshackle, cheap, faulty, shabby
