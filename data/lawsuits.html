lawsuit
lɔːsuːt ˈlɔˌsut
Word forms: plural lawsuits
N-COUNT 诉讼；官司 A lawsuit is a case in a court of law which concerns a dispute between two people or organizations. 诉讼；官司 [formal]

  • The dispute culminated last week in a lawsuit against the government. [+ against]

    上周这一纠纷达到高潮，政府被告上了法庭。

  • ...a lawsuit brought by Barclays Bank.

    由巴克莱银行提起的诉讼

SYN case, cause, action, trial
