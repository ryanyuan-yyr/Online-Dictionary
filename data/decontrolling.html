decontrol
diːkəntroʊl ˌdiːkənˈtrəʊl ˌdikənˈtroʊl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense decontrols, present participle decontrolling, past tense , past participle decontrolled
VERB 解除对…的管制 When governments decontrol an activity, they remove controls from it so that companies or organizations have more freedom. 解除对…的管制 [mainly US]

  • The Russian government chose not to decontrol oil and gas prices last January. [VERB noun]

    去年1月份俄罗斯政府决定暂不放开对石油和天然气价格的管制。

Decontrol is also a noun.

  • ...continuing decontrol of banking institutions.

    进一步解除对银行机构的管制
