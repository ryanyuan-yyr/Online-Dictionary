copier
kɒpiəʳ ˈkɑpiər
Word forms: plural copiers
1 N-COUNT 复印机;影印机 A copier is a machine which makes exact copies of writing or pictures on paper, usually by a photographic process. 复印机;影印机
2 N-COUNT 抄袭者 A copier is someone who copies what someone else has done. 抄袭者

  • ...their reputation as a copier of other countries' designs, patents, and inventions. [+ of]

    他们抄袭别国设计、专利和发明的恶名
