militarism
mɪlɪtərɪzəm ˈmɪlətəˌrɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 军国主义;黩武主义 Militarism is a country's desire to strengthen their armed forces in order to make themselves more powerful. 军国主义;黩武主义 [disapproval]

  • The country slipped into a dangerous mixture of nationalism and militarism.

    这个国家陷进了一种民族主义和军国主义并存的危险境地。
