recipe
resɪpi ˈrɛsəˌpi
Word forms: plural recipes
1 N-COUNT 烹饪配方；食谱 A recipe is a list of ingredients and a set of instructions that tell you how to cook something. 烹饪配方；食谱

  • ...a traditional recipe for oatmeal biscuits.

    燕麦饼干的传统制作配方

  • ...a recipe book.

    食谱书

2 N-SING 原因 If you say that something is a recipe for a particular situation, you mean that it is likely to result in that situation. 原因

  • Large-scale inflation is a recipe for disaster.

    大规模的通货膨胀会造成灾难。

SYN method, formula, prescription, process
