baptism of fire
Word forms: plural baptisms of fire
N-COUNT [usually singular] 严峻的考验 If someone who has just begun a new job has a baptism of fire, they immediately have to cope with very many severe difficulties and obstacles. 严峻的考验

  • It was Mark's first introduction to royal duties and he came through his baptism of fire unscathed.

    这是马克第一次承担皇室的工作，但他经受住了严峻的考验。
