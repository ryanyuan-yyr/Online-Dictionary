begging letter
Word forms: plural begging letters
N-COUNT （请求经济援助的）求助信，募捐信 A begging letter is a letter from a person or organization in which they ask you to send some money for a particular purpose. （请求经济援助的）求助信，募捐信 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • I wrote hundreds of begging letters to charities and businesses.

    我给慈善团体和商业机构写了几百封求助信。
