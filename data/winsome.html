winsome
wɪnsəm ˈwɪnsəm
ADJ-GRADED 迷人的;动人的；有魅力的 If you describe a person or their actions or behaviour as winsome, you mean that they are attractive and charming. 迷人的;动人的；有魅力的

  • ...a winsome young screen star.

    迷人的年轻影视明星

  • She gave him her best winsome smile.

    她给了他一个最为迷人的微笑。

SYN charming, taking, winning, pleasing
