ward off
PHRASAL VERB 避开，躲开，防止(危险、疾病等) To ward off a danger or illness means to prevent it from affecting you or harming you. 避开，躲开，防止(危险、疾病等) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She may have put up a fight to try to ward off her assailant. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她可能进行了顽强的反抗，试图击退袭击者。

  • Mass burials are now under way in an effort to ward off an outbreak of cholera. [VERB PREP. noun]

    为防止霍乱爆发正在进行大规模掩埋。

SYN drive off, resist, confront, fight off
