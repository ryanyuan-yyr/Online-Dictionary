devastated
devəsteɪtɪd ˈdɛvəsteɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 极为震惊难过的;非常伤心的 If you are devastated by something, you are very shocked and upset by it. 极为震惊难过的;非常伤心的

  • Bishop Daly said he was devastated by news of the Cardinal's death.

    戴利主教说他听到红衣主教去世的消息后感到极为震惊和难过。

  • Teresa was devastated, her dreams shattered.

    特雷莎伤心欲绝—她的梦想彻底破灭了。

SYN shattered, shocked, stunned, overcome
