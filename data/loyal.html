loyal
lɔɪəl ˈlɔɪəl
ADJ-GRADED 忠诚的；忠心的；忠贞不渝的 Someone who is loyal remains firm in their friendship or support for a person or thing. 忠诚的；忠心的；忠贞不渝的 [approval]

  • They had remained loyal to the president. [+ to]

    他们始终对总统忠心耿耿。

  • He'd always been such a loyal friend to us all.

    他一直是我们大家忠实的朋友。

SYN faithful, true, devoted, dependable  
loyally

  • They have loyally supported their party and their leader.

    他们忠诚地拥护他们的政党和领袖。
