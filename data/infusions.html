infusion
ɪnfjuːʒən ɪnˈfjuʒən
Word forms: plural infusions
1 N-VAR 输入;灌输 If there is an infusion of one thing into another, the first thing is added to the other thing and makes it stronger or better. 输入;灌输 [formal]

  • He brought a tremendous infusion of hope to the people. [+ of]

    他给人们带来巨大希望。

  • ...a series of capital infusions.

    一系列的资本输入

SYN injection, introduction, dose, insertion  
2 N-COUNT [usually with supplement, also by N] 浸剂;浸液 An infusion is a liquid made by leaving herbs in hot water until the flavour is strong. 浸剂;浸液

  • ...a herbal infusion.

    草药浸液

  • This remedy contains infusions of five flowers.

    这个药方中包含了 5 种花的浸剂。
