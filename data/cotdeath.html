cot death
Word forms: plural cot deaths
N-VAR 婴儿猝死 Cot death is the sudden death of a baby while it is asleep, although the baby had not previously been ill. 婴儿猝死 [British]

  • In developed countries, cot death is the main cause of death between the ages of one week and one year.

    在发达国家，婴儿猝死是一周到一岁的婴儿死亡的主要原因。

  • Doctors still aren't sure what causes cot deaths.

    医生仍不确定婴儿猝死的原因。
