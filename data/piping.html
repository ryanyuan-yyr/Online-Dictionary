piping
paɪpɪŋ ˈpaɪpɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （金属、塑料等的）管子，管材 Piping is metal, plastic, or another substance made in the shape of a pipe or tube. （金属、塑料等的）管子，管材

  • ...rolls of bright yellow plastic piping.

    成卷的明黄色塑料管

2 N-UNCOUNT （衣服、垫子等的）滚边 Piping is cloth made into a narrow tube. Piping is used to decorate the edges of clothing and things such as cushions. （衣服、垫子等的）滚边

  • The red dress had slim black piping around the neck.

    这条红裙的领口有细细的黑色滚边。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 尖声的；高声的 A piping voice is high-pitched. 尖声的；高声的 [written]

  • As she was about to follow there came a piping voice from upstairs. 'Is that Daddy back?'

    她刚要跟上，楼上便有人高声问道：“是爸爸回来了吗？”
