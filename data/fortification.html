fortification
fɔːʳtɪfɪkeɪʃən ˌfɔrtəfɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural fortifications
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 堡垒;要塞;防御工事 Fortifications are buildings, walls, or ditches that are built to protect a place and make it more difficult to attack. 堡垒;要塞;防御工事
