narcissistic
nɑːʳsɪsɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 自我陶醉的;自我欣赏的 If you describe someone as narcissistic, you disapprove of them because they think about themselves a lot and admire themselves too much. 自我陶醉的;自我欣赏的 [formal, disapproval]

  • He was insufferable at times — self-centred and narcissistic.

    他有时让人难以容忍—自我中心，自我陶醉。

  • ...the image of the vain, narcissistic man.

    自负自恋的男人形象

SYN self-loving, conceited, self-centred, self-absorbed
