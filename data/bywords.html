byword
baɪwɜːʳd ˈbaɪˌwɜrd
Word forms: plural bywords
1 N-COUNT （某一特征的）代名词，代表人物，典型事物 Someone or something that is a byword for a particular quality is well known for having that quality. （某一特征的）代名词，代表人物，典型事物

  • ...a region that had become a byword for violence and degeneracy.

    成了暴力和堕落代名词的地区

  • ...the Rolls-Royce brand name, a byword for quality. [+ for]

    劳斯莱斯品牌—品质的典范

2 N-COUNT 口头禅；俗语 A byword is a word or phrase which people often use. 口头禅；俗语

  • Loyalty and support became the bywords of the day. [+ of]

    忠诚和支持成了那个时代人们时常挂在嘴边的词儿。

SYN saying, slogan, motto, maxim
