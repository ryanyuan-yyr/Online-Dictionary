press release
Word forms: plural press releases
N-COUNT （向媒体发布的）新闻稿 A press release is a written statement about a matter of public interest which is given to the press by an organization concerned with the matter. （向媒体发布的）新闻稿

  • The government had put out a press release naming the men.

    政府已经发布了新闻稿，宣布这些人的任命。
