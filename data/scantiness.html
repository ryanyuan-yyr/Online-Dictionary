scanty
skænti ˈskænti
Word forms: comparative scantier, superlative scantiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 偏小的;偏少的;不足的;欠缺的 You describe something as scanty when there is less of it than you think there should be. 偏小的;偏少的;不足的;欠缺的

  • So far, what scanty evidence we have points to two suspects.

    迄今为止，我们掌握的能够指证两个嫌疑犯的证据少得可怜。

SYN meagre, sparse, poor, thin  
2 ADJ-GRADED (衣服)暴露的 If someone is wearing scanty clothing, he or she is wearing clothes which are sexually revealing. (衣服)暴露的

  • ...a model in scanty clothing.

    衣着暴露的模特

SYN skimpy, short, brief, tight
