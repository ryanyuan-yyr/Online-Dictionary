prettify
prɪtɪfaɪ ˈprɪtɪˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense prettifies, present participle prettifying, past tense , past participle prettified
VERB 美化；粉饰 To prettify something, especially something that is not beautiful, means to make it appear pretty. 美化；粉饰 [disapproval]

  • ...just a clever effort to prettify animal slaughter. [VERB noun]

    不过是对残杀动物的行为进行美化的狡猾之举

  • It presented an intolerably prettified view of the countryside. [VERB-ed]

    它对农村进行了过度的美化。

SYN adorn, decorate, garnish, ornament
