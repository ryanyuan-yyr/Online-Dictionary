forbear
fɔːʳbeəʳ ˈfɔːˌbɛə fɔːˈbɛə ˈfɔrˌbɛr fɔrˈbɛr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense forbears, present participle forbearing, past tense forbore, past participle forborne
VERB 忍耐;克制;避免 If you forbear to do something, you do not do it although you have the opportunity or the right to do it. 忍耐;克制;避免 [formal]

  • I forbore to comment on this. [VERB to-infinitive]

    我克制自己对此不予置评。

  • Protesters largely forbore from stone-throwing and vandalism. [VERB + from]

    抗议者绝大多数没有投掷石头和蓄意破坏。

SYN refrain, avoid, omit, hold back
