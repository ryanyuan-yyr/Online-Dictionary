ledge
ledʒ lɛdʒ
Word forms: plural ledges
1 N-COUNT （位于山崖边向外平伸的）岩架，岩脊 A ledge is a piece of rock on the side of a cliff or mountain, which is in the shape of a narrow shelf. （位于山崖边向外平伸的）岩架，岩脊
2 N-COUNT 窗台 A ledge is a narrow shelf along the bottom edge of a window. 窗台

  • She had climbed onto the ledge outside his window.

    她爬到了他窗外的窗台上。

SYN shelf, step, ridge, projection
