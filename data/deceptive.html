deceptive
dɪseptɪv diˈsɛptɪv dɪˈsɛptɪv
ADJ-GRADED 欺骗性的;造成假象的 If something is deceptive, it encourages you to believe something which is not true. 欺骗性的;造成假象的

  • Appearances can be deceptive.

    外表可能是靠不住的。

SYN misleading, false, fake, mock  
deceptively

  • The storyline is deceptively simple.

    故事情节看似简单，其实不然。
