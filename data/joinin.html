join in
PHRASAL VERB 加入，参加（活动） If you join in an activity, you take part in it or become involved in it. 加入，参加（活动）

  • I hope that everyone will be able to join in the fun. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我希望每个人都能够加入进来，玩得开心。

  • He started to sing and I joined in. [VERB PREP.]

    他开始唱起歌来，然后我也跟着唱起来。

SYN take part, contribute, participate, pitch in
