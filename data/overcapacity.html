overcapacity
oʊvəʳkəpæsɪti ˌəʊvəkəˈpæsɪtɪ ˌoʊvərkəˈpæsəti
N-UNCOUNT 生产能力过剩 If there is overcapacity in a particular industry or area, more goods have been produced than are needed, and the industry is therefore less profitable than it could be. 生产能力过剩 [business]

  • There is huge overcapacity in the world car industry.

    世界汽车工业的生产能力严重过剩。
