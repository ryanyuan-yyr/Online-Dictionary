doctrinaire
dɒktrɪneəʳ ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛə ˌdɑktrɪˈnɛr
ADJ-GRADED 空谈理论的;脱离实际的;教条主义的 If you say that someone is doctrinaire or has a doctrinaire attitude, you disapprove of them because they have fixed principles which they try to force on other people. 空谈理论的;脱离实际的;教条主义的 [formal, disapproval]

  • He is firm but not doctrinaire.

    他很坚定但并不教条。
