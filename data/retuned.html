retune
riːtjuːn -tuːn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense retunes, present participle retuning, past tense , past participle retuned
VERB 调整（频率、频道） To retune a piece of equipment such as a radio, television, or video means to adjust it so that it receives a different channel, or so that it receives the same channel on a different frequency. 调整（频率、频道）

  • ...this means that listeners in cars should not have to retune as they drive across the country. [VERB]

    这就意味着驾车的听众在全国各地行驶时都不需要重新调谐。

  • ...plans to retune VCRs to allow viewers to receive the signal. [VERB noun]

    重新调整录像机以使观众能够接受信号的计划
