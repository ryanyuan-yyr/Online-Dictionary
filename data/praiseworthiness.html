praiseworthy
preɪzwɜːʳði ˈpreɪzˌwɜrði
1 ADJ-GRADED 值得赞扬的；可嘉的 If you say that something is praiseworthy, you mean that you approve of it and it deserves to be praised. 值得赞扬的；可嘉的 [formal, approval]

  • ...the government's praiseworthy efforts to improve efficiency in health and education.

    政府在提高卫生和教育事业的效率上所付出的值得赞扬的努力

SYN creditable, fine, excellent, worthy
