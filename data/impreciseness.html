imprecise
ɪmprɪsaɪs ˌɪmprɪˈsaɪs
ADJ-GRADED 不清楚的;不精确的;不准确的 Something that is imprecise is not clear, accurate, or precise. 不清楚的;不精确的;不准确的

  • The charges were vague and imprecise.

    指控的内容模糊不清。

  • Utilitarianism is a very broad, imprecise concept that covers a multitude of underlying theoretical positions.

    实用主义是一个涵盖了许多潜在理论立场的非常宽泛的、不精确的概念。

SYN indefinite, estimated, rough, vague
