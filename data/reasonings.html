reasoning
riːzənɪŋ ˈriːzənɪŋ ˈrizənɪŋ
Word forms: plural reasonings
N-VAR 推理；论证 Reasoning is the process by which you reach a conclusion after thinking about all the facts. 推理；论证

  • ...the reasoning behind the decision.

    决定背后的论证

  • She was not really convinced by this line of reasoning.

    这样的分析论证实际上并没有让她信服。

SYN argument, proof, interpretation, hypothesis
