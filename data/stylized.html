stylized
staɪlaɪzd ˈstaɪlaɪzd

ADJ-GRADED 非写实的;有艺术效果的 Something that is stylized is shown or done in a way that is not natural in order to create an artistic effect. 非写实的;有艺术效果的

    其中一些与最近几部有着浓厚艺术气息的舞台音乐剧有关。

  • ...fabric designs which featured stylized flowers and foliage.

    由唯美的花朵和叶子构成的织物图案
