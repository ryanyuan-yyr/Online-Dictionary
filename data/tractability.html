tractable
træktəbəl ˈtræktəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 驯服的;温顺的;易驾驭的;易处理的 If you say that a person, problem, or device is tractable, you mean that they can be easily controlled or dealt with. 驯服的;温顺的;易驾驭的;易处理的 [formal]

  • He could easily manage his tractable and worshipping younger brother.

    他能轻而易举地管住听话并且崇拜自己的弟弟。

  • ...the country's least tractable social problems.

    该国最难解决的社会问题

SYN manageable, obedient, compliant, willing
