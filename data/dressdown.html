dress down
1 PHRASAL VERB (比平时)穿着随便 If you dress down, you wear clothes that are less smart than usual. (比平时)穿着随便

  • She dresses down in baggy clothes to avoid hordes of admirers. [VERB PREP.]

    她随便穿了件宽松的衣服以免被大批的粉丝认出。

SYN dress casually, dress informally, be untidy  
2 PHRASAL VERB 训斥;责骂 If you dress someone down, you speak angrily to them because they have done something bad or foolish. 训斥;责骂 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Campbell dressed them down in public. [VERB noun PREP.]

    坎贝尔当众训斥了他们。

SYN reprimand, rebuke, scold, berate
