thereabouts
ðeərəbaʊts ˈðɛərəˌbaʊts ˈðɛrəˌbaʊts
PHRASE (数量、日期等)大约，上下，左右 You add or thereabouts after a number or date to indicate that it is approximate. (数量、日期等)大约，上下，左右

  • He told us that her age was forty-eight or thereabouts.

    他告诉我们她的年龄在48岁上下。

  • By 1997 or thereabouts Athens will have a new airport, plus the best underground railway in Europe.

    到 1997 年前后，雅典将有一个新机场，还有欧洲最先进的地铁。
