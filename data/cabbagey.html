cabbage
kæbɪdʒ ˈkæbɪdʒ
Word forms: plural cabbages
1 N-VAR 卷心菜;洋白菜;甘蓝 A cabbage is a round vegetable with white, green or purple leaves that is usually eaten cooked. 卷心菜;洋白菜;甘蓝
2 N-COUNT 植物人;（脑部受损或患重病的）废人 If someone refers to a very sick or disabled person as a cabbage, they are saying in an offensive way that the sick person is so badly brain-damaged or ill that they cannot do anything. 植物人;（脑部受损或患重病的）废人
