cabal
kəbæl kəˈbɑl kəˈbæl
Word forms: plural cabals
N-COUNT 阴谋小集团;秘密团体 If you refer to a group of politicians or other people as a cabal, you are criticizing them because they meet and decide things secretly. 阴谋小集团;秘密团体 [disapproval]

  • He had been chosen by a cabal of fellow senators. [+ of]

    他已被一个由一些参议员同仁组成的小集团选中。

  • ...a secret government cabal.

    秘密的政府阴谋集团

SYN clique, set, party, league
