floral
flɔːrəl ˈflɔrəl ˈfloʊrəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有花形图案的;绘有花的 A floral fabric or design has flowers on it. 有花形图案的;绘有花的

  • ...a bright yellow floral fabric.

    有明黄色花卉图案的织物

SYN flowery, flower-patterned  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 由花组成的;有鲜花装饰的 You can use floral to describe something that contains flowers or is made of flowers. 由花组成的;有鲜花装饰的

  • ...eye-catching floral arrangements.

    引人注目的插花
