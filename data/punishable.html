punishable
pʌnɪʃəbəl ˈpʌnɪʃəbəl ˈpʌnɪʃəbəl
ADJ 可处罚的；该惩罚的 If a crime is punishable in a particular way, anyone who commits it is punished in that way. 可处罚的；该惩罚的

  • Treason in this country is still punishable by death. [+ by/with]

    在这个国家，叛国仍然是可判死罪的。

  • They called on the authorities to make slavery a punishable offence.

    他们呼吁当局将蓄奴定为一种应该受到惩罚的罪行。

SYN culpable, criminal, chargeable, indictable
