steak
steɪk steɪk
Word forms: plural steaks
1 N-VAR 牛排 A steak is a large flat piece of beef without much fat on it. You cook it by grilling or frying it. 牛排
2See also:rump steak  T-bone steak 
3 N-UNCOUNT (常指切成块出售、供炖焖用的)牛肉 Steak is beef that is used for making stews. It is often cut into cubes to be sold. (常指切成块出售、供炖焖用的)牛肉

  • ...steak and kidney pie.

    牛肉腰子馅饼

4See also:stewing steak 
5 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 鱼片;鱼排 A fish steak is a large piece of fish that contains few bones. 鱼片;鱼排

  • ...fresh salmon steaks.

    鲜鲑鱼鱼排
