refinance
riːfaɪnæns ˌriːfaɪˈnæns ˌrifəˈnæns riˈfaɪˌnæns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense refinances, present participle refinancing, past tense , past participle refinanced
VERB （为了清偿债务）重新融资，再筹资金 If a person or a company refinances a debt or if they refinance, they borrow money in order to pay the debt. （为了清偿债务）重新融资，再筹资金 [business]

  • A loan was arranged to refinance existing debt. [VERB noun]

    办理贷款以清偿现有债务。

  • It can be costly to refinance. [VERB]

    再融资有时费用会很高。

SYN take on a loan, borrow, remortgage
