bathed
beɪðd beɪðd
1 ADJ 大汗淋漓的；汗水淋淋的 If someone is bathed in sweat, they are sweating a great deal. 大汗淋漓的；汗水淋淋的

  • Chantal was writhing in pain and bathed in perspiration. [+ in]

    夏恩托痛苦地翻来覆去，汗水淋淋。

2 ADJ 沉浸在（爱等情感）中的 If someone is bathed in a particular emotion such as love, they feel it constantly in a pleasant way. 沉浸在（爱等情感）中的 [literary]

  • ...a physical sensation of being bathed in love. [+ in]

    坠入爱河的生理快感

  • ...a relationship bathed in trust.

    充满了信任的关系
