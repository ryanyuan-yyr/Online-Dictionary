aft
ɑːft æft æft ɑft
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb, be ADVERB] 向艉部（或机尾）；在艉部（或机尾） If you go aft in a boat or plane, you go to the back of it. If you are aft, you are in the back. 向艉部（或机尾）；在艉部（或机尾）

  • Clark shook hands with the pilot and walked aft to find his way off the ship.

    克拉克与舵手握手后向艉部走去，打算下船。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 艉部的；后部的 The aft end on a boat or plane is towards the back of it. 艉部的；后部的

  • Getting into the aft cabin involves a slight step down from saloon level.

    去后舱需要从餐厅的位置略微往下迈一小步。
