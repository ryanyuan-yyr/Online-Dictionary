raft
rɑːft ræft ræft rɑft
Word forms: plural rafts
1 N-COUNT 木排；木筏；筏子 A raft is a floating platform made from large pieces of wood or other materials tied together. 木排；木筏；筏子

  • ...a river trip on bamboo rafts through dense rainforest.

    坐竹筏穿过茂密雨林的河上之旅

2 N-COUNT 充气浮垫；救生筏 A raft is a small rubber or plastic boat that you blow air into to make it float. 充气浮垫；救生筏

  • The crew spent two days and nights in their raft.

    船员在救生筏上呆了两天两夜。

3See also:life raft 
4 N-COUNT [usually singular] 大堆；大量 A raft of people or things is a lot of them. 大堆；大量

  • He has surrounded himself with a raft of advisers who are very radical. [+ of]

    他周围聚集了一大群偏激的顾问。

  • This is likely to revive consumer spending and a whole raft of consumer industries.

    这可能会带动消费性支出和一大批消费工业的复苏。
