ringleader
rɪŋliːdəʳ ˈrɪŋˌlidər
Word forms: plural ringleaders
N-COUNT 挑起争端者;头目;首恶;魁首 The ringleaders in a quarrel, disturbance, or illegal activity are the people who started it and who cause most of the trouble. 挑起争端者;头目;首恶;魁首 [disapproval]

  • The soldiers were well informed about the ringleaders of the protest.

    士兵们对这次抗议活动的带头者已经了如指掌。
