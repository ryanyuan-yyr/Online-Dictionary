tidings
taɪdɪŋz ˈtaɪdɪŋz
N-PLURAL [usually adjective NOUN] 消息 You can use tidings to refer to news that someone tells you. 消息 [formal, old-fashioned] [Also + of]

  • He hated always to be the bearer of bad tidings.

    他讨厌总是带去坏消息。

  • I dare to hope that your heart will be touched by the tidings of my survival.

    我奢望你在听到我还活着的消息时心情会很激动。
