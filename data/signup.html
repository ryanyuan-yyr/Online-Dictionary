sign up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (和…)签约;雇用;报名(参加课程) If you sign up for an organization or if an organization signs you up, you sign a contract officially agreeing to do a job or course of study. (和…)签约;雇用;报名(参加课程) [Also V P + for]

  • He signed up as a steward with P&O Lines. [VERB PREP. + as]

    他签约了P&O航运公司，成为了一名乘务员。

  • He saw the song's potential, and persuaded the company to sign her up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他看到了这首歌的潜力，就说服公司和她签约。

SYN enlist, join, volunteer, register
