filth
fɪlθ fɪlθ
1 N-UNCOUNT 肮脏;污秽;污物 Filth is a disgusting amount of dirt. 肮脏;污秽;污物

  • The living-room floor was littered with filth and tin cans.

    客厅地上丢满了脏东西和罐头听。

  • Thousands of tons of filth and sewage pour into the Ganges every day.

    每天都有成千上万吨污物和废水排入恒河。

SYN dirt, refuse, pollution, muck  
2 N-UNCOUNT 淫秽;秽亵;脏话 People refer to words or pictures, usually ones relating to sex, as filth when they think they are very disgusting and rude. 淫秽;秽亵;脏话 [disapproval]

  • The dialogue was all filth and innuendo.

    这段对话全是下流的言辞和性暗示。

  • ...pornographic filth.

    色情淫秽出版物

SYN obscenity, corruption, pornography, indecency
