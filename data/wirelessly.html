wireless
waɪəʳləs waɪrlɪs
Word forms: plural wirelesses
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 无线的;用无线电波传送的 Wireless technology uses radio waves rather than electricity and therefore does not require any wires. 无线的;用无线电波传送的

  • ...the fast-growing wireless communication market.

    迅速扩张的无线通讯市场

2 N-UNCOUNT 无线电;无线电收发系统 Wireless is a system by which messages are sent over a distance by radio signals. 无线电;无线电收发系统 [British, old-fashioned]

  • ...the development of wireless and radar.

    无线电与雷达的发展

  • They confirmed by wireless that she was picking up survivors.

    他们通过无线电证实她在营救幸存者。

3 N-COUNT 无线电收音机 A wireless or wireless set is a radio. 无线电收音机 [British, old-fashioned]
