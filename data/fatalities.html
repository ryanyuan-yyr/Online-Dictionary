fatality
fətælɪti feɪˈtæləti fəˈtæləti
Word forms: plural fatalities
1 N-COUNT (事故或暴力导致的)死亡 A fatality is a death caused by an accident or by violence. (事故或暴力导致的)死亡 [formal]

  • Drunk driving fatalities have declined more than 10 percent over the past 10 years.

    酒后驾车死亡人数在过去 10 年中下降了 10%以上。

2 N-UNCOUNT 宿命感;宿命论 Fatality is the feeling or belief that human beings cannot influence or control events. 宿命感;宿命论

  • ...with a feeling of fatality.

    怀着宿命感
