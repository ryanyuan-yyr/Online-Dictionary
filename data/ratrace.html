rat race
N-SING （职场上）激烈的竞争，你死我活的竞争 If you talk about getting out of the rat race, you mean leaving a job or way of life in which people compete aggressively with each other to be successful. （职场上）激烈的竞争，你死我活的竞争

  • I had to get out of the rat race and take a look at the real world again.

    我得从疯狂的竞争中解脱出来，重新审视真实的世界。
