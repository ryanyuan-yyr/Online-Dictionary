clock up
PHRASAL VERB 达到(某一数目或总数) If you clock up a large number or total of things, you reach that number or total. 达到(某一数目或总数)

  • In two years, he clocked up over 100 victories. [VERB PREP. noun]

    两年内，他赢了一百多场。

  • Rude taxi drivers clocked up a total of 239 offences in 1990. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    1990年，出租车司机鲁莽驾驶导致的违章事件总共达239起。
