fart
fɑːʳt fɑrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense farts, present participle farting, past tense , past participle farted
1 VERB 放屁 If someone farts, air is forced out of their body through their anus. 放屁 [informal, rude]

  • He'd been farting all night. [VERB]

    他整晚都在放屁。

Fart is also a noun.

  • ...a loud fart.

    响屁

2 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] (老)东西;无聊的家伙 If someone describes another person as an old fart, they are showing that they think the person is boring and that they do not respect the person. (老)东西;无聊的家伙 [informal, rude, disapproval]
