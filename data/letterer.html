    辞职信

    我俩长期的恋爱过程基本上靠的是书信。

SYN message, line, answer, note

6 PHRASE [preposition PHRASE, PHRASE after verb] （钻）法律字眼 If you say that someone keeps to the letter of the law, you mean that they act according to what is actually written in the law, rather than according to the general principles of it, especially when you disapprove of this. （钻）法律字眼

  • They had stuck to the letter of the law and in my view they should be ashamed.

    他们死抠法律字眼。依我看他们应该感到惭愧。

7 PHRASE （执行指示）毫厘不爽地，不折不扣地 If you say that someone carries out instructions to the letter, you mean that they do exactly what they are told to do, paying great attention to every detail. （执行指示）毫厘不爽地，不折不扣地
