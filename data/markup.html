mark up
1 PHRASAL VERB 提高…的价格；抬价 If you mark something up, you increase its price. 提高…的价格；抬价

  • You can sell it to them at a set wholesale price, allowing them to mark it up for retail. [VERB noun PREP.]

    你可以以固定的批发价出售给他们，这样他们可以提高零售价格。

  • A typical warehouse club marks up its goods by only 10 to 15 percent. [VERB PREP. noun]

    典型的仓储超市仅给商品加价10%至15%。

SYN increase the price of, raise the price of
