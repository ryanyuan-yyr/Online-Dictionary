blasé blɑːzeɪ blɑːzeɪ blɑˈzeɪ ˈblɑzeɪ
(also blase)
ADJ (因司空见惯而)无动于衷的; 漠然的; 厌倦的If you describe someone as blasé, you mean that they are not easily impressed, excited, or worried by things, usually because they have seen or experienced them before. (因司空见惯而)无动于衷的; 漠然的; 厌倦的 [disapproval]

  • They are blasé about their driving skills.

    他们对自己的车技充满信心，以致漫不经心。

  • ...his seemingly blasé attitude.

    ...他的那种看上去漠不关心的态度。

SYN nonchalant, cool, bored, distant
