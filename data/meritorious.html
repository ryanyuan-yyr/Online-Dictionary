meritorious
merɪtɔːriəs ˌmɛrɪˈtɔriəs
ADJ-GRADED 优异的;有价值的 If you describe something as meritorious, you approve of it for its good or worthwhile qualities. 优异的;有价值的 [formal, approval]

  • I had been promoted for what was called gallant and meritorious service.

    我之所以得到提拔是由于立下了所谓的英勇卓越的功勋。

SYN praiseworthy, admirable, exemplary, good
