foul-up
ˈfaʊlʌp ˈfaʊlˌʌp
Word forms: plural foul-ups
N-COUNT (由于人为错误或疏忽造成的)糟糕的问题，麻烦事 A foul-up is something that has gone badly wrong as a result of someone's mistakes or carelessness. (由于人为错误或疏忽造成的)糟糕的问题，麻烦事 [informal]

  • A series of technical foul-ups delayed the launch of the new product.

    一系列技术问题延误了新产品的上市。
