border on
1 PHRASAL VERB 接近；近似 If you talk about a characteristic or situation bordering on something, usually something that you consider bad, you mean that it is almost that thing. 接近；近似

  • He has never exhibited the self-confidence, bordering on arrogance, of his predecessor. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他从未表现出他前任的那种近乎傲慢的自信。

  • The atmosphere borders on the surreal. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这种氛围近似梦幻。

SYN come close to, approach, be like, resemble
