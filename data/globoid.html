globe
gloʊb gloʊb
Word forms: plural globes
1 N-SING 地球，世界（用于强调其广大或某事在许多地区发生） You can refer to the world as the globe when you are emphasizing how big it is or that something happens in many different parts of it. 地球，世界（用于强调其广大或某事在许多地区发生）

  • ...bottles of beer from every corner of the globe.

    来自世界各个角落的一瓶瓶啤酒

  • 70% of our globe's surface is water.

    地球表面70%的地方覆盖着水。

SYN planet, world, round, ball  
2 N-COUNT 地球仪 A globe is a ball-shaped object with a map of the world on it. It is usually fixed on a stand. 地球仪

  • ...a globe of the world.

    世界地球仪

  • Three large globes stand on the floor.

    3个巨大的地球仪立在地板上。

3 N-COUNT 球状物；球体 Any ball-shaped object can be referred to as a globe . 球状物；球体

  • The overhead light was covered now with a white globe.

    现在，顶灯已装上了一个白色球状罩子。
