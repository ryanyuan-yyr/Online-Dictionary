self
self sɛlf
Word forms: plural selves
1 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 自身;本性;本我 Your self is your basic personality or nature, especially considered in terms of what you are really like as a person. 自身;本性;本我

  • You're looking more like your usual self.

    你现在看上去较像你平时的样子了。

  • She was back to her old self again.

    她又恢复了老样子。

SYN personality, character, temperament, identity  
2 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 个性;自我 A person's self is the essential part of their nature which makes them different from everyone and everything else. 个性;自我

  • I want to explore and get in touch with my inner self.

    我想认真探寻、了解内心的自我。

  • The face is the true self visible to others.

    这副面孔是他人所能见到的自我真实的一面。

  • ...our subconscious selves.

    我们潜意识中的自我

SYN soul, spirit, psyche, heart of hearts  
self- self- sɛlf
1 COMB in ADJ and N (用于构词)表示“靠自身的”，“由自身的”，“对自身的” Self- is used to form words which indicate that you do something to yourself or by yourself. (用于构词)表示“靠自身的”，“由自身的”，“对自身的”

  • He is a self-proclaimed racist.

    他自称是种族主义者。

  • ...self-destructive behaviour.

    自残行为

  • She was a woman utterly without self-knowledge.

    她是一个完全没有自知之明的女人。

2 COMB in ADJ and N (用于构词)表示“自动的”，“自行的” Self- is used to form words which describe something such as a device that does something automatically by itself. (用于构词)表示“自动的”，“自行的”

  • ...a self-loading pistol.

    自动上膛的手枪
