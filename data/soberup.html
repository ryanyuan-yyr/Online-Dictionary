sober up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (醉酒之后)醒过来 If someone sobers up, or if something sobers them up, they become sober after being drunk. (醉酒之后)醒过来

  • He was left to sober up in a police cell. [VERB PREP.]

    他被留在一间拘留室里醒酒。

  • ...the idea that a cup of strong black coffee sobers you up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    不加奶的浓咖啡可以醒酒的想法
