unmusical
ʌnmjuːzɪkəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 难听的；不悦耳的 An unmusical sound is unpleasant to listen to. 难听的；不悦耳的

  • Lainey had a terrible voice, unmusical and sharp.

    莱内的声音很恐怖，既难听又刺耳。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不懂音乐的；乐盲的 An unmusical person cannot play or appreciate music. 不懂音乐的；乐盲的

  • They're completely unmusical.

    他们完全不懂音乐。
