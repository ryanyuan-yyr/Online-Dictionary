volcano
vɒlkeɪnoʊ vɑlˈkeɪnoʊ vɔlˈkeɪnoʊ
Word forms: plural volcanoes
N-COUNT 火山 A volcano is a mountain from which hot melted rock, gas, steam, and ash from inside the earth sometimes burst. 火山

  • The volcano erupted last year killing about 600 people.

    去年那座火山喷发致使约600人遇难。

  • Etna is Europe's most active volcano.

    埃特纳山是欧洲最活跃的火山。
