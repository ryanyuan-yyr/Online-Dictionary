motivation
moʊtɪveɪʃən
Word forms: plural motivations
N-COUNT [usually with poss] 动力;诱因 Your motivation for doing something is what causes you to want to do it. 动力;诱因

  • Money is my motivation.

    金钱是我的动力。

  • The timing of the attack, and its motivations, are unknown.

    袭击发生的时间及其动机尚不清楚。

SYN incentive, inspiration, motive, stimulus
