silken
sɪlkən ˈsɪlkən
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 光洁柔软的;丝绸般的 Silken is used to describe things that are very pleasantly smooth and soft. 光洁柔软的;丝绸般的 [literary]

  • ...her long silken hair.

    她那丝滑的长发

  • ...her long, silken legs.

    她那光洁修长的双腿

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 丝质的;丝绸(般)的 A silken garment, fabric, or rope is made of silk or a material that looks like silk. 丝质的;丝绸(般)的 [literary]

  • ...a silken nightshirt.

    丝绸睡衣

  • ...silken cushions.

    丝绸垫子
