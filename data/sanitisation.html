sanitize
sænɪtaɪz ˈsænəˌtaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sanitizes, present participle sanitizing, past tense , past participle sanitized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 sanitise
VERB 美化;净化 To sanitize an activity or a situation that is unpleasant or unacceptable means to describe it in a way that makes it seem more pleasant or more acceptable. 美化;净化

  • ...crime writers who sanitise violence and make it respectable. [VERB noun]

    美化暴力使其显得正当的犯罪小说作家

  • He's worried that he's only going to get a sanitized version of what actually happened. [VERB-ed]

    他担心事情传到自己耳朵时，已经是净化过的版本。
