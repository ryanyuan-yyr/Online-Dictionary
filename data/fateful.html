fateful
feɪtfʊl feɪtfəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (行为或时刻)有重大影响的(常指负面影响)，决定性的 If an action or a time when something happened is described as fateful, it is considered to have an important, and often very bad, effect on future events. (行为或时刻)有重大影响的(常指负面影响)，决定性的

  • It was a fateful decision, one which was to break the Government.

    这是一项将会让政府垮台的重大决定。
