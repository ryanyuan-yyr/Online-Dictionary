open-door
(also open door)
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 对外开放的If a country or organization has an open-door policy toward people or goods, it allows them to come there freely, without any restrictions. 对外开放的

  • ...reformers who have advocated an open door economic policy.

    …提倡对外开放的经济政策的改革者们。

2 N-SING 对外开放Open door is also a noun. 对外开放

  • ...an open door to further foreign investment.

    …推动国外投资的对外开放。
