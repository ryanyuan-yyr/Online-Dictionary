attach
ətætʃ əˈtætʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense attaches, present participle attaching, past tense , past participle attached
1 缚上；系上；贴上 If you attach something to an object, you join it or fasten it to the object. 缚上；系上；贴上

  • We attach labels to things before we file them away. [VERB noun to noun]

    存档前，我们先贴上标签。

  • The gadget can be attached to any vertical surface. [be VERB-ed + to]

    这小玩意儿可以粘在任何垂直表面上。

  • The astronauts will attach a motor that will boost the satellite into its proper orbit. [VERB noun]

    宇航员们将安装一个发动机，以推动卫星进入正常运行轨道。

  • For further information, please contact us on the attached form. [VERB-ed]

    欲知详情，请使用所附表格联系我们。

2 （通常未受邀请而）加入，和…在一起，缠着 If someone attaches himself or herself to you, they join you and stay with you, often without being invited to do so. （通常未受邀请而）加入，和…在一起，缠着

  • Natasha attached herself to the film crew filming at her orphanage. [VERB pronoun-reflexive + to]

    娜塔莎紧紧缠着在孤儿院拍摄电影的工作人员不放。

3 赋予;认为…具有;为…所有 If people attach a quality to someone or something, or if it attaches to them, people consider that they have that quality. 赋予;认为…具有;为…所有

  • The authorities attached much significance to his visit. [VERB noun + to]

    当局非常重视他的访问。

  • ...the magic that still attaches to the word 'spy'. [VERB + to]

    “间谍”一词依然具有的神奇魅力

  • ...the stigma attached to mental illness. [VERB-ed]

    精神病所背负的污名

4 附加，附带（条件） If you attach conditions to something such as an agreement, you state that specific things must be done before the agreement is valid. 附加，附带（条件）

  • Activists are pressing the banks to attach political conditions to the signing of any new agreement. [VERB noun + to]

    激进分子强烈要求银行在签署任何新协议前要考虑政治条件。

  • Magistrates will be able to attach conditions when juveniles are remanded. [VERB noun]

    地方法官将可以在少年犯送押时提出附加条件。

5 （计算机中）附上，粘贴（附件） In computing, if you attach a file to a message that you send to someone, you send it with the message but separate from it. （计算机中）附上，粘贴（附件）

  • It is possible to attach executable program files to e-mail. [VERB noun + to]

    可以在电子邮件里附上可执行程序文件。
