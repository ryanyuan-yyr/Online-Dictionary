disenchantment
dɪsɪntʃɑːntmənt -tʃænt-
N-UNCOUNT 幻想破灭;醒悟 Disenchantment is the feeling of being disappointed with something, and no longer believing that it is good or worthwhile. 幻想破灭;醒悟

  • There's growing disenchantment with the Government. [+ with]

    大家对政府的幻想渐渐破灭了。

SYN disillusionment, disappointment, disillusion, rude awakening
