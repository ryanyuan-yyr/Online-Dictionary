tittle-tattle
tɪtəl tætəl ˈtɪtəlˌtætəl
N-UNCOUNT 闲聊;胡侃;流言蜚语 If you refer to something that a group of people talk about as tittle-tattle, you mean that you disapprove of it because it is not important, and there is no real evidence that it is true. 闲聊;胡侃;流言蜚语 [disapproval]

  • ...tittle-tattle about the private lives of minor celebrities. [+ about]

    有关二三流名人隐私生活的闲言碎语

SYN gossip, rumour, dirt [US] , goss [informal , slang]
