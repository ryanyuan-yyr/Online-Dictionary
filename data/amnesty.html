amnesty
æmnɪsti ˈæmnəsti
Word forms: plural amnesties
1 N-VAR 赦免；大赦 An amnesty is an official pardon granted to a group of prisoners by the state. 赦免；大赦

  • Activists who were involved in crimes of violence will not automatically be granted amnesty.

    参与暴力犯罪的激进分子将不会自动得到赦免。

SYN general pardon, mercy, pardoning, immunity  
2 N-COUNT 赦免期限（此期间交代罪行或交出武器可获赦免） An amnesty is a period of time during which people can admit to a crime or give up weapons without being punished. 赦免期限（此期间交代罪行或交出武器可获赦免）

  • The government has announced an immediate amnesty for rebel fighters. [+ for]

    政府已对叛乱士兵宣布了即刻生效的赦免期限。
