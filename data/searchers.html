searcher
sɜːʳtʃəʳ
Word forms: plural searchers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 搜寻者 Searchers are people who are looking for someone or something that is missing. 搜寻者

  • Searchers have found three mountain climbers missing since Saturday.

    搜救人员已经找到了周六失踪的3名登山者。

2 N-COUNT 探索者;探求者 A searcher is someone who is trying to find something such as the truth or the answer to a problem. 探索者;探求者

  • He's not a real searcher after truth. [+ after/for]

    他不是一个真正探求真理的人。
