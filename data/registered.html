registered
redʒɪstəʳd ˈrɛdʒɪstəd ˈrɛgɪstərd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （信或包裹）挂号的 A registered letter or parcel is sent by a special postal service, for which you pay extra money for insurance in case it gets lost. （信或包裹）挂号的

  • He asked his mother to send it by registered mail.

    他让他妈妈用挂号邮件寄。

  • ...an urgent registered letter.

    加急挂号信
