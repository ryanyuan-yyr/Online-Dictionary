misapplication
mɪsæplɪkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural misapplications
N-VAR 误用;滥用 If you talk about the misapplication of something, you mean it is used for a purpose it was not intended for. 误用;滥用

  • He's charged with conspiracy, misapplication of funds and other crimes. [+ of]

    他被控涉嫌阴谋破坏、滥用资金以及其他几项罪行。

  • ...a common misapplication of the law. [+ of]

    滥用法律的常见现象
