overextended
oʊvərɪkstendɪd ˌəʊvərɪkˈstɛndɪd
ADJ-GRADED （经济或体力上）参与过多活动的，捉襟见肘的 If a person or organization is overextended, they have become involved in more activities than they can financially or physically manage. （经济或体力上）参与过多活动的，捉襟见肘的

  • The British East India Tea Company was overextended and faced bankruptcy.

    英国东印度茶业公司摊子大、开支多，已濒临破产。

  • The overworked, overextended parent may be seen as unloving, but may simply be exhausted.

    工作压身、精力上捉襟见肘的家长可能会被视作对孩子缺乏关爱，但他们也许只是太疲惫了。
