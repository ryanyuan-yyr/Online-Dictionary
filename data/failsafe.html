fail-safe
ˈfeɪlˌseɪf
(also failsafe)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有自动保险装置的;具有自动防故障性能的 Something that is fail-safe is designed or made in such a way that nothing dangerous can happen if a part of it goes wrong. 有自动保险装置的;具有自动防故障性能的

  • The camera has a built-in failsafe device which prevents it from working if the right signals aren't received.

    照相机有内置自动防故障装置，该装置确保相机在未接到正确信号时不会启动。
