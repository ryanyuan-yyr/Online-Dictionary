deadpan
dedpæn ˈdɛdˌpæn
ADJ-GRADED (幽默）冷面的；(开玩笑或作弄人时）故意板着脸的，一脸严肃的 Deadpan humour is when you appear to be serious and are hiding the fact that you are joking or teasing someone. (幽默）冷面的；(开玩笑或作弄人时）故意板着脸的，一脸严肃的

  • ...her natural capacity for irony and deadpan humour.

    她天生具有的讥嘲讽刺和冷面幽默才能

  • She put the letter on the desk in front of me, her face deadpan, not a flicker of a smile.

    她把那封信放在我面前的桌子上，故意一脸严肃，没有一丝的笑容。

SYN expressionless, empty, blank, wooden
