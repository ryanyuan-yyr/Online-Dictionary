athlete
æθliːt ˈæθˌlit
Word forms: plural athletes
1 N-COUNT （尤指田径项目的）运动员，运动选手 An athlete is a person who does a sport, especially athletics, or track and field events. （尤指田径项目的）运动员，运动选手

  • Daley Thompson was a great athlete.

    戴利·汤普森是位著名的运动员。

SYN sportsperson, player, runner, competitor  
2 N-COUNT 运动健儿;身强力壮的人 You can refer to someone who is fit and athletic as an athlete . 运动健儿;身强力壮的人

  • I was no athlete.

    我根本不是当运动员的料。
