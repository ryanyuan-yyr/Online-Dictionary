repentance
rɪpentəns rɪˈpɛntəns
N-UNCOUNT 后悔;悔改;悔意;忏悔 If you show repentance for something wrong that you have done, you make it clear that you are sorry for doing it. 后悔;悔改;悔意;忏悔

  • They showed no repentance during their trial.

    在他们受审的过程中没有表现出一丝悔意。

SYN regret, guilt, grief, sorrow
