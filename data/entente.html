entente
ɒntɒnt ɑ̃tɑ̃t ɑnˈtɑnt ɑ̃ˈtɑ̃t
Word forms: plural ententes
N-VAR （国家间的）友好协议，谅解 An entente or an entente cordiale is a friendly agreement between two or more countries. （国家间的）友好协议，谅解

  • The French entente with Great Britain had already been significantly extended. [+ with]

    法国和英国之间友好协议的范围已经大幅度拓宽。

  • Electoral pacts would not work, but an entente cordiale might.

    选举协定不会起作用，但是政府间的谅解也许可以。

SYN agreement, understanding, accord, contract
