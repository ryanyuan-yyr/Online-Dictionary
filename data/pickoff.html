pick off
PHRASAL VERB 逐个瞄准射中 If someone picks off people or aircraft, they shoot them down one by one, aiming carefully at them from a distance. 逐个瞄准射中

  • Both groups on either side are just picking off innocent bystanders. [VERB PREP. noun]

    对峙双方都只是在瞄准射击无辜的旁观者。

  • Any decent shot with telescopic sights could pick us off at random. [VERB noun PREP.]

    任何借助望远镜瞄准器的像样射手都可以随意把我们逐个干掉。
