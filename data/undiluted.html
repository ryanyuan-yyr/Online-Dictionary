undiluted
ʌndaɪluːtɪd ˌʌndaɪˈluːtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 强烈的；没有掺杂的 If you describe someone's feelings or characteristics as undiluted, you are emphasizing that they are very strong and not mixed with any other feeling or quality. 强烈的；没有掺杂的

  • I will look back at this one with undiluted pleasure.

    回顾此事，我会无比地开心。

  • The report had not been received with undiluted enthusiasm.

    这份报告并未收到热烈的反响。

  • Her Irish accent, after thirty-odd years in London, is undiluted.

    她在伦敦呆了30多年，爱尔兰口音仍很浓重。

2 ADJ （液体）未稀释的 A liquid that is undiluted has not been made weak by mixing it with water. （液体）未稀释的
