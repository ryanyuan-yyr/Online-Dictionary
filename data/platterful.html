platter
plætəʳ ˈplætər
Word forms: plural platters
1 N-COUNT 大浅盘 A platter is a large, flat plate used for serving food. 大浅盘 [mainly US]

  • The food was being served on silver platters.

    食物是用银质大浅盘盛着的。

  • ...platters of ham and chicken sandwiches.

    一浅盘一浅盘的火腿和鸡肉三明治

SYN plate, dish, tray, charger  
A platter of food is the amount of food on a platter.一大浅盘之量
2 N-COUNT （尤指奶酪和水果的）大拼盘 A platter is a large flat plate which has different kinds of the same food on, especially cheese and fruit. （尤指奶酪和水果的）大拼盘

  • ...a low-calorie fruit mousse or souffle for dessert, or a cheese platter.

    低热量的水果奶油冻或蛋奶酥作甜点，或者奶酪拼盘
