block out
1 PHRASAL VERB 不去想；设法忘记 If someone blocks out a thought, they try not to think about it. 不去想；设法忘记

  • She accuses me of having blocked out the past. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她指责我把过去忘得一干二净。

  • I had to block the thought out of my mind. [VERB noun PREP. + of]

    我得把这种想法抛到脑后。

SYN remove, eradicate, excise, obliterate  
2 PHRASAL VERB 挡住，遮挡（光线） Something that blocks out light prevents it from reaching a place. 挡住，遮挡（光线）

  • Thick chipboard across the window frames blocked out the daylight. [VERB PREP. noun]

    装在窗框上的厚厚的胶合板挡住了白天的光线。

  • Those clouds would have cast shadows that would have blocked some sunlight out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    那些云朵原本会投射出一些阴影，挡住部分太阳光。

SYN cover, block, cloud, shade
