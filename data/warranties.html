warranty
wɒrənti wɔːr- ˈwɔrənti ˈwɑrənti
Word forms: plural warranties
N-COUNT [oft under N] 保修单 A warranty is a written promise by a company that, if you find a fault in something they have sold you within a certain time, they will repair it or replace it free of charge. 保修单

  • ...a twelve month warranty.

    为期12个月的保修单

  • The equipment is still under warranty.

    这套设备仍在保修期内。

SYN guarantee, promise, contract, bond
