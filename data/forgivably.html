forgivable
fəʳgɪvəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可原谅的;可宽恕的 If you say that something bad is forgivable, you mean that you can understand it and can forgive it in the circumstances. 可原谅的;可宽恕的

  • Is infidelity ever forgivable?

    不贞的行为可以宽恕吗？

  • This was a blunder by Mr Baker, but it was a forgivable one.

    这是贝克先生的一时疏忽，但是可以原谅。

SYN pardonable, allowable, excusable, condonable
