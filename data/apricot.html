apricot
eɪprɪkɒt ˈæprɪˌkɑt ˈeɪprɪˌkɑt
Word forms: plural apricots
1 N-VAR 杏 An apricot is a small, soft, round fruit with yellowish-orange flesh and a stone inside. 杏

  • ...12 oz apricots, halved and stoned.

    12盎司切成两半并去核的杏子

  • ...apricot tart.

    杏肉馅饼

2 COLOUR 杏黄色 Apricot is used to describe things that are yellowish-orange in colour. 杏黄色

  • The bridesmaids wore apricot and white organza.

    伴娘身着杏黄色和白色相间的透明硬纱礼服。

  • The heavy curtains were a warm apricot.

    这些厚重的窗帘是温暖的杏黄色。
