loudmouth
laʊdmaʊθ
Word forms: plural loudmouths/laʊdmaʊðz/
N-COUNT （尤指说话内容令人不悦、具冒犯性或愚蠢可笑的）高谈阔论者，夸夸其谈者，胡吹乱侃者 If you describe someone as a loudmouth, you are critical of them because they talk a lot, especially in an unpleasant, offensive, or stupid way. （尤指说话内容令人不悦、具冒犯性或愚蠢可笑的）高谈阔论者，夸夸其谈者，胡吹乱侃者 [disapproval]
SYN bigmouth [slang] , noisily, swaggerer, brag
