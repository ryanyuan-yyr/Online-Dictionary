insolvency
ɪnsɒlvənsi ɪnˈsɑlvənsi
Word forms: plural insolvencies
N-VAR 无力偿清债务;不足抵偿债务;破产 Insolvency is the state of not having enough money to pay your debts. 无力偿清债务;不足抵偿债务;破产 [business, formal]

  • ...companies on the brink of insolvency.

    濒临破产的公司

  • The economy has entered a sharp downturn, and unemployment and insolvencies can be expected to increase.

    经济形势急转直下，失业与破产现象预计将会增多。

SYN bankruptcy, failure, ruin, liquidation
