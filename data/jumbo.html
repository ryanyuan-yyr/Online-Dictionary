jumbo
dʒʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ
Word forms: plural jumbos
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （主要用于广告和产品名称中）巨大的，巨型的，特大的 Jumbo means very large; used mainly in advertising and in the names of products. （主要用于广告和产品名称中）巨大的，巨型的，特大的

  • ...a jumbo box of tissues.

    特大盒纸巾

  • ...grilled jumbo prawns.

    烤大虾

SYN giant, large, huge, immense  
2 N-COUNT 大型喷气式客机 A jumbo or a jumbo jet is a very large jet aircraft that can carry several hundred passengers. 大型喷气式客机

  • ...a British Airways jumbo.

    英国航空公司的大型喷气式客机
