insoluble
ɪnsɒljʊbəl ɪnˈsɑljəbəl
1 ADJ (问题等)不能解决的 An insoluble problem is so difficult that it is impossible to solve. (问题等)不能解决的

  • I pushed the problem aside; at present it was insoluble.

    我把问题抛在一边，目前它还无法解决。

  • It was an insoluble dilemma and one which I could do nothing about.

    那是个无法解决的两难局面,我无计可施。

SYN inexplicable, mysterious, baffling, obscure  
2 ADJ 不溶解的 If a substance is insoluble, it does not dissolve in a liquid. 不溶解的

  • Carotenes are insoluble in water and soluble in oils and fats.

    胡萝卜素不溶于水,但可溶于油类和脂肪。

SYN indissoluble, not soluble
