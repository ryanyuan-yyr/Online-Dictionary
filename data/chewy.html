chewy
tʃuːi ˈtʃui
Word forms: comparative chewier, superlative chewiest
ADJ-GRADED 耐嚼的;嚼不动的 If food is chewy, it needs to be chewed a lot before it becomes soft enough to swallow. 耐嚼的;嚼不动的

  • The meat was too chewy.

    这肉嚼不动。

  • ...chewy chocolate cookies.

    难嚼的巧克力饼干

SYN tough, fibrous, leathery, as tough as old boots
