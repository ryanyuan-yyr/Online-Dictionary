plop down
PHRASAL VERB 轻快地坐下 If you plop down or plop yourself down somewhere, you sit down quickly but gently. 轻快地坐下 [informal]

  • I plopped down on one of the dark red sofas. [VERB PREP.]

    我嗖的一声坐到其中一张深红色的沙发上。

  • He plopped himself down on the grass. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    他轻快地一屁股坐在草地上。
