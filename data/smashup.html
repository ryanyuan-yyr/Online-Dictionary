smash up
1 PHRASAL VERB 砸碎;砸毁;捣毁 If you smash something up, you completely destroy it by hitting it and breaking it into many pieces. 砸碎;砸毁;捣毁

  • She took revenge on her ex-boyfriend by smashing up his home. [VERB PREP. noun]

    为了报复，她砸了前任男友的家。

  • You could smash the drawer up with a hammer. [VERB noun PREP.]

    可以用锤子把抽屉砸开。

  • Office material worth hundreds of thousands of pounds was smashed up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    价值数十万英镑的办公用品都被砸烂了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 撞毁(汽车等) If you smash up your car, you damage it by crashing it into something. 撞毁(汽车等) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • All you told me was that he'd smashed up yet another car. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你告诉我的事情就是他又撞毁了一辆车。
