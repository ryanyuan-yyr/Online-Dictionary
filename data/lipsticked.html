lipstick
lɪpstɪk ˈlɪpˌstɪk
Word forms: plural lipsticks
N-MASS 口红；唇膏 Lipstick is a coloured substance in the form of a stick which women put on their lips. 口红；唇膏

  • She was wearing red lipstick.

    她抹了红色唇膏。

  • ...glossy lipsticks made from natural oils and waxes.

    由天然的油和蜡制成的亮彩唇膏

A lipstick is a small tube containing this substance.（一支）口红，唇膏
