sinister
sɪnɪstəʳ ˈsɪnɪstər
ADJ-GRADED 邪恶的;有害的;不祥的 Something that is sinister seems evil or harmful. 邪恶的;有害的;不祥的

  • There was something sinister about him that she found disturbing.

    他身上有种邪恶的东西让她很不安。

  • ...a sinister and frightening place.

    一个邪恶而恐怖的地方

  • ...the illegal and sinister trade in stolen cultural artefacts.

    非法而且邪恶的被盗文物买卖

SYN threatening, evil, menacing, forbidding
