inimical
ɪnɪmɪkəl ɪˈnɪmɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 有害的;不利的 Conditions that are inimical to something make it difficult for that thing to exist or do well. 有害的;不利的 [formal]

  • ...a false morality that is inimical to human happiness.

    对人类幸福不利的虚假道德

  • ...goals inimical to Western interests. [+ to]

    不利于西方利益的目标

SYN hostile, opposed, contrary, destructive
