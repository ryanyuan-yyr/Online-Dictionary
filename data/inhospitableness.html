inhospitable
ɪnhɒspɪtəbəl ɪnˈhɑspɪtəbəl ˌ ɪnhɑsˈpɪtəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (地方等)不适合居住的 An inhospitable place is unpleasant to live in. (地方等)不适合居住的

  • ...the earth's most inhospitable regions.

    地球上最不适合居住的地区

  • ...the island's inhospitable climate.

    岛上不适合居住的气候

SYN bleak, empty, bare, hostile  
2 ADJ-GRADED 不好客的;不友好的 If someone is inhospitable, they do not make people welcome when they visit. 不好客的;不友好的
