authorship
ɔːθəʳʃɪp ˈɔθərˌʃɪp
1 N-UNCOUNT 作者身份 The authorship of a piece of writing is the identity of the person who wrote it. 作者身份

  • Its authorship has been disputed.

    谁是作者尚无定论。

2 N-UNCOUNT 写作活动；作家职业 Authorship is the activity or job of writing books or articles. 写作活动；作家职业

  • It was her first try at authorship and proved a great success.

    这是她第一次尝试写作，结果证明非常成功。
