nymphomaniac
nɪmfəmeɪniæk
Word forms: plural nymphomaniacs
N-COUNT 女色情狂 If someone refers to a woman as a nymphomaniac, they mean that she has sex or wants to have sex much more often than they consider normal or acceptable. 女色情狂 [disapproval]

  • Lucia was a known nymphomaniac in Paris in the Thirties.

    露西娅是30年代巴黎人人皆知的女色情狂。
