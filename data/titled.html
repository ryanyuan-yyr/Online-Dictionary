titled
taɪtəld ˈtaɪtəld
ADJ 有称号的;有头衔的;有爵位的 Someone who is titled has a title such as 'Lord', 'Lady', 'Sir', or 'Princess' before their name, showing that they have a high rank in society. 有称号的;有头衔的;有爵位的

  • Her mother was a titled lady.

    她母亲是个有爵位的贵夫人。

SYN upper-class, noble, patrician, blue-blooded
