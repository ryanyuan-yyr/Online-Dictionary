exasperate
ɪgzɑːspəreɪt -zæs- ɪɡˈzɑːspəˌreɪt ɛgˈzæspərɪt ɛgˈzæspərˌeɪt ɪgˈzæspərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense exasperates, present participle exasperating, past tense , past participle exasperated
VERB 激怒;触怒;使生气 If someone or something exasperates you, they annoy you and make you feel frustrated or upset. 激怒;触怒;使生气

  • The sheer futility of it all exasperates her. [VERB noun]

    它毫无用处，这让她很生气。

SYN irritate, anger, provoke, annoy  
exasperation

  • Mahoney clenched his fist in exasperation.

    马奥尼恼怒地攥紧拳头。

  • There was a trace of exasperation in his voice.

    他的声音听上去有些愤怒。
