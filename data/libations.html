libation
laɪbeɪʃən laɪˈbeɪʃən
Word forms: plural libations
N-COUNT （古希腊和罗马供奉神的）鬯酒，祭酒，奠酒 In ancient Greece and Rome, a libation was an alcoholic drink that was offered to the gods. （古希腊和罗马供奉神的）鬯酒，祭酒，奠酒 [literary]

  • At the shrine of the god there were offerings, libations and incense.

    神殿里供奉着牺牲、鬯酒和贡香。
