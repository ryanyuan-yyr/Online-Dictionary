sing-song
(also singsong)Word forms: plural sing-songs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (声音)高低起伏的 A sing-song voice repeatedly rises and falls in pitch. (声音)高低起伏的

  • He started to speak in a nasal sing-song voice.

    他开始用一种带有鼻音的、抑扬顿挫的声调说话。

SYN droning, repetitive, monotonous, boring  
2 N-COUNT (众人自娱自乐的)合唱，歌唱会 A sing-song is an occasion on which a group of people sing songs together for pleasure. (众人自娱自乐的)合唱，歌唱会 [British]
