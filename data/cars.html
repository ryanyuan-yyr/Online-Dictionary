car
kɑːʳ kɑr
Word forms: plural cars
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 汽车;轿车 A car is a motor vehicle with room for a small number of passengers. 汽车;轿车

  • He had left his tickets in his car.

    他把票忘在车里了。

  • They arrived by car.

    他们是坐小汽车来的。

SYN vehicle, motor, wheels [informal] , auto [US]  
2 N-COUNT (火车)车厢 A car is one of the separate sections of a train. (火车)车厢 [US]

  • Tour buses have replaced railway cars.

    观光巴士已经取代了火车。

SYN (railway) carriage, coach, cable car, dining car  
in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 carriage
3 N-COUNT (火车上作特殊用途的)车厢 Railway carriages are called cars when they are used for a particular purpose. (火车上作特殊用途的)车厢 [British]

  • He made his way into the dining car for breakfast.

    他来到餐车车厢吃早饭。

4 See also: cable car
