Rep.
Rep. is a written abbreviation for (书面缩略=) Representative.

  • ...Rep. Barbara Boxer.

    众议院议员芭芭拉·博克瑟

rep
rep rɛp
Word forms: plural reps
1 N-COUNT 销售代表，销售专员（全写为 representative） A rep is a person whose job is to sell a company's products or services, especially by travelling round and visiting other companies. Rep is short for representative . 销售代表，销售专员（全写为 representative）

  • I'd been working as a sales rep for a photographic company. [+ for]

    我一直在一家摄影公司做销售代表。

2See also:holiday rep 
3 N-COUNT (组织、团体的)代表，发言人 A rep is a person who acts as a representative for a group of people, usually a group of people who work together. (组织、团体的)代表，发言人

  • Contact the health and safety rep at your union.

    与你所在工会的健康安全代表联系。

4 N-UNCOUNT (戏剧中)同repertory In the theatre, rep is the same as repertory . (戏剧中)同repertory

  • A play is tested in rep before ever hitting a West End stage.

    一部戏在登陆伦敦西区舞台前会以常备剧目试演。
