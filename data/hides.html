hide
haɪd haɪd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hides, present participle hiding, past tense hid, past participle hidden
1 VERB 把…藏起来;隐藏 If you hide something or someone, you put them in a place where they cannot easily be seen or found. 把…藏起来;隐藏

  • He hid the bicycle in the hawthorn hedge. [VERB noun]

    他把自行车藏在山楂树篱中。

  • They could see that I was terrified, and hid me until the coast was clear. [VERB noun]

    他们能看出我很害怕，就把我藏起来，直到没有危险了才让我出来。

SYN conceal, stash [informal] , secrete, cache  
2 VERB 躲藏;隐藏 If you hide or if you hide yourself, you go somewhere where you cannot easily be seen or found. 躲藏;隐藏

  • At their approach the little boy scurried away and hid. [VERB]

    他们走近时，小男孩急忙跑开藏了起来。

  • They hid themselves behind a tree. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    他们躲到一棵树后。

SYN go into hiding, take cover, keep out of sight, hole up  
3 VERB 捂住（脸）;将（脸）埋进 If you hide your face, you press your face against something or cover your face with something, so that people cannot see it. 捂住（脸）;将（脸）埋进

  • She hid her face under the collar of his jacket and she started to cry. [VERB noun]

    她将脸埋在他外套的衣领下，开始哭了起来。

  • He hid his face in his hands again, lost in his own thoughts. [VERB noun]

    他又用双手捂住脸，陷入了沉思。

4 VERB 隐瞒;使不外露 If you hide what you feel or know, you keep it a secret, so that no one knows about it. 隐瞒;使不外露

  • Lee tried to hide his excitement. [VERB noun]

    李试图掩饰自己的激动心情。

  • I have absolutely nothing to hide, I have done nothing wrong. [VERB noun]

    我绝对没有什么好隐瞒的，我没有做错什么。

  • Alison was not the sort of person to hide anything from her dad. [VERB noun]

    艾莉森不是那种有事瞒着自己父亲的人。

SYN keep secret, suppress, withhold, keep quiet about  
5 VERB 掩蔽;遮盖 If something hides an object, it covers it and prevents it from being seen. 掩蔽;遮盖

  • The man's heavy moustache hid his upper lip completely. [VERB noun]

    那个男人浓密的髭须完全遮住了他的上唇。

  • The compound was hidden by trees and shrubs. [VERB noun]

    大院掩映在树木灌丛之中。

SYN obscure, cover, screen, bury  
6 N-COUNT （狩猎或观察、拍摄野生动物等的）隐蔽处，藏身处 A hide is a place which is built to look like its surroundings. Hides are used by people who want to watch or photograph animals and birds without being seen by them. （狩猎或观察、拍摄野生动物等的）隐蔽处，藏身处 [mainly British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 blind
7 N-VAR 兽皮;毛皮 A hide is the skin of a large animal such as a cow, horse, or elephant, which can be used for making leather. 兽皮;毛皮

  • ...the process of tanning animal hides.

    鞣制兽皮的工序

  • ...kangaroo hide.

    袋鼠皮

SYN skin, fell, leather, pelt
