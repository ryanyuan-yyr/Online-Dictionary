miffed
mɪft mɪft
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 生气的;恼火的；难过的 If you are miffed, you are slightly annoyed and hurt because of something which someone has said or done to you. 生气的;恼火的；难过的 [informal]

  • I was a bit miffed about that. [+ about]

    我对那件事有点恼火。

  • Philip was pretty miffed at being cut out of his father's will.

    对于自己在父亲的遗嘱中被剥夺继承权这件事，菲利浦感到非常生气。

SYN upset, hurt, annoyed, offended
