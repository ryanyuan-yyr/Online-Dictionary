saunter
sɔːntəʳ ˈsɔntər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense saunters, present participle sauntering, past tense , past participle sauntered
VERB 闲逛;漫步 If you saunter somewhere, you walk there in a slow, casual way. 闲逛;漫步

  • We watched our fellow students saunter into the building. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我们看着同学们悠闲地走进了大楼。

  • He sauntered along the river to the mill. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他沿着河边漫步走到磨坊。

SYN stroll, wander, amble, roam  
Saunter is also a noun.

  • She began a slow saunter toward the bonfires.

    她开始向篝火处慢慢走去。
