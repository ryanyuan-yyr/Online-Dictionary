expenditure
ɪkspendɪtʃəʳ ɪkˈspɛndɪtʃə ɛkˈspɛndɪtʃər ɪkˈspɛndətʃər
Word forms: plural expenditures
1 N-VAR 花费;开销;支出 Expenditure is the spending of money on something, or the money that is spent on something. 花费;开销;支出 [formal]

  • Policies of tax reduction must lead to reduced public expenditure.

    减税政策必然导致公共支出的削减。

  • They should cut their expenditure on defence.

    他们应该缩减国防开支。

  • An expenditure for clothing will qualify as a trade or business expense.

    服装费将可算作贸易或商务开支。

SYN spending, payment, expense, outgoings  
2 N-UNCOUNT （时间、精力等的）消耗，花费 Expenditure of something such as time or energy is the using of that thing for a particular purpose. （时间、精力等的）消耗，花费 [formal]

  • The financial rewards justified the expenditure of effort. [+ of]

    经济上的回报证明付出的努力是值得的。

SYN consumption, use, using, application
