goods
gʊdz gʊdz
1 N-PLURAL 货物；商品 Goods are things that are made to be sold. 货物；商品

  • Money can be exchanged for goods or services.

    钱可以用来换取商品或服务。

  • ...a wide range of consumer goods.

    种类繁多的消费品

2 N-PLURAL 私人财产；动产 Your goods are the things that you own and that can be moved. 私人财产；动产

  • All his worldly goods were packed into a neat checked carrier bag.

    他的所有家当都装在一个干净的方格图案手提袋里。

  • You can give your unwanted goods to charity.

    你可以将不要的物品捐赠给慈善机构。

3 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 不负众望；达到要求 If you deliver the goods or come up with the goods, you do what is expected or required of you. 不负众望；达到要求 [informal]

  • As long as I deliver the goods, my boss is very happy.

    只要我做好本职工作，我的老板就很高兴。

4 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 掌握了…的罪证（或把柄） If you get the goods or have the goods on someone, you have evidence that they have done something wrong or criminal. 掌握了…的罪证（或把柄）

  • He was the victim of those in his own party who had the goods on him.

    党内一些人掌握了他的把柄，结果他成了牺牲品。
