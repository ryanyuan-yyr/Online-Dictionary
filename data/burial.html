burial
beriəl ˈbɛriəl
Word forms: plural burials
N-VAR 葬礼；埋葬 A burial is the act or ceremony of putting a dead body into a grave in the ground. 葬礼；埋葬

  • The priest prepared the body for burial.

    牧师给死者作安葬准备。

  • He can have a decent burial.

    他会有个体面的葬礼。

SYN funeral, interment, burying, obsequies
