craftsman
krɑːftsmən kræft- ˈkræftsmən ˈkrɑftsmən
Word forms: plural craftsmen
N-COUNT 工匠;手艺人;工艺师 A craftsman is a man who makes things skilfully with his hands. 工匠;手艺人;工艺师

  • The table in the kitchen was made by a local craftsman.

    厨房里的桌子是本地一位工匠制作的。

SYN skilled worker, artisan, master, maker
