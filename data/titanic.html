titanic
taɪtænɪk taɪˈtænɪk tɪˈtænɪk
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 庞大的;强大的 If you describe something as titanic, you mean that it is very big or important, and usually that it involves very powerful forces. 庞大的;强大的

  • The world had witnessed a titanic struggle between two visions of the future.

    世界见证了两种对未来的构想之间的激烈较量。

SYN gigantic, huge, giant, massive
