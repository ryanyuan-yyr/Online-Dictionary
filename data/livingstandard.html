living standard
Word forms: plural living standards
N-COUNT [usually plural] 生活水平；生活水准 Living standards or living standard is used to refer to the level of comfort in which people live, which usually depends on how much money they have. 生活水平；生活水准

  • Cheaper housing would vastly improve the living standards of ordinary people. [+ of]

    更低廉的房价可大大改善老百姓的生活水平。

  • Critics say his reforms have caused the fall in living standards.

    批评家说他的改革措施导致了生活水准下降。
