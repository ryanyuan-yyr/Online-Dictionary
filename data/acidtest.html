acid test
N-SING 决定性的试验；严峻的考验 The acid test of something is an important aspect or result that it might have, which allows you to decide whether it is true or successful. 决定性的试验；严峻的考验

  • The acid test of a school is 'would you send your own children there?' [+ of]

    “你会将自己的孩子送去那里吗？”这一问题是衡量一所学校的试金石。
