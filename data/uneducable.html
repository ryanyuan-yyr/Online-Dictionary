uneducated
ʌnedʒʊkeɪtɪd ʌnˈɛdjʊˌkeɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 未受过良好教育的；没有多少文化的 Someone who is uneducated has not received much education. 未受过良好教育的；没有多少文化的

  • Though an uneducated man, Chavez was not a stupid one.

    查维斯虽然没有受过多少教育，但并不笨。

SYN ignorant, illiterate, unread, unschooled  
The uneducated are people who are uneducated.未受过良好教育的人；没多少文化的人

  • The poor and uneducated did worst under these reforms.

    穷人和未受过良好教育的人在这些改革中表现最差。
