megalomania
megələmeɪniə ˌmɛɡələʊˈmeɪnɪə ˌmɛgəloʊˈmeɪniə ˌmɛgəloʊˈmeɪnjə
N-UNCOUNT 自大狂;夸大狂;妄自尊大 Megalomania is the belief that you are more powerful and important than you really are. Megalomania is sometimes a mental illness. 自大狂;夸大狂;妄自尊大
SYN self-importance, conceit, egotism, delusions of grandeur
