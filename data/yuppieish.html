yuppie
jʌpi ˈjʌpi
Word forms: plural yuppies
N-COUNT 雅皮士（指收入高、生活奢侈的年轻人） A yuppie is a young person who has a well-paid job and likes to show that they have a lot of money by buying expensive things and living in an expensive way. 雅皮士（指收入高、生活奢侈的年轻人） [disapproval]

  • The Porsche 911 reminds me of the worst parts of the yuppie era.

    这辆保时捷911让我想起了雅皮士时代最不堪的那些日子。

  • ...an ambitious yuppie lawyer.

    雄心勃勃的雅皮士律师
