inept
ɪnept ɪnˈɛpt ɪnˈɛpt
ADJ-GRADED 无能的;笨拙的 If you say that someone is inept, you are criticizing them because they do something with a complete lack of skill. 无能的;笨拙的 [disapproval]

  • He was inept and lacked the intelligence to govern.

    他没有什么能力,缺乏管理才干。

  • You are completely inept at writing.

    你完全拙于写作。

  • ...his inept handling of the army.

    他治军无方

SYN incompetent, bungling, clumsy, cowboy [informal]
