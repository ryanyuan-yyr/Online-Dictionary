maelstrom
meɪlstrɒm
Word forms: plural maelstroms
N-COUNT [usually singular] 大动乱;极度混乱状态；混乱不堪 If you describe a situation as a maelstrom, you mean that it is very confused or violent. 大动乱;极度混乱状态；混乱不堪 [literary]

  • ...the maelstrom of ethnic hatreds and vendetta politics. [+ of]

    种族仇恨与政治宿怨造成的大动乱

  • Inside, she was a maelstrom of churning emotions. [+ of]

    她心中的情感似波涛汹涌，起伏不定。

SYN turmoil, disorder, confusion, chaos
