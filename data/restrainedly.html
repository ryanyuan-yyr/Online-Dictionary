restrained
rɪstreɪnd rɪˈstreɪnd rɪˈstreɪnd
1 ADJ-GRADED 克制的;冷静的;不动声色的 Someone who is restrained is very calm and unemotional. 克制的;冷静的;不动声色的

  • In the circumstances he felt he'd been very restrained.

    在那种情况下，他觉得自己一直非常冷静。

  • Livy thought Caroline's greeting seemed a little restrained.

    莉薇觉得卡罗琳的问候似乎有些不冷不热。

SYN controlled, reasonable, moderate, self-controlled  
2 ADJ-GRADED (穿着、装饰等)简单的，淡雅的，朴素的 If you describe someone's clothes or the decorations in a house as restrained, you mean that you like them because they are simple and not too brightly-coloured. (穿着、装饰等)简单的，淡雅的，朴素的 [approval]

  • Her black suit was restrained and expensive.

    她的黑色套装简单而昂贵。

SYN unobtrusive, subtle, muted, discreet
