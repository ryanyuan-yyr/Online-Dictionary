pitch for
PHRASAL VERB [usually cont] 争取拿到 If someone is pitching for something, they are trying to persuade other people to give it to them. 争取拿到

  • ...laws prohibiting the state's accountants from pitching for business. [VERB PREP. noun]

    禁止州会计师招揽生意的法律

  • It was middle-class votes they were pitching for. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们争取的是中产阶级的选票。
