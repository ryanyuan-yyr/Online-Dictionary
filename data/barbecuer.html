barbecue
bɑːʳbɪkjuː ˈbɑrbəˌkju
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense barbecues, present participle barbecuing, past tense , past participle barbecued
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 barbeque，Bar-B-Q
1 N-COUNT （户外烧烤用的）烤肉架 A barbecue is a piece of equipment which you use for cooking on in the open air. （户外烧烤用的）烤肉架
2 N-COUNT 烧烤野餐 If someone has a barbecue, they cook food on a barbecue in the open air. 烧烤野餐
3 VERB 烧烤（尤指烤肉） If you barbecue food, especially meat, you cook it on a barbecue. 烧烤（尤指烤肉） [Also VERB]

  • Tuna can be grilled, fried or barbecued. [be VERB-ed]

    金枪鱼可以用烤炉烤、油炸或者烧烤。

  • Here's a way of barbecuing corn-on-the-cob that I learned in the States. [VERB noun]

    这是我在美国学的一种烤玉米的方法。

  • ...barbecued chicken. [VERB-ed]

    烤鸡

SYN chargrill, broil [US] , cook outdoors, charbroil [US]
