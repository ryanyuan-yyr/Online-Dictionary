knuckle under
PHRASAL VERB 屈服；认输 If you knuckle under, you do what someone else tells you to do or what a situation forces you to do, because you realize that you have no choice. 屈服；认输 [informal]

  • It is arguable whether the rebels will knuckle under. [VERB PREP.]

    还不能确定反叛者是否会投降。

  • The United States, he said, did not knuckle under to demands. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他说，美国不会屈从于他人的要求。

SYN give way, yield, submit, surrender
