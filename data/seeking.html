seek
siːk sik
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense seeks, present participle seeking, past tense , past participle sought
1 VERB 寻找;物色 If you seek something such as a job or a place to live, you try to find one. 寻找;物色 [formal]

  • They have had to seek work as labourers. [VERB noun]

    他们只好找体力活儿干。

  • Four people who sought refuge in the Italian embassy have left voluntarily. [VERB noun]

    4 名在意大利大使馆寻求避难的人已经自愿离开了。

  • Candidates are urgently sought for the post of Conservative party chairman. [be VERB-ed + for]

    保守党主席的职位急寻候选人。

SYN look for, pursue, search for, be after  
2 VERB 谋求;谋取;寻求 When someone seeks something, they try to obtain it. 谋求;谋取;寻求 [formal]

  • The prosecutors have warned they will seek the death penalty. [VERB noun]

    检控方律师已警告说他们会努力争取判处死刑。

  • Haemophiliacs are seeking compensation for being given contaminated blood. [VERB noun]

    血友病患者们因为被输入了受到污染的血液而正在寻求赔偿。

3 VERB 请求(帮助);征求(意见) If you seek someone's help or advice, you contact them in order to ask for it. 请求(帮助);征求(意见) [formal]

  • Always seek professional legal advice before entering into any agreement. [VERB noun]

    在签订任何协定之前一定要先征求法律专业人士的意见。

  • On important issues, they seek a second opinion. [VERB noun]

    在重要问题上，他们会征求别人的看法。

  • The couple have sought help from marriage guidance counsellors. [VERB noun + from]

    这对夫妇已经向婚姻指导顾问寻求帮助。

SYN request, invite, ask for, petition  
4 VERB 力图;想方设法 If you seek to do something, you try to do it. 力图;想方设法 [formal]

  • He also denied that he would seek to annex the country. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他还否认会试图吞并该国。

  • Moscow is seeking to slow the growth of Russian inflation. [VERB to-infinitive]

    俄罗斯政府正力图遏制本国的通货膨胀。

SYN try, attempt, aim, strive
