desist
dɪzɪst dɪˈzɪst dɪˈsɪst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense desists, present participle desisting, past tense , past participle desisted
VERB 停止（做）；不再（做） If you desist from doing something, you stop doing it. 停止（做）；不再（做） [formal]

  • Ford never desisted from trying to persuade him to return to America. [VERB + from]

    福特从未停止过劝说他返回美国。

  • The magazine will desist from such language after receiving complaints. [V from -ing/n]

    杂志接到投诉之后将杜绝使用此类语言。

  • She rubbed her arms, but they hurt and she desisted. [VERB]

    她揉了揉胳膊，感觉很疼就停下了。

SYN stop, cease, refrain from, end
