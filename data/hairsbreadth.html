hair's breadth hɛəz brɛdθ
N-SING 间不容发;细微的量 A hair's breadth is a very small degree or amount. 间不容发;细微的量

  • The dollar fell to within a hair's breadth of its all-time low.

    美元差一点跌到了最低点。

  • He did not swerve a hair's breadth from the decision he had made.

    他丝毫没有改变自己已经作出的决定。

SYN fraction, jot, hair, whisker
