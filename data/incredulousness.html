incredulous
ɪnkredʒʊləs ɪnˈkrɛdʒʊləs
ADJ-GRADED 不相信的;不轻信的;怀疑的 If someone is incredulous, they are unable to believe something because it is very surprising or shocking. 不相信的;不轻信的;怀疑的

  • 'He made you do it?' Her voice was incredulous.

    “他强迫你做的？”她的语气里带着怀疑。

  • There was a brief, incredulous silence.

    有一段短暂的充满怀疑的沉默。

SYN disbelieving, doubting, sceptical, suspicious  
incredulously

  • 'You told Pete?' Rachel said incredulously. 'I can't believe it!'

    “你告诉皮特了？”雷切尔怀疑地说：“真不敢相信！”
