bloodbath blʌdbɑːθ -bæθ ˈblʌdˌbɑːθ
(also blood bath)Word forms: plural bloodbaths
N-COUNT 大屠杀；血洗 If you describe an event as a bloodbath, you are emphasizing that a lot of people were killed very violently. 大屠杀；血洗 [emphasis]

  • The war degenerated into a bloodbath of tribal killings.

    这场战争演变为一场部落之间的大屠杀。
