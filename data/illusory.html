illusory
ɪluːzəri -səri ɪˈluːsɪv ɪˈlusəri ɪˈluzəri
ADJ-GRADED 幻觉的;虚假的;不切实际的 If you describe something as illusory, you mean that although it seems true or possible, it is in fact false or impossible. 幻觉的;虚假的;不切实际的

  • His freedom is illusory.

    他的自由是虚幻的。

  • ...the illusory nature of nationhood.

    国家的虚假本质

SYN unreal, false, misleading, untrue
