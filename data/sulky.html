sulky
sʌlki ˈsʌlki
ADJ-GRADED 面有愠色的;生闷气的;闷闷不乐的 Someone who is sulky is sulking or is unwilling to enjoy themselves. 面有愠色的;生闷气的;闷闷不乐的

  • I was quite sulky, so I didn't take part in much.

    我很烦，所以没有积极参与。

  • ...a sulky adolescent.

    闷闷不乐的少年

SYN huffy, sullen, petulant, cross  
sulkily

  • 'You haven't got the right attitude,' he said sulkily.

    他怄气道：“你态度不端正。”
