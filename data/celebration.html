celebration
selɪbreɪʃən ˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən
Word forms: plural celebrations
1 N-COUNT 庆祝活动;庆典 A celebration is a special enjoyable event that people organize because something pleasant has happened or because it is someone's birthday or anniversary. 庆祝活动;庆典

  • I can tell you, there was a celebration in our house that night.

    告诉你，那天晚上我家开了个庆祝会。

  • ...his eightieth birthday celebrations.

    他80大寿的庆祝活动

SYN party, festival, gala, jubilee  
2 N-SING 赞扬;赞美;颂扬 The celebration of something is praise and appreciation which is given to it. 赞扬;赞美;颂扬

  • This was not a memorial service but a celebration of his life. [+ of]

    这不是在开追悼会，而是在颂扬他的一生。

  • He sees the poem as a celebration of human love.

    他认为，这首诗是对人世间爱的赞美。

SYN commemoration, honouring, remembrance
