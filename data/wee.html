wee
wiː wi
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense wees, present participle weeing, past tense , past participle weed
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 很小的;极小的;一丁点儿的 Wee means small in size or extent. 很小的;极小的;一丁点儿的 [Scottish, informal]

  • I've got a wee kitten in the flat.

    我在公寓里养了一只小猫。

  • He just needs to calm down a wee bit.

    他只是需要稍微冷静一下。

SYN little, small, minute, tiny  
2 VERB 尿尿，嘘嘘（尤用于儿语） To wee means to urinate. Wee is an informal word used especially by children. 尿尿，嘘嘘（尤用于儿语） [British]

  • He said he wanted to wee. [VERB]

    他说他要撒尿。

Wee is also a noun.

  • The baby has done a wee in his potty.

    婴儿往便壶里撒了尿。

  • I put him on his pot, he did some wee.

    我把他放到便壶上，他撒了点尿。
