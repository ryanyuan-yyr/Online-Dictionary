clothes horse
Word forms: plural clothes horses
1 N-COUNT (室内)晾衣架，晒衣架 A clothes horse is a folding frame used inside someone's house to hang washing on while it dries. (室内)晾衣架，晒衣架
2 N-COUNT (尤指女性)讲究穿着但头脑简单的人,追求时尚但一无是处的人 If you describe someone, especially a woman, as a clothes horse, you mean that they are fashionable and think a lot about their clothes, but have little intelligence or no other abilities. (尤指女性)讲究穿着但头脑简单的人,追求时尚但一无是处的人 [disapproval]
