unfunny
ʌnfʌni ʌnˈfʌnɪ
ADJ-GRADED 不逗乐的；不好笑的 If you describe something or someone as unfunny, you mean that they do not make you laugh, although this was their intention or purpose. 不逗乐的；不好笑的

  • We became increasingly fed up with his increasingly unfunny and unintelligent comments.

    我们越来越反感他益发无趣又缺乏见地的言论。

  • ...another unfunny pair of comedians.

    又一对不好笑的喜剧演员
