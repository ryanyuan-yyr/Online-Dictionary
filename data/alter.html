alter
ɔːltəʳ ˈɔːltə ˈɔltər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense alters, present participle altering, past tense , past participle altered
V-ERG 改变；变更；更改 If something alters or if you alter it, it changes. 改变；变更；更改

  • Little had altered in the village. [VERB]

    村子里几乎没有什么变化。

  • They have never altered their programmes by a single day. [VERB noun]

    他们一天也未变更过他们的节目。

SYN change, turn, vary, transform
