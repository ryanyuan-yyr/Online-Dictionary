enthral
ɪnθrɔːl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enthrals, present participle enthralling, past tense , past participle enthralled
in AM, use 美国英语用 enthrall, enthralls
VERB 迷住;吸引住 If you are enthralled by something, you enjoy it and give it your complete attention and interest. 迷住;吸引住

  • The passengers were enthralled by the scenery. [be VERB-ed]

    乘客们为景致所吸引。

  • He enthralled audiences in Prague, Vienna, and Paris. [VERB noun]

    他让布拉格、维也纳和巴黎的观众们着迷。

  • In real-life, the band have memorable songs and a stage vigour that enthrals. [VERB]

    在现实生活中，这支乐队的一些歌曲令人难忘，他们的舞台魅力令人着迷。

  • The fans sat enthralled in the darkened cinema. [VERB-ed]

    影迷们坐在黑漆漆的电影院里看得如痴如醉。

SYN engross, charm, grip, fascinate  
enthralling

    吸引人的比赛
