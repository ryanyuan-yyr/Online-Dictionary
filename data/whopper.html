whopper
hwɒpəʳ hwɑpər wɑpər
Word forms: plural whoppers
1 N-COUNT 弥天大谎 If you describe a lie as a whopper, you mean that it is very far from the truth. 弥天大谎 [informal]

  • ...the biggest whopper the president told.

    总统撒的弥天大谎

2 N-COUNT 特别大的物体;庞然大物 If you refer to something as a whopper, you mean that it is an unusually large example of the thing mentioned. 特别大的物体;庞然大物 [informal]

  • As comets go, it is a whopper.

    就彗星而言，它可是个庞然大物。

SYN giant, monster, jumbo [informal] , mammoth
