intoxicating
ɪntɒksɪkeɪtɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (饮料)醉人的,致醉的 Intoxicating drink contains alcohol and can make you drunk. (饮料)醉人的,致醉的 [formal]

  • ...intoxicating liquor.

    喝了容易上头的烈酒

2 ADJ-GRADED 令人陶醉的;令人极其兴奋的 If you describe something as intoxicating, you mean that it makes you feel a strong sense of excitement or happiness. 令人陶醉的;令人极其兴奋的 [literary]

  • ...the intoxicating fragrance of lilies.

    百合花的沁人芳香

  • The music is pulsating and the atmosphere is intoxicating.

    音乐在有节奏地响着,气氛令人陶醉。

SYN exciting, thrilling, stimulating, sexy [informal]
