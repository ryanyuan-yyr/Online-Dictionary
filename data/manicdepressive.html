manic-depressive
ˈmænɪkdiˈprɛsɪv ˈmeɪnɪkdiˈprɛsɪv
(also manic depressive)Word forms: plural manic-depressives
ADJ (躁狂和抑郁交替发作的)狂躁抑郁症的，患躁郁症的 If someone is manic-depressive, they have a medical condition in which they sometimes feel excited and confident and at other times very depressed. (躁狂和抑郁交替发作的)狂躁抑郁症的，患躁郁症的

  • She told them that her daughter-in-law was manic-depressive.

    她告诉他们，她的儿媳是狂躁抑郁症患者。

  • Manic depressive illness affects men and women equally.

    男人和女人患狂躁抑郁症的概率相等。

A manic-depressive is someone who is manic-depressive.狂躁抑郁症患者

  • Her mother is a manic depressive.

    她的母亲是一名狂躁抑郁症患者。
