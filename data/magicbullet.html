magic bullet
Word forms: plural magic bullets
1 N-COUNT 魔弹 (指能快速治愈疾病的药物或疗法) In medicine, a magic bullet is a drug or treatment that can cure a disease quickly and completely. 魔弹 (指能快速治愈疾病的药物或疗法)
2 N-COUNT （能轻松解决难题的）灵丹妙药 A magic bullet is an easy solution to a difficult problem. （能轻松解决难题的）灵丹妙药 [informal]

  • A lot of people are looking for some sort of magic bullet that will solve this problem.

    许多人都在寻找能够解决这个问题的良方。
