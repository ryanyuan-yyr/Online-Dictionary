unasked
ʌnɑːskt -æskt ʌnˈɑːskt ʌnˈæskt
1 ADJ 未被提出的；未被问及的 An unasked question is one that has not been asked, although people are wondering what the answer is. 未被提出的；未被问及的

  • She was undernourished, an observation that prompted yet another unasked question.

    她看上去营养不良，这一发现又引出了一个未被问及的问题。

  • Significant questions will go unasked.

    重大问题不会问到。

2 ADJ [ADJ after verb] 未被要求的；未受邀请的；主动的 If someone says or does something unasked, they say or do it without being asked to do it. 未被要求的；未受邀请的；主动的

  • His advice, offered to her unasked, was to stay home and make the best of things.

    他主动给她提出的建议是呆在家里尽量做些事。
