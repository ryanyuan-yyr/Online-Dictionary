ballot box
Word forms: plural ballot boxes
1 N-COUNT （投）票箱 A ballot box is the box into which ballot papers are put after people have voted. （投）票箱
2 N-SING 民主选举（制度） You can refer to the system of democratic elections as the ballot box . 民主选举（制度）

  • Martinez expressed confidence of victory at the ballot box.

    马丁内斯表示了对选举获胜的信心。
