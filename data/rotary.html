rotary
roʊtəri ˈroʊtəri
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 旋转的;转动的 Rotary means turning or able to turn round a fixed point. 旋转的;转动的

  • ...turning linear into rotary motion.

    把直线运动改成旋转运动

  • ...heavy-duty rotary blades.

    重型旋转桨叶

SYN revolving, turning, spinning, rotating  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (机器)有旋转部件的 Rotary is used in the names of some machines that have parts that turn round a fixed point. (机器)有旋转部件的

  • ...a rotary engine.

    转缸式发动机
