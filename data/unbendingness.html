unbending
ʌnbendɪŋ ʌnˈbɛndɪŋ ʌnˈbɛndɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 固执的；顽固的；倔强的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as unbending, you mean that they have very strict beliefs and attitudes, which they are unwilling to change. 固执的；顽固的；倔强的

  • He was rigid and unbending.

    他很固执，态度很坚决。

  • ...her unbending opposition to the old regime.

    她对旧政权不屈不挠的反对

SYN inflexible, strict, rigid, firm
