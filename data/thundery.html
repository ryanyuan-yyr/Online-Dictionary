thundery
θʌndəri ˈθʌndəri
ADJ-GRADED 要打雷的;将要有雷电的 When the weather is thundery, there is a lot of thunder, or there are heavy clouds which make you think that there will be thunder soon. 要打雷的;将要有雷电的

  • Thundery weather is forecast.

    天气预报说会有雷雨。

  • Heavy thundery rain fell throughout Thursday.

    星期四下了一整天的雷暴雨。
