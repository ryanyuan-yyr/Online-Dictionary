stoke
stoʊk stoʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stokes, present participle stoking, past tense , past participle stoked
1 VERB 给…添煤(或柴火) If you stoke a fire, you add coal or wood to it to keep it burning. 给…添煤(或柴火)

  • She was stoking the stove with sticks of maple. [VERB noun]

    她正在往炉子里添枫树枝。

SYN fuel, rekindle, add fuel to, tend  
Stoke up means the same as stoke .stoke up 同 stoke

  • He stoked up the fire in the hearth.

    他给壁炉里的火添了点柴。

2 VERB 煽起;鼓动;激起 If you stoke something such as a feeling, you cause it to be felt more strongly. 煽起;鼓动;激起

  • These demands are helping to stoke fears of civil war. [VERB noun]

    这些要求助长了人们对内战的恐惧。
