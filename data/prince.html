prince
prɪns prɪns
Word forms: plural princes
1 N-TITLE; N-COUNT 王子；亲王；王孙 A prince is a male member of a royal family, especially the son of the king or queen of a country. 王子；亲王；王孙

  • ...Prince Edward and other royal guests.

    爱德华王子和其他皇室宾客

  • The Prince won warm applause for his ideas.

    王子的想法赢得了热烈的掌声。

2 N-TITLE; N-COUNT （小国的）君王，国君 A prince is the male royal ruler of a small country or state. （小国的）君王，国君

  • He was speaking without the prince's authority.

    他说话的时候没有君王的威严。

3 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] 大师；名家；巨匠 If someone describes a man as the prince of a particular type of work, they mean that he is the best man doing that type of work. 大师；名家；巨匠 [literary]

  • To his 19th-century peers, Robert Brown was the prince of botany.

    对于19世纪的同行来说，罗伯特·布朗是植物学巨匠。
