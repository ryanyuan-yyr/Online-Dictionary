mouse
maʊs maʊs maʊz
Word forms: plural mice

  • The plural mouses can be used for meaning 2.

    复数 mouses 可用于义项2。

1 N-COUNT 老鼠;耗子 A mouse is a small furry animal with a long tail. 老鼠;耗子

  • ...a mouse running in a wheel in its cage.

    在笼中转轮上奔跑的老鼠

  • ...the problem of rats and mice.

    鼠患

2 N-COUNT 鼠标 A mouse is a device that is connected to a computer. By moving it over a flat surface and pressing its buttons, you can move the cursor around the screen and do things without using the keyboard. 鼠标
3 game of cat and mouse→see: cat
