syrup
sɪrəp ˈsɜrəp ˈsɪrəp
Word forms: plural syrups
1 N-MASS 糖水(有时加果汁) Syrup is a sweet liquid made by cooking sugar with water, and sometimes with fruit juice as well. 糖水(有时加果汁)

  • ...canned fruit with sugary syrup.

    糖水水果罐头

2 N-UNCOUNT 糖浆 Syrup is a very sweet thick liquid made from sugar. 糖浆

  • ...a heavy syrup pudding.

    放入大量糖浆的布丁

3See also:golden syrup  maple syrup 
4 N-MASS 药用糖浆;糖浆剂 Syrup is a medicine in the form of a thick, sweet liquid. 药用糖浆;糖浆剂

  • ...cough syrup.

    止咳糖浆
