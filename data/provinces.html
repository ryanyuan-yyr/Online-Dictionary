province
prɒvɪns ˈprɑvɪns
Word forms: plural provinces
1 N-COUNT 省 A province is a large section of a country which has its own administration. 省

  • ...the Algarve, Portugal's southernmost province.

    阿尔加维， 葡萄牙最南部的省份

SYN region, section, county, district  
2 N-PLURAL 外省（首都以外的地区） The provinces are all the parts of a country except the part where the capital is situated. 外省（首都以外的地区）

  • The government plans to transfer some 30,000 government jobs from Paris to the provinces.

    政府计划将3万个左右的政府职位从巴黎转移到外省。

3 N-SING [with poss] （兴趣、知识、责任的）范围，领域 If you say that a subject or activity is a particular person's province, you mean that this person has a special interest in it, a special knowledge of it, or a special responsibility for it. （兴趣、知识、责任的）范围，领域

  • Arvo avoided committing himself. 'I'm afraid that's not my province,' he replied.

    阿尔沃不想作出承诺，“恐怕那不归我管，”他回答说。

  • Industrial research is the province of the Department of Trade and Industry.

    工业研究属贸易和工业部的职责范围。

SYN area, business, concern, responsibility
