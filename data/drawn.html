drawn
drɔːn drɔn
1 Drawn is the past participle of draw .
2 ADJ-GRADED (人或人脸)清瘦的,憔悴的,愁眉苦脸的 If someone or their face looks drawn, their face is thin and they look very tired, ill, worried, or unhappy. (人或人脸)清瘦的,憔悴的,愁眉苦脸的

  • She looked drawn and tired when she turned towards me.

    她转向我时看上去憔悴又疲惫。

SYN tense, worn, strained, stressed
