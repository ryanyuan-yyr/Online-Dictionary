peace offering
Word forms: plural peace offerings
N-COUNT [usually singular] 和解的礼物；谢罪之礼 You can use peace offering to refer to something that you give someone to show that you want to end the quarrel between you. 和解的礼物；谢罪之礼

  • 'A peace offering,' Roberts said as he handed the box of cigars to Cohen.

    “谢罪的礼物，”罗伯茨说着递给科恩一盒雪茄。
