ferret
ferɪt ˈfɛrət
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ferrets, present participle ferreting, past tense , past participle ferreted
1 N-COUNT 雪貂(用以捕兔、捕鼠) A ferret is a small, fierce animal which is used for hunting rabbits and rats. 雪貂(用以捕兔、捕鼠)
2 VERB 搜寻;寻找 If you ferret about for something, you look for it in a lot of different places or in a place where it is hidden. 搜寻;寻找 [British, informal]

  • She nonetheless continued to ferret about for possible jobs. [V about/around]

    但她还是继续寻找可能的工作。

  • She ferreted among some papers. [VERB prep.]

    她在一些文件中翻找着。

SYN search, hunt, sift, scour  
Phrasal verbs:
ferret out
