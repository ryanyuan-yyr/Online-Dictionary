facade
fəsɑːd fəˈsɑd
(also façade)Word forms: plural facades
1 N-COUNT (尤指大型建筑物的)正面，临街的一面 The facade of a building, especially a large one, is its front wall or the wall that faces the street. (尤指大型建筑物的)正面，临街的一面
2 N-SING (虚假的)外观，外表;假象 A facade is an outward appearance which is deliberately false and gives you a wrong impression about someone or something. (虚假的)外观，外表;假象

  • They hid the troubles plaguing their marriage behind a facade of family togetherness. [+ of]

    他们将困扰婚姻的问题掩藏在家庭和睦的外表之下。
