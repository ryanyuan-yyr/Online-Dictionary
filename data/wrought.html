wrought
rɔːt rɔt
1 VERB [only past] 引起;造成 If something has wrought a change, it has made it happen. 引起;造成 [journalism, literary]

  • Events in Paris wrought a change in British opinion towards France and Germany. [VERB noun]

    巴黎发生的事件改变了英国对法国和德国的看法。

2 ADJ 以…原料制造的;以…方式制作的 If something is wrought in a particular material or in a particular way, it has been created in that material or way. 以…原料制造的;以…方式制作的 [literary]

  • ...a walking stick with a gold head wrought in the form of a flower.

    金质花形包头的拐杖

3 See also: wreak
