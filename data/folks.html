folk
foʊk foʊk
Word forms: plural folks
Folk can also be used as the plural form for meaning 1. 义项1的复数形式亦可用 folk。
1 N-PLURAL 人们;大家 You can refer to people as folk or folks . 人们;大家

  • Country folk can tell you that there are certain places which animals avoid.

    老乡会告诉你有些地方动物是不会去的。

  • These are the folks from the local TV station.

    这些是当地电视台来的人。

  • ...old folks.

    老年人

2 N-PLURAL [usually poss NOUN] 亲人;家人;(尤指)双亲 You can refer to your close family, especially your mother and father, as your folks . 亲人;家人;(尤指)双亲 [informal]

  • I've been avoiding my folks lately.

    我近来一直在避开家人。

SYN family, race, parents, relations  
3 N-VOC (与人交谈时的称呼)大伙儿，诸位 You can use folks as a term of address when you are talking to several people. (与人交谈时的称呼)大伙儿，诸位 [informal]

  • 'It's a question of money, folks,' I announced.

    “诸位，这是一个钱的问题，”我郑重其事地说。

  • This is it, folks: the best record guide in the business.

    就是这个，伙计们，业内最好的唱片指南。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] (艺术或习俗)大众的，民俗的 Folk art and customs are traditional or typical of a particular community or nation. (艺术或习俗)大众的，民俗的

  • ...South American folk art.

    南美洲的民间艺术

  • ...traditional Chinese folk medicine.

    传统的中国民间医术

5 ADJ [ADJ n] (音乐)大众的，民间的 Folk music is music which is traditional or typical of a particular community or nation. (音乐)大众的，民间的

  • ...Irish folk music.

    爱尔兰民间音乐

Folk is also a noun.

  • ...a variety of music including classical, jazz, and folk.

    包括古典、爵士和民间音乐的各种音乐

6 ADJ [ADJ n] 大众的;普通百姓的 Folk can be used to describe something that relates to the beliefs and opinions of ordinary people. 大众的;普通百姓的

  • Jack was a folk hero in the Greenwich Village bars.

    在格林尼治村的酒吧里杰克是人们心目中的英雄。

  • Folk psychology comes closer to the obvious truth than the most sophisticated theories.

    比起最复杂的理论，大众心理学更接近那些显而易见的事实。
