Word forms: plural figs
1 无花果 A fig is a soft sweet fruit that grows in hot countries. It is full of tiny seeds and is often eaten dried. 无花果
2 无花果树 A fig or a fig tree is a tree on which figs grow. 无花果树
3 (对…)丝毫(不)在乎，完全(不)放在心上 If you say that someone doesn't care a fig or doesn't give a fig about something, you are emphasizing that they think it is unimportant or that they are not interested in it. (对…)丝毫(不)在乎，完全(不)放在心上

  • I do not give a fig what society thinks.

    我一点儿也不在乎社会怎么想。
