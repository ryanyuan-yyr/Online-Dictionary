unionization
juːnjənaɪzeɪʃən
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 unionisation
N-UNCOUNT （工人或行业的）加入工会，成立工会 The unionization of workers or industries is the process of workers becoming members of trade unions. （工人或行业的）加入工会，成立工会

  • Increasing unionization led to demands for higher wages and shorter hours.

    随着工会越来越壮大，有了提高工资、缩短工作时间的要求。
