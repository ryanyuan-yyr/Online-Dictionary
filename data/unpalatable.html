unpalatable
ʌnpælɪtəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （意见等）令人不快的，难以接受的 If you describe an idea as unpalatable, you mean that you find it unpleasant and difficult to accept. （意见等）令人不快的，难以接受的

  • It is an unpalatable fact that rape makes a good news story.

    强奸事件成了新闻报道的好素材，真是令人难以接受。

  • It was only then that I began to learn the unpalatable truth about John.

    直到那时我才开始了解到有关约翰的一些令人难以接受的实情。

SYN unpleasant, distasteful, disagreeable, bitter  
2 ADJ-GRADED 难吃的；不可口的 If you describe food as unpalatable, you mean that it is so unpleasant that you can hardly eat it. 难吃的；不可口的

  • ...a lump of dry, unpalatable cheese.

    一块干巴巴、难以入口的奶酪

SYN uneatable, unsavoury, inedible, unappetizing
