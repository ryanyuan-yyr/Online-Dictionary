livid
lɪvɪd ˈlɪvɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 盛怒的；暴怒的 Someone who is livid is extremely angry. 盛怒的；暴怒的 [informal]

  • I am absolutely livid about it. [+ about]

    我对此极为愤怒。

  • She is livid that I have invited Dick. [+ that]

    她因为我请了迪克而大发雷霆。

SYN angry, cross, furious, outraged  
2 ADJ-GRADED 青紫色的；黑红色的 Something that is livid is an unpleasant dark purple or red colour. 青紫色的；黑红色的

  • The scarred side of his face was a livid red.

    他结了伤疤的那侧脸青紫发红。

SYN discoloured, angry, purple, bruised
