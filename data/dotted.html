dotted
dɒtɪd ˈdɒtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 由点组成的 A dotted line is a line which is made of a row of dots. 由点组成的

  • Cut along the dotted line.

    沿虚线裁开。

If you sign on the dotted line, you formally agree to something by signing an official document. 正式签约接受

  • Once you sign on the dotted line you are committed to that property.

    一旦你签字同意，这份地产就交由你照管了。

2 ADJ [v-link ADJ + with] 遍布的;布满的 If a place or object is dotted with things, it has many of those things scattered over its surface. 遍布的;布满的

  • The maps were dotted with the names of small towns.

    地图上散布着小城镇的名字。

  • ...a pond that's dotted with water lilies.

    开满睡莲的池塘

3 ADJ 散布的;遍布的 If things are dotted around a place, they can be found in many different parts of that place. 散布的;遍布的

  • Many pieces of sculpture are dotted around the house.

    多件雕塑作品散落在房子各处。

4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 遍布大圆点的 You use dotted to describe something that is covered with large dots. 遍布大圆点的

  • ...a dotted bow tie.

    大圆点蝶形领结
