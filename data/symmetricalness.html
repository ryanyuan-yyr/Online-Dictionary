symmetrical
sɪmetrɪkəl sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl
ADJ 对称的 If something is symmetrical, it has two halves which are exactly the same, except that one half is the mirror image of the other. 对称的

  • ...the neat rows of perfectly symmetrical windows.

    一排排整齐而且完全对称的窗子

SYN balanced, regular, proportional, in proportion  
symmetrically

  • The south garden at Sissinghurst was composed symmetrically.

    锡辛赫斯特南面花园的布局工整对称。
