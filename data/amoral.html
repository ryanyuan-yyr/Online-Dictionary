amoral
eɪmɒrəl -mɔːr- eɪˈmɔrəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 无道德观念的；不辨是非的 If you describe someone as amoral, you do not like the way they behave because they do not seem to care whether what they do is right or wrong. 无道德观念的；不辨是非的 [disapproval]

    我强烈反对这种对待政治不辨是非的态度。

  • The film was violent and amoral.

    这部电影非常暴力并且无视道德标准。
