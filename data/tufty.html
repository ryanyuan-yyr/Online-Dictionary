tuft
tʌft tʌft
Word forms: plural tufts
N-COUNT 一丛;一簇;一撮 A tuft of something such as hair or grass is a small amount of it which is growing together in one place or is held together at the bottom. 一丛;一簇;一撮

  • He had a small tuft of hair on his chin. [+ of]

    他下巴上有一小撮胡须。

  • …tufts of wool torn from sheep by bushes and brambles.

    被矮丛林与荆棘粘下来的簇簇羊毛

SYN clump, bunch, shock, collection
