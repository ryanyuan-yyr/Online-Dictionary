biscuit
bɪskɪt ˈbɪskɪt
Word forms: plural biscuits
1 N-COUNT 饼干 A biscuit is a small flat cake that is crisp and usually sweet. 饼干 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 cookie
2 N-COUNT 烤饼；小圆面包 A biscuit is a small round dry cake that is made with baking powder, baking soda, or yeast. 烤饼；小圆面包 [US]
3 PHRASE （强调吃惊）竟然做出这种（愚蠢、粗野或自私的）事情 If someone has done something very stupid, rude, or selfish, you can say that they take the biscuit or that what they have done takes the biscuit, to emphasize your surprise at their behaviour. （强调吃惊）竟然做出这种（愚蠢、粗野或自私的）事情 [British, emphasis]
in AM, use 美国英语用 take the cake
