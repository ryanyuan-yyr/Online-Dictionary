allergic
əlɜːʳdʒɪk əˈlɜrdʒɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED （对…）过敏的 If you are allergic to something, you become ill or get a rash when you eat it, smell it, or touch it. （对…）过敏的

  • I'm allergic to cats. [+ to]

    我对猫过敏。

SYN sensitive, affected, susceptible, sensitized  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （反应）过敏的 If you have an allergic reaction to something, you become ill or get a rash when you eat it, smell it, or touch it. （反应）过敏的

  • Soya milk can cause allergic reactions in some children.

    有些孩子会对豆浆过敏。

SYN sensitive, affected, susceptible, sensitized  
3 ADJ-GRADED [v-link ADJ to n] （对…）十分反感的，厌恶的 If you say that you are allergic to something or someone, you mean that you dislike them very strongly and try to avoid them. （对…）十分反感的，厌恶的 [informal]

  • He was allergic to risk.

    他极其厌恶冒险。
