rejigger riːdʒɪgəʳ riˈdʒɪgər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rejiggers, present participle rejiggering, past tense , past participle rejiggered
VERB 重新安排;重新组织；调整 If someone rejiggers an organization or a piece of work, they arrange or organize it in a different way, in order to improve it. 重新安排;重新组织；调整 [US]
