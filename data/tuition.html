tuition
tjuɪʃən tjuːˈɪʃən tu- tuˈɪʃən tjuˈɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 讲授;教学;指导 If you are given tuition in a particular subject, you are taught about that subject. 讲授;教学;指导

  • The courses will give the beginner personal tuition in all types of outdoor photography. [+ in]

    该课程将为初学者一对一地讲授各种户外摄影技巧。

SYN training, schooling, education, teaching  
2 N-UNCOUNT （尤指大专院校或私立学校的）学费 You can use tuition to refer to the amount of money that you have to pay for being taught particular subjects, especially in a university, college, or private school. （尤指大专院校或私立学校的）学费

  • Angela's $7,000 tuition at University this year will be paid for with scholarships.

    安杰拉将用奖学金支付今年 7,000 美元的大学学费。
