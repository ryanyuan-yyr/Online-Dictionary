utility
juːtɪlɪti juˈtɪləti
Word forms: plural utilities
1 N-UNCOUNT 功用;效用;实用 The utility of something is its usefulness. 功用;效用;实用 [formal]

  • Belief in the utility of higher education is shared by students nationwide. [+ of]

    全国的学生都相信高等教育是有用的。

  • He inwardly questioned the utility of his work. [+ of]

    他内心质疑自己的工作是否有用。

SYN usefulness, use, point, benefit  
2 N-COUNT 公用事业 A utility is an important service such as water, electricity, or gas that is provided for everyone, and that everyone pays for. 公用事业

  • ...public utilities such as gas, electricity and phones.

    煤气、电和电话等公用事业
