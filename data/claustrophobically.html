claustrophobic
klɔːstrəfoʊbɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED (地方等)引起幽闭恐惧的 You describe a place or situation as claustrophobic when it makes you feel uncomfortable and unhappy because you are enclosed or restricted. (地方等)引起幽闭恐惧的

  • They lived in an unhealthily claustrophobic atmosphere.

    他们生活在一种令人感到幽闭恐惧的不健康氛围里。

  • The house felt too claustrophobic.

    这所房子太让人感到幽闭恐惧了。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 感觉幽闭恐惧的 If you feel claustrophobic, you feel very uncomfortable or anxious when you are in a small, crowded, or enclosed place. 感觉幽闭恐惧的

  • The churning, pressing crowds made her feel claustrophobic.

    挤来挤去的密集人群使她感到一种透不过气的恐惧。
