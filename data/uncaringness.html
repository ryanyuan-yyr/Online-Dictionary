uncaring
ʌnkeərɪŋ ʌnˈkɛərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 不关心他人疾苦的；冷漠的；无情的 If you describe someone as uncaring, you are critical of them for not caring about other people, especially people who are in a bad situation. 不关心他人疾苦的；冷漠的；无情的 [disapproval]

  • It portrays him as cold and uncaring.

    其中把他描绘成了一个冷漠无情的人。

  • ...this uncaring attitude towards the less well off.

    这种对穷人漠不关心的态度

SYN unconcerned, indifferent, negligent, unmoved
