ripple effect
Word forms: plural ripple effects
N-COUNT 连锁反应;涟漪作用 If an event or action has a ripple effect, it causes several other events to happen one after the other. 连锁反应;涟漪作用

  • Delayed flights have a ripple effect. Just one late flight could be carrying passengers for a dozen connecting services.

    航班晚点会引发连锁反应。仅仅一趟晚点的航班就可能运载着许多需要转机的乘客。
