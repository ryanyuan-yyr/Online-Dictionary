tear down
tɛə
PHRASAL VERB 拆毁;拆除 If you tear something down, you destroy it or remove it completely. 拆毁;拆除

  • The city will tear down the building and create a park.

    该市将拆掉这幢大楼，在这儿建个公园。

  • I imagine they'll be tearing the building down sooner or later. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我猜他们早晚会拆除这座楼的。

SYN demolish, knock down, pull down, level
