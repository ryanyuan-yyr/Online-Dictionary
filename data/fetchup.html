fetch up
PHRASAL VERB 偶然到达;无意中到达 If you fetch up somewhere, you arrive there, especially when you have not planned to go there. 偶然到达;无意中到达 [informal]

  • He spent the first few months on a walking and sketching tour, before fetching up in Dublin. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    他头几个月的行程都是边走边画，不期然间竟然到了都柏林。

SYN end up, reach, arrive, turn up
