prodigious
prədɪdʒəs proʊˈdɪdʒəs prəˈdɪdʒəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 巨大的；庞大的；大得惊人的 Something that is prodigious is very large or impressive. 巨大的；庞大的；大得惊人的 [literary]

  • This business generates cash in prodigious amounts.

    这种业务收益丰厚。

  • He impressed all who met him with his prodigious memory.

    他惊人的记忆力让所有见过他的人都印象深刻。

SYN huge, giant, massive, vast  
prodigiously

  • She ate prodigiously.

    她的饭量惊人。
