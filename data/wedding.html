wedding
wedɪŋ ˈwɛdɪŋ
Word forms: plural weddings
N-COUNT 婚礼 A wedding is a marriage ceremony and the party or special meal that often takes place after the ceremony. 婚礼

  • Most Britons want a traditional wedding.

    大多数英国人都想要传统的婚礼。

  • ...a wedding present.

    喜礼

  • ...the couple's 22nd wedding anniversary.

    这对夫妇的第22个结婚纪念日

SYN marriage, nuptials, wedding ceremony, marriage ceremony  
Usage Note :

  • Do not confuse wedding and marriage. A wedding is a ceremony in which a man and woman get married. It usually includes a meal or other celebration that takes place after the ceremony itself. It wasn't a formal wedding. This ceremony can also be called a marriage. ...the day of my marriage. Marriage can also be used to refer to the relationship between a husband and wife. It has been a happy marriage.

    不要混淆 wedding 和 marriage。wedding 指婚礼，通常包括结婚仪式之后的婚宴或其他庆祝活动：It wasn't a formal wedding (那不是个正式的婚礼)。这一仪式也可称为 marriage：the day of my marriage (我结婚那天)。marriage还可用于指婚姻生活：It has been a happy marriage (那是桩幸福美满的婚姻)。
