panic-stricken
ˈpænɪkˌstrɪkən
ADJ-GRADED 万分恐慌的；惊慌失措的 If someone is panic-stricken or is behaving in a panic-stricken way, they are so anxious or afraid that they may act without thinking carefully. 万分恐慌的；惊慌失措的

  • Panic-stricken travellers fled for the borders.

    惊慌失措的旅行者逃往边境。

  • A wall collapsed and 39 people, were killed in the panic-stricken stampede.

    一堵墙倒了，慌乱中有39人死于踩踏事故。

SYN frightened, alarmed, scared, terrified
