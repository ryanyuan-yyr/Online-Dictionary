crave
kreɪv kreɪv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense craves, present participle craving, past tense , past participle craved
VERB 渴望;热望 If you crave something, you want to have it very much. 渴望;热望 [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • There may be certain times of day when smokers crave their cigarette. [VERB noun]

    吸烟者可能会在一天中的某些时刻特别想吸烟。

  • You may be craving for some fresh air. [VERB + for]

    你可能很想呼吸呼吸新鲜空气。

SYN long for, yearn for, hanker after, be dying for
