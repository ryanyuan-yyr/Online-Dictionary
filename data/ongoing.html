ongoing
ɒngoʊɪŋ ˈɑnˌgoʊɪŋ
ADJ 进行中的；不间断的；继续存在的 An ongoing situation has been happening for quite a long time and seems likely to continue for some time in the future. 进行中的；不间断的；继续存在的

  • There is an ongoing debate on the issue.

    对此问题的争论一直没有间断过。

  • That research is ongoing.

    那项研究在进行中。

SYN in progress, continuing, current, growing
