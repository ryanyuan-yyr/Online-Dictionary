adorable
ədɔːrəbəl əˈdɔrəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可爱的；讨人喜爱的 If you say that someone or something is adorable, you are emphasizing that they are very attractive and you feel great affection for them. 可爱的；讨人喜爱的 [emphasis]

  • We have three adorable children.

    我们有3个可爱的孩子。

SYN lovable, pleasing, appealing, dear
