pejorative
pədʒɒrətɪv -dʒɔːr- pɪˈdʒɔrətɪv pɪˈdʒɑrətɪv ˈpidʒəˌreɪtɪv ˈpɛdʒəˌreɪtɪv
ADJ-GRADED 批评的；贬损的；贬义的 A pejorative word or expression is one that expresses criticism of someone or something. 批评的；贬损的；贬义的 [formal]

  • I agree I am ambitious, and I don't see that as a pejorative term.

    我承认我有野心，但我不认为这是个贬义词。

  • Isn't there a suggestion that 'poetess' is slightly pejorative?

    难道没有人提出“女诗人”有点儿贬义吗？

SYN derogatory, negative, slighting, unpleasant
