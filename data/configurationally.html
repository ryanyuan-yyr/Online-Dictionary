configuration
kənfɪgʊreɪʃən -fɪgjə- kənˌfɪgjəˈreɪʃən
Word forms: plural configurations
1 N-COUNT 安排;格局;布局 A configuration is an arrangement of a group of things. 安排;格局;布局 [formal]

  • ...Stonehenge, in south-western England, an ancient configuration of giant stones. [+ of]

    英格兰西南部的巨石阵—一座古老的巨石阵列

2 N-UNCOUNT 配置 The configuration of a computer system is way in which all its parts, such as the hardware and software, are connected together in order for the computer to work. 配置 [computing]

  • Prices range from $119 to $199, depending on the particular configuration.

    价格因具体配置而异，从119美元至199美元不等。
