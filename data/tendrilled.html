tendril
tendrɪl ˈtɛndrəl
Word forms: plural tendrils
1 N-COUNT 一束，一缕(头发等) A tendril is something light and thin, for example a piece of hair which hangs loose and is away from the main part. 一束，一缕(头发等)

  • Tendrils of hair strayed to the edge of her pillow. [+ of]

    她的几缕头发散落在枕边。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (攀缘植物的)卷须, 蔓 Tendrils are thin stems which grow on some plants so that they can attach themselves to supports such as walls or other plants. (攀缘植物的)卷须, 蔓
