well-wisher
ˈwɛlˌwɪʃər
(also wellwisher)Word forms: plural well-wishers
N-COUNT [usually plural] 表示良好祝愿的人 Well-wishers are people who hope that a particular person or thing will be successful, and who show this by their behaviour. 表示良好祝愿的人

  • The main street was lined with well-wishers.

    大街两旁满是祝福的人群。
