rapprochement
ræprɒʃmɒn raprɔʃmɑ̃ -proʊʃ- ˌræproʊʃˈmɑn ʀapʀɔʃˈmɑ̃
N-SING （国家、集体或个人的）重归于好，和解 A rapprochement is an increase in friendliness between two countries, groups, or people, especially after a period of unfriendliness. （国家、集体或个人的）重归于好，和解 [formal]

  • There have been growing signs of a rapprochement with Vietnam. [+ with]

    同越南恢复友好关系的迹象越来越明显了。

  • ...the process of political rapprochement between the two former foes. [+ between]

    两个宿敌之间的政治和解进程

SYN reconciliation, softening, reunion, détente
