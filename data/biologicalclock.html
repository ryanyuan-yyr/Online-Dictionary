biological clock
Word forms: plural biological clocks
N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 生物钟 Your biological clock is your body's way of registering time. It does not rely on events such as day or night, but on factors such as your habits, your age, and chemical changes taking place in your body. 生物钟

  • For women, the 'biological clock' governs the time for having children.

    对于女性来说，“生物钟”控制着怀孕的时间。
