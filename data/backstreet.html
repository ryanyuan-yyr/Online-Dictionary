back street
bæk striːt
(also back-street,backstreet)Word forms: plural back streets
1 N-COUNT 后街；小街；僻巷 A back street in a town or city is a small, narrow street with very little traffic. 后街；小街；僻巷

  • The small church of San Michel is tucked away in a narrow back street of Port-au-Prince.

    圣米歇尔小教堂隐藏在太子港一条偏僻窄巷里。

  • ...backstreet garages.

    后街车库

2 N-PLURAL 老城区；贫民区 The back streets of a town or city are the areas of small, old, poor streets rather than the richer or newer areas. 老城区；贫民区

  • ...the back streets of Berlin.

    柏林的旧城区

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 秘密的；非法的；偷偷摸摸的 Back street activities are carried out unofficially, secretly, and often illegally. 秘密的；非法的；偷偷摸摸的

  • ...back street abortions.

    非法堕胎
