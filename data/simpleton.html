simpleton
sɪmpəltən ˈsɪmpəltən
Word forms: plural simpletons
N-COUNT 傻瓜;呆子;容易受骗的人 If you call someone a simpleton, you think they are easily deceived or not very intelligent. 傻瓜;呆子;容易受骗的人 [disapproval]

  • 'But Ian's such a simpleton', she laughed.

    “可是伊恩太傻了，”她笑着说道。

  • He was a lightweight, a political simpleton.

    他是个无名之辈，头脑简单的政客。

SYN halfwit, fool, idiot, charlie [British , informal]
