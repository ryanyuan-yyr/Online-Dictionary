intone
ɪntoʊn ɪnˈtoʊn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense intones, present participle intoning, past tense , past participle intoned
VERB 缓慢庄重地说;(以单一的音调)吟咏，吟诵 If you intone something, you say it in a slow and serious way, with most of the words at one pitch. 缓慢庄重地说;(以单一的音调)吟咏，吟诵 [written]

  • He quietly intoned several prayers. [VERB noun]

    他肃穆地轻声念了几句祈祷词。

  • 'But Jesus is here!' the priest intoned. [VERB with quote]

    “但是耶稣在这里！”牧师吟诵道。

SYN chant, sing, recite, croon
