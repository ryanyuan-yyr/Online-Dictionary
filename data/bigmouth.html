big mouth
Word forms: plural big mouths
N-COUNT 大嘴巴；不能保守秘密的人 If you say that someone is a big mouth or that they have a big mouth, you mean that they tell other people things that should have been kept secret. 大嘴巴；不能保守秘密的人 [informal, disapproval]
