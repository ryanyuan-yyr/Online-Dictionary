aggression
əgreʃən əˈgrɛʃən
Word forms: plural aggressions
1 N-UNCOUNT 好斗性；攻击性 Aggression is a quality of anger and determination that makes you ready to attack other people. 好斗性；攻击性

    好斗性绝非男性才具有的特征。

    那次偷袭是一种不正当的侵略行径。

    大规模军事侵略的威胁

SYN attack, campaign, injury, assault
