preferable
prefrəbəl ˈprɛfərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 更好的；更可取的；更合适的 If you say that one thing is preferable to another, you mean that it is more desirable or suitable. 更好的；更可取的；更合适的

  • A big earthquake a long way off is preferable to a smaller one nearby. [+ to]

    远处发生大地震总比在附近发生小地震好。

  • The hazards of the theatre seemed preferable to joining the family paint business. [+ to]

    经营剧院的风险似乎比加入家族涂料产业的风险要小一些。

  • Was an evening with Peter in their company preferable to being left at home alone?

    晚上和彼得在他们的公司呆着是不是比一个人留在家里好一些？

SYN better, best, chosen, choice  
preferably

  • Do something creative or take exercise, preferably in the fresh air.

    做一些有创意的事情或者运动一下，最好是在空气新鲜的户外进行。
