horrible
hɒrɪbəl hɔːr- ˈhɔrəbəl ˈhɑrəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 讨厌的;糟透的 If you describe something or someone as horrible, you do not like them at all. 讨厌的;糟透的 [informal]

  • The record sounds horrible.

    唱片难听极了。

  • ...a horrible small boy.

    讨厌的小男孩

horribly

  • When trouble comes they behave selfishly and horribly.

    遇到麻烦时，他们表现得很自私，很差劲。

2 ADJ-GRADED 令人震惊的;使人恐惧的;让人极度反感的 You can call something horrible when it causes you to feel great shock, fear, and disgust. 令人震惊的;使人恐惧的;让人极度反感的

  • Still the horrible shrieking came out of his mouth.

    他的嘴里还是发出了恐怖的尖叫声。

SYN terrible, awful, appalling, terrifying  
horribly

  • A two-year-old boy was horribly murdered.

    一名两岁男孩惨遭杀害。

3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 极严重的 Horrible is used to emphasize how bad something is. 极严重的 [emphasis]

  • That seems like a horrible mess that will drag on for years.

    看起来那像是个会拖延多年的大烂摊子。

  • Unless you respect other people's religions, horrible mistakes and conflict will occur.

    如果不尊重别人的宗教信仰，就会祷下大错，酿成冲突。

horribly

  • Our plans have gone horribly wrong.

    我们的计划出了大岔子。

  • You got horribly drunk.

    你喝了个烂醉。
