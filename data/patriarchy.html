patriarchy
peɪtriɑːʳki ˈpeɪtriˌɑrki
Word forms: plural patriarchies
1 N-UNCOUNT 男性家长制；父权制 Patriarchy is a system in which men have all or most of the power and importance in a society or group. 男性家长制；父权制

  • The main cause of women's and children's oppression is patriarchy.

    妇女儿童受压迫的主要原因是父权制。

2 N-COUNT 父权社会；父系社会 A patriarchy is a patriarchal society. 父权社会；父系社会
