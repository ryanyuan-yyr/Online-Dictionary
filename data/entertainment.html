entertainment
entəʳteɪnmənt ˌɛntərˈteɪnmənt
Word forms: plural entertainments
N-VAR 娱乐节目；娱乐活动 Entertainment consists of performances of plays and films, and activities such as reading and watching television, that give people pleasure. 娱乐节目；娱乐活动

  • ...the world of entertainment and international stardom.

    娱乐界和国际明星圈

  • There were feasts and banquets, theatrical entertainments and sporting competitions between local groups.

    有酒席宴会、戏剧演出和地方队之间的体育竞赛。
