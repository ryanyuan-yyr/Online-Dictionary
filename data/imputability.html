impute
ɪmpjuːt ɪmˈpjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense imputes, present participle imputing, past tense , past participle imputed
1 VERB 归咎(于);归罪(于);归因(于) If you impute something such as blame or a crime to someone, you say that they are responsible for it or are the cause of it. 归咎(于);归罪(于);归因(于) [formal]

  • It is grossly unfair to impute blame to the United Nations. [VERB noun + to]

    把责任归咎于联合国极其不公。
