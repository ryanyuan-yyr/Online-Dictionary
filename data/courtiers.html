courtier
kɔːʳtiəʳ ˈkɔrtiər ˈkɔrtjər
Word forms: plural courtiers
N-COUNT 侍臣;侍从;廷臣 Courtiers were noblemen and women who spent a lot of time at the court of a king or queen. 侍臣;侍从;廷臣
SYN attendant, follower, squire, pursuivant [archaic]  
Quotations
