street smarts
striːt smɑːts
N-PLURAL (尤指在城市生存的)技能，精明;街头智慧 You can use street smarts to refer to the skills and intelligence people need to be successful in difficult situations, especially in a city. (尤指在城市生存的)技能，精明;街头智慧 [US, informal]

  • The boys learned their street smarts early.

    男孩子们很小就学会了如何在社会上混。
