mollify
mɒlɪfaɪ ˈmɑləˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mollifies, present participle mollifying, past tense , past participle mollified
VERB 抚慰;安抚 If you mollify someone, you do or say something to make them less upset or angry. 抚慰;安抚 [formal]

  • The investigation was undertaken primarily to mollify pressure groups. [VERB noun]

    展开调查主要是为了安抚那些压力集团。

SYN pacify, quiet, calm, compose  
mollified

  • He looked first mollified and then relieved as it occurred to him his plight could be worse.

    想到自己本可能会陷入更糟糕的困境，他看上去先是感到宽慰，随后又如释重负。
