family
fæmɪli ˈfæməli ˈfæmli
Word forms: plural families
1 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 家;家庭;家族 A family is a group of people who are related to each other, especially parents and their children. 家;家庭;家族

  • There's room in there for a family of five. [+ of]

    那儿能住下五口之家。

  • His family are completely behind him, whatever he decides.

    他无论作出什么决定，家人都全力支持。

  • To him the family is the core of society.

    对他来说，家庭是社会的核心。

  • Does he have any family?

    他有什么亲人吗？

SYN relations, people, children, issue  
2 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 子女;孩子 When people talk about a family, they sometimes mean children. 子女;孩子

  • They decided to start a family.

    他们决定要孩子。

  • ...couples with large families.

    儿女成群的夫妇

3 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 祖先;祖辈 When people talk about their family, they sometimes mean their ancestors. 祖先;祖辈

  • Her family came to Los Angeles at the turn of the century.

    她的祖先在世纪之交时来到洛杉矶。

  • ...homes where their families had lived for generations.

    祖祖辈辈生活过的家

  • ...the history of mental illness in the family.

    家族精神病史

SYN ancestors, forebears, parentage, forefathers  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] 家族的;家庭的;家里的 You can use family to describe things that belong to a particular family. 家族的;家庭的;家里的

  • He returned to the family home.

    他回到自己家里。

  • I was working in the family business.

    我在自家的企业工作。

5 ADJ [ADJ n] 全家共用的;全家共享的 You can use family to describe things that are designed to be used or enjoyed by both parents and children. 全家共用的;全家共享的

  • It had been designed as a family house.

    这房子的设计适合一家老小共同居住。

  • A wedding is a family event.

    婚礼是全家人的庆典。

6 N-COUNT (动植物的)科 A family of animals or plants is a group of related species. (动植物的)科

  • ...foods in the cabbage family, such as Brussels sprouts.

    抱子甘蓝之类的十字花科食物

SYN species, group, class, system
