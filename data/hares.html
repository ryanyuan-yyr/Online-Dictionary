hare
heəʳ hɛə hɛr hær
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hares, present participle haring, past tense , past participle hared
1 N-VAR 野兔 A hare is an animal like a rabbit but larger with long ears, long legs, and a small tail. 野兔
Hare is the flesh of this animal eaten as food.野兔肉
2 VERB 飞跑;飞奔 If you hare off somewhere, you go there very quickly. 飞跑;飞奔 [British, informal]

  • ...an over-protective mother who keeps haring off to ring the babysitter. [VERB adv./prep.]

    过于爱护孩子而不断地跑去给保姆打电话的母亲
