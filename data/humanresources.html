human resources
N-UNCOUNT （公司等机构的）人力资源（部）（缩略形式为HR） In a company or other organization, the department of human resources is the department with responsibility for the recruiting, training, and welfare of the staff. The abbreviation HR is often used. （公司等机构的）人力资源（部）（缩略形式为HR） [business]
SYN personnel, staff, workers, the workforce
