aggrandizement
əgrændɪzmənt
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 aggrandisement
1 N-UNCOUNT （权势、财富、地位的）扩大，增加，提高 If someone does something for aggrandizement, they do it in order to get power, wealth, and importance for themselves. （权势、财富、地位的）扩大，增加，提高 [formal, disapproval]

  • It would be the first time in human history that economic necessity has prevailed over military aggrandizement.

    那应该是人类历史上首次经济需要高于军事扩张。
