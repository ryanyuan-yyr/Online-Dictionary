indeed
ɪndiːd ɪnˈdid
1 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 确实;的确 You use indeed to confirm or agree with something that has just been said. 确实;的确 [emphasis]

  • Later, he admitted that the payments had indeed been made.

    后来,他承认确实付过款了。

  • He did indeed keep important documents inside his hat.

    他确实把重要文件放在了他的帽子里。

  • 'Did you know him?' — 'I did indeed.'.

    “你认识他吗?”—“当然。”

  • 'Know what I mean?' — 'Indeed I do.'.

    “明白我的意思吗?”—“当然明白了。”

  • 'Isn't it a gorgeous day, Father?' — 'Yes, indeed!'.

    “爸爸，今天天气是不是很棒?”—“是的,确实很棒！”

  • 'That's a topic which has come to the fore very much recently.' — 'Indeed.'

    “那是最近才受到众人关注的一个话题。”—“确实如此。”

SYN certainly, yes, definitely, surely  
2 ADV (表示语气的递进)实际上,其实,确切说来 You use indeed to introduce a further comment or statement which strengthens the point you have already made. (表示语气的递进)实际上,其实,确切说来 [emphasis]

  • We have nothing against diversity; indeed, we want more of it.

    我们一点都不抵触多样化;实际上,我们需要多样性更丰富一些。

  • When we asked to see more we were refused. Indeed we were escorted away by men with guns.

    当我们要求进一步参观时却被拒绝了。实际上，我们是被一些带枪的人护送离开的。

3 ADV [adj ADV] (位于句末修饰very或强调某词)确实,实在 You use indeed at the end of a clause to give extra force to the word 'very', or to emphasize a particular word. (位于句末修饰very或强调某词)确实,实在 [emphasis]

  • The engine began to sound very loud indeed.

    发动机开始发出极大的声响。

  • The wine was very good indeed.

    红酒确实非常好。

  • Of course, these occasions are rare indeed.

    当然,这样的场合实在稀少。

4 ADV [quest ADV] (重复问题以作强调,尤用于答案未知时)到底,究竟 You can use indeed as a way of repeating a question in order to emphasize it, especially when you do not know the answer. (重复问题以作强调,尤用于答案未知时)到底,究竟 [spoken, emphasis]

  • 'Now where are the real villains?' — 'Where indeed?'.

    “那么那些真正的恶棍在哪里？”—“是啊，到底在哪里？”

  • 'And what do we do here?' — 'What, indeed?'

    “我们在这里做什么呢?”—“是啊，到底做什么?”
