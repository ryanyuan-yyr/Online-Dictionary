vegetate
vedʒɪteɪt ˈvɛdʒəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vegetates, present participle vegetating, past tense , past participle vegetated
VERB 过单调无聊的生活;无所事事地生活 If someone vegetates, they spend their time doing boring or worthless things. 过单调无聊的生活;无所事事地生活

  • He spends all his free time at home vegetating in front of the TV. [VERB]

    他一有空闲时间就窝在家里看电视。

SYN stagnate, idle, loaf, exist
