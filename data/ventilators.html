ventilator
ventɪleɪtəʳ ˈvɛntəlˌeɪtər
Word forms: plural ventilators
1 N-COUNT 人工呼吸器 A ventilator is a machine that helps people breathe when they cannot breathe naturally, for example because they are very ill or have been seriously injured. 人工呼吸器
2 N-COUNT 通风设备 A ventilator is a device that lets fresh air into a room or building and lets old or dirty air out. 通风设备
