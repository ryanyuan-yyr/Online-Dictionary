smutty
smʌti ˈsmʌti
Word forms: comparative smuttier, superlative smuttiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 猥亵的;淫秽的 If you describe something such as a joke, book, or film as smutty, you disapprove of it because it shows naked people or refers to sex in a rude or unpleasant way. 猥亵的;淫秽的 [disapproval]

  • ...smutty jokes.

    荤段子

  • I detest smutty books.

    我对淫秽书刊痛恨之极。

SYN obscene, dirty, crude, coarse
