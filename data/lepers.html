leper
lepəʳ ˈlɛpər
Word forms: plural lepers
1 N-COUNT 麻风病患者；麻风病人 A leper is a person who has leprosy. 麻风病患者；麻风病人
2 N-COUNT （因行为令人震惊或反感）大家唯恐避之不及的人，被排斥的人 If you refer to someone as a leper, you mean that people in their community avoid them because they have done something that has shocked or offended people. （因行为令人震惊或反感）大家唯恐避之不及的人，被排斥的人

  • The newspaper article had branded her a social leper not fit to be seen in company.

    报纸上的这篇文章给她冠以“社交毒药”之名，称人皆不宜与其为伍。

SYN outcast, reject, untouchable, pariah
