gutless
gʌtləs ˈgʌtlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 缺乏勇气的；没有胆量的；无毅力的；懦弱的 If you describe someone as gutless, you think they have a weak character and lack courage or determination. 缺乏勇气的；没有胆量的；无毅力的；懦弱的 [disapproval]

  • By attacking me, by attacking my wife, he has proved himself to be a gutless coward.

    他攻击我，攻击我的妻子，这正证明他自己是个没出息的胆小鬼。

SYN faint-hearted, weak, cowardly, chicken [slang]
