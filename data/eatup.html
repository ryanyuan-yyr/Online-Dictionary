eat up
1 PHRASAL VERB 吃光;吃完 When you eat up your food, you eat all of it. 吃光;吃完

  • Eat up your lunch. [VERB PREP. noun]

    吃完你的中饭。

  • Some seed fell along the footpath, and the birds came and ate it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    有些种子散落在小路上，鸟儿飞来把它们吃光了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 耗费（大量金钱、时间或资源） If something eats up money, time, or resources, it uses them or consumes them in great quantities. 耗费（大量金钱、时间或资源）

  • Health insurance costs are eating up his income. [VERB PREP. noun]

    医疗保险费用花掉了他大量的收入。
