oxygenate
ɒksɪdʒɪneɪt ˈɒksɪdʒɪˌneɪt ˈɑksɪdʒəˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense oxygenates, present participle oxygenating, past tense , past participle oxygenated
VERB 加氧于；充氧于 To oxygenate something means to mix or dissolve oxygen into it. 加氧于；充氧于

    之前尝试过几次将鱼缸中的水过滤并充氧，但都没有成功。

  • ...freshly oxygenated blood. [VERB-ed]

    刚充氧的血液
