insider
ɪnsaɪdəʳ ˈɪnˌsaɪdər ɪnˈsaɪdər
Word forms: plural insiders
N-COUNT 知情人;了解内幕者;消息灵通人士 An insider is someone who is involved in a situation and who knows more about it than other people. 知情人;了解内幕者;消息灵通人士

  • An insider said, 'Katharine has told friends it is time to end her career.'

    一位知情人说：“凯瑟琳已告诉朋友准备结束她的事业”。

  • German banks have more insider knowledge than most.

    德国银行与大多数银行相比知晓更多的内部消息。

SYN worker, employee, staff member, workman
