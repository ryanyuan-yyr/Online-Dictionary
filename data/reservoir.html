reservoir
rezəʳvwɑːʳ ˈrɛzərˌvwɑr ˈrɛzəˌvwɑr ˈrɛzərˌvwɔr ˈrɛzəvwɔr ˈrɛzərˌvɔr
Word forms: plural reservoirs
1 N-COUNT 水库;蓄水池 A reservoir is a lake that is used for storing water before it is supplied to people. 水库;蓄水池
2 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] 储藏;蓄积 A reservoir of something is a large quantity of it that is available for use when needed. 储藏;蓄积 [Also + of]

  • ...the huge oil reservoir beneath the Kuwaiti desert.

    科威特沙漠下面巨大的石油储藏

  • ...the body's short-term reservoir of energy.

    身体的短期能量储备

SYN store, stock, source, supply
