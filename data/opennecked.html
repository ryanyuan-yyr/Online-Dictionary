open-necked ˌəʊpənˈnɛkt
(also open-neck)
ADJ [ADJ n] （衬衫等）开领的，敞领的 If you are wearing an open-necked shirt or blouse, you are wearing a shirt or blouse which has no buttons at the top or on which the top button is not done up. （衬衫等）开领的，敞领的

  • He was wearing cotton pants and an open-necked shirt.

    他穿着纯棉裤子和开领衬衫。
