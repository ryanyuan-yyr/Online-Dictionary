strait
streɪt streɪt
Word forms: plural straits
1 N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES 海峡;(连接两大海域的)水道 You can refer to a narrow strip of sea which joins two large areas of sea as a strait or the straits . 海峡;(连接两大海域的)水道

  • An estimated 1600 vessels pass through the strait annually.

    估计每年有 1,600 艘船通过该海峡。

  • ...the Straits of Gibraltar.

    直布罗陀海峡

SYN channel, sound, narrows, stretch of water  
2 N-PLURAL [adjective NOUN] (通常指因缺钱造成的)困境，窘迫 If someone is in dire or desperate straits, they are in a very difficult situation, usually because they do not have much money. (通常指因缺钱造成的)困境，窘迫

  • If we had a child, we'd be in really dire straits.

    假如我们有了小孩，我们会真正陷入困境。

  • The company's closure has left many small businessmen in desperate financial straits.

    该公司的倒闭让很多小商人陷入了经济困境。
