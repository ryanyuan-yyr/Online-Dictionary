banner headline
ˈbænə ˈhɛdlaɪn
Word forms: plural banner headlines
N-COUNT （报纸首页上的）通栏大标题 A banner headline is a large headline in a newspaper that stretches across the front page. （报纸首页上的）通栏大标题

  • Today's front page of The Sun carries a banner headline 'The adulterer, the bungler and the joker.'

    《太阳报》今日头版大标题为《奸夫、笨蛋和小丑》。
