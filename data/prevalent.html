prevalent
prevələnt ˈprɛvələnt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 流行的；盛行的；普遍的 A condition, practice, or belief that is prevalent is common. 流行的；盛行的；普遍的

  • This condition is more prevalent in women than in men.

    这种病症在女性当中比在男性当中更为普遍。

  • Smoking is becoming increasingly prevalent among younger women.

    抽烟在年轻女性中变得越来越常见。

  • The prevalent view is that interest rates will fall.

    人们普遍认为利率会下降。

SYN common, accepted, established, popular
