cloche
klɒʃ kloʊʃ
Word forms: plural cloches
1 N-COUNT (保护植物幼苗不受寒的)玻璃罩，透明塑料罩 A cloche is a long, low cover made of glass or clear plastic that is put over young plants to protect them from the cold. (保护植物幼苗不受寒的)玻璃罩，透明塑料罩
2 N-COUNT (20世纪20年代流行的)钟形女帽 A cloche or a cloche hat is a woman's hat shaped like a bell that fits closely round the head. Cloche hats were popular in the 1920s. (20世纪20年代流行的)钟形女帽
