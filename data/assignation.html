assignation

Word forms: plural assignations
N-COUNT 私会；（尤指与情人的）幽会，约会 An assignation is a secret meeting with someone, especially with a lover. 私会；（尤指与情人的）幽会，约会 [formal]

  • She had an assignation with her boyfriend. [+ with]

    她和男友有个约会。

SYN secret meeting, rendezvous, tryst [archaic] , clandestine meeting
