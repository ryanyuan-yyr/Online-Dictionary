secrete
sɪkriːt sɪˈkrit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense secretes, present participle secreting, past tense , past participle secreted
1 VERB 分泌 If part of a plant, animal, or human secretes a liquid, it produces it. 分泌

  • The sweat glands secrete water. [VERB noun]

    汗腺分泌汗液。

SYN give off, emit, emanate, exude  
2 VERB 隐藏;藏匿 If you secrete something somewhere, you hide it there so that nobody will find it. 隐藏;藏匿 [literary]

  • She secreted the gun in the kitchen cabinet. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    她把枪藏在橱柜里。

SYN hide, conceal, stash [informal] , cover
