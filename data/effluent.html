effluent
efluənt ˈɛfluənt
Word forms: plural effluents
N-MASS (工厂或污水处理厂排出的)废水，污水 Effluent is liquid waste material that comes out of factories or sewage works. (工厂或污水处理厂排出的)废水，污水 [formal]

  • The effluent from the factory was dumped into the river.

    那家工厂的废水被排入河中。

  • All industrial chemical plants produce waste effluents.

    所有工业化工厂都会排放废水。

SYN waste, discharge, flow, emission
