zealous
zeləs ˈzɛləs
1 ADJ-GRADED （尤指对政治或宗教理想）热情的，充满激情的，狂热的 Someone who is zealous spends a lot of time or energy in supporting something that they believe in very strongly, especially a political or religious ideal. （尤指对政治或宗教理想）热情的，充满激情的，狂热的

  • She was a zealous worker for charitable bodies.

    她对慈善事业充满热忱。

  • He was a recent Catholic convert, and very zealous.

    他最近才皈依天主教，是一名狂热的信徒。

SYN enthusiastic, passionate, earnest, burning  
zealously

    它以往活动的详细情况被严格保密。
