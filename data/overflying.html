overfly
oʊvəʳflaɪ ˌoʊvərˈflaɪ ˈoʊvərˌflaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overflies, present participle overflying, past tense overflew, past participle overflown
VERB 飞越；飞过 When an aircraft overflies an area, it flies over it. 飞越；飞过 [formal]

  • Permission has not yet been granted for the airline to overfly Tanzania. [VERB noun]

    这家航空公司还未获得飞越坦桑尼亚领空的许可。
