masterstroke
mɑːstəʳstroʊk mæs- ˈmæstərˌstroʊk
Word forms: plural masterstrokes
N-COUNT [usually singular] 妙举;绝招;高招 A masterstroke is something you do which is unexpected but very clever and which helps you to achieve something. 妙举;绝招;高招

  • Graham pulled a masterstroke by playing Paul Merson in the centre of midfield.

    格雷厄姆让保罗·默森打中场中路堪称神来之笔。

SYN act of genius, victory, triumph, coup
