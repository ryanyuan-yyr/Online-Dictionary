infield
ɪnfild ˈɪnˌfiːld ˈɪnˌfild
Word forms: infields
1 N-COUNT [usu sing, the N] (棒球场地的)内场In baseball, the infield is the part of the playing field that is inside the area marked by the four bases. (棒球场地的)内场

  • He put six fielders on the right side of the infield.

    他将6名接球手安排在内场的右侧。

2 ADV [ADV after v] (足球及橄榄球等运动中，移)向内场区In sports such as football and rugby, if players move infield, they move toward the centre of the playing field. (足球及橄榄球等运动中，移)向内场区

  • Farrell raced up the right touchline, dodged infield and curled a shot with his left foot.

    法雷尔跑向右边线，向内场闪躲，左脚射出一弧线球。
