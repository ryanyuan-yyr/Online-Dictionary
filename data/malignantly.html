malignant
məlɪgnənt məˈlɪgnənt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (肿瘤或疾病)恶性的 A malignant tumour or disease is out of control and likely to cause death. (肿瘤或疾病)恶性的 [medicine]

  • She developed a malignant breast tumour.

    她长了一个恶性的乳房肿瘤。

2 ADJ-GRADED （人）恶意的，恶毒的 If you say that someone is malignant, you think they are cruel and like to cause harm. （人）恶意的，恶毒的

  • He said that we were evil, malignant and mean.

    他说我们既邪恶又恶毒，还非常卑鄙。

  • ...a community overrun by a malignant minority indulging in crime and violence.

    一小撮从事违法和暴力活动的坏人横行无忌的社会

SYN hostile, harmful, bitter, vicious
