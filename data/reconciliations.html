reconciliation
rekənsɪlieɪʃən ˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural reconciliations
1 N-VAR 和解Reconciliation between two people or countries who have quarrelled is the process of their becoming friends again. A reconciliation is an instance of this. 和解 [Also + with/of]

  • ...an appeal for reconciliation between Catholics and Protestants. [+ between]

    …一个对天主教徒和新教徒之间和解的呼吁。

SYN reunion, conciliation, rapprochement, appeasement  
2 N-SING 调和; 一致The reconciliation of two beliefs, facts, or demands that seem to be opposed is the process of finding a way in which they can both be true or both be successful. 调和; 一致 [Also + with/between]

  • ...the ideal of democracy based upon a reconciliation of the values of equality and liberty. [+ of]

    …平等和自由两种价值观和谐统一基础上的民主观念。

SYN harmonizing, balancing, squaring, adjustment
