precept
priːsept ˈpriˌsɛpt
Word forms: plural precepts
N-COUNT 准则；规范；戒律 A precept is a general rule that helps you to decide how you should behave in particular circumstances. 准则；规范；戒律 [formal]

  • ...an electoral process based on the precept that all men are born equal.

    以所有人生而平等这一准则为基础的选举程序

  • ...the precepts of Buddhism.

    佛教的戒律

SYN rule, order, law, direction
