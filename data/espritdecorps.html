esprit de corps espriːdə kɔːʳ ɛˈspriː də ˈkɔː ɛspri də kɔr ɛˈspridə ˈkɔr
N-UNCOUNT 团队精神；集体荣誉感 Esprit de corps is a feeling of loyalty and pride that is shared by the members of a group who consider themselves to be different from other people in some special way. 团队精神；集体荣誉感 [formal]
SYN solidarity, rapport, team spirit, camaraderie
