beat out
1 PHRASAL VERB （用鼓等乐器）敲奏出 If you beat out sounds on a drum or similar instrument, you make the sounds by hitting the instrument. （用鼓等乐器）敲奏出

  • Drums and cymbals beat out a solemn rhythm. [VERB PREP. noun]

    鼓和铙钹奏出了庄严的节奏。

2 PHRASAL VERB （用毯子等）扑灭（火） If you beat out a fire, you cause it to go out by hitting it, usually with an object such as a blanket. （用毯子等）扑灭（火）

  • His brother beat out the flames with a blanket. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他哥哥用毯子把火扑灭了。

  • She managed to beat the fire out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她好不容易把火扑灭了。

SYN put out, stifle, smother, extinguish  
3 PHRASAL VERB 打败；击败；战胜 If you beat out someone in a competition, you defeat them. 打败；击败；战胜 [mainly US]

  • Indianapolis beat out nearly 100 other cities as the site for a huge United Airlines maintenance center. [VERB PREP. noun]

    印第安纳波利斯从近百座城市中胜出，成为美国联合航空公司大型维护中心所在地。

  • If we are certain a rival will beat us out, we are wide open to jealousy. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果我们能够确定某个选手将打败我们，我们很容易心生妒忌。
