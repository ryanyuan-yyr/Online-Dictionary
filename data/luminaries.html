luminary
luːmɪnəri -neri ˈluməˌnɛri
Word forms: plural luminaries
1 N-COUNT 方家；大家；泰斗 If you refer to someone as a luminary, you mean that they are an expert in a particular subject or activity. 方家；大家；泰斗 [literary]

  • ...the political opinions of such luminaries as Sartre or de Beauvoir.

    诸如萨特、波伏娃等大家的政见

SYN celebrity, star, expert, somebody
