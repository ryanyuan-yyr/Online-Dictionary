ghost writer
ɡəʊst ˈraɪtə
(also ghostwriter)Word forms: plural ghost writers
N-COUNT 代人写作者；捉刀人 A ghost writer is someone who writes a book or other published work instead of the person who is named as the author. 代人写作者；捉刀人

  • I suspect she'll be writing it herself; she doesn't need a ghost writer.

    我觉得她会自己动笔；无需他人捉刀。
