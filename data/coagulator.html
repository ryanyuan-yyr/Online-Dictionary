coagulate
koʊægjʊleɪt koʊˈægjuˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense coagulates, present participle coagulating, past tense , past participle coagulated
VERB (液体)凝结，凝固 When a liquid coagulates, it becomes very thick. (液体)凝结，凝固

  • As the egg whites cook, they coagulate and rise to the surface. [VERB]

    蛋白一煮就会凝结，并且浮起来。

  • The blood coagulates to stop wounds bleeding. [VERB]

    血液凝结，使伤口不再出血。

SYN congeal, clot, thicken, curdle  
coagulation

  • Blood becomes stickier to help coagulation in case of a cut.

    割伤的时候，血液会变得更为黏稠以助于凝结。
