flyer
flaɪəʳ ˈflaɪər
(also flier)Word forms: plural flyers
1 N-COUNT 飞行员 A flyer is a pilot of an aircraft. 飞行员
2 N-COUNT 飞机乘客 You can refer to someone who travels by aeroplane as a flyer . 飞机乘客

  • ...regular business flyers.

    经常乘飞机的商务旅客

  • ...nervous fliers.

    紧张的飞机乘客

SYN air traveller, air passenger  
3 N-COUNT (广告)传单 A flyer is a small printed notice which is used to advertise a particular company, service, or event. (广告)传单
