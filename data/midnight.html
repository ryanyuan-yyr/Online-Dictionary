midnight
mɪdnaɪt ˈmɪdˌnaɪt
1 N-UNCOUNT 子夜;午夜;半夜 Midnight is twelve o'clock in the middle of the night. 子夜;午夜;半夜

  • It was well after midnight by the time Anne returned to her apartment.

    安妮回到寓所的时候早就过了午夜。

  • The entrance gates were locked at midnight.

    大门午夜时上锁。

SYN twelve o'clock, middle of the night, dead of night, twelve o'clock at night  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 半夜的;午夜的;子夜的 Midnight is used to describe something which happens or appears at midnight or in the middle of the night. 半夜的;午夜的;子夜的

  • It is totally out of the question to postpone the midnight deadline.

    想要推迟午夜的最后期限是完全不可能的。

  • Well we did have a midnight feast, me and my sister.

    我和姐姐的确在半夜大吃了一顿。

3 PHRASE 开夜车;挑灯夜战;工作(或读书)至深夜 If someone is burning the midnight oil, they are staying up very late in order to study or do some other work. 开夜车;挑灯夜战;工作(或读书)至深夜

  • Chris is asleep after burning the midnight oil trying to finish his article.

    克里斯为了写完文章挑灯夜战，现在已经睡着了。
