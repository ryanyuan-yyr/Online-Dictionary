demonize
diːmənaɪz ˈdimənˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense demonizes, present participle demonizing, past tense , past participle demonized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 demonise
VERB 将…妖魔化;把…想象成魔鬼 If people demonize someone, they convince themselves that that person is evil. 将…妖魔化;把…想象成魔鬼

  • Each side began to demonize the other. [VERB noun]
