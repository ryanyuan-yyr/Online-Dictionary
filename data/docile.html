docile
doʊsaɪl ˈdoʊˌsaɪl dɑːsəl ˈdɑsəl
ADJ-GRADED 温顺的;驯良的;易控制的 A person or animal that is docile is quiet, not aggressive, and easily controlled. 温顺的;驯良的;易控制的

  • ...docile, obedient children.

    温顺、听话的孩子

  • They wanted a low-cost, docile workforce.

    他们想要廉价的、听话的劳动力。

SYN obedient, manageable, compliant, amenable  
docility

  • Her docility had surprised him.

    她的温顺让他吃惊。

docilely

  • She stood there, docilely awaiting my decision.

    她站在那儿，温顺地等着我的决定。
