pester
pestəʳ ˈpɛstər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pesters, present participle pestering, past tense , past participle pestered
VERB 不断打扰；纠缠；烦扰 If you say that someone is pestering you, you mean that they keep asking you to do something, or keep talking to you, and you find this annoying. 不断打扰；纠缠；烦扰 [disapproval] [Also VERB]

  • I thought she'd stop pestering me, but it only seemed to make her worse. [VERB noun]

    我原以为她会停止纠缠我，但结果她似乎是变本加厉了。

  • I know he gets fed up with people pestering him for money. [VERB noun prep.]

    我知道他烦透了那些缠着他要钱的人。

  • ...that creep who's been pestering you to go out with him. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    那个一直缠着要约你出去的讨厌家伙

SYN annoy, worry, bother, disturb
