stunt man
(also stuntman)Word forms: plural stunt men
N-COUNT 特技表演者;(电影中的)特技替身演员 A stunt man is a man whose job is to do dangerous things, either for publicity, or in a film instead of an actor so that the actor does not risk being injured. 特技表演者;(电影中的)特技替身演员

  • The British stunt man Eddie Kidd jumped over the Great Wall of China on a motorcycle.

    英国特技表演者埃迪·基德骑摩托车飞越了中国长城。
