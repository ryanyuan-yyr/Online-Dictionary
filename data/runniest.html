runny
rʌni ˈrʌni
Word forms: comparative runnier, superlative runniest
1 ADJ-GRADED 水分过多的;稀的 Something that is runny is more liquid than usual or than was intended. 水分过多的;稀的

  • Warm the honey until it becomes runny.

    将蜂蜜加热直到它变稀。

  • ...a runny soft cheese.

    松软的奶酪

SYN flowing, liquid, melted, fluid  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 流鼻涕的;流眼泪的 If someone has a runny nose or runny eyes, liquid is flowing from their nose or eyes. 流鼻涕的;流眼泪的

  • Symptoms are streaming eyes, a runny nose, headache and a cough.

    症状包括流泪、流鼻涕、头痛和咳嗽。
