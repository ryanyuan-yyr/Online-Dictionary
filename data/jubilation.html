jubilation
dʒuːbɪleɪʃən ˌdʒubəˈleɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT （成功带来的）欢欣鼓舞，欣喜若狂 Jubilation is a feeling of great happiness and pleasure, because of a success. （成功带来的）欢欣鼓舞，欣喜若狂 [formal]

  • His resignation was greeted by jubilation on the streets of Sofia.

    他辞职下台了，索菲亚大街小巷一片欢腾。

  • ...cheers of jubilation.

    欣喜若狂的欢呼

SYN joy, triumph, celebration, excitement
