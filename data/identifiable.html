identifiable
aɪdentɪfaɪəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可辨认的;可识别的 Something or someone that is identifiable can be recognized. 可辨认的;可识别的 [Also + by/from]

  • In the corridor were four dirty, ragged bundles, just identifiable as human beings. [+ as]

    走廊里有4团脏兮兮、破破烂烂的东西，依稀可辨是人。

  • Stan Dean, easily identifiable by his oddly-shaped hat, sat in a doorway.

    斯坦·迪安坐在门口，他那顶奇形怪状的帽子一下子就让人认出他来。

  • ...if there is a clearly identifiable cause of stress in your life and there is nothing you can do to get rid of it.

    如果你生活中有清晰可辨的压力来源，而你对此却无能为力

SYN recognizable, noticeable, known, unmistakable
