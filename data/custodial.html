custodial
kʌstoʊdiəl kəsˈtoʊdiəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 监禁的;拘留的 Custodial means relating to keeping people in prison. 监禁的;拘留的 [mainly British, formal]

  • If he is caught again he will be given a custodial sentence.

    如果他再次被捕，将会被处以监禁。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 有监护(或监管)权的 If a child's parents are divorced or separated, the custodial parent is the parent who has custody of the child. 有监护(或监管)权的 [law]
