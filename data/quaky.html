quake
kweɪk kweɪk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense quakes, present participle quaking, past tense , past participle quaked
1 N-COUNT 同earthquake A quake is the same as an earthquake . 同earthquake

  • The quake destroyed mud buildings in many remote villages.

    这次地震摧毁了许多偏远乡村的土坯房。

SYN earthquake, tremor, shock  
2 VERB (通常指因恐惧)发抖，颤抖，哆嗦 If you quake, you shake, usually because you are very afraid. (通常指因恐惧)发抖，颤抖，哆嗦

  • I just stood there quaking with fear. [VERB + with]

    我只是站在那儿，吓得直哆嗦。

  • Her shoulders quaked. [VERB]

    她的双肩瑟瑟发抖。

SYN shake, tremble, quiver, move  
3 PHRASE 十分紧张;非常害怕；吓得发抖 If you are quaking in your boots or quaking in your shoes, you feel very nervous or afraid, and may be feeling slightly weak as a result. 十分紧张;非常害怕；吓得发抖

  • If you stand up straight you'll give an impression of self-confidence even if you're quaking in your boots.

    要是你站得笔挺，就算实际上怕得要命，你都会给人留下非常自信的印象。
