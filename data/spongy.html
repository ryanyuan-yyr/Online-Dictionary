spongy
spʌndʒi ˈspʌndʒi
ADJ-GRADED 海绵般松软而有弹性的 Something that is spongy is soft and can be pressed in, like a sponge. 海绵般松软而有弹性的

  • We liked the bike's spongy handgrip for keeping our hands away from cold metal.

    我们喜欢这辆自行车的软把手，有了它，我们的双手就不用再接触冰冷的金属了。

  • The earth was spongy from rain.

    地面因下雨而变得松软。

SYN porous, light, absorbent, springy
