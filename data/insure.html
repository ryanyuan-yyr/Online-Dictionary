insure
ɪnʃʊəʳ ɪnˈʃʊə ɪnˈʃʊr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense insures, present participle insuring, past tense , past participle insured
1 VERB 为…投保;给…上保险 If you insure yourself or your property, you pay money to an insurance company so that, if you become ill or if your property is damaged or stolen, the company will pay you a sum of money. 为…投保;给…上保险 [Also VERB n + for]

  • For protection against unforeseen emergencies, you insure your house, your furnishings and your car. [VERB noun]

    为了防范不可预见的紧急情况,人们给房子、家具和汽车上保险。

  • Think carefully before you insure against accident, sickness and redundancy. [V + against/for]

    在为意外事故、疾病与失业投保之前一定要考虑清楚。

  • We automatically insure your belongings against fire and theft. [VERB noun + against]

    我们自动为您的财产上失窃险和火灾险。

SYN assure, cover, protect, guarantee  
2 VERB 使采取预防措施 If you insure yourself against something unpleasant that might happen in the future, you do something to protect yourself in case it happens, or to prevent it happening. 使采取预防措施

  • He insured himself against failure by treating only people he was sure he could cure. [V pron-refl + against]

    他仅仅治疗有把握治愈的病人，以使自己免于失败。

  • All the electronics in the world cannot insure against accidents, though. [VERB + against]

    世上没有哪种电子器材能完全避免意外事故的发生。

3 See also: ensure
