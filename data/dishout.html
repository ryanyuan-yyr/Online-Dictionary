dish out
1 PHRASAL VERB 分发 If you dish out something, you distribute it among a number of people. 分发 [informal]

  • Doctors, not pharmacists, are responsible for dishing out drugs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    负责开药的是医生，而不是药剂师。

  • The council wants to dish the money out to specific projects. [VERB noun PREP.]

    理事会想把钱分配到具体的工程。

SYN distribute, assign, allocate, designate  
2 PHRASAL VERB 给予(批评、惩罚等) If someone dishes out criticism or punishment, they give it to someone. 给予(批评、惩罚等) [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Linzi is well qualified to dish out advice. [VERB PREP. noun]

    林齐完全有资格给出意见。

3 PHRASAL VERB 大加批评;严厉惩罚 If someone dishes it out to you, they strongly criticize or punish you. 大加批评;严厉惩罚 [informal]

  • He's a big man and he's prepared to dish it out if he has to. [V it P]

    他是个大人物，迫不得已的话，他也会站出来谴责一番的。

4 PHRASAL VERB 布(菜);把(食物)装盘子上菜;从大盘中分（菜） If you dish out food, you serve it to people at the beginning of each course of a meal. 布(菜);把(食物)装盘子上菜;从大盘中分（菜） [informal]

  • Here in the dining hall the cooks dish out chicken à la king. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在餐厅这儿厨师们把皇家奶油鸡装盘。
