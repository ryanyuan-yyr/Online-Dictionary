handbook
hændbʊk ˈhændˌbʊk
Word forms: plural handbooks
N-COUNT 手册;说明书 A handbook is a book that gives you advice and instructions about a particular subject, tool, or machine. 手册;说明书

  • If you have not kept a pet parrot before, it would be wise to purchase a handbook on the subject.

    如果以前没有养过鹦鹉，最好买一本相关的饲养手册。

  • ...the AA Members' Handbook.

    《汽车协会会员手册》

SYN guidebook, guide, manual, instruction book
