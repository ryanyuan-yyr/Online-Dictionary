steel
stiːl stil
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense steels, present participle steeling, past tense , past participle steeled
1 N-MASS [oft NOUN n] 钢;钢铁 Steel is a very strong metal which is made mainly from iron. Steel is used for making many things, for example bridges, buildings, vehicles, and cutlery. 钢;钢铁

  • ...steel pipes.

    钢管

  • ...the iron and steel industry.

    钢铁工业

  • ...a fall in demand for cement, bricks, steel and glass.

    对水泥、砖、钢铁和玻璃的需求下滑

  • The front wall is made of corrugated steel.

    前面的墙是用瓦楞钢板做的。

2See also:stainless steel 
3 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 钢铁工业;钢铁生产 Steel is used to refer to the industry that produces steel and items made of steel. 钢铁工业;钢铁生产

  • ...a three-month study of European steel.

    对欧洲钢铁工业为期 3 个月的考察研究

  • The company has interests in steel and other products.

    公司在钢铁和其他产品领域都有投资。

4 VERB 准备应付(不愉快的事) If you steel yourself, you prepare to deal with something unpleasant. 准备应付(不愉快的事)

  • Those involved are steeling themselves for the coming battle. [V pron-refl + for/against]

    那些参与者正准备迎接即将到来的战斗。

  • I was steeling myself to call round when Simon arrived. [VERB pronoun-reflexive to-infinitive]

    我正下决心准备去拜访时，西蒙来了。
