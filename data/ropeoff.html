rope off
PHRASAL VERB 用绳子将(某个地方)围起来 If you rope off an area, you tie ropes between posts all around its edges so that people cannot enter it without permission. 用绳子将(某个地方)围起来 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • You should rope off a big field and sell tickets. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你应该用绳子围起一大片地方来卖票。

  • ...a large roped-off area. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    用绳子围起来的大片区域
