rocker
rɒkəʳ ˈrɑkər
Word forms: plural rockers
1 N-COUNT 摇椅 A rocker is a chair that is built on two curved pieces of wood so that you can rock yourself backwards and forwards while you are sitting in it. 摇椅 [mainly US]

  • Claire spent the morning in a wicker rocker on the screened porch with a book.

    克莱尔整个早上都在装着纱窗的走廊里坐在柳条摇椅上看书。

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 rocking chair
2 N-COUNT 摇滚歌手;摇滚音乐家 A rocker is someone who performs rock music. 摇滚歌手;摇滚音乐家

  • ...American rockers Guns 'N' Roses.

    美国摇滚乐队“枪炮玫瑰”
