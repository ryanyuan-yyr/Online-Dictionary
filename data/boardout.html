board out
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 把…送到别人家膳宿 If someone in your care is boarded out, they are sent to stay with someone else. 把…送到别人家膳宿 [British]

  • As a child in the care of a local authority he had been boarded out with a farmer in Shropshire. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他童年时由地方政府照管，曾被送到什罗普郡的一户农民家里寄宿。
