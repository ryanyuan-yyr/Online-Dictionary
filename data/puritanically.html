puritanical
pjʊərɪtænɪkəl ˌpjʊrɪˈtænɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 有严格道德标准的；道学先生的 If you describe someone as puritanical, you mean that they have very strict moral principles, and often try to make other people behave in a more moral way. 有严格道德标准的；道学先生的 [disapproval]

  • ...puritanical fathers.

    古板严厉的父亲

  • He has a puritanical attitude towards sex.

    他在性问题上主张克制，反对纵欲。

SYN strict, forbidding, puritan, stuffy
