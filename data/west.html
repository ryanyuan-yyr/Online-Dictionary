west
west wɛst
(also West)
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] 西方;西边 The west is the direction which you look towards in the evening in order to see the sun set. 西方;西边

  • I pushed on towards Flagstaff, a hundred miles to the west.

    我继续西行，向100英里外的弗拉格斯塔夫进发。

  • The sun crosses the sky from east to west.

    太阳自东向西穿过天空。

2 N-SING [ADJ n] (某个地方、国家或地区的)西部 The west of a place, country, or region is the part of it which is in the west. (某个地方、国家或地区的)西部

  • ...physicists working at Bristol University in the west of England. [+ of]

    在英格兰西部的布里斯托尔大学工作的物理学家们

3 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向西;朝西 If you go west, you travel towards the west. 向西;朝西

  • We are going West to California. [+ to]

    我们西行前往加利福尼亚。

4 ADV …以西 Something that is west of a place is positioned to the west of it. …以西

  • ...their home town of Paisley, several miles west of Glasgow. [+ of]

    他们的家乡佩斯利就在格拉斯哥以西几英里处

5 ADJ [ADJ n] 西部的;西边的 The west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the west. 西部的;西边的

  • ...a small island off the west coast of South Korea.

    韩国西海岸外的小岛

6 ADJ [ADJ n] (用于国家、州和地区的名称中)西部的 West is used in the names of some countries, states, and regions in the west of a larger area. (用于国家、州和地区的名称中)西部的

  • Mark has been working in West Africa for about six months.

    马克已在西非工作了约6个月。

  • ...his West London home.

    他在伦敦西部的家

  • ...Charleston, West Virginia.

    西弗吉尼亚州查尔斯顿市

7 ADJ (风)来自西方的 A west wind blows from the west. (风)来自西方的
8 N-SING 西方国家(指美国、加拿大和西欧、北欧、南欧的国家) The West is used to refer to the United States, Canada, and the countries of Western, Northern, and Southern Europe. 西方国家(指美国、加拿大和西欧、北欧、南欧的国家)

  • ...relations between Iran and the West.

    伊朗和西方国家的关系
