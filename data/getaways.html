getaway
getəweɪ ˈɡɛtəweɪ ˈgɛtəˌweɪ
(also get-away)Word forms: plural getaways
1 N-COUNT [usually singular, oft NOUN n] （尤指犯罪后或为逃避某人的）逃跑，逃走，离开 If someone makes a getaway, they leave a place quickly, especially after committing a crime or when trying to avoid someone. （尤指犯罪后或为逃避某人的）逃跑，逃走，离开

  • They made their getaway along a pavement on a stolen motorcycle.

    他们骑上偷来的摩托车沿着人行道逃走了。

  • ...the burglar's getaway car.

    窃贼逃跑时所驾驶的汽车

SYN escape, break, flight, break-out  
2 N-COUNT 短假 A getaway is a short holiday somewhere. 短假 [informal]

  • Weekend tours are ideal for families who want a short getaway.

    周末旅行很适合那些想短期休假的家庭。
