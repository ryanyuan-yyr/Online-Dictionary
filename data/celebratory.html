celebratory
seləbreɪtəri selɪbrətɔːri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 表示庆祝的;庆祝性的 A celebratory meal, drink, or other activity takes place to celebrate something such as a birthday, anniversary, or victory. 表示庆祝的;庆祝性的

  • That night she, Nicholson and the crew had a celebratory dinner.

    那天晚上，她、尼科尔森和全体工作人员吃了一顿庆功宴。
