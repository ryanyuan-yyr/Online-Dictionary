heaven-sent
(also heaven sent)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 适时的;天赐的 You use heaven-sent to describe something such as an opportunity which is unexpected, but which is very welcome because it occurs at just the right time. 适时的;天赐的

  • It will be a heaven-sent opportunity to prove himself.

    这将是他证明自己的天赐良机。

SYN fortunate, providential, welcome, blessed
