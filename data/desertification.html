desertification
dɪzɜːʳtɪfɪkeɪʃən dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən dɪˌzɜrtəfɪˈkeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT （土地的）沙漠化，荒漠化 Desertification is the process by which a piece of land becomes dry, empty, and unsuitable for growing trees or crops on. （土地的）沙漠化，荒漠化

  • A third of Africa is under threat of desertification.

    非洲有三分之一的土地面临荒漠化的威胁。
