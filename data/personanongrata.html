persona non grata pəʳsoʊnə nɒn grɑːtə pɜːˈsəʊnə nɒn ˈɡrɑːtə pərˈsoʊnə noʊn ˈgrɑtə
Word forms: plural personae non gratae
PHRASE 不受欢迎的人 If someone becomes or is declared persona non grata, they become unwelcome or unacceptable because of something they have said or done. 不受欢迎的人

  • The government has declared the French ambassador persona non grata and ordered him to leave the country.

    政府已经宣布法国大使为不受欢迎的人，勒令其离境。
