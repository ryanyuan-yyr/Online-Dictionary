thrall
θrɔːl θrɔl
N-UNCOUNT 控制;奴役;束缚 If you say that someone is in thrall to a person or thing, you mean that they are completely in their power or are greatly influenced by them. 控制;奴役;束缚 [formal]

  • He is not in thrall to the media. [+ to]

    他不受制于媒体。

  • Tomorrow's children will be even more in the thrall of the silicon chip. [+ of]

    将来硅片对孩子们的影响将进一步加深。

SYN slavery, bondage, servitude, enslavement
