voluble
vɒljʊbəl ˈvɑljəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 健谈的;滔滔不绝的 If you say that someone is voluble, you mean that they talk a lot with great energy and enthusiasm. 健谈的;滔滔不绝的 [formal]

  • She was voluble with excitement.

    她由于激动变得很健谈。

  • Bert is a voluble, gregarious man.

    伯特是个能说会道、善于交际的人。

SYN talkative, garrulous, loquacious, forthcoming  
volubly

  • In the next booth along he could see an elderly lady, talking volubly.

    在隔壁的电话亭里，他看见一个上了年纪的妇女在喋喋不休地说着。
