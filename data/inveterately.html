inveterate
ɪnvetərət ɪnˈvɛtərɪt
ADJ [ADJ n] 积习不改的;上瘾的;根深蒂固的 If you describe someone as, for example, an inveterate liar or smoker, you mean that they have lied or smoked for a long time and are not likely to stop doing it. 积习不改的;上瘾的;根深蒂固的

  • ...an inveterate gambler.

    嗜赌成性的赌徒

  • ...the inveterate laziness of these boys.

    这些男孩子懒惰成性

SYN deep-rooted, entrenched, ingrained, deep-seated
