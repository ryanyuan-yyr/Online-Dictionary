crystalline
krɪstəlaɪn ˈkrɪstəlɪn ˈkrɪstəlˌaɪn ˈkrɪstəlˌin
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 结晶的;晶状的;含水晶的 A crystalline substance is in the form of crystals or contains crystals. 结晶的;晶状的;含水晶的

  • Diamond is the crystalline form of the element carbon.

    钻石是碳元素的晶体结构。

  • ...hard, crystalline rock.

    坚硬的水晶石

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 清澈的;晶莹的 Crystalline means clear or bright. 清澈的;晶莹的 [literary]

  • ...a huge plain dotted with crystalline lakes.

    辽阔的平原上遍布着清澈的湖泊
