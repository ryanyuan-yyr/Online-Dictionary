disposable
dɪspoʊzəbəl dɪˈspoʊzəbəl
Word forms: plural disposables
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 用后即可丢弃的;一次性的 A disposable product is designed to be thrown away after it has been used. 用后即可丢弃的;一次性的

  • ...disposable nappies suitable for babies up to 8lb.

    适合8磅以下婴儿使用的一次性尿布

  • He shaved himself with a disposable razor.

    他用一次性刀片刮脸。

SYN throwaway, paper, plastic, one-use  
Disposable products can be referred to as disposables

  • It's estimated that around 80 per cent of babies wear disposables.

    据估计约 80% 的婴儿使用一次性尿布。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (收入)可自由支配的、扣除税款后余下的 Your disposable income is the amount of income you have left after you have paid income tax and social security charges. (收入)可自由支配的、扣除税款后余下的

  • Gerald had little disposable income.

    杰拉尔德的可支配收入很少。

  • The nation's disposable earnings in 1987 amounted to £86 billion.

    1987 年国民可自由支配收入达到了860亿英镑。

SYN available, expendable, free for use, consumable
