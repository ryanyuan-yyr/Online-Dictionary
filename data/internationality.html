international
ɪntəʳnæʃənəl ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ˌɪntərˌnæʃəˈnæl ˌɪntərˌnæʃəˈnɑl
Word forms: plural internationals
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 国际的;国际上的 International means between or involving different countries. 国际的;国际上的

  • ...an international agreement against exporting arms to that country.

    禁止向该国出口武器的国际协定

  • ...Kuwait International Airport.

    科威特国际机场

  • ...emergency aid from the international community.

    国际社会提供的紧急援助

SYN global, world, worldwide, universal  
internationally

  • There are only two internationally recognised certificates in Teaching English as a Foreign Language.

    “作为外语的英语教学”只有两种国际上认可的证书。

  • I am one of the few young women who has made it as a writer financially and internationally.

    我是为数不多的既获得财富又享有国际知名度的年轻女作家之一。

2 N-COUNT 国际比赛 In sport, an international is a game that is played between teams representing two different countries. 国际比赛 [British]

  • ...the midweek international against England.

    周三对抗英格兰的国际比赛

3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 国际比赛选手;国家队队员 An international is a member of a country's sports team. 国际比赛选手;国家队队员 [British]

  • ...a former England international.

    前英格兰国手
