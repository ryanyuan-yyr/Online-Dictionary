pallid
pælɪd ˈpælɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 苍白的；无血色的；暗淡的 Someone or something that is pallid is pale in an unattractive or unnatural way. 苍白的；无血色的；暗淡的

  • ...helpless grief on pallid faces.

    苍白面孔上无助的忧伤

  • ...pallid grey vapour.

    暗淡的灰白色水蒸气

2 ADJ-GRADED （演出、书籍等）平淡的，乏味的，了无生气的 You can describe something such as a performance or book as pallid if it is weak or not at all exciting. （演出、书籍等）平淡的，乏味的，了无生气的

  • ...a pallid account of the future of transport.

    对交通运输前景的平淡描述

  • ...a pallid unsuccessful romance.

    一段失败的平淡恋情
