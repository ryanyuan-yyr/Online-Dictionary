deflection
dɪflekʃən diˈflɛkʃən dɪˈflɛkʃən
Word forms: plural deflections
1 N-VAR 偏斜;偏转 The deflection of something means making it change direction. 偏斜;偏转 [technical]

  • ...the deflection of light as it passes through the slits in the grating.

    光线通过光栅狭缝时的偏转

SYN deviation, bending, veering, swerving  
2 N-COUNT (体育运动中球击中某物后的)转向，变向 In sport, the deflection of a ball, kick, or shot is when the ball hits an object and then travels in a different direction. (体育运动中球击中某物后的)转向，变向
