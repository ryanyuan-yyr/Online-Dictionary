overflow
oʊvəʳfloʊ oʊvəʳfloʊ ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ ˈoʊvərˌfloʊ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense overflows, present participle overflowing, past tense , past participle overflowed
The verb is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'fləʊ/. The noun is pronounced /'əʊvəfləʊ/. 动词读作 /ˌəʊvə'fləʊ/。名词读作 /'əʊvəfləʊ/。
1 VERB [no passive] 漫出；溢出；泛滥 If a liquid or a river overflows, it flows over the edges of the container or place it is in. 漫出；溢出；泛滥

  • Pour in some of the syrup, but not all of it, as it will probably overflow. [VERB]

    倒一些糖浆进去，但别全倒，因为可能会溢出来。

  • Rivers and streams have overflowed their banks in countless places. [VERB noun]

    在许多地方，河流与小溪都已经决堤。

2 VERB [usually cont] 充满；挤满；装满 If a place or container is overflowing with people or things, it is too full of them. 充满；挤满；装满

  • The great hall was overflowing with people. [VERB + with]

    大礼堂里挤满了人。

  • Jails and temporary detention camps are overflowing. [VERB]

    监狱和临时拘留所里人满为患。

  • He emptied a few overflowing ashtrays. [VERB-ing]

    他将几只满满的烟灰缸倒干净。

3 VERB 洋溢着（情感）；（情感）溢于言表 If someone is overflowing with a feeling or if the feeling overflows, the person is experiencing it very strongly and shows this in their behaviour. 洋溢着（情感）；（情感）溢于言表

  • Kenneth overflowed with friendliness and hospitality. [VERB with noun]

    肯尼思满怀友善好客之情。

  • Ridley's anger finally overflowed. [VERB]

    里德利终于按捺不住满腔的怒火。

4 N-COUNT （过多而）容纳不下的人（或东西） The overflow is the extra people or things that something cannot contain or deal with because it is not large enough. （过多而）容纳不下的人（或东西）

  • Tents have been set up next to hospitals to handle the overflow.

    医院旁搭起了帐篷以安置容纳不下的人员。

  • The loch's overflow cascades into the waterfalls of a Japanese water garden.

    溢出的湖水倾泻而下，形成了一座日式水景花园的瀑布。

SYN surplus, extra, excess, overspill  
5 N-COUNT 溢流口；溢流管 An overflow is a hole or pipe through which liquid can flow out of a container when it gets too full. 溢流口；溢流管

  • ...the overflow pipe.

    溢流管

6 PHRASE 将要溢出 If a place or container is filled to overflowing, it is so full of people or things that no more can fit in. 将要溢出

  • The kitchen garden was full to overflowing with fresh vegetables.

    家庭菜园里种满了新鲜蔬菜。

  • He was one of the few teachers who always filled the lecture room to overflowing.

    他是为数不多的课堂场场爆满的老师之一。
