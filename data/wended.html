wend
wend wɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense wends, present participle wending, past tense , past participle wended
PHRASE (尤指缓慢、悠闲或小心地)走，行，去，往 If you wend your way in a particular direction, you walk, especially slowly, casually, or carefully, in that direction. (尤指缓慢、悠闲或小心地)走，行，去，往 [literary]

  • Sleepy-eyed commuters were wending their way to work.

    睡眼惺忪的通勤族拖着沉重的步子上班去。

SYN go, move, travel, progress
