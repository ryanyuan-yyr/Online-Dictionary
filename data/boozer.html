boozer
buːzəʳ ˈbuzər
Word forms: plural boozers
1 N-COUNT 酒馆 A boozer is a pub. 酒馆 [British, informal]

  • They're in the boozer most nights.

    他们大多数晚上都在酒馆里呆着。

2 N-COUNT 酒鬼；豪饮者 A boozer is a person who drinks a lot of alcohol. 酒鬼；豪饮者 [informal]

  • I thought he was a bit of a boozer.

    我原以为他是个酒鬼。

SYN drinker, toper, drunk, soak [slang]
