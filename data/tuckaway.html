tuck away
1 PHRASAL VERB 妥善收藏;安全保管 If you tuck away something such as money, you store it in a safe place. 妥善收藏;安全保管

  • The extra income has meant Phillippa can tuck away the rent. [VERB PREP. noun]

    额外的收入意味着菲莉帕可以把房租的钱存起来。

  • I tucked the box away in the linen drawer. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我把盒子藏在放棉织品的抽屉里。

2 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 把…隐藏;把…隐匿 If someone or something is tucked away, they are well hidden in a quiet place where very few people go. 把…隐藏;把…隐匿

  • We were tucked away in a secluded corner of the room. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    我们安全地藏在房间一个隐蔽的角落里。

  • His home in Bexley is tucked away in a miniature forest. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他在贝克斯利的住宅坐落在一片小森林中。
