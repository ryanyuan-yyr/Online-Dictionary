renunciation
rɪnʌnsieɪʃən rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural renunciations
1 N-UNCOUNT （对信仰或行为方式的）放弃，抛弃 The renunciation of a belief or a way of behaving is the public declaration that you reject it and have decided to stop having that belief or behaving in that way. （对信仰或行为方式的）放弃，抛弃

  • The talks were dependent on a renunciation of terrorism. [+ of]

    会谈以放弃恐怖主义为前提。

2 N-UNCOUNT (对声明、官衔等的)正式放弃 The renunciation of a claim, title, or privilege is the act of officially giving it up. (对声明、官衔等的)正式放弃

  • ...the renunciation of territory in the Mediterranean. [+ of]

    对地中海领土的放弃

SYN giving up, resignation, surrender, waiver  
3 N-UNCOUNT (因道德或宗教原因)克己，自我克制 Renunciation is the act of not allowing yourself certain pleasures for moral or religious reasons. (因道德或宗教原因)克己，自我克制

  • Gandhi exemplified the virtues of renunciation, asceticism and restraint.

    甘地体现了克己、禁欲和自我约束的美德。
