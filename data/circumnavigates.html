circumnavigate
sɜːʳkəmnævɪgeɪt ˌsɜrkəmˈnævəˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense circumnavigates, present participle circumnavigating, past tense , past participle circumnavigated
VERB 环绕(世界或岛屿)航行 If someone circumnavigates the world or an island, they sail all the way around it. 环绕(世界或岛屿)航行 [formal]

  • For this year at least, our race to circumnavigate the globe in less than 80 days is over. [VERB noun]

    至少今年，我们80天内环游地球一周的比赛已经结束了。

circumnavigation

  • He married in Fiji during a two-year circumnavigation of the globe in his yacht, Surma.

    他在驾驶“祖尔马”号游艇进行为期两年的环球航行途中，在斐济结了婚。
