bog-standard
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 无新意的；无特色的；普通的 If you describe something as bog-standard you mean that is an ordinary example of its kind, with no exciting or interesting features. 无新意的；无特色的；普通的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • 'The Bodyguard' is a fairly bog-standard thriller.

    《保镖》是一部毫无新意的惊险电影。
