leather
leðəʳ ˈlɛðər
Word forms: plural leathers
1 N-MASS 皮革 Leather is treated animal skin which is used for making shoes, clothes, bags, and furniture. 皮革

  • He wore a leather jacket and dark trousers.

    他身穿皮夹克和深色裤子。

  • ...an impressive range of upholstered furniture, in a choice of fabrics and leathers.

    琳琅满目的覆面家具，有多种布料和皮革可供选择

2 N-PLURAL （尤指骑摩托车者穿的）皮外套，皮衣 Leathers are leather clothes such as jackets and trousers, especially those worn by motorcyclists. （尤指骑摩托车者穿的）皮外套，皮衣 [mainly British]

  • ...a couple of youths in motorcyclists' black leathers.

    两个穿黑色机车皮衣的年轻人
