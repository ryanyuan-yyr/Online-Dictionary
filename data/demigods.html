demigod
demigɒd ˈdɛmɪˌgɑd
Word forms: plural demigods
1 N-COUNT (神话中的)次神;(尤指)半神半人 In mythology, a demigod is a less important god, especially one who is half god and half human. (神话中的)次神;(尤指)半神半人
2 N-COUNT 被神化的人，像神一样受人崇拜的人(如政治家、作家、音乐家等) If you describe a famous or important person such as a politician, writer, or musician as a demigod, you mean that you disapprove of the way in which people admire them and treat them like a god. 被神化的人，像神一样受人崇拜的人(如政治家、作家、音乐家等) [disapproval]
