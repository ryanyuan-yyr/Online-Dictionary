retrain
riːtreɪn riːˈtreɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense retrains, present participle retraining, past tense , past participle retrained
V-ERG （尤指为再就业）接受再培训，对…再培训 If you retrain, or if someone retrains you, you learn new skills, especially in order to get a new job. （尤指为再就业）接受再培训，对…再培训

  • Why not retrain for a job which will make you happier?. [VERB]

    为什么不接受新的培训，找一份让你更快乐的工作？

  • Union leaders have called upon the government to help retrain workers. [VERB noun]

    工会领导人已经呼吁政府帮助对工人进行再培训。
