dame
deɪm deɪm
Word forms: plural dames
1 N-TITLE 女爵士 Dame is a title given to a woman as a special honour because of important service or work that she has done. 女爵士 [British]

  • ...Dame Judi Dench.

    朱迪·丹奇女爵士

2 N-COUNT 女人(可能具冒犯意味) A dame is a woman. This use could cause offence. 女人(可能具冒犯意味) [US, informal, old-fashioned]

  • Who does that dame think she is?

    那女人以为自己是谁？
