sugar
ʃʊgəʳ ˈʃʊgər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural sugars, present participle sugaring, past tense , past participle sugared
1 N-UNCOUNT 食糖 Sugar is a sweet substance that is used to make food and drinks sweet. It is usually in the form of small white or brown crystals. 食糖

  • ...bags of sugar.

    几袋食糖

  • Ice cream is high in fat and sugar.

    冰激凌富含脂肪和糖。

2See also:caster sugar  confectioners’ sugar  demerara sugar  granulated sugar  icing sugar 
3 N-COUNT 一匙糖;一块方糖 If someone has one sugar in their tea or coffee, they have one small spoon of sugar or one sugar lump in it. 一匙糖;一块方糖

  • How many sugars do you take?

    你加几勺糖？

  • ...a mug of tea with two sugars.

    一杯加了两块方糖的茶

4 VERB 给…加糖;在…中加糖 If you sugar food or drink, you add sugar to it. 给…加糖;在…中加糖

  • He sat down and sugared and stirred his coffee. [VERB noun]

    他坐下来，在咖啡中加糖并搅拌。

5 N-COUNT [usually plural] (食物中所含的)糖，糖分 Sugars are substances that occur naturally in food. When you eat them, the body converts them into energy. (食物中所含的)糖，糖分

  • Plants produce sugars and starch to provide themselves with energy.

    植物产生糖和淀粉来为自身提供能量。

  • ...the natural sugars found in grape juice.

    葡萄汁中所含的天然糖分 

6 N-VOC 宝贝;亲爱的;心肝儿 If one person knows another person very well and likes them a lot, they sometimes call them sugar . 宝贝;亲爱的;心肝儿 [informal, feelings]

  • I know how to make you feel better, sugar. I'll tell you a story.

    宝贝，我知道怎么能让你感觉好些，我给你讲个故事。
