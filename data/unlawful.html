unlawful
ʌnlɔːfʊl ʌnˈlɔːfʊl ʌnˈlɔfəl
ADJ 不合法的；非法的；违法的 If something is unlawful, the law does not allow you to do it. 不合法的；非法的；违法的 [formal]

  • ...employees who believe their dismissal was unlawful.

    认为将他们解雇是违法行为的雇员

  • A pushed-in window indicated unlawful entry.

    往里推开的窗户表明有人非法入内。

SYN illegal, criminal, illicit, banned  
unlawfully

  • ...the councils' assertion that the government acted unlawfully in imposing the restrictions.
