sensational
senseɪʃənəl sɛnˈseɪʃənəl sɛnˈseɪʃənəl
1 ADJ-GRADED (结果、事件、局势)轰动性的，引起哗然的 A sensational result, event, or situation is so remarkable that it causes great excitement and interest. (结果、事件、局势)轰动性的，引起哗然的

  • The world champions suffered a sensational defeat.

    世界冠军遭遇惨败，引起了一片哗然。

SYN amazing, dramatic, thrilling, revealing  
sensationally

  • The rape trial was sensationally halted yesterday.

    这起强奸案的审理昨天在群情激愤中暂停了。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (故事、报道)耸人听闻的，哗众取宠的 You can describe stories or reports as sensational if you disapprove of them because they present facts in a way that is intended to cause feelings of shock, anger, or excitement. (故事、报道)耸人听闻的，哗众取宠的 [disapproval]

  • ...sensational tabloid newspaper reports.

    哗众取宠的小报报道

SYN shocking, scandalous, exciting, yellowof the press  
3 ADJ 极好的;绝妙的 You can describe something as sensational when you think that it is extremely good. 极好的;绝妙的

  • Her voice is sensational.

    她的嗓音非常动听。

  • Experts agreed that this was a truly sensational performance.

    专家们一致认为这的确是一场精彩绝伦的演出。

SYN excellent, brilliant, superb, mean [slang]  
sensationally

  • ...sensationally good food.

    非常好吃的食物
