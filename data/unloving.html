unloving
ʌnlʌvɪŋ ʌnˈlʌvɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 无情的；无爱心的 If you describe a person as unloving, you believe that they do not love, or show love to, the people they ought to love. 无情的；无爱心的

  • The overworked, overextended parent may be seen as unloving, but may simply be exhausted.

    那位操劳过度的家长看起来好像并不疼爱孩子，但实际上可能是太累的缘故。
