non-intervention
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 nonintervention
N-UNCOUNT 不干涉;不介入 Non-intervention is the practice or policy of not becoming involved in a dispute or disagreement between other people and of not helping either side. 不干涉;不介入

  • Generally, I think the policy of non-intervention is the correct one.

    总的来说，我认为不干涉政策是正确选择。
