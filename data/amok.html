amok
əmʌk əmɒk əˈmʌk əˈmɒk əˈmʌk əˈmɑk
PHRASE （人或动物）发狂，狂暴，疯狂 If a person or animal runs amok, they behave in a violent and uncontrolled way. （人或动物）发狂，狂暴，疯狂

  • A soldier was arrested after running amok with a vehicle through Berlin.

    一名士兵在疯狂飙车穿越柏林后被逮捕。

SYN go mad, go wild, turn violent, go berserk
