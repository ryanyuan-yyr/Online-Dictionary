guilder
gɪldəʳ ˈɡɪldə ˈgɪldər
Word forms: plural guilders
N-COUNT [num NOUN] 荷兰盾（荷兰曾使用的货币单位，2002年为欧元所取代） A guilder was a unit of money that was used in the Netherlands. In 2002 it was replaced by the euro. 荷兰盾（荷兰曾使用的货币单位，2002年为欧元所取代）
The guilder was also used to refer to the Dutch currency system.荷兰货币体系

  • During the turmoil in the foreign-exchange markets the guilder remained strong.

    荷兰盾在外汇交易市场的动荡中保持坚挺。
