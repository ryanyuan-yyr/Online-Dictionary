grumpy
grʌmpi ˈgrʌmpi
Word forms: comparative grumpier, superlative grumpiest
ADJ-GRADED 脾气坏的；郁郁寡欢的 If you say that someone is grumpy, you mean that they are bad-tempered and miserable. 脾气坏的；郁郁寡欢的

  • Some folk think I'm a grumpy old man.

    有些人认为我是个性情暴躁的老头。

SYN irritable, cross, bad-tempered, grumbling  
grumpily

  • 'I know, I know,' said Ken, grumpily, without looking up.

    “我知道，我知道，”肯气呼呼地说，连头都没有抬。
