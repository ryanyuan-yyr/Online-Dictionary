excusable
ɪkskjuːzəbəl ɛkˈskjuzəbəl ɪkˈskjuzəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可原谅的；可谅解的 If you say that someone's wrong words or actions are excusable, you mean that they can be understood and forgiven. 可原谅的；可谅解的

  • I then realised that he had made a simple but excusable historical mistake.

    然后我认识到他犯了一个简单但是可以原谅的历史性错误。

SYN forgivable, understandable, justifiable, permissible
