obstruction
ɒbstrʌkʃən əbˈstrʌkʃən
Word forms: plural obstructions
1 N-COUNT 路障；障碍（物） An obstruction is something that blocks a road or path. 路障；障碍（物）

  • John was irritated by drivers parking near his house and causing an obstruction.

    约翰对那些在他家附近泊车堵住通道的司机感到恼火。

SYN obstacle, bar, block, difficulty  
2 N-VAR （体内的）梗阻 An obstruction is something that blocks a passage in your body. （体内的）梗阻

  • The boy was suffering from a bowel obstruction and he died.

    这个男孩因患肠梗阻死去了。

SYN blockage, stoppage, occlusion  
3 N-UNCOUNT （通常指对生意、法律或政府的）阻挠，妨碍 Obstruction is the act of deliberately delaying something or preventing something from happening, usually in business, law, or government. （通常指对生意、法律或政府的）阻挠，妨碍

  • Mr Guest refused to let them in and now faces a criminal charge of obstruction.

    格斯特先生因为将他们拒之门外，现在面临妨碍公务的刑事指控。
