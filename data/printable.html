printable
prɪntəbəl ˈprɪntəbəl ˈprɪntəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可印刷的；适于出版的；适于刊印的 If you say that someone's words or remarks are not printable, you mean that they are likely to offend people, and are therefore not suitable to be repeated in writing or speech. 可印刷的；适于出版的；适于刊印的 [journalism]

  • His team-mates opened hotel windows, shouting 'Jump!' and somewhat less printable banter.

    他的队友打开了酒店的窗户，大叫着“跳！”，还开了一些上不了台面的玩笑。
