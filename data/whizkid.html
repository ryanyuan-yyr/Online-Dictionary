whiz-kid
(also whiz kid)Word forms: whiz-kids
N-COUNT [usu with supp] 神童If you refer to a young person as a whiz-kid, you mean that they have achieved success at a young age because they are very clever and very good at something, especially making money. 神童 [informal]

  • ...a computer whiz-kid.

    ...一个计算机神童。

  • ...a whiz-kid physics student.

    ...一名物理神童学生。
