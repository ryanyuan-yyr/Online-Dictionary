invoice
ɪnvɔɪs ˈɪnˌvɔɪs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense invoices, present participle invoicing, past tense , past participle invoiced
1 N-COUNT 发货单;发票 An invoice is a document that lists goods that have been supplied or services that have been done, and says how much money you owe for them. 发货单;发票

  • We will then send you an invoice for the total course fees. [+ for]

    然后我们会把全部课程费用的发票寄给你。

  • His £200 invoice was settled immediately in cash.

    他立即以现金付清了 200 英镑的货款。

SYN account, charge, bill, statement  
2 VERB 开发票给… If you invoice someone, you send them a bill for goods or services you have provided them with. 开发票给…

  • The agency invoices the client. [VERB noun]

    代理处为客户开具发票。

SYN charge, bill, record, figure
