lead singer
liːd sɪŋəʳ liːd ˈsɪŋə
Word forms: plural lead singers
N-COUNT （乐队的）主唱 The lead singer of a pop group is the person who sings most of the songs. （乐队的）主唱

  • ...Mick Hucknall, lead singer of Simply Red.

    “纯红”乐队主唱米克·赫克纳尔

  • I was the lead singer in the band that became the Prefects.

    我曾是“级长”乐队前身的主唱。

SYN star, lead, leading light
