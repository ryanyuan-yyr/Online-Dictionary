switch over
1 PHRASAL VERB 换台;换频道 If you switch over when you are watching television, you change to another channel. 换台;换频道

  • I just happened to switch over although I haven't been watching the Olympics. [VERB PREP.]

    我并没有一直在看奥运会，当时只是恰好换到这个频道。

  • Let's switch over to Channel 4. [VERB PREP. + to]

    换到 4 频道吧。
