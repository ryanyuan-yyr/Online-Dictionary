parent
peərənt ˈpɛərənt ˈpɛrənt ˈpærənt
Word forms: plural parents
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 父母 Your parents are your mother and father. 父母

  • Children need their parents.

    孩子们需要父母。

  • This is where a lot of parents go wrong.

    这正是很多父母犯错的地方。

  • When you become a parent the things you once cared about seem to have less value.

    一旦你为人父母，你曾经在意的那些事情就显得不那么重要了。

2See also:foster parent  one-parent family  single parent 
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 创始的；母公司的；总公司的 An organization's parent organization is the organization that created it and usually still controls it. 创始的；母公司的；总公司的

  • Each unit including the parent company has its own, local management.

    包括母公司在内，每个单位都有自己的地方管理层。

  • ...the zoo's parent body, the Zoological Society of London.

    该动物园的创始组织，伦敦动物学会

4 ADJ [ADJ n] （动物、植物或有机体）亲本的，亲代的 The parent animal, plant, or organism of a particular animal, plant or organism is the one that it comes from or is produced by. （动物、植物或有机体）亲本的，亲代的

  • Parent birds began to hunt for food for their young.

    亲鸟开始为幼鸟寻找食物。
