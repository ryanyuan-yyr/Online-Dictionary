kick against
PHRASAL VERB （以激烈、突然或极端的方式）反对，反抗，抵抗 If you kick against a situation you dislike but cannot control, you react against it in a violent, sudden, or extreme way. （以激烈、突然或极端的方式）反对，反抗，抵抗 [mainly British]

  • North Sea operators kicked against legislation making them responsible for removing oil platforms at the end of their useful life. [VERB PREP. noun]

    北海的石油开采商激烈反对要求他们在石油钻井平台使用期结束之后将其移除的立法。

SYN oppose, resist, complain about, object to
