put by
PHRASAL VERB 积蓄；储存 If you put money by, you save it so that you can use it later. 积蓄；储存 [British]

  • Dermot's putting his money by, in a Deposit Account. [VERB noun PREP.]

    德莫特把钱存在一个定期存款账户里。

  • There was enough put by for her fare. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    存的钱足够付她的机票钱。

  • Well we need to put by £200 for the pump valves to be renewed. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我们需要存下200英镑以备更换水泵阀。
