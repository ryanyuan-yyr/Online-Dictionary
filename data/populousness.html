populous
pɒpjʊləs ˈpɑpjələs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 人口众多的；人口稠密的 A populous country or area has a lot of people living in it. 人口众多的；人口稠密的 [formal]

  • Indonesia, with 216 million people, is the fourth most populous country in the world.

    有2.16亿人的印度尼西亚是世界第四人口大国。

SYN populated, crowded, packed, swarming
