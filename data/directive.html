directive
daɪrektɪv dɪr- dɪˈrɛktɪv dəˈrɛktɪv daɪˈrɛktɪv
Word forms: plural directives
N-COUNT 指示;命令 A directive is an official instruction that is given by someone in authority. 指示;命令

  • Thanks to a new EU directive, insecticide labelling will be more specific.

    根据欧盟的一项新指令，杀虫剂的标签标注将更加明确。

SYN order, ruling, regulation, charge
