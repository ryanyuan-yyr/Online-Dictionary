transitional
trænzɪʃənəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (时期)过渡的 A transitional period is one in which things are changing from one state to another. (时期)过渡的

  • ...a transitional period following more than a decade of civil war.

    十多年的内战结束之后的过渡时期

  • We are still in the transitional stage between the old and new methods.

    我们仍然处于新旧方法的过渡阶段。

SYN changing, passing, fluid, intermediate  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 过渡性的;过渡时期的 Transitional is used to describe something that happens or exists during a transitional period. 过渡性的;过渡时期的

  • The main rebel groups have agreed to join in a meeting to set up a transitional government.

    主要叛乱集团已同意参加会议讨论建立一个过渡政府。

SYN temporary, working, acting, short-term
