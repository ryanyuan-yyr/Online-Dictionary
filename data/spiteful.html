spiteful
spaɪtfʊl ˈspaɪtfəl
ADJ-GRADED 怀有恶意的;怀恨在心的 Someone who is spiteful does cruel things to hurt people they dislike. 怀有恶意的;怀恨在心的

  • He could be spiteful.

    他可能怀有恶意。

  • ...a stream of spiteful telephone calls.

    一连串恶意电话

SYN malicious, nasty, vindictive, cruel  
spitefully

  • We crept into our little sister's bedroom and spitefully defaced her pop posters.

    我们偷偷溜进妹妹的卧室，不怀好意地把她的歌星宣传画涂脏。
