Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense revs, present participle revving, past tense , past participle revved
1 V-ERG (使)加快转速;(使)加速 When the engine of a vehicle revs, or when you rev it, the engine speed is increased as the accelerator is pressed. (使)加快转速;(使)加速

  • The engine started, revved and the car jerked away down the hill. [VERB]

    引擎发动了，油门加大后车冲下了山坡。

  • The old bus was revving its engine, ready to start the journey back towards Madrid. [VERB noun]

    这辆旧巴士发动了引擎，准备启程返回马德里。

Rev up means the same as rev.rev up 同rev

  • ...drivers revving up their engines.

    司机们加大了油门

  • ...the sound of a car revving up.

    汽车加速的声音

2 N-PLURAL (发动机的)转速 If you talk about the revs of an engine, you are referring to its speed, which is measured in revolutions per minute. (发动机的)转速

  • The engine delivers instant acceleration whatever the revs.

    不论转速如何，发动机都可以立即加速。
