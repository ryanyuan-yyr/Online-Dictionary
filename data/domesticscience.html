domestic science
N-UNCOUNT 家政学（现在称为home economics，英国学校科目名称，也常见于美国英语） In British schools, domestic science was the name used to refer to the subject which involved cookery, sewing, and other household skills. The subject is now referred to as home economics, which is also the usual American term. 家政学（现在称为home economics，英国学校科目名称，也常见于美国英语）
