paucity
pɔːsɪti ˈpɔsəti
N-SING 少量；不足；缺乏 If you say that there is a paucity of something, you mean that there is not enough of it. 少量；不足；缺乏 [formal]

  • Even the film's impressive finale can't hide the first hour's paucity of imagination. [+ of]

    这部电影的结局可圈可点，即使这样也无法掩盖前一个小时想象力贫乏的缺憾。

  • ...the paucity of good British women sprinters. [+ of]

    英国优秀女子短跑选手的匮乏

SYN scarcity, lack, poverty, shortage
