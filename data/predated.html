predate
priːdeɪt priːˈdeɪt priˈdeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense predates, present participle predating, past tense , past participle predated
VERB （在日期上）早于，先于 If you say that one thing predated another, you mean that the first thing happened or existed some time before the second thing. （在日期上）早于，先于

  • His troubles predated the recession. [VERB noun]

    他的问题在经济萧条前就出现了。

  • The monument predates the arrival of the druids in Britain. [VERB noun]

    这一遗迹在德鲁伊特来到不列颠前就已存在。
