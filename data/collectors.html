collector
kəlektəʳ kəˈlɛktər
Word forms: plural collectors
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 收集者;收藏家 A collector is a person who collects things of a particular type as a hobby. 收集者;收藏家

  • ...a stamp-collector.

    集邮爱好者

  • ...a respected collector of Indian art. [+ of]

    受人尊敬的印第安艺术品收藏家

  • His work is much sought after by collectors.

    他的作品在收藏家们那里很抢手。

SYN gatherer, acquirer, saver, amasser  
2 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] (…的)收取人;讨债人;收税员;收票员;收垃圾者 You can use collector to refer to someone whose job is to take something such as money, tickets, or rubbish from people. For example, a rent collector collects rent from people. (…的)收取人;讨债人;收税员;收票员;收垃圾者

  • He earned his living as a tax collector.

    他以收税为业。

  • ...a garbage collector.

    垃圾清运工
