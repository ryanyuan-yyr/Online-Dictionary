concurrence
kənkʌrəns -kɜːr- kənˈkɜrəns
Word forms: plural concurrences
1 N-VAR [oft with poss] 同意;赞同； 一致 Someone's concurrence is their agreement to something. 同意;赞同； 一致 [formal]

  • Any change ought not to be made without the general concurrence of all concerned. [+ of]

    未得到所有相关人士的普遍同意，不应作出任何改动。

2 N-VAR 同时发生 If there is a concurrence of two or more things, they happen at the same time. 同时发生

  • The concurrence of their disappearances had to be more than coincidental. [+ of]

    他们同时失踪肯定不仅仅是巧合。
