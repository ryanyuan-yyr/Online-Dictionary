handsome
hænsəm ˈhændsəm ˈhænsəm ˈhændsəm
1 ADJ-GRADED （男子）漂亮的，英俊的，帅气的 A handsome man has an attractive face with regular features. →see usage note at: pretty （男子）漂亮的，英俊的，帅气的

  • ...a tall, dark, handsome sheep farmer.

    高个子、黑皮肤的英俊养羊人

2 ADJ-GRADED （女子）端庄健美的 A handsome woman has an attractive appearance with features that are large and regular rather than small and delicate. （女子）端庄健美的

  • ...an extremely handsome woman with a beautiful voice.

    有着甜美嗓音的非常健美的女子

3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （金额）相当大的，可观的，优厚的 A handsome sum of money is a large or generous amount. （金额）相当大的，可观的，优厚的 [formal]

  • They will make a handsome profit on the property.

    他们会从这片地产中赚取丰厚的利润。

SYN generous, large, princely, liberal  
handsomely

  • He was rewarded handsomely for his efforts.

    他的付出获得了丰厚的回报。

4 ADJ-GRADED （建筑物、庭院等）宽敞而美观的，堂皇的，雄伟的 A place such as a building or garden that is handsome is large and well made with an attractive appearance. （建筑物、庭院等）宽敞而美观的，堂皇的，雄伟的

  • ...the ports of Dubrovnik and Zadar, with their handsome Renaissance buildings.

    拥有雄伟的文艺复兴时期建筑的杜布罗夫尼克港和扎达尔港

handsomely

  • The drawing-room is handsomely proportioned.

    客厅的比例合理，尽显大气。

5 ADJ [ADJ n] （获胜）比分悬殊的，遥遥领先的 If someone has a handsome win or a handsome victory, they get many more points or votes than their opponent. （获胜）比分悬殊的，遥遥领先的

  • The opposition won a handsome victory in the election.

    反对党在竞选中以高票获胜。

handsomely

  • The car ran perfectly to the finish, and we won handsomely.

    车圆满地跑到终点，我们遥遥领先，赢得了比赛。
