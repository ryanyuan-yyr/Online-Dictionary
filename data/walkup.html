walk-up
ˈwɔkˌʌp
Word forms: plural walk-ups
N-COUNT 无电梯的公寓大楼;无电梯直通的公寓 A walk-up is a tall apartment block which has no lift. You can also refer to an apartment in such a block as a walk-up . 无电梯的公寓大楼;无电梯直通的公寓 [US]

  • She lives in a tiny fifth floor walk-up in New York's East Village.

    她居住在纽约东村一幢公寓楼5层的一间没有电梯直达的狭小公寓里。
