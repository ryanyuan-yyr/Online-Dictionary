scarper
skɑːʳpəʳ ˈskɑrpər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense scarpers, present participle scarpering, past tense , past participle scarpered
VERB 逃跑;溜走;溜号 If someone scarpers, they leave a place quickly. 逃跑;溜走;溜号 [British, informal]

  • He owed Vince money for drugs, so he scarpered. [VERB]

    他欠文斯买毒品的钱，所以溜走了。
