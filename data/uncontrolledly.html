uncontrolled
ʌnkəntroʊld ˌʌnkənˈtrəʊld
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n, ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ] 控制不住的；未受控制的；不加约束的 If you describe someone's behaviour as uncontrolled, you mean they appear unable to stop it or to make it less extreme. 控制不住的；未受控制的；不加约束的

  • His uncontrolled behavior disturbed the entire class.

    他放肆的举动扰乱了全班的秩序。

  • Julia blows her nose, but her sobbing goes on uncontrolled.

    朱莉娅擤了擤鼻子，不过她还是不停地啜泣。

SYN unrestrained, violent, wild, undisciplined  
2 ADJ-GRADED （形势或活动）不受控制的，未加制止的 If a situation or activity is uncontrolled, no-one is controlling it or preventing it from continuing or growing. （形势或活动）不受控制的，未加制止的

  • The capital, Nairobi, is choking on uncontrolled immigration.

    移民纷纷涌入首都内罗毕，人满为患。

  • ...the central bank's uncontrolled printing of money.

    中央银行滥印钞票

SYN unchecked, unrestrained, ungoverned, uncurbed
