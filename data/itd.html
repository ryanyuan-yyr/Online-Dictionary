it'd ɪtəd ˈɪtəd
1 (it would 的一种口语形式) It'd is a spoken form of 'it would'. (it would 的一种口语形式)

  • It'd be better for a place like this to remain closed.

    像这种地方最好一直关闭。

2 (it had 的口语形式，尤用于 had 用作助动词时) It'd is a spoken form of 'it had', especially when 'had' is an auxiliary verb. (it had 的口语形式，尤用于 had 用作助动词时)

  • Marcie was watching the news. It'd just started.

    马尔西正在看新闻,才刚开始播放。
