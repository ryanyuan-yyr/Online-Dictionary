role play
(also role-play)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense role plays, present participle role playing, past tense , past participle role played
1 N-VAR 角色扮演;角色模仿 Role play is the act of imitating the character and behaviour of someone who is different from yourself, for example as a training exercise. 角色扮演;角色模仿

  • Group members have to communicate with each other through role-play.

    小组成员必须通过角色扮演互相交流。

2 VERB 进行角色扮演 If people role play, they do a role play. 进行角色扮演 [Also VERB]

  • Rehearse and role-play the interview with a friend beforehand. [VERB noun]

    事先和朋友通过角色扮演排练一下面试。

role playing

  • We did a lot of role playing.

    我们进行了多次角色扮演。
