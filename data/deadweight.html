dead weight
Word forms: plural dead weights
1 N-COUNT 重荷;（难以提举的）重物 A dead weight is a load which is surprisingly heavy and difficult to lift. 重荷;（难以提举的）重物

  • He hoisted the dead weight over his shoulder.

    他把那死沉的重物扛到了肩上。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 重负;累赘 You can refer to something that makes change or progress difficult as a dead weight . 重负;累赘

  • ...the dead weight of traditional policies.

    传统政策这一沉重包袱
