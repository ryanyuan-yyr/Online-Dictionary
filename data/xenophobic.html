xenophobic
zenəfoʊbɪk
ADJ-GRADED 仇外的;惧外的 If you describe someone as xenophobic, you disapprove of them because they show strong dislike or fear of people from other countries. 仇外的;惧外的 [formal, disapproval]

  • Xenophobic nationalism is on the rise in some West European countries.

    西欧一些国家的仇外民族主义有所抬头。

  • The French are proud and highly xenophobic when it comes to cooking.

    谈到烹饪，法国人会颇为得意并且极端排斥外国菜。

SYN racist, nationalist, bigoted, parochial
