run to
1 PHRASAL VERB 求助;询问 If you run to someone, you go to them for help or to tell them something. 求助;询问

  • What would I do? Whom would I run to? Momma was still away. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我该怎么办？我能向谁求助？妈妈还没回来。

  • If you were at a party and somebody was getting high, you didn't go running to a cop. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果参加聚会的时候有人喝醉了，可别向警察求助。

2 PHRASAL VERB 达到(一定数量或规模) If something runs to a particular amount or size, it is that amount or size. 达到(一定数量或规模)

  • The finished manuscript ran to the best part of fifty double-sided pages. [VERB PREP. noun]

    完成的手稿占了双面都写满字的足足50页纸。

3 PHRASAL VERB 支付得起;有钱购买 If you cannot run to a particular item, you cannot afford to buy it or pay for it. 支付得起;有钱购买 [mainly British]

  • If you can't run to champagne, buy sparkling wine. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果买不起香槟酒，就买汽酒好了。

4 PHRASAL VERB 喜欢;偏爱;偏好 If your tastes or interests run to a particular type of thing, that is the type of thing you like. 喜欢;偏爱;偏好

  • My own tastes run to a comfortable apartment, somewhere high in a modern building. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我个人更喜欢现代化的大楼里舒适的高层公寓。
