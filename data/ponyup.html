pony up
PHRASAL VERB （常指不情愿地）付（款） If you pony up a sum of money, you pay the money that is needed for something, often unwillingly. （常指不情愿地）付（款） [US, informal]

  • The IMF is not prepared to pony up the second half of the $4 billion. [VERB PREP. noun]

    国际货币基金组织不准备支付后一半40亿美元的款项。

  • People can't even afford to pony up for movie tickets. [V P for n]

    人们甚至买不起电影票。
