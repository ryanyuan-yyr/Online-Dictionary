becalmed
bɪkɑːmd
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] （帆船）因无风而停航的 If a sailing ship is becalmed, it is unable to move because there is no wind. （帆船）因无风而停航的

  • We were becalmed off Dungeness for several hours.

    因为没有风，我们在邓杰内斯附近停留了好几个小时。

SYN still, stuck, settled, stranded  
2 ADJ 没有进展的；停滞不前的 If something such as the economy, a company, or a series of talks is becalmed, it is not progressing at all, although it should be. 没有进展的；停滞不前的 [literary]

  • Polish industry generally is becalmed.

    波兰工业普遍陷于停滞。

  • ...the becalmed peace talks.

    陷入僵局的和谈
