compilation
kɒmpɪleɪʃən ˌkɑmpəˈleɪʃən
Word forms: plural compilations
1 N-COUNT 汇编物;辑;集子 A compilation is a book, CD, or programme that contains many different items that have been gathered together, usually ones which have already appeared in other places. 汇编物;辑;集子

  • His latest album release is a compilation of his jazz works over the past decade. [+ of]

    最近发行的专辑是他过去10年来的爵士作品合集。
