symptom
sɪmptəm ˈsɪmptəm
Word forms: plural symptoms
1 N-COUNT 症状 A symptom of an illness is something wrong with your body or mind that is a sign of the illness. 症状

  • One of the most common symptoms of schizophrenia is hearing imaginary voices.

    精神分裂症的最常见的一个症状就是幻听。

  • ...patients with flu symptoms.

    有流感症状的病人

  • If the symptoms persist, it is important to go to your doctor.

    如果这些症状持续存在，一定要去看医生。

SYN sign, mark, indication, warning  
2 N-COUNT 征候;征兆 A symptom of a bad situation is something that happens which is considered to be a sign of this situation. 征候;征兆

  • Your problem with keeping boyfriends is just a symptom of a larger problem: making and keeping friends. [+ of]

    你在维系同男友的关系时遭遇的困难暴露了一个更大的问题：如何结交朋友和维持友谊。

  • The contradictory statements are symptoms of disarray in the administration.

    这些互相矛盾的说法反映了管理的混乱。

SYN manifestation, sign, indication, mark
