trooper
truːpəʳ ˈtrupər
Word forms: plural troopers
1 N-COUNT; N-TITLE 骑兵;装甲团士兵 A trooper is a soldier of low rank in the cavalry or in an armoured regiment in the army. 骑兵;装甲团士兵

  • ...a trooper from the 7th Cavalry.

    第七骑兵团的一名骑兵

  • 'Where to, Corporal?' asked Trooper Fane respectfully.

    “下士，您去哪里？”骑兵费恩毕恭毕敬地问道。

2 N-COUNT (美国)州警(察) In the United States, a trooper is a police officer in a state police force. (美国)州警(察)

  • Once long ago he had considered becoming a state trooper.

    很久以前他曾考虑过当州警察。

3 See also: storm trooper
