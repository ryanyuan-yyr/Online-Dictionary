recollection
rekəlekʃən ˌrɛkəˈlɛkʃən
Word forms: plural recollections
N-VAR 记忆；回忆 If you have a recollection of something, you remember it. 记忆；回忆

  • Pat has vivid recollections of the trip. [+ of]

    帕特清清楚楚地记得那次旅行。

  • He had no recollection of the crash. [+ of]

    那次车祸他一点也不记得了。

  • Suddenly his mind filled with the recollection of a song she used to sing.

    突然，他记起了一首她过去经常唱的歌。

  • She had a sudden burst of recollection and had to share it with me.

    她突然记起了很多事情，要与我分享。

SYN memory, recall, impression, remembrance
