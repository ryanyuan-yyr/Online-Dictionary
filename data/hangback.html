hang back
1 PHRASAL VERB （通常指因紧张而）退缩，躲避 If you hang back, you move or stay slightly behind a person or group, usually because you are nervous about something. （通常指因紧张而）退缩，躲避

  • I saw him step forward momentarily but then hang back, nervously massaging his hands. [VERB PREP.]

    我看到他走上前，但很快又缩了回来，紧张地搓着手。

2 PHRASAL VERB 拖延;拖拉 If a person or organization hangs back, they do not do something immediately. 拖延;拖拉

  • They will then hang back on closing the deal. [VERB PREP. + on]

    他们接着会拖延生意的成交。

  • Even his closest advisers believe he should hang back no longer. [VERB PREP.]

    即便是和他关系最密切的顾问都认为他不应该再拖下去了。

SYN be reluctant, hesitate, hold back, recoil
