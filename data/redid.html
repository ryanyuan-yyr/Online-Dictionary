redo
riːduː riːˈduː riˈdu
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redoes, present participle redoing, past tense redid, past participle redone
VERB 重做；返工 If you redo a piece of work, you do it again in order to improve it or change it. 重做；返工

  • They had redone their sums. [VERB noun]

    他们已重算了一次总数。
