precedent
presɪdənt priˈsidənt prɪˈsidənt ˈprɛsədənt
Word forms: plural precedents
N-VAR 前例；先例 If there is a precedent for an action or event, it has happened before, and this can be regarded as an argument for doing it again. 前例；先例 [formal]

  • The trial could set an important precedent for dealing with large numbers of similar cases. [+ for]

    这次审判能为处理大量类似案例开创重要的先例。

  • There are plenty of precedents in Hollywood for letting people out of contracts.

    在好莱坞，允许影视人员解除合同的先例有很多。

SYN instance, example, authority, standard
