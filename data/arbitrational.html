arbitration
ɑːʳbɪtreɪʃən ˌɑrbəˈtreɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 仲裁；公断 Arbitration is the judging of a dispute between people or groups by someone who is not involved. 仲裁；公断

  • ...the independent arbitration service, ACAS.

    独立的仲裁部门，咨询调解仲裁处

  • The matter is likely to go to arbitration.

    这件事很有可能要提请仲裁。

SYN decision, settlement, judgment, determination
