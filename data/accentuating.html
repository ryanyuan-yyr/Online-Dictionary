accentuate
æksentʃueɪt ækˈsɛntʃʊˌeɪt ækˈsɛntʃuˌeɪt əkˈsɛntʃuˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense accentuates, present participle accentuating, past tense , past participle accentuated
VERB 强调;加重;使更明显 To accentuate something means to emphasize it or make it more noticeable. 强调;加重;使更明显

  • His shaven head accentuates his large round face. [VERB noun]

    光头使他的大圆脸更加突出。

  • The whole air of menace was accentuated by the fact that he was so cordial and soft-voiced. [VERB noun]

    他如此亲切，说话轻声细语，这更加重了威胁的语气。

SYN emphasize, stress, highlight, accent
