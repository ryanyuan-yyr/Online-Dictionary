founding father
Word forms: plural founding fathers
1 N-COUNT 开创者;提出者;创立人 The founding father of an institution, organization, or idea is the person who sets it up or who first develops it. 开创者;提出者;创立人 [literary]
2 N-PROPER-PLURAL 开国者，制宪元勋(美国宪法的起草者) The Founding Fathers of the United States were the members of the American Constitutional Convention of 1787. 开国者，制宪元勋(美国宪法的起草者)
