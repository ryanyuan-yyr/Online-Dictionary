publish
pʌblɪʃ ˈpʌblɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense publishes, present participle publishing, past tense , past participle published
1 VERB 出版；发行 When a company publishes a book or magazine, it prints copies of it, which are sent to shops to be sold. 出版；发行

  • They publish reference books. [VERB noun]

    他们出版工具书。

  • His latest book of poetry will be published by Faber in May. [VERB noun]

    他的最新诗集将由菲贝尔出版公司于5月出版。

SYN put out, issue, produce, print  
2 VERB 刊登；登载 When the people in charge of a newspaper or magazine publish a piece of writing or a photograph, they print it in their newspaper or magazine. 刊登；登载

  • The ban was imposed after the magazine published an article satirising the government. [VERB noun]

    这家杂志刊登了一篇讽刺政府的文章后被停刊。

  • I don't encourage people to take photographs like this without permission, but by law we can publish. [VERB]

    我不鼓励人们未经允许就拍摄这样的照片，但根据法律我们可以刊登。

3 VERB 发表；出版 If someone publishes a book or an article that they have written, they arrange to have it published. 发表；出版

  • John Lennon found time to publish two books of his humorous prose. [VERB noun]

    约翰·列侬抽时间出版了两本幽默散文。

4 VERB 公布；发布 If you publish information or an opinion, you make it known to the public by having it printed in a newspaper, magazine, or official document. 公布；发布

  • The demonstrators called on the government to publish a list of registered voters. [VERB noun]

    游行者呼吁政府公布注册选民的名单。
