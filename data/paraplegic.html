paraplegic
pærəpliːdʒɪk
Word forms: plural paraplegics
N-COUNT 截瘫者；下身瘫痪患者 A paraplegic is someone who cannot move the lower half of their body, for example because of an injury to their spine. 截瘫者；下身瘫痪患者
Paraplegic is also an adjective.

  • A passenger was injured so badly he will be paraplegic for the rest of his life.

    一位乘客受了重伤，后半生都将下身瘫痪。
