write down
PHRASAL VERB 写下;记下 When you write something down, you record it on a piece of paper using a pen or pencil. 写下;记下

  • On the morning before starting the fast, write down your starting weight. [VERB PREP. noun]

    早晨开始禁食前，记下你的体重。

  • Only by writing things down could I bring some sort of order to the confusion. [VERB noun PREP.]

    只有把东西记下来，我才能从混乱中理出些头绪来。
