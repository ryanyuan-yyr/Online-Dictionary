pull in
1 PHRASAL VERB （车）停下；（驾车者）停车 When a vehicle or driver pulls in somewhere, the vehicle stops there. （车）停下；（驾车者）停车

  • He pulled in at the side of the road. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    他靠边停了车。

  • The van pulled in and waited. [VERB PREP.]

    货车停下等候。

SYN draw in, stop, park, arrive  
2 PHRASAL VERB 逮捕；把…抓起来 If the police pull someone in, they arrest them and take them to the police station. 逮捕；把…抓起来 [informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • 'Brady looks like a suspect.' — 'I'd pull him in.' [VERB noun PREP.]

    “布雷迪看着像个嫌疑犯。”—“我会去把他抓起来的。”

3 PHRASAL VERB 挣；获得；赚取 If you pull in an amount of money, you earn or collect that amount. 挣；获得；赚取 [informal]

  • I only pull in 15,000 a year as a social worker. [V P amount]

    作为一名社工，我每年仅挣15,000。

SYN earn, make, clear, gain
