delirious
dɪlɪəriəs dɪˈlɪriəs
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (通常指因生病发烧而)谵妄的，神志不清的，说胡话的 Someone who is delirious is unable to think or speak in a sensible and reasonable way, usually because they are very ill and have a fever. (通常指因生病发烧而)谵妄的，神志不清的，说胡话的

  • I was delirious and blacked out several times.

    我神志不清，昏迷了好几次。

SYN mad, crazy, raving, insane  
2 ADJ-GRADED 欣喜若狂的;极度兴奋的 Someone who is delirious is extremely excited and happy. 欣喜若狂的;极度兴奋的

  • His tax-cutting pledge brought a delirious crowd to their feet.

    他减税的承诺让亢奋的人群欢呼雀跃起来。

  • I was delirious with joy.

    我欣喜若狂。

SYN ecstatic, wild, excited, frantic  
deliriously

  • Dora returned from her honeymoon deliriously happy.

    多拉度完蜜月回来幸福洋溢。

  • Barking deliriously, the dog bounded towards his mistress.

    那只狗兴奋地狂吠着向女主人跑去。
