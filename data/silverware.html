silverware
sɪlvəʳweəʳ ˈsɪlvəˌwɛə ˈsɪlvərˌwɛr
1 N-UNCOUNT 银器(尤指餐具) You can use silverware to refer to all the things in a house that are made of silver, especially the cutlery and dishes. 银器(尤指餐具)

  • There was a serving spoon missing when Nina put the silverware back in its box.

    尼娜将银餐具收拾到盒子里去的时候发现少了一把调羹。

2 N-UNCOUNT (新闻用语中有时指体育比赛中的)银杯 Journalists sometimes use silverware to refer to silver cups and other prizes won by sports teams or players. (新闻用语中有时指体育比赛中的)银杯

  • Everton paraded their recently acquired silverware.

    埃弗顿展示了他们最近获得的银杯。
