Technicolor
teknɪkʌləʳ ˈtɛknɪˌkʌlər
The spelling technicolour is also used in British English for meaning 2. 英国英语中拼写technicolour 亦用于义项2。
1 N-UNCOUNT (制作电影的)彩色印片法 Technicolor is a system of colour photography used in making cinema films. (制作电影的)彩色印片法 [trademark]

  • ...films in Technicolor.

    彩色影片

2 N-UNCOUNT （尤用作夸张）五彩缤纷，色彩斑斓 You can use technicolor to describe real or imagined scenes when you want to emphasize that they are very colourful, especially in an exaggerated way. （尤用作夸张）五彩缤纷，色彩斑斓 [informal]

  • I was seeing it all in glorious technicolour: mountains, valleys, lakes, summer sunshine.

    我所看见的一切—大山、山谷、湖泊和夏日的阳光全都绚丽多彩。

  • ...Technicolor dreams.

    光怪陆离的梦境
