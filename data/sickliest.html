sickly
sɪkli ˈsɪkli
Word forms: comparative sicklier, superlative sickliest
1 ADJ-GRADED 常生病的;虚弱的;健康欠佳的 A sickly person or animal is weak, unhealthy, and often ill. 常生病的;虚弱的;健康欠佳的

  • He had been a sickly child.

    他曾是个体弱多病的孩子。

SYN unhealthy, weak, delicate, ailing  
2 ADJ-GRADED (通常指因为太甜而)让人恶心的，令人作呕的 A sickly smell or taste is unpleasant and makes you feel slightly sick, often because it is extremely sweet. (通常指因为太甜而)让人恶心的，令人作呕的

  • ...the sickly smell of rum.

    朗姆酒甜得发腻的气味

SYN nauseating, revolting [informal] , cloying, icky [informal]  
3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (颜色、灯光等)暗淡的，苍白的，微弱的 A sickly colour or light is unpleasantly pale or weak. (颜色、灯光等)暗淡的，苍白的，微弱的

  • Wallpapers for children too often come only in sickly pastel shades.

    给孩子们用的墙纸往往颜色苍白暗淡。

SYN insipid, pale, washed out, colourless
