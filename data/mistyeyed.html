misty-eyed
ˌmɪstɪˈaɪd ˈmɪstiˌaɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （尤指因为回忆起过去而开心或感伤得）泪眼蒙眬的 If you say that something makes you misty-eyed, you mean that it makes you feel so happy or sentimental, especially about the past, that you feel as if you are going to cry. （尤指因为回忆起过去而开心或感伤得）泪眼蒙眬的

  • They got misty-eyed listening to records of Ruby Murray singing 'Danny Boy'.

    聆听着唱片里鲁比·默里演唱《丹尼男孩》的时候，他们泪眼模糊。
