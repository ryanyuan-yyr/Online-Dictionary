cock-and-bull story ˈkɑkənˈbʊl
Word forms: plural cock-and-bull stories
N-COUNT 荒诞的故事;无稽之谈 If you describe something that someone tells you as a cock-and-bull story, you mean that you do not believe it is true. 荒诞的故事;无稽之谈 [informal]

  • Was it him who made you come to us with this cock-and-bull story?

    是不是他让你来跟我们这么瞎说八道的?
