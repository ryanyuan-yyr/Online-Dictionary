chastity
tʃæstɪti ˈtʃæstəti
N-UNCOUNT 贞洁;贞操;禁欲 Chastity is the state of not having sex with anyone, or of only having sex with your husband or wife. 贞洁;贞操;禁欲 [old-fashioned]

SYN purity, virtue, innocence, modesty
