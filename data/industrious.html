industrious
ɪndʌstriəs ɪnˈdʌstriəs
ADJ-GRADED 勤劳的;勤奋的 If you describe someone as industrious, you mean they work very hard. 勤劳的;勤奋的

  • She was an industrious and willing worker.

    她是个勤劳肯干的员工。

SYN hard-working, diligent, active, busy  
industriously

  • Maggie paints industriously all through the summer.

    玛吉整个夏天都在勤奋作画。
