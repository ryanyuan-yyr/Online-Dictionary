do-or-die ˈduərˈdaɪ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 孤注一掷的A do-or-die battle or struggle is one that involves a determined or desperate effort to succeed. 孤注一掷的

  • ...a do-or-die effort to turn Uniq into a lean and dynamic food business.

    ...为把Uniq公司转化为效率高、有活力的食品企业而作出的一个孤注一掷的努力。

2 PHRASE [v-link PHR n] 决定胜负的If something is do-or-die for someone or something, it will determine whether they succeed or fail. 决定胜负的

  • Nobody will know the exact position until Monday, when it is do-or-die for the deal.

    没有人在周一之前知道确切的情况—那时将是决定交易胜负的时刻。
