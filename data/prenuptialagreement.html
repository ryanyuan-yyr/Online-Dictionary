prenuptial agreement
prinʌpʃəl əgrimənt -nʌptʃəl
(also pre-nuptial agreement)Word forms: prenuptial agreements
N-COUNT 婚前协议A prenuptial agreement is a written contract made between a man and a woman before they marry, in which they state how their assets such as property and money should be divided if they get divorced. 婚前协议

  • My wife and I were married in 1996 and we signed a prenuptial agreement.

    我与我夫人于1996年结婚，并签署了一个婚前协议。
