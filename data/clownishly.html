clownish
klaʊnɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 滑稽的;傻傻的 If you describe a person's appearance or behaviour as clownish, you mean that they look or behave rather like a clown, and often that they appear rather foolish. 滑稽的;傻傻的

  • He had a clownish sense of humour.

    他有一种非常滑稽的幽默感。

  • ...their clownish stupidity.

    他们滑稽愚蠢的行为
