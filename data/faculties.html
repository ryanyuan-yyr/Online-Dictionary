faculty
fækəlti ˈfækəlti
Word forms: plural faculties
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft poss NOUN] 官能;能力 Your faculties are your physical and mental abilities. 官能;能力

  • He was drunk and not in control of his faculties.

    他喝醉了，浑身都不听使唤了。

  • It is also a myth that the faculty of hearing is greatly increased in blind people. [+ of]

    盲人的听力会大幅增强也是一种谬谈。

SYN power, reason, sense, intelligence  
2 N-VAR (大学的)院系;(大学院系的)教职工 A faculty is a group of related departments in some universities, or the people who work in them. (大学的)院系;(大学院系的)教职工 [British]

  • ...the Faculty of Social and Political Sciences. [+ of]

    社会政治系

SYN department, school, discipline, profession  
3 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] (大学、学院或院系的)全体教员 A faculty is all the teaching staff of a university or college, or of one department. (大学、学院或院系的)全体教员 [US]

  • The faculty agreed on a change in the requirements.

    全体教师一致同意修改规定。

  • How can faculty improve their teaching so as to encourage creativity?

    教师们如何才能改进教学以激发创造性呢？

  • ...eminent Stanford faculty members.

    声名显赫的斯坦福大学的教师们

SYN teaching staff, staff, teachers, professors
