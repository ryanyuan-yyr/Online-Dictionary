write into
PHRASAL VERB 将(某一条款或细节)写入(合同、法律或协议) If a rule or detail is written into a contract, law, or agreement, it is included in it when the contract, law, or agreement is made. 将(某一条款或细节)写入(合同、法律或协议)

  • They insisted that a guaranteed supply of Chinese food was written into their contracts. [be VERB-ed PREP. noun]

    他们坚持要在合同中写入保证供应中餐的条款。

  • I didn't write that into the rules but I don't think it's a bad idea. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    我没有将其写进规定中，但我觉得这个主意不错。
