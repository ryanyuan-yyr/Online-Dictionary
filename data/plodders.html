plodder
plɒdəʳ
Word forms: plural plodders
N-COUNT 做事慢条斯理、死气沉沉的人 If you say that someone is a plodder, you have a low opinion of them because they work slowly and steadily but without showing enthusiasm or having new ideas. 做事慢条斯理、死气沉沉的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • He was quiet, conscientious, a bit of a plodder.

    他做事闷声不响，小心谨慎，有点死气沉沉的。
