saving
seɪvɪŋ ˈseɪvɪŋ
Word forms: plural savings
1 N-COUNT 节省;节约 A saving is a reduction in the amount of time or money that is used or needed. 节省;节约

  • Fill in the form below and you will be making a saving of £6.60 on a one-year subscription. [+ of]

    填写以下表格，全年订阅即可为您节省 6.60英镑。

  • ...a program of household savings on energy use.

    家庭节能计划

2 N-PLURAL (尤指在银行和购屋互助协会的)储蓄款，存款;积蓄 Your savings are the money that you have saved, especially in a bank or a building society. (尤指在银行和购屋互助协会的)储蓄款，存款;积蓄

  • Her savings were in the Post Office Savings Bank.

    她的钱存在邮政储蓄银行里。

  • ...a savings account.

    储蓄账户
