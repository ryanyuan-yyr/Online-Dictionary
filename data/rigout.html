rig out
PHRASAL VERB 穿上，身穿，给…穿上（某种服装） If you rig yourself out or are rigged out in a particular way, you are wearing a particular kind of clothes. 穿上，身穿，给…穿上（某种服装） [informal]

  • I rigged myself out in thick jeans and heavy belt. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    我穿上厚厚的牛仔裤，系上重重的皮带。

  • I was rigged out in my usual green suit. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    我穿着平时那套绿色套装。
