unrepresentative
ʌnreprɪzentətɪv ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv
ADJ-GRADED 不具代表性的；不典型的 If you describe a group of people as unrepresentative, you mean that their views are not typical of the community or society to which they belong. 不具代表性的；不典型的

  • The President denounced the demonstrators as unrepresentative of the Romanian people. [+ of]

    总统谴责示威者，说他们并不能代表罗马尼亚人民。
