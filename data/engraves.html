engrave
ɪngreɪv ɛnˈgreɪv ɪnˈgreɪv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense engraves, present participle engraving, past tense , past participle engraved
VERB 雕刻 If you engrave something with a design or words, or if you engrave a design or words on it, you cut the design or words into its surface. 雕刻 [Also V n, V n with quote]

  • Your wedding ring can be engraved with a personal inscription at no extra cost. [be VERB-ed + with]

    可以免费在你的婚戒上刻上你的题字。

  • Harrods will also engrave your child's name on the side. [V n + on/in]

    哈罗兹还会将你孩子的名字刻在侧面。

  • I'm having 'John Law' engraved on the cap. [have n V-ed + with]

    我要在笔帽上刻上John Law。

  • ...a bottle engraved with her name. [VERB-ed]

    刻着她名字的瓶子

  • ...an engraved crystal goblet. [VERB-ed]

    雕花水晶高脚酒杯

SYN carve, cut, etch, inscribe
