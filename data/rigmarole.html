rigmarole
rɪgməroʊl ˈrɪgməˌroʊl
Word forms: plural rigmaroles
N-COUNT [usually singular] 冗长复杂的过程;繁琐的程序 You can describe a long and complicated process as a rigmarole . 冗长复杂的过程;繁琐的程序 [disapproval]

  • Then the whole rigmarole starts over again.

    然后，整个漫长复杂的过程又重新开始了。

  • I couldn't be bothered to go through the rigmarole of changing clothes.

    我不想再费事儿换衣服了。

SYN procedure, to-do, performance [informal] , bother
