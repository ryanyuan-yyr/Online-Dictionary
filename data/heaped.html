heaped
hiːpt hiːpt
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 满满的（一勺） A heaped spoonful has the contents of the spoon piled up above the edge. 满满的（一勺）

  • Add one heaped tablespoon of salt.

    加一满勺盐。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n] 堆得满满的 A container or a surface that is heaped with things has a lot of them in it or on it in a pile, often so many that it cannot hold any more. 堆得满满的

  • The large desk was heaped with papers.

    大书桌上堆满了文件。

  • ...ashtrays heaped with cigarette butts.

    堆满了烟头的烟灰缸

SYN piled high, covered, loaded, overspread
