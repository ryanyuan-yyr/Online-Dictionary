enfant terrible
ɒnfɒn teriːblə ɑ̃fɑ̃ tɛˈʀibl(ə)
Word forms: plural enfants terribles
N-COUNT [usually singular] 聪慧却离经叛道的人 If you describe someone as an enfant terrible, you mean that they are clever but unconventional, and often cause problems or embarrassment for their friends or families. 聪慧却离经叛道的人 [literary]

  • He became known as the enfant terrible of British theater.

    他成为英国戏剧界让人头痛的天才。
