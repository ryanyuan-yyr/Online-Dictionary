interpreter
ɪntɜːʳprɪtəʳ ɪnˈtɜrprətər
Word forms: plural interpreters
1 N-COUNT 口译者;口译员 An interpreter is a person whose job is to translate what someone is saying into another language. 口译者;口译员

  • Aristide spoke to the press through an interpreter.

    阿里斯蒂德通过口译员向媒体发表讲话。

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n] 演奏者;表演者 The interpreter of something such as a piece of music is the person who performs it. 演奏者;表演者

  • Over the years Freni has been one of the supreme interpreters of Puccini's heroines.

    多年来弗雷尼一直是普契尼歌剧女主角的最佳表演者之一。

SYN performer, player, presenter, exponent
