understudy
ʌndəʳstʌdi ˈʌndərˌstʌdi
Word forms: plural understudies
N-COUNT 预备演员；替角儿 An actor's or actress's understudy is the person who has learned their part in a play and can act the part if the actor or actress is ill. 预备演员；替角儿

  • He was an understudy to Charlie Chaplin on a tour of the USA.

    他是查理·卓别麟美国巡演期间的替角儿。

SYN stand-in, reserve, substitute, double
