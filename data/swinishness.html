swine
swaɪn swaɪn
Word forms: plural swines
The form swines is used as the plural for meaning 1; swine is used as both the singular and plural for meaning 2. swines 用作义项 1 的复数形式; swine 用于义项 2 时单复数同形。
1 N-COUNT 讨厌鬼;猪猡;下流坯 If you call someone a swine, you dislike them or think that they are a bad person, usually because they have behaved unpleasantly towards you. 讨厌鬼;猪猡;下流坯 [informal, disapproval]
2 N-COUNT 猪 A swine is a pig. to cast pearls before swine→see: pearl  猪

  • ...imports of live swine from Canada.

    从加拿大进口的活猪

  • ...pigs with swine fever.

    患猪瘟的猪
