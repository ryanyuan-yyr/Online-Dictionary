yelp
jelp jɛlp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense yelps, present participle yelping, past tense , past participle yelped
VERB （通常因害怕或痛楚）尖叫;（狗）嚎叫 If a person or dog yelps, they give a sudden short cry, often because of fear or pain. （通常因害怕或痛楚）尖叫;（狗）嚎叫

  • Her dog yelped and came to heel. [VERB]

    她的狗汪地叫了一声，紧跟了上来。

SYN bark, howl, yap, yip [mainly US]  
Yelp is also a noun.

  • I had to bite back a yelp of surprise.

    我只好强忍住，没惊叫出来。
