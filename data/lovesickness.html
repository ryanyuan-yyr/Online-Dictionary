lovesick
lʌvsɪk ˈlʌvˌsɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 单相思的；苦恋的 If you describe someone as lovesick, you mean that they are so in love with someone who does not love them, that they are behaving in a strange and foolish way. 单相思的；苦恋的

  • ...a lovesick boy consumed with self-pity.

    顾影自怜的单相思男孩

SYN lovelorn, longing, desiring, pining
