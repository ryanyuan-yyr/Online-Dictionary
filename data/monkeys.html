monkey
mʌŋki ˈmʌŋki
Word forms: plural monkeys
1 N-COUNT 猴 A monkey is an animal with a long tail which lives in hot countries and climbs trees. 猴
2 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 淘气鬼;捣蛋鬼 If you refer to a child as a monkey, you are saying in an affectionate way that he or she is very lively and naughty. 淘气鬼;捣蛋鬼 [feelings]

  • She's such a little monkey.

    她真是一个小捣蛋鬼。

SYN rascal, horror [informal] , devil, rogue
