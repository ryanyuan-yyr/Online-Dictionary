finish off
1 PHRASAL VERB 吃光; 喝光If you finish off something that you have been eating or drinking, you eat or drink the last part of it with the result that there is none left. 吃光; 喝光

  • Kelly finished off his coffee. [VERB PREP. noun]

    凯利喝光了他的咖啡。

2 PHRASAL VERB 杀死 (已严重受伤的人); 毁灭 (已严重受损的物)If someone finishes off a person or thing that is already badly injured or damaged, they kill or destroy them. 杀死 (已严重受伤的人); 毁灭 (已严重受损的物)

  • They meant to finish her off, swiftly and without mercy. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们打算毫不留情地迅速杀死她。
