homage
hɒmɪdʒ ˈhɑmɪdʒ ˈɑmɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 尊敬;崇敬;敬意 Homage is respect shown towards someone or something you admire, or to a person in authority. 尊敬;崇敬;敬意

  • Palace has released two marvellous films that pay homage to our literary heritage. [+ to]

    皇宫影业公司发行了两部颂扬我们文学遗产的精彩影片。

  • At his coronation he received the homage of kings from Wales, Northumbria and Scotland.

    在他的加冕典礼上，威尔士、诺森布里亚和苏格兰的国王向他宣誓效忠。

SYN respect, honour, worship, esteem
