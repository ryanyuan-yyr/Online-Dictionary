constable
kʌnstəbəl kɒn- ˈkʌnstəbəl ˈkɑnstəbəl
Word forms: plural constables
1 N-COUNT; N-TITLE; N-VOC (英国等国家中最低级别的)警察；警员 In Britain and some other countries, a constable is a police officer of the lowest rank. (英国等国家中最低级别的)警察；警员

  • He was a constable at Sutton police station.

    他是萨顿警察局的警员。

  • ...Constable Stuart Clark.

    斯图尔特·克拉克警员

  • Thanks for your help, Constable.

    警官，谢谢您的帮助。

2See also:Chief Constable 
3 N-COUNT; N-TITLE (美国城镇中参与维持秩序、级别低于县治安官的)巡官 In the United States, a constable is an official who helps keep the peace in a town. They are lower in rank than a sheriff. (美国城镇中参与维持秩序、级别低于县治安官的)巡官
