recessed
riːsest ˈriːsɛst
ADJ （门或窗）置于墙内凹陷处的，嵌进墙里的 If something such as a door or window is recessed, it is set into the wall that surrounds it. （门或窗）置于墙内凹陷处的，嵌进墙里的

  • ...a wide passage, lit from one side by recessed windows.

    一边有凹窗、光线可以照进来的宽敞走道

  • ...thick glass recessed into the ceiling.

    天花板上嵌着的厚厚的玻璃
