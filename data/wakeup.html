wake up
1 PHRASAL VERB 激起;唤起 If something such as an activity wakes you up, it makes you more alert and ready to do things after you have been lazy or inactive. 激起;唤起 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • A cool shower wakes up the body and boosts circulation. [VERB PREP. noun]

    冷水澡可以刺激身体，促进血液循环。

SYN activate, stimulate, enliven, galvanize
