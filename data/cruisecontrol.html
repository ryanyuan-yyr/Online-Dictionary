cruise control
1 N-UNCOUNT 自动控速系统In a car or other vehicle, cruise control is a system that automatically keeps the vehicle's speed at the same level. 自动控速系统

  • It came with leather upholstery, air conditioning and cruise control as standard.

    它的标准配置包括真皮座椅、空调和自动控速系统。

2 PHRASE (在比赛中)胜券在握If you say that someone is on cruise control in a contest, you mean that they are winning the contest easily and without needing to make a lot of effort. (在比赛中)胜券在握

  • The champs were on cruise control as they eased to victory in this one-sided bore.

    在这场一边倒的无聊比赛中，冠军们稳操胜券，大获全胜。
