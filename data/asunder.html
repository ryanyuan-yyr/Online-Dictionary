asunder
əsʌndəʳ əˈsʌndər
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 成碎片地;四分五裂地 If something tears or is torn asunder, it is violently separated into two or more parts or pieces. 成碎片地;四分五裂地 [literary]

  • ...a dress rent asunder from shoulder to hem.

    从肩部到裙摆都被扯得粉碎的一件连衣裙

  • The debate is tearing Wall Street asunder.

    这场争论令华尔街分崩离析。

SYN to pieces, apart, torn, rent
