adduce
ædjuːs -duːs əˈdus əˈdjus
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adduces, present participle adducing, past tense , past participle adduced
VERB 引证,援引,举出（事实、理由等） If you adduce something such as a fact or reason, you mention it in order to support an argument. 引证,援引,举出（事实、理由等） [formal]

  • We can adduce evidence to support the claim. [VERB noun]

    我们能举出证据支持该说法。

SYN mention, offer, name, present
