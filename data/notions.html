notion
noʊʃən ˈnoʊʃən
Word forms: plural notions
1 N-COUNT [NOUN that] 看法;观念;理念 A notion is an idea or belief about something. 看法;观念;理念

  • We each have a notion of just what kind of person we'd like to be. [+ of]

    我们每人都有一个自己想成为什么样的人的想法。

  • I reject absolutely the notion that privatisation of our industry is now inevitable. [+ that]

    我绝对不能接受我们这一产业的私有化目前已成必然的看法。

  • I'd had a few notions about being a journalist.

    我以前曾有过想当记者的念头。

SYN idea, view, opinion, belief  
2 N-PLURAL 针头线脑(指针线、纽扣、拉链等小件缝纫用品) Notions are small articles for sewing, such as buttons, zips, and thread. 针头线脑(指针线、纽扣、拉链等小件缝纫用品) [US]
