clock off
PHRASAL VERB (用考勤卡)记录下班的时间;打卡下班 When you clock off at work, you leave work or put a special card into a device to show what time you left. (用考勤卡)记录下班的时间;打卡下班

  • The Night Duty Officer was ready to clock off. [VERB PREP.]

    值夜班的警员准备打卡下班。

  • They clocked off duty and left at ten to three. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们打卡下班，于2点50分离开。
