aspiration
æspɪreɪʃən ˌæspɪˈreɪʃən ˌæspəˈreɪʃən
Word forms: plural aspirations
N-VAR 抱负；志向；渴望 Some-one's aspirations are their desire to achieve things. 抱负；志向；渴望

  • ...the needs and aspirations of our pupils.

    我们的学生的需要和志向

  • He is unlikely to send in the army to quell nationalist aspirations.

    他不可能派兵去扼杀民族主义理想。

  • ...the republic's aspiration to statehood.

    共和国想成为独立国家的愿望
