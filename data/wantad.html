want ad
Word forms: plural want ads
N-COUNT [usually plural] (报刊等上的)分类广告，招聘广告 The want ads in a newspaper or magazine are small advertisements, usually offering things for sale or offering jobs. (报刊等上的)分类广告，招聘广告 [mainly US]

  • I furnished my first apartment with items from the want ads.

    我从分类广告上购置物品，布置了我的第一间公寓。

  • Nearly everybody in the group agreed that in this job market the usual avenues, like searching the want ads, are useless.

    小组中几乎所有人都认为，在这个就业市场，搜寻招聘广告这类老路是走不通的。
