augment
ɔːgment ɔgˈmɛnt ˈɔgˌmɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense augments, present participle augmenting, past tense , past participle augmented
VERB 增加；增大；加强；提高 To augment something means to make it larger, stronger, or more effective by adding something to it. 增加；增大；加强；提高 [formal]

  • While searching for a way to augment the family income, she began making dolls. [VERB noun]

    为了想法子增加家庭收入，她开始制作洋娃娃。

SYN increase, grow, raise, extend
