share out
1 PHRASAL VERB 分摊;平分;均分;分配 If you share out an amount of something, you give each person in a group an equal or fair part of it. 分摊;平分;均分;分配

  • I drain the pasta, then I share it out between two plates. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我将意大利面滤干，然后平分到两个盘子里。

  • The company will share out $1.3 billion among 500,000 policyholders. [VERB PREP. noun]

    公司将在 500,000 名保险单持有人中分摊 13 亿美元。

  • ...a formula for sharing out power among the various clans. [V P n among/between pl-n]

    在各个宗亲中平分权力的方案

SYN divide up, distribute, allocate, hand out
