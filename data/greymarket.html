grey market
Word forms: plural grey markets
in AM, use 美国英语用 gray market
1 N-SING [oft NOUN n] 灰市，水货市场（经非正式渠道购买商品再以低于市价的价格售卖出去） Grey market goods are bought unofficially and then sold to customers at lower prices than usual. 灰市，水货市场（经非正式渠道购买商品再以低于市价的价格售卖出去） [business]

  • Grey-market perfumes and toiletries are now commonly sold by mail.

    水货香水和化妆品现在常常通过邮购方式售卖。

2 N-SING [oft NOUN n] （证券）灰色市场，暗盘市场（指公开发行前将股票售给投资人） Grey market shares are sold to investors before they have been officially issued. （证券）灰色市场，暗盘市场（指公开发行前将股票售给投资人） [business]

  • At one point last week shares in the grey market touched 230p.

    上周，灰市上的股票价格一度高达230便士。
