unresponsive
ʌnrɪspɒnsɪv ˌʌnrɪˈspɒnsɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 没有回应的；漠不关心的 An unresponsive person does not react or pay enough attention to something, for example to an urgent situation or to people's needs. 没有回应的；漠不关心的 [formal]

  • He was totally unresponsive to the pressing social and economic needs of the majority of the population. [+ to]

    他对大多数人迫切的社会和经济需求漠不关心。

  • ...a cold, unresponsive man.

    冷淡的、对他人漠不关心的男人

2 ADJ 一动不动的；没反应的 If a person or their body is unresponsive, they do not react physically in a normal way, or do not make any movements. 一动不动的；没反应的 [formal]

  • I found her in a coma, totally unresponsive.

    我发现她昏倒了，完全失去了知觉。
