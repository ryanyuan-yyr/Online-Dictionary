upturned
ʌptɜːʳnd ʌpˈtɜːnd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 朝上的；朝上翘的；向上翻的 Something that is upturned points upwards. 朝上的；朝上翘的；向上翻的

  • ...the rain splashing down on her upturned face.

    溅落到她仰起的脸上的雨水

  • ...his eyes closed and his palms upturned.

    他闭上眼睛，掌心向上。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 翻转的；倒置的 Something that is upturned is upside down. 翻转的；倒置的

  • ...upturned buckets.

    倒扣的水桶

  • He clung to the upturned boat, screaming for help.

    他紧紧抓着住倾覆的小船，高呼救命。
