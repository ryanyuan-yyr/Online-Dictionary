forested
fɒrɪstɪd fɔːr- ˈfɔrɪstəd ˈfɑrɪstəd
ADJ-GRADED 遍地树木的;森林覆盖的 A forested area is an area covered in trees growing closely together. 遍地树木的;森林覆盖的

  • The road snaked through forested mountains, past the village of San Antonio.

    这条路在林木覆盖的山上蜿蜒前行，经过圣安东尼奥村。

  • ...a thickly forested valley.

    森林密布的山谷

  • Only 8 per cent of Britain is forested.

    英国的森林覆盖率只有 8%。
