pull together
1 PHRASAL VERB 齐心协力；团结起来；通力合作 If people pull together, they help each other or work together in order to deal with a difficult situation. 齐心协力；团结起来；通力合作

  • The nation was urged to pull together to avoid a slide into complete chaos. [VERB PREP.]

    对全国上下发出了呼吁，要求大家团结一致，避免陷入完全混乱的局面。

  • They would be far better off, materially and emotionally, if they all pulled together. [VERB PREP.]

    如果他们所有人都团结一致，他们在物质上和感情上的境况都将大为改善。

2 PHRASAL VERB 保持镇定；克制自己；振作起来 If you are upset or depressed and someone tells you to pull yourself together, they are telling you to control your feelings and behave calmly again. 保持镇定；克制自己；振作起来

  • Pull yourself together, you stupid woman!.

    冷静点，你这个蠢女人！

  • He pulled himself together, as always, by throwing himself back into his work. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    他和往常一样，重新投入工作使自己振作了起来。

3 PHRASAL VERB 把…串到一起；把…连成一个整体 If you pull together different facts or ideas, you link them to form a single theory, argument, or story. 把…串到一起；把…连成一个整体 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Let me now pull together the threads of my argument. [VERB PREP. noun]

    现在让我来将我的论点串起来。

  • Data exists but it needs pulling together. [VERB PREP.]

    信息是有，但需要整理。
