jeweller
dʒuːələʳ
Word forms: plural jewellers
in AM, use 美国英语用 jeweler
1 N-COUNT 珠宝商；钟表商；宝石匠；钟表匠 A jeweller is a person who makes, sells, and repairs jewellery and watches. 珠宝商；钟表商；宝石匠；钟表匠
2 N-COUNT 钟表首饰店；珠宝店 A jeweller or a jeweller's is a shop where jewellery and watches are made, sold, and repaired. 钟表首饰店；珠宝店
