confection
kənfekʃən kənˈfɛkʃən
Word forms: plural confections
1 N-COUNT 甜食;甜点 You can refer to a sweet food that someone has made as a confection . 甜食;甜点 [written]

  • ...a confection made with honey and nuts.

    用蜂蜜和坚果制成的甜食

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n] 制作精细的物品 A confection is something that has been made or built and is very complicated or decorated. 制作精细的物品 [written]

  • He found himself staring at an extraordinary architectural confection of old and new.

    他发现自己盯着一座新旧结合的建筑精品看出了神。
