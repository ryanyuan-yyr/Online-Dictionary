beef
biːf bif
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense beefs, present participle beefing, past tense , past participle beefed
1 N-UNCOUNT 牛肉 Beef is the meat of a cow, bull, or ox. 牛肉

  • ...roast beef.

    烤牛肉

  • ...beef stew.

    炖牛肉

  • ...exports of beef and powdered milk.

    牛肉和奶粉的出口

2See also:corned beef 
3 VERB [no passive] 不断抱怨；大发牢骚 If someone beefs about something, they keep complaining about it. 不断抱怨；大发牢骚 [informal, disapproval] [informal]

  • Instead of beefing about what Mrs Martin has not done, her critics might take a look at what she is trying to do.

    批评者不要老是抱怨马丁太太没有做什么事情，不妨看看她正要做什么事情。

Beef is also a noun.

  • I really don't have a beef with Wayne.

    我对韦恩真的没有什么不满的。

Phrasal verbs:
beef up
