safe conduct seɪf ˈkɒndʌkt
(also safe-conduct)
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 安全通行许可;安全通行证;安全通行权 If you are given safe conduct, the authorities officially allow you to travel somewhere, guaranteeing that you will not be arrested or harmed while doing so. 安全通行许可;安全通行证;安全通行权
