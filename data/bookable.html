bookable
bʊkəbəl ˈbʊkəbəl
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] （剧院座位或机票等）可预订的 If something such as a theatre seat or plane ticket is bookable, it can be booked in advance. （剧院座位或机票等）可预订的 [mainly British]

  • Tours leave from Palma and are bookable at some hotels or any travel agency.

    旅行从帕尔马开始，可以在一些旅馆或任何旅行社预订这些旅游线路。

2 ADJ （运动员犯规）可被记名警告的 In sports such as football, a bookable offence is an action for which a player can be officially warned by the referee. （运动员犯规）可被记名警告的

  • Both men were dismissed for a second bookable offence.

    两人由于第二次吃到黄牌而双双被罚下场。
