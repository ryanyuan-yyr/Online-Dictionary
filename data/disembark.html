disembark
dɪsɪmbɑːʳk ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑrk ˈdɪsɪmˌbærk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disembarks, present participle disembarking, past tense , past participle disembarked
VERB

  • When passengers disembark from a ship, aeroplane, or bus, they leave it at the end of their journey.

    离船;下飞机;下车

  • I looked towards the plane. Six passengers had already disembarked. [VERB]

    我朝飞机处望去，已有6名乘客下来了。
