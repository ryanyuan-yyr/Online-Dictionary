top-heavy
ˈtɑpˌhɛvi
1 ADJ-GRADED 头重脚轻的;上重下轻的 Something that is top-heavy is larger or heavier at the top than at the bottom, and might therefore fall over. 头重脚轻的;上重下轻的

  • ...top-heavy flowers such as sunflowers.

    类似向日葵这样头重脚轻的花

2 ADJ-GRADED 高级官员过多的;行政管理人员过多的 If you describe a business or other organization as top-heavy, you mean that it has too many senior managers in relation to the number of junior managers or workers. 高级官员过多的;行政管理人员过多的 [disapproval]

  • ...top-heavy bureaucratic structures.

    高级官员过多的官僚体系
