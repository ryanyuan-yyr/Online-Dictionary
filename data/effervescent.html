effervescent
efəʳvesənt ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt
1 ADJ (液体)起泡的，冒泡的 An effervescent liquid is one that contains or releases bubbles of gas. (液体)起泡的，冒泡的

  • ...an effervescent mineral water.

    含汽矿泉水

SYN fizzy, bubbling, sparkling, bubbly  
2 ADJ-GRADED 活泼欢快的;热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的 If you describe someone as effervescent, you mean that they are lively, entertaining, enthusiastic, and exciting. 活泼欢快的;热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的 [approval]

  • ...an effervescent blonde actress.

    热情活泼的金发女演员

  • America is the most intellectually, artistically and politically effervescent of nations.

    美国是学术、艺术和政治氛围最活跃的国家。

SYN lively, excited, dynamic, enthusiastic  
effervescence

  • He wrote about Gillespie's effervescence, magnetism and commitment.

    他写到了吉莱斯皮的活泼、魅力和献身精神。
