unbridled
ʌnbraɪdəld ʌnˈbraɪdəld
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无约束的；放肆的；放纵的 If you describe behaviour or feelings as unbridled, you mean that they are not controlled or limited in any way. 无约束的；放肆的；放纵的

  • ...the unbridled greed of the 1980s.

    20世纪80年代贪得无厌的社会风气

  • ...a tale of lust and unbridled passion.

    关于纵情乱性的故事

SYN unrestrained, uncontrolled, unchecked, violent
