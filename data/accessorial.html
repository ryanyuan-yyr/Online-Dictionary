accessory
æksesəri ækˈsɛsəri əkˈsɛsəri əˈsɛsəri
Word forms: plural accessories
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 附件;配件;附属物 Accessories are items of equipment that are not usually essential, but which can be used with or added to something else in order to make it more efficient, useful, or decorative. 附件;配件;附属物

  • ...an exclusive range of hand-made bedroom and bathroom accessories.

    独家经营的一系列手工卧室和浴室配件

SYN extra, addition, supplement, convenience  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （衣服的）配饰（如腰带、围巾等） Accessories are articles such as belts and scarves which you wear or carry but which are not part of your main clothing. （衣服的）配饰（如腰带、围巾等）
3 N-COUNT 同谋；帮凶；从犯 If someone is guilty of being an accessory to a crime, they helped the person who committed it, or knew it was being committed but did not tell the police. 同谋；帮凶；从犯 [law]

  • She was charged with being an accessory to the embezzlement of funds. [+ to]

    她被指控为这起挪用公款案的从犯。

SYN accomplice, partner, ally, associate  
4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 附属的；辅助的；非主要的 You can use accessory to describe something which is part of an activity or process, but not the most essential or important part of it. 附属的；辅助的；非主要的 [formal]

  • Forster established the essentiality of minerals as accessory food factors required for maintaining life.

    福斯特证实了矿物质作为辅助食物因子在维持生命方面的重要性。

SYN supplementary, aiding, extra, additional
