retort
rɪtɔːʳt rɪˈtɔrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense retorts, present participle retorting, past tense , past participle retorted
VERB 反驳;回嘴 To retort means to reply angrily to someone. 反驳;回嘴 [written] [Also VERB]

  • Was he afraid, he was asked. 'Afraid of what?' he retorted. [VERB with quote]

    有人问他是否害怕。“害怕什么？”他反问道。

  • Others retort that strong central power is a dangerous thing in Russia. [VERB that]

    其他人反驳说强大的中央集权在俄罗斯是很危险的。

Retort is also a noun.

  • His sharp retort clearly made an impact.

    他尖刻的反驳显然起了作用。
