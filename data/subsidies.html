subsidy
sʌbsɪdi ˈsʌbsədi
Word forms: plural subsidies
N-COUNT 补贴;补助金;津贴 A subsidy is money that is paid by a government or other authority in order to help an industry or business, or to pay for a public service. 补贴;补助金;津贴

  • European farmers are planning a massive demonstration against farm subsidy cuts.

    欧洲农场主正策划一场抗议削减农场补贴的大规模示威游行。

  • They've also slashed state subsidies to utilities and transportation.

    他们还大幅削减了对公共事业和交通运输的政府补贴。

SYN aid, help, support, grant
