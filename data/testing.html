testing
testɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED (问题、局势)难对付的，考验人的，棘手的 A testing problem or situation is very difficult to deal with and shows a lot about the character of the person who is dealing with it. (问题、局势)难对付的，考验人的，棘手的

  • The most testing time is undoubtedly in the early months of your return to work.

    最考验人的时期莫过于重新回来上班的头几个月。

  • The papers in maths and English are very testing.

    数学试卷和英语试卷很难。

SYN difficult, trying, demanding, taxing  
2 N-UNCOUNT 检验;试验;检测 Testing is the activity of testing something or someone in order to find out information. 检验;试验;检测

  • ...product testing and labelling.

    检验产品和给产品贴标签

  • The National Collegiate Athletic Association introduced drug testing in the mid-1980s.

    美国大学体育联合会在 20 世纪 80 年代中期开始实行药物检测。
