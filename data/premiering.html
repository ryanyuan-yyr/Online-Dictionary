premiere
premieəʳ ˈprɛmɪˌɛə ˈprɛmɪə prɪmjer
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense premieres, present participle premiering, past tense , past participle premiered
1 N-COUNT 首映；首演；初次公演 The premiere of a new play or film is the first public performance of it. 首映；首演；初次公演

  • A new Czechoslovak film has had its premiere at the Karlovy Vary film festival.

    一部捷克斯洛伐克新电影在卡罗维发利电影节上首映。

  • ...a royal film premiere.

    盛大的电影首映

SYN first night, opening, debut, first showing  
2 V-ERG （使）首映；（使）首演；（使）初次公演 When a film or show premieres or is premiered, it is shown to an audience for the first time. （使）首映；（使）首演；（使）初次公演

  • The documentary premiered at the Jerusalem Film Festival. [VERB]

    这部纪录片在耶路撒冷电影节首映。

  • The opera is due to be premiered by ENO next year. [be VERB-ed]

    这部歌剧将于明年在英国国家歌剧院首演。

SYN open, debut, be shown
