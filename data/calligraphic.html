calligraphy
kəlɪgrəfi kəˈlɪgrəfi
1 N-UNCOUNT 书法艺术 Calligraphy is the art of producing beautiful handwriting using a brush or a special pen. 书法艺术
2 N-UNCOUNT 书法;书法作品;笔迹 Calligraphy is beautiful and artistic handwriting. 书法;书法作品;笔迹

  • Her calligraphy was the clearest I'd ever seen.

    她的书法是我见过的字迹最工整的。
