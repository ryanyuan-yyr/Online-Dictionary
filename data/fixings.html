fixings
fɪksɪŋz ˈfɪkˌsɪŋz fɪkˌsɪnz
1 N-PLURAL (尤指一顿饭中的)配菜，配料 Fixings are extra items that are used to decorate or complete something, especially a meal. (尤指一顿饭中的)配菜，配料 [US]

  • He bought a hot dog and had it covered with all the fixings.

    他买了一个热狗，在上面撒满了各色配菜。

2 N-PLURAL (钉子、螺丝等)配件，零件，元件 Fixings are items such as nails and screws which are used to fix things such as furniture together. (钉子、螺丝等)配件，零件，元件

  • Have you got all the screws and fixings you need?

    你有没有弄到需要的各种螺钉和配件？
