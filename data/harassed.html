harassed
hærəst həræst
ADJ-GRADED 焦虑的;疲惫的 If you are harassed, you are anxious and tense because you have too much to do or too many problems to cope with. 焦虑的;疲惫的

  • This morning, looking harassed and drawn, Lewis tendered his resignation.

    今天早晨，刘易斯面色憔悴、疲惫不堪地递交了辞呈。

SYN hassled [informal] , worried, troubled, strained
