spew
spjuː spju
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense spews, present participle spewing, past tense , past participle spewed
1 V-ERG (使)喷出;(使)放出;(使)涌出 When something spews out a substance or when a substance spews from something, the substance flows out quickly in large quantities. (使)喷出;(使)放出;(使)涌出

  • The volcano spewed out more scorching volcanic ashes, gases and rocks. [VERB noun with adv.]

    火山喷出更多灼热的火山灰、气体和岩块。

  • Leaking oil spewed from the tanker. [VERB prep.]

    泄漏的油从油轮里涌出。

  • An oil tanker spewed its cargo into the sea. [VERB noun prep.]

    一艘油轮将装载的油全都泄入了大海。

SYN shed, discharge, send out, issue  
2 VERB 呕吐 If someone spews or spews up, they vomit. 呕吐 [informal]

  • He's pissed. Let's get out of his way before he starts spewing. [VERB]

    他很恼火。趁他还没有发作，我们赶快离他远点儿。

SYN vomit, throw up [informal] , puke [slang] , chuck [Australian , New Zealand , informal]
