breadth
bretθ brɛdθ bredθ brɛdθ
1 N-UNCOUNT 宽度 The breadth of something is the distance between its two sides. 宽度

  • The breadth of the whole camp was 400 paces. [+ of]

    整个营地的宽度为400步。

SYN width, spread, beam, span  
2 N-UNCOUNT 广度；广泛性 The breadth of something is its quality of consisting of or involving many different things. 广度；广泛性

  • Older people have a tremendous breadth of experience. [+ of]

    年长者阅历相当丰富。

  • His breadth of knowledge filled me with admiration. [+ of]

    他学识渊博，令我非常钦佩。

SYN extent, area, reach, range  
3 PHRASE 到处；处处 If you say that someone does something or something happens throughout or across the length and breadth of a place, you are emphasizing that it happens everywhere in that place. 到处；处处 [emphasis]

  • The group built their reputation by playing across the length and breadth of North America.

    这个乐队通过到北美各地演出打响了知名度。

  • She has travelled the length and breadth of Britain.

    她游历了英国各地。
