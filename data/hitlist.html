hit list
Word forms: plural hit lists
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 打击名单;处理清单 If someone has a hit list of people or things, they are intending to take action concerning those people or things. 打击名单;处理清单

  • Some banks also have a hit list of people whom they threaten to sue for damages. [+ of]

    一些银行还列出一个黑名单，威胁要控告这些人，要求赔偿损失。

2 N-COUNT 暗杀名单 A hit list is a list that someone makes of people they intend to have killed. 暗杀名单
