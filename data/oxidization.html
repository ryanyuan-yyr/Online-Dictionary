oxidize
ɒksɪdaɪz ˈɑksɪˌdaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense oxidizes, present participle oxidizing, past tense , past participle oxidized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 oxidise
1 V-ERG （使）氧化 When a substance is oxidized or when it oxidizes, it changes chemically because of the effect of oxygen on it. （使）氧化

  • Aluminium is rapidly oxidized in air. [be VERB-ed]

    铝会在空气中迅速氧化。

  • The original white lead pigments have oxidized and turned black. [VERB]

    最初的白色铅质颜料氧化后变成了黑色。
