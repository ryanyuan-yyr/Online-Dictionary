shadowy
ʃædoʊi ˈʃædoʊi
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 阴暗的;幽暗的;被阴影笼罩的 A shadowy place is dark or full of shadows. 阴暗的;幽暗的;被阴影笼罩的

  • I watched him from a shadowy corner.

    我躲在一个阴暗的角落里观察他。

  • ...a broad, shadowy room.

    一间宽敞阴暗的房间

SYN dark, shaded, dim, gloomy  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 昏暗的;模糊的;蒙的 A shadowy figure or shape is someone or something that you can hardly see because they are in a dark place. 昏暗的;模糊的;蒙的

  • ...a tall, shadowy figure silhouetted against the pale wall.

    灰白的墙上映出一个高大而模糊的人影

  • ...the shadowy shape of a big barge loaded with logs.

    装满木材的巨大驳船的模糊轮廓

SYN vague, indistinct, faint, ghostly  
3 ADJ 鲜为人知的;神秘莫测的 You describe activities and people as shadowy when very little is known about them. 鲜为人知的;神秘莫测的

  • ...the shadowy world of spies.

    间谍的神秘世界

  • ...a shadowy group calling itself the People's Army.

    一个自称为“人民军队”的神秘组织
