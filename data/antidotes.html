antidote
æntidoʊt ˈæntəˌdoʊt
Word forms: plural antidotes
1 N-COUNT 解毒药；解毒剂 An antidote is a chemical substance that stops or controls the effect of a poison. 解毒药；解毒剂

  • When he returned, he noticed their sickness and prepared an antidote.

    他回来后注意到了他们的病状，于是配制了解毒剂。

SYN remedy, cure, preventive, corrective  
2 N-COUNT 矫正方法；对抗手段 Something that is an antidote to a difficult or unpleasant situation helps you to overcome the situation. 矫正方法；对抗手段

  • Massage is a wonderful antidote to stress. [+ to]

    按摩是舒缓压力的一剂妙方。

SYN cure, remedy, corrective, nostrum
