fine-tooth comb
(also fine tooth comb)
PHRASE 彻底搜查;仔细检查 If you say that you will go over something with a fine-tooth comb or go through something with a fine-tooth comb, you are emphasizing that you will search it thoroughly or examine it very carefully. 彻底搜查;仔细检查 [emphasis]

  • We went over that area with a fine-tooth comb.

    我们对那个地区进行了彻底的搜查。
