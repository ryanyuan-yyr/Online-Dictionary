afar
əfɑːʳ əˈfɑr
ADV [usu from ADV, oft ADVERB after verb] 在远处；从远处 Afar means a long way away. 在远处；从远处 [literary]

  • Seen from afar, its towering buildings beckon the visitor in.

    从远处看，它高耸的大楼召唤着拜访者进去。

  • ...a stranger who has loved her from afar for 23 years.

    一个暗恋了她23年的陌生人

SYN a distance, a long way away
