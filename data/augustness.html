august
ɔːgʌst ɔˈgʌst
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有威严的；令人敬畏的 Someone or something that is august is dignified and impressive. 有威严的；令人敬畏的 [formal]

  • Being so near the august person of the Empress, he was overcome with awe.

    如此靠近威严的女皇，他心中充满了敬畏。

  • ...the august surroundings of the Liberal Club.

    自由党俱乐部的庄严环境

SYN noble, great, kingly, grand  
August
ɔːgəst ˈɔgəst
Word forms: plural Augusts
N-VAR 八月 August is the eighth month of the year in the Western calendar. 八月

  • The world premiere took place in August 1956.

    全球首映是在1956年8月。

  • The trial will resume on August the twenty-second.

    审讯将于8月22日继续进行。

  • This August has been the wettest for four years.

    4年来就数今年8月雨水最多。
