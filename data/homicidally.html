ADJ [usually ADJ n] 可能会杀人的;嗜杀（成性）的 Homicidal is used to describe someone who is dangerous because they are likely to kill someone. 可能会杀人的;嗜杀（成性）的

  • That man is a homicidal maniac.

    那人是个杀人狂。

  • ...an explosion of homicidal rage.

    气急败坏的杀人情绪爆发

SYN murderous, deadly, lethal, maniacal
