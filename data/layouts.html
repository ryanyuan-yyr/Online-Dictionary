layout
leɪaʊt ˈleɪaʊt ˈleɪˌaʊt
Word forms: plural layouts
N-COUNT 布置；设计；布局 The layout of a garden, building, or piece of writing is the way in which the parts of it are arranged. 布置；设计；布局

  • He tried to recall the layout of the farmhouse. [+ of]

    他努力回想农舍的布局。

  • This boat has a good deck layout making everything easy to operate.

    这艘船的甲板布局合理，使得所有操控都很方便。

SYN arrangement, design, draft, outline
