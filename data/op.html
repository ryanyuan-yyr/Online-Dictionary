    乳腺癌手术

2 军事行动；作战 Ops are military operations. 军事行动；作战

  • Flt Lt Beamont had completed a 200 hour tour of ops in December 1941.

    空军上尉比蒙特在1941年12月完成了200小时的空战任务。

  • Fusilier Jones ran towards the Ops Room to find his lieutenant.

    燧发枪团士兵琼斯奔向作战指挥室寻找他的中尉长官。

  • Many network TV reporters began using photo-ops as a jumping-off point to examine issues more in depth.

    许多电视网记者开始将拍照时间作为更深层次地审视问题的出发点。
