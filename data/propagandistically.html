propagandist
prɒpəgændɪst
Word forms: plural propagandists
N-COUNT （常给出不精确信息的）宣传者，传播者 A propagandist is a person who tries to persuade people to support a particular idea or group, often by giving inaccurate information. （常给出不精确信息的）宣传者，传播者 [disapproval]

  • He was in large part a journalist and propagandist.

    他很大程度上是个新闻记者和宣传者。

  • He was also a brilliant propagandist for free trade. [+ for]

    他也是自由贸易杰出的倡导宣传者。

SYN publicist, advocate, promoter, proponent
