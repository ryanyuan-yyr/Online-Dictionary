contingency
kəntɪndʒənsi kənˈtɪndʒənsi
Word forms: plural contingencies
1 N-VAR 可能发生的事;不测之事;突发事件 A contingency is something that might happen in the future. 可能发生的事;不测之事;突发事件 [formal]

  • I need to examine all possible contingencies.

    我需要审视一切可能出现的情况。

SYN possibility, happening, chance, event  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (计划或措施)未雨绸缪的，应急的 A contingency plan or measure is one that is intended to be used if a possible situation actually occurs. (计划或措施)未雨绸缪的，应急的 [formal]

  • We have contingency plans.

    我们有应急方案。
