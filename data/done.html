done
dʌn dʌn
1 Done is the past participle of do .
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 完成的 A task or activity that is done has been completed successfully. 完成的

  • When her deal is done, the client emerges with her purchase.

    交易完成后，委托人会出现并将她购买的东西交给她。

3 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] (食物)煮熟的，烧好的 When something that you are cooking is done, it has been cooked long enough and is ready. (食物)煮熟的，烧好的

  • As soon as the cake is done, remove it from the oven.

    蛋糕一烤好就从炉子里取出来。

SYN cooked, ready, cooked enough, cooked to a turn  
4 CONVENTION (表示同意)好，行，一言为定 You say 'Done' when you are accepting a deal, arrangement, or bet that someone has offered to make with you. (表示同意)好，行，一言为定 [spoken, formulae]

  • 'You lead and we'll look for it.' — 'Done.'

    “你领头，我们来找。”—“行。”

5 PHRASE 完全了结的;彻底完成的 If you say that something is over and done with, you mean that it is completely finished and you do not have to think about it any more. 完全了结的;彻底完成的 [spoken]

  • Once this is all over and done with you can have a rest.

    大功告成后你就可以休息了。

SYN finished, done, over, ended
