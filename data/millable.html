mill
mɪl mɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense mills, present participle milling, past tense , past participle milled
1 N-COUNT 磨坊;磨粉厂 A mill is a building in which grain is crushed to make flour. 磨坊;磨粉厂
2 N-COUNT 磨粉机;碾磨器 A mill is a small device used for grinding something such as coffee beans or pepper into powder. 磨粉机;碾磨器

  • ...a pepper mill.

    胡椒碾磨器

SYN grinder, crusher, quern  
3 N-COUNT (制造加工钢材、羊毛、棉花等的)工厂 A mill is a factory used for making and processing materials such as steel, wool, or cotton. (制造加工钢材、羊毛、棉花等的)工厂

  • ...a steel mill.

    钢铁厂

  • ...a textile mill.

    纺织厂

SYN factory, works, shop, plant  
4 VERB (用磨粉机)碾碎，磨碎(麦子、胡椒等) To mill something such as wheat or pepper means to grind it in a mill. (用磨粉机)碾碎，磨碎(麦子、胡椒等)

  • They mill 1,000 tonnes of flour a day in every Australian state. [VERB noun]

    澳大利亚各州每天都要磨1,000吨面粉。

  • ...freshly milled black pepper. [VERB-ed]

    刚刚磨好的黑胡椒粉

SYN grind, pound, press, crush
