bonny
bɒni ˈbɑni
Word forms: comparative bonnier, superlative bonniest
ADJ-GRADED 漂亮的；好看的 Someone or something that is bonny is attractive and nice to look at. 漂亮的；好看的 [mainly Scottish, or Northern England]

  • Jemima was a bonny Highland lassie of 15.

    杰迈玛是一个15岁的美丽漂亮的高地少女。

SYN beautiful, pretty, fair, sweet
