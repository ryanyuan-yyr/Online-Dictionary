slip up
1 PHRASAL VERB 出现疏漏;出小差错 If you slip up, you make a small or unimportant mistake. 出现疏漏;出小差错

  • There were occasions when we slipped up. [VERB PREP.]

    有时候我们也会出错。

  • You will see exactly where you are slipping up. [VERB PREP.]

    你会确切地知道自己哪里出了错。

SYN make a mistake, go wrong, blunder, mistake
