slim down
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 精简(机构);裁减(人员) If a company or other organization slims down or is slimmed down, it employs fewer people, in order to save money or become more efficient. 精简(机构);裁减(人员) [business] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Many firms have had little choice but to slim down. [VERB PREP.]

    许多公司几乎别无选择，只能裁员。

  • ...the plan to slim down the coal industry. [VERB PREP. noun]

    精减煤炭从业人员的计划
