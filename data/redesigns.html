redesign
riːdɪzaɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redesigns, present participle redesigning, past tense , past participle redesigned
VERB 重新设计 If a building, vehicle, or system is redesigned, it is rebuilt according to a new design in order to improve it. 重新设计

  • The hotel has recently been redesigned and redecorated. [be VERB-ed]

    这家酒店最近重新设计和装修过。

  • The second step is to redesign the school system so that it produces a well-educated population. [VERB noun]

    第二步是重新设计学校体制以造就高素质的人口。

  • ...the completely redesigned car. [VERB-ed]

    彻底重新设计的车型

SYN reorganize, rearrange, recast, reshape
