nebulous
nebjələs ˈnɛbjələs
ADJ-GRADED 模糊的;蒙的 If you describe something as nebulous, you mean that it is vague and not clearly defined or not easy to describe. 模糊的;蒙的

  • The notions we children were able to form of the great world beyond were exceedingly nebulous.

    我们这些孩子所能形成的对外部世界的认识是非常模糊的。

  • Music is such a nebulous thing.

    音乐是如此蒙之物。
