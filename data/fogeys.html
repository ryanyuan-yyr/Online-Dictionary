fogey
foʊgi ˈfoʊgi
(also fogy)Word forms: plural fogies, plural fogeys
N-COUNT 守旧者;抱残守缺的人 If you describe someone as a fogey or an old fogey, you mean that they are boring and old-fashioned. 守旧者;抱残守缺的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • I don't want to sound like I'm some old fogy.

    我不想让自己听上去像一个老古董。

SYN fuddy-duddy [informal] , square [informal] , stick-in-the-mud [informal] , antique [informal]
