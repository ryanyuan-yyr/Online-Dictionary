inquire
ɪnkwaɪəʳ ɪnˈkwaɪr
(also enquire)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inquires, present participle inquiring, past tense , past participle inquired
1 VERB 询问;查询;打听 If you inquire about something, you ask for information about it. 询问;查询;打听 [formal] [Also V + for]

  • 'Is something wrong?' he enquired. [VERB with quote]

    “有什么不对劲吗?”他问道。

  • 'Who are you?' he enquired of the first man. [VERB + of]

    “你是谁?”他问第一个人。

  • I rang up to inquire about train times. [VERB + about]

    我打了电话查询列车时刻。

  • He inquired whether there had been any messages for him. [VERB wh]

    他询问是否有留给他的口信。

  • He was so impressed that he inquired the young shepherd's name. [VERB noun]

    他深受震动，就问那个年轻的牧羊人叫什么名字。

SYN ask, question, query, quiz  
2 VERB 调查;查究 If you inquire into something, you investigate it carefully. 调查;查究

  • Inspectors were appointed to inquire into the affairs of the company. [VERB + into]

    督查员受委派调查该公司的事务。
