giro
dʒaɪəroʊ
(also Giro)Word forms: plural giros
1 N-COUNT （政府提供给失业者或病人的）直接转账救济支票 In Britain, a giro or a giro cheque is a cheque that is given by the government to a person who is unemployed or ill. （政府提供给失业者或病人的）直接转账救济支票

  • He lived on an invalidity pension which came as a weekly giro.

    他靠每周领取的作为病残养老金的直接转账救济支票过日子。

2 N-UNCOUNT 银行（或邮局）直接转账体系 Giro is a system in which banks and post offices transfer money directly from one bank account to another using computers. 银行（或邮局）直接转账体系 [British]

  • There will be no further costs as long as the bank is part of the giro network.

    只要该银行属于直接转账系统，就不会额外收取费用。
