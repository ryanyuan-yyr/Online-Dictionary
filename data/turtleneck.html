turtleneck
tɜːʳtəlnek ˈtɜrtəlˌnɛk
Word forms: plural turtlenecks
1 N-COUNT 圆领套衫;低领套衫 A turtleneck or turtleneck sweater is a sweater with a short round collar that fits closely around your neck. 圆领套衫;低领套衫 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 mock turtleneck
2 N-COUNT 高翻领套衫 A turtleneck or turtleneck sweater is a sweater with a high neck which folds over. 高翻领套衫 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 polo neck
