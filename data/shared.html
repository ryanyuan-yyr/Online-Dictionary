share
ʃeəʳ ʃɛə ʃɛr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shares, present participle sharing, past tense , past participle shared
1 N-COUNT 股份;股票 A company's shares are the many equal parts into which its ownership is divided. Shares can be bought by people as an investment. 股份;股票 [business]

  • This is why Sir Colin Marshall, British Airways' chairman, has been so keen to buy shares in US-AIR. [+ in]

    这就是为什么英国航空公司主席科林·马歇尔爵士一直如此热衷于购买美国航空公司的股票。

  • For some months the share price remained fairly static.

    有那么几个月，股票价格一直非常地稳定。

2 V-RECIP 共享;共有;合用 If you share something with another person, you both have it, use it, or occupy it. You can also say that two people share something. 共享;共有;合用

  • ...the small income he had shared with his brother from his father's estate. [VERB noun + with]

    他兄弟二人从父亲那里继承的一小笔遗产

  • Two Americans will share this year's Nobel Prize for Medicine. [VERB noun]

    两名美国人将共同摘得今年的诺贝尔医学奖。

  • Scarce water resources are shared between states who cannot trust each other. [be V-ed + between]

    稀有的水资源被两个互不信任的国家所共享。

  • Most hostel tenants would prefer single to shared rooms. [VERB-ed]

    住在招待所的人大多更喜欢单人间，而不想和别人共住一间。

SYN divide, split, distribute, assign  
3 V-RECIP 共同承担，分担，分摊(任务、责任等) If you share a task, duty, or responsibility with someone, you each carry out or accept part of it. You can also say that two people share something. 共同承担，分担，分摊(任务、责任等)

  • You can find out whether they are prepared to share the cost of the flowers with you. [VERB noun + with]

    你可以弄清楚他们是否愿意和你一起分担买花的费用。

  • The republics have worked out a plan for sharing control of nuclear weapons. [VERB noun]

    一些共和国已经制订出共同控制核武器的计划。

4 V-RECIP 分享，共同拥有(同样的经历) If you share an experience with someone, you have the same experience, often because you are with them at the time. You can also say that two people share something. 分享，共同拥有(同样的经历)

  • Yes, I want to share my life with you. [VERB noun + with]

    是的，我想和你共度一生。

  • I felt we both shared the same sense of loss, felt the same pain. [VERB noun]

    我觉得我们两个有着共同的失落感，经受着同样的痛苦。

5 VERB [no cont] 同意;和…观点一致;赞同 If you share someone's opinion, you agree with them. 同意;和…观点一致;赞同

  • We share his view that business can be a positive force for change. [VERB noun]

    我们同意他的观点，认为商业可以是促进变革的一种积极力量。

  • Prosperity and economic success remain popular and broadly shared goals. [VERB-ed]

    经济繁荣昌盛一直是各国普遍追求的共同目标。

6 V-RECIP [no cont] 共同具有(某种品质和特点);和…有共同之处 If one person or thing shares a quality or characteristic with another, they have the same quality or characteristic. You can also say that two people or things share something. 共同具有(某种品质和特点);和…有共同之处

  • ...newspapers which share similar characteristics with certain British newspapers. [VERB noun + with]

    和英国的某些报纸风格相似的一些报纸

  • ...two groups who share a common language. [VERB noun]

    说同一种语言的两个人群

7 VERB 与…分享;与…共享 If you share something that you have with someone, you give some of it to them or let them use it. 与…分享;与…共享 [Also VERB]

  • The village tribe is friendly and they share their water supply with you. [VERB noun + with]

    村里的部落很友善，他们会和你一起分享水源。

  • Scientists now have to compete for funding, and do not share information among themselves. [V n + among]

    科学家们现在为了资金不得不相互竞争，互相之间也不会共享信息。

  • Toddlers are notoriously antisocial when it comes to sharing toys. [VERB noun]

    众所周知，刚学会走路的孩子不愿和别人分享玩具。

8 VERB 向…诉说,与…分享(想法、消息等) If you share something personal such as a thought or a piece of news with someone, you tell them about it. 向…诉说,与…分享(想法、消息等)

  • It can be beneficial to share your feelings with someone you trust. [VERB noun + with]

    向自己信任的人倾诉感情是很有益处的。

  • Film critic Bob Mondello shares his thoughts on the movie 'City of Hope'. [VERB noun]

    影评家鲍勃·蒙德洛和大家分享他对电影《希望之城》的看法。

9 N-COUNT [usually singular] (应得或应承担的)一份 If something is divided or distributed among a number of different people or things, each of them has, or is responsible for, a share of it. (应得或应承担的)一份 [Also + in]

  • Sara also pays a share of the gas, electricity and phone bills. [+ of]

    萨拉也分摊燃气费、电费和电话费。

  • He is counting on winning seats and perhaps a share in the new government of Macedonia.

    他正期望着能赢得一些席位，或许还能成为马其顿新政府的一员。

10 N-COUNT 应得的一份;应做的一份 If you have or do your share of something, you have or do an amount that seems reasonable to you, or to other people. 应得的一份;应做的一份

  • Women must receive their fair share of training for good-paying jobs. [+ of]

    女性必须获得她们理应得到的高薪工作培训。

  • I have had more than my full share of adventures.

    我已经有了太多丰富多彩的历险。

SYN part, portion, quota, ration
