rue
ruː ru
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rues, present participle ruing, past tense , past participle rued
1 VERB 对…感到后悔;懊悔 If you rue something that you have done, you are sorry that you did it, because it has had unpleasant results. 对…感到后悔;懊悔 [literary]

  • Tavare was probably ruing his decision. [VERB noun]

    塔瓦雷可能正在为自己作出的决定感到后悔。

SYN regret, mourn, grieve, lament  
2 PHRASE 后悔;遗憾;懊悔 If you rue the day that you did something, you are sorry that you did it, because it has had unpleasant results. 后悔;遗憾;懊悔 [literary] [literary]

  • You'll live to rue the day you said that to me, my girl.

    总有一天你会后悔对我说了那些话的，小姐。
