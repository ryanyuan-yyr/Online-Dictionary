empowerment
ɪmpaʊəʳmənt
N-UNCOUNT 授权 The empowerment of a person or group of people is the process of giving them power and status in a particular situation. 授权

  • This government believes very strongly in the empowerment of women. [+ of]

    这届政府坚决信奉女性应享有更大的权力。

SYN enabling, equipping, emancipation, enfranchising
