predatory
predətri -tɔːri ˈprɛdəˌtɔri
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （动物）捕食性的 Predatory animals live by killing other animals for food. （动物）捕食性的

  • ...predatory birds like the eagle.

    像鹰一样的捕食性动物

SYN hunting, ravening, carnivorous, rapacious  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 掠夺性的；弱肉强食的；损人利己的 Predatory people or organizations are eager to gain something out of someone else's weakness or suffering. 掠夺性的；弱肉强食的；损人利己的

  • People who run small businesses are frightened by the predatory behaviour of the banks.

    小公司经营者被银行的掠夺行为吓坏了。
