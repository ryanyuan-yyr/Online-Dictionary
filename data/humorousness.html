humorous
hjuːmərəs ˈhjumərəs ˈjumərəs
ADJ-GRADED 滑稽的;（尤指）幽默的,诙谐的 If someone or something is humorous, they are amusing, especially in a clever or witty way. 滑稽的;（尤指）幽默的,诙谐的

  • He was quite humorous, and I liked that about him.

    他很幽默，我喜欢他这一点。

  • ...a humorous magazine.

    幽默杂志

SYN funny, comic, amusing, entertaining  
humorously

  • He looked at me humorously as he wrestled with the door.

    他一边和那扇门较着劲，一边表情滑稽地看着我。

  • Occasionally he made a humorously sardonic remark.

    他时不时说一句幽默嘲讽的话。
