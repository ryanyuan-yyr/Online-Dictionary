thumping
θʌmpɪŋ ˈθʌmpɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 极大的;了不起的;严重的 Thumping is used to emphasize that something is very great or severe. 极大的;了不起的;严重的 [British, informal, emphasis]

  • The Right has a thumping majority.

    右派占绝大多数。

  • The gloom deepened after a thumping £45m loss at British Rail.

    英国铁路公司遭受了 4,500 万英镑的巨额损失后境况更加惨淡。

SYN huge, massive, enormous, great  
Thumping is also an adverb.

  • A thumping good time was had by all.

    大家都很尽兴。
