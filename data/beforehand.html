beforehand
bɪfɔːʳhænd biˈfɔrˌhænd bɪˈfɔrˌhænd
ADV [usually ADVERB after verb] 预先；事先；提前 If you do something beforehand, you do it earlier than a particular event. 预先；事先；提前

  • How could she tell beforehand that I was going to go out?.

    她怎么会事先知道我要出去？

  • Saunas can be hazardous if misused. Avoid a big meal beforehand.

    洗桑拿浴如果方法不当会有危险，浴前切忌饱食。

SYN in advance, before, earlier, already
