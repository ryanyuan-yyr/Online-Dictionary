lacklustre
læklʌstəʳ
in AM, use 美国英语用 lackluster
ADJ-GRADED 无生气的；无活力的；单调乏味的 If you describe something or someone as lacklustre, you mean that they are not exciting or energetic. 无生气的；无活力的；单调乏味的

  • He has already been blamed for his party's lackluster performance during the election campaign.

    他的政党在竞选活动中表现平平，他因此已受到指责。

SYN flat, boring, dull, dim
