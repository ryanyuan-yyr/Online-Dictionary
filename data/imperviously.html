impervious
ɪmpɜːʳviəs ɪmˈpɜːvɪəbəl ɪmˈpɜrviəs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 无动于衷的;不受影响的 If you are impervious to someone's actions, you are not affected or influenced by them. 无动于衷的;不受影响的

  • She seems almost impervious to the criticism from all sides. [+ to]

    对于来自各方的指责,她似乎无动于衷。

  • The political system there has been impervious to all suggestions of change.

    那里的政治体制对于所有的改革建议一概置之不理。

2 ADJ 防渗漏的;不能渗透的;不能穿过的 Something that is impervious to water, heat, or a particular object is able to resist it or stop it passing through it. 防渗漏的;不能渗透的;不能穿过的

  • The floorcovering you select will need to be impervious to water. [+ to]

    挑选的地板材料必须是防水的。

  • ...a layer of impervious rock.

    一层防渗漏的岩石

SYN resistant, sealed, impenetrable, invulnerable
