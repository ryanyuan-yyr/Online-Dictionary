knee-jerk
ˈniˌdʒɜrk
ADJ [ADJ n] 未经思考作出的；依靠本能作出的 If you call someone's response to a question or situation a knee-jerk reaction, you mean that they react in a very predictable way, without thinking. 未经思考作出的；依靠本能作出的 [disapproval]

  • The knee-jerk reaction to this is to call for proper security in all hospitals.

    对此类状况的第一反应会是要求所有医院都采取适当的安全措施。

SYN automatic, unconscious, predictable, spontaneous
