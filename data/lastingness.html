lasting
lɑːstɪŋ læst- ˈlæstɪŋ
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 持续的；持久的；长久的 You can use lasting to describe a situation, result, or agreement that continues to exist or have an effect for a very long time. 持续的；持久的；长久的

  • We are well on our way to a lasting peace.

    我们已在实现持久和平上取得很大进展。

  • She left a lasting impression on him.

    她给他留下了难以磨灭的印象。

  • ...but there was no lasting damage.

    不过没有造成长久的损害

SYN continuing, long-term, permanent, enduring
