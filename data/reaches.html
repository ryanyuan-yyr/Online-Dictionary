reaches
riːtʃiz ˈriːtʃɪz
1 N-PLURAL 河段;流域;（上、中或下）游 The upper, middle, or lower reaches of a river are parts of a river. The upper reaches are nearer to the river's source and the lower reaches are nearer to the sea into which it flows. 河段;流域;（上、中或下）游

  • This year water levels in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze are unusually high. [+ of]

    长江今年中下游的水位都过高。

2 N-PLURAL （外）围；（远）处 You can refer to the distant or outer parts of a place or area as the far, farthest, or outer reaches. （外）围；（远）处 [formal]

  • ...the outer reaches of the solar system. [+ of]

    太阳系的外围

  • ...the farthest reaches of Cambodia.

    柬埔寨的最边远地区

3 N-PLURAL （组织或机构中的）层级 You can refer to the higher or lower levels of an organization as its upper or lower reaches. （组织或机构中的）层级 [formal]

  • ...the upper reaches of the legal profession. [+ of]

    法律行业的上层

  • ...the lower reaches of the accounting world.

    会计行业中较低的层级
