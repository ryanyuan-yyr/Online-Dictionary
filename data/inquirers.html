inquirer
ɪnkwaɪərəʳ
(also enquirer)Word forms: plural inquirers
1 N-COUNT 询问者;查询者 An inquirer is a person who asks for information about something or someone. 询问者;查询者 [formal]

  • I send each inquirer a packet of information.

    我给每位查询者发送了一份资料。

2 N-IN-NAMES (用于某些报刊名称) Inquirer is used in the names of some newspapers and magazines. (用于某些报刊名称)

  • ...the National Enquirer.

    《国家询问者》
