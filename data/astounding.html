astounding
əstaʊndɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人惊骇的；令人震惊的 If something is astounding, you are shocked or amazed that it could exist or happen. 令人惊骇的；令人震惊的

  • The results are quite astounding.

    结果令人无比震惊。

SYN amazing, striking, surprising, brilliant  
astoundingly

  • ...astoundingly blue eyes.

    蓝得惊人的一双眼睛

  • Astoundingly, an American had won the Tour de France.

    令人惊讶的是，一个美国人获得了环法自行车赛的冠军。
