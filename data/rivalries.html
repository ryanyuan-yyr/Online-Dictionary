rivalry
raɪvəlri ˈraɪvəlri
Word forms: plural rivalries
N-VAR 竞争;敌对 Rivalry is competition or fighting between people, businesses, or organizations who are in the same area or want the same things. 竞争;敌对

  • ...the rivalry between the Inkatha and the ANC. [+ between]

    英卡塔自由党和非洲国民大会之间的敌对

  • He had a lot of rivalry with his brothers and sisters.

    他和兄弟姐妹间经常较劲。

  • ...a city torn by deep ethnic rivalries.

    被深厚的种族仇恨弄得四分五裂的城市

SYN competition, competitiveness, vying, opposition
