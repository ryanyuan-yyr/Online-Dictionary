aver
əvɜːʳ əˈvɜr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense avers, present participle averring, past tense , past participle averred
VERB 断言;坚称 If you aver that something is the case, you say very firmly that it is true. 断言;坚称 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • He avers that chaos will erupt if he loses. [VERB that]

    他断言，如果他失败将会爆发动乱。

  • 'Enter-taining is something that everyone in the country can enjoy,' she averred. [VERB with quote]

    “娱乐消遣是每个国民都能享受的事，”她坚定地说。

SYN state, say, maintain, declare
