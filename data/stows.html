stow
stoʊ stoʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stows, present participle stowing, past tense , past participle stowed
VERB 保存;妥善放置 If you stow something somewhere, you carefully put it there until it is needed. 保存;妥善放置 [Also VERB n]

  • I helped her stow her bags in the boot of the car. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    我帮她将包放进汽车行李箱里。

  • Alistair had carefully folded up the paper and stowed it away under the sacks. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    阿利斯泰尔已小心翼翼地将那张报纸折起，放在厚纸袋下面。

SYN pack, load, put away, store  
Phrasal verbs:
stow away
