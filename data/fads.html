fad
fæd fæd
Word forms: plural fads
N-COUNT 一时的风尚;风靡一时之物 You use fad to refer to an activity or topic of interest that is very popular for a short time, but which people become bored with very quickly. 一时的风尚;风靡一时之物

  • Hamnett does not believe environmental concern is a passing fad.

    哈姆内特不相信对环境的关注是昙花一现。

SYN craze, fashion, trend, fancy
