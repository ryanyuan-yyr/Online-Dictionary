valet
væleɪ -lɪt ˈvælɪt ˈvæleɪ væˈleɪ ˈvæleɪ ˈvælɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense valets, present participle valeting, past tense , past participle valeted
1 N-COUNT （男子的）贴身男仆,男仆从 A valet is a male servant who looks after his employer by doing things such as caring for his clothes and cooking for him. （男子的）贴身男仆,男仆从
SYN manservant, man, attendant, gentleman's gentleman  
2 VERB (受雇）洗（车） If someone valets a vehicle, they are paid to clean it thoroughly inside and out. (受雇）洗（车）
