foment
foʊment fəˈmɛnt foʊˈmɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense foments, present participle fomenting, past tense , past participle fomented
VERB 煽动，助长(事端或暴力) If someone or something foments trouble or violent opposition, they cause it to develop. 煽动，助长(事端或暴力) [formal]

  • They accused strike leaders of fomenting violence. [VERB noun]

    他们指责罢工领导人煽动暴力。

SYN stir up, raise, encourage, promote
