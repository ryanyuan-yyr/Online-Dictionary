slightly
slaɪtli
ADV [ADVERB adjective] [ADVERB with verb] 略微;稍微 Slightly means to some degree but not to a very large degree. 略微;稍微

  • His family then moved to a slightly larger house.

    然后他全家搬到了一个稍微大一点的房子里。

  • They will be slightly more expensive but they last a lot longer.

    它们的价钱要贵一些，可是更加耐用。

  • Each person learns in a slightly different way.

    每个人的学习方法都稍有不同。

  • You can adjust it slightly.

    你可以稍加调整。

  • The best activities for stamina are fairly energetic; they need to get you slightly out of breath.

    最能够锻炼耐力的是那些需要一定体能、能让人呼吸稍感急促的活动。

SYN a little, a bit, somewhat, moderately
