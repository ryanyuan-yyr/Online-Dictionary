laudatory
lɔːdətri -tɔːri ˈlɔdəˌtɔri
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （文章或言谈）表示赞美的，颂扬性的 A laudatory piece of writing or speech expresses praise or admiration for someone. （文章或言谈）表示赞美的，颂扬性的 [formal]

  • The New York Times has this very laudatory article about your retirement.

    《纽约时报》发文谈及你的隐退，文中对你大加赞扬。

  • Beth spoke of Dr. Hammer in laudatory terms.

    贝丝谈及哈默医生时赞不绝口。

SYN eulogistic, approving, complimentary, adulatory
