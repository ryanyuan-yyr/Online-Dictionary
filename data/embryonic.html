embryonic
embriɒnɪk ˈɛmbrɪənəl ˌɛmbriˈɑnɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 早期的;处于萌芽阶段的;胚胎期的 An embryonic process, idea, organization, or organism is one at a very early stage in its development. 早期的;处于萌芽阶段的;胚胎期的 [formal]

  • ...Romania's embryonic democracy.

    罗马尼亚处于萌芽阶段的民主政体

  • ...the embryonic European central bank.

    处于酝酿阶段的欧洲中央银行

  • ...embryonic plant cells.

    植物胚胎细胞

SYN rudimentary, early, beginning, primary
