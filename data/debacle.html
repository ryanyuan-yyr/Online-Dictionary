debacle
deɪbɑːkəl dɪb- dɪˈbɑkəl dɪˈbækəl deɪˈbɑkəl
Word forms: plural debacles
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 débâcle
N-COUNT 彻底的失败;惨败 A debacle is an event or attempt that is a complete failure. 彻底的失败;惨败

  • After the debacle of the war the world was never the same again. [+ of]

    经历了这场战争的惨败之后，世界再也不是原来的那个样子了。

  • The convention was a debacle.

    这次大会开得非常失败。

SYN disaster, catastrophe, fiasco
