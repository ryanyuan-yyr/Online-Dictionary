change down
PHRASAL VERB 减挡;挂低速挡 When you change down, you move the gear lever in the vehicle you are driving in order to use a lower gear. 减挡;挂低速挡 [British]

  • Changing down, he turned into the drive. [VERB PREP.]

    他减挡拐入了私家车道。

  • I braked at the second corner and changed down to third. [VERB PREP. + to]

    我在第二个拐弯处踩了刹车，挂上三挡。
