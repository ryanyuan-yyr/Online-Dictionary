depute
dɪpjuːt diˈpjut dɪˈpjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deputes, present participle deputing, past tense , past participle deputed
VERB [usually passive] 委派…为代表 If you are deputed to do something, someone tells or allows you to do it on their behalf. 委派…为代表 [formal]

  • A sub-committee was deputed to investigate the claims. [be VERB-ed to-infinitive]

    一个委员会专门小组受委派调查这些说法。

SYN appoint, choose, commission, select
