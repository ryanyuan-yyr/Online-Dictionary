crystal
krɪstəl ˈkrɪstəl
Word forms: plural crystals
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 结晶;晶体 A crystal is a small piece of a substance that has formed naturally into a regular symmetrical shape. 结晶;晶体

  • ...salt crystals.

    盐晶体

  • ...ice crystals.

    冰晶

  • ...a single crystal of silicon.

    硅的单晶体

2See also:liquid crystal  liquid crystal display 
3 N-VAR 水晶 Crystal is a transparent rock that is used to make jewellery and ornaments. 水晶

  • ...a strand of crystal beads.

    一串水晶珠子

4 N-UNCOUNT 水晶玻璃 Crystal is a high quality glass, usually with patterns cut into its surface. 水晶玻璃

  • Some of the finest drinking glasses are made from lead crystal.

    一些最高档的酒杯是用铅晶质玻璃制成的。

  • ...crystal glasses.

    水晶玻璃杯

  • ...an immense crystal chandelier.

    巨大的水晶玻璃枝形吊灯

5 N-UNCOUNT 水晶玻璃杯;水晶玻璃容器 Glasses and other containers made of crystal are referred to as crystal . 水晶玻璃杯;水晶玻璃容器

  • Get out your best china and crystal.

    拿出你最好的瓷器和水晶玻璃器皿。
