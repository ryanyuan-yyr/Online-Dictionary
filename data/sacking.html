sacking
sækɪŋ ˈsækɪŋ
Word forms: plural sackings
1 N-UNCOUNT 麻袋布;粗麻布 Sacking is rough woven material that is used to make sacks. 麻袋布;粗麻布
2 N-COUNT 解雇;开除;炒鱿鱼 A sacking is when an employer tells a worker to leave their job. 解雇;开除;炒鱿鱼 [business]

  • ...the sacking of twenty-three thousand miners. [+ of]

    23,000 名矿工被解雇
