game plan
(also game-plan)Word forms: plural game plans
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] （体育比赛中运动队的）战术，对策 In sport, a team's game plan is their plan for winning a match. （体育比赛中运动队的）战术，对策

  • Leeds kept quiet, stuck to their game plan and quietly racked up the points.

    利兹队毫不张扬，坚持自己的战术，不知不觉中得分在增加。

2 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 行动计划；策略；方针 Someone's game plan is the actions they intend to take and the policies they intend to adopt in order to achieve a particular thing. 行动计划；策略；方针

  • If he has a game plan for winning the deal, only he understands it.

    假如他有拿下这笔交易的策略，也只有他自己才明白。

  • He is unlikely to alter his game plan.

    他不大可能改变自己的策略。
