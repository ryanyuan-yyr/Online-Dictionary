foodie
fuːdi ˈfudi
(also foody)Word forms: plural foodies
N-COUNT 美食家;老饕 Foodies are people who enjoy cooking and eating different kinds of food. 美食家;老饕 [informal]

  • Other neighbourhoods in the city offer foodies a choice of Chinese, Portuguese or Greek food.

    城里的其他街区为食客们提供了中国菜、葡式菜、希腊菜等多种选择。

SYN gourmet, connoisseur, bon viveur, gourmand
