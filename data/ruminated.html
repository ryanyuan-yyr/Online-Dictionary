ruminate
ruːmɪneɪt ˈruməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ruminates, present participle ruminating, past tense , past participle ruminated
1 VERB 沉思;琢磨;认真考虑 If you ruminate on something, you think about it very carefully. 沉思;琢磨;认真考虑 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • He ruminated on the terrible wastage that typified American life. [V + on/about/over]

    他认真地考虑了美国生活中典型的严重浪费问题。

  • Obsessional personalities commonly ruminate excessively about death. [V on/about/over n]

    有强迫症的人通常过多地思考死亡的问题。

2 VERB （动物）反刍 When animals ruminate, they bring food back from their stomach into their mouth and chew it again. （动物）反刍 [technical]

  • He wanted to have a look at the two oxen, both ruminating without raising their eyes. [VERB]

    他想看看那两头牛，它们都在反刍，眼睛都不抬一下。
