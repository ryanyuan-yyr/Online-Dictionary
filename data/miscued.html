miscue
mɪskyu ˌmɪsˈkjuː ˈmɪsˌkju mɪsˈkju
Word forms: miscues, miscueing, miscued
1 N-COUNT (球类运动中的)失误; 撞歪; 滑杆In sports such as American football, baseball, and billiards, a miscue is an instance of failing to kick, hit, or strike the ball properly. (球类运动中的)失误; 撞歪; 滑杆 [US]
2 V-ERG (美式足球中)踢球失误In American football, if a player miscues a shot, or if he miscues, he fails to kick the ball properly. (美式足球中)踢球失误

  • The Bulgarian slightly miscued his shot and McGuire hooked it away.

    那个保加利亚人踢球时出现失误，麦奎尔一个倒勾把球踢开了。

  • Tresor Kandol miscued from six yards.

    特雷索·坎多尔在6码远的地方踢球失误。

3 N-COUNT 踢球失误Miscue is also a noun. 踢球失误

  • It was tough on the visitors because the goal stemmed from Ryan Lowe's miscue.

    观众难以接受的是，这个进球是因瑞恩·洛的踢球失误造成的。
