humanly
hjuːmənli ˈhjumənli ˈjumənli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective/-ed, ADVERB after verb] 对人而言地;与人相关地 Humanly means relating to human beings. 对人而言地;与人相关地

  • A mother is not allowed to be humanly flawed; she has to be perfect.

    作为母亲不容许有常人的缺点，她必须十全十美。

2 PHRASE 人力能及的;尽最大努力的 If something is humanly possible, it is possible for people to do it. 人力能及的;尽最大努力的

  • She has gained a reputation for creating books as perfect as is humanly possible.

    她因在著书上追求极致之美而享有盛名。

  • They had done everything humanly possible for their son.

    他们已经为儿子竭尽了全力。
