deem
diːm dim
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deems, present participle deeming, past tense , past participle deemed
VERB 认为;视为;相信 If something is deemed to have a particular quality or to do a particular thing, it is considered to have that quality or do that thing. 认为;视为;相信 [formal] [Also VERB n to-infinitive]

  • French and German were deemed essential. [be V-ed adj/n]

    法语和德语被认为是必不可少的。

  • He says he would support the use of force if the UN deemed it necessary.

    他说如果联合国认为有必要使用武力，他将予以支持。

  • I was deemed to be a competent shorthand typist. [be VERB-ed to-infinitive]

    我被视为是一名能干的速记打字员。

SYN consider, think, believe, hold
