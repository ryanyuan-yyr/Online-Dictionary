whinny
hwɪni ˈhwɪni ˈwɪni
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense whinnies, present participle whinnying, past tense , past participle whinnied
VERB (马)嘶鸣 When a horse whinnies, it makes a series of high-pitched sounds, usually not very loudly. (马)嘶鸣

  • The girl's horse whinnied. [VERB]

    那个女孩的马咴儿咴儿地叫着。

Whinny is also a noun.

  • With a terrified whinny the horse shied.

    那匹马惊了，发出一声可怕的嘶叫。
