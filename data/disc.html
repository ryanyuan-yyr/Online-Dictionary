disc
dɪsk dɪsk dɪsk
Word forms: plural discs
The spelling disk is also used in American English, mainly for meaning 1. 美国英语中亦拼作disk，主要用于义项1。
1 N-COUNT 圆盘状物;圆盘形状 A disc is a flat, circular shape or object. 圆盘状物;圆盘形状

  • Most shredding machines are based on a revolving disc fitted with replaceable blades.

    大多数粉碎机都是基于一个装有可替换刀片的旋转圆盘来实现作业的。

SYN circle, plate, saucer, discus  
2 N-COUNT 椎间盘 A disc is one of the thin, circular pieces of cartilage which separates the bones in your back. 椎间盘

  • I had slipped a disc and was frozen in a spasm of pain.

    我有一个椎间盘错位了，疼得我动弹不得。

3 N-COUNT 唱片 A disc is a record that you play on a record player. 唱片 [old-fashioned]

  • This disc includes the piano sonata in C minor.

    这张唱片包括了 C小调的钢琴奏鸣曲。

SYN record, vinyl, gramophone record, phonograph record [US , Canadian]  
4 See also: disk  compact disc  slipped disc
