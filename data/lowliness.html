lowly
loʊli ˈloʊli
Word forms: comparative lowlier, superlative lowliest
ADJ-GRADED 卑微的；低级的；无足轻重的 If you describe someone or something as lowly, you mean that they are low in rank, status, or importance. 卑微的；低级的；无足轻重的

  • ...lowly bureaucrats pretending to be senators.

    假称是参议员的低级官员

  • He was irked by his lowly status.

    他为自己地位卑微而恼恨。

SYN lowborn, obscure, subordinate, inferior
