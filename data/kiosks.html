kiosk
kiːɒsk ˈkiˌɑsk kiˈɑsk
Word forms: plural kiosks
1 N-COUNT （出售三明治或报纸等的）售货亭，小亭 A kiosk is a small building or structure from which people can buy things such as sandwiches or newspapers through an open window. （出售三明治或报纸等的）售货亭，小亭

  • I was getting cigarettes at the kiosk.

    我正在售货亭买烟。

SYN booth, stand, counter, stall  
2 N-COUNT 公用电话亭 A kiosk or a telephone kiosk is a public telephone box. 公用电话亭 [British]

  • He phoned me from a kiosk.

    他在公用电话亭给我打了电话。
