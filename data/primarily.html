primarily
praɪmərɪli ˈpraɪmərəlɪ praɪmeərɪli praɪˈmɛrəli ˈpraɪˌmɛrəli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 主要地；根本地 You use primarily to say what is mainly true in a particular situation. 主要地；根本地

  • ...a book aimed primarily at high-energy physicists.

    一本主要针对高能物理学家的书

  • Public order is primarily an urban problem.

    社会治安主要是城市的问题。

  • Investment remains tiny primarily because of the exorbitant cost of land.

    投资仍然微乎其微，主要原因在于土地成本过高。

SYN chiefly, largely, generally, mainly
