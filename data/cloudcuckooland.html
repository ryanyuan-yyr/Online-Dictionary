cloud-cuckoo-land ˌklaʊdˈkuˌku lænd
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 脱离实际的幻境 If you say that someone is living in cloud-cuckoo-land, you are criticizing them because they think there are no problems and that things will happen exactly as they want them to, when this is obviously not the case. 脱离实际的幻境 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • I was living in cloud-cuckoo-land about my salary expectations.

    我对自己的薪水充满了不切实际的幻想。
