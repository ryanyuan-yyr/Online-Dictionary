steely
stiːli ˈstili
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人)坚强的，坚决的，强硬的 Steely is used to emphasize that a person is hard, strong, and determined. (人)坚强的，坚决的，强硬的 [emphasis]

  • Their indecision has been replaced by confidence and steely determination.

    他们的优柔寡断已经被自信和钢铁般的决心所取代。

  • Captain Grenville stared at him steely-eyed.

    格伦维尔上尉用坚毅的目光凝视着他。

SYN determined, firm, dogged, persevering  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 铁灰色的;灰蓝色的 You use steely to describe something that has the grey colour of steel. 铁灰色的;灰蓝色的

  • ...steely grey hair.

    铁灰色头发

SYN blue-grey, grey, steel-grey, iron-grey
