disclaimer
dɪskleɪməʳ dɪsˈkleɪmər
Word forms: plural disclaimers
N-COUNT 不承担责任的声明 A disclaimer is a statement in which a person says that they did not know about something or that they are not responsible for something. 不承担责任的声明 [formal]

  • The disclaimer asserts that the company won't be held responsible for any inaccuracies.

    免责声明书明确声明公司将不对任何差错负责。

SYN denial, rejection, renunciation, retraction
