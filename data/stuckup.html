stuck-up
ˈstʌkˌʌp
ADJ-GRADED 傲慢无礼的;自命不凡的 If you say that someone is stuck-up, you mean that are very proud and unfriendly because they think they are very important. 傲慢无礼的;自命不凡的 [informal, disapproval]

  • She was a famous actress, but she wasn't a bit stuck-up.

    她是著名演员，却丝毫没有明星架子。

SYN snobbish, arrogant, conceited, proud
