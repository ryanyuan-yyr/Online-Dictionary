minutiae
maɪnjuːʃiiː mɪˈnjuːʃɪˌiː mɪnuːʃ- mɪˈnuʃə mɪnˈnjuʃə mɪˈnuʃiˌi mɪˈnuʃiˌaɪ
N-PLURAL 琐碎细节;细枝末节 The minutiae of something such as someone's job or life are the very small details of it. 琐碎细节;细枝末节 [formal]

  • Much of his early work is concerned with the minutiae of rural life. [+ of]

    他早期的许多作品关注的是农村生活中一些鸡毛蒜皮的琐事。
