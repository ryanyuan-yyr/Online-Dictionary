switchboard
swɪtʃbɔːʳd swɪtʃˌbɔrd
Word forms: plural switchboards
N-COUNT (电话的)交换台，总台,总机 A switchboard is a place in a large office or business where all the telephone calls are connected. (电话的)交换台，总台,总机

  • He asked to be connected to the central switchboard at London University.

    他要求转接到伦敦大学的电话总机。

  • The switchboard operator was the hotel owner's wife.

    电话总台的接线员是旅馆老板的妻子。
