eventful
ɪventfʊl iˈvɛntfəl ɪˈvɛntfəl
ADJ-GRADED 经历丰富的;多事的;充满事件的;多变故的 If you describe an event or a period of time as eventful, you mean that a lot of interesting, exciting, or important things have happened during it. 经历丰富的;多事的;充满事件的;多变故的

  • Her eventful life included holding senior positions in the Colonial Service.

    她一生经历丰富，曾在殖民服务局担任高级职位。

  • It was an eventful and controversial race.

    那是一场充满变数而且颇具争议的比赛。

  • Our next journey was longer and much more eventful.

    我们接下来的那次旅行时间更长，更加丰富多彩。

SYN exciting, active, busy, dramatic
