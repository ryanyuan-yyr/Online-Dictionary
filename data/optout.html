opt out
PHRASAL VERB 决定退出；决定不参加 If you opt out of something, you choose to be no longer involved in it. 决定退出；决定不参加

  • ... powers for hospitals to opt out of health authority control. [VERB PREP. + of]

    医院选择退出医疗管理体系的权力

  • Under the agreement the Vietnamese can opt out at any time. [VERB PREP.]

    依据该协定，越南有权随时选择退出。

  • ...his decision to send his son to an opted-out school. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    他送儿子去一所独立学校就读的决定

SYN withdraw, leave, pull out, drop out  
opt-out ˈɒptˌaʊt
Word forms: plural opt-outs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （学校或医院）选择不受当地政府管辖的，依靠国家资助的 An opt-out school or hospital has chosen to leave local government control and manage itself using national government money. （学校或医院）选择不受当地政府管辖的，依靠国家资助的 [British]

  • ...teachers at opt-out schools.

    财政自主学校的教师

2 N-COUNT （学校或医院）选择不受当地政府管辖 You can refer to the action taken by a school or hospital in which they choose not to be controlled by a local government authority as an opt-out. （学校或医院）选择不受当地政府管辖 [British]

  • More freedom and choice will be given to parents, and the school opt-outs will be stepped up.

    家长将会获得更多的自由和选择权，选择脱离地方政府管辖的学校将会越来越多。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] （条款）允许退出的，同意不参与的 An opt-out clause in an agreement gives people the choice not to be involved in one part of that agreement. （条款）允许退出的，同意不参与的 [mainly British]

  • ...an opt-out clause.

    退出条款

4 N-COUNT （针对协议某一部分的）退出，不参与 You can refer to the action of choosing not to be involved in a particular part of an agreement as an opt-out. （针对协议某一部分的）退出，不参与

  • ...a list of demands, such as opt-outs from some parts of the treaty.

    诸如退出条约部分条款的一系列要求
