jazzy
dʒæzi ˈdʒæzi
Word forms: comparative jazzier, superlative jazziest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 绚丽的；花哨的；现代的 If you describe something as jazzy, you mean that it is colourful and modern. 绚丽的；花哨的；现代的

  • ...a jazzy tie.

    花哨的领带

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 爵士乐风格的 Jazzy music is music in the style of jazz. 爵士乐风格的

  • 'Pop-Pop' is her jazziest album ever.

    Pop-Pop 是她最具有爵士乐风格的一张专辑。
