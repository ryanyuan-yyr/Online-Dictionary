mitigating
mɪtɪgeɪtɪŋ ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] (情节、因素)可考虑从轻处置的，可减轻罪责的 Mitigating circumstances or factors make a bad action, especially a crime, easier to understand and excuse, and may result in the person responsible being punished less severely. (情节、因素)可考虑从轻处置的，可减轻罪责的 [law, formal]

  • The judge found that in her case there were mitigating circumstances.

    法官认为她这个案子存在可以减轻罪责的情节。

  • There are various mitigating factors.

    有各种可以减轻罪责的因素。

SYN extenuating, qualifying, justifying, moderating
