bewildered
bɪwɪldəʳd bɪˈwɪldəd
ADJ-GRADED 困惑不解的；不知所措的；晕头转向的 If you are bewildered, you are very confused and cannot understand something or decide what you should do. 困惑不解的；不知所措的；晕头转向的

  • Some shoppers looked bewildered by the sheer variety of goods on offer.

    光是摆在那里的各式商品就让一些购物者晕头转向了。

SYN confused, surprised, stunned, puzzled
