truffle
trʌfəl ˈtrʌfəl
Word forms: plural truffles
1 N-COUNT (通常带朗姆酒味的)圆形巧克力软糖 A truffle is a soft round sweet made with chocolate and usually flavoured with rum. (通常带朗姆酒味的)圆形巧克力软糖
2 N-COUNT 松露;块菌 A truffle is a round type of fungus which is expensive and considered very good to eat. 松露;块菌
