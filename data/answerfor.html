answer for
1 PHRASAL VERB 为了…而受惩罚；为…付出代价 If you have to answer for something bad or wrong you have done, you are punished for it. 为了…而受惩罚；为…付出代价

  • He must be made to answer for his terrible crimes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他必须为他的严重罪行付出代价。

SYN pay for, suffer for, atone for, make amends for  
2 PHRASE （对问题）负有很大责任 If you say that someone has a lot to answer for, you are saying that their actions have led to problems which you think they are responsible for. （对问题）负有很大责任
