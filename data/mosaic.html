mosaic
moʊzeɪɪk məˈzeɪɪk moʊˈzeɪɪk
Word forms: plural mosaics
N-VAR 马赛克;镶嵌图案;拼花图案 A mosaic is a design which consists of small pieces of coloured glass, pottery, or stone set in concrete or plaster. 马赛克;镶嵌图案;拼花图案

  • ...a Roman villa which once housed a fine collection of mosaics.

    曾收藏有一批精美的马赛克镶嵌画的罗马别墅

  • He has used a mixture of mosaic, collage and felt-tip pen.

    他混合使用了马赛克、拼贴画和毡头笔。
