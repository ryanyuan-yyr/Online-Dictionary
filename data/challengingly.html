challenging
tʃælɪndʒɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED (任务或工作)艰巨的，具有挑战性的，要求高的 A challenging task or job requires great effort and determination. (任务或工作)艰巨的，具有挑战性的，要求高的

  • Mike found a challenging job as a computer programmer.

    迈克找到一份做计算机程序员的富有挑战性的工作。

  • I'm ready to do all those things which are more challenging.

    我愿意接受更大的挑战。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 挑战性的;挑衅的 If you do something in a challenging way, you seem to be inviting people to argue with you or compete against you in some way. 挑战性的;挑衅的

  • Mona gave him a challenging look.

    莫娜挑衅地看了他一眼。
