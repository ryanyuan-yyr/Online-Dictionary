out-of-state ˈaʊtəvˌsteɪt
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 外州的Out-of-state is used to describe people who do not live permanently in a particular state within a country, but have travelled there from somewhere else. 外州的

  • 95% of our students are out-of-state students.

    我们学生中的95%是外州的。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 国外的Out-of-state companies are based outside a particular state but conduct business within that state. 国外的

  • ...the impact of new competition from out-of-state banks.

    ...来自国外银行新竞争的影响。
