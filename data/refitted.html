refit
riːfɪt riːfɪt riˈfɪt ˈriˌfɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense refits, present participle refitting, past tense , past participle refitted
The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'fɪt/. The noun is pronounced /'riːfɪt/. 动词读作/ˌriː'fɪt/。名词读作/'riːfɪt/。
VERB [usually passive] 改装，整修（船只） When a ship is refitted, it is repaired or is given new parts, equipment, or furniture. 改装，整修（船只）

  • During the war, Navy ships were refitted here. [be VERB-ed]

    战争期间海军的船只在这里进行修理。

SYN repair, fix, restore, renew  
Refit is also a noun.

  • The ship finished an extensive refit last year.

    这艘船去年进行了大修。
