tit-for-tat ˌtɪtfəˈtæt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 针锋相对的；以牙还牙的 A tit-for-tat action is one where someone takes revenge on another person for what they have done by doing something similar to them. 针锋相对的；以牙还牙的

  • The two countries have each expelled another diplomat following a round of tit-for-tat expulsions.

    在一轮针锋相对的驱逐事件之后，两国各又驱逐了一名外交官。
