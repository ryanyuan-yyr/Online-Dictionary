slosh
slɒʃ slɑʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sloshes, present participle sloshing, past tense , past participle sloshed
1 V-ERG (使)(液体)来回晃荡 If a liquid sloshes around or if you slosh it around, it moves around in different directions. (使)(液体)来回晃荡 [Also VERB n]

  • The water sloshed around the bridge. [VERB adv./prep.]

    河水在桥的周围荡漾。

  • He took a mouthful of the cheap wine and sloshed it around his mouth. [VERB noun adv./prep.]

    他喝了一口廉价的葡萄酒，在嘴巴里来回咂摸着。

  • The champagne sloshed and spilt. [VERB]

    香槟酒晃洒了。

SYN splash, wash, slop, break  
2 VERB 踩着(水或泥浆等)走;扑哧扑哧地走 If you slosh through mud or water, you walk through it in an energetic way, so that the mud or water makes sounds as you walk. 踩着(水或泥浆等)走;扑哧扑哧地走

  • The two girls joined arms and sloshed through the mud together. [VERB adv./prep.]

    两个女孩挽着胳膊，一道趟过了泥浆。

SYN wade, splash, flounder, paddle
