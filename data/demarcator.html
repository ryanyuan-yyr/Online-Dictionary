demarcation
diːmɑːʳkeɪʃən ˌdimɑrˈkeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 界线(或界限)的划定 Demarcation is the establishment of boundaries or limits separating two areas, groups, or things. 界线(或界限)的划定 [formal]

  • ...the demarcation line between Indian and Pakistani Kashmir.

    克什米尔的印度控制区和巴基斯坦控制区之间的分界线

  • Talks were continuing about the demarcation of the border between the two countries.

    关于两国之间边界划定问题的谈判仍在继续。

SYN limit, bound, margin, boundary
