hick
hɪk hɪk
Word forms: plural hicks
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 乡巴佬;土包子 If you refer to someone as a hick, you are saying in a rude way that you think they are uneducated and stupid because they come from the countryside. 乡巴佬;土包子 [informal, disapproval]

  • He is an obnoxious hick.

    他是个讨厌的乡巴佬。

  • ...a crummy little hick hotel.

    脏兮兮的乡下小旅店

SYN yokel, peasant [informal] , rustic, redneck
