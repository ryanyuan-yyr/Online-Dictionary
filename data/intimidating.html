intimidating
ɪntɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 令人惊恐的;骇人的;令人胆怯的 If you describe someone or something as intimidating, you mean that they are frightening and make people lose confidence. 令人惊恐的;骇人的;令人胆怯的

  • He was a huge, intimidating figure.

    他是个身材高大、令人生畏的人。

SYN frightening, alarming, terrifying, menacing
