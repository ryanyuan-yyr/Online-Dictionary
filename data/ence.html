-ence -əns -ənsi əns
(also -ency)
SUFFIX （用于形容词后构成名词，通常是代替-ent）表示“状态”，“性质”，“态度”，“行为” -ence and -ency are added to adjectives, usually in place of -ent, to form nouns referring to states, qualities, attitudes, or behaviour. For example, 'affluence' is the state of being affluent. （用于形容词后构成名词，通常是代替-ent）表示“状态”，“性质”，“态度”，“行为”
