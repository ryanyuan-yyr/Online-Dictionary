curry
kʌri kɜːri ˈkɜri
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense curries, present participle currying, past tense , past participle curried
1 N-VAR 咖喱菜(印度主要菜肴，常与米饭一起食用) Curry is a dish composed of meat and vegetables, or just vegetables, in a sauce containing hot spices. It is usually eaten with rice and is one of the main dishes of India. 咖喱菜(印度主要菜肴，常与米饭一起食用)

  • ...vegetable curry.

    咖喱蔬菜

  • I went for a curry last night.

    昨晚我去吃了咖喱菜。

2 PHRASE 讨好;奉承;拍…的马屁 If one person tries to curry favour with another, they do things in order to try to gain their support or co-operation. 讨好;奉承;拍…的马屁

  • Politicians are eager to promote their 'happy family' image to curry favour with voters. [+ with]

    政客们极力宣传他们的“幸福家庭”形象以讨好选民。
