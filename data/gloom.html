gloom
gluːm glum
1 N-SING [oft in/into N] 阴暗；昏暗 The gloom is a state of near darkness. 阴暗；昏暗

  • ...the gloom of a foggy November morning.

    11月份一个雾蒙蒙的早晨，天空昏暗阴沉

  • I was peering about me in the gloom.

    我在暗处端详着自己。

SYN darkness, dark, shadow, cloud  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 忧郁；愁闷；无望 Gloom is a feeling of sadness and lack of hope. 忧郁；愁闷；无望

  • ...the deepening gloom over the economy.

    对经济的忧虑日益加重

SYN depression, despair, misery, sadness
