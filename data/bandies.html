bandy
bændi ˈbændi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bandies, present participle bandying, past tense , past participle bandied
VERB （与…）争吵，争论 If you bandy words with someone, you argue with them. （与…）争吵，争论

  • Brand shook his head. He was tired of bandying words with the man. [VERB noun + with]

    布兰德摇了摇头。他已经厌烦了和这个男人斗嘴。

  • The prosecution and defense were bandying accusations back and forth. [VERB noun adv.]

    原告和被告你来我往，互相指责。

SYN exchange, trade, pass, throw
