poky
poʊki ˈpoʊki
Word forms: comparative pokier, superlative pokiest
The spelling pokey is also used, especially for meanings 1 and 3. 亦拼作 pokey，尤用于义项1和3。
1 ADJ-GRADED （房间或屋子）狭小的，窄小的 A room or house that is poky is uncomfortably small. （房间或屋子）狭小的，窄小的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...apartments which were poky, dimly lit and poorly furnished.

    空间狭小、光线昏暗、陈设简陋的公寓

  • ...pokey little offices.

    窄小的办公室

2 ADJ 慢吞吞的；缓慢的 If you say that someone is poky, you are criticizing them for moving or reacting very slowly. 慢吞吞的；缓慢的 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • 'Move!' she cried. 'Don't be so darn poky!'

    “动起来！”她叫道：“别这么慢吞吞的！”

3 N-SING 监狱；监牢 If someone is in the pokey, they are in prison. 监狱；监牢 [mainly US, informal]

  • They threw him in the pokey, kept him under very harsh conditions.

    他们把他投进监狱，服刑条件非常恶劣。
