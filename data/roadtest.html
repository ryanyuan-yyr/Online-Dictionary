road test
(also road-test)Word forms: road tests, road testing, road tested
1 V-T 车辆驾驶测试If you road test a car or other vehicle, for example, after it has been repaired, you drive it on roads in order to make sure that it is working properly. 车辆驾驶测试

  • While road testing the car, a mechanic injures another motorist and is sued for $50,000.

    技师在试车的时候伤了另一名驾驶员，起诉赔赏为五万美元。

2 N-COUNT 试车Road test is also a noun 试车

  • ...during a recent road test of the Fiesta LX 5-door.

    ...在最近试开福特Fiesta-LX五门车期间。

3 V-T 驾照考试A road test is a driving test that you must pass in order to get a driving licence. 驾照考试 [US]
4 V-T

  • When I went for my road test, I got utterly confused and flunked.

    去考驾照的时候，我变得昏头昏脑结果没有通过。

5 V-T 实地测验If someone road tests a new product, they use it in order to make sure that it works properly. 实地测验

  • Catherine Young road tests affordable waterproof jackets.

    凯瑟琳·杨试穿了一件价格合适的雨衣。

6 N-COUNT 实地测验Road test is also a noun. 实地测验

  • The new format is great but the show needs more road tests.

    这个节目的总体编排非常好，但需要多预演几次。
