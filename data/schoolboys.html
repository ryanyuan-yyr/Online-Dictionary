schoolboy
skuːlbɔɪ ˈskulˌbɔɪ
Word forms: plural schoolboys
1 N-COUNT (中、小学的)男生 A schoolboy is a boy who goes to school. (中、小学的)男生

  • ...a group of ten-year-old schoolboys.

    一群 10 岁的男学生

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (幽默)愚蠢的，粗鲁的,幼稚的 If you think a man's sense of humour is silly or rude, you can describe it as schoolboy humour. (幽默)愚蠢的，粗鲁的,幼稚的 [disapproval]

  • ...tiresome schoolboy jokes.

    令人厌烦的愚蠢笑话
