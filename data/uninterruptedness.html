uninterrupted
ʌnɪntərʌptɪd ˌʌnɪntəˈrʌptɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ, ADJ n] 不间断的；连续不断的 If something is uninterrupted, it is continuous and has no breaks or interruptions in it. 不间断的；连续不断的

  • This enables the healing process to continue uninterrupted.

    这样能使恢复过程不间断地进行下去。

  • His hearing remained good, so that his contact with the world was uninterrupted.

    他的听力尚佳，因此他与外界的联系没有中断。

  • ...five years of rapid and uninterrupted growth.

    5年持续的快速增长

SYN continuous, constant, steady, sustained  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （景色）不受阻挡的 An uninterrupted view of something is a clear view of it, without any obstacles in the way. （景色）不受阻挡的

  • Diners can enjoy an uninterrupted view of the garden.

    用餐者对花园的景色一览无余。

SYN clear, open, unrestricted, unobstructed
