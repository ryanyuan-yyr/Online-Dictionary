transient
trænziənt -nʃənt ˈtrænʃənt ˈtrænsiənt ˈtrænʒənt ˈtrænziənt
Word forms: plural transients
1 ADJ-GRADED 短暂的;倏忽的;无常的 Transient is used to describe a situation that lasts only a short time or is constantly changing. 短暂的;倏忽的;无常的 [formal]

  • ...the transient nature of high fashion.

    顶级时装的瞬息万变性

  • In most cases, pain is transient.

    大多数情况下，疼痛是短暂的。

SYN brief, passing, short-term, temporary  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 临时旅客;流动短工;暂居者 Transients are people who stay in a place for only a short time and then move somewhere else. 临时旅客;流动短工;暂居者 [formal]

  • …a hotel for transients.

    为过路客人开设的旅馆
