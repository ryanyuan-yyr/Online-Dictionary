tan
tæn tæn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tans, present participle tanning, past tense , past participle tanned
1 N-SING 晒黑的皮肤 If you have a tan, your skin has become darker than usual because you have been in the sun. 晒黑的皮肤

  • She is tall and blonde, with a permanent tan.

    她高个儿，一头金发，皮肤黝黑。

2 V-ERG (使)(皮肤)晒黑 If a part of your body tans or if you tan it, your skin becomes darker than usual because you spend a lot of time in the sun. (使)(皮肤)晒黑

  • I have very pale skin that never tans. [VERB]

    我皮肤很白，怎么也晒不黑。

  • I don't tan. [VERB]

    我不愿晒黑。

  • Leigh rolled over on her stomach to tan her back. [VERB noun]

    莉翻了个身趴下，开始晒后背。

SYN brown, bronze, go brown, become suntanned  
tanned

  • Their skin was tanned and glowing from their weeks at the sea.

    他们在海边呆了几个星期，皮肤晒得黝黑发亮。

3 COLOUR 棕黄色;黄褐色 Something that is tan is a light brown colour. 棕黄色;黄褐色

  • ...a tan leather jacket.

    棕黄色皮夹克

4 VERB 鞣(革);硝(皮) To tan animal skins means to make them into leather by treating them with tannin or other chemicals. 鞣(革);硝(皮)

  • ...the process of tanning animal hides. [VERB noun]

    鞣革的过程
