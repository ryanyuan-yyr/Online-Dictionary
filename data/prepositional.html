preposition
prepəzɪʃən ˌprɛpəˈzɪʃən
Word forms: plural prepositions
1 N-COUNT 介词；前置词 A preposition is a word such as 'by', 'for', 'into', or 'with' which usually has a noun group as its object. 介词；前置词

  • There is nothing in the rules of grammar to suggest that ending a sentence with a preposition is wrong.

    语法规则中并没有规定句子以介词结尾是错误的。
