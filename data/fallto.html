fall to
PHRASAL VERB (责任、机会等) 落到…身上If a responsibility, duty, or opportunity falls to someone, it becomes their responsibility, duty, or opportunity. (责任、机会等) 落到…身上

  • He's been very unlucky that no chances have fallen to him. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他很不走运，没有任何机会落到他身上。

SYN be the responsibility of, be up to, come down to, devolve upon
