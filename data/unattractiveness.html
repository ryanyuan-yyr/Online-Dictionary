unattractive
ʌnətræktɪv ˌʌnəˈtræktɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 不漂亮的；难看的；不吸引人的 Unattractive people and things are unpleasant in appearance. 不漂亮的；难看的；不吸引人的

  • I'm 27, have a nice flat, a good job and I'm not unattractive.

    本人27岁，有一所不错的公寓，一份像样的工作，长得也不难看。

  • ...an unattractive shade of orange.

    一抹难看的橙色

  • ...an unattractive and uninteresting city.

    一座又难看又无趣的城市

SYN ugly, homely [US] , plain, hideous  
2 ADJ-GRADED 无吸引力的；不受欢迎的 If you describe something as unattractive, you mean that people do not like it and do not want to be involved with it. 无吸引力的；不受欢迎的

  • The market is still unattractive to many insurers. [+ to]

    市场对许多保险公司依然没有吸引力。

  • It is not an unattractive option to make programmes for other companies.

    为其他公司制作节目也不失为一个好的选择。
