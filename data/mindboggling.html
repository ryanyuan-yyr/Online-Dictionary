mind-boggling
ˈmaɪndˌbɑglɪŋ
(also mind boggling)
ADJ-GRADED (巨大、复杂、极端得)令人难以想象的 If you say that something is mind-boggling, you mean that it is so large, complicated, or extreme that it is very hard to imagine. (巨大、复杂、极端得)令人难以想象的 [informal]

  • The amount of paperwork involved is mind-boggling.

    相关的书面工作量大得令人难以想象。
