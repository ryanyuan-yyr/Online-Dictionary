pamper
pæmpəʳ ˈpæmpər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pampers, present participle pampering, past tense , past participle pampered
VERB 宠爱；溺爱；娇惯 If you pamper someone, you make them feel comfortable by doing things for them or giving them expensive or luxurious things, sometimes in a way which has a bad effect on their character. 宠爱；溺爱；娇惯

  • Why don't you let your mother pamper you for a while?. [VERB noun]

    何不让你母亲宠你一段时间呢？

  • Pamper yourself with our luxury gifts. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    用我们的精美礼物宠爱一下自己吧。

  • The only son had been pampered and spoiled. [VERB noun]

    这个独子娇生惯养被宠坏了。

SYN spoil, indulge, gratify, baby
