laser disc
(also laser disk)Word forms: plural laser discs
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n, oft on NOUN] 激光（或镭射）视盘 A laser disc is a shiny flat disc which can be played on a machine which uses lasers to convert signals on the disc into television pictures and sound of a very high quality. 激光（或镭射）视盘
