offending
əfendɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 惹麻烦的;造成问题的 You can use offending to describe something that is causing a problem that needs to be dealt with. 惹麻烦的;造成问题的

  • The book was withdrawn for the offending passages to be deleted.

    这本书因含有需要删除的问题章节而被撤回。

  • The dentist commenced to dig, drill and finally fill the offending tooth.

    牙医开始在那颗有问题的牙齿上挖洞，钻孔，直到最后把它补好。

SYN upsetting, disturbing, offensive, unpleasant  
2 N-UNCOUNT 犯罪行为 Offending is the act of committing a crime. 犯罪行为

  • Ms Mann is working with young offenders and trying to break cycles of offending.

    曼女士正在从事帮助少年犯的工作，试图打破犯罪的恶性循环。
