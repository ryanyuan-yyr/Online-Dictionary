knuckle down
PHRASAL VERB （尤指前期不努力之后）开始努力工作（或学习） If someone knuckles down, they begin to work or study very hard, especially after a period when they have done very little work. （尤指前期不努力之后）开始努力工作（或学习） [informal]

  • The only thing to do was knuckle down and get on with some serious hard work. [VERB PREP.]

    唯一能做的就是开始努力工作，好好干出点儿成绩来。

  • He managed to knuckle down to his lessons long enough to pass his examination. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他在离考试还有足够长时间的时候就开始认真学习，从而通过了考试。

SYN apply yourself, try, study, concentrate
