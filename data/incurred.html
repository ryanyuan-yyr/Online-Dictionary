incur
ɪnkɜːʳ ɪnˈkɜr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense incurs, present participle incurring, past tense , past participle incurred
VERB 招致;带来;遭受 If you incur something unpleasant, it happens to you because of something you have done. 招致;带来;遭受 [written]

  • The government had also incurred huge debts. [VERB noun]

    政府也已负债累累。

  • She falls in love and incurs the wrath of her father. [VERB noun]

    她恋爱了,这引起了父亲的愤怒。

  • ...the terrible damage incurred during the past decade. [VERB-ed]

    在过去 10 年间遭受的严重损伤

SYN sustain, experience, suffer, gain
