wait in
PHRASAL VERB 在家等候 If you wait in, you deliberately stay at home and do not go out, for example because someone is coming to see you. 在家等候 [mainly British]

  • If I'd waited in for you I could have waited all day. [VERB PREP. + for]

    要是我真留在家等你，那早都等上一整天了。

  • There's no need to wait in all day. [VERB PREP.]

    没必要一整天在家等着。
