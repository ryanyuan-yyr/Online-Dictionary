licentious
laɪsentʃəs laɪˈsɛnʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 不道德的；（尤指）放荡的，淫乱的 If you describe a person as licentious, you mean that they are very immoral, especially in their sexual behaviour. 不道德的；（尤指）放荡的，淫乱的 [formal, disapproval]

  • ...alarming stories of licentious behaviour.

    关于放荡淫乱行为的骇人听闻的故事

SYN promiscuous, immoral, lewd, debauched
