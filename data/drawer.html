drawer
drɔːəʳ ˈdrɔər drɔr
Word forms: plural drawers
1 N-COUNT 抽屉 A drawer is part of a desk, chest, or other piece of furniture that is shaped like a box and is designed for putting things in. You pull it towards you to open it. 抽屉

  • He opened a drawer in his writing-table.

    他打开了写字台的一个抽屉。

2 N-PLURAL [also a pair of N] (尤指女式)内裤 Drawers are underpants, especially ones worn by women. (尤指女式)内裤 [old-fashioned]
3 See also: chest of drawers
