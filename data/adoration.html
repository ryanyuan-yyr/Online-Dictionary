adoration
ædɔːreɪʃən ˌædəˈreɪʃən ˌædəˈreɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 热爱；爱慕；敬慕；崇拜 Adoration is a feeling of great admiration and love for someone or something. 热爱；爱慕；敬慕；崇拜

  • She needs and wants to be loved with overwhelming passion and adoration.

    她需要并想要被爱的滔滔激情和倾慕所包围。

  • He had been used to female adoration all his life.

    他一生中已经对女性的爱慕习以为常了。

SYN love, honour, worship, worshipping
