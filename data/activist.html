activist
æktɪvɪst
Word forms: plural activists
N-COUNT 积极分子；活跃分子 An activist is a person who works to bring about political or social changes by campaigning in public or working for an organization. 积极分子；活跃分子

  • The police say they suspect the attack was carried out by animal rights activists.

    警方说他们怀疑袭击是由动物权益保护的积极分子们发动的。

SYN militant, partisan, organizer, warrior
