quarry
kwɒri kwɔːri ˈkwɔri ˈkwɑri
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense quarries, present participle quarrying, past tense , past participle quarried
1 N-COUNT 采石场;露天矿场 A quarry is an area that is dug out from a piece of land or the side of a mountain in order to get stone or minerals. 采石场;露天矿场

  • ...an old limestone quarry.

    废旧的石灰岩采石场

2 VERB 开采(石料或矿产);在…开采 When stone or minerals are quarried or when an area is quarried for them, they are removed from the area by digging, drilling, or using explosives. 开采(石料或矿产);在…开采

  • The large limestone caves are also quarried for cement. [be VERB-ed]

    同时还在这些大石灰岩洞里开采水泥原料。

  • ...locally quarried stone. [VERB-ed]

    当地采掘的石头

SYN excavate, mine, dig up, dig out  
quarrying

  • Farming, quarrying and other local industries have declined.

    农业、采矿业以及其他地方工业都已陷入衰退。

3 N-SING 追捕的对象；猎物 A person's or animal's quarry is the person or animal that they are hunting. 追捕的对象；猎物
