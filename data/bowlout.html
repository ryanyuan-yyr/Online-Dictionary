bowl out
1 PHRASAL VERB [no cont] （板球等运动中一方球队所有队员）被投杀出局 In a sport such as cricket, if a team is bowled out, each player in that team has had to stop batting and leave the pitch and there is nobody left to bat. （板球等运动中一方球队所有队员）被投杀出局 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • India were bowled out for 209. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    印度队得了209分后所有队员都被投杀出局。

  • Middlesex defeated Derbyshire by bowling out the opposition. [VERB PREP. noun]

    米德尔塞克斯队将德比郡队的所有队员都投杀出局，击败了对方。
