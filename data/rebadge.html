rebadge riːbædʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rebadges, present participle rebadging, past tense , past participle rebadged
VERB （给产品）重新命名，改换商标，更换标识 If a product is rebadged, it is given a new name, brand, or logo. （给产品）重新命名，改换商标，更换标识 [British] [Also V-ed]

  • The car was rebadged as a Vauxhall and sold in Britain. [be VERB-ed + as]

    这种汽车被重新命名为沃克斯豪尔在英国出售。
