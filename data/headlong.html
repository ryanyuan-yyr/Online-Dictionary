headlong
hedlɒŋ ˈhɛdˌlɒŋ -lɔːŋ ˈhɛdˌlɔŋ
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 快速地 If you move headlong in a particular direction, you move there very quickly. 快速地

  • He ran headlong for the open door.

    他飞快地朝敞开着的门跑去。

SYN hastily, hurriedly, helter-skelter, pell-mell  
Headlong is also an adjective.

  • The army was in headlong flight.

    部队当时正仓皇奔逃。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 头朝下地 If you fall or move headlong, you fall or move with your head furthest forward. 头朝下地

  • She missed her footing and fell headlong down the stairs.

    她一脚踩空，头朝下摔下了楼梯。

SYN headfirst, head-on, headforemost  
3 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 莽撞地;轻率地 If you rush headlong into something, you do it quickly without thinking carefully about it. 莽撞地;轻率地

  • Do not leap headlong into decisions.

    不要轻率地作决定。

  • The country, they say, will inevitably now plunge headlong into decadence.

    他们说，现在整个国家会不可避免地一头陷入衰退中。

SYN rashly, wildly, hastily, precipitately  
Headlong is also an adjective.

  • ...the headlong rush to independence.

    寻求独立的仓促行动
