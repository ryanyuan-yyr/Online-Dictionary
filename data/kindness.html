kindness
kaɪndnəs ˈkaɪndnɪs
Word forms: plural kindnesses
1 N-UNCOUNT 仁慈；体贴；友好 Kindness is the quality of being gentle, caring, and helpful. 仁慈；体贴；友好

  • We have been treated with such kindness by everybody.

    每个人对我们都很好。

SYN goodwill, understanding, charity, grace  
2 N-COUNT 友好（或体贴）的举动 A kindness is a helpful or considerate act. 友好（或体贴）的举动
