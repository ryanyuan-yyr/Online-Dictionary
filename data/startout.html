start out
1 PHRASAL VERB 起初是… If someone or something starts out as a particular thing, they are that thing at the beginning although they change later. 起初是… [Also V P n as n]

  • Daly was a fast-talking Irish-American who had started out as a salesman. [VERB PREP. + as]

    达利是个花言巧语的爱尔兰裔美国人，最初是干推销员的。

  • What started out as fun quickly became hard work. [VERB PREP. + as]

    起初好玩的事很快就变成了艰苦的工作。

2 PHRASAL VERB 从…开始;从…着手 If you start out by doing something, you do it at the beginning of an activity. 从…开始;从…着手

  • We started out by looking at ways in which big projects such as railways could be financed by the private sector. [V P + by]

    我们先研究了让私营企业投资铁路等大型项目的方式。

  • The child'll start out by making relatively few distinctions in the language. [V P by -ing]

    孩子开始时不太能辨别语言中的差别。
