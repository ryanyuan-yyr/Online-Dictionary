beg
beg bɛg
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense begs, present participle begging, past tense , past participle begged
1 VERB 请求；恳求；恳请 If you beg someone to do something, you ask them very anxiously or eagerly to do it. 请求；恳求；恳请 [Also V n with quote]

  • I begged him to come back to England with me. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    我恳求他和我一起回英格兰。

  • I begged to be allowed to leave. [VERB to-infinitive]

    我请求允许我离开。

  • We are not going to beg for help any more. [VERB + for]

    我们不会再乞求帮助。

  • They dropped to their knees and begged forgiveness. [VERB noun]

    他们跪下请求原谅。

SYN implore, plead with, beseech, desire [formal]  
2 VERB [oft cont] 乞讨；乞求施舍 If someone who is poor is begging, they are asking people to give them food or money. 乞讨；乞求施舍

  • I was surrounded by people begging for food. [VERB + for]

    我被一群讨饭的围住了。

  • There are thousands like him in Los Angeles, begging on the streets and sleeping rough. [VERB]

    洛杉矶有数以千计像他这样的人，沿街乞讨，露宿街头。

  • She was living alone, begging food from neighbors. [VERB noun]

    她孑然一身，向邻居们讨饭吃。

SYN scrounge [informal] , bum [informal] , blag [slang] , touch (someone) for [slang]  
3 PHRASE 恕难苟同；斗胆唱反调 You say 'I beg to differ' when you are politely emphasizing that you disagree with someone. 恕难苟同；斗胆唱反调 [politeness]
4 PHRASE 没人要；没人用 If you say that something is going begging, you mean that it is available but no one is using it or accepting it. 没人要；没人用

  • There is other housing going begging in town.

    城里还有些住宅无人问津。

5 PHRASE 引起疑问，使人想问（一些人认为此用法不正确） If you say that something begs a particular question, you mean that it makes people want to ask that question; some people consider that this use is incorrect. 引起疑问，使人想问（一些人认为此用法不正确）

  • Hopewell's success begs the question: why aren't more companies doing the same?

    霍普韦尔的成功让人不禁要问：为什么其他公司不效仿呢？

6 PHRASE （认为问题已获解决而）绕过问题 If you say that something begs a particular question, you mean that it assumes that the question has already been answered and so does not deal with it. （认为问题已获解决而）绕过问题

  • The research begs a number of questions.

    研究绕过了一些问题。

7 I beg your pardon→see: pardon
