inconsiderate
ɪnkənsɪdərət ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərɪt
ADJ-GRADED 不体谅别人的;不替别人着想的 If you accuse someone of being inconsiderate, you mean that they do not take enough care over how their words or actions will affect other people. 不体谅别人的;不替别人着想的 [disapproval]

  • Motorists were criticised for being inconsiderate to pedestrians. [+ to]

    开车的人因不考虑行人而受到指责。

  • ...his inconsiderate behaviour.

    他不替别人着想的行为

SYN selfish, rude, insensitive, self-centred
