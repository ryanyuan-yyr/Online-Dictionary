decompose
diːkəmpoʊz ˌdikəmˈpoʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense decomposes, present participle decomposing, past tense , past participle decomposed
V-ERG (使）(死去的动植物)腐烂;（使）分解 When things such as dead plants or animals decompose, or when something decomposes them, they change chemically and begin to decay. (使）(死去的动植物)腐烂;（使）分解

  • ...a dead body found decomposing in a wood. [VERB]

    在树林里发现的一具正在腐烂的尸体

  • The debris slowly decomposes into compost. [VERB + into]

    这些垃圾慢慢地分解成了堆肥。

  • The fertiliser releases nutrients gradually as bacteria decompose it. [VERB noun]

    随着细菌的分解，肥料逐渐释放出各种营养成分。
