off-the-wall ˈɔfðəˈwɔl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 稀奇古怪的；不同寻常的；搞怪的 If you describe something as off-the-wall, you mean that it is unusual and rather strange but in an amusing or interesting way. 稀奇古怪的；不同寻常的；搞怪的 [informal]

  • ...surreal off-the-wall humor.

    荒诞不经的超现实幽默

2 ADJ-GRADED （人、思想、行为方式）疯狂的，愚蠢的 If you say that a person, their ideas, or their ways of doing something are off-the-wall, you are critical of them because you think they are mad or very foolish. （人、思想、行为方式）疯狂的，愚蠢的 [disapproval]

  • It can be done without following some absurd, off-the-wall investment strategy.

    不用遵照那套荒唐愚蠢的投资策略也可以做到。

SYN foolish, silly, unwise, unreasonable
