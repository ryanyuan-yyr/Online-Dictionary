awake
əweɪk əˈweɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense awakes, present participle awaking, past tense awoke, past participle awoken
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 醒着的；没睡着的 Someone who is awake is not sleeping. 醒着的；没睡着的

  • I don't stay awake at night worrying about that.

    我并没有因为那件事而担心得彻夜不眠。

  • Nightmares kept me awake all night.

    噩梦扰得我整夜未眠。

SYN not sleeping, sleepless, wide-awake, aware  
2 PHRASE 毫无睡意的;失眠的 Someone who is wide awake is fully awake and unable to sleep. 毫无睡意的;失眠的

  • I could not relax and still felt wide awake.

    我没法放松下来，还是觉得睡意全无。

SYN conscious, fully awake, roused, wakened  
3 V-ERG 醒来；唤醒；叫醒 When you awake or when something awakes you, you wake up. 醒来；唤醒；叫醒 [literary] [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • At midnight he awoke and listened to the radio for a few minutes. [VERB]

    他半夜醒来，听了一会儿收音机。

  • I awoke to the sound of the wind in the trees. [VERB prep.]

    外面风吹树响把我吵醒了。

  • The sound of many voices awoke her with a start. [VERB noun]

    一阵杂乱的说话声把她惊醒了。

SYN wake up, come to, wake, stir
