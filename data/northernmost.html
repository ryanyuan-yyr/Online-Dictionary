northernmost
nɔːʳðəʳnmoʊst ˈnɔːθˌməʊst ˈnɔːðəˌməʊst ˈnɔrðərnˌmoʊst
ADJ-SUPERL [usually ADJ n] 最北的;极北的 The northernmost part of an area or the northernmost place is the one that is farthest towards the north. 最北的;极北的

  • ...the northernmost tip of the British Isles.

    不列颠群岛的最北端

  • The Chablis vineyard is the northernmost in Burgundy.

    沙布利葡萄园在勃艮第的最北边。
