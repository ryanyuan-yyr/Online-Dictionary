latchkey
lætʃkiː ˈlætʃˌki
(also latch-key)
ADJ [ADJ n] 挂钥匙的（形容儿童因父母都是上班族放学回家后不得不自行开门） If you refer to a child as a latchkey kid, you disapprove of the fact that they have to let themselves into their home when returning from school because their parents are out at work. 挂钥匙的（形容儿童因父母都是上班族放学回家后不得不自行开门） [disapproval]
