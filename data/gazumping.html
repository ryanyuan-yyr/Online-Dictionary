gazump
gəzʌmp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gazumps, present participle gazumping, past tense , past participle gazumped
VERB [usually passive] （房价议定后因有人出高价而）对…食言，对…悔约 If you are gazumped by someone, they agree to sell their house to you, but then sell it to someone else who offers to pay a higher price. （房价议定后因有人出高价而）对…食言，对…悔约 [British, informal]

  • In France you cannot be gazumped. [be VERB-ed]

    在法国买房不会出现抬价毁约的情况。

gazumping

  • During the 1980s property boom, gazumping was common.

    在20世纪80年代的房地产繁荣时期，抬价毁约是司空见惯的现象。
