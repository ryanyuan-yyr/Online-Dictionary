displeasure
dɪspleʒəʳ dɪsˈplɛʒər
N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 不悦;不满;生气 Someone's displeasure is a feeling of annoyance that they have about something that has happened. 不悦;不满;生气

  • The population has already begun to show its displeasure at the slow pace of change. [+ with/at]

    人们已经开始表露出对改革步伐缓慢的不满。

SYN annoyance, anger, resentment, irritation
