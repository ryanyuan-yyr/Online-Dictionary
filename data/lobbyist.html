lobbyist
lɒbiɪst ˈlɑbiɪst
Word forms: plural lobbyists
N-COUNT （对政府或委员会进行游说的） 说客 A lobbyist is someone who tries actively to persuade a government or council that a particular law should be changed or that a particular thing should be done. （对政府或委员会进行游说的） 说客
SYN persuader, manager, influencer, publicist
