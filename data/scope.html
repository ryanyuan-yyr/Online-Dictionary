scope
skoʊp skoʊp
1 N-UNCOUNT [NOUN to-infinitive] 机会;施展余地 If there is scope for a particular kind of behaviour or activity, people have the opportunity to behave in this way or do that activity. 机会;施展余地

  • He believed in giving his staff scope for initiative. [+ for]

    他认为应给员工提供发挥主动性的机会。

  • Banks had increased scope to develop new financial products.

    银行已为开发新的金融产品留出了更大余地。

SYN opportunity, room, freedom, play  
2 N-SING (活动、话题或作品的)范围 The scope of an activity, topic, or piece of work is the whole area which it deals with or includes. (活动、话题或作品的)范围

  • Mr Dobson promised to widen the organisation's scope of activity. [+ of]

    多布森先生许诺拓宽该组织的活动范围。

  • ...the scope of a novel.

    小说所涉及的范围

SYN range, capacity, reach, area
