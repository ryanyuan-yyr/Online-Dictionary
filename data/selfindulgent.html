self-indulgent
ADJ-GRADED 放纵自己的;任性的 If you say that someone is self-indulgent, you mean that they allow themselves to have or do the things that they enjoy very much. 放纵自己的;任性的

  • Why give publicity to this self-indulgent, adolescent oaf?.

    为什么要宣传报道这个行为任性、青春期未满的白痴呢？

  • To buy flowers for myself seems wildly self-indulgent.

    为自己买花似乎太放纵自己了。

SYN extravagant, excessive, lavish, intemperate
