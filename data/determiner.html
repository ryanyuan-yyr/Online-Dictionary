determiner
dɪtɜːʳmɪnəʳ diˈtɜrmɪnər dɪˈtɜrmɪnər
Word forms: plural determiners
N-COUNT (语法中的)限定词 In grammar, a determiner is a word which is used at the beginning of a noun group to indicate, for example, which thing you are referring to or whether you are referring to one thing or several. Common English determiners are 'a', 'the', 'some', 'this', and 'each'. (语法中的)限定词
