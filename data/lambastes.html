lambaste
læmbeɪst læmˈbeɪst læmˈbæst
(also lambast)Word forms: lambastes, lambasting, lambasted
V-T (公开地)严厉斥责If you lambaste someone, you criticize them severely, usually in public. (公开地)严厉斥责 [formal]

  • And you can be sure that when the referee cheats our boys, I'll be ready to lambaste him.

    而且你可以放心，如果裁判对孩子们不公平，我会公开斥责他。
