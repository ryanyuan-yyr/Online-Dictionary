paraglide
pærəglaɪd ˈpærəɡlaɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense paraglides, present participle paragliding, past tense , past participle paraglided
VERB 进行滑翔伞运动 If a person paraglides, they jump from an aircraft or off a hill or tall building while wearing a special parachute which allows them to control the way they float to the ground. 进行滑翔伞运动 [Also VERB]

  • They planned to paraglide from Long Mountain. [VERB prep.]

    他们计划从长山滑翔而下。

paragliding

  • Hang gliding and paragliding are allowed from the top of Windy Hill.

    温迪山顶允许进行悬挂式滑翔和滑翔伞运动。
