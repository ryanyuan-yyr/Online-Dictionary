stickler
stɪkləʳ ˈstɪklər
Word forms: plural sticklers
N-COUNT 坚持…的人 If you are a stickler for something, you always demand or require it. 坚持…的人

  • I'm a bit of a stickler for accuracy.

    我总是要求精确。

  • Lucy was a stickler for perfection, and everything had to be exactly right. [+ for]

    露西是个追求完美的人，凡事都得完全符合要求。

SYN fanatic, nut [slang] , maniac [informal] , purist
