do out of
PHRASAL VERB （以不正当的手段）剥夺，使丧失 If you do someone out of something, you unfairly cause them not to have or get a particular thing that they were expecting to have. （以不正当的手段）剥夺，使丧失 [informal]

  • He complains that the others have done him out of his share. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    他抱怨其他人骗走了他应得的那份。

SYN cheat out of, deprive of, rob of, dispossess of
