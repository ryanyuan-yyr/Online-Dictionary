hanger-on
ˈhæŋərˈɑn
Word forms: plural hangers-on
N-COUNT （尤指为获取好处的）逢迎者，攀附权贵者 If you describe someone as a hanger-on, you are critical of them because they are trying to be friendly with a richer or more important person, especially in order to gain an advantage for themselves. （尤指为获取好处的）逢迎者，攀附权贵者 [disapproval]

  • For every one or two talented people in any group of artists, there are hordes of talentless hangers-on.

    在任何一群艺术家中，每一两个有才华的人周围总有一帮平庸无能的溜须拍马者。

SYN parasite, follower, cohort [mainly US] , leech
