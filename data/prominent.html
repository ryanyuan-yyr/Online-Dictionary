prominent
prɒmɪnənt ˈprɑmənənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 重要的；杰出的；著名的 Someone who is prominent is important. 重要的；杰出的；著名的

  • ...a prominent member of the Law Society.

    法律协会的重要成员

  • ...the children of very prominent or successful parents.

    杰出或成功人士的孩子

SYN famous, leading, top, chief  
2 ADJ-GRADED 显著的；突出的；鲜艳的；引人注目的 Something that is prominent is very noticeable or is an important part of something else. 显著的；突出的；鲜艳的；引人注目的

  • Here the window plays a prominent part in the design.

    这里窗户在设计中起着非常重要的作用。

  • ...Romania's most prominent independent newspaper.

    罗马尼亚最著名的独立报纸

prominently

  • Trade will figure prominently in the second day of talks in Washington.

    华盛顿会谈的第二天将重点讨论贸易问题。

  • Entries will be prominently displayed in the exhibition hall.

    参赛作品将陈列在展厅显眼的地方。
