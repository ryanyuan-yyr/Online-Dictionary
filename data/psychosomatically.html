psychosomatic
saɪkoʊsoʊmætɪk ˌsaɪkəʊsəˈmætɪk ˌsaɪkoʊsoʊˈmætɪk
ADJ 由心理负担导致的；由精神压力引起的 If someone has a psychosomatic illness, their symptoms are caused by worry or unhappiness rather than by a physical problem. 由心理负担导致的；由精神压力引起的

  • Doctors refused to treat her, claiming that her problems were all psychosomatic.

    医生拒绝对她进行治疗，声称她的问题完全是心理作用引起的。

SYN (all) in the mind, psychological, unconscious, subconscious
