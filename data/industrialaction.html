industrial action
N-UNCOUNT 劳工行动(如怠工、罢工等) If workers take industrial action, they join together and do something to show that they are unhappy with their pay or working conditions, for example refusing to work. 劳工行动(如怠工、罢工等) [mainly British]

  • Prison officers have decided to take industrial action.

    狱警们已决定举行罢工。
