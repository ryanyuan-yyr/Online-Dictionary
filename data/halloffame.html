hall of fame
Word forms: plural halls of fame
1 N-SING （某领域中的）杰出人物，佼佼者 If you say that someone is a member of a particular hall of fame, you mean that they are one of the most famous people in that area of activity. （某领域中的）杰出人物，佼佼者

  • Vivienne Westwood has scaled the heights of fashion's hall of fame.

    薇薇恩·韦斯特伍德已成为时装界的翘楚。

2 N-COUNT （美国的）名人纪念堂 In the United States, a hall of fame is a type of museum where people can see things relating to famous people who are connected with a particular area of activity. （美国的）名人纪念堂
