blood-letting
ˈblʌdˌlɛtɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （尤指敌军之间的）武力冲突，流血事件，杀戮 Blood-letting is violence or killing between groups of people, especially between rival armies. （尤指敌军之间的）武力冲突，流血事件，杀戮

  • Once again there's been ferocious blood-letting in the township.

    镇区再次发生了残忍的流血事件。

2 N-UNCOUNT （新闻用语）内讧，同室操戈 Journalists sometimes refer to a bitter quarrel between two groups of people from within the same organization as blood-letting. （新闻用语）内讧，同室操戈

  • Hopefully a satisfactory solution can be reached without much blood letting.

    有望在不引起严重内讧的情况下达成一个满意的解决方案。
