cheer on
PHRASAL VERB 为…加油;为…喝彩 When you cheer someone on, you shout loudly in order to encourage them, for example when they are taking part in a game. 为…加油;为…喝彩

  • A thousand supporters packed into the stadium to cheer them on. [VERB noun PREP.]

    1,000名支持者挤进体育馆里为他们加油。

  • Most will probably be cheering on their favourite players. [VERB PREP. noun]

    多数人大概都会为自己喜欢的球员加油。
