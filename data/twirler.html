twirl
twɜːʳl twɜrl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense twirls, present participle twirling, past tense , past participle twirled
1 V-ERG (使)快速转动；（使）轻快地旋转 If you twirl something or if it twirls, it turns around and around with a smooth, fairly fast movement. (使)快速转动；（使）轻快地旋转 [Also VERB]

  • Bonnie twirled her empty glass in her fingers. [VERB noun]

    邦妮用手指旋转着她的空杯子。

  • All around me leaves twirl to the ground. [VERB prep./adv.]

    在我周围，树叶打着转儿纷纷飘落到地上。

SYN twiddle, turn, rotate, wind  
2 VERB (跳舞等时)旋转，转动 If you twirl, you turn around and around quickly, for example when you are dancing. (跳舞等时)旋转，转动

  • Several hundred people twirl around the ballroom dance floor. [VERB prep./adv.]

    几百人在舞厅的舞池里旋转。

SYN turn, whirl, wheel, spin  
3 VERB （用手指）缠绕，盘绕，卷曲 If you twirl something such as your hair, you twist it around your finger. （用手指）缠绕，盘绕，卷曲

  • Sarah lifted her hand and started twirling a strand of hair. [VERB noun]

    萨拉抬起手来开始用手指缠绕一缕头发。
