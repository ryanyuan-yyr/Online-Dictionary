rehouse
riːhaʊz riːˈhaʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rehouses, present participle rehousing, past tense , past participle rehoused
VERB 给…重新安置住房 If someone is rehoused, their council, local government, or other authority provides them with a different house to live in. 给…重新安置住房

  • Many of the 100,000 or so families who lost their homes in the earthquake have still not been rehoused. [be VERB-ed]

    有10万户左右的家庭在地震中失去了家园，其中许多家庭还没有得到安置。

  • The council has agreed to rehouse the family. [VERB noun]

    委员会同意给这个家庭重新安排住房。
