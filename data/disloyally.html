disloyal
dɪslɔɪəl dɪsˈlɔɪəl dɪsˈlɔɪəl
ADJ-GRADED 不忠实的;不忠诚的 Someone who is disloyal to their friends, family, or country does not support them or does things that could harm them. 不忠实的;不忠诚的

  • She was so disloyal to her deputy she made his position untenable. [+ to]

    她对她的副手很不守信义，使得他的职位岌岌可危。

  • Brian sided with his sister, which led his mother to accuse him of being disloyal.

    布赖恩站在他姐姐这一边，他母亲就此指责他不忠。

SYN treacherous, false, unfaithful, subversive
