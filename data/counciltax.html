council tax
N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] (英国地方政府根据个人住房的价值收取，用于支付地方公共服务的)市政税，家庭税 In Britain, council tax is a tax that you pay to your local authority in order to pay for local services such as schools, libraries, and rubbish collection.
