interlude
ɪntəʳluːd ˈɪntərˌlud
Word forms: plural interludes
N-COUNT 插曲;间歇;幕间表演 An interlude is a short period of time when an activity or situation stops and something else happens. 插曲;间歇;幕间表演

  • It was a happy interlude in the Kents' life.

    那是肯特一家的生活中一段幸福的插曲。

  • Superb musical interludes were provided by Sinclair.

    辛克莱献上了精彩的幕间音乐表演。

SYN interval, break, spell, stop
