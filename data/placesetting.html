place setting
Word forms: plural place settings
1 N-COUNT （摆放在餐桌上供一个人用的）一副餐具 A place setting is an arrangement of knives, forks, spoons, and glasses that has been laid out on a table for the use of one person at a meal. （摆放在餐桌上供一个人用的）一副餐具

  • He saw the note on the kitchen table, next to one of the two place settings.

    他看到了餐桌上的那张便条，紧挨着两副餐具中的一副。

2 N-COUNT （一个人可能用到的）一套餐具 A place setting of china or of knives, forks, and spoons is a complete set of all the things that one person might use at a meal. （一个人可能用到的）一套餐具

  • A seven-piece place setting costs about £45.

    一套7头餐具要花45英镑。
