alcove
ælkoʊv ˈælˌkoʊv
Word forms: plural alcoves
N-COUNT 凹室；壁龛 An alcove is a small area of a room which is formed by one part of a wall being built further back than the rest of the wall. 凹室；壁龛

  • In the alcoves on either side of the fire were bookshelves.

    火炉两边的凹室里是书架。

SYN recess, corner, bay, niche
