divide up
1 PHRASAL VERB 划分;分割 If you divide something up, you separate it into smaller or more useful groups. 划分;分割 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The idea is to divide up the country into four sectors. [V P n + into]

    该主张是将国家分成4个防区。

  • The Trust needs a new law to divide it up into smaller bodies. [V n P + into]

    该托管组织需要有新法律来将它分成若干小型机构。

SYN group, sort, separate, arrange  
2 PHRASAL VERB 均分;平分 If you divide something up, you share it out among a number of people or groups in approximately equal parts. 均分;平分 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The aim was to divide up the business, give everyone an equal stake in its future. [VERB PREP. noun]

    目的是为了平分公司股份，让每个人将来承担均等的风险。
