crying
kraɪɪŋ ˈkraɪɪŋ
1 PHRASE 迫切的需要 If you say that there is a crying need for something, you mean that there is a very great need for it. 迫切的需要

  • There is a crying need for more magistrates from the ethnic minority communities.

    亟须更多来自少数民族群体的治安官。

2 PHRASE 让人不安的事;使人烦恼的事;奇耻大辱 You can say that something is a crying shame if you are annoyed and upset about it. 让人不安的事;使人烦恼的事;奇耻大辱 [feelings]

  • It's a crying shame that police have to put up with these mindless attacks.

    令人气愤的是，警察不得不忍受这些无知的批评。
