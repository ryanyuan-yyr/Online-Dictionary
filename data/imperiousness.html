imperious
ɪmpɪəriəs ɪmˈpɪriəs
ADJ-GRADED 专横的;傲慢的;飞扬跋扈的 If you describe someone as imperious, you mean that they have a proud manner and expect to be obeyed. 专横的;傲慢的;飞扬跋扈的 [written]

  • Her attitude is imperious at times.

    她有时态度傲慢。

  • From across the desk she gave him a witheringly imperious look.

    她从桌子对面扫了他一眼，目光咄咄逼人，凌利而专横。

SYN domineering, dictatorial, bossy [informal] , haughty  
imperiously

  • Imperiously she beckoned me out of the room.

    她傲慢地招招手，把我叫出房间。
