apogee
æpədʒiː ˈæpəˌdʒi
N-SING 顶峰；顶点；最高点 The apogee of something such as a culture or a business is its highest or its greatest point. 顶峰；顶点；最高点 [formal]

  • The Alliance for Progress reached its apogee during the first half of the decade.

    这十年中的前五年是进步联盟迈向顶峰的时期。

SYN highest point, top, tip, crown
