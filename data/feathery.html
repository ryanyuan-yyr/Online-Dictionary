feathery
feðəri ˈfɛðəri
1 ADJ-GRADED 羽毛似的;羽状的;轻薄的;绒绒的 If something is feathery, it has an edge divided into a lot of thin parts so that it looks soft. 羽毛似的;羽状的;轻薄的;绒绒的

  • The foliage was soft and feathery.

    叶子如羽毛般轻软柔和。

SYN downy, soft, feathered, fluffy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 轻软的;轻柔的 Feathery is used to describe things that are soft and light. 轻软的;轻柔的

  • ...flurries of small, feathery flakes of snow.

    飘洒着轻柔小雪花的阵雪
