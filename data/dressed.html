dressed
drest drɛst
1 ADJ 穿着衣服的;穿着整齐的 If you are dressed, you are wearing clothes rather than being naked or wearing your night clothes. If you get dressed, you put on your clothes. →see usage note at: wear 穿着衣服的;穿着整齐的

  • He was fully dressed, including shoes.

    他穿戴整齐,鞋都穿好了。

  • He went into his bedroom to get dressed.

    他进卧室穿衣服。

2 ADJ [adverb ADJ] 穿着…衣服的;作…打扮的 If you are dressed in a particular way, you are wearing clothes of a particular colour or kind. 穿着…衣服的;作…打扮的

  • ...a tall thin woman dressed in black. [+ in]

    一个穿着黑衣服的瘦高女子

  • ...a tall, elegantly dressed man.

    一个穿着考究的高个男子

3See also:well-dressed 
4 PHRASE 打扮得引人注目;打扮得花枝招展;衣着光鲜 If someone is dressed to kill, they are wearing very smart or fashionable clothes because they want people to notice them and think they are attractive. 打扮得引人注目;打扮得花枝招展;衣着光鲜 [informal]

  • ...a solitary blonde, beautiful, haughty and dressed to kill.

    一位漂亮孤傲、打扮时髦的金发女郎
