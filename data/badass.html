badass
bædæs ˈbædˌæs ˈbædˌæs
(also bad-ass)Word forms: badasses
1 N-COUNT 粗鲁的人; 坏蛋If you describe someone as a badass, you mean that they are very tough or violent. 粗鲁的人; 坏蛋 [mainly US, informal, vulgar]

  • ...the legendary Memphis badass George Tiller.

    ...传说中的孟斐斯坏蛋乔治提勒。

2 ADJ 粗鲁的; 无法无天的Badass is also an adjective. 粗鲁的; 无法无天的

  • They want to be somebody, want to be able to go home a big, badass Marine.

    他们希望能成为大人物，以能呼风唤雨的海军陆战队军官的身份返乡。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 了不起的Some people use badass to describe something they think is very good or impressive. 了不起的 [mainly US, informal, approval]

  • Recent Emmy-winner Joey Pants is one bust-out, badass dude who deserves a hit.

    最新的艾美奖得奖人乔依潘兹是一个突然串红的人，成为风云人物名副其实。
