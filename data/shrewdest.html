shrewd
ʃruːd ʃrud
Word forms: comparative shrewder, superlative shrewdest
ADJ-GRADED 精明的;敏锐的;有眼光的;精于盘算的 A shrewd person is able to understand and judge a situation quickly and to use this understanding to their own advantage. 精明的;敏锐的;有眼光的;精于盘算的

  • She's a shrewd businesswoman.

    她是个精明的女商人。

  • His grey eyes were shrewd but kindly.

    他灰色的眼睛目光锐利却很亲切。

  • It should prove a shrewd investment.

    这将是一项精明的投资。

SYN astute, clever, sharp, knowing  
shrewdly

  • She looked at him shrewdly.

    她目光敏锐地看着他。

  • ...a shrewdly worded statement.

    一份措辞得体的声明

shrewdness

  • His natural shrewdness tells him what is needed to succeed.

    他与生俱来的精明告诉他成功所需要的一切。
