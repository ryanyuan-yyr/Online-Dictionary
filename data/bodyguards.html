bodyguard
bɒdigɑːʳd ˈbɑdiˌgɑrd
Word forms: plural bodyguards
N-COUNT 保镖；警卫（队） A bodyguard is a person or a group of people employed to protect someone. 保镖；警卫（队）

  • Three of his bodyguards were injured in the attack.

    在这次袭击事件中，他有3名保镖受了伤。

  • The King had brought his own bodyguard of twenty armed men.

    国王带了20名持枪的贴身侍卫。

SYN guard, minder, defender, guardian
