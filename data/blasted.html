blasted
blɑːstɪd blæstɪd ˈblæstɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 讨厌的；该死的 Some people use blasted to express anger or annoyance at something or someone. 讨厌的；该死的 [informal, old-fashioned, feelings]

  • I couldn't get that blasted door open.

    我打不开那扇该死的门。

SYN damned [slang] , bloody [slang] , freaking [slang , mainly US] , confounded  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 荒凉的 A blasted landscape has very few plants or trees, and makes you feel sad or depressed when you look at it. 荒凉的 [literary]

  • ...the blasted landscape where the battle was fought.

    战地荒凉的景象

SYN ruined, destroyed, wasted, devastated
