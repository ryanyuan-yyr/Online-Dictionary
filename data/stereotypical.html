stereotypical
sterioʊtɪpɪkəl ˌstɛriəˈtɪpɪkəl ˌ stɪriəˈtɪpɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 典型的;带有成见的;已成定型的 A stereotypical idea of a type of person or thing is a fixed general idea that a lot of people have about it, that may be false in many cases. 典型的;带有成见的;已成定型的

  • Dara challenges our stereotypical ideas about gender and femininity.

    达拉对我们老一套的性别和女性特质观念提出了异议。
