diagonal
daɪægənəl daɪˈægənəl daɪˈægnəl
Word forms: plural diagonals
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 斜的;对角的 A diagonal line or movement goes in a sloping direction, for example, from one corner of a square across to the opposite corner. 斜的;对角的

  • ...a pattern of diagonal lines.

    斜纹图案

SYN slanting, angled, oblique, cross  
diagonally

  • Vaulting the stile, he headed diagonally across the paddock.

    翻身跃过栅栏后，他便朝斜穿围场的方向走去。

  • In a moment he was seated, diagonally opposite her, brow furrowed.

    很快他便眉头紧蹙地在她斜对面落座了。

2 N-COUNT 斜线 A diagonal is a line that goes in a sloping direction. 斜线

  • The bed linen is patterned in stylish checks, stripes, diagonals and triangles.

    床单有漂亮的方格形、条纹、斜纹和三角形图案。

3 N-COUNT (几何学中的)对角线 In geometry, a diagonal is a straight line that joins two opposite corners in a flat four-sided shape such as a square. (几何学中的)对角线

  • Mark five points an equal distance apart along the diagonals.

    在对角线上等距地标出5点。
