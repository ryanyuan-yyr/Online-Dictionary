firebomb
faɪəʳbɒm ˈfaɪrˌbɑm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense firebombs, present participle firebombing, past tense , past participle firebombed
1 N-COUNT 燃烧弹 A firebomb is a type of bomb which is designed to cause fires. 燃烧弹
2 VERB 用燃烧弹焚烧 To firebomb a building, vehicle, or place means to set fire to it using a firebomb. 用燃烧弹焚烧

  • Protestors firebombed the embassy building yesterday. [VERB noun]

    抗议者昨天用燃烧弹焚烧了使馆建筑。

firebombing

  • The homes bore evidence of firebombing.

    这些房屋留有燃烧弹焚烧的痕迹。
