revitalize
riːvaɪtəlaɪz riˈvaɪtəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense revitalizes, present participle revitalizing, past tense , past participle revitalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 revitalise
1 VERB 使新生;使复兴;使恢复活力（或健康） To revitalize something that has lost its activity or its health means to make it active or healthy again. 使新生;使复兴;使恢复活力（或健康）

  • This hair conditioner is excellent for revitalizing dry, lifeless hair. [VERB noun]

    这种护发素能够非常有效地修复干枯的头发。

  • ...the revitalized Democratic Party. [VERB-ed]

    复兴的民主党

  • ...a revitalizing mid-afternoon treat. [VERB-ing]

    能使人恢复精神的午后茶点

SYN reanimate, restore, renew, refresh
