triad
traɪæd ˈtraɪæd ˈtraɪˌæd
Word forms: plural triads
The spelling Triad is also used for meaning 1. 拼写 Triad 亦用于义项 1。
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft NOUN n] 三合会(旧中国秘密组织) The Triads were Chinese secret societies in old China. 三合会(旧中国秘密组织)

  • …gangs, known as the Triads.

    称作“三合会”的帮派

2 N-COUNT 三个一组;三件一套;三位一体 A triad is a group of three similar things. 三个一组;三件一套;三位一体 [formal]

  • For the faculty, there exists the triad of responsibilities: teaching, research, and service. [+ of]

    教员要承担三重责任:教学、研究与服务。

SYN threesome, triple, trio, trinity
