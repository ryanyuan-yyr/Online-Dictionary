conflate
kənfleɪt kənˈfleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense conflates, present participle conflating, past tense , past participle conflated
1 V-RECIP-ERG 合并;混合 If you conflate two or more descriptions or ideas, or if they conflate, you combine them in order to produce a single one. 合并;混合 [formal]

    在她的信中过去和现在融为一体。

    不幸的是，公众把小说和现实混为一谈，把她当成了圣徒。

    这两个意思混在一起了。
