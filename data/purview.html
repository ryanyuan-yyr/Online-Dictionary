purview
pɜːʳvjuː ˈpɜrˌvju
N-SING （组织或活动的）范围，权限 The purview of something such as an organization or activity is the range of things it deals with. （组织或活动的）范围，权限 [formal]

  • That, however, was beyond the purview of the court; it was a diplomatic matter. [+ of]

    但是，那已不在法庭权限之内；那是个外交问题。
