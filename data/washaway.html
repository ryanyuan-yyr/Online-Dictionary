wash away
PHRASAL VERB 冲垮;冲走 If rain or floods wash away something, they destroy it and carry it away. 冲垮;冲走 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Flood waters washed away one of the main bridges in Pusan. [VERB PREP. noun]

    洪水冲垮了釜山的一座主要桥梁。

  • This causes environmental damage when the topsoil is washed away by the rains. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    雨水冲走表层土壤会造成环境破坏。

SYN erode, corrode, eat into, wear something away
