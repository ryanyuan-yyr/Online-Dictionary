feckless
fekləs ˈfɛklɪs
ADJ-GRADED 无能的;软弱的;不中用的 If you describe someone as feckless, you mean that they lack determination or strength, and are unable to do anything properly. 无能的;软弱的;不中用的 [formal, disapproval]

  • He regarded the young man as feckless and irresponsible.

    他认为这个年轻人不光无能，还不负责任。

SYN irresponsible, useless [informal] , hopeless [informal] , incompetent
