free-for-all ˈfrifərˌɔl
Word forms: plural free-for-alls
1 N-SING 可自由参加的活动;众人同时争抢的情形 A free-for-all is a situation in which several people or groups are trying to get something for themselves and there are no controls on how they do it. 可自由参加的活动;众人同时争抢的情形
2 N-COUNT 混战;群架;众人都参与的争吵 A free-for-all is a disorganized fight or argument which lots of people join in. 混战;群架;众人都参与的争吵
