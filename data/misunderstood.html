misunderstood
mɪsʌndəʳstʊd ˌmɪsʌndərˈstʊd
1 Misunderstood is the past tense and past participle of misunderstand .
2 ADJ-GRADED 被误解的;被曲解的 If you describe someone or something as misunderstood, you mean that people do not understand them and have a wrong impression or idea of them. 被误解的;被曲解的

  • Eric is very badly misunderstood.

    人们对埃里克误会很深。

  • The cost of capital is widely misunderstood.

    对资本成本存在着广泛的误解。

  • ...a misunderstood genius.

    未被承认的天才

SYN misjudged, misinterpreted, misread, misconstrued
