public domain
N-SING 公有领域；公有产业 If information is in the public domain, it is not secret and can be used or discussed by anyone. 公有领域；公有产业

  • It is outrageous that the figures are not in the public domain.

    让人愤慨的是那些数字并未公开。

  • The state of their marriage has been put into the public domain.

    他们的婚姻状况已公开。
