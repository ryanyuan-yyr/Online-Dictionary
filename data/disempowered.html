disempower
dɪsɪmpaʊəʳ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense disempowers, present participle disempowering, past tense , past participle disempowered
VERB [oft passive] 使没有权力（或影响力）；剥夺…的权力 If someone or something disempowers you, they take away your power or influence. 使没有权力（或影响力）；剥夺…的权力

  • She feels that women have been disempowered throughout history. [be VERB-ed]

    她认为在历史的长河中女性一直是被剥夺了权利的。
