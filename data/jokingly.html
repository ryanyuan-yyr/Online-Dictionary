jokingly
dʒoʊkɪŋli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 开玩笑地；闹着玩地；戏谑地 If you say or do something jokingly, you say or do it with the intention of amusing someone, rather than with any serious meaning or intention. 开玩笑地；闹着玩地；戏谑地

  • Sarah jokingly called her 'my monster'.

    萨拉戏称她为“我的怪兽”。

  • She frowned at him, only half-jokingly.

    她半开玩笑地冲他皱了皱眉。
