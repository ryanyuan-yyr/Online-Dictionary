comp
kɒmp kɒmp kɑmp
Word forms: comps, comping, comped
1 N-UNCOUNT compensation的缩写Comp is short for compensation. compensation的缩写 [informal]

  • Workers' comp pays for work-related medical problems.

    工人们的赔偿金支付了职业病的医疗费。

2 V-T 免费招待If someone, or if a place such as a hotel or a restaurant comps you, or if they comp you to something, they give you a room or a meal without charging you for it. 免费招待 [US, informal]

  • I comped him his lunch and lent him petrol money to get home.

    我免费招待了他一顿午饭，并借给了他回家的油费。
