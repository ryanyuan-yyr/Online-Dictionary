reproachful
rɪproʊtʃfʊl rɪˈproʊtʃfəl
ADJ-GRADED （表情或话语）责备的，责怪的，谴责的 Reproachful expressions or remarks show that you are disappointed, upset, or angry because someone has done something wrong. （表情或话语）责备的，责怪的，谴责的

  • She gave Isabelle a reproachful look.

    她责备地看了伊莎贝尔一眼。

  • He turned to Alex and his tone was reproachful.

    他转向亚历克斯，语气里充满了责备。

SYN critical, disappointed, abusive, disapproving  
reproachfully

  • Luke's mother stopped smiling and looked reproachfully at him.

    卢克的妈妈收起了笑容，用责备的眼光看着他。
