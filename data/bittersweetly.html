bittersweet
bɪtəʳswiːt ˈbɪtərˌswit
(also bitter-sweet)
1 ADJ 苦乐参半的 If you describe an experience as bittersweet, you mean that it has some happy aspects and some sad ones. 苦乐参半的

  • ...bittersweet memories of his first appearance for the team.

    他首次代表球队登场亮相时苦乐参半的记忆

2 ADJ 又苦又甜的 A bittersweet taste seems bitter and sweet at the same time. 又苦又甜的

  • ...a wine with a bitter-sweet flavour.

    苦中带甜的酒
