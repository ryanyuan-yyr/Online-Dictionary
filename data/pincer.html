pincer
pɪnsəʳ ˈpɪnsə
Word forms: plural pincers
1 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] 钳子；胡桃钳 Pincers consist of two pieces of metal that are hinged in the middle. They are used as a tool for gripping things or for pulling things out. 钳子；胡桃钳

  • His surgical instruments were a knife and a pair of pincers.

    他的外科手术器械是一把刀和一把钳子。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （蟹、虾等的）螯 The pincers of an animal such as a crab or a lobster are its front claws. （蟹、虾等的）螯
