profusion
prəfjuːzən proʊˈfjuʒən prəˈfjuʒən
N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb, oft in NOUN] 大量；丰富 If there is a profusion of something or if it occurs in profusion, there is a very large quantity or variety of it. 大量；丰富 [formal]

  • The Dart is a delightful river with a profusion of wild flowers along its banks. [+ of]

    达特河是一条令人心旷神怡的河，岸边到处开着野花。

  • Olive groves, grapes, and citrus fruits grow in profusion.

    橄榄、葡萄和柑橘类水果产量丰富。

SYN abundance, wealth, excess, quantity
