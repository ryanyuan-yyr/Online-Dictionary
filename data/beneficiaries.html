beneficiary
benɪfɪʃəri -ʃieri ˌbɛnəˈfɪʃiˌɛri ˌbɛnəˈfɪʃəri
Word forms: plural beneficiaries
1 N-COUNT 受益人；受惠者 Someone who is a beneficiary of something is helped by it. 受益人；受惠者

  • The main beneficiaries of pension equality so far have been men. [+ of]

    迄今为止，退休金均等政策的主要受益者一直是男性。

SYN recipient, receiver, payee, assignee  
2 N-COUNT （遗嘱的）受益人 The beneficiaries of a will are legally entitled to receive money or property from someone when that person dies. （遗嘱的）受益人
