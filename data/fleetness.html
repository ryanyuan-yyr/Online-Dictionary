fleet
fliːt flit
Word forms: plural fleets
1 N-COUNT 船队;舰队 A fleet is a group of ships organized to do something together, for example to fight battles or to catch fish. 船队;舰队

  • The damage inflicted upon the British fleet was devastating.

    英国舰队遭到了毁灭性的重创。

  • ...restaurants supplied by local fishing fleets.

    由当地捕鱼船队供货的饭馆

SYN navy, vessels, task force, squadron  
2 N-COUNT (尤指同一机构或统一行动的)车队 A fleet of vehicles is a group of them, especially when they all belong to a particular organization or business, or when they are all going somewhere together. (尤指同一机构或统一行动的)车队

  • With its own fleet of trucks, the company delivers most orders overnight. [+ of]

    这个公司拥有自己的货车车队，大多数订购货品可以隔日送到。

  • A fleet of ambulances took the injured to hospital.

    一队救护车把伤者送往医院。
