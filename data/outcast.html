outcast
aʊtkɑːst -kæst ˈaʊtˌkæst
Word forms: plural outcasts
N-COUNT 被排斥者；被抛弃者 An outcast is someone who is not accepted by a group of people or by society. 被排斥者；被抛弃者

  • He had always been an outcast, unwanted and alone.

    他一直都被视为异类，无人理睬，形单影只。

  • All of us felt like social outcasts.

    我们都觉得自己像是社会的弃儿。

SYN pariah, exile, outlaw, undesirable
