bathing costume
beɪðɪŋ kɒstjuːm ˈbeɪðɪŋ -tuːm
Word forms: plural bathing costumes
N-COUNT （尤指女式）游泳衣,泳装 A bathing costume is a piece of clothing that is worn for swimming, especially by women and girls. （尤指女式）游泳衣,泳装 [British, old-fashioned]

  • She wore a one-piece white bathing costume.

    她穿着白色的连体泳衣。

SYN swimming costume, bikini, cossie [informal] , swimming togs [informal]
