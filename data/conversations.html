conversation
kɒnvəʳseɪʃən ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən
Word forms: plural conversations
1 N-COUNT 交谈;谈话;会话 If you have a conversation with someone, you talk with them, usually in an informal situation. 交谈;谈话;会话

  • He's a talkative guy, and I struck up a conversation with him. [+ with]

    他是一个健谈的人，于是我开始和他攀谈起来。

  • I waited for her to finish a telephone conversation.

    我等着她打完电话。

SYN talk, exchange, discussion, dialogue  
2 PHRASE 正在谈话;正在交谈 If you say that people are in conversation, you mean that they are talking together. 正在谈话;正在交谈

  • When I arrived I found her in conversation with Mrs Williams. [+ with]

    我到的时候，发现她正在和威廉斯夫人谈话。

3 PHRASE 搭话;找话说;说应酬话 If you make conversation, you talk to someone in order to be polite and not because you really want to. 搭话;找话说;说应酬话

  • He had been trying to make conversation.

    他一直在尽量找话说。
