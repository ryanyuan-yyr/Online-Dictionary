map out
PHRASAL VERB 设计;计划;规划;安排 If you map out something that you are intending to do, you work out in detail how you will do it. 设计;计划;规划;安排

  • I went home and mapped out my strategy. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我回到家里制定出了我的策略。

  • I cannot conceive of anybody writing a play by sitting down and mapping it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我想象不出有谁能闭门造车写出剧本。

  • This whole plan has been most carefully mapped out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    整个方案的规划极为精细。

SYN set out, plan, detail, plot
