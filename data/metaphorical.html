metaphorical
metəfɒrɪkəl -fɔːr-
ADJ-GRADED 隐喻的;比喻意义的;象征的 You use the word metaphorical to indicate that you are not using words with their ordinary meaning, but are describing something by means of an image or symbol. 隐喻的;比喻意义的;象征的

  • It turns out Levy is talking in metaphorical terms.

    利维原来说的是一种比喻。

  • The ship may be heading for the metaphorical rocks unless a buyer can be found.

    除非可以找到买家，否则这条船也许就像朝着岩石撞过去一样下场悲惨。

SYN figurative, symbolic, emblematic, allegorical  
metaphorically

  • You're speaking metaphorically, I hope.

    我希望你是在打比方。
