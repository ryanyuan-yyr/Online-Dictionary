hippie
hɪpi ˈhɪpi
(also hippy)Word forms: plural hippies
N-COUNT 嬉皮士（20世纪60、70年代的青年颓废派，摈弃传统的生活、着装和行为方式，提倡和平与友爱，常蓄长发并吸毒） Hippies were young people in the 1960s and 1970s who rejected conventional ways of living, dressing, and behaving, and tried to live a life based on peace and love.
