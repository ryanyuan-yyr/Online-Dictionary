perjure
pɜːʳdʒəʳ ˈpɜrdʒər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense perjures, present participle perjuring, past tense , past participle perjured
VERB 作伪证；发假誓 If someone perjures themselves in a court of law, they lie, even though they have promised to tell the truth. 作伪证；发假誓

  • Witnesses lied and perjured themselves. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    证人撒谎作伪证。

  • She would rather perjure herself than admit to her sins. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    她宁愿在法庭上撒谎也不愿承认她的罪行。
