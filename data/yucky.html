yucky
yʌki ˈjʌkɪ ˈjʌki
ADJ 令人作呕的 If you describe a food or other substance as yucky, you mean that it disgusts you. 令人作呕的 [informal, disapproval]

  • She says it tastes yucky, so nurses add sweetener to make it go down easier.

    她说这吃起来很恶心，所以护士加了增甜剂，让她能吃下去。

  • ...yucky cigar smoke.

    ...令人恶心的香烟味道。
