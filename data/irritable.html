irritable
ɪrɪtəbəl ˈɪrɪtəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 易怒的;急躁的 If you are irritable, you are easily annoyed. →see usage note at: angry 易怒的;急躁的

  • He had been waiting for over an hour and was beginning to feel irritable.

    他已经等了一个多小时,开始变得急躁了。

SYN bad-tempered, cross, snappy, hot  
irritably

  • 'Why are you whispering?' he asked irritably.

    “你为什么窃窃私语？”他生气地问。

  • The judge shook his head irritably and Juicks hushed.

    法官生气地摇摇头,朱克斯安静下来。

irritability

  • Patients usually suffer from increased irritability.

    病人通常变得越来越烦躁易怒。
