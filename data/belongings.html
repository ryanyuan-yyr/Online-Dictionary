belongings
bɪlɒŋɪŋz -lɔːŋ-
N-PLURAL [usually poss NOUN] 所有物；（随身）财物 Your belongings are the things that you own, especially things that are small enough to be carried. 所有物；（随身）财物

  • I collected my belongings and left.

    我收拾好随身物品就离开了。

  • He was identified only by his uniform and personal belongings.

    他的身份只是通过制服和个人财物确定了下来。
