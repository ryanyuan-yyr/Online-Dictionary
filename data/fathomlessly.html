fathomless
fæðəmləs ˈfæðəmlɪs
ADJ 深不可测的;深邃的;摸不透的 Something that is fathomless cannot be measured or understood because it gives the impression of being very deep, mysterious, or complicated. 深不可测的;深邃的;摸不透的

  • ...the fathomless space of the universe.

    宇宙神秘莫测的空间

  • The silence was fathomless and overwhelming.

    这种寂静深不可测，令人不知所措。

SYN profound, deep, impenetrable, abysmal
