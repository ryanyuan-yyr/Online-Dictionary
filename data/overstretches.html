overstretch
oʊvəʳstretʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overstretches, present participle overstretching, past tense , past participle overstretched
V-ERG （使）超出承受能力；（使）超负荷运行 If you overstretch something or someone or if they overstretch, you force them to do something they are not really capable of, and they may be harmed as a result. （使）超出承受能力；（使）超负荷运行

  • Dr Boutros Ghali said the operation would overstretch resources. [VERB noun]

    布特罗斯·加利博士说这一行动没有足够的资源支持。

  • Do what you know you can do well and don't overstretch yourself. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    做你拿手的事情，不要勉为其难。

  • Never force your legs to overstretch, or you can cause injuries. [VERB]

    千万不要迫使双腿过度拉伸，否则会受伤的。
