ungrateful
ʌngreɪtfʊl
ADJ-GRADED 不感激的；不领情的；忘恩负义的 If you describe someone as ungrateful, you are criticizing them for not showing thanks or for being unkind to someone who has helped them or done them a favour. 不感激的；不领情的；忘恩负义的 [disapproval]

  • I thought it was rather ungrateful.

    我觉得这样做有些忘恩负义。

  • You ungrateful brat.

    你这个没良心的小坏蛋。

SYN unappreciative, thankless, unthankful, heedless
