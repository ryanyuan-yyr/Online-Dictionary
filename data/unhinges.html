unhinge
ʌnhɪndʒ ʌnˈhɪndʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unhinges, present participle unhinging, past tense , past participle unhinged
VERB 使精神错乱（或失常） If you say that an experience has unhinged someone, you mean that it has affected them so deeply that they have become mentally ill. 使精神错乱（或失常）

  • The stress of war temporarily unhinged him. [VERB noun]

    战争的压力让他一时丧失了神志。

SYN unbalance, confuse, derange, disorder  
unhinged

  • ...feelings that make you feel completely unhinged and crazy.

    令人彻底发疯发狂的情绪
