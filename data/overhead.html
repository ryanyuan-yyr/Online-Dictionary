overhead
oʊvəʳhed oʊvəʳhed ˈoʊvərˌhɛd ˌ oʊvərˈhɛd
The adjective is pronounced /'əʊvəhed/. The adverb is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'hed/. 形容词读作 /'əʊvəhed/。副词读作 /ˌəʊvə'hed/。
ADJ [ADJ n] 在头顶上的；在上面的；高架的 You use overhead to indicate that something is above you or above the place that you are talking about. 在头顶上的；在上面的；高架的

  • She turned on the overhead light and looked around the little room.

    她打开头顶上方的灯，环视了一圈这个小房间。

  • ...people who live under or near overhead cables.

    住在高架电缆下或其附近的人

SYN raised, suspended, elevated, aerial  
Overhead is also an adverb.

  • ...planes passing overhead.

    从头顶上空飞过的飞机

  • Now there were only the stars overhead.

    现在抬头只能看见星星。
