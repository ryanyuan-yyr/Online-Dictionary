squarely
skweəʳli ˈskwɛəlɪ
1 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 径直;正中地 Squarely means directly or in the middle, rather than indirectly or at an angle. 径直;正中地

  • I kept the gun aimed squarely at his eyes.

    我一直将枪径直瞄准他的双眼。

SYN directly, straight, exactly, precisely  
2 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 完全地;毫无疑问地 If something such as blame or responsibility lies squarely with someone, they are definitely the person responsible. 完全地;毫无疑问地

  • The president put the blame squarely on his opponent.

    总统将责任完全归咎于对手。

  • Responsibility for success or failure lies squarely with the Nigerians.

    成败完全取决于尼日利亚人。

SYN fully, completely, totally, entirely  
3 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 直截了当地;不回避地 If you face something squarely, you face it directly, without trying to avoid it. 直截了当地;不回避地

  • The management committee have faced the situation squarely.

    管理委员会直面当前形势。

  • We have to drop our pretences and look squarely at schooling as it really is.

    我们必须抛开一切伪装，正视学校教育的真实情况。

SYN resolutely, firmly, directly, boldly
