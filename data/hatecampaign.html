hate campaign
heɪt ˌkæmˈpeɪn
Word forms: plural hate campaigns
N-COUNT [usually singular] 仇恨运动;诋毁运动 A hate campaign is a series of actions which are intended to harm or upset someone, or to make other people have a low opinion of them. 仇恨运动;诋毁运动

  • The media has waged a virulent hate campaign against her.

    新闻界向她发起了恶毒攻击。
