mediocre
miːdioʊkəʳ ˌmidiˈoʊkər ˈmidiˌoʊkər
ADJ-GRADED （事物）普通的，平庸的，一般的 If you describe something as mediocre, you mean that it is of average quality but you think it should be better. （事物）普通的，平庸的，一般的 [disapproval]

  • His school record was mediocre.

    他在学校成绩平平。

  • ...mediocre music.

    不尽如人意的音乐

SYN second-rate, average, ordinary, indifferent
