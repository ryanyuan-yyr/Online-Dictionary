pummel
pʌməl ˈpʌməl ˈpʌməl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pummels, present participle pummelling, past tense , past participle pummelled
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 pummeling, pummeled
VERB 连续猛击；反复拳打；捶打 If you pummel someone or something, you hit them many times using your fists. 连续猛击；反复拳打；捶打

  • He trapped Conn in a corner and pummeled him ferociously for thirty seconds. [VERB noun]

    他将康恩逼进一个角落，用拳头暴打了他半分钟。

SYN beat, punch, pound, strike
