simian
sɪmiən ˈsɪmiən
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 像猿猴的;长得像猿猴似的 If someone has a simian face, they look rather like a monkey. 像猿猴的;长得像猿猴似的 [formal]

  • Ada had a wrinkled, simian face.

    埃达有一张布满皱纹、长得像猿猴的脸。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 猴的;类人猿的 Simian is used to describe things relating to monkeys or apes. 猴的;类人猿的 [technical]

  • ...a simian virus.

    一种猴病毒
