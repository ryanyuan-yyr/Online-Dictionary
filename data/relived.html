relive
riːlɪv riːˈlɪv riˈlɪv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense relives, present participle reliving, past tense , past participle relived
VERB （在记忆或想象中）再次体验，回味，重温 If you relive something that has happened to you in the past, you remember it and imagine that you are experiencing it again. （在记忆或想象中）再次体验，回味，重温

  • There is no point in reliving the past. [VERB noun]

    回味过去没有任何意义。

  • Last night he relived his terrifying ordeal. [VERB noun]

    昨夜他又一次体验了那种可怕的折磨。
