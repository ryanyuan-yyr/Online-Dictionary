calamity
kəlæmɪti kəˈlæməti
Word forms: plural calamities
N-VAR 灾难;灾祸; 祸患 A calamity is an event that causes a great deal of damage, destruction, or personal distress. 灾难;灾祸; 祸患 [formal]

  • He described drugs as the greatest calamity of the age.

    他称毒品是这个时代最大的祸害。

  • ...the calamity of war.

    兵燹战祸

  • It could only end in calamity.

    它只会落得个灾难性的下场。

SYN disaster, tragedy, ruin, distress  
Quotations
Calamities are of two kinds: misfortune to ourselves, and good fortune to othersAmbrose Bierce The Devil's Dictionary
