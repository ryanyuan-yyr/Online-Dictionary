beep
biːp bip
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense beeps, present participle beeping, past tense , past participle beeped
1 N-COUNT; SOUND （汽车喇叭、电话答录机等发出的）嘟嘟声，哔哔声 A beep is a short, loud sound like that made by a car horn or a telephone answering machine. （汽车喇叭、电话答录机等发出的）嘟嘟声，哔哔声
2 V-ERG （使）发出嘟嘟声；（使）发出哔哔声 If something such as a horn beeps, or you beep it, it makes a short, harsh sound. （使）发出嘟嘟声；（使）发出哔哔声

  • A cellular telephone beeped. [VERB]

    有个手机响了。

  • He beeped the horn. [VERB noun]

    他鸣了一下喇叭。
