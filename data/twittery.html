twitter
twɪtəʳ ˈtwɪtər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense twitters, present participle twittering, past tense , past participle twittered
1 VERB (鸟)吱喳，唧啾 When birds twitter, they make a lot of short high-pitched sounds. (鸟)吱喳，唧啾

  • There were birds twittering in the eucalyptus trees. [VERB]

    桉树上鸟儿在吱吱喳喳地叫。

  • ...a tree filled with twittering birds. [VERB-ing]

    枝头落满了啁啾鸟雀的树

SYN chirrup, whistle, chatter, trill  
Twitter is also a noun.

  • Naomi would waken to the twitter of birds.

    娜奥米会被鸟儿吱吱喳喳的叫声唤醒。

2 VERB 嘁嘁喳喳地说 If you say that someone is twittering about something, you mean that they are speaking about silly or unimportant things, usually rather fast or in a high-pitched voice. 嘁嘁喳喳地说 [Also VERB]

  • ...debutantes twittering excitedly about Christian Dior dresses. [VERB + about]

    嘁嘁喳喳兴奋地谈论着克里斯汀·迪奥女装的初入社交界的富家小姐们

  • She laughs, blushes and twitters: 'Oh, doesn't Giles have just the most charming sense of humour?' [VERB with quote]

    她羞赧地笑了，尖声细气地说道：“哦，难道贾尔斯的幽默感不正是特招人喜欢的那种吗？”
