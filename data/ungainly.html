ungainly
ʌngeɪnli ʌnˈgeɪnli
ADJ-GRADED （人、兽、车船）笨拙的，难看的 If you describe a person, animal, or vehicle as ungainly, you mean that they look awkward or clumsy, often because they are big. （人、兽、车船）笨拙的，难看的

  • The dog, an ungainly mongrel pup, was loping about the road.

    那只丑陋的杂种幼犬在马路上到处乱跑。

  • Paul swam in his ungainly way to the side of the pool.

    保罗笨拙地游到池子边。

SYN awkward, clumsy, inelegant, lumbering
