rearward
rɪəʳwəʳd ˈrɪrwərd ˈrɪrˌwɔrd
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 向后面；朝后面 If something moves or faces rearward, it moves or faces backwards. 向后面；朝后面

  • ...a rearward facing infant carrier.

    后向式婴儿背带

  • The centre of pressure moves rearward and the aeroplane becomes unbalanced.

    气压中心后移使飞机失去平衡。

Rearward is also an adjective.

  • ...the rearward window.

    后边的窗户
