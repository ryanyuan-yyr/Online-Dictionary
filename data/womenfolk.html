womenfolk
wɪmɪnfoʊk ˈwɪmɪnˌfəʊk ˈwɪmɪnˌfoʊk
N-PLURAL [oft poss NOUN] (尤指由男子统治或组织的群体中的)妇女们，女人们 Some people refer to the women of a particular community as its womenfolk, especially when the community is ruled or organized by men. (尤指由男子统治或组织的群体中的)妇女们，女人们

  • Men never notice anything in a house run by their womenfolk.

    男人们从不留意女人们打理的家。
