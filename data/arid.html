arid
ærɪd ˈærɪd ˈɛrɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 干旱的;不毛的 Arid land is so dry that very few plants can grow on it. 干旱的;不毛的

  • ...new strains of crops that can withstand arid conditions.

    能抵抗干旱条件的新品种作物

  • ...the arid zones of the country.

    该国的干旱区域

SYN dry, desert, dried up, barren  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 枯燥无味的；乏善可陈的 If you describe something such as a period of your life or an academic subject as arid, you mean that it has so little interest, excitement, or purpose that it makes you feel bored or unhappy. 枯燥无味的；乏善可陈的

  • She had given him the only joy his arid life had ever known.

    她为他枯燥无味的生活带来了从未有过的乐趣。

  • ...the politically arid years of military dictatorship in the 1960s and '70s.

    在20世纪60和70年代军事独裁期间政事荒芜的岁月

SYN boring, dull, tedious, dreary
