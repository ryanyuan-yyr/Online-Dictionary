superstition
suːpəʳstɪʃən ˌsupərˈstɪʃən
Word forms: plural superstitions
N-VAR 迷信;迷信观念 Superstition is belief in things that are not real or possible, for example magic. 迷信;迷信观念

  • Fortune-telling is a very much debased art surrounded by superstition.

    算命是一种非常低级的把戏，充满了迷信色彩。

  • The phantom of the merry-go-round is just a local superstition.

    旋转木马的幽灵只不过是当地的迷信说法。

SYN myth, story, belief, legend  
Quotations
