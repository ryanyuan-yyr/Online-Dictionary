crossover
krɒsoʊvəʳ krɔːs- ˈkrɔsˌoʊvər
Word forms: plural crossovers
1 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] (尤指音乐或时尚风格上的)混合，融合 A crossover of one style and another, especially in music or fashion, is a combination of the two different styles. (尤指音乐或时尚风格上的)混合，融合

  • ...the contemporary crossover of pop, jazz and funk. [+ of]

    当今流行音乐、爵士乐和乡土爵士乐的融合

  • ...dance/rock crossover bands.

    结合舞蹈和摇滚乐的乐队

2 N-SING (在音乐或时尚上的)转型，改变风格 In music or fashion, if someone makes a crossover from one style to another, they become successful outside the style they were originally known for. (在音乐或时尚上的)转型，改变风格

  • I told her the crossover from actress to singer is easier than singer to actress. [+ from/to]

    我告诉她从演员转型到歌手比从歌手转型到演员更容易。
