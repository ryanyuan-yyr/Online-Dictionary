sedation
sɪdeɪʃən sɪˈdeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [oft under N] (服镇静剂后的)镇静(状态) If someone is under sedation, they have been given medicine or drugs in order to calm them or make them sleep. (服镇静剂后的)镇静(状态)

  • His mother was under sedation after the boy's body was brought back from Germany.

    男孩的尸体从德国运回后，他的母亲一直靠服药保持镇静。
