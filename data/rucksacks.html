rucksack
rʌksæk ˈrʌkˌsæk ˈrʊkˌsæk
Word forms: plural rucksacks
N-COUNT （登山或远足时用的）背包，旅行包 A rucksack is a bag with straps that go over your shoulders, so that you can carry things on your back, for example when you are walking or climbing. （登山或远足时用的）背包，旅行包 [British]
SYN backpack, pack, knapsack, kitbag  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 knap-sack, pack, backpack
