readiness
redɪnəs ˈrɛdinɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT 愿意；乐意 If someone is very willing to do something, you can talk about their readiness to do it. 愿意；乐意

  • ...their readiness to co-operate with the new US envoy.

    他们乐意和新任美国特使合作

SYN willingness, inclination, eagerness, keenness  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually in NOUN] 准备就绪;以备 If you do something in readiness for a future event, you do it so that you are prepared for that event. 准备就绪;以备

  • Security tightened in the capital in readiness for the president's arrival. [+ for]

    首都加强了警备，准备迎接总统的到来。
