middle name
Word forms: plural middle names
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 中名(名与姓之间的名字) Your middle name is the name that comes between your first name and your surname. 中名(名与姓之间的名字)

  • His middle name is Justin.

    他的中名是贾斯廷。

2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 突出的个性；明显的特征 You can use middle name in expressions such as 'discretion was her middle name' and 'his middle name is loyalty' to indicate that someone always behaves with a great deal of a particular quality. 突出的个性；明显的特征 [humorous]

  • Geniality is my middle name. I rarely write a fierce word about any restaurant.

    与人为善是我最大的特点。我在给饭店写评语时很少用到激烈的言辞。
