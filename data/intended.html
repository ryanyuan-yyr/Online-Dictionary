intended
ɪntendɪd ɪnˈtɛndɪd
ADJ [ADJ n] 打算中的;预期的;预定的 You use intended to describe the thing you are trying to achieve or person you are trying to affect. 打算中的;预期的;预定的

  • The intended target had been a military building.

    原定目标是一座军事大楼。

  • Keith hoped the obvious sarcasm would have its intended effect.

    基思希望这一明显的讽刺能达到预期的效果。

  • It's never occurred to me that the intended victim of the fire was Margaret.

    我从未想过这把火竟然是冲玛格丽特放的。

SYN planned, proposed
