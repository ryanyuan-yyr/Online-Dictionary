source
sɔːʳs sɔrs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense sources, present participle sourcing, past tense , past participle sourced
1 N-COUNT 来源;产地 The source of something is the person, place, or thing which you get it from. 来源;产地

  • ...over 40 per cent of British adults use television as their major source of information about the arts.

    40 %以上的英国成年人把电视当作获取艺术文化信息的主要途径。

  • Renewable sources of energy must be used where practical.

    必须在可行的地方使用可再生能源。

  • Tourism, which is a major source of income for the city, may be seriously affected.

    作为该市主要收入来源的旅游业可能会受到严重影响。

SYN informant, authority, documentation  
2 VERB 寻找(产品或原料的)货源 In business, if a person or firm sources a product or a raw material, they find someone who will supply it. 寻找(产品或原料的)货源 [business] [Also V from n]

  • Together they travel the world, sourcing clothes for the small, privately owned company. [VERB noun]

    他们一道走遍世界各地，为那家私营小公司寻觅服装货源。

  • About 60 per cent of an average car is sourced from outside of the manufacturer. [VERB noun]

    一辆普通汽车上大约有60%的部件是由制造商以外的其他供应商提供的。

  • ...furniture sourced from all over the world. [VERB-ed]

    来自世界各地的家具

SYN obtain, get, acquire, score [slang]  
sourcing

  • The union is particularly concerned at the sourcing of products abroad.

    该联盟尤其关注海外的产品货源。

3 N-COUNT 消息来源;消息人士;(论文中引文的)出处，原始资料 A source is a person or book that provides information for a news story or for a piece of research. 消息来源;消息人士;(论文中引文的)出处，原始资料

  • Military sources say the boat was heading south at high speed.

    军方称这艘船当时正高速向南行进。

  • She quotes secondary and primary sources without distinction.

    她对二手资料和一手资料的引用毫无区别。

4 N-COUNT (困难的)根源，原因 The source of a difficulty is its cause. (困难的)根源，原因

  • This gave me a clue as to the source of the problem. [+ of]

    这件事给我提供了找出问题根源的线索。

SYN cause, origin, derivation, beginning  
5 N-COUNT [usually singular] (河流的)源头 The source of a river or stream is the place where it begins. (河流的)源头

  • ...the source of the Tiber. [+ of]

    台伯河的源头

SYN origin, spring, fount, fountainhead
