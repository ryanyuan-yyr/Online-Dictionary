polygraph
pɒligrɑːf -græf ˈpɑlɪˌgræf
Word forms: plural polygraphs
N-COUNT 测谎（试验） A polygraph or a polygraph test is a test which is used by the police to try to find out whether someone is telling the truth. 测谎（试验）

  • Hill's lawyers announced she had taken and passed a polygraph test.

    希尔的律师宣布她已接受并通过了测谎。
