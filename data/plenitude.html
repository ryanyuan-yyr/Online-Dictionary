plenitude
plenɪtjuːd -tuːd ˈplɛnəˌtud ˈplɛnəˌtjud
1 N-UNCOUNT 完满 Plenitude is a feeling that an experience is satisfying because it is full or complete. 完满 [formal]

  • The music brought him a feeling of plenitude and freedom.

    这音乐带给他一种完满和自由的感觉。

  • ...the safety and plenitude of their life.

    他们生活的安定和无忧

SYN completeness, fullness, amplitude, repletion  
2 N-SING 丰富；充足；大量 If there is a plenitude of something, there is a great quantity of it. 丰富；充足；大量 [formal]

  • What is the use of a book about interior design without a plenitude of pictures in color? [+ of]

    要是没有大量的彩色图片，一本关于室内设计的书还有什么用途呢？

SYN abundance, wealth, excess, bounty
