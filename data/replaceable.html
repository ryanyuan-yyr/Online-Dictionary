replaceable
rɪpleɪsəbəl
1 ADJ 可替换的;可替代的 If something is replaceable, you can throw it away when it is finished and put a new one in its place. 可替换的;可替代的

  • ...replaceable butane gas cartridges.

    可替换丁烷气贮气瓶

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 可被取代的 If you say that someone is replaceable, you mean that they are not so important that someone else could not take their place. 可被取代的

  • He would see I was not so easily replaceable.

    他会看到我不是那么容易被替代的。
