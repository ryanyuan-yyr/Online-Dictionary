slash and burn slæʃ əndbɜːn
(also slash-and-burn)
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 刀耕火种农业(指把地上的草木烧成灰做肥料，就地挖坑下种的原始耕作方法) Slash and burn is a method of farming that involves clearing land by destroying and burning all the trees and plants on it, farming there for a short time, and then moving on to clear a new piece of land. 刀耕火种农业(指把地上的草木烧成灰做肥料，就地挖坑下种的原始耕作方法)

  • Traditional slash and burn farming methods have exhausted the soil.

    传统的刀耕火种农业方式耗尽了土地的肥力。
