go back on
PHRASAL VERB 违（约）；食（言）；违背（诺言等） If you go back on a promise or agreement, you do not do what you promised or agreed to do. 违（约）；食（言）；违背（诺言等）

  • The budget crisis has forced the President to go back on his word. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    预算危机迫使总统违背自己的承诺。

SYN repudiate, break, forsake, retract
