regress
rɪgres ˈrigrɛs rɪˈgrɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense regresses, present participle regressing, past tense , past participle regressed
VERB 倒退;退步;退化 When people or things regress, they return to an earlier and less advanced stage of development. 倒退;退步;退化 [formal]

  • ...if your child regresses to babyish behaviour. [V + to/into]

    如果你的孩子退行到婴儿状态

  • Such countries are not 'developing' at all, but regressing. [VERB]

    这些国家根本不是在“发展中”，而是在倒退。

SYN revert, deteriorate, return, go back  
regression

  • This can cause regression in a pupil's learning process.

    这可能会使学生在学习过程中退步。
