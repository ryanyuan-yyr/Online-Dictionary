cotton to
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 开始喜欢 If you cotton to someone or something, you start to like them. 开始喜欢 [US, informal]

  • His style of humor was very human, and that's why people cotton to him. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他的幽默很有人情味，这让人们开始喜欢上他。

  • It seemed to me that I was being shut out of the dialogue and that's something I just don't cotton to. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我觉得自己完全插不上话，这让我很不爽。
