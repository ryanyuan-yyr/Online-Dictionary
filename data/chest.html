chest
tʃest tʃɛst
Word forms: plural chests
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 胸;胸部;胸口;胸膛 Your chest is the top part of the front of your body where your ribs, lungs, and heart are. 胸;胸部;胸口;胸膛

  • He crossed his arms over his chest.

    他双手交叉，放在胸前。

  • He was shot in the chest.

    他胸部中弹。

  • He complained of chest pain.

    他说胸口痛。

SYN breast, front  
-chested

  • He was bare-chested and barefoot.

    他袒胸赤足。

  • I'm 15 and completely flat-chested.

    我现在15岁，胸部扁平。

2 N-COUNT 大箱子；大柜子 A chest is a large, heavy box used for storing things. 大箱子；大柜子

  • At the very bottom of the chest were his carving tools.

    箱子最底下是他的雕刻工具。

  • ...a treasure chest.

    藏宝箱

  • ...a medicine chest.

    药柜

SYN box, case, trunk, crate  
3 PHRASE 倾诉胸中的不快;吐苦水 If you get something off your chest, you talk about something that has been worrying you. 倾诉胸中的不快;吐苦水

  • I feel it's done me good to get it off my chest.

    我感觉吐吐苦水对我有好处。
