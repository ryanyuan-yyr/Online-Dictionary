selfish
selfɪʃ ˈsɛlfɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 自私的;自利的 If you say that someone is selfish, you mean that he or she cares only about himself or herself, and not about other people. 自私的;自利的 [disapproval]

  • I think I've been very selfish. I've been mainly concerned with myself.

    我觉得我一直非常自私。总是只关心自己。

  • ...the selfish interests of a few people.

    少数人的私利

SYN self-centred, self-interested, greedy, mercenary  
selfishly

  • Cabinet Ministers are selfishly pursuing their own vested interests.

    内阁大臣们正在自私地追逐他们自己的既得利益。

selfishness

  • The arrogance and selfishness of different interest groups never ceases to amaze me.

    不同利益集团的傲慢和自私总是会让我大吃一惊。
