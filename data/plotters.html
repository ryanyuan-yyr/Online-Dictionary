plotter
plɒtəʳ ˈplɑtər
Word forms: plural plotters
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] （通常指针对个人或政府的）密谋者，阴谋分子 A plotter is a person who secretly plans with others to do something that is illegal or wrong, usually against a person or government. （通常指针对个人或政府的）密谋者，阴谋分子

  • Coup plotters tried to seize power in Moscow.

    政变谋划者试图夺取莫斯科的政权。

2 N-COUNT 标绘者；标绘器 A plotter is a person or instrument that marks the position of something such as a ship on a map or chart. 标绘者；标绘器
