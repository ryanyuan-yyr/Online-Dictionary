snotty
snɒti ˈsnɑti
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 流着鼻涕的;满是鼻涕的 Something that is snotty produces or is covered in snot. 流着鼻涕的;满是鼻涕的 [informal, rude]

  • He suffered from a snotty nose, runny eyes and a slight cough.

    他又是流鼻涕，又是流眼泪，还有点咳嗽。

2 ADJ-GRADED 傲慢自大的;自以为高人一等的 If you describe someone as snotty, you disapprove of them because they have a very proud and superior attitude to other people. 傲慢自大的;自以为高人一等的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...snotty college kids.

    目空一切的大学生

  • She smiled a snotty smile.

    她高傲地笑了一下。
