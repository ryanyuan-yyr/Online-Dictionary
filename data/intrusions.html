intrusion
ɪntruːʒən ɪnˈtruʒən
Word forms: plural intrusions
1 N-VAR 打扰;侵扰 If someone disturbs you when you are in a private place or having a private conversation, you can call this event an intrusion . 打扰;侵扰

  • I hope you don't mind this intrusion, Jon.

    乔恩,希望你别介意这次打扰。

SYN interruption, interference, infringement, trespass  
2 N-VAR 干扰;影响 An intrusion is something that disturbs your mood or your life in a way you do not like. 干扰;影响

  • ...intrusion into private grief.

    对个人不幸的搅扰

  • I felt it was a grotesque intrusion into our lives. [+ into]

    我感觉那是对我们生活的一种干扰，让人感到荒唐可气。

SYN invasion, breach, infringement, infiltration
