gun-shy
ˈgʌnˌʃaɪ
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 提心吊胆的；如惊弓之鸟的 If someone is gun-shy, they are nervous or afraid. 提心吊胆的；如惊弓之鸟的

  • Everyone I talked to in the company seemed a little gun-shy, perhaps fearing that I was yet another snide reporter.

    公司里同我交谈过的人看上去都有一些提心吊胆，也许担心我是又一位用心险恶的记者。

  • The electric-power industry is gun-shy about building more large plants.

    电力行业对新建大型电厂十分谨慎。
