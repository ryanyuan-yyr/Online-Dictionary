roast
roʊst roʊst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense roasts, present participle roasting, past tense , past participle roasted
1 VERB 烤;烘烤 When you roast meat or other food, you cook it by dry heat in an oven or over a fire. 烤;烘烤

  • I personally would rather roast a chicken whole. [VERB noun]

    我个人更愿意烤整只鸡。

SYN cook, bake, grill, broil [US]  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 烤熟的;烤制的 Roast meat has been cooked by roasting. 烤熟的;烤制的

  • ...roast beef.

    烤牛肉

3 N-COUNT 烤肉 A roast is a piece of meat that is cooked by roasting. →see usage note at: cook 烤肉

  • Come into the kitchen. I've got to put the roast in.

    到厨房来。我得把烤肉放进去。
