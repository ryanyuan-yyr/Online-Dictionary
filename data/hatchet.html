hatchet
hætʃɪt ˈhætʃɪt
Word forms: plural hatchets
1 N-COUNT 短柄小斧 A hatchet is a small axe that you can hold in one hand. 短柄小斧
SYN axe, machete, tomahawk, cleaver  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （脸）瘦削而棱角分明的 Someone with a hatchet face has a long, narrow face with sharp features. （脸）瘦削而棱角分明的

  • ...an old naval sergeant, with a hatchet face and drooping moustaches.

    面庞瘦削、留着两撇八字须的老海军中士

3 PHR-RECIP （争吵后）和解，言归于好，化干戈为玉帛 If two people bury the hatchet, they become friendly again after a quarrel or disagreement. （争吵后）和解，言归于好，化干戈为玉帛
SYN make up, make peace, cease hostilities
