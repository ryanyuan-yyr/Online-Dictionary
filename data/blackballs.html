blackball

Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blackballs, present participle blackballing, past tense , past participle blackballed
VERB 投票反对（某人）加入俱乐部 If the members of a club blackball someone, they vote against that person being allowed to join their club. 投票反对（某人）加入俱乐部

  • Members can blackball candidates in secret ballots. [VERB noun]

    成员可以通过无记名投票反对候选人加入。

SYN remove, bar, ban, exclude
