liqueur
lɪkjʊəʳ likœr -kɜːr lɪˈkɜr lɪˈkʊr lɪˈkjʊr
Word forms: plural liqueurs
1 N-MASS （餐后的）利口酒，烈性甜酒 A liqueur is a strong alcoholic drink with a sweet taste. You drink it after a meal. （餐后的）利口酒，烈性甜酒

  • ...liqueurs such as Grand Marnier and Kirsch.

    柑曼怡和樱桃酒等利口酒

  • ...small glasses of liqueur.

    小杯甜酒

A liqueur is a glass of liqueur.一杯利口酒（或烈性甜酒）

  • 'What about a liqueur with your coffee?' suggested the waitress.

    “咖啡里加杯甜酒怎么样？”女服务员建议。

2 N-COUNT 酒心巧克力 Liqueurs are a type of chocolate. They contain a sweet substance that has the flavour of an alcoholic liqueur. 酒心巧克力

  • ...two boxes of liqueurs.

    两盒酒心巧克力
