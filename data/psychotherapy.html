psychotherapy
saɪkoʊθerəpi ˌsaɪkəʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪks ˌsaɪkoʊˈθɛrəpi
N-UNCOUNT 心理治疗；精神治疗 Psychotherapy is the use of psychological methods in treating people who are mentally ill, rather than using physical methods such as drugs or surgery. 心理治疗；精神治疗

  • For milder depressions, certain forms of psychotherapy do work well.

    对较轻微的抑郁症，某些形式的心理治疗效果的确不错。

  • ...monthly psychotherapy sessions.

    每月一次的心理治疗时间
