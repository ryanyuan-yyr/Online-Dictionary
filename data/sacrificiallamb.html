sacrificial lamb
ˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəl læm
Word forms: plural sacrificial lambs
N-COUNT 替罪羊;代人受过者;牺牲品 If you refer to someone as a sacrificial lamb, you mean that they have been blamed unfairly for something they did not do, usually in order to protect another more powerful person or group. 替罪羊;代人受过者;牺牲品

  • He was a sacrificial lamb to a system that destroyed him.

    他是那个令他走向毁灭的制度的牺牲品。
