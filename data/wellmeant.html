well-meant
ˌwɛlˈmɛnt
(also well meant)
ADJ-GRADED (决定、行为或意见)出于好心的，善意的，本意良好的 A well-meant decision, action, or comment is intended to be helpful or kind but is unsuccessful or causes problems. (决定、行为或意见)出于好心的，善意的，本意良好的

  • Any decision taken by them now, however well meant, could complicate the peace process.

    现在他们作的任何一个决定，不管本意多么好，都可能使和平进程复杂化。

  • ...a well-meant experiment gone wrong.

    出发点不错却出了岔子的实验
