maker
meɪkəʳ ˈmeɪkər
Word forms: plural makers
1 N-COUNT 生产商;制造商 The maker of a product is the firm that manufactures it. 生产商;制造商

  • ...Japan's two largest car makers.

    日本最大的两家汽车制造商

  • ...the makers of chocolates, sweets and biscuits.

    巧克力、糖果和饼干生产商

SYN manufacturer, producer, builder, constructor  
2 N-COUNT 制造者;制作人 You can refer to the person who makes something as its maker . 制造者;制作人

  • ...the makers of news and current affairs programmes.

    新闻和时事节目制作人

3 See also: peacemaker
