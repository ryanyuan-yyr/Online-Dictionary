natural food
Word forms: natural foods
N-VAR 天然食品Natural food is food which has not been processed much and has not had artificial ingredients added to it. 天然食品

  • Her diet consisted of natural food, uncontaminated by pesticides, salt or refined sugar.

    她的膳食是由未受农药、盐或精制糖污染过的天然食品构成的。

  • ...the organic and natural foods movement.

    ...有机天然食品运动。
