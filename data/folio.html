folio
foʊlioʊ ˈfoʊliˌoʊ ˈfoʊlˌjoʊ
Word forms: plural folios
N-COUNT (尤指欧洲早期印刷的)对开本 A folio is a book made with paper of a large size, used especially in the early centuries of European printing. (尤指欧洲早期印刷的)对开本

  • Richard told me of three 16th-century folio volumes on alchemy.

    理查德告诉过我 16 世纪的三卷关于炼金术的对开本书籍的事。
