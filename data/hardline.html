hardline
hɑːʳdlaɪn ˌhɑːdˈlaɪn
(also hard-line)
ADJ-GRADED （政策或态度）强硬的，不妥协的，坚定的 If you describe someone's policy or attitude as hardline, you mean that it is strict or extreme, and they refuse to change it. （政策或态度）强硬的，不妥协的，坚定的

  • The United States has taken a lot of criticism for its hard-line stance.

    美国因其强硬立场遭到众多非议。

  • East Germany's last hardline Communist leader.

    东德最后一位强硬派共产党领袖

SYN tough, extreme, strict, definite
