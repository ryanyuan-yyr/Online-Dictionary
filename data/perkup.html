perk up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）振奋；（使）活跃；（使）快活 If something perks you up or if you perk up, you become cheerful and lively, after feeling tired, bored, or depressed. （使）振奋；（使）活跃；（使）快活 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • He perks up and jokes with them. [VERB PREP.]

    他很活跃，还和他们开玩笑。

  • ...suggestions to make you smile and perk you up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    让你发笑、使你开心的建议

2 PHRASAL VERB 使更有趣 If you perk something up, you make it more interesting. 使更有趣

  • To make the bland taste more interesting, the locals began perking it up with local produce. [VERB noun PREP.]

    为了使这淡而无味的东西更有味道，当地人开始加些土特产调味。

  • Psychological twists perk up an otherwise predictable story line.

    心理转折使原本老套的故事情节变得生动有趣。

3 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）增加；（使）上涨；（使）增值 If sales, prices, or economies perk up, or if something perks them up, they begin to increase or improve. （使）增加；（使）上涨；（使）增值 [journalism] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • House prices could perk up during the autumn. [VERB PREP.]

    秋季房价可能上涨。

  • Anything that could save the company money and perk up its cash flow was examined. [VERB PREP. noun]

    所有能够为公司节省开支、增加流动资金的办法都研究过了。
