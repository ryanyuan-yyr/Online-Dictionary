wick
wɪk wɪk
Word forms: plural wicks
1 N-COUNT 蜡烛芯 The wick of a candle is the piece of string in it which burns when it is lit. 蜡烛芯
2 N-COUNT （煤油灯的）灯芯;（打火机的）棉芯 The wick of a paraffin lamp or cigarette lighter is the part which supplies the fuel to the flame when it is lit. （煤油灯的）灯芯;（打火机的）棉芯
3 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 惹怒;激怒 If you say that someone or something gets on your wick, you mean that they annoy and irritate you. 惹怒;激怒 [British, informal]

  • The Professor was beginning to get on Molly's wick.

    教授快要把莫莉惹毛了。
