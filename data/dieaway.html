die away
PHRASAL VERB （声音）变弱，逐渐消失 If a sound dies away, it gradually becomes weaker or fainter and finally disappears completely. （声音）变弱，逐渐消失

  • The firing finally began to die away in the late afternoon. [VERB PREP.]

    到了临近傍晚时枪炮声终于渐渐停息。

  • The sound died away and silence reigned. [VERB PREP.]

    那个声音渐渐消失，四周又恢复了寂静。

SYN fade away, decline, disappear, dwindle
