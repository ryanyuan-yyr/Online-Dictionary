magic wand
Word forms: plural magic wands
1 N-COUNT 魔杖;魔术棒 A magic wand or a wand is a long thin rod that magicians and fairies wave when they are performing tricks and magic. 魔杖;魔术棒
2 N-COUNT 灵丹妙药 You use magic wand, especially in the expression there is no magic wand, to indicate that someone is dealing with a difficult problem which cannot be solved quickly and easily. 灵丹妙药

  • There is no magic wand to secure a just peace.

    能够保证公正的和平的灵丹妙药是不存在的。

  • People can't expect him to wave a magic wand.

    人民不能指望他创造奇迹。
