water-borne
(also waterborne)
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （疾病等）经水传染的，由水传播的 A water-borne disease or infection is one that people can catch from infected water. （疾病等）经水传染的，由水传播的

  • UN officials are warning of an outbreak of cholera and other water-borne diseases.

    美国官方发出了霍乱和其他经水传播的疾病可能会爆发的警告。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 水上的;水运的 Something that is water-borne travels or is transported on water. 水上的;水运的

  • ...a waterborne safari down the Nile.

    尼罗河水上观光游

  • Environmental pressures are strengthening the case for waterborne freight.

    环境的压力使人们更加认可水上运输。
