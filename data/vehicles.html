vehicle
viːɪkəl ˈviəkəl ˈviˌhɪkəl
Word forms: plural vehicles
1 N-COUNT 车辆;交通工具;运输工具 A vehicle is a machine such as a car, bus, or truck which has an engine and is used to carry people from place to place. 车辆;交通工具;运输工具

  • The vehicle would not have the capacity to make the journey on one tank of fuel.

    这辆车单靠一箱燃料无法跑完整趟旅程。

  • ...a vehicle which was somewhere between a tractor and a truck.

    一种介于拖拉机和卡车之间的机动车

SYN conveyance [old-fashioned] , machine, motor vehicle, means of transport  
2 N-COUNT （实现目的的）工具，手段,媒介 You can use vehicle to refer to something that you use in order to achieve a particular purpose. （实现目的的）工具，手段,媒介

  • Her art became a vehicle for her political beliefs. [+ for]

    艺术成了她表达政治信仰的工具。

  • The vehicle that permitted both communication and acceptability was social revolution.

    既能实现交流又能被广为接受的手段就是社会革命。

SYN medium, means, channel, mechanism
