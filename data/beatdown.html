beat down
1 PHRASAL VERB （烈日）暴晒，强烈地照射 When the sun beats down, it is very hot and bright. （烈日）暴晒，强烈地照射
2 PHRASAL VERB （雨）滂沱而下，倾盆而下 When the rain beats down, it rains very hard. （雨）滂沱而下，倾盆而下

  • Even in the winter with the rain beating down, it's nice and cosy in there. [VERB PREP.]

    即使在大雨倾盆的冬天，那里也非常舒适。

3 PHRASAL VERB 压价；砍价；杀价 If you beat down a person who is selling you something, you force them to accept a lower price for it than they wanted to get. 压价；砍价；杀价

  • A fair employer, when arranging for the pay of a carpenter, does not try to beat him down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    一个公道的雇主在商定木匠的工钱时不会使劲压价。

  • Beat down the seller to the price that suits you. [VERB PREP. noun]

    把卖家的要价砍到你能接受的价格。
