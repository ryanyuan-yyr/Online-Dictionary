juice
dʒuːs dʒus
Word forms: plural juices
1 N-MASS 果汁 Juice is the liquid that can be obtained from a fruit. 果汁

  • ...fresh orange juice.

    鲜榨橙汁

  • Soak the couscous overnight in the juice of about six lemons.

    将粗麦粉浸于大约6个柠檬榨出的果汁中，浸泡整夜。

SYN liquid, extract, fluid, liquor  
2 N-PLURAL 肉汁 The juices of a piece of meat are the liquid that comes out of it when you cook it. 肉汁

  • When cooked, drain off the juices and put the meat in a processor or mincer.

    肉煮熟后，沥干肉汁，将其放入食品加工机或绞肉机中。

3 N-PLURAL [with supplement] 胃液;消化液 The juices in your stomach are the fluids that help you to digest food. 胃液;消化液
