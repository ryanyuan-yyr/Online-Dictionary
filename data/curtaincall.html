curtain call
(also curtain-call)Word forms: plural curtain calls
N-COUNT (演员的)谢幕 In a theatre, when actors or performers take a curtain call, they come forward to the front of the stage after a performance in order to receive the applause of the audience. (演员的)谢幕

  • They took 23 curtain calls.

    他们谢幕多达23次。
