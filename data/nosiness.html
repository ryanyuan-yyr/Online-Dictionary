nosy
noʊzi ˈnoʊzi
(also nosey)Word forms: comparative nosier, superlative nosiest
ADJ-GRADED 爱管闲事的;喜欢打听的;八卦的 If you describe someone as nosy, you mean that they are interested in things which do not concern them. 爱管闲事的;喜欢打听的;八卦的 [informal, disapproval]

  • He was having to whisper in order to avoid being overheard by their nosy neighbours.

    他不得不小声说，免得被爱管闲事的邻居们听到。

  • I agree that the press is often too nosy about a candidate's personal history.

    的确，媒体常常过分关注候选人的个人经历。
