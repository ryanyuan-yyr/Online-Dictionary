derivation
derɪveɪʃən ˌdɛrəˈveɪʃən
Word forms: plural derivations
N-VAR 起源;由来;(尤指)词源 The derivation of something, especially a word, is its origin or source. 起源;由来;(尤指)词源

  • The derivation of its name is obscure. [+ of]

    其名字的由来鲜为人知。

  • The word is of old French derivation.

    该词源于古法语。

SYN origin, source, basis, beginning
