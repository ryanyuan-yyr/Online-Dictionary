neighbour
neɪbəʳ
Word forms: plural neighbours
in AM, use 美国英语用 neighbor
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 邻居;邻人 Your neighbour is someone who lives near you. 邻居;邻人

  • I got chatting with my neighbour in the garden.

    我在花园里和邻居聊了一会。

2 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 邻座;身边的人;邻近的人 You can refer to the person who is standing or sitting next to you as your neighbour . 邻座;身边的人;邻近的人

  • The woman prodded her neighbour and whispered urgently in his ear.

    那位女士捅了捅邻座，急切地在他耳边低语。

3 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 邻近的同类物 You can refer to something which stands next to something else of the same kind as its neighbour . 邻近的同类物

  • Each house was packed close behind its neighbour.

    每幢房子都紧挨着前面的房子而立。

4 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 邻国 A country's neighbour is a country that is next to it. 邻国

  • ...the United States' northern neighbour, Canada.

    美国北部的邻国，加拿大
