pedagogue
pedəgɒg ˈpɛdəˌgɑg
Word forms: plural pedagogues
N-COUNT 好为人师者；卖弄学问的人 If you describe someone as a pedagogue, you mean that they like to teach people things in a firm way as if they know more than anyone else. 好为人师者；卖弄学问的人 [formal]

  • De Gaulle was a born pedagogue who used the public platform and the television screen to great effect.

    戴高乐生来喜欢教育人，他充分利用了公共讲坛和电视荧屏。

SYN teacher, instructor, educator, pedant
