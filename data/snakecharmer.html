snake charmer
(also snake-charmers)Word forms: plural snake charmers
N-COUNT (用奏乐等方式控制蛇的活动以娱乐他人的)弄蛇人，玩蛇者 A snake charmer is a person who entertains people by controlling the behaviour of a snake, for example by playing music and causing the snake to rise out of a basket and drop back in again. (用奏乐等方式控制蛇的活动以娱乐他人的)弄蛇人，玩蛇者
