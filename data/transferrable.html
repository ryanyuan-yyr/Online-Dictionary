transfer
trænsfɜːʳ trænsfɜːʳ ˈtrænsfər trænsˈfɜr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense transfers, present participle transferring, past tense , past participle transferred
The verb is pronounced /træns'fɜː/. The noun is pronounced /'trænsfɜː/. 动词读作 /træns'fɜː/。名词读作 /'trænsfɜː/。
1 V-ERG (使)转移;(使)迁移 If you transfer something or someone from one place to another, or they transfer from one place to another, they go from the first place to the second. (使)转移;(使)迁移

  • Remove the wafers with a spoon and transfer them to a plate. [V n + from/to]

    用勺子抄起薄饼，把它们转盛到盘子里。

  • He was transferred from Weston Hospital to Frenchay… [V n from/to n]

    他从韦斯顿医院转院到了法兰查。

  • He wants to transfer some money to the account of his daughter… [V n from/to n]

    他想把一些钱转到女儿的账户上。

  • The person can transfer from wheelchair to seat with relative ease. [VERB + from/to]

    那个人可以比较轻松地从轮椅上移到座位上。

SYN move, carry, remove, transport  
Transfer is also a noun.

  • Arrange for the transfer of medical records to your new doctor.

    安排一下把病历转给你的新医生的事宜。

  • The bank reserves the right to reverse any transfers or payments.

    银行保留撤销任何转账或支付操作的权利。

2 V-ERG (使)转移，传播，移交 If something is transferred, or transfers ,from one person or group of people to another, the second person or group gets it instead of the first. (使)转移，传播，移交

  • I realized she'd transferred all her love from me to you. [V n + from/to]

    我意识到她已把所有的爱从我身上转移到了你身上。

  • The chances of the disease being transferred to humans is extremely remote… [V n from/to n]

    该疾病传播到人类身上的几率微乎其微。

  • On 1 December the presidency of the Security Council automatically transfers from the US to Yemen.

    12 月 1 日，联合国安全理事会的轮值主席国职位自动地由美国移交给也门。

Transfer is also a noun.

  • ...the transfer of power from the old to the new regimes.

    新旧政权的权力移交

3 N-VAR (技术)转让 Technology transfer is the process or act by which a country or organization which has developed new technology enables another country or organization to use the technology. (技术)转让 [Also + of]

  • The Philippines needs capital and technology transfer.

    菲律宾需要资本与技术转让。

  • There will be a transfer of technology from this country to Mongolia.

    这个国家将会向蒙古国进行技术转让。

4 V-ERG (使)(运动员)转会 In professional sports, if a player transfers or is transferred from one club to another, they leave one club and begin playing for another. (使)(运动员)转会 [British] [Also V n + from/to]

  • ...Nick Barmby who transferred from Spurs to Middlesbrough. [VERB + from/to]

    从热刺队转会到米德尔斯伯勒队的尼克·巴尔姆比

  • He was transferred from Crystal Palace to Arsenal for £2.5 million. [be V-ed + from/to]

    他以 250 万英镑的身价从水晶宫队转入阿森纳队。

Transfer is also a noun.

  • ...Gascoigne's transfer to the Italian club, Lazio.

    加斯科因转会到意大利俱乐部拉齐奥

5 V-ERG （使）调动;（使）换岗 If you are transferred, or if you transfer ,to a different job or place, you move to a different job or start working in a different place. （使）调动;（使）换岗

  • I was transferred to the book department. [be V-ed + from/to]

    我被调到图书部。

  • I suspect that she is going to be transferred. [be VERB-ed]

    我猜想她要被调走了。

  • Anton was able to transfer from Lavine's to an American company. [VERB + from/to]

    安东成功地从拉文调到了一家美国公司。

Transfer is also a noun.

  • They will be offered transfers to other locations.

    他们将会被安排调任到其他地方。

6 VERB 转存;转录 When information is transferred onto a different medium, it is copied from one medium to another. 转存;转录

  • Such information is easily transferred onto microfilm. [be V-ed + onto/to]

    这种信息很容易转存到缩微胶卷上。

  • ...systems to create film-quality computer effects and then transfer them to film. [V n + onto/to]

    创造胶片级电脑画质并将之转录到胶片的系统

Transfer is also a noun.

  • It can be connected to a PC for the transfer of information.

    它可以与个人电脑连接进行信息转存。

  • ...data transfer.

    数据转存

7 VERB 转让，让与(财产、土地) When property or land is transferred, it stops being owned by one person or institution and becomes owned by another. 转让，让与(财产、土地) [law] [Also VERB n]

  • He has already transferred ownership of most of the works to a British foundation. [V n + from/to]

    他已把大部分作品的所有权转让给一家英国基金会。

  • Certain kinds of property are transferred automatically at death. [be VERB-ed]

    死亡时某些财产会自动转让。

SYN hand over, give, commit, surrender  
Transfer is also a noun.

  • ...an outright transfer of property.

    财产的全部让与

8 V-ERG (使)转乘;(使)换乘 If you transfer or are transferred when you are on a journey, you change from one vehicle to another. (使)转乘;(使)换乘

  • He likes to transfer from the bus to the Blue Line at 103rd Street in Watts. [VERB + from/to]

    他喜欢在华兹区第 103 大街下公交车转乘地铁蓝线。

  • 1,654 passengers were transferred at sea to a Norwegian cruise ship. [be V-ed + from/to]

    1,654 名乘客在海上转搭了一艘挪威游轮。

9 N-COUNT 转印纸(纸上面印有图案，可通过加热或压力转印至其他材料上) Transfers are pieces of paper with a design on one side. The design can be transferred by heat or pressure onto material, paper, or china for decoration. 转印纸(纸上面印有图案，可通过加热或压力转印至其他材料上)

  • ...gold letter transfers.

    金字转印纸

10 N-COUNT 换乘票;转车票;通票 A transfer is a ticket that you get when you leave a bus or train that allows you to go on a different bus or train without paying again. 换乘票;转车票;通票
