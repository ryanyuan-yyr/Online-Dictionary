subversive
səbvɜːʳsɪv səbˈvɜːʃənərɪ səbˈvɜrsɪv
Word forms: plural subversives
1 ADJ-GRADED 颠覆性的;破坏性的 Something that is subversive is intended to weaken or destroy a political system or government. 颠覆性的;破坏性的

  • This courageous and subversive movie has attracted widespread critical support.

    这部大胆而具颠覆性的电影引起了评论界广泛的支持。

SYN seditious, inflammatory, incendiary, underground  
2 N-COUNT 颠覆分子;破坏分子 Subversives are people who attempt to weaken or destroy a political system or government. 颠覆分子;破坏分子

  • Agents regularly rounded up suspected subversives.

    特工定期围捕可疑的颠覆分子。

SYN dissident, terrorist, saboteur, insurrectionary
