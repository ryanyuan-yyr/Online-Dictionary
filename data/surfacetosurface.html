ADJ [ADJ n] (导弹)地对地(或舰)的，舰对舰(或地)的 Surface-to-surface missiles are fired from the ground or a boat and aimed at targets on the ground or at other boats. (导弹)地对地(或舰)的，舰对舰(或地)的
