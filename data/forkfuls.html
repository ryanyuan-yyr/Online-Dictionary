forkful
fɔːʳkfʊl
Word forms: plural forkfuls
N-COUNT (食物)一叉的量，一叉 You can refer to an amount of food on a fork as a forkful of food. (食物)一叉的量，一叉

  • I put a forkful of fillet steak in my mouth. [+ of]

    我叉了一块无骨牛排放进嘴里。

  • He lingered over the chocolate cake, letting each forkful slowly dissolve in his mouth.

    他细细地品尝巧克力蛋糕，让每一口蛋糕在嘴里慢慢溶化。
