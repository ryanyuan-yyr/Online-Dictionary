purchasing power
ˈpɜːtʃɪsɪŋ ˈpaʊə
1 N-UNCOUNT （货币的）购买力 The purchasing power of a currency is the amount of goods or services that you can buy with it. （货币的）购买力 [business]

  • The real purchasing power of the rouble has plummeted.

    卢布的实际购买力骤然下降。

2 N-UNCOUNT （人的）购买力 The purchasing power of a person or group of people is the amount of goods or services that they can afford to buy. （人的）购买力 [business]

  • ...the purchasing power of their customers.

    他们顾客的购买力
