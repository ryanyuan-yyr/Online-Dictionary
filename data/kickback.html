kickback
kɪkbæk ˈkɪkˌbæk
Word forms: plural kickbacks
N-COUNT （不合法的）回扣，酬金，佣金 A kickback is a sum of money that is paid to someone illegally, for example money which a company pays someone to arrange for the company to be chosen to do an important job. （不合法的）回扣，酬金，佣金

  • ...alleged kickbacks and illegal party financing.

    涉嫌收受佣金和非法政党筹资

SYN bribe, payoff, backhander [slang] , enticement
