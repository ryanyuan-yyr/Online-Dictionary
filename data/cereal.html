cereal
sɪəriəl ˈsɪərɪəl ˈsɪriəl
Word forms: plural cereals
1 N-MASS (拌以牛奶作早餐的)谷类食品，麦片 Cereal or breakfast cereal is a food made from grain. It is mixed with milk and eaten for breakfast. (拌以牛奶作早餐的)谷类食品，麦片

  • I have a bowl of cereal every morning.

    我每天早上喝一碗麦片。

2 N-COUNT 谷类作物;谷物 Cereals are plants such as wheat, corn, or rice that produce grain. 谷类作物;谷物

  • ...the rich cereal-growing districts of the Paris Basin.

    巴黎盆地盛产谷物的地区
