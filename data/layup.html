lay up
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] （疾病）使卧床 If someone is laid up with an illness, the illness makes it necessary for them to stay in bed. （疾病）使卧床 [informal]

  • I was laid up in bed with acute rheumatism. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    我染上了急性风湿，卧病在床。

  • Powell ruptured a disc in his back and was laid up for a year. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    鲍威尔的椎间盘破裂，在床上躺了一年。

SYN confine (to bed), hospitalize, incapacitate
