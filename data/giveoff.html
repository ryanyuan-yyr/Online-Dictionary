give off
(also give out)
PHRASAL VERB 释放，放出，发出（气体、热量、气味等） If something gives off or gives out a gas, heat, or a smell, it produces it and sends it out into the air. 释放，放出，发出（气体、热量、气味等） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • ...natural gas, which gives off less carbon dioxide than coal. [VERB PREP. noun]

    二氧化碳释放量低于煤炭的天然气

  • Substances such as ammonia give out heat when they dissolve. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    诸如氨气之类的物质在溶解时会释放出热量。
