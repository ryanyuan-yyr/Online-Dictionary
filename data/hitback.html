hit back
1 PHRASAL VERB 还击;（向…）还手 If you hit back when someone hits you, or hit them back, you hit them in return. 还击;（向…）还手

  • Some violent men beat up their sons, until the boys are strong enough to hit back. [VERB PREP.]

    一些粗暴的人对自己的儿子大打出手，直到他们有了还手之力才肯罢休。

  • If somebody hit me, I'd hit him back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果有人打我，我会还手。

2 PHRASAL VERB 反击;还击 If you hit back at someone who has criticized or harmed you, you criticize or harm them in return. 反击;还击 [journalism]

  • The President has hit back at those who have criticised his economic reforms. [VERB PREP. + at]

    总统对那些批评过其经济改革措施的人进行了反击。

  • British Rail immediately hit back with their own cheap fares scheme. [VERB PREP.]

    英国铁路公司马上推出自己的低票价方案予以反击。
