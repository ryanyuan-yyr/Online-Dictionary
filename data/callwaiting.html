call waiting
kɔːl ˈweɪtɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 呼叫等待(服务) Call waiting is a telephone service that sends you a signal if another call arrives while you are already on the phone. 呼叫等待(服务)

  • ICM is an internationally patented call waiting service suited to the domestic market.

    因特网呼叫管理(ICM)是一项获得国际专利的、适合国内市场需求的呼叫等待服务。
