diagnose
daɪəgnoʊz -noʊs ˌdaɪəgˈnoʊs ˌdaɪəgˈnoʊz ˈdaɪəgˌnoʊs ˈdaɪəgˌnoʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense diagnoses, present participle diagnosing, past tense , past participle diagnosed
1 VERB 诊断;确诊;判断;断定 If someone or something is diagnosed as having a particular illness or problem, their illness or problem is identified. If an illness or problem is diagnosed, it is identified. 诊断;确诊;判断;断定 [Also be V-ed adj]

  • The soldiers were diagnosed as having flu. [be VERB-ed + as]

    这些士兵经诊断患了流感。

  • Susan had a mental breakdown and was diagnosed with schizophrenia. [be VERB-ed + with]

    苏珊精神崩溃，被诊断为得了精神分裂症。

  • In 1894 her illness was diagnosed as cancer. [be VERB-ed + as]

    1894 年，她的病确诊为癌症。

  • He could diagnose an engine problem simply by listening. [VERB noun]

    他仅靠听就能判断发动机出了什么问题。

  • This disorder is easily diagnosed but not so easily treated. [VERB noun]

    这种疾病容易诊断，但却不容易治疗。

SYN identify, determine, recognize, distinguish
