gibberish
dʒɪbərɪʃ ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ
N-UNCOUNT 莫名其妙的话；令人费解的观点；胡话 If you describe some-one's words or ideas as gibberish, you mean that they do not make any sense. 莫名其妙的话；令人费解的观点；胡话

  • When he was talking to a girl he could hardly speak, and when he did speak he talked gibberish.

    他跟女孩儿讲话时几乎张不了口，即便张了口，说起话来也词不达意。

SYN nonsense, bull [slang] , crap [slang] , garbage [informal]
