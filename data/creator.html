creator
krieɪtəʳ kriːˈeɪtə kriˈeɪtər
Word forms: plural creators
1 N-COUNT [usually with poss] 创造者;创作者;发明者 The creator of something is the person who made it or invented it. 创造者;创作者;发明者

  • ...Ian Fleming, the creator of James Bond. [+ of]

    詹姆斯·邦德这一形象的创作者伊恩·弗莱明

  • I have always believed that a garden dies with its creator.

    我一直认为花园的灵魂是与其创造者共存亡的。

2 N-PROPER 上帝;造物主 God is sometimes referred to as the Creator . 上帝;造物主

  • This was the first object placed in the heavens by the Creator.

    这是上帝安放在天堂里的第一件东西。
