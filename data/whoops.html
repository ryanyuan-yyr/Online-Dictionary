whoops
hwʊps wʊps ˈwʊpsəˌdeɪzɪ hwʊps wʊps hwups wups
EXCLAM (出了小事故或向某人道歉时的感叹语)哎呀 You say 'whoops' to indicate that there has been a slight accident or mistake, or to apologize to someone for it. (出了小事故或向某人道歉时的感叹语)哎呀 [informal, feelings]

  • Whoops, that was a mistake.

    哎呀，出了个小差错。

  • Whoops, it's past 11, I'd better be off home.

    哎呀，11点多了，我最好还是回家吧。
