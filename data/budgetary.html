budgetary
bʌdʒɪtəri -teri
ADJ [ADJ n] 预算的 A budgetary matter or policy is concerned with the amount of money that is available to a country or organization, and how it is to be spent. 预算的 [formal]

  • There are huge budgetary pressures on all governments in Europe to reduce their armed forces.

    欧洲各国政府都面临着巨大的裁军预算压力。

SYN financial, money, economic, monetary
