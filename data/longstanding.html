long-standing ˈlɔŋˌstændɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 长期存在的；由来已久的 A long-standing situation has existed for a long time. 长期存在的；由来已久的

  • They are on the brink of resolving their long-standing dispute over money.

    他们很快就能解决长期以来的金钱纠纷。

  • ...long-standing economic links between Europe and much of Africa.

    欧洲和非洲大部分地区由来已久的经贸联系

SYN established, fixed, enduring, abiding
