time-worn
(also timeworn)
ADJ-GRADED 久经使用的;陈旧的;古旧的 Something that is time-worn is old or has been used a lot over a long period of time. 久经使用的;陈旧的;古旧的

  • Even in the dim light the equipment looked old and time-worn.

    即便在昏暗的光线下，那台设备也显得陈旧不堪。

  • These time-worn techniques are often very effective approaches to the illnesses of the present day.

    这些古老的疗法在治疗当今的疾病时往往非常有效。
