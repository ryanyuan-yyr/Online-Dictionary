doughty
daʊti ˈdaʊti
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 骁勇的;彪悍的;顽强的 If you describe someone as a doughty fighter, you mean they are brave, determined, and not easily defeated. 骁勇的;彪悍的;顽强的 [old-fashioned, approval]

  • His doughty campaigns for the underprivileged have earned him national respect.

    他为弱势群体奔走呼号，赢得了全国的尊敬。

SYN intrepid, brave, daring, bold
