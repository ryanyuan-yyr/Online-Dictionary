dancer
dɑːnsəʳ dæns-
Word forms: plural dancers
1 N-COUNT 舞蹈演员;舞蹈家;跳舞者 A dancer is a person who earns money by dancing, or a person who is dancing. 舞蹈演员;舞蹈家;跳舞者

  • His previous girlfriend was a dancer with the Royal Ballet.

    他的前女友是皇家芭蕾舞团的舞蹈演员。

  • The dancers began to walk away from the floor.

    跳舞的人们开始离开舞池。

SYN ballerina, hoofer [slang] , Terpsichorean  
2 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] （出色或蹩脚的）舞者 If you say that someone is a good dancer or a bad dancer, you are saying how well or badly they can dance. （出色或蹩脚的）舞者

  • He was the best dancer in LA.

    他是洛杉矶舞跳得最好的人。
