vibrato
vɪbrɑːtoʊ vɪˈbrɑtoʊ viˈbrɑtoʊ
Word forms: plural vibratos
N-VAR （乐曲中的）颤音,颤动效果 Vibrato is a rapidly repeated slight change in the pitch of a musical note. Singers and musicians use vibrato to make the music sound more emotional. （乐曲中的）颤音,颤动效果

  • I encourage oboe and clarinet players to use plenty of vibrato.

    我鼓励演奏双簧管和单簧管的乐手们多使用颤音。
