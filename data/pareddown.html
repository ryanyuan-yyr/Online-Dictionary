pared-down
ˌpɛədˈdaʊn
ADJ-GRADED 压缩的；简化的 If you describe something as pared-down, you mean that it has no unnecessary features, and has been reduced to a very simple form. 压缩的；简化的

  • Her style is pared-down and simple.

    她风格简约而质朴。

  • The new NATO will be a pared-down military organization.

    新的北大西洋公约组织将是一个精简了的军事组织。
