headache
hedeɪk ˈhɛdˌeɪk
Word forms: plural headaches
1 N-COUNT 头痛 If you have a headache, you have a pain in your head. 头痛

  • I have had a terrible headache for the last two days.

    两天来我一直头痛得厉害。

SYN migraine, head [informal] , neuralgia, cephalalgia [medicine]  
2 N-COUNT 使人头痛的事;棘手的事;难题 If you say that something is a headache, you mean that it causes you difficulty or worry. 使人头痛的事;棘手的事;难题

  • The airline's biggest headache is the increase in the price of aviation fuel.

    令航空公司最头痛的事就是航空燃料价格的上涨。

  • This is a real headache for us.

    这对我们来说真是个难题。

SYN problem, worry, trouble, bother
