hard-boiled
ˈhɑrdˈbɔɪld
(also hard boiled)
1 ADJ （蛋）煮得老的，煮硬的 A hard-boiled egg has been boiled in its shell until the whole of the inside is solid. （蛋）煮得老的，煮硬的
2 ADJ-GRADED （人）不动感情的，强硬的 You use hard-boiled to describe someone who is tough and does not show much emotion. （人）不动感情的，强硬的

  • She's hard-boiled, tough and funny.

    她冷峻、坚韧而风趣。
