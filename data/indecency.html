indecency
ɪndiːsənsi ɪnˈdiːsənsɪ ɪnˈdisənsi
1 N-UNCOUNT 下流;有伤风化;猥亵 If you talk about the indecency of something or someone, you are indicating that you find them morally or sexually offensive. 下流;有伤风化;猥亵

  • ...the indecency of their language. [+ of]

    他们言语粗俗

2 N-UNCOUNT 非法性行为 In law, an act of indecency is an illegal sexual act. 非法性行为

  • They were found guilty of acts of gross indecency.

    他们因存在严重猥亵行为而被判有罪。

SYN obscenity, impurity, lewdness, impropriety
