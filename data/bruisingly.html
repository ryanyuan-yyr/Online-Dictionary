bruising
bruːzɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 淤伤；青肿 If someone has bruising on their body, they have bruises on it. 淤伤；青肿 [formal]

  • She had quite severe bruising and a cut lip.

    她身上有严重的淤伤，嘴唇也破了。

SYN discoloration, marking, swelling, contusion [formal]  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 头破血流的；激烈的 In a bruising battle or encounter, people fight or compete with each other in a very aggressive or determined way. 头破血流的；激烈的 [journalism]

  • The administration hopes to avoid another bruising battle over civil rights.

    政府希望避免因民权问题再次发生激烈冲突。
