chic
ʃiːk ʃɪk ʃik
1 ADJ-GRADED 时髦的;别致的 Something or someone that is chic is fashionable and sophisticated. 时髦的;别致的

  • Her gown was very French and very chic.

    她的礼服颇具法国风情，非常别致。

SYN stylish, smart, elegant, fashionable  
2 N-UNCOUNT 时尚;雅致;别致 Chic is used to refer to a particular style or to the quality of being chic. 时尚;雅致;别致

  • ...French designer chic.

    法国设计时尚

  • He radiates charm and chic.

    他魅力四射，风度翩翩。
