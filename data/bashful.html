bashful
bæʃfʊl ˈbæʃfəl
ADJ-GRADED 害羞的；腼腆的；忸怩的 Someone who is bashful is shy and easily embarrassed. 害羞的；腼腆的；忸怩的

  • He seemed bashful and awkward.

    他看起来腼腆且局促不安。

  • In our culture we tend to be bashful about our talents and skills.

    在我们的文化中，我们往往羞于谈论自己的才干和技能。

  • ...a bashful young lady.

    腼腆的年轻女子

SYN shy, reserved, retiring, nervous  
bashfully

  • 'No,' Wang Fu said bashfully.

    “不。”王福腼腆地说。

bashfullness

  • I was overcome with bashfulness when I met her.

    遇到她时我显得极其羞怯。
