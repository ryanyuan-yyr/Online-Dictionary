misstatement
mɪssteɪtmənt
Word forms: plural misstatements
N-COUNT 错误的陈述;虚假的报道;谎报 A misstatement is an incorrect statement, or the giving of false information. 错误的陈述;虚假的报道;谎报 [mainly US]

  • He finally corrected his misstatement and offered to reduce the fee.

    他终于纠正了自己的错误说法，提出要减少费用。

  • While this booklet has become an official source of information, it is filled with misstatements of fact. [+ of]

    尽管这本小册子已经成为正式的信息来源，里面仍然错误百出。
