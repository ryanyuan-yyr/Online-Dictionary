soviet
ˈsəʊvɪət ˈsoʊviˌɛt ˈsoʊviɪt ˌ soʊviˈɛt
1 ADJ 苏联的 Soviet is used to describe something that belonged or related to the former Soviet Union. 苏联的

  • ...Soviet athletes.

    苏联的运动员

2 N-PLURAL 苏联人 The Soviets were the people of the former Soviet Union. 苏联人

  • In 1957, the Soviets launched Sputnik 1 into outer space.

    1957 年，苏联人将“斯普特尼克1号”人造卫星送入了外层空间。

3 N-COUNT (苏联的各级)苏维埃，委员会，代表会议 A soviet was an elected local, regional, or national council in the former Soviet Union. (苏联的各级)苏维埃，委员会，代表会议

  • ...the supreme soviet.

    最高苏维埃
