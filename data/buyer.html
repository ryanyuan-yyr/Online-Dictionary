buyer
baɪəʳ ˈbɪər
Word forms: plural buyers
1 N-COUNT 购买者；买主；买方 A buyer is a person who is buying something or who intends to buy it. 购买者；买主；买方

  • Car buyers are more interested in safety and reliability than speed.

    购车者们更感兴趣的是汽车的安全性和可靠性，而不是它的速度。

2 N-COUNT （大商店的）采购员，进货员；买手 A buyer is a person who works for a large store deciding what goods will be bought from manufacturers to be sold in the store. （大商店的）采购员，进货员；买手

  • I was a buyer for the women's clothing department.

    我是女装部的进货员。
