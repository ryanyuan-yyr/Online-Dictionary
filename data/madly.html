madly
mædli ˈmædli
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 狂热地；疯狂地 You can use madly to indicate that one person loves another a great deal. 狂热地；疯狂地

  • She has fallen madly in love with him.

    她疯狂地爱上了他。

  • She was devoted to him, but she no longer loved him madly.

    她一心一意地爱他，但是这份爱不再疯狂。

SYN passionately, wildly, desperately, intensely  
2 ADV [ADVERB adjective] 非常;很;十分 You can use madly in front of an adjective in order to emphasize the quality expressed by the adjective. 非常;很;十分 [mainly British, emphasis]

  • Inside it is madly busy.

    里面异常忙碌。

  • This seemed madly dangerous.

    这看上去岌岌可危。

SYN foolishly, wildly, absurdly, ludicrously
