ill-starred
ˈɪlˈstɑrd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 运气不佳的;注定失败的 If you describe something or someone as ill-starred, you mean that they were unlucky or unsuccessful. 运气不佳的;注定失败的 [literary]

  • ...an ill-starred attempt to create jobs in Northern Ireland.

    在北爱尔兰创造就业机会的很不走运的尝试

SYN doomed, unfortunate, unlucky, unhappy
