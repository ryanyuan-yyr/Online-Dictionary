nugget
nʌgɪt ˈnʌgət
Word forms: plural nuggets
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 小块东西;(尤指)小金块 A nugget is a small lump of something, especially gold. 小块东西;(尤指)小金块

  • ...pure high-grade gold nuggets.

    高纯度金块

  • ...a small nugget of butter. [+ of]

    小块黄油

2 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] 有趣的消息;有价值的情报 A nugget of information is an interesting or useful piece of information. 有趣的消息;有价值的情报

  • He had felt on the telephone that Jordan had a little nugget of information tucked away somewhere.

    他已从电话里感到乔丹掖着点儿什么有价值的情报。
