slowdown
sloʊdaʊn ˈsloʊˌdaʊn
Word forms: plural slowdowns
1 N-COUNT 放慢;放缓 A slowdown is a reduction in speed or activity. 放慢;放缓

  • There has been a sharp slowdown in economic growth. [+ in]

    经济增长的速度明显放慢。

2 N-COUNT 怠工 A slowdown is a protest in which workers deliberately work slowly and cause problems for their employers. 怠工 [US, business]

  • It's impossible to assess how many officers are participating in the slowdown.

    要估算出究竟多少高级职员参与了怠工是不可能的。
