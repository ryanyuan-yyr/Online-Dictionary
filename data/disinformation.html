disinformation
dɪsɪnfəʳmeɪʃən ˌdɪsˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 假情报;假消息 If you accuse someone of spreading disinformation, you are accusing them of spreading false information in order to deceive people. 假情报;假消息

  • They spread disinformation in order to discredit politicians.

    他们散布假消息，企图败坏政治家的名声。

  • The government has been involved in a disinformation campaign, deliberately misleading and harassing its citizens.

    政府已经卷入了一场造假运动，他们有意误导和扰乱民众生活。
