autograph
ɔːtəgrɑːf -græf ˈɔtəˌgræf
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense autographs, present participle autographing, past tense , past participle autographed
1 N-COUNT [oft with poss] （名人的）亲笔签名 An autograph is the signature of someone famous which is specially written for a fan to keep. （名人的）亲笔签名

  • He went backstage and asked for her autograph.

    他去后台跟她要了签名。

  • Young autograph hunters clustered around the players' entrance.

    年轻人都挤在演员入口处索要签名。

SYN signature, moniker [informal]  
2 VERB （名人）签名于 If someone famous autographs something, they put their signature on it. （名人）签名于

  • I autographed a copy of one of my books. [VERB noun]

    我在自己的一本书上签了名。

  • ...an autographed photo of Clark Gable. [VERB-ed]

    克拉克·盖博的签名照片
