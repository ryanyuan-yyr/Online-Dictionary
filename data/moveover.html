move over
1 PHRASAL VERB 转变;变更 If you move over to a new system or way of doing something, you change to it. 转变;变更 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • The government is having to introduce some difficult changes, particularly in moving over to a market economy. [VERB PREP. + to]

    政府不得不推行一些艰难的变革，尤其是向市场经济体制的转变。

2 PHRASAL VERB 让位;离职 If someone moves over, they leave their job or position in order to let someone else have it. 让位;离职

  • They said Mr Jenkins should make balanced programmes about the Black community or move over and let someone else who can. [VERB PREP.]

    他们说，詹金斯先生应该针对黑人社区制订协调计划，或干脆离职，把位置让给能谋此政的人。

3 PHRASAL VERB (为给别人腾出空间而)变换位置，挪开 If you move over, you change your position in order to make room for someone else. (为给别人腾出空间而)变换位置，挪开

  • Move over and let me drive. [VERB PREP.]

    挪开，让我来开车。
