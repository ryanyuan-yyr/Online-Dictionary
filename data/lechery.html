lechery
letʃəri ˈlɛtʃəri
N-UNCOUNT （男子的）好色，纵欲 Lechery is the behaviour of men who are only interested in women sexually. （男子的）好色，纵欲 [disapproval]

  • His lechery made him the enemy of every self-respecting husband and father in the county.

    他的好色放浪使他成为全县所有自尊自重的丈夫和父亲的公敌。

SYN lustfulness, lust, licentiousness, salaciousness
