day care
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] （针对儿童、老人、病人等的）日托，日间护理，日间看护 Day care is care that is provided during the day for people who cannot look after themselves, such as small children, old people, or people who are ill. Day care is provided by paid workers. （针对儿童、老人、病人等的）日托，日间护理，日间看护
