participant
pɑːʳtɪsɪpənt pɑrˈtɪsəpənt
Word forms: plural participants
N-COUNT 参与者；参加者 The participants in an activity are the people who take part in it. 参与者；参加者

  • 40 of the course participants are offered employment with the company.

    上这门课的人中有40个被该公司聘用。

  • You are expected to be an active participant.

    你应该成为积极的参与者。

SYN participator, party, member, player
