aged
eɪdʒd eɪdʒɪd ˈeɪdʒɪd eɪdʒd
Pronounced /eɪdʒd/ for meaning 1, and /'eɪdʒɪd/ for meanings 2 and 3. 义项1读作 /eɪdʒd/，义项2和3读作 /'eɪdʒɪd/。
1 ADJ …岁的 You use aged followed by a number to say how old someone is. …岁的

  • Alan has two children, aged eleven and nine.

    艾伦有两个孩子，分别为11岁和9岁。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 年迈的 Aged means very old. 年迈的

  • She has an aged parent who's capable of being very difficult.

    她有一个年迈的父亲，可能会很不好相处。

SYN old, getting on, grey, ancient  
3 N-PLURAL 老年人 You can refer to all people who are very old as the aged . 老年人 [old-fashioned]

  • ...people who work with the aged.

    为老年人服务的人们

4 See also: middle-aged
