cadre
kɑːdəʳ ˈkɑːdə -dreɪ ˈkædri ˈkɑdreɪ ˈkædreɪ
Word forms: plural cadres
1 N-COUNT 骨干队伍；基干人员 A cadre is a small group of people who have been specially chosen, trained, and organized for a particular purpose. 骨干队伍；基干人员

  • ...an elite cadre of international managers. [+ of]

    国际经理人组成的精英队伍

2 N-COUNT [usually with supplement] (尤指共产党的)党务工作者，干部 In some political parties, especially the Communist Party, a cadre is a party worker or official. (尤指共产党的)党务工作者，干部
