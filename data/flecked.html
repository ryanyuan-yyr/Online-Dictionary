flecked
flekt
ADJ 有斑点的;布满斑点的 Something that is flecked with something is marked or covered with small bits of it. 有斑点的;布满斑点的

  • His hair was increasingly flecked with grey. [+ with]

    他头上的白发越来越多。

Flecked is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • He was attired in a plain, mud-flecked uniform.

    他身穿一件朴素的制服，上面满是泥点。
