schooled skuːld
1 ADJ-GRADED [oft adverb ADJ] 受过培训的;受过教育的;有经验的 If you are schooled in something, you have learned about it as the result of training or experience. 受过培训的;受过教育的;有经验的 [written]

  • They were both well schooled in the ways of the Army. [+ in]

    他们俩都受过良好的陆军训练。

  • She is a professional economist and therefore schooled in the arguments against that sort of state intervention.

    她是个专业经济学家，因此在驳斥类似国家干预行为方面经验丰富。
