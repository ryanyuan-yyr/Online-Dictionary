defunct
dɪfʌŋkt diˈfʌŋkt dɪˈfʌŋkt
ADJ 不再存在的;停止运转的;坏了的 If something is defunct, it no longer exists or has stopped functioning or operating. 不再存在的;停止运转的;坏了的

  • ...the leader of the now defunct Social Democratic Party.

    现已不复存在的社会民主党的领导人

  • They bought all their equipment from a defunct brewery in Manhattan.

    他们那些设备都是从曼哈顿一家已经关闭的啤酒厂购买的。

SYN dead, extinct, gone, departed [euphemistic]
