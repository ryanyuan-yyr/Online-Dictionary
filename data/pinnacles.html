pinnacle
pɪnɪkəl ˈpɪnəkəl
Word forms: plural pinnacles
1 N-COUNT 尖锥形石；尖岩 A pinnacle is a pointed piece of stone or rock that is high above the ground. 尖锥形石；尖岩

  • A walker fell 80ft from a rocky pinnacle.

    一位徒步者从一块80英尺高的尖锥形岩石上摔了下来。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 顶峰；极点；顶点 If someone reaches the pinnacle of their career or the pinnacle of a particular area of life, they are at the highest point of it. 顶峰；极点；顶点

  • John Major has reached the pinnacle of British politics.

    约翰·梅杰已攀上了英国政界的顶峰。

  • She was still a screen goddess at the pinnacle of her career. [+ of]

    她仍然是一位处于事业巅峰期的银幕女神。

  • ...trophies that represent the pinnacle of sporting achievement.

    代表体育界最高成就的奖杯
