relinquish
rɪlɪŋkwɪʃ rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense relinquishes, present participle relinquishing, past tense , past participle relinquished
VERB 放弃，出让(权力或控制) If you relinquish something such as power or control, you give it up. 放弃，出让(权力或控制) [formal]

  • He does not intend to relinquish power. [VERB noun]

    他没有打算放弃权力。

SYN give up, leave, release, drop
