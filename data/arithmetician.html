arithmetic
ərɪθmɪtɪk ærɪθmetɪk əˈrɪθmətɪk ˌ ærɪθˈmɛtɪk ˌ ɛrɪθˈmɛtɪk
The noun is pronounced /ə'rɪθmətɪk/. The adjective is pronounced /ˌærɪθ'metɪk/. 名词读作/ə'rɪθmətɪk/，形容词读作/ˌærɪθ'metɪk/。
1 N-UNCOUNT 算术 Arithmetic is the part of mathematics that is concerned with the addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of numbers. 算术

  • ...teaching the basics of reading, writing and arithmetic.

    教授阅读、写作和算术的基础知识

  • ...an arithmetic test.

    算术测验

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 演算；计算 You can use arithmetic to refer to the process of doing a particular sum or calculation. 演算；计算

  • 4,000 women put in ten rupees each, which if my arithmetic is right adds up to 40,000 rupees.

    4,000名妇女每人放入10个卢比，如果我算得对的话，总数将达到4万卢比。

3 N-UNCOUNT （某形势的）数据统计 If you refer to the arithmetic of a situation, you are concerned with those aspects of it that can be expressed in numbers, and how they affect the situation. （某形势的）数据统计

  • The budgetary arithmetic suggests that government borrowing is set to surge.

    预算数字表明政府借款将要大幅增加。

  • The arithmetic is finely balanced: the socialists and their allies do not have an overall majority.

    该估算是经过仔细权衡的：社会主义者和他们的同盟并不占绝大多数。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] 算术的;运算的 Arithmetic means relating to or consisting of calculations involving numbers. 算术的;运算的

  • ...a processor which performs simple arithmetic operations such as adding or multiplying numbers.

    可进行加法或乘法等简单运算的处理器
