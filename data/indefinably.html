indefinable
ɪndɪfaɪnəbəl ˌɪndiˈfaɪnəbəl ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbəl
ADJ 难以确切表达的;难以确定的;模糊不清的 An indefinable quality or feeling cannot easily be described. 难以确切表达的;难以确定的;模糊不清的 [written]

  • There was something indefinable in her eyes.

    她的眼神里有种难以确定的东西。

  • His head felt lighter in some indefinable way.

    他的头不知为何有点晕眩。

SYN inexpressible, vague, indescribable, obscure
