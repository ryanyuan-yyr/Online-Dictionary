marksman
mɑːʳksmən ˈmɑrksmən
Word forms: plural marksmen
1 N-COUNT 神枪手;神射手 A marksman is a person who can shoot very accurately. 神枪手;神射手

  • Police marksmen opened fire.

    警察中的神枪手开了火。

2 N-COUNT (足球运动中善于射门得分的)神射手 In sports journalism, a marksman is a football player who scores a lot of goals. (足球运动中善于射门得分的)神射手 [British]

  • City's principal marksman was Joe Harvey who scored seventeen goals.

    市队的头号射手是打进17粒进球的乔·哈维。
