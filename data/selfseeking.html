self-seeking
ADJ-GRADED 追逐私利的;只顾自己的 If you describe someone as self-seeking, you disapprove of them because they are interested only in doing things which give them an advantage over other people. 追逐私利的;只顾自己的 [disapproval]

  • He said that democracy would open the way for self-seeking politicians to abuse the situation.

    他说民主会为追逐私利的政客大开方便之门，使他们趁机以公谋私。

SYN selfish, self-interested, mercenary, calculating
