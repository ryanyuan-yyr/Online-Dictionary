shaman
ʃeɪmən ˈʃɑmən ˈʃeɪmən ˈʃæmən
Word forms: plural shamans
1 N-COUNT 萨满教法师 A shaman is a priest or priestess in shamanism. 萨满教法师
2 N-COUNT 萨满(美洲土著居民认为能祛病降魔之人) Among some Native American peoples, a shaman is a person who is believed to have powers to heal sick people or to remove evil spirits from them. 萨满(美洲土著居民认为能祛病降魔之人)
