inoffensive
ɪnəfensɪv ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv
ADJ-GRADED (尽管可能乏味但)无害的，不冒犯人的，不让人讨厌的 If you describe someone or something as inoffensive, you mean that they are not unpleasant or unacceptable in any way, but are perhaps rather dull. (尽管可能乏味但)无害的，不冒犯人的，不让人讨厌的

  • He's a mild inoffensive man.

    他是个温文随和的人。

  • It's a very nice song. Catchy, and inoffensive.

    这是首挺不错的歌，朗朗上口又不难听。

SYN harmless, mild, innocuous, retiring
