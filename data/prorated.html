prorate
proʊreɪt prəʊˈreɪt ˈprəʊreɪt proʊˈreɪt ˈproʊˌreɪt
(also pro-rate)Word forms: prorates, prorating, prorated
V-T [usu passive] 按比例分配; 摊派If a cost is prorated, it is divided or assessed in a proportional way. 按比例分配; 摊派 [mainly US]

  • If weather and/or sea conditions cause your trip to return early, the boat fare will be prorated.

    如果因天气和/或海上的原因导致提前返程，会按比例退还船费。
