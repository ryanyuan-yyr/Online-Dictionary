crochet
kroʊʃeɪ kroʊʃeɪ kroʊˈʃeɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense crochets, present participle crocheting, past tense , past participle crocheted
1 N-UNCOUNT 钩针编织 Crochet is a way of making cloth out of cotton or wool by using a needle with a small hook at the end. 钩针编织

  • ...a black crochet waistcoat.

    用钩针编织的黑色马甲

2 VERB 用钩针编织 If you crochet, you make cloth by using a needle with a small hook at the end. 用钩针编织

  • She offered to teach me to crochet. [VERB]

    她提出要教我钩织东西。

  • Ma and I crocheted new quilts. [VERB noun]

    我和妈妈钩织了新床罩。

  • ...crocheted rugs. [VERB-ed]

    用钩针编织的小地毯
