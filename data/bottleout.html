bottle out
PHRASAL VERB （在最后时刻）因胆怯而放弃，打退堂鼓 If you bottle out, you lose your courage at the last moment and do not do something you intended to do. （在最后时刻）因胆怯而放弃，打退堂鼓 [British, informal]

  • I haven't come all this way to bottle out. [VERB PREP.]

    我一路走来不是为了在最后关头打退堂鼓。

SYN get cold feet, give up, withdraw, back out
