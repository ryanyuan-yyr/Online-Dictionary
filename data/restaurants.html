restaurant
restərɒnt -rənt ˈrɛstəˌrɑnt ˈrɛstərənt ˈrɛsˌtrɑnt
Word forms: plural restaurants
N-COUNT 餐馆;饭店 A restaurant is a place where you can eat a meal and pay for it. In restaurants your food is usually served to you at your table by a waiter or waitress. →see usage note at: café 餐馆;饭店

  • They ate in an Italian restaurant in Forth Street.

    他们在第四大街的一家意大利餐馆吃了饭。

  • We had dinner in the hotel's restaurant.

    我们在这家宾馆的餐厅吃了晚饭。
