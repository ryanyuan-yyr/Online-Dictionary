shambolic
ʃæmbɒlɪk ʃæmˈbɑlɪk
ADJ-GRADED 混乱无序的;乱七八糟的 If you describe a situation, person, or place as shambolic, you mean that they are very disorganized. 混乱无序的;乱七八糟的 [British]

  • ...a shambolic public relations disaster.

    一次混乱无序的公关灾难

  • John lived in a stylishly shambolic artist's studio.

    约翰住在一个新潮而又混乱无序的艺术家的工作室里。

SYN disorganized, disordered, chaotic, confused
