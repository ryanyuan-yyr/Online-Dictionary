invigilate
ɪnvɪdʒɪleɪt ɪnˈvɪdʒəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense invigilates, present participle invigilating, past tense , past participle invigilated
VERB 监(考) Someone who invigilates an examination supervises the people who are taking it in order to ensure that it starts and finishes at the correct time, and that there is no cheating. 监(考) [British] [Also VERB]

    我教过六年级学生并监考。

SYN watch over, run, conduct, oversee
