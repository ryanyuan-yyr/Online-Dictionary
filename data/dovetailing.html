dovetail
dʌvteɪl ˈdʌvˌteɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dovetails, present participle dovetailing, past tense , past participle dovetailed
1 V-RECIP-ERG (与…)吻合;(与…)切合 If two things dovetail or if one thing dovetails with another, the two things fit together neatly or have some common characteristics. (与…)吻合;(与…)切合

  • I'm following up a few things that might dovetail. [VERB]

    我正追查几件可能互相关联的事。

  • ...an attempt to look for areas where U.S. interests can dovetail with Japanese concerns. [VERB + with]

    寻找既符合美国利益又是日本关注点的领域的企图

  • It is important that we dovetail our respective interests. [VERB noun]

    让我们各自的利益相吻合很重要。

  • The management of local affairs should dovetail regional interests with those of the country as a whole. [VERB noun with noun]

    地方事务的管理应该使地方利益和国家的整体利益相吻合。

SYN correspond, match, agree, accord  
2 N-COUNT 鸠形榫（接头） A dovetail or a dovetail joint is a joint used for fitting two pieces of wood tightly together. 鸠形榫（接头）
