themself
ðəmself ðəmˈsɛlf
PRON-REFL (当明确指代单数主语时，用以代替 themselves;有人认为这种用法不对) Themself is sometimes used instead of 'themselves' when it clearly refers to a singular subject. Some people consider this use to be incorrect. (当明确指代单数主语时，用以代替 themselves;有人认为这种用法不对)

  • No one perceived themself to be in a position to hire such a man.

    没有人认为自己能做主雇用这样一个人。

  • ...if the person themself wouldn't give me the permission to talk to their GP.

    如果那个人自己不同意我和他的医生交谈
