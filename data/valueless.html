valueless
væljuːləs ˈvæljulɪs
ADJ 无价值的;无用的;无效的 If you describe something as valueless, you mean that it is not at all useful. 无价值的;无用的;无效的

  • Such attitudes are valueless unless they reflect inner cognition and certainty.

    这种态度如果不能反映内心的认知和把握就毫无价值。

  • ...commercially valueless trees.

    毫无商业价值的树木
