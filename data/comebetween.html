come between
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 离间;挑拨;妨碍 If someone or something comes between two people, or comes between a person and a thing, they make the relationship or connection between them less close or happy. 离间;挑拨;妨碍

  • It's difficult to imagine anything coming between them. [VERB PREP. noun]

    很难想象会有什么事能使他们反目。

  • He's coming between you and your work. [V P pl-n]

    他在妨碍你的工作。
