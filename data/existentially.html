existential
egzɪstenʃəl ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl ˌɛgzɪsˈtɛnʃəl ˌ ɛksɪsˈtɛnʃəl ˌɛgzɪsˈtɛntʃəl ˈɛksɪsˈtɛntʃəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 关于人类存在的;基于存在（或经验）的 Existential means relating to human existence and experience. 关于人类存在的;基于存在（或经验）的 [formal]

  • Existential questions requiring religious answers still persist.

    需要通过宗教来解释的关乎人类存在的问题仍然存留。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 有关(生死)存在的 You use existential to describe fear, anxiety, and other feelings that are caused by thinking about human existence and death. 有关(生死)存在的 [formal]

  • 'What if there's nothing left at all?' he cries, lost in some intense existential angst.

    “要是什么都不会留下呢？”他叫喊道，陷入了对自身存在的深度焦虑。
