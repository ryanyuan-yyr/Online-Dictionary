brutality
bruːtælɪti bruˈtæləti
Word forms: plural brutalities
N-VAR 野蛮行径；残暴行为 Brutality is cruel and violent treatment or behaviour. A brutality is an instance of cruel and violent treatment or behaviour. 野蛮行径；残暴行为

  • Her experience of men was of domination and brutality.

    男人给她的感受是支配一切，野蛮残暴。

  • ...police brutality.

    警察的残暴行为

  • ...the atrocities and brutalities committed by a former regime.

    前政权犯下的残暴罪行

SYN cruelty, atrocity, ferocity, savagery
