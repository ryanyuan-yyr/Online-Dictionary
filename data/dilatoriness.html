dilatory
dɪlətri -tɔːri ˈdɪləˌtɔri
ADJ-GRADED 拖拉的;拖沓的;延误的 Someone or something that is dilatory is slow and causes delay. 拖拉的;拖沓的;延误的 [formal]

  • You might expect politicians to smooth things out when civil servants are being dilatory.

    公务员办事拖沓，你可能认为政治家会把事情顺利解决。

SYN slow, delaying, lingering, putting off
