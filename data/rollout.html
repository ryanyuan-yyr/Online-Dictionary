roll out
PHRASAL VERB 推出 (新产品或服务)If a company rolls out a new product or service, or if the product or service rolls out, it is made available to the public. 推出 (新产品或服务)

  • On Thursday Microsoft rolls out its new operating system.

    在星期四微软推出了它的新操作系统。

  • Northern Telecom says its products will roll out over 18 months beginning early next year.

    北方电讯公司表示其产品将从明年初起用18个多月的时间推出。
