actuate
æktʃueɪt ˈæktʃʊˌeɪt ˈæktʃuˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense actuates, present participle actuating, past tense , past participle actuated
VERB 激励;驱使;开动 If a person is actuated by an emotion, that emotion makes them act in a certain way. If something actuates a device, the device starts working. 激励;驱使;开动

  • They were actuated by desire. [be VERB-ed]

    他们受到欲望的驱使。

  • The flow of current actuates the signal. [VERB noun]

    电流发出了信号。

SYN motivate, move, drive, influence
