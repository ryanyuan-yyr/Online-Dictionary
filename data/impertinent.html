impertinent
ɪmpɜːʳtɪnənt ɪmˈpɜrtənənt
ADJ-GRADED 不礼貌的;傲慢的;莽撞的 If someone talks or behaves in a rather impolite and disrespectful way, you can say that they are being impertinent . 不礼貌的;傲慢的;莽撞的

  • Would it be impertinent to ask where exactly you were?.

    能冒昧问一下您所在的确切位置吗？

  • I don't like strangers who ask impertinent questions.

    我不喜欢冒昧提问的陌生人。

SYN rude, forward, cheeky [informal] , saucy [informal]
