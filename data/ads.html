公元 You use AD in dates to indicate the number of years or centuries that have passed since the year in which Jesus Christ is believed to have been born. 公元

  • The cathedral was destroyed by the Great Fire of 1136 AD.

    大教堂在公元1136年的大火中被付之一炬。

    最初的城堡可能建于公元860年左右。

    罗马帝国于公元4世纪分裂。
