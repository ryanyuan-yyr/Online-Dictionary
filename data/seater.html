-seater -siːtəʳ ˈsitər
Word forms: plural -seaters
1 COMB in ADJ and N-COUNT (与数词连用构成形容词和名词)表示“有…个座位的”，“可容纳…人的事物” -seater combines with numbers to form adjectives and nouns which indicate how many people something such as a car has seats for. (与数词连用构成形容词和名词)表示“有…个座位的”，“可容纳…人的事物”

  • ...a two-seater sports car.

    一辆双座跑车

  • ...a three-seater sofa.

    能坐 3 个人的沙发

  • The plane is an eight-seater with twin propellers.

    这架飞机有 8 个座位，靠双螺旋桨推进。
