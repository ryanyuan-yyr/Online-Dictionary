tease out
PHRASAL VERB 套取，套出，巧妙获得(信息或答案) If you tease out information or a solution, you succeed in obtaining it even though this is difficult. 套取，套出，巧妙获得(信息或答案) [Also V n P + of]

  • They try to tease out the answers without appearing to ask. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们试图不动声色地套出答案。

  • There had to be an answer — he was sure he could tease it out if only he had time. [VERB noun PREP.]

    一定会有答案—他肯定，只要他有时间就一定能弄个清楚。
