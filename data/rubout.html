rub out
1 PHRASAL VERB （用橡皮等）擦掉，擦去 If you rub out something that you have written on paper or a board, you remove it using a rubber or eraser. （用橡皮等）擦掉，擦去 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She began rubbing out the pencilled marks in the margin. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她开始擦掉空白处的铅笔笔迹。

SYN erase, remove, cancel, wipe out  
2 PHRASAL VERB 除掉;杀掉 If one person rubs out another, they kill them. 除掉;杀掉 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Nobody else believed that they had tried to rub out the pope. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    其他人都不相信他们试图杀死教皇。

SYN murder [US , slang] , hit [slang] , kill, waste [informal]
