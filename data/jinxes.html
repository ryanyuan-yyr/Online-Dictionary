jinx
dʒɪŋks dʒɪŋks
Word forms: plural jinxes
N-COUNT [usually singular] 倒霉蛋；扫把星；不祥的人（或物） You can call something or someone that is considered to be unlucky or to bring bad luck a jinx . 倒霉蛋；扫把星；不祥的人（或物）

  • He was beginning to think he was a jinx.

    他开始认为自己是个倒霉蛋。

SYN curse, plague [informal] , voodoo, nemesis
