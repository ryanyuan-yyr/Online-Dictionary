trauma
trɔːmə traʊmə ˈtrɔmə ˈtrɑmə
Word forms: plural traumas
N-VAR 精神创伤;惊骇(或痛苦)经历 Trauma is a very severe shock or very upsetting experience, which may cause psychological damage. 精神创伤;惊骇(或痛苦)经历

  • I'd been through the trauma of losing a house. [+ of]

    我经历过失去房子的痛苦。

  • The officers are claiming compensation for trauma after the disaster.

    灾难过后军官们正在要求赔偿精神损失。

SYN shock, suffering, worry, pain
