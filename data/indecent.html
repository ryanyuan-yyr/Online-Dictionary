indecent
ɪndiːsənt ɪnˈdisənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 下流的;有伤风化的;猥亵的 If you describe something as indecent, you mean that it is shocking and offensive, usually because it relates to sex or nakedness. 下流的;有伤风化的;猥亵的

  • He accused Mrs Moore of making an indecent suggestion.

    他指责摩尔夫人做下流的性暗示。

indecently

  • He behaved indecently.

    他举止下流。

  • ...an indecently short skirt.

    有伤风化的短裙

2 ADJ (速度或数量)不合常规的，离谱的 If you describe the speed or amount of something as indecent, you are indicating, often in a humorous way, that it is much quicker or larger than is usual or desirable. (速度或数量)不合常规的，离谱的

  • The opposition says the legislation was drafted with indecent haste.

    反对人士认为该法律的起草太过草率。

SYN unbecoming, unsuitable, vulgar, improper  
indecently

  • ...an indecently large office.

    大得离谱的办公室
