laugh off
PHRASAL VERB 对…一笑置之；笑言以对 If you laugh off a difficult or serious situation, you try to suggest that it is amusing and unimportant, for example by making a joke about it. 对…一笑置之；笑言以对

  • The couple laughed off rumours that their marriage was in trouble. [VERB PREP. noun]

    夫妻两人对他们婚姻出现问题的谣言一笑置之。

  • Whilst I used to laugh it off, I'm now getting irritated by it. [VERB noun PREP.]

    这种事情我从前会付之一笑，但现在开始觉得挺烦的。

SYN disregard, ignore, dismiss, overlook
