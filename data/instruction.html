instruction
ɪnstrʌkʃən ɪnˈstrʌkʃən
Word forms: plural instructions
1 N-COUNT 指示;命令 An instruction is something that someone tells you to do. 指示;命令

  • Two lawyers were told not to leave the building but no reason for this instruction was given.

    两名律师被告知不得离开大楼,但对这一命令并没有提供任何理由。

SYN order, ruling, command, rule  
2 N-UNCOUNT 教学;讲授 If someone gives you instruction in a subject or skill, they teach it to you. 教学;讲授 [formal]

  • Each candidate is given instruction in safety. [+ in]

    向每位申请人都讲授了安全知识。

  • All schoolchildren must now receive some religious instruction.

    现在所有的在校儿童都必须接受一定的宗教教育。

SYN teaching, schooling, training, classes  
3 N-PLURAL 用法说明;操作指南 Instructions are clear and detailed information on how to do something. 用法说明;操作指南

  • Always read the instructions before you start taking the medicine.

    在服药之前一定要先阅读说明书。
