counselling
kaʊnsəlɪŋ
in AM, use 美国英语用 counseling
N-UNCOUNT (治疗师或其他专家提供的)咨询，建议 Counselling is advice which a therapist or other expert gives to someone about a particular problem. (治疗师或其他专家提供的)咨询，建议

  • She will need medical help and counselling to overcome the tragedy.

    她需要借助医学治疗和心理咨询来克服惨剧带来的影响。

  • She brought her husband in for marriage counseling.

    她带丈夫来进行婚姻咨询。
