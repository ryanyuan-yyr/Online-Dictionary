wingtip wɪŋtɪp ˈwɪŋˌtɪp ˈwɪŋˌtɪp
Word forms: wingtips
1 N-COUNT (飞机)翼尖The wingtips of an aircraft are the ends of its wings. (飞机)翼尖

  • The Tornado was about thirty feet from Mace's right wingtip.

    “龙卷风”离“梅斯”的右机翼尖约三十英尺。

2 N-COUNT [usu pl] (正装)男鞋Wingtips are formal men's shoes with laces and a pattern in the shape of a wing on the toe. (正装)男鞋 [US]

  • ...a pair of well-polished brown wingtips.

    ...一双擦得很亮的棕色正装男鞋。
