picturesque
pɪktʃəresk ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk
1 ADJ-GRADED 风景如画的；有自然美景的 A picturesque place is attractive and interesting, and has no ugly modern buildings. 风景如画的；有自然美景的

  • Alte, in the hills northwest of Loule, is the Algarve's most picturesque village.

    位于洛莱西北部山区的阿尔特，是阿尔加维风景最美丽的村庄。

SYN interesting, pretty, beautiful, attractive  
You can refer to picturesque things as the picturesque .如画的事物

  • ...lovers of the picturesque.

    热爱如画美景的人

picturesquely

  • ...the shanty-towns perched picturesquely on the hillsides.

    山坡上错落有致的棚户区

2 ADJ-GRADED （词语或表达）别致的，生动的，富有诗意的 Picturesque words and expressions are unusual or poetic. （词语或表达）别致的，生动的，富有诗意的

  • Every inn had a picturesque name — the Black Locust Inn, the Blueberry Inn.

    每个旅馆都有一个别致的名字，如“洋槐树旅馆”、“蓝莓旅馆”。

SYN vivid, striking, graphic, colourful  
picturesquely

  • The historian Yakut described it picturesquely as a 'mother of castles'.

    历史学家雅库特把它形象地描述为“城堡之母”。
