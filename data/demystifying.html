demystify
diːmɪstɪfaɪ diˈmɪstəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense demystifies, present participle demystifying, past tense , past participle demystified
VERB 使非神秘化;阐明;澄清 If you demystify something, you make it easier to understand by giving a clear explanation of it. 使非神秘化;阐明;澄清

  • This book aims to demystify medical treatments. [VERB noun]

    该书旨在揭开医学治疗的神秘面纱。

  • We urge colleges and universities to demystify the selection process. [VERB noun]

    我们敦促各学院和大学澄清其甄选学生的过程。
