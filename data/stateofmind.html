state of mind steɪt əv maɪnd
Word forms: plural states of mind
N-COUNT [usually singular] (某一特定时刻的)情绪，心态 Your state of mind is your mood or mental state at a particular time. (某一特定时刻的)情绪，心态

  • I want you to get into a whole new state of mind.

    我想让你有一个全新的心态。

  • He's in hospital, and in a confused state of mind.

    他住院了，意识仍然不清。

SYN attitude, perspective, outlook, approach
