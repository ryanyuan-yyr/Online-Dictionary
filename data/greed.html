greed
griːd grid
N-UNCOUNT 贪婪；贪心；贪欲 Greed is the desire to have more of something, such as food or money, than is necessary or fair. 贪婪；贪心；贪欲

  • ...an insatiable greed for personal power. [+ for]

    对个人权力的贪得无厌

  • I get fed up with other people's greed.

    我对别人的贪婪厌烦透了。

SYN avarice, longing, desire, hunger  
Quotations
There is enough in the world for everyone's need, but not enough for everyone's greedFrank Buchman Remaking the World
