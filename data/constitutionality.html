constitutionality
kɒnstɪtjuːʃənælɪti ˌkɒnstɪˌtjuːʃəˈnælɪtɪ -tuː- ˌkɑnstəˌtuʃəˈnæləti ˌkɑnstəˌtjuʃəˈnæləti
N-UNCOUNT 符合宪法;合宪性 In a particular political system, the constitutionality of a law or action is the fact that it is allowed by the constitution. 符合宪法;合宪性 [formal]

  • They plan to challenge the constitutionality of the law. [+ of]

    他们计划对该法的合宪性提出质疑。
