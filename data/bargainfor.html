bargain for
(also bargain on)
PHRASAL VERB 预料到；估计到；考虑到 If you have not bargained for or bargained on something that happens, you did not expect it to happen and so feel surprised or worried by it. 预料到；估计到；考虑到

  • The effects of this policy were more than the government had bargained for. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这项政策带来的影响是政府始料未及的。

  • He had not bargained on the mad jealousy which both women developed. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他没料到这两个女人会嫉妒得发狂。
