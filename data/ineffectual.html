ineffectual
ɪnɪfektʃuəl ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃʊəl ˌɪnɛˈfɛktʃuəl ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəl
ADJ-GRADED 不起作用的;徒劳无益的 If someone or something is ineffectual, they fail to do what they are expected to do or are trying to do. 不起作用的;徒劳无益的

  • The mayor had become ineffectual in the struggle to clamp down on drugs.

    市长打击毒品的努力未见成效。

  • ...the well-meaning but ineffectual jobs programs of the past.

    过去的一些出发点很好但却无成效的就业计划

SYN unproductive, useless, ineffective, vain  
ineffectually

  • Her voice trailed off ineffectually.

    她的声音越来越微弱了。
