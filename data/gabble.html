gabble
gæbəl ˈɡæbəl ˈgæbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gabbles, present participle gabbling, past tense , past participle gabbled
VERB 急促而含混不清地说 If you gabble, you say things so quickly that it is difficult for people to understand you. 急促而含混不清地说 [informal] [Also VERB with quote]

  • Marcello sat on his knee and gabbled excitedly. [VERB]

    马塞洛跪坐在地上，激动地哇哩哇啦地说着。

  • She gabbles on about drug dealers and journalists. [VERB adv.]

    她叽里咕噜地说着什么毒贩和记者。

  • One of the soldiers gabbled something and pointed at the front door. [VERB noun]

    其中一个士兵急促不清地说了些什么，然后指向了前门。

SYN prattle, chatter, babble, gibber
