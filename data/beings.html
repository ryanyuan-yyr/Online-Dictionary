being
biːɪŋ ˈbiɪŋ
Word forms: plural beings
1 Being is the present participle of be .
2 V-LINK （用于说明原因的非限定性从句中）因为，由于 Being is used in non-finite clauses where you are giving the reason for something. （用于说明原因的非限定性从句中）因为，由于 [Also VERB preposition]

  • It being a Sunday, the old men had the day off. [VERB noun]

    因为是星期天，所以老人都休息。

  • Little boys, being what they are, might decide to play on it. [VERB noun]

    出于天性，小男孩们可能会决定利用这一点。

  • Of course, being young, I did not worry. [VERB adj.ive]

    当然，因为还年轻，所以我并不担心。

3 N-COUNT 生物 You can refer to any real or imaginary creature as a being . 生物

  • People expect a horse to perform like a car, with no thought for its feelings as a living being.

    人们希望马能像汽车一样，完全没有考虑到马作为一种有生命的动物会有什么样的感受。

  • ...beings from outer space.

    来自外太空的生物

SYN individual, thing, body [informal] , animal  
4See also:human being 
5 N-UNCOUNT 存在 Being is existence. Something that is in being or comes into being exists. 存在

  • Abraham Maslow described psychology as 'the science of being.'.

    亚伯拉罕·马斯洛形容心理学是“存在的科学”。

  • The Kingdom of Italy formally came into being on 17 March 1861.

    意大利王国于1861年3月17日正式成立。

  • ...the complex process by which the novel is brought into being.

    这部小说复杂的写作过程

SYN life, living, reality, animation  
6 PHR-CONJ-SUBORD （用于解释所说内容的原因）因为，由于 You can use being as to introduce a reason for what you are saying. （用于解释所说内容的原因）因为，由于 [mainly British, informal, spoken]

  • I used to go everywhere with my mother being as I was the youngest.

    我以前到哪儿都跟着我妈，因为我是老小。
