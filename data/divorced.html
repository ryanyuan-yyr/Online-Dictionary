divorced dɪvɔːʳst
1 ADJ 离婚的;离异的 Someone who is divorced from their former husband or wife has separated from them and is no longer legally married to them. 离婚的;离异的

  • Princess Margaret is divorced from Lord Snowdon.

    玛格丽特公主与斯诺登勋爵离婚了。

  • He is divorced, with a young son.

    他离婚了，带着一个年幼的儿子。

SYN separated, deserted, living apart  
2 ADJ-GRADED 脱离…的;与…完全不一样的 If you say that one thing is divorced from another, you mean that the two things are very different and separate from each other. 脱离…的;与…完全不一样的

  • ...speculative theories divorced from political reality. [+ from]

    与政治事实相脱离的投机理论

3 ADJ-GRADED 不受…影响的;置身世外的 If you say that someone is divorced from a situation, you mean that they act as if they are not affected by it in any way. 不受…影响的;置身世外的

  • This just shows how divorced from reality she's become. [+ from]

    这恰恰说明了她变得多么不切实际。
