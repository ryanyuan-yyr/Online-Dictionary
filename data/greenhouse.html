greenhouse
griːnhaʊs ˈgrinˌhaʊs
Word forms: plural greenhouses
1 N-COUNT 温室；暖房 A greenhouse is a glass building in which you grow plants that need to be protected from bad weather. 温室；暖房
SYN glasshouse, conservatory, hothouse  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 温室效应的；引起温室效应的 Greenhouse means relating to or causing the greenhouse effect. 温室效应的；引起温室效应的
