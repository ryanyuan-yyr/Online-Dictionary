report card
rɪˈpɔːt kɑːd
Word forms: plural report cards
1 N-COUNT 学年（或学期）成绩单 A report card is an official written account of how well or how badly a pupil has done during the term or year that has just finished. 学年（或学期）成绩单 [US]

  • The only time I got their attention was when I brought home straight A's on my report card.

    我唯一一次引起他们注意是我把全优成绩单带回家的时候。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 report
2 N-COUNT 工作情况报告 A report card is a report on how well a person, organization, or country has been doing recently. 工作情况报告 [US, journalism]

  • The President today issued his final report card on the state of the economy.

    总统今天发表了关于经济状况的最终报告。
