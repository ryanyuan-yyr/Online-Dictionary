explore
ɪksplɔːʳ ɪkˈsplɔː ɛkˈsplɔr ɪkˈsplɔr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense explores, present participle exploring, past tense , past participle explored
1 VERB 在…探险;探测;勘察;考察 If you explore a place, you travel around it to find out what it is like. 在…探险;探测;勘察;考察

  • I just wanted to explore Paris, read Sartre, listen to Sidney Bechet. [VERB noun]

    我就想逛逛巴黎，读读萨特的作品，听听悉尼·贝谢的音乐。

  • After exploring the old part of town there is a guided tour of the cathedral. [VERB noun]

    游览完老城区之后，会由导游带队参观大教堂。

  • We've come to this country, let's explore! [VERB]

    我们已经来到这个国家，让我们好好探访一番吧！

SYN travel around, tour, survey, scout  
exploration

  • We devote several days to the exploration of the magnificent Maya sites of Copan.

    我们安排了几天时间考察科潘宏伟的玛雅遗址。

  • We set out on this voyage of exploration with an open mind.

    我们以开放的心态开始了这次探索之旅。

2 VERB 探讨，研究（观点或建议） If you explore an idea or suggestion, you think about it or comment on it in detail, in order to assess it carefully. 探讨，研究（观点或建议）

  • The secretary is expected to explore ideas for post-war reconstruction of the area. [VERB noun]

    部长预计将会探讨这个地区战后重建的意见。

  • The film explores the relationship between artist and instrument. [VERB noun]

    这部电影探究了艺术家和乐器之间的关系。

SYN investigate, consider, research, survey  
exploration

  • I looked forward to the exploration of their theories.

    我期待着研究他们的理论。

3 VERB 勘探 If people explore an area for a substance such as oil or minerals, they study the area and do tests on the land to see whether they can find it. 勘探 [Also V n for n]

  • Central to the operation is a mile-deep well, dug originally to explore for oil. [VERB + for]

    那次行动的重点是一口深达1英里的井，最初钻那口井是为了勘探石油。

  • The government is allowing the areas of inshore coastal waters to be explored for oil and gas. [be V-ed for n]

    当局允许在近海海域勘探石油和天然气。

exploration

  • Oryx is a Dallas-based oil and gas exploration and production concern.

    奥瑞克斯是一家设在达拉斯的石油和天然气勘探生产公司。

4 VERB （用手等）摸索，触碰 If you explore something with your hands or fingers, you touch it to find out what it feels like. （用手等）摸索，触碰

  • He explored the wound with his finger, trying to establish its extent. [VERB noun]

    他用手指摸了摸伤口，想要确定受伤的程度。
