therapy
θerəpi ˈθɛrəpi
Word forms: plural therapies
1 N-UNCOUNT (非药物、非手术的)疗法，治疗 Therapy is the treatment of someone with mental or physical illness without the use of drugs or operations. (非药物、非手术的)疗法，治疗

  • In therapy, she began to let go of her obsession with Mike.

    在接受治疗的过程中，她开始逐渐从对迈克的迷恋中解脱出来。

  • He is having therapy to conquer his phobia.

    他在接受治疗以克服自己的恐惧症。

SYN psychotherapy, analysis, psychoanalysis  
2 N-VAR (针对某人特定病情的)治疗方案，治疗方法 A therapy is a particular treatment of someone with a particular illness. (针对某人特定病情的)治疗方案，治疗方法 [medicine]

  • ...hormonal therapies.

    激素疗法

  • ...conventional drug therapy.

    常规药物疗法
