buff
bʌf bʌf
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense buffs, present participle buffing, past tense , past participle buffed
1 COLOUR 浅棕色；米色 Something that is buff is pale brown in colour. 浅棕色；米色

  • He took a largish buff envelope from his pocket.

    他从口袋里掏出一个相当大的米色信封。

2 N-COUNT 迷；行家 You use buff to describe someone who knows a lot about a particular subject. For example, if you describe someone as a film buff, you mean that they know a lot about films. 迷；行家 [informal]

  • Judge Lanier is a real film buff.

    拉尼尔法官是个真正的影迷。

  • Any competent computer buff should be able to do it for you in a few minutes.

    任何一个有点儿水平的电脑行家都能在几分钟内为你解决这个问题。

SYN expert, fan, addict, enthusiast  
3 VERB 用软布擦亮 If you buff the surface of something, for example your car or your shoes, you rub it with a piece of soft material in order to make it shine. 用软布擦亮

  • He was already buffing the car's hubs. [VERB noun]

    他已经在擦汽车轮毂了。

SYN polish, clean, smooth, brush  
buffing

  • Regular buffing helps prevent nails from splitting.

    经常抛光保养指甲可以预防指甲劈裂。
