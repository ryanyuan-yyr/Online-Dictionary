old hand
Word forms: plural old hands
N-COUNT 老手；经验丰富的人 If someone is an old hand at something, they are very skilled at it because they have been doing it for a long time. 老手；经验丰富的人

  • An old hand at photography, Tim has been shooting wildlife as a hobby for the last 13 years. [+ at]

    蒂姆摄影经验丰富，在过去13年里一直以拍摄野生生物为业余爱好。

SYN veteran, expert, master, pro [informal]
