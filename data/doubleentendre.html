double entendre duːbəl ɒntɒndrə dubl ɑ̃tɑ̃drə
Word forms: plural double entendres
N-VAR (常指带有猥亵含义的)双关语 A double entendre is a word or phrase that has two meanings, one of which is rude and often sexual. (常指带有猥亵含义的)双关语

  • He is a master of the pun and the double entendre.

    他出口就是俏皮话，说话下流不带脏字。

  • He has a fondness for outrageous double entendre.

    他很喜欢用无礼、下流的双关语。

SYN double meaning, ambiguity, pun, innuendo
