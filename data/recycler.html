recycle
riːsaɪkəl riˈsaɪkəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recycles, present participle recycling, past tense , past participle recycled
VERB 回收利用；循环利用 If you recycle things that have already been used, such as bottles or sheets of paper, you process them so that they can be used again. 回收利用；循环利用

  • The objective would be to recycle 98 per cent of domestic waste. [VERB noun]

    目标是对 98%的生活垃圾进行回收利用。

  • All glass bottles which can't be refilled can be recycled. [VERB noun]

    所有不能再次装用的玻璃瓶子都可以被回收利用。

  • It is printed on recycled paper. [VERB-ed]

    它是用再生纸打印的。

SYN reprocess, reuse, salvage, reclaim  
recycling

    回收利用的计划
