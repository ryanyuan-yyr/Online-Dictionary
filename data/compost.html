compost
kɒmpɒst -poʊst ˈkɑmˌpoʊst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense composts, present participle composting, past tense , past participle composted
1 N-UNCOUNT 堆肥;混合肥料 Compost is a mixture of decayed plants and vegetable waste which is added to the soil to help plants grow. 堆肥;混合肥料

  • ...a small compost heap.

    一小堆堆肥

2 N-MASS (在花盆中栽培用的)特制土 Compost is specially treated soil that you buy and use to grow seeds and plants in pots. (在花盆中栽培用的)特制土
3 VERB 把…制成堆肥 To compost things such as unwanted bits of plants means to make them into compost. 把…制成堆肥

  • Cut down and compost spent cucumbers, tomatoes and other crops. [VERB noun]

    将已收获过的黄瓜、西红柿和其他作物的秧蔓割下，制成堆肥。

  • All garden waste should be composted and returned to the garden. [VERB noun]

    应该把花园里所有枯萎的花草制成堆肥，然后再用它们给花园施肥。

composting

  • Composting is the ideal way of getting rid of vegetable, garden and organic waste.

    制造堆肥是处理菜根菜叶、败花朽木和有机废物的理想方式。
