lead on to
1 PHRASAL VERB 导致；引致 If one event or action leads on to another, it causes it or makes it possible. 导致；引致 [mainly British]

  • It is often the case that early interests lead on to a career. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    职业生涯往往由早期爱好而来。

  • This discovery led on to studies of the immune system. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    此发现使人们开始对免疫系统进行研究。

2 PHRASAL VERB 通连；通到；通往 If a door, gate, or bridge leads on to a place, the place is on the other side of it. 通连；通到；通往

  • There were glass doors leading on to this balcony. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    有玻璃门通到这个阳台。
