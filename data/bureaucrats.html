bureaucrat
bjʊərəkræt ˈbjʊərəˌkræt ˈbjʊrəˌkræt
Word forms: plural bureaucrats
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 官僚；官僚主义者 Bureaucrats are officials who work in a large administrative system. You can refer to officials as bureaucrats especially if you disapprove of them because they seem to follow rules and procedures too strictly. 官僚；官僚主义者 [disapproval]

  • The economy is still controlled by bureaucrats.

    经济依然被官僚们所掌控。

SYN official, minister, officer, administrator
