panoply
pænəpli ˈpænəpli
N-SING （尤指令人印象深刻的）一整套，全套 A panoply of things is a wide range of them, especially one that is considered impressive. （尤指令人印象深刻的）一整套，全套 [formal]

  • He was attended, as are all heads of state, by a full panoply of experts. [+ of]

    和所有国家元首一样，他身边跟随着各个领域的专家。

  • ...the marvellous panoply of exhibitions laid on this year.

    今年异彩纷呈的全面展出
