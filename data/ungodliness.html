ungodly
ʌngɒdli ʌnˈgɑdli
1 ADJ-GRADED 不道德的；违背宗教教义的 If you describe someone or something as ungodly, you mean that they are morally bad or are opposed to religion. 不道德的；违背宗教教义的

  • Such a view implies that our bodies and sexual nature are inherently ungodly.

    这样的观点暗示我们的身体和性本能天生就是邪恶的。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （时间）过早的 If you refer to a time as an ungodly hour, you are emphasizing that it is very early in the morning. （时间）过早的 [emphasis]

  • ...at the ungodly hour of 4.00am.

    凌晨4点钟

SYN unreasonable, outrageous, unearthly, dreadful  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 过度的；不合理的 If you refer to the amount or volume of something as ungodly, you mean that it is excessive or unreasonable. 过度的；不合理的

  • ...a power struggle of ungodly proportions.

    过多的权力斗争
