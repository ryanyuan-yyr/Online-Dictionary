batter
bætəʳ ˈbætər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense batters, present participle battering, past tense , past participle battered
1 VERB 殴打；痛打；毒打 If someone is battered, they are regularly hit and badly hurt by a member of their family or by their partner. 殴打；痛打；毒打

  • ...evidence that the child was being battered. [be VERB-ed]

    这个孩子被家人毒打的证据

  • ...boys who witness fathers battering their mothers. [VERB noun]

    目睹父亲殴打母亲的男孩们

  • ...battered wives. [VERB-ed]

    受到毒打的妻子

battering

  • Leaving the relationship does not mean that the battering will stop.

    结束这段感情并不意味着这种殴打伤害就会停止。

2 VERB （用拳头或重物）多次重击，连续猛打（某人） To batter someone means to hit them many times, using fists or a heavy object. （用拳头或重物）多次重击，连续猛打（某人） [Also VERB n]

  • He battered her around the head. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    他连续重击她的头部。

  • A karate expert battered a man to death. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    一位空手道高手将一名男子毒打致死。

  • He was battered unconscious. [be VERB-ed]

    他被打晕了。

SYN beat, hit, strike, knock  
battered

  • Her battered body was discovered in a field.

    在田野里发现了她的尸体，生前曾遭到重击。

3 VERB [usually passive] （风、雨或暴风雪等）肆虐，严重破坏 If a place is battered by wind, rain, or storms, it is seriously damaged or affected by very bad weather. （风、雨或暴风雪等）肆虐，严重破坏

  • The country has been battered by winds of between fifty and seventy miles an hour. [be VERB-ed]

    该国遭受到时速高达50至70英里的大风袭击，破坏严重。

  • ...a storm that's been battering the Northeast coastline. [VERB noun]

    在东北海岸肆虐的暴风雨

SYN damage, destroy, hurt, injure  
4 VERB （用拳头或重物）多次重击,连续猛打（某物） If you batter something, you hit it many times, using your fists or a heavy object. （用拳头或重物）多次重击,连续猛打（某物）

  • They were battering the door, they were breaking in. [VERB noun]

    他们正在使劲捶门，就要闯进来了。

  • Batter the steaks flat. [VERB noun adj.ive]

    把牛排捶扁。

5 N-VAR （面粉、鸡蛋和牛奶和成的）糊，面糊 Batter is a mixture of flour, eggs, and milk that is used in cooking. （面粉、鸡蛋和牛奶和成的）糊，面糊

  • ...pancake batter.

    做煎饼的面糊

  • ...fish in batter.

    裹着面糊的鱼

6 N-COUNT （棒球和垒球等运动中的）击球员，击球手 In sports such as baseball and softball, a batter is a person who hits the ball with a wooden bat. （棒球和垒球等运动中的）击球员，击球手

  • ...batters and pitchers.

    击球手和投球手们

7 See also: battered  battering
