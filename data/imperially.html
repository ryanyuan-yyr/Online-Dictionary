imperial
ɪmpɪəriəl ɪmˈpɪriəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 帝国的;皇帝的 Imperial is used to refer to things or people that are or were connected with an empire. 帝国的;皇帝的

  • ...the Imperial Palace in Tokyo.

    东京的皇宫

  • They executed Russia's imperial family in 1918.

    他们 1918 年处死了俄国沙皇皇族。

SYN royal, regal, kingly, queenly  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (度量衡)英制的 The imperial system of measurement uses inches, feet, and yards to measure length, ounces and pounds to measure weight, and pints and gallons to measure volume. (度量衡)英制的
