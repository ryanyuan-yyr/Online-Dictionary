underpaid
ʌndəʳpeɪd ˌʌndəˈpeɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 所得报酬偏低的；工资偏低的 People who are underpaid are not paid enough money for the job that they do. 所得报酬偏低的；工资偏低的

    女性工作所得的报酬常常偏低。

  • ...underpaid factory workers.

    工资偏低的工厂工人
