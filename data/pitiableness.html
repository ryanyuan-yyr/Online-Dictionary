pitiable
pɪtiəbəl ˈpɪtiəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 值得怜悯（或同情）的；可怜的 Someone who is pitiable is in such a sad or weak state that you feel pity for them. 值得怜悯（或同情）的；可怜的 [written]

  • Her grandmother seemed to her a pitiable figure.

    祖母在她看来是个可怜的人。

SYN pathetic, distressing, miserable, poor  
pitiably

  • Their main grievance was that they had not received their pitiably low pay.

    他们主要的抱怨是没有拿到那少得可怜的工资。

  • She found Frances lying on the bed crying pitiably.

    她发现弗朗西丝躺在床上，哭得十分可怜。
