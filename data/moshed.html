mosh
mɒʃ mɒʃ mɑʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense moshes, present participle moshing, past tense , past participle moshed
VERB (摇滚音乐会上，观众推搡着)狂舞，劲舞，来回跳动 If people at a rock concert mosh, they jump up and down together in front of the stage, often pushing each other. (摇滚音乐会上，观众推搡着)狂舞，劲舞，来回跳动

  • Moshing down the front, crushed against the stage in a sweat-drenched T-shirt is all part of the gig experience. [VERB]
