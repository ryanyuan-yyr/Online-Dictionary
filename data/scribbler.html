scribbler
skrɪbələʳ ˈskrɪblər
Word forms: plural scribblers
N-COUNT [usually plural] 蹩脚文人;三流作家 People sometimes refer to writers as scribblers when they think they are not very good writers. 蹩脚文人;三流作家 [mainly journalism, disapproval]

  • The world is full of scribblers. I don't intend to join their ranks.

    这个世上净是些蹩脚文人。我不想与他们为伍。
