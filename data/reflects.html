reflect
rɪflekt rɪˈflɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reflects, present participle reflecting, past tense , past participle reflected
1 VERB 反映；表现；显示 If something reflects an attitude or situation, it shows that the attitude or situation exists or it shows what it is like. 反映；表现；显示

  • The Los Angeles riots reflected the bitterness between the black and Korean communities in the city. [VERB noun]

    洛杉矶的暴乱反映出这个城市的黑人社区和韩裔社区之间的仇恨。

  • Concern at the economic situation was reflected in the government's budget. [VERB noun]

    政府的预算反映了对经济运行状况的关注。

SYN show, reveal, express, display  
2 V-ERG （光、热或其他射线）反射 When light, heat, or other rays reflect off a surface or when a surface reflects them, they are sent back from the surface and do not pass through it. （光、热或其他射线）反射

  • The sun reflected off the snow-covered mountains. [VERB prep.]

    冰雪覆盖的山峰反射着阳光。

  • The glass appears to reflect light naturally. [VERB noun]

    玻璃似乎可以自然反射光线。

SYN throw back, return, mirror, echo  
3 VERB [usually passive] （在镜子或水中）映出 When something is reflected in a mirror or in water, you can see its image in the mirror or in the water. （在镜子或水中）映出

  • His image seemed to be reflected many times in the mirror. [be VERB-ed]

    那面镜子里好像映现出很多个他的影像。

4 VERB 沉思；深思 When you reflect on something, you think deeply about it. 沉思；深思

  • We should all give ourselves time to reflect. [VERB]

    我们都应该给自己时间好好想想。

  • I reflected on the child's future. [VERB + on/upon]

    我认真考虑了孩子的将来。

SYN consider, think, contemplate, deliberate  
5 VERB 认为;想 You can use reflect to indicate that a particular thought occurs to someone. 认为;想

  • Things were very much changed since before the war, he reflected. [VERB that]

    情况与战前大不同了，他想。

6 VERB 使对…产生某种印象 If an action or situation reflects in a particular way on someone or something, it gives people a good or bad impression of them. 使对…产生某种印象

  • The affair hardly reflected well on the British. [V adv + on]

    这个事件很难让人们对英国人有好印象。

  • Your own personal behavior as a teacher, outside of school hours, reflects on the school itself. [VERB + on]

    作为教师，你在课堂以外的个人行为会影响人们对学校的印象。
