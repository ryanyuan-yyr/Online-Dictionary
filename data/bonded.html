bonded
bɒndɪd ˈbɑndɪd
ADJ （公司） 有担保的 A bonded company has entered into a legal agreement which offers its customers some protection if the company does not fulfil its contract with them. （公司） 有担保的 [business]

  • The company is a fully bonded member of the Association of British Travel Agents.

    该公司是英国旅行社协会的完全担保会员。

  • The company was not bonded in any way and as such there is no compensation scheme.

    该公司没有任何形式的担保，因此没有补偿方案。
