seek out
PHRASAL VERB 寻找到;找出 If you seek out someone or something or seek them out, you keep looking for them until you find them. 寻找到;找出

  • Now is the time for local companies to seek out business opportunities in Europe. [VERB PREP. noun]

    对于当地公司来说，现在是时候去欧洲寻求商机了。

  • Ellen spent the day in the hills and sought me out when she returned. [VERB noun PREP.]

    埃伦在山里呆了一整天，并于回来后找到了我。
