wittingly
wɪtɪŋli
ADV [usually ADVERB with verb, oft ADVERB adjective] 有意地;故意地;蓄意地 If you do something wittingly, you are fully aware of what you are doing and what its consequences will be. 有意地;故意地;蓄意地 [formal]

  • When she had an affair with her friend's husband, she wittingly set off a chain of crises.

    她和朋友的丈夫私通，故意引发了一连串危机。
