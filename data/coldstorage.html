cold storage
1 N-UNCOUNT 冷藏 If something such as food is put in cold storage, it is kept in an artificially cooled place in order to preserve it. 冷藏

  • The strawberries are kept in cold storage to prevent them spoiling during transportation.

    草莓在运输过程中要冷藏，以防止变质。

2 PHRASE [verb-link PHRASE, PHRASE after verb] (想法或计划的)暂时搁置 If you put an idea or plan into cold storage or in cold storage, you delay it for a while rather than acting on it as you originally intended. (想法或计划的)暂时搁置

  • A few years ago I was asked by a publisher to consider writing a novel, and the idea has been in cold storage ever since.

    几年前，一位出版商要我考虑写一部小说，但之后这事就搁置下来了。
