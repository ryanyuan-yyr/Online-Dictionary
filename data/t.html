The spelling tee is also used for meaning 3. 义项3亦可拼作 tee。
1 N-VAR 英语字母表的第 20 个字母。 T is the twentieth letter of the English alphabet. 英语字母表的第 20 个字母。
2 (书写时，用于首字母为 t 的一些单词的缩写，如 ton 和 time) T or t is a written abbreviation for words beginning with 't', such as 'ton' and 'time'. (书写时，用于首字母为 t 的一些单词的缩写，如 ton 和 time)
3 PHRASE 恰好;完美地;精确地 You can use to a T or to a tee to mean perfectly or exactly right. For example, if something suits you to a T, it suits you perfectly. If you have got an activity or a skill down to a T, you have succeeded in doing it exactly right. 恰好;完美地;精确地

  • The description fits us to a tee.

    这样的描述再适合我们不过了。

  • Lucy was a stickler for perfection, and everything had to be exactly right, rehearsed down to a T.

    露西什么都追求十全十美，样样都不能出一点问题，要准备得滴水不漏。
