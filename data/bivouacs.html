bivouac
bɪvuæk ˈbɪvˌwæk ˈbɪvuˌæk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bivouacs, present participle bivouacking, past tense , past participle bivouacked
1 N-COUNT （士兵或登山者的）露营，临时宿营 A bivouac is a temporary camp made by soldiers or mountain climbers. （士兵或登山者的）露营，临时宿营
2 VERB 露营；野营；露宿 If you bivouac in a particular place, you stop and stay in a bivouac there. 露营；野营；露宿 [Also VERB]

  • We bivouacked on the outskirts of the city. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我们在市郊露营。
