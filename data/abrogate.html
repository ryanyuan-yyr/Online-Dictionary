abrogate
æbrəgeɪt ˈæbrəˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abrogates, present participle abrogating, past tense , past participle abrogated
VERB 废除，取消（法令、协议、习俗等） If someone in a position of authority abrogates something such as a law, agreement, or practice, they put an end to it. 废除，取消（法令、协议、习俗等） [formal]

  • The next prime minister could abrogate the treaty. [VERB noun]

    下一任首相可能会废除这个条约。

SYN revoke, end, recall, withdraw
