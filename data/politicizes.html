politicize
pəlɪtɪsaɪz pəˈlɪtəˌsaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense politicizes, present participle politicizing, past tense , past participle politicized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 politicise
VERB 使政治化；使具有政治性；使关心（或参与）政治 If you politicize someone or something, you make them more interested in politics or more involved with politics. 使政治化；使具有政治性；使关心（或参与）政治

  • Some feminists had attempted to politicize personal life. [VERB noun]

    一些女权主义者尝试过将个人生活政治化。
