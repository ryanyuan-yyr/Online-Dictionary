double up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (因大笑、剧痛等)（使）弯腰，（使）弓身 If something doubles you up, or if you double up, you bend your body quickly or violently, for example because you are laughing a lot or because you are feeling a lot of pain. (因大笑、剧痛等)（使）弯腰，（使）弓身

  • ...a savage blow in the crutch which doubled him up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    对他下身凶狠的一击疼得他直不起腰

  • They laugh so hard they double up with laughter. [V P + with/in]

    他们捧腹大笑，直不起身来。

Double over means the same as double up .

  • Everyone was doubled over in laughter.

    每个人都笑弯了腰。
