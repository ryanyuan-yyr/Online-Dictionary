soundly
saʊndli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB -ed] 彻底地;完全地 If someone is soundly defeated or beaten, they are severely defeated or beaten. 彻底地;完全地

  • Duke was soundly defeated in this month's Louisiana governor's race.

    杜克在本月的路易斯安那州长竞选中大败而归。

2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB -ed] (决定、观点或陈述等)正确地，明智地，可靠地 If a decision, opinion, or statement is soundly based, there are sensible or reliable reasons behind it. (决定、观点或陈述等)正确地，明智地，可靠地 [approval]

  • Are today's hopes more soundly based than the false ones of 1990?.

    今天的希望是否比 1990 年那些虚幻的希望更靠谱呢？

  • Changes must be soundly based in economic reality.

    改革必须以经济现状作为切实的依据。

SYN sensibly, wisely, prudently, correctly  
3 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb, ADVERB adjective] 酣睡地;熟睡地 If you sleep soundly, you sleep deeply and do not wake during your sleep. 酣睡地;熟睡地

  • How can he sleep soundly at night?.

    他晚上怎么能睡得着觉？

  • She was too soundly asleep to hear Stefano's return.

    她睡得太熟，没有听见斯蒂芬诺回来。

SYN deeply, peacefully, without waking
