close-knit
kloʊs nɪt ˌkləʊsˈnɪt ˈkloʊsˌnɪt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 紧密相连的;密切合作的；志同道合的 A close-knit group of people are closely linked, do things together, and take an interest in each other. 紧密相连的;密切合作的；志同道合的

  • We're a very close-knit family.

    我们家非常团结。

  • Events over the last year have created a close-knit community.

    去年发生的一些事让整个社会紧密团结在一起。
