estimation
estɪmeɪʃən ˌɛstəˈmeɪʃən
Word forms: plural estimations
1 N-SING [usually with poss] 评价;看法;判断 Your estimation of a person or situation is the opinion or impression that you have formed about them. 评价;看法;判断 [formal]

  • He has gone down considerably in my estimation.

    我对他的评价已经一落千丈。

  • ...Lee Dixon, the best player on the pitch in his manager's estimation.

    教练心目中球场上最好的球员李·狄克逊

SYN opinion, view, regard, belief  
2 N-COUNT 估算;估价 An estimation is an approximate calculation of a quantity or value. 估算;估价

  • In fact, the first group were absolutely correct in their estimation of this man's height.

    事实上，第一组人对这名男子身高的估计完全正确。

  • ...estimations of pre-tax profits of £2.25 million. [+of]

    估计税前利润达225万英镑

SYN estimate, reckoning, assessment, appreciation
