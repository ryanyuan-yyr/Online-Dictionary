thrive
θraɪv θraɪv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense thrives, present participle thriving, past tense , past participle thrived
1 VERB 欣欣向荣;茁壮成长;兴旺发达 If someone or something thrives, they do well and are successful, healthy, or strong. 欣欣向荣;茁壮成长;兴旺发达

  • Today his company continues to thrive. [VERB]

    今天，他的公司继续蓬勃发展。

  • Lavender thrives in poor soil. [VERB]

    薰衣草在贫瘠的土壤中茁壮生长。

  • ...the river's thriving population of kingfishers. [VERB-ing]

    那条河上不断繁衍的翠鸟群

SYN prosper, do well, flourish, increase  
2 VERB (尤指在别人觉得困难的时候)以…为乐，可以出色应对 If you say that someone thrives on a particular situation, you mean that they enjoy it or that they can deal with it very well, especially when other people find it unpleasant or difficult. (尤指在别人觉得困难的时候)以…为乐，可以出色应对

  • Many people thrive on a stressful lifestyle. [VERB + on]

    有许多人喜欢充满压力的生活方式。

  • Creative people are usually very determined and thrive on overcoming obstacles. [V on n/-ing]

    富有创造力的人一般都意志非常坚定，能不断克服困难，事业蒸蒸日上。
