get into
1 PHRASAL VERB 从事；致力于 If you get into a particular kind of work or activity, you manage to become involved in it. 从事；致力于

  • He was eager to get into politics. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他渴望进入政界。

2 PHRASAL VERB 获准（入学）；被录取 If you get into a school, college, or university, you are accepted there as a student. 获准（入学）；被录取

  • I was working hard to get into Cambridge. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我为能上剑桥大学而刻苦学习。

3 PHRASAL VERB 使行为异常；使着魔 If you ask what has got into someone, you mean that they are behaving very differently from the way they usually behave. 使行为异常；使着魔 [informal]

  • What has got into you today? Why are you behaving like this? [VERB PREP. noun]

    你今天疯了吗？为什么要这样做呢？
