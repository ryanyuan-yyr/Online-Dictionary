quango
kwæŋgoʊ ˈkwæŋgoʊ
Word forms: plural quangos
N-COUNT (英国的)准自治管理机构，半官方机构 In Britain, a quango is a committee which is appointed by the government but works independently. A quango has responsibility for a particular area of activity, for example the giving of government grants to arts organizations. (英国的)准自治管理机构，半官方机构
