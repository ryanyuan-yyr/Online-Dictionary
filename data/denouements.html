denouement
deɪnuːmɒn deɪˈnuːmɒn denumɑ̃ ˌdeɪnuˈmɑ̃ ˌdeɪnuˈmɑ̃
(also dénouement)Word forms: plural denouements
N-COUNT [usually singular] (书或戏剧的)结局，收场;(一系列事件的)结果 In a book, play, or series of events, the denouement is the sequence of events at the end, when things come to a conclusion. (书或戏剧的)结局，收场;(一系列事件的)结果

  • The book's sentimental denouement is pure Hollywood.

    该书的煽情结局纯粹是好莱坞式的。

  • ...an unexpected denouement.

    出人意料的收场
