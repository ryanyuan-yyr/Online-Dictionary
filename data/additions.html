addition
ədɪʃən əˈdɪʃən
Word forms: plural additions
1 PHRASE 另外;加之;除…之外 You use in addition when you want to mention another item connected with the subject you are discussing. 另外;加之;除…之外

  • Part-time English classes are offered. In addition, students can take classes in word-processing and computing.

    开设了非全日制的英语班。另外，学生可以修读文字处理和计算机课程。

  • There's a postage and packing fee in addition to the repair charge. [+ to]

    除修理费之外还有邮寄和包装费。

2 N-COUNT 增加物；添加物 An addition to something is a thing which is added to it. 增加物；添加物

  • This is a fine book; a worthy addition to the Cambridge Encyclopedia series. [+ to]

    这是一本好书，对剑桥百科全书系列是一个很好的补充。

  • This plywood addition helps to strengthen the structure.

    多层胶合板有助于加固结构。

SYN extra, supplement, complement, adjunct  
3 N-UNCOUNT 增加；添加 The addition of something is the fact that it is added to something else. 增加；添加

  • It was completely refurbished in 1987, with the addition of a picnic site. [+ of]

    它在1987年经过了彻底翻修，加盖了一个野餐点。

SYN inclusion, adding, increasing, extension  
4 N-UNCOUNT 加；算总数 Addition is the process of calculating the total of two or more numbers. 加；算总数

  • ...simple addition and subtraction problems.

    简单的加减题

SYN counting up, totalling, reckoning, summing up
