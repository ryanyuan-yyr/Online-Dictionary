presumably
prɪzjuːməbli -zuːm-
ADV [ADVERB before verb] 大概；很可能；也许；据推测 If you say that something is presumably the case, you mean that you think it is very likely to be the case, although you are not certain. 大概；很可能；也许；据推测 [vagueness]

  • Presumably the front door was locked when you came down this morning?.

    早上你下楼的时候，前门大概是锁上的吧？

  • The spear is presumably the murder weapon.

    这支鱼叉可能就是凶器。

  • He had gone to the reception desk, presumably to check out.

    他已经去前台了，可能是要办理退房手续。

SYN it would seem, probably, likely, apparently
