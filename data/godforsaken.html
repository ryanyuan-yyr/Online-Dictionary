godforsaken
gɒdfəʳseɪkən
(also God-forsaken)
ADJ [ADJ n] （地方）无聊的，乏味的，沉闷的 If you say that somewhere is a godforsaken place, you dislike it a lot because you find it very boring and depressing. （地方）无聊的，乏味的，沉闷的 [disapproval]

  • I don't want to stay here, in this job and in this God-forsaken country.

    我不想在这无聊的乡下干这样的工作，我不想呆在这里了。

SYN desolate, abandoned, deserted, remote
