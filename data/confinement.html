confinement
kənfaɪnmənt kənˈfaɪnmənt
Word forms: plural confinements
1 N-UNCOUNT 监禁;关押;禁闭 Confinement is the state of being forced to stay in a prison or another place which you cannot leave. 监禁;关押;禁闭

  • She had been held in solitary confinement for four months.

    她被单独关押了4个月。

  • He'd obviously kept himself fit despite his years of confinement.

    显然，尽管监禁多年，他依旧保持着身体健康。

SYN imprisonment, custody, detention, incarceration  
2 N-VAR 分娩;生产 A woman's confinement is the period of time just before and during which she gives birth to a child. 分娩;生产 [formal]

  • There has been a movement to support women seeking home confinement.

    一场支持女性在家分娩的运动一直在进行。
