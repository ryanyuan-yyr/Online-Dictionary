watchword
wɒtʃwɜːʳd wɑtʃˌwɜrd wɔtʃˌwɜrd
Word forms: plural watchwords
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 口号;格言 Someone's watchword is a word or phrase that sums up their attitude or approach to a particular subject or to things in general. 口号;格言

  • Caution has been one of Mr Allan's watchwords.

    “小心谨慎”是艾伦先生的座右铭。

  • You don't have to deny yourself everything that's nice when you're pregnant. Moderation is the watchword.

    怀孕时没有必要抗拒一切平时喜欢的东西。关键是要适度。

SYN motto, slogan, maxim, byword
