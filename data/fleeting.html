fleeting
fliːtɪŋ ˈflitɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 短暂的;飞逝的 Fleeting is used to describe something which lasts only for a very short time. 短暂的;飞逝的

  • The girls caught only a fleeting glimpse of the driver.

    女孩们只匆匆瞥了一眼司机。

  • She wondered for a fleeting moment if he would put his arm around her.

    他会不会搂住自己，这个念头在她脑子里一闪而过。

SYN momentary, short, passing, flying  
fleetingly

  • A smile passed fleetingly across his face.

    他脸上闪过一丝笑容。

  • I think I saw her, fleetingly, at a football match.

    我想我在一场足球赛上匆匆见过她一次。
