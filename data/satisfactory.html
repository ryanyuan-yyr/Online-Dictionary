satisfactory
sætɪsfæktəri ˌsætɪsˈfæktəri ˌsætɪsˈfæktri
ADJ-GRADED 令人满意的;恰当的;合适的 Something that is satisfactory is acceptable to you or fulfils a particular need or purpose. 令人满意的;恰当的;合适的

  • I never got a satisfactory answer.

    我从未得到令我满意的答复。

  • It seemed a very satisfactory arrangement.

    这似乎是个非常令人满意的安排。

  • Neither solution seemed satisfactory.

    两个解决方案似乎都不合适。

SYN adequate, acceptable, good enough, average  
satisfactorily

  • Their motives have never been satisfactorily explained.

    他们的动机一直未得到令人满意的解释。
