mace
meɪs meɪs
Word forms: plural maces
1 N-COUNT 权杖;权标 A mace is an ornamental stick carried by an official or placed somewhere as a symbol of authority. 权杖;权标
2 N-UNCOUNT 肉豆蔻干皮(用作调味品) Mace is a spice, usually in the form of a powder, made from the shell of nutmegs. 肉豆蔻干皮(用作调味品)
3 N-UNCOUNT 梅斯催泪型毒剂 Mace is a substance that causes tears and sickness, and that is used in sprays as a defence against rioters or attackers. 梅斯催泪型毒剂 [trademark]
