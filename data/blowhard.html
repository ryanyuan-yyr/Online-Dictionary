blowhard
bloʊhɑrd ˈbləʊˌhɑːd ˈbloʊˌhɑrd
Word forms: blowhards
N-COUNT 吹牛者If you describe someone as a blowhard, you mean that they express their opinions very forcefully, and usually in a boastful way. 吹牛者 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • He doesn't like to be a blowhard about what he's developed.

    他不喜欢吹嘘他研制的东西。
