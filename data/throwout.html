2 PHRASAL VERB (法官)不受理，不立案 If a judge throws out a case, he or she rejects it and the accused person does not have to stand trial. (法官)不受理，不立案 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The defense wants the district Judge to throw out the case. [VERB PREP. noun]

    辩护律师希望区法官不受理那个案子。

3 PHRASAL VERB 撵走;赶走;开除;将…除名 If you throw someone out, you force them to leave a place or group. 撵走;赶走;开除;将…除名 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He was thrown out of the Olympic team after testing positive for drugs. [be/get V-ed P + of]

    他在药检呈阳性后被奥林匹克运动队除名。

  • I wanted to kill him, but instead I just threw him out of the house. [VERB noun PREP. + of]

    我本想杀了他，不过我只是把他撵出了门。

  • The party threw out the Trotskyist Militant Tendency. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该党开除了托洛茨基主义的激进派。

SYN expel, eject, evict, dismiss
