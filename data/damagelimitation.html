damage limitation
ˈdæmɪdʒ ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 损害控制;损失控制 Damage limitation is action that is taken to make the bad results of something as small as possible, when it is impossible to avoid bad results completely. 损害控制;损失控制 [British]

  • The meeting was merely an exercise in damage limitation.

    这次会议只是控制损失的一项举措。

  • Damage limitation measures have been introduced.

    已经推出了损害控制措施。
