officialdom

N-UNCOUNT （尤指奉行不合理规章制度的）官僚，官员，职员 Officialdom is used to refer to officials who work for the government or in other organizations, especially when you think that their rules are unhelpful. （尤指奉行不合理规章制度的）官僚，官员，职员 [disapproval]
