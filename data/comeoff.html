come off
1 PHRASAL VERB 成功;达到效果 If something comes off, it is successful or effective. 成功;达到效果

  • It was a good try but it didn't quite come off. [VERB PREP.]

    这是一次不错的尝试，但效果欠佳。

  • Slovo said it was a great occasion which he hoped would come off in an orderly and peaceful way. [VERB PREP.]

    斯洛沃说这是一次重大的活动，他希望活动能够井然有序，不出乱子。

SYN succeed, work out, be successful, pan out [informal]  
2 PHRASAL VERB (在比赛或冲突中)表现得 If someone comes off worst in a contest or conflict, they are in the worst position after it. If they come off best, they are in the best position. (在比赛或冲突中)表现得

  • Some Democrats still have bitter memories of how, against all odds, they came off worst during the inquiry. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    一些民主党人士至今仍然记得当时困难重重、在调查中惨败的痛苦经历。

  • In these circumstances, it is the managers who come off best. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    在这种情况下，几个经理表现得最好。

3 PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 戒除(毒品);停止(服药) If you come off a drug or medicine, you stop taking it. 戒除(毒品);停止(服药)

  • ...people trying to come off tranquillizers. [VERB PREP. noun]

    尝试停用止痛药的人

4 CONVENTION 别撒谎了！别瞎说！ You say 'come off it' to someone to show them that you think what they are saying is untrue or wrong. 别撒谎了！别瞎说！ [informal, spoken]
