reinstatement
riːɪnsteɪtmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 复职;复位 Reinstatement is the act of giving someone back a job or position which has been taken away from them. 复职;复位

  • Parents campaigned in vain for her reinstatement.

    父母们呼吁给她复职，但结果却只是徒劳。

SYN restoration, bringing back, re-establishment, reinstitution  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] (法律、做法等的)恢复;（设施等的）修复 The reinstatement of a law, facility, or practice is the act of causing it to exist again. (法律、做法等的)恢复;（设施等的）修复

  • He welcomed the reinstatement of the 10 per cent bank base rate. [+ of]

    他对恢复10%的银行基准利率表示欢迎。

SYN restoration
