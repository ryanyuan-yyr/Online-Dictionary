missionary
mɪʃənri -neri ˈmɪʃənərɪ ˈmɪʃənˌɛri
Word forms: plural missionaries
1 N-COUNT (派往国外的)传教士 A missionary is a Christian who has been sent to a foreign country to teach people about Christianity. (派往国外的)传教士
SYN evangelist, preacher, apostle, converter  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 传教的;布道的 Missionary is used to describe the activities of missionaries. 传教的;布道的

  • You should be in missionary work.

    你应该从事传教工作。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (热情)传教士般的 If you refer to someone's enthusiasm for an activity or belief as missionary zeal, you are emphasizing that they are very enthusiastic about it. (热情)传教士般的 [emphasis]

  • She had a kind of missionary zeal about bringing culture to the masses.

    她对于向大众传播文化有着满腔的热忱。
