dumbbell
dʌmbɛl ˈdʌmˌbɛl ˈdʌmˌbɛl
(also dumb-bell)Word forms: dumbbells
1 N-COUNT 哑铃A dumbbell is a short bar with weights on either side which people use for physical exercise to strengthen their arm and shoulder muscles. 哑铃
2 N-COUNT 笨蛋If you call a person a dumbbell, you mean that they are stupid or foolish. 笨蛋 [informal, disapproval]
