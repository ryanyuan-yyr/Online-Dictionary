microwave
maɪkroʊweɪv ˈmaɪkroʊˌweɪv
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense microwaves, present participle microwaving, past tense , past participle microwaved
1 N-COUNT 微波炉 A microwave or a microwave oven is an oven which cooks food very quickly by electromagnetic radiation rather than by heat. 微波炉
2 VERB 用微波炉烹调(或加热) To microwave food or drink means to cook or heat it in a microwave oven. 用微波炉烹调(或加热)

  • Steam or microwave the vegetables until tender. [VERB noun]

    蒸或者用微波炉加热蔬菜，直至蔬菜变软。

  • ...microwaved food. [VERB-ed]

    用微波炉加热过的食物
