ram
ræm ræm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense rams, present participle ramming, past tense , past participle rammed
1 VERB 和…相撞;猛撞;撞击 If a vehicle rams something such as another vehicle, it crashes into it with a lot of force, usually deliberately. 和…相撞;猛撞;撞击

  • The thieves fled, ramming the policeman's car. [VERB noun]

    小偷撞了警车后逃走了。

  • They used a lorry to ram the main gate. [VERB noun]

    他们用卡车猛撞大门。

SYN hit, force, drive into, strike  
2 VERB 猛推 If you ram something somewhere, you push it there with great force. 猛推

  • He rammed the key into the lock and kicked the front door open. [VERB noun adv./prep.]

    他把钥匙猛力插进锁里，踢开了前门。

SYN cram, pound, force, stuff  
3 N-COUNT 成年公羊 A ram is an adult male sheep. 成年公羊
4 See also: battering ram 
5 PHRASE 充分说明;使透彻理解 If something rams home a message or a point, it makes it clear in a way that is very forceful and that people are likely to listen to. to ram something down someone's throat→see: throat  充分说明;使透彻理解

  • Railway lines are dangerous places and it is up to parents to ram home the dangers to their children.

    铁路是危险的地方，父母有责任让孩子们清楚地认识到这一点。

  • The report by Marks & Spencer's chairman will ram this point home.

    玛莎百货公司的主席作的报告会透彻地讲明这一点。

N-UNCOUNT 随机存储器;内存 RAM is the part of a computer in which information is stored while you are using it. RAM is an abbreviation for 'Random Access Memory'. 随机存储器;内存 [computing]

  • ...a PC with 256k RAM minimum.

    带有最小256K内存的个人计算机

  • 512k RAM is recommended and 640k RAM is preferred.

    推荐配置为512K内存，640K内存则更佳。
