thaw out
1 →see: thaw 3 
2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)(人)暖和起来 If someone who is very cold thaws out, or if another person or thing thaws them out, they begin to feel warmer. (使)(人)暖和起来

  • Amy's feet were beginning to thaw out. [VERB PREP.]

    埃米的双脚慢慢暖和了起来。

  • Peter thawed us all out with coffee. [VERB noun PREP.]

    彼得给我们冲了咖啡喝，让我们都暖和了过来。

  • Bob and Louise had prepared a sumptuous meal to thaw out our bodies. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    鲍勃和路易丝为我们准备了一顿丰盛的饭菜给我们暖暖身子。
