keep on at
PHRASAL VERB 对…纠缠不休；对…说个没完 If you keep on at someone, you repeatedly ask or tell them something in a way that annoys them. 对…纠缠不休；对…说个没完 [British, informal]

  • You've constantly got to keep on at people about that. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    你总是得没完没了地和别人说那件事。

  • She kept on at him to get some qualifications. [V P P n to-inf]

    她不停跟他说，让他去考取一些资格证书。

SYN nag, badger, pester, hassle [informal]
