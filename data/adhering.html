adhere
ædhɪəʳ ædˈhɪr ədˈhɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adheres, present participle adhering, past tense , past participle adhered
1 VERB 遵守，遵循（规定或协议） If you adhere to a rule or agreement, you act in the way that it says you should. 遵守，遵循（规定或协议）

  • All members of the association adhere to a strict code of practice. [VERB + to]

    协会的所有成员都遵守一套严格的行为规范。

  • She adhered to the strict Islamic dress code. [VERB to noun]

    她恪守伊斯兰教严格的着装规定。

2 VERB 支持，拥护，持有（观点或信仰） If you adhere to an opinion or belief, you support or hold it. 支持，拥护，持有（观点或信仰）

  • He urged them to adhere to the values of Islam which defend the dignity of man. [VERB + to]

    他鼓励他们坚守伊斯兰教维护人类尊严的价值观。

3 VERB 黏附；附着 If something adheres to something else, it sticks firmly to it. 黏附；附着 [Also VERB]

  • Small particles adhere to the seed. [VERB + to]

    小颗粒牢牢附着在种子上。

  • This sticky compound adheres well on this surface. [VERB adv./prep.]

    这种黏性的化合物能牢牢地粘在这个表面上。
