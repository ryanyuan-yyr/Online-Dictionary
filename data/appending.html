append
əpend əˈpɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense appends, present participle appending, past tense appended
1 VERB （尤指在文章的末尾）附加，增补 When you append something to something else, especially a piece of writing, you attach it or add it to the end of it. （尤指在文章的末尾）附加，增补 [formal]

  • Violet appended a note at the end of the letter. [VERB noun]

    维奥莱特在信的末尾附加了一条备注。

  • It was a relief that his real name hadn't been appended to the manuscript. [be VERB-ed + to]

    令人庆幸的是，手稿未落上他的真实姓名。

SYN add, attach, join, hang
