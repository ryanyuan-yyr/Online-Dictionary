bereavement
bɪriːvmənt
Word forms: plural bereavements
N-VAR 丧亲之痛；新丧 Bereavement is the sorrow you feel or the state you are in when a relative or close friend dies. 丧亲之痛；新丧

  • When Mary died Anne did not share her brother's sense of bereavement.

    玛丽去世后，安妮并没有像她弟弟那样哀痛。

  • ...those who have suffered a bereavement.

    那些遭受丧亲之痛的人

SYN loss, death, misfortune, deprivation
