oversubscribed
oʊvəʳsəbskraɪbd ˌəʊvəsəbˈskraɪbd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （活动、服务等）过量预订的，火爆的 If something such as an event or a service is oversubscribed, too many people apply to attend the event or use the service. （活动、服务等）过量预订的，火爆的

  • The popular schools — the sort you really might drive across town for — tend to be heavily oversubscribed.

    那些受欢迎的学校—那种真的会想要驾车穿越城区去上的学校—常常让人挤破了头。
