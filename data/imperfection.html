imperfection
ɪmpəʳfekʃən ˌɪmpərˈfɛkʃən
Word forms: plural imperfections
1 N-VAR 缺点;弱点;缺陷 An imperfection in someone or something is a fault, weakness, or undesirable feature that they have. 缺点;弱点;缺陷

  • By his own statement, there have been imperfections in his judgment.

    据他自己声称,他的判断存在不足之处。

  • I was obsessed by my physical imperfections.

    我的身体缺陷令我很苦恼。

SYN fault, failing, weakness, defect  
2 N-COUNT 污点;瑕疵 An imperfection in something is a small mark or damaged area which may spoil its appearance. 污点;瑕疵

  • ...imperfections in the cloth. [+ in]

    衣料上的瑕疵

SYN blemish, fault, defect, flaw
