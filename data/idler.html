idler
aɪdləʳ ˈaɪdlər ˈaɪdələr
Word forms: plural idlers
N-COUNT 游手好闲者;懒汉 If you describe someone as an idler, you are criticizing them because you think they are lazy and should be working. 游手好闲者;懒汉 [disapproval]

  • The Duke resents being seen as a moneyed idler.

    公爵对别人把他看作钱包鼓鼓、游手好闲的人感到气愤。

SYN loafer, lounger, piker [Australian , New Zealand , slang] , drone
