unmercifully
ʌnmɜːʳsɪfʊli
ADV [usually ADVERB with verb, oft ADVERB adjective] 不仁慈地；残酷地 If you do something unmercifully, you do it a lot, showing no pity. 不仁慈地；残酷地

  • Uncle Sebastian used to tease Mother and Daddy unmercifully that all they could produce was girls.

    塞巴斯蒂安叔叔过去常无情地嘲讽爸爸和妈妈，说他们只会生女儿。
