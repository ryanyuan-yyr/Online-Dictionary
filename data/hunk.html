hunk
hʌŋk hʌŋk
Word forms: plural hunks
1 N-COUNT 大块;大片 A hunk of something is a large piece of it. 大块;大片

  • ...a thick hunk of bread. [+ of]

    厚厚一大块面包

  • ...hunks of wood.

    大块木头

SYN lump, piece, chunk, block  
2 N-COUNT 高大健壮的男子;魁梧性感的男子;猛男 If you refer to a man as a hunk, you mean that he is big, strong, and sexually attractive. 高大健壮的男子;魁梧性感的男子;猛男 [informal, approval]

  • ...a blond, blue-eyed hunk.

    金发碧眼、魁梧性感的男子

SYN stud [informal] , he-man [informal] , macho man, tough guy [informal]
