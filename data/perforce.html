perforce
pəʳfɔːʳs pərˈfɔrs
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 必然；必定；势必 Perforce is used to indicate that something happens or is the case because it cannot be prevented or avoided. 必然；必定；势必 [old-fashioned]

  • The war in 1939 perforce ushered in an era of more grime and drabness.

    1939年的战争必然导致一个更加肮脏、更加灰暗的时代的到来。

SYN necessarily, inevitably, of necessity, unavoidably
