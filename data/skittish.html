skittish
skɪtɪʃ ˈskɪtɪʃ
1 ADJ-GRADED (人或动物)易激动的，易受惊的 If you describe a person or animal as skittish, you mean they are easily made frightened or excited. (人或动物)易激动的，易受惊的

  • The declining dollar gave heart to skittish investors.

    美元的衰落给总是担惊受怕的投资者们带来了信心。

SYN nervous, lively, excitable, jumpy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 没长性的;轻佻的;善变的;靠不住的 Someone who is skittish does not concentrate on anything or take life very seriously. 没长性的;轻佻的;善变的;靠不住的

  • ...his relentlessly skittish sense of humour.

    他那永远变化不定的幽默感
