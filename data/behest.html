behest
bɪhest biˈhɛst bɪˈhɛst
Word forms: plural behests
PHRASE 按…的吩咐；应…的要求；遵照…的命令 If something is done at someone's behest, it is done because they have ordered or requested it. 按…的吩咐；应…的要求；遵照…的命令 [formal]

  • In 1970, at his new wife's behest, they moved to Southampton.

    1970年，应他新任妻子的要求，他们搬到了南安普敦。

  • The policy document was produced at the behest of John Major and other world leaders.

    这份政策文件是在约翰·梅杰和其他国家领导人的授意下拟定的。
