encamped
ɪnkæmpt ɪnˈkæmpt
ADJ 露营的；(尤指士兵)扎营的 If people, especially soldiers, are encamped somewhere, they have set up camp there. 露营的；(尤指士兵)扎营的

  • Railways could now bring food to encamped armies.

    现在通过铁路能够给扎营部队运送食物了。

  • He made his way back to the farmyard where his regiment was encamped.

    他设法回到了自己的团驻扎的农家场院。
