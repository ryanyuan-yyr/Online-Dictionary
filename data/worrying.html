worrying
wʌriɪŋ wɜːriɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人担心的;使人发愁的 If something is worrying, it causes people to worry. →see usage note at: worried 令人担心的;使人发愁的

  • It's worrying that they're doing things without training.

    他们没经过培训就做这些事情，这让人很担心。

  • ...a new and worrying report about smoking.

    一份令人忧心的有关吸烟的最新报告

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 worrisome
worryingly

  • The rate of assaults was worryingly high.

    袭击案频发令人担忧。

  • Worryingly for those in favour of competition, the Minister has been resistant to this argument.

    令那些赞成竞争的人担心的是，部长始终拒绝接受这一论点。
