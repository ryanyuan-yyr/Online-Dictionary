tinny
tɪni ˈtɪni
1 ADJ-GRADED 尖细的;刺耳的 If you describe a sound as tinny, you mean that it has an irritating, high-pitched quality. 尖细的;刺耳的

  • He could hear the tinny sound of a radio playing a pop song.

    他能听到收音机播放流行歌曲的刺耳声音。

  • …a small tinny voice.

    细小尖利的嗓音

SYN jangling, thin, metallic, jingling  
2 ADJ-GRADED 质量差的;劣质金属制成的 If you use tinny to describe something such as a cheap car, you mean that it is made of thin metal and is of poor quality. 质量差的;劣质金属制成的

  • It is one of the cheapest cars on the market, with tinny bodywork.

    那是市场上最廉价的汽车之一，车身用劣质金属制成。
