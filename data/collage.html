collage
kɒlɑːʒ kəˈlɑːʒ kɔlaʒ kəlɑːʒ kəˈlɑʒ
Word forms: plural collages
1 N-COUNT (用彩色纸片、布块粘贴而成的)拼贴画 A collage is a picture that has been made by sticking pieces of coloured paper and cloth onto paper. (用彩色纸片、布块粘贴而成的)拼贴画
2 N-UNCOUNT 拼贴艺术 Collage is the method of making pictures by sticking pieces of coloured paper and cloth onto paper. 拼贴艺术
3 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] (风格、特性迥异的事物构成的)大杂烩 You can refer to something that has been made by combining a number of very different things as a collage of a particular kind. (风格、特性迥异的事物构成的)大杂烩

  • Rego's work is a rich collage of 20th-century painting styles.

    瑞格的作品糅合了20世纪各种绘画风格。
