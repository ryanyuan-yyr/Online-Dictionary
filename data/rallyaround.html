rally around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 rally round
PHRASAL VERB 团结支持；集体声援 When people rally around or rally round, they work as a group in order to support someone or something at a difficult time. 团结支持；集体声援

  • So many people have rallied round to help the family. [VERB PREP.]

    很多人都团结起来帮助那家人。

  • Connie's friends rallied round her. [VERB PREP. noun]

    康妮的朋友都一起支持她。
