discernible
dɪsɜːʳnəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 看得清的;辨别得出的 If something is discernible, you can see it or recognize that it exists. 看得清的;辨别得出的 [formal]

  • Far away the outline of the island is just discernible.

    远处，岛屿轮廓仅依稀可辨。

  • There has been no discernible overall trend since 1975.

    自 1975 年以来一直没有一个明朗的大趋势。

SYN clear, obvious, apparent, plain
