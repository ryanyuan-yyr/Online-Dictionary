replacement
rɪpleɪsmənt rɪˈpleɪsmənt
Word forms: plural replacements
1 N-UNCOUNT 替代;替换;取代 If you refer to the replacement of one thing by another, you mean that the second thing takes the place of the first. 替代;替换;取代

  • ...the replacement of damaged or lost books. [+ of]

    受损或丢失图书的更换或补充

SYN replacing, renewal, substitution  
2See also:hormone replacement therapy 
3 N-COUNT 替补(成员或队员)；接替者 Someone who takes someone else's place in an organization, government, or team can be referred to as their replacement . 替补(成员或队员)；接替者

  • Taylor has nominated Adams as his replacement.

    泰勒提名亚当斯接替他。

  • The 18-year-old made his debut last week as a replacement for the injured Gordon Durie.

    这个18岁选手作为受伤的戈登·迪里的替补在上周首次出场。
