splatter
splætəʳ ˈsplætər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense splatters, present participle splattering, past tense , past participle splattered
V-ERG 溅泼；洒落 If a thick wet substance splatters on something or is splattered on it, it drops or is thrown over it. 溅泼；洒落

  • The rain splattered against the french windows. [VERB adv./prep.]

    雨溅落在落地窗上。

  • 'Sorry Edward,' I said, splattering the cloth with jam. [VERB noun]

    “对不起，爱德华，”我说道，因为我把果酱洒在了桌布上。

  • ...a mud-splattered white shirt. [VERB-ed]

    溅上了泥浆的白衬衫

SYN stain, spatter, mark, smear
