fish out
PHRASAL VERB 找出或取出(某物)If you fish something out from somewhere, you take or pull it out, often after searching for it for some time. 找出或取出(某物) [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Kelly fished out another beer from his cooler. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    凯利从他的冰箱里又翻出一瓶啤酒。

  • She fished out a pair of David's socks for her cold feet. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她翻出一双大卫的袜子穿在冰冻的脚上。

SYN pull out, produce, take out, extract
