peripatetic
perɪpətetɪk ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪkəl ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不固定的；经常出差的；流动的 If someone has a peripatetic life or career, they travel around a lot, living or working in places for short periods of time. 不固定的；经常出差的；流动的 [formal]

  • Her father was in the army and the family led a peripatetic existence.

    她父亲是军人，所以全家人随军过着一种流动的生活。

  • ...a hectic, peripatetic life full of people and parties.

    整天疲于周旋、忙于应酬的紧张生活

SYN travelling, wandering, roaming, migrant
