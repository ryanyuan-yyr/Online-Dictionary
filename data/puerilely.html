puerile
pjʊəraɪl -rəl ˈpjuərəl ˈpjʊrəl ˈpjuərˌaɪl
ADJ-GRADED 幼稚的；孩子气的；愚蠢的 If you describe someone or something as puerile, you mean that they are silly and childish. 幼稚的；孩子气的；愚蠢的 [disapproval]

  • Concert organisers branded the group's actions as puerile.

    音乐会的组织者指称该乐队的行为愚蠢幼稚。

  • The story is simple, even puerile.

    故事很简单，甚至有些幼稚。

  • ...puerile, schoolboy humour.

    既幼稚又学生气的幽默

SYN childish, juvenile, naive, weak
