consternation
kɒnstəʳneɪʃən ˌkɑnstərˈneɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 不安;忧虑;害怕 Consternation is a feeling of anxiety or fear. 不安;忧虑;害怕 [formal]

  • His decision caused consternation in the art photography community.

    他的决定在艺术摄影圈内引起了恐慌。

  • Sam stared at him in consternation.

    萨姆惊恐不安地注视着他。

SYN dismay, shock, alarm, horror
