manual
mænjuəl ˈmænjuəl
Word forms: plural manuals
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 人力的;体力的；手工的 Manual work is work in which you use your hands or your physical strength rather than your mind. 人力的;体力的；手工的

  • ...skilled manual workers.

    熟练的体力劳动者

  • They have no reservations about taking factory or manual jobs.

    他们对在工厂做工人或者干体力活都没有意见。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 用手的;手工的 Manual is used to talk about movements which are made by someone's hands. 用手的;手工的 [formal]

  • ...toys designed to help develop manual dexterity.

    用来培养手的灵活性的玩具

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 手动的;用手操作的 Manual means operated by hand, rather than by electricity or a motor. 手动的;用手操作的

  • There is a manual pump to get rid of the water.

    有一只手摇水泵用来排水。

SYN hand-operated, hand, non-automatic  
manually

  • The device is manually operated, using a simple handle.

    一只普通的手柄就可以手动操作这个装置。

4 N-COUNT 使用手册;说明书 A manual is a book which tells you how to do something or how a piece of machinery works. 使用手册;说明书

  • ...the instruction manual.

    使用说明书

SYN handbook, guide, instructions, bible
