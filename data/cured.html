cure
kjʊəʳ kjʊr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cures, present participle curing, past tense , past participle cured
1 VERB 治愈，治好(疾病或创伤) If doctors or medical treatments cure an illness or injury, they cause it to end or disappear. 治愈，治好(疾病或创伤)

  • An operation finally cured his shin injury. [VERB noun]

    手术最终治好了他胫部的伤。

  • Her cancer can only be controlled, not cured. [be VERB-ed]

    她的癌症只能控制，无法治愈。

SYN make better, correct, heal, relieve  
2 VERB 治好，治愈(病人);使恢复健康 If doctors or medical treatments cure a person, they make the person well again after an illness or injury. 治好，治愈(病人);使恢复健康

  • MDT is an effective treatment and could cure all the leprosy sufferers worldwide. [VERB noun]

    联合化疗是一种有望治愈全世界所有麻风病患者的有效治疗方法。

  • Almost overnight I was cured. [be VERB-ed]

    几乎是一夜间我就被治好了。

  • Now doctors believe they have cured him of the disease. [VERB noun + of]

    现在医生们确信已经治好了他的病。

SYN restore to health, restore, heal  
3 N-COUNT 药;药物;疗法 A cure for an illness is a medicine or other treatment that cures the illness. 药;药物;疗法

  • There is still no cure for a cold.

    尚没有治疗感冒的特效药。

  • Atkinson has been told rest is the only cure for his ankle injury. [+ for]

    阿特金森被告知休息是唯一能治好他脚踝伤的办法。

SYN remedy, treatment, medicine, healing  
4 VERB 消除，解决(问题) If someone or something cures a problem, they bring it to an end. 消除，解决(问题)

  • Private firms are willing to make large scale investments to help cure Russia's economic troubles. [VERB noun]

    私有企业愿意投巨资帮助俄罗斯渡过经济难关。

  • We need to cure our environmental problems. [VERB noun]

    我们需要解决环境问题。

SYN rectify, improve, fix, remedy  
5 N-COUNT 解决方法;措施;对策 A cure for a problem is something that will bring it to an end. 解决方法;措施;对策

  • Punishment can never be an effective cure for acute social problems.

    惩罚绝不是解决严重社会问题的有效办法。

  • The magic cure for inflation does not exist. [+ for]

    抑制通货膨胀没有什么灵丹妙药。

6 VERB 矫正，使放弃(习惯或态度) If an action or event cures someone of a habit or an attitude, it makes them stop having it. 矫正，使放弃(习惯或态度)

  • The experience was a detestable ordeal, and it cured him of any ambitions to direct again. [VERB noun + of]

    那次经历是一次令人厌恶的煎熬，使得他放弃了任何再当导演的想法。

  • He went to a clinic to cure his drinking and overeating. [VERB noun]

    他去诊所戒酒并控制饮食。

7 VERB [usually passive] (用干燥、熏、腌等方法)加工贮藏 When food, tobacco, or animal skin is cured, it is dried, smoked, or salted so that it will last for a long time. (用干燥、熏、腌等方法)加工贮藏

  • Legs of pork were cured and smoked over the fire. [be VERB-ed]

    在火上熏制猪腿。

  • ...sliced cured ham. [VERB-ed]

    切成片的熏火腿

SYN preserve, smoke, dry, salt
