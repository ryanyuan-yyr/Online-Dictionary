ratchet down
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）有一定减少;（使）有一定降低 If something ratchets down or is ratcheted down, it decreases by a fixed amount or degree, and seems unlikely to increase again. （使）有一定减少;（使）有一定降低 [mainly journalism] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We're trying to ratchet down the administrative costs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们正在想法控制管理成本。
