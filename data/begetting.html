beget
bɪget biˈgɛt bɪˈgɛt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense begets, present participle begetting, past tense begot, past participle begotten
1 VERB 招致；导致；引起 To beget something means to cause it to happen or be created. 招致；导致；引起 [formal]

  • Poverty begets debt. [VERB noun]

    穷则债聚。

  • Economic tensions beget political ones. [VERB noun]

    经济紧张导致政治紧张。

SYN cause, bring, produce, create  
2 VERB 成为…的父亲 When a man begets a child, he becomes the father of that child. 成为…的父亲 [old-fashioned]

  • He wanted to beget an heir. [VERB noun]

    他想生个子嗣。

SYN father, breed, generate, sire
