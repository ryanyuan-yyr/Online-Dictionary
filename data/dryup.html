dry up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)枯萎;(使)干透 If something dries up or if something dries it up, it loses all its moisture and becomes completely dry and shrivelled or hard. (使)枯萎;(使)干透 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • As the day goes on, the pollen dries up and becomes hard. [VERB PREP.]

    日子一天天过去,花粉干结成了硬块。

  • Warm breezes from the South dried up the streets. [VERB PREP. noun]

    来自南方的温暖和风吹干了街道。
