going-over ˈgoʊɪŋˈoʊvər
1 N-SING 仔细的检查；彻底审查 If you give someone or something a going-over, you examine them thoroughly. 仔细的检查；彻底审查 [informal]

  • Michael was given a complete going-over and then treated for glandular fever.

    迈克尔接受了全面体检，随后又进行了腺热治疗。

SYN examination, study, check, review  
2 N-SING 痛打；痛骂；猛烈抨击 A going-over is a violent attack on or criticism of someone. 痛打；痛骂；猛烈抨击 [British, informal]

  • He gets a terrible going-over in these pages.

    他在这几页中遭到了一顿痛批。
