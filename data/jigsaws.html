jigsaw
dʒɪgsɔː ˈdʒɪgˌsɔ
Word forms: plural jigsaws
1 N-COUNT 拼图；拼图玩具 A jigsaw or jigsaw puzzle is a picture on cardboard or wood that has been cut up into odd shapes. You have to make the picture again by putting the pieces together correctly. 拼图；拼图玩具
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 错综复杂的情况；谜团 You can describe a complicated situation as a jigsaw . 错综复杂的情况；谜团

  • ...the jigsaw of high-level diplomacy.

    高层外交的错综复杂
