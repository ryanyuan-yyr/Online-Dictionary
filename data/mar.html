mar
mɑːʳ mɑr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mars, present participle marring, past tense , past participle marred
VERB 破坏;损坏;毁坏 To mar something means to spoil or damage it. 破坏;损坏;毁坏

  • A number of problems marred the smooth running of this event. [VERB noun]

    许多问题影响了这件事的顺利进行。

  • That election was marred by massive cheating. [VERB noun]

    大范围的舞弊破坏了那次选举。

SYN harm, damage, hurt, spoil
