flight
flaɪt flaɪt
Word forms: plural flights
1 N-COUNT 飞行;航行 A flight is a journey made by flying, usually in an aeroplane. 飞行;航行

  • The flight will take four hours.

    这次航行要飞 4 个小时。

SYN journey, trip, voyage  
2 N-COUNT 航班;客机 You can refer to an aeroplane carrying passengers on a particular journey as a particular flight . 航班;客机

  • I'll try to get on the flight down to Karachi tonight.

    我尽量赶上今晚去卡拉奇的航班。

  • BA flight 286 was two hours late.

    英国航空公司 286 次航班晚点两个小时。

3 N-UNCOUNT 飞行;飞行能力 Flight is the action of flying, or the ability to fly. 飞行;飞行能力

  • These hawks are magnificent in flight, soaring and circling for long periods.

    这些鹰飞行能力极其出色，可以长时间在空中翱翔和盘旋。

  • Supersonic flight could become a routine form of travel in the 21st century.

    搭乘超音速飞机会成为 21 世纪常规的旅行方式。

SYN aviation, flying, air transport, aeronautics  
4 N-COUNT 鸟群 A flight of birds is a group of them flying together. 鸟群

  • A flight of green parrots shot out of the cedar forest. [+ of]

    一群绿鹦鹉从杉木林里疾速飞出。

5 N-UNCOUNT [oft in NOUN] 逃避;躲开 Flight is the act of running away from a dangerous or unpleasant situation or place. 逃避;躲开

  • Frank was in full flight when he reached them.

    弗兰克正在拼命奔逃，这时他联系到了他们。

  • The family was often in flight, hiding out in friends' houses.

    这一家人常常东躲西藏，在朋友家里避风头。

  • ...her hurried flight from the palace in a cart.

    她坐着马车匆匆逃离王宫

6 N-COUNT 一段(台阶或楼梯) A flight of steps or stairs is a set of steps or stairs that lead from one level to another without changing direction. 一段(台阶或楼梯)

  • We walked in silence up a flight of stairs and down a long corridor. [+ of]

    我们默默地走上一段楼梯，穿过一个长长的走廊。

7 PHRASE [flight inflects] 想入非非;异想天开 An idea or statement that is very imaginative but complicated, silly, or impractical can be referred to as a flight of fancy . 想入非非;异想天开

  • Cockburn engaged in a flight of fancy, never once allowing facts to get in the way.

    科伯恩一直在想入非非，从不曾考虑现实状况。

8 PHRASE 逃走;逃离;逃避 If someone takes flight, they run away from an unpleasant situation or place. 逃走;逃离;逃避

  • He was told of the raid and decided to take flight immediately.

    他得知要突击搜查，决定立刻逃走。
