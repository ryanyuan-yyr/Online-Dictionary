stipend
staɪpend ˈstaɪpənd staɪˌpɛnd
Word forms: plural stipends
1 N-COUNT (尤指地方法官或神职人员的)薪俸，生活津贴 A stipend is a sum of money that is paid regularly, especially to a magistrate or a member of the clergy, as a salary or for their living expenses. (尤指地方法官或神职人员的)薪俸，生活津贴 [mainly British]
2 N-COUNT 助学金;奖学金 A stipend is a sum of money that is paid to a student for their living expenses. 助学金;奖学金 [mainly US]
