startling
stɑːʳtəlɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人吃惊的;惊人的;不寻常的 Something that is startling is so different, unexpected, or remarkable that people react to it with surprise. 令人吃惊的;惊人的;不寻常的

  • Sometimes the results may be rather startling.

    有时结果可能会令人吃惊。

  • ...startling new evidence.

    惊人的新证据

  • His hair was dyed a startling black.

    他把头发染成了令人吃惊的黑色。

SYN surprising, shocking, alarming, extraordinary  
startlingly

  • He was startlingly handsome.

    他帅气逼人。

  • Then, startlingly, the back door was jerked open by Binton.

    然后，令人吃惊的是，后门被宾顿猛地拉开了。
