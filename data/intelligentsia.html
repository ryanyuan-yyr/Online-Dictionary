intelligentsia
ɪntelɪdʒentsiə ɪnˈtɛləˈdʒɛntsiə ɪnˈtɛləˈgɛntsiə
N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 知识分子;知识阶层;知识界 The intelligentsia in a country or community are the most educated people there, especially those interested in the arts, philosophy, and politics. 知识分子;知识阶层;知识界

  • I was not high up enough in the intelligentsia to be invited to such exalted meetings.

    我在知识界的资历还没有高到能受邀参加这种盛会。

SYN intellectuals, highbrows, literati, masterminds
