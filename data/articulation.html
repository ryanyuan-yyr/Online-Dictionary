articulation
ɑːʳtɪkjʊleɪʃən ɑrˌtɪkjuˈleɪʃən ɑrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 清楚的咬字（或发音） Articulation is the action of producing a sound or word clearly, in speech or music. 清楚的咬字（或发音） [formal]

  • ...a singer able to sustain a full tone and clear articulation over extremely long periods.

    可以很长时间保持声音洪亮并且吐字清楚的歌手

SYN expression, delivery, pronunciation, speech  
2 N-UNCOUNT （思想或感情的）表达 The articulation of an idea or feeling is the expression of it, especially in words. （思想或感情的）表达 [formal]

  • This was seen as a way of restricting women's articulation of grievances. [+ of]

    这被认为是一种限制妇女发牢骚的方式。

SYN voicing, statement, expression, verbalization  
3 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN of n] （不同部分、元素之间的）连接，联系，相互关系 The articulation of a structure or system is the way in which its different parts or elements are connected. （不同部分、元素之间的）连接，联系，相互关系 [formal]

  • Capitalist social formations reflect the interaction, or articulation, of different modes of production.

    资本主义社会结构反映了不同形式的生产方式之间的互动，或者说相互联系。

SYN joint, coupling, jointing, connection
