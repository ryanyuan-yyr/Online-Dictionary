doodah
duːdɑː ˈduːdæd
Word forms: plural doodahs
N-COUNT 小装置，小玩意儿(尤指叫不出名字的电器) You can refer to something, especially an electronic device, as a doodah when you do not know exactly what is called. 小装置，小玩意儿(尤指叫不出名字的电器) [British, informal]

  • The car has all the latest electronic doodahs.

    这辆车配有各种最新的电子小玩意儿。
