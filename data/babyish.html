babyish
beɪbiɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 婴儿般的；孩子气的；幼稚的 Babyish actions, feelings, or looks are like a baby's, or are immature. 婴儿般的；孩子气的；幼稚的

  • ...babyish behaviour.

    幼稚的行为

  • ...a fat, babyish face.

    胖乎乎的娃娃脸

  • I'm ashamed of the babyish nonsense I write.

    我对自己写出这么幼稚的东西感到羞愧。

SYN childish, young, simple, soft [informal]
