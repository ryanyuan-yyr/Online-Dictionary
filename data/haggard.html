haggard
hægəʳd ˈhæɡərd ˈhægərd
ADJ-GRADED （尤指因病或睡眠不足）憔悴的，形容枯槁的，困倦的 Someone who looks haggard has a tired expression and shadows under their eyes, especially because they are ill or have not had enough sleep. （尤指因病或睡眠不足）憔悴的，形容枯槁的，困倦的

  • He was pale and a bit haggard.

    他面黄肌瘦。

  • Nick glanced around at the haggard faces watching him.

    尼克环视着盯着自己的那一张张憔悴的面孔。

SYN gaunt, wasted, drawn, thin
