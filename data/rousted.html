roust
raʊst raʊst raʊst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rousts, present participle rousting, past tense , past participle rousted
VERB 烦扰;驱逐;撵 If you roust someone, you disturb, upset, or hit them, or make them move from their place. 烦扰;驱逐;撵 [US] [Also V n + from]

  • Relax, kid, we're not about to roust you. We just want some information. [VERB noun]

    别紧张，孩子，我们不会为难你的。我们只是需要一些信息。

  • Bruce had gone to bed, but they rousted him out. [V n + out]

    布鲁斯已经上床睡觉了，但是他们把他又叫了起来。
