backcourt
bækkɔrt ˈbækˌkɔːt ˈbækˌkɔrt
Word forms: backcourts
N-COUNT [usu the N] (网球和羽毛球等运动)距球网最远处的球场两侧; (篮球)球场后场; 后场球员In sports such tennis and badminton, the backcourt is the section of each side of the court that is furthest from the net. In basketball, the backcourt is the rear part of the court, where the defence plays. You can also use backcourt to refer to the members of a team who play mainly in this part of the court.
