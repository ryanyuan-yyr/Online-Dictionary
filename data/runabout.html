runabout
rʌnəbaʊt ˈrʌnəraʊnd ˈrʌnəˌbaʊt
Word forms: plural runabouts
1 N-COUNT 轻便汽车;小汽车 A runabout is a small car used mainly for short journeys. In American English, runabout is used of cars with open tops. 轻便汽车;小汽车

  • ...a small 1-litre runabout.

    百公里油耗为1升的小型轻便汽车

2 N-COUNT 小汽艇;小型摩托艇 A runabout is a small, light boat with a motor. 小汽艇;小型摩托艇 [US]
