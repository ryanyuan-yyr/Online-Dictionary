guffaw
gʌfɔː gəˈfɔ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense guffaws, present participle guffawing, past tense , past participle guffawed
1 N-COUNT 狂笑；大笑；哄笑 A guffaw is a very loud laugh. 狂笑；大笑；哄笑

  • He bursts into a loud guffaw.

    他突然大笑起来。

SYN laugh, roar of laughter, bellow of laughter  
2 VERB 哈哈大笑；狂笑 To guffaw means to laugh loudly. 哈哈大笑；狂笑 [Also V + at]

  • As they guffawed loudly, the ticket collector arrived. [VERB]

    他们正哈哈大笑的时候，检票员到了。

  • 'Ha, ha,' everyone guffawed. 'It's one of Viv's shock tactics.' [VERB with quote]

    “哈哈，”所有人都大笑起来，“这是维夫的一招出奇制胜的战术。”

SYN laugh, roar with laughter, bellow with laughter
