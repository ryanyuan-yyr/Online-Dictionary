lifeblood
laɪfblʌd ˈlaɪfˈblʌd
(also life-blood)
N-SING [usually with poss] 生命线；命根子；生命力 The lifeblood of an organization, area, or person is the most important thing that they need in order to exist, develop, or be successful. 生命线；命根子；生命力

  • Small businesses are the lifeblood of the economy. [+ of]

    小型企业是经济的生命力所在。

  • Coal and steel were the region's lifeblood.

    煤炭和钢铁曾是这个地区的生命线。

SYN animating force, life, heart, inspiration
