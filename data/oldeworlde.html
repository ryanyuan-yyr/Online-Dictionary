olde worlde
oʊldi wɜːldi
ADJ-GRADED 旧时的；古色古香的；古雅的 Olde worlde is used to describe places and things that are or seem to be from an earlier period of history, and that look interesting or attractive. 旧时的；古色古香的；古雅的 [British]

  • ...the quaint olde worlde part of town.

    古色古香的老城区

  • There is an olde worlde look about the clothes for summer.

    这些夏装看上去有复古风范。
