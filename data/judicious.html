judicious
dʒuːdɪʃəs dʒuˈdɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 明智的；有见地的；审慎的 If you describe an action or decision as judicious, you approve of it because you think that it shows good judgment and sense. 明智的；有见地的；审慎的 [formal, approval]

  • The President authorizes the judicious use of military force to protect our citizens.

    总统授权可以明智而审慎地动用军队保护我们的公民。

SYN sensible, considered, reasonable, discerning  
judiciously

  • Modern fertilisers should be used judiciously.

    使用现代化肥应该非常谨慎。
