emergency services
ɪˈmɜːdʒənsɪ ˈsɜːvɪsɪz
N-PLURAL 紧急救援部门，应急服务机构（尤指消防队、警察局、救护车等） The emergency services are the public organizations whose job is to take quick action to deal with emergencies when they occur, especially the fire brigade, the police, and the ambulance service. 紧急救援部门，应急服务机构（尤指消防队、警察局、救护车等）
