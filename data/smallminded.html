small-minded
smɔlˌmaɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED 思想狭隘的;气量小的;顽固不化的 If you say that someone is small-minded, you are critical of them because they have fixed opinions and are unwilling to change them or to think about more general subjects. 思想狭隘的;气量小的;顽固不化的 [disapproval]

  • ...their small-minded preoccupation with making money.

    他们鼠目寸光，一心只想赚钱

SYN petty, mean, rigid, grudging  
small-mindedness

  • Helen's small-mindedness bored and disgusted her.

    海伦的狭隘思想让她深感无聊和厌烦。
