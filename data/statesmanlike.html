statesmanlike
steɪtsmənlaɪk
ADJ-GRADED 有政治家风范的;有政治家才干的 If you describe someone, especially a political leader, as statesmanlike, you approve of them because they give the impression of being very able and experienced. 有政治家风范的;有政治家才干的 [approval]

  • He was widely respected as a wise and statesmanlike governor.

    他作为一位睿智、具备政治家才干的州长而广受尊崇。

  • He is trying to project a more dignified, statesmanlike image in this election year.

    他正试图在这个选举年中树立一种更加威严、更有政治家风范的形象。
