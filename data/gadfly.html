gadfly
gædflaɪ ˈgædˌflaɪ
Word forms: plural gadflies
N-COUNT 讨人厌者；有意搅扰他人者;（尤指）故意挑战权威者 If you refer to someone as a gadfly, you believe that they deliberately annoy or challenge other people, especially people in authority. 讨人厌者；有意搅扰他人者;（尤指）故意挑战权威者

  • It still remains to be seen whether Buchanan will be just a gadfly or a real threat to the president.

    布坎南只是故意搅局还是对总统构成真正的威胁，这还有待观察。
