reawaken
riːəweɪkən ˌriːəˈweɪkən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reawakens, present participle reawakening, past tense , past participle reawakened
VERB 重新唤起;使再度复苏;再次引发 If something reawakens an issue, or an interest or feeling that you used to have, it makes you think about it or feel it again. 重新唤起;使再度复苏;再次引发

  • The King's stand is bound to reawaken the painful debate about abortion. [VERB noun]

    国王的立场必然会再次引发关于堕胎的令人头痛的辩论。

  • The food reawakens memories of dishes that their mothers once cooked. [VERB noun]

    这种食物使他们再次想起母亲曾经做过的菜。

reawakening

  • ...a reawakening of interest in stained glass.

    对彩色玻璃重新燃起兴趣
