vessel
vesəl ˈvɛsəl
Word forms: plural vessels
1 N-COUNT 舰;轮船;大船 A vessel is a ship or large boat. 舰;轮船;大船 [formal]

  • A Moroccan fishing vessel and a South Korean cargo ship collided in rough seas.

    一艘摩洛哥渔船与一艘韩国货船在波涛汹涌的大海上相撞了。

  • ...a New Zealand navy vessel.

    一艘新西兰海军军舰

2 N-COUNT （盛液体用的）容器，器皿 A vessel is a bowl or other container in which liquid is kept. （盛液体用的）容器，器皿 [formal]

  • He makes decorative vessels in copper, stainless steel and silver.

    他用铜、不锈钢和银制造装饰性器皿。

  • ...storage vessels.

    储物容器
