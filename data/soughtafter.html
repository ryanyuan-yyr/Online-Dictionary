sought-after
ADJ-GRADED (因稀有或质优而)受欢迎的，吃香的 Something that is sought-after is in great demand, usually because it is rare or of very good quality. (因稀有或质优而)受欢迎的，吃香的

  • An Olympic gold medal is the most sought-after prize in world sport.

    在世界体坛上，一枚奥运会金牌是最令人向往的奖项。

SYN in demand, wanted, desirable, longed-for
