ship
ʃɪp ʃɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ships, present participle shipping, past tense , past participle shipped
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 大船;轮船;舰 A ship is a large boat which carries passengers or cargo. 大船;轮船;舰

  • Within ninety minutes the ship was ready for departure.

    90分钟之内轮船准备出发。

  • We went by ship over to America.

    我们搭轮船去美国。

  • ...merchant ships.

    商船

SYN vessel, boat, craft, liner  
2 VERB [usually passive] 运送;运输;船运 If people or things are shipped somewhere, they are sent there on a ship or by some other means of transport. 运送;运输;船运

  • Food is being shipped to drought-stricken Southern Africa. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    食物正被运往遭受旱灾的非洲南部。

SYN send, take, run, bring  
3 See also: shipping
