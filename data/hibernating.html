hibernate
haɪbəʳneɪt ˈhaɪbərˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hibernates, present participle hibernating, past tense , past participle hibernated
VERB 蛰伏;冬眠 Animals that hibernate spend the winter in a state like a deep sleep. 蛰伏;冬眠

  • Dormice hibernate from October to May. [VERB]

    榛睡鼠从10月冬眠到5月。

  • Hibernating insects begin to move. [VERB-ing]

    蛰伏的昆虫们开始活动。

SYN sleep, lie dormant, winter, overwinter
