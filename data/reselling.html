resell
riːsel riːˈsɛl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense resells, present participle reselling, past tense , past participle resold
VERB 转售;转卖;再卖 If you resell something that you have bought, you sell it again. 转售;转卖;再卖

  • Shopkeepers buy them in bulk and resell them for £150 each. [VERB noun]

    店主们先大量购进，然后再以150英镑的单价将它们转卖出去。

  • It makes sense to buy at dealer prices so you can maximize your profits if you resell. [VERB]

    明智的做法是以批发价购进，这样可以在转卖时获得最大的利润。
