indoctrinate
ɪndɒktrɪneɪt ɪnˈdɑktrəˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense indoctrinates, present participle indoctrinating, past tense , past participle indoctrinated
1 VERB 向…灌输(信仰等) If people are indoctrinated, they are taught a particular belief with the aim that they will reject other beliefs. 向…灌输(信仰等) [disapproval]

  • They have been completely indoctrinated. [be VERB-ed]

    他们完全被洗脑了。

  • I wouldn't say that she was trying to indoctrinate us. [VERB noun]

    我不认为她在竭力向我们灌输一种信仰。
