combat
kɒmbæt kəmbæt kəmˈbæt ˈkɑmˌbæt ˈkʌmˌbæt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense combats, present participle combating, present participle combatting, past tense , past participle combated, past tense , past participle combatted
The noun is pronounced /'kɒmbæt/. The verb is pronounced /kəm'bæt/. 名词读作 /'kɒm-bæt/。动词读作 /kəm'bæt/。
1 N-UNCOUNT 战斗 Combat is fighting that takes place in a war. 战斗

  • Over 16 million men had died in combat.

    1,600多万人在战斗中牺牲。

  • Yesterday saw hand-to-hand combat in the city.

    昨天该市发生了肉搏战。

  • ...combat aircraft.

    战斗机

SYN fight, war, action, battle  
2 N-COUNT 斗争;格斗 A combat is a battle, or a fight between two people. 斗争;格斗

  • It was the end of a long combat.

    这是一场长期斗争的结束。

3 VERB 与…作斗争;打击 If people in authority combat something, they try to stop it happening. 与…作斗争;打击

  • Congress has criticised new government measures to combat crime. [VERB noun]

    国会对政府打击犯罪的新措施提出了批评。

SYN fight, battle against, oppose, contest
