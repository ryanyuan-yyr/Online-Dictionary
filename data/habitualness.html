habitual
həbɪtʃuəl həˈbɪtʃuəl
1 ADJ （动作、状态或行为方式）习惯性的，惯常的，典型的 A habitual action, state, or way of behaving is one that someone usually does or has, especially one that is considered to be typical or characteristic of them. （动作、状态或行为方式）习惯性的，惯常的，典型的

  • He soon recovered his habitual geniality.

    他不久就恢复了自己一贯的和蔼可亲的态度。

  • If bad posture becomes habitual, you risk long-term effects.

    不良姿势一旦定型，日后可就有你受的了。

SYN customary, normal, usual, common  
habitually

  • His mother had a patient who habitually flew into rages.

    他母亲有个老是大发雷霆的病人。

  • ...her habitually prim expression.

    她那一向一本正经的表情

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （人）积习难改的，习以为常的 You use habitual to describe someone who usually or often does a particular thing. （人）积习难改的，习以为常的

  • She was a habitual daydreamer.

    她总是耽于幻想。

  • ...the home secretary's plans for minimum sentences for habitual criminals.

    内政大臣关于设置惯犯起刑点的打算
