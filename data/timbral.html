timbre
tæmbəʳ ˈtɪmbə tɛ̃brə ˈtæmbər ˈtɪmbər ˈtɛ̃bʀ(ə)
Word forms: plural timbres
N-COUNT [usually singular] 音色;音质;音品 The timbre of someone's voice or of a musical instrument is the particular quality of sound that it has. 音色;音质;音品 [formal]

  • His voice had a deep timbre.

    他嗓音低沉。

  • The timbre of the violin is far richer than that of the mouth organ. [+ of]

    小提琴的音色远比口琴丰富。

SYN tone, sound, ring, resonance
