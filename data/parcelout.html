parcel out
PHRASAL VERB 分享；瓜分 If you parcel out something, you divide it into several parts or amounts and give them to different people. 分享；瓜分 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They signed an agreement that parcelled out the Middle East into several spheres of influence. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们签订了一项协议，将中东瓜分为几个势力范围。

SYN distribute, divide, portion, allocate
