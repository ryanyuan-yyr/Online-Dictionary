muddled
mʌdəld
ADJ-GRADED 困惑的;糊涂的 If someone is muddled, they are confused about something. 困惑的;糊涂的

  • I'm afraid I'm a little muddled. I'm not exactly sure where to begin.

    恐怕我有点糊涂了，我不确定该从何处着手。

  • ...the muddled thinking of the Government's transport policy in recent years.

    近几年政府交通政策的混乱

SYN bewildered, confused, at sea, dazed
