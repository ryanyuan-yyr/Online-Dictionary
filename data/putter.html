putter
pʌtəʳ ˈpʊtə ˈpʌtə ˈpʌtər ˈpʊtər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense putters, present participle puttering, past tense , past participle puttered
1 N-COUNT 轻击（球）棒 A putter is a club used for hitting a golf ball a short distance once it is on the green. 轻击（球）棒
2 VERB 慢条斯理地工作；悠然自得地做 If you putter around, you do unimportant but quite enjoyable things, without hurrying. 慢条斯理地工作；悠然自得地做 [US]

  • I started puttering around outside, not knowing what I was doing.

    我开始在外面闲荡，不知道自己在做什么。

  • She liked to putter in the kitchen. [VERB]

    她喜欢在厨房里磨洋工。
