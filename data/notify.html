notify
noʊtɪfaɪ ˈnoʊtəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense notifies, present participle notifying, past tense , past participle notified
VERB 通知;告知 If you notify someone of something, you officially inform them about it. 通知;告知 [formal] [Also V n that]

  • The skipper notified the coastguard of the tragedy. [V n + of/about]

    船长向海岸警卫队报告了这起灾难。

  • Earlier this year they were notified that their homes were to be cleared away. [be VERB-ed that]

    今年早些时候他们接到通知说他们的房屋要拆迁。

  • She confirmed that she would notify the police and the hospital. [VERB noun]

    她确认说她会通知警方和医院。

SYN inform, tell, advise, alert to
