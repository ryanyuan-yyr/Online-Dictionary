categorize
kætɪgəraɪz ˈkætəgəˌraɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense categorizes, present participle categorizing, past tense , past participle categorized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 categorise
VERB 对…进行分类;把…归类 If you categorize people or things, you divide them into sets or you say which set they belong to. 对…进行分类;把…归类

  • Lindsay, like his films, is hard to categorise. [VERB noun]

    林赛这个人和他的电影一样，很难归类。

  • Make a list of your child's toys and then categorise them as sociable or antisocial. [VERB noun]

    把孩子的玩具列个单子，然后将其分为交际型和非交际型两类。

  • ...new ways of categorizing information. [VERB-ing]

    信息分类的新方法
