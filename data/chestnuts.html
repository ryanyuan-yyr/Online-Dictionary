chestnut
tʃesnʌt ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt ˈtʃɛstˌnʌt
Word forms: plural chestnuts
1 N-COUNT 栗子树 A chestnut or chestnut tree is a tall tree with broad leaves. 栗子树
2See also:horse chestnut 
3 N-COUNT 栗子 Chestnuts are the reddish-brown nuts that grow on chestnut trees. You can eat chestnuts. 栗子
4 COLOUR 栗色;红棕色 Something that is chestnut is dark reddish-brown in colour. 栗色;红棕色

  • ...a woman with chestnut hair.

    栗色头发的女子

  • ...a chestnut mare.

    枣红色母马

5 PHRASE [NOUN inflects] 老掉牙的话(或故事、笑话);老一套 If you refer to a statement, a story, or a joke as an old chestnut or a hoary chestnut, you mean that it has been repeated so often that it is no longer interesting. 老掉牙的话(或故事、笑话);老一套

  • ...that old chestnut, the war between man and machines.

    又是老掉牙的故事：人机大战
