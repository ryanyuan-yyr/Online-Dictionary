no one
(also no-one)
PRON-INDEF-NEG 没有人;无人 No one means not a single person, or not a single member of a particular group or set. 没有人;无人

  • Everyone wants to be a hero, but no one wants to die.

    每个人都想成为英雄，但没有人想死。

  • No one can open mail except the person to whom it has been addressed.

    除收信人本人外，没人能打开邮件。

SYN nobody, no man, not a soul
