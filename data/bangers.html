banger
bæŋəʳ ˈbæŋər
Word forms: plural bangers
1 N-COUNT 香肠 Bangers are sausages. 香肠 [British, informal]

  • ... bangers and mash.

    香肠土豆泥

2 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 老爷车；破旧的汽车 You can describe a car as a banger if it is old and in very bad condition. 老爷车；破旧的汽车 [British, informal]

  • ...this clapped-out old banger.

    这辆破旧不堪的老爷车

3 N-COUNT 鞭炮；爆竹 Bangers are fireworks that make a lot of noise. 鞭炮；爆竹 [British]
