messiah
mɪsaɪə
Word forms: plural messiahs
1 N-PROPER 弥赛亚For Jews, the Messiah is the king of the Jews, who will be sent to them by God. 弥赛亚
2 N-PROPER 耶稣基督For Christians, the Messiah is Jesus Christ. 耶稣基督
3 N-COUNT 救星; 救世主If you refer to someone as a messiah, you mean that they are expected to do wonderful things, especially to rescue people from a very difficult or dangerous situation, or that they are thought to have done these things. 救星; 救世主

  • People saw Mandela as their messiah.

    人民把曼德拉视为他们的救星。
