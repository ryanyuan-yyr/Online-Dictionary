inwards
ɪnwəʳdz ˈɪnwərdz
(also inward)
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向内地;向中心地 If something moves or faces inwards, it moves or faces towards the inside or centre of something. 向内地;向中心地

  • She pressed back against the door until it swung inwards.

    她用背顶门，直到门向内打开。

  • Keeping your heels on the ground, turn your feet inwards.

    脚跟不要离地,脚向内转。

SYN inside, inward
