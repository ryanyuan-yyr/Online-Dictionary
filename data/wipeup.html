wipe up
PHRASAL VERB （用布）擦掉，擦去 If you wipe up dirt or liquid from something, you remove it using a cloth. （用布）擦掉，擦去

  • I spilled my coffee all over the table and Mom leaned across me to wipe it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我把咖啡洒得满桌都是，妈妈俯过身来把它擦掉了。

  • Wipe up spills immediately. [VERB PREP. noun]

    马上把洒出来的东西擦掉。

SYN clean something up, mop something up, sop something up
