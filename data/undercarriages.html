undercarriage
ʌndəʳkærɪdʒ ˈʌndərˌkærɪdʒ
Word forms: plural undercarriages
N-COUNT （飞机的）起落架 The undercarriage of an aeroplane is the part, including the wheels, which supports the aeroplane when it is on the ground and when it is landing or taking off. （飞机的）起落架 [mainly British]
