unglued
ʌngluːd ʌnˈɡluːd
1 PHRASE 脱落的；分离的 If something comes unglued, it becomes separated from the thing that it was attached to. 脱落的；分离的

  • I wear my old shoes every day. One sole has come unglued.

    我每天都穿那双旧鞋，有一只鞋底已经脱胶了。

2 PHRASE 失败 To come unglued means to fail. 失败

  • Their marriage finally came unglued.

    他们的婚姻最终破裂了。

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 come unstuck
3 PHRASE 心烦意乱；崩溃 If someone comes unglued, they become very upset and emotional, and perhaps confused or mentally ill. 心烦意乱；崩溃

  • If she hears what you're saying, she's going to come unglued.

    她听到你们的话会崩溃的。
