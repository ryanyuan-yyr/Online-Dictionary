burgeon
bɜːʳdʒən ˈbɜrdʒən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense burgeons, present participle burgeoning, past tense , past participle burgeoned
VERB 迅速生长；快速发展 If something burgeons, it grows or develops rapidly. 迅速生长；快速发展 [literary]

  • Plants burgeon from every available space. [VERB]

    只要有一点空隙，植物就会生根发芽。

  • My confidence began to burgeon later in life. [VERB]

    我年纪大些之后，信心才开始增强。

  • ...Japan's burgeoning satellite-TV industry. [VERB-ing]

    日本迅速发展的卫星电视产业

SYN develop, increase, grow, flower
