pollution
pəluːʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT （尤指有毒化学品造成的）污染 Pollution is the process of polluting water, air, or land, especially with poisonous chemicals. （尤指有毒化学品造成的）污染

  • The fine was for the company's pollution of the air near its plants. [+ of]

    罚款是因该公司对厂房周边空气造成了污染。

  • Recycling also helps control environmental pollution by reducing the need for waste dumps.

    对废弃物的回收利用能够减少对垃圾场的需求，从而也有助于控制环境污染。

2 N-UNCOUNT 污染物 Pollution is poisonous or dirty substances that are polluting the water, air, or land somewhere. 污染物

  • The level of pollution in the river was falling.

    这条河中的污染物水平在下降。

SYN waste, poisons, dirt, impurities
