iconoclastic
aɪkɒnəklæstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 有悖传统信仰的 If you describe someone or their words or ideas as iconoclastic, you mean that they contradict established beliefs. 有悖传统信仰的 [formal]

  • Is it utopian to hope that such iconoclastic ideas will gain ground?.

    希望如此有悖传统观念的思想会发展普及，这是不是太理想化了？

  • His iconoclastic tendencies can get him into trouble.

    他与传统信仰相悖的思想倾向可能会给他带来麻烦。

SYN subversive, radical, rebellious, questioning
