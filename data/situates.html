situate
sɪtʃueɪt ˈsɪtʃuɪt ˈsɪtʃuˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense situates, present participle situating, past tense , past participle situated
VERB (尤指为了更好地理解某事)把…放在(特殊的环境中)，把…跟(具体情境)联系 If you situate something such as an idea or fact in a particular context, you relate it to that context, especially in order to understand it better. (尤指为了更好地理解某事)把…放在(特殊的环境中)，把…跟(具体情境)联系 [formal]

  • How do we situate Christianity in the context of modern physics and psychology? [VERB noun adv./prep.]

    我们怎样把基督教放在现代物理学和心理学的背景下来考虑呢？

SYN locate, set, base, position
