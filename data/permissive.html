permissive
pəʳmɪsɪv pərˈmɪsɪv
ADJ-GRADED 纵容的；放任的；姑息的 A permissive person, society, or way of behaving allows or tolerates things which other people disapprove of. 纵容的；放任的；姑息的

  • ...the permissive tolerance of the 1960s.

    20世纪60年代的“姑息纵容”

  • Single parents are more likely to be permissive.

    单亲家庭的父母更容易放任自己的孩子。

SYN tolerant, liberal, open-minded, indulgent  
permissiveness

  • Permissiveness and democracy go together.

    自由放任和民主相辅相成。
