garrote
gərɒt gəˈrɑt gəˈroʊt
(also garrotte)Word forms: garrotes, garroting, garroted
1 V-T 绞死If someone is garroted, they are killed by having something such as a piece of wire or cord pulled tightly around their neck. 绞死

  • The two guards had been garroted.

    两个看守已经被绞死了。

2 N-COUNT 绞刑具A garrote is a piece of wire or cord used to garrote someone. 绞刑具
