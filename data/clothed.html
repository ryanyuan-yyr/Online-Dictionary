clothed
kloʊðd kləʊðd
1 ADJ [adverb ADJ] 穿着…衣服的 If you are clothed in a certain way, you are dressed in that way. 穿着…衣服的

  • He lay down on the bed fully clothed.

    他合衣躺在床上。

  • She was clothed in a flowered dress.

    她穿着一条花裙子。

  • ...women clothed in black. [+ in]

    穿着黑衣服的女人们

2 ADJ [v-link ADJ in n] 被…覆盖的;裹着…的 If a place or thing is clothed in something, it is covered in that thing. 被…覆盖的;裹着…的 [literary]

  • The south side of the gorge is now clothed in trees.

    现在峡谷的南边已长满树木。
