glaring
gleərɪŋ ˈɡlɛərɪŋ ˈglɛrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 非常显眼的；昭然的 If you describe something bad as glaring, you are emphasizing that it is very obvious and easily seen or noticed. 非常显眼的；昭然的 [emphasis]

  • I never saw such a glaring example of misrepresentation.

    我从未见过像这样明目张胆地歪曲事实。

SYN obvious, open, outstanding, patent  
glaringly
See also: glare 

  • It was glaringly obvious.

    这是显而易见的。

  • He told a glaringly different story.

    他的说法截然不同。
