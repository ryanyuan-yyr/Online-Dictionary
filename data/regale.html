regale
rɪgeɪl rɪˈgeɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense regales, present participle regaling, past tense , past participle regaled
VERB （以讲故事或笑话的方式）取悦 If someone regales you with stories or jokes, they tell you a lot of them, whether you want to hear them or not. （以讲故事或笑话的方式）取悦

  • He was constantly regaled with tales of woe. [be VERB-ed + with]

    别人老是给他讲些倒霉事儿来逗他开心。

SYN entertain, delight, amuse, divert
