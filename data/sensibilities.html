sensibility
sensɪbɪlɪti ˌsɛnsəˈbɪləti
Word forms: plural sensibilities
1 N-UNCOUNT 感受力;感悟力;鉴赏力 Sensibility is the ability to experience deep feelings. 感受力;感悟力;鉴赏力

  • Everything he writes demonstrates the depth of his sensibility.

    他笔下的一切都显示出他深深的感悟。

  • ...a man of sensibility.

    懂感情的男子

SYN awareness, insight, intuition, taste  
2 N-VAR [usually poss NOUN] 敏感;善感;感情 Someone's sensibility is their tendency to be influenced or offended by things. 敏感;善感;感情

  • He was unable to control his sensibility.

    他不能控制自己的过分敏感。

  • The challenge offended their sensibilities.

    这一质疑伤害了他们的感情。

SYN feelings, emotions, sentiments, susceptibilities
