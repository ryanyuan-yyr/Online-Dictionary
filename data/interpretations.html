interpretation

Word forms: plural interpretations
1 N-VAR 解释;说明;阐明 An interpretation of something is an opinion about what it means. 解释;说明;阐明

  • The opposition Conservative Party put a different interpretation on the figures.

    反对党保守党对这些数字提出一番不同的解释。

  • Analysis and interpretation is a very personal thing.

    分析与说明是个很主观的事情。

SYN explanation, meaning, reading, understanding  
2 N-COUNT 表演;演绎;演奏 A performer's interpretation of something such as a piece of music or a role in a play is the particular way in which they choose to perform it. 表演;演绎;演奏

  • ...her full-bodied interpretation of the role of Micaela. [+ of]

    她对米凯拉这个角色全身心的演绎

SYN performance, portrayal, presentation, rendering
