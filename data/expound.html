expound
ɪkspaʊnd ɪkˈspaʊnd ɛkˈspaʊnd ɪkˈspaʊnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense expounds, present participle expounding, past tense , past participle expounded
VERB 详述;阐述;详细说明 If you expound an idea or opinion, you give a clear and detailed explanation of it. 详述;阐述;详细说明 [formal]

  • Schmidt continued to expound his views on economics and politics. [VERB noun]

    施密特继续阐述他的经济和政治观点。

SYN explain, describe, illustrate, interpret  
Expound on means the same as expound .expound on 同 expound

  • Lawrence expounded on the military aspects of guerrilla warfare.

    劳伦斯详述了游击战的军事特点。
