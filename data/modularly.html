modular
mɒdʒʊləʳ ˈmɑdʒələr
1 ADJ (建筑中)组合的，组件的，模件的 In building, modular means relating to the construction of buildings in parts called modules. (建筑中)组合的，组件的，模件的

  • They ended up buying a prebuilt modular home on a two-acre lot.

    最后他们购买了一幢建在两英亩土地上的预制组合式房屋。

2 ADJ (大学课程教学)单元的，分单元的 Modular means relating to the teaching of courses at college or university in units called modules. (大学课程教学)单元的，分单元的 [British]

  • The course is modular in structure.

    该课程在结构上以单元划分。
