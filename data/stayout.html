stay out
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤指该在家时)不在家;(晚上)不回家 If you stay out at night, you remain away from home, especially when you are expected to be there. (尤指该在家时)不在家;(晚上)不回家

  • That was the first time Elliot stayed out all night. [VERB PREP.]

    那是埃利奥特第一次彻夜未归。

  • I met some friends and stayed out until eleven or twelve. [VERB PREP.]

    我见了几个朋友，直到夜里十一二点才回家。

2 PHRASAL VERB (工人)继续罢工 If workers who are striking stay out, they remain on strike. (工人)继续罢工 [mainly British]

  • The electricians at the power stations went on strike and stayed out nearly five days. [VERB PREP.]

    发电厂的电工们举行了罢工，一直持续了近 5 天。
