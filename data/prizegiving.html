prize-giving
ˈpraɪzɡɪvɪŋ
(also prizegiving)Word forms: plural prize-givings
N-COUNT 颁奖典礼；颁奖仪式 also prizegiving. A prize-giving is a ceremony where prizes are awarded to people who have produced a very high standard of work. 颁奖典礼；颁奖仪式 [British]

  • Neil had been at a prize giving ceremony at a school in Birmingham.

    尼尔之前在伯明翰的一所学校参加颁奖仪式。

  • ...a prize-giving for cattle-breeding.

    养牛展的颁奖仪式
