creative accounting
krɪˈeɪtɪv əˈkaʊntɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 创造性做账;伪造账目 Creative accounting is when companies present or organize their accounts in such a way that they gain money for themselves or give a false impression of their profits. 创造性做账;伪造账目 [disapproval]

  • Much of the apparent growth in profits that occurred in the 1980s was the result of creative accounting.

    20世纪80年代很多表面上的利润增长都是靠做假账做出来的。
