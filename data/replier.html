reply
rɪplaɪ rɪˈplaɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense replies, present participle replying, past tense , past participle replied
1 VERB 回复;回答 When you reply to something that someone has said or written to you, you say or write an answer to them. 回复;回答

  • 'That's a nice dress,' said Michael. 'Thanks,' she replied solemnly. [VERB with quote]

    “那件衣服很好看，”迈克尔说。“谢谢，”她严肃地回答。

  • He replied that this was absolutely impossible. [VERB that]

    他回复说这是绝对不可能的。

  • Grace was too terrified to reply. [VERB]

    格雷斯吓坏了，不能作答。

  • I've not replied to Lee's letter yet. [VERB to noun]

    我还没有给李回信呢。

  • To their surprise, hundreds replied to the advertisement. [VERB + to]

    令他们吃惊的是，有几百人对广告作出了回应。

SYN answer, respond, retort, return  
2 N-COUNT [oft in NOUN] 回信;回复;回应 A reply is something that you say or write when you answer someone or answer a letter or advertisement. 回信;回复;回应 [Also + from]

  • I called out a challenge, but there was no reply.

    我提出挑战，但是没人回应。

  • David has had 12 replies to his ad. [+ to]

    戴维的广告已经有12条回复了。

  • They went ahead without waiting for a reply from the Germans.

    他们没等德国人答复就接着往下推进。

  • He said in reply that the question was unfair.

    他回答说这个问题不公平。

SYN answer, response, reaction, counter  
3 VERB (在体育比赛中)以得分回应，回敬， In sports, if you reply to a goal or a number of points scored by your opponents, you also score a goal or a number of points. (在体育比赛中)以得分回应，回敬， [journalism]

  • Graeme Hick scored an unbeaten 58 as Worcestershire replied to Middlesex's 202 with 132-3. [VERB to noun]

    格雷姆·希克未出局拿得58分，从而使伍斯特郡队以132分3人出局的成绩回敬了米德尔塞克斯队的202分。

  • Deryck Fox gave Featherstone the lead early in the first half with a penalty, but Saints were quick to reply. [VERB]

    德里克·福克斯在上半场的罚球使费瑟斯通队领先，但是圣徒队很快进行了反击。

4 VERB (以行动)作出回应，回击，反击 If you reply to something such as an attack with violence or with another action, you do something in response. (以行动)作出回应，回击，反击

  • Farmers threw eggs and empty bottles at police, who replied with tear gas. [VERB + with]

    农民向警察扔鸡蛋和空瓶子，警察则以催泪弹回击。

  • The National Salvation Front has already replied to this series of opposition moves with its own demonstrations. [V + to/with]

    民族救亡阵线已经组织了示威，以此回击反对派的一系列活动。
