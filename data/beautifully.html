beautiful
bjuːtɪfʊl ˈbjutəfəl ˈbjutifəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 美丽的；漂亮的 A beautiful person is very attractive to look at. 美丽的；漂亮的

  • She was a very beautiful woman.

    她是个大美女。

  • To me he is the most beautiful child in the world.

    对我来说，他是世界上最美丽的孩子。

2 ADJ-GRADED 美好的；极好的；美妙的 If you describe something as beautiful, you mean that it is very attractive or pleasing. 美好的；极好的；美妙的

  • New England is beautiful.

    新英格兰很美。

  • It was a beautiful morning.

    那是一个美妙的早晨。

  • He has beautiful manners.

    他举止优雅。

SYN bright, clear, fine, summery  
beautifully

  • The children behaved beautifully.

    孩子们很乖。

  • ...a beautifully clear, sunny day.

    晴好的艳阳天

3 ADJ-GRADED 巧妙的；娴熟的；漂亮的 You can describe something that someone does as beautiful when they do it very skilfully. 巧妙的；娴熟的；漂亮的

  • That's a beautiful shot!

    射得漂亮！

SYN wonderful, great [informal] , excellent, superb  
beautifully

  • Arsenal played beautifully.

    阿森纳队踢得赏心悦目。

Usage Note :

  • When you are describing someone's appearance, you usually use beautiful and pretty to describe women, girls, and babies. Beautiful is a much stronger word than pretty. The equivalent word for a man is handsome. Good-looking and attractive can be used to describe people of either sex.

    在描述人的外貌时，通常用 beautiful 和 pretty 来形容女人、女孩和婴儿。beautiful 语气比 pretty 强烈得多。形容男性的对应词是 handsome。good-looking 和 attractive 既可以形容女性，也可以形容男性。
