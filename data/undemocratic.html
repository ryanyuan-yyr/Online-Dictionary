undemocratic
ʌndeməkrætɪk
ADJ-GRADED 不民主的；非民主的；独裁的 A system, process, or decision that is undemocratic is one that is controlled or made by one person or a small number of people, rather than by all the people involved. 不民主的；非民主的；独裁的

  • ...the undemocratic rule of the former political establishment.

    原统治集团的独裁统治

  • Opponents denounced the decree as undemocratic and unconstitutional.

    反对派抨击该法令是不民主的、违反宪法的。

  • ...the undemocratic seizure of power by the military.

    军方以非民主方式夺取政权
