entrée ɒntreɪ
(also entree)Word forms: plural entrées
1 N-COUNT 进入; 融入; 接受If you have an entrée to a social group, you are accepted and made to feel welcome by them. 进入; 融入; 接受

  • She had an entree into the city's cultivated society. [+ into]

    她得到了城中高雅社会的接受。

SYN entry, access, way in, introduction  
2 N-COUNT 主菜; 前菜At restaurants or formal dinners, the entrée is the main course, or sometimes a dish before the main course. 主菜; 前菜

  • Dinner features a hot entrée of chicken, veal, or lamb.

    晚餐主菜是一道热菜，有鸡肉、小牛肉或小羊肉可供选择。

SYN starter, appetizer
