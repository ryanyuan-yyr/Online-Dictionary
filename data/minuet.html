minuet
mɪnjuet ˌmɪnjuˈɛt
Word forms: plural minuets
1 N-COUNT （流行于17、18世纪的）米奴哀舞曲;小步舞曲 In the music of the seventeenth and eighteenth centuries, a minuet is a piece of music with three beats in a bar which is played at moderate speed. （流行于17、18世纪的）米奴哀舞曲;小步舞曲
2 N-COUNT 米奴哀舞;小步舞 A minuet is a fairly slow and formal dance which was popular in the seventeenth and eighteenth centuries. 米奴哀舞;小步舞
