smarten up
PHRASAL VERB 修饰;打扮;装修 If you smarten yourself or a place up, you make yourself or the place look neater and tidier. 修饰;打扮;装修

  • ...a 10-year programme to smarten up the London Underground. [VERB PREP. noun]

    旨在让伦敦地铁旧貌换新颜的 10 年规划

  • She had wisely smartened herself up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她很聪明地把自己修饰打扮了一番。

  • ...a medical student who refused to smarten up. [VERB PREP.]

    拒绝打扮的医科学生
