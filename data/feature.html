feature
fiːtʃəʳ ˈfitʃər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense features, present participle featuring, past tense , past participle featured
1 N-COUNT 特征;特点;特色 A feature of something is an interesting or important part or characteristic of it. 特征;特点;特色

  • Patriotic songs have long been a feature of Kuwaiti life. [+ of]

    爱国歌曲长期以来一直是科威特生活的一个特色。

  • The spacious gardens are a special feature of this property. [+ of]

    大花园是这处房产的特色。

  • Perhaps the most unusual feature in the room is an extraordinary pair of candles.

    也许，这间屋子最与众不同之处就是一对非同寻常的蜡烛。

SYN aspect, quality, characteristic, attribute  
2 N-PLURAL [usually poss NOUN] 面容;五官 Your features are your eyes, nose, mouth, and other parts of your face. 面容;五官

  • His features seemed to change.

    他的容貌似乎有所改变。

  • Her features were strongly defined.

    她的五官轮廓分明。

3 VERB 由…主演;以…为主要内容;重点介绍 When something such as a film or exhibition features a particular person or thing, they are an important part of it. 由…主演;以…为主要内容;重点介绍

  • It's a great movie and it features a Spanish actor who is going to be a world star within a year. [VERB noun]

    这是一部精彩的影片，主演是一位西班牙演员，他不出一年就会成为国际巨星。

  • The hour-long programme will be updated each week and feature highlights from recent games. [VERB noun]

    这个时长一小时的节目将每周更新，重点介绍最近比赛的精彩片断。

  • This spectacular event, now in its 5th year, features a stunning catwalk show. [VERB noun]

    这一盛事如今已是第 5 个年头，其重头戏是一场精彩纷呈的时装秀。

SYN spotlight, present, promote, set off  
4 VERB 出演重头戏;担任主演;(在展览、杂志中)为重点推出的部分 If someone or something features in something such as a show, exhibition, or magazine, they are an important part of it. 出演重头戏;担任主演;(在展览、杂志中)为重点推出的部分

  • Jon featured in one of the show's most thrilling episodes. [V + in/on]

    乔恩主演剧中最引人入胜的剧集之一。

5 N-COUNT (报刊或杂志的)特写，专题;(电台或电视台的)特别节目 A feature is a special article in a newspaper or magazine, or a special programme on radio or television. (报刊或杂志的)特写，专题;(电台或电视台的)特别节目

  • We are delighted to see the Sunday Times running a long feature on breast cancer.

    我们高兴地看到《星期天时报》发表了有关乳腺癌的长篇专题文章。

  • ...a special feature on the fund-raising project. [+ on]

    有关集资项目的专题报道

6 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] 正片;影片;故事片 A feature or a feature film or movie is a full-length film about a fictional situation, as opposed to a short film or a documentary. 正片;影片;故事片

  • ...the first feature-length cartoon, Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.

    第一部卡通长片《白雪公主和七个小矮人》

7 N-COUNT 地理特征;地形地物;地貌 A geographical feature is something noticeable in a particular area of country, for example a hill, river, or valley. 地理特征;地形地物;地貌
