muddle along
PHRASAL VERB 虚度光阴;糊里糊涂过日子;混日子 If you muddle along, you live or exist without a proper plan or purpose in your life. 虚度光阴;糊里糊涂过日子;混日子

  • I've started going to evening classes to learn how to do things properly, rather than just muddling along. [VERB PREP.]

    我已经开始去夜校听课了，学习如何做事，而不是瞎混日子。
