amused
əmjuːzd əˈmjuːzd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 被逗乐的 If you are amused by something, it makes you want to laugh or smile. 被逗乐的

  • Sara was not amused by Franklin's teasing. [+ by/at]

    被富兰克林揶揄之后，萨拉的脸上全无笑意。

  • We were amused to see how assiduously the animal groomed its fur.

    看着这只动物如此认真地梳理自己的毛，我们被逗乐了。

2 PHRASE 使…快乐;为…提供消遣 If you keep someone amused, you find things to do which stop them getting bored. 使…快乐;为…提供消遣

  • Having pictures to colour will keep children amused for hours.

    有画可用来涂色会让孩子们玩很长时间。

  • Archie kept us amused with his stories.

    阿奇一直给我们讲故事来逗我们开心。
