accordance
əkɔːʳdəns əˈkɔrdəns
PREP-PHRASE 按照;依据 If something is done in accordance with a particular rule or system, it is done in the way that the rule or system says that it should be done. 按照;依据

  • Entries which are not in accordance with the rules will be disqualified.

    不符合规定的参赛作品将被取消资格。

SYN in agreement with, accord, correspondence, consistent with
