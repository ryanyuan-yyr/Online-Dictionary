provocation
prɒvəkeɪʃən ˌprɑvəˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural provocations
N-VAR 挑衅；煽动；刺激；激怒 If you describe a person's action as provocation or a provocation, you mean that it is a reason for someone else to react angrily, violently, or emotionally. 挑衅；煽动；刺激；激怒

  • He denies murder on the grounds of provocation.

    他否认激情杀人。

  • The soldiers fired without provocation.

    士兵无缘无故就开枪了。

  • The Deputy Commander has condemned this weekend's protest as deliberate provocation.

    副指挥官谴责本周末的抗议活动是蓄意挑衅。

SYN cause, reason, grounds, motivation
