unintelligent
ʌnɪntelɪdʒənt ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt ˌʌnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt
ADJ-GRADED 缺乏才智的；愚蠢的；无知的 If you describe a person as unintelligent, you mean that they are stupid, or do not show any sensible ideas or thoughts. 缺乏才智的；愚蠢的；无知的

  • He believes him to be a weak and unintelligent man.

    他认为他是一个软弱无知的人。

  • He certainly was not unintelligent.

    他一点不蠢。

SYN stupid, obtuse, dull, slow
