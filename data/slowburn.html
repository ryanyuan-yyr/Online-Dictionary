slow burn
1 N-SING 放慢; 减速If something is a slow burn, or if it happens on a slow burn, it develops slowly. 放慢; 减速

  • This death had been a slow burn.

    这种死亡过程缓慢。

  • Because it worked on a slow burn before becoming a hit around the country, many people missed out on seeing the early episodes.

    因为这部剧很缓慢地闻名全国，很多人错过了看以前的片段。

  • This is a slow-burn romance.

    这是一场发展缓慢的恋情。

2 PHRASE 慢慢发火; 越来越生气If someone does a slow burn, their angry feelings grow slowly but steadily. 慢慢发火; 越来越生气

  • It was the sort of thing that might make anyone do a slow burn.

    这是那种可能会让每个人越想越生气的事。
