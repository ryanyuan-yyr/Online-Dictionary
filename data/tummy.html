tummy
tʌmi ˈtʌmi
Word forms: plural tummies
1 N-COUNT 肚子(儿童用语或对儿童说话时用) Your tummy is the part of the front of your body below your waist. Tummy is often used by children or by adults talking to children. 肚子(儿童用语或对儿童说话时用)

  • Your baby's tummy should feel warm, but not hot.

    你宝宝的肚子摸起来应是暖和的，而不是发烫的。

SYN stomach, belly, abdomen, corporation [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 肚肚(儿语或对儿童说话时用) You can use tummy to refer to the parts inside your body where food is digested. Tummy is often used by children or by adults talking to children. 肚肚(儿语或对儿童说话时用)

  • I've got a sore tummy…

    我肚子疼。

  • It's easy to get a tummy upset from river water.

    喝河水很容易闹肚子。
