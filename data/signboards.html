signboard
saɪnbɔːʳd ˈsaɪnˌbɔrd
Word forms: plural signboards
N-COUNT 招牌;告示牌;广告牌 A signboard is a piece of wood which has been painted with pictures or words and which gives some information about a particular place, product, or event. 招牌;告示牌;广告牌

  • The signboard outside the factory read 'baby milk plant'.

    工厂外的招牌上写着“婴儿乳品厂”。
