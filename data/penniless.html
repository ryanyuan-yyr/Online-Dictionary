penniless
peniləs ˈpɛnəlɪs ˈpɛnilɪs
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 一文不名的；身无分文的 Someone who is penniless has hardly any money at all. 一文不名的；身无分文的

  • They'd soon be penniless and homeless if she couldn't find suitable work.

    她要找不到合适的工作，他们很快就会身无分文，无家可归。

  • ...a penniless refugee.

    一文不名的难民

SYN poor, broke [informal] , bankrupt, impoverished
