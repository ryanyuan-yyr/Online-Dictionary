self-denial
ˈsɛlfdɪˈnaɪəl
N-UNCOUNT 自我剥夺;自我牺牲;克己忘我 Self-denial is the habit of refusing to do or have things that you would like, either because you cannot afford them, or because you believe it is morally good for you not to do them or have them. 自我剥夺;自我牺牲;克己忘我

  • Should motherhood necessarily mean sacrifice and self-denial?

    做母亲就一定要牺牲和忘我吗？

  • ...an unprecedented act of self-denial.

    闻所未闻的克己行为

SYN self-sacrifice, renunciation, asceticism, abstemiousness
