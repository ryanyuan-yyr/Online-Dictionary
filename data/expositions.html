exposition
ekspəzɪʃən ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən
Word forms: plural expositions
1 N-COUNT 阐述;解释;说明 An exposition of an idea or theory is a detailed explanation or account of it. 阐述;解释;说明 [formal]

  • The fullest exposition of Coleridge's thought can be found in the States-man's Manual. [+ of]

    柯尔律治的思想可以在《政治家手册》中找到最全面的阐述。

2 N-COUNT 展览会;博览会 An exposition is an exhibition in which something such as goods or works of art are shown to the public. 展览会;博览会

  • ...an art exposition.

    艺术博览会

SYN exhibition, show, fair, display
