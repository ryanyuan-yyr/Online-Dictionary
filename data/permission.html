permission
pəʳmɪʃən pərˈmɪʃən
Word forms: plural permissions
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN to-infinitive] 准许；许可；批准 If someone who has authority over you gives you permission to do something, they say that they will allow you to do it. 准许；许可；批准

  • He asked permission to leave the room.

    他请求准许离开房间。

  • Finally his mother relented and gave permission for her youngest son to marry. [+ for]

    最后他母亲让步了,准许她的小儿子结婚。

  • Police said permission for the march had not been granted.

    警方说游行并未得到批准。

  • They cannot leave the country without permission.

    他们不能擅自离开该国家。

SYN authorization, sanction, licence, approval  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 许可证；书面许可 A permission is a formal, written statement from an official group or place allowing you to do something. 许可证；书面许可

  • ...oil exploration permissions.

    石油勘探许可证

3See also:planning permission
