adore
ədɔːʳ əˈdɔr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adores, present participle adoring, past tense , past participle adored
1 VERB [no cont] 热爱；爱慕；敬慕；崇拜 If you adore someone, you feel great love and admiration for them. 热爱；爱慕；敬慕；崇拜

  • She adored her parents and would do anything to please them. [VERB noun]

    她很爱自己的父母，为让他们高兴愿意做任何事。

SYN love, honour, admire, worship  
2 VERB [no cont] 热爱，喜爱(某事物） If you adore something, you like it very much. 热爱，喜爱(某事物） [informal]

  • My mother adores bananas and eats two a day. [VERB noun]

    我妈妈非常喜欢吃香蕉，每天要吃两根。

  • I adore good books and the theatre. [VERB noun]

    我爱读好书，也喜欢看戏。

SYN like, love, enjoy, go for
