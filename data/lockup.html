lock up
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…锁起妥藏 If you lock something up in a place or container, you put or hide it there and fasten the lock. 把…锁起妥藏

  • Give away any food you have on hand, or lock it up and give the key to the neighbours. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把手头的所有食物都送给别人，或者把食物藏好锁起，然后将钥匙交给邻居。

  • Control of materials could be maintained by locking up bombs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    把炸弹锁起妥藏可实现对物资的控制。

2 PHRASAL VERB 把…关入监狱（或精神病院） To lock someone up means to put them in prison or a secure psychiatric hospital. 把…关入监狱（或精神病院） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Mr Milner persuaded the federal prosecutors not to lock up his client. [VERB PREP. noun]

    米尔纳先生说服联邦检察官不把他的当事人关起来。

  • You are mad, Isabel. You should be locked up. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    你疯了，伊莎贝尔。应该把你关起来才是。

SYN imprison, jail, confine, cage  
3 PHRASAL VERB 锁好（建筑物或汽车的）门窗 When you lock up a building or car or lock up, you make sure that all the doors and windows are locked so that nobody can get in. 锁好（建筑物或汽车的）门窗

  • Don't forget to lock up. [VERB PREP.]

    别忘了锁好门窗。

  • Leave your car here and lock it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把车停在这里锁好。

lock-up ˈlɒkʌp
(also lockup)Word forms: plural lock-ups
1 N-COUNT 同jail A lock-up is the same as a jail . 同jail [US, informal]

  • ...the 450 inmates at the maximum-security lock-up in Lucasville.

    被关在卢卡斯维尔一级警戒监狱的450名囚犯

2 N-COUNT （不与车主房子相连的）车库 A lock-up is a garage that is used by someone, but is not next to their house. （不与车主房子相连的）车库 [British]

  • 25lb of Semtex explosive was found in the lock-up she had rented to Mr Henderson.

    在她租给亨德森先生的车库里发现了25磅塞姆汀塑胶炸药。

Lock-up is also an adjective.

  • ...a lock-up garage.

    独立车库
