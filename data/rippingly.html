rip
rɪp rɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense rips, present participle ripping, past tense , past participle ripped
1 V-ERG （使）撕开;（使）扯开;（使）撕裂;（使）划破 When something rips or when you rip it, you tear it forcefully with your hands or with a tool such as a knife. （使）撕开;（使）扯开;（使）撕裂;（使）划破

  • I felt the banner rip as we were pushed in opposite directions. [VERB]

    当我们被推向相反的方向时，我感觉横幅被撕裂了。

  • I tried not to rip the paper as I unwrapped it. [VERB noun]

    我把纸打开的时候，尽量不把它撕破。

SYN be torn, tear, split, burst  
2 N-COUNT 裂口;裂缝;口子 A rip is a long cut or split in something made of cloth or paper. 裂口;裂缝;口子

  • Looking at the rip in her new dress, she flew into a rage. [+ in]

    看到新连衣裙上的口子，她勃然大怒。

SYN tear, cut, hole, split  
3 VERB 撕去;扯掉;剥去 If you rip something away, you remove it quickly and forcefully. 撕去;扯掉;剥去

  • He ripped away a wire that led to the alarm button. [VERB noun with adv.]

    他把连着报警按钮的电线扯掉了。

  • She ripped off her dress and let it fall to the floor. [VERB noun with adv.]

    她一把扯下连衣裙，任其滑落到地上。

  • He ripped the phone from her hand. [VERB noun prep.]

    他把电话从她手中抢了过来。

4 VERB （迅猛地）穿入，穿透，冲过 If something rips into someone or something or rips through them, it enters that person or thing so quickly and forcefully that it often goes completely through them. （迅猛地）穿入，穿透，冲过

  • A volley of bullets ripped into the facing wall. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一排子弹射进对面的墙上。

  • Their minibus was ripped apart by a bomb. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他们的面包车被炸弹炸得粉碎。

  • The fire ripped through the living room. [VERB prep./adv.]

    大火吞噬了客厅。

  • A violent streak of pain ripped through her whole body. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一阵剧烈的疼痛传遍了她的全身。

5 PHRASE 尽情地做;忘乎所以地做;用尽力气做 If you let rip, you do something forcefully and without trying to control yourself. 尽情地做;忘乎所以地做;用尽力气做 [informal]

  • Turn the guitars up full and let rip.

    把吉他的音量调到最大，尽情欢乐吧。

  • 'Yaaaaaaa,' Carla let rip with the cry of the Valkyries.

    “呀—”卡拉放开喉咙，发出女武神瓦尔基里一般的嘶喊声。

6 PHRASE 迅速地做;强有力地做;(车辆)全速前进，开足马力 If you let something rip, you do it as quickly or as forcefully as possible. You can say 'let it rip' or 'let her rip' to someone when you want them to make a vehicle go as fast as it possibly can. 迅速地做;强有力地做;(车辆)全速前进，开足马力

  • The ecological disaster is partly a product of letting everything rip in order to increase production.

    出现生态灾难的部分原因是人们为了增产而不惜一切代价。

R.I.P.
ɑːr aɪ piː
CONVENTION (写在墓碑上)愿死者安息 R.I.P. is written on gravestones and expresses the hope that the person buried there may rest in peace. R.I.P. is an abbreviation for the Latin expression 'requiescat in pace' or 'requiescant in pace'. (写在墓碑上)愿死者安息
