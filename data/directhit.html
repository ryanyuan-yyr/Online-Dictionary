direct hit
dɪˈrɛkt hɪt
Word forms: plural direct hits
N-COUNT (炸弹、子弹等的)直接命中，直接击中 If a place suffers a direct hit, a bomb, bullet, or other missile that has been aimed at it lands exactly in that place, rather than some distance away. (炸弹、子弹等的)直接命中，直接击中

  • The dugouts were secure from everything but a direct hit.

    除非是直接被击中，否则这个地下掩体是非常安全的。
