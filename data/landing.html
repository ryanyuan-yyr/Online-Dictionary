landing
lændɪŋ ˈlændɪŋ
Word forms: plural landings
1 N-COUNT 楼梯平台 In a house or other building, the landing is the area at the top of the staircase which has rooms leading off it. 楼梯平台

  • I ran out onto the landing.

    我冲出去到了楼梯口。

2 N-VAR 着陆；降落 A landing is an act of bringing an aircraft or spacecraft down to the ground. 着陆；降落

  • I had to make a controlled landing into the sea. [+ into]

    我只好将飞机迫降在海上。

  • The plane had been cleared for landing at Brunswick's Glynco Airport. [+ at]

    飞机获准降落于不伦瑞克的格林科机场。

SYN coming in, arrival, touchdown, disembarkation  
3 N-COUNT （军队的）登陆 When a landing takes place, troops are unloaded from boats or aircraft at the beginning of a military invasion or other operation. （军队的）登陆

  • American forces have begun a big landing.

    美军已开始大规模登陆。

4 N-COUNT 同landing stage A landing is the same as a landing stage . 同landing stage
