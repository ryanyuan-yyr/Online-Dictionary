breather
briːðəʳ ˈbriðər
Word forms: plural breathers
N-COUNT [usually singular] 短暂休息 If you take a breather, you stop what you are doing for a short time and have a rest. 短暂休息 [informal]

  • Relax and take a breather whenever you feel that you need one.

    要放松，什么时候觉得需要休息就休息一下。

SYN rest, break, halt, pause
