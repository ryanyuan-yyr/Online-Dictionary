put about
The forms put around and put round are also used in British English. put around 和 put round亦用于英国英语中。
PHRASAL VERB 传播；散布 If you put something about, you tell it to people that you meet and cause it to become well-known. 传播；散布 [mainly British] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Moderates are putting it about that people shouldn't take the things said at the Republican Convention too seriously. [V n P that]

    温和派散布说人们不应该把共和党大会上说的话太当真。

  • The King had been putting about lurid rumours for months. [VERB PREP. noun]

    国王连续数月都在散布骇人听闻的谣言。

SYN spread, circulate, broadcast, pass on
