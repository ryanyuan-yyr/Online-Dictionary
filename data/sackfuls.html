sackful
sækfʊl ˈsækfʊl ˈsækləʊd ˈsækˌfʊl
Word forms: plural sackfuls
N-COUNT 一满袋;一整包 A sackful is the amount of something that a sack contains or could contain. 一满袋;一整包

    一大袋子礼物
