gas
gæs gæs
Word forms: plural gases, 3rd person singular present tense gasses, present participle gassing, past tense , past participle gassed
The form gases is the plural of the noun. The form gasses is the third person singular of the verb. gases为名词的复数形式，gasses为动词的第三人称单数形式。
1 N-UNCOUNT 气体燃料；天然气；煤气 Gas is a substance like air that is neither liquid nor solid and burns easily. It is used as a fuel for cooking and heating. 气体燃料；天然气；煤气

  • Coal is actually cheaper than gas.

    煤实际上比煤气便宜。

  • Shell signed a contract to develop oil and gas reserves near Archangel.

    壳牌石油公司签订了一份开采阿尔汉格尔附近的石油和天然气资源的合同。

2 N-VAR 气体 A gas is any substance that is neither liquid nor solid, for example oxygen or hydrogen. 气体

  • Helium is a very light gas.

    氦是一种非常轻的气体。

  • ...a huge cloud of gas and dust from the volcanic eruption.

    火山喷发形成的混杂着气体和尘埃的巨大云团

3 N-MASS 毒气，瓦斯（可用作武器） Gas is a poisonous gas that can be used as a weapon. 毒气，瓦斯（可用作武器）

  • ...mustard gas.

    芥子气

  • The problem was that the exhaust gases contain many toxins.

    问题在于废气中含有很多毒素。

4 N-MASS （医用）麻醉气 Gas is a gas used for medical purposes, for example to make patients feel less pain or go to sleep during an operation. （医用）麻醉气 [informal]

  • ...an anaesthetic gas used by many dentists.

    为许多牙医使用的一种麻醉气

5 N-UNCOUNT （车辆用的）汽油 Gas is the fuel which is used to drive motor vehicles. （车辆用的）汽油 [US]

  • ...a tank of gas.

    一桶汽油

  • ...gas stations.

    加油站

SYN petrol, gasoline [US , Canadian , New Zealand]  
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 petrol
6 VERB 用毒气杀死 To gas a person or animal means to kill them by making them breathe poisonous gas. 用毒气杀死

  • Her husband ran a pipe from her car exhaust to the bedroom in an attempt to gas her. [VERB noun]

    她的丈夫将一根与她的汽车排气管相通的管子导入卧室，企图用毒气将她杀死。

7 N-SING [a NOUN] 活泼有趣的事；令人愉快的场面 You can describe a situation or event as a gas when it is very lively, amusing, and enjoyable. 活泼有趣的事；令人愉快的场面 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • It was really a gas to find someone I could talk with.

    找到一个能和我谈得来的人真是一件让人高兴的事。

8 See also: gas chamber  gas mask  greenhouse gas  laughing gas  natural gas  tear gas 
9 PHRASE 加大油门；加速 If you step on the gas when you are driving a vehicle, you go faster. 加大油门；加速 [mainly US, informal]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 step on it
