propound
prəpaʊnd prəˈpaʊnd proʊˈpaʊnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense propounds, present participle propounding, past tense , past participle propounded
VERB 提出（观点）供考虑 If someone propounds an idea or point of view they have, they suggest it for people to consider. 提出（观点）供考虑 [formal]

  • Zoologist Eugene Morton has propounded a general theory of the vocal sounds that animals make. [VERB noun]

    动物学家尤金·莫顿提出了一个有关动物发声的概括性理论。

SYN put forward, present, advance, propose
