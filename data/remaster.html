remaster
riːmɑːstəʳ -mæstəʳ riˈmæstər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense remasters, present participle remastering, past tense , past participle remastered
VERB 重新录制(电影);重新灌录(唱片) If a film or musical recording is remastered, a new recording is made of the old version, using modern technology to improve the quality. 重新录制(电影);重新灌录(唱片) [Also VERB n]

  • A special remastered version of Casablanca is being released. [VERB-ed]
