virulence
vɪrjʊləns ˈvɪrjʊləns ˈvɪrʊləns
1 N-UNCOUNT 恶毒;狠毒;敌意 Virulence is great bitterness and hostility. 恶毒;狠毒;敌意 [formal]

  • The virulence of the café owner's anger had appalled her. [+ of]

    咖啡店老板怒气冲天，充满敌意，把她吓坏了。

2 N-UNCOUNT 毒力;毒性;致命性 The virulence of a disease or poison is its ability to harm or kill people or animals. 毒力;毒性;致命性

  • Medical authorities were baffled, both as to its causes and its virulence.

    医疗当局对其病因及有多致命都还不甚了解。
