omnibus
ɒmnɪbʌs ˈɒmnɪˌbʌs ˈɑmnɪbəs
Word forms: plural omnibuses
1 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] （广播、电视的）汇播节目，综合节目 An omnibus edition of a radio or television programme contains two or more similar programmes that were originally broadcast separately. （广播、电视的）汇播节目，综合节目 [British]

  • I enjoy the omnibus edition of Eastenders on Sunday.

    我喜欢看《伦敦东区》的周日连播版。

2 N-COUNT （某一作家或主题作品的）选集，汇编 An omnibus is a book which contains a large collection of stories or articles, often by a particular person or about a particular subject. （某一作家或主题作品的）选集，汇编

  • ...a new omnibus edition of three Ruth Rendell chillers.

    露丝·伦德尔3篇恐怖故事的新选集

3 N-COUNT 同 bus An omnibus is a bus . 同 bus [old-fashioned]
