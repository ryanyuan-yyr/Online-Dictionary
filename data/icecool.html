ice-cool
ˌaɪsˈkuːl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 处变不惊的;临危不乱的 If you describe someone as ice-cool, you admire them because they are calm and do not show emotion in difficult situations. 处变不惊的;临危不乱的 [journalism, approval]

  • The ice-cool driver has built a reputation for pulling out his best performances under pressure.

    那个沉稳应变的司机在重压下仍能发挥最佳水平，因而赢得了美誉。
