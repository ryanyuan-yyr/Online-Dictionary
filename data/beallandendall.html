be-all and end-all ˈbiˌɔl ənd ˈɛndˌɔl
PHRASE 唯一重要的事物；全部；根本 If something is the be-all and end-all to you, it is the only important thing in your life, or the only important feature of a particular activity. 唯一重要的事物；全部；根本

  • For some people, competing is the be-all and end-all of their running.

    对于有些人来说，赛跑的全部意义就是竞争。
