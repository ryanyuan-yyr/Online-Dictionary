redbrick
redbrɪk ˈrɛdˌbrɪk ˈrɛdˌbrɪk
ADJ [ADJ n] （英国大学）红砖的，建于19世纪末20世纪初的（与牛津、剑桥等更为古老的大学形成对照） In Britain, a redbrick university is one of the universities that were established in large cities outside London in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, as opposed to much older universities such as Oxford and Cambridge. （英国大学）红砖的，建于19世纪末20世纪初的（与牛津、剑桥等更为古老的大学形成对照）
