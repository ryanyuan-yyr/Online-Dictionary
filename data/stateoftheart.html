state-of-the-art ˈsteɪtəvðɪˌɑːt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 使用最先进技术的;体现最高水平的 If you describe something as state-of-the-art, you mean that it is the best available because it has been made using the most modern techniques and technology. 使用最先进技术的;体现最高水平的

    最先进军事装备的生产
