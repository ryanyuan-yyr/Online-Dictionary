futurist
fjuːtʃərɪst ˈfjutʃərɪst
Word forms: plural futurists
1 N-COUNT 未来主义者;未来派艺术家;未来派作家 Futurists were artists and writers who were followers of futurism. 未来主义者;未来派艺术家;未来派作家
2 N-COUNT 预测未来的人;未来学家 A futurist is someone who makes predictions about what is going to happen, on the basis of facts about what is happening now. 预测未来的人;未来学家 [mainly US]

  • Futurists agree that these trends will grow as telecommunications networks become cheaper and more efficient.

    未来学家一致认为，随着电信网络资费的降低和效率的提高，这些趋势将会越来越明显。
