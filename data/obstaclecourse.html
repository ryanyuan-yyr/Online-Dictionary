obstacle course
ˈɒbstəkəl kɔːs
Word forms: plural obstacle courses
1 N-COUNT 障碍赛跑赛场（或跑道） In a race, an obstacle course is a series of obstacles that people have to go over or round in order to complete the race. 障碍赛跑赛场（或跑道）
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 困难重重的过程 If you refer to a situation as an obstacle course, you mean that it is full of difficulties which have to be overcome before you can get or achieve what you want. 困难重重的过程

  • The Government seems to have created a more demanding obstacle course for aspiring students than ever before.

    看来政府已为胸怀抱负的学生设下了要求之高前所未有的重重难关。
