ineffable
ɪnefəbəl ɪnˈɛfəbəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 言语难以表达的;不可言喻的 You use ineffable to say that something is so great or extreme that it cannot be described in words. 言语难以表达的;不可言喻的 [formal]

  • ...the ineffable sadness of many of the portraits.

    许多肖像流露出的难以形容的悲伤

SYN indescribable, unspeakable, indefinable, beyond words  
ineffably

  • Walters is ineffably entertaining.

    沃尔特斯真是说不出地风趣。

  • ...his ineffably powerful brain.

    他那无法形容的超强智慧
