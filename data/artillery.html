artillery
ɑːʳtɪləri ɑrˈtɪləri
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 火炮；大炮 Artillery consists of large, powerful guns which are transported on wheels and used by an army. 火炮；大炮

  • Using tanks and heavy artillery, they seized the town.

    他们倚仗坦克和重型火炮占领了这个镇。

  • ...the sound of artillery fire.

    炮火声

SYN big guns, battery, cannon, ordnance  
2 N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 炮兵（部队） The artillery is the section of an army which is trained to use large, powerful guns. 炮兵（部队）

  • At some stage he left the Artillery to command a radar unit.

    有段时间他离开了炮兵部队去指挥一支雷达小分队。
