misspend
mɪsspend mɪsˈspɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misspends, present participle misspending, past tense , past participle misspent
VERB 浪费，滥用，挥霍（时间或金钱） If you say that time or money has been misspent, you disapprove of the way in which it has been spent. 浪费，滥用，挥霍（时间或金钱） [disapproval]

  • Much of the money was grossly misspent. [be VERB-ed]

    大部分的钱都被大笔挥霍掉了。

  • Ruby recalled getting stoned during her misspent youth. [VERB-ed]

    鲁比还记得，在虚度光阴的少女时代她曾经因为吸食毒品而飘飘欲仙。
