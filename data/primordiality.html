primordial
praɪmɔːʳdiəl praɪˈmɔrdiəl
ADJ 原始的；远古的；人类最初的 You use primordial to describe things that belong to a very early time in the history of the world. 原始的；远古的；人类最初的 [formal]

  • Twenty million years ago, Idaho was populated by dense primordial forest.

    2,000万年前，爱达荷州到处都是茂密的原始森林。

  • ...the original primordial explosion.

    最初的宇宙大爆炸

SYN primeval, primitive, first, earliest
