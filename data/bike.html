bike
baɪk bəɪk baɪk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bikes, present participle biking, past tense , past participle biked
1 N-COUNT 自行车；摩托车 A bike is a bicycle or a motorcycle. 自行车；摩托车 [informal]
2 VERB 骑自行车（或摩托车）去（某处） To bike somewhere means to go there on a bicycle. 骑自行车（或摩托车）去（某处） [informal]

  • I biked home from the beach. [VERB adv./prep.]

    我从海边骑自行车回家。
