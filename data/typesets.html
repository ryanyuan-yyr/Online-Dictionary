typeset
taɪpsɛt ˈtaɪpˌsɛt ˈtaɪpˌsɛt
Word forms: typesets, typesetting, typeset
1 V-T [usu pass] 排版When something written is typeset, it is arranged and prepared for printing either by hand or by the use of a computer. 排版

  • The newspaper was being typeset and printed.

    这份报纸曾被排版并打印出来。

  • I design the layout and fax it over, then they typeset it.

    我设计了版面并将其传真过去，然后他们将之排版。

2 typesetter

  • For a number of years I worked as a typesetter.

    我做了几年排字工人。
