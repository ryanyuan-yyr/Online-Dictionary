downtown
daʊntaʊn ˈdaʊnˈtaʊn ˈdaʊnˌtaʊn
ADJ [ADJ n] 商业区的;市中心的 Downtown places are in or towards the centre of a large town or city, where the shops and places of business are. 商业区的;市中心的 [mainly US] [Also be ADV]

  • ...an office in downtown Chicago.

    位于芝加哥市中心的办事处

Downtown is also an adverb.

  • By day he worked downtown for American Standard.

    白天他在市中心为美标公司工作。

  • You have to be downtown in a hurry.

    你得赶快去市中心。

Downtown is also a noun.

  • ...in a large vacant area of the downtown.

    在市中心的一大块空地上

  • ...Ardastra Gardens, a short taxi ride from downtown.

    阿德斯特拉公园，离市区车程不远
