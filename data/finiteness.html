finite
faɪnaɪt ˈfaɪˌnaɪt
1 ADJ 有限的;有尽的 Something that is finite has a definite fixed size or extent. 有限的;有尽的 [formal]

  • ...a finite set of elements.

    有限元素集

  • Only a finite number of situations can arise.

    只有有限的几种情况可能会发生。

  • The fossil fuels (coal and oil) are finite resources.

    化石燃料(如煤和石油)属于有限资源。

SYN limited, bounded, restricted, demarcated  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (从句)限定的，定式的 A finite clause is a clause based on a verb group which indicates tense, such as 'went', 'is waiting', or 'will be found', rather than on an infinitive or a participle. (从句)限定的，定式的
