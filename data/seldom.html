seldom
seldəm ˈsɛldəm
ADV-BRD-NEG [ADVERB before verb] 不常;很少;难得 If something seldom happens, it happens only occasionally. 不常;很少;难得

  • They seldom speak.

    他们很少发言。

  • I've seldom felt so happy.

    我很少感觉如此幸福。

  • We were seldom at home.

    我们常不在家。

SYN rarely, occasionally, not often, once in a while
