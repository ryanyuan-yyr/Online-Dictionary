ingenious
ɪndʒiːniəs ɪnˈdʒiːnjəs ɪnˈdʒinjəs
ADJ-GRADED 巧妙的;制作精巧的;新颖独特的 Something that is ingenious is very clever and involves new ideas, methods, or equipment. 巧妙的;制作精巧的;新颖独特的

  • ...a truly ingenious invention.

    真正有创意的发明

  • Gautier's solution to the puzzle is ingenious.

    戈蒂埃破解这道难题的方法很巧妙。

SYN creative, original, brilliant, clever  
ingeniously

  • The roof has been ingeniously designed to provide solar heating.

    房顶经过巧妙设计，可提供太阳能取暖。
