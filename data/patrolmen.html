patrolman
pətroʊlmən pəˈtroʊlmən
Word forms: plural patrolmen
1 N-COUNT 巡警 A patrolman is a policeman who patrols a particular area. 巡警 [US]

  • In Petersburg, 30 patrolmen have died violently in the past five years.

    在彼得斯堡，过去5年中有30名巡警死于暴力事件。

2 N-COUNT （车友会帮助会员解决车辆故障的）巡回修理工，巡视员 A patrolman is a person employed by a motoring organization to help members of the organization when their cars break down. （车友会帮助会员解决车辆故障的）巡回修理工，巡视员 [British]

  • Mal's car had broken down en route and the AA patrolman had taken an hour to arrive.

    马尔的车在路上抛了锚，AA巡视员1个小时后才到达。
