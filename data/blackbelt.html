black belt
Word forms: plural black belts
1 N-COUNT （柔道、空手道等运动中表示段级的）黑带 A black belt is worn by someone who has reached a very high standard in a sport such as judo or karate. （柔道、空手道等运动中表示段级的）黑带

  • He holds a black belt in karate.

    他是空手道黑带级选手。

2 N-COUNT （柔道、空手道等运动中的）黑带选手，黑带大师 You can refer to someone who has a black belt in judo or karate as a black belt. （柔道、空手道等运动中的）黑带选手，黑带大师

  • Murray is a judo black belt.

    默里是柔道黑带级选手。
