paramount
pærəmaʊnt ˈpærəˌmaʊnt
ADJ 最重要的；首要的 Something that is paramount or of paramount importance is more important than anything else. 最重要的；首要的

  • The child's welfare must be seen as paramount.

    必须把儿童福利摆在首位。

  • Nitrogen is of paramount importance to life on earth.

    氮对地球上的生命至关重要。

  • ...the paramount need for a peaceful solution to the crisis.

    和平解决危机的迫切性

SYN principal, prime, first, chief
