propagandize
prɒpəgændaɪz ˌprɑpəˈgænˌdaɪz ˈprɑpəˌgænˌdaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense propagandizes, present participle propagandizing, past tense , past participle propagandized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语用 propagandise
VERB （欺骗性地）宣传，鼓吹 If you say that a group of people propagandize, you think that they are dishonestly trying to persuade other people to share their views. （欺骗性地）宣传，鼓吹 [disapproval]

  • You can propagandize just by calling attention to something. [VERB]

    你只要通过唤起人们对某事的注意就能起到宣传的作用。

  • This government shouldn't propagandize its own people. [VERB noun]

    这个政府不应鼓吹自己的民族。

SYN persuade, convince, instil, brainwash
