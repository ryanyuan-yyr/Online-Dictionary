shilly-shally ʃɪli ʃæli ˈʃɪliˌʃæli
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shilly-shallies, present participle shilly-shallying, past tense , past participle shilly-shallied
VERB [usually cont] 犹豫;踌躇不决 If you say that someone is shilly-shallying, you disapprove of the fact that they are hesitating when they should make a decision. 犹豫;踌躇不决 [informal, disapproval]

  • It's time for Brooke to stop shilly-shallying. [VERB]

    这时候布鲁克不能再犹豫不决了。
