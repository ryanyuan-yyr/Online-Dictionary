blood money
1 N-UNCOUNT （给被害人家属的）抚恤金，补偿金 If someone makes a payment of blood money to the family of someone who has been killed, they pay that person's family a sum of money as compensation. （给被害人家属的）抚恤金，补偿金

  • Defence lawyers have still not agreed to terms for payment of blood money to the victims' families.

    辩护律师仍然没有认可受害者家属补偿金支付的相关条款。

2 N-UNCOUNT （付给杀手的）血腥钱，酬金 Blood money is money that is paid to someone for murdering someone. （付给杀手的）血腥钱，酬金
