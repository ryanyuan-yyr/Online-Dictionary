PREFIX (用于某些词之前构成新词)表示“坏”，“不良”，“不当” Mal- is added to words in order to form new words which describe things that are bad or unpleasant, or that are unsuccessful or imperfect. (用于某些词之前构成新词)表示“坏”，“不良”，“不当”

  • Forty per cent of the population is suffering from malnutrition.

    人口中有40%的人营养不良。

  • The animals were seriously maltreated.

    这些动物遭到残酷的虐待。
