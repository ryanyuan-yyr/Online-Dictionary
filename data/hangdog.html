hangdog
hæŋdɒg -dɔːg ˈhæŋˌdɔg
(also hang-dog)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 面有愧色的;羞愧的;惭愧的 If you say that someone has a hangdog expression on their face, you mean that they look sad, and often guilty or ashamed. 面有愧色的;羞愧的;惭愧的

  • 'Everybody missed her,' Stan said with a hangdog look.

    “大家都想念她，”斯坦面带愧色地说。

SYN guilty, defeated, cowed, sneaking
