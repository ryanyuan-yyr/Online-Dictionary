raspy
rɑːspi ræs- ˈræspi ˈrɑspi
ADJ （声音）刺耳的，粗嘎的，嘶哑的 If someone has a raspy voice, they make rough sounds as if they have a sore throat or have difficulty in breathing. （声音）刺耳的，粗嘎的，嘶哑的 [literary]

  • Both men sang in a deep, raspy tone.

    两个男人的歌声都很低沉、沙哑。

  • Her voice was raspy with nicotine and whiskey.

    她因为抽烟喝酒声音变得嘶哑。
