descending
dɪsendɪŋ dɪˈsɛndɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] （次序）下降的，递减的 When a group of things is listed or arranged in descending order, each thing is smaller or less important than the thing before it. （次序）下降的，递减的

  • All the other ingredients, including water, have to be listed in descending order by weight.

    所有其他原料，包括水在内，都需要根据重量依次降序排列。
