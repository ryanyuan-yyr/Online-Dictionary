overweening
oʊvəʳwiːnɪŋ ˌoʊvərˈwinɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （雄心、骄傲等）过度的，过分的 If you want to emphasize your disapproval of someone's great ambition or pride, you can refer to their overweening ambition or pride. （雄心、骄傲等）过度的，过分的 [formal, disapproval]

  • 'Your modesty is a cover for your overweening conceit,' she said.

    “你表面谦虚，骨子里目中无人。”她说道。
