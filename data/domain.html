domain
doʊmeɪn dəˈmeɪn doʊˈmeɪn dəˈmeɪn
Word forms: plural domains
1 N-COUNT (尤指某人有控制力、影响力或权利的)领域，范围，范畴 A domain is a particular field of thought, activity, or interest, especially one over which someone has control, influence, or rights. (尤指某人有控制力、影响力或权利的)领域，范围，范畴 [formal]

  • ...the great experimenters in the domain of art. [+ of]

    艺术领域伟大的实验者们

  • This information should be in the public domain.

    这一消息应该为公众所知。

SYN area, world, field, department [informal]  
2 N-COUNT [usually with poss] 领土;势力范围 Someone's domain is the area they own or have control over. 领土;势力范围 [literary]

  • ...the mighty king's domain.

    势力强大的国王的领土

SYN kingdom, lands, region, territory  
3 N-COUNT (因特网上的)域 On the Internet, a domain is a set of addresses that shows, for example, the category or geographical area that an Internet address belongs to. (因特网上的)域 [computing]
