woman-to-woman
(also woman to woman)
ADJ [ADJ n] (谈话)女人间推心置腹的 If you talk about a woman-to-woman conversation, you are talking about an honest and open discussion between two women. (谈话)女人间推心置腹的

  • She had had a woman-to-woman chat with Mrs Hardie.

    她与哈迪太太进行了一番女人之间的交心之谈。

Woman to woman is also an adverb.

  • Maybe she would talk to her mother one day, woman to woman.

    也许有一天她会和她妈妈进行一次女人之间的谈心。
