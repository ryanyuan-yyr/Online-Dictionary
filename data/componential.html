component
kəmpoʊnənt kəmˈpoʊnənt
Word forms: plural components
1 N-COUNT 组成部分;成分 The components of something are the parts that it is made of. 组成部分;成分

  • Enriched uranium is a key component of a nuclear weapon. [+ of]

    浓缩铀是核武器的关键组成部分。

  • The management plan has four main components.

    管理计划有4个主要部分。

  • They were automotive component suppliers to motor manufacturers.

    他们为汽车制造商供应汽车零件。

SYN part, piece, unit, item  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 组成的;构成的 The component parts of something are the parts that make it up. 组成的;构成的

  • Gorbachev failed to keep the component parts of the Soviet Union together.

    戈尔巴乔夫没能把苏联各成员国团结在一起。

  • Polish workers will now be making component parts for Boeing 757s.

    现在波音757飞机的组成部件将由波兰工人制造。

SYN constituent, composing, inherent, intrinsic
