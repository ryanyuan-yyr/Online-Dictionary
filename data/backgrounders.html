backgrounder
bækgraʊndər ˈbækˌɡraʊndə ˈbækˌgraʊndər
Word forms: backgrounders
1 N-COUNT 报纸或杂志中提供背景资料的简介A backgrounder is a short article in a newspaper or magazine that provides background information about a particular subject. 报纸或杂志中提供背景资料的简介 [US]

  • ...a backgrounder on business prospects for the Asia-Pacific region.

    ...一篇关于泛亚洲地区商机的简报。

2 N-COUNT 记者招待会A backgrounder is a meeting in which government officials give reporters background information about an issue or policy. 记者招待会 [mainly US]

  • ...a State Department backgrounder.

    ...一次国务院记者招待会。
