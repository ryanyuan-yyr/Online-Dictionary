butt
bʌt bʌt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense butts, present participle butting, past tense , past participle butted
1 N-COUNT 屁股 Someone's butt is their bottom. 屁股 [US, informal]

  • Frieda grinned, pinching him on the butt.

    弗里达咧嘴笑了，捏了捏他的屁股。

SYN bottom [informal] , behind [informal] , bum [British , slang] , seat  
2 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] （武器或工具）较粗的一端，柄 The butt or the butt end of a weapon or tool is the thick end of its handle. （武器或工具）较粗的一端，柄

  • Troops used tear gas and rifle butts to break up the protests.

    军队使用催泪瓦斯和枪托驱散示威者。

  • Your left hand should be wrapped fairly firmly around the butt end of the club.

    你的左手应该紧紧握住棒子较粗的一端。

SYN end, handle, shaft, stock  
3 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 烟蒂；烟头 The butt of a cigarette or cigar is the small part of it that is left when someone has finished smoking it. 烟蒂；烟头

  • He dropped his cigarette butt into the street below.

    他把烟头扔到下面的街道上。

  • He paused to stub out the butt of his cigar.

    他停下来，踩灭了手中的烟头。

SYN stub, end, base, foot  
4 N-COUNT （装液体用的）大桶 A butt is a large barrel used for collecting or storing liquid. （装液体用的）大桶
5 N-SING （嘲笑或批评的）对象，笑柄 If someone or something is the butt of jokes or criticism, people often make fun of them or criticize them. （嘲笑或批评的）对象，笑柄

  • He is still the butt of cruel jokes about his humble origins. [+ of]

    他仍然因出身低微而被人无情嘲笑。

SYN target, victim, object, point  
6 VERB （用头）顶，撞 If a person or animal butts you, they hit you with the top of their head. （用头）顶，撞 [Also VERB n preposition]

  • Lawrence kept on butting me but the referee did not warn him. [VERB noun]

    劳伦斯不停地用头撞我，但是裁判并没有警告他。

SYN knock, push, bump, punch
