nodule
nɒdjuːl -dʒuːl ˈnɑdʒul
Word forms: plural nodules
1 N-COUNT 小结节;小瘤 A nodule is a small round lump that can appear on your body and is a sign of an illness. 小结节;小瘤 [medicine]

  • In a typical case, there is a small inflamed nodule just under the skin.

    典型病例中，皮肤下面会有一个发炎的小结节。

2 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (植物的)根瘤，根结 A nodule is a small round lump which is found on the roots of certain plants. (植物的)根瘤，根结

  • ...bacteria that live in root nodules on certain plants.

    寄居在某些植物根结里的细菌
