privatize
praɪvətaɪz ˈpraɪvəˌtaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense privatizes, present participle privatizing, past tense , past participle privatized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 privatise
VERB 使私有化；使归私有 If a company, industry, or service that is owned by the state is privatized, the government sells it and makes it a private company. 使私有化；使归私有 [business]

  • The water boards are about to be privatized. [be VERB-ed]

    水务委员会将实行私有化。

  • ...a pledge to privatise the rail and coal industries. [VERB noun]

    将铁路和煤炭工业私有化的保证

  • ...the newly privatized FM radio stations. [VERB-ed]

    新转为私有化的调频广播电台

privatization

  • ...the privatisation of British Rail.

    英国铁路的私有化

  • ...fresh rules governing the conduct of future privatizations.

    制约未来私有化行为的新规定
