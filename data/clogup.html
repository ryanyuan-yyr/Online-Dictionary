clog up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）阻塞;（使）堵塞 When something clogs up a place, or when it clogs up, it becomes blocked so that little or nothing can pass through. （使）阻塞;（使）堵塞

  • 22,000 tourists were clogging up the pavements. [VERB PREP. noun]

    2.2万名游客将人行道堵得水泄不通。

  • The result is that the lungs clog up with a thick mucus. [VERB PREP.]

    检验结果是肺部被厚厚的黏液堵住了。
