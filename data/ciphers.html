cipher
saɪfəʳ ˈsaɪfər
(also cypher)Word forms: plural ciphers
1 N-COUNT 密码;暗号 A cipher is a secret system of writing that you use to send messages. 密码;暗号

  • ...converting their messages into ciphers.

    把他们的信息转换成密码

  • ...a cipher clerk.

    译电员

2 N-COUNT 无足轻重的人；走狗;小兵小卒 If you describe someone as a cipher, you mean that they have no power and are used by other people to achieve a particular purpose. 无足轻重的人；走狗;小兵小卒

  • He was little more than a cipher who faithfully carried out the Fuehrer's commands.

    他只不过是个走狗，忠实执行元首的命令。

SYN nobody, nonentity, non-person
