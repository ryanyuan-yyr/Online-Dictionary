uplifting
ʌplɪftɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 振奋人心的；鼓舞人心的；令人兴奋不已的 You describe something as uplifting when it makes you feel very cheerful and happy. 振奋人心的；鼓舞人心的；令人兴奋不已的

  • ...a charming and uplifting love story.

    引人入胜的、令人振奋的爱情故事

  • I like a film to be uplifting.

    我喜欢振奋人心的电影。

SYN inspiring, moving, touching, affecting
