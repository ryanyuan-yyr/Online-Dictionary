nail down
1 PHRASAL VERB 弄清;确定 If you nail down something unknown or uncertain, you find out exactly what it is. 弄清;确定 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • It would be useful if you could nail down the source of this tension. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果你能弄清这种紧张的根源，将会大有益处。

2 PHRASAL VERB 确定(协议) If you nail down an agreement, you manage to reach a firm agreement with a definite result. 确定(协议) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The Secretary of State and his Russian counterpart met to try to nail down the elusive accord. [VERB PREP. noun]

    美国国务卿和俄罗斯外交部长进行了会晤，试图敲定这项难以达成的协议。

3 PHRASAL VERB 钉牢;钉住 If you nail something down, you fix it firmly onto something. 钉牢;钉住 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Lay strips of 4mm ply over the mesh and nail these down with panel pins. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把4毫米厚的胶合板条铺在网子上，然后用镶板钉钉牢。
