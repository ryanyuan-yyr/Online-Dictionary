swanky
swæŋki ˈswæŋki
Word forms: comparative swankier, superlative swankiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 时髦的;奢华的 If you describe something as swanky, you mean that it is fashionable and expensive. 时髦的;奢华的 [informal]

  • ...one of the swanky hotels that line the Pacific shore at Acapulco.

    坐落在太平洋沿岸的阿卡普尔科的豪华酒店之一

SYN ostentatious, grand, posh [informal , mainly British] , rich
