free fall
(also free-fall)Word forms: plural free falls
1 N-VAR [oft into/in N] (价值或价格的)直线下落，暴跌 If the value or price of something goes into free fall, it starts to fall uncontrollably. (价值或价格的)直线下落，暴跌 [journalism]

  • Sterling went into free fall.

    英镑暴跌。

  • The price did a free fall.

    价格一落千丈。

2 N-UNCOUNT (降落伞张开前的)自由下落 In parachuting, free fall is the part of the jump before the parachute opens. (降落伞张开前的)自由下落
