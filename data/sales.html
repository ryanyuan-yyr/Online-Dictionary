sale
seɪl seɪl
Word forms: plural sales
1 N-SING 卖;销售 The sale of goods is the act of selling them for money. 卖;销售

  • Efforts were made to limit the sale of alcohol. [+ of]

    过去曾采取措施限制酒的销售。

  • ...a proposed arms sale to Saudi Arabia. [+ to]

    拟议对沙特阿拉伯出售武器

  • He had never intended living there after his wife's death and immediately set about trying to make a sale.

    妻子去世后他就不打算住在那里了，因此马上着手要将其卖掉。

SYN selling, marketing, dealing, trading  
2 N-PLURAL 销售额;销售量 The sales of a product are the quantity of it that is sold. 销售额;销售量

  • The newspaper has sales of 1.72 million. [+ of]

    该报纸有 172 万份的销量。

  • ...the huge Christmas sales of computer games.

    电脑游戏在圣诞期间的巨额销量

  • ...retail sales figures.

    零售额

3 N-PLURAL (公司产品的)销售工作 The part of a company that deals with sales deals with selling the company's products. (公司产品的)销售工作

  • Until 1983 he worked in sales and marketing.

    1983 年之前，他一直在市场营销部工作。

  • She was their Dusseldorf sales manager.

    她是他们驻杜塞尔多夫的销售部经理。

4 N-COUNT 减价出售;大减价;贱卖 A sale is an occasion when a shop sells things at less than their normal price. 减价出售;大减价;贱卖

  • ...a pair of jeans bought half-price in a sale.

    打折时半价买的一条牛仔裤

  • Many stores have started their January sales a month early.

    许多商店提前一个月就开始了一月份的特价促销活动。

5 N-COUNT 拍卖;拍卖会 A sale is an event when goods are sold to the person who offers the highest price. 拍卖;拍卖会

  • The painting was bought by dealers at the Christie's sale.

    这幅油画在佳士得的拍卖会上被交易商拍走。

SYN auction, fair, mart, bazaar  
6 See also: car boot sale  jumble sale 
7 PHRASE 供出售;待售 If something is for sale, it is being offered to people to buy. 供出售;待售

  • His former home is for sale at £495,000. [+ at]

    他的旧居要以 49.5 万英镑的价格出售。

  • There was a Leica camera for sale in the window.

    橱窗里有架莱卡相机待售。

  • The company is not for sale.

    该公司不准备出售。

SYN available to buy, on sale, on offer, on the market  
8 PHRASE 出售;上市 Products that are on sale can be bought in shops. 出售;上市 [mainly British] [mainly British]

  • English textbooks and dictionaries are on sale everywhere.

    英语教材和词典到处都有卖的。

  • All tickets go on sale this Friday.

    所有票在本星期五开始销售。

9 PHRASE 折价销售;减价出售 If products in a shop are on sale, they can be bought for less than their normal price. 折价销售;减价出售

  • He bought a sports jacket on sale at Gowings Men's Store.

    他在高英斯男装店买了一件打折的运动上衣。

10 PHRASE 供出售;待售;转让 If a property or company is up for sale, its owner is trying to sell it. 供出售;待售;转让

  • The castle has been put up for sale.

    该城堡已经对外出售。
