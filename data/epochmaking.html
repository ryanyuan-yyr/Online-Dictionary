epoch-making
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 划时代的;开创新纪元的 An epoch-making change or declaration is considered to be the extremely important because it is likely to have a significant effect on a particular period of time. 划时代的;开创新纪元的

  • It was meant to sound like an epoch-making declaration.

    有意让它听起来像是一个划时代的宣言。

  • ...the epoch-making changes now taking place in Eastern Europe.

    当下正在东欧发生的划时代的变革
