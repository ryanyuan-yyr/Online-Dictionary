question tag ˈkwɛstʃən tæɡ
Word forms: plural question tags
N-COUNT 疑问尾句；附加疑问句 In grammar, a question tag is a very short clause at the end of a statement which changes the statement into a question. For example, in 'She said half price, didn't she?', the words 'didn't she' are a question tag. 疑问尾句；附加疑问句
