necklace
neklɪs ˈnɛklɪs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural necklaces, present participle necklacing, past tense , past participle necklaced
1 N-COUNT 项圈;项链 A necklace is a piece of jewellery such as a chain or a string of beads which someone, usually a woman, wears round their neck. 项圈;项链

  • ...a diamond necklace and matching earrings.

    钻石项链和与之相配的耳环

SYN chain, beads, pendant, choker  
2 VERB 给…戴火项链(指将淋了汽油的轮胎套在某人脖子上并点燃的私刑) To necklace someone means to kill them by putting a tyre soaked in petrol around their neck and then setting fire to it. 给…戴火项链(指将淋了汽油的轮胎套在某人脖子上并点燃的私刑) [Also VERB n]

  • Alleged strike breakers had their houses petrol-bombed or were hacked to death or necklaced. [be VERB-ed]

    破坏罢工的嫌疑人，其房屋遭汽油弹炸毁，人被砍死或被套上火项链烧死。
