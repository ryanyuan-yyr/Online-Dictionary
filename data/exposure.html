exposure
ɪkspoʊʒəʳ ɪkˈspəʊʒə ɛkˈspoʊʒər ɪkˈspoʊʒər
Word forms: plural exposures
1 N-UNCOUNT 暴露;接触 Exposure to something dangerous means being in a situation where it might affect you. 暴露;接触

  • Exposure to lead is known to damage the brains of young children. [+ to]

    众所周知，接触铅对幼儿的大脑有害。

  • ...the potential exposure of people to nuclear waste.

    核废料对人们造成辐射的潜在风险

SYN vulnerability, subjection, susceptibility, laying open  
2 N-UNCOUNT （身体）受冻 Exposure is the harmful effect on your body caused by very cold weather. （身体）受冻

  • He was suffering from exposure and shock but his condition was said to be stable.

    他被冻伤了，而且出现了休克，但是据说情况稳定。

  • At least two people died of exposure in Chicago overnight.

    昨天晚上，芝加哥至少有两人冻死。

SYN hypothermia, frostbite, extreme cold, intense cold  
3 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 曝光;揭发;揭露 The exposure of a well-known person is the revealing of the fact that they are bad or immoral in some way. 曝光;揭发;揭露

  • ...the exposure of Anthony Blunt as a former Soviet spy. [+ of]

    安东尼·布伦特前苏联间谍身份的曝光

  • Their sporting reputation has suffered enormously from Johnson's exposure.

    由于约翰逊东窗事发，他们的体育声誉受到了严重损害。

SYN revelation, exposé, uncovering, disclosure  
4 N-UNCOUNT 报道；宣传 Exposure is publicity that a person, company, or product receives. 报道；宣传

  • All the candidates have been getting an enormous amount of exposure on television and in the press.

    所有候选人都在电视和报刊上频频亮相。

SYN publicity, promotion, attention, advertising  
5 N-COUNT 单张胶片 In photography, an exposure is a single photograph. 单张胶片 [technical]

  • Larger drawings tend to require two or three exposures to cover them.

    较大的图画往往需要两三张胶片才能拍下来。

6 N-VAR 曝光量 In photography, the exposure is the amount of light that is allowed to enter a camera when taking a photograph. 曝光量 [technical]

  • A tripod also lets you shoot long exposures at night.

    三脚架也可以让你在晚上长时间曝光。

  • ...an exposure of 1/18sec at f/11.

    1/18秒f/11光圈的曝光量

  • Against a deep blue sky or dark storm-clouds, you may need to reduce the exposure.

    拍摄背景为湛蓝的天空或者乌黑的暴风云时，可能需要减少曝光量。
