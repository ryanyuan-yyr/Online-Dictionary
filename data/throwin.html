throw in
1 PHRASAL VERB 无意中说;随口说 If you throw in a remark when having a conversation, you add it in a casual or unexpected way. 无意中说;随口说 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Occasionally Farling threw in a question. [VERB PREP. noun]

    法林有时会突然冒出个问题。

2 PHRASAL VERB 免费赠送;额外奉送 If a person who is selling something throws in something extra, they give you the extra thing and only ask you to pay for the first thing. 免费赠送;额外奉送 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Pay £4.80 for larger prints and they throw in a free photo album. [VERB PREP. noun]

    付 4.8 英镑冲印更大尺寸的照片就会获赠一本相册。

  • They were offering me a weekend break in Paris — with free beer thrown in. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    他们让我去巴黎休息一个周末—外加免费啤酒。
