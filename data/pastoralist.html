pastoral
pɑːstərəl pæst- ˈpæstərəl ˈpɑstərəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 牧师的；牧灵的；教牧的 The pastoral duties of a priest or other religious leader involve looking after the people he or she has responsibility for, especially by helping them with their personal problems. 牧师的；牧灵的；教牧的

  • ...the pastoral care of the sick.

    牧师对病人的关怀

  • Many churches provide excellent pastoral counselling.

    很多教堂提供出色的牧灵辅导。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （教师对学生）生活辅导的 If a school offers pastoral care, it is concerned with the personal needs and problems of its pupils, not just with their schoolwork. （教师对学生）生活辅导的 [mainly British]

  • A few schools now offer counselling sessions; all have some system of pastoral care.

    一些学校现在开设咨询课；所有学校都有某种教导关怀体制。

3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 田园式的 A pastoral place, atmosphere, or idea is characteristic of peaceful country life and scenery. 田园式的

  • ...the pastoral beauty of a park.

    公园里的田园式美景

  • ...a tranquil pastoral scene.

    恬静的田园风光

  • ...the pastoral joys of rural districts.

    农村地区的田园乐趣

SYN rustic, country, simple, rural  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] 畜牧的；放牧的 A pastoral way of life is one in which people keep animals such as cows and sheep that feed off the land. 畜牧的；放牧的

  • ...the Israelites, who were a pastoral people.

    曾经是游牧民族的以色列人

  • ...a system of pastoral farming.

    畜牧体系
