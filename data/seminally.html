seminal
semɪnəl ˈsɛmɪnəl ˈsɛmənəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (书、作品等在某一领域)有重大影响的，影响深远的 Seminal is used to describe things such as books, works, events, and experiences that have a great influence in a particular field. (书、作品等在某一领域)有重大影响的，影响深远的 [formal]

  • ...author of the seminal book 'Animal Liberation'.

    有着巨大影响的《动物解放》一书的作者

  • The reforms have been a seminal event in the history of the NHS.

    这些改革已成为英国国民保健制度史上影响深远的一件大事。

SYN influential, important, ground-breaking, original
