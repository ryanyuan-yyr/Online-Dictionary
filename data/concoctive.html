concoct
kənkɒkt kənˈkɑkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense concocts, present participle concocting, past tense , past participle concocted
1 VERB 编造;捏造 If you concoct an excuse or explanation, you invent one that is not true. 编造;捏造

  • Mr Ferguson said the prisoner concocted the story to get a lighter sentence. [VERB noun]

    弗格森先生说犯人捏造了这件事儿以求轻判。

SYN make up, design, prepare, manufacture  
2 VERB 调制，调配(尤指不寻常之物) If you concoct something, especially something unusual, you make it by mixing several things together. 调制，调配(尤指不寻常之物)

  • Eugene was concocting Rossini Cocktails from champagne and pureed raspberries. [VERB noun]

    尤金当时正在用香槟和覆盆子酱调配罗西尼鸡尾酒。

  • ...a specially concocted massage oil. [VERB-ed]

    特制按摩精油
