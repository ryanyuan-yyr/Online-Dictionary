belted
beltɪd ˈbɛltɪd
ADJ 有腰带的；系腰带的 If someone's jacket or coat, for example, is belted, it has a belt fastened round it. 有腰带的；系腰带的

  • He wore a belted raincoat that accentuated his portly middle.

    他穿着一件系腰带的雨衣，使他发福的腰身更加显眼。

  • She wore a brown suede jacket, belted at the waist.

    她穿着一件带束腰的棕色绒面革夹克。
