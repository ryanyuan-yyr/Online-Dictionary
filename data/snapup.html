snap up
PHRASAL VERB 抢购，赶紧买下(便宜或心仪之物) If you snap something up, you buy it quickly because it is cheap or is just what you want. 抢购，赶紧买下(便宜或心仪之物)

  • Every time we get a new delivery of clothes, people are queuing to snap them up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    每次新到一批服装时，人们都在排起长队来抢购。

  • One eagle-eyed collector snapped up a pair of Schiaparelli earrings for just £6. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一个眼疾手快的收藏家以仅仅 6 英镑的价钱抢到了一副斯基亚帕雷利设计的耳环。

SYN grab, seize, take advantage of, swoop down on
