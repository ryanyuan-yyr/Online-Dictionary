devolve
dɪvɒlv dɪˈvɑlv dɪˈvɔlv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense devolves, present participle devolving, past tense , past participle devolved
V-ERG （使）(权力、职责等)下放;（使） 移交 If you devolve power, authority, or responsibility to a less powerful person or group, or if it devolves upon them, it is transferred to them. （使）(权力、职责等)下放;（使） 移交

  • ...the need to decentralize and devolve power to regional governments. [VERB noun + to]

    将权力分散并下放给地方政府的需要

  • We have made a conscious effort to devolve responsibility. [VERB noun]

    我们已有意识地下放职责。

  • A large portion of this cost devolves upon the patient. [V + upon/on]

    这笔费用的很大一部分落在病人身上。

SYN transfer, surrender, pass on, transmit
