scattergun skætəʳgʌn
(also scatter-gun)Word forms: plural scatterguns
1 N-COUNT 机关枪 A scattergun is a gun that fires a lot of small metal balls at the same time. 机关枪 [US]
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 同 scattershot Scattergun means the same as scattershot . 同 scattershot

  • They advocated a scattergun approach of posting dozens of letters.

    他们主张采取向四处随意邮寄大量信件的方式。
