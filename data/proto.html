proto- proʊtoʊ- ˈproʊtoʊ ˈproʊtə
PREFIX （用于构成形容词和名词）表示“最初的”，“原始的” Proto- is used to form adjectives and nouns which indicate that something is in the early stages of its development. （用于构成形容词和名词）表示“最初的”，“原始的”

  • ...the proto-fascist tendencies of some of its supporters.

    一些其支持者最初的法西斯倾向

  • ...Albion, whose own legend stretches back to the mists of proto-history.

    阿尔比恩，关于其本身的传说可追溯到早期的茫茫历史中
