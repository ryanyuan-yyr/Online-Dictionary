self-interested
ADJ-GRADED 自私的;只为自己着想的 If you describe someone as self-interested, you disapprove of them because they always want to do what is best for themselves rather than for other people. 自私的;只为自己着想的 [disapproval]

  • Narrowly self-interested behaviour is ultimately self-defeating.

    狭隘自私的行为最终会自拆台脚。

SYN selfish, self-centred, egotistical, greedy
