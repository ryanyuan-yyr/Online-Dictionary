plain-spoken
ˌpleɪnˈspəʊkən ˈpleɪnˈspoʊkən
(also plainspoken)
ADJ-GRADED 直言不讳的；直截了当的 If you say that someone is plain-spoken, you mean that they say exactly what they think, even when they know that what they say may not please other people. 直言不讳的；直截了当的 [approval]

  • ...a plain-spoken American full of scorn for pomp and pretense.

    看不惯炫耀和虚伪、直言不讳的一个美国人

SYN blunt, direct, frank, straightforward
