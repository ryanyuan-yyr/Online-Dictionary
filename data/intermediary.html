intermediary
ɪntəʳmiːdiəri ˌɪntərˈmidiˌɛri ˌɪntərˈmidiəri
Word forms: plural intermediaries
1 N-COUNT 调解人;中间人 An intermediary is a person who passes messages or proposals between two people or groups. 调解人;中间人

  • She wanted him to act as an intermediary in the dispute with Moscow.

    她想让他充当中间人来调解与莫斯科的争端。

SYN mediator, agent, middleman, broker
