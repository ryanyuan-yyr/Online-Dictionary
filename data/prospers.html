prosper
prɒspəʳ ˈprɑspər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense prospers, present participle prospering, past tense , past participle prospered
VERB 兴旺；繁荣；昌盛；成功 If people or businesses prosper, they are successful and do well. 兴旺；繁荣；昌盛；成功 [formal]

  • The high street banks continue to prosper. [VERB]

    商业街上的银行仍旧一派繁荣景象。

  • His teams have always prospered in cup competitions. [VERB]

    他的队伍在杯赛上总是成绩不俗。

SYN succeed, advance, progress, thrive
