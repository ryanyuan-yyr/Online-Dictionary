interactive
ɪntəræktɪv ˌɪntərˈæktɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 交互的;人机对话的 An interactive computer program or television system is one which allows direct communication between the user and the machine. 交互的;人机对话的

  • This will make videogames more interactive than ever.

    这将使电子游戏的互动性更胜以往。

interactivity

  • ...cable broadcast companies that offer interactivity.

    提供交互服务的有线广播公司

2 ADJ-GRADED 互动的;互相交流的 If you describe a group of people or their activities as interactive, you mean that the people communicate with each other. 互动的;互相交流的

  • ...flexible, interactive teaching in the classroom.

    课堂上灵活互动的教学
