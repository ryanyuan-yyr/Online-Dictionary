unsatisfactory
ʌnsætɪsfæktəri ˌʌnsætɪsˈfæktərɪ
1 ADJ-GRADED 不够好的；不能令人满意的；不能接受的 If you describe something as unsatisfactory, you mean that it is not as good as it should be, and cannot be considered acceptable. 不够好的；不能令人满意的；不能接受的

  • The inspectors said just under a third of lessons were unsatisfactory.

    督察员说只有不到1/3的课程不够好。

  • He asked a few more questions, to which he received unsatisfactory answers.

    他又问了几个问题，得到的答案都不令人满意。

SYN not good enough, inadequate, unacceptable, poor
