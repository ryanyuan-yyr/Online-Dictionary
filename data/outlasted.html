outlast
aʊtlɑːst -læst ˈaʊtˌlɑːst aʊtˈlæst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outlasts, present participle outlasting, past tense , past participle outlasted
VERB [no passive] 比…活得长；比…持久 If one thing outlasts another thing, the first thing lives or exists longer than the second. 比…活得长；比…持久

  • These naturally dried flowers will outlast a bouquet of fresh blooms. [VERB noun]

    这些自然风干的花会比一束鲜花更加持久。

  • People should listen to his music —it will outlast us all. [VERB noun]

    人们应该听听他的音乐—它会是超越时间的经典。

SYN outlive, survive, live after, outstay
