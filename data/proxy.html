proxy
prɒksi ˈprɑksi
Word forms: plural proxies
1 N-UNCOUNT [usu by N] 代表；代替 If you do something by proxy, you arrange for someone else to do it for you. 代表；代替

  • Those not attending the meeting may vote by proxy.

    那些未出席会议的可以请他人代为投票。

2 N-COUNT [usu N for n] 代理人；代替者；替代物 A proxy is a person or thing that is acting or being used in the place of someone or something else. 代理人；代替者；替代物

  • Price differences are used as a proxy for differences in quality.

    一分钱一分货。
