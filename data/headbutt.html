head-butt
(also headbutt)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense head-butts, present participle head-butting, past tense , past participle head-butted
VERB 用头撞 If someone head-butts you, they hit you with the top of their head. 用头撞

  • He was said to have head-butted one policeman and stamped on another's hand. [VERB noun]

    据说，他用头撞了一名警察，还踩了另一名警察的手。

Head-butt is also a noun.

  • The cuts on Colin's head could only have been made by head-butts.

    科林头上的那些伤口只能是用头撞击时造成的。
