twentieth
twentiəθ ˈtwɛntiɪθ ˈtwɛniɪθ ˈtwʌniɪθ
Word forms: plural twentieths
1 ORD 第二十 The twentieth item in a series is the one that you count as number twenty. 第二十

  • ...the twentieth century.

    20 世纪

2 FRACTION 二十分之一 A twentieth is one of twenty equal parts of something. 二十分之一

  • A few twentieths of a gram can be critical.

    即使重量仅有1克的二十分之几都可能是关键性的。
