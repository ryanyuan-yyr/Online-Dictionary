sap
sæp sæp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense saps, present participle sapping, past tense , past participle sapped
1 VERB 消耗(精力);削弱(信心) If something saps your strength or confidence, it gradually weakens or destroys it. 消耗(精力);削弱(信心)

  • I was afraid the sickness had sapped my strength. [VERB noun]

    我担心患病已让我元气大伤。

  • Analysts say the recession in Japan has sapped investor confidence. [VERB noun]

    分析家称日本的经济衰退已经打击了投资者的信心。

SYN weaken, drain, undermine, rob  
2 N-UNCOUNT (植物的)液，汁 Sap is the watery liquid in plants and trees. (植物的)液，汁

  • The leaves, bark and sap are also common ingredients of local herbal remedies.

    树叶、树皮和树汁也是当地草药的常用药材。

SYN juice, essence, vital fluid, secretion  
3 N-COUNT 笨蛋;傻瓜 If you describe someone as a sap, you think they are foolish. 笨蛋;傻瓜 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...her poor sap of a husband.

    她可怜的傻瓜丈夫

SYN fool, jerk [slang , mainly US , Canadian] , idiot, noodle
