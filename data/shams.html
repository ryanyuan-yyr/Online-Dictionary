sham
ʃæm ʃæm
Word forms: plural shams
N-COUNT [usually singular] 假象;虚伪;虚假 Something that is a sham is not real or is not really what it seems to be. 假象;虚伪;虚假 [disapproval]

  • The government's promises were exposed as a hollow sham.

    实情被揭穿了:政府的承诺原来是空洞的谎言。

  • Many of the world's leaders have already denounced this election as a sham.

    许多国家的领导人已经指责这次大选弄虚作假。

  • ...sham marriages.

    假结婚

SYN fraud [informal] , imitation, hoax, pretence
