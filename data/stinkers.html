stinker
stɪŋkəʳ ˈstɪŋkər
Word forms: plural stinkers
N-COUNT 令人讨厌的人;恶劣之事;坏蛋 If you describe someone or something as a stinker, you mean that you think they are very unpleasant or bad. 令人讨厌的人;恶劣之事;坏蛋 [informal, disapproval]

  • I think he's an absolute stinker to do that to her.

    我认为他那样对待她真太缺德了。

  • I thought it was a right stinker.

    我认为这事太恶劣了。

SYN scoundrel [old-fashioned] , heel [slang] , sod [slang] , cad [British , informal]
