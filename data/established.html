established
ɪstæblɪʃt ɪˈstæblɪʃt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 已被认可的;已被接受的 If you use established to describe something such as an organization, you mean that it is officially recognized or generally approved of because it has existed for a long time. 已被认可的;已被接受的

  • Their religious adherence is not to the established church.

    他们信仰的不是国教。

  • ...qualified lawyers with established and prestigious business addresses.

    在一些颇有声望的老牌事务所工作的资深律师们

  • ...the established names of Paris fashion.

    巴黎时装界的知名品牌

SYN accepted, traditional, conventional, historic
