elbow room
ˈɛlbəʊ rʊm
1 N-UNCOUNT 行动自由；活动空间 Elbow room is the freedom to do what you want to do or need to do in a particular situation. 行动自由；活动空间 [informal]

  • His speech was designed to give himself more political elbow room.

    他的演讲旨在给自己更多的政治空间。

SYN scope, room, space, freedom  
2 N-UNCOUNT 宽裕的空间;充足的活动空间 If there is enough elbow room in a place or vehicle, it is not too small or too crowded. 宽裕的空间;充足的活动空间 [informal]

  • There was not much elbow room in the cockpit of a Snipe.

    “沙锥鸟”战斗机的驾驶舱空间不太宽敞。
