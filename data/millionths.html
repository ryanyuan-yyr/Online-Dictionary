millionth
mɪliənθ ˈmɪljənθ
Word forms: plural millionths
1 ORD 第一百万 The millionth item in a series is the one you count as number one million. 第一百万

  • Last year the millionth truck rolled off the assembly line.

    去年，第100万台卡车驶下流水线。

2 FRACTION 百万分之一 A millionth of something is one of a million equal parts of it. 百万分之一

  • The bomb must explode within less than a millionth of a second. [+ of]

    炸弹必须在不到百万分之一秒的时间内爆炸。
