distribute
dɪstrɪbjuːt dɪˈstrɪbjuːt dɪˈstrɪbjut dɪˈstrɪbjʊt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense distributes, present participle distributing, past tense , past participle distributed
1 VERB 分发;分配;分送 If you distribute things, you hand them or deliver them to a number of people. 分发;分配;分送

  • Students shouted slogans and distributed leaflets. [VERB noun]

    学生们喊着口号，分发着传单。

  • Thousands of soldiers are working to distribute food and blankets to the refugees. [VERB noun to noun]

    数以千计的士兵正在给难民们分发食物和毯子。

  • In the move most of the furniture was left to the neighbours or distributed among friends. [be V-ed + among]

    大部分家具在搬家的时候都留给了邻居或送了朋友。

SYN hand out, dispense, give out, dish out [informal]  
2 VERB 配销;运销;分销 When a company distributes goods, it supplies them to the shops or businesses that sell them. 配销;运销;分销 [business]

  • We didn't understand how difficult it was to distribute a national paper. [VERB noun]

    我们不了解分销一份全国发行的报纸有多难。

3 VERB （在成员中）分配，分派 If you distribute things among the members of a group, you share them among those members. （在成员中）分配，分派 [Also VERB n]

  • Immediately after his election he began to distribute major offices among his friends and supporters. [V n + among]

    他当选后立即着手将要职分派给他的朋友和支持者们。

SYN share, give, deal, divide  
4 VERB 撒;播 To distribute a substance over something means to scatter it over it. 撒;播 [formal]

  • Distribute the topping evenly over the fruit. [V n + over]

    将配料均匀地撒在水果上。
