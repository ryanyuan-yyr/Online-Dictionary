recorder
rɪkɔːʳdəʳ rɪˈkɔrdər
Word forms: plural recorders
1 N-COUNT （卡式或磁带）录音机;录像机 You can refer to a cassette recorder, a tape recorder, or a video recorder as a recorder. （卡式或磁带）录音机;录像机

  • Rodney put the recorder on the desk top and pushed the play button.

    罗德尼把录音机放在桌面上，按下了播放键。

SYN tape recorder, video, video recorder, cassette recorder  
2See also:cassette recorder  tape recorder  video recorder 
3 N-VAR 直笛；竖笛 A recorder is a wooden or plastic musical instrument in the shape of a pipe. You play the recorder by blowing into the top of it and covering and uncovering the holes with your fingers. 直笛；竖笛
4 N-COUNT; N-TITLE 刑事法院兼职法官（英格兰和威尔士的法律体制下被任命在刑事法庭兼任法官的律师） In the legal system of England and Wales, a recorder is a lawyer who is appointed as a part-time judge in the Crown Court. 刑事法院兼职法官（英格兰和威尔士的法律体制下被任命在刑事法庭兼任法官的律师）
5 N-COUNT 记录装置;记录器 A recorder is a machine or instrument that keeps a record of something, for example in an experiment or on a vehicle. 记录装置;记录器

  • Data recorders also pinpoint mechanical faults rapidly, reducing repair times.

    数据记录仪还能迅速查明机械故障，减少维修次数。

6See also:flight recorder
