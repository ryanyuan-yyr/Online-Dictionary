daytime
deɪtaɪm ˈdeɪˌtaɪm
1 N-SING 白天;日间 The daytime is the part of a day between the time when it gets light and the time when it gets dark. 白天;日间

  • In the daytime he stayed up in his room, sleeping, or listening to music.

    他白天呆在自己的屋子里睡觉或听音乐。

  • Please give a daytime telephone number.

    请留一个日间的电话号码。

  • Even at the end of the war, German factories mainly operated only in daytime.

    即使在战争末期，德国工厂也基本上只在白天开工。

SYN day, daylight, waking hours, daylight hours  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (电视或广播节目周一至周五)白天播出的，日间播放的 Daytime television and radio is broadcast during the morning and afternoon on weekdays. (电视或广播节目周一至周五)白天播出的，日间播放的

  • ...ITV's new package of daytime programmes.

    独立电视台的新一整套日间档节目
