blood lust
(also blood-lust)
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 嗜血；杀戮欲 If you say that someone is driven by a blood lust, you mean that they are acting in an extremely violent way because their emotions have been aroused by the events around them. 嗜血；杀戮欲

  • The mobs became driven by a crazed blood-lust to take the city.

    杀红了眼的暴徒们意欲占领这座城市。
