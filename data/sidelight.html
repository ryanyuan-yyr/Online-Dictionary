sidelight
saɪdlaɪt ˈsaɪdˌlaɪt
Word forms: plural sidelights
1 N-COUNT (车辆前面的)位置灯，边灯 The sidelights on a vehicle are the small lights at the front that help other drivers to notice the vehicle and to judge its width. (车辆前面的)位置灯，边灯 [British]
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 parking lights
2 N-COUNT (车辆两侧的)侧灯 The sidelights on a vehicle are lights on its sides. (车辆两侧的)侧灯 [US]
3 N-COUNT (有关某情况的)侧面消息，趣闻，附带信息 A sidelight on a particular situation is a piece of information about that situation which is interesting but which is not particularly important. (有关某情况的)侧面消息，趣闻，附带信息

  • The book is full of amusing sidelights on his family background. [+ on]
