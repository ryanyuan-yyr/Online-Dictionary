pure
pjʊəʳ pjʊr
Word forms: comparative purer, superlative purest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 纯粹的；不掺杂的 A pure substance is not mixed with anything else. 纯粹的；不掺杂的

  • ...a carton of pure orange juice.

    一盒纯橙汁

2 ADJ-GRADED 纯净的；洁净的；不含有害物质的 Something that is pure is clean and does not contain any harmful substances. 纯净的；洁净的；不含有害物质的

  • In remote regions, the air is pure and the crops are free of poisonous insecticides.

    在偏远地区，空气很纯净，庄稼也不施用有毒的杀虫剂。

  • ...demands for purer and cleaner river water.

    需要更纯净、更清洁的河水

SYN clean, immaculate, sterile, wholesome  
purity

  • They worried about the purity of tap water.

    他们担心自来水是否纯净。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 纯净的；纯正的；完美的 If you describe something such as a colour, a sound, or a type of light as pure, you mean that it is very clear and represents a perfect example of its type. 纯净的；纯正的；完美的

  • ...flowers in a whole range of blues with the occasional pure white.

    各种深深浅浅的蓝色花朵，偶尔有纯白色花朵点缀其间

purity

  • The soaring purity of her voice conjured up the frozen bleakness of the Far North.

    她高昂纯净的嗓音使人想起极北地区滴水成冰的寒冷与清冽。

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 纯粹的；标准的；真正的 If you describe a form of art or a philosophy as pure, you mean that it is produced or practised according to a standard or form that is expected of it. 纯粹的；标准的；真正的 [formal]

  • Nicholson never swerved from his aim of making pure and simple art.

    尼科尔森始终坚持创作纯粹简单的艺术，从未动摇过。

purity

  • ...verse of great purity, sonority of rhythm, and symphonic form.

    节奏铿锵有力、气势恢宏、地道纯正的诗句

5 ADJ [ADJ n] （科学或研究）纯理论的，抽象的 Pure science or pure research is concerned only with theory and not with how this theory can be used in practical ways. （科学或研究）纯理论的，抽象的

  • Physics isn't just about pure science with no immediate applications.

    物理学并非只是一门不能直接运用的纯科学。

  • They did not approach their subject solely as a matter of 'pure' theory.

    他们并没有把他们的学科完全当作一门“纯”理论学科来对待。

SYN theoretical, abstract, philosophical, speculative  
6 ADJ-GRADED 完全的；十足的 Pure means complete and total. 完全的；十足的 [emphasis]

  • The old man turned to give her a look of pure surprise.

    老人转身十分惊奇地看了她一眼。

  • To sleep on my own and not hear the boys snore or grunt was pure bliss.

    自己一个人睡、不用听男孩们的鼾声或咕哝声简直是幸福极了。

SYN complete, total, perfect, absolute  
7 ADJ-GRADED 纯洁的；清白的；贞洁的 A person, especially a woman, who is described as pure is considered to be morally good, especially because they have no sexual experience or sexual thoughts. 纯洁的；清白的；贞洁的 [literary]

  • She was baptized and she was pure and clean of sin.

    接受了洗礼的她纯洁无瑕。

purity

  • The American Female Reform Society promoted sexual purity.

    “美国女性改革社团”倡导性贞洁。

8 PHRASE [n PHRASE, PHRASE n] 完全的；纯粹的；不折不扣的 You use pure and simple to emphasize that the thing you are mentioning is the only thing that is involved or that should be considered. 完全的；纯粹的；不折不扣的 [emphasis]

  • It's blackmail, pure and simple.

    这纯粹是敲诈。
