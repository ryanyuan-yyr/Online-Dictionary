obliterate
əblɪtəreɪt əˈblɪtərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense obliterates, present participle obliterating, past tense , past participle obliterated
1 VERB 消灭;毁掉;清除 If something obliterates an object or place, it destroys it completely. 消灭;毁掉;清除

  • Their warheads are enough to obliterate the world several times over. [VERB noun]

    他们拥有的弹头足以将世界毁灭好几次。

  • Whole villages were obliterated by fire. [VERB noun]

    整座整座的村庄都被大火所吞噬。

SYN destroy, eliminate, devastate, waste  
obliteration

  • ...the obliteration of three isolated rainforests.

    3处孤立雨林的毁灭

2 VERB 抹除，忘却（回忆等） If you obliterate something such as a memory, emotion, or thought, you remove it completely from your mind. 抹除，忘却（回忆等） [literary]

  • There was time enough to obliterate memories of how things once were for him. [VERB noun]

    时间足以抹去他对过去经历的记忆。
