communist
kɒmjʊnɪst ˈkɑmjunɪst ˈkɑmjənɪst
Word forms: plural communists
1 N-COUNT 共产主义者 A communist is someone who believes in communism. 共产主义者
SYN socialist, Red [informal] , Marxist, Bolshevik  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 共产主义的 Communist means relating to communism. 共产主义的

  • ...the Communist Party.

    共产党
