infancy
ɪnfənsi ˈɪnfənsi
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually poss NOUN] 婴儿期;幼年 Infancy is the period of your life when you are a very young child. 婴儿期;幼年

  • ...minute details of Deborah's infancy.

    德博拉幼年生活的详尽细节

  • ...the development of the mind from infancy onwards.

    从婴儿期开始的心智发展

SYN early childhood, babyhood  
2 N-UNCOUNT 初期;初创阶段 If something is in its infancy, it is new and has not developed very much. 初期;初创阶段

  • Computing science was still in its infancy.

    计算科学仍然处于初期阶段。
