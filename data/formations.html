formation
fɔːʳmeɪʃən fɔrˈmeɪʃən
Word forms: plural formations
1 N-UNCOUNT 创建;设立;创造 The formation of something is the starting or creation of it. 创建;设立;创造

  • ...the formation of a new government. [+ of]

    新政府的组建

SYN establishment, founding, forming, setting up  
2 N-UNCOUNT 形成;养成;建立 The formation of an idea, habit, relationship, or character is the process of developing and establishing it. 形成;养成;建立

  • My profession had an important influence in the formation of my character and temperament. [+ of]

    我的职业对我性格和脾气的形成有重要的影响。

SYN development, shaping, constitution, evolution  
3 N-COUNT [usually in NOUN] 编队;列队 If people or things are in formation, they are arranged in a particular pattern as they move. 编队;列队

  • He was flying in formation with seven other jets.

    他和其他7架喷气机一起列队飞行。

  • They saw a squadron of fifteen motorcycle policemen driving in V-formation.

    他们看见 15 名警察骑着摩托车排成 V 字形前行。

  • The dancers step into a formation which represents the human being.

    舞蹈演员们排成一个人形队列。

SYN arrangement, grouping, figure, design  
4 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 形成物;组成物;(岩或云)层 A rock or cloud formation is rock or cloud of a particular shape or structure. 形成物;组成物;(岩或云)层

  • ...a vast rock formation shaped like a pillar.

    柱状的巨大岩层

  • Enormous cloud formations formed a purple mass.

    巨大的云层形成紫色云团。
