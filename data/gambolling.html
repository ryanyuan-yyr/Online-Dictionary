gambol
gæmbəl ˈgæmbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gambols, present participle gambolling, past tense , past participle gambolled
in AM, use 美国英语用 gamboling, gamboled
VERB 欢快地跳跃；嬉戏 If animals or people gambol, they run or jump about in a playful way. 欢快地跳跃；嬉戏

  • They gamboled down the passageway. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他们沿着走廊蹦蹦跳跳地走过来。

  • ...the sight of newborn lambs gambolling in the fields. [VERB prep./adv.]

    新生的小羊羔在地里嬉戏的景象

SYN frolic, jump, hop, skip
