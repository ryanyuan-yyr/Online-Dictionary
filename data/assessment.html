assessment
əsesmənt əˈsɛsmənt
Word forms: plural assessments
1 N-VAR 评估;评定;鉴定 An assessment is a consideration of someone or something and a judgment about them. 评估;评定;鉴定

  • There is little assessment of the damage to the natural environment. [+ of]

    几乎未对自然环境破坏程度作出评估。

  • The tests are supposed to provide a basis for the assessment of children.

    这些测试应该可以为评价儿童提供一个基础。

  • Heggie was remanded to a mental hospital for assessment by doctors.

    赫吉被押往一所精神病院接受医生检查。

SYN judgment, analysis, determination, evaluation  
2 N-VAR （价值的）评估，评定;估价 An assessment of the amount of money that something is worth or that should be paid is a calculation or estimate of the amount. （价值的）评估，评定;估价

  • Price Waterhouse have traced the losses to lenders' inflated assessments of mortgaged property. [+ of]

    普华永道会计师事务所已经查明损失源于贷方对按揭财产估值过高。

  • ...income assessment.

    收入评估
