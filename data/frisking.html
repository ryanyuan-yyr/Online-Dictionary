frisk
frɪsk frɪsk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense frisks, present participle frisking, past tense , past participle frisked
VERB (通常指用手)搜…的身 If someone frisks you, they search you, usually with their hands in order to see if you are hiding a weapon or something else such as drugs in your clothes. (通常指用手)搜…的身

  • Drago pushed him up against the wall and frisked him. [VERB noun]

    德拉戈把他推到墙边搜他的身。

SYN search, check, inspect, run over
