defensible
dɪfensɪbəl diˈfɛnsəbəl dɪˈfɛnsəbəl
ADJ-GRADED (观点、制度或行动)可辩护的，正当有理的，情有可原的 An opinion, system, or action that is defensible is one that people can argue is right or good. (观点、制度或行动)可辩护的，正当有理的，情有可原的

  • Her reasons for acting are morally defensible.

    她的举动从道义上来讲是合情合理的。

SYN justifiable, right, sound, reasonable
