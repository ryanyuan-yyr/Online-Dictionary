kvetch
kvɛtʃ kvɛtʃ kəˈvɛtʃ
Word forms: kvetches, kvetching, kvetched
1 V-I 抱怨If someone kvetches about something, they complain about it in a bad-tempered way. 抱怨 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • Denverites often kvetch about sleep disrupted by the Denver Police Department's helicopter orbiting overhead.

    丹佛人经常抱怨他们睡觉的时候会受到头顶上方盘旋的丹佛警察局的直升飞机的干扰。

  • I just really love to kvetch.

    我就是喜欢抱怨。

2 N-COUNT 爱抱怨的人A kvetch is someone who kvetches. 爱抱怨的人 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • I'm sorry. I don't mean to be a kvetch.

    对不起，我并不想一直抱怨。
