hunt out
PHRASAL VERB 找寻到;搜寻直至找到 If you hunt out something that is hidden or difficult to find, you search for it and eventually find it. 找寻到;搜寻直至找到 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I'll try and hunt out the information you need. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我会努力找到你要的信息。

  • American consumers are accustomed to hunting out bargains. [VERB PREP. noun]

    美国消费者习惯淘一些便宜货。
