jealous
dʒeləs ˈdʒɛləs
1 ADJ-GRADED 妒忌的；吃醋的；小心守护的 If someone is jealous, they feel angry or bitter because they think that another person is trying to take a lover or friend, or a possession, away from them. 妒忌的；吃醋的；小心守护的

  • She got insanely jealous and there was a terrible fight.

    她妒忌得发疯，结果是大吵一架。

SYN suspicious, suspecting, guarded, protective  
jealously

  • The formula is jealously guarded.

    配方被小心翼翼地收藏着。

2 ADJ-GRADED 妒羡的；羡慕的；忌妒的 If you are jealous of another person's possessions or qualities, you feel angry or bitter because you do not have them. 妒羡的；羡慕的；忌妒的

  • She was jealous of his wealth. [+ of]

    她忌妒他的富有。

  • You're jealous because the record company rejected your idea.

    你之所以妒忌是因为唱片公司拒绝接受你的想法。

jealously

  • Gloria eyed them jealously.

    格洛丽亚羡慕地望着他们。
