navigable
nævɪgəbəl ˈnævɪgəbəl
ADJ (河流)可航行的，适于航行的 A navigable river is wide and deep enough for a boat to travel along safely. (河流)可航行的，适于航行的 [formal]

  • ...the navigable portion of the Nile.

    尼罗河适合航行的河段

  • ...the Tiber, which was then still navigable.

    当时仍可以通航的台伯河

SYN passable, negotiable, traversable, crossable
