debase
dɪbeɪs diˈbeɪs dɪˈbeɪs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense debases, present participle debasing, past tense , past participle debased
VERB 降低…的价值（或质量）；使贬值 To debase something means to reduce its value or quality. 降低…的价值（或质量）；使贬值 [formal]

  • Politicians have debased the meaning of the word 'freedom'. [VERB noun]

    政客们贬低了“自由”一词的意义。

  • He said parliament and the process of democracy had been debased. [VERB noun]

    他说议会和民主程序已遭贬值。
