readable
riːdəbəl ˈridəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 有可读性的；值得一读的 If you say that a book or article is readable, you mean that it is enjoyable and easy to read. 有可读性的；值得一读的

  • This is an impeccably researched and very readable book.

    这本书中的研究缜密透彻，可读性很强。

SYN enjoyable, interesting, gripping, entertaining  
2 ADJ-GRADED 易读的;字迹清晰的 A piece of writing that is readable is written or printed clearly and can be read easily. 易读的;字迹清晰的

  • My secretary worked long hours translating my almost illegible writing into a typewritten and readable script.

    我的秘书花了很长时间把我写的几乎无法辨认的手稿用打字机打印成易读的文本。
