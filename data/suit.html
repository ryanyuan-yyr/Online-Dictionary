suit
suːt sjuːt sut
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense suits, present participle suiting, past tense , past participle suited
1 N-COUNT (男士的)一套外衣，套装，西装 A man's suit consists of a jacket, trousers, and sometimes a waistcoat, all made from the same fabric. (男士的)一套外衣，套装，西装

  • ...a dark pinstriped business suit.

    黑色的细条纹正装

  • ...a smart suit and tie.

    帅气的西装和领带

SYN outfit, costume, ensemble, dress  
2 N-COUNT (女士的)一套外衣，套装，套裙 A woman's suit consists of a jacket and skirt, or sometimes trousers, made from the same fabric. (女士的)一套外衣，套装，套裙

  • I was wearing my tweed suit.

    我穿着我的花呢套装。

3 N-COUNT [n NOUN] (从事特定活动时穿的)成套服装 A particular type of suit is a piece of clothing that you wear for a particular activity. (从事特定活动时穿的)成套服装

  • ...a completely revolutionary atmospheric diving suit.

    令人耳目一新的大气压潜水服

4 VERB [no cont] 对…方便;满足…需要;合…心意 If something suits you, it is convenient for you or is the best thing for you in the circumstances. 对…方便;满足…需要;合…心意

  • They will only release information if it suits them. [VERB noun]

    他们只会发布对他们有利的信息。

  • They should be able to find you the best package to suit your needs. [VERB noun]

    他们应该能提供满足你需要的最佳套餐。

SYN be acceptable to, please, satisfy, do  
5 VERB [no cont] 讨…喜欢;中…的意 If something suits you, you like it. 讨…喜欢;中…的意

  • I don't think a sedentary life would altogether suit me. [VERB noun]

    我认为长期伏案的生活一点也不适合我。

SYN agree with, become, match, go with  
6 VERB [no cont] (服装、风格、颜色)使显得漂亮，与…相配;适合 If a piece of clothing or a particular style or colour suits you, it makes you look attractive. (服装、风格、颜色)使显得漂亮，与…相配;适合

  • Green suits you. [VERB noun]

    绿色很配你。

SYN look attractive on, become, flatter, look good on  
Usage Note :

  • You do not use the verb suit if clothes are simply not the right size for you. The verb you need is fit. The size 12 gown was gorgeousand fitted perfectly... The gloves didn't fit. You can say that something suits a person or place if it looks attractive on that person or in that place. It is really feminine and pretty and it certainly suits you. However, you cannot usually say that one colour, pattern, or object suits another. The verb you need is match. She wears a straw hat with a yellow ribbon to match her yellow cheesecloth dress... His clothes don't quite match.

    表示衣服的尺码是否合适不用 suit，应该用 fit，如：The size 12 gown was gorgeous and fitted perfectly (那件 12 码的礼服看上去光彩夺目，而且大小正合适)，The gloves didn't fit (手套大小不合适)。可用 suit 来表示某物穿在某人身上或放在某处非常漂亮，如：It is really feminine and pretty and it certainly suits you (它真是又漂亮又有女人味，你穿上去一定好看)。然而通常不能用 suit 来表示颜色、图形或物件或其他事物相匹配，此时应该用的动词为 match，如：She wears a straw hat with a yellow ribbon to match her yellow cheesecloth dress (她戴了一顶缀黄丝带的草帽来搭配黄色的薄纱连衣裙)，His clothes don't quite match (他的着装不是很搭)。

7 VERB 随自己的意愿;随性子;自便 If you suit yourself, you do something just because you want to do it, without bothering to consider other people. 随自己的意愿;随性子;自便

  • These large institutions make — and change—the rules to suit themselves. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    这些大机构总是随意制定规定，而且说变就变。

  • He made a dismissive gesture. 'Suit yourself.' [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    他做了一个很不屑的手势。“随你便。”

8 N-COUNT 诉讼;讼案 In a court of law, a suit is a case in which someone tries to get a legal decision against a person or company, often so that the person or company will have to pay them money for having done something wrong to them. 诉讼;讼案

  • Up to 2,000 former employees have filed personal injury suits against the company.

    多达 2,000 名旧职员以个人伤害为由向该公司提起诉讼。

  • The judge dismissed the suit.

    法官驳回了诉讼。

SYN lawsuit, case, trial, proceeding  
In American English, you can say that someone files or brings suit against another person.(美国英语)起诉，控告

  • One insurance company has already filed suit against the city of Chicago.

    一家保险公司已经对芝加哥市提起诉讼。

9 N-COUNT (扑克牌中的)同花色牌，花色 A suit is one of the four types of card in a set of playing cards. These are hearts, diamonds, clubs, and spades. (扑克牌中的)同花色牌，花色
10 See also: bathing suit  birthday suit  boiler suit  trouser suit 
11 PHRASE 跟着做;照着做;仿效 If people follow suit, they do the same thing that someone else has just done. 跟着做;照着做;仿效

  • Efforts to persuade the remainder to follow suit have continued.

    说服其余人跟着效仿的努力仍在继续。

SYN copy someone, emulate someone, accord with someone, take your cue from someone  
12 PHRASE 对…非常方便;很合…心意 If something suits you down to the ground, you like it a great deal or find it very convenient. 对…非常方便;很合…心意

  • Their London house suits them down to the ground.

    他们在伦敦的房子很合他们心意。
