unhelpful
ʌnhelpfʊl ʌnˈhɛlpfʊl
ADJ-GRADED 没有帮助的；无益的 If you say that someone or something is unhelpful, you mean that they do not help you or improve a situation, and may even make things worse. 没有帮助的；无益的

  • The criticism is both unfair and unhelpful.

    批评既不公正又无济于事。

  • ...unhelpful hotel staff.

    没用的旅馆员工
