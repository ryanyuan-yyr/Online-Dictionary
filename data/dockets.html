docket
dɒkɪt ˈdɑkɪt
Word forms: plural dockets
1 N-COUNT (包裹、货物等上的)标签，单据 A docket is a certificate or ticket which shows the contents of something such as a parcel or cargo, and proves who the goods belong to. (包裹、货物等上的)标签，单据 [British]

  • The clerk asked me to sign the docket.

    店员让我在单据上签字。

SYN label, bill, ticket, certificate  
2 N-COUNT 备审案件目录表 A docket is a list of cases waiting for trial in a law court. 备审案件目录表 [mainly US]

  • The Court has about 1,400 appeals on its docket.

    法庭的备审案件约有 1,400 宗。

SYN file, index, register
