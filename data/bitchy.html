bitchy
bɪtʃi ˈbɪtʃi
ADJ-GRADED 恶意的；恶毒的；刻薄的 If someone is being bitchy or is making bitchy remarks, they are saying unkind things about someone. 恶意的；恶毒的；刻薄的 [informal, disapproval]

  • I'm sorry. I know I was bitchy on the phone.

    对不起。我知道我在电话里说得太刻薄了。

  • Women are not the only ones who say bitchy things about each other.

    不只是女人会对彼此说些恶毒的话。

SYN spiteful, mean, nasty, cruel  
bitchiness

  • There's a lot of bitchiness.

    说了很多刻薄的话。
