talk through
1 PHR-V-RECIP 详细讨论;讨论清楚 If you talk something through with someone, you discuss it with them thoroughly. 详细讨论;讨论清楚

  • He and I have talked through this whole tricky problem. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他和我把这个棘手的问题整个儿都讨论清楚了。

  • Now her children are grown-up and she has talked through with them what happened. [VERB PREP. + with]

    现在孩子们长大了，所以她就把以前的事儿悉数告诉了他们。

  • It had all seemed so simple when they'd talked it through, so logical. [VERB noun PREP.]

    等他们把问题说清楚了才发现原来一切如此简单、如此合乎常理。

  • He had talked it through with Judith. [VERB noun PREP. + with]

    他和朱迪丝就此深谈过。

2 PHRASAL VERB 向…仔细讲解;向…详细解释 If someone talks you through something that you do not know, they explain it to you carefully. 向…仔细讲解;向…详细解释

  • Now she must talk her sister through the process a step at a time. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    现在她得向妹妹一个步骤一个步骤地把整个过程解释清楚。
