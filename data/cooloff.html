cool off
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)变凉;(使)凉快下来 If someone or something cools off, or if you cool them off, they become cooler after having been hot. (使)变凉;(使)凉快下来

  • Maybe he's trying to cool off out there in the rain. [VERB PREP.]

    可能他是想在雨里凉快一下。

  • She made a fanning motion, pretending to cool herself off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她做了个扇风的动作，假装想让自己凉快一点。

  • Cool off the carrots quickly. [VERB PREP. noun]

    将胡萝卜迅速冷却。
