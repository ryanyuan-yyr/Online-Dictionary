socialization
soʊʃəlaɪzeɪʃən
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 socialisation
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤指儿童的)社会化，合群 Socialization is the process by which people, especially children, are made to behave in a way which is acceptable in their culture or society. (尤指儿童的)社会化，合群 [technical]

  • Female socialization emphasizes getting along with others.

    女性的社会化强调与人相处。

2 N-UNCOUNT 社会所有化;社会主义化 Socialization is the process by which something is made to operate on socialist principles. 社会所有化;社会主义化 [technical]
