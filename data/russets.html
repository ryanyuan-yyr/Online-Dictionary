russet
rʌsɪt ˈrʌsɪt
Word forms: plural russets
COLOUR 赤褐色的;红褐色的 Russet is used to describe things that are reddish-brown in colour. 赤褐色的;红褐色的

  • ...a russet apple.

    红褐色的苹果

  • The maple trees were in their autumn glory of russets, reds and browns.

    正值秋季，赤褐色、红色和棕色的枫树绚烂夺目。
