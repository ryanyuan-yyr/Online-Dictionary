echo
ekoʊ ˈɛkoʊ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense echoes, present participle echoing, past tense , past participle echoed
1 N-COUNT 回声;回响 An echo is a sound which is caused by a noise being reflected off a surface such as a wall. 回声;回响

  • He heard nothing but the echoes of his own voice.

    他只听见了自己的回音。

SYN reverberation, ringing, repetition, answer  
2 VERB （声音）发出回响 If a sound echoes, it is reflected off a surface and can be heard again after the original sound has stopped. （声音）发出回响

  • His feet echoed on the bare board floor. [VERB]

    他的脚步声在没铺地毯的木地板上发出回响。

  • The bang came suddenly, echoing across the buildings, shattering glass. [VERB prep./adv.]

    突如其来的一声巨响在建筑物间回荡着，震碎了玻璃。

3 VERB 发出回声；产生回响 In a place that echoes, a sound is reflected off a surface, and is repeated after the original sound has stopped. 发出回声；产生回响 [Also + in]

  • The room echoed. [VERB]

    屋子里回荡着声音。

  • The corridor echoed with the barking of a dozen dogs. [VERB + with]

    十来只狗的叫声在走廊里回响。

  • ...the bare stone floors and the echoing hall. [VERB-ing]

    光秃秃的石头地面和有回音的大厅

4 VERB 重复;附和 If you echo someone's words, you repeat them or express agreement with their attitude or opinion. 重复;附和

  • Many phrases in the last two chapters echo earlier passages. [VERB noun]

    最后两章中的很多说法是对前面段落的呼应。

  • Their views often echo each other. [VERB noun]

    他们常常你唱我和。

  • 'That was a truly delicious piece of pork,' he said. 'Yes, wasn't it?' echoed Penelope. [VERB with quote]

    “那块猪肉真好吃，”他说。“没错，可不是么？”佩内洛普附和道。

SYN recall, reflect, copy, mirror  
5 N-COUNT [usually with supplement] （态度、观点或说法的）重复，附和 An echo is an expression of an attitude, opinion, or statement which has already been expressed. （态度、观点或说法的）重复，附和

  • I hear an echo of the thinking that got us into this mess in the first place.

    我听到有人重提那个最先使我们陷入这一困境的想法。

  • Political attacks work only if they find an echo with voters.

    政治攻击只有在选民中引起共鸣才会有作用。

6 N-COUNT 相似之处;再现 A detail or feature which reminds you of something else can be referred to as an echo . 相似之处;再现

  • The accident has echoes of past disasters. [+ of]

    那起事故仿佛是过去灾难的重现。

SYN reminder, suggestion, trace, hint  
7 VERB 模仿;仿效 If one thing echoes another, the first is a copy of a particular detail or feature of the other. 模仿;仿效

  • Pinks and beiges were chosen to echo the colours of the ceiling. [VERB noun]

    选用了粉红色和米色，以跟从天花板的颜色。

8 VERB 讨论不休;持续影响 If something echoes, it continues to be discussed and remains important or influential in a particular situation or among a particular group of people. 讨论不休;持续影响

  • The old fable continues to echo down the centuries. [VERB prep.]

    这则古老的寓言流传了数个世纪。
