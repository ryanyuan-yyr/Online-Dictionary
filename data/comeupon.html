come upon
1 PHRASAL VERB 偶然碰到(或发现) If you come upon someone or something, you meet them or find them by chance. 偶然碰到(或发现)

  • I came upon an irresistible item at a yard sale. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在一次庭院拍卖会上，我偶然发现了一个忍不住想买的东西。

2 PHRASAL VERB 产生(某种态度或感觉) If an attitude or feeling comes upon you, it begins to affect you. 产生(某种态度或感觉) [literary]

  • A sense of impending doom came upon all of us. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们所有人都有一种大难临头的感觉。
