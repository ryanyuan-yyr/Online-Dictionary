press on
(also press ahead)
1 PHRASAL VERB 坚定地继续 If you press on or press ahead, you continue with a task or activity in a determined way, and do not allow any problems or difficulties to delay you. 坚定地继续

  • Organizers of the strike are determined to press on. [VERB PREP.]

    罢工的组织者决心将罢工进行到底。

  • He was persuaded by his advisers to press ahead. [VERB PREP.]

    顾问们说服他继续干下去。

  • Poland pressed on with economic reform. [VERB PREP. + with]

    波兰坚定地继续进行经济改革。

SYN continue, proceed, carry on, keep going  
2 PHRASAL VERB 继续前进 If you press on, you continue with a journey, even though it is becoming more difficult or more dangerous. 继续前进

  • I considered turning back, but it was getting late, so I pressed on. [VERB PREP.]

    我想往回走，但天色已晚，只好继续前行。
