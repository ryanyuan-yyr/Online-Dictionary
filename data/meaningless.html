meaningless
miːnɪŋləs ˈminɪŋlɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED （指话语、所写内容）意思不明确的，无法理解的，没有意义的 If something that someone says or writes is meaningless, it has no meaning, or appears to have no meaning. （指话语、所写内容）意思不明确的，无法理解的，没有意义的

  • The sentence 'kicked the ball the man' is meaningless.

    kicked the ball the man 这句话毫无意义。

  • She is fascinated by algebra while he considers it meaningless nonsense.

    她对代数非常着迷，而他却认为代数毫无意义。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不重要的;不相关的 Something that is meaningless in a particular situation is not important or relevant. 不重要的;不相关的

  • Widespread political and economic disarray threatens to make the constitution meaningless.

    四处蔓延的政治和经济混乱可能会使宪法变得可有可无。

  • Fines are meaningless to guys earning millions.

    罚款对挣大钱的人来说无关紧要。

3 ADJ-GRADED 无目的的;无价值的 If something that you do is meaningless, it has no purpose and is not at all worthwhile. 无目的的;无价值的

  • They seek strong sensations to dull their sense of a meaningless existence.

    为了冲淡生活毫无意义的感觉，他们追求强烈的刺激。

SYN pointless, empty, useless, hollow
