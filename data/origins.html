origin
ɒrɪdʒɪn ɔːr- ˈɔrədʒɪn ˈɑrədʒɪn
Word forms: plural origins
1 N-COUNT [usually with poss, oft in/of N] 起源；来源；起因；由来 You can refer to the beginning, cause, or source of something as its origin or origins . 起源；来源；起因；由来

  • ...theories about the origin of life.

    有关生命起源的各种理论

  • The disorder in military policy had its origins in Truman's first term.

    军事政策的混乱可追溯到杜鲁门的第一届任期。

  • Their medical problems are basically physical in origin.

    他们的病症主要是由于身体原因引起的。

  • Most of the thickeners are of plant origin.

    大部分增稠剂来源于植物。

SYN beginning, start, birth, source  
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN, oft of/in N] 出身；身世；血统 When you talk about a person's origin or origins, you are referring to the country, race, or social class of their parents or ancestors. 出身；身世；血统

  • Thomas has not forgotten his humble origins.

    托马斯没有忘记自己卑微的出身。

  • ...people of Asian origin.

    亚裔民众

  • They are forced to return to their country of origin.

    他们被迫返回出生国。

SYN ancestry, family, race, beginnings
