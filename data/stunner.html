stunner
stʌnəʳ ˈstʌnər
Word forms: plural stunners
1 N-COUNT 极有魅力的女人;大美人 A stunner is an extremely attractive woman. 极有魅力的女人;大美人 [informal]

  • One of the girls was an absolute stunner.

    其中有一个女孩非常迷人。

SYN beauty, looker [informal , mainly US] , lovely [slang] , dish [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 令人震惊的事情;令人印象深刻的事物 If you say that something is a stunner, you mean that it is very surprising or impressive. 令人震惊的事情;令人印象深刻的事物 [informal]

  • Their debut single is a stunner.

    他们的首张单曲成绩斐然。
