clanger
klæŋəʳ ˈklæŋər
Word forms: plural clangers
N-COUNT 蠢事;失言;失礼;尴尬的错误 You can refer to something stupid or embarrassing that someone does or says as a clanger . 蠢事;失言;失礼;尴尬的错误 [British, informal]
If you say that you have dropped a clanger, you mean that you have done or said something stupid or embarrassing.做蠢事;说错话；失礼
