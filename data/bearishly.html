bearish
beərɪʃ ˈbɛərɪʃ ˈbɛrɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 行情看跌的；熊市的（Compare 比较 bullish.) On the stock market, if there is a bearish mood, prices are expected to fall. 行情看跌的；熊市的（Compare 比较 bullish.)

  • Dealers said investors remain bearish.

    交易员说投资者依然看跌。

  • Many traders forecast a continuation of the market's recent bearish trend.

    很多交易商都预测最近市场的下跌趋势还将延续。
