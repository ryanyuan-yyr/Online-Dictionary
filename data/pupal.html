pupa
pjuːpə ˈpjupə
Word forms: plural pupae/pjuːpiː/
N-COUNT 蛹 A pupa is an insect that is in the stage of development between a larva and a fully grown adult. It has a protective covering and does not move. 蛹 [technical]

  • The pupae remain dormant in the soil until they emerge as adult moths in the winter.

    蛹蛰伏在土壤中，直到冬天它们才蜕变成蛾子飞出来。
