shelf life
Word forms: plural shelf lives
N-COUNT [usually singular] (尤指食品等的)货架期，保存期 The shelf life of a product, especially food, is the length of time that it can be kept in a shop or at home before it becomes too old to sell or use. (尤指食品等的)货架期，保存期

  • Mature flour has a longer shelf life.

    熟面粉保存期要更长。
