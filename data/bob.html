bob
bɒb bɑb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bobs, present participle bobbing, past tense , past participle bobbed
The plural form for meaning 4 is bob. 义项4的复数形式为bob。
1 VERB （像物体在水中飘浮似的）上下移动，起伏 If something bobs, it moves up and down, like something does when it is floating on water. （像物体在水中飘浮似的）上下移动，起伏

  • Huge balloons bobbed about in the sky above. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一个个巨大的气球在头顶的蓝天中漂浮着。

  • The raft bobbed along more quietly. [VERB prep./adv.]

    小木筏更加平静地漂浮向前。

SYN bounce, duck, leap, hop  
2 VERB 快速移动；突然消失（或出现） If you bob somewhere, you move there quickly so that you disappear from view or come into view. 快速移动；突然消失（或出现）

  • She handed over a form, then bobbed down again behind a typewriter. [VERB adv./prep.]

    她递过来一张表格，随后又立刻埋头在打字机后面忙活起来。

SYN duck, drop, lower yourself, bend  
3 VERB （快速）点（头）（如打招呼等） When you bob your head, you move it quickly up and down once, for example when you greet someone. （快速）点（头）（如打招呼等）

  • A hostess stood at the top of the steps and bobbed her head at each passenger. [VERB noun]

    一位空姐站在最高的阶梯上向每位旅客点头致意。

SYN nod, lower, bend, bow  
Bob is also a noun.

  • The young man smiled with a bob of his head.

    那个小伙子微笑着点了点头。

4 N-COUNT （英国旧时货币单位）先令 In Britain, people used to refer to a shilling as a bob. （英国旧时货币单位）先令 [informal]

  • I've only got ten bob on me.

    我身上只有10先令。

5 N-COUNT （女式）齐短发，蘑菇头，波波头 A bob is a fairly short hair style for women in which the hair is the same length all the way round, except for the front. （女式）齐短发，蘑菇头，波波头
6 PHRASE 零碎物件；小玩意儿 Bits and bobs are small objects or parts of something. 零碎物件；小玩意儿 [mainly British, informal]

  • The microscope contains a few hundred dollars-worth of electronic bits and bobs.

    这架显微镜内有价值数百美元的电子元件。
