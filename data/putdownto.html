put down to
PHRASAL VERB 归咎于…；归因于… If you put something down to a particular thing, you believe that it is caused by that thing. 归咎于…；归因于…

  • You may be a sceptic and put it down to life's inequalities. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    你或许是个怀疑论者，会把它归因于生活的不公正。

SYN attribute, blame, ascribe, set down
