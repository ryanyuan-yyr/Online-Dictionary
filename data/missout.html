miss out
1 PHRASAL VERB 没有参加，错过(对自己有益或有趣的事) If you miss out on something that would be enjoyable or useful to you, you are not involved in it or do not take part in it. 没有参加，错过(对自己有益或有趣的事)

  • We're missing out on a tremendous opportunity. [VERB PREP. + on]

    我们在坐失良机。

  • Well, I'm glad you could make it. I didn't want you to miss out. [VERB PREP.]

    嗯，很高兴你赶来了，我可不希望你错过机会。

2 PHRASAL VERB 遗漏;漏掉;没把…包括在内 If you miss out something or someone, you fail to include them. 遗漏;漏掉;没把…包括在内 [British]

  • There should be an apostrophe here, and look, you've missed out the word 'men' altogether!. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这里应该有一个撇号，还有你看，你把men这个单词整个儿漏掉了！

  • What about Sally? You've missed her out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    萨莉呢？你把她给漏掉了。
