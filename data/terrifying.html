terrifying
terɪfaɪɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ to-infinitive] 吓人的;令人害怕的 If something is terrifying, it makes you very frightened. 吓人的;令人害怕的

  • I still find it terrifying to find myself surrounded by large numbers of horses.

    我仍旧觉得被一大群马包围很可怕。

  • Rabies has been described as one of the most terrifying diseases known to man.

    狂犬病被称为人类已知的最可怕的疾病之一。

SYN frightening, scary [informal] , alarming, appalling  
terrifyingly

  • Below was a terrifyingly deep crevasse.

    下面是个令人毛骨悚然的深不见底的裂隙。

  • Costs can escalate terrifyingly.

    成本的上涨速度会很惊人。
