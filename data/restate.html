restate
riːsteɪt riˈsteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense restates, present participle restating, past tense , past participle restated
1 VERB 重申;再次声明;重说 If you restate something, you say it again in words or writing, usually in a slightly different way. 重申;再次声明;重说 [formal]

  • He continued throughout to restate his opposition to violence. [VERB noun]

    他一直不断重申他反对暴力。

  • The letter merely restated the law of the land. [VERB noun]

    这封信只不过重复了一下土地法。
