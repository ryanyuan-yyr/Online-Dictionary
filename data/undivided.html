undivided
ʌndɪvaɪdɪd ˌʌndɪˈvaɪdɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 专注的；专一的；完全的 If you give someone or something your undivided attention, you concentrate on them fully and do not think about anything else. 专注的；专一的；完全的

  • Eldest children are the only ones to have experienced the undivided attention of their parents.

    只有老大得到过父母一心一意的照顾。

  • Adults rarely give the television their undivided attention.

    成年人很少会专心地看电视。

  • ...any task that requires undivided concentration.

    任何需要全神贯注的任务

SYN complete, full, whole, total  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （情感）专一的，专心的 Undivided feelings are ones that are very strong and not mixed with other feelings. （情感）专一的，专心的

  • The paintings she produced in those months won undivided admiration.

    她那几个月里创作的画作备受推崇。

  • He has my undivided loyalty.

    我对他忠心耿耿。

3 ADJ （国家或组织）统一的，未分裂的 An undivided country or organization is one that is not separated into smaller parts or groups. （国家或组织）统一的，未分裂的

  • Mandela said, 'We want a united, undivided South Africa'.

    曼德拉说道：“我们想要一个团结统一的南非。”

  • ...the goal of an undivided Church.

    一统基督教教会的目标

SYN united, whole, entire, unanimous
