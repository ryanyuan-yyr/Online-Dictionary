nail up
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…钉到(墙上、柱子上等) If you nail something up, you fix it to a wall, post, or tree using nails. 把…钉到(墙上、柱子上等)

  • He fished and nailed up his catch to dry on the wall of the byre. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他去钓了鱼，并把钓来的鱼钉在牛棚的墙上晒干。

2 PHRASAL VERB 钉死;钉住 If you nail up a wooden container, you secure the lid with nails. 钉死;钉住 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The cabins were then nailed up and concealed. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    接着这些小屋被钉死，并隐藏了起来。
