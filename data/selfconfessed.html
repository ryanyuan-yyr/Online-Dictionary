self-confessed ˈsɛlfkənˈfɛst
ADJ [ADJ n] 自己承认的;自己坦白的 If you describe someone as a self-confessed murderer or a self-confessed romantic, for example, you mean that they admit openly that they are a murderer or a romantic. 自己承认的;自己坦白的

  • The self-confessed drug addict was arrested 13 months ago.

    那个自己坦白的瘾君子在 13 个月前被逮捕。

  • She is a self-confessed workaholic.

    她自认是一个工作狂。
