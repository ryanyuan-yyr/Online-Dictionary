big city
bɪɡ ˈsɪtɪ
N-SING 大城市 The big city is used to refer to a large city which seems attractive to someone because they think there are many exciting things to do there, and many opportunities to earn a lot of money. 大城市

  • ...a country girl who dreams of the big city and bright lights.
