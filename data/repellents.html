repellent
rɪpelənt rɪˈpɛlənt
Word forms: plural repellents
The spelling repellant is also used for meaning 2. 义项2亦拼作repellant。
1 ADJ-GRADED 讨厌的;令人厌恶的;令人排斥的 If you think that something is horrible and disgusting you can say that it is repellent . 讨厌的;令人厌恶的;令人排斥的 [formal]

  • ...a very large, very repellent toad.

    个头很大、令人生厌的癞蛤蟆

  • She still found the place repellent.

    她还是觉得这个地方令人厌恶。

SYN disgusting, offensive, revolting, obscene  
2 N-MASS [usually n NOUN] 驱虫剂 Insect repellent is a product containing chemicals that you spray into the air or on your body in order to keep insects away. 驱虫剂

  • ...mosquito repellent.

    驱蚊剂
