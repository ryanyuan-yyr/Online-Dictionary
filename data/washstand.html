washstand
wɒʃstænd ˈwɔʃˌstænd
Word forms: plural washstands
N-COUNT 脸盆架 A washstand is a piece of furniture designed to hold a bowl for washing your hands and face in, which was used in former times before washbasins had taps on them. 脸盆架

  • ...a Victorian marble-topped washstand.

    维多利亚时代的大理石台面脸盆架
