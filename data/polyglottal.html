polyglot
pɒliglɒt ˈpɑlɪˌglɑt
Word forms: plural polyglots
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （书、社会等）（使用）多种语言的 Polyglot is used to describe something such as a book or society in which several different languages are used. （书、社会等）（使用）多种语言的 [formal]

  • ...Chicago's polyglot population.

    芝加哥的多语种人口

2 N-COUNT 通晓数种语言的人 A polyglot is a person who speaks or understands many languages. 通晓数种语言的人

  • ...a polyglot who speaks four languages.

    能说四门语言的人
