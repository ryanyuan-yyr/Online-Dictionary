duress
djʊəres djʊˈrɛs dʊr- dʊˈrɛs djʊˈrɛs
N-UNCOUNT [usu under N] 胁迫;强迫 To do something under duress means to do it because someone forces you to do it or threatens you. 胁迫;强迫 [formal]

  • He thought her confession had been made under duress.

    他认为她是被刑讯逼供的。

SYN pressure, threat, constraint, compulsion
