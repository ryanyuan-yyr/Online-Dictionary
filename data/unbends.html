unbend
ʌnbend ʌnˈbɛnd ʌnˈbɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unbends, present participle unbending, past tense , past participle unbent
VERB （人）态度变缓和，变随和 If someone unbends, their attitude becomes less strict than it was. （人）态度变缓和，变随和

  • In her dying days the old Queen unbent a little. [VERB]

    在临死的那些日子里，年迈的女王态度稍缓和了一些。

SYN relax, loosen up, lighten up [slang] , unwind
