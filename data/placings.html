placings
pleɪsɪŋz ˈpleɪsɪŋz
N-PLURAL （竞赛中的）排名，名次 The placings in a competition are the relative positions of the competitors at the end or at a particular stage of the competition. （竞赛中的）排名，名次

  • The placings remained unaltered.

    排名未有变化。

  • Northampton were third in the League placings.

    北安普敦在联赛中名列第三。
