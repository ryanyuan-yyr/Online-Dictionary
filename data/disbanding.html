disband
dɪsbænd dɪsˈbænd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disbands, present participle disbanding, past tense , past participle disbanded
V-ERG 解散;解体;（使）散伙 If someone disbands a group of people, or if the group disbands, it stops operating as a single unit. 解散;解体;（使）散伙 [Also VERB n]

  • All the armed groups will be disbanded. [be VERB-ed]

    所有的武装团体都将被解散。

  • The rebels were to have fully disbanded by June the tenth. [VERB]

    叛乱分子会在 6 月 10日前全部解散。

SYN dismiss, separate, break up, scatter
