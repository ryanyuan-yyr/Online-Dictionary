scary
skeəri ˈskɛərɪ ˈskɛri
Word forms: comparative scarier, superlative scariest
ADJ-GRADED 引起恐慌的;骇人的 Something that is scary is rather frightening. 引起恐慌的;骇人的 [informal]

  • I think prison is going to be a scary thing for Harry.

    我觉得蹲监狱对于哈里会是件很可怕的事。

  • There's something very scary about him. [+ about]

    他身上有某种东西让人不寒而栗。

  • We watched scary movies.

    我们看了几部恐怖片。

SYN frightening, alarming, terrifying, shocking  
scarily

  • ...the scarily unstable new world order.

    令人恐慌的动荡不安的世界新秩序
