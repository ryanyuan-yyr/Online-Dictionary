mishandle
mɪshændəl mɪsˈhændəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mishandles, present participle mishandling, past tense , past participle mishandled
VERB 对…处理不当 If you say that someone has mishandled something, you are critical of them because you think they have dealt with it badly. 对…处理不当 [disapproval]

  • She completely mishandled an important project purely through lack of attention. [VERB noun]

    她之所以彻底搞砸了一个重大项目纯粹是因为不够上心。

  • The judge said the police had mishandled the siege. [VERB noun]

    法官说警方对围攻安排不当。

SYN mismanage, bungle, botch, mess up [informal]  
mishandling

  • ...the Government's mishandling of the economy.

    政府对经济问题的处理不当
