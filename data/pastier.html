pasty
peɪsti pæsti ˈpeɪsti ˈpæsti
Word forms: plural pasties, comparative pastier, superlative pastiest
The adjective is pronounced /'peɪsti/. The noun is pronounced /'pæsti/. 形容词读作/'peɪsti/。名词读作/'pæsti/。
1 ADJ-GRADED 脸色苍白的 If you are pasty or if you have a pasty face, you look pale and unhealthy. 脸色苍白的

  • My complexion remained pale and pasty.

    我的脸色依旧苍白。

  • Ron Freeman appeared pasty faced and nervous.

    罗恩·弗里曼脸色苍白，显得很紧张。

SYN pale, unhealthy, wan, sickly  
2 N-COUNT 馅饼 In Britain, a pasty is a small pie which consists of pastry folded around meat, vegetables, or cheese. 馅饼

  • ...meat pasties.

    肉馅饼

3See also:Cornish pasty
