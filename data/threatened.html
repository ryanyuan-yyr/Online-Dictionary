threatened
θretənd ˈθrɛtənd
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 受到威胁的;感到危险的 If you feel threatened, you feel as if someone is trying to harm you. 受到威胁的;感到危险的 [Also + by]

  • Anger is the natural reaction we experience when we feel threatened or frustrated.

    我们感觉受到威胁或遭遇挫折时，生气是正常反应。

  • The survey found that 4 per cent of people felt threatened by the sight of beggars.

    调查发现，4% 的人看见乞丐时会觉得危险。
