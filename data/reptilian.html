reptilian
reptɪliən rɛpˈtɪliən rɛpˈtɪljən
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 爬行的;爬行动物的 A reptilian creature is a reptile. 爬行的;爬行动物的

  • ...a prehistoric jungle occupied by reptilian creatures.

    爬行动物主宰的史前丛林

SYN reptile-like, reptile, cold-blooded, crocodilian  
2 ADJ 像爬行动物的;有爬行动物特征的 You can also use the word reptilian to describe something that is characteristic of a reptile or that is like a reptile. 像爬行动物的;有爬行动物特征的

  • The chick is ugly and almost reptilian in its appearance.

    这只小鸡长得很丑，看起来几乎像个爬行动物。
