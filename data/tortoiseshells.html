tortoiseshell
tɔːʳtəsʃel ˈtɔrtəsˌʃɛl
Word forms: plural tortoiseshells
1 N-UNCOUNT 龟甲;玳瑁壳 Tortoiseshell is the hard shell of a kind of sea turtle. It is brown and yellow in colour and is often polished and used to make jewellery and ornaments. 龟甲;玳瑁壳
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 龟甲做的;仿玳瑁的 Tortoiseshell means made of tortoiseshell or made of a material which resembles tortoiseshell. 龟甲做的;仿玳瑁的

  • He wears huge spectacles with thick tortoiseshell frames.

    他戴着玳瑁粗框大眼镜。

3 N-COUNT (翅膀呈棕色和橘黄色的)蛱蝶 A tortoiseshell is a butterfly with brown and orange wings. (翅膀呈棕色和橘黄色的)蛱蝶
