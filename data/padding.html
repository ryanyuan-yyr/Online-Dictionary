padding
pædɪŋ ˈpædɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （柔软的）垫料，衬料 Padding is soft material which is put on something or inside it in order to make it less hard, to protect it, or to give it a different shape. （柔软的）垫料，衬料

  • ...the foam rubber padding on the headphones.

    头戴式耳机上的泡沫橡胶垫料

  • Players must wear padding to protect them from injury.

    运动员必须戴防护垫以免受伤。

  • ...the fat on our bodies, which acts as padding.

    我们身体里起衬垫作用的脂肪

2 N-UNCOUNT 冗词赘句；凑篇幅的废话 Padding is unnecessary words or information used to make a piece of writing or a speech longer. 冗词赘句；凑篇幅的废话

  • Of the sonnet eleven of the lines are mere padding and say nothing.

    那首十四行诗中有11行都是废话，毫无意义。

  • ...the kind of subject that politicians put in their speeches for a bit of padding.

    政客在演讲中凑篇幅的那类话题

SYN waffle [informal , mainly British] , hot air [informal] , verbiage, wordiness
