downcast
daʊnkɑːst -kæst ˈdaʊnˌkæst
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 沮丧的;悲观的 If you are downcast, you are feeling sad and without hope. 沮丧的;悲观的

  • Barbara looked increasingly downcast as defeat loomed.

    当败象已呈时，芭芭拉看上去越来越沮丧。

  • ...a glum, downcast expression.

    忧郁、沮丧的表情

SYN dejected, sad, depressed, unhappy  
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (眼睛通常因难过或尴尬)向下看的，低垂的 If your eyes are downcast, you are looking towards the ground, usually because you are feeling sad or embarrassed. (眼睛通常因难过或尴尬)向下看的，低垂的

  • She was silent, her eyes downcast.

    她一言不发，两眼低垂。
