ivory
aɪvəri ˈaɪvəri ˈaɪvri
Word forms: plural ivories
1 N-UNCOUNT 象牙 Ivory is a hard cream-coloured substance which forms the tusks of elephants. It is valuable and can be used for making carved ornaments. 象牙

  • ...the international ban on the sale of ivory.

    禁止销售象牙的国际禁令

  • ...intricate ivory carvings.

    精致的象牙雕刻品

2 COLOUR 象牙色;乳白色 Ivory is a creamy-white colour. 象牙色;乳白色
