attuned
ətjuːnd əˈtjuːnd ətuːnd
1 ADJ 适应的;理解的;与…合拍的 If you are attuned to something, you can understand and appreciate it. 适应的;理解的;与…合拍的

  • I have become attuned to Carlisle's industrial past.

    我对于卡莱尔市过去的工业史已经有所了解。

  • He seemed unusually attuned to people's feelings.

    他似乎特别善解人意。

SYN accustomed, adjusted, coordinated, in tune  
2 ADJ-GRADED 听得出的;习惯于（某种声音）的 If your ears are attuned to a sound, you can hear it and recognize it quickly. 听得出的;习惯于（某种声音）的

  • Their ears were still attuned to the sounds of the London suburb. [+ to]

    他们依然很熟悉伦敦市郊的喧闹声。
