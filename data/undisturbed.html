undisturbed
ʌndɪstɜːʳbd ˌʌndɪˈstɜːbd
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] 未被动过的；未被移动的；未用过的 Something that remains undisturbed is not touched, moved, or used by anyone. 未被动过的；未被移动的；未用过的

  • The desk looked undisturbed.

    那张桌子看上去没人动过。

  • Peonies react badly to being moved and are best left undisturbed.

    牡丹花不宜挪移，最好别去动它。

SYN untouched, not moved  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] （地方）平静的，未受扰乱的 A place that is undisturbed is peaceful and has not been affected by changes that have happened in other places. （地方）平静的，未受扰乱的

  • In the Balearics pockets of rural life and inland villages are undisturbed.

    在巴利阿里群岛上，零星散布在岛内的村庄和那里的生活平静而祥和。

  • The war had not left Bargate undisturbed.

    巴盖特古城门未能躲过战火的荼毒。

3 ADJ [ADJ after verb, ADJ n, verb-link ADJ] （人）未被打扰的，未受干扰的 If you are undisturbed in something that you are doing, you are able to continue doing it and are not affected by something that is happening. （人）未被打扰的，未受干扰的

  • I can spend the whole day undisturbed at the warehouse.

    我可以一整天都呆在仓库里而不受打扰。

  • There was a small restaurant on Sullivan Street where we could talk undisturbed.

    沙利文大街上有一家小餐馆，我们可以在那里不受打扰地聊。

  • They want undisturbed rest.

    他们想不受打扰地好好休息一下。

SYN uninterrupted, without interruption  
4 ADJ 不烦恼的；不着急的；不担忧的 If someone is undisturbed by something, it does not affect, bother, or upset them. 不烦恼的；不着急的；不担忧的

  • Victoria was strangely undisturbed by this symptom, even though her husband and family were frightened. [+ by]

    尽管丈夫和家人都吓坏了，维多利亚却对这一症状出奇地淡定。

SYN unperturbed, untroubled, calm, collected
