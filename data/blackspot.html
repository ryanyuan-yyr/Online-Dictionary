black spot
(also blackspot)Word forms: plural black spots
1 N-COUNT 危机焦点；隐患；雷区 If you describe a place, time, or part of a situation as a black spot, you mean that it is particularly bad or likely to cause problems. 危机焦点；隐患；雷区 [British]

  • There are recognised black spots in marriages which can lead to trouble.

    婚姻中有一些公认的容易引发问题的雷区。

  • Gainsborough is known as an unemployment blackspot.

    众所周知，盖恩斯伯勒失业严重。

2 N-COUNT 交通事故多发地段 A black spot is a place on a road where accidents often happen. 交通事故多发地段 [British]

  • The accident happened on a notorious black spot on the A43.

    事故发生在A43号公路上一个出了名的事故多发地段。
