recovery
rɪkʌvəri rɪˈkʌvəri
Word forms: plural recoveries
1 N-VAR （身体的）恢复，康复 If a sick person makes a recovery, he or she becomes well again. （身体的）恢复，康复

  • He made a remarkable recovery from a shin injury. [+ from]

    他的胫骨伤恢复得相当不错。

  • He had been given less than a one in 500 chance of recovery by his doctors.

    他的医生们认为他康复的几率不足1/500。

SYN improvement, return to health, rally, healing  
2 N-VAR （经济的）复苏，好转 When there is a recovery in a country's economy, it improves. （经济的）复苏，好转

  • Interest-rate cuts have failed to bring about economic recovery.

    利率下调没有带来经济复苏。

  • In many sectors of the economy the recovery has started.

    许多经济领域已经开始复苏。

SYN revival, improvement, rally, restoration  
3 N-UNCOUNT 收回；取回；失而复得 You talk about the recovery of something when you get it back after it has been lost or stolen. 收回；取回；失而复得

  • A substantial reward is being offered for the recovery of a painting by Turner. [+ of]

    为追回特纳的一幅画，已发布了重金悬赏令。

  • She has a reasonable prospect of recovery from the insurer.

    她有望从保险公司拿到赔偿。

SYN retrieval, repossession, reclamation, restoration  
4 N-UNCOUNT （身体或精神状态的）恢复 You talk about the recovery of someone's physical or mental state when they return to this state. （身体或精神状态的）恢复

  • ...the abrupt loss and recovery of consciousness. [+ of]

    意识的突然丧失和恢复

5 PHRASE 在恢复当中；正在好转 If someone is in recovery, they are being given a course of treatment to help them recover from something such as a drug habit or mental illness. 在恢复当中；正在好转

  • ...Carole, a compulsive pot smoker and alcoholic in recovery.

    卡萝尔，一位正在戒断康复当中的大麻吸食者和酗酒者

SYN in rehabilitation, in rehab [informal] , returning to health, in convalescence
