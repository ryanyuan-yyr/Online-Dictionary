table
teɪbəl ˈteɪbəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense tables, present participle tabling, past tense , past participle tabled
1 N-COUNT 桌子;台子 A table is a piece of furniture with a flat top that you put things on or sit at. 桌子;台子

  • She was sitting at the kitchen table eating a currant bun.

    她正坐在餐桌旁，吃着葡萄干圆面包。

  • I placed his drink on the small table at his elbow.

    我把他的饮料放在他肘边的小桌子上。

SYN counter, bench, stand, board  
2 N-COUNT (饭店的)饭桌，餐桌 If you ask for a table in a restaurant, you want to have a meal there. (饭店的)饭桌，餐桌

  • I'd like a table for two at about 8:30, please.

    我想订一张 8:30 左右的双人桌。

  • I booked a table at the Savoy Grill.

    我在萨沃伊烧烤店订了张桌子。

  • You will join us at our table, won't you?

    你会过来和我们一起吃，对吗？

3 VERB 正式提出，提交(议案) If someone tables a proposal, they say formally that they want it to be discussed at a meeting. 正式提出，提交(议案) [mainly British]

  • They've tabled a motion criticising the Government for doing nothing about the problem. [VERB noun]

    他们提交了一项动议，批评政府在该问题上无所作为。

SYN submit, propose, put forward, move  
4 VERB 搁置(提案、计划) If someone tables a proposal or plan which has been put forward, they decide to discuss it or deal with it at a later date, rather than straight away. 搁置(提案、计划) [US]

  • We will table that for later. [VERB noun]

    那个问题我们留到以后再讨论吧。

5 N-COUNT 表;表格;目录 A table is a written set of facts and figures arranged in columns and rows. 表;表格;目录

  • Consult the table on page 104.

    参阅 104 页的表格。

  • Other research supports the figures in Table 3.3.

    其他研究也可证实表 3.3 中的数据。

SYN list, chart, tabulation, record  
6 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] (1 至 12 的)乘法表 A table is a list of the multiplications of numbers between one and twelve. Children often have to learn tables at school. (1 至 12 的)乘法表

  • He didn't know his eleven-times table.

    他不知道11的乘法口诀。

  • I attempted to learn my tables.

    我努力背诵乘法表。

7 See also: coffee table  dressing table  negotiating table  round table  tea table 
8 PHRASE 提交讨论;摆到桌面上 If you put something on the table, you present it at a meeting for it to be discussed. 提交讨论;摆到桌面上

  • This is one of the best packages we've put on the table in years.

    这是我们几年来提交的最好的几套方案之一。

  • It means that all the options are at least on the table.

    这就意味着，至少所有的选择都已经摆到了桌面上。

9 PHRASE 扭转局面;转败为胜 If you turn the tables on someone, you change the situation completely, so that instead of them causing problems for you, you are causing problems for them. 扭转局面;转败为胜

  • The only question is whether the President can use his extraordinary political skills to turn the tables on his opponents.

    唯一的问题是，总统能否运用他杰出的政治手腕化被动为主动，击败对手。
