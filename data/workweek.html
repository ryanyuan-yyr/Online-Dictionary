work week
Word forms: plural work weeks
N-COUNT 工作周;一周的工作时间 A work week is the amount of time during a normal week which you spend doing your job. 工作周;一周的工作时间 [mainly US]

  • The union had sought a wage increase and a shorter work week.

    工会寻求增加工资，缩短每周工作时间。
