tear off tɛə
PHRASAL VERB (胡乱地)脱掉，扯下(衣服) If you tear off your clothes, you take them off in a rough and violent way. (胡乱地)脱掉，扯下(衣服)

  • Totally exhausted, he tore his clothes off and fell into bed. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他疲惫不堪，胡乱扯掉衣服，倒在床上。

  • Fuentes tore off his hat and flung it to the ground. [VERB PREP. noun]

    富恩特斯一把扯下帽子，扔在地上。
