tongue depressor tʌŋ dɪprɛsər tʌŋ dɪˈprɛsə
Word forms: tongue depressors
N-COUNT 压舌板A tongue depressor is a medical instrument that is used to press a patient's tongue down so that their mouth or throat can be examined more easily. 压舌板 [medicine]

  • Dr. Avery took the tongue depressor out of Rae's mouth.
