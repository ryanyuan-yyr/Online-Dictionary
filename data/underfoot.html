underfoot
ʌndəʳfʊt ˌʌndəˈfʊt ˌʌndərˈfʊt
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb, n ADVERB] 在脚下；在地上 You describe something as being underfoot when you are standing or walking on it. 在脚下；在地上

  • ...a room, high and square with carpet underfoot and tapestries on the walls.

    一个高高的四方房间，地上铺有地毯，墙上挂着挂毯

  • It was still wet underfoot.

    地上仍是湿的。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 踩在脚下；践踏 If you trample or crush something underfoot, you spoil or destroy it by stepping on it. 踩在脚下；践踏

  • Morgan dropped his cigarette and crushed it underfoot.

    摩根把香烟丢到地上，用脚踩灭。

  • ...half-ripe apples that were being trampled underfoot.

    被踩踏的半熟的苹果
