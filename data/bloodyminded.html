bloody-minded ˈblʌdiˌmaɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED 有意刁难的；故意作对的 If you say that someone is being bloody-minded, you are showing that you disapprove of their behaviour because you think they are being deliberately difficult instead of being helpful. 有意刁难的；故意作对的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • He had a reputation for being bloody-minded and difficult.

    他为人刻薄、难相处是出了名的。

SYN difficult, contrary, annoying, awkward  
bloody-mindedness

  • This is sheer bloody-mindedness.

    这纯粹是故意刁难。

  • ...a rare mixture of courage and bloody-mindedness.

    很少有人这样一边鼓励一边又故意刁难
