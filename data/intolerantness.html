intolerant
ɪntɒlərənt ɪnˈtɑlərənt
ADJ-GRADED 不容忍的;不容异己的;偏狭的 If you describe someone as intolerant, you mean that they do not accept behaviour and opinions that are different from their own. 不容忍的;不容异己的;偏狭的 [disapproval]

  • He was intolerant of both suggestions and criticisms.

    他对建议与批评都不能容忍。

  • ...intolerant attitudes toward non-Catholics.

    对非天主教徒的不宽容态度
