death row
deθ roʊ
N-UNCOUNT [oft on NOUN] (监狱中的）死囚区 If someone is on death row, they are in the part of a prison which contains the cells for criminals who have been sentenced to death. (监狱中的）死囚区 [US]

  • He has been on Death Row for 11 years.

    他已经在死囚区关了 11 年。

  • Most death row inmates avoid execution for many years by filing several appeals.

    大多数死囚通过进行数次上诉将行刑时间拖延许多年。
