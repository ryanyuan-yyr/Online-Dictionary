electorate
ɪlektərət iˈlɛktərɪt ɪlɛktərɪt
Word forms: plural electorates
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] （一国或一地区的）全体选民 The electorate of a country or area is all the people in it who have the right to vote in an election. （一国或一地区的）全体选民

  • He has the backing of almost a quarter of the electorate.

    他得到了几乎1/4选民的支持。

  • ...the Maltese electorate.

    马耳他全体选民
