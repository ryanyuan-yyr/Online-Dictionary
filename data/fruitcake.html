fruitcake
fruːtkeɪk ˈfrutˌkeɪk
(also fruit cake)Word forms: plural fruitcakes
1 N-VAR 干果蛋糕 A fruitcake is a cake that contains raisins, currants, and other dried fruit. 干果蛋糕
2 N-COUNT 疯子;怪人 If you refer to someone as a fruitcake, you mean that they are mad or that their behaviour is very strange. 疯子;怪人 [informal, disapproval]
