ecclesiastical
ɪkliːziæstɪkəl ɪˌkliːzɪəl ɛˌkliziˈæstɪkəl ɪˌkliziˈæstɪkəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （基督教）教会的 Ecclesiastical means belonging to or connected with the Christian Church. （基督教）教会的

  • My ambition was to travel upwards in the ecclesiastical hierarchy.

    我的雄心是在教会中一步一步往上走。

SYN clerical, religious, church, churchly
