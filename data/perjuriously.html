perjury
pɜːʳdʒəri ˈpɜrdʒəri
N-UNCOUNT 伪证；伪证罪 If someone who is giving evidence in a court of law commits perjury, they lie. 伪证；伪证罪 [law]

  • This witness has committed perjury and no reliance can be placed on her evidence.

    这名证人作了伪证，她的证词不可信。

  • ...charges of perjury.

    伪证罪指控

SYN lying under oath, false statement, forswearing, bearing false witness
