play-by-play ˈpleɪbaɪˈpleɪ ˈpleɪbaɪˈpleɪ
N-SING [oft N n] 详细报道; 实况报道A play-by-play is a commentary on a sports game or other event that describes every part of it in great detail. 详细报道; 实况报道 [US]

  • Gene Deckerhoff does radio play-by-play for Florida State.

    吉恩·德克尔霍夫为佛罗里达州的体育赛事进行实况广播。

  • He also was the play-by-play announcer for the Chicago Fire American football team.

    他同时也是美国橄榄球队芝加哥火焰队的比赛实况广播员。
