constellation
kɒnstəleɪʃən ˌkɒnstɪˈleɪʃən ˌkɑnstəˈleɪʃən
Word forms: plural constellations
1 N-COUNT 星座 A constellation is a group of stars which form a pattern and have a name. 星座

  • ...a planet orbiting a star in the constellation of Cepheus. [+ of]

    围绕仙王座中一颗恒星旋转的行星

2 N-COUNT [with supplement, usually NOUN of n] 一群，一组(相似的事物) A constellation of similar things is a group of them. 一群，一组(相似的事物) [formal]

  • Most patients have a constellation of diseases, with few clear-cut distinctions between them.

    大多数患者的病症都很相似，鲜有明显的区别。

  • The largest gallery contains at its centre a constellation of photographs called My Wishes.

    最大的画廊在中心位置展出了一组名为《我的心愿》的摄影作品。
