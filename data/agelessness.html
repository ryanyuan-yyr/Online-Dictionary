ageless
eɪdʒləs ˈeɪdʒlɪs
1 ADJ 永不衰老的 If you describe someone as ageless, you mean that they never seem to look any older. 永不衰老的 [literary]

  • She was rich, beautiful and seemingly ageless.

    她富有而美丽，永远显得那么年轻。

2 ADJ 永恒的；长存的；古老的 If you describe something as ageless, you mean that it is impossible to tell how old it is, or that it seems to have existed for ever. 永恒的；长存的；古老的 [literary]

  • ...the ageless oceans.

    古老的海洋

SYN eternal, enduring, abiding, perennial
