crammed
kræmd kræmd
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 挤满的;塞满的 If a place is crammed with things or people, it is full of them, so that there is hardly room for anything or anyone else. 挤满的;塞满的

  • The house is crammed with priceless furniture and works of art. [+ with/full of]

    房子里摆满了昂贵的家具和艺术品。

  • Living in a divided city crammed with foreign soldiers is not a matter we joke about.

    住在一个已经四分五裂而且到处是外国士兵的城市里，不是一件开玩笑的事。

2 ADJ 拥挤不堪的 If people or things are crammed into a place or vehicle, it is full of them. 拥挤不堪的

  • Between two and three thousand refugees were crammed into the church buildings. [+ into]

    有两三千难民挤进教堂内。
