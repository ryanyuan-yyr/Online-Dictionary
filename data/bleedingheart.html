bleeding heart
(also bleeding-heart)Word forms: plural bleeding hearts
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 假装同情者；滥发善心的人 If you describe someone as a bleeding heart, you are criticizing them for being sympathetic towards people who are poor and suffering, without doing anything practical to help. 假装同情者；滥发善心的人 [disapproval]

  • I'm not a bleeding heart liberal.

    我可不是只说不做的自由主义者。
