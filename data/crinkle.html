crinkle
krɪŋkəl ˈkrɪŋkəl ˈkrɪŋkəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense crinkles, present participle crinkling, past tense , past participle crinkled
1 V-ERG (使)变皱;(使)起皱纹 If something crinkles or if you crinkle it, it becomes slightly creased or folded. (使)变皱;(使)起皱纹

  • He shrugged whimsically, his eyes crinkling behind his glasses. [VERB]

    他滑稽地耸了耸肩，眼镜片后面的双眼眯了起来。

  • When she laughs, she crinkles her perfectly-formed nose. [VERB noun]

    她一笑，俊俏的鼻子就会皱起来。

  • If a plant has spotted or crinkled leaves, do not use it for propagation. [VERB-ed]

    如果植物的叶子有斑点或者萎缩，就不要用来繁殖。

2 N-COUNT 褶痕;皱纹 Crinkles are small creases or folds. 褶痕;皱纹
SYN crease, wrinkle, crumple, ruffle
