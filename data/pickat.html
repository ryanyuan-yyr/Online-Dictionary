pick at
PHRASAL VERB 少量地吃；吃一点点 If you pick at the food that you are eating, you eat only very small amounts of it. 少量地吃；吃一点点

  • Sarah picked at a plate of cheese for supper, but she wasn't really hungry. [VERB PREP. noun]

    萨拉晚餐时只吃一盘奶酪，但她并没有真觉得饿。

SYN nibble (at), peck at, have no appetite for, play or toy with
