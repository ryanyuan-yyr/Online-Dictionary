washed-out ˌwɒʃtˈaʊt ˈwɔʃtˈaʊt
(also washed out)
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (颜色)暗淡的，没有光泽的 Washed-out colours are very pale. (颜色)暗淡的，没有光泽的

  • He stared at me out of those washed-out blue eyes.

    他用暗淡无神的蓝眼睛盯着我看。

  • ...suits in washed-out pastels.

    一套褪了色的衣服

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 精疲力竭的;疲惫不堪的 If someone looks washed-out, they look very tired and lacking in energy. 精疲力竭的;疲惫不堪的

  • She looked washed out and listless.

    她看上去精疲力竭而且情绪低落。
