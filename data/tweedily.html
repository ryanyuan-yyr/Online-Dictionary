tweedy
twiːdi ˈtwidi
1 ADJ-GRADED 乡绅形象的;乡绅派头的 If you describe someone as tweedy, you mean that they have an upper-class but plain appearance, and look as if they live in the country, for example because they are wearing tweed. 乡绅形象的;乡绅派头的

  • An older woman, pink-cheeked and tweedy, appeared in the doorway.

    一位面颊绯红、一副乡下贵族打扮的老妇人出现在门口。

2 ADJ-GRADED 粗花呢制的 Tweedy clothes are made from tweed. 粗花呢制的
