hulk
hʌlk hʌlk
Word forms: plural hulks
1 N-COUNT 巨大的残骸;大型废弃物 The hulk of something is the large, ruined remains of it. 巨大的残骸;大型废弃物

  • ...the ruined hulk of the old church tower. [+ of]

    古老教堂钟楼的残垣断壁

  • I could make out the gutted hulk of the tanker.

    我可以辨认出油船破烂不堪的残骸。

SYN wreck, shell, hull, derelict  
2 N-COUNT （令人恐惧的）庞然大物 You use hulk to describe anything which is large and seems threatening to you. （令人恐惧的）庞然大物

  • I followed his big hulk into the house.

    我跟在他魁梧的身躯后面进了屋子。
