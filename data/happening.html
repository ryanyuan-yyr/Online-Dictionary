happening
hæpənɪŋ ˈhæpənɪŋ ˈhæpənɪŋ ˈhæpnɪŋ
Word forms: plural happenings
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 发生的事情;意外事件;难以解释的事 Happenings are things that happen, often in a way that is unexpected or hard to explain. 发生的事情;意外事件;难以解释的事

  • The Budapest office plans to hire freelance reporters to cover the latest happenings.

    布达佩斯办事处计划聘用自由撰稿记者来报道最近发生的一些事件。

SYN event, incident, occasion, case  
2 ADJ-GRADED 热闹的;活跃的;新潮的 If you describe something or someone as happening, you mean that they are exciting or lively, and involved in the newest fashions or trends. 热闹的;活跃的;新潮的 [informal]

  • ...a definitive area-by-area guide to the hip and happening bands.

    介绍各个地区新潮流行乐队的权威指南

  • ...the most happening place at the moment, the Que Club.

    时下最热门的去处—Que俱乐部

SYN fashionable, modern, popular, cool [informal]
