yea
jeɪ jeɪ
1 CONVENTION （yes的过时用法，用于诗歌或宗教语言）是，对 Yea is an old-fashioned, poetic, or religious word for 'yes'. （yes的过时用法，用于诗歌或宗教语言）是，对
2 CONVENTION （用于口头表决或表示同意）对，是 Yea is sometimes used to mean 'yes' when people are talking about voting for or agreeing to do something. （用于口头表决或表示同意）对，是

  • The House of Commons can merely say yea or nay to the executive judgment.

    下议院对决策层的决定只能表示同意与否。
