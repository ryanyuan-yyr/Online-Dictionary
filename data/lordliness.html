lordly
lɔːʳdli ˈlɔrdli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 趾高气扬的；不可一世的；傲慢的 If you say that someone's behaviour is lordly, you are critical of them because they treat other people in a proud and arrogant way. 趾高气扬的；不可一世的；傲慢的 [disapproval]

  • ...their usual lordly indifference to patients.

    他们对病人那种一贯的高傲冷漠

  • ...the lordly elder brother.

    傲慢的兄长

SYN proud, arrogant, lofty, stuck-up [informal]  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 贵族气派的；高贵的；威严的 Lordly means impressive and suitable for a lord. 贵族气派的；高贵的；威严的

  • ...the site of a lordly mansion.

    一座宏伟宅第的所在地

SYN noble, grand, princely, imperial
