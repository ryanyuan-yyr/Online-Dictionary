sound out
PHRASAL VERB 试探，探询(…的意见) If you sound someone out, you question them in order to find out what their opinion is about something. 试探，探询(…的意见)

  • He is sounding out Middle Eastern governments on ways to resolve the conflict. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他正在探询中东国家的政府要如何解决这次冲突。

  • Sound him out gradually. Make sure it is what he really wants. [VERB noun PREP.]

    要慢慢地打探他的口风，要确保是他的心里话。
