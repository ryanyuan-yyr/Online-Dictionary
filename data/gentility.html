gentility
dʒentɪlɪti dʒɛnˈtɪlɪti
N-UNCOUNT 高贵的出身（或外表） Gentility is the fact or appearance of belonging to a high social class. 高贵的出身（或外表）

  • He surrounds himself with all the trappings of gentility — dogs, horses, and fine paintings.

    他身边满是象征高贵身份的东西—狗，马以及精美的画。

  • The hotel has an air of faded gentility.

    这家宾馆有一种没落的贵族氛围。

SYN refinement, culture, breeding, courtesy
