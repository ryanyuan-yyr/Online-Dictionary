insouciance
ɪnsuːsiəns
N-UNCOUNT 漠不关心;漫不经心 Insouciance is lack of concern shown by someone about something which they might be expected to take more seriously. 漠不关心;漫不经心 [formal]

  • He replied with characteristic insouciance: 'So what?'

    他以一贯的漫不经心回答道：“那又怎样？”

SYN nonchalance, light-heartedness, jauntiness, airiness
