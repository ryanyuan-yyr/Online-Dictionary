begrudge
bɪgrʌdʒ biˈgrʌdʒ bɪˈgrʌdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense begrudges, present participle begrudging, past tense , past participle begrudged
1 VERB 妒忌；嫉妒 If you do not begrudge someone something, you do not feel angry, upset, or jealous that they have got it. 妒忌；嫉妒

  • I certainly don't begrudge him the Nobel Prize. [VERB noun noun]

    我当然不会嫉妒他得了诺贝尔奖。

SYN resent, envy, grudge, be jealous of  
2 VERB 吝啬；吝惜；舍不得 If you do not begrudge something such as time or money, you do not mind giving it up. 吝啬；吝惜；舍不得

  • I do not begrudge the money I have lost. [VERB noun]

    我不在乎自己丢的钱。
