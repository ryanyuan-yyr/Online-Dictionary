memoirs
memwɑːʳz ˈmɛmwɑːz
N-PLURAL [usually with poss] 回忆录 A person's memoirs are a written account of the people who they have known and events that they remember. 回忆录

  • If you've read my earlier memoirs you'll know all about it.

    如果你读过我早期的回忆录，就全都明白了。

  • In his memoirs, De Gaulle wrote that he had come to London determined to save the French nation.

    戴高乐在他的回忆录中写道，他怀着拯救法兰西民族的坚定信念来到了伦敦。

Quotations
To write one's memoirs is to speak ill of everybody except oneselfMarshal Pétain
