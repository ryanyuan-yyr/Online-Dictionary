hearth
hɑːʳθ hɑrθ
Word forms: plural hearths
1 N-COUNT 壁炉地面;壁炉炉床 The hearth is the floor of a fireplace, which sometimes extends into the room. 壁炉地面;壁炉炉床

  • It was winter and there was a huge fire roaring in the hearth.

    时值冬天，壁炉里炉火熊熊。

2 PHRASE 温暖舒适的家庭生活 A person's home and family life can be referred to as their hearth and home . 温暖舒适的家庭生活 [literary]

  • ...a man who leaves his hearth and home to labour as a miner in the inhospitable north.

    离开温暖舒适的家到荒凉的北方做矿工的男子
