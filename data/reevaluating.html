reevaluate riɪvælyueɪt
Word forms: reevaluates, reevaluating, reevaluated
V-T 再度评估If you reevaluate something or someone, you consider them again in order to reassess your opinion of them, for example, about how good or bad they are. 再度评估

  • This may be the time to reevaluate the whole issue.

    这也许是再度评估整个问题的时机。

  • Williams will be reevaluated on a weekly basis, with surgery a possible option.

    威廉姆斯将在每周被重新评估，必要时可能会动手术。
