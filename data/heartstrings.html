heartstrings
hɑːʳtstrɪŋz ˈhɑːtˌstrɪŋz ˈhɑrtˌstrɪŋz
N-PLURAL [oft with poss] 心弦;内心最深处的感情 If you say that someone or something tugs at your heartstrings, you mean that they cause you to feel strong emotions, usually sadness or pity. 心弦;内心最深处的感情

  • She knows exactly how to tug at readers' heartstrings.

    她对如何牵动读者的心弦了如指掌。
