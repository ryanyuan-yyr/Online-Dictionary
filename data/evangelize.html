evangelize
ɪvændʒəlaɪz iˈvændʒəˌlaɪz ɪˈvændʒəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense evangelizes, present participle evangelizing, past tense , past participle evangelized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 evangelise
VERB 使皈依;向…传教；（尤指）使皈依基督教 If someone evangelizes a group or area, they try to convert people to their religion, especially Christianity. 使皈依;向…传教；（尤指）使皈依基督教 [Also VERB]

  • In AD 586 St Kentigern evangelized Tweeddale. [VERB noun]

    公元586年，圣肯蒂格恩使特威代尔郡皈依基督教。
