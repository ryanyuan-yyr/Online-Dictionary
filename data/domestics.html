domestic
dəmestɪk doʊˈmɛstɪk dəˈmɛstɪk
Word forms: plural domestics
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 国内的;本国的 Domestic political activities, events, and situations happen or exist within one particular country. 国内的;本国的

  • ...over 100 domestic flights a day to 15 UK destinations.

    每天超过100架次国内航班飞往英国境内15个目的地

  • ...sales in the domestic market.

    国内市场的销售量

SYN home, state, national, internal  
2See also:gross domestic product 
domestically

  • Opportunities will improve as the company expands domestically and internationally.

    随着国内业务和国际业务的扩展，该公司将获得更好的机会。

  • Domestically, he's going to make some compromises that he doesn't want to make.

    他打算在国内做些他不想做的让步。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 家务的 Domestic duties and activities are concerned with the running of a home and family. 家务的

  • ...a plan for sharing domestic chores.

    分担家务活的计划

SYN household, home, family, private  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] 家用的 Domestic items and services are intended to be used in people's homes rather than in factories or offices. 家用的

  • ...domestic appliances.

    家用器具

5 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 家里的;家庭的;居家的 A domestic situation or atmosphere is one which involves a family and their home. 家里的;家庭的;居家的

  • It was a scene of such domestic bliss.

    这是一幅家庭如此幸福的画面。

  • I was called out to attend a domestic dispute.

    我被叫出去解决一场家庭纠纷。

6 ADJ-GRADED 喜爱家庭生活的;乐于操持家务的 Someone who is domestic enjoys being at home and running a family. 喜爱家庭生活的;乐于操持家务的

  • She was kind and domestic and put her family before her part-time job.

    她人很好也顾家，把家庭看得比兼职工作更为重要。

SYN home-loving, homely, housewifely, stay-at-home  
7 ADJ 非野生的;驯养的;作为宠物饲养的 A domestic animal is one that is not wild and is kept either on a farm to produce food or in someone's home as a pet. 非野生的;驯养的;作为宠物饲养的

  • ...a domestic cat.

    家猫

SYN domesticated, trained, tame, house  
8 N-COUNT 家政工 A domestic, a domestic help, or a domestic worker is a person who is paid to come to help with the work that has to be done in a house such as the cleaning, washing, and ironing. 家政工
SYN servant, help, maid, woman [informal]
