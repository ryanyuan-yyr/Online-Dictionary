chairman
tʃeəʳmən ˈtʃɛəmən ˈtʃɛrmən
Word forms: plural chairmen
1 N-COUNT [oft with poss] (委员会、组织机构的)主席;(公司)总裁 The chairman of a committee, organization, or company is the head of it. (委员会、组织机构的)主席;(公司)总裁

  • Glyn Ford is chairman of the Committee which produced the report. [+ of]

    格林·福特是提交这份报告的委员会的主席。

  • I had done business with the company's chairman.

    我和那家公司的总裁有过生意往来。

SYN director, president, chief, executive  
2 N-COUNT; N-VOC (会议、辩论的)主持人，主席 The chairman of a meeting or debate is the person in charge, who decides when each person is allowed to speak. (会议、辩论的)主持人，主席

  • The chairman declared the meeting open.

    大会主席宣布会议开幕。

  • I hear you, Mr. Chairman.

    我明白，主席先生！

SYN master of ceremonies, spokesman, chair, speaker
