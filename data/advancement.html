advancement
ædvɑːnsmənt -væns- ædˈvænsmənt ədˈvænsmənt
Word forms: plural advancements
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 提升；晋升 Advancement is progress in your job or in your social position. 提升；晋升

  • He cared little for social advancement.

    他不太在意社会地位的提升。

SYN promotion, rise, gain, growth  
2 N-VAR 促进;推动 The advancement of something is the process of helping it to progress or the result of its progress. 促进;推动

  • ..her work for the advancement of the status of women. [+ of]

    她为提高妇女地位所作的工作

SYN progress, advance, headway, forward movement
