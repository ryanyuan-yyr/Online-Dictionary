festive
festɪv ˈfɛstɪvəs ˈfɛstɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 喜庆的;欢乐的;充满节日气氛的 Something that is festive is special, colourful, or exciting, especially because of a holiday or celebration. 喜庆的;欢乐的;充满节日气氛的

  • The town has a festive holiday atmosphere.

    镇子上充盈着一种节日的喜庆气氛。

  • The Captain's Party on Saturday evening is the cruise's most festive event.

    周六晚上的船长晚会是游轮上最喜庆的活动。

SYN celebratory, happy, holiday, carnival  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 与节日有关的;与节庆有关的;(尤指)与圣诞节有关的 Festive means relating to a holiday or celebration, especially Christmas. 与节日有关的;与节庆有关的;(尤指)与圣诞节有关的

  • The factory was due to shut for the festive period.

    节日期间工厂将关门。

  • The government's armed forces have halved their strength for the festive break.

    由于节日休假，政府武装部队人数减半。
