bank card
(also bankcard)Word forms: plural bank cards
1 N-COUNT 银行卡，借记卡，取款机提款卡（美国英语中亦称ATM card）；（英国的）支票保付卡 A bank card is a plastic card which your bank gives you so you can get money from your bank account using a cash machine. It is also called an ATM card in American English. In Britain, you also use bank cards to prove who you are when you pay for something by cheque. 银行卡，借记卡，取款机提款卡（美国英语中亦称ATM card）；（英国的）支票保付卡
2 N-COUNT 信用卡 A bank card is a credit card that is supplied by a bank. 信用卡 [US]
