razor-thin
ADJ (多数地位)微弱的; (利润)极小的A razor-thin majority or profit is a very small one. (多数地位)微弱的; (利润)极小的 [mainly US]

  • There is a razor-thin Democratic majority of one seat in the Senate.

    民主党在参议院中只占一席的微弱多数。

  • ...the consumer retail arena, where profits are razor-thin.

    ...只有微薄利润的消费者零售领域。
