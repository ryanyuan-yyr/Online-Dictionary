doss
dɒs dɒs dɑs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dosses, present participle dossing, past tense , past participle dossed
VERB (通常因无处可去而在不舒适的地方)睡觉，过夜 If someone dosses somewhere, they sleep in a place which is uncomfortable, usually because they have nowhere else to live. (通常因无处可去而在不舒适的地方)睡觉，过夜 [British, informal]

  • ...young people dossing in the streets of our cities. [VERB prep./adv.]

    在我们城市露宿街头的年轻人

Doss down means the same as doss .

  • When we had eaten, we dossed down in the lounge.

    我们吃过后，就在休息室里睡下了。
