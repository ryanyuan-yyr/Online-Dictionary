euphoria
juːfɔːriə juˈfɔriə
N-UNCOUNT 狂喜;兴高采烈 Euphoria is a feeling of intense happiness and excitement. 狂喜;兴高采烈 [Also + of/over]

  • There was euphoria after the elections.

    选举后一片兴高采烈。

  • After the euphoria of yesterday's celebrations, the country will come down to earth today.

    经过昨天热烈的庆祝之后，这个国家今天将会回到现实中来。

SYN elation, joy, ecstasy, bliss
