roadblock
roʊdblɒk ˈroʊdˌblɑk
(also road block)Word forms: plural roadblocks
N-COUNT (警方或军队设置的)路障 When the police or the army put a roadblock across a road, they stop all the traffic going through, for example because they are looking for a criminal. (警方或军队设置的)路障

  • The city police set up roadblocks to check passing vehicles.

    该市警察设置了路障以检查过往车辆。

SYN barrier, block, barricade
