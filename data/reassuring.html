reassuring
riːəʃʊərɪŋ ˌriːəˈʃʊərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED (语言或动作）令人放心的，令人欣慰的 If you find some-one's words or actions reassuring, they make you feel less worried about something. (语言或动作）令人放心的，令人欣慰的

  • It was reassuring to hear John's familiar voice.

    听到约翰熟悉的声音让人放心。

  • She gave me some reassuring news.

    她告诉了我一些令我欣慰的消息。

SYN encouraging, comforting, cheering, heartening  
reassuringly

  • 'It's okay now,' he said reassuringly.

    “现在好了，”他安慰说。
