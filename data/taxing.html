taxing

  • They were comparing notes on each other's progress towards solving that most taxing of all puzzles: the riddle of the human genome.

    他们就解决人类所面临的最大难题，即人类基因组之谜的进展情况交换了意见。

    不大可能叫你做什么太费劲的事儿。
