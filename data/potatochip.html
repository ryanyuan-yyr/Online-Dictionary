potato chip
Word forms: plural potato chips
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 炸土豆片 Potato chips are very thin slices of potato that have been fried until they are hard, dry, and crisp. 炸土豆片 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 crisps
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 炸土豆条 Potato chips are long, thin pieces of potato fried in oil or fat and eaten hot, usually with a meal. 炸土豆条 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 French fries
