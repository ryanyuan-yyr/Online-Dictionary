direct action
N-UNCOUNT 直接行动(如罢工或游行示威) Direct action involves doing something such as going on strike or demonstrating in order to put pressure on an employer or government to do what you want, instead of trying to talk to them. 直接行动(如罢工或游行示威)

  • Only with mass direct action will we obtain such change.

    只有采取大规模直接行动，我们才会取得这样的改变。
