beef up
PHRASAL VERB 改善；加强；改进；提高 If you beef up something, you increase, strengthen, or improve it. 改善；加强；改进；提高 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • ...a campaign to beef up security. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    加强安全的运动

  • Both sides are still beefing up their military strength. [VERB PREP. noun]

    双方仍在增强各自的军事力量。

  • ...a beefed up police presence. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    有所增强的警力

SYN strengthen, increase, build up, enhance
