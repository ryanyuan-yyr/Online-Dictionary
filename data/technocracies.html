technocracy
teknɒkrəsi tɛkˈnɑkrəsi
Word forms: plural technocracies
1 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 技术专家统治群体;技术专家政治集团 A technocracy is a group of scientists, engineers, and other experts who have political power as well as technical knowledge. 技术专家统治群体;技术专家政治集团

  • ...the power of the Brussels technocracy.

    布鲁塞尔技术专家政治集团的权力

2 N-COUNT 技术专家政府;技术官僚政治 A technocracy is a country or society that is controlled by scientists, engineers, and other experts. 技术专家政府;技术官僚政治

  • ...a centralised technocracy.

    技术专家统治的中央集权政府
