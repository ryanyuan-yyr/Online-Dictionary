reversion
rɪvɜːʳʃən rɪˈvɜrʒən rɪˈvɜrʃən
Word forms: plural reversions
1 N-SING (以前的状态、制度或行为的)恢复，回复 A reversion to a previous state, system, or kind of behaviour is a change back to it. (以前的状态、制度或行为的)恢复，回复

  • This is a reversion to the system under which the Royals were paid for nearly 300 years. [+ to]

    又恢复了旧时的体制，王室就是在这种制度下接受了近300年的俸禄。

  • ...a reversion to the emotions of her baby years.

    她向婴儿期情感的回归

2 N-VAR (土地或财产的)归还 The reversion of land or property to a person, family, or country is the return to them of the ownership or control of the land or property. (土地或财产的)归还 [law]
