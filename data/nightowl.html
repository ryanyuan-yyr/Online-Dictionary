night owl
Word forms: plural night owls
N-COUNT 晚睡的人;夜里工作的人;夜猫子 A night owl is someone who regularly stays up late at night, or who prefers to work at night. 晚睡的人;夜里工作的人;夜猫子 [informal]

  • The late-night parties make the hotel a haven for night owls and a hell for anyone with children.

    那些深夜派对把酒店变成了夜猫子的乐园，对于带着孩子的人们则成了地狱。
