ill will
(also ill-will)
N-UNCOUNT 恶意;敌意;怨恨 Ill will is unfriendly or hostile feelings that you have towards someone. 恶意;敌意;怨恨

  • He didn't bear anyone any ill will.

    他对任何人都无恶意。

  • All this has created considerable ill-will towards the armed forces.

    所有这些都让人们对武装力量产生了相当深的怨恨。

SYN hostility, spite, dislike, hatred
