abrade
əbreɪd əˈbreɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abrades, present participle abrading, past tense , past participle abraded
VERB 磨损；磨蚀；擦伤 To abrade something means to scrape or wear down its surface by rubbing it. 磨损；磨蚀；擦伤 [formal]

  • My skin was abraded and very tender. [be VERB-ed]

    我擦破皮了，一碰就疼。

SYN scrape, grind, skin, file
