embezzle
ɪmbezəl ɛmˈbɛzəl ɪmˈbɛzəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense embezzles, present participle embezzling, past tense , past participle embezzled
VERB 贪污，挪用（公款） If someone embezzles money that their organization or company has placed in their care, they take it and use it illegally for their own purposes. 贪污，挪用（公款） [Also VERB]

  • One former director embezzled $34 million in company funds. [VERB noun]

    一名前主管挪用了3,400万美元的公司资金。

SYN misappropriate, steal, appropriate, rob
