coloured
kʌləʳd ˈkʌləd
Word forms: plural coloureds
in AM, use 美国英语用 colored
1 ADJ 有…色的 Something that is coloured a particular colour is that colour. 有…色的

  • The illustration shows a cluster of five roses coloured apricot orange.

    插图上是一簇杏橙色的玫瑰，一共5朵。

  • ...a cheap gold-coloured bracelet.

    廉价的金色手链

2 ADJ 彩色的;有颜色的 Something that is coloured is a particular colour or combination of colours, rather than being just white, black, or the colour that it is naturally. 彩色的;有颜色的

  • You can often choose between plain white or coloured and patterned scarves.

    常可在纯白色或带图案的彩色围巾中作出选择。

  • ...brightly coloured silks laid out on market stalls.

    市场摊位上摆放的颜色鲜艳的丝绸

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有色人种的 A coloured person belongs to a race of people with dark skins. 有色人种的 [offensive, old-fashioned]
4 N-COUNT 有色人种 People whose skin is dark are sometimes referred to as coloureds . 有色人种 [offensive, old-fashioned]
