milky
mɪlki ˈmɪlki
Word forms: comparative milkier, superlative milkiest
1 ADJ 乳白色的;苍白的;淡白色的 If you describe something as milky, you mean that it is pale white in colour. You can describe other colours as milky when they are very pale. 乳白色的;苍白的;淡白色的

  • A milky mist filled the valley.

    白蒙蒙的薄雾弥漫山谷。

  • ...the distant view of mountain ranges, white against a milky blue sky.

    映衬在淡蓝色天空背景下的白茫茫的山脉远景

SYN white, clouded, opaque, cloudy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 含奶多的；掺奶的 Drinks or food that are milky contain a lot of milk. 含奶多的；掺奶的

  • ...a large bowl of milky coffee.

    一大杯拿铁咖啡
