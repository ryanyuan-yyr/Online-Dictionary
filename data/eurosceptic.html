Eurosceptic
jʊəroʊskeptɪk ˌ jʊərəʊˈskɛptɪk
(also Euro-sceptic,eurosceptic)
N-COUNT 欧洲统一怀疑论者;反对英国亲近欧盟的人 A Eurosceptic is someone, especially a politician, who is opposed to closer links between Britain and the European Union. 欧洲统一怀疑论者;反对英国亲近欧盟的人 [British]
Eurosceptic is also an adjective.

  • ...Eurosceptic MPs.

    反对欧洲统一的议员们
