engine room
Word forms: plural engine rooms
1 N-COUNT 轮机舱 On a boat or a ship, the engine room is the place where the engines are. 轮机舱
2 N-COUNT （组织、机构等的）最重要部分，最有影响力部分 If you refer to something as the engine room of an organization or institution, you mean it is the most important or influential part of that organization or institution. （组织、机构等的）最重要部分，最有影响力部分

  • These firms are regarded as the engine room of the British economy. [+ of]

    这些公司被认为是英国经济至关重要的组成部分。
