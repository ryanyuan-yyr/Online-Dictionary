heft
hɛft hɛft hɛft
Word forms: hefts, hefting, hefted
1 V-T 举起If you heft something, you lift it upward. 举起

  • Emmy straightened, hefting her burden.

    艾米挺直身子，举起了身上的担子。

  • He hefted the heavy camera up onto his shoulder.

    他把重重的相机扛到了自己的肩上。

2 N-UNCOUNT 重量; 体积The heft of something is its weight or bulk. 重量; 体积

  • ...a friendly wireless Internet gadget about the size and heft of a thick pork chop.

    ...一个好用的无线网络小装置，和一份厚猪排的重量大小差不多。

3 N-UNCOUNT (观点、辩论等的)分量; 影响力; 重要性If something such as an idea or argument has heft, it has the power to influence or impress people. (观点、辩论等的)分量; 影响力; 重要性

  • Goldstone wished Tom Hill were there to add heft to his argument.

    戈德斯通曾希望汤姆·希尔能在那里给他的辩论增加分量。

  • The 155-page American edition of the book packs the intellectual heft of a much longer text.

    此书的美国版本只有155页，但具有加长版的知智分量。
