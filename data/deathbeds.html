deathbed
deθbed ˈdɛθˌbɛd
Word forms: plural deathbeds
N-COUNT [usually singular, usually with poss, oft on NOUN] 临终之病榻 If someone is on their deathbed, they are in a bed and about to die. 临终之病榻

  • He promised his mother on her deathbed that he would never marry.

    他向垂危的母亲保证他绝不结婚。

  • ...after the man who murdered him nearly 40 years ago made a deathbed confession.

    在那个近40 年前谋杀了他的男子临终供认了罪行之后
