start on
PHRASAL VERB 开始做(需要做的事) If you start on something that needs to be done, you start dealing with it. 开始做(需要做的事)

  • No need for you to start on the washing-up yet. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你没有必要现在就开始洗餐具。

  • He has not finished his drama, in fact, he has not started on it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他还没有完成自己的剧本，实际上，他还没开始动手写。
