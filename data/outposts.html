outpost
aʊtpoʊst ˈaʊtˌpoʊst
Word forms: plural outposts
N-COUNT 贸易站；前哨基地；（设在国外的）军事基地 An outpost is a small group of buildings used for trading or military purposes, either in a distant part of your own country or in a foreign country. 贸易站；前哨基地；（设在国外的）军事基地 [Also + of]

  • ...a remote mountain outpost, linked to the outside world by the poorest of roads.

    靠一条崎岖艰险的山路与外界相连的偏远山区哨站

SYN frontier, border, borderline, borderland
