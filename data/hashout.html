hash out
1 PHRASAL VERB [also V n P] 充分讨论后决定If people hash out something such as a plan or an agreement, they decide on it after a lot of discussion. 充分讨论后决定 [US]

  • The House and Senate are to begin soon hashing out an agreement for sanctions legislation.

    参众两院很快将通过充分讨论就制裁立法达成一项协议。

2 PHRASAL VERB 充分协商后解决If people hash out a problem or a dispute, they discuss it thoroughly until they reach an agreement. 充分协商后解决 [US]

  • ...while the parties try to hash out their differences in court.

    …然而各党派试图在法庭上解决他们的分歧。
