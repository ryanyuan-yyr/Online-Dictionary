third
θɜːʳd θɜrd
Word forms: plural thirds
1 ORD 第三 The third item in a series is the one that you count as number three. 第三

  • I sleep on the third floor.

    我睡在三楼。

  • It was the third time one of his cars had gone up in flames.

    这是他的车第三次起火。

  • He came third in the poll with 149 votes.

    他在选举中获得 149 票，名列第三。

  • The attack was the third so far this year.

    这是今年迄今为止的第三次袭击。

2 FRACTION 三分之一 A third is one of three equal parts of something. 三分之一

  • A third of the cost went into technology and services.

    有三分之一的支出投入了技术和服务。

  • Only one third get financial help from their fathers.

    只有三分之一的人得到父亲的经济援助。

  • He divided their kingdom into thirds.

    他把他们的王国分成了三个部分。

3 ADV 第三(点) You say third when you want to make a third point or give a third reason for something. 第三(点)

  • First, interest rates may take longer to fall than is hoped. Second, in real terms, lending may fall. Third, bad loans could wipe out much of any improvement.

    首先，利率的下降可能没有希望的那样快;第二，实际贷款额可能会减少;第三，坏账很可能抵消大部分增长。

4 N-COUNT [usually singular] 三等学位(英国大学颁发的最低级荣誉学位) A third is the lowest honours degree that can be obtained from a British university. 三等学位(英国大学颁发的最低级荣誉学位)
