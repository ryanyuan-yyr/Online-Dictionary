stubble
stʌbəl ˈstʌbəl
1 N-UNCOUNT (作物收割后的)茬 Stubble is the short stalks which are left standing in fields after corn or wheat has been cut. (作物收割后的)茬

  • The stubble was burning in the fields.

    地里的麦茬正在燃烧。

SYN straw, stalks  
2 N-UNCOUNT 胡子茬 The very short hairs on a man's face when he has not shaved recently are referred to as stubble . 胡子茬

  • His face was covered with the stubble of several nights.

    他几天没刮胡子，脸上胡子拉碴。

SYN bristles, hair, whiskers, beard
