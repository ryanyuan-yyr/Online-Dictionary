lackey
læki ˈlæki
Word forms: plural lackeys
N-COUNT 唯命是从之徒；哈巴狗；应声虫 If you describe someone as a lackey, you are critical of them because they follow someone's orders completely, without ever questioning them. 唯命是从之徒；哈巴狗；应声虫 [disapproval]

  • I'm not staying as a paid lackey to act as your yes-man.

    我不要再做拿钱任你使唤的应声虫。

SYN hanger-on, fawner, pawn, attendant
