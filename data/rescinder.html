rescind
rɪsɪnd rɪˈsɪnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rescinds, present participle rescinding, past tense , past participle rescinded
VERB 废除;废止;撤销;停止 If a government or a group of people in power rescind a law or agreement, they officially withdraw it and state that it is no longer valid. 废除;废止;撤销;停止 [formal]

  • Trade Union leaders have demanded the government rescind the price rise. [VERB noun]

    工会领导已经要求政府阻止价格上涨。

SYN annul, recall, reverse, cancel
