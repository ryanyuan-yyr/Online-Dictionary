approachable
əproʊtʃəbəl əˈprəʊtʃəbəl əˈproʊtʃəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 平易近人的;友善的 If you describe someone as approachable, you think that they are friendly and easy to talk to. 平易近人的;友善的 [approval]

  • We found him very approachable and easy to talk with.

    我们认为他非常和蔼可亲，容易交谈。

SYN friendly, open, cordial, sociable  
2 ADJ-GRADED 易于理解的；容易接受的 If you describe an idea or piece of work as approachable, you think that it is presented in a way which people find enjoyable and easy to understand. 易于理解的；容易接受的 [approval]

  • The text is approachable, coping well with quite complicated subjects.

    这篇课文把相当复杂的主题讲述得深入浅出、通俗易懂。
