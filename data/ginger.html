ginger
dʒɪndʒəʳ ˈdʒɪndʒər
1 N-UNCOUNT 姜 Ginger is the root of a plant that is used to flavour food. It has a sweet spicy flavour and is often sold in powdered form. 姜
2 COLOUR 姜黄色 Ginger is used to describe things that are orangey-brown in colour. 姜黄色

  • She was a mature lady with dyed ginger hair.

    她是一位头发染成姜黄色的成熟女性。
