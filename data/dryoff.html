dry off
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)变干;弄干 If something dries off or if you dry it off, the moisture on its surface disappears or is removed. (使)变干;弄干 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • They are then scrubbed with clean water and left to dry off for an hour or two in a warm room. [VERB PREP.]

    然后用清水擦洗它们，再放到一间暖和的屋子里放一到两小时晾干。

  • When the bath water started to cool I got out, dried myself off, and dressed. [VERB noun PREP.]

    洗澡水开始变凉时我出来了,擦干身子后穿上了衣服。
