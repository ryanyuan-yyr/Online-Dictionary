unspectacular
ʌnspektækjʊləʳ
ADJ-GRADED 不引人注意的；不起眼的 If you describe something as unspectacular, you mean that it is rather dull and not remarkable in any way. 不引人注意的；不起眼的

  • His progress at school had been unspectacular compared to his brother.

    和弟弟相比，他在学校里的进步很不起眼。

  • ...pleasant, if largely unspectacular, countryside.

    不甚起眼但令人愉悦的田园风光

SYN average, mediocre, unremarkable, middling
