yellowy
jeloʊi ˈjɛloʊi
ADJ 淡黄的;微黄的;略带黄色的 Something that is yellowy is slightly yellow in colour. 淡黄的;微黄的;略带黄色的

  • She had long hair she'd bleached herself, all yellowy and orangey.

    她一头长发，全部被她自己给染成了淡黄色和浅橙色。

Yellowy is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...black ink, fading now to a yellowy brown.

    现在已褪成棕黄色的黑墨水
