medley
medli ˈmɛdli
Word forms: plural medleys
1 N-COUNT 集成曲;集锦;音乐串烧 In music, a medley is a collection of different tunes or songs that are played one after the other as a single piece of music. 集成曲;集锦;音乐串烧

  • ...a medley of traditional songs. [+ of]

    传统歌曲集锦

SYN mixture, confusion, jumble, assortment  
2 N-COUNT 混合泳 In sport, a medley is a swimming race in which the four main strokes are used one after the other. 混合泳

  • Japan won the Men's 200 metres Individual Medley.

    日本赢得了男子200米个人混合泳比赛。

3 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] 杂烩;什锦;混合物 A medley of different foods or other things is a mixture of them. 杂烩;什锦;混合物

  • ...a medley of four fish in a cream sauce.

    奶油四喜鱼

  • We communicated in a medley of foreign words and universal gestures.

    我们边说着外语，边打着通用的手势进行交流。
