livery
lɪvəri ˈlɪvəri
Word forms: plural liveries
1 N-VAR （仆人的）制服，号衣 A servant's livery is the special uniform that he or she wears. （仆人的）制服，号衣

  • She was attended by servants in splendid livery and powdered wigs.

    她由穿着华丽制服、头戴扑粉假发的仆人们伺候着。

SYN costume, dress, clothing, suit  
2 N-COUNT [usually with poss] （公司的）徽记，专色 The livery of a particular company is the special design or set of colours associated with it that is put on its products and possessions. （公司的）徽记，专色

  • ...buffet cars in the railway company's bright red and yellow livery.

    上有铁路公司醒目的红黄专色的餐车
