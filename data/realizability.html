realizable
riːəlaɪzəbəl
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 realisable
1 ADJ 可实现的;可行的 If your hopes or aims are realizable, there is a possibility that the things that you want to happen will happen. 可实现的;可行的 [formal]

  • ...the reasonless assumption that one's dreams and desires were realizable.

    认为人的梦想和欲望可以实现的毫无根据的假设

2 ADJ （财富）可变现的 Realizable wealth is money that can be easily obtained by selling something. （财富）可变现的 [formal]

  • In many cases this realizable wealth is not realized during the lifetime of the home owner.

    许多情况下业主有生之年都没有把这笔可变现的财产变现。

  • They must prove they own £250,000 of realisable assets.

    他们必须证明拥有25万英镑的可变现财产。
