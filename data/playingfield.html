playing field
Word forms: plural playing fields
1 N-COUNT 操场；运动场 A playing field is a large area of grass where people play sports. 操场；运动场

  • ...the playing fields of the girls' Grammar School.

    女子文法学校的运动场

2 PHRASE 公平竞争的环境；机会均等的环境 You talk about a level playing field to mean a situation that is fair, because no competitor or opponent in it has an advantage over another. 公平竞争的环境；机会均等的环境

  • We ask for a level playing field when we compete with foreign companies.

    我们寻求一个能与外国公司公平竞争的环境。
