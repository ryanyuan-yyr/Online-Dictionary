expedition
ekspɪdɪʃən ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən
Word forms: plural expeditions
1 N-COUNT 远征;探险 An expedition is an organized journey that is made for a particular purpose such as exploration. 远征;探险

  • ...Byrd's 1928 expedition to Antarctica. [+ to]

    伯德1928年前往南极洲的探险

SYN journey, exploration, mission, voyage  
2 N-COUNT 远征队;探险队 You can refer to a group of people who are going on an expedition as an expedition . 远征队;探险队

  • Forty-three members of the expedition were killed.

    探险队中43名成员遇难。

SYN team, crew, party, group  
3 N-COUNT 远足;短途旅行 An expedition is a short journey or trip that you make for pleasure. 远足;短途旅行

  • Caroline joined them on the shopping expeditions.

    卡罗琳加入了他们的购物之旅。

  • ...a fishing expedition.

    垂钓之旅
