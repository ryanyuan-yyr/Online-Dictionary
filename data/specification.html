specification
spesɪfɪkeɪʃən ˌspɛsəfɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural specifications
N-COUNT 规格;具体要求 A specification is a requirement which is clearly stated, for example about the necessary features in the design of something. 规格;具体要求

  • Troll's exclusive, personalized luggage is made to our own exacting specifications in heavy-duty PVC/nylon.

    特罗尔专用的、个性化的行李箱是按照我们自己严格的规格要求用耐用聚氯乙烯/尼龙材料定制的。

  • Legislation will require UK petrol companies to meet an EU specification for petrol.

    法规将要求英国的汽油公司必须达到欧盟对汽油的规格要求。

  • ...officials constrained by rigid job specifications.

    受到严格的岗位要求限制的官员们
