breakage
breɪkɪdʒ ˈbreɪkˌɪdʒ
Word forms: plural breakages
1 N-VAR 破损；毁坏 Breakage is the act of breaking something. 破损；毁坏

  • Brushing wet hair can cause stretching and breakage.

    梳理湿头发会拉抻头发，造成损伤。

  • Check that your insurance policy covers breakages and damage during removals.

    查看一下你的保险单，看其是否对搬运过程中的破损和毁坏赔偿。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 破损物品 A breakage is something that has been broken. 破损物品

  • Check that everything is in good repair before moving in, as you have to replace breakages.

    在搬进来前查看一下屋里的东西是否都完好无损，因为以后破损的物件要由你来更换。
