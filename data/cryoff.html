cry off
PHRASAL VERB 变卦;取消诺言;打退堂鼓 If you cry off, you tell someone that you cannot do something that you have agreed or arranged to do. 变卦;取消诺言;打退堂鼓

  • Barron invited her to the races and she agreed, but she caught flu and had to cry off at the last minute. [VERB PREP.]

    她接受了巴伦一起去看比赛的邀请，但她感冒了，最后不得不取消约定。

SYN back out, withdraw, quit, cop out [slang]
