lined
laɪnd
1 ADJ-GRADED 有皱纹的 If someone's face or skin is lined, it has lines on it as a result of old age, tiredness, worry, or illness. 有皱纹的

  • His lined face was that of an old man.

    他的脸像老人一样布满皱纹。

SYN wrinkled, worn, furrowed, wizened  
2 ADJ （纸张）画有横线的 Lined paper has lines printed across it to help you write neatly. （纸张）画有横线的
3 See also: line
