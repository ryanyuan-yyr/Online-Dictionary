life's work laɪfs wɜːk
N-SING [usually poss NOUN] 毕生的事业；一生中最大的成就 Someone's life's work or life work is the main activity that they have been involved in during their life, or their most important achievement. 毕生的事业；一生中最大的成就

  • An exhibition of his life's work is being shown in the garden of his home.

    他平生的得意之作现正在他家的花园里展出。

  • My father's life work was devoted to the conservation of the Longleat estate.

    我父亲一生致力于保护朗利特庄园。
