surrealist
səriːəlɪst
Word forms: plural surrealists
1 ADJ-GRADED 超现实主义的 Surrealist means related to or in the style of surrealism. 超现实主义的

  • Dali's shoe hat was undoubtedly the most surrealist idea he ever worked on with Schiaparelli.

    达利的鞋状帽子无疑是他与夏帕瑞丽合作出的最具超现实主义色彩的创意。

2 N-COUNT 超现实主义作家;超现实主义艺术家 A surrealist is an artist or writer whose work is based on the ideas of surrealism. 超现实主义作家;超现实主义艺术家
