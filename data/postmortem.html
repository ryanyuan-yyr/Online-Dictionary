post-mortem poʊst mɔːʳtəm
(also post mortem,postmortem)Word forms: plural post-mortems
1 N-COUNT 验尸；尸体检验；尸体解剖 A post-mortem is a medical examination of a dead person's body in order to find out how they died. 验尸；尸体检验；尸体解剖
2 N-COUNT （尤指对失败或出差错的事情的）事后分析；事后检讨 A post-mortem is an examination of something that has recently happened, especially something that has failed or gone wrong. （尤指对失败或出差错的事情的）事后分析；事后检讨
