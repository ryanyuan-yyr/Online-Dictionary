you
juː juː jʊ ju
Word forms: plural yous
You is the second person pronoun. You can refer to one or more people and is used as the subject of a verb or the object of a verb or preposition. you是第二人称代词。you可指一个或多个人，用作动词主语，或者是动词或介词宾语。
1 PRON 你,您,你们（可用于名词前指定谈话对象） A speaker or writer uses you to refer to the person or people that they are talking or writing to. It is possible to use you before a noun to make it clear which group of people you are talking to. 你,您,你们（可用于名词前指定谈话对象）

  • When I saw you across the room I knew I'd met you before.

    看到你穿过房间时，我就知道我以前曾经见过你。

  • You two seem very different to me.

    你们两个似乎与我大不一样。

  • I could always talk to you about anything in the world.

    我总是可以和你谈论世界上的任何事情。

  • What is alternative health care? What can it do for you?.

    什么是另类医疗？它能为你做些什么?

  • What you kids need is more exercise.

    你们这些孩子需要的是多做运动。

2 PRON （用于口语和非正式书面语中表示泛指）你，任何人 In spoken English and informal written English, you is sometimes used to refer to people in general. （用于口语和非正式书面语中表示泛指）你，任何人

  • Getting good results gives you confidence.

    取得好的结果会给人以信心。

  • In those days you did what you were told.

    那时候，你只能听命于人。

3 PRON-PLURAL （某些方言中指两个或多个人时有时用yous代替you）你们 In some dialects of English, yous is sometimes used instead of 'you' when talking to two or more people. （某些方言中指两个或多个人时有时用yous代替you）你们

  • 'Yous two are no 'gettin' paid,' he said. 'Ye're too lazy!'

    “你们两个别指望拿到工钱，”他说。“你们太懒了！”
