dutiful
djuːtɪfʊl duː- ˈdutɪfəl ˈdjutɪfəl
ADJ-GRADED 顺从的;尽职的;恭敬的 If you say that someone is dutiful, you mean that they do everything that they are expected to do. 顺从的;尽职的;恭敬的

  • The days of the dutiful wife, who sacrifices her career for her husband, are over.

    贤妻为了丈夫而牺牲自己事业的时代已经结束了。

SYN conscientious, devoted, obedient, respectful  
dutifully

  • The inspector dutifully recorded the date in a large red book.

    检查员很尽职地在一本大红册子上记下了日期。
