madhouse
mædhaʊs ˈmædˌhaʊs
Word forms: plural madhouses
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 乱哄哄的地方；喧闹的场所 If you describe a place or situation as a madhouse, you mean that it is full of confusion and noise. 乱哄哄的地方；喧闹的场所

  • That place is a madhouse.

    那个地方乱哄哄的。

SYN chaos, turmoil, bedlam, Babel  
2 N-COUNT 精神病院；疯人院 A madhouse is a mental hospital. 精神病院；疯人院 [old-fashioned]
