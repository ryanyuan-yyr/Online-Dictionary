collision
kəlɪʒən kəˈlɪʒən
Word forms: plural collisions
1 N-VAR 碰撞;相撞 A collision occurs when a moving object crashes into something. 碰撞;相撞

  • They were on their way to the Shropshire Union Canal when their van was involved in a collision with a car. [+ with]

    在去往什罗普郡联盟运河的路上，他们的小货车与一辆小车相撞。

  • I saw a head-on collision between two aeroplanes. [+ between]

    我看见两架飞机迎面相撞。

SYN crash, impact, accident, smash  
2 N-COUNT (不同文化或思想的)碰撞，冲突，抵触 A collision of cultures or ideas occurs when two very different cultures or people meet and conflict. (不同文化或思想的)碰撞，冲突，抵触 [Also + between/with]

  • It's the collision of disparate ideas that alters one's perspective.

    是迥然不同的观点之间的碰撞改变了人们的看法。

  • The play represents the collision of three generations. [+ of]

    这部戏表现了三代人之间的矛盾冲突。
