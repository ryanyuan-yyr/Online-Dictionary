monolithic
mɒnəlɪθɪk ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED (组织或体系)庞大而僵化的，单一的 If you refer to an organization or system as monolithic, you are critical of it because it is very large and very slow to change, and does not seem to have different parts with different characters. (组织或体系)庞大而僵化的，单一的 [disapproval]

  • ...an authoritarian and monolithic system.

    庞大僵化的独裁体制

SYN inflexible, rigid, impenetrable, intractable  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 庞大而无特点的;巨大而单调的 If you describe something such as a building as monolithic, you do not like it because it is very large and plain with no character. 庞大而无特点的;巨大而单调的 [disapproval]

  • ...a huge monolithic concrete building.

    毫无特色的庞大混凝土建筑

SYN huge, giant, massive, imposing
