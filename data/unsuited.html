unsuited
ʌnsuːtɪd ʌnˈsuːtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 不适宜的；不相称的 If someone or something is unsuited to a particular job, situation, or place, they do not have the right qualities or characteristics for it. 不适宜的；不相称的

  • He's totally unsuited to the job. [+ to]

    他完全不适合做这份工作。

  • The snow cruiser proved hopelessly unsuited to Antarctic conditions. [+ to]

    事实证明雪地车根本不适合在南极地区使用。

2 ADJ-GRADED （男女）不般配的，彼此不合适的 If two people, especially a man and a woman, are unsuited to each other, they have different personalities or interests, and so are unlikely to have a successful relationship. （男女）不般配的，彼此不合适的

  • By the end of that first year, I knew how totally unsuited we were to each other. [+ to]

    到了第一年年底，我明白了我们俩根本不般配。
