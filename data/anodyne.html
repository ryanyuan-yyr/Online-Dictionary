anodyne
ænədaɪn ˈænəˌdaɪn ˈænoʊˌdaɪn ˈænəˌdaɪn
ADJ-GRADED 平淡无奇的；四平八稳的；乏味无趣的 If you describe something as anodyne, you are criticizing it because it has no strong characteristics and is not likely to excite, interest, or upset anyone. 平淡无奇的；四平八稳的；乏味无趣的 [formal, disapproval]

  • Their quarterly meetings were anodyne affairs.

    他们的季度会议乏善可陈。

SYN bland, dull, boring, insipid
