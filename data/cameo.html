cameo
kæmioʊ ˈkæmiˌoʊ
Word forms: plural cameos
1 N-COUNT 精彩描写;小品文;精彩片断 A cameo is a short description or piece of acting which expresses cleverly and neatly the nature of a situation, event, or person's character. 精彩描写;小品文;精彩片断

  • ...a succession of memorable cameos of Scottish history.

    一系列反映苏格兰历史的令人难忘的精彩片断

  • He played a cameo role, that of a young Aids patient in hospital.

    他扮演一个非常出彩的小角色:一名住院的年轻艾滋病患者。

2 N-COUNT (通常为椭圆形的)多彩浮雕首饰 A cameo is a piece of jewellery, usually oval in shape, consisting of a raised stone figure or design fixed on to a flat stone of another colour. (通常为椭圆形的)多彩浮雕首饰

  • ...a cameo brooch.

    多彩浮雕胸针
