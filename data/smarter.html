smart
smɑːʳt smɑrt
Word forms: comparative smarter, superlative smartest, 3rd person singular present tense smarts, present participle smarting, past tense , past participle smarted
1 ADJ-GRADED 外表整洁的 Smart people and things are pleasantly neat and clean in appearance. 外表整洁的 [mainly British]

  • He was smart and well groomed but not good looking.

    他仪容整洁，穿戴漂亮，就是人长得不好看。

  • I was dressed in a smart navy blue suit.

    我身穿一套帅气的藏青色西服。

  • ...smart new offices.

    整洁的新办公室

SYN chic, trim, neat, fashionable  
smartly

  • He dressed very smartly which was important in those days.

    他衣冠楚楚—这一点在那个时候很重要。

  • ...a smartly-painted door.

    漆刷一新的门

smartness

  • The jumper strikes the perfect balance between comfort and smartness.

    这件套头毛衣又舒服又漂亮。

2 ADJ-GRADED 机灵的;聪明的 You can describe someone who is clever as smart . 机灵的;聪明的

  • He thinks he's smarter than Sarah is.

    他认为自己比萨拉聪明。

  • Buying expensive furniture is not necessarily the smartest move to make.

    购买昂贵的家具不见得就是最明智之举。

SYN clever, bright [informal] , intelligent, quick  
3See also:smartly  street smart 
4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 时髦的;气派的 A smart place or event is connected with wealthy and fashionable people. 时髦的;气派的

  • ...smart London dinner parties.

    气派不凡的伦敦晚宴

  • ...a smart residential district.

    豪华的住宅区

5 ADJ [ADJ n] (炸弹、武器等)灵巧的,精密制导的 Smart bombs and weapons are guided by computers and lasers so that they hit their targets accurately. (炸弹、武器等)灵巧的,精密制导的
6 VERB (身体的某个部位或伤口)剧痛，刺痛 If a part of your body or a wound smarts, you feel a sharp stinging pain in it. (身体的某个部位或伤口)剧痛，刺痛

  • My eyes smarted from the smoke. [VERB]

    我的眼睛被烟薰得很疼。

SYN sting, burn, tingle, pain  
7 VERB [usually cont] (因批评、失败等)感到不高兴，感到难受 If you are smarting from something such as criticism or failure, you feel upset about it. (因批评、失败等)感到不高兴，感到难受 [journalism]

  • The Americans were still smarting from their defeat in the Vietnam War. [VERB + from]

    美国人仍然对越南战争的失败耿耿于怀。

  • He is still smarting over criticism of his victorious but clumsy performance. [VERB prep.]

    他还在为别人批评他尽管最终胜出但表现笨拙感到闷闷不乐。

SYN feel resentful, feel annoyed, feel offended, feel wounded  
8 PHRASE 时尚有钱人的圈子 The smart set is a group of fashionable and wealthy people. 时尚有钱人的圈子 [journalism]

  • ...Spago, favourite eating place of the Los Angeles smart set.

    思派古—洛杉矶的时尚富人最喜欢光顾的用餐之地

9 the smart money→see: money
