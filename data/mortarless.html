mortar
mɔːʳtəʳ ˈmɔrtər
Word forms: plural mortars
1 N-COUNT 迫击炮 A mortar is a big gun which fires missiles high into the air over a short distance. 迫击炮

  • The two sides exchanged fire with artillery, mortars and small arms.

    双方交火时动用了大炮、迫击炮和轻武器。

  • He was killed in a mortar attack.

    他死于迫击炮炮击。

2 N-UNCOUNT 灰浆;砂浆;胶泥 Mortar is a mixture of sand, water, and cement or lime which is put between bricks to hold them together. 灰浆;砂浆;胶泥
3 N-COUNT 研钵;臼 A mortar is a bowl in which you can crush things such as herbs, spices, or grain using a rod called a pestle. 研钵;臼
