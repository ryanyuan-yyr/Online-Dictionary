up-to-the-minute ˌʌptəðəˈmɪnɪt
(also up to the minute)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 最近的；最新的 Up-to-the-minute information is the latest information that you can get about something. 最近的；最新的

  • ...24 hours a day up-to-the-minute instant news.

    一天24小时最新消息即时播报

  • Computers give them up-to-the-minute information on sales and stocks.

    计算机为他们提供最新的销量和股票方面的信息。
