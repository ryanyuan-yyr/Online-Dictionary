insect
ɪnsekt ˈɪnˌsɛkt
Word forms: plural insects
N-COUNT 昆虫;虫 An insect is a small animal that has six legs. Most insects have wings. Ants, flies, butterflies, and beetles are all insects. 昆虫;虫
SYN bug, creepy-crawly [British , informal] , gogga [South Africa , informal]
