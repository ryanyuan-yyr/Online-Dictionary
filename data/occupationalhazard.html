occupational hazard
ˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃənəl ˈhæzəd
Word forms: plural occupational hazards
N-COUNT 职业性危害；业余爱好的风险 An occupational hazard is something unpleasant that you may suffer or experience as a result of doing your job or hobby. 职业性危害；业余爱好的风险

  • Catching colds is unfortunately an occupational hazard in this profession.

    不幸的是，容易感冒是这个行业的职业性风险。
