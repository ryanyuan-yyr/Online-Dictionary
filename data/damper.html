damper
dæmpəʳ ˈdæmpər
Word forms: plural dampers
1 N-COUNT (火炉、锅炉或熔炉的)调节风门，风挡 A damper is a small sheet of metal in a fire, boiler, or furnace that can be moved to increase or reduce the amount of air that enters. (火炉、锅炉或熔炉的)调节风门，风挡
2 N-COUNT (钢琴等乐器的)制音器，减音器 A damper is a device in a piano or similar musical instrument which makes the sound less loud by restricting the movement of the strings. (钢琴等乐器的)制音器，减音器
3 PHRASE 使扫兴;抑制 To put a damper on something means to have an effect on it which stops it being as enjoyable or as successful as it should be. 使扫兴;抑制 [informal]

  • The cold weather put a damper on our plans.

    寒冷的天气给我们的计划泼了冷水。

  • That would put a damper on the future growth of U.S. steel exports.

    那将会抑制美国钢铁出口未来的增长。
