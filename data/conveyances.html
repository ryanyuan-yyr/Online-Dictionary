conveyance
kənveɪəns kənˈveɪəns
Word forms: plural conveyances
1 N-COUNT 运输工具;交通工具 A conveyance is a vehicle. 运输工具;交通工具 [literary]

  • Mahoney had never seen such a conveyance before.

    马奥尼以前从来没有见过这样的运输工具。

SYN vehicle, transport  
2 N-UNCOUNT 运输;运送;输送 The conveyance of something is the process of carrying or transporting it from one place to another. 运输;运送;输送 [formal]

  • ...the conveyance of bicycles on Regional Railways trains. [+ of]

    通过地区铁路公司的火车运输自行车

SYN transportation, movement, transfer, transport
