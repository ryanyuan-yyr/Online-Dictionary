genesis
dʒenɪsɪs ˈdʒɛnəsɪs
N-SING [usually with poss] （事物的）开端，诞生，创始 The genesis of something is its beginning, birth, or creation. （事物的）开端，诞生，创始 [formal]

  • The project had its genesis two years earlier.

    该项目是两年前开始的。

  • His speech was an exposition of the genesis of the conflict.

    他的演讲阐述了冲突的起源。

SYN beginning, source, root, origin
