clandestine
klændestɪn klænˈdɛstɪn
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (通常因违法而)偷偷摸摸的，暗地里的，秘密的 Something that is clandestine is hidden or kept secret, often because it is illegal. (通常因违法而)偷偷摸摸的，暗地里的，秘密的 [formal]

  • ...their clandestine meetings.

    他们的秘密会晤

SYN secret, private, hidden, underground  
clandestinely

  • He left the country clandestinely.

    他偷偷地离开了该国。
