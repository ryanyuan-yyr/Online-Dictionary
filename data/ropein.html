rope in
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 说服…帮忙 If you say that you were roped in to do a particular task, you mean that someone persuaded you to help them do that task. 说服…帮忙 [mainly British, informal]

  • Visitors were roped in for potato picking and harvesting. [be V-ed P + for]

    参观者们被说动来帮忙挖土豆和收土豆。

  • I got roped in to help with the timekeeping. [get V-ed P to-inf]

    我被拉来帮忙计时。
