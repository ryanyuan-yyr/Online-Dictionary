schizophrenic
skɪtsəfrenɪk ˌskɪtsɛˈfrɛnɪk ˌskɪzəˈfrɛnɪk ˌskɪtsɛˈfrinɪk ˌskɪzəˈfrinɪk
Word forms: plural schizophrenics
1 N-COUNT 精神分裂症患者 A schizophrenic is a person who is suffering from schizophrenia. 精神分裂症患者

  • He was diagnosed as a paranoid schizophrenic.

    他被确诊患有偏执型精神分裂症。

Schizophrenic is also an adjective.

  • ...a schizophrenic patient.

    精神分裂症患者

  • ...schizophrenic tendencies.

    精神分裂倾向

2 ADJ-GRADED (态度、行为)自相矛盾的，前后不一致的，反复无常的 Someone's attitude or behaviour can be described as schizophrenic when they seem to have very different opinions or purposes at different times. (态度、行为)自相矛盾的，前后不一致的，反复无常的

  • ...the schizophrenic mood of the American public.

    美国公众前后矛盾的情绪
