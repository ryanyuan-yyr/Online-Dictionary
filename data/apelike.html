ape
eɪp eɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense apes, present participle aping, past tense , past participle aped
1 N-COUNT 无尾猿；类人猿；猿 Apes are chimpanzees, gorillas, and other animals in the same family. 无尾猿；类人猿；猿

  • ...the theory that man is descended from the apes.

    认为人类是从类人猿演变而来的理论

2 VERB 模仿；学…的样子 If you ape someone's speech or behaviour, you imitate it. 模仿；学…的样子

  • Modelling yourself on someone you admire is not the same as aping all they say or do. [VERB noun]

    以偶像为榜样并不是说要模仿他们的一言一行。

  • ...French films which merely aped Hollywood. [VERB noun]

    只是照搬好莱坞模式的法国电影

SYN imitate, copy, mirror, echo  
3 PHRASE 怒不可遏;暴跳如雷 If someone goes ape, they become very angry and upset. 怒不可遏;暴跳如雷 [informal]

  • When Colonel Harper found out, he would go ape.

    哈珀上校发现了一定会勃然大怒。

SYN be furious, storm, rage, rave
