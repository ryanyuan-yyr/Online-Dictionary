abound
əbaʊnd əˈbaʊnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abounds, present participle abounding, past tense , past participle abounded
VERB 大量存在;盛产;充足 If things abound, or if a place abounds with things, there are very large numbers of them. 大量存在;盛产;充足 [formal]

  • Stories abound about when he was in charge. [VERB]

    关于他在任时的传闻多不胜数。

  • Venice abounds in famous hotels. [V with/in n]

    威尼斯有许多知名的酒店。

  • The book abounds with close-up images from space. [V + with/in]

    这本书里有大量拍自太空的特写照片。

SYN be plentiful, thrive, flourish, be numerous
