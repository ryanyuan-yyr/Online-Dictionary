impenetrable
ɪmpenɪtrəbəl ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不能穿过的;刺不进的;透不过的 If you describe something such as a barrier or a forest as impenetrable, you mean that it is impossible or very difficult to get through. 不能穿过的;刺不进的;透不过的

  • ...the Caucasus range, an almost impenetrable barrier between Europe and Asia.

    高加索山脉,几乎难以穿越的欧亚屏障

SYN impassable, solid, impervious, thick  
2 ADJ-GRADED 费解的;难以理解的 If you describe something such as a book or a theory as impenetrable, you are emphasizing that it is impossible or very difficult to understand. 费解的;难以理解的 [emphasis]

  • His books are notoriously impenetrable.

    他的书出了名的晦涩难懂。

  • ...these impenetrable poems.

    这些隐晦难懂的诗

impenetrably

  • ...impenetrably detailed reports on product sales.

    详细得令人费解的产品销售报告
