rechargeable
riːtʃɑːʳdʒəbəl
Word forms: plural rechargeables
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （电池或电器）可充电的 Rechargeable batteries can be recharged and used again. Some electrical products are described as rechargeable when they contain rechargeable batteries. （电池或电器）可充电的

  • ...a rechargeable battery.

    充电电池

  • ...a rechargeable drill.

    充电钻孔机

Rechargeable is also a noun.

  • It is more expensive to run a personal stereo on disposable batteries than on rechargeables.

    随身听使用一次性电池比用充电电池更贵。
