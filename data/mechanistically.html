mechanistic
mekənɪstɪk ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED （观点或解释）机械论的 If you describe a view or explanation of something as mechanistic, you are criticizing it because it describes a natural or social process as if it were a machine. （观点或解释）机械论的 [disapproval]

  • ...a mechanistic view of things that ignores the emotional realities in people's lives.

    置人们生活中各种情感现实于不顾的机械论观点

  • Most of my colleagues in biology are still very mechanistic in their thinking.

    我在生物学领域的大多数同事思想仍十分僵化。
