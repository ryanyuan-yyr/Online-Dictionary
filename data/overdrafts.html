overdraft
oʊvəʳdrɑːft -dræft ˈoʊvərˌdræft
Word forms: plural overdrafts
N-COUNT 透支；透支额 If you have an overdraft, you have spent more money than you have in your bank account, and so you are in debt to the bank. 透支；透支额

  • When I left Cambridge I had a £10,000 overdraft.

    我离开剑桥时，透支了1万英镑。

  • Her bank warned that unless she repaid the overdraft she could face legal action.

    银行警告她如果不偿还透支钱款，她将面临诉讼。
