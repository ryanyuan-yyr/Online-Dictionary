slurp
slɜːʳp slɜrp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense slurps, present participle slurping, past tense , past participle slurped
1 VERB 大声地啜;出声地喝 If you slurp a liquid, you drink it noisily. 大声地啜;出声地喝

  • He blew on his soup before slurping it off the spoon. [V n + from/off]

    他吹了吹勺子里的汤，然后啧啧地喝了下去。

  • He slurped down a cup of sweet, black coffee. [V adv n]

    他咕嘟咕嘟地喝下了一杯加糖的清咖啡。

2 N-COUNT (喝东西时发出的)咕嘟声 A slurp is a noise that you make with your mouth when you drink noisily, or a mouthful of liquid that you drink noisily. (喝东西时发出的)咕嘟声

  • He takes a slurp from a cup of black coffee.

    他咕嘟喝了一口清咖啡。
