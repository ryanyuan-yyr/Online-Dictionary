unconvinced
ʌnkənvɪnst ˌʌnkənˈvɪnst
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ that] 不信服的；不确定的 If you are unconvinced that something is true or right, you are not at all certain that it is true or right. 不信服的；不确定的

  • Most consumers seem unconvinced that the recession is over.

    大多数消费者似乎都不相信经济衰退已经过去。

  • Many academics remain unconvinced of the need for change.

    许多学者仍怀疑是否有变革的需要。

SYN sceptical, doubtful, disbelieving, doubting
