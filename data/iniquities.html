iniquity
ɪnɪkwɪti ɪˈnɪkwɪti
Word forms: plural iniquities
N-VAR 邪恶;极不公正 You can refer to wicked actions or very unfair situations as iniquity . 邪恶;极不公正 [formal]

  • He rails against the iniquities of capitalism. [+ of]

    他怒斥资本主义的种种罪恶。

  • A disco isn't exactly a den of iniquity.

    迪斯科舞厅并非藏污纳垢之处。

SYN wickedness, wrong, crime, evil
