ghostly
goʊstli ˈɡəʊstlɪ ˈgoʊstli
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 虚幻的；恐怖的；可怕的 Something that is ghostly seems unreal or unnatural and may be frightening because of this. 虚幻的；恐怖的；可怕的

  • The moon shone, shedding a ghostly light on the fields.

    月亮闪耀，田野上洒下幽幽的亮光。

  • ...Sonia's ghostly laughter.

    索尼娅瘆人的笑声

SYN unearthly, weird, phantom, eerie  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 鬼似的；幽灵般的；鬼魂萦绕的 A ghostly presence is the ghost or spirit of a dead person. 鬼似的；幽灵般的；鬼魂萦绕的

  • ...the ghostly presences which haunt these islands.

    在这些岛屿上出没的鬼魂

  • Charles is haunted by ghostly children.

    孩子们的幽灵缠上了查尔斯。
