unctuous
ʌŋktʃuəs ˈʌŋktʃuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED (人)虚情假意的，虚与委蛇的 If you describe someone as unctuous, you are critical of them because they seem to be full of praise, kindness, or interest, but are obviously insincere. (人)虚情假意的，虚与委蛇的 [formal, disapproval]

  • ...the kind of unctuous tone that I've heard often at diplomatic parties.

    我常在外交聚会上听到的那种虚与委蛇的腔调

SYN obsequious, smooth, slick, plausible  
2 ADJ-GRADED （食品、饮料）含乳脂的，油的，油腻的 If you describe food or drink as unctuous, you mean that it is creamy or oily. （食品、饮料）含乳脂的，油的，油腻的 [formal]

  • Goose fat gives the most unctuous flavour but you can use chicken fat.

    鹅油最肥美，不过你也可以用鸡油。

SYN oily, creamy, greasy, oleaginous
