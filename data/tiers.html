tier
tɪəʳ ˈtaɪə ˈtaɪər tɪr
Word forms: plural tiers
1 N-COUNT 一排;一行;一列 A tier is a row or layer of something that has other layers above or below it. 一排;一行;一列

  • ...the auditorium with the tiers of seats around and above it. [+ of]

    四周和上方有一排排座位的礼堂

SYN row, bank, layer, line  
Tier is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...a three-tier wedding cake.

    3层的结婚蛋糕

2 N-COUNT (组织或系统的)层，级 A tier is a level in an organization or system. (组织或系统的)层，级

  • Islanders have campaigned for the abolition of one of the three tiers of municipal power on the island. [+ of]

    岛上居民发起一场运动，要求废除岛上三级市政管理层中的一级。

Tier is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...the possibility of a two-tier system of universities.

    大学实行双重体制的可能性
