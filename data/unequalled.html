unequalled
ʌniːkwəld
in AM, use 美国英语用 unequaled
ADJ 无与伦比的；无敌的 If you describe something as unequalled, you mean that it is greater, better, or more extreme than anything else of the same kind. 无与伦比的；无敌的

  • This record figure was unequalled for 13 years.

    这项纪录保持了13年，无人能破。

  • ...an unequalled level of service.

    最上乘的服务

  • ...a feat unequaled in the history of polar exploration.

    极地探险史上无人能及的成就

SYN incomparable, supreme, unparalleled, paramount
