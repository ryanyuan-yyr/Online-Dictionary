grasping
grɑːspɪŋ græsp- ˈgræspɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 一味攫取的；贪婪的；吝啬的 If you describe someone as grasping, you are criticizing them for wanting to get and keep as much money as possible, and for being unwilling to spend it. 一味攫取的；贪婪的；吝啬的 [disapproval]

  • ...a greedy grasping drug-ridden individual.

    贪婪且嗜财如命的瘾君子

  • They were grasping and manipulative.

    他们贪得无厌而且喜欢摆布人。

SYN greedy, acquisitive, rapacious, mean
