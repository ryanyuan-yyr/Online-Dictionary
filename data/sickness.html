sickness
sɪknəs ˈsɪknɪs
Word forms: plural sicknesses
1 N-UNCOUNT 患病;生病;不健康 Sickness is the state of being ill or unhealthy. 患病;生病;不健康

  • In fifty-two years of working he had one week of sickness.

    在52 年的工作中他仅病过一周。

  • There appears to be another outbreak of sickness among seals in the North Sea.

    看来北海的海豹又要爆发另一场疾病了。

2 N-UNCOUNT 恶心;呕吐感 Sickness is the uncomfortable feeling that you are going to vomit. 恶心;呕吐感

  • He felt a great rush of sickness.

    他忽然觉得非常想吐。

  • After a while, the sickness gradually passed and she struggled to the mirror.

    过了一会儿，她渐渐不那么恶心了，挣扎着到了镜子前。

SYN nausea, queasiness, biliousness  
3See also:morning sickness  travel sickness 
4 N-VAR 病症;疾病 A sickness is a particular illness. 病症;疾病

  • ...radiation sickness.

    辐射病
