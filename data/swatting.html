swat
swɒt swɑt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense swats, present participle swatting, past tense , past participle swatted
VERB 拍，击，打(昆虫等) If you swat something such as an insect, you hit it with a quick, swinging movement, using your hand or a flat object. 拍，击，打(昆虫等)

  • Hundreds of flies buzz around us, and the workman keeps swatting them. [VERB noun]

    数百只苍蝇围着我们转，工作人员不停地拍打。

Swat is also a noun.

  • Mother took a swat at Jack's arm.

    母亲打了杰克胳膊一下。
