brush up
(also brush up on)
PHRASAL VERB 重温；复习 If you brush up something or brush up on it, you practise it or improve your knowledge of it. 重温；复习

  • I had hoped to brush up my Spanish. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我曾打算好好温习一下我的西班牙语。

  • Eleanor spent much of the summer brushing up on her driving. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    埃莉诺这个夏天大部分时间都在练习驾驶技术。
