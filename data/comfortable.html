comfortable
kʌmftəbəl ˈkʌmftəbəl ˈkʌmftərbəl ˈkʌmfərtəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED (家具、衣服等)让人舒服的，舒适的 If a piece of furniture or an item of clothing is comfortable, it makes you feel physically relaxed when you use it, for example because it is soft. (家具、衣服等)让人舒服的，舒适的

  • ...a comfortable fireside chair.

    舒适的炉边座椅

  • Trainers are so comfortable to wear.

    便鞋穿着特别舒服。

2 ADJ-GRADED (建筑物或房间)令人放松的，安逸的，舒适的 If a building or room is comfortable, it makes you feel physically relaxed when you spend time in it, for example because it is warm and has nice furniture. (建筑物或房间)令人放松的，安逸的，舒适的

  • A home should be comfortable and friendly.

    家应该让人觉得舒服、亲切。

  • ...somewhere warm and comfortable.

    某个温暖舒服的地方

SYN pleasant, homely, easy, relaxing  
comfortably

  • ...the comfortably furnished living room.

    布置得很舒适的客厅

3 ADJ-GRADED (人)愉快放松的，舒服的 If you are comfortable, you are physically relaxed because of the place or position you are sitting or lying in. (人)愉快放松的，舒服的

  • Lie down on your bed and make yourself comfortable.

    躺在床上，身体放松。

  • She tried to maneuver her body into a more comfortable position.

    她试着把身子挪了挪，换了一个较为舒服的姿势。

SYN at ease, happy, at home, contented  
comfortably

  • Are you sitting comfortably?.

    你坐得舒服吗？

  • He would be tucked comfortably into bed.

    会有人给他掖好被子，让他舒舒服服地睡觉。

4 ADJ-GRADED 宽裕的;小康的 If you say that someone is comfortable, you mean that they have enough money to be able to live without financial problems. 宽裕的;小康的

  • 'Is he rich?' — 'He's comfortable.'.

    “他很有钱吗？”“他生活挺宽裕的。”

  • She came from a stable, comfortable, middle-class family.

    她出身于一个收入稳定、生活宽裕的中产阶级家庭。

SYN well-off, prosperous, affluent, well-to-do  
comfortably

  • Cayton describes himself as comfortably well-off.

    凯顿形容自己衣食无忧。

5 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 轻松领先的;易获胜的 In a race, competition, or election, if you have a comfortable lead, you are likely to win it easily. If you gain a comfortable victory or majority, you win easily. 轻松领先的;易获胜的

  • By half distance we held a comfortable two-lap lead.

    到半程时，我们轻松领先两圈。

  • He appeared to be heading for a comfortable victory.

    看起来他会轻松获胜。

comfortably

  • ...the Los Angeles Raiders, who comfortably beat the Bears earlier in the season.

    本赛季早些时候轻松战胜熊队的洛杉矶突击者队

6 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 自信的;放心的;自在的 If you feel comfortable with a particular situation or person, you feel confident and relaxed with them. 自信的;放心的;自在的

  • Nervous politicians might well feel more comfortable with a step-by-step approach. [+ with]

    神经脆弱的政治家们似乎更容易接受一种循序渐进的做法。

  • He liked me and I felt comfortable with him. [+ with]

    他喜欢我，我跟他在一起觉得很自在。

  • I'll talk to them, but I won't feel comfortable about it. [+ about]

    我会跟他谈一谈，但是我觉得有些尴尬。

comfortably

  • They talked comfortably of their plans.

    他们畅谈各自的计划。

7 ADJ (病人或受伤者)情况稳定的，状况良好的 When a sick or injured person is said to be comfortable, they are in a stable physical condition. (病人或受伤者)情况稳定的，状况良好的

  • He was described as comfortable in hospital last night.

    据说昨晚他在医院状况良好。

8 ADJ-GRADED (生活、工作或所处的环境)轻松的，无忧无虑的 A comfortable life, job, or situation does not cause you any problems or worries. (生活、工作或所处的环境)轻松的，无忧无虑的

  • ...a comfortable teaching job at a university.

    在大学教书的轻松工作

  • Kohl's retirement looks far from comfortable.

    看来科尔的退休生活一点都不自在。
