struggle on
PHRASAL VERB 勉力维持;在困难中坚持 If you struggle on, you continue doing something rather than stopping, even though it is difficult. 勉力维持;在困难中坚持

  • Why should I struggle on to please my parents?. [VERB PREP.]

    为什么我要强装笑颜取悦我的父母？

  • The rest of the world struggles on with its perpetual problems, poverty and debt. [VERB PREP. + with]

    世界上其他国家仍在苦苦同永恒的贫困和债务问题作斗争。
