tenant
tenənt ˈtɛnənt
Word forms: plural tenants
1 N-COUNT 承租人;租户;房客 A tenant is someone who pays rent for the place they live in, or for land or buildings that they use. 承租人;租户;房客

  • Regulations placed clear obligations on the landlord for the benefit of the tenant.

    为维护房客的利益，条例明确规定了房东负有的各项义务。

  • Landowners frequently left the management of their estates to tenant farmers.

    地主们经常将土地的管理交给佃农。

SYN leaseholder, resident, renter, occupant
