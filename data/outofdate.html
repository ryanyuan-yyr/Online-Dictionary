out of date
(also out-of-date)
ADJ-GRADED 过时的；陈旧的；老式的；废弃的 Something that is out of date is old-fashioned and no longer useful. 过时的；陈旧的；老式的；废弃的

  • Think how rapidly medical knowledge has gone out of date in recent years.

    想一想近些年医学知识更新得有多快。

  • ...out-of-date nuclear power stations.

    废弃的核电站
