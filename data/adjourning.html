adjourn
ədʒɜːʳn əˈdʒɜrn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adjourns, present participle adjourning, past tense , past participle adjourned
V-ERG 休（会）；休（庭） If a meeting or trial is adjourned or if it adjourns, it is stopped for a short time. 休（会）；休（庭）

  • The proceedings have now been adjourned until next week. [be VERB-ed]

    会议休会至下周重开。

  • I am afraid the court may not adjourn until three or even later. [VERB]

    我担心法庭要到3点或更晚时才会休庭。

SYN postpone, delay, suspend, interrupt
