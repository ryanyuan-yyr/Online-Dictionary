face down
PHRASAL VERB (威风凛凛地)挫败，战胜 If you face someone down, you oppose them or defeat them by being confident and looking at them boldly. (威风凛凛地)挫败，战胜 [mainly US] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • He's confronted crowds before and faced them down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他曾面对众人，把他们镇住了。

SYN intimidate, defeat, confront, subdue
