scaremongering
skeəʳmʌŋgərɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 散布恐怖消息;制造恐慌;危言耸听 If one person or group accuses another person or group of scaremongering, they accuse them of deliberately spreading worrying stories to try and frighten people. 散布恐怖消息;制造恐慌;危言耸听

  • The Government yesterday accused Greenpeace of scaremongering.

    昨日政府指责“绿色和平”组织危言耸听。
