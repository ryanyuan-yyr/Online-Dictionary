clause
klɔːz klɔz
Word forms: plural clauses
1 N-COUNT (法律文书的)条款 A clause is a section of a legal document. (法律文书的)条款

  • He has a clause in his contract which entitles him to a percentage of the profits.

    他的合同中有一项条款，使他有权分得一定比例的利润。

  • ...a compromise document sprinkled with escape clauses.

    不时出现些免责条款的折中文件

  • ...a complaint alleging a breach of clause 4 of the code.

    声称违犯了法规第4条有关规定的投诉

SYN section, condition, article, item  
2 N-COUNT (语法)分句，从句，子句 In grammar, a clause is a group of words containing a verb. Sentences contain one or more clauses. There are finite clauses and non-finite clauses. (语法)分句，从句，子句
