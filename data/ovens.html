oven
ʌvən ˈʌvən
Word forms: plural ovens
N-COUNT 烤箱；烤炉 An oven is a device for cooking that is like a box with a door. You heat it and cook food inside it. 烤箱；烤炉

  • Put the onions and ginger in the oven and let them roast for thirty minutes.

    将洋葱和姜放进烤箱烘烤30分钟。

SYN stove, range, kiln, kitchen stove
