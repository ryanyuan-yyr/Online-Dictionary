waxwork
wækswɜːʳk ˈwæksˌwɜrk
Word forms: plural waxworks
1 N-COUNT （尤指名人的）蜡像 A waxwork is a model of a person, especially a famous person, made out of wax. （尤指名人的）蜡像
2 N-COUNT （名人）蜡像馆 A waxworks is a place where waxworks are displayed for the public to look at. Waxworks is both the singular and the plural form. （名人）蜡像馆
