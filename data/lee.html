lee
liː li
Word forms: plural lees
1 N-SING [with poss] 庇护所；背风处 The lee of a place is the shelter that it gives from the wind or bad weather. 庇护所；背风处 [literary]

  • ...the cathedral, which nestles in the lee of a hill beneath the town. [+ of]

    坐落于小镇下面一座小山背风处的大教堂

  • The sea started to ease as we came under Cuba's lee.

    我们靠近古巴的背风海岸时风浪开始平息。

SYN shelter, cover, screen, protection  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （船）背风（面）的，下风的 In sailing, the lee side of a ship is the one that is away from the wind. （船）背风（面）的，下风的 [technical]
3 N-PLURAL （酒瓶或酒桶底部的）渣滓，沉淀物 The lees are the substance that collects at the bottom of a bottle or barrel of wine. （酒瓶或酒桶底部的）渣滓，沉淀物

  • ...a glass-fronted barrel showing the wine resting on its lees.

    可以看到沉有渣滓的葡萄酒的玻璃面酒桶
