acquaint
əkweɪnt əˈkweɪnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense acquaints, present participle acquainting, past tense , past participle acquainted
1 VERB 使认识;介绍;使了解 If you acquaint someone with something, you tell them about it so that they know it. If you acquaint yourself with something, you learn about it. 使认识;介绍;使了解 [formal]

  • ...efforts to acquaint the public with their rights under the new law. [VERB noun + with]

    为使公众熟悉新法律所赋予的权利而作出的努力

  • I want to acquaint myself with your strengths and weaknesses. [VERB pronoun-reflexive + with]

    我想知道你有哪些长处以及不足。

SYN tell, announce, reveal, advise
