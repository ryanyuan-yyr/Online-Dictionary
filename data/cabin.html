cabin
kæbɪn ˈkæbɪn
Word forms: plural cabins
1 N-COUNT (船上的)小舱 A cabin is a small room in a ship or boat. (船上的)小舱

  • He showed her to a small cabin.

    他把她领到了一个小船舱。

SYN room, berth, quarters, compartment  
2 N-COUNT (飞机上的)机舱,客舱 A cabin is one of the areas inside a plane. (飞机上的)机舱,客舱

  • He sat quietly in the First Class cabin, looking tired.

    他一脸倦意,静静地坐在头等舱里。

3 N-COUNT (尤指森林、群山中的)小木屋 A cabin is a small wooden house, especially one in an area of forests or mountains. (尤指森林、群山中的)小木屋

  • ...a log cabin.

    原木小屋
