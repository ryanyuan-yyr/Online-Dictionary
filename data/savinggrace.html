saving grace
ˈseɪvɪŋ ɡreɪs
Word forms: plural saving graces
N-COUNT 可资弥补的特点;补偿因素;可取之处;仅有的优点 A saving grace is a good quality or feature in a person or thing that prevents them from being completely bad or worthless. 可资弥补的特点;补偿因素;可取之处;仅有的优点

  • Ageing's one saving grace is you worry less about what people think.

    上了年纪的一个好处是不再那般担心别人的看法。
