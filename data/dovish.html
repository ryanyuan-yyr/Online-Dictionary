dovish
dʌvɪʃ ˈdʌvɪʃ
(also doveish)
ADJ-GRADED (新闻中指政治家或政府)温和派的，鸽派的 Journalists use dovish to describe politicians or governments who are in favour of using peaceful and diplomatic methods to achieve something, rather than using force and violence. (新闻中指政治家或政府)温和派的，鸽派的

  • It must be said that the defence minister is a little bit more dovish than other people in the ruling elite.

    需要指出的是国防大臣比上层统治集团中其他成员都要温和。
