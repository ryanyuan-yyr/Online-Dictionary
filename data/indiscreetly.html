indiscreet
ɪndɪskriːt ˌɪndɪˈskriːt ˌɪndɪˈskrit
ADJ-GRADED 不慎重的;轻率的 If you describe someone as indiscreet, you mean that they do or say things in public which they should only do or say secretly or in private. 不慎重的;轻率的

  • He is notoriously indiscreet about his private life. [+ about]

    他在私生活方面的不检点是出了名的。

  • Had he been indiscreet in what he had said?

    他曾轻率地说过什么吗？

  • ...the duchess's indiscreet behaviour.

    公爵夫人的轻率行为

SYN tactless, foolish, rash, reckless
