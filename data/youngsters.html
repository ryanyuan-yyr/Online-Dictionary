youngster
jʌŋstəʳ ˈjʌŋstər
Word forms: plural youngsters
N-COUNT 年轻人;（尤指）少年，儿童 Young people, especially children, are sometimes referred to as youngsters . 年轻人;（尤指）少年，儿童

  • Other youngsters are not so lucky.

    其他的孩子则没有这么幸运。

  • I was only a youngster in 1935.

    1935年时我还只是个孩子。

SYN youth, girl, boy, kid [informal]
