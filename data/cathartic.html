cathartic
kəθɑːʳtɪk kəˈθɑrtɪk
ADJ-GRADED 起到发泄作用的;宣泄情绪的 Something that is cathartic has the effect of catharsis. 起到发泄作用的;宣泄情绪的 [formal]

  • His laughter was cathartic, an animal yelp that brought tears to his eyes.

    他哈哈大笑以宣泄情绪，声音如野兽般尖厉，眼泪都笑出来了。

  • ...a liberating and cathartic experience.

    释放情感、宣泄情绪的体验
