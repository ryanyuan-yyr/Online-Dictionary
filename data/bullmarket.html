bull market
bʊl ˈmɑːkɪt
Word forms: plural bull markets
N-COUNT （股市的）牛市，看涨行情，多头市场 A bull market is a situation on the stock market when people are buying a lot of shares because they expect that the shares will increase in value and that they will be able to make a profit by selling them again after a short time. （股市的）牛市，看涨行情，多头市场 [business]
