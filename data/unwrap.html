unwrap
ʌnræp ʌnˈræp ʌnˈræp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unwraps, present participle unwrapping, past tense , past participle unwrapped
1 VERB 打开…的包裹物;拆开…的包装 When you unwrap something, you take off the paper, plastic, or other covering that is around it. 打开…的包裹物;拆开…的包装

  • I untied the bow and unwrapped the small box. [VERB noun]

    我解开蝴蝶结，拆开小盒子的包装。

  • Vacuum-packed ham slices should be unwrapped 30 minutes before serving. [VERB noun]

    真空包装的火腿片应在食用前30分钟把包装除去。
