wow
waʊ wəʊ waʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense wows, present participle wowing, past tense , past participle wowed
1 EXCLAM (表示赞叹、非常吃惊或喜悦)哇，呀 You can say 'wow' when you are very impressed, surprised, or pleased. (表示赞叹、非常吃惊或喜悦)哇，呀 [informal, feelings]

  • I thought, 'Wow, what a good idea'.

    我想：“哇，多好的主意呀。”

2 VERB 使叫绝;博得…的称赞 You say that someone wows people when they give an impressive performance and fill people with enthusiasm and admiration. 使叫绝;博得…的称赞 [informal]

  • Ben Tankard wowed the crowd with his jazz. [VERB noun]

    本·坦卡德的爵士乐使众人为之叫绝。
