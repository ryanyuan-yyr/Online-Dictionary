overeat
oʊvəʳiːt ˌoʊvərˈit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overeats, present participle overeating, past tense overate, past participle overeaten
VERB 暴食；吃得过量；吃撑 If you say that someone overeats, you mean they eat more than they need to or more than is healthy. 暴食；吃得过量；吃撑

  • If you tend to overeat because of depression, first take steps to recognize the source of your sadness. [VERB]

    如果你常常因为沮丧而暴饮暴食，首先应该设法找到悲伤的原因。

  • ...people who overeat spicy foods. [VERB noun]

    辛辣食品吃得过多的人

SYN gorge, binge [informal] , drink or eat too much, guzzle  
overeater

  • She eats in secret like most compulsive overeaters.

    她像大多数强迫性暴食者那样偷偷地吃东西。

overeating

  • If you have a serious problem with overeating you should get together with others who share this problem.

    如果你暴饮暴食很严重，应当同有类似问题的人一起来探讨。
