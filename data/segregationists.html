segregationist
segrɪgeɪʃənɪst ˌsɛgrəˈgeɪʃənɪst
Word forms: plural segregationists
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 种族隔离主义者 A segregationist is someone who thinks people of different races should be kept apart. 种族隔离主义者

  • ...a segregationist on the far Right.

    极右派种族隔离主义者

  • Alabama may have been legally forced by the courts to kill off its segregationist policies.

    亚拉巴马州可能已被法院依法强令终止其种族隔离政策。
