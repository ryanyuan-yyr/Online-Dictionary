tuck up
PHRASAL VERB 给(孩子)盖好被子 If you tuck a child up in bed, you tuck them in. 给(孩子)盖好被子 [British]

  • She tucked them up in bed. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她安顿他们上床睡觉。

  • He mostly stayed at home tucking up the children. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他主要待在家里，帮孩子们盖好被子睡觉。

  • She had gone to work believing Helen was safely tucked up in bed. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    她相信海伦已经安然入睡后就去上班了。
