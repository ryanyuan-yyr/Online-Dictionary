consume
kənsjuːm -suːm kənˈsum kənˈsjum
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense consumes, present participle consuming, past tense , past participle consumed
1 VERB 吃;喝;饮用 If you consume something, you eat or drink it. 吃;喝;饮用 [formal]

  • Many people experienced a drop in their cholesterol levels when they consumed oat bran. [VERB noun]

    很多人食用燕麦麸后胆固醇水平下降了。

  • ...serving chocolate ice-creams for the children to consume in the kitchen. [VERB noun]

    端上巧克力冰激凌让孩子们在厨房里吃

2 VERB 消耗，耗费(燃料、能源或时间) To consume an amount of fuel, energy, or time means to use it up. 消耗，耗费(燃料、能源或时间)

  • Some of the most efficient refrigerators consume 70 percent less electricity than traditional models. [VERB noun]

    一些极为节能的冰箱比传统型号耗电减少70%。

  • ...plans which will consume hours of time and deplete your cash reserves. [VERB noun]

    要花费若干小时并消耗你留存备用的钱的计划

SYN use up, use, spend, waste  
-consuming

  • ...oil-consuming countries.

    石油消费国

  • It is very space-consuming.

    这东西很占地方。

3 VERB (火)烧毁，吞没，吞噬 If a fire consumes a building, it completely destroys it. (火)烧毁，吞没，吞噬 [formal]

  • ...the fire which consumed the dwelling. [VERB noun]

    吞噬了居所的火灾

4 VERB (使)沉溺，(使)沉迷，(使)充满(感情或思想) If a feeling or idea consumes you, it affects you very strongly indeed. (使)沉溺，(使)沉迷，(使)充满(感情或思想)

  • The memories consumed him. [VERB noun]

    他沉浸在回忆中。
