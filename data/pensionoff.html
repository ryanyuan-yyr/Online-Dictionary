pension off
PHRASAL VERB 发给…养老金使其退休 If someone is pensioned off, they are made to retire from work and are given a pension. 发给…养老金使其退休 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • Many successful women do not want to be pensioned off at 60. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    很多成功女性不想60岁就领养老金退休。

  • When his employees were no longer of use to him, he pensioned them off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    员工对他不再有利用价值时，他就发养老金迫使他们退休。
