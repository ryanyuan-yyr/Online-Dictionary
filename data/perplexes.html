perplex
pəʳpleks pərˈplɛks
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense perplexes, present participle perplexing, past tense , past participle perplexed
VERB 使困惑；使担心；迷惑 If something perplexes you, it confuses and worries you because you do not understand it or because it causes you difficulty. 使困惑；使担心；迷惑

  • It perplexed him because he was tackling it the wrong way. [VERB noun]

    由于处理不当，这个问题令他大伤脑筋。
