think out
PHRASAL VERB 仔细思考;全面考虑 If you think something out, you consider all the aspects and details of it before doing anything or making a decision. 仔细思考;全面考虑

  • I need time alone to think things out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我需要独自一个人好好想想。

  • The book is detailed and well thought out. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    这本书内容详细，考虑全面。

  • He chewed at the end of his pencil, thinking out the next problem. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他咬着铅笔头，仔细思考着下一道题。
