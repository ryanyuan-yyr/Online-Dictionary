psychosis
saɪkoʊsɪs saɪˈkoʊsɪs
Word forms: plural psychoses
N-VAR 精神病；精神错乱；精神失常 Psychosis is mental illness of a severe kind which can make people lose contact with reality. 精神病；精神错乱；精神失常 [medicine]

    他晚年可能患有某种神经症或精神病。

  • ...senile psychoses.

    老年性精神病
