competing
kəmpiːtɪŋ kəmˈpiːtɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 相互冲突的;相互矛盾的 Competing ideas, requirements, or interests cannot all be right or satisfied at the same time. 相互冲突的;相互矛盾的

  • They talked about the competing theories of the origin of life.

    他们讨论了几种相互矛盾的生命起源理论。

  • ...the competing interests of beach development and sea turtle protection.

    开发海滩与保护海龟之间相互冲突的利益

  • ...the competing demands of work and family.

    工作与家庭难以同时满足的需求

2See also:compete
