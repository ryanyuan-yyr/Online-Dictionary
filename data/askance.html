askance
əskæns əˈskæns əˈskænt əˈskæns
1 PHRASE 质疑;怀疑 If you look askance at someone or something, you have a doubtful or suspicious attitude towards them. 质疑;怀疑

  • They have always looked askance at the western notion of democracy.

    他们一直都怀疑西方的民主观。

2 PHRASE （带着怀疑的眼光）侧目，瞟视 If you look askance at someone, you look at them in a doubtful or suspicious way. （带着怀疑的眼光）侧目，瞟视

  • 'Do you play chess?' he asked, looking askance at Miguel.

    “你会下棋吗？”他斜睨着米格尔问道。
