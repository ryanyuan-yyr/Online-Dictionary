insubordinate
ɪnsəbɔːʳdɪnət ˌɪnsəˈbɔːdɪnɪt ˌɪnsəˈbɔrdənɪt
ADJ-GRADED 不服从的;不顺从的;反抗的 If you say that someone is insubordinate, you mean that they do not obey someone of higher rank. 不服从的;不顺从的;反抗的 [formal]

  • In industry, a worker who is grossly insubordinate is threatened with discharge.

    在企业内部,拒不服从指令的工人会面临遭解雇的威胁。

SYN disobedient, defiant, rebellious, disorderly
