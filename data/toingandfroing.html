to-ing and fro-ing ˈtuɪŋ ən ˈfroʊɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 来来回回;往复 If you say that there is a lot of to-ing and fro-ing, you mean that the same actions or movements or the same arguments are being repeated many times. 来来回回;往复 [mainly British]

  • After some toing and fro-ing, Elsie and the children moved back to London.

    来回搬了几次之后，埃尔茜和孩子们又搬回了伦敦。
