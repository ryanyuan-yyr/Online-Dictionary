collateral damage
N-UNCOUNT 附带损失，附带的破坏(指军事行动造成的平民伤亡或非军事设施的破坏) Collateral damage is accidental injury to non-military people or damage to non-military buildings which occurs during a military operation. 附带损失，附带的破坏(指军事行动造成的平民伤亡或非军事设施的破坏)

  • To minimize collateral damage maximum precision in bombing was required.

    为了尽量减低附带性破坏，轰炸须做到最大程度的精确。
