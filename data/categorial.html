category
kætɪgri -gɔːri ˈkætəˌgɔri
Word forms: plural categories
1 N-COUNT 种类;类别 If people or things are divided into categories, they are divided into groups in such a way that the members of each group are similar to each other in some way. 种类;类别

  • This book clearly falls into the category of fictionalised autobiography. [+ of]

    这本书很显然属于自传体小说。

  • The tables were organised into six different categories.

    餐桌被编为6个不同的类别。

  • Designer wedding dresses make wedding fashion a separate category from mainstream fashion.

    著名设计师设计的婚纱使得婚纱时尚有别于主流服装时尚。

SYN class, grouping, heading, head
