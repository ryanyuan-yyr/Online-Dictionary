teaching
tiːtʃɪŋ ˈtitʃɪŋ
Word forms: plural teachings
1 N-UNCOUNT 教学;教书 Teaching is the work that a teacher does in helping students to learn. 教学;教书

  • The Government funds university teaching.

    政府为高校教学提供资金。

  • ...the teaching of English in schools. [+ of]

    学校的英语教学

2 N-COUNT [usually plural, with poss] 教义;教导;学说 The teachings of a particular person, group of people, or religion are all the ideas and principles that they teach. 教义;教导;学说

  • ...the teachings of Jesus.

    耶稣的教义

  • ...their teachings on sexuality and marriage. [+ on]

    他们关于性和婚姻的教义
