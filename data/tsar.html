tsar
zɑːʳ tsɑr zɑr
(also czar)Word forms: plural tsars
1 N-COUNT; N-TITLE 沙皇 In former times, the tsar was the king of Russia. 沙皇
2 N-COUNT 特使 A particular kind of tsar is a person who has been appointed by the government to deal with a particular problem that is affecting the country. 特使

  • ...the former New York police chief who was appointed as 'drug tsar' by Bill Clinton.

    被比尔·克林顿任命为“反毒特使”的前纽约警察局局长

  • He promised to appoint an AIDS czar to deal with the disease.

    他许诺任命一位艾滋病大使来应对这种疾病。

SYN head, chief, boss, big cheese [informal]
