re-run riːrʌn riːrʌn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense re-runs, present participle re-running, past tense , past participle re-ran
The spelling rerun is also used. The form re-run is used in the present tense and is also the past participle of the verb. The noun is pronounced /'riː rʌn/. The verb is pronounced /ˌriː 'rʌn/. re-run亦拼作rerun。re-run的过去分词与原形相同。名词读作/'riː rʌn/。动词读作/ˌriː 'rʌn/。
1 N-SING 重演;再现 If you say that something is a re-run of a particular event or experience, you mean that what happens now is very similar to what happened in the past. 重演;再现

  • It was the world's second worst air disaster, a horrific re-run of the runway collision in 1977. [+ of]

    这是世界上第二大空难，是1977年飞机跑道相撞事件的可怕重演。

  • It seems likely that this year will see a re-run of last year's fight over the elementary-school budget.

    今年的小学预算问题很可能会像去年那样再次引发争论。

2 VERB 重复;重做;再运行 If someone re-runs a process or event, they do it or organize it again. 重复;重做;再运行

  • Edit the input text and re-run the software. [VERB noun]

    修改输入的文本，重新运行软件。

  • He re-ran in his mind his conversation with Smith. [VERB noun]

    他脑子里回顾了一遍他和史密斯的对话。

Re-run is also a noun.重复;重做;再运行

  • In the re-run he failed to make the final at all, finishing sixth.

    在重赛中，他根本没有进入决赛，排名第六。

3 VERB [usually passive] 重新(选举) If an election is re-run, it is organized again, for example because the correct procedures were not followed or because no candidate got a large enough majority. 重新(选举)

  • The ballot was re-run on Mr Todd's insistence after accusations of malpractice. [be VERB-ed]

    因被控舞弊,在托德先生的坚持下重新进行了投票。

Re-run is also a noun.

  • The opposition has demanded a re-run of parliamentary elections held yesterday.

    反对派要求重新举行昨天进行的议会选举。

4 VERB 重演;重播;重映 To re-run a film, play, or television programme means to show it or put it on again. 重演;重播;重映

  • They re-ran the World Cup final on a big screen. [VERB noun]

    他们在大屏幕上重播了世界杯决赛。

5 N-COUNT 重演;重播;重映 A re-run is a film, play, or television programme that is shown or put on again. 重演;重播;重映 [Also + of]

  • Viewers will have to make do with tired re-runs and second-rate movies.

    电视观众只能将就着看一下已经看厌了的重播节目和二流电影。

  • Thames television announced a re-run of the Benny Hill Show.

    泰晤士电视台宣布重播《班尼·希尔秀》。
