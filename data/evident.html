evident
evɪdənt ˈɛvədənt ˈɛvəˌdɛnt
1 ADJ-GRADED 明显的;显然的;清楚的 If something is evident, you notice it easily and clearly. 明显的;显然的;清楚的

  • His footprints were clearly evident in the heavy dust.

    厚厚的尘土中他的脚印清晰易见。

  • The threat of inflation is already evident in bond prices.

    通货膨胀的危险在证券价格上已经表现得很明显。

  • ...the best-publicised cases of evident injustice.

    最广为人知的明显不公的案例

2 ADJ-GRADED 显然的;明显的 You use evident to show that you are certain about a situation or fact and your interpretation of it. 显然的;明显的 [emphasis]

  • It was evident that she had once been a beauty.

    显然她曾是个美人儿。

  • The cities are bombarded day after day in an evident effort to force their surrender.

    那些城市遭到日复一日的炮轰，目的显然是要逼他们投降。

3 See also: self-evident
