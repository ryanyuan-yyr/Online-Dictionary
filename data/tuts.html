tut
tʌt tʌt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tuts, present participle tutting, past tense , past participle tutted
1 (书面语中表示反对、厌烦、同情等的)咂嘴声，啧啧声 Tut is used in writing to represent the sound that you make with your tongue touching the top of your mouth when you want to indicate disapproval, annoyance, or sympathy. (书面语中表示反对、厌烦、同情等的)咂嘴声，啧啧声
2 VERB (表示反对、厌烦、同情等时)发啧啧声，咂嘴 If you tut, you make a sound with your tongue touching the top of your mouth when you want to indicate disapproval, annoyance, or sympathy. (表示反对、厌烦、同情等时)发啧啧声，咂嘴

  • He tutted and shook his head. [VERB]
