type up
PHRASAL VERB 把(手写稿)打印成文 If you type up a text that has been written by hand, you produce a typed copy of it. 把(手写稿)打印成文

  • They didn't get around to typing up the letter. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们没能抽出时间把信打印出来。

  • When the first draft was completed, Nichols typed it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    当初稿完成以后，尼科尔斯将它打印了出来。
