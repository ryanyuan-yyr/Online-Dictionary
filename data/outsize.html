outsize
aʊtsaɪz ˈaʊtˌsaɪz
(also outsized)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 超大型的；极大的 Outsize or outsized things are much larger than usual or much larger than you would expect. 超大型的；极大的 [British]

  • ...an outsize pair of scissors.

    特大剪刀

  • An outsized photograph hung above her bedroom fireplace.

    一幅巨大的照片挂在她卧室的壁炉上方。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （衣服）超大号的，加大码的 Outsize clothes are clothes for very large people. （衣服）超大号的，加大码的 [British]

  • Often outsize clothes are made from cheap fabric and look like ugly tents.

    超大号服装常常质地较差，而且看上去好似丑陋的帐篷。

SYN extra-large, large, generous, ample
