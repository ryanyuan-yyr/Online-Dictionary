self-image ˈsɛlfˈɪmɪdʒ
Word forms: plural self-images
N-COUNT [usually singular] 自我形象;自我印象 Your self-image is the set of ideas you have about your own qualities and abilities. 自我形象;自我印象

  • Children who have a positive self-image are less likely to present behaviour and discipline problems.

    自我感觉良好的孩子较少出现行为和纪律上的问题。
