enticing
ɪntaɪsɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 迷人的;具有诱惑力的 Something that is enticing is extremely attractive and makes you want to get it or to become involved with it. 迷人的;具有诱惑力的

  • A prospective premium of about 30 per cent on their initial investment is enticing.

    初始投资30%左右的预期溢价非常具有诱惑力。

  • ...many enticing illustrations.

    很多迷人的插图

SYN attractive, appealing, inviting, charming  
enticingly

  • ...laying out their stall enticingly.

    把他们的货摊布置得很吸引人
