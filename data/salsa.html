salsa
sælsə sɑːlsə ˈsɑlsə
Word forms: plural salsas
1 N-MASS 沙司,辛香番茄酱(通常吃墨西哥或西班牙食物时用) Salsa is a hot, spicy sauce made from onions and tomatoes, usually eaten with Mexican or Spanish food. 沙司,辛香番茄酱(通常吃墨西哥或西班牙食物时用)
2 N-UNCOUNT 萨尔萨舞曲(在拉丁美洲尤为盛行) Salsa is a type of dance music especially popular in Latin America. 萨尔萨舞曲(在拉丁美洲尤为盛行)

  • A band played salsa, and spectators danced wildly.

    乐队演奏萨尔萨舞曲，观众们纵情跳舞。
