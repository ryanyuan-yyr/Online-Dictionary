unit
juːnɪt ˈjunɪt
Word forms: plural units
1 N-COUNT 单位；整体 If you consider something as a unit, you consider it as a single, complete thing. 单位；整体

  • Agriculture was based in the past on the family as a unit.

    过去的农业是以家庭为单位的。

SYN entity, whole, item, feature  
2 N-COUNT （工作）班组，部门 A unit is a group of people who work together at a specific job, often in a particular place. （工作）班组，部门

  • ...the health services research unit.

    医疗卫生服务研究部门

  • The results from this unit are staggering.

    这个团队得出的结果令人吃惊。

3 N-COUNT （武装部队或警察机关的）小队，分队 A unit is a group within an armed force or police force, whose members fight or work together or carry out a particular task. （武装部队或警察机关的）小队，分队

  • One secret military unit tried to contaminate the drinking water of the refugees.

    一个秘密军事分队企图污染难民的饮用水。

  • Two small Marine units have been trapped inside the city for the last 36 hours.

    两支海军小分队在过去的36个小时被困在城内。

4 N-COUNT （常为更大机器一部分的）装置，设备，部件 A unit is a small machine which has a particular function, often part of a larger machine. （常为更大机器一部分的）装置，设备，部件

  • The unit plugs into any TV set.

    这个部件能插进任何电视机。

SYN system, assembly  
5 N-COUNT （计量）单位 A unit of measurement is a fixed standard quantity, length, or weight that is used for measuring things. The litre, the centimetre, and the ounce are all units. （计量）单位
6 N-COUNT （课本）单元 A unit is one of the parts that a textbook is divided into. （课本）单元
