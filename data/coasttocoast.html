coast-to-coast
1 ADJ 海岸到海岸的A coast-to-coast journey or route is one that goes from one coast of a country or region to the opposite coast. 海岸到海岸的

  • The Massachusetts senator is finishing his coast-to-coast tour across the United States.

    这位马萨诸塞州的参议员正在结束他横穿美国东西海岸的行程。

2 ADV 海岸到海岸地Coast-to-coast is also an adverb. 海岸到海岸地

  • I drove coast-to-coast in just over two hours.

    我在两岸间开车只用了两个小时多一点儿。
