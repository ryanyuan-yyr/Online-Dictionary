undismayed
ʌndɪsmeɪd ˌʌndɪsˈmeɪd
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 不害怕的；不气馁的；不沮丧的 If you say that someone is undismayed by something unpleasant or unexpected, you mean that they do not feel any fear, worry, or sadness about it. 不害怕的；不气馁的；不沮丧的 [formal]

  • He was undismayed by the prospect of failure. [+ by]

    想到可能会失败，他并不气馁。
