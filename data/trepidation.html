trepidation
trepɪdeɪʃən ˌtrɛpəˈdeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [oft with NOUN] 惊恐;惶恐;焦虑;不安 Trepidation is fear or anxiety about something that you are going to do or experience. 惊恐;惶恐;焦虑;不安 [formal]

  • It was with some trepidation that I viewed the prospect of cycling across Uganda.

    我带着些许惶恐考虑了一下骑自行车周游乌干达的可能性。

SYN anxiety, fear, worry, alarm
