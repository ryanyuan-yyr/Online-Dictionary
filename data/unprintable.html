unprintable
ʌnprɪntəbəl ʌnˈprɪntəbəl
ADJ （因粗鲁或惊世骇俗）难以启齿的 If you describe something that someone has said or done as unprintable, you mean that it is so rude or shocking that you do not want to say exactly what it was. （因粗鲁或惊世骇俗）难以启齿的

  • Her reply was unprintable.

    她的回答实在难以启齿。

  • ...some quite unprintable stories.

    一些耻于说出口的故事
