oftentimes
ɒfəntaɪmz ˈɒfəntaɪmz ɔːf- ˈɔfənˌtaɪmz ˈɔftənˌtaɪmz
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 常常；经常 If something oftentimes happens, it happens many times or much of the time. 常常；经常 [US]

  • Oftentimes, these fossils are not made available to science at all.

    科学界常常压根接触不到这些化石。

  • Oftentimes, I wouldn't even return the calls.

    我甚至经常不回电话。

  • It was oftentimes difficult to discuss certain issues while he was in the room.

    他在场的时候，有些问题常常没法讨论。
