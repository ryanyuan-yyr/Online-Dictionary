psychiatric
saɪkiætrɪk ˌsaɪkiˈætrɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 精神病学的 Psychiatric means relating to psychiatry. 精神病学的

  • We finally insisted that he seek psychiatric help.

    我们最后坚持要求他寻求精神科医师的帮助。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 精神疾病的 Psychiatric means involving mental illness. 精神疾病的

  • About 4% of the prison population have chronic psychiatric illnesses.

    大约4%的犯人有慢性精神疾病。
