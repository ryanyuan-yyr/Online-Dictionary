damnation
dæmneɪʃən dæmˈneɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT (某些宗教中人死后的)罚入地狱 According to some religions, if someone suffers damnation, they have to stay in hell for ever after they have died because of their sins. (某些宗教中人死后的)罚入地狱

  • ...a fear of eternal damnation.

    对死后永堕地狱的恐惧

SYN condemnation, damning, sending to hell, consigning to perdition  
2 EXCLAM (表示愤怒或不耐烦) 该死，见鬼去吧 Some people say 'damnation!' to express anger or impatience. (表示愤怒或不耐烦) 该死，见鬼去吧 [informal, rude, feelings]
Quotations
For what shall it profit a man, if he shall gain the whole world, and lose his own soul? Bible: St. Mark
