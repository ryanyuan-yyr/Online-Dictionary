afternoon
ɑːftəʳnuːn æf- ˌɑːftəˈnuːn ˌæftərˈnun ˈæftərˌnun
Word forms: plural afternoons
N-VAR 下午；午后 The afternoon is the part of each day which begins at lunchtime and ends at about six o'clock. 下午；午后

  • He's arriving in the afternoon.

    他下午会到。

  • He had stayed in his room all afternoon.

    他整个下午都呆在房间里。

  • ...an afternoon news conference.

    午后的新闻发布会
