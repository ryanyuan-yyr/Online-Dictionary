plank
plæŋk plæŋk
Word forms: plural planks
1 N-COUNT 厚（木）板；板条 A plank is a long, flat, rectangular piece of wood. 厚（木）板；板条

  • It was very strong, made of three solid planks of wood. [+ of]

    它由3块坚固的木板做成，很结实。

SYN board, beam, timber, stave  
2 N-COUNT （团体或政党的）政纲条目；（政策或纲领的）要点 The main plank of a particular group or political party is the main principle on which it bases its policy, or its main aim. （团体或政党的）政纲条目；（政策或纲领的）要点 [journalism]

  • Encouraging people to shop locally is a central plank of his environment policy. [+ of]

    他的环境政策的核心是鼓励人们购买当地产品。
