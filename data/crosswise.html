ADV [ADVERB after verb] 对角横穿地;斜穿地 Crosswise means diagonally across something. 对角横穿地;斜穿地

  • Rinse and slice the courgettes crosswise.

    将西葫芦洗干净，斜着切成片。

SYN across, sideways, diagonally, from side to side
