violin
vaɪəlɪn ˌvaɪəˈlɪn
Word forms: plural violins
N-VAR 小提琴 A violin is a musical instrument. Violins are made of wood and have four strings. You play the violin by holding it under your chin and moving a bow across the strings. 小提琴

  • Lizzie used to play the violin.

    莉齐曾经拉过小提琴。

  • ...the Brahms violin concerto in D.

    勃拉姆斯D调小提琴协奏曲
