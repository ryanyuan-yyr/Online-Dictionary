deformity
dɪfɔːʳmɪti diˈfɔrməti dɪˈfɔrməti
Word forms: plural deformities
1 N-COUNT (身体的)畸形部位（或器官） A deformity is a part of someone's body which is not the normal shape because of injury or illness, or because they were born this way. (身体的)畸形部位（或器官）

  • ...facial deformities in babies.

    婴儿的面部畸形处

SYN abnormality, defect, malformation, disfigurement  
2 N-UNCOUNT 畸形;变形 Deformity is the condition of having a deformity. 畸形;变形

  • The bones begin to grind against each other, leading to pain and deformity.

    这些骨头开始互相挤压，造成疼痛和畸形。

SYN distortion, irregularity, misshapenness, misproportion
