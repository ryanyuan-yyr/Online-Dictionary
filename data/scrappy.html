scrappy
skræpi ˈskræpi
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 计划不周的;不连贯的;散乱的 If you describe something as scrappy, you disapprove of it because it seems to be badly planned or untidy. 计划不周的;不连贯的;散乱的 [disapproval]

  • ...a scrappy affair.

    计划不周的事件

  • The final chapter is no more than a scrappy addition.

    最后一章不过是一些支离破碎的补充内容。

SYN incomplete, sketchy, piecemeal, disjointed
