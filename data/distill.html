distil
dɪstɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense distils, present participle distilling, past tense , past participle distilled
in AM, use 美国英语用 distill
1 VERB 蒸馏;用蒸馏法提取 If a liquid such as whisky or water is distilled, it is heated until it changes into steam or vapour and then cooled until it becomes liquid again. This is usually done in order to make it pure. 蒸馏;用蒸馏法提取

  • The whisky had been distilled in 1926 and sat quietly maturing until 1987. [be VERB-ed]

    这种威士忌是在1926年通过蒸馏提取的，尔后静置到 1987年才酿熟。

  • You can't actually drink the water from the marshland. But you can distil it. [VERB noun]

    沼泽地的水不能饮用，但是可以将它蒸馏净化。

  • When water is used this must be distilled water or spring water; never tap water. [VERB-ed]

    用水的时候必须是蒸馏水或泉水，绝不能用自来水。

SYN purify, process, filter, refine  
distillation

  • Any faults in the original cider stood out sharply after distillation.

    原先苹果酒里的任何杂质在蒸馏后都会凸现出来。

2 VERB 提取(植物的)精华;提炼 If an oil or liquid is distilled from a plant, it is produced by a process which extracts the most essential part of the plant. To distil a plant means to produce an oil or liquid from it by this process. 提取(植物的)精华;提炼 [Also VERB n from n]

  • The oil is distilled from the berries of this small tree. [be VERB-ed + from]

    油是从这种小树的浆果中提炼出来的。

  • ...the art of distilling rose petals. [VERB noun]

    提取玫瑰花瓣精油的技巧

  • A skin lotion distilled from the root is a marvellous cure for sore rashes and spots. [VERB-ed]

    从这种根部提取的护肤液是治疗皮疹和丘疹的极好药物。

SYN extract, express, squeeze, obtain  
distillation

  • ...the distillation of rose petals to produce rosewater.

    提取玫瑰花瓣精油以制作玫瑰水

3 VERB 精炼，浓缩（想法等）;吸收…为一部分 If a thought or idea is distilled from previous thoughts, ideas, or experiences, it comes from them. If it is distilled into something, it becomes part of that thing. 精炼，浓缩（想法等）;吸收…为一部分

  • Reviews are distilled from articles previously published in the main column. [be VERB-ed + from]

    述评是从先前刊登在主要专栏的文章中提炼出来的。

  • Eventually passion was distilled into the natural beauty of a balmy night. [be V-ed into n]

    最后激情融入了美丽宜人的夜色中。

  • Roy distills these messages into something powerful. [VERB noun + into]

    罗伊将这些信息提炼成一种非常有感染力的东西。

  • The advice is based on the distilled wisdom of a panel of heart specialists. [VERB-ed]

    该建议是在汇集提取了心脏专家小组的意见后作出的。

distillation

  • The material below is a distillation of his work.

    下面的材料是他工作的摘要。
