subway
sʌbweɪ ˈsʌbˌweɪ
Word forms: plural subways
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n, oft by NOUN] 地铁 A subway is an underground railway. 地铁 [mainly US]

  • ...the Bay Area Rapid Transit subway system.

    旧金山海湾地区地铁捷运系统

  • I don't ride the subway late at night.

    我深夜不坐地铁。

SYN underground, tube [British , informal] , metro, underground railway  
in BRIT, usually 英国英语通常用 underground, tube
2 N-COUNT (穿越马路、铁路的)地下通道，地下人行道 A subway is a passage underneath a busy road or a railway track for people to walk through. (穿越马路、铁路的)地下通道，地下人行道 [British]

  • The majority of us feel worried if we walk through a subway.

    大多数人在走地下通道时都感到害怕。

SYN underpass, tunnel, underground passage, pedestrian tunnel  
in AM, use 美国英语用 underpass
