1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] （以非常逼真的手法按事物正常比例描绘的）错视画法 Trompe l'oeil is a technique used in art in which objects are painted their normal size in a very realistic way, to make people think that the objects are solid and real. （以非常逼真的手法按事物正常比例描绘的）错视画法

  • ...a trompe l'oeil painting.
