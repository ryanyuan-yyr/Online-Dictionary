sole trader
səʊl ˈtreɪdə
Word forms: plural sole traders
N-COUNT 个体户 A sole trader is a person who owns their own business and does not have a partner or any shareholders. 个体户 [business]

  • Finance for a sole trader usually comes from the individual's own savings or from family and friends.

    个体户的资金通常来自个人积蓄或者来自亲朋好友。
