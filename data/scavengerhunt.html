scavenger hunt
Word forms: scavenger hunts
N-COUNT 寻物游戏A scavenger hunt is a game, usually played outdoors, in which the players must collect various objects from a list of things they have been given. 寻物游戏

  • On scavenger hunts I would always ask Mrs. Martin for empty coffee cans.

    寻物游戏中，我总会找马丁夫人要空咖啡罐。
