woody
wʊdi ˈwʊdi
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (植物)木本的，木质的 Woody plants have very hard stems. (植物)木本的，木质的

  • Care must be taken when trimming around woody plants like shrubs and trees.

    修剪灌木和乔木这样的木本植物时必须小心。

  • Trim any tough or woody stalks from the asparagus.

    剪掉芦笋上粗硬或老掉的梗子。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 长满树木的;林木覆盖的 A woody area has a lot of trees in it. 长满树木的;林木覆盖的

  • ...the wet and woody Vosges mountains.

    湿润葱郁的孚日山脉

3 ADJ 木头味的；木香的 Something that smells woody smells like wood. 木头味的；木香的

  • This oil has a lovely woody fragrance.

    这种精油散发着怡人的木香。
