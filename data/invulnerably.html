invulnerable
ɪnvʌlnərəbəl ɪnˈvʌlnərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不会受伤害的;刀枪不入的;攻不破的 If someone or something is invulnerable, they cannot be harmed or damaged. 不会受伤害的;刀枪不入的;攻不破的 [Also + to]

  • Many daughters assume that their mothers are invulnerable.

    很多女儿都认为她们的母亲无比坚强。

  • ...a system that would make the U.S. invulnerable to nuclear attack.

    会使美国免受核袭击的系统

SYN safe, secure, invincible, impenetrable  
invulnerability

  • They have a sense of invulnerability to disease.

    他们似乎百病不侵。
