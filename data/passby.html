pass by
从…旁边走过；经过 If you pass by something, you go past it or near it on your way to another place. 从…旁边走过；经过

  • I see them pass by my house every day. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我每天看见他们从我家旁边经过。

  • They were injured when a parked car exploded as their convoy passed by. [VERB PREP.]

    他们的车队经过时，一辆停放着的汽车爆炸了，他们受了伤。
