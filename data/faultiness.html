faulty
fɔːlti ˈfɔlti
1 ADJ-GRADED (设备等)有问题的，有毛病的,出故障的 A faulty piece of equipment has something wrong with it and is not working properly. (设备等)有问题的，有毛病的,出故障的

  • The money will be used to repair faulty equipment.

    这笔钱将用来修理出故障的设备。

2 ADJ (论证或推理)错误的，谬误的,不完善的 If you describe someone's argument or reasoning as faulty, you mean that it is wrong or contains mistakes, usually because they have not been thinking in a logical way. (论证或推理)错误的，谬误的,不完善的

  • Their interpretation was faulty — they had misinterpreted things.

    他们的理解是错误的—他们将事情曲解了。
