obduracy
ɒbdjʊrəsi -dʊr-
N-UNCOUNT 顽固不化；倔强；执拗 If you accuse someone of obduracy, you think their refusal to change their decision or opinion is unreasonable. 顽固不化；倔强；执拗 [formal, disapproval]

  • MPs have accused the government of obduracy and called on ministers to reverse their decision.

    议员指责政府顽固不化，呼吁部长们改变决定。
