en masse
ɒn mæs ɑ̃ mas ɛn ˈmæs ɑnˈmæs ɑ̃ ˈmas
ADV [ADVERB after verb, n ADVERB] 全体;一起 If a group of people do something en masse, they do it all together and at the same time. 全体;一起

  • The people marched en masse.

    人们齐步前进。

  • ...the arrival en masse of the Latin American delegates.

    拉美国家代表的同时到达

SYN all together, together, as one, as a whole
