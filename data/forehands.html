forehand
fɔːʳhænd ˈfɔrˌhænd
Word forms: plural forehands
N-COUNT (网球，壁球运动的)正手击球，正拍 A forehand is a shot in tennis or squash in which the palm of your hand faces the direction in which you are hitting the ball. (网球，壁球运动的)正手击球，正拍

  • Agassi saw his chance and, with another lightning forehand, reached match point.

    阿加西看到了机会，再次以一个闪电式的正手击球拿到了赛点。
