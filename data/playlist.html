playlist
pleɪlɪst ˈpleɪˌlɪst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense playlists, present participle playlisting, past tense , past participle playlisted
1 N-COUNT （电台的）音乐播放表 A playlist is a list of songs, albums, and artists that a radio station broadcasts. （电台的）音乐播放表

  • Radio 1's playlist is dominated by top-selling youth-orientated groups.

    广播一台主要播放一些面向年轻人的乐团所演唱的畅销曲目。

2 VERB 把…排入播放表 If a song, album, or artist is playlisted, it is put on a radio station's playlist. 把…排入播放表

  • We've playlisted many artists like Beth Orton who got picked up down the line by others. [VERB noun]

    我们把很多像贝丝·奥顿这样的艺术家纳入了播放表，他们之后也逐渐为其他人所认识。
