flabby
flæbi ˈflæbi
Word forms: comparative flabbier, superlative flabbiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (人)肌肉松弛的，肥胖的 Flabby people are rather fat, with loose flesh over their bodies. (人)肌肉松弛的，肥胖的 [disapproval]

  • This exercise is brilliant for getting rid of flabby tums.

    这个练习对减除小肚子上的赘肉极为有效。

  • My bulging thighs and flabby stomach made me depressed.

    我大腿太粗，肚子上也净是赘肉，让我很发愁。

SYN limp, hanging, loose, slack  
2 ADJ-GRADED 冗余的;杂乱的 If you describe something as flabby, you are criticizing it for being disorganized or wasteful. 冗余的;杂乱的 [disapproval]

  • You hear talk about American business being flabby.

    你会听到有关美国经济发展无序、后劲乏力的议论。
