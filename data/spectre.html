spectre
spektəʳ
Word forms: plural spectres
in AM, use 美国英语用 specter
1 N-COUNT 萦绕心头的恐惧 If you refer to the spectre of something unpleasant, you are referring to something that you are frightened might occur. 萦绕心头的恐惧

  • Failure to arrive at a consensus over the issue raised the spectre of legal action.

    无法就此事达成共识引起了人们对可能会诉诸法律手段的担忧。

  • Like many others, Handford was relieved to see the spectre of 15% interest rates evaporate by the end of the day.

    和其他很多人一样，当看到15%的利率的阴霾随着那天的结束而散尽时，汉德福感到一阵欣慰。

2 N-COUNT 鬼魂;幽灵;亡灵 A spectre is a ghost . 鬼魂;幽灵;亡灵 [literary]
