colourful
kʌləʳfʊl ˈkʌləfʊl
in AM, use 美国英语用 colorful
1 ADJ-GRADED 色彩鲜艳的;富于色彩的 Something that is colourful has bright colours or a lot of different colours. 色彩鲜艳的;富于色彩的

  • The flowers were colourful and the scenery magnificent.

    花儿五颜六色，景色蔚为壮观。

  • People wore colorful clothes and seemed to be having a good time.

    人们穿着五颜六色的衣服，似乎玩得很开心。

colourfully

  • ...the sight of dozens of colourfully dressed people.

    看到很多衣着鲜艳的人

2 ADJ-GRADED 生动有趣的;丰富多彩的 A colourful story is full of exciting details. 生动有趣的;丰富多彩的

  • The story she told was certainly colourful, and extended over her life in England, Germany and Spain.

    她讲的故事确实很有趣，涉及了她在英格兰、德国和西班牙的生活。

  • ...the country's colourful and often violent history.

    这个国家丰富多彩而又常常充满暴力的历史

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人)有趣的 A colourful character is a person who behaves in an interesting and amusing way. (人)有趣的

  • Casey Stengel was probably the most colorful character in baseball.

    凯西·施滕格尔可能是棒球界最有趣的人物。

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 多姿多彩的;富于刺激性的 If someone has had a colourful past or a colourful career, they have been involved in exciting but often slightly shocking things. 多姿多彩的;富于刺激性的

  • More details surfaced of her colourful past as the story developed.

    随着故事的发展，更多有关她不同寻常的过去的细节开始为人所知。

  • ...a well-known City business man with a rather colourful background.

    经历相当丰富的伦敦知名企业家

5 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (言语)粗鲁的，满是脏话的 Colourful language is rude or offensive language. (言语)粗鲁的，满是脏话的 [politeness]

  • Bryant is alleged to have used colourful language.

    据称布赖恩特言语粗鲁。
