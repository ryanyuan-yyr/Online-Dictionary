allegedly
əˈlɛdʒɪdlɪ
ADJ 声称的；被断言的；涉嫌的 An alleged fact has been stated but has not been proved to be true. 声称的；被断言的；涉嫌的

  • They have begun a hunger strike in protest at the alleged beating.

    他们开始绝食以抗议有人所宣称的殴打行为。

  • ...a list of alleged war criminals.

    战争嫌犯名单
