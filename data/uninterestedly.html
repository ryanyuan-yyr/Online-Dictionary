uninterested
ʌnɪntrəstɪd ʌnˈɪntrəstɪd ʌnˈɪntərɪstɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不感兴趣的；不关心的 If you are uninterested in something or someone, you do not want to know any more about them, because you think they have no special or exciting qualities. 不感兴趣的；不关心的

  • I was so uninterested in the result that I didn't even bother to look at it. [+ in]

    我对结果完全没兴趣，甚至都懒得看一眼。

  • ...unhelpful and uninterested shop staff.

    既没用又冷漠的店员

SYN indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic, bored
