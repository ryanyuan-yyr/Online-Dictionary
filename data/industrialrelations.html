industrial relations
N-PLURAL 劳资关系 Industrial relations refers to the relationship between employers and employees in industry, and the political decisions and laws that affect it. 劳资关系 [business]

  • The offer is seen as an attempt to improve industrial relations.

    该提议被视为一种改善劳资关系的尝试。

  • The new industrial relations legislation curbed the power of the unions.

    新的劳资关系法规限制了工会的权力。
