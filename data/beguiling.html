beguiling
bɪgaɪlɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 迷人的；吸引人的 Something that is beguiling is charming and attractive. 迷人的；吸引人的 [written]

  • Mombasa is a town with a beguiling Arabic flavour.

    蒙巴萨是一座充满迷人的阿拉伯风情的城市。

SYN charming, interesting, pleasing, attractive  
beguilingly

  • He was beguilingly boyish and attractive.

    他散发着迷人的孩子气，非常有魅力。
