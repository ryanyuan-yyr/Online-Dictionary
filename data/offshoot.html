offshoot
ɒfʃuːt ɔːf- ˈɔfˌʃut
Word forms: plural offshoots
N-COUNT [usually with poss] 分支；衍生物 If one thing is an offshoot of another, it has developed from that other thing. 分支；衍生物

  • Psychology began as a purely academic offshoot of natural philosophy.

    心理学最初是自然哲学一个纯粹的学术分支。

  • Firstdirect, Midland Bank's telephone banking offshoot, has cut its Visa card rate to APR 22.2%.

    米特兰银行的分支机构，“第一自助”电话银行，将维萨卡的年利率降至22.2%。

SYN by-product, development, product, branch
