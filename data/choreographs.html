choreograph
kɒriəgrɑːf kɔːriəgræf ˈkɔriəˌgræf
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense choreographs, present participle choreographing, past tense , past participle choreographed
VERB 为(芭蕾舞等)设计舞蹈动作;为…编舞 When someone choreographs a ballet or other dance, they invent the steps and movements and tell the dancers how to perform them. 为(芭蕾舞等)设计舞蹈动作;为…编舞

  • Achim had choreographed the dance in Act II himself. [VERB noun]

    阿希姆自己设计了第2幕的舞蹈动作。

  • She has danced, choreographed, lectured and taught all over the world. [VERB]

    她在世界各地跳过舞、编过舞、讲过学、也教过书。
