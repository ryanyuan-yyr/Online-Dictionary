pomposity
pɒmpɒsɪti pɑmˈpɑsəti
Word forms: plural pomposities
N-UNCOUNT 自负；自命不凡 Pomposity means speaking or behaving in a very serious manner which shows that you think you are more important than you really are. 自负；自命不凡 [disapproval]

  • He hated pomposity and disliked being called a genius.

    他憎恶自负的作派，而且不喜欢被称为天才。
