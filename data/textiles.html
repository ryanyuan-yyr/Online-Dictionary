textile
tekstaɪl ˈtɛksˌtaɪl ˈtɛkstəl
Word forms: plural textiles
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 织物;纺织品 Textiles are types of cloth or fabric, especially ones that have been woven. 织物;纺织品

  • ...decorative textiles for the home.

    家用装饰织品

  • ...the Scottish textile industry.

    苏格兰纺织业

2 N-PLURAL [no det] 纺织业 Textiles are the industries concerned with the manufacture of cloth. 纺织业

  • Another 75,000 jobs will be lost in textiles and clothing.

    纺织和服装业又要减少 75,000 个工作岗位。
