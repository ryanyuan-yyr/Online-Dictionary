continuation
kəntɪnjueɪʃən kənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural continuations
1 N-VAR [usually with poss] 持续;继续;延续 The continuation of something is the fact that it continues, rather than stopping. 持续;继续;延续

  • It's the coalition forces who are to blame for the continuation of the war. [+ of]

    联合部队应当对战争的延续负责。

  • What we'll see in the future is, in fact, a continuation of that trend.

    事实上，将来我们看到的是那种趋势的延续。

SYN continuing, lasting, carrying on, maintenance  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 续;延续部分 Something that is a continuation of something else is closely connected with it or forms part of it. 续;延续部分

  • ...since this chapter is a continuation of Chapter 8.

    既然这一章是第8章的延续

  • It would just be a continuation of previous visits he has made to Israel. [+ of]

    这不过是他之前对以色列访问的延续。

SYN addition, extension, supplement, sequel
