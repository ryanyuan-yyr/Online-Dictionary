gooey
guːi ˈgui
Word forms: comparative gooier, superlative gooiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 软而黏的；黏糊糊的 If you describe a food or other substance as gooey, you mean that it is very soft and sticky. 软而黏的；黏糊糊的 [informal]

  • These cakes are fudgy, gooey, and delicious.

    这些蛋糕绵软可口。

  • ...a lovely, gooey, sticky mess.

    松软香糯的一团

SYN sticky, soft, tacky, viscous  
2 ADJ-GRADED 过于煽情的；黏腻缠绵的 Gooey is sometimes used to describe very foolish, exaggerated ways of expressing love or affection. 过于煽情的；黏腻缠绵的 [informal]

  • I'm gonna write you a long, gooey letter.

    我要给你写一封情意绵绵的长信。

  • Women went gooey over him.

    女人们对他缠绵多情。
