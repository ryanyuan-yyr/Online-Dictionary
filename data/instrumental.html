instrumental
ɪnstrəmentəl ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəl
Word forms: plural instrumentals
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 有助益的;有帮助的;起作用的 Someone or something that is instrumental in a process or event helps to make it happen. 有助益的;有帮助的;起作用的

  • In his first years as chairman he was instrumental in raising the company's wider profile. [+ in]

    他任董事长的最初几年为提升公司的知名度起到了积极的推动作用。

  • Jesse Jackson was instrumental in the release of some of the hostages.

    其中一些人质得以获释多亏了杰西·杰克逊。

SYN active, involved, influential, useful  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 用乐器演奏(而非声乐)的;为器乐谱写的 Instrumental music is performed by instruments and not by voices. 用乐器演奏(而非声乐)的;为器乐谱写的

  • ...a cassette recording of vocal and instrumental music.

    有声乐也有器乐的磁带录音

Instrumentals are pieces of instrumental music.器乐曲

  • The last track on the CD is an instrumental.

    CD 上的最后一支曲目是一首器乐曲。
