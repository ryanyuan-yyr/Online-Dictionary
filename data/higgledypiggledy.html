higgledy-piggledy
hɪgəldi pɪgəldi ˈhɪɡəldɪˈpɪɡəldɪ ˈhɪgəlˌdiˈ pɪgəlˌdi
ADJ-GRADED 乱七八糟的;杂乱无章的 If you say that things are higgledy-piggledy, you mean that they are very disorganized and untidy. 乱七八糟的;杂乱无章的 [informal]

  • Books are often stacked in higgledy-piggledy piles on the floor.

    书本经常乱七八糟地堆在地板上。

SYN haphazard, muddled, jumbled, indiscriminate  
Higgledy-piggledy is also an adverb.

  • A whole valley of boulders tossed higgledy-piggledy as though by some giant.

    整个山谷里巨石翻滚，就像有巨人在狂扔乱掷。
