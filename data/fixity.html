fixity
fɪksɪti ˈfɪksɪti
N-UNCOUNT 固定性;稳定性;恒定性 If you talk about the fixity of something, you talk about the fact that it does not change or weaken. 固定性;稳定性;恒定性 [written]

  • I have been working on exploding the myth of fixity of meaning.

    我一直在致力于推翻有关意义恒定性的谬谈。

  • She believed in the fixity of the class system.

    她相信阶级制度恒久不变。

SYN steadiness, stability, persistence, perseverance
