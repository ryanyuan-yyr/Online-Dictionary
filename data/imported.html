import
ɪmpɔːʳt ɪmpɔːʳt ɪmˈpɔrt ˈɪmˌpɔrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense imports, present participle importing, past tense , past participle imported
The verb is pronounced /ɪm'pɔːt/. The noun is pronounced /'ɪmpɔːt/. 动词读作 /ɪm'pɔːt/。名词读作 /'ɪmpɔːt/。
1 VERB 进口;引进 To import products or raw materials means to buy them from another country for use in your own country. 进口;引进

  • Britain last year spent nearly £5000 million more on importing food than selling abroad. [VERB noun]

    去年英国的进口食品支出比其出口食品收入多出近50亿英镑。

  • To import from Russia, a Ukrainian firm needs Russian roubles. [VERB + from]

    要从俄罗斯进口商品,乌克兰公司就需要俄罗斯卢布。

  • ...imported goods from Mexico and India. [VERB-ed]

    自墨西哥和印度进口的货物

SYN bring in, buy in, ship in, land  
Import is also a noun.

  • Germany, however, insists on restrictions on the import of Polish coal.

    但是德国坚持对进口波兰煤炭加以限制。

  • On July 3rd the government slashed import duties on cars.

    7月3号,政府大幅度降低了汽车进口税。

importation

  • ...restrictions concerning the importation of birds.

    关于禽类进口的限制

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 进口商品;进口货物 Imports are products or raw materials bought from another country for use in your own country. 进口商品;进口货物

  • ...farmers protesting about cheap imports.

    抗议进口廉价商品的农民

SYN imported goods, foreign goods  
3 N-UNCOUNT 重要(性) The import of something is its importance. 重要(性) [formal]

  • Who leads Canada is also of some import to the rest of the world.

    谁领导加拿大对世界来讲也具有一定的重要性。

  • Such arguments are of little import.

    这种争论没有什么意义。

SYN significance, concern, value, worth  
4 VERB 导入 If you import files or information into one type of software from another type, you open them in a format that can be used in the new software. 导入 [computing]

  • You can import files from Microsoft Word 5.1 or MacWrite II. [VERB noun]

    可以从 Microsoft Word 5.1 或 MacWrite II 程序导入文件。

5 N-SING [with poss] 意思;含意 The import of something is its meaning, especially when the meaning is not clearly expressed. 意思;含意 [formal]

  • I have already spoken about the import of his speech. [+ of]

    有关他的讲话的含意我刚刚已经谈过了。

SYN meaning, implication, significance, sense
