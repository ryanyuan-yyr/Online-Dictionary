topcoat
tɒpkoʊt ˈtɑpˌkoʊt
(also top coat)Word forms: plural topcoats
1 N-COUNT 外套；轻便大衣;宽大衣 A topcoat is a coat that you wear over your other clothes. 外套；轻便大衣;宽大衣
2 N-VAR (油漆等的)外涂层;外涂层漆 A topcoat is the final layer of paint or varnish that is put on something. Topcoat is the type of paint or varnish that you use for the final layer. (油漆等的)外涂层;外涂层漆
