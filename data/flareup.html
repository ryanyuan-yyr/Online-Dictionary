flare-up
flɛrˌʌp
Word forms: plural flare-ups
N-COUNT (暴乱或疾病的)突发，突然加剧，突然恶化 If there is a flare-up of violence or of an illness, it suddenly starts or gets worse. (暴乱或疾病的)突发，突然加剧，突然恶化

  • There's been a flare-up of violence in South Africa.

    南非暴力冲突突然加剧。

  • ...a flare-up in her arthritis.

    她的关节炎突然加重
