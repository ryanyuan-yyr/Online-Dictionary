dig around
1 PHRASAL VERB 到处翻找;四处搜寻 If you dig around in a place or container, you search for something in every part of it. 到处翻找;四处搜寻 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • I went home to dig around in my closets for some old tapes. [V P + in]

    我回到家后在我的壁橱里四处翻找旧磁带。

2 PHRASAL VERB 到处搜集信息;四处探听消息 If you dig around, you try to find information about someone or something. 到处搜集信息;四处探听消息

  • They said, after digging around, the photo was a fake. [VERB PREP.]

    在搜集了有关情况后，他们说照片是伪造的。
