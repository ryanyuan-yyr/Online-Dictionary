white
hwaɪt hwaɪt waɪt
Word forms: comparative whiter, superlative whitest, plural whites
1 COLOUR 白色;白 Something that is white is the colour of snow or milk. 白色;白

  • He had nice square white teeth.

    他的牙齿洁白而整齐，十分好看。

  • Issa's white beach hat gleamed in the harsh lights.

    伊萨的白色沙滩帽在刺眼的阳光下闪闪发亮。

  • He was dressed in white from head to toe.

    他从头到脚穿了一身白色。

whiteness
to bleed someone white→see: bleed 

  • Her scarlet lipstick emphasized the whiteness of her teeth.

    她鲜红色的口红更衬出了她牙齿的洁白。

2 ADJ 白种人的;白肤色的 A white person has a pale skin and belongs to a race which is of European origin. 白种人的;白肤色的

  • Working with white people hasn't been a problem for me or for them.

    和白人共事对我、对他们来说都不是个问题。

  • He was white, with brown shoulder-length hair and a moustache.

    他是个白人，留着棕色的齐肩发并蓄着小胡子。

Whites are white people.白种人

  • It's a school that's brought blacks and whites and Hispanics together.

    那所学校里会聚了黑人、白人和西班牙裔人。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (因情绪变化或生病等)脸色苍白的 If someone goes white, the skin on their face becomes very pale, for example because of fear, shock, anger, or illness. (因情绪变化或生病等)脸色苍白的

  • Richard had gone very white, but he stood his ground.

    理查德脸都白了，但还是坚持自己的立场。

  • He turned white and began to stammer.

    他脸色发白，说话开始结巴。

  • His face was white with fury. [+ with]

    他脸都白了。

If someone looks white as a sheet or as white as a sheet, they look very frightened, shocked, or ill.(因惊吓或生病而)脸色苍白的

  • He appeared in the doorway, white as a sheet, eyes wide with horror.

    他出现在门口，脸色苍白，睁大的双眼里充满了恐惧。

4 ADJ (葡萄酒)白的，淡黄色的 White wine is pale yellow in colour. (葡萄酒)白的，淡黄色的

  • Gregory poured another glass of white wine and went back to his bedroom.

    格雷戈里又倒了一杯白葡萄酒后回到了卧室。

You can refer to white wine as white .白葡萄酒

  • I bought a bottle of Californian white.

    我买了一瓶加州白葡萄酒。

5 ADJ (咖啡)加牛奶(或奶油)的 White coffee has had milk or cream added to it. (咖啡)加牛奶(或奶油)的 [British]

  • Wayne has a large white coffee in front of him.

    韦恩面前摆着一大杯加了牛奶的咖啡。

8 N-VAR 蛋白;蛋清 The white of an egg is the transparent liquid that surrounds the yellow part called the yolk. 蛋白;蛋清
9 N-COUNT 眼白 The white of someone's eye is the white part that surrounds the coloured part called the iris. 眼白
