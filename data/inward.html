inward
ɪnwəʳd ˈɪnwərd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (思想、感情)内在的,内心的 Your inward thoughts or feelings are the ones that you do not express or show to other people. (思想、感情)内在的,内心的

  • I sighed with inward relief.

    我心里舒了一口气。

  • ...a glow of inward satisfaction.

    内心强烈的满足感

SYN internal, inner, private, personal  
inwardly

  • Chantal smiled inwardly.

    尚塔尔内心窃喜。

  • Sara, while remaining outwardly amiable toward all concerned, was inwardly furious.

    萨拉尽管表面上对所有相关的人依然和蔼可亲,内心却怒火中烧。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 向内的;向中心的 An inward movement is one towards the inside or centre of something. 向内的;向中心的

  • ...a sharp, inward breath like a gasp.

    像倒抽气一样猛地吸入一口气

  • The athlete takes off from one leg from an inward twist.

    这名运动员向内转体一周单腿起跳。

SYN incoming, entering, penetrating, inbound  
3 See also: inwards
