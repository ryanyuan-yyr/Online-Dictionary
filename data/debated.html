debate
dɪbeɪt diˈbeɪt dɪˈbeɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense debates, present participle debating, past tense , past participle debated
1 N-VAR （各自发表不同观点的）辩论，争论，讨论 A debate is a discussion about a subject on which people have different views. （各自发表不同观点的）辩论，争论，讨论 [Also + on/over]

  • An intense debate is going on within the Israeli government.

    以色列政府内部正在进行激烈的辩论。

  • There has been a lot of debate among scholars about this. [+ about]

    学者之间对此一直争论纷纷。

SYN discussion, talk, argument, dispute  
2 N-COUNT (议会或其他机构就某问题在投票前进行的)辩论 A debate is a formal discussion, for example in a parliament or institution, in which people express different opinions about a particular subject and then vote on it. (议会或其他机构就某问题在投票前进行的)辩论 [Also + on/about]

  • There are expected to be some heated debates in parliament over the next few days.

    预计接下来的几天里议会将会进行一些激烈的辩论。

debating

  • It is an excellent idea to encourage them to join a school debating society.

    鼓励他们加入学校的辩论社是个绝佳的主意。

  • ...debating skills.

    辩论技巧

3 V-RECIP （与…）辩论，争论，讨论 If people debate a topic, they discuss it fairly formally, putting forward different views. You can also say that one person debates a topic with another person. （与…）辩论，争论，讨论 [Also V with n]

  • The United Nations Security Council will debate the issue today. [VERB noun]

    联合国安理会今天将就这个问题展开讨论。

  • The causes of anorexia are much debated. [pl-n V n]

    关于厌食的起因存在颇多争论。

  • Scholars have debated whether or not Yagenta became a convert. [VERB wh]

    学者们就弥源太是否皈依进行过辩论。

  • He likes to debate issues with his friends. [VERB noun + with]

    他喜欢跟朋友辩论各种问题。

SYN discuss, question, talk about, argue about  
4 VERB 考虑;盘算;权衡 If you debate whether to do something or what to do, you think or talk about possible courses of action before deciding exactly what you are going to do. 考虑;盘算;权衡

  • Taggart debated whether to have yet another double vodka. [VERB wh]

    塔格特思忖着是否再要一杯双份伏特加。

  • At the moment we are debating what furniture to buy for the house. [VERB wh]

    我们眼下正盘算着为房子购置什么样的家具。

  • I debated going back inside, but decided against it. [VERB verb-ing]

    我寻思着是否再进去，最后还是决定离开。

SYN consider, reflect, think about, weigh  
5 PHRASE 存在争论的;有待讨论的;未确定的 If you say that a matter is open to debate, you mean that people have different opinions about it, or it has not yet been firmly decided. 存在争论的;有待讨论的;未确定的

  • Which of them has more musical talent is open to debate.

    他们之中谁更有音乐天赋还未有定论。
