recommence
riːkəmens ˌriːkəˈmɛns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recommences, present participle recommencing, past tense , past participle recommenced
V-ERG （使）重新开始，（使）继续 If you recommence something or if it recommences, it begins again after having stopped. （使）重新开始，（使）继续 [written]

  • He recommenced work on his novel. [VERB noun]

    他继续写他的小说。

  • His course at Sheffield University will not recommence until next year. [VERB]

    他在谢菲尔德大学的课程要到明年才能继续。
