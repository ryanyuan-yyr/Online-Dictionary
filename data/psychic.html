psychic
saɪkɪk ˈsaɪkɪk
Word forms: plural psychics
1 ADJ-GRADED 通灵的；有特异功能的 If you believe that someone is psychic or has psychic powers, you believe that they have strange mental powers, such as being able to read the minds of other people or to see into the future. 通灵的；有特异功能的

  • Trevor helped police by using his psychic powers.

    特雷弗用他的通灵能力帮助了警方。

SYN supernatural, mystic, occult, clairvoyant  
A psychic is someone who seems to be psychic.通灵的人；有特异功能的人
2 ADJ 鬼魂的；灵魂的 Psychic means relating to ghosts and the spirits of the dead. 鬼魂的；灵魂的

  • He declared his total disbelief in psychic phenomena.

    他宣称自己完全不相信鬼魂现象。

SYN mystical, spiritual, magical, other-worldly  
3 ADJ 心灵的；精神的 Psychic means relating to the mind rather than the body. 心灵的；精神的 [formal]

  • These truths cause individuals much psychic pain.

    这些真相给个体带来巨大的精神痛苦。
