governorship
gʌvnəʳʃɪp ˈɡʌvənəˌʃɪp ˈgʌvərnərˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural governorships
N-COUNT 州长职位；州长任期 The governorship of a particular country or state is the position of being its governor. Governorship is also used to refer to the period of time a particular person spends being the governor of a country or state. 州长职位；州长任期

  • The governorship went to a Democrat, Mrs Anne Richards.

    民主党人安妮·理查兹夫人当选为州长。

  • He had worked closely with the President during his governorship.

    他在任州长期间与总统紧密合作。
