fey
feɪ feɪ
ADJ-GRADED 忸怩的;幼稚的;神经兮兮的 If you describe someone as fey, you mean that they behave in a shy, childish, or unpredictable way, and you are often suggesting that this is unnatural or insincere. 忸怩的;幼稚的;神经兮兮的 [literary]

  • They are no longer anything like as fey and reserved as they once were.

    他们不再像从前那样羞怯矜持了。

  • Her fey charm and eccentric ways were legendary.

    她难以捉摸的魅力和怪异的行事方式都成了传奇。
