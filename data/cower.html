cower
kaʊəʳ ˈkaʊər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cowers, present participle cowering, past tense , past participle cowered
VERB (因恐惧而)蜷缩，畏缩 If you cower, you bend forward and downwards because you are very frightened. (因恐惧而)蜷缩，畏缩

  • The hostages cowered in their seats. [VERB]

    人质们蜷缩在座位上。

SYN cringe, shrink, tremble, crouch
