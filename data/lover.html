lover
lʌvəʳ ˈlʌvər
Word forms: plural lovers
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] （非婚的）爱人，情人 Someone's lover is someone who they are having a sexual relationship with but are not married to. （非婚的）爱人，情人

  • Every Thursday she would meet her lover Leon.

    每周四她都会和情人利昂幽会。

  • He and Liz became lovers soon after they first met.

    他和莉兹初次见面后不久就成了情侣。

SYN sweetheart, beloved, loved one, beau [US]  
2 N-COUNT 热爱者；爱好者；发烧友 If you are a lover of something such as animals or the arts, you enjoy them very much and take great pleasure in them. 热爱者；爱好者；发烧友

  • She is a great lover of horses and horse racing. [+ of]

    她热爱马匹和赛马。

  • Are you an opera lover?

    你是歌剧发烧友吗？
