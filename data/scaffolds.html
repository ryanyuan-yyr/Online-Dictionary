scaffold
skæfoʊld ˈskæfəld ˈskæfˌoʊld
Word forms: plural scaffolds
1 N-COUNT 断头台;绞刑架 A scaffold was a raised platform on which criminals were hanged or had their heads cut off. 断头台;绞刑架

  • Moore ascended the scaffold and addressed the executioner.

    穆尔走上断头台，和刽子手说话。

2 N-COUNT 脚手架;建筑架;鹰架 A scaffold is a temporary raised platform on which workers stand to paint, repair, or build high parts of a building. 脚手架;建筑架;鹰架
