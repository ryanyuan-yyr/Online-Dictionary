ninth
naɪnθ naɪnθ
Word forms: plural ninths
1 ORD 第九 The ninth item in a series is the one that you count as number nine. 第九

  • ...January the ninth.

    1月9日

  • ...students in the ninth grade.

    9年级学生

  • ...ninth century illustrated manuscripts.

    9世纪配有插图的手稿

2 FRACTION 九分之一 A ninth is one of nine equal parts of something. 九分之一

  • In Brussels the dollar rose by a ninth of a cent.

    在布鲁塞尔美元升值了1/9美分。

  • What you see is only the tip. Eight ninths of it is under the sea.

    你看到的只是一角，8/9的部分在海里。
