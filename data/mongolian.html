mongolian mɒŋˈɡəʊlɪən
1 ADJ 蒙古国的;蒙古国人的;蒙古语的;蒙古国文化的 Mongolian means belonging or relating to Mongolia, or to its people, language, or culture. 蒙古国的;蒙古国人的;蒙古语的;蒙古国文化的

  • ...the Mongolian capital, Ulan Bator.

    蒙古国首都，乌兰巴托

2 N-COUNT 蒙古国人;蒙古族人 A Mongolian is a Mongolian citizen, or a person of Mongolian origin. 蒙古国人;蒙古族人
3 N-UNCOUNT 蒙古语 Mongolian is the language that is spoken in Mongolia. 蒙古语

  • Until August of this year there was no complete translation of the New Testament in Mongolian.

    直到今年8月，还没有《新约全书》的蒙古语全译本。
