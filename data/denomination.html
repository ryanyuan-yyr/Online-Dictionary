denomination
dɪnɒmɪneɪʃən diˌnɑməˈneɪʃən dɪˌnɑməˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural denominations
1 N-COUNT (同一宗教中的)教派，宗派 A particular denomination is a particular religious group which has slightly different beliefs from other groups within the same faith. (同一宗教中的)教派，宗派

  • Acceptance of women preachers varies greatly from denomination to denomination.

    在接受女性传教士的问题上，不同教派的态度大相径庭。

SYN religious group, belief, sect, persuasion  
2 N-COUNT (钞票或硬币的)面额，面值 The denomination of a banknote or coin is its official value. (钞票或硬币的)面额，面值

  • ...a pile of bank notes, mostly in small denominations.

    其中大部分都是小面额的一沓钞票

SYN unit, value, size, grade
