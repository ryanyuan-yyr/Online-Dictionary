after-sales service ˈɑːftəˌseɪlz
Word forms: plural after-sales services
N-VAR 售后服务 A company's after-sales service is all the help and information that it provides to customers after they have bought a particular product. 售后服务 [business]

  • ...a local retailer who offers a good after-sales service.

    提供高质量售后服务的当地零售商

  • They are also attempting to keep the car buyer as a long-term customer by offering after-sales service.

    他们也正试图通过提供售后服务来使该汽车采购商成为长期客户。
