inequity
ɪnekwɪti ɪnˈɛkwɪti
Word forms: plural inequities
N-VAR 不公正;不公平 If you refer to the inequity of something, you are criticizing it because it is unfair or unjust. 不公正;不公平 [formal, disapproval]

  • Social imbalance worries him more than inequity of income. [+ of]

    社会失衡比收入不公平更令他忧虑。

  • ...the inequities in our health care system.

    我们卫生保健制度的不公正

SYN unfairness, prejudice, bias, injustice
