eddy
edi ˈɛdi
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural eddies, present participle eddying, past tense , past participle eddied
1 N-COUNT （水或气流的）漩涡，涡流 An eddy is a movement in water or in the air which goes round and round instead of flowing in one continuous direction. （水或气流的）漩涡，涡流
SYN swirl, whirlpool, vortex, undertow  
2 VERB 起漩涡;旋转;杂乱无章地移动 To eddy means to move round and round, or to move in a disorganized way. 起漩涡;旋转;杂乱无章地移动 [literary]

  • The dust whirled and eddied in the sunlight. [VERB]

    灰尘在阳光下旋转翻腾。

  • The crowds were eddying into the road. [VERB prep./adv.]

    人群乱哄哄地涌上街头。

SYN swirl, turn, roll, spin
