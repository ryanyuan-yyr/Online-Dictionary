delectable
dɪlektəbəl dɪˈlɛktəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 使人愉快的;令人高兴的;(尤指食物或饮料)美味的，可口的 If you describe something, especially food or drink, as delectable, you mean that it is very pleasant. 使人愉快的;令人高兴的;(尤指食物或饮料)美味的，可口的

  • ...delectable wine.

    美酒

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （人）性感迷人的，秀色可餐的 If you describe someone as delectable, you think that they are very attractive. （人）性感迷人的，秀色可餐的 [literary]

  • He didn't seem to notice the delectable Miss Campbell.

    他似乎并未注意到妩媚动人的坎贝尔小姐。
