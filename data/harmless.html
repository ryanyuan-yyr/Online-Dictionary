harmless
hɑːʳmləs ˈhɑrmlɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED 无害的;（尤指对健康）不造成伤害的 Something that is harmless does not have any bad effects, especially on people's health. 无害的;（尤指对健康）不造成伤害的

  • Industry has been working at developing harmless substitutes for these gases.

    该行业一直在努力开发一些可以替代这些气体的无害物质。

  • This experiment was harmless to the animals.

    这个试验不会对动物造成伤害。

SYN safe, benign, wholesome, innocuous  
harmlessly

  • Another missile exploded harmlessly outside the town.

    又一枚导弹在城外爆炸，没有造成任何伤害。

2 ADJ-GRADED 无关紧要的;不讨人厌的;不会惹麻烦的 If you describe someone or something as harmless, you mean that they are not important and therefore unlikely to annoy other people or cause trouble. 无关紧要的;不讨人厌的;不会惹麻烦的

  • He seemed harmless enough.

    他看起来不会惹什么麻烦。

  • I would not want to deny them a harmless pleasure.

    我不想剥夺他们这点无伤大雅的乐趣。

SYN inoffensive, innocent, innocuous, gentle  
harmlessly

  • It started harmlessly enough, with a statement from the Secretary of State for Social Security.

    开始时并没有什么重要内容，由社会保障大臣作了一个发言。
