frame-up
ˈfreɪmˌʌp
Word forms: plural frame-ups
N-COUNT 诬陷;陷害 A frame-up is a situation where someone pretends that an innocent person has committed a crime by deliberately lying or inventing evidence. 诬陷;陷害 [informal]

  • He was innocent and the victim of a frame-up.

    他是清白的，是受人诬陷的。

SYN false charge, set-up [slang] , fabrication, fit-up [slang]
