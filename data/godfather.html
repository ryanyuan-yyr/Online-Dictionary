godfather
gɒdfɑːðəʳ ˈgɑdˌfɑðər
Word forms: plural godfathers
1 N-COUNT 教父 A godfather is a male godparent. 教父
2 N-COUNT 犯罪集团的头目；黑帮老大 A powerful man who is at the head of a criminal organization is sometimes referred to as a godfather . 犯罪集团的头目；黑帮老大

  • ...the feared godfather of the Mafia.

    令人畏惧的黑手党头目

3 N-COUNT 开拓者；发起者；创始人 You can refer to a man who started or developed something such as a style of music as the godfather of that thing. 开拓者；发起者；创始人 [journalism]

  • ...the godfather of soul, James Brown. [+ of]

    灵乐的开创者詹姆斯· 布朗
