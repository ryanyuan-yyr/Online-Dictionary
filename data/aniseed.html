aniseed
ænɪsiːd ˈænɪˌsid
N-UNCOUNT 茴芹籽（用作糖果、饮料和药物的调味剂） Aniseed is a substance made from the seeds of the anise plant. It is used as a flavouring in sweets, drinks, and medicine. 茴芹籽（用作糖果、饮料和药物的调味剂）

  • Fennel has a very distinctive aniseed flavour.

    茴香有一种与众不同的茴芹籽的味道。
