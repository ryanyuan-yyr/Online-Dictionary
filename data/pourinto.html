pour into
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 大量投入（金钱或供应品）；大量涌来 If you pour money or supplies into an activity or organization, or if it pours in, a lot of money or supplies are given in order to do the activity or help the organization. 大量投入（金钱或供应品）；大量涌来

  • The Government continues to pour billions of pounds into its massive road-building programme. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    政府继续向庞大的筑路项目中投入数十亿英镑。

  • Food donations have poured in from all over the country. [VERB PREP.]

    捐赠的食物已从全国各地涌来。
