ravage
rævɪdʒ ˈrævɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ravages, present participle ravaging, past tense , past participle ravaged
VERB [usually passive] 毁坏;摧毁;搞垮 A town, country, or economy that has been ravaged is one that has been damaged so much that it is almost completely destroyed. 毁坏;摧毁;搞垮

  • For two decades the country has been ravaged by civil war and foreign intervention. [be VERB-ed]

    20年来，这个国家一直被内战外侵所蹂躏。

  • ...the ravaged streets of Sarajevo. [VERB-ed]

    萨拉热窝那些被毁坏的街道

  • ...Nicaragua's ravaged economy. [VERB-ed]

    尼加拉瓜被摧毁的经济

SYN destroy, ruin, devastate, wreck
