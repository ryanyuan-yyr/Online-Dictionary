myriad
mɪriəd ˈmɪriəd
1 QUANT 无数;大量;各种各样 A myriad or myriads of people or things is a very large number or great variety of them. 无数;大量;各种各样

  • They face a myriad of problems bringing up children. [+ of]

    他们在抚养孩子的过程中遇到了各种各样的问题。

  • These myriads of fish would be enough to keep any swimmer entranced for hours. [+ of]

    这些不同种类的鱼足以让任何一个游泳者数小时流连忘返。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 无数的;大量的；各种各样的 Myriad means having a large number or great variety. 无数的;大量的；各种各样的

  • ...British pop and culture in all its myriad forms.

    以多种不同形式出现的英国流行音乐和文化

  • ...the myriad tiny animals and plants living in the ice.

    在冰里生存着的无数微小动植物

SYN innumerable, countless, untold, incalculable
