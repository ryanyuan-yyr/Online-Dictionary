mournful
mɔːʳnfʊl ˈmɔrnfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 悲哀的;悲伤的 If you are mournful, you are very sad. 悲哀的;悲伤的

  • He looked mournful, even near to tears.

    他看起来很悲伤，几乎要流泪了。

  • Miss Dickerman was tall and somewhat mournful-looking.

    迪克曼小姐个子高高的，看上去有些苦相。

SYN dismal, sad, unhappy, miserable  
mournfully

  • He stood mournfully at the gate waving bye bye.

    他伤心地站在门口，挥手告别。

2 ADJ-GRADED (声音)凄楚的，凄厉的，悲切的 A mournful sound seems very sad. (声音)凄楚的，凄厉的，悲切的

  • ...the mournful wail of bagpipes.

    风笛凄厉的哀诉声

SYN sad, distressing, unhappy, tragic
