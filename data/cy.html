-cy -si si sɪ
Word forms: plural -cies
1 SUFFIX (代替某些形容词词尾的 -te，-t,和 -tic，置于形容词后构成名词)表示“状态”，“性质” -cy replaces '-te', '-t', and '-tic' at the end of some adjectives to form nouns referring to the state or quality described by the adjective. (代替某些形容词词尾的 -te，-t,和 -tic，置于形容词后构成名词)表示“状态”，“性质”

  • ...the emotional intimacy of a family.

    一家人感情上的亲密

  • They were sworn to secrecy.

    他们依照要求起誓保密。

2 SUFFIX (用于名词后构成名词)表示“等级”，“职务” -cy is added to some nouns referring to people with a particular rank or post in order to form nouns that refer to this rank or post. (用于名词后构成名词)表示“等级”，“职务”

  • He is likely to retain the England captaincy.

    他有望继续担任英格兰队队长。

  • ...the university chaplaincy.

    大学特遣牧师职位
