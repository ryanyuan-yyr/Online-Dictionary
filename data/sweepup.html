sweep up
PHRASAL VERB 扫除;清扫 If you sweep up rubbish or dirt, you push it together with a brush and then remove it. 扫除;清扫 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Get a broom and sweep up that glass will you?. [VERB PREP. noun]

    拿扫帚把那些玻璃清扫干净好吗？

  • He started working for a gallery sweeping up and making the tea. [VERB PREP.]

    他开始在画廊做扫地沏茶的工作。
