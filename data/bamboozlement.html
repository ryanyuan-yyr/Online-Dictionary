bamboozle
bæmbuːzəl bæmˈbuzəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bamboozles, present participle bamboozling, past tense , past participle bamboozled
VERB 迷惑；糊弄；欺骗 To bamboozle someone means to confuse them greatly and often trick them. 迷惑；糊弄；欺骗

  • He bamboozled Mercer into defeat. [VERB noun + into]

    他骗得默瑟认了输。

  • He was bamboozled by con men. [be VERB-ed]

    他被骗子骗了。

SYN cheat, do [informal] , kid [informal] , skin [slang]
