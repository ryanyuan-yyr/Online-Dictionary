battlefield
bætəlfiːld ˈbætəlˌɡraʊnd ˈbætəlˌfild
Word forms: plural battlefields
1 N-COUNT 战场；沙场；战地 A battlefield is a place where a battle is fought. 战场；沙场；战地

  • ...the battlefields of the Somme.

    索姆河战役的战场

SYN battleground, front, field, war zone  
2 N-COUNT 争论的主题；斗争的领域 You can refer to an issue or field of activity over which people disagree or compete as a battlefield . 争论的主题；斗争的领域

  • ...the domestic battlefield of family life.

    家庭生活内部争论的主题
