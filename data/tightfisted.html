tight-fisted
ADJ-GRADED 吝啬的;小气的;抠门的 If you describe someone as tight-fisted, you disapprove of them because they are unwilling to spend money. 吝啬的;小气的;抠门的 [disapproval]

  • He had the reputation of being one of the most tight-fisted and demanding of employers.

    人们都知道他是最抠门、最苛刻的雇主之一。

  • ...the government's tight-fisted monetary policy.

    政府锱铢必较的货币政策

SYN miserly, mean, stingy, close
