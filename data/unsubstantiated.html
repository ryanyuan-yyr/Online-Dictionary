unsubstantiated
ʌnsəbstænʃieɪtɪd ˌʌnsəbˈstænʃɪˌeɪtɪd
ADJ 未经证实的；无根据的；不确凿的 A claim, accusation, or story that is unsubstantiated has not been proved to be valid or true. 未经证实的；无根据的；不确凿的

  • I do object to their claim, which I find totally unsubstantiated.

    我强烈反对他们的说法，那是毫无根据的。

  • ...unsubstantiated rumours about his private life.

    有关他私生活的捕风捉影的谣言

SYN unconfirmed, unproven, unsupported, speculative
