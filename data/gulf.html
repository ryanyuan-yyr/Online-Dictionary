gulf
gʌlf gʌlf
Word forms: plural gulfs
1 N-COUNT 鸿沟；严重分歧；巨大的差距 A gulf is an important or significant difference between two people, things, or groups. 鸿沟；严重分歧；巨大的差距

  • Within society, there is a growing gulf between rich and poor. [+ between]

    社会上的贫富差距正在逐渐加大。

  • ...the gulf between rural and urban life. [+ between]

    城乡生活的巨大差异

SYN chasm, opening, split, gap  
2 N-COUNT 海湾 A gulf is a large area of sea which extends a long way into the surrounding land. 海湾

  • Hurricane Andrew was last night heading into the Gulf of Mexico.

    昨晚，飓风“安德鲁”进入了墨西哥湾。

SYN bay, bight, sea inlet
