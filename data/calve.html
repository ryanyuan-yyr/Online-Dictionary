calve
kɑːv kæv kæv kɑv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense calves, present participle calving, past tense , past participle calved
1 VERB 产犊;生小牛 When a cow calves, it gives birth to a calf. 产犊;生小牛

  • When his cows calve each year he keeps one or two calves for his family. [VERB]

    他的母牛每年产犊时,他都给自家留上一两头。

2 VERB (象、鲸等)产崽 Some other female animals, including elephants and whales, are said to calve when they give birth to their young. (象、鲸等)产崽

  • The whales migrate some 6,000 miles to breed and calve in the warm lagoons. [VERB]

    鲸会迁移6,000海里左右,到温暖的环礁湖生殖繁衍。

3 （calf 的复数） Calves is the plural of calf. （calf 的复数）
