well advised wɛl ædˈvaɪzd
(also well-advised)
ADJ-GRADED 明智的;稳妥的 If someone says that you would be well advised to do a particular thing, they are advising you to do it. 明智的;稳妥的

  • Moderates believe the party would be well advised to talk to the government.

    温和派认为该政党的明智做法是去和政府谈判。

SYN wise, advised, sensible, prudent
