throw down
PHRASAL VERB 公然挑衅;大胆挑战 If you throw down a challenge to someone, you do something new or unexpected in a bold or forceful manner that will probably cause them to reply or react equally strongly. 公然挑衅;大胆挑战

  • The regional parliament threw down a new challenge to the central authorities by passing a law allowing private ownership of businesses. [VERB PREP. noun]

    地方议会通过了一项法律，允许企业私有，这向中央政权发起了新的挑战。

  • Government ministers have been responding to the challenge thrown down by their former colleague. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    政府部长们一直在应对从前的同事的公然挑战。
