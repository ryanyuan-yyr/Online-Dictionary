superannuated
suːpərænjueɪtɪd ˌsuːpərˈænjʊˌeɪtɪd ˈsupərˈænjuˌeɪtɪd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 废弃的;陈旧不中用的;过时的 If you describe something as superannuated, you mean that it is old and no longer used for its original purpose. 废弃的;陈旧不中用的;过时的 [formal]

  • ...the superannuated idealism of the Sixties.

    早已过时的 60 年代的理想主义

SYN obsolete, antiquated, outmoded, old
