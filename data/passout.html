pass out
1 昏倒；晕过去 If you pass out, you faint or collapse. 昏倒；晕过去

  • He felt sick and dizzy and then passed out. [VERB PREP.]

    他感到恶心、眩晕，然后就昏了过去。

  • She passed out drunk. [VERB PREP.]

    她醉倒了。

2 （警校、军校学员）毕业 When a police, army, navy, or air force cadet passes out, he or she completes his or her training. （警校、军校学员）毕业

  • He passed out in November 1924 and was posted to No 24 Squadron. [VERB PREP.]

    他于1924年11月毕业，被派往空军第24中队。
