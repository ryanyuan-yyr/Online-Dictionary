exclamation
ekskləmeɪʃən ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən
Word forms: plural exclamations
N-COUNT 惊叫;感叹;感叹词;感叹句 An exclamation is a sound, word, or sentence that is spoken suddenly, loudly, or emphatically and that expresses excitement, admiration, shock, or anger. 惊叫;感叹;感叹词;感叹句

  • Sue gave an exclamation as we got a clear sight of the house.

    我们清楚看到那所房子时，休惊叹不已。

SYN cry, call, shout, yell
