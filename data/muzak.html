Muzak
mjuːzæk ˈmjuːzæk ˈmjuˌzæk
(also muzak)
1 N-UNCOUNT 米尤扎克(商店或餐馆播放的背景音乐) Muzak is recorded music that is played as background music in shops or restaurants. 米尤扎克(商店或餐馆播放的背景音乐) [trademark]
2 N-UNCOUNT 让人提不起兴趣的音乐 If you describe music as muzak, you dislike it because you think it is dull or unnecessary. 让人提不起兴趣的音乐 [disapproval]
