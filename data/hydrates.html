hydrate
haɪdreɪt ˈhaɪˌdreɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hydrates, present participle hydrating, past tense , past participle hydrated
1 N-MASS 水合物 A hydrate is a chemical compound that contains water. 水合物

  • ...aluminium hydrate.

    氢氧化铝

2 VERB 为（皮肤）补水 If a substance hydrates your skin, it makes it softer and less dry. 为（皮肤）补水

  • After-sun products will cool and hydrate your skin. [VERB noun]

    晒后护肤品会清凉肌肤并为其补水。

SYN moisten, wet, water, soak
