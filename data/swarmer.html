swarm
swɔːʳm swɔrm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense swarms, present participle swarming, past tense , past participle swarmed
1 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (蜜蜂等昆虫的)一大群，一大窝 A swarm of bees or other insects is a large group of them flying together. (蜜蜂等昆虫的)一大群，一大窝
2 VERB (蜜蜂等)成群飞行 When bees or other insects swarm, they move or fly in a large group. (蜜蜂等)成群飞行 [Also VERB]

  • A dark cloud of bees comes swarming out of the hive. [VERB prep./adv.]

    黑压压的一大群蜜蜂从蜂巢中飞出来。

3 VERB (人)成群地涌动，蜂拥 When people swarm somewhere, they move there quickly in a large group. (人)成群地涌动，蜂拥 [Also VERB n]

  • People swarmed to the shops, buying up everything in sight. [VERB prep./adv.]

    人们成群地涌向商店，见什么买什么。

SYN crowd, flock, throng, mass  
4 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (快速移动的)人群 A swarm of people is a large group of them moving about quickly. (快速移动的)人群

  • A swarm of people encircled the hotel.

    一大群人围住了这个酒店。

  • Today at the crossing there were swarms of tourists taking photographs.

    今天在十字路口有大群的游客在拍照。

SYN multitude, crowd, mass, army  
5 VERB [usually cont] 挤满，聚集(来来往往的人) If a place is swarming with people, it is full of people moving about in a busy way. 挤满，聚集(来来往往的人)

  • Within minutes the area was swarming with officers who began searching a nearby wood. [VERB + with]

    几分钟内很多警察就来到了这一地区，开始搜查附近的树林。

SYN teem, crawl, be alive, abound
