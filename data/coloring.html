colouring
kʌlərɪŋ ˈkʌlərɪŋ
in AM, use 美国英语用 coloring
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 颜色;色彩 The colouring of something is the colour or colours that it is. 颜色;色彩

  • Other countries vary the coloring of their bank notes as well as their size. [+ of]

    其他国家的纸币色彩不一，大小也不同。

  • ...the scenery was losing its bright colouring.

    景色逐渐褪去鲜艳的色彩。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] (头发、皮肤和眼睛的)颜色 Someone's colouring is the colour of their hair, skin, and eyes. (头发、皮肤和眼睛的)颜色

  • None of them had their father's dark colouring.

    他们都不像父亲那样黑。

  • Choose shades which tone in with your natural colouring.

    选择与你本身的肤色相衬的颜色。

3 N-UNCOUNT 食物色素;着色剂 Colouring is a substance that is used to give colour to food. 食物色素;着色剂

  • A few drops of green food coloring were added.

    加了几滴绿色的食物色素。
