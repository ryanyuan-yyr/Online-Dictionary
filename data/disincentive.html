disincentive
dɪsɪnsentɪv ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪv
Word forms: plural disincentives
N-VAR 制止因素;阻碍因素 A disincentive is something which discourages people from behaving or acting in a particular way. 制止因素;阻碍因素 [formal]

  • High marginal tax rates may act as a disincentive to working longer hours. [+ to]

    高额的边际税率可能会对加班起到抑制作用。

SYN discouragement, deterrent, impediment, damper
