roundly
raʊndli ˈraʊndli
ADV-GRADED 严厉地;狠狠地;彻底地 If you are roundly condemned or criticized, you are condemned or criticized forcefully or by many people. If you are roundly defeated, you are defeated completely. 严厉地;狠狠地;彻底地

  • Political leaders have roundly condemned the shooting.

    政治领袖强烈谴责了此次枪杀事件。

  • Liz Davies took the issue to the party but was roundly defeated.

    利兹·戴维斯向政党提出了这个问题，但被断然否决了。

SYN thoroughly, sharply, severely, bitterly
