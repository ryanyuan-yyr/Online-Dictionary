quiescent
kwiesənt kwaɪ- kwaɪˈɛsənt kwiˈɛsənt
ADJ-GRADED 沉寂的;静态的;不活动的 Someone or something that is quiescent is quiet and inactive. 沉寂的;静态的;不活动的 [literary]

  • ...a society which was politically quiescent and above all deferential.

    政治上波澜不兴、甚至马首是瞻的社会

  • ...a quiescent Southern seaside town.

    宁静的南方海滨小城

SYN quiet, still, peaceful, calm  
quiescence

  • ...a long period of quiescence.

    长期的沉寂
