witch-hunt
Word forms: plural witch-hunts
N-COUNT (针对异见群体的)迫害，搜捕，“猎巫” A witch-hunt is an attempt to find and punish a particular group of people who are being blamed for something, often simply because of their opinions and not because they have actually done anything wrong. (针对异见群体的)迫害，搜捕，“猎巫” [disapproval]
