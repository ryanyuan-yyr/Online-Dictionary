figure of speech
Word forms: plural figures of speech
N-COUNT 比喻;修辞格 A figure of speech is an expression or word that is used with a metaphorical rather than a literal meaning. 比喻;修辞格

  • Of course I'm not. It was just a figure of speech.

    我当然不是，这不过是打个比方。

SYN expression, image, turn of phrase, trope
