studded
stʌdɪd ˈstʌdɪd
1 ADJ 用类似饰钉物装饰的;带饰钉的 Something that is studded is decorated with studs or things that look like studs. 用类似饰钉物装饰的;带饰钉的 [Also + with]

  • ...studded leather jackets.

    镶有饰钉的皮夹克

  • ...a beautiful gold bracelet studded with diamonds.

    漂亮的镶钻金手镯

Studded is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • ...a gold and diamond-studded trophy.

    镶钻的金奖杯

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ with n] 布满…的;有很多…的 If you say that something is studded with another thing, you mean that there are a lot of the second thing in or on the first thing. 布满…的;有很多…的

  • His collection was studded with Flemish masterpieces.

    他的收藏里有许多佛兰芒人的杰作。

  • ...a metal panel studded with small microphones.

    装有很多小扩音器的金属嵌板

SYN covered, dotted, scattered, spotted  
3 See also: star-studded
