perplexed
pəʳplekst pəˈplɛkst pərˈplɛkst
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 困惑的；迷惑不解的 If you are perplexed, you feel confused and slightly worried by something because you do not understand it. 困惑的；迷惑不解的

  • She is perplexed about what to do for her daughter. [+ about]

    她不知道该为女儿做些什么。

  • Japan's perplexed bankers need not worry.

    迷惑不解的日本银行家不必担心。

SYN puzzled, confused, stumped, baffled
