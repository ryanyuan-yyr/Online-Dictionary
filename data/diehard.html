diehard daɪhɑːʳd
(also die-hard)Word forms: plural diehards
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 顽固分子;死硬派;铁杆支持者 A diehard is someone who is very strongly opposed to change and new ideas, or who is a very strong supporter of a person or idea. 顽固分子;死硬派;铁杆支持者

  • The president hopes the diehards will resign over the reform proposals.

    总统希望那些顽固分子会因为这些改革提案而辞职。

  • Not even their diehard supporters can pretend that this was a great game.

    即使是他们的铁杆支持者也无法假装这是一场精彩的比赛。
