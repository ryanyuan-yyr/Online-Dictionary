unfashionable
ʌnfæʃənəbəl ʌnˈfæʃənəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不流行的；过时的 If something is unfashionable, it is not approved of or done by most people. 不流行的；过时的

  • Wearing fur has become deeply unfashionable.

    穿毛皮衣物已经相当过时了。

  • The couple hold the unfashionable view that marriage is a sacred union.

    夫妇俩对婚姻的看法很传统，认为婚姻是神圣的结合。

SYN passé, out of date, outmoded, out  
unfashionably

  • He wears his blonde hair unfashionably long.

    他留着金色的长发，很不入时。
