vote down
PHRASAL VERB 投票否决;投票拒绝 If people vote down a person or their proposal, they reject that person or proposal, usually as a result of a formal vote. 投票否决;投票拒绝

  • The Congress voted down a motion to change the union's structure. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    国会投票否决了改变工会结构的动议。

  • If he demands too much, the unions will vote him down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果他要求太多，工会就会投票撤换他。
