underexposed
ʌndərɪkspoʊzd ˌʌndərɪkˈspəʊzd
ADJ-GRADED （照片）曝光不足的 If a photograph is underexposed, it is darker than it should be because the film was not exposed to enough light. （照片）曝光不足的

  • Photos taken by compact cameras are often dark because they're slightly underexposed.

    袖珍相机所拍的照片往往比较暗，因为存在轻微的曝光不足。
