inky
ɪŋki ˈɪŋki
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 墨黑的;漆黑的 Inky means black or very dark blue. 墨黑的;漆黑的 [literary]

  • The moon was rising in the inky sky.

    月亮升起在墨似的夜空中。

  • ...the inky black of night.

    漆黑的夜晚

SYN black, dark, jet, murky  
Inky is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • ...looking out over an inky blue ocean.

    眺望深蓝色的海洋

2 ADJ 沾有墨水的 If something is inky, it is covered in ink. 沾有墨水的

  • ...inky fingers.

    沾了墨水的手指
