hallowed
hæloʊd ˈhæloʊd ˈhæloʊɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （通常因古老、重要或有名望而）受崇敬的，受尊崇的 Hallowed is used to describe something that is respected and admired, usually because it is old, important, or has a good reputation. （通常因古老、重要或有名望而）受崇敬的，受尊崇的

  • The hallowed turf of Twickenham is the venue for the Middlesex Rugby Sevens Finals.

    备受尊崇的特威肯纳姆体育场是米德尔塞克斯七人制橄榄球赛决赛的比赛场地。

  • They protested that there was no place for a school of commerce in their hallowed halls of learning.

    他们抗议说，在他们神圣不可侵犯的学术殿堂里没有商学院的位置。

2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 神圣的 Hallowed is used to describe something that is considered to be holy. 神圣的

  • ...hallowed ground.

    圣地
