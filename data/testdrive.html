test drive tɛst draɪv
(also test-drive)Word forms: test drives, test driving, test drove
1 V-T 试驾If you test drive a car or other vehicle, you drive it for a short period in order to assess its performance before deciding whether to buy it. 试驾

  • ...three invitations to test drive expensive cars.

    ...试驾高档轿车的三次邀请。

2 N-COUNT 试车Test drive is also a noun. 试车

  • Consumers are buying cars from websites without ever going for a test drive.

    消费者现在都不试就直接从网站上买车。

3 V-T 试用If you test drive a product, you try it for a short period in order to assess its qualities before deciding whether to buy it. 试用

  • Kids can test drive the latest games on large-screen TVs with state-of-the-art sound.

    孩子们可以在有高级音响效果的大屏幕电视上试玩新游戏。
