penalty shoot-out
Word forms: plural penalty shoot-outs
N-COUNT （足球的）点球大战，点球决胜 In football, a penalty shoot-out is a way of deciding the result of a game that has ended in a draw. Players from each team try to score a goal in turn until one player fails to score and their team loses the game. （足球的）点球大战，点球决胜 [mainly British]
