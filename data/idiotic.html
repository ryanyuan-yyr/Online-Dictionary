idiotic
ɪdiɒtɪk ˌɪdiˈɑtɪk
ADJ-GRADED 十分愚蠢的;白痴般的 If you call someone or something idiotic, you mean that they are very stupid or silly. 十分愚蠢的;白痴般的 [disapproval]

  • What an idiotic thing to say!

    多么愚蠢透顶的话！

SYN foolish, crazy, stupid, dumb [informal]  
idiotically

  • ...his idiotically romantic views.

    他愚蠢可笑、不切实际的观点
