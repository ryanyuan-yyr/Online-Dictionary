blow away
1 PHRASAL VERB 使印象深刻 If you say that you are blown away by something, or if it blows you away, you mean that you are very impressed by it. 使印象深刻 [informal]

  • I was blown away by the tone and the quality of the story. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    这个故事的基调和内容打动了我。

  • She just totally blew me away with her singing. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她的歌声让我完全陶醉其中。

SYN bowl over, amaze, stun, stagger  
2 PHRASAL VERB 枪杀 If someone blows another person away, they kill them by shooting them. 枪杀 [informal]

  • He'd like to get hold of a gun and blow them all away. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他恨不得拿把枪，把他们全干掉。

SYN open fire on, kill, blast [slang] , bring down
