visibility
vɪzɪbɪlɪti ˌvɪzəˈbɪləti
1 N-UNCOUNT 可见度;能见度;能见距离 Visibility means how far or how clearly you can see in particular weather conditions. 可见度;能见度;能见距离

  • Visibility was poor.

    能见度很差。

2 N-UNCOUNT 可见性;关注程度;引人注目的程度 If you refer to the visibility of something such as a situation or problem, you mean how much it is seen or noticed by other people. 可见性;关注程度;引人注目的程度

  • The plight of the Kurds gained global visibility.

    库尔德人的苦难引起了全球关注。
