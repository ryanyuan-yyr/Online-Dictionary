warble
wɔːʳbəl ˈwɔrbəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , 3rd person singular present tense warbles, present participle warbling, past tense , past participle warbled
1 VERB (鸟)啭鸣，啁啾 When a bird warbles, it sings pleasantly. (鸟)啭鸣，啁啾

  • The bird continued to warble. [VERB]

    鸟儿继续啁啾。

  • A flock of birds was already warbling a cheerful morning chorus. [VERB noun]

    一群鸟儿已经齐声唱起了欢快的晨曲。

SYN sing, trill, chirp, twitter  
2 VERB 用颤音唱 If someone warbles, they sing in a high-pitched, rather unsteady voice. 用颤音唱

  • She warbled as she worked. [VERB]

    她边工作边用颤音唱歌。

  • ...singers warbling 'Over the Rainbow'. [VERB noun]

    用颤音唱《彩虹彼端》的歌手们

Warble is also a noun.

  • ...the soft warble of her speaking voice.

    她轻柔而带颤音的说话声

3 VERB (电话等)发出颤音 When machines such as telephones warble, they make high-pitched sounds. (电话等)发出颤音

  • The telephone on his desk warbled. [VERB]

    他桌上的电话叮铃铃响了。
