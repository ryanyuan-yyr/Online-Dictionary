panegyric
pænɪdʒɪrɪk ˌpænəˈdʒɪrɪk ˌpænəˈdʒaɪrɪk
Word forms: plural panegyrics
N-COUNT 颂词；颂文 A panegyric is a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something. 颂词；颂文 [formal]

  • ...Prince Charles's panegyric on rural living. [+ on]

    查尔斯王子对农村生活的颂词

SYN tribute, praise, homage, accolade
