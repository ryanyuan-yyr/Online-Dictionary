enamel
ɪnæməl ɛˈnæməl ɪˈnæməl
Word forms: plural enamels
1 N-MASS [oft NOUN n] 搪瓷;珐琅;釉 Enamel is a substance like glass which can be heated and put onto metal, glass, or pottery in order to decorate or protect it. 搪瓷;珐琅;釉

  • ...a white enamel saucepan on the oil stove.

    煤油炉上的白色搪瓷炖锅

  • ...enamel baths.

    搪瓷浴缸

2 N-MASS [oft NOUN n] （尤指用于金属和木材上漆的）瓷漆,瓷釉 Enamel is a hard, shiny paint that is used especially for painting metal and wood. （尤指用于金属和木材上漆的）瓷漆,瓷釉

  • ...enamel polymer paints.

    聚合瓷漆

  • He relied on translucent enamels to produce vivid, glowing pictures.

    他利用半透明瓷漆绘制出鲜艳生动的图画。

3 N-UNCOUNT （牙齿的）珐琅质，釉质 Enamel is the hard white substance that forms the outer part of a tooth. （牙齿的）珐琅质，釉质
