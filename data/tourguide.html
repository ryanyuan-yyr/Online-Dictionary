tour guide
tʊə ɡaɪd
Word forms: tour guides
1 N-COUNT 导游A tour guide is a person employed by a travel company to assist people who are on holiday. 导游

  • There is a tour guide throughout the trip who will organize optional dinners and sightseeing.

    有一位全程导游，可安排非强制性的进餐和观光。

2 N-COUNT 导览员A tour guide is someone who shows tourists around places such as museums or cities. 导览员

  • ...an old tour guide at the temple.

    ...这座庙里的一位老导览员。
