pacemaker
peɪsmeɪkəʳ ˈpeɪsˌmeɪkər
Word forms: plural pacemakers
1 N-COUNT 心脏起搏器 A pacemaker is a device that is placed inside someone's body in order to help their heart beat in the right way. 心脏起搏器

  • She was fitted with a pacemaker after suffering serious heart trouble.

    她患上严重的心脏病后安装了心脏起搏器。

2 N-COUNT （赛跑中的）领跑员 A pacemaker is a competitor in a race whose task is to start the race very quickly in order to help the other runners achieve a very fast time. Pacemakers usually stop before the race is finished. （赛跑中的）领跑员
