stay-at-home
Word forms: plural stay-at-homes
N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] (不去工作或旅行)守在家里的人 If you describe someone as a stay-at-home, you mean that they stay at home rather than going out to work or travelling. (不去工作或旅行)守在家里的人

  • I was a stay-at-home mum until 1980 when my husband lost his job.

    1980 年我丈夫失业之前，我一直是个家庭主妇。
