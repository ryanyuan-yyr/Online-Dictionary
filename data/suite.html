suite
swiːt swit sut
Word forms: plural suites
1 N-COUNT 一套房间;套间 A suite is a set of rooms in a hotel or other building. 一套房间;套间

  • They had a fabulous time during their week in a suite at the Paris Hilton.

    他们在巴黎希尔顿酒店的套房里度过了非常愉快的一周。

  • ...a new suite of offices.

    新的办公套间

SYN rooms, apartment, set of rooms, living quarters  
2See also:en suite 
3 N-COUNT 一套家具 A suite is a set of matching armchairs and a sofa. 一套家具

  • ...a three-piece suite.

    三件套家具

4 N-COUNT 一套浴室家具 A bathroom suite is a matching bath, washbasin, and toilet. 一套浴室家具
