occupancy
ɒkjʊpənsi ˈɑkjupənsi ˈɑkjəpənsi
N-UNCOUNT （通常指一段固定时间内对房屋、土地等的）占用 Occupancy is the act of using a room, building, or area of land, usually for a fixed period of time. （通常指一段固定时间内对房屋、土地等的）占用 [formal]

  • Hotel occupancy has been as low as 40%.

    酒店入住率最低达到40％。

  • Tour operators report low occupancy rates.

    旅游业经营者反映客房入住率低。

SYN occupation, use, residence, holding
