erasure
ɪreɪʒəʳ -reɪʃ- iˈreɪʃər ɪˈreɪʃər
N-UNCOUNT 删除;丢失;破坏;消灭 The erasure of something is the removal, loss, or destruction of it. 删除;丢失;破坏;消灭 [formal]

  • ...a further erasure of the UK's thin manufacturing base.

    对英国制造业薄弱基础的进一步破坏

  • ...the final and cataclysmic erasure of all the remaining dinosaurs 65 million years ago.

    6,500万年前所有残存恐龙的最终灾难性灭绝

SYN deletion, wiping, wiping out, cancellation
