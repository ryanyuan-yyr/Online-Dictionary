miaow
miaʊ miˈaʊ mjaʊ
(also meow)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense miaows, present participle miaowing, past tense , past participle miaowed
N-COUNT; SOUND 喵(猫的叫声) Miaow is used to represent the noise that a cat makes. 喵(猫的叫声)

  • He made a frightened noise a little like the miaow of a cat.

    他发出了一个吓人的声音，有点像猫在叫唤。

Miaow is also a verb.

  • Cats miaow when they are unhappy, purr when they are happy.

    猫不高兴时会喵喵叫，高兴时会发出呜呜声。
