criminalize
krɪmɪnəlaɪz ˈkrɪmənəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense criminalizes, present participle criminalizing, past tense , past participle criminalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 criminalise
VERB 使不合法;使非法 If a government criminalizes an action or person, it officially declares that the action or the person's behaviour is illegal. 使不合法;使非法

  • There is no move to criminalise alcohol. [VERB noun]

    没有采取禁酒措施。

  • ...a deliberate campaign to criminalise members of the former government. [VERB noun]

    蓄意给前政府成员定罪的运动
