striptease
strɪptiːz -tiːz ˈstrɪpˌtiz
(also strip-tease)Word forms: plural stripteases
N-VAR 脱衣舞(表演) Striptease is a form of entertainment in which someone slowly takes off their clothes in a sexually exciting way, usually while music is played. 脱衣舞(表演)

  • Oscar, much the worse for vodka, did a striptease.

    奥斯卡喝了伏特加后醉醺醺地跳起了脱衣舞。

  • ...a striptease artist.

    脱衣舞演员
