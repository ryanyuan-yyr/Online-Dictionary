astonishing
əstɒnɪʃɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人惊讶的；非常惊人的 Something that is astonishing is very surprising. 令人惊讶的；非常惊人的

  • It's astonishing, he's learned Latin in three hours!

    他花了3个小时就学会了拉丁文，真让人吃惊！

  • ...an astonishing display of physical strength.

    表现出的惊人体力

SYN amazing, striking, surprising, brilliant  
astonishingly

  • Isabella was an astonishingly beautiful young woman.

    伊莎贝拉是个美貌惊人的年轻女人。

  • He was wearing, astonishingly, a frilly pink apron over shirt and trousers.

    他穿得很奇怪，衬衫和裤子外面套着件镶褶边的粉红围裙。
