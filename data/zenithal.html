zenith
zenɪθ ˈzɛnɪθ ziː- ˈzinɪθ ˈziːnɪθ
1 N-SING [usually with poss] 全盛时期;鼎盛时期 The zenith of something is the time when it is most successful or powerful. 全盛时期;鼎盛时期

  • His career is now at its zenith.

    他的事业现在处于鼎盛时期。

  • The zenith of Perugia's influence came with the defeat of Siena in 1358.

    1358年击败锡耶纳之后佩鲁贾的影响力达到了顶峰。

SYN height, summit, peak, top  
2 N-SING 天顶（太阳或月亮在天空中的最高点） The zenith is the point at which the sun or moon is directly above you and seems to be at its highest. 天顶（太阳或月亮在天空中的最高点）

  • The sun rises, reaches its zenith and sets.

    太阳升起，达到最高点，然后落下。
