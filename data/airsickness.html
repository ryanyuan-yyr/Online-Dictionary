airsick
ɛərsɪk ˈɛəˌsɪk ˈɛrˌsɪk
(also air sick,air-sick)
1 ADJ 晕机的If you are airsick when you are travelling on an aircraft, you experience nausea as a result of the aircraft's motion. 晕机的

  • I was violently airsick all the way.

    一路上我都晕机晕得厉害。

2 N-UNCOUNT airsickness

  • A few suffered airsickness on the connecting flight.

    一些人在转接班机上晕机了。
