secret weapon
ˈsiːkrɪt ˈwɛpən
Word forms: plural secret weapons
N-COUNT 秘密武器;杀手锏 Someone's secret weapon is a thing or person which they believe will help them achieve something and which other people do not know about. 秘密武器;杀手锏

  • Discipline was the new coach's secret weapon.

    严格的训练是新任教练的秘密武器。
