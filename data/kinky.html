kinky
kɪŋki ˈkɪŋki
Word forms: comparative kinkier, superlative kinkiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (尤指性行为)变态的,反常的 If you describe something, usually a sexual practice or preference, as kinky, you mean that it is unusual and would be considered strange by most people. (尤指性行为)变态的,反常的 [informal]

  • He had been engaging in some kind of kinky sexual activity.

    他一直在进行一些变态的性活动。

  • ...kinky underwear.

    情趣内衣

SYN perverted, warped, deviant, unnatural  
2 ADJ-GRADED 卷曲的；缠绕的 Something that is kinky has a lot of curves or twists. 卷曲的；缠绕的

  • He had red kinky hair.

    他有一头红色的卷发。

SYN twisted, curled, curly, frizzy
