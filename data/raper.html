rape
reɪp reɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense rapes, present participle raping, past tense , past participle raped
1 VERB 强奸;强暴 If someone is raped, they are forced to have sex, usually by violence or threats of violence. 强奸;强暴

  • A young woman was brutally raped in her own home. [be VERB-ed]

    一名年轻女子在自己家中惨遭强暴。

SYN sexually assault, violate, abuse, ravish [literary]  
2 N-VAR 强奸罪 Rape is the crime of forcing someone to have sex. 强奸罪

  • Her party opposes abortion, except in cases of rape or incest.

    她的党派反对堕胎，但强奸或乱伦引起的怀孕除外。

  • Almost ninety per cent of all rapes and violent assaults went unreported.

    几乎90%的强奸案和暴力侵害案都没有报案。

SYN sexual assault, violation, ravishment, outrage  
3 N-SING 破坏;损坏 The rape of an area or of a country is the destruction or spoiling of it. 破坏;损坏 [literary]

  • As a result of the rape of the forests, parts of the country are now short of water. [+ of]

    由于森林遭到破坏，目前该国的部分地区水源短缺。

SYN plundering, pillage, depredation, despoliation [formal]
