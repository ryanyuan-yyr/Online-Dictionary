bog down
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）拖延；（使）停滞不前 If a plan or process bogs down or if something bogs it down, it is delayed and no progress is made. （使）拖延；（使）停滞不前

  • We intended from the very beginning to bog the prosecution down over who did this. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们从一开始就打算延缓对责任人提出诉讼。

  • The talks have bogged down over the issue of military reform. [VERB PREP.]

    谈判在军队改革问题上陷入了僵局。
