pessimism
pesɪmɪzəm ˈpɛsəˌmɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 悲观；悲观主义 Pessimism is the belief that bad things are going to happen. 悲观；悲观主义

  • ...universal pessimism about the economy. [+ about/over]

    对经济所持的普遍悲观态度

  • My first reaction was one of deep pessimism.

    我的第一反应非常悲观。

SYN gloominess, depression, despair, gloom
