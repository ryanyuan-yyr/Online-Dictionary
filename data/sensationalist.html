sensationalist
senseɪʃənəlɪst
ADJ-GRADED (新闻报道、电视广播节目)耸人听闻的，追求轰动效应的 Sensationalist news reports and television and radio programmes present the facts in a way that makes them seem worse or more shocking than they really are. (新闻报道、电视广播节目)耸人听闻的，追求轰动效应的 [disapproval]

  • ...sensationalist headlines.

    耸人听闻的新闻标题

  • The pictures were sensationalist and could seriously disturb younger readers.

    这些图片大肆渲染，会让一些比较年轻的读者们惶恐不安。
