fetching
fetʃɪŋ ˈfɛtʃɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 吸引人的;迷人的;引人注目的 If you describe someone or something as fetching, you think that they look very attractive. 吸引人的;迷人的;引人注目的

  • Sue was sitting up in bed, looking very fetching in a flowered bedjacket.

    休坐在床上，穿着印花宽松短睡衣，显得非常迷人。

  • Beckham wore a fetching outfit in purple and green.

    贝克汉姆穿了一件抢眼的紫绿两色套装。

SYN attractive, sweet, charming, enchanting
