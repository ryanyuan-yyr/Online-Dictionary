limelight
laɪmlaɪt ˈlaɪmˌlaɪt
N-UNCOUNT 公众注意的中心 If someone is in the limelight, a lot of attention is being paid to them, because they are famous or because they have done something very unusual or exciting. 公众注意的中心

  • Tony has now been thrust into the limelight, with a high-profile job.

    托尼现在一下子成了众人关注的中心，因为他有一份经常出镜、见报的工作。

SYN publicity, recognition, fame, the spotlight
