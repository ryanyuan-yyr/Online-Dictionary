vitamin
vɪtəmɪn ˈvɪtəmɪn vaɪt- ˈvaɪtəmɪn
Word forms: plural vitamins
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 维生素;维他命 Vitamins are substances that you need in order to remain healthy, which are found in food or can be eaten in the form of pills. 维生素;维他命

  • Butter, margarine, and oily fish are all good sources of vitamin D.

    黄油、人造黄油和多脂鱼都含有丰富的维生素D。

  • Healthy people do not need vitamin supplements.

    健康的人不需要额外补充维生素。
