demagogy
deməgɒdʒi ˈdɛməˌgɑdʒi ˈdɛməˌgɑgi ˈdɛməˌgoʊdʒi
(also demagoguery)
N-UNCOUNT （政治上的）蛊惑民心，煽动民情 You can refer to a method of political rule as demagogy if you disapprove of it because you think it involves appealing to people's emotions rather than using reasonable arguments. （政治上的）蛊惑民心，煽动民情 [disapproval]
