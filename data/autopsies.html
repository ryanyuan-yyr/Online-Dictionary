autopsy
ɔːtɒpsi ˈɔːtəpsɪ ˈɔˌtɑpsi ˈɔtəpsi
Word forms: plural autopsies
N-COUNT 尸体剖检;验尸 An autopsy is an examination of a dead body by a doctor who cuts it open in order to try to discover the cause of death. 尸体剖检;验尸

  • The autopsy report gave the cause of death as poisoning.

    验尸报告上显示死因为中毒。

SYN postmortem, dissection, postmortem examination, necropsy
