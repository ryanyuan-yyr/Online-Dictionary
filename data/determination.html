determination
dɪtɜːʳmɪneɪʃən diˌtɜrmɪˈneɪʃən dɪˌtɜrmɪˈneɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN to-infinitive] 决心;坚决;坚定;果断 Determination is the quality that you show when you have decided to do something and you will not let anything stop you. 决心;坚决;坚定;果断

  • Everyone concerned acted with great courage and determination.

    所有相关人员都表现出了极大的勇气和决心。

  • He reaffirmed their determination to tackle inflation.

    他重申了他们要解决通货膨胀问题的决心。

  • His cooking reflects a determination to acknowledge his northern roots.

    他的烹饪风格表明他决心承认自己根在北方的坚定决心。

  • There was an expression of fierce determination on her face.

    她的脸上流露出异常坚决的表情。

SYN resolution, purpose, resolve, drive
