well-behaved
ˌwɛlbiˈheɪvd
(also well behaved)
ADJ-GRADED 彬彬有礼的;(尤指儿童)规规矩矩的 If you describe someone, especially a child, as well-behaved, you mean that they behave in a way that adults generally like and think is correct. 彬彬有礼的;(尤指儿童)规规矩矩的

  • ...well-behaved little boys.

    乖巧的小男孩们

  • The troops have been remarkably well-behaved so far.

    迄今为止，这支军队作风端正。

SYN obedient, good, disciplined, restrained
