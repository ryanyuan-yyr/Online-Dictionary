throttle back
PHRASAL VERB 减速;调节油门使减速 If you throttle back, or you throttle back the engine, when driving a motor vehicle or flying an aircraft, you make it go slower by reducing the quantity of fuel entering the engine. 减速;调节油门使减速

  • The pilot throttles back slightly to maintain level flight. [VERB PREP.]

    飞行员稍稍减速，以保持水平飞行。

  • He stepped swiftly to the controls to throttle back the engine. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他快步来到控制台跟前，减小了油门。
