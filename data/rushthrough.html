rush through
PHRASAL VERB 加急处理;匆忙处理 If you rush something through, you deal with it quickly so that it is ready in a shorter time than usual. 加急处理;匆忙处理

  • The government rushed through legislation aimed at Mafia leaders. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政府匆忙通过了针对黑手党首领的立法。

  • They rushed the burial through so no evidence would show up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们仓促举行了葬礼，掩盖了所有证据。
