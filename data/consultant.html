consultant
kənsʌltənt kənˈsʌltənt
Word forms: plural consultants
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 高级顾问医师;会诊医师 A consultant is an experienced doctor with a high position, who specializes in one area of medicine. 高级顾问医师;会诊医师 [mainly British]

  • Shirley's brother is now a consultant heart surgeon in Sweden.

    雪利的兄弟现在在瑞典做心脏外科高级顾问医师。

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 specialist
2 N-COUNT 顾问 A consultant is a person who gives expert advice to a person or organization on a particular subject. 顾问

  • He was a consultant to the Swedish government.

    他是瑞典政府顾问。

  • ...a team of management consultants sent in to reorganise the department.

    派来对部门进行重组的管理顾问工作组

SYN specialist, adviser, counsellor, authority
