hit on
(also hit upon)
1 PHRASAL VERB 突然想出（主意或解决办法） If you hit on an idea or a solution to a problem, or hit upon it, you think of it. 突然想出（主意或解决办法）

  • After running through the numbers in every possible combination, we finally hit on a solution. [VERB PREP. noun]

    将这些数字进行各种可能的排列组合后，我们终于找到了答案。

2 PHRASAL VERB 对…进行性挑逗 If someone hits on you, they speak or behave in a way that shows they want to have a sexual relationship with you. 对…进行性挑逗 [informal]

  • She was hitting on me and I was surprised and flattered. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她在挑逗我，这让我感到既惊讶又受宠若惊。

SYN make a pass at, make an indecent proposal to, make an improper suggestion to
