paint
peɪnt peɪnt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense paints, present participle painting, past tense , past participle painted
1 N-MASS 油漆；涂料 Paint is a coloured liquid that you put onto a surface with a brush in order to protect the surface or to make it look nice, or that you use to produce a picture. 油漆；涂料

  • ...a pot of red paint.

    一罐红漆

  • They saw some large letters in white paint.

    他们看见了用白漆写的几个大大的字母。

  • ...water-based artist's paints.

    水性美术颜料

SYN colouring, colour, stain, dye  
2 N-SING （已干的）油漆层，涂层 On a wall or object, the paint is the covering of dried paint on it. （已干的）油漆层，涂层

  • The paint was peeling on the window frames.

    窗框上的油漆在剥落。

  • They'll probably scrape the paint off and make it look like a regular patrol car.

    他们可能会将油漆刮掉，使它看起来像一部普通的巡逻警车。

3 VERB 给…上油漆（或涂料） If you paint a wall or an object, you cover it with paint. 给…上油漆（或涂料） [Also VERB]

  • They started to mend the woodwork and paint the walls. [VERB noun]

    他们开始修补木建部分，粉刷墙壁。

  • I made a guitar and painted it red. [V n colour]

    我做了一把吉他，并把它漆成了红色。

  • ...painted furniture. [VERB-ed]

    上漆的家具

4 VERB （用颜料）绘，画 If you paint something or paint a picture of it, you produce a picture of it using paint. （用颜料）绘，画

  • He is painting a huge volcano. [VERB noun]

    他正在画一座巨大的火山。

  • Why do people paint pictures?. [VERB noun]

    人们为什么画画？

  • I had come here to paint. [VERB]

    我来这里作画。

5 VERB 用漆写（或画） When you paint a design or message on a surface, you put it on the surface using paint. 用漆写（或画）

  • ...a machine for painting white lines down roads. [VERB noun prep.]

    用漆在马路上划白线的机器

  • They went around painting rude slogans on cars. [VERB noun prep.]

    他们到处在汽车上涂写粗鲁的标语。

  • The recesses are decorated with gold stars, with smaller stars painted along the edges. [VERB-ed]

    那些壁龛用金色的星星作装饰，边缘则用漆绘着小一些的星星。

6 VERB 涂，抹（嘴唇、指甲等） If a woman paints her lips or nails, she puts a coloured cosmetic on them. 涂，抹（嘴唇、指甲等）

  • She propped the mirror against her handbag and began to paint her lips. [VERB noun]

    她把镜子靠在手包上支好，开始涂口红。

  • She painted her fingernails bright red. [V n colour]

    她把指甲涂成了亮红色。

7 VERB 描绘；描述 If you paint a grim or vivid picture of something, you give a description of it that is grim or vivid. 描绘；描述

  • The report paints a grim picture of life there. [VERB noun]

    那则报道把那里的生活描绘得很凄惨。

  • He went on to paint a rosy picture about how much has already been accomplished. [VERB noun]

    他继续描绘着已经取得多大成就的美好画卷。

SYN describe, capture, portray, depict
