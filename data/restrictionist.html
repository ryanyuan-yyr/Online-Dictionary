restriction
rɪstrɪkʃən rɪˈstrɪkʃən rɪˈstrɪkʃən
Word forms: plural restrictions
1 N-COUNT 限制;约束;规定 A restriction is an official rule that limits what you can do or that limits the amount or size of something. 限制;约束;规定

  • ...the lifting of restrictions on political parties and the news media. [+ on]

    取消对政治党派和新闻媒体的限制

  • The relaxation of travel restrictions means they are free to travel and work.

    旅游限制的放松意味着他们可以自由地出行和工作。

2 N-COUNT （对行动的）限定，约束，限制因素 You can refer to anything that limits what you can do as a restriction. （对行动的）限定，约束，限制因素

  • His parents are trying to make up to him for the restrictions of urban living. [+ of]

    他的父母正试图弥补他在城市生活的局限。

  • The second restriction upon the president's power is the limited time at his disposal.

    总统权力受到的第二项限制就是可以自由支配的时间有限。

3 See also: restrict
