pagan
peɪgən ˈpeɪgən
Word forms: plural pagans
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 异教（徒）的 Pagan beliefs and activities do not belong to any of the main religions of the world and take nature and a belief in many gods as a basis. They are older, or are believed to be older, than other religions. 异教（徒）的
SYN heathen, infidel, irreligious, polytheistic  
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 异教徒（旧时指不信奉基督教的人） In former times, pagans were people who did not believe in Christianity and who many Christians considered to be inferior people. 异教徒（旧时指不信奉基督教的人）

  • The new religion was eager to convert the pagan world.

    那种新宗教急于使异教徒皈依本教。

  • ...female saints who took vows of virginity rather than submit to an undesired marriage with a pagan.

    立誓独身也不愿屈服与异教徒结婚的女圣人

SYN heathen [old-fashioned] , infidel, unbeliever, polytheist
