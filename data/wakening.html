waken
weɪkən ˈweɪkən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense wakens, present participle wakening, past tense , past participle wakened
V-ERG 醒来;唤醒 When you waken, or when someone or something wakens you, you wake from sleep. 醒来;唤醒 [literary]

  • The noise of a door slamming wakened her. [VERB noun]

    砰的摔门声把她吵醒了。

  • Women are much more likely than men to waken because of noise. [VERB]

    女性通常比男性更易被噪音吵醒。

Waken up means the same as waken .waken up同waken

  • 'Drink this coffee — it will waken you up.'.

    “把这杯咖啡喝了—它会让你清醒过来。”

  • If you do waken up during the night, start the exercises again.

    如果你夜里真的醒了，就再从头开始做这套动作。
