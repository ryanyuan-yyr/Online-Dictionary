journalism
dʒɜːʳnəlɪzəm ˈdʒɜrnəlˌɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 新闻工作；新闻业 Journalism is the job of collecting news and writing about it for newspapers, magazines, television, or radio. 新闻工作；新闻业

  • He began a career in journalism, working for the North London Press Group.

    他开始从事新闻工作，为北伦敦报业集团工作。

  • It was an accomplished piece of investigative journalism.

    这是一个成功的调查研究报道。

SYN reporting, writing, reportage, article writing
