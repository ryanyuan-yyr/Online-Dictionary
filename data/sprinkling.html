sprinkling
sprɪŋklɪŋ ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ
N-SING (尤指分布在较大面积上的)少量，少许 A sprinkling of something is a small quantity or amount of it, especially if it is spread over a large area. (尤指分布在较大面积上的)少量，少许

  • ...a light sprinkling of snow. [+ of]

    薄薄的一层雪

  • Norway has a fair sprinkling of women ministers.

    挪威有好几位女部长。

SYN scattering, dusting, scatter, few
