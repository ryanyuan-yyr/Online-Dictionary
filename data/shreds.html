shred
ʃred ʃrɛd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shreds, present participle shredding, past tense , past participle shredded
1 VERB 切碎;撕碎 If you shred something such as food or paper, you cut it or tear it into very small, narrow pieces. 切碎;撕碎

  • They may be shredding documents. [VERB noun]

    他们可能是在粉碎文件。

  • Finely shred the carrots, cabbage and cored apples. [VERB noun]

    将胡萝卜、卷心菜切成细丝并给苹果去核。

SYN tear up, rip up, cut up, mince  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (撕或切成的)细条，碎片 If you cut or tear food or paper into shreds, you cut or tear it into small, narrow pieces. (撕或切成的)细条，碎片

  • Cut the cabbage into fine long shreds.

    将卷心菜切成又细又长的丝。

SYN tear up, rip up, cut up, mince  
3 N-COUNT 极少量;些许;一丁点儿 If there is not a shred of something, there is not even a small amount of it. 极少量;些许;一丁点儿

  • He said there was not a shred of evidence to support such remarks. [+ of]

    他说没有丝毫证据证明这样的说法。

  • There is not a shred of truth in the story. [+ of]

    这则报道中没有一句实话。

  • He was left without a shred of self-esteem.

    他的自尊荡然无存。

SYN particle, trace, scrap, grain  
4 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 把…批得体无完肤 If you tear someone to shreds or rip them to shreds, you criticize them very thoroughly and severely. 把…批得体无完肤 [emphasis]
