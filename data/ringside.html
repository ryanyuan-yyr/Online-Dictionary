ringside
rɪŋsaɪd ˈrɪŋˌsaɪd
1 N-SING (马戏台、拳击台或超越障碍比赛场地的)台边区，场边 The ringside is the area around the edge of a circus ring, boxing ring, or show jumping ring. (马戏台、拳击台或超越障碍比赛场地的)台边区，场边

  • Most of the top British trainers were at the ringside.

    英国最顶尖的教练大都坐在台边区。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （座位或视角）台边上的，台边前排的 If you have a ringside seat or a ringside view, you are close to an event and can see it clearly. （座位或视角）台边上的，台边前排的

  • I had a ringside seat for the whole performance.

    整场表演我都坐在场边前排座位上。
