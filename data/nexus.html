nexus
neksəs ˈnɛksəs
Word forms: plural nexus
N-COUNT 联系;关系 A nexus is a connection or series of connections within a particular situation or system. 联系;关系 [formal]

  • The Prayer Book has provided a flexible enough nexus of beliefs to hold together the different church parties.

    祈祷书为各信仰之间建起了足够灵活的联系纽带，以期将各个不同教派凝聚在一起。

  • ...the nexus between the dominant class and the State. [+ between]

    主导阶层和国家之间的关系

SYN connection, link, tie, bond
