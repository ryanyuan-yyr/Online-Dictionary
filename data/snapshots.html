snapshot
snæpʃɒt ˈsnæpˌʃɑt
Word forms: plural snapshots
1 N-COUNT 快照;抓拍的照片 A snapshot is a photograph that is taken quickly and casually. 快照;抓拍的照片
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 大致印象;简况 If something provides you with a snapshot of a place or situation, it gives you a brief idea of what that place or situation is like. 大致印象;简况

  • The interviews present a remarkable snapshot of Britain in these dark days of recession. [+ of]

    这些访谈生动地展现了英国在黑暗的萧条时期的概貌。
