review
rɪvjuː rɪˈvjuː rɪˈvju ˈriˈvju
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense reviews, present participle reviewing, past tense , past participle reviewed
1 N-COUNT 检查;审查;审核 A review of a situation or system is its formal examination by people in authority. This is usually done in order to see whether it can be improved or corrected. 检查;审查;审核

  • The president ordered a review of US economic aid to Jordan.

    总统下令对美国向约旦提供的经济援助进行审查。

  • The White House quickly announced that the policy is under review.

    白宫很快宣布该项政策正在审核中。

2 VERB 仔细研究;审视;细查;详查 If you review a situation or system, you consider it carefully to see what is wrong with it or how it could be improved. 仔细研究;审视;细查;详查

  • The Prime Minister reviewed the situation with his Cabinet yesterday. [VERB noun]

    昨天，首相和他的内阁对局势进行了审度。

  • The next day we reviewed the previous day's work. [VERB noun]

    第二天，我们仔细检查了头一天的工作。

SYN reconsider, revise, rethink, run over  
3 N-COUNT （对书、电影等的）评论文章;书评;影评 A review is a report in the media in which someone gives their opinion of something such as a new book or film. （对书、电影等的）评论文章;书评;影评

  • Disney's 'Beauty And The Beast' has won rave reviews.

    迪斯尼出品的电影《美女与野兽》赢得了众多好评。

  • We've never had a good review in the music press.

    我们从未在音乐媒体获得过好评。

SYN critique, commentary, evaluation, critical assessment  
4 VERB 写（新书、新上映电影等）的评论 If someone reviews something such as a new book or film, they write a report or give a talk on television or radio in which they express their opinion of it. 写（新书、新上映电影等）的评论

  • Richard Coles reviews all of the latest video releases. [VERB noun]

    理查德·科尔斯为所有最新发行的录影带都写了评论。

  • His book about Afghanistan is reviewed here by Anthony Hyman. [VERB noun]

    安东尼·海曼在此就他那本关于阿富汗的书写了书评。

5 VERB 检阅(军队)；阅（兵） When a military or political leader reviews troops, they examine them, or watch them marching. 检阅(军队)；阅（兵）

  • When he reviewed the troops they cheered him as he smiled and raised his hat. [VERB noun]

    他检阅军队时微笑着举起帽子，人们与此同时都向他欢呼。

6 VERB （为备考）复习，温习 When you review for an examination, you read things again and make notes in order to be prepared for the examination. （为备考）复习，温习 [US] [Also VERB]

  • Reviewing for exams gives you a chance to bring together all the individual parts of the course. [VERB + for]

    考前复习让你有机会把一门课程所有零碎的知识都融会贯通。

  • Review all the notes you need to cover for each course. [VERB noun]

    把每一门课程需要掌握的笔记都复习一下。

SYN look back on, remember, recall, reflect on  
Review is also a noun.

  • Begin by planning on three two-hour reviews with four chapters per session.

    先安排3次温习时间，每次用2个小时复习4个章节。
