unaided
ʌneɪdɪd ʌnˈeɪdɪd
ADJ [usu ADJ after v, oft ADJ n] 无外援的；独立的 If you do something unaided, you do it without help from anyone or anything else. 无外援的；独立的

  • There have been at least thirteen previous attempts to reach the North Pole unaided.

    之前至少已经有过13次想独立到达北极的尝试。

  • She brought us up completely unaided.

    完全是她一手把我们拉扯大的。

  • ...the smallest speck of matter visible to the unaided eye.

    肉眼可见的最小的颗粒

SYN alone, solo, single-handed, unaccompanied
