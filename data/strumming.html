strum
strʌm strʌm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense strums, present participle strumming, past tense , past participle strummed
VERB 弹奏，拨弄(吉他等弦乐器) If you strum a stringed instrument such as a guitar, you play it by moving your fingers backwards and forwards across the strings. 弹奏，拨弄(吉他等弦乐器)

  • In the corner, one youth sat alone, softly strumming a guitar. [VERB noun]

    一个青年独自坐在角落里，轻轻地弹着吉他。

  • Vaska strummed away on his guitar. [VERB prep./adv.]

    瓦斯卡弹拨着自己的吉他。

SYN pluck, twang, thrum, strike  
Strum is also a noun.

  • A little while later, I heard the strum of my father's guitar as he began to sing.

    过了一会，我听见父亲边唱歌边弹起了吉他。
