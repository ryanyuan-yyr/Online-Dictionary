crowd out
PHRASAL VERB 把…排挤在外 If one thing crowds out another, it is so successful or common that the other thing does not have the opportunity to be successful or exist. 把…排挤在外 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • In the 1980s American exports crowded out European films. [VERB PREP. noun]

    20世纪80年代美国出口影片把欧洲影片挤出了市场。
