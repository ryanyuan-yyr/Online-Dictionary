mismanagement
mɪsmænɪdʒmənt
N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 处置不当;管理不善 Some-one's mismanagement of a system or organization is the bad way they have dealt with it or organized it. 处置不当;管理不善

  • ...the Government's economic mismanagement.

    政府对经济的管理不当

  • His gross mismanagement left the company desperately in need of restructuring.

    他管理上的重大失误使公司陷入亟须重组的境地。

SYN maladministration, inefficiency, mishandling, misdirection
