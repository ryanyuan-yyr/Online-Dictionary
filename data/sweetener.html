sweetener
swiːtənəʳ ˈswiːtənə ˈswitənər
Word forms: plural sweeteners
1 N-MASS (人造的)甜味剂 Sweetener is an artificial substance that can be used in drinks instead of sugar. (人造的)甜味剂
2 N-COUNT 好处;用以拉拢人的钱物;贿赂 A sweetener is something that you give or offer someone in order to persuade them to accept an offer or business deal. 好处;用以拉拢人的钱物;贿赂
