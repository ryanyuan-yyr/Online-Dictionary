archetypal
ɑːʳkɪtaɪpəl ˌɑːkɪˈtaɪpəl ˌɑːkɪˈtɪpɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 典型的;有代表性的 Someone or something that is archetypal has all the most important characteristics of a particular kind of person or thing and is a perfect example of it. 典型的;有代表性的 [formal]

  • Cricket is the archetypal English game.

    板球是典型的英格兰运动。

SYN typical, standard, model, original
