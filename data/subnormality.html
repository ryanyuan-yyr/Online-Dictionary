subnormal
sʌbnɔːʳməl sʌbˈnɔːməl sʌbˈnɔrməl
(also sub-normal)
ADJ 低能的;弱智的 If someone is subnormal, they have less ability or intelligence than a normal person of their age. 低能的;弱智的 [old-fashioned]

  • ...educationally subnormal children.

    学习能力低下的儿童

The subnormal are people who are subnormal.弱智者;低能人

  • She attended a school for the educationally subnormal.

    她在一所智障学校上学。
