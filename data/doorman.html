doorman
dɔːʳmən ˈdɔːˌmæn ˈdɔrˌmæn dɔrmən
Word forms: plural doormen
1 N-COUNT (俱乐部等的)门卫，看门人 A doorman is a man who stands at the door of a club, prevents unwanted people from coming in, and makes people leave if they cause trouble. (俱乐部等的)门卫，看门人

  • Des, 18, was working as a club doorman.

    德，18 岁，是一家俱乐部的门卫。

2 N-COUNT 门厅侍者;门卫 A doorman is a person whose job is to stay by the main entrance of a large building, and help people visiting the building. 门厅侍者;门卫
