hyper
haɪpəʳ ˈhaɪpər
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 亢奋的;精力旺盛的 If someone is hyper, they are very excited and energetic. 亢奋的;精力旺盛的 [informal]

  • I was incredibly hyper. I couldn't sleep.

    我太亢奋了，根本无法入睡。

hyper- haɪpəʳ- ˈhaɪpər
PREFIX （用于构成形容词和名词，描述人的特性）表示“高度”，“过于” Hyper- is used to form adjectives and nouns that describe someone as having a lot or too much of a particular quality. （用于构成形容词和名词，描述人的特性）表示“高度”，“过于”

  • I hated my father. He was hyper-critical and mean.

    我讨厌我父亲。他吹毛求疵，刻薄无情。

  • He is one of those lean, hyper-fit people.

    他属于那种瘦削、过于健美的人。
