prime mover
Word forms: plural prime movers
N-COUNT 发起人；倡导者；主导者 The prime mover behind a plan, idea, or situation is someone who has an important influence in starting it. 发起人；倡导者；主导者

  • He was the prime mover behind the coup. [+ behind/in]

    他是这次政变的幕后指使人。

  • He has been named as the prime mover in the conspiracy to murder Carroll.

    他已被公布是阴谋杀害卡罗尔的主谋。
