fist
fɪst fɪst
Word forms: plural fists
1 N-COUNT 拳;拳头 Your hand is referred to as your fist when you have bent your fingers in towards the palm in order to hit someone, to make an angry gesture, or to hold something. 拳;拳头

  • Angry protestors with clenched fists shouted their defiance.

    愤怒的抗议者们紧握拳头大声抗议。

  • Gary clutched a penny in his fist.

    加里将一枚便士攥在手中。

2 PHRASE [usu PHR n] (政策或方法)铁拳的，残酷无情的，强硬的 An iron fist policy or approach is one which deals with people and situations in a very strict and cruel way. (政策或方法)铁拳的，残酷无情的，强硬的

  • The iron-fist policy towards the fundamentalists is unlikely to be interrupted.

    对原教旨主义者采取的铁拳政策不可能中止。

3 hand over fist→see: hand
