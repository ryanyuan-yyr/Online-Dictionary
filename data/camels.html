camel
kæməl ˈkæməl
Word forms: plural camels
1 N-COUNT 骆驼 A camel is a large animal that lives in deserts and is used for carrying goods and people. Camels have long necks and one or two lumps on their backs called humps. 骆驼
2 the straw that broke the camel's back →see: straw
