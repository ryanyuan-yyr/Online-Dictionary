brainpower
breɪnpaʊəʳ ˈbreɪnˌpaʊər
1 N-UNCOUNT 脑力；智能；智力 Brainpower is intelligence or the ability to think. 脑力；智能；智力 [journalism]

  • She admired Robert's brainpower.

    她羡慕罗伯特的聪明睿智。

2 N-UNCOUNT （某组织或国家的）人才队伍，人才 You can refer to the intelligent people in an organization or country as its brainpower . （某组织或国家的）人才队伍，人才 [journalism]

  • A country's principal resource is its brainpower.

    一个国家的首要资源是其人才资源。
