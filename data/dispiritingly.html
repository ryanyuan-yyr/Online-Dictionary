dispiriting
dɪspɪrɪtɪŋ dɪˈspɪrɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人沮丧的;令人气馁的;令人心灰意懒的 Something that is dispiriting causes you to lose your enthusiasm and excitement. 令人沮丧的;令人气馁的;令人心灰意懒的

  • It's very dispiriting for anyone to be out of a job.

    失业对每一个人来说都很懊丧。

SYN disheartening, disappointing, depressing, crushing
