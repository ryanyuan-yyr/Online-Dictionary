maturation
mætjʊreɪʃən ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT (葡萄酒、奶酪等的)酿熟过程 The maturation of something such as wine or cheese is the process of its being left for a time to become mature. (葡萄酒、奶酪等的)酿熟过程 [formal]

  • The period of maturation is determined by the cellar master.

    酿熟期的长短由地窖的主人决定。

2 N-UNCOUNT (年轻人)长大成人，发育成熟 The maturation of a young person's body is the process of it becoming like an adult's. (年轻人)长大成人，发育成熟 [formal]
