espouse
ɪspaʊz ɪˈspaʊz ɛˈspaʊz ɪˈspaʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense espouses, present participle espousing, past tense , past participle espoused
VERB 支持;拥护 If you espouse a particular policy, cause, or belief, you become very interested in it and give your support to it. 支持;拥护 [formal]

  • She ran away with him to Mexico and espoused the revolutionary cause. [VERB noun]

    她跟着他逃到墨西哥，并支持革命事业。

SYN support, back, champion, promote
