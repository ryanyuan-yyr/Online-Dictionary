peachy
piːtʃi ˈpitʃi
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 桃子味的；桃子颜色的 If you describe something as peachy, you mean that it tastes or smells like a peach or is similar in colour to a peach. 桃子味的；桃子颜色的

  • ...a rich, peachy dessert wine.

    带醇厚桃味的餐后甜酒

  • ...peachy pink.

    桃红色

2 ADJ-GRADED 非常好的；极棒的 If you say that something is peachy or peachy keen, you mean that it is very nice. 非常好的；极棒的 [US, informal]

  • Everything in her life is just peachy.

    她生活中的一切都非常棒。
