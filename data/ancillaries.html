ancillary
ænsɪləri ænsəleri ˈænsəˌlɛri ænˈsɪləri
Word forms: plural ancillaries
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 辅助的；补助的；补充的 The ancillary workers in an institution are the people such as cleaners and cooks whose work supports the main work of the institution. 辅助的；补助的；补充的

  • ...ancillary staff.

    后勤人员

  • ...ancillary services like cleaning.

    清洁等后勤工作

Ancillary is also a noun.

  • ...ancillaries who look after the children in the playground.

    在操场上看护孩子们的后勤人员

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 附属的；附带的 Ancillary means additional to something else. 附属的；附带的 [formal]

  • Ancillary charges are at least $30 per day.

    附加收费每天至少30美元。

  • Scientific development meant the growth of numerous professions ancillary to medicine.

    科技发展意味着许多医学衍生职业的增长。

Ancillary is also a noun.

  • Ancillaries can force the bill up rather alarmingly.

    附加项目能使账单费用出现惊人的增长。
