divinity
dɪvɪnɪti dəˈvɪnəti
Word forms: plural divinities
1 N-UNCOUNT 神学 Divinity is the study of religion. 神学

  • He entered Otago University to study arts and divinity.

    他进入奥特哥大学学习艺术和神学。

SYN theology, religion, religious studies  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 神性;神圣性 Divinity is the quality of being divine. 神性;神圣性

  • ...a lasting faith in the divinity of Christ's word.

    对基督话语的神圣性的持久信仰

SYN godliness, holiness, sanctity, godhead  
3 N-COUNT 神;女神 A divinity is a god or goddess. 神;女神

  • The three statues above are probably Roman divinities.

    上面的3尊雕像可能是罗马诸神。

SYN deity, spirit, genius, guardian spirit
