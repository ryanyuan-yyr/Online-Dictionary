rebuttal
rɪbʌtəl rɪˈbʌtəl
Word forms: plural rebuttals
N-COUNT 辩驳；反驳；回击 If you make a rebuttal of a charge or accusation that has been made against you, you make a statement which gives reasons why the accusation is untrue. 辩驳；反驳；回击 [formal]

  • He is conducting a point-by-point rebuttal of charges from former colleagues. [+ of/to]

    他正在对前同事的指责进行逐条反驳。

  • Pakistan has still not issued an official rebuttal to the latest Indian statements.

    巴基斯坦仍然没有对最近印度发表的声明进行官方驳斥。

SYN disproof, negation, refutation, invalidation
