trade down
PHRASAL VERB 变卖(旧物)换进同类中较廉价的东西 If someone trades down, they sell something such as their car or house and buy a less expensive one. 变卖(旧物)换进同类中较廉价的东西

  • They are selling their five-bedroom house and trading down to a two-bedroom cottage. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他们要把 5 居室的房子卖掉，换一所较便宜的 2 居室小屋。
