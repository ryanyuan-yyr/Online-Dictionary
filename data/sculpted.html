sculpt
skʌlpt skʌlpt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sculpts, present participle sculpting, past tense , past participle sculpted
1 VERB 雕刻;雕塑 When an artist sculpts something, they carve or shape it out of a material such as stone or clay. 雕刻;雕塑

  • An artist sculpted a full-size replica of her head. [VERB noun]

    一位艺术家按实际大小雕刻了一个她的头部模型。

  • When I sculpt, my style is expressionistic. [VERB]

    我的雕刻风格是表现主义。

2 VERB 使成形 If something is sculpted, it is made into a particular shape. 使成形

  • More familiar landscapes have been sculpted by surface erosion. [be VERB-ed]

    还有更多熟悉的地貌是因地表侵蚀形成的。

  • Michael smoothed and sculpted Jane's hair into shape. [VERB noun + into]

    迈克尔把简的头发梳理平整并做出发型。
