fig leaf
Word forms: plural fig leaves
1 N-COUNT 无花果树叶(在绘画和雕塑中有时被用作遮羞物) A fig leaf is a large leaf which comes from the fig tree. A fig leaf is sometimes used in painting and sculpture to cover the genitals of a naked body. 无花果树叶(在绘画和雕塑中有时被用作遮羞物)
2 N-COUNT 遮羞布 People sometimes refer disapprovingly to something which is intended to hide or prevent an embarrassing situation as a fig leaf . 遮羞布 [journalism, disapproval]

  • This deal is little more than a fig leaf for the continued destruction of the landscape.

    这项协议不过是块遮羞布而已，用来掩盖对自然景观的持续破坏。
