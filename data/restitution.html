restitution
restɪtjuːʃən -tuː- ˌrɛstəˈtuʃən ˌrɛstəˈtjuʃən
N-UNCOUNT 归还;赔偿;偿还 Restitution is the act of giving back to a person something that was lost or stolen, or of paying them money for the loss. 归还;赔偿;偿还 [formal]

  • The victims are demanding full restitution.

    受害人要求全额赔偿。

  • We have asked that they rehire the people that were fired and that they make restitution to them.

    我们已经要求他们重新雇用那些被开除的人，并对他们进行补偿。

SYN compensation, satisfaction, amends, refund
