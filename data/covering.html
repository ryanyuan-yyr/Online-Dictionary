N-COUNT (用来保护或隐藏的)覆盖物，遮盖物 A covering is a layer of something that protects or hides something else. (用来保护或隐藏的)覆盖物，遮盖物

  • Leave a thin covering of fat. [+ of]

    留下薄薄一层脂肪。

  • Sawdust was used as a hygienic floor covering.

    锯屑曾被用来铺在地上保持地面清洁卫生。

SYN cover, protection, coating, overlay
