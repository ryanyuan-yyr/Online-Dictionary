bisect
baɪsekt baɪˈsɛkt ˈbaɪˌsɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bisects, present participle bisecting, past tense , past participle bisected
VERB 将…一分为二；把…分为两部分 If something long and thin bisects an area or line, it divides the area or line in half. 将…一分为二；把…分为两部分

  • The main street bisects the town from end to end. [VERB noun]

    这条主干道把整个小镇一分为二。

SYN cut in two, cross, separate, split
