trashy
træʃi ˈtræʃi
Word forms: comparative trashier, superlative trashiest
ADJ-GRADED 质量低劣的;蹩脚的;廉价的 If you describe something as trashy, you think it is of very poor quality. 质量低劣的;蹩脚的;廉价的 [informal, disapproval]

  • I was reading some trashy romance novel.

    我正在读一本写得很烂的言情小说。

SYN worthless, cheap, inferior, shabby
