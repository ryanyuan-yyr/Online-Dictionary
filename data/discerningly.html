discerning
dɪsɜːʳnɪŋ dɪˈzɜrnɪŋ dɪsˈsɜrnɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 有识别力的;有眼力的;有洞察力的 If you describe someone as discerning, you mean that they are able to judge which things of a particular kind are good and which are bad. 有识别力的;有眼力的;有洞察力的 [approval]

  • ...tailor-made holidays to suit the more discerning traveller.

    为懂行的旅游者量身打造的假期

  • Her childhood passion for collecting has not dimmed, but now she is more discerning.

    她孩提时培养起的对收藏的痴迷丝毫未减，现在只是变得更有眼力了。

SYN discriminating, knowing, sharp, critical
