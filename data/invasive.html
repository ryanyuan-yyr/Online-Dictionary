invasive
ɪnveɪsɪv ɪnˈveɪsɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 侵袭的;扩散的 You use invasive to describe something undesirable which spreads very quickly and which is very difficult to stop from spreading. 侵袭的;扩散的

  • They found invasive cancer during a routine examination.

    他们在常规体检中发现了扩散性的癌变。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 开刀的;手术的 An invasive medical procedure involves operating on a patient or examining the inside of their body. 开刀的;手术的

  • Many people find the idea of any kind of invasive surgery unbearable.

    很多人认为任何开刀手术都不堪忍受。
