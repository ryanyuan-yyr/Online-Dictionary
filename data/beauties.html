beauty
bjuːti ˈbjuti
Word forms: plural beauties
1 N-UNCOUNT 美丽；漂亮 Beauty is the state or quality of being beautiful. 美丽；漂亮

  • ...an area of outstanding natural beauty.

    自然美景美不胜收的景区

  • Everyone admired her elegance and her beauty.

    人人都羡慕她的优雅和美丽。

SYN attractiveness, appeal, charm, grace  
2 N-COUNT 美女；美人 A beauty is a beautiful woman. 美女；美人 [journalism]

  • She is known as a great beauty.

    她是出了名的大美女。

SYN good-looker, looker [informal , mainly US] , lovely [slang] , sensation  
3 N-COUNT 极好的事物 You can say that something is a beauty when you think it is very good. 极好的事物 [informal]

  • The pass was a real beauty, but the shot was poor.

    传得非常漂亮，但是临门一脚太臭了。

4 N-COUNT [usually plural] 魅力；美好；吸引人之处 The beauties of something are its attractive qualities or features. 魅力；美好；吸引人之处 [literary]

  • He was beginning to enjoy the beauties of nature.

    他开始享受大自然之美。

5 ADJ [ADJ n] 美容的 Beauty is used to describe people, products, and activities that are concerned with making women look beautiful. 美容的

  • Additional beauty treatments can be booked in advance.

    额外的美容服务可以提前预订。

6 N-COUNT 好处；妙处；优点 If you say that a particular feature is the beauty of something, you mean that this feature is what makes the thing so good. 好处；妙处；优点

  • There would be no effect on animals—that's the beauty of such water-based materials.

    对动物毫无影响—水基材料妙就妙在这儿。

SYN advantage, good, use, benefit
