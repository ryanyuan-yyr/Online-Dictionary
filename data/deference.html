deference
defrəns ˈdɛfərəns
N-UNCOUNT （尤指对位重者的）敬重，尊敬，遵从 Deference is a polite and respectful attitude towards someone, especially because they have an important position. （尤指对位重者的）敬重，尊敬，遵从 [Also + to]

  • The old sense of deference and restraint in royal reporting has vanished.

    在对皇室进行的新闻报道中，那种由来已久的尊重和收敛意识已经消失了。

  • Out of deference to him, I lowered my head as he prayed.

    出于对他的敬重，在他祈祷时我低下了头。
