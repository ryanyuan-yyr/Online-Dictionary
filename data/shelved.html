shelve
ʃelv ʃɛlv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shelves, present participle shelving, past tense , past participle shelved
1 VERB 搁置，停止执行，暂不进行(计划、项目) If someone shelves a plan or project, they decide not to continue with it, either for a while or permanently. 搁置，停止执行，暂不进行(计划、项目)

  • Atlanta has shelved plans to include golf in the 1996 Games. [VERB noun]

    亚特兰大已搁置了将高尔夫球纳入 1996 年奥运会的计划。

  • Sadly, the project has now been shelved. [be VERB-ed]

    不幸的是，计划现在已经搁浅。

SYN postpone, put off, defer, table [US]  
2 VERB (近海或海底陆地)向下倾斜,成斜坡 If an area of ground next to or under the sea shelves, it slopes downwards. (近海或海底陆地)向下倾斜,成斜坡

  • The shoreline shelves away steeply. [VERB adv./prep.]

    海岸线顺势陡直而下。

  • ...a gently shelving beach. [VERB-ing]

    坡度平缓的海滩

SYN fall, drop, plunge, descend  
3 (shelf 的复数) Shelves is the plural of shelf . (shelf 的复数)
