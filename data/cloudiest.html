cloudy
klaʊdi ˈklaʊdi
Word forms: comparative cloudier, superlative cloudiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 多云的;阴天的;阴云密布的 If it is cloudy, there are a lot of clouds in the sky. 多云的;阴天的;阴云密布的

  • ...a windy, cloudy day.

    多云的大风天

SYN dull, dark, dim, gloomy  
2 ADJ-GRADED (液体)浑浊的，不清澈的 A cloudy liquid is less clear than it should be. (液体)浑浊的，不清澈的
3 ADJ-GRADED (想法、观点或问题)模糊不清的，含糊的 Ideas, opinions, or issues that are cloudy are confused or uncertain. (想法、观点或问题)模糊不清的，含糊的

  • ...an absurdly cloudy political debate.

    极为含糊不清的政治辩论

  • The legal position is very cloudy.

    法律对此的态度十分模糊。

SYN vague, confused, obscure, blurred
