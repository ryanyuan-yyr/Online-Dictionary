upbraid
ʌpbreɪd ʌpˈbreɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense upbraids, present participle upbraiding, past tense , past participle upbraided
VERB 责难；非难；责骂 If you upbraid someone, you tell them that they have done something wrong and criticize them for doing it. 责难；非难；责骂 [formal]

  • His mother summoned him, upbraided him, wept and prayed. [VERB noun]

    他母亲把他叫到跟前训斥了一番，又哭哭啼啼地向上帝祷告。

  • His wife set about upbraiding him for neglecting the children. [VERB noun + for]

    他妻子开始指责他不照顾孩子。

SYN scold, rebuke, reprimand, blame
