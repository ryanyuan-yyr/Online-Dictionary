sold out
səʊld aʊt
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] (演出、比赛、娱乐节目等的门票)售罄的,销售一空的 If a performance, sports event, or other entertainment is sold out, all the tickets for it have been sold. (演出、比赛、娱乐节目等的门票)售罄的,销售一空的

  • The premiere on Monday is sold out.

    周一首映式的门票已经全部售完。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] (商店)卖断货的 If a shop is sold out of something, it has sold all of it that it had. (商店)卖断货的

  • The stores are sometimes sold out of certain groceries. [+ of]

    有时商店里的某些杂货会有脱销的现象。
