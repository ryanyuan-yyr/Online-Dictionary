gob
gɒb gɑb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense gobs, present participle gobbing, past tense , past participle gobbed
1 N-COUNT 嘴 A person's gob is their mouth. 嘴 [British, informal, rude]

  • Shut your gob.

    闭上你的嘴。

2 N-COUNT （黏性液体的）少许 A gob of a thick, unpleasant liquid is a small amount of it. （黏性液体的）少许 [informal]

  • ...a gob of spit. [+ of]

    一口唾沫

3 VERB 吐，啐（唾沫） If someone gobs, they spit . 吐，啐（唾沫） [British, informal] [Also VERB]

  • At a concert in Leeds, some punks gobbed at them and threw beer cans. [VERB prep.]

    在利兹举办的一场演唱会上，一些小流氓朝他们吐口水，扔啤酒罐。

4 N-COUNT 一块；一团 A gob of something is a lump of it. 一块；一团 [US]

  • ...a gob of ice.

    一块冰

SYN piece, lump, chunk, hunk  
5 N-PLURAL 大量 Gobs of something means a lot of it. 大量 [US, informal]

  • We're getting input from gobs of sources.

    我们从多方渠道得到信息。
