light-hearted
1 ADJ-GRADED 轻松愉快的；无忧无虑的 Someone who is light-hearted is cheerful and happy. 轻松愉快的；无忧无虑的

  • They were light-hearted and prepared to enjoy life.

    他们无忧无虑，随时准备享受生活。

SYN carefree, happy, bright, glad  
2 ADJ-GRADED 不严肃的；随便的；为了好玩的 Something that is light-hearted is intended to be entertaining or amusing, and not at all serious. 不严肃的；随便的；为了好玩的

  • There have been many attempts, both light-hearted and serious, to locate the Loch Ness Monster.

    有很多人或随意或认真地尝试过，想要找到尼斯湖水怪。
