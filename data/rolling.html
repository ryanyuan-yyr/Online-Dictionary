rolling
roʊlɪŋ ˈroʊlɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （山丘）起伏的，绵延的 Rolling hills are small hills with gentle slopes that extend a long way into the distance. （山丘）起伏的，绵延的

  • ...the rolling countryside of south western France.

    法国西南部绵延起伏的乡村

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (走路)左右摇晃的，摇摆的 If someone has a rolling walk, they move from side to side as they walk. (走路)左右摇晃的，摇摆的

  • Burns is a big lad with a rolling gait.

    伯恩斯是一个走起路来一摇一晃的高大小伙子。

3 PHRASE 非常有钱;十分富有 If you say that someone is rolling in it or is rolling in money, you mean that they are very rich. 非常有钱;十分富有 [informal, emphasis]
