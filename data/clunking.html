clunk
klʌŋk klʌŋk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural clunks, present participle clunking, past tense , past participle clunked
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] (重物相撞的)哐的一声，哐啷声 A clunk is a sound made by a heavy object hitting something hard. (重物相撞的)哐的一声，哐啷声

  • Something fell to the floor with a clunk.

    不知什么东西哐啷一声掉在地上。

2 VERB 哐啷一声撞上 If a heavy object clunks on or against something, it hits it and makes a dull sound. 哐啷一声撞上

  • His feet clunked on the wooden steps. [VERB prep.]

    他的脚噔噔噔地踩在木头台阶上。

  • ...a slight clunking noise. [VERB-ing]

    哐啷哐啷轻微的撞击声
