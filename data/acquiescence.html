acquiescence
ækwiesəns ˌækwiˈɛsəns
N-UNCOUNT 同意；默认；默许；顺从 Acquiescence is agreement to do what someone wants, or acceptance of what they do even though you do not agree with it. 同意；默认；默许；顺从 [formal]

  • Deirdre smiled her acquiescence.

    戴尔德丽微笑着表示同意。

  • Fear of rapid social change made temporary acquiescence in slavery tolerable in the South. [+ in/to]

    对飞速的社会变革的恐惧使得南部对奴隶制暂时的默许变得可以忍受。

SYN agreement, yielding, approval, acceptance
