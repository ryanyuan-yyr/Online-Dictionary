wooden spoon
Word forms: plural wooden spoons
1 N-COUNT 木匙;木勺 A wooden spoon is a spoon that is used for stirring sauces and for mixing ingredients in cooking. It is made of wood and has a long handle. 木匙;木勺
2 N-COUNT 末名;最后一名;倒数第一 If someone gets the wooden spoon, they come last in a race or competition. 末名;最后一名;倒数第一 [British]
