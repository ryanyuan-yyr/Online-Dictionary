uninvited
ʌnɪnvaɪtɪd ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪd
ADJ [ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ, ADJ n] 未经邀请的；不请自到的；未经要求的 If someone does something or goes somewhere uninvited, they do it or go there without being asked, often when their action or presence is not wanted. 未经邀请的；不请自到的；未经要求的

  • He came uninvited to one of Stein's parties.

    在斯坦举办的一个聚会上他不请自到。

  • ...a hundred invited guests and many more who were uninvited.

    100个受邀嘉宾和更多没被邀请的客人

  • ...an uninvited question from a reporter.

    记者突如其来的问题

SYN unasked, unbidden, unwanted, not asked
