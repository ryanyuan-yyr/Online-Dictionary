recommend
rekəmend ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recommends, present participle recommending, past tense , past participle recommended
1 VERB 推荐;介绍 If someone recommends a person or thing to you, they suggest that you would find that person or thing good or useful. 推荐;介绍 [Also VERB n + as]

  • I have just spent a holiday there and would recommend it to anyone. [V n + to/for]

    我刚刚在那里度过假，很想把它推荐给每一个人。

  • 'You're a good worker, boy,' he told him. 'I'll recommend you for a promotion.'. [V n + for/as]

    “你是个好员工，孩子，”他告诉他，“我将推荐你升职。”

  • Ask your doctor to recommend a suitable therapist. [VERB noun]

    让你的医生给你推荐一个合适的治疗专家。

SYN advocate, suggest, propose, approve  
recommended

  • Though ten years old, this book is highly recommended.

    尽管是10年前出版的，这本书仍值得大力推荐。

2 VERB 建议;劝说（做…) If you recommend that something is done, you suggest that it should be done. 建议;劝说（做…) [Also VERB n to-infinitive]

  • The judge recommended that he serve 20 years in prison. [VERB that]

    法官建议判处他20年监禁。

  • We strongly recommend reporting the incident to the police. [VERB noun/verb-ing]

    我们强烈建议报警。

  • It is recommended that you should consult your doctor. [it be VERB-ed that]

    建议你去咨询医生。

  • The recommended daily dose is 12 to 24 grams. [VERB-ed]

    建议每天的服用剂量为12到24克。

  • Many financial planners now recommend against ever fully paying off your home loan. [VERB + against]

    现在许多理财专家不建议人们把住房贷款全部还清。

SYN advise, suggest, advance, propose  
3 VERB 使受欢迎;使有优势 If something or someone has a particular quality to recommend them, that quality makes them attractive or gives them an advantage over similar things or people. 使受欢迎;使有优势

  • La Noblesse restaurant has much to recommend it.

    “贵族”餐馆有很多值得称道之处。

  • He had little but his enthusiasm to recommend him. [VERB noun]

    他拥有的优势只有他的热情。

  • These qualities recommended him to Olivier. [VERB noun + to]

    这些品质让他受到奥利维尔的青睐。

SYN make attractive, make interesting, make appealing, make acceptable  
Usage Note :

  • Be careful with the spelling of this word.

    注意该词的拼写。
