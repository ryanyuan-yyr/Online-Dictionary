loll
lɒl lɑl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lolls, present participle lolling, past tense , past participle lolled
1 VERB 懒洋洋地坐（或躺） If you loll somewhere, you sit or lie in a very relaxed position. 懒洋洋地坐（或躺）

  • He was lolling on the sofa in the shadows near the fire. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他懒洋洋地躺在炉火边一个背光的沙发上。

  • He lolled back in his comfortable chair. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他懒洋洋地靠在舒适的椅子上。

SYN lounge, relax, lean, slump  
2 VERB （尤指头或舌）耷拉，垂下 If something fairly heavy, especially someone's head or tongue, lolls, it hangs down in a loose, uncontrolled way. （尤指头或舌）耷拉，垂下

  • When he let go the head lolled sideways. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他一放手，脑袋便垂向一侧。

  • His tongue was lolling out of the side of his mouth. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他的舌头从嘴角耷拉出来。

  • Tongue lolling, the dog came lolloping back from the forest. [VERB]

    那只狗伸着舌头从树林里蹒跚地跑回来。

SYN droop, drop, hang, flop  
Phrasal verbs:
loll around
