wet dream
Word forms: plural wet dreams
1 N-COUNT 梦遗;遗精 If a man has a wet dream, he has a dream about sex which causes him to have an orgasm while he is asleep. 梦遗;遗精
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 带来很大乐趣的人(或物);乐子 If someone says that a person or thing is a particular person's wet dream, they are saying in an unkind and mocking way that this person or thing would give that person a lot of pleasure. 带来很大乐趣的人(或物);乐子 [informal, rude]
