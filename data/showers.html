shower
ʃaʊəʳ ˈʃəʊə ˈʃaʊər ˈʃoʊər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense showers, present participle showering, past tense , past participle showered
1 N-COUNT 淋浴器;花洒;淋浴喷头 A shower is a device for washing yourself. It consists of a pipe which ends in a flat cover with a lot of holes in it so that water comes out in a spray. 淋浴器;花洒;淋浴喷头

  • She heard him turn on the shower.

    她听见他打开了淋浴喷头。

2 N-COUNT 淋浴间;淋浴房 A shower is a small enclosed area containing a shower. 淋浴间;淋浴房
3 N-COUNT (运动中心等的)浴室 The showers or the shower in a place such as a sports centre is the area containing showers. (运动中心等的)浴室

  • The showers are a mess.

    浴室里一片狼藉。

  • We all stood in the women's shower.

    我们都站在女浴室里。

4 N-COUNT 淋浴 If you have a shower, you wash yourself by standing under a spray of water from a shower. 淋浴

  • I think I'll have a shower before dinner.

    我想晚餐前洗个淋浴。

  • She took two showers a day.

    她每天洗两次淋浴。

5 VERB (洗)淋浴 If you shower, you wash yourself by standing under a spray of water from a shower. (洗)淋浴

  • There wasn't time to shower or change clothes. [VERB]

    没时间淋浴换衣服了。

6 N-COUNT 阵雨;(尤指)小阵雨 A shower is a short period of rain, especially light rain. 阵雨;(尤指)小阵雨

  • There'll be bright or sunny spells and scattered showers this afternoon.

    今天下午天气晴好，间有零星阵雨。

7 N-COUNT (落下的东西)一大批，一阵，一连串 You can refer to a lot of things that are falling as a shower of them. (落下的东西)一大批，一阵，一连串

  • Showers of sparks flew in all directions. [+ of]

    一大串火星四处飞溅。

  • ...a shower of meteorites.

    一阵流星雨

SYN storm, rain, volley, barrage  
8 VERB [usually passive] 向…抛撒;撒落 If you are showered with a lot of small objects or pieces, they are scattered over you. 向…抛撒;撒落

  • They were showered with rice in the traditional manner. [be VERB-ed + with]

    人们按传统习俗朝他们抛撒大米。

  • Mr Reagan was showered with glass. [be V-ed with n]

    里根先生被溅了一身的碎玻璃。

SYN cover, dust, spray, sprinkle  
9 VERB 大量给予;慷慨给予 If you shower a person with presents or kisses, you give them a lot of presents or kisses in a very generous and extravagant way. 大量给予;慷慨给予

  • He showered her with emeralds and furs. [VERB noun + with]

    他送给她很多绿宝石和裘皮大衣。

  • Her parents showered her with kisses. [VERB noun + with]

    她父母不停地亲吻她。

SYN inundate, load, heap, lavish  
10 N-COUNT 送礼聚会 A shower is a party or celebration at which the guests bring gifts. 送礼聚会 [mainly US]

  • ...a bridal shower.

    为新娘举行的送礼聚会

  • ...a baby shower.

    为新生儿举行的送礼聚会

11 N-SING [usually singular] 乌合之众 If you refer to a group of people as a particular kind of shower, you disapprove of them. 乌合之众 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • ...a shower of wasters. [+ of]

    一群饭桶
