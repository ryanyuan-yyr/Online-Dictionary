blurt
blɜːʳt blɜrt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blurts, present participle blurting, past tense , past participle blurted
VERB 脱口而出；说漏嘴 If someone blurts something, they say it suddenly, after trying hard to keep quiet or to keep it secret. 脱口而出；说漏嘴 [Also VERB that]

  • 'I was looking for Sally', he blurted, and his eyes filled with tears. [VERB with quote]

    “我当时正在在找萨莉”，他脱口而出，眼里噙满了泪水。

blurt out
