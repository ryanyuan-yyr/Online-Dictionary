andante
ændænti ænˈdænteɪ ɑnˈdɑnteɪ ænˈdænti
Word forms: plural andantes
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb] 徐缓地；用行板地 Andante written above a piece of music means that it should be played fairly slowly. 徐缓地；用行板地
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 行板；行板乐段 An andante is a piece of music that is played fairly slowly. 行板；行板乐段

  • ...the lovely central Andante.

    中间部动听的行板

  • ...the violas' Andante theme.

    中提琴的行板主旋律
