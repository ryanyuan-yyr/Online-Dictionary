canker
kæŋkəʳ ˈkæŋkər
Word forms: plural cankers
1 N-COUNT 祸患;恶痈 A canker is something evil that spreads and affects things or people. 祸患;恶痈 [formal]

  • ...the canker of jealousy. [+ of]

    嫉妒作祟

2 N-VAR (灌木、树木的)溃疡病；枯枝病 Canker is a disease which affects the wood of shrubs and trees, making the outer layer come away to expose the inside of the stem. (灌木、树木的)溃疡病；枯枝病

  • In gardens, cankers are most prominent on apples and pear trees.

    花园里,苹果树和梨树患枯枝病最明显。

SYN fungal disease
