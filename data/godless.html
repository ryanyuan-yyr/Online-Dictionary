godless
gɒdləs ˈgɑdlɪs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不信奉上帝的；无宗教信仰的 If you say that a person or group of people is godless, you disapprove of them because they do not believe in God. 不信奉上帝的；无宗教信仰的 [disapproval]

  • ...a godless and alienated society.

    没有宗教信仰、陷入孤立的社会

SYN wicked, depraved, profane, unprincipled
