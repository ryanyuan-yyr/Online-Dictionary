unbreakable
ʌnbreɪkəbəl ʌnˈbreɪkəbəl
1 ADJ 打不碎的;牢不可破的 Unbreakable objects cannot be broken, usually because they are made of a very strong material. 打不碎的;牢不可破的

  • Tableware for outdoor use should ideally be unbreakable.

    室外用的餐具最好是打不破的。

SYN durable, indestructible, shatterproof, lasting  
2 ADJ （规则、限制等）不能违反的，必须服从的 An unbreakable rule or limit must be obeyed. （规则、限制等）不能违反的，必须服从的

  • One unbreakable rule in our school is that no child can be tested without written parental permission.

    我们学校有一条金科玉律：未经父母书面同意，不得对学生进行测试。
