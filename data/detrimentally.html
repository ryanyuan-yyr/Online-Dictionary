detrimental
detrɪmentəl ˌdɛtrəˈmɛntəl
ADJ-GRADED 有害的;不利的 Something that is detrimental to something else has a harmful or damaging effect on it. 有害的;不利的

  • ...foods suspected of being detrimental to health. [+ to]

    被怀疑有害健康的食品

  • The government's policy of high interest rates is having a detrimental effect on industry.

    政府的高利率政策正对工业产生不利影响。

SYN damaging, destructive, harmful, adverse
