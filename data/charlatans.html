charlatan
ʃɑːʳlətən ˈʃɑrlətən
Word forms: plural charlatans
N-COUNT 骗子;假充内行的人 You describe someone as a charlatan when they pretend to have skills or knowledge that they do not really possess. 骗子;假充内行的人 [formal, disapproval]

  • He was exposed as a charlatan.

    他被揭穿，原来是个骗子。

SYN fraud [informal] , cheat, fake, sham
