drag into
PHRASAL VERB 拖进;扯进 To drag something or someone into an event or situation means to involve them in it when it is not necessary or not desirable. 拖进;扯进

  • Why should Carmela have dragged him into the argument?. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    为什么卡梅拉把他扯进这场争论中?

  • We may find ourselves dragged into new wars and new threats of wars. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    我们可能会发现我们自己被扯到新的战争或新战争的威胁中去了。
