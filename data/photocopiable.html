Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense photocopies, present participle photocopying, past tense , past participle photocopied

SYN copy, reproduction, duplicate, Xerox [trademark]  
2 VERB 复印 If you photocopy a document, you make a copy of it using a photocopier. 复印
