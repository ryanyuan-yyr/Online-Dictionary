loads
ləʊdz
COMB in N-COUNT （与车辆或容器名词连用，构成名词）表示“总装载量”，“总容纳量” -load combines with nouns referring to a vehicle or container to form nouns that refer to the total amount of something that the vehicle or container mentioned can hold or carry. （与车辆或容器名词连用，构成名词）表示“总装载量”，“总容纳量”

  • The first planeloads of food, children's clothing and medical supplies began arriving.

    首批飞机运送的食物、儿童衣物和药品开始抵达。

  • ...a lorry-load of sheep on their way across Europe.

    穿越欧洲途中的一卡车羊
