holy
hoʊli ˈhoʊli
Word forms: comparative holier, superlative holiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 与上帝（或宗教）有关的;神圣的 If you describe something as holy, you mean that it is considered to be special because it is connected with God or a particular religion. 与上帝（或宗教）有关的;神圣的

  • To them, as to all Christians, this is a holy place.

    对他们以及所有基督徒来说，这里是一处圣地。

  • ...Yom Kippur, the holiest day in the Jewish calendar.

    赎罪日，犹太历中最神圣的日子

SYN sacred, blessed, hallowed, dedicated  
2 ADJ-GRADED 虔诚的;圣洁的;献身于宗教（或教会）的 A holy person is a religious leader or someone who leads a religious life. 虔诚的;圣洁的;献身于宗教（或教会）的

  • The Indians think of him as a holy man, a combination of doctor and priest.

    印度人把他视为圣人，他既是医生又是僧侣。

SYN devout, godly, religious, pure  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] （表示惊讶、恐慌等）天啊，上帝啊 Holy is used in exclamations such as 'Holy cow!' and 'Holy smoke!' to express an emotion such as surprise or panic. （表示惊讶、恐慌等）天啊，上帝啊 [feelings]
4 See also: holier-than-thou 
Quotations
