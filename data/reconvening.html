reconvene
riːkənviːn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reconvenes, present participle reconvening, past tense , past participle reconvened
V-ERG 重新召集;重新召开 If a parliament, court, or conference reconvenes or if someone reconvenes it, it meets again after a break. 重新召集;重新召开

  • The conference might reconvene after its opening session. [VERB]

    大会在开幕式结束后可能要再开会。

  • It was certainly serious enough for him to reconvene Parliament. [VERB noun]

    他重新召集国会当然是件非常重大的事了。

  • He insisted that the security council be reconvened that night to authorise the rapid reaction force. [VERB noun]

    他坚持安理会连夜再次召开会议以授权快速反应部队介入。

SYN meet again
