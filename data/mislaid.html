mislay
mɪsleɪ mɪsˈleɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mislays, present participle mislaying, past tense , past participle mislaid
VERB 忘了把…放在何处 If you mislay something, you put it somewhere and then forget where you have put it. 忘了把…放在何处

  • I appear to have mislaid my jumper. [VERB noun]

    我好像忘了把套头衫放哪儿了。

SYN lose, misplace, miss, be unable to find
