high noon haɪ nuːn
1 N-UNCOUNT 同 noon High noon means the same as noon. 同 noon [literary]
2 N-UNCOUNT （新闻用语）紧要关头，关键时刻 Journalists sometimes use high noon to refer to a crisis or event which is likely to decide finally what is going to happen in a conflict or situation. （新闻用语）紧要关头，关键时刻

  • It looks like high noon for the nation's movie theaters, now we are in the age of the home video. [+ for ]

    我们现在正处在家庭录像大行其道的时代，这对国内的电影院来说似乎是个关乎生死存亡的关键时刻。
