untutored
ʌntjuːtəʳd ʌnˈtjuːtəd -tuːt- ʌnˈtutərd
ADJ 非科班出身的；未经正式训练的 If someone is untutored, they have not been formally trained to do something, although they may be quite skilled at it. 非科班出身的；未经正式训练的 [formal]

  • This untutored mathematician had an obsession with numbers.

    这位非科班出身的数学家对数字特别痴迷。

  • They had left school at fifteen and were quite untutored in writing. [+ in]

    他们15岁就辍学了，几乎未接受过正式的写作训练。

SYN uneducated, ignorant, untrained, illiterate
