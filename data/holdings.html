holding
hoʊldɪŋ ˈhoʊldɪŋ
Word forms: plural holdings
1 N-COUNT 股份 If you have a holding in a company, you own shares in it. 股份 [business]

  • That would increase Olympia & York's holding to 35%.

    那样就会使奥林匹亚约克公司的股份上升到35%。

  • Sid's amazing wealth comes from holdings in oil, gas, land and property.

    锡德惊人的财富来自他在石油、天然气和房地产领域持有的股份。

SYN property, securities, investments, resources  
2 N-COUNT （自有或租用的）农田，耕地 A holding is an area of farm land which is owned or rented by the person who grows crops on it. （自有或租用的）农田，耕地
3 N-PLURAL （博物馆、图书馆、美术馆等的）收藏品，馆藏 The holdings of a place such as a museum, library, or art gallery are the items such as books or paintings which are kept there. （博物馆、图书馆、美术馆等的）收藏品，馆藏
4 ADJ [ADJ n] （活动或行动）暂时用作维持现状的 A holding operation or action is a temporary one that is intended to keep a situation under control and to prevent it from becoming worse. （活动或行动）暂时用作维持现状的

  • A garden is, at best, a holding operation against nature.

    花园充其量不过是抗拒自然的一时之法。
