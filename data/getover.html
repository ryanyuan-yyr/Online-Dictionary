get over
1 PHRASAL VERB 从（不快或疾病中）恢复过来 If you get over an unpleasant or unhappy experience or an illness, you recover from it. 从（不快或疾病中）恢复过来

  • It took me a very long time to get over the shock of her death. [VERB PREP. noun]

    得知她去世的消息我惊愕不已，很长时间才缓过劲来。

SYN recover from, survive, get better from, come round  
2 PHRASAL VERB 克服，战胜（问题、困难） If you get over a problem or difficulty, you overcome it. 克服，战胜（问题、困难）

  • How would they get over that problem, he wondered? [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们会怎么解决那个问题？他寻思着。

SYN overcome, deal with, solve, resolve  
3 PHRASAL VERB （向…）讲清；使明白 If you get your message over to people, they hear and understand it. （向…）讲清；使明白

  • We have got to get the message over to the young that smoking isn't cool. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    我们要让年轻人认识到抽烟并不是什么很酷的事情。

SYN communicate, spread, pass on, convey
