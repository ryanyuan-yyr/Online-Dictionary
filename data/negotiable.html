negotiable
nɪgoʊʃəbəl nɪˈgoʊʃiəbəl nɪˈgoʊʃəbəl
1 ADJ 可讨论的;可协商的 Something that is negotiable can be changed or agreed when people discuss it. 可讨论的;可协商的

  • He warned that his economic programme for the country was not negotiable.

    他提醒说，他为这个国家制定的经济计划没有商量的余地。

  • The Manor is for sale at a negotiable price.

    该庄园现在出售，价格可议。

SYN debatable, flexible, unsettled, undecided  
2 ADJ (契约或财产)可转让的，可兑现的 Contracts or assets that are negotiable can be transferred to another person in exchange for money. (契约或财产)可转让的，可兑现的

  • The bonds may no longer be negotiable.

    这些债券或许不能再行转让了。

  • ...negotiable bearer bonds.

    可转让无记名债券

SYN valid, transferable, transactional  
3 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (地区)可通行的 An area of land that is negotiable is easy to travel across. (地区)可通行的

  • Parts of the road had been washed away by streams, but it was negotiable.

    部分路面已被河水冲毁，但仍可以通行。

SYN passable, open, clear, navigable
