speakerphone
spiːkəʳfɔʊn ˈspiːkəˌfəʊn ˈspikərˌfoʊn
Word forms: plural speakerphones
免提电话（内置麦克风和扬声器，无需手持） A speakerphone is a telephone that has a microphone and a loudspeaker, allowing you to talk to someone without putting the phone to your ear, as well as allowing other people to hear the person you are talking to. 免提电话（内置麦克风和扬声器，无需手持）

    可储存13个电话号码的10信道无绳免提电话

    她打开免提电话让我说话，这样他那边便听到我讲的话了。
