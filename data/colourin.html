colour in
PHRASAL VERB 给(画)着色;为…上色 If you colour in a drawing, you give it different colours using crayons or paints. 给(画)着色;为…上色 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Someone had coloured in all the black and white pictures. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    有人给所有的黑白照片都上了色。

  • Draw simple shapes for your child to colour in. [VERB PREP. noun]

    画一些简单的形状让孩子涂上颜色。
