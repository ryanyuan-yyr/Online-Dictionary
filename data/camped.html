1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 露宿的;扎营的;野营的 If people are camped or camped out somewhere in the open air, they are living, staying, or waiting there, often in tents. 露宿的;扎营的;野营的

  • Most of the refugees are camped high in the mountains.

    多数难民在高山上露宿。

  • You will wake to find film crews camped in your backyard.

    一觉醒来,你会发现电影摄制组进驻了你家后院。

  • They are camped out in tents on the front lawn.

    他们在门前的草坪上支起帐篷宿营了。
