specify
spesɪfaɪ ˈspɛsəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense specifies, present participle specifying, past tense , past participle specified
1 VERB 明确说明;具体指定 If you specify something, you give information about what is required or should happen in a certain situation. 明确说明;具体指定

  • They specified a spacious entrance hall. [VERB noun]

    他们专门指定要一个宽敞的大门厅。

  • He has not specified what action he would like them to take. [VERB wh]

    他尚未明确说明自己希望他们采取什么样的行动。

SYN state, designate, spell out, stipulate  
2 VERB 具体说明（或规定） If you specify what should happen or be done, you explain it in an exact and detailed way. 具体说明（或规定）

  • Each recipe specifies the size of egg to be used. [VERB noun]

    每种菜谱都具体规定了所用鸡蛋的大小。

  • One rule specifies that learner drivers must be supervised by adults. [VERB that]

    一条规则明确规定，见习驾驶员必须有成人的监督。

  • Patients eat together at a specified time. [VERB-ed]

    病人在规定的时间集体就餐。
