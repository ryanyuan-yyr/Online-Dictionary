fictitious
fɪktɪʃəs fɪkˈtɪʃəs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 虚幻的;子虚乌有的 Fictitious is used to describe something that is false or does not exist, although some people claim that it is true or exists. 虚幻的;子虚乌有的

  • We're interested in the source of these fictitious rumours.

    我们对这些子虚乌有的谣言从何而来很感兴趣。

2 ADJ 虚构的;小说的 A fictitious character, thing, or event occurs in a story, play, or film but never really existed or happened. 虚构的;小说的

  • The persons and events portrayed in this production are fictitious.

    这部作品中描述的人物和事件均属虚构。

SYN imaginary, imagined, made-up, assumed
