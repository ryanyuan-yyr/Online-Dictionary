diner
daɪnəʳ ˈdaɪnər
Word forms: plural diners
1 N-COUNT (全天营业的)小餐馆，小饭馆 A diner is a small cheap restaurant that is open all day. (全天营业的)小餐馆，小饭馆 [US]
2 N-COUNT (餐馆里的)进餐者 The people who are having dinner in a restaurant can be referred to as diners . (餐馆里的)进餐者

  • They sat in a corner, away from other diners.

    他们坐在一个角落里，远离其他进餐者。

SYN customer, guest, client, eater
