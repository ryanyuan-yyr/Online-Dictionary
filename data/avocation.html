avocation
ævoʊkeɪʃən ˌævəˈkeɪʃən ˌævəˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural avocations
N-VAR 业余爱好;副业 Your avocation is a job or activity that you do because you are interested in it, rather than to earn your living. 业余爱好;副业 [formal]

  • He was a printer by trade and naturalist by avocation.

    他从事印刷业，同时是个博物学爱好者。
