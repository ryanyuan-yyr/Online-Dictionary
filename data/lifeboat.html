lifeboat
laɪfboʊt ˈlaɪfˌboʊt
Word forms: plural lifeboats
1 N-COUNT （派往海上救助的）救生船 A lifeboat is a medium-sized boat that is sent out from a port or harbour in order to rescue people who are in danger at sea. （派往海上救助的）救生船
2 N-COUNT （船上备用的）救生艇 A lifeboat is a small boat that is carried on a ship, which people on the ship use to escape when the ship is in danger of sinking. （船上备用的）救生艇

  • The captain ordered all passengers and crew into lifeboats.

    船长命令所有乘客和船员都进入救生艇。
