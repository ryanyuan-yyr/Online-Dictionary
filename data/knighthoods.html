knighthood
naɪthʊd ˈnaɪtˌhʊd
Word forms: plural knighthoods
N-COUNT （英国国王或女王授予的）爵士头衔，骑士头衔，爵士称号，骑士称号（其名前冠以Sir） A knighthood is a title that is given to a man by a British king or queen for his achievements or his service to his country.
