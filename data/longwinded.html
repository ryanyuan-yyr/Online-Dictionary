long-winded
ˈlɔŋˈwɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 长篇累牍的；冗长的；啰的 If you describe something that is written or said as long-winded, you are critical of it because it is longer than necessary. 长篇累牍的；冗长的；啰的 [disapproval]

  • The manifesto is long-winded, repetitious and often ambiguous or poorly drafted.

    宣言长篇大论，内容叠床架屋，词句常常含混不清或不知所云。

  • I hope I'm not being too long-winded.

    我希望我不是太啰。

SYN rambling, prolonged, lengthy, tedious
