cast down
PHRASAL VERB-PASSIVE 使沮丧;使伤心;使忧愁 If someone is cast down by something, they are sad or worried because of it. 使沮丧;使伤心;使忧愁 [literary]

  • I am not cast down by it because I believe in the fundamental strength of the business. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    这件事并没有使我灰心，因为我相信企业的深厚根基。

  • Ever since I saw the diary excerpts I've been cast down. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    自从看到日记摘录，我就一直非常沮丧。

SYN discourage, depress, desolate, dishearten
