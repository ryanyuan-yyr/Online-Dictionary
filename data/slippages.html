slippage
slɪpɪdʒ ˈslɪpɪdʒ
Word forms: plural slippages
N-VAR 下降;下跌 Slippage is a failure to maintain a steady position or rate of progress, so that a particular target or standard is not achieved. 下降;下跌

  • ...a substantial slippage in the value of sterling. [+ in]

    英镑价值的大幅下跌

  • We want to stop the slippage of the quality of public services.

    我们想要止住公共服务质量下滑的局面。
