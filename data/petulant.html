petulant
petʃʊlənt ˈpɛtʃələnt
ADJ-GRADED 大发脾气的；任性的 Someone who is petulant is unreasonably angry and upset in a childish way. 大发脾气的；任性的

  • His critics say he's just being silly and petulant.

    批评家说他就是愚蠢和任性。

  • He picked the pen up with a petulant gesture.

    他生气地拿起那支钢笔。

SYN sulky, cross, moody, sour  
petulantly

  • 'I don't need help,' he said petulantly.

    “我不需要帮助，”他没好气地说。
