stringer
strɪŋəʳ ˈstrɪŋər
Word forms: plural stringers
N-COUNT 特约记者 A stringer is a journalist who is employed part-time by a newspaper or news service in order to report on a particular area. 特约记者 [journalism]

  • He picked up extra money as a local stringer for the New York Herald.

    他做《纽约先驱报》的当地特约记者来赚些外快。
