loathe
loʊð loʊð
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense loathes, present participle loathing, past tense , past participle loathed
VERB 厌恶；讨厌；憎恨 If you loathe something or someone, you dislike them very much. 厌恶；讨厌；憎恨

  • The two men loathe each other. [VERB noun]

    两个男人互相看不顺眼。

  • She loathed being the child of impoverished labourers. [VERB verb-ing]

    她憎恨自己是贫困工人的孩子。

SYN hate, dislike, despise, detest
