cash
kæʃ kæʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cashes, present participle cashing, past tense , past participle cashed
1 N-UNCOUNT 现金;现款 Cash is money in the form of notes and coins rather than cheques. 现金;现款

  • ...two thousand pounds in cash.

    2,000英镑现金

2See also:hard cash  petty cash 
3 N-UNCOUNT (尤指现成的)资金，钱 Cash means the same as money, especially money which is immediately available. (尤指现成的)资金，钱 [informal]

  • ...a state-owned financial-services group with plenty of cash.

    资金雄厚的国有金融服务集团

4 VERB 兑现(支票) If you cash a cheque, you exchange it at a bank for the amount of money that it is worth. 兑现(支票)

  • There are similar charges if you want to cash a cheque at a branch other than your own. [VERB noun]

    如果你想在开户行以外的网点兑现支票，也要收取类似的费用。
