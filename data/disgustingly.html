disgusting
dɪsgʌstɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 令人厌恶的;令人作呕的 If you say that something is disgusting, you are criticizing it because it is extremely unpleasant. 令人厌恶的;令人作呕的

  • It tasted disgusting.

    这味道令人作呕。

  • Smoking is a disgusting habit.

    吸烟是一个不良嗜好。

2 ADJ-GRADED 完全不能接受的;可怕的 If you say that something is disgusting, you mean that you find it completely unacceptable. 完全不能接受的;可怕的

  • It's disgusting that the taxpayer is subsidising this project.

    由纳税人来资助这一项目实在是说不过去。
