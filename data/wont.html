won't
woʊnt woʊnt
（will not 的常用口语形式） Won't is the usual spoken form of 'will not'. （will not 的常用口语形式）

  • His parents won't let him come.

    她父母不会让他来的。

wont
woʊnt wɔːnt wɔnt woʊnt wɑnt wʌnt
1 经常的;惯常的 If someone is wont to do something, they often or regularly do it. 经常的;惯常的

  • Both have committed their indiscretions, as human beings are wont to do.

    两人都有失慎重，此乃人之常情。

2 按惯常的做法;常常 If someone does a particular thing as is their wont, they do that thing often or regularly. 按惯常的做法;常常

  • Paul woke early, as was his wont.

    保罗醒得很早，历来如此。
