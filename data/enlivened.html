enliven
ɪnlaɪvən ɛnˈlaɪvən ɪnˈlaɪvən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enlivens, present participle enlivening, past tense , past participle enlivened
VERB 使有趣;使生动；使更有活力 To enliven events, situations, or people means to make them more lively or cheerful. 使有趣;使生动；使更有活力

  • I love the way a good flirtation can enliven the most mundane situation. [VERB noun]

    恰到好处的打情骂俏能够使最平淡无奇的时刻变得生动有趣起来，我很喜欢这种感觉。

  • Even the most boring meeting was enlivened by Dan's presence. [be VERB-ed]

    即使是最无聊的会议，只要丹一出现，也会变得有趣起来。

SYN cheer up, excite, inspire, cheer
