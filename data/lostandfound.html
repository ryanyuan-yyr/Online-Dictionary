lost and found lɒst ənd faʊnd
1 N-SING 失物招领处 Lost and found is the place where lost property is kept. 失物招领处 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 lost property
2 ADJ 拾获的；捡获的；捡到的 Lost and found things are things which someone has lost and which someone else has found. 拾获的；捡获的；捡到的

  • ...the shelf where they stored lost-and-found articles.

    他们存放拾获物品的架子

  • ...the local paper's lost-and-found column.

    当地报纸的失物招领专栏
