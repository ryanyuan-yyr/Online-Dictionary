craze
kreɪz kreɪz
Word forms: plural crazes
N-COUNT (一时的)狂热，风行 If there is a craze for something, it is very popular for a short time. (一时的)狂热，风行

  • ...the craze for Mutant Ninja Turtles.

    《忍者神龟》的风靡一时

  • Walking is the latest fitness craze.

    散步是最近非常流行的健身方式。

SYN fad, thing [informal] , fashion, trend
