impoverish
ɪmpɒvərɪʃ ɪmˈpɑvərɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense impoverishes, present participle impoverishing, past tense , past participle impoverished
1 VERB 使贫困;使赤贫 Something that impoverishes a person or a country makes them poor. 使贫困;使赤贫

  • We need to reduce the burden of taxes that impoverish the economy. [VERB noun]

    我们需要减轻导致经济困顿的税收负荷。

  • ...a society impoverished by wartime inflation. [VERB-ed]

    因战时通货膨胀导致贫困的社会

SYN bankrupt, ruin, beggar, break  
impoverished

  • ...an attempt to lure businesses into impoverished areas.

    把企业吸引到贫困地区的尝试

2 VERB 使贫瘠;使枯竭 A person or thing that impoverishes something makes it worse in quality. 使贫瘠;使枯竭

  • ...plants that impoverish the soil quickly. [VERB noun]

    使土壤迅速贫瘠的植物
