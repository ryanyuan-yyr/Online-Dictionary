ingenuous
ɪndʒenjuəs ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs
ADJ-GRADED 坦率的;真诚的;单纯的 If you describe someone as ingenuous, you mean that they are innocent, trusting, and honest. 坦率的;真诚的;单纯的 [formal]

  • He seemed too ingenuous for a reporter.

    作为一名记者，他太老实了。

  • With ingenuous sincerity, he captivated his audience.

    他以自己的率真迷住了观众。

SYN naive, open, trusting, simple  
ingenuously

  • Somewhat ingenuously, he explains how the crime may be accomplished.

    他有点天真地解释了这一罪行可能是如何实施的。

  • He smiled, eyes ingenuously wide.

    他露出微笑,眼睛睁得大大的，一副单纯神态。
