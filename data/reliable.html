reliable
rɪlaɪəbəl rɪˈlaɪəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 可靠的;可信赖的 People or things that are reliable can be trusted to work well or to behave in the way that you want them to. 可靠的;可信赖的

  • She was efficient and reliable.

    她办事很有效率，也很可靠。

  • Japanese cars are so reliable.

    日本产的汽车质量非常可靠。
