quintessence
kwɪntesəns kwɪnˈtɛsəns kwɪnˈtɛsəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 典范;典型 The quintessence of something is the most perfect or typical example of it. 典范;典型 [formal]

  • He was the quintessence of all that Eva most deeply loathed. [+ of]

    他是伊娃最深恶痛绝的那种人中的典型。

SYN epitome, representation, embodiment, type  
2 N-UNCOUNT 精髓;精华;本质 The quintessence of something is the aspect of it which seems to represent its central nature. 精髓;精华;本质 [formal]

  • ...an old stone cottage, the quintessence of rural England. [+ of]

    一座堪称英格兰乡村风格之典范的石砌农舍
