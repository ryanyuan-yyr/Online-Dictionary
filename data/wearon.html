wear on
PHRASAL VERB (时间)流逝 If you say that time wears on, you mean that it passes, especially when it seems to pass slowly. (时间)流逝

  • As the day wore on Brand found himself increasingly impressed. [VERB PREP.]

    白天慢慢过去，布兰德发现自己兴趣愈发浓厚。

  • The summer days wore on and life returned to its boring routine. [VERB PREP.]

    夏天的时光已经流逝，生活又恢复了昔日的无聊。

SYN pass, progress, proceed, go by or past
