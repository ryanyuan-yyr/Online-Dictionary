nonchalant
nɒnʃələnt -lɑːnt ˌnɑnʃəˈlɑnt ˈnɑnʃəˌlɑnt ˌnɑnʃələnt ˈnɑnʃələnt
ADJ-GRADED 无动于衷的;漠不关心的 If you describe someone as nonchalant, you mean that they appear not to worry or care about things and that they seem very calm. 无动于衷的;漠不关心的

  • Clark's mother is nonchalant about her role in her son's latest work. [+ about]

    克拉克的母亲对自己在儿子最新作品中的角色漠不关心。

  • Denis tried to look nonchalant and uninterested.

    丹尼斯竭力表现出漠不关心、不感兴趣的样子。

  • It merely underlines our rather more nonchalant attitude to life.

    这仅仅是更凸显了我们相当漠然的生活态度。

SYN indifferent, cool, calm, casual  
nonchalance

  • Affecting nonchalance, I handed her two hundred dollar bills.

    我装作一副无动于衷的样子，递给她200美元钞票。

nonchalantly

  • 'Does Will intend to return with us?' Joanna asked as nonchalantly as she could.

    “威尔打算和我们一起回去吗?”乔安娜尽量装作若无其事地问道。
