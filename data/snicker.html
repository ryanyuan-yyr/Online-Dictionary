snicker
snɪkəʳ ˈsnɪkər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense snickers, present participle snickering, past tense , past participle snickered
VERB 窃笑;暗笑 If you snicker, you laugh quietly in a disrespectful way, for example at something rude or embarrassing. 窃笑;暗笑 [Also VERB]

  • We all snickered at Mrs. Swenson. [VERB + at]

    我们都暗暗嘲笑斯温森太太。

SYN snigger, laugh, giggle, smirk  
Snicker is also a noun.

  • ...a chorus of jeers and snickers.

    一阵嗤笑
