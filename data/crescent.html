crescent
kresənt krez- ˈkrɛsənt ˈkrɛsənt
Word forms: plural crescents
1 N-COUNT 新月形，月牙形(伊斯兰教最重要的象征) A crescent is a curved shape that is wider in the middle than at its ends, like the shape of the moon during its first and last quarters. It is the most important symbol of the Islamic faith. 新月形，月牙形(伊斯兰教最重要的象征)

  • A glittering Islamic crescent tops the mosque.

    新月形标志在清真寺顶端闪闪发光。

  • ...a narrow crescent of sand dunes. [+ of]

    狭长的新月形沙丘带

  • ...a crescent moon.

    新月

2 N-IN-NAMES (常用于街道或一排房子的名称中)新月形街区 Crescent is sometimes used as part of the name of a street or row of houses that is usually built in a curve. (常用于街道或一排房子的名称中)新月形街区 [mainly British]

  • ...44 Colville Crescent.

    科尔维尔新月街44号
