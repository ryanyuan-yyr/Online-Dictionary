spacious
speɪʃəs ˈspeɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 广阔的;宽敞的 A spacious room or other place is large in size or area, so that you can move around freely in it. 广阔的;宽敞的

  • The house has a spacious kitchen and dining area.

    这座房子有一个宽敞的厨房和用餐区。

SYN roomy, large, huge, broad  
spaciousness

  • A high ceiling creates a feeling of spaciousness.

    高高的天花板会给人一种宽敞的感觉。
