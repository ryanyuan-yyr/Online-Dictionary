dividing line
dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ laɪn
Word forms: plural dividing lines
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 分界线;界限 A dividing line is a distinction or set of distinctions which marks the difference between two types of thing or two groups. 分界线;界限

  • There's a very thin dividing line between joviality and hysteria. [+ between]

    激动和歇斯底里之间就一线之隔。

2 N-SING (两个地区间的)界限 The dividing line between two areas is the boundary between them. (两个地区间的)界限

  • ...people on both sides of the dividing line between Israel and the occupied territories. [+ between]

    居住在以色列和占领区分界线两边的人民
