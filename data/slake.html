slake
sleɪk sleɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense slakes, present participle slaking, past tense , past participle slaked
VERB 解(渴) If you slake your thirst, you drink something that stops you being thirsty. 解(渴)

  • I slaked my thirst with three cans of Coke. [VERB noun]

    我喝了 3 罐可乐解渴。

SYN satisfy, gratify, assuage, quench
