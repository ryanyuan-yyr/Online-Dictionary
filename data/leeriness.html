leery
lɪəri ˈlɪri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 猜疑的；警觉的；有戒心的 If you are leery of something, you are cautious and suspicious about it and try to avoid it. 猜疑的；警觉的；有戒心的 [informal]

  • Executives say they are leery of the proposed system. [+ of]

    行政人员们说他们对拟建中的系统心怀顾虑。

  • They were leery about investing in a company controlled by a single individual. [+ about]

    把资金投向一个由一人独自操控的公司，他们不大放心。

SYN wary, cautious, uncertain, suspicious  
2 ADJ 不怀好意的；色迷迷的 If someone looks or smiles at you in a leery way, they look or smile at you in an unpleasant way, usually because they are sexually interested in you. 不怀好意的；色迷迷的 [disapproval]

  • ...a leery grin.

    淫笑
