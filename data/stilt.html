stilt
stɪlt stɪlt
Word forms: plural stilts
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft on NOUN] (尤指在潮湿或松软的地面竖起的)木桩，金属桩，支柱 Stilts are long upright pieces of wood or metal on which some buildings are built, especially where the ground is wet or very soft. (尤指在潮湿或松软的地面竖起的)木桩，金属桩，支柱

  • They inhabit reed huts built on stilts above the water.

    他们住在建于水中木桩之上的芦苇草屋里。

2 N-COUNT 高跷 Stilts are two long pieces of wood with pieces for the feet fixed high up on the sides so that people can stand on them and walk high above the ground. 高跷
