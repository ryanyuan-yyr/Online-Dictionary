yellow
jeloʊ ˈjɛloʊ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense yellows, present participle yellowing, past tense , past participle yellowed
1 COLOUR 黄;黄色 Something that is yellow is the colour of lemons, butter, or the middle part of an egg. 黄;黄色

  • The walls have been painted bright yellow.

    墙壁漆成了亮黄色。

  • Kim opted for cooler blues and yellows in the master bedroom.

    金为主卧室挑选了偏冷色调的蓝色和黄色。

2 VERB （通常因陈旧）变黄，发黄 If something yellows, it becomes yellow in colour, often because it is old. （通常因陈旧）变黄，发黄

  • The flesh of his cheeks seemed to have yellowed. [VERB]

    他似乎有些面色发黄。

  • She sat scanning the yellowing pages. [VERB-ing]

    她坐着浏览发黄的书页。
