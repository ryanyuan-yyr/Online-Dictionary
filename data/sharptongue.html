sharp tongue
ˌʃɑːp ˈtʌŋ
Word forms: plural sharp tongues
N-COUNT 刀子嘴;说话刻薄;言辞尖刻 If you say that someone has a sharp tongue, you are critical of the fact that they say things which are unkind though often clever. 刀子嘴;说话刻薄;言辞尖刻 [disapproval]

  • Despite her sharp tongue, she inspires loyalty from her friends.

    尽管她说话尖刻，却得到了朋友们的忠心。
