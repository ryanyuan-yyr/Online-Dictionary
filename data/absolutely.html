absolutely
æbsəluːtli ˌæbsəˈluːtlɪ ˈæbsəˌlutli ˌ æbsəˈlutli
1 ADV [usually ADVERB adjective/adverb, oft ADVERB with verb] 完全地；彻底地 Absolutely means totally and completely. 完全地；彻底地 [emphasis]

  • Jill is absolutely right.

    吉尔完全正确。

  • I absolutely refuse to get married.

    我决不结婚。

  • There is absolutely no difference!

    这绝对没有任何不同！

SYN completely, totally, perfectly, quite  
2 ADV （用于强调）确实地，绝对地 Some people say absolutely as an emphatic way of saying yes or of agreeing with someone. They say absolutely not as an emphatic way of saying no or of disagreeing with someone. （用于强调）确实地，绝对地 [emphasis]

  • 'It's worrying, isn't it?' – 'Absolutely.'.

    “这让人担忧，不是吗？”—“确实是。”

  • 'Did they approach you?' – 'No, absolutely not.'

    “他们找过你了吗？”—“没有，绝对没有。”

SYN definitely, surely, certainly, clearly
