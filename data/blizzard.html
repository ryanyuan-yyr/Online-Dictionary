blizzard
blɪzəʳd ˈblɪzərd
Word forms: plural blizzards
1 N-COUNT 大风雪；暴风雪 A blizzard is a very bad snowstorm with strong winds. 大风雪；暴风雪
2 N-COUNT [usually singular, usually NOUN of n] （指令人反感或恼火的事情）大量，铺天盖地 You can refer to a large number of things that you do not like or which annoy you as a blizzard of those things. （指令人反感或恼火的事情）大量，铺天盖地 [written, disapproval]

  • Retailers were hit with a blizzard of new products and brands in all shapes, sizes and colours.

    各种形色不一、大小各异的新产品和新品牌铺天盖地，让零售商们疲于应付。

  • ...the annual blizzard of bills and amendments.

    每年大量的议案和修正案
