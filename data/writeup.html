write up
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…整理成文；详细写出（所做的事或所说的话） If you write up something that has been done or said, you record it on paper in a neat and complete form, usually using notes that you have made. 把…整理成文；详细写出（所做的事或所说的话）

  • He wrote up his visit in a report of over 600 pages. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他将自己的访问整理成了一份600多页的报告。

  • Mr Sadler conducted interviews, and his girlfriend wrote them up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    萨德勒先生进行采访，他的女朋友将采访内容整理成文。
