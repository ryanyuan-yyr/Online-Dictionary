-footed -fʊtid
1 COMB in ADJ (与 heavy, light 等形容词连用构成形容词)表示“行动…的”，“走路…的” -footed combines with words such as 'heavy' or 'light' to form adjectives which indicate how someone moves or walks. (与 heavy, light 等形容词连用构成形容词)表示“行动…的”，“走路…的”

  • ...a slim, light-footed little man.

    身材瘦削、步履轻盈的小个子男人

  • He was a nimble-footed boy of ten.

    他是个步伐敏捷的10岁男孩。

2See also:foot  flat-footed  sure-footed
