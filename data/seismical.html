seismic
saɪzmɪk ˈsaɪzmɪk ˈsaɪsmɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 地震的;地震引起的 Seismic means caused by or relating to an earthquake. 地震的;地震引起的

  • Earthquakes produce two types of seismic waves.

    地震产生两种地震波。

  • The latest seismic activity was also felt in northern Kenya.

    肯尼亚北部也感觉到了最近的地震活动。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (转变、变化)突如其来的，巨大的，急剧的 A seismic shift or change is a very sudden or dramatic change. (转变、变化)突如其来的，巨大的，急剧的

  • I have never seen such a seismic shift in public opinion in such a short period of time.

    我从未见过公众舆论在如此短的时间内发生如此巨大的转变。
