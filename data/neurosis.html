neurosis
njʊəroʊsɪs njʊˈrəʊsɪs nʊr- nʊˈroʊsɪs njʊˈroʊsɪs
Word forms: plural neuroses/njʊəroʊsɪːz, US nʊr-/
N-VAR 神经症 Neurosis is a mental condition which causes people to have unreasonable fears and worries over a long period of time. 神经症

  • He was anxious to the point of neurosis.

    他焦虑得都要得神经症了。

  • She got a neurosis about chemicals and imagined them everywhere doing her harm. [+ about]

    她对化学品有神经症，认为它们无处不在地伤害着自己。

SYN obsession, instability, mental illness, abnormality
