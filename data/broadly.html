broadly
brɔːdli
1 ADV-GRADED 一般说来；大致上 You can use broadly to indicate that something is generally true. 一般说来；大致上

  • The President broadly got what he wanted out of his meeting.

    总统通过这次会议基本上达到了他的目的。

  • The idea that software is capable of any task is broadly true in theory.

    认为软件能够处理任何任务的观念从理论上说基本是正确的。

SYN in general, largely, generally, mainly
