patchwork
pætʃwɜːʳk ˈpætʃˌwɜrk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 拼缀的；拼缝的 A patchwork quilt, cushion, or piece of clothing is made by sewing together small pieces of material of different colours or patterns. 拼缀的；拼缝的

  • ...beds covered in patchwork quilts.

    盖着百衲被的床

Patchwork is also a noun.

  • For centuries, quilting and patchwork have been popular needlecrafts.

    几个世纪以来，缝被子和做布拼一直是许多人都会做的针线活儿。

2 N-SING 拼凑物；混杂物 If you refer to something as a patchwork, you mean that it is made up of many different parts, pieces or colours. 拼凑物；混杂物

  • The low mountains were a patchwork of green and brown. [+ of]

    低矮的群山上，绿色和棕色混杂着。

  • ...this complex republic, a patchwork of cultures, religions and nationalities. [+ of]

    这个复杂的共和国，各种文化、宗教和民族的混合体

SYN mixture, confusion, jumble, medley
