nutshell
nʌtʃel ˈnʌtˌʃɛl
PHRASE 概括地说;简言之 You can use in a nutshell to indicate that you are saying something in a very brief way, using few words. 概括地说;简言之

  • In a nutshell, the owners thought they knew best.

    简而言之，业主们认为自己最了解情况。

  • This, in a nutshell, is what Richard Chaplin appears to have done.

    简言之，这好像就是理查德·查普林所做的一切。
