unwitting
ʌnwɪtɪŋ ʌnˈwɪtɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不知情的；不知不觉的；无意的 If you describe a person or their actions as unwitting, you mean that the person does something or is involved in something without realizing it. 不知情的；不知不觉的；无意的

  • We're unwitting victims of the system.

    我们都是该系统的不知情的受害者。

  • It had been an unwitting blunder on Blair's part.

    它曾是布莱尔的无心之失。

SYN unknowing, innocent, unsuspecting, unconscious  
unwittingly

  • He was unwittingly caught up in the confrontation.

    他无意间卷入了这场冲突之中。
