impudent
ɪmpjʊdənt ˈɪmpjudənt
ADJ-GRADED 放肆的;无礼的 If you describe someone as impudent, you mean they are rude or disrespectful, or do something they have no right to do. 放肆的;无礼的 [formal, disapproval]

  • Some of them were impudent and insulting.

    他们当中的一些人放肆无礼,目中无人。

SYN bold, rude, cheeky [informal] , forward  
impudence

  • One sister had the impudence to wear the other's clothes.

    一个姐妹竟擅自穿另一个姐妹的衣服。
