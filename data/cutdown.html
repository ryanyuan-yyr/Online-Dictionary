cut down
1 PHRASAL VERB 削减;减少使用 If you cut down on something or cut down something, you use or do less of it. 削减;减少使用 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He cut down on coffee and cigarettes, and ate a balanced diet. [VERB PREP. + on]

    他喝咖啡和吸烟都比以前少了，饮食也均衡了。

  • Car owners were asked to cut down travel. [VERB PREP. noun]

    要求车主们减少驾车出行的次数。

  • If you spend more than your income, can you try to cut down? [VERB PREP.]

    如果你入不敷出，可以试着减少开支吗？

SYN reduce, moderate, decrease, lessen  
2 PHRASAL VERB 砍倒(树木) If you cut down a tree, you cut through its trunk so that it falls to the ground. 砍倒(树木) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • A vandal with a chainsaw cut down a tree. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一个故意破坏公物的人用链锯伐倒了一棵树。

SYN fell, level, hew, lop
