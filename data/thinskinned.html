thin-skinned
ˈθɪnˈskɪnd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 脸皮薄的;敏感的;易生气的 If you say that someone is thin-skinned, you mean that they are easily upset by criticism or unpleasantness. 脸皮薄的;敏感的;易生气的 [disapproval]

  • Some fear he is too thin-skinned to survive the rough-and-tumble of a presidential campaign.

    有人担心他脸皮太薄，经受不住总统选举的激烈竞争。

SYN sensitive, vulnerable, easily hurt, touchy
