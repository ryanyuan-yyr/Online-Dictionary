cheer up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)高兴起来;(使)振作起来 When you cheer up or when something cheers you up, you stop feeling depressed and become more cheerful. (使)高兴起来;(使)振作起来 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • I think he misses her terribly. You might cheer him up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我估计他是太想念她了。也许你可以让他打起点儿精神来。

  • I wrote that song just to cheer myself up. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    我写那首歌是给我自己打气的。

  • Cheer up, better times may be ahead. [VERB PREP.]

    打起精神来，好日子也许还在后头呢。
