strainer
streɪnəʳ ˈstreɪnər
Word forms: plural strainers
N-COUNT 滤器;滤网 A strainer is an object with holes which you pour a liquid through in order to separate the liquid from the solids in it. 滤器;滤网

  • Pour the broth through a strainer.

    把肉汤倒进滤网滤一下。

  • ...a tea strainer.

    滤茶网

SYN sieve, filter, colander, riddle
