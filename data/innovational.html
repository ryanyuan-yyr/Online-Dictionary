innovation
ɪnəveɪʃən ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən
Word forms: plural innovations
1 N-COUNT 新事物;新方法 An innovation is a new thing or a new method of doing something. 新事物;新方法

  • The vegetarian burger was an innovation which was rapidly exported to Britain.

    素食汉堡是一种迅速传到了英国的新开发食品。

  • ...the transformation wrought by the technological innovations of the industrial age.

    由工业时代的新技术方法促成的变化

SYN change, revolution, departure, introduction  
2 N-UNCOUNT 革新;创新;改革 Innovation is the introduction of new ideas, methods, or things. 革新;创新;改革

  • We must promote originality and encourage innovation.

    我们必须提倡创意,鼓励革新。

SYN newness, novelty, originality, freshness
