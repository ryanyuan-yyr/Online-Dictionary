architect
ɑːʳkɪtekt ˈɑrkəˌtɛkt
Word forms: plural architects
1 N-COUNT 建筑师;设计师 An architect is a person who designs buildings. 建筑师;设计师
2 N-COUNT （景观美化、铁路等大型项目的）规划者，设计者 You can use architect to refer to a person who plans large projects such as landscaping or railways. （景观美化、铁路等大型项目的）规划者，设计者

  • ...Merrick Denton-Thompson, the landscape architect for Hampshire county council.

    梅里克·登顿-汤普森，汉普郡议会的园林设计师

  • ...Paul Andreu, chief architect of French railways.

    保罗·安德鲁，法国铁路的总设计师

3 N-COUNT （想法、事件或组织的）发起者，创立者 The architect of an idea, event, or institution is the person who invented it or made it happen. （想法、事件或组织的）发起者，创立者 [formal]

  • ...Russia's chief architect of economic reform. [+ of]

    俄罗斯经济改革的总规划师

SYN creator, father, shaper, engineer
