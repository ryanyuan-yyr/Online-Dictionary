nameless
neɪmləs ˈneɪmlɪs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不知其名的;无名的 You describe people or things as nameless when you do not know their name or when they do not have a name. 不知其名的;无名的

  • They can have their cases rejected, without reasons being given, by nameless officials.

    他们的诉求可能会被一些不知姓名的官员们莫名其妙地驳回。

SYN unnamed, unknown, obscure, anonymous  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 不宜说出名字（或名称）的 If you say that someone or something will remain nameless, you mean that you will not mention their name, often because you do not want to embarrass them. 不宜说出名字（或名称）的

  • A local friend who shall be nameless warned me that I was in for trouble soon.

    一个不便说出其名的当地朋友警告我，我不久肯定会有麻烦。
