ripen
raɪpən ˈraɪpən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ripens, present participle ripening, past tense , past participle ripened
V-ERG (使)成熟 When crops ripen or when the sun ripens them, they become ripe. (使)成熟

  • I'm waiting for the apples to ripen. [VERB]

    我正在等待苹果成熟。

  • You can ripen the tomatoes on a sunny windowsill. [VERB noun]

    把西红柿放在有阳光的窗台上可以让它们成熟。

  • ...an abundance of sun ripened fruit. [VERB-ed]

    大量自然成熟的水果

SYN mature, season, develop, get ready
