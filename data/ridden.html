ridden
rɪdən ˈrɪdən
Ridden is the past participle of ride.
-ridden -rɪdən
COMB in ADJ-GRADED (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“为…所苦的”，“为…所累的”，“充满…的” -ridden combines with nouns to form adjectives that describe something as having a lot of a particular undesirable thing or quality, or suffering very much because of it. (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“为…所苦的”，“为…所累的”，“充满…的”

  • ...the debt-ridden economies of Latin America.

    拉丁美洲负债累累的经济

  • Too many women are like me, guilt ridden about the kids.

    很多女人都像我一样，对孩子充满了内疚。
