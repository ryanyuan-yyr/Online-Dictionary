wholesome
hoʊlsəm ˈhoʊlsəm
1 ADJ-GRADED (尤指不包含不道德的性内容)健康的，有益身心的 If you describe something as wholesome, you approve of it because you think it is likely to have a positive influence on people's behaviour or mental state, especially because it does not involve anything sexually immoral. (尤指不包含不道德的性内容)健康的，有益身心的 [approval]

  • ...good, wholesome fun.

    健康有益的娱乐

  • ...a very decent and wholesome bunch of lads.

    一群非常正派、思想健康的少年

2 ADJ-GRADED (食物)有益健康的 If you describe food as wholesome, you approve of it because you think it is good for your health. (食物)有益健康的 [approval]

  • ...fresh, wholesome ingredients.

    新鲜的、有利于健康的烹饪材料

  • The food is filling and wholesome.

    这种食物既能充饥又有营养。

SYN healthy, good, strengthening, beneficial
