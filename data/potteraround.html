potter around
(also potter about)
PHRASAL VERB 悠闲地做些琐碎事；慢条斯理地干轻松活 If you potter around or potter about, you do pleasant but unimportant things, without hurrying. 悠闲地做些琐碎事；慢条斯理地干轻松活 [British]

  • I was perfectly happy just pottering around doing up my flat. [VERB PREP.]

    我特别愿意悠然自得地收拾公寓。

  • At weekends he would potter around the garden. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他周末会在花园里干些零碎活儿。

in AM, use 美国英语用 putter around
