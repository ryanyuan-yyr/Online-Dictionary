agog
əgɒg əˈgɑg
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 渴望的；急切的；热切期待的 If you are agog, you are excited about something, and eager to know more about it. 渴望的；急切的；热切期待的

  • The city was agog with rumours last night that the two had been executed.

    那两人已被处决的传言昨晚搞得全城沸沸扬扬。

SYN eager, excited, curious, enthusiastic
