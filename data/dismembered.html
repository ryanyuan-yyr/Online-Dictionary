dismember
dɪsmembəʳ dɪsˈmɛmbər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dismembers, present participle dismembering, past tense , past participle dismembered
1 VERB 肢解;分割…的躯体 To dismember the body of a dead person or animal means to cut or pull it into pieces. 肢解;分割…的躯体

  • He then dismembered her, hiding parts of her body in the cellar. [VERB noun]

    他接着将她肢解，将部分尸体藏在了地窖里。

  • His dismembered body was found in a rubbish bin. [VERB-ed]

    在垃圾桶里发现了他被肢解的尸体。

SYN cut into pieces, divide, rend [literary] , sever  
2 VERB 分割;瓜分 To dismember a country or organization means to break it up into smaller parts. 分割;瓜分

  • ...Hitler's plans to occupy and dismember Czechoslovakia. [VERB noun]

    希特勒占领并瓜分捷克斯洛伐克的计划

  • Many investors must be hoping that he will be dethroned, his publishing empire dismembered. [VERB noun]

    众多投资者此时一定希望他能被撵下台，他的出版帝国四分五裂。
