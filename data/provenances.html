provenance
prɒvɪnəns ˈprɑvənəns prəʊˈviːnɪəns
Word forms: plural provenances
N-VAR [usually with poss] 起源；来源；出处 The provenance of something is the place that it comes from or that it originally came from. 起源；来源；出处 [formal]

  • Kato was fully aware of the provenance of these treasures. [+ of]

    加藤完全清楚这些珍宝的来源。

  • He had no idea of its provenance.

    他不知道其出处。

SYN origin, source, birthplace, derivation
