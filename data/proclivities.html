proclivity
prəklɪvɪti proʊ- proʊˈklɪvəti
Word forms: plural proclivities
N-COUNT （常指不好的）倾向，偏好，嗜好 A proclivity is a tendency to behave in a particular way or to like a particular thing, often a bad way or thing. （常指不好的）倾向，偏好，嗜好 [formal]

  • He was indulging his own sexual proclivities.

    他沉浸在自己的过度纵欲中。

  • ...a proclivity to daydream.

    好做白日梦

SYN tendency, liking, leaning, inclination
