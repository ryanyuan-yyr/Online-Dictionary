passionless
pæʃənləs ˈpæʃənlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 冷淡的；没有激情的 If you describe someone or something as passionless, you mean that they do not have or show strong feelings. 冷淡的；没有激情的

  • ...a passionless academic.

    毫无激情的学者

  • ...their late and apparently passionless marriage.

    他们已经结束的那段明显毫无激情的婚姻

SYN unemotional, calm, neutral, restrained
