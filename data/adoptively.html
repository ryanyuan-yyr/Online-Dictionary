adoptive
ədɒptɪv əˈdɑptɪv
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 收养的；有收养关系的 Someone's adoptive family is the family that adopted them. 收养的；有收养关系的

  • He was brought up by adoptive parents in London.

    他由养父母在伦敦抚养长大。

  • ...her adoptive mother.

    她的养母

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 所选择居住的；移居的 Some-one's adoptive country or city is the one that they choose for their home, although they were not born there. 所选择居住的；移居的

  • They threatened to expel him from his adoptive country.

    他们威胁将他驱逐出他的移居国。

  • Galway in Ireland is my adoptive home.

    爱尔兰的戈尔韦是我的第二故乡。
