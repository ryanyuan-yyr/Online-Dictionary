pitch invasion pɪtʃ ɪnˈveɪʒən
Word forms: plural pitch invasions
N-COUNT 硬闯球场；涌入球场 If there is a pitch invasion during or after a football, rugby, or cricket match, fans run on to the pitch. 硬闯球场；涌入球场 [British]

  • There was a peaceful pitch invasion after Milan's eighth goal.

    米兰队攻入第八粒进球后，观众有序地涌进球场。
