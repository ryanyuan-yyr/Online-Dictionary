own brand
Word forms: plural own brands
N-COUNT （尤指连锁超市通常较为廉价的）自有品牌，自家品牌 Own brands are products which have the trademark or label of the shop which sells them, especially a supermarket chain. They are normally cheaper than other popular brands. （尤指连锁超市通常较为廉价的）自有品牌，自家品牌 [business]

  • This range is substantially cheaper than any of the other own brands available.

    这一系列的产品比所有其他超市的自有品牌都要便宜得多。

  • ...own-brand cola.

    自家品牌的可乐
