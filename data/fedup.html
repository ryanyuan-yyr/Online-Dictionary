fed up
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 厌倦的;厌烦的;腻味的 If you are fed up, you are unhappy, bored, or tired of something, especially something that you have been experiencing for a long time. 厌倦的;厌烦的;腻味的 [informal]

  • I am fed up with reading how women should dress to please men.

    我已经看腻了女人该如何穿戴来取悦男人的书。

  • He had become fed up with city life. [+ with]

    他已经厌倦了城市生活。

  • I'm just fed up and I don't know what to do.

    我就是腻味了，不知道该怎么办。

SYN cheesed off, down, depressed, bored
