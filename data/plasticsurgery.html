plastic surgery
N-UNCOUNT 整形外科 Plastic surgery is the practice of performing operations to repair or replace skin which has been damaged, or to improve people's appearance. 整形外科

  • She even had plastic surgery to change the shape of her nose.

    为改变鼻子的形状，她甚至接受了整形手术。

SYN cosmetic surgery, face lift [informal]
