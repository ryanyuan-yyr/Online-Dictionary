choice
tʃɔɪs tʃɔɪs
Word forms: plural choices, comparative choicer, superlative choicest
1 N-COUNT 选择范围;选择余地 If there is a choice of things, there are several of them and you can choose the one you want. 选择范围;选择余地

  • It's available in a choice of colours. [+ of]

    有多种颜色可供选择。

  • At lunchtime, there's a choice between the buffet or the set menu. [+ between]

    午餐可以吃自助餐，也可以选套餐。

  • Club Sportif offer a wide choice of holidays. [+ of]

    斯波蒂夫俱乐部提供多种度假选择。

SYN range, variety, selection, assortment  
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 选定的人(或物) Your choice is someone or something that you choose from a range of things. 选定的人(或物)

  • Although he was only grumbling, his choice of words made Rodney angry. [+ of]

    尽管他只是在咕哝，可是他的措词却让罗德妮恼火。

SYN selection, preference, election, pick  
3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 优选的;高品质的 Choice means of very high quality. 优选的;高品质的 [formal]

  • ...Fortnum and Mason's choicest chocolates.

    福楠—梅森百货商店的上等巧克力

SYN best, bad [slang] , special, prime  
4 PHRASE 不得不;只好 If you have no choice but to do something or have little choice but to do it, you cannot avoid doing it. 不得不;只好

  • They had little choice but to agree to what he suggested.

    他们别无选择，只好接受他的提议。

5 PHRASE …选中的;…看中的 The thing or person of your choice is the one that you choose. …选中的;…看中的

  • ...tickets to see the football team of your choice.

    观看自己喜欢的足球队比赛的门票

  • In many societies children still marry someone of their parents' choice.

    在许多社会，子女结婚仍然只能听从父母之命。

6 PHRASE 首选的;广受欢迎的 The item of choice is the one that most people prefer. 首选的;广受欢迎的

  • The drug is set to become the treatment of choice for asthma worldwide.

    这种药将会成为全世界治疗哮喘的首选。

  • The drink of choice seems to be vodka.

    最受欢迎的酒似乎是伏特加。
