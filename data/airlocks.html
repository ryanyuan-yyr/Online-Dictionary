airlock
eəʳlɒk ˈɛəˌlɒk
(also air lock)Word forms: plural airlocks
1 N-COUNT 气闸室 An airlock is a small room that is used to move between areas which do not have the same air pressure, for example in a spacecraft or submarine. 气闸室
2 N-COUNT 气塞；气闸 An airlock is a bubble of air in a pipe that prevents liquid from flowing through. 气塞；气闸
