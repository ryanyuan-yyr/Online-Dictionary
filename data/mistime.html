mistime
mɪstaɪm ˌmɪsˈtaɪm mɪsˈtaɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mistimes, present participle mistiming, past tense , past participle mistimed
VERB 在不适当的时候做(因而无法成功) If you mistime something, you do it at the wrong time, so that it is not successful. 在不适当的时候做(因而无法成功)

  • You're bound to mistime a tackle every so often. [VERB noun]

    有时候你肯定会错估铲球的时机。

  • ...a certain mistimed comment. [VERB-ed]

    不合时宜的评论
