been
bɪn biːn biːn bɪn bɪn bɛn bin
1 Been is the past participle of be .
2 VERB 曾经到过（某地） If you have been to a place, you have gone to it or visited it. 曾经到过（某地）

  • He's already been to Tunisia, and is to go on to Morocco and Mauritania. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他已经去过突尼斯了，还要去摩洛哥和毛里塔尼亚。

  • I've been there before. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我以前去过那儿。
