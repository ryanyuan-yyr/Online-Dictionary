burglary
bɜːʳgləri ˈbɜrgləri
Word forms: plural burglaries
N-VAR 入室盗窃（罪） If someone commits a burglary, they enter a building by force and steal things. Burglary is the act of doing this. 入室盗窃（罪）

  • An 11-year-old boy committed a burglary.

    一个11岁的小男孩犯了入室盗窃罪。

  • He's been arrested for burglary.

    他因入室盗窃而被捕。

SYN breaking and entering, housebreaking, break-in, home invasion [Australian , New Zealand]
