inexcusable
ɪnɪkskjuːzəbəl ˌɪnɛkˈskjuzəbəl ˌɪnɪkˈskjuzəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 无法辩解的;不可原谅的;不可宽恕的 If you say that something is inexcusable, you are emphasizing that it cannot be justified or tolerated because it is extremely bad. 无法辩解的;不可原谅的;不可宽恕的 [emphasis]

  • He said the killing of innocent people was inexcusable.

    他说滥杀无辜是不可原谅的。

  • The Home Office has been accused of inexcusable incompetence.

    内政部被指责能力极其低下。

SYN unforgivable, indefensible, unjustifiable, outrageous  
inexcusably

  • She had been inexcusably careless.

    她太粗心大意了。
