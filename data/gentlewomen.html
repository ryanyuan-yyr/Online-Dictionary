gentlewoman
dʒentəlwʊmən ˈdʒɛntəlˌwʊmən
Word forms: plural gentlewomen
N-COUNT 贵妇人；有教养的女人；淑女 A gentlewoman is a woman of high social standing, or a woman who is cultured, educated, and well-mannered. 贵妇人；有教养的女人；淑女 [old-fashioned]

  • She decided to set up a training scheme for 'gentlewomen who aspired to be teachers'.

    她决定为那些“渴望当教师的淑女们”制订一项培训计划。
