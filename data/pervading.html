pervade
pəʳveɪd pərˈveɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pervades, present participle pervading, past tense , past participle pervaded
VERB 贯穿；弥漫；渗透 If something pervades a place or thing, it is a noticeable feature throughout it. 贯穿；弥漫；渗透 [formal]

  • The smell of sawdust and glue pervaded the factory. [VERB noun]

    工厂里弥漫着锯屑和胶水的气味。

  • ...the corruption that pervades every stratum of the country. [VERB noun]

    渗透到社会各阶层的腐败

  • Throughout the book there is a pervading sense of menace. [VERB-ing]

    全书弥漫着一种危险的气息。

SYN spread through, fill, affect, penetrate
