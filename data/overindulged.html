overindulge
oʊvərɪndʌldʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overindulges, present participle overindulging, past tense , past participle overindulged
VERB 过于沉溺（于）；（通常指）过多地吃（或喝） If you overindulge, or overindulge in something that you like very much, usually food or drink, you allow yourself to have more of it than is good for you. 过于沉溺（于）；（通常指）过多地吃（或喝）

  • We all overindulge occasionally. [VERB]

    我们偶尔都会放纵一下自己。

  • Don't abuse your body by overindulging in alcohol. [VERB + in]

    不要纵酒伤身。

SYN drink or eat too much, overdo it, live it up [informal] , pig out [slang]
