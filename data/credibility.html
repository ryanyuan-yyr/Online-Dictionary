credibility
kredɪbɪlɪti ˌkrɛdɪˈbɪlɪtɪ
1 N-UNCOUNT 信用;可信性;可靠性 If someone or something has credibility, people believe in them and trust them. 信用;可信性;可靠性

  • The police have lost their credibility.

    警方已经失去了公众的信任。

  • The president will have to work hard to restore his credibility.

    总统将不得不努力使公众恢复对他的信任。

SYN believability, reliability, cred [slang] , plausibility
