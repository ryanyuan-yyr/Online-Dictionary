polemic
pəlemɪk pəˈlɛmɪk poʊˈlɛmɪk pəˈlɛmɪk
Word forms: plural polemics
1 N-VAR （书面或口头的）争辩，辩论，论战 A polemic is a very strong written or spoken attack on, or defence of, a particular belief or opinion. （书面或口头的）争辩，辩论，论战

  • ...a polemic against the danger of secret societies.

    抨击秘密社团危险性的论辩

  • The book is both a history and a passionate polemic for tolerance.

    这本书既是一段历史，也是一篇主张宽容的激情论辩。

SYN argument, attack, debate, dispute  
2 N-UNCOUNT 争辩术；辩论法 Polemics is the skill or practice of arguing very strongly for or against a belief or opinion. 争辩术；辩论法

  • He enjoys polemics, persuasion, and controversy.

    他喜欢辩论、说服和争议。
