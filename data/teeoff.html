tee off
1 PHRASAL VERB 激怒;惹恼 If someone or something tees you off, they make you angry or annoyed. 激怒;惹恼 [mainly US, informal]

  • Something the boy said to him teed him off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    不知那个男孩说了些什么，惹恼了他。

  • That really teed off the old boy. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那可确实激怒了那个老家伙。

2 PHRASAL VERB (高尔夫运动中)开球，发球 In golf, when you tee off, you hit the ball from a tee at the start of a hole. (高尔夫运动中)开球，发球

  • In a few hours time most of the world's top golfers tee off in the US Masters.

    再过几个小时，世界上的大多数顶级高尔夫球手将在美国大师赛上开球。
