go with
1 PHRASAL VERB 与…一起存在；与…不可分离 If one thing goes with another thing, the two things officially belong together, so that if you get one, you also get the other. 与…一起存在；与…不可分离

  • ...the lucrative $250,000 salary that goes with the job. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该工作提供的 25 万美元的优厚薪水

2 PHRASAL VERB 伴随；与…相伴而生 If one thing goes with another thing, it is usually found or experienced together with the other thing. 伴随；与…相伴而生

  • For many women, the status which goes with being a wife is important. [VERB PREP. noun]

    对很多女性而言，为人之妻的名分很重要。

3 PHRASAL VERB [VERB PREPOSITION n] 与（异性）交往；和…谈恋爱（或保持性关系） If someone is going with another person, they are having a sexual or romantic relationship with that person. 与（异性）交往；和…谈恋爱（或保持性关系） [informal]
