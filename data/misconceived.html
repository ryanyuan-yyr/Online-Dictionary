misconceived
mɪskənsiːvd
ADJ-GRADED (计划、方法)设想错误的，不合适的 If you describe a plan or method as misconceived, you mean it is not the right one for dealing with a particular problem or situation. (计划、方法)设想错误的，不合适的

  • The teachers say the tests for 14-year-olds are misconceived.

    老师们说这些测试并不适合14岁的孩子。

  • ...Lawrence's worthy but misconceived idea.

    劳伦斯值得一提但考虑不周的想法
