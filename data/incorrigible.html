incorrigible
ɪnkɒrɪdʒəbəl -kɔːr- ɪnˈkɔrədʒəbəl ɪnˈkɑrədʒəbəl
ADJ (通常幽默)屡教不改的，不可改造的 If you tell someone they are incorrigible, you are saying, often in a humorous way, that they have faults which will never change. (通常幽默)屡教不改的，不可改造的

  • 'Sue, you are incorrigible!' he said.

    “休,你真是不可救药！”他说道。

  • Gamblers are incorrigible optimists.

    嗜赌的人是死不悔改的乐天派。

SYN incurable, hardened, hopeless, intractable
