hawkish
hɔːkɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED （政客或政府）鹰派的，主战的，主张采取强硬政策的 Journalists use hawkish to describe politicians or governments who are in favour of using force to achieve something, rather than using peaceful and diplomatic methods. （政客或政府）鹰派的，主战的，主张采取强硬政策的

  • He is one of the most hawkish members of the new cabinet.

    他是新内阁中鹰派色彩最浓的成员之一。
