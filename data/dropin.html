drop in
PHRASAL VERB 顺道拜访;突然拜访 If you drop in on someone, you visit them informally, usually without having arranged it. 顺道拜访;突然拜访

  • Whenever I'm up there I always drop in. [VERB PREP.]

    每次我到那儿都会顺便去看看。

  • Why not drop in for a chat?. [VERB PREP.]

    为什么不过来聊聊天呢？

  • She spent most of the day dropping in on friends in Edinburgh. [VERB PREP. + on]

    她一天的大部分时间都用来拜访在爱丁堡的朋友了。

SYN visit, call, stop, turn up

  • ...a drop-in centre for young mothers.
