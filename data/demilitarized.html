demilitarize
diːmɪlɪtəraɪz diˈmɪlətəˌraɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense demilitarizes, present participle demilitarizing, past tense , past participle demilitarized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 demilitarise
1 VERB 使(某一地区)非军事化;撤出(某一地区)的全部军事力量 To demilitarize an area means to ensure that all military forces are removed from it. 使(某一地区)非军事化;撤出(某一地区)的全部军事力量

  • He said the UN had made remarkable progress in demilitarizing the region.

    他说联合国在促使该地区非军事化的进程中已经取得了显著进展。

  • The area could be turned into a demilitarized zone. [VERB-ed]

    该地区可能会成为非军事区。
