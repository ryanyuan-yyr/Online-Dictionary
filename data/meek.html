meek
miːk mik
Word forms: comparative meeker, superlative meekest
ADJ-GRADED 温顺的;顺从的 If you describe a person as meek, you think that they are gentle and quiet, and likely to do what other people say. 温顺的;顺从的

  • He was a meek, mild-mannered fellow.

    他是一个温顺随和的人。

  • ...the meekest and most docile of people.

    众人之中最温顺驯良的一个

SYN submissive, soft, yielding, gentle  
The meek are people who are meek.温顺的人

  • The meek shall inherit the earth.

    谦卑的人必承受地土。
