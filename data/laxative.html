laxative
læksətɪv ˈlæksətɪv
Word forms: plural laxatives
1 N-MASS 泻剂；通便剂 A laxative is food or medicine that you take to make you go to the toilet. 泻剂；通便剂

  • Foods that ferment quickly in the stomach are excellent natural laxatives.

    能在肠胃里迅速发酵的食物是绝佳的天然通便剂。

SYN purgative, salts, purge, cathartic  
2 ADJ-GRADED 有泻剂作用的；有通便作用的 A laxative food or medicine is one that you take to make you go to the toilet. 有泻剂作用的；有通便作用的

  • The artificial sweetener sorbitol has a laxative effect.

    人工甜味剂山梨糖醇有通便作用。

  • Molasses are mildly laxative and something of a general tonic.

    糖蜜有些许通便作用，兼具强身功能。
