garland
gɑːʳlənd ˈgɑrlənd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural garlands, present participle garlanding, past tense , past participle garlanded
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 花环；花冠；环状花饰 A garland is a circular decoration made from flowers and leaves. People sometimes wear garlands of flowers on their heads or around their necks. 花环；花冠；环状花饰

  • They wore garlands of summer flowers in their hair. [+ of]

    他们头戴用夏季鲜花编成的花环。

SYN wreath, band, bays, crown  
2 VERB [usually passive] 用花环装饰；给…戴上花冠（或饰以花环） If people, places, or objects are garlanded, people hang garlands or similar decorations around them. 用花环装饰；给…戴上花冠（或饰以花环）

  • Players and officials were garlanded with flowers. [be V-ed with n]

    运动员和官员们戴着花环。

  • The wagons were decorated with flowers and pulled by garlanded horses. [VERB-ed]

    马车都用鲜花装点，由佩戴花环的几匹马拉着。

SYN adorn, crown, deck, festoon
