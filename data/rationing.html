rationing
ræʃənɪŋ ˈræʃənɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT （食物等的）配给机制 Rationing is the system of limiting the amount of food, water, petrol, or other necessary substances that each person is allowed to have or buy when there is not enough of them. （食物等的）配给机制

  • The municipal authorities here are preparing for food rationing.

    这里的市政当局正准备实施食物配给制度。

SYN restriction, control, regulation, limitation
