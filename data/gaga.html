gaga
gɑːgɑː ˈɡɑːɡɑː ˈgɑˌgɑ
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 糊涂的；迷糊的；（尤指）老朽的 If you say that someone is gaga, you mean that they cannot think clearly any more, especially because they are old. 糊涂的；迷糊的；（尤指）老朽的 [informal]

  • If you don't keep your brain working you go gaga.

    要是不常用脑子，你就会变得糊涂起来。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 着迷的；狂热的 If someone goes gaga over a person or thing, they like them very much. 着迷的；狂热的 [informal]

  • My daughter is just gaga over men with hairy chests.

    我女儿就是痴迷于那些胸毛浓密的男子。
