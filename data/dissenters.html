dissenter dɪsentəʳ dɪˈsɛntər
Word forms: plural dissenters
N-COUNT 持不同意见者;持异议者 Dissenters are people who say that they do not agree with something that other people agree with or that is official policy. 持不同意见者;持异议者

  • The Party does not tolerate dissenters in its ranks.

    该政党不允许其成员中存在异见分子。

SYN objector, dissident, nonconformist, protestant
