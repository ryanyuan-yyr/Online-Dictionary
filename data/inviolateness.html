inviolate
ɪnvaɪələt ɪnˈvaɪəlɪt ɪnˈvaɪəˌleɪt
ADJ 不受侵害的;未受损害的;不受影响的 If something is inviolate, it has not been or cannot be harmed or affected by anything. 不受侵害的;未受损害的;不受影响的 [formal]

  • We believed our love was inviolate.

    我们相信我们的爱情坚如磐石。

  • ...their retreat into an inviolate private domain.

    他们退回到一个不受侵害的秘密领地

SYN intact, whole, untouched, entire
