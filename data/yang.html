yang

N-UNCOUNT (中国哲学中的)阳In Chinese philosophy, yang is one of the two opposing principles whose interaction is believed to influence everything in the universe. Yang is positive, bright, and masculine while is negative, dark, and feminine. (中国哲学中的)阳
