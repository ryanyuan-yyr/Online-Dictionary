come up with
1 PHRASAL VERB 想出，提出(计划、想法等) If you come up with a plan or idea, you think of it and suggest it. 想出，提出(计划、想法等)

  • Several of the members have come up with suggestions of their own. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    有几位成员提出了自己的建议。

  • 30 years ago, scientists came up with the theory that protons and neutrons are composed of three smaller particles. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    30年前，科学家们提出了质子和中子是由三种更小的粒子组成的理论。

SYN produce, offer, provide, present  
2 PHRASAL VERB 设法拿出(所需钱款) If you come up with a sum of money, you manage to produce it when it is needed. 设法拿出(所需钱款)

  • If Warren can come up with the $15 million, we'll go to London. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    如果沃伦能拿出那1500万美元，我们就去伦敦。
