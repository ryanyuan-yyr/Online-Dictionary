nitty-gritty
nɪti grɪti ˈnɪtɪˈɡrɪtɪ ˈnɪtiˈgrɪti
(also nitty gritty)
N-SING 本质;实质;基本事实 If people get down to the nitty-gritty of a matter, situation, or activity, they discuss the most important, basic parts of it or facts about it. 本质;实质;基本事实 [informal]

  • The city's newspapers still attempt to get down to the nitty gritty of investigative journalism.

    该市报纸仍在试图厘清调查性新闻的实质。

  • ...the nitty gritty of everyday politics. [+ of]

    日常政治生活的本质

SYN basics, facts, reality, essentials
