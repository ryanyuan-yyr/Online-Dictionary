resurgent
rɪsɜːʳdʒənt rɪˈsɜrdʒənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 重新兴起的;复兴的,复苏的 You use resurgent to say that something is becoming stronger and more popular after a period when it has been weak and unimportant. 重新兴起的;复兴的,复苏的 [formal]

  • ...the threat from the resurgent nationalist movement.

    重新兴起的民族主义运动的威胁
