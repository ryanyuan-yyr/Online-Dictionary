moribund
mɒrɪbʌnd mɔːr- ˈmɔrɪˌbʌnd
ADJ-GRADED 垂死的;奄奄一息的;停滞不前的 If you des- cribe something as moribund, you mean that it is in a very bad condition. 垂死的;奄奄一息的;停滞不前的 [formal]

  • ...the moribund economy.

    毫无生机的经济

  • ...the moribund housing market.

    死气沉沉的房地产市场

  • The British music scene is nostalgic, decrepit and moribund.

    英国音乐呈现出感伤怀旧、暮气沉沉、行将就木的景象。

SYN declining, weak, waning, standing still
