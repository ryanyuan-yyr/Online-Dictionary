middling
mɪdəlɪŋ ˈmɪdlɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 中等的;普通的;不好不坏的 If you describe a quality such as the size of something as middling, you mean that it is average. 中等的;普通的;不好不坏的

  • The Beatles enjoyed only middling success until 1963.

    直到1963年，甲壳虫乐队还只是半红不紫。

  • ...a man of middling height.

    中等身材的男子

SYN moderate, medium, average, fair
