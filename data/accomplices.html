accomplice
əkʌmplɪs əˈkɒmplɪs əkɒm- əˈkɑmplɪs
Word forms: plural accomplices
N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 共犯;同谋;帮凶 Someone's accomplice is a person who helps them to commit a crime. 共犯;同谋;帮凶

  • The gunman escaped on a motorcycle being ridden by an accomplice.

    持枪歹徒坐上一辆由同伙驾驶的摩托车逃逸。

  • His accomplice was arrested after a high-speed police chase.

    经过警方的一番飞车追逐，他的同伙遭到逮捕。

SYN partner in crime, ally, associate, assistant
