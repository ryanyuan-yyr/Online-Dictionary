shirtsleeve
ʃɜːʳtsliːv ˈʃɜːtˌsliːv
Word forms: plural shirtsleeves
N-COUNT [usually plural] 衬衫的袖子 Shirtsleeves are the sleeves of a shirt. If a man is in shirtsleeves or in his shirtsleeves, he is wearing a shirt but not a jacket. 衬衫的袖子

  • He rolled up his shirtsleeves.

    他挽起了衬衫袖子。

  • Franklin went to work in his shirtsleeves.

    富兰克林穿着衬衫去上班了。
