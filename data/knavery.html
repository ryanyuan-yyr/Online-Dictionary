knave
neɪv neɪv
Word forms: plural knaves
1 N-COUNT 不诚实的人；无赖；恶棍 If someone calls a man a knave, they mean that he is dishonest and should not be trusted. 不诚实的人；无赖；恶棍 [old-fashioned, disapproval]
2 N-COUNT （纸牌游戏中jack的另一种说法） In card games, knave is another word for jack. （纸牌游戏中jack的另一种说法） [mainly British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 jack
