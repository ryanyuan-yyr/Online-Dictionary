alluring
əljʊərɪŋ əlʊrɪŋ əˈlʊrɪŋ æˈlʊrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 吸引人的；迷人的 Someone or something that is alluring is very attractive. 吸引人的；迷人的

  • Why are the contents of the next person's shopping trolley always more alluring than one's own?

    为什么旁边人购物车里的东西看起来总是比自己车里的更诱人呢？

  • ...the most alluring city in South-East Asia.

    东南亚地区最迷人的城市

SYN attractive, fascinating, enchanting, seductive  
alluringly

  • She turned and smiled alluringly at Douglas.

    她转过身对道格拉斯报以迷人的一笑。
