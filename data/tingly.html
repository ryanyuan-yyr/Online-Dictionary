tingly
tɪŋgli
1 ADJ-GRADED 刺痛的 If something makes your body feel tingly, it gives you a slight stinging feeling. 刺痛的

  • These lotions tend to give the skin a tingly sensation.

    这些润肤乳往往会使皮肤有刺痛感。

2 ADJ-GRADED 舒心的;惬意的;愉悦的 If something pleasant or exciting makes you feel tingly, it gives you a pleasant warm feeling. 舒心的;惬意的;愉悦的

  • He had a way of sounding so sincere. It made me warm and tingly.

    他的话听起来总是十分诚恳，让我感到既温暖又舒心。

  • I go all tingly when I think of what might happen.

    我一想到可能发生的事就满心欢喜。
