codify
koʊdɪfaɪ kɑːd- ˈkɑdəˌfaɪ ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense codifies, present participle codifying, past tense , past participle codified
VERB 编纂;整理;将(法规等)整理成典 If you codify a set of rules, you define them or present them in a clear and ordered way. 编纂;整理;将(法规等)整理成典

  • The latest draft of the agreement codifies the panel's decision. [VERB noun]

    最新的协议草案把专家小组的决定加以整理，形成了条文。

SYN systematize, catalogue, classify, summarize  
codification

  • The codification of the laws began in the 1840s.
