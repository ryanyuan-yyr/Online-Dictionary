hose down
PHRASAL VERB 用水管冲洗 When you hose something or someone down, you clean them using a hose. 用水管冲洗

  • In one driveway a chauffeur wearing rubber boots was hosing down a limousine. [VERB PREP. noun]

    其中一条私人车道上，一位穿着橡胶靴的专职司机正用水管冲洗一辆豪华轿车。

  • When the children come in covered in sand you can just hose them down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    当孩子全身沾满沙子进门时，你可以直接用水龙带将他们冲洗干净。
