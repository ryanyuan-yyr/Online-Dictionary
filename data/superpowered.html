superpower
suːpəʳpaʊəʳ ˈsupərˌpaʊər
Word forms: plural superpowers
N-COUNT 超级大国 A superpower is a very powerful and influential country, usually one that is rich and has nuclear weapons. 超级大国

  • The United States could claim to be both a military and an economic superpower.

    美国在军事和经济上都称得上是超级大国。
