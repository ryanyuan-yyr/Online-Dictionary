momentarily
moʊmənteərɪli ˈməʊməntərəlɪ ˌmoʊmənˈtɛrəli
1 ADV [usually ADVERB with verb] 片刻地;短暂地 Momentarily means for a short time. 片刻地;短暂地 [mainly British, written]

  • She paused momentarily when she saw them.

    看到他们，她停了片刻。

SYN briefly, for a moment, temporarily, for a second  
2 ADV [usually ADVERB after verb] 不久;很快 Momentarily means very soon. 不久;很快 [US]

  • The Senate Judiciary Committee is expected to vote momentarily on his nomination to the Supreme Court.

    参议院司法委员会有望很快就提名他进入最高法院进行投票。
