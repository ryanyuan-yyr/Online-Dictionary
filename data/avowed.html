avowed
əvaʊd əˈvaʊd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 宣称的；坦率承认的 If you are an avowed supporter or opponent of something, you have declared that you support it or oppose it. 宣称的；坦率承认的 [formal]

  • She is an avowed vegetarian.

    她宣称是个素食主义者。

SYN declared, open, admitted, acknowledged  
avowedly

  • He remained for some years avowedly radical in his political outlook.

    他多年来保持公然激进的政治观。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （信念、目标）公开宣布的 An avowed belief or aim is one that you have declared formally or publicly. （信念、目标）公开宣布的 [formal]

  • ...the council's avowed intention to stamp on racism.

    理事会宣布要消灭种族歧视
