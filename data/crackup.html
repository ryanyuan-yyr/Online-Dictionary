crack up
1 PHRASAL VERB (因精神压力大而)垮掉，崩溃 If someone cracks up, they are under such a lot of emotional strain that they become mentally ill. (因精神压力大而)垮掉，崩溃 [informal]

  • She would have cracked up if she hadn't allowed herself some fun. [VERB PREP.]

    她若是没给自己找点乐趣的话，早就崩溃了。

2 PHRASAL VERB (使)大笑 If you crack up or if someone or something cracks you up, you laugh a lot. (使)大笑 [informal]

  • She told stories that cracked me up and I swore to write them down so you could enjoy them too. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她讲了几个令我捧腹大笑的故事，我一定要写下来，好让你也乐乐。

  • We all just cracked up laughing. [VERB PREP.]

    我们都不禁哈哈大笑起来。

SYN burst out laughing, laugh, fall about (laughing), guffaw
