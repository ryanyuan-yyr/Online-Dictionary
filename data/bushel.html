bushel
bʊʃəl ˈbʊʃəl ˈbʊʃəl
Word forms: plural bushels
N-COUNT 蒲式耳（农作物的容量单位，相当于8加仑） A bushel is a unit of volume that is used for measuring agricultural produce such as corn or beans. A bushel is equivalent in volume to eight gallons. 蒲式耳（农作物的容量单位，相当于8加仑）
If you hide your light under a bushel, you keep your abilities or good qualities hidden from other people.不露锋芒；（才华）藏而不露
