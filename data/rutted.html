rutted
rʌtɪd ˈrʌtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [oft adverb ADJ] (道路)有车辙的 A rutted road or track is very uneven because it has long, deep, narrow marks in it made by the wheels of vehicles. (道路)有车辙的

  • ...an uncomfortable ride along deeply rutted roads.

    沿着有很深车辙的道路行驶的颠簸旅程

SYN grooved, cut, marked, scored
