creepy
kriːpi ˈkripi
Word forms: comparative creepier, superlative creepiest
ADJ-GRADED 令人紧张不安的;令人毛骨悚然的 If you say that something or someone is creepy, you mean they make you feel very nervous or frightened. 令人紧张不安的;令人毛骨悚然的 [informal]

  • There were certain places that were really creepy at night.

    有些地方晚上真是让人不寒而栗。

  • Was she still married to that creepy guy, Dennis?

    她和那个让人毛骨悚然的家伙丹尼斯还没离婚吗？

SYN disturbing, threatening, frightening, terrifying
