basketball
bɑːskɪtbɔːl bæs- ˈbæskɪtˌbɔl ˈbɑskɪtˌbɔl
Word forms: plural basketballs
1 N-UNCOUNT 篮球运动 Basketball is a game in which two teams of five players each try to score goals by throwing a large ball through a circular net fixed to a metal ring at each end of the court. 篮球运动
2 N-COUNT 篮球 A basketball is a large ball which is used in the game of basketball. 篮球
