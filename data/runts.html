runt
rʌnt rʌnt
Word forms: plural runts
1 N-COUNT （一胎中）最弱小的仔畜 The runt of a group of animals born to the same mother at the same time is the smallest and weakest of them. （一胎中）最弱小的仔畜

  • Animals reject the runt of the litter. [+ of]

    动物会丢弃最弱小的幼仔。

2 N-COUNT （表厌恶或不满）矮子 You call a small person a runt when you are annoyed with them and are expressing your dislike for them. （表厌恶或不满）矮子 [British, informal, offensive, disapproval]

  • You little runt!.

    你这个小矬子！

  • My research owes nothing to anybody, least of all to a little runt like you.

    我的研究不归功于任何人，尤其不会归功于像你这样的小矮子。
