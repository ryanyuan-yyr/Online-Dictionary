enrolment
ɪnroʊlmənt
Word forms: plural enrolments
in AM, use 美国英语用 enrollment
1 N-UNCOUNT 登记;注册 Enrolment is the act of enrolling at an institution or on a course. 登记;注册

  • A fee is charged for each year of study and is payable at enrolment.

    学费每年收取，可在登记注册时缴纳。

SYN enlistment, admission, acceptance, engagement  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 登记人数;注册人数;入学人数 An enrolment is the number of people who are enrolled at an institution or on a course. 登记人数;注册人数;入学人数

  • By 1922 the public schools had an enrolment of nearly 16,000 students.

    到1922年，公学的注册学生数接近16,000。

SYN enlistment, admission, acceptance, engagement
