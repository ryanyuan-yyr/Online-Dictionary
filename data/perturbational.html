perturbation
pɜːʳtəʳbeɪʃən ˌpɜːtəˈbeɪʃən pəˈtɜːbəns ˌpɜrtərˈbeɪʃən
Word forms: plural perturbations
1 N-VAR （尤指不常出现的）小变化 A perturbation is a small change in the movement, quality, or behaviour of something, especially an unusual change. （尤指不常出现的）小变化 [technical]

  • ...perturbations in Jupiter's gravitational field. [+ in]

    木星重力场的微小变化

2 N-UNCOUNT （某事引起的）忧虑，不安，烦恼 Perturbation is worry caused by some event. （某事引起的）忧虑，不安，烦恼 [formal]

  • This message caused perturbation in the Middle East Headquarters.

    这则消息在中东总部引起了不安。
