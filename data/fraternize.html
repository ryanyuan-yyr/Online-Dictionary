fraternize
frætəʳnaɪz ˈfrætərˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fraternizes, present participle fraternizing, past tense , past participle fraternized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 fraternise
1 V-RECIP 和…亲如兄弟;友善交往 If you fraternize with someone, you associate with them in a friendly way. 和…亲如兄弟;友善交往

  • At these conventions, executives fraternized with the key personnel of other banks. [VERB + with]

    在这些集会上，主管们和其他银行的重要人员友善往来。

  • Mrs Zuckerman does not fraternize widely. [VERB]

    朱克曼夫人结交并不广泛。

  • The recession has created an atmosphere where disparate groups fraternise in an atmosphere of mutual support. [VERB]

    大萧条使得不同集团在友爱互助的气氛中如兄弟般亲密往来。

SYN associate, mix, socialize, keep company
