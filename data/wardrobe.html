wardrobe
wɔːʳdroʊb ˈwɔrˌdroʊb
Word forms: plural wardrobes
1 N-COUNT 衣柜;衣橱 A wardrobe is a tall cupboard or cabinet in which you can hang your clothes. 衣柜;衣橱
2 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] (某人的)全部服装 Someone's wardrobe is the total collection of clothes that they have. (某人的)全部服装

  • Her wardrobe consists primarily of huge cashmere sweaters and tiny Italian sandals.

    她的衣物主要是宽大的开司米毛衣和小巧的意大利凉鞋。

3 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] (剧团演员的)全部戏装，行头 The wardrobe in a theatre company is the actors' and actresses' costumes. (剧团演员的)全部戏装，行头

  • In the wardrobe department were rows of costumes.

    戏装部有一排排的戏装。
