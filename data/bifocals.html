bifocals
baɪfoʊkəlz baɪˈfəʊkəlz ˈbaɪˌfoʊkəlz
The form bifocal is used as a modifier. bifocal 用作修饰语。
N-PLURAL 双光眼镜；远近两用眼镜 Bifocals are glasses with lenses made in two halves. The top part is for looking at things some distance away, and the bottom part is for reading and looking at things that are close. 双光眼镜；远近两用眼镜

  • Mrs Bierce wears thick bifocal lenses.

    比耶尔斯太太戴着厚厚的双光眼镜。
