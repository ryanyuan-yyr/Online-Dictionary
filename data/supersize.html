supersize
suːpərsaɪz ˈsuːpəˌsaɪz ˈsupərˌsaɪz
1 ADJ 超大的Supersize or supersized things are very large. 超大的

  • A supersize portion of fries contains 600 calories.

    一个超大份炸薯条含600卡路里。

  • ...a supersized mug of coffee.

    ...一杯超大杯的咖啡。

2 V-T (快餐店)加量If a fast-food restaurant supersizes a portion of food, it offers the customer a larger portion. (快餐店)加量

  • Fast-food restaurants encourage people to supersize their orders.

    快餐店鼓励人们加大他们的点餐量。
