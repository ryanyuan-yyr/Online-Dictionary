barmy
bɑːʳmi ˈbɑrmi
Word forms: comparative barmier, superlative barmiest
ADJ-GRADED 有点疯的；傻头傻脑的；愚蠢的 If you say that someone or something is barmy, you mean that they are slightly crazy or very foolish. 有点疯的；傻头傻脑的；愚蠢的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • Bill used to say I was barmy, and that would really get to me.

    比尔过去老说我傻，这话确实伤到我了。

  • This policy is absolutely barmy.

    这项政策愚蠢至极。

  • ...a barmy idea.

    一个近乎疯狂的想法

SYN stupid, bizarre, foolish, silly
