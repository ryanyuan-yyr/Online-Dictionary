repugnant
rɪpʌgnənt rɪˈpʌgnənt
ADJ-GRADED 让人反感的;令人厌恶的 If you think that something is horrible and disgusting, you can say that it is repugnant . 让人反感的;令人厌恶的 [formal]

  • The odour of vitamin in skin is repugnant to insects. [+ to]

    昆虫很讨厌皮肤内维生素的气味。

  • The Committee said his actions were improper and repugnant.

    委员会称他行为失当，令人反感。

SYN distasteful, offensive, foul, disgusting  
repugnance

  • She felt a deep sense of shame and repugnance.

    她深感羞耻和厌恶。
