reactivate
riæktɪveɪt rɪˈæktɪˌveɪt riˈæktəˌveɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reactivates, present participle reactivating, past tense , past participle reactivated
VERB 使恢复工作;使恢复活力;重启 If people reactivate a system or organization, they make it work again after a period in which it has not been working. 使恢复工作;使恢复活力;重启

  • ...a series of economic reforms to reactivate the economy. [VERB noun]

    一系列旨在重振经济的经济改革

  • It was also agreed to reactivate two joint committees on negotiations. [VERB noun]

    磋商中还达成了重启两个联委会的决议。
