appetizing
æpɪtaɪzɪŋ ˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 appetising
ADJ-GRADED 色香俱佳的；引起食欲的 Appetizing food looks and smells good, so that you want to eat it. 色香俱佳的；引起食欲的

  • ...the appetising smell of freshly baked bread.

    新鲜出炉的面包发出的诱人香味

  • Kippers are dyed yellow to make them more appetizing.

    烟熏鲱鱼被腌成了黄色，令人垂涎欲滴。

SYN delicious, appealing, inviting, tempting  
appetizingly

  • The tables were simply and appetisingly laid out.

    餐桌摆好了，食物虽然简单但很能激起食欲。
