countrified
kʌntrɪfaɪd ˈkʌntrəˌfaɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 像乡下的;乡土气息的;土里土气的 You use countrified to describe something that seems or looks like something in the country, rather than in a town. 像乡下的;乡土气息的;土里土气的

  • The house was so handsome, with a lovely countrified garden.

    这座房子很漂亮，带一个田园风情的美丽花园。

SYN rural, pastoral, picturesque, rustic  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (流行音乐)乡村风味的，有乡土乐特征的 Countrified is used to describe pop music that sounds similar to country music. (流行音乐)乡村风味的，有乡土乐特征的 [journalism]

  • The sound veers between jazz and countrified blues.

    音乐在爵士乐和乡村布鲁斯乐之间来回转换。
