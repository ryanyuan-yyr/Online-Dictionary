catwalk
kætwɔːk ˈkætˌwɔk
Word forms: plural catwalks
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 时装表演台;T型台 At a fashion show, the catwalk is a narrow platform that models walk along to display clothes. 时装表演台;T型台
2 N-COUNT (楼房、大型建筑外或舞台上方的)狭窄天桥，高空走道 A catwalk is a narrow bridge high in the air, for example between two parts of a tall building, on the outside of a large structure, or over a stage. (楼房、大型建筑外或舞台上方的)狭窄天桥，高空走道
