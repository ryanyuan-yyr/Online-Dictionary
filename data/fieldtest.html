field-test
ˈfiːldtɛst ˈfɪɛldˌtɛst
(also field test)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense field-tests, present participle field-testing, past tense , past participle field-tested
VERB 对(新设备等)做现场试验 If you field-test a new piece of equipment, you test it in a real, natural environment. 对(新设备等)做现场试验

  • We've field-tested them ourselves and are happy that they work. [VERB noun]

    我们自己对其进行了实地试验，很高兴没有出现问题。

Field test is also a noun.

  • Field tests are to be carried out at Blair Castle and Burghley.

    现场测试将在布莱尔城堡和伯利庄园进行。
