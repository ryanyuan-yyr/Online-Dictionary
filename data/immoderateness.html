immoderate
ɪmɒdərət ɪˈmɑdərɪt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 极端的;不合理的 If you describe something as immoderate, you disapprove of it because it is too extreme. 极端的;不合理的 [formal, disapproval]

  • He launched an immoderate tirade on Turner.

    他针对特纳发表了一篇极端激愤的长篇演说。

SYN excessive, extreme, over the top [slang] , enormous
