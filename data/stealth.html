stealth stelθ stɛlθ
N-UNCOUNT [oft by NOUN] 暗中行动;偷偷摸摸;鬼鬼祟祟 If you use stealth when you do something, you do it quietly and carefully so that no one will notice what you are doing. 暗中行动;偷偷摸摸;鬼鬼祟祟

  • He claimed Tony Blair is trying to get us into the euro by stealth.

    他声称托尼·布莱尔正试图神不知鬼不觉地让我们加入欧元体系。

  • Both sides advanced by stealth.

    双方都暗中向前推进。

SYN secrecy, furtiveness, slyness, sneakiness
