gang up
PHRASAL VERB 结伙，拉帮结派，联合起来（对抗某人） If people gang up on someone, they unite against them for a particular reason, for example in a fight or argument. 结伙，拉帮结派，联合起来（对抗某人） [informal]

  • Harrison complained that his colleagues ganged up on him. [VERB PREP. + on]

    哈里森抱怨说他的同事联手对付他。

  • All the other parties ganged up to keep them out of power. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    其他政党全部联合起来使他们无法掌权。

  • All the girls in my class seemed to gang up against me. [V P + against]

    我们班的女生好像都联合起来对付我。
