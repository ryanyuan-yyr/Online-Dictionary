heckle
hekəl ˈhɛkəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense heckles, present participle heckling, past tense , past participle heckled
VERB 对…起哄;诘问 If people in an audience heckle public speakers or performers, they interrupt them, for example by making rude remarks. 对…起哄;诘问

  • They heckled him and interrupted his address with angry questions. [VERB noun]

    他们冲他起哄，以激愤的问题打断他的发言。

  • He was insulted and heckled mercilessly. [VERB noun]

    他遭到无情的羞辱和诘问。

  • A small group of youths stayed behind to heckle and shout abuse. [VERB]

    一小群小青年没走，在起哄、叫骂。

SYN jeer, interrupt, shout down, disrupt  
Heckle is also a noun.

  • The offending comment was in fact a heckle from an audience member.

    这番冒犯性的话实际上是一名观众的诘难。

heckling

    仪式因前所未有的起哄和喊口号声而中断。

heckler

    他刚开始演讲，一名起哄者就大声地质问他对枪械管制的看法。
