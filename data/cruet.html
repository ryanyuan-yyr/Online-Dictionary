cruet
kruːɪt ˈkruɪt
Word forms: plural cruets
1 N-COUNT (餐桌上的)调味品瓶，一组调味品瓶 A cruet is a small container, or set of containers, for salt, pepper, or mustard which is used at meals. (餐桌上的)调味品瓶，一组调味品瓶 [British]

  • ...a cruet set.

    一组调味品瓶

2 N-COUNT (餐桌上盛油或醋的)调味品瓶 A cruet is a small glass bottle that contains oil or vinegar and is used at the table at meals. (餐桌上盛油或醋的)调味品瓶 [US]
