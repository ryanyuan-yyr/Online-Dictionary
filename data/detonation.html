detonation
detəneɪʃən
Word forms: plural detonations
1 N-COUNT （大）爆炸 A detonation is a large or powerful explosion. （大）爆炸 [formal]
2 N-UNCOUNT 引爆;起爆 Detonation is the action of causing a device such as a bomb to explode. 引爆;起爆 [formal]

  • ...accidental detonation of nuclear weapons.

    核武器的意外引爆

SYN blowing-up, explosion, discharge
