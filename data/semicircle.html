semi-circle
(also semicircle)Word forms: plural semi-circles
1 N-COUNT 半圆;半圆弧;半圆形 A semi-circle is one half of a circle, or something having the shape of half a circle. 半圆;半圆弧;半圆形

  • They stood in a semi-circle round the teacher's chair and answered questions.

    他们围着老师的椅子站成半圆形回答问题。
