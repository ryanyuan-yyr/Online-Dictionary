floret
flɒrɪt ˈflɔːrɪt ˈflɔrɪt ˈfloʊrɪt
Word forms: plural florets
1 N-COUNT (复花花序的)小花 On a flowering plant, a floret is a small flower that is part of a larger flower. (复花花序的)小花
2 N-COUNT (花椰菜等蔬菜的)花部 On vegetables such as broccoli and cauliflower, a floret is one of the small, flower-shaped pieces which make up the part of the vegetable that you eat. (花椰菜等蔬菜的)花部
