objectionable
əbdʒekʃənəbəl əbˈdʒɛkʃənəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 令人不快的;令人反感的;讨厌的 If you describe someone or something as objectionable, you consider them to be extremely offensive and unacceptable. 令人不快的;令人反感的;讨厌的 [formal]

  • I don't like your tone young woman, in fact I find it highly objectionable.

    小姑娘，我不喜欢你说话的口气，实际上我觉得这让人非常不快。

  • Such power is politically dangerous and morally objectionable.

    这样的权力在政治上极其危险，在道义上也令人反感。

SYN offensive, annoying, irritating, unacceptable
