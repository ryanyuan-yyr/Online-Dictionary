demo
demoʊ ˈdɛmoʊ
Word forms: plural demos
1 N-COUNT 示威;示威游行（或集会） A demo is a demonstration by a group of people to show their opposition to something or their support for something. 示威;示威游行（或集会） [British, informal]

  • ...an anti-racist demo.

    反种族主义示威

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 试样唱片;录音样带 A demo is a CD or tape with a sample of someone's music recorded on it. 试样唱片;录音样带 [informal]

  • He listened to one of my demo tapes.

    他听了我的一盘录音样带。

  • Send us a demo with one or two of your best songs.

    把你最好的一两首歌的小样寄给我们。
