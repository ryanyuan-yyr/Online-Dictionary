shank
ʃæŋk ʃæŋk
Word forms: plural shanks
1 N-COUNT (物体)长而细直的部分，杆，柄 The shank of an object is the long, thin, straight part of the object. (物体)长而细直的部分，杆，柄

  • These hooks are sharp with long shanks.

    这些钩身长的鱼钩很锋利。

  • ...the shank of the club.

    球杆的长柄

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 小腿;胫;(尤指)小腿肉 Shanks are the lower parts of the legs; used especially with reference to meat. 小腿;胫;(尤指)小腿肉

  • Turn the shanks and baste them once or twice as they cook.

    将小腿肉翻一下，烹调时在上面抹一两次油。
