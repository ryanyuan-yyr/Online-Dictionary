upswing
ʌpswɪŋ ˈʌpˌswɪŋ ʌpˈswɪŋ
Word forms: plural upswings
N-COUNT [usually singular] 进步；提高；上升 An upswing is a sudden improvement in something such as an economy, or an increase in an amount or level. 进步；提高；上升

  • ...an upswing in the economy. [+ in]

    经济的迅速回升

  • Violent crime is on the upswing.

    暴力犯罪率正在攀升。
