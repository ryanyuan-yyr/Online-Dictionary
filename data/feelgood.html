feel-good
ˈfilˌgʊd
(also feelgood)
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (电影)令人愉悦的 A feel-good film is a film which presents people and life in a way which makes the people who watch it feel happy and optimistic. (电影)令人愉悦的

  • This could be the feel-good movie of the autumn.

    这可以称得上是今秋令人赏心悦目的影片了。

  • ...a bright and enjoyable feelgood romance.

    基调明快、让人乐观向上的爱情故事

2 PHRASE 愉悦感，对未来的乐观态度（新闻用语) When journalists refer to the feel-good factor, they mean that people are feeling hopeful and optimistic about the future. 愉悦感，对未来的乐观态度（新闻用语)

  • There were obvious signs of the feel-good factor in the last survey taken in the wake of the election result.

    选举结果公布后的最近一次调查显示，人们普遍对未来持乐观态度。
