winter
wɪntəʳ ˈwɪntər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural winters, present participle wintering, past tense , past participle wintered
1 N-VAR 冬天;冬季 Winter is the season between autumn and spring. In the winter the weather is usually cold. 冬天;冬季

  • In winter the nights are long and cold.

    冬夜漫长而寒冷。

  • ...the winter months.

    冬季月份

  • ...the late winter of 1941. [+ of]

    1941年冬末

2 V-ERG （使）(动植物)过冬 If an animal or plant winters somewhere or is wintered there, it spends the winter there. （使）(动植物)过冬 [Also VERB n preposition/adverb]

  • The birds will winter outside in an aviary. [VERB adv./prep.]

    鸟儿们将在外面的大鸟笼里过冬。

  • The young seedlings are usually wintered in a cold frame. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    幼苗通常都会放在冷床里越冬。

  • ...one of the most important sites for wintering wildfowl. [VERB-ing]

    最重要的野禽过冬地之一

3 VERB (人)过冬 If you winter somewhere, you spend the winter there. (人)过冬 [formal]

  • The family decided to winter in Nice again. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一家人决定还是到尼斯过冬。
