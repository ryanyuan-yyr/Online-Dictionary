whoop
hwuːp wuːp huːp hwup wup hup
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense whoops, present participle whooping, past tense , past participle whooped
1 VERB (高兴地)大喊，大叫 If you whoop, you shout loudly in a very happy or excited way. (高兴地)大喊，大叫 [written]

  • She whoops with delight at a promise of money. [VERB]

    她因为有可能获得一笔钱而高兴得大叫。

Whoop is also a noun.

  • Scattered groans and whoops broke out in the crowd.

    人群里不时发出几声抱怨和欢呼。
