reciprocal
rɪsɪprəkəl rɪˈsɪprəkəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 互相的；相应的；互惠的 A reciprocal action or agreement involves two people or groups who do the same thing to each other or agree to help each another in a similar way. 互相的；相应的；互惠的 [formal]

  • They expected a reciprocal gesture before more hostages could be freed.

    在更多人质获得自由之前他们期望看到对方也有相应的表示。

  • The department said many countries had reciprocal agreements for health care with Britain.

    该部门称许多国家和英国签订了医疗卫生互惠协议。

SYN mutual, corresponding, reciprocative, reciprocatory  
reciprocally

  • Both sides had reciprocally observed restraints.

    双方都遵守了限制性规定。
