coterie
koʊtəri ˈkoʊtəri
Word forms: plural coteries
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (志趣相投而排外的)小集团，小圈子 A coterie of a particular kind is a small group of people who are close friends or have a common interest, and who do not want other people to join them. (志趣相投而排外的)小集团，小圈子 [formal]

  • The songs he recorded were written by a small coterie of dedicated writers. [+ of]

    他录制的这些歌是由几名酷爱音乐的作曲家谱写的。

SYN clique, group, set, camp
