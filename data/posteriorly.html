posterior
pɒstɪəriəʳ pɒˈstɪərɪə pɑsˈtɪriər
Word forms: plural posteriors
1 N-COUNT 臀部；屁股 Some-one's bottom can be referred to as their posterior . 臀部；屁股 [mainly humorous]
SYN bottom [informal] , behind [informal] , bum [British , slang] , seat  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 背部的；后部的 Posterior describes something that is situated at the back of something else. 背部的；后部的 [medicine]

  • ...the posterior leg muscles.

    腿部后侧的肌肉
