artless
ɑːʳtləs ˈɑrtlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 单纯的；诚实的；不耍手段的 Someone who is artless is simple and honest, and does not think of deceiving other people. 单纯的；诚实的；不耍手段的

  • She was curiously artless.

    她单纯得令人难以置信。

  • ...Hemingway's artless air and charming smile.

    海明威的纯朴气质和迷人微笑

SYN natural, simple, fair, frank
