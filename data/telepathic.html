telepathic
telɪpæθɪk
ADJ 会传心术的;有读心能力的;能心灵感应的 If you believe that someone is telepathic, you believe that they have mental powers which cannot be explained by science, such as being able to communicate with other people's minds, and know what other people are thinking. 会传心术的;有读心能力的;能心灵感应的

  • About half the subjects considered themselves to be telepathic.

    实验对象中有一半左右自认为有心灵感应能力。

  • I could not know that. I'm not telepathic.

    那个我哪知道啊，我又不会心灵感应。

SYN psychic, clairvoyant, having sixth sense, having second sight  
telepathically

  • I used to communicate with her telepathically.

    我以前和她通过心灵感应交流。
