empire
empaɪəʳ ˈɛmˌpaɪr ɔmˈpiər oʊmˈpɪr ɑmˈpiər ɑmˈpɪr
Word forms: plural empires
1 N-COUNT 帝国 An empire is a number of individual nations that are all controlled by the government or ruler of one particular country. 帝国

  • ...the Roman Empire.

    罗马帝国

2 N-COUNT 企业王国;企业集团 You can refer to a group of companies controlled by one person as an empire . 企业王国;企业集团

  • ...the big Mondadori publishing empire.

    庞大的蒙达多利出版帝国
