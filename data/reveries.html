reverie
revəri ˈrɛvəri
Word forms: plural reveries
N-COUNT 幻想;白日梦 A reverie is a state of imagining or thinking about pleasant things, as if you are dreaming. 幻想;白日梦 [formal]

  • The announcer's voice brought Holden out of his reverie.

    播音员的声音将霍尔登从幻想中惊醒。

SYN daydream, musing, preoccupation, trance
