adhesive
ædhiːsɪv ədˈhiːsɪv ædˈhisɪv ædˈhizɪv ədˈhizɪv
Word forms: plural adhesives
1 N-MASS 黏合剂;胶黏剂 An adhesive is a substance such as glue, which is used to make things stick firmly together. 黏合剂;胶黏剂

  • Glue the mirror in with a strong adhesive.

    用强力胶将镜子固定到位。

SYN glue, cement, gum, paste  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 黏合的；黏着的；有附着力的 An adhesive substance is able to stick firmly to something else. 黏合的；黏着的；有附着力的

  • ...adhesive tape.

    胶带

SYN sticky, holding, sticking, attaching
