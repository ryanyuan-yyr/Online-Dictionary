solitude
sɒlɪtjuːd -tuːd ˈsɑləˌtud ˈsɑləˌtjud
N-UNCOUNT (尤指平静愉快的)独居，独处 Solitude is the state of being alone, especially when this is peaceful and pleasant. (尤指平静愉快的)独居，独处

  • He enjoyed his moments of solitude before the pressures of the day began.

    他喜欢在白天的压力到来之前享受独处的时光。

  • Imagine long golden beaches where you can wander in solitude.

    想象一下那长长的金色海滩吧，在那里你可以独自徜徉。

SYN isolation, privacy, seclusion, retirement
