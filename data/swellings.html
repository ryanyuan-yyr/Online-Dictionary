swelling
swelɪŋ ˈswɛlɪŋ
Word forms: plural swellings
N-VAR (身体因受伤或患病产生的)肿胀,浮肿 A swelling is a raised, curved shape on the surface of your body which appears as a result of an injury or an illness. (身体因受伤或患病产生的)肿胀,浮肿

  • His eye was partly closed, and there was a swelling over his lid.

    他的眼睛半闭着，眼睑上有一个肿块。

  • There is some swelling and he is being detained for observation.

    他身上有些浮肿，被留院观察。

SYN enlargement, lump, puffiness, bump
