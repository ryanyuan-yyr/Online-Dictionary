thorax
θɔːræks ˈθɔrˌæks
Word forms: plural thoraxes, plural thoraces/θɔːrəsiːz/
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 胸部;胸腔;胸廓 Your thorax is the part of your body between your neck and your waist. 胸部;胸腔;胸廓 [medicine]
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] (昆虫的)胸部，胸节 An insect's thorax is the central part of its body to which the legs and wings are attached. (昆虫的)胸部，胸节 [technical]
