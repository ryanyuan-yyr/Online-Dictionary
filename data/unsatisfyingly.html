unsatisfying
ʌnsætɪsfaɪɪŋ ʌnˈsætɪsˌfaɪɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 不令人满意的 If you find something unsatisfying, you do not get any satisfaction from it. 不令人满意的

  • Rose says so far the marriage has been unsatisfying.

    罗丝说到目前为止她的婚姻并不令人满意。

  • The boredom is caused as much by people's unsatisfying home lives as by lack of work.

    不幸福的家庭生活和失业都会导致心生倦怠。
