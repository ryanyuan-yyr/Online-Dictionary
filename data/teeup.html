tee up
PHRASAL VERB 将(高尔夫球)置于球座上 In golf, when you tee up a ball, you place it on a tee so that it is ready for you to hit it. 将(高尔夫球)置于球座上 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I bent down to tee up my ball. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我俯下身把球放在球座上。

  • I never dreamed that I'd tee up with Bob Hope. [VERB PREP.]

    我做梦也没想到能和鲍勃·霍普一起打高尔夫。
