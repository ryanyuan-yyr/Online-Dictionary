step down
(also step aside)
PHRASAL VERB 辞职;下台;让位 If someone steps down or steps aside, they resign from an important job or position, often in order to let someone else take their place. 辞职;下台;让位

  • Judge Ito said that if his wife was called as a witness, he would step down as trial judge. [VERB PREP. + as]

    伊藤法官说，如果他的妻子被传作证，他将不再担任初审法官。

  • Many would prefer to see him step aside in favour of a younger man. [VERB PREP.]

    很多人更希望看到他下台，以让位给更年轻的人。
