sacrilegious
sækrɪlɪdʒəs ˌsækrəˈlɪdʒəs
ADJ-GRADED 亵渎神圣的;大不敬的;无礼的 If someone's behaviour or actions are sacrilegious, they show great disrespect towards something holy or towards something that people think should be respected. 亵渎神圣的;大不敬的;无礼的

  • A number of churches were sacked and sacrilegious acts committed.

    很多教堂都被洗劫一空，而且作出了诸多渎神之举。

SYN profane, irreverent, blasphemous, unholy
