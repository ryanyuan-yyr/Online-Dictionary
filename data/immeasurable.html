immeasurable
ɪmeʒərəbəl ɪˈmɛʒərəbəl
ADJ 不可计量的;无边无际的;无限的 If you describe something as immeasurable, you are emphasizing how great it is. 不可计量的;无边无际的;无限的 [formal, emphasis]

  • I felt an immeasurable love for him.

    当时我无限深爱他。

  • His contribution is immeasurable.

    他居功至伟。

SYN incalculable, vast, immense, endless
