ex gratia
eks greɪʃə ˈɡreɪʃə ɛksˈgreɪʃiə
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （付款）作为恩惠的，非出于法律义务的 An ex gratia payment is one that is given as a favour or gift and not because it is legally necessary. （付款）作为恩惠的，非出于法律义务的 [mainly British, formal]

  • Otherwise, they would not feel compelled to make ex gratia payments to customers they have wronged.

    否则，他们不会觉得必须向那些没有受到公平待遇的顾客提供补偿款。
