bankroll
bæŋkroʊl ˈbæŋkˌrəʊl ˈbæŋkˌroʊl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bankrolls, present participle bankrolling, past tense , past participle bankrolled
1 VERB 为…提供资金；对…进行融资；资助 To bankroll a person, organization, or project means to provide the financial resources that they need. 为…提供资金；对…进行融资；资助 [mainly US, informal]

  • The company has bankrolled a couple of local movies. [VERB noun]

    这家公司已经资助了当地好几部电影。

2 N-SING 资金 A bankroll is the financial resources used to back a person, project, or institution. 资金 [US]

  • We have a guaranteed minimum bankroll of £1.7m over the five albums.

    对于这5张专辑，我们有至少170万英镑的保证资金。
