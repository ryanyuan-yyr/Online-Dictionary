refute
rɪfjuːt rɪˈfjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense refutes, present participle refuting, past tense , past participle refuted
1 VERB 驳倒，驳斥，批驳（观点、指责或理论） If you refute an argument, accusation, or theory, you prove that it is wrong or untrue. 驳倒，驳斥，批驳（观点、指责或理论） [formal]

  • It was the kind of rumour that it is impossible to refute. [VERB noun]

    这是那种让人根本没法批驳的谣言。

SYN disprove, counter, discredit, prove false  
2 VERB 否认（论点或指责） If you refute an argument or accusation, you say that it is not true. 否认（论点或指责） [formal]

  • Isabelle is quick to refute any suggestion of intellectual snobbery. [VERB noun]

    伊莎贝尔对于任何关于她恃才傲物的暗示都迅速予以否认。
