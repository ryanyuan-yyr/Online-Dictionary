hammer out
PHRASAL VERB （经长时间或艰难的讨论）达成（一致意见或协定） If people hammer out an agreement or treaty, they succeed in producing it after a long or difficult discussion. （经长时间或艰难的讨论）达成（一致意见或协定） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I think we can hammer out a solution. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我想我们可以通过讨论找到一个解决方案。

  • The new fixture package has been hammered out by the Premiership clubs in talks over the last ten days. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    新的一揽子赛事安排已由各英超俱乐部在过去10天的会谈中经反复商讨确定。

SYN work out, produce, finish, complete
