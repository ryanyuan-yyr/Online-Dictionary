ancien regime
ɑːnsjɒn reɪʒiːm
1 N-SING （法国1789年革命前的）旧制度 The an-cien regime was the political and social system in France before the revolution of 1789. （法国1789年革命前的）旧制度
2 N-SING 旧秩序；过时的制度 If a country has had the same political system for a long time and you disapprove of it, you can refer to it as the ancien regime . 旧秩序；过时的制度 [disapproval]
