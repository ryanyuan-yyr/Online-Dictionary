self-deprecating
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 自我贬低的;过分谦虚的 If you describe someone's behaviour as self-deprecating, you mean that they criticize themselves or represent themselves as foolish in a light-hearted way. 自我贬低的;过分谦虚的

  • Sharon tells the story of that night with self-deprecating humour.

    沙伦以幽默自嘲的口吻讲述那一夜的故事。
