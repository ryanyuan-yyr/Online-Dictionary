land office
Word forms: land offices
N-COUNT 国有土地管理局In the United States, a land office is a government office where records are kept about the sale of public land, including information about who owns it. 国有土地管理局 [US]

  • Line-ups formed daily at the land offices as township after township was opened for settlement.
