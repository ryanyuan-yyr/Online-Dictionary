greeting
griːtɪŋ ˈgritɪŋ
Word forms: plural greetings
1 N-VAR 问候；招呼；致意 A greeting is something friendly that you say or do when you meet someone. 问候；招呼；致意

  • His greeting was familiar and friendly.

    他的问候让人觉得随意又亲切。

  • They exchanged greetings.

    他们互致问候。

  • He raised a hand in greeting.

    他挥挥手打了个招呼。

SYN welcome, reception, hail, salute  
2 CONVENTION （旧时的）祝词，贺词，问候语 'Greetings' is an old-fashioned greeting. （旧时的）祝词，贺词，问候语
