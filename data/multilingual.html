multilingual
mʌltilɪŋgwəl ˌmʌltɪˈlɪŋgwəl
(also multi-lingual)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 用多种语言表达的; (能)使用多种语言的 Multilingual means involving several different languages. 用多种语言表达的; (能)使用多种语言的

  • ...a multilingual country.

    多语种国家

  • ...multilingual dictionaries.

    多语辞典

2 ADJ 懂多种语言的，能说多种语言的 A multilingual person is able to speak more than two languages very well. 懂多种语言的，能说多种语言的

  • He recruited two multilingual engineers.

    他招聘了两个懂多种语言的工程师。
