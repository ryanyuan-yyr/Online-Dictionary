addiction
ədɪkʃən əˈdɪkʃən
Word forms: plural addictions
1 N-VAR [oft n NOUN] 毒瘾 Addiction is the condition of taking harmful drugs and being unable to stop taking them. 毒瘾

  • She helped him fight his drug addiction.

    她帮助他戒毒。

  • ...long-term addiction to nicotine. [+ to]

    对尼古丁成瘾已久

SYN dependence, need, habit, weakness  
2 N-VAR 着迷；嗜好 An addiction to something is a very strong desire or need for it. 着迷；嗜好

  • He needed money to feed his addiction to gambling. [+ to]

    他需要钱来满足他的赌瘾。

  • I discovered an addiction to housework which I had never felt before.

    我发现自己迷上了做家务，之前我从没有这种感觉。
