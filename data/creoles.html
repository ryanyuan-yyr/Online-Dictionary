creole
kriːoʊl ˈkriːəʊl
(also Creole)Word forms: plural creoles
1 N-VAR 克里奥尔语(某些地方通用的混合语) A creole is a language that has developed from a mixture of different languages and has become the main language in a particular place 克里奥尔语(某些地方通用的混合语)

  • She begins speaking in the Creole of Haiti.

    她开始说海地克里奥尔语。

  • ...French Creole.

    克里奥尔法语

2 N-COUNT 克里奥尔人(指居住在西印度群岛的非洲人和欧洲人的混血儿) A Creole is a person of mixed African and European race, who lives in the West Indies and speaks a creole language. 克里奥尔人(指居住在西印度群岛的非洲人和欧洲人的混血儿)
3 N-COUNT 克里奥尔人(指首批定居在西印度群岛或美国南部的欧洲人的后裔) A Creole is a person descended from the Europeans who first settled in the West Indies or the southern United States of America. 克里奥尔人(指首批定居在西印度群岛或美国南部的欧洲人的后裔)
4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 克里奥尔的;克里奥尔人的 Creole means belonging to or relating to the Creole community. 克里奥尔的;克里奥尔人的

  • Coconut Rice Balls is a Creole dish.

    椰丝粉团是一道克里奥尔菜。

  • ...Creole culture.

    克里奥尔文化
