annulment
ənʌlmənt əˈnʌlmənt
Word forms: plural annulments
N-VAR 废除；取消；宣告无效 The annulment of a contract or marriage is an official declaration that it is invalid, so that legally it is considered never to have existed. 废除；取消；宣告无效

  • ...the annulment of the elections. [+ of]

    选举被宣告无效

  • He may appeal to the Pope for an annulment of his 24-year marriage.

    他可能会请求主教宣告他那长达24年的婚姻无效。

SYN invalidation, voiding, abolition, reversal
