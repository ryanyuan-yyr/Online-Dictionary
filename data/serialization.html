serialization
sɪəriəlaɪzeɪʃən
Word forms: plural serializations
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 serialisation
1 N-UNCOUNT (书的)连续发表，连续刊载 Serialization is the act of serializing a book. (书的)连续发表，连续刊载
2 N-COUNT 连载小说;连续剧 A serialization is a story, originally written as a book, which is being published or broadcast in a number of parts. 连载小说;连续剧

  • ...in the serialisation of Jane Austen's Pride and Prejudice. [+ of]

    在根据简·奥斯汀的小说《傲慢与偏见》改编的系列剧中
