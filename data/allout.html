all-out ˈɔlˈaʊt
(also all out)
ADJ [ADJ n] （行动）全力的，大举的 You use all-out to describe actions that are carried out in a very energetic and determined way, using all the resources available. （行动）全力的，大举的

  • He launched an all-out attack on his critics.

    他全力抨击批评他的人。

  • ...an all-out effort to bring the fire under control.

    全力以赴控制火势

SYN total, full, complete, determined  
All out is also an adverb.

  • We will be going all out to ensure it doesn't happen again.

    我们要全力保证此事不再发生。
