pull through
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （大病后）康复；（经历困境后）恢复正常 If someone with a serious illness or someone in a very difficult situation pulls through, they recover. （大病后）康复；（经历困境后）恢复正常

  • Everyone was very concerned whether he would pull through or not. [VERB PREP.]

    每个人都很关心他是否能够挺过去。

  • It is only our determination to fight that has pulled us through. [VERB noun PREP.]

    正是我们坚持战斗的决心帮我们渡过了难关。

  • ...ways of helping Russia pull through its upheavals. [VERB PREP. noun]

    帮助俄罗斯从动乱中恢复元气的方法

SYN survive, improve, recover, rally
