pinch-hit ˈpɪntʃˌhɪt
(also pinch hit)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pinch-hits, present participle pinch-hitting, past tense , past participle pinch-hit
1 VERB （意外情况下）充当替代者，代替 If you pinch-hit for someone, you do something for them because they are unexpectedly unable to do it. （意外情况下）充当替代者，代替 [US]

  • The staff here can pinch hit for each other when the hotel is busy. [VERB + for]

    宾馆客人多时，这里的职员可以互相替班。

2 VERB （棒球比赛中替补队员在关键时刻）替补击球 In a game of baseball, if you pinch-hit for another player, you hit the ball instead of them. （棒球比赛中替补队员在关键时刻）替补击球 [US]

  • Davalillo goes up to pinch-hit. [VERB]

    达瓦里洛上场替补击球。

pinch-hitter

  • Pinch-hitter Francisco Cabrera lashed a single to left field.

    替补队员弗朗西斯科·卡布雷拉向左外场猛地击出一个一垒打。
