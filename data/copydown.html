copy down
PHRASAL VERB 记下;抄下 If you copy down something that someone has said or written, you write it down exactly. 记下;抄下

  • Instructors read slowly and students copied down what was said. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    老师慢慢地念，学生们把所念的记下来。

  • I copied it down the way my lawyer read it to me this morning. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我把今天早上律师念的一字不差地记了下来。
