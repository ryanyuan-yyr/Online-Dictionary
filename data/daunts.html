dɔːnt dɔnt dɑnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense daunts, present participle daunting, past tense , past participle daunted
VERB 使胆怯;使气馁 If something daunts you, it makes you feel slightly afraid or worried about dealing with it. 使胆怯;使气馁

  • ...a gruelling journey that would have daunted a woman half her age. [VERB noun]

    比她年轻一半的女子都会胆怯的累人旅程

  • I'm somewhat daunted by the size of the task. [VERB noun]

    我有点儿被这项任务的规模吓着了。

SYN discourage, alarm, shake, frighten
