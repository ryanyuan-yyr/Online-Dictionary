portal
pɔːʳtəl ˈpɔrtəl
Word forms: plural portals
1 N-COUNT 正门；宏伟的大门 A portal is a large impressive doorway at the entrance to a building. 正门；宏伟的大门 [literary]

  • I went in through the royal portal.

    我从宏伟的正门进去。

SYN doorway, door, entry, way in  
2 N-COUNT 门户（网站） On the Internet, a portal is a site that consists of links to other websites. 门户（网站） [computing]
