aphrodisiac
æfrədɪziæk ˌæfrəˈdɪzɪæk ˌæfrəˈdiziˌæk ˌæfrəˈdɪziˌæk
Word forms: plural aphrodisiacs
N-COUNT 催情剂;春药 An aphrodisiac is a food, drink, or drug which is said to make people want to have sex. 催情剂;春药

  • Asparagus is reputed to be an aphrodisiac.

    据说芦笋可以催情。

SYN love potion, philtre  
Aphrodisiac is also an adjective.

  • ...plants with narcotic or aphrodisiac qualities.

    具有催眠或催情作用的植物
