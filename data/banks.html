FINANCE AND STORAGE 金融；储藏

1 N-COUNT 银行 A bank is an institution where people or businesses can keep their money. 银行

  • Which bank offers you the service that best suits your financial needs?.

    哪家银行提供的服务最适合您的理财需求？

  • I had £10,000 in the bank.

    我在银行有1万英镑的存款。

SYN financial institution, building society, merchant bank, repository  
2 N-COUNT 银行营业厅；银行办事处 A bank is a building where a bank offers its services. 银行营业厅；银行办事处
3 VERB 把（钱）存入银行 If you bank money, you pay it into a bank. 把（钱）存入银行

  • Once you have registered your particulars with an agency and it has banked your cheque, the process begins. [VERB noun]

    一旦在某个代理行登记了详细信息并由它将支票存入银行，这一程序就开始了。

SYN deposit, keep, clear, save  
4 VERB 与（银行）有业务来往；在（银行）有账户 If you bank with a particular bank, you have an account with that bank. 与（银行）有业务来往；在（银行）有账户

  • My husband has banked with the Co-op since before the war. [VERB + with]

    我丈夫自战前以来就在合作银行有户头。

5 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 库（如血库等） You use bank to refer to a store of something. For example, a blood bank is a store of blood that is kept ready for use. 库（如血库等）

  • ...Britain's National Police Computer, one of the largest data banks in the world.

    英国国家警务计算机系统，世界上最大的数据库之一

SYN store, fund, stock, source  
6 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 斥巨资；花费大笔的钱；倾家荡产 If you say that the cost of something will not break the bank, you mean that it will not cost a large sum of money. 斥巨资；花费大笔的钱；倾家荡产

  • Prices starting at £6 a bottle won't break the bank.

    每瓶6英镑的起价不算太贵。

AREAS AND MASSES 地区；大团物质

1 N-COUNT （河、湖等的）岸，堤岸 The banks of a river, canal, or lake are the raised areas of ground along its edge. （河、湖等的）岸，堤岸

  • ...30 miles of new developments along both banks of the Thames. [+ of]

    泰晤士河两岸30英里的新开发区

  • ...an old warehouse on the banks of a canal. [+ of]

    运河河畔的一座旧仓库

SYN side, edge, margin, shore  
2 N-COUNT 垄；埂 A bank of ground is a raised area of it with a flat top and one or two sloping sides. 垄；埂

  • ...resting indolently upon a grassy bank.

    懒洋洋地躺在草埂上休息

SYN mound, banking, rise, hill  
3 N-COUNT （长条形的）堆，团 A bank of something is a long high mass of it. （长条形的）堆，团

  • On their journey south they hit a bank of fog off the north-east coast of Scotland. [+ of]

    南行途中，他们在苏格兰东北海岸外遭遇了大雾。

SYN mass, accumulation  
4 N-COUNT （尤指机器、开关、仪表的）（一）排，（一）组 A bank of things, especially machines, switches, or dials, is a row of them, or a series of rows. （尤指机器、开关、仪表的）（一）排，（一）组

  • The typical laborer now sits in front of a bank of dials. [+ of]

    现如今，工人通常都是坐在一排仪表前。

SYN row, group, line, train  
5 See also: banked 

OTHER VERB USES 其他动词用法

VERB （通常指飞机在改变方向时）倾斜飞行 When an aircraft banks, one of its wings rises higher than the other, usually when it is changing direction. （通常指飞机在改变方向时）倾斜飞行

  • A plane took off and banked above the highway in front of him. [VERB]

    一架飞机起飞后在他前方的公路上空侧飞转向。

SYN tilt, tip, pitch, heel

bank on
