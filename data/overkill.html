overkill
oʊvəʳkɪl ˈoʊvərˌkɪl
N-UNCOUNT 过分；过火的行为 You can say that something is overkill when you think that there is more of it than is necessary or appropriate. 过分；过火的行为

  • Every time I switch on the TV, there's football. It's overkill.

    我每次打开电视都是足球节目，真是受不了。

  • Such security measures may well be overkill.

    这样的安保措施很可能过头了。
