clear out
1 PHRASAL VERB 滚开；滚出去 If you tell someone to clear out of a place or to clear out, you are telling them rather rudely to leave the place. 滚开；滚出去 [informal, disapproval]

  • She turned to the others in the room. 'The rest of you clear out of here.'. [VERB PREP. + of]

    她转身对房间里的其他人说：“你们其余人给我滚出去。”

  • 'Clear out!' he bawled. 'Private property!' [VERB PREP.]

    “滚出去！”他高声骂道，“这是私人住宅！”

SYN go away, leave, retire, withdraw  
2 PHRASAL VERB 清理，整理(容器、房间等) If you clear out a container, room, or house, you tidy it and throw away the things in it that you no longer want. 清理，整理(容器、房间等) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I took the precaution of clearing out my desk before I left. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我临走时特地事先把办公桌清理了一下。

SYN empty, sort, tidy up
