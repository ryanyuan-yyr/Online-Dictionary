heart-rending
ˈhɑrtˌrɛndɪŋ
(also heartrending)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 使人悲痛的;令人心碎的 You use heart-rending to describe something that causes you to feel great sadness and pity. 使人悲痛的;令人心碎的

  • ...heart-rending pictures of refugees.

    令人心碎的难民照片

  • I heard the most heartrending screams and moans.

    我听到了最让人揪心的尖叫和呻吟。

SYN moving, sad, distressing, affecting
