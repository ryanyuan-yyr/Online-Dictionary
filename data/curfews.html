curfew
kɜːʳfjuː ˈkɜrˌfju
Word forms: plural curfews
N-VAR (战争等时期的)宵禁令 A curfew is a law stating that people must stay inside their houses after a particular time at night, for example during a war. (战争等时期的)宵禁令

  • The village was placed under curfew.

    在该村实行了宵禁。

  • In Lucknow crowds of people defied the curfew to celebrate on the streets.

    在勒克瑙，成群的人们违抗宵禁令到街上去庆祝。
