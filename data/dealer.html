dealer
diːləʳ ˈdilər
Word forms: plural dealers
1 N-COUNT 商人;交易商 A dealer is a person whose business involves buying and selling things. 商人;交易商

  • ...an antique dealer.

    古董商

  • ...dealers in commodities and financial securities.

    商品和金融证券交易商

SYN trader, marketer, merchant, supplier  
2See also:wheeler-dealer 
3 N-COUNT 毒品贩子 A dealer is someone who buys and sells illegal drugs. 毒品贩子

  • They aim to clear every dealer from the street.

    他们打算把街上所有的毒品贩子都清走。
