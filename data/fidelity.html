fidelity
fɪdelɪti fəˈdɛləti faɪˈdɛləti
1 N-UNCOUNT (对人、组织或信仰的)忠诚，忠实 Fidelity is loyalty to a person, organization, or set of beliefs. (对人、组织或信仰的)忠诚，忠实 [formal]

  • I had to promise fidelity to the Queen. [+ to]

    我必须承诺效忠女王。

SYN loyalty, faith, integrity, devotion  
2 N-UNCOUNT (对配偶或伴侣的)忠贞，忠诚 Fidelity is being loyal to your husband, wife, or partner by not having a sexual relationship with anyone else. (对配偶或伴侣的)忠贞，忠诚

  • Wanting fidelity implies you're thinking about a major relationship.

    要求忠诚意味着你想发展一段严肃认真的恋爱关系。

3 N-UNCOUNT [with poss] (报告或翻译的)准确度，精确度，确切度 The fidelity of something such as a report or translation is the degree to which it is accurate. (报告或翻译的)准确度，精确度，确切度 [formal]

  • ...the fidelity of these early documents. [+ of]

    这些早期文件的准确性

SYN accuracy, precision, correspondence, closeness  
Quotations
