anthem
ænθəm ˈænθəm
Word forms: plural anthems
1 N-COUNT 国歌；（代表某一社会或团体的）颂歌，赞歌 An anthem is a song which is used to represent a particular nation, society, or group and which is sung on special occasions. 国歌；（代表某一社会或团体的）颂歌，赞歌

  • The band played the Czech anthem.

    乐队演奏了捷克国歌。

  • ...the Olympic anthem.

    奥运会会歌

SYN song of praise, carol, chant, hymn
