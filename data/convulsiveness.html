convulsive
kənvʌlsɪv kənˈvʌlsɪv
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 突然的;不能自控的;不由自主地 A convulsive movement or action is sudden and cannot be controlled. 突然的;不能自控的;不由自主地 [formal]

  • She thought she could never stop until convulsive sobs racked her even more.

    要不是自己已经泣不成声，她想她可能永远也不会停下来。

SYN jerky, violent, sporadic, fitful  
convulsively

  • His arms and legs jerked convulsively.

    他的手臂和腿突然抽搐起来。
