alike
əlaɪk əˈlaɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 相同的；相似的 If two or more things are alike, they are similar in some way. 相同的；相似的

  • We looked very alike.

    我们长得很像。

SYN similar, close, the same, equal  
2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb] 相同地；相似地 Alike means in a similar way. 相同地；相似地

  • They even dressed alike.

    他们甚至连穿戴都相似。

  • ...their assumption that all men and women think alike.

    他们关于所有男人和女人思考问题的方式都类似的假定

SYN similarly, identically, equally, uniformly  
3 ADV 同等地；同样地 You use alike after mentioning two or more people, groups, or things in order to emphasize that you are referring to both or all of them. 同等地；同样地 [emphasis]

  • The techniques are being applied almost everywhere by big and small firms alike.

    无论在大公司还是小公司，这些技术的应用几乎无处不在。

4 See also: lookalike
