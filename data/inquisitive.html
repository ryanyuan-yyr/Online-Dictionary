inquisitive
ɪnkwɪzɪtɪv ɪnˈkwɪzətɪv
ADJ-GRADED 爱打听的;好探问的;过于好奇的 An inquisitive person likes finding out about things, especially secret things. 爱打听的;好探问的;过于好奇的

  • Barrow had an inquisitive nature.

    巴罗天生就爱打听别人隐私。

  • Bears are very inquisitive and must be kept mentally stimulated.

    熊非常好奇,必须不断地给予其智力上的刺激。

SYN curious, questioning, inquiring, peering  
inquisitively

  • Molly looked at Ann inquisitively. 'Where do you want to go?'

    莫莉好奇地看着安,“你想去哪里?”

inquisitiveness

  • I liked children, loved their innocence and their inquisitiveness.

    我喜欢孩子,喜欢他们的天真与好奇。
