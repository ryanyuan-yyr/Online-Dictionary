rewind
riːwaɪnd riːwaɪnd riˈwaɪnd ˈriˌwaɪnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rewinds, present participle rewinding, past tense , past participle rewound
The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'waɪnd/. The noun is pronounced /'riːwaɪnd/. 动词读作/ˌriː'waɪnd/。名词读作/'riːwaɪnd/。
1 V-ERG 将(录像带、录音带）倒回;倒带;倒片 When the tape in a video or tape recorder rewinds or when you rewind it, the tape goes backwards so that you can play it again. 将(录像带、录音带）倒回;倒带;倒片

  • Waddington rewound the tape and played the message again. [VERB noun]

    沃丁顿把录音带倒回去，把留言又放了一遍。

  • He switched the control to the answer-play mode and waited for the tape to rewind. [VERB]

    他把控制键转换到回答－播放模式，然后等待倒带。

2 N-UNCOUNT 倒带(键) If you put a video or cassette tape on rewind, you make the tape go backwards. 倒带(键)

  • Press the rewind button.

    按下倒带键。

  • Press rewind or fast-forward.

    按下倒带键或快进键。
