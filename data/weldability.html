weld
weld wəʊld wɛld
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , 3rd person singular present tense welds, present participle welding, past tense , past participle welded
1 VERB 焊接;熔接;锻接 To weld one piece of metal to another means to join them by heating the edges and putting them together so that they cool and harden into one piece. 焊接;熔接;锻接 [Also VERB n]

  • It's possible to weld stainless steel to ordinary steel. [VERB noun + to]

    不锈钢是有可能和普通钢焊接在一起的。

  • They will also be used on factory floors to weld things together. [VERB noun with together]

    它们也可用于工厂车间，用来焊接物体。

  • Where did you learn to weld? [VERB]

    你在哪儿学会的焊接技术？

SYN join, link, bond, bind  
welding

  • All the welding had been done from inside the car.

    所有的焊接都在车内完成。

  • ...welding equipment.

    焊接设备

2 N-COUNT 焊缝;焊接点 A weld is a join where two pieces of metal have been welded together. 焊缝;焊接点
SYN joint, bond, seam, juncture  
3 VERB 使紧密结合;使成整体 If you weld people together, you join them together to form a united organization. 使紧密结合;使成整体 [formal]

  • She has both the authority and the personality to weld the party together. [VERB noun with together]

    团结全党所需的权威和个人魅力她两者兼具。

  • The miracle was that Rose had welded them into a team. [VERB noun into noun]

    令人称叹的是罗斯把他们打造成了一个团队。

SYN unite, combine, blend, consolidate
