marine
məriːn məˈrin
Word forms: plural marines
1 N-COUNT 水兵;海军陆战队士兵 A marine is a member of an armed force, for example the US Marine Corps or the Royal Marines, who is specially trained for military duties at sea as well as on land. 水兵;海军陆战队士兵
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 海洋的;海生的;海产的 Marine is used to describe things relating to the sea or to the animals and plants that live in the sea. 海洋的;海生的;海产的

  • ...breeding grounds for marine life.

    海洋生物的繁殖地

  • ...research in marine biology.

    海洋生物学研究

SYN nautical, sea, maritime, oceanic  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 海船的;海运的;海事的 Marine is used to describe things relating to ships and their movement at sea. 海船的;海运的;海事的

  • ...a solicitor specialising in marine law.

    专攻海事法的律师

  • ...marine insurance claims.

    海事保险索赔
