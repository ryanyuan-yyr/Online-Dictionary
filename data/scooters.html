scooter
skuːtəʳ ˈskutər
Word forms: plural scooters
1 N-COUNT 小型摩托车 A scooter is a small light motorcycle which has a low seat. 小型摩托车
2 N-COUNT (儿童)踏板车，滑板车 A scooter is a type of child's bicycle which has two wheels joined by a wooden board and a handle on a long pole attached to the front wheel. The child stands on the board with one foot, and uses the other foot to move forwards. (儿童)踏板车，滑板车
