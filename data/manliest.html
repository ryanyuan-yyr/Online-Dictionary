manly
mænli ˈmænli
Word forms: comparative manlier, superlative manliest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有男子气概的;阳刚的 If you describe a man's behaviour or appearance as manly, you approve of it because it shows qualities that are considered typical of a man, such as strength or courage. 有男子气概的;阳刚的 [approval]

  • He set himself manly tasks and expected others to follow his example.

    他给自己定下了男子汉的任务，并希望别人效之。

  • He was the ideal of manly beauty.

    他是理想中的有阳刚美的男人。

SYN virile, male, masculine, macho  
manliness

  • He has no doubts about his manliness.

    他毫不怀疑自己的男人气概。
