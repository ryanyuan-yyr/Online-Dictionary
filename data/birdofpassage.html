bird of passage
Word forms: plural birds of passage
N-COUNT 漂泊不定的人 If you refer to someone as a bird of passage, you mean that they are staying in a place for a short time before going to another place. 漂泊不定的人

  • Most of these emigrants were birds of passage who returned to Spain after a relatively short stay.

    这些移民大多数都是作短暂停留后即返回西班牙的匆匆过客。
