extol
ɪkstoʊl ɛkˈstoʊl ɛkˈstɑl ɪkˈstoʊl ɪkˈstɑl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense extols, present participle extolling, past tense , past participle extolled
VERB 颂扬;赞颂;赞美 If you extol something or someone, you praise them enthusiastically. 颂扬;赞颂;赞美

  • Now experts are extolling the virtues of the humble potato. [VERB noun]

    现在专家们都在赞美其貌不扬的马铃薯的种种好处。

  • They kept extolling my managerial skills. [VERB noun]

    他们不停地赞美我的管理技能。

SYN praise, acclaim, applaud, pay tribute to
