obsession
əbseʃən əbˈsɛʃən
Word forms: plural obsessions
N-VAR 着迷；困扰；强迫观念 If you say that someone has an obsession with a person or thing, you think they are spending too much time thinking about them. 着迷；困扰；强迫观念

  • She would try to forget her obsession with Christopher. [+ with]

    她会努力忘记对克里斯托弗的迷恋。

  • 95% of patients know their obsessions are irrational.

    95％的病人都知道他们的痴迷是不理智的。

SYN preoccupation, thing [informal] , complex, enthusiasm
