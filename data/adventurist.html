adventurist
ædventʃərɪst
Word forms: plural adventurists
ADJ 冒险主义的 If you describe someone or something as adventurist, you disapprove of them because they are willing to take risks in order to gain an unfair advantage in business or politics. 冒险主义的 [disapproval]

  • ...aggressive and adventurist foreign policy.

    激进冒险的外交政策

An adventurist is someone who behaves in an adventurist way.冒险主义者

  • ...political adventurists.

    政治冒险主义者
