noticeboard
noʊtɪsbɔːʳd ˈnəʊtɪsbɔːd
Word forms: plural noticeboards
N-COUNT 布告牌;布告栏 A noticeboard is a board which is usually attached to a wall in order to display notices giving information about something. 布告牌;布告栏 [British]

  • She added her name to the list on the noticeboard.

    她把自己的名字加在了布告栏上的名单里。

SYN pinboard, bulletin board
