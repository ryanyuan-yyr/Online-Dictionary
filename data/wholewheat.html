wholewheat hoʊlhwiːt
(also whole wheat)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 用全麦做的;用全麦面粉做的 Wholewheat flour is made from the complete grain of the wheat plant, including the outer part. Wholewheat bread or pasta is made from wholewheat flour. 用全麦做的;用全麦面粉做的

  • ...vegetables with wholewheat noodles.

    全麦面条拌蔬菜

2 N-UNCOUNT 全麦面包;全麦面粉 Wholewheat means wholewheat bread or wholewheat flour. 全麦面包;全麦面粉

  • ...a chicken salad sandwich on whole wheat.

    鸡肉色拉全麦三明治
