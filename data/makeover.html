makeover
meɪkoʊvəʳ ˈmeɪkəʊvə ˈmeɪkˌoʊvər
Word forms: plural makeovers
1 N-COUNT （通常指通过专业人士作出的）外表的改观，美容，改头换面 If a person or room is given a makeover, their appearance is improved, usually by an expert. （通常指通过专业人士作出的）外表的改观，美容，改头换面

  • She received a cosmetic makeover at a beauty salon as a birthday gift.

    她得到了一份生日礼物，可以在美容院享受一次美容。

2 N-COUNT 重大改造；革新 If an organization or system is given a makeover, important changes are made in order to improve it. 重大改造；革新

  • The biggest makeover has been in TV drama.

    最大的变革发生在电视剧行业。
