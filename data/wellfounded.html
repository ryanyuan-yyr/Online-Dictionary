well-founded
ˈwɛlˈfaʊndɪd
(also well founded)
ADJ-GRADED (报告、观念或感觉)理由充足的，有根据的 If you say that a report, opinion, or feeling is well-founded, you mean that it is based on facts and can therefore be justified. (报告、观念或感觉)理由充足的，有根据的

  • If the reports are well founded, the incident could seriously aggravate relations between the two nations.

    如果这些报道是有根据的，那么该事件将有可能导致两国之间的关系严重恶化。

  • We must respond to well-founded criticism with a willingness to change.

    对于有根有据的批评，我们必须乐意作出改变。

SYN justifiable, justified, reasonable, valid
