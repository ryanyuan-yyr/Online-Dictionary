coercion
koʊɜːʳʃən koʊˈɜrʃən koʊˈɜrʒən
N-UNCOUNT 强迫;胁迫;逼迫 Coercion is the act or process of persuading someone forcefully to do something that they do not want to do. 强迫;胁迫;逼迫

  • It was vital that the elections should be free of coercion or intimidation.

    选举中不应有强迫或恐吓行为，这一点至关重要。

SYN force, pressure, threats, bullying
