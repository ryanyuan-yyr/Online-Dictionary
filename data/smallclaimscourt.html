small claims court
(also small-claims court)Word forms: small claims courts
N-VAR 小额索偿法庭Small claims court is a local law court which settles disputes between people that involve relatively small amounts of money. 小额索偿法庭

  • ...the Los Angeles mother who took a caregiver to small claims court over a $70 phone bill.

    ...那位来自洛杉矶的母亲，她因为70美元的电话费把护理人员告上了小额赔偿法庭。

  • The 27-year-old was taken to a small claims court.

    这个27岁的年轻人被带上了小额赔偿法庭。
