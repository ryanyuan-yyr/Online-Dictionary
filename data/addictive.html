addictive
ədɪktɪv əˈdɪktɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 使人成瘾的 If a drug is addictive, people who take it cannot stop taking it. 使人成瘾的

  • Cigarettes are highly addictive.

    香烟很容易使人上瘾。

  • Crack is the most addictive drug on the market.

    强效纯可卡因是市面上最容易让人上瘾的毒品。

SYN habit-forming, compelling, compulsive, causing addiction or dependency  
2 ADJ-GRADED 使人着迷的 Something that is addictive is so enjoyable that it makes you want to do it or have it a lot. 使人着迷的

  • Video movie-making can quickly become addictive.

    制作视频电影能很快让人入迷。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 易着迷的；易上瘾的 If someone has an addictive personality, they easily become addicted to something. 易着迷的；易上瘾的

  • I have an eating disorder and an addictive personality.

    我饮食没有规律，而且性格上容易沉迷某事。
