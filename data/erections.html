erection
ɪrekʃən iˈrɛkʃən ɪˈrɛkʃən
Word forms: plural erections
1 N-COUNT （阴茎的）勃起 If a man has an erection, his penis is stiff, swollen, and sticking up because he is sexually aroused. （阴茎的）勃起
2 N-UNCOUNT 建造;建筑;竖起 The erection of something is the act of building it or placing it in an upright position. 建造;建筑;竖起

  • ...the erection of temporary fencing to protect hedges under repair. [+ of]

    为保护正在养护的树篱而竖起的临时围栏

  • ...the erection of the first temple.

    第一座寺庙的建造

SYN building, setting-up, manufacture, construction
