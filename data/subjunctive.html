subjunctive
səbdʒʌŋktɪv səbˈdʒʌŋktɪv
N-SING 虚拟式;虚拟语气 In English, a clause expressing a wish or suggestion can be put in the subjunctive, or in the subjunctive mood, by using the base form of a verb or 'were'. Examples are 'He asked that they be removed' and 'I wish I were somewhere else'. These structures are formal. 虚拟式;虚拟语气 [technical]
