sheet
ʃiːt ʃit
Word forms: plural sheets
1 N-COUNT 被单;床单 A sheet is a large rectangular piece of cotton or other cloth that you sleep on or cover yourself with in a bed. 被单;床单

  • Once a week, a maid changes the sheets.

    女仆每星期换一次床单。

  • ...the luxury of silk sheets.

    丝绸床单的奢华

SYN bedding, bed linen, bed clothes  
2 N-COUNT 一页，一张(纸) A sheet of paper is a rectangular piece of paper. 一页，一张(纸)

  • ...a sheet of newspaper. [+ of]

    一张报纸

  • I was able to fit it all on one sheet.

    我能将全部内容都放在一页上。

SYN page, leaf, folio, piece of paper  
3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 简要记录;纪要 You can use sheet to refer to a piece of paper which gives information about something. 简要记录;纪要

  • ...information sheets on each country in the world. [+ on]

    有关世界各国信息的简要记录

4 N-COUNT (玻璃、金属、木头等的)大块薄片，薄板 A sheet of glass, metal, or wood is a large, flat, thin piece of it. (玻璃、金属、木头等的)大块薄片，薄板

  • ...a cracked sheet of glass. [+ of]

    一片碎玻璃

  • Overhead cranes were lifting giant sheets of steel. [+ of]

    高架起重机正吊起一块块巨大的钢板。

  • Vinyl can be laid in sheet or tile form.

    塑料地板分为卷材和块材两种。

SYN plate, piece, panel, slab  
5 N-COUNT (覆盖在其他物体上的)一大片，一层 A sheet of something is a thin wide layer of it over the surface of something else. (覆盖在其他物体上的)一大片，一层

  • ...a sheet of ice. [+ of]

    一层冰

  • ...a blue-grey sheet of dust. [+ of]

    一层蓝灰色的灰尘

SYN coat, film, layer, membrane  
6 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] 一团，一片，一股(迅速移动的火或水) A sheet of fire or water is a fast-moving mass of it that is difficult to see through. 一团，一片，一股(迅速移动的火或水)

  • The streets were now in one fierce sheet of flame.

    街道上现在是一片火海。

  • Sheets of rain slanted across the road.

    倾盆大雨斜着打落在路上。

SYN expanse, area, stretch, sweep  
7 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 帆脚索;拉帆绳 In sailing, a sheet is a line or rope used for controlling the position of a sail on a boat. 帆脚索;拉帆绳
