hairline
heəʳlaɪn ˈhɛəˌlaɪn ˈhɛrˌlaɪn
Word forms: plural hairlines
1 N-COUNT [usually singular, oft poss NOUN] 发际线 Your hairline is the edge of the area where your hair grows on your head. 发际线

  • Joanne had a small dark birthmark near her hairline.

    乔安妮的发际线旁有个小小的深色胎记。

  • His hairline had receded slightly over the years.

    这些年来他的前额已经有点秃了。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （裂缝、缝隙等）极细的，极细微的 A hairline crack or gap is very narrow or fine. （裂缝、缝隙等）极细的，极细微的

  • There's a hairline crack in the rim of that jar.

    那只罐子的口沿有条极细的裂缝。

  • He suffered a hairline fracture of the right index finger.

    他右手食指骨裂。
