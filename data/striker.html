striker
straɪkəʳ ˈstraɪkər
Word forms: plural strikers
1 N-COUNT (足球等的)前锋 In football and some other team sports, a striker is a player who mainly attacks and scores goals, rather than defends. (足球等的)前锋

  • ...and the England striker scored his sixth goal of the season.

    这名英格兰队前锋踢进了他在本赛季的第 6 个进球
