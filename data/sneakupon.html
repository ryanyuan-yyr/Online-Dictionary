sneak up on
1 PHRASAL VERB 偷偷地走近;偷偷地挨近 If someone sneaks up on you, they try and approach you without being seen or heard, perhaps to surprise you or do you harm. 偷偷地走近;偷偷地挨近

  • I managed to sneak up on him when you knocked on the door. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    你敲门的时候，我设法悄悄靠近了他。

2 PHRASAL VERB 突然降临;出其不意地发生 If something sneaks up on you, it happens or occurs when you are not expecting it. 突然降临;出其不意地发生

  • Sometimes our expectations sneak up on us unawares. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    有些时候，我们期待的事情会不期而至。
