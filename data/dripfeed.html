drip-feed
(also drip feed)
VERB (钱等)分作小量点滴给予，细水长流 If you drip-feed money into something, you pay the money a little at a time rather than paying it all at once. (钱等)分作小量点滴给予，细水长流

  • ...investors who adopt the sensible policy of drip feeding money into shares. [VERB noun + into]

    采取明智策略分批买入股票的投资者
