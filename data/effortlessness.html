effortless
efəʳtləs ˈɛfərtlɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不费力的;轻松的 Something that is effortless is done easily and well. 不费力的;轻松的

  • In a single effortless motion, he scooped Frannie into his arms.

    他一下子就轻松地把弗兰尼拥入怀里。

  • ...effortless and elegant Italian cooking.

    简单精致的意大利烹饪

SYN easy, simple, flowing, smooth  
effortlessly

  • Her son Peter adapted effortlessly to his new surroundings.

    她儿子彼得很快就适应了新环境。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (品质)自然的，天生具有的 You use effortless to describe a quality that someone has naturally and does not have to learn. (品质)自然的，天生具有的

  • She liked him above all for his effortless charm.

    她最喜欢他身上那种与生俱来的魅力。

SYN natural, simple, spontaneous, instinctive
