acting
æktɪŋ ˈæktɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 表演;演艺业;演技;演戏 Acting is the activity or profession of performing in plays or films. 表演;演艺业;演技;演戏

  • She pursued an acting career after four years of modelling.

    当了4年的模特之后她踏上了演艺之路。

SYN performance, playing, performing, theatre  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 临时代理的 You use acting before the title of a job to indicate that someone is doing that job temporarily. 临时代理的

  • ...the new acting President.

    新任代总统

SYN temporary, substitute, intervening, interim
