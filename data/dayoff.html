day off
deɪ ɒf
Word forms: plural days off
N-COUNT 休假日;休息日 A day off is a day when you do not go to work, even though it is usually a working day. 休假日;休息日

  • It was Mrs Dearden's day off, and Paul was on duty in her place.

    那天迪尔登夫人休息，保罗替她当班。

SYN holiday, accumulated day off or ADO [Australian]
