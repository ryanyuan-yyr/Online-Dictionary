kiss-and-tell ˈkɪsənˈtɛl
ADJ [ADJ n] 泄露与名人的私情的 If someone who has had a love affair with a famous person tells the story of that affair in public, for example in a newspaper or book, you can refer to this as a kiss-and-tell story. 泄露与名人的私情的

  • ...intimate photographs and kiss-and-tell revelations.

    暧昧照片及与名人私情的披露
