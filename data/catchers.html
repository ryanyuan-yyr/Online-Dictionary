catcher
kætʃəʳ ˈkɛtʃər
Word forms: plural catchers
1 N-COUNT (棒球)接球手 In baseball, the catcher is the player who stands behind the batter. The catcher has a special glove for catching the ball. (棒球)接球手
2 N-COUNT [usually with supplement] 捕捉的人;捕获者 You can refer to someone who catches something as a catcher . 捕捉的人;捕获者

  • ...the catcher of the largest fish.

    捕到最大的鱼的那个人

  • ...a rat-catcher.

    捕鼠者
