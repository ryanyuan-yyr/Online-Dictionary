comb
koʊm kum koʊm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense combs, present participle combing, past tense , past participle combed
1 N-COUNT 梳子 A comb is a flat piece of plastic or metal with narrow pointed teeth along one side, which you use to tidy your hair. 梳子
2 VERB 梳理(头发) When you comb your hair, you tidy it using a comb. 梳理(头发)

  • Salvatore combed his hair carefully. [VERB noun]

    萨尔瓦托认真地梳了梳头。

  • Her reddish hair was cut short and neatly combed. [VERB-ed]

    她淡红色的头发剪得短短的，梳得很整齐。

SYN untangle, arrange, groom, dress  
3 VERB 在…四处搜寻 If you comb a place, you search everywhere in it in order to find someone or something. 在…四处搜寻

  • Officers combed the woods for the murder weapon. [VERB noun + for]

    警察在树林里四处搜寻凶器。

  • They fanned out and carefully combed the temple grounds. [VERB noun]

    他们呈扇形散开，在寺庙周围仔细搜寻。

SYN search, hunt through, sweep, rake  
4 VERB 在(信息)中仔细搜寻 If you comb through information, you look at it very carefully in order to find something. 在(信息)中仔细搜寻

  • Eight policemen then spent two years combing through the evidence. [VERB + through]

    之后8位警察花了两年时间搜寻证据。
