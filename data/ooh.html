ooh
uː u
(also oo)
EXCLAM （表示吃惊、企盼、高兴或不快）嗬 People say 'ooh' when they are surprised, looking forward to something, or find something pleasant or unpleasant. （表示吃惊、企盼、高兴或不快）嗬 [informal, feelings]

  • 'Ooh dear me, that's a bit of a racist comment isn't it.'.

    “嗬，天哪！那评论有点种族歧视，不是吗？”

  • 'Red? Ooh how nice.'

    “红色？嗬，真不错。”
