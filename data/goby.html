go by
1 PHRASAL VERB （时间）流逝，过去 If you say that time goes by, you mean that it passes. （时间）流逝，过去

  • My grandmother was becoming more and more sad and frail as the years went by. [VERB PREP.]

    随着时间的流逝，我的祖母变得日益伤感和虚弱。

SYN pass, proceed, elapse, flow on  
2 PHRASAL VERB 遵循；遵照 If you go by something, you use it as a basis for a judgment or action. 遵循；遵照

  • If they prove that I was wrong, then I'll go by what they say. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果他们能证明我不对，我就听他们的。

SYN obey, follow, adopt, observe
