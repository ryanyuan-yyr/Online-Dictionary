uninspiring
ʌnɪnspaɪərɪŋ ˌʌnɪnˈspaɪrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 不振奋精神的；引不起兴趣的 If you describe something or someone as uninspiring, you are criticizing them because they have no special or exciting qualities, and make you feel bored. 不振奋精神的；引不起兴趣的 [disapproval]

  • The series of speeches on the economy was uninspiring and a rehash of old subjects.

    这一系列关于经济的演讲平常无奇，都是一些陈词滥调。

  • The house had a tiny kitchen with an uninspiring view.

    这房子有一间狭小的厨房，窗外也没什么特别的景色。

  • ...his image as a dull and uninspiring leader.

    他呆板无趣的领导者形象

SYN unexciting, boring, dull, flat
