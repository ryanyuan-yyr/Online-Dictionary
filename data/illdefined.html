ill-defined ˈɪldiˈfaɪnd
ADJ-GRADED 不清楚的;不分明的;不确定的 If you describe something as ill-defined, you mean that its exact nature or extent is not as clear as it should be or could be. 不清楚的;不分明的;不确定的

  • ...staff with ill-defined responsibilities.

    职责划分不明确的员工队伍

SYN unclear, vague, indistinct, blurred
