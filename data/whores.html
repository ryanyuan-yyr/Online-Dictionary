who're
huːəʳ huːəʳ ˈhuər hʊr
（who are 的一种口语形式） Who're is a spoken form of 'who are'. （who are 的一种口语形式）

  • I've got loads of friends who're unemployed.

    我有很多失业的朋友。

  • Who're you going to the pictures with?

    你要跟谁一起去电影院？

whore
hɔːʳ hɔː hɔr hur hʊr
Word forms: plural whores
1 同 prostitute A whore is the same as a prostitute . 同 prostitute
2 淫妇;婊子 If you call a woman a whore, you disapprove of her because you consider her sexual behaviour to be immoral or unacceptable. 淫妇;婊子
