reminder
rɪmaɪndəʳ rɪˈmaɪndər
Word forms: plural reminders
1 N-COUNT [usually singular, NOUN that] 提示;使人回忆起某事的事物 Something that serves as a reminder of another thing makes you think about the other thing. 提示;使人回忆起某事的事物 [written]

  • The British are about to be given a sharp reminder of what fighting abroad really means. [+ of]

    有些事将很快让英国人清楚意识到海外作战究竟意味着什么。

  • Violence has broken out in the capital, a stark reminder that the religious tensions are refusing to go away.

    首都爆发了暴力事件，这给了人们一个严酷的提醒—宗教紧张局势还在持续。

SYN prompt, prompting, cue, nudge  
2 N-COUNT 缴费单;催缴单;催缴通知 A reminder is a letter or note that is sent to tell you that you have not done something such as pay a bill or return library books. 缴费单;催缴单;催缴通知 [mainly British]

  • ...the final reminder for the gas bill.

    最后一次煤气催缴通知单
