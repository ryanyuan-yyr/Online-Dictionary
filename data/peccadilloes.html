peccadillo
pekədɪloʊ ˌpɛkəˈdɪloʊ
Word forms: plural peccadilloes, plural peccadillos
N-COUNT [usually plural] 轻罪；过失；失误 Peccadilloes are small, unimportant sins or faults. 轻罪；过失；失误 [written]

  • People are prepared to be tolerant of extra-marital peccadilloes by public figures.

    人们可以容忍公众人物婚外的不检点行为。

SYN misdeed, slip, error, lapse
