combatant
kɒmbətənt kəmbæt- kəmˈbætənt ˈkɑmbətənt
Word forms: plural combatants
N-COUNT [usually plural] 参战者;参战组织;参战国 A combatant is a person, group, or country that takes part in the fighting in a war. 参战者;参战组织;参战国

  • I have never suggested that UN forces could physically separate the combatants in the region.

    我从未暗示过联合国部队实际上能够将该地区各参战方隔离开。

  • They come from the combatant nations.

    他们来自各参战国。

SYN fighter, soldier, warrior, contender
