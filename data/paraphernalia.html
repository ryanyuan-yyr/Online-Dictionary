paraphernalia
pærəfəʳneɪliə ˌpærəfəˈneɪlɪə ˌpærəfərˈneɪljə ˌpærəfərˈneɪliə
1 N-UNCOUNT （某人携带的或与特定活动有关的）大量物品，全套装备 You can refer to a large number of objects that someone has with them or that are connected with a particular activity as paraphernalia. （某人携带的或与特定活动有关的）大量物品，全套装备

  • ...a large courtyard full of builders' paraphernalia.

    堆满建筑工人所需装备的大庭院

  • Get rid of all cigarettes and ashtrays and other paraphernalia associated with smoking.

    扔掉所有香烟、烟灰缸和与吸烟有关的其他物品。

SYN equipment, things, effects, material  
2 N-UNCOUNT 繁琐手续；繁文缛节 If you disapprove of the things and events that are involved in a particular system or activity, and you think they are unnecessary, you can refer to them as paraphernalia . 繁琐手续；繁文缛节 [disapproval]

  • The public don't necessarily want the paraphernalia of a full hearing. [+ of]

    公众并不一定希望走全面听证的复杂程序。
