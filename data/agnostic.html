agnostic
ægnɒstɪk ægˈnɑstɪk
Word forms: plural agnostics
1 N-COUNT 不可知论者（Compare 比较 atheist.) An agnostic believes that it is not possible to know whether God exists or not. 不可知论者（Compare 比较 atheist.)

  • Vasari claimed with horror that he was, if not an atheist, then an agnostic.

    瓦萨里惊恐地说他若非无神论者，就是不可知论者。

2 ADJ 不可知论（者）的 Agnostic means relating to agnostics or to their beliefs. 不可知论（者）的

  • You grew up in an agnostic household and have never been able to bring yourself to believe in God.

    你在一个信奉不可知论的家庭里长大，一直都无法信仰上帝。

SYN sceptical, questioning, doubting, cynical
