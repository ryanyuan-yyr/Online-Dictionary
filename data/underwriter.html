underwriter
ʌndəʳraɪtəʳ ˈʌndərˌraɪtər
Word forms: plural underwriters
1 N-COUNT 保险商；承销商 An underwriter is someone whose job involves agreeing to provide money for a particular activity or to pay for any losses that are made. 保险商；承销商 [business]

  • If the market will not buy the shares, the underwriter buys them.

    如果市场上没有人买这些股票，承销商就会买下来。

  • Global 2000 became an underwriter for small farmers.

    “全球2000”成为了小农场主的承保公司。

2 N-COUNT 核保师（对投保项目进行风险评估并决定保险费率） An underwriter is someone whose job is to judge the risks involved in certain activities and decide how much to charge for insurance. 核保师（对投保项目进行风险评估并决定保险费率） [business]
