repossession
riːpəzeʃən
Word forms: plural repossessions
1 N-VAR 收回;重新占有 The repossession of someone's house is the act of repossessing it. 收回;重新占有

  • ...the problem of home repossessions.

    收回住房的问题

  • ...families facing repossession of their homes.

    面临住房被收回的家庭

2 N-COUNT 被收回之物 You can refer to a house or car that has been repossessed as a repossession . 被收回之物

  • Many of the cars you will see at auction are repossessions.

    你将在拍卖会上见到的许多车辆都是被收回物品。
