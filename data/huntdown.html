hunt down
PHRASAL VERB 追捕到，穷追直至找到（罪犯、敌人等） If you hunt down a criminal or an enemy, you find them after searching for them. 追捕到，穷追直至找到（罪犯、敌人等）

  • Last December they hunted down and killed one of the gangsters. [VERB PREP. noun]

    去年12月他们追捕到其中一名匪徒并将其击毙。

  • Those who do not come forward should be hunted down. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    对于那些不肯自首的罪犯应予缉拿归案。

  • It took her four months to hunt him down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她花了4个月才把他找出来。
