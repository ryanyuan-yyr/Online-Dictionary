leisurewear
leʒəʳweəʳ ˈlɛʒəwɛə liːʒ-
N-UNCOUNT 便服；休闲服 Leisurewear is informal clothing which you wear when you are not working, for example at weekends or on holiday. 便服；休闲服 [British, written]

  • Their range of leisurewear is aimed at fashion-conscious 13 to 25 year-olds.

    他们的休闲服系列瞄准的是追求时髦的13到25岁青少年。
