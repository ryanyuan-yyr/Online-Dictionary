accoutrement
əkuːtrəmənt əˈkuːtrəmənt əˈkuːtərmənt
Word forms: plural accoutrements
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 accouterment
N-COUNT [usually plural] 行李；行头；装备 Accoutrements are all the things you have with you when you travel or when you take part in a particular activity. 行李；行头；装备 [humorous, or old-fashioned]

  • I loved stationery and all the accoutrements of writing.

    我喜爱信笺信封和所有文房用具。
