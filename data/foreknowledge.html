foreknowledge
fɔːʳnɒlɪdʒ ˈfɔrˌnɑlɪdʒ fɔrˈnɑlɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 预知;事先知道 If you have foreknowledge of an event or situation, you have some knowledge of it before it actually happens. 预知;事先知道

  • She has maintained that the General had foreknowledge of the plot. [+ of]

    她坚称将军已经预先知晓了这个阴谋。

SYN prior knowledge, advance knowledge, previous understanding
