brigade
brɪgeɪd brɪˈgeɪd
Word forms: plural brigades
1 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 旅 A brigade is one of the groups which an army is divided into. 旅

  • ...the men of the Seventh Armoured Brigade.

    第七装甲旅的士兵们

SYN corps, company, force, unit  
2See also:fire brigade 
3 N-SING [n NOUN] （信奉某事或具有共同特点的）一帮人，族 You can use brigade to refer to a group of people who believe strongly in a particular thing or who share a particular characteristic. （信奉某事或具有共同特点的）一帮人，族 [humorous]

  • ...the healthy eating brigade.

    健康饮食族

  • ...the black T-shirt brigade.

    黑T恤一族

SYN group, party, body, band
