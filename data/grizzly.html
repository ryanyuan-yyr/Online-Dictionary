grizzly
grɪzli ˈgrɪzli
Word forms: plural grizzlies
1 N-COUNT 灰熊；银光熊 A grizzly or a grizzly bear is a large, fierce, greyish-brown bear. 灰熊；银光熊

  • ...two grizzly bear cubs.

    两只灰熊幼崽

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （小孩）不断啼哭的，耍脾气的 If children are grizzly, they complain or cry a lot, often because they are unwell or tired. （小孩）不断啼哭的，耍脾气的 [informal]

  • All babies need quiet times on their own and get grizzly if they've had too much stimulation.

    婴儿都需要自己安静地呆一会儿，如果受到太多的刺激他们就会大声啼哭。

3 See also: grisly
