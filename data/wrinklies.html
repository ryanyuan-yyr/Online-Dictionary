wrinkly
rɪŋkli
Word forms: plural wrinklies
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 起皱的;有皱痕的 A wrinkly surface has a lot of wrinkles on it. 起皱的;有皱痕的

  • ...wrinkly cotton and wool stockings.

    起了褶子的棉毛长筒袜

  • ...a smallish, greying man, with a wrinkly face.

    头发花白、满脸皱纹的小老头

2 N-COUNT (年轻人对老人的戏称，尤用于拿老人开玩笑或取笑他们的举止时)老皱皮，老东西 Young people sometimes refer to older people as wrinklies, especially when they are teasing them or making fun of the way they behave. (年轻人对老人的戏称，尤用于拿老人开玩笑或取笑他们的举止时)老皱皮，老东西 [British, informal, offensive]
