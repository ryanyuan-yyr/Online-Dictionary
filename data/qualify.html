qualify
kwɒlɪfaɪ ˈkwɔləˌfaɪ ˈkwɑləˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense qualifies, present participle qualifying, past tense , past participle qualified
1 VERB (通过考试)取得资格，合格，达到标准 When someone qualifies, they pass the examinations that they need to be able to work in a particular profession. (通过考试)取得资格，合格，达到标准 [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • But when I'd qualified and started teaching it was a different story. [VERB]

    可是等我获得资格开始教书的时候，却是另一番情形了。

  • I qualified as a doctor from London University over 30 years ago. [V + as/in]

    30多年前，我从伦敦大学毕业，取得了行医资格。

SYN gain qualifications, pass, graduate, be licensed  
2 V-ERG (使)有资格获得;(使)有权去做 If you qualify for something or if something qualifies you for it, you have the right to do it or have it. (使)有资格获得;(使)有权去做

  • To qualify for maternity leave you must have worked for the same employer for two years. [VERB + for]

    你必须为同一个雇主工作满两年才能享受产假。

  • The basic course does not qualify you to practise as a therapist. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    这门基础课程并不能让你获得治疗师的从业资格。

  • ...skills that qualify foreigners for work visas. [VERB noun + for]

    使外国人有资格获得工作签证的技能

  • ...highly trained staff who are well qualified to give unbiased, practical advice. [VERB-ed]

    受过良好训练、能够提供客观而切实的建议的工作人员

SYN certify, equip, empower, train  
3 V-ERG 可称得上;符合；可算作 To qualify as something or to be qualified as something means to have all the features that are needed to be that thing. 可称得上;符合；可算作 [Also VERB]

  • 13 percent of American households qualify as poor, says Mr. Mishel. [VERB + as]

    米歇尔先生说，13%的美国家庭符合贫困家庭的标准。

  • These people seem to think that reading a few books on old age qualifies them as experts. [VERB noun + as]

    这些人似乎以为自己读了几本关于老年人的书就可以算作专家了。

SYN be described, count, be considered as, be named  
4 VERB 通过预赛;取得(下一轮)比赛资格 If you qualify in a competition, you are successful in one part of it and go on to the next stage. 通过预赛;取得(下一轮)比赛资格

  • Nottingham Forest qualified for the final by beating Tranmere on Tuesday. [VERB + for]

    诺丁汉森林队于星期二打败了特兰米尔队，取得了决赛资格。

  • Cameroon have also qualified after beating Sierra Leone. [VERB]

    喀麦隆队击败塞拉利昂队之后也成功晋级。

  • ...a World Cup qualifying match. [VERB-ing]

    世界杯预选赛

qualifier

  • Kenya's Robert Kibe was the fastest qualifier for the 800 metres final.

    肯尼亚的罗伯特·基布是800米决赛入围者中速度最快的选手。

5 VERB 具体说明;使语气缓和 If you qualify a statement, you make it less strong or less general by adding a detail or explanation to it. 具体说明;使语气缓和

  • I would qualify that by putting it into context. [VERB noun]

    我会将它放入上下文中进行具体解释。

SYN restrict, limit, reduce, vary
