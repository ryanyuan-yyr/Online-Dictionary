acclaim
əkleɪm əˈkleɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense acclaims, present participle acclaiming, past tense , past participle acclaimed
1 VERB [usually passive] 称赞;赞扬 If someone or something is acclaimed, they are praised enthusiastically. 称赞;赞扬 [formal]

  • She has been acclaimed for the TV drama 'Prime Suspect'. [be V-ed for n/v-ing]

    她凭借电视剧《头号嫌疑犯》而受到好评。

  • He was acclaimed as England's greatest modern painter. [be VERB-ed + as]

    他被誉为英格兰现代最伟大的油画家。

  • The group's debut album was immediately acclaimed a hip hop classic. [be VERB-ed noun]

    这个组合的首张专辑一经推出便被盛赞为嘻哈乐的经典之作。

SYN praise, celebrate, honour, cheer
