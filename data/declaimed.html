declaim
dɪkleɪm diˈkleɪm dɪˈkleɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense declaims, present participle declaiming, past tense , past participle declaimed
VERB 慷慨陈词;高声朗诵 If you declaim, you speak dramatically, as if you were acting in a theatre. 慷慨陈词;高声朗诵 [written]

  • He raised his right fist and declaimed: 'Liar and cheat!'. [VERB with quote]

    他举起右拳高喊：“骗子，骗子！”

  • He used to declaim French verse to us. [VERB noun]

    他过去常给我们朗诵法语诗歌。

SYN speak, lecture, proclaim, recite
