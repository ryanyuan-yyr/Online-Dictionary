wheel clamp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense wheel clamps, present participle wheel clamping, present participle wheel clamped
1 N-COUNT (用以锁住非法停靠车辆的)车轮固定夹，轮夹 A wheel clamp is a large metal device which is fitted to the wheel of an illegally parked car or other vehicle in order to prevent it from being driven away. The motorist has to pay to have the clamp removed. (用以锁住非法停靠车辆的)车轮固定夹，轮夹 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 Denver boot
2 VERB 用轮夹锁住 If a car is wheel clamped, a wheel clamp is fixed to one of its wheels so that it cannot be driven away. 用轮夹锁住 [British]

  • Unauthorized vehicles will be wheel clamped or towed away. [be VERB-ed]

    未经许可停放的车辆将会被轮夹锁住或拖走。
