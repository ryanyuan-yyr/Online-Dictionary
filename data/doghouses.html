doghouse
dɒghaʊs dɔːg- ˈdɔgˌhaʊs
(also dog-house)Word forms: plural doghouses
1 N-COUNT 狗窝;犬舍 A doghouse is a small building made especially for a dog to sleep in. 狗窝;犬舍 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 kennel
2 PHRASE 受冷落的;受排斥的 If you are in the doghouse, people are annoyed or angry with you. 受冷落的;受排斥的 [informal]

  • Her husband was in the doghouse for leaving her to cope on her own.

    她丈夫因让她独自应付把她惹毛了。
