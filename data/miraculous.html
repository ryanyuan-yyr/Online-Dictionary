miraculous
mɪrækjʊləs mɪˈrækjuləs mɪˈrækjələs
1 ADJ-GRADED 神奇的;奇迹般的;不可思议的 If you describe a good event as miraculous, you mean that it is very surprising and unexpected. 神奇的;奇迹般的;不可思议的

  • The horse made a miraculous recovery to finish a close third.

    这匹马奇迹般地恢复了状态，以微弱的劣势位居第三。

  • ...a miraculous escape.

    奇迹般的逃脱

  • The change in Felicity was miraculous.

    费莉西蒂的变化不可思议。

SYN wonderful, amazing, extraordinary, incredible [informal]  
miraculously

  • Miraculously, the guards escaped death or serious injury.

    警卫们奇迹般地死里逃生，也没有受重伤。

  • We miraculously survived the crossing to Muscat.

    我们奇迹般地活着来到了马斯喀特。

2 ADJ 超自然的；神奇的 If someone describes a wonderful event as miraculous, they believe that the event was caused by God. 超自然的；神奇的

  • ...miraculous healing.

    奇迹般的痊愈

  • ...miraculous powers.

    神奇的力量

miraculously

  • He was miraculously healed of a severe fever.

    他的高烧奇迹般地退了。
