swoon
swuːn swun
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense swoons, present participle swooning, past tense , past participle swooned
VERB 痴迷;对…神魂颠倒 If you swoon, you are strongly affected by your feelings for someone you love or admire very much. 痴迷;对…神魂颠倒 [Also V adv]

  • Virtually every woman in the '20s swooned over Valentino. [VERB + over]

    在 20 年代，几乎每位女性都痴迷于华伦天奴这个品牌。

  • The ladies shriek and swoon at his every word. [VERB]

    他的每一句话都令那些女士为之尖叫，为之痴狂。
