lacerate
læsəreɪt ˈlæsərˌeɪt ˈlæsərɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lacerates, present participle lacerating, past tense , past participle lacerated
VERB 划破，撕裂，割伤（皮肤） If something lacerates your skin, it cuts it badly and deeply. 划破，撕裂，割伤（皮肤） [Also VERB]

    它的爪子挠伤了他的大腿。

SYN tear, cut, wound, rend [literary]
