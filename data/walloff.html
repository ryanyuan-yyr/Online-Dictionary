wall off
PHRASAL VERB （用墙）隔开，分隔 If part of a place is walled off, it is separated from the rest of the place by a wall. （用墙）隔开，分隔

  • The side alley was walled off from the back garden. [be V-ed P from n]

    侧巷和后花园之间用墙隔开了。

  • ...a ring of cliffs that walled off the surrounding wilderness. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    把它同四周荒野隔开的一圈峭壁
