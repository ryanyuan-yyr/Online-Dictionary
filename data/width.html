width
wɪdθ wɪdθ wɪtθ
Word forms: plural widths
1 N-VAR 宽度;广度 The width of something is the distance it measures from one side or edge to the other. 宽度;广度

  • Measure the full width of the window. [+ of]

    测量一下窗户的全宽。

  • The road was reduced to 18ft in width by adding parking bays.

    这条路增加了停车区后宽度缩减到了18英尺。

  • Saddles are made in a wide range of different widths.

    鞍具有各种不同的宽度。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 宽阔；宽大 The width of something is its quality of being wide. 宽阔；宽大

  • The best utensil for steaming is a wok because its width easily accommodates a whole fish.

    锅是蒸鱼的最好器具，因为它很宽，可以轻松放下一整条鱼。

3 N-COUNT （游泳池的）池宽 A width is the distance from one side of a swimming pool to the other. （游泳池的）池宽

  • We swam several widths.

    我们游了好几个来回。
