bastardized
bɑːstəʳdaɪzd bæs- ˈbɑːstəˌdaɪzd
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 bastardised
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 次等的；劣质的 If you refer to something as a bastardized form of something else, you mean that the first thing is similar to or copied from the second thing, but is of much poorer quality. 次等的；劣质的 [formal, disapproval]
