made
meɪd meɪd
1 Made is the past tense and past participle of make .
2 ADJ （由…）构成的；（由…）制成的 If something is made of or made out of a particular substance, that substance was used to build it. （由…）构成的；（由…）制成的

  • The top of the table is made of glass. [+ of]

    桌面由玻璃制成。

  • What is the statue made out of? [+ out of]

    这个塑像是用什么做的？

3 PHRASE 有成功的把握;具备所需的一切条件 If you say that someone has it made or has got it made, you mean that they are certain to be rich or successful. 有成功的把握;具备所需的一切条件 [informal]

  • When I was at school, I thought I had it made.

    上学的时候，我觉得自己一定会出人头地。

-made -meɪd
COMB in ADJ [usually ADJ n] (与factory等词连用构成形容词)表示“…制造的”，“…生产的” -made combines with words such as 'factory' to make adjectives that indicate that something has been made or produced in a particular place or in a particular way. (与factory等词连用构成形容词)表示“…制造的”，“…生产的”

  • ...a British-made car.

    英国产的轿车

  • ...specially-made footwear.

    特制的鞋
