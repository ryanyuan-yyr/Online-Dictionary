foreign exchange
Word forms: plural foreign exchanges
1 N-PLURAL 外汇交易所;外汇机制 Foreign exchanges are the institutions or systems involved with changing one currency into another. 外汇交易所;外汇机制

  • On the foreign exchanges, the US dollar is up point forty-five.

    在外汇交易中，美元上涨了 0.45 个百分点。

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 外汇 Foreign exchange is used to refer to foreign currency that is obtained through the foreign exchange system. 外汇

  • ...an important source of foreign exchange.

    外汇的重要来源

  • ...foreign-exchange traders.

    外汇交易者
