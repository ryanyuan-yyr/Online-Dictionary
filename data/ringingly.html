ringing
rɪŋɪŋ ˈrɪŋɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 响亮的;洪亮的;嘹亮的 A ringing sound is loud and can be heard very clearly. 响亮的;洪亮的;嘹亮的

  • He hit the metal steps with a ringing crash.

    只听他咣当一声，摔在了金属台阶上。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (声明或宣言)强有力的，铿锵有力的，令人印象深刻的 A ringing statement or declaration is one that is made forcefully and is intended to make a powerful impression. (声明或宣言)强有力的，铿锵有力的，令人印象深刻的

  • So far we have had ringing declarations, but only limited action.

    目前，我们已经发表了一些有力的声明，但只采取了有限的行动。

  • ...the party's 14th Congress, which gave a ringing endorsement to capitalist-style economic reforms.

    该党第14届代表大会大力支持推行资本主义模式的经济改革
