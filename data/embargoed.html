embargo
ɪmbɑːʳgoʊ ɛmˈbɑːɡəʊ ɛmˈbɑrgoʊ ɪmˈbɑrgoʊ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense embargoes, present participle embargoing, past tense , past participle embargoed
1 N-COUNT 禁运;贸易禁令 If one country or group of countries imposes an embargo against another, it forbids trade with that country. 禁运;贸易禁令

  • The United Nations imposed an arms embargo against the country. [+ against]

    联合国对那个国家实施了武器禁运。

  • He has called on the government to lift its embargo on trade with Vietnam. [+ on]

    他呼吁政府解除对越南的贸易禁运。

SYN ban, bar, block, barrier  
2 VERB 禁运（货物） If goods of a particular kind are embargoed, people are not allowed to import them from a particular country or export them to a particular country. 禁运（货物）

  • The fruit was embargoed. [be VERB-ed]

    对水果实施禁运。

  • They embargoed oil shipments to the US.

    他们对美国实施石油禁运。

  • ...embargoed goods. [VERB-ed]

    禁运物资

SYN block, stop, bar, ban
