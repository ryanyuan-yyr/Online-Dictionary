unnoticed
ʌnnoʊtɪst ʌnˈnəʊtɪst
ADJ [usu ADJ after v, oft v-link ADJ, ADJ n] 未被看见的；未引起注意的；被忽视的 If something happens or passes unnoticed, it is not seen or noticed by anyone. 未被看见的；未引起注意的；被忽视的

  • I tried to slip up the stairs unnoticed.

    我试图趁没人注意溜上楼去。

  • Her forty-fourth birthday had just passed, unnoticed by all but herself. [+ by]

    她44岁的生日就这样过去了，除了她自己没人注意到。

SYN unobserved, disregarded, unseen, ignored
