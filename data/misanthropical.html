misanthropic
mɪzənθrɒpik ˌmɪsənˈθrɑpɪk ˌ mɪzənˈθrɑpɪk
ADJ-GRADED 遁世的;厌恶世人的 If you describe a person or their feelings as misanthropic, you mean that they do not like other people. 遁世的;厌恶世人的 [formal]

  • His father was a misanthropic but successful businessman.

    他的父亲不爱与人交往，却又是个成功的商人。

SYN antisocial, suspicious, cynical, sceptical
