softie
sɒfti ˈsɔfti ˈsɑfti
(also softy)Word forms: plural softies
N-COUNT 容易动恻隐之心的人;多愁善感的人 If you describe someone as a softie, you mean that they are very emotional or that they can easily be made to feel sympathy towards other people. 容易动恻隐之心的人;多愁善感的人 [informal]

  • He's just a big softie.

    他心太软。

  • Oh, so you're one of those softies who believe you should reason with criminals!

    哦，那么你也是那种认为可以跟罪犯理论的烂好人喽！
