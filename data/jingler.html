jingle
dʒɪŋgəl ˈdʒɪŋɡəl ˈdʒɪŋgəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense jingles, present participle jingling, past tense , past participle jingled
1 V-ERG （使）叮当响；（使)发出叮当声 When something jingles or when you jingle it, it makes a gentle ringing noise, like small bells. （使）叮当响；（使)发出叮当声

  • Brian put his hands in his pockets and jingled some change. [VERB noun]

    布赖恩把手插进口袋里，把几枚硬币拨弄得叮当作响。

  • Her bracelets jingled like bells. [VERB]

    她的手镯叮当作响，如铃声般悦耳。

  • ...jingling bottles. [VERB-ing]

    叮当作响的瓶子

SYN ring, rattle, clatter, chime  
Jingle is also a noun.

  • ...the jingle of money in a man's pocket.

    一个男人口袋里钱币叮当作响的碰撞声

2 N-COUNT （广播或电视中宣传产品或节目的）短歌，短曲，广告歌 A jingle is a short, simple tune, often with words, which is used to advertise a product or programme on radio or television. （广播或电视中宣传产品或节目的）短歌，短曲，广告歌

  • ...advertising jingles.

    广告短歌
