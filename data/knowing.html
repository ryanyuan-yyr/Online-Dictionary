knowing
noʊɪŋ ˈnoʊɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 会意的；心照不宣的；知情的 A knowing gesture or remark is one that shows that you understand something, for example the way that someone is feeling or what they really mean, even though it has not been mentioned directly. 会意的；心照不宣的；知情的

  • Ron gave her a knowing smile.

    罗恩朝她会意地一笑。

  • Dan exchanged a knowing look with Harry.

    丹和哈丽心照不宣地交换了一下眼神。

SYN meaningful, significant, expressive, eloquent  
knowingly

  • He smiled knowingly.

    他会意地笑了。
