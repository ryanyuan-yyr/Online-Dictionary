amenity
əmiːnɪti -men- əˈmɛnəti əˈminəti
Word forms: plural amenities
N-COUNT [usually plural] 生活福利设施；便利设施 Amenities are things such as shopping centres or sports facilities that are provided for people's convenience, enjoyment, or comfort. 生活福利设施；便利设施

  • The hotel amenities include health clubs, conference facilities, and banqueting rooms.

    饭店的便利设施包括健身俱乐部、会议设施和宴会厅。

SYN facility, service, advantage, comfort
