opiate
oʊpiət ˈoʊpiɪt ˈoʊpiˌeɪt
Word forms: plural opiates
1 N-COUNT （用于镇痛或安眠的）鸦片制剂 An opiate is a drug that contains opium. Opiates are used to reduce pain or to help people to sleep. （用于镇痛或安眠的）鸦片制剂

  • One woman suffered such severe pain she had to take opiates to control it.

    有一位妇女剧痛难忍，不得不服用鸦片制剂来镇痛。

SYN narcotic, drug, downer [slang] , painkiller  
2 N-COUNT （使人忽视重要事情的）麻醉品，慰藉物 If you call something an opiate, you disapprove of it because it makes people think less or spend less time on important activities. （使人忽视重要事情的）麻醉品，慰藉物 [disapproval]

  • ...the opiate of mass entertainment.

    起到麻痹作用的大众娱乐

  • It was Karl Marx who said, 'Religion is the opiate of the people'.

    卡尔·马克思曾说，“宗教是人民的精神鸦片。”
