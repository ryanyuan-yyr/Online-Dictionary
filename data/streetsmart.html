street smart
(also street-smart)
ADJ-GRADED (尤指)在大城市混得开的;能在艰难环境中生存的 Someone who is street smart knows how to deal with difficult or dangerous situations, especially in big cities. (尤指)在大城市混得开的;能在艰难环境中生存的 [mainly US, informal]

  • He is street smart and is not afraid of this neighborhood.

    他有都市人的精明劲儿，不怕在这附近混。
