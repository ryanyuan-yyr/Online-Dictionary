unrealistic
ʌnriəlɪstɪk ˌʌnrɪəˈlɪstɪk ˌʌnriəlˈɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 不切实际的；不可能达成的 If you say that someone is being unrealistic, you mean that they do not recognize the truth about a situation, especially about the difficulties involved in something they want to achieve. 不切实际的；不可能达成的

  • There are many who feel that the players are being completely unrealistic in their demands. [+ in]

    很多人都觉得这些球员的要求是完全不现实的。

  • It would be unrealistic to expect such a process ever to be completed.

    想要完成这个步骤是不可能的。

  • ...their unrealistic expectations of parenthood.

    他们在为人父母方面不切实际的期望

SYN impractical, romantic, improbable, unworkable  
unrealistically

  • Tom spoke unrealistically of getting a full-time job that paid an enormous sum.

    汤姆不切实际地谈到要找一份高薪的全职工作。

  • ...unrealistically high standards of expectation.

    不切实际的过高期望
