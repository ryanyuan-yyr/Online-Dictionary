unclassified
ʌnklæsɪfaɪd ʌnˈklæsɪˌfaɪd ʌnˈklæsɪˌfaɪd
1 ADJ （信息或文件）非保密的，无密级的 If information or a document is unclassified, it is not secret and is available to the general public. （信息或文件）非保密的，无密级的

  • The material consisted only of already published, unclassified information.

    这份材料里只有一些已公开的、非保密性质的信息。

2 ADJ 未定级的；未归类的 If something is unclassified, it has not been given a grade or put into a category, for example because it is of a low or basic standard. 未定级的；未归类的

  • ...a winding unclassified road.

    一条蜿蜒起伏、未编号的公路

  • ...an unclassified honours degree.

    未分级的荣誉学位
