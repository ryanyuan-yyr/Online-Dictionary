veiled
veɪld veɪld
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 间接表示的;含蓄的;不明言的 A veiled comment is expressed in a disguised form rather than directly and openly. 间接表示的;含蓄的;不明言的

  • He made only a veiled reference to international concerns over human rights issues.

    他只是含蓄地提到了国际社会对人权问题的关注。

  • This last clause is a thinly-veiled threat to those who might choose to ignore the decree.

    这最后一项条款明显是对那些可能会无视法令之人的威慑。

2 ADJ 蒙面纱的;戴面罩的 A woman or girl who is veiled is wearing a veil. 蒙面纱的;戴面罩的

  • A veiled woman gave me a kindly smile.

    一位戴面纱的女士朝我友好地微笑。
