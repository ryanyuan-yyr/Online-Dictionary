misconstrue
mɪskənstruː ˌmɪskənˈstru
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misconstrues, present participle misconstruing, past tense , past participle misconstrued
VERB 曲解;误解 If you misconstrue something that has been said or something that happens, you interpret it wrongly. 曲解;误解 [formal]

  • An outsider might misconstrue the nature of the relationship. [VERB noun]

    外人也许会误解这种关系的本质。

  • Jordan's policy has been misconstrued. [VERB noun]

    约旦的政策被曲解了。

SYN misinterpret, misunderstand, misjudge, misread
