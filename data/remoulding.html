remould
riːmoʊld riːmoʊld
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense remoulds, present participle remoulding, past tense , past participle remoulded
The spelling remold is used in American English. The noun is pronounced /'riːməʊld/. The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'məʊld/. 美国英语用remold。名词读作/'riːməʊld/。动词读作/ˌriː'məʊld/。
1 N-COUNT 翻新轮胎 A remould is an old tyre which has been given a new surface or tread and can be used again. 翻新轮胎 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 retread
2 VERB 改造，重塑(想法或经济) To remould something such as an idea or an economy means to change it so that it has a new structure or is based on new principles. 改造，重塑(想法或经济)

  • ...a new phase in the attempt to remould Labour's image. [VERB noun]

    重塑工党形象举措的新阶段
