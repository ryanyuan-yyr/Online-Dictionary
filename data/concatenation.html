concatenation
kɒnkætəneɪʃən kənˌkætənˈeɪʃən kɑnˌkætəneɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 一连串相关联的事情；接二连三的相关事件 A concatenation of things or events is their occurrence one after another, because they are linked. 一连串相关联的事情；接二连三的相关事件 [formal]

  • ...the Internet, the world's biggest concatenation of computing power. [+ of]

    因特网，世界上最强大的计算机连接

SYN series, linking, chain, connection
