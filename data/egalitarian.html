egalitarian
ɪgælɪteəriən ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛərɪən iˌgæləˈtɛriən
ADJ-GRADED 主张人人平等的;平等主义的 Egalitarian means supporting or following the idea that all people are equal and should have the same rights and opportunities. 主张人人平等的;平等主义的

  • I still believe in the notion of an egalitarian society.

    我仍然相信构建人人平等的社会的理念。

SYN equal, just, fair, equable
