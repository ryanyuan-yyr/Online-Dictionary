embroidery
ɪmbrɔɪdəri ɛmˈbrɔɪdəri ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri
Word forms: plural embroideries
1 N-VAR 绣花;刺绣品 Embroidery consists of designs stitched into cloth. 绣花;刺绣品

  • The shorts had blue embroidery over the pockets.

    这条短裤的口袋上有蓝色刺绣。

  • The panel contains an embroidery.

    这块饰片上有刺绣。

SYN sewing, tapestry, needlework, needlepoint  
2 N-UNCOUNT 刺绣 Embroidery is the activity of stitching designs onto cloth. 刺绣

  • She learned sewing, knitting and embroidery.

    她学了缝纫、编织和刺绣。
