death warrant
(also death-warrant)Word forms: plural death warrants
1 N-COUNT 死刑执行令 A death warrant is an official document which orders that someone is to be executed as a punishment for a crime. 死刑执行令
2 PHRASE 自寻死路;自取灭亡 If you say that someone is signing their own death warrant, you mean that they are behaving in a way which will cause their ruin or death. 自寻死路;自取灭亡

  • By accusing the King of murder, he signed his own death warrant.

    他指控国王谋杀，从而让自己走上了绝路。
