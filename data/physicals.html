physical
fɪzɪkəl ˈfɪzɪkəl
Word forms: plural physicals
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 身体的；肉体的；躯体的 Physical qualities, actions, or things are connected with a person's body, rather than with their mind. 身体的；肉体的；躯体的

  • ...the physical and mental problems caused by the illness.

    这种病引起的生理和心理的问题

  • Physical activity promotes good health.

    体育活动促进身体健康。

  • The attraction between them is physical.

    他们之间的彼此吸引是肉体上的。

SYN corporal, fleshly, bodily, carnal  
physically

  • You may be physically and mentally exhausted after a long flight.

    长途飞行后，你可能会感到身心疲惫。

  • ...disabled people who cannot physically use a telephone.

    不能亲手使用电话的残疾人

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 物质的；有形的；实物的 Physical things are real things that can be touched and seen, rather than ideas or spoken words. 物质的；有形的；实物的

  • Physical and ideological barriers had come down in Eastern Europe.

    物质和意识形态上的障碍在东欧已不复存在。

  • ...physical evidence to support the story.

    支持这种说法的实证

  • ...the physical similarities among the towns.

    城市之间外在的相似之处

SYN material, real, substantial [formal] , natural  
physically

  • ...physically cut off from every other country.

    地理上同其他国家隔绝

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 外部的；外观的；有形的 Physical means relating to the structure, size, or shape of something that can be touched and seen. 外部的；外观的；有形的

  • ...the physical characteristics of the terrain.

    这种地形的外在特征

  • ...the physical properties (weight, volume, hardness, etc.) of a substance.

    物质的外部特性（重量、体积、硬度等）

4 ADJ [ADJ n] 物理学的 Physical means connected with physics or the laws of physics. 物理学的

  • ...the physical laws of combustion and thermodynamics.

    燃烧和热力学物理定律

5 ADJ-GRADED 喜欢动手动脚的；有大量身体接触（或碰撞）的 Someone who is physical touches people a lot, either in an affectionate way or in a rough way. 喜欢动手动脚的；有大量身体接触（或碰撞）的

  • We decided that in the game we would be physical and aggressive.

    我们决定在比赛中积极拼抢，大胆进攻。

6 ADJ [ADJ n] 肉体的；性的 Physical is used in expressions such as physical love and physical relationships to refer to sexual relationships between people. 肉体的；性的

  • the book celebrated the sublime joys of physical love.

    本书赞美了性爱带来的无比美妙的愉悦。

  • It had been years since they had shared any meaningful form of physical relationship.

    他们已多年没有真正意义上的性生活了。

7 N-COUNT 体格检查；身体检查 A physical is a medical examination, done in order to see if someone is fit and well enough to do a particular job or to join the army. 体格检查；身体检查

  • Bob failed his physical.

    鲍勃体检不合格。

  • Routine physicals are done by a nurse.

    由一名护士做常规体检。
