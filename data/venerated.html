venerate
venəreɪt ˈvɛnəˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense venerates, present participle venerating, past tense , past participle venerated
VERB 尊重;敬仰;崇敬 If you venerate someone or something, you value them or feel great respect for them. 尊重;敬仰;崇敬 [formal]

  • My father venerated General Eisenhower. [VERB noun]

    我父亲十分敬仰艾森豪威尔将军。

SYN respect, honour, esteem, revere  
venerated

  • Jerusalem is Christianity's most venerated place.

    耶路撒冷被基督徒奉为圣地。

veneration
