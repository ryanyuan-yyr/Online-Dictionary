dupe
djuːp duːp dup djup
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dupes, present participle duping, past tense , past participle duped
1 VERB 愚弄;欺骗;诈骗 If a person dupes you, they trick you into doing something or into believing something which is not true. 愚弄;欺骗;诈骗

  • ...a plot to dupe stamp collectors into buying fake rarities. [VERB noun + into]

    诱骗集邮者购买珍稀邮票赝品的阴谋

  • We know some sex offenders dupe the psychologists who assess them. [VERB noun]

    我们知道有些性犯罪者会愚弄给他们做测评的心理学家。

SYN deceive, trick, cheat, con [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 受骗者;被愚弄者 A dupe is someone who is tricked by someone else. 受骗者;被愚弄者

  • He becomes an innocent dupe in a political scandal.

    他在一起政治丑闻中成了一名无辜的受骗者。

SYN victim, mug [British , slang] , sucker [slang] , pigeon [slang]
