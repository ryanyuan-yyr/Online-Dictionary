penchant
pɒnʃɒn pentʃənt ˈpɒŋʃɒŋ ˈpɛntʃənt
N-SING 爱好；嗜好；偏好 If someone has a penchant for something, they have a special liking for it or a tendency to do it. 爱好；嗜好；偏好 [formal]

  • ...a stylish woman with a penchant for dark glasses. [+ for]

    喜欢戴墨镜的时髦女子

  • He had a penchant for playing jokes on people.

    他喜欢拿人开玩笑。

SYN liking, taste, tendency, turn
