cross-check ˈkrɔsˌtʃɛk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cross-checks, present participle cross-checking, past tense , past participle cross-checked
VERB （用不同方法或从不同出处）反复核对,交叉检查 If you cross-check information, you check that it is correct using a different method or source from the one originally used to obtain it. （用不同方法或从不同出处）反复核对,交叉检查

  • You have to scrupulously check and cross-check everything you hear. [VERB noun]

    你必须仔细检查并多方核对你所听到的每一件事。

  • His version will later be cross-checked against that of the university. [be V-ed + against]

    之后会对他的说法与校方的说法进行多方核对。

  • They want to ensure such claims are justified by cross-checking with other records. [VERB + with]

    他们想要通过核对其他记录来确保这种主张是合理的。
