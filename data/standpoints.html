standpoint
stændpɔɪnt ˈstændˌpɔɪnt
Word forms: plural standpoints
N-COUNT [usu from N] 角度;立场;立足点 From a particular standpoint means looking at an event, situation, or idea in a particular way. 角度;立场;立足点

  • He believes that from a military standpoint, the situation is under control.

    他认为从军事角度看，形势已得到了控制。

  • From my standpoint, you know, this thing is just ridiculous.

    你要知道，依我看，这事简直太荒唐了。

SYN point of view, position, angle, viewpoint
