left-click
ˈlɛftklɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense left-clicks, present participle left-clicking, past tense , past participle left-clicked
VERB 左击；点击（鼠标）左键 To left-click or to left-click on something means to press the left-hand button on a computer mouse. 左击；点击（鼠标）左键 [computing]

  • When the menu has popped up you should left-click on one of the choices to make it operate. [VERB + on]

    弹出菜单后，须左击其中一个选项方可运行。
