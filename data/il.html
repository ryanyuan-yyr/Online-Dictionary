il-ɪl ɪl ɪl
Usually pronounced /'ɪl/ before an unstressed syllable, and /ɪl/ before a stressed syllable. 通常在非重读音节前重读,在重读音节前弱读。
PREFIX (用于以 l 开头的单词前,构成反义词)表示“不”,“非”,“无” Il- is added to words that begin with the letter 'l' to form words with the opposite meaning. (用于以 l 开头的单词前,构成反义词)表示“不”,“非”,“无”

  • ...an awful illegible signature.

    字迹潦草、难以辨认的签名

  • He could face a charge of illegally importing weapons.

    他可能面临非法进口武器的指控。
