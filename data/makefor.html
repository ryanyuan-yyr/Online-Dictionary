make for
1 PHRASAL VERB 向…走去;朝…前进 If you make for a place, you move towards it. 向…走去;朝…前进

  • He rose from his seat and made for the door. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他从椅子上起身向门口走去。

SYN head for, aim for, head towards, set out for  
2 PHRASAL VERB 导致;有利于 If something makes for another thing, it causes or helps to cause that thing to happen or exist. 导致;有利于 [informal]

  • A happy parent makes for a happy child. [VERB PREP. noun]

    有快乐的父母，才会有快乐的孩子。

SYN contribute to, produce, further, forward
