sinuous
sɪnjʊəs ˈsɪnjuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 摇曳生姿的;动作柔软的 Something that is sinuous moves with smooth twists and turns. 摇曳生姿的;动作柔软的 [literary]

  • ...the silent, sinuous approach of a snake through the long grass.

    一条蛇灵活地扭动身躯，穿过高高的草丛悄悄地靠近

  • He has a distinctively sinuous way of walking.

    他走路扭来扭去，很有特点。

SYN supple, flexible, lithe, graceful  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 蜿蜒的;弯弯曲曲的 Something that is sinuous has many smooth turns and curves. 蜿蜒的;弯弯曲曲的 [literary]

  • I drove along sinuous mountain roads.

    我行驶在蜿蜒的山路上。

SYN curving, winding, meandering, crooked
