teething
tiːðɪŋ ˈtiːðɪŋ
VERB [only cont] (婴儿)出牙，长乳牙 When babies are teething, their teeth are starting to appear through their gums, often causing them pain. (婴儿)出牙，长乳牙

  • Emma broke off a bit of rusk and gave it to Jacinta, who was teething. [VERB]

    埃玛掰了一块脆饼干给正在出牙的贾辛塔。

  • Camomile has long been used as a remedy for teething babies. [VERB-ing]

    春黄菊长期以来一直用作减轻婴儿出牙时的不适的药物。

Teething is also a noun.

  • Teething can be painful and make your baby irritable.

    长牙会很难受，婴儿会烦躁不安。
