formalism
fɔːʳməlɪzəm ˈfɔrməlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT (尤指艺术中的)形式主义 Formalism is a style, especially in art, in which great attention is paid to the outward form or appearance rather than to the inner reality or significance of things. (尤指艺术中的)形式主义

  • ...the rigid formalism of classical ballet.

    古典芭蕾僵化的形式主义

formalist

  • ...art based on formalist principles.

    基于形式主义原则的艺术
