mind-numbing
ADJ-GRADED （糟糕、无聊或程度强烈到）令人头脑麻木的 If you describe an event or experience as mind-numbing, you mean that it is so bad, boring, or great in extent that you are unable to think about it clearly. （糟糕、无聊或程度强烈到）令人头脑麻木的

  • It was another day of mind-numbing tedium.

    又是一个令人麻木的单调乏味的日子。

mind-numbingly

  • ...a mind-numbingly boring sport.

    无聊透顶的运动
