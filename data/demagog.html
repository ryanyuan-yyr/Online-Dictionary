demagogue
deməgɒg -gɔːg ˈdɛməˌgɑg ˌdɛməˌgɔg
Word forms: plural demagogues
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 demagog
N-COUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 蛊惑民心者;煽动民情的政客 If you say that someone such as a politician is a demagogue you are criticizing them because you think they try to win people's support by appealing to their emotions rather than using reasonable arguments. 蛊惑民心者;煽动民情的政客 [disapproval]
SYN agitator, firebrand, haranguer, rabble-rouser
