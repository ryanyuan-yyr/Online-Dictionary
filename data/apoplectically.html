apoplectic
æpəplektɪk ˌæpəˈplɛktɪkəl ˌæpəˈplɛktɪk
ADJ 怒不可遏的；愤怒至极的 If someone is apoplectic, they are extremely angry about something. 怒不可遏的；愤怒至极的 [formal]

  • It's enough to make them choke with apoplectic rage.

    足以气得让他们说不出话来。

  • My father was apoplectic when he discovered the truth.

    我父亲在发现真相后勃然大怒。

SYN furious, mad [informal] , raging, boiling
