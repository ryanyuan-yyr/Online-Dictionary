code of conduct kəʊd əv ˈkɒndʌkt
Word forms: plural codes of conduct
N-COUNT 行为准则 The code of conduct for a group or organization is an agreement on rules of behaviour for the members of that group or organization. 行为准则

  • Doctors in Britain say a new code of conduct is urgently needed to protect the doctor-patient relationship.

    英国的医生说急需新的行为准则来保护医患关系。
