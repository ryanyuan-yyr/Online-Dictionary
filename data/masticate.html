masticate
mæstɪkeɪt ˈmæstɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense masticates, present participle masticating, past tense , past participle masticated
VERB 咀嚼;嚼碎 When you masticate food, you chew it. 咀嚼;嚼碎 [formal]

  • Her mouth was working, as if she was masticating some tasty titbit. [VERB noun]

    她的嘴在动，好像在嚼什么好吃的。

  • Don't gulp everything down without masticating. [VERB]

    不要不加咀嚼就把什么都给一股脑地吞下去。

SYN chew, eat, champ, crunch
