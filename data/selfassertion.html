self-assertion
ˈsɛlfəˈsɜrʃən
N-UNCOUNT 主见;自我主张 Self-assertion is confidence that you have in speaking firmly about your opinions and demanding the rights that you believe you should have. 主见;自我主张

  • ...her silence and lack of self-assertion.

    她的沉默和缺乏主见

  • They are driven on partly by a wish for democracy, but also by a desire for national self-assertion.

    驱动他们的一方面是对民主的期待，另一方面是对民族自立的渴望。
