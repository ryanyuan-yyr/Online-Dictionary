shiver
ʃɪvəʳ ˈʃɪvər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shivers, present participle shivering, past tense , past participle shivered
1 VERB (因寒冷或惧怕而)哆嗦，颤抖，发抖 When you shiver, your body shakes slightly because you are cold or frightened. (因寒冷或惧怕而)哆嗦，颤抖，发抖

  • He shivered in the cold. [VERB]

    他冻得发抖。

  • I was sitting on the floor shivering with fear. [VERB + with]

    我坐在地板上，吓得发抖。

SYN shudder, shake, tremble, quake  
Shiver is also a noun.

  • The emptiness here sent shivers down my spine.

    这里的空寂让我脊梁骨发凉。

  • Alice gave a shiver of delight.

    艾丽斯乐得直抖。
