greatly
greɪtli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb, ADVERB adjective] 非常地；极大地 You use greatly to emphasize the degree or extent of something. 非常地；极大地 [formal, emphasis]

  • People would benefit greatly from a pollution-free vehicle.

    人们将享受到无污染汽车的诸多好处。

  • We were greatly honoured that Sheik Hasina took the trouble to visit us.

    谢赫·哈西娜专程前来看望我们，对此我们深感荣幸。

SYN very much, much, hugely, vastly
