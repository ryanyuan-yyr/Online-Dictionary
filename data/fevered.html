fevered
fiːvəʳd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 亢奋的;躁动不安的 Fevered is used to describe feelings of great excitement, and the activities that result from them. 亢奋的;躁动不安的 [written]

  • Meg was in a state of fevered anticipation.

    梅格处于一种兴奋的期待中。

  • ...fevered speculation over the leadership.

    对领导班子的热烈猜测

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 发热的;发烧的 If a person is fevered, or they have a fevered brow, they are suffering from a fever. 发热的;发烧的 [literary]

  • ...her fevered brow.

    她发烫的额头
