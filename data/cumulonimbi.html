cumulonimbus
kyumyələnimbəs ˌkjuːmjʊləʊˈnɪmbəs ˌkjumjəˌloʊˈnɪmbəs
(also cumulo-nimbus)Word forms: cumulonimbi
N-VAR 积雨云Cumulonimbus is a type of cloud, similar to cumulus, that extends to a great height and is associated with thunderstorms. 积雨云 [technical]
