notch up
PHRASAL VERB 赢得;达到 If you notch up something such as a score or total, you achieve it. 赢得;达到 [journalism] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He had notched up more than 25 victories worldwide. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他已赢得了超过25次国际级别赛事的胜利。

  • The economy is expanding, notching up high growth rates. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    经济在发展，实现了高增长率。

SYN achieve, make, score, gain
