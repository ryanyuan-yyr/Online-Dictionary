baccalaureate
bækəlɔːriət ˌbækəˈlɔriɪt
Word forms: plural baccalaureates
1 N-SING （法国和其他一些国家的）中学毕业会考 The baccalaureate is an examination taken by students at the age of eighteen in France and some other countries. （法国和其他一些国家的）中学毕业会考
2 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] （美国的）学位授予仪式（或演讲） In the United States, a baccalaureate service or address is a service that is held or a talk that is given during the ceremony when students receive their degrees. （美国的）学位授予仪式（或演讲）
