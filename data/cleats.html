stud
stʌd stʌd
Word forms: plural studs
1 N-COUNT (固定在物体表面用于装饰的)嵌钉，饰钉 A studs are small pieces of metal which are attached to a surface for decoration. (固定在物体表面用于装饰的)嵌钉，饰钉

  • You see studs on lots of London front doors.

    在伦敦，你能看到许多人家的大门上都装有饰钉。

SYN rivet, tack, press-stud, tintack  
2 N-COUNT 耳钉 A stud is an earring consisting of a small shape attached to a bar which goes through a hole in your ear. 耳钉

  • ...plain gold studs.

    素金耳钉

3 N-COUNT (尤指运动靴的)防滑钉，鞋钉 Studs are small round objects attached to the bottom of boots, especially sports boots, so that the person wearing them does not slip. (尤指运动靴的)防滑钉，鞋钉 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 cleats
4 N-UNCOUNT 种马;种畜 Horses or other animals that are kept for stud are kept to be used for breeding. 种马;种畜

  • He was voted horse of the year and then was retired to stud.

    这匹马被评为年度冠军后就退役成了种马。

5 N-COUNT 性欲旺盛的男子;风流男子 If you refer to a man as a stud, you mean that he is thought to be very active sexually and good at satisfying his partner's sexual desires. 性欲旺盛的男子;风流男子 [informal]
