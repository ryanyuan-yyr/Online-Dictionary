ease off
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （在程度、速度、强度等上）缓和，减轻，减弱 If something eases off, or a person or thing eases it off, it is reduced in degree, speed, or intensity. （在程度、速度、强度等上）缓和，减轻，减弱 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • These days, the pressure has eased off. [VERB PREP.]

    最近，压力已经减轻。

  • The rain had eased off. [VERB PREP.]

    雨小了。

  • Kelly eased off his pace as they reached the elevator. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当他们走到电梯前时，凯利放慢了步子。

SYN reduce, decrease, lessen, curtail
