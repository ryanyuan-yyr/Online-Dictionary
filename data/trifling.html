trifling
traɪfəlɪŋ ˈtraɪflɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 不重要的;微不足道的 A trifling matter is small and unimportant. 不重要的;微不足道的

  • The guests had each paid £250, no trifling sum…

    客人每位付了250英镑，可不是一笔小数目。

  • Outside California these difficulties may seem fairly trifling.

    在别处，加利福尼亚的这些困难可能看起来相当微不足道。

  • ...a comparatively trifling 360 yards.

    相较而言咫尺距离的 360 码

SYN insignificant, small, tiny, empty
