mummify
mʌmɪfaɪ ˈmʌməˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mummifies, present participle mummifying, past tense , past participle mummified
VERB [usually passive] 在(尸体)上涂敷香料再裹布保存;将(尸体)制成木乃伊 If a dead body is mummified, it is preserved, for example by rubbing it with special oils and wrapping it in cloth. 在(尸体)上涂敷香料再裹布保存;将(尸体)制成木乃伊

  • In America, people are paying up to $150,000 to be mummified after death. [be VERB-ed]

    在美国，死后将尸体制成木乃伊的费用高达15万美元。

  • ...the mummified pharaoh. [VERB-ed]

    法老木乃伊
