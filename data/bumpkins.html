bumpkin
bʌmpkɪn ˈbʌmpkɪn
Word forms: plural bumpkins
N-COUNT 乡巴佬；土包子 If you refer to someone as a bumpkin, you think they are uneducated and stupid because they come from the countryside. 乡巴佬；土包子 [disapproval]

  • ...unsophisticated country bumpkins.

    不通世故的乡巴佬

SYN yokel, peasant [informal] , hick [informal , mainly US , Canadian] , rustic
