fraction
frækʃən ˈfrækʃən
Word forms: plural fractions
1 N-COUNT 少量;小份;一点儿 A fraction of something is a tiny amount or proportion of it. 少量;小份;一点儿

  • She hesitated for a fraction of a second before responding. [+ of]

    她在回答之前犹豫了一下。

  • Here's how to eat like the stars, at a fraction of the cost.

    这就是如何花少量钱却能像明星一样吃喝的方法。

  • I opened my eyes just a fraction.

    我微微睁开了眼睛。

SYN bit, little bit, mite, jot  
2 N-COUNT 分数 A fraction is a number that can be expressed as a proportion of two whole numbers. For example, ½ and ⅓ are both fractions. 分数

  • The students had a grasp of decimals, percentages and fractions.

    学生们掌握了小数、百分数和分数。
