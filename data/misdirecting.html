misdirect
mɪsdɪrekt -daɪr- ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkt ˌmɪsdəˈrɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misdirects, present participle misdirecting, past tense , past participle misdirected
1 VERB [usually passive] 对…使用不当;错用;误用 If resources or efforts are misdirected, they are used in the wrong way or for the wrong purposes. 对…使用不当;错用;误用

  • Many of the aid projects in the developing world have been misdirected in the past. [be VERB-ed]

    过去，许多对发展中国家的援助计划没有得到合理实施。

misdirected

  • ...a misdirected effort to mollify the bishop.

    试图安抚主教的白费力气的做法

2 VERB 将…引错方向;给…指错方向 If you misdirect someone, you send them in the wrong direction. 将…引错方向;给…指错方向

  • He had deliberately misdirected the reporters. [VERB noun]

    他故意给记者们指错方向。
