fusty
fʌsti ˈfʌsti
Word forms: comparative fustier, superlative fustiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (想法或人)过时的，守旧的 If you describe something or someone as fusty, you disapprove of them because they are old-fashioned in attitudes or ideas. (想法或人)过时的，守旧的 [disapproval]

  • The fusty old establishment refused to recognise the demand for popular music.

    这一古板的老旧机构拒绝承认人们对流行音乐的需求。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 发霉味的;朽霉的 A fusty place or thing has a smell that is not fresh or pleasant. 发霉味的;朽霉的

  • ...fusty old carpets.

    发霉味的旧地毯
