inadmissible
ɪnədmɪsɪbəl ˌɪnædˈmɪsəbəl ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbəl
1 ADJ (证据)不予采信的 Inadmissible evidence cannot be used in a court of law. (证据)不予采信的

  • The judge ruled that the evidence was inadmissible.

    法官裁定该证据不能被采信。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 绝不可接受的;绝对不行的 If you say that something that someone says or does is inadmissible, you think that it is totally unacceptable. 绝不可接受的;绝对不行的 [disapproval]

  • He said the use of force would be inadmissible.

    他说使用武力是万万不可的。
