heresy
herɪsi ˈhɛrəsi
Word forms: plural heresies
1 N-VAR （与大多数人的观点不合的）异端，邪说 Heresy is a belief or action that most people think is wrong, because it disagrees with beliefs that are generally accepted. （与大多数人的观点不合的）异端，邪说

  • It might be considered heresy to suggest such a notion.

    提出这样一个观点可能会被视为异端邪说。

SYN unorthodoxy, apostasy, dissidence, impiety  
2 N-VAR （与某种宗教的教义严重不合的）异教，异端，邪说 Heresy is a belief or action which seriously disagrees with the principles of a particular religion. （与某种宗教的教义严重不合的）异教，异端，邪说

  • He said it was a heresy to suggest that women should not conduct services.

    他说认为女性不应该主持宗教仪式的观点是一种异端邪说。
