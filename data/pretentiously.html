pretentious
prɪtenʃəs priˈtɛnʃəs prɪˈtɛnʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 自命不凡的；自负的；矫饰的；炫耀的 If you say that someone or something is pretentious, you mean that they try to seem important or significant, but you do not think that they are. 自命不凡的；自负的；矫饰的；炫耀的 [disapproval]

  • His response was full of pretentious nonsense.

    他的答复是一派荒谬的虚夸之词。

  • This pub was of a very different type, smaller, less pretentious.

    这个酒吧风格独特，面积不大，比较低调。

SYN affected, mannered, exaggerated, pompous  
pretentiousness

  • He has a tendency towards pretentiousness.

    他总是想表现得自命不凡。
