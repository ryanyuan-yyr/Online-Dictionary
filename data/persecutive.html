persecute
pɜːʳsɪkjuːt ˈpɜrsəˌkjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense persecutes, present participle persecuting, past tense , past participle persecuted
1 VERB (因种族、信仰）迫害，残害，压迫 If someone is persecuted, they are treated cruelly and unfairly, often because of their race or beliefs. (因种族、信仰）迫害，残害，压迫

  • Mr Weaver and his family have been persecuted by the authorities for their beliefs. [be VERB-ed]

    韦弗先生和他的家人由于其信仰而遭到当局的迫害。

  • They began by brutally persecuting the Catholic Church. [VERB noun]

    他们开始残酷迫害天主教。

  • ...a persecuted minority. [VERB-ed]

    受迫害的少数派

SYN victimize, hunt, injure, pursue  
2 VERB 找…的麻烦；骚扰；纠缠 If you say that someone is persecuting you, you mean that they are deliberately making your life difficult. 找…的麻烦；骚扰；纠缠

  • He said his first wife persecuted him with her unreasonable demands. [VERB noun]

    他说他的第一任妻子总是拿无理要求纠缠他。

  • Vic was bullied by his father and persecuted by his sisters. [VERB noun]

    维克受到父亲的欺压和姊妹们的纠缠。
