poor relation
Word forms: plural poor relations
N-COUNT （相似或从属但）相形见绌的东西 If you describe one thing as a poor relation of another, you mean that it is similar to or part of the other thing, but is considered to be inferior to it. （相似或从属但）相形见绌的东西

  • Watercolour still seems somehow to be the poor relation of oil painting. [+ of]

    水彩画似乎仍比油画略逊一筹。
