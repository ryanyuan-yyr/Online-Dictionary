jiggery-pokery
dʒɪgəri poʊkəri ˈdʒɪgəriˈpoʊkəri
N-UNCOUNT 欺骗行为；骗局 If you describe behaviour as jiggery-pokery, you mean that it involves tricking people or being dishonest. 欺骗行为；骗局 [British, informal, old-fashioned]

  • It seems astonishing that Bond got away with so much jiggery-pokery for as long as he did.

    虽然邦德遭遇的诡计层出不穷，但最后却总能脱离险境，令人惊叹。
