cool down
1 →see: cool 5 
2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)冷静;(使)镇定 If someone cools down or if you cool them down, they become less angry than they were. (使)冷静;(使)镇定

  • He has had time to cool down and look at what happened more objectively. [VERB PREP.]

    他已经冷静了一段时间，能更加客观地看待所发生的事情。

  • First McNeil had to cool down the volatile Australian 20-year old. [VERB PREP. noun]

    首先麦克尼尔得让那个脾气暴躁的20岁澳大利亚小伙子冷静下来。
