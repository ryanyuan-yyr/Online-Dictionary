error
erəʳ ˈɛrər
Word forms: plural errors
1 N-VAR 错误;差错 An error is something you have done which is considered to be incorrect or wrong, or which should not have been done. 错误;差错

  • NASA discovered a mathematical error in its calculations. [+ in]

    美国国家航空航天局在它的计算结果中发现了一个数学错误。

  • MPs attacked lax management and errors of judgment. [+ of]

    议员们抨击管理的松散和判断的失误。

SYN mistake, slip, fault, blunder  
2 PHRASE 错误地；失误地 If you do something in error or if it happens in error, you do it or it happens because you have made a mistake, especially in your judgment. 错误地；失误地

  • The plane was shot down in error by a NATO missile.

    一枚北约的导弹误将那架飞机击落。

SYN accidentally, casually, unexpectedly, incidentally  
3 PHRASE （认识到）错误;（意识到）失误 If someone sees the error of their ways, they realize or admit that they have made a mistake or behaved badly. （认识到）错误;（意识到）失误

  • I wanted an opportunity to talk some sense into him and try to make him see the error of his ways.

    我想找个机会开导他一下，好让他认识到自己做错了。
