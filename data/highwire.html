high wire
(also high-wire)Word forms: plural high wires
1 N-COUNT （表演平衡动作用的）高空钢丝，空中绳索 A high wire is a length of rope or wire stretched tight high above the ground and used for balancing acts. （表演平衡动作用的）高空钢丝，空中绳索
2 N-SING [oft NOUN n] （报刊用语）危险的状态，走钢丝，（行为）容易犯错误的 Journalists talk about a person being on a high wire or performing a high-wire act when he or she is dealing with a situation in which it would be easy to do the wrong thing. （报刊用语）危险的状态，走钢丝，（行为）容易犯错误的

  • This year's Budget looks set to be a precarious high-wire act for the Chancellor.

    编制今年的政府预算对财政大臣来说就好像是在走钢丝。
