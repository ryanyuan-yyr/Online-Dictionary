calorie
kæləri ˈkæləri
Word forms: plural calories
1 N-COUNT 卡，卡路里(热量单位) Calories are units used to measure the energy value of food. People who are on diets try to eat food that does not contain many calories. 卡，卡路里(热量单位)

  • A glass of wine does have quite a lot of calories.

    一杯葡萄酒的热量确实不低。

  • ...calorie controlled diets.

    控制热量的饮食
