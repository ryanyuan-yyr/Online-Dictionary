municipality
mjuːnɪsɪpælɪti mjuˌnɪsəˈpæləti
Word forms: plural municipalities
1 N-COUNT (英国的)自治市，市政府 In Britain, a municipality is a city or town which is governed by its own locally-appointed officials. You can also refer to a city's or town's local government as a municipality . (英国的)自治市，市政府
2 N-COUNT (美国能自行选举政府的)自治市 In the United States, a municipality is a city or town that is incorporated and can elect its own government, which is also called a municipality . (美国能自行选举政府的)自治市
