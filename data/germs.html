germ
dʒɜːʳm dʒɜrm
Word forms: plural germs
1 N-COUNT 细菌；病菌 A germ is a very small organism that causes disease. 细菌；病菌

  • Chlorine is widely used to kill germs.

    氯气被广泛用于杀菌。

  • ...a germ that destroyed hundreds of millions of lives.

    夺去了无数生命的细菌

SYN microbe, virus, bug [informal] , bacterium  
2 N-SING 起源；发端；萌芽 The germ of something such as an idea is something which developed or might develop into that thing. 起源；发端；萌芽

  • The germ of an idea took root in Rosemary's mind. [+ of]

    罗斯玛丽心里萌生了一个念头。

  • This was the germ of a book.

    这是一本书的源起。

SYN beginning, root, seed, origin  
3 See also: wheatgerm
