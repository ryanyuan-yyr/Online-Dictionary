nightly
naɪtli ˈnaɪtlɪ ˈnaɪtli
ADJ [ADJ n] 每夜的;每晚的 A nightly event happens every night. 每夜的;每晚的

  • I'm sure we watched the nightly news, and then we turned on the movie.

    我肯定我们是先看了晚间新闻，然后才开始看的电影。

  • For months at a time, air raids were a nightly occurrence.

    一度有几个月，每天晚上都会发生空袭。

Nightly is also an adverb.

  • She appears nightly on the television news.

    她每晚在电视新闻里露面。

  • A new and younger crowd filled the bar nightly.

    一群新来的更年轻的人每晚都挤满了酒吧。
