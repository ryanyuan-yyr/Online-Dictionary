wall up
PHRASAL VERB 砌墙封闭（或堵塞） If someone walls up a room, or if someone is walled up in it, walls are built blocking every door so that nobody can get in or out. 砌墙封闭（或堵塞） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They had walled up her room because of the fear that things might be infected. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    因为担心物品可能带上病菌，他们砌墙堵死了她的屋子。

SYN close, block off or up, secure, seal
