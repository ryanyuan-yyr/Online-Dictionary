short-sighted
(also shortsighted)
1 ADJ-GRADED 近视的 If you are short-sighted, you cannot see things properly when they are far away, because there is something wrong with your eyes. 近视的 [mainly British]

  • Testing showed her to be very short-sighted.

    检查表明她近视得很厉害。

SYN near-sighted, myopic, blind as a bat  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 near-sighted
short-sightedness

  • Radical eye surgery promises to cure short-sightedness.

    彻底的眼部手术有望治愈近视。

2 ADJ-GRADED 目光短浅的;无远见的 If someone is short-sighted about something, or if their ideas are short-sighted, they do not make proper or careful judgments about the future. 目光短浅的;无远见的

  • Environmentalists fear that this is a short-sighted approach to the problem of global warming.

    环境保护主义者担心这并非解决全球变暖问题的长久之计。

  • I think we're being very short-sighted.

    我认为我们这样做目光很短浅。

SYN imprudent, injudicious, ill-advised, unthinking  
short-sightedness

  • The government now recognises the short-sightedness of this approach.

    现在政府认识到这个方法缺乏远见。
