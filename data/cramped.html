cramped
kræmpt kræmpt
ADJ-GRADED 狭窄的;狭小的 A cramped room or building is not big enough for the people or things in it. 狭窄的;狭小的

  • There are hundreds of families living in cramped conditions on the floor of the airport lounge.

    数百个家庭住在机场候机室拥挤的地板上。

  • In later years he lived in a rather cramped little flat in Bristol.

    他晚年住在布里斯托尔一间相当狭小的公寓内。

SYN restricted, confined, overcrowded, crowded
