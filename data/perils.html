peril
perɪl ˈpɛrəl
Word forms: plural perils
1 N-VAR 重大危险 Perils are great dangers. 重大危险 [formal]

  • ...the perils of the sea. [+ of]

    海洋的凶险

  • In spite of great peril, I have survived.

    虽然处境非常危险,我还是挺过来了。

  • We are in the gravest peril.

    我们身处非常严重的危机之中。

2 N-PLURAL [with poss] （某活动或行为的）危险，问题 The perils of a particular activity or course of action are the dangers or problems it may involve. （某活动或行为的）危险，问题

  • ...the perils of starring in a television commercial. [+ of]

    拍电视广告的危险

SYN pitfall, problem, risk, hazard  
3 PHRASE （用于警告）自冒风险 If you say that someone does something at their peril, you are warning them that they will probably suffer as a result of doing it. （用于警告）自冒风险

  • Ignore it at your peril.

    无视该问题要自担风险。

  • Anyone who breaks the law does so at their peril.

    违法者要自担后果。
