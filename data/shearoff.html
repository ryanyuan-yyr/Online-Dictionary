shear off
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 切断，剪切;(使金属等)断裂 If something such as a piece of metal shears off, or if it is sheared off, it breaks. 切断，剪切;(使金属等)断裂

  • It was not yet clear how the rudder had sheared off. [VERB PREP.]

    还不清楚船舵是如何断裂的。

  • The aircraft's wings were sheared off in the crash. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    机翼在坠机时断裂了。
