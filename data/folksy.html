folksy
foʊksi ˈfoʊksi
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 朴素的;有民间风格的;土里土气的 If you describe something as folksy, you mean that it is simple and has a style characteristic of folk craft and tradition. You sometimes use folksy to show disapproval of something because it seems unsophisticated. 朴素的;有民间风格的;土里土气的

  • ...folksy country furniture.

    朴素的乡村风味的家具

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 友好的;随意的;和气的 If you describe someone as folksy, you mean that they are friendly and informal in their behaviour. 友好的;随意的;和气的 [US, approval]

  • ...an elderly, folksy postman.

    为人随和的老邮递员
