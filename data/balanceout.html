balance out
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）平衡；（使）相抵；（使）抵消 If two or more opposite things balance out or if you balance them out, they become equal in amount, value, or effect. （使）平衡；（使）相抵；（使）抵消 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Outgoings and revenues balanced out. [VERB PREP.]

    收支相抵。

  • The strenuous exercise undergone could balance out the increased calories. [VERB PREP. noun]

    所进行的剧烈运动可以消耗掉增加的热量。
