peremptory
pəremptəri pərˈɛmptəri
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 跋扈的；专横的；霸道的 Someone who does something in a peremptory way does it in a way that shows that they expect to be obeyed immediately. 跋扈的；专横的；霸道的 [formal, disapproval]

  • With a brief, almost peremptory gesture he pointed to a chair.

    他做了一个简单的手势，近乎霸道地指着椅子。

peremptorily

  • 'Hello!' the voice said, more peremptorily. 'Who is it? Who do you want?'

    “喂！”那声音更加霸道地说道，“谁啊？你要干什么？”
