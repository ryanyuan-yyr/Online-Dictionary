administrator
ædmɪnɪstreɪtəʳ ædˈmɪnɪsˌtreɪtər ədˈmɪnɪsˌtreɪtər
Word forms: plural administrators
N-COUNT 经营者；管理者；监督者 An administrator is a person whose job involves helping to organize and supervise the way that an organization or institution functions. 经营者；管理者；监督者
SYN manager, head, official, director
