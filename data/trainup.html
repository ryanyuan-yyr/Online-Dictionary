train up
PHRASAL VERB 充分训练(或培训) If someone trains you up, they teach you new skills or give you the necessary preparation so that you will reach the standard required for a particular job or activity. 充分训练(或培训) [British, informal]

  • The first companies to go in took a policy of employing East Germans and training them up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    首批进驻的公司采取了雇佣东德人并对他们进行全面培训的方针。

  • He usually preferred to train up a crew of enthusiastic young sailors from scratch. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他通常更喜欢从零开始对一班满怀热情的年轻船员从头到脚进行训练。
