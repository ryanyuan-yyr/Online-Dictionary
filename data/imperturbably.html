imperturbable
ɪmpəʳtɜːʳbəbəl ˌɪmpərˈtɜrbəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不易激动的;沉着的;冷静的 If you describe someone as imperturbable, you mean that they remain calm, even in disturbing or dangerous situations. 不易激动的;沉着的;冷静的 [written]

  • Thomas, of course, was cool and aloof and imperturbable.

    当然,托马斯沉着、冷漠,不易激动。

SYN calm, cool, collected, composed
