thwack
θwæk θwæk
Word forms: plural thwacks
N-COUNT; SOUND 撞击声;拍打声 A thwack is a sound made when two solid objects hit each other hard. 撞击声;拍打声

  • I listened to the thwack of the metal balls. [+ of]

    我听着金属球相撞的声音。

  • Then the woodcutter let his axe fly — Thwack! Everyone heard it.

    然后那个伐木工脱手甩出了斧头。哐！每个人都听见了。

SYN smack, blow, whack, bash [informal]
