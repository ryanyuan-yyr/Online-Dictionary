ragtag
rægtæg ˈræɡˌtæɡ ˈrægˌtæg
(also rag-tag)
ADJ [ADJ n] 混杂的；无组织纪律的 If you want to say that a group of people or an organization is badly organized and not very respectable, you can describe it as a ragtag group or organization. 混杂的；无组织纪律的 [informal]

  • We started out with a little rag-tag team of 30 people.

    我们最初只是一支由30人组成的散兵游勇的队伍。
