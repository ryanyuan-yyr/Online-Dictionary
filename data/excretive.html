excrete
ɪkskriːt ɪkˈskriːt ɛksˈkrit ɪksˈkrit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense excretes, present participle excreting, past tense , past participle excreted
VERB 排泄 When a person or animal excretes waste matter from their body, they get rid of it in faeces, urine, or sweat. 排泄 [technical, formal] [Also VERB]

  • Your open pores excrete sweat and dirt. [VERB noun]

    通过张开的毛孔排出汗液和污垢。

  • Calcium is excreted in the urine and stools. [VERB noun]

    钙通过尿液和粪便排出体外。

SYN defecate, discharge, expel, evacuate
