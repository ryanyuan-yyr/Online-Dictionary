purvey
pəʳveɪ pərˈveɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense purveys, present participle purveying, past tense , past participle purveyed
1 VERB 散布；传播 If you purvey something such as information, you tell it to people. 散布；传播 [formal]

  • ...one who would, for a hefty fee, purvey strategic advice to private corporations. [VERB noun]

    一个会为了一笔数目可观的酬金而将策略性建议透露给私营企业的人

2 VERB 提供；供应 If someone purveys goods or services, they provide them. 提供；供应 [formal]

  • They have two restaurants that purvey dumplings and chicken noodle soup. [VERB noun]

    他们那里有两家供应饺子和鸡肉汤面的餐馆。
