irrational
ɪræʃənəl ɪˈræʃənəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 不合逻辑的;不合理的;荒谬的 If you describe someone's feelings and behaviour as irrational, you mean they are not based on logical reasons or clear thinking. 不合逻辑的;不合理的;荒谬的

  • ...an irrational fear of science.

    对科学的莫名恐惧

SYN illogical, crazy [informal] , silly, absurd  
irrationally

  • The market is behaving irrationally.

    市场已失去了理性。

  • My husband is irrationally jealous over my past loves.

    我丈夫无端地嫉妒我曾经的恋人。

irrationality

  • ...the irrationality of his behaviour.

    他行为的荒谬
