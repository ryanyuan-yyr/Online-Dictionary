glassy
glɑːsi glæsi ˈglæsi ˈglɑsi
1 ADJ-GRADED 像玻璃一样的；光滑的；光亮透明的 If you describe something as glassy, you mean that it is very smooth and shiny, like glass. 像玻璃一样的；光滑的；光亮透明的 [written]

  • The water was glassy.

    水面清澈明亮。

  • ...glassy green pebbles.

    光滑的绿卵石

SYN smooth, clear, slick, shiny  
2 ADJ-GRADED （眼神或表情）呆滞的，无表情的 If you describe someone's eyes or expression as glassy, you mean that they are showing no feeling, emotion, or awareness. （眼神或表情）呆滞的，无表情的 [mainly written]

  • He was very still, his eyes glassy.

    他纹丝不动，目光呆滞。

  • Henry gave Paul a glassy-eyed stare.

    亨利木然地盯着保罗。
