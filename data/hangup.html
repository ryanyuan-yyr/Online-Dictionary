hang up
1 →see: hang 1 
2 PHRASAL VERB 挂断（电话）/突然挂断…的电话 If you hang up or you hang up the phone, you end a phone call. If you hang up on someone you are speaking to on the phone, you end the phone call suddenly and unexpectedly. 挂断（电话）/突然挂断…的电话

  • Mum hung up the phone. [VERB PREP. noun]

    妈妈挂上了电话。

  • Don't hang up!. [VERB PREP.]

    别挂电话！

  • He said he'd call again, and hung up on me. [VERB PREP. + on]

    他说他会再打过来，就突然挂断了电话。

3 PHRASAL VERB 结束，终止（运动生涯或某项活动） You can use hang up to indicate that someone stops doing a particular sport or activity that they have regularly done over a long period. For example, when a footballer hangs up his boots, he stops playing football. 结束，终止（运动生涯或某项活动） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Keegan announced he was hanging up his boots for good. [VERB PREP. noun]

    基冈宣布自己将挂靴，永远退出足坛。

  • Looking back, she feels she should never have hung up her backpack. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    回首过去，她觉得自己真不该放下背包不再徒步旅行。
