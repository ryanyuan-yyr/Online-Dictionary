agricultural
ægrɪkʌltʃərəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 农业的；农用的 Agricultural means involving or relating to agriculture. 农业的；农用的

  • ...agricultural land.

    农田

  • ...corn and other agricultural products.

    玉米和其他农产品

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （地区、社会）以农业为主的，农业高度发达的 An agricultural place or society is one in which agriculture is important or highly developed. （地区、社会）以农业为主的，农业高度发达的

  • ...traditional agricultural societies.

    传统的农耕社会

SYN farming, country, rural, rustic
