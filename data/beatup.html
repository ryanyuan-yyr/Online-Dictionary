beat up
Word forms: plural beatings-up
1 PHRASAL VERB 痛打；殴打 If someone beats a person up, they hit or kick the person many times. 痛打；殴打

  • Then they actually beat her up as well. [VERB noun PREP.]

    接着他们对她竟然也是一顿暴打。

  • The government supporters are beating up anyone they suspect of favouring the demonstrators. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政府的支持者痛打任何一个被他们怀疑支持示威者的人。

SYN assault, attack, batter, thrash  
2 PHRASAL VERB 对…忧心忡忡；因为…而自责 If you beat yourself up about something, you worry about it a lot or blame yourself for it. 对…忧心忡忡；因为…而自责 [informal]

  • Tell them you don't want to do it any more. Don't beat yourself up about it. [V n P + about]

    去跟他们说你不想干了。别为了这个难为自己。

  • I don't beat myself up. I don't deal with things I can't handle. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我不会为难自己的，力所不及的事我是不会干的。
