fugitive
fjuːdʒɪtɪv ˈfjudʒɪtɪv
Word forms: plural fugitives
N-COUNT 逃亡者;亡命者;(通常指)逃犯 A fugitive is someone who is running away or hiding, usually in order to avoid being caught by the police. 逃亡者;亡命者;(通常指)逃犯

  • The rebel leader was a fugitive from justice.

    叛军头目是一名在逃的不法之徒。

  • ...the fugitive train robber.

    逃亡的火车劫匪

SYN runaway, refugee, deserter, outlaw
