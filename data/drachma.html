drachma
drækmə ˈdrækmə
Word forms: plural drachmas
N-COUNT 德拉克马(希腊货币单位，现被欧元所代替) The drachma was the unit of money used in Greece before it was replaced by the euro. 德拉克马(希腊货币单位，现被欧元所代替)
The drachma was also used to refer to the Greek currency system.

  • In April 1992 the Greek drachma was the only Community currency not yet part of the EMS exchange-rate mechanism.

    1992 年 4 月间希腊的德拉克马是唯一一种还未纳入欧洲货币体系兑换机制的欧共体成员国货币。
