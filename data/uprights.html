upright
ʌpraɪt ˈʌpˌraɪt ʌpˈraɪt
Word forms: plural uprights
1 ADJ [usu ADJ after v, verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ n] 垂直的；挺直的；直挺挺的 If you are sitting or standing upright, you are sitting or standing with your back straight, rather than bending or lying down. 垂直的；挺直的；直挺挺的

  • Helen sat upright in her chair. [+ in]

    海伦笔直地坐在椅子上。

  • ...those who had managed to remain upright.

    那些尽力保持腰背挺直的人

  • Jerrold pulled himself upright on the bed. [+ on]

    杰罗尔德从床上坐了起来。

  • He moved into an upright position.

    他挺直了身子。

SYN vertical, straight, standing up, erect  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （吸尘器、冰箱等）竖式的，立式的 An upright vacuum cleaner or freezer is tall rather than wide. （吸尘器、冰箱等）竖式的，立式的

  • ...the latest state-of-the-art upright vacuum cleaners.

    最新款、最先进的立式吸尘器

3 ADJ （椅子）直靠背的，没有扶手的 An upright chair has a straight back and no arms. （椅子）直靠背的，没有扶手的

  • He was sitting on an upright chair beside his bed, reading.

    他正坐在床前一张直背椅上看书。

4 N-COUNT 立柱；立放构件 You can refer to vertical posts or the vertical parts of an object as uprights . 立柱；立放构件

  • ...the uprights of a four-poster bed. [+ of]

    四柱床的立柱

5 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 诚实的，正直的；规矩的 You can describe people as upright when they are careful to follow acceptable rules of behaviour and behave in a moral way. 诚实的，正直的；规矩的

  • ...a very upright, trustworthy man.

    一个非常正直可靠的人

SYN honest, good, principled, just
