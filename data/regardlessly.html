regardless
rɪgɑːʳdləs rɪˈgɑrdlɪs
1 PREP-PHRASE 不顾；不管；不论 If something happens regardless of something else, it is not affected or influenced at all by that other thing. 不顾；不管；不论

  • It takes in anybody regardless of religion, colour, or creed.

    任何人，不论宗教、肤色或信仰，都可以加入。

  • Regardless of whether he is right or wrong, we have to abide by his decisions.

    不管他正确与否，我们都得服从他的决定。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 不管怎样;无论如何;不顾一切地 If you say that someone did something regardless, you mean that they did it even though there were problems or factors that could have stopped them, or perhaps should have stopped them. 不管怎样;无论如何;不顾一切地

  • Despite her recent surgery she has been carrying on regardless.

    尽管最近做了手术，她还是不顾一切地进行下去。

SYN in spite of everything, anyway, nevertheless, nonetheless
