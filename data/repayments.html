repayment
rɪpeɪmənt
Word forms: plural repayments
1 N-COUNT 分期偿付款 Repayments are amounts of money which you pay at regular intervals to a person or organization in order to repay a debt. 分期偿付款

  • They were unable to meet their mortgage repayments.

    他们不能按时偿还抵押贷款。

  • You can pay it off or make a minimum repayment.

    你可以一次付清或先付最低分期偿付款。

2 N-UNCOUNT 偿还;偿付 The repayment of money is the act or process of paying it back to the person you owe it to. 偿还;偿付

  • He failed to meet last Friday's deadline for repayment of a 114m loan. [+ of]

    他没能在上星期五的最后期限前偿还1.14亿的贷款。

  • Brazil is putting forward a new debt repayment plan.

    巴西即将提出一个新的债务偿付计划。
