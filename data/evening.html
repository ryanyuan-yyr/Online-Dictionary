evening
iːvnɪŋ ˈivnɪŋ
Word forms: plural evenings
N-VAR 傍晚;晚上 The evening is the part of each day between the end of the afternoon and the time when you go to bed. 傍晚;晚上

  • All he did that evening was sit around the flat.

    那天晚上他一直在公寓里无所事事地坐着。

  • Supper is from 5.00 to 6.00 in the evening.

    晚餐在傍晚5点到6点之间。

  • Towards evening the carnival entered its final stage.

    临近傍晚时，狂欢节进入最后阶段。

SYN dusk [archaic] , night, sunset, twilight
