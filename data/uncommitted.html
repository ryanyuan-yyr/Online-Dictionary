uncommitted
ʌnkəmɪtɪd ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd
1 ADJ 未作承诺的；未表态的 If you are uncommitted, you have not yet decided to support a particular idea, belief, group, or person, or you are unwilling to show your support. 未作承诺的；未表态的

  • The allegiance of uncommitted voters will be crucial.

    未表态的选民支持谁将是至关重要的。

  • I was still uncommitted to the venture when we reached Kanpur. [+ to]

    我们到达坎普尔时，我仍未拿定主意是否去冒险。

SYN undecided, uninvolved, nonpartisan, nonaligned  
The uncommitted are people who are uncommitted.未表态者；未作出承诺的人

  • It was the uncommitted that Labour needed to reach.

    工党需要拉拢的是未表态者。

2 ADJ （资源等）未指定用途的，未明确用途的 If resources are uncommitted, it has not yet been decided what to use them for. （资源等）未指定用途的，未明确用途的

  • ...uncommitted gas reserves.

    用途待定的汽油储备

  • ...£2.3m of uncommitted loans.

    230万英镑用途未定的贷款
