personal space
ˈpɜːsənəl speɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 个人空间 If someone invades your personal space, they stand or lean too close to you, so that you feel uncomfortable. 个人空间

  • I felt my body involuntarily stiffen against her invasion of my personal space.

    随着她不断侵入我的个人空间，我感觉全身都不由自主地紧绷起来。

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 私人空间 If you need your personal space, you need time on your own, with the freedom to do something that you want to do or to think about something. 私人空间

  • Self-confidence means being relaxed enough to allow your lover their personal space.

    自信就是放宽心，给爱人足够的私人空间。
