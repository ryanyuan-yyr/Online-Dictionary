sprawled
sprɔːld sprɔːld
ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 四肢摊开地坐着(或躺着)的 If you are sprawled somewhere, you are sitting or lying with your legs and arms spread out in a careless way. 四肢摊开地坐着(或躺着)的

  • People are sprawled on makeshift beds in the cafeteria.

    人们懒散地躺在食堂里搭起的临时床铺上。

  • Rolando lay sprawled on his stomach, snoring.

    罗兰多趴着睡着了，四肢摊开，打着呼噜。
