sketchy
sketʃi skɛtʃi
Word forms: comparative sketchier, superlative sketchiest
ADJ-GRADED (信息)粗略的，大概的，不完全的，不充分的 Sketchy information about something does not include many details and is therefore incomplete or inadequate. (信息)粗略的，大概的，不完全的，不充分的

  • Details of what actually happened are still sketchy.

    对于已发生事实的详细情况知道的仍然有限。

  • Only sketchy information exists on the stock of natural resources such as fish.

    人们对鱼类等自然资源的总储备量所知甚少。

SYN incomplete, rough, vague, slight  
sketchily

  • The ideas seem sketchily developed, the textures thin, the images vague.

    观点阐述不充分，结构单薄，意象也模糊不清。
