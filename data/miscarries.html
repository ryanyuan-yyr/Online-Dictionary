miscarry
mɪskæri -kæri mɪsˈkæri ˈmɪsˌkæri
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense miscarries, present participle miscarrying, past tense , past participle miscarried
VERB 小产;流产 If a woman miscarries, she has a miscarriage. 小产;流产 [Also VERB n]

  • Many women who miscarry eventually have healthy babies. [VERB]

    许多曾经小产的女人最终都生下了健康的宝宝。

SYN have a miscarriage, lose your baby, have a spontaneous abortion
