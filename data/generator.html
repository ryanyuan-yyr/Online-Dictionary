generator
dʒenəreɪtəʳ ˈdʒɛnərˌeɪtər
Word forms: plural generators
1 N-COUNT 发电机 A generator is a machine which produces electricity. 发电机
2 N-COUNT 促使…发生的人（或事物）；产生者 A generator of something is a person, organization, product, or situation which produces it or causes it to happen. 促使…发生的人（或事物）；产生者

  • The US economy is still an impressive generator of new jobs.

    美国经济依然在创造着数量惊人的新就业机会。

  • The company has been a very good cash generator.

    这家公司盈利状况一直相当好。
