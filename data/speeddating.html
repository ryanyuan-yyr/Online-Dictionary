speed dating
N-UNCOUNT 快速交友(指一次和几位异性见面以寻找合适的伴侣) Speed dating is a method of introducing unattached people to potential partners by arranging for them to meet a series of people on a single occasion. 快速交友(指一次和几位异性见面以寻找合适的伴侣)

  • If you're a busy person, looking to meet several potential mates at the same event, speed dating could be for you.

    如果你是个忙人，想在同一场合和几位可能成为自己伴侣的人见面，那么快速交友可能就适合你。
