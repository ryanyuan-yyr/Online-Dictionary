scrutinize
skruːtɪnaɪz ˈskrutənˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense scrutinizes, present participle scrutinizing, past tense , past participle scrutinized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 scrutinise
1 VERB 详细检查;仔细察看 If you scrutinize something, you examine it very carefully, often to find out some information from it or about it. 详细检查;仔细察看

  • Her purpose was to scrutinize his features to see if he was an honest man. [VERB noun]

    她的目的是通过仔细观察他的相貌以判断他是否诚实。

  • Lloyds' results were carefully scrutinised as a guide to what to expect from the other banks. [VERB noun]

    对劳埃德银行的业绩进行了仔细分析，以确定对其他银行的表现可以作何种期待。

SYN examine, study, inspect, research
