all-inclusive
ˈɔlɪnˈklusɪv
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （尤指度假的费用或价格）无所不包的，包括一切的 All-inclusive is used to indicate that a price, especially the price of a holiday, includes all the charges and all the services offered. （尤指度假的费用或价格）无所不包的，包括一切的

  • An all-inclusive two-week holiday costs around £880 per person.

    为期两周、包括所有费用的度假大约每人花费880英镑。
