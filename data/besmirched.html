besmirch
bɪsmɜːʳtʃ biˈsmɜrtʃ bɪˈsmɜrtʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense besmirches, present participle besmirching, past tense , past participle besmirched
VERB 诋毁；诬蔑；玷污 If you besmirch someone or their reputation, you say that they are a bad person or that they have done something wrong, usually when this is not true. 诋毁；诬蔑；玷污 [literary]

  • He has accused local people of trying to besmirch his reputation.

    他指责当地人企图败坏他的名声。

SYN tarnish, damage, soil, stain
