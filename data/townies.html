townie
taʊni taʊˈniː ˈtaʊni
Word forms: plural townies
N-COUNT (对乡村一无所知的)城里人 If someone who lives in the countryside refers to someone from a town or city as a townie, they disapprove of that person because they think they have no knowledge of the countryside or country life. (对乡村一无所知的)城里人 [disapproval]
