infrastructure
ɪnfrəstrʌktʃəʳ ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃə ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃər
Word forms: plural infrastructures
N-VAR (国家、社会、组织赖以行使职能的)基础建设,基础设施 The infrastructure of a country, society, or organization consists of the basic facilities such as transport, communications, power supplies, and buildings, which enable it to function. (国家、社会、组织赖以行使职能的)基础建设,基础设施

  • ...investment in infrastructure.

    对基础设施的投资

  • The infrastructure, from hotels to transport, is old and decrepit.

    从宾馆到交通，所有的基础设施都已陈旧失修。
