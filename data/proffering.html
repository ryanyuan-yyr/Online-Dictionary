proffer
prɒfəʳ ˈprɑfər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense proffers, present participle proffering, past tense , past participle proffered
1 VERB 提供；拿出；递给 If you proffer something to someone, you hold it towards them so that they can take it or touch it. 提供；拿出；递给 [formal] [Also VERB n + to]

  • He rose and proffered a silver box full of cigarettes. [VERB noun]

    他站起身，伸手递过一个装满香烟的银盒子。

SYN offer, hand over, present, extend  
2 VERB 提出，提供（建议等） If you proffer something such as advice to someone, you offer it to them. 提出，提供（建议等） [formal]

  • The army has not yet proffered an explanation of how and why the accident happened. [VERB noun]

    军队还没有对这次事件发生的经过和原因给出解释。

SYN suggest, propose, volunteer, submit
