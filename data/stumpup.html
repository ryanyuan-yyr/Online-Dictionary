stump up
PHRASAL VERB (常指不情愿地)付钱，掏腰包 If you stump up a sum of money, you pay it, often unwillingly. (常指不情愿地)付钱，掏腰包 [British, informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • Customers do not have to stump up any cash for at least four weeks. [VERB PREP. noun]

    顾客们至少四周内都不必付钱。

SYN pay, fork out [slang] , shell out [informal] , contribute
