inhuman
ɪnhjuːmən ɪnˈhjumən
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不人道的;野蛮的;残酷的 If you describe treatment or an action as inhuman, you mean that it is extremely cruel. 不人道的;野蛮的;残酷的

  • The detainees are often held in cruel and inhuman conditions.

    被拘留者的关押环境常常恶劣严酷、很不人道。

  • The barbaric slaughter of whales is unnecessary and inhuman.

    对鲸的野蛮屠杀既不必要也不人道。

SYN cruel, savage, brutal, vicious  
2 ADJ-GRADED 非人类的;怪异可怕的 If you describe someone or something as inhuman, you mean that they are strange or bad because they do not seem human in some way. 非人类的;怪异可怕的

  • ...inhuman shrieks that chilled my heart.

    让我心惊胆寒的、异常恐怖的尖叫声
