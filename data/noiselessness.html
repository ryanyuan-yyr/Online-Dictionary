noiseless
nɔɪzləs ˈnɔɪzlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 无声的;安静的 Something or someone that is noiseless does not make any sound. 无声的;安静的

  • The snow was light and noiseless as it floated down.

    雪花轻盈飘落，无声无息。

SYN silent, soundless, quiet, mute  
noiselessly

  • I shut the door noiselessly behind me.

    我把门轻轻地带上。
