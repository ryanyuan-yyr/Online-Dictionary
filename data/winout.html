win out
(also win through)
PHRASAL VERB (经过比赛或斗争)获胜，胜出 If something or someone wins out or wins through, they are successful after a competition or struggle. (经过比赛或斗争)获胜，胜出

  • Sometimes perseverance does win out. [VERB PREP.]

    有时候坚持就是胜利。

  • Stick to your principles, and you will win through. [VERB PREP.]

    坚持你的原则，就会胜利。

  • Here is a chance for greengrocers to win out over the supermarkets by selling local produce. [V P over/against n]

    现在是个机会，蔬果店可通过销售土产胜过超市。
