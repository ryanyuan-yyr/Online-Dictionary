inapplicable
ɪnəplɪkəbəl ɪnæplɪk- ɪnˈæplɪkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不适用的;不相关的;不适宜的 Something that is inapplicable to what you are talking about is not relevant or appropriate to it. 不适用的;不相关的;不适宜的

  • His theory was inapplicable to many underdeveloped economies. [+ to]

    他的理论对很多欠发达经济体都不适用。

SYN irrelevant, inappropriate, unsuitable, unsuited
