sinful
sɪnfʊl ˈsɪnfəl
ADJ-GRADED 不道德的;邪恶的 If you describe someone or something as sinful, you mean that they are wicked or immoral. 不道德的;邪恶的

  • 'I am a sinful man,' he said.

    “我是个邪恶的人，”他说。

  • ...this is a sinful world.

    这是个邪恶的世界

  • He reminded us that smoking was sinful.

    他提醒我们吸烟是不道德的。

SYN wicked, bad, criminal, guilty  
sinfulness

  • ...the sinfulness of apartheid.

    种族隔离的罪恶
