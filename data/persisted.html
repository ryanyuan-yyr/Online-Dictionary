persist
pəʳsɪst pərˈsɪst pərˈzɪst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense persists, present participle persisting, past tense , past participle persisted
1 VERB （不喜欢的事物）继续存在 If something undesirable persists, it continues to exist. （不喜欢的事物）继续存在

  • Contact your doctor if the cough persists. [VERB]

    如果还是咳嗽就去看医生。

  • These problems persisted for much of the decade. [VERB]

    这些问题在那10年的大部分时间里一直存在。

2 VERB 顽强坚持；执著 If you persist in doing something, you continue to do it, even though it is difficult or other people are against it. 顽强坚持；执著

  • Why does Britain persist in running down its defence forces?. [VERB + in]

    为什么英国要坚持消减其军事防御力量？

  • He urged the United States to persist with its efforts to bring about peace. [V + with/in]

    他敦促美国继续进行和平斡旋。

  • 'You haven't answered me,' she persisted. [VERB with quote]

    “你还没有回答我,”她追问道。

  • When I set my mind to something, I persist. [VERB]

    一旦我对某事拿定主意,我就会坚持到底。

SYN persevere, continue, go on, carry on
