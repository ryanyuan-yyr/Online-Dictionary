reversible
rɪvɜːʳsɪbəl rɪˈvɜrsəbəl
1 ADJ 可逆的;可恢复原状的 If a process or an action is reversible, its effects can be reversed so that the original situation returns. 可逆的;可恢复原状的

  • Heart disease is reversible in some cases, according to a study published last summer.

    根据去年夏天公布的一项研究，心脏病在某些情况下是可以治愈的。

2 ADJ 可两面穿的;正反两用的 Reversible clothes or materials have been made so that either side can be worn or shown as the outside. 可两面穿的;正反两用的

  • ...a reversible waistcoat.

    可两面穿的背心
