insightful
ɪnsaɪtfʊl ɪnˈsaɪtfəl ˈɪnˌsaɪtfəl
ADJ-GRADED 富有洞察力的;有深刻见解的 If you describe a person or their remarks as insightful, you mean that they show a very good understanding of people and situations. 富有洞察力的;有深刻见解的 [approval]

  • She offered some really interesting, insightful observations.

    她给出了一些非常有趣又富有洞察力的评论。

SYN perceptive, shrewd, discerning, understanding
