visitation rights
N-PLURAL (离婚后去对方家里和孩子会面的)探视权If a parent who is divorced and does not live with their child has visitation rights, they officially have the right to spend time with their child. (离婚后去对方家里和孩子会面的)探视权

  • He was not given any visitation rights by the divorce court.

    离婚法院没有给予他任何探视权。
