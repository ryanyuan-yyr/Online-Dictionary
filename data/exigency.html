Word forms: plural exigencies
N-COUNT [usually plural] 紧急情况;危急关头;迫切需要 The exigencies of a situation or a job are the demands or difficulties that you have to deal with as part of it. 紧急情况;危急关头;迫切需要 [formal]

  • ...the exigencies of a wartime economy. [+ of]

SYN need, demand, requirement, necessity
