thrilling
θrɪlɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 扣人心弦的;激动人心的;引人入胜的 Something that is thrilling is very exciting and enjoyable. 扣人心弦的;激动人心的;引人入胜的

  • Our wildlife trips offer a thrilling encounter with wildlife in its natural state.

    野生动物园之旅使我们得以接触自然状态下的野生动物，让人感觉很刺激。

SYN exciting, gripping, stimulating, stirring  
thrillingly

  • Watson has a wonderful voice, with thrillingly clear top notes.

    沃森的嗓音美妙，高音嘹亮动听。

  • I have seen them play many times, but never as thrillingly and flawlessly as tonight.

    他们的演出我看过很多次，可从未有一次像今晚这样令人心醉，完美无缺。
