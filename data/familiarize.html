familiarize
fəmɪliəraɪz fəˈmɪljərˌaɪz fəˈmɪliərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense familiarizes, present participle familiarizing, past tense , past participle familiarized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 familiarise
VERB 使熟悉;使了解 If you familiarize yourself with something, or if someone familiarizes you with it, you learn about it and start to understand it. 使熟悉;使了解

  • I was expected to familiarise myself with the keyboard. [VERB pronoun-reflexive + with]

    对我的要求是熟悉键盘。

  • The goal of the experiment was to familiarize the people with the new laws. [VERB noun + with]

    试行的目的就是要让人们熟悉新法规。

SYN accustom, instruct, habituate, make used to
