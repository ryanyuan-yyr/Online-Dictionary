untreated
ʌntriːtɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ after verb, ADJ n, verb-link ADJ] 未经治疗的 If an injury or illness is left untreated, it is not given medical treatment. 未经治疗的

  • If left untreated the condition may become chronic.

    这种病如果不治疗可能会发展成慢性病。

  • ...the consequences of untreated tuberculosis.

    肺结核得不到治疗的后果

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （有害物质）未经处理的 Untreated materials, water, or chemicals are harmful and have not been made safe. （有害物质）未经处理的

  • ...the dumping of nuclear waste and untreated sewage.

    倾倒核废料和未经处理的污水

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （天然或原始物质）未经处理的，未加工过的 Untreated materials are in their natural or original state, often before being prepared for use in a particular process. （天然或原始物质）未经处理的，未加工过的

  • All the bedding is made of simple, untreated cotton.

    所有床上用品全部用未经处理的天然棉花制成。

  • In its untreated state the carbon fibre material is rather like cloth.

    原始的碳纤维材料很像布料。
