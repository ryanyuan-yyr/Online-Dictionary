strike off
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 取消(医生、律师等)的执业资格;把…除名 If someone such as a doctor or lawyer is struck off, their name is removed from the official register and they are not allowed to do medical or legal work any more. 取消(医生、律师等)的执业资格;把…除名 [British]

  • ...a company lawyer who had been struck off for dishonest practices. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    因行为不轨而被取消执业资格的公司律师

  • He could be struck off the medical register. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他可能会被取消医生执业资格。
