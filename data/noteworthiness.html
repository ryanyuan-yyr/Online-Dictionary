noteworthy
noʊtwɜːʳði ˈnoʊtˌwɜrði
ADJ-GRADED 值得注意的;显著的;有趣的 A fact or event that is noteworthy is interesting, remarkable, or significant in some way. 值得注意的;显著的;有趣的 [formal]

  • It is noteworthy that the programme has been shifted from its original August slot to July.

    值得注意的是该节目已从原来8月份的档期换到了7月份。

  • I found nothing particularly noteworthy to report.

    我并无特别重要的事情要报告。

  • The most noteworthy feature of the list is that there are no women on it.

    这份名单最显著的特点是上面没有女性。

SYN remarkable, interesting, important, significant
