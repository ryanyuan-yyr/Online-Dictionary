gesticulate
dʒestɪkjʊleɪt dʒɛˈstɪkjʊˌleɪt dʒɛsˈtɪkjuˌleɪt ˈdʒɛsˈtɪkjəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gesticulates, present participle gesticulating, past tense , past participle gesticulated
VERB 做手势；用手势表达；用动作示意 If you gesticulate, you make movements with your arms or hands, often while you are describing something that is difficult to express in words. 做手势；用手势表达；用动作示意 [mainly written]

  • A man with a paper hat upon his head was gesticulating wildly. [VERB]

    一个戴纸帽的男子在拼命地打手势。

  • The architect was gesticulating at a hole in the ground. [VERB prep.]

    那位建筑师在指着地上的一个洞打手势。

SYN signal, sign, wave, indicate  
gesticulation

  • We communicated mainly by signs, gesticulation and mime.

    我们主要通过动作、手势和哑语进行交流。
