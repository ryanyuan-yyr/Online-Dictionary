chilly
tʃɪli ˈtʃɪli
Word forms: comparative chillier, superlative chilliest
1 ADJ-GRADED 阴冷的;寒冷的 Something that is chilly is unpleasantly cold. →see usage note at: cold 阴冷的;寒冷的

  • It was a chilly afternoon.

    那是个阴冷的下午。

  • The rooms had grown chilly.

    房间慢慢变得阴冷起来。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 冷的;有寒意的 If you feel chilly, you feel rather cold. 冷的;有寒意的

  • I'm a bit chilly.

    我有点冷。

3 ADJ-GRADED (人际关系、反应)冷淡的，冷冰冰的，不热情的 You say that relations between people are chilly or that a person's response is chilly when they are not friendly, welcoming, or enthusiastic. (人际关系、反应)冷淡的，冷冰冰的，不热情的

  • I was slightly afraid of their chilly distant politeness.

    他们那种冷冰冰的、敬而远之的客套让我有点憷头。

SYN unfriendly, hostile, unsympathetic, frigid
