regal
riːgəl ˈrigəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 王者的；豪华的；富丽堂皇的；帝王般的 If you describe something as regal, you mean that it is suitable for a king or queen, because it is very impressive or beautiful. 王者的；豪华的；富丽堂皇的；帝王般的

  • He sat with such regal dignity.

    他端坐在那里，浑身透着王者的威严。

  • Never has she looked more regal.

    她从没有如此雍容华贵过。

SYN royal, majestic, kingly or queenly, noble  
regally

  • He inclined his head regally.

    他很有帝王风范地点了点头。
