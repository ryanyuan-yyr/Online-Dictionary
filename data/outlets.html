outlet
aʊtlet ˈaʊtˌlɛt
Word forms: plural outlets
1 N-COUNT 经销店；专营店 An outlet is a shop or organization which sells the goods made by a particular manufacturer. 经销店；专营店

  • ...the largest retail outlet in the city. [+ in]

    该市最大的零售商店

SYN shop, store, supermarket, market  
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 廉价商品经销店；折扣店 An outlet or an outlet store is a place which sells slightly damaged or outdated goods from a particular manufacturer, or goods that it made in greater quantities than needed. 廉价商品经销店；折扣店

  • ...the factory outlet store in Belmont.

    位于贝尔蒙特的厂家直销型折扣店

3 N-COUNT （情感的）发泄途径；（思想的）表达方式 If someone has an outlet for their feelings or ideas, they have a means of expressing and releasing them. （情感的）发泄途径；（思想的）表达方式

  • Her father had found an outlet for his ambition in his work. [+ for]

    她父亲在工作中找到了施展抱负的机会。

SYN channel, release, medium, avenue  
4 N-COUNT 出口；排放管 An outlet is a hole or pipe through which liquid or air can flow away. 出口；排放管

  • ...a warm air outlet.

    热气出口

  • ...an underwater outlet pipe discharging waste into the sea.

    排污入海的水下管道

5 N-COUNT （通常指墙上的）电源插座 An outlet is a place, usually in a wall, where you can connect electrical devices to the electricity supply. （通常指墙上的）电源插座 [mainly US]
