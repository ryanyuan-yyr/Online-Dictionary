blatantly
bleɪtəntli
ADV-GRADED [usually ADVERB adjective] [oft ADVERB with verb] （强调糟糕）非常，极为，完全 Blatantly is used to add emphasis when you are describing states or situations which you think are bad. （强调糟糕）非常，极为，完全 [emphasis]

  • It became blatantly obvious to me that the band wasn't going to last.

    我觉得很明显该乐队长久不了。

  • For years, blatantly false assertions have gone unchallenged.

    多年来，一些明显错误的断言都没有受到质疑。

  • This argument is only too blatantly an alibi for domestic repression.

    这一辩解完全是在为国内的镇压活动开脱。
