collectivize
kəlektɪvaɪz kəˈlɛktəˌvaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense collectivizes, present participle collectivizing, past tense , past participle collectivized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 collectivise
VERB 使公有化；使集体化 If farms or factories are collectivized, they are brought under state ownership and control, usually by combining a number of small farms or factories into one large one. 使公有化；使集体化

    他强迫该国对农业实行公有化。

  • ...large collectivised farms. [VERB-ed]

    大型集体农场
