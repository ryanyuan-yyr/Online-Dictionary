divination
dɪvɪneɪʃən ˌdɪvəˈneɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 占卜（术）;预测;预言 Divination is the art or practice of discovering what will happen in the future using supernatural means. 占卜（术）;预测;预言 [formal]
SYN prediction, divining, prophecy, presage
