mercenary
mɜːʳsənri -neri ˈmɜrsəˌnɛri
Word forms: plural mercenaries
1 N-COUNT (外籍)雇佣兵 A mercenary is a soldier who is paid to fight by a country or group that they do not belong to. (外籍)雇佣兵
SYN hireling, freelance [history] , soldier of fortune, condottiere [history]  
2 ADJ-GRADED 唯利是图的;见钱眼开的 If you describe someone as mercenary, you are criticizing them because you think that they are only interested in the money that they can get from a particular person or situation. 唯利是图的;见钱眼开的 [disapproval]
