onstage
ɒnsteɪdʒ ˌɒnˈsteɪdʒ ˈɑnˈsteɪdʒ
ADV [ADVERB after verb] [be ADVERB] 在（或到）舞台上 When someone such as an actor or musician goes onstage, they go onto the stage in a theatre to give a performance. 在（或到）舞台上

  • When she walked onstage she was given a standing ovation.

    当她走上台时，观众起立鼓掌欢迎。

  • You have to be onstage at eight o'clock.

    你务必8点钟上台演出。

  • Onstage, the musicians are actually sitting behind the loudspeakers.

    舞台演出时，乐师实际上坐在扬声器后面演奏。
