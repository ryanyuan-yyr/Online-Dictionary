perverted
pəʳvɜːʳtɪd pərˈvɜrtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED （性）反常的；（性）变态的 If you say that someone is perverted, you mean that you consider their behaviour, especially their sexual behaviour, to be immoral or unacceptable. （性）反常的；（性）变态的 [disapproval]

  • You've been protecting sick and perverted men.

    你一直在保护有病的性变态者。

  • His actions, to his small perverted mind, were surely forgivable.

    对他狭隘扭曲的心灵来说，他的行为当然是可以原谅的。

2 ADJ-GRADED 错误的；不正常的；有害的 You can use perverted to describe actions or ideas which you think are wrong, unnatural, or harmful. 错误的；不正常的；有害的 [disapproval]

  • ...a perverted form of knowledge.

    错误的知识

  • ...the assertion of a perverted patriotism.

    扭曲的爱国主义主张
