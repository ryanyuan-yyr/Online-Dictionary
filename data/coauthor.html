co-author
(also coauthor)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense co-authors, present participle co-authoring, past tense , past participle co-authored
1 N-COUNT (书、戏剧或报告的)合著者 The co-authors of a book, play, or report are the people who have written it together. (书、戏剧或报告的)合著者

  • He is co-author, with Andrew Blowers, of 'The International Politics of Nuclear Waste'. [+ of]

    他与安德鲁·布洛尔斯合著了《国际政治视角下的核废料》一书。

2 VERB 合著(书、戏剧或报告) If two or more people co-author a book, play, or report, they write it together. 合著(书、戏剧或报告)

  • He's co-authored a book on Policy for Tourism. [VERB noun]

    他与人合著了一本有关旅游业政策的书。

  • Karen Matthews co-authored the study with Lewis Kullers. [VERB noun + with]

    卡伦·马修斯与刘易斯·库勒斯合作完成了这个研究课题。
