shoddy
ʃɒdi ˈʃɑdi
Word forms: comparative shoddier, superlative shoddiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 劣等的;粗制滥造的;做工粗糙的 Shoddy work or a shoddy product has been done or made carelessly or badly. 劣等的;粗制滥造的;做工粗糙的

  • I'm normally quick to complain about shoddy service.

    对劣质服务我通常会立刻投诉。

  • American customers no longer tolerate shoddy goods.

    美国的顾客不再容忍劣等货了。

SYN inferior, poor, second-rate, cheap  
shoddily

  • These products are shoddily produced.

    这些产品是粗制滥造的。

  • ...shoddily-built cars.

    做工粗糙的汽车
