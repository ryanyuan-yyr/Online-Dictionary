assuredly
əʃʊərɪdli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB before verb] 确定地；一定地；毫无疑问地 If something is assuredly true, it is definitely true. 确定地；一定地；毫无疑问地

  • He is, assuredly, not alone in believing they will win.

    毫无疑问，不止他一个人认为他们会赢。

  • The government most assuredly does believe in organic farming.

    政府确实坚信有机耕作切实可行。
