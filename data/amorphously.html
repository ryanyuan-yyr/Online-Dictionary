amorphous
əmɔːʳfəs əˈmɔrfəs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 无固定形状（或结构）的；不规则的；无组织的 Something that is amorphous has no clear shape or structure. 无固定形状（或结构）的；不规则的；无组织的 [formal]

  • A dark, strangely amorphous shadow filled the room.

    一团形状怪异的黑影笼罩了整个房间。

  • ...the amorphous mass of the unemployed.

    散漫的失业人群

SYN shapeless, vague, irregular, nondescript
