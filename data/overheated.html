overheated
oʊvəʳhiːtɪd ˌəʊvəˈhiːtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 十分愤怒的；过于激烈的 Someone who is overheated is very angry about something. 十分愤怒的；过于激烈的

  • I think the reaction has been a little overheated.

    我认为这一反应有些过激。

  • In America, overheated drivers have been known to shoot each other.

    在美国，据说司机在盛怒之下会拔枪互射。

SYN angry, furious, incensed, enraged
