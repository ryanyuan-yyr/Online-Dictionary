countrywoman
kʌntriwʊmən ˈkʌntriˌwʊmən
Word forms: plural countrywomen
1 N-COUNT 乡下女人;农村女人 A countrywoman is a woman who lives in the country rather than in a city or a town. 乡下女人;农村女人

  • She had the slow, soft voice of a countrywoman.

    她有着乡下女人所特有的那种和缓、轻柔的声音。

2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 女同胞;女性国人;女同乡 Your country-women are women from your own country. 女同胞;女性国人;女同乡

  • Britain's Martine Le Moignan defeated her countrywoman Suzanne Horner in four games.

    英国的马蒂娜·勒穆瓦昂南通过四局的比赛击败了同胞苏珊娜·霍纳。
