bigoted
bɪgətɪd
ADJ-GRADED 固执的；执拗的；顽固不化的 Someone who is bigoted has strong, unreasonable prejudices or opinions and will not change them, even when they are proved to be wrong. 固执的；执拗的；顽固不化的 [disapproval]

  • He was bigoted and racist.

    他非常偏执，而且具有种族歧视思想。

SYN intolerant, twisted, racist, prejudiced
