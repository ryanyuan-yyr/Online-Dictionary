clientele
kliːɒntel klaɪən- ˌkliːɒnˈtɛl ˈklaɪəntɪdʒ ˌklaɪənˈtɛl ˌ kliənˈtɛl ˌkliɑnˈtɛl
N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 顾客；客户；主顾 The clientele of a place or organization are its customers or clients. 顾客；客户；主顾

  • This pub had a mixed clientele.

    这家酒吧什么样的客人都有。

  • I have built up a loyal satisfied clientele for both African and European clothes.

    我已经发展了一批对非洲和欧洲的服装都很满意的忠实客户。

SYN customers, market, business, following
