disruptive technology
Word forms: plural disruptive technologies
N-COUNT 突破性科技;颠覆性技术 A disruptive technology is a new technology, such as computers and the Internet, which has a rapid and major effect on technologies that existed before. 突破性科技;颠覆性技术 [business]
