article
ɑːʳtɪkəl ˈɑrtɪkəl
Word forms: plural articles
1 N-COUNT （报纸、杂志的）文章 An article is a piece of writing that is published in a newspaper or magazine. （报纸、杂志的）文章

  • ...a newspaper article.

    一篇报载文章

  • ...a travel article.

    一篇游记

  • According to an article in The Economist the drug could have side effects.

    《经济学家》杂志上的一篇文章讲到这种药可能有副作用。

  • ...Canning's article about the Buxton Festival.

    坎宁的一篇关于巴克斯顿艺术节的文章

SYN feature, story, paper, piece  
2 N-COUNT 物品；物件 You can refer to objects as articles of some kind. 物品；物件

  • ...articles of clothing. [+ of]

    衣物

  • He had stripped the house of all articles of value.

    他把房间里值钱的东西洗劫一空。

  • ...household articles.

    家居用品

3 PHRASE 正品;真货 If you describe something as the genuine article, you are emphasizing that it is genuine, and often that it is very good. 正品;真货 [emphasis]

  • The vodka was the genuine article.

    这种伏特加酒是正品。

SYN authentic, real, genuine, legitimate  
4 N-COUNT （契约、文件的）条款，条文，项目 An article of a formal agreement or document is a section of it which deals with a particular point. （契约、文件的）条款，条文，项目

  • The country appears to be violating several articles of the convention.

    这个国家看起来违反了公约中的好几项条款。

  • ...Article 50 of the UN charter.

    《联合国宪章》第50条

5 N-PLURAL 见习契约 Someone who is in articles is being trained as a lawyer or accountant by a firm with whom they have a written agreement. 见习契约 [British]

  • In 1986, 44 per cent of those admitted to articles were women.

    1986年，44％的实习生为女性。

6 N-COUNT 冠词（a和an是不定冠词，the是定冠词） In grammar, an article is a kind of determiner. In English, 'a' and 'an' are called the indefinite article, and 'the' is called the definite article . 冠词（a和an是不定冠词，the是定冠词）
