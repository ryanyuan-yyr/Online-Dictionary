exceedingly
ɪksiːdɪŋli ɪkˈsiːdɪŋlɪ ɛkˈsidɪŋli ɪkˈsidɪŋli
ADV [usually ADVERB adjective, oft ADVERB after verb] 非常;极其 Exceedingly means very or very much. 非常;极其 [old-fashioned]

  • We had an exceedingly good lunch.

    我们吃了一顿极为丰盛的午餐。

  • This was an exceedingly difficult decision to take.

    作这个决定非常地难。

  • I have a case that troubles me exceedingly.

    我有一件非常棘手的案子。

SYN extremely, very, highly, greatly
