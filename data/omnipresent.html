omnipresent
ɒmniprezənt ˌɑmnɪˈprɛzənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 无所不在的；普遍存在的 Something that is omnipresent is present everywhere or seems to be always present. 无所不在的；普遍存在的 [formal]

  • The sound of sirens was an omnipresent background noise in New York.

    在纽约，警报声是无处不在的背景噪音。

  • The obsessive thoughts became so omnipresent that her memory was affected.

    这些强迫性念头变得无时不在，以至影响到了她的记忆。

SYN ubiquitous, ever-present, pervasive
