poison pill
Word forms: plural poison pills
N-COUNT （公司为防止被兼并而采取的）“毒丸”防御 A poison pill refers to what some companies do to reduce their value in order to prevent themselves being taken over by another company. （公司为防止被兼并而采取的）“毒丸”防御 [business]

  • Some believe this level of compensation is essentially a poison pill to put off any rival bidders.

    有些人认为这种程度的补偿实质上是阻止任何投标对手的“毒丸”防御。
