1 N-UNCOUNT 经济学 Economics is the study of the way in which money, industry, and trade are organized in a society. 经济学

  • He gained a first class Honours degree in economics.

    他获得了经济学一等荣誉学位。

SYN finance, commerce, the dismal science

3 N-UNCOUNT 经济体制 The economics of a society or industry is the system of organizing money and trade in it. 经济体制

  • He is regarded as a committed supporter of a radical free-market economics policy.

    他被视为激进的自由市场经济体制政策的坚定支持者。

    第三世界的经济体制
