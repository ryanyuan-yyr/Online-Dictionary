contaminate
kəntæmɪneɪt kənˈtæməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense contaminates, present participle contaminating, past tense , past participle contaminated
VERB 污染;弄脏 If something is contaminated by dirt, chemicals, or radiation, they make it dirty or harmful. 污染;弄脏 [Also VERB n]

  • Have any fish been contaminated in the Arctic Ocean? [be VERB-ed]

    北冰洋的鱼受到过污染吗？

  • ...vast tracts of empty land, much of it contaminated by years of army activity. [VERB-ed]

    大片撂荒的土地，其中大部分由于经年累月的战火而受到了污染

SYN pollute, infect, stain, corrupt  
contaminated

  • Nuclear weapons plants across the country are heavily contaminated with toxic wastes.

    全国的核武器工厂均受到了有毒废弃物的严重污染。

  • More than 100,000 people could fall ill after drinking contaminated water.

    超过10万人可能会在饮用受到污染的水后患病。

contamination

  • The contamination of the sea around Capri may be just the beginning.

    卡普里岛附近海域的污染也许仅仅是个开始。
