authorize
ɔːθəraɪz ˈɔθərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense authorizes, present participle authorizing, past tense , past participle authorized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 authorise
VERB 批准；准许；认可 If someone in a position of authority authorizes something, they give their official permission for it to happen. 批准；准许；认可

  • It would certainly be within his power to authorize a police raid like that. [VERB noun]

    他绝对有权批准那样的一次警方突袭行动。

  • We are willing to authorize the president to use force if necessary. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    我们愿意授权总统在必要的时候使用武力。

SYN empower, commission, enable, entitle  
authorization

  • The United Nations will approve his request for authorization to use military force to deliver aid.

    联合国将批准他动用军队运送救援物资的请求。
