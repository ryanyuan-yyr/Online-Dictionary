annoyed
ənɔɪd əˈnɔɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] [ADJ that] 生气的；恼怒的 If you are annoyed, you are fairly angry about something. 生气的；恼怒的

  • She tapped her forehead and looked annoyed with herself.

    她拍打着脑门，看上去在生自己的气。

  • She is hurt and annoyed that the authorities have banned her from working with children.

    当局禁止她从事照管儿童的工作，她为此感到很伤心也很恼怒。

SYN irritated, bothered, harassed, hassled [informal]  
2See also:annoy 
Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse annoyed and annoying. See also note at angry.

    注意不要混淆annoyed和annoying，亦见angry词条下的说明。
