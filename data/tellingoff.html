telling-off
(also telling off)Word forms: plural tellings-off
N-COUNT [usually singular] 责备;斥责;训斥 If you give someone a telling-off, you tell them that you are very angry with them about something they have done. 责备;斥责;训斥 [informal]

  • I got a severe telling off for not phoning him. [+ for]

    我因为没给他打电话，被狠狠训斥了一顿。

SYN reprimand, talking-to [informal] , row, criticism
