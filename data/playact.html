play-act
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense play-acts, present participle play-acting, past tense , past participle play-acted
VERB [usually cont] 装扮；假装；装腔作势 If someone is play-acting, they are pretending to have attitudes or feelings that they do not really have. 装扮；假装；装腔作势

  • The 'victim' revealed he was only play acting. [VERB]

    这名“受害者”透露他只是假装的。
