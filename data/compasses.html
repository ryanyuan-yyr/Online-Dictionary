compass
kʌmpəs ˈkʌmpəs ˈkɑmpəs
Word forms: plural compasses
1 N-COUNT 指南针;罗盘 A compass is an instrument that you use for finding directions. It has a dial and a magnetic needle that always points to the north. 指南针;罗盘

  • We had to rely on a compass and a lot of luck to get here.

    我们不得不依靠指南针和不错的运气找到这儿来。

2 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] 圆规 Compasses are a hinged V-shaped instrument that you use for drawing circles. 圆规
3 N-COUNT [usually singular, with supplement] 界限;范围 If something is within the compass of something or someone, it is within their limits or abilities. 界限;范围 [formal]

  • Within the compass of a normal sized book such a comprehensive survey was not practicable.

    在通常一本书的篇幅中要作如此全面的调查不太实际。

SYN range, field, area, reach
