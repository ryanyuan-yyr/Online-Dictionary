televangelist
telɪvændʒəlɪst ˌtɛlɪˈvændʒəlɪst ˌtɛləˈvændʒəlɪst
Word forms: plural televangelists
N-COUNT (定期在电视上宣扬基督教并筹款的)电视福音布道者 A televangelist is someone who makes regular television broadcasts to promote a particular form of Christianity and raise money for particular Christian groups or projects. (定期在电视上宣扬基督教并筹款的)电视福音布道者
