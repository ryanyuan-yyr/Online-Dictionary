nomad
noʊmæd ˈnoʊˌmæd
Word forms: plural nomads
N-COUNT 游牧民;游牧部落的人 A nomad is a member of a group of people who travel from place to place rather than living in one place all the time. 游牧民;游牧部落的人

  • ...a country of nomads who raise cattle and camels.

    饲养牛和骆驼的游牧国度

SYN wanderer, migrant, rover, rambler
