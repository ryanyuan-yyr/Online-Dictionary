gateau
gætoʊ ˈɡætəʊ
Word forms: plural gateaux
N-VAR 味道油腻的精致蛋糕；（尤指）奶油大蛋糕 A gateau is a very rich, elaborate cake, especially one with cream in it. 味道油腻的精致蛋糕；（尤指）奶油大蛋糕 [mainly British]

  • ...a large slice of gateau.

    一大块奶油蛋糕

  • ...a huge selection of gateaux, cakes and pastries.

    种类繁多的奶油蛋糕、糕饼和油酥糕
