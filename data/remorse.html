remorse
rɪmɔːʳs rɪˈmɔrs
N-UNCOUNT 懊悔;自责;悔恨 Remorse is a strong feeling of sadness and regret about something wrong that you have done. 懊悔;自责;悔恨

  • He was full of remorse.

    他充满了悔恨。

  • He has shown no remorse for his actions.

    他对自己的行为没有任何悔恨之意。

SYN regret, shame, guilt, pity
