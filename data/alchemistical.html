alchemy
ælkəmi ˈælkəmɪ ˈælkəmi
1 N-UNCOUNT 炼金术；炼丹术 Alchemy was a form of chemistry studied in the Middle Ages, which was concerned with trying to discover ways to change ordinary metals into gold. 炼金术；炼丹术
2 N-UNCOUNT （改变事物的）魔力 Alchemy is the power to change or create things in a way which seems mysterious and magical. （改变事物的）魔力 [literary]

  • Let us imagine that by some political alchemy it had been possible to make all men equal.

    让我们想象通过某种政治魔力能使人人平等。

SYN magic, witchcraft, wizardry, sorcery
