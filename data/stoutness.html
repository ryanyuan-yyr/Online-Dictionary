stout
staʊt staʊt
Word forms: comparative stouter, superlative stoutest, plural stouts
1 ADJ-GRADED 肥胖的;肥壮的 A stout person is rather fat. 肥胖的;肥壮的

  • He was a tall, stout man with gray hair.

    他是个又高又胖、头发灰白的男子。

2 ADJ-GRADED 结实的;厚实的;粗壮的 Stout shoes, branches, or other objects are thick and strong. 结实的;厚实的;粗壮的

  • I hope you've both got stout shoes.

    我希望你们俩都有结实的鞋子。

  • The old man picked up a stout stick that lay by his feet.

    老头捡起脚边的一根粗棍子。

  • ...a stout oak door.

    结实的橡木门

3 ADJ-GRADED (行动、态度或信念)坚定的，坚决的，强有力的 If you use stout to describe someone's actions, attitudes, or beliefs, you approve of them because they are strong and determined. (行动、态度或信念)坚定的，坚决的，强有力的 [approval]

  • He produced a stout defence of the car business.

    他为汽车业进行了坚决的辩护。

  • The invasion was held up by unexpectedly stout resistance.

    入侵行动因遭遇到了出乎意料的顽强抵抗而受阻。

SYN brave, bold, courageous, fearless  
stoutly

  • She stoutly defended her husband during the trial.

    她在审判中为自己的丈夫进行了坚决的辩护。

  • ...stoutly anti-imperialist nations.

    坚决反对帝国主义的一些国家

4 N-MASS 烈性黑啤酒 Stout is a strong dark-coloured beer. 烈性黑啤酒 [British]

  • ...Guinness and other Irish stouts.

    吉尼斯黑啤酒及其他爱尔兰烈性黑啤酒
