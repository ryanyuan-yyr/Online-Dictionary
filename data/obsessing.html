obsess
əbses əbˈsɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense obsesses, present participle obsessing, past tense , past participle obsessed
V-ERG （使）着迷；（使）迷恋；（使）心神不宁 If something obsesses you or if you obsess about something, you keep thinking about it and find it difficult to think about anything else. （使）着迷；（使）迷恋；（使）心神不宁

  • I must admit that maps obsess me. [VERB noun]

    我得承认我对地图十分着迷。

  • A string of scandals is obsessing America. [VERB noun]

    美国正被一系列丑闻所困扰。

  • She stopped drinking but began obsessing about her weight. [V + about/over]

    她戒了酒，但又开始执著于体重问题。

  • I started obsessing that Trish might die. [VERB that]

    我开始心神不宁，想着特里希可能会死。

SYN preoccupy, dominate, grip, absorb
