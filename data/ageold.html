age-old
ˈeɪdʒˌoʊld
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （故事、传统或问题）古老的，已存在很久的 An age-old story, tradition, or problem has existed for many generations or centuries. （故事、传统或问题）古老的，已存在很久的 [written]

  • This age-old struggle for control had led to untold bloody wars.

    这场由来已久的对控制权的争夺已经引发了无数流血的战争。

SYN ancient, old, aged, timeworn
