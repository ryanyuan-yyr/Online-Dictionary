weakness
wiːknəs wiknɪs
Word forms: plural weaknesses
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 嗜好;癖好;迷恋 If you have a weakness for something, you like it very much, although this is perhaps surprising or undesirable. 嗜好;癖好;迷恋

  • Stephen himself had a weakness for cats. [+ for]

    史蒂芬本人偏爱猫。

  • His one weakness, apart from aeroplanes, is ice cream.

    除了对飞机情有独钟，他还嗜好冰激凌。
