stack up
1 PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 与…相比较 If you ask how one person or thing stacks up against other people or things, you are asking how the one compares with the others. 与…相比较 [informal]

  • The British will be out to see how they stack up to the competition. [V P against/to n]

    英国人将全力以赴，一较高低。

  • How does this final presidential debate stack up and compare to the others, do you think? [VERB PREP.]

    你认为这最后一次总统竞选辩论和其他几次相比怎么样？

2 PHRASAL VERB 站得住脚;符合预期 If facts or figures do not stack up, they do not make sense or give the results you expect. 站得住脚;符合预期

  • There have been a number of explanations, but none of them stack up. [VERB PREP.]

    已经提出了好几种解释，但没有一种能站得住脚。
