gaffe
gæf gæf
(also gaff)Word forms: plural gaffes
1 N-COUNT 失态；失礼 A gaffe is a stupid or careless mistake, for example when you say or do something that offends or upsets people. 失态；失礼

  • He made an embarrassing gaffe at the convention last weekend.

    他在上周末的会议上出了洋相，狼狈不堪。

  • ...social gaffes committed by high-ranking individuals.

    高层人士在社交场合的一些失态行为

SYN blunder, mistake, error, indiscretion  
2 PHRASE 泄露秘密；走漏消息 If you blow the gaffe or blow the gaff, you tell someone something that other people wanted you to keep secret. 泄露秘密；走漏消息 [British, informal]
Quotations
