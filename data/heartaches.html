heartache
hɑːʳteɪk ˈhɑrtˌeɪk
(also heart-ache)Word forms: plural heartaches
N-VAR 伤心;巨大的悲痛;痛心 Heartache is very great sadness and emotional suffering. 伤心;巨大的悲痛;痛心

  • ...after suffering the heartache of her divorce from her first husband.

    经过和第一任丈夫离婚的伤心经历后

  • ...all the joys and heartaches of parenthood.

    为人父母的所有快乐和痛苦

SYN sorrow, suffering, pain, torture
