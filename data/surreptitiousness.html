surreptitious
sʌrəptɪʃəs sɜːr- ˌsɜrəpˈtɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 秘密的;鬼鬼祟祟的;偷偷摸摸的 A surreptitious action is done secretly. 秘密的;鬼鬼祟祟的;偷偷摸摸的

  • He made a surreptitious entrance to the club through the little door in the brick wall.

    他从砖墙的小门偷偷进入俱乐部。

  • They had several surreptitious conversations.

    他们有过几次秘密会谈。

SYN secret, clandestine, furtive, sneaking  
surreptitiously

  • Surreptitiously Mark looked at his watch.

    马克偷偷看了看表。
