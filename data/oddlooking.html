odd-looking
ˈɒdlʊkɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 相貌奇特的；外表怪异的;奇形怪状的 If you describe someone or something as odd-looking, you think that they look strange or unusual. 相貌奇特的；外表怪异的;奇形怪状的

  • They were an odd-looking couple.

    这对夫妇长相奇特。

  • One of the waitresses arrived with a very odd-looking dish.

    一位女招待端来一道样子相当古怪的菜。
