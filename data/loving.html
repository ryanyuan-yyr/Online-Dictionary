loving
lʌvɪŋ ˈlʌvɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 充满爱的；钟爱的；关爱的 Someone who is loving feels or shows love to other people. 充满爱的；钟爱的；关爱的

  • Jim was a most loving husband and father.

    吉姆是个非常体贴的丈夫和慈爱的父亲。

  • The children there were very loving to me.

    那里的孩子都很爱我。

SYN affectionate, kind, warm, dear  
lovingly

  • Brian gazed lovingly at Mary Ann.

    布赖恩爱怜地凝视着玛丽·安。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （行为）爱护有加的，细心周到的 Loving actions are done with great enjoyment and care. （行为）爱护有加的，细心周到的

  • The house has been restored with loving care.

    此屋已被悉心修复。

SYN tender, kind, caring, warm  
lovingly

  • I lifted the box and ran my fingers lovingly over the top.

    我拎起盒子，爱惜地抚摩着盒盖。
