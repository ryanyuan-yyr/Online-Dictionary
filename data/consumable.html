consumable
kənsjuːməbəl -suː-
Word forms: plural consumables
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 消耗性的 Consumable goods are items which are intended to be bought, used, and then replaced. 消耗性的

  • ...demand for consumable articles.

    对消耗品的需求

Consumable is also a noun.

  • Suppliers add computer consumables, office equipment and furniture to their product range.

    供货商在其产品范围中增加了计算机耗材、办公设备和家具。
