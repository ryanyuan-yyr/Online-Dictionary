perky
pɜːʳki ˈpɜrki
Word forms: comparative perkier, superlative perkiest
ADJ-GRADED 高兴的；快活的；活跃的 If someone is perky, they are cheerful and lively. 高兴的；快活的；活跃的

  • He wasn't quite as perky as normal.

    他并不像往常那么高兴。

  • She had a perky, independent spirit.

    她个性开朗活泼而又独立。

SYN lively, spirited, bright, sunny
