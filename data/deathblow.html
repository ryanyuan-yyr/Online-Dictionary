death blow dɛθ bləʊ
(also death-blow)
N-SING 致命的打击;导致终结的原因 If you say that an event or action deals a death blow to something such as a plan or hope, or is a death blow to something, you mean that it puts an end to it. 致命的打击;导致终结的原因 [journalism]

  • The deportations would be a death blow to the peace process. [+ to]

    这些驱逐行动将给和平进程带来致命的一击。

  • The people of Hatfield went into shock as they learned their town had been dealt a death blow.

    哈特菲尔德的人们得知他们的镇子遭遇灾难性事故的消息后都深感震惊。
