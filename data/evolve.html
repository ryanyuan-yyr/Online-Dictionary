evolve
ɪvɒlv iˈvɑlv iˈvɔlv ɪˈvɑlv ɪˈvɔlv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense evolves, present participle evolving, past tense , past participle evolved
1 VERB 进化；演变 When animals or plants evolve, they gradually change and develop into different forms. 进化；演变

  • The bright plumage of many male birds has evolved to attract females. [VERB]

    很多雄鸟进化出鲜艳的羽毛是为了吸引雌鸟。

  • Maize evolved from a wild grass in Mexico. [VERB + from]

    玉米是从墨西哥的一种野生禾本植物进化而来的。

  • ...when amphibians evolved into reptiles. [VERB + into]

    当两栖动物进化成爬行动物

2 V-ERG （使）逐步发展;（使）演化 If something evolves or you evolve it, it gradually develops over a period of time into something different and usually more advanced. （使）逐步发展;（使）演化

  • ...a tiny airline which eventually evolved into Pakistan International Airlines. [VERB + into]

    最终发展成巴基斯坦国际航空公司的一家小型航空公司

  • Popular music evolved from folk songs. [VERB + from]

    流行音乐从民歌演变而来。

  • As medical knowledge evolves, beliefs change. [VERB]

    随着医学知识的逐步发展，观念也在发生变化。

  • This was when he evolved the working method from which he has never departed. [VERB noun]

    就在这个时候，他逐步形成了那种他一直沿用至今的工作方法。

SYN grow, develop, advance, progress
