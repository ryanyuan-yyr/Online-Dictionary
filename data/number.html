number
nʌmbəʳ ˈnʌmbər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense numbers, present participle numbering, past tense , past participle numbered
1 N-COUNT 数;数字 A number is a word such as 'two', 'nine', or 'twelve', or a symbol such as 1, 3, or 47. You use numbers to say how many things you are referring to or where something comes in a series. 数;数字

  • No, I don't know the room number.

    不，我不知道房间号。

  • Stan Laurel was born at number 3, Argyll Street.

    斯坦·劳雷尔出生在阿盖尔街3号。

  • The number 47 bus leaves in 10 minutes.

    47路公共汽车10分钟后开车。

2 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] (同 large 或 small 连用，表示约数)数量，数目 You use number with words such as 'large' or 'small' to say approximately how many things or people there are. (同 large 或 small 连用，表示约数)数量，数目

  • Quite a considerable number of interviews are going on. [+ of]

    大量的采访在继续。

  • I have had an enormous number of letters from single parents. [+ of]

    我已经收到了大量单亲家长的来信。

  • Growing numbers of people in the rural areas are too frightened to vote. [+ of]

    越来越多生活在乡下的人被吓得不敢投票了。

SYN amount, quantity, collection, total  
3 N-SING 若干/许多 If there are a number of things or people, there are several of them. If there are any number of things or people, there is a large quantity of them. 若干/许多

  • I seem to remember that Sam told a number of lies. [+ of]

    我好像记得萨姆说过不少谎话。

  • There must be any number of people in my position. [+ of]

    一定有很多人处在我这种境况。

SYN several, a few, various  
4 N-UNCOUNT (排名)第…号 You can refer to someone's or something's position in a list of the most successful or most popular of a particular type of thing as, for example, number one or number two. (排名)第…号

  • ...the world number one, Tiger Woods.

    世界排名第一的泰格·伍兹

  • Before you knew it, the single was at Number 90 in the US singles charts.

    转眼之间这张单曲唱片已在美国单曲唱片排行榜上排名第90位了。

  • Vikram Seth's 'A Suitable Boy' is number two in the best-seller lists.

    维克拉姆·塞思的《如意郎君》在畅销书排行榜中排名第二。

5 VERB 总计;计有 If a group of people or things numbers a particular total, that is how many there are. 总计;计有 [Also V n + in]

  • They told me that their village numbered 100. [V num]

    他们告诉我他们村子有100人。

  • This time the dead were numbered in hundreds, not dozens. [be VERB-ed + in]

    这次死亡人数是数以百计，而非几十人。

SYN amount to, come to, total, add up to  
6 N-COUNT 电话号码 A number is the series of numbers that you dial when you are making a telephone call. 电话号码

  • Sarah sat down and dialled a number.

    萨拉坐下来拨了一个电话号码。

  • ...a list of names and telephone numbers.

    姓名和电话号码表

  • My number is 414-3925.

    我的电话号码是414-3925。

  • 'You must have a wrong number,' she said. 'There's no one of that name here.'

    “你一定是拨错电话号码了，”她说：“这儿没有这个人。”

7 N-COUNT 一段音乐;一首歌曲;一支舞 You can refer to a short piece of music, a song, or a dance as a number . 一段音乐;一首歌曲;一支舞

  • ...'Unforgettable', a number that was written and performed in 1951.

    《难以忘怀》，一首1951年创作并演唱的歌曲

  • Responsibility for the dance numbers was split between Robert Alton and the young George Balanchine.

    舞蹈节目分别由罗伯特·奥尔顿和年轻的乔治·巴兰钦负责。

8 VERB 把…算作;把…归入 If someone or something is numbered among a particular group, they are believed to belong in that group. 把…算作;把…归入 [formal]

  • The Leicester Swannington Railway is numbered among Britain's railway pioneers. [be V-ed + among]

    莱斯特—斯旺宁顿铁路被看作是英国最早的铁路之一。

  • He numbered several Americans among his friends. [V n + among]

    他把几个美国人看成朋友。

SYN include, count  
9 VERB 把…编号;给…标号 If you number something, you mark it with a number, usually starting at 1. 把…编号;给…标号

  • He cut his paper up into tiny squares, and he numbered each one. [VERB noun]

    他把纸剪成了小方块，然后逐一编号。

  • Each factor has been numbered. [VERB noun]

    每个要素都编了号。

10 See also: opposite number  prime number  serial number 
11 PHRASE [with poss] 寿命不长;时日无多 If you say that someone's or something's days are numbered, you mean that they will not survive or be successful for much longer. 寿命不长;时日无多

  • Critics believe his days are numbered because audiences are tired of watching him.

    批评家猜想他走红的时间不会长了，因为观众现在厌倦了他的表演。

12 PHRASE …中的一员 One of your number is a member of your group. …中的一员

  • Scientists like the idea that one of their number is close to the seat of power.

    科学家们欢迎他们中的一员接近权力中心的想法。

  • One of our number has made a very interesting design of flooring, which has won a prize.

    我们中的一名成员设计出了非常有趣的地板图案，还获了奖。

13 PHRASE (非法的)彩票赌博，博彩 If you refer to the numbers game ,the numbers racket, or the numbers, you are referring to an illegal lottery or illegal betting. (非法的)彩票赌博，博彩 [US]
