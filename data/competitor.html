competitor
kəmpetɪtəʳ kəmˈpɛtətər
Word forms: plural competitors
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 竞争者;对手 A company's competitors are companies who are trying to sell similar goods or services to the same people. 竞争者;对手

  • The bank isn't performing as well as some of its competitors.

    这家银行表现得不如几家竞争对手出色。

SYN rival, competition, opposition, adversary  
2 N-COUNT 选手;参赛者 A competitor is a person who takes part in a competition or contest. 选手;参赛者

  • Herbert Blocker of Germany, one of the oldest competitors, won the individual silver medal.

    德国的赫伯特·布洛克尔是年纪最大的选手之一，赢得了个人项目的银牌。

SYN contestant, participant, contender, challenger
