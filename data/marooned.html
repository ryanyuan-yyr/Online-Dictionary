marooned
məruːnd məˈruːnd
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 被围困的;孤立无援的;无法脱身的 If you say that you are marooned, you mean that you feel alone and helpless and you cannot escape from the place or situation you are in. 被围困的;孤立无援的;无法脱身的

  • ...families marooned in decaying inner-city areas.

    无法脱离日益衰败的内城区的家庭

  • ...temporarily marooned at home by my injured knee.

    我由于膝伤暂时无法出门
