legitimate
lɪdʒɪtɪmət lɪdʒɪtɪmət lɪdʒɪtɪmeɪt ləˈdʒɪtəmət ləˈdʒɪtəˌmeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense legitimates, present participle legitimating, past tense , past participle legitimated
The adjective is pronounced /lɪ'dʒɪtɪmət/. The verb is pronounced /lɪ'dʒɪtɪmeɪt/. 形容词读作 /lɪ'dʒɪtɪmət/。动词读作/lɪ'dʒɪtɪmeɪt/。
1 ADJ-GRADED 合法的；依法的；法律认可的 Something that is legitimate is acceptable according to the law. 合法的；依法的；法律认可的

  • The French government has condemned the coup in Haiti and has demanded the restoration of the legitimate government.

    法国政府对海地政变表示谴责，并要求恢复合法政府的地位。

  • The government will not seek to disrupt the legitimate business activities of the defendant.

    政府无意干扰被告的合法经营活动。

SYN lawful, real, true, legal  
legitimacy

  • The opposition parties do not recognise the political legitimacy of his government.

    反对党不承认他的政府在政治上的合法性。

legitimately

  • The government has been legitimately elected by the people.

    政府是由人民依法选出的。

2 ADJ-GRADED 合情理的；有道理的；正当的 If you say that something such as a feeling or claim is legitimate, you think that it is reasonable and justified. 合情理的；有道理的；正当的

  • That's a perfectly legitimate fear.

    怀有这种恐惧完全在情理之中。

  • The New York Times has a legitimate claim to be a national newspaper.

    《纽约时报》有理由称自己是一份全国性报纸。

SYN reasonable, just, correct, sensible  
legitimacy

  • As if to prove the legitimacy of these fears, the Cabinet of Franz von Papen collapsed on December 2.

    仿佛是要印证这些恐惧事出有因似的，弗朗茨·冯·巴本的内阁于12月2日倒台了。

legitimately

  • They could quarrel quite legitimately with some of my choices.

    他们大有理由不同意我的一些选择。

3 ADJ 婚生的 A legitimate child is one whose parents were married before he or she was born. 婚生的

  • We only married in order that the child should be legitimate.

    我们结婚纯粹是为了给孩子一个合法身份。

4 VERB 同legitimize To legitimate something means the same as to legitimize it. 同legitimize [formal]

  • We want to legitimate this process by passing a law. [VERB noun]

    我们希望通过立法来使这一程序合法化。

SYN legitimize, allow, permit, sanction  
legitimation

  • ...the legitimation of state constitutions.

    国家宪法的正式通过
