schedule
ʃedjuːl ˈʃɛdʒʊl ˈʃɛdjʊl skedʒuːl ˈskɛdʒʊl ˈskɛdʒʊəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense schedules, present participle scheduling, past tense , past participle scheduled
1 N-COUNT 工作计划;日程安排 A schedule is a plan that gives a list of events or tasks and the times at which each one should happen or be done. 工作计划;日程安排

  • He has been forced to adjust his schedule.

    他被迫调整了自己的日程安排。

  • We both have such hectic schedules.

    我们俩日程安排得都很紧。

SYN plan, programme, agenda, calendar  
2 N-UNCOUNT 计划的时间(或方式) You can use schedule to refer to the time or way something is planned to be done. For example, if something is completed on schedule, it is completed at the time planned. 计划的时间(或方式)

  • The jet arrived in Johannesburg two minutes ahead of schedule.

    喷气式飞机提前两分钟到达了约翰内斯堡。

  • Everything went according to schedule.

    一切都按计划进行。

  • It will be completed several weeks behind schedule.

    这将比原计划晚几个星期完成。

3 VERB [usually passive] (在时间上)安排，计划 If something is scheduled to happen at a particular time, arrangements are made for it to happen at that time. (在时间上)安排，计划

  • The space shuttle had been scheduled to blast off at 04:38. [be VERB-ed to-infinitive]

    航天飞机已经预定于凌晨 4 点 38 分发射升空。

  • A presidential election was scheduled for last December. [be VERB-ed + for]

    总统选举原计划在去年 12 月举行。

  • No new talks are scheduled. [be VERB-ed]

    没有安排新的会谈。

SYN plan, set up, book, programme  
4 N-COUNT (价格、细节或条件等的)一览表，明细表，清单 A schedule is a written list of things, for example a list of prices, details, or conditions. (价格、细节或条件等的)一览表，明细表，清单
5 N-COUNT (火车、客轮、公共汽车或航班的)时刻表,时间表 A schedule is a list of all the times when trains, boats, buses, or aircraft are supposed to arrive at or leave a particular place. (火车、客轮、公共汽车或航班的)时刻表,时间表 [mainly US]

  • ...a bus schedule.

    公共汽车时刻表

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 timetable
6 N-COUNT (学校的)课程表 In a school or college, a schedule is a diagram that shows the times in the week at which particular subjects are taught. (学校的)课程表 [US]
in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 timetable
