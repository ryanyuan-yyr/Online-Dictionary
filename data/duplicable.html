duplicate
djuːplɪkeɪt djuːplɪkət duː- duː- ˈduplɪkɪt ˈdjuplɪkɪt ˈduplɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense duplicates, present participle duplicating, past tense , past participle duplicated
The verb is pronounced /'djuːplɪkeɪt, am 'duː-/. The noun and adjective are pronounced /'djuːplɪkət, am 'duː-/. 动词读作 /'djuːplɪkeɪt, 美 'duː-/,名词和形容词读作 /'djuːplɪkət, 美 'duː-/。
1 VERB 重复;复制 If you duplicate something that has already been done, you repeat or copy it. 重复;复制

  • His task will be to duplicate his success overseas here at home. [VERB noun]

    他在海外取得了成功，现在的任务就是要在国内再创辉煌。

  • Scientists hope the work done in collaboration with other researchers may be duplicated elsewhere. [VERB noun]

    科学家们希望与其他研究人员合作的成果可以适用于别处。

SYN repeat, reproduce, echo, copy  
Duplicate is also a noun.

  • Charles scored again, with an exact duplicate of his first goal.

    查尔斯梅开二度,与第一次破门如出一辙。

2 VERB 复制;复印;复写 To duplicate something which has been written, drawn, or recorded onto tape means to make exact copies of it. 复制;复印;复写

  • She found Ned alone in the photocopy room, duplicating some articles. [VERB noun]

    她发现内德一个人在复印室里复印一些文章。

  • ...a business which duplicates video and cinema tapes for the movie makers. [VERB noun]

    为电影制片商复制录像带和影带的行业

Duplicate is also a noun.

  • I'm on my way to Switzerland, but I've lost my card. I've got to get a duplicate.

    我正在去瑞士的路上,可是我把卡弄丢了，得去补办一张。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 完全一样的;复制的;副本的 Duplicate is used to describe things that have been made as an exact copy of other things, usually in order to serve the same purpose. 完全一样的;复制的;副本的

  • He let himself in with a duplicate key.

    他用一把复制的钥匙打开门进去。

  • ...a duplicate copy of the loan contract.

    贷款合同的副本

SYN identical, matched, matching, twin
