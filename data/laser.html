laser
leɪzəʳ ˈleɪzər
Word forms: plural lasers
1 N-COUNT 激光 A laser is a narrow beam of concentrated light produced by a special machine. It is used for cutting very hard materials, and in many technical fields such as surgery and telecommunications. 激光

  • ...new laser technology.

    新的激光技术

  • Researchers realised that a tunable laser beam might be useful in surgery.

    研究人员意识到，可调谐激光束也许可以用在外科手术中。

2 N-COUNT 激光器 A laser is a machine that produces a laser beam. 激光器

  • ...the first-ever laser, built in 1960.

    有史以来第一台激光器，于1960年制造
