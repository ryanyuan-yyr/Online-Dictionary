isolationism
aɪsəleɪʃənɪzəm ˌaɪsəˈleɪʃəˌnɪzəm
Word forms: plural isolationists
N-UNCOUNT 孤立主义(指避免与别国关系过密或在别国之间的冲突中持中立态度) If you refer to isolationism, you are referring to a country's policy of avoiding close relationships with other countries and of not taking sides in disputes between other countries. 孤立主义(指避免与别国关系过密或在别国之间的冲突中持中立态度)

  • ...the perils of isolationism.

    孤立主义的危险
