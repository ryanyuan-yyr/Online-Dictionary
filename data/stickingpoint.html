sticking point
(also sticking-point)Word forms: plural sticking points
N-COUNT [usually singular] (讨论或谈判中的)僵持点，障碍;(问题的)症结 A sticking point in a discussion or series of negotiations is a point on which the people involved cannot agree and which may delay or stop the talks. A sticking point is also one aspect of a problem which you have trouble dealing with. (讨论或谈判中的)僵持点，障碍;(问题的)症结

  • The main sticking point was the question of taxes.

    主要的症结是税收问题。
