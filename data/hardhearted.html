hard-hearted
ADJ-GRADED 硬心肠的;冷酷的;无同情心的 If you describe someone as hard-hearted, you disapprove of the fact that they have no sympathy for other people and do not care if people are hurt or made unhappy. 硬心肠的;冷酷的;无同情心的 [disapproval]

  • You would have to be pretty hard-hearted not to feel something for him.

    只有铁石心肠的人才会对他无动于衷。

SYN unsympathetic, hard, cold, cruel
