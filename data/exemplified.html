exemplify
ɪgzemplɪfaɪ ɛgˈzɛmpləˌfaɪ ɪgˈzɛmpləˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense exemplifies, present participle exemplifying, past tense , past participle exemplified
VERB 作为…的例证;是…的典范 If a person or thing exemplifies something such as a situation, quality, or class of things, they are a typical example of it. 作为…的例证;是…的典范 [formal]

  • The room's style exemplifies Conran's ideal of 'beauty and practicality'. [VERB noun]

    那个房间的风格是体现康兰“美观和实用”观念的典范。

  • ...the emotional expressiveness of modern dance as exemplified by the work of Martha Graham. [VERB-ed]

    以玛莎·格雷厄姆的作品为代表的现代舞的情感表现力

SYN show, represent, display, demonstrate
