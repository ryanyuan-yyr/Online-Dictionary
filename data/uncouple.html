uncouple
ʌnkʌpəl ʌnˈkʌpəl ʌnˈkʌpəl
Word forms: uncouples, uncoupling, uncoupled
1 V-T 使脱离If two vehicles or pieces of equipment are uncoupled, they have been unfastened and are no longer joined together. 使脱离

  • They uncoupled the passenger cars from the train engine.

    他们让乘客车厢与火车头脱离开。

2 V-T 使分离If two things that were connected or combined are uncoupled, they have been separated from each other. 使分离

  • The government uncoupled the peso from the dollar yesterday for the first time in 11 years.

    昨天政府十一年来第一次使比索脱离美元。
