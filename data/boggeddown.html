bogged down
bɒɡd daʊn
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 深陷泥沼的；停滞不前的 If you get bogged down in something, it prevents you from making progress or getting something done. 深陷泥沼的；停滞不前的

  • But why get bogged down in legal details?. [+ in]

    可是为什么要纠缠于那些法律细节呢？

  • Sometimes this fact is obscured because churches get so bogged down by unimportant rules.

    有时候教会因为过分拘泥于那些无关紧要的规则而无法看清这一事实。

SYN entangled, involved, overwhelmed, mixed up
