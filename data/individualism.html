individualism
ɪndɪvɪdzʊlɪzəm ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəlˌɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 个性;特立独行 You use individualism to refer to the behaviour of someone who likes to think and do things in their own way, rather than imitating other people. 个性;特立独行

  • He is struck by what he calls the individualism of American officers.

    他称其为美国军官的个性给他留下了很深的印象。

SYN independence, self-interest, originality, self-reliance  
2 N-UNCOUNT (国家对经济、政治的)不干涉主义，自由放任主义 Individualism is the belief that economics and politics should not be controlled by the state. (国家对经济、政治的)不干涉主义，自由放任主义

  • ...the strong individualism in their political culture.

    他们的政治文化中浓厚的个人自由主义
