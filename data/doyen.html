doyen
dɔɪən dɔɪen dway ˈdɔɪən ˈdɔɪˌɛn dwɑˈjɛn
Word forms: plural doyens
N-COUNT [usually singular] (某团体或职业中的)老前辈，资格最老者，元老 If you refer to a man as the doyen of a group or profession, you mean that he is the oldest and most experienced and respected member of it. (某团体或职业中的)老前辈，资格最老者，元老 [formal, approval]

  • ...the doyen of political interviewers.

    资深的政治采访记者
