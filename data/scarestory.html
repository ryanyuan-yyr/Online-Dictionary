scare story
skɛə ˈstɔːrɪ
Word forms: plural scare stories
N-COUNT 恐怖作品;恐怖故事 A scare story is something that is said or written to make people feel frightened and think that a situation is much more unpleasant or dangerous than it really is. 恐怖作品;恐怖故事

  • He described talk of sackings as scare stories.

    他说人们谈起解雇犹如在讲恐怖故事。
