instrument panel
Word forms: plural instrument panels
N-COUNT 仪表板 The instrument panel of a plane, car, or machine is the panel where the dials and switches are located. 仪表板

  • They had failed to recognise signs on their instrument panel indicating a serious problem in the left engine.

    他们没有意识到仪表板上的指示信号表明左引擎出现了严重问题。
