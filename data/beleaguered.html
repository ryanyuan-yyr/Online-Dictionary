beleaguered
bɪliːgəʳd bɪˈliːɡəd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 处境艰难的；饱受非议的；备受指责的 A beleaguered person, organization, or project is experiencing a lot of difficulties, opposition, or criticism. 处境艰难的；饱受非议的；备受指责的 [formal]

  • There have been seven coup attempts against the beleaguered government.

    风雨飘摇的政府已经遭到了7次未遂政变的打击。

SYN harassed, troubled, plagued, tormented  
2 ADJ 被敌人包围的；四面楚歌的；陷入重围的 A beleaguered place or army is surrounded by its enemies. 被敌人包围的；四面楚歌的；陷入重围的 [formal]

  • The rebels continue their push towards the beleaguered capital.

    叛军继续向四面被围的首都推进。

SYN besieged, surrounded, blockaded, encompassed
