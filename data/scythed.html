scythe
saɪð saɪð
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense scythes, present participle scything, past tense , past participle scythed
1 N-COUNT 长柄大镰刀;大钐镰 A scythe is a tool with a long curved blade at right angles to a long handle. It is used to cut long grass or grain. 长柄大镰刀;大钐镰
2 VERB 用长柄大镰刀割(草、庄稼) If you scythe grass or grain, you cut it with a scythe. 用长柄大镰刀割(草、庄稼)

  • Two men were attempting to scythe the long grass. [VERB noun]

    两个人正试图割掉疯长的草。
