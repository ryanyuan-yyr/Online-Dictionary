titular
tɪtʃʊləʳ ˈtɪtjʊlərɪ ˈtɪtʃələr ˈtɪtjələr
ADJ [ADJ n] 名义上的;有名无实的;徒有虚名的 A titular job or position has a name that makes it seem important, although the person who has it is not really important or powerful. 名义上的;有名无实的;徒有虚名的

  • He is titular head, and merely signs laws occasionally.

    他是名义上的首脑，不过偶尔签字批准法律法规。

SYN in name only, so-called, token, theoretical
