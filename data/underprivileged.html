underprivileged
ʌndəʳprɪvɪlɪdʒd ˌʌndəˈprɪvɪlɪdʒd ˌʌndərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd ˌʌndərˈprɪvlɪdʒd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 贫困的；弱势的 Underprivileged people have less money and fewer possessions and opportunities than other people in their society. 贫困的；弱势的

  • ...helping underprivileged children to learn to read.

    帮助贫困儿童读书识字

  • ...the hideous effects of government cuts on underprivileged families.

    政府削减支出给贫困家庭带来的巨大影响

SYN disadvantaged, poor, deprived, in need  
The underprivileged are people who are underprivileged.贫困人口；弱势群体

  • ...government plans to make more jobs available to the underprivileged.

    旨在为贫困人口创造更多就业机会的政府计划
