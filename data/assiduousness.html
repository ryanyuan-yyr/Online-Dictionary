assiduous
əsɪdʒuəs əˈsɪdʒuəs
ADJ-GRADED 刻苦的；勤奋的；兢兢业业的 Someone who is assiduous works hard or does things very thoroughly. 刻苦的；勤奋的；兢兢业业的

  • ...an assiduous student.

    勤勉的学生

  • Podulski had been assiduous in learning his adopted language.

    波多斯基一直很努力地学习外语。

SYN diligent, constant, steady, hard-working  
assiduously

  • They planned their careers and worked assiduously to see them achieved.

    他们规划了自己的事业，并且勤奋工作以实现目标。

  • He liked beautiful women and courted them assiduously.

    他喜欢美女，并且对她们大献殷勤。
