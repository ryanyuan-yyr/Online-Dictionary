halfwit
hɑːfwɪt hæf-
(also half-wit)Word forms: plural halfwits
1 N-COUNT 傻瓜;笨蛋 If you describe someone as a halfwit, you think they have behaved in a stupid, silly, or irresponsible way. 傻瓜;笨蛋 [informal, disapproval]
2 N-COUNT 弱智者;智残者;智力有缺陷者 A halfwit is a person who has little intelligence. 弱智者;智残者;智力有缺陷者 [old-fashioned]
