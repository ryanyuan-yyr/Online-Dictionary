lard
lɑːʳd lɑrd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lards, present participle larding, past tense , past participle larded
1 N-UNCOUNT 猪油 Lard is soft white fat obtained from pigs. It is used in cooking. 猪油

  • ...lard or beef fat.

    猪油或牛油

2 VERB （在言谈或文章中）充斥，大量夹杂（某类词语） If speech or writing is larded with particular types of words, it contains a lot of them or too many of them. （在言谈或文章中）充斥，大量夹杂（某类词语） [Also V n with n]

  • Official speeches in recent days have been larded with promises of democracy. [be V-ed with n]

    近日官方在讲话中满口对民主的承诺。

  • ...a long phone call, larded with 'darlings' and a sickening amount of baby-talk. [VERB-ed]

    一句一个“亲爱的”且充斥着令人反胃的儿语情话的电话粥
