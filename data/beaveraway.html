beaver away
PHRASAL VERB 卖力干；勤奋工作 If you are beavering away at something, you are working very hard at it. 卖力干；勤奋工作

  • They had a team of architects beavering away at a scheme for the rehabilitation of District 6. [V P + at/on]

    他们有一支建筑师队伍正在全力制订修复第6区的计划。

  • They are beavering away to get everything ready for us. [VERB PREP.]

    他们正在竭尽全力准备我们所需的一切。

SYN work, sweat, slave, persist
