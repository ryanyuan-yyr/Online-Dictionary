patterned
pætəʳnd ˈpætənd
1 ADJ 有图案装饰的；饰有花样的 Something that is patterned is covered with a pattern or design. 有图案装饰的；饰有花样的

  • ...a plain carpet with a patterned border.

    有饰边的纯色地毯

  • ...bone china patterned with flowers. [+ with]

    饰有鲜花图案的骨瓷

2 V-PASSIVE 仿造；模仿 If something new is patterned on something else that already exists, it is deliberately made so that it has similar features. 仿造；模仿 [mainly US]

  • New York City announced a 10-point policy patterned on the federal bill of rights for taxpayers. [be V-ed + on]

    纽约市模仿联邦纳税人权利法案公布了10点政策。

  • He says this contract should not be patterned after the Deere pact. [be V-ed + after]

    他说这个合同不应该模仿《迪尔协定》。
