minor league
Word forms: minor leagues
1 N-COUNT [oft N n] 职业棒球次级联盟In baseball, a minor league is a professional league that is not one of the major leagues. 职业棒球次级联盟 [US]

  • In 1952, there were 43 minor leagues and 324 teams nationwide.

    1952年，全国共有职业棒球次级联盟43个，棒球队324个。

  • After military service he played some minor league baseball.

    退伍后，他参加了一些职业棒球次级联盟的比赛。

2 ADJ 次要的; 二流的; 不怎么成功的Minor-league people are not very important or not very successful. 次要的; 二流的; 不怎么成功的 [mainly US]

  • ...minor-league celebrities.

    ...二流明星。

  • His economic team is minor-league compared with the heavy hitters such as Robert Rubin who advised Bill Clinton.

    和诸如罗伯特·鲁宾这类为比尔·克林顿出谋划策的显赫人物相比，他的经济顾问组显得有些逊色。
