dig over
PHRASAL VERB 深翻，犁开(土地) If you dig over an area of soil, you dig it thoroughly, so that the soil becomes looser and free from lumps. 深翻，犁开(土地)

  • Dig over any ground that is clear of crops and plants. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    把没种庄稼和植物的土地都深翻一遍。

  • Dig the soil over thoroughly. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把土地彻底犁开。
