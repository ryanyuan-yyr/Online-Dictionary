rascally
rɑːskəli ræs- ˈræskəli
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 流氓的；无赖的；卑鄙的；狡诈的 If you describe someone as a rascally person, you mean that they behave badly and are wicked or dishonest. 流氓的；无赖的；卑鄙的；狡诈的 [literary]

  • They stumble across a ghost town inhabited by a rascally gold prospector.

    他们偶然来到一个居住着一位狡诈的淘金者的废墟之城。

SYN villainous, mean, bad, low
