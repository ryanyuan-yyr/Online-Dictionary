shamefaced
ʃeɪmfeɪst -feɪst ˈʃeɪmˌfeɪst
ADJ-GRADED 面带愧色的;羞愧的;惭愧的 If you are shamefaced, you feel embarrassed because you have done something that you know you should not have done. 面带愧色的;羞愧的;惭愧的 [formal]

  • There was a long silence, and my father looked shamefaced.

    沉默持续了很长时间，而我父亲看上去面带愧色。

SYN embarrassed, humiliated, ashamed, red-faced
