leafy
liːfi ˈlifi
1 ADJ-GRADED （植物）叶茂的，多叶的 Leafy trees and plants have lots of leaves on them. （植物）叶茂的，多叶的

  • His two-story brick home was graced with a patio and surrounded by tall, leafy trees.

    他那两层高的砖房有一个漂亮的露台，四周环拥绿叶成阴的大树。

SYN green, leaved, leafed, shaded  
2 ADJ-GRADED （地方）草木葱茏的，树木繁茂的 You say that a place is leafy when there are lots of trees and plants there. （地方）草木葱茏的，树木繁茂的

  • ...semi-detached homes with gardens in leafy suburban areas.

    位于草木葱茏的市郊住宅区、带花园的半独立房子
