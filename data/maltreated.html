maltreat
mæltriːt mælˈtrit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense maltreats, present participle maltreating, past tense , past participle maltreated
VERB [usually passive] 虐待;粗暴对待 If a person or animal is maltreated, they are treated badly, especially by being hurt. 虐待;粗暴对待

  • He said that he was not tortured or maltreated during his detention. [be VERB-ed]

    他说自己在押期间并未遭受拷问和虐待。

  • Only when the animals were seriously maltreated was any inspection or prosecution called for. [be VERB-ed]

    只有在动物遭到严重虐待时才有必要进行调查或起诉。

SYN abuse, damage, hurt, injure
