scalper
skælpəʳ
Word forms: plural scalpers
N-COUNT 倒票者;黄牛;票贩子 A scalper is someone who sells tickets outside a sports ground or theatre, usually for more than their original value. 倒票者;黄牛;票贩子 [US]

  • Another scalper said he'd charge $1000 for a $125 ticket.

    另一个票贩子说 125 美元的票他要价 1,000 美元。
