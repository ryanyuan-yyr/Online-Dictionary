redistribute
riːdɪstrɪbjuːt ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːt ˌridɪsˈtrɪbjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redistributes, present participle redistributing, past tense , past participle redistributed
VERB 重新分配;再分配 If something such as money or property is redistributed, it is shared among people or organizations in a different way from the way that it was previously shared. 重新分配;再分配

  • Wealth was redistributed more equitably among society. [be VERB-ed]

    财富实现了在社会中更加公平地再分配。

  • Taxes could be used to redistribute income. [VERB noun]

    税收可以用来对收入进行再分配。

SYN re-allocate, divide up, share out, reassign  
redistribution

  • ...some redistribution of income so that the better off can help to keep the worse off out of poverty.

    某种形式的收入再分配以使富人可以帮助穷人脱贫
