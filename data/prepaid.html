prepaid
priːpeɪd ˌpriːˈpeɪd priˈpeɪd
(also pre-paid)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 预付费的 Prepaid items are paid for in advance, before the time when you would normally pay for them. 预付费的

  • Return the enclosed Donation Form today in the prepaid envelope provided.

    请于今日将随函附上的捐款表格用所提供的邮资预付的信封寄回。

  • ...prepaid funerals.

    预付费的葬礼
