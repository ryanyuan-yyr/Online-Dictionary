fill out
1 PHRASAL VERB 填写If you fill out a form or other document requesting information, you write information in the spaces on it. 填写 [mainly US] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Fill out the application carefully, and keep copies of it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    仔细填写申请表，并保留备份。

SYN complete, answer, fill in  
2 PHRASAL VERB 变胖; 变厚; 变圆If someone or something fills out, they become fuller, thicker, or rounder. 变胖; 变厚; 变圆

  • A girl may fill out before she reaches her full height. [VERB PREP.]

    女孩子长足个头之前可能会变胖。
