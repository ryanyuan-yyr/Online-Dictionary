clapped-out ˈklæptˈaʊt ˈklæptˈaʊt
(also clapped out)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人)老态龙钟的;(机器)破旧的，废旧的 If you describe a person or a machine as clapped-out, you mean that they are old and no longer able to work properly. (人)老态龙钟的;(机器)破旧的，废旧的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • ...his clapped-out old car.

    他那辆又旧又破的车

  • ...clapped out comedians.

    年老体衰的喜剧演员
