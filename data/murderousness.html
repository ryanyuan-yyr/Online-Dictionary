murderous
mɜːʳdərəs ˈmɜrdərəs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 蓄意谋杀的;杀人的 Someone who is murderous is likely to murder someone and may already have murdered someone. 蓄意谋杀的;杀人的

  • This murderous lunatic could kill them both without a second thought.

    这个杀人狂会毫不犹豫地把他俩都杀了。

SYN deadly, savage, brutal, destructive  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 凶残的;残忍的 A murderous attack or other action is very violent and intended to result in someone's death. 凶残的;残忍的

  • He made a murderous attack on his wife that evening.

    他那天晚上想置妻子于死地。
