armoured
ɑːʳməʳd ˈɑːməd
in AM, use 美国英语用 armored
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （车辆）装甲的，装钢板的 Armoured vehicles are fitted with a hard metal covering in order to protect them from gunfire and other missiles. （车辆）装甲的，装钢板的

  • More than forty armoured vehicles carrying troops have been sent into the area.

    载着部队官兵的40多辆装甲车被派往该地区。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （部队）装甲的，配备装甲车辆的 Armoured troops are troops in armoured vehicles. （部队）装甲的，配备装甲车辆的

  • These front-line defences are backed up by armoured units in reserve.

    前线的防务得到后备装甲部队的支援。
