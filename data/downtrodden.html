downtrodden
daʊntrɒdən ˈdaʊnˌtrɑdən
ADJ 受压迫的;遭蹂躏的;被践踏的 People who are downtrodden are treated very badly by people with power, and do not have the ability or the energy to do anything about it. 受压迫的;遭蹂躏的;被践踏的

  • The owner is making huge profits at the expense of downtrodden peasants.

    物主靠压榨农民而赢得暴利。

  • His mother was old, badly dressed and obviously downtrodden.

    他母亲老了，衣着破旧，显然尝尽了苦难。

The downtrodden are people who are downtrodden.

  • ...support for the downtrodden and underprivileged.

    对受压迫者和弱势群体的支持
