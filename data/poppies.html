poppy
pɒpi ˈpɑpi
Word forms: plural poppies
1 N-COUNT 罂粟 A poppy is a plant with a large, delicate flower, usually red in colour. The drug opium is obtained from one type of poppy. 罂粟

  • ...a field of poppies.

    罂粟地

2 N-COUNT （英国每年11月某日为悼念两次世界大战的死难者而佩戴的）人造罂粟花 In Britain, on a particular day in November, people wear an artificial poppy in memory of the people who died in the two world wars. （英国每年11月某日为悼念两次世界大战的死难者而佩戴的）人造罂粟花

  • She pinned the poppy to his raincoat.

    她把罂粟花别在了他的雨衣上。

  • ...a wreath of poppies.

    人造罂粟花圈
