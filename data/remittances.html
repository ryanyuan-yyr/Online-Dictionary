remittance
rɪmɪtəns rɪˈmɪtəns
Word forms: plural remittances
N-VAR (一笔)汇款 A remittance is a sum of money that you send to someone. (一笔)汇款 [formal]

  • Please enclose your remittance, making cheques payable to Thames Valley Technology.

    请附上汇款，支票收款人为泰晤士河谷科技公司。

SYN payment, money, fee, settlement
