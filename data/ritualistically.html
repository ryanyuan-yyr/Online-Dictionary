ritualistic
rɪtʃuəlɪstɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 例行公事般的;惯常的;例行的 Ritualistic actions or behaviour follow a similar pattern every time they are used. 例行公事般的;惯常的;例行的

  • Each evening she bursts into her apartment with a ritualistic shout of 'Honey I'm home!'

    每天晚上她都会像往常一样一边嚷着“亲爱的，我回来啦！”一边冲进公寓。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 宗教仪式的;典礼仪式的 Ritualistic acts are the fixed patterns of behaviour that form part of a religious service or ceremony. 宗教仪式的;典礼仪式的

  • ...the meditative and ritualistic practices of Buddhism.

    佛教的冥想和仪式
