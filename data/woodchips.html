woodchip
wʊdtʃɪp ˈwʊdˌtʃɪp
Word forms: plural woodchips
1 N-UNCOUNT （表层有许多木屑粘成的小块的）凹凸墙纸 Woodchip is a type of wallpaper which has lots of small lumps on its surface that are formed by tiny pieces of wood glued to the underneath. （表层有许多木屑粘成的小块的）凹凸墙纸
2 N-VAR 木屑 Woodchips are very small pieces of wood, usually made from waste wood, which are used in processes such as making paper. 木屑
