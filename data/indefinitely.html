indefinitely
ɪndefɪnɪtli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 无限期地 If a situation will continue indefinitely, it will continue for ever or until someone decides to change it or end it. 无限期地

  • The visit has now been postponed indefinitely.

    访问如今被无限期推迟。

  • I couldn't stay there indefinitely.

    我不能无限期地呆在那里。

SYN endlessly, continually, for ever, ad infinitum
