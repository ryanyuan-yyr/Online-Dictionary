medical examiner
Word forms: plural medical examiners
1 N-COUNT 法医;验尸官 A medical examiner is a medical expert who is responsible for investigating the deaths of people who have died in a sudden, violent, or unusual way. 法医;验尸官 [US]
2 N-COUNT (申请健康保险或求职时负责体检的)特约体检医生 A medical examiner is a doctor whose job is to examine people, for example when they apply for a job or for health insurance. (申请健康保险或求职时负责体检的)特约体检医生 [US]
