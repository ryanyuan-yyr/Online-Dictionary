apocalyptic
əpɒkəlɪptɪk əˈpɑkəˈlɪptɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 毁灭性的；世界末日的 Apocalyptic means relating to the total destruction of something, especially of the world. 毁灭性的；世界末日的

  • ...the reformer's apocalyptic warnings that the nation was running out of natural resources.

    改革者发出的国家自然资源即将枯竭的末世警诫

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 预示世界末日的；预示世界覆灭的；预示大灾难的 Apocalyptic means relating to or involving predictions about future disasters and the destruction of the world. 预示世界末日的；预示世界覆灭的；预示大灾难的

  • ...a gloomy and apocalyptic vision of a world hastening towards ruin.

    未来世界加速迈向毁灭的暗淡而恐怖的景象
