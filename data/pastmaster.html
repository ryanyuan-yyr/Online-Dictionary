past master
Word forms: plural past masters
N-COUNT 能手；老手；行家 If you are a past master at something, you are very skilful at it because you have had a lot of experience doing it. 能手；老手；行家 [Also + in]

  • He was a past-master at manipulating the media for his own ends. [+ at]

    他是操纵媒体为自己服务的老手。

  • She is an adept rock-climber and a past master of the assault course. [+ of]

    她擅长攀岩，是野战训练场上的老手。

SYN expert, ace [informal] , wizard, artist
