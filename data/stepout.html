step out
1 PHRASAL VERB 离开;退出 If someone steps out of a role or situation, they leave it. 离开;退出 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • I don't regret stepping out of the security of marriage. [VERB PREP. + of]

    我并不后悔放弃了婚姻所带来的安全感。

2 PHRASAL VERB 公开露面 If someone steps out, they appear in public, especially wearing particular clothes or with a particular boyfriend or girlfriend. 公开露面 [informal]

  • Raise a few eyebrows by stepping out in these tiny shorts this summer. [VERB PREP.]

    今年夏天穿上这条超短裤出门，让别人惊讶去吧。

  • She is said to be stepping out with actor Matt Dillon. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    据说她和演员马特·狄龙一起公开露面了。
