novelty
nɒvəlti ˈnɑvəlti
Word forms: plural novelties
1 N-UNCOUNT 新奇性;新颖 Novelty is the quality of being different, new, and unusual. 新奇性;新颖

  • In the contemporary western world, rapidly changing styles cater to a desire for novelty and individualism.

    在当代西方社会，快速变化的时尚迎合了人们追求新奇和个性的需求。

2 N-COUNT 新奇的事物 A novelty is something that is new and therefore interesting. 新奇的事物

  • Seeing people queuing for food was a novelty.

    看到人们排队买食品是件新鲜事。

  • It came from the days when a motor car was a novelty.

    这要从轿车还是新鲜玩意儿那会儿说起。

SYN curiosity, marvel, rarity, oddity  
3 N-COUNT 廉价小饰物;小玩意 Novelties are cheap toys, ornaments, or other objects that are sold as presents or souvenirs. 廉价小饰物;小玩意

  • At Easter, we give them plastic eggs filled with small toys, novelties and coins.

    复活节时，我们给他们分发塑料彩蛋，里面装着小玩具、新颖的小玩意儿和硬币。

SYN trinket, souvenir, memento, bauble
