bellied
ˈbɛlid
1 COMB in ADJ （和形容词连用）有…的肚子的，有…的腹部的 -bellied can be added to an adjective to describe someone or something that has a stomach of a particular kind. （和形容词连用）有…的肚子的，有…的腹部的

  • The fat-bellied officer stood near the door.

    大腹便便的官员站在门边。

  • ...the yellow-bellied sea-snake.

    黄腹海蛇

2See also:pot-bellied
