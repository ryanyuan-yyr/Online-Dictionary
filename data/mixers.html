mixer
mɪksəʳ ˈmɪksər
Word forms: plural mixers
1 N-COUNT 搅拌机;混合器 A mixer is a machine used for mixing things together. 搅拌机;混合器

  • ...an electric mixer.

    电动搅拌器

2See also:cement mixer  food mixer 
3 N-COUNT 调酒用的饮料 A mixer is a non-alcoholic drink such as fruit juice that you mix with strong alcohol such as gin. 调酒用的饮料
4 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] (善于)交际的人 If you say that someone is a good mixer, you mean that they are good at talking to people and making friends. (善于)交际的人

  • Cooper was a good mixer, he was popular.

    库珀擅长交际，人缘很好。

5 N-COUNT 音频（或图像）混合器 A mixer is a piece of equipment that is used to make changes to recorded music or film. 音频（或图像）混合器

  • ...a three channel audio mixer.

    有3个频道的音频混合器
