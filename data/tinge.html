tinge
tɪndʒ tɪndʒ
Word forms: plural tinges
N-COUNT 微量，少许，一丝，几分(颜色、感情或性质) A tinge of a colour, feeling, or quality is a small amount of it. 微量，少许，一丝，几分(颜色、感情或性质)

  • His skin had an unhealthy greyish tinge.

    他的皮肤有种不健康的淡灰色。

  • Could there have been a slight tinge of envy in Eva's voice? [+ of]

    伊娃的声音里可能会有一丝羡慕吗？

SYN tint, colour, shade, cast
