-induced -ɪndjuːst -duːs-
COMB in ADJ (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“由…引起的” -induced combines with nouns to form adjectives which indicate that a state, condition, or illness is caused by a particular thing. (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“由…引起的”

  • ...stress-induced disorders.

    由压力引起的失调

  • ...a drug-induced hallucination.

    吸食毒品产生的幻觉
