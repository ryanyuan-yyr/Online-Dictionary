smudge
smʌdʒ smʌdʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense smudges, present participle smudging, past tense , past participle smudged
1 N-COUNT 污迹;污点 A smudge is a dirty mark. 污迹;污点

  • There was a dark smudge on his forehead.

    他的额头上有一个黑乎乎的污点。

  • ...smudges of blood.

    斑斑血污

SYN smear, blot, smut, smutch  
2 VERB 使模糊不清;弄花 If you smudge a substance such as ink, paint, or make-up that has been put on a surface, you make it less neat by touching or rubbing it. 使模糊不清;弄花 [Also VERB]

  • Smudge the outline using a cotton-wool bud. [VERB noun]

    用一根棉签把轮廓抹花。

  • Her lipstick was smudged. [VERB-ed]

    她抹的口红花了。

SYN smear, blur, blot  
3 VERB 弄脏;涂污 If you smudge a surface, you make it dirty by touching it and leaving a substance on it. 弄脏;涂污

  • She kissed me, careful not to smudge me with her fresh lipstick. [VERB noun]

    她小心翼翼地吻了我，以免把刚刚抹上的口红弄到我脸上。

  • She stood there in the old coat and woollen cap, her face smudged with dirt. [VERB-ed]

    她站在那里，身穿旧外套，头戴羊皮帽，脸上脏兮兮的。

SYN mark, soil, dirty, daub
