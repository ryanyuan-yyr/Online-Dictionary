uncool
ʌnkul ʌnˈkuːl ʌnˈkul
ADJ [oft v-link ADJ] 不酷的If you say that a person, thing, or activity is uncool, you disapprove of them because they are not fashionable, sophisticated, or attractive. 不酷的 [informal, disapproval]

  • People are very laid-back about spotting celebrities here - nobody wants to look uncool by pointing and shouting.

    在这里见到明星人们都表现得很放松-没有人指指点点或大声喊叫，这样看起来不酷。
