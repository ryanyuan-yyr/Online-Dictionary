double-glaze ˌdʌbəlˈɡleɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense double-glazes, present participle double-glazing, past tense , past participle double-glazed
VERB 给…装双层玻璃窗 If someone double-glazes a house or its windows, they fit windows that have two layers of glass which keeps the inside of the house warmer and quieter. 给…装双层玻璃窗 [mainly British]
