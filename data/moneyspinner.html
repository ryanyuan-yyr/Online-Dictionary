money-spinner
(also moneyspinner)Word forms: plural money-spinners
N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 赚大钱的事;赚大钱的生意 If you say that something is a money-spinner, you mean that it earns a lot of money for someone. 赚大钱的事;赚大钱的生意 [informal]

  • The films have been fantastic money-spinners.

    这些电影已成为大大的摇钱树。
