ethnocentric
eθnoʊsentrɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 种族（或民族）中心主义的;种族（或民族）优越感的 If you describe something as ethnocentric, you disagree with it because it is based on the belief that one particular race or nationality of people is superior to all others. 种族（或民族）中心主义的;种族（或民族）优越感的 [disapproval]

  • Her work is open to the criticism that it is ethnocentric.

    她的作品可能会被指责带有种族中心主义色彩。
