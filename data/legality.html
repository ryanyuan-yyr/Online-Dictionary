legality
ligælɪti lɪˈgælɪti
N-UNCOUNT 合法（性） If you talk about the legality of an action or situation, you are talking about whether it is legal or not. 合法（性）

  • The auditor has questioned the legality of the contracts. [+ of]

    审计员质疑这些合同是否合法。

SYN lawfulness, validity, legitimacy, accordance with the law
