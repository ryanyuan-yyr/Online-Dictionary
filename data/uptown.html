uptown
ʌptaʊn ˈʌpˈtaʊn ˈʌpˌtaʊn
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 往(或在)城外;往(或在)城镇的非商业区 If you go uptown, or go to a place uptown, you go away from the centre of a town or city towards the edge. Uptown sometimes refers to a part of the city other than the main business district. 往(或在)城外;往(或在)城镇的非商业区 [mainly US]

  • He rode uptown and made his way to Bob's apartment.

    他乘车前往城外鲍勃的公寓。

  • Susan continued to live uptown.

    苏珊继续住在市郊。

  • There's a skating rink uptown.

    市郊有一个溜冰场。

Uptown is also an adjective.

  • ...uptown clubs.

    非商业区的俱乐部

  • ...a small uptown radio station.

    市郊的一座小电台

  • ...uptown New York.

    纽约非闹市区
