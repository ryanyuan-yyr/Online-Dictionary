arterial
ɑːʳtɪəriəl ɑrˈtɪriəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 动脉的 Arterial means involving or relating to your arteries and the movement of blood through your body. 动脉的

  • ...people with arterial disease.

    动脉疾病患者

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （道路或铁路）干线的，主干的 An arterial road or railway is a main road or railway within a complex road or railway system. （道路或铁路）干线的，主干的

  • ...one of the main arterial roads through the city.

    该城市的主干道之一
