daredevil
deəʳdevəl ˈdɛəˌdɛvəl dɛrˌdɛvəl dærˌdɛvəl
Word forms: plural daredevils
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (人)蛮勇的，大胆鲁莽的 Daredevil people enjoy doing physically dangerous things. (人)蛮勇的，大胆鲁莽的

  • A daredevil parachutist jumped from the top of Tower Bridge today.

    一位大胆鲁莽的跳伞者今天从伦敦塔桥的桥顶跳了下去。

Daredevil is also a noun.

  • He was a daredevil when young.

    他年轻时一身蛮勇。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (行动等)危险的，冒险的 You use daredevil to describe actions that are physically dangerous and require courage. (行动等)危险的，冒险的

  • The show's full of daredevil feats.

    表演中有很多惊险的特技。
