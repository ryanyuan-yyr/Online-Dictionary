bow down
1 PHRASAL VERB 向…致敬；向…示弱 If you refuse to bow down to another person, you refuse to show them respect or to behave in a way which you think would make you seem weaker or less important than them. 向…致敬；向…示弱

  • We should not have to bow down to anyone. [VERB PREP. + to]

    我们不必向任何人示弱。
