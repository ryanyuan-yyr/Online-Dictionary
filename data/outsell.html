outsell
aʊtsel ˌaʊtˈsɛl ˌaʊtˈsɛl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outsells, present participle outselling, past tense , past participle outsold
VERB 比…卖得快；销量胜过 If one product outsells another product, the first product is sold more quickly or in larger quantities than the second. 比…卖得快；销量胜过 [business]

  • The team's products easily outsell those of other American baseball clubs overseas. [VERB noun]

    该棒球队产品的海外销量要远超过美国其他棒球俱乐部。

  • Armani consistently outsells all other European designers. [VERB noun]

    阿玛尼设计的时装一直都比欧洲其他所有时装设计师的作品更畅销。
