pitta
pɪtə pitə
Word forms: plural pittas
The spelling pita is used in American English, pronounced /'piːtə/. 美国英语中拼作pita，读作/'piːtə/。
N-VAR （中间可填馅的）空心圆面包 Pitta or pitta bread is a type of bread in the shape of a flat oval. It can be split open and filled with food such as meat and salad. （中间可填馅的）空心圆面包

  • ...pitta bread stuffed with slices of home-cooked ham.

    填有自制火腿片的空心圆面包
