disproportionate
dɪsprəpɔːʳʃənət ˌdɪsprəˈpɔrʃənɪt ˈdɪsprəˈpɔrʃənət
ADJ-GRADED (数量或尺寸)不成比例的，不相称的 Something that is disproportionate is surprising or unreasonable in amount or size, compared with something else. (数量或尺寸)不成比例的，不相称的

  • A disproportionate amount of time was devoted to one topic. [+ to]

    纠缠在同一个话题上的时间太长了。

  • This sentence is totally disproportionate to the alleged offence.

    从所犯罪行来看，这一量刑完全不合理。

SYN excessive, too much, unreasonable, uneven  
disproportionately

  • ...a disproportionately high suicide rate among young prisoners.

    年轻囚犯特别高的自杀率
