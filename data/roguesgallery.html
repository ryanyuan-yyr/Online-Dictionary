rogues' gallery
1 N-SING 罪犯照片陈列库A rogues' gallery is a collection of photographs of criminals that is kept by the police and used when they want to identify someone. 罪犯照片陈列库 [journalism]

  • ...a rogues' gallery of juvenile crime gangs. [+ of]

    ...一个少年犯罪团伙的照片陈列库。

2 N-SING 不良分子; 危险物品You can refer to a group of people or things that you consider undesirable as a rogues' gallery. 不良分子; 危险物品 [journalism, disapproval]

  • He and others in the rogues' gallery of international terrorists may be running out of time. [+ of]

    他和另外那些已经备案的国际恐怖分子可能没多少时间了。
