unfailing
ʌnfeɪlɪŋ ʌnˈfeɪlɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 一贯的；永恒的 If you describe someone's good qualities or behaviour as unfailing, you mean that they never change. 一贯的；永恒的

  • He had the unfailing care and support of Erica, his wife.

    他的妻子埃丽卡始终如一地关心和支持着他。

  • ...a man of unfailing courtesy and kindness.

    始终彬彬有礼、心地善良的人

  • He continued to appear in the office with unfailing regularity thereafter.

    从那以后他照常出现在办公室，风雨无阻。

SYN reliable, constant, dependable, sure  
unfailingly

  • He was unfailingly polite to customers.

    他对顾客总是以礼相待。

  • Foreigners unfailingly fall in love with the place.

    外国人都爱上了这个地方。
