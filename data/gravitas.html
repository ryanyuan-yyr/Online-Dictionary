gravitas
grævɪtæs ˈgrævɪˌtɑs
1 N-UNCOUNT 庄严;严肃;睿智 If you say that someone has gravitas, you mean that you respect them because they seem serious and intelligent. 庄严;严肃;睿智 [formal]

  • He is pale, dark, and authoritative, with the gravitas you might expect of a Booker prize winner.

    他脸色苍白，皮肤较黑，颇有威信，浑身透着一种布克奖得主应有的庄重。

SYN seriousness, gravity, solemnity
