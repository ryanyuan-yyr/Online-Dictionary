recognizance
rɪkɒgnɪzəns rɪˈkɒɡnɪzəns rɪˈkɑgnɪzəns rɪˈkɑnɪzəns
N-UNCOUNT 保证书In law, if someone who is due to stand trial is released on their own recognizance, they promise to return to the court at a particular time. 保证书 [US]

  • Emery was released on his own recognizance and will make a court appearance next week.

    埃默里在签署保证书后被释放，他将在下周再度回到法庭。
