ether
iːθəʳ ˈiθər
1 N-UNCOUNT 醚;乙醚 Ether is a colourless liquid that burns easily. It is used in industry and in medicine as an anaesthetic. 醚;乙醚

  • ...a sweetish smell of ether and iodine.

    乙醚和碘酒的香甜气味

2 N-SING [the NOUN] 以太，能媒(尤指声音传播的介质) The air is sometimes referred to as the ether, usually when talking about sounds being communicated or broadcast through it. 以太，能媒(尤指声音传播的介质) [literary]

  • ...vocals floating through the ether.

    空中飘着的歌声
