shod
ʃɒd ˈʃɒdən ʃɑd
1 ADJ [adverb ADJ] 穿…鞋的 You can use shod when you are describing the kind of shoes that a person is wearing. 穿…鞋的 [formal]

  • He has demonstrated a strong preference for being shod in running shoes. [+ in/with]

    他给人的感觉是特别偏爱穿跑步鞋。

  • ...her stoutly shod feet.

    她脚上穿着一双结实的鞋

2 Shod is the past participle of shoe.
