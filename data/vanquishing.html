vanquish
væŋkwɪʃ ˈvæŋkwɪʃ ˈvænkwɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vanquishes, present participle vanquishing, past tense , past participle vanquished
VERB （彻底）征服，击败，战胜 To vanquish someone means to defeat them completely in a battle or a competition. （彻底）征服，击败，战胜 [literary]

  • A happy ending is only possible because the hero has first vanquished the dragons. [VERB noun]

    只有主人公先把那些恶龙制服，才有可能实现大团圆结局。

  • With knowledge and wisdom, evil could be vanquished on this earth. [VERB noun]

    拥有了知识和智慧就能够把恶势力从这个世界上铲除。

SYN defeat, beat, conquer, reduce
