unarmed
ʌnɑːʳmd ʌnˈɑːmd ʌnˈɑrmd
ADJ 未携带武器的；未武装的；徒手的 If a person or vehicle is unarmed, they are not carrying any weapons. 未携带武器的；未武装的；徒手的

  • The soldiers concerned were unarmed at the time.

    涉及其中的士兵当时并未携带武器。

  • Thirteen unarmed civilians died in that attack.

    13名手无寸铁的平民在那场袭击中丧生。

SYN defenceless, helpless, unprotected, without arms  
Unarmed is also an adverb.

  • He says he walks inside the prison without guards, unarmed.

    他说他走进那座监狱时没有警卫跟随，也未携带武器。
