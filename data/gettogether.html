get together
1 PHRASAL VERB 开会；聚会 When people get together, they meet in order to discuss something or to spend time together. 开会；聚会

  • This is the only forum where East and West can get together. [VERB PREP.]

    这是东西方可以聚首的唯一论坛。

SYN meet, unite, join, collect  
2See also:get-together 
3 PHRASAL VERB 组织；安排 If you get something together, you organize it. 组织；安排

  • Paul and I were getting a band together, and we needed a new record deal. [VERB noun PREP.]

    当时我和保罗在组建一个乐队，我们需要签一份新唱片合约。

SYN arrange, set up, organize, put together  
4 PHRASAL VERB 凑集（钱） If you get an amount of money together, you succeed in getting all the money that you need in order to pay for something. 凑集（钱） [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Now you've finally got enough money together to put down a deposit on your dream home. [VERB noun PREP.]

    现在，你们终于凑足了钱，可以去为梦寐以求的房子交订金了。

    在我家里进行的聚会
