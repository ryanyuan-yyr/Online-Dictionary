ill-equipped ˌɪlɪˈkwɪpt ˈɪliˈkwɪpt
ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ to-infinitive] 装备不良的;设备不足的;能力不足的 Someone who is ill-equipped to do something does not have the ability, the qualities, or the equipment necessary to do it. 装备不良的;设备不足的;能力不足的 [Also + for]

  • Universities were ill-equipped to meet the massive intake of students.

    各大学设备不足,难以满足招入的大量学生的要求。

  • They often leave prison ill-equipped for life and work on the outside.

    他们离开监狱时常常是一无所长,没有足够能力应付监狱外面的生活和工作。
