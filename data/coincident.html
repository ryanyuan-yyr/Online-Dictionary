coincident
koʊɪnsɪdənt koʊˈɪnsədənt
1 ADJ 同时发生的 Coincident events happen at the same time. 同时发生的 [formal]

  • ...coincident birth times.

    出生时间相同

  • Coincident with the talks, the bank was permitted to open a New York branch. [+ with]

    会谈的同时，该银行已获准在纽约开设分行。

2 ADJ-GRADED 相符的;一致的 Coincident opinions, ideas, or policies are the same or are very similar to each other. 相符的;一致的 [formal]

  • Their aims are coincident with ours. [+ with]

    他们的目标与我们的一致。

  • Coincident interests with the corporate rich and political directorate are pointed out.

    有人指出这合乎的是公司富豪和政治官员的利益。
