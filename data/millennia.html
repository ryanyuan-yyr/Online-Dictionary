millennium
mɪleniəm mɪˈlɛniəm
Word forms: plural millenniums, plural millennia
1 N-COUNT 一千年;千年期 A millennium is a period of one thousand years, especially one which begins and ends with a year ending in '000', for example the period from the year 1000 to the year 2000. 一千年;千年期 [formal]
2 N-SING 2000年;千禧年 Many people refer to the year 2000 as the Millennium . 2000年;千禧年

  • ...the eve of the Millennium.

    千禧年前夜

  • ...the cost of Millennium celebrations and festivals.

    千禧年庆典与节日活动的费用

Usage Note :

  • Be careful with the spelling of this word.

    注意该词的拼写。
