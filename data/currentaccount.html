current account
Word forms: plural current accounts
1 N-COUNT 活期(存款)账户;往来账户 A current account is a personal bank account which you can take money out of at any time using your cheque book or cash card. 活期(存款)账户;往来账户 [British]

  • His current account was seriously overdrawn.

    他的活期账户严重透支。

in AM, use 美国英语用 checking account
2 N-COUNT [usually singular, oft NOUN n] 经常项目，经常账户(指一国一定时期内进出口贸易的差额) A country's current account is the difference in value between its exports and imports over a particular period of time. 经常项目，经常账户(指一国一定时期内进出口贸易的差额) [business]

  • Portugal will probably have a small current-account surplus for 1992.

    葡萄牙1992年经常项目可能会出现小额顺差。
