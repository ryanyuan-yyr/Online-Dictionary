garrotte
gərɒt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense garrottes, present participle garrotting, past tense , past participle garrotted
1 VERB （用铁丝等）勒死，绞杀 If someone is garrotted, they are killed by having something such as a piece of wire or cord pulled tightly round their neck. （用铁丝等）勒死，绞杀 [Also VERB n]

  • The two guards had been garrotted. [be VERB-ed]

    那两名警卫被人勒死了。

2 N-COUNT （勒杀他人用的）铁丝（或绳索）；绞索 A garrotte is a piece of wire or cord used to garrotte someone. （勒杀他人用的）铁丝（或绳索）；绞索
