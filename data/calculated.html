calculated
kælkjʊleɪtɪd ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪd
1 ADJ 有意的;精心计划的;蓄谋的 If something is calculated to have a particular effect, it is specially done or arranged in order to have that effect. 有意的;精心计划的;蓄谋的

  • Their movements through the region were calculated to terrify landowners into abandoning their holdings.

    他们在该地区的行动是精心谋划的，意在恐吓土地所有者放弃手中的地产。

  • These tracks were calculated to be controversial and attract attention to the album.

    这些曲子意在引起争议,吸引人们关注这张专辑。

  • ...sleek cars calculated to attract female buyers.

    专为吸引女性顾客而设计的造型优美的汽车

2 ADJ-GRADED 很可能的;可能性大的 If you say that something is not calculated to have a particular effect, you mean that it is unlikely to have that effect. 很可能的;可能性大的

  • Such a statement was hardly calculated to deter future immigrants.

    这样的声明不太可能吓住将来的移民。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 精心策划的;蓄谋的 You can describe a clever or dishonest action as calculated when it is very carefully planned or arranged. 精心策划的;蓄谋的

  • Irene's cleaning the floor had been a calculated attempt to cover up her crime.

    艾琳擦洗地板就是为了蓄意掩盖她的罪行。

  • ...a calculated and coherent strategy for winning power.

    蓄谋已久、环环相扣的夺权策略

SYN deliberate, planned, considered, studied  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] (风险)认为值得一试的，权衡考虑过的 If you take a calculated risk, you do something which you think might be successful, although you have fully considered the possible bad consequences of your action. (风险)认为值得一试的，权衡考虑过的

  • The President took a calculated political risk in throwing his full support behind the rebels.

    总统全力支持反叛分子,对所冒的政治风险心中有数。
