draw on
1 PHRASAL VERB 凭借;利用;动用 If you draw on or draw upon something such as your skill or experience, you make use of it in order to do something. 凭借;利用;动用

  • He drew on his experience as a yachtsman to make a documentary programme. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他凭借曾经做过快艇驾驶员的经验制作了一档纪实节目。

2 PHRASAL VERB (时光)渐渐过去 As a period of time draws on, it passes and the end of it gets closer. (时光)渐渐过去

  • As the afternoon drew on we were joined by more of the regulars. [VERB PREP.]

    随着午后时光的渐渐消逝,又有些老主顾加入到我们当中来了。
