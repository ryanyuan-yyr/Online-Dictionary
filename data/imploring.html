imploring
ɪmplɔːrɪŋ ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 恳求的;乞求的;哀求的 An imploring look, cry, or letter shows that you very much want someone to do something and are afraid they may not do it. 恳求的;乞求的;哀求的

  • Frank looked at Jim with imploring eyes.

    弗兰克以恳求的眼神看着吉姆。

imploringly

  • Michael looked at him imploringly, eyes brimming with tears.

    迈克尔哀求地看着他,眼里噙着泪水。
