curmudgeonly
kəʳmʌdʒənli
ADJ-GRADED 恶毒的;脾气坏的 If you describe someone as curmudgeonly, you do not like them because they are mean or bad-tempered. 恶毒的;脾气坏的 [old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • ...her curmudgeonly cynic of a boss.

    她作为一个老板所具有的愤世嫉俗和坏脾气

  • He displayed a curmudgeonly unwillingness to accept the voters' verdict.

    他态度恶劣，显得很不情愿接受选举人的意见。
