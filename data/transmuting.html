transmute
trænzmjuːt trænsˈmjut trænzˈmjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense transmutes, present participle transmuting, past tense , past participle transmuted
V-ERG (使)变形;(使)变化 If something transmutes or is transmuted into a different form, it is changed into that form. (使)变形;(使)变化 [formal] [Also VERB n]

  • She ceased to think, as anger transmuted into passion. [VERB + into]

    当怒火转化为激情，她停止了思考。

  • Scientists transmuted matter into pure energy and exploded the first atomic bomb. [VERB noun + into]

    科学家将物质转化为纯粹的能量，引爆了第一颗原子弹。

SYN transform, change, convert, alter  
transmutation

  • ...the transmutation of food into energy.

    食物到能量的转化
