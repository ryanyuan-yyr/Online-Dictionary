classify
klæsɪfaɪ ˈklæsəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense classifies, present participle classifying, past tense , past participle classified
VERB 把…分类;为…归类 To classify things means to divide them into groups or types so that things with similar characteristics are in the same group. 把…分类;为…归类

  • It is necessary initially to classify the headaches into certain types. [VERB noun]

    首先，必须将头痛分为几个类型。

  • Rocks can be classified according to their mode of origin. [VERB noun]

    可以根据岩石的成因对其进行分类。

  • The coroner immediately classified his death as a suicide. [VERB noun + as]

    验尸官立即断定他的死亡属于自杀。

SYN categorize, sort, file, rank
