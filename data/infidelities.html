infidelity
ɪnfɪdelɪti ˌɪnfəˈdɛləti
Word forms: plural infidelities
N-VAR (夫妇间的)不忠实,不贞行为 Infidelity occurs when a person who is married or in a steady relationship has sex with another person. (夫妇间的)不忠实,不贞行为

  • George ignored his partner's infidelities.

    乔治对伴侣的不忠行为视而不见。

  • I divorced him for infidelity.

    我因他的不忠而与他离婚。

SYN unfaithfulness, cheating [informal] , adultery, betrayal
