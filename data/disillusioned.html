disillusioned
dɪsɪluːʒənd
ADJ-GRADED 失望的;醒悟的;幻想破灭的 If you are disillusioned with something, you are disappointed, because it is not as good as you had expected or thought. 失望的;醒悟的;幻想破灭的

  • I've become very disillusioned with politics. [+ with]

    我对政治完全不再抱幻想了。

  • He had become disillusioned because he could not find a job.

    他因找不到工作而备感失望。

SYN disenchanted, disappointed, enlightened, indifferent
