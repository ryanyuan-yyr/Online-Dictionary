simplistic
sɪmplɪstɪk sɪmˈplɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED (观点、阐述)过分简单化的 A simplistic view or interpretation of something makes it seem much simpler than it really is. (观点、阐述)过分简单化的

  • He has a simplistic view of the treatment of eczema.

    他对湿疹治疗的看法过于简单化。

  • The whole process is flawed because the logic behind the questions is too simplistic.

    整个过程都有缺陷，因为问题背后的逻辑太过简单了。

SYN oversimplified, shallow, facile, naive  
simplistically

  • The impact of religion on voting has been analysed far too simplistically.

    有关宗教对选举的影响的分析太过简单了。
