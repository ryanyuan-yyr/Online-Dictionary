odds-on
ˈɑdzˈɑn
(also odds on)
ADJ 大有可能发生的；大有希望的 If there is an odds-on chance that something will happen, it is very likely that it will happen. 大有可能发生的；大有希望的 [informal]

  • Gerald was no longer the odds-on favourite to win the contest.

    杰拉尔德不再是有望赢得比赛的热门人选。

  • It was odds-on that there was no killer.

    十之八九根本就没有凶手。

SYN likely, expected, anticipated, probable
