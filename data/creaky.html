creaky
kriːki ˈkriki
1 ADJ-GRADED 嘎吱作响的 A creaky object creaks when it moves. 嘎吱作响的

  • She pushed open a creaky door.

    她推开一扇嘎吱作响的门。

SYN squeaky, creaking, squeaking, unoiled  
2 ADJ-GRADED 老朽的;破旧的;不时兴的 If you describe something as creaky, you think it is bad in some way because it is old or old-fashioned. 老朽的;破旧的;不时兴的

  • ...its creaky and corrupt political system.

    它陈旧而腐朽的政治体系

  • ...this creaky old British thriller.

    这本老掉牙的英国惊险小说
