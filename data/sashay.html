sashay
sæʃeɪ sæʃeɪ sæˈʃeɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sashays, present participle sashaying, past tense , past participle sashayed
VERB 优雅而招摇地走;大模大样地走 If someone sashays, they walk in a graceful but rather noticeable way. 优雅而招摇地走;大模大样地走

  • The models sashayed down the catwalk. [VERB prep./adv.]

    模特们优雅自如地走下了T型台。
