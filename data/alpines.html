alpine
ælpaɪn
Word forms: plural alpines
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （尤指位于瑞士的）高山的 Alpine means existing in or relating to mountains, especially the ones in Switzerland. （尤指位于瑞士的）高山的

  • ...grassy, alpine meadows.

    高山上长满青草的草地

  • ...picturesque alpine villages.

    景色如画的高山村落

SYN mountainous, high, towering, soaring  
2 N-COUNT 高山植物 Alpines are small flowering plants that grow high up on mountains and are sometimes grown in gardens. There are many different types of alpines. 高山植物
