baronet
bærənɪt ˈbærənət bɛrənət
Word forms: plural baronets
N-COUNT （称号世袭制）准男爵，从男爵 A baronet is a man who has been made a knight. When a baronet dies, the title is passed on to his son. （称号世袭制）准男爵，从男爵 [British]

  • Born in 1860, the son of a weaver, Barrie was created a baronet in 1913.

    1860年出生于一个织工家庭的巴里于1913年被封为准男爵。

  • ...the fifth baronet, Sir Thomas Grosvenor.

    五世准男爵，托马斯·格罗夫纳爵士
