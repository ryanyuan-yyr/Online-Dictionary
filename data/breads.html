bread
bred brɛd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense breads, present participle breading, past tense , past participle breaded
1 N-MASS 面包 Bread is a very common food made from flour, water, and yeast. 面包

  • ...a loaf of bread.

    一条面包

  • ...bread and butter.

    抹黄油的面包

  • There is more fibre in wholemeal bread than in white bread.

    全麦面包纤维含量比白面包高。

2 to know what side your bread is buttered on→see: butter 
3 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] （通过工作等挣得的）钱财，金钱 If you earn your bread doing a particular job or activity, you earn your money doing it. （通过工作等挣得的）钱财，金钱 [informal]

  • There's not a living soul in Colorado who doesn't depend for his bread on silver.

    科罗拉多的每一个人都是靠着开采银矿养家糊口。

4 VERB [usually passive] 给（鱼或肉）抹面包屑 If food such as fish or meat is breaded, it is covered in tiny pieces of dry bread called breadcrumbs. It can then be fried or grilled. 给（鱼或肉）抹面包屑

  • It is important that food be breaded just minutes before frying. [be VERB-ed]

    把食物抹上面包屑后过几分钟就炸，这一点很重要。

breaded

  • ...breaded fish.

    裹有面包屑的炸鱼
