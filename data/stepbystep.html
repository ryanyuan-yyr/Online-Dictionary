step by step
PHRASE 一步接着一步；循序渐进 If you do something step by step, you do it by progressing gradually from one stage to the next. 一步接着一步；循序渐进

  • I am not rushing things and I'm taking it step by step.

    处理这事我不急于求成而是循序渐进。

  • Follow our simple step-by-step instructions.

    跟随我们循序渐进的指令。

SYN gradually, bit by bit, one step at a time, slowly but surely
