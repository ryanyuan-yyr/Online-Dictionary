aɪdiːəlaɪz aɪˈdiəlˌaɪz

1 VERB 把…理想化;理想化地呈现 If you idealize something or someone, you think of them, or represent them to other people, as being perfect or much better than they really are. 把…理想化;理想化地呈现

  • People idealize the past. [VERB noun]

    人们爱把过去理想化。

  • I'm not trying to glorify or idealise women. [VERB noun]

    我不是要美化女人或把她们理想化。

SYN romanticize, glorify, exalt, worship
