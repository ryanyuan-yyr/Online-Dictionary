ablaze
əbleɪz əˈbleɪz
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 炽燃的；熊熊燃烧的 Something that is ablaze is burning very fiercely. 炽燃的；熊熊燃烧的

  • Shops, houses, and vehicles were set ablaze.

    店铺、房子和车辆全都陷于火海。

SYN on fire, lighted, burning, flaming  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 闪耀的；闪亮的 If a place is ablaze with lights or colours, it is very bright because of them. 闪耀的；闪亮的

  • The chamber was ablaze with light. [+ with]

    会议厅里灯火辉煌。

  • In spring, the valleys are ablaze with colour.

    春天，山谷里春色绚烂。

SYN bright, brilliant, flashing, glowing  
3 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ with n] （目光、神色等）激动的，兴奋的，愤怒的 If someone is ablaze, or if their eyes are ablaze, their expression shows that they are feeling a strong emotion, such as great excitement or anger. （目光、神色等）激动的，兴奋的，愤怒的

  • He was ablaze with enthusiasm.

    他热情洋溢。

  • Her voice is passionate. Her eyes are ablaze.

    她声音激昂，目光如火。

SYN passionate, excited, stimulated, fierce
