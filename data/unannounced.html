unannounced
ʌnənaʊnst ˌʌnəˈnaʊnst
ADJ [usu ADJ after v, ADJ n, oft v-link ADJ] 未事先通知的；未提前说明的；未打招呼的 If someone arrives or does something unannounced, they do it unexpectedly and without anyone having being told about it beforehand. 未事先通知的；未提前说明的；未打招呼的

  • He had just arrived unannounced from South America.

    他刚刚从南美来到这里，事先没有打招呼。

  • My first night in Saigon I paid an unannounced visit to my father's cousins.

    到西贡的第一个晚上，我给父亲的堂兄堂弟们来了个突然袭击。

SYN unexpected, unforeseen, unheralded
