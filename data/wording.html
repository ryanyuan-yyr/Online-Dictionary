wording
wɜːʳdɪŋ ˈwɜrdɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 措辞;用词 The wording of a piece of writing or a speech are the words used in it, especially when these are chosen to have a particular effect. 措辞;用词

  • The two sides failed to agree on the wording of a final report. [+ of]

    双方未能就最终报告的措辞达成一致。

  • The wording is so vague that no one actually knows what it means.

    措辞十分模糊，谁也不知道究竟是什么意思。

SYN phraseology, words, language, phrasing
