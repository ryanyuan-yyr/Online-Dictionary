snowfall
snoʊfɔːl ˈsnoʊˌfɔl
Word forms: plural snowfalls
1 N-UNCOUNT 降雪量 The snowfall in an area or country is the amount of snow that falls there during a particular period. 降雪量

  • The total rain and snowfall amounted to 50mm.

    降雨量和降雪量共达 50 毫米。

2 N-COUNT (一场)降雪 A snowfall is a fall of snow. (一场)降雪
