squirrel away
PHRASAL VERB 储存;贮藏 If you squirrel things away, you hide or store them so that you can use them in the future. 储存;贮藏

  • She says the kid's been squirrelling money away like there's no tomorrow. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她说这孩子一直在拼命存钱。

  • Arlott squirrelled away books, pictures and porcelain plates. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    阿洛特将书本、图片和瓷盘瓷碟都藏起来。

SYN save, reserve, set aside, hoard
