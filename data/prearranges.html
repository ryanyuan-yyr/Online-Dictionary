prearrange
priːəreɪndʒ ˌpriːəˈreɪndʒ ˌpriəˈreɪndʒ
(also pre-arrange)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense prearranges, present participle prearranging, past tense , past participle prearranged
1 VERB 预先安排；提前准备 If you prearrange something, you plan or arrange it before the time when it actually happens. 预先安排；提前准备

  • When you prearrange your funeral, you can pick your own flowers and music. [VERB noun]

    如果预先准备自己的葬礼，就能选择自己喜欢的鲜花和音乐。
