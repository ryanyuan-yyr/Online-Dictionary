inflect
ɪnflekt ɪnˈflɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inflects, present participle inflecting, past tense , past participle inflected
VERB (词形、语言)有屈折变化 If a word inflects, its ending or form changes in order to show its grammatical function. If a language inflects, it has words in it that inflect. (词形、语言)有屈折变化
