eighth
eɪtθ eɪtθ eɪθ
Word forms: plural eighths
1 ORD 第八的 The eighth item in a series is the one that you count as number eight. 第八的

  • ...the eighth prime minister of India.

    印度第八任总理

2 FRACTION 八分之一 An eighth is one of eight equal parts of something. 八分之一

  • The Kuban produces an eighth of Russia's grain, meat and milk.

    库班地区出产俄罗斯1/8的粮食、肉类和牛奶。
