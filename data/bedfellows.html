bedfellow
bedfeloʊ ˈbɛdˌfɛloʊ
Word forms: plural bedfellows
N-COUNT [usually plural] 伙伴；盟友；相关的事物 You refer to two things or people as bedfellows when they have become associated or related in some way. 伙伴；盟友；相关的事物

  • It's possible to be bedfellows with someone on one issue, and at odds with them on another.

    可能在一个问题上与某人情投意合，在另一个问题上又意见相左。

  • Sex and death are strange bedfellows.

    性爱和死亡奇怪地纠结在一起。
