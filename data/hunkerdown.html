hunker down
1 PHRASAL VERB 蹲;蹲下;蹲坐 If you hunker down, you bend your knees so that you are in a low position, balancing on your feet. 蹲;蹲下;蹲坐 [US]

  • Betty hunkered down on the floor. [VERB PREP. + on]

    贝蒂蹲坐在地板上。

  • He ended up hunkering down beside her. [V P + beside]

    他最终在她身边蹲了下来。

2 PHRASAL VERB 保持低调;避免引人注目 If you say that someone hunkers down, you mean that they are trying to avoid doing things that will make them noticed or put them in danger. 保持低调;避免引人注目 [US]

  • Their strategy for the moment is to hunker down and let the fuss die down. [VERB PREP.]

    他们目前的策略是保持低调，让事态慢慢平息。
