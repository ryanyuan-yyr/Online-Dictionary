palm off with
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] （用谎话或借口）欺骗搪塞，打发 If you say that you are palmed off with a lie or an excuse, you are annoyed because you are told something in order to stop you asking any more questions. （用谎话或借口）欺骗搪塞，打发 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • Mark was palmed off with a series of excuses. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    马克被一连串借口打发了。
