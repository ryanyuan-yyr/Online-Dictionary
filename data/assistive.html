assist
əsɪst əˈsɪst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense assists, present participle assisting, past tense , past participle assisted
1 VERB 帮助，协助(完成工作) If you assist someone, you help them to do a job or task by doing part of the work for them. 帮助，协助(完成工作)

  • Julia was assisting him to prepare his speech. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    朱莉娅正在帮他准备讲演稿。

  • The family decided to assist me with my chores. [VERB noun + with]

    全家人决定帮助我做家务。

  • Dr Amid was assisted by a young Asian nurse. [be VERB-ed]

    阿米德医生的助手是一位年轻的亚裔护士。

SYN help, back, support, further  
2 VERB （提供信息、意见或金钱）帮助，援助 If you assist someone, you give them information, advice, or money. （提供信息、意见或金钱）帮助，援助 [Also VERB n to-infinitive]

  • The public is urgently requested to assist police in tracing this man. [VERB noun + in]

    紧急要求公众帮助警方追踪此人。

  • Foreign Office officials assisted with transport and finance problems. [VERB + with]

    外交部官员帮助解决运输和经费问题。

  • The Authority will provide a welfare worker to assist you. [VERB noun]

    当局会派一名义工来帮助你。

3 VERB 有助于;促进 If something assists in doing a task, it makes the task easier to do. 有助于;促进 [Also VERB n to-infinitive]

  • ...a chemical that assists in the manufacture of proteins. [V + in/with]

    有助于生成蛋白质的一种化学物质

  • Here are some good sources of information to assist you in making the best selection. [V n + in/with]

    这里是一些有价值的信息来源，可以帮助你作出最佳选择。

  • Salvage operations have been greatly assisted by the good weather conditions. [be VERB-ed]

    天公作美，这大大促进了救援行动的顺利进行。

SYN facilitate, help, further, serve
