grotto
grɒtoʊ ˈgrɑtoʊ
Word forms: plural grottoes, plural grottos
N-COUNT 洞穴；岩洞；岩穴 A grotto is a small cave with interesting or attractively shaped rocks. 洞穴；岩洞；岩穴

  • Water trickles through an underground grotto.

    水沿着地下岩洞流淌。

SYN cave, tunnel, hollow, cavern
