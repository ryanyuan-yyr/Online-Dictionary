bony
boʊni ˈboʊni
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 骨瘦如柴的；瘦骨嶙峋的 Someone who has a bony face or bony hands, for example, has a very thin face or very thin hands, with very little flesh covering their bones. 骨瘦如柴的；瘦骨嶙峋的

  • ...an old man with a bony face and white hair.

    一位面部瘦削、头发花白的老者

  • He poked a long bony finger in Billy's chest.

    他用细长的手指捅了一下比利的胸口。

SYN thin, lean, skinny, angular  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （人体或动物的器官）骨质的 The bony parts of a person's or animal's body are the parts made of bone. （人体或动物的器官）骨质的

  • ...the bony ridge of the eye socket.

    眼窝的骨架

3 ADJ-GRADED （鱼）多骨的，多刺的 If you describe fish that you are eating as bony, you mean that it has a lot of bones. （鱼）多骨的，多刺的

  • ...a delicious but extremely bony fish.

    味美但刺极多的鱼
