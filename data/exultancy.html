exultant
ɪgzʌltənt ɪɡˈzʌltənt ɛgˈzʌltənt ɪgˈzʌltənt
ADJ-GRADED 欢欣的;欢喜的;洋洋得意的 If you are exultant, you feel very happy and proud about something you have done. 欢欣的;欢喜的;洋洋得意的 [formal]

  • An exultant party leader said: 'He will be an excellent MP.'

    一位满面喜色的政党领袖说：“他会成为一名出色的议员。”

SYN joyful, delighted, flushed, triumphant  
exultantly

  • 'We cannot lose the war!' he shouted exultantly.

    “我们不能输掉这场战争！”他得意地喊道。
