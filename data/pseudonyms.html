pseudonym
sjuːdənɪm suː- ˈsudəˌnɪm
Word forms: plural pseudonyms
N-COUNT 假名；笔名 A pseudonym is a name which someone, usually a writer, uses instead of his or her real name. 假名；笔名

  • Both plays were published under the pseudonym of Philip Dayre. [+ of/for]

    两个剧本都是以菲利普·戴尔的笔名出版的。

SYN false name, alias, incognito, stage name
