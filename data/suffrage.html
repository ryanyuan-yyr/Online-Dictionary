suffrage
sʌfrɪdʒ ˈsʌfrɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 选举权;投票权 Suffrage is the right of people to vote for a government or national leader. 选举权;投票权 [formal]

  • He was an advocate of universal suffrage as a basis for social equality.

    他提倡普选权，认为这是社会公平的基础。

  • ...the women's suffrage movement.

    争取妇女选举权的运动

SYN right to vote, vote, franchise, voice
