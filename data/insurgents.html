insurgent
ɪnsɜːʳdʒənt ɪnˈsɜrdʒənt
Word forms: plural insurgents
N-COUNT [usually plural] 起义者;暴动者;叛乱者 Insurgents are people who are fighting against the government or army of their own country. 起义者;暴动者;叛乱者 [formal]

  • By early yesterday, the insurgents had taken control of the country's main military air base.

    到昨天清晨为止,叛乱者已经控制了该国的主要空军基地。

SYN rebel, revolutionary, revolter, rioter
