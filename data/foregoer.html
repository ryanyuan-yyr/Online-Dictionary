forego
fɔːʳgoʊ fɔrˈgoʊ
(also forgo)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense foregoes, present participle foregoing, past tense forewent, past participle foregone
VERB 摒弃;摒绝;放弃 If you forego something, you decide to do without it, although you would like it. 摒弃;摒绝;放弃 [formal]

  • Keen skiers are happy to forego a summer holiday to go skiing. [VERB noun]

    滑雪爱好者乐于利用暑期假日去滑雪。
