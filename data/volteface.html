volte-face vɒlt fɑːs ˌvɔltˈfɑs
Word forms: plural volte-faces
N-COUNT [usually singular] （观点或决定的）大转变，彻底转变，180度大转弯 If you say that someone's behaviour is a volte-face, you mean that they have changed their opinion or decision completely, so that it is the opposite of what it was before. （观点或决定的）大转变，彻底转变，180度大转弯 [formal]

  • The day's events were a remarkable volte-face.

    当日发生的事件是一次惊人的大转折。
