tokenism
toʊkənɪzəm ˈtoʊkənˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 装点门面;表面文章 If you refer to an action as tokenism, you disapprove of it because you think it is just done for effect, in order to show a particular intention or to impress a particular type of person. 装点门面;表面文章 [disapproval]

  • Is his promotion evidence of the minorities' advance, or mere tokenism?

    他的晋升是证明了少数族裔的进步，还是仅仅为了装点门面？
