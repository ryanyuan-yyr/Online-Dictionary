superannuation
suːpərænjueɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 退休金;养老金 Superannuation is money which people pay regularly into a special fund so that when they retire from their job they will receive money regularly as a pension. 退休金;养老金 [mainly British, business]

  • The union pressed for a superannuation scheme.

    工会敦促制定一个退休金方案。
