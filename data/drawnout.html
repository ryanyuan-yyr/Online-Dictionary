drawn-out
ˌdrɔːnˈaʊt
ADJ-GRADED 拖得太久的;冗长的 You can describe something as drawn-out when it lasts or takes longer than you would like it to. 拖得太久的;冗长的

  • Pulling out of a recession is a lengthy and drawn-out process.

    从经济衰退中走出来需要漫长的过程。

  • The road to peace will be long and drawn-out.

    通往和平的道路将很漫长。
