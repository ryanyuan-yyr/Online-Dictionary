milk round
Word forms: plural milk rounds
1 N-COUNT 挨家挨户送牛奶的工作 If someone has a milk round, they work as a milkman, going from house to house delivering milk. 挨家挨户送牛奶的工作 [British]

  • Milk rounds are threatened as customers switch to buying from supermarkets.

    随着顾客转而从超市购买牛奶，送牛奶的生意受到了威胁。

2 N-SING （每年一度的）校园招聘，巡回招聘 The milk round is an event that happens once a year when people from large companies visit colleges and universities and interview students who are interested in working for them. （每年一度的）校园招聘，巡回招聘 [British]

  • He obtained his first job through the milk round.

    他在校园招聘会上找到了第一份工作。
