overbook
oʊvəʳbʊk ˈəʊvəˌbuːk ˌoʊvərˈbʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overbooks, present participle overbooking, past tense , past participle overbooked
VERB （航空公司、剧团等）超额预订 If an organization such as an airline or a theatre company overbooks, they sell more tickets than they have places for. （航空公司、剧团等）超额预订 [Also VERB n]

  • Planes are crowded, airlines overbook, and departures are almost never on time. [VERB]

    飞机拥挤不堪，航空公司超额售票，起飞时间几乎从未准点过。
