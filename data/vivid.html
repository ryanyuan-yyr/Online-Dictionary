vivid
vɪvɪd ˈvɪvɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED （记忆、描述等）生动的,逼真的,清晰的 If you describe memories and descriptions as vivid, you mean that they are very clear and detailed. （记忆、描述等）生动的,逼真的,清晰的

  • People of my generation who lived through World War II have vivid memories of confusion and incompetence.

    我们这一代经历过二战的人都真切地记得当时的迷惘和无助。

  • On Wednesday night I had a very vivid dream which really upset me.

    周三晚上，我做了个十分逼真的梦，让我很不安。

SYN clear, detailed, realistic, telling  
vividly

  • I can vividly remember the feeling of panic.

    我能清楚地记起那种恐慌感。

vividness

  • The vividness of the characterisation came as a complete surprise.

    人物刻画的生动程度完全出乎意料。

2 ADJ-GRADED （色彩）明亮的,鲜艳的 Something that is vivid is very bright in colour. （色彩）明亮的,鲜艳的

  • ...a vivid blue sky.

    蔚蓝的天空

SYN bright, brilliant, intense, clear  
vividly

  • ...vividly coloured birds.

    羽毛色泽艳丽的鸟儿
