rumple
rʌmpəl ˈrʌmpəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rumples, present participle rumpling, past tense , past participle rumpled
VERB (因喜爱而)弄乱，抚弄(某人的头发) If you rumple someone's hair, you move your hand backwards and forwards through it as your way of showing affection to them. (因喜爱而)弄乱，抚弄(某人的头发)

  • I leaned forward to rumple his hair, but he jerked out of the way. [VERB noun]

    我探身过去抚弄他的头发，但他马上躲开了。

SYN ruffle, crush, disorder, dishevel
