legislature
ledʒɪslətʃəʳ ˈlɛdʒɪsˌleɪtʃə -leɪ- ˈlɛdʒɪsˌleɪtʃər
Word forms: plural legislatures
N-COUNT 立法机关；议会 The legislature of a particular state or country is the group of people in it who have the power to make and pass laws. 立法机关；议会 [formal]

  • The proposals before the legislature include the creation of two special courts to deal exclusively with violent crimes.

    呈交立法机关的提案内容包括成立两个专门审理暴力犯罪的特别法庭。

SYN parliament, house, congress, diet
