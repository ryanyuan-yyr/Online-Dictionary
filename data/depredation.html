depredation
deprɪdeɪʃən ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən ˌdɛprəˈdeɪʃən
Word forms: plural depredations
N-VAR 掠夺;破坏;蹂躏 The depredations of a person, animal, or force are their harmful actions, which usually involve taking or damaging something. 掠夺;破坏;蹂躏 [formal]

  • Crops can be all too easily decimated by unchecked depredations by deer.

    任由鹿糟蹋会很容易把庄稼都毁了。

  • Much of the region's environmental depredation is a result of poor planning.

    该地区的环境破坏大都是由于规划不善造成的。

SYN destruction, ravaging, devastation, ransacking
