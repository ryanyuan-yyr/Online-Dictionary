hand back
PHRASAL VERB 归还;交还 If you hand back something that you have borrowed or taken from someone, you return it to them. 归还;交还 [Also V P n + to]

  • The management handed back his few possessions. [VERB PREP. noun]

    管理部门把他仅有的那点财物还给了他。

  • He took a saxophone from the Salvation Army but was caught and had to hand it back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他从救世军那里拿了一支萨克斯管，但却被抓住了，只得将其交还。

  • He handed the book back to her. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    他把书还给了她。

  • He unlocked her door and handed her back the key. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    他给她打开门锁，并把钥匙交还给她。

SYN return, restore, send back, give back
