no claims
(also no-claims)
ADJ [ADJ n] (保险折扣或赠金)因上一年无索赔而获得的 A no claims discount or bonus is a reduction in the money that you have to pay for an insurance policy, which you get when you have not made any claims in the previous year. (保险折扣或赠金)因上一年无索赔而获得的

  • Motorists could lose their no-claims discount, even if they are not at fault in an accident.

    即便驾驶员在事故中没有过错，他们也可能失去无索赔折扣。
