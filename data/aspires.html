aspire
əspaɪəʳ əˈspaɪə əˈspaɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense aspires, present participle aspiring, past tense , past participle aspired
1 VERB 向往；渴望；有志于 If you aspire to something such as an important job, you have a strong desire to achieve it. 向往；渴望；有志于

  • ...people who aspire to public office. [VERB + to]

    渴望担任公职的人

  • They aspired to be gentlemen, though they fell far short of the ideal. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他们想要成为君子，虽然离这个理想还差得很远。
