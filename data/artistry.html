artistry
ɑːʳtɪstri ˈɑrtɪstri
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 艺术才能；艺术造诣 Artistry is the creative skill of an artist, writer, actor, or musician. 艺术才能；艺术造诣

  • ...his artistry as a cellist.

    他作为大提琴演奏家的艺术造诣

  • ...portrait sculptors of considerable skill and artistry.

    具有高超技巧和艺术造诣的人像雕塑家

SYN skill, art, style, taste  
2 N-UNCOUNT 技能；技巧 You can use artistry to refer to a high level of skill in a sport or activity. 技能；技巧

  • John McEnroe beat Jimmy Connors with the most complete display of artistry the game had ever seen.

    约翰·麦肯罗将球技发挥得淋漓尽致，从而击败了吉米·康纳斯，这在比赛中前所未见。

  • Tea processing requires both technique and artistry.

    茶叶加工对工艺和手法都有要求。
