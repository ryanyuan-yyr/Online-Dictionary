touch up
PHRASAL VERB 修饰If you touch something up, you improve its appearance by covering up small marks with paint or another substance. 修饰

  • ...editing tools to help people touch up photos.

    …一些帮助人们修饰照片的编辑工具。

  • The painting has yellowed but the gallery has resisted pressure to touch it up.

    那幅画已经发黄了，但该画廊顶住了要对其进行修饰的压力。
