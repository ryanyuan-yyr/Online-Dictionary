transfix
trænsfɪks trænsˈfɪks
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense transfixes, present participle transfixing, past tense , past participle transfixed
VERB 使呆住;使动弹不得 If you are transfixed by something, it captures all of your interest or attention, so that you are unable to think of anything else or unable to act. 使呆住;使动弹不得

  • We were all transfixed by the images of the war. [be VERB-ed]

    战争的种种景象令我们都呆住了。

SYN stun, hold, fascinate, paralyse  
transfixed

  • Her eyes were transfixed with terror.

    她被吓呆了，双眼直瞪着。

  • For hours he stood transfixed.

    一连几个小时他呆若木鸡地站着。
