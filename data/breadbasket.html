bread basket
brɛd ˈbɑːskət
(also breadbasket)Word forms: plural bread baskets
N-COUNT [usually with poss] （一国的）产粮区，粮仓 If an area or region is described as the bread basket of a country, it provides a lot of the food for that country because crops grow very easily there. It therefore produces wealth for the country. （一国的）产粮区，粮仓

  • The north-west became the country's bread-basket.

    西北地区成为该国的大粮仓。
