concert
kɒnsəʳt kənˈsɜrt ˈkɑnsərt
Word forms: plural concerts
1 N-COUNT 音乐会 A concert is a performance of music. 音乐会

  • ...a short concert of piano music. [+ of]

    一场短暂的钢琴音乐会

  • I've been to plenty of live rock concerts.

    我去现场看过很多摇滚音乐会。

  • ...a new concert hall.

    新的音乐厅

SYN show  
2 PHRASE 现场演出 If a musician or group of musicians appears in concert, they are giving a live performance. 现场演出

  • I want people to remember Elvis in concert.

    我希望大家记住现场演唱会上的猫王。

3 PHRASE 合作;同心协力 If a number of people do something in concert, they do it together. 合作;同心协力

  • He wants to act in concert with other nations.

    他想和其他国家采取一致行动。
