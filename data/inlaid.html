inlaid
ɪnleɪd ˈɪnˌleɪd ɪnˈleɪd ˈɪnˌleɪd ɪnˈleɪd
ADJ 镶嵌的;嵌饰的;嵌花样的 An object that is inlaid has a design on it which is made by putting materials such as wood, gold, or silver into the surface of the object. 镶嵌的;嵌饰的;嵌花样的

  • ...a box delicately inlaid with little triangles. [+ with]

    精巧地嵌有小三角的盒子

SYN inset, set, lined, panelled
