potato
pəteɪtoʊ pəˈteɪtoʊ pəˈteɪtə
Word forms: plural potatoes
1 N-VAR 马铃薯；土豆 Potatoes are quite round vegetables with brown or red skins and white insides. They grow under the ground. 马铃薯；土豆
2See also:sweet potato 
3 PHRASE 棘手的问题；烫手山芋；意见不一的复杂问题 You can refer to a difficult subject that people disagree on as a hot potato . 棘手的问题；烫手山芋；意见不一的复杂问题

  • ...a political hot potato such as abortion.

    类似堕胎这样政治上的烫手山芋
