defiance
dɪfaɪəns diˈfaɪəns dɪfaɪəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 违抗;蔑视;挑战 Defiance is behaviour or an attitude which shows that you are not willing to obey someone. 违抗;蔑视;挑战

  • ...his courageous defiance of the government. [+ of]

    他对政府的勇敢挑战

SYN resistance, challenge, opposition, confrontation  
2 PHRASE 违抗（某人）；无视，不顾(规定、法律等) If you do something in defiance of a person, rule, or law, you do it even though you know that you are not allowed to do it. 违抗（某人）；无视，不顾(规定、法律等)

  • Thousands of people have taken to the streets in defiance of the curfew.

    成千上万的人不顾宵禁涌上街头。
