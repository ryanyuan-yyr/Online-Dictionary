undying
ʌndaɪɪŋ ʌnˈdaɪɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 永恒的；不朽的 If you refer to someone's undying feelings, you mean that the feelings are very strong and are unlikely to change. 永恒的；不朽的 [literary]

  • Dianne declared her undying love for Sam.

    黛安娜表示她对萨姆的爱永远不会变。

  • He had won her undying gratitude.

    他让她感念一生。

  • You need an undying belief in your own ability.

    你要坚定不移地相信自己的能力。

SYN eternal, everlasting, perpetual, continuing
