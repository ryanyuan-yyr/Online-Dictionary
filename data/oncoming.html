oncoming
ɒnkʌmɪŋ ˈɑnˌkʌmɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 迎面而来的；即将到来的 Oncoming means moving towards you. 迎面而来的；即将到来的

  • She was thrown from his car after it skidded into the path of an oncoming car.

    他的车滑行到正有车迎面驶来的逆向车道后，她被抛出了汽车。

  • ...the oncoming cold of winter.

    即将到来的寒冬

SYN approaching, advancing, looming, onrushing
