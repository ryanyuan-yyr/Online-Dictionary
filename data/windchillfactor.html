wind chill factor
(also wind-chill factor,windchill factor)
N-SING 风寒系数A wind chill factor is a measure of the cooling effect of the wind on the temperature of the air. 风寒系数

  • Gusty winds combined with a minus 40 temperature created a wind-chill factor of 80 degrees below zero.

    烈风加上零下40度的气温使风寒系数达到了零下80度。
