artist
ɑːʳtɪst ˈɑrtɪst
Word forms: plural artists
1 N-COUNT 画家;雕塑家;艺术家 An artist is someone who draws or paints pictures or creates sculptures as a job or a hobby. 画家;雕塑家;艺术家

  • ...the studio of a great artist.

    一个伟大的艺术家的工作室

  • Each poster is signed by the artist.

    每幅海报都有这个艺术家的签名。

  • I'm not a good artist.

    我不是优秀的艺术家。

SYN creator, master, maker, craftsman  
2 N-COUNT 艺术家（如小说家、诗人、导演等） An artist is a person who creates novels, poems, films, or other things which can be considered as works of art. 艺术家（如小说家、诗人、导演等）

  • His books are enormously easy to read, yet he is a serious artist.

    虽然他是一个不折不扣的艺术家，但是他的作品极其通俗易懂。

  • Engel is quoted as saying that balanced people do not become artists.

    引用恩格尔的话说就是理性的人是成不了艺术家的。

3 N-COUNT 表演艺术家;艺人 An artist is a performer such as a musician, actor, or dancer. 表演艺术家;艺人

  • ...a popular artist who has sold millions of records.

    唱片销量达到数百万张的当红音乐家

4 N-COUNT 能手;高手;大师 If you say that someone is an artist at a particular activity, you mean they are very skilled at it. 能手;高手;大师

  • He is an exceptional footballer — an artist.

    他是一名出色的足球运动员，一个足球高手。
