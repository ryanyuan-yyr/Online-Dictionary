over the top
PHRASE 过火的， 不得当的 You describe something as over the top when you think that it is exaggerated, and therefore unacceptable. 过火的， 不得当的 [mainly British, informal]

  • The special effects are a bit over the top but I enjoyed it.

    特技稍微夸张了一点，不过我喜欢这点。

SYN excessive, too much, going too far, inordinate
