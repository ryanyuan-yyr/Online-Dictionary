cynic
sɪnɪk ˈsɪnɪk
Word forms: plural cynics
N-COUNT 认为世人皆自私的人;愤世嫉俗者 A cynic is someone who believes that people always act selfishly. 认为世人皆自私的人;愤世嫉俗者

  • I have come to be very much of a cynic in these matters.

    在这些事上我变得相当愤世嫉俗。

SYN sceptic, doubter, pessimist, misanthrope
