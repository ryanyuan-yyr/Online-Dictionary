reconnoitre
rekənɔɪtəʳ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reconnoitres, present participle reconnoitring, past tense , past participle reconnoitred
in AM, use 美国英语用 reconnoiter
VERB 侦察;勘测 To reconnoitre an area means to obtain information about its geographical features or about the size and position of an army there. 侦察;勘测

  • He was sent to Eritrea to reconnoitre the enemy position. [VERB noun]

    他被派往厄立特里亚侦察敌军位置。

  • I left a sergeant in command and rode forward to reconnoitre. [VERB]

    我留下一名中士指挥，自己策马前去侦察敌情。

SYN inspect, investigate, make a reconnaissance (of), see how the land lies
