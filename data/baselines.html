baseline
beɪslaɪn beɪs laɪn
(also base-line)Word forms: plural baselines
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] （网球场、羽毛球场、篮球场等的）底线 The baseline of a tennis, badminton, or basketball court is one of the lines at each end of the court that mark the limits of play. （网球场、羽毛球场、篮球场等的）底线

  • Martinez, when she served, usually stayed on the baseline.

    马丁内兹发球的时候通常站在底线处。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （棒球场的）垒线 In baseball, the baseline is the line that a player must not cross when running between bases. （棒球场的）垒线
3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 基线；基准 A baseline is a value or starting point on a scale with which other values can be compared. 基线；基准

  • You'll need such information to use as a baseline for measuring progress. [+ for]

    你会需要这些信息作为衡量进展的基准。
