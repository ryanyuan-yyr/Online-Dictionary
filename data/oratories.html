oratory
ɒrətəri ɔːrətɔːri ˈɔrəˌtɔri ˈɑrəˌtɔri
Word forms: plural oratories
1 N-UNCOUNT 演讲术；雄辩术 Oratory is the art of making formal speeches which strongly affect people's feelings and beliefs. 演讲术；雄辩术 [formal]

  • He displayed determination as well as powerful oratory.

    他既展示出震撼人心的雄辩术，又显露了矢志不移的决心。

2 N-COUNT [oft in names] （基督徒做礼拜的）祈祷室，小礼拜堂 An oratory is a room or building where Christians go to pray. （基督徒做礼拜的）祈祷室，小礼拜堂

  • The wedding will be at the Brompton Oratory next month.

    婚礼将于下月在布朗普顿礼拜堂举行。
