curly
kɜːʳli ˈkɜrli
Word forms: comparative curlier, superlative curliest
1 ADJ-GRADED (头发)卷曲的，鬈曲的 Curly hair is full of curls. (头发)卷曲的，鬈曲的

  • I've got naturally curly hair.

    我的头发是自来卷儿。

  • Her hair was dark and curly.

    她的头发乌黑卷曲。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 弧形的;螺旋形的 Curly is sometimes used to describe things that are curved or spiral in shape. 弧形的;螺旋形的

  • ...cauliflowers with extra long curly leaves.

    叶子极长而卷曲的花椰菜

  • ...dragons with curly tails.

    卷尾龙

  • ...spectacular curly water slides.

    壮观的螺旋水滑道
