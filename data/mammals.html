mammal
mæməl ˈmæməl
Word forms: plural mammals
N-COUNT 哺乳动物 Mammals are animals such as humans, dogs, lions, and whales. In general, female mammals give birth to babies rather than laying eggs, and feed their young with milk. 哺乳动物
