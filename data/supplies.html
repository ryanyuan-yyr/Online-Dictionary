supply
səplaɪ ˈsʌplɪ ˈsʌpəlɪ ˈsʌpli səˈplaɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense supplies, present participle supplying, past tense , past participle supplied
1 VERB 供给;供应;提供 If you supply someone with something that they want or need, you give them a quantity of it. 供给;供应;提供

  • ...an agreement not to produce or supply chemical weapons. [VERB noun]

    不生产或供应化学武器的协议

  • ...a pipeline which will supply the major Greek cities with Russian natural gas. [VERB noun + with]

    从俄罗斯向希腊主要城市输送天然气的管道

  • ...the blood vessels supplying oxygen to the brain. [VERB noun + to]

    向脑部供氧的血管

SYN furnish, provide, equip, serve  
2 N-PLURAL [oft n NOUN] (尤指大量的)补给，补给品，日用物资 You can use supplies to refer to food, equipment, and other essential things that people need, especially when these are provided in large quantities. (尤指大量的)补给，补给品，日用物资

  • What happens when food and gasoline supplies run low?.

    如果食物和汽油供应减少，会出现什么情况呢？

  • The country's only supplies are those it can import by lorry from Vietnam.

    这个国家仅有的补给品是用卡车从越南进口来的。

3 N-VAR [n NOUN] 供应量;供给量;储备 A supply of something is an amount of it which someone has or which is available for them to use. 供应量;供给量;储备

  • The brain requires a constant supply of oxygen. [+ of]

    大脑需要持续供氧。

  • Most urban water supplies in the United States now contain fluoride in varying amounts.

    现在美国大部分城市供水中都存在含量不一的氟化物。

SYN store, fund, stock, source  
4 N-UNCOUNT (商品、服务的)供应量，供给量 Supply is the quantity of goods and services that can be made available for people to buy. (商品、服务的)供应量，供给量 [business]

  • Prices change according to supply and demand.

    价格受供求关系的影响。

SYN supplying, provision, distribution, sending out  
5 VERB 补充;填充 If you supply a missing word or piece of information, for example in a puzzle, you say or write it because you know it. 补充;填充

  • Supply the missing word(s) and you could win a T-shirt. [VERB noun]

    填出所缺少的单词，你就会赢得一件T恤。

6 PHRASE 供应不足 If something is in short supply, there is very little of it available and it is difficult to find or obtain. 供应不足

  • Food is in short supply all over the country.

    全国普遍食品供应不足。

  • Nowadays that sort of innocence is in short supply.

    现在那种天真烂漫很少见。
