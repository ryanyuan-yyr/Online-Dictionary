transference
trænsfərəns ˈtrænsfərəns trænsˈfɜrəns
N-UNCOUNT 转移;迁移;转存 The transference of something such as power, information, or affection from one person or place to another is the action of transferring it. 转移;迁移;转存

  • It is a struggle for a transference of power. [+ of]

    那是为权力移交进行的斗争。

  • His transference of devotion from Nicola to Susan was complete.

    他对妮古拉的挚爱完全转移到了苏珊身上。
