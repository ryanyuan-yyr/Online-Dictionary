perennial
pəreniəl pəˈrɛniəl
Word forms: plural perennials
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （尤指问题或困难）永恒的，持续的，亘古不变的 You use perennial to describe situations or states that keep occurring or which seem to exist all the time; used especially to describe problems or difficulties. （尤指问题或困难）永恒的，持续的，亘古不变的

  • ...the perennial urban problems of drugs and homelessness.

    毒品和无家可归这两个一直困扰城市的问题

  • There's a perennial shortage of teachers with science qualifications.

    有理科教学资格的老师一直都很短缺。

SYN continual, lasting, continuing, permanent  
perennially

  • Both services are perennially short of staff.

    两个机构一直都是人手不够。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （植物）多年生的 A perennial plant lives for several years and has flowers each year. （植物）多年生的

  • ...a perennial herb with greenish-yellow flowers.

    开绿黄色花的多年生香草

Perennial is also a noun.

  • ...a low-growing perennial.

    多年生矮小植物
