nocturnal
nɒktɜːʳnəl nɑkˈtɜrnəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 夜间发生的;夜间的 Nocturnal means occurring at night. 夜间发生的;夜间的

  • ...long nocturnal walks.

    远途夜路

  • ...the immensity of the nocturnal sky.

    夜空的浩瀚

2 ADJ (动物)夜间活动的，夜行性的 Nocturnal creatures are active mainly at night. (动物)夜间活动的，夜行性的

  • When there is a full moon, this nocturnal rodent is careful to stay in its burrow.

    月圆之夜，这种夜间活动的啮齿类动物会小心地呆在地洞里不出来。
