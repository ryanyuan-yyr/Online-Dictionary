leisured
leʒəʳd liːʒ-
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （通常指人因为富有而）有闲的，清闲自在的 Leisured people are people who do not work, usually because they are rich. （通常指人因为富有而）有闲的，清闲自在的

  • ...the leisured classes.

    有闲阶级

2 ADJ-GRADED （活动）从容不迫的，慢悠悠的 Leisured activities are done in a relaxed way or do not involve work. （活动）从容不迫的，慢悠悠的

  • ...this leisured life of reading and writing.

    读书和写作的逍遥日子
