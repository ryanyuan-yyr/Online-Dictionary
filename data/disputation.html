disputation
dɪspjʊteɪʃən ˌdɪspjʊˈteɪʃən ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural disputations
N-VAR 争论;辩论 Disputation is discussion on a subject which people cannot agree about. 争论;辩论 [formal]

  • After much legal disputation our right to resign was established.

    经过大量法庭辩论后，我们辞职的权利得到了认可。

SYN dispute, debate, controversy, polemics
