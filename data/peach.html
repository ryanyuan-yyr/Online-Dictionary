peach
piːtʃ pitʃ
Word forms: plural peaches
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 桃子 A peach is a soft, round, slightly furry fruit with sweet yellow flesh and pinky-orange skin. Peaches grow in warm countries. 桃子
2 COLOUR 桃红色的；粉红色 Something that is peach is pale pinky-orange in colour. 桃红色的；粉红色

  • ...the romantic Tower Suite, decorated throughout in peach and ivory.

    完全用桃红色和象牙白装饰的浪漫塔楼套房

  • ...a peach silk blouse.

    桃红色的丝绸上衣

3 N-SING [oft a N of n] 极可爱的人（或事物）；非常迷人的人（或事物） If you describe someone or something as a peach, you find them very pleasing or attractive. 极可爱的人（或事物）；非常迷人的人（或事物） [informal, approval]

  • Frank was there and he is a perfect peach.

    弗兰克在那儿，他是个非常可爱的人。

  • ...a peach of a goal from Beardsley.

    比尔兹利的绝妙进球
