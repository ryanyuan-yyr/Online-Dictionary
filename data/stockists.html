stockist
stɒkɪst
Word forms: plural stockists
N-COUNT 零售商;经销商 A stockist of a particular product is someone who sells this product in their shop. 零售商;经销商 [British]

  • The name of your nearest stockist is available from the company.

    从公司可以查到离你最近的零售商的名字。

SYN store, shop, supplier, retail outlet
