unlock
ʌnlɒk ʌnˈlɒk ʌnˈlɑk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unlocks, present participle unlocking, past tense , past participle unlocked
1 VERB （用钥匙）打开，开启(门、房间、箱匣等的锁) If you unlock something such as a door, a room, or a container that has a lock, you open it using a key. （用钥匙）打开，开启(门、房间、箱匣等的锁)

  • He unlocked the car and threw the coat on to the back seat. [VERB noun]

    他用钥匙打开车门，把外套扔到了后座上。

  • She unlocked the case and carefully lifted out the vase. [VERB noun]

    她用钥匙打开了箱子，小心翼翼地拿出那只花瓶。

SYN open, undo, unfasten, release  
2 VERB 释放（潜能）；揭开（秘密） If you unlock the potential or the secrets of something or someone, you release them. 释放（潜能）；揭开（秘密）

  • Education and training is the key that will unlock our nation's potential. [VERB noun]

    教育和培训是发掘我们国家潜力的关键。

  • ...the man who dedicated his life to unlocking the secrets of the universe. [VERB noun]

    终生致力于揭开宇宙奥秘的人

SYN discover, uncover, unearth, lay bare
