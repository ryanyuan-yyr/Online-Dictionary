plebiscite
plebɪsaɪt -sɪt ˈplɛbɪˌsaɪt ˈplɛbəˌsaɪt
Word forms: plural plebiscites
N-COUNT 公民投票 A plebiscite is a direct vote by the people of a country or region in which they say whether they agree or disagree with a particular policy, for example whether a region should become an independent state. 公民投票
SYN vote, poll, referendum, ballot
