scrawl
skrɔːl skrɔl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense scrawls, present participle scrawling, past tense , past participle scrawled
1 VERB 潦草地写;马马虎虎地写 If you scrawl something, you write it in a careless and untidy way. 潦草地写;马马虎虎地写 [Also VERB n]

  • He scrawled a hasty note to his wife. [VERB noun]

    他给妻子草草写了个便条。

  • Someone had scrawled 'Scum' on his car. [VERB with quote]

    有人在他的车上涂写了“人渣”的字样。

  • ...racist graffiti scrawled on school walls. [VERB-ed]

    学校墙壁上带有种族主义色彩的涂鸦

SYN scribble, doodle, squiggle  
2 N-VAR 潦草的笔迹;不工整的字迹 You can refer to writing that looks careless and untidy as a scrawl . 潦草的笔迹;不工整的字迹

  • The letter was handwritten, in a hasty, barely decipherable scrawl.

    这封信是仓促手写而成，潦草得几乎无法辨认。

SYN scribble, doodle, squiggle
