appliance
əplaɪəns əˈplaɪəns
Word forms: plural appliances
1 N-COUNT （家用）电器，器械，装置 An appliance is a device or machine in your home that you use to do a job such as cleaning or cooking. Appliances are often electrical. （家用）电器，器械，装置 [formal]

  • He could also learn to use the vacuum cleaner, the washing machine and other household appliances.

    他可以再学学使用吸尘器、洗衣机和其他家用电器。

  • ...Maytag, one of America's biggest domestic appliance manufacturers.

    美泰克公司，美国最大的家电制造商之一

SYN device, machine, tool, instrument  
2 N-SING （技术或知识的）使用，应用 The appliance of a skill or of knowledge is its use for a particular purpose. （技术或知识的）使用，应用

  • These advances were the result of the intellectual appliance of science. [+ of]

    这些进步是巧妙应用科学的结果。

SYN application, use, practice, exercise
