handyman
hændimæn ˈhændɪˌmæn ˈhændiˌmæn
Word forms: plural handymen
N-COUNT 杂活工;家里的动手能手 A handyman is a man who earns money by doing small jobs for people such as making and repairing things in their houses. You can also describe a man who is good at making or repairing things in his home as a handyman . 杂活工;家里的动手能手
SYN odd-jobman, jack-of-all-trades, handy Andy [informal] , DIY expert
