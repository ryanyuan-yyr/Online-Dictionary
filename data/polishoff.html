polish off
1 PHRASAL VERB 吃（或喝）光；吃（或喝）完 If you polish off food or drink, you eat or drink all of it, or finish it. 吃（或喝）光；吃（或喝）完 [informal]

  • No matter what he is offered to eat he polishes it off in an instant. [VERB noun PREP.]

    不管给他吃什么，他都能眨眼就下肚。

  • He polished off his scotch and slammed the glass down. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他一仰脖喝完苏格兰威士忌，砰的一声把杯子放下。

2 PHRASAL VERB （尤指在体育运动中）击败，战胜 If you polish off an opponent, especially in sports, you beat them. （尤指在体育运动中）击败，战胜 [mainly journalism]

  • He polished off his opponents 21-2, 21-8 and 21-8. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他以21比2、21比8和21比8直落三局击败了对手。

  • South Africa resume this morning on 278 for seven and England could well polish them off first thing. [VERB noun PREP.]

    南非队今天上午以278分7人出局继续比赛，英格兰队应该很容易就能击败他们。
