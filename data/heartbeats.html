heartbeat
hɑːʳtbiːt ˈhɑrtˌbit
Word forms: plural heartbeats
1 N-SING [oft poss NOUN] 心搏;心跳 Your heartbeat is the regular movement of your heart as it pumps blood around your body. 心搏;心跳

  • Your baby's heartbeat will be monitored continuously.

    宝宝的心搏将会受到持续监控。

2 N-COUNT （一次）心搏，心跳 A heartbeat is one of the movements of your heart. （一次）心搏，心跳

  • Smoking could lead to irregular heartbeats.

    吸烟会引起心律失常。
