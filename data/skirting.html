skirt
skɜːʳt skɜrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense skirts, present participle skirting, past tense , past participle skirted
1 N-COUNT 裙子;短裙 A skirt is a piece of clothing worn by women and girls. It fastens at the waist and hangs down around the legs. 裙子;短裙
SYN border, edge, margin, fringe  
2 VERB 位于…的边缘;位于…的外围;围绕 Something that skirts an area is situated around the edge of it. 位于…的边缘;位于…的外围;围绕

  • We raced across a large field that skirted the slope of a hill. [VERB noun]

    我们从山坡下的一大片田地里飞奔而过。

SYN border, edge, lie alongside, line  
3 VERB 沿着…的边缘走;沿着…的外围走;绕着…走 If you skirt something, you go around the edge of it. 沿着…的边缘走;沿着…的外围走;绕着…走

  • We shall be skirting the island on our way. [VERB noun]

    我们将一路环岛而行。

  • She skirted round the edge of the room to the door. [V + round/around]

    她贴着房间的墙边走到了门口。

SYN go round, bypass, walk round, circumvent  
4 VERB 避开，回避(困难、问题等) If you skirt a problem or question, you avoid dealing with it. 避开，回避(困难、问题等)

  • He skirted the hardest issues, concentrating on areas of possible agreement. [VERB noun]

    他避开了最棘手的问题，将精力集中于有可能达成一致的领域。

  • He skirted round his main differences with her. [V + round/around]

    他避开了和她之间的主要分歧。
