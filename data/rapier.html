rapier
reɪpieʳ ˈreɪpiər ˈreɪpjər
Word forms: plural rapiers
1 N-COUNT 长剑 A rapier is a very thin sword with a long sharp point. 长剑
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 机敏的 If you say that someone has a rapier wit, you mean that they are very intelligent and quick at making clever comments or jokes in a conversation. 机敏的

  • Julie Burchill is famous for her precocity and rapier wit.

    朱莉·伯奇尔因其早慧和机敏而闻名。
