pact
pækt pækt
Word forms: plural pacts
N-COUNT 协定；契约；盟约；条约 A pact is a formal agreement between two or more people, organizations, or governments to do a particular thing or to help each other. 协定；契约；盟约；条约

  • Last month he signed a new non-aggression pact with Germany. [+ with]

    上个月，他与德国签订了新的互不侵犯条约。

  • The other two opposition parties cannot agree on an electoral pact between themselves.

    另外两个反对党之间无法就选举达成协议。

SYN agreement, contract, alliance, treaty
