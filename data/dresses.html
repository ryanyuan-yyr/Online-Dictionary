dress
dres drɛs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dresses, present participle dressing, past tense , past participle dressed
1 N-COUNT 连衣裙 A dress is a piece of clothing worn by a woman or girl. It covers her body and part of her legs. 连衣裙

  • She was wearing a black dress.

    她穿一条黑色连衣裙。

SYN frock, shift, gown, garment  
2 N-UNCOUNT 衣服 You can refer to clothes worn by men or women as dress . 衣服

  • He's usually smart in his dress.

    他通常穿得很精神。

  • ...hundreds of Cambodians in traditional dress.

    数百名身着传统服装的柬埔寨人

3See also:evening dress  fancy dress  full dress  morning dress 
4 VERB 穿衣 When you dress or dress yourself, you put on clothes. 穿衣

  • He told Sarah to wait while he dressed. [VERB]

    他让萨拉等他穿好衣服。

  • Sue had dressed herself neatly for work. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    休穿戴整洁地上班去了。

SYN put on clothes, don clothes, slip on or into something  
5 VERB 给…穿衣服 If you dress someone, for example a child, you put clothes on them. →see usage note at: wear 给…穿衣服

  • She bathed her and dressed her in clean clothes.

    她给她洗澡并穿上干净的衣服。

6 VERB 穿着;打扮 If someone dresses in a particular way, they wear clothes of a particular style or colour. 穿着;打扮

  • He dresses in a way that lets everyone know he's got authority. [VERB + in]

    他的穿着打扮让大家都知道他已经掌权了。

  • She used to dress in jeans. [VERB in noun]

    她过去常穿牛仔裤。

7 VERB 穿礼服 If you dress for something, you put on special clothes for it. 穿礼服

  • We don't dress for dinner here. [VERB + for]

    我们这儿赴宴不穿礼服。

8 VERB 清洗,包扎,敷裹(伤口) When someone dresses a wound, they clean it and cover it. 清洗,包扎,敷裹(伤口)

  • The poor child never cried or protested when I was dressing her wounds. [VERB noun]

    我给这个可怜的孩子包扎伤口时,她不哭也不闹。

9 VERB 调制,加作料于(色拉) If you dress a salad, you cover it with a mixture of oil, vinegar, and herbs or flavourings. 调制,加作料于(色拉)

  • Scatter the tomato over, then dress the salad. [VERB noun]

    把西红柿散放在上面,然后给色拉加调味汁。

  • ...a bowl of dressed salad. [VERB-ed]

    一碗已调制好的色拉

10 VERB (在烹饪前)清洗,加工处理(鸡、鱼或肉类) To dress meat, chicken, or fish means to prepare it for cooking by cleaning it and removing the parts that you cannot eat. (在烹饪前)清洗,加工处理(鸡、鱼或肉类)

  • Her mother dressed the meat. [VERB noun]

    她母亲把肉洗好了。

  • ...dressed crab. [VERB-ed]

    洗好的螃蟹
