vacancy
veɪkənsi ˈveɪkənsi
Word forms: plural vacancies
1 N-COUNT （工作或职位的）空缺 A vacancy is a job or position which has not been filled. （工作或职位的）空缺

  • They had a short-term vacancy for a person on the foreign desk.

    他们在国际新闻组有一个短期的空缺职位。

  • Most vacancies are at senior level, requiring appropriate qualifications.

    大多数空缺属于高层职位，要求具备相应的资历。

SYN opening, job, post, place  
2 N-COUNT （旅馆里待租的）空房 If there are vacancies at a building such as a hotel, some of the rooms are available to rent. （旅馆里待租的）空房

  • This year hotels that usually are jammed had vacancies all summer.

    今年,那些通常爆满的旅店整个夏天都有空客房。
