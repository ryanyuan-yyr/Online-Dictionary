defender
dɪfendəʳ
Word forms: plural defenders
1 N-COUNT 捍卫者;保护者;维护者 If someone is a defender of a particular thing or person that has been criticized, they argue or act in support of that thing or person. 捍卫者;保护者;维护者

  • ...the most ardent defenders of conventional family values. [+ of]

    传统家庭价值观最热烈的捍卫者

  • ...a strong defender of human rights or religious freedom.

    人权或宗教自由的坚强卫士

SYN supporter, champion, advocate, sponsor  
2 N-COUNT (足球、曲棍球等运动中的)防守队员，防守球员 A defender in a game such as football or hockey is a player whose main task is to try and stop the other side scoring. (足球、曲棍球等运动中的)防守队员，防守球员
