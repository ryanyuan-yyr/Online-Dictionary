security blanket
Word forms: plural security blankets
1 N-COUNT 给人安全感之物;慰藉 If you refer to something as a security blanket, you mean that it provides someone with a feeling of safety and comfort when they are in a situation that worries them or makes them feel nervous. 给人安全感之物;慰藉

  • Alan sings with shy intensity, hiding behind the security blanket of his guitar. [+ of]

    艾伦非常害羞地唱着，用怀里的吉他挡住自己以获得此许安全感。

2 N-COUNT 安乐毯(一种供婴儿抓摸吮咬使其获得安慰的布料或衣服) A baby's security blanket is a piece of cloth or clothing which the baby holds and chews in order to feel comforted. 安乐毯(一种供婴儿抓摸吮咬使其获得安慰的布料或衣服)
