kit out
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 使…装备起来 If someone or something is kitted out, they have everything they need at a particular time, such as clothing, equipment, or furniture. 使…装备起来 [British, informal]

  • She was kitted out with winter coat, skirts, jumpers. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    她全副武装，穿着冬天的外套、裙子、套头衫。

  • The place is kitted out in upmarket Italian cafe style. [be V-ed P + in]

    整个地方被装饰成高档意大利咖啡馆风格。
