anywhere
enihweəʳ ˈɛnɪˌwɛə ˈəniˌhwɛr ˈəniˌwɛr
1 ADV-INDEF [ADVERB after verb] [be ADVERB] （用于否定句）哪儿，到处（都没有） You use anywhere in statements with negative meaning to indicate that a place does not exist. （用于否定句）哪儿，到处（都没有）

  • I haven't got anywhere to live.

    我还没找到住的地方。

  • There had never been such a beautiful woman anywhere in the world.

    世界上从未有过如此美丽的女人。

2 ADV-INDEF [ADVERB after verb] [be ADVERB, from ADVERB] （用于疑问句和条件从句）某个地方，哪里 You use anywhere in questions and conditional clauses to ask or talk about a place without saying exactly where you mean. （用于疑问句和条件从句）某个地方，哪里

  • Did you try to get help from anywhere?.

    你尝试过从哪里寻求帮助吗？

  • If she wanted to go anywhere at all she had to wait for her father to drive her.

    她想去任何地方都只能等父亲开车送她去。

SYN anyplace, NEwer [text messaging]  
3 ADV-INDEF （用于描述所谈论的某个地方的词语前）…地方 You use anywhere before words that indicate the kind of place you are talking about. （用于描述所谈论的某个地方的词语前）…地方

  • In America most leisure-time activities are about a million times better than anywhere else.

    美国大多数休闲娱乐活动比其他地方要丰富得多的多。

  • He'll meet you anywhere you want.

    他随你在哪儿见面都行。

  • Let us know if you come across anywhere that has something special to offer.

    如果你碰上哪儿有什么特别的东西出售，就告诉我们。

4 ADV-INDEF [ADVERB after verb, be ADVERB] 任何地方；到处 You use anywhere to refer to a place when you are emphasizing that it could be any of a large number of places. 任何地方；到处 [emphasis]

  • Rachel would have known Julia Stone anywhere.

    甭管到哪儿，雷切尔都会认出朱莉娅·斯通。

  • ...jokes that are so funny they always work anywhere.

    屡试不爽的极为有趣的笑话

5 ADV-INDEF （指某个范围内）任何一点上 When you do not want to be exact, you use anywhere to refer to a particular range of things. （指某个范围内）任何一点上 [Also + between/to]

  • His shoes cost anywhere from $200 up. [+ from]

    他买的鞋子总在200美元以上。

  • My visits lasted anywhere from three weeks to two months. [+ from]

    我每次逗留时间从3个星期到两个月不等。

6 ADV-INDEF [ADVERB adjective/adverb] （用于强调）根本，一点儿，将近，几乎 You use anywhere in expressions such as anywhere near and anywhere close to to emphasize a statement that you are making. （用于强调）根本，一点儿，将近，几乎 [emphasis]

  • There weren't anywhere near enough empty boxes.

    空箱子数量远远不够。

  • The only one who's anywhere close to the truth is my mother.

    唯一算得上了解真相的人是我的母亲。

7 PHRASE 毫无进展;毫无成就 If you say that someone or something is not getting anywhere or is not going anywhere, you mean that they are not making progress or achieving a satisfactory result. 毫无进展;毫无成就

  • The conversation did not seem to be getting anywhere.

    会谈好像没有取得任何进展。

  • I didn't see that my career as a film-maker was going anywhere.

    我发现我作为电影制片人的职业没有什么发展前途。

Usage Note :

  • Anywhere is mainly used in questions and negative sentences. You use not anywhere instead of somewhere in negative sentences. He isn't going anywhere.

    anywhere主要用于疑问句和否定句。在否定句中，用not anywhere代替somewhere：He isn't going anywhere（他哪儿也不去）。
