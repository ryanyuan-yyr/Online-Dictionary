shoulder strap
Word forms: plural shoulder straps
1 N-COUNT (裙子等衣服上的)肩带The shoulder straps on a piece of clothing such as a dress are two narrow straps that go over the shoulders. (裙子等衣服上的)肩带
2 N-COUNT (背包上的)背带A shoulder strap on a bag is a long strap that you put over your shoulder to carry the bag. (背包上的)背带
