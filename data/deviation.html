deviation
diːvieɪʃən ˌdiviˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural deviations
1 N-VAR 偏常;反常;离经叛道 Deviation means doing something that is different from what people consider to be normal or acceptable. 偏常;反常;离经叛道

  • Deviation from the norm is not tolerated. [+ from]

    不能容忍离经叛道行为。

  • ...sexual deviation.

    性变态

  • To abstain from meat was a serious deviation.

    茹素是比较严重的反常行为。

SYN departure, change, variation, shift  
2 N-VAR (统计学中的)离差，偏差 In statistics, deviation is the difference between the value of one number in a series of numbers and the average value of all the numbers in the series. (统计学中的)离差，偏差

  • A 10 to 15 percent deviation is considered acceptable.

    10%至15% 的离差是可以接受的。
