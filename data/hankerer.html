hanker
hæŋkəʳ ˈhæŋkər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hankers, present participle hankering, past tense , past participle hankered
VERB 渴望;热望 If you hanker after something, you want it very much. 渴望;热望 [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • I hankered after a floor-length brown suede coat. [V + after/for]

    我渴望有一件长及脚跟的棕色绒面革大衣。
