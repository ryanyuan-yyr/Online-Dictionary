Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense sexes, present participle sexing, past tense , past participle sexed
1 N-COUNT 男性;女性;雌性;雄性 The two sexes are the two groups, male and female, into which people and animals are divided according to the function they have in producing young. 男性;女性;雌性;雄性

  • ...an entertainment star who appeals to all ages and both sexes.

    男女老幼都喜欢的娱乐明星

  • ...differences between the sexes.

    两性之间的差异

SYN gender  
2See also:fair sex  opposite sex  same-sex  single-sex 
3 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 性别;性别特征 The sex of a person or animal is their characteristic of being either male or female. 性别;性别特征

  • She continually failed to gain promotion because of her sex.

    她因为是女性而迟迟得不到晋升。

  • The new technique has been used to identify the sex of foetuses. [+ of]

    这一新技术已经被用来鉴别胎儿的性别。

  • ...sex discrimination.

    性别歧视

4 N-UNCOUNT 性行为;性交;性活动 Sex is the physical activity by which people can produce young. 性行为;性交;性活动

  • He was very open in his attitudes about sex.

    他对待性的态度非常开放。

  • The entire film revolves around drugs, sex and violence.

    整个影片围绕着毒品、性和暴力展开。

  • We have a very active sex life.

    我们的性生活非常活跃。

SYN lovemaking, sexual relations, copulation, the other [informal]  
5 VERB [usually passive] 辨别…的性别;辨识…的雌雄 If an animal is sexed, someone finds out whether it is male or female. 辨别…的性别;辨识…的雌雄

  • The birds must be weighed, sexed and tagged. [be VERB-ed]

    必须对这些鸟称重，辨识雌雄并挂上标牌。

6 PHR-RECIP 发生性关系;做爱 If two people have sex, they perform the act of sex. 发生性关系;做爱

  • I had no intention of having sex with him on a first date.

    我并没想第一次约会就和他上床。

SYN have sexual intercourse, sleep with, make love with, lie with [archaic]
