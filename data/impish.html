impish
ɪmpɪʃ ˈɪmpɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 似顽童的;顽皮的;调皮的 If you describe someone or their behaviour as impish, you mean that they are rather disrespectful or naughty in a playful way. 似顽童的;顽皮的;调皮的

  • Gillespie is well known for his impish sense of humour.

    吉莱斯皮以他精灵搞怪的幽默感而广为人知。

SYN mischievous, devilish, roguish, rascally  
impishly

  • He smiled at me impishly.

    他顽皮地冲我微笑。
