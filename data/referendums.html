referendum
refərendəm ˌrɛfəˈrɛndəm
Word forms: plural referendums, plural referenda/refərendə/
N-COUNT 公民投票；全民公决 If a country holds a referendum on a particular policy, they ask the people to vote on the policy and show whether or not they agree with it. 公民投票；全民公决

  • Estonia said it too planned to hold a referendum on independence. [+ on]

    爱沙尼亚称其也计划就独立问题进行全民公决。

SYN public vote, poll, ballot, popular vote
