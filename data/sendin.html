send in
1 PHRASAL VERB 呈递，提交，邮寄(参赛作品、求职信等) If you send in something such as a competition entry or a letter applying for a job, you post it to the organization concerned. 呈递，提交，邮寄(参赛作品、求职信等) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Applicants are asked to send in a CV and a covering letter. [VERB PREP. noun]

    申请人需要提交一份简历和附函。

  • We're hoping that readers will send in their ideas for saving money. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我们希望读者能来信说说他们省钱的点子。

2 PHRASAL VERB 派遣,调来(警察、军队等以应付危机或困难) When a government sends in troops or police officers, it orders them to deal with a crisis or problem somewhere. 派遣,调来(警察、军队等以应付危机或困难) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He has asked the government to send in troops to end the fighting. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他已经要求政府派军队来平息这场战斗。
