guesswork
geswɜːʳk ˈgɛsˌwɜrk
N-UNCOUNT 猜测；推测 Guesswork is the process of trying to guess or estimate something without knowing all the facts or information. 猜测；推测

  • The question of who planted the bomb remains a matter of guesswork.

    到底是谁安放了炸弹依然只是猜测。

SYN speculation, theory, presumption, conjecture
