commiserate
kəmɪzəreɪt kəˈmɪzərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense commiserates, present participle commiserating, past tense , past participle commiserated
1 VERB 同情;怜悯 If you commiserate with someone, you show them pity or sympathy when something unpleasant has happened to them. 同情;怜悯

  • When I lost, he commiserated with me. [VERB + with]

    我落败的时候，他向我表示同情。

SYN sympathize, pity, feel for, console
