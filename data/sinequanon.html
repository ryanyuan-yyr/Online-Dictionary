sine qua non sɪni kwɑː noʊn ˈsaɪnɪ kweɪ ˈnɒn - nɑːn ˈsaɪni kweɪ ˈnɑn ˈsɪneɪ kwɑ ˈnoʊn
N-SING 必要条件;先决条件;必不可少的事物 A sine qua non is something that is essential if you want to achieve a particular thing. 必要条件;先决条件;必不可少的事物 [formal]

  • Successful agricultural reform is also a sine qua non of Mexico's modernisation. [+ of]

    成功的农业改革也是墨西哥实现现代化的必要条件。
