feminism
femɪnɪzəm ˈfɛməˌnɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 女权主义;女权运动;男女平等主义 Feminism is the belief and aim that women should have the same rights, power, and opportunities as men. 女权主义;女权运动;男女平等主义

  • Feminism may have liberated the feminists, but it has still to change the lives of the majority of women.

    女性运动也许解放了女权主义者，但尚未改变大多数女性的生活。

  • ...Barbara Johnson, that champion of radical feminism.

    芭芭拉·约翰逊—激进女权主义的捍卫者

SYN female emancipation, women's rights, the women's movement, women's liberation
