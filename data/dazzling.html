dazzling
dæzlɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 辉煌的;灿烂的;艳丽的;动人的 Something that is dazzling is very impressive or beautiful. 辉煌的;灿烂的;艳丽的;动人的

  • He gave Alberg a dazzling smile.

    他冲着阿尔伯格粲然一笑。

SYN splendid, brilliant, stunning [informal] , superb  
dazzlingly

  • The view was dazzlingly beautiful.

    景色美轮美奂。

2 ADJ （光）炫目的，耀眼的 A dazzling light is very bright and makes you unable to see properly for a short time. （光）炫目的，耀眼的

  • He shielded his eyes against the dazzling declining sun.

    他用手遮挡眼前落日耀眼的余晖。

SYN brilliant, bright, intense, shining  
dazzlingly

  • The loading bay seemed dazzlingly bright.

    装货区看上去灯火通明。
