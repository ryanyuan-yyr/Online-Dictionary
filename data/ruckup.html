ruck up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG (衣服)起皱，起褶 If cloth or someone's clothing rucks up, it forms folds so that it covers a smaller area than it did before. (衣服)起皱，起褶 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • His designer suits ruck up round his middle. [VERB PREP.]

    他的名牌套装在腰部起了褶皱。

  • His shoe had rucked up one corner of the pale rug. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他的鞋子把浅色地毯的一角弄皱了。
