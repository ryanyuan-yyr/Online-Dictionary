nuzzle
nʌzəl ˈnʌzəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense nuzzles, present participle nuzzling, past tense , past participle nuzzled
VERB (用鼻子和嘴表示亲热地)轻擦，轻触 If you nuzzle someone or something, you gently rub your nose and mouth against them to show affection. (用鼻子和嘴表示亲热地)轻擦，轻触

  • She nuzzled me and I cuddled her. [VERB noun]

    她用嘴轻轻蹭蹭我，我搂住了她。

  • The dog came and nuzzled up against me. [VERB adv./prep.]

    小狗跑过来，依偎在我身边。

SYN snuggle, cuddle, nudge, burrow
