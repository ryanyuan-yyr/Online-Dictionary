engender
ɪndʒendəʳ ɛnˈdʒɛndər ɪnˈdʒɛndər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense engenders, present participle engendering, past tense , past participle engendered
VERB 导致，造成（某种感觉、气氛、状况等） If someone or something engenders a particular feeling, atmosphere, or situation, they cause it to occur. 导致，造成（某种感觉、气氛、状况等） [formal]

  • It helps engender a sense of common humanity. [VERB noun]

    这有助于营造一种博爱的感觉。

  • Mr Bowles could engender delight in students and musicians alike. [VERB noun]

    鲍尔斯先生能够让学生和音乐家都感到快乐。
