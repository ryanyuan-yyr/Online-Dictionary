wedlock
wedlɒk ˈwɛdˌlɑk
1 N-UNCOUNT 已婚（状态） Wedlock is the state of being married. 已婚（状态） [old-fashioned]
SYN marriage, matrimony, holy matrimony, married state  
2 PHRASE （指孩子）婚生的/非婚生的 If a baby is born in wedlock, it is born while its parents are married. If it is born out of wedlock, it is born at a time when its parents are not married. （指孩子）婚生的/非婚生的 [formal]
