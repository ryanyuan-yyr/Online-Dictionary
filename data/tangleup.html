tangle up
1 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 被绕住;被缠住 If a person or thing is tangled up in something such as wire or ropes, they are caught or trapped in it. 被绕住;被缠住

  • Sheep kept getting tangled up in it and eventually the wire was removed. [get/be V-ed P + in]

    不断有绵羊被那根铁丝缠住，最后就把它拆掉了。

  • The teeth are like razors. Once you get tangled up it will never let you go. [get/be V-ed P]

    其牙齿就像剃刀，一旦咬住你它就绝不会松开。

2 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 陷入，卷进(困境) If you are tangled up in a complicated or unpleasant situation, you are involved in it and cannot get free of it. 陷入，卷进(困境)

  • Politicians normally avoid getting tangled up in anything to do with their electorate's savings. [get/be V-ed P + in/with]

    政治家们通常都避免卷进任何与选民储蓄有关的事情里去。
