flux
flʌks flʌks
Word forms: plural fluxes
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft in NOUN] 变动;波动 If something is in a state of flux, it is constantly changing. 变动;波动

  • Education remains in a state of flux which will take some time to settle down.

    教育还处于不断变化中，需要一段时间才能稳定下来。

  • ...a period of economic flux.

    一段时间的经济波动

SYN instability, change, transition, unrest  
2 N-VAR [oft NOUN of n] 流体 You can refer to a flowing mass as a flux . 流体 [technical]

  • ...the flux of cosmic rays.

    宇宙射线流

SYN flow, movement, motion, fluidity
