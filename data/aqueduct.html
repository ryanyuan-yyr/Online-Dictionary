aqueduct
ækwɪdʌkt ˈækwəˌdʌkt
Word forms: plural aqueducts
1 N-COUNT 高架渠；渡槽；桥管 An aqueduct is a long bridge with many arches, which carries a water supply or a canal over a valley. 高架渠；渡槽；桥管

  • ...an old Roman aqueduct.

    一道古老的罗马时代的高架渠

SYN conduit, channel, passage, canal  
2 N-COUNT 沟渠;导水管 An aqueduct is a large pipe or canal which carries a water supply to a city or a farming area. 沟渠;导水管

  • ...a nationwide system of aqueducts to carry water to the arid parts of this country.

    将水输送到该国干旱地区的全国性导水系统
