unimpressive
ʌnɪmpresɪv ˌʌnɪmˈprɛsɪv
ADJ-GRADED 给人印象不深的；平常的；不起眼的 If you describe someone or something as unimpressive, you mean they appear very ordinary, without any special or exciting qualities. 给人印象不深的；平常的；不起眼的

  • ...even though Manchester United have looked unimpressive over recent weeks.

    尽管曼彻斯特联队在近几周表现平平

  • Rainey was an unimpressive, rather dull lecturer.

    雷尼是个平庸而且相当乏味的老师。
