backcloth
bækklɒθ -klɔːθ ˈbækˌklɔθ
Word forms: plural backcloths
1 N-COUNT （挂在舞台后部的）彩画幕布，背景幕布 A backcloth is a large piece of cloth, often with scenery or buildings painted on it, that is hung at the back of a stage while a play is being performed. （挂在舞台后部的）彩画幕布，背景幕布 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 backdrop
2 N-SING （事件的）背景 The backcloth to an event is the general situation in which it happens. （事件的）背景 [British, journalism, literary]

  • I'm not impressed by the promise of tax cuts against the backcloth of a public-spending deficit. [+ of]

    在公共开支出现赤字的情况下承诺减税让我不以为然。
