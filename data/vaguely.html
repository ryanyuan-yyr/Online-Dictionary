vaguely
veɪgli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective] 轻微地;有点儿 Vaguely means to some degree but not to a very large degree. 轻微地;有点儿

  • The voice on the line was vaguely familiar, but Crook couldn't place it at first.

    电话里的声音有点儿熟悉，但是克鲁克一开始想不起来到底是谁。

  • Arnold felt vaguely embarrassed.

    阿诺德感到有点儿尴尬。

  • Most farm workers were only vaguely aware that there was a storm on its way.

    绝大多数农场工人只是隐约感觉到一场风暴将要来临。

2 See also: vague
