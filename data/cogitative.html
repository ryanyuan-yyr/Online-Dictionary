cogitate
kɒdʒɪteɪt ˈkɑdʒəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cogitates, present participle cogitating, past tense , past participle cogitated
VERB 仔细考虑;深入考虑 If you are cogitating, you are thinking deeply about something. 仔细考虑;深入考虑 [formal]

  • He sat silently cogitating. [VERB]

    他静静地坐着沉思。

  • ...to cogitate on the meaning of life. [V + on/about]

    认真思考生命的意义

SYN think, consider, reflect, contemplate  
cogitation

  • After much cogitation, we decided to move to the Isle of Wight.

    经过慎重考虑后，我们决定搬到怀特岛去。
