backwash
bækwɒʃ ˈbækˌwɔʃ
N-SING 余波；不良后果 The backwash of an event or situation is an unpleasant situation that exists after it and as a result of it. 余波；不良后果

  • ...the enduring backwash of another family scandal.

    另一桩家庭丑闻经久不息的余波

  • The Treasury had been blaming the pound's weakness on the backwash from the falling dollar.

    财政部一直将英镑的疲软归咎于美元贬值。
