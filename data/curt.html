curt
kɜːʳt kɜrt
ADJ-GRADED 简短而失礼的;唐突无礼的 If you describe someone as curt, you mean that they speak or reply in a brief and rather rude way. 简短而失礼的;唐突无礼的

  • Her tone of voice was curt.

    她说话的语气唐突无礼。

  • 'The matter is closed,' was the curt reply.

    得到的答复简短而无礼：“事情已经结束了。”

SYN terse, short, brief, sharp  
curtly

  • 'I'm leaving,' she said curtly.

    “我要走了，”她冷冰冰地抛出几个字。
