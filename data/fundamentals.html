fundamentals
fʌndəmentəlz ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlz
N-PLURAL 基础;基本原则(或原理) The fundamentals of something are its simplest, most important elements, ideas, or principles, in contrast to more complicated or detailed ones. 基础;基本原则(或原理)

  • ...teaching small children the fundamentals of road safety. [+ of]

    教给小孩子交通安全的基本规则

  • They agree on fundamentals, like the need for further political reform.

    他们在需要深化政治改革等基本问题上意见一致。

SYN basics, laws, rules, principles
