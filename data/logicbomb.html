logic bomb
Word forms: plural logic bombs
N-COUNT 逻辑炸弹（一种计算计算同病毒，启动时会破坏系统运行） A logic bomb is an unauthorized program that is inserted into a computer system so that when it is started it affects the operation of the computer. 逻辑炸弹（一种计算计算同病毒，启动时会破坏系统运行） [computing]

  • Viruses and logic bombs can doubtless do great damage under some circumstances.

    病毒和逻辑炸弹在某些情况下无疑会造成巨大危害。
