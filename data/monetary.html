monetary
mʌnɪtri mɑːnɪteri ˈmɑnəˌtɛri ˈmʌnəˌtɛri
ADJ [ADJ n] （尤指一国）货币的，金融的 Monetary means relating to money, especially the total amount of money in a country. （尤指一国）货币的，金融的 [business]

  • Some countries tighten monetary policy to avoid inflation.

    一些国家实行紧缩银根的货币政策，以避免通货膨胀。

  • The courts will be asked to place a monetary value on his unfinished career.

    将要求法院对他未竟的事业进行资产评估。

SYN financial, money, economic, capital
