pull down
PHRASAL VERB 拆毁；摧毁；拆掉 To pull down a building or statue means to deliberately destroy it. 拆毁；摧毁；拆掉

  • They'd pulled the registry office down which then left an open space. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们拆掉户籍登记处后，那里留下了一片空地。

  • A small crowd attempted to pull down a statue. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一小伙人试图推倒一尊塑像。

SYN demolish, level, destroy, dismantle
