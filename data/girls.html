girl
gɜːʳl gɜrl
Word forms: plural girls
1 N-COUNT 女孩；姑娘 A girl is a female child. 女孩；姑娘

  • ...an eleven year old girl.

    11岁的女孩

  • I must have been a horrid little girl.

    我一定是个人见人烦的小女孩。

SYN female child, schoolgirl, lass, lassie [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 女儿 You can refer to someone's daughter as a girl . 女儿

  • We had a little girl.

    我们有一个小女儿。

3 N-COUNT 年轻女子，小妞儿（可能具冒犯意味） Young women are often referred to as girls. This use could cause offence. 年轻女子，小妞儿（可能具冒犯意味）

  • ...a pretty twenty-year-old girl.

    20岁的漂亮姑娘

SYN young woman, maiden, chick [slang] , maid  
4 N-COUNT （男人的）女朋友 Some people refer to a man's girlfriend as his girl. （男人的）女朋友 [informal]

  • I've been with my girl for nine years.

    我同我女朋友交往9年了。

+−Usage
Usagegirl
Don't refer to an adult female as a girl. This may cause offence. Use woman. I 'm studying with Diana. She's a woman from my English class.
