abnormality
æbnɔːʳmælɪti ˌæbnɔrˈmæləti
Word forms: plural abnormalities
N-VAR （尤指身体或行为上让人担忧或危险的）反常情况，变态特征 An abnormality in something, especially in a person's body or behaviour, is an unusual part or feature of it that may be worrying or dangerous. （尤指身体或行为上让人担忧或危险的）反常情况，变态特征 [formal]

  • Further scans are required to confirm the diagnosis of an abnormality.

    需要进一步扫描来确诊是否出现异常病变。

  • Genetic abnormalities are more often associated with faults in paternal DNA than in maternal DNA.

    相对于母本DNA,基因畸变往往更多地与父本DNA上的缺陷相关。

SYN strangeness, deviation, eccentricity, aberration
