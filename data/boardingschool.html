boarding school
(also boarding-school)Word forms: plural boarding schools
N-VAR 寄宿学校 A boarding school is a school which some or all of the pupils live in during the school term. 寄宿学校

  • She sent her son to a boarding school in the East.

    她送儿子上了东部的一所寄宿学校。

  • Of course, now she is away at boarding school.

    当然，她现在住在寄宿学校那边。
