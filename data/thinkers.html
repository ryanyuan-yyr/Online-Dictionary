thinker
θɪŋkəʳ
Word forms: plural thinkers
N-COUNT 思考者;(尤指)思想家 A thinker is a person who spends a lot of time thinking deeply about important things, especially someone who is famous for thinking of new or interesting ideas. 思考者;(尤指)思想家

  • ...some of the world's greatest thinkers.

    世界上一些最伟大的思想家

SYN philosopher, intellect [informal] , wise man, sage
