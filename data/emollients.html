emollient
ɪmɒliənt iˈmɑljənt iˈmɑliənt ɪˈmɑljənt ɪˈmɑliənt iˈmoʊljənt iˈmoʊliənt ɪˈmoʊljənt ɪˈmoʊliənt
Word forms: plural emollients
1 N-MASS 润肤剂;护肤霜;镇痛药膏 An emollient is a liquid or cream which you put on your skin to make it softer or to reduce pain. 润肤剂;护肤霜;镇痛药膏 [formal]
SYN moisturizer, oil, cream, lotion  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (乳霜或膏剂)护肤的,润肤的,镇痛的 An emollient cream or other substance makes your skin softer or reduces pain. (乳霜或膏剂)护肤的,润肤的,镇痛的 [formal]
3 ADJ-GRADED 安抚的;抚慰的 An emollient person or emollient speech tries to make things calm and reduce conflict. 安抚的;抚慰的 [formal]

  • The central character is a deceptively emollient senior figure in a Conservative Government.

    核心人物是保守党政府内一个貌似仁和的高层人物。
