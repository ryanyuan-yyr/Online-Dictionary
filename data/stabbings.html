stabbing
stæbɪŋ ˈstæbɪŋ
Word forms: plural stabbings
1 N-COUNT 持刀伤人事件 A stabbing is an incident in which someone stabs someone else with a knife. 持刀伤人事件
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (疼痛)剧烈突发的 A stabbing pain is a sudden sharp pain. (疼痛)剧烈突发的

  • He was struck by a stabbing pain in his midriff.

    他突然感到腹部一阵剧烈疼痛。

SYN sharp, shooting, violent, acute
