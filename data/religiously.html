religiously
rɪlɪdʒəsli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 笃信地;严谨地;认真地 If you do something religiously, you do it very regularly because you feel you have to. 笃信地;严谨地;认真地

  • She didn't stick religiously to the program.

    她并没有严格遵守项目规定。

  • Do these exercises religiously every day.

    每天要认真地做这些练习。
