curl
kɜːʳl kɜrl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense curls, present participle curling, past tense , past participle curled
1 N-COUNT 鬈发;卷发 If you have curls, your hair is in the form of tight curves and spirals. 鬈发;卷发

  • ...the little girl with blonde curls.

    长着金色卷发的小女孩

  • A curl of black hair fell loosely across his forehead.

    他的额头上垂着一绺散开的黑色卷发。

SYN ringlet, lock  
2 N-UNCOUNT 卷;弯曲 If your hair has curl, it is full of curls. 卷;弯曲

  • Dry curly hair naturally for maximum curl and shine.

    让卷发自然变干，才能使头发卷最多，最有光泽。

3 V-ERG 卷;(使)卷曲;(使)鬈曲 If your hair curls or if you curl it, it is full of curls. 卷;(使)卷曲;(使)鬈曲

  • She has hair that refuses to curl. [VERB]

    她头发不易出卷。

  • Maria had curled her hair for the event. [VERB noun]

    玛丽亚为出席那次活动烫了头发。

  • Afro hair is short and tightly curled. [VERB-ed]

    非洲式发型是浓密的小短卷。

SYN crimp, wave, perm, frizz  
4 N-COUNT 卷曲物;螺旋状物 A curl of something is a piece or quantity of it that is curved or spiral in shape. 卷曲物;螺旋状物

  • A thin curl of smoke rose from a rusty stove. [+ of]

    生锈的炉子里冒出一缕青烟。

  • ...curls of lemon peel. [+ of]

    螺旋状的柠檬皮

SYN twist, spiral, coil, kink  
5 V-ERG (使)(脚趾、手指等)蜷曲；(使)蜷缩 If your toes, fingers, or other parts of your body curl, or if you curl them, they form a curved or round shape. (使)(脚趾、手指等)蜷曲；(使)蜷缩

  • His fingers curled gently round her wrist. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他轻轻地握住她的手腕。

  • Raise one foot, curl the toes and point the foot downwards. [VERB noun]

    抬起一只脚，弯曲脚趾，使脚尖朝下。

  • She sat with her legs curled under her. [VERB-ed]

    她盘腿坐着。

SYN wind, entwine, twine  
6 V-ERG (使)呈螺旋(或卷曲)状移动;(使)旋绕 If something curls somewhere, or if you curl it there, it moves there in a spiral or curve. (使)呈螺旋(或卷曲)状移动;(使)旋绕

  • Smoke was curling up the chimney. [VERB prep./adv.]

    烟从烟囱里袅袅升起。

  • He curled the ball into the net. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    他一脚弧线球射门成功。

SYN twirl, turn, bend, twist  
7 VERB 蜷缩 If a person or animal curls into a ball, they move into a position in which their body makes a rounded shape. 蜷缩

  • He wanted to curl into a tiny ball. [VERB + into]

    他想蜷缩成小小的一团。

  • The kitten was curled on a cushion on the sofa. [VERB-ed]

    那只小猫在沙发垫子上蜷缩成了一团。

Curl up means the same as curl .curl up 同 curl

  • In colder weather, your cat will curl up into a tight, heat-conserving ball.

    天气更冷时，猫会为了保存热量而紧紧地缩成一团。

  • She curled up next to him.

    她蜷缩在他身边。

  • He was asleep there, curled up in the fetal position.

    他像胎儿一样蜷曲地睡在那儿。

8 VERB (叶子、纸等)卷边 When a leaf, a piece of paper, or another flat object curls, its edges bend towards the centre. (叶子、纸等)卷边

  • The rose leaves have curled because of an attack by grubs. [VERB]

    玫瑰的叶子因为闹虫害而卷边了。

Curl up means the same as curl .curl up 同 curl

  • The corners of the lino were curling up.

    油地毡卷角了。

9 V-ERG 撇(嘴) If you curl your lip, you raise your upper lip slightly at one side, as a way of showing anger or contempt. 撇(嘴)

  • He curled his upper lip in a show of scepticism. [VERB noun]

    他撇嘴表示怀疑。

  • She had a small, mean mouth that curled disapprovingly each time her husband wrote something in his diary. [VERB]

    每次丈夫写日记时，她都会不满地撇撇那张刻薄的小嘴。
