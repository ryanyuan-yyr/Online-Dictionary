secondary school
Word forms: plural secondary schools
N-VAR 中学 A secondary school is a school for pupils between the ages of 11 or 12 and 17 or 18. 中学

  • She taught history at a secondary school.

    她在一所中学教历史。

  • One in four pupils leaving secondary school can't read or write properly.

    中学毕业生中有 1/4 的人读写有问题。
