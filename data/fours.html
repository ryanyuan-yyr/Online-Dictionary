four
fɔːʳ fɔː fɔr foʊr
Word forms: plural fours
1 NUM (数字)4 Four is the number 4. (数字)4

  • Judith is married with four children.

    朱迪丝已经结婚了，有四个孩子。

2 N-COUNT (板球中)得 4 分的一击 In cricket, if a player hits a four, they score four runs by hitting the ball along the ground so that it crosses the boundary at the edge of the playing area. (板球中)得 4 分的一击

  • Taylor hit 13 fours and batted for 140 minutes.

    泰勒打了 13 个 4 分球，击球时间为 140 分钟。

3 N-COUNT 四人赛艇 A four is a narrow racing boat that is rowed by a team of four people. 四人赛艇
4 PHRASE 四肢着地;趴着 If you are on all fours, your knees, feet, and hands are on the ground. 四肢着地;趴着

  • She crawled on all fours over to the window.

    她爬到窗边。
