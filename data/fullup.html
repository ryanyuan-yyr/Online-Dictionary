full up
fʊl ʌp
(also full-up)
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 挤满的;占满的 Something that is full up has no space left for any more people or things. 挤满的;占满的

  • The prisons are all full up.

    各监狱人满为患。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 吃饱的;喝足的 If you are full up you have eaten or drunk so much that you do not want to eat or drink anything else. 吃饱的;喝足的 [informal]
