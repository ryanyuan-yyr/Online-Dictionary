bear down
1 PHRASAL VERB 向…逼近；冲向 If someone or something bears down on you, they move quickly towards you in a threatening way. 向…逼近；冲向 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • A group of half a dozen men entered the pub and bore down on the bar. [VERB PREP. + on]

    一伙六个人走进酒吧，冲向吧台。

  • The girl flashed a dazzling smile at the television crew and cameras bearing down on her. [V P on n]

    这个女孩对着向她围拢的电视台工作人员和摄影机灿烂一笑。

2 PHRASAL VERB 用力下压；使劲往下推 To bear down on something means to push or press downwards with steady pressure. 用力下压；使劲往下推 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • The roof support structure had collapsed and the entire weight was bearing down on the ceiling. [VERB PREP. + on]

    屋顶的支架倒塌了，全部重量都压到了天花板上。
