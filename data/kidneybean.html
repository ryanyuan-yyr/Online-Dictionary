kidney bean
Word forms: plural kidney beans
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 菜豆；芸豆 Kidney beans are small, reddish-brown beans that are eaten as a vegetable. They are the seeds of a bean plant. 菜豆；芸豆
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 菜豆豆荚 Kidney beans are long, very narrow beans that are green in colour and are eaten as a vegetable. They grow on a tall climbing plant and are the cases that contain the seeds of the plant. 菜豆豆荚 [US]
