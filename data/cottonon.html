cotton on
PHRASAL VERB 明白;领悟;意识到 If you cotton on to something, you understand it or realize it, especially without people telling you about it. 明白;领悟;意识到 [British, informal]

  • She had already cottoned on to the fact that the nanny was not all she appeared. [VERB PREP. + to]

    她已经意识到这个保姆不只是她看起来的那样。

  • It wasn't until he started laughing that they cottoned on! [VERB PREP.]

    他开始笑起来，他们才明白是怎么回事。
