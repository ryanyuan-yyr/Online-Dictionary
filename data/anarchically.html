anarchic
ænɑːʳkɪk ænˈɑrkɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 无政府的；无法无天的；无秩序的 If you describe someone or something as anarchic, you disapprove of them because they do not recognize or obey any rules or laws. 无政府的；无法无天的；无秩序的 [disapproval]

  • ...anarchic attitudes and complete disrespect for authority.

    无政府态度和对权威的全然不尊重

SYN lawless, rioting, confused, disordered
