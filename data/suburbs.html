suburb
sʌbɜːʳb ˈsʌbərb
Word forms: plural suburbs
1 N-COUNT 城郊;郊区 A suburb of a city or large town is a smaller area which is part of the city or large town but is outside its centre. 城郊;郊区

  • Anna was born in 1923 in Ardwick, a suburb of Manchester. [+ of]

    安娜于1923年出生在曼彻斯特的郊区阿德维克。

  • ...the north London suburbs of Harrow, Barnet and Enfield.

    伦敦北部郊区哈罗区、巴尼特区和恩菲尔德区。

2 N-PLURAL [oft in the N] 城郊住宅区 If you live in the suburbs, you live in an area of houses outside the centre of a large town or city. 城郊住宅区

  • His family lived in the suburbs.

    他家住在城郊。

  • ...Bombay's suburbs.

    孟买城郊住宅区
