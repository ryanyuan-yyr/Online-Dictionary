last hurrah
N-COUNT [usually singular] （尤指职业生涯的）最后一次作为；告别表演 Someone's last hurrah is the last occasion on which they do something, especially at the end of their career. （尤指职业生涯的）最后一次作为；告别表演

  • I haven't even begun to think about quitting, or having a last hurrah, or allowing my career to wind down.

    不论是突然退出、来个告别演出，还是逐渐淡出，我甚至完全都没有考虑过。
