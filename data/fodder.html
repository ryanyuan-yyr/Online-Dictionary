fodder
fɒdəʳ ˈfɑdər
1 N-UNCOUNT 饲料;草料;秣 Fodder is food that is given to cows, horses, and other animals. 饲料;草料;秣

  • ...fodder for horses.

    马饲料

  • The alfalfa plant is widely used as animal fodder.

    苜蓿科植物广泛用作动物饲料。

SYN feed, food, rations, tack [informal]  
2 N-UNCOUNT 素材;材料 If you say that something is fodder for a particular purpose, you mean that it is useful for that purpose and perhaps nothing else. 素材;材料 [disapproval]

  • The press conference simply provided more fodder for another attack on his character.

    记者招待会只不过是提供了更多的再次击他人格的口实。

  • Old movies were the cheapest broadcast fodder.

    老电影是最廉价的播放素材。
