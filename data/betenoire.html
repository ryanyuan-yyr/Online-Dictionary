bete noire
bet nwɑːʳ bɛt nwɑː
(also bête noire)
N-SING [oft with poss] 讨厌鬼；眼中钉，肉中刺 If you refer to someone or something as your bete noire, you mean that you have a particular dislike for them or that they annoy you a great deal. 讨厌鬼；眼中钉，肉中刺

  • Our real bete noire is the car boot sale.

    我们最烦那些用汽车后备箱摆摊卖旧货的。
