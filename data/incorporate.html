incorporate
ɪnkɔːʳpəreɪt ɪnˈkɔːpərɪt ɪnˈkɔrpərɪt ɪnˈkɔrpəˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense incorporates, present participle incorporating, past tense , past participle incorporated
1 VERB 包含;吸收 If one thing incorporates another thing, it includes the other thing. 包含;吸收 [formal]

  • The new cars will incorporate a number of major improvements. [VERB noun]

    新款汽车会有若干重大改进。

SYN include, contain, take in, embrace  
2 VERB 把…合并;使并入 If someone or something is incorporated into a large group, system, or area, they become a part of it. 把…合并;使并入 [formal]

  • The agreement would allow the rebels to be incorporated into a new national police force. [be VERB-ed + into]

    协议将允许把叛军收编入一支新的国家警备部队。

  • The party vowed to incorporate environmental considerations into all its policies. [VERB noun + into]

    该党宣誓要把环境因素纳入它所有的政策当中。

SYN integrate, include, absorb, unite
