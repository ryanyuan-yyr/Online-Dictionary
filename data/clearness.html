clear
klɪəʳ klɪr
Word forms: comparative clearer, superlative clearest, 3rd person singular present tense clears, present participle clearing, past tense , past participle cleared
1 ADJ-GRADED 易懂的;明白的;清楚的 Something that is clear is easy to understand, see, or hear. 易懂的;明白的;清楚的

  • The book is clear, readable and adequately illustrated.

    这本书明白易懂，可读性强，插图丰富。

  • The space telescope has taken the clearest pictures ever of Pluto.

    太空望远镜拍摄到了迄今为止最清晰的冥王星的照片。

  • He repeated his answer, this time in a clear, firm tone of voice.

    他重复了一遍自己的回答，这次声音清晰、语气坚定。

SYN comprehensible, explicit, articulate, understandable  
clearly

  • Whales journey up the coast of Africa, clearly visible from the beach.

    鲸鱼群沿着非洲海岸往北游去，站在海滩上就能看得很清楚。

  • It was important for children to learn to express themselves clearly.

    孩子学会清楚地表达自己很重要。

2 ADJ-GRADED 明显的;显然的;明白无误的 Something that is clear is obvious and impossible to be mistaken about. 明显的;显然的;明白无误的

  • It was a clear case of homicide.

    这显然是一宗杀人案。

  • The clear message of the scientific reports is that there should be a drastic cut in car use.

    那些科学报告清清楚楚地告诉人们应该大幅削减汽车使用量。

  • A spokesman said the British government's position is perfectly clear.

    一位发言人说，英国政府的立场是非常明确的。

  • It became clear that I hadn't been able to convince Mike.

    我显然没有能够说服迈克。

  • It's not clear whether the incident was an accident or deliberate.

    还不清楚这起事件是意外还是蓄意所为。

SYN obvious, plain, apparent, bold  
clearly

  • Clearly, the police cannot break the law in order to enforce it.

    显然，警方不能为了执法而犯法。

  • He clearly believes that India should have de-valued its currency.

    他显然认为印度应将其货币贬值。

3 ADJ-GRADED 完全了解的;明白的;清楚的 If you are clear about something, you understand it completely. 完全了解的;明白的;清楚的 [Also + on]

  • It is important to be clear about what Chomsky is doing here. [+ about]

    很有必要搞清乔姆斯基在这儿干什么。

  • He is not entirely clear on how he will go about it.

    他还没完全搞清楚该如何处理这件事。

  • People use scientific terms with no clear idea of their meaning.

    人们使用科学术语，但并非很清楚其含义。

SYN certain, sure, convinced, positive  
4 ADJ-GRADED (头脑或思路)清楚的，清晰的，清醒的，不糊涂的 If your mind or your way of thinking is clear, you are able to think sensibly and reasonably, and you are not affected by confusion or by a drug such as alcohol. (头脑或思路)清楚的，清晰的，清醒的，不糊涂的

  • She needed a clear head to carry out her instructions.

    她需要一个头脑清醒的人来执行自己的指令。

clearly

  • The only time I can think clearly is when I'm alone.

    我只有一个人的时候才会思路清晰。

5 VERB 理清(思路);使(头脑)清醒 To clear your mind or your head means to free it from confused thoughts or from the effects of a drug such as alcohol. 理清(思路);使(头脑)清醒

  • He walked up Fifth Avenue to clear his head. [VERB noun]

    他沿着第五大道走了走，好让大脑清醒清醒。

  • Our therapists will show you how to clear your mind of worries. [VERB noun + of]

    我们的治疗师会教你如何消除心中的忧虑。

6 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 透明的;清澈的 A clear substance is one which you can see through and which has no colour, like clean water. 透明的;清澈的

  • ...a clear glass panel.

    透明的玻璃板

  • ...a clear gel.

    透明的凝胶

  • The water is clear and plenty of fish are visible.

    水清澈见底，能看见许多鱼。

SYN transparent, see-through, translucent, crystalline  
7 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (颜色)明艳的，鲜亮的 A clear colour is bright and strong. (颜色)明艳的，鲜亮的

  • He has clear blue eyes and a dazzling smile.

    他有一双明亮的蓝眼睛和一脸灿烂的微笑。

8 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (表面)收拾干净的;(地方)畅通的;(视野)无阻碍的 If a surface, place, or view is clear, it is free of unwanted objects or obstacles. (表面)收拾干净的;(地方)畅通的;(视野)无阻碍的

  • The runway is clear — go ahead and land.

    跑道畅通无阻，开始着陆吧。

  • All exits must be kept clear in case of fire or a bomb scare.

    所有出口必须保持畅通，以防有火灾或炸弹威吓发生。

  • Caroline prefers her worktops to be clear of clutter. [+ of]

    卡罗琳不喜欢厨房操作台上乱七八糟的。

  • The windows will allow a clear view of the beach.

    透过窗户，海滩一览无余。

SYN unobstructed, open, free, empty  
9 VERB 清除;清理 When you clear an area or place or clear something from it, you remove things from it that you do not want to be there. 清除;清理 [Also V n + off]

  • To clear the land and harvest the bananas they decided they needed a male workforce. [VERB noun]

    为了清理那片地和收割香蕉，他们决定雇请男劳力。

  • Stewart was trying to clear a path for the stretcher. [VERB noun]

    斯图尔特在努力给担架开路。

  • Workers could not clear the tunnels of smoke. [VERB noun + of]

    工人们无法清除隧道里的烟雾。

  • Firemen were still clearing rubble from apartments damaged at the scene of the attack. [VERB noun + from]

    消防队员仍在从袭击现场遭到毁坏的公寓里往外清理瓦砾。

SYN remove, clean, wipe, cleanse  
10 VERB (为…)清理道路;(为…)扫清障碍 If something or someone clears the way or the path for something to happen, they make it possible. (为…)清理道路;(为…)扫清障碍

  • The Prime Minister resigned today, clearing the way for the formation of a new government. [VERB noun + for]

    首相今天的辞职为新政府的组建扫清了道路。

  • A court in Berlin has dropped the charges against him, clearing the way for him to leave Germany. [V n for n to-inf]

    柏林的一个法庭今天撤销了对他的指控，这样他就可以离开德国了。

11 ADJ-GRADED (天气或天空)晴朗的 If it is a clear day or if the sky is clear, there is no mist, rain, or cloud. (天气或天空)晴朗的

  • On a clear day you can see the French coast.

    天气晴朗时可以望见法国海岸。

  • The winter sky was clear.

    冬天的天空万里无云。

12 VERB (雾霭)消散，退去 When fog or mist clears, it gradually disappears. (雾霭)消散，退去

  • The early morning mist had cleared. [VERB]

    晨雾散去了。

13 ADJ-GRADED (眼睛)清澈的，有神的，明亮的 Clear eyes look healthy, attractive, and shining. (眼睛)清澈的，有神的，明亮的

  • ...clear blue eyes.

    清澈的蓝眼睛

  • Her eyes were clear and steady.

    她的眼神清澈而坚定。

14 ADJ-GRADED (皮肤)光洁的，无斑点的 If your skin is clear, it is healthy and free from spots. (皮肤)光洁的，无斑点的
15 ADJ (良心)无愧的，不内疚的 If you say that your conscience is clear, you mean you do not think you have done anything wrong. (良心)无愧的，不内疚的

  • Mr Garcia said his conscience was clear over the jail incidents.

    加西亚先生说他对监狱中发生的事件问心无愧。

  • I can look back on things with a clear conscience. I did everything I could.

    我可以问心无愧地回首过去，我已经尽力了。

SYN untroubled, clean, pure, innocent  
16 ADJ 不接触的;远离的;在安全距离以外的 If something or someone is clear of something else, it is not touching it or is a safe distance away from it. 不接触的;远离的;在安全距离以外的

  • As soon as he was clear of the terminal building he looked round. [+ of]

    他一离开机场大楼，就环顾了一下四周。

  • She placed a towel on a cluster of rocks just clear of the tidemark.

    她把一条毛巾放在刚没过涨潮线的一堆石头上。

  • He lifted him clear of the deck with one arm.

    他用一只手就将他从甲板上拎了起来。

17 ADV [usu V after ADV] (尤指开车到远处)一直，径直 If you drive clear to a place, especially a place that is far away, you go all the way there without delays. (尤指开车到远处)一直，径直 [US]

  • After that they drove clear over to St Paul.

    之后，他们一口气开到圣保罗。

18 VERB 跳过;越过 If an animal or person clears an object or clears a certain height, they jump over the object, or over something that height, without touching it. 跳过;越过

  • Sotomayor, the Cuban holder of the world high jump record, cleared 2.36 metres. [VERB noun]

    古巴的世界跳高纪录保持者索托马约尔跳过了2.36米。

19 V-ERG 兑现(支票)；（支票）结算 When a bank clears a cheque or when a cheque clears, the bank agrees to pay the sum of money mentioned on it. 兑现(支票)；（支票）结算

  • Polish banks can still take two or three weeks to clear a cheque. [VERB noun]

    在波兰，银行兑现支票可能还需要两三个星期。

  • Allow time for the cheque to clear. [VERB]

    留出时间兑现支票。

20 VERB [usually passive] 批准，准许(行动) If a course of action is cleared, people in authority give permission for it to happen. 批准，准许(行动) [Also be V-ed to-inf]

  • Linda Gradstein has this report from Jerusalem, which was cleared by an Israeli censor. [be VERB-ed]

    琳达·格拉德斯坦的这份报道来自耶路撒冷，它已经通过了以色列方面的审查。

  • Within an hour, the helicopter was cleared for take-off. [be VERB-ed + for]

    不到一个小时，直升机获准起飞。

  • The hormone, developed by US drug companies, is expected to be cleared for use in the US soon.

    这种由美国制药公司研制开发的激素可望不久在美国获准使用。

21 VERB 证明无罪；证实无过错 If someone is cleared, they are proved to be not guilty of a crime or mistake. 证明无罪；证实无过错

  • She was cleared of murder and jailed for just five years for manslaughter. [be V-ed of n/v-ing]

    她被判谋杀罪名不成立，仅以过失杀人罪判处5年监禁。

  • In a final effort to clear her name, Eunice has written a book. [VERB noun]

    尤妮斯写了本书,最后一次试图证明自己的清白。

SYN absolve, acquit, vindicate, exonerate

23 CONVENTION (用于确定自己的话被理解以及强调权威性)听懂了吗？我说明白了吗？ You can say 'Is that clear?' or 'Do I make myself clear?' after you have told someone your wishes or instructions, to make sure that they have understood you, and to emphasize your authority. (用于确定自己的话被理解以及强调权威性)听懂了吗？我说明白了吗？

  • We're only going for half an hour, and you're not going to buy anything. Is that clear?

    我们只去半个小时，而且你什么都不能买。听清楚了没有？

24 PHRASE 没有危险的;不受责备的;不受怀疑的 If someone is in the clear, they are not in danger, or are not blamed or suspected of anything. 没有危险的;不受责备的;不受怀疑的

  • The Audit Commission said that the ministry was in the clear.

    审计署称该政府部门没有问题。

25 PHRASE 把…表达清楚;把…说明白 If you make something clear, you say something in a way that makes it impossible for there to be any doubt about your meaning, wishes, or intentions. 把…表达清楚;把…说明白

  • Mr O'Friel made it clear that further insults of this kind would not be tolerated. [PHRASE that]

    奥弗里尔先生明确表示，绝不容忍这样的侮辱行为再次发生。

  • The far-right has now made its intentions clear.

    极右势力已经清楚地表明了其意图。

26 PREP-PHRASE 高出，超出，多出（竞争对手） If something or someone is a certain amount clear of a competitor, they are that amount ahead of them in a competition or race. 高出，超出，多出（竞争对手） [British]

  • Keegan's team are now seven points clear of West Ham. [+ of]

    基冈率领的球队现在领先西汉姆联队7分。

  • He crossed the line three seconds clear of Tom Snape. [+ of]

    他领先汤姆·斯内普3秒冲过终点线。

27 PHRASE 避开;绕开 If you steer clear or stay clear of someone or something, you avoid them. 避开;绕开

  • The rabbis try to steer clear of political questions. [+ of]

    拉比们努力避开政治问题。
