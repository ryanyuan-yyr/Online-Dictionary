voyeuristic
vwaɪərɪstɪk vɔɪ-
1 ADJ-GRADED 有窥淫癖的 Voyeuristic behaviour involves getting sexual pleasure from secretly watching other people having sex or taking their clothes off. 有窥淫癖的
2 ADJ-GRADED 好刺探他人隐私的 If you describe someone's behaviour as voyeuristic, you disapprove of them because you think they enjoy watching other people's suffering or problems. 好刺探他人隐私的 [disapproval]

  • We as a society are growing more commercial and voyeuristic all the time.

    我们的社会正变得越来越商业化，更好打探别人隐私。
