dice
daɪs daɪs daɪs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dices, present participle dicing, past tense , past participle diced
1 N-COUNT 骰子;色子 A dice is a small cube which has between one and six spots or numbers on its sides, and which is used in games to provide random numbers. In old-fashioned English, 'dice' was used only as a plural form, and the singular was die, but now 'dice' is used as both the singular and the plural form. 骰子;色子
2 N-UNCOUNT 掷骰子游戏 Dice is a game which is played using dice. 掷骰子游戏
3 VERB 把(食物)切成小块;将…切丁 If you dice food, you cut it into small cubes. 把(食物)切成小块;将…切丁

  • Dice the onion. [VERB noun]

    把洋葱切成丁。

  • Add the crushed garlic and remaining diced vegetables. [VERB-ed]

    加入捣碎的大蒜和剩余的蔬菜丁。
