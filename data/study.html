study
stʌdi ˈstʌdi
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense studies, present participle studying, past tense , past participle studied
1 VERB 学习;研读 If you study, you spend time learning about a particular subject or subjects. 学习;研读

  • ...a relaxed and happy atmosphere that will allow you to study to your full potential. [VERB]

    能让人充分发挥学习潜质的轻松愉快的环境

  • He went to Hull University, where he studied History and Economics. [VERB noun]

    他去了赫尔大学学习历史和经济学。

  • The rehearsals make it difficult for her to study for law school exams. [VERB + for]

    排练使她难以专心准备法学院的考试。

SYN learn, cram [informal] , swot (up) [British , informal] , read up  
2 N-UNCOUNT 学习;研究 Study is the activity of studying. 学习;研究

  • ...the use of maps and visual evidence in the study of local history.

    利用地图和图像资料研究当地历史

  • She gave up her studies to have Alexander.

    为了生亚历山大，她放弃了学业。

SYN examination, investigation, analysis, consideration  
3 N-COUNT 研究;调查;研究成果 A study of a subject is a piece of research on it. 研究;调查;研究成果

  • Recent studies suggest that as many as 5 in 1000 new mothers are likely to have this problem.

    最近的研究成果表明在 1000 个刚生产完的妈妈中，有 5 个可能存在这样的问题。

  • ...the first study of English children's attitudes.

    首个关于英国儿童观念的研究

SYN piece of research, survey, report, paper  
4 N-PLURAL 研究科目;学科;课题 You can refer to educational subjects or courses that contain several elements as studies of a particular kind. 研究科目;学科;课题

  • ...a new centre for Islamic studies.

    新的伊斯兰教研究中心

  • She is currently doing a business studies course at Leeds.

    她目前在利兹大学修读一门商科课程。

5 VERB 审视;端详;细看 If you study something, you look at it or watch it very carefully, in order to find something out. 审视;端详;细看

  • Debbie studied her friend's face for a moment. [VERB noun]

    黛比仔细端详了一会她朋友的脸。

SYN examine, survey, look at, scrutinize  
6 VERB 研究;调查;仔细观察 If you study something, you consider it or observe it carefully in order to be able to understand it fully. 研究;调查;仔细观察

  • I know that you've been studying chimpanzees for thirty years now. [VERB noun]

    我知道你研究黑猩猩至今已有30年了。

  • I invite every citizen to carefully study the document. [VERB noun]

    我请每位市民都仔细研读该文件。

SYN contemplate, read, examine, consider  
7 N-COUNT 习作;试画;试作 A study by an artist is a drawing which is done in preparation for a larger picture. 习作;试画;试作
8 N-COUNT 书房 A study is a room in a house which is used for reading, writing, and studying. 书房
9 See also: studied  case study
