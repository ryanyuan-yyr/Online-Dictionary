disparaging
dɪspærɪdʒɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 贬低的;诋毁的;轻蔑的 If you are disparaging about someone or something, or make disparaging comments about them, you say things which show that you do not have a good opinion of them. 贬低的;诋毁的;轻蔑的

  • He was critical of the people, disparaging of their crude manners.

    他对那些人很有看法，看不起他们粗俗的举止。

  • The Minister was alleged to have made disparaging remarks about the rest of the Cabinet.

    据说这位部长发表了一些贬低其他内阁成员的言论。

SYN contemptuous, damaging, critical, slighting  
disparagingly

  • Do not talk disparagingly about your company in public.

    不要公开诋毁你的公司。

  • ...what businessmen disparagingly call 'cut-throat competition'.

    商人们所贬损的“你死我活的竞争”
