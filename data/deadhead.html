dead-head
(also deadhead)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dead-heads, present participle dead-heading, past tense , past participle dead-headed
1 VERB 摘去（植物）的枯花 To dead-head a plant which is flowering means to remove all the dead flowers from it. 摘去（植物）的枯花 [British]

  • Dead-head roses as the blooms fade. [VERB noun]

    玫瑰花开败后就把枯花摘去。

2 N-COUNT 笨蛋;傻瓜;猪脑子 If you say that someone is a deadhead, you mean that they are stupid or slow. 笨蛋;傻瓜;猪脑子 [US, informal]
