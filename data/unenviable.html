unenviable
ʌnenviəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不值得羡慕的；令人不快的；尴尬的 If you describe a situation or task as unenviable, you mean that nobody would enjoy dealing with it because it is very difficult, dangerous, or unpleasant. 不值得羡慕的；令人不快的；尴尬的

  • She had the unenviable task of making the first few phone calls.

    她接受了这个令人为难的差事，负责打头几个电话。

  • It put me in the unenviable position of having to lie.

    这令我陷入了不得不说谎的尴尬境地。

SYN unpleasant, disagreeable, undesirable, painful
