low-down
(also lowdown)
1 N-SING 内幕；实情；真相 If someone gives you the low-down on a person or thing, they tell you all the important information about them. 内幕；实情；真相 [informal]

  • We want you to give us the low-down on your team-mates. [+ on]

    我们希望你告诉我们你的队友的真实情况。

SYN information, intelligence, info [informal] , inside story  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 卑鄙的；无耻的；下流的 You can use low-down to emphasize how bad, dishonest, or unfair you consider a particular person or their behaviour to be. 卑鄙的；无耻的；下流的 [informal, emphasis]

  • ...a lowdown, evil drunkard.

    卑鄙下流的醉汉

  • They will stoop to every low-down trick.

    他们什么卑劣的手段都使得出来。

SYN mean, low, base, cheap [informal]
