knockabout
nɒkəbaʊt ˈnɒkəˌbaʊt ˈnɑkəˌbaʊt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 喧闹喜剧的；闹剧的 Knockabout comedy is lively and often involves people doing funny things, rather than saying them. 喧闹喜剧的；闹剧的 [mainly British]

  • He gave one of his best knockabout performances in a long time.

    他表演了很长时间，那是他最精彩的闹剧之一。

SYN boisterous, riotous, rollicking, rough-and-tumble
