half-yearly
ˌhɑːfˈjɪəlɪ
in AM, use 美国英语用 semiannual
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （历年或财年）年度中期的 Half-yearly means happening in the middle of a calendar year or a financial year. （历年或财年）年度中期的 [British]

  • This is the half-yearly meeting of the interim committee.

    这是临时委员会召开的年中例会。

  • ...the Central Bank's half-yearly report on the state of the economy.

    中央银行关于经济状况的年中报告

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 半年的;六个月的 A company's half-yearly profits are the profits that it makes in six months. 半年的;六个月的 [British]

  • The company announced a half-yearly profit of just £2 million.

    该公司公布其半年利润仅为200万英镑。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 每半年的 Half-yearly means happening twice a year, with six months between each event. 每半年的 [British]

  • ...half-yearly payments.

    每半年支付的款项
