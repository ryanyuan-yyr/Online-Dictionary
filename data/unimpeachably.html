unimpeachable
ʌnɪmpiːtʃəbəl ˌʌnɪmˈpitʃəbəl
ADJ 无可怀疑的；完全可靠的 If you describe someone as unimpeachable, you mean that they are completely honest and reliable. 无可怀疑的；完全可靠的 [formal]

  • He said all five were men of unimpeachable character.

    他说这五个都是品格完美无缺的人。

  • ...an unimpeachable source.

    可靠的来源

SYN beyond criticism, blameless, unquestionable, perfect
