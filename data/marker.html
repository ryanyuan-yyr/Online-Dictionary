marker
mɑːʳkəʳ ˈmɑrkər
Word forms: plural markers
1 N-COUNT (表示方位的)标记，路标，记号;（起提示作用的）标示物 A marker is an object which is used to show the position of something, or is used to help someone remember something. (表示方位的)标记，路标，记号;（起提示作用的）标示物

  • He put a marker in his book and followed her out.

    他在书里夹了一个书签后便随她出去了。

  • ...lines of weathered stone markers sticking up from the desert.

    矗立在沙漠里的一排排饱经风吹日晒的石头路标

2 N-COUNT （品质或特点的）标志，代表 If you refer to something as a marker for a particular quality or feature, you mean that it demonstrates the existence or presence of that quality or feature. （品质或特点的）标志，代表

  • Vitamin C is a good marker for the presence of other vitamins and nutrients in frozen food. [+ for]

    维生素C的存在是冷冻食品里存在其他维生素和营养成分的很好说明。

3 N-COUNT 记号笔，毡笔(笔头较粗，由毡毛制成，用于绘画和着色) A marker or a marker pen is a pen with a thick tip made of felt, which is used for drawing and for colouring things. 记号笔，毡笔(笔头较粗，由毡毛制成，用于绘画和着色)

  • Draw your child's outline with a heavy black marker.

    用浓黑色的毡笔画出你孩子的轮廓。
