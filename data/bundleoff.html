bundle off
PHRASAL VERB 匆忙送走；打发 If someone is bundled off somewhere, they are sent there or taken there in a hurry. 匆忙送走；打发 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The pair were then bundled off to a neighbour's house by waiting police. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    留守的警察随后把夫妇俩打发到邻居家去了。

  • We want to bundle them off to bed quickly. [V n P to n]

    我们想尽快打发他们上床睡觉。
