detente deɪtɒnt
(also détente)
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] (两国间紧张关系的)缓和 Detente is a state of friendly relations between two countries when previously there had been problems between them. (两国间紧张关系的)缓和 [formal]

  • ...their desire to pursue a policy of detente.

    他们寻求缓和两国关系的政策的愿望

  • They have made the first move towards a detente.

    他们已经迈出缓和两国关系的第一步。
