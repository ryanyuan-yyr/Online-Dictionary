despondent
dɪspɒndənt dɪˈspɒndənt dɪˈspɑndənt
ADJ-GRADED 沮丧的;泄气的;失望的 If you are despondent, you are very unhappy because you have been experiencing difficulties that you think you will not be able to overcome. 沮丧的;泄气的;失望的

  • I feel despondent when my work is rejected.

    作品被拒后我感到很沮丧。

SYN dejected, sad, depressed, down  
despondently

  • Despondently, I went back and told Bill the news.

    我深感泄气地回去把这消息告诉了比尔。
