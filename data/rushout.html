rush out
PHRASAL VERB 赶制（产品）；赶印（文件） If a document or product is rushed out, it is produced very quickly. 赶制（产品）；赶印（文件） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • A statement was rushed out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    一份声明匆忙赶印了出来。

  • Studios are rushing out monster movies to take advantage of our new-found enthusiasm for dinosaurs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    电影公司趁着刚刚兴起的恐龙热正忙着赶拍怪兽电影。
