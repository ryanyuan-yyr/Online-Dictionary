imponderable
ɪmpɒndərəbəl ɪmˈpɑndərəbəl
Word forms: plural imponderables
N-COUNT 难以解释的事情;无法估计的事物 An imponderable is something unknown which it is difficult or impossible to estimate or make correct guesses about. 难以解释的事情;无法估计的事物

  • They are speculating on the imponderables of the future.

    他们猜测着未来难以预见的事情。
