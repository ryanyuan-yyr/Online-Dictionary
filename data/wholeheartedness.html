wholehearted
hoʊlhɑːʳtɪd ˌhəʊlˈhɑːtɪd hoʊlˌhɑrtɪd
(also whole-hearted)
ADJ-GRADED 全心全意的 If you support or agree to something in a wholehearted way, you support or agree to it enthusiastically and completely. 全心全意的 [emphasis]

  • The Government deserves our wholehearted support for having taken a step in this direction.

    政府已朝这个方向迈进了一步，理应得到我们的全力支持。

SYN sincere, complete, committed, genuine  
wholeheartedly

  • That's exactly right. I agree wholeheartedly with you.

    太对了。我打心眼里赞同你。
