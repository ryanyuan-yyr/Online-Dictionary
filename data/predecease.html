predecease
priːdɪsiːs ˌpriːdɪˈsiːs ˌpridiˈsis ˌpridɪˈsis
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense predeceases, present participle predeceasing, past tense , past participle predeceased
VERB 比…先死亡；死于…之前 If one person predeceases another, they die before them. 比…先死亡；死于…之前 [formal]

  • His wife of 63 years, Mary, predeceased him by 11 months. [VERB noun]

    和他结发63年的妻子玛丽先于他11个月离开人世。
