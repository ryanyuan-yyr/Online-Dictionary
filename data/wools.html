wool
wʊl wʊl
Word forms: plural wools
1 N-UNCOUNT (羊等的)毛 Wool is the hair that grows on sheep and on some other animals. (羊等的)毛
2 N-MASS 羊毛织物;毛料 Wool is a material made from animal's wool that is used to make things such as clothes, blankets, and carpets. 羊毛织物;毛料

  • ...a wool overcoat.

    羊毛大衣

  • The carpets are made in wool and nylon.

    这些地毯是用毛料和尼龙制成的。

3 See also: cotton wool  steel wool  wire wool 
4 PHRASE 蒙骗；欺骗 If you say that someone is pulling the wool over your eyes, you mean that they are trying to deceive you, in order to have an advantage over you. 蒙骗；欺骗

  • Stop trying to pull the wool over my eyes! What were you two fighting about just now?

    休想蒙我！你们俩刚才在争执什么呢？
