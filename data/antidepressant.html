anti-depressant
(also antidepressant)Word forms: plural anti-depressants
1 N-COUNT 抗抑郁药 An anti-depressant is a drug which is used to treat people who are suffering from depression. 抗抑郁药
2 N-COUNT 使人放松（或快乐）的事物;解忧良药 You can use anti-depressant to talk about something that makes people feel happier and more relaxed. 使人放松（或快乐）的事物;解忧良药

  • Exercise is a powerful antidepressant.

    锻炼能够使人一扫阴霾。
