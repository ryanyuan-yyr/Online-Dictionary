linkage
lɪŋkɪdʒ ˈlɪŋkɪdʒ
Word forms: plural linkages
1 N-VAR 联系；关联；连接 A linkage between two things is a link or connection between them. The linkage of two things is the act of linking or connecting them. 联系；关联；连接 [Also + of]

  • No one disputes the direct linkage between the unemployment rate and crime. [+ between]

    谁都不会质疑失业率与犯罪之间存在直接联系。

  • We're trying to establish linkages between these groups and financial institutions. [+ between]

    我们正努力在这些团体和金融机构之间建立联系。

  • ...the creation of new research materials by the linkage of previously existing sources. [+ of]

    通过整合已有资源而创造出新的研究材料

2 N-UNCOUNT （国际关系中，一方同意做某事，以换取对方同意做另一件事的）关联原则 Linkage is an arrangement where one country agrees to do something only if another country agrees to do something in return. （国际关系中，一方同意做某事，以换取对方同意做另一件事的）关联原则

  • There is no formal linkage between the two agreements. [+ between]

    这两项协议之间不存在正式的关联。

  • He insisted that there could be no linkage with other Mideast problems. [+ with]

    他坚持主张不把它与其他中东问题挂钩。

  • Western diplomats firmly reject any linkage.

    西方外交官坚决拒绝任何关联协议。
