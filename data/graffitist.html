graffiti
grəfiːti ɡræˈfiːtiː grəˈfiti
N-UNCOUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] （在公共场所的）涂画，涂鸦 Graffiti is words or pictures that are written or drawn in public places, for example on walls or posters. （在公共场所的）涂画，涂鸦

  • Buildings old and new are thickly covered with graffiti.

    新老建筑上都有密密麻麻的涂鸦。

  • There's no vandalism, no graffiti, no rubbish left lying about.

    没有损害公共财物的行为，也没有涂鸦和随处丢弃的垃圾。
