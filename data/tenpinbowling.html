(also tenpin bowling)
N-UNCOUNT 保龄球(运动);十柱保龄球戏 Ten-pin bowling is a game in which you try to knock down ten objects shaped like bottles by rolling a heavy ball towards them. It is usually played in a place called a bowling alley. 保龄球(运动);十柱保龄球戏 [mainly British]
