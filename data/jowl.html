jowl
dʒaʊl dʒaʊl dʒoʊl
Word forms: plural jowls
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 下颌垂肉；（尤指）双下巴 You can refer to someone's lower cheeks as their jowls, especially when they hang down towards their jaw. 下颌垂肉；（尤指）双下巴 [literary]
2 PHRASE 紧紧靠着；紧挨着 If you say that people or things are cheek by jowl with each other, you are indicating that they are very close to each other. 紧紧靠着；紧挨着

  • She and her family have to live cheek by jowl with these people. [+ with]

    她和家人不得不紧挨着这些人住着。
