perceptible
pəʳseptɪbəl pərˈsɛptəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 看得见的；察觉得到的 Something that is perceptible can only just be seen or noticed. 看得见的；察觉得到的

  • Pasternak gave him a barely perceptible smile.

    帕斯特纳克给了他一个几乎无法察觉的微笑。

  • There was a slight but perceptible air of neglect.

    有一种微弱但可察觉的冷落意味。

SYN noticeable, clear, obvious, apparent  
perceptibly

  • The tension was mounting perceptibly.

    紧张状况明显在加剧。
