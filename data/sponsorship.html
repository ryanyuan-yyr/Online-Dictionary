sponsorship
spɒnsəʳʃɪp
1 N-UNCOUNT 资助(金);赞助(金);资金支持 Sponsorship is financial support given by a sponsor. 资助(金);赞助(金);资金支持

  • Campbell is one of an ever-growing number of skiers in need of sponsorship.

    坎贝尔是越来越多需要得到资助的滑雪运动员之一。

  • Private sponsorships only accounted for a third of all arts funding last year.

    个人赞助只占去年所有艺术基金的三分之一。

2 N-UNCOUNT 赞助行为;资助行为 Sponsorship of something is the act of sponsoring it. 赞助行为;资助行为

  • When it is done properly, arts sponsorship can be more effective than advertising.

    如果方式得当的话，艺术赞助可能比做广告更有效。
