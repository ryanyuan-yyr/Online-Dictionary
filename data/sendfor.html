send for
1 PHRASAL VERB 请…来;派人去叫 If you send for someone, you send them a message asking them to come and see you. 请…来;派人去叫

  • I've sent for the doctor. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我已经派人去请医生了。

SYN summon, demand, call for, call in  
2 PHRASAL VERB 去信索要;寄信索取 If you send for something, you write and ask for it to be sent to you. 去信索要;寄信索取

  • Send for your free catalogue today. [VERB PREP. noun]

    今天寄信索要你们的免费目录。
