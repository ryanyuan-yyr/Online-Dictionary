bestride
bɪstraɪd biˈstraɪd bɪˈstraɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bestrides, present participle bestriding, past tense bestrode, past participle bestridden
VERB 支配；凌驾；控制 To bestride something means to be the most powerful and important person or thing in it. 支配；凌驾；控制 [literary]

  • America's media companies bestride the globe. [VERB noun]

    美国的传媒公司称雄寰球。

SYN straddle, extend, bridge, mount
