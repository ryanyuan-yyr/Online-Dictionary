womanizing
wʊmənaɪzɪŋ
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 womanising
1 N-UNCOUNT 风流成性；玩弄女性 If you talk about a man's womanizing, you disapprove of him because he likes to have many short sexual relationships with women. 风流成性；玩弄女性 [disapproval]
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 风流成性的;喜欢玩弄女性的 A womanizing man likes to have many short sexual relationships with women. 风流成性的;喜欢玩弄女性的 [disapproval]
