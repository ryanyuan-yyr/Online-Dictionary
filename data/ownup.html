own up
PHRASAL VERB 承认；供认；坦白 If you own up to something wrong that you have done, you admit that you did it. 承认；供认；坦白

  • The headmaster is waiting for someone to own up. [VERB PREP.]

    校长在等着有人站出来承认。

  • Last year my husband owned up to a secret affair with his secretary. [VERB PREP. + to]

    去年丈夫向我坦白了他与秘书的私情。

SYN confess, admit, tell the truth, come clean
