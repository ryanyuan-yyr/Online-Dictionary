lone
loʊn loʊn
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 孤身的；单独的；独自的 If you talk about a lone person or thing, you mean that they are alone. 孤身的；单独的；独自的

  • A lone woman motorist waited for six hours for help yesterday because of a name mix-up.

    由于名字搞错了，一位独自开车的女子昨天等候救援的时间长达6小时。

  • He was shot by a lone gunman.

    他被一名独行枪手射杀。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 单亲的 A lone parent is a parent who is looking after her or his child or children and who is not married or living with a partner. 单亲的 [mainly British]

  • Ninety per cent of lone parent families are headed by mothers.

    90%的单亲家庭由母亲持家。
