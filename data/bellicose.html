bellicose
belɪkoʊs -koʊz ˈbɛlɪˌkoʊs
ADJ-GRADED 好斗的；好战的；好争吵的 You use bellicose to refer to aggressive actions or behaviour that are likely to start an argument or a fight. 好斗的；好战的；好争吵的 [literary]

  • He expressed alarm about the government's increasingly bellicose statements.

    他对政府越来越具挑衅性的声明表示担忧。

SYN aggressive, offensive, hostile, destructive
