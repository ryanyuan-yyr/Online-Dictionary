rationalist
ræʃənəlɪst
Word forms: plural rationalists
1 ADJ 理性主义者的;理性至上者的 If you describe someone as rationalist, you mean that their beliefs are based on reason and logic rather than emotion or religion. 理性主义者的;理性至上者的

  • ...the rationalist philosopher Lakatos.

    理性主义哲学家拉卡托斯

  • ...the rationalist and liberal ideas of the nineteenth century.

    19世纪的理性主义和自由主义观点

  • White was both visionary and rationalist.

    怀特既有远见又充满理性。

2 N-COUNT 理性主义者 If you describe someone as a rationalist, you mean that they base their life on rationalist beliefs. 理性主义者
3 See also: rationalism
