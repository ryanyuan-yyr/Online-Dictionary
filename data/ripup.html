rip up
PHRASAL VERB 把…撕碎 If you rip something up, you tear it into small pieces. 把…撕碎

  • If we wrote I think he would rip up the letter. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果我们写信的话，我想他会把信撕得粉碎。

  • She took every photograph of me that was in our house and ripped it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她拿走了家里所有我的照片，并把它们撕得粉碎。
