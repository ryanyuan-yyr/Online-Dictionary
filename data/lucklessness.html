luckless
lʌkləs ˈlʌklɪs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不幸的；带来厄运的；失败的 If you describe someone or something as luckless, you mean that they are unsuccessful or unfortunate. 不幸的；带来厄运的；失败的 [written]

  • ...the luckless parent of an extremely difficult child.

    一个孩子极其难哄的不幸家长

  • ...the President's luckless tour of the East.

    总统失败的东方之行

SYN unlucky, unfortunate, unsuccessful, hapless
