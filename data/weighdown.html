weigh down
1 PHRASAL VERB 使负荷太重;压得…行动不便 If something that you are wearing or carrying weighs you down, it stops you moving easily by making you heavier. 使负荷太重;压得…行动不便

  • He took off his shoes. If they had to swim, he didn't want anything weighing him down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他脱下了鞋。如果他们不得不游泳的话，他不想让任何东西成为负担。

  • These nests increase in size each year, and can eventually weigh down the branch. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这些巢每年都在变大，最终可能会把树枝压弯。

  • ...soldiers weighed down by their heavy packs. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    背着沉重背包的士兵们

SYN burden, overload, encumber, overburden  
2 PHRASAL VERB 使烦恼;使焦虑 If you are weighed down by something, it makes you extremely worried or causes you great problems. 使烦恼;使焦虑

  • The merchant bank is being weighed down by a £1.3 billion book of bad debts. [be V-ed by/with n]

    这家商业银行因13亿英镑的呆账而忧心忡忡。

  • ...the depression that weighed him down each morning. [VERB noun PREP.]

    每天早上困扰他的沮丧情绪
