ineligible
ɪnelɪdʒəbəl ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 无资格的;不合格的 If you are ineligible for something, you are not qualified for it or entitled to it. 无资格的;不合格的 [formal]

  • ...a ruling that he would be ineligible for any social security benefits.

    他无资格申领任何社会保险福利的裁定

  • They were ineligible to remain in the USA.

    他们不符合条件，不能继续留在美国。

SYN unqualified, ruled out, unacceptable, disqualified
