blissful
blɪsfʊl
1 ADJ-GRADED 极为高兴的；极乐的 A blissful situation or period of time is one in which you are extremely happy. 极为高兴的；极乐的

  • We spent a blissful week together.

    我们一起度过了非常快乐的一周。

  • There's just nothing more blissful than lying by that pool.

    没有比躺在那个泳池边更惬意的事情了。

SYN delightful, pleasing, satisfying, heavenly [informal]  
blissfully

  • We're blissfully happy.

    我们无比幸福。

  • The summer passed blissfully.

    夏天过得快乐极了。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （对不好或严重的事情）丝毫没有察觉的，毫不知情的 If someone is in blissful ignorance of something unpleasant or serious, they are totally unaware of it. （对不好或严重的事情）丝毫没有察觉的，毫不知情的

  • Many country parishes were still living in blissful ignorance of the post-war crime wave.

    庆幸的是，许多乡村教区仍然没有感受到战后的犯罪浪潮。

blissfully

  • At first, he was blissfully unaware of the conspiracy against him.

    起初，他幸好不知道那些针对他的阴谋。
