moisten
mɔɪsən ˈmɔɪsən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense moistens, present participle moistening, past tense , past participle moistened
VERB 使湿润;弄湿 To moisten something means to make it slightly wet. 使湿润;弄湿

  • She took a sip of water to moisten her dry throat. [VERB noun]

    她抿了一口水，润一下发干的喉咙。

  • ...a moistened flannel. [VERB-ed]

    沾湿的毛巾

SYN dampen, water, wet, soak
