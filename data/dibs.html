dibs
dɪbz dɪbz dɪbz
N-PLURAL 优先权利If someone has dibs on something, or if they have first dibs on it, they have the right to have it before anyone else. 优先权利 [mainly US, informal]

  • Barnett wanted the job, but employees with more years on the job had dibs on the position.

    巴尼特想要这份工作，但是在这份工作上有更有年资的员工有这个职位的优先权利。

  • ...a deal that would give her first dibs on anything the non-profit institution produces.

    ...会给她优先权利得到非营利机构所带来一切的一个交易。
