afterthought
ɑːftəʳθɔːt æf- ˈæftərˌθɔt
Word forms: plural afterthoughts
N-COUNT [usually singular] 事后的想法;追加的事物 If you do or say something as an afterthought, you do or say it after something else as an addition, perhaps without careful thought. 事后的想法;追加的事物

  • Almost as an afterthought he added that he missed her.

    他又几乎不假思索地补充说他想念她。
