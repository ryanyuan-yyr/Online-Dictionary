pervert
pəʳvɜːʳt pɜːʳvɜːʳt pərˈvɜrt ˈpɜrˌvɜrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense perverts, present participle perverting, past tense , past participle perverted
The verb is pronounced /pə'vɜːt/. The noun is pronounced /'pɜːvɜːt/. 动词读作/pə'vɜːt/, 名词读作/'pɜːvɜːt/。
1 VERB 破坏；颠覆；使走样；误导 If you pervert something such as a process or society, you interfere with it so that it is not as good as it used to be or as it should be. 破坏；颠覆；使走样；误导 [formal, disapproval]

  • Any reform will destroy and pervert our constitution. [VERB noun]

    任何改革都会破坏并歪曲我们的宪法。

  • He perverted her mind. [VERB noun]

    他误导了她的思想。

2 PHRASE 妨碍司法公正 If someone perverts the course of justice, they deliberately do something that will make it difficult to discover who really committed a particular crime, for example, destroying evidence or lying to the police. 妨碍司法公正 [law]

  • He was charged with conspiring to pervert the course of justice.

    他被控谋划妨碍司法公正。

3 N-COUNT 性变态者 If you say that someone is a pervert, you mean that you consider their behaviour, especially their sexual behaviour, to be immoral or unacceptable. 性变态者 [disapproval]
SYN deviant, degenerate, sicko [informal] , sleazeball [slang]
