wreathe
riːð rið
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense wreathes, present participle wreathing, past tense , past participle wreathed
1 VERB (烟、雾等)笼罩，缭绕 If something is wreathed in smoke or mist, it is surrounded by it. (烟、雾等)笼罩，缭绕 [literary]

  • The ship was wreathed in smoke. [be VERB-ed + in]

    船笼罩在烟雾中。

  • Fog wreathes the temples. [VERB noun]

    雾气笼罩着寺院。

2 VERB [usually passive] （花草）环绕;用（花环等）装饰 If something is wreathed with flowers or leaves, it has a circle or chain of flowers or leaves put round it. （花草）环绕;用（花环等）装饰

  • Its huge columns were wreathed with laurel and magnolia. [be V-ed + with/in]

    其巨大的立柱为月桂和木兰花所环绕。
