maul
mɔːl mɔl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mauls, present participle mauling, past tense , past participle mauled
1 VERB (动物)袭击，严重伤害 If you are mauled by an animal, you are violently attacked by it and badly injured. (动物)袭击，严重伤害

  • He had been mauled by a bear. [be VERB-ed + by]

    他被熊袭击过。

  • The dog went berserk and mauled one of the girls. [VERB noun]

    狗突然发疯，把其中一个女孩咬伤了。

SYN mangle, claw, lacerate, tear  
2 VERB [usually passive] 猛烈攻击;抨击;重创 If someone is mauled, they are attacked aggressively, and often harmed in some way. 猛烈攻击;抨击;重创

  • The troops were severely mauled and lost a quarter of their strength before evacuating the island. [be VERB-ed]

    部队遭到重创，从岛上撤退前损失了四分之一的兵力。

  • The cable-TV and health-care industries are both being mauled by government. [be VERB-ed]

    有线电视和保健业正在遭受政府的严厉批评。

SYN ill-treat, beat, abuse, batter
