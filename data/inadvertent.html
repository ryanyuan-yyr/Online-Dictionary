inadvertent
ɪnədvɜːʳtənt ˌɪnædˈvɜrtənt ˌɪnədˈvɜrtənt
ADJ 无意的;并非故意的;因疏忽造成的 An inadvertent action is one that you do without realizing what you are doing. 无意的;并非故意的;因疏忽造成的

  • The government has said it was an inadvertent error.

    政府声称那是因疏忽造成的过失。

SYN unintentional, accidental, unintended, chance  
inadvertently

  • I inadvertently pressed the wrong button.

    我一疏忽按错了按钮。
