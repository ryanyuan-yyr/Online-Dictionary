fortunate
fɔːʳtʃʊnɪt ˈfɔrtʃənət
ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ to-infinitive] 幸运的;有幸的 If you say that someone or something is fortunate, you mean that they are lucky. 幸运的;有幸的

  • He was extremely fortunate to survive.

    他能活下来真是万幸。

  • Central London is fortunate in having so many large parks and open spaces.

    伦敦市中心有幸拥有许多大型公园和大片空地。

  • It was fortunate that the water was shallow.

    幸运的是水很浅。

  • She is in the fortunate position of having plenty of choice.

    好在她有很多选择。

SYN providential, auspicious, fortuitous, felicitous
