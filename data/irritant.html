irritant
ɪrɪtənt ˈɪrɪtənt
Word forms: plural irritants
1 N-COUNT 令人生气的事;让人恼火的事 If you describe something as an irritant, you mean that it keeps annoying you. 令人生气的事;让人恼火的事 [formal]

  • He said the issue was not a major irritant.

    他说那个问题不是什么大问题。

SYN irritation, nuisance, annoyance, pain [informal]  
2 N-COUNT (令身体部位发痒或疼痛的)刺激物 An irritant is a substance which causes a part of your body to itch or become sore. (令身体部位发痒或疼痛的)刺激物 [formal]

  • Many pesticides are irritants.

    很多杀虫剂都具有刺激性。
