third-class
ˈθɜrdˈklæs
1 ADJ 第三类邮件的In the United States, third-class mail is mail consisting of advertising material such as brochures and fliers, which is cheaper to send than other types of mail. 第三类邮件的

  • The cost of third-class mail has gone up by 50 per cent.

    第三类邮件的邮资上涨了50%。

2 ADJ 三等舱的; 三等车厢的; 最低等舱、厢的In the past, the third-class accommodations on a train or ship were the cheapest and least comfortable accommodations. 三等舱的; 三等车厢的; 最低等舱、厢的

  • ...third-class passengers.

    ...三等舱乘客

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (英国大学学位)三等的A third-class degree is the lowest honours degree that can be obtained from a British university. (英国大学学位)三等的
