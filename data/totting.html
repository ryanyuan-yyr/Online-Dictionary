tot
tɒt tɑt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense tots, present participle totting, past tense , past participle totted
1 N-COUNT 小孩;幼儿 A tot is a very young child. 小孩;幼儿 [informal]
2 N-COUNT (杯中烈酒的)少量 A tot of a strong alcoholic drink such as whisky or brandy is a small amount of it in a glass. (杯中烈酒的)少量 [mainly British]
Phrasal verbs:
tot up
