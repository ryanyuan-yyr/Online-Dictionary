incommunicado
ɪnkəmjuːnɪkɑːdoʊ ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkɑːdəʊ ˌɪnkəˌmjunɪˈkɑdoʊ
1 ADJ 不得与外界接触的;被单独监禁的 If someone is being kept incommunicado, they are not allowed to talk to anyone outside the place where they are. 不得与外界接触的;被单独监禁的

  • He was held incommunicado in prison for ten days before being released without charge.

    在被无指控释放以前,他在狱中被单独监禁了 10 天。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 隐逸的;闭门独处的;不愿受打扰的 If someone is incommunicado, they do not want to be disturbed, or are in a place where they cannot be contacted. 隐逸的;闭门独处的;不愿受打扰的

  • Yesterday she was incommunicado, putting the finishing touches to her autobiography.

    昨天她闭门独处,对她的自传作最后的润色。

  • He is incommunicado in a secluded cottage in Wales.

    他隐居在威尔士一个偏僻的小村舍里。
