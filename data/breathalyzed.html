breathalyze breθəlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense breathalyzes, present participle breathalyzing, past tense , past participle breathalyzed
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 breathalyse
VERB [usually passive] 对…作呼气测醉检验 If the driver of a car is breathalyzed by the police, they ask him or her to breathe into a special bag or device in order to test whether he or she has drunk too much alcohol. 对…作呼气测醉检验 [mainly British]

  • She was breathalysed and found to be over the limit. [be VERB-ed]

    她接受了呼气测醉检查，结果超过了限定值。
