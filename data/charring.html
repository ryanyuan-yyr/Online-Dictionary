char
tʃɑːʳ tʃɑr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense chars, present participle charring, past tense , past participle charred
1 V-ERG (将…)略微烧焦;(把…)烤煳 If food chars or if you char it, it burns slightly and turns black as it is cooking. (将…)略微烧焦;(把…)烤煳

  • Toast hazelnuts on a baking sheet until the skins char. [VERB]

    在烘板上将榛子烤至表皮微煳。

  • Halve the peppers and char the skins under a hot grill. [VERB noun]

    将甜椒剖成两半，在滚热的烤架上烤至外壳微焦。

SYN scorch, sear, singe, carbonize  
charring

  • The chops should be cooked over moderate heat to prevent excessive charring.

    肉块应该用文火烹煮，以免烧过了头。

2 VERB 当清洁工 If a woman chars for someone, she works as their cleaner. 当清洁工 [British, old-fashioned]

  • I'm calling round at the Rodings to ask if Mrs Higgins will char for you. [VERB for noun]

    我将去一趟罗丁家，打听希金斯夫人愿不愿意为你当清洁工。

3 N-UNCOUNT 茶 Tea that you drink can be referred to as char . 茶 [British, informal, old-fashioned]

  • Is there any char going?

    有茶吗？
