blast away
1 PHRASAL VERB 连续射击；连续开枪 If a gun, or a person firing a gun, blasts away, the gun is fired continuously for a period of time. 连续射击；连续开枪

  • Suddenly all the men pull out pistols and begin blasting away. [VERB PREP.]

    突然，所有人都拔出手枪，连续射击。

SYN open fire, shoot, shell, pull the trigger  
2 PHRASAL VERB （收音机等）发出巨响；（流行乐队）带来喧闹的乐声 If something such as a radio or a pop group is blasting away, it is producing a loud noise. （收音机等）发出巨响；（流行乐队）带来喧闹的乐声

  • Clock-radios blast away until you get up. [VERB PREP.]

    收音机闹钟会一直响到你起床为止。

SYN roar, scream, boom, resound
