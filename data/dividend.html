dividend
dɪvɪdend ˈdɪvəˌdɛnd ˈdɪvədənd
Word forms: plural dividends
1 N-COUNT 红利;股息;股利 A dividend is the part of a company's profits which is paid to people who have shares in the company. 红利;股息;股利 [business]

  • The first quarter dividend has been increased by nearly 4 per cent.

    第一季度的股息增长了近 4%。

SYN bonus, share, return, cut [informal]  
2 PHRASE 有回报;产生效益 If something pays dividends, it brings advantages at a later date. 有回报;产生效益

  • Steps taken now to maximise your health will pay dividends later on.

    现在采取措施增强体质会使你日后受益。

3 See also: peace dividend
