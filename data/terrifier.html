terrify
terɪfaɪ ˈtɛrəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense terrifies, present participle terrifying, past tense , past participle terrified
VERB 吓坏;使极度恐慌;使非常害怕 If something terrifies you, it makes you feel extremely frightened. 吓坏;使极度恐慌;使非常害怕

  • Flying terrifies him. [VERB noun]

    坐飞机让他害怕得要命。

  • The thought of dying slowly and painfully terrified me. [VERB noun]

    缓慢而痛苦地死去的想法使我恐惧不已。

SYN frighten, scare, petrify, alarm  
terrified

  • He was terrified of heights.

    他恐高。

  • She was terrified that Ronnie would kidnap Sam.

    她吓坏了，生怕龙尼会绑架萨姆。

  • I'm terrified to think about the fact that my mother will die someday.

    一想到母亲有一天会去世，我就非常恐惧。

Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse terrified and terrifying.

    注意不要混淆 terrified 和 terrifying。
