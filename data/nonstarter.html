non-starter ˈnɑnˈstɑrtər
(also nonstarter)Word forms: plural non-starters
N-COUNT 无望成功的计划(或想法) If you describe a plan or idea as a non-starter, you mean that it has no chance of success. 无望成功的计划(或想法) [informal]

  • The United States is certain to reject the proposal as a non-starter.

    美国肯定会以无成功希望为由拒绝这项提案。

  • Some of the points that she put were indeed non-starters but some of them were really quite reasonable.

    她提出的有些观点的确不可行，但有一些确实很有道理。
