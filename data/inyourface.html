in-your-face ˌɪnˌjʊrˈfeɪs
(also in-yer-face)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 惊世骇俗的;我行我素的;特立独行的 Someone who has an in-your-face attitude seems determined to behave in a way that is unusual or shocking, and does not care what people think of them. 惊世骇俗的;我行我素的;特立独行的 [informal]

  • It's in-your-face feminism, and it's meant to shock.

    这是特立独行的女性主义,它本来就是想惊世骇俗。
