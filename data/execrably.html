execrable
eksɪkrəbəl ˈɛksɪkrəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 令人讨厌的;可恶的;非常糟糕的 If you describe something as execrable, you mean that it is very bad or unpleasant. 令人讨厌的;可恶的;非常糟糕的 [formal]

  • Accusing us of being disloyal to cover his own sorry behavior is truly execrable.

    他指责我们不忠诚以掩盖他自己的错误行为，真令人厌恶。

  • ...an execrable meal.

    非常糟糕的一顿饭

SYN repulsive, offensive, disgusting, horrible
