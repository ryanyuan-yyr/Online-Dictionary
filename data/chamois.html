chamois
ʃæmi ʃæmwɑː ʃamwa ˈʃæmi
Word forms: plural chamois
Pronounced /'ʃæmwɑː/ for meaning 1 in British English. 在英国英语中，义项1读作 /'ʃæmwɑː/。
1 N-COUNT (欧洲、西南亚山区的)岩羚羊 Chamois are small animals rather like goats that live in the mountains of Europe and South West Asia. (欧洲、西南亚山区的)岩羚羊
2 N-COUNT (用于清洁、擦拭的)软皮革 A chamois or a chamois leather is a soft leather cloth used for cleaning and polishing. (用于清洁、擦拭的)软皮革
