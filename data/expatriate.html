expatriate
ekspætriət -peɪt- ɛksˈpeɪtriˌeɪt ɛksˈpeɪtriɪt
Word forms: plural expatriates
1 N-COUNT 移居国外者;侨民 An expatriate is someone who is living in a country which is not their own. 移居国外者;侨民

  • ...British expatriates in Spain.

    在西班牙的英国侨民

SYN exile, refugee, emigrant, émigré  
Expatriate is also an adjective.

  • The French military is preparing to evacuate women and children of expatriate families.

    法国军方正准备撤离侨民家庭的妇女儿童。
