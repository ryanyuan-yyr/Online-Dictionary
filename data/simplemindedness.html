simple-minded
ˈsɪmpəlˈmaɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED 头脑简单的;天真的;愚蠢的 If you describe someone as simple-minded, you believe that they interpret things in a way that is too simple and do not understand how complicated things are. 头脑简单的;天真的;愚蠢的 [disapproval]

  • Sylvie was a simple-minded romantic.

    西尔维是个天真的爱幻想的人。

  • His politics were also simple-minded.

    他的政治观点也很愚蠢。

  • ...those simple-minded solutions.

    那些愚蠢的解决办法

SYN stupid, simple, foolish, backward  
simple-mindedness

  • ...the simple-mindedness of the theme in this film.

    这部电影主题简单
