impede
ɪmpiːd ɪmˈpid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense impedes, present participle impeding, past tense , past participle impeded
VERB 妨碍;阻碍;阻止 If you impede someone or something, you make their movement, development, or progress difficult. 妨碍;阻碍;阻止 [formal]

  • Fallen rock is impeding the progress of rescue workers. [VERB noun]

    坠落的石头阻滞了救援人员的救援进程。

SYN hinder, stop, slow (down), check
