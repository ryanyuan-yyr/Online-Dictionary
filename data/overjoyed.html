overjoyed
oʊvəʳdʒɔɪd
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ to-infinitive] 欣喜若狂的；万分高兴的 If you are overjoyed, you are extremely pleased about something. 欣喜若狂的；万分高兴的

  • Shelley was overjoyed to see me.

    谢利见到我万分高兴。

  • He was overjoyed at his son's return. [+ at]

    他看到儿子回来欣喜若狂。

SYN delighted, happy, pleased, thrilled
