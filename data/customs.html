customs
kʌstəmz
1 N-PROPER [oft NOUN n] 海关(部门) Customs is the official organization responsible for collecting taxes on goods coming into a country and preventing illegal goods from being brought in. 海关(部门)

  • ...components similar to those seized by British customs.

    与英国海关截获的那些物品相似的部件

  • ...customs officers.

    海关官员

2 N-UNCOUNT 海关 Customs is the place where people arriving from a foreign country have to declare goods that they bring with them. 海关

  • He walked through customs.

    他步行通过海关。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (税收)海关的，进出口的 Customs duties are taxes that people pay for importing and exporting goods. (税收)海关的，进出口的
