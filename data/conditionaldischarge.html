conditional discharge
kənˈdɪʃənəl ˈdɪstʃɑːdʒ
Word forms: plural conditional discharges
N-COUNT [usually singular] 有条件释放(指被判有罪者在保证不再犯罪的前提下不受惩罚) If someone who is convicted of an offence is given a conditional discharge by a court, they are not punished unless they later commit a further offence. 有条件释放(指被判有罪者在保证不再犯罪的前提下不受惩罚) [British, law]
