terminus
tɜːʳmɪnəs ˈtɜrmənəs
Word forms: plural termini
N-COUNT (公共汽车、火车的)终点站 On a bus or train route, the terminus is the last stop, where the bus or train turns round or starts a journey in the opposite direction. (公共汽车、火车的)终点站

  • ...the London terminus of the Channel Tunnel rail link.

    英吉利海峡隧道连接铁路的伦敦终点站

SYN end of the line, terminal, station, depot [US , Canadian]
