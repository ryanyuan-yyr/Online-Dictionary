believer
bɪliːvəʳ
Word forms: plural believers
1 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 相信…的人 If you are a great believer in something, you think that it is good, right, or useful. 相信…的人

  • Mum was a great believer in herbal medicines. [+ in]

    妈妈特别相信草药。

2 N-COUNT （宗教）信徒，信仰者 A believer is someone who is sure that God exists or that their religion is true. （宗教）信徒，信仰者

  • I made no secret of the fact that I was not a believer.

    我毫不掩饰我不信教。

SYN follower, supporter, convert, disciple
