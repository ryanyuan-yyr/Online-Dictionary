avarice
ævərɪs ˈævərɪs
N-UNCOUNT 贪财;贪婪;贪得无厌 Avarice is extremely strong desire for money and possessions. 贪财;贪婪;贪得无厌 [literary]

  • He paid a month's rent in advance, just enough to satisfy the landlord's avarice.

    他预交了一个月的房租，正好满足房东的贪心。

SYN greed, meanness, penny-pinching, parsimony [formal]
