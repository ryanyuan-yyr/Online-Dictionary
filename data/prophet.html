prophet
prɒfɪt ˈprɑfɪt
Word forms: plural prophets
1 N-COUNT 先知 A prophet is a person who is believed to be chosen by God to say the things that God wants to tell people. 先知

  • ...the sacred name of the Holy Prophet of Islam.

    伊斯兰先知的圣名

2 N-COUNT 预言者；预言家 A prophet is someone who predicts that something will happen in the future. 预言者；预言家 [literary]

  • I promised myself I'd defy all the prophets of doom and battle back to fitness. [+ of]

    我发誓要打破所有的死亡预言，力争恢复健康。

Quotations
A prophet is not without honour, but in his own country Bible: St. Mark
