dogfight
dɒgfaɪt dɔːg- ˈdɔgˌfaɪt
(also dog fight)Word forms: plural dogfights
1 N-COUNT (战斗机的)近距离激战 A dogfight is a fight between fighter planes, in which they fly close to one another and move very fast. (战斗机的)近距离激战
2 N-COUNT 混战;激战;格斗 If you say that organizations or people are involved in a dogfight, you mean they are struggling very hard against each other in order to succeed. 混战;激战;格斗

  • The three leading contenders were locked in a dogfight.

    三名领先的竞争者陷入一场鏖战。
