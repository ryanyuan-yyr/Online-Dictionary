queen
kwiːn kwin
Word forms: plural queens
1 N-TITLE; N-COUNT 女王;女皇 A queen is a woman who rules a country as its monarch. 女王;女皇

  • ...Queen Victoria.

    维多利亚女王

  • She met the Queen last week.

    上周她受到了女王的接见。

2 N-TITLE; N-COUNT 皇后；王后 A queen is a woman who is married to a king. 皇后；王后

  • The king and queen had fled.

    国王和王后都逃之夭夭了。

3 N-COUNT [n NOUN] (某一活动的)女王，出众的女子 If you refer to a woman as the queen of a particular activity, you mean that she is well-known for being very good at it. (某一活动的)女王，出众的女子

  • ...the queen of crime writing. [+ of]

    犯罪小说女王

SYN leading light, star, favourite, celebrity  
4See also:beauty queen 
5 N-COUNT (女性化的)同性恋男子 A queen is a male homosexual who dresses and speaks rather like a woman. (女性化的)同性恋男子 [informal]
6 N-COUNT (国际象棋中的)后 In chess, the queen is the most powerful piece. It can be moved in any direction. (国际象棋中的)后
7 N-COUNT (纸牌中的)王后(牌)，Q（牌） A queen is a playing card with a picture of a queen on it. (纸牌中的)王后(牌)，Q（牌）

  • ...the queen of spades. [+ of]

    黑桃王后

8 N-COUNT 蜂王 A queen or a queen bee is a large female bee which can lay eggs. 蜂王
