landmark
lændmɑːʳk ˈlændˌmɑrk
Word forms: plural landmarks
1 N-COUNT 地标；陆标 A landmark is a building or feature which is easily noticed and can be used to judge your position or the position of other buildings or features. 地标；陆标

  • The Ambassador Hotel is a Los Angeles landmark.

    国宾大饭店是洛杉矶的地标性建筑之一。

SYN feature, spectacle, monument  
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 里程碑；转折点 You can refer to an important stage in the development of something as a landmark . 里程碑；转折点

  • ...a landmark arms control treaty.

    里程碑式的军备控制条约

  • The baby was one of the big landmarks in our relationship. [+ in]

    孩子的出世是我们俩关系中的一个重要转折点。
