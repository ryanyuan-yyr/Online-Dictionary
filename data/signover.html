sign over
PHRASAL VERB 签字转让(财产、权利等) If you sign something over, you sign documents that give someone else property, possessions, or rights that were previously yours. 签字转让(财产、权利等)

  • Two years ago, he signed over his art collection to the New York Metropolitan Museum of Art. [VERB PREP. noun]

    两年前，他正式签字将他的艺术收藏品转让给了纽约大都会艺术博物馆。

  • Last June, he closed his business voluntarily and signed his assets over to someone else. [VERB noun PREP.]

    去年6月，他主动关闭了自己的公司并签字将资产转让给了其他人。
