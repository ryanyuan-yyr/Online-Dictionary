traverse
trævɜːʳs trəvɜːʳs ˈtrævɜːs trəˈvɜːs trəˈvɜrs ˈtrævərs &
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense traverses, present participle traversing, past tense , past participle traversed
VERB 横穿;横越;穿过 If someone or something traverses an area of land or water, they go across it. 横穿;横越;穿过 [literary]

  • I traversed the narrow pedestrian bridge. [VERB noun]

    我走过狭窄的人行天桥。

  • …a steep-sided valley traversed by streams. [VERB-ed]

    溪流横贯、两侧山势陡峭的山谷

SYN cross, go across, travel over, make your way across
