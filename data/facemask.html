face mask feɪs mɑːsk
Word forms: plural face masks
1 N-COUNT 口罩 A face mask is a device that you wear over your face, for example to prevent yourself from breathing bad air or from spreading germs, or to protect your face when you are in a dangerous situation. 口罩
2 N-COUNT 同 face pack A face mask is the same as a face pack . 同 face pack [mainly US]
