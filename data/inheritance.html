inheritance
ɪnherɪtəns ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns
Word forms: plural inheritances
1 N-VAR 遗产;继承物 An inheritance is money or property which you receive from someone who has died. 遗产;继承物

  • She feared losing her inheritance to her stepmother.

    她担心她继承的遗产会被继母夺走。

SYN legacy, estate, heritage, provision  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] [oft with poss] 转接物;沿袭物 If you get something such as job, problem, or attitude from someone who used to have it, you can refer to this as an inheritance . 转接物;沿袭物

  • ...the situation that was Truman's inheritance as President.

    杜鲁门继任总统时面临的状况

3 N-SING 得自遗传的特征 Your inheritance is the particular characteristics or qualities which your family or ancestors had and which you are born with. 得自遗传的特征

  • Eye colour shows your genetic inheritance.

    眼睛的颜色显示你的基因遗传特征。
