refuel
riːfjuːəl riːˈfjuːəl riˈfjuəl riˈfjul
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense refuels, present participle refuelling, past tense , past participle refuelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 refueling, refueled
V-ERG （给飞机或其他交通工具）（再）加油;（给…）续燃料 When an aircraft or other vehicle refuels or when someone refuels it, it is filled with more fuel so that it can continue its journey. （给飞机或其他交通工具）（再）加油;（给…）续燃料

  • His plane stopped in France to refuel. [VERB]

    他的飞机经停法国续加燃油。

  • The airline's crew refuelled the plane. [VERB noun]

    航空公司的机务人员给飞机加了油。
