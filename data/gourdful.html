gourd
gʊəʳd gɔːʳd ɡʊəd gɔrd gʊrd
Word forms: plural gourds
1 N-COUNT 葫芦；葫芦科植物 A gourd is a large round fruit with a hard skin. You can also use gourd to refer to the plant on which this fruit grows. 葫芦；葫芦科植物
2 N-COUNT 葫芦容器 A gourd is a container made from the hard dry skin of a gourd fruit. Gourds are often used to carry water or for decoration. 葫芦容器

  • The shop imports goods such as painted gourd containers.

    这家商店进口诸如彩绘葫芦容器等商品。
