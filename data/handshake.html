handshake
hændʃeɪk ˈhændˌʃeɪk
Word forms: plural handshakes
1 N-COUNT 握手 If you give someone a handshake, you take their right hand with your own right hand and hold it firmly or move it up and down, as a sign of greeting or to show that you have agreed about something such as a business deal. 握手

  • John smiled and gave him a hearty handshake.

    约翰微笑着和他热情地握手。

  • He has a strong handshake.

    他握手时很有力。
