germane
dʒɜːʳmeɪn dʒərˈmeɪn
ADJ-GRADED 有密切关系的；有重要关联的 Something that is germane to a situation or idea is connected with it in an important way. 有密切关系的；有重要关联的 [formal]

  • ...the suppression of a number of documents which were very germane to the case. [+ to]

    对几份与本案密切相关的文件的隐瞒

  • Fenton was a good listener, and his questions were germane.

    芬顿听得聚精会神，提问也切中要害。

SYN relevant, related, significant, appropriate
