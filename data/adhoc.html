ad hoc
æd hɒk æd ˈhɒk æd ˈhɑk æd ˈhoʊk
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （形势所需而）临时安排的，特别的，专门的 An ad hoc activity or organization is done or formed only because a situation has made it necessary and is not planned in advance. （形势所需而）临时安排的，特别的，专门的

  • The Council meets on an ad hoc basis to discuss problems.

    议会临时安排会议讨论问题。

  • ...ad hoc committees to examine specific problems.

    研究具体问题的专门委员会

SYN makeshift, emergency, improvised, impromptu
