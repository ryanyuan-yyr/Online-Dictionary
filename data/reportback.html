report back
1 PHRASAL VERB 报告;汇报 If you report back to someone, you tell them about something that they asked you to find out about. 报告;汇报

  • The teams are due to report back to the Prime Minister early next year. [VERB PREP. + to]

    这些团队应于明年年初向首相汇报情况。

  • I'll report back the moment I have located him. [VERB PREP.]

    我一找到他就马上汇报。

  • He would, of course, report back on all deliberations. [VERB PREP. + on]

    当然，他会汇报整个审议过程的情况。

  • The repairman reported back that the computer had a virus. [VERB PREP. that]

    修理工报告说电脑染了病毒。

2 PHRASAL VERB 返回…报到 If you report back to a place, you go back there and say that you are ready to start work or say that you are present. 返回…报到 [Also V P + for]

  • The authorities have ordered all soldiers who have returned from the front line to report back to barracks. [VERB PREP. + to]

    当局已经下令让所有从前线返回的士兵回营地报到。

  • They were sent home and told to report back in the afternoon. [VERB PREP.]

    他们被送回家，并要求下午回来报到。

3 PHRASAL VERB 向…转述（评论或意见） If you report back a comment or remark, often a critical one, you repeat it to the person it was made about. 向…转述（评论或意见）

  • We reported this back to Krajicek. [V n P to n]

    我们把这些转述给了克拉伊切克。

  • They're scared that their sentiments might be reported back to the government. [V n P to n]

    他们很害怕自己的看法有可能被人告发到政府。
