hormone replacement therapy
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 激素替代治疗（缩略形式为HRT） If a woman has hormone replacement therapy, she takes the hormone oestrogen, usually in order to control the symptoms of the menopause. The abbreviation HRT is often used. 激素替代治疗（缩略形式为HRT）

  • She has been on hormone replacement therapy for four years and looks fantastic.

    她接受激素替代治疗已经4年了，看起来容光焕发。
