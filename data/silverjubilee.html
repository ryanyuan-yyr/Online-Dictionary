silver jubilee
ˈsɪlvə ˌdʒuːbɪˈliː
Word forms: plural silver jubilees
N-COUNT 25 周年纪念;银禧 A silver jubilee is the 25th anniversary of an important event such as a person becoming king or queen, or an organization being started. 25 周年纪念;银禧

  • She arrived in St Ives to celebrate the Queen's Silver Jubilee.

    她抵达了圣艾夫斯以庆祝女王登基 25 周年。

  • ...the group's silver jubilee concert.

    纪念乐队成立 25 周年音乐会
