embossed
ɪmbɒst -bɔːst
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 有凸饰的;饰以浮雕图案的 If a surface such as paper or wood is embossed with a design, the design stands up slightly from the surface. 有凸饰的;饰以浮雕图案的

  • The paper on the walls was pale gold, embossed with swirling leaf designs. [+ with]

    墙纸是淡黄色的，上面饰以漩涡状叶片凸起图案。
