weighted
weɪtɪd ˈweɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED （体制）对…有利的，向…倾斜的 A system that is weighted in favour of a particular person or group is organized so that this person or group has an advantage. （体制）对…有利的，向…倾斜的

  • The current electoral law is still heavily weighted in favour of the ruling party. [+ in]

    当前的选举法仍然对执政党非常有利。

  • They say the peace process is so heavily weighted against them that it will never achieve results.

    他们说和平进程对他们十分不利，根本不会取得任何成果。
