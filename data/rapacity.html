rapacity
rəpæsɪti
N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 贪婪；贪得无厌 Rapacity is very greedy or selfish behaviour. 贪婪；贪得无厌 [formal, disapproval]

  • He argued that the overcrowded cities were the product of a system based on 'selfishness' and 'rapacity'.

    他认为，过于拥挤的城市是一个建立在“自私”和“贪婪”基础上的体系的产物。

  • ...the rapacity of landlords and the misery of tenants.

    房东的贪得无厌与房客的不幸

  • ...their sexual desire and rapacity.

    他们的性欲与贪欲

SYN greed, greediness, voraciousness, insatiableness
