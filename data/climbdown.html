climb down
PHRASAL VERB (在辩论、争论中)认错，服输，改变意图（或要求） If you climb down in an argument or dispute, you admit that you are wrong, or change your intentions or demands. (在辩论、争论中)认错，服输，改变意图（或要求）

  • If Lafontaine is forced to climb down, he may wish to reconsider his position. [VERB PREP.]

    如果拉方丹被迫让步，他可能愿意重新考虑自己的立场。

  • He has climbed down on pledges to reduce capital gains tax. [V P + on/over]

    他已经作出退让，许诺降低资本收益税。

SYN back down, withdraw, yield, concede
