nerd
nɜːʳd nɜrd
Word forms: plural nerds
N-COUNT (尤指穿着落伍或过于迷恋计算机或科学的)蠢人，(书)呆子 If you say that someone is a nerd, you mean that they are stupid or ridiculous, especially because they wear unfashionable clothes or show too much interest in computers or science. (尤指穿着落伍或过于迷恋计算机或科学的)蠢人，(书)呆子 [informal, offensive, disapproval]

  • Mark claimed he was made to look a nerd.

    马克称自己被搞得像个傻瓜。

  • ...the notion that users of the Internet are all sad computer nerds.

    认为因特网用户全是可悲的计算机呆子的看法
