lowbrow
loʊbraʊ ˈloʊˌbraʊ
(also low-brow)
ADJ-GRADED 浅薄的；庸俗的；格调低的 If you say that something is lowbrow, you mean that it is easy to understand or appreciate rather than intellectual and is therefore perhaps inferior. 浅薄的；庸俗的；格调低的

  • His choice of subject matter has been regarded as lowbrow.

    他选择的主题被认为是浅薄的。

  • ...low-brow novels.

    低俗小说

SYN unsophisticated, popular, shallow, lightweight
