gift
gɪft gɪft
Word forms: plural gifts
1 N-COUNT 礼物；礼品 A gift is something that you give someone as a present. 礼物；礼品

  • ...a gift of $50.00.

    价值50美元的礼物

  • They believed the unborn child was a gift from God.

    他们认为腹中的孩子是上帝赐予的礼物。

  • ...gift shops.

    礼品店

SYN donation, offering, present, contribution  
2 N-COUNT 天赋；天才；才能 If someone has a gift for doing something, they have a natural ability for doing it. 天赋；天才；才能

  • As a youth he discovered a gift for teaching. [+ for]

    他年轻时就表现出教书的天赋。

  • Her grandmother had the gift of making people happy.

    她奶奶有逗人开心的本事。

SYN talent, ability, capacity, genius  
3 God's gift→see: God
