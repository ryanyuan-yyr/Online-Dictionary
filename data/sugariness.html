sugary
ʃʊgəri ˈʃʊgəri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 含糖量高的;甜的 Sugary food or drink contains a lot of sugar. 含糖量高的;甜的

  • Sugary canned drinks rot your teeth.

    喝含糖的罐装饮料会滋生蛀牙。

  • ...sugary tea.

    甜茶

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (电影或音乐)煽情的，甜腻腻的，矫情的 If you describe a film or piece of music as sugary, you mean that it is sentimental and insincere. (电影或音乐)煽情的，甜腻腻的，矫情的 [disapproval]

  • The programme seemed false and sugary, and the characters smug.

    这个节目看起来矫揉造作，而其中的人物还自我感觉良好。

SYN sentimental, sloppy [informal] , mushy [informal] , touching
