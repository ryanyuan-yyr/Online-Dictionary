ground floor
Word forms: plural ground floors
1 N-COUNT 底层；一楼 The ground floor of a building is the floor that is level or almost level with the ground outside. 底层；一楼

  • She showed him around the ground floor of the empty house.

    她带他参观了这处空房子的底楼。

  • Jenny now lives in a terraced ground floor flat.

    珍妮现住在一幢连排公寓的一层。

in AM, use 美国英语用 first floor
