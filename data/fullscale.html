full-scale
ˈfʊlˈskeɪl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 完全的;彻底的;最大限度的 Full-scale means as complete, intense, or great in extent as possible. 完全的;彻底的;最大限度的

  • ...the possibility of a full-scale nuclear war.

    爆发全面核战争的可能性

SYN major, extensive, wide-ranging, all-out  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 与实物一样大小的;原尺寸的 A full-scale drawing or model is the same size as the thing that it represents. 与实物一样大小的;原尺寸的

  • ...working, full-scale prototypes.

    能够工作的原尺寸样机

SYN full-size, full-sized, life-size
