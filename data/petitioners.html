petitioner
pətɪʃənəʳ pɪˈtɪʃənə
Word forms: plural petitioners
1 N-COUNT 请愿者 A petitioner is a person who presents or signs a petition. 请愿者
2 N-COUNT 诉愿人；上诉人 A petitioner is a person who brings a legal case to a court of law. 诉愿人；上诉人 [law]

  • The judge awarded the costs of the case to the petitioners.

    法官判定由这起案件的上诉人支付诉讼费用。

3 N-COUNT 上诉请求离婚者 A petitioner is someone who goes to a court of law to ask for a divorce. 上诉请求离婚者 [British, law]

  • Both the petitioner and respondent provide for the upkeep of the children.

    离婚上诉方和被告方共同抚养子女。
