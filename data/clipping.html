clipping
klɪpɪŋ ˈklɪpɪŋ
Word forms: plural clippings
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 剪报 A clipping is an article, picture, or advertisement that has been cut from a newspaper or magazine. 剪报

  • ...bulletin boards crowded with newspaper clippings.

    贴满报纸剪报的布告栏

2 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft n NOUN] 剪下物 Clippings are small pieces of something that have been cut from something larger. 剪下物

  • Having mown the lawn, there are all those grass clippings to get rid of.

    修剪草坪后有很多剪下的碎草需要清理。

  • ...nail clippings.

    剪下的指甲
