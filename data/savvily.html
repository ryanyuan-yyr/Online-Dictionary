savvy
sævi ˈsævɪ ˈsævi
1 N-UNCOUNT 悟性;见识;实际知识 If you describe someone as having savvy, you think that they have a good understanding and practical knowledge of something. 悟性;见识;实际知识 [informal]

  • He is known for his political savvy and strong management skills.

    他以睿智的政治头脑和强势的管理手腕出名。

SYN understanding, perception, grasp, ken  
2 ADJ-GRADED 有见识的;具有实际知识的;有经验的 If you describe someone as savvy, you think that they show a lot of practical knowledge. 有见识的;具有实际知识的;有经验的 [informal]

  • She was a pretty savvy woman.

    她是个见过世面的漂亮女人。

SYN shrewd, sharp, astute, knowing
