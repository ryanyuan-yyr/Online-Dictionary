definition
defɪnɪʃən ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən
Word forms: plural definitions
1 N-COUNT (尤指词典中给出的)定义，释义 A definition is a statement giving the meaning of a word or expression, especially in a dictionary. (尤指词典中给出的)定义，释义

  • There is no general agreement on a standard definition of intelligence. [+ of]

    “智力”一词目前还没有一个普遍认可的标准释义。

  • My definition of a good hospice is one where some of the patients start feeling too good to die.

    我对“好的晚期病人安养院”的定义是一个能让一些病人开始感到生有所恋的地方。

If you say that something has a particular quality by definition, you mean that it has this quality simply because of what it is.

  • Human perception is highly imperfect and by definition subjective.

    人的感知具有高度缺陷，而且天性主观。

2 N-UNCOUNT 清晰;分明;明确 Definition is the quality of being clear and distinct. 清晰;分明;明确

  • Give your brows extra definition with Outdoor Girl's Eyebrow Pencil in Brown.

    用“户外女孩”品牌的棕色眉笔更加清晰地勾勒出眉形。

  • The speakers criticised his new programme for lack of definition.

    几位发言者批评他的新方案不够清楚明确。

SYN description, settling, fixing, determination
