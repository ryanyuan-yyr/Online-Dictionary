wholegrains
hoʊlgreɪnz ˈhəʊlˌɡreɪnz
(also whole grains)
The forms wholegrain and whole-grain are used as modifiers. wholegrain 和 whole-grain 用作修饰语。
N-PLURAL 全谷物;全麦 Wholegrains are the grains of cereals such as wheat and maize that have not been processed. 全谷物;全麦

  • Fruits, vegetables, and wholegrains are rich in potassium.

    水果、蔬菜和全谷物富含钾元素。

  • ...crusty wholegrain bread.

    全麦硬皮面包
