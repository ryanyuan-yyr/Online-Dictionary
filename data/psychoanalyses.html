Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense psychoanalyses, present participle psychoanalysing, past tense , past participle psychoanalysed

VERB 对…进行精神分析；用精神分析法治疗 When a psychotherapist or psychiatrist psychoanalyses someone who has mental problems, he or she examines or treats them using psychoanalysis. 对…进行精神分析；用精神分析法治疗
