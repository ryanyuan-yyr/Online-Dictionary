palm off
PHRASAL VERB 硬塞；骗卖 If you say that someone has palmed something off on you, you feel annoyed because they have made you accept it although it is not valuable or is not your responsibility. 硬塞；骗卖 [disapproval]

  • I couldn't keep palming her off on friends. [V n P + on]

    我不能总是把她硬塞给朋友们。

  • Joseph made sure that he was never palmed off with inferior stuff. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    约瑟夫确保自己从不会被骗买一些次等原料。

SYN fob off, dismiss, disregard, pooh-pooh [informal]
