angle
æŋgəl ˈæŋɡəl ˈæŋgəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense angles, present participle angling, past tense , past participle angled
1 N-COUNT 角；角度 An angle is the difference in direction between two lines or surfaces. Angles are measured in degrees. 角；角度

  • The boat is now leaning at a 30 degree angle.

    船正以30度角倾斜。

SYN gradient, bank, slope, incline  
2See also:right angle 
3 N-COUNT （两条线或两个平面相交的）夹角，边角 An angle is the shape that is created where two lines or surfaces join together. （两条线或两个平面相交的）夹角，边角

  • ...the angle of the blade.

    刀口的夹角

  • ...brackets to adjust the steering wheel's angle.

    用于调整方向盘角度的支架

SYN intersection, point, edge, corner  
4 N-COUNT 角度 An angle is the direction from which you look at something. 角度

  • Thanks to the angle at which he stood, he could just see the sunset.

    由于站的位置视角好，他刚好能看见日落。

  • His face will be discreetly concealed by camera angles.

    通过调整照相机的角度，他的脸将被巧妙地遮住。

5 N-COUNT 角度；观点；立场；侧重点；倾向性 You can refer to a way of presenting something or thinking about it as a particular angle . 角度；观点；立场；侧重点；倾向性

  • We had to do the scene over and over again, from different angles.

    我们不得不翻来覆去地从不同的角度拍这个场面。

  • He was considering the idea from all angles.

    他正从各个角度考虑这个想法。

SYN point of view, position, approach, direction  
6 VERB [usually cont] （间接迂回地）猎取，谋取，博取 If someone is angling for something, they are trying to get it without asking for it directly. （间接迂回地）猎取，谋取，博取

  • It sounds as if he's just angling for sympathy. [VERB + for]

    听起来好像他只是在博取同情。

7 V-ERG （使）朝向；（使）转向 If you angle something or if it angles in a particular direction, it faces or points in that direction. （使）朝向；（使）转向

  • You can open the slats for a bright light or angle them for more shade. [VERB noun]

    可以调节百叶窗使光线亮一些，也可以暗一些。

  • The path angled downhill and northwards. [VERB adv./prep.]

    小路向山下伸展，然后向北延伸。

  • He drove down the long, steeply angled driveway. [VERB-ed]

    他驱车从又长又陡的车道驶下。

SYN slant, aim, bend, incline  
8 PHRASE 成角度的;歪的;斜的 If something is at an angle, it is leaning in a particular direction so that it is not straight, horizontal, or vertical. 成角度的;歪的;斜的

  • An iron bar stuck out at an angle.

    一根铁棍斜伸出来。

  • The wall's at a slight angle.

    这面墙有点歪。
