brand name
Word forms: plural brand names
N-COUNT 商标；品牌名称 The brand name of a product is the name the manufacturer gives it and under which it is sold. 商标；品牌名称 [business]

  • Drugs can be sold under different brand names throughout the EU.

    药品可以用不同的商标在欧盟各国出售。

  • When it comes to soft drinks Coca-Cola is the biggest selling brand name in Britain.

    说到软饮料，可口可乐在英国是最大的畅销品牌。
