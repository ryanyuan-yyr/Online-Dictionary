backlash
bæklæʃ ˈbækˌlæʃ
N-SING 强烈反应；强烈抵制；激烈反对 A backlash against a tendency or recent development in society or politics, is a sudden, strong reaction against it. 强烈反应；强烈抵制；激烈反对

  • ...the male backlash against feminism.

    男性对女权运动的强烈抵制

  • ...a right-wing backlash.

    右翼的激烈反对

SYN reaction, response, resistance, resentment
