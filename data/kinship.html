kinship
kɪnʃɪp ˈkɪnˌʃɪp
1 N-UNCOUNT 亲属关系 Kinship is the relationship between members of the same family. 亲属关系

  • The ties of kinship may have helped the young man find his way in life.

    亲情可能帮这个年轻人找到了生活的方向。

  • ...her kinship to the English King.

    她同英格兰国王的亲属关系

2 N-UNCOUNT （因背景、感觉或想法相似而产生的）亲切感 If you feel kinship with someone, you feel close to them, because you have a similar background or similar feelings or ideas. （因背景、感觉或想法相似而产生的）亲切感

  • She evidently felt a sense of kinship with the woman. [+ with]

    她明显地感到对这个女人有一种亲切感。

  • ...the warmth and kinship one farmer feels for another.

    农民相互之间的温暖和亲切感

SYN similarity, relationship, association, bearing
