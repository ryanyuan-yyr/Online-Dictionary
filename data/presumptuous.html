presumptuous
prɪzʌmptʃuəs priˈzʌmptʃuəs prɪˈzʌmptʃuəs priˈzʌmptʃəwəs prɪˈzʌmptʃʊwəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 放肆的；冒昧的；自作主张的 If you describe someone or their behaviour as presumptuous, you disapprove of them because they are doing something that they have no right or authority to do. 放肆的；冒昧的；自作主张的 [disapproval]

  • It would be presumptuous to judge what the outcome will be.

    现在就判断结果将会怎样未免有些冒昧。

SYN pushy [informal] , forward, bold, arrogant
