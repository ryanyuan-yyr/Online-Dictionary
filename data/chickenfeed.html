chicken feed
(also chickenfeed)
N-UNCOUNT 微不足道的钱;很少的一点钱 If you think that an amount of money is so small it is hardly worth having or considering, you can say that it is chicken feed . 微不足道的钱;很少的一点钱

  • I was making a million a year, but that's chicken feed in the pop business.

    我一年能赚上一百万，不过在流行音乐产业中，这点钱不值一提。
