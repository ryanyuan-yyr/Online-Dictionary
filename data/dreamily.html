dreamily
driːmɪli
ADV [usually ADVERB with verb] 出神地;想入非非地;心不在焉地 If you say or do something dreamily, you say or do it in a way that shows your mind is occupied with pleasant, relaxing thoughts. 出神地;想入非非地;心不在焉地

  • 'They were divine,' she sighs, dreamily.

    “他们是神圣的,”她神情恍惚地叹了口气。

  • She stared dreamily out of the small window at the blue horizon.

    她出神地看着小窗子外面的蓝色地平线。
