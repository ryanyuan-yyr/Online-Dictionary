write out
1 PHRASAL VERB 写出(篇幅较长的报告或清单) When you write out something fairly long such as a report or a list, you write it on paper. 写出(篇幅较长的报告或清单)

  • We had to write out a list of ten jobs we'd like to do. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们得列出10种我们想做的工作。

  • The application form is important. Sit down and write it out properly. [VERB noun PREP.]

    申请表十分重要，坐下来好好填写。

2 PHRASAL VERB 使(系列剧中的人物)不再出场 If a character in a drama series is written out, he or she is taken out of the series. 使(系列剧中的人物)不再出场 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • When Angie was written out of 'Eastenders' her character went to Spain to open a bar. [be V-ed P + of]

    在《伦敦东区人》中扮演的角色到西班牙开酒吧去了后, 安吉就不再出场了。

  • Maybe soon the scriptwriters will have to write her out of the series. [V n P of n]

    也许很快剧作家就得结束掉她在该系列剧中的戏份，不再让她露面。
