sheaf
ʃiːf ʃif
Word forms: plural sheaves
1 N-COUNT 一叠，一沓，一扎(纸) A sheaf of papers is a number of them held or fastened together. 一叠，一沓，一扎(纸)

  • He took out a sheaf of papers and leafed through them. [+ of]

    他拿出一沓报纸，匆匆翻阅了一下。

SYN bundle, mass, pile, bunch  
2 N-COUNT 一束，一捆(收割的谷物) A sheaf of corn or wheat is a number of corn or wheat plants that have been cut down and tied together. 一束，一捆(收割的谷物)
