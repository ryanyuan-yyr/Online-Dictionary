six-pack
sɪksˌpæk
Word forms: plural six-packs
1 N-COUNT 六瓶装;六罐装 A six-pack is a pack containing six bottles or cans sold together. 六瓶装;六罐装

  • He picked up a six-pack of beer. [+ of]

    他拿起一箱 6 瓶装的啤酒。

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 发达的腹肌 If a man has a six-pack, his stomach muscles are very well developed. 发达的腹肌

  • He has a six-pack stomach and is extremely well-proportioned.

    他腹肌发达，体型完美。
