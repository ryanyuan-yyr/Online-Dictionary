pencil pusher ˈpɛnsəl ˈpʊʃə
Word forms: plural pencil pushers
N-COUNT 文员；文书；耍笔杆子的人 If you call someone a pencil pusher, you mean that their work consists of writing or dealing with documents, and does not seem very useful or important. 文员；文书；耍笔杆子的人 [US, disapproval]

  • ...the pencil pushers who decide the course of people's lives.

    决定人们生活轨迹的文员
