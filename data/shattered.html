shattered
ʃætəʳd ˈʃætɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 无比惊愕难过的;遭受极大打击的 If you are shattered by something, you are extremely shocked and upset about it. 无比惊愕难过的;遭受极大打击的

  • It is desperately sad news and I am absolutely shattered to hear it.

    这是个令人悲痛欲绝的消息，我听后彻底崩溃了。

SYN devastated, shocked, stunned, crushed  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 筋疲力尽的 If you say you are shattered, you mean you are extremely tired and have no energy left. 筋疲力尽的 [British, informal]

  • He was shattered and too tired to concentrate on schoolwork.

    他筋疲力尽，累得都无法集中注意力做作业。

SYN exhausted, drained, worn out, spent
