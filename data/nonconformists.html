nonconformist
nɒnkənfɔːʳmɪst ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːmɪst ˌnɑnkənˈfɔrmɪst
(also non-conformist)Word forms: plural nonconformists
1 ADJ-GRADED 不墨守成规的;不遵循主流思想的 If you say that someone's way of life or opinions are nonconformist, you mean that they are different from those of most people. 不墨守成规的;不遵循主流思想的

  • Their views are non-conformist and their political opinions are extreme.

    他们的看法与众不同，政治见解也很极端。

  • Your non-conformist ways will probably get you into trouble.

    你特立独行的行事方式可能会给自己惹来麻烦。

  • ...a nonconformist lifestyle.

    与众不同的生活方式

SYN dissenting, dissident, heterodox, schismatic  
A nonconformist is someone who is nonconformist.不墨守成规的人;不合主流者

  • Victoria stood out as a dazzling non-conformist.

    维多利亚以令人惊叹的打破陈规者的形象而引人注目。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (英国)不信奉英国国教的，不遵奉圣公会的 In Britain, nonconformist churches are Protestant churches which are not part of the Church of England. (英国)不信奉英国国教的，不遵奉圣公会的

  • His father was a Nonconformist minister.

    他父亲是新教牧师。

A nonconformist is a member of a nonconformist church.(不信奉英国国教的)新教徒

  • Although he seems to be an old-fashioned non-conformist, he is in fact a very devout Catholic.

    虽然他看起来像守旧的新教徒，实际是非常虔诚的天主教徒。
