drainpipe
dreɪnpaɪp ˈdreɪnˌpaɪp
Word forms: plural drainpipes
N-COUNT 雨水管;排水管 A drainpipe is a pipe attached to the side of a building, through which rainwater flows from the roof into a drain. 雨水管;排水管

  • He evaded police by climbing through a window and shinning down a drainpipe.

    他翻窗从下水道管子爬下，从而躲过了警察。
