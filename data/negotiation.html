negotiation
nɪgoʊʃieɪʃən nɪˌgoʊʃiˈeɪʃən nɪˌgoʊsiˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural negotiations
1 N-VAR (尤指商业或政治方面的)谈判，协商，磋商 Negotiations are formal discussions between people who have different aims or intentions, especially in business or politics, during which they try to reach an agreement. (尤指商业或政治方面的)谈判，协商，磋商

  • We have had meaningful negotiations and I believe we are very close to a deal.

    我们已进行了有益的磋商，我相信我们很快能成交。

  • After 10 years of negotiation, the Senate ratified the strategic arms reduction treaty.

    经过10年协商，参议院已经正式批准了《削减战略武器条约》。

SYN bargaining, debate, discussion, transaction
