smock
smɒk smɑk
Word forms: plural smocks
1 N-COUNT (通常指女式)宽身袍子 A smock is a loose garment, rather like a long blouse, usually worn by women. (通常指女式)宽身袍子

  • She was wearing wool slacks and a paisley smock.

    她穿着羊毛宽松裤和涡旋花纹宽袍。

2 N-COUNT (画家等穿的)罩衫，工作服 A smock is a loose garment worn by people such as artists to protect their clothing. (画家等穿的)罩衫，工作服
