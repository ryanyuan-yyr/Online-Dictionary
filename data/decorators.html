decorator
dekəreɪtəʳ ˈdɛkəˌreɪtər
Word forms: plural decorators
1 N-COUNT 粉刷匠;油漆匠;裱糊匠 A decorator is a person whose job is to paint houses or put wallpaper up. 粉刷匠;油漆匠;裱糊匠 [British]
2 N-COUNT （室内）装饰设计师，装潢设计师 A decorator is a person who is employed to design and decorate the inside of people's houses. （室内）装饰设计师，装潢设计师 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 interior decorator
