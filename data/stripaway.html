strip away
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤指)揭穿，披露;揭开 To strip away something, especially something that hides the true nature of a thing, means to remove it completely. (尤指)揭穿，披露;揭开

  • Altman strips away the pretence and mythology to expose the film industry as a business like any other. [VERB PREP. noun]

    奥尔特曼揭去了电影产业的伪装和神秘光环，暴露了其与其他产业并无二致的商业本质。

2 PHRASAL VERB 剥去;剥下;揭去 To strip away a layer of something means to remove it completely. 剥去;剥下;揭去

  • Sensitive Cream will not strip away the skin's protective layer. [VERB PREP. noun]

    敏感肌肤专用面霜不会破坏皮肤表面的保护层。

  • She'd managed to strip the bloodied rags away from Nellie's body. [V n P + from]

    她已经设法将内利身上沾满血污的破烂衣衫脱掉了。
