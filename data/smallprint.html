small print
N-UNCOUNT (合同、协议中用小号字体印刷的)附属细则(可能含有容易忽略或不易理解的不利条件) The small print of a contract or agreement is the part of it that is written in very small print. You refer to it as the small print especially when you think that it might include unfavourable conditions which someone might not notice or understand. (合同、协议中用小号字体印刷的)附属细则(可能含有容易忽略或不易理解的不利条件)
