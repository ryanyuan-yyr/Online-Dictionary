contraband
kɒntrəbænd ˈkɑntrəˌbænd
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 违禁物品;禁运品;走私货 Contraband refers to goods that are taken into or out of a country illegally. 违禁物品;禁运品;走私货

  • The ship was found not to be carrying any contraband.

    并未发现这艘船上载有任何违禁物品。

  • Most of the city markets were flooded with contraband goods.

    大多数的城市市场上都充斥着走私货。

SYN smuggling, trafficking, black-marketing, moonshine [US]
