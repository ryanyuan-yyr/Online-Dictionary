Cupid
ˈkjuːpɪd ˈkjupɪd
1 N-PROPER 丘比特(罗马神话中的爱神) Cupid is the Roman god of love. He is usually shown as a baby boy with wings and a bow and arrow. 丘比特(罗马神话中的爱神)
If you say that someone is playing cupid, you mean that they are trying to bring two people together to start a romantic relationship.扮演丘比特角色;牵线撮合;做媒人

  • ...the aristocrat who played Cupid to the Duke and Duchess.

    撮合公爵和公爵夫人的贵族成员

2 N-COUNT 爱神丘比特的画像(或雕像) A cupid is a picture or statue of a pretty little boy with wings, often holding a bow and arrow. 爱神丘比特的画像(或雕像)

  • I would like my wedding cake decorated with cupids.

    我想在我的结婚蛋糕上装饰一些丘比特的造型。
