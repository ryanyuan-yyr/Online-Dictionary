fiddler
fɪdləʳ ˈfɪdlər
Word forms: plural fiddlers
1 N-COUNT (尤指演奏民乐的)小提琴手，小提琴家 A fiddler is someone who plays the violin, especially one who plays folk music. (尤指演奏民乐的)小提琴手，小提琴家

  • And the fiddler played another little tune.

    小提琴手又演奏了一首短曲。

2 N-COUNT 骗子;诈骗者;伪造账目者;骗取钱财的人 A fiddler is someone who lies or dishonestly alters financial documents in order to get money for themselves. 骗子;诈骗者;伪造账目者;骗取钱财的人 [British, informal]

  • Other benefits such as dole money could be frozen and there will be a huge push to weed out fiddlers.

    其他像失业救济金这样的补贴可能会被冻结,大家会努力把骗子清除掉。
