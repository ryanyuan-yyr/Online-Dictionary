aghast
əgɑːst əgæst əˈgæst əˈgɑst
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ, ADJ n] 吃惊的;惊恐的 If you are aghast, you are filled with horror and surprise. 吃惊的;惊恐的 [formal] [Also + at]

  • She watched aghast as his life flowed away.

    看着他的生命逐渐消逝，她惊骇万分。

  • His colleagues were aghast at the sackings.

    他的同事们对于这些解雇感到很吃惊。

  • Tania stared at him aghast, unable to speak.

    塔妮娅吃惊地看着他，说不出话来。

SYN horrified, shocked, amazed, stunned
