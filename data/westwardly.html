westward
westwəʳd ˈwɛstwərd
(also westwards)
ADV 向西;往西 Westward means towards the west. 向西;往西

  • He sailed westward from Palos de la Frontera. [+ from]

    他从帕洛斯-德拉弗龙特拉向西航行。

  • Within hours, she was free to resume her journey westward.

    几小时以后她便抽出身来重新开始西行的旅程。

Westward is also an adjective.

  • ...the one-hour westward flight over the Andes to Lima.

    向西飞行一小时越过安第斯山脉到达利马的航程
