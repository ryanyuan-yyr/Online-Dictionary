measured
meʒəʳd ˈmɛʒərd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 仔细的;谨慎小心的 You use measured to describe something that is careful and deliberate. 仔细的;谨慎小心的

  • The men spoke in soft, measured tones.

    这些人说话的语气轻柔而平稳。

  • Her more measured response will appeal to voters.

    她那更为慎重的答复会受到选民的欢迎。

  • They have to proceed at a measured pace.

    他们不得不小心谨慎地继续前进。

SYN steady, even, slow, regular
