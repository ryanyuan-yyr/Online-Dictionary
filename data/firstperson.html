first person
N-SING 第一人称 A statement in the first person is a statement about yourself, or about yourself and someone else. The subject of a statement like this is 'I' or 'we'. 第一人称

  • He tells the story in the first person.

    他用第一人称讲述了这个故事。

  • His books are always first person narratives.

    他的书总是采用第一人称叙述。
