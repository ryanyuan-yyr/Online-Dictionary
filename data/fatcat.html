fat cat
Word forms: plural fat cats
N-COUNT 大亨;阔佬;大人物 If you refer to a businessman or politician as a fat cat, you are indicating that you disapprove of the way they use their wealth and power. 大亨;阔佬;大人物 [informal, business, disapproval]

  • ...the fat cats who run the bank.

    经营银行的大亨们

  • ...fat-cat corporate types.

    企业大亨的类型
