diabolic
daɪəbɒlɪk ˌdaɪəˈbɑlɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 撒旦引起的;魔鬼般的;残忍的;邪恶的 Diabolic is used to describe things that people think are caused by or belong to the Devil. 撒旦引起的;魔鬼般的;残忍的;邪恶的 [formal]

  • ...the diabolic forces which lurk in all violence.

    潜伏在一切暴力之中的邪恶力量

SYN satanic, demonic, hellish, devilish  
2 ADJ 糟透的;极其恶劣的;非常讨厌的 If you describe something as diabolic, you are emphasizing that it is very bad, extreme, or unpleasant. 糟透的;极其恶劣的;非常讨厌的 [mainly US, emphasis]

  • Pitt's smile returned, and it was hideously diabolic.

    皮特的脸上重新浮现出笑容，但却狰狞可怕。
