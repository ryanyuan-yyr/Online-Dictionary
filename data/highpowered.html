high-powered
ˈhaɪˈpaʊərd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （机器、设备等）大功率的，高效的 A high-powered machine or piece of equipment is very powerful and efficient. （机器、设备等）大功率的，高效的

  • ...high powered lasers.

    大功率激光器

  • ...high-powered binoculars.

    高倍双筒望远镜

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （人）身居要职的，肩负重任的;（职位）重要的，需要很强能力的 Someone who is high-powered or has a high-powered job has a very important and responsible job which requires a lot of ability. （人）身居要职的，肩负重任的;（职位）重要的，需要很强能力的

  • ...a high-powered lawyer.

    精明强干的律师

  • I had a very high-powered senior job in publishing.

    我在出版界担任高层要职。

SYN dynamic, driving, powerful, enterprising
