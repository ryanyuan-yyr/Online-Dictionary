egocentric
iːgoʊsentrɪk eg- ˌiːɡəʊˈsɛntrɪk ˌɛɡəʊˈsɛntrɪk(əl) ˌigoʊˈsɛntrɪk ˌ ɛgoʊˈsɛntrɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 以自我为中心的;个人主义的;自私自利的 Someone who is egocentric thinks only of themselves and their own wants, and does not consider other people. 以自我为中心的;个人主义的;自私自利的 [disapproval]

  • He was egocentric, a man of impulse who expected those around him to serve him.

    他是个以自我为中心、易冲动的人，希望身边的人都为他服务。

SYN self-centred, vain, selfish, narcissistic
