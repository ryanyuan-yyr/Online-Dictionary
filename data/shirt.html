shirt
ʃɜːʳt ʃɜrt
Word forms: plural shirts
1 N-COUNT 衬衫 A shirt is a piece of clothing that you wear on the upper part of your body. Shirts have a collar, sleeves, and buttons down the front. 衬衫
2 See also: dress shirt  stuffed shirt  sweatshirt  T-shirt 
+−Usage
Usageshirt and blouse
Be careful not to use blouse when you should use shirt. Both men and women wear shirts, but only women wear blouses, which are usually thought of as more loose fitting and a little fancier than shirts: Reynaldo put on a fancy shirt to go to the party, but Alma was afraid she'd get her new blouse dirty, so she put on one of the shirts she often wore to work.
