quoit
kɔɪt kɔɪt kɔɪt kwɔɪt kwɔɪt
Word forms: plural quoits
1 N-UNCOUNT (通常在船上玩的)掷环游戏，套圈游戏 Quoits is a game which is played by throwing rings over a small post. Quoits is usually played on board ships. (通常在船上玩的)掷环游戏，套圈游戏
2 N-COUNT (掷环游戏中用的)环，圈 A quoit is a ring used in the game of quoits. (掷环游戏中用的)环，圈
