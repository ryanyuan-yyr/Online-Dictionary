hold in
PHRASAL VERB 压抑，克制（感情、情绪等） If you hold in an emotion or feeling, you do not allow yourself to express it, often making it more difficult to deal with. 压抑，克制（感情、情绪等）

  • Depression can be traced to holding in anger. [VERB PREP. noun]

    抑郁症可以追溯到对愤怒情绪的压抑。

  • Go ahead and cry. Don't hold it in. [VERB noun PREP.]

    只管哭吧。别忍着。

SYN suppress, repress, smother, hold something back
