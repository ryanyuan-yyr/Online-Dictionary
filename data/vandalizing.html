vandalize
vændəlaɪz ˈvændəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vandalizes, present participle vandalizing, past tense , past participle vandalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 vandalise
VERB 故意破坏;肆意捣毁 If something such as a building or part of a building is vandalized by someone, it is damaged on purpose. 故意破坏;肆意捣毁

  • The walls had been horribly vandalized with spray paint. [be VERB-ed]

    墙壁被用喷漆喷得一塌糊涂。

  • About 1,000 rioters vandalized buildings and looted stores. [VERB noun]

    大约1,000名暴徒一路捣毁建筑，抢劫商店。
