isolated
aɪsəleɪtɪd ˈaɪsəleɪtɪd ˈaɪsəˌleɪtəd
1 ADJ-GRADED 偏远的;偏僻的 An isolated place is a long way away from large towns and is difficult to reach. 偏远的;偏僻的

  • Many of the refugee villages are in isolated areas.

    很多难民村落都在偏远地区。

  • Aubrey's family's farm is very isolated.

    奥布里家的农场非常偏僻。

SYN remote, far, distant, lonely  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 孤独的;孤寂的;孤立无援的 If you feel isolated, you feel lonely and without friends or help. 孤独的;孤寂的;孤立无援的

  • Men can feel isolated at work.

    人们工作时可能感到孤立无助。

  • Some patients may become very isolated and depressed.

    一些病人可能变得非常孤独沮丧。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (事例)孤立的,个别的 An isolated example is an example of something that is not very common. (事例)孤立的,个别的

  • They said the allegations related to an isolated case of cheating.

    他们说这些指控与另一件欺诈案有关。

SYN single, individual, unique, unusual
