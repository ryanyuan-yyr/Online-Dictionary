lasso
læsuː læsoʊ ˈlæsoʊ ˈlæsu læˈsoʊ læˈsu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense lassoes, present participle lassoing, past tense , past participle lassoed
1 N-COUNT （尤指牛仔捕牛用的）套索 A lasso is a long rope with a loop at one end, used especially by cowboys for catching cattle. （尤指牛仔捕牛用的）套索
2 VERB 抛套索套捕（动物） If you lasso an animal, you catch it by throwing a lasso round its neck and pulling it tight. 抛套索套捕（动物）

  • Cowboys drove covered wagons and rode horses, lassoing cattle. [VERB noun]

    牛仔们有的赶着大篷车，有的骑着马，抛套索捕牛。
