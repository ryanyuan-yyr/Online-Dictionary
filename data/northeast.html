northeast
nɔrθist ˌnɔːθˈiːst ˌnɔːrˈiːst ˌnɔrθˈist ˌ nɔrˈist
1 N-UNCOUNT [also the N] 东北;东北方 The north-east is the direction which is halfway between north and east. 东北;东北方

  • The land to the north-east fell away into meadows.

    陆地向东北方向倾斜延伸成了牧场。

2 N-SING 东北部;东北部地区 The north-east of a place, country, or region is the part which is in the north-east. 东北部;东北部地区

  • The north-east, with 60 million people, is the most densely populated part of the United States.

    有6,000万人口的东北部是美国人口最稠密的地区。

  • They're all from Newcastle in the North East of England.

    他们都来自英格兰东北部的纽卡斯尔。

3 ADV 向东北 If you go north-east, you travel towards the north-east. 向东北

  • The streets were jammed with slow moving traffic, army convoys moving north-east.

    军车队向东北方向行驶，致使街道上车流缓慢，交通拥挤。

4 ADV 在东北 Something that is north-east of a place is positioned to the north-east of it. 在东北

  • This latest attack was at Careysburg, twenty miles north-east of the capital, Monrovia.

    最近的这次袭击发生在首都蒙罗维亚东北20英里处的凯里斯堡。

5 ADJ 在东北的;东北部的;向东北的;面朝东北的 The north-east edge, corner, or part of a place is the part which is towards the north-east. 在东北的;东北部的;向东北的;面朝东北的

  • These islands of the north-east coast can only be reached in small boats.

    东北部沿海的这些岛屿只有坐小船才能抵达。

  • ...Waltham Abbey on the north-east outskirts of London.

    位于伦敦东北市郊的沃尔瑟姆修道院

6 ADJ (风)从东北方吹来的 A north-east wind is a wind that blows from the north-east. (风)从东北方吹来的

  • By 9.15 a bitter north-east wind was blowing.

    9点15分时刮起了寒冷的东北风。
