gopher
goʊfəʳ ˈgoʊfər
Word forms: plural gophers
1 N-COUNT 囊地鼠，囊鼠(见于加拿大和美国) A gopher is a small animal which looks a bit like a rat and lives in holes in the ground. Gophers are found in Canada and the USA. 囊地鼠，囊鼠(见于加拿大和美国)
2 N-PROPER; also N-COUNT also Gopher 程序，地鼠程序（用于在因特网上查询资料） In computing, Gopher is a program that collects information for you from many databases across the Internet. Gopher 程序，地鼠程序（用于在因特网上查询资料）
