question mark
Word forms: plural question marks
1 N-COUNT 问号 A question mark is the punctuation mark? which is used in writing at the end of a question. 问号
2 N-COUNT 不确定的事;尚有疑问的事 If there is doubt or uncertainty about something, you can say that there is a question mark over it. 不确定的事;尚有疑问的事

  • There are bound to be question marks over his future. [+ over]

    他的未来必然还存有很多不确定的因素。

  • There's now a huge question mark hanging over the success of the negotiations.

    谈判能否成功目前还是一个大大的问号。
