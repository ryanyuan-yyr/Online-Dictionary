large-scale
ˈlɑrdʒˈskeɪl
(also large scale)
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 大规模的；大范围的；大型的 A large-scale action or event happens over a very wide area or involves a lot of people or things. 大规模的；大范围的；大型的

  • ...a large scale military operation.

    大规模的军事行动

SYN wide-ranging, global, sweeping, broad  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （地图或图表）大比例尺的 A large-scale map or diagram represents a small area of land or a building or machine on a scale that is large enough for small details to be shown. （地图或图表）大比例尺的

  • ...a large-scale map of the county.

    一张大比例尺的郡地图
