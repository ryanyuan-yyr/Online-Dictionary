hardball
hɑːʳdbɔːl ˈhɑːdbɔːl ˈhɑrdˌbɔl
PHRASE 采取强硬手段;绝不手软 If someone plays hardball, they will do anything that is necessary to achieve or get what they want, even if this involves being harsh or unfair. 采取强硬手段;绝不手软 [mainly US]

  • She is playing hardball in a world dominated by men 20 years her senior.

    她在一个由比自己大20岁的男人们主宰的世界里作风强硬。
