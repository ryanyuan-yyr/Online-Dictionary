2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 沉默寡言的;孤僻的;内向的 Someone who is withdrawn is very quiet, and does not want to talk to other people. 沉默寡言的;孤僻的;内向的

    她丈夫变得沉默寡言、喜怒无常。
