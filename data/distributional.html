distributional
dɪstrɪbjuːʃənəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 配销的;运销的 Distributional means relating to the distribution of goods. 配销的;运销的

  • What they're doing is setting up distributional networks.

    他们正在建立配销网络。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (国家财富)分配的，分布的 Distributional effects and policies relate to the share of a country's wealth that different groups of people have. (国家财富)分配的，分布的 [formal]

  • ...the distributional effects of free markets, which lead to inequalities in income.

    导致收入不均的自由市场的分配效应
