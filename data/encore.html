encore
ɒŋkɔːʳ -kɔːʳ ˈɒŋkɔː ˈɑnˌkɔr ɑnˈkɔr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural encores, present participle encoring, past tense , past participle encored
1 N-COUNT （应观众要求的）加演节目 An encore is a short extra performance at the end of a longer one, which an entertainer gives because the audience asks for it. （应观众要求的）加演节目

  • Lang's final encore last night was 'Barefoot'.

    朗昨晚最后的加演节目是《赤足》。

SYN repeat performance, repetition, rerun, curtain call  
2 VERB 加演节目；返场 If an entertainer encores, they perform an encore. 加演节目；返场 [informal]

  • They encore with a superlative version of The Who's 'The Kids Are Alright'. [VERB]

    他们加演了谁人乐队的《真是不赖的小伙子》，非常精彩。

3 CONVENTION 再来一个,再奏一曲（古典音乐会观众要求加演节目时用语） 'Encore' is the word shouted by classical concert audiences when they want a performer to perform an encore. 再来一个,再奏一曲（古典音乐会观众要求加演节目时用语）
