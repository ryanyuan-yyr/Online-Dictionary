shambles
ʃæmbəlz ˈʃæmbəlz ˈʃæmbəlz
N-SING (地方、事件或局势)混乱无序，凌乱不堪，一片狼藉 If a place, event, or situation is a shambles or is in a shambles, everything is in disorder. (地方、事件或局势)混乱无序，凌乱不堪，一片狼藉

  • The ship's interior was an utter shambles.

    船里面一片狼藉。

  • The economy is in a shambles.

    经济混乱无序。

SYN chaos, mess, disorder, confusion
