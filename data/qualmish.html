qualm
kwɑːm kwɑm
Word forms: plural qualms
N-COUNT 忧虑;不安;担忧 If you have no qualms about doing something, you are not worried that it may be wrong in some way. 忧虑;不安;担忧

  • I have no qualms about recommending this approach. [+ about]

    我会毫不犹豫地推荐这一方法。

  • Did she see her husband as capable of murder? She had used the word without a qualm.

    她觉得自己的丈夫可能杀人吗？在使用这个字眼的时候，她没有丝毫的不安。
