self-promotion
N-UNCOUNT 自抬身价 If you accuse someone of self-promotion, you disapprove of them because they are trying to make themselves seem more important than they actually are. 自抬身价 [disapproval]

  • Simpson's ruthless ambition and weakness for self-promotion has not made him the most popular journalist in the BBC.

    辛普森不择手段的勃勃野心和自抬身价的毛病使他没能成为英国广播公司最受欢迎的记者。
