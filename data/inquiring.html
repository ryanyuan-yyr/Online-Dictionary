inquiring
ɪnkwaɪərɪŋ
(also enquiring)
1 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 好问的;爱探索的 If you have an inquiring mind, you have a great interest in learning new things. 好问的;爱探索的

  • I have always had an enquiring mind where food is concerned.

    我在吃的方面一向很爱探究求索。

  • All this helps children to develop an inquiring attitude to learning.

    所有这些都有助于培养孩子好学爱问的态度。

SYN inquisitive, interested, curious, questioning  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (表情等)探询的,审视的 If someone has an inquiring expression on their face, they are showing that they want to know something. (表情等)探询的,审视的 [written]

  • ...an enquiring glance.

    探询的一瞥

inquiringly

  • She looked at me inquiringly. 'Well?'

    她用探问的目光看看我说：“怎么样？”
