culture
kʌltʃəʳ ˈkʌltʃər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cultures, present participle culturing, past tense , past participle cultured
1 N-UNCOUNT 文化，文明(如艺术、哲学) Culture consists of activities such as the arts and philosophy, which are considered to be important for the development of civilization and of people's minds. 文化，文明(如艺术、哲学)

  • There is just not enough fun and frivolity in culture today.

    当今的文化恰恰是不够轻松有趣。

  • ...aspects of popular culture.

    大众文化的方方面面

  • ...France's Minister of Culture and Education.

    法国文化和教育部部长

SYN the arts  
2 N-COUNT 文化(尤指拥有特定信仰、生活方式或艺术形式的社会或文明） A culture is a particular society or civilization, especially considered in relation to its beliefs, way of life, or art. 文化(尤指拥有特定信仰、生活方式或艺术形式的社会或文明）

  • ...people from different cultures.

    来自不同文化背景的人们

  • I was brought up in a culture that said you must put back into the society what you have taken out.

    我成长于其中的那个社会文化认为，取之社会必须回报社会。

3 N-COUNT 文化(某组织或群体的一致习惯和行为方式) The culture of a particular organization or group consists of the habits of the people in it and the way they generally behave. 文化(某组织或群体的一致习惯和行为方式)

  • But social workers say that this has created a culture of dependency, particularly in urban areas. [+ of]

    然而社会工作者认为这使得人们普遍产生了依赖，尤其是在城市地区。

  • The institutions have realised they need to change their culture to improve efficiency and service.

    这些机构已经意识到需要改变他们的内部文化以提高效率、改进服务。

SYN lifestyle, habit, way of life, mores  
4 N-COUNT 培养物;培养菌;培养细胞 In science, a culture is a group of bacteria or cells which are grown, usually in a laboratory as part of an experiment. 培养物;培养菌;培养细胞 [technical]

  • ...a culture of human cells. [+ of]

    人类细胞的培养物

  • ...a number of tissue culture experiments.

    多次组织细胞培养实验

5 VERB 培养(细菌);培植(细胞) In science, to culture a group of bacteria or cells means to grow them, usually in a laboratory as part of an experiment. 培养(细菌);培植(细胞) [technical]

  • To confirm the diagnosis, the hospital laboratory must culture a colony of bacteria. [VERB noun]

    为进一步确诊，医院实验室必须培养一个细菌菌落。

  • ...cultured human blood cells. [VERB-ed]

    培养的人体血细胞
