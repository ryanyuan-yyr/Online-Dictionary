sell on
PHRASAL VERB 转卖;倒卖 If you buy something and then sell it on, you sell it to someone else soon after buying it, usually in order to make a profit. 转卖;倒卖

  • Mr Farrier bought cars at auctions and sold them on. [VERB noun PREP.]

    法里尔先生拍得汽车后再将其转手。

  • The arms had been sold to a businessman; he sold them on to paramilitary groups. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    武器出售给了一个商人，他又将其倒卖给了一些准军事组织。
