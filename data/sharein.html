share in
PHRASAL VERB 分享;分担 If you share in something such as a success or a responsibility, you are one of a number of people who achieve or accept it. 分享;分担

  • The company is offering you the chance to share in its success. [VERB PREP. noun]

    公司正向你提供分享其成功的机会。

  • Everybody shares in the cooking chores. [VERB PREP. noun]

    每个人都帮着做饭。

SYN take part in, be involved in, contribute to, participate in
