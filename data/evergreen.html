evergreen
evəʳgriːn ˈɛvərˌgrin
Word forms: plural evergreens
N-COUNT 常青树;常绿植物 An evergreen is a tree or bush which has green leaves all the year round. 常青树;常绿植物

  • Holly, like ivy and mistletoe, is an evergreen.

    冬青和常春藤、槲寄生一样，都是常绿植物。

Evergreen is also an adjective.

  • Plant evergreen shrubs around the end of the month.

    在月末前后种植常绿灌木。
