finalist
faɪnəlɪst ˈfaɪnəlɪst
Word forms: plural finalists
N-COUNT 参加决赛者;决赛选手 A finalist is someone who reaches the last stages of a competition or tournament by doing well or winning in its earlier stages. 参加决赛者;决赛选手

  • The twelve finalists will be listed in the Sunday Times.

    12 名决赛选手的名单将刊登在《星期日泰晤士报》上。
