Word forms: plural valves
1 N-COUNT 阀;活门 A valve is a device attached to a pipe or a tube which controls the flow of air or liquid through the pipe or tube. 阀;活门
2 N-COUNT （心脏的）瓣，瓣膜 A valve is a small piece of tissue in your heart or in a vein which controls the flow of blood and keeps it flowing in one direction only. （心脏的）瓣，瓣膜
