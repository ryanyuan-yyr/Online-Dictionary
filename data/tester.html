tester
testəʳ ˈtɛstər
Word forms: plural testers
1 N-COUNT 测试员;试验员 A tester is a person who has been asked to test a particular thing. 测试员;试验员
2 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 测试器;测试仪 A tester is a machine or device that you use to test whether another machine or device is working properly. 测试器;测试仪

  • I have a battery tester in my garage.

    我的车库里有台电池测试仪。
