appreciable
əpriːʃəbəl əˈpriʃəbəl əˈpriʃiəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 可觉察的；相当大的；明显的 An appreciable amount or effect is large enough to be important or clearly noticed. 可觉察的；相当大的；明显的 [formal]

  • It contains less than 1 per cent fat, an appreciable amount of protein, and a high content of minerals.

    它所含脂肪不到1%，富含蛋白质，矿物质含量也很高。

  • This has not had an appreciable effect on production.

    此事未对生产造成显著影响。

SYN significant, marked, obvious, considerable  
appreciably

  • Travel had not mellowed him appreciably.

    游历并未使他明显成熟。

  • Summer temperatures are appreciably more comfortable there.

    那里夏天的气温明显舒服得多。
