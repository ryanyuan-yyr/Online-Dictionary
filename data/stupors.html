stupor
stjuːpəʳ stuː- ˈstupər ˈstjupər
Word forms: plural stupors
N-COUNT [usually singular, oft in/into a N] (尤指由于醉酒或吸毒而出现的)神志不清，恍惚，不省人事 Someone who is in a stupor is almost unconscious and is unable to act or think normally, especially as a result of drink or drugs. (尤指由于醉酒或吸毒而出现的)神志不清，恍惚，不省人事

  • He fell back onto the sofa in a drunken stupor.

    他烂醉如泥，瘫倒在沙发上。

  • He was drinking himself into a stupor every night.

    他每晚都喝得不省人事。

SYN daze, numbness, unconsciousness, trance
