awakening
əweɪkənɪŋ əˈweɪkənɪŋ əˈweɪkənɪŋ
Word forms: plural awakenings
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 觉悟；觉醒；醒悟 The awakening of a feeling or realization is the start of it. 觉悟；觉醒；醒悟

  • ...the awakening of national consciousness in people. [+ of]

    民众中国民意识的觉醒

  • ...a young woman's sexual awakening.

    少女的性萌动

SYN stirring up, birth, waking, waking up  
2 PHRASE （对不愉快之事的）猛然觉醒 If you have a rude awakening, you are suddenly made aware of an unpleasant fact. （对不愉快之事的）猛然觉醒

  • It was a rude awakening to learn after I left home that I wasn't so special anymore.

    离家后我才猛然意识到自己不再有什么特别。
