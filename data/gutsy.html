gutsy
gʌtsi ˈgʌtsi
Word forms: comparative gutsier, superlative gutsiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 勇敢的；有胆量的；有决心的 If you describe someone as gutsy, you mean they show courage or determination. 勇敢的；有胆量的；有决心的 [informal, approval]

  • I've always been drawn to tough, gutsy women.

    我总是为果敢坚毅的女性所吸引。

  • They admired his gutsy and emotional speech.

    他们很欣赏他信心十足、充满激情的演讲。

2 ADJ-GRADED 强劲的；热烈的；有趣的 If you describe something as gutsy, you mean that it is powerful and interesting. 强劲的；热烈的；有趣的 [informal]

  • ...the rich, gutsy flavours of mature autumn vegetables.

    秋天成熟蔬菜的浓郁味道

  • 'The Commitments' is the gutsy story of a Dublin rock band.

    《追梦者》这部影片讲述了都柏林一个摇滚乐队的激荡人心的故事。
