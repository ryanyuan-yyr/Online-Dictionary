forgiveness
fəʳgɪvnəs fərˈgɪvnɪs
N-UNCOUNT 原谅;宽恕 If you ask for forgiveness, you ask to be forgiven for something wrong that you have done. 原谅;宽恕

  • I offered up a short prayer for forgiveness.

    我做了个简短的祷告，祈求宽恕。

  • ...a spirit of forgiveness and national reconciliation.

    宽容与民族和解的精神

SYN pardon, mercy, absolution, exoneration
