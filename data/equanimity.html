equanimity
ekwənɪmɪti iːk- ˌiːkwəˈnɪmɪtɪ ˌɛkwəˈnɪməti ˌ ikwəˈnɪməti
N-UNCOUNT [oft with NOUN] 平静;镇定;坦然 Equanimity is a calm state of mind and attitude to life, so that you never lose your temper or become upset. 平静;镇定;坦然 [formal]

  • His sense of humour allowed him to face adversaries with equanimity.

    他的幽默感让他能够平静地面对对手。

  • The defeat was taken with equanimity by the leadership.

    领导层坦然地接受了失败。

SYN composure, peace, calm, poise
