poke at
PHRASAL VERB （用尖物反复地）捅，戳，拨弄 If you poke at something, you make lots of little pushing movements at it with a sharp object. （用尖物反复地）捅，戳，拨弄

  • She poked at her food with a fork. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她拿叉子拨弄着食物。

  • Reggie threw more wood on the fire and poked at it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    雷吉又往火上扔了些木柴。
