consul
kɒnsəl ˈkɑnsəl
Word forms: plural consuls
N-COUNT 领事 A consul is an official who is sent by his or her government to live in a foreign city in order to look after all the people there that belong to his or her own country. 领事

  • The British Consul in Zurich has confirmed that a British man was among the people killed.

    英国驻苏黎世领事证实被害人中有一名英国籍男子。
