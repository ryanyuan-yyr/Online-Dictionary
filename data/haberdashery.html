haberdashery
hæbəʳdæʃəri ˈhæbəˌdæʃərɪ ˈhæbərˈdæʃəri ˈhæbəˌdæʃəri
Word forms: plural haberdasheries
1 N-UNCOUNT 缝纫用品 Haberdashery is small articles for sewing, such as buttons, zips, and thread, which are sold in a haberdasher's shop. 缝纫用品 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 notions
2 N-UNCOUNT （商店里出售的）男士服装 Haberdashery is men's clothing sold in a shop. （商店里出售的）男士服装 [US]
3 N-COUNT 缝纫用品店；男装店 A haberdashery is a shop selling haberdashery. 缝纫用品店；男装店
