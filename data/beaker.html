beaker
biːkəʳ ˈbikər
Word forms: plural beakers
1 N-COUNT （通常无柄的）塑料杯 A beaker is a plastic cup used for drinking, usually one with no handle. （通常无柄的）塑料杯 [British]
2 N-COUNT 大口杯；大玻璃杯 A beaker is a large cup or glass. 大口杯；大玻璃杯 [US]
3 N-COUNT 烧杯 A beaker is a glass or plastic jar which is used in chemistry. 烧杯
