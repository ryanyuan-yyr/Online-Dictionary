discrepancy
dɪskrepənsi dɪˈskrɛpənsɪ dɪˈskrɛpənsi
Word forms: plural discrepancies
N-VAR 差异;不符;不一致;出入 If there is a discrepancy between two things that ought to be the same, there is a noticeable difference between them. 差异;不符;不一致;出入 [Also + in]

  • ...the discrepancy between press and radio reports. [+ between]

    新闻和广播报道之间的出入

  • ...major discrepancies in payments made to claimants in similar circumstances.

    同类情况下索赔人所获款项的巨大差异

SYN disagreement, difference, variation, conflict
