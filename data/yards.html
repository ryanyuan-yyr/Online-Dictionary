yard
jɑːʳd jɑrd
Word forms: plural yards
1 N-COUNT [num NOUN] 码（长度单位，等于36英寸或约91.4厘米） A yard is a unit of length equal to thirty-six inches or approximately 91.4 centimetres. 码（长度单位，等于36英寸或约91.4厘米）

  • The incident took place about 500 yards from where he was standing. [+ from]

    事件发生在距他所站位置大约500码远的地方。

  • A few yards away, Jos Vargas stands beside his small home.

    几码之外，乔斯·瓦尔加斯站在他的小房子旁边。

  • ...a long narrow strip of linen two or three yards long.

    两三码长的细长亚麻布条

  • ...a yard of silk. [+ of]

    一码丝绸

2 N-COUNT 院子;庭院 A yard is a flat area of concrete or stone that is next to a building and often has a wall around it. 院子;庭院

  • I saw him standing in the yard.

    我看到他站在院子里。

SYN courtyard, court, garden, backyard  
3 N-COUNT （进行某项作业的）区域，场地，工场 You can refer to a large open area where a particular type of work is done as a yard. （进行某项作业的）区域，场地，工场

  • ...a railway yard.

    铁路调车场

  • ...a ship repair yard.

    船舶修理场

SYN workshop, works, plant, industrial unit  
4 N-COUNT （住宅旁的）园圃，花园 A yard is a piece of land next to someone's house, with grass and plants growing in it. （住宅旁的）园圃，花园 [US]

  • He dug a hole in our yard on Edgerton Avenue to plant a maple tree when I was born.

    我出生的时候，他在埃杰顿大街我们家的园圃里挖了个坑，种了棵枫树。
