unbiased
ʌnbaɪəst ʌnˈbaɪəst
(also unbiassed)
ADJ-GRADED 无偏见的；不偏不倚的；公正的 If you describe someone or something as unbiased, you mean they are fair and not likely to support one particular person or group involved in something. 无偏见的；不偏不倚的；公正的

  • There is no clear and unbiased information available for consumers.

    消费者没有清晰明确、不偏不倚的信息可以参考。

  • The researchers were expected to be unbiased.

    调查人员应该是不带偏见的。

  • ...an unbiased jury.

    公正的陪审团

SYN fair, just, objective, neutral
