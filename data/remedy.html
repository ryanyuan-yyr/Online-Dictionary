remedy
remədi ˈrɛmɪdɪ ˈrɛmədi
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense remedies, present participle remedying, past tense , past participle remedied
1 N-COUNT (问题的)解决方法，解决良方 A remedy is a successful way of dealing with a problem. (问题的)解决方法，解决良方

  • The remedy lies in the hands of the government.

    解决良策掌握在政府手中。

  • ...a remedy for economic ills.

    解决经济问题的良方妙策

SYN solution, relief, redress, antidote  
2 N-COUNT 药品;治疗方法 A remedy is something that is intended to cure you when you are ill or in pain. 药品;治疗方法

  • ...natural remedies to help overcome winter infections.

    有助于治疗冬季传染病的自然疗法

SYN cure, treatment, specific, medicine  
3 VERB 纠正;补救;矫正 If you remedy something that is wrong or harmful, you correct it or improve it. 纠正;补救;矫正

  • A great deal has been done internally to remedy the situation. [VERB noun]

    已经做了很多内部工作对这种情形进行补救。

SYN put right, redress, rectify, reform
