portend
pɔːʳtend pɔrˈtɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense portends, present participle portending, past tense , past participle portended
VERB 预示；预兆；预告 If something portends an event or occurrence, it indicates that it is likely to happen in the future. 预示；预兆；预告 [formal]

  • The change did not portend a basic improvement in social conditions. [VERB noun]

    该变化没有预示社会状况的根本改观。

SYN foretell, promise, threaten, indicate
