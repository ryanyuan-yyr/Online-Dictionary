hash
hæʃ ˈhæʃˌmɑːk hæʃ
1 PHRASE 把…弄糟;搞砸 If you make a hash of a job or task, you do it very badly. 把…弄糟;搞砸 [informal]

  • The Government made a total hash of things and squandered a small fortune.

    政府把事情搞砸了，还白白浪费了一大笔钱。

  • Watson had made a thorough hash of it.

    沃森把这事儿弄得一团糟。

SYN mess up, muddle, bungle, botch  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft n NOUN] 蔬菜肉丁;肉沫马铃薯 Hash is a dish made from meat cut into small lumps and fried with other ingredients such as onions or potato. 蔬菜肉丁;肉沫马铃薯

  • ...corned beef hash.

    腌牛肉烘土豆泥

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] （电话机按键中或计算机键盘上的）#号 A hash is the sign '#', found on telephone keypads and computer keyboards. （电话机按键中或计算机键盘上的）#号 [mainly British, spoken]
4 N-UNCOUNT 同hashish Hash is hashish . 同hashish [informal]
