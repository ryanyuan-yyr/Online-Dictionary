strongman
strɒŋgmæn strɔːŋ- strɔŋˈmæn
Word forms: plural strongmen
N-COUNT 铁腕人物;独裁者 If you refer to a male political leader as a strongman, you mean that he has great power and control over his country, although his methods may sometimes be violent or morally wrong. 铁腕人物;独裁者 [journalism]

  • He was a military strongman who ruled the country after a coup.

    他是个军事独裁者，在一场军事政变后成为这个国家的统治者。
