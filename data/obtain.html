obtain
ɒbteɪn əbˈteɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense obtains, present participle obtaining, past tense , past participle obtained
1 VERB 得到；获得;实现 To obtain something means to get it or achieve it. 得到；获得;实现 [formal]

  • Evans was trying to obtain a false passport and other documents. [VERB noun]

    埃文斯正试图取得一本假护照和其他证件。

  • The perfect body has always been difficult to obtain. [VERB noun]

    完美的身材总是很难拥有。

SYN achieve, get, gain, realize  
2 VERB 存在；流行；通行 If a situation obtains, it exists. 存在；流行；通行 [formal]

  • The longer this situation obtains, the more extensive the problems become. [VERB]

    这种局面持续的时间越长，问题波及的范围就越广。

SYN prevail, hold, stand, exist
