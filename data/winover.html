win over
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 win round
PHRASAL VERB 赢得…的支持;说服;把…争取过来 If you win someone over or win them round, you persuade them to support you or agree with you. 赢得…的支持;说服;把…争取过来

  • He has won over a significant number of the left-wing deputies. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他争取到了相当数量的左翼代表的支持。

  • They still hope to win him round. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们仍然希望把他争取过来。
