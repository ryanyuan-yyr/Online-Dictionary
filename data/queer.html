queer
kwɪəʳ kwɪr
Word forms: comparative queerer, superlative queerest, plural queers
1 ADJ-GRADED 古怪的;奇特的;异常的 Something that is queer is strange. 古怪的;奇特的;异常的 [old-fashioned]

  • If you ask me, there's something a bit queer going on.

    要是你问我的话，我觉得有点儿不太对劲。

SYN strange, odd, funny, unusual  
2 N-COUNT 同性恋男子 People sometimes call homosexual men queers . 同性恋男子 [informal, offensive]
Queer is also an adjective.

  • ...queer men.

    同性恋男子

3 ADJ [ADJ n] (某些同性恋者用语)同性恋的 Queer means relating to homosexual people, and is used by some homosexuals. (某些同性恋者用语)同性恋的

  • ...contemporary queer culture.

    当代同性恋文化

  • ...queer activism.

    同性恋激进主义
