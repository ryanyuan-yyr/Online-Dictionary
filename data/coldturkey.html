cold turkey
N-UNCOUNT (吸毒成瘾者)突然戒毒时的不适;戒毒反应 Cold turkey is the unpleasant physical reaction that people experience when they suddenly stop taking a drug that they have become addicted to. (吸毒成瘾者)突然戒毒时的不适;戒毒反应 [informal]

  • The quickest way to get her off the drug was to let her go cold turkey.

    要使她摆脱毒品，最快的方法是让她彻底戒毒。
