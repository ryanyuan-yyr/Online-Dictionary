downhearted
daʊnhɑːʳtɪd ˌdaʊnˈhɑːtɪd ˈdaʊnˌhɑrtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 情绪低落的;垂头丧气的;沮丧的 If you are downhearted, you are feeling sad and discouraged. 情绪低落的;垂头丧气的;沮丧的

  • Max sighed, sounding even more downhearted.

    马克斯叹了口气，听上去更沮丧了。

  • Don't be too downhearted. There's always a way.

    别太悲观了，总有办法的。

SYN dejected, sad, depressed, unhappy
