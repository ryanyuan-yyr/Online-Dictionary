tamper
tæmpəʳ ˈtæmpər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tampers, present participle tampering, past tense , past participle tampered
VERB 干预;篡改 If someone tampers with something, they interfere with it or try to change it when they have no right to do so. 干预;篡改

  • I don't want to be accused of tampering with the evidence. [VERB + with]

    我不希望被人控告篡改证据。

  • He found his computer had been tampered with. [VERB with noun]

    他发现他的电脑被人动过手脚。

SYN influence, fix [informal] , rig, corrupt
