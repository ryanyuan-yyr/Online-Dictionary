cut across
PHRASAL VERB 影响，涉及(两个或多个群体) If an issue or problem cuts across the division between two or more groups of people, it affects or matters to people in all the groups. 影响，涉及(两个或多个群体)

  • The problem cuts across all socioeconomic lines and affects all age groups. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该问题涉及社会经济的方方面面，并影响到所有年龄段的人。

  • School crime and violence cuts across urban, rural and suburban areas. [VERB PREP. noun]

    城市、乡村和郊区都出现了校园犯罪和校园暴力现象。
