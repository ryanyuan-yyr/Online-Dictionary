polyp
pɒlɪp ˈpɑlɪp
Word forms: plural polyps
1 N-COUNT （人体内、尤指鼻内生长的）息肉 A polyp is a small unhealthy growth on a surface inside your body, especially inside your nose. （人体内、尤指鼻内生长的）息肉

  • It takes ten years or more for a benign polyp to turn malignant.

    良性息肉转为恶性要经过10年或更长的时间。

2 N-COUNT 水螅（体） A polyp is a small animal that lives in the sea. It has a hollow body like a tube and long parts called tentacles around its mouth. 水螅（体）
