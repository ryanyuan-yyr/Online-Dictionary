insensitive
ɪnsensɪtɪv ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 不敏感的;感觉迟钝的;麻木不仁的 If you describe someone as insensitive, you are criticizing them for being unaware of or unsympathetic to other people's feelings. 不敏感的;感觉迟钝的;麻木不仁的 [disapproval]

  • I feel my husband is very insensitive about my problem. [+ about]

    我感觉丈夫对我的问题麻木不仁。

SYN unfeeling, indifferent, unconcerned, uncaring  
insensitivity

  • I was ashamed at my insensitivity towards her.

    我为自己忽视她的感受而羞愧。

2 ADJ-GRADED 漠视的;不在乎的;毫不在意的 Someone who is insensitive to a situation or to a need does not think or care about it. 漠视的;不在乎的;毫不在意的

  • Women's and Latino organizations that say he is insensitive to civil rights. [+ to]

    指责他漠视人权的女性组织与拉美裔组织

insensitivity

  • ...insensitivity to the environmental consequences.

    对环境后果的毫不顾及

3 ADJ (对体感)没有感觉的,麻木的 Someone who is insensitive to a physical sensation is unable to feel it. (对体感)没有感觉的,麻木的

  • He had become insensitive to cold. [+ to]

    他对寒冷已经没有感觉了。
