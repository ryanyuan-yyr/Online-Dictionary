immaturity
1 N-UNCOUNT 不成熟Immaturity is the state of being not yet completely grown or fully developed. 不成熟

  • Photographs of the boy showed his physical immaturity.

    该男孩的那些照片显示了他生理上的不成熟。

2 N-UNCOUNT 幼稚Immaturity is a lack of the qualities and behaviour that you would expect from a sensible adult. 幼稚

  • I am disgusted by the immaturity and stupidity presented in this column.

    我对这个专栏的幼稚和愚蠢感到厌恶。
