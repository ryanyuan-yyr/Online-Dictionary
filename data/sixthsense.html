sixth sense
N-SING 第六感;直觉 If you say that someone has a sixth sense, you mean that they seem to have a natural ability to know about things before other people, or to know things that other people do not know. 第六感;直觉

  • The interesting thing about O'Reilly is his sixth sense for finding people who have good ideas. [+ for]

    奥赖利的有趣之处在于，他能够凭直觉找到有好点子的人。

SYN intuition, second sight, clairvoyance, feyness
