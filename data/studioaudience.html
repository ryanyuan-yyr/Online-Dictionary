studio audience ˈstjuːdɪəʊ ˈɔːdɪəns
Word forms: plural studio audiences
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 演播室观众;录制现场的观众 A studio audience is a group of people who are in a television or radio studio watching while a programme is being made, so that their clapping, laughter, or questions are recorded on the programme. 演播室观众;录制现场的观众
