bicycle
baɪsɪkəl ˈbaɪˌsɪkəl ˈbaɪsɪkəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural bicycles, present participle bicycling, past tense , past participle bicycled
1 N-COUNT 自行车；脚踏车 A bicycle is a vehicle with two wheels which you ride by sitting on it and pushing two pedals with your feet. You steer it by turning a bar that is connected to the front wheel. 自行车；脚踏车
SYN bike, cycle, racer, mountain bike  
2 VERB 骑自行车 If you bicycle somewhere, you cycle there. 骑自行车 [old-fashioned]

  • I bicycled on towards the sea. [VERB adv./prep.]

    我继续骑着自行车朝海边走。

SYN cycle
