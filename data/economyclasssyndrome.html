economy-class syndrome
N-UNCOUNT 经济舱综合征（尤指长距离飞行造成腿部血栓进入肺部的严重病症） Economy-class syndrome is a serious medical condition caused by blood clots in the legs moving up to the lungs; used especially in connection with long-haul flights. 经济舱综合征（尤指长距离飞行造成腿部血栓进入肺部的严重病症）

  • Lemon juice can help to prevent economy-class syndrome by improving blood circulation.

    柠檬汁能够改善血液循环，有助于预防经济舱综合征。
