novice
nɒvɪs ˈnɑvɪs
Word forms: plural novices
1 N-COUNT [NOUN n] 新手;生手;初学者 A novice is someone who has been doing a job or other activity for only a short time and so is not experienced at it. 新手;生手;初学者

  • I'm a novice at these things, Lieutenant. You're the professional. [+ at]

    中尉，做这些事情我是新手，你是行家。

  • As a novice writer, this is something I'm interested in.

    作为初涉写作的人，我对此很感兴趣。

SYN beginner, pupil, amateur, newcomer  
2 N-COUNT 见习修士(或修女) In a monastery or convent, a novice is a person who is preparing to become a monk or nun. 见习修士(或修女)
