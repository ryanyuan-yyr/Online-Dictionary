continue
kəntɪnjuː kənˈtɪnju
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense continues, present participle continuing, past tense , past participle continued
1 VERB 继续;持续 If someone or something continues to do something, they keep doing it and do not stop. 继续;持续

  • I hope they continue to fight for equal justice after I'm gone. [VERB to-infinitive]

    我希望我走了以后他们能继续为司法公正而战。

  • Interest rates continue to fall. [VERB to-infinitive]

    利率持续下调。

  • They are determined to continue working when they reach retirement age. [VERB verb-ing]

    他们决定在达到退休年龄后继续工作。

  • There is no reason why you should not continue with any sport or activity you already enjoy. [VERB + with]

    没有理由停下你一直在进行的体育运动或活动。

2 V-ERG 延续;继续 If something continues or if you continue it, it does not stop happening. 延续;继续

  • He insisted that the conflict would continue until conditions were met for a ceasefire. [VERB]

    他坚持认为除非满足停火条件，否则冲突会继续下去。

  • But as the investigation continued, the plot began to thicken. [VERB]

    但是随着调查的继续深入，情况开始变得复杂起来。

  • Outside the building people continue their vigil, huddling around bonfires. [VERB noun]

    在房子外面，人们挤在篝火旁边，继续守夜。

  • ...the continued existence of a species. [VERB-ed]

    一个物种的延续

SYN go on, advance, progress, proceed  
3 VERB (中断后)继续，再开始 If you continue with something, you start doing it again after a break or interruption. (中断后)继续，再开始

  • I went up to my room to continue with my packing. [VERB + with]

    我走进房间，继续打包。

  • She looked up for a moment, then continued drawing. [VERB verb-ing]

    她抬头看了一下，然后继续画画。

SYN resume, return to, take up again, proceed  
4 V-ERG (使)(中断后)继续 If something continues or if you continue it, it starts again after a break or interruption. (使)(中断后)继续

  • He denies 18 charges. The trial continues today. [VERB]

    他否认了18项指控。审判今天继续。

  • Once, he did dive for cover but he soon reappeared and continued his activities. [VERB noun]

    他确实一度销声匿迹，但是很快就重出江湖，继续他的各种活动。

SYN resume, return to, take up again, proceed  
5 VERB 继续说;接着说 If you continue, you begin speaking again after a pause or interruption. 继续说;接着说

  • 'You have no right to intimidate this man,' Alison continued. [VERB with quote]

    “你没有权利威胁这个人，”艾莉森接着说。

  • Tony drank some coffee before he continued. [VERB]

    托尼喝了点咖啡继续说。

  • Please continue. [VERB]

    请接着讲。

6 VERB 继续(做某项工作或处于某种状态) If you continue as something or continue in a particular state, you remain in a particular job or state. 继续(做某项工作或处于某种状态)

  • He had hoped to continue as a full-time career officer. [VERB + as]

    他曾经希望继续当一名全职的职业事务员。

  • For ten days I continued in this state. [VERB prep.]

    我的这种状况持续了10天。

SYN remain, last, stay, rest  
7 VERB 继续走;继续旅行 If you continue in a particular direction, you keep walking or travelling in that direction. 继续走;继续旅行

  • He continued rapidly up the path, not pausing until he neared the Chapter House. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他匆匆地继续沿着这条路走，一直快到牧师会礼堂时才停下来。

8 VERB (路等)延伸 If a road or path continues somewhere, it goes there after the place you have mentioned. (路等)延伸

  • The main road continues towards Viterbo before turning right to Bolsena. [VERB prep./adv.]

    大路一直延伸到维泰博，之后右转通向博尔塞纳。
