house lights
ˈhaʊsˌlaɪts
N-PLURAL （剧院或影院的）观众席照明灯 In a theatre or cinema, when the house lights dim or go down, the lights where the audience sits are switched off. When the house lights come up, the lights are switched on. （剧院或影院的）观众席照明灯

  • The house lights dimmed, flickered and went out.

    观众席的灯光暗了下来，闪了几下，然后就熄灭了。
