grapevine
greɪpvaɪn ˈgreɪpˌvaɪn
Word forms: plural grapevines
1 N-SING 小道消息;传闻 If you hear or learn something on the grapevine, you hear it or learn it in casual conversation with other people. 小道消息;传闻

  • He'd doubtless heard rumours on the grapevine.

    他无疑是听到了小道消息。

  • I had heard through the grapevine that he was quite critical of what we were doing.

    我听到有传言说他对我们在做的工作非常不满。

2 N-COUNT 葡萄藤 A grapevine is a climbing plant on which grapes grow. 葡萄藤
