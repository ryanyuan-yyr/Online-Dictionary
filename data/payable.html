payable
peɪəbəl ˈpeɪəbəl
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 应支付的；可支付的 If an amount of money is payable, it has to be paid or it can be paid. 应支付的；可支付的 [Also + to]

  • Purchase tax was not payable on goods for export. [+ on]

    出口商品不需要交购买税。

2 ADJ [n ADJ] （支票或邮政汇票）应付予…的 If a cheque or postal order is made payable to you, it has your name written on it to indicate that you are the person who will receive the money. （支票或邮政汇票）应付予…的

  • Write, enclosing a cheque made payable to Cobuild Limited. [+ to]

    写封信，附上一张支付给Cobuild有限公司的支票。
