weirdo
wɪəʳdoʊ ˈwɪədɪ ˈwɪrdoʊ
Word forms: plural weirdos
N-COUNT 怪人;怪物 If you describe someone as a weirdo, you disapprove of them because they behave in an unusual way which you find difficult to understand or accept. 怪人;怪物 [informal, mainly spoken, disapproval]
SYN eccentric, nut [slang] , freak [informal] , flake [slang , mainly US]
