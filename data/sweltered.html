swelter
sweltəʳ ˈswɛltər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense swelters, present participle sweltering, past tense , past participle sweltered
VERB 热得难受;热得发昏 If you swelter, you are very uncomfortable because the weather is extremely hot. 热得难受;热得发昏

  • They sweltered in temperatures rising to a hundred degrees. [VERB]

    接近华氏 100 度的高温让他们感觉酷热难耐。

  • Fred and Volodia sweltered at night in the stuffy, crowded cabins. [VERB]

    弗雷德和博洛迪亚晚上挤在不通风的船舱里热得要命。
