burgundy
bɜːʳgəndi
Word forms: plural burgundies
1 COLOR 紫红色Burgundy is used to describe things that are purplish-red in colour. 紫红色

  • He was wearing a burgundy polyester jacket.

    他穿着一件紫红色聚酯纤维夹克。

  • ...burgundy-coloured armchairs.

    ...紫红色扶手椅。

2 N-MASS 勃艮第葡萄酒Burgundy is a type of wine. It can be white or red in colour and comes from the region of France called Burgundy. 勃艮第葡萄酒

  • ...a bottle of white burgundy.

    ...一瓶勃艮第白葡萄酒。
