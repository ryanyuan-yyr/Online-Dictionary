grating
greɪtɪŋ ˈgreɪtɪŋ
Word forms: plural gratings
1 N-COUNT 栅栏；格子；格栅 A grating is a flat metal frame with rows of bars across it, which is fastened over a window or over a hole in a wall or the ground. 栅栏；格子；格栅

  • ...an open grating in the sidewalk.

    人行道上敞着的下水道格栅

SYN grille, grid, grate, lattice  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （声音）刺耳的 A grating sound is harsh and unpleasant. （声音）刺耳的

  • She recognized the grating voice of Dr. Sarnoff.

    她听出了萨尔诺夫博士刺耳的嗓音。

SYN irritating, grinding, harsh, annoying
