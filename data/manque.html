manqué
mɒŋkeɪ mɑ̃ke ˈmɒŋkeɪ -keɪ mɑ̃ˈkeɪ
ADJ [n ADJ] 未实现愿望的；遭受挫折的 You use manqué to describe someone who has never had the type of job indicated, although they had the ability for it or wanted it. 未实现愿望的；遭受挫折的

  • ...his inescapable feeling that he is a great actor manqué.

    ...他不禁感觉到自己有能力成为一名伟大的演员。
