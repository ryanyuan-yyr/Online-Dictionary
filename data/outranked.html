outrank
aʊtræŋk ˈaʊtˈræŋk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outranks, present participle outranking, past tense , past participle outranked
VERB 级别高于…；职位在…之上 If one person outranks another person, he or she has a higher position or grade within an organization than the other person. 级别高于…；职位在…之上

  • The most junior executive officer outranked the senior engineer officer aboard ship. [VERB noun]

    在舰船上，级别最低的副舰长也位列级别最高的轮机官之上。
