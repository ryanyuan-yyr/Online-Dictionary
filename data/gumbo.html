gumbo
gʌmboʊ ˈgʌmboʊ
Word forms: plural gumbos
1 N-VAR 秋葵汤（美国南部用肉或鱼以及秋葵荚做的汤或炖菜） Gumbo is a type of soup or stew from the southern United States. It can be made with meat or fish, and usually contains okra. 秋葵汤（美国南部用肉或鱼以及秋葵荚做的汤或炖菜）
2 N-UNCOUNT （美国某些地方）同okra In parts of the United States, gumbo is another name for okra. （美国某些地方）同okra
