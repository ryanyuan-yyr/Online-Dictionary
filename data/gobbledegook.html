gobbledygook
gɒbəldiguːk ˈgɑbəldiˌgʊk ˈgɑbəldəgʊk ˈgɑbəldiˌguk ˈgɑbəldəˌguk
(also gobbledegook)
N-UNCOUNT 废话；官样文章；天书 If you describe a speech or piece of writing as gobbledygook, you are criticizing it for seeming like nonsense and being very technical or complicated. 废话；官样文章；天书 [informal, disapproval]

  • When he asked questions, the answers came back in Wall Street gobbledygook.

    他但凡提出疑问，得到的回答全是华尔街的官样文章。
