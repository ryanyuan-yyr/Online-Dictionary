ponce around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 ponce about
PHRASAL VERB 马虎拖拉；不务正业；游手好闲 If you say that someone is poncing around or poncing about, you mean that they are not doing something properly, quickly, or seriously. 马虎拖拉；不务正业；游手好闲 [British, informal, rude, disapproval]

  • I spent my working life poncing around on a beach instead of doing a proper job. [VERB PREP.]

    我把该用于工作的时间都在海滩上游手好闲掉了，而没去从事一份像样的工作。
