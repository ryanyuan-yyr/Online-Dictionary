affecting
əfektɪŋ əˈfɛktɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人感动的；动人的；激起怜悯（或悲伤）的 If you describe something such as a story or a piece of music as affecting, you think it is good because it makes you feel a strong emotion, especially sadness or pity. 令人感动的；动人的；激起怜悯（或悲伤）的 [literary, approval]

  • ...an affecting drama about a woman with a terminal illness.

    关于一位身患绝症的女人的感人戏剧

SYN emotionally moving, touching, sad, pathetic
