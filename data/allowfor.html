allow for
PHRASAL VERB 考虑到；将…计算在内；为…酌留余地 If you allow for certain problems or expenses, you include some extra time or money in your planning so that you can deal with them if they occur. 考虑到；将…计算在内；为…酌留余地

  • You have to allow for a certain amount of error. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你必须将一定量的误差考虑在内。

  • The Agency's budget simply did not allow for such a massive increase. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该机构的预算根本没有将如此大额的超支计算在内。

SYN take into account, consider, plan for, accommodate
