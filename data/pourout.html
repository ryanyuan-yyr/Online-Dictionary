pour out
1 PHRASAL VERB 斟，倒（饮料） If you pour out a drink, you put some of it in a cup or glass. 斟，倒（饮料）

  • Larry was pouring out four glasses of champagne. [VERB PREP. noun]

    拉里正把香槟酒倒入4只玻璃杯里。

  • Carefully and slowly he poured the beer out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他小心而缓慢地倒上啤酒。

2 PHRASAL VERB 倾吐，倾诉，毫无保留地诉说（思想、感情或经历） If you pour out your thoughts, feelings, or experiences, you tell someone all about them. 倾吐，倾诉，毫无保留地诉说（思想、感情或经历）

  • They have started pouring out their hearts and expressing their hopes and fears about the present situation. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们开始敞开心扉，诉说他们对目前局势的希望和担忧。

  • I poured my thoughts out on paper in an attempt to rationalize my feelings. [VERB noun PREP.]

    为了对自己的感情作出合理的解释，我把想法全都写在纸上。

SYN tell, reveal, relate, divulge
