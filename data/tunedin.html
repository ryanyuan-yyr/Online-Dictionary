tuned in
ADJ-GRADED 注意的;专注的 If someone is tuned in to something, they are aware of it and concentrating on it. 注意的;专注的

  • He's just not tuned in to the child's feelings. [+ to]

    他完全没有注意孩子的感受。

  • They were tuned in to their own needs and didn't care about the feelings of other people.

    他们只顾自己的需求，不在乎别人的感受。
