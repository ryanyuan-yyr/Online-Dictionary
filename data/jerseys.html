Word forms: plural jerseys
1 N-COUNT 针织套头毛衣；紧身套衫 A jersey is a knitted piece of clothing that covers the upper part of your body and your arms and does not open at the front. Jerseys are usually worn over a shirt or blouse. 针织套头毛衣；紧身套衫 [old-fashioned]

  • His grey jersey and trousers were sodden with the rain.

    他的灰色针织毛衫和长裤全都被雨水淋透了。

2 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] （尤用于制作女性服装的）平针织物，略有弹性的织物 Jersey is a knitted, slightly stretchy fabric used especially to make women's clothing. （尤用于制作女性服装的）平针织物，略有弹性的织物

  • Sheila had come to dinner in a black jersey top.

    希拉身着一件黑色针织上装来参加晚宴。
