lad
læd læd
Word forms: plural lads
1 N-COUNT; N-VOC 小伙子；年轻人；少年；男孩 A lad is a young man or boy. 小伙子；年轻人；少年；男孩 [informal]

  • When I was a lad his age I would laugh at the strangest things.

    我是他那样的小毛孩时，莫明其妙地就会发笑。

  • Come along, lad. Time for you to get home.

    来吧，小伙子。你该回家了。

2 N-PLURAL 伙计；兄弟；哥们儿 Some men refer to their male friends or colleagues as the lads . 伙计；兄弟；哥们儿 [British, informal]

  • ...a drink with the lads.

    与哥们儿一块喝一杯

  • The lads don't join the union because they're frightened of being victimized.

    那几个伙计没加入工会是因为害怕成为牺牲品。
