overbalance
oʊvəʳbæləns ˌoʊvərˈbæləns ˈoʊvərˌbæləns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overbalances, present participle overbalancing, past tense , past participle overbalanced
VERB 失去平衡；跌倒 If you overbalance, you fall over or nearly fall over, because you are not standing properly. 失去平衡；跌倒

  • He overbalanced and fell head first. [VERB]

    他站立不稳，摔了个脑壳着地。

SYN lose your balance, slip, tumble, tip over
