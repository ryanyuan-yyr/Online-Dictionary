blister
blɪstəʳ ˈblɪstər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense blisters, present participle blistering, past tense , past participle blistered
1 N-COUNT （皮肤上因烫伤或反复摩擦而起的）水疱 A blister is a painful swelling on the surface of your skin. Blisters contain a clear liquid and are usually caused by heat or by something repeatedly rubbing your skin. （皮肤上因烫伤或反复摩擦而起的）水疱
SYN sore, boil, swelling, cyst  
2 V-ERG （使）（皮肤）长水疱 When your skin blisters or when something blisters it, blisters appear on it. （使）（皮肤）长水疱

  • The affected skin turns red and may blister. [VERB]

    受感染的皮肤会变红，还有可能起水疱。

  • The sap of this plant blisters the skin. [VERB noun]

    这种植物的汁液会使皮肤起水疱。

  • ...pausing to bathe their blistered feet. [VERB-ed]

    停下来浸洗他们起了水疱的双脚

SYN swell
