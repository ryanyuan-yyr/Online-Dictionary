high-definition ˌhaɪdɛfɪˈnɪʃən ˈhaɪˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən
(also high definition)
（电视或技术）高清晰度的，高分辨率的 High-definition television or technology is a digital system that gives a much clearer picture than traditional television systems. （电视或技术）高清晰度的，高分辨率的

  • ...high-definition TV, with its sharper images and better sound.

    图像更为清晰、音效更为出众的高清电视

High definition is also a noun.

  • These games are more popular now that they are available in high definition.

    这些游戏现在有了高清版，更受欢迎了。
