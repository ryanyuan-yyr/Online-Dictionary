delirium
dɪlɪəriəm dɪˈlɪriəm
N-UNCOUNT 谵妄;神志不清;说胡话 If someone is suffering from delirium, they are not able to think or speak in a sensible and reasonable way because they are very ill and have a fever. 谵妄;神志不清;说胡话

  • In her delirium, she had fallen to the floor several times.

    她在神志不清的状态下几次摔倒在地上。

SYN madness, raving, insanity, lunacy
