narrow down
PHRASAL VERB 缩减;压缩;减小 If you narrow down a range of things, you reduce the number of things included in it. 缩减;压缩;减小 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • What's happened is that the new results narrow down the possibilities. [VERB PREP. noun]

    现有的情况是，新的结果缩小了可能性的范围。

  • I've managed to narrow the list down to twenty-three. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    我已设法把清单上的项目压缩到了23项。
