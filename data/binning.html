bin
bɪn bɪn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bins, present participle binning, past tense , past participle binned
1 N-COUNT 垃圾桶；垃圾箱 A bin is a container that you put rubbish in. 垃圾桶；垃圾箱 [mainly British]

  • He screwed the paper small and chucked it in the bin.

    他把纸揉成小团，扔进了垃圾桶。

SYN dustbin, bucket [Scottish] , garbage can, wastepaper basket  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 garbage can, trash can
2 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 箱子；储物箱；储藏箱 A bin is a container that you keep or store things in. 箱子；储物箱；储藏箱

  • ...a bread bin.

    面包箱

  • ...big steel storage bins.

    钢质大储藏箱

3 VERB 扔掉 If you bin something, you throw it away. 扔掉 [British, informal]

  • He decided to bin his paintings. [VERB noun]

    他决定把他的画扔掉。
