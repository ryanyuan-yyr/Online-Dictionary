unearthly
ʌnɜːʳθli ʌnˈɜrθli
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 怪异的；非自然的 You use unearthly to describe something that seems very strange and unnatural. 怪异的；非自然的

  • For a few seconds we watched the unearthly lights on the water.

    有那么几秒钟，我们注视着水面上那些奇异的光。

  • The sound was so serene that it seemed unearthly.

    那种声音听起来是那么宁静，仿佛天外之音。

SYN eerie, strange, supernatural, ghostly  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 过早的 If you refer to a time as an unearthly hour, you are emphasizing that it is very early in the morning. 过早的 [emphasis]

  • They arranged to meet in Riverside Park at the unearthly hour of seven in the morning.

    他们约定一大清早7点在河滨公园见面。

SYN unreasonable, ridiculous, absurd, strange  
3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （声音）可怕的，恐怖的，怪异的 An unearthly noise is unpleasant because it sounds frightening and unnatural. （声音）可怕的，恐怖的，怪异的

  • She heard the sirens scream their unearthly wail.

    她听到警报器发出可怕的尖叫声。
