riːkɑːst -kæst riːˈkɑːst riˈkæst riˈkɑst ˈriˌkæst ˈriˌkɑst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recasts, present participle recasting
The form recast is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. recast的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
1 VERB 重组;重塑;重新安排 If you recast something, you change it by organizing it in a different way. 重组;重塑;重新安排

  • The shake-up aims to recast IBM as a federation of flexible and competing subsidiaries. [VERB noun]

    改组的目标是把IBM重组为一个灵活的、相互竞争的子公司联盟。

recasting

2 VERB 重新选择（角色扮演者）;更换（演员） To recast an actor's role means to give the role to another actor. 重新选择（角色扮演者）;更换（演员）

  • Stoppard had to recast four of the principal roles. [VERB noun]

    斯托帕德只好更换4个主要角色的人选。
