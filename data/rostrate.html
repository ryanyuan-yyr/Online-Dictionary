rostrum
rɒstrəm ˈrɑstrəm
Word forms: plural rostrums, plural rostra/rɒstrə/
N-COUNT 演讲台;领奖台;指挥台 A rostrum is a raised platform on which someone stands when they are speaking to an audience, receiving a prize, or conducting an orchestra. 演讲台;领奖台;指挥台

  • As he stood on the winner's rostrum, he sang the words of the national anthem.

    他站在冠军领奖台上，唱起了国歌。

SYN stage, stand, platform, podium
