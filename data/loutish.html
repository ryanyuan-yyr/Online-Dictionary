loutish
laʊtɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （男子或男孩）粗野的，粗鲁的 If you describe a man or a boy as loutish, you are critical of them because their behaviour is impolite and aggressive. （男子或男孩）粗野的，粗鲁的 [disapproval]

  • I was appalled by the loutish behaviour.

    这种粗野行为令我大为震惊。

SYN oafish, rough, gross, coarse
