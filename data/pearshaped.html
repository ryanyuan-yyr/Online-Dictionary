pear-shaped
ˈpɛəʃeɪpt ˈpɛrˌʃeɪpt
1 ADJ 梨形的 Something that is pear-shaped has a shape like a pear. 梨形的

  • ...her pear-shaped diamond earrings.

    她的梨形钻石耳环

2 ADJ （尤指女子体形）梨形的（臀部比上半身宽大） If someone, especially a woman, is pear-shaped, they are wider around their hips than around the top half of their body. （尤指女子体形）梨形的（臀部比上半身宽大）
3 PHRASE 出毛病；出问题 If a situation goes pear-shaped, bad things start happening. 出毛病；出问题 [informal]

  • He feared his career had gone a bit pear-shaped.

    他害怕自己的事业已经出了点儿问题。
