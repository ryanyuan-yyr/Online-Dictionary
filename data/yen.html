yen
jen jɛn
Word forms: plural yen
1 N-COUNT 日元（日本货币单位） The yen is the unit of currency used in Japan. 日元（日本货币单位）

  • She's got a part-time job for which she earns 2,000 yen a month.

    她有一份月收入2,000日元的兼职工作。

The yen is also used to refer to the Japanese currency system.日元（日本货币体系）

  • ...sterling's devaluation against the dollar and the yen.

    英镑对美元和日元的贬值

2 N-SING [NOUN to-infinitive] 渴望;强烈的欲望;热望 If you have a yen to do something, you have a strong desire to do it. 渴望;强烈的欲望;热望

  • Mike had a yen to try cycling. [+ for]

    迈克非常想试试骑自行车。

  • He's a natural with any kind of engine but he has an unfortunate yen for speed.

    他天生对各种发动机就非常在行，但却过分追求速度。

SYN longing, desire, craving, yearning
