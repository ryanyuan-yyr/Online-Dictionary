two-piece
ˈtuˈpis
(also two piece)Word forms: plural two-pieces
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (尤指衣服)两件套的，两部分的 You can use two-piece to describe something, especially a set of clothing, that is in two parts. (尤指衣服)两件套的，两部分的

  • She was wearing a simple light-grey two-piece suit.

    她穿了身朴素的浅灰色两件式套装。

  • ...a two-piece bathing suit.

    两件套的泳装

  • …a two-piece telescopic truncheon made of aluminium.

    铝制伸缩式双节警棍

2 N-COUNT (上衣配裙子或裤子的)两件套女式套装 A two-piece is a woman's suit which consists of a jacket and a skirt or pair of trousers. (上衣配裙子或裤子的)两件套女式套装

  • …a woman in a check two-piece.

    身穿格子两件式套装的女子
