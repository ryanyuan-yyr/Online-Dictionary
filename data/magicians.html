magician
mədʒɪʃən məˈdʒɪʃən məˈdʒɪʃən
Word forms: plural magicians
1 N-COUNT 魔术师;变戏法的人 A magician is a person who entertains people by doing magic tricks. 魔术师;变戏法的人
2 N-COUNT 巫师;术士 In fairy stories, a magician is a person, usually a man, who has magic powers. 巫师;术士
3 N-COUNT [oft N of/with n] 奇才;能手 If you call someone a magician, you admire the skilful and exciting way they do something. 奇才;能手 [approval]

  • Bevan was a magician with words.

    贝文用词出神入化。

  • ...a magician of the keyboard.

    键盘高手

SYN miracle-worker, genius, marvel, wizard
