poof
pʊf pʊf puf
(also pouf)Word forms: plural poofs
1 N-COUNT 男同性恋者 A poof is a homosexual man. 男同性恋者 [British, informal, offensive]
2 EXCLAM （表示某事突然发生）噗的一声 Some people say poof to indicate that something happened very suddenly. （表示某事突然发生）噗的一声

  • They approach, embrace, and poof! they disappear in a blinding flash of light.

    他们走近、拥抱，然后噗的一下就在一道炫目的光亮中消失了。
