advocacy
ædvəkəsi ˈædvəkəsi
1 N-SING 拥护；提倡；主张 Someone's advocacy of a particular action or plan is their act of recommending it publicly. 拥护；提倡；主张 [formal]

  • I support your advocacy of free trade. [+ of]

    我支持你自由贸易的主张。

SYN recommendation, support, defence, championing  
2 N-UNCOUNT 辩护；辩护术 Advocacy is the way in which lawyers deal with cases in court. 辩护；辩护术 [formal]

  • Sir Peter would also like to see the current adversarial system of advocacy examined by the royal commission.

    彼得爵士也乐于见到王室委员会审查当前对抗式的辩护制度。

3 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] （试图影响政府或其他权力机构决策的）游说（团体或组织） An advocacy group or organization is one that tries to influence the decisions of a government or other authority. （试图影响政府或其他权力机构决策的）游说（团体或组织） [US]

  • Consumer advocacy groups are not so enthusiastic about removing restrictions on the telephone companies.

    保护消费者团体并不热衷于取消对电话公司的限制。
