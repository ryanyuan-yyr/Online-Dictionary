flight deck
(also flight-deck)Word forms: plural flight decks
1 N-COUNT (航空母舰上的)飞行甲板 On an aircraft carrier, the flight deck is the flat open surface on the deck where aircraft take off and land. (航空母舰上的)飞行甲板
2 N-COUNT (飞机的)驾驶舱 On a large aeroplane, the flight deck is the area at the front where the pilot works and where all the controls are. (飞机的)驾驶舱
