butterscotch
bʌtəʳskɒtʃ ˈbʌtərˌskɑtʃ
1 N-UNCOUNT 奶油硬糖 Butterscotch is a hard yellowish-brown sweet made from butter and sugar boiled together. 奶油硬糖
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 有奶油硬糖味的东西；黄褐色的东西 A butterscotch flavoured or coloured thing has the flavour or colour of butterscotch. 有奶油硬糖味的东西；黄褐色的东西
