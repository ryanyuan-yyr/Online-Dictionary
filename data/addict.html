addict
ædɪkt əˈdɪkt ˈædɪkt
Word forms: plural addicts
1 N-COUNT 吸毒成瘾者；瘾君子 An addict is someone who takes harmful drugs and cannot stop taking them. 吸毒成瘾者；瘾君子

  • He's only 24 years old and a drug addict.

    他只有24岁，却是个瘾君子。

SYN junkie [informal] , abuser, user [informal] , druggie [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 对…入迷的人;…迷 If you say that someone is an addict, you mean that they like a particular activity very much and spend as much time doing it as they can. 对…入迷的人;…迷

  • She is a TV addict and watches as much as she can.

    她是个电视迷，看起电视来没完没了。

SYN fan, lover, nut [slang] , follower
