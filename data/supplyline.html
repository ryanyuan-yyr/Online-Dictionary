supply line səˈplaɪ laɪn
Word forms: plural supply lines
N-COUNT (战时军需品的)补给线，运输线 A supply line is a route along which goods and equipment are transported to an army during a war. (战时军需品的)补给线，运输线

  • The bombing campaign appears aimed at cutting the supply lines between Germany and its army in occupied France.

    这次轰炸看起来意在切断德国和占领法国的德军部队之间的补给线。
