livestock
laɪvstɒk ˈlaɪvˌstɑk
N-UNCOUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] （牛、羊等）家畜，牲畜，牲口 Animals such as cattle and sheep which are kept on a farm are referred to as livestock. （牛、羊等）家畜，牲畜，牲口

  • The heavy rains and flooding killed scores of livestock.

    暴雨和大水淹死了许多牲口。

SYN farm animals, stock, sheep, cattle
