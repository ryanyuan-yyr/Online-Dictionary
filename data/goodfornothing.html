good-for-nothing ˈgʊdfərˈnʌθɪŋ
Word forms: plural good-for-nothings
ADJ [ADJ n] 懒散的；一无是处的 If you describe someone as good-for-nothing, you think that they are lazy or irresponsible. 懒散的；一无是处的

  • ...a good-for-nothing fourteen-year-old son who barely knows how to read and count.

    大字不识几个、数数要掰指头的14岁饭桶儿子

SYN worthless, useless, idle, irresponsible  
Good-for-nothing is also a noun.

  • ...lazy good-for-nothings.

    一无是处的懒人
