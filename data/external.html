external
ɪkstɜːʳnəl ɪkˈstɜːnəl ɛkˈstɜrnəl ɪkˈstɜrnəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 外部的;外面的;外来的 External is used to indicate that something is on the outside of a surface or body, or that it exists, happens, or comes from outside. 外部的;外面的;外来的

  • ...a much reduced heat loss through external walls.

    大幅降低的外墙热损失

  • ...internal and external allergic reactions.

    内外变态反应

externally

  • Vitamins can be applied externally to the skin.

    维生素可以外用于皮肤。

  • ...externally imposed conditions.

    外部施加的条件

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 对外的;与外国有关的 External means involving or intended for foreign countries. 对外的;与外国有关的

  • ...the commissioner for external affairs.

    对外事务专员

  • ...Jamaica's external debt.

    牙买加的外债

  • ...the republic's external borders.

    该共和国与外国的边界

SYN foreign, international, alien, exotic  
externally

  • ...protecting the value of the mark both internally and externally.

    维持马克在国内外的币值

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 外界的;外部的 External means happening or existing in the world in general and affecting you in some way. 外界的;外部的

  • ...a reaction to external events.

    对外界事件的反应

  • Such events occur only when the external conditions are favorable.

    这种事情只有在外部条件有利时才会发生。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] （专家等）外来的，外聘的 External experts, for example external examiners, come into an organization from outside in order to do a particular job fairly and impartially, or to check that a particular job was done properly. （专家等）外来的，外聘的 [mainly British]
externally

  • There must be externally moderated tests.

    一定要有外聘人员负责审核评分的考试。

5 PHRASE （药物）外用的 If medicine is for external use, it is intended to be used only on the outside of your body, and not to be eaten or drunk. （药物）外用的
