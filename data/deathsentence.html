death sentence
dɛθ ˈsɛntəns
Word forms: plural death sentences
N-COUNT 死刑（判决） A death sentence is a punishment of death given by a judge to someone who has been found guilty of a serious crime such as murder. 死刑（判决）

  • His original death sentence was commuted to life in prison.

    他最初的死刑被减为终身监禁。
