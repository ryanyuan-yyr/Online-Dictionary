mythic
mɪθɪk ˈmɪθɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 神话的;只存在于神话中的 Someone or something that is mythic exists only in myths and is therefore imaginary. 神话的;只存在于神话中的 [literary]

  • ...the mythic figure of King Arthur.

    亚瑟王这一传奇人物

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 很著名的;很重要的 If you describe someone or something as mythic, you mean that they have become very famous or important. 很著名的;很重要的

  • ...a team whose reputation has achieved mythic proportions.

    声名显赫的球队

  • His rapid rise to power has given him a near-mythic status in the industry.

    他迅速升任要职，这让他在业界近乎被神化了。
