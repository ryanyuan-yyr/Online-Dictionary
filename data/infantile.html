infantile
ɪnfəntaɪl ˈɪnfənˌtaɪl ˈɪnfəntɪl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 婴儿(期)的;幼儿(期)的 Infantile behaviour or illnesses are typical of very young children. 婴儿(期)的;幼儿(期)的 [formal]

  • ...infantile aggression.

    婴儿期的攻击行为

  • ...children with infantile eczema.

    患婴儿湿疹的儿童

2 ADJ-GRADED 幼稚的;孩子气的 If you accuse someone or something of being infantile, you think that they are foolish and childish. 幼稚的;孩子气的 [disapproval]

  • This kind of humour is infantile and boring.

    这种幽默既幼稚又无聊。

SYN childish, immature, puerile, babyish
