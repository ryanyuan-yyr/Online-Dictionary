pituitary gland pɪtjuːɪtri glænd -tuːɪteri -
Word forms: plural pituitary glands
N-COUNT [usually singular] 垂体腺；脑垂体 The pituitary gland or the pituitary is a gland that is attached to the base of the brain. It produces hormones which affect growth, sexual development, and other functions of the body. 垂体腺；脑垂体 [technical]
