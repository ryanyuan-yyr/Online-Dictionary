unrewarding
ʌnrɪwɔːʳdɪŋ ˌʌnrɪˈwɔːdɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 没有成就感的；无回报的 If you describe an activity as unrewarding, you mean that it does not give you any feelings of achievement or pleasure. 没有成就感的；无回报的

  • ...dirty and unrewarding work.

    既辛苦又没有成就感的工作

  • Listening to it in its entirety is also fairly unrewarding.

    把它完整地听下来后还是没多少收获。
