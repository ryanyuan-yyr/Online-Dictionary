childhood
tʃaɪldhʊd ˈtʃaɪldˌhʊd
Word forms: plural childhoods
1 N-VAR [oft poss NOUN, NOUN n] 童年;幼年时代 A person's childhood is the period of their life when they are a child. 童年;幼年时代

  • She had a happy childhood.

    她曾有过一个幸福的童年。

  • He was remembering a story heard in childhood.

    他在回忆儿时听过的一个故事。

  • ...childhood illnesses.

    儿童疾病

SYN youth, minority, infancy, schooldays
