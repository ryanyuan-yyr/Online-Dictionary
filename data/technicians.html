technician
teknɪʃən tɛkˈnɪʃən
Word forms: plural technicians
1 N-COUNT 技术员;技师 A technician is someone whose job involves skilled practical work with scientific equipment, for example in a laboratory. 技术员;技师

  • ...a laboratory technician.

    实验室技术员

2 N-COUNT 巧手;能人 A technician is someone who is very good at the detailed technical aspects of an activity. 巧手;能人

  • ...a versatile, veteran player, a superb technician.

    技术全面、经验丰富、技巧娴熟的运动能手
