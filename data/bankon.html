bank on
PHRASAL VERB 指望；依靠 If you bank on something happening, you expect it to happen and rely on it happening. 指望；依靠

  • The Berlin government is banking on the Olympics to save the city money. [VERB PREP. noun]

    柏林市政府指望着奥运会能给这座城市省钱。

  • 'He's not still there, I suppose?' — 'I wouldn't bank on that,' she said. [VERB PREP. noun]

    “我想他不在那儿了吧？”—“我觉得希望不大，”她说。

SYN rely on, trust (in), depend on, look to
