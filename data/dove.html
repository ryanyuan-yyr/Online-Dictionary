dove
dʌv doʊv dəʊv doʊv dʌv
Word forms: plural doves
Pronounced /dʌv/ for meanings 1 and 2, and /dəʊv/ for meaning 3. 义项 1 和义项 2 读作 /dʌv/，义项 3 读作 /dəʊv/。
1 N-COUNT 鸽(常作和平的象征) A dove is a bird that looks like a pigeon but is smaller and lighter in colour. Doves are often used as a symbol of peace. 鸽(常作和平的象征)
2See also:turtle dove 
3 N-COUNT (政治上的)温和派人物，鸽派 In politics, you can refer to people who support the use of peaceful methods to solve difficult situations as doves . (政治上的)温和派人物，鸽派

  • A clear split over tactics appears to be emerging between doves and hawks in the party.

    党内鸽派和鹰派在策略上似乎将要出现明显的分歧。

4 （美国英语中有时用作 dive 的过去式） In American English, dove is sometimes used as the past tense of dive. （美国英语中有时用作 dive 的过去式）
