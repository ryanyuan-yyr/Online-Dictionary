scale up
PHRASAL VERB 增大;增加;提高 If you scale up something, you make it greater in size, amount, or extent than it used to be. 增大;增加;提高 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Simply scaling up a size 10 garment often leads to disaster. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    只是将 10 号衣服改大的话结果往往会很不理想。

  • Since then, Wellcome has been scaling up production to prepare for clinical trials. [VERB PREP. noun]

    从那以后，威康公司一直在增加产量，为临床试验作准备。

SYN expand, extend, blow up, enlarge
