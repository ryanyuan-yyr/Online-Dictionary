stationary
steɪʃənri -neri ˈsteɪʃəˌnɛri
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 静止的;不动的 Something that is stationary is not moving. 静止的;不动的

  • Stationary cars in traffic jams cause a great deal of pollution.

    交通堵塞中静止不动的汽车产生了大量污染。

  • The train was stationary for 90 minutes.

    火车停了 90 分钟。

SYN motionless, standing, at a standstill, parked  
Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse stationary and stationery.

    注意不要混淆 stationary 和 stationery。
