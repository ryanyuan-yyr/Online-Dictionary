peacemaking
piːsmeɪkɪŋ
(also peace-making)
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 调停；调解 Peacemaking efforts are attempts to persuade countries or groups to stop fighting with each other. 调停；调解

  • ...the failure of international peacemaking efforts.

    国际调停努力的失败

  • Greece is a peacemaking, stabilizing force in the area.

    希腊是该地区调解冲突、稳定局势的一支力量。
