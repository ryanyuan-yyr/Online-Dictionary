appliqued əpliːkeɪd æplɪkeɪd
(also appliquéd)
ADJ 缝饰的；嵌花的；贴花的 Appliqued shapes or fabric are formed from pieces of fabric which are stitched on to clothes or larger pieces of cloth. 缝饰的；嵌花的；贴花的

  • ...a centrepiece of appliqued flowers and birds.

    摆在中心、缝着花鸟的装饰品

  • ...a magnificent appliqued bedspread.

    华美的嵌花床罩
