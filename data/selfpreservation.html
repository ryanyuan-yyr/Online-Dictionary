self-preservation
ˈsɛlfˌprɛzərˈveɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 自我保全;自我保护 Self-preservation is the action of keeping yourself safe or alive in a dangerous situation, often without thinking about what you are doing. 自我保全;自我保护

  • The police have the same human urge for self-preservation as the rest of us.

    警察和我们其他人一样，都有自我保护的人类本能。
