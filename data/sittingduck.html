sitting duck
ˈsɪtɪŋ dʌk
Word forms: plural sitting ducks
N-COUNT 易受攻击的目标;容易受骗者;容易被利用者 If you say that someone is a sitting duck, you mean that they are easy to attack, cheat, or take advantage of. 易受攻击的目标;容易受骗者;容易被利用者 [informal]

  • Nancy knew she'd be a sitting duck when she raised the trap door.

    南希明白，只要一打开天窗，她马上就会成为受攻击的目标。
