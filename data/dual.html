dual

ADJ [ADJ n] 两部分的;双重的;双的 Dual means having two parts, functions, or aspects. 两部分的;双重的;双的

  • ...his dual role as head of the party and head of state.

    他作为政党领袖和国家元首的双重身份

  • Rob may be entitled to dual nationality.

    罗布或许能拥有双重国籍。

SYN twofold, double, twin, matched
