borough

Word forms: plural boroughs
N-COUNT [NOUN n] 自治市镇；（大城市中的）行政区 A borough is a town, or a district within a large town, which has its own council. 自治市镇；（大城市中的）行政区

  • ...the South London borough of Lambeth.

    伦敦南部的兰贝斯区

  • ...the New York City borough of Brooklyn.

    纽约市的布鲁克林区

  • ...Kirklees Borough Council.

    柯克利斯自治区委员会

SYN district, area, community, quarter
