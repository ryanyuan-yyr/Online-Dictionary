narrow-minded
ˈnæroʊˈmaɪndɪd ˈnɛroʊˈmaɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED 思想狭隘的;心胸狭窄的 If you describe someone as narrow-minded, you are criticizing them because they are unwilling to consider new ideas or other people's opinions. 思想狭隘的;心胸狭窄的 [disapproval]

  • ...a narrow-minded bigot.

    偏执的盲从者

SYN intolerant, conservative, prejudiced, biased  
narrow-mindedness

  • It is unbelievable that as a result of this narrow-mindedness a group of people should suffer.

    就因为这种狭隘的思想造成某一群体的痛苦，这简直令人难以置信。
