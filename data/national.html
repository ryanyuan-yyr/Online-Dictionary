national
næʃənəl ˈnæʃənəl
Word forms: plural nationals
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 国家的;全国的 National means relating to the whole of a country or nation rather than to part of it or to other nations. 国家的;全国的

  • Ruling parties have lost ground in national and local elections.

    执政党在全国和地区选举中均败北。

  • ...major national and international issues.

    国内和国际重大问题

SYN nationwide, state, public, federal  
nationally

  • ...a nationally televised speech.

    全国电视讲话

  • Duncan Campbell is nationally known for his investigative work.

    邓肯·坎贝尔因其调查工作闻名全国。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 国民的;民族的 National means typical of the people or customs of a particular country or nation. 国民的;民族的

  • ...the national characteristics and history of the country.

    这个国家的民族特征和历史

  • Baseball is the national pastime.

    棒球是全民性的休闲运动。

SYN ethnic, social, native, racial  
3 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 国民;国人 You can refer to someone who is legally a citizen of a country as a national of that country. 国民;国人

  • ...a Sri-Lankan-born British national.

    出生于斯里兰卡的英国国民

SYN citizen, subject, resident, native
