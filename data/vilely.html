vile
vaɪl vaɪl
Word forms: comparative viler, superlative vilest
ADJ-GRADED 糟糕透顶的;讨厌的;恶劣的 If you say that someone or something is vile, you mean that they are very unpleasant. 糟糕透顶的;讨厌的;恶劣的

  • The weather was consistently vile.

    天气一直很糟糕。

  • She was in too vile a mood to work.

    她心情很差，没心思工作。
