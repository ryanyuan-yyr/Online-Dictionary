rosary
roʊzəri ˈroʊzəri
Word forms: plural rosaries
N-COUNT （尤指天主教徒念经时用的）念珠;玫瑰经 A rosary is a string of beads that members of certain religions, especially Catholics, use for counting prayers. A series of prayers counted in this way is also called a rosary. （尤指天主教徒念经时用的）念珠;玫瑰经

  • Estrada took a rosary from his tunic and ran the beads through the fingers of one hand.

    埃斯特拉达从法衣里取出一串念珠，用一只手捻动起来。

  • He's saying three rosaries a day.

    他现在每天念3次玫瑰经。
