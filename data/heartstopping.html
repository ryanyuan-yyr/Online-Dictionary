heart-stopping ˈhɑrtˌstɑpɪŋ
(also heartstopping)
1 ADJ [usu ADJ n] 令人心悸的; 惊心动魄的A heart-stopping moment is one that makes you anxious or frightened because it seems that something bad is likely to happen. 令人心悸的; 惊心动魄的

  • There was a heart-stopping moment when she lost her balance and fell backwards.

    她失去平衡向后倒的那一刻着实令人心悸。

2 ADJ [usu ADJ n] (事件或景观)令人震撼的A heart-stopping event or sight is very impressive or exciting. (事件或景观)令人震撼的

  • ...worth a visit for both the radical cooking and the heart-stopping sea view.

    ...那里的绝妙美食和震撼海景都值得一游。
