ham-fisted
ADJ-GRADED 笨手笨脚的;（尤指双手）笨拙的 If you describe someone as ham-fisted, you mean that they are clumsy, especially in the way that they use their hands. 笨手笨脚的;（尤指双手）笨拙的

  • They can all be made in minutes by even the most ham-fisted of cooks.

    即使是最笨手笨脚的厨师也能在几分钟之内把它们全部做好。

SYN clumsy [informal] , awkward, bungling, inept
