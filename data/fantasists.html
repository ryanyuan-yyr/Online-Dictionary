fantasist
fæntəzɪst ˈfæntəsɪst ˈfæntəsɪst ˈfæntəzɪst fænˈteɪʒɪst
Word forms: plural fantasists
N-COUNT 爱吹牛的人;牛皮大王 A fantasist is someone who constantly tells lies about their life and achievements in order to make them sound more exciting than they really are. 爱吹牛的人;牛皮大王

  • Singleton was a fantasist who claimed to have a karate blackbelt.

    辛格尔顿是个牛皮大王，他自称有一条空手道黑带。
