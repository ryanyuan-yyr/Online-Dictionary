grind down
PHRASAL VERB 虐待；折磨；欺压 If you say that someone grinds you down, you mean that they treat you very harshly and cruelly, reducing your confidence or your will to resist them. 虐待；折磨；欺压

  • There are people who want to humiliate you and grind you down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    有一些人会想要让你难堪，欺负你。

SYN oppress, suppress, harass, subdue
