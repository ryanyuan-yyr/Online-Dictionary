hitch up
PHRASAL VERB 扯起，拽起（衣服） If you hitch up a piece of clothing such as a skirt or pair of trousers, you pull it up into a higher position. 扯起，拽起（衣服）

  • She leapt from the car, hitched up her dress and sprinted down the road after him. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    她从车上跳下来，提起连衣裙，对他一路紧追不放。

  • He hitched his trousers up over his potbelly. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他将裤子向上提，盖住了自己的啤酒肚。

SYN pull up, tug, jerk, yank
