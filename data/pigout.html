pig out
PHRASAL VERB 大吃特吃；吃得过量 If you say that people are pigging out, you are criticizing them for eating a very large amount at one meal. 大吃特吃；吃得过量 [informal, disapproval]

  • I stopped pigging out on chips and crisps. [VERB PREP.]

    我不再暴食薯条和薯片了。

SYN overindulge, overdo it, eat too much, have a binge [informal]
