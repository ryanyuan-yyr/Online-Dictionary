gravy train
Word forms: plural gravy trains
N-COUNT [oft on the N] 轻松赚大钱的机会；美差；肥缺 If an organization or person earns a lot of money without doing much work, you can say that they are on the gravy train . 轻松赚大钱的机会；美差；肥缺 [journalism, disapproval]

  • We were disgusted when bosses awarded themselves a massive pay rise. How can they get on the gravy train, but ask us to take a wage freeze?

    看到老板为自己大幅加薪，我们感到十分气愤。他们凭什么轻轻松松捞油水却要求我们不涨工资？
