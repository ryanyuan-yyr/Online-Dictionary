interested
ɪntrestɪd ˈɪntrəstɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 感兴趣的;有兴趣的;关心的 If you are interested in something, you think it is important and want to learn more about it or spend time doing it. 感兴趣的;有兴趣的;关心的

  • I thought she might be interested in Paula's proposal. [+ in]

    我觉得她对葆拉的提议可能会感兴趣。

  • I think the young man is getting interested in gardening.

    我认为这个年轻人开始对园艺产生兴趣了。

  • I'd be interested to meet her.

    我有意见见她。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 有利害关系的;利益相关的;当事的 An interested party or group of people is affected by or involved in a particular event or situation. 有利害关系的;利益相关的;当事的

  • All the interested parties eventually agreed to the idea.

    所有利益相关方最终都同意了这一想法。

SYN involved, concerned, affected, prejudiced  
3 See also: self-interested 
Take care not to confuse interested and interesting. 注意不要混淆 interested 和 interesting。
