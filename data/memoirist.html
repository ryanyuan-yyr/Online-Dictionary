memoir
memwɑːʳ ˈmɛmwɑː ˈmɛmˌwɑr ˈmɛmˌwɔr
Word forms: plural memoirs
N-COUNT 传记;传略 A memoir is a book or article that you write about someone who you have known well. 传记;传略 [formal]

  • He has just published a memoir in honour of his captain.

    他刚刚出了一本传记来纪念他的队长。

SYN account, life, record, register
