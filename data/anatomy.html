anatomy
ənætəmi əˈnætəmi
Word forms: plural anatomies
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 解剖学 Anatomy is the study of the structure of the bodies of people or animals. 解剖学
SYN structure, build, make-up, frame  
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] （人的）身体 You can refer to your body as your anatomy. （人的）身体 [humorous]

  • The ball hit him in the most sensitive part of his anatomy.

    球击中了他身上最敏感的部位。

3 N-COUNT [oft with poss] （动物体的）结构 An animal's anatomy is the structure of its body. （动物体的）结构

  • He had worked extensively on the anatomy of living animals.

    他在活体解剖方面经验丰富。

SYN structure, build, make-up, frame  
4 N-SING [usually the NOUN of n] 剖析；分析 The anatomy of a subject or an idea is an examination or investigation of it. 剖析；分析

  • This was a troubling essay on the anatomy of nationhood.

    这是一篇剖析民族主义的让人不安的文章。

SYN examination, study, division, inquiry
