brink
brɪŋk brɪŋk
N-SING 边缘 If you are on the brink of something, usually something important, terrible, or exciting, you are just about to do it or experience it. 边缘

  • Their economy is teetering on the brink of collapse. [+ of]

    他们的经济徘徊在崩溃的边缘。

  • Failure to communicate had brought the two nations to the brink of war. [+ of]

    沟通不畅使这两个国家濒于战争的边缘。

SYN edge, point, limit, border
