hedgerow
hedʒroʊ ˈhɛdʒˌroʊ
Word forms: plural hedgerows
N-VAR （通常指乡间路边或田边的）树篱 A hedgerow is a row of bushes, trees, and plants, usually growing along a bank bordering a country lane or between fields. （通常指乡间路边或田边的）树篱

  • He crouched behind a low hedgerow.

    他蹲在一排低矮的树篱后面。

  • More than a hundred thousand miles of hedgerow have been lost since 1945.

    自1945年以来，已有10万多英里的树篱被砍掉了。
