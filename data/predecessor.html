predecessor
priːdɪsesəʳ pred- ˈprɛdəˌsɛsər ˈpridəˈsɛsər ˌ prɛdəˈsɛsər
Word forms: plural predecessors
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 前任；前辈 Your predecessor is the person who had your job before you. 前任；前辈

  • He maintained that he learned everything he knew from his predecessor Kenneth Sisam.

    他坚称他所知道的一切都获自前任肯尼思·赛瑟姆。

SYN previous job holder, precursor, forerunner, antecedent  
2 N-COUNT [usually with poss] （物件、机器的）前身 The predecessor of an object or machine is the object or machine that came before it in a sequence or process of development. （物件、机器的）前身

  • The car is some 40mm shorter than its predecessor.

    这辆轿车比原先的车型短了约40毫米。
