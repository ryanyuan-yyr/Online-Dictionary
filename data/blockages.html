blockage
blɒkɪdʒ ˈblɑkɪdʒ
Word forms: plural blockages
N-COUNT 堵塞物；障碍物；堵塞 A blockage in a pipe, tube, or tunnel is an object which blocks it, or the state of being blocked. 堵塞物；障碍物；堵塞

  • The logical treatment is to remove this blockage.

    合理的治疗方法就是清除堵塞物。

  • ...a total blockage in one of the coronary arteries.

    其中一根冠状动脉的完全阻塞

SYN obstruction, block, blocking, stoppage
