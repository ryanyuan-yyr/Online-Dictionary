reassess
riːəses ˌriːəˈsɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reassesses, present participle reassessing, past tense , past participle reassessed
VERB 重新考虑；再次决定；再次评价 If you reassess something, you think about it and decide whether you need to change your opinion about it. 重新考虑；再次决定；再次评价

  • I will reassess the situation when I get home. [VERB noun]

    我回家的时候会再看看情形。

  • Security in the area will have to be reassessed. [VERB noun]

    这个地区的治安将会被重新评估。

SYN reappraise, reconsider, weigh up again, get a feel for again
