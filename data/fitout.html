fit out
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 fit up
PHRASAL VERB 为…提供配备If you fit someone or something out, or you fit them up, you provide them with equipment and other things that they need. 为…提供配备 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We helped to fit him out for a trip to the Baltic. [V n P + for]

    我们帮他准备了去波罗的海旅行所需的物品。

  • I suggest we fit you up with an office suite. [V n P with n]

    我建议我们为你配备一间办公套房。
