market test
ˈmɑːkɪt tɛst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense market tests, present participle market testing, past tense , past participle market tested
1 N-COUNT 市场测试;销售实验 If a company carries out a market test, it asks a group of people to try a new product or service and give their opinions on it. 市场测试;销售实验 [business]

  • Results from market tests in the US and Europe show little enthusiasm for the product.

    美国和欧洲的市场测试结果显示，人们对该产品不是很感兴趣。

2 VERB 对…进行市场测试 If a new product or service is market tested, a group of people are asked to try it and then asked for their opinions on it. 对…进行市场测试 [business]

  • These nuts have been market tested and found to be most suited to the Australian palate. [be VERB-ed]

    这些坚果已经做过市场测试，结果发现它们最合澳大利亚人的口味。

  • The company uses the simulator to market test new designs. [VERB noun]

    这家公司使用模拟装置对新设计进行市场测试。

    他们从最初的市场测试中了解了许多信息。
