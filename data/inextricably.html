inextricably
ɪnekstrɪkəbəli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 分不开地;解不开地;紧密地 If two or more things are inextricably linked, they cannot be considered separately. 分不开地;解不开地;紧密地 [formal]

  • Religion was for her inextricably linked with life itself.

    宗教对她来说是与生活本身紧密联系在一起的。

SYN inseparably, totally, intricately, irretrievably
