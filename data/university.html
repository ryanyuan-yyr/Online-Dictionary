university
juːnɪvɜːʳsɪti ˌjunəˈvɜrsəti
Word forms: plural universities
N-VAR; N-IN-NAMES （综合性）大学 A university is an institution where students study for degrees and where academic research is done. （综合性）大学

  • Patrick is now at London University.

    帕特里克现在就读于伦敦大学。

  • They want their daughter to go to university, but they are also keen that she get a summer job.

    他们想要女儿上大学，但也希望她能打一份暑期工。

  • The university refused to let Dick Gregory speak on campus.

    这所大学不让迪克·格雷戈里在校园里发表演说。
