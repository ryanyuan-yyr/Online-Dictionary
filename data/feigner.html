feign
feɪn feɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense feigns, present participle feigning, past tense , past participle feigned
VERB 装作;假装;冒充 If someone feigns a particular feeling, attitude, or physical condition, they try to make other people think that they have it or are experiencing it, although this is not true. 装作;假装;冒充 [formal] [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • One morning, I didn't want to go to school, and decided to feign illness. [VERB noun]

    有天早晨我不想上学，于是决定装病。

  • 'Giles phoned this morning,' Mirella said with feigned indifference. [VERB-ed]

    “贾尔斯今早来电话了，”米雷拉装作若无其事地说。

SYN pretend, affect, assume, put on
