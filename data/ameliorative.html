ameliorate
əmiːliəreɪt əˈmiːljəˌreɪt əˈmiljəˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ameliorates, present participle ameliorating, past tense , past participle ameliorated
VERB 改善；使变好；减轻 If someone or something ameliorates a situation, they make it better or easier in some way. 改善；使变好；减轻 [formal]

  • Nothing can be done to ameliorate the situation. [VERB noun]

    做什么也无法使情况好转。

  • He expected me to do something to ameliorate his depression. [VERB noun]

    他希望我做点什么好让他不那么沮丧。

SYN improve, better, benefit, reform  
amelioration
