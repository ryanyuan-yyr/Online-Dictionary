tribalism
traɪbəlɪzəm ˈtraɪbəlˌɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 部落制度;部族制度 Tribalism is the state of existing as a tribe. 部落制度;部族制度

  • Apartheid used tribalism as the basis of its 'divide-and-rule' homeland policies.

    南非种族隔离制将部落制度作为其对国土“分而治之”的政策基础。

2 N-UNCOUNT 集团意识;部落意识;宗族主义 You can use tribalism to refer to the loyalties that people feel towards particular social groups and to the way these loyalties affect their behaviour and their attitudes towards others. 集团意识;部落意识;宗族主义 [disapproval]

  • His argument was that multi-party systems encourage tribalism.

    他的论点是多党制会助长集团主义。

  • …the evils of tribalism, disunity and disintegration.

    宗族主义、内讧和分裂的危害
