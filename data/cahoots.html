cahoots
kəhuːts kəˈhuts
PHRASE 与…密谋;与…勾结 If you say that one person is in cahoots with another, you do not trust the first person because you think that they are planning something secretly with the other. 与…密谋;与…勾结 [disapproval]

  • In his view they were all in cahoots with the police. [+ with]

    照他看,他们和警察都是一伙的。

  • I am not having you and him in cahoots against me.

    我不会让你和他勾结起来害我的。
