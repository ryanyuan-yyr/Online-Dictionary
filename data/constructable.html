construct
kənstrʌkt kənstrʌkt kɒnstrʌkt kənˈstrʌkt ˈkɑnˌstrʌkt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , 3rd person singular present tense constructs, present participle constructing, past tense , past participle constructed
The verb is pronounced /kən'strʌkt/. The noun is pronounced /'kɒnstrʌkt/. 动词读作 /kən'strʌkt/，名词读作 /'kɒnstrʌkt/。
1 VERB 建筑;建造;修建 If you construct something such as a building, road, or machine, you build it or make it. 建筑;建造;修建

  • The French constructed a series of fortresses from Dunkirk on the Channel coast to Douai. [VERB noun]

    法国人在从敦克尔刻到杜埃的英吉利海峡沿岸修筑了一系列要塞。

  • The boxes should be constructed from rough-sawn timber. [be V-ed + from/of/out of]

    这些箱子应该用粗锯木料来做。

  • They thought he had escaped through a specially constructed tunnel. [VERB-ed]

    他们认为他是通过一条专门修建的地道逃跑的。

SYN build, make, form, create  
2 VERB 构想，构筑，构思(思想、作品、体系等) If you construct something such as an idea, a piece of writing, or a system, you create it by putting different parts together. 构想，构筑，构思(思想、作品、体系等)

  • You will find it difficult to construct a spending plan without first recording your spending. [VERB noun]

    你会发现不先把自己的开销记录下来就很难制订支出计划。

  • He eventually constructed a business empire which ran to Thailand and Singapore. [VERB noun]

    他最终构筑起了一个延伸至泰国和新加坡的商业帝国。

  • The novel is constructed from a series of on-the-spot reports. [be V-ed + from/out of]

    小说是基于一系列现场报道构思而成的。

  • ...using carefully constructed tests. [VERB-ed]

    使用精心设计的测试

SYN create, make, form, set up  
3 N-COUNT 复杂的构想(或思想);观念 A construct is a complex idea. 复杂的构想(或思想);观念 [formal]

  • ...the underlying constructs (beliefs, philosophy, etc.) which influence action and behaviour.

    影响行动和行为的根本思想(信仰、处世哲学等)

  • It was a re-enactment of the same mental construct under which slavery was justified.

    这是为奴隶制辩护的同一思想观念的再现。

4 N-COUNT 建筑物;建造物;创造物 A construct is something that is built, made, or created. 建筑物;建造物;创造物 [formal]

  • As the flimsy constructs soared, the men paid out twine until the kites reached three or four thousand feet.

    随着这轻薄的东西向上飞升，这些人慢慢放出细绳，直到风筝飞到三四千英尺高。

  • The country was an artificial construct held together by force and intimidation for more than 70 years.

    这个国家是靠武力和恐吓维系达七十余年的人治政权。
