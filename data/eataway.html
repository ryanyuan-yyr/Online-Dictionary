eat away
PHRASAL VERB 侵蚀;使消耗殆尽 If one thing eats away another or eats away at another, it gradually destroys or uses it up. 侵蚀;使消耗殆尽 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Rot is eating away the interior of the house. [VERB PREP. noun]

    房子内部正逐渐受到侵蚀。

  • The recession is eating away at their revenues. [VERB PREP. + at]

    经济衰退正在消耗他们的财政收入。

SYN destroy, dissolve, crumble, rot
