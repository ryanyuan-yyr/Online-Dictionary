faceless
feɪsləs ˈfeɪslɪs
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 千篇一律的;无个性的 If you describe someone or something as faceless, you dislike them because they are uninteresting and have no character. 千篇一律的;无个性的 [disapproval]

  • Ordinary people are at the mercy of faceless bureaucrats.

    普通人的命运任凭那些平庸刻板的官僚们摆布。

SYN impersonal, remote, unknown, unidentified
