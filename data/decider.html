decider
dɪsaɪdəʳ
Word forms: plural deciders
1 N-COUNT 决胜场;决胜局 In sport, a decider is one of the games in a series, which establishes which player or team wins the series. 决胜场;决胜局 [British, journalism]

  • He won the decider which completed England's 3-2 victory over Austria.

    他取得了决赛局的胜利，帮助英格兰最终以 3:2 战胜奥地利。

2 N-COUNT (足球、曲棍球等比赛中）制胜的一球，决定胜负的一分 In games such as football and hockey, the decider is the last goal to be scored in a match that is won by a difference of only one goal. (足球、曲棍球等比赛中）制胜的一球，决定胜负的一分 [British, journalism]

  • McGrath scored the decider in Villa's 2-1 home win over Forest.

    在阿斯顿维拉队主场以 2 比 1 战胜诺丁汉森林队的比赛中，麦格拉思打入了制胜的一球。
