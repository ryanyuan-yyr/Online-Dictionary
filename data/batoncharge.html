baton charge
ˈbætən tʃɑːdʒ
(also baton-charge)Word forms: plural baton charges, present participle baton charging, past tense , past participle baton charged
N-COUNT （警察）挥舞警棍的冲锋 A baton charge is an attacking forward movement made by a large group of policemen carrying batons. （警察）挥舞警棍的冲锋 [British] [journalism]
Baton-charge is also a verb.

  • Police in riot gear baton-charged the crowd.

    暴乱中，警察挥舞着警棍对人群发起冲击。
