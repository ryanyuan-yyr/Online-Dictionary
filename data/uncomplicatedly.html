uncomplicated
ʌnkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd
ADJ 不复杂的；简单的 If you describe someone or something as uncomplicated, you approve of them because they are easy to deal with or understand. 不复杂的；简单的 [approval]

  • She is a beautiful, uncomplicated girl.

    她是一个美丽而率真的女孩。

  • ...good, fresh British cooking with its uncomplicated, direct flavours.

    新鲜可口的英式菜肴，简单而又原汁原味

SYN simple, clear, easy, straightforward [British]
