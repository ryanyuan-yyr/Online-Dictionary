front bench
Word forms: plural front benches
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 前座议员(英国议会中坐在前排座位的政府大臣和反对党要员的总称) In Britain, the front bench or people who sit on the front bench are members of Parliament who are ministers in the Government or who hold official positions in an opposition party. 前座议员(英国议会中坐在前排座位的政府大臣和反对党要员的总称)

  • Some of the Government front bench still believe our relationship with the US is paramount.

    一些政府前座议员仍然相信我们和美国的关系高于一切。

  • Both men had argued that their trips had been undertaken in their capacity as front bench spokesmen.

    两人都争辩说他们的旅行是在作为前座议员发言人的权限内进行的。
