wind-blown wɪnd bloʊn
(also windblown)
1 ADJ 被风吹动的;随风飘动的 You can use wind-blown to indicate that something has been blown from one place to another by the wind. 被风吹动的;随风飘动的

  • ...the wind-blown sand which forms the 60 ft dunes.

    形成了60英尺高的沙丘的风沙

2 ADJ-GRADED (头发等)被风吹乱的 If something such as someone's hair is wind-blown, it is untidy because it has been blown about by the wind. (头发等)被风吹乱的

  • His blond hair was windblown.

    他的一头金发被风吹得乱蓬蓬的。
