eggshell
egʃel ˈɛgˌʃɛl
(also egg shell)Word forms: plural eggshells
The spelling egg shell is also used for meaning 1. 拼写 egg shell 亦用于义项1。
1 N-VAR 蛋壳 An eggshell is the hard covering on the outside of an egg. 蛋壳
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 蛋壳漆的 Eggshell paint makes surfaces dull, not shiny, when it is painted on them. 蛋壳漆的

  • The walls had been painted an eggshell white.

    墙面用蛋壳漆刷成了白色。
