unrecognizable
ʌnrekəgnaɪzəbl -naɪz-
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 unrecognisable
ADJ 无法辨别的；难以辨认的 If someone or something is unrecognizable, they have become impossible to recognize or identify, for example because they have been greatly changed or damaged. 无法辨别的；难以辨认的

  • The corpses of the prisoners were nearly unrecognizable from the number of bullet wounds they'd received.

    囚犯的尸体上中枪太多，几乎无法辨认。

  • The new town would have been unrecognisable to the original inhabitants. [+ to]

    原来的居民可能会认不出这个崭新的城镇了。

SYN unidentifiable, disguised, incognito, changed
