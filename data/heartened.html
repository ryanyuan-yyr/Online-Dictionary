hearten
hɑːʳtən ˈhɑrtən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense heartens, present participle heartening, past tense , past participle heartened
VERB 鼓舞;使振奋 If someone is heartened by something, it encourages them and makes them cheerful. 鼓舞;使振奋

  • He will have been heartened by the telephone opinion poll published yesterday. [be VERB-ed]

    他会为昨天公布的电话民意调查结果感到鼓舞。

  • The news heartened everybody. [VERB noun]

    该消息让每个人都感到振奋。

SYN encourage, inspire, cheer, comfort  
heartened
