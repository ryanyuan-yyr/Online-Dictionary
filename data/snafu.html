snafu
snæfu snæˈfuː snæˈfu ˈsnæfu
Word forms: snafus
N-COUNT [usu adj N] 无秩序的; 混乱的If you describe a situation as a snafu, you mean that it is disorderly or disorganized and that it is usually like this. 无秩序的; 混乱的 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • Her internship was cut short because of a technical snafu.

    因为技术事故，她的实习期缩短了。

  • It may be the judge's fault. It may be a lawyer's fault. It may be a procedural snafu.

    这可能是法官的错误，或许是律师的问题，也可能是程序上的混乱。
