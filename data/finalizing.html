finalize
faɪnəlaɪz ˈfaɪnəlˌaɪz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense finalizes, present participle finalizing, past tense , past participle finalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 finalise
1 VERB (尤指经与他人讨论)最后确定，最终敲定 If you finalize something such as a plan or an agreement, you complete the arrangements for it, especially by discussing it with other people. (尤指经与他人讨论)最后确定，最终敲定

  • Negotiators from the three countries finalized the agreement in August. [VERB noun]

    8 月里三国谈判人员最终敲定了协议。

  • We are saying nothing until all the details have been finalised. [VERB noun]

    在所有细节最后确定下来之前我们无可奉告。

  • They have not finalized the deal with the government. [VERB noun + with]

    他们还没有和政府达成最终交易。

SYN complete, settle, conclude, tie up
