beginner
bɪgɪnəʳ biˈgɪnər bɪˈgɪnər
Word forms: plural beginners
N-COUNT 初学者；新手；生手 A beginner is someone who has just started learning to do something and cannot do it very well yet. 初学者；新手；生手

  • The course is suitable for beginners and advanced students.

    这门课程适合初学者和高级程度的学生。

  • I am a complete beginner to bird-keeping.

    在养鸟方面，我完全是一个新手。

SYN novice, student, pupil, convert
