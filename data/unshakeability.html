unshakeable
ʌnʃeɪkəbəl
(also unshakable)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不可动摇的；坚定的 If you describe someone's beliefs as unshakeable, you are emphasizing that they are so strong that they cannot be destroyed or altered. 不可动摇的；坚定的 [emphasis]

  • William has acquired an unshakeable belief in himself.

    威廉对自己有绝对的信心。

  • She had an unshakeable faith in human goodness and natural honesty.

    她坚信人的本性是善良诚实的。

SYN firm, staunch, resolute, sure
