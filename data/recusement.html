recuse
rɪkyuz rɪˈkjuːz rɪˈkjuz
Word forms: recuses, recusing, recused
V-T 要求撤换If someone such as a judge recuses himself or herself from a legal case, they state that they will not be involved in making decisions about the case, for example because they consider themselves to be biased. 要求撤换

  • If her husband became governor, Judge Rendell would have to recuse herself from cases involving the state.

    如果她的丈夫当选州长，伦德尔法官将不得不在本州的案件中将自己撤换出来。
