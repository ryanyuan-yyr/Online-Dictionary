ream
riːm rim
Word forms: plural reams
N-COUNT [usually plural] 大量；许多 If you say that there are reams of paper or reams of writing, you mean that there are large amounts of it. 大量；许多 [informal]

  • Their specific task is to sort through the reams of information and try to determine what it may mean. [+ of]

    他们的具体任务就是将大量信息分类并努力弄清它们的意思。

  • Kelly spent three hours going through reams of paper.

    凯利花了3个小时翻阅大量文件。
