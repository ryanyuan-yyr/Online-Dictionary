stomp
stɒmp stɑmp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stomps, present participle stomping, past tense , past participle stomped
VERB (常指因生气而)跺着脚走，迈着重重的步伐 If you stomp somewhere, you walk there with very heavy steps, often because you are angry. (常指因生气而)跺着脚走，迈着重重的步伐

  • He turned his back on them and stomped off up the hill. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他转身不理他们，噔噔噔地爬上了山。

  • He stomped out of the room. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他跺着脚走出了房间。
