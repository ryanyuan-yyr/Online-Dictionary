disembodied
dɪsɪmbɒdid
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 似脱离人而存在的;无实体的;脱离现实的 Disembodied means seeming not to be attached to or to come from anyone. 似脱离人而存在的;无实体的;脱离现实的

  • A disembodied voice sounded from the back of the cabin.

    从柜子后面传来了一个不见其人的声音。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 脱离肉体的;与躯体分离的 Disembodied means separated from or existing without a body. 脱离肉体的;与躯体分离的

  • ...a disembodied head.

    一颗与躯体分离的头颅

SYN bodiless, unbodied
