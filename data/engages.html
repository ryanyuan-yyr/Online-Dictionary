engage
ɪngeɪdʒ ɛnˈgeɪdʒ ɪnˈgeɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense engages, present participle engaging, past tense , past participle engaged
1 VERB 参与;从事 If you engage in an activity, you do it or are actively involved with it. 参与;从事 [formal]

  • I have never engaged in the drug trade. [VERB + in]

    我从未参与过毒品交易。

  • You can engage in croquet on the south lawn. [VERB in noun]

    你可以参加南边草坪上的槌球游戏。

2 VERB 吸引（人）;引起（注意、兴趣） If something engages you or your attention or interest, it keeps you interested in it and thinking about it. 吸引（人）;引起（注意、兴趣）

  • They never learned skills to engage the attention of the others. [VERB noun]

    他们从未学过吸引他人注意的技巧。

3 VERB 使参与，使参加（谈话） If you engage someone in conversation, you have a conversation with them. 使参与，使参加（谈话）

  • They tried to engage him in conversation. [VERB noun + in]

    他们试图跟他交谈。

  • We want to engage recognized leaders in discussion. [V n in n]

    我们想和公认的领袖们进行讨论。

SYN occupy, involve, draw, busy  
4 VERB 适应；（与…）建立密切关系 If you engage with something or with a group of people, you get involved with that thing or group and feel that you are connected with it or have real contact with it. 适应；（与…）建立密切关系

  • She found it hard to engage with office life. [VERB + with]

    她发现很难适应办公室生活。

  • She had vowed to go out of her way to engage with the Irish people at local community level. [VERB with noun]

    她已经发誓要努力和当地的爱尔兰人建立关系。

engagement

  • And she, too, suffers from a lack of critical engagement with the literary texts.

    她也因为对文学作品缺乏鉴赏力而非常痛苦。

5 VERB 任命;聘用;雇用 If you engage someone to do a particular job, you appoint them to do it. 任命;聘用;雇用 [formal]

  • We engaged the services of a recognised engineer. [VERB noun]

    我们雇用了一个公认的杰出工程师。

  • He had been able to engage some staff. [VERB noun]

    他已经能够雇用一些员工。

SYN employ, commission, appoint, take on  
6 V-ERG （使）啮合;（使）咬合 When a part of a machine or other mechanism engages or when you engage it, it moves into a position where it fits into something else. （使）啮合;（使）咬合

  • Press the lever until you hear the catch engage. [VERB]

    压下挡杆，直到听到挡挂好。

  • ...a lesson in how to engage the four-wheel drive. [VERB noun]

    如何使四轮驱动啮合的课程

SYN interlock, join, interact, mesh  
7 VERB 与…交战 When a military force engages the enemy, it attacks them and starts a battle. 与…交战

  • It could engage the enemy beyond the range of hostile torpedoes. [VERB noun]

    从这可以避开敌方鱼雷区对敌人发动进攻。

SYN begin battle with, attack, take on, encounter
