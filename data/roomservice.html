room service
N-UNCOUNT (旅馆的)客房服务，客房送餐服务 Room service is a service in a hotel by which meals or drinks are provided for guests in their rooms. (旅馆的)客房服务，客房送餐服务

  • The hotel did not normally provide room service.

    这家旅馆一般不提供客房送餐服务。

  • Let's call room service, I need a bottle of wine.

    我们叫客房服务吧，我要一瓶红酒。
