injustice
ɪndʒʌstɪs ɪnˈdʒʌstɪs
Word forms: plural injustices
1 N-VAR 非正义;不公平;不公正 Injustice is a lack of fairness in a situation. 非正义;不公平;不公正

  • They'll continue to fight injustice.

    他们将继续与不公平现象作斗争。

  • They resented the injustices of the system.

    他们怨恨制度的种种不公。

SYN unfairness, discrimination, prejudice, bias  
2 PHRASE 不公正地评判;不公正地对待 If you say that someone has done you an injustice, you mean that they have been unfair in the way that they have judged you or treated you. 不公正地评判;不公正地对待

  • The article does them both an injustice.

    那篇文章对他们俩都不公。
