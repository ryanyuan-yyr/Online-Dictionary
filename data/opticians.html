optician
ɒptɪʃən ɑpˈtɪʃən
Word forms: plural opticians
1 N-COUNT 眼镜商；验光配镜师 An optician is someone whose job involves testing people's sight, and making or selling glasses and contact lenses. 眼镜商；验光配镜师
2 N-COUNT 眼镜店 An optician or an optician's is a shop where you can have your eyes tested and buy glasses and contact lenses. 眼镜店

  • Some may need specialist treatment at the optician's.

    有些人可能需要到眼镜店进行专业验光。
