subcontractor
sʌbkəntræktəʳ -kɑːntræk- ˈsʌbˌkɑntræktər ˌ sʌbkənˈtræktər
(also sub-contractor)Word forms: plural subcontractors
N-COUNT 分包人;分包公司 A subcontractor is a person or firm that has a contract to do part of a job which another firm is responsible for. 分包人;分包公司 [business]

  • The company was considered as a possible subcontractor to build the aeroplane.

    该公司被视为能够承担该飞机制造任务的潜在分包商之一。
