frippery
frɪpəri ˈfrɪpəri
Word forms: plural fripperies
N-UNCOUNT 俗丽的装饰;无用的玩意儿;华而不实之物 If you refer to something as frippery, you mean that it is silly or unnecessary, and only done or worn for pleasure 俗丽的装饰;无用的玩意儿;华而不实之物 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • ...all the fripperies with which the Edwardian woman indulged herself.

    这个爱德华七世时期的女人披挂上身的所有那些俗丽的饰品

  • ...a sombre display, with no frills or frippery.

    没有多余或虚华的装饰的朴实的展示

SYN decoration, toy, ornament, trinket
