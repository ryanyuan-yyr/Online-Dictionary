peacemaker
piːsmeɪkəʳ pisˌmeɪkər
(also peace-maker,peace maker)Word forms: plural peacemakers
N-COUNT 调停者；调解人 You can describe an organization, a country or a person as a peacemaker when they try to persuade countries or people to stop fighting or quarrelling. 调停者；调解人

  • ...the Labour government's vision of acting as a peacemaker and mediator.

    工党政府充当调停者和斡旋者的想法

  • Daniel tried to act as peacemaker.

    丹尼尔想做调停人。

SYN mediator, appeaser, arbitrator, conciliator  
Quotations
Blessed are the peacemakers; for they shall be called the children of God Bible: St. Matthew
