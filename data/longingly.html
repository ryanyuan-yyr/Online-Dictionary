longingly
lɒŋɪŋli lɔːŋ-
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 渴望地；盼望地 If you look longingly at something you want, or think longingly about it, you look at it or think about it with a feeling of desire. 渴望地；盼望地

  • Claire looked longingly at the sunlit gardens outside the window. [+ at]

    克莱尔望着窗外洒满阳光的花园，内心充满渴望。
