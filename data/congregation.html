congregation
kɒŋgrɪgeɪʃən ˌkɑŋgrəˈgeɪʃən ˌ kɑngrəˈgeɪʃən
Word forms: plural congregations
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 教堂会众 The people who are attending a church service or who regularly attend a church service are referred to as the congregation . 教堂会众

  • Most members of the congregation begin arriving a few minutes before services.

    礼拜仪式开始前几分钟，大部分教堂会众陆续到来。

SYN parishioners, host, brethren, crowd
