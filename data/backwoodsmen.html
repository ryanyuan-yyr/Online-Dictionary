backwoodsman
bækwʊdzmən ˈbækˌwʊdzmən
Word forms: plural backwoodsmen
N-COUNT （尤指政客中）守旧的人，地方组织成员 Backwoodsmen are people, especially politicians, who like the old ways of doing things, or who are involved in an organization at a local level. （尤指政客中）守旧的人，地方组织成员 [mainly British]

  • ...Republican Party backwoodsmen in the United States.

    美国共和党地方成员
