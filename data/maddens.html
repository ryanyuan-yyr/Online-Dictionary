madden
mædən ˈmædən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense maddens, present participle maddening, past tense , past participle maddened
VERB 使极为恼火;使狂怒 To madden a person or animal means to make them very angry. 使极为恼火;使狂怒

  • He knew that what he was saying did not reach her. And the knowledge of it maddened him. [VERB noun]

    他知道她没有听他的话，这让他大为光火。

  • He is calmed by a lullaby, but maddened by any sudden loud noise. [VERB noun]

    他在催眠曲的安抚下平静下来，但任何突发的巨响又会让他大发脾气。

  • ...the maddened dogs. [VERB-ed]

    被激怒的狗群

SYN infuriate, irritate, incense, enrage
