soap
soʊp soʊp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense soaps, present participle soaping, past tense , past participle soaped
1 N-MASS 肥皂 Soap is a substance that you use with water for washing yourself or sometimes for washing clothes. 肥皂

  • ...a bar of lavender soap.

    一块薰衣草香皂

  • ...a large packet of soap powder.

    一大盒肥皂粉

  • ...a soap bubble.

    肥皂泡

2 VERB 用肥皂擦洗;往…上面抹肥皂 If you soap yourself, you rub soap on your body in order to wash yourself. 用肥皂擦洗;往…上面抹肥皂

  • She soaped herself all over. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    她把全身涂满肥皂。

3 N-COUNT 同 soap opera A soap is the same as a soap opera . 同 soap opera [informal]
