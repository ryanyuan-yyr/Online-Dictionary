misplace
mɪspleɪs mɪsˈpleɪs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misplaces, present participle misplacing, past tense , past participle misplaced
VERB 把…放在一时记不起的地方;(因记不起放在何处而)暂时丢失 If you misplace something, you lose it, usually only temporarily. 把…放在一时记不起的地方;(因记不起放在何处而)暂时丢失

  • He misplaces his reading glasses with such regularity that aides carry extras. [VERB noun]

    他老是记不起把自己阅读时用的放大镜放在什么地方，所以助手们会随身携带备用的。

  • Somehow the suitcase with my clothes was misplaced. [be VERB-ed]

    不知怎的，我忘了把装衣服的箱子放到哪里去了。

SYN lose, mislay, miss, be unable to find
