detonate
detəneɪt ˈdɛtənˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense detonates, present participle detonating, past tense , past participle detonated
V-ERG 引爆；起爆;（使）爆炸 If someone detonates a device such as a bomb, or if it detonates, it explodes. 引爆；起爆;（使）爆炸

  • France is expected to detonate its first nuclear device in the next few days. [VERB noun]

    法国预计将在几天后引爆其首枚核装置。

  • An explosive device detonated on the roof of the building. [VERB]

    一个爆炸装置在该楼的楼顶爆炸了。
