unutterable
ʌnʌtərəbəl ʌnˈʌtərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （尤指不好的事情）无以名状的，难以形容的，莫名的 You can use unutterable to emphasize that something, especially a bad quality, is great in degree or intensity. （尤指不好的事情）无以名状的，难以形容的，莫名的 [written, emphasis]

  • I am at the beginning of a new and unutterable loneliness.

    我再次陷入莫名的孤独寂寞中。

  • ...unutterable rubbish.

    一派胡言

SYN indescribable, extreme, overwhelming, unimaginable  
unutterably

  • I suddenly felt unutterably depressed.

    我突然感到莫名的惆怅。
