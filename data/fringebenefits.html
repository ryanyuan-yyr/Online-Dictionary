fringe benefit
Word forms: plural fringe benefits
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (用车等工资之外的)附加福利 Fringe benefits are extra things that some people get from their job in addition to their salary, for example a car. (用车等工资之外的)附加福利 [business]
2 N-COUNT 附带好处 The fringe benefits of doing something are the extra advantages which you get from it, although you may not have expected them and they were not the main reason for doing it. 附带好处

  • His support was one of the nicest fringe benefits of pursuing this research. [+ of]

    开展这项研究附带的一个最大好处就是得到他的支持。
