pathological
pæθəlɒdʒɪkəl ˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 走极端的；无法控制的；病态的 You describe a person or their behaviour as pathological when they behave in an extreme and unacceptable way, and have very powerful feelings which they cannot control. 走极端的；无法控制的；病态的

  • He experiences chronic, almost pathological jealousy.

    他长期心怀近乎病态的嫉妒。

  • He's a pathological liar.

    他撒谎成性。

  • ...a pathological fear of snakes.

    对蛇病态的恐惧

2 ADJ 病理学的 Pathological means relating to pathology or illness. 病理学的 [medicine]

  • ...pathological conditions in animals.

    动物的病理状况
