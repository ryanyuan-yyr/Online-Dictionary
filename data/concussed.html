concussed
kənkʌst kənˈkʌst
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 脑震荡的 If someone is concussed, they lose consciousness or feel sick or confused because they have been hit hard on the head. 脑震荡的

  • My left arm is badly bruised and I was slightly concussed.

    我左臂严重擦伤，并有轻微脑震荡。

  • He was badly winded and concussed.

    他呼吸特别困难，而且有严重的脑震荡。
