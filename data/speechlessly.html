speechless
spiːtʃləs ˈspitʃlɪs
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (因惊愕)一时语塞的 If you are speechless, you are temporarily unable to speak, usually because something has shocked you. (因惊愕)一时语塞的

  • Alex was almost speechless with rage and despair. [+ with]

    亚历克斯又生气又绝望，几乎说不出话来。

SYN dumb, dumbfounded, lost for words, dumbstruck
