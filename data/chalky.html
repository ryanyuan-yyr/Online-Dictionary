chalky
tʃɔːki ˈtʃɔki
1 ADJ-GRADED 含白垩的;白垩覆盖的 Something that is chalky contains chalk or is covered with chalk. 含白垩的;白垩覆盖的

  • The chalky soil around Saumur produces the famous Anjou wines.

    索米尔周围的白垩质土壤出产著名的安茹葡萄酒。

2 ADJ-GRADED 苍白的;粉质的 Something that is chalky is a pale dull colour or has a powdery texture. 苍白的;粉质的

  • Her face became a chalky white.

    她的脸变得煞白。
