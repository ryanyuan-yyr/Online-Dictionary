deterrence
dɪterəns diˈtɛrəns dɪˈtɛrəns -tɜːr- diˈtɜrəns dɪˈtɜrəns
N-UNCOUNT （尤指对战争或犯罪的）威慑，吓阻，遏制 Deterrence is the prevention of something, especially war or crime, by having something such as weapons or punishment to use as a threat. （尤指对战争或犯罪的）威慑，吓阻，遏制

  • ...policies of nuclear deterrence.

    核威慑政策

SYN prevention, obstruction, inhibition, hindrance
