cry out
1 PHRASAL VERB (因害怕、不快或疼痛而)大叫，喊叫 If you cry out, you call out loudly because you are frightened, unhappy, or in pain. (因害怕、不快或疼痛而)大叫，喊叫

  • He was crying out in pain on the ground when the ambulance arrived. [V P + in]

    救护车赶到时他正疼得在地上大叫。

  • Hart cried out as his head struck rock. [VERB PREP.]

    哈特的头撞到石头上，疼得他大叫起来。
