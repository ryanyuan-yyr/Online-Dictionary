infantry
ɪnfəntri ˈɪnfəntri
N-UNCOUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 步兵(部队) Infantry are soldiers who fight on foot rather than in tanks or on horses. 步兵(部队)

  • The infantry were advancing to attack the ridge.

    步兵部队正在向前挺进攻打山脊。

  • The enemy infantry was hiding.

    敌军步兵正在隐蔽。

  • ...an infantry division.

    一个步兵师

  • ...regiments of infantry.

    步兵团

SYN infantrymen, foot soldiers
