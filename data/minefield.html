minefield
maɪnfiːld ˈmaɪnˌfild
Word forms: plural minefields
1 N-COUNT 地雷区;水雷区 A minefield is an area of land or water where explosive mines have been hidden. 地雷区;水雷区
2 N-COUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 危机四伏的局势；充满问题的情况 If you describe a situation as a minefield, you are emphasizing that there are a lot of hidden dangers or problems, and where people need to behave with care because things could easily go wrong. 危机四伏的局势；充满问题的情况 [emphasis] [Also + of]

  • The whole subject is a political minefield.

    整个话题就是一个政治上的雷区。

  • The kitchen is a minefield of potential hazards.

    厨房里充满了潜在的隐患。

SYN danger zone
