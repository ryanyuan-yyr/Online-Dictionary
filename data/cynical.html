cynical
sɪnɪkəl ˈsɪnɪkəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 认为世人皆自私的;愤世嫉俗的 If you describe someone as cynical, you mean they believe that people always act selfishly. 认为世人皆自私的;愤世嫉俗的

  • ...his cynical view of the world.

    他那愤世嫉俗的世界观

SYN sceptical, mocking, ironic, sneering  
cynically

  • As one former customer said cynically, 'He's probably pocketed the difference!'

    正如一位愤世嫉俗的旧主顾所说的那样，“他很可能把差额塞进了自己的腰包！”

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (对成功或人的真善)怀疑的，悲观的 If you are cynical about something, you do not believe that it can be successful or that the people involved are honest. (对成功或人的真善)怀疑的，悲观的

  • It's hard not to be cynical about reform. [+ about]

    很难相信改革会成功。

  • It has also made me more cynical about relationships.

    这也使得我愈加不相信爱情。

SYN unbelieving, sceptical, disillusioned, pessimistic
