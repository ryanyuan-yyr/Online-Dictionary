caller
kɔːləʳ ˈkælə ˈkælər ˈkɒlər ˈkælər ˈkɔlər ˈkɑlər
Word forms: plural callers
1 N-COUNT 打电话的人 A caller is a person who is making a telephone call. 打电话的人

  • An anonymous caller told police what had happened.

    有人打匿名电话向警方报告了所发生的事。

2 N-COUNT 来访者;探望者 A caller is a person who comes to see you for a short visit. 来访者;探望者

  • She ushered her callers into a cluttered living-room.

    她把来人领进凌乱不堪的客厅。
