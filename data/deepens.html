deepen
diːpən ˈdipən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deepens, present participle deepening, past tense , past participle deepened
1 V-ERG (使)变强烈(或紧张);(使)加剧 If a situation or emotion deepens or if something deepens it, it becomes stronger and more intense. (使)变强烈(或紧张);(使)加剧

  • If this is not stopped, the financial crisis will deepen. [VERB]

    如果不对其加以阻止，财政危机将会加剧。

  • If anything, Sloan's uneasiness deepened. [VERB]

    如果说有什么不一样的话，那就是斯隆变得越来越不安了。

  • Surviving tough times can really deepen your relationship. [VERB noun]

    共度患难能够切实地加深你们之间的关系。

2 VERB 加深，深化，增进（知识或理解） If you deepen your knowledge or understanding of a subject, you learn more about it and become more interested in it. 加深，深化，增进（知识或理解）

  • He did not get a chance to deepen his knowledge of Poland. [VERB noun]

    他没有机会更深入地了解波兰。

3 V-ERG （使）(光线或颜色)变暗;（使）变深 When light or a colour deepens or is deepened, it becomes darker. （使）(光线或颜色)变暗;（使）变深 [literary]

  • Dusk was deepening as they drove back to the lights of Shillingham. [VERB]

    他们驱车返回华灯初上的希灵汉姆时，暮色渐浓。

  • This spice is used particularly in poultry feed to deepen the colour of egg yolks. [VERB noun]

    这种香料尤用于家禽的饲料中，以加深蛋黄的颜色。

SYN darken, shade, make dark, make darker  
4 V-ERG （使）(声音)变低沉 When a sound deepens or is deepened, it becomes lower in tone. （使）(声音)变低沉

  • Her voice has deepened and coarsened with the years. [VERB]

    她的声音随着岁月流逝变得低沉而沙哑。

  • The music room had been made to reflect and deepen sounds. [VERB noun]

    这间音乐室经过特殊设计处理，能够反射声音，使声音变得低沉厚重。

5 V-ERG (呼吸)变深长;加深(呼吸) When your breathing deepens, or you deepen it, you take more air into your lungs when you breathe. (呼吸)变深长;加深(呼吸)

  • He heard her breathing deepen. [VERB]

    他听到她的呼吸变深了。

  • When you are ready to finish the exercise, gradually deepen your breathing. [VERB noun]

    准备结束练习时，要慢慢地深呼吸。

6 VERB 挖深 If people deepen something, they increase its depth by digging out its lower surface. 挖深

  • ...a major project to deepen the channel. [VERB noun]

    挖深水道的重大工程

  • The tunnels have been widened and deepened. [VERB noun]

    各条隧道都已拓宽加深。

SYN dig out, excavate, scoop out, hollow out  
7 VERB (河流、海洋等)变深 Something such as a river or a sea deepens where the bottom begins to slope downwards. (河流、海洋等)变深

  • As we drew nearer to it the water gradually deepened. [VERB]

    我们靠近它时，水渐渐深了起来。
