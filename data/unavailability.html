unavailable
ʌnəveɪləbəl
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 弄不到的；得不到的；不方便的 When things or people are unavailable, you cannot obtain them, meet them, or talk to them. 弄不到的；得不到的；不方便的

  • Mr Hicks is out of the country and so unavailable for comment.

    希克斯先生目前不在国内，所以无法发表评论。

  • Basic food products are frequently unavailable in the state shops.

    在国营商店里经常连一些基本食品都买不到。
