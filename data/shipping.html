shipping
ʃɪpɪŋ ˈʃɪpɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 运输(业);(尤指)航运(业) Shipping is the transport of cargo as a business, especially on ships. 运输(业);(尤指)航运(业)

  • ...the international shipping industry.

    国际航运业

  • The Greeks are still powerful players in world shipping.

    希腊依然是世界航运业强有力的竞争者。

2 N-UNCOUNT 运输费 You can refer to the amount of money that you pay to a company to transport cargo as shipping . 运输费

  • It is $39.95 plus $3 shipping.

    共 39.95 美元，外加 3 美元运费。

3 N-UNCOUNT (总称)船舶 You can refer to ships as shipping when considering them as a group. (总称)船舶

  • They sent naval forces to protect merchant shipping.

    他们派出了海军保护商船。
