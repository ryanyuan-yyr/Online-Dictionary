bilingual
baɪlɪŋgwəl baɪˈlɪŋgwəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 双语的；涉及两种语言的；使用两种语言的 Bilingual means involving or using two languages. 双语的；涉及两种语言的；使用两种语言的

  • ...bilingual education.

    双语教育

  • ...the Collins bilingual dictionaries.

    柯林斯双语词典

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 双语的；能说两种语言的 Someone who is bilingual can speak two languages equally well, usually because they learned both languages as a child. 双语的；能说两种语言的

  • He is bilingual in an Asian language and English.

    他会说一门亚洲语言和一门英语。
