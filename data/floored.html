floored flɔːʳd
ADJ 以…为地面材料的;铺有…地板的 A room or part of a room that is floored with a particular material has a floor made of that material. 以…为地面材料的;铺有…地板的

  • The aisle was floored with ancient bricks.

    过道由古旧的砖块铺就。

Floored is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • They had to cross the large marble-floored hall.

    他们得穿过一个铺有大理石地面的宽敞大厅。
