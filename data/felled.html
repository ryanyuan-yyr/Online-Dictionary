fell
fel fɛl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fells, present participle felling, past tense , past participle felled
1 Fell is the past tense of fall .
2 VERB [usually passive] 砍伐，砍倒(树木) If trees are felled, they are cut down. 砍伐，砍倒(树木)

  • Badly infected trees should be felled and burned. [be VERB-ed]

    感染严重的树应该砍倒焚烧。

SYN cut down, cut, level, demolish  
3 VERB 击倒;打倒 If you fell someone, you knock them down, for example in a fight. 击倒;打倒

  • ...a blow on the forehead which felled him to the ground. [VERB noun]

    打在额头上、将他击倒在地的一拳

SYN knock down, floor, flatten [informal] , strike down  
4 in one fell swoop→see: swoop
