post-production
(also post production)
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] （电影或电视的）后期制作 In film and television, post-production is the work such as editing that takes place after the film has been shot. （电影或电视的）后期制作

  • The film's post-production will be completed early next year.

    这部电影的后期制作将于来年年初完成。

  • ...a film post-production company.

    电影后期制作公司
