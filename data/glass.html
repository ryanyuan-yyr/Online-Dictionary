glass
glɑːs glæs glæs glɑs
Word forms: plural glasses
1 N-UNCOUNT 玻璃 Glass is a hard transparent substance that is used to make things such as windows and bottles. 玻璃

  • ...a pane of glass.

    一块玻璃

  • ...a sliding glass door.

    玻璃推拉门

2 N-COUNT 玻璃杯 A glass is a container made from glass, which you can drink from and which does not have a handle. 玻璃杯

  • Grossman raised the glass to his lips.

    格罗斯曼把杯子举到唇边。

The contents of a glass can be referred to as a glass of something.一杯的量

  • ...a glass of milk.

    一杯牛奶

3 N-UNCOUNT 玻璃制品 Glass is used to mean objects made of glass, for example drinking containers and bowls. 玻璃制品

  • There's a glittering array of glass to choose from at markets.

    市场上有很多晶莹闪亮的玻璃制品可供挑选。

4 N-PLURAL 眼镜 Glasses are two lenses in a frame that some people wear in front of their eyes in order to help them see better. 眼镜

  • He took off his glasses.

    他摘掉眼镜。

5 See also: dark glasses  magnifying glass
