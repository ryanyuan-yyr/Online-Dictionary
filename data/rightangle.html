right angle
(also right-angle)Word forms: plural right angles
1 N-COUNT 直角 A right angle is an angle of ninety degrees. A square has four right angles. 直角
2 PHRASE （与…）成直角；(与…)垂直 If two things are at right angles, they are situated so that they form an angle of 90˚ where they touch each other. You can also say that one thing is at right angles to another. （与…）成直角；(与…)垂直

  • ...two lasers at right angles.

    两束成直角的激光
