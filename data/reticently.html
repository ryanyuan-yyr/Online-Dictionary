reticent
retɪsənt ˈrɛtəsənt
ADJ-GRADED 沉默寡言的;不与人交谈的 Someone who is reticent does not tell people about things. 沉默寡言的;不与人交谈的

  • She is so reticent about her achievements. [+ about/on]

    她对自己的成就缄口不言。

  • Mrs. Smith, normally a reticent woman, took it upon herself to write to the President.

    通常都很沉默的史密斯夫人决定自己给总统写信。

SYN uncommunicative, reserved, secretive, unforthcoming  
reticence

  • Pearl didn't mind his reticence; in fact she liked it.

    珀尔并不介意他沉默寡言;事实上，她喜欢这一点。
