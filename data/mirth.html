mirth
mɜːʳθ mɜrθ
N-UNCOUNT 欢笑;欢乐 Mirth is amusement which you express by laughing. 欢笑;欢乐 [literary]

  • That caused considerable mirth amongst pupils and sports masters alike.

    那个举动引得学生和体育老师大笑不止。

  • It was all he could do to stop tears of mirth falling down his cheeks.

    他能做的只有不让快乐的泪水滑落脸庞。

SYN merriment, amusement, fun, pleasure
