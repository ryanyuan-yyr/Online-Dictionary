machine
məʃiːn məˈʃin
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense machines, present participle machining, past tense , past participle machined
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 机器;机械 A machine is a piece of equipment which uses electricity or an engine in order to do a particular kind of work. 机器;机械

  • I put the coin in the machine and pulled the lever.

    我把硬币投入机器，拉动控制杆。

  • The machine can be remotely operated and monitored.

    这台机器可以远程操作和监控。

  • ...a color photograph of the sort taken by machine to be pasted in passports.

    那种由机器拍摄的护照用彩色照片

SYN appliance, device, apparatus, engine [obsolete]  
2 VERB [usually passive] 用机器制造;用机器加工 If you machine something, you make it or work on it using a machine. 用机器制造;用机器加工

  • The material is machined in a factory. [be VERB-ed]

    这种材料在一家工厂里用机器加工成型。

  • All parts are machined from top grade, high tensile aluminium. [be VERB-ed + from]

    所有零件都是用顶级高强度铝加工而成。

  • ...machined brass zinc alloy gears. [VERB-ed]

    机器加工成型的铜锌合金齿轮

machining

  • ...our machining, fabrication and finishing processes.

    我们的机器加工、装配和精加工程序

3 N-COUNT 大型机构;严密组织 You can use machine to refer to a large and well-controlled system or organization. 大型机构;严密组织

  • ...Nazi Germany's military machine.

    纳粹德国的军事机构

  • He has put the party publicity machine behind another candidate.

    他为另一位参选者在幕后组织政党宣传。

SYN system, agency, structure, organization  
4 N-COUNT （由于工作过于劳累或乏味）机械般工作的人，做工作不动脑筋的人 If you say that someone is a machine, you mean that they are so tired or bored that they do their work without thinking. （由于工作过于劳累或乏味）机械般工作的人，做工作不动脑筋的人

  • I think I have got to stop or I might turn into a machine.

    我想我得停下来了，否则我就会变成一台机器。

  • He has dedicated himself to his work and become just a writing machine.

    他忘我地投入工作，就像是一台写作机器。

5 See also: fruit machine  sewing machine  slot machine  vending machine
