persona
pəʳsoʊnə pɜːˈsəʊnə pərˈsoʊnə
Word forms: plural personas, plural personae/pəʳsoʊnaɪ/
1 N-COUNT 表象人格；伪装；假象 Someone's persona is the aspect of their character or nature that they present to other people, perhaps in contrast to their real character or nature. 表象人格；伪装；假象 [formal]

  • ...the contradictions between her private life and the public persona.

    她私生活和公众形象间的冲突

  • From time to time he will take on a new persona.

    他时常伪装自己。

SYN personality, part, face, front [informal]  
2See also:persona non grata
