instrumentation
ɪnstrəmenteɪʃən ˌɪnstrəmɛnˈteɪʃən ˌɪnstrəˌmɛnˈteɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT (一组或一套)仪器，仪表 Instrumentation is a group or collection of instruments, usually ones that are part of the same machine. (一组或一套)仪器，仪表

  • Basic flight instrumentation was similar on both planes.

    两架飞机的基本飞行仪表相类似。

  • Now with this particular piece of instrumentation, you can actually spot individual carbon atoms.

    现在利用这件特定的仪器,就可以真正观察到单个碳原子了。

2 N-UNCOUNT 器乐谱写 The instrumentation of a piece of music is the way in which it is written for different instruments. 器乐谱写

  • Some of the instrumentation is exquisite, particularly for harp and flute.

    有些器乐段落谱写得非常优美,尤其是竖琴和长笛的部分。
