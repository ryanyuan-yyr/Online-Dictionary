kilohertz
kɪləhɜːʳts ˈkɪloʊˌhɛrts ˈkɪləˌhɜrts
Word forms: plural kilohertz
N-COUNT [num NOUN] 千赫（兹） A kilohertz is a unit of measurement of radio waves. One kilohertz is a thousand hertz. 千赫（兹）

  • Their instruments detected very faint radiowaves at a frequency of 3 kilohertz.

    他们的仪器检测到一个3千赫的微弱无线电波。
