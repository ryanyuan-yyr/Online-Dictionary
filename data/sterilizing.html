sterilize
sterɪlaɪz ˈstɛrəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sterilizes, present participle sterilizing, past tense , past participle sterilized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 sterilise
1 VERB 对…作灭菌(或消毒)处理 If you sterilize a thing or a place, you make it completely clean and free from germs. 对…作灭菌(或消毒)处理

  • Sulphur is also used to sterilize equipment. [VERB noun]

    硫也可用来给器材消毒。

  • The milk was sterilized and sealed in bottles. [VERB noun]

    牛奶经过灭菌处理后被封装在瓶子里。

SYN disinfect, clean, purify, fumigate  
sterilization

  • ...the pasteurization and sterilization of milk.

    牛奶的巴氏消毒和灭菌处理

2 VERB [usually passive] 使绝育 If a person or an animal is sterilized, they have a medical operation that makes it impossible for them to have or produce babies. 使绝育

  • My wife was sterilized after the birth of her fourth child. [be VERB-ed]

    我妻子生完第4个孩子后做了绝育手术。

sterilization

  • In some cases, a sterilization is performed through the vaginal wall.

    有些情况下，可以经阴道壁施行绝育手术。
