watery
wɔːtəri ˈwɔtəri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 微弱的;淡薄的;无力的 Something that is watery is weak or pale. 微弱的;淡薄的;无力的

  • A watery light began to show through the branches.

    一缕微弱的光线透过树枝。

  • Martha managed to produce a dim, watery smile.

    玛莎勉强挤出一丝笑容。

SYN pale, thin, weak, faint  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 水分过多;(味)太淡的 If you describe food or drink as watery, you dislike it because it contains too much water, or has no flavour. 水分过多;(味)太淡的 [disapproval]

  • ...a plateful of watery soup.

    一盘寡淡无味的汤

  • ...watery beer.

    味道很淡的啤酒

SYN diluted, thin, weak, dilute  
3 ADJ-GRADED 含水的;似水的;由水组成的 Something that is watery contains, resembles, or consists of water. 含水的;似水的;由水组成的

  • There was a watery discharge from her ear.

    她耳朵里流出水样分泌物。

  • Emma's eyes went red and watery.

    埃玛双眼发红，泪水盈盈。

SYN liquid, fluid, aqueous, hydrous
