incremental
ɪnkrɪmentəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 增加的;递增的 Incremental is used to describe something that increases in value or worth, often by a regular amount. 增加的;递增的 [formal]

  • ...our ability to add production capacity at relatively low incremental cost.

    我们以相对较低的边际成本增加产能的能力

  • We are seeking continuous, incremental improvements, not great breakthroughs.

    我们在寻求持续的、渐进的改善,而不是重大的突破。
