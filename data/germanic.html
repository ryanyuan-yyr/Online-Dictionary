germanic dʒɜːˈmænɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 德国人的；德国式的 If you describe someone or something as Germanic, you think that their appearance or behaviour is typical of German people or things. 德国人的；德国式的

  • He asked in his Germanic English if I was enjoying France.

    他操着一口带有德国腔的英语问我在法国玩得是否愉快。

2 ADJ 日耳曼文化的；日耳曼语的 Germanic is used to describe the ancient culture and language of the peoples of northern Europe. 日耳曼文化的；日耳曼语的

  • ...the Germanic tribes of pre-Christian Europe.

    基督教创立之前定居在欧洲的日耳曼部落
