predatory pricing
N-UNCOUNT （为击垮竞争对手而制定的）掠夺性定价 If a company practises predatory pricing, it charges a much lower price for its products or services than its competitors in order to force them out of the market. （为击垮竞争对手而制定的）掠夺性定价 [business]

  • Predatory pricing by large supermarkets was threatening the livelihood of smaller businesses.

    大型超市的掠夺性价格正威胁着小商店的生计。
