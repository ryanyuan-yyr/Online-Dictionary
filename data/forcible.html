forcible
fɔːʳsɪbəl ˈfɔrsəbəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 强制的;暴力的 Forcible action involves physical force or violence. 强制的;暴力的

  • Reports are coming in of the forcible resettlement of villagers from the countryside into towns.

    不断有报道称村民被强制从农村迁入城镇定居。

forcibly

  • Two student leaders were forcibly removed from the university president's office.

    两名学生领袖被强行从大学校长办公室赶了出来。
