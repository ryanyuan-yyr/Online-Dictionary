cross off
PHRASAL VERB (从名单上)画掉，删掉 If you cross off words on a list, you decide that they no longer belong on the list, and often you draw a line through them to indicate this. (从名单上)画掉，删掉 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I checked the chart and found I had crossed off the wrong thing. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我检查了一下图表，结果发现我错删了东西。

  • They have enough trouble finding nutritious food without crossing meat off their shopping lists. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    不把肉从他们的购物单中画掉，他们就很难买到有营养的食物。
