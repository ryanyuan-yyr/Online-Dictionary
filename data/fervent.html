fervent
fɜːʳvənt ˈfɜːvɪd ˈfɜrvənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 热情的;热忱的;热烈的 A fervent person has or shows strong feelings about something, and is very sincere and enthusiastic about it. 热情的;热忱的;热烈的

  • ...a fervent admirer of Morisot's work.

    摩里索作品热忱的欣赏者

  • ...the fervent hope that matters will be settled promptly.

    对事情将迅速得到解决的热切希望

SYN ardent, earnest, enthusiastic, fervid  
fervently

  • Their claims will be fervently denied.

    他们的要求将遭到严词拒绝。
