fry
fraɪ fraɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fries, present participle frying, past tense , past participle fried
1 VERB 油煎;油炸;油炒 When you fry food, you cook it in a pan that contains hot fat or oil. →see usage note at: cook 油煎;油炸;油炒

  • Fry the breadcrumbs until golden brown.

    把面包屑炸成金褐色为止。

  • ...fried rice.

    炒饭

2 N-PLURAL 鱼苗;鱼秧 Fry are very small, young fish. 鱼苗;鱼秧
3 N-PLURAL fries 同 French fries Fries are the same as French fries. fries 同 French fries
4See also:small fry 
5 PHRASAL VERB (尤指为尽快做好便饭)煎一下，炸一下 If you fry up food, you fry it, especially in order to make a quick, casual meal. (尤指为尽快做好便饭)煎一下，炸一下

  • I fried up the beef.

    我把牛肉煎了一下。

  • She cuts and fries the mixture up into a potato doughnut called Quin-Kuria.

    她把混料切好后放进油里炸成一种叫做Quin-Kuria 的土豆炸面圈。

6See also:fry-up
