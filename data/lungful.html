lungful
lʌŋfʊl ˈlʌŋfʊl
Word forms: plural lungfuls
N-COUNT （新鲜空气、香烟等）深吸的一大口 If someone takes a lungful of something such as fresh air or smoke, they breathe in deeply so that their lungs feel as if they are full of that thing. （新鲜空气、香烟等）深吸的一大口 [written]

  • I bobbed to the surface and gasped a lungful of air. [+ of]

    我浮上水面，深吸了一口气。
