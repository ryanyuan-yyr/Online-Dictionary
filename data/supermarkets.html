supermarket
suːpəʳmɑːʳkɪt ˈsupərˌmɑrkɪt
Word forms: plural supermarkets
N-COUNT 超级市场;超市 A supermarket is a large shop which sells all kinds of food and some household goods. 超级市场;超市

  • Most of us do our food shopping in the supermarket.

    我们大多数人去超市购买食品。

  • How do those prawns find their way from Norway to the supermarket shelf?

    这些对虾是如何从挪威跑到超市货架上来的呢？

SYN hypermarket, superstore, cash and carry
