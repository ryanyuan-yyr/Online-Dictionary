Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tamps, present participle tamping, past tense , past participle tamped
VERB 压实;夯实 If you tamp something, you press it down by tapping it several times so that it becomes flatter and more solid. 压实;夯实 [Also VERB n]

  • Then I tamp down the soil with the back of a rake. [VERB noun with adv.]

    然后我用耙子的背将土壤拍实。

  • Philpott tamped a wad of tobacco into his pipe. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    菲尔波特往烟斗里塞了一卷碎烟叶。
