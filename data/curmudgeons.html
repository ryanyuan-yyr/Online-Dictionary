curmudgeon
kəʳmʌdʒən kərˈmʌdʒən
Word forms: plural curmudgeons
N-COUNT 恶毒的人;脾气坏的人 If you call someone a curmudgeon, you do not like them because they are mean or bad-tempered. 恶毒的人;脾气坏的人 [old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • ...such a terrible old curmudgeon.

    脾气相当暴躁的老倔头

SYN grump [informal] , bear, grumbler, grouser
