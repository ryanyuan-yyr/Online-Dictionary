generalization
dʒenrəlaɪzeɪʃən ˌdʒɛnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən ˌ dʒɛnrəlɪˈzeɪʃən
Word forms: plural generalizations
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 generalisation
N-VAR 概括；概论；归纳 A generalization is a statement that seems to be true in most situations or for most people, but that may not be completely true in all cases. 概括；概论；归纳

  • He is making sweeping generalisations to get his point across.

    为了让大家领会他的观点，他正在作全面的概括。

  • The evaluation of conduct involves some amount of generalization.

    对操行的评价会含有一些泛泛之论。

SYN generality, abstraction, sweeping statement, loose statement
