undiscovered
ʌndɪskʌvəʳd ˌʌndɪˈskʌvəd
ADJ 未被发现的；未知的；未被注意的 Something that is undiscovered has not been discovered or noticed. 未被发现的；未知的；未被注意的

  • The name Vulcan was given to the undiscovered planet.

    一颗不为人知的行星被命名为“祝融”。

  • This site remained undiscovered, though long sought, until recent times.

    虽经长期寻找，但该遗址直到最近才被发现。
