skyrocket
skaɪrɒkɪt ˈskaɪˌrɑkɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense skyrockets, present participle skyrocketing, past tense , past participle skyrocketed
VERB 剧增;猛涨;猛升 If prices or amounts skyrocket, they suddenly increase by a very large amount. 剧增;猛涨;猛升

  • Production has dropped while prices and unemployment have skyrocketed. [VERB]

    物价和失业率猛涨的同时，生产却下降了。

  • ...the skyrocketing costs of health care. [VERB-ing]

    暴涨的医疗费用
