prima donna priːmə dɒnə ˈpriːmə ˈdɒnə ˌprimə ˈdɑnə ˈprɪmə
Word forms: plural prima donnas
1 N-COUNT （歌剧中的）女主角，首席女歌手 A prima donna is the main female singer in an opera. （歌剧中的）女主角，首席女歌手

  • Her career began as prima donna with the Royal Carl Rosa Opera Company.

    事业刚起步的时候，她在皇家卡尔·罗莎歌剧团担任首席女歌手。

SYN diva, star, leading lady, female lead  
2 N-COUNT 恃才傲物的人；自命不凡的人；妄自尊大的人 If you describe someone as a prima donna, you disapprove of them because they think they can behave badly or get what they want because they have a particular talent. 恃才傲物的人；自命不凡的人；妄自尊大的人 [disapproval]

  • Nobody who comes to this club is allowed to behave like a prima donna.

    加入这个俱乐部的任何人都不许自以为是。

  • ...prima donna behaviour.

    自命不凡的行为
