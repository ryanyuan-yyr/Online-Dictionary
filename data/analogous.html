analogous
ənæləgəs əˈnæləgəs
ADJ-GRADED 相似的；类似的；可比拟的 If one thing is analogous to another, the two things are similar in some way. 相似的；类似的；可比拟的 [formal]

  • Marine construction technology like this is very complex, somewhat analogous to trying to build a bridge under water. [+ to]

    像这样的海底建造技术很复杂，与尝试在水下建桥有些相似。

SYN similar, like, related, equivalent
