research
rɪsɜːʳtʃ ˈriːsɜːtʃ ˈriˌsɜrtʃ rɪˈsɜrtʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense researches, present participle researching, past tense , past participle researched
1 N-UNCOUNT 研究;探索 Research is work that involves studying something and trying to discover facts about it. 研究;探索

  • 65 percent of the 1987 budget went for nuclear weapons research and production.

    1987年65％的预算经费被用于核武器的研究和生产。

  • ...cancer research.

    癌症研究

  • ...his researches into which kinds of flowers bees get their best honey from.

    他对蜜蜂能从何种花中采到最好的蜜进行的研究

SYN investigation, study, inquiry, analysis  
2 VERB 调查;发掘 If you research something, you try to discover facts about it. 调查;发掘

  • She spent two years in South Florida researching and filming her documentary. [VERB noun]

    她花了两年时间在佛罗里达州南部做研究，拍摄纪录片。

  • So far we haven't been able to find anything, but we're still researching. [VERB]

    目前我们还没有任何发现，但是我们仍在研究。

SYN investigate, study, examine, experiment
