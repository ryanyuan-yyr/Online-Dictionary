brutalize
bruːtəlaɪz ˈbrutəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense brutalizes, present participle brutalizing, past tense , past participle brutalized
in BRIT, use 英国英语亦用 brutalise
1 VERB 使变残忍；使变粗暴 If an unpleasant experience brutalizes someone, it makes them cruel or violent. 使变残忍；使变粗暴

  • He was brutalized by the experience of being in prison. [be VERB-ed]

    牢狱生活让他变得残忍粗暴。

2 VERB 虐待；残忍对待 If one person brutalizes another, they treat them in a cruel or violent way. 虐待；残忍对待

  • ... a 15th century explorer who brutalized people and enslaved them. [VERB noun]

    一个虐待奴役他人的15世纪的探险者

  • Here's a man who has brutalized his own people. [VERB noun]

    这是一个残暴对待自己的人民的人。
