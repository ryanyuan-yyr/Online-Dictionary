exclaim
ɪkskleɪm ɪkˈskleɪm ɛkˈskleɪm ɪkˈskleɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense exclaims, present participle exclaiming, past tense , past participle exclaimed
1 VERB （由于激动、震惊、愤怒等）突然呼喊，惊叫，大声喊 Writers sometimes use exclaim to show that someone is speaking suddenly, loudly, or emphatically, often because they are excited, shocked, or angry. （由于激动、震惊、愤怒等）突然呼喊，惊叫，大声喊

  • 'He went back to the lab', Iris exclaimed impatiently. [VERB with quote]

    “他回实验室去了，”艾里斯不耐烦地大声说。

  • He exclaims that it must be a typing error. [VERB that]

    他惊呼道那一定是个打字错误。

SYN cry out, call, declare, cry
