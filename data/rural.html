rural
rʊərəl ˈrʊrəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 乡下的;乡村的;农村的 Rural places are far away from large towns or cities. 乡下的;乡村的;农村的

  • These plants have a tendency to grow in the more rural areas.

    这些植物大多生长在偏远的乡村。

  • ...the closure of rural schools.

    农村学校的关闭

SYN agricultural, country, farming, agrarian  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 有乡村特色的;田园的;有乡土气息的 Rural means having features which are typical of areas that are far away from large towns or cities. 有乡村特色的;田园的;有乡土气息的

  • ...the old rural way of life.

    昔日的田园生活

  • He spoke with a heavy rural accent.

    他说话带着浓重的农村口音。

SYN rustic, country, hick [informal , mainly US , Canadian] , pastoral
