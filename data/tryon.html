try on
1 PHRASAL VERB 试穿;试戴 If you try on a piece of clothing, you put it on to see if it fits you or if it looks nice. 试穿;试戴 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Try on clothing and shoes to make sure they fit. [VERB PREP. noun]

    试穿一下衣服和鞋子，看看是否合适。

2 PHRASAL VERB [usually cont] 耍花招;蒙骗 If you say that a person is trying it on, you mean that they are trying to obtain something or to impress someone, often in a slightly dishonest way and without much hope of success. 耍花招;蒙骗 [British, informal]

  • They're just trying it on — I don't believe they'll go this far. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们只是在耍花招—我不相信他们会走到这一步。
