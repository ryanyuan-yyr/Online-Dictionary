marry off
PHRASAL VERB 把…嫁出去;为…娶亲 If you marry someone off, you find a suitable person for them to marry. 把…嫁出去;为…娶亲 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They advised her mother to marry her off to the old man as he was very rich. [V n P to n]

    由于那个老头非常有钱，他们便建议她母亲把她嫁给他。

  • He had the good fortune to marry off his daughter to the local chief. [V P n (not pron) to n]

    他很幸运地把女儿嫁给了当地首领。

  • The poor are taking advantage of this edict to marry off their daughters without enormous expenditure while they can. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    穷人们利用这个法令，尽量不花费巨额开销就把女儿嫁出去。

  • Tradition dictates that girls should be married off early. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    传统习俗要求女孩子必须尽早结婚。
