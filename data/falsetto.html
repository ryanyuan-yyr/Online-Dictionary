falsetto
fɔːlsetoʊ fɔlˈsɛtoʊ

N-COUNT [usually singular, oft in NOUN, NOUN n] (男高音的)假声 If a man sings or speaks in a falsetto, his voice is high-pitched, and higher than a man's normal voice. (男高音的)假声
