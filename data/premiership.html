premiership
premiəʳʃɪp ˈprɛmjəʃɪp prɪmɪr-
1 N-SING 总理任期；首相任期 The premiership of a leader of a government is the period of time during which they are the leader. 总理任期；首相任期

  • ...the final years of Margaret Thatcher's premiership.

    玛格丽特·撒切尔首相任期的最后几年

  • So far, his premiership has been dominated by crisis-management.

    到目前为止，他在其首相任期内主要是在应对危机。

2 N-SING （英格兰）足球超级联赛 In England, the Premiership is the league in which the best football teams play. （英格兰）足球超级联赛 [British]

  • ...their position at the bottom of the Premiership.

    他们在英超比赛中垫底
