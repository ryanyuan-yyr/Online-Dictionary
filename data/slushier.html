slushy
slʌʃi ˈslʌʃɪ
Word forms: comparative slushier, superlative slushiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 融雪覆盖的;满是雪泥的 Slushy ground is covered in dirty, wet snow. 融雪覆盖的;满是雪泥的

  • Here and there a drift across the road was wet and slushy.

    路上不时会有吹聚起的积雪，泥泞而湿滑。

2 ADJ-GRADED (故事或想法)过于浪漫伤感的，无聊言情的 If you describe a story or idea as slushy, you mean you dislike it because it is extremely romantic and sentimental. (故事或想法)过于浪漫伤感的，无聊言情的 [disapproval]
