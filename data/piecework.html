piecework
piːswɜːʳk ˈpisˌwɜrk
(also piece-work)
N-UNCOUNT 计件工作 If you do piecework, you are paid according to the amount of work that you do rather than the length of time that you work. 计件工作

  • All my men are on piece-work.

    我所有的工人做的都是计件工。

  • The tobacco workers were paid on a piecework basis.

    烟草工人拿的是计件报酬。
