sit in on
PHRASAL VERB 旁听(课程);列席(会议) If you sit in on a lesson, meeting, or discussion, you are present while it is taking place but do not take part in it. 旁听(课程);列席(会议)

  • Will they permit you to sit in on a few classes?. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他们会同意你旁听几节课吗？

  • People can sit in on meetings, even if it's not really in their subject area. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    即使不在其研究专题范围之内，人们也可以列席会议。

SYN attend, watch, observe, be present at
