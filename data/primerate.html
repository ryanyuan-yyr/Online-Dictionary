prime rate
Word forms: plural prime rates
N-COUNT 最低贷款利率；最优惠贷款利率 A bank's prime rate is the lowest rate of interest which it charges at a particular time and which is offered only to certain customers. 最低贷款利率；最优惠贷款利率 [business]

  • At least one bank cut its prime rate today.

    今天，至少有一家银行降低了其最低贷款利率。
