face-saving ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 保全面子的;挽回面子的 A face-saving action is one which prevents damage to your reputation or the loss of people's respect for you. 保全面子的;挽回面子的

  • The decision appears to be a face-saving compromise which will allow the government to remain in office.

    这个决定看来是一种保全面子的妥协，让政府不至于下台。
