upland
ʌplənd ˈʌplənd ˈʌpˌlænd
Word forms: plural uplands
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 高地的；高原的 Upland places are situated on high land. 高地的；高原的

  • ...San Marino, the tiny upland republic.

    圣马力诺，面积狭小的高地共和国

  • It's important that these upland farms continue to survive.

    这些高原农场能继续存在下去非常重要。

2 N-PLURAL 高地；高原；山地 Uplands are areas of high land. 高地；高原；山地

  • Flooding was caused by water gushing into rivers from uplands.

    从山地奔流而下的水涌入河中引发了洪水。

  • ...a deep valley ringed about by green uplands.

    绿色高地环抱的深谷
