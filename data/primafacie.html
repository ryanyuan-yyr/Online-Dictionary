prima facie
praɪmə feɪʃi ˈpraɪmə ˈfeɪʃɪ ˌpraɪmə ˈfeɪʃiˌi ˈfeɪˌʃi ˈfeɪʃə
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 表面上的；基于初步印象的 Prima facie is used to describe something which appears to be true when you first consider it. 表面上的；基于初步印象的 [formal]

  • There was a prima facie case that a contempt of court had been committed.

    表面上看这是一宗藐视法庭的案件。
