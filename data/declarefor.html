declare for
PHRASAL VERB 宣布支持;表示赞成 If you declare for something or someone, you say that you are in favour of them. 宣布支持;表示赞成

  • The Catalans declared for Charles and a civil war erupted in Valencia and Aragon. [VERB PREP. noun]

    加泰罗尼亚人宣布支持查理，于是一场内战在巴伦西亚和阿拉贡两地爆发。

  • Only a month earlier, Mr. Stenholm had declared for the tax cut. [VERB PREP. noun]

    就在一个月前，斯滕霍尔姆先生刚刚表示过赞成减税。
