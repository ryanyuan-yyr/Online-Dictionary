indecisive
ɪndɪsaɪsɪv ˌɪndiˈsaɪsɪv ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 优柔寡断的;无决断力的;迟疑不决的 If you say that someone is indecisive, you mean that they find it very difficult to make decisions. 优柔寡断的;无决断力的;迟疑不决的

  • Michael was indecisive about how to decorate the room.

    关于如何装饰房间,迈克尔犹豫不决。

  • He was criticised as a weak and indecisive leader.

    作为领导他被指责作风软弱，缺乏决断力。

SYN hesitating, uncertain, wavering, doubtful  
indecisiveness

  • The mayor was criticized by radical reformers for his indecisiveness.

    市长因优柔寡断而受到激进改革派的批评。

2 ADJ 不明确的;非结论性的 An indecisive result in a contest or election is one which is not clear or definite. 不明确的;非结论性的

  • An indecisive result would force a second round of voting.

    尚无定论的结果会迫使进行第二轮投票。

  • The outcome of the battle was indecisive.

    此役结果尚未明朗。

SYN inconclusive, unclear, undecided, indefinite
