censorship
sensəʳʃɪp ˈsɛnsərˌʃɪp
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤指政府官员对书刊、戏剧、电影、报道等的)审查，检查 Censorship is the censoring of books, plays, films, or reports, especially by government officials, because they are considered immoral or secret in some way. (尤指政府官员对书刊、戏剧、电影、报道等的)审查，检查

  • The government today announced that press censorship was being lifted.

    政府今天宣布将取消新闻审查制度。

SYN expurgation, blue pencil, purgation, bowdlerization or bowdlerisation
