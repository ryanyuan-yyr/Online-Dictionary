statute book
Word forms: plural statute books
N-COUNT 法令全书;法规汇编 The statute book is a record of all the laws made by the government. 法令全书;法规汇编 [mainly British]

  • The Bill could reach the statute book by the summer if it attracts the support of Home Office ministers.

    该法案如果能得到内政部各部长的支持，将可能在今年夏天之前被收入法令全书。

  • Germany still has no insider-dealing offence in its statute books.

    德国的法律条文中仍未设立内幕交易罪。
