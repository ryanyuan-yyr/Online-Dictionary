burble
bɜːʳbəl ˈbɜrbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense burbles, present participle burbling, past tense , past participle burbled
1 VERB 发汩汩声 If something burbles, it makes a low continuous bubbling sound. 发汩汩声

  • The water burbled over gravel. [VERB prep.]

    水流过卵石，汩汩作响。

  • The river gurgled and burbled. [VERB]

    河水汩汩地流淌着。

2 VERB 语无伦次地说；嘟囔；喋喋不休地说 If you say that someone is burbling, you mean that they are talking in a confused way. 语无伦次地说；嘟囔；喋喋不休地说

  • He burbled something incomprehensible. [VERB noun]

    他不知咕哝了些什么东西。

  • Key burbled about the wonderful people who contribute to tourism. [VERB + about]

    基喋喋不休地谈论着那些对旅游业作出贡献的了不起的人物。

  • He burbles on about freedom. [V + on about]

    他喋喋不休地大谈自由。
