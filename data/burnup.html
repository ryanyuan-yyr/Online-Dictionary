burn up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 烧毁；烧光 If something burns up or if fire burns it up, it is completely destroyed by fire or strong heat. 烧毁；烧光 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The satellite re-entered the atmosphere and burned up. [VERB PREP.]

    卫星重新进入大气层，烧成灰烬。

  • Fires have burned up 180,000 acres of timber. [VERB PREP. noun]

    大火烧光了18万英亩的木材。

2 PHRASAL VERB 消耗；耗费 If something burns up fuel or energy, it uses it. 消耗；耗费 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Brisk walking burns up more calories than slow jogging. [VERB PREP. noun]

    快走比慢跑能消耗更多的热量。
