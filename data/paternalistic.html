paternalistic
pətɜːʳnəlɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 家长式的；家长作风的 Someone who is paternalistic takes all the decisions for the people they govern, employ, or are responsible for. 家长式的；家长作风的

  • The doctor is being paternalistic. He's deciding what information the patient needs to know.

    那位医生很专断。患者需要了解什么信息全由他说了算。

  • IBM has always been a paternalistic employer.

    国际商用机器公司一直是个家长作风很强的雇主。
