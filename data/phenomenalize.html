phenomenal
fɪnɒmɪnəl fəˈnɑmənəl
ADJ-GRADED 非凡的；不寻常的；惊人的 Something that is phenomenal is so great or good that it is very unusual indeed. 非凡的；不寻常的；惊人的 [emphasis]

  • Exports of Australian wine are growing at a phenomenal rate.

    澳大利亚葡萄酒出口正在以惊人的速度增长。

  • The performances have been absolutely phenomenal.

    表演绝对精彩。

SYN extraordinary, outstanding, remarkable, fantastic [informal]  
phenomenally

  • Scots-born Annie, 37, has recently relaunched her phenomenally successful singing career.

    生于苏格兰的安妮37岁，最近复出，再度投身于其辉煌的歌唱事业中。

  • Food production once again rose phenomenally, by 4 per cent or more a year.

    食品生产再次迅速增长，年增长率达4%或更高。
