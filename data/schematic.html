schematic
skiːmætɪk skiˈmætɪk skəˈmætɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 略图的;简表的 A schematic diagram or picture shows something in a simple way. 略图的;简表的

  • This is represented in the schematic diagram below.

    这正如下面的这幅简略图所示。

  • ...a schematic picture of the solar system.

    太阳系的略图

SYN graphic, representational, illustrative, diagrammatic  
schematically

  • Let me schematically show what happens.

    我来简要介绍一下发生的事情。
