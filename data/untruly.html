untrue
ʌntruː ʌnˈtruː ʌnˈtru ˈʌnˌtru
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 不真实的；虚假的 If a statement or idea is untrue, it is false and not based on facts. 不真实的；虚假的

  • The allegations were completely untrue.

    这些指控纯属捏造。

  • It was untrue to say that all political prisoners have been released.

    并不是所有政治犯都被释放了。

  • Such remarks are both offensive and untrue.

    这种言论既令人恼火又不属实。

SYN false, lying, wrong, mistaken
