unmissable
ʌnmɪsəbəl
ADJ 不可错过的 If you say that something such as an event or a film is unmissable, you are emphasizing that it is so good that everyone should try to go to it or see it. 不可错过的 [British, informal, emphasis]

  • His new show is unmissable.

    他的新节目不容错过。

  • Book your place on this unmissable conference.

    此次会议不可错过，现在就预订位置吧。
