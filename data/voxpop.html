vox pop
vɒks pɒp
Word forms: plural vox pops
N-VAR （广播或电视节目中的）公众采访，街头民意调查 In a radio or television programme, a vox pop is an item consisting of a series of short interviews with ordinary members of the public. （广播或电视节目中的）公众采访，街头民意调查 [mainly British, journalism]

  • The film also made use of newsreel footage and vox pop.

    电影还利用了新闻片片段和街头采访的表现手法。
