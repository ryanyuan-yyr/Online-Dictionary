eye-popping
ADJ [usu ADJ n] 令人瞠目的Something that is eye-popping is very impressive or striking. 令人瞠目的

  • ...a plan to raise property taxes by an eye-popping $2 billion.

    ...一个将地产税提高到令人瞠目的20亿美元的计划。

  • For sure, there is little shortage of eye-popping scenery around here.

    可以肯定的是，这里不缺乏令人着迷的景象。
