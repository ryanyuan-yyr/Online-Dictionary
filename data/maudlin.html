maudlin
mɔːdlɪn ˈmɔdlɪn
1 ADJ-GRADED （尤指酒后）伤感的，多愁善感的 If you describe someone as maudlin, you mean that they are being sad and sentimental in a foolish way, perhaps because of drinking alcohol. （尤指酒后）伤感的，多愁善感的

  • Jimmy turned maudlin after three drinks.

    3杯酒下肚，吉米愁上心头。

  • ...maudlin self-pity.

    自艾自怜

SYN sentimental, tearful, mushy [informal] , soppy [British , informal]  
2 ADJ-GRADED (歌曲、书、电影等)伤感的，凄哀的 If you describe a song, book, or film as maudlin, you are criticizing it for being very sentimental. (歌曲、书、电影等)伤感的，凄哀的 [disapproval]

  • ...the most maudlin song of all time.

    有史以来最伤感的歌曲

  • ...a hugely entertaining (if overlong and maudlin) movie.

    (尽管超长又伤感但)极其引人入胜的影片
