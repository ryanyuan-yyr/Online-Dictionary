agreed
əgriːd əˈgrid
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ that] 达成协议的;商定的;意见一致的 If people are agreed on something, they have reached a joint decision on it or have the same opinion about it. 达成协议的;商定的;意见一致的

  • Okay, so are we agreed on going north?. [+ on]

    好，那么我们已经一致同意向北走了？

  • Everyone is agreed that something needs to be done about the situation.

    所有人一致认为需要采取措施改变这种局面。

SYN settled, given, established, guaranteed  
2 CONVENTION 同意吗（用于征求他人意见）/ 同意（用于赞同他人意见） When you are discussing something, you can say 'Agreed?' to check whether the other people agree with what you have just said. You can say 'Agreed' if you agree with what someone has just said. 同意吗（用于征求他人意见）/ 同意（用于赞同他人意见） [formal, spoken, formulae]

  • 'That means we move out today. Agreed?'—'Agreed.'

    “那就意味着我们要今天搬出去，同意吗？”—“同意。”

  • 'One thing you can never insure against is corruption among your staff.'—'Agreed.'

    “永远也防不胜防的就是员工内部的贪污腐败。”—“同意。”

3 See also: agree
