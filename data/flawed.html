flawed
flɔːd flɔːd
ADJ-GRADED 有瑕疵的;有错误的;有缺陷的 Something that is flawed has a mark, fault, or mistake in it. 有瑕疵的;有错误的;有缺陷的

  • ...the unique beauty of a flawed object.

    有瑕疵物品的独特美

  • These tests were so seriously flawed as to render the results meaningless.

    这些测试存在严重错误，致使结果变得毫无意义。

SYN damaged, defective, imperfect, blemished
