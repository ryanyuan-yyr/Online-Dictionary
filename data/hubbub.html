hubbub
hʌbʌb ˈhʌbˌbʌb ˈhʌbʌb
Word forms: plural hubbubs
1 N-VAR （人群的）喧嚷，吵闹声 A hubbub is a noise made by a lot of people all talking or shouting at the same time. （人群的）喧嚷，吵闹声 [written]

  • There was a hubbub of excited conversation from over a thousand people. [+ of]

    有一千多人在兴高采烈地大声谈话,场面无比喧闹。

SYN noise, racket, din, uproar  
2 N-SING 混乱;骚动 You can describe a situation where there is great confusion or excitement as a hubbub . 混乱;骚动

  • In all the hubbub over the election, one might be excused for missing yesterday's announcement.

    在选举造成的一片混乱中，没有听到昨天的公告也是情有可原的。
