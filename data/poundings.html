pounding
paʊndɪŋ ˈpaʊndɪŋ
Word forms: plural poundings
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 重创；严重损坏；重伤 If someone or something takes a pounding, they are severely injured or damaged. 重创；严重损坏；重伤 [informal]

  • Sarajevo took one of its worst poundings in weeks.

    萨拉热窝遭受了几个星期以来最严重的一次破坏。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular, usually supplement NOUN] 惨败；大败 If a person or team gets a pounding, they are severely defeated. 惨败；大败 [informal]

  • The prospects are that he will give opponents a thorough pounding.

    他很有可能使对手们一败涂地。
