publicity
pʌblɪsɪti pʌˈblɪsɪtɪ pʌbˈlɪsəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 宣传；报道 Publicity is information or actions that are intended to attract the public's attention to someone or something. 宣传；报道

  • Much advance publicity was given to the talks.

    对于这次会谈有很多前期报道。

  • ...government publicity campaigns.

    政府宣传活动

  • It was all a publicity stunt.

    这完全是个宣传噱头。

SYN advertising, press, promotion, hype  
2 N-UNCOUNT （媒体或公众的）关注 When the news media and the public show a lot of interest in something, you can say that it is receiving publicity. （媒体或公众的）关注

  • The case has generated enormous publicity in Brazil.

    这个案件已经在巴西引起了极大的关注。

  • ...the renewed publicity over the Casey affair.

    对凯西事件的再次关注

SYN attention, exposure, fame, celebrity
