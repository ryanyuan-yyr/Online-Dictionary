strained
streɪnd streɪnd
1 ADJ-GRADED 焦虑的;紧张的;担忧的 If someone's appearance, voice, or behaviour is strained, they seem worried and nervous. 焦虑的;紧张的;担忧的

  • She looked a little pale and strained.

    她看起来面色有点苍白，神情忧虑。

  • Gil sensed something wrong from her father's strained voice.

    吉尔从父亲紧张的声音中感觉到出了什么事了。

  • His laughter seemed a little strained.

    他的笑声好像有点不自然。

SYN forced, put on, false, artificial  
2 ADJ-GRADED （关系）紧张的，不友好的，互相猜测的 If relations between people are strained, those people do not like or trust each other. （关系）紧张的，不友好的，互相猜测的

  • ...a period of strained relations between the prime minister and his deputy.

    总理和副总理之间关系紧张的时期
