wake up to
PHRASAL VERB 觉察到;意识到 If you wake up to something, you become aware of it. 觉察到;意识到

  • Lithuanians are waking up to a world of increasing shortages. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    立陶宛人开始意识到物资日益匮乏。

  • People should wake up to the fact that people with disabilities have got a vote as well. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    人们应该认识到残疾人同样也享有投票权。

SYN realize, understand, recognize, appreciate
