subcontract
sʌbkəntrækt sʌbkɒntrækt ˈsʌbˌkɑntrækt ˌ sʌbkənˈtrækt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense subcontracts, present participle subcontracting, past tense , past participle subcontracted
The verb is pronounced /ˌsʌbkən'trækt/. The noun is pronounced /sʌb'kɒntrækt/. 动词读作 /ˌsʌbkən'trækt/，名词读作 /sʌb'kɒntrækt/。
1 VERB 将…分包；将…转包 If one firm subcontracts part of its work to another firm, it pays the other firm to do part of the work that it has been employed to do. 将…分包；将…转包 [business]

  • The company is subcontracting production of most of the parts. [VERB noun]

    该公司外包了大部分零部件的生产。

  • They are cutting costs by subcontracting work out to other local firms. [VERB noun + to]

    他们通过将任务转包给当地其他公司来减少成本。

2 N-COUNT 分包合同;转包契约 A subcontract is a contract between a firm which is being employed to do a job and another firm which agrees to do part of that job. 分包合同;转包契约
