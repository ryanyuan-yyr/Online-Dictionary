rationalism
ræʃənəlɪzəm ˈræʃənəˌlɪzəm ˈræʃənəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 理性主义；唯理论 Rationalism is the belief that your life should be based on reason and logic, rather than emotions or religious beliefs. 理性主义；唯理论

  • Coleridge was to spend the next thirty years attacking rationalism.

    柯尔律治在之后的30年里一直抨击理性主义。

  • ...the scientific rationalism of the West.

    西方的科学理性主义
