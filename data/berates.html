berate
bɪreɪt biˈreɪt bɪˈreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense berates, present participle berating, past tense , past participle berated
VERB 怒斥；训斥 If you berate someone, you speak to them angrily about something they have done wrong. 怒斥；训斥 [formal] [Also VERB n]

  • Marion berated Joe for the noise he made. [VERB noun + for]

    玛丽昂严厉斥责乔吵吵闹闹。

SYN scold, rebuke, reprimand, reproach
