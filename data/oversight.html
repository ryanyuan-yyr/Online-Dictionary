oversight
oʊvəʳsaɪt ˈoʊvərˌsaɪt
Word forms: plural oversights
1 N-COUNT 疏忽；忽略；失察 If there has been an oversight, someone has forgotten to do something which they should have done. 疏忽；忽略；失察

  • William was angered and embarrassed by his oversight.

    威廉因为自己的疏忽而羞愤交加。

  • By an unfortunate oversight, full instructions do not come with the product.

    由于一个不幸的疏忽，产品未能附上完整的说明。

SYN mistake, error, slip, fault  
2 N-UNCOUNT 照管；监督；负责 If someone has oversight of a process or system, they are responsible for making sure that it works efficiently and correctly. 照管；监督；负责

  • ...a new system, where there'll be greater oversight of doctors. [+ of]

    加强医生监管的新体制

  • I had the oversight of their collection of books and manuscripts.

    我负责照管他们收藏的书籍和手稿。

  • The UN will also be given a loose oversight role.

    联合国还将被赋予宽泛的监督职责。

SYN supervision, keeping, control, charge
