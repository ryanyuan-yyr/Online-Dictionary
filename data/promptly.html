promptly
prɒmptli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 立即；马上；及时 If you do something promptly, you do it immediately. 立即；马上；及时

  • Sister Francesca entered the chapel, took her seat, and promptly fell asleep.

    弗朗西丝卡修女走进小教堂，坐下来，很快就睡着了。

2 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 准时地 If you do something promptly at a particular time, you do it at exactly that time. 准时地

  • Promptly at a quarter past seven, we left the hotel. [+ at/on]

    我们于7点一刻准时离开了宾馆。

SYN punctually, on time, spot on [informal] , bang on [informal]
