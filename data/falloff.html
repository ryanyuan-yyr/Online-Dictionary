fall off
1 PHRASAL VERB 脱落If something falls off, it separates from the thing to which it was attached and moves toward the ground. 脱落

  • When your exhaust pipe falls off, you have to replace it. [VERB PREP.]

    当你的排气管脱落时，你只能更换它。

2 PHRASAL VERB 降低; 减小If the degree, amount, or size of something falls off, it decreases. 降低; 减小

  • Unemployment is rising again and retail buying has fallen off. [VERB PREP.]

    失业率又在上升，而零售购买量已经减少。

SYN decrease, drop, reduce, decline
