intercession
ɪntəʳseʃən ˌɪntərˈsɛʃən
Word forms: plural intercessions
N-VAR 求情;调解;斡旋 Intercession is the act of interceding with someone. 求情;调解;斡旋 [formal]

  • His intercession could be of help to the tribe.

    他的调解可能会帮上该部落的忙。

  • Many claimed to have been cured as a result of the Madonna's intercessions.

    很多人声称他们被治愈是圣母马利亚代他们祈祷的应验。

SYN pleading, prayer, intervention, plea
