protester
prətestəʳ
(also protestor)Word forms: plural protesters
N-COUNT 抗议者；反对者 Protesters are people who protest publicly about an issue. 抗议者；反对者

  • The protesters say the government is corrupt and inefficient.

    抗议者称政府腐败无能。

  • ...anti-abortion protesters.

    反对堕胎的抗议者

SYN demonstrator, rebel, dissident, dissenter
