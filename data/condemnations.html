condemnation
kɒndemneɪʃən ˌkɑndɛmˈneɪʃən ˌkɑndəmˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural condemnations
N-VAR 谴责；指责 Condemnation is the act of saying that something or someone is very bad and unacceptable. 谴责；指责

  • There was widespread condemnation of Saturday's killings. [+ of]

    周六的杀人事件受到普遍谴责。

  • The raids have drawn a strong condemnation from the United Nations Security Council. [+ from]

    突袭行动受到联合国安理会的强烈谴责。

SYN denunciation, blame, censure, disapproval
