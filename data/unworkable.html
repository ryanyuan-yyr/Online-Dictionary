unworkable
ʌnwɜːʳkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不可行的；行不通的；办不成的 If you describe something such as a plan, law, or system as unworkable, you believe that it cannot be successful. 不可行的；行不通的；办不成的

  • There is the strong possibility that such cooperation will prove unworkable.

    这种合作很有可能会行不通。

  • Washington is unhappy with the peace plan which it views as unworkable.

    美国政府对和平计划不满，认为它不可行。

SYN impracticable, unfeasible, unachievable, impossible
