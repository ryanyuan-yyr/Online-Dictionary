explain away
PHRASAL VERB 为…辩解；搪塞 If someone explains away a mistake or a bad situation they are responsible for, they try to indicate that it is unimportant or that it is not really their fault. 为…辩解；搪塞

  • He evaded her questions about the war and tried to explain away the atrocities. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他回避了她有关战争的问题，极力为那些暴行辩解。

  • I had noticed blood on my husband's clothing but he explained it away. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我注意到丈夫的衣服上有血迹，但是被他搪塞过去了。
