defiant
dɪfaɪənt diˈfaɪɛnt dɪˈfaɪənt
ADJ-GRADED 违抗的;蔑视的;挑战的;不服的 If you say that someone is defiant, you mean they show aggression or independence by refusing to obey someone. 违抗的;蔑视的;挑战的;不服的

  • The players are in defiant mood as they prepare for tomorrow's game.

    球员在备战明天的比赛时带着蔑视对手的情绪。

  • Despite the risk of suspension, he remained defiant.

    尽管可能会被停职，他仍然桀骜不驯。

SYN resisting, challenging, rebellious, daring  
defiantly

  • They defiantly rejected any talk of a compromise.

    他们轻蔑地拒绝任何提及妥协的内容。
