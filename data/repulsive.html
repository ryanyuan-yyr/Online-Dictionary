repulsive
rɪpʌlsɪv rɪˈpʌlsɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 令人憎恶的;让人厌恶的;使人反感的 If you describe something or someone as repulsive, you mean that they are horrible and disgusting and you want to avoid them. 令人憎恶的;让人厌恶的;使人反感的

  • ...repulsive fat white slugs.

    让人恶心的白白胖胖的鼻涕虫

  • ...the most repulsive mass murderer America has known.

    美国历史上最令人憎恶的杀人恶魔

SYN disgusting, offensive, foul, ugly  
repulsively

  • ...a repulsively large rat.

    恶心的大老鼠

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 相斥的;排斥的;斥力的 A repulsive force is a force which pushes away what is around it. 相斥的;排斥的;斥力的 [technical]

  • The repulsive force within the nucleus is enormous.

    核子内部的斥力是巨大的。
