gritty
grɪti ˈgrɪti
Word forms: comparative grittier, superlative grittiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 含沙砾的；覆有沙砾的；沙砾般的 Something that is gritty contains grit, is covered with grit, or has a texture like that of grit. 含沙砾的；覆有沙砾的；沙砾般的

  • The sheets fell on the gritty floor, and she just let them lie.

    床单掉到满是沙砾的地板上，她都没去捡。

2 ADJ-GRADED 坚毅的；勇敢的 Someone who is gritty is brave and determined. 坚毅的；勇敢的

  • We have to prove how gritty we are.

    我们必须证明我们有多么勇敢无畏。

  • ...a gritty determination to avoid humiliation.

    坚决避免蒙受羞辱

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （对艰难或令人反感的情形的描述）逼真的，真实的，活生生的 A gritty description of a tough or unpleasant situation shows it in a very realistic way. （对艰难或令人反感的情形的描述）逼真的，真实的，活生生的

  • ...gritty social comment.

    洞悉现实的社会评论

  • His most celebrated work is the film classic, 'Woman in the Dunes,' a gritty look at survival in an extreme environment.

    他最负盛名的作品是经典影片《砂之女》，该片对极端环境下的艰难求生进行了逼真的刻画。
