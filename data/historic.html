historic
hɪstɒrɪk hɪˈstɒrɪk -tɔːr- hɪsˈtɔrɪk hɪsˈtɑrɪk
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 历史(性）的;具有重大历史意义的 Something that is historic is important in history, or likely to be considered important at some time in the future. 历史(性）的;具有重大历史意义的

  • ...the historic changes in Eastern Europe.

    东欧的历史性变革

  • ...a fourth historic election victory.

    第4次具有重大历史意义的竞选胜利

SYN significant, notable, momentous, famous  
Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse historic and historical.

    注意不要混淆 historic 和 historical 。
