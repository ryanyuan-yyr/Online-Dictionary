beer
bɪəʳ bɪr
Word forms: plural beers
1 N-MASS 啤酒 Beer is a bitter alcoholic drink made from grain. 啤酒

  • He sat in the kitchen drinking beer.

    他坐在厨房里喝啤酒。

  • We have quite a good range of beers.

    我们有多种多样的啤酒。

SYN ale, brew, swipes [British , slang] , wallop [British , slang]  
A glass of beer can be referred to as a beer .一杯啤酒

  • Would you like a beer?

    来杯啤酒吗？
