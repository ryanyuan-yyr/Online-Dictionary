kilter
kɪltəʳ ˈkɪltər
1 PHRASE 不匹配；不适合 If one thing is out of kilter with another, the first thing does not agree with or fit in with the second. 不匹配；不适合

  • Her lifestyle was out of kilter with her politics.

    她的生活方式与她的政治活动格格不入。

  • President, parliament and public are out of kilter.

    总统、议会和公众之间的关系协调得不好。

SYN incompatible, conflicting, contrary, at odds  
2 PHRASE 不完全正确；有问题 If something or someone is out of kilter or off kilter, they are not completely right. 不完全正确；有问题

  • Ignoring feelings of tiredness knocks our body clocks out of kilter.

    忽视疲劳感会打乱我们的生物钟。

  • I'm a bit off-kilter at the moment.

    我此刻有点不对劲。
