thrash out
1 PHRASAL VERB 经反复讨论制订出(计划、协议) If people thrash out something such as a plan or an agreement, they decide on it after a great deal of discussion. 经反复讨论制订出(计划、协议) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The foreign ministers have thrashed out a suitable compromise formula. [VERB PREP. noun]

    外交部长们经过反复讨论，已经制订出合适的折中方案。

  • How foreign fund-managers will be compensated has yet to be thrashed out. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    如何对外资管理者进行补偿还有待磋商。

2 PHRASAL VERB 讨论解决，商讨解决(问题、争端) If people thrash out a problem or a dispute, they discuss it thoroughly until they reach an agreement. 讨论解决，商讨解决(问题、争端) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • ...a sincere effort by two people to thrash out differences about which they have strong feelings. [VERB PREP. noun]

    两个人为解决双方的严重分歧而作出的真诚努力

SYN settle, resolve, discuss, debate
