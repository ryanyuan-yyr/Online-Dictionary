contract out
1 PHRASAL VERB 订立合同把…承包出去 If a company contracts out work, they employ other companies to do it. 订立合同把…承包出去 [business]

  • Firms can contract out work to one another. [V P n + to]

    公司可以互相承包业务。

  • When Barclays Bank contracted out its cleaning, the new company was cheaper. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当巴克莱银行外包其结算业务后，新公司的价格更为便宜。

  • ...the trend of contracting services out rather than performing them in-house. [VERB noun PREP.]

    将服务外包而不是由内部员工来完成的趋势

2 PHRASAL VERB (正式)退出，同意不参加 If a person or group contracts out of a system or scheme, they formally say that they do not want to take part in it. (正式)退出，同意不参加 [British]

  • Employees can contract out of their employer's occupational pension scheme. [VERB PREP. + of]

    雇员可以退出雇主的职业养老金计划。

  • ...a free deal which automatically converts into a pay as-you-go service unless you contract out. [VERB PREP.]

    除非自己退出，否则将自动转换成充值付费服务的免费协议
