jokey
dʒoʊki ˈdʒoʊki
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 逗乐的；举止滑稽的；可笑的 If someone behaves in a jokey way, they do things in a way that is intended to be amusing, rather than serious. 逗乐的；举止滑稽的；可笑的 [informal]

  • Bruno has not got his younger brother's jokey manner.

    布鲁诺还无法理解他弟弟诙谐的举止。

  • He was still his old jokey self.

    他还是像以前一样爱搞笑。

SYN playful, funny, amusing, teasing
