bovine
boʊvaɪn ˈboʊˌvaɪn ˈboʊˌvin
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 牛的；与牛有关的 Bovine means relating to cattle. 牛的；与牛有关的 [technical]
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 蠢笨的；反应迟钝的；呆头呆脑的 If you describe someone's behaviour or appearance as bovine, you think that they are stupid or slow. 蠢笨的；反应迟钝的；呆头呆脑的 [disapproval]

  • I'm depressed by the bovine enthusiasm of the crowd's response.

    人群毫无热情的迟钝反应使我感到很沮丧。

SYN dull, heavy, slow, thick
