wipe away
(also wipe off)
PHRASAL VERB 擦去;擦掉;揩干 If you wipe away or wipe off dirt or liquid from something, you remove it, for example by using a cloth or your hand. 擦去;擦掉;揩干

  • He wiped away the blood with a paper napkin. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他用纸巾将血迹擦去。

  • She applied a little lipstick, wiped it off, but it left a pink tinge. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    她抹了一点口红，又擦掉了，但却留下了一丝粉红色。
