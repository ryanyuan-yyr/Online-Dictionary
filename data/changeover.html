changeover
tʃeɪndʒoʊvəʳ ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvər
Word forms: plural changeovers
N-COUNT (活动或体制的)转变，变更，过渡 A changeover is a change from one activity or system to another. (活动或体制的)转变，变更，过渡

  • He again called for a faster changeover to a market economy. [+ to]

    他再次呼吁加快向市场经济过渡。

  • Right now we are in the changeover period between autumn and winter.

    眼下，我们正处于秋冬交替的时候。
