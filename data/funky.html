funky
fʌŋki ˈfʌŋki
Word forms: comparative funkier, superlative funkiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (音乐)乡土爵士乐风格的，放克风格的 Funky jazz, blues, or pop music has a very strong, repeated bass part. (音乐)乡土爵士乐风格的，放克风格的

  • It's a funky sort of rhythm.

    这种节奏有些乡土爵士乐风格。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不从习俗(或传统)的;不寻常的;与众不同的 If you describe something or someone as funky, you like them because they are unconventional or unusual. 不从习俗(或传统)的;不寻常的;与众不同的 [mainly US, informal, approval]

  • It had a certain funky charm, I guess, but it wasn't much of a place to raise a kid.

    我想那个地方是有种怪怪的独特魅力，但是不适合抚养孩子。
