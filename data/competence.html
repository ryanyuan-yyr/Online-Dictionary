competence
kɒmpɪtəns ˈkɑmpətəns
N-UNCOUNT 能力;才干 Competence is the ability to do something well or effectively. 能力;才干

  • His competence as an economist had been reinforced by his successful fight against inflation. [+ as]

    成功地扼制制通货膨胀的经历加强了他作为经济学家的才能。

  • We've always regarded him as a man of integrity and high professional competence.

    我们一直都认为他是个正直而且业务能力很强的人。

SYN ability, skill, talent, capacity
