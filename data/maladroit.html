maladroit
mælədrɔɪt ˌmæləˈdrɔɪt ˌmæləˈdrɔɪt
ADJ-GRADED 不灵巧的;笨拙的;迟钝的;不老练的 If you describe someone as maladroit, you mean that they are clumsy or handle situations badly. 不灵巧的;笨拙的;迟钝的;不老练的 [formal]

  • Some of his first interviews with the press were rather maladroit.

    他最早接受媒体采访时有几次显得很生涩。

SYN clumsy, awkward, bungling, inept
