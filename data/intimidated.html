intimidated
ɪntɪmɪdeɪtɪd ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 受到惊吓的;胆怯的;怯场的 Someone who feels intimidated feels frightened and lacks confidence because of the people they are with or the situation they are in. 受到惊吓的;胆怯的;怯场的

  • Women can come in here and not feel intimidated.

    女性来到这里不会感到害怕。

SYN frightened, afraid, alarmed, scared
