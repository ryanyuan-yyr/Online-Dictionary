phrase book
Word forms: plural phrase books
N-COUNT （出国旅行者用的）外语常用语手册 A phrase book is a book used by people travelling to a foreign country. It has lists of useful words and expressions, together with the translation of each word or expression in the language of that country. （出国旅行者用的）外语常用语手册
