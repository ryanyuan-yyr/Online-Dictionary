imminent
ɪmɪnənt ˈɪmənənt
ADJ (尤指不愉快的事)临近的，即将发生的，逼近的 If you say that something is imminent, especially something unpleasant, you mean it is almost certain to happen very soon. (尤指不愉快的事)临近的，即将发生的，逼近的

  • There appeared no imminent danger.

    眼前似乎没有危险。

  • They warned that an attack is imminent.

    他们警告袭击即将发生。

SYN near, coming, close, approaching  
imminence

  • The imminence of war was on everyone's mind.

    每个人都感受到了战争的迫近。
