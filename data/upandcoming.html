up-and-coming ˈʌpənˈkʌmɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 有作为的；有希望的；有前途的 Up-and-coming people are likely to be successful in the future. 有作为的；有希望的；有前途的

SYN promising, ambitious, go-getting [informal] , pushing
