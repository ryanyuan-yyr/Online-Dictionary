long-lived
ˈlɔŋˌlaɪvd ˈlɔŋˌlɪvd
(also long lived)
ADJ-GRADED 长命的；持久的；耐久的 Something that is long-lived lives or lasts for a long time. 长命的；持久的；耐久的

  • The flowers may only last a day but the plants are long-lived.

    这些花也许只开一天，但植物本身却很长寿。

  • ...huge piles of long-lived radioactive material.

    一大堆耐久的放射性物质

SYN long-lasting, enduring, full of years, old as Methuselah
