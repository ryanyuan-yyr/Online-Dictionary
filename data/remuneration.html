remuneration
rɪmjuːnəreɪʃən rɪˌmjunəˈreɪʃən
Word forms: plural remunerations
N-VAR 酬劳;薪酬 Someone's remuneration is the amount of money that they are paid for the work that they do. 酬劳;薪酬 [formal]

  • ...the continuing marked increase in the remuneration of the company's directors. [+ of]

    公司董事薪酬的持续显著增长

  • $31,000 is a generous remuneration.

    31,000美元的薪水很不错了。

SYN payment, income, earnings, salary
