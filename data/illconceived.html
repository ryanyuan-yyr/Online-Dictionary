ill-conceived
ˌɪlkənˈsiːvd ɪlkənˈsivd
ADJ-GRADED 筹划不周的;计划欠周详的 If you describe a plan or action as ill-conceived, you mean that it is likely to fail or have bad consequences because it has not been thought about carefully enough. 筹划不周的;计划欠周详的

  • ...an ill-conceived plan to close the coal mine.

    一个考虑不周的关闭煤矿的计划
