wave aside
PHRASAL VERB 对（建议、解释、想法等）置之不理 If you wave aside something such as a suggestion, explanation, or idea, you decide that it is not important enough to consider seriously. 对（建议、解释、想法等）置之不理

  • Wolfe waved my suggestion aside. [VERB noun PREP.]

    沃尔夫对我的建议置若罔闻。

  • Rachel waved aside the explanation. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    雷切尔全然不听解释。
