ordeal
ɔːʳdiːl ɔrˈdil ˈɔrˌdil
Word forms: plural ordeals
N-COUNT [usually singular, oft with poss] 煎熬；磨难；折磨 If you describe an experience or situation as an ordeal, you think it is difficult and unpleasant. 煎熬；磨难；折磨

  • ...the painful ordeal of the last eight months.

    过去8个月的痛苦煎熬

  • She described her agonising ordeal.

    她描述了自己备受折磨的苦难经历。

SYN hardship, trial, difficulty, test
