glean
gliːn glin
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gleans, present participle gleaning, past tense , past participle gleaned
VERB 耐心学习，仔细收集（信息、知识等） If you glean something such as information or knowledge, you learn or collect it slowly and patiently, and perhaps indirectly. 耐心学习，仔细收集（信息、知识等）

  • At present we're gleaning information from all sources. [VERB noun + from]

    目前，我们正从各种渠道收集信息。

  • 10,000 pages of evidence were gleaned from hundreds and hundreds of interviews. [be VERB-ed + from]

    从无数次的采访中收集到了长达一万页的证据。

SYN gather, learn, pick up, collect
