snorkel
snɔːʳkəl ˈsnɔrkəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense snorkels, present participle snorkelling, past tense , past participle snorkelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 snorkeling, snorkeled
1 N-COUNT (潜水者使用的)通气管，水下呼吸管 A snorkel is a tube through which a person swimming just under the surface of the sea can breathe. (潜水者使用的)通气管，水下呼吸管
2 VERB 用通气管潜泳 When someone snorkels, they swim under water using a snorkel. 用通气管潜泳

  • We went snorkelling, and then returned for lunch. [VERB]

    我们去潜泳了，然后回来吃午饭。
