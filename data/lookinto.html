look into
PHRASAL VERB 考察；调查；研究 If a person or organization is looking into a possible course of action, a problem, or a situation, they are finding out about it and examining the facts relating to it. 考察；调查；研究

  • He had once looked into buying his own island off Nova Scotia. [V P v-ing/n]

    他曾一度考察过在新斯科舍附近购买岛屿。

  • It should also look into the possibilities of wind-generated electricity. [V P -ing/n]

    还会对风力发电的可能性进行研究。

SYN investigate, study, research, go into
