premeditated
priːmedɪteɪtɪd priːˈmɛdɪteɪtɪd
ADJ （犯罪）有预谋的，预先策划的 A premeditated crime is planned or thought about before it is done. （犯罪）有预谋的，预先策划的

  • In a case of premeditated murder a life sentence is mandatory.

    蓄意杀人依法要判无期徒刑。

  • The attack was premeditated and preplanned.

    这次袭击是预先策划好的。

SYN planned, calculated, deliberate, considered
