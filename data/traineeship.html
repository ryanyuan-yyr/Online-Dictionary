trainee
treɪniː treɪˈni
Word forms: plural trainees
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 受培训者;实习生 A trainee is someone who is employed at a low level in a particular job in order to learn the skills needed for that job. 受培训者;实习生

  • He is a 24-year-old trainee reporter.

    他 24 岁，是一名实习记者。

  • My first job was as a graduate trainee with a bank.

    我的第一份工作是在一家银行做大学毕业实习生。
