sterling
stɜːʳlɪŋ ˈstɜrlɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 英国货币;英镑 Sterling is the money system of Great Britain. 英国货币;英镑

  • The stamps had to be paid for in sterling.

    这些邮票必须用英镑购买。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (人的品格或工作)优异的，出色的 Sterling means very good in quality; used to describe someone's work or character. (人的品格或工作)优异的，出色的 [formal, approval]

  • Those are sterling qualities to be admired in anyone.

    那些是在任何人身上都值得敬佩的优秀品质。

  • ...his years of sterling service.

    他多年出色的工作表现

SYN excellent, sound, fine, first-class
