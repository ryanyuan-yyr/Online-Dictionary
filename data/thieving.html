thieving
θiːvɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 偷窃;偷盗 Thieving is the act of stealing things from people. 偷窃;偷盗 [old-fashioned]

  • ...an ex-con who says he's given up thieving.

    自称已洗手不干的有盗窃前科的人

SYN theft, stealing, robbery, burglary  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 偷窃的;意欲偷盗的 Thieving means involved in stealing things or intending to steal something. 偷窃的;意欲偷盗的

  • He vowed to wreak vengeance on his unfaithful, thieving wife.

    他发誓要报复对自己不忠、爱偷东西的妻子。

  • ...a string of convictions from a thieving career.

    以偷盗为生背上的一连串罪名

SYN dishonest, bent [informal] , crooked [informal] , light-fingered
