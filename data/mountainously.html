mountainous
maʊntɪnəs ˈmaʊntɪnɪ ˈmaʊntənəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 多山的 A mountainous place has a lot of mountains. 多山的

  • ...the mountainous region of Campania.

    坎帕尼亚的多山地区

2 ADJ 波涛汹涌的；万丈波涛的 If you refer to mountainous seas, you are emphasizing that the sea is very rough. 波涛汹涌的；万丈波涛的 [emphasis]

  • The fishermen set off in mountainous seas.

    渔夫们冒着汹涌的海浪出海了。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 庞大的;巨大的;大量的 You use mountainous to emphasize that something is great in size, quantity, or degree. 庞大的;巨大的;大量的 [emphasis]

  • In the lighted doorway stood the mountainous figure of a woman.

    一个身材高大的女人站在灯光明亮的门口。

  • The plan is designed to reduce some of the company's mountainous debt.

    该计划旨在减少公司堆积如山的债务。

SYN huge, great, enormous, mighty
