colleague
kɒliːg ˈkɑlˌig
Word forms: plural colleagues
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 同事;同僚 Your colleagues are the people you work with, especially in a professional job. 同事;同僚

  • Without consulting his colleagues he flew from Lisbon to Split.

    没跟同事们商量，他就从里斯本飞到了斯普利特。

  • A colleague urged him to see a psychiatrist, but Faulkner refused.

    一位同事竭力劝福克纳去看精神病医生，但他拒绝了。

SYN fellow worker, partner, ally, associate
