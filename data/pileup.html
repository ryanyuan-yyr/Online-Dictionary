pile up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）堆放；（使）堆积；（使）成堆 If you pile up a quantity of things or if they pile up, they gradually form a pile. （使）堆放；（使）堆积；（使）成堆

  • Bulldozers piled up huge mounds of dirt. [VERB PREP. noun]

    推土机推积起大堆大堆的泥土。

  • Mail was still piling up at the office. [VERB PREP.]

    办公室里的邮件仍在往上堆。

SYN gather (up), collect, assemble, stack (up)  
2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）（工作、问题、损失等）积聚 If you pile up work, problems, or losses or if they pile up, you get more and more of them. （使）（工作、问题、损失等）积聚

  • Problems were piling up at work. [VERB PREP.]

    工作中的问题越积越多。

  • He piled up huge debts. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他债台高筑。
