unroll
ʌnroʊl ʌnˈrəʊl ʌnˈroʊl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unrolls, present participle unrolling, past tense , past participle unrolled
V-ERG 铺开；展开 If you unroll something such as a sheet of paper or cloth, or if it unrolls, it opens up and becomes flat when it was previously rolled in a cylindrical shape. 铺开；展开

  • I unrolled my sleeping bag as usual. [VERB noun]

    我像往常一样铺开我的睡袋。

  • Guests bring movies on tape, and show them on the screen that unrolls from the ceiling. [VERB]

    客人带来了录影带，并在从天花板上放下来的幕布上放映。
