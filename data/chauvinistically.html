chauvinistic
ʃoʊvɪnɪstɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 沙文主义的;本国至上主义的 If you describe someone as chauvinistic, you believe that they think their own country is more important and morally better than any other. 沙文主义的;本国至上主义的 [disapproval]

  • ...national narrow-mindedness and chauvinistic arrogance.

    民族主义的狭隘和大国沙文主义的傲慢

  • ...the highly chauvinistic coverage of the war.

    大国沙文主义色彩浓厚的战争报道

2 ADJ-GRADED 大男子主义的;男人至上的 If you describe a man or his behaviour as chauvinistic, you disapprove of him for believing that men are naturally better and more important than women. 大男子主义的;男人至上的 [disapproval]

  • My ex-boyfriend Anthony was very chauvinistic.

    我的前男友安东尼大男子主义思想很重。
