suppertime
sʌpəʳtaɪm ˈsʌpətaɪm
N-UNCOUNT 晚饭时间;夜宵时间 Suppertime is the period of the day when people have their supper. It can be in the early part of the evening or just before they go to bed at night. 晚饭时间;夜宵时间

  • They'll be back by suppertime.

    他们晚饭前会回来。

  • Sausages, eggs, chips and baked beans are available at suppertime in the dining room.

    餐厅晚饭时间提供香肠、鸡蛋、薯片和烘豆。
