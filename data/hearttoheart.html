heart-to-heart ˈhɑrtˈ tu hɑrt
Word forms: plural heart-to-hearts
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 贴心的谈话;坦率的谈心 A heart-to-heart is a conversation between two people, especially close friends, in which they talk freely about their feelings or personal problems. 贴心的谈话;坦率的谈心

  • I've had a heart-to-heart with him.

    我和他进行了一次推心置腹的谈话。

  • Before the wedding, I had a heart-to-heart talk with my mother.

    婚礼前，我和母亲好好谈了谈心。

SYN tête-à-tête, cosy chat, one-to-one, private conversation
