salubrious
səluːbriəs səˈlubriəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 有益健康的;环境宜人的 A place that is salubrious is pleasant and healthy. 有益健康的;环境宜人的 [formal]

  • ...your salubrious lochside hotel.

    你入住的那家舒适宜人的湖边旅馆

SYN healthy, beneficial, good for you, wholesome  
2 ADJ-GRADED 受尊敬的;得体的 Something that is described as salubrious is respectable or socially desirable. 受尊敬的;得体的 [formal]

  • ...London's less salubrious quarters.

    伦敦贫穷落后的区域

SYN agreeable, respectable, grand, pleasant
