workman
wɜːʳkmən ˈwɜrkmən
Word forms: plural workmen
N-COUNT (建造、维修房屋等的)工匠，工人 A workman is a man who works with his hands, for example building or repairing houses or roads. (建造、维修房屋等的)工匠，工人

  • In University Square workmen are building a steel fence.

    在大学广场上，工人们正在修建钢栅栏。

  • Millson saw the workman, Terry, descending the ladder.

    米尔森看见工人特里正从梯子上下来。

SYN labourer, hand, worker, employee
