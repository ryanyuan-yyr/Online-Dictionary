ill wind
N-SING (能够因祸得福的)坏事 You can describe an unfortunate event as an ill wind if someone benefits from it. The expression occurs in the proverb 'It's an ill wind that blows nobody any good'. (能够因祸得福的)坏事

  • But it's an ill wind; I recovered and married one of my nurses from that hospital.

    但是因祸得福：我恢复了健康，并娶了那所医院护理我的一位护士。
