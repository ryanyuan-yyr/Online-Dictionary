ad infinitum
æd ɪnfɪnaɪtəm æd ˌɪnfɪˈnaɪtəm æd ˌɪnfəˈnaɪtəm
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 无限地；无止境地 If something happens ad infinitum, it is repeated again and again in the same way. 无限地；无止境地

  • This cycle repeats itself ad infinitum.

    该循环周而复始，永无止境。

SYN endlessly, always, for ever (and ever), infinitely
