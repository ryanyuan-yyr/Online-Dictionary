mad
mæd mæd
Word forms: comparative madder, superlative maddest
1 ADJ 疯狂的;精神错乱的 Someone who is mad has a mind that does not work in a normal way, with the result that their behaviour is very strange. 疯狂的;精神错乱的

  • She was afraid of going mad.

    她担心自己会疯掉。

  • ...the mad old lady from down the street.

    沿街走来的那位精神失常的老太太

SYN insane, mental [slang] , crazy [informal] , nuts [slang]  
madness

  • He was driven to the brink of madness.

    他快被逼疯了。

2 ADJ-GRADED 愚蠢无比的 You use mad to describe people or things that you think are very foolish. 愚蠢无比的 [disapproval]

  • You'd be mad to work with him again.

    你要再和他一起工作那简直是疯了。

  • Isn't that a rather mad idea?

    那难道不是一个愚蠢透顶的想法吗？

SYN foolish, absurd, wild, stupid  
madness

  • It is political madness.

    这是政治愚昧。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 愤怒的;异常恼火的 If you say that someone is mad, you mean that they are very angry. 愤怒的;异常恼火的 [informal] [Also + about]

  • You're just mad at me because I don't want to go. [+ at]

    因为我不想去，你就对我火冒三丈。

  • I'm pretty mad about it, I can tell you.

    告诉你，我对这件事感到非常恼火。

SYN angry, cross, furious, irritated  
4 ADJ-GRADED 着迷的;迷恋的 If you are mad about or mad on something or someone, you like them very much indeed. 着迷的;迷恋的 [informal]

  • She's not as mad about sport as I am. [+ about]

    她不像我对体育那么痴迷。

  • He's mad about you. [+ about]

    他很迷恋你。

  • He's mad on trains. [+ on]

    他非常喜欢火车。

SYN enthusiastic, wild, crazy [informal] , nuts [slang]  
Mad is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • ...his football-mad son.

    他的那个足球迷儿子

  • He's not power-mad.

    他并不贪恋权力。

5 ADJ 发狂的;失控的 Mad behaviour is wild and uncontrolled. 发狂的;失控的

  • You only have an hour to complete the game so it's a mad dash against the clock.

    你只有一个小时来完成这场比赛，所以得铆足劲头争分夺秒。

  • The audience went mad.

    观众们十分狂热。

SYN frenzied, wild, excited, energetic  
madly

  • Down in the streets people were waving madly.

    在下面的大街上，人们正在兴奋地挥手。

6 PHRASE 使发狂;惹…发怒 If you say that someone or something drives you mad, you mean that you find them extremely annoying. 使发狂;惹…发怒 [informal]

  • There are certain things he does that drive me mad.

    他的某些举动让我很生气。

  • This itching is driving me mad.

    这种瘙痒简直快把我折磨疯了。

7 PHRASE 拼命地;全力以赴地 If you do something like mad, you do it very energetically or enthusiastically. 拼命地;全力以赴地 [informal]

  • He was weight training like mad.

    他在拼命地进行负重训练。

SYN enthusiastically, wildly, madly, furiously
