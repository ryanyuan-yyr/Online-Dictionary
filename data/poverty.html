poverty
pɒvəʳti ˈpɑvərti
1 N-UNCOUNT 贫穷；贫困 Poverty is the state of being extremely poor. 贫穷；贫困

  • According to World Bank figures, 41 per cent of Brazilians live in absolute poverty.

    根据世界银行的统计数字，41%的巴西人生活在赤贫中。

  • Garvey died in loneliness and poverty.

    加维在孤独和穷困中死去。

SYN pennilessness, want, need, distress  
2 N-SING 贫乏；短缺；低劣 You can use poverty to refer to any situation in which there is not enough of something or its quality is poor. 贫乏；短缺；低劣 [formal]

  • Britain has suffered from a poverty of ambition. [+ of]

    英国一直缺乏远大理想。

  • ...a poverty of ideas.

    思想贫乏

SYN scarcity, lack, absence, want
