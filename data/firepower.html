firepower
faɪəʳpaʊəʳ ˈfaɪəpaʊə ˈfaɪrˈpaʊər
N-UNCOUNT 火力;弹药量 The firepower of an army, ship, tank, or aircraft is the amount of ammunition it can fire. 火力;弹药量

  • The US also had superior firepower.

    美国在火力上也占据上风。

  • America has enough firepower in the area to mount sustained air strikes.

    美国在该地区拥有足够的火力发动持续的空中打击。
