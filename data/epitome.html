epitome
ɪpɪtəmi iˈpɪtəˌmi ɪˈpɪtəmi
N-SING 典型;缩影 If you say that a person or thing is the epitome of something, you are emphasizing that they are the best possible example of a particular type of person or thing. 典型;缩影 [formal, emphasis]

  • Maureen was the epitome of sophistication. [+ of]

    莫琳是个典型的老油条。

  • The Dorchester Hotel is the epitome of luxury in the heart of London.

    多切斯特酒店是伦敦市中心豪华奢侈的缩影。

SYN personification, essence, embodiment, type
