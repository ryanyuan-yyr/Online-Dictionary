levitate
levɪteɪt ˈlɛvəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense levitates, present participle levitating, past tense , past participle levitated
VERB 升空；悬浮；飘浮 If someone or something levitates, they appear to rise and float in the air without any support from other people or objects. 升空；悬浮；飘浮

  • He has claimed he can levitate. [VERB]

    他声称自己可以悬浮在空中。

  • Nina can, apparently, levitate a small ball between her hands. [VERB noun]

    尼娜看起来好像能够使一个小球悬浮在双手之间。
