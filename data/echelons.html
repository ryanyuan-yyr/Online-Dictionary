echelon
eʃəlɒn ˈɛʃəˌlɑn
Word forms: plural echelons
1 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 等级;阶层 An echelon in an organization or society is a level or rank in it. 等级;阶层 [formal]

  • ...the lower echelons of society. [+ of]

    社会等级较低的阶层

SYN level, place, office, position  
2 N-COUNT [usually supplement NOUN] （士兵、车辆、军舰、飞机等的）梯形编队,梯队 An echelon is a military formation in which soldiers, vehicles, ships, or aircraft follow each other but are spaced out sideways so that they can see ahead. （士兵、车辆、军舰、飞机等的）梯形编队,梯队
