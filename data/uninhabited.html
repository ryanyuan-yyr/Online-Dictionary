uninhabited
ʌnɪnhæbɪtɪd ˌʌnɪnˈhæbɪtɪd
ADJ 无人居住的；杳无人迹的 An uninhabited place is one where nobody lives. 无人居住的；杳无人迹的

  • ...an uninhabited island in the North Pacific.

    北太平洋上一个无人居住的岛

  • The area is largely uninhabited.

    这个地区的大部分荒无人烟。

  • ...a charred, uninhabited farmhouse.

    被烧焦的、无人居住的农舍

SYN deserted, waste, barren, desolate
