elope
ɪloʊp iˈloʊp ɪˈloʊp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense elopes, present participle eloping, past tense , past participle eloped
V-RECIP 私奔 When two people elope, they go away secretly together to get married. 私奔

  • My girlfriend Lynn and I eloped. [VERB]

    我和女友琳恩私奔了。

  • In 1912 he eloped with Frieda von Richthofen. [VERB + with]

    1912年，他和弗丽达·冯·里希特霍芬私奔了。

SYN run away, leave, escape, disappear
