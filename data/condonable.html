condone
kəndoʊn kənˈdoʊn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense condones, present participle condoning, past tense , past participle condoned
VERB 容忍，纵容（不道德行为） If someone condones behaviour that is morally wrong, they accept it and allow it to happen. 容忍，纵容（不道德行为）

  • I have never encouraged nor condoned violence. [VERB noun]

    我从不煽动也不纵容暴力行为。

  • I couldn't condone what she was doing. [VERB noun]

    我无法容忍她在做的事。

SYN overlook, excuse, forgive, pardon
