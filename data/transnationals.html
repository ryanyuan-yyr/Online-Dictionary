transnational
trænznæʃənəl trænzˈnæʃənəl trænsˈnæʃənəl trænzˈnæʃənəl
Word forms: transnationals
1 ADJ [usu ADJ n] 跨国的A transnational company has branches or owns companies in many different countries. 跨国的

  • ...large transnational corporations like those in the pharmaceutical industry.

    ...像那些制药公司的大型跨国公司。

2 N-COUNT 跨国公司Transnational is also a noun. 跨国公司

  • ...pharmaceutical transnationals such as Glaxo-Wellcome.

    ...跨国制药公司比如葛兰素威尔康。

3 ADJ 跨越国界的Something that is transnational extends beyond the borders of a single country. 跨越国界的

  • ...the activities of counterfeiting and piracy, which are transnational in nature.

    ...本质上跨越国界的造假和盗版行为。
