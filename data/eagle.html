eagle
iːgəl ˈiːɡəl ˈigəl
Word forms: plural eagles
1 N-COUNT 雕 An eagle is a large bird that lives by eating small animals. 雕
2 PHRASE [usually with poss] 锐利的目光;敏锐的观察力 If you talk about a person's eagle eye, you mean that they are watching someone or something carefully or are very good at noticing things. 锐利的目光;敏锐的观察力

  • He did the work under the eagle eye of his teacher.

    他在老师锐利目光的注视下完成了作业。

  • The Captain's eagle eye swept the room.

    船长目光如炬地扫过房间。
