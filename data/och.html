och
ɒx
CONVENTION （表示惊讶，或强调赞同或反对）啊，哦，呀，哟 Och is used to express surprise at something, or to emphasize agreement or disagreement with what has just been said. （表示惊讶，或强调赞同或反对）啊，哦，呀，哟 [Irish, Scottish]

  • 'Och be quiet then,' Shawn said.

    “哎呀，安静一下吧，”肖恩说道。

  • Och aye. I always liked him.

    口欧耶，我一直都很喜欢他。
