rejig

Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rejigs, present participle rejigging, past tense , past participle rejigged
VERB 重新安排;重新组织;调整 If someone rejigs an organization or a piece of work, they arrange or organize it in a different way, in order to improve it. 重新安排;重新组织;调整 [British]

  • ...adjustments needed to rejig the industry. [VERB noun]

    对这个行业进行重组所需要进行的调整
