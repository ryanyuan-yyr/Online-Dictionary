visitor
vɪzɪtəʳ ˈvɪzɪtər
Word forms: plural visitors
N-COUNT 访问者;参观者 A visitor is someone who is visiting a person or place. 访问者;参观者

  • The other day we had some visitors from Switzerland. [+ from]

    前几天我们接待了几位来自瑞士的参观者。

  • As a student I lived in Oxford but was a frequent visitor to Belfast. [+ to]

    求学时我住在牛津，但常常去贝尔法斯特。

SYN guest, caller, company, visitant
