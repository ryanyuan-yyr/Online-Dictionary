stand-offish
(also standoffish)
ADJ-GRADED 冷漠的;冷淡的;疏远的 If you say that someone is stand-offish, you mean that they behave in a formal and rather unfriendly way. 冷漠的;冷淡的;疏远的 [disapproval]

  • He can be quite stand-offish and rude, even to his friends.

    他有时会很冷淡，很粗鲁，甚至对自己的朋友也是如此。

SYN reserved, remote, distant, aloof
