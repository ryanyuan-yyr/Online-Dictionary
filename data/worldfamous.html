world-famous
ˌwɜːldˈfeɪməs
ADJ 世界著名的;举世闻名的 Someone or something that is world-famous is known about by people all over the world. 世界著名的;举世闻名的

  • ...the world-famous Hollywood Bowl.

    举世闻名的好莱坞露天剧场

  • Harefield Hospital has become world-famous for its pioneering heart transplant surgery.

    哈瑞福德医院以其开创性的心脏移植手术闻名于世。
