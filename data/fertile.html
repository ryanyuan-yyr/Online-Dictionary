fertile
fɜːʳtaɪl ˈfɜrˌtaɪl -təl ˈfɜrtəl
1 ADJ-GRADED (土地、土壤)肥沃的，丰腴的 Land or soil that is fertile is able to support the growth of a large number of strong healthy plants. (土地、土壤)肥沃的，丰腴的

  • ...fertile soil.

    肥沃的土地

  • ...the rolling fertile countryside of East Cork.

    科克郡东部起伏的乡间沃土

SYN productive, rich, flowering, lush  
fertility

  • He was able to bring large sterile acreages back to fertility.

    他能将大片的不毛之地重新变为沃土。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 主意多的;富有创意的 A fertile mind or imagination is able to produce a lot of good, original ideas. 主意多的;富有创意的

  • ...a product of Flynn's fertile imagination.

    弗林丰富想象力的产物

  • A chess player must have a fertile imagination and rich sense of fantasy.

    一个棋手必须有丰富的想象力，并善于奇思妙想。

SYN creative, productive, prolific, inventive  
3 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] (环境或条件)易于…的，有助于…的，适于…的 A situation or environment that is fertile in relation to a particular activity or feeling encourages the activity or feeling. (环境或条件)易于…的，有助于…的，适于…的

  • ...a fertile breeding ground for this kind of violent racism.

    易于滋生这种极端种族主义的温床

4 ADJ-GRADED 能生育的;可繁殖的 A person or animal that is fertile is able to reproduce and have babies or young. 能生育的;可繁殖的

  • The operation cannot be reversed to make her fertile again.

    手术结果不可逆转，无法让她恢复生育能力。

SYN able to conceive, able to reproduce, able to have children, able to have babies  
fertility

  • Doctors will tell you that pregnancy is the only sure test for fertility.

    医生会告诉你怀孕是检测生育能力的唯一可靠方法。
