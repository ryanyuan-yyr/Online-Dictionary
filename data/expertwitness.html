expert witness
ˈɛkspɜːt ˈwɪtnəs
Word forms: expert witnesses
N-COUNT 专家证人In a court case, an expert witness is someone such as a doctor or other professional who testifies about and gives opinions on subjects and issues that have been raised in the particular court case. 专家证人

  • Flynn often testifies as an expert witness in court cases.

    弗莱恩经常以专家证人的身份出庭作证。
