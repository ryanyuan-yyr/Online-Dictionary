idle away
PHRASAL VERB 虚度，消磨(时光) If you idle away a period of time, you spend it doing very little. 虚度，消磨(时光)

  • Residents were mowing their lawns, washing their cars and otherwise idling away a pleasant, sunny day. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    居民们有的在刈草,有的在洗车,还有的在悠闲地享受阳光灿烂的怡人时光。

  • He idled the time away in dreamy thought. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他在虚幻的空想中虚度了光阴。
