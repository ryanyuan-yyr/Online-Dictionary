short-wave ˈʃɔːtweɪv
(also short wave,shortwave)
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 短波 Short-wave is a range of short radio wavelengths used for broadcasting. 短波

  • I use the short-wave radio to get the latest war news.

    我用短波收音机收听最新的战事新闻。

  • Until now, the BBC has only been available in South Africa on short wave.

    直到目前为止，南非只能以短波形式收听英国广播公司的节目。
