repression
rɪpreʃən rɪˈprɛʃən rɪˈprɛʃən
Word forms: plural repressions
1 N-UNCOUNT 镇压;压制;控制 Repression is the use of force to restrict and control a society or other group of people. 镇压;压制;控制 [disapproval]

  • ...a society conditioned by violence and repression.

    受暴力和镇压制约的社会

  • ...the repressions of the 1930s.

    20世纪30年代的镇压

SYN subjugation, control, constraint, domination  
2 N-UNCOUNT (情感，尤指性欲的)压抑，控制 Repression of feelings, especially sexual ones, is a person's unwillingness to allow themselves to have natural feelings and desires. (情感，尤指性欲的)压抑，控制

  • ...the repression of his feelings about men. [+ of]

    他对男人情感的压抑
