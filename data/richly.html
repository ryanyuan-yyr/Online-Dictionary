richly
rɪtʃli ˈrɪtʃli
1 ADV-GRADED [usu ADV -ed/adj] （色彩）艳丽地，强烈地;（味道、香味）浓郁地 If something is richly coloured, flavoured, or perfumed, it has a pleasantly strong colour, flavour, or perfume. （色彩）艳丽地，强烈地;（味道、香味）浓郁地

  • ...an opulent display of richly coloured fabrics.

    各种色彩艳丽的织物的华美展示

  • ...the richly flavoured, hearty foods of the region.

    该地区风味浓郁、品种丰盛的食物

2 ADV-GRADED [usu ADV -ed/adj] （装饰）精美地，华美地，华丽地 If something is richly decorated, patterned, or furnished, it has a lot of elaborate and beautiful decoration, patterns, or furniture. （装饰）精美地，华美地，华丽地

  • Coffee steamed in the richly decorated silver pot.

    咖啡在装饰精美的银壶中冒着热气。

  • ...a richly illustrated introduction to the art of the Islamic world.

    对伊斯兰国家艺术的配有精美插图的介绍

3 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB before verb, ADVERB -ed] 非常;充分;完全 If you say that someone richly deserves an award, success, or victory, you approve of what they have done and feel very strongly that they deserve it. 非常;充分;完全 [feelings]

  • He achieved the success he so richly deserved.

    他取得了自己完全应得的成功。

  • ...a deeply affecting performance that won him a richly deserved Oscar.

    让他理所当然地获得了奥斯卡奖的那感人至深的表演

SYN fully, well, thoroughly, amply  
4 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB -ed] 充分地;丰富地 If a person or place is richly endowed or supplied with something, they have a lot of it. 充分地;丰富地

  • ...a boy richly endowed with courage.

    一个充满勇气的男孩

  • The hands are richly supplied with nerve endings.

    手上布满了神经末梢。

5 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB before verb, ADVERB -ed] （回报）丰厚地，令人惬意地 If you are richly rewarded for doing something, you get something very valuable or pleasant in return for doing it. （回报）丰厚地，令人惬意地

  • It is a difficult book to read, but it richly rewards the effort.

    这本书很难读懂，但是非常值得一读。

  • Their caution has been richly rewarded.

    他们的谨慎得到了很好的回报。
