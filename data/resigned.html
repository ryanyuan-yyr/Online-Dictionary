resigned
rɪzaɪnd rɪˈzaɪnd
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 屈从的;顺从的;勉强接受的;逆来顺受的 If you are resigned to an unpleasant situation or fact, you accept it without complaining because you realize that you cannot change it. 屈从的;顺从的;勉强接受的;逆来顺受的

  • He is resigned to the noise and mess. [+ to]

    他勉强接受了这里的喧嚣和脏乱。

  • Pauline was already resigned to losing her home.

    保利娜已经无奈地接受了失去家园的事实。

SYN stoical, patient, subdued, long-suffering  
resignedly

  • 'I know you don't believe me,' I said resignedly.

    “我知道你不相信我，”我无可奈何地说。
