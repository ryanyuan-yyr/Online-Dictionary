warlike
wɔːʳlaɪk ˈwɔrˌlaɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 好战的 Warlike people seem aggressive and eager to start a war. 好战的

  • The Scythians were a fiercely warlike people.

    西徐亚人勇猛好战。

  • ...hopes of a peaceful solution despite increased warlike rhetoric from both sides.

    双方唇枪舌剑，火药味儿越来越浓，但仍然存在和平解决的希望

SYN belligerent, military, aggressive, hostile  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 作战用的;打仗用的 A warlike object or activity relates to fighting a war. 作战用的;打仗用的

  • They were armed with spears and other warlike implements.

    他们用矛和其他作战工具武装自己。

  • ...the warlike preparations of Mr Churchill.

    丘吉尔先生的应战准备
