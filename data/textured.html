textured
tekstʃəʳd ˈtɛkstʃərd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 质地粗糙的;有纹理的 A textured surface is not smooth, but has a particular texture, for example, it feels rough. 质地粗糙的;有纹理的

  • ...a mixture of textured and lacy stitches.

    织纹状与网眼状交织的针脚

  • The shoe's sole had a slightly textured surface.

    鞋底表面稍感粗糙。

Textured is also a combining form.(亦可用于构词)

  • This kind of mixer produces light, silky, even-textured batters.

    这种搅拌机能搅拌出稀薄、柔滑、质地均匀的面糊。

  • ...a medium-textured toothbrush.

    中毛牙刷
