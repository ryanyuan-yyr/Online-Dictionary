swimmer
swɪməʳ
Word forms: plural swimmers
N-COUNT 游泳运动员;游泳爱好者;游泳者 A swimmer is a person who swims, especially for sport or pleasure, or a person who is swimming. 游泳运动员;游泳爱好者;游泳者

  • You don't have to worry about me. I'm a good swimmer.

    你不用担心我。我水性很好。

  • She wants to become an Olympic swimmer.

    她想成为一名能够参加奥运会的游泳运动员。

  • Apart from a few swimmers, the rest of us stretched out on the bank to sunbathe.

    除了几个人在游泳外，我们其他人伸开四肢躺在岸边晒日光浴。
