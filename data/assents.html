assent
əsent əˈsɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense assents, present participle assenting, past tense , past participle assented
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 同意；准许；批准 If someone gives their assent to something that has been suggested, they formally agree to it. 同意；准许；批准

  • He gave his assent to the proposed legislation. [+ to/for]

    他批准了提请审议的法案。

  • Without their assent a political settlement cannot be reached.

    没有他们的同意，不可能达成政治上的和解。

SYN agreement, accord, sanction, approval  
2 VERB 同意；赞成 If you assent to something, you agree to it or agree with it. 同意；赞成 [Also VERB]

  • I assented to the request of the American publishers to write this book. [VERB + to]

    我同意了美国出版商的要求，答应写这本书。

  • 'But it's good grub,' he added. 'You're right,' Pantieri assented. [VERB with quote]

    “但是伙食不错，”他补充道。“没错，”潘蒂埃里表示同意。
