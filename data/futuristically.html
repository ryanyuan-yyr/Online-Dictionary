futuristic
fjuːtʃərɪstɪk ˌfjutʃərˈɪstɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 未来派的;极其前卫现代的 Something that is futuristic looks or seems very modern and unusual, like something from the future. 未来派的;极其前卫现代的

  • The theatre is a futuristic steel and glass structure.

    这家剧院是钢筋和玻璃结构的未来派建筑。

  • ...futuristic cars.

    超前设计的汽车

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (电影或小说)幻想未来的 A futuristic film or book tells a story that is set in the future, when things are different. (电影或小说)幻想未来的

  • ...the futuristic hit film, 'Terminator 2'.

    轰动一时的幻想片《终结者 2》
