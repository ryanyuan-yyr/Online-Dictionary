activism
æktɪvɪzəm ˈæktəˌvɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 激进主义；行动主义 Activism is the process of campaigning in public or working for an organization in order to bring about political or social change. 激进主义；行动主义

  • He believed in political activism to achieve justice.

    他相信通过政治激进主义可以赢得正义。

SYN action, force, exertion
