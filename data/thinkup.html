think up
PHRASAL VERB 想出，想到(主意、计划) If you think something up, for example an idea or plan, you invent it using mental effort. 想出，想到(主意、计划)

  • Julian has been thinking up new ways of raising money. [VERB PREP. noun]

    朱利安想出了不少筹款的新办法。

  • 'Where do you get that idea about the piano?' — 'Well, I just thought it up.' [VERB noun PREP.]

    “关于钢琴的那个主意你是从哪儿得来的？”—“哦，就这么想出来的。”

SYN devise, create, imagine, manufacture
