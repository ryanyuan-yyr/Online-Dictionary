resonant
rezənənt ˈrɛzənənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 洪亮的;嘹亮的 A sound that is resonant is deep and strong. 洪亮的;嘹亮的

  • His voice sounded oddly resonant in the empty room.

    他的声音在这空荡荡的房间里听起来异常嘹亮。

  • He responded with a resonant laugh.

    他报以洪亮的笑声。

SYN sonorous, full, clear, rich  
2 ADJ 有特殊意义的;引起共鸣的 Something that is resonant has a special meaning or is particularly important to people. 有特殊意义的;引起共鸣的 [literary]

  • It is a country resonant with cinematic potential, from its architecture to its landscape. [+ with]
