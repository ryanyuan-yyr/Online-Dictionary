barter
bɑːʳtəʳ ˈbɑrtər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense barters, present participle bartering, past tense , past participle bartered
VERB 以物易物；交换（货物） If you barter goods, you exchange them for other goods, rather than selling them for money. 以物易物；交换（货物）

  • They have been bartering wheat for cotton and timber. [VERB noun + for]

    他们一直用小麦交换棉花和木材。

  • The market-place and street were crowded with those who'd come to barter. [VERB]

    市场和街道上挤满了前来做物物交换的人。

  • Traders came to barter horses. [VERB noun]

    商人们前来用马匹交换其他物品。

SYN trade, sell, exchange, switch  
Barter is also a noun.

  • Overall, barter is a very inefficient means of organizing transactions.

    总之，易货贸易是一种效率很低的交易形式。

  • ...a barter economy.

    易货经济
