thresh
θreʃ θrɛʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense threshes, present participle threshing, past tense , past participle threshed
VERB [usually passive] 给(玉米、小麦、水稻等)脱粒;打谷 When a cereal such as corn, wheat, or rice is threshed, it is beaten in order to separate the grains from the rest of the plant. 给(玉米、小麦、水稻等)脱粒;打谷 [Also VERB]

  • The corn was still sown, cut and threshed as it was a hundred years ago. [be VERB-ed]

    玉米的播种、收割、脱粒方法还和一百年前一样。
