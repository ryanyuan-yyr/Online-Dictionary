nuptial
nʌpʃəl ˈnʌpʃəl ˈnʌptʃəl
Word forms: plural nuptials
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 结婚的;婚姻的 Nuptial is used to refer to things relating to a wedding or to marriage. 结婚的;婚姻的 [old-fashioned]

  • I went to the room which he had called the nuptial chamber.

    我走进了他称之为洞房的房间。

SYN marital, wedding, wedded, bridal  
2 N-PLURAL [usually with poss] 结婚典礼;婚礼 Someone's nuptials are their wedding celebrations. 结婚典礼;婚礼 [old-fashioned]

  • She became immersed in planning her nuptials.

    她一门心思筹备自己的婚礼。
