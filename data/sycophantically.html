sycophantic
sɪkəfæntɪk
ADJ-GRADED 阿谀奉承的;谄媚的;拍马屁的 If you describe someone as sycophantic, you disapprove of them because they flatter people who are more important and powerful than they are in order to gain an advantage for themselves. 阿谀奉承的;谄媚的;拍马屁的 [disapproval]

  • ...his clique of sycophantic friends.

    他那些互相吹捧的朋党

  • We heard the sound of sycophantic laughter.

    我们听到了谄媚的笑声。

SYN obsequious, grovelling, ingratiating, servile
