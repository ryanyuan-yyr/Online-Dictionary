smiley
smaɪli ˈsmaɪli
Word forms: plural smileys
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 爱笑的;微笑的 A smiley person smiles a lot or is smiling. 爱笑的;微笑的 [informal]

  • Two smiley babies are waiting for their lunch.

    两个宝宝正笑眯眯地等着他们的午饭。

2 N-COUNT (电子邮件中用来表示心情的)表情符 A smiley is a symbol used in e-mail to show how someone is feeling. :-) is a smiley showing happiness. (电子邮件中用来表示心情的)表情符 [computing]
