subsidiarity
səbsɪdiærɪti səbˌsɪdɪˈærɪtɪ
N-UNCOUNT 权力自主原则，权力下放原则(允许成员针对影响自身的问题自作主张) Subsidiarity is the principle of allowing the individual members of a large organization to make decisions on issues that affect them, rather than leaving those decisions to be made by the whole group. 权力自主原则，权力下放原则(允许成员针对影响自身的问题自作主张) [technical]

  • The chancellor knows that the principle of subsidiarity must be guaranteed and shown to work.

    财政大臣很清楚，必须保证权力下放原则得到贯彻执行并使之奏效。
