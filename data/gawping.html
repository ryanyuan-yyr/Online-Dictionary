gawp
gɔːp gɔp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gawps, present participle gawping, past tense , past participle gawped
VERB 同gawk To gawp means the same as to gawk . 同gawk [British, informal]

  • At weekends the roads are jammed with holidaymakers coming to gawp at the parade.

    周末的时候，公路上便挤满了前来观看游行的度假游客。

  • Thorpe could only stand and gawp. [VERB]

    索普只是站着傻看。
