push aside
PHRASAL VERB 把…搁置一边；忽视 If you push something aside, you ignore it or refuse to think about it. 把…搁置一边；忽视

  • By pushing aside unpleasant thoughts they merely repress these thoughts. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们对不愉快的想法不予理会，其结果仅仅是压制了这些想法。

  • For a moment her husband came to mind, but she pushed the guilt aside. [VERB noun PREP.]

    偶尔她会想起丈夫，但她很快会把愧疚抛到一边。
