reappear
riːəpɪəʳ ˌriːəˈpɪə
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reappears, present participle reappearing, past tense , past participle reappeared
VERB 重现；再现；再次发生 When people or things reappear, they return again after they have been away or out of sight for some time. 重现；再现；再次发生

  • Thirty seconds later she reappeared and beckoned them forward. [VERB]

    30秒后她又出现了，示意他们往前来。
