immobile
ɪmoʊbaɪl -bəl ɪˈmoʊbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 固定的;静止的 Someone or something that is immobile is completely still. 固定的;静止的

  • Joe remained as immobile as if he had been carved out of rock.

    乔一动不动,仿佛一尊石头雕像。

SYN motionless, still, stationary, fixed  
immobility

  • Hyde maintained the rigid immobility of his shoulders.

    海德保持双肩一动不动。

2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 瘫痪的;不能移动的 Someone or something that is immobile is unable to move or unable to be moved. 瘫痪的;不能移动的

  • A riding accident left him immobile.

    一场骑马意外事故导致他瘫痪。

  • ...a very heavy or immobile object.

    非常沉重或难以移动的物体

SYN crippled, paralysed, incapacitated, laid up [informal]  
immobility

  • Again, the pain locked me into immobility.

    我又一次痛得动弹不得。
