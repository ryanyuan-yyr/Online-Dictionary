art-house
(also arthouse)
ADJ [ADJ n] （电影）严肃的，艺术的，非娱乐性的 An art-house film is a film that is intended to be a serious artistic work rather than a piece of popular entertainment. （电影）严肃的，艺术的，非娱乐性的

  • ...a quirky art-house film.

    一部另类艺术片

  • The movies he made became art-house classics.

    他拍摄的影片成了艺术片的经典之作。
