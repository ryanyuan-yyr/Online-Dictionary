expansionary
ɪkspænʃənri ˈɛkˈspænʃəˌnɛri ɪkˈspænʃəˌnɛri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 扩张性的；刺激经济扩张的 Expansionary economic policies are intended to expand the economy of a country. 扩张性的；刺激经济扩张的

  • They demanded a more expansionary economy to combat rising unemployment.

    他们要求发展扩张性的经济以应对升高的失业率。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （领土）扩张的 Expansionary policies or actions are intended to increase the amount of land that a particular country rules. （领土）扩张的 [disapproval]

  • ...America's concerns about Soviet expansionary objectives.

    美国对苏联领土扩张目标的担忧
