greasy

Word forms: comparative greasier, superlative greasiest
ADJ-GRADED 有油渍的；油污的；涂了油脂的 Something that is greasy has grease on it or in it. 有油渍的；油污的；涂了油脂的

  • ...the problem of greasy hair.

    油性头发的问题

    他的双肘撑在满是油污的柜台上。

SYN fatty, slick, slippery, oily
