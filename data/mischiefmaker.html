mischief-maker
ˈmɪstʃɪfˌmeɪkə ˈmɪstʃɪfˌmeɪkər
Word forms: plural mischief-makers
N-COUNT 挑拨离间的人;搬弄是非者 If you say that someone is a mischief-maker, you are criticizing them for saying or doing things which are intended to cause trouble between people. 挑拨离间的人;搬弄是非者 [disapproval]

  • The letter had come from an unknown mischief-maker.

    这封信来自一个匿名的离间者。
