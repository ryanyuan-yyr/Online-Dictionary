yaw
jɔː jɔ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense yaws, present participle yawing, past tense , past participle yawed
VERB （飞机或船）偏航，偏离航线 If an aircraft or a ship yaws, it turns to one side so that it changes the direction in which it is moving. （飞机或船）偏航，偏离航线 [technical]

  • As the plane climbed to 370 feet, it started yawing. [VERB]

    飞机攀升到370英尺时，开始偏航。

  • He spun the steering-wheel so that we yawed from side to side. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他急速打着舵轮，把我们搞得摇来晃去的。
