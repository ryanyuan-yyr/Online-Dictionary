auditorium
ɔːdɪtɔːriəm ˌɔdəˈtɔriəm
Word forms: plural auditoriums, plural auditoria/ɔːdɪtɔːriə/
1 N-COUNT （剧院或音乐厅的）观众席 An auditorium is the part of a theatre or concert hall where the audience sits. （剧院或音乐厅的）观众席
SYN hall, theatre, chamber, concert hall  
2 N-COUNT 会堂;礼堂;音乐厅 An auditorium is a large room, hall, or building which is used for events such as meetings and concerts. 会堂;礼堂;音乐厅 [US]

  • The Albert Hall is a huge auditorium.

    艾伯特音乐厅是一个大型音乐厅。
