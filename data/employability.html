employable
ɪmplɔɪəbəl ɛmˈplɔɪəbəl ɪmˈplɔɪəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可雇用的;具备受雇条件的 Someone who is employable has skills or abilities that are likely to make someone want to give them a job. 可雇用的;具备受雇条件的

  • People need basic education if they are to become employable.

    想要有人雇用，人们需要接受基础教育。

  • ...employable adults.

    具备就业条件的成年人
