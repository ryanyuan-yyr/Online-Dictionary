tenuous
tenjuəs ˈtɛnjuəs ˈtɛnjəwəs
ADJ-GRADED (联系)脆弱的;(理由)站不住脚的，牵强的;(地位)不稳固的 If you describe something such as a connection, a reason, or someone's position as tenuous, you mean that it is very uncertain or weak. (联系)脆弱的;(理由)站不住脚的，牵强的;(地位)不稳固的

  • The cultural and historical links between the many provinces were seen to be very tenuous.

    许多省份之间的文化和历史纽带被认为是很脆弱的。

  • This decision puts the President in a somewhat tenuous position.

    这个决定使得总统的位置有些岌岌可危。

SYN slight, weak, dubious, shaky  
tenuously

  • The sub-plots are only tenuously interconnected.

    次要情节之间的联系有点牵强。
