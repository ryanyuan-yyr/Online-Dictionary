insurmountable
ɪnsəʳmaʊntəbəl ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbəl
ADJ 无法解决的;难以克服的;不可逾越的 A problem that is insurmountable is so great that it cannot be dealt with successfully. 无法解决的;难以克服的;不可逾越的

  • The crisis doesn't seem like an insurmountable problem.

    这次危机似乎并不难以克服。

SYN insuperable, impossible, overwhelming, hopeless
