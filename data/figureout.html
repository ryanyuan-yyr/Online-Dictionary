figure out
PHRASAL VERB 想出; 弄明白If you figure out a solution to a problem or the reason for something, you succeed in solving it or understanding it. 想出; 弄明白 [informal]

  • It took them about one month to figure out how to start the equipment. [VERB PREP. wh]

    他们用了大约一个月的时间才弄明白如何启动这台设备。

  • They're trying to figure out the politics of this whole situation. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们正试图弄明白整个形势的利害关系。

SYN calculate, reckon, work out, compute
