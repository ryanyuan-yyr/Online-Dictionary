pallor
pæləʳ ˈpælər
N-SING （脸色或肤色的）苍白 If you refer to the pallor of someone's face or skin, you mean that it is pale and unhealthy. （脸色或肤色的）苍白

  • The deathly pallor of her skin had been replaced by the faintest flush of color. [+ of]

    她如死灰般苍白的皮肤开始现出淡淡的血色。

  • My face had a greenish pallor.

    我的脸色白中带绿。

SYN paleness, whiteness, lack of colour, wanness
