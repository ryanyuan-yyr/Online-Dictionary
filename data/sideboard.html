sideboard
saɪdbɔːʳd saɪdˌbɔrd
Word forms: plural sideboards
1 N-COUNT 餐具柜;餐柜 A sideboard is a long cupboard which is about the same height as a table. Sideboards are usually kept in dining rooms to put plates and glasses in. 餐具柜;餐柜
2 N-PLURAL 同 sideburns Sideboards are the same as sideburns . 同 sideburns [British]
