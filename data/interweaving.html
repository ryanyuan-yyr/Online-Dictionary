interweave
ɪntəʳwiːv ˌɪntərˈwiv ˈɪntərˌwiv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense interweaves, present participle interweaving, past tense interwove, past participle interwoven
V-RECIP-ERG (使)交织;(使)紧密结合 If two or more things are interwoven or interweave, they are very closely connected or are combined with each other. (使)交织;(使)紧密结合 [Also VERB n + with]

  • For these people, land is inextricably interwoven with life itself. [be VERB-ed + with]

    对这些人来说,土地与生活本身有着不可分割的密切关系。

  • Complex family relationships interweave with a murder plot in this ambitious new novel. [VERB + with]

    在这本布局恢宏的新小说中,错综复杂的家庭关系与一起谋杀阴谋交织在一起。

  • The programme successfully interweaves words and pictures. [VERB noun]

    节目成功地把语言和图片结合了起来。

  • Social structures are not discrete objects; they overlap and interweave. [VERB]

    社会结构之间不是离散的,他们相互重叠交织。

SYN intertwine, blend, cross, braid
