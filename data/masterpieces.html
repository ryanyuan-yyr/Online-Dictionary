masterpiece
mɑːstəʳpiːs mæs- ˈmɑːstəˌwɜːk ˈmæstərˌpis
Word forms: plural masterpieces
1 N-COUNT 杰作;名著 A masterpiece is an extremely good painting, novel, film, or other work of art. 杰作;名著

  • His book, I must add, is a masterpiece.

    我必须补充一点：他的书是一部杰作。

  • ...masterpieces by artists like Rembrandt, Raphael and Ingres.

    伦勃朗、拉斐尔和安格尔这些画家的杰作

SYN classic, tour de force, pièce de résistance, magnum opus  
2 N-COUNT [with poss] (画家、作家或作曲家的)最佳作品，代表作 An artist's, writer's, or composer's masterpiece is the best work that they have ever produced. (画家、作家或作曲家的)最佳作品，代表作
3 N-COUNT 典范 A masterpiece is an extremely clever or skilful example of something. 典范

  • The whole thing was a masterpiece of crowd management. [+ of]

    整件事情就是人群管理的典范。
