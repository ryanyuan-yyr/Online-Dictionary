sceptic
skeptɪk ˈskɛptɪk ˈskɛptɪk
Word forms: plural sceptics
in AM, use 美国英语用 skeptic
N-COUNT 持怀疑态度的人;怀疑论者 A sceptic is a person who has doubts about things that other people believe. 持怀疑态度的人;怀疑论者

  • He was a born sceptic.

    他天生多疑。

  • But he now has to convince sceptics that he has a serious plan.

    但是他现在得让怀疑者相信他有一个严肃的计划。

SYN doubter, cynic, scoffer, questioner  
Quotations
I am too much of a sceptic to deny the possibility of anythingT.H. Huxley
