abate
əbeɪt əˈbeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abates, present participle abating, past tense , past participle abated
VERB 减弱；减轻；减退 If something bad or undesirable abates, it becomes much less strong or severe. 减弱；减轻；减退 [formal]

  • The storms had abated by the time they rounded Cape Horn. [VERB]

    他们绕过合恩角时，暴风雨已经减弱。

  • ...a crime wave that shows no sign of abating. [VERB]

    丝毫没有减弱迹象的犯罪势头
