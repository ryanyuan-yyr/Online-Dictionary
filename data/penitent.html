penitent
penɪtənt ˈpɛnɪtənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 悔过的； 忏悔的；愧疚的 Someone who is penitent is very sorry for something wrong that they have done, and regrets their actions. 悔过的； 忏悔的；愧疚的 [literary]

  • Robert Gates sat before them, almost penitent about the past. [+ about]

    罗伯特·盖茨坐在他们面前，对过去的事几乎无颜以对。

  • She is deeply penitent.

    她深感愧疚。

  • ...penitent criminals.

    悔过的罪犯

SYN repentant, sorry, apologetic, abject  
penitently

  • He sat penitently in his chair by the window.

    他懊悔地坐在靠窗的椅子上。
