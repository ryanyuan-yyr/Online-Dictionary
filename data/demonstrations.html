demonstration
demənstreɪʃən ˈdɛmənˈstreɪʃən
Word forms: plural demonstrations
1 N-COUNT 示威;示威游行（或集会） A demonstration is a march or gathering which people take part in to show their opposition to something or their support for something. 示威;示威游行（或集会）

  • Riot police broke up a demonstration by students.

    防暴警察驱散了示威的学生。

  • ...mass demonstrations.

    群众性示威

SYN march, protest, rally, sit-in  
2 N-COUNT 示范;演示 A demonstration of something is a talk by someone who shows you how to do it or how it works. 示范;演示

  • ...a cookery demonstration.

    烹饪示范

  • ...demonstrations of new products.

    新产品的演示

SYN display, show, performance [informal] , explanation  
3 N-COUNT 证明;论证;表明;说明 A demonstration of a fact or situation is a clear proof of it. 证明;论证;表明;说明

  • This is a clear demonstration of how technology has changed. [+ of]

    这清楚地表明了技术已有了怎样的进步。

  • This was a very practical demonstration of why the Army trained people to be disciplined.

    这很实际地说明了为什么军队要将士兵训练得遵规守纪。

4 N-COUNT (特质的)显示;(情感的)表露 A demonstration of a quality or feeling is an expression of it. (特质的)显示;(情感的)表露

  • There's been no public demonstration of opposition to the President. [+ of]

    没有人对总统公开表示反对。

  • ...physical demonstrations of affection.

    身体上的亲昵

SYN exhibition, display, showing, expression
