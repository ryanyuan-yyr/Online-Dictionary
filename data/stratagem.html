stratagem
strætədʒəm ˈstrætədʒəm
Word forms: plural stratagems
N-COUNT (通常指蒙蔽别人的)花招，计谋，诡计 A stratagem is a plan that is intended to achieve a particular effect, often by deceiving people. (通常指蒙蔽别人的)花招，计谋，诡计 [formal]

  • Trade discounts may be used as a competitive stratagem to secure customer loyalty.

    商业折扣可以用作维护顾客忠诚度的一种竞争手段。

SYN trick, scheme, manoeuvre, plan
