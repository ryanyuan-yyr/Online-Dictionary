ground zero
N-UNCOUNT 核爆点; 也指在9.11恐怖袭击中被毁的世贸中心People sometimes use ground zero to refer to the site of a disaster such as a nuclear explosion. However, it is used especially in relation to the destruction of the World Trade Center in New York on September 11, 2001. 核爆点; 也指在9.11恐怖袭击中被毁的世贸中心 [mainly US]

  • Within a half-mile from ground zero, death from radiation poisoning will occur within twelve hours.

    在核爆点半英里内，辐射中毒引起的死亡会在十二小时内发生。

  • ...Liberty Travel, located just blocks from ground zero in Manhattan.

    ...自由旅行社位于曼哈顿，距离在911恐怖袭击中被毁的世贸中心仅几个街区。
