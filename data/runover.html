run over
PHRASAL VERB (车辆)撞倒，碾过，轧过 If a vehicle or its driver runs a person or animal over, it knocks them down or drives over them. (车辆)撞倒，碾过，轧过

  • You can always run him over and make it look like an accident. [VERB noun PREP.]

    你总可以选择撞死他，并使之看起来像一起车祸。

  • He ran over a six-year-old child as he was driving back from a party. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当他参加完聚会开车回家时，撞倒了一个6岁的小孩。

  • ...if I were ever run over by a bus. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果我被公交车撞倒
