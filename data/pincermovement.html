pincer movement
ˈpɪnsə
Word forms: plural pincer movements
N-COUNT 钳形攻势；夹攻 A pincer movement is an attack by an army or other group in which they attack their enemies in two places at once with the aim of surrounding them. 钳形攻势；夹攻

  • They are moving in a pincer movement to cut the republic in two.

    他们正展开钳形攻势，要把该共和国一分为二。
