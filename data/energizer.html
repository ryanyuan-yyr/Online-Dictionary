energize
enəʳdʒaɪz ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense energizes, present participle energizing, past tense , past participle energized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 energise
VERB 使充满活力;使精力充沛 To energize someone means to give them the enthusiasm and determination to do something. 使充满活力;使精力充沛

  • He helped energize and mobilize millions of people around the nation. [VERB noun]

    他帮助激励和动员了全国数百万人。

  • I am completely energized and feeling terrific. [be VERB-ed]

    我充满了活力，感觉非常好。
