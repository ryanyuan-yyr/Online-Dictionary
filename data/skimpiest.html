skimpy
skɪmpi ˈskɪmpi
Word forms: comparative skimpier, superlative skimpiest
ADJ-GRADED 短小的;少量的 Something that is skimpy is too small in size or quantity. 短小的;少量的

  • ...skimpy underwear.

    太过暴露的内衣

  • They suffered long hours, unsafe working conditions and skimpy pay.

    他们在不安全的工作环境下长时间辛苦劳作，却只有微薄的工资。

SYN inadequate, insufficient, scant, meagre
