credence
kriːdəns ˈkridəns
1 N-UNCOUNT (理论或故事的)可信性，真实性 If something lends or gives credence to a theory or story, it makes it easier to believe. (理论或故事的)可信性，真实性 [formal]

  • Good studies are needed to lend credence to the notion that genuine progress can be made in this important field. [+ to]

    需要进行认真的研究，以让人们相信在这一重要领域可以取得真正的进展。

SYN credibility, credit, plausibility, believability  
2 N-UNCOUNT 信任;相信 If you give credence to a theory or story, you believe it. 信任;相信 [formal]

  • You're surely not giving any credence to this story of Hythe's? [+ to]

    你肯定不会相信海斯的说法吧？
