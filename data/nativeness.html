native
neɪtɪv ˈneɪtɪv
Word forms: plural natives
1 ADJ [ADJ n] . 出生地的;儿时居住的 Your native country or area is the country or area where you were born and brought up . 出生地的;儿时居住的

  • It was his first visit to his native country since 1948.

    这是自1948年以来他首次回到自己的祖国。

  • Mother Teresa visited her native Albania.

    特雷莎修女访问了她的祖国阿尔巴尼亚。

2 N-COUNT 本国人;本地人 A native of a particular country or region is someone who was born in that country or region. 本国人;本地人

  • Dr Aubin is a native of St Blaise. [+ of]

    奥宾博士是圣布莱斯人。

  • ...two Dutch volunteer workmen, natives of Tilburg.

    来自蒂尔堡的两个荷兰志愿工人

Native is also an adjective.

  • Joshua Halpern is a native Northern Californian.

    乔舒亚·哈尔彭是土生土长的北加州人。

  • ...men and women native to countries such as Japan.

    出生在像日本这样国家的人们

3 N-COUNT 土人，土著(一些欧洲人指非西方国家的原住民，可能具冒犯意味) Some European people use native to refer to a person living in a non-Western country who belongs to the race or tribe that the majority of people there belong to. This use could cause offence. 土人，土著(一些欧洲人指非西方国家的原住民，可能具冒犯意味)

  • They used force to banish the natives from the more fertile land.

    他们使用武力把土著居民驱逐出了比较肥沃的土地。

Native is also an adjective.

  • Native people were allowed to retain some sense of their traditional culture and religion.

    当地原住民获准可以保留一些自己的传统文化和宗教。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] (语言)本国的，出生地的 Your native language or tongue is the first language that you learned to speak when you were a child. (语言)本国的，出生地的

  • She spoke not only her native language, Swedish, but also English and French.

    她不仅讲自己的母语瑞典语，还讲英语和法语。

  • French is not my native tongue.

    法语不是我的母语。

SYN mother, indigenous, vernacular  
5 ADJ [ADJ n] (动植物)当地土生的，原产地的 Plants or animals that are native to a particular region live or grow there naturally and were not brought there. (动植物)当地土生的，原产地的

  • ...a project to create a 50 acre forest of native Caledonian pines.

    营造50英亩喀里多尼亚土生松树林的项目

  • Many of the plants are native to Brazil. [+ to]

    这些植物中有很多原产地在巴西。

Native is also a noun.

  • The coconut palm is a native of Malaysia.

    椰子树原产于马来西亚。

6 ADJ [ADJ n] (能力或品质)生来就有的，天赋的 A native ability or quality is one that you possess naturally without having to learn it. (能力或品质)生来就有的，天赋的

  • We have our native inborn talent, yet we hardly use it.

    我们有天生的才能，但我们却几乎未曾使用过。

7 PHRASE [VERB inflects] (移居他国的人)入乡随俗，同化 If you say that someone who is living away from their own country goes native, you mean that they try to live and dress like the local people. (移居他国的人)入乡随俗，同化 [disapproval]

  • You don't think he's gone native, do you? Perhaps he has married out there and decided he can't come home.

    你觉得他并没有入乡随俗，是吗？可能他和那里的人结了婚，认定回不了家了。
