equal opportunities employer ˈiːkwəl ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪtɪz ɪmˈplɔɪə
Word forms: plural equal opportunities employers
N-COUNT 提供均等机会的雇主 An equal opportunities employer is an employer who gives people the same opportunities for employment, pay, and promotion, without discrimination against anyone. 提供均等机会的雇主 [business]

  • The police force is committed to being an equal opportunities employer.

    警方承诺向所有警员提供均等机会。
