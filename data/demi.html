demi- demi- ˈdɛmɪ ˈdɛmə ˈdɛmi
PREFIX (用于单词之前)表示“半” Demi- is used at the beginning of some words to refer to something equivalent to half of the object or amount indicated by the rest of the word. (用于单词之前)表示“半”
