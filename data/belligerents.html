belligerent
bɪlɪdʒərənt bəˈlɪdʒərənt
Word forms: plural belligerents
1 ADJ-GRADED （人）好战的，好斗的，寻衅的 A belligerent person is hostile and aggressive. （人）好战的，好斗的，寻衅的

  • ...the belligerent statements from both sides which have led to fears of war.

    双方充满火药味的声明引发了人们对战争的担心

  • He was almost back to his belligerent mood of twelve months ago.

    他故态复萌，几乎又像一年前那样咄咄逼人了。

SYN aggressive, hostile, contentious, combative  
belligerently

  • 'Why not?' he asked belligerently.

    “为什么不行？”他挑衅地问道。

belligerence

  • He could be accused of passion, but never belligerence.

    可以说他很冲动，但不能说他爱挑事。

2 N-COUNT 交战国；交战方 The belligerents in a war are the countries or groups that are fighting each other. 交战国；交战方 [formal]

  • The belligerents were due, once again, to try to settle their differences.

    预计交战双方要再一次试图解决彼此的分歧了。

SYN fighter, battler, militant, contender
