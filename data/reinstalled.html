reinstall
riɪnstɔl ˌriːɪnˈstɔːl
Word forms: reinstalls, reinstalling, reinstalled
V-T 重新安装If you reinstall something such as software on your computer, you set it up again, usually because you have been having problems with it. 重新安装

  • It will be best to wipe the system and reinstall everything.

    最好是格式化系统，然后全部重装。
