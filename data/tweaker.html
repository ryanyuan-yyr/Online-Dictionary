tweak
twiːk twik
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense tweaks, present participle tweaking, past tense , past participle tweaked
1 VERB 扭;扯;拧 If you tweak something, especially part of someone's body, you hold it between your finger and thumb and twist it or pull it. 扭;扯;拧

  • He tweaked Guy's ear roughly. [VERB noun]

    他粗暴地扯盖伊的耳朵。

  • 'A handsome offer', she replied, tweaking his cheek. [VERB noun]

    “一个慷慨的提议”，她拧着他的面颊回答道。

SYN twist, pull, pinch, jerk  
2 VERB 稍稍改进;对…作微调 If you tweak something such as a system or a design, you improve it by making a slight change. 稍稍改进;对…作微调 [informal]

  • He expects the system to get even better as the engineers tweak its performance. [VERB noun]

    他预计在工程师对其性能稍加改进后，该系统会运行得更加良好。

SYN adjust, improve, alter, adapt  
Tweak is also a noun.

  • The camera has undergone only two minor tweaks since its introduction.

    那款相机自从推出以来仅经过两次小的改进。
