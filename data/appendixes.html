appendix
əpendɪks əpendɪsiːz əˈpɛndɪks
Word forms: plural appendixes
In British English, the plural form appendices /ə'pendɪsiːz/ is usually used for meaning 2. 英国英语中，义项2的复数形式通常为appendices /ə'pendɪsiːz/。
1 N-COUNT 阑尾；盲肠 Your appendix is a small closed tube inside your body which is attached to your digestive system. 阑尾；盲肠

  • ...a burst appendix.

    阑尾穿孔

2 N-COUNT （书末的）附录 An appendix to a book is extra information that is placed after the end of the main text. （书末的）附录
