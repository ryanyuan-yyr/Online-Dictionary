appetizer
æpɪtaɪzəʳ ˈæpəˌtaɪzər
Word forms: plural appetizers
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 appetiser
N-COUNT （餐前的）开胃小吃 An appetizer is the first course of a meal. It consists of a small amount of food. （餐前的）开胃小吃

  • Seafood soup is a good appetizer.

    海鲜汤是一道很好的开胃菜。

SYN hors d'oeuvre, titbit, antipasto, canapé
