tasteful
teɪstfʊl ˈteɪstfəl

  • The decor is tasteful and restrained.

    装饰风格素雅简约。

  • ...tasteful jewelry.

    雅致的珠宝

SYN refined, stylish, elegant, cultured  
tastefully

    装饰别致的大房子

  • The rectory has been sensitively restored and tastefully furnished.

    教区长的故居已被精心修复，里面的陈设也颇为雅致。
