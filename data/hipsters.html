hipster
hɪpstəʳ ˈhɪpstər
Word forms: plural hipsters
1 N-COUNT 赶时髦的人;潮人 If you refer to someone as a hipster, you mean that they are very fashionable, often in a way that you think is rather silly. 赶时髦的人;潮人 [humorous]
2 N-PLURAL [oft NOUN n] 低腰长裤 Hipsters are trousers which are designed so that the highest part of them is around your hips, rather than around your waist. 低腰长裤 [mainly British]
