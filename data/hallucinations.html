hallucination
həluːsɪneɪʃən həˌlusɪˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural hallucinations
1 N-VAR （因生病或吸毒所致的）幻觉（体验） A hallucination is the experience of seeing something that is not really there because you are ill or have taken a drug. （因生病或吸毒所致的）幻觉（体验）

  • The drug induces hallucinations at high doses.

    大剂量地服用该药会致幻。

  • Hallucination is common in patients who have suffered damage to the brain.

    脑部受损的病人常会产生幻觉。

2 N-COUNT （因生病或吸毒所见到的）幻象 A hallucination is something that is not real that someone sees because they are ill or have taken a drug. （因生病或吸毒所见到的）幻象

  • Perhaps the footprint was a hallucination.

    脚印可能只是幻象。

SYN illusion, dream, vision, fantasy
