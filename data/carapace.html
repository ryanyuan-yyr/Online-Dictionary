carapace
kærəpeɪs ˈkærəˌpæks ˈkærəˌpeɪs
Word forms: plural carapaces
1 N-COUNT (龟、蟹等的)甲壳,背甲 A carapace is the protective shell on the back of some animals such as tortoises or crabs. (龟、蟹等的)甲壳,背甲 [formal]
2 N-COUNT (用以自我保护的)外壳，外衣,外表 You can refer to an attitude that someone has in order to protect themselves as their carapace . (用以自我保护的)外壳，外衣,外表 [literary]

  • The arrogance became his protective carapace.

    这种傲慢态度成了他的保护壳。
