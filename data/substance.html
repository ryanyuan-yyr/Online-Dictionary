substance
sʌbstəns ˈsʌbstəns
Word forms: plural substances
1 N-COUNT 物质;物品;东西 A substance is a solid, powder, liquid, or gas with particular properties. 物质;物品;东西

  • There's absolutely no regulation of cigarettes to make sure that they don't include poisonous substances.

    根本没有法规来确保香烟不含有毒物质。

  • The substance that's causing the problem comes from the barley.

    引起该问题的物质来自大麦。

SYN material, body, stuff, element  
2 N-UNCOUNT 重要性;重大意义 Substance is the quality of being important or significant. 重要性;重大意义 [formal]

  • It's questionable whether anything of substance has been achieved.

    是否已经取得了任何实质性进展还是个问题。

  • Syria will attend only if the negotiations deal with issues of substance.

    只有在谈判涉及重大问题时叙利亚才会参加。

SYN importance, significance, moment, meaningfulness  
3 N-SING 主旨;要点;实质;基本内容 The substance of what someone says or writes is the main thing that they are trying to say. 主旨;要点;实质;基本内容

  • The substance of his discussions doesn't really matter. [+ of]

    他讨论的要点实际上根本不重要。

SYN meaning, main point, gist, matter  
4 N-UNCOUNT 事实基础;根据 If you say that something has no substance, you mean that it is not true. 事实基础;根据 [formal]

  • There is no substance in any of these allegations.

    这些指控都毫无根据。

SYN truth, fact, reality, certainty  
5 PHRASE 财力雄厚的;有权有势的；有影响力的 A person of substance has a lot of money, power, or influence. 财力雄厚的;有权有势的；有影响力的 [formal]

  • ...mature men of substance.

    有钱有势的成熟男人
