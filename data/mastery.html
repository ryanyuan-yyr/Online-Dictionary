mastery
mɑːstəri mæs- ˈmæstəri ˈmɑstəri
1 N-UNCOUNT 精通;掌握 If you show mastery of a particular skill or language, you show that you have learned or understood it completely and have no difficulty using it. 精通;掌握

  • He doesn't have mastery of the basic rules of grammar. [+ of]

    他还没有完全掌握基本的语法规则。

  • He demonstrated his mastery of political manoeuvring.

    他表现出了高超的政治手段。

SYN understanding, knowledge, comprehension, ability  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft N of/over n] 统治;控制 Mastery is power or control over something. 统治;控制

  • Mesopotamia was probably the first region of the world where humans gained mastery over major rivers.

    美索不达米亚地区可能是世界上人类最早征服主干河流的地区。

SYN control, authority, command, rule
