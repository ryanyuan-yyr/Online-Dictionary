relent
rɪlent rɪˈlɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense relents, present participle relenting, past tense , past participle relented
1 VERB 变宽厚;变温和;心软 If you relent, you allow someone to do something that you had previously refused to allow them to do. 变宽厚;变温和;心软

  • Finally his mother relented and gave permission for her youngest son to marry. [VERB]

    最后他母亲作出让步，允许她的小儿子结婚。

2 VERB (天气)转晴，缓和，转好 If bad weather relents, it improves. (天气)转晴，缓和，转好

  • If the weather relents, the game will be finished today. [VERB]

    如果天气转好的话，比赛将在今天结束。

SYN ease, die down, let up, fall
