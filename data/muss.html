muss
mʌs mʌs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense musses, present participle mussing, past tense , past participle mussed
VERB 使凌乱，弄乱(尤指头发) To muss something, especially someone's hair, or to muss it up, means to make it untidy. 使凌乱，弄乱(尤指头发) [mainly US]

  • He reached out and mussed my hair. [VERB noun]

    他伸手弄乱了我的头发。

  • His clothes were all mussed up. [be V-ed up]

    他的衣服全被弄皱了。

SYN mess (up), disarrange, dishevel, ruffle
