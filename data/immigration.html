immigration
ɪmɪgreɪʃən ˌɪməˈgreɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 移居 Immigration is the coming of people into a country in order to live and work there. 移居

  • The government has decided to tighten its immigration policy.

    政府决定实施更加严格的移民政策。

  • ...immigration into Europe.

    移居欧洲

2 N-UNCOUNT 移民局检查处 Immigration or immigration control is the place at a port, airport, or international border where officials check the passports of people who wish to come into the country. 移民局检查处
