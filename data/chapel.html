chapel
tʃæpəl ˈtʃæpəl
Word forms: plural chapels
1 N-COUNT (教堂里的)私人祈祷室 A chapel is a part of a church which has its own altar and which is used for private prayer. (教堂里的)私人祈祷室

  • ...the chapel of the Virgin Mary.

    圣母堂的私人祈祷室

2 N-COUNT (附属于医院、学校、监狱的)小教堂 A chapel is a small church attached to a hospital, school, or prison. (附属于医院、学校、监狱的)小教堂

  • We married in the chapel of Charing Cross Hospital in London. [+ of]

    我们是在伦敦查令十字医院的小教堂里结的婚。

3 N-VAR (基督教某些派别的)教堂，宗教仪式 A chapel is a building used for worship by members of some Christian churches. Chapel refers to the religious services that take place there. (基督教某些派别的)教堂，宗教仪式

  • ...a Methodist chapel.

    卫理公会教堂

  • On Sundays, the family went three times to chapel.

    每到星期天，这家人都会去做3次礼拜。
