diktat
dɪktæt ˈdɪktɑːt dɪktɑːt dɪkˈtɑt ˈdɪkˌtɑt
Word forms: plural diktats
N-VAR （尤指不合理但又必须服从的）勒令，强制命令，专制统治 You use diktat to refer to something such as a law or government which people have to obey even if they do not agree with it, especially one which seems unfair. （尤指不合理但又必须服从的）勒令，强制命令，专制统治 [disapproval]
