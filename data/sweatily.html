sweaty
sweti ˈswɛti
Word forms: comparative sweatier, superlative sweatiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 满是汗的;汗淋淋的 If parts of your body or your clothes are sweaty, they are soaked or covered with sweat. 满是汗的;汗淋淋的

  • ...sweaty hands.

    汗津津的手

  • She was hot and sweaty.

    她热得大汗淋漓。

SYN perspiring, sweating, sticky, clammy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 热得(或累得)让人出汗的 A sweaty place or activity makes you sweat because it is hot or tiring. 热得(或累得)让人出汗的

  • ...a sweaty nightclub.

    热得让人冒汗的夜总会
