rootless
ruːtləs ˈrutlɪs ˈrʊtlɪs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 居无定所的;无固定工作的;无归属的;漂泊的 If someone has no permanent home or job and is not settled in any community, you can describe them as rootless . 居无定所的;无固定工作的;无归属的;漂泊的

  • These rootless young people have nowhere else to go.

    这些漂泊不定的年轻人没有其他地方可去。

  • ...people who refused to integrate within society and instead lived rootless, jobless lives.

    那些不愿意融入社会而过着漂泊不定、没有工作的生活的人

SYN footloose, homeless, roving, transient  
rootlessness

  • ...a social mobility that threatens to become rootlessness.

    可能会造成归属感缺失的社会流动性
