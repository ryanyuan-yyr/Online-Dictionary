summer
sʌməʳ ˈsʌmər
Word forms: plural summers
1 N-VAR 夏天;夏季 Summer is the season between spring and autumn. In the summer the weather is usually warm or hot. 夏天;夏季

  • I escaped the heatwave in London earlier this summer and flew to Cork.

    今年初夏我逃离了伦敦的炎热，飞往科克去避暑。

  • It was a perfect summer's day.

    那是一个美好的夏日。

  • ...in the summer of 1987.

    在 1987 年的夏天

  • ...the summer holidays.

    暑假

  • He used to spend childhood summers with his grandparents.

    他小时候夏天都是和爷爷奶奶一起度过的。
