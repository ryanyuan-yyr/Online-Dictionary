aunt
ɑːnt ænt ænt ɑnt
Word forms: plural aunts
1 N-FAMILY; N-TITLE 姨妈；姑妈；伯母；舅妈 Someone's aunt is the sister of their mother or father, or the wife of their uncle. 姨妈；姑妈；伯母；舅妈

  • She wrote to her aunt in America.

    她给远在美国的姨妈写了封信。

  • It was a present from Aunt Vera.

    这是薇拉姑妈送的礼物。

2See also:agony aunt 
+−Usage
Usageaunt and ant
Be sure not to confuse aunt and ant, which many English speakers pronounce the same way. Your aunt is a sister of your parent; an ant is an insect: Linh's aunt has an unusual fear-she's terrified of stepping on ants.
