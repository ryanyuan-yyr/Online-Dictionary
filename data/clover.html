clover
kloʊvəʳ ˈkləʊvəˌɡrɑːs ˈkloʊvər
Word forms: plural clovers
1 N-VAR 三叶草;苜蓿 Clover is a small plant with pink or white ball-shaped flowers. 三叶草;苜蓿

  • ...a four leaf clover.

    四叶草

2 PHRASE [verb-link PHRASE, PHRASE after verb] 奢侈舒适的生活 If you say that someone is in clover, you mean that they are living a luxurious and comfortable life. 奢侈舒适的生活

  • ...a contract that Ford hoped would keep him in clover for the rest of his life.

    一份福特希望能保证他舒舒服服度完余生的合同
