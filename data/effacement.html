efface
ɪfeɪs əˈfeɪs ɪˈfeɪs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense effaces, present participle effacing, past tense , past participle effaced
1 VERB 消除;抹去;擦掉 To efface something means to destroy or remove it so that it cannot be seen any more. 消除;抹去;擦掉 [formal]

  • ...an event that has helped efface the country's traditional image. [VERB noun]

    帮助该国改变了以往形象改观的事件

  • The name of the ship had been effaced from the menus. [VERB noun]

    那艘船的名字已经从选择菜单中删除了。

SYN obliterate, remove, destroy, cancel  
2See also:self-effacing
