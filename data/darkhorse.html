dark horse
Word forms: plural dark horses
N-COUNT 黑马（最近刚取得或将要取得成功的新人) If you describe someone as a dark horse, you mean that people know very little about them, although they may have recently had success or may be about to have success. 黑马（最近刚取得或将要取得成功的新人)

  • Until recently A. S. Byatt was a dark horse, known only by those steeped in literature.

    一直到前不久，A.S.拜厄特都还是只为潜心研究文学的人所知晓的一匹黑马。
