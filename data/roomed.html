-roomed -ruːmd
COMB in ADJ [usually ADJ n] （与数词连用构成形容词）表示“有…个房间的” -roomed combines with numbers to form adjectives which tell you how many rooms a house or flat contains. （与数词连用构成形容词）表示“有…个房间的”

  • They found a little two-roomed flat to rent.
