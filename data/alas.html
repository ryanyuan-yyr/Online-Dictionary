alas
əlæs əˈlæs əˈlɑs
ADV （表示悲伤、怜悯等）哎呀，唉 You use alas to say that you think that the facts you are talking about are sad or unfortunate. （表示悲伤、怜悯等）哎呀，唉 [formal, feelings]

  • Such scandals have not, alas, been absent.

    这样的丑事，唉，也不是没有。

  • Alas, it's not that simple.

    哎呀，事情不是那么简单的。

SYN sadly, unfortunately, inopportunely
