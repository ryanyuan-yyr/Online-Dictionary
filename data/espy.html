espy
ɪspaɪ ɪˈspaɪ ɛˈspaɪ ɪˈspaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense espies, present participle espying, past tense , past participle espied
VERB 看到;注意到 If you espy something, you see or notice it. 看到;注意到 [old-fashioned]

  • Here, from a window, did Guinevere espy a knight standing in a woodman's cart. [VERB noun]

    吉尼维尔是从这里透过窗户看到了站在樵夫车上的骑士。

SYN catch sight of, see, discover, spot
