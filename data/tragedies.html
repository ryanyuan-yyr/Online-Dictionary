tragedy
trædʒɪdi ˈtrædʒədi
Word forms: plural tragedies
1 N-VAR 不幸;灾难;惨剧 A tragedy is an extremely sad event or situation. 不幸;灾难;惨剧

  • They have suffered an enormous personal tragedy.

    他们遭逢了巨大的个人不幸。

  • Maskell's life had not been without tragedy.

    马斯克尔的生活亦有过变故。

SYN disaster, catastrophe, misfortune, adversity  
2 N-VAR 悲剧（作品） Tragedy is a type of literature, especially drama, that is serious and sad, and often ends with the death of the main character. 悲剧（作品）

  • The story has elements of tragedy and farce.

    这个故事兼有悲剧与闹剧的元素。

  • …a classic Greek tragedy.

    古典希腊悲剧

SYN tragic drama, play
