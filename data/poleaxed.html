pole-axed ˈpəʊlækst
(also poleaxed)
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] （因震惊而）目瞪口呆的，不知所措的 If someone is pole-axed, they are so surprised or shocked that they do not know what to say or do. （因震惊而）目瞪口呆的，不知所措的 [mainly British, informal]

  • Sitting pole-axed on the sofa, Mahoney stared in astonishment at the spectacle before him.

    马奥尼不知所措地坐在沙发上，吃惊地盯着眼前的景象。
