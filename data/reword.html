reword
riːwɜːʳd riːˈwɜːd riˈwɜrd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rewords, present participle rewording, past tense , past participle reworded
VERB 重述;改写;改变…的措辞 When you reword something that is spoken or written, you try to express it in a way that is more accurate, more acceptable, or more easily understood. 重述;改写;改变…的措辞

  • All right, I'll reword my question. [VERB noun]

    好的，我会换个说法来问。

  • The rules were reworded in 1986. [VERB noun]

    在1986年这些规则进行了修改。

SYN put in other words, paraphrase, recast, rephrase
