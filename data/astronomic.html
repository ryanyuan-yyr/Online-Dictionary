astronomical
æstrənɒmɪkəl ˌæstrəˈnɑmɪkəl
1 ADJ （数量，尤指费用）极其巨大的,天文数字的 If you describe an amount, especially the cost of something as astronomical, you are emphasizing that it is very large indeed. （数量，尤指费用）极其巨大的,天文数字的 [emphasis]

  • Houses in the village are selling for astronomical prices.

    乡村的房价正在飙升。

  • The cost will be astronomical.

    费用将是一笔天文数字。

SYN huge, great, giant, massive  
astronomically

  • He was astronomically wealthy.

    他富甲一方。

  • House prices had risen astronomically.

    房价已经暴涨。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 天文学的；天体的 Astronomical means relating to astronomy. 天文学的；天体的

  • ...the British Astronomical Association.

    英国天文协会
