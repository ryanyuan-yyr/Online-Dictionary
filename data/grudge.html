grudge
grʌdʒ grʌdʒ
Word forms: plural grudges
N-COUNT 不满；积怨；怨恨 If you have or bear a grudge against someone, you have unfriendly feelings towards them because of something they did in the past. 不满；积怨；怨恨

  • He appears to have a grudge against certain players. [+ against]

    他看来对某些选手怀恨在心。

  • There is no doubt it was an accident and I bear no grudges.

    这无疑是个意外，我没有怨言。

SYN resentment, bitterness, grievance, malice
