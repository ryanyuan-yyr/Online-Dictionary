drag and drop dræɡ ənd drɒp
(also drag-and-drop)Word forms: drags and drops, dragging and dropping, dragged and dropped
1 V-T 拖放If you drag and drop computer files or images, you move them from one place to another by clicking on them with the mouse and moving them across the screen. 拖放

  • When installing Office, users simply drag and drop the Office folder to their hard drive.

    在安装Office软件时，用户只需拖放Office软件至他们的硬盘即可。

2 N-UNCOUNT 拖放功能Drag and drop is a method of moving computer files or images from one place to another by clicking on them with the mouse and moving them across the screen. 拖放功能

  • Copying software onto an iPod is as easy as drag and drop.

    复制软件至iPod就和拖放功能一样简单。

3 ADJ 拖放的Drag and drop is also an adjective. 拖放的

  • ...a drag-and-drop text and graphics.

    ...可拖放的文本和图形。
