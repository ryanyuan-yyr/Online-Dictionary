reflexive pronoun
rɪˈflɛksɪv ˈprəʊˌnaʊn
Word forms: plural reflexive pronouns
N-COUNT 反身代词 A reflexive pronoun is a pronoun such as 'myself' which refers back to the subject of a sentence or clause. For example, in the sentence 'He made himself a cup of tea', the reflexive pronoun 'himself' refers back to 'he'. 反身代词
