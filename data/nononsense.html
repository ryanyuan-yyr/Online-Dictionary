no-nonsense
ˈnoʊˈnɑnsɛns
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 讲究实际的;直截了当的;不容胡闹的 If you describe someone as a no-nonsense person, you approve of the fact that they are efficient, direct, and quite tough. 讲究实际的;直截了当的;不容胡闹的 [approval]

  • She saw herself as a direct, no-nonsense modern woman.

    她认为自己是个直截了当、不拖泥带水的现代女性。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 简单实用的 If you describe something as a no-nonsense thing, you approve of the fact that it is plain and does not have unnecessary parts. 简单实用的 [approval]

  • You'll need no-nonsense boots for the jungle.

    你需要一双实用的靴子穿越丛林。
