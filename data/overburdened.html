overburdened
oʊvəʳbɜːʳdənd ˌəʊvəˈbɜːdənd
1 ADJ-GRADED （系统、机构等）负担过重的，不堪重负的 If a system or organization is overburdened, it has too many people or things to deal with and so does not function properly. （系统、机构等）负担过重的，不堪重负的 [Also + with]

  • The city's hospitals are overburdened by casualties. [+ by]

    大量伤员使该市的医院不堪重负。

  • ...an overburdened air traffic control system.

    超负荷运转的空中交通管制系统

2 ADJ-GRADED （人因工作、问题等）负担过重的，不堪应付的 If you are overburdened with something such as work or problems, you have more of it than you can cope with. （人因工作、问题等）负担过重的，不堪应付的 [Also + by]

  • The Chief Inspector disliked being overburdened with insignificant detail. [+ with]

    总督察讨厌在细枝末节上没完没了地纠缠。

  • ...overburdened teachers.

    不堪应付教学任务的教师
