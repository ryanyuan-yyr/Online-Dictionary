run out
1 PHRASAL VERB 用完;耗尽 If you run out of something, you have no more of it left. to run out of steam→see: steam  用完;耗尽

  • They have run out of ideas. [VERB PREP. + of]

    他们已经想不出任何办法了。

  • We're running out of time. [V P of n]

    我们快没有时间了。

  • By now the plane was running out of fuel. [V P of n]

    现在，这架飞机的燃料快用完了。

  • We had lots before but now we've run out. [VERB PREP.]

    我们以前有很多，但是现在已经用完了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 被用完;耗尽 If something runs out, it becomes used up so that there is no more left. 被用完;耗尽

  • Conditions are getting worse and supplies are running out. [VERB PREP.]

    情况越来越糟糕，贮备物资也快用完了。

  • Time is running out. [VERB PREP.]

    时间不多了。

3 PHRASAL VERB (法律文件)到期，失效 When a legal document runs out, it stops being valid. (法律文件)到期，失效

  • When the lease ran out the family moved to Campigny. [VERB PREP.]

    租约到期后，这家人搬到了坎皮格尼。

  • ...the day my visa ran out. [VERB PREP.]

    我的签证的到期日

SYN expire, end, lapse, terminate
