under age
ˈʌndəreɪdʒ
(also underage)
1 ADJ 未成年的；未到法定年龄的 A person who is under age is legally too young to do something, for example to drink alcohol, have sex, or vote. 未成年的；未到法定年龄的

  • Underage youths can obtain alcohol from their older friends.

    未到法定年龄的青少年可以从年龄较大的朋友那里弄到酒。

  • ...girls who have babies when they are under age.

    还未成年就已生下孩子的女孩

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 未成年人所做的 Under age activities such as drinking or smoking are carried out by people who are legally too young to do them. 未成年人所做的

  • ...his efforts to stop under age drinking and drug abuse.

    他为制止未成年人酗酒吸毒而作的努力
