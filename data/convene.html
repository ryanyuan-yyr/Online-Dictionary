convene
kənviːn kənˈvin
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense convenes, present participle convening, past tense , past participle convened
1 V-ERG 召集;集合;开会 If someone convenes a meeting or conference, they arrange for it to take place. You can also say that people convene or that a meeting convenes . 召集;集合;开会 [formal]

  • Last August he convened a meeting of his closest advisers at Camp David. [VERB noun]

    去年8月，他召集自己最亲近的顾问在戴维营开会。

  • Senior officials convened in October 1991 in London. [VERB]

    1991年10月，高级官员在伦敦会齐。

  • ...the convening of an international peace conference. [VERB-ing]

    一次国际和平会议的召开

SYN meet, gather, rally, assemble
