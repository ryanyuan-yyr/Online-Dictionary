pitted
pɪtɪd ˈpɪtɪd ˈpɪtɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （水果）去核的 Pitted fruits have had their stones removed. （水果）去核的

  • ...green and black pitted olives.

    绿色和黑色的去核橄榄

2 ADJ 坑坑洼洼的；有凹陷的 If the surface of something is pitted, it is covered with a lot of small, shallow holes. 坑坑洼洼的；有凹陷的

  • Everywhere building facades are pitted with shell and bullet holes. [+ with]

    任一处的建筑物正面都布满了弹洞。

  • ...the pitted surface of the moon.

    坑洼不平的月球表面

SYN scarred, marked, rough, scratched
