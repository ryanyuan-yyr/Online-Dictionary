distressed
dɪstrest dɪˈstrɛst dɪˈstrɛst
1 ADJ-GRADED 烦恼的;忧虑的;苦恼的 If someone is distressed, they are upset or worried. 烦恼的;忧虑的;苦恼的

  • I feel very alone and distressed about my problem.

    对自己的问题我觉得很无助、很苦恼。

SYN upset, worried, troubled, anxious  
2 ADJ 仿旧的;做旧的 A distressed object such as a piece of furniture or an item of clothing has been deliberately made to look old. 仿旧的;做旧的

  • ...distressed jeans.

    仿旧牛仔裤

  • ...distressed wooden benches.

    仿古木制长凳

SYN aged
