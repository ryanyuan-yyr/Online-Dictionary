cliffhanger
klɪfhæŋəʳ ˈklɪfˌhæŋər

N-COUNT (局势、戏剧或电影的)悬念，最惊险刺激的部分 A cliffhanger is a situation or part of a play or film that is very exciting or frightening because you are left for a long time not knowing what will happen next. (局势、戏剧或电影的)悬念，最惊险刺激的部分
