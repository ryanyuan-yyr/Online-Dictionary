thankful
θæŋkfʊl ˈθæŋkfəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ that] 感激的;感谢的;欣慰的 When you are thankful, you are very happy and relieved that something has happened. 感激的;感谢的;欣慰的 [Also + for]

  • Most of the time I'm just thankful that I've got a job.

    多数时候我只是庆幸自己有份工作。

  • I was so thankful for his support.

    我对他的支持非常感激。

  • She's thankful to be alive.

    她庆幸自己能活着。

SYN grateful, pleased, relieved, obliged  
thankfully

  • Simon thankfully slipped off his uniform and relaxed.

    西蒙满怀庆幸地迅速脱下制服，放松了下来。
