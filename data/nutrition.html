nutrition
njuːtrɪʃən nuː- nuˈtrɪʃən njuˈtrɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 营养(作用);滋养(过程) Nutrition is the process of taking food into the body and absorbing the nutrients in those foods. 营养(作用);滋养(过程)

  • There are alternative sources of nutrition to animal meat.

    存在替代肉类的其他营养来源。

  • As in all experimental sciences, we still do not know everything about nutrition.

    就像所有实验科学一样，我们仍然没有完全认识营养作用。

SYN food, nourishment, sustenance, nutriment
