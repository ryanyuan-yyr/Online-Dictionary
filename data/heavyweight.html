heavyweight
heviweɪt ˈhɛvɪˌweɪt ˈhɛviˌweɪt
Word forms: plural heavyweights
1 N-COUNT （体重175磅以上的）最重量级拳击手 A heavyweight is a boxer weighing more than 175 pounds and therefore in the heaviest class. （体重175磅以上的）最重量级拳击手
2 N-COUNT 重量级人物（或组织）;有影响力的人物（或组织） If you refer to a person or organization as a heavyweight, you mean that they have a lot of influence, experience, and importance in a particular field, subject, or activity. 重量级人物（或组织）;有影响力的人物（或组织）

  • He was a political heavyweight.

    他是一名政界要人。

  • ...jazz heavyweights.

    爵士乐坛的重量级人物
