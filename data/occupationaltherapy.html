occupational therapy
N-UNCOUNT （帮助患者或伤者培养或恢复技能的）职业疗法 Occupational therapy is a method of helping people who have been ill or injured to develop skills or get skills back by giving them certain activities to do. （帮助患者或伤者培养或恢复技能的）职业疗法

  • She will now begin occupational therapy to regain the use of her hands.

    她现在将开始接受职业疗法以恢复双手的活动能力。
