jig
dʒɪg dʒɪg
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense jigs, present participle jigging, past tense , past participle jigged
1 N-COUNT 吉格舞（一种轻快的舞蹈） A jig is a lively dance. 吉格舞（一种轻快的舞蹈）

  • She danced an Irish jig.

    她跳起了爱尔兰吉格舞。

2 VERB 精力充沛地跳舞（或走动）；（尤指）蹦跳 To jig means to dance or move energetically, especially bouncing up and down. 精力充沛地跳舞（或走动）；（尤指）蹦跳 [Also VERB]

  • You didn't just jig about by yourself, I mean you danced properly. [VERB adv./prep.]

    你可不是一个人在那儿瞎蹦，我的意思是你跳得很不错。

SYN skip, bob, prance, jiggle  
3 N-COUNT 夹具；钻模 A jig is a device that holds something in position when it is being machined by a tool such as a drill. 夹具；钻模
