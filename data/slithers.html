slither
slɪðəʳ ˈslɪðər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense slithers, present participle slithering, past tense , past participle slithered
1 VERB (摇摇晃晃地)滑行，滑动 If you slither somewhere, you slide along in an uneven way. (摇摇晃晃地)滑行，滑动

  • Robert lost his footing and slithered down the bank. [VERB prep./adv.]

    罗伯特失足滑下了河岸。

2 VERB (蛇等)蜿蜒而行 If an animal such as a snake slithers, it moves along in a curving way. (蛇等)蜿蜒而行 [Also VERB]

  • The snake slithered into the water. [VERB prep./adv.]

    蛇滑入了水中。
