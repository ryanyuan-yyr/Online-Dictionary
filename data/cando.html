can-do
ˈkænˈdu
ADJ [ADJ n] 能干的;积极进取的;勇往直前的 If you say that someone has a can-do attitude, you approve of them because they are confident and willing to deal with problems or new tasks, rather than complaining or giving up. 能干的;积极进取的;勇往直前的 [informal, approval]

  • He is known for his optimistic can-do attitude.

    他因乐观敢闯而闻名。

  • America is once again being seen as a strong, can-do nation.

    美国再次被认为是个实力强大、乐于尝试的国家。
