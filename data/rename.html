rename
riːneɪm riːˈneɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense renames, present participle renaming, past tense , past participle renamed
1 VERB 重命名;改名;更名 If you rename something, you change its name to a new name. 重命名;改名;更名

  • Tel Aviv's Kings Square was renamed Yitzhak Rabin Square. [be VERB-ed noun]

    特拉维夫的国王广场更名为伊扎克·拉宾广场。

  • The party leader said the street should be renamed Freedom Avenue. [VERB noun noun]

    政党领导人说这条街道应该重新命名为自由大道。

  • The former Communist party, now renamed Socialists, have won nearly half the vote. [VERB-ed]

    以前的共产党，现在更名为社会党，赢得了近半数的选票。
