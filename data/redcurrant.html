redcurrant
redkʌrənt -kɜːr-
Word forms: plural redcurrants
N-COUNT 红浆果;红醋栗;红浆果树 Redcurrants are very small, bright red berries that grow in bunches on a bush and can be eaten as a fruit or cooked to make a sauce for meat. The bush on which they grow can also be called a redcurrant . 红浆果;红醋栗;红浆果树 [British]

  • ...roast lamb and redcurrant jelly.

    烤羊羔肉和红浆果果冻
