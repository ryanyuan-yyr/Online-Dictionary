voter
voʊtəʳ ˈvoʊtər
Word forms: plural voters
N-COUNT [usually plural] 选民;投票人;选举人 Voters are people who have the legal right to vote in elections, or people who are voting in a particular election. 选民;投票人;选举人

  • The turnout was at least 62 percent of registered voters.

    至少有62％的登记选民参加了投票。

  • Austrian voters went to the polls this weekend to elect a successor to the President.

    奥地利选民这周末去投票中心选举下届总统。
