write back
PHRASAL VERB 写回信 If you write back to someone who has sent you a letter, you write them a letter in reply. 写回信

  • Macmillan wrote back saying that he could certainly help. [VERB PREP.]

    麦克米伦回信说他肯定能帮忙。

  • I wrote back to Meudon at once to fix up a meeting. [VERB PREP. + to]

    我马上给缪顿回信安排会面。
