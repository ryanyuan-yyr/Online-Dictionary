count on
(also count upon)
1 PHRASAL VERB 期望;预计 If you count on something or count upon it, you expect it to happen and include it in your plans. 期望;预计

  • The government thought it could count on the support of the trades unions. [VERB PREP. noun/verb-ing]

    政府认为可以指望获得工会的支持。

  • I'll be back. You can count on it. [V P n/-ing]

    我会回来的。你要相信。

  • He is counting on winning seats and perhaps a share in the new government. [V P n/-ing]

    他期望能获得一些席位，或许还能参与新政府的执政。

2 PHRASAL VERB 指望;依靠;依赖 If you count on someone or count upon them, you rely on them to support you or help you. 指望;依靠;依赖

  • Don't count on Lillian. [VERB PREP. noun]

    别指望莉莲。

  • I can always count on you to cheer me up. [V P n to-inf]

    你总能使我振作起来。

  • Diana seemed a strong young girl who could be counted upon to produce an heir. [V P n to-inf]

    戴安娜看起来是个年轻健壮的女孩，应该能生下继承人。
