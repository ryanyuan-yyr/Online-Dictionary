photograph
foʊtəgrɑːf -græf ˈfəʊtəˌɡrɑːf ˈfoʊtəˌgræf
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense photographs, present participle photographing, past tense , past participle photographed
1 N-COUNT 照片；相片 A photograph is a picture that is made using a camera. 照片；相片

  • He wants to take some photographs of the house. [+ of]

    他想给这幢房子拍一些照片。

  • Her photograph appeared on the front page of The New York Times.

    她的照片登在了《纽约时报》的头版上。

SYN picture, photo [informal] , shot, image  
2 VERB 给…拍照；为…拍照 When you photograph someone or something, you use a camera to obtain a picture of them. 给…拍照；为…拍照

  • She photographed the children. [VERB noun]

    她给孩子们拍了照。

  • I hate being photographed. [VERB noun]

    我讨厌照相。

  • They were photographed kissing on the platform. [be V-ed v-ing]

    有人拍到了他们在站台接吻的照片。

SYN take a picture of, record, film, shoot
