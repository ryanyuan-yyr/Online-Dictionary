calmly
kɑːmli
1 ADV [ADVERB with verb] 镇定自若地;镇静地 You can use calmly to emphasize that someone is behaving in a very controlled or ordinary way in a frightening or unusual situation. 镇定自若地;镇静地 [written, emphasis]

  • The gunmen calmly walked away and escaped in a waiting car.

    持枪歹徒不慌不忙地走开,坐上一辆等候的汽车逃走了。
