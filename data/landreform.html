land reform
Word forms: plural land reforms
N-VAR 土地改革 Land reform is a change in the system of land ownership, especially when it involves giving land to the people who actually farm it and taking it away from people who own large areas for profit. 土地改革

  • ...the new land reform policy under which thousands of peasant families are to be resettled.

    将有数以千计农民家庭被重新安置的新土改政策
