indefatigable
ɪndɪfætɪgəbəl ˌɪndɪˈfætɪgəbəl
ADJ 不倦的;不屈不挠的;坚持不懈的 You use indefatigable to describe someone who never gets tired of doing something. 不倦的;不屈不挠的;坚持不懈的 [formal]

  • His indefatigable spirit helped him to cope with his illness.

    他不屈不挠的精神帮助他对抗病魔。

  • He was indefatigable in his efforts to secure funding for new projects.

    他不知疲倦地努力为新项目寻求资金。

SYN tireless, dogged, persevering, patient  
indefatigably

  • She worked indefatigably and enthusiastically to interest the young in music.

    为了激发年轻人对音乐的兴趣，她一直孜孜不倦、积极热情地工作。
