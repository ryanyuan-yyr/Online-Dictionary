uproarious
ʌprɔːriəs ʌpˈrɔriəs
ADJ 喧嚣的；热闹的；充满喧闹哄笑声的 When events or people are uproarious, they make people laugh in a very noisy way. 喧嚣的；热闹的；充满喧闹哄笑声的 [literary]

  • He had spent several uproarious evenings at the Embassy Club.

    他在使馆俱乐部度过了几个狂欢夜。

  • The noise of talk and laughter was uproarious.

    说笑声很吵人。

SYN riotous, wild, rowdy, disorderly  
uproariously

  • Bob laughed uproariously.

    鲍勃开怀大笑。

  • ...an uproariously funny story.

    令人捧腹的有趣故事
