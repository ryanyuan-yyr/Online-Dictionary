eye up
PHRASAL VERB 色迷迷地看；目光在…身上打转 If someone eyes you up, they look at you in a way that shows they consider you attractive. 色迷迷地看；目光在…身上打转 [British, informal]

  • ...a slob who eyes up the women and makes lewd comments. [VERB PREP. noun]

    色迷迷地看着那些女人、满嘴荤话的懒鬼

  • The women sit in the corner and men eye them up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    那些女人坐在角落里，男人们的目光在她们身上打转。
