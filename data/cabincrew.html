cabin crew
ˈkæbɪn kruː
Word forms: plural cabin crews
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (飞机上的)乘务人员；机组乘务员 The cabin crew on an aircraft are the people whose job is to look after the passengers. (飞机上的)乘务人员；机组乘务员

  • He was assured by a senior member of the cabin crew that there definitely was not an emergency.

    一位高级乘务人员向他保证绝对没有发生紧急情况。
