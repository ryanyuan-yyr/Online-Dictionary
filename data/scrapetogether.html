scrape together
PHRASAL VERB (艰难地)凑齐，筹集 If you scrape together an amount of money or a number of things, you succeed in obtaining it with difficulty. (艰难地)凑齐，筹集

  • They only just managed to scrape the money together. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们只是勉强凑够了钱。

  • It's possible the Congress Party will scrape together a majority. [VERB PREP. noun]

    国大党可能会勉强获得多数票。

SYN collect, save, muster, get hold of
