nannying
næniɪŋ ˈnænɪɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 保姆工作;照看小孩 Nannying is the job of being a nanny. 保姆工作;照看小孩 [mainly British]

  • ...low-paid jobs such as nannying.

    诸如保姆之类的低收入工作

2 N-UNCOUNT (帮助、建议等)过多干涉，过度保护 If you refer to activities such as helping and advising people as nannying, you disapprove of these activities because you think that they are protecting people too much. (帮助、建议等)过多干涉，过度保护 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • ...governmental nannying and interference in markets.

    政府对市场的过度保护和干涉
