boil over
1 PHRASAL VERB 沸溢；潽出 When a liquid that is being heated boils over, it rises and flows over the edge of the container. 沸溢；潽出

  • Heat the liquid in a large, wide container rather than a high narrow one, or it can boil over. [VERB PREP.]

    把液体放在大而宽的容器里加热，不要用高而窄的，否则液体沸腾后会溢出来。

2 PHRASAL VERB （感情）爆发；失去理智；大怒 When someone's feelings boil over, they lose their temper or become violent. （感情）爆发；失去理智；大怒

  • Sometimes frustration and anger can boil over into direct and violent action. [VERB PREP.]

    有时挫折和愤怒会以直接的暴力形式爆发出来。
