requisition
rekwɪzɪʃən ˌrɛkwəˈzɪʃən
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense requisitions, present participle requisitioning, past tense , past participle requisitioned
1 VERB （官方）正式要求，征用 If people in authority requisition a vehicle, building, or food, they formally demand it and take it for official use. （官方）正式要求，征用 [formal]

  • Authorities requisitioned hotel rooms to lodge more than 3,000 stranded Christmas vacationers. [VERB noun]

    当局征用旅馆房间安顿了3,000多名无处落脚的圣诞度假者。

2 N-COUNT 征用令;授权书 A requisition is a written document which allows a person or organization to obtain goods. 征用令;授权书

  • ...a requisition for a replacement photocopier. [+ for]

    更换影印机的授权书

SYN demand, request, call, order
