rough out
PHRASAL VERB 草拟;画草图;勾勒 If you rough out a drawing or an idea, you draw or write the main features of it before you do it in detail. 草拟;画草图;勾勒 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Wood roughed out a possible framework for their story. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    伍德为他们的故事勾勒出了一个合理的梗概。

SYN outline, plan, draft, sketch
