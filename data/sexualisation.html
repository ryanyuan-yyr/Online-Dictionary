sexualize
sekʃuəlaɪz ˈsɛksjʊəˌlaɪz ˈsɛkʃuəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sexualizes, present participle sexualizing, past tense , past participle sexualized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 sexualise
VERB 使有性别;使有性征;使发生性关系 To sexualize something or someone means to make them sexual or consider them in a sexual way. 使有性别;使有性征;使发生性关系

  • Referring to children's friends as girlfriends and boyfriends sexualizes them. [VERB noun]

    将孩子们的朋友称为女朋友或男朋友就把他们的性别差异凸显出来了。

  • Rape is sexualised violence. [VERB-ed]

    强奸是性暴力行为。
