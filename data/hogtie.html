hog-tie hɔgtaɪ
(also hogtie)Word forms: hog-ties, hog-tying, hog-tied
1 V-T 捆住(手脚)If someone hog-ties an animal or a person, they tie their legs together, or they tie their arms and legs together. 捆住(手脚) [US]

  • The cops come in, hog-tie him, chuck him in the back of an ambulance.

    这些警察进来，捆住他的手脚，把他扔到救护车后面。

2 V-T 束缚If you are hog-tied by something, it restricts you or stops you from doing something. 束缚 [US]

  • She had been hog-tied by the system but she had never lost her compassion.

    她被制度束缚，但她从未失去她的热情。
