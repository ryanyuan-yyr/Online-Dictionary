sedition
sɪdɪʃən sɪˈdɪʃən sɪˈdɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 煽动反政府的言论(或文章、行为） Sedition is speech, writing, or behaviour intended to encourage people to fight against or oppose the government. 煽动反政府的言论(或文章、行为）

  • Government officials charged him with sedition.

    政府官员指控他煽动人们造反。

SYN rabble-rousing, treason, subversion, agitation
