toot
tuːt tut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense toots, present participle tooting, past tense , past participle tooted
V-ERG (使)(汽车喇叭)发出嘟嘟声 If someone toots their car horn or if a car horn toots, it produces a short sound or series of sounds. (使)(汽车喇叭)发出嘟嘟声

  • People set off fireworks and tooted their car horns. [VERB noun]

    人们点燃烟花，按响汽车喇叭。

  • Car horns toot as cyclists dart precariously through the traffic. [VERB]

    骑车人危险地在车流中飞速穿行时，汽车喇叭的嘟嘟声响成一片。

  • A man behind her tooted angrily. [VERB]

    她后面的一名男子愤怒地摁着汽车喇叭。

Toot is also a noun.

  • The driver gave me a wave and a toot.

    司机冲我挥挥手，按了下喇叭。
