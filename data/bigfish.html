big fish
Word forms: plural big fish
1 N-COUNT 大人物；重要人物；大鱼 If you describe someone as a big fish, you believe that they are powerful or important in some way. 大人物；重要人物；大鱼 [informal]

  • The four men arrested were described as really big fish by the U.S. Drug Enforcement Agency.

    美国禁毒署称被捕的4个人都是分量十足的大鱼。

SYN powerful person, authority, leader, important person  
2 PHRASE 小池塘里的大鱼；小地方的大人物 If you say that someone is a big fish in a small pond, you mean that they are powerful or important but only within a small group of people. 小池塘里的大鱼；小地方的大人物 [informal]

  • In South Africa, Jani was a big fish in a small pond.

    在南非，贾尼可谓塘中无鱼虾为大。
