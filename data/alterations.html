alteration
ɔːltəreɪʃən ˌɔːltəˈreɪʃən ˌɔltərˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural alterations
1 N-COUNT 改变；变更 An alteration is a change in or to something. 改变；变更 [Also + of]

  • Making some simple alterations to your diet will make you feel fitter. [+ to/in]

    对日常饮食做些简单的调整就会让你身体更健康。

  • The structural alterations made to the house were planned with Gail's help.

    对房屋结构调整的规划是在盖尔的帮助下完成的。

SYN change, adjustment, shift, amendment  
2 N-UNCOUNT 更改；改动 The alteration of something is the process of changing it. 更改；改动

  • Her jacket was at the boutique waiting for alteration.

    她的短上衣放在时装店里，要做些改动。
