fictionalize
fɪkʃənəlaɪz ˈfɪkʃənəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fictionalizes, present participle fictionalizing, past tense , past participle fictionalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 fictionalise
VERB 把…编成小说;使小说化 To fictionalize an account of something that really happened means to tell it as a story, with some details changed or added. 把…编成小说;使小说化

  • We had to fictionalize names. [VERB noun]

    我们得化用假名。

  • ...a fictionalised account of a true and horrific story. [VERB-ed]

    对一个真实的恐怖故事小说化的叙述
