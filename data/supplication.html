supplication
sʌplɪkeɪʃən ˌsʌpləˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural supplications
N-VAR 恳求;祈愿;哀求 A supplication is a prayer to God or a respectful request to someone in authority for help. 恳求;祈愿;哀求 [formal]

  • He raised his arms in a gesture of supplication.

    他举起双手，做祈求状。

  • The Labour government has to date resisted all supplications.

    到目前为止工党政府回绝了所有的请愿求情。

SYN plea, appeal, prayer, pleading
