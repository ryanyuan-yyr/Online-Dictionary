unstructured
ʌnstrʌktʃəʳd ʌnˈstrʌktʃəd ʌnˈstrʌktʃərd
ADJ-GRADED （会议、会面、活动等）未组织的，未作详细安排的 Something such as a meeting, interview, or activity that is unstructured is not organized in a complete or detailed way. （会议、会面、活动等）未组织的，未作详细安排的

  • Our aim was that these meetings be unstructured and informal.

    我们的目的是，这些会议不事先进行精心组织，要随意一些。

  • As seminars go, these are loose, unstructured affairs.

    和多数研讨会一样，这些研讨会也比较松散随意。

  • ...the highly unstructured nature of the graduate recruitment procedures.

    高度缺乏系统性的研究生招生程序
