craggy
krægi ˈkræɡɪd ˈkrægi
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 多峭壁的;陡峭的 A craggy cliff or mountain is steep and rocky. 多峭壁的;陡峭的

  • ...tiny villages on craggy cliffs.

    悬崖峭壁上的小村庄

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (脸)轮廓分明皱纹明显的 A craggy face has large features and deep lines. (脸)轮廓分明皱纹明显的

  • He's a very small man with a lined, craggy face.

    他是个脸部轮廓分明、皱纹明显的矮个子男人。

SYN rugged, lined, weathered, furrowed
