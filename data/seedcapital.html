seed capital
N-UNCOUNT 种子资金;原始资本 Seed capital is an amount of money that a new company needs to pay for the costs of producing a business plan so that they can raise further capital to develop the company. 种子资金;原始资本 [business]

  • I am negotiating with financiers to raise seed capital for my latest venture. [+ for]

    我正在同一些金融家协商，为我最新的风险项目募集种子资金。
