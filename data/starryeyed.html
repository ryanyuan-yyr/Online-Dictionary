starry-eyed ˈstɑriˌɪd
ADJ-GRADED 充满幻想的;想入非非的;不切实际的 If you say that someone is starry-eyed, you mean that they have such a positive or hopeful view of a situation that they do not see what it is really like. 充满幻想的;想入非非的;不切实际的

  • I'm not starry-eyed about Europe.

    我对欧洲没抱什么幻想。

  • ...a starry-eyed young couple.

    充满幻想的年轻夫妇
