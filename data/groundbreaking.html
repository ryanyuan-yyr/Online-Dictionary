groundbreaking graʊndbreɪkɪŋ ˈgraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ
(also ground-breaking)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 开创性的；创新的；革新的 You use groundbreaking to describe things which you think are significant because they provide new and positive ideas, and influence the way people think about things. 开创性的；创新的；革新的

  • ...his groundbreaking novel on homosexuality.

    他关于同性恋题材的开创性小说

  • ...groundbreaking research.

    创新研究
