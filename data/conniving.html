conniving
kənaɪvɪŋ kəˈnaɪvɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 惯使阴谋的;狡诈的 If you describe someone as conniving, you mean you dislike them because they make secret plans in order to get things for themselves or harm other people. 惯使阴谋的;狡诈的 [disapproval]

  • Edith was seen as a conniving, greedy woman.

    人们认为伊迪丝是一个诡计多端的贪婪女人。

SYN scheming, designing, plotting, calculating
