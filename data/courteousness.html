courteous
kɜːʳtiəs ˈkɜrtiəs
ADJ-GRADED 有礼貌的;谦恭的;恭敬的;客气的 Someone who is courteous is polite and respectful to other people. 有礼貌的;谦恭的;恭敬的;客气的

  • He was a kind and courteous man.

    他为人友善，而且彬彬有礼。

  • My friend's reply was courteous but firm.

    我朋友的回答很客气，但很坚决。

SYN polite, civil, respectful, mannerly  
courteously

  • Then he nodded courteously to me and walked off to perform his unpleasant duty.

    然后他礼貌地向我点了点头，便走开去干他那乏味的工作了。
