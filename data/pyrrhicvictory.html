Pyrrhic victory
pɪrɪk vɪktəri ˈpɪrɪk
(also pyrrhic victory)Word forms: plural Pyrrhic victories
N-COUNT 皮洛士式的胜利；得不偿失的胜利 If you describe a victory as a Pyrrhic victory, you mean that although someone has won or gained something, they have also lost something which was worth even more. 皮洛士式的胜利；得不偿失的胜利
