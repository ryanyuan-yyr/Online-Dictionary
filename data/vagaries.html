vagary
veɪgəri ˈveɪgəri vəˈgɛri ˈveɪˈgæri
Word forms: plural vagaries
N-COUNT [usually plural] 出乎意料的变化;难以预测的转变 Vagaries are unexpected and unpredictable changes in a situation or in someone's behaviour which you have no control over. 出乎意料的变化;难以预测的转变 [formal]

  • I take an assortment of clothes on holiday, as a provision against the vagaries of the weather.

    我带了各种衣服去度假，以防天气突变。

  • ...the perplexing vagaries of politics. [+ of]

    复杂难解、变幻莫测的政坛风云

SYN whim, caprice, unpredictability, sport
