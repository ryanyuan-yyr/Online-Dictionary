imposing
ɪmpoʊzɪŋ ɪmˈpoʊzɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 使人难忘的;仪表堂堂的;壮观的 If you describe someone or something as imposing, you mean that they have an impressive appearance or manner. 使人难忘的;仪表堂堂的;壮观的

  • He was an imposing man.

    他仪表堂堂。

  • ...the imposing wrought-iron gates at the entrance to the estate.

    庄园入口处气派的锻铁大门

SYN impressive, striking, grand, august
