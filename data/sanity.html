sanity
sænɪti ˈsænəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 心智健全;精神正常 A person's sanity is their ability to think and behave normally and reasonably. 心智健全;精神正常

  • He and his wife finally had to move from their apartment just to preserve their sanity.

    为了不致发疯，他和妻子最终只得从公寓中搬出。

SYN mental health, reason, rationality, stability  
2 N-UNCOUNT 清醒;明智;通情达理 If there is sanity in a situation or activity, there is a purpose and a regular pattern, rather than confusion and worry. 清醒;明智;通情达理

  • Rafsanjani has been considering various ways of introducing some sanity into the currency market.

    拉夫桑贾尼一直考虑通过各种方式使货币市场趋于理性。

SYN common sense, sense, good sense, rationality
