joystick
dʒɔɪstɪk ˈdʒɔɪˌstɪk
Word forms: plural joysticks
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] （计算机游戏的）控制杆，游戏杆 In some computer games, the joystick is the lever which the player uses in order to control the direction of the things on the screen. （计算机游戏的）控制杆，游戏杆
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （飞机的）操纵杆，驾驶杆 In an aircraft, the joystick is the lever which the pilot uses to control the direction and height of the aeroplane. （飞机的）操纵杆，驾驶杆
