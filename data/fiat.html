fiat
fiːæt fai- ˈfaɪət ˈfiɑt ˈfiæt ˈfiət ˈfaɪˌæt ˈfaɪət
Word forms: plural fiats
N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 命令;指令 If something is done by fiat, it is done because of an official order given by someone in authority. 命令;指令 [formal]
