law and order lɔː ənd ˈɔːdə
N-UNCOUNT 法律和秩序；法治 When there is law and order in a country, the laws are generally accepted and obeyed, so that society there functions normally. 法律和秩序；法治

  • If there were a breakdown of law and order, the army might be tempted to intervene.

    如果法治被践踏，军队可能会介入。

  • ...people who had no respect for law and order.

    不尊重法治的那些人
