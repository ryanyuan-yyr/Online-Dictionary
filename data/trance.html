trance
trɑːns træns træns trɑns
Word forms: plural trances
N-COUNT [oft preposition NOUN] 催眠状态;出神状态 A trance is a state of mind in which someone seems to be asleep and to have no conscious control over their thoughts or actions, but in which they can see and hear things and respond to commands given by other people. 催眠状态;出神状态

  • Like a man in a trance, Blake found his way back to his rooms.

    布莱克神思恍惚地回到了自己的房间。

  • They went into a trance to communicate with the spirit world.

    他们进入出神状态与灵界交流。

SYN daze, dream, spell, ecstasy
