look forward to
1 PHRASAL VERB 期待；盼望 If you look forward to something that is going to happen, you want it to happen because you think you will enjoy it. 期待；盼望

  • He was looking forward to working with the new Prime Minister. [V P P v-ing/n]

    他期待着与新首相共事。

SYN anticipate, expect, look for, wait for  
2 PHRASAL VERB 预期；预料 If you say that someone is looking forward to something useful or positive, you mean they expect it to happen. 预期；预料

  • Motor traders are looking forward to a further increase in vehicle sales. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    汽车经销商预期汽车销量会进一步攀升。
