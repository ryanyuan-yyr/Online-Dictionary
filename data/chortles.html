chortle
tʃɔːʳtəl ˈtʃɔːtəl ˈtʃɔrtəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense chortles, present participle chortling, past tense , past participle chortled
VERB 哈哈笑;呵呵笑 To chortle means to laugh in a way that shows you are very pleased. 哈哈笑;呵呵笑 [written]

  • There was silence for a moment, then Larry began chortling like an idiot. [VERB]

    先是一阵沉默，然后拉里像个白痴似的呵呵笑起来。

Chortle is also a noun.

  • He gave a chortle.

    他咯咯笑了笑。
