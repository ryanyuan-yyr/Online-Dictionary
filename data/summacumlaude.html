summa cum laude sʊmɑ kʊm laʊdeɪ ˈsʊmɑː kʊm ˈlaʊdeɪ ˈsʊmə kʊm ˈlaʊdɛ ˈsʌmə kʌm ˈlɔdi
1 ADV 享有最高荣誉If a college student graduates summa cum laude, they receive the highest honour that is possible. 享有最高荣誉 [US]

  • Jeremy Heyl graduated summa cum laude with a degree in Astrophysics.

    杰洛米以最高荣誉毕业，获天体物理学学位。

2 ADJ 享有最高荣誉的Summa cum laude is also an adjective. 享有最高荣誉的

  • ...a summa cum laude graduate of Princeton.

    ...普林斯顿最高荣誉毕业生。
