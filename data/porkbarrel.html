pork barrel
(also pork-barrel)
N-SING [usually NOUN n] 分肥拨款（指为赢得当地选民的选票而在地方工程上花费的大笔政府拨款） If you say that someone is using pork barrel politics, you mean that they are spending a lot of government money on a local project in order to win the votes of the people who live in that area. 分肥拨款（指为赢得当地选民的选票而在地方工程上花费的大笔政府拨款） [mainly US, disapproval]

  • Pork-barrel politicians hand out rents to win votes and influence people.

    搞分肥拨款的政客为获得选票、影响民情而发放房租补贴。
