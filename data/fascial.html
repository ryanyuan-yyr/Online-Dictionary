fascia
feɪʃə ˈfæʃiə ˈfæʃə ˈfeɪshiə ˈfeɪʃə
Word forms: plural fascias
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] (汽车的)仪表板 In a car, the fascia is the part surrounding the instruments and dials. (汽车的)仪表板 [British, formal]
in AM, use 美国英语用 instrument panel
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 招牌;门匾 The fascia on a shop front is the flat surface above the shop window, on which the name of the shop is written. 招牌;门匾 [British]
3 N-COUNT (可拆卸的)手机盖，手机壳 The fascia of a mobile phone is its detachable cover. (可拆卸的)手机盖，手机壳
