samizdat
sæmɪzdæt sɑːm- ˌsɑmɪzˈdɑt səmizˈdat
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 萨密兹达;(苏联和东欧的)地下出版发行系统 Samizdat referred to a system in the former USSR and Eastern Europe by which books and magazines forbidden by the state were illegally printed by groups who opposed the state. 萨密兹达;(苏联和东欧的)地下出版发行系统 [formal]

  • ...a publisher specialising in samizdat literature.

    专门出版和发行地下文学的出版商
