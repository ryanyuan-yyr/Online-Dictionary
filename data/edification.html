edification
edɪfɪkeɪʃən ˌɛdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 教诲;教化;启迪;陶冶 If something is done for your edification, it is done to benefit you in some way, for example by teaching you about something. 教诲;教化;启迪;陶冶 [formal]

  • Demonstrations, films, and videotapes are shown for your edification.

    采用了演示及播放电影和录像带的形式，以起到教育作用。

SYN instruction, schooling, education, information
