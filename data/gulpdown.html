gulp down
PHRASAL VERB 把…一口吞下（或咽下）；吞服 If you gulp down food or drink, you quickly eat or drink it all by swallowing large quantities of it at once. 把…一口吞下（或咽下）；吞服

  • She gulped down a mouthful of coffee. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她喝了一大口咖啡。

  • He'd gulped it down in one bite. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他一口就把它吞了下去。
