mirror image
(also mirror-image)Word forms: plural mirror images
N-COUNT 映像;翻版;倒影；反像;完全相同之物 If something is a mirror image of something else, it is like a reflection of it, either because it is exactly the same or because it is the same but reversed. 映像;翻版;倒影；反像;完全相同之物

  • I saw in him a mirror image of my younger self. [+ of]

    我在他身上看见了年轻时的自己。

  • This is almost the mirror image of the situation in Scotland.

    这几乎就是苏格兰局势的翻版。

SYN reflection, double, image, copy
