revisit
riːvɪzɪt riːˈvɪzɪt riˈvɪzɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense revisits, present participle revisiting, past tense , past participle revisited
VERB 再访;重游 If you revisit a place, you return there for a visit after you have been away for a long time, often after the place has changed a lot. 再访;重游

  • In the summer, when we returned to Canada, we revisited this lake at dawn. [VERB noun]

    夏天我们回到加拿大后，在黎明时分重游了这个湖泊。
