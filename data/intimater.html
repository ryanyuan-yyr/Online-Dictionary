intimate
ɪntɪmət ɪntɪmeɪt ɪntɪmət ɪntɪmeɪt ˈɪntəmət ˈɪntəˌmeɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense intimates, present participle intimating, past tense , past participle intimated
The adjective and noun are pronounced /'ɪntɪmət/. The verb is pronounced /'ɪntɪmeɪt/. 形容词读作 /'ɪntɪmət/,动词读作 /'ɪntɪmeɪt/。
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 亲密的;密切的 If you have an intimate friendship with someone, you know them very well and like them a lot. 亲密的;密切的 [formal]

  • I discussed with my intimate friends whether I would immediately have a baby.

    我与密友们讨论是否我要马上生孩子。

SYN close, dear, loving, near  
An intimate is an intimate friend.密友;至交;知己

  • They are to have an autumn wedding, an intimate of the couple confides.

    他们的婚礼将在秋季举行,这对情侣的一位密友透露说。

intimately

  • He did not feel he had got to know them intimately.

    他认为他没必要和他们密切来往。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有恋爱关系的;有性关系的 If two people are in an intimate relationship, they are involved with each other in a loving or sexual way. 有恋爱关系的;有性关系的

  • I just won't discuss my intimate relationships.

    我就是不想讨论我的恋爱关系。

  • ...their intimate moments with their boyfriends.

    她们与男友共浴爱河的时刻

SYN sexual, romantic, private, sexy  
intimately

  • You have to be willing to get to know yourself and your partner intimately.

    你必须愿意在恋爱中逐渐认识自己和伴侣。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 个人的;私下的;隐私的 An intimate conversation or detail, for example, is very personal and private. 个人的;私下的;隐私的

  • He wrote about the intimate details of his family life.

    他写到了关于他家庭生活的一些不为人知的细节。

  • I hate to interrupt your intimate conversation but we do have an assignment to discuss.

    我实在不愿打断你们的私人谈话,但是我们确实有事要谈。

SYN private, personal, confidential, special  
intimately

  • It was the first time they had attempted to talk intimately.

    那是他们第一次试图私下交谈。

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 宁静怡人的;温馨的 If you use intimate to describe an occasion or the atmosphere of a place, you like it because it is quiet and pleasant, and seems suitable for close conversations between friends. 宁静怡人的;温馨的 [approval]

  • ...an intimate candlelit dinner for two.

    温馨的二人烛光晚餐

SYN cosy, relaxed, friendly, informal  
5 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (联系)密切的，紧密的 An intimate connection between ideas or organizations, for example, is a very strong link between them. (联系)密切的，紧密的

  • ...an intimate connection between madness and wisdom.

    疯狂与智慧的密切关联

  • France has kept the most intimate links with its former African territories.

    法国与其前非洲属地一直保持着最密切的联系。

intimately

  • Property and equities are intimately connected in Hong Kong.

    在香港，房地产与证券密切关联。

6 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 深刻的;详尽的;精通的 An intimate knowledge of something is a deep and detailed knowledge of it. 深刻的;详尽的;精通的

  • He surprised me with his intimate knowledge of Kierkegaard and Schopenhauer.

    他对克尔恺郭尔与叔本华的深刻了解让我很吃惊。

SYN detailed, minute, full, experienced  
intimately

  • ...a golden age of musicians whose work she knew intimately.

    其作品她耳熟能详的黄金一代的音乐家

7 VERB 暗示;提示 If you intimate something, you say it in an indirect way. 暗示;提示 [formal] [Also VERB n]

  • He went on to intimate that he was indeed contemplating a shake-up of the company. [VERB that]

    他继续暗示他确实在考虑对公司进行一次大改组。

  • He had intimated to the French and Russians his readiness to come to a settlement. [VERB + to]

    他已经向法国人和俄国人暗示他愿意达成协议。

SYN announce, state, declare, communicate
