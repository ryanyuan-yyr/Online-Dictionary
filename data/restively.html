restive
restɪv ˈrɛstɪv
ADJ-GRADED 不耐烦的;厌烦的;不满的 If you are restive, you are impatient, bored, or dissatisfied. 不耐烦的;厌烦的;不满的 [formal]

  • The audience grew restive.

    观众变得不耐烦了。

  • ...restive national minorities.

    心怀不满的少数民族

SYN restless, nervous, uneasy, impatient  
restiveness

  • There were signs of restiveness among the younger members.

    年轻成员流露出了不满的情绪。
