eraser
ɪreɪzəʳ -reɪs- iˈreɪsər ɪˈreɪsər
Word forms: plural erasers
N-COUNT 橡皮擦;黑板擦 An eraser is an object, usually a piece of rubber or plastic, which is used for removing something that has been written using a pencil or a pen. 橡皮擦;黑板擦 [US, also British, formal]

  • ...a large, flat, pink India-rubber eraser.

    一大块粉红色的扁平橡皮擦
