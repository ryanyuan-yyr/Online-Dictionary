purposeless
pɜːʳpəsləs ˈpɜrpəsləs
ADJ-GRADED 漫无目的的；无意义的 If an action is purposeless, it does not seem to have a sensible purpose. 漫无目的的；无意义的

  • Time may also be wasted in purposeless meetings.

    时间也可能浪费在毫无意义的会议当中。

  • Surely my existence cannot be so purposeless?

    当然我的存在不能如此没有意义吧？

SYN pointless, empty, unnecessary, useless
