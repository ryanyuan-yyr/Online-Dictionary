slip in
PHRASAL VERB 不失时机地插入 If you slip in a question or comment, you ask or make it without interrupting the flow of the conversation. 不失时机地插入 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Slip in a few questions about other things. [VERB PREP. noun]

    见缝插针地提几个题外的问题。

  • I cannot resist slipping in a word of advice. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我忍不住想插上一句建议。
