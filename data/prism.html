prism
prɪzəm ˈprɪzəm
Word forms: plural prisms
1 N-COUNT 棱镜 A prism is a block of clear glass or plastic which separates the light passing through it into different colours. 棱镜
2 N-COUNT [usually singular, NOUN of n] 曲解；扭曲 If you see something through a prism of something such as time or memory, your idea of it is affected by that thing. 曲解；扭曲 [literary]

  • Through the smoky prism of time, I could just barely make out my father as a young man.

    时间模糊了记忆，我几乎想不起父亲年轻时的样子。
