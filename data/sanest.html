sane
seɪn seɪn
Word forms: comparative saner, superlative sanest
1 ADJ-GRADED 心智健全的;神志正常的 Someone who is sane is able to think and behave normally and reasonably, and is not mentally ill. 心智健全的;神志正常的

  • He seemed perfectly sane.

    他似乎神志完全正常。

  • It wasn't the act of a sane person.

    心智健全的人做不出这种事来。

SYN rational, normal, all there [informal] , lucid  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 清醒的;明智的;合情合理的 If you refer to a sane person, action, or system, you mean one that you think is reasonable and sensible. 清醒的;明智的;合情合理的

  • No sane person wishes to see conflict or casualties.

    理智的人都不愿意看到冲突或伤亡。

  • ...a sane and safe energy policy.

    安全合理的能源政策

SYN sensible, sound, reasonable, balanced
