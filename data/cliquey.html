cliquey
kliːki
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 cliquish
ADJ-GRADED 派系的;小集团的;排他的 If you describe a group of people or their behaviour as cliquey, you mean they spend their time only with other members of the group and seem unfriendly towards people who are not in the group. 派系的;小集团的;排他的 [disapproval]

  • ...cliquey gossip.
