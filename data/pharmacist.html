pharmacist
fɑːʳməsɪst ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪst ˈfɑrməsɪst
Word forms: plural pharmacists
1 N-COUNT 药剂师；药商 A pharmacist is a person who is qualified to prepare and sell medicines. 药剂师；药商
2 N-COUNT 药店；药房 A pharmacist or a pharmacist's is a shop in which drugs and medicines are sold by a pharmacist. 药店；药房 [mainly British]
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 pharmacy
