itemize
aɪtəmaɪz ˈaɪtəmˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense itemizes, present participle itemizing, past tense , past participle itemized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 itemise
VERB 详细登记;逐条记录 If you itemize a number of things, you make a list of them. 详细登记;逐条记录

  • Itemise your gear and mark major items with your name and post code. [VERB noun]

    详细登记你的个人物品,在重要物品上标明你的姓名和邮编。

  • ...a fully itemised bill. [VERB-ed]

    一张完整列出明细的账单

SYN list, record, detail, count
