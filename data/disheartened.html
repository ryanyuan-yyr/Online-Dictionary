disheartened
dɪshɑːʳtənd dɪsˈhɑːtənd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 灰心的;失望的;气馁的 If you are disheartened, you feel disappointed about something and have less confidence or less hope about it than you did before. 灰心的;失望的;气馁的

  • He was disheartened by their hostile reaction. [+ by]

    他们的敌对反应让他打了退堂鼓。

SYN discouraged, depressed, crushed, dismayed
