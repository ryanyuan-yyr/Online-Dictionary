cobalt
koʊbɔːlt ˈkəʊbɔːlt ˈkoʊˌbɔlt
1 N-UNCOUNT 钴 Cobalt is a hard silvery-white metal which is used to harden steel and for producing a blue dye. 钴

  • ...a country rich in copper, cobalt and diamonds.

    铜、钴和钻石资源丰富的国家

2 COLOUR 钴蓝色;深蓝色 Cobalt or cobalt blue is a deep blue colour. 钴蓝色;深蓝色

  • They walked past stalls selling huge sprays of crimson, saffron and cobalt flowers.

    他们经过很多卖花的摊位，摊位上摆着大把大把的鲜花，有深红色的，有金黄色的，还有深蓝色的。

  • ...a woman in a soft cobalt blue dress.

    穿着质地柔软的钴蓝色连衣裙的女人
