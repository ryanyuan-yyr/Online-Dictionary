state of siege steɪt əv siːdʒ
N-SING 戒严状态 A state of siege is a situation in which a government or other authority puts restrictions on the movement of people into or out of a country, town, or building. 戒严状态

  • Under the state of siege, the police could arrest suspects without charges or warrants.

    在戒严时，警方无需指控或逮捕令就可以逮捕嫌疑人。
