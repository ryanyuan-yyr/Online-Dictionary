drift off
PHRASAL VERB 迷迷糊糊地睡去;逐渐入睡 If you drift off to sleep, you gradually fall asleep. 迷迷糊糊地睡去;逐渐入睡 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • It was only when he finally drifted off to sleep that the headaches eased. [VERB PREP. + to]

    只有当他最后迷迷糊糊睡着时,他的头痛才有所减轻。

SYN fall asleep, go off, drop off [informal] , crash out [informal]
