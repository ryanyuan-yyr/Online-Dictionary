monopoly
mənɒpəli məˈnɑpəli
Word forms: plural monopolies
1 N-VAR [oft with poss] 垄断;专营服务 If a company, person, or state has a monopoly on something such as an industry, they have complete control over it, so that it is impossible for others to become involved in it. 垄断;专营服务 [business]

  • ...Russian moves to end a state monopoly on land ownership. [+ on]

    俄国人采取的终止国家垄断土地所有权的措施

  • ...the governing party's monopoly over the media. [+ over]

    执政党对媒体的垄断

  • ...an inquiry by the Monopolies Commission.

    垄断委员会的调查

2 N-COUNT 垄断企业;专卖者 A monopoly is a company which is the only one providing a particular product or service. 垄断企业;专卖者 [business]

  • ...a state-owned monopoly.

    国有垄断企业

3 N-SING 独有;独具 If you say that someone does not have a monopoly on something, you mean that they are not the only person who has that thing. 独有;独具

  • Women do not have a monopoly on feelings of betrayal. [+ on]

    被出卖的感觉并非只有女性才有。
