set in
PHRASAL VERB (不好的事情)产生，开始，到来，恶化 If something unpleasant sets in, it begins and seems likely to continue or develop. (不好的事情)产生，开始，到来，恶化

    当他们发现魔力已失的时候，失望的情绪开始蔓延。

  • Winter is setting in and the population is facing food and fuel shortages. [VERB PREP.]
