script
skrɪpt skrɪpt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense scripts, present participle scripting, past tense , past participle scripted
1 N-COUNT (戏剧、电影)剧本;(电视节目)广播稿 The script of a play, film, or television programme is the written version of it. (戏剧、电影)剧本;(电视节目)广播稿

  • Jenny's writing a film script.

    珍妮在写一个电影剧本。

SYN text, lines, words, book  
2 VERB 为(电影等)写剧本;为(广播、电视节目)撰稿 The person who scripts a film or a radio or television play writes it. 为(电影等)写剧本;为(广播、电视节目)撰稿

  • ...James Cameron, who scripted and directed both films. [VERB noun]

    詹姆斯·卡梅伦，这两部电影的编剧兼导演

SYN write, draft, pen, compose  
3 N-VAR [usually adjective NOUN] (一种语言的)书写系统，字母表 You can refer to a particular system of writing as a particular script . (一种语言的)书写系统，字母表

  • ...a text in the Malay language but written in Arabic script.

    一篇使用阿拉伯字母书写的马来语演讲稿

4 N-SING 期待;计划 If you say that something which has happened is not in the script, or that someone has not followed the script, you mean that something has happened which was not expected or intended to happen. 期待;计划

  • Losing was not in the script.

    没想到会输掉比赛。

  • The game plan was right. We just didn't follow the script.

    比赛战术是正确的。只是我们没有照部署去打。
