attachment
ətætʃmənt əˈtætʃmənt
Word forms: plural attachments
1 N-VAR 喜欢;爱慕;忠诚 If you have an attachment to someone or something, you are fond of them or loyal to them. 喜欢;爱慕;忠诚

  • As a teenager she formed a strong attachment to one of her teachers. [+ to]

    少女时代的她曾迷上了自己的一个老师。

  • Mother and child form a close attachment.

    母子情深。

  • ...a feeling of attachment to the land where their ancestors have lived.

    他们对祖祖辈辈生活过的土地的深深依恋

SYN fondness, liking, feeling, love  
2 N-COUNT （机器的）附件，附加装置 An attachment is a device that can be fixed onto a machine in order to enable it to do different jobs. （机器的）附件，附加装置

  • Some models come with attachments for dusting. [+ for]

    一些模型上附带了除尘器。

SYN accessory, fitting, extra, addition  
3 N-VAR [usu on N] （临时的）隶属，委派 If someone is on attachment to another company, department, or place, they are working there temporarily. （临时的）隶属，委派 [mainly British]

  • During her course she worked on attachment for six months at Kew Gardens.

    在上学期间，她曾临时到基尤植物园工作了6个月。

  • Before coming on my attachment to a City law firm, I spent a year at Strasbourg University.

    在被临时派往伦敦的一家律师事务所工作前，我在斯特拉斯堡大学学习了一年。

SYN assignment, charge, commission, transfer  
4 N-COUNT （文件）附件 An attachment is an extra document that is added to another document. （文件）附件

  • Justice Fitzgerald included a 120-page discussion paper as an attachment to the annual report. [+ to]

    在年度报告上，法官菲茨杰拉德附上了一篇长达120页的讨论稿。

5 N-COUNT （邮件）附件 In computing, an attachment is a file which is attached separately to a message that you send to someone. （邮件）附件

  • When you send an e-mail you can also send a sound or graphic file as an attachment.

    在发送电子邮件时，你也可以附上一份声音或图片文件。
