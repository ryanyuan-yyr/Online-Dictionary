transfer list
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural transfer lists, present participle transfer listing, past tense , past participle transfer listed
The verb is usually spelled transfer-list. 动词通常拼作 transfer-list。
1 N-COUNT (职业足球俱乐部的)转会名单 In football, a transfer list is a list of players at a club who may be sold to other clubs. (职业足球俱乐部的)转会名单 [British]
2 VERB (职业足球俱乐部)把(球员)列入转会名单 If a football club transfer-lists a player, it puts their name on the transfer list. (职业足球俱乐部)把(球员)列入转会名单 [British]

  • Oxford United have transfer-listed two midfield players. [VERB noun]

    牛津联足球俱乐部已把两名中场球员列入转会名单。
