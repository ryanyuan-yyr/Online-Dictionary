symbolize
sɪmbəlaɪz ˈsɪmbəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense symbolizes, present participle symbolizing, past tense , past participle symbolized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 symbolise
VERB 象征;代表 If one thing symbolizes another, it is used or regarded as a symbol of it. 象征;代表

  • The fall of the Berlin Wall symbolised the end of the Cold War between East and West. [VERB noun]

    柏林墙的倒塌象征着东西方冷战的结束。

  • ...the post-war world order symbolised by the United Nations. [VERB-ed]

    以联合国为代表的战后世界秩序

SYN represent, signify, stand for, mean
