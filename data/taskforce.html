task force
(also taskforce)Word forms: plural task forces
1 N-COUNT (军队的)特别行动小组，特遣部队 A task force is a small section of an army, navy, or air force that is sent to a particular place to deal with a military crisis. (军队的)特别行动小组，特遣部队

  • The United States is sending a naval task force to the area to evacuate American citizens.

    美国正要派遣一支海军特遣队赶赴该地区疏散美国公民。

2 N-COUNT (执行特定任务的)工作组 A task force is a group of people working together on a particular task. (执行特定任务的)工作组

  • We have set up a task force to look at the question of women returning to work.

    我们已经成立了一个工作组去调查妇女复工的问题。
