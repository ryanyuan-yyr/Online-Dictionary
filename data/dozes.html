doze
doʊz doʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dozes, present participle dozing, past tense , past participle dozed
VERB (尤指在白天)小睡，打盹儿 When you doze, you sleep lightly or for a short period, especially during the daytime. (尤指在白天)小睡，打盹儿

  • For a while she dozed fitfully. [VERB]

    有一阵子，她时睡时醒。

SYN nap, sleep, slumber, nod  
Doze is also a noun.

  • After lunch I had a doze.

    吃过午饭，我小睡了会儿。

Phrasal verbs:
doze off
