self-expression
ˈsɛlfɛkˈsprɛʃən
N-UNCOUNT (对性格、情感、意见的)自我表现，自我抒发 Self-expression is the expression of your personality, feelings, or opinions, for example through an artistic activity such as drawing or dancing. (对性格、情感、意见的)自我表现，自我抒发

  • The conflict between the urge for self-expression and the pressure to conform is a central one for her.

    想要表现自我的欲望和被迫从众的压力对她来说是一个最主要的冲突。

  • Clothes are a fundamental form of self-expression.

    衣服是一种基本的自我表现形式。
