revivalist
rɪvaɪvəlɪst rɪˈvaɪvəlɪst
Word forms: plural revivalists
ADJ [ADJ n] 宗教复兴运动的 Revivalist people or activities are involved in trying to make a particular religion more popular and more influential. 宗教复兴运动的

  • ...the Hindu revivalist party.

    印度教复兴派

  • They attended a revivalist meeting and became born-again Christians.

    他们参加了一个奋兴布道会，成为了获得重生的基督徒。

Revivalist is also a noun.

  • Booth was a revivalist intent on his Christian vocation.

    布思是一个坚定不移地奉行其基督徒天职的宗教复兴运动者。
