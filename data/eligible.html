eligible
elɪdʒɪbəl ˈɛlɪdʒəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 有资格的;符合条件的;有能力的 Someone who is eligible to do something is qualified or able to do it, for example because they are old enough. 有资格的;符合条件的;有能力的

  • Almost half the population are eligible to vote in today's election.

    大约有一半人有资格参加今天的选举投票。

  • You could be eligible for a university scholarship. [+ for]

    你也许有资格获得大学奖学金。

SYN entitled, fit, qualified, suited  
eligibility

  • The rules covering eligibility for benefits changed in the 1980s.

    20世纪80年代，有关救济金领取资格的规定变了。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （作为结婚对象）合适的，中意的 An eligible man or woman is not yet married and is thought by many people to be a suitable partner. （作为结婚对象）合适的，中意的

  • He's the most eligible bachelor in Japan.

    他是日本最为抢手的单身汉。
