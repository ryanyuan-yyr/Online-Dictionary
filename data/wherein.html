wherein
hweərɪn wɛərˈɪn hwɛrˈɪn wɛrˈɪn
1 PRON-REL 在那里;在其中 Wherein means in which place or thing. 在那里;在其中 [formal, literary, or old-fashioned]

  • ...a riding school wherein we could learn the art of horsemanship.

    一所我们可以在那里学习马术的骑术学校

  • Adequate housing is possible in developed, mixed economies wherein the interests of the poor have prevailed.

    在发达的混合经济体中，充足的房源供给是可能的，在那里贫困阶层的利益较有保障。

2 QUEST 在哪里;在哪方面 Wherein means in which part or respect. 在哪里;在哪方面 [formal]

  • Wherein lies the truth?

    真理到底在哪里？

Wherein is also a conjunction.

  • It is difficult to know wherein Mr Ritchie hoped to find salvation for his country.

    很难弄清里奇先生希望从哪里为他的国家求得救助。
