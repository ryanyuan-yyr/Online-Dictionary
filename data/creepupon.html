creep up on
1 PHRASAL VERB 缓慢地悄悄靠近 If you creep up on someone, you move slowly closer to them without being seen by them. 缓慢地悄悄靠近

  • They'll creep up on you while you're asleep. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他们会在你睡着的时候悄悄接近你。

2 PHRASAL VERB (感觉或状态)不知不觉地开始影响(某人)，不知不觉地产生 If a feeling or state creeps up on you, you hardly notice that it is beginning to affect you or happen to you. (感觉或状态)不知不觉地开始影响(某人)，不知不觉地产生

  • The desire to be a mother may creep up on you unexpectedly. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    你会不知不觉地产生想当母亲的渴望。
