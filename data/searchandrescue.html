search and rescue sɜːtʃ ənd ˈrɛskjuː
(also search-and-rescue)
N-UNCOUNT [usu N n] (如战争或自然灾害后的)搜索救援Search and rescue operations involve looking for people who are lost or in danger, for example, after a war or a natural disaster, and bringing them back safely. (如战争或自然灾害后的)搜索救援

  • An alpine search and rescue team found Lancaster the day after he disappeared.

    在兰卡斯特失踪的第二天，一支高山搜救队发现了他。
