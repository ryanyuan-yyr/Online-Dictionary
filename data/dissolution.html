dissolution
dɪsəluːʃən ˌdɪsəˈluʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] (议会等机构的)解散 Dissolution is the act of breaking up officially an organization or institution, or of formally ending a parliament. (议会等机构的)解散 [formal]

  • He stayed on until the dissolution of the firm in 1948.

    他一直呆到 1948 年公司解散。

  • Politicians say it could lead to a dissolution of parliament.

    政客们说这可能会导致议会解散。

SYN ending, end, finish, conclusion  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] (商业协议的)终止;(婚姻关系的)解除 Dissolution is the act of officially ending a formal agreement, for example a marriage or a business arrangement. (商业协议的)终止;(婚姻关系的)解除 [formal]

  • ...the statutory requirement for granting dissolution of a marriage. [+ of]

    准予婚姻关系解除的法律规定

SYN breaking up, parting, divorce, separation  
3 N-UNCOUNT [also a N, oft NOUN of n] 解体;瓦解;分裂 Dissolution is a process in which something becomes weaker and then disappears. 解体;瓦解;分裂 [formal]

  • ...the dissolution of traditional family life.

    传统家庭生活的解体

SYN disintegration, fall, decline, degradation
