whorl
hwɜːʳl hwɔːʳl hwɔrl hwɜrl
Word forms: plural whorls
N-COUNT (指纹等的)涡，螺旋状 A whorl is a spiral shape, for example the pattern on the tips of your fingers. (指纹等的)涡，螺旋状 [literary]

  • He stared at the whorls and lines of her fingertips.

    他盯着她指尖的涡纹和流纹。

  • ...dense whorls of red-purple flowers.

    层层叠叠的紫红色花朵

SYN swirl, spiral, coil, twist
