surmount
səʳmaʊnt sərˈmaʊnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense surmounts, present participle surmounting, past tense , past participle surmounted
1 VERB 克服;解决 If you surmount a problem or difficulty, you deal successfully with it. 克服;解决

  • I realized I had to surmount the language barrier. [VERB noun]

    我意识到我不得不克服语言障碍。

SYN overcome, master, conquer, pass  
2 VERB [usually passive] 处于…上面;置于…顶端 If something is surmounted by a particular thing, that thing is on top of it. 处于…上面;置于…顶端 [formal]

  • The island is surmounted by a huge black castle. [be VERB-ed]

    岛的最高处耸立着一座巨大的黑色城堡。

SYN cap, top, crown, tip
