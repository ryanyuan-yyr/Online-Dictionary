protrusion
prətruːʒən proʊ- proʊˈtruʒən prəˈtruʒən
Word forms: plural protrusions
N-COUNT 凸出物；凸起部分 A protrusion is something that sticks out from something. 凸出物；凸起部分 [formal]

  • He grabbed at a protrusion of rock with his right hand. [+ of]

    他用右手抓住了一块凸起的岩石。

SYN protuberance, swelling, lump, bump
