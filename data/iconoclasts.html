iconoclast
aɪkɒnəklæst aɪˈkɑnəˌklæst
Word forms: plural iconoclasts
N-COUNT 打破传统信仰(或习惯)者 If you describe someone as an iconoclast, you mean that they often criticize beliefs and things that are generally accepted by society. 打破传统信仰(或习惯)者 [formal]

  • Cage was an iconoclast. He refused to be bound by western musical traditions of harmony and structure.

    凯奇是个反传统的人。他拒绝接受西方有关和声和结构的音乐传统的束缚。

SYN rebel, radical, dissident, heretic
