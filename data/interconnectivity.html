interconnect
ɪntəʳkənekt ˌɪntərkəˈnɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense interconnects, present participle interconnecting, past tense , past participle interconnected
1 V-RECIP-ERG (使)相互连接;(使)互相联系 Things that interconnect or are interconnected are connected to or with each other. You can also say that one thing interconnects with another. (使)相互连接;(使)互相联系 [Also VERB n + with]

  • The causes are many and may interconnect. [VERB]

    原因有很多而且可能互相关联。

  • Their lives interconnect with those of celebrated figures of the late eighteenth-century. [VERB + with]

    他们的生活与18世纪晚期的名人联系在一起。

  • The regions are interconnected by an excellent highway system. [be VERB-ed]

    那些地区通过完善的公路系统相互连接。

  • ...a huge mesh of interconnecting wires. [VERB-ing]

    铁丝绞缠的巨网

  • ...a dense network of nerve fibres that interconnects neurons in the brain. [VERB noun]

    使大脑内部神经元相互连接的密集的神经纤维网络
