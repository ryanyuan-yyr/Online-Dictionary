turbulent
tɜːʳbjʊlənt ˈtɜrbjələnt
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 变幻莫测的;动荡的;骚乱的;混乱的 A turbulent time, place, or relationship is one in which there is a lot of change, confusion, and disorder. 变幻莫测的;动荡的;骚乱的;混乱的

  • They had been together for five or six turbulent years of rows and reconciliations.

    他们在一起生活了五六年，一直吵吵闹闹，分分合合，没有片刻安宁。

  • The turbulent world of Middle Eastern politics defies prediction.

    中东动荡不安的政治局势让人难以预测。

SYN wild, violent, disorderly, agitated  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 湍急的;狂暴的;汹涌的 Turbulent water or air contains strong currents which change direction suddenly. 湍急的;狂暴的;汹涌的

  • I had to have a boat that could handle turbulent seas.

    我必须有一艘能经得起狂风骇浪的船。

SYN stormy, rough, raging, tempestuous
