stony
stoʊni ˈstoʊni
Word forms: comparative stonier, superlative stoniest
1 ADJ-GRADED (地)多石的，不平的 Stony ground is rough and contains a lot of stones. (地)多石的，不平的

  • The steep, stony ground is well drained.

    陡峭的石头地面排水性能很好。

  • ...a stony track.

    碎石小路

SYN rocky, rough, gritty, gravelly  
2 ADJ-GRADED (表情或态度)冷酷的，无情的，冰冷的 A stony expression or attitude does not show any sympathy or friendliness. (表情或态度)冷酷的，无情的，冰冷的

  • She gave me the stoniest look I ever got.

    她看我的眼神冷酷无比。

  • He drove us home in stony silence.

    他开车送我们回家，一路冷冰冰地一言不发。
