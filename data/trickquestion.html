trick question
trɪk ˈkwɛstʃən
Word forms: plural trick questions
N-COUNT 存有陷阱的问题;带套问题 If someone asks you a trick question, they ask you a question which is very difficult to answer, for example because there is a hidden difficulty or because the answer that seems obvious is not the correct one. 存有陷阱的问题;带套问题
