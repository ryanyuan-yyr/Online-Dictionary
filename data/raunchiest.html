raunchy
rɔːntʃi ˈrɔntʃi ˈrɑntʃi
Word forms: comparative raunchier, superlative raunchiest
ADJ-GRADED 色情的;淫秽的;惹火的;挑逗的 If a film, a person, or the way that someone is dressed is raunchy, they are sexually exciting. 色情的;淫秽的;惹火的;挑逗的 [informal]

  • ...her raunchy new movie.

    她新拍的惹火电影

  • ...bands playing slow raunchy dance tunes.

    弹奏慢拍挑逗舞曲的乐队

SYN sexy, sexual, steamy [informal] , earthy
