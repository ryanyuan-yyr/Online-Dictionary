skill
skɪl skɪl
Word forms: plural skills
1 N-COUNT 技术性工作;手艺 A skill is a type of work or activity which requires special training and knowledge. 技术性工作;手艺

  • Most of us will know someone who is always learning new skills, or studying new fields.

    我们大多数人都会认识某个总是在不停地学习新技术或研究新领域的人。

2 N-UNCOUNT 技巧;技能;本领;技艺 Skill is the knowledge and ability that enables you to do something well. 技巧;技能;本领;技艺

  • The cut of a diamond depends on the skill of its craftsman. [+ of]

    钻石切割靠的是工匠的技艺。
