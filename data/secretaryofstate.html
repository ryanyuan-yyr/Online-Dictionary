secretary of state
1 N-COUNT (美国)国务卿 In the United States, the Secretary of State is the head of the government department which deals with foreign affairs. (美国)国务卿
2 N-COUNT (英国)大臣 In Britain, the Secretary of State for a particular government department is the head of that department. (英国)大臣
