unstinting
ʌnstɪntɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 慷慨的；大方的 Unstinting help, care, or praise is great in amount or degree and is given generously. 慷慨的；大方的

  • The task of producing the text was made easier by the unstinting help and generosity extended to me.

    在人们的慷慨帮助下，我写这篇文章的工作变得轻松了许多。

  • ...her unstinting charity work.

    她慷慨助人的慈善事业

SYN lavish, liberal, generous, full
