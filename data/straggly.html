straggly
strægəli ˈstrægəlli
ADJ-GRADED (头发)散乱的;(植物)蔓生的 Straggly hair or a straggly plant is thin and grows or spreads out untidily in different directions. (头发)散乱的;(植物)蔓生的

  • Her long fair hair was knotted and straggly.

    她那长长的金发散乱而打缕。

  • The yard held a few straggly bushes.

    庭院里有几处蔓生的灌木丛。

SYN spread out, spreading, rambling, untidy
