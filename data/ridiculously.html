ridiculously
rɪdɪkjʊləsli
ADV-GRADED [usually ADVERB adjective/adverb] 荒唐地;荒谬地;可笑地;令人惊奇地 You use ridiculously to emphasize the fact that you think something is unreasonable or very surprising. 荒唐地;荒谬地;可笑地;令人惊奇地 [emphasis]

  • Dena bought rolls of silk that seemed ridiculously cheap.

    德娜买了几卷看起来非常次的丝绸。

  • She looked ridiculously young to be a mother.

    她作为妈妈实在是太年轻了。
