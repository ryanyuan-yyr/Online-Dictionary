camerawork
kæmrəwɜːʳk ˈkæmərəˌwɜːk ˈkæmərəˌwɜrk
N-UNCOUNT (尤指别具匠心、与众不同的)摄影技巧，摄影手法 The camerawork in a film is the way it has been filmed, especially if the style is interesting or unusual in some way. (尤指别具匠心、与众不同的)摄影技巧，摄影手法

  • The director employs sensuous, atmospheric camerawork and deft dramatic touches.
