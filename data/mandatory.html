mandatory
mændətri Am -tɔːri ˈmændəˌtɔri
1 ADJ 强制性的; 法定的;义务的 If an action or procedure is mandatory, people have to do it, because it is a rule or a law. 强制性的; 法定的;义务的 [formal]

  • ...the mandatory retirement age of 65.

    65岁的法定退休年龄

  • Attendance is mandatory.

    务必参加。

SYN compulsory, required, binding, obligatory  
2 ADJ (惩罚)强制性的，法定的 If a crime carries a mandatory punishment, that punishment is fixed by law for all cases, in contrast to crimes for which the judge or magistrate has to decide the punishment for each particular case. (惩罚)强制性的，法定的 [formal]

  • ...the mandatory life sentence for murder.

    谋杀罪必然会面临的终身监禁

  • ...the four-year ban which is mandatory.

    为期四年的强制禁令
