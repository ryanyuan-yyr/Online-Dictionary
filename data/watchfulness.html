watchful
wɒtʃfʊl wɑtʃfəl wɔtʃfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 警觉的;警惕的 Someone who is watchful notices everything that is happening. 警觉的;警惕的

  • The best thing is to be watchful and see the family doctor for any change in your normal health.

    最佳的做法是保持警惕，一旦你的身体状况有任何变化就立即去看家庭医生。

  • He looked tall and powerful and, with his dark, watchful face, a little threatening.

    他看上去又高又壮，面色黝黑，满脸警觉，真叫人有点害怕。

SYN alert, attentive, vigilant, observant  
2 PHRASE [NOUN inflects, PHRASE n] 在(某人)的警觉目光下；在…的监视下 If you do something under the watchful eye of someone who has authority over you, they watch you carefully to make sure there are no problems. 在(某人)的警觉目光下；在…的监视下

  • There were demonstrations in the streets today, under the watchful eye of police.

    今天，在警方的监视下大街上进行了游行。

  • Children swim at the pool, under the watchful eye of lifeguards.

    孩子们在救生员的监护下在水池里游泳。

3 PHRASE [VERB inflects, PHRASE n] 看护;提防 If you keep a watchful eye on someone or something, you watch carefully to make sure there are no problems. 看护;提防

  • Keep a watchful eye on babies and toddlers.

    要看好婴儿和刚会走路的孩子。
