intractable
ɪntræktəbəl ɪnˈtræktəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 难驾驭的;难对付的 Intractable people are very difficult to control or influence. 难驾驭的;难对付的 [formal]

  • What may be done to reduce the influence of intractable opponents?

    能做些什么来减少那些难缠的反对者的影响呢？

SYN difficult, contrary, awkward, wild  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (问题或情况)棘手的,难处理的 Intractable problems or situations are very difficult to deal with. (问题或情况)棘手的,难处理的 [formal]

  • The economy still faces intractable problems.

    经济仍然面临着一些棘手的问题。

SYN unmanageable, difficult, insoluble, out of hand
