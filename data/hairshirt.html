hair shirt
Word forms: plural hair shirts
1 N-COUNT (旧时僧侣苦行修炼时穿的）刚毛衬衫 A hair shirt is a shirt made of rough uncomfortable cloth which some religious people used to wear to punish themselves. (旧时僧侣苦行修炼时穿的）刚毛衬衫

  • He was sent barefoot wearing only a hair shirt into the wilderness as a penance.

    为了赎罪，他跣足褐衣，被流放到了荒野中。

2 N-COUNT 自我惩罚 If you say that someone is wearing a hair shirt, you mean that they are trying to punish themselves to show they are sorry for something they have done. 自我惩罚

  • No one is asking you to put on a hair shirt and give up all your luxuries.

    没人要跟你过不去，非要你放弃那些奢华的享受。
