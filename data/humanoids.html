humanoid
hyumənɔɪd ˈhjuːməˌnɔɪd ˈhjuməˌnɔɪd ˈjuməˌnɔɪd
Word forms: humanoids
1 ADJ 像人的Something that is humanoid looks or acts like a human being, although it is not human. 像人的

  • ...humanoid robots that could mimic a range of human activities.

    ...可以模拟一系列人类活动的类人机器人。

2 N-COUNT 类人生物A humanoid is a nonhuman creature that looks or acts like a human being, for example, in science fiction. 类人生物

  • ...a spaceship whose only occupants are humanoids killed in mysterious circumstances.

    ...一艘宇宙飞船，上面唯一居住者是在神秘条件下被杀的类人生物。
