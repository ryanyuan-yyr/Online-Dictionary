go along
1 PHRASAL VERB 出席（会议等）；去（某地） If you go along to a meeting, event, or place, you attend or visit it. 出席（会议等）；去（某地）

  • I went along to the meeting. [VERB PREP. + to]

    我去参加了会议。

  • You should go along and have a look. [VERB PREP.]

    你应该去瞧瞧。

2 PHRASAL VERB [usually cont] 进展 If you describe how something is going along, you describe how it is progressing. 进展

  • Things were going along fairly well. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    事情进展十分顺利。
