tangle with
PHRASAL VERB 与…争吵;与…打架 If you tangle with another person, you get involved in a conflict with them. 与…争吵;与…打架

  • Better not tangle with the censors. They're very vindictive. [VERB PREP. noun]

    最好别和检查员发生冲突，他们可爱记仇了。

  • All the newspapers wanted to do was to photograph the police officers tangling with the demonstrators. [VERB PREP. noun]

    报纸最希望拍到的就是警察和示威者扭打的场面。

SYN come into conflict with, come up against, cross swords with, dispute with
