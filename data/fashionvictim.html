fashion victim
Word forms: plural fashion victims
N-COUNT 一味赶时髦而不顾衣着是否得体者;时尚牺牲品 A fashion victim is someone who thinks that being fashionable is more important than looking nice, and as a result often wears very fashionable clothes that do not suit them or that make them look silly. 一味赶时髦而不顾衣着是否得体者;时尚牺牲品 [disapproval]

  • I'm not a fashion victim; I stick to what suits me.

    我不是一味赶时髦的人，我只穿适合自己的服装。
