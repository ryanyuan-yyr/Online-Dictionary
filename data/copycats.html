copycat
kɒpikæt ˈkɑpiˌkæt
(also copy-cat)Word forms: plural copycats
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 模仿犯罪的 A copycat crime is committed by someone who is copying someone else. 模仿犯罪的

  • ...a series of copycat attacks by hooligan gangs.

    流氓团伙的一系列模仿性袭击

2 N-COUNT 模仿者;好跟人学的人 If you call someone a copycat, you are accusing them of copying your behaviour, dress, or ideas. 模仿者;好跟人学的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • The Beatles have copycats all over the world.

    披头士乐队的模仿者遍布全世界。
