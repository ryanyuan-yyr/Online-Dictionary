impetuosity
ɪmpetʃuɒsɪti ɪmˌpɛtʃuˈɑsɪti
N-UNCOUNT 急躁的行动(或情绪);冲动 Impetuosity is the quality of being impetuous. 急躁的行动(或情绪);冲动

  • With characteristic impetuosity, he announced he was leaving school.

    一贯性急冲动的他又宣布要退学了。

SYN haste, violence, vehemence, impulsiveness
