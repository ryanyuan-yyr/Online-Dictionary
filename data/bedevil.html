bedevil
bɪdevəl biˈdɛvəl bɪˈdɛvəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bedevils, present participle bedevilling, past tense , past participle bedevilled
in AM, use 美国英语用 bedeviling, bedeviled
VERB 烦扰；困扰；使苦恼 If you are bedevilled by something unpleasant, it causes you a lot of problems over a period of time. 烦扰；困扰；使苦恼 [formal]

  • His career was bedevilled by injury. [be VERB-ed]

    他的职业生涯深受伤病的困扰。

  • The development has been bedevilled with problems. [be VERB-ed]

    发展受到了诸多问题的困扰。

  • ...a problem that has bedevilled service industries for decades. [VERB noun]

    困扰了服务行业几十年的问题

SYN plague, worry, trouble, frustrate
