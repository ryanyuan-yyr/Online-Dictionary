pricey
praɪsi ˈpraɪsɪ ˈpraɪsi
(also pricy)Word forms: comparative pricier, superlative priciest
ADJ-GRADED 价格高的；昂贵的 If you say that something is pricey, you mean that it is expensive. 价格高的；昂贵的 [informal]

  • Medical insurance is very pricey.

    医疗保险费用昂贵。

SYN expensive, dear, steep [informal] , costly
