indecipherable
ɪndɪsaɪfərəbəl ˌɪndiˈsaɪfərəbəl ˌɪndɪˈsaɪfərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED (字迹、话语等)难懂的，难辨认的，模糊的 If writing or speech is indecipherable, you cannot understand what the words are. (字迹、话语等)难懂的，难辨认的，模糊的

  • Maggie's writing was virtually indecipherable.

    玛吉的笔迹几乎无法辨认。

  • He uttered little indecipherable sounds.

    他声音很小，听不清楚。

SYN illegible, unintelligible, indistinguishable, unreadable
