canter
kæntəʳ ˈkæntər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense canters, present participle cantering, past tense , past participle cantered
VERB (马)小跑,慢跑 When a horse canters, it moves at a speed that is slower than a gallop but faster than a trot. (马)小跑,慢跑 [Also VERB]

  • The competitors cantered into the arena to conclude the closing ceremony. [VERB + into

    选手们慢跑进入竞技场,为闭幕式画上了句号。

SYN jog, lope
