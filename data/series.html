series
sɪəriːz ˈsɪəriːz ˈsɪriz ˈsiriz
Word forms: plural series
1 N-COUNT 一连串;一系列;连续;接连 A series of things or events is a number of them that come one after the other. 一连串;一系列;连续;接连

  • ...a series of meetings with students and political leaders. [+ of]

    与学生和政治领袖的一系列会面

  • ...a series of explosions. [+ of]

    一连串的爆炸事件

SYN sequence, course, chain, succession  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] (广播或电视的)系列节目,系列片 A radio or television series is a set of programmes of a particular kind which have the same title. (广播或电视的)系列节目,系列片

  • ...the TV series 'The Trials of Life' presented by David Attenborough.

    由戴维·阿滕伯勒主持的电视系列片《生命之源》

  • ...the world's longest-running radio series, Britain's 'The Archers'.

    世界上最长的广播剧，英国的《阿彻一家》

Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse series and serial

    注意不要混淆 series 和 serial。
