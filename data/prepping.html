prep
prep prɛp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense preps, present participle prepping, past tense , past participle prepped
1 VERB 准备；预备 If you prep something, you prepare it. 准备；预备 [mainly US, informal]

  • After prepping the boat, they sailed it down to Carloforte. [VERB noun]

    备好船后，他们就驶向了南边的卡洛福泰。

2 VERB 为（患者）做手术准备 If a doctor or nurse preps a patient, they get them ready for surgery or another procedure. 为（患者）做手术准备 [mainly US, informal] [Also VERB n]

  • I was already prepped for surgery so I went straight into theatre. [be VERB-ed]

    我已经做好了接受外科手术的准备，于是就直接进入了手术室。

3 N-UNCOUNT （英国一些私立学校的）家庭作业 In some British private schools, prep is the name given to school work that children do in the evening after school has finished. （英国一些私立学校的）家庭作业 [British]

  • Anne struggled to help Peter and Zara with their prep.

    安妮努力帮助彼得和扎拉完成他们的家庭作业。
