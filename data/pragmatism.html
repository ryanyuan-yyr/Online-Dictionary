pragmatism
prægmətɪzəm ˈprægməˌtɪzəm
Word forms: plural pragmatists
N-UNCOUNT 实用主义；务实主义 Pragmatism means thinking of or dealing with problems in a practical way, rather than by using theory or abstract principles. 实用主义；务实主义 [formal]

  • She had a reputation for clear thinking and pragmatism.

    她曾以思路清晰、观点务实而闻名。

pragmatist

  • He is a political pragmatist, not an idealist.

    他是政治上的实用主义者，而不是理想主义者。
