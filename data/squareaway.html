square away
PHRASAL VERB 成功应付;圆满完成 If you square something or someone away, you deal with them so that the situation is satisfactory. 成功应付;圆满完成 [US] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Negotiators have already squared away a lot of the agreements that will be signed at the Earth Summit. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    谈判人员已经把很多将在地球峰会上签署的协议准备妥当。
