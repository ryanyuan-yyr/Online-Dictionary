regard
rɪgɑːʳd rɪˈgɑrd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense regards, present participle regarding, past tense , past participle regarded
1 VERB 认为;把…看作 If you regard someone or something as being a particular thing or as having a particular quality, you believe that they are that thing or have that quality. 认为;把…看作

  • He was regarded as the most successful Chancellor of modern times. [be VERB-ed + as]

    他被认为是现代最为成功的财政大臣。

  • I regard creativity both as a gift and as a skill. [VERB noun + as]

    我认为创造力既是一种天赋也是一种技巧。

SYN consider, see, hold, rate  
2 VERB 对…怀有（某种感情）;以（某种感情）看待 If you regard something or someone with a feeling such as dislike or respect, you have that feeling about them. 对…怀有（某种感情）;以（某种感情）看待

  • He regarded drug dealers with loathing. [VERB noun + with]

    他对贩毒分子深恶痛绝。

  • Displays of emotion are regarded with suspicion. [VERB noun with noun]

    情感的流露会招致猜疑。

  • He was a highly regarded scholar. [adv V-ed]

    他是一位声誉很高的学者。

3 VERB （以某种方式）看，注视 If you regard someone in a certain way, you look at them in that way. （以某种方式）看，注视 [literary]

  • She regarded him curiously for a moment. [VERB noun]

    她好奇地看了他一会儿。

  • The clerk regarded him with benevolent amusement. [VERB noun + with]

    这个职员好心而又饶有兴趣地看着他。

SYN look at, view, eye, watch  
4 N-UNCOUNT 尊重;重视;敬爱 If you have regard for someone or something, you respect them and care about them. If you hold someone in high regard, you have a lot of respect for them. 尊重;重视;敬爱

  • I have a very high regard for him and what he has achieved.

    我非常钦佩他的为人和成就。

  • There were armed people about, people with little regard for human life.

    到处都是武装分子，视人命如草芥。

  • The Party ruled the country without regard for the people's views.

    该党治国对民意视而不见。

SYN respect, esteem, deference, store  
5 N-PLURAL 问候，致意（尤用于信中表示问好） Regards are greetings. You use regards in expressions such as best regards and with kind regards as a way of expressing friendly feelings towards someone, especially in a letter. 问候，致意（尤用于信中表示问好） [formulae]

  • Give my regards to your family. [+ to]

    请代我向你家人问好。

  • My best regards to Mary.

    请转达我对玛丽的良好祝愿。

SYN good wishes, respects, greetings, compliments  
6 PREP-PHRASE 至于;关于 You can use as regards to indicate the subject that is being talked or written about. 至于;关于

  • As regards the war, Haig believed in victory at any price.

    至于这场战争，黑格认为要不惜任何代价取得胜利。

SYN concerning, regarding, relating to, pertaining to  
7 PREP-PHRASE 关于;至于 You can use with regard to or in regard to to indicate the subject that is being talked or written about. 关于;至于

  • The department is reviewing its policy with regard to immunisation.

    这个部门正在审度其有关疫苗接种的政策。

8 PHRASE 在这/那方面；就这/那一点 You can use in this regard or in that regard to refer back to something that you have just said. 在这/那方面；就这/那一点

  • In this regard nothing has changed.

    在这方面没什么变化。

  • I may have made a mistake in that regard.

    我在那一点上可能已经犯了一个错误。
