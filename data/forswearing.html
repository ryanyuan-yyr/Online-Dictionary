forswear
fɔːʳsweəʳ fɔːˈswɛə fɔrˈswɛr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense forswears, present participle forswearing, past tense forswore, past participle forsworn
VERB 发誓抛弃;承诺放弃 If you forswear something, you promise that you will stop doing it, having it, or using it. 发誓抛弃;承诺放弃 [formal, or literary]

  • The party was offered a share of government if it forswore violence. [VERB noun]
