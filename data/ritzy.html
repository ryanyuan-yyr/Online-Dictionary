ritzy
rɪtsi ˈrɪtsi
Word forms: comparative ritzier, superlative ritziest
ADJ-GRADED 时髦的;阔气的;豪华的 If you describe something as ritzy, you mean that it is fashionable or expensive. 时髦的;阔气的;豪华的 [informal]

  • Palm Springs has ritzy restaurants and glitzy nightlife.

    棕榈泉有高档的餐厅和奢华的夜生活。

SYN luxurious, grand, luxury, elegant
