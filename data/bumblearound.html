bumble around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语用 bumble about
PHRASAL VERB 踉跄；跌跌撞撞地行走 When someone bumbles around or bumbles about, they behave in a confused, disorganized way, making mistakes and usually not achieving anything. 踉跄；跌跌撞撞地行走

  • Most of us are novices on the computer — just bumbling about on them. [VERB PREP.]

    我们大多数人都是电脑新手—只会到处瞎鼓捣。
