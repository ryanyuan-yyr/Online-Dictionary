Word forms: plural age groups
N-COUNT 年龄层，年龄组（指某地或某团体中所有生于某一时期的人） An age group is the people in a place or organization who were born during a particular period of time, for example all the people aged between 18 and 25. 年龄层，年龄组（指某地或某团体中所有生于某一时期的人）
