fuzzy
fʌzi ˈfʌzɪ ˈfʌzi
Word forms: comparative fuzzier, superlative fuzziest
1 ADJ-GRADED (毛发)柔软卷曲的 Fuzzy hair sticks up in a soft, curly mass. (毛发)柔软卷曲的

  • He had fuzzy black hair and bright black eyes.

    他一头柔软卷曲的黑发，眼睛又黑又亮。

2 ADJ-GRADED 覆有细绒毛的;绒毛状的;毛茸茸的 If something is fuzzy, it has a covering that feels soft and like fur. 覆有细绒毛的;绒毛状的;毛茸茸的

  • ...fuzzy material.

    绒毛料子

3 ADJ-GRADED (图片、声音等)不清楚的，模糊的 A fuzzy picture, image, or sound is unclear and hard to see or hear. (图片、声音等)不清楚的，模糊的

  • A couple of fuzzy pictures have been published.

    有几张模糊的照片刊登了出来。

  • ...fuzzy bass lines.

    含糊的低音乐段

4 ADJ-GRADED (人或头脑)稀里糊涂的，迷迷糊糊的 If you or your thoughts are fuzzy, you are confused and cannot think clearly. (人或头脑)稀里糊涂的，迷迷糊糊的

  • He had little patience for fuzzy ideas.

    他没什么耐心听那些糊里糊涂的想法。

5 ADJ-GRADED 不明确的;含混的 You describe something as fuzzy when it is vague and not clearly defined. 不明确的;含混的

  • The border between science fact and science fiction gets a bit fuzzy.

    科学事实和科幻小说之间的界限变得有点儿模糊了。

SYN unclear, confused, blurred, vague  
6 ADJ [ADJ n] (计算机)模糊(逻辑)的 Fuzzy logic is a type of computer logic that is supposed to imitate the way that humans think, for example by adapting to changing circumstances rather than always following the same procedure. (计算机)模糊(逻辑)的

  • ...domestic appliances that also use fuzzy logic to mimic the way a person would do the job manually.

    同样使用模糊逻辑模拟手动操作的家用电器

  • ...research on fuzzy systems.

    对模糊系统的研究
