pernickety
pəʳnɪkɪti pərˈnɪkəti
ADJ-GRADED 爱挑剔的；吹毛求疵的 If you describe someone as pernickety, you think that they pay too much attention to small, unimportant details. 爱挑剔的；吹毛求疵的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • Customs officials can get extremely pernickety about things like that. [+ about]

    海关官员可能会对那种东西极度吹毛求疵。
