rector
rektəʳ ˈrɛktər
Word forms: plural rectors
1 N-COUNT (英国国教的)教区长 A rector is a priest in the Church of England who is in charge of a particular area. (英国国教的)教区长

  • He was rector of All Hallows Church in Wellingborough.

    他是韦灵伯勒万圣教堂的教区长。

2 N-COUNT (一些大学或学院的)校长,院长 A rector is an important official in some universities or colleges. (一些大学或学院的)校长,院长

  • ...the rector of Imperial College, London.

    伦敦帝国理工学院院长
