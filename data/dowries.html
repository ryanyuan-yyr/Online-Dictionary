dowry
daʊəri ˈdaʊərɪ ˈdaʊri
Word forms: plural dowries
N-COUNT 嫁妆;陪嫁 A woman's dowry is the money and goods which, in some cultures, her family gives to the man that she marries. 嫁妆;陪嫁

  • The money from her dowry was invested in her mother's store.

    她的嫁妆钱都投到了母亲的铺子里。

SYN portion, marriage settlement, dot [archaic] , lobola [South Africa]
