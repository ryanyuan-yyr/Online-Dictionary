self-regulation
ˌsɛlfrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 自我管理;自我调节 Self-regulation is the controlling of a process or activity by the people or organizations that are involved in it rather than by an outside organization such as the government. 自我管理;自我调节

  • Competition between companies is too fierce for self-regulation to work.

    公司间的竞争过于激烈，自我调节机制已无法发挥作用。
