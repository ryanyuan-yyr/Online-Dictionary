oddity
ɒdɪti ˈɑdəti
Word forms: plural oddities
1 N-COUNT 怪人；怪事 An oddity is someone or something that is very strange. 怪人；怪事

  • Losing my hair made me feel an oddity.

    脱发使我觉得自己是个怪人。

  • Carlson noticed another oddity; his plant had bloomed twice.

    卡尔森注意到另外一件怪事：他的植物开了两次花。

2 N-COUNT 奇怪；古怪；奇特 The oddity of something is the fact that it is very strange. 奇怪；古怪；奇特

  • ...the oddities of the Welsh legal system. [+ of]

    威尔士法律体系的奇特之处

SYN irregularity, phenomenon, anomaly, freak
