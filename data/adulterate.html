adulterate
ədʌltəreɪt əˈdʌltərˌeɪt əˈdʌltərɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adulterates, present participle adulterating, past tense , past participle adulterated
VERB [usually passive] 在（饮食中）掺入杂质;掺假 If something such as food or drink is adulterated, someone has made its quality worse by adding water or cheaper products to it. 在（饮食中）掺入杂质;掺假

  • The food had been adulterated to increase its weight. [be VERB-ed]

    食物被掺了杂质以增加分量。

  • There is a regulation against adulterated cosmetics. [VERB-ed]

    规定禁止出售假冒化妆品。

SYN debase [formal] , thin, weaken, corrupt  
adulteration

  • ...the adulteration of tobacco.

    假烟
