TBA
待宣布TBA is sometimes written in announcements to indicate that something such as the place where something will happen or the people who will take part is not yet known and will be announced at a later date. TBA is an abbreviation for "to be announced." 待宣布

  • When a manufacturer could not supply requested information, we have shown TBA.

    制造商不能提供所需信息时，我们已表示“有待宣布”。
