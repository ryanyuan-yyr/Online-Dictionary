incongruity
ɪnkɒŋgruːɪti ˌɪnkɑnˈgruɪti ˌɪnkənˈgruɪti
Word forms: plural incongruities
N-VAR 不协调;不适宜;不相称 The incongruity of something is its strangeness when considered together with other aspects of a situation. 不协调;不适宜;不相称 [formal]

  • She smiled at the incongruity of the question. [+ of]

    面对这样突兀的问题,她笑了。

  • ...the almost absurd incongruity between her wealth and her lifestyle.

    她的财富与生活方式之间几近荒谬的不相称

  • ...the incongruities of life in war-time London.

    战时伦敦生活的不协调

SYN inappropriateness, discrepancy, inconsistency, disparity
