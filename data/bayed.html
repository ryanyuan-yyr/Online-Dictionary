bay
beɪ beɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bays, present participle baying, past tense , past participle bayed
1 N-COUNT [oft in names] 湾；海湾 A bay is a part of a coast where the land curves inwards. 湾；海湾

  • ...a short ferry ride across the bay.

    乘渡船横渡海湾的短途旅行

  • ...the Bay of Bengal.

    孟加拉海湾

  • ...the San Francisco Bay area.

    旧金山湾地区

SYN inlet, sound, gulf, entrance  
2 N-COUNT （建筑物的）开间，隔间 A bay is a partly enclosed area, inside or outside a building, that is used for a particular purpose. （建筑物的）开间，隔间

  • The animals are herded into a bay, then butchered.

    动物们被赶到一个隔间里，然后进行宰杀。

  • The car reversed into the loading bay.

    汽车倒入装卸间。

3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] （飞机或船只的）货舱，分隔舱 On an aircraft or ship, a bay is a section that is used for carrying cargo or equipment. （飞机或船只的）货舱，分隔舱

  • ...in the cargo bays of aircraft.

    在飞机的货舱里

4 N-COUNT （建筑物的）凸出部分；（尤指）飘窗，凸窗 A bay is an area of a room which extends beyond the main walls of a house, especially an area with a large window at the front of a house. （建筑物的）凸出部分；（尤指）飘窗，凸窗
5 ADJ （马）棕红色的 A bay horse is reddish-brown in colour. （马）棕红色的
6 VERB [usually cont] 大声疾呼；强烈要求 If a number of people are baying for something, they are demanding something angrily, usually that someone should be punished. 大声疾呼；强烈要求

  • The referee ignored voices baying for a penalty. [VERB + for]

    裁判对要求判罚点球的叫喊声不予理睬。

  • Opposition politicians have been baying for his blood. [VERB for noun]

    反对派的政治家们一直呼吁对其严惩。

  • ...the baying crowd. [VERB-ing]

    怒吼的人群

7 VERB （犬）连续吠；（狼）连续嚎叫 If a dog or wolf bays, it makes loud, long cries. （犬）连续吠；（狼）连续嚎叫 [Also VERB]

  • A dog suddenly howled, baying at the moon. [VERB + at]

    一只狗突然对着月亮狂吠不止。

SYN howl, cry, roara hound, bark  
8 See also: sick bay 
9 PHRASE 使…不能靠近；防止…侵袭 If you keep something or someone at bay, or hold them at bay, you prevent them from reaching, attacking, or affecting you. 使…不能靠近；防止…侵袭

  • Eating oranges keeps colds at bay.

    吃橘子可以预防感冒。

  • Prisoners armed with baseball bats used the hostages to hold police at bay.

    手持棒球棒的囚犯们挟持了人质，使警察无法靠近。
