schoolmate
skuːlmeɪt ˈskulˌmeɪt
Word forms: plural schoolmates
N-COUNT [oft with poss] (尤指关系要好的)同学，同窗，校友 A schoolmate is a child who goes to the same school as you, especially one who is your friend. (尤指关系要好的)同学，同窗，校友

  • He started the magazine with a schoolmate.

    他和一个同学创办了那份杂志。

  • He was a favorite with his schoolmasters but not his schoolmates.

    学校的老师们很喜欢他，但同学们对他并不待见。
