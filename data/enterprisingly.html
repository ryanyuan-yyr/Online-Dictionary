enterprising
entəʳpraɪzɪŋ ˈɛntərˌpraɪzɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有事业心的;有创新精神的;有胆识的 An enterprising person is willing to try out new, unusual ways of doing or achieving something. 有事业心的;有创新精神的;有胆识的

  • Debra is a very enterprising young black businesswoman who is involved in a lot of activities.

    德布拉是个年青而又非常有胆识的黑人女商人，其涉猎广泛。

  • Some enterprising members found ways of reducing their expenses or raising their incomes.

    一些有创新精神的成员找到各种增收减支的方法。

SYN resourceful, original, spirited, keen
