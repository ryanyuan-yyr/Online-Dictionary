sporty
spɔːʳti ˈspɔrti
Word forms: comparative sportier, superlative sportiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (汽车)速度快的，像跑车似的 You can describe a car as sporty when it performs like a racing car but can be driven on normal roads. (汽车)速度快的，像跑车似的

  • The steering and braking are exactly what you want from a sporty car.

    其转向和制动装置都完全符合一辆运动型汽车的标准。

2 ADJ-GRADED 爱好体育运动的 Someone who is sporty likes playing sports. 爱好体育运动的
