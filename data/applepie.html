apple pie
ˈæpəl paɪ
Word forms: plural apple pies
1 N-COUNT 苹果派 An apple pie is a kind of pie made with apples. 苹果派
2 PHRASE 整整齐齐的;井井有条的 If a room or a desk is in apple pie order, it is neat and tidy, and everything is where it should be. 整整齐齐的;井井有条的

  • They found everything in apple-pie order.

    他们发现一切都整整齐齐。

3 PHRASE 十足美国味的；地道美国式的 If you say that something is as American as apple pie, you mean that it is typically American. 十足美国味的；地道美国式的

  • Jeans are as American as apple pie.

    牛仔裤极具美国特色。
