turn away
1 PHRASAL VERB 拒绝…进入 If you turn someone away, you do not allow them to enter your country, home, or other place. 拒绝…进入

  • Turning refugees away would be an inhumane action. [VERB noun PREP.]

    拒绝收容难民是不人道的行为。

  • Hard times are forcing community colleges to turn away students. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当下时局艰难，一些社区学院被迫停止招生。

SYN send someone away, reject, repel, rebuff  
2 PHRASAL VERB 弃用(方法、主意) To turn away from something such as a method or an idea means to stop using it or to become different from it. 弃用(方法、主意)

  • Japanese corporations have been turning away from production and have diverted into finance and real estate. [VERB PREP. + from]

    日本公司已经由生产型企业转而从事金融和房地产业。

  • Medicine began to turn away from botany in the scientific revolution of the 17th and 18th centuries. [V P from n]

    在 17、18 世纪的科学革命中，医学开始从植物学转而研究其他方向。
