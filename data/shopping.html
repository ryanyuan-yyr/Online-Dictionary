shopping
ʃɒpɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 购物;买东西 When you do the shopping, you go to shops and buy things. 购物;买东西

  • I'll do the shopping this afternoon.

    今天下午我会去买东西。

2See also:window shopping 
3 N-UNCOUNT 从商店购买来的东西(尤指食品) Your shopping is the things that you have bought from shops, especially food. 从商店购买来的东西(尤指食品)

  • We put the shopping away.

    我们把买回来的东西放好。

SYN purchases, buys, acquisitions
