stammer
stæməʳ ˈstæmər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stammers, present participle stammering, past tense , past participle stammered
1 VERB 口吃;结结巴巴地说 If you stammer, you speak with difficulty, hesitating and repeating words or sounds. 口吃;结结巴巴地说

  • Five per cent of children stammer at some point. [VERB]

    5%的儿童在某个时期会口吃。

  • 'Forgive me,' I stammered. [VERB with quote]

    “原谅我，”我期期艾艾地说。

  • People cursed and stammered apologies. [VERB noun]

    大家骂了几句，然后结结巴巴地道歉。

SYN stutter, falter, splutter, pause  
stammering
