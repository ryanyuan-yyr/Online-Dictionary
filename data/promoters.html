promoter
prəmoʊtəʳ prəˈmoʊtər proʊˈmoʊtər
Word forms: plural promoters
1 N-COUNT （尤指体育赛事的）主办者，承办者 A promoter is a person who helps organize and finance an event, especially a sports event. （尤指体育赛事的）主办者，承办者

  • ...one of the top boxing promoters in Britain.

    英国最顶尖的拳击比赛主办者之一

SYN organizer, arranger, entrepreneur, impresario  
2 N-COUNT 提倡者；倡导者 The promoter of a cause or idea tries to make it become popular. 提倡者；倡导者

  • Aaron Copland was an energetic promoter of American music. [+ of]

    阿伦·科普兰从来都是美国音乐的积极倡导者。

SYN supporter, champion, advocate, campaigner
