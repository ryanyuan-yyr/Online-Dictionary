indigestible
ɪndɪdʒestɪbəl ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstəbəl ˌɪndaɪˈdʒɛstəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 难消化的 Food that is indigestible cannot be digested easily. 难消化的

  • Fried food is very indigestible.

    油炸食品非常难消化。

2 ADJ-GRADED 难理解的;难吸收的 If you describe facts or ideas as indigestible, you mean that they are difficult to understand, complicated, and dull. 难理解的;难吸收的 [disapproval]

  • ...a dense, indigestible and wordy book.

    一本冗长难懂的厚书
