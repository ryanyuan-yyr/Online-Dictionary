umpteen
ʌmptiːn ˌʌmpˈtiːn ˈʌmpˈtin
DET 无数的；大量的；许许多多的 Umpteen can be used to refer to an extremely large number of things or people. 无数的；大量的；许许多多的 [informal, emphasis]

  • He was interrupted by applause umpteen times.

    他无数次被掌声打断。

  • He has produced umpteen books, plays and television series.

    他推出了众多图书、戏剧和电视连续剧作品。
