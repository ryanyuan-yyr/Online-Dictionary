surroundings
səraʊndɪŋz
N-PLURAL [oft poss NOUN] 环境 When you are describing the place where you are at the moment, or the place where you live, you can refer to it as your surroundings . 环境

  • Schumacher adapted effortlessly to his new surroundings.

    舒马赫非常轻松地适应了新环境。

  • ...a peaceful holiday home in beautiful surroundings.

    环境优美宁静的度假屋
