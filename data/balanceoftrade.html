balance of trade
Word forms: plural balances of trade
N-COUNT [usually singular] （一国的）贸易差额 A country's balance of trade is the difference in value, over a period of time, between the goods it imports and the goods it exports. （一国的）贸易差额 [business]

  • The deficit in Britain's balance of trade in March rose to more than 2100 million pounds.

    英国3月份的贸易逆差已经增长到21亿英镑以上。
