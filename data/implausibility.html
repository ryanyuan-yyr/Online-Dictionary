implausible
ɪmplɔːzɪbəl ɪmˈplɔzəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 难以置信的;不像真实的 If you describe something as implausible, you believe that it is unlikely to be true. 难以置信的;不像真实的

  • I had to admit it sounded like an implausible excuse.

    我得承认那听起来像是个难以叫人相信的借口。

  • It seems implausible that the projects would have gone ahead without her backing.

    这些项目没有得到她的支持就开始进行了，这似乎不合情理。

SYN improbable, unlikely, weak, incredible  
implausibly

  • They are, rather implausibly, close friends.

    令人难以置信的是,他们竟是亲密朋友。
