occupation
ɒkjʊpeɪʃən ˌɑkjuˈpeɪʃən ˌɑkjəˈpeɪʃən
Word forms: plural occupations
1 N-COUNT 职业;工作 Your occupation is your job or profession. 职业;工作

  • I suppose I was looking for an occupation which was going to be an adventure.

    我想我在找的是一份具有冒险性的工作。

  • Occupation: administrative assistant.

    职业：行政助理

SYN job, work, calling, business  
2 N-COUNT 消遣；日常需做的事务 An occupation is something that you spend time doing, either for pleasure or because it needs to be done. 消遣；日常需做的事务

  • Parachuting is a dangerous occupation.

    跳伞是一项危险的消遣运动。

SYN hobby, pastime, diversion, relaxation  
3 N-UNCOUNT 占领;占据;侵占 The occupation of a country happens when it is entered and controlled by a foreign army. 占领;占据;侵占

  • Prii had become fluent in German during the Wehrmacht's occupation of Estonia in 1942.

    在1942年德军侵占爱沙尼亚期间，普里学得了一口流利的德语。

  • ...the deportation of Jews from Paris during the German occupation.

    德国人占领巴黎期间对犹太人的驱逐

SYN invasion, seizure, conquest, incursion  
4 N-UNCOUNT （对建筑物的）占用,居住,使用 The occupation of a building is the act or fact of someone living or working in it. （对建筑物的）占用,居住,使用

  • ...people who sell their home and buy another one for their own occupation.

    出售房屋再另购一套自住的人
