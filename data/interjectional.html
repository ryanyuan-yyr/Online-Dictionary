interjection
ɪntəʳdʒekʃən ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃən
Word forms: plural interjections
1 N-COUNT (突然的)插话 An interjection is something you say which interrupts someone else who is speaking. (突然的)插话

  • ...the moronic and insensitive interjections of the disc jockey. [+ of]

    电台音乐节目主持人既白痴又不顾及他人感受的插话

SYN exclamation, cry, ejaculation [literary] , interpolation  
2 N-COUNT 感叹词;感叹语 In grammar, an interjection is a word or expression which you use to express a strong feeling such as surprise, pain, or horror. 感叹词;感叹语
