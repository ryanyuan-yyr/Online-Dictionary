bedside
bedsaɪd ˈbɛdˌsaɪd
1 N-SING [usually NOUN n] 床旁；床边；床侧 Your bedside is the area beside your bed. 床旁；床边；床侧

  • She put a cup of tea down on the bedside table.

    她在床头柜上放了一杯茶。

  • He drew a chair up to the bedside and sat down.

    他拉了把椅子到床边坐了下来。

2 N-SING [usually with poss] （某人的）病床边 If you talk about being at someone's bedside, you are talking about being near them when they are ill in bed. （某人的）病床边

  • She kept vigil at the bedside of her critically ill son.

    她整夜都守在病重的儿子床边。

  • She was called to her brother's bedside.

    她被叫到了哥哥的病榻前。
