send on
PHRASAL VERB 转寄，转发,转达(尤指文件) If you send on something you have received, especially a document, you send it to another place or person. 转寄，转发,转达(尤指文件) [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • We coordinate the reports from the overseas divisions, and send them on to headquarters in Athens. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们整理了海外各部门的报告然后再将其转发给雅典的总部。
