beloved
bɪlʌvɪd bɪlʌvd bɪˈlʌvɪd bɪˈlʌvd
When the adjective is not followed by a noun it is pronounced /bɪ'lʌvd/ and is hyphenated be|loved. 该形容词后面没有接名词时读作/bɪ'lʌvd/，分音节形式为be|loved。
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n, Also v-link ADJ of/by n] 深爱的；钟爱的 A beloved person, thing, or place is one that you feel great affection for. 深爱的；钟爱的

  • He lost his beloved wife last year.

    他去年痛失爱妻。

  • The rose is the most romantic of flowers, beloved of poets, singers, and artists.

    玫瑰是最浪漫的花，深受诗人、歌手和艺术家的喜爱。

SYN dear, loved, valued, prized  
2 N-SING [usually poss NOUN] 爱人；心爱的人 Your beloved is the person that you love. 爱人；心爱的人 [old-fashioned]

  • He takes his beloved into his arms.

    他将心爱之人拥入怀中。

SYN darling, love, sweet, lover
