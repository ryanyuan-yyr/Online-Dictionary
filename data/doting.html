doting
doʊtɪŋ ˈdəʊtɪŋ ˈdoʊtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 溺爱的;宠爱的 If you say that someone is, for example, a doting mother, husband, or friend, you mean that they show a lot of love for someone. 溺爱的;宠爱的

  • His doting parents bought him his first racing bike at 13.

    宠爱他的父母在他13岁时就给他买了第一辆竞速自行车。

SYN adoring, devoted, fond, foolish
