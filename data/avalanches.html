avalanche
ævəlɑːntʃ -læntʃ ˈævəˌlɑːntʃ ˈævəˌlæntʃ
Word forms: plural avalanches
1 N-COUNT 雪崩 An avalanche is a large mass of snow that falls down the side of a mountain. 雪崩
2 N-SING 纷至沓来；蜂拥而至 You can refer to a very large quantity of things that all arrive or happen at the same time as an avalanche of them. 纷至沓来；蜂拥而至

  • The newcomer was greeted with an avalanche of publicity.

    新来者受到了广泛的关注。
