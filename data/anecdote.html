anecdote
ænɪkdoʊt ˈænɪkˌdoʊt ˈænɛkˌdoʊt
Word forms: plural anecdotes
1 N-VAR 轶事；趣闻 An anecdote is a short, amusing account of something that has happened. 轶事；趣闻

  • Pete was telling them an anecdote about their mother.

    皮特正在给他们讲一件他们母亲的趣闻。

  • He has a talent for recollection and anecdote.

    他在记忆力和讲述趣闻轶事方面颇具天赋。

SYN story, tale, sketch, short story  
2 N-VAR （无根据的）传闻 Anecdotes are individual accounts of something that are not reliable evidence. （无根据的）传闻

  • The image of the fox as a pest is grossly exaggerated in anecdote and folklore.

    狐狸的害人精形象在传闻和民间故事中遭到严重的夸大。
