lust after
(also lust for)
1 PHRASAL VERB 对…充满性欲；渴望与…做爱 If you lust after someone or lust for them, you feel a very strong sexual desire for them. 对…充满性欲；渴望与…做爱

  • From what I hear, half the campus is lusting after her. [VERB PREP. noun]

    从我听到的来看，半个学校的人都想和她上床。

2 PHRASAL VERB 渴望；渴求 If you lust after or lust for something, you have a very strong desire to possess it. 渴望；渴求

  • Sheard lusted after the Directorship. [VERB PREP. noun]

    希尔德一心想当董事。
