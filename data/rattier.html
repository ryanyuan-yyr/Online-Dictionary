ratty
ræti ˈræti
Word forms: comparative rattier, superlative rattiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 易怒的；火气大的 If someone is ratty, they get angry and irritated easily. 易怒的；火气大的 [British, informal]

  • I had spent too many hours there and was beginning to get a bit ratty and fed up.

    我在那儿呆了很长时间，开始有点恼火和不耐烦了。

SYN irritable, cross, angry, annoyed  
2 ADJ-GRADED 破旧的；破烂的 Ratty clothes and objects are torn or in bad condition, especially because they are old. 破旧的；破烂的 [US]

  • ...my ratty old flannel pyjamas.

    我那破旧的法兰绒睡衣
