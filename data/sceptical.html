sceptical
skeptɪkəl ˈskɛptɪkəl
in AM, use 美国英语用 skeptical
ADJ-GRADED 怀疑的 If you are sceptical about something, you have doubts about it. 怀疑的

  • Other archaeologists are sceptical about his findings. [+ about]

    其他考古学家对他的发现表示怀疑。

  • ...scientists who are sceptical of global warming and its alleged consequences.

    对全球变暖和各种断言的后果持怀疑态度的科学家

  • The party has always had a cautious and sceptical attitude to Europe.

    该党一直对欧洲持有谨慎而怀疑的态度。

SYN doubtful, cynical, dubious, questioning  
sceptically

  • I looked at him skeptically, sure he was exaggerating.

    我怀疑地看着他，确信他在夸大其词。

  • 'What's your point?' demanded the old man sceptically.

    “你想说什么？”老人疑惑地问道。
