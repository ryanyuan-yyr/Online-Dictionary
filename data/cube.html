cube
kjuːb ˈkjuːbeɪ ˈkjubeɪ kjub ˈkubeɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cubes, present participle cubing, past tense , past participle cubed
1 N-COUNT 立方体;立方形的东西 A cube is a solid object with six square surfaces which are all the same size. 立方体;立方形的东西

  • ...cold water with ice cubes in it.

    放了冰块的冷水

  • ...a box of sugar cubes.

    一盒方糖

  • The cabinet comes with locks and key and is shaped like a cube.

    储藏柜带有锁和钥匙，形状像个立方体。

2 VERB 把(食物)切成小方块 When you cube food, you cut it into cube-shaped pieces. 把(食物)切成小方块

  • Remove the seeds and stones and cube the flesh. [VERB noun]

    把籽和果核弄掉，将果肉切成小方块。

  • Serve with cubed bread. [VERB-ed]

    和小面包块一起食用。

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 立方;三次幂 The cube of a number is another number that is produced by multiplying the first number by itself twice. For example, the cube of 2 is 8. 立方;三次幂
