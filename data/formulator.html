formulate
fɔːʳmjʊleɪt ˈfɔrmjuˌleɪt ˈfɔrmjəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense formulates, present participle formulating, past tense , past participle formulated
1 VERB 规划;策划;构想 If you formulate something such as a plan or proposal, you invent it, thinking about the details carefully. 规划;策划;构想

  • Little by little, he formulated his plan for escape. [VERB noun]

    他一点一点地设计构思出了逃跑的方案。

SYN devise, plan, develop, prepare  
2 VERB 明确表达;系统阐述 If you formulate a thought, opinion, or idea, you express it or describe it using particular words. 明确表达;系统阐述

  • I was impressed by the way he could formulate his ideas. [VERB noun]

    他陈述观点的方式让我印象深刻。

SYN express, detail, frame, define
