told
toʊld toʊld
1 Told is the past tense and past participle of tell .
2 PHRASE 总共;合计;总之 You can use all told to introduce or follow a summary, general statement, or total. 总共;合计;总之

  • All told there were 104 people on the payroll.

    在职的总共有 104 人。

  • All told, it seems like an awful mess…

    总之似乎一团糟。

  • The cost of the immunizations was about $600,000 all told.

    接种疫苗的成本合计约 60 万美元。
