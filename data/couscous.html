couscous
kuːskuːs ˌkuːskuːˈsuː ˈkusˌkus kusˈkus
N-UNCOUNT (北非的)蒸粗麦粉食物，含有蒸粗麦粉的饭菜 Couscous is a type of food that is made from crushed steamed wheat, or a dish consisting of this food served with a spicy stew. It is traditionally eaten in North Africa. (北非的)蒸粗麦粉食物，含有蒸粗麦粉的饭菜
