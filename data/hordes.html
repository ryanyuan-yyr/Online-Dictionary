horde
hɔːʳd hɔrd
Word forms: plural hordes
N-COUNT （熙攘、纷扰的）一大帮人，一大伙人 If you describe a crowd of people as a horde, you mean that the crowd is very large and excited and, often, rather frightening or unpleasant. （熙攘、纷扰的）一大帮人，一大伙人

  • This attracts hordes of tourists to Las Vegas. [+ of]

    这吸引了成群结队的游客到拉斯韦加斯来。

  • ...a horde of people was screaming for tickets.

    一大群人在嚷着要票。

SYN crowd, mob, swarm, press
