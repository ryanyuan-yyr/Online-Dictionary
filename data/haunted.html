haunted
hɔːntɪd ˈhɔntɪd
1 ADJ （房屋等）有鬼魂出没的，闹鬼的 A haunted building or other place is one where a ghost regularly appears. （房屋等）有鬼魂出没的，闹鬼的

  • Tracy said the cabin was haunted.

    特蕾西说小木屋闹鬼。

  • ...a haunted house.

    闹鬼的房子

2 ADJ-GRADED （表情）焦虑的，忧心忡忡的 Someone who has a haunted expression looks very worried or troubled. （表情）焦虑的，忧心忡忡的

  • She looked so haunted, I almost didn't recognize her.

    她愁容满面，我差点儿没认出她来。

SYN preoccupied, worried, troubled, plagued
