duck out
PHRASAL VERB 逃避;推脱;回避 If you duck out of something that you are supposed to do, you avoid doing it. 逃避;推脱;回避 [informal]

  • George ducked out of his forced marriage to a cousin. [VERB PREP. + of]

    乔治逃脱了和他表亲的包办婚姻。

  • You can't duck out once you've taken on a responsibility. [VERB PREP.]

    一旦承担起责任你就不能逃避。
