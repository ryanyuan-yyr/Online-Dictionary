discard
dɪskɑːʳd dɪsˈkɑrd ˈdɪsˌkɑrd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense discards, present participle discarding, past tense , past participle discarded
VERB 丢弃;抛弃 If you discard something, you get rid of it because you no longer want it or need it. 丢弃;抛弃

  • Read the manufacturer's guidelines before discarding the box. [VERB noun]

    在丢掉盒子前先看一下制造商的使用说明。

  • ...discarded cigarette butts. [VERB-ed]

    丢弃的烟头

SYN get rid of, drop, remove, throw away or out
