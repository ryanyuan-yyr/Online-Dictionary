reset
riːset riˈsɛt ˈriˌsɛt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense resets, present participle resetting
The form reset is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. reset的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
1 VERB 重新设置;重新调试 If you reset a machine or device, you adjust or set it, so that it is ready to work again or ready to perform a particular function. 重新设置;重新调试

  • As soon as you arrive at your destination, step out of the aircraft and reset your wristwatch.

    你一到达目的地，就走出飞机并重新设置手表时间。

2 VERB (骨头)复位，重新接上 If a doctor resets a broken bone, they put it back into its correct position. (骨头)复位，重新接上

  • He is recovering from an operation to reset his arm. [VERB noun]

    他做了一个手臂复位手术，正在恢复。
