schizoid
skɪtsɔɪd ˈskɪtˌsɔɪd ˈskɪzˌɔɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED (人)自相矛盾的，前后不一致的，反复无常的 If you describe someone as schizoid, you mean that they seem to have very different opinions and purposes at different times. (人)自相矛盾的，前后不一致的，反复无常的 [offensive, disapproval]

  • ...a rather schizoid fellow.

    一个相当反复无常的家伙

2 ADJ 患精神分裂症的 Someone who is schizoid suffers from schizophrenia. 患精神分裂症的

  • ...a schizoid personality.

    精神分裂型人格
