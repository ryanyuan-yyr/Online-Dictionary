knob
nɒb nɑb
Word forms: plural knobs
1 N-COUNT （门或抽屉的）球形把手 A knob is a round handle on a door or drawer which you use in order to open or close it. （门或抽屉的）球形把手

  • He turned the knob and pushed against the door.

    他扭动把手推了推门。

SYN handle, grip, door handle, handgrip  
2 N-COUNT （柱子或棍子顶部的）疙瘩，节，球状物 A knob is a rounded lump or ball on top of a post or stick. （柱子或棍子顶部的）疙瘩，节，球状物

  • A loose brass knob on the bedstead rattled.

    床架上一个松动的球形铜扣发出嘎啦声。

SYN ball, stud, nub, protuberance  
3 N-COUNT （机器或设备的）旋钮 A knob is a round switch on a piece of machinery or equipment. （机器或设备的）旋钮

  • ...the volume knob.

    音量旋钮

SYN dial, switch, button, control switch  
4 N-COUNT 小块（黄油） A knob of butter is a small amount of it. 小块（黄油） [mainly British]

  • Top the steaming hot potatoes with a knob of butter. [+ of]

    在冒热气的马铃薯上放一小块黄油。
