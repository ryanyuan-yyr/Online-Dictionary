firm up
1 PHRASAL VERB 使变结实; 变结实If you firm up something or if it firms up, it becomes firmer and more solid. 使变结实; 变结实

  • This treatment helps tone the body, firm up muscles and tighten the skin. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这种治疗可以帮助保持体态，使肌肉变结实、皮肤紧致。

SYN tone, shape up, get in shape, get into condition  
2 PHRASAL VERB 使变明确; 变明确If you firm something up or if it firms up, it becomes clearer, stronger, or more definite. 使变明确; 变明确 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We can give you more detail as our plans firm up.

    等我们的计划明确时，我们可以给你更多细节。

SYN confirm, establish, settle, fix
