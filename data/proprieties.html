proprieties
prəpraɪɪtiz prəˈpraɪɪtɪz
N-PLURAL 礼节；行为的规矩；礼貌 The proprieties are the standards of social behaviour which most people consider socially or morally acceptable. 礼节；行为的规矩；礼貌 [old-fashioned]
