outer
aʊtəʳ ˈaʊtər
ADJ [ADJ n] 外面的；外部的；远离中心的 The outer parts of something are the parts which contain or enclose the other parts, and which are furthest from the centre. 外面的；外部的；远离中心的

  • He heard a voice in the outer room.

    他听到外屋有人说话。

  • ...the outer suburbs of the city.

    城市远郊住宅区

SYN outlying, remote, distant, provincial
