tripe
traɪp traɪp
1 N-UNCOUNT （可食用的）猪肚，牛肚，百叶 Tripe is the stomach of a pig, cow, or ox which is eaten as food. （可食用的）猪肚，牛肚，百叶
2 N-UNCOUNT 蠢话；废话;无聊文字 You refer to something that someone has said or written as tripe when you think that it is silly and worthless. 蠢话；废话;无聊文字 [informal]

  • I've never heard such a load of tripe in all my life.

    我一辈子还从没听到过这样一堆废话。

SYN nonsense, rot [informal] , trash, twaddle
