consecutive
kənsekjʊtɪv kənˈsɛkjutɪv kənˈsɛkjətɪv
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 连续的;不间断的 Consecutive periods of time or events happen one after the other without interruption. 连续的;不间断的

  • The Cup was won for the third consecutive year by the Toronto Maple Leafs.

    多伦多枫叶队连续第三年捧杯。

  • Photographs taken at the same time on two consecutive sunny days can be quite different from one another.

    在连续两个艳阳天的同一时间拍摄的照片也可能大不相同。

SYN successive, running, following, succeeding  
consecutively

  • ...a CD player which plays six CDs consecutively.

    可以连续播放6张CD的CD播放机
