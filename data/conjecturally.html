conjectural
kəndʒektʃərəl kənˈdʒɛktʃərəl
ADJ-GRADED 凭推测的;凭猜测的 A statement that is conjectural is based on information that is not certain or complete. 凭推测的;凭猜测的 [formal]

  • There is something undeniably conjectural about such claims.

    这类声明中有些东西绝对是凭空臆测。

SYN speculative, theoretical, tentative, hypothetical
