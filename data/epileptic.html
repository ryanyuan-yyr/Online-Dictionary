epileptic
epɪleptɪk ˌɛpəˈlɛptɪk
Word forms: plural epileptics
1 ADJ-GRADED 癫痫的;患有癫痫的 Someone who is epileptic suffers from epilepsy. 癫痫的;患有癫痫的

  • He was epileptic and refused to take medication for his condition.

    他患有癫痫，但是拒绝服药治疗。

An epileptic is someone who is epileptic.癫痫患者

  • His wife is an epileptic.

    他妻子是个癫痫患者。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 由癫痫引发的 An epileptic fit is caused by epilepsy. 由癫痫引发的

  • He suffered an epileptic fit.

    他癫痫发作了。

  • Paula had never seen an epileptic seizure.

    葆拉从没见过有人癫痫发作。
