deface
dɪfeɪs diˈfeɪs dɪˈfeɪs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense defaces, present participle defacing, past tense , past participle defaced
VERB 损伤…的外观;在…上乱涂乱画 If someone defaces something such as a wall or a notice, they spoil it by writing or drawing things on it. 损伤…的外观;在…上乱涂乱画

  • It's illegal to deface banknotes. [VERB noun]

    在钞票上乱涂乱画是违法的。

SYN vandalize, damage, destroy, total [slang]
