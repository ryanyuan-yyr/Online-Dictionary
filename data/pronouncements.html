pronouncement
prənaʊnsmənt prəˈnaʊnsmənt proʊˈnaʊnsmənt
Word forms: plural pronouncements
N-COUNT [usually plural] 声明；公告 Pronouncements are public or official statements on an important subject. 声明；公告

  • ...the President's latest pronouncements about the protection of minorities.

    总统最近发布的保护少数民族的声明

SYN announcement, statement, declaration, judgment
