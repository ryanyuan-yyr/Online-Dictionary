premeditation
priːmedɪteɪʃən prɪˌmɛdɪˈteɪʃən priˌmɛdəˈteɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 预谋；预先策划 Premeditation is thinking about something or planning it before you actually do it. 预谋；预先策划 [formal]

  • The judge finally concluded there was insufficient evidence of premeditation.

    法官最终作出判定，没有足够证据证明那是有预谋的。

SYN planning, design, purpose, plotting
