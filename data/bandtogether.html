band together
PHRASAL VERB 联合；联手 If people band together, they meet and act as a group in order to try and achieve something. 联合；联手

  • Women banded together to protect each other. [VERB PREP.]

    妇女团结一致互相保护。

  • They banded together in their own small communities. [VERB PREP.]

    他们在各自的小社区中抱成一团。

SYN unite, group, join, league
