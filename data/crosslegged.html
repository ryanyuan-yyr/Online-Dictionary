cross-legged
ˈkrɒsˈlɛɡɪd krɔsˌlɛgɪd ˈkrɔsˌlɛgd
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 盘腿地 If someone is sitting cross-legged, they are sitting on the floor with their legs bent so that their knees point outwards. 盘腿地

  • He sat cross-legged on the floor.

    他盘腿坐在地上。

Cross-legged is also an adjective.

  • The cross-legged deity exudes wisdom and composure.

    盘腿而坐的神像散发出智慧与沉静的气息。
