confluence
kɒnfluəns ˈkɒnflʌks ˈkɑnfluəns
Word forms: plural confluences
1 N-SING (河流的)汇合处，交汇处 The confluence of two rivers is the place where they join and become one larger river. (河流的)汇合处，交汇处

  • The 160-metre falls mark the dramatic confluence of the rivers Nera and Velino. [+ of]

    内拉河和韦利诺河的汇合处是落差达160米的瀑布，景象十分壮观。

SYN convergence, junction, concurrence, conflux  
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n] 汇集;汇合 If there is a confluence of two things, they join, combine, or come together. 汇集;汇合 [formal]

  • Like most cases of extreme weather, its severity was due to an unusual confluence of events.

    如同大多数极端天气状况一样，其剧烈程度是由于几种气象条件反常地汇集在一起造成的。

  • ...the confluence of African and Portuguese cultures in Brazil.

    非洲文化与葡萄牙文化在巴西的汇合
