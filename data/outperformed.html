outperform
aʊtpəʳfɔːʳm ˌaʊtpəˈfɔːm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outperforms, present participle outperforming, past tense , past participle outperformed
VERB 做得比…好；比…更高效 If one thing outperforms another, the first is more successful or efficient than the second. 做得比…好；比…更高效 [journalism]

  • In recent years the Austrian economy has outperformed most other industrial economies. [VERB noun]

    近几年，奥地利的经济发展超过了其他多数工业国。

  • It was yet another case where the human eye outperformed radar. [VERB noun]

    这是肉眼胜过雷达的又一事例。

SYN surpass, top, eclipse, outstrip
