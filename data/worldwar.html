world war
Word forms: plural world wars
N-VAR 世界大战 A world war is a war that involves countries all over the world. 世界大战

  • Many senior citizens have been through two world wars.

    很多年长的公民都经历了两次世界大战。

  • At the end of the second world war he was working as a docker.

    第二次世界大战结束时，他是一名码头工人。

  • There is a risk of world war.

    有爆发世界大战的危险。
