beholder
bɪhoʊldəʳ
Word forms: plural beholders
1 PHRASE 个人观点；个人看法 If you say that something such as beauty or art is in the eye of the beholder, you mean that it is a matter of personal opinion. 个人观点；个人看法

  • Beauty is in the eye of the beholder.

    情人眼里出西施。

2 N-COUNT 观看者；旁观者 The beholder of something is the person who is looking at it. 观看者；旁观者 [old-fashioned]

  • The caves cannot but inspire wonder in the beholder.

    这些洞穴让观者无不叹为观止。
