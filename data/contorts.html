contort
kəntɔːʳt kənˈtɔrt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense contorts, present participle contorting, past tense , past participle contorted
V-ERG (面部、躯体等)扭曲，歪曲 If someone's face or body contorts or is contorted, it moves into an unnatural and unattractive shape or position. (面部、躯体等)扭曲，歪曲

  • His face contorts as he screams out the lyrics. [VERB]

    当他吼出这些歌词的时候，脸都扭曲了。

  • The gentlest of her caresses would contort his already tense body. [VERB noun]

    她最温柔的爱抚会让他已然紧绷的身体变得扭曲。

  • Brenner was breathing hard, his face contorted with pain. [VERB-ed]

    勃伦纳艰难地呼吸着，面部因为痛苦而变得扭曲。

  • ...their contorted bodies. [VERB-ed]

    他们扭曲的身体

SYN twist, knot, distort, warp
