fitted
fɪtɪd ˈfɪtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 紧身的;收身的;合身的 A fitted piece of clothing is designed so that it is the same size and shape as your body rather than being loose. 紧身的;收身的;合身的

  • ...baggy trousers with fitted jackets.

    宽松的裤子配上紧身夹克

SYN tailored, shaped  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (家具)定做的，配制的 A fitted piece of furniture, for example a cupboard, is designed to fill a particular space and is fixed in place. (家具)定做的，配制的

  • I've re-carpeted our bedroom and added fitted wardrobes.

    我重铺了卧室的地毯，又添置了定做的壁橱。

SYN built-in, permanent  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (地毯)铺满房间的，定做的，大小合适的 A fitted carpet is cut to the same shape as a room so that it covers the floor completely. (地毯)铺满房间的，定做的，大小合适的

  • ...fitted carpets, central heating and double glazing.

    定制的地毯、中央供暖和双层玻璃窗

4 ADJ [ADJ n] (床单)大小合适的，按尺寸缝边的 A fitted sheet has the corners sewn so that they fit over the corners of the mattress and do not have to be folded. (床单)大小合适的，按尺寸缝边的
5 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ to-infinitive] 合适的;有能力的;胜任的 A person who is fitted to do something or fitted for something has the right qualities for it. 合适的;有能力的;胜任的

  • Helen was ill fitted to fulfil her daughter's ideal of a gentle mother-figure.

    海伦与她女儿理想中的温柔母亲形象相差甚远。

SYN suited  
6 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n, ADJ n] (房间)配有，装有 If a room is fitted with objects, those objects are in the room and are normally fixed in place. (房间)配有，装有

  • Bedrooms are fitted with alarm pull cords to alert the manager in an emergency.

    卧室中装有警报器拉绳，遇到紧急情况时可向经理报警。

SYN equipped, provided, supplied, set up
