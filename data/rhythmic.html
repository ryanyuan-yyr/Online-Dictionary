rhythmic
rɪðmɪk rɪðmɪkəl
(also rhythmical)
ADJ-GRADED 有节奏的;有韵律的 A rhythmic movement or sound is repeated at regular intervals, forming a regular pattern or beat. 有节奏的;有韵律的

  • Good breathing is slow, rhythmic and deep.

    健康的呼吸方式缓慢深沉而有节奏。

  • ...the rhythmical beat of the drum.

    有节奏的鼓声

SYN cadenced, throbbing, periodic, pulsating  
rhythmically

  • She stood, swaying her hips, moving rhythmically.

    她站在那里，摇摆着臀部，有节奏地晃动着。
