delinquent
dɪlɪŋkwənt dɪˈlɪŋkwənt
Word forms: plural delinquents
1 ADJ (通常指青少年)常有违法行为的，屡犯轻罪的 Someone, usually a young person, who is delinquent repeatedly commits minor crimes. (通常指青少年)常有违法行为的，屡犯轻罪的

  • ...remand homes for delinquent children.

    少年犯拘留所

SYN unruly, wild, rebellious, anarchic  
Delinquent is also a noun.

  • ...a nine-year-old delinquent.

    九岁的少年犯

2See also:juvenile delinquent 
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 拖欠债务的;未缴税款的 A delinquent borrower or taxpayer is someone who has failed to pay their debts or taxes. 拖欠债务的;未缴税款的 [US, technical]

  • ...a delinquent borrower.

    欠债未还的借款者

SYN nonpaying
