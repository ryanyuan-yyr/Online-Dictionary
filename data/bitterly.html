bitterly
bɪtəʳli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective] 强烈地，激烈地，极为（愤恨、憎恶等） You use bitterly when you are describing an attitude which involves strong, unpleasant emotions such as anger or dislike. 强烈地，激烈地，极为（愤恨、憎恶等）

  • We are bitterly upset at what has happened.

    发生的事情让我们极为心烦。

  • ...Lucy Gannon's fine, bitterly funny play.

    露西·甘农令人捧腹又倍感心酸的优秀剧本
