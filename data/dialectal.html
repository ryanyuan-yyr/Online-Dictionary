dialect
daɪəlekt ˈdaɪəˌlɛkt
Word forms: plural dialects
N-COUNT [oft in NOUN] 方言;地方话;土话 A dialect is a form of a language that is spoken in a particular area. 方言;地方话;土话

  • In the fifties, many Italians spoke only local dialect.

    在 20 世纪 50 年代，许多意大利人只会说当地方言。

  • They began to speak rapidly in dialect.

    他们开始叽里呱啦地说起地方话来。

SYN language, speech, tongue, jargon
