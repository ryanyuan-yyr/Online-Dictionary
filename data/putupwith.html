put up with
PHRASAL VERB 忍受；容忍 If you put up with something, you tolerate or accept it, even though you find it unpleasant or unsatisfactory. 忍受；容忍

  • They had put up with behaviour from their son which they would not have tolerated from anyone else. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他们容忍了儿子的行为，如果换作别人他们可做不到。
