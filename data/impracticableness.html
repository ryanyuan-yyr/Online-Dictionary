impracticable
ɪmpræktɪkəbəl ɪmˈpræktɪkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 不能实行的;行不通的 If something such as a course of action is impracticable, it is impossible to do. 不能实行的;行不通的

  • Such measures would be highly impracticable and almost impossible to apply.

    这样的措施非常不切实际,几乎不可能付诸实施。

SYN unfeasible, impossible, out of the question, unworkable
