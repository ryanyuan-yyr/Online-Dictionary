world view
wɜːld vjuː
(also world-view)Word forms: plural world views
N-COUNT 世界观 A person's world view is the way they see and understand the world, especially regarding issues such as politics, philosophy, and religion. 世界观

  • ...their Christian world view.

    他们的基督教世界观

  • Many artists express their world view in their work.

    很多艺术家通过作品表达自己对世界的看法。
