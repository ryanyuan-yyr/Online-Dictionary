acutely
əkjuːtli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective, ADVERB with verb] 深深地（感觉到），强烈地(意识到) If you feel or notice something acutely, you feel or notice it very strongly. 深深地（感觉到），强烈地(意识到)

  • He was acutely aware of the odour of cooking oil.

    他闻到一股浓浓的油烟味。

  • Those tensions have been felt most acutely by women.

    最能深切感受到这种紧张氛围的是女性。

SYN painfully, clearly, markedly, excessively  
2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective, ADVERB with verb] （感觉、性质）极其 If a feeling or quality is acutely unpleasant, it is extremely unpleasant. （感觉、性质）极其

  • It was an acutely uncomfortable journey back to London.

    那是一次极其痛苦的返回伦敦的旅行。

SYN very, highly, greatly, severely
