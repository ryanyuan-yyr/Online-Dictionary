pore
pɔːʳ pɔr poʊr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pores, present participle poring, past tense , past participle pored
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] （皮肤上的）毛孔 Your pores are the tiny holes in your skin. （皮肤上的）毛孔

  • The size of your pores is determined by the amount of oil they produce.

    毛孔大小取决于其出油多少。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （植物表面的）气孔 The pores of a plant are the tiny holes on its surface. （植物表面的）气孔

  • A plant's lungs are the microscopic pores in its leaves.

    植物的肺就是其叶片上微细的气孔。

SYN opening, hole, outlet, orifice  
3 VERB 仔细阅读；认真钻研 If you pore over or through information, you look at it and study it very carefully. 仔细阅读；认真钻研

  • We spent hours poring over travel brochures. [V + over/through]

    我们花了数小时仔细阅读旅游宣传册。

  • It will take several more months to pore through the volumes of documents. [V over/through n]

    详细研究这大堆的文献还要多花好几个月的时间。

SYN study, read, examine, go over  
4 PHRASE 极度；非常 You can say that someone has a certain quality or emotion coming from every pore to emphasize the strength of that quality or emotion. 极度；非常 [emphasis]

  • She oozes sexuality from every pore.

    她浑身上下每个毛孔都散发着性感。

  • Waves of misery penetrated every pore.

    痛苦深彻骨髓。
