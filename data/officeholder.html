office-holder
(also office holder)Word forms: plural office-holders
N-COUNT （机构或政府中的）任要职者，高官 An office-holder is a person who has an important official position in an organization or government. （机构或政府中的）任要职者，高官

    他们在选举中似乎不愿选身居高位的人。
