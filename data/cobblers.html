cobbler
kɒbləʳ ˈkɑblər
Word forms: plural cobblers
1 N-COUNT 鞋匠 A cobbler is a person whose job is to make or mend shoes. 鞋匠 [old-fashioned]
2 N-UNCOUNT 胡说;废话 If you describe something that someone has just said as cobblers, you mean that you think it is nonsense. 胡说;废话 [British, informal]

  • The idea that greens are good for you is a load of cobblers.

    青菜对人体有益的说法是一派胡言。

  • These guys talk an awful load of old cobblers.

    这些人纯粹是胡说八道。
