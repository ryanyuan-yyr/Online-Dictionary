colossal
kəlɒsəl kəˈlɑsəl
ADJ-GRADED 巨大的;庞大的 If you describe something as colossal, you are emphasizing that it is very large. 巨大的;庞大的 [emphasis]

  • There has been a colossal waste of public money.

    一直存在巨大的公款浪费。

  • The task they face is colossal.

    他们面临的任务十分艰巨。

SYN huge, massive, vast, enormous  
colossally

  • Their policies have been colossally destructive.

    他们的政策极具破坏性。
