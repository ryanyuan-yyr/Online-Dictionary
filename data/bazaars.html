bazaar
bəzɑːʳ bəˈzɑr
Word forms: plural bazaars
1 N-COUNT 集市；市场 In areas such as the Middle East and India, a bazaar is a place where there are many small shops and stalls. 集市；市场

  • Kamal was a vendor in Egypt's open-air bazaar.

    卡迈勒是埃及露天集市里的一个小贩。

SYN market, exchange, fair, marketplace  
2 N-COUNT 义卖 A bazaar is a sale to raise money for charity. 义卖

  • ...a church bazaar.

    教堂义卖

SYN fair, fête, gala, festival
