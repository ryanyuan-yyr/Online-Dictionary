smoking
smoʊkɪŋ ˈsməʊkɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 吸烟;抽烟 Smoking is the act or habit of smoking cigarettes, cigars, or a pipe. 吸烟;抽烟

  • Smoking is now banned in many places of work.

    现在许多工作场所禁止吸烟。

  • ...a no-smoking area.

    禁烟区

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 允许吸烟的 A smoking area is intended for people who want to smoke. 允许吸烟的

  • ...the decision to scrap smoking compartments on Kent trains.

    取消肯特列车上的吸烟车厢的决定

3 See also: smoke  passive smoking
