tenement
tenəmənt ˈtɛnəmənt ˈtɛnəmənt
Word forms: plural tenements
1 N-COUNT (大而旧的)公寓大楼 A tenement is a large, old building which is divided into a number of individual flats. (大而旧的)公寓大楼

  • ...elegant 19th century tenement buildings.

    几幢典雅的 19 世纪公寓大楼

2 N-COUNT (公寓大楼里的)公寓，房间 A tenement is one of the flats in a tenement. (公寓大楼里的)公寓，房间

  • ...the cramped Edinburgh tenement in which Connery grew up.

    康纳利小时候居住的位于爱丁堡的狭小公寓
