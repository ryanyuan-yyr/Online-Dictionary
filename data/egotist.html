egotist
iːgətɪst eg- ˈiːɡətɪst ˈigoʊtɪst ˈɛgoʊtɪst ˈigətɪst ˈɛgətɪst
Word forms: plural egotists
N-COUNT 自我中心者;自私自利者;自我主义者 An egotist is someone who is egotistic. 自我中心者;自私自利者;自我主义者 [disapproval]
SYN egomaniac, boaster, swaggerer, self-seeker  
Quotations
