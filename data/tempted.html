tempted temptɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ, usually ADJ to-infinitive] 想做…的;希望做的 If you say that you are tempted to do something, you mean that you would like to do it. 想做…的;希望做的

  • I'm very tempted to sell my house.

    我很想把我的房子卖掉。

  • She'd never even felt tempted to return.

    她甚至从未动过回来的念头。
