buckle down
PHRASAL VERB 开始认真做;全力以赴 If you buckle down to something, you start working seriously at it. 开始认真做;全力以赴 [informal]

  • He has buckled down to work in the reserves. [V P to n]

    他已经安心在预备役部队服役。

  • I just buckled down and got on with playing. [VERB PREP.]

    我干脆静下心来继续玩。

SYN apply yourself, set to, fall to, pitch in
