regionalism
riːdʒənəlɪzəm ˈridʒənəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 区域主义；地区主义；地方分权主义 Regionalism is a strong feeling of pride or loyalty that people in a region have for that region, often including a desire to govern themselves. 区域主义；地区主义；地方分权主义

  • A grass-roots regionalism appears to be emerging.

    基层地方主义开始露头。
