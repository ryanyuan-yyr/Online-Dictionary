frailty
freɪlti ˈfreɪlti
Word forms: plural frailties
1 N-VAR 弱点;脆弱 If you refer to the frailties or frailty of people, you are referring to their weaknesses. 弱点;脆弱

  • ...the frailties of human nature. [+ of]

    人性的弱点

  • ...a triumph of will over human frailty.

    意志战胜人性弱点

SYN weakness, susceptibility, fallibility, peccability  
2 N-UNCOUNT (体质的)虚弱 Frailty is the condition of having poor health. (体质的)虚弱

  • She died after a long period of increasing frailty.

    在很长一段时间里，她身体日渐虚弱，最后离开人世。

SYN infirmity, poor health, feebleness, puniness
