plutocrat
pluːtəkræt ˈpluːtəˌkræt ˈplutəˌkræt
Word forms: plural plutocrats
N-COUNT 富豪；财阀 If you describe someone as a plutocrat, you disapprove of them because you believe they are powerful only because they are rich. 富豪；财阀 [formal, disapproval]

  • He proclaimed his fellow-feeling with workers, and denounced plutocrats and the idle rich in terms which caused them alarm.

    他声明了自己对工人的同情，并谴责了财阀和游手好闲的富人，其言辞之厉令这些人惶惶不安。

SYN rich man, millionaire, capitalist, tycoon
