noisome
nɔɪsəm ˈnɔɪsəm
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 令人不愉快的;令人讨厌的 If you describe something or someone as noisome, you mean that you find them extremely unpleasant. 令人不愉快的;令人讨厌的 [literary]

  • Noisome vapours arise from the mud left in the docks.

    难闻的蒸汽由船坞中余留的泥浆散发出来。

  • His noisome reputation for corruption had already begun to spread.

    他贪污的恶名已经四处传播开了。

SYN poisonous, bad, harmful, unhealthy
