perfumery
pəʳfjuːməri pərˈfjuməri
Word forms: plural perfumeries
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 香水业;香水生产 Perfumery is the activity or business of producing perfume. 香水业;香水生产

  • ...the perfumery trade.

    香水贸易

  • Provence is the home of perfumery.

    普罗旺斯是香水业的发源地。

2 N-COUNT 香水店；香水专柜 A perfumery is a shop or a department in a store where perfume is the main product that is sold. 香水店；香水专柜

  • ...a perfumery called Bayley's of Bond Street.

    邦德大街巴利香水店

  • ...the perfumery department.

    香水专柜
