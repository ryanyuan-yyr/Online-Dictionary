smooth-talking
ˈsmuːðˌtɔːkɪŋ
ADJ 能说会道的;巧舌如簧的 A smooth-talking man talks very confidently in a way that is likely to persuade people, but may not be sincere or honest. 能说会道的;巧舌如簧的

  • ...the smooth-talking conman who has wrecked their lives.

    这个凭借三寸不烂之舌毁掉了他们的生活的骗子

SYN slick, suave, glib, ingratiating
