reassemble
riːəsembəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reassembles, present participle reassembling, past tense , past participle reassembled
1 VERB 重组；重新整合；重新装配 If you reassemble something, you put it back together after it has been taken apart. 重组；重新整合；重新装配

  • We will now try to reassemble pieces of the wreckage. [VERB noun]

    我们现在会尽量把残骸重新组合起来。

  • The table had to be taken apart and hoisted through the window before it was reassembled. [VERB noun]

    桌子得被拆开，通过窗户吊进来，然后再重新组装。

2 V-ERG 重新召集；重新集合 If a group of people reassembles or if you reassemble them, they gather together again in a group. 重新召集；重新集合

  • We shall reassemble in the car park in thirty minutes. [VERB]

    我们30分钟后在停车场再碰面。

  • Mr Lucas reassembled his team in September. [VERB noun]

    卢卡斯先生在9月重新召集了他的队伍。
