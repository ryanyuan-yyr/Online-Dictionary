immortalize
ɪmɔːʳtəlaɪz ɪˈmɔrtəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense immortalizes, present participle immortalizing, past tense , past participle immortalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 immortalise
VERB 使不朽;使名垂千古 If someone or something is immortalized in a story, film, or work of art, they appear in it, and will be remembered for it. 使不朽;使名垂千古 [written]

  • The town of whitby was immortalised in Bram Stoker's famous Dracula story. [be VERB-ed]

    惠特比镇因为布拉姆·斯托克著名的吸血鬼故事而名传后世。

  • D H Lawrence immortalised her in his novel 'Women in Love'. D.H. [VERB noun]

    劳伦斯在小说《恋爱中的女人》中把她塑造成了一个不朽的角色。

  • ...Colditz, the grim fortress immortalised by films and TV. [VERB-ed]

    科尔迪茨，经电影电视的刻画和塑造而名扬后世的恐怖城堡

SYN commemorate, celebrate, perpetuate, glorify
