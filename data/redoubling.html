redouble
riːdʌbəl riˈdʌbəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redoubles, present participle redoubling, past tense , past participle redoubled
V-ERG 加倍；加强 If you redouble your efforts, you try much harder to achieve something. If something redoubles, it increases in volume or intensity. 加倍；加强

  • The president also called on nations to redouble their efforts to negotiate an international trade agreement. [VERB noun]

    总统还呼吁各国就签订国际贸易协定加倍努力进行磋商。

  • The applause redoubled. [VERB]

    掌声加倍热烈。
