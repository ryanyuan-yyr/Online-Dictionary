kudos
kjuːdɒs kuːdoʊz ˈkjuˌdɑs ˈkuˌdɑs ˈkjuˌdoʊs ˈkuˌdoʊs
N-UNCOUNT （因行为或成就而获得的）荣誉，赞誉，名声 Kudos is admiration or recognition that someone or something gets as a result of a particular action or achievement. （因行为或成就而获得的）荣誉，赞誉，名声

  • ...a new hotel chain that has won kudos for the way it treats guests. [+ for]

    因其待客方式受到好评的新的连锁酒店

SYN prestige, regard, honour, praise
