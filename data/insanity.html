insanity
ɪnsænɪti ɪnˈsænəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 精神错乱;精神失常;疯狂 Insanity is the state of being insane. 精神错乱;精神失常;疯狂

  • The film is a powerful study of a woman's descent into insanity.

    那部电影深入剖析了一个女人堕入疯狂的过程。

  • The defence pleaded insanity, but the defendant was found guilty and sentenced.

    辩方以精神错乱为由进行申辩,但被告被判有罪并获刑。

SYN madness, mental disorder, dementia, aberration  
2 N-UNCOUNT 极端的愚蠢(行为);荒唐(事) If you describe a decision or an action as insanity, you think it is very foolish. 极端的愚蠢(行为);荒唐(事) [disapproval]

  • It was a period of collective insanity, of legalized murder and mayhem.

    那是个集体丧失理智、谋杀与暴力合法化的时期。

  • ...the final financial insanity of the 1980s.

    20 世纪 80 年代最后的财政疯狂

SYN stupidity, folly, lunacy, irresponsibility
