format
fɔːʳmæt ˈfɔrˌmæt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense formats, present participle formatting, past tense , past participle formatted
1 N-COUNT 版式;格式;形式 The format of something is the way or order in which it is arranged and presented. 版式;格式;形式

  • I had met with him to explain the format of the programme and what we had in mind. [+ of]

    我已经和他当面解释了我们节目的形式以及我们的想法。

  • ...a large-format book.

    大开本的书

SYN arrangement, form, style, make-up  
2 N-COUNT (电脑软件或音乐的)载体形式 The format of a piece of computer software or a musical recording is the type of equipment on which it is designed to be used or played. For example, possible formats for a musical recording are CD and cassette. (电脑软件或音乐的)载体形式

  • His latest album is available on all formats.

    他的最新专辑有各种载体形式。

3 VERB 格式化(磁盘) To format a computer disk means to run a program so that the disk can be written on. 格式化(磁盘) [computing]
4 VERB 编排(计算机文本或图表)的格式 To format a piece of computer text or graphics means to arrange the way in which it appears when it is printed or is displayed on a screen. 编排(计算机文本或图表)的格式 [computing]

  • When text is saved from a Web page, it is often very badly formatted with many short lines. [be VERB-ed]

    从网页上转存文本时，往往会出现许多短行，格式也乱了。
