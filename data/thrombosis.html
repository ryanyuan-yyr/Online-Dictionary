thrombosis
θrɒmboʊsɪs θrɑmˈboʊsɪs
Word forms: plural thromboses/θrɒmboʊsiːz/
1 N-VAR 血栓形成 Thrombosis is the formation of a blood clot in a person's heart or in one of their blood vessels, which can cause death. 血栓形成 [medicine]

  • It is generally accepted that thinning of the blood reduces the chances of thrombosis.

    一般认为，稀释血液可降低血栓形成的可能性。

  • ...a lady with a thrombosis in her lung.

    肺部有血栓的女士

SYN blood clot, embolism, infarction, coronary thrombosis  
2See also:coronary thrombosis  deep vein thrombosis
