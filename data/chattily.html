chatty
tʃæti ˈtʃæti
1 ADJ-GRADED 健谈的;爱闲聊的 Someone who is chatty talks a lot in a friendly, informal way. 健谈的;爱闲聊的

  • She's quite a chatty person.

    她很健谈。

SYN talkative, informal, effusive, garrulous  
2 ADJ-GRADED (写作或说话)随意的，闲谈似的 A chatty style of writing or talking is friendly and informal. (写作或说话)随意的，闲谈似的

  • He wrote a chatty letter to his wife.

    他给妻子写了封闲话家常的信。
