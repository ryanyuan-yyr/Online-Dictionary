askew
əskjuː əˈskju
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 斜的；歪的；不平的 Something that is askew is not straight or not level with what it should be level with. 斜的；歪的；不平的

  • She stood there, hat askew.

    她站在那里，歪戴着帽子。

  • There were no shutters at the windows, and some of the doors hung askew.

    窗户上没有百叶窗，有几扇门也歪歪扭扭的。

SYN crooked, awry, oblique, lopsided
