strangled
stræŋgəld ˈstræŋɡəld
ADJ [ADJ n] (说话声、哭声)哽塞的，哽咽的，顿住的 A strangled voice or cry sounds unclear because the throat muscles of the person speaking or crying are tight. (说话声、哭声)哽塞的，哽咽的，顿住的 [literary]

  • In a strangled voice he said, 'This place is going to be unthinkable without you.'

    他哽咽地说道：“没有你这个地方会变得不可想象。”

  • Sue let out a strangled cry of shock.

    苏震惊得哽咽起来。
