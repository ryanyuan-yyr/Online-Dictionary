leak
liːk lik
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense leaks, present participle leaking, past tense , past participle leaked
1 V-ERG 漏；渗漏；泄漏 If a container leaks, there is a hole or crack in it which lets a substance such as liquid or gas escape. You can also say that a container leaks a substance such as liquid or gas. 漏；渗漏；泄漏 [Also VERB n]

  • The roof leaked. [VERB]

    屋顶漏雨。

  • The gas had apparently leaked from a cylinder. [VERB prep./adv.]

    气体似乎由一汽缸漏出。

  • The pool's fiberglass sides had cracked and the water had leaked out. [VERB prep./adv.]

    泳池四周的玻璃钢面开裂，水渗了出来。

  • A large diesel tank mysteriously leaked its contents into the river. [VERB noun + into]

    一个大型柴油罐的柴油莫名其妙地泄漏到河里去了。

SYN escape, pass, spill, release  
Leak is also a noun.

  • It's thought a gas leak may have caused the blast.

    据认为爆炸可能由气体泄漏引起。

2 N-COUNT 裂缝；漏隙；漏洞 A leak is a crack, hole, or other gap that a substance such as a liquid or gas can pass through. 裂缝；漏隙；漏洞

  • ...a leak in the radiator. [+ in]

    暖气片上的一条裂缝

  • In May engineers found a leak in a hydrogen fuel line. [+ in]

    5月份工程师们在一条氢燃料管线上发现一条裂隙。

SYN hole, opening, crack, puncture  
3 V-ERG 走漏；泄露；透露 If a secret document or piece of information leaks or is leaked, someone lets the public know about it. 走漏；泄露；透露

  • Last year, a civil servant was imprisoned for leaking a document to the press. [VERB noun + to]

    去年，一名公务员因向报界泄露了一份文件而被判入狱。

  • He revealed who leaked a confidential police report. [VERB noun]

    他说出了泄露警方机密报告内容的人。

  • We don't know how the transcript leaked. [VERB]

    我们不知道文字本是怎么泄露出去的。

  • ...a leaked report. [VERB-ed]

    走漏了风声的报告

SYN disclose, tell, reveal, pass on  
Leak is also a noun.

  • More serious leaks, possibly involving national security, are likely to be investigated by the police.

    可能涉及国家安全的更严重泄密多半会交由警方调查。

Leak out means the same as leak .leak out同leak

  • More details are now beginning to leak out.

    更多的细节正透露出来。

  • He said it would leak out to the newspapers and cause a scandal.

    他说这件事会泄露给报界，引起丑闻。
