unlovely
ʌnlʌvli ʌnˈlʌvlɪ ʌnˈlʌvli
ADJ-GRADED 不可爱的；讨人嫌的 If you describe something as unlovely, you mean that it is unattractive or unpleasant in some way. 不可爱的；讨人嫌的 [written]

  • She found a small, inexpensive motel on the outskirts of the town; it was barren and unlovely.

    她在城郊找了一家便宜的小汽车旅馆，那旅馆既简陋又难看。
