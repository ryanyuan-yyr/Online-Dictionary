foxy
fɒksi ˈfɑksi
Word forms: comparative foxier, superlative foxiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 狡猾的;滑头的;狡诈的 If you describe someone as foxy, you mean that they are deceitful in a clever, secretive way. 狡猾的;滑头的;狡诈的

  • He had wary, foxy eyes.

    他的眼睛机警而又狡诈。

SYN crafty, knowing, sharp, tricky  
2 ADJ-GRADED 迷人的;娇媚的;狐媚的 If a man calls a woman foxy, he means that she is physically attractive. 迷人的;娇媚的;狐媚的 [mainly US, informal]

  • ...a foxy blonde in a turtleneck sweater.

    一位娇媚的身穿高领羊毛衫的金发美女

SYN sexy, attractive, tempting, provoking
