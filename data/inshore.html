inshore
ɪnʃɔːʳ ɪnʃɔːʳ ˈɪnˌʃɔr ɪnˈʃɔr
The adverb is pronounced /ˌɪn'ʃɔ/. The adjective is pronounced /'ɪnʃɔ/. 副词读作/ˌɪn'ʃɔ/。形容词读作 /'ɪnʃɔ/。
ADV [be ADVERB, ADVERB after verb] 近海岸;向海岸 If something is inshore, it is in the sea but quite close to the land. If something moves inshore, it moves from the sea towards the land. 近海岸;向海岸

  • A barge was close inshore about a hundred yards away.

    一艘驳船离海岸仅约 100 码远。

  • There was still a strong wind blowing inshore.

    仍有强风吹向海岸。

Inshore is also an adjective.

  • ...inshore reefs and islands.

    近岸的礁石与岛屿
