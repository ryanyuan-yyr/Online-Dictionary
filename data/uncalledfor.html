uncalled for ʌnkɔːld fɔːʳ ʌnˈkɔːld fɔː
ADJ-GRADED 不得当的；唐突无礼的 If you describe a remark or criticism as uncalled for, you mean that it should not have been made, because it was unkind or unfair. 不得当的；唐突无礼的

  • I'm sorry. That was uncalled for.

    对不起，失言了。

  • ...Leo's uncalled-for remarks about her cousin.

    利奥关于她表姐的一些不当言辞
