freshwater
freʃwɔːtəʳ ˈfrɛʃˌwɔtər
ADJ [ADJ n] (湖)淡水的;(生物)淡水中生长的 A freshwater lake contains water that is not salty, usually in contrast to the sea. Freshwater creatures live in water that is not salty. (湖)淡水的;(生物)淡水中生长的

  • ...Lake Balaton, the largest freshwater lake in Europe.

    巴拉顿湖，欧洲最大的淡水湖

  • The perch is a freshwater fish.

    鲈鱼是淡水鱼。
