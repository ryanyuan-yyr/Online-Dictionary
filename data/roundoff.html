round off
PHRASAL VERB 使…圆满结束 If you round off an activity with something, you end the activity by doing something that provides a clear or satisfactory conclusion to it. 使…圆满结束

  • The Italian way is to round off a meal with an ice-cream. [VERB PREP. noun]

    意大利人总是在用餐结束时来份冰激凌。

  • ...a dazzling firework display which rounded off a lovely day. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    为美好的一天画上圆满句号的绚烂烟花

  • This rounded the afternoon off perfectly. [VERB noun PREP.]

    这使得下午的活动圆满结束。

  • He rounds off by proposing a toast to the attendants. [V P + by]

    他向来宾祝酒，圆满结束了活动。
