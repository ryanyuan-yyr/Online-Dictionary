intimacy
ɪntɪməsi ˈɪntəməsi
Word forms: plural intimacies
1 N-UNCOUNT 亲密;密切 Intimacy between two people is a very close personal relationship between them. 亲密;密切 [Also + between]

  • ...a means of achieving intimacy with another person. [+ with]

    与别人建立亲密关系的途径

SYN familiarity, closeness, understanding, confidence  
2 N-UNCOUNT 性;性关系 You sometimes use intimacy to refer to sex or a sexual relationship. 性;性关系

  • Her sunburn made intimacy too painful.

    她的皮肤晒伤了，使得想要亲热一下都变得很痛苦。

  • The truth was he did not feel like intimacy with any woman. [+ with]

    事实是他不想与任何女人发生性关系。

SYN sexual intercourse, sex [informal] , lovemaking, the other [informal]  
3 N-COUNT [usually plural] 亲昵的言语(或行为) Intimacies are things that you say or do to someone you have a very close personal relationship with. 亲昵的言语(或行为)

  • ...the intimacies of a love scene.

    恋爱场面的亲昵行为
