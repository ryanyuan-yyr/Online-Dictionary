kick up
1 PHRASAL VERB 开始（大吵大闹） If you kick up a fuss about something, you make it very obvious that you are annoyed or dissatisfied. 开始（大吵大闹）

  • Those customers who have kicked up a fuss have received refunds. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那些大吵大闹的顾客得到了退款。

  • They kick up a rumpus and throw things at each other. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他们吵了起来并互相朝对方扔东西。

2 PHRASAL VERB 扬起（灰尘或土） If you kick up dust or dirt, you create a cloud of dust or dirt as you move along a dusty road. 扬起（灰尘或土）

  • She shuffled along, kicking up clouds of dust. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她拖着脚走路，带起一团团灰。
