    尽管他试图寻求和平解决，但现在却面临使用更多武力的压力。

SYN answer, resolution, key, result  
2 N-COUNT 谜底;答案 The solution to a puzzle is the answer to it. 谜底;答案

  • ...the solution to crossword No. 19721. [+ to]

    第 19721 号纵横字谜的答案

3 N-COUNT 溶液 A solution is a liquid in which a solid substance has been dissolved. 溶液

  • ...a warm solution of liquid detergent.

    热洗涤剂溶液

  • Vitamins in solution are more affected than those in solid foods.

    溶液里的维生素比固体食物里的维生素受到的影响更大。

SYN mixture, mix, compound, blend
