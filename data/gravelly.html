gravelly
grævəli ˈgrævəli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （声音）沙哑低沉的 A gravelly voice is low and rather rough and harsh. （声音）沙哑低沉的

  • There's a triumphant note in his gravelly voice.

    他低沉沙哑的声音中透露出一丝洋洋得意。

SYN husky, rough, harsh, rasping  
2 ADJ-GRADED 砂砾多的；多碎石的；铺满碎石的 A gravelly area of land is covered in or full of small stones. 砂砾多的；多碎石的；铺满碎石的

  • Water runs through the gravelly soil very quickly.

    水从砂砾地上奔流而过。
