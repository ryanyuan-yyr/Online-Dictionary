touchy-feely
tʌtʃi fiːli ˈtʌtʃɪˈfiːlɪ ˈtʌtʃiˈfili
ADJ-GRADED 煽情的;矫情的;肉麻的 If you describe something as touchy-feely, you mean that it involves people expressing emotions such as love and affection openly in a way which you find embarrassing and silly. 煽情的;矫情的;肉麻的 [disapproval]

  • ...a touchy-feely song about making your life worth living.

    一支让生活更有价值的煽情歌曲
