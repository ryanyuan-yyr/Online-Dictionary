beseech
bɪsiːtʃ biˈsitʃ bɪˈsitʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense beseeches, present participle beseeching, past tense , past participle beseeched
VERB 恳求；央求；哀求 If you beseech someone to do something, you ask them very eagerly and anxiously. 恳求；央求；哀求 [literary]

  • She beseeched him to cut his drinking and his smoking. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    她哀求他戒掉烟酒。

  • 'Please stay and read to me, mummy,' he beseeched. [VERB with quote]

    “求求你留下来给我读点东西吧，妈妈，”他央求道。

SYN beg, ask, petition, call upon
