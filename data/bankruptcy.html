bankruptcy
bænkrʌptsi ˈbæŋkrʌptsɪ bæŋkˌrʌptsi bæŋkrəpsi
Word forms: plural bankruptcies
1 N-UNCOUNT 破产；倒闭 Bankruptcy is the state of being bankrupt. 破产；倒闭 [business]

  • It is the second airline in two months to file for bankruptcy.

    这是两个月内第二家申请破产的航空公司。

  • Many established firms were facing bankruptcy.

    许多成熟的企业正面临倒闭。

SYN insolvency, failure, crash, disaster  
2 N-COUNT 破产事件；倒闭事件 A bankruptcy is an instance of an organization or person going bankrupt. 破产事件；倒闭事件 [business]

  • The number of corporate bankruptcies climbed in August.

    8月份企业破产数字攀升。

3 N-UNCOUNT 彻底；缺乏 If you refer to something's bankruptcy, you are emphasizing that it is completely lacking in value or worth. 彻底；缺乏 [emphasis]

  • The massacre laid bare the moral bankruptcy of the regime.

    大屠杀使该政权的道德沦丧暴露无遗。

SYN emptiness, want, vacuum, deficiency
