weaponize wepənaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense weaponizes, present participle weaponizing, past tense , past participle weaponized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 weaponise
VERB 使…武器化;把…改装成武器;把…用作弹药库 If a substance or material is weaponized, it is used as a weapon or made into a weapon. If an area is weaponized, it is used as a location for weapons. 使…武器化;把…改装成武器;把…用作弹药库

  • They were close to weaponizing ricin—a lethal plant toxin. [VERB noun]

    他们快把蓖麻毒素—一种致命的植物毒素—研发为武器了。

  • ...the plan to weaponize outer space. [VERB noun]

    太空武器化的计划
