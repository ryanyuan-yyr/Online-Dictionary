dingy
dɪndʒi ˈdɪŋɪ ˈdɪndʒi
Word forms: comparative dingier, superlative dingiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (建筑或地方)昏暗的，破败的 A dingy building or place is rather dark and depressing, and perhaps dirty. (建筑或地方)昏暗的，破败的

  • Shaw took me to his rather dingy office.

    肖带我去了他那个陈旧昏暗的办公室。

SYN dull, dark, dim, gloomy  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (衣服、室内陈设等)肮脏的，褪色的，暗淡的 Dingy clothes, curtains, or furnishings look dirty or dull. (衣服、室内陈设等)肮脏的，褪色的，暗淡的

  • ...wallpaper with stripes of dingy yellow.

    有暗淡的黄色条纹的墙纸

SYN discoloured, soiled, dirty, shabby
