love affair
Word forms: plural love affairs
1 N-COUNT 风流韵事；（非夫妻或同居情侣间的）恋爱（或性）关系 A love affair is a romantic and usually sexual relationship between two people who love each other but who are not married or living together. 风流韵事；（非夫妻或同居情侣间的）恋爱（或性）关系 [Also + between]

  • ...a stressful love affair with a married man. [+ with]

    与一个已婚男人的痛苦婚外情

SYN romance, relationship, affair, intrigue  
2 N-SING 极大的喜爱；强烈的的兴趣 If you refer to someone's love affair with something, you mean that they like it a lot and are very enthusiastic about it. 极大的喜爱；强烈的的兴趣

  • ...the American love affair with firearms. [+ with]

    美国人对枪支的癖好

  • Tom's love affair with France and most things French knew no bounds.

    汤姆对法国和大多数法国东西都无比热爱。

SYN enthusiasm, love, passion, appreciation
