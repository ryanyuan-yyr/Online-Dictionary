disclose
dɪskloʊz dɪsˈkloʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense discloses, present participle disclosing, past tense , past participle disclosed
VERB 揭露;泄露;使公开 If you disclose new or secret information, you tell people about it. 揭露;泄露;使公开 [Also VERB wh]

  • Neither side would disclose details of the transaction. [VERB noun]

    双方均不肯披露交易细节。

  • The company disclosed that he will retire in May. [VERB that]

    公司透露他将在5月退休。

SYN make known, tell, reveal, publish
