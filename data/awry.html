awry
əraɪ əˈraɪ
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 出岔子的;出错的 If something goes awry, it does not happen in the way it was planned. 出岔子的;出错的

  • She was in a fury over a plan that had gone awry.

    计划出了问题，她很愤怒。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 歪的；斜的 If something is awry, it is not in its normal or proper position. 歪的；斜的 [written]

  • His dark hair was all awry.

    他的黑头发全乱了。

SYN askew, twisted, crooked, to one side
