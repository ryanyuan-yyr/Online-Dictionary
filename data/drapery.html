drapery
dreɪpəri ˈdreɪpəri
Word forms: plural draperies
1 N-UNCOUNT (打褶悬挂的)织物;窗帘;衣物 You can refer to cloth, curtains, or clothing hanging in folds as drapery or draperies . (打褶悬挂的)织物;窗帘;衣物

  • In the dining-room the draperies create an atmosphere of elegance.

    在餐厅里，打褶装饰物能营造雅致的氛围。

2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 布料，布匹 Drapery is cloth that you buy in a shop. 布料，布匹 [British]

  • My mother ran a couple of drapery shops.

    我母亲经营着几家布料店。
