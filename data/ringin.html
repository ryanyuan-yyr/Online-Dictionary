ring in
PHRASAL VERB （给工作单位等）打电话 If you ring in, you phone a place, such as the place where you work. （给工作单位等）打电话 [mainly British]

  • Cecil wasn't there, having rung in to say he was taking the day off. [VERB PREP.]

    塞西尔不在单位，他已经打电话请假了。

SYN celebrate, announce, introduce, proclaim  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 call in
