loaded
loʊdɪd ˈloʊdɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED （问题或词语）哄骗性的，意味深长的，另有所指的 A loaded question or word has more meaning or purpose than it appears to have, because the person who uses it hopes it will cause people to respond in a particular way. （问题或词语）哄骗性的，意味深长的，另有所指的

  • That's a loaded question.

    这是个诱导性的问题。

  • ...the loaded word 'sexist'.

    言外有言的“性别歧视者”一词

SYN tricky, charged, sensitive, delicate  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （特点）浓烈的，深厚的，丰富的 If something is loaded with a particular characteristic, it has that characteristic to a very great degree. （特点）浓烈的，深厚的，丰富的

  • The President's visit is loaded with symbolic significance. [+ with]

    总统之行充满象征性意义。

  • The phrase is loaded with irony. [+ with]

    此语满含讽刺意味。

3 ADJ-GRADED （不公平地）有…倾向的，有…偏向的，倒向…的 If you say that something is loaded in favour of someone, you mean it works unfairly to their advantage. If you say it is loaded against them, you mean it works unfairly to their disadvantage. （不公平地）有…倾向的，有…偏向的，倒向…的 [disapproval]

  • The press is loaded in favour of this present government. [+ in favour of]

    新闻界偏袒本届政府。

  • The article was heavily loaded against Morrissey. [+ against]

    文章对莫里西非常不利。

  • ...very loaded experiments carried out by General Bobby Marshall.

    博比·马歇尔将军搞的有严重倾向性的试验

SYN biased, weighted, rigged, distorted
