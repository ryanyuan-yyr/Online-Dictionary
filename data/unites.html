unite

Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unites, present participle uniting, past tense , past participle united
1 V-ERG （使）团结；（使）联合 If a group of people or things unite or if something unites them, they join together and act as a group. （使）团结；（使）联合

  • The two parties have been trying to unite since the New Year. [VERB]

    这两个党从新年开始就试图联合。

  • The vast majority of nations have agreed to unite their efforts to bring peace. [VERB noun]

    绝大多数国家都同意团结起来为实现和平而努力。

SYN cooperate, ally, join forces, league
