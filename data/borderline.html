borderline
bɔːʳdəʳlaɪn ˈbɔrdərˌlaɪn
Word forms: plural borderlines
1 N-COUNT （两种不同或相反事物之间的）分界线 The borderline between two different or opposite things is the division between them. （两种不同或相反事物之间的）分界线 [Also + of]

  • ...a task which involves exploring the borderline between painting and photography. [+ between]

    一项探究绘画与摄影之间界线的工作

SYN dividing line, divide, boundary, cut-off point  
2 ADJ-GRADED 勉强可以的；刚刚够格的 Something that is borderline is only just acceptable as a member of a class or group. 勉强可以的；刚刚够格的

  • Some were obviously unsuitable and could be ruled out at once. Others were borderline cases.

    一些明显不合适，可以马上排除。另一些则勉强合格。

SYN marginal, bordering, doubtful, peripheral
