trait
treɪt treɪ treɪt treɪ treɪt
Word forms: plural traits
N-COUNT 特征;特点;特性 A trait is a particular characteristic, quality, or tendency that someone or something has. 特征;特点;特性

  • The study found that some alcoholics had clear personality traits showing up early in childhood.

    研究发现一些酗酒者在童年早期就显现出鲜明的人格特征。

  • Creativity is a human trait.

    创造力是人类的一种特性。

SYN characteristic, feature, quality, attribute
