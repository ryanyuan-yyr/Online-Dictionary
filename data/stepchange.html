step change
Word forms: plural step changes
N-COUNT [usually singular] 突变;巨变;重大改革 A step change is a sudden or major change in the way that something happens or the way that someone behaves. 突变;巨变;重大改革

  • We now need a step change in our secondary schools to match that achieved in our primaries. [+ in]

    我们现在需要大力改革中学，以便与我们在小学里所取得的成果同步。
