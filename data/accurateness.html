accurate
ækjʊrət ˈækjərɪt ˈækjərət
1 ADJ-GRADED （信息、测量、数据或仪器等）精确的，准确的 Accurate information, measurements, and statistics are correct to a very detailed level. An accurate instrument is able to give you information of this kind. （信息、测量、数据或仪器等）精确的，准确的

  • Police have stressed that this is the most accurate description of the killer to date.

    警方强调这是迄今对凶手最精确的描述。

  • This may provide a quick and accurate way of monitoring the amount of carbon dioxide in the air.

    这也许能提供一个监测空气中二氧化碳含量的既快又准的方法。

  • Quartz timepieces are very accurate, to a minute or two per year.

    石英钟表走时非常准，年误差仅为一两分钟。

SYN precise, right, close, nice  
accurately

  • The test can accurately predict what a bigger explosion would do.

    该试验能准确地预测一次更大爆炸的威力。

2 ADJ-GRADED （说法、描述）确切的，恰当的 An accurate statement or account gives a true or fair judgment of something. （说法、描述）确切的，恰当的

  • I think it may take sometime before we can have an accurate assessment of the damage.

    我认为我们对损失作出准确的估算可能需要一段时间。

  • They were accurate in their prediction that he would change her life drastically.

    他们预测得没错，他使她的生活发生了翻天覆地的变化。

accurately

  • What many people mean by the word 'power' could be more accurately described as 'control'.

    多数人所谓的“权力”，其实更确切地说是“控制”。

3 ADJ-GRADED （行动、模仿）正确的，到位的 You can use accurate to describe the results of someone's actions when they do or copy something correctly or exactly. （行动、模仿）正确的，到位的

  • Marks were given for accurate spelling and punctuation.

    因拼写及标点正确而得分。

  • ...his maliciously accurate imitation of Hubert de Burgh.

    他对休伯特·德伯格到位的恶搞模仿

4 ADJ-GRADED （武器、投掷）精准的；（人）射击（或投掷）准的 An accurate weapon or throw reaches the exact point or target that it was intended to reach. You can also describe a person as accurate if they fire a weapon or throw something in this way. （武器、投掷）精准的；（人）射击（或投掷）准的

  • The rifle was extremely accurate.

    这把步枪准头极高。

  • The pilots, however, were not as accurate as they should be.

    然而飞行员并不像他们该达到的水平那样精准。

SYN on target, exact, precise, deadly  
accurately

  • ...the technology to aim bombs accurately from aircraft.

    高空精确投弹的技术
