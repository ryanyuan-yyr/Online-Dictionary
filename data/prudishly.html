prudish
pruːdɪʃ ˈprudɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED （在性问题上）大惊小怪的，过分正经的；谈性色变的 If you describe someone as prudish, you mean that they are too easily shocked by things relating to sex. （在性问题上）大惊小怪的，过分正经的；谈性色变的 [disapproval]

  • I'm not prudish but I think these photographs are obscene.

    我并不是假正经的人，但我觉得这些照片非常淫秽。

SYN prim, formal, proper, stuffy  
prudishness

  • Older people will have grown up in a time of greater sexual prudishness.

    年纪稍大的人是在一个性观念更为保守的时代中成长起来的。
