multimedia
mʌltimiːdiə ˌmʌltɪˈmiːdɪə ˌmʌltɪˈmidiə
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 多媒体 You use multimedia to refer to computer programs and products which involve sound, pictures, and film, as well as text. 多媒体

  • ...the case of an insurance company using multimedia to improve customer service in its branches.

    保险公司使用多媒体改进其分支机构的客户服务的例子

  • ...the next generation of computers, which will be 'multimedia machines' that allow users to control and manipulate sound, video, text and graphics.

    将来的新一代电脑，即允许用户操控声音、录像、文本和制图的“多媒体机”

2 N-UNCOUNT (教学中)多媒体的使用 In education, multimedia is the use of television and other different media in a lesson, as well as books. (教学中)多媒体的使用
3 N-UNCOUNT (艺术中)多种材料的使用 In art, multimedia is the use of different kinds of material in a painting, sculpture or performance. (艺术中)多种材料的使用
