frail
freɪl freɪl
Word forms: comparative frailer, superlative frailest
1 ADJ-GRADED 虚弱的;体弱的 Someone who is frail is not very strong or healthy. 虚弱的;体弱的

  • She lay in bed looking particularly frail.

    她躺在床上，看上去特别虚弱。

SYN feeble, weak, puny, decrepit  
2 ADJ-GRADED 易碎的;易坏的 Something that is frail is easily broken or damaged. 易碎的;易坏的

  • The frail craft rocked as he clambered in.

    他爬进来的时候，那件易碎的工艺品摇晃起来。

SYN flimsy, weak, vulnerable, delicate
