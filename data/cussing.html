cuss
kʌs kʌs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cusses, present participle cussing, past tense , past participle cussed
VERB 诅咒;咒骂 If someone cusses, they swear at someone or use bad language. 诅咒;咒骂 [informal, old-fashioned] [Also VERB n]

  • Tosh was known to be a man who would cuss and shout. [VERB]

    托什是一个遇事喜欢又嚷又骂的人。

  • He rails and cusses at those pop stars. [VERB + at]

    他辱骂并诅咒那些流行音乐歌手。
