attentive
ətentɪv əˈtɛntɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 注意的；专心的；留意的 If you are attentive, you are paying close attention to what is being said or done. 注意的；专心的；留意的

  • The vast majority of the attentive audience applauded these sentiments.

    大多数聚精会神听讲的观众都对这些观点表示赞赏。

  • He wishes the government would be more attentive to detail in their response.

    他希望政府的回应能更加关注细节。

SYN intent, listening, concentrating, careful  
attentively

  • He questioned Chrissie, and listened attentively to what she told him.

    他询问了克里茜，并仔细聆听她所说的话。

2 ADJ-GRADED 关心的;体贴的;殷勤的 Someone who is attentive is helpful and polite. 关心的;体贴的;殷勤的

  • At society parties he is attentive to his wife. [+ to]

    在社交聚会上，他对妻子体贴周到。

  • The staff is well trained in courteous and attentive service to each and every guest.

    员工训练有素，为每一位客人提供礼貌周到的服务。

SYN considerate, kind, civil, devoted  
attentiveness

  • Anne was both flattered and surprised by Danny's attentiveness to her.

    丹尼的大献殷勤让安妮受宠若惊。
