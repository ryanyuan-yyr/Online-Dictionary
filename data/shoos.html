shoo
ʃuː ʃu
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shoos, present participle shooing, past tense , past participle shooed
1 VERB (挥手)赶走 If you shoo an animal or a person away, you make them go away by waving your hands or arms at them. (挥手)赶走

  • You'd better shoo him away. [VERB noun with adv.]

    你最好把他赶走。

  • I shooed him out of the room. [VERB noun prep.]

    我把他赶出了房间。

2 EXCLAM (赶动物走的声音)嘘！去！走！ You say 'shoo!' to an animal when you want it to go away. (赶动物走的声音)嘘！去！走！

  • Shoo, bird, shoo.

    嘘！小鸟，嘘！
