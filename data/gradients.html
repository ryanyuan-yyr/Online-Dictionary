gradient
greɪdiənt ˈgreɪdiənt
Word forms: plural gradients
1 N-COUNT 斜坡；坡度；倾斜度 A gradient is a slope, or the degree to which the ground slopes. 斜坡；坡度；倾斜度 [British]

  • ...a gradient of 1 in 3.

    1比3的倾斜度

  • The courses are long and punishing, with steep gradients.

    这些赛道既长又有陡坡，极具挑战性。

SYN slope, hill, rise, grade  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 grade
2 N-COUNT [with supplement] 变化率 The gradient of a graph or series of measurements is the rate at which one set of amounts changes in relation to another. 变化率 [technical]
