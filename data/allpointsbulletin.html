all-points bulletin
Word forms: all-points bulletins
N-COUNT 全面通緝An all-points bulletin is a message sent by a police force to all its officers. The abbreviation APB is also used. 全面通緝 [US]

  • His capture comes just eight hours after the FBI issued an all-points bulletin for Helder.

    在联邦调查局发布全面通缉令8小时后，海尔德就被缉拿归案。
