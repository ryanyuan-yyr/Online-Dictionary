vegetation

N-UNCOUNT 植被;植物;草木 Plants, trees, and flowers can be referred to as vegetation . 植被;植物;草木 [formal]

  • The inn has a garden of semi-tropical vegetation.

    这家小旅馆里有一个亚热带植物园。

  • ...a smell of gently-rotting vegetation.

    一股植物轻微腐烂的味道

SYN plants, flora, greenery, foliage
