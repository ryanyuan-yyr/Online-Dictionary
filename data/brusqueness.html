brusque
brʌsk brusk brʊsk brʌsk
ADJ-GRADED 粗鲁的；无理的；唐突的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as brusque, you mean that they deal with things, or say things, quickly and shortly, so that they seem to be rude. 粗鲁的；无理的；唐突的

  • The doctors are brusque and busy .

    医生们说话简短生硬，十分忙碌。

  • They received a characteristically brusque reply from him.

    他们从他那里得到的是一个粗鲁无礼的答复，他一贯都是那样的。

SYN curt, short, sharp, blunt  
brusquely

  • 'It's only a sprain,' Paula said brusquely.

    “只是扭伤了筋，”葆拉简短生硬地说。
