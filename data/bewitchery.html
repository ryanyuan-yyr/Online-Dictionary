bewitch
bɪwɪtʃ biˈwɪtʃ bɪˈwɪtʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bewitches, present participle bewitching, past tense , past participle bewitched
VERB 使着迷；使着魔 If someone or something bewitches you, you are so attracted to them that you cannot think about anything else. 使着迷；使着魔

  • She was not moving, as if someone had bewitched her. [VERB noun]

    她一动不动，好像着了魔。

  • The doctor is bewitched by Maya's beauty. [VERB noun]

    医生被马娅的美貌深深吸引。

SYN enchant, attract, charm, fascinate  
bewitching

  • Frank was a quiet young man with bewitching brown eyes.

    弗兰克是一个文静的年轻人，有着一双迷人的褐色眼睛。
