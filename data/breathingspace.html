breathing space
Word forms: plural breathing spaces
N-VAR （两项活动之间）喘息的时间 A breathing space is a short period of time between two activities in which you can recover from the first activity and prepare for the second one. （两项活动之间）喘息的时间

  • Firms need a breathing space if they are to recover.

    这些公司需要短暂休整才能恢复元气。

  • We hope that it will give us some breathing space.

    我们希望这能给我们以喘息的机会。

SYN respite, break, rest, time to think
