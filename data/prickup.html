prick up
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 竖起耳朵听;耳朵支起 If someone pricks up their ears or if their ears prick up, they listen eagerly when they suddenly hear an interesting sound or an important piece of information. 竖起耳朵听;耳朵支起

  • She stopped talking to prick up her ears. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她不再说话，竖起耳朵听着。

  • ...ears which prick up at the mention of royalty. [VERB PREP.]

    一提到皇室就竖起耳朵的人

SYN raise, point, rise, stand erect
