limitless
lɪmɪtləs ˈlɪmɪtlɪs
ADJ 无限的；无穷无尽的 If you describe something as limitless, you mean that there is or appears to be so much of it that it will never be exhausted. 无限的；无穷无尽的

  • ...a cheap and potentially limitless supply of energy.

    廉价而且可能永远不会枯竭的能源供应

  • The opportunities are limitless.

    机会无限。

SYN infinite, endless, unlimited, never-ending
