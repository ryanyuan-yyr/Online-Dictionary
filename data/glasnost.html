glasnost
glæznɒst ˈɡlæsˌnɒst ˈglɑsnɔst ˈglɑsnoʊst
N-UNCOUNT 公开性，开放性（最初指前苏联20世纪80年代戈尔巴乔夫总统提出的政策） Glasnost is a policy of making a government more open and democratic. The word glasnost was originally used to describe the policies of President Gorbachev in the former Soviet Union in the 1980s. 公开性，开放性（最初指前苏联20世纪80年代戈尔巴乔夫总统提出的政策）
