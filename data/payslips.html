payslip
peɪslɪp ˈpeɪslɪp
(also pay slip)Word forms: plural payslips
N-COUNT 工资单；工资条 A payslip is a piece of paper given to an employee at the end of each week or month, which states how much money he or she has earned and how much has been taken from that sum for things such as tax and national insurance. 工资单；工资条
