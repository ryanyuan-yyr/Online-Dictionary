demise
dɪmaɪz diˈmaɪz dɪˈmaɪz
N-SING [usually with poss] 终止;消亡;死亡 The demise of something or someone is their end or death. 终止;消亡;死亡 [formal]

  • ...the demise of the reform movement.

    改革运动的消亡

  • Smoking, rather than genetics, was the cause of his early demise.

    导致他英年早逝的原因是吸烟而非遗传。

SYN failure, end, fall, defeat
