bash up
PHRASAL VERB 击伤；打伤 If someone bashes you up, they attack you violently and injure you. 击伤；打伤 [British, informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • The two sisters apparently bashed each other up when their long overdue reconciliation turned sour. [VERB noun PREP.]

    显然，这姐妹俩早就无法再互相迁就，索性厮打起来。

  • I've been bashed up by people with baseball bats because they said I was thieving on their patch. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我被人用棒球球棒打伤了，因为他们说我在他们的地盘上偷东西。
