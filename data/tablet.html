tablet
tæblət ˈtæblɪt
Word forms: plural tablets
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 药片;片剂 A tablet is a small solid round mass of medicine which you swallow. 药片;片剂

  • It's time for your tablets, dear.

    该吃药了，亲爱的。

  • It is never a good idea to take sleeping tablets regularly.

    频繁服用安眠药绝对不是好办法。

SYN pill, capsule, pellet, tab  
2 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (黏土、石头制成的)牌，匾，碑 Clay tablets or stone tablets are the flat pieces of clay or stone which people used to write on before paper was invented. tablets of stone→see: stone  (黏土、石头制成的)牌，匾，碑

  • He also studied the ancient stone tablets from around the pyramids.

    他还研究了在金字塔周围发现的古代石匾。

SYN plaque, slab, panel, stone
