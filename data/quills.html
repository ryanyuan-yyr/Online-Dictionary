quill
kwɪl kwɪl
Word forms: plural quills
1 N-COUNT 羽毛笔 A quill is a pen made from a bird's feather. 羽毛笔

  • She dipped a quill in ink, then began to write.

    她将羽毛笔在墨水里蘸了一下，随后开始书写。

2 N-COUNT (鸟翼或鸟尾的)大羽毛;翎 A bird's quills are large, stiff feathers on its wings and tail. (鸟翼或鸟尾的)大羽毛;翎
3 N-COUNT (豪猪的)棘刺 The quills of a porcupine are the long sharp points on its body. (豪猪的)棘刺
