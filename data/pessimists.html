pessimist
pesɪmɪst
Word forms: plural pessimists
N-COUNT 悲观主义者；悲观论者 A pessimist is someone who thinks that bad things are going to happen. 悲观主义者；悲观论者

  • I'm a natural pessimist; I usually expect the worst.

    我是天生的悲观主义者。我总是做最坏的打算。

  • It looks as if the pessimists are being proved right.

    现在看来似乎悲观论者是正确的。

SYN defeatist, cynic, melancholic, worrier
