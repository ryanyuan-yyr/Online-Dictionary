vibrant
vaɪbrənt ˈvaɪbrənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 充满生气的;精力充沛的;活跃的 Someone or something that is vibrant is full of life, energy, and enthusiasm. 充满生气的;精力充沛的;活跃的

  • Tom felt himself being drawn towards her vibrant personality.

    汤姆感觉自己被她充满朝气的个性所吸引。

  • ...Shakespeare's vibrant language.

    莎士比亚鲜活的语言

  • Orlando itself is vibrant, full of affordable accommodation and great places to eat.

    奥兰多本身充满了朝气，有很多便宜的旅馆和吃饭的好去处。

SYN energetic, dynamic, sparkling, vivid  
vibrancy

  • She was a woman with extraordinary vibrancy and extraordinary knowledge.

    她是一位充满活力、博学多识的女子。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （色彩）明亮的，鲜亮的 Vibrant colours are very bright and clear. （色彩）明亮的，鲜亮的

  • Horizon Blue, Corn Yellow and Pistachio Green are just three of the vibrant colours in this range.

    天际蓝、玉米黄、淡草绿色只是这一色系内的3种亮色。

  • The grass was a vibrant green.

    草地绿油油的。

  • ...vibrant turquoise scarfs.

    明亮的青绿色围巾

vibrantly

  • ...a selection of vibrantly coloured French cast-iron saucepans.

    可供选择的色彩鲜亮的法国铸铁平底锅
