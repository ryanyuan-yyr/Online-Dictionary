enigma
ɪnɪgmə ɪˈnɪgmə ɛˈnɪgmə
Word forms: plural enigmas
N-COUNT [usually singular] 难解之谜;费解的事;费解的人 If you describe something or someone as an enigma, you mean they are mysterious or difficult to understand. 难解之谜;费解的事;费解的人

  • Iran remains an enigma for the outside world.

    对于外界而言，伊朗仍是个谜。

  • ...a story which makes clear the modern enigma of spirituality.

    解开现代灵性之谜的故事

SYN mystery, problem, puzzle, riddle
