moose
muːs mus
Word forms: plural moose
N-COUNT 麋(出现在北欧、亚洲和北美的一种大型鹿，头上长有大而扁的鹿角。一些英国人用该词指北美亚种，用 elk 指欧洲和亚洲亚种) A moose is a large type of deer. Moose have big flat horns called antlers and are found in Northern Europe, Asia, and North America. Some British speakers use moose to refer to the North American variety of this animal, and elk to refer to the European and Asian varieties. 麋(出现在北欧、亚洲和北美的一种大型鹿，头上长有大而扁的鹿角。一些英国人用该词指北美亚种，用 elk 指欧洲和亚洲亚种)
