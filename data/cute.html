cute
kjuːt kjut
Word forms: comparative cuter, superlative cutest
1 ADJ-GRADED 可爱的;漂亮迷人的 Something or someone that is cute is very pretty or attractive, or is intended to appear pretty or attractive. 可爱的;漂亮迷人的 [informal]

  • Oh, look at that dog! He's so cute.

    哦，看看那条狗!它太可爱了。

  • ...a cute little house.

    一栋漂亮的小房子

  • ...a cute little baby.

    可爱的小宝宝

SYN appealing, sweet, attractive, engaging  
2 ADJ-GRADED 妩媚的;性感的 If you describe someone as cute, you think they are sexually attractive. 妩媚的;性感的 [mainly US, informal]

  • There was this girl, and I thought she was really cute.

    就是这个女孩，我认为她非常性感。

3 ADJ-GRADED 精明的;机灵的 If you describe someone as cute, you mean that they deal with things cleverly. 精明的;机灵的 [US]

  • The President, trying to be cute, said his liberal arts education had not really prepared him for such complicated issues as this.

    总统故作精明地说道，自己所接受的文科教育并不能使自己有能力应付如此复杂的问题。

  • That's a cute trick.

    这是个设计巧妙的骗局。
