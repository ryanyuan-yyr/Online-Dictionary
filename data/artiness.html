arty
ɑːʳti ˈɑrti
ADJ-GRADED 附庸风雅的;装作对艺术感兴趣的 Someone who is arty seems very interested in drama, film, music, poetry, or painting. People often describe someone as arty when they want to suggest that the person is pretentious. 附庸风雅的;装作对艺术感兴趣的 [informal]

  • Didn't you find her a little bit too arty?

    你不觉得她有点太附庸风雅了吗？

  • ...an arty French film.

    一部附庸风雅的法国电影

SYN artistic, arty-farty [informal] , arty-crafty [informal]
