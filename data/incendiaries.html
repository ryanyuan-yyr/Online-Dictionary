incendiary
ɪnsendiəri -eri ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri ɪnˈsɛndiəri
Word forms: plural incendiaries
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 纵火的;放火的;能引起燃烧的 Incendiary weapons or attacks are ones that cause large fires. 纵火的;放火的;能引起燃烧的

  • Five incendiary devices were found in her house.

    在她家里发现了5个纵火装置。

  • ...incendiary attacks on shops.

    对商店纵火袭击

SYN inflammatory, provocative, subversive, seditious  
2 N-COUNT 燃烧弹 An incendiary is an incendiary bomb. 燃烧弹

  • A shower of incendiaries struck the Opera House.

    燃烧弹密集落下，击中了歌剧院。
