ravishing
rævɪʃɪŋ ˈrævɪʃɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 非常美丽的；销魂的；引人入胜的 If you describe someone or something as ravishing, you mean that they are very beautiful. 非常美丽的；销魂的；引人入胜的 [literary]

  • She looked ravishing.

    她看起来漂亮极了。

  • ...driving through the ravishing scenery of Cumbria and Yorkshire.

    开车经过风景如画的坎布里亚郡和约克郡

SYN enchanting, beautiful, lovely, stunning [informal]  
ravishingly

  • The Beaujolais hills are ravishingly pretty.

    博若莱山风景十分美丽。
