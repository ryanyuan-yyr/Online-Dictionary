culpable
kʌlpəbəl ˈkʌlpəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 应受责备的;难辞其咎的 If someone or their conduct is culpable, they are responsible for something wrong or bad that has happened. 应受责备的;难辞其咎的 [formal]

  • Their decision to do nothing makes them culpable.

    他们不采取任何行动的决定使他们难辞其咎。

  • ...manslaughter resulting from culpable negligence.

    应该受到处罚的因为疏忽导致的过失杀人罪

SYN blameworthy, wrong, guilty, to blame  
culpability

  • He added there was clear culpability on the part of the government.

    他补充说政府方面明显负有责任。
