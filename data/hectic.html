hectic
hektɪk ˈhɛktɪk
ADJ-GRADED 紧张忙碌的;忙乱的 A hectic situation is one that is very busy and involves a lot of rushed activity. 紧张忙碌的;忙乱的

  • Despite his hectic work schedule, Benny has rarely suffered poor health.

    尽管工作繁忙，本尼却少有身体不好的时候。

  • The two days we spent there were enjoyable but hectic.

    我们在那里度过的两天愉快但忙乱。

SYN frantic, chaotic, frenzied, heated
