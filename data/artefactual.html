N-COUNT 人工制品；（尤指具有历史或文化价值的）手工制品，手工艺品 An artefact is an ornament, tool, or other object that is made by a human being, especially one that is historically or culturally interesting. 人工制品；（尤指具有历史或文化价值的）手工制品，手工艺品
SYN item, thing, article, object
