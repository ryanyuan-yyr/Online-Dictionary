familiarity
fəmɪliærɪti fəˌmɪliˈɛrəti fəˌmɪliˈærəti
1 PHRASE 亲密生嫌隙；亲不敬，熟生蔑 Familiarity is used especially in the expression familiarity breeds contempt to say that if you know a person or situation very well, you can easily lose respect for that person or become careless in that situation. 亲密生嫌隙；亲不敬，熟生蔑

  • Familiarity with evil breeds not contempt but acceptance.

    对罪恶熟悉之后，生出的不是蔑视，而是习以为常。
