decrepit
dɪkrepɪt diˈkrɛpɪt dɪˈkrɛpɪt
ADJ-GRADED 破旧的;年久失修的;衰老的 Something that is decrepit is old and in bad condition. Someone who is decrepit is old and weak. 破旧的;年久失修的;衰老的

  • The film had been shot in a decrepit old police station.

    该影片是在一所破旧不堪的警察局里拍摄的。

  • ..a decrepit old man.

    年迈体衰的老人

SYN ruined, broken-down, battered, crumbling
