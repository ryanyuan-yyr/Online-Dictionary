dictionary
dɪkʃənri ˈdɪkʃənərɪ -neri ˈdɪkʃəˌnɛri
Word forms: plural dictionaries
1 N-COUNT 词典;字典;辞书 A dictionary is a book in which the words and phrases of a language are listed alphabetically, together with their meanings or their translations in another language. 词典;字典;辞书

  • ...a Welsh-English dictionary.

    一本威尔士语-英语词典

2 N-COUNT [with supplement, oft NOUN of n] 专业词典;术语大全 A dictionary is an alphabetically ordered reference book on one particular subject or limited group of subjects. 专业词典;术语大全

  • ...the Dictionary of National Biography.

    《英国传记大词典》

To make dictionaries is dull workSamuel Johnson Dictionary
