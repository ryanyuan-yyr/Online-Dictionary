payer
peɪəʳ ˈpeɪər
Word forms: plural payers
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 付款人；交款人 You can refer to someone as a payer if they pay a particular kind of bill or fee. For example, a mortgage payer is someone who pays a mortgage. 付款人；交款人

  • Lower interest rates pleased millions of mortgage payers.

    利率下调让数百万抵押贷款还款人感到高兴。

2See also:ratepayer  taxpayer 
3 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] 支付人；还账人 A good payer pays you quickly or pays you a lot of money. A bad payer takes a long time to pay you, or does not pay you very much. 支付人；还账人

  • I have always been a good payer and have never gone into debt.

    我一直及时付款，从未欠债。

  • The firm was owed around £300,000 by late payers.

    该事务所被拖欠了约30万英镑。
