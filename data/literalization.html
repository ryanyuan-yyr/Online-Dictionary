literal
lɪtərəl ˈlɪtərəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 字面上的；原义的 The literal sense of a word or phrase is its most basic sense. 字面上的；原义的

  • In many cases, the people there are fighting, in a literal sense, for their homes.

    很多情况下，那里的人们是真刀真枪地在为家园而抗争。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 逐字的；直（译）的 A literal translation is one in which you translate each word of the original work rather than giving the meaning of each expression or sentence using words that sound natural. 逐字的；直（译）的

  • A literal translation of the name Tapies is 'walls.'

    Tapies这个名字直译过来是“墙”。

3 ADJ-GRADED 言辞刻板的；听话不听音的 You use literal to describe someone who uses or understands words in a plain and simple way. 言辞刻板的；听话不听音的

  • Dennis is a very literal person.

    丹尼斯是个听话不听音的人

SYN unimaginative, boring, dull, down-to-earth  
4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 确确实实的；完完全全的 If you describe something as the literal truth or a literal fact, you are emphasizing that it is true. 确确实实的；完完全全的 [emphasis]

  • He was saying no more than the literal truth.

    他说的全是不折不扣的事实。

SYN actual, real, true, simple
