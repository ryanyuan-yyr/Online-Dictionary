presenter
prɪzentəʳ priˈzɛntər prɪˈzɛntər
Word forms: plural presenters
N-COUNT （广播或电视节目的）主持人 A radio or television presenter is a person who introduces the items in a particular programme. （广播或电视节目的）主持人 [mainly British]

  • Most people think being a television presenter is exciting.

    很多人认为当电视节目主持人是一件刺激的事情。

  • Marcel Berlins is the presenter of the BBC radio programme Law in Action.

    马塞尔·柏林斯是英国广播公司《法治进行时》节目的主持人。

SYN host, announcer, compere [British] , anchorman or anchorwoman  
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 host, anchor
