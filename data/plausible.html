plausible
plɔːzɪbəl ˈplɔzəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （解释或说法）似乎真实的，貌似合理的 An explanation or statement that is plausible seems likely to be true or valid. （解释或说法）似乎真实的，貌似合理的

  • A more plausible explanation would seem to be that people are fed up with the Conservative government.

    貌似更加合理的解释似乎是，人们已经厌倦了保守党政府。

  • That explanation seems entirely plausible to me.

    那个解释在我看来完全说得通。

SYN believable, possible, likely, reasonable  
plausibly

  • Having bluffed his way in without paying, he could not plausibly demand his money back.

    他没花钱就蒙混了进来，没道理再要求退钱。

plausibility

  • ...the plausibility of the theory.

    该理论的貌似合理性

2 ADJ-GRADED （人）貌似可信的，花言巧语的 If you say that someone is plausible, you mean that they seem to be telling the truth and to be sincere and honest. （人）貌似可信的，花言巧语的

  • He was so plausible that he conned everybody.

    他那么会花言巧语，以至于骗过了所有的人。

SYN glib, smooth, specious, smooth-talking
