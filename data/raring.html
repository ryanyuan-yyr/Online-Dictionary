raring
reərɪŋ ˈrɛərɪŋ
1 PHRASE 性急的；迫不及待的 If you say that you are raring to go, you mean that you are very eager to start doing something. 性急的；迫不及待的

  • After a good night's sleep, Paul said he was raring to go.

    睡了一夜好觉后，保罗说他已经迫不及待了。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 急切的；热衷于去做…的 If you are raring to do something or are raring for it, you are very eager to do it or very eager that it should happen. 急切的；热衷于去做…的

  • Sarah's here and raring to meet you.

    莎拉来了，她急着要见你。

  • He is raring to charge into the fray and lay down the law.

    他急切地想管管这场闹剧，教训他们一顿。

  • Baker suggested the administration wasn't raring for a fight.

    贝克暗示行政部门并不急于卷入争斗中。
