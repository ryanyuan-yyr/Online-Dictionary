poetic licence
N-UNCOUNT （作家、电影导演等的）破格，不拘一格 If someone such as a writer or film director uses poetic licence, they break the usual rules of language or style, or they change the facts, in order to create a particular effect. （作家、电影导演等的）破格，不拘一格

  • All that stuff about catching giant fish was just a bit of poetic licence.

    有关捕捉大鱼的所有内容只是一点破格罢了。
