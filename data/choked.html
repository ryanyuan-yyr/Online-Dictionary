choked
tʃoʊkt tʃəʊkt
1 ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ with n] 哽住的;哽咽的 If you say something in a choked voice or if your voice is choked with emotion, your voice does not have its full sound, because you are upset or frightened. 哽住的;哽咽的

  • 'Why did Ben do that?' she asked, in a choked voice.

    “本为什么要那样做？”她哽咽着问。

  • One young conscript rose with a message of thanks, his voice choked with emotion. [+ with]

    一个年轻的新兵起立致谢，激动得声音哽咽。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 非常生气的;非常伤心的 If you feel choked about something, you are very angry or upset about it. 非常生气的;非常伤心的 [British, informal]

  • I still feel choked about him leaving.

    我还在为他的离去感到难过。
