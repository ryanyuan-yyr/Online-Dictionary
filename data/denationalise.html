denationalize
diːnæʃənəlaɪz diˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense denationalizes, present participle denationalizing, past tense , past participle denationalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 denationalise
VERB 使非国有化;使转为私营 To denationalize an industry or business means to transfer it into private ownership so that it is no longer owned and controlled by the state. 使非国有化;使转为私营 [old-fashioned, business]

  • The government started to denationalize financial institutions. [VERB noun]

    政府开始对金融机构进行私有化。

  • Eventually Sunderland Shipbuilders was denationalised. [VERB noun]

    桑德兰造船厂最终转为了私营。
