kitsch
kɪtʃ kɪtʃ
N-UNCOUNT 庸俗的艺术作品；迎合低级趣味的拙劣作品 You can refer to a work of art or an object as kitsch if it is showy and thought by some people to be in bad taste. 庸俗的艺术作品；迎合低级趣味的拙劣作品

  • ...a hideous ballgown verging on the kitsch.

    近乎俗丽的难看的舞会礼服

  • Collectors of Fifties kitsch should pop over to Brussels for the flea market.

    50年代庸俗艺术作品的收藏者应该到布鲁塞尔的跳蚤市场去看看。

SYN bad taste, vulgarity, coarseness, tastelessness  
Kitsch is also an adjective.

  • Blue and green eyeshadow has long been considered kitsch.

    蓝色和绿色的眼影一直被认为是俗气的。

  • ...kitsch pop culture.

    品位不高的通俗文化
