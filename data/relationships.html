relationship
rɪleɪʃənʃɪp rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural relationships
1 N-COUNT (人、团体等之间的)关系，关联 The relationship between two people or groups is the way in which they feel and behave towards each other. (人、团体等之间的)关系，关联

  • ...the friendly relationship between France and Britain.

    法国和英国之间的友好关系

  • ...family relationships.

    家庭关系

2 N-COUNT 恋爱关系；性关系；感情关系 A relationship is a close friendship between two people, especially one involving romantic or sexual feelings. 恋爱关系；性关系；感情关系

  • We had been together for two years, but both of us felt the relationship wasn't really going anywhere.

    我们在一起已经两年了，但我们双方都觉得这段感情不会有什么结果。

3 N-COUNT (事物之间的)关系，关联，联系 The relationship between two things is the way in which they are connected. (事物之间的)关系，关联，联系 [Also + to]

  • There is a relationship between diet and cancer. [+ between/of]

    饮食结构和癌症之间有一定关联。

  • ...an analysis of market mechanisms and their relationship to state capitalism and political freedom.

    对于市场机制及其与国家资本主义和政治自由之间关系的分析
