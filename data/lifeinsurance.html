life insurance
N-UNCOUNT 人寿保险 Life insurance is a form of insurance in which a person makes regular payments to an insurance company, in return for a sum of money to be paid to them after a period of time, or to their family if they die. 人寿保险

  • I have also taken out a life insurance policy on him just in case. [+ on]

    为防万一，我还给他买了一份人寿保险。
