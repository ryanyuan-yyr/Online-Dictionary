undoing
ʌnduːɪŋ ʌnˈduːɪŋ ʌnˈduɪŋ
N-SING [with poss] 失败的原因；毁灭的原因 If something is someone's undoing, it is the cause of their failure. 失败的原因；毁灭的原因

  • His lack of experience may prove to be his undoing.

    他缺乏经验可能会最终导致他的失败。

  • Issues of national defense have been the undoing of Democratic candidates.

    国防问题导致了民主党候选人的失利。

SYN downfall, weakness, curse, trouble
