replacement value
rɪˈpleɪsmənt ˈvæljuː
N-SING 重置价值;替换价值 The replacement value of something that you own is the amount of money it would cost you to replace it, for example if it was stolen or damaged. 重置价值;替换价值

  • With conventional insurance policies it is difficult to work out accurately the replacement value of your possessions.

    在传统的保险条款下，很难准确计算财产的重置价值。
