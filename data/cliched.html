clichéd
kliːʃeɪd kliːʃeɪd kliˈʃeɪd
ADJ 老生常谈的If you describe something as clichéd, you mean that it has been said, done, or used many times before, and is boring or untrue. 老生常谈的 [disapproval]

  • The dialogue and acting in Indecent Proposal are tired, clichéd and corny.

    《桃色交易》中的对话和表演既陈腐、老套又很过时。
