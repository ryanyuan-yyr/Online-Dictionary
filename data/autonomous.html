autonomous
ɔːtɒnəməs ɔˈtɑnəməs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 自治的 An autonomous country, organization, or group governs or controls itself rather than being controlled by anyone else. 自治的

  • They proudly declared themselves part of a new autonomous province.

    他们自豪地宣布成为新自治省的一部分。

SYN self-ruling, free, independent, sovereign  
autonomously

  • ...a highly decentralised company, with each of its subsidiaries operating autonomously.

    一个权力高度分散的公司，各子公司自主经营

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 独立自主的；有主见的 An autonomous person makes their own decisions rather than being influenced by someone else. 独立自主的；有主见的

  • He treated us as autonomous individuals who had to learn to make up our own minds about important issues.

    他把我们当作独立自主的个体来看待，认为我们必须学会在重大问题面前自己拿主意。
