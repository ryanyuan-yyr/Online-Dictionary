didactic
daɪdæktɪk dɪˈdæktɪk dɪˈdæktɪkəl daɪˈdæktɪk dɪˈdæktɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 教导的;教诲的;(尤指)道德说教的 Something that is didactic is intended to teach people something, especially a moral lesson. 教导的;教诲的;(尤指)道德说教的 [formal]

  • In totalitarian societies, art exists for didactic purposes.

    在极权社会里，艺术只为教化的目的而存在。

SYN instructive, educational, enlightening, moral  
2 ADJ-GRADED 好灌输的;好说教的 Someone who is didactic tells people things rather than letting them find things out or discussing things. 好灌输的;好说教的 [formal]

  • He is more didactic in his approach to the learning process.

    他在教学过程中侧重于灌输。
