booby-trap
buːbi træp ˈbuːbɪˌtræp
(also booby trap)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense booby-traps, present participle booby-trapping, past tense , past participle booby-trapped
1 N-COUNT 饵雷；陷阱 A booby-trap is something such as a bomb which is hidden or disguised and which causes death or injury when it is touched. 饵雷；陷阱

  • Police were checking the area for booby traps.

    警察在这个地区探测饵雷。

2 VERB [usually passive] 设置陷阱；布下饵雷；安放炸弹 If something is booby-trapped, a booby-trap is placed in it or on it. 设置陷阱；布下饵雷；安放炸弹

  • ...fears that the area may have been booby trapped. [be VERB-ed]

    对该地区可能布有饵雷的忧惧

  • His booby-trapped car exploded. [VERB-ed]

    他那辆被安有炸弹的汽车爆炸了。
