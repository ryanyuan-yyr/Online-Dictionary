print run
prɪnt rʌn
Word forms: plural print runs
N-COUNT 印量；印数 In publishing, a print run of something such as a book or a newspaper is the number of copies of it that are printed and published at one time. 印量；印数

  • It was launched last year in paperback with an initial print run of 7,000 copies. [+ of]

    这本书的平装本在去年推出，首次印数为7,000册。
