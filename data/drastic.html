drastic
dræstɪk ˈdræstɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED （行为）严厉的，极端的 If you have to take drastic action in order to solve a problem, you have to do something extreme and basic to solve it. （行为）严厉的，极端的

  • Drastic measures are needed to clean up the profession.

    行业清理整顿需要严厉的措施。

  • He's not going to do anything drastic about economic policy.

    他不打算对经济政策作任何重大调整。

2 ADJ-GRADED （变化）激烈的，巨大的 A drastic change is a very great change. （变化）激烈的，巨大的

  • Elderly people are not in a position to make drastic changes at this stage of their life.

    上了年纪的人在他们这个人生阶段不会再有什么实质性的改变。

  • ...a drastic reduction in the numbers of people dying.

    死亡人数的大幅下降
