shelter
ʃeltəʳ ˈʃɛltər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shelters, present participle sheltering, past tense , past participle sheltered
1 N-COUNT (躲避恶劣天气或危险的)遮蔽物，庇护处，掩体 A shelter is a small building or covered place which is made to protect people from bad weather or danger. (躲避恶劣天气或危险的)遮蔽物，庇护处，掩体

  • The city's bomb shelters were being prepared for possible air raids.

    为防御可能发生的空袭，该城市正在建防空洞。

  • ...a bus shelter.

    公共汽车候车亭

SYN cover, screen, awning, shiel [Scottish]  
2 N-UNCOUNT (尤指躲避恶劣天气或危险的)遮蔽，庇护，掩蔽 If a place provides shelter, it provides you with a place to stay or live, especially when you need protection from bad weather or danger. (尤指躲避恶劣天气或危险的)遮蔽，庇护，掩蔽

  • The number of families seeking shelter rose by 17 percent.

    寻求避难的家庭户增加了 17％。

  • Although horses do not generally mind the cold, shelter from rain and wind is important.

    尽管马一般不怕冷，但是一定要有个能遮风挡雨的马棚。

  • ...the hut where they were given food and shelter.

    给他们提供食物和庇护的小屋

SYN protection, safety, refuge, cover  
3 N-COUNT 收容所;庇护所 A shelter is a building where homeless people can sleep and get food. 收容所;庇护所

  • ...a shelter for homeless women. [+ for]

    收留无家可归的妇女的收容所

SYN refuge, haven, sanctuary, retreat  
4 VERB 躲避;避难 If you shelter in a place, you stay there and are protected from bad weather or danger. 躲避;避难

  • ...a man sheltering in a doorway. [VERB prep./adv.]

    藏在门口的一名男子

  • Twelve Cubans left the embassy after sheltering there for several days. [VERB prep./adv.]

    12 名古巴人在大使馆藏身了几天后离开了。

SYN take shelter, hide, seek refuge, take cover  
5 VERB [usually passive] 遮蔽;遮挡 If a place or thing is sheltered by something, it is protected by that thing from wind and rain. 遮蔽;遮挡

  • ...a wooden house, sheltered by a low pointed roof. [VERB-ed]

    一所有着低矮的尖顶遮风挡雨的木房子

6 VERB 为(受到警察或其他人追捕的人)提供避难所;窝藏;庇护 If you shelter someone, usually someone who is being hunted by police or other people, you provide them with a place to stay or live. 为(受到警察或其他人追捕的人)提供避难所;窝藏;庇护

  • A neighbor sheltered the boy for seven days. [VERB noun]

    一个邻居收留了这个男孩在他家藏了 7 天。

  • ...people sheltering illegal immigrants. [VERB noun]

    为非法移民提供庇护的人们

SYN protect, shield, harbour, safeguard  
7 See also: sheltered
