hand-held
ˈhændˈhɛld
(also handheld)Word forms: plural hand-helds
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 手持式的;便携式的;掌上的 A hand-held device such as a camera or a computer is small and light enough to be used while you are holding it. 手持式的;便携式的;掌上的

  • Saivonsac shot the entire film with a hand-held camera.

    塞冯萨克使用便携式摄像机拍摄了整部影片。

  • ...a hand-held electric mixer.

    手持电动搅拌器

Hand-held is also a noun.

  • Users will be able to use their hand-helds to check their bank accounts.

    用户将能使用自己的掌上电脑查看自己的银行账户。
