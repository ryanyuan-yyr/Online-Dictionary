sorrow
sɒroʊ ˈsɑroʊ ˈsɔroʊ
N-UNCOUNT 悲哀;伤心;悔恨;惋惜 Sorrow is a feeling of deep sadness or regret. 悲哀;伤心;悔恨;惋惜

  • It was a time of great sorrow.

    这是一个非常悲伤的时刻。

  • Words cannot express my sorrow.

    言语无法表达我的哀伤。

SYN grief, sadness, woe, regret
