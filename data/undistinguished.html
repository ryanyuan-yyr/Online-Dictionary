undistinguished
ʌndɪstɪŋgwɪʃt ˌʌndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt
ADJ-GRADED 不出色的；平凡的；平庸的 If you describe someone or something as undistinguished, you mean they are not attractive, interesting, or successful. 不出色的；平凡的；平庸的

  • ...his short and undistinguished career as an art student.

    他短暂而平庸的艺术专业学习生涯

  • ...this rather undistinguished, grimy industrial town.

    这座毫无特色、尘灰飞扬的工业城镇

SYN ordinary, mediocre, unremarkable, everyday
