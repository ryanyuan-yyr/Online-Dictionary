era
ɪərə ˈɪrə ˈɛrə ˈirə
Word forms: plural eras
N-COUNT 时代；年代；时期 You can refer to a period of history or a long period of time as an era when you want to draw attention to a particular feature or quality that it has. 时代；年代；时期

  • ...the nuclear era.

    核时代

  • ...the Reagan-Bush era.

    里根-布什时期

  • It was an era of austerity. [+ of]

    那是个艰苦的年代。

SYN age, time, period, stage
