point of sale
Word forms: plural points of sale
1 N-COUNT 销售点；售货点（缩略形式为POS） The point of sale is the place in a shop where a product is passed from the seller to the customer. The abbreviation POS is also used. 销售点；售货点（缩略形式为POS） [business]

  • Demand-chain management captures information on consumer behaviour at the point of sale and feeds it up the supply chain.

    需求环节的管理层会捕捉销售点顾客购物行为的信息，然后把信息再反馈给供应环节。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] （用作修饰成分）销售点,售货点（缩略形式为POS） Point of sale is used to describe things which occur or are located or used at the place where you buy something. The abbreviation POS is also used. （用作修饰成分）销售点,售货点（缩略形式为POS） [business]

  • Introduction of electronic point-of-sale systems is improving efficiency.

    引进销售点电子系统提高了效率。

    售货处的商品广告
