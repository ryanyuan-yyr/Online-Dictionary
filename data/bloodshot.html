bloodshot
blʌdʃɒt ˈblʌdˌʃɑt
ADJ-GRADED （眼睛）充血的，布满血丝的 If your eyes are bloodshot, the parts that are usually white are red or pink. Your eyes can be bloodshot for a variety of reasons, for example because you are tired or you have drunk too much alcohol. （眼睛）充血的，布满血丝的

  • John's eyes were bloodshot and puffy.

    约翰双眼红肿。
