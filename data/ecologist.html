ecologist
ɪkɒlədʒɪst
Word forms: plural ecologists
1 N-COUNT 生态学家 An ecologist is a person who studies ecology. 生态学家

  • Ecologists argue that the benefits of treating sewage with disinfectants are doubtful.

    生态学家认为用消毒剂处理污水是否有益值得怀疑。

2 N-COUNT 环境保护主义者；生态保护论者 An ecologist is a person who believes that the environment and natural resources should be preserved and used in a sensible way, rather than being wasted. 环境保护主义者；生态保护论者

  • In the opinion polls the ecologists reached 20 per cent.

    在民意调查中，自认是环境保护主义者的人达到了20%。
