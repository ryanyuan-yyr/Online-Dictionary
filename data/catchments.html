catchment
kætʃmənt ˈkɛtʃmənt
Word forms: plural catchments
N-COUNT (地理学上)汇流，径流，集水；汇流的水 In geography, catchment is the process of collecting water, in particular the process of water flowing from the ground and collecting in a river. Catchment is also the water that is collected in this way. (地理学上)汇流，径流，集水；汇流的水 [technical]
