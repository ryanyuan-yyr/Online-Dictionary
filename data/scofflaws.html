scofflaw
skɒflɔ ˈskɒflɔː ˈskɑfˌlɔ
Word forms: scofflaws
N-COUNT 常违反法规者If you refer to someone as a scofflaw, you mean that they refuse to obey the law, for example, by failing to pay their debts or by ignoring minor legal regulations. 常违反法规者 [US, disapproval]

  • We want unsafe trucks off the road and scofflaws brought to justice.

    我们希望安全车上路，而常违反法规者可以受到法律的制裁。
