parting
pɑːʳtɪŋ ˈpɑrtɪŋ
Word forms: plural partings
1 N-VAR 离别；分手；分别 Parting is the act of leaving a particular person or place. A parting is an occasion when this happens. 离别；分手；分别

  • Parting from any one of you for even a short time is hard.

    离开你们中任何一个人哪怕只是一小段时间都舍不得。

  • It was a dreadfully emotional parting.

    那是一次非常动情的分别。

SYN farewell, departure, goodbye, leave-taking  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 离别的；分手的 Your parting words or actions are the things that you say or do as you are leaving a place or person. 离别的；分手的

  • Her parting words left him feeling empty and alone.

    她分手时说的话让他感觉空虚孤独。

  • ...his bold parting kiss.

    他大胆的吻别

3 N-COUNT （头发的）分缝 The parting in someone's hair is the line running from the front to the back of their head where their hair lies in different directions. （头发的）分缝 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 part
4 PHRASE 分道扬镳 When there is a parting of the ways, two or more people or groups of people stop working together or travelling together. 分道扬镳

  • ...a negotiated parting of the ways for the three Baltic republics.

    这三个波罗的海共和国协议分道扬镳

SYN divorce, parting, separation, dissolution
