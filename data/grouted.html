grout
graʊt graʊt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense grouts, present participle grouting, past tense , past participle grouted
1 N-UNCOUNT （用于墙上瓷砖间抹缝的）勾缝剂，薄胶泥 Grout is a thin mixture of sand, water, and cement or lime, which is used to fill in the spaces between tiles that are fixed to a wall. （用于墙上瓷砖间抹缝的）勾缝剂，薄胶泥
2 VERB 给（瓷砖等）灌浆；为…涂薄胶泥 If you grout the tiles on a wall, you use grout to fill in the spaces between the tiles. 给（瓷砖等）灌浆；为…涂薄胶泥 [Also VERB n]

  • Make sure that your tiles are thoroughly grouted and sealed. [be VERB-ed]

    要确保这些瓷砖经过彻底灌浆并且密封好。
