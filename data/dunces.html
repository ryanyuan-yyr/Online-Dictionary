dunce
dʌns dʌns
Word forms: plural dunces
N-COUNT （人）愚笨的，迟钝的 If you say that someone is a dunce, you think they are rather stupid because they find it difficult or impossible to learn what someone is trying to teach them. （人）愚笨的，迟钝的 [disapproval]

  • Michael may have been a dunce at mathematics, but he was gifted at languages.

    迈克尔在数学上可能不太灵光，但在语言方面很有天赋。

SYN simpleton, moron [slang] , duffer [informal] , bonehead [slang]
