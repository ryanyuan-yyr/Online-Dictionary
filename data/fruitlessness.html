fruitless
fruːtləs ˈfrutlɪs
ADJ-GRADED 无结果的;无成效的;徒劳的 Fruitless actions, events, or efforts do not achieve anything at all. 无结果的;无成效的;徒劳的

  • It was a fruitless search.

    搜寻工作毫无结果。

  • Talks have so far have been fruitless.

    会谈到目前为止未见成果。

SYN useless, vain, unsuccessful, in vain  
fruitlessly

  • Four years of negotiation ended fruitlessly last December.

    历时4 年的谈判在去年 12 月无果而终。
