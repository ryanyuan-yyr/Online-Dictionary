existentialist
egzɪstenʃəlɪst
Word forms: plural existentialists
1 N-COUNT 存在主义者 An existentialist is a person who agrees with the philosophy of existentialism. 存在主义者
2 ADJ 存在主义的 If you describe a person or their philosophy as existentialist, you mean that their beliefs are based on existentialism. 存在主义的

  • ...existentialist theories.
