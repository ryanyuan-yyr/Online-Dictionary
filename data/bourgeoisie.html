bourgeoisie
bʊəʳʒwɑːziː ˌbʊrʒwɑˈzi
1 N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] （马克思主义理论中的）资产阶级 In Marxist theory, the bourgeoisie are the middle-class people who own most of the wealth in a capitalist system. （马克思主义理论中的）资产阶级 [technical]

  • ...the suppression of the proletariat by the bourgeoisie.

    资产阶级对无产阶级的压迫
