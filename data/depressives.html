depressive
dɪpresɪv diˈprɛsɪv dɪˈprɛsɪv
Word forms: plural depressives
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 抑郁的;沮丧的;消沉的 Depressive means relating to depression or to being depressed. 抑郁的;沮丧的;消沉的

  • He's no longer a depressive character.

    他不再是一个消沉的人。

  • ...a severe depressive disorder.

    重度抑郁症

2 N-COUNT 抑郁症患者 A depressive is someone who suffers from depression. 抑郁症患者
3See also:manic-depressive
