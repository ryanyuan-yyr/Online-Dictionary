N-COUNT [usu N to n] (卷首或章节前的)格言; 引语An epigraph is a quotation at the beginning of a book or chapter of a book. (卷首或章节前的)格言; 引语

  • The epigraph to O'Rourke's book is from de Quincey's Confessions of an English Opium Eater.
