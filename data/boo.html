boo
buː bu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense boos, present participle booing, past tense , past participle booed
1 VERB 发嘘声；喝倒彩 If you boo a speaker or performer, you shout 'boo' or make other loud sounds to indicate that you do not like them, their opinions, or their performance. 发嘘声；喝倒彩

  • People were booing and throwing things at them. [VERB]

    人们向他们喝倒彩、掷东西。

  • Demonstrators booed and jeered him. [VERB noun]

    示威者们都发嘘声嘲笑他。

  • He was booed off the stage. [be VERB-ed]

    他被哄下了舞台。

Boo is also a noun.

  • She was greeted with boos and hisses.

    迎接她的是一片嘘声。

booing

  • The fans are entitled to their opinion but booing doesn't help anyone.

    粉丝有权表达他们的观点，但起哄对任何人都没有好处。

2 EXCLAM 嘿，砰（用于吓唬人时发出的声音） You say 'Boo!' loudly and suddenly when you want to surprise someone who does not know that you are there. 嘿，砰（用于吓唬人时发出的声音）
