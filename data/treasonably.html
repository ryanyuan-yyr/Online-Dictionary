treasonable
triːzənəbəl ˈtrizənəbəl
ADJ 谋反的;涉及叛国罪的 Treasonable activities are criminal activities which someone carries out with the intention of helping their country's enemies or removing its government using violence. 谋反的;涉及叛国罪的

  • They were brought to trial for treasonable conspiracy.

    他们因密谋叛国被送上法庭。

SYN disloyal, false, subversive, treacherous
