feral
ferəl fɪər- ˈfɪərəl ˈfɪrəl ˈfɛrəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (尤指通常由人饲养的动物)野生的，未驯服的 Feral animals are wild animals that are not owned or controlled by anyone, especially ones that belong to species which are normally owned and kept by people. (尤指通常由人饲养的动物)野生的，未驯服的 [formal]

  • ...feral cats.

    野猫

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 狂野的;狂暴的;桀骜不驯的 If you describe something or someone as feral, you mean that they seem wild, fierce, and uncontrolled. 狂野的;狂暴的;桀骜不驯的 [literary]

  • ...the prowling, feral guitars underpinned by metronomic drum beats.

    在有节奏的鼓声伴奏下悄然而至、狂烈激越的吉他声
