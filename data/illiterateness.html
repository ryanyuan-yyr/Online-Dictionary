illiterate
ɪlɪtərət ɪˈlɪtərɪt
Word forms: plural illiterates
1 ADJ 文盲的;不会读写的 Someone who is illiterate does not know how to read or write. 文盲的;不会读写的

  • A large percentage of the population is illiterate.

    文盲人口占有相当高的比例。

SYN uneducated, ignorant, unlettered, unable to read and write  
An illiterate is someone who is illiterate.文盲

  • ...an educational centre for illiterates.

    扫盲中心

2 ADJ [usually adverb ADJ] (音乐、科技、经济上)知之甚少的,接近无知的 If you describe someone as musically, technologically, or economically illiterate, you mean that they do not know much about music, technology, or economics. (音乐、科技、经济上)知之甚少的,接近无知的

  • Many senior managers are technologically illiterate.

    许多高级经理都对技术知之甚少。

SYN ignorant, unaware, inexperienced, blind
