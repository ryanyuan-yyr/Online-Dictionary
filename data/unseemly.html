unseemly
ʌnsiːmli ʌnˈsimli
ADJ-GRADED 不合适的；不得体的 If you say that some-one's behaviour is unseemly, you disapprove of it because it is not polite or not suitable for a particular situation or occasion. 不合适的；不得体的 [literary, disapproval]

  • It would be unseemly for judges to receive pay increases when others are having to tighten their belts. [+ for]

    在其他人正不得不勒紧裤腰带的时候，给法官提高薪水是不合适的。

  • ...unseemly drinking, brawling and gambling.

    遭非议的酗酒、争吵和赌博

SYN improper, inappropriate, unsuitable, out of place
