go under
1 PHRASAL VERB （商行等）倒闭，垮掉；（项目）中止，失败 If a business or project goes under, it becomes unable to continue in operation or in existence. （商行等）倒闭，垮掉；（项目）中止，失败 [business]

  • If one firm goes under it could provoke a cascade of bankruptcies. [VERB PREP.]

    如果一家公司倒闭，便有可能引发一连串的破产。

SYN fail, die, sink, go down  
2 PHRASAL VERB （船）沉没；（人）沉入水中 If a boat, ship, or person in a sea or river goes under, they sink below the surface of the water. （船）沉没；（人）沉入水中

  • The ship went under, taking with her all her crew. [VERB PREP.]

    轮船连同其所有的船员一起沉没了。

SYN sink, go down, founder, submerge
