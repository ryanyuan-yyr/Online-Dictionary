honeyed
hʌnid ˈhʌnid
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （声音或言辞）甜蜜的，悦耳的;（尤指）虚伪的，阿谀的 You can describe some-one's voice or words as honeyed when they are very pleasant to listen to, especially if you want to suggest that they are insincere. （声音或言辞）甜蜜的，悦耳的;（尤指）虚伪的，阿谀的

  • His gentle manner and honeyed tones reassured Andrew.

    他温和的态度和悦耳的语调使安德鲁消除了疑虑。

  • They could not understand how anyone could believe her honeyed words.

    他们无法理解怎么会有人相信她的甜言蜜语。

SYN flattering, sweet, soothing, enticing  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 蜂蜜味的;蜜糖色的 You can describe something as honeyed when it tastes or smells of honey, or is the pale yellowish colour of honey. 蜂蜜味的;蜜糖色的 [literary]

  • I could smell the honeyed ripeness of melons and peaches.

    我能闻到瓜桃熟透了的香甜味道。

  • ...a warm, honeyed light.

    温暖的金黄色光线

SYN sweet, sweetened, luscious, sugary
