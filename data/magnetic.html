magnetic
mægnetɪk mægˈnɛtɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 磁的;磁性的 If something metal is magnetic, it acts like a magnet. 磁的;磁性的

  • ...magnetic particles.

    磁性微粒

2 ADJ 磁力(作用)的 You use magnetic to describe something that is caused by or relates to the force of magnetism. 磁力(作用)的

  • The electrically charged gas particles are affected by magnetic forces.

    带电气体粒子会受磁力影响。

magnetically

  • ...metal fragments held together magnetically.

    由于磁力而吸在一起的金属碎片。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 磁化的;带磁性的 You use magnetic to describe tapes and other objects which have a coating of a magnetic substance and contain coded information that can be read by computers or other machines. 磁化的;带磁性的

  • ...her magnetic strip ID card.

    她的磁条身份证

  • ...magnetic recording tape.

    录音磁带

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 引人注目的;有吸引力的;有魅力的 If you describe something as magnetic, you mean that it is very attractive to people because it has unusual, powerful, and exciting qualities. 引人注目的;有吸引力的;有魅力的

  • ...the magnetic effect of the prosperous German economy on would-be immigrants.

    繁荣的德国经济对未来移民的吸引力

  • ...the magnetic pull of his looks and her personality.

    他外表的魅力与她个性的魅力

SYN attractive, irresistible, seductive, captivating
