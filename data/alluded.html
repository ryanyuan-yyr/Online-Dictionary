allude
əluːd əˈlud æˈlud
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense alludes, present participle alluding, past tense , past participle alluded
VERB 间接提到；暗指；影射 If you allude to something, you mention it in an indirect way. 间接提到；暗指；影射 [formal]

  • She also alluded to her rival's past marital troubles. [VERB + to]

    她还影射了对手过去的婚姻问题。
