drippy
drɪpi ˈdrɪpi
ADJ-GRADED (指人)愚蠢的,软弱的;(书、音乐等)无聊的，枯燥的,过于伤感的 If you describe someone as drippy, you mean that they are rather stupid and weak. If you describe something such as a book or a type of music as drippy, you mean that you think it is rather stupid, dull, and sentimental. (指人)愚蠢的,软弱的;(书、音乐等)无聊的，枯燥的,过于伤感的 [informal, disapproval]

    这些人看上去有点笨。
