tidy away
PHRASAL VERB 收拾;拾掇 When you tidy something away, you put it in something else so that it is not in the way. 收拾;拾掇 [mainly British]

  • The large log basket can be used to tidy toys away. [VERB noun PREP.]

    大木篮子可以用来装玩具。

  • When they'd gone, McMinn tidied away the glasses and teacups.

    他们走后，麦克明把玻璃杯、茶杯收拾了起来。
