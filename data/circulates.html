circulate
sɜːʳkjʊleɪt ˈsɜrkjuˌleɪt ˈsɜrkjəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense circulates, present participle circulating, past tense , past participle circulated
1 V-ERG (文章等)传递，传阅，散发 If a piece of writing circulates or is circulated, copies of it are passed round among a group of people. (文章等)传递，传阅，散发

  • The document was previously circulated in New York at the United Nations. [be VERB-ed]

    这份文件先前在纽约的联合国总部传阅过。

  • Public employees, teachers and liberals are circulating a petition for his recall. [VERB noun]

    公务员、教师和自由派人士正在散发请愿书，要求将他召回。

  • An e-mail is already circulating amongst news staff calling for voluntary redundancies.

    在新闻部员工中已经在转发一封电子邮件，呼吁大家自愿裁汰。

SYN spread, issue, publish, broadcast  
circulation

  • ...an inquiry into the circulation of 'unacceptable literature'.

    对“不良文学作品”的流传进行的调查

2 V-ERG (谣言等)流传;散布 If something such as a rumour circulates or is circulated, the people in a place tell it to each other. (谣言等)流传;散布 [Also VERB n]

  • Rumours were already beginning to circulate that the project might have to be abandoned. [VERB]

    已传出谣言说，该项目可能会被迫取消。

  • I deeply resented those sort of rumours being circulated at a time of deeply personal grief. [be VERB-ed]

    我特别憎恨那种在人家伤心透顶的时候还在传播的谣言。

SYN get around, spread, go around  
3 VERB (在密闭场所或系统内)循环，流转，传播 When something circulates, it moves easily and freely within a closed place or system. (在密闭场所或系统内)循环，流转，传播 [Also VERB preposition]

  • ...a virus which circulates via the bloodstream and causes ill health in a variety of organs. [VERB]

    通过血液循环传播、造成多种器官病变的病毒

  • Cooking odors can circulate throughout the entire house.

    做饭的香味可以传到家里的各个角落。

SYN flow, revolve, rotate, radiate  
circulation

  • The north pole is warmer than the south and the circulation of air around it is less well contained.

    北极比南极温度高，周围的空气流动也更为顺畅。

  • ...the principle of free circulation of goods.

    商品自由流通的原则

4 VERB (在聚会上)周旋，往来应酬 If you circulate at a party, you move among the guests and talk to different people. (在聚会上)周旋，往来应酬

  • Let me get you something to drink, then I must circulate. [VERB]

    我给你找点喝的，然后我得去应酬一下。

SYN mingle, socialize
