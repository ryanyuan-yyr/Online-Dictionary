tablespoon
teɪbəlspuːn ˈteɪbəlˌspun
Word forms: plural tablespoons
1 N-COUNT 大汤匙;大调羹 A tablespoon is a fairly large spoon used for serving food and in cooking. 大汤匙;大调羹
2 N-COUNT 一大汤匙(或大调羹)的量 You can refer to an amount of food resting on a tablespoon as a tablespoon of food. 一大汤匙(或大调羹)的量

  • ...a tablespoon of sugar. [+ of]

    一大汤匙糖
