unambiguous
ʌnæmbɪgjuəs ˌʌnæmˈbɪɡjʊəs
ADJ-GRADED 不含糊的；清晰的；明确的 If you describe a message or comment as unambiguous, you mean that it is clear and cannot be understood wrongly. 不含糊的；清晰的；明确的

  • ...an election result that sent the party an unambiguous message.

    向该党传递明确信息的选举结果

  • The close-up photography and commentary are clear and unambiguous.

    那组特写照片和附带的评论一目了然，毫无含糊之处。

unambiguously

  • He has failed to dissociate himself clearly and unambiguously from the attack.

    他未能干净利落地同那起攻击事件撇清关系。
