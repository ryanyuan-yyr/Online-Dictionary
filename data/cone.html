cone
koʊn koʊn
Word forms: plural cones
1 N-COUNT 圆锥体 A cone is a shape with a circular base and smooth curved sides ending in a point at the top. 圆锥体
2 N-COUNT (松树、冷杉树等的)球果 A cone is the fruit of a tree such as a pine or fir. (松树、冷杉树等的)球果

  • ...a bowl of fir cones.

    一碗冷杉树球果

3 N-COUNT 冰激凌蛋卷筒;蛋筒冰激凌 A cone is a thin, cone-shaped biscuit that is used for holding ice cream. You can also refer to an ice cream that you eat in this way as a cone . 冰激凌蛋卷筒;蛋筒冰激凌

  • She stopped by the ice-cream shop and had a chocolate cone.

    她顺便去了趟冰激凌店，买了一个巧克力蛋筒冰激凌。
