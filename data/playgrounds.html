playground
pleɪgraʊnd ˈpleɪˌgraʊnd
Word forms: plural playgrounds
1 N-COUNT （学校的）操场，运动场；（儿童）游乐场 A playground is a piece of land, at school or in a public area, where children can play. （学校的）操场，运动场；（儿童）游乐场
2See also:adventure playground 
3 N-COUNT [usually singular] （某一群体的）游憩胜地，度假场所 If you describe a place as a playground for a certain group of people, you mean that those people like to enjoy themselves there or go on holiday there. （某一群体的）游憩胜地，度假场所
