irate
aɪreɪt aɪˈreɪt ˈaɪˌreɪt
ADJ-GRADED 发怒的;极其愤怒的;被惹火的 If someone is irate, they are very angry about something. 发怒的;极其愤怒的;被惹火的

  • The owner was so irate he almost threw me out of the place.

    店主非常恼火,差点把我从那个地方扔出去。

  • She then wrote an extremely irate letter to the New Statesman about me.

    她接着给《新政治家》杂志写了一封关于我的信，言辞极其激烈。

SYN angry, cross, furious, angered
