uncork
ʌnkɔːʳk ʌnˈkɔːk ʌnˈkɔrk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense uncorks, present participle uncorking, past tense , past participle uncorked
VERB 拔去…的塞子；打开（瓶子） When you uncork a bottle, you open it by pulling the cork out of it. 拔去…的塞子；打开（瓶子）

  • Steve uncorked bottles of champagne to toast the achievement. [VERB noun]
