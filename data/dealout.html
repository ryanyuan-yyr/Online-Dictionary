deal out
1 PHRASAL VERB 给予，予以(惩罚或伤害) If someone deals out a punishment or harmful action, they punish or harm someone. 给予，予以(惩罚或伤害) [written] [Also V P n (not pron), V n P]

  • ...a failure to deal out effective punishment to aggressors. [V P n + to]

    没能对侵略者进行有力的惩罚

  • ...the tale of a woman who refuses to accept the injustice dealt out to her husband. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    讲述一个女人拒绝接受自己丈夫遭遇的不公正对待的故事

SYN distribute, give, administer, share
