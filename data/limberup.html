limber up
PHRASAL VERB （比赛等前）做准备活动，热身 If you limber up, you prepare for an energetic physical activity such as a sport by moving and stretching your body. （比赛等前）做准备活动，热身

  • Next door, 200 girls are limbering up for their ballet exams. [VERB PREP.]

    隔壁屋里，200名女孩正在为她们的芭蕾考试做热身。

  • She ran a little way, limbering up. [VERB PREP.]

    她跑了一小段，活动一下身子。

  • A short walk will limber up the legs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    散一小会儿步能放松腿部。

SYN loosen up, prepare, exercise, warm up
