intoxicated
ɪntɒksɪkeɪtɪd ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 喝醉的 Someone who is intoxicated is drunk. 喝醉的 [formal]

  • He appeared intoxicated, police said.

    他看上去喝醉了,警察说。

SYN drunk, tight [informal] , smashed [slang] , pissed [British , Australian , New Zealand , slang]  
2 ADJ-GRADED 陶醉的;极其兴奋的;忘乎所以的 If you are intoxicated by or with something such as a feeling or an event, you are so excited by it that you find it hard to think clearly and sensibly. 陶醉的;极其兴奋的;忘乎所以的 [literary]

  • They seem to have become intoxicated by their success. [+ by/with]

    他们似乎陶醉在成功的喜悦中。

  • These leaders can become intoxicated with a sense of their own omnipotence.

    这些领袖可能会自感无所不能而得意忘形。

SYN euphoric, excited, exhilarated, high [informal]
