well done
1 CONVENTION 做得好 You say 'Well done' to indicate that you are pleased that someone has done something good. 做得好 [feelings]

  • 'Well done,' said Claud in admiration.

    “干得太棒了，”克劳德钦佩地说。

  • 'Daddy! I came second in history' — 'Well done, sweetheart!'

    “爸爸！我的历史得了第二名。”—“考得好，亲爱的！”

2 ADJ-GRADED (尤指肉类)熟透的，煮烂的 If something that you have cooked, especially meat, is well done, it has been cooked thoroughly. (尤指肉类)熟透的，煮烂的

  • Allow an extra 10-15 min if you prefer lamb well done.

    如果想让羊羔肉完全熟透还需要再煮10到15分钟。
