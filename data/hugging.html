-hugging -hʌgɪŋ
COMB in ADJ [usually ADJ n] （用于名词后构成形容词，修饰衣物）表示“紧包着身体的”，“紧身的”，“修身的” -hugging combines with nouns to form adjectives which describe an item of clothing that fits very tightly and clearly reveals the shape of your body. （用于名词后构成形容词，修饰衣物）表示“紧包着身体的”，“紧身的”，“修身的”

  • ...a figure-hugging dress.

    修身连衣裙

  • ...hip-hugging flares.

    包臀喇叭裤
