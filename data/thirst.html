thirst
θɜːʳst θɜrst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense thirsts, present participle thirsting, past tense , past participle thirsted
1 N-VAR 渴;口渴 Thirst is the feeling that you need to drink something. 渴;口渴

  • Coca is well-known for reducing hunger, thirst and fatigue.

    人们都知道可乐能消饿、止渴和解乏。

  • Instead of tea or coffee, drink water to quench your thirst.

    渴了喝水，别喝茶或咖啡。

  • I had such a thirst.

    我渴得难受。

SYN dryness, thirstiness, drought, craving to drink  
2 N-UNCOUNT 干渴;缺水 Thirst is the condition of not having enough to drink. 干渴;缺水

  • They died of thirst on the voyage.

    他们在航行途中因缺水而死亡。

3 N-SING 渴望;渴求 A thirst for something is a very strong desire for that thing. 渴望;渴求

  • Children show a real thirst for learning. [+ for]

    孩子们对于学习表现出真诚的渴望。

  • ...their ever-growing thirst for cash.

    他们对于金钱越来越强烈的渴求

SYN craving, hunger, appetite, longing  
4 VERB 渴望;渴求 If you say that someone thirsts for something, you mean that they have a strong desire for it. 渴望;渴求 [literary]

  • We all thirst for the same things. [V + for/after]

    我们都渴望得到同样的东西。
