spring-clean
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense spring-cleans, present participle spring-cleaning, past tense , past participle spring-cleaned
VERB 彻底打扫 When you spring-clean a house, you thoroughly clean everything in it. 彻底打扫

  • It's almost as easy these days to give your rooms a new coat of paint as it is to spring-clean them. [VERB noun]

    如今，重新粉刷一下房间就像彻底打扫一次房间一样简单。

Spring-clean is also a noun.

  • It needs a thorough spring clean.

    它需要一次彻底的大扫除。

spring-cleaning

  • The rooms inside were undergoing a spring-cleaning.

    里面的房间正在进行大扫除。
