scenic
siːnɪk ˈsiːnɪkəl ˈsinɪk ˈsɛnɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (地方)景色优美的，风景秀丽的 A scenic place has attractive scenery. (地方)景色优美的，风景秀丽的

  • This is an extremely scenic part of America.

    这是美国风景非常秀丽的一个地区。

  • ...a 2-hour drive through scenic country.

    穿越景色优美的乡间的两个小时车程

SYN picturesque, beautiful, spectacular, striking  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (路线)景色优美的，观光的 A scenic route goes through attractive scenery and has nice views. (路线)景色优美的，观光的

  • It was even marked on the map as a scenic route.

    它甚至还作为观光路线标在了地图上。

  • I went some miles out of my way to take the scenic road into Macon.

    我绕行了几英里，想要经由那条风景秀丽的公路进入梅肯。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] [usually ADJ n] 景色迷人的 If a place has scenic beauty, its scenery is attractive. 景色迷人的

  • ...a land of unparalleled scenic beauty.

    风光无比秀丽的土地

SYN picturesque, beautiful, spectacular, striking  
scenically

  • The Azores are scenically stunning.

    亚速尔群岛的景色美得让人惊叹。
