epicentre
episentəʳ
Word forms: plural epicentres
in AM, use 美国英语用 epicenter
N-COUNT [usually with poss] 震中 The epicentre of an earthquake is the place on the earth's surface directly above the point where it starts, and is the place where it is felt most strongly. 震中

  • The earthquake had its epicentre two-hundred kilometres north-east of the capital.

    那次地震的震中位于首都东北200公里处。
