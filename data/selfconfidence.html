self-confidence ˈsɛlfˈkɑnfədəns
N-UNCOUNT 自信 If you have self-confidence, you behave confidently because you feel sure of your abilities or value. 自信

  • With the end of my love affair, I lost all the self-confidence I once had.

    恋爱告吹后，我一时间自信全无。

  • Richard's self confidence is growing steadily.

    理查德变得越来越自信。

SYN self-assurance, confidence, poise, nerve
