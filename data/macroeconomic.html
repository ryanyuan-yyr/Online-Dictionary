macroeconomics
mækroʊiːkənɒmɪks -ek- ˌmækrəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks ˌmækroʊˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks ˌmækroʊˌikəˈnɑmɪks
(also macro-economics)
1 N-UNCOUNT 宏观经济学Macroeconomics is the branch of economics that is concerned with the major, general features of a country's economy, such as the level of inflation, unemployment, or interest rates. 宏观经济学[business]

    太多的政治家们忽视宏观经济学的重要性。
