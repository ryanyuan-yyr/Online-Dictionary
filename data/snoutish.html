snout
snaʊt snaʊt
Word forms: plural snouts
1 N-COUNT (猪等的)长鼻，口鼻部 The snout of an animal such as a pig is its long nose. (猪等的)长鼻，口鼻部

  • Two alligators rest their snouts on the water's surface.

    两只鳄鱼的大嘴栖息在水面上。

SYN proboscis, nose, jaws, trunk  
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n] 口吻状物(如车头、枪管等) Writers sometimes refer to the front of a car or the barrel of a gun as its snout . 口吻状物(如车头、枪管等)

  • The snout of the Mercedes poked through the gates.

    奔驰轿车的前部把大门洞穿了。

  • The tank stopped with the long snout of its gun turned to the ridge.

    坦克停了下来，长长的炮管转过来瞄准了山脊。
