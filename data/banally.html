banal
bənɑːl -næl ˈbeɪnəl bəˈnæl bəˈnɑl
Word forms: plural banalities
ADJ-GRADED 平庸的；平淡无奇的 If you describe something as banal, you do not like it because you think that it is so ordinary that it is not at all effective or interesting. 平庸的；平淡无奇的 [disapproval]

  • The text is banal.

    课文索然无味。

  • Bland, banal music tinkled discreetly from hidden loudspeakers.

    隐藏的喇叭里幽幽地传出平淡乏味的音乐。

SYN unoriginal, stock, ordinary, boring  
You can refer to banal things as the banal .平庸的事物；平淡无奇的东西

  • The allegations ranged from the banal to the bizarre.

    从平淡无奇到离奇百怪的各种说法都有。

banality

  • ...the banality of life.

    生活的平淡无趣

  • Neil's ability to utter banalities never ceased to amaze me.

    每次我都很惊讶，尼尔怎么能讲出这么索然无味的东西。
