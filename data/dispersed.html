dispersed
dɪspɜːʳst dɪˈspɜːst
ADJ-GRADED 分散的;散布的 Things that are dispersed are situated in many different places, a long way apart from each other. 分散的;散布的

  • ...his widely dispersed businesses.

    他的布局广泛的企业网点

  • They live high in the Andes, in small and dispersed groups.

    他们零星散居在安第斯山脉的高处。

SYN spread-out, extensive, scattered, sprawling
