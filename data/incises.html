incise
ɪnsaɪz ɪnˈsaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense incises, present participle incising, past tense , past participle incised
VERB [usually passive] 切入;刻入 If an object is incised with a design, the design is carefully cut into the surface of the object with a sharp instrument. 切入;刻入 [formal]

  • After the surface is polished, a design is incised or painted. [be VERB-ed]

    表面磨光以后,刻入或画上图案。

  • ...a set of chairs incised with Grecian scrolls. [VERB-ed]

    一套雕有古希腊涡卷形装饰花纹的椅子

SYN cut, carve, etch, engrave
