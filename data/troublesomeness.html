troublesome
trʌbəlsəm ˈtrʌbəlsəm
1 ADJ-GRADED 令人烦恼的;令人头疼的;烦人的 You use troublesome to describe something or someone that causes annoying problems or difficulties. 令人烦恼的;令人头疼的;烦人的

  • He needed surgery to cure a troublesome back injury.

    他需要做手术来治好烦人的背伤。

  • Parents may find that a troublesome teenager becomes unmanageable.

    父母会发现令人头疼的青少年变得难以管教。

SYN disorderly, violent, turbulent, rebellious  
2 ADJ-GRADED (形势、问题等)麻烦的，困难的，棘手的 A troublesome situation or issue is full of complicated problems or difficulties. (形势、问题等)麻烦的，困难的，棘手的

  • The economy has become a troublesome issue for the Conservative Party.

    经济状况已成为保守党的一大难题。

SYN bothersome, trying, taxing, demanding
