tell-tale
(also telltale)
ADJ [ADJ n] 泄露内情的;露馅的 Something that is described as telltale gives away information, often about something bad that would otherwise not be noticed. 泄露内情的;露馅的

  • Only occasionally did the telltale redness around his eyes betray the fatigue he was suffering.

    只有从他发红的眼圈上偶尔能看出他非常疲惫。
