target market ˈtɑːɡɪt ˈmɑːkɪt
Word forms: plural target markets
N-COUNT 目标市场;所针对的市场 A target market is a market in which a company is trying to sell its products or services. 目标市场;所针对的市场 [business]

  • We decided that we needed to change our target market from the over-45s to the 35-45s.

    我们认定有必要把目标市场从45岁以上人群调整为 35–45 岁之间的人群。
