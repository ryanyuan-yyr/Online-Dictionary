caked
keɪkt
ADJ 涂满…的；沾满…的 If something is caked with mud, blood, or dirt, it is covered with a thick dry layer of it. 涂满…的；沾满…的

  • Her shoes were caked with mud. [+ with/in]

    她的鞋上全是泥。

Caked is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...blood-caked bandages.

    沾满血的绷带

  • ...herds of mud-caked cattle and sheep.

    一群群浑身是泥的牛羊
