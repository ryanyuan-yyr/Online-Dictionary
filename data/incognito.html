incognito
ɪnkɒgniːtoʊ ˌɪnkɒɡˈniːtəʊ ˌɪnkɑgˈniˌtoʊ ɪnˈkɑgnɪˌtoʊ
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 隐姓埋名的;隐匿身份的 Someone who is incognito is using a false name or wearing a disguise, in order not to be recognized or identified. 隐姓埋名的;隐匿身份的

  • Hotel inspectors have to travel incognito.

    酒店检查员巡查时必须隐瞒真实身份。

  • He preferred to remain incognito.

    他更喜欢继续隐姓埋名下去。

SYN in disguise, unknown, disguised, unrecognized
