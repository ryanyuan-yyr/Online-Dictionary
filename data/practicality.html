practicality
præktɪkælɪti
Word forms: plural practicalities
N-VAR 实际方面；实际情况 The practicalities of a situation are the practical aspects of it, as opposed to its theoretical aspects. 实际方面；实际情况

  • Decisions about your children should be based on the practicalities of everyday life. [+ of]

    有关孩子们的决定，应该从日常生活的实际情况出发。
