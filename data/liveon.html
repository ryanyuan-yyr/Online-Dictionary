live on
(also live off)
1 →see: live 7 
2 PHRASAL VERB 继续活着 If someone lives on, they continue to be alive for a long time after a particular point in time or after a particular event. 继续活着

  • I know my life has been cut short by this terrible virus but Daniel will live on after me. [VERB PREP.]

    我知道这种可怕的病毒会让我的生命提前结束，但丹尼尔会在我死后继续活下去。
