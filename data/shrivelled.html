shrivel
ʃrɪvəl ˈʃrɪvəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shrivels, present participle shrivelling, past tense , past participle shrivelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 shriveling, shriveled
V-ERG (使)枯萎;(使)干枯;(使)皱缩 When something shrivels or when something shrivels it, it becomes dryer and smaller, often with lines in its surface, as a result of losing the water it contains. (使)枯萎;(使)干枯;(使)皱缩

  • The plant shrivels and dies. [VERB]

    植物干枯而死。

  • ...dry weather that shrivelled this summer's crops. [VERB noun]

    造成今夏作物枯萎的干旱天气

SYN wither, dry (up), wilt, shrink

    叶子开始枯萎。
