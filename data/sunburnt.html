sunburnt
sʌnbɜːʳnt
(also sunburned)
1 ADJ-GRADED 晒伤的;皮肤被太阳灼伤的 Someone who is sunburnt has sore bright pink skin because they have spent too much time in hot sunshine. 晒伤的;皮肤被太阳灼伤的

  • A badly sunburned face or back is extremely painful.

    严重的脸部或背部晒伤会非常疼痛。

SYN burnt, red, peeling, scarlet  
2 ADJ-GRADED 晒得黝黑的;皮肤晒成深褐色的 Someone who is sunburnt has very brown skin because they have spent a lot of time in the sunshine. 晒得黝黑的;皮肤晒成深褐色的

  • Mr Cooper looked fit and sunburnt.

    库珀先生皮肤黝黑，看起来很健康。

SYN tanned, brown, bronzed, brown as a berry
