rakish
reɪkɪʃ ˈreɪkɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不拘俗套的；潇洒得意的 A rakish person or appearance is stylish in a confident, bold way. 不拘俗套的；潇洒得意的

  • ...a soft-brimmed hat which he wore at a rakish angle.

    他潇洒地歪戴着的一顶软边帽

  • ...rakish young gentlemen.

    潇洒的年轻绅士

SYN dashing [old-fashioned] , smart, sporty, flashy  
rakishly

  • ...a hat cocked rakishly over one eye.

    在一只眼睛上方俏皮地翘起的帽子
