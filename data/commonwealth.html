commonwealth
kɒmənwelθ ˈkɑmənˌwɛlθ
1 N-PROPER 英联邦 The Commonwealth is an organization consisting of the United Kingdom and most of the countries that were previously under its rule. 英联邦
2 N-IN-NAMES 联邦，联合体(用于正式名称中) Commonwealth is used in the official names of some countries, groups of countries, or parts of countries. 联邦，联合体(用于正式名称中)

  • ...the Commonwealth of Australia. [+ of]

    澳大利亚联邦

  • ...the Commonwealth of Independent States, which replaced the Soviet Union. [+ of]

    取代了苏联的独立国家联合体

3 N-SING [usually NOUN of n] (关系友好、有共同点的国家组成的)团体 If you refer to a commonwealth of nations, you are referring to a group of countries who are friendly towards each other and have something in common. (关系友好、有共同点的国家组成的)团体 [formal]

  • ...a commonwealth of nations without economic borders.

    经济上自由往来的几个国家组成的联盟
