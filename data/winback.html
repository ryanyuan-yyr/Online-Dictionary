win back
PHRASAL VERB （尤指经过千辛万苦）赢回，夺回 If you win back something that you have lost, you get it again, especially as a result of a great effort. （尤指经过千辛万苦）赢回，夺回

  • The Government will have to work hard to win back the confidence of the people. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政府必须勤奋工作以重新赢得人民的信任。

  • So he went and filed a suit and won his job back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    于是，他去打官司赢回了他的工作。
