feelingly fiːlɪŋli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb] 激动地;满怀深情地 If someone says something feelingly, they say it in a way which shows that they have very strong feelings about what they are saying. 激动地;满怀深情地

  • 'It's what I want,' she said feelingly.

    “这就是我想要的。”她激动地说。

  • She spoke more feelingly of her horses than she did of her own children.

    她说起马来比说起她的孩子还有感情。
