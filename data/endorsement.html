endorsement
ɪndɔːʳsmənt ɛnˈdɔrsmənt ɪnˈdɔrsmənt
Word forms: plural endorsements
1 N-COUNT 赞同;支持 An endorsement is a statement or action which shows that you support or approve of something or someone. 赞同;支持

  • That adds up to an endorsement of the status quo.

    那算是对现状的赞同。

  • This is a powerful endorsement for his softer style of government. [+ for]

    这是对他较温和的执政风格强有力的支持。

SYN approval, backing, support, championing  
2 N-COUNT （驾驶执照上的）违章记录 An endorsement is a note on someone's driving licence saying that they have been found guilty of a driving offence. （驾驶执照上的）违章记录 [British]
3 N-COUNT (对产品、公司的)广告宣传，支持 An endorsement for a product or company involves appearing in advertisements for it or showing support for it. (对产品、公司的)广告宣传，支持
