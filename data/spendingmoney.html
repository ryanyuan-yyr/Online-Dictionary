spending money
N-UNCOUNT (尤指用于度假的)零用钱，零花钱 Spending money is money that you have or are given to spend on personal things for pleasure, especially when you are on holiday. (尤指用于度假的)零用钱，零花钱

  • Jo will use her winnings as spending money on her holiday to the Costa Brava.

    乔要把自己赢的钱作为去布拉沃海岸度假时的零花钱。
