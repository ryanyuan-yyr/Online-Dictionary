sassy
sæsi ˈsæsɪ ˈsæsˌwʊd ˈsæsɪˌwʊd ˈsæsi
1 ADJ-GRADED 年少粗鲁的;轻狂无礼的 If an older person describes a younger person as sassy, they mean that they are disrespectful in a lively, confident way. 年少粗鲁的;轻狂无礼的 [US, informal]

  • Are you that sassy with your parents, young lady?

    小姑娘，你对父母那样无礼吗？

2 ADJ-GRADED 时髦的;漂亮的 Sassy is used to describe things that are smart and stylish. 时髦的;漂亮的 [US, informal]

  • ...his sharp sassy style.

    他那极为时髦的风格

  • ...colourful and sassy fashion accessories.

    各种五颜六色的精美时尚饰品
