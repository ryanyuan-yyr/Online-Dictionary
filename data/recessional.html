recessional
rɪseʃənəl rɪˈsɛʃənəl rɪˈsɛʃənəl
1 N-SING (做完礼拜后的)退场赞美诗 The recessional is a religious song which is sung at the end of a church service. (做完礼拜后的)退场赞美诗
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 经济衰退的;经济不景气的 Recessional means related to an economic recession. 经济衰退的;经济不景气的

  • Many home sellers remain stuck in a recessional rut.

    许多房屋的卖主仍然陷在经济衰退的困境中。
