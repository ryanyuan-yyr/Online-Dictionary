sub-tropical
(also subtropical)
1 ADJ (地区)副热带的，亚热带的 Sub-tropical places have a climate that is warm and wet, and are often near tropical regions. (地区)副热带的，亚热带的

  • ...the sub-tropical region of the Chapare.

    查帕尔省的亚热带地区

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (植物)亚热带的 Sub-tropical plants and trees grow in places that are warm and wet. (植物)亚热带的

  • ...a remarkable garden of sub-tropical plants.

    景致迷人的亚热带植物园
