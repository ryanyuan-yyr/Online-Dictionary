unbidden
ʌnbɪdən ʌnˈbɪdən ʌnˈbɪdən
ADJ [ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ, ADJ n] 自发的；不由自主的 If something happens unbidden, it happens without you expecting or wanting it to happen. 自发的；不由自主的 [literary]

  • The name came unbidden to Cook's mind — Ashley Stoker.

    库克的脑海里不由自主地闪现出一个名字—阿什利·斯托克。

  • Unbidden, his thoughts turned to his wife, who had died two years ago.

    他不禁想到了两年前去世的妻子。

SYN spontaneous, free, voluntary, uninvited
