lemon
lemən ˈlɛmən
Word forms: plural lemons
1 N-VAR 柠檬 A lemon is a bright yellow fruit with very sour juice. Lemons grow on trees in warm countries. 柠檬

  • ...a slice of lemon.

    一片柠檬

  • ...oranges, lemons and other citrus fruits.

    橙子、柠檬和其他柑橘类水果

  • ...lemon juice.

    柠檬汁

2 N-UNCOUNT 柠檬汁；柠檬味饮料 Lemon is a drink that tastes of lemons. 柠檬汁；柠檬味饮料
3 COLOUR 同lemon yellow Lemon is the same as lemon yellow . 同lemon yellow
4 N-COUNT 蹩脚货；次品 If you think that something is a failure, or not as good or as useful as it should be, you can say that it is a lemon . 蹩脚货；次品 [informal, disapproval]

  • He took a little test drive and agreed the car was a lemon.

    他试驾了一下，也认为这车不行。

5 N-COUNT 傻乎乎的人；棒槌 If you think that someone looks foolish because they are shy or slow to take action, you can say that they are like a lemon . 傻乎乎的人；棒槌 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • I just stood there like a lemon.

    我就傻乎乎地站在那儿。
