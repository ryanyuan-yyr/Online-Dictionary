ingratiating
ɪngreɪʃieɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 讨人喜欢的;迎合的;奉承的 If you describe someone or their behaviour as ingratiating, you mean that they try to make people like them. 讨人喜欢的;迎合的;奉承的 [disapproval]

  • He said this with an ingratiating smile.

    他面带奉承地笑着说了这番话。

  • His fellow students had found him too ingratiating.

    他的同学们发现他太爱奉承了。

SYN sycophantic, servile, obsequious, crawling
