lightning rod
Word forms: plural lightning rods
1 N-COUNT 同lightning conductor A lightning rod is the same as a lightning conductor . 同lightning conductor [US]
2 PHRASE 招引…的人 If you say that someone is a lightning rod for something, you mean that they attract that thing to themselves. 招引…的人 [US]

  • He is a lightning rod for controversy.

    他是个争议性人物。
