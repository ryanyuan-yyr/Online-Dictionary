alcoholic
ælkəhɒlɪk -hɔːl- ˌælkəˈhɔlɪk ˌælkəˈhɑlɪk
Word forms: plural alcoholics
1 N-COUNT 酗酒者;嗜酒如命的人 An alcoholic is someone who cannot stop drinking large amounts of alcohol, even when this is making them ill. 酗酒者;嗜酒如命的人

  • He showed great courage by admitting that he is an alcoholic.

    他以极大的勇气承认自己酗酒。

SYN drunkard, drinker, drunk, boozer [informal]  
2 ADJ-GRADED 酒精的；含酒精的 Alcoholic drinks are drinks that contain alcohol. 酒精的；含酒精的

  • ...the serving of alcoholic drinks.

    供应含酒精的饮料

  • ...tea, coffee, and alcoholic beverages.

    茶、咖啡和酒类

SYN intoxicating, hard, strong, stiff
