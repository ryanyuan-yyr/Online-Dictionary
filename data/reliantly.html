reliant
rɪlaɪənt rɪˈlaɪənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 依赖的;依靠的 A person or thing that is reliant on something needs it and often cannot live or work without it. 依赖的;依靠的

  • These people are not wholly reliant on Western charity. [+ on/upon]

    这些人并非完全依赖西方慈善团体。

  • Lithuania is heavily reliant on Moscow for almost all its oil.

    立陶宛的石油供应几乎完全依赖莫斯科。

SYN dependent, relying  
2See also:self-reliant
