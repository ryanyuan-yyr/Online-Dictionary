narrative
nærətɪv ˈnærətɪv
Word forms: plural narratives
1 N-COUNT 故事;叙事 A narrative is a story or an account of a series of events. 故事;叙事

  • ...a fast-moving narrative.

    快节奏的叙事

  • Sloan began his narrative with the day of the murder.

    斯隆以发生谋杀案的那天作为故事的开端。

SYN story, report, history, detail  
2 N-UNCOUNT (通常指小说中一系列事件的)记叙，叙述，讲述 Narrative is the description of a series of events, usually in a novel. (通常指小说中一系列事件的)记叙，叙述，讲述

  • Neither author was very strong on narrative.

    两个作者都不是很善于讲故事。

  • ...Nye's simple narrative style.

    奈简约的叙事风格
