hurly-burly
hɜːʳli bɜːʳli ˈhɜrˈli bɜrli
N-SING 喧哗;吵闹;骚动;繁忙 If you talk about the hurly-burly of a situation, you are emphasizing how noisy or busy it is. 喧哗;吵闹;骚动;繁忙 [emphasis]

  • No one expects him to get involved in the hurly-burly of campaigning. [+ of]

    谁也没有料到他会加入到乱哄哄的竞选活动。

SYN commotion, confusion, chaos, turmoil
