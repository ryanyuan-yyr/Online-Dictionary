parlour
pɑːʳləʳ
Word forms: plural parlours
in AM, use 美国英语用 parlor
1 N-COUNT 客厅；起居室 A parlour is a room in a house for sitting in. 客厅；起居室 [old-fashioned]
2 N-COUNT [n NOUN] （提供服务而不销售货物的）店铺（用于店名中） Parlour is used in the names of some types of shops which provide a service, rather than selling things. （提供服务而不销售货物的）店铺（用于店名中）

  • ...a funeral parlour.

    殡仪馆

  • ...a notorious massage parlour.

    声名狼藉的按摩院

SYN establishment, shop, store, salon
