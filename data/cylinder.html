cylinder
sɪlɪndəʳ ˈsɪləndər
Word forms: plural cylinders
1 N-COUNT 圆柱体;圆筒 A cylinder is an object with flat circular ends and long straight sides. 圆柱体;圆筒

  • ...a cylinder of foam. [+ of]

    泡沫橡胶圆柱体

  • It was recorded on a wax cylinder.

    它被记录在一个蜡质圆柱上。

2 N-COUNT (存放气体的)圆筒状物 A gas cylinder is a cylinder-shaped container in which gas is kept under pressure. (存放气体的)圆筒状物

  • ...oxygen cylinders.

    氧气瓶

3 N-COUNT (发动机的)汽缸 In an engine, a cylinder is a cylinder-shaped part in which a piston moves backwards and forwards. (发动机的)汽缸

  • ...a 2.5 litre, four-cylinder engine.

    2.5升的四缸发动机
