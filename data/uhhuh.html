uh huh
əˈhə
(also uh-huh)
CONVENTION （表示赞同、领悟、同意等）嗯，呃，噢 Uh huh is used in writing to represent a sound that people make when they are agreeing with you, when they want to show that they understand what you are saying, or when they are answering 'yes' to a question. （表示赞同、领悟、同意等）嗯，呃，噢 [informal]

  • 'Did she?' — 'Uh huh.'

    “她做了？”—“嗯。”

  • 'Oh that one.' — 'Uh huh.'

    “哦，那一个。”—“噢。”
