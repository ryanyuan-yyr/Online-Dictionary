fatty
fæti ˈfæti
Word forms: comparative fattier, superlative fattiest, plural fatties
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (食物)含脂肪多的,肥的,高脂的 Fatty food contains a lot of fat. (食物)含脂肪多的,肥的,高脂的

  • Don't eat fatty food or chocolates.

    不要吃高脂食物或巧克力。

  • The report dispels the myth that Northerners have a fattier diet than people in the south.

    该报告消除了北方人比南方人膳食脂肪含量高的谬论。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (酸、组织等)含脂肪的 Fatty acids or fatty tissues, for example, contain or consist of fat. (酸、组织等)含脂肪的

  • ...fatty acids.

    脂肪酸

  • The woman lost about 1.8kg of fatty tissue during the week's fast.

    那个女人在禁食一星期后减脂约 1.8 公斤。

3 N-COUNT 胖子;胖墩;肥仔 If you call someone a fatty, you are criticizing or insulting them for being fat. 胖子;胖墩;肥仔 [informal, disapproval]

  • Consuming this amount of food could turn these fit players into fatties.

    这些体格健壮的运动员吃这么多会变成胖子的。

  • 'Get another one, Fatty,' said Jerry.

    “再拿一个，胖子。”杰里说。
