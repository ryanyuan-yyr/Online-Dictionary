domesticate
dəmestɪkeɪt doʊˈmɛstɪˌkeɪt dəˈmɛstɪˌsaɪz dəˈmɛstəˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense domesticates, present participle domesticating, past tense , past participle domesticated
VERB 驯养;驯化 When people domesticate wild animals or plants, they bring them under control and use them to produce food or as pets. 驯养;驯化

  • We domesticated the dog to help us with hunting. [VERB noun]

    我们驯狗来帮我们打猎。

  • ...sheep, cattle, horses, and other domesticated animals. [VERB-ed]

    羊、牛、马及其他驯养的动物

domestication

  • Sheep are particularly well suited for domestication.

    绵羊特别适合驯养。
