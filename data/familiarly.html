familiarly
fəmɪljəʳli
PHRASE 昵称为;俗名为;诨名是 If you say that something or someone is familiarly known as a particular thing or familiarly called a particular thing, you are giving the name that people use informally to refer to it. 昵称为;俗名为;诨名是

  • ...Ann Hamilton's father, familiarly known as 'Dink'.

    安·汉密尔顿的父亲，昵称为“丁克”
