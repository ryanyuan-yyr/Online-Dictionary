faltering
fɔːltərɪŋ ˈfɔːltərɪŋ
ADJ (尝试、努力或行动)犹疑的，彷徨的，踉跄的 A faltering attempt, effort, or movement is uncertain because the person doing it is nervous or weak, or does not really know what to do. (尝试、努力或行动)犹疑的，彷徨的，踉跄的

  • 'Now I feel I can do it,' he said in faltering English.

    “现在我觉得我能做了。”他用结结巴巴巴的英语说道。

  • Leaning on Jon, Michael took faltering steps to the bathroom.

    迈克尔倚着乔恩，踉踉跄跄地走到浴室。

SYN hesitant, broken, weak, uncertain
