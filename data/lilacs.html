lilac
laɪlək ˈlaɪlək ˈlaɪˌlɑk ˈlaɪˌlæk
Word forms: plural lilacs, plural lilac
1 N-VAR 丁香（树） A lilac or a lilac tree is a small tree which has sweet-smelling purple, pink, or white flowers in large, cone-shaped groups. 丁香（树）

  • Lilacs grew against the side wall.

    丁香树倚着边墙生长。

  • ...a twig of lilac.

    丁香细枝

Lilacs are the flowers which grow on this tree.丁香花

  • ...a vase of tulips, lilies, lilacs and primroses.

    插有郁金香、百合花、丁香花和报春花的花瓶

  • Her hair smelt of lilac.

    她的头发散发出丁香花的香味。

2 COLOUR 丁香紫；淡紫色；淡雪青 Something that is lilac is pale pinkish-purple in colour. 丁香紫；淡紫色；淡雪青

  • All shades of mauve, lilac, lavender and purple were fashionable.

    深深浅浅的各种木槿色、丁香色、熏衣草色和紫色都是流行色。
