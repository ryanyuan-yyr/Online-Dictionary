choke
tʃoʊk tʃoʊk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense chokes, present participle choking, past tense , past participle choked
1 V-ERG (使)噎住;(使)哽住;(使)卡住 When you choke or when something chokes you, you cannot breathe properly or get enough air into your lungs. (使)噎住;(使)哽住;(使)卡住

  • The coffee was almost too hot to swallow and made him choke for a moment. [VERB]

    咖啡烫得几乎咽不下去，呛得他好一会儿喘不过气来。

  • A small child could choke on the doll's hair. [VERB + on]

    洋娃娃的头发可能会让小孩窒息。

  • Dense smoke swirled and billowed, its rank fumes choking her. [VERB noun]

    滚滚浓烟盘旋翻腾，恶臭味呛得她喘不过气来。

  • The girl choked to death after breathing in smoke. [VERB + to]

    那个女孩吸入烟雾，窒息而死。

  • Within minutes the hall was full of choking smoke. [VERB-ing]

    几分钟不到，大厅里就弥漫着呛人的烟雾了。

SYN suffocate, stifle, smother, overpower  
2 VERB 掐死;扼死;勒死 To choke someone means to squeeze their neck until they are dead. 掐死;扼死;勒死

  • The men pushed him into the entrance of a nearby building where they choked him with his tie. [VERB noun]

    那些人把他推进附近大楼的入口处，用他的领带勒死了他。

SYN strangle, throttle, asphyxiate  
3 VERB [usually passive] 挤满;塞满 If a place is choked with things or people, it is full of them and they prevent movement in it. 挤满;塞满

  • The village's roads are choked with traffic. [be VERB-ed + with]

    村里的路上挤满了车辆行人。

  • His pond has been choked by the fast-growing weed. [be VERB-ed + by]

    他的池塘里长满了恣意蔓生的杂草。

SYN block, dam, clog, obstruct  
4 N-COUNT [usually singular] (汽车、卡车等的)阻气门 The choke in a car, truck, or other vehicle is a device that reduces the amount of air going into the engine and makes it easier to start. (汽车、卡车等的)阻气门
