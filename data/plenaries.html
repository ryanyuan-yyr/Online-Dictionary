plenary
pliːnəri plen- ˈpliːnərɪ ˈplinəri ˈplɛnəri
Word forms: plural plenaries
ADJ [ADJ n] 全体出席的 A plenary session or plenary meeting is one that is attended by everyone who has the right to attend. 全体出席的 [technical]

  • The programme was approved at a plenary session of the Central Committee last week.

    这个方案在上周的中央委员会全体会议上获得了通过。

SYN full, open, general, whole  
Plenary is also a noun.

  • There'll be another plenary at the end of the afternoon after the workshop.

    今天下午五六点钟在研讨会结束后还有一次全体会议。
