lackadaisical
lækədeɪzɪkəl ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 怠惰的；懒洋洋的；不热心的 If you say that someone is lackadaisical, you mean that they are rather lazy and do not show much interest or enthusiasm in what they do. 怠惰的；懒洋洋的；不热心的

  • Dr. Jonsen seemed a little lackadaisical at times.

    琼森医生有时候似乎有点懈怠。

  • ...the lackadaisical attitude of a number of the principal players.

    几名主力队员的懒散态度

SYN lazy, lethargic, indifferent, idle
