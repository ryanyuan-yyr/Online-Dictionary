burst out
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 突然…起来 If someone bursts out laughing, crying, or making another noise, they suddenly start making that noise. You can also say that a noise bursts out . 突然…起来

  • The class burst out laughing. [VERB PREP. verb-ing]

    全班突然大笑起来。

  • Then the applause burst out. [VERB PREP.]

    然后突然掌声雷动。

  • Everyone burst out into conversation. [V P + into/in]

    大家顿时纷纷交谈起来。

2 PHRASAL VERB 突然说出；大声喊出 If someone bursts out something, they say it suddenly and loudly. 突然说出；大声喊出 [written]

  • 'I want to be just like you', she bursts out. [VERB PREP. with quote]

    “我想就像你一样。”她大声喊道。

SYN exclaim, cry, call, declare  
3 PHRASAL VERB 突然出现；突然发生 If a situation or problem bursts out, it suddenly appears. 突然出现；突然发生

  • Malaria is bursting out again all over the world. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    疟疾突然又在全世界流行起来。

  • Then war burst out. [VERB PREP.]

    战争突然爆发。

SYN begin, start, happen, appear
