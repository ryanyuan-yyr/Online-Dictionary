mesmerize
mezməraɪz ˈmɛzmərˌaɪz ˈmɛsmərˌɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mesmerizes, present participle mesmerizing, past tense , past participle mesmerized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 mesmerise
VERB 吸引;迷住 If you are mesmerized by something, you are so interested in it or so attracted to it that you cannot think about anything else. 吸引;迷住

  • He was absolutely mesmerised by Pavarotti on television. [be VERB-ed]

    他完全被电视上的帕瓦罗蒂迷住了。

  • There was something about Pearl that mesmerised her. [VERB noun]

    珀尔身上有什么东西吸引住了她。

SYN entrance, fascinate, absorb, captivate
