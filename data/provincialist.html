provincialism
prəvɪnʃəlɪzəm prəˈvɪnʃəlˌɪzəm proʊˈvɪnʃəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 地方主义；地方观念；偏狭 Provincialism is the holding of old-fashioned attitudes and opinions, which some people think is typical of people in areas away from the capital city of a country. 地方主义；地方观念；偏狭 [disapproval]

  • ...the stifling bourgeois provincialism of Buxton.

    巴克斯顿令人窒息的资产阶级狭隘观念
