grouch
graʊtʃ graʊtʃ
Word forms: plural grouches
1 N-COUNT 好抱怨的人；常发牢骚的人 A grouch is someone who is always complaining in a bad-tempered way. 好抱怨的人；常发牢骚的人 [informal, disapproval]

  • He's an old grouch but she puts up with him.

    他是个喜欢抱怨的老头，但是她对他很包容。

SYN moaner, complainer, grumbler, whiner  
2 N-COUNT 抱怨；牢骚 A grouch is a bad-tempered complaint. 抱怨；牢骚 [informal]

  • One of the biggest grouches is the new system of payment.

    人们抱怨最多的一点就是这种新的支付方式。

SYN complaint, protest, objection, grievance
