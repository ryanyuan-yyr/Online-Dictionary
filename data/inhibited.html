inhibited
ɪnhɪbɪtɪd ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 受抑制的;受约束的;拘谨的 If you say that someone is inhibited, you mean they find it difficult to behave naturally and show their feelings, and that you think this is a bad thing. 受抑制的;受约束的;拘谨的 [disapproval]

  • We are rather inhibited about touching each other. [+ about]

    我们对身体接触感觉很不自在。

SYN shy, reserved, guarded, withdrawn
