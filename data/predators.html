predator
predətəʳ ˈprɛdətər
Word forms: plural predators
1 N-COUNT 捕食性动物 A predator is an animal that kills and eats other animals. 捕食性动物
2 N-COUNT 掠夺者；掠夺成性的人（或组织） People sometimes refer to predatory people or organizations as predators . 掠夺者；掠夺成性的人（或组织）

  • The company is worried about takeovers by various predators.

    公司担心被各种虎视眈眈的集团收购。
