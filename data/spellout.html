spell out
1 PHRASAL VERB 详细说明;讲清楚 If you spell something out, you explain it in detail or in a very clear way. 详细说明;讲清楚

  • Be assertive and spell out exactly how you feel. [VERB PREP. noun]

    要自信点儿，把自己的感受讲清楚。

  • How many times do I have to spell it out? [VERB noun PREP.]

    我要仔仔细细讲多少遍？

SYN make clear or plain, specify, make explicit, clarify
