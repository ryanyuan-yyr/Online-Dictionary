pan
pæn pɑːn pɑn pæn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pans, present participle panning, past tense , past participle panned
1 N-COUNT 平底锅 A pan is a round metal container with a long handle, which is used for cooking things in, usually on top of a cooker or stove. 平底锅

  • Heat the butter and oil in a large pan.

    把黄油和食用油放到大平底锅里加热。

SYN pot, vessel, container, saucepan  
2 N-COUNT 烤盘 A pan is a shallow metal container used for baking foods. 烤盘 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 baking tray
3 VERB [usually passive] 抨击，严厉批评（影片、书籍等） If something such as a film or a book is panned by journalists, they say it is very bad. 抨击，严厉批评（影片、书籍等） [informal]

  • His first high-budget movie, called 'Brain Donors', was panned by the critics. [be VERB-ed]

    他的第一部高成本制作电影《撞板三舞男》遭到恶评。

SYN criticize, knock [informal] , blast, hammer [British , informal]  
4 V-ERG 移动拍摄；摇摄 If you pan a film or television camera or if it pans somewhere, it moves slowly round so that a wide area is filmed. 移动拍摄；摇摄 [Also VERB]

  • The camera panned along the line of players. [VERB prep./adv.]

    摄像机沿着那排选手移动拍摄。

  • He panned over the crowd for a few minutes before swivelling back to the right. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他从头到尾移动拍摄了那群人好几分钟，才转回右侧。

  • A television camera panned the stadium. [VERB noun]

    一台电视摄像机摇摄整座体育场。

  • He panned the camera, giving a sense of motion. [VERB noun]

    他用摄像机移动拍摄，营造一种运动的效果。

SYN move along or across, follow, track, sweep  
5 VERB 用淘选盘淘（金） If someone pans for gold, they use a shallow metal container to try to find small pieces of gold from a river. 用淘选盘淘（金）

  • People came westward in the 1800s to pan for gold. [VERB + for]

    在19世纪，人们前往西部淘金。

  • Every year they panned about a ton and a half of gold. [VERB noun]

    每年他们淘出大约1.5吨金子。

SYN sift out, look for, wash, search for

PREFIX （用于形容词和名词前构成新的形容词和名词）表示“全”，“泛” pan- is added to the beginning of adjectives and nouns to form other adjectives and nouns that describe something as being connected with all places or people of a particular kind. （用于形容词和名词前构成新的形容词和名词）表示“全”，“泛”

  • ...a pan-European defence system.

    泛欧洲防御体系

  • ...the ideology of pan-Arabism.

    泛阿拉伯主义思想
