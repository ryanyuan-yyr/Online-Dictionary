patience
peɪʃəns ˈpeɪʃəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 容忍；耐心；忍耐力 If you have patience, you are able to stay calm and not get annoyed, for example when something takes a long time, or when someone is not doing what you want them to do. 容忍；耐心；忍耐力

  • He doesn't have the patience to wait.

    他没有耐心等待。

  • It was exacting work and required all his patience.

    工作很艰巨，需要他有极大的耐心。

2 N-UNCOUNT 单人纸牌游戏 Patience is a card game for only one player. 单人纸牌游戏 [British]

  • He would often sit and play patience.

    他常常坐着玩单人纸牌游戏。

in AM, use 美国英语用 solitaire
3 PHRASE 考验…的耐心；使忍无可忍 If someone tries your patience or tests your patience, they annoy you so much that it is very difficult for you to stay calm. 考验…的耐心；使忍无可忍

  • He tended to stutter, which tried her patience.

    他老结巴，这叫她难以忍受。

  • I feel that she would try the patience of a saint.

    我觉得圣人都能被她逼疯。
