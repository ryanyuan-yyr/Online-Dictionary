cuddly
kʌdəli ˈkʌdli
Word forms: comparative cuddlier, superlative cuddliest
1 ADJ-GRADED (人或动物)令人想拥抱的，惹人怜爱的 A cuddly person or animal makes you want to cuddle them. (人或动物)令人想拥抱的，惹人怜爱的 [approval]

  • He is a small, cuddly man with spectacles.

    他戴着眼镜，是个令人想拥抱一下的小个子男人。

  • ...these cuddly creatures from South East Asia.

    这些惹人怜爱的东南亚动物

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (动物玩具)柔软的，适合搂抱的 Cuddly toys are soft toys that look like animals. (动物玩具)柔软的，适合搂抱的
