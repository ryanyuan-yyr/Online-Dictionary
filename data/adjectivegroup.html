adjective group
Word forms: plural adjective groups
N-COUNT 形容词词组（如very nice，interested in football，也可为单个形容词） An adjective group or adjectival group is a group of words based on an adjective, such as 'very nice' or 'interested in football'. An adjective group can also consist simply of an adjective. 形容词词组（如very nice，interested in football，也可为单个形容词）
