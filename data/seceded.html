secede
sɪsiːd sɪˈsid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense secedes, present participle seceding, past tense , past participle seceded
VERB (从国家、大集团中正式)退出，脱离，分离 If a region or group secedes from the country or larger group to which it belongs, it formally becomes a separate country or stops being a member of the larger group. (从国家、大集团中正式)退出，脱离，分离

  • Singapore seceded from the Federation of Malaysia and became an independent sovereign state. [VERB + from]

    新加坡脱离马来西亚联邦成为一个独立的主权国家。

  • On 20 August 1960 Senegal seceded. [VERB]

    塞内加尔于 1960年 8 月 20 日独立出去。

SYN withdraw, leave, resign, separate
