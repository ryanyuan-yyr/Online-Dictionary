epiphany
ɪpɪfəni
Word forms: plural epiphanies
1 N-UNCOUNT （基督教1月6日为纪念贤士朝拜基督的）主显节，显现节 Epiphany is a Christian festival on the 6th of January which celebrates the arrival of the wise men who came to see Jesus Christ soon after he was born. （基督教1月6日为纪念贤士朝拜基督的）主显节，显现节
2 N-COUNT 顿悟；突然明白 An epiphany is a moment of sudden insight or understanding. 顿悟；突然明白

  • ...Isaac Newton's epiphany about gravity and a falling apple.

    艾萨克·牛顿关于地心引力和苹果落地的顿悟
