appreciate
əpriːʃieɪt əˈpriʃiˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense appreciates, present participle appreciating, past tense , past participle appreciated
1 VERB 欣赏；鉴赏；赏识 If you appreciate something, for example a piece of music or good food, you like it because you recognize its good qualities. 欣赏；鉴赏；赏识

  • Anyone can appreciate our music. [VERB noun]

    任何人都能欣赏我们的音乐。

  • In time you'll appreciate the beauty and subtlety of this language. [VERB noun]

    总有一天你会体会到这门语言的优美和微妙之处。

SYN enjoy, like, value, regard  
2 VERB 领会；了解；认识 If you appreciate a situation or problem, you understand it and know what it involves. 领会；了解；认识

  • She never really appreciated the depth and bitterness of the Irish conflict. [VERB noun]

    她从来没有真正认识到爱尔兰矛盾的深刻和激烈。

  • He appreciates that co-operation with the media is part of his professional duties. [VERB that]

    他懂得与媒体合作是自己工作职责的一部分。

SYN be aware of, know, understand, estimate  
3 VERB 感谢;感激 If you appreciate something that someone has done for you or is going to do for you, you are grateful for it. 感谢;感激

  • Peter stood by me when I most needed it. I'll always appreciate that. [VERB noun]

    当我最需要的时候，彼得在我身边支持我。我将永存感激。

  • Thanks, lads. I appreciate it. [VERB noun]

    谢了，哥们儿。我很感激。

  • I'd appreciate it if you wouldn't mention it. [VERB noun]

    如果你不提它，我会很感激。

SYN be grateful for, be obliged for, be thankful for, give thanks for  
4 VERB 升值；增值 If something that you own appreciates over a period of time, its value increases. 升值；增值

  • They don't have any confidence that houses will appreciate in value. [VERB]

    他们完全不相信房子会升值。

SYN increase, rise, grow, gain
