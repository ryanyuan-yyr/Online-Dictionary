open-air
(also open air)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 户外的An open-air place or event is outside rather than in a building. 户外的

  • ...an open air concert in brilliant sunshine.

    …明媚阳光下的一场露天音乐会。

2 N-SING 户外If you are in the open air, you are outside rather than in a building. 户外

  • We sleep out under the stars, and eat our meals in the open air.

    我们露宿在星空下，在户外用餐。
