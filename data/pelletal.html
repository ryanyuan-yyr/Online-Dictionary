pellet
pelɪt ˈpɛlɪt
Word forms: plural pellets
N-COUNT 小团；小球；丸 A pellet is a small ball of paper, mud, lead, or other material. 小团；小球；丸

  • He was shot in the head by an air gun pellet.

    他被气枪弹丸击中头部。

  • A beetle was rolling a pellet of dried dung up the hill.

    一只屎壳郎正在把一个干粪球往山上滚。

SYN little ball, little piece
