surcharge
sɜːʳtʃɑːʳdʒ ˈsɜrˌtʃɑrdʒ sərˈtʃɑrdʒ
Word forms: plural surcharges
1 N-COUNT 额外费用;附加费;增收费 A surcharge is an extra payment of money in addition to the usual payment for something. It is added for a specific reason, for example by a company because costs have risen or by a government as a tax. 额外费用;附加费;增收费

  • The government introduced a 15% surcharge on imports.

    政府对进口产品加收 15 %的附加费。

  • The prices of overseas holidays are subject to surcharges.

    海外度假游的价格受各种附加费的影响。
