unflinching
ʌnflɪntʃɪŋ ʌnˈflɪntʃɪŋ ʌnˈflɪntʃɪŋ
ADJ 不退缩的；坚定的 You can use unflinching in expressions such as unflinching honesty and unflinching support to indicate that a good quality which someone has is strong and steady, and never weakens. 不退缩的；坚定的

  • ...the armed forces, all of whom had pledged their unflinching support and loyalty to the government.

    武装力量一致保证坚决支持并效忠政府

SYN determined, firm, steady, constant  
unflinchingly

  • They were unflinchingly loyal to their friends.

    他们对朋友忠贞不渝。
