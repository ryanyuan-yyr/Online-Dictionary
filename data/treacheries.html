treachery
tretʃəri ˈtrɛtʃəri
Word forms: plural treacheries
N-UNCOUNT 背叛;变节;背信弃义 Treachery is behaviour or an action in which someone betrays their country or betrays a person who trusts them. 背叛;变节;背信弃义

  • He was deeply wounded by the treachery of close aides and old friends.

    他因贴身助手和老朋友的背叛而深受伤害。

SYN betrayal, infidelity, treason, duplicity
