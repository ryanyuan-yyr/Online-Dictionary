italic
ɪtælɪk ɪˈtælɪk aɪˈtælɪk
Word forms: plural italics
1 N-PLURAL 斜体;斜体字 Italics are letters which slope to the right. Italics are often used to emphasize a particular word or sentence. The examples in this dictionary are printed in italics. 斜体;斜体字
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 斜体的 Italic letters slope to the right. 斜体的

  • She addressed them by hand in her beautiful italic script.

    她用漂亮的斜体字写明了收信人姓名和地址。
