munch
mʌntʃ mʌntʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense munches, present participle munching, past tense , past participle munched
VERB 用力嚼;出声地嚼 If you munch food, you eat it by chewing it slowly, thoroughly, and rather noisily. 用力嚼;出声地嚼 [Also V + away at/on]

  • Luke munched the chicken sandwiches. [VERB noun]

    卢克津津有味地吃着鸡肉三明治。

  • Across the table, his son Benjie munched appreciatively. [VERB]

    桌子对面，他儿子本吉有滋有味地吃着。

  • Sheep were munching their way through a yellow carpet of leaves. [V + way through]

    绵羊边吃边走，穿行在厚厚的一层黄色落叶中间。

SYN chew, champ, crunch, chomp
