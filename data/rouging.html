rouge
ruːʒ ruʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rouges, present participle rouging, past tense , past participle rouged
1 N-UNCOUNT 胭脂;腮红 Rouge is a red powder or cream which women and actors can put on their cheeks in order to give them more colour. 胭脂;腮红 [old-fashioned]
2 VERB 涂胭脂;抹腮红;涂口红 If a woman or an actor rouges their cheeks or lips, they put red powder or cream on them to give them more colour. 涂胭脂;抹腮红;涂口红

  • Florentine women rouged their earlobes. [VERB noun]

    佛罗伦萨的女人在耳垂上涂胭脂。

  • She had curly black hair and rouged cheeks. [VERB-ed]

    她的头发乌黑卷曲，脸上涂了腮红。
