broker
broʊkəʳ ˈbroʊkər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense brokers, present participle brokering, past tense , past participle brokered
1 N-COUNT (股票、外汇或商品）经纪人，代理商 A broker is a person whose job is to buy and sell shares, foreign money, or goods for other people. (股票、外汇或商品）经纪人，代理商 [business]
SYN dealer, marketer, agent, trader  
2 VERB （国家或政府作为中间人）协调，安排 If a country or government brokers an agreement, a ceasefire, or a round of talks, they try to negotiate or arrange it. （国家或政府作为中间人）协调，安排

  • The United Nations brokered a peace in Mogadishu at the end of March. [VERB noun]

    3月末，联合国出面在摩加迪沙进行了和平调解。

SYN organize, run, plan, set up
