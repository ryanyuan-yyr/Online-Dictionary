tint
tɪnt tɪnt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense tints, present participle tinting, past tense , past participle tinted
1 N-COUNT (少许)色彩；色调 A tint is a small amount of colour. (少许)色彩；色调

  • Its large leaves often show a delicate purple tint.

    它的大叶子往往带点紫色。

  • Green has many different shades and tints.

    绿色分很多不同的色度与色调。

SYN shade, colour, tone, hue  
2 N-COUNT 染发剂;染发 If you put a tint on your hair, you dye it a slightly different colour. 染发剂;染发

  • You've had a tint on your hair.

    你已经染过头发了。

  • Haircuts cost £2, tints £6.

    理发 2 英镑，染发 6 英镑。

SYN dye, wash, stain, rinse  
3 VERB [usually passive] 将…轻微染色;使略有…颜色 If something is tinted, it has a small amount of a particular colour or dye in it. 将…轻微染色;使略有…颜色

  • Eyebrows can be tinted with the same dye. [be VERB-ed]

    眉毛可以染成同样的颜色。

  • Glass bottles are filled with water tinted with food colourings… [VERB-ed]

    玻璃瓶中盛满了被食用色素染过色的水。

  • Most of the dirt was on the outside of the tinted glass. [VERB-ed]

    大部分灰尘在有色玻璃的外部。

SYN dye, colour, stain, rinse  
-tinted

  • He wore green-tinted glasses.

    他戴绿色镜片的眼镜。
