mope around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 mope about
PHRASAL VERB 没精打采地走;闷闷不乐地闲荡 If you mope around or mope about a place, you wander around there not doing anything, looking and feeling unhappy. 没精打采地走;闷闷不乐地闲荡

  • He moped around the office for a while, feeling bored. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他在办公室闲荡了一会儿，感到百无聊赖。

  • He mopes about all day. [VERB PREP.]

    他整天游手好闲。
