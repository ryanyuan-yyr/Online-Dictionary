steering
stɪərɪŋ ˈstɪərɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT (车辆等的)转向装置 The steering in a car or other vehicle is the mechanical parts of it which make it possible to steer. (车辆等的)转向装置
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (委员会或小组)筹划指导的 A steering committee or a steering group is a group of people that organizes the early stages of a project, and makes sure it progresses in a satisfactory way. (委员会或小组)筹划指导的

  • There will be an economic steering committee with representatives of each of the republics.

    将设立一个由各共和国代表组成的经济指导委员会。
