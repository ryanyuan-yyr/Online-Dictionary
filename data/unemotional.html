unemotional
ʌnɪmoʊʃənəl
ADJ-GRADED 不动感情的；无动于衷的；冷漠的；平静的 If you describe someone as unemotional, you mean that they do not show any feelings. 不动感情的；无动于衷的；冷漠的；平静的

  • British men are often seen as being reserved and unemotional.

    英国男人常被认为是矜持而冷漠的。

  • She began to read in a brisk, unemotional voice.

    她念了起来，语调利落而平静。

  • I know it's nothing serious and I feel quite unemotional about it.

    我知道那根本没什么大不了的，所以有些无动于衷。

SYN impassive, cold, reserved, cool  
unemotionally

  • 'I'd like to have their names,' said Johnson unemotionally.

    “我想知道他们叫什么，”约翰逊平静地说道。

  • McKinnon looked at him unemotionally.

    麦金农不动声色地看着他。
