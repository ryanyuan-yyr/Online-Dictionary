intrusive
ɪntruːsɪv ɪntrusɪv
ADJ-GRADED 打扰的;侵扰的 Something that is intrusive disturbs your mood or your life in a way you do not like. 打扰的;侵扰的

  • The cameras were not an intrusive presence.

    那些摄像机的存在并不令人反感。

  • Staff are courteous but never intrusive.

    员工谦恭有礼却从不让人感到唐突。

SYN interfering, disturbing, invasive, unwanted
