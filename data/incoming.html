incoming
ɪnkʌmɪŋ ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (短信、电话)发过来的,打进来的 An incoming message or phone call is one that you receive. (短信、电话)发过来的,打进来的

  • We keep a tape of incoming calls.

    我们对来电进行录音。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (飞机、乘客)即将到达的 An incoming plane or passenger is one that is arriving at a place. (飞机、乘客)即将到达的

  • The airport was closed for incoming flights.

    机场对进港航班关闭了。

SYN arriving, landing, approaching, entering  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (官员、政府)新当选的，新上任的，新一任的 An incoming official or government is one that has just been appointed or elected. (官员、政府)新当选的，新上任的，新一任的

  • ...the problems confronting the incoming government.

    新一届政府面临的问题

SYN new, next, succeeding, elected  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] (潮汐、波浪)涌向岸边的 An incoming tide or wave is coming towards the shore. (潮汐、波浪)涌向岸边的
