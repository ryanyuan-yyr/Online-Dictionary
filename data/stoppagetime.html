stoppage time
N-UNCOUNT (足球等比赛中的)伤停补时In football and some other sports, stoppage time is the period of time that is added to the end of a game because play was stopped during the game as a result of, for example, injuries to players. (足球等比赛中的)伤停补时 [mainly British]

  • ...a spectacular goal in stoppage time.

    ...补时阶段的一个精彩进球。
