chunky
tʃʌŋki ˈtʃʌŋki
Word forms: comparative chunkier, superlative chunkiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人)粗壮的，背阔胸宽的 A chunky person is broad and heavy. (人)粗壮的，背阔胸宽的

  • The soprano was a chunky girl from California.

    女高音歌手是个来自加利福尼亚的壮硕姑娘。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 粗重的;大而重的 A chunky object is large and thick. 粗重的;大而重的

  • Her taste in fiction was for chunky historical romances.

    她喜欢的小说是大部头的历史言情故事。

  • ...a chunky sweater.

    厚实的套头毛衣

  • ...chunky jewellery.

    沉甸甸的首饰
