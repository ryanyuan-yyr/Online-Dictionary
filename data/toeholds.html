toehold
toʊhoʊld ˈtoʊˌhoʊld
(also toe-hold)Word forms: plural toeholds
N-COUNT [usually singular] 初步的立足点 If you have a toehold in a situation, you have managed to gain an uncertain position or a small amount of power in it, which you hope will give you the opportunity to get a better or more powerful position. 初步的立足点 [Also + on]

  • Mitsubishi Motors were anxious to get a toehold in the European market. [+ in]

    三菱汽车急于在欧洲市场上取得初步的立足之地。
