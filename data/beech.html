beech
biːtʃ bitʃ
Word forms: plural beeches
1 N-VAR 山毛榉 A beech or a beech tree is a tree with a smooth grey trunk. 山毛榉

  • ...the branch of a huge beech.

    一棵巨型山毛榉的树枝

2 N-UNCOUNT 山毛榉木材 Beech is the wood of this tree. 山毛榉木材

  • The worktop is made of solid beech.

    操作台是由结实的山毛榉木做成的。
