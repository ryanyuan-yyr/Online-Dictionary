chancellor
ˈtʃɑːnsələ ˈtʃænsələr
1 N-TITLE; N-COUNT (德国或奥地利的)总理 Chancellor is the title of the head of government in Germany and Austria. (德国或奥地利的)总理

  • ...Chancellor Gerhard Schröder of Germany.

    德国总理施罗德

  • ...as the Chancellor arrived.

    总理到达的时候

2 N-COUNT (英国的)财政大臣 In Britain, the Chancellor is the Chancellor of the Exchequer. (英国的)财政大臣
3 N-COUNT (英国大学的)名誉校长 The Chancellor of a British university is the official head of the university. The Chancellor does not take part in running the university. (英国大学的)名誉校长
4 N-COUNT (美国某些大学的)校长 The head of some American universities is called the Chancellor . (美国某些大学的)校长
5 See also: vice-chancellor
