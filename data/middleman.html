middleman
mɪdəlmæn ˈmɪdəlˌmæn
Word forms: plural middlemen
1 N-COUNT 经纪人;中间商；经销商 A middleman is a person or company which buys things from the people who produce them and sells them to the people who want to buy them. 经纪人;中间商；经销商 [business]

  • Why don't they cut out the middleman and let us do it ourselves?

    他们为什么不甩开中间商，让我们自己干呢？

SYN intermediary, broker, entrepreneur, distributor  
2 N-COUNT 中间人;掮客；调解人 A middleman is a person who helps in negotiations between people who are unwilling to meet each other directly. 中间人;掮客；调解人

  • The two sides would only meet indirectly, through middlemen.

    双方只会通过中间人间接接触。
