give up on
PHRASAL VERB 对…绝望;对…不抱希望 If you give up on something or someone, you decide that you will never succeed in doing what you want to with them, and you stop trying to. 对…绝望;对…不抱希望

  • He urged them not to give up on peace efforts. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他力劝他们不要放弃对和平的努力。

  • My teachers gave up on me. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    老师们对我不抱任何希望了。
