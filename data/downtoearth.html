down-to-earth ˈdaʊntəˈɜrθ
ADJ-GRADED 现实的;务实的;切合实际的 If you say that someone is down-to-earth, you approve of the fact that they concern themselves with practical things and actions, rather than with abstract theories. 现实的;务实的;切合实际的 [approval]

  • Gloria is probably the most down to earth person I've ever met.

    格洛丽亚也许是我见过的最脚踏实地的人了。

  • ...her sincerity and her down-to-earth common sense.

    她的真诚和切合实际的判断力

  • Their ideas seem to be far more down to earth and sensible.

    他们的想法貌似更务实也更合理。

SYN sensible, practical, realistic, common-sense
