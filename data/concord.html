concord
kɒŋkɔːʳd ˈkɒnkɔːd ˈkɑnˌkɔrd ˈkɑŋkɔrd
1 N-UNCOUNT 和谐;协调 Concord is a state of peaceful agreement. 和谐;协调 [formal]

  • They expressed the hope that he would pursue a neutral and balanced policy for the sake of national concord.

    他们希望，为了国家的和谐，他会奉行中立、不偏不倚的政策。

SYN harmony, accord, peace, agreement  
2 N-UNCOUNT (语法中数、性等的)一致 In grammar, concord refers to the way that a word has a form appropriate to the number or gender of the noun or pronoun it relates to. For example, in 'He hates it', there is concord between the singular form of the verb and the singular pronoun 'he'. (语法中数、性等的)一致
