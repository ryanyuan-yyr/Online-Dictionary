tenterhooks
tentəʳhʊks ˈtɛntəhʊks
PHRASE 紧张不安;提心吊胆 If you are on tenterhooks, you are very nervous and excited because you are wondering what is going to happen in a particular situation. 紧张不安;提心吊胆

  • He was still on tenterhooks waiting for his directors' decision about the job.

    他仍紧张不安地等待着主任们对他的工作作出的决定。
