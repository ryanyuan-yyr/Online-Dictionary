mat
mæt mæt
Word forms: plural mats
1 N-COUNT (杯、盘的)垫,垫子 A mat is a small piece of something such as cloth, card, or plastic which you put on a table to protect it from plates or cups. (杯、盘的)垫,垫子

  • The food is served on polished tables with mats.

    饭菜摆放在擦得铮亮、铺着衬垫的餐桌上。

SYN table mat, coaster, place mat, doily  
2 N-COUNT 地毯;地垫;地席 A mat is a small piece of carpet or other thick material which is put on the floor for protection, decoration, or comfort. 地毯;地垫;地席

  • There was a letter on the mat.

    地毯上有一封信。

  • Bring a sleeping bag and foam mat.

    带上睡袋和泡沫垫。

SYN carpet, runner, rug, doormat  
3 N-COUNT [with supplement, usually NOUN of n] 一簇;一丛;一团 A mat of something such as grass or moss is a thick, untidy layer of it. 一簇;一丛;一团

  • The houses are well spaced out, each on its own plot of ground and mat of coarse grass.

    房屋稀稀疏疏地坐落着，每栋都有自己的一片地和粗草坪。

  • She touched the thick mat of sandy hair on his chest.

    她摸着他那浓密的淡棕色胸毛。

SYN mass, shock, cluster, tangle
