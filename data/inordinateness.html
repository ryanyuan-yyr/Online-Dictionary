inordinate
ɪnɔːʳdɪnɪt ɪnˈɔːdɪnɪt ɪnˈɔrdənɪt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 极度的;非常的;过度的 If you describe something as inordinate, you are emphasizing that it is unusually or excessively great in amount or degree. 极度的;非常的;过度的 [formal, emphasis]

  • They spend an inordinate amount of time talking.

    他们花在说上的时间太多了。

  • ...their inordinate number of pets.

    他们过多的宠物

SYN excessive, unreasonable, disproportionate, extravagant  
inordinately

  • He is inordinately proud of his wife's achievements.

    他对妻子的成就骄傲非常。
