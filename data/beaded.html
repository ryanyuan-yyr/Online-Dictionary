beaded
biːdɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 饰以小珠的；带珠子的 A beaded dress, cushion, or other object is decorated with beads. 饰以小珠的；带珠子的
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n] 布满（某种液体的）液滴的 If something is beaded with a liquid, it is covered in small drops of that liquid. 布满（某种液体的）液滴的

  • The man's bald head was beaded with sweat.

    这个男人的秃头上满是汗珠。

  • The cave walls were beaded with moisture.

    这个洞穴壁上布满了水珠。
