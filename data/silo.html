silo
saɪloʊ ˈsaɪloʊ
Word forms: plural silos
1 N-COUNT (农场储存草料、谷物等的)筒仓 A silo is a tall round metal tower on a farm, in which grass, grain, or some other substance is stored. (农场储存草料、谷物等的)筒仓

  • Before silos were invented, cows gave less milk during winter because they had no green grass to eat.

    筒仓发明前，因为没有青草可以吃，奶牛在冬天产奶量减少。

  • ...a grain silo.

    盛谷物的筒仓

2 N-COUNT (核导弹等的)发射井 A silo is a specially built place underground where a nuclear missile is kept. (核导弹等的)发射井

  • ...underground nuclear missile silos.

    地下核导弹发射井
