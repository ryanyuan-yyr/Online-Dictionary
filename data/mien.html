mien
miːn min
N-SING [usually poss NOUN] 仪表；举止；神态；（尤指）面部表情 Someone's mien is their general appearance and manner, especially the expression on their face, which shows what they are feeling or thinking. 仪表；举止；神态；（尤指）面部表情 [literary]

  • It was impossible to tell from his mien whether he was offended.

    从他的神态中难以看出他是否生气了。

  • ...his mild manner and aristocratic mien.

    他温文尔雅的举止和高贵风度

SYN demeanour, look, air, bearing
