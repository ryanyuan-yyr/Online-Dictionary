subdivision
sʌbdɪvɪʒən ˌsʌbdɪˈvɪʒən ˈsʌbdɪˌvɪʒən
(also sub-division)Word forms: plural subdivisions
1 N-COUNT 细分部分;分支;分部 A subdivision is an area, part, or section of something which is itself a part of something larger. 细分部分;分支;分部

  • Months are a conventional subdivision of the year.

    通常可将一年再按月细分。

2 N-COUNT 分割成的小块土地(供开发住房) A subdivision is an area of land for building houses on. 分割成的小块土地(供开发住房) [US]

  • Rammick lives high on a ridge in a 400-home subdivision.

    拉米克住在高耸的山脊上一片有 400 户人家的地方。
