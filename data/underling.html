underling
ʌndəʳlɪŋ ˈʌndərlɪŋ
Word forms: plural underlings
N-COUNT 跟班儿；手下；羽翼；喽啰；走卒 You refer to someone as an underling when they are inferior in rank or status to someone else and take orders from them. You use this word to show that you do not respect someone. 跟班儿；手下；羽翼；喽啰；走卒 [disapproval]

  • Every underling feared him.

    所有的手下都怕他。

  • ...underlings who do the dirty work.

    行卑劣之事的走卒

SYN subordinate, inferior, minion, servant
