deportment
dɪpɔːʳtmənt diˈpɔrtmənt dɪˈpɔrtmənt
N-UNCOUNT 行为;举止;(尤指)步态，仪态 Your deportment is the way you behave, especially the way you walk and move. 行为;举止;(尤指)步态，仪态 [formal]

  • Deportment and poise were as important as good marks for young ladies.

    对妙龄少女来说，仪态身姿和优秀成绩一样重要。

SYN bearing, conduct, behaviour, manner
