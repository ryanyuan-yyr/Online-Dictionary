blank out
PHRASAL VERB 不再去想；刻意忘却 If you blank out a particular feeling or thought, you do not allow yourself to experience that feeling or to have that thought. 不再去想；刻意忘却

  • I learned to blank those feelings out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我学着刻意忘记那些感觉。

  • I was trying to blank out previous situations from my mind. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我试图将先前的境遇从大脑中抹去。

SYN forget, overlook, think no more of, consign to oblivion
