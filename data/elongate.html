elongate
iːlɒŋgeɪt ɪlɔːŋ- iˈlɔŋˌgeɪt ɪˈlɔŋˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense elongates, present participle elongating, past tense , past participle elongated
V-ERG （使）拉长;（使）延长 If you elongate something or if it elongates, you stretch it so that it becomes longer. （使）拉长;（使）延长 [formal]

  • 'Mom,' she intoned, elongating the word. [VERB noun]

    “妈妈，”她拖长了声音，平静地说道。

  • Corn is treated when the stalk starts to elongate. [VERB]

    在玉米秆开始拔节时，给玉米打药。

SYN lengthen, extend, stretch (out), make longer
