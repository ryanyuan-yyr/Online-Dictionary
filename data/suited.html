suited
suːtɪd ˈsuːtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] [ADJ to-infinitive] 合适的;适宜的;适当的 If something is well suited to a particular purpose, it is right or appropriate for that purpose. If someone is well suited to a particular job, they are right or appropriate for that job. 合适的;适宜的;适当的

  • The area is well suited to road cycling as well as off-road riding. [+ to]

    这个地方很适合举行自行车公路赛和越野赛。

  • Satellites are uniquely suited to provide this information. [+ to]

    只有人造卫星能提供此类信息。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ, usually adverb ADJ, oft ADJ to n] (尤指情侣)般配的，志同道合的 If two people, especially a man and a woman, are well suited, they are likely to have a successful relationship because they have similar personalities or interests. (尤指情侣)般配的，志同道合的

  • They were well suited to each other.

    他们俩是天造地设的一对。
