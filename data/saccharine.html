saccharine
sækərɪn -riːn ˈsækəˌraɪn ˈsækəˌrɪn ˈsækəˌraɪn
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 甜得发腻的;过于感伤的;故作多情的 You describe something as saccharine when you find it unpleasantly sweet and sentimental. 甜得发腻的;过于感伤的;故作多情的 [disapproval]

  • ...a saccharine sequel to the Peter Pan story.

    《彼得·潘》这个故事煽情造作的续集

  • She smiled with saccharine sweetness.

    她的笑容太嗲了。

SYN sickly, honeyed [poetic] , sentimental, sugary
