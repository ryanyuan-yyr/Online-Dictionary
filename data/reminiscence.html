reminiscence
remɪnɪsəns ˌrɛməˈnɪsəns
Word forms: plural reminiscences
N-VAR [oft poss NOUN] 回忆;旧事;追忆（的往事） Someone's reminiscences are things that they remember from the past, and which they talk or write about. Reminiscence is the process of remembering these things and talking or writing about them. 回忆;旧事;追忆（的往事） [formal] [Also + of]

  • Here I am boring you with my reminiscences.

    我要拿旧事来烦你了。

  • ...reminiscences of her Jewish childhood.

    她童年犹太生活的旧事

  • A faint smile of reminiscence appeared on her face..

    一丝怀旧的微笑浮现在她的脸上。
