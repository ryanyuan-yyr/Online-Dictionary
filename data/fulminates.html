fulminate
fʊlmɪneɪt fʌl- ˈfʌlmɪˌneɪt ˈfʌlməˌneɪt ˈfʊlməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fulminates, present participle fulminating, past tense , past participle fulminated
VERB 强烈谴责;怒斥 If you fulminate against someone or something, you criticize them angrily. 强烈谴责;怒斥 [formal]

  • They all fulminated against the new curriculum. [V + against/about]

    他们都对新课表表示强烈不满。

SYN criticize, rage, curse, denounce
