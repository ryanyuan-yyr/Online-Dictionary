sultry
sʌltri ˈsʌltri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 湿热的;闷热的 Sultry weather is hot and damp. 湿热的;闷热的 [written]

  • The climax came one sultry August evening.

    在 8 月一个闷热的夜晚，高潮到来了。

SYN humid, close, hot, sticky  
2 ADJ-GRADED 迷人的;撩人的;风情万种的 Someone who is sultry is attractive in a way that suggests hidden passion. 迷人的;撩人的;风情万种的 [written]

  • ...a dark-haired sultry woman.

    性感迷人的黑发女郎

SYN seductive, sexy [informal] , sensual, voluptuous
