fill in
1 PHRASAL VERB 填写If you fill in a form or other document requesting information, you write information in the spaces on it. 填写 [mainly British]

  • Fill in the coupon and send it first class to the address shown. [VERB PREP. noun]

    填写好赠券，用一类邮件寄到所显示的地址。

SYN complete, answer, fill up, fill out [US]  
2 PHRASAL VERB 涂满If you fill in a shape, you cover the area inside the lines with colour or shapes so that none of the background is showing. 涂满

  • With a lip pencil, outline lips and fill them in. [VERB noun PREP.]

    用一支唇线笔画出唇线，然后涂上口红。

3 PHRASAL VERB 提供详情If you fill someone in, you give them more details about something that you know about. 提供详情 [informal]

  • He filled her in on Wilbur Kantor's visit. [V n P + on]

    他告诉了她威尔伯·坎特造访的详情。

SYN inform, acquaint, advise of, apprise of  
4 PHRASAL VERB 临时顶替If you fill in for someone, you do the work or task that they normally do because they are unable to do it. 临时顶替

  • Vice-presidents' wives would fill in for first ladies. [VERB PREP. + for]

    副总统们的妻子会临时顶替第一夫人们。
