quality of life ˈkwɒlɪtɪ əv laɪf
N-SING [the/poss N] 生活质量Someone's quality of life is the extent to which their life is comfortable or satisfying. 生活质量

  • "Would you go back to England?"—"Never, the quality of life is so much better here."

    “你会再回到英格兰吗？”—“绝不，这里的生活质量要好太多了。”

  • A vibrant and growing economy does not improve everyone's quality of life.

    一个生机勃发的经济体并不能提升每个人的生活质量。
