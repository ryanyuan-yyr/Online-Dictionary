unescorted
ʌnɪskɔːtɪd
ADJ [ADJ n, ADJ after verb, verb-link ADJ] 无人护送的；没人陪同的 If someone or something is unescorted, they are not protected or supervised. 无人护送的；没人陪同的

  • Unescorted children are not allowed beyond this point.

    小孩无人陪同不允许走出这个地方。

  • He worked as a dancing partner for unescorted ladies at Manhattan cabarets.

    他的工作是在曼哈顿的夜总会陪没有舞伴的女士跳舞。

  • The President's unescorted vehicle was ambushed just outside the capital.

    刚出首都，总统无人护卫的汽车就遭到了伏击。

    他们建议外国代表在没人陪同时不要在城镇四处游逛。
