rend
rend rɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rends, present participle rending, past tense , past participle rent
1 VERB 撕碎;撕裂;扯破 To rend something means to tear it. 撕碎;撕裂;扯破 [literary]

  • ...pain that rends the heart. [VERB noun]

    钻心的疼痛

  • ...a twisted urge to rend and tear. [VERB]

    反常的狂乱撕扯的冲动

SYN tear, break, split, rip  
2 VERB (声音)响彻，刺破 If a loud sound rends the air, it is sudden and violent. (声音)响彻，刺破 [literary]

  • He bellows, rends the air with anguish. [VERB noun]

    他咆哮着，痛苦的喊声划破长空。

3 See also: heart-rending
