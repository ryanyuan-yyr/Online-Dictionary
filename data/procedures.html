procedure

1 N-VAR 程序；步骤；手续 A procedure is a way of doing something, especially the usual or correct way. 程序；步骤；手续

  • A biopsy is usually a minor surgical procedure.

    活组织检查通常是一个很小的外科手术。

  • Police insist that Michael did not follow the correct procedure in applying for a visa.

    警方坚持称迈克尔没有按照正确的程序申请签证。

  • The White House said there would be no change in procedure.

    白宫说程序上不会发生变化。
