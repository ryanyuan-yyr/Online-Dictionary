subsume
səbsjuːm səbˈsjuːm -suːm səbˈsum sʌbˈsjum
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense subsumes, present participle subsuming, past tense , past participle subsumed
VERB 将…归入，将…纳入(更大的组织或范畴) If something is subsumed within a larger group or class, it is included within it, rather than being considered as something separate. 将…归入，将…纳入(更大的组织或范畴) [formal]

  • After that the two alliances might be subsumed into a new European security system. [be VERB-ed prep.]

    从那以后，这两个联盟将可能会被纳入一个新的欧洲安全体系之中。

  • Scottish football would be subsumed by a British League.

    苏格兰足球将被纳入某个英国足球联赛。
