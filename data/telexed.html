telex
teleks ˈtɛlˌɛks
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense telexes, present participle telexing, past tense , past participle telexed
1 N-UNCOUNT 电传系统(通过电力或无线电信号收发信息的通讯方式) Telex is an international system of sending written messages. Messages are converted into signals which are transmitted, either by electricity or by radio signals, and then printed out by a machine in another place. 电传系统(通过电力或无线电信号收发信息的通讯方式)
2 N-COUNT 电传机;电传收发机 A telex is a machine that transmits and receives telex messages. 电传机;电传收发机
3 N-COUNT 电传;通过电传收发的信息 A telex is a message that you send or that has been received and printed by telex. 电传;通过电传收发的信息

  • He sent a telex to the British High Commission in Delhi.

    他给德里的英国高级专员公署发了份电传。

4 VERB 给…发电传;用电传发送 If you telex a message to someone, you send it to them by telex. 给…发电传;用电传发送

  • The embassy says it has telexed their demands to the foreign ministry. [VERB noun + to]

    大使馆表示已通过电传将他们的要求发送给外交部了。

  • They telexed British Airways. [VERB noun]

    他们给英国航空公司发了电传。
