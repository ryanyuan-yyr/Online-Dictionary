abreast
əbrest əˈbrɛst
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 并排，并肩（行进） If people or things walk or move abreast, they are next to each other, side by side, and facing in the same direction. 并排，并肩（行进）

  • The steep pavement was too narrow for them to walk abreast.

    陡坡太窄，他们没法并排走。

  • ...a group of youths riding their motorbikes four abreast.

    驾着摩托车，四个一排齐头并进的一群年轻人

SYN alongside, level, beside, in a row  
2 PREP-PHRASE 与…并列；与…并排 If you are abreast of someone or something, you are level with them or in line with them. 与…并列；与…并排

  • As he drew abreast of the man he pretended to stumble.

    当他赶上那个男人时，他装出了要跌倒的样子。

3 PREP-PHRASE 了解，跟上（最新情况） If you keep abreast of a subject, you know all the most recent facts about it. 了解，跟上（最新情况）

  • He will be keeping abreast of the news.

    他将时刻关注最新消息。

  • We'll keep you abreast of developments.

    我们会随时告知你最新进展。
