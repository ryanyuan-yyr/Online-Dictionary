indiscriminate
ɪndɪskrɪmɪnət ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnɪt ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnɪt
ADJ-GRADED 不加区别的;不加选择的 If you describe an action as indiscriminate, you are critical of it because it does not involve any careful thought or choice. 不加区别的;不加选择的 [disapproval]

  • The indiscriminate use of fertilisers is damaging to the environment.

    乱用化肥会破坏环境。

  • ...the indiscriminate killing of refugees.

    对难民不分青红皂白的杀戮

SYN random, general, wholesale, mixed  
indiscriminately

  • The men opened fire indiscriminately.

    那些人不分青红皂白地开火射击。

  • I'm afraid this disease strikes indiscriminately.

    恐怕各年龄段的人都有可能得这种病。
