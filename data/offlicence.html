off-licence
ɔfˈlaɪsəns
Word forms: plural off-licences
N-COUNT 售酒商店；外卖酒店 An off-licence is a shop which sells beer, wine, and other alcoholic drinks. 售酒商店；外卖酒店 [British]

  • I'm going to the off-licence to buy some whisky.

    我去售酒商店买几瓶威士忌。

SYN offie or offy [British , informal] , liquor store [US , Canadian] , bottle shop [Australian , New Zealand] , bottle store [South Africa]  
in AM, use 美国英语用 liquor store
