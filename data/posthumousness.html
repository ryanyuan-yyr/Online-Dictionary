posthumous
pɒstʃʊməs ˈpɑstʃʊməs ˈpɑstjʊməs
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 死后的；身后的 Posthumous is used to describe something that happens after a person's death but relates to something they did before they died. 死后的；身后的

  • His family and supporters have campaigned for many years for a posthumous pardon.

    他的家人和支持者为争取其获得身后赦免而开展了多年的运动。

  • ...the posthumous publication of his first novel.

    他的第一部小说在他死后的出版

posthumously

  • After the war she was posthumously awarded the George Cross.

    战争结束后，她被追授乔治十字勋章。
