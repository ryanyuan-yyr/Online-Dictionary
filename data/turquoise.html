turquoise
tɜːʳkwɔɪz ˈtɜrˌkɔɪz ˈtɜrˌkwɔɪz
Word forms: plural turquoises
1 COLOUR 绿松石色;青绿色 Turquoise or turquoise blue is used to describe things that are of a light greenish-blue colour. 绿松石色;青绿色

  • ...a clear turquoise sea.

    清澈碧蓝的大海

2 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] 绿松石（通常用于首饰中） Turquoise is a bright blue stone that is often used in jewellery. 绿松石（通常用于首饰中）

  • …beautiful silver and turquoise jewelry and pottery.

    美丽的银饰和绿松石珠宝与陶器
