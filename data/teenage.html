teenage
tiːneɪdʒ ˈtinˌeɪdʒ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 十几岁的;青少年的 Teenage children are aged between thirteen and nineteen years old. 十几岁的;青少年的

  • Almost one in four teenage girls now smoke.

    如今，十几岁的女孩几乎 4 个当中就有 1 个抽烟。

SYN youthful, adolescent, juvenile, immature  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 面向十几岁青少年的;由十几岁青少年完成的 Teenage is used to describe things such as films, magazines, bands, or activities that are aimed at or are done by teenage children. 面向十几岁青少年的;由十几岁青少年完成的

  • ...'Smash Hits', a teenage magazine.

    青少年杂志《流行金曲》

  • ...teenage pregnancies.

    少女怀孕
