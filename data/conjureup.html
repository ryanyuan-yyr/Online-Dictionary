conjure up
1 PHRASAL VERB 使浮现于脑海;想起 If you conjure up a memory, picture, or idea, you create it in your mind. 使浮现于脑海;想起 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • When he closed his eyes, he could conjure up in exact colour almost every event of his life. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他闭上眼睛时，几乎他一生中经历的每一件事情都会真切地浮现在他的脑海。

  • When we think of adventurers, many of us conjure up images of larger-than-life characters trekking to the North Pole. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一想到探险者，我们很多人脑海中浮现的便是长途跋涉前往北极的非凡人物形象。

SYN bring to mind, recall, evoke, recreate  
2 PHRASAL VERB (字词、声音等)使想起，使联想到 If something such as a word or sound conjures up particular images or ideas, it makes you think of them. (字词、声音等)使想起，使联想到

  • Jimmy Buffett's music conjures up a warm night in the tropics. [VERB PREP. noun]

    吉米·巴菲特的音乐让人想起热带地区温暖的夜晚。

  • What does the word 'feminist' conjure up for you? [VERB PREP. noun]

    “女权主义者”这个词会让你想到什么？
