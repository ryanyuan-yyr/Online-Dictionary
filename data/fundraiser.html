fundraiser
fʌndreɪzəʳ ˈfʌndˌreɪzə ˈfʌndˌreɪzər
(also fund-raiser)Word forms: plural fundraisers
1 N-COUNT 筹款活动;募捐会 A fundraiser is an event which is intended to raise money for a particular purpose, for example, for a charity. 筹款活动;募捐会

  • Organize a fundraiser for your church. [+ for]

    为你的教堂组织一次募捐活动。

2 N-COUNT 筹集资金者;募捐者 A fundraiser is someone who works to raise money for a particular purpose, for example, for a charity. 筹集资金者;募捐者

  • Sir Anthony was a keen fundraiser for the Liberal Democrats. [+ for]

    安东尼爵士是自由民主党热心的资金筹集者。
