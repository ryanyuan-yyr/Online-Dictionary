enlightened
ɪnlaɪtənd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 开明的;开通的;有见识的 If you describe someone or their attitudes as enlightened, you mean that they have sensible, modern attitudes and ways of dealing with things. 开明的;开通的;有见识的 [approval]

  • Enlightened companies include its human resources in their estimation of the firm's worth.

    有见识的公司将人力资源包括在公司价值评估中。

  • ...an enlightened policy.

    开明政策

SYN informed, aware, liberal, reasonable
