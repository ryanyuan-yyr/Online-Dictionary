silent partner
Word forms: plural silent partners
N-COUNT 隐名合伙人，隐名股东(在企业中投入资金却不积极参与经营的人) A silent partner is a person who provides some of the capital for a business but who does not take an active part in managing the business. 隐名合伙人，隐名股东(在企业中投入资金却不积极参与经营的人) [US, business]
