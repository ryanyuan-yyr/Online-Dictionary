inverse
ɪnvɜːʳs ɪnˈvɜːs ɪnˈvɜrs ˈɪnˌvɜrs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 相反的;成反比的;相逆的 If there is an inverse relationship between two things, one of them becomes larger as the other becomes smaller. 相反的;成反比的;相逆的

  • The tension grew in inverse proportion to the distance from their final destination.

    拉力的大小与他们离终点的距离成反比。

SYN opposite, reverse, reversed, contrary  
inversely

  • The size of the nebula at this stage is inversely proportional to its mass.

    这个阶段星云的大小与其质量成反比。

2 N-SING 相反的事物;对立面 The inverse of something is its exact opposite. 相反的事物;对立面 [formal]

  • There is no sign that you bothered to consider the inverse of your logic. [+ of]

    你根本就不想把你的逻辑反过来考虑。

SYN converse, opposite, reverse, contrary  
Inverse is also an adjective.

  • The hologram can be flipped to show the inverse image.

    全息图可以通过反转显现出逆像。
