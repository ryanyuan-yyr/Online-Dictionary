eyesore
aɪsɔːʳ ˈaɪˌsɔr
Word forms: plural eyesores
N-COUNT [usually singular] 碍眼的东西（如建筑物等） You describe a building or place as an eyesore when it is extremely ugly and you dislike it or disapprove of it. 碍眼的东西（如建筑物等） [disapproval]

  • Poverty leads to slums, which are an eyesore and a health hazard.

    贫困产生了贫民窟，那里不仅有碍观瞻，而且卫生隐患严重。

SYN mess, blight, blot, blemish
