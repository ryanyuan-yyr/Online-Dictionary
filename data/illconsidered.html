ill-considered
ˈɪlkənˈsɪdərd
ADJ-GRADED 考虑不周的;不妥的;不明智的 If you describe something that someone says or does as ill-considered, you mean that it is not sensible or not appropriate. 考虑不周的;不妥的;不明智的

  • He made some ill-considered remarks about the cost.

    他们对成本发表了一些很不妥当的评论。

SYN unwise, rash, imprudent, careless
