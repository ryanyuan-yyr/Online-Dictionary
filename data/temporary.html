temporary
tempərəri ˈtɛmprərɪ -reri ˈtɛmpəˌrɛri
1 ADJ 暂时的;临时的;短暂的 Something that is temporary lasts for only a limited time. 暂时的;临时的;短暂的

  • His job here is only temporary.

    他在这儿的工作只是临时的。

  • Most adolescent problems are temporary.

    多数青少年问题是暂时性的。

  • ...a temporary loss of memory.

    暂时失记

SYN impermanent, passing, transitory, brief  
temporarily

  • The peace agreement has at least temporarily halted the civil war.

    该和平协定至少暂时停止了内战。

  • Checkpoints between the two zones were temporarily closed.

    那两个地区之间的检查站被临时关闭。
