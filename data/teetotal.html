teetotal
tiːtoʊtəl tiːtoʊtəl ˈtiˈtoʊtəl ˈtiˌtoʊtəl
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 不喝酒的;滴酒不沾的 Someone who is teetotal does not drink alcohol. 不喝酒的;滴酒不沾的

  • He will not be having a celebratory drink, as he is teetotal.

    他滴酒不沾，不会喝庆功酒的。

SYN abstinent, on the wagon [informal] , abstemious, dry [informal]
