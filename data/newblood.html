new blood njuː blʌd
N-UNCOUNT 新鲜血液;新生力量 If people talk about bringing new blood into an organization or sports team, they are referring to new people who are likely to improve the organization or team. 新鲜血液;新生力量

  • There should be major changes in the government to bring in new blood.

    政府机构应该进行重大改革，引进新生力量。

  • That's what we need, some new blood in the team. [+ of]

    这正是我们所需要的，为团队注入新鲜血液。
