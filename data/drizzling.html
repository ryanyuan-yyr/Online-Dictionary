drizzle
drɪzəl ˈdrɪzəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense drizzles, present participle drizzling, past tense , past participle drizzled
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 毛毛雨;蒙蒙细雨 Drizzle is light rain falling in fine drops. 毛毛雨;蒙蒙细雨

  • The drizzle had now stopped and the sun was breaking through.

    毛毛雨现在已经停了,太阳正破云而出。

SYN fine rain, spray, Scotch mist, mizzle  
2 VERB 下毛毛雨;下蒙蒙细雨 If it is drizzling, it is raining very lightly. 下毛毛雨;下蒙蒙细雨

  • Clouds had come down and it was starting to drizzle. [VERB]

    云层压低,开始飘起了毛毛雨。

  • I walked home in the drizzling rain. [VERB-ing]

    我冒着毛毛雨走回了家。

3 VERB (在食物上)滴，淋，洒 If you drizzle a liquid over food or drizzle food with a liquid, you pour a small quantity of the liquid all over the food. (在食物上)滴，淋，洒 [Also V n with over]

  • Drizzle the remaining dressing over the duck and salad. [V n over n]

    将剩下的调料淋在鸭肉和色拉上。

  • Drizzle them with warmed extra virgin olive oil. [VERB noun with noun]

    将加热过的特级初榨橄榄油淋在上面。

SYN trickle, pour, dribble, splash
