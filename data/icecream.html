ice cream
(also ice-cream)Word forms: plural ice creams
1 N-MASS 冰激凌 Ice cream is a very cold sweet food which is made from frozen cream or a substance like cream and has a flavour such as vanilla, chocolate, or strawberry. 冰激凌

  • I'll get you some ice cream.

    我会给你买些冰激凌。

  • ...vanilla ice cream.

    香草冰激凌

2 N-COUNT 一客冰激凌;一份冰激凌 An ice cream is an amount of ice cream sold in a small container or a cone made of thin biscuit. 一客冰激凌;一份冰激凌

  • Do you want an ice cream?.

    想来份冰激凌吗？…

  • They stuffed themselves with ice creams, chocolate and lollies.

    他们填了一肚子的冰激凌、巧克力和棒棒糖。
