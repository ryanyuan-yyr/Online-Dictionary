nit
nɪt nɪt
Word forms: plural nits
1 N-PLURAL 头虱卵 Nits are the eggs of insects called lice which live in people's hair. 头虱卵

  • Borrowed combs were said to be the cause of spreading nits from one to another.

    借用梳子据说是虱卵在人之间传播的原因。

2 N-COUNT 笨人;蠢人 If you refer to someone as a nit, you think they are stupid or silly. 笨人;蠢人 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • I'd rather leave the business than work with such a nit.

    我宁愿离开公司，也不愿和这样的笨蛋一起工作。
