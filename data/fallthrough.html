fall through
PHRASAL VERB (安排)没有落实;(计划)未能实现;(交易)没有达成 If an arrangement, plan, or deal falls through, it fails to happen. (安排)没有落实;(计划)未能实现;(交易)没有达成

  • They wanted to turn the estate into a private golf course and offered £20 million, but the deal fell through. [VERB PREP.]

    他们出价两千万英镑，想把这块地变成高尔夫球场，但交易泡汤了。

SYN fail, be unsuccessful, come to nothing, fizzle out [informal]
