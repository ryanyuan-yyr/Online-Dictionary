hereabouts
hɪərəbaʊts
ADV [ADVERB after verb, n ADVERB] 在这附近;在这一带 You use hereabouts to indicate that you are talking about something near you or in the same area as you. 在这附近;在这一带

  • It's a bit chilly and empty hereabouts.

    这附近略显阴冷空旷。

  • The mountains hereabouts reach heights of over 2000 metres.

    这一带的山高达两千多米。
