felicitous
fɪlɪsɪtəs fəˈlɪsɪtəs
ADJ-GRADED (话语、想法等)妥当的，合宜的，贴切的 If you describe a remark or idea as felicitous, you approve of it because it seems particularly suitable in the circumstances. (话语、想法等)妥当的，合宜的，贴切的 [formal, approval]

  • Her prose style is not always felicitous; she tends to repetition.

    她的行文风格并非总是恰到好处，往往爱重复。

SYN fitting, timely, appropriate, inspired
