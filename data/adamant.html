adamant
ædəmənt ˈædəmənt ˈædəˌmænt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ that] 坚决的;坚定不移的 If someone is adamant about something, they are determined not to change their mind about it. 坚决的;坚定不移的

  • The prime minister is adamant that he will not resign.

    首相坚决不辞职。

  • Sue was adamant about that job in Australia. [+ about]

    对澳大利亚的那份工作，休的态度很坚决。

SYN determined, firm, fixed, stiff  
adamantly

  • She was adamantly opposed to her husband travelling to Brussels.

    她坚决反对丈夫前往布鲁塞尔。
