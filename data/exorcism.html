exorcism

Word forms: plural exorcisms
N-VAR 驱邪;伏魔；驱邪仪式 Exorcism is the removing of evil spirits from a person or place by the use of prayer. 驱邪;伏魔；驱邪仪式

  • The exorcism was broadcast on television.

    电视上播放了驱邪仪式。

SYN driving out, cleansing, expulsion, purification
