ballet
bæleɪ bæˈleɪ bæleɪ bæˈleɪ ˈbæleɪ
Word forms: plural ballets
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN, oft NOUN n] 芭蕾舞 Ballet is a type of very skilled and artistic dancing with carefully planned movements. 芭蕾舞

  • I trained as a ballet dancer.

    我受过芭蕾舞训练。

  • She is also keen on the ballet.

    她也热衷于芭蕾舞。

2 N-COUNT 芭蕾舞剧；芭蕾舞段 A ballet is an artistic work that is performed by ballet dancers. 芭蕾舞剧；芭蕾舞段

  • The performance will include the premiere of three new ballets.

    三段新编芭蕾舞将在演出中首演。
