statewide
steɪtwaɪd ˌsteɪtˈwaɪd ˈsteɪtˈwaɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (美国)全州范围的，遍及全州的 Statewide means across or throughout the whole of one of the states of the United States. (美国)全州范围的，遍及全州的

  • Each year they compete in a prominent statewide bicycle race.

    每年他们都要参加著名的全州自行车大赛。

  • These voters often determine the outcome of statewide elections.

    这些选民们往往决定着全州大选的结果。

Statewide is also an adverb.

  • In the weeks since flooding began, 16 people have died statewide.

    洪水暴发几周以来，全州已有16 人死亡。
