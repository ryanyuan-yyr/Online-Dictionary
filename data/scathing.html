scathing
skeɪðɪŋ ˈskeɪðɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 严厉的;尖锐的;刻薄的 If you say that someone is being scathing about something, you mean that they are being very critical of it. 严厉的;尖锐的;刻薄的

  • His report was scathing about Loyalist and Republican terror groups. [+ about]

    他的报告猛烈抨击了忠英派和共和军的恐怖组织。

  • He then launched a scathing attack on previous leaders.

    他接着严厉地斥责了几位前任领导者。

SYN critical, cutting, biting, harsh  
scathingly

  • 'Oh, they want to be excused,' the other girl said scathingly.

    “噢，他们想得到原谅，”另一个女孩尖刻地说。
