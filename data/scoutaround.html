scout around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 scout round
PHRASAL VERB 四处寻觅 If you scout around or scout round for something, you go to different places looking for it. 四处寻觅

  • They scouted around for more fuel. [VERB PREP. + for]

    他们四处寻觅更多的燃料。

  • I scouted round in the bushes. [VERB PREP.]

    我在灌木丛中四下查找。
