extravagance
ɪkstrævəgəns ɪkˈstrævəɡənsɪ ɛkˈstrævəgəns ɪkˈstrævəgəns
Word forms: plural extravagances
1 N-UNCOUNT 奢侈;铺张;浪费 Extravagance is the spending of more money than is reasonable or than you can afford. 奢侈;铺张;浪费

  • ...gross mismanagement and financial extravagance.

    严重的管理不善和资金浪费

  • When the company went under, tales of his extravagance surged through the industry.

    公司破产后，有关他铺张浪费的种种传闻传遍了整个行业。

SYN overspending, squandering, profusion, profligacy  
2 N-COUNT 奢侈品 An extravagance is something that you spend money on but cannot really afford. 奢侈品

  • Her only extravagance was horses.

    她唯一的奢侈品就是马。

  • Why waste money on such extravagances?

    为什么把钱浪费在这些奢侈品上呢？

SYN luxury, treat, indulgence, extra
