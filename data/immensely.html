immensely
ɪmensli
ADV [usually ADVERB adjective, oft ADVERB after verb] 非常;很 You use immensely to emphasize the degree or extent of a quality, feeling, or process. 非常;很 [emphasis]

  • Wind surfing can be strenuous but immensely exciting.

    风帆冲浪可能紧张吃力,但却非常刺激。

  • I enjoyed this movie immensely.

    我非常喜欢这部电影。

SYN extremely, very, highly, greatly
