old boy
Word forms: plural old boys
1 N-COUNT 男校友 You can refer to a man who used to be a pupil at a particular school or university as an old boy . 男校友 [British]

  • ...Eton College, with all its traditions and long list of famous old boys.

    拥有各种悠久传统和众多知名校友的伊顿公学

  • ...Cardiff High School Old Boys rugby team.

    加的夫中学校友橄榄球队

SYN alumnus or alumna, former pupil  
2 N-COUNT 老家伙；老头儿；中年老男人 If you refer to an old or middle-aged man as an old boy, you are referring to him in a disrespectful way. 老家伙；老头儿；中年老男人 [British, informal, old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • Outside, two old boys lingered on the street corner discussing cattle.

    两个老头儿在外面街角迟迟不走，聊着牛的事情。
