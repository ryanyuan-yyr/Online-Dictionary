dependency
dɪpendənsi diˈpɛndənsi dɪˈpɛnˈdənsi
Word forms: plural dependencies
1 N-COUNT 附属国;附属地;托管地 A dependency is a country which is controlled by another country. 附属国;附属地;托管地

  • ...the tiny British dependency of Montserrat in the eastern Caribbean.

    位于加勒比海东部巴掌大的英国附属领地蒙特塞拉特

2 N-UNCOUNT （尤指过分或不利的）依靠，依赖 You talk about someone's dependency when they have a deep emotional, physical, or financial need for a particular person or thing, especially one that you consider excessive or undesirable. （尤指过分或不利的）依靠，依赖

  • We worried about his dependency on his mother. [+ on]

    我们担心他可能对母亲太过依赖。

  • Ukraine is handicapped by its near-total dependency on Russian oil.

    乌克兰受制于对俄罗斯石油近乎完全的依赖。

SYN overreliance, need, attachment, reliance  
3 N-VAR [usually n NOUN] (对酒精、毒品等的)依赖，瘾，癖 If you talk about alcohol dependency or chemical dependency, you are referring to a situation where someone is an alcoholic or is addicted to drugs. (对酒精、毒品等的)依赖，瘾，癖 [mainly US]

  • In 1985, he began to show signs of alcohol and drug dependency.

    在1985年，他开始表现出酗酒嗜毒的迹象。

  • In 1979 she quit her teaching job because her dependency had affected her work.

    她于1979 年辞去了教书一职，因为她的瘾症已经影响到了工作。

SYN addiction, dependence, craving, need
