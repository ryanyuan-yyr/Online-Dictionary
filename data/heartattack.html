heart attack
Word forms: plural heart attacks
1 N-COUNT 心脏病发作 If someone has a heart attack, their heart begins to beat very irregularly or stops completely. 心脏病发作

  • He died of a heart attack brought on by overwork.

    他死于过劳引起的心脏病发作。

2 N-SING [a NOUN] 震惊;大怒 If you say that someone will have a heart attack about something, you are emphasizing that they will be very shocked or angry. 震惊;大怒 [informal, emphasis]

  • She'll have a heart attack if I tell her.

    如果我告诉她，她会气坏的。
