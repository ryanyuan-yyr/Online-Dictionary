causal
kɔːzəl ˈkɔzəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有因果关系的;存在因果联系的 If there is a causal relationship between two things, one thing is responsible for causing the other thing. 有因果关系的;存在因果联系的 [formal]

  • Rawlins stresses that it is impossible to prove a causal link between the drug and the deaths.

    罗林斯强调无法证明这种药物和多起死亡事件之间存在因果关系。

  • He would dearly love to show a causal relationship between culture and imperialism, but cannot.

    他倒是十分想揭示文化与帝国主义之间的因果联系，可是却无能为力。
