breezy
briːzi ˈbrizi
1 ADJ-GRADED 随意的；乐天的；自信的 If you describe someone as breezy, you mean that they behave in a casual, cheerful, and confident manner. 随意的；乐天的；自信的

  • ...his bright and breezy personality.

    他开朗活泼的个性

  • Mona tried to sound breezy.

    莫娜竭力使自己的声音听上去轻松随意。

SYN carefree, casual, lively, sparkling  
breezily

  • 'Hi,' he said breezily.

    “嗨，”他友好地说道。

2 ADJ-GRADED 明快的；生动的；活泼的 If you describe something as breezy, especially music or clothing, you mean that it is bright, lively, and cheerful. 明快的；生动的；活泼的 [journalism]

  • This album is bright, breezy and playful.

    这张专辑音乐轻松明快，趣味十足。

3 ADJ-GRADED （天气）有微风的 When the weather is breezy, there is a fairly strong but pleasant wind blowing. （天气）有微风的

  • The day was breezy and warm.

    这一天微风习习，暖洋洋的。

SYN windy, fresh, airy, blustery
