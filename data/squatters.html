squatter
skwɒtəʳ ˈskwɑtər
Word forms: plural squatters
1 N-COUNT 擅自占住空房者 A squatter is someone who lives in an unused building without having a legal right to do so and without paying any rent or any property tax. 擅自占住空房者
2 N-COUNT 擅自占用空地者 A squatter is someone who occupies unused land, either to farm it or to build a house on it, without having a legal right to do so. 擅自占用空地者

  • When they returned to Tuol Sakor, they found all the best land taken by squatters.

    当回到图尔沙科尔时，他们发现那些最好的土地全都被人侵占了。
