mull over
PHRASAL VERB 反复思考;仔细琢磨 If you mull something over, you think about it for a long time before deciding what to do. 反复思考;仔细琢磨

  • McLaren had been mulling over an idea to make a movie. [VERB PREP. noun]

    麦克拉伦琢磨着要拍一部电影已经有段时间了。

  • I'll leave you alone here so you can mull it over. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我不打扰你了，你在这儿可以仔细考虑考虑这事儿。
