analyse
ænəlaɪz ˈænəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense analyses, present participle analysing, past tense , past participle analysed
in AM, use 美国英语用 analyze
1 VERB 细察；分析 If you analyse something, you consider it carefully or use statistical methods in order to fully understand it. 细察；分析

  • McCarthy was asked to analyse the data from the first phase of trials of the vaccine. [VERB noun]

    麦卡锡被要求对第一阶段疫苗试验的数据作出分析。

  • This book teaches you how to analyse what is causing the stress in your life. [VERB wh]

    这本书教你分析生活压力形成的原因。

SYN examine, test, study, research  
2 VERB 分析；分解；化验 If you analyse something, you examine it using scientific methods in order to find out what it consists of. 分析；分解；化验 [Also VERB wh]

  • We haven't had time to analyse those samples yet. [VERB noun]

    我们还没有时间分析那些样本。

  • They had their tablets analysed to find out whether they were getting the real drug or not. [have noun VERB-ed]

    他们将药片拿去化验以查明他们得到的是否是真药。

SYN break down, consider, study, separate
