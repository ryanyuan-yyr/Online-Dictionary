optical illusion
Word forms: plural optical illusions
N-COUNT 视错觉；错视；光幻觉 An optical illusion is something that tricks your eyes so that what you think you see is different from what is really there. 视错觉；错视；光幻觉

  • Sloping walls on the bulk of the building create an optical illusion.

    大楼主体的斜墙给人造成一种视错觉。
