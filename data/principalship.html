principal
prɪnsɪpəl ˈprɪnsəpəl
Word forms: plural principals
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 首要的；最主要的；最重要的 Principal means first in order of importance. 首要的；最主要的；最重要的

  • The principal reason for my change of mind is this.

    这是我改变主意的最主要原因。

  • ...the country's principal source of foreign exchange earnings.

    这个国家最主要的外汇收入来源

  • Their principal concern is bound to be that of winning the next general election.

    他们最关心的问题肯定是赢得下一次大选。

SYN main, leading, chief, prime  
2 N-COUNT （中小学）校长；（英国）大学校长，学院院长 The principal of a school, or in Britain the principal of a college, is the person in charge of the school or college. （中小学）校长；（英国）大学校长，学院院长

  • Donald King is the principal of Dartmouth High School. [+ of]

    唐纳德·金是达特茅斯中学的校长。
