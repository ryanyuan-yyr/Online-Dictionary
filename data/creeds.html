creed
kriːd krid
Word forms: plural creeds
1 N-COUNT 信条;原则;纲领 A creed is a set of beliefs, principles, or opinions that strongly influence the way people live or work. 信条;原则;纲领 [formal]

  • ...their devotion to their creed of self-help. [+ of]

    他们坚信自力更生的原则

2 N-COUNT 宗教 A creed is a religion. 宗教 [formal]

  • The centre is open to all, no matter what race or creed.

    该中心向所有人开放，不论种族和宗教信仰如何。

SYN belief, principles, professionof faith, doctrine
