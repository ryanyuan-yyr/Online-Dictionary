peerage
pɪərɪdʒ ˈpɪrɪdʒ
Word forms: plural peerages
1 N-COUNT 贵族头衔；贵族爵位 If someone has a peerage, they have the rank of a peer. 贵族头衔；贵族爵位

  • The Prime Minister offered him a peerage.

    首相授予他贵族爵位。

  • It's thought she may eventually accept a peerage and move to the House of Lords.

    人们认为她最终可能接受贵族头衔，进入上议院。

2 N-SING （泛指一国的）贵族 The peers of a particular country are referred to as the peerage. （泛指一国的）贵族
