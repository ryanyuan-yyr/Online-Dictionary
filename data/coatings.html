coating
koʊtɪŋ ˈkoʊtɪŋ
Word forms: plural coatings
N-COUNT 涂层;覆盖层 A coating of a substance is a thin layer of it spread over a surface. 涂层;覆盖层

  • Under the coating of dust and cobwebs, he discovered a fine French Louis XVI clock. [+ of]

    在一层灰尘和蜘蛛网底下，他发现了一座精美的法国路易十六时期的时钟。

  • ...fluffy chocolate mousse, topped off with a crisp chocolate coating.

    松软的巧克力慕斯，顶上盖着一层脆皮巧克力

SYN layer, covering, finish, skin
