discouragement
dɪskʌrɪdʒmənt -kɜːr- dɪˈskɜrɪdʒmənt
Word forms: plural discouragements
1 N-UNCOUNT 阻止;阻拦;劝阻 Discouragement is the act of trying to make someone not want to do something. 阻止;阻拦;劝阻

  • He persevered despite discouragement from those around him.

    尽管周围一片劝阻声，他还是锲而不舍。

2 N-COUNT 使人泄气的事物;挫折 A discouragement is something that makes you unwilling to do something because you are afraid of the consequences. 使人泄气的事物;挫折

  • Uncertainty is a discouragement to investment.

    不确定性令投资者裹足不前。
