legend
ledʒənd ˈlɛdʒənd
Word forms: plural legends
1 N-VAR 传说；传奇 A legend is a very old and popular story that may be true. 传说；传奇

  • ...the legends of ancient Greece. [+ of]

    古希腊传说

  • ...the Robin Hood legend.

    罗宾汉传奇

  • The play was based on Irish legend.

    这部戏取材于爱尔兰传说。

SYN myth, story, tale, fiction  
2 N-COUNT 传奇人物；巨匠；大师 If you refer to someone as a legend, you mean that they are very famous and admired by a lot of people. 传奇人物；巨匠；大师 [approval]

  • ...blues legends John Lee Hooker and B.B. King.

    布鲁斯音乐大师约翰·李·胡克和B. B. 金

3 N-VAR 传奇故事；家喻户晓的故事；热门故事 A legend is a story that people talk about, concerning people, places, or events that exist or are famous at the present time. 传奇故事；家喻户晓的故事；热门故事

  • The incident has since become a family legend.

    此事自此成为了家族传奇。

  • His frequent brushes with death are the stuff of legend among the press.

    他频繁地与死神擦肩而过，成了报章杂志的热门猛料。
