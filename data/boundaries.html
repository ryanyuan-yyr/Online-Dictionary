boundary
baʊndəri ˈbaʊndri ˈbaʊndəri
Word forms: plural boundaries
1 N-COUNT （土地的）分界线，边界 The boundary of an area of land is an imaginary line that separates it from other areas. （土地的）分界线，边界 [Also + between]

  • ...the Bow Brook which forms the western boundary of the wood. [+ of]

    构成了这片树林西部边界的鲍溪

  • Drug traffickers operate across national boundaries.

    贩毒分子进行跨国贩毒活动。

SYN frontier, edge, border, march  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （学科或活动的）界限，边界 The boundaries of something such as a subject or activity are the limits that people think that it has. （学科或活动的）界限，边界

  • The boundaries between history and storytelling are always being blurred and muddled. [+ between]

    历史和轶闻的分界向来是模糊而混乱的。

  • ...extending the boundaries of press freedom. [+ of]

    扩大新闻自由的界限

SYN limits, bounds, confines, threshold
