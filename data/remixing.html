remix
riːmɪks riːmɪks riˈmɪks ˈriˌmɪks
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense remixes, present participle remixing, past tense , past participle remixed
The noun is pronounced /'riːmɪks/. The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'mɪks/. 名词读作/'riːmɪks/。动词读作/ˌriː'mɪks/。
1 N-COUNT 混音版音乐;重混乐 A remix is a new version of a piece of music which has been created by putting together the individual instrumental and vocal parts in a different way. 混音版音乐;重混乐

  • Their new album features remixes of some of their previous hits.

    新专辑主要收录了一些他们曾风靡一时的老歌的混音版本。

SYN reworking  
2 VERB 重新合成（乐曲） To remix a piece of music means to make a new version of it by putting together the individual instrumental and vocal parts in a different way. 重新合成（乐曲）

  • The band are remixing some tracks. [VERB noun]

    这个乐队正对一些曲目进行重新合成。

  • Both tracks are remixed versions of tracks from her forthcoming album. [VERB-ed]

    两首曲目都是她即将推出的专辑歌曲的混音版本。

SYN rework
