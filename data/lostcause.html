lost cause
Word forms: plural lost causes
N-COUNT 必败无疑的事；无望之人 If you refer to something or someone as a lost cause, you mean that people's attempts to change or influence them have no chance of succeeding. 必败无疑的事；无望之人

  • They do not want to expend energy in what, to them, is a lost cause.

    他们不想把精力投到他们认为注定不会成功的事中去。
