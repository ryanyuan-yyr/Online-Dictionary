distracting
dɪstræktɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人分心的; 让人思想不集中的 If you say that something is distracting, you mean that it makes it difficult for you to concentrate properly on what you are doing. 令人分心的; 让人思想不集中的

  • It's distracting to have someone watching me while I work.

    我工作的时候要是有人看着就会分心。

SYN disturbing, bothering, confusing, dismaying
