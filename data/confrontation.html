confrontation
kɒnfrʌnteɪʃən ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃən kɒnˈfrʌntmənt
Word forms: plural confrontations
N-VAR 对抗;冲突;战斗;战役 A confrontation is a dispute, fight, or battle between two groups of people. 对抗;冲突;战斗;战役

  • The issue has caused great tension between the two countries and could lead to a military confrontation.

    这个问题使得两国关系非常紧张，可能会导致军事对抗。

  • The commission remains so weak that it will continue to avoid confrontation with governments. [+ with]

    该委员会的力量仍然十分薄弱，所以将继续避免与各国政府发生冲突。

SYN conflict, fight, crisis, contest
