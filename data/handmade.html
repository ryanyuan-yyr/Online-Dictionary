handmade
hændmeɪd ˌhændˈmeɪd ˈhændˌmeɪd
(also hand-made)
1 ADJ 手工制的 Handmade objects have been made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines. 手工制的

  • As they're handmade, each one varies slightly.

    因为是手工制作，所以每件都有细微差别。

  • ...handmade chocolates.

    手工制作的巧克力

SYN hand-crafted  
2 V-PASSIVE 手工制作 If something is handmade, it is made by someone using their hands or using tools rather than by machines. 手工制作

  • The beads they use are handmade in the Jura mountains in central France. [be VERB-ed]

    他们用的珠子是法国中部的侏罗山山民手工制作的。

  • These exquisitely ornate boxes and cabinets have been handmade and hand-painted by Indian craftspeople. [be VERB-ed]

    这些精美华丽的箱子和柜子都是印度工匠手工制作并彩绘的。
