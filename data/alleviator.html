alleviate
əliːvieɪt əˈliviˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense alleviates, present participle alleviating, past tense , past participle alleviated
VERB 减轻，缓解（痛苦等） If you alleviate pain, suffering, or an unpleasant condition, you make it less intense or severe. 减轻，缓解（痛苦等） [formal]

  • Nowadays, a great deal can be done to alleviate back pain. [VERB noun]

    如今，减轻背部疼痛可以有许多方法。

  • ...the problem of alleviating mass poverty. [VERB noun]

    摆脱普遍贫困的难题

SYN ease, reduce, relieve, moderate  
alleviation

  • Their energies were focussed on the alleviation of the refugees' misery.

    他们把精力集中在减轻难民们的苦难上。
