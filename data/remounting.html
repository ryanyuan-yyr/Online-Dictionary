remount
riːmaʊnt riˈmaʊnt ˈriˌmaʊnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense remounts, present participle remounting, past tense , past participle remounted
VERB 再骑上(自行车或马) When you remount a bicycle or horse, you get back on it after you have got off it or fallen off it. 再骑上(自行车或马)

  • He was told to remount his horse and ride back to Lexington. [VERB noun]

    他被告知再次骑上马回列克星敦。

  • The pony scrabbled up and waited for the rider, who remounted and carried on. [VERB]

    小马挣扎着站了起来等着骑车，直到他再次上马赶路。
