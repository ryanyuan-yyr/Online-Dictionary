backer
bækəʳ ˈbækər
Word forms: plural backers
N-COUNT 支持者；资助者 A backer is someone who helps or supports a project, organization, or person, often by giving or lending money. 支持者；资助者

  • I was looking for a backer to assist me in the attempted buy-out.

    我正在寻找一位支持我收购的出资人。

SYN supporter, second, ally, angel [informal]
