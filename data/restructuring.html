restructure
riːstrʌktʃəʳ riˈstrʌktʃər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense restructures, present participle restructuring, past tense , past participle restructured
VERB 改组;重新组织;调整 To restructure an organization or system means to change the way it is organized, usually in order to make it work more effectively. 改组;重新组织;调整 [Also VERB]

    总统号召教育家和政治家们帮助他重新调整美国的教育体系。

    我怎样才能开始调整自己的生活，让自己更快乐一些？
