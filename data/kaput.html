kaput
kəpʊt kæˈpʊt kəˈpʊt kəˈput
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 坏的；无用的；完蛋的 If you say that something is kaput, you mean that it is completely broken, useless, or finished. 坏的；无用的；完蛋的 [informal]

  • 'What's happened to your car? —'It's kaput.'.

    “你的车怎么了？”—“坏了。”

  • He finally admitted that his film career was kaput.

    他最终承认他的电影生涯走到尽头了。

SYN broken, finished, dead, destroyed
