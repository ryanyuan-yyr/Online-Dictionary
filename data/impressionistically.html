impressionistic
ɪmpreʃənɪstɪk ɪmˌprɛʃənˈɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 印象派的 An impressionistic work of art or piece of writing shows the artist's or writer's impressions of something rather than giving clear details. 印象派的

  • His paintings had become more impressionistic as his eyesight dimmed.

    伴随着他视力的下降,他的画作变得更富印象派风格。
