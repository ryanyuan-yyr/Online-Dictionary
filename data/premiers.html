premier
premiəʳ ˈprɛmjə ˈprɛmjər prɪmɪr prɪˈmɪr prɪˈmjɪr
Word forms: plural premiers
1 N-COUNT 总理；首相 The leader of the government of a country is sometimes referred to as the country's premier . 总理；首相

  • ...Australian premier Paul Keating.

    澳大利亚总理保罗·基廷

SYN head of government, prime minister, chancellor, chief minister  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 最好的；最佳的；最重要的 Premier is used to describe something that is considered to be the best or most important thing of a particular type. 最好的；最佳的；最重要的

  • ...the country's premier opera company.

    这个国家的顶尖歌剧团

SYN chief, leading, top, first
