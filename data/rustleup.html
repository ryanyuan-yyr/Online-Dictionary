rustle up
1 PHRASAL VERB 很快弄到，匆匆准备好(食物或饮料) If you rustle up something to eat or drink, you make or prepare it quickly, with very little planning. 很快弄到，匆匆准备好(食物或饮料) [informal]

  • Let's see if somebody can rustle up a cup of coffee. [VERB PREP. noun]

    让我们看看有没有人能很快弄到一杯咖啡。

  • Many tasty and nutritious meals can be rustled up in next to no time. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    转眼之间就能准备好很多营养可口的饭菜。

SYN prepare, make, produce, fix  
2 PHRASAL VERB 很快弄到 If you rustle something up, you provide or obtain it quickly, with very little planning. 很快弄到 [informal]

  • He managed to rustle up a couple of blankets. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他很快想办法弄到了几张毯子。

  • He has had no trouble rustling up 35 friends and colleagues to invite to his wedding. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他很快便邀请到35个朋友和同事参加他的婚礼。
