unsociable
ʌnsoʊʃəbəl ʌnˈsoʊʃəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不善交际的；不合群的 Someone who is unsociable does not like talking to other people and tries to avoid meeting them. 不善交际的；不合群的

  • My marriage has broken up. It has made me reclusive and unsociable.

    我的婚姻破裂了。这让我变得孤僻、不愿与人来往。

  • I am by no means an unsociable person.

    我绝对不是一个不合群的人。

SYN unfriendly, cold, withdrawn, retiring
