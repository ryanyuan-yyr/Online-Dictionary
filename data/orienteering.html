orienteering
ɔːrientɪərɪŋ ˌɔriɛnˈtɪrɪŋ ˌ oʊriɛnˈtɪrɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 定向赛跑；定向越野比赛；越野识途比赛 Orienteering is a sport in which people run from one place to another, using a compass and a map to guide them between points that are marked along the route. 定向赛跑；定向越野比赛；越野识途比赛
