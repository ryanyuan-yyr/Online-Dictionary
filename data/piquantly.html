piquant
piːkənt -kɑːnt ˈpiːkənt ˈpikənt ˈpikɑnt ˈpikwənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 辛辣的；开胃的 Food that is piquant has a pleasantly spicy taste. 辛辣的；开胃的 [written]

  • ...a crisp mixed salad with an unusually piquant dressing.

    浇上特别辛辣的调料的脆口什锦色拉

SYN spicy, biting, sharp, stinging  
piquancy

  • A little mustard is served on the side to add further piquancy.

    边上备有一点芥末，可增加辣味。

2 ADJ-GRADED 引人入胜的；刺激有趣的 Something that is piquant is interesting and exciting. 引人入胜的；刺激有趣的 [written]

  • There may well have been a piquant novelty about her books when they came out.

    她的书问世时可能确有引人入胜的新颖之处。

SYN interesting, spirited, stimulating, lively  
piquancy

  • Piquancy was added to the situation because Dr Porter was then on the point of marrying Hugh Miller.

    当时的情形因正赶上波特博士要和休·米勒结婚，便又多了几分趣味。
