unmanly
ʌnmænli ʌnˈmænlɪ ʌnˈmænli
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 无男子气概的；怯懦的 If you describe a boy's or man's behaviour as unmanly, you are critical of the fact that they are behaving in a way that you think is inappropriate for a man. 无男子气概的；怯懦的 [disapproval]

  • Your partner can feel the loss as acutely as you, but may feel that it is unmanly to cry.

    你的搭档跟你一样极度地失落，但他也许会觉得男儿有泪不轻弹。

SYN effeminate, soft [informal] , weak, feeble
