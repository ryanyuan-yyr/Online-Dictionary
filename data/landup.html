land up
PHRASAL VERB 抵达；到达；最终落到（某种处境） If you say that you land up in a place or situation, you mean that you arrive there after a long journey or at the end of a long series of events. 抵达；到达；最终落到（某种处境） [mainly British, informal]

  • Half of those who went east seem to have landed up in southern India. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    前往东方的人似乎有半数到了印度南部。

  • We landed up at the Las Vegas at about 6.30. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    我们6点30分左右抵达拉斯维加斯。

SYN end up, arrive, turn up, wind up
