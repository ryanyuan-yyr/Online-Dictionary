firmament
fɜːʳməmənt ˈfɜrməmənt
1 N-SING 天;天空;苍穹 The firmament is the sky or heaven. 天;天空;苍穹 [literary]

  • There are no stars in the firmament.

    天空没有一颗星星。

SYN sky, skies, heaven, heavens  
2 N-SING (某一组织或领域的)最高层，最上层 If you talk about the firmament in a particular organization or field of activity, you mean the top of it. (某一组织或领域的)最高层，最上层

  • He was rich, and a rising star in the political firmament.

    他十分富有，并且是政治高层一颗冉冉升起的新星。

SYN arena, province, sphere, realm
