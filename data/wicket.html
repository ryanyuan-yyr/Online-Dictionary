wicket
wɪkɪt ˈwɪkɪt
Word forms: plural wickets
1 N-COUNT (板球运动中的)三柱门 In cricket, a wicket is a set of three upright sticks with two small sticks on top of them at which the ball is bowled. There are two wickets on a cricket pitch. (板球运动中的)三柱门
2 N-COUNT (板球场中)两个三柱门之间的场地 In cricket, a wicket is the area of grass in between the two wickets on the pitch. (板球场中)两个三柱门之间的场地
3 N-COUNT （板球运动中）击球手击球的一轮，击球手的避免出局 In cricket, when a wicket falls or is taken, a batsman is out. （板球运动中）击球手击球的一轮，击球手的避免出局
