retrograde
retrəgreɪd ˈrɛtrəˌgreɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 倒退的;后退的;退化的 A retrograde action is one that you think makes a situation worse rather than better. 倒退的;后退的;退化的 [formal]

  • The Prime Minister described transferring education to central government funding as 'a retrograde step'.

    首相称将教育投入转由中央政府拨款是“一种倒退”。
