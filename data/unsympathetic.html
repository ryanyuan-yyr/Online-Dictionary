unsympathetic
ʌnsɪmpəθetɪk ˌʌnsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 冷漠的；不体恤人的；无同情心的 If someone is unsympathetic, they are not kind or helpful to a person in difficulties. 冷漠的；不体恤人的；无同情心的

  • Her husband was unsympathetic and she felt she had no one to turn to.

    她丈夫并不懂得体恤人，她觉得无人可依靠。

  • ...an unsympathetic doctor.

    冷漠的医生

SYN insensitive, callous, heartless, hard  
2 ADJ-GRADED 讨厌的；难缠的 An unsympathetic person is unpleasant and difficult to like. 讨厌的；难缠的

  • ...a very unsympathetic main character.

    讨人厌的主角

  • He's unsympathetic, but charismatic and complex.

    他很讨人厌，但很有感召力，令人捉摸不透。

SYN unsavoury, nasty, unpleasant, unattractive  
3 ADJ-GRADED （对主张或目标）不支持的，不赞成的 If you are unsympathetic to a particular idea or aim, you are not willing to support it. （对主张或目标）不支持的，不赞成的

  • I'm highly unsympathetic to what you are trying to achieve. [+ to]

    我极不赞成你试图达成的目标。

SYN against, anti, opposed to, set against
