boy
bɔɪ bɔɪ
Word forms: plural boys
1 N-COUNT 男孩；男童 A boy is a child who will grow up to be a man. 男孩；男童

  • I knew him when he was a little boy.

    他还是个小男孩的时候我就认识他了。

  • He was still just a boy.

    他还只是个孩子。

SYN lad, kid [informal] , youth, fellow [old-fashioned]  
2 N-COUNT 男青年，小伙子（尤用于指青年男女恋爱关系时） You can refer to a young man as a boy, especially when talking about relationships between boys and girls. 男青年，小伙子（尤用于指青年男女恋爱关系时）

  • ...the age when girls get interested in boys.

    姑娘开始对小伙子感兴趣的年龄

3 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 儿子 Someone's boy is their son. 儿子 [informal]

  • Eric was my cousin Edward's boy.

    埃里克是我表兄爱德华的儿子。

  • I have two boys.

    我有两个儿子。

SYN son  
4 N-COUNT 家伙，伙计（尤用于亲切谈论某人时） You can refer to a man as a boy, especially when you are talking about him in an affectionate way. 家伙，伙计（尤用于亲切谈论某人时） [informal, feelings]

  • ...the local boy who made President.

    当上总统的本地小伙子

  • 'Come on boys', he shouted to the sailors.

    “加把劲儿，伙计们”，他向船员们喊道。

5 N-VOC 家伙，伙计（用于吆喝马或狗时） You can use boy when giving instructions to a horse or dog. 家伙，伙计（用于吆喝马或狗时）

  • Down, boy, down!

    趴下，小家伙，趴下！

6 See also: backroom boy  blue-eyed boy  bully-boy  head boy  messenger boy  office boy  old boy  stable boy  Teddy boy 
7 PHRASE 警察 The police are sometimes referred to as the boys in blue . 警察 [old-fashioned]
8 EXCLAM 好家伙(表示激动、羡慕等情感） Some people say 'boy' or 'oh boy' in order to express feelings of excitement or admiration. 好家伙(表示激动、羡慕等情感） [mainly US, informal, feelings]

  • Oh Boy! Just think what I could tell him.

    哇，好家伙！想想看我会告诉他什么。

9 PHRASE 男人总归是男人（用以表示男人的典型行为不会改变） If you say boys will be boys, for example when a group of men are behaving noisily or aggressively, you are suggesting in a light-hearted way that this is typical male behaviour and will never change. 男人总归是男人（用以表示男人的典型行为不会改变）
10 PHRASE [usually verb-link PHRASE] 男子汉中的一员 If a man is described as one of the boys, he is accepted by a group of male friends who do things that are thought of as typically masculine. 男子汉中的一员

  • He wants to be accepted as one of the boys.

    他想被人看作一个男子汉。
