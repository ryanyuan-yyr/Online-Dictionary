disparity
dɪspærɪti dɪˈspærɪtɪ dɪˈspærəti
Word forms: plural disparities
N-VAR 不同;不等;差异;悬殊 If there is a disparity between two or more things, there is a noticeable difference between them. 不同;不等;差异;悬殊 [formal]

  • ...the economic disparities between East and West Berlin. [+ between/in]

    东西柏林之间经济上的悬殊

  • ...the great disparity of wealth between rich and poor countries.

    贫富国家间的财富鸿沟

SYN difference, gap, inequality, distinction
