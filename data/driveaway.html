drive away
PHRASAL VERB 使不愿久留;使想离去 To drive people away means to make them want to go away or stay away. 使不愿久留;使想离去

  • Patrick's boorish rudeness soon drove Monica's friends away. [VERB noun PREP.]

    帕特里克的粗鲁无礼很快使得莫妮卡的朋友们想走了。

  • Increased crime is driving away customers. [VERB PREP. noun]

    不断攀升的犯罪率把顾客们吓跑了。
