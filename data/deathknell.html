death knell
(also death-knell)
N-SING 丧钟;导致失败（或终结、毁灭）的原因 If you say that something sounds the death knell for a particular person or thing, you mean it will cause that person or thing to fail, end, or cease to exist. 丧钟;导致失败（或终结、毁灭）的原因

  • The tax increase sounded the death knell for the business. [+ for]

    税赋的增加为这个企业敲响了丧钟。

  • It's going to be the death knell of the red deer.

    这将导致马鹿的灭绝。
