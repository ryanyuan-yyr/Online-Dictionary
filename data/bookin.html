book in
(also book into)
PHRASAL VERB 登记入住（旅馆） When you book into a hotel or when you book in, you officially state that you have arrived to stay there, usually by signing your name in a register. 登记入住（旅馆） [British] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He was happy to book into the Royal Pavilion Hotel. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他很高兴能入住圣庭苑酒店。

  • Today Mahoney booked himself into one of the best hotels in Sydney. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    今天，马奥尼自己到悉尼最好的一家旅馆办理了入住手续。

  • The three men stayed at two hotels in Nottingham, booking in at one the day before the crime. [VERB PREP.]

    这3个人住在诺丁汉的两家旅馆里，而且都是在案发的前一天登记入住。

SYN register, enter, enrol
