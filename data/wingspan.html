wingspan
wɪŋspæn ˈwɪŋˌsprɛd ˈwɪŋˌspæn
(also wing span)Word forms: plural wingspans
N-COUNT [usually singular] (鸟、昆虫或飞机的)翼展，翼幅 The wingspan of a bird, insect, or aeroplane is the distance from the end of one wing to the end of the other wing. (鸟、昆虫或飞机的)翼展，翼幅

  • ...a glider with an 18-foot wingspan.

    翼展18英尺的滑翔机
