cybersquatting
saɪbəʳskwɒtɪŋ
Word forms: plural cybersquatters
N-UNCOUNT 抢注网络域名 Cybersquatting involves buying an Internet domain name that might be wanted by another person, business, or organization with the intention of selling it to them and making a profit. 抢注网络域名 [computing]
cybersquatter

  • The old official club website address has been taken over by cybersquatters.
