grandee
grændiː grænˈdi
Word forms: plural grandees
1 N-COUNT 大公（旧时西班牙的最高爵位） In the past, a grandee was a Spanish prince of the highest rank. 大公（旧时西班牙的最高爵位）
2 N-COUNT 大人物；显贵；（尤指）政要 You can refer to someone, especially a politician, who is upper class and has a lot of influence as a grandee . 大人物；显贵；（尤指）政要 [mainly British]

  • He is a former defence secretary of the United States and a grandee of the Democratic Party.

    他是美国前国防部长，也是民主党的显要人物。
