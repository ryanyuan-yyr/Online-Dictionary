short-staffed
ADJ-GRADED 人手短缺的;人员不足的 A company or place that is short-staffed does not have enough people working there. 人手短缺的;人员不足的 [mainly British]

  • The hospital is desperately short-staffed.

    这所医院人手极为短缺。

SYN undermanned, understaffed, short-handed, below strength
