moniker
mɒnɪkəʳ ˈmɒnɪkə ˈmɑnɪkər
Word forms: plural monikers
N-COUNT 名字；(尤指)曾用名；名称 The moniker of a person or thing is their name, especially when they have changed it. 名字；(尤指)曾用名；名称 [informal]

  • She's the author of three detective novels under the moniker of Janet Neel.

    她是3本署名为珍妮特·尼尔的侦探小说的作者。
