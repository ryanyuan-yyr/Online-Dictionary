concourse
kɒŋkɔːʳs ˈkɒnkɔːs ˈkɑnˌkɔrs
Word forms: plural concourses
1 N-COUNT (酒店、机场、车站等内的)大厅 A concourse is a wide hall in a public building, for example a hotel, airport, or station. (酒店、机场、车站等内的)大厅
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n] 一大群人 A concourse is a large group of people gathered together. 一大群人 [formal]

  • The great concourse of people seemed to have been similarly impressed.
