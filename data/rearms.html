rearm
riːɑːʳm riːˈɑːm riˈɑrm
(also re-arm)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rearms, present participle rearming, past tense , past participle rearmed
V-ERG 重新武装；重整军备 If a country rearms or is rearmed, it starts to build up a new stock of military weapons. 重新武装；重整军备

  • They neglected to rearm in time and left Britain exposed to disaster. [VERB]

    他们由于疏忽没有及时重整军备，结果使英国面临灾祸。

  • ...NATO's decision to rearm West Germany. [VERB noun]

    北约重新武装西德的决定
