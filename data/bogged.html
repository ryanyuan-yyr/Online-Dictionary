bog
bɒg bɑg bɔg
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bogs, present participle bogging, past tense , past participle bogged
1 N-COUNT 沼泽；湿地；泥塘 A bog is an area of land which is very wet and muddy. 沼泽；湿地；泥塘
2 N-COUNT [usually the NOUN] 厕所；茅厕 The bog is another name for the toilet. 厕所；茅厕 [British, informal]
Phrasal verbs:
bog down
