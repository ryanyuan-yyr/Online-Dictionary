feminist
femɪnɪst
Word forms: plural feminists
1 N-COUNT 女权主义者;男女平等主义者 A feminist is a person who believes in and supports feminism. 女权主义者;男女平等主义者

  • Only 16 per cent of young women in a 1990 survey considered themselves feminists.

    在 1990 年的一项调查中，只有 16％ 的年轻女性认为自己是女权主义者。

  • ...a daunting feminist academic.

    一位令人望而生畏的女性主义研究者

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 女权主义的;与女权主义相关的 Feminist groups, ideas, and activities are involved in feminism. 女权主义的;与女权主义相关的

  • ...the concerns addressed by the feminist movement.

    女权运动致力解决的问题

  • ...the reconstruction of history from a feminist perspective.

    从女性主义角度对历史的重构

Quotations
People call me a feminist whenever I express sentiments that differentiate me from a doormat or a prostituteRebecca West
