joint venture
dʒɔɪnt ˈvɛntʃə
Word forms: plural joint ventures
N-COUNT 合资企业（或项目）；合营企业（或项目） A joint venture is a business or project in which two or more companies or individuals have invested, with the intention of working together. 合资企业（或项目）；合营企业（或项目） [business]

  • It will be sold to a joint venture created by Dow Jones and Westinghouse Broadcasting.

    它将会出售给一家由道琼斯公司和西屋广播公司合资的公司。
