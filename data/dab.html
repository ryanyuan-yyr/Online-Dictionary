Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dabs, present participle dabbing, past tense , past participle dabbed
1 VERB 轻拍;轻触;轻拭;轻敷;轻涂 If you dab something, you touch it several times using quick, light movements. If you dab a substance onto a surface, you put it there using quick, light movements. 轻拍;轻触;轻拭;轻敷;轻涂 [Also VERB]

  • She arrived weeping, dabbing her eyes with a tissue. [VERB noun]

    她来的时候一边哭着，一边用纸巾抹着眼泪。

  • She spread the icing over the cake, dabbing it with a knife. [VERB noun]

    她把糖霜浇在蛋糕上面，并用刀子轻轻涂抹开。

  • She dabbed iodine on the cuts on her forehead. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    她把碘酒轻搽在前额的伤口上。

  • He dabbed at his lips with the napkin. [VERB + at]

    他用餐巾拭了拭嘴唇。

SYN apply, spread, smudge, daub  
2 N-COUNT (表面的)少量，少许，一点儿 A dab of something is a small amount of it that is put onto a surface. (表面的)少量，少许，一点儿 [informal]

  • She wore no make-up, not even a dab of lipstick.

    她没有化妆，甚至连一点儿口红都没涂。

  • ...a dab of glue. [+ of]

    点滴胶水

SYN spot [British] , little, bit, drop  
3 N-VAR 孙鲽;黄盖鲽 A dab is a small flat fish with rough scales. 孙鲽;黄盖鲽
