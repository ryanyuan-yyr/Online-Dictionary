reparation
repəreɪʃən ˌrɛpəˈreɪʃən
Word forms: plural reparations
1 N-UNCOUNT (战败国支付的)赔款 Reparations are sums of money that are paid after a war by the defeated country for the damage and injuries it caused in other countries. (战败国支付的)赔款

  • Israel accepted billions of dollars in war reparations.

    以色列得到了几十亿美元的战争赔款。

2 N-UNCOUNT 赔偿;弥补 Reparation is help or payment that someone gives you for damage, loss, or suffering that they have caused you. 赔偿;弥补

  • There is a clear demand amongst victims for some sort of reparation from offenders.

    受害者明确要求责任人给予一定的赔偿。

SYN compensation, damages, repair, satisfaction
