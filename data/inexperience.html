inexperience
ɪnɪkspɪəriəns ˌɪnɪkˈspɪərɪəns ˌɪnɛkˈspɪriəns ˌɪnɪkˈspɪriəns
N-UNCOUNT 缺乏经验;无经验 If you refer to someone's inexperience, you mean that they have little knowledge or experience of a particular situation or activity. 缺乏经验;无经验

  • Critics attacked the youth and inexperience of his staff. [+ of]

    批评家们抨击他的员工年纪太轻，缺乏经验。

SYN unfamiliarity, ignorance, newness, rawness
