bystander
baɪstændəʳ ˈbaɪˌstændər
Word forms: plural bystanders
N-COUNT 旁观者；局外人 A bystander is a person who is present when something happens and who sees it but does not take part in it. 旁观者；局外人

  • It looks like an innocent bystander was killed instead of you.

    看起来是一个无辜的旁观者当了你的替死鬼。

SYN onlooker, passer-by, spectator, witness
