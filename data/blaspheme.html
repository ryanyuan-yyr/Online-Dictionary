blaspheme
blæsfiːm blæsˈfim ˈblæsˌfim
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blasphemes, present participle blaspheming, past tense , past participle blasphemed
VERB 亵渎 If someone blasphemes, they say rude or disrespectful things about God or religion, or they use God's name as a swear word. 亵渎

  • 'Don't blaspheme,' my mother said. [VERB]

    “不要亵渎神明，”我母亲说。

  • The spiritual leader charged that the book blasphemed against Islam. [VERB + against]

    宗教领袖指责该书亵渎了伊斯兰教。

SYN curse, swear, abuse, revile  
blasphemer

  • Such a figure is liable to be attacked as a blasphemer.

    这样的人很容易被人抨击为亵渎神明。
