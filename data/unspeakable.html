unspeakable
ʌnspiːkəbəl ʌnˈspikəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 恶劣得说不出口的；坏透了的 If you describe something as unspeakable, you are emphasizing that it is extremely unpleasant. 恶劣得说不出口的；坏透了的 [emphasis]

  • ...the unspeakable horrors of chemical weapons.

    化学武器难以名状的可怖

  • The pain is unspeakable.

    疼起来厉害得无法形容。

  • ...unspeakable crimes.

    令人说不出口的罪行

SYN dreadful, shocking, appalling, evil  
unspeakably

  • The novel was unspeakably boring.

    这部小说极度无聊。
