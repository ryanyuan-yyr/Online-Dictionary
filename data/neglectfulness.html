neglectful
nɪglektfʊl nɪˈglɛktfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 疏忽的;不注意的;马虎的 If you describe someone as neglectful, you think they fail to do everything they should do to look after someone or something properly. 疏忽的;不注意的;马虎的

  • ...neglectful parents.

    漫不经心的父母

2 ADJ-GRADED 不负责的;不尽职的 If someone is neglectful of something, they do not give it the attention or consideration that it should be given. 不负责的;不尽职的

  • Have I been neglectful of my friend, taking him for granted? [+ of]

    我是不是对朋友不够尽心，太不把他当回事了？
