contender
kəntendəʳ kənˈtɛndər
Word forms: plural contenders
N-COUNT 竞争者;争夺者 A contender is someone who takes part in a competition. 竞争者;争夺者 [journalism] [Also + in]

  • Her trainer said yesterday that she would be a strong contender for a place in Britain's Olympic squad. [+ for]

    她的教练昨天说，她将是英国奥运代表队席位的有力争夺者。

  • Our British Affairs correspondent reports on how the three contenders reacted to the news of the resignation.

    我们的“英国国内事务”记者报道了3名竞争者对辞职消息的反应。

SYN competitor, rival, candidate, applicant
