streetlight
striːtlaɪt ˈstriːtˌlaɪt ˈstriːtˌlæmp ˈstritˌlaɪt
(also street light)Word forms: plural streetlights
N-COUNT 街灯;路灯 A streetlight is a tall post with a light at the top, which stands by the side of a road to light it up, usually in a town. 街灯;路灯

  • As the day darkened the streetlights came on.

    天色渐暗，街灯亮了。
