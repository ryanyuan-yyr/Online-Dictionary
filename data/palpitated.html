palpitate
pælpɪteɪt ˈpælpəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense palpitates, present participle palpitating, past tense , past participle palpitated
1 VERB （心脏）悸动，急速而不规则地跳动 If someone's heart palpitates, it beats very fast in an irregular way, because they are frightened or anxious. （心脏）悸动，急速而不规则地跳动

  • He felt suddenly faint, and his heart began to palpitate. [VERB]

    他突然感到眩晕，心脏开始悸动。

2 VERB 颤动；抖动 If something palpitates, it shakes or seems to shake. 颤动；抖动 [literary] [Also VERB]

  • She lay on the bed, her eyes closed and her bosom palpitating. [VERB-ing]

    她躺在床上，闭着眼，胸部起伏不定。
