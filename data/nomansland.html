no-man's land
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 无人地带;无人管辖地区 No-man's land is an area of land that is not owned or controlled by anyone, for example the area of land between two opposing armies. 无人地带;无人管辖地区

  • In Tobruk, leading a patrol in no-man's land, he was blown up by a mortar bomb.

    他带领巡逻队在图卜鲁格无人区巡逻时遭迫击炮弹击中身亡。

  • ...the no-man's land between the Jordanian and Iraqi frontier posts. [+ between]

    约旦和伊拉克边境哨所之间的无人地带

2 N-SING 尚未确定的情况;中间状态 If you refer to a situation as a no-man's land between different things, you mean that it seems unclear because it does not fit into any of the categories. 尚未确定的情况;中间状态

  • The play is set in the dangerous no-man's land between youth and adolescence. [+ between]

    这部戏描写的是青年时期和青春期之间那段危险、暖昧的时光。

  • The failure has helped to drive Labour even further into a no-man's land of ideological muddle.

    失败使得工党内部的意识形态混乱局面进一步加剧。
