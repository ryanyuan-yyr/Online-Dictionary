untrustworthy
ʌntrʌstwɜːʳði ʌnˈtrʌstˌwɜːðɪ
ADJ-GRADED 不可信的；不可靠的 If you say that someone is untrustworthy, you think they are unreliable and cannot be trusted. 不可信的；不可靠的

  • I think he is shallow, vain and untrustworthy.

    我觉得他为人浅薄，好虚荣，靠不住。

  • His opponents still say he's a fundamentally untrustworthy figure.

    他的对手依然说他根本不可信。

SYN unreliable, treacherous, deceitful, false
