fib
fɪb fɪb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fibs, present participle fibbing, past tense , past participle fibbed
1 N-COUNT 小谎;无伤大雅的谎话 A fib is a small, unimportant lie. 小谎;无伤大雅的谎话 [informal]

  • She told innocent fibs like anyone else.

    她也像别人一样，说些无伤大雅的谎话。

SYN lie, story [informal] , fiction, untruth  
2 VERB 说谎;撒谎;说假话 If someone is fibbing, they are telling lies. 说谎;撒谎;说假话 [informal]

  • He laughs loudly when I accuse him of fibbing. [VERB]

    当我指责他说谎时，他大笑起来。

SYN lie, invent, fabricate, misrepresent  
Quotations
fib: a lie that has not cut its teethAmbrose Bierce The Devil's Dictionary
