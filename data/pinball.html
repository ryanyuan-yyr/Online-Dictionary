pinball
pɪnbɔːl
N-UNCOUNT 弹球戏；弹珠台游戏 Pinball is a game in which a player presses two buttons on each side of a pinball machine in order to hit a small ball to the top of the machine. The aim of the game is to prevent the ball reaching the bottom of the machine by pressing the buttons. 弹球戏；弹珠台游戏
