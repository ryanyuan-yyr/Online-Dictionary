tiddlywink
tɪdəliwɪŋk ˈtɪdəlɪˌwɪŋk ˈtɪdəldɪˌwɪŋk
Word forms: plural tiddlywinks
1 N-UNCOUNT 弹塑料片游戏 Tiddlywinks is a game in which the players try to make small round pieces of plastic jump into a container, by pressing their edges with a larger piece of plastic. 弹塑料片游戏
2 N-COUNT (弹塑料片游戏所用的)塑料圆片 Tiddlywinks are the small round piece of plastic used in the game of tiddlywinks. (弹塑料片游戏所用的)塑料圆片
