purdah
pɜːʳdə ˈpɜrdə
N-UNCOUNT [oft in NOUN] （伊斯兰或印度教社会的）置身深闺，深闺制度 Purdah is a custom practised in some Muslim and Hindu societies, in which women either remain in a special part of the house or cover their faces and bodies to avoid being seen by men who are not related to them. If a woman is in purdah, she lives according to this custom. （伊斯兰或印度教社会的）置身深闺，深闺制度
