marrow bone
ˈmærəʊ bəʊn
(also marrowbone)Word forms: plural marrow bones
N-VAR (某些动物的)髓骨（尤指牛骨，供烹饪或制狗食用） Marrow bones are the bones of certain animals, especially cows, that contain a lot of bone marrow. They are used in cooking and in dog food. (某些动物的)髓骨（尤指牛骨，供烹饪或制狗食用）

    跟肉铺老板要煲汤用的骨头(最好是髓骨)。
