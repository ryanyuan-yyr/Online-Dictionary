self-addressed
ˈsɛlfəˈdrɛst
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (信封)写明发信人地址的，回邮的 A self-addressed envelope is an envelope which you have written your address on and which you send to someone in another envelope so that they can send something back to you. (信封)写明发信人地址的，回邮的
