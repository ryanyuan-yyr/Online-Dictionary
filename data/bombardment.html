bombardment
bɒmbɑːʳdmənt
Word forms: plural bombardments
1 N-VAR （猛烈而持久的）炮击，轰炸 A bombardment is a strong and continuous attack of gunfire or bombing. （猛烈而持久的）炮击，轰炸

  • The city has been flattened by heavy artillery bombardments.

    城市已被猛烈的炮火夷为平地。

  • The capital is still under constant bombardment by the rebel forces.

    首都仍在遭受叛军的持续轰炸。

SYN bombing, attack, fire, assault  
2 N-VAR 一连串（想法、要求、质问或批评） A bombardment of ideas, demands, questions, or criticisms is an aggressive and exhausting stream of them. 一连串（想法、要求、质问或批评）

  • ...the constant bombardment of images urging that work was important. [+ of]

    铺天盖地的图片，强调工作是至关重要的
