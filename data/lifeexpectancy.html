life expectancy
Word forms: plural life expectancies
N-UNCOUNT 预期寿命；平均（期望）寿命 The life expectancy of a person, animal, or plant is the length of time that they are normally likely to live. 预期寿命；平均（期望）寿命

  • The average life expectancy was 40.

    平均期望寿命曾是40岁。

  • They had longer life expectancies than their parents.

    他们比上一辈人的预期寿命要长。
