repot
riːpɒt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense repots, present participle repotting, past tense , past participle repotted
VERB 移植；给（所养植物)换大容器 If you repot a plant, you take it out of its pot and put it in a larger one. 移植；给（所养植物)换大容器

  • As your plants flourish, you'll need to repot them in bigger pots. [VERB noun]

    随着植物越来越茂盛，你需要把它们移植到更大的花盆里。
