proviso
prəvaɪzoʊ prəˈvaɪˌzoʊ proʊˈvaɪˌzoʊ
Word forms: plural provisos
N-COUNT [oft NOUN that] 限制性条件；附文；（附带）条件；但书 A proviso is a condition in an agreement. You agree to do something if this condition is fulfilled. 限制性条件；附文；（附带）条件；但书

  • I told Norman I would invest in his venture as long as he agreed to one proviso.

    我告诉诺曼只要他答应一个附带条件，我就会为他的项目投资。

  • Okay, with the proviso that Jane agrees, I accept.

    好，如果简答应，我就接受。

SYN condition, requirement, provision, strings
