permutation
pɜːʳmjuːteɪʃən ˌpɜrmjuˈteɪʃən
Word forms: plural permutations
N-COUNT [usually plural] 排列 A permutation is one of the ways in which a number of things can be ordered or arranged. 排列

  • Variation among humans is limited to the possible permutations of our genes. [+ of]

    人类的变异受限于我们的基因可能存在哪些排列。

SYN transformation, change, shift, variation
