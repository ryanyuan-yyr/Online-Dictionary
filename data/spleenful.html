spleen
spliːn splin
Word forms: plural spleens
1 N-COUNT 脾;脾脏 Your spleen is an organ near your stomach that controls the quality of your blood. 脾;脾脏
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually poss NOUN] 大发脾气;大怒 Spleen is great and bitter anger. 大发脾气;大怒 [formal]

  • Paul Fussell's latest book vents his spleen against everything he hates about his country.

    保罗·富塞尔在新作中发泄了他对国家的所有不满。

  • There were other targets for Mr Livingston's spleen.

    让利文斯顿先生大怒的还有其他一些人。

SYN spite, anger, bitterness, hostility
