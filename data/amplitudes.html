amplitude
æmplɪtjuːd -tuːd ˈæmpləˌtud ˈæmpləˌtjud
Word forms: plural amplitudes
1 N-VAR （声波或电流信号的）振幅 In physics, the amplitude of a sound wave or electrical signal is its strength. （声波或电流信号的）振幅 [technical]

  • As we fall asleep the amplitude of brain waves slowly becomes greater.

    进入睡眠状态时，我们的脑电波振幅慢慢变大。

2 N-UNCOUNT 广大;广阔;大量 Amplitude is the quality of being large in size or quantity. 广大;广阔;大量 [formal]

  • ...a man of nineteenth-century amplitude.

    有着19世纪的广阔思维的男子
