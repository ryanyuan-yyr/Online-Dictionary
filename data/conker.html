conker
kɒŋkəʳ ˈkɑŋkər
Word forms: plural conkers
1 N-COUNT 七叶树果 Conkers are round brown nuts which come from horse chestnut trees. 七叶树果 [British]
2 N-UNCOUNT 康克戏(一种儿童游戏，双方用系在细绳上的七叶树果猛击对方的七叶树果，击破对方者获胜） Conkers is a children's game in which you tie a conker to a piece of string and try to break your opponent's conker by hitting it as hard as you can with your own. 康克戏(一种儿童游戏，双方用系在细绳上的七叶树果猛击对方的七叶树果，击破对方者获胜） [British]
