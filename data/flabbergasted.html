flabbergasted
flæbəʳgɑːstɪd -gæst-
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 大为惊讶的;目瞪口呆的 If you say that you are flabbergasted, you are emphasizing that you are extremely surprised. 大为惊讶的;目瞪口呆的 [emphasis]

  • Everybody was flabbergasted when I announced I was going to emigrate to Australia.

    我宣布要移民去澳大利亚时，所有人都惊呆了。

SYN astonished, amazed, stunned, overcome
