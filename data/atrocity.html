atrocity
ətrɒsɪti əˈtrɑsəti
Word forms: plural atrocities
N-VAR 暴行；滔天罪行 An atrocity is a very cruel, shocking action. 暴行；滔天罪行

  • The killing was cold-blooded, and those who committed this atrocity should be tried and punished.

    杀戮异常残暴。那些犯下如此暴行的人都应该受到审判和惩罚。

SYN act of cruelty, wrong, crime, horror
