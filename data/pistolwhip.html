pistol-whip ˈpɪstəlˌhwɪp ˈpɪstəlˌwɪp
Word forms: pistol-whips, pistol-whipping, pistol-whipped
V-T 用手枪柄打If someone pistol-whips another person, they hit them or beat them with the barrel of a handgun. 用手枪柄打 [mainly US]

  • Three of the victims were pistol-whipped and two were shot.

    三个被害者被用手枪柄打过，两个遭到枪击。

  • He allegedly pistol-whipped a man he saw kissing his wife.

    据说，他用手枪柄打了他亲眼所见亲吻他妻子的男子。
