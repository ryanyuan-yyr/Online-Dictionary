rate of return
Word forms: plural rates of return
N-COUNT 回报率；收益率 The rate of return on an investment is the amount of profit it makes, often shown as a percentage of the original investment. 回报率；收益率 [business]

  • High rates of return can be earned on these investments.

    这些投资可能会有很高的回报率。

  • ...an 8 per cent average rate of return.

    平均8%的收益率
