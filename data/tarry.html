tarry
tæri tɑːri ˈtɑri ˈtæri
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense tarries, present participle tarrying, past tense , past participle tarried
The verb is pronounced /'tæri/. The adjective is pronounced /'tɑːri/. 动词读作 /'tæri/。形容词读作 /'tɑːri/。
1 VERB 逗留;停留;徘徊 If you tarry somewhere, you stay there longer than you meant to and delay leaving. 逗留;停留;徘徊 [old-fashioned]

  • Two old boys tarried on the street corner discussing cattle. [VERB]

    两个大男孩在街角停留许久，一直在讨论牛。

SYN linger, remain, loiter, wait  
2 ADJ 柏油含量高的;像柏油的 If you describe something as tarry, you mean that it has a lot of tar in it or is like tar. 柏油含量高的;像柏油的

  • I smelled tarry melted asphalt.

    我闻到了柏油熔化了的味道。

  • ...cups of tarry coffee.

    几杯苦咖啡
