paradigm
pærədaɪm ˈpærəˌdaɪm ˈpærədɪm
Word forms: plural paradigms
1 N-VAR 样板；范式 A paradigm is a model for something which explains it or shows how it can be produced. 样板；范式 [formal]

  • ...a new paradigm of production. [+ of]

    新的生产范式

2 N-COUNT 典范；范例 A paradigm is a clear and typical example of something. 典范；范例 [formal]

  • He had become the paradigm of the successful man. [+ of]

    他已经成为成功人士的典范。

SYN model, example, original, pattern
