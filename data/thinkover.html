think over
PHRASAL VERB 仔细考虑;认真思考 If you think something over, you consider it carefully before making a decision. 仔细考虑;认真思考

  • She said she needs time to think it over. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她说，她需要时间好好想一想。

  • I suggest you think over your position very carefully. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我建议你认真考虑一下你的处境。

SYN consider, contemplate, ponder, reflect upon
