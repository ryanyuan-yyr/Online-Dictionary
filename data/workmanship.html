workmanship
wɜːʳkmənʃɪp ˈwɜrkmənˌʃɪp
N-UNCOUNT 工艺;手艺 Workmanship is the skill with which something is made and which affects the appearance and quality of the finished object. 工艺;手艺

  • The problem may be due to poor workmanship.

    问题可能在于工艺太差。

  • The standard of workmanship is very high.

    工艺精湛。

SYN skill, work, art, technique
