injurious
ɪndʒʊəriəs ɪnˈdʒʊriəs
ADJ-GRADED 有害的;致伤的 Something that is injurious to someone or to their health or reputation is harmful or damaging to them. 有害的;致伤的 [formal]

  • ...substances that are injurious to health. [+ to]

    有害健康的物质

  • Stress in itself is not necessarily injurious.

    压力本身未必有害。

SYN harmful, bad, damaging, corrupting
