hem in
1 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 包围;环绕 If a place is hemmed in by mountains or by other places, it is surrounded by them. 包围;环绕

  • Manchester is hemmed in by greenbelt countryside and by housing and industrial areas. [be V-ed P + by]

    曼彻斯特周围环绕着绿色乡村带、住宅区和工业区。

  • The brick path to the door was hemmed in on either side by tall, unkempt boxwood hedges. [be V-ed P by n]

    通往门口的砖路两旁是高大、杂乱的黄杨树篱。

2 PHRASAL VERB 困住;限制;约束 If someone is hemmed in or if someone hems them in, they are prevented from moving or changing, for example because they are surrounded by people or obstacles. 困住;限制;约束 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The company's competitors complain that they are hemmed in by rigid legal contracts. [be V-ed P + by]

    公司的竞争对手抱怨他们受到严格的法律合同的约束。
