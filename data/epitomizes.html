epitomize
ɪpɪtəmaɪz ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense epitomizes, present participle epitomizing, past tense , past participle epitomized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 epitomise
VERB 成为…的典范;作为…的缩影 If you say that something or someone epitomizes a particular thing, you mean that they are a perfect example of it. 成为…的典范;作为…的缩影

  • Lyonnais cooking is epitomized by the so-called 'bouchons'. [be VERB-ed + by]

    被称作bouchon的里昂小餐馆是里昂饮食的缩影。

  • ...the sleek lift that epitomized the hotel's glossy decor. [VERB noun]

    作为酒店浮华装潢之缩影的豪华电梯
