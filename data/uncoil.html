uncoil
ʌnkɔɪl ʌnˈkɔɪl ʌnˈkɔɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense uncoils, present participle uncoiling, past tense , past participle uncoiled
V-ERG （卷绕之物）展开，伸展；展开，将…伸展开 If something uncoils or if you uncoil it, it becomes straight after it has been wound or curled up. If someone who is curled up uncoils, they move so that their body becomes straight. （卷绕之物）展开，伸展；展开，将…伸展开

  • He uncoiled the hose and gave them a thorough drenching. [VERB noun]

    他把软管铺展开来，把它们彻底冲洗了一下。

  • Dan played with the tangerine peel, letting it uncoil and then coil again. [VERB]

    丹摆弄着那片橘子皮，将它不停地展开来卷回去。

  • Mack seemed to uncoil slowly up into a standing position. [VERB]

    麦克慢慢直起身站了起来。
