consolation prize
Word forms: plural consolation prizes
1 N-COUNT 鼓励奖;安慰奖 A consolation prize is a small prize which is given to a person who fails to win a competition. 鼓励奖;安慰奖
2 N-COUNT 给人安慰的事物 A consolation prize is something that is arranged for or is given to a person to make them feel happier when they have failed to achieve something better. 给人安慰的事物

  • Her appointment was seen as a consolation prize after she lost the election.

    大家认为对她的任命只不过是落选之后的安慰罢了。
