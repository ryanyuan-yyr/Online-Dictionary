present-day ˈprɛzəntˈdeɪ
(also present day)
ADJ [ADJ n] 现时的；当前的；当代的 Present-day things, situations, and people exist at the time in history we are now in. 现时的；当前的；当代的

  • Even by present-day standards these were large aircraft.

    即使按照现在的标准，这些飞机也挺大的。

  • ...a huge area of northern India, stretching from present-day Afghanistan to Bengal.

    印度北部的大片地区，从现在的阿富汗一直延伸到孟加拉

  • ...present-day champions of the cause.

    目前这项事业的捍卫者

SYN current, modern, present, recent
