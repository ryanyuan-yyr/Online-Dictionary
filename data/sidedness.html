-sided -saɪdɪd ˈsaɪdɪd
1 COMB in ADJ [usually ADJ n] (与数词或形容词连用)表示“有…个边的”，“有…面的” -sided combines with numbers or adjectives to describe how many sides something has, or what kind of sides something has. (与数词或形容词连用)表示“有…个边的”，“有…面的”

  • ...a three-sided pyramid.

    三面棱锥

  • We drove up a steep-sided valley.

    我们沿着陡峭的峡谷向上开。

2See also:one-sided
