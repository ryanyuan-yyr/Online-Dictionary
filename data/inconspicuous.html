inconspicuous
ɪnkənspɪkjuəs ˌɪnkənˈspɪkjuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 不引人注目的;不惹人注意的 Someone who is inconspicuous does not attract attention to themselves. 不引人注目的;不惹人注意的

  • I'll try to be as inconspicuous as possible.

    我会尽量不惹人注意。

  • He had ridden out of Cambridge, inconspicuous among the hundreds of young cyclists.

    他已骑车出了剑桥,淹没在几百名年轻的自行车手当中。

SYN unobtrusive, hidden, unnoticeable, retiring  
inconspicuously

  • I sat inconspicuously in a corner.

    我不起眼地坐在角落里。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不显眼的;不显著的 Something that is inconspicuous is not easily seen or does not attract attention because it is small, ordinary, or hidden away. 不显眼的;不显著的

  • ...an inconspicuous grey building.

    不显眼的灰色建筑

SYN plain, ordinary, modest, unobtrusive
