dangerous
deɪndʒərəs ˈdeɪndʒərəs ˈdeɪndʒərəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 危险的;可能带来危害的;不安全的 If something is dangerous, it is able or likely to hurt or harm you. 危险的;可能带来危害的;不安全的

  • It's a dangerous stretch of road.

    那段路很不安全。

  • ...dangerous drugs.

    危险的毒品

  • It's dangerous to jump to early conclusions.

    过早地下结论可能会带来严重后果。

SYN perilous, threatening, risky, hazardous  
dangerously

  • He is dangerously ill.

    他病得很重。

  • He rushed downstairs dangerously fast.

    他以极危险的速度猛冲下楼。

  • The coach rocked dangerously.

    那辆长途汽车危险地颠簸摇晃。
