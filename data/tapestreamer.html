tape streamer
Word forms: plural tape streamers
N-COUNT 磁带机(利用磁带备份硬盘的设备) A tape streamer is a piece of computer equipment that you use for copying data from a hard disk onto magnetic tape for security or storage. 磁带机(利用磁带备份硬盘的设备) [computing]

  • Do not use a bit-image method of backup (like a tape streamer), because this will copy the virus.

    不要使用位元影像的方法(如磁带机)备份，因为这样会将病毒一起拷贝下来。
