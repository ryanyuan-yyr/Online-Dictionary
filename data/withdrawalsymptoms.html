withdrawal symptoms
wɪðˈdrɔːəl ˈsɪmptəmz
N-PLURAL 戒断症状;脱瘾综合征 When someone has withdrawal symptoms, they feel ill after they have stopped taking a drug which they were addicted to. 戒断症状;脱瘾综合征

  • If these drugs are stopped abruptly then some withdrawal symptoms may occur.

    如果突然停服这些药，则可能出现一些戒断症状。
