black and white blæk ənd waɪt
(also black-and-white)
1 COLOUR （照片或电影）黑白的 In a black and white photograph or film, everything is shown in black, white, and grey. （照片或电影）黑白的

  • ...a black-and-white photo of the two of us together.

    我俩的黑白合影

  • ...old black and white film footage.

    老黑白电影胶片

  • The pictures were in black and white.

    这些照片是黑白的。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （电视机）显示黑白图像的 A black and white television set shows only black-and-white pictures. （电视机）显示黑白图像的
3 ADJ 简单明了的；容易判别的；泾渭分明的 A black and white issue or situation is one which involves issues which seem simple and therefore easy to make decisions about. 简单明了的；容易判别的；泾渭分明的

  • But this isn't a simple black and white affair, Marianne.

    但这不是一个非黑即白的问题，玛丽安娜。

  • She saw things in black and white.

    她总是用简单绝对的眼光看待事物。

4 PHRASE 白纸黑字；以书面形式 You say that something is in black and white when it has been written or printed, and not just said. 白纸黑字；以书面形式

  • He'd seen the proof in black and white.

    他已经看过书面证据。

  • Maybe you don't want to read about Doug's death in black and white.

    也许你不想看有关道格死亡的书面材料。
