quotient
kwoʊʃənt ˈkwoʊʃənt
Word forms: plural quotients
N-COUNT [usually singular, usually n NOUN] 程度;系数 Quotient is used when indicating the presence or degree of a characteristic in someone or something. intelligence quotient→see: IQ  程度;系数

  • Being rich doesn't actually increase your happiness quotient.

    有钱不见得能提高你的幸福指数。

  • The island has a high quotient of clergymen. [+ of]

    牧师占了小岛人口的很大一部分。
