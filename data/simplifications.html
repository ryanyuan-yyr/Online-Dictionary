simplification
sɪmplɪfɪkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural simplifications
1 N-COUNT 简化的事物 You can use simplification to refer to the thing that is produced when you make something simpler or when you reduce it to its basic elements. 简化的事物

  • Like any such diagram, it is a simplification.

    像其他这种表格一样，这是简化了的。

2 N-UNCOUNT 简化 Simplification is the act or process of making something simpler. 简化

  • Everyone favours the simplification of court procedures. [+ of]

    每个人都希望法庭程序能够简化。
