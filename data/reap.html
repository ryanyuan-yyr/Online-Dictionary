reap
riːp rip
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reaps, present participle reaping, past tense , past participle reaped
1 VERB 获得；收获；得到；取得 If you reap the benefits or the rewards of something, you enjoy the good things that happen as a result of it. 获得；收获；得到；取得

  • You'll soon begin to reap the benefits of being fitter. [VERB noun]

    你很快就会体会到身体更健康带来的好处。

  • We are not in this to reap immense financial rewards. [VERB noun]

    我们从事这一行不是为了获得巨大的经济回报。

SYN get, win, gain, obtain  
2 VERB 收割，收获（农作物） To reap crops means to cut them down and gather them. 收割，收获（农作物）

  • The painting depicted a group of peasants reaping a harvest of fruits and vegetables. [VERB noun]

    这幅画描绘了一群农民收获水果和蔬菜的情景。

SYN collect, gather, bring in, harvest
