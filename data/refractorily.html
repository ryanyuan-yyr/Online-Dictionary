refractory
rɪfræktəri rɪˈfræktəri
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不服管束的；难以控制的 Refractory people are difficult to deal with or control, for example because they are unwilling to obey orders. 不服管束的；难以控制的 [formal]

  • ...refractory priests who refused to side with the king.

    拒绝支持国王的桀骜不驯的牧师

SYN unmanageable, difficult, stubborn, contentious
