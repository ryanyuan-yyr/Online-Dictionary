goings-on
ˈgoʊɪŋzˈɑn
N-PLURAL 怪异的行为；有趣的事；令人发笑的举动；见不得人的行为 If you describe events or activities as goings-on, you mean that they are strange, interesting, amusing, or dishonest. 怪异的行为；有趣的事；令人发笑的举动；见不得人的行为

  • The girl had found out about the goings-on in the factory.

    这个女孩已经发现了工厂里的勾当。

  • ...strange goings-on between Cantrell and his men.

    坎特雷尔和他的手下之间的反常表现

SYN incidents, happenings, events, carry-on
