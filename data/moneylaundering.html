money laundering
ˈmʌnɪ ˈlɔːndərɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 洗钱 Money laundering is the crime of processing stolen money through a legitimate business or sending it abroad to a foreign bank, to hide the fact that the money was illegally obtained. 洗钱

  • ...the largest money-laundering scandal in history.
