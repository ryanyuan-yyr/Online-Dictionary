discontent
dɪskəntent ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt ˈdɪskənˈtɛnt
Word forms: plural discontents
N-UNCOUNT 不满;不满意 Discontent is the feeling that you have when you are not satisfied with your situation. 不满;不满意

  • There are reports of widespread discontent in the capital.

    有报道称首都弥漫着不满的情绪。

SYN dissatisfaction, unhappiness, displeasure, regret
