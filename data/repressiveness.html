repressive
rɪpresɪv
ADJ-GRADED (政府)压制的，专制的，高压的 A repressive government is one that restricts people's freedom and controls them by using force. (政府)压制的，专制的，高压的 [disapproval]

  • The military regime in power was unpopular and repressive.

    当权的军政府压制人民，不得人心。

SYN oppressive, tough, severe, absolute  
repressively

  • ...the country, which had been repressively ruled for ten years.

    10年来一直受高压统治的国家
