rove
roʊv roʊv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense roves, present participle roving, past tense , past participle roved
1 VERB 漫游;流浪;漂泊 If someone roves about an area or roves an area, they wander around it. 漫游;流浪;漂泊 [literary]

  • ...roving about the town in the dead of night and seeing something peculiar. [VERB prep./adv.]

    夜深人静时在小镇游走，看见一些怪异的景象

  • She became a photographer, roving the world with her camera in her hand. [VERB noun]

    她成了一个摄影师，拿着相机周游世界。

SYN wander, range, cruise, drift  
2 VERB (眼睛)环视，扫视 If you say that someone's eyes rove round a place, you mean that they are looking around to see what is interesting. (眼睛)环视，扫视 [literary]

  • Houston's eyes roved restlessly about the room. [VERB prep.]

    休斯敦不安地扫视着这个房间。

  • His eyes roved to see how many of the group appreciated his heavy humour. [VERB]

    他环视四周，看看有多少人能理解他重口味的幽默。
