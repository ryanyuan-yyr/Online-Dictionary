laundry
lɔːndri ˈlɔndri
Word forms: plural laundries
1 N-UNCOUNT 将要（或正在）洗的衣物；刚洗好的衣物 Laundry is used to refer to clothes, sheets, and towels that are about to be washed, are being washed, or have just been washed. 将要（或正在）洗的衣物；刚洗好的衣物

  • I'll do your laundry.

    我来帮你洗衣服。

  • ...the room where I hang the laundry.

    我晾挂衣服的房间

  • He'd put his dirty laundry in the clothes basket. [+ in]

    他会把自己的脏衣服扔进洗衣篮里。

2 N-COUNT 洗衣公司；洗衣店 A laundry is a firm that washes and irons clothes, sheets, and towels for people. 洗衣公司；洗衣店

  • We had to have the washing done at the laundry.

    我们得把衣服拿到洗衣店去洗。

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 洗衣间；洗衣房 A laundry or a laundry room is a room in a house, hotel, or institution where clothes, sheets, and towels are washed. 洗衣间；洗衣房

  • He worked in the laundry at Oxford prison. [+ at]

    他在牛津监狱的洗衣房工作。
