brevity
brevɪti ˈbrɛvəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 简洁；短暂；短促 The brevity of something is the fact that it is short or lasts for only a short time. 简洁；短暂；短促 [formal]

  • The bonus of this homely soup is the brevity of its cooking time. [+ of]

    这种家常汤的额外优点是烹制时间短。

  • The brevity of the letter concerned me.

    我担心这封信写得太短。

SYN conciseness, economy, crispness, concision  
Quotations
Brevity is the soul of witWilliam Shakespeare Hamlet
