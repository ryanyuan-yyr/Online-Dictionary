ballsy
bɔːlzi ˈbɔlzi
Word forms: comparative ballsier, superlative ballsiest
ADJ-GRADED （对某人或其行为表示赞赏）充满活力的，有胆量的 You can describe a person or their behaviour as ballsy if you admire them because you think they are energetic and brave. （对某人或其行为表示赞赏）充满活力的，有胆量的 [informal, approval]

  • ...the most ballsy woman I know.

    我认识的最敢作敢为的女子

  • ...ballsy, gutsy live rap music.

    狂野热烈的现场说唱乐
