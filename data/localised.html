localized
loʊkəlaɪzd ˈləʊkəˌlaɪzd
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 localised
ADJ-GRADED 局部的；未扩散的 Something that is localized remains within a small area and does not spread. 局部的；未扩散的

  • She had localized breast cancer and both of her doctors had advised surgery.

    她的乳癌尚未扩散，两位主刀医生均建议她动手术。

  • Community radio is a concept which is much more localised.

    社区广播是一个将服务深度局域化的概念。

SYN contained, limited, restricted, confined
