satisfaction
sætɪsfækʃən ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 满意;满足;称心 Satisfaction is the pleasure that you feel when you do something or get something that you wanted or needed to do or get. 满意;满足;称心

  • She felt a small glow of satisfaction.

    她有了一丝满足感。

  • Both sides expressed satisfaction with the progress so far. [+ with]

    双方对目前的进展都表示满意。

  • I doubt I'll ever get rich, but I get job satisfaction.

    我觉得我不大可能会腰缠万贯，但我得到了工作上的满足。

SYN fulfilment, pleasure, achievement, joy  
2 N-UNCOUNT 赔偿;补偿 If you get satisfaction from someone, you get money or an apology from them because you have been treated badly. 赔偿;补偿

  • If you can't get any satisfaction, complain to the park owner.

    如果你得不到任何赔偿，向园区所有者投诉。

3 PHRASE 令…满意 If you do something to someone's satisfaction, they are happy with the way that you have done it. 令…满意

  • She never could seem to do anything right or to his satisfaction.

    她似乎从没能做过一件正确或令他满意的事。

  • It is hard to see how the issue can be resolved to everyone's satisfaction.

    很难想象这个问题如何解决才能让每个人都满意。
