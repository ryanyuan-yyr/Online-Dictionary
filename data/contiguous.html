contiguous
kəntɪgjuəs kənˈtɪgjuəs
ADJ 相接的;相邻的 Things that are contiguous are next to each other or touch each other. 相接的;相邻的 [formal]

  • Its vineyards are virtually contiguous with those of Ausone. [+ with]

    其葡萄园差不多紧挨着欧颂堡的葡萄园。

  • ...two years of travel throughout the 48 contiguous states. [+ to]

    两年游遍美国本土的48州

SYN near, touching, bordering, neighbouring
