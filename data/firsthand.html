first hand fɜːst hænd
(also first-hand,firsthand)
ADJ [ADJ n] 第一手的;直接的;亲身的 First hand information or experience is gained or learned directly, rather than from other people or from books. →see: first  第一手的;直接的;亲身的

  • School trips give children firsthand experience not available in the classroom.

    学校组织的出游能让孩子们获得在课堂上得不到的亲身体验。

First-hand is also an adverb.

  • We've been through Germany and seen first-hand what's happening there.

    我们遍游德国,亲眼目睹了那里发生的一切。at first hand
