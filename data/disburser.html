disburse
dɪsbɜːʳs dɪsˈbɜrs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disburses, present participle disbursing, past tense , past participle disbursed
VERB (通常指从专用资金中)支付，支出 To disburse an amount of money means to pay it out, usually from a fund which has been collected for a particular purpose. (通常指从专用资金中)支付，支出 [formal]

  • The aid will not be disbursed until next year. [be VERB-ed]

    援助款到明年才会下拨。

  • The bank has disbursed over $350m for the project. [VERB noun]

    银行已经为这个项目支付了超过 3.5亿美元。

SYN pay out, spend, lay out, fork out [slang]
