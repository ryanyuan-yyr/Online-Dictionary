single out
PHRASAL VERB 单独挑出;特别选出 If you single someone out from a group, you choose them and give them special attention or treatment. 单独挑出;特别选出 [Also V n P + as]

  • The gunman had singled Debilly out and waited for him. [VERB noun PREP.]

    那名枪手瞄准了德比利，伺机而动。

  • His immediate superior has singled him out for a special mention. [V n P + for]

    他的顶头上司特别提到了他。

  • We wanted to single out the main threat to civilisation. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们想特别指出威胁文明的主要因素。
