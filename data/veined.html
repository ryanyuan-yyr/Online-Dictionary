veined
veɪnd veɪnd veɪnd
1 ADJ （皮肤）显出静脉的,青筋暴露的 Veined skin has a lot of veins showing through it. （皮肤）显出静脉的,青筋暴露的

  • Helen's hands were thin and veined.

    海伦的双手枯瘦，青筋凸起。

Veined is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...a man who had blue-veined cheeks.

    脸颊上青筋暴露的男子

2 ADJ 有纹理的;有脉纹的 Something that is veined has a pattern or colouring like that of veins showing through skin. 有纹理的;有脉纹的

  • ...a bronze ashtray shaped like a veined leaf.

    外形如一片叶脉清晰的叶子的铜制烟灰缸

Veined is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...this distinctive blue-veined cheese.

    这种有蓝色纹理的独特奶酪
