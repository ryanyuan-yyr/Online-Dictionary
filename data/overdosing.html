overdose
oʊvəʳdoʊs ˈoʊvərˌdoʊs ˌ oʊvərˈdoʊs
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense overdoses, present participle overdosing, past tense , past participle overdosed
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] （药物等的）过量；致死（或中毒）剂量 If someone takes an overdose of a drug, they take more of it than is safe. （药物等的）过量；致死（或中毒）剂量

  • Each year, one in 100 girls aged 15-19 takes an overdose.

    每年，每100名15至19岁的少女中就有1位嗑药过量。

  • In 1970, guitarist Jimi Hendrix died of a drug overdose.

    1970年，吉他手吉米·亨德里克斯因服药过量而死亡。

2 VERB 过量服用（药物）；过量吸食（毒品） If someone overdoses on a drug, they take more of it than is safe. 过量服用（药物）；过量吸食（毒品）

  • He'd overdosed on heroin. [VERB + on]

    他曾过量吸食海洛因。

  • Medical opinion varies on how many tablets it takes to overdose. [VERB]

    医生对于服用多少药片算作过量意见不一。

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] （尤指有害物的）过量，过多 You can refer to too much of something, especially something harmful, as an overdose. （尤指有害物的）过量，过多

  • An overdose of sun, sea, sand and chlorine can give lighter hair a green tinge. [+ of]

    过量接触阳光、海水、沙粒和氯气会使浅色头发略呈绿色。

4 VERB 过多拥有；沉溺 You can say that someone overdoses on something if they have or do too much of it. 过多拥有；沉溺 [Also VERB]

  • The city, he concluded, had overdosed on design. [VERB + on]

    他的结论是这座城市过分重视规划设计了。

  • ...tourists that tend to overdose on the regional specialities of goose, duck and Armagnac. [VERB on noun]

    纵情享受鹅肉、鸭肉、阿马尼亚克酒等当地美食的游客
