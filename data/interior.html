interior
ɪntɪəriəʳ ɪnˈtɪriər
Word forms: plural interiors
1 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 内部 The interior of something is the inside part of it. 内部

  • The interior of the house was furnished with heavy, old-fashioned pieces.

    房子里摆放着笨重的旧式家具。

  • The boat's interior badly needed painting.

    该船内部亟需油漆。

SYN inside, centre, heart, middle  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (建筑物、车辆等)内部的 You use interior to describe something that is inside a building or vehicle. (建筑物、车辆等)内部的

  • The interior walls were painted green.

    内墙漆成了绿色。

  • There is more interior space than in some rival cars.

    车内空间比有些同类车宽敞。

  • He pulled the car over, turned on the interior light, examined the map.

    他把车开到路边,打开车内的照明灯查看地图。

SYN inside, internal, inner  
3 N-SING [oft with poss] 内地;腹地;内陆 The interior of a country or continent is the central area of it. 内地;腹地;内陆

  • ...a 5-day hike into the interior.

    为期 5 天、深入内地的远足

  • ...the unknown interior of South America.

    南美洲不为人知的腹地

  • Many areas in the country's interior are unable to report Aids cases immediately.

    该国内地的很多地区无法即时报告艾滋病病例。

SYN heartland, centre, hinterland, upcountry  
4 ADJ [ADJ n] 国内的;内政的;内务的 A country's interior minister, ministry, or department deals with affairs within that country, such as law and order. 国内的;内政的;内务的

  • The French Interior Minister has intervened in a scandal over the role of a secret police force.

    法国内政部长对一起涉及秘密警察部队所扮演角色的丑闻进行了干预。

SYN domestic, home, national, civil  
5 N-SING [ADJ n] 内政 A country's minister or ministry of the interior deals with affairs within that country, such as law and order. 内政

  • An official from the Ministry of the Interior said six people had died.

    内政部的一位官员说已有 6 人丧生。

SYN domestic, home, national, civil  
6 ADJ [ADJ n] 内心的;心灵的 Interior thoughts or processes go on inside someone's head and are not expressed aloud. 内心的;心灵的

  • She depicted the interior life of human beings, and particularly their moral stresses and strains.

    她描写人类的内心生活,尤其是他们所承受的道德压力。

  • ...the mind's interior space.

    心灵的内在空间

SYN mental, emotional, psychological, private
