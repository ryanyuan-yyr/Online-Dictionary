opinion
əpɪnjən əˈpɪnjən oʊˈpɪnjən
Word forms: plural opinions
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN, NOUN that] 意见；看法；主张；见解 Your opinion about something is what you think or believe about it. 意见；看法；主张；见解

  • I wasn't asking for your opinion, Dick.

    我并不是在征求你的意见，迪克。

  • He held the opinion that a government should think before introducing a tax.

    他认为政府在开征新税前应仔细斟酌。

  • Most who expressed an opinion spoke favorably of Thomas.

    大多数发表看法的人都支持托马斯。

SYN belief, feeling, view, idea  
2 N-SING （对某人的）评价，印象 Your opinion of someone is your judgment of their character or ability. （对某人的）评价，印象

  • That improved Mrs Goole's already favourable opinion of him. [+ of]

    那件事更加深了古尔太太对他的好印象。

SYN estimation, view, impression, assessment  
3 N-UNCOUNT 舆论；（群体的）信仰，观点 You can refer to the beliefs or views that people have as opinion . 舆论；（群体的）信仰，观点

  • Some, I suppose, might even be in positions to influence opinion.

    有的人，我认为，甚至有可能影响舆论。

  • There is a broad consensus of opinion about the policies which should be pursued. [+ about]

    对应当实行何种政策，舆论达成了广泛一致的意见。

4 N-COUNT [usually singular] 专家意见；鉴定 An opinion from an expert is the advice or judgment that they give you in the subject that they know a lot about. 专家意见；鉴定

  • Even if you have had a regular physical check-up recently, you should still seek a medical opinion.

    即使近期做过一次常规体检，也应当征求医生的意见。

5 See also: public opinion  second opinion 
6 PHRASE 在…看来；据…的意见 You add expressions such as 'in my opinion' or 'in their opinion' to a statement in order to indicate that it is what you or someone else thinks, and is not necessarily a fact. 在…看来；据…的意见

  • The book is, in Henry's opinion, the best book on the subject.

    依照亨利的看法，这本书是关于该主题的最佳著述。

  • Well he's not making a very good job of it in my opinion.

    然而，在我看来，这件事他做得不尽如人意。

7 PHRASE 持…观点；认为；相信 If someone is of the opinion that something is the case, that is what they believe. 持…观点；认为；相信 [formal]

  • Frank is of the opinion that the 1934 yacht should have won.

    弗兰克认为本该是那艘1934年的帆船获胜。

8 a matter of opinion→see: matter
