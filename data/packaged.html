package
pækɪdʒ ˈpækɪdʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense packages, present participle packaging, past tense , past participle packaged
1 N-COUNT 包裹 A package is a small parcel. 包裹

  • I tore open the package.

    我撕开了那个包裹。

  • ...a package addressed to Miss Claire Montgomery.

    寄给克莱尔·蒙哥马利小姐的包裹

SYN parcel, box, container, packet  
2 N-COUNT 包装盒；包装袋 A package is a small container in which a quantity of something is sold. Packages are either small boxes made of thin cardboard, or bags or envelopes made of paper or plastic. 包装盒；包装袋 [mainly US]

  • ...a package of doughnuts. [+ of]

    一包炸面圈

  • It is listed among the ingredients on the package.

    它是包装盒标明的成分之一。

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 packet
3 N-COUNT 一组建议；一揽子提议 A package is a set of proposals that are made by a government or organization and which must be accepted or rejected as a group. 一组建议；一揽子提议

  • The government has announced a package of measures to help the British film industry. [+ of]

    政府已经公布了帮助英国电影产业的一揽子措施。

  • They put together a Western economic aid package for Moscow.

    他们为莫斯科准备了一整套西方经济援助计划。

4 VERB [usually passive] （销售前）包装（产品） When a product is packaged, it is put into containers to be sold. （销售前）包装（产品）

  • The beans are then ground and packaged for sale as ground coffee. [be VERB-ed]

    然后那些咖啡豆被磨碎，包装好作为咖啡粉出售。

  • Packaged foods have to show a list of ingredients. [VERB-ed]

    包装食品必须列出食品成分。

SYN pack, box, wrap up, parcel (up)  
5 VERB [usually passive] （为提高吸引力或趣味性而）包装 If something is packaged in a particular way, it is presented or advertised in that way in order to make it seem attractive or interesting. （为提高吸引力或趣味性而）包装

  • A city has to be packaged properly to be attractive to tourists. [be VERB-ed]

    城市必须进行适当的包装以吸引游客。

  • ...entertainment packaged as information. [be VERB-ed + as]

    被包装成知识性节目的娱乐节目

SYN promote, advertise, publicize, present  
6 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] 包价（旅游） A package tour, or in British English a package holiday, is a holiday arranged by a travel company in which your travel and your accommodation are booked for you. 包价（旅游）

  • If you are on a package holiday, your travel company's rep should act on your behalf.

    如果你是参加包价旅游，你的旅行社代表将代为行事。
