patterning
pætəʳnɪŋ ˈpætənɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT （行为方式或习惯的）模式形成，模式化 Patterning is the forming of fixed ways of behaving or of doing things by constantly repeating something or copying other people. （行为方式或习惯的）模式形成，模式化 [formal]

  • ...social patterning.

    社会模式形成

  • ...the patterning of behaviour. [+ of]

    行为模式形成

2 N-UNCOUNT 图案；花纹；造型 You can refer to lines, spots, or other patterns as patterning . 图案；花纹；造型

  • ...geometric patterning.

    几何图案

  • ...a jazzy patterning of lights. [+ of]

    绚丽的灯光造型
