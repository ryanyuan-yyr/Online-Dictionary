overthrow
oʊvəʳθroʊ oʊvəʳθroʊ ˌoʊvərˈθroʊ ˈoʊvərˌθroʊ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overthrows, present participle overthrowing, past tense overthrew, past participle overthrown
The verb is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'θrəʊ/. The noun is pronounced /'əʊvəθrəʊ/. 动词读作 /ˌəʊvə'θrəʊ/。名词读作 /'əʊvəθrəʊ/。
VERB 推翻；打倒；将…赶下台 When a government or leader is overthrown, they are removed from power by force. 推翻；打倒；将…赶下台

  • That government was overthrown in a military coup three years ago. [be VERB-ed]

    那个政府在3年前的军事政变中被推翻。

  • ...an attempt to overthrow the president. [VERB noun]

    推翻总统的企图

SYN defeat, beat, master, overcome  
Overthrow is also a noun.

  • They were charged with plotting the overthrow of the state.

    他们被控阴谋颠覆国家政权。
