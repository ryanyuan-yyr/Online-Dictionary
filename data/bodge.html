bodge
bɒdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bodges, present participle bodging, past tense , past participle bodged
VERB 粗制滥造；拙劣地修补 If you bodge something, you make it or mend it in a way that is not as good as it should be. 粗制滥造；拙劣地修补 [British, informal]

  • I thought he had bodged the repair. [VERB noun]

    我觉得他越修越糟。
