old age
əʊld eɪdʒ
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 老年；晚年 Your old age is the period of years towards the end of your life. 老年；晚年

  • They worry about how they will support themselves in their old age.

    他们担心自己晚年的生活保障问题。

  • In old age the two men wrote each other wistful letters.

    这两个人晚年时互通信函，诉说怀旧感伤之情。

SYN declining years, age, senility, advancing years  
2 N-UNCOUNT 老龄；衰老；垂暮 Old age is the quality or state of being old and near the end of one's life. 老龄；衰老；垂暮

  • In Britain today we tend to consider old age as a social problem.

    在当今英国我们往往将老龄视为社会问题。

  • He is unable to travel much because of old age.

    他年事已高，不能频繁出游。
