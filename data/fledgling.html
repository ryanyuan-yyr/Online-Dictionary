fledgling
fledʒlɪŋ ˈflɛdʒlɪŋ
Word forms: plural fledglings
1 N-COUNT 刚长羽毛的小鸟;刚学飞的幼鸟 A fledgling is a young bird that has its feathers and is learning to fly. 刚长羽毛的小鸟;刚学飞的幼鸟
SYN chick, nestling, young bird  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 新手的;(组织或系统)新建的，没有经验的 You use fledgling to describe a person, organization, or system that is new or without experience. 新手的;(组织或系统)新建的，没有经验的

  • ...the sound practical advice he gave to fledgling writers.

    他给初出茅庐的作家提出的可靠实用的建议

  • ...Russia's fledgling democracy.

    俄罗斯新兴的民主

SYN new, beginning, developing, emerging
