sparkling
spɑːʳklɪŋ ˈspɑːklɪŋ
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (饮料)起泡的 Sparkling drinks are slightly fizzy. (饮料)起泡的

  • ...a glass of sparkling wine.

    一杯葡萄汽酒

  • ...a new lightly sparkling drink.

    一杯刚刚斟上的微微冒着泡的饮料

SYN fizzy, bubbly, effervescent, frothy  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (公司业绩)斐然的，辉煌的 If a company is described as having sparkling figures or sparkling results, it has performed very well and made a lot of money. (公司业绩)斐然的，辉煌的 [journalism]

  • Top retailer Marks & Spencer has romped in with another set of sparkling results.

    零售巨头玛莎百货再次凭借一系列耀眼的成绩轻松胜出。
