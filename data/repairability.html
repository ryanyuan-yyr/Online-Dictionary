repair
rɪpeəʳ rɪˈpɛə rɪˈpɛr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense repairs, present participle repairing, past tense , past participle repaired
1 VERB 修理;修补 If you repair something that has been damaged or is not working properly, you mend it. 修理;修补

  • Goldsmith has repaired the roof to ensure the house is wind-proof. [VERB noun]

    戈德史密斯修理了屋顶，以确保房屋能够抗风。

  • The cost of repairing earthquake damage could be more than seven-thousand-million dollars. [VERB noun]

    弥补地震造成的损失需要超过70亿美元。

  • A woman drove her car to the garage to have it repaired. [have noun VERB-ed]

    一位女士把车开到修理厂修理。

repairer

  • ...TV repairers.

    电视修理工

2 VERB 补救，挽救(关系或声誉) If you repair a relationship or someone's reputation after it has been damaged, you do something to improve it. 补救，挽救(关系或声誉)

  • The government continued to try to repair the damage caused by the minister's interview. [VERB noun]

    政府不断努力弥补因该部长接受采访带来的不良影响。

  • The first and most important thing was to repair my relationship with my father. [VERB noun]

    最首要和最重要的是挽救我和父亲的关系。

SYN put right, make up for, compensate for, rectify  
3 N-VAR 修理;修补;补救 A repair is something that you do to mend a machine, building, piece of clothing, or other thing that has been damaged or is not working properly. 修理;修补;补救

  • Many women know how to carry out repairs on their cars.

    许多妇女懂得怎样修自己的车。

  • Many of the buildings are in need of repair.

    许多建筑需要整修。

  • There is no doubt now that her marriage is beyond repair.

    毫无疑问她的婚姻无法挽救。

SYN mending, restoration, overhaul, adjustment  
4 VERB 到…去;前去 If someone repairs to a particular place, they go there. 到…去;前去 [formal]

  • We then repaired to the pavilion for lunch. [VERB + to]

    然后我们就去凉亭吃午饭了。

SYN go, retire, withdraw, head for  
5 PHRASE (建筑等)维修良好/糟糕，状况良好/ 糟糕 If something such as a building is in good repair, it is in good condition. If it is in bad repair, it is in bad condition. (建筑等)维修良好/糟糕，状况良好/ 糟糕

  • The monks of Ettal keep the abbey in good repair.

    埃塔尔的修道士把修道院维护得很好。

  • The road was in bad repair in parts.

    公路的部分路段路况很差。
