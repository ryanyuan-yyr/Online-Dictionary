blather
blæðəʳ ˈblæðər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blathers, present participle blathering, past tense , past participle blathered
VERB 喋喋不休；废话连篇；絮絮叨叨 If someone is blathering on about something, they are talking for a long time about something that you consider boring or unimportant. 喋喋不休；废话连篇；絮絮叨叨

  • The old men blather on and on. [V with on]

    这几个老头喋喋不休。

  • Stop blathering. [VERB]

    别唠唠叨叨了。

  • He kept on blathering about police incompetence. [VERB + about]

    他喋喋不休地念叨着警察不称职。

SYN chatter, ramble, mumble, babble  
Blather is also a noun.

  • Anyone knows that all this is blather.

    谁都知道这完全是废话连篇。
