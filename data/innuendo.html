innuendo
ɪnjuendoʊ ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊ
Word forms: plural innuendoes, plural innuendos
N-VAR 影射;暗讽 Innuendo is indirect reference to something rude or unpleasant. 影射;暗讽

  • The report was based on rumours, speculation, and innuendo.

    这份报告建立在谣言、臆断和含沙射影的基础之上。

  • ...magazines which are full of sexual innuendo.

    充满性暗示的杂志

SYN insinuation, suggestion, hint, implication
