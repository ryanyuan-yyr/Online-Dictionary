yearning
jɜːʳnɪŋ ˈjɜrnɪŋ
Word forms: plural yearnings
N-VAR [NOUN to-infinitive] 渴望;强烈愿望;向往 A yearning for something is a very strong desire for it. 渴望;强烈愿望;向往

  • He spoke of his yearning for another child. [+ for]

    他说起了自己想再要一个孩子的强烈愿望。

  • He always had a yearning to be a schoolteacher.

    他一直渴望当老师。

SYN longing, wish, desire, need
