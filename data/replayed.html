replay
riːpleɪ riːpleɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense replays, present participle replaying, past tense , past participle replayed
The verb is pronounced /ˌriː'pleɪ/. The noun is pronounced /'riːpleɪ/. 动词读作/ˌriː'pleɪ/。名词读作/'riːpleɪ/。
1 VERB [usually passive] 重新比赛 If a match between two sports teams is replayed, the two teams play it again, because neither team won the first time, or because the match was stopped because of bad weather. 重新比赛 [mainly British]

  • Drawn matches were replayed three or four days later. [be VERB-ed]

    没有分出胜负的比赛在三四天后重赛。

SYN play again  
You can refer to a match that is replayed as a replay .重新进行的比赛

  • If there has to be a replay we are confident of victory.

    如果重新比赛，我们有信心取得胜利。

2 VERB (影片或磁带的)重播，重放 If you replay something that you have recorded on film or tape, you play it again in order to watch it or listen to it. (影片或磁带的)重播，重放

  • He stopped the machine and replayed the message. [VERB noun]

    他停下机器，重新播放了一遍信息。

SYN play back, play again, listen to again  
Replay is also a noun.

  • I watched a slow-motion videotape replay of his fall.

    我看了一遍录像带上他摔倒时的慢动作重放。

3 VERB (反复在脑海里)重放;不断想起 If you replay an event in your mind, you think about it again and again. (反复在脑海里)重放;不断想起

  • She spends her nights lying in bed, replaying the fire in her mind. [VERB noun]

    她好多个晚上躺在床上，不断想起火灾场面。

SYN go over, relive
