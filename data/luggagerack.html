luggage rack
ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ræk
Word forms: plural luggage racks
1 N-COUNT （火车或公共汽车等上的）行李架 A luggage rack is a shelf for putting luggage on, on a vehicle such as a train or bus. （火车或公共汽车等上的）行李架
2 N-COUNT （车顶上的）行李架 A luggage rack is a metal frame that is fixed on top of a car and used for carrying large objects. （车顶上的）行李架 [US]
