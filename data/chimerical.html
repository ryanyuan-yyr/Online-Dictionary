chimera
kaɪmɪərə kaɪˈmɪrə kɪˈmɪrə
Word forms: plural chimeras
1 N-COUNT 幻想;妄想 A chimera is an unrealistic idea that you have about something or a hope that you have that is unlikely to be fulfilled. 幻想;妄想 [formal]

  • Religious unity remained as much a chimera as ever.

    宗教统一仍然和从前一样，不过是个妄想。

2 N-COUNT 喀迈拉(希腊神话中狮头、羊身、蛇尾的怪物) In Greek mythology, a chimera is a creature with the head of a lion, the body of a goat, and the tail of a snake. 喀迈拉(希腊神话中狮头、羊身、蛇尾的怪物)
