rectitude
rektɪtjuːd -tuːd ˈrɛktəˌtud ˈrɛktəˌtjud
N-UNCOUNT 正直;公正;诚实 Rectitude is a quality or attitude that is shown by people who behave honestly and morally according to accepted standards. 正直;公正;诚实 [formal]

  • ...people of the utmost moral rectitude.

    最为正直的人

SYN morality, principle, honour, virtue
