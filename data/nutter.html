nutter
nʌtəʳ ˈnʌtər
Word forms: plural nutters
N-COUNT 疯子;狂人;怪人 If you refer to someone as a nutter, you mean that they are mad or that their behaviour is very strange. 疯子;狂人;怪人 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • Is he a nutter, or is he a genius?.

    他是疯子，还是天才？

  • He was a bit of a nutter.

    他有点怪。
