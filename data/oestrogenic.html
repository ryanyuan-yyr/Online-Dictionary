oestrogen
iːstrədʒən ˈiːstrədʒən e- ˈistrəgən ˈɛstrəgən
(also estrogen)
N-UNCOUNT 雌激素 Oestrogen is a hormone produced in the ovaries of female animals. Oestrogen controls the reproductive cycle and prepares the body for pregnancy. 雌激素

  • As ovulation gets nearer, oestrogen levels rise.

    临近排卵期，雌激素的分泌量随之增加。
