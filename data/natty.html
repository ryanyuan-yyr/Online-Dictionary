natty
næti ˈnæti
Word forms: comparative nattier, superlative nattiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (尤指男士服装)整洁漂亮的，整洁时髦的 If you describe clothes, especially men's clothes, as natty, you mean that they are smart and neat. (尤指男士服装)整洁漂亮的，整洁时髦的 [informal, approval]

  • ...a natty pin stripe suit.

    笔挺时尚的细条纹西装

  • Cliff was a natty dresser.

    克利夫是讲究衣着整洁美观的人。

SYN smart, sharp [informal] , dashing [old-fashioned] , elegant  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 灵巧的;小巧方便的 If you describe something as natty, you think it is smart and cleverly designed. 灵巧的;小巧方便的 [informal, approval]

  • ...natty little houses.

    设计精巧的小屋
