scepticism
skeptɪsɪzəm
in AM, use 美国英语用 skepticism
N-UNCOUNT 怀疑态度;怀疑主义 Scepticism is great doubt about whether something is true or useful. 怀疑态度;怀疑主义

  • There was considerable scepticism about the Chancellor's forecast of a booming economy.

    有相当多的人对财政大臣作出的经济会迅猛发展的预测表示怀疑。

  • The report has inevitably been greeted with scepticism.

    那份报告不可避免地受到了质疑。

SYN doubt, suspicion, disbelief, cynicism
