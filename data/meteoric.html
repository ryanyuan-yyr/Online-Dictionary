meteoric
miːtiɒrɪk -ɔːr- ˌmitiˈɔrɪk
ADJ-GRADED (指某人的事业)迅速成功的，一举成名的 If you use meteoric when you are describing someone's career, you mean that they achieved success very quickly. (指某人的事业)迅速成功的，一举成名的

  • ...his meteoric rise to fame.

    他的一举成名

  • His early career had been meteoric.

    他的早期生涯平步青云。

SYN spectacular, sudden, overnight, rapid
