innocently
ɪnəsəntli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 故作天真地;佯装无知地 If you say that someone does or says something innocently, you mean that they are pretending not to know something about a situation. 故作天真地;佯装无知地

  • I caught Chrissie's eye, but she only smiled back at me innocently.

    我与克丽茜对视了一眼,但她只是一脸天真地对我微笑。

  • 'Any chance you'd be heading out that way again, soon?' I asked innocently.

    “你很快会再往那边去吗?”我故作不知地问道。
