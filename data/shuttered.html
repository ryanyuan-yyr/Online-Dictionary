shuttered
ʃʌtəʳd ˈʃʌtəd
1 ADJ 关上百叶窗的 A shuttered window, room, or building has its shutters closed. 关上百叶窗的

  • I opened a shuttered window.

    我打开了一扇百叶窗已合上的窗户。

  • Schools and government offices have been closed, and many shops remain shuttered.

    学校和政府办公楼都关闭了，许多商店的百叶窗也一直关着。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 装有百叶窗的 A shuttered window, room, or building has shutters fitted to it. 装有百叶窗的

  • The stone-built property is in good condition, with a new tiled roof, shuttered windows and electric heating.

    这栋石砌房屋的条件很不错，有新铺的瓦片屋顶，装有百叶窗的窗户还有电力供暖系统。

  • ...green-shuttered colonial villas.

    装有绿色百叶窗的殖民时期风格的别墅
