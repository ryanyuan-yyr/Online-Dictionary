preposterous
prɪpɒstərəs priˈpɑstərəs prɪˈpɑstərəs
ADJ-GRADED 极其荒谬的；愚蠢至极的 If you describe something as preposterous, you mean that it is extremely unreasonable and foolish. 极其荒谬的；愚蠢至极的 [disapproval]

  • The whole idea was preposterous.

    整个想法都荒唐透顶。

  • ...their preposterous claim that they had unearthed a plot.

    他们无比愚蠢地声称自己发现了一个阴谋

SYN ridiculous, bizarre, incredible, outrageous  
preposterously

  • Some prices are preposterously high.

    有些价格高得离谱。
