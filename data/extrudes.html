extrude
ɪkstruːd ɪkˈstruːd ɛkˈstrud ɪkˈstrud
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense extrudes, present participle extruding, past tense , past participle extruded
1 VERB [usually passive] 挤压出;挤压成;压制 If a substance is extruded, it is forced or squeezed out through a small opening. 挤压出;挤压成;压制 [technical]

  • These crystals are then embedded in a plastic, and the plastic is extruded as a wire. [be VERB-ed]

    然后将这些晶体嵌入一块塑料，再把塑料挤压成线。

  • I work in the extruded tube business. [VERB-ed]

    我做挤压管生意。
