peroration
perəreɪʃən ˌpɛrəˈreɪʃən
Word forms: plural perorations
1 N-COUNT （讲话的）结尾，结论，总结 A peroration is the last part of a speech, especially the part where the speaker sums up his or her argument. （讲话的）结尾，结论，总结 [formal]
2 N-COUNT 冗长乏味的演说 If someone describes a speech as a peroration, they mean that they dislike it because they think it is very long and not worth listening to. 冗长乏味的演说 [formal, disapproval]
