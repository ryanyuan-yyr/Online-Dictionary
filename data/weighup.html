weigh up
1 →see: weigh 3 
2 PHRASAL VERB （尤指心生怀疑时）评估，品评，对…作出评价 If you weigh someone up, you try and find out what they are like and form an opinion of them, especially when you are suspicious of them. （尤指心生怀疑时）评估，品评，对…作出评价 [mainly British] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • My recruiting sergeant weighed me up when I first walked into his office. [VERB noun PREP.]

    当我第一次走进招募官的办公室时，他考量了我一番。

SYN assess, judge, gauge, appraise
