chop off
PHRASAL VERB 砍掉;割掉 To chop off something such as a part of someone's body means to cut it off. 砍掉;割掉

  • She chopped off her golden, waist-length hair. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她剪掉了她那齐腰的金发。

  • They dragged him to the village square and chopped his head off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们将他拖到村里的广场上，把他的头砍了下来。
