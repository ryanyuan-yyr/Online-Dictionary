innocence
ɪnəsəns ˈɪnəsəns ˈɪnəsəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 天真;单纯 Innocence is the quality of having no experience or knowledge of the more complex or unpleasant aspects of life. 天真;单纯

  • ...the sweet innocence of youth. [+ of]

    青春时代的可爱与单纯

  • Youngsters are losing their childhood innocence too quickly.

    青少年正过早地失去他们童年的纯真。

SYN naiveté, simplicity, inexperience, freshness  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft poss NOUN] 清白;无罪 If someone proves their innocence, they prove that they are not guilty of a crime. 清白;无罪

  • He claims he has evidence which could prove his innocence.

    他声称有证据证明自己是无辜的。

SYN blamelessness, righteousness, clean hands, uprightness
