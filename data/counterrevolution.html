counter-revolution
ˌkaʊntəˌrɛvəˈluːʃən
Word forms: plural counter-revolutions
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 counter-revolution
1 N-COUNT 反革命 A counter-revolution is a revolution that is intended to reverse the effects of a previous revolution. 反革命

  • The consequences of the counter-revolution have been extremely bloody.

    反革命的结果是十分血腥的。

2 N-UNCOUNT 反革命活动 You can refer to activities that are intended to reverse the effects of a previous revolution as counter-revolution . 反革命活动

  • Such actions would be regarded as counter-revolution.

    这种举动将会被视为反革命活动。
