butt in
PHRASAL VERB 插嘴；干涉；插手 If you say that someone is butting in, you are criticizing the fact that they are joining in a conversation or activity without being asked to. 插嘴；干涉；插手 [disapproval] [Also + on]

  • Sorry, I don't mean to butt in. [VERB PREP.]

    对不起，我并不是想插嘴。

  • 'I should think not,' Sarah butted in. [VERB PREP. with quote]

    “我不这么认为，”萨拉插嘴说。
