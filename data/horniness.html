horny
hɔːʳni ˈhɔrni
Word forms: comparative hornier, superlative horniest
1 ADJ-GRADED 欲火中烧的;性欲旺盛的 If you describe someone as horny, you mean that they are sexually aroused or that they easily become sexually aroused. 欲火中烧的;性欲旺盛的 [informal]

  • But don't you get frustrated sometimes? I mean, don't you get a bit, you know, horny?

    但你难道不会有时觉得不满足吗？我的意思是，你难道不会感到，嗯，有点欲火中烧吗？

  • ...horny adolescent boys.

    满脑子都是性的青春期男孩

SYN aroused, excited, turned on [slang] , randy [informal , mainly British]  
2 ADJ 角质的;角质物制的 Something that is horny is hard, strong, and made of horn or of a hard substance like horn. 角质的;角质物制的

  • His fingernails had grown long, and horny.

    他的手指甲长得又长又硬。

  • This cuttlefish has a horny internal shell like a pen.

    这只乌贼有一个笔状的角质内壳。
