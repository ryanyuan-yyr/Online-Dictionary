fall over
PHRASAL VERB 摔倒;跌倒;倒下 If a person or object that is standing falls over, they accidentally move from their upright position so that they are then lying on the ground or on the surface supporting them. 摔倒;跌倒;倒下

  • If he drinks more than two glasses of wine he falls over. [VERB PREP.]

    如果他喝酒超过两杯，就会醉倒。
