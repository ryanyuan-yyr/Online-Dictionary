causative
kɔːzətɪv ˈkɔzəˌtɪv
ADJ [ADJ n] 起因的;诱因的 Causative factors are ones which are responsible for causing something. 起因的;诱因的 [formal]

  • Both nicotine and carbon monoxide inhaled with cigarette smoking have been incriminated as causative factors.

    抽烟时吸入的尼古丁和一氧化碳都已经被标定为致病因素。

  • ...the prime causative agent of AIDS.

    艾滋病的主要诱因
