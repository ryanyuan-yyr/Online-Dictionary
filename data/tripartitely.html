tripartite
traɪpɑːʳtaɪt traɪˈpɑːtaɪt traɪˈpɑrtaɪt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 分为三部分的;涉及三方的 You can use tripartite to describe something that has three parts or that involves three groups of people. 分为三部分的;涉及三方的 [formal]

  • ...tripartite meetings between Government ministers, trades union leaders and industrialists.

    政府部长、工会领袖和工厂主之间的三方会谈
