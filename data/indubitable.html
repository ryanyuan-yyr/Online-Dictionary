indubitable
ɪndjuːbɪtəbəl -duːb- ɪnˈdubɪtəbəl ɪnˈdjubɪtəbəl
ADJ 明确无疑的;毋庸置疑的 You use indubitable to describe something when you want to emphasize that it is definite and cannot be doubted. 明确无疑的;毋庸置疑的 [formal, emphasis]

  • His brilliance renders this film an indubitable classic.

    他的出色表现让这部电影成为了毋庸置疑的经典。

SYN certain, sure, undoubted, obvious  
indubitably

  • His behaviour was indubitably ill-judged.

    他的行为明显是欠思量。
