athletic
æθletɪk æθˈlɛtɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 运动的;体育的;运动员的 Athletic means relating to athletes and athletics. 运动的;体育的;运动员的

  • They have been given college scholarships purely on athletic ability.

    他们完全是凭体育特长才获得了大学奖学金。

  • Most athletic activities are about individual effort.

    大多数体育项目是靠个人的拼搏。

SYN sporting, games, sports, competitive  
athletically

  • ...she's academically able and athletically outstanding.

    她学业优秀，体育方面也很突出。

2 ADJ-GRADED 运动型的；身体健壮的 An athletic person is fit, and able to perform energetic movements easily. 运动型的；身体健壮的

  • Xandra is an athletic 36-year-old with a 21-year-old's body.

    36岁的汉德拉身体健壮，有着21岁小伙子的体能。

  • He was tall, with an athletic build.

    他高大健壮。

SYN fit, strong, powerful, healthy  
athletically

  • He jumped athletically out of the car.

    他身手矫捷地跳出了车子。
