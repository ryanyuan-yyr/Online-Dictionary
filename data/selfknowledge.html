self-knowledge ˈsɛlfˈnɑlɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 自知;自我认识 Self-knowledge is knowledge that you have about your own character and nature. 自知;自我认识

  • The more self-knowledge we have, the more control we can exert over our feelings and behaviour.

    我们越有自知之明就越能控制自己的感情和行为。
