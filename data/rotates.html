rotate
roʊteɪt roʊteɪt ˈroʊˌteɪt roʊˈteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rotates, present participle rotating, past tense , past participle rotated
1 V-ERG (使)旋转;(使)转动 When something rotates or when you rotate it, it turns with a circular movement. (使)旋转;(使)转动

  • The Earth rotates round the sun. [VERB]

    地球围绕太阳转。

  • Take each foot in both your hands and rotate it to loosen and relax the ankle. [VERB noun]

    双手抓住自己的双脚，转动一下，以放松脚踝。

SYN revolve, turn, wheel, spin  
2 V-ERG （使）轮流;（使）轮换 If people or things rotate, or if someone rotates them, they take it in turns to do a particular job or serve a particular purpose. （使）轮流;（使）轮换

  • The members of the club can rotate and one person can do all the preparation for the evening. [VERB]

    俱乐部的成员可以轮流工作，而且一个人就能做好晚上的所有准备工作。

  • They will swap posts in a year's time, according to new party rules which rotate the leadership. [VERB noun]

    根据轮流担任领导的新政党章程，他们在一年后会轮换职位。

SYN follow in sequence, switch, alternate, interchange  
rotating

  • The European Union's rotating presidency passed from Sweden to Belgium.

    欧盟轮值主席国由瑞典换成了比利时。
