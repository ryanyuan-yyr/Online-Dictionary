paranoia
pærənɔɪə ˌpærəˈnɔɪə
1 N-UNCOUNT 疑惧；多疑 If you say that someone suffers from paranoia, you think that they are too suspicious and afraid of other people. 疑惧；多疑

  • The mood is one of paranoia and expectation of war.

    那是一种对战争既畏惧又期待的心理。

  • ...the mounting paranoia with which he viewed the world around him.

    他看待周围世界时越来越重的疑惧心理

SYN obsession, suspicion, delusion, persecution complex  
2 N-UNCOUNT 偏执狂；妄想症 In psychology, if someone suffers from paranoia, they wrongly believe that other people are trying to harm them, or believe themselves to be much more important than they really are. 偏执狂；妄想症
