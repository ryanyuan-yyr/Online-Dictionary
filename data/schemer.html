schemer

Word forms: plural schemers
N-COUNT 搞阴谋的人;施诡计的人 If you refer to someone as a schemer, you mean that they make secret plans in order to get some benefit for themselves. 搞阴谋的人;施诡计的人 [disapproval]

    只想着自己晋升的阴险狡诈的办公室职员

SYN plotter, intriguer, conniver, Machiavelli
