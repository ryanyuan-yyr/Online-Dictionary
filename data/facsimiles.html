facsimile
fæksɪmɪli fækˈsɪməli
Word forms: plural facsimiles
1 N-COUNT [NOUN n] 复制品;仿制品;摹本 A facsimile of something is an copy or imitation of it. 复制品;仿制品;摹本 [formal]

  • ...a facsimile of his writing desk.

    他的写字台的仿制品

  • ...a facsimile edition of Beethoven's musical manuscripts.

    贝多芬音乐手稿的摹本

2 N-COUNT 同 fax A facsimile is the same as a fax . 同 fax [formal]
