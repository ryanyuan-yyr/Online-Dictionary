1 N-COUNT 半决赛 A semi-final is one of the two matches or races in a competition that are held to decide who will compete in the final. 半决赛

  • Steve Lewis won the first semi-final.

    史蒂夫·刘易斯赢了第一场半决赛。

The semi-finals is the round of a competition in which these two matches or races are held.半决赛阶段

  • He was beaten in the semi-finals by Chris Dittmar.

    他在半决赛中输给了克里斯·迪特马尔。
