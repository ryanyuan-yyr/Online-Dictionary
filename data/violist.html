viola
vioʊlə ˈvaɪələ ˈvaɪələ viˈoʊlə vaɪˈoʊlə
Word forms: plural violas
1 N-VAR 中提琴 A viola is a musical instrument with four strings that is played with a bow. It is like a violin, but is slightly larger and can play lower notes. 中提琴
2 N-COUNT 堇菜属植物;三色堇 Violas are small plants with white, yellow, or purple flowers. 堇菜属植物;三色堇
