muck up
PHRASAL VERB 把…弄糟;搞乱 If you muck up or muck something up, you do something very badly so that you fail to achieve what you wanted to. 把…弄糟;搞乱 [mainly British, informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I mucked up at the 13th hole and told myself that this was getting stupid. [VERB PREP.]

    在第13个洞时，我搞砸了，心说这下没戏了。

  • Scientists should figure out how to keep the natural world from mucking up the affairs of people. [VERB PREP. noun]

    科学家应该想办法防止大自然扰乱人类的活动。

SYN ruin, bungle, botch, make a mess of
