adjoin
ədʒɔɪn əˈdʒɔɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense adjoins, present participle adjoining, past tense , past participle adjoined
VERB 紧挨；邻接；毗连 If one room, place, or object adjoins another, they are next to each other. 紧挨；邻接；毗连 [formal]

  • Fields adjoined the garden and there were no neighbours. [VERB noun]

    田园相连，四下无邻。

  • We waited in an adjoining office. [VERB-ing]

    我们在旁边的办公室里等候。

SYN connect with or to, join, neighbour (on), link with
