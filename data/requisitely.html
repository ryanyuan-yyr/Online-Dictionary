requisite
rekwɪzɪt ˈrɛkwəzɪt
Word forms: plural requisites
1 ADJ 必需的;必备的;必不可少的 You can use requisite to indicate that something is necessary for a particular purpose. 必需的;必备的;必不可少的 [formal]

  • She filled in the requisite paperwork.

    她完成了必要的书面工作。

SYN necessary, needed, required, called for  
2 N-COUNT 必需品;必备品;必要条件 A requisite is something which is necessary for a particular purpose. 必需品;必备品;必要条件 [formal]

  • An understanding of accounting techniques is a major requisite for the work of the analysts. [+ for]

    懂得会计知识是从事分析员工作的一个必要条件。

  • ...his little bag of hunting requisites.

    他的一小包狩猎必需品

SYN necessity, condition, requirement, precondition
