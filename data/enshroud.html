enshroud
ɪnʃraʊd ɛnˈʃraʊd ɪnˈʃraʊd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enshrouds, present participle enshrouding, past tense , past participle enshrouded
VERB 遮蔽;掩盖;笼罩 To enshroud something means to cover it completely so that it can no longer be seen. 遮蔽;掩盖;笼罩 [literary]

  • ...dispiriting clouds that enshrouded us in twilight. [VERB noun + in]

    黄昏时令人心生沮丧的密布云团

  • ...the culture of secrecy which enshrouds our politics. [VERB noun]

    笼罩我们政治的保密文化

SYN cover, hide, cloud, wrap
