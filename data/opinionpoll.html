opinion poll
Word forms: plural opinion polls
N-COUNT （尤指政治议题的）民意测验，民意调查 An opinion poll involves asking people's opinions on a particular subject, especially one concerning politics. （尤指政治议题的）民意测验，民意调查

  • Nearly three-quarters of people questioned in an opinion poll agreed with the government's decision.

    将近3/4的民意调查受访者赞同政府的决定。

SYN poll, survey, ballot, census
