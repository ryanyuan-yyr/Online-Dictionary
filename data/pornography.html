pornography
pɔːʳnɒgrəfi pɔrˈnɑgrəfi
N-UNCOUNT （总称）色情（或淫秽）作品 Pornography refers to books, magazines, and films that are designed to cause sexual excitement by showing naked people or referring to sexual acts. （总称）色情（或淫秽）作品 [disapproval]

  • A nationwide campaign against pornography began in the summer.

    一场全国性打击淫秽作品的行动在夏季打响了。

SYN obscenity, porn [informal] , erotica, dirt
