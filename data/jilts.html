jilt
dʒɪlt dʒɪlt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense jilts, present participle jilting, past tense , past participle jilted
VERB 抛弃，遗弃，甩掉（情人） If someone is jilted, the person they are having a romantic relationship with suddenly ends the relationship in a surprising and upsetting way. 抛弃，遗弃，甩掉（情人） [informal] [Also VERB n]

  • She was jilted by her first fiancé. [be VERB-ed]

    她被她第一个未婚夫甩了。

  • Driven to distraction, he murdered the woman who jilted him. [VERB noun]

    他被逼得发狂，于是杀死了抛弃他的那个女人。

  • Police want to interview her jilted lover. [VERB-ed]

    警方想找那个被她抛弃的情人来问话。

SYN reject, drop, disappoint, abandon
