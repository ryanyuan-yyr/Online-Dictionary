comprehension
kɒmprɪhenʃən ˌkɑmprəˈhɛnʃɛn
Word forms: plural comprehensions
1 N-UNCOUNT 理解力；领悟力 Comprehension is the ability to understand something. 理解力；领悟力 [formal]

  • This was utterly beyond her comprehension.

    这让她完全无法理解。

SYN understanding, grasp, conception, realization  
2 N-UNCOUNT 充分了解;完全明白 Comprehension is full knowledge and understanding of the meaning of something. 充分了解;完全明白 [formal]

  • They turned to one another with the same expression of dawning comprehension, surprise, and relief.

    他们相互看了看，脸上都是同一种表情，恍然大悟、惊讶，而又如释重负。

3 N-VAR (听力或阅读)理解测试 When pupils do comprehension, they do an exercise to find out how well they understand a piece of spoken or written language. (听力或阅读)理解测试

  • The course also features creative writing exercises and listening comprehension.

    这门课程也包括写作练习和听力理解。
