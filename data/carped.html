carp
kɑːʳp kɑrp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense carps, present participle carping, past tense , past participle carped
Carp can also be used as the plural form for meaning 1. 义项1中 carp 亦可用作复数名词。
1 N-VAR 鲤鱼 A carp is a kind of fish that lives in lakes and rivers. 鲤鱼
2 VERB 挑剔；吹毛求疵；发牢骚 If you say that someone is carping, you mean that they keep criticizing or complaining about someone or something, especially in a way you think is unnecessary or annoying. 挑剔；吹毛求疵；发牢骚 [disapproval] [Also V about]

  • He cannot understand why she's constantly carping at him. [VERB + at]

    他不明白为什么她总是找他的茬儿。

  • This was the man whom other trainers love to carp about. [V at/about n]

    这就是老被其他教练挑毛病的那个人。

SYN find fault, complain, beef [slang] , criticize  
carping

  • She was in no mood to put up with Blanche's carping.

    她心情不好，受不了布兰奇的吹毛求疵。
