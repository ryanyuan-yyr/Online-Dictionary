garner
gɑːʳnəʳ ˈɡɑːnə ˈgɑrnər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense garners, present participle garnering, past tense , past participle garnered
VERB 获得；得到；收集 If someone has garnered something useful or valuable, they have gained it or collected it. 获得；得到；收集 [formal]

  • Durham had garnered three times as many votes as Carey. [VERB noun]

    德拉姆获得的选票是凯里的3倍。

  • He has garnered extensive support for his proposals. [VERB noun]

    他的提议得到了广泛的支持。

  • His priceless collection of Chinese art and artefacts was garnered over three decades. [VERB noun]

    他那批价值连城的中国艺术品和手工艺品是花了30多年才收集起来的。

SYN collect, assemble, gather, accumulate
