supplementary
sʌplɪmentri -teri ˌsʌpləˈmɛntəri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 增补性的;补充性的;额外的 Supplementary things are added to something in order to improve it. 增补性的;补充性的;额外的

  • ...the question of whether or not we need to take supplementary vitamins.

    是否需要补充维生素的问题

  • Provide them with additional background or with supplementary information.

    给他们提供额外的背景资料或补充信息。

SYN additional, extra, complementary, accompanying
