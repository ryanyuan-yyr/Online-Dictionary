rung
rʌŋ rʌŋ
Word forms: plural rungs
1 Rung is the past participle of ring .
2 N-COUNT 梯级;横档 The rungs on a ladder are the wooden or metal bars that form the steps. 梯级;横档

  • I swung myself onto the ladder and felt for the next rung.

    我纵身跳到梯子上，摸索着下一根横档。

3 N-COUNT (事业、组织、进程中的)级别，等级，地位 If you reach a particular rung in your career, in an organization, or in a process, you reach that level in it. (事业、组织、进程中的)级别，等级，地位

  • I first worked with him in 1971 when we were both on the lowest rung of our careers. [+ of]

    我第一次和他共事是在1971年，那时我们的事业都刚刚起步。

  • There has never been a better time to get on the first rung of the property ladder.

    迈出置业第一步的最佳时机莫过于此。
