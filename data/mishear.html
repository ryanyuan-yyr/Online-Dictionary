mishear
mɪshɪəʳ mɪsˈhɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mishears, present participle mishearing, past tense , past participle misheard
VERB 误听;听错 If you mishear what someone says, you hear it incorrectly, and think they said something different. 误听;听错

  • You misheard me, Frank. [VERB noun]

    弗兰克，你听错了。

  • She must have misheard. [VERB]

    她一定是听错了。
