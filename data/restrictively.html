restrictive
rɪstrɪktɪv rɪˈstrɪktɪv rɪˈstrɪktɪv
ADJ-GRADED 限制性的;约束性的 Something that is restrictive prevents people from doing what they want to do, or from moving freely. 限制性的;约束性的

  • Britain is to adopt a more restrictive policy on arms sales.

    英国将对武器出售采取更具限制性的政策。

  • ...increasingly restrictive immigration laws.

    限制条件越来越多的移民法

  • Do not wear restrictive clothing.

    不要穿紧身衣。
