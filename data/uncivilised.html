uncivilized
ʌnsɪvɪlaɪzd ʌnˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzd ʌnˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 uncivilised
ADJ-GRADED 粗野的；无礼的；无教养的；不文明的 If you describe someone's behaviour as uncivilized, you find it unacceptable, for example because it is very cruel or very rude. 粗野的；无礼的；无教养的；不文明的 [disapproval]

  • The campaign has abounded in mutual accusations of uncivilised behaviour.

    整个活动中双方频频指责对方粗野。

  • I think any sport involving animals where the animals do not have a choice is barbaric and uncivilized.

    我认为任何有动物参与其中并且它们别无选择的运动都是野蛮的、不文明的。
