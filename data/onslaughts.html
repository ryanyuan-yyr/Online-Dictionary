onslaught
ɒnslɔːt ˈɑnˌslɔt
Word forms: plural onslaughts
1 N-COUNT 攻击；袭击;猛攻 An onslaught on someone or something is a very violent, forceful attack against them. 攻击；袭击;猛攻 [Also + by]

  • The attackers launched another vicious onslaught on their victim.

    袭击者再度凶残地攻击受害人。

  • The rebels responded to a military onslaught against them by launching a major assault on an army camp. [+ against]

    叛乱分子为报复政府的军事打击，向一处军营发动猛攻。

  • ...a media onslaught.

    媒体的狂轰滥炸

2 N-COUNT （常指难以应付的）大批，大量 If you refer to an onslaught of something, you mean that there is a large amount of it, often so that it is very difficult to deal with. （常指难以应付的）大批，大量

  • The onslaught of orders should keep aircraft manufacturers busy for some time.

    纷至沓来的订单应该会让飞机制造商忙碌一段时间。

  • ...the constant onslaught of ads on American TV. [+ of]

    美国电视上让人应接不暇的广告
