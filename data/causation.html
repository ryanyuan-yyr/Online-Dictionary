causation
kɔːzeɪʃən kɔˈzeɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 诱因;起因;祸端 The causation of something, usually something bad, is the factors that have caused it. 诱因;起因;祸端 [formal]

  • The gene is only part of the causation of illness. [+ of]

    基因只是致病的部分原因。

2 N-UNCOUNT 因果关系；因果律 Causation is a study of the factors involved in causing something. 因果关系；因果律 [formal]

  • ...assumptions concerning social causation.

    有关社会因果律的假设
