haystack
heɪstæk ˈheɪˌrɪk ˈheɪˌstæk
Word forms: plural haystacks
1 N-COUNT （露天）干草堆，干草垛 A haystack is a large, solid pile of hay, often covered with a straw roof to protect it, which is left in the field until it is needed. （露天）干草堆，干草垛
2 PHRASE 落进干草垛里的一根针（比喻如大海捞针般难找的东西） If you are trying to find something and say that it is like looking for a needle in a haystack, you mean that you are very unlikely indeed to find it. 落进干草垛里的一根针（比喻如大海捞针般难找的东西）
