inset
ɪnset ɪnˈsɛt ˈɪnˌsɛt
Word forms: plural insets
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 镶嵌着…的 Something that is inset with a decoration or piece of material has the decoration or material set inside it. 镶嵌着…的

  • ...a gold pendant, inset with a diamond. [+ with]

    镶嵌着一粒钻石的金制项链坠儿

  • ...the glass bricks inset high along the corridor's opposite wall.

    高高地嵌在走廊另一面墙上的玻璃砖

2 N-COUNT 嵌图;套印小图;画中画 An inset is a small picture, diagram, or map that is inside a larger one. 嵌图;套印小图;画中画

  • I frequently paint between 10 and 20 insets for my murals.

    我经常会为我的壁画画上 10 到 20 个嵌套图画。
