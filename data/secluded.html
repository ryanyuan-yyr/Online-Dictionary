secluded
sɪkluːdɪd sɪˈkludɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 僻静的;隐蔽的 A secluded place is quiet and private. 僻静的;隐蔽的

  • We were tucked away in a secluded corner of the room.

    我们被藏匿到房间里一个隐蔽的角落。

  • We found a secluded beach a few miles further on.

    我们又前行了几英里，发现了一处僻静的海滩。

SYN private, sheltered, isolated, remote
