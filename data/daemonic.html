demonic
dɪmɒnɪk dɪˈmɒnɪkəl
(also daemonic)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 魔鬼（似）的;恶魔（般）的;邪恶的 Demonic means coming from or belonging to a demon or being like a demon. 魔鬼（似）的;恶魔（般）的;邪恶的

  • ...demonic forces.

    邪恶力量

  • ...a demonic grin.

    狞笑

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (精力、决心、能力等)超乎常人的 If someone has demonic energy, drive, or abilities, they are more energetic, determined, or clever than most people. (精力、决心、能力等)超乎常人的

  • ...a demonic drive to succeed.

    想要成功的决心超乎常人

SYN frenzied, mad, furious, frantic
