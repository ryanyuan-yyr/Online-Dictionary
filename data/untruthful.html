untruthful
ʌntruːθfʊl ʌnˈtruːθfʊl ʌnˈtruθfəl
ADJ-GRADED 不真实的；虚伪的；虚假的 If someone is untruthful or if they say untruthful things, they are dishonest and say things that they know are not true. 不真实的；虚伪的；虚假的

  • He must not be untruthful, or a coward.

    他绝不能是虚伪或胆怯之徒。

  • Some people may be tempted to give untruthful answers.

    有些人也许会不由得想要作出不实回答。

SYN dishonest, lying, false, deceptive
