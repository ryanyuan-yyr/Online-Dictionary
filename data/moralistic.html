moralistic
mɒrəlɪstɪk mɔːr- ˌmɔrəlˈɪstɪk ˌ mɑrəlˈɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 道学的;说教的 If you describe someone or something as moralistic, you are critical of them for making harsh judgments of other people on the basis of their own ideas about what is right and wrong. 道学的;说教的 [disapproval]

  • He has become more moralistic.

    他变得更喜欢说教了。

  • To me moralistic films are just unbearable.

    对我来说，说教电影简直难以忍受。
