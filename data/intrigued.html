intrigued ɪntriːgd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 被吸引住的;被迷住的 If you are intrigued by something, especially something strange, it interests you and you want to know more about it. 被吸引住的;被迷住的

  • They are intrigued by her story.

    他们被她的故事迷住了。

  • I would be intrigued to hear others' views.

    我很想听听别人的意见。

SYN interested, keen, fascinated, curious
