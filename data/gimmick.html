gimmick
gɪmɪk ˈgɪmɪk
Word forms: plural gimmicks
N-COUNT （引人注意或关注的）噱头，花招 A gimmick is an unusual and unnecessary feature or action whose purpose is to attract attention or publicity. （引人注意或关注的）噱头，花招 [disapproval]

  • It is just a public relations gimmick.

    这只不过是一种公关伎俩。

  • The exhibition is informative, up to date, and mercifully free of gimmicks.

    这个展览既能让人增长见识，又紧跟时代前沿，而且幸运的是没有华而不实的东西。

SYN stunt, trick, device, scheme
