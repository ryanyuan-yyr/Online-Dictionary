adversarial
ædvəʳseəriəl ˌædvɜːˈsɛərɪəl ˌædvərˈsɛriəl
ADJ-GRADED 对立的；敌对的 If you describe something as adversarial, you mean that it involves two or more people or organizations who are opposing each other. 对立的；敌对的 [formal]

  • In our country there is an adversarial relationship between government and business.

    在我国，官商之间存在一种对立关系。

  • ...an adversarial legal system.

    两派对立的法律制度
