mirror site ˈmɪrə saɪt
Word forms: plural mirror sites
N-COUNT 镜像网站（与另一网站内容相同，但网址略有不同，方便更多人登录受欢迎的网站） A mirror site is a website which is the same as another website operated by the same person or organization but has a slightly different address. Mirror sites are designed to make it easier for more people to visit a popular website. 镜像网站（与另一网站内容相同，但网址略有不同，方便更多人登录受欢迎的网站）
