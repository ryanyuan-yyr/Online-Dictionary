circumspect
sɜːʳkəmspekt ˈsɜrkəmˌspɛkt
ADJ-GRADED 小心的;谨慎的;谨言慎行的 If you are circumspect, you are cautious in what you do and say and do not take risks. 小心的;谨慎的;谨言慎行的 [formal]

  • The banks should have been more circumspect in their dealings. [+ in]

    那些银行在交易中本该更加谨慎的。

  • You seem to be implying, in your usual circumspect manner, that perhaps it might not be a wonderful idea.

    你好像正以你一贯谨言慎行的方式暗示，那个主意也许不怎么样。

SYN cautious, politic, guarded, careful  
circumspectly

  • I would suggest that for the time being you behave as circumspectly as possible in political matters.

    我建议，目前在政治事务上你还是尽量小心为妙。
