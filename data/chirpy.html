chirpy
tʃɜːʳpi ˈtʃɜrpi
Word forms: comparative chirpier, superlative chirpiest
ADJ-GRADED 欢快的;活泼的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as chirpy, you mean they are very cheerful and lively. 欢快的;活泼的 [informal]

  • Hutson is a small, chirpy bloke.

    赫特森是个快活的小个子男人。

  • She sounded quite chirpy, all she needs is rest.

    她之前听起来兴高采烈的，她需要的只是休息。

SYN cheerful, happy, bright, enthusiastic
