decode
diːkoʊd diːˈkəʊd diˈkoʊd ˈdiˈkoʊd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense decodes, present participle decoding, past tense , past participle decoded
1 VERB 破译，译解(密码信息) If you decode a message that has been written or spoken in a code, you change it into ordinary language. 破译，译解(密码信息)

  • All he had to do was decode it and pass it over. [VERB noun]

    他需要做的就是将它破译然后转给他人。

  • The secret documents were intercepted and decoded. [VERB noun]

    机密文件遭截获并被破译。

SYN decipher, crack, work out, solve  
2 VERB 解读，揣摩(戏剧、艺术作品、行为等) If you decode something such as a play or a work of art, or someone's behaviour, you manage to understand its meaning or what it implies, although this is not obvious. 解读，揣摩(戏剧、艺术作品、行为等)

  • You don't need a Ph.D to decode their work, but they do try to challenge audiences with unconventional material. [VERB noun]

    解读他们的作品并不需要有博士学位，但他们的确在尝试用一些非传统素材来挑战观众的接受度。

SYN understand, explain, interpret, make sense of  
3 VERB 破解转换(电视广播信号) A device that decodes a broadcast signal changes it into a form that can be displayed on a television screen. 破解转换(电视广播信号)

  • About 60,000 subscribers have special adapters to receive and decode the signals. [VERB noun]

    大约6万用户拥有特殊的适配器来接收和转换信号。
