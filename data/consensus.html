consensus
kənsensəs kənˈsɛnsəs
1 N-SING 一致看法;共识 A consensus is general agreement among a group of people. 一致看法;共识

  • The consensus amongst the world's scientists is that the world is likely to warm up over the next few decades. [+ amongst]

    世界各国科学家一致认为在未来几十年里地球有可能变暖。

  • The question of when the troops should leave would be decided by consensus.

    部队应该何时撤离的问题将由大家共同决定。

SYN agreement, general agreement, unanimity, common consent
