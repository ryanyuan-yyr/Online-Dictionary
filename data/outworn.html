outworn
aʊtwɔːʳn ˈaʊtwɔːn ˌaʊtˈwɔːn
ADJ （信仰、习俗）过时的，陈腐的，废弃的，无用的 If you describe a belief or custom as outworn, you mean that it is old-fashioned and no longer has any meaning or usefulness. （信仰、习俗）过时的，陈腐的，废弃的，无用的

  • ...an ancient nation irretrievably sunk in an outworn culture.

    深陷陈腐文化中不可自拔的古老国家

SYN outdated, passé, old-fashioned, tired
