bomber
bɒməʳ ˈbɑmər
Word forms: plural bombers
1 N-COUNT 轰炸机 A bomber is a military aircraft which drops bombs. 轰炸机

  • ...a high speed bomber with twin engines.

    双引擎高速轰炸机

2 N-COUNT （在公共场所）引爆炸弹的人 Bombers are people who cause bombs to explode in public places. （在公共场所）引爆炸弹的人

  • Detectives hunting the London bombers will be keen to interview him.

    追查伦敦爆炸案凶犯的侦探们急于对他进行讯问。
