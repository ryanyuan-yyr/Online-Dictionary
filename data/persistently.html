persistently
pəʳsɪstəntli
1 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb, ADVERB adjective] 一次又一次地；一直 If something happens persistently, it happens again and again or for a long time. 一次又一次地；一直

  • The allegations have been persistently denied by ministers.

    这些说法被部长们一再否认。

  • People with rail season tickets will get refunds if trains are persistently late.

    如果火车一再晚点，持铁路季票的人会得到退款。

2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB with verb] 坚持不懈地；锲而不舍地；不屈不挠地 If someone does something persistently, they do it with determination even though it is difficult or other people are against it. 坚持不懈地；锲而不舍地；不屈不挠地

  • Rachel gently but persistently imposed her will upon Douglas.

    雷切尔委婉但执著地把自己的意愿强加给道格拉斯。

  • Slowly, persistently, patiently, we will end this conflict.

    慢慢来，耐心坚持，我们会解决这场冲突的。

SYN determinedly, steadily, resolutely, doggedly
