abbreviate
əbriːvieɪt əˈbriviˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abbreviates, present participle abbreviating, past tense , past participle abbreviated
1 VERB 缩写;缩简;缩短 If you abbreviate something, especially a word or a piece of writing, you make it shorter. 缩写;缩简;缩短 [Also VERB n]

  • He abbreviated his first name to Alec. [VERB noun + to]

    他将自己的名字缩写为亚历克。

SYN shorten, reduce, contract, trim  
abbreviated

  • It was an abbreviated document without detailed proposals.

    这是一份省去了建议细节的文件简本。
