repute
rɪpjuːt rɪˈpjut
1 PHRASE 名声好的;有良好名誉的;享有盛名的 A person or thing of repute or of high repute is respected and known to be good. 名声好的;有良好名誉的;享有盛名的 [formal]

  • He was a writer of repute.

    他曾经是一位颇有名望的作者。

  • Chicago has 6 graduate and professional schools of high repute.

    芝加哥有6所颇负盛名的研究生院和专业院校。

2 N-UNCOUNT 好名声;名誉;名望 A person's or organization's repute is their reputation, especially when this is good. 好名声;名誉;名望 [formal]

  • Under his stewardship, the UN's repute has risen immeasurably.

    在他的管理下，联合国的声望得到了极大提升。

SYN reputation, standing, fame, celebrity  
3 PHRASE 听说;耳闻 If you know someone by repute, you have never met them but you have heard or read about them. 听说;耳闻 [formal]

  • I only knew him by repute.

    我只是听说过他。
