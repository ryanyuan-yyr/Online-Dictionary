underfunded
ʌndəʳfʌndɪd
(also under-funded)
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (组织或机构)资金短缺的，财力不足的，经费拮据的 An organization or institution that is underfunded does not have enough money to spend, and so it cannot function properly. (组织或机构)资金短缺的，财力不足的，经费拮据的

  • For years we have argued that the health service is underfunded.

    多年来我们一直都在说卫生医疗方面资金投入不足。

  • Most adults believe state schools are underfunded.

    大多数成年人都认为公立学校经费不足。

  • ...underfunded pensions.

    资金短缺的养老金
