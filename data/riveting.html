riveting
rɪvɪtɪŋ ˈrɪvətɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 吸引人的;引人入胜的;令人着迷的 If you describe something as riveting, you mean that it is extremely interesting and exciting, and that it holds your attention completely. 吸引人的;引人入胜的;令人着迷的

  • I find snooker riveting though I don't play myself.

    虽然我自己不打斯诺克，但是我觉得它挺令人着迷。

SYN enthralling, arresting, gripping, fascinating
