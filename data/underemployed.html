underemployed
ʌndərɪmplɔɪd ˌʌndərɪmˈplɔɪd ˌʌndərɛmˈplɔɪd
ADJ （人）未尽其才的，大材小用的 If someone is underemployed, they have not got enough work to do, or their work does not make full use of their skills or abilities. （人）未尽其才的，大材小用的

  • He was underemployed and not satisfied with his work.

    他的才干得不到充分施展，因此他不满意自己的工作。
