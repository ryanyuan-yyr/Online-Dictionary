whammy
hwæmi hwæmi wæmi
N-SING [adjective NOUN] (困难或打击)同时发生，相继发生;祸不单行 Whammy is used in expressions such as double whammy and triple whammy to indicate that two or three unpleasant or difficult situations occur at the same time, or occur one after the other. (困难或打击)同时发生，相继发生;祸不单行 [mainly journalism]

  • This is a double whammy for public sector workers. [+ for]

    对于公共部门的工作人员而言可谓祸不单行。

  • We're suffering a triple whammy at the moment.

    我们目前正遭受到了接二连三的打击。
