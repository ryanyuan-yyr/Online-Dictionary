phase out
PHRASAL VERB 逐步淘汰；逐步放弃 If something is phased out, people gradually stop using it. 逐步淘汰；逐步放弃 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They said the present system of military conscription should be phased out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他们说，当前的征兵体系应当逐步废除。

  • They phased out my job in favor of a computer. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们逐步用一台计算机淘汰了我的工作岗位

SYN eliminate, close, pull, remove
