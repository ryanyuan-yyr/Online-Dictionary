espionage
espiənɑːʒ ˈɛspɪəˌnɑːʒ ˈɛspiəˌnɑʒ ˈɛspiəˌnɑdʒ
1 N-UNCOUNT 间谍行为;谍报活动 Espionage is the activity of finding out the political, military, or industrial secrets of your enemies or rivals by using spies. 间谍行为;谍报活动 [formal]

  • The authorities have arrested several people suspected of espionage.

    官方已经逮捕了几个涉嫌从事间谍活动的人。

  • ...industrial espionage.

    工业间谍活动

SYN spying, intelligence, surveillance, counter-intelligence
