day one
deɪ wʌn
N-SING 第一天;最开始 If something happens from day one of a process, it happens right from the beginning. If it happens on day one, it happens right at the beginning. 第一天;最开始

  • This has been a bad inquiry from day one.

    调查工作从一开始就进行不力。

  • On day one of the election campaign the National and Liberal Party leaders were neck and neck.

    从竞选活动的第一天起，国家党和自由党的领导人就难分高下。
