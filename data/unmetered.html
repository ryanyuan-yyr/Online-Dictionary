unmetered
ʌnmiːtəʳd ʌnˈmiːtəd
ADJ 不按用量计费的 An unmetered service for something such as water supply or telephone access is one that allows you to use as much as you want for a basic cost, rather than paying for the amount you use. 不按用量计费的

  • Clients are not charged by the minute but given unmetered access to the Internet for a fixed fee.

    客户使用因特网不是按分钟计费的，而是交固定的费用后便可以随意使用。
