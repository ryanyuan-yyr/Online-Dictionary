short-handed
ˈʃɔrtˈhændɪd
(also shorthanded)
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 人手不足的 If a company, organization, or group is short-handed, it does not have enough people to work on a particular job or for a particular purpose. 人手不足的

  • We're actually a bit short-handed at the moment.

    眼下我们确实有点人手不足。
