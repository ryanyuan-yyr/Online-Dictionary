insolent
ɪnsələnt ˈɪnsələnt
ADJ-GRADED 傲慢的;无礼的;侮慢的 If you say that someone is being insolent, you mean they are being rude to someone they ought to be respectful to. 傲慢的;无礼的;侮慢的

  • ...her insolent stare.

    她无礼的瞪视

  • The officer stamped his boot. 'Don't be insolent with me, mademoiselle.'

    军官跺跺靴子：“小姐,别对我无礼。”

SYN rude, cheeky, impertinent, fresh [informal]  
insolence

  • Pupils could be excluded from school for insolence.

    学生可能会因目无师长而被开除。
