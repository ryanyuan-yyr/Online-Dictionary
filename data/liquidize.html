liquidize
lɪkwɪdaɪz ˈlɪkwɪdˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense liquidizes, present participle liquidizing, past tense , past participle liquidized

VERB （用电动器具）将（食物等）打成泥状；使成液态物 If you liquidize food, you process it using an electrical appliance in order to make it liquid. （用电动器具）将（食物等）打成泥状；使成液态物
