life-support machine laɪf səˈpɔːt məˈʃiːn
Word forms: plural life-support machines
N-COUNT 呼吸机; 生命维持设备 A life-support machine is the equipment that is used to keep a person alive when they are very ill and cannot breathe without help. 呼吸机; 生命维持设备 [mainly British]

  • He is in a coma and on a life-support machine.

    他处于昏迷中，靠呼吸机维持生命。
