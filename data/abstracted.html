abstracted
æbstræktɪd æbˈstræktɪd
ADJ-GRADED 出神的；心不在焉的 Someone who is abstracted is thinking so deeply that they are not fully aware of what is happening around them. 出神的；心不在焉的 [written]

  • Meg was so abstracted she scarcely noticed that the train had stopped.

    梅格想得出了神，几乎没有注意到火车已经停了。

  • The same abstracted look was still on his face.

    他仍是一脸恍惚。

SYN preoccupied, withdrawn, remote, absorbed  
abstractedly

  • She nodded abstractedly.

    她若有所思地点点头。
