lining
laɪnɪŋ ˈlaɪnɪŋ
Word forms: plural linings
1 N-VAR （衣服、帘子等的）衬里，里子，衬料 The lining of something such as a piece of clothing or a curtain is a layer of cloth attached to the inside of it in order to make it thicker or warmer, or in order to make it hang better. （衣服、帘子等的）衬里，里子，衬料

  • ...a padded satin jacket with quilted lining.

    带夹棉衬里的缎面短外套

2 N-VAR （纸张、塑料、金属等材质的）内衬，衬垫，衬层 You can use lining to refer to a layer of paper, plastic, metal, or another substance that is attached to the inside of something, for example in order to protect it. （纸张、塑料、金属等材质的）内衬，衬垫，衬层

  • ...brake linings.

    刹车片

  • Moss makes an attractive lining to wire baskets. [+ to]

    用苔藓垫在铁丝篮里很漂亮。

3 N-COUNT （身体器官的）内膜 The lining of your stomach or other organ is a layer of tissue on the inside of it. （身体器官的）内膜

  • ...a bacterium that attacks the lining of the stomach. [+ of]

    攻击胃黏膜的细菌

  • ...the uterine lining.

    子宫内膜
