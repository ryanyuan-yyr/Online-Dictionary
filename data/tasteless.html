tasteless
teɪstləs ˈteɪstlɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED (家具、服装、房屋装修等)俗气的，难看的，没品位的 If you describe something such as furniture, clothing, or the way that a house is decorated as tasteless, you consider it to be vulgar and unattractive. (家具、服装、房屋装修等)俗气的，难看的，没品位的

  • ...a flat crammed with spectacularly tasteless objets d'art.

    摆满了俗不可耐的小艺术品的公寓

SYN gaudy, cheap, vulgar, tacky [informal]  
2 ADJ-GRADED (话语、玩笑等)粗俗的，不雅的，不得体的 If you describe something such as a remark or joke as tasteless, you mean that it is offensive. (话语、玩笑等)粗俗的，不雅的，不得体的

  • I think that is the most vulgar and tasteless remark I ever heard in my life.

    我觉得那是我平生听过的最粗俗不堪的话了。

3 ADJ-GRADED (食物、饮料)淡而无味的，没滋味的 If you describe food or drink as tasteless, you mean that it has very little or no flavour. (食物、饮料)淡而无味的，没滋味的

  • The fish was mushy and tasteless.

    鱼做得太烂了，没滋没味的。

SYN insipid, bland, flat, boring
