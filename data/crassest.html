crass
kræs kræs
Word forms: comparative crasser, superlative crassest
ADJ-GRADED (行为)愚蠢而无同情心的，不为他人考虑的 Crass behaviour is stupid and does not show consideration for other people. (行为)愚蠢而无同情心的，不为他人考虑的

  • The government has behaved with crass insensitivity.

    该政府行事愚蠢而且麻木不仁。

  • Pop records can be crass and cynical.

    流行音乐唱片可能会是冷漠而且愤世嫉俗的。

SYN insensitive, stupid, gross, blundering  
crassly

  • ...one of the most crassly stupid political acts of modern times.

    现代社会最愚不可及的政治行为之一

  • These teachings can be crassly misinterpreted.

    这些教义可能会被愚蠢地曲解。

crassness

  • ...the crassness of his conversation.

    他谈话时的愚钝
