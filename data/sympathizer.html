sympathizer
sɪmpəθaɪzəʳ
Word forms: plural sympathizers
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 sympathiser
N-COUNT [usually plural] 赞同者;支持者 The sympathizers of an organization or cause are the people who approve of it and support it. 赞同者;支持者

  • These villagers are guerrilla sympathizers.

    这些村民拥护游击队。

SYN supporter, partisan, protagonist, fellow traveller
