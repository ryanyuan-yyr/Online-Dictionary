piece together
1 PHRASAL VERB 拼凑出（真相） If you piece together the truth about something, you gradually discover it. 拼凑出（真相）

  • They've pieced together his movements for the last few days before his death. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们已理清了他去世前几天的活动情况。

  • In the following days, Francis was able to piece together what had happened. [VERB PREP. wh]

    在随后的几天里，弗朗西斯得以把发生的事情拼凑出来。

  • Frank was beginning to piece things together. [VERB noun PREP.]

    弗兰克开始拼凑出事情的真相。

SYN work out, understand, figure out, make out  
2 PHRASAL VERB 拼合；凑集 If you piece something together, you gradually make it by joining several things or parts together. 拼合；凑集 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • This process is akin to piecing together a jigsaw puzzle. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这个过程和拼图相似。

  • Doctors painstakingly pieced together the broken bones. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    医生们精心地把碎骨拼合起来。

SYN mend, unite, join, fix
