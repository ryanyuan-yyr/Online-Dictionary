manna
mænə ˈmænə
PHRASE 天赐之物;(从天而降的)恩赐 If you say that something unexpected is manna from heaven, you mean that it is good and happened just at the time that it was needed. 天赐之物;(从天而降的)恩赐

  • Ex-forces personnel could be the manna from heaven employers are seeking.

    当过兵的人大概是雇主们寻找的宝贝了。

  • The revealed documents were manna for journalists.

    这些披露出的文件让记者们如获至宝。
