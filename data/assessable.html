assess
əses əˈsɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense assesses, present participle assessing, past tense , past participle assessed
1 VERB 评估；评价；考查 When you assess a person, thing, or situation, you consider them in order to make a judgment about them. 评估；评价；考查

  • Our correspondent has been assessing the impact of the sanctions. [VERB noun]

    我们的记者一直在评估制裁带来的影响。

  • The test was to assess aptitude rather than academic achievement. [VERB noun]

    这次测试是考查能力，而不是学习成绩。

  • It would be a matter of assessing whether she was well enough to travel. [VERB wh]

    问题是要看她是否已康复到可以旅行。

SYN judge, determine, estimate, fix  
2 VERB 评估（价值）；估价 When you assess the amount of money that something is worth or should be paid, you calculate or estimate it. 评估（价值）；估价 [Also VERB wh]

  • Ask them to send you information on how to assess the value of your belongings. [VERB noun]

    让他们把怎样评估你的财产价值的资料寄来。

  • What's the property's assessed value? [VERB-ed]

    这些财产的估值是多少？

SYN evaluate, rate, tax, value
