diagram
daɪəgræm ˈdaɪəˌgræm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense diagrams, present participle diagramming, past tense , past participle diagrammed
in AM, use 美国英语用 diagraming, diagramed
1 N-COUNT 图表;简图;示意图 A diagram is a simple drawing which consists mainly of lines and is used, for example, to explain how a machine works. 图表;简图;示意图

  • ...a circuit diagram.

    电路图

  • You can reduce long explanations to simple charts or diagrams.

    你可以把冗长的解释简化成一目了然的图表。

SYN plan, figure, drawing, chart  
2 VERB 绘制…的示意图;用图表表示（或说明） To diagram something means to draw a diagram of it or to explain it using a diagram. 绘制…的示意图;用图表表示（或说明） [mainly US, formal]

  • The sound waves of the voice could be diagramed as in B. [be VERB-ed]

    这个声音的声波如图B所示。

  • ...diagramming the movement of a system's variables. [VERB noun]

    用图表表示系统各个变量的变化情况
