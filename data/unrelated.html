unrelated
ʌnrɪleɪtɪd ˌʌnrɪˈleɪtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 没关联的；没联系的 If one thing is unrelated to another, there is no connection between them. You can also say that two things are unrelated . 没关联的；没联系的

  • My line of work is entirely unrelated to politics. [+ to]

    我的职业与政治完全无关。

  • Two of them died from apparently unrelated causes.

    他们中的两个人死因明显不同。

SYN unconnected, different, dissimilar, unlike  
2 ADJ 不属于同一家族的；没有亲属关系的 If one person is unrelated to another, they are not members of the same family. You can also say that two people are unrelated . 不属于同一家族的；没有亲属关系的 [written]

  • Jimmy is adopted and thus unrelated to Beth by blood. [+ to]

    吉米是被收养的，因此和贝丝没有血缘关系。
