disenchanted
dɪsɪntʃɑːntɪd -tʃænt-
ADJ-GRADED 不再着迷的;不再抱有幻想的;感到幻灭的 If you are disenchanted with something, you are disappointed with it and no longer believe that it is good or worthwhile. 不再着迷的;不再抱有幻想的;感到幻灭的

  • I'm disenchanted with the state of British theatre at the moment. [+ with]

    我对英国戏剧当下的状况不再抱有幻想。

SYN disillusioned, disappointed, soured, cynical
