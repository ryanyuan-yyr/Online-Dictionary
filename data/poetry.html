poetry
poʊɪtri ˈpoʊətri
1 N-UNCOUNT （作为文学形式的）诗，诗歌，韵文 Poems, considered as a form of literature, are referred to as poetry . （作为文学形式的）诗，诗歌，韵文

  • ...Russian poetry.

    俄罗斯诗歌

  • Lawrence Durrell wrote a great deal of poetry.

    劳伦斯·达雷尔创作了大量的诗歌。

  • Since when have you been interested in poetry?

    你从什么时候开始对诗歌感兴趣的？

2 N-UNCOUNT 诗的意境；诗一般美的事物 You can describe something very beautiful as poetry . 诗的意境；诗一般美的事物

  • His music is purer poetry than a poem in words.

    他的音乐比真正的诗歌更具诗意。
