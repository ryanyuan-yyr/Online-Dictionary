summarize
sʌməraɪz ˈsʌməˌraɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense summarizes, present participle summarizing, past tense , past participle summarized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 summarise
VERB 总结;概括;概述 If you summarize something, you give a summary of it. 总结;概括;概述 [Also VERB with quote]

  • Table 3.1 summarizes the information given above. [VERB noun]

    表格 3.1 是对以上信息的总结。

  • Basically, the article can be summarized in three sentences. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    这篇文章基本上可以概述为 3 句话。

  • To summarise, this is a clever approach to a common problem. [VERB]

    总而言之，这是一个解决常见问题的巧妙方法。

SYN sum up, recap, review, outline
