sit back
PHRASAL VERB 不插手;袖手旁观 If you sit back while something is happening, you relax and do not become involved in it. 不插手;袖手旁观 [informal]

  • They didn't have to do anything except sit back and enjoy life. [VERB PREP.]

    他们只需置身事外，享受生活。

  • American firms handed over technologies to their partners and then sat back to enjoy the cash flow. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    美国公司把技术移交给了合作方，然后就坐等着收钱了。
