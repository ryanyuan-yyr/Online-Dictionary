counter
kaʊntəʳ ˈkaʊntər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense counters, present participle countering, past tense , past participle countered
1 N-COUNT (商店或酒吧的)柜台 In a place such as a shop or café a counter is a long narrow table or flat surface at which customers are served. (商店或酒吧的)柜台

  • ...those fellows we see working behind the counter at our local video rental store.

    我们见到在我们当地录像带出租店里做店员的那些人

  • ...the cosmetics counter.

    化妆品柜台

  • We were sitting on stools at the counter having coffee.

    我们坐在柜台边的凳子上喝着咖啡。

2 VERB 抵制;抵消 If you do something to counter a particular action or process, you do something which has an opposite effect to it or makes it less effective. 抵制;抵消

  • The leadership discussed a plan of economic measures to counter the effects of such a blockade. [VERB noun]

    领导层讨论了计划采取的一揽子经济措施，以减轻这一封锁带来的影响。

  • Congestion could be countered by persuading more drivers to get on their bikes. [VERB noun]

    通过劝说更多的驾车者改骑自行车可以缓解交通拥堵。

  • Sears then countered by filing an antitrust lawsuit. [VERB + by]

    于是西尔斯提起反托拉斯诉讼进行反击。

SYN oppose, meet, block, resist  
3 N-SING （…的）对立面;（与…）相对的事物 Something that is a counter to something else has an opposite effect to it or makes it less effective. （…的）对立面;（与…）相对的事物

  • ...NATO's traditional role as a counter to the military might of the Warsaw pact. [+ to]

    北约作为与华沙公约军事力量相抗衡的传统角色

4 VERB 反驳;驳斥 If you counter something that someone has said, you say something which shows that you disagree with them or which proves that they are wrong. 反驳;驳斥 [Also VERB that]

  • Both of them had to counter fierce criticism. [VERB noun]

    他们两个人都不得不对尖锐的批评进行反击。

  • The union countered with letters rebutting the company's claims. [VERB + with]

    工会写信驳回了公司的要求。

  • The Prime Minister countered by stating that he had grave misgivings about the advice he had been given. [VERB + by]

    总理反驳说他对自己所收到的那些建议颇为顾虑。

  • 'But Peter, it's not that simple,' Goldstone countered in a firm voice. [VERB with quote]

    “但是，彼得，事情并非那么简单，”戈德斯通语气坚决地驳斥道。

SYN retaliate, return, answer, reply  
5 N-COUNT (机械或电子)计数器，计算器 A counter is a mechanical or electronic device which keeps a count of something and displays the total. (机械或电子)计数器，计算器

  • ...an answerphone with an LED display call counter.

    带发光二极管来电显示屏的电话答录机

6 N-COUNT (棋盘游戏的)筹码 A counter is a small, flat, round object used in board games. (棋盘游戏的)筹码
7 See also: bargaining counter  bean counter  Geiger counter  rev counter 
8 PHRASE (买药)不用处方地 If a medicine can be bought over the counter, you do not need a prescription to buy it. (买药)不用处方地

  • Are you taking any other medicines whether on prescription or bought over the counter?

    你在服用其他什么处方药或非处方药吗？

  • ...basic over-the-counter remedies.

    基本的非处方药物

9 PHRASE (股票)场外交易的，不通过交易所买卖的 Over-the-counter shares are bought and sold directly rather than on a stock exchange. (股票)场外交易的，不通过交易所买卖的
10 PHRASE 与…相对;与…冲突 If one thing runs counter to another, or if one thing is counter to another, the first thing is the opposite of the second thing or conflicts with it. 与…相对;与…冲突 [formal]

  • Much of the plan runs counter to European agriculture and environmental policy.

    该计划的很多内容与欧洲农业及环境方面的政策相冲突。

  • The finding ran counter to all expectations.

    该发现出乎所有人的预料。

  • ...the introduction of social legislation in Europe, which is counter to American practice.

    与美国的做法相对立的欧洲社会立法的推行

11 PHRASE 秘密地，暗地里，非法地(销售商品) If someone buys or sells goods under the counter, they buy or sell them secretly and illegally. 秘密地，暗地里，非法地(销售商品)

  • The smugglers allegedly sold the gold under the counter, cheating the VAT man out of £5 million.

    据说走私者暗地销售黄金，偷逃增值税达500万英镑。

counter- kaʊntəʳ- ˈkaʊntər
PREFIX (用于构词)表示“逆”，“相反”，“相应”，“对应” Counter- is used to form words which refer to actions or activities that are intended to prevent other actions or activities or that respond to them. (用于构词)表示“逆”，“相反”，“相应”，“对应”

  • The army now appears to have launched a counter-offensive.

    军队现在似乎已经发起了反攻。

  • ...the chief of counterterrorist operations.

    反恐军事行动的负责人

  • ...a counter-demonstration by anti-war protesters.

    反战抗议者举行的反游行活动

  • In recent weeks, Abrams has counterattacked.

    最近几周，艾布拉姆斯进行了反击。
