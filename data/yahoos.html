yahoo
jɑːhuː jɑːhuː jəˈhuː
Word forms: plural yahoos
Pronounced /jɑː'huː/ for meaning 1, and /'jɑːhuː/ for meaning 2. 义项1读作 /jɑː'huː/, 义项2读作 /'jɑːhuː/。
1 EXCLAM （用于表示极度高兴或兴奋）哇，啊 People sometimes shout 'yahoo!' when they are very happy or excited about something. （用于表示极度高兴或兴奋）哇，啊
2 N-COUNT 招摇的年轻人；富家公子 Some people refer to young rich people as yahoos when they disapprove of them because they behave in a noisy, extravagant, and unpleasant way. 招摇的年轻人；富家公子 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • ..a typical City merchant banking yahoo.

    伦敦商业区典型的奢侈挥霍的年轻商业银行家

SYN philistine, savage, lout, beast
