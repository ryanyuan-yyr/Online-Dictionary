hoi polloi
hɔɪpəlɔɪ ˌhɔɪ pəˈlɔɪ ˈhɔɪpəˈlɔɪ
N-PLURAL 草民;乌合之众;老百姓 If someone refers to the hoi polloi, they are referring in a humorous or rather rude way to ordinary people, in contrast to rich, well-educated, or upper-class people. 草民;乌合之众;老百姓

  • Monstrously inflated costs are designed to keep the hoi polloi at bay.

    居高不下的生活开支势必会把老百姓逼入走投无路的境地。

SYN the common people, the masses, the (common) herd, the underclass
