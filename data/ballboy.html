ball boy
Word forms: plural ball boys
N-COUNT （网球或棒球比赛中负责拾球的）球童，捡球员 In a tennis match, the ball boys pick up any balls that go into the net or off the court and throw them back to the players. In a baseball game, the ball boys are in charge of collecting the balls that are hit out of the field. （网球或棒球比赛中负责拾球的）球童，捡球员
