symmetry
sɪmɪtri ˈsɪmətri
Word forms: plural symmetries
1 N-VAR (形状、设计或结构的)对称，匀称 Something that has symmetry is symmetrical in shape, design, or structure. (形状、设计或结构的)对称，匀称

  • ...the incredible beauty and symmetry of a snowflake.

    雪花难以置信的美丽和对称

  • I loved the house because it had perfect symmetry.

    我喜欢这所房子，因为它非常对称。

  • Their own lives already seemed to possess the symmetries of narrative art.

    他们自己的生活似乎已经拥有叙事艺术的对称性。

SYN balance, proportion, regularity, form  
2 N-UNCOUNT (数量的)相等，相当 Symmetry in a relationship or agreement is the fact of both sides giving and receiving an equal amount. (数量的)相等，相当

  • The superpowers pledged to maintain symmetry in their arms shipments.

    超级大国承诺在武器运输方面要保持数量均衡。

SYN equality, agreement, balance, proportion  
3 N-VAR (国家、机构、局势的)相似,相仿 You can refer to symmetry between countries, institutions, or situations if you think that there is a close similarity between them. (国家、机构、局势的)相似,相仿

  • He said that Britain would not rejoin the ERM 'until there is much greater symmetry between our economy and other European economies.'

    他说在英国经济与其他欧洲国家的经济实现更大的趋同性之前，英国不会重新加入欧洲汇率机制。
