scared
skeəʳd ˈskɛəd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 惊恐的;恐惧的;害怕的 If you are scared of someone or something, you are frightened of them. 惊恐的;恐惧的;害怕的

  • I'm certainly not scared of him. [+ of]

    我当然不怕他。

  • I was too scared to move.

    我吓得动弹不得。

  • Why are you so scared?

    你为什么如此害怕？

SYN afraid, alarmed, frightened, terrified  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ that] 担心的;害怕的;焦虑的 If you are scared that something unpleasant might happen, you are nervous and worried because you think that it might happen. 担心的;害怕的;焦虑的

  • I was scared that I might be sick.

    我担心自己可能病了。

  • He was scared of letting us down. [+ of]

    他怕让我们失望。

3 PHRASE 非常恐惧的;极度害怕的;吓得要死的;吓傻的 If you are scared to death or scared stiff, you are extremely scared. 非常恐惧的;极度害怕的;吓得要死的;吓傻的 [emphasis]

  • In high school I was scared to death of you.

    高中时我怕你怕得要命。

  • He's scared stiff of the relationship breaking down.

    他非常害怕关系破裂。
