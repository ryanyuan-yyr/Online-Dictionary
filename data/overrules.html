overrule
oʊvəʳruːl ˌoʊvərˈrul
(also over-rule)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overrules, present participle overruling, past tense , past participle overruled
VERB 否决；驳回；宣布…无效 If someone in authority overrules a person or their decision, they officially decide that the decision is incorrect or not valid. 否决；驳回；宣布…无效

  • In 1991, the Court of Appeal overruled this decision. [VERB noun]

    1991年，上诉法院驳回了这一判决。

  • I told them it was a lousy idea, but I was overruled. [VERB noun]

    我告诉他们这个主意糟糕透顶，但是他们却拒不听从。

SYN reverse, alter, cancel, recall
