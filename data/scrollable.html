scroll
skroʊl skroʊl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense scrolls, present participle scrolling, past tense , past participle scrolled
1 N-COUNT (带有文字的)卷轴，长纸卷 A scroll is a long roll of paper or a similar material with writing on it. (带有文字的)卷轴，长纸卷

  • Ancient scrolls were found in caves by the Dead Sea.

    死海旁边的山洞里发现了古代的卷轴。

SYN roll, paper, parchment  
2 N-COUNT (绘制或雕刻的)涡卷形装饰 A scroll is a painted or carved decoration made to look like a scroll. (绘制或雕刻的)涡卷形装饰

  • ...a handsome suite of chairs incised with Grecian scrolls.

    一套雕刻着古希腊式涡卷形装饰的造型美观的椅子

3 VERB (在屏幕上)滚动(文档),滚屏 If you scroll through text on a computer screen, you move the text up or down to find the information that you need. (在屏幕上)滚动(文档),滚屏 [computing]

  • I scrolled down to find 'United States of America'. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我向下滚动文档搜索“美利坚合众国”。

SYN move,
