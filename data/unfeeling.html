unfeeling
ʌnfiːlɪŋ ʌnˈfilɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED （人）没同情心的，无情的 If you describe someone as unfeeling, you are criticizing them for their lack of kindness or sympathy for other people. （人）没同情心的，无情的 [written, disapproval]

  • He was branded an unfeeling bully.

    他被说成冷酷的恶棍。

  • There's no way anyone could accuse this woman of being cold and unfeeling.

    谁都不能指责这个女人冷血无情。

  • It was a flippant and unfeeling remark.

    这番话既轻率又无情。
