asking price
Word forms: plural asking prices
N-COUNT [usually singular] (卖方的）开价，要价 The asking price of something is the price which the person selling it says that they want for it, although they may accept less. (卖方的）开价，要价

  • Offers 15% below the asking price are unlikely to be accepted.

    低于要价15％的报价不大可能被接受。
