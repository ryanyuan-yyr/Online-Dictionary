sunken
sʌŋkən ˈsʌŋkən
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 沉没的;沉入水底的 Sunken ships have sunk to the bottom of a sea, ocean, or lake. 沉没的;沉入水底的

  • The sunken sailing-boat was a glimmer of white on the bottom.

    沉没的帆船在海底闪烁着白光。

  • Try diving for sunken treasure.

    试着潜入水下找寻沉没的财宝。

SYN submerged, immersed, submersed  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (花园、公路等)低于周围地面的，低洼处的 Sunken gardens, roads, or other features are below the level of their surrounding area. (花园、公路等)低于周围地面的，低洼处的

  • Steps lead down to the sunken garden.

    台阶通向低处的花园。

  • The room was dominated by a sunken bath.

    嵌入地面的浴缸占了房间的大半空间。

SYN lowered, buried, depressed, recessed  
3 ADJ (眼睛、面颊等)凹陷的，深陷的 Sunken eyes, cheeks, or other parts of the body curve inwards and make you look thin and unwell. (眼睛、面颊等)凹陷的，深陷的

  • Her eyes were sunken and black-ringed.

    她眼圈发黑，眼窝深陷。

  • ...an elderly man with sunken cheeks.

    脸颊凹陷的老人

SYN hollow, drawn, haggard, hollowed
