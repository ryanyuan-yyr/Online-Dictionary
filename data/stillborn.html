stillborn
stɪlbɔːʳn ˈstɪlˈbɔrn
1 ADJ 死产的;出生时已夭折的 A stillborn baby is dead when it is born. 死产的;出生时已夭折的

  • It was a miracle that she survived the birth of her stillborn baby.

    她生了死胎还能活下来，真是奇迹。

  • Their first child was stillborn.

    他们的第一个孩子出生时就夭折了。

2 ADJ 完全无效的;完全失败的 An idea, action, or attempt which is stillborn is completely ineffective or unsuccessful. 完全无效的;完全失败的

  • The ceasefire itself could prove stillborn if rebel units in the bush keep on fighting.

    如果丛林地带的叛军继续开火，那停火协议本身就可能起不到任何作用。

  • ...stillborn agreements.

    不起任何作用的协议
