pension plan ˈpɛnʃən plæn
Word forms: plural pension plans
N-COUNT 养老金计划；退休金制度 A pension plan is an arrangement to receive a pension from an organization such as an insurance company or a former employer in return for making regular payments to them over a number of years. 养老金计划；退休金制度 [business]

  • I would have been much wiser to start my own pension plan when I was younger.

    我要是年轻点时就开始自己的养老金计划就明智多了。
