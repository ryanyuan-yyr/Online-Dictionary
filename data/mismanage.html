mismanage
mɪsmænɪdʒ mɪsˈmænɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mismanages, present participle mismanaging, past tense , past participle mismanaged
VERB 对…处置不当;对…管理不善 To mismanage something means to manage it badly. 对…处置不当;对…管理不善

  • 75% of voters think the President has mismanaged the economy. [VERB noun]

    75%的选民认为总统对经济管理不善。

SYN mishandle, bungle, botch, mess up
