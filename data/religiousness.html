religious
rɪlɪdʒəs rɪˈlɪdʒəs
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 宗教的;信教的 You use religious to describe things that are connected with religion or with one particular religion. 宗教的;信教的

  • ...religious groups.

    宗教团体

  • ...different religious beliefs.

    不同的宗教信仰

SYN spiritual, holy, sacred, divine  
religiously

  • India has always been one of the most religiously diverse countries.

    印度一直都是宗教信仰最多元的国家之一。

2 ADJ-GRADED 虔诚的;宗教信仰坚定的 Someone who is religious has a strong belief in a god or gods. 虔诚的;宗教信仰坚定的

  • They are both very religious and felt it was a gift from God.

    他们俩都很虔诚，感觉这是上帝的恩赐。

SYN devout, believing, godly, committed  
3 See also: religiously
