jittery
dʒɪtəri ˈdʒɪtəri
ADJ-GRADED 紧张不安的;举止慌张的 If someone is jittery, they feel nervous or are behaving nervously. 紧张不安的;举止慌张的 [informal]

  • International investors have become jittery about the country's economy. [+ about]

    国际投资者已对该国的经济状况感到紧张不安。

SYN nervous, anxious, jumpy, twitchy [informal]
