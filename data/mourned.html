mourn
mɔːʳn mɔrn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mourns, present participle mourning, past tense , past participle mourned
1 VERB 悼念;哀悼;向…致哀 If you mourn someone who has died or mourn for them, you are very sad that they have died and show your sorrow in the way that you behave. 悼念;哀悼;向…致哀

  • Joan still mourns her father. [VERB noun]

    琼仍在为父亲的死伤心。

  • The whole nation had mourned the death of their great leader. [VERB noun]

    举国向去世的伟大领袖表示了深深的哀悼。

  • He mourned for his valiant men. [VERB + for]

    他向他的勇士们致哀。

  • As the nation continued to mourn, the new President of South Africa paid his own tribute. [VERB]

    举国仍在哀悼之际，南非新任总统也表达了自己的怀念之情。

2 VERB 为失去…而悲哀;为…感到遗憾 If you mourn something or mourn for it, you regret that you no longer have it and show your regret in the way that you behave. 为失去…而悲哀;为…感到遗憾

  • We mourned the loss of our cities. [VERB noun]

    我们为失去多座城市而痛心。

  • She mourned for the beloved past. [VERB + for]

    她怀念美好的过去。

SYN bemoan, rue [literary] , deplore, bewail
