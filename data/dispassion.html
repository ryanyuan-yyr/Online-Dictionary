dispassionate
dɪspæʃənət dɪsˈpæʃənɪt dɪsˈpæʃənət
ADJ-GRADED 不动感情的；不带偏见的 Someone who is dispassionate is calm and reasonable, and not affected by emotions. 不动感情的；不带偏见的

  • We, as prosecutors, try to be dispassionate about the cases we bring.

    作为检察官，我们对经手的案件会尽力做到不偏不倚。

  • He spoke in the flat, dispassionate tone of a lecturer.

    他以一个讲师般平板、不动感情的口吻说话。

SYN objective, fair, neutral, detached  
dispassionately

  • He sets out the facts coolly and dispassionately.

    他冷静、客观地摆出了事实。
