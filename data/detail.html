detail
diːteɪl dɪteɪl dɪˈteɪl ˈdiˌteɪl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense details, present participle detailing, past tense , past participle detailed
The pronunciation /dɪ'teɪl/ is also used in American English. 美国英语亦读作 /dɪ'teɪl/。
1 N-COUNT 细节 The details of something are its individual features or elements. 细节

  • The details of the plan are still being worked out. [+ of]

    计划的细节仍在制订中。

  • No details of the discussions have been given.

    没有透露讨论的细节。

  • I recall every detail of the party.

    我回想起聚会的每一个细节。

SYN point, fact, feature, particular  
2 N-PLURAL [adjective NOUN] 详情;信息;资料 Details about someone or something are facts or pieces of information about them. 详情;信息;资料

  • See the bottom of this page for details of how to apply for this exciting offer. [+ of]

    有关如何申请这一令人兴奋的工作的详情请参见本页底部。

  • Full details will be announced soon.

    具体信息很快就会公布。

3 N-COUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 枝节;末节;琐事 A detail is a minor point or aspect of something, as opposed to the central ones. 枝节;末节;琐事

  • Only minor details now remain to be settled.

    现在仅剩一些细枝末节的问题需要解决。

SYN fine point, part, particular, trifle  
4 N-UNCOUNT (难以觉察的)细微之处 You can refer to the small features of something which are often not noticed as detail . (难以觉察的)细微之处

  • We like his attention to detail and his enthusiasm.

    我们喜欢他的细心和热情。

5 N-COUNT (画等的)细部 A detail of a picture is a part of it that is printed separately and perhaps made bigger, so that smaller features can be clearly seen. (画等的)细部
6 VERB 详述;详细列举（或说明） If you detail things, you list them or give information about them. 详述;详细列举（或说明） [Also V how]

  • The report detailed the human rights abuses committed during the war. [VERB noun]

    这份报告详述了战争期间所发生的践踏人权的情况。

SYN list, describe, relate, catalogue  
7 VERB 派遣;指示 If someone is detailed to do a task or job, they are officially ordered to do it. 派遣;指示 [formal] [Also V n to n]

  • He detailed a constable to take it to the Incident Room. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    他派了一名警察把它送到重案调查室。

SYN appoint, name, choose, commission  
8 PHRASE 详细叙述;逐一说明 If someone does not go into details about a subject, or does not go into the detail, they mention it without explaining it fully or properly. 详细叙述;逐一说明

  • He said he had been in various parts of Britain but did not go into details.

    他说曾去过英国的许多地方，但没有细讲。

  • Neither of them were prepared to go into the detail of their talks.

    他们俩都没准备要详细说明他们会谈的情况。

9 PHRASE 详细地;全面地;仔细地 If you examine or discuss something in detail, you do it thoroughly and carefully. 详细地;全面地;仔细地

  • We examine the wording in detail before deciding on the final text.

    我们彻底仔细地检查了措词后才最终定稿。

SYN comprehensively, completely, fully, thoroughly
