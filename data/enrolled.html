enrol
ɪnroʊl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enrols, present participle enrolling, past tense , past participle enrolled
in AM, use 美国英语用 enroll
V-ERG 招收（成员）；招（生）登记入学;注册（课程） If you enrol or are enrolled at an institution or on a course, you officially join it and pay a fee for it. 招收（成员）；招（生）登记入学;注册（课程） [Also VERB]

  • Cherny was enrolled at the University in 1945. [be VERB-ed prep.]

    彻尼1945年在该大学登记入学。

  • She enrolled on a local Women Into Management course. [VERB prep.]

    她注册学习当地的“女性参与管理”课程。

  • At 33 he enrolled in a glass-making degree course at London's Royal College of Art. [VERB prep.]

    33岁时，他在伦敦皇家艺术学院注册学习玻璃制作学位课程。

  • I thought I'd enrol you with an art group at the school. [VERB noun prep.]

    我想我会吸收你参加学校的一个艺术团。

SYN recruit, take on, engage, enlist
