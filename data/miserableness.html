miserable
mɪzərəbəl ˈmɪzərəbəl ˈmɪzrəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 苦恼的;痛苦的 If you are miserable, you are very unhappy. 苦恼的;痛苦的

  • I took a series of badly paid secretarial jobs which made me really miserable.

    我做了几份薪酬微薄的秘书工作，这让我非常郁闷。

  • She went to bed, miserable and depressed.

    她伤心而沮丧地上床睡觉。

SYN sad, down, low, depressed  
miserably

  • He looked miserably down at his plate.

    他忧郁地低下头看着盘子。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (地方、形势)令人难受的，令人抑郁的 If you describe a place or situation as miserable, you mean that it makes you feel unhappy or depressed. (地方、形势)令人难受的，令人抑郁的

  • There was nothing at all in this miserable place to distract him.

    这个鬼地方根本没有什么东西可以让他分心。

SYN dreary, bleak, desolate, sorry  
3 ADJ-GRADED (天气)阴郁的，令人苦恼的 If you describe the weather as miserable, you mean that it makes you feel depressed, because it is raining or dull. (天气)阴郁的，令人苦恼的

  • It was a grey, wet, miserable day.

    那是阴郁灰暗、潮湿多雨的一天。

  • It was very cold, damp and miserable.

    天气非常寒冷潮湿，令人难受。

SYN unpleasant, wet, rainy, stormy  
4 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 脾气坏的;不友善的;乖戾的 If you describe someone as miserable, you mean that you do not like them because they are bad-tempered or unfriendly. 脾气坏的;不友善的;乖戾的

  • He always was a miserable man. He never spoke to me nor anybody else.

    他的脾气总是很糟。他从不跟我或其他任何人讲话。

SYN sullen, sour, moody, grumpy  
5 ADJ 远远不足的;非常差劲的;少得可怜的 You can describe a quantity or quality as miserable when you think that it is much smaller or worse than it ought to be. 远远不足的;非常差劲的;少得可怜的 [emphasis]

  • Our speed over the ground was a miserable 2.2 knots.

    我们在接近地面时的航速仅为2.2节。

  • It has so far accepted a miserable 1,100 refugees from the former Yugoslavia.

    迄今为止，从前南斯拉夫接收的难民只有区区1,100人。

SYN pathetic, low, sorry, disgraceful  
miserably

  • ...the miserably inadequate supply of books now provided for schools.

    目前提供给学校的数量远远不够的图书

6 ADJ [ADJ n] （失败）悲惨的，彻底的 A miserable failure is a very great one. （失败）悲惨的，彻底的 [emphasis]

  • The film was a miserable commercial failure both in Italy and in the United States.

    这部电影在意大利和美国均遭遇票房惨败。

miserably

  • Some manage it. Some fail miserably.

    有些人成功了，而有些人则遭遇惨败。
