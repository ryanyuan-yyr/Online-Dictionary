utterly
ʌtəʳli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 全然；完全地；彻底地 You use utterly to emphasize that something is very great in extent, degree, or amount. 全然；完全地；彻底地 [emphasis]

  • Everything about the country seemed utterly different from what I'd experienced before.

    这个国家的一切似乎与我以前的经历完全不同。

  • The new laws coming in are utterly ridiculous.

    新颁布的法律荒唐至极。

  • Such an allegation is utterly without foundation.

    这样的指控完全没有根据。

SYN totally, completely, absolutely, just
