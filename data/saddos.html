saddo
sædoʊ
Word forms: plural saddos
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 笨蛋;蠢货;不中用的东西 If you say that someone is a saddo, you do not have any respect for them and think their behaviour or ideas are ridiculous. 笨蛋;蠢货;不中用的东西 [British, informal]

  • Look at those saddos going to the Royal Opera House, I would jeer.

    看到那些去皇家歌剧院的蠢家伙，我会不屑一顾。
