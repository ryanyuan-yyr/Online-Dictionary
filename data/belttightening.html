belt-tightening ˈbɛltˌtaɪtənɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 紧缩开支；省吃俭用；节衣缩食 If you need to do some belt-tightening, you must spend less money and manage without things because you have less money than you used to have. 紧缩开支；省吃俭用；节衣缩食

  • This will cause further belt-tightening in the public services.

    这会导致公共事业单位进一步紧缩开支。
