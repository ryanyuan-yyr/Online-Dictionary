blag
blæg
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blags, present participle blagging, past tense blagged
VERB 通过花言巧语获得；巧妙地免费得到 To blag something such as a concert ticket means to persuade someone to give it to you free. 通过花言巧语获得；巧妙地免费得到 [British, informal]

  • She'd heard he was a musician and blagged a tape off a friend of his. [VERB noun]

    她听说他是个音乐家，就花言巧语从他朋友那里要了一盘带子。
