appendage
əpendɪdʒ əˈpɛndɪdʒ
Word forms: plural appendages
N-COUNT 附属物；附加物 An appendage is something that is joined to or connected with something larger or more important. 附属物；附加物 [formal]

  • ...the growing demand in Wales for recognition that it was not just an appendage to England. [+ to/of]

    有越来越多的威尔士人要求得到认可，即威尔士不只是英格兰的附属

  • Macmillan must have loathed being judged as a mere appendage to domestic politics.

    麦克米伦肯定极不喜欢只被当成国内政治的附属品。
