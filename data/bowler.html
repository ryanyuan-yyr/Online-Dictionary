bowler
boʊləʳ ˈbaʊlə ˈbəʊlə hæt ˈboʊlər
Word forms: plural bowlers
1 N-COUNT （板球等运动中的）投球手，投球员 The bowler in a sport such as cricket is the player who is bowling the ball. （板球等运动中的）投球手，投球员

  • He's a rather good fast bowler.

    他是一个非常棒的快投手。

2 N-COUNT 玩草地滚木球游戏的人；保龄球选手 A bowler is someone who plays bowls or goes bowling. 玩草地滚木球游戏的人；保龄球选手
3 N-COUNT 同bowler hat A bowler is the same as a bowler hat . 同bowler hat [mainly British]
