unmatched
ʌnmætʃt ʌnˈmætʃt
ADJ 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 If you describe something as unmatched, you are emphasizing that it is better or greater than all other things of the same kind. 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的 [emphasis]

  • ...a landscape of unmatched beauty.

    无与伦比的美景

  • Brian's old-fashioned cuisine was unmatched for flavour. [+ for]

    布赖恩的传统风味菜肴味道简直无与伦比。

SYN unequalled, supreme, unparalleled, paramount
