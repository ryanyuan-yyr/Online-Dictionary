cluster
klʌstəʳ ˈklʌstər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense clusters, present participle clustering, past tense , past participle clustered
1 N-COUNT (人或物的)组，群，簇 A cluster of people or things is a small group of them close together. (人或物的)组，群，簇

  • ...clusters of men in formal clothes.

    一群群身着正装的男人

  • There's no town here, just a cluster of shops, cabins and motels at the side of the highway. [+ of]

    这儿没有城镇，只在公路边上有一片商店、小木屋和汽车旅馆。

SYN gathering, group, collection, bunch [informal]  
2 VERB (人)聚集 If people cluster together, they gather together in a small group. (人)聚集 [Also VERB preposition]

  • The passengers clustered together in small groups. [V + together]

    乘客们三五成群聚在一起。

  • The children clustered around me. [V + around/round]

    孩子们聚集在我的周围。

SYN gather, group, collect, bunch  
3See also:clustered
