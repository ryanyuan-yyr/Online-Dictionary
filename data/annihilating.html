annihilate
ənaɪɪleɪt əˈnaɪəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense annihilates, present participle annihilating, past tense , past participle annihilated
1 VERB 歼灭；消灭；毁灭；彻底击溃 To annihilate something means to destroy it completely. 歼灭；消灭；毁灭；彻底击溃

  • There are lots of ways of annihilating the planet. [VERB noun]

    毁灭地球有很多方法。

  • The Army was annihilated. [be VERB-ed]

    陆军被彻底击溃了。

SYN destroy, abolish, wipe out, erase  
annihilation

  • ...the threat of nuclear war and annihilation of the human race.

    核战争的威胁和人类的灭绝

2 VERB （在竞赛或争论中）彻底击败，大胜 If you annihilate someone in a contest or argument, you totally defeat them. （在竞赛或争论中）彻底击败，大胜

  • The Dutch annihilated the Olympic champions 5-0. [VERB noun]

    荷兰队以5比0完胜奥运冠军队。
