cut in
PHRASAL VERB 打断(谈话);插嘴 If you cut in on someone, you interrupt them when they are speaking. 打断(谈话);插嘴 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • Immediately, Daniel cut in on Joanne's attempts at reassurance. [VERB PREP. + on]

    乔安妮刚要作保证，丹尼尔就把她打断了。

  • 'Not true,' the Duchess cut in. [VERB PREP. with quote]

    “不是真的，”公爵夫人插话道。

SYN interrupt, break in, butt in, interpose
