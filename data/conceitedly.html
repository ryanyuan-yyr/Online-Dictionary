conceited
kənsiːtɪd kənˈsitɪd
ADJ-GRADED 自负的;傲慢的;自高自大的 If you say that someone is conceited, you are showing your disapproval of the fact that they are far too proud of their abilities or achievements. 自负的;傲慢的;自高自大的 [disapproval]

  • I thought him conceited and arrogant.

    我认为他既自负又傲慢。

  • You conceited idiot.

    你这个自命不凡的蠢货。

SYN self-important, vain, arrogant, stuck up [informal]
