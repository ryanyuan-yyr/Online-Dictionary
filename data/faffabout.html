faff about
(also faff around)
PHRASAL VERB 做事杂乱无章;东一榔头西一棒子;瞎忙活 If you say that someone is faffing about or faffing around, you mean that they are doing things in a disorganized way and not achieving very much. 做事杂乱无章;东一榔头西一棒子;瞎忙活 [British, informal]

  • It was annoying to watch them faffing around when a more direct response was required. [VERB PREP.]

    需要直言相答时，看他们东拉西扯，真让人恼火。
