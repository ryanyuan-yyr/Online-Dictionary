radicalize
rædɪkəlaɪz ˈrædɪkəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense radicalizes, present participle radicalizing, past tense , past participle radicalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 radicalise
VERB 使激进化；使变得激进 If something radicalizes a process, situation, or person, it makes them more radical. 使激进化；使变得激进

  • He says the opposition will radicalize its demands if these conditions aren't met. [VERB noun]

    他说如果这些条件不能得到满足，反对派的要求会变得更激进。

  • ...women radicalized by feminism. [VERB-ed]

    因女权主义而变得激进的妇女

  • The trial was a radicalizing experience for her. [VERB-ing]

    这次审讯对她来讲是一次激进化的经历。
