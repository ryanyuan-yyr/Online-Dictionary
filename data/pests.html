pest
pest pɛst
Word forms: plural pests
1 N-COUNT 害虫；害兽 Pests are insects or small animals which damage crops or food supplies. 害虫；害兽

  • ...crops which are resistant to some of the major insect pests and diseases.

    对一些主要病虫害有抵御能力的作物

  • Each year ten percent of the crop is lost to a pest called corn rootworm.

    一种叫玉米根虫的害虫每年会让粮食减产10%。

  • ...new and innovative methods of pest control.

    防治虫害的创新办法

2 N-COUNT 讨厌鬼（尤指儿童） You can describe someone, especially a child, as a pest if they keep bothering you. 讨厌鬼（尤指儿童） [informal, disapproval]

  • He climbed on the table, pulled my hair, and was generally a pest.

    他爬桌子，拽我的头发，总之是个讨厌鬼。
