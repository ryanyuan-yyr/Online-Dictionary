originate
ərɪdʒɪneɪt əˈrɪdʒɪˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense originates, present participle originating, past tense , past participle originated
V-ERG 发源；来自；产生；创始；开创 When something originates or when someone originates it, it begins to happen or exist. 发源；来自；产生；创始；开创 [formal]

  • The disease originated in Africa. [VERB prep./adv.]

    这种病源于非洲。

  • All carbohydrates originate from plants. [VERB prep./adv.]

    所有的碳水化合物均来自植物。

  • I suppose no one has any idea who originated the story? [VERB noun]

    我想没有人知道是谁编了这个故事？

SYN begin, start, emerge, come
