bulwark
bʊlwəʳk ˈbʊlwərk ˈbʌlwərk
Word forms: plural bulwarks
N-COUNT 防护；壁垒 A bulwark against something protects you against it. A bulwark of something protects it. 防护；壁垒 [Also + of]

  • The abbeys were founded in the 12th century by King David as a bulwark against the English. [+ against]

    这些修道院是12世纪大卫王用来抵御英国人而修建的堡垒。

  • The House of Lords is the only bulwark of democracy in this country.

    在这个国家，上议院是唯一的民主堡垒。
