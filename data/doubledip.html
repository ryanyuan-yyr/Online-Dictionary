double dip
(also double-dip)
N-SING [oft N n] 再度衰退In economics, a double dip is a period when an economy goes into recession, then briefly recovers, but then goes into another recession. 再度衰退

  • Dismal economic findings have analysts fearing a double dip.

    对经济低潮期调查的结果使分析家们担忧会有一个再度衰退。

  • ...a double-dip recession.

    ...一个经济的再度衰退。
