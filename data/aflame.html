aflame
əfleɪm əˈfleɪm
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 着火的；燃烧着的 If something is on fire, you can say it is aflame . 着火的；燃烧着的 [literary]

  • Hundreds of tightly rolled newspapers were set aflame among the 50,000 crowd.

    几百张卷紧的报纸在为数5万的人群中被点燃。

SYN burning, lighted, lit, flaming  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ with n] （因光或颜色）灯火辉煌的，明亮的 If you say that something is aflame, you mean it is filled with light or colour. （因光或颜色）灯火辉煌的，明亮的 [literary]

  • The shop windows were aflame with Christmas lights.

    商店橱窗装饰着圣诞节彩灯，显得灯火辉煌。

SYN red, flushed, inflamed, ruddy
