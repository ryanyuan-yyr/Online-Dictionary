abnormal
æbnɔːʳməl æbˈnɔrməl əbˈnɔrməl
ADJ-GRADED 反常的；异常的；不规则的 Someone or something that is abnormal is unusual, especially in a way that is worrying. 反常的；异常的；不规则的 [formal]

  • ...abnormal heart rhythms and high anxiety levels.

    心律不齐和高度焦虑

  • ...a child with an abnormal fear of strangers.

    过于认生的小孩

SYN unusual, different, odd, strange  
abnormally

  • ...abnormally high levels of glucose.

    异常高的葡萄糖水平

  • This stops the cells from growing abnormally.

    这阻止了细胞畸形发育。
