ladle
leɪdəl ˈleɪdəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ladles, present participle ladling, past tense , past participle ladled
1 N-COUNT 长柄勺 A ladle is a large, round, deep spoon with a long handle, used for serving soup, stew, or sauce. 长柄勺
SYN spoon, scoop, dipper  
2 VERB （尤指用长柄勺）舀，盛 If you ladle food such as soup or stew, you serve it, especially with a ladle. （尤指用长柄勺）舀，盛 [Also VERB n]

  • Barry held the bowls while Liz ladled soup into them. [VERB noun prep.]

    巴里负责端着碗，由利兹向碗里盛汤。

  • Mrs King went to the big black stove and ladled out steaming soup. [VERB noun with adv.]

    金太太走到黑色的大炉子前，舀出热气腾腾的汤来。

SYN serve, dish out, dish up, scoop out  
Phrasal verbs:
ladle out
