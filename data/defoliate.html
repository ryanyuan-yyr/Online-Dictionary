defoliate
diːfoʊlieɪt diˈfoʊliˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense defoliates, present participle defoliating, past tense , past participle defoliated
1 VERB （尤指战时）使落叶，去掉…的叶子，毁坏(某一地区)的绿色植物 To defoliate an area or the plants in it means to cause the leaves on the plants to fall off or be destroyed. This is done especially in war to remove protection from an enemy. （尤指战时）使落叶，去掉…的叶子，毁坏(某一地区)的绿色植物
