protect
prətekt prəˈtɛkt proʊˈtɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense protects, present participle protecting, past tense , past participle protected
1 VERB 保护；防护 To protect someone or something means to prevent them from being harmed or damaged. 保护；防护

  • So, what can women do to protect themselves from heart disease?. [V n + from/against]

    那么，女性怎样做才能使自己不得心脏病呢？

  • A long thin wool coat and a purple headscarf protected her against the wind. [V n from/against n]

    一件薄薄的羊毛长外套和一条紫色的头巾为她挡了风。

  • The government is committed to protecting the interests of tenants. [VERB noun]

    政府承诺保护租户们的利益。

2 VERB （保险单）为…提供保险 If an insurance policy protects you against an event such as death, injury, fire, or theft, the insurance company will give you or your family money if that event happens. （保险单）为…提供保险 [Also V + against]

  • Many manufacturers have policies to protect themselves against blackmailers. [VERB noun + against]

    许多厂家都购买了保险，以防遭到敲诈。
