reckon on
PHRASAL VERB 指望，依赖（某事的发生） If you reckon on something, you feel certain that it will happen and are therefore prepared for it. 指望，依赖（某事的发生）

  • They are typical of couples who plan a family without reckoning on the small fortune it will cost. [V-ing P]

    他们是那种典型的想要孩子但不考虑花销有多大的夫妻。

  • He reckons on being world heavyweight champion. [V P n/-ing (not pron)

    他相信自己能成为世界重量级冠军。
