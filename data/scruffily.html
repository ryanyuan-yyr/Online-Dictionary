scruffy
skrʌfi ˈskrʌfi
Word forms: comparative scruffier, superlative scruffiest
ADJ-GRADED 邋遢的;不整洁的 Someone or something that is scruffy is dirty and untidy. 邋遢的;不整洁的

  • ...a young man, pale, scruffy and unshaven.

    脸色苍白、邋邋遢遢、胡子拉碴的年轻人

  • ...a scruffy basement flat in London.

    伦敦一个脏乱的地下公寓

SYN shabby, untidy, ragged, rundown
