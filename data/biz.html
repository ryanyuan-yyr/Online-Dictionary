biz
bɪz bɪz
1 N-SING [oft n NOUN] 娱乐业（尤指流行音乐或电影业） Biz is sometimes used to refer to the entertainment business, especially pop music or films. 娱乐业（尤指流行音乐或电影业） [journalism, informal]

  • We asked women in the biz for their low-down on film-making.

    我们问了娱乐圈里的女性有关电影制作的内幕。

  • ...a girl in the music biz.

    音乐界的一个女孩
