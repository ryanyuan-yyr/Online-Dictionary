fatalism
feɪtəlɪzəm ˈfeɪtəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 宿命感;宿命论;宿命情绪 Fatalism is a feeling that you cannot control events or prevent unpleasant things from happening, especially when this feeling stops you from making decisions or making an effort. 宿命感;宿命论;宿命情绪

  • There's a certain mood of fatalism now among the radicals.

    现在激进分子中间无疑弥漫着一种宿命情绪。

SYN resignation, acceptance, passivity, determinism
