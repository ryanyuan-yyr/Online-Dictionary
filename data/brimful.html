brimful
brɪmfʊl ˈbrɪmˌfʊl
1 ADJ 充满…的 Someone who is brimful of an emotion or quality feels or seems full of it. An object or place that is brimful of something is full of it. 充满…的

    她精力充沛，热情洋溢。

    美国有很多薪酬很高的医生。
