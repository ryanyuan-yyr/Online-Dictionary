compiler
kəmpaɪləʳ kəmˈpaɪlər
Word forms: plural compilers
1 N-COUNT 汇编者;编纂者 A compiler is someone who compiles books, reports, or lists of information. 汇编者;编纂者

  • ...the compilers of dictionaries and grammars.

    词典和语法书的编纂者

2 N-COUNT 编译程序 A compiler is a computer program which converts language that people can use into a code that the computer can understand. 编译程序 [computing]
