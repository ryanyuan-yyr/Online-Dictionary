letter of credit
Word forms: plural letters of credit
1 N-COUNT （银行授权另一家银行付款的）信用证，信用状 A letter of credit is a letter written by a bank authorizing another bank to pay someone a sum of money. Letters of credit are often used by importers and exporters. （银行授权另一家银行付款的）信用证，信用状 [business]
2 N-COUNT （银行为债券担保的）信用证，信用状 A letter of credit is a written promise from a bank stating that they will repay bonds to lenders if the borrowers are unable to pay them. （银行为债券担保的）信用证，信用状 [business]

  • The project is being backed by a letter of credit from Lasalle Bank. [+ from]
