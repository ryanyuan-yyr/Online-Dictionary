detractor
dɪtræktəʳ
Word forms: plural detractors
N-COUNT [usually plural, usually with poss] 诋毁者;贬低者;诽谤者 The detractors of a person or thing are people who criticize that person or thing. 诋毁者;贬低者;诽谤者 [journalism]

  • This performance will silence many of his detractors.

    这一表演将会让很多诋毁他的人闭嘴。

  • The news will have delighted detractors of the scheme.

    这条消息会让该方案的批评者感到高兴。

SYN slanderer, belittler, disparager, defamer
