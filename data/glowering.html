glowering
glaʊərɪŋ ˈɡlaʊərɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 气愤的；脾气暴躁的 If you describe a person as glowering, you mean they look angry and bad tempered. 气愤的；脾气暴躁的 [written]

  • ...his glowering good looks.

    他那气呼呼的英俊模样

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 阴森昏暗的 If you describe a place as glowering, you mean that it looks dark and threatening. 阴森昏暗的 [written]

  • ...glowering castle walls.

    阴森昏暗的古堡墙壁
