plough back
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 把（利润）再投资（到） If profits are ploughed back into a business, they are used to increase the size of the business or to improve it. 把（利润）再投资（到） [business]

  • About 70 per cent of its profits are being ploughed back into the investment programme. [be V-ed P + into]

    它大约70%的利润又重新投回到该投资项目。
