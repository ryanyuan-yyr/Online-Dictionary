baffle
bæfəl ˈbæfəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense baffles, present participle baffling, past tense , past participle baffled
VERB 使困惑；把…难住 If something baffles you, you cannot understand it or explain it. 使困惑；把…难住

  • An apple tree producing square fruit is baffling experts. [VERB noun]

    一棵结方形苹果的树令专家大惑不解。

SYN puzzle, beat [slang] , amaze, confuse
