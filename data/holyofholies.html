holy of holies hoʊli əv hoʊliz
N-SING 最神圣的地方；常人去不了的场所 A holy of holies is a place that is so sacred that only particular people are allowed to enter; often used in informal English to refer humorously to a place where only a few special people can go. 最神圣的地方；常人去不了的场所

  • ...the holy of holies in the Temple.

    寺院里最神圣的地方

  • ...the Aldeburgh Festival, the holy of holies in the contemporary British music scene.

    奥尔德堡音乐节，当代英国乐坛最神圣的聚会
