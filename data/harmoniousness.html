harmonious
hɑːʳmoʊniəs hɑrˈmoʊniəs
1 ADJ-GRADED （关系、协定、讨论）融洽的，和睦的，友好的 A harmonious relationship, agreement, or discussion is friendly and peaceful. （关系、协定、讨论）融洽的，和睦的，友好的

  • Their harmonious relationship resulted in part from their similar goals.

    他们关系融洽的部分原因是他们有着相似的目标。

  • It is a harmonious community where pupils are happy and industrious.

    这是一个和睦的集体，学生们开心玩耍，用功读书。

harmoniously

  • To live together harmoniously as men and women is an achievement.

    夫妻能和睦相处就是一种成就。

2 ADJ-GRADED （各组成部分）和谐的，协调的 Something that is harmonious has parts which go well together and which are in proportion to each other. （各组成部分）和谐的，协调的

  • The architecture is harmonious and no building is over five or six floors high.

    这里的建筑风格和谐统一，没有哪座楼房高于五六层。

  • ...a harmonious balance of mind, body, and spirit.

    头脑、身体和心灵的协调

harmoniously

  • ...a pure, harmoniously proportioned face.

    一张五官端正的纯真脸庞

  • ...stone paths that blend harmoniously with the scenery.

    与景色和谐融为一体的石子路

3 ADJ-GRADED （音调）和谐的，悦耳的 Musical notes that are harmonious produce a pleasant sound when played together. （音调）和谐的，悦耳的

  • ...the mysterious skill involved in producing harmonious sounds.

    发出悦耳声音的神秘技巧
