buddy
bʌdi ˈbʌdi
Word forms: plural buddies
1 N-COUNT 朋友，同伴（通常指男子的男性伙伴） A buddy is a close friend, usually a male friend of a man. 朋友，同伴（通常指男子的男性伙伴） [mainly US]

  • We became great buddies.

    我们成了非常好的朋友。

SYN friend, mate [informal] , pal, companion  
2 N-VOC 老兄；老弟 Men sometimes address other men as buddy . 老兄；老弟 [US, informal]

  • Hey, no way, buddy.

    嘿，没门儿，老兄。

3 PHRASE [verb-link PHRASE] 跟…关系要好 If one person is buddy buddy with another, they are very close friends. 跟…关系要好 [US, informal]
