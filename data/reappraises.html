reappraise
riːəpreɪz ˌriːəˈpreɪz ˌriəˈpreɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reappraises, present participle reappraising, past tense , past participle reappraised
VERB 重新估计；重新评价 If you reappraise something such as an idea or a plan, you think carefully about it and decide whether it needs to be changed. 重新估计；重新评价 [formal]

  • It did not persuade them to abandon the war but it did force them to reappraise their strategy. [VERB noun]

    那并没有说服他们放弃战争，但的确迫使他们重新评估了自己的战略。
