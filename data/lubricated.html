lubricate
luːbrɪkeɪt ˈlubrɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lubricates, present participle lubricating, past tense , past participle lubricated
1 VERB 润滑；加润滑油于 If you lubricate something such as a part of a machine, you put a substance such as oil on it so that it moves smoothly. 润滑；加润滑油于 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • Mineral oils are used to lubricate machinery. [VERB noun]

    矿物油被用来润滑机器。

  • ...lubricating oil. [VERB-ing]

    润滑油

SYN oil, grease, smear, smooth the way  
lubrication

  • Use a touch of linseed oil for lubrication.

    用一点亚麻籽油来润滑一下。

2 VERB 促进；使顺畅 If you say that something lubricates a particular situation, you mean that it helps things to happen without any problems. 促进；使顺畅

  • Franklin's task was to lubricate the discussions with the French. [VERB noun]

    富兰克林的任务是促使与法国方面的会谈顺畅进行。
