unconnected
ʌnkənektɪd ˌʌnkəˈnɛktɪd ˌʌnkəˈnɛktɪd
ADJ-GRADED 无关联的；不相关的 If one thing is unconnected with another or the two things are unconnected, the things are not related to each other in any way. 无关联的；不相关的 [Also + to]

  • She was known to have had personal problems unconnected with her marriage. [+ with]

    据悉，她有过一些与婚姻不相干的个人问题。

  • I can't believe that those two murders are unconnected.

    我不相信那两桩谋杀案之间毫无关联。

SYN separate, independent, unrelated, divided
