stagnate
stægneɪt stægneɪt ˈstægˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stagnates, present participle stagnating, past tense , past participle stagnated
VERB 停滞不前;不发展;无变化 If something such as a business or society stagnates, it stops changing or progressing. 停滞不前;不发展;无变化 [disapproval]

  • Industrial production is stagnating. [VERB]

    工业生产正停滞不前。

  • His career had stagnated. [VERB]

    他的事业已经陷入停滞。

SYN vegetate, decline, deteriorate, rot  
stagnation

  • ...the stagnation of the steel industry.

    钢铁工业死气沉沉
