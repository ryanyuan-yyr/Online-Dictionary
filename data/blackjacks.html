blackjack
blækdʒæk ˈblækˌdʒæk ˈblækˌdʒæk
Word forms: blackjacks
1 N-UNCOUNT 点扑克游戏Blackjack is a card game in which players try to obtain a combination of cards worth 21 points. 21 点扑克游戏

  • Vicky has lost five hundred dollars playing blackjack.

    维琪玩21点扑克游戏输了500美元。

2 N-COUNT (用作武器的短粗)棍棒A blackjack is a short, thick stick that is used as a weapon. (用作武器的短粗)棍棒 [US, informal]

  • Police confiscated guns, knives and blackjacks.

    警察没收了各种枪支、刀具和棍棒。

3 N 黑闪锌矿a dark iron-rich variety of the mineral sphalerite 黑闪锌矿
4 N 栎树 (Also called blackjack oak)a small oak tree, Quercus marilandica, of the southeastern US, with blackish bark and fan-shaped leaves 栎树 (Also called blackjack oak)
5 N 外涂柏油的革制盛酒容器或者罐子a tarred leather tankard or jug 外涂柏油的革制盛酒容器或者罐子
