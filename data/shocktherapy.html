shock therapy
1 N-UNCOUNT (为迅速解决某问题采取的)应急行动，极端政策 You can refer to the use of extreme policies or actions to solve a particular problem quickly as shock therapy . (为迅速解决某问题采取的)应急行动，极端政策

  • ...Prague's policy of economic shock therapy.

    布拉格的应急经济政策

2 N-UNCOUNT (通过电击来治疗精神病患者的)休克疗法，电击治疗 Shock therapy is a way of treating mentally ill patients by passing an electric current through their brain. (通过电击来治疗精神病患者的)休克疗法，电击治疗

  • ...the electrodes used for shock therapy.

    用于休克疗法的电极
