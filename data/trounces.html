trounce
traʊns traʊns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense trounces, present participle trouncing, past tense , past participle trounced
VERB 轻松战胜;大胜;击溃 If you trounce someone in a competition or contest, you defeat them easily or by a large score. 轻松战胜;大胜;击溃 [informal]

  • In Rugby League, Australia trounced France by sixty points to four. [VERB noun]

    在联盟橄榄球赛上，澳大利亚队以 60:4 的比分大胜法国队。

SYN defeat someone heavily or utterly, beat, thrash, slaughter [informal]
