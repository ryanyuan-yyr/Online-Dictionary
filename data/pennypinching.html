penny-pinching
1 N-UNCOUNT 小气；吝啬 Penny-pinching is the practice of trying to spend as little money as possible. 小气；吝啬 [disapproval]

  • Government penny-pinching is blamed for the decline in food standards.

    政府吝啬被指责是食品标准下降的原因。

SYN meanness, selfishness, parsimony [formal] , stinginess  
2 ADJ 小气的；吝啬的 Penny-pinching people spend as little money as possible. 小气的；吝啬的 [disapproval]

  • ...small-minded penny-pinching administrators.

    心胸狭隘、一毛不拔的管理者

SYN mean, close, near [informal] , frugal
