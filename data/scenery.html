scenery
siːnəri ˈsinəri
1 N-UNCOUNT 风景;景色;风光 The scenery in a country area is the land, water, or plants that you can see around you. →see usage note at: countryside 风景;景色;风光

  • ...the island's spectacular scenery.

    岛上壮丽的风景

  • Sometimes they just drive slowly down the lane enjoying the scenery.

    有时他们只是沿着小路慢慢地开车，欣赏两旁的风景。

2 N-UNCOUNT (舞台)布景 In a theatre, the scenery consists of the structures and painted backgrounds that show where the action in the play takes place. (舞台)布景
3 PHRASE 换个环境 If you have a change of scenery, you go somewhere different after being in a particular place for a long time. 换个环境

  • A change of scenery might do you the power of good.

    换个地方或许对你大有裨益。
