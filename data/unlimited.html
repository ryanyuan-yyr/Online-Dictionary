unlimited
ʌnlɪmɪtɪd ʌnˈlɪmɪtɪd
ADJ 无限的；（数量）极大的 If there is an unlimited quantity of something, you can have as much or as many of that thing as you want. 无限的；（数量）极大的

  • An unlimited number of copies can still be made from the original.

    仍可以由原件复印出无数份副本来。

  • You'll also have unlimited access to the swimming pool.

    进泳池的次数是没有限制的。

  • Career prospects are virtually unlimited.

    职业生涯几乎是前途无量。

SYN total, full, complete, absolute
