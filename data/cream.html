cream
kriːm krim
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense creams, present participle creaming, past tense , past participle creamed
1 N-UNCOUNT (用于烹饪或放于水果或甜点上的)奶油，乳脂 Cream is a thick yellowish-white liquid taken from milk. You can use it in cooking or put it on fruit or desserts. (用于烹饪或放于水果或甜点上的)奶油，乳脂

  • ...strawberries and cream.

    奶油草莓

2See also:clotted cream  double cream  single cream  sour cream  whipping cream 
3 N-UNCOUNT (用于含奶油或牛奶的汤的名称中) Cream is used in the names of soups that contain cream or milk. (用于含奶油或牛奶的汤的名称中)

  • ...cream of mushroom soup. [+ of]

    奶油蘑菇汤

4 N-VAR 护肤霜;润肤霜;药膏 A cream is a substance that you rub into your skin, for example to keep it soft or to heal or protect it. 护肤霜;润肤霜;药膏

  • Gently apply the cream to the affected areas.

    把药膏轻轻涂抹在患处。

  • ...sun protection creams.

    防晒霜

SYN lotion, ointment, oil, essence  
5See also:face cream 
6 COLOUR 奶油色;淡黄色;米色 Something that is cream is yellowish-white in colour. 奶油色;淡黄色;米色

  • ...cream silk stockings.

    米色丝袜

  • ...a cream-coloured Persian cat.

    米色的波斯猫

7 N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 精英;精华 Cream is used in expressions such as the cream of society and the cream of British athletes to refer to the best people or things of a particular kind. 精英;精华

  • The Ball was attended by the cream of Hollywood society. [+ of]

    好莱坞的大牌影星们出席了那个舞会。

  • ...the cream of Chicago's 200 jazz and blues clubs.

    芝加哥200家爵士乐和蓝调乐俱乐部中最优秀的成员

You can refer to the best people or things of a particular kind as the cream of the crop .精华;精英
