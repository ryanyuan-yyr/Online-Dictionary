number cruncher ˈkrʌntʃər
Word forms: plural number crunchers
N-COUNT [usually plural] 捣鼓数字的人;和数字打交道的人 If you refer to number crunchers, you mean people whose jobs involve dealing with numbers or mathematical calculations, for example in finance or statistics. 捣鼓数字的人;和数字打交道的人 [informal]
