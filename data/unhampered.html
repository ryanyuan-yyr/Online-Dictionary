unhampered
ʌnhæmpəʳd ʌnˈhæmpəd
ADJ-GRADED [usu ADJ after v] 无阻碍的；随心所欲的 If you are unhampered by a problem or obstacle, you are free from it, and so you are able to do what you want to. 无阻碍的；随心所欲的 [written]
