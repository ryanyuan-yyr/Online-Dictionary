poke around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 poke about
PHRASAL VERB （通常指挪动很多物品）寻找，翻找 If you poke around or poke about for something, you search for it, usually by moving lots of objects around. （通常指挪动很多物品）寻找，翻找 [informal]

  • He poked around the top of his cupboard for the bottle of whisky. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他翻遍了食橱的上层，想找那瓶威士忌。

  • We opened up the car bonnet and he started poking around in my engine. [VERB PREP.]

    我们打开了车子的引擎盖，他开始仔细查看发动机。
