watercolour
wɔːtəʳkʌləʳ
Word forms: plural watercolours
in AM, use 美国英语用 watercolor
1 N-VAR 水彩 Watercolours are coloured paints, used for painting pictures, which you apply with a wet brush or dissolve in water first. 水彩

  • ...a collection of rich paintings in watercolour, acrylic and oil.

    一批色彩饱满生动的水彩画、丙烯画和油画藏品

2 N-COUNT 水彩画 A watercolour is a picture which has been painted with watercolours. 水彩画

  • ...a lovely watercolour by J. M. W. Turner.

    一幅由J.M.W.特纳创作的漂亮的水彩画
