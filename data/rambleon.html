ramble on
PHRASAL VERB 漫无边际地闲扯;喋喋不休地说 If you say that someone is rambling on, you mean that they have been talking for a long time in a boring and rather confused way. 漫无边际地闲扯;喋喋不休地说

  • She only half-listened as Ella rambled on. [VERB PREP.]

    埃拉漫无边际地闲扯，她心不在焉地听着。

  • He stood in my kitchen drinking beer, rambling on about Lillian. [V P + about]

    他站在我的厨房里喝着啤酒，漫无边际地讲着有关莉莲的事情。
