catty
kæti ˈkæti
Word forms: comparative cattier, superlative cattiest
ADJ-GRADED (尤指女子)刁钻刻薄的，搬弄是非的 If someone, especially a woman or girl, is being catty, they are being unpleasant and unkind. (尤指女子)刁钻刻薄的，搬弄是非的 [informal]

  • His mother was catty, status-conscious and loud.

    她母亲为人刻薄，势利眼，嗓门又大。

  • ...catty remarks.

    尖刻的言辞

SYN spiteful, mean [informal] , malicious, malevolent
