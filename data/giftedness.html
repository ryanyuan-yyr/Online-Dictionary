gifted gɪftɪd ˈgɪftɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 有天赋的；有天才的；有天资的 Someone who is gifted has a natural ability to do something well. 有天赋的；有天才的；有天资的

  • ...one of the most gifted players in the world.

    世界上最有天赋的运动员之一

  • He was witty, amusing and gifted with a sharp business brain.

    他机智诙谐，具有敏锐的商业头脑。

SYN talented, able, skilled, expert  
2 ADJ-GRADED （儿童）天资聪颖的 A gifted child is much more intelligent or talented than average. （儿童）天资聪颖的

  • ...a state program for gifted children.

    国家培养天才儿童的方案
