punch-drunk
ˈpʌntʃˌdrʌŋk
(also punch drunk)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 被击晕的；晕头转向的 A punch-drunk boxer shows signs of brain damage, for example by being unsteady and unable to think clearly, after being hit too often on the head. 被击晕的；晕头转向的
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 精疲力竭的；头昏脑涨的 If you say that someone is punch-drunk, you mean that they are very tired or confused, for example because they have been working too hard. 精疲力竭的；头昏脑涨的

  • He was punch-drunk with fatigue and depressed by the rain.

    他疲惫不堪，头昏沉沉的，因为下雨情绪也很低落。

SYN groggy, confused, dazed [informal] , in a daze
