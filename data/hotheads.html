hothead
hɒthed ˈhɑtˌhɛd
Word forms: plural hotheads
N-COUNT 头脑容易发热的人;鲁莽的人;急性子 If you refer to someone as a hothead, you are criticizing them for acting too quickly, without thinking of the consequences. 头脑容易发热的人;鲁莽的人;急性子 [disapproval]

  • He is a hothead and a bully just like his dad.

    他和他爸爸一样都是个恃强凌弱的莽夫。

SYN madcap, hotspur, tearaway [British , informal] , daredevil
