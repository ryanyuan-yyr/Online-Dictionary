cuckold

  • Arthur himself has been portrayed as a weak cuckold.

    亚瑟王本人被描绘成一个被人戴绿帽子的软弱之人。

2 VERB 给…戴绿帽子;使…当王八 If a married woman is having an affair, she and her lover are cuckolding her husband. 给…戴绿帽子;使…当王八 [literary]

    妻子给他戴绿帽子了。
