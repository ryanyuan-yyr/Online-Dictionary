horse around
PHRASAL VERB 胡闹;哄闹 If you horse around, you play roughly and rather carelessly, so that you could hurt someone or damage something. 胡闹;哄闹 [informal]

  • Later that day I was horsing around with Katie when she accidentally stuck her finger in my eye. [VERB PREP.]

    那天晚些时候，我正和凯蒂一起胡闹时，她不小心用手指戳到了我的一只眼睛。
