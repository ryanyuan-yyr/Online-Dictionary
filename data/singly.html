singly
sɪŋgli ˈsɪŋgli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 单个地;单独地;一个接一个地 If people do something singly, they each do it on their own, or do it one by one. 单个地;单独地;一个接一个地

  • They marched out singly or in pairs.

    他们一个一个地或成双成对地走出来。

  • Patients went singly into the consulting room.

    病人一个接一个地进入诊疗室。

SYN one by one, individually, one at a time, separately
