shrug
ʃrʌg ʃrʌg
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shrugs, present participle shrugging, past tense , past participle shrugged
VERB 耸肩(表示不感兴趣、不知道或不在乎) If you shrug, you raise your shoulders to show that you are not interested in something or that you do not know or care about something. 耸肩(表示不感兴趣、不知道或不在乎)

  • I shrugged, as if to say, 'Why not?'. [VERB]

    我耸了耸肩，仿佛在说，“为什么不?”

  • The man shrugged his shoulders. [VERB noun]

    那男子耸了耸肩。

Shrug is also a noun.

  • 'I suppose so,' said Anna with a shrug.

    “我想是吧，”安娜耸了耸肩说道。

Phrasal verbs:
shrug off
