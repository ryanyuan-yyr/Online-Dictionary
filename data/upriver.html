up-river
(also upriver)
ADV [ADVERB after verb, be ADVERB] 往上游；向上游；在上游 Something that is moving up-river is moving towards the source of a river, from a point down the river. Something that is up-river is towards the source of a river. 往上游；向上游；在上游

  • Heavy goods could be brought up-river in barges.

    较重的货物可用驳船运到上游。

  • He has a house down there but it's miles up river.

    他在那里有一栋房子，但在河上游几英里处。

  • The vineyards of Anjou extend from west of Angers to up-river of Saumur. [+ of]

    安茹葡萄园从昂热西部一直延伸到索米尔的上游。

  • ...La Reole, up-river from St-Macaire. [+ from]

    拉雷奥勒，从圣马凯尔往上游走

Up-river is also an adjective.

  • ...an upriver trip in Central Africa.

    在中部非洲的一次溯流而上的旅行
