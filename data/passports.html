passport
pɑːspɔːʳt pæs- ˈpæsˌpɔrt
Word forms: plural passports
1 N-COUNT 护照 Your passport is an official document containing your name, photograph, and personal details, which you need to show when you enter or leave a country. 护照

  • You should take your passport with you when changing money.

    兑换货币时应该带着护照。

  • ...a South African businessman travelling on a British passport.

    持英国护照旅行的南非商人

SYN travel document, papers, visa, identity card  
2 N-COUNT 进身之阶；（获取成功或幸福的）途径，手段 If you say that a thing is a passport to success or happiness, you mean that this thing makes success or happiness possible. 进身之阶；（获取成功或幸福的）途径，手段

  • Victory would give him a passport to the riches he craves. [+ to]

    胜利将使他有机会获得自己梦寐以求的财富。

  • If the interview goes well it could be the passport to an exciting new career.

    面试顺利的话，将获得令人兴奋的新工作。
