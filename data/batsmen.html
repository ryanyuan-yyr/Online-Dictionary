batsman
bætsmən ˈbætsmən
Word forms: plural batsmen
N-COUNT （板球运动中的）击球手 The batsman in a game of cricket is the player who is batting. （板球运动中的）击球手

  • The batsman rose on his toes and played the rising ball down into the ground.

    击球手踮起脚将这记上旋球击到场地中。

  • He was the greatest batsman of his generation.

    他是他们那一代最出色的击球手。
