voluptuous
vəlʌptʃuəs vəˈlʌptʃuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 体态丰满的;性感的 If you describe a woman as voluptuous, you mean that she has large breasts and hips and is considered attractive in a sexual way. 体态丰满的;性感的

  • ...a voluptuous, well-rounded lady with glossy black hair.

    头发乌黑亮丽、身材丰满性感的女士

SYN buxom, shapely, curvaceous [informal] , erotic  
2 ADJ-GRADED 给人以感官享受的;满足感官的 Something that is voluptuous gives you a great deal of pleasure from the rich way it is experienced through your senses. 给人以感官享受的;满足感官的

  • 'Opium' is a provocative, sensual, and voluptuous fragrance which makes all your senses vibrate.

    “鸦片”是一种刺激性的、能引起感官快感的香料，能使你所有的感官兴奋起来。
