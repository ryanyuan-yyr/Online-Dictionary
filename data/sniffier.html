sniffy
snɪfi ˈsnɪfi
Word forms: comparative sniffier, superlative sniffiest
ADJ-GRADED 轻蔑的;鄙夷的 Someone who is sniffy about something does not think it is of high quality, perhaps unfairly. 轻蔑的;鄙夷的 [informal]

  • Some people are a bit sniffy about television. [+ about]

    某些人对电视有点嗤之以鼻。

  • ...sniffy art critics.

    自命不凡的艺术评论家

SYN contemptuous, superior, condescending, haughty  
sniffily

  • The broadcast media sniffily affects to distance itself from the press.

    广播媒体态度轻蔑地故作姿态，要跟新闻媒体保持距离。
