intermarry
ɪntəʳmæri ˌɪntəˈmærɪ ˌɪntərˈmæri
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense intermarries, present participle intermarrying, past tense , past participle intermarried
V-RECIP (不同社会、种族、宗教群体间)通婚 When people from different social, racial, or religious groups intermarry, they marry each other. You can also say that one group intermarries with another group. (不同社会、种族、宗教群体间)通婚

  • They were allowed to intermarry. [VERB]

    他们被获许通婚。

  • Some of the traders settled and intermarried with local women. [VERB + with]

    一些商人定居下来，并与当地妇女通婚。
