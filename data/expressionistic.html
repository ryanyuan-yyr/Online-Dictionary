expressionist
ɪkspreʃənɪst
Word forms: plural expressionists
1 N-COUNT 表现主义者；表现派 An expressionist is an artist, writer, or composer who uses the style of expressionism. 表现主义者；表现派
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 表现主义的;表现派的 Expressionist artists, writers, composers, or works use the style of expressionism. 表现主义的;表现派的

  • ...an extraordinary collection of expressionist paintings.

    一批非凡的表现主义画作藏品
