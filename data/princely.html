princely
prɪnsli ˈprɪnsli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （金钱）大量的，巨额的 A princely sum of money is a large sum of money. （金钱）大量的，巨额的

  • It'll cost them the princely sum of seventy-five pounds.

    这将花费他们75英镑巨款。

SYN substantial, considerable, goodly, large  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 王子的；亲王的；适合王公贵族的 Princely means belonging to a prince or suitable for a prince. 王子的；亲王的；适合王公贵族的

  • It was the embodiment of princely magnificence.

    这体现了和亲王相称的富丽堂皇。

SYN regal, royal, imposing, magnificent
