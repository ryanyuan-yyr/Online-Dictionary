raptures
ræptʃəʳz ˈræptʃəz
PHRASE 欣喜若狂;狂喜 If you are in raptures or go into raptures about something, you are extremely impressed by it and enthusiastic about it. 欣喜若狂;狂喜 [mainly British, written]

  • They will be in raptures over the French countryside.

    他们会深深爱上法国的乡村。

  • Davies goes into raptures over the brilliantly coloured paintwork.

    戴维斯深深喜欢上了那幅色彩绚丽的油画作品。

  • His goal sent the crowd into raptures.

    他的进球使观众们欣喜若狂。
