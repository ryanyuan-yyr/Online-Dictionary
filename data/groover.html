groove
gruːv gruv
Word forms: plural grooves
1 N-COUNT 沟；槽；辙 A groove is a deep line cut into a surface. 沟；槽；辙

  • Their wheels left grooves in the ground.

    他们的车轮在地面上留下了车辙印。

2 N-COUNT [usually supplement NOUN] （流行音乐的）节奏 In popular music, a groove is a rhythm. （流行音乐的）节奏 [informal]

  • ...Latin and African grooves.

    拉丁乐和非洲音乐的节奏
