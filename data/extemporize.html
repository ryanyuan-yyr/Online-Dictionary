extemporize
ɪkstempəraɪz ɛksˈtɛmpəˌraɪz ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense extemporizes, present participle extemporizing, past tense , past participle extemporized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 extemporise
VERB 即席演说;即兴表演 If you extemporize, you speak, act, or perform something immediately, without rehearsing or preparing it beforehand. 即席演说;即兴表演 [formal]

  • He completely departed from the text and extemporized in a very energetic fashion. [VERB]

    他完全脱稿，慷慨激昂地进行即席演说。
