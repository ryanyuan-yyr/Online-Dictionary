futile
fjuːtaɪl ˈfjuˌtaɪl -təl ˈfjutəl
ADJ-GRADED 无益的;无用的;徒劳的 If you say that something is futile, you mean there is no point in doing it, usually because it has no chance of succeeding. 无益的;无用的;徒劳的

  • He brought his arm up in a futile attempt to ward off the blow.

    他扬起胳膊徒劳地想要挡住这一拳。

  • It would be futile to sustain his life when there is no chance of any improvement.

    如果没有任何好转的机会，继续维持他的生命也是无益的。

SYN useless, vain, unsuccessful, pointless
