soundproof
saʊndpruːf ˈsaʊndˌpruf
(also sound-proof)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense soundproofs, present participle soundproofing, past tense , past participle soundproofed
1 ADJ 隔音的 A soundproof room, door, or window is designed to prevent all sound from getting in or out. 隔音的

  • The studio isn't soundproof.

    这个摄影棚并不隔音。

2 VERB 使隔音 If you soundproof a room, you line it with special materials to stop all sound from getting in or out. 使隔音

  • We've soundproofed our home studio. [VERB noun]

    我们已将家里的录音室作了隔音处理。

  • The dog was placed in a soundproofed room. [VERB-ed]

    狗被安置在一个隔音的房间里。

soundproofing

  • We did make a mistake in not having enough soundproofing upstairs.

    我们犯了一个错误—没有对楼上作足够的隔音处理。
