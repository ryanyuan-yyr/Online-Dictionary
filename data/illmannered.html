ill-mannered
ˈɪlˈmænərd
ADJ-GRADED 无礼的;粗鲁的 If you describe someone as ill-mannered, you are critical of them because they are impolite or rude. 无礼的;粗鲁的 [formal, disapproval]

  • ...this rude, ill-mannered individual who assumes he has the force of the law behind him.

    这个粗鲁无礼、态度恶劣的人自以为他拥有法律的支持

  • Chantal would have considered it ill-mannered to show surprise.

    钱特尔会认为表现出吃惊是失态的。

SYN rude, impolite, discourteous, coarse
