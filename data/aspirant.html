aspirant
əspaɪrənt æspɪrənt ˈæspərənt əˈspaɪrənt
Word forms: plural aspirants
1 N-COUNT 有抱负者；（政治权力或重要职位的）追求者；雄心勃勃的人 Someone who is an aspirant to political power or to an important job has a strong desire to achieve it. 有抱负者；（政治权力或重要职位的）追求者；雄心勃勃的人 [formal]

  • ...the young aspirant to power.

    追逐权势的年轻人

  • Any aspirant to the presidency here must be seriously rich.

    要想当这儿的主席一定要家财万贯。

  • He is among the few aspirants with administrative experience.

    他是为数不多的几个志向远大而且有管理经验的人之一。

SYN candidate, applicant, hopeful, aspirer  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 同aspiring Aspirant means the same as aspiring . 同aspiring [formal]

  • ...aspirant politicians.

    野心勃勃的政客们

SYN hopeful, longing, ambitious, eager
