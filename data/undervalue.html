undervalue
ʌndəʳvæljuː ˌʌndəˈvæljuː ˌʌndərˈvælju
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense undervalues, present participle undervaluing, past tense , past participle undervalued
VERB 低估；轻视；小看 If you undervalue something or someone, you fail to recognize how valuable or important they are. 低估；轻视；小看

  • We must never undervalue freedom. [VERB noun]

    我们决不能低估自由的价值。

  • Many companies deal with their female employees in a way that undervalues them. [VERB noun]

    许多公司轻视女员工。

SYN underrate, underestimate, minimize, look down on  
undervalued

  • ...greatly undervalued German wines.

    估价过低的德国葡萄酒

  • Even the best teacher can feel undervalued.

    即使是最好的老师也会感觉受了轻视。
