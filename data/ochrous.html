ochre
oʊkəʳ ˈoʊkər ˈəʊkə
(also ocher)
1 COLOUR 赭色；淡橘黄色 Something that is ochre is a yellowish orange colour. 赭色；淡橘黄色

  • For our dining room I have chosen ochre yellow walls.

    我决定把我们餐厅的墙壁刷成赭黄色。

2 N-UNCOUNT 赭石（用于制作染料或颜料，通常为红色或黄色） Ochre is coloured earth, usually red or yellow, that is used to make dyes and paints. 赭石（用于制作染料或颜料，通常为红色或黄色）
