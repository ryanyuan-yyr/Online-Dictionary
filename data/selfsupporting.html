self-supporting
ADJ 资金自给的;经济独立的 Self-supporting is used to describe organizations, schemes, and people who earn enough money to not need financial help from anyone else. 资金自给的;经济独立的

  • The income from visitors makes the museum self-supporting.

    参观者带来的收入使得这个博物馆能够独立运营。

  • The parents were determined that the two girls should be fully self-supporting.

    这对父母下定决心要让两个女儿彻底自立。

SYN independent, self-contained, self-sufficient, self-reliant
