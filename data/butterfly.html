butterfly
bʌtəʳflaɪ ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ
Word forms: plural butterflies
1 N-COUNT 蝴蝶 A butterfly is an insect with large colourful wings and a thin body. 蝴蝶
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft the NOUN] 蝶泳 Butterfly is a swimming stroke which you do lying on your front, kicking your legs and bringing your arms over your head together. 蝶泳
3 PHRASE 情绪紧张；心慌意乱；忐忑不安 If you have butterflies in your stomach or have butterflies, you are very nervous or excited about something. 情绪紧张；心慌意乱；忐忑不安 [informal]

  • An exam, or even an exciting social event may produce butterflies in the stomach.

    一场考试，甚至是一次令人兴奋的社会活动，都有可能让人心里发慌。
