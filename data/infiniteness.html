infinite
ɪnfɪnɪt ˈɪnfənɪt
1 ADJ 极多的;极度的 If you describe something as infinite, you are emphasizing that it is extremely great in amount or degree. 极多的;极度的 [emphasis]

  • ...an infinite variety of landscapes.

    变化万千的风景

  • With infinite care, John shifted position.

    约翰小心翼翼地挪动了位置。

  • The choice is infinite.

    选择是极其多的。

SYN vast, enormous, immense, wide  
infinitely

  • His design was infinitely better than anything I could have done.

    他的设计比我所能设计出的不知要好上多少倍。

2 ADJ 无限的;无穷的;无边的 Something that is infinite has no limit, end, or edge. 无限的;无穷的;无边的

  • ...an infinite number of atoms.

    无穷多的原子

  • Obviously, no company has infinite resources.

    显然,没有哪个公司的资源是无限的。

  • ...God's infinite mercy.

    上帝无限的仁慈

SYN limitless, endless, unlimited, eternal  
The infinite is something which is infinite.无限(或无穷)的事物

  • ...pondering on the infinite.

    对无极的思考

infinitely

  • A centimeter can be infinitely divided into smaller units.

    一厘米可以无穷尽地分成更小的单位。
