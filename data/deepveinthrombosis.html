deep vein thrombosis diːp veɪn θrɒmˈbəʊsɪs
Word forms: plural deep vein thromboses
N-VAR 深静脉血栓（缩略形式为DVT） Deep vein thrombosis is a serious medical condition caused by blood clots in the legs moving up to the lungs. The abbreviation DVT is also used. 深静脉血栓（缩略形式为DVT） [medicine]

  • He could have died after developing deep vein thrombosis during a flight to Sydney.

    他在一次飞往悉尼的航行中得了深静脉血栓，险些丧命。
