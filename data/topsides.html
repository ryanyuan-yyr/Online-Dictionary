topside
tɒpsaɪd ˈtɑpˌsaɪd
Word forms: plural topsides
1 N-UNCOUNT （牛的）大腿肉 Topside is a joint of beef that is cut from the upper part of the cow's leg. It is usually cooked by roasting or stewing. （牛的）大腿肉 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 top round
2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 到(或向、在)甲板上 On a ship, if you go topside, you go up onto the top deck. 到(或向、在)甲板上 [technical]

  • He left the control station and went topside.

    他离开控制台，走到甲板上。

3 N-COUNT [usually plural] (船体的)主甲板以上部分，水线以上部分 The topside or topsides of a ship or boat are the top deck or the parts which you can see above the water. (船体的)主甲板以上部分，水线以上部分 [technical]
