sparring partner
ˈspɑːrɪŋ
Word forms: plural sparring partners
1 N-COUNT (拳击手的)练习对手,陪练 A boxer's sparring partner is another boxer who he or she fights regularly in training. (拳击手的)练习对手,陪练
2 N-COUNT 进行友好争论(或辩论)的对手 Your sparring partner is a person with whom you regularly have friendly arguments. 进行友好争论(或辩论)的对手
