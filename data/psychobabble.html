psychobabble
saɪkoʊbæbəl ˈsaɪkoʊˌbæbəl
N-UNCOUNT 心理学呓语（在谈论情感或行为时用词深奥但空洞的语言） If you refer to language about people's feelings or behaviour as psychobabble, you mean that it is very complicated and perhaps meaningless. 心理学呓语（在谈论情感或行为时用词深奥但空洞的语言） [disapproval]

  • Beneath the sentimental psychobabble, there's a likeable movie trying to get out.

    在多愁善感的心理学呓语之下，一部本有可能受到喜爱的电影挣扎欲出。
