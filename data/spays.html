spay
speɪ speɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense spays, present participle spaying, past tense , past participle spayed
VERB [usually passive] 切除(雌畜)的卵巢 When a female animal is spayed, it has its ovaries removed so that it cannot become pregnant. 切除(雌畜)的卵巢

  • All bitches should be spayed unless being used for breeding. [be VERB-ed]

    除非是用作种犬，所有的母犬都应摘除卵巢。
