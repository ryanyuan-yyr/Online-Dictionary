pathos
peɪθɒs ˈpeɪˌθɑs ˈpeɪˌθɔs
N-UNCOUNT （情形、电影、戏剧等中的）引人怜悯的特质，感伤力 Pathos is a quality in a situation, film, or play that makes people feel sadness and pity. （情形、电影、戏剧等中的）引人怜悯的特质，感伤力

  • ...the pathos of man's isolation. [+ of]

    人类所处的孤立状态令人感伤

  • With touching pathos he described the pangs of hunger.

    他以极具感伤力的笔触描述了饥饿的痛苦。

SYN sadness, poignancy, plaintiveness, pitifulness
