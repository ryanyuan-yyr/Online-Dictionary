rotten apple
ˈrɒtən ˈæpəl
Word forms: plural rotten apples
N-COUNT 害群之马 You can use rotten apple to talk about a person who is dishonest and therefore causes a lot of problems for the group or organization they belong to. 害群之马

  • Police corruption is not just a few rotten apples.

    警方的腐败不仅仅是几个害群之马造成的。
