robotic
roʊbɒtɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 自动操作的;机械的 Robotic equipment can perform certain tasks automatically. 自动操作的;机械的

  • Astronaut Pierre Thuot tried to latch the 15-foot robotic arm onto the satellite.

    宇航员皮埃尔·索特想要把这个长15英尺的机械手安到人造卫星上。

2 ADJ-GRADED 像机器人的;机械呆板的 If you describe someone as robotic, you mean that they speak or move in a stiff and mechanical way like a robot. 像机器人的;机械呆板的 [disapproval]

  • There is something a little robotic about him.

    他有点呆板。
