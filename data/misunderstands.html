misunderstand
mɪsʌndəʳstænd ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misunderstands, present participle misunderstanding, past tense , past participle misunderstood
1 VERB 误解;误会 If you misunderstand someone or something, you do not understand them properly. 误解;误会

  • They have simply misunderstood what rock and roll is. [VERB wh]

    他们根本没有理解什么是摇滚乐。

  • Maybe I misunderstood you. [VERB noun]

    也许我误会你了。

  • I'm not saying what he did was good, don't misunderstand me.

    别误会，我并不是说他所做的是好事。
