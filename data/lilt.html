lilt
lɪlt lɪlt
N-SING （说话声调的）欢快，抑扬顿挫 If someone's voice has a lilt in it, the pitch of their voice rises and falls in a pleasant way, as if they were singing. （说话声调的）欢快，抑扬顿挫

  • Her voice is childlike, with a West Country lilt.

    她的声音如孩童般，带有英格兰西南部诸郡的那种抑扬顿挫。

SYN rhythm, intonation, cadence, beat
