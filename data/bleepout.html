bleep out
PHRASAL VERB （电视或广播节目中）用哔哔声盖过（粗话） In a television or radio programme, when someone bleeps out an offensive word, they use an electronic device to make the sound of a bleep so that people cannot hear the word. （电视或广播节目中）用哔哔声盖过（粗话）

  • Quick-thinking TV bosses bleeped out the abuse. [VERB PREP. noun]

    反应敏捷的电台台长们用哔哔声盖过了辱骂声。

  • I hope Channel 4 doesn't bleep it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我希望第4频道没有把这段话盖掉。
