catatonic
kætətɒnɪk
ADJ-GRADED 毫无知觉的；呆若木鸡的；患紧张症的 If you describe someone as being in a catatonic state, you mean that they are not moving or responding at all, usually as a result of illness, shock, or drugs. 毫无知觉的；呆若木鸡的；患紧张症的 [medicine, literary]

  • ...and the traumatised heroine sinks into a catatonic trance.

    而且身受重伤的女主人公陷入了毫无知觉的昏迷状态。
