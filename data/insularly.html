insular
ɪnsjʊləʳ ˈɪnsjʊlə -sə- ˈɪnsələr ˈɪnsʊlər ˈɪnsjʊlər
1 ADJ-GRADED 孤立的;保守的;思想狭隘的 If you say that someone is insular, you are being critical of them because they are unwilling to meet new people or to consider new ideas. 孤立的;保守的;思想狭隘的 [disapproval]

  • ...the old image of the insular, xenophobic Brit.

    孤立保守而又恐外的英国人的旧有形象

SYN narrow-minded, prejudiced, provincial, closed  
insularity

  • But at least they have started to break out of their old insularity.

    但是他们至少已经开始打破过去孤立保守的心态。
