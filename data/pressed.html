pressed
prest prɛst
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] （时间、金钱）紧张的，匮乏的，不足的 If you say that you are pressed for time or pressed for money, you mean that you do not have enough time or money at the moment. （时间、金钱）紧张的，匮乏的，不足的

  • Are you pressed for time, Mr Bayliss? If not, I suggest we have lunch. [+ for]

    你时间紧吗，贝利斯先生？如果不紧，不如我们一起吃午饭吧。
