self-assembly
ˌsɛlfəˈsɛmblɪ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (家具等)自行组装的 Self-assembly is used to refer to furniture and other goods that you buy in parts and that you have to put together yourself. (家具等)自行组装的

  • ...a range of self-assembly bedroom furniture.

    一整套自行组装的卧室家具

SYN DIY, do-it-yourself, self-build, prefabricated
