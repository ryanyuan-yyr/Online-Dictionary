arranged əreɪndʒd
ADJ 安置于…的;建于…的;…布置的 If you say how things are arranged, you are talking about their position in relation to each other or to something else. 安置于…的;建于…的;…布置的

  • The house itself is three stories high and arranged around a courtyard.

    房子本身有三层高，绕着一个庭院而建。

  • They lived in neatly-arranged little houses.

    他们住在布置整洁的小房子里。
