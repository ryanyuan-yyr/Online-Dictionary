comical
kɒmɪkəl ˈkɑmɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 滑稽的;令人发笑的 If you describe something as comical, you mean that it makes you want to laugh because it seems funny or silly. 滑稽的;令人发笑的

  • Her expression is almost comical.

    她的表情近乎滑稽。

  • Events took a comical turn.

    事态出现了有趣的转折。

SYN funny, entertaining, comic, silly  
comically

  • She raised her eyebrows comically.

    她滑稽地挑了挑眉毛。

  • The display of prehistoric monsters is comically naive.

    史前怪物的展览幼稚可笑。
