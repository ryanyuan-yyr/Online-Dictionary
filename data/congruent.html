congruent
kɒŋgruənt ˈkɑŋgruənt ˈkɑngruənt
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 相似的;相称的;一致的 If one thing is congruent with another thing, they are similar or fit together well. 相似的;相称的;一致的 [formal]

  • They want to work in an organisation whose values are congruent with their own. [+ with]

    他们想在一个符合自己价值观的机构里工作。

SYN compatible, agreeing, according, consistent
