overland
oʊvəʳlænd ˈoʊvərˌlænd ˈoʊvərlənd
ADJ [ADJ n] 经由陆路的；横跨陆地的 An overland journey is made across land rather than by ship or aeroplane. 经由陆路的；横跨陆地的

  • ...an overland journey through Iraq, Turkey, Iran and Pakistan.

    途径伊拉克、土耳其、伊朗和巴基斯坦的陆路旅程

  • The overland route is across some really tough mountains.

    这条陆上路线要跨越一些非常险峻的大山。

  • Militia leaders had halted overland food convoys in the region.

    民兵首领已拦阻了该地区经由陆上运送食品的车队。

Overland is also an adverb.

  • They're travelling to Baghdad overland.

    他们正经由陆路去往巴格达。
