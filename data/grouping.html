grouping
gruːpɪŋ ˈgrupɪŋ
Word forms: plural groupings
N-COUNT （具有共同点的）一类人（或事物）；小团体；小圈子 A grouping is a set of people or things that have something in common. （具有共同点的）一类人（或事物）；小团体；小圈子

  • There were two main political groupings pressing for independence.

    有两大政治团体力主独立。

SYN organization, group, body, association
