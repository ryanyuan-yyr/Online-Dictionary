muzzy
mʌzi ˈmʌzi
1 ADJ-GRADED (通常由于生病或饮酒过多而)头脑糊涂的，昏昏沉沉的 If someone feels muzzy, they are confused and unable to think clearly, usually because they are ill or have drunk too much alcohol. (通常由于生病或饮酒过多而)头脑糊涂的，昏昏沉沉的 [mainly British, informal]
2 ADJ-GRADED (照片或电视图像)模糊不清的，蒙眬的 If a photograph or television picture is muzzy, it is unclear. (照片或电视图像)模糊不清的，蒙眬的 [mainly British, informal]
