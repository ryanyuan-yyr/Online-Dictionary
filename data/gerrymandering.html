gerrymandering
dʒerimændərɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT （为使某一党获得优势）不公正的改划选区 Gerrymandering is the act of altering political boundaries in order to give an unfair advantage to one political party or group of people. （为使某一党获得优势）不公正的改划选区 [disapproval]

  • With the help of skilful gerrymandering, the Party has never lost an election since.

    这个政党通过巧妙地改划选区而使此后的选举无往不胜。
