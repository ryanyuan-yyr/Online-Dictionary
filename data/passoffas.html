pass off as
把…装成；把…冒充为 If you pass something off as another thing, you convince people that it is that other thing. 把…装成；把…冒充为

  • He passed himself off as a senior psychologist. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    他冒充成资深心理学家。

  • I've tried to pass off my accent as a convent school accent. [V P n P n]

    我努力将口音装成女修会学校的口音。

  • ...horse meat being passed off as ground beef. [be V-ed P P n]

    冒充牛肉糜的马肉
