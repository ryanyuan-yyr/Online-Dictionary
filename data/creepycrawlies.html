creepy-crawly
kriːpi krɔːli ˌkriˈpi krɔli
Word forms: plural creepy-crawlies
N-COUNT [usually plural] (常为儿语，指让人害怕或厌恶的)爬虫，蠕虫 You can refer to insects as creepy-crawlies when they give you a feeling of fear or disgust. This word is mainly used by children. (常为儿语，指让人害怕或厌恶的)爬虫，蠕虫 [mainly British, informal, disapproval]
