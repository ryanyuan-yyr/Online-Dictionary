call in
1 PHRASAL VERB 请来;找来 If you call someone in, you ask them to come and help you or do something for you. 请来;找来 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Call in an architect or surveyor to oversee the work. [VERB PREP. noun]

    找个建筑师或监理员来监工。

2 PHRASAL VERB 顺路拜访;顺道走访 If you call in somewhere, you make a short visit there. 顺路拜访;顺道走访

  • He just calls in occasionally. [VERB PREP.]

    他只是偶尔过来坐坐。

  • I got into the habit of calling in on Gloria on my way home. [VERB PREP. + on]

    我养成了回家路上顺便去看格洛丽亚的习惯。
