androgynous
ændrɒdʒɪnəs ænˈdrɑdʒənəs
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （人、动物或植物）有两性特征的，雌雄同体的，雌雄同序的 In biology, an androgynous person, animal, or plant has both male and female sexual characteristics. （人、动物或植物）有两性特征的，雌雄同体的，雌雄同序的 [technical]
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 兼具两性的；双性并存的；男女不分的 If you describe someone as androgynous, you mean that they are not distinctly masculine or feminine in appearance or in behaviour. 兼具两性的；双性并存的；男女不分的

  • Belinda was always attracted to men with an androgynous quality to them.

    贝琳达常能以自己的中性特质令男人倾心。

  • The style was quite androgynous.

    这种风格较为中性。

SYN hermaphrodite, bisexual, androgyne, hermaphroditic
