numb
nʌm nʌm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense numbs, present participle numbing, past tense , past participle numbed
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 麻木的;失去知觉的 If a part of your body is numb, you cannot feel anything there. 麻木的;失去知觉的

  • He could feel his fingers growing numb at their tips.

    他能感到指尖正在变得麻木。

  • My legs felt numb and my toes ached.

    我双腿麻木，脚趾疼痛。

SYN unfeeling, dead, frozen, paralysed  
numbness

  • I have recently been suffering from pain and numbness in my hands.

    我近来双手一直疼痛麻木。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (因震惊、害怕、悲痛等)失态的，木然的 If you are numb with shock, fear, or grief, you are so shocked, frightened, or upset that you cannot think clearly or feel any emotion. (因震惊、害怕、悲痛等)失态的，木然的

  • The mother, numb with grief, has trouble speaking. [+ with]

    那位母亲因过于悲痛而说不出话来。

  • I was so shocked I went numb.

    我太过震惊，呆若木鸡。

SYN stupefied, deadened, unfeeling, insensible  
numbness

  • Many men become more aware of emotional numbness in their 40s.

    很多男性到了不惑之年以后会愈加意识到自己的情感麻木。

numbly

  • He walked numbly into the cemetery.

    他糊里糊涂地走进了墓地。

3 VERB 使木然;使迟钝 If an event or experience numbs you, you can no longer think clearly or feel any emotion. 使木然;使迟钝

  • For a while the shock of Philippe's letter numbed her. [VERB noun]

    菲利普来信的打击让她愣了好一会儿。

  • The horror of my experience has numbed my senses. [VERB noun]

    我经历的那种恐怖已使我感觉迟钝。

SYN stun, knock out, paralyse, daze  
4See also:mind-numbing 
numbed

  • I'm so numbed with shock that I can hardly think.

    我惊呆了，几乎无法思考。

  • ...the sort of numbed hush which usually follows an automobile accident.

    通常在车祸之后出现的那种死寂

5 VERB (因天气、药品或重击)使(身体某部位)麻木，使失去知觉 If cold weather, a drug, or a blow numbs a part of your body, you can no longer feel anything in it. (因天气、药品或重击)使(身体某部位)麻木，使失去知觉

  • The cold numbed my fingers. [VERB noun]

    严寒冻得我的手指都没有知觉了。

  • An injection of local anaesthetic is usually given first to numb the area. [VERB noun]

    通常先注射局部麻醉药，使该部位失去知觉。

  • She awoke with a numbed feeling in her left leg. [VERB-ed]

    她醒来觉得左腿麻木。

SYN deaden, freeze, dull, paralyse
