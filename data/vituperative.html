vituperative
vɪtjuːpərətɪv vaɪtuːp-
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 责骂的;辱骂的;谩骂的 Vituperative remarks are full of hate, anger, or insults. 责骂的;辱骂的;谩骂的 [formal]

  • He is often the victim of vituperative remarks concerning his wealth.

    他经常因为富有而受到辱骂。

  • ...one of journalism's most vituperative critics.

    新闻界最爱发表辱骂攻击言辞的评论家之一

SYN abusive, vitriolic, virulent, insulting
