liberalize
lɪbrəlaɪz ˈlɪbərəlˌaɪz ˈlɪbrəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense liberalizes, present participle liberalizing, past tense , past participle liberalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 liberalise
VERB 放宽(对…的)限制；（使）自由化 When a country or government liberalizes, or liberalizes its laws or its attitudes, it becomes less strict and allows people more freedom in their actions. 放宽(对…的)限制；（使）自由化

  • ...authoritarian states that have only now begun to liberalise. [VERB]

    才刚刚开始实行自由化的独裁国家

  • ...the decision to liberalize travel restrictions. [VERB noun]

    放宽旅游限制的决定

SYN relax, ease, moderate, modify
