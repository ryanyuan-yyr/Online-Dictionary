modify
mɒdɪfaɪ ˈmɑdəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense modifies, present participle modifying, past tense , past participle modified
1 VERB 修改;变更;改进 If you modify something, you change it slightly, usually in order to improve it. 修改;变更;改进

  • The club members did agree to modify their recruitment policy. [VERB noun]

    俱乐部成员的确同意修改吸纳新成员的政策。

  • The plane was a modified version of the C-130. [VERB-ed]

    这种飞机是C-130的改进机型。

SYN change, reform, vary, convert  
modification

  • Relatively minor modifications were required.

    只需稍作改动。

  • ...behaviour modification techniques.

    行为矫正方法

2 VERB 修饰;限定 A word or group of words that modifies another word describes or classifies something, or restricts the meaning of the word. 修饰;限定 [technical]

  • It is a rule of English that adjectives generally precede the noun they modify: we say 'a good cry', not 'a cry good'. [VERB noun]

    英语规定形容词通常放在其修饰的名词前：我们说 a good cry，不说 a cry good。
