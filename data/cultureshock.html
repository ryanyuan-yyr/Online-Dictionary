culture shock
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 文化冲击，文化休克(指初到异国时的焦虑、孤独和困惑感) Culture shock is a feeling of anxiety, loneliness, and confusion that people sometimes experience when they first arrive in another country. 文化冲击，文化休克(指初到异国时的焦虑、孤独和困惑感)

  • Callum, recently arrived in Glasgow, is jobless, homeless, friendless, and suffering from culture shock.

    卡勒姆初到格拉斯哥，没有工作，无家可归，无人倾诉，在文化冲击中挣扎。
