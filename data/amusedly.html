amuse
əmjuːz əˈmjuz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense amuses, present participle amusing, past tense , past participle amused
1 VERB 逗乐；逗笑 If something amuses you, it makes you want to laugh or smile. 逗乐；逗笑

  • The thought seemed to amuse him. [VERB noun]

    这种想法好像把他逗乐了。

  • Their antics never fail to amuse. [VERB]

    他们滑稽的举止总是让人发笑。

SYN entertain, please, delight, charm  
2 VERB 给…提供娱乐（或消遣） If you amuse yourself, you do something in order to pass the time and not become bored. 给…提供娱乐（或消遣）

  • I need distractions. I need to amuse myself so I won't keep thinking about things. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    我需要分散我的注意力。需要消遣一下，让自己不去想这样那样的事。

  • Put a selection of baby toys in his cot to amuse him if he wakes early. [VERB noun]

    要在婴儿床里放一些玩具，以便他醒得早时可以自己玩。

SYN occupy, interest, involve, engage
