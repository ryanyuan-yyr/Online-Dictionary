pattern
pætəʳn ˈpætərn ˈpætərn
Word forms: plural patterns
1 N-COUNT 模式；方式 A pattern is the repeated or regular way in which something happens or is done. 模式；方式

  • All three attacks followed the same pattern.

    3次袭击模式相同。

  • A change in the pattern of his breathing became apparent. [+ of]

    他的呼吸方式明显起了变化。

SYN order, plan, system, method  
2 N-COUNT 图案；花样 A pattern is an arrangement of lines or shapes, especially a design in which the same shape is repeated at regular intervals over a surface. 图案；花样

  • ...a golden robe embroidered with red and purple thread stitched into a pattern of flames. [+ of]

    用红线和紫线绣着火焰图案的金色礼袍

3 N-COUNT 模型；样板 A pattern is a diagram or shape that you can use as a guide when you are making something such as a model or a piece of clothing. 模型；样板

  • ...cutting out a pattern for trousers. [+ for]

    裁出裤子的样板

  • Send for our free patterns to knit yourself.

    欢迎函索免费式样体验自己编织的乐趣。

  • ...sewing patterns.

    缝纫样板
