morality
mərælɪti moʊˈrælɪti mɔˈrælɪti məˈrælɪti
Word forms: plural moralities
1 N-UNCOUNT 道德；道义;伦理 Morality is the belief that some behaviour is right and acceptable and that other behaviour is wrong. 道德；道义;伦理

  • ...standards of morality and justice in society.

    社会道德和正义的标准

  • ...an effort to preserve traditional morality.

    维护传统道德的努力

SYN virtue, justice, principles, morals  
2 N-COUNT 道义准则;道德规范;伦理学 A morality is a system of principles and values concerning people's behaviour, which is generally accepted by a society or by a particular group of people. 道义准则;道德规范;伦理学

  • ...a morality that is sexist.

    存在性别歧视的道德规范

  • ...communities and their shared moralities.

    社区和其共同的道义准则

3 N-UNCOUNT 道德性；道德 The morality of something is how right or acceptable it is. 道德性；道德

  • ...the arguments about the morality of blood sports. [+ of]

    有关猎杀动物行为是否道德的争论

SYN rights and wrongs, ethics, ethicality, ethicalness
