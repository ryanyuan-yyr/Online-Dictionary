lay into
PHRASAL VERB 痛打；攻击；抨击 To lay into someone or something means to start attacking or criticizing them. 痛打；攻击；抨击 [informal]

  • A mob of women laid into him with handbags and pointed shoes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一帮女人用手提包砸他，用鞋尖踢他。

  • She used to lay into Gareth about how much he spent. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她以前常常责备加雷思花钱如何如何厉害。

SYN attack, hit, set about, hit out at
