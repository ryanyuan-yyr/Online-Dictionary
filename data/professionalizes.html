professionalize
prəfeʃənəlaɪz proʊˈfɛʃənəlˌaɪz prɛˈfɛʃənəlˌaɪz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense professionalizes, present participle professionalizing, past tense , past participle professionalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 professionalise
VERB 使专业化；使职业化 To professionalize an organization, an institution, or an activity means to make it more professional, for example by paying the people who are involved in it. 使专业化；使职业化

  • ...the possibility of professionalising local government by offering salaries to senior councillors. [VERB noun]

    通过给高级议员发薪水而将地方政府职业化的可能性
