cheapskate
tʃiːpskeɪt ˈtʃipˌskeɪt
Word forms: plural cheapskates
N-COUNT 小气鬼;吝啬鬼 If you say that someone is a cheapskate, you think that they are mean and do not like spending money. 小气鬼;吝啬鬼 [disapproval]

  • Tell your husband not to be a cheapskate.

    劝劝你丈夫，叫他别那么小气。

  • ...cheapskate employers.

    吝啬的雇主
