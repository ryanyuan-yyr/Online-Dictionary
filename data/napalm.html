napalm
neɪpɑːm ˈneɪˌpɑm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense napalms, present participle napalming, past tense , past participle napalmed
1 N-UNCOUNT 凝固汽油(弹) Napalm is a substance containing petrol which is used to make bombs that burn people, buildings, and plants. 凝固汽油(弹)

  • The government has consistently denied using napalm.

    政府始终否认使用过凝固汽油弹。

2 VERB 用凝固汽油弹攻击 If people napalm other people or places, they attack and burn them using napalm. 用凝固汽油弹攻击

  • Why napalm a village now? [VERB noun]

    为什么现在用凝固汽油弹攻击一个村庄？
