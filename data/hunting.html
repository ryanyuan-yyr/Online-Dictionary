hunting
hʌntɪŋ ˈhʌntɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 打猎;狩猎;（野兽的）猎食 Hunting is the chasing and killing of wild animals by people or other animals, for food or as a sport. 打猎;狩猎;（野兽的）猎食

  • Hunting is one of Italy's most popular sports.

    狩猎是意大利最受欢迎的体育运动之一。

  • Deer hunting was banned in Scotland in 1959.

    猎鹿于1959年在苏格兰被禁止。

  • ...a hunting accident.

    打猎事故

2 N-UNCOUNT [n NOUN] 搜寻;寻找 Hunting is the activity of searching for a particular thing. 搜寻;寻找

  • Jobclub can help you with job hunting.

    失业救助所可以为您在求职方面提供帮助。

Hunting is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • Lee has divided his time between flat-hunting and travelling.

    李平时不是在找公寓，就是在旅行。
