fruity
fruːti ˈfruti
Word forms: comparative fruitier, superlative fruitiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 果香的;水果味的 Something that is fruity smells or tastes of fruit. 果香的;水果味的

  • This shampoo smells fruity and leaves the hair beautifully silky.

    这款洗发水是果香型的，洗后头发如丝般顺滑。

  • ...a lovely rich fruity wine.

    散发着浓郁水果芬芳的美酒

SYN rich, full, mellow  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (声音或笑声)圆润的，甜美的 A fruity voice or laugh is pleasantly rich and deep. (声音或笑声)圆润的，甜美的

  • Jerrold laughed again, a solid, fruity laugh.

    杰罗尔德又笑了，声音浑厚而圆润。
