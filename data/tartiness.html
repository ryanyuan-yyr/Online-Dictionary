tarty
tɑːʳti
Word forms: comparative tartier, superlative tartiest
ADJ-GRADED (女人或其衣着)妖艳的，风骚的，花枝招展的 tartiest If you describe a woman or her clothes as tarty, you are critical of her because she tries to make herself look sexually attractive in a vulgar way. (女人或其衣着)妖艳的，风骚的，花枝招展的 [informal, disapproval]

  • That coat made her look so tarty.

    那件大衣使她显得十分妖艳。
