hysteria
hɪstɪəriə hɪˈstɪərɪə -ster- hɪˈstɛriə hɪˈstɪriə
1 N-UNCOUNT （一群人的）情绪失控，狂野情绪的爆发 Hysteria among a group of people is a state of uncontrolled excitement, anger, or panic. （一群人的）情绪失控，狂野情绪的爆发

  • No one could help getting carried away by the hysteria.

    所有人都不由自主地受到狂热情绪的感染。

  • Several were hurt in the panic. 'It was mass hysteria,' said Rev John Borders.

    数人在恐慌中受了伤。“人们都疯了，”约翰·博德斯牧师说。

SYN frenzy, panic, madness, agitation  
2 N-UNCOUNT 癔病;歇斯底里 A person who is suffering from hysteria is in a state of violent and disturbed emotion as a result of shock. 癔病;歇斯底里 [medicine]

  • By now, she was screaming, completely overcome with hysteria.

    现在她正在尖叫，完全处于癔病发作状态。
