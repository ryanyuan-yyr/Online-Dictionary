extracurricular
ekstrə kərɪkjʊləʳ ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjulər ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 extracurricular
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 课外的 Extracurricular activities are activities for students that are not part of their course. 课外的 [formal]

  • Each child had participated in extracurricular activities at school.

    每个孩子都参加了学校的课外活动。

  • ...extracurricular sport.

    课外体育活动

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 工作之余的 Extracurricular activities are activities that someone does that are not part of their normal work. 工作之余的 [informal]

  • The money he made from these extracurricular activities enabled him to pursue other ventures.

    他从这些额外工作中赚取的钱使他可以从事其他事业。
