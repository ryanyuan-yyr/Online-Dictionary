hatred
heɪtrɪd ˈheɪtrɪd
Word forms: plural hatreds
N-UNCOUNT 仇恨;憎恶;敌意 Hatred is an extremely strong feeling of dislike for someone or something. 仇恨;憎恶;敌意

  • Her hatred of them would never lead her to murder. [+ of/for]

    她虽说仇恨他们，但绝不至于去杀人。

  • My hatred for her is so intense it seems to be destroying me.

    我对她恨之入骨，都快要无法自拔了。

  • He has been accused of inciting racial hatred.

    他被控煽动种族仇恨。

SYN hate, dislike, animosity, aversion  
Quotations
