consumerism
kənsjuːmərɪzəm -suː- kənˈsumərˌɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 消费主义 Consumerism is the belief that it is good to buy and use a lot of goods. 消费主义

  • They have clearly embraced Western consumerism.

    他们显然完全接受了西方的消费主义观念。

2 N-UNCOUNT 消费者权益保护 Consumerism is the protection of the rights and interests of consumers. 消费者权益保护
