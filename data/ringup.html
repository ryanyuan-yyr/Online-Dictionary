ring up
1 →see: ring 1 
2 PHRASAL VERB 把(钱款)记入(收款机) If a shop assistant rings up a sale on a cash register, he or she presses the keys in order to record the amount that is being spent. 把(钱款)记入(收款机) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She was ringing up her sale on an ancient cash register. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她正用一台老掉牙的收银机记账。

3 PHRASAL VERB 赚取;盈利 If a company rings up an amount of money, usually a large amount of money, it makes that amount of money in sales or profits. 赚取;盈利

  • The advertising agency rang up 1.4 billion dollars in yearly sales. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这家广告代理商每年销售额达14亿美元。
