downward
daʊnwəʳd ˈdaʊnwərd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 下降的;向下的 A downward movement or look is directed towards a lower place or a lower level. 下降的;向下的

  • ...a firm downward movement of the hands.

    双手有力的向下摆动

2See also:downwards 
3 ADJ [ADJ n] 趋向没落的;衰退的;局势变坏的 If you refer to a downward trend, you mean that something is decreasing or that a situation is getting worse. 趋向没落的;衰退的;局势变坏的

  • The downward trend in home ownership is likely to continue.

    拥有房屋所有权者人数减少的趋势可能会持续。

  • ...a decline in the economy, resulting in a general downward spiral.

    经济上的衰退导致了总体上的螺旋式下降。
