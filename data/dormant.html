dormant
dɔːʳmənt ˈdɔːmɪənt ˈdɔrmənt
1 ADJ 暂停活动的;暂搁不用的;休眠的;蛰伏的 Something that is dormant is not active, growing, or being used at the present time but is capable of becoming active later on. 暂停活动的;暂搁不用的;休眠的;蛰伏的

  • ...when the long dormant volcano of Mount St Helens erupted in 1980.

    当长期休眠的圣海伦斯火山在 1980 年爆发时

  • The virus remains dormant in nerve tissue until activated.

    病毒潜伏于神经组织里直到被激活。

  • The United Nations is resuming a diplomatic effort that has lain dormant for almost two decades.

    联合国正重新开始暂停了近 20 年的外交努力。

SYN latent, inactive, lurking, quiescent  
dormancy

  • During dormancy the plants must be kept very dry.

    在休眠期，植物必须保持非常干燥。
