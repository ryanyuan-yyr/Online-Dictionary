bandy about
(also bandy around)
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 散布；传播 If someone's name or something such as an idea is bandied about or is bandied around, that person or that thing is discussed by many people in a casual way. 散布；传播 [disapproval]

  • He whispered my name to newspapermen, knowing that it would be bandied about. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他把我的名字悄声告诉了记者，明知道这会到处传开的。

  • Young players now hear various sums bandied around about how much players are getting. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    年轻球员现在听到的四下流传的球员收入版本各不相同。
