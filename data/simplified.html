simplify
sɪmplɪfaɪ ˈsɪmpləˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense simplifies, present participle simplifying, past tense , past participle simplified
VERB 简化;使易懂;使简洁 If you simplify something, you make it easier to understand or you remove the things which make it complex. 简化;使易懂;使简洁

  • ...a plan to simplify the complex social security system. [VERB noun]

    简化复杂的社会保障体系的计划

  • He reduced his needs to the minimum by simplifying his life. [VERB noun]

    他过上了一种简单的生活，将自己的需求降到最低限度。

SYN make simpler, facilitate, streamline, disentangle  
simplified

  • Last night, the president presented a shorter, simplified version of his speech.

    昨晚，总统发表了一番更加简短明了的演讲。
