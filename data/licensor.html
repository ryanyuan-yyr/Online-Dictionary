license
laɪsəns ˈlaɪsəns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense licenses, present participle licensing, past tense , past participle licensed
VERB 批准；许可 To license a person or activity means to give official permission for the person to do something or for the activity to take place. 批准；许可 [Also VERB n + to]

  • ...a proposal that would require the state to license guns. [VERB noun]

    要求该州对枪支实行许可管理的议案

  • Under the agreement, the council can license a U.S. company to produce the drug. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    根据协议，委员会可授权一家美国公司生产这种药品。

SYN permit, commission, enable, sanction
