raconteur
rækɒntɜːʳ ˌrækɑnˈtɜr ˌrækənˈtɜr
Word forms: plural raconteurs
N-COUNT 擅长讲故事者 A raconteur is someone, usually a man, who can tell stories in an interesting or amusing way. 擅长讲故事者

  • He spoke eight languages and was a noted raconteur.

    他能说8种语言，是个出了名的讲故事高手。

SYN storyteller, relater, recounter, teller of stories
