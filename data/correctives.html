corrective
kərektɪv kəˈrɛktɪv
Word forms: plural correctives
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 改正的;纠正的;矫正的 Corrective measures or techniques are intended to put right something that is wrong. 改正的;纠正的;矫正的

  • Scientific institutions have been reluctant to take corrective action.

    科学机构一直不愿作出更正。

  • He has received extensive corrective surgery to his skull.

    他的头骨做过大面积的矫形手术。

SYN remedial, therapeutic, palliative, restorative  
2 N-COUNT 起纠正作用的东西;修正 If something is a corrective to a particular view or account, it gives a more accurate or fairer picture than there would have been without it. 起纠正作用的东西;修正 [formal]

  • ...a useful corrective to the mistaken view that all psychologists are behaviourists. [+ to]

    对所有的心理学者都是行为主义者这一错误观点的有效纠正
