cage
keɪdʒ keɪdʒ
Word forms: plural cages
1 N-COUNT 笼子;鸟笼;兽笼 A cage is a structure of wire or metal bars in which birds or animals are kept. 笼子;鸟笼;兽笼

  • I hate to see birds in cages.

    我不愿意看到鸟被关在笼子里。

SYN enclosure, pen, coop, hutch  
2See also:rib cage 
3 PHRASE 使焦躁不安;令心神不宁 If someone rattles your cage, they do something which is intended to make you feel nervous. 使焦躁不安;令心神不宁

  • If he's trying to rattle your cage, it's working.

    如果他存心要让你不得安宁的话，那他是得逞了。
