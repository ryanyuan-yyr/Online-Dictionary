paper over
PHRASAL VERB 掩盖，掩饰（分歧） If people paper over a disagreement between them, they find a temporary solution to it in order to give the impression that things are going well. 掩盖，掩饰（分歧）

  • ...his determination to paper over the cracks in his party and avoid confrontation. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他掩盖党内分歧、避免冲突的决心

  • Differences were papered over but by no means were they fully resolved. [VERB PREP. noun]

    分歧被掩盖了，但是绝没有得到完全解决。
