themed
θiːmd θiːmd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (地方、活动)围绕特定主题的，特定历史时期的 A themed place or event has been created so that it shows a particular historical time or way of life, or tells a well-known story. (地方、活动)围绕特定主题的，特定历史时期的 [mainly British]

  • ...themed restaurants, bars, and nightclubs.

    主题饭店、酒吧和夜总会

  • ...a huge space laid out as a series of small themed areas.

    分布着一系列小型主题区域的巨大空间

-themed θimd
COMB in ADJ 表示“有...主题的”-Themed can be added to adjectives and nouns to form adjectives which describe the particular theme that a place or event has. 表示“有...主题的”

  • ...a movie-themed amusement park.

    ...一个电影主题的游乐园。

  • ...a Hawaiian-themed party.

    ...一个夏威夷主题的聚会。
