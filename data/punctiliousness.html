punctilious
pʌŋktɪliəs pʌŋkˈtɪliəs
ADJ-GRADED 一丝不苟的；循规蹈矩的 Someone who is punctilious is very careful to behave correctly. 一丝不苟的；循规蹈矩的 [formal]

  • He was punctilious about being ready and waiting in the entrance hall exactly on time.

    他一丝不苟地作好准备并准时地在门厅等候。

  • He was a punctilious young man.

    他是个非常拘礼的年轻人。

SYN particular, careful, strict, exact  
punctiliously

  • Given the circumstances, his behaviour to Laura had been punctiliously correct.

    考虑当时的情况，他对劳拉的举止非常得体。
