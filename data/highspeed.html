high-speed
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 高速的 (车辆或设备)A high-speed vehicle or piece of equipment moves or operates very quickly. 高速的 (车辆或设备)

  • Japan's high-speed trains travel a long way in a short time.

    日本高速列车可以在短时间内行驶很远的路程。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 因高速行驶导致的 (事故)A high-speed accident happens when the vehicles involved are travelling very fast. 因高速行驶导致的 (事故)

  • They were killed in a high-speed crash in a tunnel in Paris.

    他们在一起发生在巴黎一座隧道里的高速行驶引发的撞车事故中丧生。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 高速的 (因特网接入)A high-speed Internet connection allows users to access websites very quickly. 高速的 (因特网接入)

  • Most of our customers have now upgraded to a high-speed broadband connection.

    我们大多数的客户现已升级至高速宽带连接。
