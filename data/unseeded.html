unseeded
ʌnsiːdɪd ʌnˈsiːdɪd
ADJ （选手）非种子的，没进前16强的 In tennis and badminton competitions, an unseeded player is someone who has not been ranked among the top 16 players by the competition's organizers. （选手）非种子的，没进前16强的

  • He was understandably dejected after losing in the first round to an unseeded American.

    第一轮就输给了一位美国的非种子选手，他的沮丧是可以理解的。
