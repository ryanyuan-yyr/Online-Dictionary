wintry
wɪntri ˈwɪntrɪ ˈwɪntərɪ ˈwɪntəlɪ ˈwɪntri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 冬天的;冬令的;寒冷的 Wintry weather is cold and has features that are typical of winter. 冬天的;冬令的;寒冷的

  • Wintry weather continues to sweep across Britain.

    寒冷的天气继续席卷整个英国。

  • A wintry wind was blowing.

    寒风呼啸。

  • ...a dark wintry day.

    阴郁的冬日

SYN cold, freezing, frozen, harsh  
2 ADJ-GRADED (态度或行为)冷冰冰的，冷淡的 If you describe someone's attitude or behaviour as wintry, you mean that they seem very unfriendly. (态度或行为)冷冰冰的，冷淡的

  • He was, according to witnesses, extremely wintry with Her Royal Highness.

    据目击者称，他对女王殿下极其冷淡。
