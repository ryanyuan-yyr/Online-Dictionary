come around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 come round
1 PHRASAL VERB 拜访;探访 If someone comes around or comes round to your house, they call there to see you. 拜访;探访

  • Beryl came round this morning to apologize. [VERB PREP.]

    贝丽尔今天上午来登门道歉了。

  • Quite a lot of people came round to the house. [VERB PREP. + to]

    宾客盈门。

2 PHRASAL VERB 改变观点;改变看法(以接受) If you come around or come round to an idea, you eventually change your mind and accept it or agree with it. 改变观点;改变看法(以接受)

  • It looks like they're coming around to our way of thinking. [VERB PREP. + to]

    看来他们会改变看法，同意我们的思路。

  • She will eventually come round. [VERB PREP.]

    最终她会回心转意的。

3 PHRASAL VERB (按照预期)发生,再度出现 When something comes around or comes round, it happens as a regular or predictable event. (按照预期)发生,再度出现

  • I hope still to be in the side when the World Cup comes around next year. [VERB PREP.]

    我希望明年世界杯举行的时候我仍然效力于该队。

4 PHRASAL VERB 苏醒 When someone who is unconscious comes around or comes round, they recover consciousness. 苏醒

  • When I came round I was on the kitchen floor. [VERB PREP.]

    醒来时，我躺在厨房地板上。
