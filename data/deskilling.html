deskill
diːskɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deskills, present participle deskilling, past tense , past participle deskilled
VERB [oft passive] 使（工人）的技能过时 If workers are deskilled, they no longer need special skills to do their work, especially because of modern methods of production. 使（工人）的技能过时

  • Administrative staff may be deskilled through increased automation and efficiency. [be VERB-ed]

    随着自动化程度和效率的不断提高，对管理人员的技能要求可能会大大降低。
