opine
oʊpaɪn oʊˈpaɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense opines, present participle opining, past tense , past participle opined
VERB 认为；发表意见 To opine means to express your opinion. 认为；发表意见 [formal] [Also V + on/about]

  • 'She's probably had a row with her boyfriend,' Charles opined. [VERB with quote]

    “她大概跟男朋友吵架了，”查尔斯说道。

  • He opined that the navy would have to start again from the beginning. [VERB that]

    他认为海军将不得不再度从零开始。

SYN suggest, say, think, believe
