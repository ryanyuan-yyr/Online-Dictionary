Word forms: plural syllables
1 N-COUNT 音节 A syllable is a part of a word that contains a single vowel sound and that is pronounced as a unit. So, for example, 'book' has one syllable, and 'reading' has two syllables. 音节

  • We children called her Oma, accenting both syllables.

    我们这群小孩叫她Oma，两个音节都重读。
