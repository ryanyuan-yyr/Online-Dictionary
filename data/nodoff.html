nod off
PHRASAL VERB (尤指不打算入睡时)打瞌睡，打盹 If you nod off, you fall asleep, especially when you had not intended to. (尤指不打算入睡时)打瞌睡，打盹 [informal]

  • The judge appeared to nod off yesterday while a witness was being cross-examined. [VERB PREP.]

    昨天一名证人接受盘问时，法官好像在打盹。

  • He was nodding off to sleep in an armchair. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他坐在扶手椅上打盹，睡着了。

SYN fall asleep, sleep, nap, doze
