annals
ænəlz ˈænəlz
1 N-PLURAL [usu in the N of n] 史册;历史记载 If something is in the annals of a nation or field of activity, it is recorded as part of its history. 史册;历史记载

  • He has become a legend in the annals of military history.

    他已成为军事史上的一个传奇。

2 N-PLURAL [with supplement, usually the NOUN of n] （用于学术杂志的名称）年鉴 Annals is used in the titles of journals of a particular field of academic research. （用于学术杂志的名称）年鉴

  • It was published in the 'Annals of Internal Medicine'.

    它发表在《内科医学年鉴》上。
