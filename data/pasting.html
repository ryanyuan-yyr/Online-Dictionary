pasting
peɪstɪŋ
1 N-SING 严厉批评 If something or someone takes a pasting, they are severely criticized. 严厉批评 [mainly British, informal]

  • The people who run Lloyd's of London took a pasting yesterday.

    伦敦劳埃德保险社的经营者昨天受到了严厉批评。

  • ...the critical pasting that the film received.

    那部电影受到的严厉批评

2 N-SING 惨败 If a sports team or political party is given a pasting, they are heavily defeated. 惨败 [mainly British, informal]
