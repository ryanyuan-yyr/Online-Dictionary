reminiscent
remɪnɪsənt ˌrɛməˈnɪsənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 使人想起的;使人忆起的 If you say that one thing is reminiscent of another, you mean that it reminds you of it. 使人想起的;使人忆起的 [formal]

  • The decor was reminiscent of a municipal arts-and-leisure centre. [+ of]

    这种布置让人想起都市里的艺术休闲中心。

  • She bowed her head in a gesture somehow reminiscent of royalty.

    她低头的姿势让人想起皇室风范。

SYN suggestive, evocative, redolent, remindful  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 怀旧的;缅怀过去的 A reminiscent look or smile is a look or smile which shows that you are remembering something from the past, usually with pleasure. 怀旧的;缅怀过去的 [formal]

  • A slow, reminiscent smile spread over her face.

    怀旧的微笑慢慢浮现在她脸上。

reminiscently

  • Both of them smiled at the picture reminiscently.

    他们俩都微笑着看着这幅画，追忆着往事。
