leisurely
leʒəʳli liːʒ- ˈliʒərli ˈlɛʒərli
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 悠闲的；从容的；慢悠悠的 A leisurely action is done in a relaxed and unhurried way. 悠闲的；从容的；慢悠悠的

  • Lunch was a leisurely affair.

    午饭吃得很悠闲。

  • Tweed walked at a leisurely pace.

    特威德漫步而行。

SYN unhurried, relaxed, slow, easy  
Leisurely is also an adverb.

  • We walked leisurely into the hotel.

    我们慢悠悠地走回酒店。
