masculinize
mæskjʊlɪnaɪz ˈmæskjəlɪˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense masculinizes, present participle masculinizing, past tense , past participle masculinized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 masculinise
VERB [usually passive] 使男性化;使具备男性特征;使主要与男性相关 To masculinize something means to make it into something that involves mainly men or is thought suitable for or typical of men. 使男性化;使具备男性特征;使主要与男性相关 [formal]

  • Not all plantation work has been masculinized. [be VERB-ed]

    种植园里的工作并非全是些男性化的工作。
