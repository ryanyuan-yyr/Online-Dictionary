shell out
为…花费一大笔钱 If you shell out for something, you spend a lot of money on it. 为…花费一大笔钱

  • You won't have to shell out a fortune for it. [V P n + for/on]

    你不必为它花一大笔钱。

  • If I'm shelling out a few hundred pounds, I don't want someone telling me what I can or can't do. [VERB PREP. noun]

    如果我要花费几百英镑，我不希望有人告诉我什么能做，什么不能做。

  • ...an insurance premium which saves you from having to shell out for repairs. [V P + for/on]

    为你省去大笔维修费用的保险费

  • The very fact that you shelled out money to come and see us is a good sign. [V P n to-inf]

    你舍得花钱来看我们，这正是个好迹象。
