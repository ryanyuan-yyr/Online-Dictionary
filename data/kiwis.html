kiwi
kiːwiː ˈkiwi
Word forms: plural kiwis
1 N-COUNT 同kiwi fruit A kiwi is the same as a kiwi fruit . 同kiwi fruit
2 N-COUNT 几维（新西兰鸟，不能飞） A kiwi is a type of bird that lives in New Zealand. Kiwis cannot fly. 几维（新西兰鸟，不能飞）
3 N-COUNT 新西兰人（可能具冒犯意味） People who come from New Zealand are sometimes referred to as Kiwis. This use could cause offence. 新西兰人（可能具冒犯意味） [British, Australian]
