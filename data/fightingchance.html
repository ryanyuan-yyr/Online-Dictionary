fighting chance
N-SING [a N; oft N of n/-ing] (经过努力奋斗可能获得成功的)一线生机If you say that someone has a fighting chance of success, you are emphasizing that they have some chance of success after a hard struggle. (经过努力奋斗可能获得成功的)一线生机
