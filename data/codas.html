coda
koʊdə ˈkoʊdə
Word forms: plural codas
1 N-COUNT (书或演讲的)结尾，结束语 A coda is a separate passage at the end of something such as a book or a speech that finishes it off. (书或演讲的)结尾，结束语
2 N-COUNT (乐曲的)尾声，结束乐段 In music, a coda is the final part of a fairly long piece of music which is added in order to finish it off in a pleasing way. (乐曲的)尾声，结束乐段
