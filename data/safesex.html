safe sex
(also safer sex)
N-UNCOUNT (通常指使用安全套的)安全性交 Safe sex is sexual activity in which people protect themselves against the risk of AIDS and other diseases, usually by using condoms. (通常指使用安全套的)安全性交

  • People who have multiple sexual partners are now more apt to practise safe sex.
