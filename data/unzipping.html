unzip
ʌnzɪp ʌnˈzɪp ʌnˈzɪp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense unzips, present participle unzipping, past tense , past participle unzipped
1 V-ERG 拉开（拉链） When you unzip something which is fastened by a zip or when it unzips, you open it by pulling open the zip. 拉开（拉链）

  • James unzipped his bag. [VERB noun]

    詹姆斯把自己包上的拉链拉开。

  • This padded changing bag unzips to form a convenient and comfortable mat for nappy changing. [VERB]

    这种加厚型妈咪袋把拉链打开就是一块方便、舒适的垫子，可给宝宝换尿布。

2 VERB 解压缩 To unzip a computer file means to open a file that has been compressed. 解压缩 [computing]

  • Unzip the icons into a sub-directory. [VERB noun]

    把图标解压到一个子目录里。
