blow-dry ˈbloʊˌdraɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blow-dries, present participle blow-drying, past tense , past participle blow-dried
VERB （用吹风机）吹干（头发）；把（头发）吹出发型 If you blow-dry your hair, you dry it with a hairdryer, often to give it a particular style. （用吹风机）吹干（头发）；把（头发）吹出发型

  • I find it hard to blow-dry my hair. [VERB noun]

    我发现我的头发很难吹干。

  • He has blow-dried blonde hair. [VERB-ed]

    他有一头精心吹出发型的金发。

Blow-dry is also a noun.

  • The price of a cut and blow-dry varies widely.

    剪发并吹干的价格高低不等。
