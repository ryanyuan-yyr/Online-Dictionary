endgame
endgeɪm ˈɛndˌɡeɪm ˈɛndˌgeɪm
Word forms: plural endgames
1 N-VAR （国际象棋中的）残局 In chess, endgame refers to the final stage of a game, when only a few pieces are left on the board and one of the players must win soon. （国际象棋中的）残局
2 N-COUNT （战争、争论、竞赛等的）最后阶段 Journalists sometimes refer to the final stages of something such as a war, dispute, or contest, as an endgame. （战争、争论、竞赛等的）最后阶段 [journalism]

  • The political endgame is getting closer.

    政治活动正在临近尾声。
