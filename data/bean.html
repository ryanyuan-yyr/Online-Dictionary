bean
biːn bin
Word forms: plural beans
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, usually adjective NOUN] 豆；豆子；豆荚 Beans such as green beans, French beans, or broad beans are the seeds of a climbing plant or the long thin cases which contain those seeds. 豆；豆子；豆荚
2 N-COUNT [usually plural, usually n NOUN] 干豆 Beans such as soya beans and kidney beans are the dried seeds of a bean plant. 干豆
3 N-COUNT [usually plural, usually n NOUN] （咖啡）豆；（可可）豆；（巧克力）豆 Beans such as coffee beans or cocoa beans are the seeds of plants that are used to produce coffee, cocoa, and chocolate. （咖啡）豆；（可可）豆；（巧克力）豆
4 N-COUNT 烘豆 Beans are baked beans . 烘豆

  • ...sausage and beans.

    香肠和烘豆

5 N-SING （一文）钱 If someone has not got a bean, they have no money at all. （一文）钱 [British, informal]

  • It's quite incredible to think that he now hasn't got a bean.

    很难相信他现在身无分文。

  • It doesn't cost a bean.

    这一分钱也不用花。

6 PHRASE 充满活力的；兴高采烈的；生机勃勃的 If someone is full of beans, they are very lively and have a lot of energy and enthusiasm. 充满活力的；兴高采烈的；生机勃勃的

  • Jem was full of beans after a long sleep.

    杰姆好好睡了一觉之后又变得精力充沛了。

7 PHRASE 泄露秘密 If you spill the beans, you tell someone something that people have been trying to keep secret. 泄露秘密
SYN blab, inform, squeal [slang] , tell all
