apologia
æpəloʊdʒiə ˌæpəˈləʊdʒɪə ˌæpəˈloʊdʒiə
Word forms: plural apologias
N-COUNT [usually singular] 辩解；辩护；自辩书 An apologia is a statement in which you defend something that you strongly believe in, for example a way of life, a person's behaviour, or a philosophy. 辩解；辩护；自辩书 [formal]

  • The left have seen the work as an apologia for privilege and property.

    左派将此作品看成是为特权和所有权作的辩护。

SYN defence, case, argument, explanation
