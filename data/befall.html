befall
bɪfɔːl biˈfɔl bɪˈfɔl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense befalls, present participle befalling, past tense befell, past participle befallen
VERB （不幸或不好的事）降临到…头上，发生在…身上 If something bad or unlucky befalls you, it happens to you. （不幸或不好的事）降临到…头上，发生在…身上 [literary]

  • ...the disaster that befell the island of Flores. [VERB noun]

    降临到弗洛里斯岛的灾难

SYN happen to, fall upon, occur in, take place in
