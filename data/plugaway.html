plug away
PHRASAL VERB 埋头苦干；孜孜不倦地做 If you plug away, you keep trying very hard to do something or achieve something even though you find it difficult. 埋头苦干；孜孜不倦地做

  • My confidence is still there and I'll just keep plugging away. [VERB PREP.]

    我仍有信心，会继续坚持不懈。

SYN slog away, labour, toil away, grind away [informal]
