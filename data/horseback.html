horseback
hɔːʳsbæk ˈhɔrsˌbæk
1 N-UNCOUNT [usu on/by N] 马背 If you do something on horseback, you do it while riding a horse. 马背

  • In remote mountain areas, voters arrived on horseback.

    在偏远山区，选民骑着马赶来了。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 在马背上的;骑马的 A horseback ride is a ride on a horse. 在马背上的;骑马的

  • ...a horseback ride into the mountains.

    骑马进山

Horseback is also an adverb.

  • Many people in this area ride horseback.

    这个地区有很多人骑马。
