mortality
mɔːʳtælɪti mɔrˈtæləti
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 死亡人数;死亡率 The mortality in a particular place or situation is the number of people who die. 死亡人数;死亡率

  • ...the relationships between mortality and unemployment.

    死亡人数和失业人数之间的关系

  • The nation's infant mortality rate has reached a record low.

    该国的婴儿死亡率已降至历史最低水平。
