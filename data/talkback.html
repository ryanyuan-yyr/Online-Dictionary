talk back
PHRASAL VERB 回嘴;顶撞;犟嘴 If you talk back to someone in authority such as a parent or teacher, you answer them in a rude way. 回嘴;顶撞;犟嘴

  • How dare you talk back to me!. [VERB PREP. + to]

    你竟敢跟我顶嘴!

  • I talked back and asked questions. [VERB PREP.]

    我顶了嘴，反问了几句。

SYN answer back, argue, be rude, be cheeky
