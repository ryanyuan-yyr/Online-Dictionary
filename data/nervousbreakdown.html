nervous breakdown
Word forms: plural nervous breakdowns
N-COUNT 神经崩溃;精神崩溃 If someone has a nervous breakdown, they become extremely depressed and cannot cope with their normal life. 神经崩溃;精神崩溃

  • His wife would not be able to cope and might suffer a nervous breakdown.

    他的妻子将无法接受，也许会精神崩溃。

SYN collapse, breakdown, crack-up [informal] , neurasthenia [obsolete]
