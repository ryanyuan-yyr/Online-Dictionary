eviction
ɪvɪkʃən
Word forms: plural evictions
N-VAR 驱逐;驱赶 Eviction is the act or process of officially forcing someone to leave a house or piece of land. 驱逐;驱赶

  • He was facing eviction, along with his wife and family.

    他和妻子、家人正面临着被驱逐。

  • He also criticised the evictions for being violent and ruthless.

    他也批评这些驱逐暴力无情。

  • ...an eviction order.

    驱逐令

SYN expulsion, removal, clearance, ouster [law]
