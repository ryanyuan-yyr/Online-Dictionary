chase down
1 PHRASAL VERB 追到;抓到 If you chase someone down, you run after them or follow them quickly and catch them. 追到;抓到 [mainly US]

  • Ness chased the thief down and held him until police arrived. [VERB noun PREP.]

    内斯追上小偷，按住他，一直等到警察赶到。

  • For thousands of years chasing down game was the main activity in which humans were involved. [VERB PREP. noun]

    狩猎是数千年中人类从事的主要活动。

2 PHRASAL VERB (搜寻后)找到，找出 If you chase someone or something down, you manage to find them after searching for them. (搜寻后)找到，找出

  • That's when I chased her down to be the singer in my band. [VERB noun PREP.]

    就是那时候，我找到了她，让她在我的乐队里担任歌手。

  • Bank officials argued that it is not their job to chase down every asset of every bank debtor. [VERB PREP. noun]

    银行官员称，他们没有义务追踪每个贷款户的每笔资产。
