unashamed
ʌnəʃeɪmd ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmd
ADJ 不害羞的；不难为情的；无羞耻心的 If you describe someone's behaviour or attitude as unashamed, you mean that they are open and honest about things that other people might find embarrassing or shocking. 不害羞的；不难为情的；无羞耻心的

  • I grinned at him in unashamed delight.

    我高兴地朝他笑了笑，一点也不觉得难为情。

  • ...a man rightly unashamed of his own talent. [+ of]

    对自己的能力毫不避讳的人

SYN blatant, open, frank, plain  
unashamedly

  • Drugs are sold unashamedly in broad daylight.

    在光天化日之下公然出售毒品。

  • ...an unashamedly traditional view of geology.

    极其传统的地质学观点
