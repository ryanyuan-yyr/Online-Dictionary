via
vaɪə viːə ˈvaɪə ˈvaɪə ˈviə
1 PREP 经由;经过 If you go somewhere via a particular place, you go through that place on the way to your destination. 经由;经过

  • We drove via Lovech to the old Danube town of Ruse.

    我们开车经由洛维奇到达了多瑙河畔的古镇鲁塞。

  • Mr Baker will return home via Britain and France.

    贝克先生将取道英国和法国回国。

SYN through, by way of, by, by means of  
2 PREP 通过;凭借;靠 If you do something via a particular means or person, you do it by making use of that means or person. 通过;凭借;靠

  • The technology to allow relief workers to contact the outside world via satellite already exists.

    能够让救援人员通过卫星与外界联系的技术已经存在。

  • Translators can now work from home, via electronic mail systems.

    翻译人员现在可以利用电子邮件系统在家里工作了。

  • The executive's meeting had finished and Sir Marcus had reported its conclusions to the prime minister via Richard Ryder.

    领导层会议已经结束，马库斯爵士已通过理查德·赖德把会议结论报告给了首相。

SYN using, by means of, with the help of
