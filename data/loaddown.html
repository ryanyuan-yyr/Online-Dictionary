load down
PHRASAL VERB （尤指以重物）使（某人）大量承受 If you load someone down with things, especially heavy things, you give them a large number of them or put a large number of them on them. （尤指以重物）使（某人）大量承受 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • She loaded me down with around a dozen cassettes. [V n P with n]

    她塞给我十几盒磁带。

  • They had come up from London loaded down with six suitcases. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    他们拖着6只箱子从伦敦过来。

SYN burden, worry, trouble, hamper
