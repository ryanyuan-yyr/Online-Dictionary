tot up
PHRASAL VERB 计算…的总和;把…加起来 To tot up a total or a list of numbers means to add up several numbers in order to reach a total. 计算…的总和;把…加起来 [mainly British] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I finally sat down to tot up the full extent of my debt. [V P n + of

    我终于坐下来把我的全部债务合计了一下。

  • Now tot up the points you've scored. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    现在把你所得的分数相加。

SYN add up, calculate, sum (up), total
