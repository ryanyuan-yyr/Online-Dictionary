rim
rɪm rɪm
Word forms: plural rims
1 N-COUNT （容器的）边沿，边缘，外缘 The rim of a container such as a cup or glass is the edge that goes all the way round the top. （容器的）边沿，边缘，外缘

  • She looked at him over the rim of her glass. [+ of]

    她的目光越过玻璃杯的边沿看着他。

SYN edge, lip, brim, flange  
2 N-COUNT （圆形物体的）边框，边缘 The rim of a circular object is its outside edge. （圆形物体的）边框，边缘

  • ...a round mirror with white metal rim.

    镶着白色金属边框的圆镜子

SYN border, edge, trim, circumference  
3 N-COUNT [usually NOUN of n] (污垢等)一周，一圈 If there is a rim of dirt around a surface there is a dirty mark around it. (污垢等)一周，一圈

  • There was already a rim of dark hairs and soap round the basin.

    洗脸盆边上已经有一圈黑头发和肥皂沫。

4 N-COUNT [usually the NOUN] （篮球的）篮筐，篮圈 In basketball, the rim is the metal ring that holds the net through which players have to try to throw the ball to score. （篮球的）篮筐，篮圈
5 See also: rimmed  -rimmed
