inbred
ɪnbred ˈɪnˈbrɛd ˈɪnˌbrɛd
1 ADJ 同 inborn Inbred means the same as inborn . 同 inborn

  • She had that inbred politeness, it was a part of her.

    她拥有那种天生的优雅，举手投足都彬彬有礼。

  • ...behaviour patterns that are inbred.

    与生俱来的行为模式

SYN innate, natural, constitutional, native  
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 同系交配的;近亲繁殖的 People who are inbred have ancestors who are all closely related to each other. 同系交配的;近亲繁殖的

  • The whole population is so inbred that no genetic differences remain.

    整个族群都是同系繁殖，以至于根本不存在基因差异。

  • He came from an old inbred family.

    他来自一个古老的近亲通婚的家族。
