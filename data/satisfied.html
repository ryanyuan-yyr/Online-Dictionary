satisfied
sætɪsfaɪd ˈsætɪsfaɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 满意的;满足的;称心的 If you are satisfied with something, you are happy because you have got what you wanted or needed. 满意的;满足的;称心的

  • We are not satisfied with these results. [+ with]

    我们对这些结果并不满意。

  • ...satisfied customers.

    心满意足的顾客

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ, oft ADJ that] 确信的;信服的 If you are satisfied that something is true or has been done properly, you are convinced about this after checking it. 确信的;信服的

  • People must be satisfied that the treatment is safe.

    人们必须确信这种治疗方法是安全的。

SYN sure, smug, certain, convinced
