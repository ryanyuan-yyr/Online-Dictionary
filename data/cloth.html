cloth
klɒθ klɔːθ klɔθ klɑθ
Word forms: plural cloths
1 N-MASS (尤用于制衣的)布，织物，布料 Cloth is fabric which is made by weaving or knitting a substance such as cotton, wool, silk, or nylon. Cloth is used especially for making clothes. →see usage note at: clothes (尤用于制衣的)布，织物，布料

  • She began cleaning the wound with a piece of cloth.

    她开始用一块布清洗伤口。

2 N-COUNT (用于特定用途的)布 A cloth is a piece of cloth which you use for a particular purpose, such as cleaning something or covering something. (用于特定用途的)布

  • Clean the surface with a damp cloth.

    用湿布清洁表面。

  • ...a tray covered with a cloth.

    盖着布的托盘

3 N-SING (统称)牧师，神父 The cloth is sometimes used to refer to Christian priests and ministers. (统称)牧师，神父

  • I've got as much respect for the cloth as the next man.

    我和任何人一样都对神父充满敬意。

  • ...a man of the cloth.

    一名教士
