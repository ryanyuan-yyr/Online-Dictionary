relevant
reləvənt ˈrɛlɪvənt ˈrɛləvənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 相关的;相应的;有重要性的 Something that is relevant to a situation or person is important or significant in that situation or to that person. 相关的;相应的;有重要性的

  • We are trying to make politics more relevant to younger people.

    我们试图使政治活动更加贴近年轻人。

  • We have passed all relevant information on to the police.

    我们已经把所有相关信息都告知了警方。

SYN pertinent, fitting, fit, material  
2 ADJ 适当的;合适的;确切的 The relevant thing of a particular kind is the one that is appropriate. 适当的;合适的;确切的

  • Make sure you enclose all the relevant certificates.

    要保证把所有相关证书都放在里面。

SYN significant, appropriate, proper, related
