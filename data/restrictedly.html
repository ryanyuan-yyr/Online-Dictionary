restricted
rɪstrɪktɪd rɪˈstrɪktɪd rɪˈstrɪktɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 有限的;很少的 Something that is restricted is quite small or limited. 有限的;很少的

  • ...the monotony of a heavily restricted diet.

    受到严格限制的饮食的单调

  • Plants, like animals, often have restricted habitats.

    植物和动物一样，生活环境常常有限。

2 ADJ 仅限…使用的;仅限…所有的 If something is restricted to a particular group, only members of that group have it. If it is restricted to a particular place, it exists only in that place. 仅限…使用的;仅限…所有的

  • Discipline problems are by no means restricted to children in families dependent on benefits. [+ to]

    绝不只是依靠救济金生活的家庭的孩子才有纪律问题。

  • The problem is not restricted to the southeast.

    这个问题并不只存在于东南部。

3 ADJ-GRADED (地区)不对外开放的 A restricted area is one that only people with special permission can enter. (地区)不对外开放的

  • ...a highly restricted area close to the old Khodinka airfield.

    老科丁卡机场附近的一个禁区

4 ADJ (文件等)保密的，仅限于内部的 A restricted document is one that only people with special permission can read. (文件等)保密的，仅限于内部的 [British]

  • The charge was that, in February 1975, he had leaked a restricted document to a journalist.

    对他的指控是他在1975年2月将一份保密文件泄露给了一位记者。
