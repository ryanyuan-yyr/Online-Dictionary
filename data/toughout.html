tough out
PHRASAL VERB 将…坚持到底;挨过 If you tough out a difficult situation, you do not give in or show any weakness in that situation. 将…坚持到底;挨过

  • I think it was very brave of him to tough it out… [VERB noun PREP.]

    我认为他坚持到底非常勇敢。

  • Cabinet ministers signalled their determination to tough out the controversy. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    内阁大臣们表明他们将不顾争议坚持下去。
