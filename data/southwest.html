southwest
saʊθwɛst ˌsaʊθˈwɛst ˌsaʊˈwɛst ˌsaʊθˈwɛst ˌsaʊwɛst
1 N-UNCOUNT [also the N] 西南;西南方 The south-west is the direction which is halfway between south and west. 西南;西南方

  • ...the village of Popplewell, some six miles to the south-west.

    位于西南方约6英里处的波普尔维尔村

2 N-SING (地方、国家或地区的)西南部 The south-west of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-west. (地方、国家或地区的)西南部

  • ...the mountains in the south west of the USA.

    美国西南部的山脉

3 ADV 向西南方;朝西南 If you go south-west, you travel towards the south-west. 向西南方;朝西南

  • We took a plane south-west across the Anatolian plateau to Cappadocia.

    我们乘飞机向着西南方向越过安纳托利亚高原，前往卡帕多西亚。

4 ADV 位于…的西南方;在…的西南 Something that is south-west of a place is positioned to the south-west of it. 位于…的西南方;在…的西南

  • ...a gold mine at Orkney, south-west of Johannesburg.

    位于约翰内斯堡西南奥克尼地区的一座金矿

5 ADJ 西南部的；西南方的 The south-west part of a place, country, or region is the part which is towards the south-west. 西南部的；西南方的

  • ...a light aircraft crash near Stranraer in South-West Scotland.

    在苏格兰西南部斯特兰拉尔附近发生的一次轻型飞机的坠机事故

  • ...in the south-west corner of my garden.

    在我家花园的西南角

6 ADJ (风)来自西南方的 A south-west wind is a wind that blows from the south-west. (风)来自西南方的
