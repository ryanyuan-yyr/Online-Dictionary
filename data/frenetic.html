frenetic
frɪnetɪk frɪˈnɛtɪkəl frəˈnɛtɪk
ADJ-GRADED 发狂似的;狂热的 If you describe an activity as frenetic, you mean that it is fast and energetic, but rather uncontrolled. 发狂似的;狂热的

  • ...the frenetic pace of life in New York.

    纽约疯狂的生活节奏

  • This frenetic activity is the sign of a worried man.

    这种发了疯似的行为活动是一个人极度焦虑的表现。

SYN frantic, wild, excited, crazy  
frenetically

  • Steve and I worked frenetically to ensure that everything would go smoothly.

    我和史蒂夫发狂似的忙碌以保证一切顺利进行。
