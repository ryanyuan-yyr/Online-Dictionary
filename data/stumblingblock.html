stumbling block
Word forms: plural stumbling blocks
N-COUNT 障碍物;绊脚石 A stumbling block is a problem which stops you from achieving something. 障碍物;绊脚石

  • Perhaps the major stumbling block to reunification is the military presence in South Korea. [+ to/in]

    也许实现统一最大的绊脚石是驻扎在韩国的军队。

SYN obstacle, difficulty, problem, bar
