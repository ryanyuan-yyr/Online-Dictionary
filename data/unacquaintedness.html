unacquainted
ʌnəkweɪntɪd ˌʌnəˈkweɪntɪd
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ with n] 不熟悉…的；不知道…的 If you are unacquainted with something, you do not know about it or do not have any experience of it. 不熟悉…的；不知道…的

  • Professor Baker is unacquainted with the idea of representative democracy.

    贝克教授并不熟悉“代议制民主”这一思想。

  • I was then totally unacquainted with his poems. [+ with]

    当时我根本不知道他的诗。
