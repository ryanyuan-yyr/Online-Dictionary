approving
əpruːvɪŋ əˈpruːvɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （反应、话语等）表示赞许的，表示嘉许的 An approving reaction or remark shows support for something, or satisfaction with it. （反应、话语等）表示赞许的，表示嘉许的

  • His mother leaned forward and gave him an approving look.

    他的母亲探身向前，对他投以赞许的目光。

SYN favourable, admiring, applauding, respectful  
approvingly

  • He nodded approvingly.

    他点头表示赞许。
