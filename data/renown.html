renown
rɪnaʊn rɪˈnaʊn
N-UNCOUNT [oft n of N] 名望;声望 A person of renown is well known, usually because they do or have done something good. 名望;声望

  • She used to be a singer of some renown.

    她曾是位小有名气的歌手。

  • Mailly's work achieved little renown.

    梅利的工作没有获得什么声誉。

SYN fame, note, distinction, repute
