envelope
envəloʊp ɒn- ˈɛnvəˌləʊp ˈɑnvəˌloʊp ˈɛnˈvəˌloʊp
Word forms: plural envelopes
1 N-COUNT 信封 An envelope is the rectangular paper cover in which you send a letter to someone through the post. 信封
SYN wrapping, casing, case, covering  
2 PHRASE 挑战限制尺度；突破原有底线 If someone pushes the envelope, they do something to a greater degree or in a more extreme way than it has ever been done before. 挑战限制尺度；突破原有底线

  • There's a valuable place for fashion and design that pushes the envelope a bit.

    在原有尺度上有所突破的时尚和设计受到人们的重视。

  • No cinema director had a more profound effect in pushing the envelope of acceptable family entertainment.

    在挑战家庭娱乐片的接受程度方面，没有哪个电影导演比他的影响更深远。
