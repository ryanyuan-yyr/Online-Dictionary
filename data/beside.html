beside
bɪsaɪd biˈsaɪd bɪˈsaɪd
1 PREP 在…旁边；在…附近 Something that is beside something else is at the side of it or next to it. 在…旁边；在…附近

  • On the table beside an empty plate was a pile of books.

    桌上空盘子的旁边是一堆书。

  • I moved from behind my desk to sit beside her.

    我从桌子后面走到她身边坐了下来。

SYN next to, near, close to, neighbouring  
2See also:besides 
3 PHRASE 非常；极度 If you are beside yourself with anger or excitement, you are extremely angry or excited. beside the point→see: point  非常；极度

  • He had shouted down the phone at her, beside himself with anxiety.

    他焦急万分地对着电话那头的她大喊大叫。

  • Cathy was beside herself with excitement.

    凯茜兴奋异常。

Usage Note :

  • Do not confuse beside with besides.

    不要混淆 beside 和 besides。
