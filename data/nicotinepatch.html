nicotine patch
ˈnɪkətiːn pætʃ
Word forms: nicotine patches
N-COUNT 尼古丁贴片A nicotine patch is a small piece of sticky material that you can stick to your skin if you are trying to give up smoking. The patch slowly releases nicotine into your bloodstream so that your desire for tobacco is reduced. 尼古丁贴片
