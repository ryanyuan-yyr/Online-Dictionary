doomed
duːmd duːmd
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 注定的;命定的 If something is doomed to happen, or if you are doomed to a particular state, something unpleasant is certain to happen, and you can do nothing to prevent it. 注定的;命定的

  • Their plans seemed doomed to failure. [+ to]

    他们的计划好像注定要失败。

  • He knew that if he lived, he would be doomed to spend the war as a prisoner.

    他知道如果他活下来，他将注定以战俘的身份度过战争。

2 ADJ 注定要失败(或毁灭)的 Someone or something that is doomed is certain to fail or be destroyed. 注定要失败(或毁灭)的

  • Fireman battled through the smoke in a doomed attempt to rescue the children.

    消防队员奋力穿过烟雾企图去救孩子们，而这注定是要失败的。

  • I used to pour time and energy into projects that were doomed from the start.

    我过去常常将大量时间和精力投入到从开始就注定要失败的计划中。

SYN hopeless, condemned, ill-fated, fated
