betrayal
bɪtreɪəl
Word forms: plural betrayals
N-VAR 辜负；背叛；出卖 A betrayal is an action which betrays someone or something, or the fact of being betrayed. 辜负；背叛；出卖

  • She felt that what she had done was a betrayal of Patrick. [+ of]

    她觉得自己的所作所为是对帕特里克的背叛。

  • He acknowledged the sense of betrayal by civil rights leaders.

    他承认自己有被民权领袖出卖了的感觉。

SYN disloyalty, sell-out [informal] , deception, treason
