genitive
dʒenɪtɪv ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv
N-SING (语法中的）属格，所有格 In the grammar of some languages, the genitive, or the genitive case, is a noun case which is used mainly to show possession. In English grammar, a noun or name with 's added to it, for example 'dog's' or 'Anne's', is sometimes called the genitive form . (语法中的）属格，所有格
