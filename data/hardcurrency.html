hard currency
hɑːd ˈkʌrənsɪ
Word forms: plural hard currencies
N-VAR 硬通货，强势货币（指价值稳定的货币） A hard currency is one which is unlikely to lose its value and so is considered to be a good one to have or to invest in. 硬通货，强势货币（指价值稳定的货币）

  • The government is running short of hard currency to pay for imports.

    政府目前缺少可以支付进口产品的硬通货。
