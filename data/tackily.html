tacky
tæki ˈtæki
Word forms: comparative tackier, superlative tackiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 做工粗糙的;劣质的;俗气的 If you describe something as tacky, you dislike it because it is cheap and badly made or vulgar. 做工粗糙的;劣质的;俗气的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...a woman in a fake leopard-skin coat and tacky red sunglasses.

    身穿假豹皮大衣、戴着俗气的大红色太阳镜的女人

  • The whole thing is dreadfully tacky.

    整件东西糟糕透顶。

SYN seedy, shabby, shoddy  
2 ADJ-GRADED (油漆、胶水)发黏的，未干的 If something such as paint or glue is tacky, it is slightly sticky and not yet dry. (油漆、胶水)发黏的，未干的

  • Test to see if the finish is tacky, and if it is, leave it to harden.

    看看最后一道漆干了没有，要是没干，就先让它干透。

SYN sticky, wet, adhesive, gummy
