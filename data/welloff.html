well-off ˈwɛlˈɔf
(also well off)
ADJ-GRADED 富裕的;家境好的 Someone who is well-off is rich enough to be able to do and buy most of the things that they want. 富裕的;家境好的 [informal]

  • My grandparents were quite well off.

    我的祖父母很富有。

The well-off are people who are well-off.有钱人；富人

  • ...higher tax rates on the well-off.

    对有钱人征收的更高的税率
