documentary
dɒkjəmentri ˌdɑkjuˈmɛntəri ˌdɑkjəˈmɛntəri
Word forms: plural documentaries
1 N-COUNT （电视、广播等）纪实节目;纪录片 A documentary is a television or radio programme, or a film, which shows real events or provides information about a particular subject. （电视、广播等）纪实节目;纪录片

  • ...a TV documentary on homelessness.

    探讨无家可归问题的电视纪录片

SYN report, film, programme, account  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （证据）有文件记录的，来源于文件的，书面的 Documentary evidence consists of things that are written down. （证据）有文件记录的，来源于文件的，书面的

  • We have documentary evidence that they were planning military action.

    我们有书面证据证明他们正在策划军事行动。

SYN documented, recorded, registered, archived
