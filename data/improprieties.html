impropriety
ɪmprəpraɪɪti ˌɪmprəˈpraɪɪtɪ ˌɪmproʊˈpraɪəti
Word forms: plural improprieties
N-VAR 不合适举止;不正当行为 Impropriety is improper behaviour. 不合适举止;不正当行为 [formal]

  • He resigned amid allegations of financial impropriety.

    他在财务作假的质疑声中辞职了。

SYN lapse, mistake, slip, blunder
