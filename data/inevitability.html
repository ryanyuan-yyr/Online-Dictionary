inevitability
ɪnevɪtəbɪlɪti
Word forms: plural inevitabilities
N-VAR 不可避免;无法规避;必然发生 The inevitability of something is the fact that it is certain to happen and cannot be prevented or avoided. 不可避免;无法规避;必然发生

  • We are all bound by the inevitability of death. [+ of]

    我们早晚都会有一死。

  • Success is an inevitability for us.

    胜利对我们来说是必然的。

SYN certainty, fate, shoo-in [US , Canadian]
