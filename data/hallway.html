hallway
hɔːlweɪ ˈhɔlˌweɪ
Word forms: plural hallways
1 N-COUNT （大楼里的）走廊，过道 A hallway in a building is a long passage with doors into rooms on both sides of it. （大楼里的）走廊，过道
2 N-COUNT （房子或公寓的）门厅 A hallway in a house or flat is the area just inside the front door, into which some of the other rooms open. （房子或公寓的）门厅 [British]
