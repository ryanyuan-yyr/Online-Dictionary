emetic
ɪmetɪk iˈmɛtɪk ɪˈmɛtɪk
Word forms: plural emetics
1 N-COUNT 催吐剂 An emetic is something that is given to someone to swallow, in order to make them vomit. 催吐剂
2 ADJ 催吐的;令人作呕的 Something that is emetic makes you vomit. 催吐的;令人作呕的

  • Caution: large doses of this remedy are emetic.

    注意：大量服用这种药物会引致呕吐。

SYN vomitory, vomitive
