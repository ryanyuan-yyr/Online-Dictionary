unrecorded
ʌnrɪkɔːʳdɪd ˌʌnrɪˈkɔːdɪd
ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 未被正式记录的 You use unrecorded to describe something that has not been written down or recorded officially, especially when it should have been. 未被正式记录的

  • The statistics don't reveal of course unrecorded crime.

    这项统计数据显然没有披露未登记的罪案。

  • Much of Poland's private industry goes unrecorded.

    波兰大多数私营企业都未注册在案。

  • Nothing is left unrecorded.

    没有未被记录的。
