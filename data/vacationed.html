vacation
vəkeɪʃən veɪˈkeɪʃən veɪ- veɪˈkeɪʃən vəˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense vacations, present participle vacationing, past tense , past participle vacationed
1 N-COUNT （大学或美国大中小学的）假期 A vacation is a period of the year when universities and colleges, and in the United States also schools, are officially closed. （大学或美国大中小学的）假期

  • During his summer vacation he visited Russia.

    暑假期间他去俄罗斯了。

  • Did you have a lot of reading during the vacation?

    放假期间你读了很多书吗？

SYN holiday(s), leave, break, rest  
2 N-COUNT [oft on/from N] 假日;休假 A vacation is a period of time during which you relax and enjoy yourself away from home. 假日;休假 [US]

  • They planned a late summer vacation in Europe.

    他们打算夏末去欧洲度假。

  • We went on vacation to Puerto Rico.

    我们去了波多黎各度假。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 holiday
3 N-UNCOUNT 休息日;假期 If you have a particular number of days' or weeks' vacation, you do not have to go to work for that number of days or weeks. 休息日;假期 [US]

  • The French get five to six weeks' vacation a year.

    法国人每年有5到6周的假期。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 holiday
4 VERB （离家在某地）度假 If you are vacationing in a place away from home, you are on vacation there. （离家在某地）度假 [US]

  • Myles vacationed in Jamaica. [VERB prep./adv.]

    迈尔斯去了牙买加度假。

  • He was vacationing and couldn't be reached for comment. [VERB]

    他当时正在度假，无法联系到他征求意见。

SYN holiday, visit, travel, tour  
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 holiday
