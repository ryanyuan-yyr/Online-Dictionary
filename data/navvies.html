navvy
nævi ˈnævi
Word forms: plural navvies
N-COUNT 苦工;筑路工 A navvy is a person who is employed to do hard physical work, for example building roads or canals. 苦工;筑路工 [British, old-fashioned]

  • ...a blackened young navvy, swinging a pickaxe in the sweating tunnel.

    在闷热的隧道里挥舞着丁字镐干活的一个肤色黝黑的年轻挖土工

SYN labourer, worker, ganger, workman
