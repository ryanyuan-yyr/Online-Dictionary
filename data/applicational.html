application
æplɪkeɪʃən ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural applications
1 N-COUNT [NOUN to-infinitive] [oft on/upon N] 申请；请求；申请书；申请表 An application for something such as a job or membership of an organization is a formal written request for it. 申请；请求；申请书；申请表

  • His application for membership of the organisation was rejected. [+ for]

    他想要加入该组织的申请遭到了拒绝。

  • ...Turkey's application to join the European Community.

    土耳其要求加入欧共体的申请

  • Applications should be submitted as early as possible.

    申请表应尽早提交。

  • Tickets are available on application.

    票可以通过申请获得。

SYN request, claim, demand, appeal  
2 N-VAR 应用；运用 The application of a rule or piece of knowledge is the use of it in a particular situation. 应用；运用 [Also + to]

  • Students learned the practical application of the theory they had learned in the classroom. [+ of]

    学生们学会了将课堂上学到的理论付诸实际应用。

  • Simon's book provides a succinct outline of artificial intelligence and its application to robotics.

    西蒙的书简明扼要地概括了人工智能及其在机器人技术方面的应用。

SYN relevance, use, value, practice  
3 N-COUNT （计算机的）应用软件 In computing, an application is a piece of software designed to carry out a particular task. （计算机的）应用软件
4 N-UNCOUNT 勤奋;专心;努力 Application is hard work and concentration on what you are doing over a period of time. 勤奋;专心;努力

  • ...his immense talent, boundless energy and unremitting application.

    他横溢的才华、充沛的精力和不懈的努力

SYN effort, work, study, industry  
5 N-VAR 涂抹；敷用；施用 The application of something to a surface is the act or process of putting it on or rubbing it into the surface. 涂抹；敷用；施用

  • With repeated applications of weedkiller, the weeds were overcome. [+ of]

    经过反复施用除草剂，杂草得以彻底根除。

SYN utilization, use, practice, exercise
