unproductive
ʌnprədʌktɪv ˌʌnprəˈdʌktɪv
ADJ-GRADED 没有好结果的；徒然的 Something that is unproductive does not produce any good results. 没有好结果的；徒然的

  • Research workers are well aware that much of their time and effort is unproductive.

    研究人员很清楚，他们的很多时间和精力全都是白费。

  • ...increasingly unproductive land.

    越来越贫瘠的土地

SYN useless, futile, fruitless, vain
