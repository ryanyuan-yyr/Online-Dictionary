assemblage
əsemblɪdʒ əˈsɛmˌblɪdʒ ˌ ɑsɛmˈblɑʒ asɑ̃ˈblaʒ
Word forms: plural assemblages
N-COUNT （人或物的）组合，集合，聚集 An assemblage of people or things is a collection of them. （人或物的）组合，集合，聚集 [formal]

  • He had an assemblage of old junk cars filling the backyard. [+ of]

    他的后院里堆满了废旧汽车。

SYN group, company, meeting, body
