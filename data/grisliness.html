grisly
grɪzli ˈɡrɪzlɪ ˈgrɪzli
(also grizzly)Word forms: comparative grislier, superlative grisliest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 令人极度厌恶的；恐怖的；可怕的 also grizzly. Something that is grisly is extremely unpleasant, and usually involves death and violence. 令人极度厌恶的；恐怖的；可怕的

  • ...two horrifically grisly murders.

    两起骇人听闻的谋杀案

  • ...a grizzly crime.

    令人发指的罪行

SYN gruesome, shocking, terrible, awful
