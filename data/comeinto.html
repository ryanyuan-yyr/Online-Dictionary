come into
1 PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 继承(财产、封号等) If someone comes into some money, some property, or a title, they inherit it. 继承(财产、封号等)

  • My father has just come into a fortune in diamonds. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我父亲刚刚继承一大批钻石。

SYN inherit, be left, acquire, succeed to  
2 PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 在…中起作用 If someone or something comes into a situation, they have a role in it. 在…中起作用

  • We don't really know where Hortense comes into all this. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们真的不清楚霍滕斯在这中间起什么作用。

  • It's an unrestricted journey, and rules of any sort don't come into it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这是一次没有任何限制的旅行，所有的规则都不起作用。
