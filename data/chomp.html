chomp
tʃɒmp tʃɑmp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense chomps, present participle chomping, past tense , past participle chomped
VERB (人、动物进食时)大声地咬，嚼得很响 If a person or animal chomps their way through food or chomps on food, they chew it noisily. to chomp at the bit→see: bit  (人、动物进食时)大声地咬，嚼得很响

  • On the diet I would chomp my way through breakfast, even though I'm never hungry in the morning. [V + way through]

    根据这一饮食计划，早餐时我需要大吃一顿，尽管我早上从来不饿。

  • I chomped hungrily through the large steak. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我狼吞虎咽地吃着那一大块牛排。

  • I lost a tooth while chomping on a French baguette! [VERB prep./adv.]

    我啃法棍面包时，崩掉了一颗牙！
