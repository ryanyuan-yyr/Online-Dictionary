vibrate
vaɪbreɪt vaɪbreɪt ˈvaɪˌbreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vibrates, present participle vibrating, past tense , past participle vibrated
V-ERG （使）颤动;(使)振动;(使)抖动 If something vibrates or if you vibrate it, it shakes with repeated small, quick movements. （使）颤动;(使)振动;(使)抖动

  • The ground shook and the cliffs seemed to vibrate. [VERB]

    大地在摇晃，悬崖好像在颤动。

  • The noise vibrated the table. [VERB noun]

    噪音使桌子颤动起来。
