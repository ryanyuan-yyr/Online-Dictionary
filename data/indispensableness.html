indispensable
ɪndɪspensəbəl ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 必不可少的;不可或缺的 If you say that someone or something is indispensable, you mean that they are absolutely essential and other people or things cannot function without them. 必不可少的;不可或缺的

  • She was becoming indispensable to him. [+ to]

    他已变得越来越离不开她了。

  • ...the indispensable guide for any traveller in France.

    在法国对任何游客来说都不可缺少的向导

SYN essential, necessary, needed, key
