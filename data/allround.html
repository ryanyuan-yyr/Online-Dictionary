all-round
ˈɔlˈraʊnd
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 all-around
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 全能的；多面的；多才多艺的 An all-round person is good at a lot of different skills, academic subjects, or sports. 全能的；多面的；多才多艺的

  • He is a great all-round player.

    他是个出色的全能运动员。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 全面的；包括一切的 All-round means doing or relating to all aspects of a job or activity. 全面的；包括一切的

  • He demonstrated the all-round skills of a quarterback.

    他展示了一个枢纽前卫非常全面的技术。

  • ...if you want the best all-round quality of life.

    如果你想要全方位最优质的生活

  • ...an excellent all-round guide on how to shop for the very best foods.

    一本关于如何买到最佳食品的全面而实用的指南
