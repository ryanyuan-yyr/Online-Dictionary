seam
siːm sim
Word forms: plural seams
1 N-COUNT (两块布的)缝，线缝,接缝 A seam is a line of stitches which joins two pieces of cloth together. (两块布的)缝，线缝,接缝
2 N-COUNT 煤层 A seam of coal is a long, narrow layer of it underneath the ground. 煤层

  • The average UK coal seam is one metre thick.

    英国煤层的平均厚度为 1 米。

SYN layer, vein, stratum, lode  
3 PHRASE 停止正常运行;崩溃 If something is coming apart at the seams or is falling apart at the seams, it is no longer working properly and may soon stop working completely. 停止正常运行;崩溃

  • Britain's university system is in danger of falling apart at the seams.

    英国的大学体系面临分崩离析的危险。

4 PHRASE (场所)过于拥挤，人满为患 If a place is very full, you can say that it is bursting at the seams . (场所)过于拥挤，人满为患

  • The hotels of Warsaw, Prague and Budapest were bursting at the seams.

    华沙、布拉格和布达佩斯的旅馆均是人满为患。

SYN full (to bursting), crowded, packed, jammed
