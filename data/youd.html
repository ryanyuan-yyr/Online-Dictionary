you'd juːd jʊd jud
1 （尤指当had为助动词时，you had的常用口语形式） You'd is the usual spoken form of 'you had', especially when 'had' is an auxiliary verb. （尤指当had为助动词时，you had的常用口语形式）

  • I think you'd better tell us why you're asking these questions.

    我想你最好告诉我们你为什么要问这些问题。

2 （you would的常用口语形式） You'd is the usual spoken form of 'you would'. （you would的常用口语形式）

  • With your hair and your beautiful skin, you'd look good in red and other bright colors.

    以你的发色和漂亮的肤色，穿红色或者其他亮色的衣服会很好看的。
