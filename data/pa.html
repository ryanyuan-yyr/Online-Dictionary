p.a.
p.a. is a written abbreviation for (书面缩略=) per annum .

  • ...dentists with an average net income of £48,000 p.a.

    年均纯收入达4.8万英镑的牙医们

  • ... a yield of 10.5% p.a.

    10.5%的年收益

pa
pɑː pɑ pɔ
Word forms: plural pas
爸 Some people address or refer to their father as pa . 爸

  • 'Pa,' he said, 'I don't feel well.'.

    “爸，”他说，“我觉得不舒服。”

  • Pa used to be in the army.

    老爸当过兵。

PA
piːeɪ
Word forms: plural PAs
1 N-COUNT 同 personal assistant A PA is the same as a personal assistant . 同 personal assistant [business]
2 N-COUNT 有线广播系统；扩音系统 If you refer to the PA or the PA system in a place, you are referring to the public address system. 有线广播系统；扩音系统

  • A voice came booming over the PA.

    从有线广播系统中传出一个瓮声瓮气的声音。

  • Elvis comes on the coffee shop PA system.

    咖啡店的广播中播放着猫王的歌。
