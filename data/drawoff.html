draw off
PHRASAL VERB 抽出;排掉;汲出 If a quantity of liquid is drawn off from a larger quantity, it is taken from it, usually by means of a needle or pipe. 抽出;排掉;汲出 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The fluid can be drawn off with a syringe. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    可用注射器将液体汲出。

  • Doctors drew off a pint of his blood. [VERB PREP. noun]

    医生从他身上抽了一品脱的血。
