unaffected
ʌnəfektɪd ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪd ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 未受影响的；未被改变的 If someone or something is unaffected by an event or occurrence, they are not changed by it in any way. 未受影响的；未被改变的

  • She seemed totally unaffected by what she'd drunk. [+ by]

    她所喝的似乎对她没有丝毫影响。

  • The strike shut down 50 airports, but most international flights were unaffected.

    罢工迫使50个机场关闭，但大部分国际航班并未受到影响。

SYN impervious to, unchanged, untouched, unimpressed  
2 ADJ-GRADED 不矫揉造作的；自然的；率真的 If you describe someone as unaffected, you mean that they are natural and genuine in their behaviour, and do not act as though they are more important than other people. 不矫揉造作的；自然的；率真的 [approval]

  • ...this unaffected, charming couple.

    这对率真迷人的夫妇

SYN natural, genuine, unpretentious, simple
