dominion
dəmɪnjən dəˈmɪnjən dəˈmɪnjən doʊˈmɪnjən
Word forms: plural dominions
1 N-UNCOUNT 控制;统治(权);支配(权) Dominion is control or authority. 控制;统治(权);支配(权) [formal]

  • They truly believe they have dominion over us. [+ over]

    他们真的以为他们对我们有统治权。

SYN control, government, power, rule  
2 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 领土;版图;领地 A dominion is an area of land that is controlled by a ruler. 领土;版图;领地

  • The Republic is a dominion of the Brazilian people. [+ of]

    这个共和国是巴西人民的领土。

SYN kingdom, territory, province, country  
3 N-COUNT (旧时的)英联邦自治领 The Dominions were the nations which in the past were part of the British Empire but which had their own government. (旧时的)英联邦自治领
