baritone
bærɪtoʊn ˈbærəˌtoʊn ˈbɛrəˌtoʊn
Word forms: plural baritones
1 N-COUNT 男中音歌手；男中音歌唱家 In music, a baritone is a man with a fairly deep singing voice that is lower than that of a tenor but higher than that of a bass. 男中音歌手；男中音歌唱家

  • ...the young American baritone Monte Pederson.

    年轻的美国男中音歌唱家蒙特·佩德森

2 N-SING [oft NOUN n] 低沉悦耳的声音 If a man has a baritone speaking voice, his voice is low and pleasant to listen to. 低沉悦耳的声音

  • ...a baritone voice.

    低沉悦耳的嗓音

  • He speaks in a melodious baritone.

    他的声音浑厚而有磁性。
