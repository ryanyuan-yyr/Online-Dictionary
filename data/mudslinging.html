mud-slinging
N-UNCOUNT 诽谤;中伤 If you accuse someone of mud-slinging, you are accusing them of making insulting, unfair, and damaging remarks about their opponents. 诽谤;中伤 [disapproval]

  • There will be no mudslinging, they promise.

    他们许诺不会再有诽谤了。

  • Voters are disillusioned with the mud-slinging campaigns run by many candidates in recent years.

    选民们对近几年很多候选人在竞选活动中互相诋毁深感失望。
