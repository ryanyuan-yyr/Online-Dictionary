imprimatur
ɪmprɪmɑːtəʳ ˌɪmprɪˈmɑtər ˌɪmprɪˈmeɪtər
Word forms: plural imprimaturs
N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] (某人给予的)特许，批准(如准许其签名显示在产品上) If something such as a product has someone's imprimatur, that person has given it their official approval, for example by allowing their name to be shown on it. (某人给予的)特许，批准(如准许其签名显示在产品上)

  • ...a tennis racket bearing Andre Agassi's imprimatur.

    上有安德烈·阿加西特许签名的网球拍
