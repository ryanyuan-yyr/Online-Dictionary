merry-go-round ˈmɛrigoʊˌraʊnd
Word forms: plural merry-go-rounds
1 N-COUNT 旋转木马 A merry-go-round is a large circular platform at a fairground on which there are model animals or vehicles for people to sit on or in as it turns round. 旋转木马
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 一连串的相关活动;走马灯似的活动 You can refer to a continuous series of activities as a merry-go-round . 一连串的相关活动;走马灯似的活动

  • ...a merry-go-round of teas, fetes, musical events and the like. [+ of]

    一连串的茶会、游乐会、音乐活动以及诸如此类的事情

SYN whirl, round, series, succession
