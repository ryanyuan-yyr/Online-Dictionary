well-earned
(also well earned)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 理应得到的;当之无愧的 You can use well-earned to indicate that you think something is deserved, usually because the person who gets it has been working very hard. 理应得到的;当之无愧的

  • Take a well-earned rest and go out and enjoy yourself.

    你理应休息一下，出去好好玩玩。

  • ...his well-earned win in Sunday's race.

    周日的比赛中他当之无愧的胜利
