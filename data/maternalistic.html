maternal
mətɜːʳnəl məˈtɜːnəl məˈtɜrnəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 母亲般的;慈母般的 Maternal is used to describe feelings or actions which are typical of those of a kind mother towards her child. 母亲般的;慈母般的

  • She had little maternal instinct.

    她缺乏母性。

  • Her feelings towards him were almost maternal.

    她对他的感情是近乎母亲般的。

SYN motherly, protective, nurturing, maternalistic  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 母亲的;母性的 Maternal is used to describe things that relate to the mother of a baby. 母亲的;母性的

  • Maternal smoking can damage the unborn child.

    母亲吸烟会对胎儿造成不良影响。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 母亲一方的;母系的 A maternal relative is one who is related through a person's mother rather than their father. 母亲一方的;母系的

  • Her maternal grandfather was Mayor of Karachi.

    她的外公是卡拉奇市的市长。
