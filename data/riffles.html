riffle
rɪfəl ˈrɪfəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense riffles, present participle riffling, past tense , past participle riffled
VERB 快速翻阅;迅速浏览 If you riffle through the pages of a book or riffle them, you turn them over quickly, without reading everything that is on them. 快速翻阅;迅速浏览 [Also VERB n]

  • I riffled through the pages until I reached the index. [VERB + through]

    我快速翻至索引部分。
