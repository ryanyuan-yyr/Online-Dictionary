available
əveɪləbəl əˈveɪləbəl
1 ADJ 能找到的；可获得的 If something you want or need is available, you can find it or obtain it. 能找到的；可获得的

  • Since 1978, the amount of money available to buy books has fallen by 17%.

    自1978年以来，可用于采购图书的经费已经减少了17％。

  • There are three small boats available for hire. [+ for]

    有3艘小船可供出租。

  • According to the best available information, the facts are these.

    根据最可靠的消息，事实如下。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 有空的;有暇的 Someone who is available is not busy and is therefore free to talk to you or to do a particular task. 有空的;有暇的

  • Mr Leach is on holiday and was not available for comment. [+ for]

    利奇先生休假了，不方便作出评论。
