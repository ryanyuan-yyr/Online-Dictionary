terrorize
terəraɪz ˈtɛrərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense terrorizes, present participle terrorizing, past tense , past participle terrorized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 terrorise
VERB 使惊恐;恐吓;威胁 If someone terrorizes you, they keep you in a state of fear by making it seem likely that they will attack you. 使惊恐;恐吓;威胁

  • Bands of gunmen have hijacked food shipments and terrorized relief workers. [VERB noun]

    几伙持枪歹徒劫持了运输的食品，还恐吓了救援人员。

  • ...pensioners terrorised by anonymous telephone calls. [VERB-ed]

    受到匿名电话恐吓的领取养老金的人
