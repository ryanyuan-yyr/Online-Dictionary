perfume
pɜːʳfjuːm pəʳfjuːm pərˈfjum ˈpɜrˌfjum
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense perfumes, present participle perfuming, past tense , past participle perfumed
1 N-MASS 香水 Perfume is a pleasant-smelling liquid which women put on their skin to make themselves smell nice. 香水

  • The hall smelled of her mother's perfume.

    大厅里弥漫着她母亲身上的香水味。

  • ...a bottle of perfume.

    一瓶香水

  • ...the manufacture of soaps and perfumes.

    肥皂和香水的制造

SYN fragrance, scent, essence, incense  
2 N-MASS 香精；香料 Perfume is the ingredient that is added to some products to make them smell nice. 香精；香料

  • ...a delicate white soap without perfume.

    不含香料、味道淡雅的白色肥皂

  • ...a perfume for skin creams.

    润肤霜中使用的香料

3 N-COUNT [with poss] 芳香；香味；馨香 The perfume of something is the pleasant smell it has. 芳香；香味；馨香 [literary]

  • ...the perfume of roses.

    玫瑰花的芳香

  • There were two lemon trees and I paused to enjoy their perfume.

    那里有两棵柠檬树，我不禁驻足品味柠檬的芬芳。

SYN scent, smell, fragrance, bouquet  
4 VERB 使香气弥漫 If the smell of something perfumes a place or area, it makes it smell nice. 使香气弥漫 [literary]

  • Flowers started to perfume the air. [VERB noun]

    花儿的香气开始弥漫在空气中。

  • As they bake, they perfume the whole house with the aroma of apples and spices. [VERB noun with noun]

    他们烤蛋糕的时候，整幢房子都飘着苹果和香料的香气。

  • ...gardens perfumed with jasmine. [VERB noun with noun]

    茉莉飘香的花园

5 VERB 为…增加香味 If something is used to perfume a product, it is added to the product to make it smell nice. 为…增加香味

  • The oil is used to flavour and perfume soaps, foam baths, and scents. [VERB noun]

    这种精油用来给肥皂、沐浴液和香水添加香味。

  • ...shower gel perfumed with the popular Paris fragrance. [V-ed + with]

    添加了流行的巴黎香水的沐浴啫喱
