allure
əljʊəʳ əˈlʊə əlʊr əˈlʊr æˈlʊr
N-UNCOUNT 诱惑力；吸引力；魅力 The allure of something or someone is the pleasing or exciting quality that they have. 诱惑力；吸引力；魅力

  • It's a game that has really lost its allure.

    这是一项的确已失去了魅力的游戏。

  • ...the captivating allure of Isabelle Adjani.

    伊莎贝拉·阿佳妮令人倾倒的魅力

SYN attractiveness, appeal, charm, attraction
