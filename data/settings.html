setting
setɪŋ ˈsɛtɪŋ
Word forms: plural settings
1 N-COUNT 环境;背景;地点 A particular setting is a particular place or type of surroundings where something is or takes place. 环境;背景;地点

  • Rome is the perfect setting for romance. [+ for]

    罗马是最适合谈情说爱的地方。

  • Perth was the setting for the SNP's conference this year.

    珀斯是今年苏格兰国民党大会的召开地。

  • The house is in a lovely setting in the Malvern hills.

    房子坐落在莫尔文丘陵地区，周围景色迷人。

SYN surroundings, site, location, set  
2 N-COUNT (炉具、热水器等上可供调节的)挡，级，点，刻度 A setting is one of the positions to which the controls of a device such as a cooker, stove, or heater can be adjusted. (炉具、热水器等上可供调节的)挡，级，点，刻度

  • You can boil the fish fillets on a high setting.

    可以用高火煮无骨鱼片。

3 N-COUNT 一套餐具 A table setting is the complete set of equipment that one person needs to eat a meal, including knives, forks, spoons, and glasses. 一套餐具
