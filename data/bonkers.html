bonkers
bɒŋkəʳz ˈbɑŋkərz
ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 愚蠢的；疯狂的 If you say that someone is bonkers, you mean that they are silly or act in a crazy way. 愚蠢的；疯狂的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • The man must be bonkers to take such a risk.

    这人一定是疯了，居然去冒这种风险。

  • I nearly went bonkers with frustration.

    挫败感让我几近疯狂。
