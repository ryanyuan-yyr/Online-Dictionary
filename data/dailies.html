daily
deɪli ˈdeɪli
Word forms: plural dailies
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 每日;每天 If something happens daily, it happens every day. 每日;每天

  • Cathay Pacific flies daily non-stop to Hong Kong from Heathrow.

    国泰航空公司每天都有从希思罗机场直飞香港的航班。

  • The Visitor Centre is open daily 8.30 a.m. – 4.30 p.m.

    游客中心每日上午 8:30 至下午 4:30 开放。

Daily is also an adjective.

  • They held daily press briefings.

    他们每天召开新闻发布会。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 一天的;按天计算的 Daily quantities or rates relate to a period of one day. 一天的;按天计算的

  • ...a diet containing adequate daily amounts of fresh fruit.

    包含了足量一天所需新鲜水果的饮食

  • Our average daily turnover is about £300.

    我们的平均日营业额约为 300 英镑。

3 N-COUNT 日报 A daily is a newspaper that is published every day of the week except Sunday. 日报

  • Copies of the local daily had been scattered on a table.

    几份当地的日报散放在桌子上。

Daily is also an adjective.

  • He studied the daily papers.

    他仔细阅读这些日报。

4 PHRASE 日常生活 Your daily life is the things that you do every day as part of your normal life. 日常生活

  • ...the failure of the government to improve most people's daily lives.

    政府没能改善大多数人的日常生活
