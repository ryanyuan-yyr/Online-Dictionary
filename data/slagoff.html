slag off
PHRASAL VERB 贬损;臭骂;辱骂 To slag someone off means to criticize them in an unpleasant way. 贬损;臭骂;辱骂 [British, informal]

  • All bands slag off their record companies. It's just the way it is. [VERB PREP. noun]

    所有乐队都会臭骂自己的唱片公司，都这样。

  • People have been slagging me off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    人们一直在贬损我。
