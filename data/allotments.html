allotment
əlɒtmənt əˈlɑtmənt
Word forms: plural allotments
1 N-COUNT （英国城镇里租给个人种菜用的）小块园地 In Britain, an allotment is a small area of land in a town which a person rents to grow plants and vegetables on. （英国城镇里租给个人种菜用的）小块园地
2 N-COUNT 分配物；份额；拨款 An allotment of something is a share or amount of it that is given to someone. 分配物；份额；拨款

  • His meager allotment of gas had to be saved for emergencies. [+ of]

    他分到的那一点点煤气必须留着急用。

SYN assignment, share, measure, grant
