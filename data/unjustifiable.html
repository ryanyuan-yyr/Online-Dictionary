unjustifiable
ʌndʒʌstɪfaɪəbəl ʌndʒʌstɪfaɪəbəl
ADJ-GRADED （尤指伤害他人的行为）不能证明为正当的，无可辩解的，没有道理的 If you describe an action, especially one that harms someone, as unjustifiable, you mean there is no good reason for it. （尤指伤害他人的行为）不能证明为正当的，无可辩解的，没有道理的

  • Using these missiles to down civilian aircraft is simply immoral and totally unjustifiable.

    用这些导弹击落民用飞机是极不道德也是完全没道理的。

SYN inexcusable, wrong, indefensible, unforgivable  
unjustifiably

  • The press invade people's privacy unfairly and unjustifiably every day.

    新闻媒体每天都在不公正地且没有正当理由地侵犯人们的隐私。
