out-of-pocket ˌaʊtəvˈpɑkɪt
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （费用）自掏腰包垫付的 Out-of-pocket expenses are those which you pay out of your own money on behalf of someone else, and which are often paid back to you later. （费用）自掏腰包垫付的

  • I charge twenty dollars an hour plus out-of-pocket expenses.

    我每小时收取20美元，另加垫付的现金开支。
