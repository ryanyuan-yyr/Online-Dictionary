top out
PHRASAL VERB (价格等) 达到最高水平If something such as a price tops out at a particular amount, that is the highest amount that it reaches. (价格等) 达到最高水平 [US]

  • The stock topped out at more than $25.

    股票达到了最高价，超过$25。

  • Last Friday was a warm day, topping out at 85 degrees.

    上周五天气很暖和，最高气温达85华氏度。
