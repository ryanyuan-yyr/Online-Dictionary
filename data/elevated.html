elevated
elɪveɪtɪd ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （人、工作、角色等）重要的，地位高的 A person, job, or role that is elevated is very important or of very high rank. （人、工作、角色等）重要的，地位高的

  • His career has blossomed and that has given him a certain elevated status.

    他的事业蓬勃发展，这在一定程度上提升了他的地位。

SYN exalted, high, important, august  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （想法、思想等）高尚的，崇高的,睿智的 If thoughts or ideas are elevated, they are on a high moral or intellectual level. （想法、思想等）高尚的，崇高的,睿智的

  • ...the magazine's elevated British tone.

    杂志中高贵的英伦情调

SYN high-minded, high, fine, grand  
3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （土地、建筑等）高出周围的，高架的 If land or buildings are elevated, they are raised up higher than the surrounding area. （土地、建筑等）高出周围的，高架的

  • An elevated platform on the stage collapsed during rehearsals.

    在排练过程中舞台上垫高的一个平台塌了。
