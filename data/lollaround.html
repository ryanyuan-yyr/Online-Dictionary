loll around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 loll about
PHRASAL VERB 懒洋洋地坐着（或躺着） If you loll around or loll about, you enjoy yourself by sitting or lying in a relaxed way. 懒洋洋地坐着（或躺着）

  • Her husband dreams about lolling about on the deck and taking long snoozes in the afternoon sun. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    她丈夫梦想着自己懒洋洋地躺在甲板上，在午后的阳光下打长盹。

  • ...spending afternoons lolling around a swimming pool. [VERB PREP. noun]

    每天下午在泳池边闲躺着
