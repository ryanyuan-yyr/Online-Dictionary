plaque
plæk plɑːk plæk
Word forms: plural plaques
1 N-COUNT 匾额；饰板 A plaque is a flat piece of metal or stone with writing on it which is fixed to a wall or other structure to remind people of an important person or event. 匾额；饰板

  • After touring the hospital, Her Majesty unveiled a commemorative plaque.

    女王陛下巡视完这家医院后，为纪念匾揭了幕。

SYN plate, panel, medal, tablet  
2 N-UNCOUNT 牙斑；齿菌斑 Plaque is a substance containing bacteria that forms on the surface of your teeth. 牙斑；齿菌斑

  • Deposits of plaque build up between the tooth and the gum.

    牙齿和牙龈之间会滋生牙斑。
