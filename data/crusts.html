crust
krʌst krʌst
Word forms: plural crusts
1 N-COUNT 面包皮 The crust on a loaf of bread is the outside part. 面包皮
2 N-COUNT (馅饼的)酥皮 A pie's crust is its cooked pastry. (馅饼的)酥皮
3 N-COUNT (尤指较软或较湿的物质上的)硬层，硬皮 A crust is a hard layer of something, especially on top of a softer or wetter substance. (尤指较软或较湿的物质上的)硬层，硬皮

  • As the water evaporates, a crust of salt is left on the surface of the soil. [+ of]

    随着水分的蒸发，地面上留下了一层结晶盐。

SYN layer, covering, coating, incrustation  
4 N-COUNT 地壳 The earth's crust is its outer layer. 地壳

  • Earthquakes leave scars in the earth's crust.

    地震会在地壳上留下裂痕。

5 PHRASE [VERB inflects] (尤指通过做不愿意做的工作)谋生 If you earn a crust, you earn enough money to live on, especially by doing work you would prefer not to do. (尤指通过做不愿意做的工作)谋生 [British]

  • In his early days, he would do almost anything to earn a crust from the sport.

    早年为了能在体育界混口饭吃，他几乎什么都做。

6 See also: upper crust
