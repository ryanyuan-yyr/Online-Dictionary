celibate
selɪbət ˈsɛləbət
Word forms: plural celibates
1 ADJ (因宗教信仰)禁欲的，独身的 Someone who is celibate does not marry or have sex, because of their religious beliefs. (因宗教信仰)禁欲的，独身的

  • The Pope bluntly told the world's priests yesterday to stay celibate.

    教皇昨天直言全世界的神父都应该禁欲。

A celibate is someone who is celibate.独身主义者;禁欲者
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] (特定时期)禁欲的，节制性欲的 Someone who is celibate does not have sex during a particular period of their life. (特定时期)禁欲的，节制性欲的

  • I was celibate for two years.

    我有两年没有性生活。
