driving seat
ˈdraɪvɪŋ siːt
1 N-SING 驾驶员座位 In a vehicle such as a car or a bus, the driving seat is the seat where the person who is driving the vehicle sits. 驾驶员座位

  • He got into the driving seat and started the engine.

    他坐上了驾驶员的位置发动了引擎。

2 PHRASE 处于控制地位;主管;负责 If you say that someone is in the driving seat, you mean that they are in control in a situation. 处于控制地位;主管;负责

  • At 69 he is as firmly in the driving seat of the company as ever.

    在 69 岁时他仍一如既往地牢牢掌握着公司的控制权。
