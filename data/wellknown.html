well-known
ˈwɛlˈnoʊn
(also well known)
1 ADJ-GRADED 众所周知的;著名的;出名的 A well-known person or thing is known about by a lot of people and is therefore famous or familiar. If someone is well-known for a particular activity, a lot of people know about them because of their involvement with that activity. →see usage note at: famous 众所周知的;著名的;出名的

  • He surrounds himself with attractive, intelligent, or well-known people.

    他混迹于有魅力、有学问或有名气的人当中。

  • He is well-known to the local police.

    当地警察都知道他。

  • Hubbard was well known for his work in the field of drug rehabilitation.

    哈伯德因在戒毒康复领域所做的工作而为人所熟知。

2 ADJ-GRADED 为人所熟知的 A well-known fact is a fact that is known by people in general. 为人所熟知的

  • It may be a well-known fact, but I didn't know it.

    这可能是人所共知的事实，但我却不知道。

  • It is well known that bamboo shoots are a panda's staple diet.

    竹笋是熊猫的主要食物，这是人所共知的。

SYN familiar, common, established, popular
