variant
veəriənt ˈvɛərɪənt ˈvɛriənt ˈværiənt
Word forms: plural variants
N-COUNT 变体;变种;变化形式 A variant of a particular thing is something that has a different form to that thing, although it is related to it. 变体;变种;变化形式

  • The quagga was a strikingly beautiful variant of the zebra. [+ of]

    斑驴是一种极美丽的斑马变种。

  • An intriguing variant to pipelines would be to carry the water using the existing system of canals.

    除用管道外，利用现有的运河系统来输送水也很值得一试。

  • There are so many variant spellings of his name.

    他的名字有很多不同的拼写形式。

SYN variation, form, version, development
