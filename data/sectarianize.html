sectarian
sekteəriən sɛkˈtɛərɪən sɛkˈtɛriən
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 宗派的;教派的 Sectarian means resulting from the differences between different religions. 宗派的;教派的

  • He was the fifth person to be killed in sectarian violence.

    他是宗派暴力中的第 5 个罹难者。

  • The police said the murder was sectarian.

    警方称这起谋杀是由宗派纷争引起的。

  • ...both sides of the sectarian divide.

    宗派分歧的双方

SYN narrow-minded, partisan, fanatic, fanatical
