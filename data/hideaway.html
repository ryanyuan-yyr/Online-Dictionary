hideaway
haɪdəweɪ ˈhaɪdəˌweɪ
Word forms: plural hideaways
N-COUNT 躲藏处;藏匿处;退隐处 A hideaway is a place where you go to hide or to get away from other people. 躲藏处;藏匿处;退隐处

  • The bandits fled to a remote mountain hideaway.

    匪徒们逃到了一个偏远的山间藏身。

  • ...the £5 million hideaway holiday home on the exclusive island of Mustique.

    位于奢华的马斯蒂克岛上的价值500万英镑的幽静度假屋

SYN hiding place, haven, retreat, refuge
