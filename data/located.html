located
loʊkeɪtɪd ləʊˈkeɪtɪd loʊkeɪt-
ADJ [adverb ADJ] 位于；坐落于 If something is located in a particular place, it is present or has been built there. 位于；坐落于 [formal]

  • The restaurant is located near the cathedral.

    餐馆位于大教堂附近。

  • A boutique and beauty salon are conveniently located within the grounds. [+ within]

    时装店和美容院就在区内，非常方便。

  • ...well-located buildings.

    位置上佳的大厦

SYN situated, placed, positioned, sited
