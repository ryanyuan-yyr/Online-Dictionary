air-cooled ˈɛəˌkuːld ˈɛrˌkuld
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （引擎等）用空气冷却的，气冷的 An air-cooled engine is prevented from getting too hot when it is running by cool air that passes over it, rather than being cooled by a liquid. （引擎等）用空气冷却的，气冷的
