tap out
PHRASAL VERB 敲出，打出(节奏、密码、数字等) If you tap out a rhythm, a code, or a number, you indicate it by hitting a surface or a machine such as a telephone. 敲出，打出(节奏、密码、数字等)

  • Dermot joined her, his eyes on the dance floor, his fingers tapping out a rhythm on the table. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    德莫特加入了她的行列，眼睛盯着舞池，手指在桌子上敲着节拍。

  • I picked up the phone immediately and tapped out the number Julie had jotted down for me. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我立即拿起电话，拨打了朱莉帮我记下的号码。
