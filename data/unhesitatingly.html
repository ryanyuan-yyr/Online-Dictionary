unhesitatingly
ʌnhezɪteɪtɪŋli
ADV [usually ADVERB with verb] 果断地;毫不犹豫地 If you say that someone does something unhesitatingly, you mean that they do it immediately and confidently, without any doubt or anxiety. 果断地;毫不犹豫地

  • I would unhesitatingly choose the latter option.

    我会毫不犹豫地选择后者。

  • So is there any taboo she wouldn't touch? Unhesitatingly she replies, 'Politics.'

    那么有什么她不愿触及的禁忌吗？她毫不犹豫地回答：“政治。”
