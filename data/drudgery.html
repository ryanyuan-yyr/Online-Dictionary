drudgery
drʌdʒəri ˈdrʌdʒəri
N-UNCOUNT 单调乏味的苦差事;无聊的工作 You use drudgery to refer to jobs and tasks which are boring or unpleasant but which must be done. 单调乏味的苦差事;无聊的工作

  • People want to get away from the drudgery of their everyday lives.

    人们想摆脱日常生活中单调乏味的工作。

SYN labour, grind [informal] , sweat [informal] , hard work
