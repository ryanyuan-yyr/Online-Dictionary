stock up
1 →see: stock 5 
2 PHRASAL VERB 大量贮备;囤积 If you stock up on something, you buy a lot of it, in case you cannot get it later. 大量贮备;囤积

  • The authorities have urged people to stock up on fuel. [V P + on/with]

    当局已经敦促人们储备燃料。

  • New Yorkers have been stocking up with bottled water. [V P on/with n]

    纽约人一直在贮备瓶装水。

SYN fill, pack, load, cram
