burp
bɜːʳp bɜrp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense burps, present participle burping, past tense , past participle burped
VERB 打嗝 When someone burps, they make a noise because air from their stomach has been forced up through their throat. 打嗝

  • Charlie burped loudly. [VERB]

    查利大声地打嗝。

SYN belch, bring up wind, eructate, hiccup or hiccough  
Burp is also a noun.

  • There followed a barely audible burp.

    接着是几乎听不见的打嗝声。
