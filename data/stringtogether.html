string together
PHRASAL VERB 把…连起来;把…串在一起 If you string things together, you form something from them by adding them to each other, one at a time. 把…连起来;把…串在一起

  • As speech develops, the child starts to string more words together. [VERB noun PREP.]

    随着语言能力不断发展，孩子开始把更多的词连起来说。

  • The speaker strung together a series of jokes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    演说者讲了一连串的笑话。
