alimony
ælɪməni -moʊni ˈæləˌmoʊni
N-UNCOUNT （分居或离婚后根据法院指令定期付给对方的）赡养费 Alimony is money that a court of law orders someone to pay regularly to their former wife or husband after they have got divorced. （分居或离婚后根据法院指令定期付给对方的）赡养费

  • A great deal of Jeff's money went in alimony to his three former wives.

    杰夫的很大一部分钱都用在了向他的前三任妻子支付赡养费。

SYN maintenance, keep, support, allowance
