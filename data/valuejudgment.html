value judgment
Word forms: plural value judgments
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 value judgement
N-COUNT （根据个人观念而非事实作出的）价值判断 If you make a value judgment about something, you form an opinion about it based on your principles and beliefs and not on facts which can be checked or proved. （根据个人观念而非事实作出的）价值判断

  • Social scientists have grown extremely unwilling to make value judgments about cultures. [+ about]

    社会科学家们已经变得十分不愿意对文化作出价值判断。

  • That isn't a value judgement, it's a fact.

    那不是一个价值判断，而是一个事实。
