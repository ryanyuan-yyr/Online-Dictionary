liven up
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使)(某地或某事）有生气；（使）变热闹 If a place or event livens up, or if something livens it up, it becomes more interesting and exciting. （使)(某地或某事）有生气；（使）变热闹

  • How could we decorate the room to liven it up?. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们怎么才能把这房间装饰得更有生气呢？

  • The multicoloured rag rug was chosen to liven up the grey carpet. [VERB PREP. noun]

    选择这块五彩缤纷的碎呢小毯是为了给这灰色的地毯添些生气。

  • The arena livens up only on Saturdays and Sundays when a flea market is open there. [VERB PREP.]

    只有在周六周日跳蚤市场开市时，这片场地才会热闹起来。

2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）（某人）愉快起来；（使）活跃起来 If people liven up, or if something livens them up, they become more cheerful and energetic. （使）（某人）愉快起来；（使）活跃起来

  • Talking about her daughters livens her up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    谈到她的女儿时，她的心情愉快起来。

  • George livens up after midnight, relaxing a little. [VERB PREP.]

    午夜过后，乔治来了劲，人也活跃起来。
