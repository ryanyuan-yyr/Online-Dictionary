subscriber
səbskraɪbəʳ
Word forms: plural subscribers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (杂志或报纸的)订阅人，订购者，订户 A magazine's or a newspaper's subscribers are the people who pay to receive copies of it regularly. (杂志或报纸的)订阅人，订购者，订户

  • I have been a subscriber to Railway Magazine for many years. [+ to]

    我订阅《铁路杂志》已有很多年了。

SYN reader, customer, regular reader  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 消费者;用户 Subscribers to a service are the people who pay to receive the service. 消费者;用户

  • China has almost 15 million subscribers to satellite and cable television. [+ to]

    中国有将近 1,500 万卫星电视和有线电视用户。

3 N-COUNT [usually plural, oft N to n] (慈善机构、活动的)定期捐款者，定期捐助者;(组织的)定期缴费者，会员 The subscribers to a charity, campaign, or organization are the people who support it by sending money regularly to it. (慈善机构、活动的)定期捐款者，定期捐助者;(组织的)定期缴费者，会员
