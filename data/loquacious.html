loquacious
ləkweɪʃəs lɒˈkweɪʃəs loʊˈkweɪʃəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 喋喋不休的；话多的；饶舌的 If you describe someone as loquacious, you mean that they talk a lot. 喋喋不休的；话多的；饶舌的 [formal]

  • The normally loquacious Mr O'Reilly has said little.

    平常话多的奥赖利先生几乎没说什么。

SYN talkative, chattering, babbling, chatty
