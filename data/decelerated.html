decelerate
diːseləreɪt diˈsɛlərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense decelerates, present participle decelerating, past tense , past participle decelerated
1 VERB 降低速度;减速行驶（或动转） When a vehicle or machine decelerates or when someone in a vehicle decelerates, the speed of the vehicle or machine is reduced. 降低速度;减速行驶（或动转）

  • ...the sensation of the train decelerating. [VERB]

    列车正在减速的感觉

deceleration

  • The harder the brake pedal is pressed, the greater the car's deceleration.

    刹车踩得越用力，车子减速越猛。

2 VERB (通货膨胀、经济增长等的速度)减缓，放慢 When the rate of something such as inflation or economic growth decelerates, it slows down. (通货膨胀、经济增长等的速度)减缓，放慢

  • Inflation has decelerated remarkably over the past two years. [VERB]

    过去的两年里通货膨胀已经明显放缓。

deceleration

  • ...a significant deceleration in the annual rate of growth.

    年增长率的显著下降
