disunity
dɪsjuːnɪti dɪsˈjunəti
N-UNCOUNT 不统一;不和;分裂 Disunity is lack of agreement among people which prevents them from working together effectively. 不统一;不和;分裂 [formal]

  • He had been accused of promoting disunity within the armed forces.

    人们指责他在军队内部搞分裂。

SYN disagreement, split, breach, dissent
