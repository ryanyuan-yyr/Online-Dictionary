turn back
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)返回;(使)折回 If you turn back or if someone turns you back when you are going somewhere, you change direction and go towards where you started from. (使)返回;(使)折回 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She turned back towards the crossroads. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    她折回十字路口。

  • They were very nearly forced to turn back. [VERB PREP.]

    他们差点被迫返回。

  • Police attempted to turn back protesters marching towards the offices of President Ershad. [VERB PREP. noun]

    警方试图迫使向艾尔沙德总统官邸进发的抗议者原路返回。

SYN go back, return, retreat, retrace your steps  
2 PHRASAL VERB 无法改变计划;无法收回成命 If you cannot turn back, you cannot change your plans and decide not to do something, because the action you have already taken makes it impossible. 无法改变计划;无法收回成命

  • The administration has now endorsed the bill and can't turn back. [VERB PREP.]

    政府部门如今已经签署了那项法案，没有挽回的余地。
