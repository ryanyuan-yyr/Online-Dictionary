bask
bɑːsk bæsk bæsk bɑsk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense basks, present participle basking, past tense , past participle basked
1 VERB （舒适地）晒太阳（取暖） If you bask in the sunshine, you lie somewhere sunny and enjoy the heat. （舒适地）晒太阳（取暖）

  • All through the hot, still days of their holiday Amy basked in the sun. [VERB + in]

    在整个炎热、安静的假日，埃米一直在晒太阳。

  • Crocodiles bask on the small sandy beaches. [VERB]

    鳄鱼在小沙滩上晒太阳。

SYN lie, relax, lounge, sprawl  
2 VERB 沉浸于，尽情享受（赞美、宠爱或崇拜） If you bask in someone's approval, favour, or admiration, you greatly enjoy their positive reaction towards you. 沉浸于，尽情享受（赞美、宠爱或崇拜）

  • He has spent a month basking in the adulation of the fans back in Jamaica. [VERB + in]

    他整整一个月都沉浸在牙买加影迷们的追捧中。

  • Livy smiled and basked in Rachel's approval. [VERB in noun]

    莉薇微笑着，雷切尔的赞许令她如沐春风。
