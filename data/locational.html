location
loʊkeɪʃən loʊˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural locations
1 N-COUNT （某事发生或某物坐落的）位置，场所 A location is the place where something happens or is situated. （某事发生或某物坐落的）位置，场所

  • The first thing he looked at was his office's location.

    他首先看的是自己办公室的位置。

  • Macau's newest small luxury hotel has a beautiful location.

    澳门最新的小型豪华饭店地理位置优美。

SYN place, point, setting, position  
2 N-COUNT [with poss] （某人或某物所在的确切）位置，地点 The location of someone or something is their exact position. （某人或某物所在的确切）位置，地点

  • She knew the exact location of The Eagle's headquarters. [+ of]

    她知道“飞鹰”总部的确切地点。

3 N-VAR [oft on NOUN] 外景拍摄地 A location is a place away from a studio where a film or part of a film is made. 外景拍摄地

  • ...an art movie with dozens of exotic locations.

    在众多异域之地取景的艺术片

  • We're shooting on location.

    我们正在拍摄外景。
