breakfast
brekfəst ˈbrɛkfəst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense breakfasts, present participle breakfasting, past tense breakfasted
1 N-VAR 早餐；早饭 Breakfast is the first meal of the day. It is usually eaten in the early part of the morning. 早餐；早饭

  • What's for breakfast?

    早餐吃什么？

  • ...breakfast cereal.

    谷类早餐食品

2See also:bed and breakfast  continental breakfast  English breakfast 
3 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] （咸肉、蛋类等）熟食早餐 A cooked breakfast or a hot breakfast is a breakfast that consists of cooked food, such as bacon and eggs. （咸肉、蛋类等）熟食早餐
4 VERB 吃早饭 When you breakfast, you have breakfast. →see usage note at: meal 吃早饭

  • All the ladies breakfasted in their rooms.

    所有女士都在她们的房间里吃早餐。
