comings and goings ˈkʌmɪŋz ənd ˈɡəʊɪŋz
N-PLURAL [with poss] 来来往往;进进出出 Comings and goings refers to the way people keep arriving at and leaving a particular place. 来来往往;进进出出

  • They noted the comings and goings of the journalists. [+ of]

    他们注意到很多记者进进出出。

  • She had to report her comings and goings to Sister Giuseppe.

    她必须向朱塞佩修女汇报自己的活动。
