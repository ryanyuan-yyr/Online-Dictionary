waft
wɒft wæft wɑːft wɒft wɑft wæft
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense wafts, present participle wafting, past tense , past participle wafted
V-ERG (使)飘荡;吹拂 If sounds or smells waft through the air, or if something such as a light wind wafts them, they move gently through the air. (使)飘荡;吹拂

  • The scent of climbing roses wafts through the window. [VERB prep./adv.]

    藤本月季的芳香飘进了窗户。

  • The music from the party wafts out to the terrace. [VERB prep./adv.]

    晚会的音乐飘到了外面的露台上。

  • A slight breeze rose, wafting the heavy scent of flowers past her. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    清风吹拂，浓郁的花香向她飘来。

SYN transport, bring, carry, bear  
Waft is also a noun.

  • A waft of perfume drifted into Ingrid's nostrils.

    一股香水味飘进了英格丽德的鼻子里。
