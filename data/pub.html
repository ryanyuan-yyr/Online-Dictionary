pub
pʌb pʌb
Word forms: plural pubs
N-COUNT 酒馆；酒吧 A pub is a building where people can have drinks, especially alcoholic drinks, and talk to their friends. Many pubs also serve food. →see usage note at: bar 酒馆；酒吧

  • He was in the pub until closing time.

    他在酒吧里一直呆到打烊。

  • Richard used to run a pub.

    理查德曾经开过酒吧。

Quotations
There is nothing which has yet been contrived by man, by which so much happiness is produced, as by a good tavern or innSamuel Johnson
