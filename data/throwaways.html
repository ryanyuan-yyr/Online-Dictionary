throwaway
θroʊəweɪ ˈθroʊəˌweɪ
Word forms: plural throwaways
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 一次性的;用完即弃的 A throwaway product is intended to be used only for a short time, and then to be thrown away. 一次性的;用完即弃的

  • Now they are producing throwaway razors.

    他们正在生产一次性剃须刀。

SYN disposable, one-use, expendable  
A throwaway is a throwaway product.一次性产品

  • She's taken surplus goods and throwaways and given them useful new lives with the aid of paint and imagination.

    借助于油漆和想象力，她赋予了剩余商品和一次性产品新的功用和生命。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (讲话、动作)不经意的，随意的 If you say that someone makes a throwaway remark or gesture, you mean that they make it in a casual way, although it may be important, or have some serious or humorous effect. (讲话、动作)不经意的，随意的

  • ...a throwaway remark she later regretted.

    她脱口而出后就后悔的话

  • The humour and throwaway lines ensure that the piece never loses its pace.

    幽默和即兴的台词确保了这出戏自始至终的流畅。

SYN casual, passing, offhand, careless
