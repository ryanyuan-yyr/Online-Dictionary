one-piece
ˈwʌnˈpis
Word forms: plural one-pieces
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （衣服）上下身相连的，连体式的 A one-piece article of clothing consists of one piece only, rather than two or more separate parts. （衣服）上下身相连的，连体式的

  • ...a blue one-piece bathing suit.

    蓝色的一件式泳装

2 N-COUNT （女子的）一件式游泳衣 A one-piece is a type of woman's swimming costume that consists of one piece of material and which covers her chest. （女子的）一件式游泳衣

  • A one-piece is more flattering than a bikini.

    一件式泳装比比基尼穿上去更好看。
