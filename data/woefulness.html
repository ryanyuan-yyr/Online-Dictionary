woeful
woʊfʊl ˈwəʊsəm ˈwoʊfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （人或事物）悲惨的，忧伤的，哀伤的 If someone or something is woeful, they are very sad. （人或事物）悲惨的，忧伤的，哀伤的

  • ...a woeful ballad.

    忧伤的情歌

woefully

  • He said woefully: 'I love my country, but it does not give a damn about me.'

    他哀伤地说道：“我爱我的祖国，但祖国并不爱我。”

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 恶劣的;糟糕的;不合意的 You can use woeful to emphasize that something is very bad or undesirable. 恶劣的;糟糕的;不合意的 [emphasis]

  • Such attitudes are the product of woeful ignorance.

    这样的态度是极度无知造成的。

  • ...the woeful state of the economy.

    糟糕的经济状况

woefully

  • Public expenditure on the arts is woefully inadequate.

    艺术方面的公共支出严重不足。

  • Most of the ships were woefully short of ammunition.

    大多数舰船弹药极度匮乏。
