odium
oʊdiəm ˈoʊdiəm
N-UNCOUNT （通常指因他人做过某事而对其产生的）憎恨，厌恶，不满 Odium is the dislike, disapproval, or hatred that people feel for a particular person, usually because of something that the person has done. （通常指因他人做过某事而对其产生的）憎恨，厌恶，不满 [formal]
