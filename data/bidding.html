bidding
bɪdɪŋ ˈbɪdɪŋ
1 PHRASE 应…的要求；按…的吩咐 If you do something at someone's bidding, you do it because they have asked you to do it. 应…的要求；按…的吩咐 [formal]

  • At his bidding, the delegates rose and sang the national anthem.

    按照他的要求，代表们起立唱国歌。

2 PHRASE 对…唯命是从 If you say that someone does another person's bidding, you disapprove of the fact that they do exactly what the other person asks them to do, even when they do not want to. 对…唯命是从 [formal, disapproval]

  • She is very clever at getting men to do her bidding!

    她很善于让男人听她的吩咐！
