spill out
PHRASAL VERB-ERG 泄露;泄密 If you spill out information or if it spills out, you tell someone about it in a hurried way, because you cannot or do not want to keep it secret. 泄露;泄密 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The words spilled out in a rush. [VERB PREP.]

    这番话很快被泄露了出去。

  • He was tempted to spill out his problems to Philip. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他很想把自己的问题透露给菲利普。
