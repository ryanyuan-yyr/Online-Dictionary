furnish
fɜːʳnɪʃ ˈfɜrnɪʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense furnishes, present participle furnishing, past tense , past participle furnished
1 VERB 布置;为(房间或建筑物)配备家具陈设 If you furnish a room or building, you put furniture and furnishings into it. 布置;为(房间或建筑物)配备家具陈设 [Also VERB n]

  • Many proprietors try to furnish their hotels with antiques. [VERB noun + with]

    许多经营者都想用古董装饰他们的酒店。

SYN decorate, fit, fit out, appoint  
2 VERB 提供;供应 If you furnish someone with something, you provide or supply it. 提供;供应 [formal]

  • They'll be able to furnish you with the rest of the details. [VERB noun + with]

    他们将可向你提供其余的细节。

SYN supply, give, offer, provide
