truce
truːs trus
Word forms: plural truces
N-COUNT 停战(协定);休战(协定) A truce is an agreement between two people or groups of people to stop fighting or quarrelling for a short time. 停战(协定);休战(协定)

  • The fighting of recent days has given way to an uneasy truce between the two sides. [+ between]

    最近几天的交战结束了，双方达成了暂时的休战协议。

  • Let's call a truce.

    我们别争论了。

SYN ceasefire, break, stay, rest
