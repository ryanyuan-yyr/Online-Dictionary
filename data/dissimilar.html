dissimilar
dɪsɪmɪləʳ dɪˈsɪmɪlə dɪsˈsɪmələr ˈdɪsˈsɪmələr
Word forms: plural dissimilarities
ADJ-GRADED 不一样的;不相同的;不相似的 If one thing is dissimilar to another, or if two things are dissimilar, they are very different from each other. 不一样的;不相同的;不相似的

  • His methods were not dissimilar to those used by Freud. [+ to]

    他的方法和弗洛伊德的并无二致。

  • It would be difficult to find two men who were more dissimilar.

    很难找到彼此间差异更大的人了。

  • The identical treatment of such dissimilar items is totally illogical.

    对如此不同的东西用一模一样的处理方法是完全不合逻辑的。

SYN different, contrasting, unlike, various  
dissimilarity

  • One of his main themes is the dissimilarity between parents and children.

    他的主题之一就是父母和孩子的不同之处。
