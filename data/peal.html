peal
piːl pil
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense peals, present participle pealing, past tense , past participle pealed
1 VERB （钟等）鸣，响 When bells peal, they ring one after another, making a musical sound. （钟等）鸣，响

  • Church bells pealed at the stroke of midnight. [VERB]

    教堂大钟在午夜敲响。

SYN ring, sound, toll, resound  
Peal is also a noun.

  • ...the great peal of the Abbey bells.

    威斯敏斯特大教堂洪亮的钟鸣

2 N-COUNT 持久响亮的声音；轰轰的响声 A peal of laughter or thunder consists of a long, loud series of sounds. 持久响亮的声音；轰轰的响声

  • I heard a peal of merry laughter. [+ of]

    我听到一阵响亮的欢笑声。

  • ...great peals of thunder.

    轰隆作响的雷声

SYN clap, sound, crash, blast
