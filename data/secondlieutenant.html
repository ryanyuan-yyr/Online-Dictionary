second lieutenant
Word forms: plural second lieutenants
1 N-COUNT 陆军少尉 A second lieutenant is an officer in the army who ranks directly below a lieutenant. 陆军少尉 [British]
2 N-COUNT (陆军、空军或海军的)少尉 A second lieutenant is an officer in the army, air force, or marines who ranks directly below a first lieutenant. (陆军、空军或海军的)少尉 [US]
