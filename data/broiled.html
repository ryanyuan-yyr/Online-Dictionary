broil
brɔɪl brɔɪl brɔɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense broils, present participle broiling, past tense , past participle broiled
VERB 烤；焙；炙 When you broil food, you cook it using very strong heat directly above or below it. 烤；焙；炙

  • I'll broil the lobster.

    我会把这只龙虾烤着吃。

  • ...broiled chicken.

    烤鸡
