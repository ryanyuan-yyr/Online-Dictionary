adultery
ədʌltəri əˈdʌltəri
N-UNCOUNT 通奸；婚外性行为 If a married person commits adultery, they have sex with someone that they are not married to. 通奸；婚外性行为

  • She is going to divorce him on the grounds of adultery.

    她以丈夫搞婚外情为由要求离婚。

SYN unfaithfulness, infidelity, cheating [informal] , fornication
