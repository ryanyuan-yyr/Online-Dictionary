demonstrator
demənstreɪtəʳ ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər
Word forms: plural demonstrators
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 示威者;示威游行（或集会）者 Demonstrators are people who are marching or gathering somewhere to show their opposition to something or their support for something. 示威者;示威游行（或集会）者

  • I saw the police using tear gas to try and break up a crowd of demonstrators.

    我看见警察正试图用催泪弹驱散一群示威者。

SYN protester, rebel, dissident, dissenter  
2 N-COUNT 示范者;演示者 A demonstrator is a person who shows people how something works or how to do something. 示范者;演示者
3 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 样品;试用品 A demonstrator is an item for sale that customers can use and test so they can decide whether they want to buy it. 样品;试用品

  • Saab dealers are currently giving away unaccompanied 24-hour test drives in posh demonstrator models.

    萨博汽车的经销商目前正推出高档样品车无人陪同的 24 小时试驾活动。
