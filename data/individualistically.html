individualistic
ɪndɪvɪdʒʊlɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 强调个人独特性的;奉行个人主义的 If you say that someone is individualistic, you mean that they like to think and do things in their own way, rather than imitating other people. You can also say that a society is individualistic if it encourages people to behave in this way. 强调个人独特性的;奉行个人主义的

  • Most artists are very individualistic.

    大多数艺术家都非常强调个人独特性。

  • Spain remains a very individualistic country.

    西班牙一直都是个非常强调个人主义的国家。

SYN individual, original, unique, particular
