birth rate
(also birth-rate)Word forms: plural birth rates
N-COUNT 出生率 The birth rate in a place is the number of babies born there for every 1000 people during a particular period of time. 出生率

  • The UK has the highest birth rate among 15 to 19-year-olds in Western Europe.

    在西欧，英国女性在15到19岁之间生育率最高。

  • ...a falling birth-rate.

    正在下降的出生率
