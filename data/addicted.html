addicted
ədɪktɪd əˈdɪktɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] （吸毒）成瘾的，上瘾的 Someone who is addicted to a harmful drug cannot stop taking it. （吸毒）成瘾的，上瘾的

  • Many of the women are addicted to heroin and cocaine. [+ to]

    这些女人中很多人都吸食海洛因和可卡因成瘾了。

  • After about three months, I was no longer addicted to nicotine.

    大约3个月后，我就不再对尼古丁上瘾了。

SYN hooked [informal] , dependent  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 入迷的;着迷的 If you say that someone is addicted to something, you mean that they like it very much and want to spend as much time doing it as possible. 入迷的;着迷的

  • I went through about four years of being addicted to video games.

    我大约有4年时间沉迷在电子游戏中。

  • She had become addicted to golf. [+ to]

    她已经迷上高尔夫球了。

SYN devoted, in love, dedicated, fond
