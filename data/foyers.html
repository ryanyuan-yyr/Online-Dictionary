foyer
fɔɪəʳ fwaɪeɪ ˈfɔɪər ˈfɔɪˌeɪ fɔɪˌjeɪ fɔɪˈeɪ fɔɪˈjeɪ
Word forms: plural foyers
N-COUNT 门厅;大厅;休息室 The foyer is the large area where people meet or wait just inside the main doors of a building such as a theatre, cinema, or hotel. 门厅;大厅;休息室

  • I went and waited in the foyer.

    我进去在大厅里等着。

SYN entrance hall, lobby, reception area, vestibule
