furthermore
fɜːʳðəʳmɔːʳ ˈfɜːðəˌmɔː ˈfɜrðərˌmɔr
ADV 而且;此外;再者 Furthermore is used to introduce a piece of information or opinion that adds to or supports the previous one. 而且;此外;再者 [formal]

  • Furthermore, they claim that any such interference is completely ineffective.

    此外，他们还声称任何这样的干预都是完全无效的。

SYN moreover, further, in addition, besides
