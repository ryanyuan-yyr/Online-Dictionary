nightlife
naɪtlaɪf
(also night-life)
N-UNCOUNT 夜生活 Nightlife is all the entertainment and social activities that are available at night in towns and cities, such as nightclubs and theatres. 夜生活

  • ...Hamburg's energetic nightlife.

    汉堡活跃的夜生活

  • There are free buses around the resort and plenty of nightlife.

    这个游览胜地到处都有免费大巴，夜生活也很丰富。
