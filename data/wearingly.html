wearing
weərɪŋ ˈwɛərɪŋ ˈwɛrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 令人疲倦的;使人身心疲惫的 If you say that a situation or activity is wearing, you mean that it requires a lot of energy and makes you feel mentally or physically tired. 令人疲倦的;使人身心疲惫的

  • She finds the continual confrontation very wearing.

    她发现持续的对峙让人精疲力竭。

  • Being in demand can be rather wearing.

    受欢迎也可能是件累人的事。

SYN tiresome, trying, taxing, tiring
