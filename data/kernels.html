kernel
kɜːʳnəl ˈkɜːnəl ˈkɜrnəl
Word forms: plural kernels
1 N-COUNT （坚果的）仁，核 The kernel of a nut is the part that is inside the shell. （坚果的）仁，核
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 核心；中心；要点 The kernel of something is the central and most important part of it. 核心；中心；要点

  • The kernel of that message was that peace must not be a source of advantage or disadvantage for anyone. [+ of]

    那一信息的核心是和平绝不应该成为对任何人有利或不利的根源。

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 一小部分 A kernel of something is a small element of it. 一小部分

  • For all I know, there may be a kernel of truth in what he says. [+ of]

    据我所知，他的话可能有一点儿道理。
