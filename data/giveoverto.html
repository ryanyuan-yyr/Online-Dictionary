give over to
(also give up to)
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 专门用于 If something is given over or given up to a particular use, it is used entirely for that purpose. 专门用于

  • Much of the garden was given over to vegetables. [be V-ed P P n]

    这个花园大部分用来种蔬菜。

  • One third of their entire site is given up to a water-purification plant. [be V-ed P P n]

    他们将整块地的1/3用于建净水厂。
