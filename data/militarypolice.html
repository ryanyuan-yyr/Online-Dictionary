military police
1 N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 宪兵部队 The military police are the part of an army, navy, or air force that act as its police force. 宪兵部队

  • The government has said it will reform the military police.

    政府已经声称要改革宪兵部队。

2 N-PLURAL 宪兵 Military police are men and women who are members of the part of an army, navy, or air force that act as its police force. 宪兵

  • The camp is surrounded by razor-wire fences and guarded by military police.

    营地周围架着尖利的铁丝网，且有宪兵看守。
