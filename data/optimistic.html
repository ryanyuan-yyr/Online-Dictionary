optimistic
ɒptɪmɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ that] 乐观的 Someone who is optimistic is hopeful about the future or the success of something in particular. 乐观的 [Also + about]

  • The President says she is optimistic that an agreement can be worked out soon.

    总统说她对很快达成协议持乐观态度。

  • Michael was in a jovial and optimistic mood.

    迈克尔情绪愉快乐观。

SYN hopeful, positive, confident, encouraged  
optimistically

  • Both sides have spoken optimistically about the talks.

    双方都对会谈表示乐观。
