empiricism
ɪmpɪrɪsɪzəm ɛmˈpɪrɪˌsɪzəm
Word forms: plural empiricists
N-UNCOUNT 经验主义;经验论 Empiricism is the belief that people should rely on practical experience and experiments, rather than on theories as, a basis for knowledge. 经验主义;经验论 [formal]
empiricist

  • He was an unswerving empiricist with little time for theory.

    他是个坚定不移的经验论者，不屑研究理论。
