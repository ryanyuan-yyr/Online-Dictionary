sanctimonious
sæŋktɪmoʊniəs ˌsæŋktəˈmoʊniəs
ADJ-GRADED 假装高尚的;伪善的 If you say that someone is sanctimonious, you disapprove of them because you think that they are trying to appear morally better than other people. 假装高尚的;伪善的 [disapproval]

  • He writes smug, sanctimonious rubbish.

    他写的都是些自我陶醉、故作清高的垃圾作品。

  • You sanctimonious little hypocrite!

    你这个道貌岸然的伪君子！

SYN pious, smug, hypocritical, pi [British , slang]  
sanctimoniousness

  • She displays none of the sanctimoniousness often associated with spirituality.
