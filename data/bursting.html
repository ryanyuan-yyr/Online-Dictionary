bursting
bɜːʳstɪŋ ˈbɜːstɪŋ
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 充满…的 If a place is bursting with people or things, it is full of them. 充满…的

  • The place appears to be bursting with women directors. [+ with]

    这里似乎到处都是女性主管。

  • ...a terraced vegetable garden, bursting with produce. [+ with]

    种满了蔬菜的梯田形菜园

SYN overflowing, full, filled, packed  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n] 充满（感情或特点）的 If you say that someone is bursting with a feeling or quality, you mean that they have a great deal of it. 充满（感情或特点）的

  • I was bursting with curiosity. [+ with]

    我充满了好奇。

  • ...a character bursting with energy and vivacity. [+ with]

    充满了活力和生气的角色

SYN passionate, blazing, excited, aroused  
3 ADJ 急于，渴望（做…） If you are bursting to do something, you are very eager to do it. 急于，渴望（做…） [informal]

  • She was bursting to tell everyone.

    她急于告诉每个人。

  • We'll go there bursting to give it our all.

    我们将去那里，渴望献出我们的一切。

4 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 尿急的 If someone says they are bursting, they are emphasizing that they need to urinate very soon. 尿急的 [informal, emphasis]
5 PHRASE 过满的；过于拥挤的 If you say that a place is bursting at the seams or full to bursting, you are emphasizing that it is very full indeed. 过满的；过于拥挤的 [emphasis]

  • The camps are said to be bursting at the seams.

    据说帐篷的缝合处快炸线了。

  • The room was full to bursting.

    房间快要挤爆了。
