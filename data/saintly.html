saintly
seɪntli ˈseɪntli
ADJ-GRADED 圣洁的;似圣徒的;善良的 A saintly person behaves in a very good or very holy way. 圣洁的;似圣徒的;善良的 [approval]

    他圣洁善良的母亲

  • She has been saintly in her self-restraint.

    她一直过着自我节制的圣徒式生活。

SYN virtuous, godly, holy, religious

    他们的勇敢和善良受到人们的称赞。
