put away
1 PHRASAL VERB 把…收起；放好 If you put something away, you put it into the place where it is normally kept when it is not being used, for example in a drawer. 把…收起；放好

  • She finished putting the milk away and turned around. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她把牛奶收好后转过身来。

  • 'Yes, Mum,' replied Cheryl as she slowly put away her doll. [VERB PREP. noun]

    “是的，妈妈。”谢里尔一边慢慢地收起布娃娃一边回答。

  • Her bed was crisply made, her clothes put away. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    她的床收拾得很整洁，衣服也收起来了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 把…关进监狱；把…送进精神病院 If someone is put away, they are sent to prison or to a mental hospital for a long time. 把…关进监狱；把…送进精神病院 [informal]

  • He's an animal! He should be put away. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他是一个禽兽！他应当被关起来。

  • His testimony could put Drago away for life. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他的证词足以使德拉戈被判终身监禁。

SYN imprison, commit, confine, cage [informal]
