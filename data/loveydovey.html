lovey-dovey
lʌvi dʌvi ˈlʌviˈdʌvi
ADJ-GRADED 恩爱缠绵的；卿卿我我的 You can use lovey-dovey to describe, in a humorous or slightly disapproving way, lovers who show their affection for each other very openly. 恩爱缠绵的；卿卿我我的 [informal, disapproval]

  • All my friends were either lovey-dovey couples or wild, single girls.

    我的朋友不是卿卿我我的情侣，就是狂野的单身女郎。
