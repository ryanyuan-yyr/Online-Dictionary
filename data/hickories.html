hickory
hɪkəri ˈhɪkərɪ ˈhɪkəri ˈhɪkri
Word forms: hickories
1 N-VAR 山胡桃树A hickory is a tree which has large leaves, greenish flowers, and nuts with smooth shells. 山胡桃树

  • It would have meant cutting down over 200 trees, including oaks and hickories.

    这就意味着砍伐200多棵树，包括栎树和山胡桃树。

2 N-UNCOUNT 山胡桃木Hickory is the wood of this tree. 山胡桃木

  • The first skis were long, thin strips of hickory.

    第一批滑雪板是又长又薄的山胡桃木板子。
