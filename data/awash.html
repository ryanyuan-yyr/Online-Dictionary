awash
əwɒʃ əˈwɔʃ
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 被水浸漫的;被淹没的 If the ground or a floor is awash, it is covered in water, often because of heavy rain or as the result of an accident. 被水浸漫的;被淹没的

  • The bathroom floor was awash.

    浴室地板浸在水中。

SYN flooded, drowned, engulfed, submerged  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 充斥的;充沛的;充满的 If a place is awash with something, it contains a large amount of it. 充斥的;充沛的;充满的

  • This, after all, is a company which is awash with cash. [+ with]

    这毕竟是一家现金充裕的公司。

SYN overburdened, overwhelmed, swamped
