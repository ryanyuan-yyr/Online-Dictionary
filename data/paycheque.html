N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 薪水支票；工资；薪水 Your pay cheque is a piece of paper that your employer gives you as your wages or salary, and which you can then cash at a bank. You can also use pay cheque as a way of referring to your wages or salary. 薪水支票；工资；薪水
