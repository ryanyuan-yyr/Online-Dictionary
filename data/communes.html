commune
kɒmjuːn kəmjuːn ˈkɑmˌjun kəˈmjun
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense communes, present participle communing, past tense , past participle communed
The noun is pronounced /'kɒmjuːn/. The verb is pronounced /kə'mjuːn/. 名词读作 /'kɒmjuːn/。动词读作 /kə'mjuːn/。
1 N-COUNT 群居团体;社群;公社 A commune is a group of people who live together and share everything. 群居团体;社群;公社

  • Mack lived in a commune.

    麦克生活在一个群居团体里。

2 N-COUNT (法国等国家有自己的地方议会的)市镇 In France and some other countries, a commune is a town, village, or area which has its own council. (法国等国家有自己的地方议会的)市镇
3 VERB 与(动物、精灵或大自然)交流;与…融为一体 If you say that someone is communing with an animal or spirit, or with nature, you mean that they appear to be communicating with it. 与(动物、精灵或大自然)交流;与…融为一体 [literary]

  • He was so happy communing with the dolphin in Dingle Bay. [VERB with noun]

    他在丁格尔湾与海豚交流的时候特别开心。

  • She would happily trot behind him as he set off to commune with nature. [VERB + with]

    他出去亲近大自然的时候，她就会一路小跑，快活地跟在他后面。
