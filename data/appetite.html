appetite
æpɪtaɪt ˈæpəˌtaɪt
Word forms: plural appetites
1 N-VAR 胃口;食欲 Your appetite is your desire to eat. 胃口;食欲

  • He has a healthy appetite.

    他胃口很好。

  • Symptoms are a slight fever, headache and loss of appetite.

    症状包括低烧、头痛和食欲不振。

SYN hunger, taste, palate, voracity  
2 N-COUNT 强烈欲望;渴望 Someone's appetite for something is their strong desire for it. 强烈欲望;渴望

  • ...his appetite for success.

    他对成功的强烈渴望

  • ...Americans' growing appetite for scandal.

    美国人对丑闻日益增强的好奇心

  • She gave him just enough information to whet his appetite.

    她向他透露的信息刚好能吊起他的胃口。
