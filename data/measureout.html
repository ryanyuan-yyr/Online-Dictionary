measure out
PHRASAL VERB (按所需)量出,量取 If you measure out a certain amount of something, you measure that amount and take it or mark it because it is the amount that you want or need. (按所需)量出,量取 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I'd already measured out the ingredients. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我已经量好了配料。

  • He was on the front lawn, measuring out two circles. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他在前面的草坪上丈量并标记出两个圆圈。

SYN dispense, divide, distribute, assign
