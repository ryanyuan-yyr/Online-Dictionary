fluctuate
flʌktʃueɪt ˈflʌktʃuˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fluctuates, present participle fluctuating, past tense , past participle fluctuated
VERB 变动;波动;上下浮动 If something fluctuates, it changes a lot in an irregular way. 变动;波动;上下浮动

  • Body temperature can fluctuate if you are ill. [VERB]

    人患病后体温可能会上下波动。

  • ...the fluctuating price of oil. [VERB-ing]

    油价的浮动

SYN change, swing, vary, alter  
fluctuation

  • Don't worry about tiny fluctuations in your weight.

    不要为体重的细微变化担心。

  • The calculations do not take into account any fluctuation in the share price.

    这些计算没有考虑到股价的波动。
