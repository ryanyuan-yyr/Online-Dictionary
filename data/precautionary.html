precautionary
prɪkɔːʃənri -neri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 预防的；防备的；警惕的 Precautionary actions are taken in order to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant from happening. 预防的；防备的；警惕的 [formal]

  • The local administration says the curfew is a precautionary measure.

    地方当局说宵禁是一种预防措施。

SYN preventative, safety, protective, preventive
