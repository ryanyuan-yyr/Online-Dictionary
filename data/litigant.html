litigant
lɪtɪgənt ˈlɪtɪgənt
Word forms: plural litigants
N-COUNT 诉讼当事人 A litigant is a person who is involved in a civil legal case, either because they are making a formal complaint about someone, or because a complaint is being made about them. 诉讼当事人 [law]
SYN claimant, party, plaintiff, contestant
