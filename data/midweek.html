midweek
mɪdwiːk ˈmɪdˌwik
ADJ [ADJ n] 一周中间的；在周中的 Midweek describes something that happens in the middle of the week. 一周中间的；在周中的

  • The package includes midweek flights from Gatwick.

    套餐里包括在一周中间从盖特威克机场出发的航班机票。

Midweek is also an adverb.

  • They'll be able to go up to London midweek.

    他们可以在一周的中间去伦敦。

  • By midweek officials were speaking hopefully of a 'compromise'.

    一周过半时，官员们谈论到“折中方案”都满怀希望。
