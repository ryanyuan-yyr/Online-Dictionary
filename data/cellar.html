cellar
seləʳ ˈsɛlər
Word forms: plural cellars
1 N-COUNT 地窖;地下室 A cellar is a room underneath a building, which is often used for storing things in. 地窖;地下室

  • The box of papers had been stored in a cellar at the family home.

    那箱文件一直藏在家中的地窖里。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] (私人或饭店的)酒窖 A person's or restaurant's cellar is the collection of different wines that they have. (私人或饭店的)酒窖

  • ...the restaurant's extensive wine cellar.

    饭店藏酒品种繁多的酒窖
