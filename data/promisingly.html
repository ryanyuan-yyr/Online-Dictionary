promisingly
prɒmɪsɪŋli
ADV-GRADED [usually ADVERB with verb, oft ADVERB adjective] （通常只是开头）给人以希望地，良好地 If something or someone starts promisingly, they begin well but often fail in the end. （通常只是开头）给人以希望地，良好地

  • It all started so promisingly when Speed scored a tremendous first goal.

    比赛开始的时候形势大好，斯皮德以一记精彩的进球先拔头筹。
