ambitious
æmbɪʃəs æmˈbɪʃəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 有雄心的；有抱负的；有野心的 Someone who is ambitious has a strong desire to be successful, rich, or powerful. 有雄心的；有抱负的；有野心的

  • Chris is so ambitious, so determined to do it all.

    克里斯踌躇满志，决心要将它都做完。

  • He's a very ambitious lad and he wants to play at the highest level.

    他是个很有抱负的小伙子，想参加最高水平的比赛。

SYN enterprising, spirited, keen, active  
2 ADJ-GRADED 规模宏大的；艰巨的 An ambitious idea or plan is on a large scale and needs a lot of work to be carried out successfully. 规模宏大的；艰巨的

  • The ambitious project was completed in only nine months.

    这个规模宏大的项目只用了9个月就完成了。

  • Their goal was extraordinarily ambitious.

    他们的目标极其宏伟。

SYN demanding, trying, hard, taxing  
ambitiously

  • Its trade and industrial policies should be used more ambitiously.

    其贸易和工业政策的适用范围应该扩大。

  • He is working on his life story, ambitiously planned as a 50-volume work.

    他正着力于写作他的传记，雄心勃勃地计划要写50卷。
