fingertip
fɪŋgəʳtɪp ˈfɪŋgərˌtɪp
(also finger-tip)Word forms: plural fingertips
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 指尖 Your fingertips are the ends of your fingers. 指尖

  • The fat and flour are rubbed together with the fingertips as for pastry.

    像做油酥面团那样用指尖把脂油和面粉揉搓到一起。

2 PHRASE 近在手边;随时可用 If you say that something is at your fingertips, you approve of the fact that you can reach it easily or that it is easily available to you. 近在手边;随时可用 [approval]

  • I had the information at my fingertips and hadn't used it.

    资料就在我手边，但一直都没有用。
