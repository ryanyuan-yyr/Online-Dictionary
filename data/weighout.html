weigh out
PHRASAL VERB 称出…的重量 If you weigh something out, you measure a certain weight of it in order to make sure that you have the correct amount. 称出…的重量

  • I agreed to help him weigh it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我答应帮他称一下。

  • I learned how to weigh out packages of seed. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我学会了如何称出袋装种子的重量。

SYN measure, dole out, apportion, deal out
