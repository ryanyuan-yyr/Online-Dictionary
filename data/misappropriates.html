misappropriate
mɪsəproʊprieɪt ˌmɪsəˈproʊpriˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense misappropriates, present participle misappropriating, past tense , past participle misappropriated
VERB 盗用;挪用;侵吞 If someone misappropriates money which does not belong to them, they take it without permission and use it for their own purposes. 盗用;挪用;侵吞

  • I took no money for personal use and have not misappropriated any funds whatsoever. [VERB noun]

    我没有把钱款挪为己用，也没有挪用任何资金。

  • A total of $500 million is alleged to have been misappropriated. [VERB noun]

    据称总共有5亿美元被挪用。

SYN steal, embezzle, pocket, misuse  
misappropriation

  • He pleaded guilty to charges of misappropriation of bank funds.

    他对挪用银行资金的指控供认不讳。
