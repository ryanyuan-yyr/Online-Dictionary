re-elect
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense re-elects, present participle re-electing, past tense , past participle re-elected
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 reelect
VERB 再度选举;使再次当选 When someone such as a politician or an official who has been elected is re-elected, they win another election and are therefore able to continue in their position as, for example, president or an official in an organization. 再度选举;使再次当选

  • The president will pursue lower taxes if he is re-elected. [be VERB-ed]

    如果再度当选，总统将推行减税政策。

  • ...Ramon Mendoza was re-elected president of Real for a third successive four-year term. [be VERB-ed noun]

    拉蒙·门多萨再度当选为皇马主席，这是他连续第3次当选这个任期为4年的职位。

  • He was overwhelmingly re-elected as party leader. [be VERB-ed + as]

    他以压倒性多数再度当选为该党领袖。

SYN re-appoint, re-select, pick again, choose again
