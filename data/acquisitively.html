acquisitive
əkwɪzɪtɪv əˈkwɪzətɪv
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 渴望得到的;迫切求取的;贪婪的 If you describe a person or an organization as acquisitive, you do not approve of them because you think they are too concerned with getting new possessions. 渴望得到的;迫切求取的;贪婪的 [disapproval]

  • We live in an acquisitive society.

    我们生活在一个物欲横流的社会。

  • The most acquisitive firms tend to be engineering groups.

    最贪得无厌的公司往往是工程队。

SYN greedy, grabbing, grasping, hungry  
acquisitiveness

  • His villa is filled with evidence of his acquisitiveness.

    他的别墅里满是他贪婪成性的证据。
