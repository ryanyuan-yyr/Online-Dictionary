readjustment
riːədʒʌstmənt ˌriəˈdʒʌstmənt
Word forms: plural readjustments
1 N-VAR 重新适应（或调整） Readjustment is the process of adapting to a new situation, usually one that you have been in before. 重新适应（或调整）

  • The next few weeks will be a period of readjustment.

    接下来的几周将是适应阶段。

  • The readjustment to peace and home is slow and painful.

    重新适应和平以及家庭生活是缓慢而痛苦的。

2 N-VAR （重新）调整 A readjustment of something is a change that you make to it so that it is more effective or appropriate. （重新）调整

  • The organization denies that it is seeking any readjustment of state borders. [+ of]

    该组织否认其正在寻求对国界进行调整。

  • ...the effects of economic readjustment.

    经济调整的影响
