orange
ɒrɪndʒ ɔːr- ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈɑrɪndʒ
Word forms: plural oranges
1 COLOUR 橙色；橙黄色 Something that is orange is of a colour between red and yellow. 橙色；橙黄色

  • ...men in bright orange uniforms.

    身着亮橙色制服的人

2 N-VAR [oft NOUN n] 橙；甜橙 An orange is a round juicy fruit with a thick, orange coloured skin. 橙；甜橙

  • An orange a day will give you all the vitamin C you need.

    每天吃一个橙子能充分摄取所需的维生素C。

  • ...orange trees.

    橙树

  • ...fresh orange juice.

    鲜橙汁

3 N-UNCOUNT 橙汁；橘子水 Orange is a drink that is made from or tastes of oranges. 橙汁；橘子水

  • ...vodka and orange.

    橙汁伏特加酒
