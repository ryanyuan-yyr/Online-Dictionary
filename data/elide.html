elide
ɪlaɪd iˈlaɪd ɪˈlaɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense elides, present participle eliding, past tense , past participle elided
1 VERB 忽略，省略（尤指区别） If you elide something, especially a distinction, you leave it out or ignore it. 忽略，省略（尤指区别） [formal]

  • These habits of thinking elide the difference between what is common and what is normal. [VERB noun]

    这些思维习惯忽略了普通和正常之间的区别。

2 VERB （语言学中）省略 In linguistics, if you elide a word, you do not pronounce or write it fully. （语言学中）省略 [technical]

  • He complained about BBC announcers eliding their words. [VERB noun]

    他抱怨说英国广播公司播音员省略了他们的话。
