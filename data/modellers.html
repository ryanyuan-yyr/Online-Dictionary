modeller
mɒdələʳ
Word forms: plural modellers
in AM, use 美国英语用 modeler
1 N-COUNT 制造模型者;塑像者;造型者 A modeller is someone who makes shapes or figures out of substances such as wood or clay. 制造模型者;塑像者;造型者
2 N-COUNT 系统（或过程）模型创立者 A modeller is someone who makes theoretical descriptions of systems or processes in order to understand them and be able to predict how they will develop. 系统（或过程）模型创立者
