maim
meɪm meɪm
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense maims, present participle maiming, past tense , past participle maimed
VERB 使终身残疾;使受重伤 To maim someone means to injure them so badly that part of their body is permanently damaged. 使终身残疾;使受重伤

  • Mines have been scattered in rice paddies and jungles, maiming and killing civilians. [VERB noun]

    地雷遍布稻田与林间，很多平民被炸死或炸成重伤。

  • One man has lost his life, another has been maimed. [VERB noun]

    一名男子丧生，另一名重伤。

SYN cripple, hurt, injure, wound
