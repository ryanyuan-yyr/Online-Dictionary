terracing
terəsɪŋ ˈtɛrəsɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 梯田;阶地 Terracing is a sloping piece of land that has had flat areas like steps built on it, for example so that people can grow crops there. 梯田;阶地

  • The traditional lawn has been replaced by low-cost terracing, with a raised pool and waterfall.

    传统的草坪已经被低成本梯田所取代，梯田上有个高位水塘和瀑布似的水流。

2 N-UNCOUNT (足球赛观众可站立的)阶梯看台 At a football stadium, terracing is an area of wide steps that people can stand on when they are watching the game. (足球赛观众可站立的)阶梯看台 [British]
