police state
Word forms: plural police states
N-COUNT （尤指通过秘密警察限制人民自由的）警察国家，极权国家 A police state is a country in which the government controls people's freedom by means of the police, especially secret police. （尤指通过秘密警察限制人民自由的）警察国家，极权国家 [disapproval]

  • Their land has been turned into a police state.

    他们的国家已经变成了一个警察国家。
