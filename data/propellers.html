propeller
prəpeləʳ prəˈpɛlər proʊˈpɛlər
Word forms: plural propellers
N-COUNT （轮船或飞机上的）螺旋桨，推进器 A propeller is a device with blades which is attached to a boat or aircraft. The engine makes the propeller spin round and causes the boat or aircraft to move. （轮船或飞机上的）螺旋桨，推进器

  • ...a fixed three-bladed propeller.

    固定住的三叶型螺旋桨

SYN prop [informal] , rotor, vane
