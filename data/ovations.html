ovation
oʊveɪʃən oʊˈveɪʃən
Word forms: plural ovations
1 N-COUNT 热烈欢迎；热烈鼓掌 An ovation is a large amount of applause from an audience for a particular performer or speaker. 热烈欢迎；热烈鼓掌 [formal]

  • They had lost by a wide margin, but their supporters gave them a defiant, loyal ovation.

    他们以较大的差距败北，但支持者们还是给予了热烈的掌声，以示对对手的不服和对他们的忠诚。

SYN applause, hand, cheering, cheers  
2See also:standing ovation
