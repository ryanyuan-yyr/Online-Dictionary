move out
PHRASAL VERB 迁出;搬走 If you move out, you stop living in a particular house or place and go to live somewhere else. 迁出;搬走

  • The harassment had become too much to tolerate and he decided to move out. [VERB PREP.]

    骚扰越来越过分，他难以忍受，决定搬走。

  • They had a huge row and Sally moved out of the house. [VERB PREP. + of]

    他们大吵一架之后萨莉搬了出来。
