downy
daʊni ˈdaʊni
Word forms: comparative downier, superlative downiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 绒羽覆盖的;填满绒羽的;毛茸茸的 Something that is downy is filled or covered with small soft feathers. 绒羽覆盖的;填满绒羽的;毛茸茸的

  • ...the warm downy quilt.

    暖和的羽绒被

  • ...the white downy bodies of two chicks.

    两只小鸡仔白色的毛茸茸的身体

SYN fluffy, woolly, fleecy, feathery  
2 ADJ-GRADED 被绒毛覆盖的;毛茸茸的 Something that is downy is covered with very fine hairs. 被绒毛覆盖的;毛茸茸的

  • ...tiny toes and fingers, his downy head and beautiful skin.

    小小的脚趾和手指，他那毛茸茸的脑袋和光洁的皮肤

  • ...leaves that are often downy underneath.

    底部常常多茸毛的树叶

SYN soft, smooth, sleek, silky
