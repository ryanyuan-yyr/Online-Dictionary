encrusted
ɪnkrʌstɪd ɪnˈkrʌstɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 有硬壳覆盖的;形成硬外壳的 If an object is encrusted with something, its surface is covered with a layer of that thing. 有硬壳覆盖的;形成硬外壳的

  • ...a blue uniform coat that was thickly encrusted with gold loops. [+ with]

    缀满金链的蓝色制服外套

Encrusted is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...a jewel-encrusted ring.

    镶满宝石的戒指

  • ...snow-encrusted mountain paths.

    白雪覆盖的山路
