situated
sɪtʃueɪtɪd ˈsɪtʃʊeɪtɪd ˈsɪtʃuˌeɪtɪd
ADJ [adverb ADJ] 坐落在…的;位于…的 If something is situated in a particular place or position, it is in that place or position. 坐落在…的;位于…的

  • His hotel is situated in one of the loveliest places on the Loire. [+ in]

    他下榻的旅馆位于卢瓦河畔最漂亮的地方之一。

  • The pain was situated above and around the eyes.

    眼睛的上方和四周都很疼。

  • The new store is better situated to attract customers.

    新店所处的位置优越，更能吸引客源。
