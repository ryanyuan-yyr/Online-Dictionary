fitful
fɪtfʊl ˈfɪtfəl
ADJ 断断续续的;间歇性的 Something that is fitful happens for irregular periods of time or occurs at irregular times, rather than being continuous. 断断续续的;间歇性的

  • Colin drifted off into a fitful sleep.

    科林迷迷糊糊地，时睡时醒。

  • The government is making slow and fitful progress in these negotiations.

    政府从这些谈判中正取得缓慢的、间断性的进展。

SYN irregular, broken, disturbed, erratic  
fitfully

  • The sun shone fitfully and then light snow blew into our faces.

    太阳时隐时现，之后片片小雪飘落到我们脸上。
