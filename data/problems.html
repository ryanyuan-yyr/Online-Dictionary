problem
prɒbləm ˈprɑbləm
Word forms: plural problems
1 N-COUNT 问题；难题；困难 A problem is a situation that is unsatisfactory and causes difficulties for people. 问题；难题；困难 [Also + with]

  • ...the economic problems of the inner city. [+ of]

    内城区的经济问题

  • The main problem is unemployment.

    主要的问题是失业。

  • He told Americans that solving the energy problem was very important.

    他告诉美国人解决能源问题非常重要。

  • I do not have a simple solution to the drug problem.

    我没有解决毒品问题的简单办法。

SYN difficulty, trouble, dispute, plight  
2 N-COUNT （通过逻辑思考或数学运算解决的）问题，习题，题 A problem is a puzzle that requires logical thought or mathematics to solve it. （通过逻辑思考或数学运算解决的）问题，习题，题

  • With mathematical problems, you can save time by approximating.

    对于数学题，可以用取近似值的办法节约时间。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] （儿童或家庭）有严重问题的，引起严重问题的 Problem children or problem families have serious problems or cause serious problems for other people. （儿童或家庭）有严重问题的，引起严重问题的

  • In some cases a problem child is placed in a special school.

    有时，问题儿童被放在特殊的学校。

  • She is afraid to contact the social services in case they are labelled a problem family.

    她不敢联系社会服务部门，以免被当作问题家庭。

4 CONVENTION 没问题 You say 'no problem' to show that you are willing to do what you have been asked. 没问题 [informal, spoken] [informal, spoken]

  • 'Can you repair it?' — 'No problem'.

    “你能修好它吗？”—“没问题。”

  • If the property needs a new dishwasher, no problem, just put it on a credit card.

    如果住所需要一个新的洗碗机，没问题，用信用卡买一个就可以了。

5 CONVENTION 没关系 You say 'no problem' to let someone know that you do not mind them doing something they have said they are going to do. 没关系

  • 'I ought to think about going actually. If that's all right with you.' — 'Yeah. No problem.'.

    “事实上，我应该考虑去，如果你不介意的话。”—“嗯，没关系。”

  • If they don't want to speak to me, fine. No problem.

    如果他们不想和我讲话，好的。我不介意。
