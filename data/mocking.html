mocking
mɒkɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 嘲笑的;讥笑的;嘲弄的 A mocking expression or mocking behaviour indicates that you think someone or something is stupid or inferior. 嘲笑的;讥笑的;嘲弄的

  • She gave a mocking smile.

    她露出了嘲弄的笑容。

  • Behind the mocking laughter lurks a growing sense of unease.

    嘲笑声的背后潜伏着一种越来越强烈的不安。

SYN scornful, insulting, taunting, scoffing  
mockingly

  • 'Isn't that sweet?' he says mockingly.

    “真是甜蜜啊！”他嘲弄道。
