dried-up
ˌdraɪdˈʌp
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人)干瘪衰老的，老态龙钟的 If you describe someone as dried-up, you are saying rudely that they are old and dull, and not worth paying attention to. (人)干瘪衰老的，老态龙钟的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...her fears of becoming a dried-up old prune.

    她对变成一个干瘪老太太的恐惧
