inference
ɪnfərəns ˈɪnfərəns
Word forms: plural inferences
1 N-COUNT 推断结果;结论 An inference is a conclusion that you draw about something by using information that you already have about it. 推断结果;结论

  • There were two inferences to be drawn from her letter.

    由她的信可以得出两个结论。

SYN deduction, conclusion, assumption, reading  
2 N-UNCOUNT 推论;推理;推断 Inference is the act of drawing conclusions about something on the basis of information that you already have. 推论;推理;推断

  • It had an extremely tiny head and, by inference, a tiny brain.

    它的头极小，由此推断其脑容量也非常小。
