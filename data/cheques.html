cheque
tʃek tʃɛk tʃɛk
Word forms: plural cheques
in AM, use 美国英语用 check
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 支票 A cheque is a printed form on which you write an amount of money and who it is to be paid to. Your bank then pays the money to that person from your account. 支票

  • He wrote them a cheque for £10,000. [+ for]

    他给他们开了张1万英镑的支票。

  • I'd like to pay by cheque.

    我想用支票结账。
