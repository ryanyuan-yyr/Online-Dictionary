pastille
pæstəl pæstiːl pæsˈtil
Word forms: plural pastilles
N-COUNT 糖锭；锭剂；含片 A pastille is a small, round sweet or piece of candy that has a fruit flavour. Some pastilles contain medicine and you can suck them if you have a sore throat or a cough. 糖锭；锭剂；含片
SYN lozenge, tablet, troche [medicine] , cough drop
