banister
bænɪstəʳ ˈbænɪstə ˈbænɪstər
(also bannister)Word forms: plural banisters
N-COUNT （楼梯的）栏杆，扶手 A banister is a rail supported by posts and fixed along the side of a staircase. The plural banisters can be used to refer to one of these rails. （楼梯的）栏杆，扶手

  • I still remember sliding down the banisters.

    我仍然记得从栏杆上滑下来的事。
