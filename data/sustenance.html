sustenance
sʌstɪnəns ˈsʌstənəns ˈsʌstənəns
N-UNCOUNT 食物;营养;养料 Sustenance is food or drink which a person, animal, or plant needs to remain alive and healthy. 食物;营养;养料 [formal]

  • The state provided a basic quantity of food for daily sustenance, but little else.

    国家提供基本的日常食物供应，但除此之外就没什么了。

SYN nourishment, food, provisions, rations
