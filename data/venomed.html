venom
venəm ˈvɛnəm
Word forms: plural venoms
1 N-UNCOUNT 怨恨;痛恨；恶意 You can use venom to refer to someone's feelings of great bitterness and anger towards someone. 怨恨;痛恨；恶意

  • He reserved particular venom for critics of his foreign policy. [+ for]

    他对那些批评他外交政策的人格外心怀怨恨。

  • There was no mistaking the venom in his voice.

    他的声音中明显透着敌意。

SYN malice, hate, spite, bitterness  
2 N-MASS （毒蛇、蜘蛛等生物的）毒液，毒汁 The venom of a creature such as a snake or spider is the poison that it puts into your body when it bites or stings you. （毒蛇、蜘蛛等生物的）毒液，毒汁

  • ...snake handlers who grow immune to snake venom.

    对蛇毒产生免疫力的玩蛇人

SYN poison, toxin, bane
