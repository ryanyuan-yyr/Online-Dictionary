intersperse
ɪntəʳspɜːʳs ˌɪntərˈspɜrs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense intersperses, present participle interspersing, past tense , past participle interspersed
VERB 散布;散置;点缀 If you intersperse one group of things with another or among another, you put or include the second things between or among the first things. 散布;散置;点缀

  • Originally the intention was to intersperse the historical scenes with modern ones. [VERB noun + with]

    最初是想用现代景观点缀历史景观。

  • ...skillfully interspersing jokes and gossipy anecdotes among his instructions. [V n among n]

    他的讲课中巧妙穿插的笑话和趣闻闲谈

SYN scatter, sprinkle, intermix, pepper
