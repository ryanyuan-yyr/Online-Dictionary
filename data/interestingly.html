interestingly
ɪntrestɪŋli
ADV-GRADED 有趣的是;令人关注的是;想不到的是 You use interestingly to introduce a piece of information that you think is interesting or unexpected. 有趣的是;令人关注的是;想不到的是

  • Interestingly enough, a few weeks later, Benjamin remarried.

    有意思的是,几个星期以后,本杰明又结婚了。

  • Interestingly, it is not clear which solution the prime minister favors.

    耐人寻味的是,现在还不清楚首相支持哪个解决方案。
