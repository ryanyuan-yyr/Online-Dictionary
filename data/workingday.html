working day
Word forms: working days
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 工作日A working day is the amount of time during a normal day that you spend doing your job. 工作日 [mainly British]

  • His working day starts at 3:30 a.m. and lasts 12 hours.

    他每天的工作时间从早上3:30开始，持续12个小时。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 工作的日子A working day is a day on which people go to work. 工作的日子 [British]

  • What's he doing home on a working day?

    工作日他在家里干什么？
