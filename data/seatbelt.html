seat belt
(also seatbelt)Word forms: plural seat belts
N-COUNT (汽车、飞机的)座椅安全带 A seat belt is a strap attached to a seat in a car or an aircraft. You fasten it across your body in order to prevent yourself being thrown out of the seat if there is a sudden movement. (汽车、飞机的)座椅安全带

  • The fact I was wearing a seat belt saved my life.

    我系着安全带，所以幸免于难。
