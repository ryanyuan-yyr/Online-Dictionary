orthodox
ɔːʳθədɒks ˈɔrθəˌdɑks
The spelling Orthodox is also used for meaning 3. 拼写 Orthodox 亦用于义项3。
1 ADJ-GRADED 正统的；规范的；广为接受的 Orthodox beliefs, methods, or systems are ones which are accepted or used by most people. 正统的；规范的；广为接受的

  • Payne gained a reputation for sound, if orthodox, views.

    虽然观点正统，但佩恩合乎情理的见解还是令他为人称道。

  • Many of these ideas are now being incorporated into orthodox medical treatment.

    现在这些理念中有很多正被吸收至正统医学治疗中。

  • ...orthodox police methods.

    警方的常规手段

SYN established, official, accepted, received  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （人）正统派的，传统的，循规蹈矩的 If you describe someone as orthodox, you mean that they hold the older and more traditional ideas of their religion or party. （人）正统派的，传统的，循规蹈矩的

  • ...orthodox Jews.

    正统派的犹太教徒

  • ...orthodox conservatives.

    循规蹈矩的保守派

3 ADJ 东正教（会）的 The Orthodox churches are Christian churches in Eastern Europe which separated from the western church in the eleventh century. 东正教（会）的

  • ...the Greek Orthodox Church.

    希腊东正教会
