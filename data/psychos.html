psycho
saɪkoʊ ˈsaɪkoʊ
Word forms: plural psychos
N-COUNT 精神变态者；变态人格者 A psycho is someone who has serious mental problems and who may act in a violent way without feeling sorry for what they have done. 精神变态者；变态人格者 [informal]

  • Some psycho picked her up, and killed her.

    有个变态搭上了她，然后把她杀了。

psycho- saɪkoʊ- ˈsaɪkoʊ ˈsaɪkə
PREFIX （用于构词）表示“精神”，“灵魂”，“心理” Psycho- is added to words in order to form other words which describe or refer to things connected with the mind or with mental processes. （用于构词）表示“精神”，“灵魂”，“心理”

  • ...the psycho-social aspects of youth unemployment.

    青年人失业的社会心理方面
