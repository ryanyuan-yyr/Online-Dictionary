admiring
ədmaɪərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 赞赏的；钦佩的 An admiring expression shows that you like or respect someone or something. 赞赏的；钦佩的

  • He cast her an admiring glance.

    他向她投以钦慕的目光。

  • Within moments, he'd attracted an admiring audience.

    不出片刻，他就赢得了全场观众的倾心。

admiringly
See also: admire 

  • He glanced admiringly at the design.

    他钦佩地看了看那个设计。
