amateur
æmətəʳ ˌæməˈtɜː -tʃɜːr ˈæmətʃər ˈæməˌtʃʊr ˈæməˌtɜr ˈæmətər
Word forms: plural amateurs
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 业余爱好者 An amateur is someone who does something as a hobby and not as a job. 业余爱好者

  • Jerry is an amateur who dances because he feels like it.

    杰里是个业余跳舞爱好者，他跳舞是因为自己喜欢。

  • Taylor began his playing career as an amateur goalkeeper.

    泰勒的运动生涯是从一名业余守门员开始的。

SYN nonprofessional, outsider, layman, dilettante  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （体育运动或活动）业余爱好的 Amateur sports or activities are done by people as a hobby and not as a job. （体育运动或活动）业余爱好的

  • ...the local amateur dramatics society.

    地方业余戏剧社团

  • At college he studied English and did amateur boxing.

    大学时，他学习英语，同时还参加业余拳击。
