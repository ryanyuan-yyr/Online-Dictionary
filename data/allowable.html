allowable
əlaʊəbəl əˈlaʊəbəl əˈlaʊəbəl
1 ADJ 可允许的；可准许的；可容许的 If people decide that something is allowable, they let it happen without trying to stop it. 可允许的；可准许的；可容许的

  • Capital punishment is allowable only under exceptional circumstances.

    只有特殊情况才允许执行死刑。

2 ADJ （费用等）免税的 Allowable costs or expenses are amounts of money that you do not have to pay tax on. （费用等）免税的 [business]

  • Her expenses were allowable deductions.

    她的费用开支是免税的。
