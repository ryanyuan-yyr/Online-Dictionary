biennial
baɪeniəl baɪˈɛniəl
Word forms: plural biennials
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 两年一次的 A biennial event happens or is done once every two years. 两年一次的

  • ...the biennial Commonwealth conference.

    两年一届的英联邦会议

2 N-COUNT 二年生植物 A biennial is a plant that lives for two years. It flowers, produces seed, and dies in its second year. 二年生植物
