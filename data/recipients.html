recipient
rɪsɪpiənt rɪˈsɪpiənt
Word forms: plural recipients
N-COUNT 接受者；领受人 The recipient of something is the person who receives it. 接受者；领受人 [formal]

  • ...the largest recipient of American foreign aid. [+ of]

    美国对外援助的最大受惠者

  • A suppressed immune system puts a transplant recipient at risk of other infections.

    接受器官移植的病人免疫系统受到抑制后很可能会感染其他疾病。

SYN receiver, beneficiary, donee, legatee
