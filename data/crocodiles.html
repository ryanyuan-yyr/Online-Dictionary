crocodile
krɒkədaɪl ˈkrɑkəˌdaɪl
Word forms: plural crocodiles
1 N-COUNT 鳄鱼 A crocodile is a large reptile with a long body and strong jaws. Crocodiles live in rivers and eat meat. 鳄鱼
2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN of n, also in N] (尤指小学生排成的)长队;（行进车辆排成的）长蛇阵 A crocodile of people, especially school children, or vehicles is a long line of them, moving together. (尤指小学生排成的)长队;（行进车辆排成的）长蛇阵 [British]

  • The children walk in crocodiles from the schoolhouse to the dining-room for lunch.

    孩子们排队从校舍去餐厅吃午饭。

  • ...a long crocodile of coaches.

    排成一长串的长途客车
