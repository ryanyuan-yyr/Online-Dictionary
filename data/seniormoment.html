senior moment
Word forms: senior moments
N-COUNT 老年失忆症If an elderly person forgets something or makes a mistake and you refer to this as a senior moment, you mean that the person forgot the thing or made the mistake because they are old and their mental abilities are declining. 老年失忆症 [mainly US, informal]

  • He is 69 in February and sometimes has a senior moment, when the flow of words dries up.

    他二月份69岁，偶尔出现老年失忆症，不再滔滔不绝了。
