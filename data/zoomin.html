zoom in
PHRASAL VERB （摄像机或摄影机）拉近镜头，进行近景拍摄 If a camera zooms in on something that is being filmed or photographed, it gives a close-up picture of it. （摄像机或摄影机）拉近镜头，进行近景拍摄

  • ...a tracking system which can follow a burglar round a building and zoom in on his face. [VERB PREP. + on]

    可对入户窃贼在整栋楼里进行跟踪并将其面部图像放大的追踪系统

  • He trained his camera on nature, sometimes zooming in to examine single leaves. [VERB PREP.]

    他把自己的照相机对准了大自然，时而拉近镜头仔细观察一片片的叶子。
