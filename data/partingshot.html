parting shot
Word forms: plural parting shots
N-COUNT 临走时说的尖刻话；最后的狠话 If someone makes a parting shot, they make an unpleasant or forceful remark at the end of a conversation, and then leave so that no-one has the chance to reply. 临走时说的尖刻话；最后的狠话

  • He turned to face her for his parting shot. 'You're one coldhearted woman, you know that?'

    他临走时转过身，向她甩下一句“你是个冷漠无情的女人，你知道吗？”
