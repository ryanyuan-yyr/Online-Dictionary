decorous
dekərəs ˈdɛkərəs dɪˈkɔrəs
ADJ-GRADED 符合礼仪的;得体的;庄重的 Decorous behaviour is very respectable, calm, and polite. 符合礼仪的;得体的;庄重的 [formal]

  • They go for decorous walks every day in parks with their nanny.

    他们每天都和保姆一起在公园里散步，表现得很有教养。

SYN proper, becoming, seemly, fitting  
decorously

  • He sipped his drink decorously.

    他很有风度地小口喝着饮料。
