proʊnaʊn ˈproʊˌnaʊn
Word forms: plural pronouns
1 N-COUNT 代词；代名词 A pronoun is a word that you use to refer to someone or something when you do not need to use a noun, often because the person or thing has been mentioned earlier. Examples are 'it', 'she', 'something', and 'myself'. 代词；代名词
