dismissal
dɪsmɪsəl dɪsˈmɪsəl
Word forms: plural dismissals
1 N-VAR [oft with poss] 解雇;开除;免职 When an employee is dismissed from their job, you can refer to their dismissal . 解雇;开除;免职

  • ...Mr Low's dismissal from his post at the head of the commission.

    免去洛先生的委员会主任一职

2 N-UNCOUNT 不予考虑;不予理会 Dismissal of something means deciding or saying that it is not important. 不予考虑;不予理会

  • ...their high-handed dismissal of public opinion. [+ of]

    他们对公众意见的蛮横漠视

SYN rejection, refusal, rebuff, knock-back [slang]
