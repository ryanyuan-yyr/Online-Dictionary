innate
ɪneɪt ɪˈneɪt ˈɪnˌeɪt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 天生的;天然的;固有的 An innate quality or ability is one which a person is born with. 天生的;天然的;固有的

  • Americans have an innate sense of fairness.

    美国人有一种天生的公平观。

  • ...a society in which individuals could develop their innate abilities and capacities.

    个人可以发挥其天生才干和能力的社会

SYN inborn, natural, inherent, essential  
innately

  • I believe everyone is innately psychic.

    我相信每个人天生都有超自然的能力。
