fast forward
fɑːst ˈfɔːwəd
(also fast-forward)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fast forwards, present participle fast forwarding, past tense , past participle fast forwarded
1 VERB [oft on NOUN] (使录像带或录音带)快速进带，快进 When you fast forward the tape in a video or tape recorder or when you fast forward, you make the tape go forwards. (使录像带或录音带)快速进带，快进 [Also V prep/adv]

  • Just fast forward the video. [VERB noun]

    让录像带快进就行了。

  • He fast-forwarded the tape past the explosion. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    他让带子快进，跳过爆炸场景。

  • The urge to fast-forward is almost irresistible. [VERB]

    快速进带的冲动几乎压制不住。

2 N-UNCOUNT 快进键;快进按钮 If you put a video or cassette tape on fast forward, you make the tape go forwards. 快进键;快进按钮

  • Before recording onto a new tape, wind it on fast forward, then rewind.

    新带使用之前按快进键进带，然后倒带。

  • I really tried with this film, but kept pushing the fast-forward button — I found it unwatchable.

    我的确尝试过看这部片子，但不停地按快进键—我觉得不好看。
