retell
riːtel riːˈtɛl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense retells, present participle retelling, past tense , past participle retold
VERB 复述;重讲 If you retell a story, you write it, tell it, or present it again, often in a different way from its original form. 复述;重讲

  • Lucilla often asks her sisters to retell the story. [VERB noun]

    露西拉经常要姐姐们把这个故事反复讲给她听。

  • It is a tale which has often been retold within West Indian literature. [VERB noun]

    这是西印度群岛文学中广为流传的故事。
