censorious
sensɔːriəs sɛnˈsɔriəs
ADJ-GRADED 挑剔的;苛责的 If you describe someone as censorious, you do not like the way they strongly disapprove of and criticize someone else's behaviour. 挑剔的;苛责的 [formal, disapproval]

  • Despite strong principles he was never censorious.

    虽然他原则性很强，可从来不会吹毛求疵。

SYN critical, severe, carping, disapproving
