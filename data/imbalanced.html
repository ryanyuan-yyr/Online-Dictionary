imbalanced
ɪmbælənst ɪmˈbælənst
ADJ-GRADED 失衡的;失调的 If you describe a situation as imbalanced, you mean that the elements within it are not evenly or fairly arranged. 失衡的;失调的

  • ...a markedly imbalanced geographical distribution of immigrants.

    显著失衡的移民地理分布

  • ...the present imbalanced structure of world trade.

    当前世界贸易的失衡结构
