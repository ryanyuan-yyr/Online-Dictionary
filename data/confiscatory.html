confiscate
kɒnfɪskeɪt ˈkɑnfɪsˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense confiscates, present participle confiscating, past tense , past participle confiscated
VERB (通常作为惩罚)没收，把…充公 If you confiscate something from someone, you take it away from them, usually as a punishment. (通常作为惩罚)没收，把…充公

  • There is concern that police use the law to confiscate assets from people who have committed minor offences. [VERB noun + from]

    有人担心警方利用该法罚没犯下轻罪的人的财产。

  • They confiscated weapons, ammunition and propaganda material. [VERB noun]

    他们没收了武器、弹药和宣传材料。

SYN seize, appropriate, impound, commandeer
