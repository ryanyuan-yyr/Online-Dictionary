glazed
gleɪzd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （目光）木然的，呆滞的，无神采的 If you describe someone's eyes as glazed, you mean that their expression is dull or dreamy, usually because they are tired or are having difficulty concentrating on something. （目光）木然的，呆滞的，无神采的

  • Doctors with glazed eyes sat chain-smoking in front of a television set.

    医生们目光呆滞地坐在电视机前一支接一支地抽着烟。

  • There was a glazed look in her eyes.

    她眼神呆滞。

SYN expressionless, cold, fixed, empty  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （陶器）上釉的 Glazed pottery is covered with a thin layer of a hard shiny substance. （陶器）上釉的
3 ADJ （门、窗）装有玻璃的 A glazed window or door has glass in it. （门、窗）装有玻璃的
