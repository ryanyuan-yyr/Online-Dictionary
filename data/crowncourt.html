crown court
N-COUNT (英格兰和威尔士的有法官和陪审团列席的)刑事法庭 In England and Wales, a Crown Court is a court in which criminal cases are tried by a judge and jury rather than by a magistrate. (英格兰和威尔士的有法官和陪审团列席的)刑事法庭
