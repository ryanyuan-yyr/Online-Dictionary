obtainable
ɒbteɪnəbəl
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 可得到的;可获得的;可实现的 If something is obtainable, it is possible to get or achieve it. 可得到的;可获得的;可实现的

  • The dried herb is obtainable from health shops. [+ from]

    这种干药草能在保健店里买到。

  • ...delicious cheeses which are obtainable anywhere in France.

    在法国随处可以找到的美味奶酪

SYN available, to be had, procurable
