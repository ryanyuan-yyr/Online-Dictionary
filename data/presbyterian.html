presbyterian
ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪərɪən ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪriən ˌprɛsbɪˈtɪriən
1 ADJ 长老制的，长老会的（尤指苏格兰和美国的新教教会） Presbyterian means belonging or relating to a Protestant church, found especially in Scotland or the United States, which is governed by a body of official people all of equal rank. 长老制的，长老会的（尤指苏格兰和美国的新教教会）

  • ...a Presbyterian minister.

    长老会牧师

  • ...long Presbyterian sermons.

    长时间的长老会布道

2 N-COUNT 长老会教徒 A Presbyterian is a member of the Presbyterian church. 长老会教徒
