unemployment line
ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt laɪn
Word forms: plural unemployment lines
N-COUNT 失业状态；失业人群；失业队伍 When people talk about the unemployment line, they are talking about the state of being unemployed, especially when saying how many people are unemployed. 失业状态；失业人群；失业队伍 [US]

  • Many white-collar workers, like stock brokers and investment bankers, find themselves in the unemployment lines.

    许多白领，像股票经纪人和投资银行家，发现自己也失业了。
