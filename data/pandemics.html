pandemic
pændemɪk pænˈdɛmɪk
Word forms: plural pandemics
1 N-COUNT （疾病的）大流行，广泛传播 A pandemic is an occurrence of a disease that affects many people over a very wide area. （疾病的）大流行，广泛传播 [formal]

  • They feared a new cholera pandemic.

    他们担心新一轮的霍乱大流行。

  • One pandemic of Spanish flu took nearly 22 million lives worldwide.

    西班牙流感的大爆发夺去了全球近2,200万人的生命。
