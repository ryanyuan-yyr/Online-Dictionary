walk-in
ˈwɔkˌɪn
Word forms: walk-ins
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (壁橱或衣橱)人进得去的A walk-in closet or wardrobe is a closet or wardrobe that is large enough for someone to walk into. (壁橱或衣橱)人进得去的

  • Upstairs, the master bedroom has a walk-in wardrobe.

    楼上主卧室有一个可容纳一个人的大衣橱。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (诊所或医药中心)无需预约的A walk-in clinic or medical centre is one where you can get medical attention without making an appointment. (诊所或医药中心)无需预约的

  • Should blood tests be made more accessible to the public, such as through walk-in clinics in shopping malls?

    血液测试是否应该更面向大众呢，比如在大型购物中心里免预约的诊所内人们就可以接受血液测试？

3 N-COUNT 无需预约Walk-in is also a noun. 无需预约

  • They told him they didn't take walk-ins and shooed him away.

    他们说他们不接待没有预约过的人，然后把他赶走了。
