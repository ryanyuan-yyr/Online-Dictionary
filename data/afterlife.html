afterlife
ɑːftəʳlaɪf æf- ˈæftərˌlaɪf ˈɑftərˌlaɪf
(also after-life)Word forms: plural afterlives
N-COUNT [usually singular] 来世；来生 The afterlife is a life that some people believe begins when you die, for example a life in heaven or as another person or animal. 来世；来生
SYN life after death, heaven, paradise, nirvana [Buddhism , Hinduism]
