rub along
PHRASAL VERB-RECIP 与…保持友好关系;与…友好相处 If two people rub along or if one person rubs along with another, they are able to live or work together in a fairly friendly way, usually when you would not expect them to. 与…保持友好关系;与…友好相处 [British, informal]

  • North and South had officials at the meeting and they rubbed along tolerably. [plural-noun VERB PREP.]

    南北双方都有官员参加了这次会议，而且他们勉强和平相处。

  • Mr Nicholson respects soldiers, and he rubs along with them. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    尼科尔森先生对士兵很尊重，而且跟他们相处融洽。

SYN cope, manage, get along, make do
