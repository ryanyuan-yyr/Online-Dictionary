sell up
PHRASAL VERB 卖光(财物);变卖(家当) If you sell up, you sell everything you have, such as your house or your business, because you need the money. 卖光(财物);变卖(家当) [British]

  • ...all these farmers going out of business and having to sell up. [VERB PREP.]

    所有这些破产而不得不变卖家当的农场主

  • He advised Evans to sell up his flat and move away to the country. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他建议埃文斯卖掉公寓搬到乡下去住。
