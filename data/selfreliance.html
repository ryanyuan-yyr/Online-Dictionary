self-reliance
ˈsɛlfrɪˈlaɪəns
N-UNCOUNT 自主;自立;自力更生 Self-reliance is the ability to do things and make decisions by yourself, without needing other people to help you. 自主;自立;自力更生

    人们被迫学会了自力更生。
