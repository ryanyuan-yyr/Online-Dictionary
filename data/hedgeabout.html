hedge about
(also hedge around)
PHR-V-PASSIVE （以各种规则或条件）束缚，限制 If you say that something such as an offer is hedged about or is hedged around with rules or conditions, you mean that there are a lot of rules or conditions. （以各种规则或条件）束缚，限制

  • The offer was hedged around with conditions. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    这一提议附有诸多条件。

  • Many reduced fares are hedged around with restrictions. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    很多优惠费率都带有限制条件。
