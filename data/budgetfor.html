budget for
PHRASAL VERB 在编制预算时考虑 If you budget for something, you take account of it when you are deciding how much you can afford to spend on different things. 在编制预算时考虑

  • The authorities had budgeted for some non-payment. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政府在预算中考虑到了一些收不回来的款项。

  • The zoo was budgeting for 850,000 visitors this year. [VERB PREP. noun]

    动物园在编制预算时预计今年游客会达85万。
