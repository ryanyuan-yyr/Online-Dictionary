Word forms: plural safe areas
N-COUNT (参战国内由中立部队维持和平的)安全区 If part of a country that is involved in a war is declared to be a safe area, neutral forces will try to keep peace there so that it is safe for people. (参战国内由中立部队维持和平的)安全区
