collaborationist
kəlæbəreɪʃənɪst kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənɪst
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 通敌的;与敌人勾结的 A collaborationist government or individual is one that helps or gives support to the enemy during the war. 通敌的;与敌人勾结的 [disapproval]

  • Quinn headed the collaborationist government throughout the war.

    奎因在战争期间领导汉奸政府。
