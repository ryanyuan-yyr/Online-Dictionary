ghost-write
(also ghostwrite)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ghost-writes, present participle ghost-writing, past tense ghost-wrote, past participle ghost-written
VERB [usually passive] 由人代笔写（书或文章） If a book or other piece of writing is ghost-written, it is written by a writer for another person, for example a politician or sportsman, who then publishes it as his or her own work. 由人代笔写（书或文章）

  • Articles were ghost-written by company employees. [be VERB-ed]

    这些文章由公司的职员捉刀。
