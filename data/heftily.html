hefty
hefti ˈhɛfti
Word forms: comparative heftier, superlative heftiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （体积、重量或数量）巨大的 Hefty means large in size, weight, or amount. （体积、重量或数量）巨大的 [informal]

  • She was quite a hefty woman.

    她是个身材高大的女子。

  • If he is found guilty he faces a hefty fine.

    如果被判有罪，他就要面临巨额罚款。

SYN big, strong, massive, strapping  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有力的;猛烈的 A hefty movement is done with a lot of force. 有力的;猛烈的 [informal]

  • Lambert gave Luckwell a hefty shove to send him on his way.

    兰伯特用力推了勒克韦尔一把，把他送上了路。

SYN forceful, heavy, powerful, vigorous [slang]
