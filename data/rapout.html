rap out
PHRASAL VERB 大声急促地说出（命令或问题） If you rap out an order or a question, you say it quickly and sharply. 大声急促地说出（命令或问题）

  • She kept rapping out orders: 'Up a bit, down a bit, do it like that.'. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    她不断大声地发号施令：“向上点，向下点，就那样做。”

  • 'How do we know that?' rapped out Lester. [VERB PREP. with quote]

    “我们怎么会知道？”莱斯特突然大声说道。
