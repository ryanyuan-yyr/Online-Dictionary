benighted
bɪnaɪtɪd biˈnaɪtɪd bɪˈnaɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 不幸的；蒙昧的；无知的 If you describe people or the place where they live as benighted, you think they are unfortunate or do not know anything. 不幸的；蒙昧的；无知的 [literary, disapproval]

  • Famine hit that benighted country once more.

    饥荒再次席卷了那个蒙昧的国家。

SYN uncivilized, crude, primitive, backward
