introductory
ɪntrədʌktəri ˌɪntrəˈdʌktəri
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 引言的;导论的;介绍性的 An introductory remark, talk, or part of a book gives a small amount of general information about a particular subject, often before a more detailed explanation. 引言的;导论的;介绍性的

  • ...an introductory course in religion and theology.

    宗教与神学入门课程

SYN preliminary, elementary, first, early  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (新品)促销的，特惠的 An introductory offer or price on a new product is something such as a free gift or a low price that is meant to attract new customers. (新品)促销的，特惠的 [business]

  • ...just out on the shelves at an introductory price of £2.99.

    刚刚以2.99英镑的新品促销价上架

SYN starting, opening, initial, early
