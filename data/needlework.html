needlework
niːdəlwɜːʳk ˈnidəlˌwɜrk
1 N-UNCOUNT 缝制品;女红;刺绣品 Needlework is sewing or stitching that is done by hand. 缝制品;女红;刺绣品

  • She did beautiful needlework and she embroidered table napkins.

    她的针线活很漂亮，并且还会刺绣台布。

SYN embroidery, tailoring, stitching, sewing  
2 N-UNCOUNT 缝纫;刺绣;针线活 Needlework is the activity of sewing or stitching. 缝纫;刺绣;针线活

  • ...watching my mother and grandmothers doing needlework.

    看我妈妈和祖母外祖母做针线活
