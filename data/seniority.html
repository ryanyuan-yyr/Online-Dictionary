seniority
siːniɒrɪti -ɔːrɪti sinˈjɔrəti sinˈjɑrəti
N-UNCOUNT 资历;地位;级别 A person's seniority in an organization is the importance and power that they have compared with others, or the fact that they have worked there for a long time. 资历;地位;级别

  • He has said he will fire editorial employees without regard to seniority.

    他说过不管资历如何,任何编辑人员他都有可能解雇。

SYN superiority, rank, priority, precedence
