engaged
ɪngeɪdʒd ɛnˈgeɪdʒd ɪnˈgeɪdʒd
1 ADJ 从事…的;忙于…的 Someone who is engaged in or engaged on a particular activity is doing that thing. 从事…的;忙于…的 [formal]

  • They found the three engaged in target practice. [+ in]

    他们发现那3个人在练习打靶。

  • ...the various projects he was engaged on. [+ on]

    他参与的各种项目

SYN occupied, working, involved, committed  
2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 订婚的 When two people are engaged, they have agreed to marry each other. 订婚的 [Also + to]

  • We got engaged on my eighteenth birthday.

    在我18岁生日那天我们订婚了。

  • He was engaged to Miss Julia Maria Boardman.

    他和朱莉娅·玛丽亚·博德曼小姐订婚了。

  • ...the engaged couple.

    那对订婚的情侣

SYN betrothed [old-fashioned] , promised, pledged, affianced [old-fashioned]  
3 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （电话）占线的 If a telephone or a telephone line is engaged, it is already being used by someone else so that you are unable to speak to the person you are phoning. （电话）占线的 [British]

  • The line is engaged.

    电话占线。

  • We tried to call you back but you were engaged.

    我们试着给你回电，但是你正在通话中。

SYN in use, busy, tied up, unavailable  
in AM, use 美国英语用 busy
4 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （公共厕所）被占用的，使用中的 If a public toilet is engaged, it is already being used by someone else. （公共厕所）被占用的，使用中的 [British]
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 occupied
