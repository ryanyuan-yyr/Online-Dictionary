creamy
kriːmi ˈkrimi
Word forms: comparative creamier, superlative creamiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 含奶油的;含乳脂多的 Food or drink that is creamy contains a lot of cream or milk. 含奶油的;含乳脂多的

  • ...rich, creamy coffee.

    香浓诱滑的咖啡

  • ...a creamy chocolate and nut candy bar.

    果仁奶油巧克力棒糖

2 ADJ-GRADED (食物)光滑细腻的，柔软滑腻的 Food that is creamy has a soft smooth texture and appearance. (食物)光滑细腻的，柔软滑腻的

  • ...creamy mashed potato.

    柔软滑腻的土豆泥

  • Whisk the mixture until it is smooth and creamy.

    把混合物搅拌至均匀细腻。

  • ...a delicious soft blue cheese with a creamy texture.

    质地柔软滑腻的美味蓝纹奶酪

SYN smooth, soft, creamed, lush
