helm
helm hɛlm
Word forms: plural helms
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 舵柄;舵轮 The helm of a boat or ship is the part that is used to steer it. 舵柄;舵轮

  • I got into our dinghy while Willis took the helm and positioned the boat for the photograph.

    我钻进我们的小艇，威利斯掌舵，将船摆好位置准备拍照。

SYN tiller, wheel, rudder, steering gear  
2 N-SING （政府或组织中的）领导位置 You can say that someone is at the helm when they are leading or running a country or organization. （政府或组织中的）领导位置

  • He has been at the helm of Lonrho for 31 years.

    他执掌伦罗公司已达31年。

  • ...Yutaka Kume, who took the helm as Nissan's president in June 1985.

    1985年6月成为日产汽车社长的久米丰
