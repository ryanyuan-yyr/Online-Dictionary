stuck
stʌk stʌk
1 Stuck is the past tense and past participle of stick .
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 卡住的;动不了的;陷入…的 If something is stuck in a particular position, it is fixed tightly in this position and is unable to move. 卡住的;动不了的;陷入…的

  • He said his car had got stuck in the snow.

    他说他的汽车陷在了雪地里。

  • She had got something stuck between her teeth.

    她的牙缝里塞进了东西。

SYN fastened, fast, fixed, joined  
3 ADJ 困住的;无法离开的 If you are stuck in a place, you want to get away from it, but are unable to. 困住的;无法离开的

  • I was stuck at home with flu.

    感冒把我困在了家中。

  • Certainly, I wouldn't have wanted to be stuck on a desert island with her for any length of time.

    当然，我根本一刻也不想和她一起被困在荒岛上。

4 ADJ 陷入(无聊或不愉快局面)的;受困的;无法脱身的 If you are stuck in a boring or unpleasant situation, you are unable to change it or get away from it. 陷入(无聊或不愉快局面)的;受困的;无法脱身的

  • I don't want to get stuck in another job like that.

    我不想陷入另一份那样的工作中。

  • I am stuck in a relationship which I don't enjoy.

    我被一段自己并不喜欢的恋情困住了。

SYN trapped, caught, ensnared  
5 ADJ 停滞不前的;无所变化的 If something is stuck at a particular level or stage, it is not progressing or changing. 停滞不前的;无所变化的

  • The negotiations have got stuck on a number of key issues.

    谈判在一些重大问题上陷入了僵局。

  • US unemployment figures for March showed the jobless rate stuck at 7 per cent.

    3 月份的美国失业数据显示失业率一直徘徊在 7%上下。

  • The economy is still stuck in recession.

    经济仍处于萧条阶段。

6 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n] 无法摆脱的;甩不掉的 If you are stuck with something that you do not want, you cannot get rid of it. 无法摆脱的;甩不掉的

  • Many people are now stuck with expensive fixed-rate mortgages.

    许多人目前都有高额的定息按揭缠身。

  • I know you think I'm stupid, but we're stuck with each other, aren't we?

    我知道你认为我愚蠢，但我们俩谁都摆脱不了谁，不是吗？

SYN burdened, saddled, lumbered, landed  
7 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 难以继续的;被难倒的 If you get stuck when you are trying to do something, you are unable to continue doing it because it is too difficult. 难以继续的;被难倒的

  • They will be there to help if you get stuck.

    如果你遇到难题的话他们会帮助你。

  • If he gets stuck on a word, he can make the computer prompt him.

    如果他被某个单词绊住，他可以从电脑上获得提示。

SYN baffled, stumped, at a loss, beaten  
8 PHRASE 起劲地干;下定决心做 If you get stuck in, you do something with enthusiasm and determination. 起劲地干;下定决心做 [British, informal]

  • We're bottom of the league and we have to get stuck in.

    我们是联赛的最后一名，所以我们必须拼命赶超。
