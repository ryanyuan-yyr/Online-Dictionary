buy off
PHRASAL VERB 收买；贿赂 If you say that a person or organization buys off another person or group, you are criticizing the fact that they are giving them something such as money so that they will not complain or cause trouble. 收买；贿赂 [disapproval]

  • ...policies designed to buy off the working-class vote. [VERB PREP. noun]

    旨在收买劳工阶层选票的政策

  • In buying your children all these things, you are in a sense buying them off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    给孩子买下所有这些东西，从某种意义上说你是在收买他们。

SYN bribe, square, fix [informal] , pay off [informal]
