caulk
kɔk kɔːk kɔk
Word forms: caulks, caulking, caulked
1 V-T 补漏洞If you caulk something such as a boat, you fill small cracks in its surface in order to prevent it from leaking. 补漏洞

  • He'd offered to caulk the windows.

    他主动提出来帮助修补窗户漏缝。

2 N-UNCOUNT caulking

  • ...menial jobs like caulking.

    ...象填补缝隙一样的卑微工种。

3 N-UNCOUNT 填补物Caulk is a soft substance that is used to caulk something. 填补物

  • ...a caulk product that isn't sticky to the touch and can be used for quick fill-in jobs.

    ...一种摸上去不黏手并能用于快速填补工作的产品。
