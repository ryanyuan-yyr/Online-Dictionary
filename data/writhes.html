writhe
raɪð raɪð
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense writhes, present participle writhing, past tense , past participle writhed
VERB (通常指由于剧痛或不适而)扭动，翻滚 If you writhe, your body twists and turns violently backwards and forwards, usually because you are in great pain or discomfort. (通常指由于剧痛或不适而)扭动，翻滚

  • He was writhing in agony. [VERB]

    他痛苦地翻滚着。

  • The subject makes her writhe with embarrassment. [VERB]

    这个话题让她难堪得坐立不安。

  • The shark was writhing around wildly, trying to get free. [VERB adv./prep.]

    鲨鱼拼命挣扎，试图逃脱。

SYN squirm, struggle, twist, toss
