quicken
kwɪkən ˈkwɪkən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense quickens, present participle quickening, past tense , past participle quickened
V-ERG (使)加快;(使)加速;(使)变快 If something quickens or if you quicken it, it becomes faster or moves at a greater speed. (使)加快;(使)加速;(使)变快

  • Ainslie's pulse quickened in alarm. [VERB]

    由于惊恐，安斯利的脉搏加快了。

  • He quickened his pace a little. [VERB noun]

    他稍稍加快了步伐。

SYN speed up, hurry, accelerate, hasten
