slipstream
slɪpstriːm ˈslɪpˌstrim
Word forms: plural slipstreams
1 N-COUNT (汽车、飞机或轮船等高速运动的物体后形成的)滑流，向后气流，切向流 The slipstream of a fast-moving object such as a car, plane, or boat is the flow of air directly behind it. (汽车、飞机或轮船等高速运动的物体后形成的)滑流，向后气流，切向流

  • He left a host of other riders trailing in his slipstream.

    他把其他的摩托车手甩在身后。

2 N-COUNT [with poss] 追随;效仿 If you are in the slipstream of someone, you are following their example. 追随;效仿

  • ...in the slipstream of various literary greats.

    效仿多位文豪大家
