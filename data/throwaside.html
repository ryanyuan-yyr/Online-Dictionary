throw aside
PHRASAL VERB 放弃，拒绝接受(生活方式、原则、思想) If you throw aside a way of life, a principle, or an idea, you abandon it or reject it. 放弃，拒绝接受(生活方式、原则、思想)

  • Detectives threw aside professional training and caution, and looked for a different explanation. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    侦探们将受过的专业训练和小心谨慎抛之脑后，开始寻求另一种解释。

  • Exceptional patients have the ability to throw statistics aside to say, 'I can be a survivor'. [VERB noun PREP.]

    尽管统计数字凿凿，仍有个别病人能够做到将它抛在一边，相信自己可以活下来。
