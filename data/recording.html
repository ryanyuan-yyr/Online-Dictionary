recording
rɪkɔːʳdɪŋ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ
Word forms: plural recordings
1 N-COUNT 唱片；录音带；录像带 A recording of something is a record, CD, tape, or video of it. 唱片；录音带；录像带

  • ...a video recording of a police interview. [+ of]

    警方讯问的录像带

SYN record, video, tape, disc [old-fashioned]  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 录制 Recording is the process of making records, CDs, tapes, or videos. 录制

  • ...the recording industry.

    音像业
