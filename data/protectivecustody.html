protective custody
prəˈtɛktɪv ˈkʌstədɪ
N-UNCOUNT 保护性拘留；保护性监禁 If a witness in a court case is being held in protective custody, they are being kept in prison in order to prevent them from being harmed. 保护性拘留；保护性监禁

  • They might be doing me a good turn if they took me into protective custody.

    如果他们对我实行保护性拘留，可能对我是件好事。
