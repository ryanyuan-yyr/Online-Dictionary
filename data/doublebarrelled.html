double-barrelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 double-barreled
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (枪)双管的 A double-barrelled gun has two barrels. (枪)双管的

  • ...a double-barrelled shotgun.

    双管猎枪

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (姓氏)由两部分组成的 A double-barrelled surname has two parts which are joined by a hyphen, for example 'Miss J. Heydon-Smith'. (姓氏)由两部分组成的 [British]
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (计划等)有两个主要部分的，双重目的的 Double-barrelled is used to describe something such as a plan which has two main parts. (计划等)有两个主要部分的，双重目的的 [journalism]

  • The company announced a double-barreled investment and management-compensation plan.

    公司宣布了一份投资和管理补偿的双重计划。
