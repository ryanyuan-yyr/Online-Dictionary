cuddle
kʌdəl ˈkʌdəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cuddles, present participle cuddling, past tense , past participle cuddled
V-RECIP 拥抱;搂抱 If you cuddle someone, you put your arms round them and hold them close as a way of showing your affection. 拥抱;搂抱

  • He cuddled the newborn girl. [VERB noun]

    他抱着刚出生的女婴。

  • They used to kiss and cuddle in front of everyone. [VERB]

    他们过去经常在人前搂抱亲吻。

SYN pet [informal] , hug, canoodle [slang] , bill and coo  
Cuddle is also a noun.

  • Give her a cuddle.

    抱抱她。

Phrasal verbs:
cuddle up
