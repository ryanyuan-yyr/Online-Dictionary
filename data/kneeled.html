kneel
niːl nil
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense kneels, present participle kneeling, past tense , past participle kneeled, past tense , past participle knelt
The forms kneeled and knelt can both be used for the past tense and past participle.kneeled 和 knelt均可用作过去式和过去分词。
VERB 跪下；跪着 When you kneel, you bend your legs so that your knees are touching the ground. 跪下；跪着

  • She knelt by the bed and prayed. [VERB prep./adv.]

    她跪在床边祈祷。

  • Other people were kneeling, but she just sat. [VERB]

    其他人跪着，而她只是坐着。

  • ...a kneeling position. [VERB-ing]

    跪着的姿势

SYN genuflect, bow, stoop, curtsy or curtsey  
Kneel down means the same as kneel .kneel down 同 kneel

  • She kneeled down beside him.

    她在他旁边跪下。
