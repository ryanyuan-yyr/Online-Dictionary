rewarding
rɪwɔːʳdɪŋ rɪˈwɔrdɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 有益的;值得的;有意义的;令人满意的 An experience or action that is rewarding gives you satisfaction or brings you benefits. 有益的;值得的;有意义的;令人满意的

  • ...a career which she found stimulating and rewarding.

    她觉得刺激而有意义的职业

  • Life for the successful doctor can be emotionally and financially rewarding.

    对于事业有成的医生来说，生活在精神和物质两方面都能令人满足。

SYN satisfying, fulfilling, gratifying, edifying
