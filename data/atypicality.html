atypical
eɪtɪpɪkəl əˈtɪpɪk eɪˈtɪpɪk eɪˈtɪpɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 非典型的 Someone or something that is atypical is not typical of its kind. 非典型的

  • The economy of the province was atypical because it was particularly small.

    该省的经济比较特殊，因为它的规模特别小。

  • He was an atypical English schoolboy.

    他跟一般的英国男学生不太一样。

SYN unusual, exceptional, uncommon, singular
