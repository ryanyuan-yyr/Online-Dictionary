mousse
muːs mus
Word forms: plural mousses
1 N-VAR (用鸡蛋、奶油制成，通常加入水果和巧克力的)奶油冻，慕斯 Mousse is a sweet light food made from eggs and cream. It is often flavoured with fruit or chocolate. (用鸡蛋、奶油制成，通常加入水果和巧克力的)奶油冻，慕斯
2 N-MASS 定型发胶；摩丝 Mousse is a soft substance containing a lot of tiny bubbles, for example one that you can put in your hair to make it easier to shape into a particular style. 定型发胶；摩丝
