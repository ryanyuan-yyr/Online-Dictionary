capitalism
kæpɪtəlɪzəm ˈkæpətəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 资本主义(制度) Capitalism is an economic and political system in which property, business, and industry are owned by private individuals and not by the state. 资本主义(制度)

  • ...the return of capitalism to Hungary.

    匈牙利资本主义制度的回归

SYN private enterprise, free enterprise, private ownership, laissez faire or laisser faire
