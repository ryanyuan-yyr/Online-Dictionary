reload
riːloʊd riːˈləʊd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reloads, present participle reloading, past tense , past participle reloaded
VERB 再次给（枪）上膛;再装填 If someone reloads a gun, they load it again by putting in more bullets or explosive. If you reload a container, you fill it again. 再次给（枪）上膛;再装填

  • She reloaded the gun as quickly as she could. [VERB noun]

    她以最快的速度再次给枪装上子弹。

  • He reloaded and nodded to the gamekeeper. [VERB]

    他给枪重新装上子弹，然后朝猎场看守人点了点头。
