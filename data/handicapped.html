ADJ-GRADED 残疾的，有生理缺陷的，弱智的（可能具冒犯意味） Someone who is handicapped has a physical or mental disability that prevents them living a totally normal life. Many people who have a disability find this word offensive. 残疾的，有生理缺陷的，弱智的（可能具冒犯意味） [offensive]

    亚历克斯智力上有缺陷。
