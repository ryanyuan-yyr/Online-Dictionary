cobble together
PHRASAL VERB 粗制滥造;匆匆拼凑 If you say that someone has cobbled something together, you mean that they have made or produced it roughly or quickly. 粗制滥造;匆匆拼凑 [disapproval]

  • The group had cobbled together a few decent songs. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这个组合整过几首像样的歌。

  • You can cobble it together from any old combination of garments. [VERB noun PREP.]

    把以前的衣服随便搭配一下就能拼凑出一套。

SYN improvise, devise, put together, contrive
