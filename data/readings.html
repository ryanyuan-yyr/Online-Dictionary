reading
riːdɪŋ ˈridɪŋ
Word forms: plural readings
1 N-UNCOUNT 阅读；读书 Reading is the activity of reading books. 阅读；读书

  • I have always loved reading.

    我一直喜欢读书。

  • ...young people who find reading and writing difficult.

    觉得阅读和写作很难的年轻人

2 N-COUNT 读书会;朗诵会 A reading is an event at which poetry or extracts from books are read to an audience. 读书会;朗诵会

  • This year's event consisted of readings, lectures and workshops.

    今年的活动包括读书会、讲座和研讨会。

  • ...a poetry reading.

    诗歌朗诵会

SYN recital, performance, rendering, rendition  
3 N-COUNT 理解方法;解读方法 Your reading of a word, text, or situation is the way in which you understand or interpret it. 理解方法;解读方法

  • My reading of her character makes me feel that she was too responsible a person to do those things. [+ of]

    照我对她的性格的了解来看，她是个特别负责任的人，不会做出那些事情的。

  • Local public housing authorities disagree with this reading of the law.

    当地的公共住房管理当局不同意对该法律的这种解读。

4 N-COUNT （测量仪器上的）读数，计量 The reading on a measuring device is the figure or measurement that it shows. （测量仪器上的）读数，计量

  • Once you have recorded the reading, shake the thermometer down to below 36 degrees.

    一旦完成计数，把温度计标数甩到36度以下。

  • The gauge must be giving a faulty reading.

    量表的显示肯定有误。

SYN measurement, record, figure, indication  
5 N-COUNT （英国议会或美国国会讨论一项新法案以获通过的三步立法程序之一）议案宣读 In the British Parliament or the US Congress, a reading is one of the three stages of introducing and discussing a new bill before it can be passed as law. （英国议会或美国国会讨论一项新法案以获通过的三步立法程序之一）议案宣读

  • The bill is expected to pass its second reading with a comfortable majority.

    预计这项法案将以绝大多数赞成而通过议会第二次宣读。

6 PHRASE 读起来感觉… If you say that a book or an article makes interesting reading or makes for interesting reading, you mean that it is interesting to read. 读起来感觉…

  • The list of drinks, a dozen pages long, makes fascinating reading.

    酒水单一共有十几页长，读起来很有趣。

  • The report, called 'Child Poverty and Deprivation in the UK', makes for depressing reading.

    题为“英国儿童的贫困状况”的报告让人读起来心情沉重。
