unionism
juːnjənɪzəm ˈjunjənˌɪzəm
Word forms: plural unionists
1 N-UNCOUNT 联合主义；统一主义 Unionism is any set of political principles based on the idea that two or more political or national units should be joined or remain together, for example that Northern Ireland should remain part of the United Kingdom. 联合主义；统一主义
unionist

  • ...traditional unionists fearful of home rule.

    害怕地方自治的传统联合主义者

2 N-UNCOUNT 同 trade unionism Unionism is the same as trade unionism . 同 trade unionism
unionist

  • As a former unionist, he'll be more sensitive to the demands of the people.

    作为曾经的工会主义者，他会对民众的要求更加敏感。
