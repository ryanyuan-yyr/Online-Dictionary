realignment
riːəlaɪnmənt
Word forms: plural realignments
N-VAR （公司、经济或体系的）重组 If a company, economy, or system goes through a realignment, it is organized or arranged in a new way. （公司、经济或体系的）重组

  • ...a realignment of the existing political structure. [+ of]

    现存政治结构的重组

SYN readjustment, restructuring, shake-up, reshuffling
