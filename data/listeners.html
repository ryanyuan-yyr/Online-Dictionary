listener
lɪsnəʳ
Word forms: plural listeners
1 N-COUNT （电台广播的）收听者，听众之一 A listener is a person who listens to the radio or to a particular radio programme. （电台广播的）收听者，听众之一

  • I'm a regular listener to her show. [+ to]

    我经常收听她的节目。

  • Each week Dr Buczacki chooses a listener's question and gives a detailed answer.

    每个星期布柴齐医生都会选取一位听众的问题，给予详细回答。

2 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] 倾听他人说话的人 If you describe someone as a good listener, you mean that they listen carefully and sympathetically to you when you talk, for example about your problems. 倾听他人说话的人

  • Dr Brian was a good listener.

    布莱恩医生会耐心倾听他人说话。

  • If you can be a sympathetic listener, it may put your own problems in perspective.

    如果你能充满同情心地倾听别人的说话，也许就能够正确地认识自己的问题。
