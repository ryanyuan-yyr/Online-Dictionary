some day
sʌm deɪ
(also someday)
ADV [ADVERB with verb] （将来的）某一天;有朝一日 Some day means at a date in the future that is unknown or that has not yet been decided. （将来的）某一天;有朝一日

  • Some day I'll be a pilot.

    有朝一日，我会成为一名飞行员。

  • He took her left hand, hoping that it would someday bear a gold ring on the third finger.

    他捧起她的左手，希望这只手的中指有朝一日会戴上一枚金戒指。
