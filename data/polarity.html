polarity
poʊlærɪti poʊˈlærəti
Word forms: plural polarities
N-VAR 完全相反；截然对立 If there is apolarity between two people or things, they are completely different from each other in some way. 完全相反；截然对立 [formal]

  • ...the ideological polarity between nations.

    国家间意识形态的截然对立

  • ...the polarities of good and evil.

    善与恶的针锋相对

  • In him the polarities of life are resolved and balanced: male and female, strength and compassion, severity and mercy.

    在他身上，生命的两面性达到了调和与平衡：阳刚和阴柔、强悍和怜悯，还有严厉和仁慈。

SYN opposition, contradiction, paradox, ambivalence
