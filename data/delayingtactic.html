delaying tactic
dɪˈleɪɪŋ ˈtæktɪk
Word forms: plural delaying tactics
N-COUNT [usually plural] 拖延战术 Delaying tactics are things that someone does in order to deliberately delay the start or progress of something. 拖延战术

  • Ministers are using delaying tactics to postpone the report yet again.

    部长们再次使用拖延战术来推后报告的时间。
