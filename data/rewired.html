rewire
riːwaɪəʳ riːˈwaɪə riˈwaɪr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rewires, present participle rewiring, past tense , past participle rewired
VERB 给…更换电线;给…重新布线 If someone rewires a building or an electrical appliance, a new system of electrical wiring is put into it. 给…更换电线;给…重新布线

  • Their first job was to rewire the whole house and install central heating. [VERB noun]

    他们的第一份工作就是给整个房子重新布线，并安装中央供暖系统。

  • I have had to spend a lot of money having my house replumbed and rewired. [have noun VERB-ed]

    我只好花了一大笔钱给房子重新布线铺管。
