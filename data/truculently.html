truculent
trʌkjʊlənt ˈtrʌkjulənt ˈtrʌkjələnt
ADJ-GRADED 暴躁的;易怒的;好斗的;尖刻的 If you say that someone is truculent, you mean that they are bad-tempered and aggressive. 暴躁的;易怒的;好斗的;尖刻的
SYN hostile, defiant, belligerent, bad-tempered
