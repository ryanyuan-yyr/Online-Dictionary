intemperate
ɪntempərət ɪnˈtɛmpərɪt ɪnˈtɛmprɪt
ADJ-GRADED 无节制的;过度的;过激的 If you describe someone's words as intemperate, you are critical of them because they are too forceful and uncontrolled. 无节制的;过度的;过激的 [formal, disapproval]

  • The tone of the article is intemperate.

    文章的语气过激了。

  • ...the unwisely intemperate language of the party chairman.

    该党主席愚蠢失当的言辞

SYN excessive, extreme, over the top [slang] , wild
