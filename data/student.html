student
stjuːdənt stuː- ˈstudənt ˈstjudənt
Word forms: plural students
1 N-COUNT (大学或学院的)学生;大学生 A student is a person who is studying at a university or college. (大学或学院的)学生;大学生

  • Warren's eldest son is an art student, at St Martin's.

    沃伦的大儿子是圣马丁学院艺术系的学生。

  • ...a 23-year-old medical student.

    23 岁的医科大学生

SYN undergraduate, scholar, postgraduate, fresher [British , informal]  
2See also:mature student 
3 N-COUNT 中学生 A student is a child who is studying at a secondary school. 中学生
4 N-COUNT 研究者;学者 Someone who is a student of a particular subject is interested in the subject and spends time learning about it. 研究者;学者

  • ...a passionate student of nineteenth century history. [+ of]

    热衷于 19 世纪历史的研究者

SYN learner, observer, trainee, apprentice
