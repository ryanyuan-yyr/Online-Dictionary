blend
blend blɛnd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense blends, present participle blending, past tense , past participle blended
1 V-RECIP-ERG （使）混合；调和；掺和 If you blend substances together or if they blend, you mix them together so that they become one substance. （使）混合；调和；掺和

  • Blend the butter with the sugar and beat until light and creamy. [VERB noun + with]

    把糖掺入黄油然后搅拌至滑软细腻。

  • Blend the ingredients until you have a smooth cream. [VERB noun]

    将各种配料调和均匀做成乳脂。

  • Put the soap and water in a pan and leave to stand until they have blended. [VERB]

    将肥皂和水放入平底锅中不动，然后让它们溶成一体。

  • Most whiskies are blended whiskies. [VERB-ed]

    大多数的威士忌都是调和而成的。

SYN mix, join, combine, compound  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] （有用或令人愉快的事物的）融合，混合，组合 A blend of things is a mixture or combination of them that is useful or pleasant. （有用或令人愉快的事物的）融合，混合，组合

  • The public areas offer a subtle blend of traditional charm with modern amenities. [+ of]

    这些公共场所将传统魅力和现代设施巧妙地融合在了一起。

  • ...a blend of wine and sparkling water.

    酒和汽水的混合饮料

  • He makes up his own blends of flour.

    他自己和了一些面粉。

SYN mixture, cross, mix, combination  
3 V-RECIP （颜色、声音或风格）融合，交融，协调 When colours, sounds, or styles blend, they come together or are combined in a pleasing way. （颜色、声音或风格）融合，交融，协调

  • You could paint the walls and ceilings the same colour so they blend together. [VERB]

    你可以把墙和天花板粉刷成同一种颜色，这样看上去浑然一体。

  • ...the picture, furniture and porcelain collections that blend so well with the house itself. [VERB + with]

    与房间本身非常搭调的图画、家具和瓷器收藏品

4 VERB 结合，融合，综合（想法、政策和风格） If you blend ideas, policies, or styles, you use them together in order to achieve something. 结合，融合，综合（想法、政策和风格）

  • His 'cosmic vision' is to blend Christianity with 'the wisdom of all world religions'. [VERB noun + with]

    他的“远大设想”就是将基督教和“全世界所有宗教的智慧”融为一体。

  • ...a band that blended jazz, folk and classical music. [VERB noun]

    将爵士乐、民乐以及古典乐糅合在一起的乐队

SYN combine, mix, link, integrate
