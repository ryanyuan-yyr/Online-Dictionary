paddy
pædi ˈpædi
Word forms: plural paddies
1 N-COUNT 稻田；水田 A paddy or a paddy field is a field that is kept flooded with water and is used for growing rice. 稻田；水田

  • ...the paddy fields of China.

    中国的水田

2 N-COUNT; N-VOC 爱尔兰佬（可能具冒犯意味） A Paddy is an Irishman. This use could cause offence. 爱尔兰佬（可能具冒犯意味） [informal]
