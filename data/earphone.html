earphone
ɪəʳfoʊn ˈɪəˌfəʊn ˈɪrˌfoʊn
Word forms: plural earphones
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 耳机 Earphones are a small piece of equipment which you wear over or inside your ears so that you can listen to a radio or cassette recorder without anyone else hearing. 耳机
2 N-COUNT (电话)听筒 An earphone is the part of a telephone receiver or other device that you hold up to your ear or put into your ear. (电话)听筒 [mainly US]
