gain on
PHRASAL VERB 接近，逼近（前面的人或事物） If you gain on someone or something that is moving in front of you, you gradually get closer to them. 接近，逼近（前面的人或事物）

  • In some places he was quicker and at others I'd gain on him. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他在某些地方比我快，而我会在其他地方追上他。

  • The Mercedes began to gain on the van. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那辆奔驰开始逼近小货车。
