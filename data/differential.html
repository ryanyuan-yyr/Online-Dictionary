differential
dɪfərenʃəl ˌdɪfərˈɛnʃəl
Word forms: plural differentials
1 N-COUNT (数学中的)微分;(经济学中的)差价，差额 In mathematics and economics, a differential is a difference between two values in a scale. (数学中的)微分;(经济学中的)差价，差额

  • Germany and France pledged to maintain the differential between their two currencies.

    德国和法国承诺保持两国货币之间的差别汇率。

2 N-COUNT 差别;（尤指）工资级差 A differential is a difference between things, especially rates of pay. 差别;（尤指）工资级差 [mainly British]

  • During the Second World War, industrial wage differentials in Britain widened.

    第二次世界大战期间，英国的劳工工资级差进一步拉大了。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 差别的;级差的;依差别而定的 Differential means relating to or using a difference between groups or things. 差别的;级差的;依差别而定的 [formal]

  • ...differential voting rights.

    差别投票

SYN distinctive, different, contrasting, distinguishing
