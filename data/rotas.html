rota
roʊtə ˈroʊtə
Word forms: plural rotas
N-COUNT 值班表;值勤表;勤务薄 A rota is a list which gives details of the order in which different people have to do a particular job. 值班表;值勤表;勤务薄 [mainly British]

  • I suggest that you work out a careful rota which will make it clear who tidies the room on which day.

    我建议你制定一个详细的值班表，明确哪一天由谁整理房间。

  • ...the washing-up rota.

    洗碗轮值表

  • The tea is prepared on a rota basis by the lady members.

    女成员根据值班表轮流准备茶水。

SYN schedule, list, calendar, timetable
