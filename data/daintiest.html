dainty
deɪnti ˈdeɪnti
Word forms: comparative daintier, superlative daintiest
ADJ-GRADED （动作）文雅的;（人）娇小的，秀丽的;（物）小巧的，精致的 If you describe a movement, person, or object as dainty, you mean that they are small, delicate, and pretty. （动作）文雅的;（人）娇小的，秀丽的;（物）小巧的，精致的

  • The girls were dainty and feminine.

    这些女孩子娇美温柔。

  • ...dainty pink flowers.

    娇艳的粉色花朵

SYN delicate, pretty, charming, fine  
daintily

  • She walked daintily down the steps.

    她优雅地走下台阶。
