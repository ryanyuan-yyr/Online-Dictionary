undertow
ʌndəʳtoʊ ˈʌndərˌtoʊ
Word forms: plural undertows
1 N-COUNT 潜在的情绪 If there is an undertow of a feeling, that feeling exists in such a weak form that you are hardly aware of it, but it influences the way you think or behave. 潜在的情绪

  • The existence of an emotional undertow is an aspect of all politics.

    具有情绪感召力是所有政治的一个方面。

  • ...an undertow of sadness. [+ of]

    隐隐的悲伤

2 N-COUNT 潜流;下层逆流 An undertow is a strong current of water that is moving below the surface current and in a different direction to it. 潜流;下层逆流

  • Dangerous undertows make swimming unsafe along most of the coastline.

    由于存在着危险的下层逆流，在大部分海岸线附近游泳都不安全。
