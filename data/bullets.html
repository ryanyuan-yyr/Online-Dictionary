bullet
bʊlɪt ˈbʊlɪt
Word forms: plural bullets
1 N-COUNT 子弹 A bullet is a small piece of metal with a pointed or rounded end, which is fired out of a gun. 子弹
SYN projectile, ball, shot, missile  
2See also:plastic bullet  rubber bullet 
3 PHRASE 硬着头皮做；咬紧牙关应付 If someone bites the bullet, they accept that they have to do something unpleasant but necessary. 硬着头皮做；咬紧牙关应付 [journalism]

  • Tour operators may be forced to bite the bullet and cut prices.

    旅行社的经营者们可能不得不忍痛降价。

SYN grasp the nettle
