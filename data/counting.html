counting
kaʊntɪŋ ˈkaʊntɪŋ
1 PREP 包括…在内 Not counting a particular thing means not including that thing. Counting a particular thing means including that thing. 包括…在内

  • ...an average operating profit of 15% to 16% of sales, not counting administrative expenses.

    不包括管理开支在内，销售营业利润平均为15%到16%

2 PHRASE (数量)继续增长的 If you say and counting after a number or an amount of something, you mean that the number or amount is continuing to increase. (数量)继续增长的

  • There is a 1,700-year-old tea tree still living in southern China which is more than 100 feet tall and counting.

    中国南方生长着一棵已有1,700年历史的茶树，高100多英尺，而且还在不断长高。
