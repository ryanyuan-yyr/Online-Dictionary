enterprise zone
Word forms: plural enterprise zones
N-COUNT （常指贫困地区的）创业园，企业振兴区 An enterprise zone is an area, usually a depressed or inner-city area, where the government offers incentives in order to attract new businesses. （常指贫困地区的）创业园，企业振兴区 [business]

  • Because it is in an enterprise zone, taxes on non-food items are 3.5% instead of the usual 7%.

    因为是在创业园区，非食品类产品的税率是3.5%，而不是通常的7%。
