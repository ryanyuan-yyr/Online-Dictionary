psychotic
saɪkɒtɪk saɪˈkɑtɪk
Word forms: plural psychotics
ADJ 患有精神病的；精神错乱的 Someone who is psychotic has a type of severe mental illness. 患有精神病的；精神错乱的 [medicine]

  • The man, who police believe is psychotic, is thought to be responsible for eight attacks.

    这个警方认定为精神错乱的男子被认为是8起袭击事件的制造者。

Psychotic is also a noun.

  • A religious psychotic in Las Vegas has killed four people.

    拉斯维加斯一名患有精神病的宗教狂热分子已经杀死了4人。
