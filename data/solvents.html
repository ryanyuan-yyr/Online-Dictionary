solvent
sɒlvənt ˈsɑlvənt
Word forms: plural solvents
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 有清偿能力的 If a person or a company is solvent, they have enough money to pay all their debts. 有清偿能力的 [business]

  • They're going to have to show that the company is now solvent.

    他们必须要证明公司目前具备偿还能力。

SYN financially sound, secure, in the black, solid  
2 N-MASS 溶剂 A solvent is a liquid that can dissolve other substances. 溶剂

  • ...a small amount of cleaning solvent.

    少量的洗洁精

  • ...industrial solvents.

    工业溶剂

SYN resolvent, dissolvent [rare]
