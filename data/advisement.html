advisement
ædvaɪzmənt ədˈvaɪzmənt ædˈvaɪzmənt ədˈvaɪzmənt
PHRASE （常指请专家）对…作周密考虑 If someone in authority takes a matter under advisement, they decide that the matter needs to be considered more carefully, often by experts. （常指请专家）对…作周密考虑 [US, formal]

  • I will take the suggestion under advisement, and refer it to the board.

    对于这项建议，我将咨询专家意见作周密考虑并且提交给董事会处理。
