median
miːdiən ˈmidiən
Word forms: plural medians
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 中间值的;中位数的;中间的 The median value of a set of values is the middle one when they are arranged in order. For example, if a group of five students take a test and their marks are 5, 7, 7, 8, and 10, the median mark is 7. 中间值的;中位数的;中间的 [technical]
SYN middle, mid, halfway, medial  
2 N-COUNT 同 median strip A median is the same as a median strip . 同 median strip [US]
