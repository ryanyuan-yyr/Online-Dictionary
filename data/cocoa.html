cocoa
koʊkoʊ ˈkoʊˌkoʊ
1 N-UNCOUNT 可可粉 Cocoa is a brown powder made from the seeds of a tropical tree. It is used in making chocolate. 可可粉

  • The Ivory Coast became the world's leading cocoa producer.

    象牙海岸成为世界上可可粉的主要产地。

  • ...cocoa beans.

    可可豆

2 N-UNCOUNT 热可可(饮料) Cocoa is a hot drink made from cocoa powder and milk or water. 热可可(饮料)

  • My wife was tucked up in bed with her cup of cocoa.

    我老婆端着一杯热可可窝在床上。
