gram
græm ɡrɑːm græm
Word forms: plural grams
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 gramme
N-COUNT 克（重量单位） A gram is a unit of weight. One thousand grams are equal to one kilogram. 克（重量单位）

  • A football weighs about 400 grams.

    足球重约400克。

-gram -græm græm
Word forms: plural -grams
COMB in N-COUNT （用以构成复合名词）表示“乔装打扮成向…传递讯息的人” -gram combines with nouns to form other nouns which refer to someone who dresses up in order to bring a message to someone else, as a practical joke. （用以构成复合名词）表示“乔装打扮成向…传递讯息的人”

  • Now he has only six or seven kissogram girls on his books.

    现在他手头只有六七个香吻女郎。
