curate
kjʊərət kjʊreɪt kjʊəˈreɪt ˈkjʊrɪt ˈkjʊrˌeɪt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense curates, present participle curating, past tense , past participle curated
The noun is pronounced /'kjʊərət/. The verb is pronounced /kjʊ'reɪt/. 名词读作 /'kjʊərət/，动词读作 /kjʊ'reɪt/。
1 N-COUNT (圣公会的)助理牧师 A curate is a clergyman in the Anglican Church who helps the priest. (圣公会的)助理牧师
2 VERB [usually passive] 组织(展览) If an exhibition is curated by someone, they organize it. 组织(展览)

  • The Hayward exhibition has been curated by the artist Bernard Luthi. [be VERB-ed + by]

    海沃德艺术展是由艺术家伯纳德·吕蒂组织的。
