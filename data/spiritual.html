spiritual
spɪrɪtʃuəl ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl
Word forms: plural spirituals
1 ADJ 精神(上)的;心灵(上)的 Spiritual means relating to people's thoughts and beliefs, rather than to their bodies and physical surroundings. 精神(上)的;心灵(上)的

  • She lived entirely by spiritual values, in a world of poetry and imagination.

    她追求精神上的满足，完全生活在一个诗歌和想象的世界里。

SYN nonmaterial, metaphysical, other-worldly, ethereal  
spiritually

  • Our whole programme is spiritually oriented but not religious.

    我们的整个节目都是关注心灵的，但却不是宗教性的。

spirituality

  • ...the peaceful spirituality of Japanese culture.

    日本文化的和平精神

2 ADJ 宗教(上)的；宗教信仰(上)的 Spiritual means relating to people's religious beliefs. 宗教(上)的；宗教信仰(上)的

  • The spiritual leader of Ireland's 3.7 million Catholics.

    爱尔兰 370 万天主教徒的宗教领袖

  • A man in priestly clothes offered spiritual guidance.

    一位身着牧师袍服的男子进行了宗教布道。

SYN sacred, religious, holy, divine  
3 N-COUNT 灵歌(原为美国黑奴所唱的一种宗教歌曲) A spiritual is a religious song of the type originally sung by black slaves in America. 灵歌(原为美国黑奴所唱的一种宗教歌曲)
4 PHRASE [NOUN inflects] 精神家园;精神故乡 Your spiritual home is the place where you feel that you belong, usually because your ideas or attitudes are the same as those of the people who live there. 精神家园;精神故乡
