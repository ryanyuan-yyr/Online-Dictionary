unsure
ʌnʃʊəʳ ʌnˈʃʊə
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 心虚的；缺乏信心的 If you are unsure of yourself, you lack confidence. 心虚的；缺乏信心的

  • He made her feel hot, and awkward, and unsure of herself. [+ of]

    他令她内心躁动，手足无措，对自己没有信心。

  • The evening show was terrible, with hesitant unsure performances from all.

    晚上的演出很糟，所有人都缩手缩脚，显得很不自信。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 不确定的；不清楚的；拿不准的 If you are unsure about something, you feel uncertain about it. 不确定的；不清楚的；拿不准的

  • Fifty-two per cent were unsure about the idea. [+ about]

    五成二的人对这个想法存有疑虑。

  • Scientists are becoming increasingly unsure of the validity of this technique. [+ of]

    科学家们越来越拿不准这一技术是否是正当合法的。

SYN doubtful, sceptical, dubious, suspicious
