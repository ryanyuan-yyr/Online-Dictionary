quadriplegic
kwɒdrɪpliːdʒɪk
Word forms: plural quadriplegics
1 N-COUNT 四肢麻痹者;四肢瘫痪者 A quadriplegic is a person who is permanently unable to use their arms and legs. 四肢麻痹者;四肢瘫痪者
Quadriplegic is also an adjective.

  • He is now quadriplegic and confined to a wheelchair.

    现在的他四肢麻痹，只能呆在轮椅上。
