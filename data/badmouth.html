bad-mouth
bædmaʊð ˈbædˌmaʊθ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bad-mouths, present participle bad-mouthing, past tense , past participle bad-mouthed
VERB （尤指背后）说…的坏话，指责，诽谤 If someone bad-mouths you, they say unpleasant things about you, especially when you are not there to defend yourself. （尤指背后）说…的坏话，指责，诽谤
