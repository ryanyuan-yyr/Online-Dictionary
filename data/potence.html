potent
poʊtənt ˈpoʊtənt
ADJ-GRADED 很有效的；强有力的 Something that is potent is very effective and powerful. 很有效的；强有力的

  • Their most potent weapon was the Exocet missile.

    飞鱼导弹是他们最有攻击力的武器。

  • The drug is extremely potent, but causes unpleasant side effects.

    这种药药效极强，但会产生不良的副作用。

SYN strong, powerful, mighty, vigorous
