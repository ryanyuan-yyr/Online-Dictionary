hue and cry
1 N (旧)大喊大叫着追缉嫌犯的做法(formerly) the pursuit of a suspected criminal with loud cries in order to raise the alarm (旧)大喊大叫着追缉嫌犯的做法
2 PHRASE 愤怒抗议If people raise a hue and cry about something, they protest angrily about it. 愤怒抗议 [written]

  • Just as the show ended, he heard a huge hue and cry outside.

    正当演出结束的时候，他听到外面一阵喧嚣的愤怒抗议。
