holiness
hoʊlinəs ˈhoʊlinɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT 神圣 Holiness is the state or quality of being holy. 神圣

  • The holiness of God reveals one's own sin.

    上帝的神圣揭示世人自身的罪孽。

  • We were immediately struck by this city's holiness.

    我们立即就被这座城市的神圣氛围所打动。

SYN sanctity, spirituality, sacredness, purity  
2 N-VOC （对天主教教皇或某些其他宗教领袖的尊称）陛下，圣座 You say Your Holiness or His Holiness when you address or refer respectfully to the Pope or to leaders of some other religions. （对天主教教皇或某些其他宗教领袖的尊称）陛下，圣座 [politeness]

  • The President received His Holiness at the White House.

    总统在白宫接见了教皇陛下。
