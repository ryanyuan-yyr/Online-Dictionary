responsible
rɪspɒnsɪbəl rɪˈspɑnsəbəl
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 负有责任的;应承担责任的 If someone or something is responsible for a particular event or situation, they are the cause of it or they can be blamed for it. 负有责任的;应承担责任的

  • He still felt responsible for her death. [+ for]

    他仍然觉得自己对她的死负有责任。

  • I want you to do everything you can to find out who's responsible.

    我希望你能尽一切可能找出责任人。

SYN to blame, guilty, at fault, culpable  
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 对(某事物)负责的 If you are responsible for something, it is your job or duty to deal with it and make decisions relating to it. 对(某事物)负责的

  • ...the minister responsible for the environment. [+ for]

    负责环境工作的部长

  • The man responsible for finding the volunteers is Dr. Charles Weber.

    负责寻找志愿者的人是查尔斯·韦伯博士。

SYN in charge, in control, at the helm, in authority  
3 ADJ 对(某人或某团体)负责的 If you are responsible to a person or group, they have authority over you and you have to report to them about what you do. 对(某人或某团体)负责的

  • I'm responsible to my board of directors. [+ to]

    我对董事会负责。

  • The government will be responsible to the President alone.

    政府只对总统一人负责。

SYN accountable, subject, bound, liable  
4 ADJ-GRADED 负责的;有责任心的;可靠的 Responsible people behave properly and sensibly, without needing to be supervised. 负责的;有责任心的;可靠的

  • He's a very responsible sort of person.

    他是个非常有责任心的人。

  • He feels that the media should be more responsible in what they report.

    他觉得媒体应该对报道的内容更负责任些。

SYN sensible, sound, adult, stable  
responsibly

  • He urged everyone to act responsibly.

    他敦促大家做事要有责任心。

5 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] 责任重大的 Responsible jobs involve making important decisions or carrying out important tasks. 责任重大的

  • I work in a government office. It's a responsible position, I suppose, but not very exciting.

    我在政府机关工作。我想那是一个责任重大的职位，但并不是很有意思。

  • They have been demoted to less responsible jobs.

    他们被降职去做不太重要的工作。

SYN authoritative, high, important, executive
