freehold
friːhoʊld ˈfriˌhoʊld
Word forms: plural freeholds
1 N-VAR (对建筑物或土地的)终身保有权 If you have the freehold of a building or piece of land, it is yours for life and there are no conditions regarding your ownership. (对建筑物或土地的)终身保有权

  • People owning leasehold homes will be given a new right to buy the freehold of their property.

    房屋租赁者将获得购买所住房产终身保有权的新权利。

2 ADJ （建筑物或土地）可终身保有的 If a building or piece of land is freehold, you can own it for life. （建筑物或土地）可终身保有的

  • The property register will also say whether the property is freehold or leasehold.

    房产注册簿上也会说明房产是终身保有的还是租借的。
