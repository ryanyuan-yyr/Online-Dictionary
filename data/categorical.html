categorical
kætɪgɒrɪkəl -gɔːr- ˌkætəˈgɔrɪkəl ˌkætəˈgɑrɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 确定无疑的;肯定的;坚定的 If you are categorical about something, you state your views very definitely and firmly. 确定无疑的;肯定的;坚定的

  • ...his categorical denial of the charges of sexual harassment.

    他对性骚扰指控的断然否认

  • He is quite categorical that the UN should only help the innocent civilian population.

    他非常坚定地认为，联合国应该只援助无辜的平民。

SYN absolute, direct, express, positive  
categorically

  • They totally and categorically deny the charges.

    他们彻底而又坚决地否认了那些指控。

  • He stated categorically that this would be his last season in Formula One.

    他明确宣布，这将是他参加一级方程式赛车的最后一个赛季。
