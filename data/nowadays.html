nowadays
naʊədeɪz ˈnaʊəˌdeɪz
ADV 如今;现今 Nowadays means at the present time, in contrast with the past. 如今;现今

  • Nowadays it's acceptable for women to be ambitious. But it wasn't then.

    如今，女性胸怀大志也能被接受，但那时候不是这样。

  • I don't see much of Tony nowadays.

    我现在不经常见到托尼了。

SYN now, today, at the moment, these days
