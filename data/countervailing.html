countervailing
kaʊntəʳveɪlɪŋ ˈkaʊntəveɪlɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 抗衡的;抵消的 A countervailing force, power, or opinion is one which is of equal strength to another one but is its opposite or opposes it. 抗衡的;抵消的 [formal]

  • Their strategy is expansionist and imperialist, and it is greatest in effect, of course, when there is no countervailing power.

    他们的策略是扩张性的、带有帝国主义特色，当没有抗衡力量的时候自然也就被发挥到了极致。

  • There were two central and countervailing forces in the life of Nikola Tesla.

    在尼古拉·特斯拉的生活中存在着两股非常重要而且相互抗衡的力量。
