pillar
pɪləʳ ˈpɪlər
Word forms: plural pillars
1 N-COUNT 柱；柱子 A pillar is a tall solid structure, which is usually used to support part of a building. 柱；柱子

  • ...the pillars supporting the roof.

    支撑屋顶的柱子

SYN support, post, column, piling  
2 N-COUNT （制度、协议等的）支柱，核心 If something is the pillar of a system or agreement, it is the most important part of it or what makes it strong and successful. （制度、协议等的）支柱，核心

  • The pillar of her economic policy was keeping tight control over money supply. [+ of]

    她经济政策的核心是严格控制货币的供应。

  • ...the last pillar of apartheid.

    种族隔离制最后的支柱

3 N-COUNT 中坚；栋梁；台柱 If you describe someone as a pillar of society or as a pillar of the community, you approve of them because they play an important and active part in society or in the community. 中坚；栋梁；台柱 [approval]

  • My father had been a pillar of the community. [+ of]

    我父亲一直是社区的顶梁柱。

  • ...well-respected pillars of society.

    深受尊重的社会栋梁

SYN supporter, leader, rock, worthy
