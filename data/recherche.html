ADJ 精致的; 与喜欢不寻常之物及珍品的人有关的 If you describe something as recherché, you mean that it is very sophisticated or is associated with people who like things that are unusual and of a very high quality. 精致的; 与喜欢不寻常之物及珍品的人有关的 [formal]

  • Only extra-virgin olive oil will do on recherché dinner tables.

    只有特纯橄榄油才能上讲究的餐桌。

SYN refined, rare, exotic, esoteric
