thirsty
θɜːʳsti ˈθɜrsti
Word forms: comparative thirstier, superlative thirstiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 渴的;干渴的 If you are thirsty, you feel a need to drink something. 渴的;干渴的

  • If a baby is thirsty, it feeds more often.

    婴儿要是渴了，就会吃得更频繁。

  • Drink whenever you feel thirsty during exercise.

    运动过程中渴了就喝点水。

SYN parched, dry, dehydrated  
thirstily

  • The child nodded, drinking her milk thirstily.

    那个孩子边大口地喝着牛奶边点了点头。

2 ADJ-GRADED 渴望的;渴求的 If you are thirsty for something, you have a strong desire for it. 渴望的;渴求的 [literary]

  • People should understand how thirsty for revenge they are. [+ for]

    人们应该理解他们复仇的愿望是多么强烈。

SYN eager for, longing for, hungry for, dying for
