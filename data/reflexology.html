reflexology
riːfleksɒlədʒi ˌriflɛksˈɑlədʒi
Word forms: plural reflexologists
N-UNCOUNT （按摩足部等身体部位以达到治疗效果的）反射疗法 Reflexology is the practice of massaging particular areas of the body, especially the feet, in the belief that it can heal particular organs. （按摩足部等身体部位以达到治疗效果的）反射疗法
reflexologist

  • A reflexologist can often tell what is wrong with his client by the condition of certain parts of the feet.
