grandstanding
grændstændɪŋ ˈɡrstændɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 哗众取宠；作秀 Grandstanding means behaving in a way that makes people pay attention to you instead of thinking about more important matters. 哗众取宠；作秀 [mainly US]

  • Opponents of the measure say it's political grandstanding that could prove devastating to the economy.

    反对该措施的人士认为这是一场政治作秀，会对经济发展带来严重的打击。
