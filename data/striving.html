strive
straɪv straɪv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense strives, present participle striving
The past tense is either strove or strived, and the past participle is either striven or strived. 过去式为 strove 或 strived，过去分词为 striven 或 strived
VERB 努力;奋斗;力争;力求 If you strive to do something or strive for something, you make a great effort to do it or get it. 努力;奋斗;力争;力求 [Also VERB]

  • He strives hard to keep himself very fit. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他努力地保持身体健康。

  • She strove to read the name on the stone pillar. [VERB to-infinitive]

    她费劲地读着石柱上的名字。

  • Mr Annan said the region must now strive for economic development as well as peace. [VERB + for]

    安南先生说该地区现在必须为经济发展与和平而奋斗。

SYN try, labour, struggle, fight  
striving

  • ...a politician consumed by his own passionate striving for leadership.

    一心为当权而拼命往上爬的政客
