interchange
ɪntəʳtʃeɪndʒ ɪntəʳtʃeɪndʒ ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒ ˈɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense interchanges, present participle interchanging, past tense , past participle interchanged
The noun is pronounced /'ɪntətʃeɪndʒ/. The verb is pronounced /ˌɪntə'tʃeɪndʒ/. 名词读作 /'ɪntətʃeɪndʒ/。动词读作 /ˌɪntə'tʃeɪndʒ/。
1 N-VAR (思想、信息等的)交换，交流 If there is an interchange of ideas or information among a group of people, each person talks about his or her ideas or gives information to the others. (思想、信息等的)交换，交流

  • What made the meeting exciting was the interchange of ideas from different disciplines. [+ of]

    会议上令人兴奋的是来自不同学科的思想交流。

  • Ministers want to see more interchange between the private sector and the civil service.

    部长们希望看到私营部门与行政机构之间更多的交流。

SYN exchange, give and take, alternation, reciprocation  
2 V-RECIP-ERG 把…(与…)交换位置;互换;交替 If you interchange one thing with another, or you interchange two things, each thing takes the place of the other or is exchanged for the other. You can also say that two things interchange . 把…(与…)交换位置;互换;交替 [Also + with]

  • She likes to interchange her furnishings at home with the stock in her shop. [VERB noun + with]

    她想要把家里的家具与店里的存货调换一下。

  • Your task is to interchange words so that the sentence makes sense. [VERB noun]

    你们要做的就是调换单词的位置以使句子意义通顺。

  • ...the point where the illusions of the stage and reality begin to interchange. [VERB]

    舞台幻象与现实开始相易位的时刻

SYN exchange, switch, swap, alternate  
Interchange is also a noun.

  • ...the interchange of matter and energy at atomic or subatomic levels.

    物质与能量在原子或亚原子层面的交换

3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] (公路或高速公路的)互通式立交桥，交换道 An interchange on a motorway, freeway, or road is a place where it joins a main road or another motorway or freeway. (公路或高速公路的)互通式立交桥，交换道
