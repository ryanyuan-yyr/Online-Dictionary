blasphemy
blæsfəmi ˈblæsfəmi
Word forms: plural blasphemies
N-VAR 亵渎上帝（或宗教）的言行 You can describe something that shows disrespect for God or a religion as blasphemy . 亵渎上帝（或宗教）的言行

  • He was found guilty of blasphemy and sentenced to three years in jail.

    他被判犯有亵渎神明罪，刑期3年。

  • The MP described the killings as a blasphemy before God.

    这位下院议员称这些谋杀是在上帝面前的公然亵渎。

SYN irreverence, swearing, cursing, indignity in to God
