year-round ˈjɪrˈraʊnd
ADJ [ADJ n] 全年的;整整一年的 Year-round is used to describe something that happens, exists, or is done throughout the year. 全年的;整整一年的

  • Cuba has a tropical climate with year-round sunshine.

    古巴属于热带气候，终年阳光明媚。

Year-round is also an adverb.

  • They work 7 days a week year-round.

    他们一年到头每周工作7天。
