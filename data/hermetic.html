hermetic
hɜːʳmetɪk hərˈmɛtɪk
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 密封的；不透气的 If a container has a hermetic seal, the seal is very tight so that no air can get in or out. 密封的；不透气的 [technical]

  • This would permit air to enter, breaking the hermetic seal of the jar.

    这会造成漏气，破坏罐子的密封性。

SYN airtight, sealed, shut  
hermetically

  • The batteries are designed to be leak-proof and hermetically sealed.

    电池的设计防渗漏，密封性很好。

2 ADJ-GRADED 与外界隔绝的;不受外界干扰的 You use hermetic to describe something which you disapprove of because it seems to be totally separate from other people and things in society. 与外界隔绝的;不受外界干扰的 [written, disapproval]

  • Its film industry operates in its own curiously hermetic way.

    其电影产业以它奇特的与世隔绝的方式运作。

  • Their work is more cosily hermetic than ever.

    他们的工作比任何时候都要更加安逸，更加不受外界干扰。

SYN isolated, solitary, abnormal, anomalous
