come down on
1 PHRASAL VERB 支持(争论的一方) If you come down on one side of an argument, you declare that you support that side. 支持(争论的一方)

  • He clearly and decisively came down on the side of President Rafsanjani. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他清楚地表明自己坚决支持拉夫桑贾尼总统。

SYN decide on, choose, favour  
2 PHRASAL VERB 痛斥;斥责;严格对待 If you come down on someone, you criticize them severely or treat them strictly. 痛斥;斥责;严格对待

  • If Douglas came down hard enough on him, Dale would rebel. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    如果道格拉斯对戴尔太过严厉，他就会反抗。

SYN reprimand, blast, carpet [informal] , flame [informal]
