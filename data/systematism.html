systematic
sɪstəmætɪk ˌsɪstɪˈmætɪk ˌsɪstəˈmætɪk
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 成体系的;系统的;有条理的;按既定计划的 Something that is done in a systematic way is done according to a fixed plan, in a thorough and efficient way. 成体系的;系统的;有条理的;按既定计划的

  • They went about their business in a systematic way.

    他们按部就班地做生意。

  • They had not found any evidence of a systematic attempt to rig the ballot.

    他们没有发现任何证据证明有人企图全面操纵投票。

SYN methodical, organized, efficient, precise  
systematically

  • The army has systematically violated human rights.

    该部队蓄意侵犯人权。

  • She began applying systematically to colleges.

    她开始有计划地向各个大学提出入学申请。
