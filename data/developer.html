developer
dɪveləpəʳ dɪˈvɛləpər
Word forms: plural developers
1 N-COUNT （房地产）开发商，开发公司 A developer is a person or a company that buys land and builds houses, offices, shops, or factories on it, or buys existing buildings and makes them more modern. （房地产）开发商，开发公司 [business]

  • ...common land which would have a high commercial value if sold to developers.

    如果卖给开发商会有很高商业价值的公共用地

  • ...a corrupt, self-made property developer.

    一个白手起家、营私舞弊的房地产开发商

2 N-COUNT (想法、设计、产品等的)开发者 A developer is someone who develops something such as an idea, a design, or a product. (想法、设计、产品等的)开发者

  • John Bardeen was also co-developer of the theory of superconductivity. [+ of]

    约翰·巴丁也是超导电性理论的创立者之一。

  • ...a software developer.

    软件开发者

3 N-UNCOUNT 显影剂;显色剂 Developer is a chemical used for developing photographs or films. 显影剂;显色剂
