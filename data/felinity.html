feline
fiːlaɪn ˈfilaɪn
Word forms: plural felines
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 猫的;猫科的 Feline means belonging or relating to the cat family. 猫的;猫科的
2 N-COUNT 猫;猫科动物 A feline is an animal that belongs to the cat family. 猫;猫科动物

  • The 14lb feline is so fat she can hardly walk.

    这只 14 磅的猫这么胖，几乎连路都走不动了。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 像猫一样(优雅)的 You can use feline to describe someone's appearance or movements if they are elegant or graceful in a way that makes you think of a cat. 像猫一样(优雅)的 [literary]

  • ...a woman with large feline eyes and a sexy voice.

    有着猫一样的大眼睛和性感声音的女人

  • She moves with feline grace.

    她举止像猫一样优雅。
