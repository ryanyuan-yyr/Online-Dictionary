coaster
koʊstəʳ ˈkoʊstər
Word forms: plural coasters
1 N-COUNT 杯垫 A coaster is a small mat that you put underneath a glass or cup to protect the surface of a table. 杯垫
2 N-COUNT 沿海港口间航行的货船 A coaster is a ship that sails along the coast taking goods to ports. 沿海港口间航行的货船 [British]
