corollary
kərɒləri kəˈrɑləri kɔːrəleri ˈkɔrəˌlɛri ˈkɑrəˌlɛri
Word forms: plural corollaries
N-COUNT [oft with poss] 必然的想法(或结果);直接推论 A corollary of something is an idea, argument, or fact that results directly from it. 必然的想法(或结果);直接推论 [formal]

  • The number of prisoners increased as a corollary of the government's determination to combat violent crime. [+ of]

    政府决心打击暴力犯罪，直接导致了囚犯数量的增多。

SYN consequence, result, effect, outcome
