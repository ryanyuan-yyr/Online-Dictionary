stopover
stɒpoʊvəʳ stɑpˌoʊvər
Word forms: plural stopovers
N-COUNT 中途停留 A stopover is a short stay in a place in between parts of a journey. 中途停留

  • The President had talks during a brief stopover in Lisbon with his Portuguese counterpart.

    总统在里斯本作短暂停留期间和葡萄牙总统进行了会谈。

  • The Sunday flights will make a stopover in Paris.

    周日的航班将中途经停巴黎。

SYN stop, break, stop-off, stay
