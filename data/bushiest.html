bushy
bʊʃi ˈbʊʃi
Word forms: comparative bushier, superlative bushiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 浓密的 Bushy hair or fur is very thick. 浓密的

  • ...bushy eyebrows.

    浓眉

  • ...a bushy tail.

    毛茸茸的尾巴

SYN thick, bristling, spreading, rough  
2 ADJ-GRADED （枝叶）茂密的 A bushy plant has a lot of leaves very close together. （枝叶）茂密的

  • ...strong, sturdy, bushy plants.

    挺拔、茁壮、茂密的植物
