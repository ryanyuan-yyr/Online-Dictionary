rough up
PHRASAL VERB 攻击;殴打;对…动粗 If someone roughs you up, they attack you and hit or beat you. 攻击;殴打;对…动粗 [informal]

  • They threw him in a cell and roughed him up a bit. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们把他扔进牢房，揍了他几下。

  • He was fired from his job after roughing up a colleague. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他因为对一位同事动粗而被开除了。

SYN beat up, batter, thrash, do over [British , Australian , New Zealand , slang]
