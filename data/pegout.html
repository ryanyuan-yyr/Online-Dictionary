peg out
1 PHRASAL VERB 累倒；累得无法继续 If someone pegs out, they are too exhausted to carry on with what they have been doing. 累倒；累得无法继续 [British, informal]

  • I nipped round the corner for a quick beer and nearly pegged out on the spot. [VERB PREP.]

    我匆匆转过街角去痛快地喝杯啤酒，差点儿当场累趴下。

2 PHRASAL VERB 死；断气 If you say that someone pegs out, you mean that they die. 死；断气 [British, informal]

  • I thought the oldest were going to peg out. [VERB PREP.]

    我想最老的那些人快蹬腿了。
