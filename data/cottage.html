cottage
kɒtɪdʒ ˈkɑtɪdʒ
Word forms: plural cottages
N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES (乡间)小屋;村舍 A cottage is a small house, usually in the country. (乡间)小屋;村舍

  • They used to have a cottage in N.W. Scotland.

    他们原来在苏格兰西北部有一间小屋。

  • My sister Yvonne also came to live at Ockenden Cottage with me.

    姐姐伊冯娜也来到奥克肯登小屋和我一起住了。

SYN cabin, lodge, hut, shack
