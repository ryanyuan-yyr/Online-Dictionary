coat-tails
(also coattails)
1 N-PLURAL [oft poss NOUN] (燕尾服的)燕尾，后下摆 Coat-tails are the two long pieces at the back of a tailcoat . (燕尾服的)燕尾，后下摆
2 PHRASE 沾…的光;靠…的提携 If you do something on the coat-tails of someone else, you are able to do it because of the other person's success, and not because of your own efforts. 沾…的光;靠…的提携

  • They accused him of riding on the coat-tails of the president.

    他们指责他是沾了总统的光。
