step in
PHRASAL VERB 介入，干预，插手(棘手问题) If you step in, you get involved in a difficult situation because you think you can or should help with it. 介入，干预，插手(棘手问题)

  • If no agreement was reached, the army would step in. [VERB PREP.]

    如果无法达成协议，军队将会介入。

  • There are circumstances in which the State must step in to protect children. [VERB PREP.]

    有些情况下，国家必须出面保护儿童。

SYN intervene, take action, become involved, chip in [informal]
