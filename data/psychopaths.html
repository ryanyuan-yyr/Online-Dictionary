psychopath
saɪkoʊpæθ ˈsaɪkoʊˌpæθ ˈsaɪkəˌpæθ
Word forms: plural psychopaths
N-COUNT 精神变态者；变态人格者 A psychopath is someone who has serious mental problems and who may act in a violent way without feeling sorry for what they have done. 精神变态者；变态人格者

  • She was abducted by a dangerous psychopath.

    她被一个危险的精神变态狂给绑架了。

SYN madman, lunatic, maniac, psychotic
