logjam
lɒgdʒæm ˈlɒɡdʒæm ˈlɔgˌdʒæm
Word forms: plural logjams
N-COUNT [usually singular] 僵局；困局；死局 To break the logjam means to change or deal with a difficult situation which has existed for a long time. 僵局；困局；死局 [journalism]

  • A new initiative was needed to break the logjam.

    要打破僵局，就需要采取新的行动。
