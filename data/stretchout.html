stretch out
1 PHRASAL VERB (使)伸直身子躺下 If you stretch out or stretch yourself out, you lie with your legs and body in a straight line. (使)伸直身子躺下 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • The jacuzzi was too small to stretch out in. [VERB PREP. adv./prep.]

    这个“极可意”浴缸太小了，躺不下。

  • Moira stretched herself out on the lower bench.

    莫伊拉在较矮的长椅上躺下了。

SYN lie down, lounge, lean back, recline  
2 PHRASAL VERB 伸出，伸开(身体的某一部分) If you stretch out a part of your body, you hold it out straight. 伸出，伸开(身体的某一部分) [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He was about to stretch out his hand to grab me. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他正要伸手抓我。
