technicality
teknɪkælɪti ˌtɛknɪˈkæləti
Word forms: plural technicalities
1 N-PLURAL (过程、活动的)技术细节，详细步骤 The technicalities of a process or activity are the detailed methods used to do it or to carry it out. (过程、活动的)技术细节，详细步骤

  • ...the technicalities of classroom teaching. [+ of]

    课堂教学的具体步骤

2 N-COUNT (尤指法律上的)技术性细则 A technicality is a point, especially a legal one, that is based on a strict interpretation of the law or of a set of rules. (尤指法律上的)技术性细则

  • The earlier verdict was overturned on a legal technicality.

    由于诉讼程序上的一个技术细则，原先的判决被推翻了。
