mum
mʌm mʌm
Word forms: plural mums
1 N-FAMILY 妈妈 Your mum is your mother. 妈妈

  • He misses his mum.

    他想念他的妈妈。

  • Mum and Dad are coming for lunch.

    爸爸和妈妈要来吃午饭。

  • Don't worry, Mum.

    妈妈，别担心。

  • You're about to become a mum.

    你要当妈妈了。

in AM, usually use 美国英语用 mom
2 PHRASE 保守秘密;闭口不谈 If you keep mum or stay mum about something, you do not tell anyone about it. 保守秘密;闭口不谈 [informal]

  • He is keeping mum about his feelings on the matter. [+ about]

    他闭口不谈他对此事的个人感受。
