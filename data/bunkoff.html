bunk off
PHRASAL VERB 逃学；旷工 If you bunk off from school or work, you leave without permission and do something else. 逃学；旷工 [British, informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • We thought nothing of bunking off school and travelling 100 miles to find this or that record. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们觉得旷课逃学、跑上100英里去找各种唱片根本就不算什么。
