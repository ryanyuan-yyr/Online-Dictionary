keep on about
PHRASAL VERB 对…唠叨个不停 If you say that someone keeps on about something, you mean that they keep talking about it in a boring way. 对…唠叨个不停 [British, informal]

  • He kept on about me being 'defensive'. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他唠叨个不停，说我“有戒心”。

SYN go on about, go on and on about, ramble on about, rant on about
