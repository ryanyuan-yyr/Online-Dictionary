hotbed
hɒtbed ˈhɑtˌbɛd
Word forms: plural hotbeds
N-COUNT （不良活动的）滋长地，温床 If you say that somewhere is a hotbed of an undesirable activity, you are emphasizing that a lot of the activity is going on there or being started there. （不良活动的）滋长地，温床 [emphasis]

  • ...a state now known worldwide as a hotbed of racial intolerance. [+ of]

    举世皆知现在是滋生种族褊狭的温床的国家

SYN breeding ground, nest, den
