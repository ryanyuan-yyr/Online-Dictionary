chippings
tʃɪpɪŋz ˈtʃɪpɪŋz
N-PLURAL [usually n NOUN] (用于铺路等的)木屑，碎石 Wood chippings or stone chippings are small pieces of wood or stone which are used, for example, to cover surfaces such as paths or roads. (用于铺路等的)木屑，碎石

  • Paths of bark chippings will help to give the impression of a woodland walkway.

    铺满碎树皮的小路有助于制造林间小道的印象。
