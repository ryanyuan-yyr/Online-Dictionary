benediction
benɪdɪkʃən ˌbɛnəˈdɪkʃən
Word forms: plural benedictions
1 N-VAR （基督教的）祝福，赐福祈祷 A benediction is a kind of Christian prayer. （基督教的）祝福，赐福祈祷 [formal]

  • The minister pronounced the benediction.

    牧师做了赐福祈祷。

  • The Pope's hands were raised in benediction.

    教皇举起手来祝祷。

SYN blessing, favour, grace, prayer  
2 N-VAR 祝福 You can refer to something that makes people feel protected and at peace as a benediction . 祝福

  • She could only raise her hand in a gesture of benediction.

    她只得举起手来做了一个祝福的手势。

  • The song hovered, like a whispered benediction, above the crowd.

    歌声缭绕在人群上方，如同低声祝福。

SYN beatitude, favour, grace, felicity
