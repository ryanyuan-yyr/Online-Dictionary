slink
slɪŋk slɪŋk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense slinks, present participle slinking, past tense , past participle slunk
VERB 偷偷摸摸地走;悄悄走;溜走 If you slink somewhere, you move there quietly because you do not want to be seen. 偷偷摸摸地走;悄悄走;溜走

  • He decided that he couldn't just slink away, so he went and sat next to his wife. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他决定不能偷偷溜走，于是便走到妻子身边坐下。

SYN creep, steal, sneak, slip
