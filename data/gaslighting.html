gaslight
gæslaɪt ˈgæsˌlaɪt
(also gas light)Word forms: plural gaslights
N-COUNT 煤气灯 A gaslight is a lamp that produces light by burning gas. 煤气灯

  • The gaslights in the passage would be on, turned low.

    走廊里的煤气灯应该是开着的，调得比较暗。

Gaslight is also the light that the lamp produces.煤气灯光

  • He would show his collection by gaslight.

    他会在煤气灯光下展示他的收藏品。
