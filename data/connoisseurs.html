connoisseur
kɒnəsɜːʳ ˌkɒnɪˈsɜː ˌkɑnəˈsɜr ˌkɑnəˈsur
Word forms: plural connoisseurs
N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (艺术、食品、酒等的)鉴赏家，鉴定家，行家 A connoisseur is someone who knows a lot about the arts, food, drink, or some other subject. (艺术、食品、酒等的)鉴赏家，鉴定家，行家

  • Sarah tells me you're something of an art connoisseur.

    萨拉告诉我你是一位艺术鉴赏家。

  • ...connoisseurs of good food. [+ of]

    美食家

SYN expert, authority, judge, specialist
