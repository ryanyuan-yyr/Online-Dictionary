gauche
goʊʃ goʊʃ
ADJ-GRADED 不善交际的；拘谨的 If you describe someone as gauche, you mean that they are awkward and uncomfortable in the company of other people. 不善交际的；拘谨的

  • We're all a bit gauche when we're young.

    年轻的时候我们都有点放不开。

  • She was a rather gauche, provincial creature.

    她是个非常不善交际、偏狭守旧的人。

SYN awkward, clumsy, inept, unsophisticated
