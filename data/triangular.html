triangular
traɪæŋgjʊləʳ traɪˈæŋgjələr
1 ADJ 三角(形)的 Something that is triangular is in the shape of a triangle. 三角(形)的

  • ...cottages around a triangular green.

    坐落在一块三角形绿地周围的小别墅

  • …triangular bandages to make slings.

    用作吊腕带的三角绷带

SYN three-sided, three-cornered, trilateral, triangle-shaped  
2 ADJ 三角关系的;三人间的;三方面的 You can describe a relationship or situation as triangular if it involves three people or things. 三角关系的;三人间的;三方面的

  • One particular triangular relationship became the model of Simone's first novel.

    一段特殊的三角关系成了西蒙娜第一本小说的原型。
