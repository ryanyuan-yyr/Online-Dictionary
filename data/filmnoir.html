film noir fɪlm nwɑːʳ nwɑː ˌfɪlmˈ nwɑr
Word forms: plural films noir
N-VAR 黑色电影 Film noir refers to a type of film or a style of film-making which shows the world as a dangerous or depressing place where many people suffer, especially because of the greed or cruelty of others. 黑色电影

  • ...a remake of the 1947 film noir classic, Kiss of Death.

    1947 年经典黑色电影《死亡之吻》的翻拍

  • ...a fast-moving, often implausible, always stylish film noir.

    具有常常不合情理的跳跃情节、总是高度风格化的黑色电影
