quaff
kwɒf kwɑf kwæf
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense quaffs, present participle quaffing, past tense , past participle quaffed
VERB 狂饮;痛饮;畅饮 If you quaff an alcoholic drink, you drink a lot of it in a short time. 狂饮;痛饮;畅饮 [old-fashioned]

  • He's quaffed many a glass of champagne in his time. [VERB noun]

    他年轻时曾经开怀畅饮过不少香槟美酒。

SYN drink, gulp, swig [informal] , have
