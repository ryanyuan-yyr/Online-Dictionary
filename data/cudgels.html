cudgel
kʌdʒəl ˈkʌdʒəl
Word forms: plural cudgels
1 N-COUNT (用作武器的)粗短棍，短棒 A cudgel is a thick, short stick that is used as a weapon. (用作武器的)粗短棍，短棒
SYN club, stick, baton, bludgeon  
2 PHRASE 替…说话；支持;保卫 If you take up the cudgels for someone or something, you speak or fight in support of them. 替…说话；支持;保卫

  • The trade unions took up the cudgels for the 367 staff made redundant. [+ for/against]

    工会极力支持被裁的367名员工。
