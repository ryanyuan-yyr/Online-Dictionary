untamed
ʌnteɪmd ʌnˈteɪmd
ADJ 原始的；天然淳朴的；未开发的 An untamed area or place is in its original or natural state and has not been changed or affected by people. 原始的；天然淳朴的；未开发的 [literary]

  • ...the wild, untamed undergrowth.

    野生的、原始的灌木丛

  • The interior of Corsica is high and untamed.

    科西嘉岛腹地地势高企，环境荒芜。

SYN wild, fierce, savage, unbroken
