surveyor
səʳveɪəʳ sɜːˈveɪə sərˈveɪər
Word forms: plural surveyors
1 N-COUNT (土地)测量员，勘测员 A surveyor is a person whose job is to survey land. (土地)测量员，勘测员 [British]

  • ...the surveyor's maps of the Queen Alexandra Range.

    测量员绘制亚历山德拉皇后山脉勘测图

2 N-COUNT (建筑物的)鉴定人，评估人 A surveyor is a person whose job is to survey buildings. (建筑物的)鉴定人，评估人 [British]

  • Our surveyor warned us that the house needed totally rebuilding.

    我们的鉴定人警告我们这栋房子需要彻底重建。

in AM, use 美国英语用 structural engineer, inspector
