rubber bullet
ˈrʌbə ˈbʊlɪt
Word forms: plural rubber bullets
N-COUNT (防暴用的)橡皮子弹 A rubber bullet is a bullet made of a metal ball coated with rubber. It is intended to injure people rather than kill them, and is used by police or soldiers to control crowds during a riot. (防暴用的)橡皮子弹

  • Rubber bullets were used to break up the demonstration.

    橡皮子弹被用来驱散示威人群。
