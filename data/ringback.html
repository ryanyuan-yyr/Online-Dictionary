ring back
PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 给…回电话;给…再打电话 If you ring someone back, you phone them either because they phoned you earlier and you were not there or because you did not finish an earlier telephone conversation. 给…回电话;给…再打电话 [mainly British]

  • Tell her I'll ring back in a few minutes. [VERB PREP.]

    告诉她，我过几分钟就会给她回电话。

  • If there's any problem I'll ring you back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果有什么问题的话，我再给你打电话。
