heretic
herɪtɪk ˈhɛrətɪk
Word forms: plural heretics
1 N-COUNT （与大多数人观点不合的）异端分子 A heretic is someone whose beliefs or actions are considered wrong by most people, because they disagree with beliefs that are generally accepted. （与大多数人观点不合的）异端分子

  • He was considered a heretic and was ridiculed and ostracized for his ideas.

    他被视为异端分子，并且因为自己的观点遭到取笑和排斥。

SYN nonconformist, dissident, separatist, sectarian  
2 N-COUNT （与所属宗教之教义严重不合的）异教徒；离经叛道者 A heretic is a person who belongs to a particular religion, but whose beliefs or actions seriously disagree with the principles of that religion. （与所属宗教之教义严重不合的）异教徒；离经叛道者

  • Thousands of heretics were burned at the stake.

    几千名异教徒被烧死在火刑柱上。
