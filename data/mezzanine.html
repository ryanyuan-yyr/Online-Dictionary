mezzanine
mezəniːn ˈmɛzəˌnin ˌ mɛzəˈnin
Word forms: plural mezzanines
1 N-COUNT (两层楼之间的)夹层楼面,夹楼 A mezzanine is a small floor which is built between two main floors of a building. (两层楼之间的)夹层楼面,夹楼

  • ...the dining room on the mezzanine.

    位于夹层的餐厅

  • ...the mezzanine floor.

    夹楼层

2 N-COUNT (剧院的)最底层楼厅;最底层楼厅的前座 The mezzanine is the lowest balcony in a theatre, or the front rows in the lowest balcony. (剧院的)最底层楼厅;最底层楼厅的前座 [US]
