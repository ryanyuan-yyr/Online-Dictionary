beaver
biːvəʳ ˈbivər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense beavers, present participle beavering, past tense , past participle beavered
1 N-COUNT 河狸 A beaver is a furry animal with a big flat tail and large teeth. Beavers use their teeth to cut wood and build dams in rivers. 河狸
2 N-UNCOUNT 河狸毛皮 Beaver is the fur of a beaver. 河狸毛皮

  • ...a coat with a huge beaver collar.

    大河狸领外套

Phrasal verbs:
beaver away
