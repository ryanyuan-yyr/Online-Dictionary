furnace
fɜːʳnɪs ˈfɜrnəs
Word forms: plural furnaces
1 N-COUNT 火炉;熔炉 A furnace is a container or enclosed space in which a very hot fire is made, for example to melt metal, burn rubbish, or produce steam. 火炉;熔炉
2 N-SING 火炉般的地方;极闷热的地方 If you say that a place is a furnace, you mean that it is very hot there. 火炉般的地方;极闷热的地方 [informal, emphasis]

  • How can we walk? It's a furnace out there.

    我们怎么能走路呢？外面跟火炉似的。
