fury
fjʊəri ˈfjʊri
1 N-UNCOUNT 狂怒;暴怒 Fury is violent or very strong anger. 狂怒;暴怒

  • She screamed, her face distorted with fury and pain.

    她尖叫着，脸部因狂怒和痛苦而扭曲。

SYN anger, passion, rage, madness [informal]  
2 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb, PHRASE with cl] 异常愤怒;怒不可遏 If you are in a fury, you are very angry. 异常愤怒;怒不可遏

  • I had reacted in a fury of grief.

    我的反应是悲愤交加。

  • He rose to his feet in a fury to leave no doubt about where he stood on the issue.

    他怒不可遏地站起身来，这就清楚地表明了他在这个问题上的态度。
