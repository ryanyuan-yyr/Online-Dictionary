self-pity
ˈsɛlfˈpɪti
N-UNCOUNT (尤指不必要或过度夸张的)自怜，自哀 Self-pity is a feeling of unhappiness that you have about yourself and your problems, especially when this is unnecessary or greatly exaggerated. (尤指不必要或过度夸张的)自怜，自哀 [disapproval]

  • I was unable to shake off my self-pity.

    我无法摆脱自怜自哀的情绪。

  • Throughout, he showed no trace of self-pity.

    他从始至终都没有表现出一丝的自怜。

SYN self-indulgence
