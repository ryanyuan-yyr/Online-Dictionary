proverbial
prəvɜːʳbiəl prəˈvɜrbiəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 谚语的；出自谚语的；常说的 You use pro- verbial to show that you know the way you are describing something is one that is often used or is part of a popular saying. 谚语的；出自谚语的；常说的

  • The limousine sped off down the road in the proverbial cloud of dust.

    豪华轿车沿公路飞驰而去，那真是卷起一路风尘。

  • My audience certainly isn't the proverbial man in the street.

    我的观众当然不是街上的平头百姓。

2 ADJ 出名的；众所周知的 Something that is proverbial is very well-known by a lot of people. 出名的；众所周知的

  • His mastery of the French language was proverbial.

    他对法语的精通是众所周知的。
