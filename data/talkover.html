talk over
PHRASAL VERB 详细探讨;坦诚交谈 If you talk something over, you discuss it thoroughly and honestly. 详细探讨;坦诚交谈

  • He always talked things over with his friends. [VERB noun PREP. + with]

    他有事总和朋友推心置腹地拿出来谈。

  • We should go somewhere quiet, and talk it over. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们应该找个安静的地方好好谈一谈。

  • Talk over problems, don't bottle them up inside. [VERB PREP. noun]

    有问题就说出来，别憋在心里。
