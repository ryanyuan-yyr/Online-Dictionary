slush
slʌʃ slʌʃ
1 N-UNCOUNT 半融雪;雪泥 Slush is snow that has begun to melt and is therefore very wet and dirty. 半融雪;雪泥

  • Becker's eyes were as cold and grey as the slush on the pavements outside.

    贝克尔的目光冷淡而灰暗，一如外面人行道上的雪泥。

2 N-UNCOUNT 浪漫伤感的爱情故事;无聊的言情故事 If you describe a love story as slush, you mean that you dislike it because it is too sentimental and cannot be taken seriously. 浪漫伤感的爱情故事;无聊的言情故事 [disapproval]
