myopic
maɪɒpɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 目光短浅的;缺乏远见的 If you describe someone as myopic, you are critical of them because they seem unable to realize that their actions might have negative consequences. 目光短浅的;缺乏远见的 [disapproval]

  • The Government still has a myopic attitude to spending.

    政府在开支问题上仍然目光短浅。

SYN narrow-minded, short-sighted, narrow, unimaginative  
2 ADJ-GRADED 近视的;看不远的 If someone is myopic, they are unable to see things which are far away from them. 近视的;看不远的 [formal]
