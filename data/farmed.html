farm
fɑːʳm fɑrm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense farms, present participle farming, past tense , past participle farmed
1 N-COUNT 农场;牧场 A farm is an area of land, together with the buildings on it, that is used for growing crops or raising animals, usually in order to sell them. 农场;牧场

  • Farms in France are much smaller than those in the United States or even Britain.

    法国的农场比美国甚至是英国的农场都要小很多。

2 VERB 耕种;在…上从事畜牧业 If you farm an area of land, you grow crops or keep animals on it. 耕种;在…上从事畜牧业

  • They farmed some of the best land in Scotland. [VERB noun]

    他们耕种着苏格兰最好的一部分土地。

  • He has lived and farmed in the area for 46 years. [VERB]

    他已在此地生活耕作了 46 年。

SYN cultivate, work, plant, operate  
3 N-COUNT [n NOUN] 养殖场;饲养地 A mink farm or a fish farm, for example, is a place where a particular kind of animal or fish is bred and kept in large quantities in order to be sold. 养殖场;饲养地

  • ...trout fresh from a local trout farm.

    刚刚从当地鲑鱼养殖场捕捞的新鲜鲑鱼
