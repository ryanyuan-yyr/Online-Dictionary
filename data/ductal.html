duct
dʌkt dʌkt
Word forms: plural ducts
1 N-COUNT (输送液体或气体的)管道,管子 A duct is a pipe, tube, or channel which carries a liquid or gas. (输送液体或气体的)管道,管子

  • ...a big air duct in the ceiling.

    天花板上巨大的通风管道

SYN pipe, channel, passage, tube  
2 N-COUNT (人体内输送泪水、胆汁等的)导管 A duct is a tube in your body which carries a liquid such as tears or bile. (人体内输送泪水、胆汁等的)导管

  • ...tear ducts.

    泪管
