repent
rɪpent ˈriːpənt ˈripənt rɪˈpɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense repents, present participle repenting, past tense , past participle repented
VERB 后悔;忏悔;悔悟 If you repent, you show or say that you are sorry for something wrong you have done. 后悔;忏悔;悔悟 [Also VERB n]

  • Those who refuse to repent, he said, will be punished. [VERB]

    那些拒不悔改的人，他说，将会受到惩罚。

  • Did he repent of anything in his life? [V + of/for]

    他一生中有什么后悔的事情吗？

SYN regret, lament, rue [literary] , sorrow  
Quotations
When I consider how my life is spent,
I hardly ever repentOgden Nash Reminiscent Reflection
