confused
kənfjuːzd
1 ADJ-GRADED 困惑的;糊涂的 If you are confused, you do not know exactly what is happening or what to do. 困惑的;糊涂的

  • A survey showed people were confused about what they should eat to stay healthy. [+ about/by]

    一项调查表明，人们对该吃什么才能保持健康这个问题感到困惑。

  • Things were happening too quickly and Brian was confused.

    事情发生得太快，把布赖恩给弄糊涂了。

SYN bewildered, puzzled, baffled, at sea  
confusedly

  • He shook his head confusedly.

    他迷惑不解地摇了摇头。

2 ADJ-GRADED 混乱的;难懂的 Something that is confused does not have any order or pattern and is difficult to understand. 混乱的;难懂的

  • The situation remains confused as both sides claim success.

    由于双方均宣称获胜，形势依然不明朗。

  • ...a modern society in which values have become increasingly confused.

    价值观变得越来越混乱的现代社会

SYN disorderly, disordered, chaotic, mixed up  
Usage Note :

  • Note the difference in meaning between confused and confusing.

    注意 confused 和 confusing 意义上的区别。

Quotations
If you are sure you understand everything that is going on, you are hopelessly confusedWalter Mondale
Anyone who isn't confused doesn't really understand the situationEd Murrow (on the Vietnam War)
