counter-culture
(also counterculture)Word forms: plural counter-cultures
1 N-VAR 反主流文化;反正统文化 Counter-culture is a set of values, ideas, and ways of behaving that are completely different from those of the rest of society. 反主流文化;反正统文化

  • ...a history of British counter-culture.

    英国反主流文化的历史

2 N-COUNT 反主流文化群体 A counter-culture is a group in society whose values, ideas, and ways of behaving are completely different from those of the rest of society. 反主流文化群体

  • Not everyone joined the counterculture, not everyone demonstrated, dropped out, took drugs, or dodged the draft.

    不是所有人都加入反主流文化群体，不是所有人都示威游行，退学，吸毒，或逃避服兵役。
