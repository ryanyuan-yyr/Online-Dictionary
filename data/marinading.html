marinade
mærɪneɪd ˌmærəˈneɪd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense marinades, present participle marinading, past tense , past participle marinaded
1 N-COUNT (用油、醋、香料、草药等调成的)腌泡汁 A marinade is a sauce of oil, vinegar, spices, and herbs, which you pour over meat or fish before you cook it, in order to add flavour, or to make the meat or fish softer. (用油、醋、香料、草药等调成的)腌泡汁
2 V-ERG 同 marinate To marinade means the same as to marinate . 同 marinate

  • Marinade the chicken breasts in the tandoori paste. [VERB noun]

    把鸡胸脯肉放入唐杜里烧烤酱里腌泡。

  • Leave to marinade for 24 hours. [VERB]

    腌制24小时。
