conglomeration
kənglɒməreɪʃən kənˌglɑmərˈeɪʃən
Word forms: plural conglomerations
N-COUNT 聚集物;混合物 A conglomeration of things is a group of many different things, gathered together. 聚集物;混合物 [formal]

  • ...a conglomeration of buildings, all tightly packed together.

    鳞次栉比的建筑群

  • ...a conglomeration of peoples speaking different languages. [+ of]

    汇聚一地的南腔北调的民族

SYN mass, combination, composite, accumulation
