incomplete
ɪnkəmpliːt ˌɪnkəmˈplit
ADJ-GRADED 未完成的;未结束的 Something that is incomplete is not yet finished, or does not have all the parts or details that it needs. 未完成的;未结束的

  • The clearing of rubbish and drains is still incomplete.

    垃圾与污水的清理工作还未完成。

  • European political union would be incomplete without a defence element.

    如果没有防御体系,欧洲政治联盟就不完整。

  • Some offices had incomplete information on spending.

    一些办事处的开支信息不全。

SYN unfinished, partial, insufficient, wanting
