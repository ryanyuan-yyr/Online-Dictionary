unforeseeable
ʌnfɔːʳsiːəbəl
ADJ-GRADED （问题或坏事）不可预见的，难以预测的 An unforeseeable problem or unpleasant event is one which you did not expect and could not have predicted. （问题或坏事）不可预见的，难以预测的

  • ...severe unforeseeable weather conditions.

    不可预测的恶劣天气

  • This is such an unforeseeable situation that anything could happen.

    这种情况很难预测，什么事都有可能发生。
