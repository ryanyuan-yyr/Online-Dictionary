fallout
fɔːlaʊt ˈfɔlˌaʊt
1 N-UNCOUNT 核辐射 Fallout is the radiation that affects a particular place or area after a nuclear explosion has taken place. 核辐射

  • They were exposed to radioactive fallout during nuclear weapons tests.

    在核武器试验中他们暴露在放射性尘埃之下。

2 N-UNCOUNT 余波;后果 If you refer to the fallout from something that has happened, you mean the unpleasant consequences that follow it. 余波;后果

  • Grundy lost his job in the fallout from the incident. [+ from]

    格伦迪受该事件影响丢了工作。
