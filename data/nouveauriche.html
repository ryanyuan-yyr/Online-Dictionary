nouveau riche
nuːvoʊ riːʃ ˌnuːvəʊ ˈriːʃ nuvo riʃ ˌnuvoʊˈriʃ
(also nouveau-riche)Word forms: plural nouveau riche, plural nouveaux riches
1 N-PLURAL 暴发户 The nouveaux riches are people who have only recently become rich and who have tastes and manners that some people consider vulgar. 暴发户 [disapproval]

  • The nouveau riche have to find a way to be accepted.

    暴发户不得不去想法获得大家的接受和认可。

  • As for India's nouveaux riches, they will now have to pay still higher duties on luxury goods.

    对印度的暴发户来说，他们现在不得不为奢侈品支付更为高昂的税款。

SYN arriviste, upstart, new-rich, parvenu  
2 ADJ-GRADED 暴发户的;与暴发户有关的 Nouveau-riche means belonging or relating to the nouveaux riches. 暴发户的;与暴发户有关的 [disapproval]

  • ...critics who did not appreciate his nouveau-riche taste.

    不屑于其暴发户品位的批评家们

SYN arriviste, upstart, newly-rich, parvenu
