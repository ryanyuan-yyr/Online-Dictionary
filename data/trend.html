trend
trend trɛnd
Word forms: plural trends
1 N-COUNT 趋势;倾向;动向 A trend is a change or development towards something new or different. 趋势;倾向;动向

  • This is a growing trend.

    这是日益显著的趋势。

  • ...a trend towards part-time employment. [+ towards]

    倾向于兼职就业的趋势

  • …the downward trend in gasoline prices.

    汽油价格下滑的趋势

SYN tendency, swing, drift, inclination  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 规范;风尚;时尚 To set a trend means to do something that becomes accepted or fashionable, and that a lot of other people copy. 规范;风尚;时尚

  • The record has already proved a success and may well start a trend.

    该唱片已证明大获成功，并且很可能会引导潮流。

SYN fashion, craze, fad [informal] , mode
