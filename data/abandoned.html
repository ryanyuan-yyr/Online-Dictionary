abandoned
əbændənd əˈbændənd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] (场所或建筑物)舍弃不用的，无人居住的 An abandoned place or building is no longer used or occupied. (场所或建筑物)舍弃不用的，无人居住的

  • All that digging had left a network of abandoned mines and tunnels.

    东挖西掘后留下了一片废弃的矿坑。

  • ...abandoned buildings that become a breeding ground for crime.

    沦为了犯罪温床的弃置楼群

SYN unoccupied, empty, deserted, vacant
