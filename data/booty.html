booty
buːti ˈbuti
1 N-UNCOUNT 赃物；（尤指）战利品 Booty is a collection of valuable things stolen from a place, especially by soldiers after a battle. 赃物；（尤指）战利品

  • Troops destroyed the capital and confiscated many works of art as war booty.

    军队摧毁了这座都城，并把大量艺术品收缴为战利品。

SYN plunder, winnings, gains, haul  
2 屁股 Someone's booty is their bottom. 屁股
