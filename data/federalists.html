federalist
fedərəlɪst ˈfɛdərəlɪst
Word forms: plural federalists
1 ADJ 联邦主义的Someone or something that is federalist believes in, supports, or follows a federal system of government. 联邦主义的

  • The new constitution includes federalist principles.

    新宪法包括联邦主义的原则。

2 N-COUNT 联邦主义者Federalist is also a noun. 联邦主义者

  • Many Quebeckers are federalists.

    很多魁北克人是联邦主义者。
