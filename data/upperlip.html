upper lip ˈʌpə lɪp
Word forms: plural upper lips
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 上唇部位 Your upper lip is the part of your face between your mouth and your nose. 上唇部位

  • The beginnings of a moustache showed on his upper lip.

    他的上唇部位开始长胡子了。

2 N-COUNT 上唇 Your upper lip is the higher of your two lips. a stiff upper lip→see: lip  上唇

  • His upper lip was flat, but the lower one sagged.

    他上唇扁平,下唇耷拉。
