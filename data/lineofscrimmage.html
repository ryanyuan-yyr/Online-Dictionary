line of scrimmage
N-SING 争球线In American football, the line of scrimmage is an imaginary line on either side of which the offence and defence line up. 争球线

  • The Bears stacked the line of scrimmage with extra defenders to stop the run.

    熊队在争球线积聚了额外的防守队员来阻挡进跑。
