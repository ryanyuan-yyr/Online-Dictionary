sheathe
ʃiːð ʃið
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sheathes, present participle sheathing, past tense , past participle sheathed
1 VERB [usually passive] 用…盖住;将…包起来 If something is sheathed in a material or other covering, it is closely covered with it. 用…盖住;将…包起来 [literary]

  • The television was sheathed in a snug coverlet. [be VERB-ed + in]

    电视机用一个床罩严严实实地盖住了。

  • ...her long legs, sheathed in sheer black tights. [VERB-ed]

    她那裹在一条纯黑的紧身裤下的修长的双腿

2 VERB 将(刀)插入鞘中 When someone sheathes a knife, they put it in its sheath. 将(刀)插入鞘中 [literary]

  • He sheathed the knife and strapped it to his shin. [VERB noun]

    他将刀插入鞘中,绑在小腿上。
