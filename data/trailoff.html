trail off
(also trail away)
PHRASAL VERB (声音)逐渐减弱，越来越小 If a speaker's voice or a speaker trails off or trails away, their voice becomes quieter and they hesitate until they stop speaking completely. (声音)逐渐减弱，越来越小

  • 'But he had no reason. He of all men...' Kate's voice trailed off. [VERB PREP.]

    “但是他没有任何理由。在所有人当中…” 凯特的声音越来越轻。
