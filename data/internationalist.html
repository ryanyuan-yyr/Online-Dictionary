internationalist
ɪntəʳnæʃənəlɪst ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəlɪst ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪst
Word forms: plural internationalists
ADJ 国际主义的 If someone has internationalist beliefs or opinions, they believe that countries should work with, help, and be friendly with one another. 国际主义的

  • As a Liberal Democrat I subscribe to the internationalist approach.

    作为自由民主党成员,我支持国际主义路线。

An internationalist is someone who has internationalist views.国际主义者
