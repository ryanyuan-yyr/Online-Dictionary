unqualified
ʌnkwɒlɪfaɪd ʌnˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪd ʌnˈkwɔləˌfaɪd
1 ADJ 没资格的；资格不够的；不合格的 If you are unqualified, you do not have any qualifications, or you do not have the right qualifications for a particular job. 没资格的；资格不够的；不合格的

  • She was unqualified for the job. [+ for]

    她没资格做这份工作。

  • Unqualified members of staff at the hospital were not sufficiently supervised.

    医院里一些还未取得资格的员工没有受到充分的督导。

SYN unfit, incapable, incompetent, not up to  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 完全的；绝对的；无保留的；不受限制的 Unqualified means total or unlimited. 完全的；绝对的；无保留的；不受限制的 [emphasis]

  • The event was an unqualified success.

    这一活动取得了极大成功。

  • Egypt has given almost unqualified backing to Washington.

    埃及几乎全力支持华盛顿。

SYN unconditional, complete, total, absolute
