homeroom
hoʊmrʊm ˈhəʊmˌruːm ˈhoʊmˌrum
Word forms: homerooms
N-VAR 指导教室In a school, homeroom is the class or room where students in the same grade meet to get general information and be checked for attendance. 指导教室 [US]
