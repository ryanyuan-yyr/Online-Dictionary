MD
em diː
Word forms: plural MDs
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 M.D.
1 医学博士 MD is written after someone's name to indicate that they have been awarded a degree in medicine and are qualified to practise as a doctor. 医学博士
2 N-COUNT MD is an abbreviation for (缩略 =) managing director. [mainly British, business]

  • He's going to be the MD of the Park Lane company.

    他即将出任柏宁公司的总经理。
