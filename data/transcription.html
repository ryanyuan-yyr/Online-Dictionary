transcription
trænskrɪpʃən trænˈskrɪpʃən
Word forms: plural transcriptions
1 N-UNCOUNT 笔录;转写 Transcription of speech or text is the process of transcribing it. 笔录;转写
2 N-COUNT (根据录音或笔记整理的)笔录本，文字本，转写本 A transcription of a conversation or speech is a written text of it, based on a recording or notes. (根据录音或笔记整理的)笔录本，文字本，转写本

  • The transcriptions of the text were available as early as 1960.

    原文的转写本早在 1960 年就可获取。
