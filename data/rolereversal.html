role reversal
rəʊl rɪˈvɜːsəl
Word forms: plural role reversals
N-VAR 角色颠倒;角色互换;角色对调 Role reversal is a situation in which two people have chosen or been forced to exchange their duties and responsibilities, so that each is now doing what the other used to do. 角色颠倒;角色互换;角色对调

  • ...men who have undertaken the most extreme role reversal and become house-husbands.

    那些进行了最极端的角色互换、做起了家庭主夫的男人们
