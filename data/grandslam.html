grand slam
1 ADJ 大赛的;大奖赛的 In sport, a Grand Slam tournament is a major one. 大赛的;大奖赛的

  • ...her 39 Grand Slam titles.

    她的39项大赛冠军头衔

Grand Slam is also a noun.

  • It's my first Grand Slam and I was hoping to make a good impression.

    那是我首次参加大奖赛，我当时希望能表现出色。

2 N-COUNT （棒球、橄榄球等运动中）大满贯 If someone wins a Grand Slam, they win all the major tournaments in a season in a particular sport, for example in rugby or tennis. （棒球、橄榄球等运动中）大满贯

  • They won the Grand Slam in 1990.

    他们1990年赢得了大满贯。
