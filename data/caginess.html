cagey
keɪdʒi ˈkeɪdʒi
ADJ-GRADED 讳莫如深的;守口如瓶的 If you say that someone is being cagey about something, you mean that you think they are deliberately not giving you much information or expressing an opinion about it. 讳莫如深的;守口如瓶的

  • He is cagey about what he was paid for the business. [+ about]

    他对自己在这桩买卖中所得的报酬不愿多提。

SYN guarded, reserved, careful, cautious  
cagily

  • Smart reacted cagily when Chelsea were mentioned.

    当提到切尔茜时,斯马特就不吱声了。
