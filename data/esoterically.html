esoteric
iːsoʊterɪk esə- ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk
ADJ-GRADED 深奥的;难懂的;常人难以理解的 If you describe something as esoteric, you mean it is known, understood, or appreciated by only a small number of people. 深奥的;难懂的;常人难以理解的 [formal]

  • ...esoteric knowledge.

    深奥的知识

  • ...a spoiled aristocrat with pretentious airs and esoteric tastes.

    自命不凡、品位刁钻且被惯坏了的贵族成员

SYN obscure, private, secret, hidden
