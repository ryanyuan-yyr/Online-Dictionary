pay television
(also pay-television)
N-UNCOUNT 收费电视Pay television is television that you can watch only if you pay a fee such as a subscription to a satellite or cable television company. 收费电视

  • Pay television is beginning to produce the best original programming.

    收费电视开始制作出最好的原创节目单。

  • ...one of Europe's leading pay-television companies.

    ...一个占主导地位的欧洲付费电视公司。
