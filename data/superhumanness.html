superhuman
suːpəʳhjuːmən ˌsupərˈhjumən
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 超出常人的;非凡的;超人的 If you describe a quality that someone has as superhuman, you mean that it seems to be much greater than that of ordinary people. 超出常人的;非凡的;超人的

  • Officers were terrified of his superhuman strength.

    军官们被他超凡的力量吓坏了。

  • They saw their bills rising steadily, in spite of superhuman efforts to save water.

    尽管他们已想方设法节约用水，水费仍旧在稳步上升。

SYN heroic, phenomenal, prodigious, stupendous
