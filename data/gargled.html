gargle
gɑːʳgəl ˈɡɑːɡəl ˈgɑrgəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense gargles, present participle gargling, past tense , past participle gargled
1 VERB 漱口；漱喉 If you gargle, you wash your mouth and throat by filling your mouth with a liquid, tipping your head back and using your throat to blow bubbles through the liquid, and finally spitting it out. 漱口；漱喉

  • Try gargling with salt water as soon as a cough begins. [VERB]

    一开始咳嗽就马上试着用盐水漱口。

  • At the sink, Neil noisily gargled something medicinal. [VERB noun]

    尼尔在洗脸池边用某种药液咕噜咕噜地漱口。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 漱口液 A gargle is a liquid which is used for gargling. 漱口液

  • The mixture can be used as a gargle several times a day.

    每天可用这种混合液漱几次口。
