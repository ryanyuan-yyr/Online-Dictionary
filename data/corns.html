corn
kɔːʳn kɔrn
Word forms: plural corns
1 N-UNCOUNT 麦类作物;谷物;谷粒 Corn is used to refer to crops such as wheat and barley. It can also be used to refer to the seeds from these plants. 麦类作物;谷物;谷粒 [British]

  • ...fields of corn.

    麦田

  • He filled the barn to the roof with corn.

    他将谷仓装满了谷物。

in AM, use 美国英语用 grain
2 N-UNCOUNT 同maize Corn is the same as maize . 同maize

  • ...rows of corn in an Iowa field.

    艾奥瓦州农田里成排的玉米

3 N-COUNT [usually plural] (尤指脚趾周边的)鸡眼，钉胼 Corns are small, painful areas of hard skin which can form on your foot, especially near your toes. (尤指脚趾周边的)鸡眼，钉胼
