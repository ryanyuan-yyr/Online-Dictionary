reason with
PHRASAL VERB 和…理论;和…说理;规劝 If you try to reason with someone, you try to persuade them to do or accept something by using sensible arguments. 和…理论;和…说理;规劝

  • I have watched parents trying to reason with their children and have never seen it work. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我曾经见到家长试图和他们的孩子讲道理，但是从没有见过这招奏效。

SYN persuade, debate with, remonstrate with, bring round
