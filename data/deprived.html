deprived
dɪpraɪvd diˈpraɪvd dɪˈpraɪvd
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 贫困的;贫穷的 Deprived people or people from deprived areas do not have the things that people consider to be essential in life, for example acceptable living conditions or education. 贫困的;贫穷的

  • ...probably the most severely deprived children in the country.

    可能是这个国家里最贫困的儿童

  • ...the problems associated with life in a deprived inner city area.

    与市中心贫民区生活联系在一起的问题

SYN poor, disadvantaged, needy, in need
