insistent
ɪnsɪstənt ɪnˈsɪstənt
1 ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ that] 坚持的;坚决要求的 Someone who is insistent keeps insisting that a particular thing should be done or is the case. 坚持的;坚决要求的 [Also + on]

  • The men are very insistent that you leave right away.

    他们坚决要求你立即离开。

  • He is most insistent on this point.

    他在这点上很坚持。

  • 'When can I see him?' Her tone was insistent now.

    “我什么时候能见他？”她的语气变得坚决。

SYN emphatic, persistent, demanding, pressing  
insistently

  • 'What is it?' his wife asked again, gently but insistently.

    “怎么回事？”他妻子又问了一遍,语气温柔却坚决。

2 ADJ-GRADED (噪音、节奏等)持续的,经久不息的 An insistent noise or rhythm keeps going on for a long time and holds your attention. (噪音、节奏等)持续的,经久不息的

  • ...the insistent rhythms of the Caribbean and Latin America.

    加勒比海与拉丁美洲经久不息的韵律节拍
