ha ha
(also ha ha ha)
1 EXCLAM 哈哈（笑声） Ha ha is used in writing to represent the sound that people make when they laugh. 哈哈（笑声）

  • I dropped my bag at the officer's feet. The bank notes fell out. 'Ha ha ha!' he laughed. 'Got no money, uh?'

    我的包掉在了警官的脚下。钞票撒了一地。“哈哈哈！”他大笑道。“还说没有钱，嗯？”

2 EXCLAM 哈哈，哼（表示嘲笑或不相信） People sometimes say 'ha ha' to show that they are not amused by what you have said, or do not believe it. 哈哈，哼（表示嘲笑或不相信） [spoken]

  • He said 'vegetarians unite', and I looked at him and said 'yeah, ha ha'.

    “我行我‘素’，”他说道，我瞅着他，说：“随你喽，呵呵。”
