creek
kriːk krik krɪk
Word forms: plural creeks
1 N-COUNT [oft in names] 小海湾;小港湾 A creek is a narrow place where the sea comes a long way into the land. 小海湾;小港湾 [British]
2 N-COUNT [oft in names] 小河;小溪 A creek is a small stream or river. 小河;小溪 [US]

  • Follow Austin Creek for a few miles.

    沿着奥斯汀河走上几英里。

SYN stream, brook, tributary, bayou  
3 PHRASE 处于困境(或窘境);犯错误 If someone is up the creek, they are in a bad or difficult situation, or are wrong in some way. You can also say that someone is up the creek without a paddle . 处于困境(或窘境);犯错误 [informal]
