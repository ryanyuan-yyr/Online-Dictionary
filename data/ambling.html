amble
æmbəl ˈæmbəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense ambles, present participle ambling, past tense , past participle ambled
VERB 缓行；漫步 When you amble, you walk slowly and in a relaxed manner. 缓行；漫步

  • We ambled along in front of the houses. [VERB adv./prep.]

    我们在房子前面漫步。

  • Slowly they ambled back to the car. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他们慢慢地走回到汽车那儿。

SYN stroll, walk, wander, ramble  
Amble is also a noun.

  • ...an afternoon's amble around the oldest parts of Paris.

    在巴黎最古老街区午后漫步
