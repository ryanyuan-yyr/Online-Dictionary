integrity
ɪntegrɪti ɪnˈtɛgrəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 正直;诚实 If you have integrity, you are honest and firm in your moral principles. 正直;诚实

  • I have always regarded him as a man of integrity.

    我一直认为他正直诚实。

  • He was praised for his fairness and high integrity.

    他的公正和正直操守受人称道。

SYN honesty, principle, honour, virtue  
2 N-UNCOUNT [with poss] 完整;完全;完善 The integrity of something such as a group of people or a text is its state of being a united whole. 完整;完全;完善 [formal]

  • Separatist movements are a threat to the integrity of the nation. [+ of]

    分离主义运动对国家统一是个威胁。

SYN unity, unification, cohesion, coherence
