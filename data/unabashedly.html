unabashed
ʌnəbæʃt ˌʌnəˈbæʃt ˌʌnəˈbæʃt
ADJ 不害臊的；不难为情的 If you describe someone as unabashed, you mean that they are not ashamed, embarrassed, or shy about something, especially when you think most people would be. 不害臊的；不难为情的

  • He seems unabashed by his recent defeat. [+ by]

    他似乎对最近的失利并不在乎。

  • He's an unabashed, old-fashioned romantic.

    他是一个丝毫不觉难为情的老派的浪漫主义者。

SYN unembarrassed, blatant, brazen, confident
