creative
krieɪtɪv kriˈeɪtɪv
Word forms: plural creatives
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (尤指在艺术方面)有创造力的，有想象力的 A creative person has the ability to invent and develop original ideas, especially in the arts. (尤指在艺术方面)有创造力的，有想象力的

  • Like so many creative people he was never satisfied.

    像许多富于创造力的人一样，他从不满足。

  • ...her obvious creative talents.

    她显而易见的创作才能

SYN imaginative, gifted, artistic, inventive  
creativity

  • American art reached a peak of creativity in the '50s and 60s.

    美国艺术在五六十年代达到了一次创作顶峰。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 创造(性)的;创作的 Creative activities involve the inventing and making of new kinds of things. 创造(性)的;创作的

  • ...creative writing.

    创造性写作

  • ...creative arts.

    创作型艺术

  • Cooking is creative.

    烹饪富有创造性。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 创新的;富有创意的 If you use something in a creative way, you use it in a new way that produces interesting and unusual results. 创新的;富有创意的

  • ...his creative use of words.

    他对词语的创造性运用

creatively

  • Genet teaches you to think creatively.

    吉尼特教你创造性地思考问题。

4 N-COUNT (尤指广告)创意人员，创作者 A creative is someone whose job is to be creative, especially someone who creates advertisements. (尤指广告)创意人员，创作者 [mainly journalism]

  • Along with scores of other advertising creatives, he will be taking part in an exhibition at the Saatchi gallery.

    他将和许多广告创意人员一起参加在萨奇画廊举行的画展。
