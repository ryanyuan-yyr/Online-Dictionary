machismo
mætʃɪzmoʊ mæˈkɪzməʊ mɑːtʃiːz- mɑˈtʃizmoʊ mɑˈtʃismoʊ mɑˈtʃɪzmoʊ
N-UNCOUNT 男子汉气概;大男子主义 You use machismo to refer to men's behaviour or attitudes when they are very conscious and proud of their masculinity. 男子汉气概;大男子主义

  • Hooky, naturally, has to prove his machismo by going on the scariest rides twice.

    胡基自然要坐两次最恐怖的过山车来表现一下自己的男子汉气概了。

SYN masculinity, toughness, virility, manliness
