humidity
hjuːmɪdɪti hjuˈmɪdəti juˈmɪdəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 潮湿 You say there is humidity when the air feels very heavy and damp. 潮湿

  • The heat and humidity were insufferable.

    高温和潮湿让人难以忍受。

SYN damp, moisture, dampness, wetness  
2 N-UNCOUNT （空气）湿度 Humidity is the amount of water in the air. （空气）湿度

  • The humidity is relatively low.

    湿度相对较低。
