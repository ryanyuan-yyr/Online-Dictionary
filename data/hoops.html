hoop
huːp hup hʊp
Word forms: plural hoops
1 N-COUNT （木头、金属或塑料制成的）大环，大圈 A hoop is a large ring made of wood, metal, or plastic. （木头、金属或塑料制成的）大环，大圈
2 N-COUNT （槌球游戏中的）拱门 In croquet, the hoops are the small metal arches which players hit the ball through. （槌球游戏中的）拱门
3 N-COUNT （篮球运动中的）篮圈，篮筐 A basketball hoop is the ring that players try to throw the ball into in order to score points for their team. （篮球运动中的）篮圈，篮筐
4 PHRASE （为取悦他人或达到目的而）经受苦难，作出很大努力 If someone makes you jump through hoops, they make you do lots of difficult or boring things in order to please them or achieve something. （为取悦他人或达到目的而）经受苦难，作出很大努力

  • He had the duty receptionist almost jumping through hoops for him. But to no avail.

    值班的接待员几乎为他费尽了力气，但还是没用。
