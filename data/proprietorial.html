proprietorial
prəpraɪətɔːriəl prəˌpraɪəˈtɔriəl proʊˌpraɪəˈtɔriəl
ADJ-GRADED 所有者的；自以为是所有者的 If your behaviour is proprietorial, you are behaving in a proud way because you are, or feel like you are, the owner of something. 所有者的；自以为是所有者的 [formal]

  • The longer I live alone the more proprietorial I become about my home.

    我独自住得越久，就越有业主的感觉。
