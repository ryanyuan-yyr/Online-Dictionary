twitch
twɪtʃ twɪtʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense twitches, present participle twitching, past tense , past participle twitched
V-ERG (使)(尤指身体部位)抽搐;(使)抽动;急拉;急扯 If something, especially a part of your body, twitches or if you twitch it, it makes a little jumping movement. (使)(尤指身体部位)抽搐;(使)抽动;急拉;急扯

  • When I stood up to her, her right cheek would begin to twitch. [VERB]

    当我作出反抗时，她右侧脸庞就会开始抽搐。

  • His left eyelid twitched involuntarily… [VERB]

    他的左眼皮不由自主地跳动。

  • As they turned to leave, they saw the curtains twitch in the house next door… [VERB]

    当他们转身要离开时，他们看到隔壁房子的窗帘被猛地扯了一下。

  • Stern twitched his shoulders. [VERB noun]

    斯特恩抖动着肩膀。

SYN jerk, blink, flutter, jump  
Twitch is also a noun.

  • He developed a nervous twitch and began to blink constantly.

    他身体出现了神经性抽搐，开始不停地眨眼睛。
