ponytail
poʊniteɪl ˈpoʊniˌteɪl
(also pony-tail)Word forms: plural ponytails
N-COUNT 马尾辫 A ponytail is a hairstyle in which someone's hair is tied up at the back of the head and hangs down like a tail. 马尾辫

  • Her long, fine hair was swept back in a ponytail.

    她纤细的长发在脑后梳成了马尾辫。
