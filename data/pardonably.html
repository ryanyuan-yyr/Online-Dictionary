pardonable
pɑːʳdənəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 可原谅的；可宽恕的 You describe someone's action or attitude as pardonable if you think it is wrong but you understand why they did that action or have that attitude. 可原谅的；可宽恕的

  • 'I have', he remarked with pardonable pride, 'done what I set out to do.'

    “我已经，”他以情有可原的骄傲语气说，“完成了我要做的。”

SYN forgivable, understandable, permissible, not serious
