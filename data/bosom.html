bosom
bʊzəm ˈbʊzəm ˈbuzəm
Word forms: plural bosoms
1 N-COUNT （女子的）乳房 A woman's breasts are sometimes referred to as her bosom or her bosoms. （女子的）乳房 [old-fashioned]

  • ...a large young mother with a baby resting against her ample bosom.

    让怀中婴儿靠在她丰满胸部上的身材高大的年轻母亲

2 N-SING [the NOUN of n] 怀抱；关怀 If you are in the bosom of your family or of a community, you are among people who love, accept, and protect you. 怀抱；关怀 [literary]

  • Joan was delighted to welcome her boyfriend into the bosom of her large, close-knit family.

    琼十分高兴地欢迎她的男朋友加入她亲密无间的大家庭。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] （朋友）知心的，亲密的 A bosom friend is a friend who you know very well and like very much indeed. （朋友）知心的，亲密的

  • They were bosom friends.

    他们是知心好友。

  • Sakota was her cousin and bosom pal.

    沙科塔是她的表妹和亲密伙伴。

SYN intimate, close, warm, dear  
4 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb] 接纳；关怀；关爱 If you take someone or something to your bosom, you accept them and treat or regard them with great affection. 接纳；关怀；关爱 [literary]

  • We, at least, have taken her to our bosom.

    至少我们对她是关爱有加的。
