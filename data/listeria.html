listeria
lɪstɪəriə lɪsˈtɪərɪə lɪsˈtɪriə
N-UNCOUNT 李斯特菌食物中毒; 李斯特菌Listeria is a serious form of food poisoning caused by bacteria in food. You can also refer to the bacteria itself as listeria. 李斯特菌食物中毒; 李斯特菌

  • My baby was born with listeria.

    我的宝宝出生时感染有李斯特菌。

  • Tests showed it was contaminated by listeria and other bacteria.

    检测表明它感染有李斯特菌和其他细菌。
