maladjusted
mælədʒʌstɪd ˌmæləˈdʒʌstɪd ˌmæləˈdʒʌstɪd
ADJ-GRADED 心理失调的;不能适应环境的;适应不良的 If you describe a child as maladjusted, you mean that they have psychological problems and behave in a way which is not acceptable to society. 心理失调的;不能适应环境的;适应不良的

  • ...a school for maladjusted children.

    为心理失调的儿童开设的学校

SYN disturbed, hung-up [slang] , alienated, unstable
