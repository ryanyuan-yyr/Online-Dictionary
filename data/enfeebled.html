enfeebled
ɪnfiːbəld ɪnˈfiːbəld
ADJ-GRADED 变得衰弱的;羸弱的 If someone or something is enfeebled, they have become very weak. 变得衰弱的;羸弱的 [formal]

  • He finds himself politically enfeebled.

    他发现自己的政治势力变得衰弱。

  • ...the already enfeebled newspaper.

    那家已经式微的报纸

SYN weakened, undermined, exhausted, diminished
