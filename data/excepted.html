excepted
ɪkseptɪd ɪkˈsɛptɪd
ADV [n ADVERB] 除…外 You use excepted after you have mentioned a person or thing to show that you do not include them in the statement you are making. 除…外 [formal]

  • Jeremy excepted, the men seemed personable.

    除杰里米外，那些男人看起来都风度翩翩。

  • You normally receive 4 treatments each day, Sundays excepted.

    通常每天接受4次治疗，星期日除外。
