smart growth
N-UNCOUNT [oft N n] (城镇)智能发展; 城市规划理论，倡议新兴建筑及道路集中在已有中心，避免空地绿地的开发People such as architects and environmentalists use smart growth to refer to the construction of new buildings and roads within a town or city so that they are close to people's workplaces and mass transit systems and so that open spaces are not built on.
