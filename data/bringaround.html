bring around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语用 bring round
1 PHRASAL VERB 使恢复知觉 If you bring someone around when they are unconscious, you make them become conscious again. 使恢复知觉

  • I'd passed out and he'd brought me round. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我昏了过去，他使我恢复了知觉。

2 PHRASAL VERB 改变…的观点；说服（某人） If you bring someone around, you cause them to change their opinion about something so that they agree with you. 改变…的观点；说服（某人） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We will do everything we can to bring parliament round to our point of view. [V n P to n]

    我们将尽己所能说服议会支持我们的观点。
