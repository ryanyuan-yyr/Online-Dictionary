vocabulary
voʊkæbjʊləri vəˈkæbjʊlərɪ -leri voʊˈkæbjəˌlɛri
Word forms: plural vocabularies
1 N-VAR [oft with poss] （某人所掌握的）词汇,词汇量 Your vocabulary is the total number of words you know in a particular language. （某人所掌握的）词汇,词汇量

  • His speech is immature, his vocabulary limited.

    他说话很幼稚，使用的词汇有限。

  • We read to improve our vocabularies.

    我们阅读以便扩大词汇量。

SYN language, words, lexicon, word stock  
2 N-SING (某一语言的）词汇，词汇量 The vocabulary of a language is all the words in it. (某一语言的）词汇，词汇量

  • ...a new word in the German vocabulary.

    德语词汇中的一个新词

3 N-VAR 专业词汇 The vocabulary of a subject is the group of words that are typically used when discussing it. 专业词汇

  • ...the vocabulary of natural science. [+ of]

    自然科学词汇
