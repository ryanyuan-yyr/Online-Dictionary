drivel
drɪvəl ˈdrɪvəl ˈdrɪvəl
N-UNCOUNT 蠢话;废话 If you describe something that is written or said as drivel, you are critical of it because you think it is very silly. 蠢话;废话 [disapproval]

  • What absolute drivel!

    一派胡言！

  • She is still writing mindless drivel.

    她还在写些没头没脑的东西。

SYN nonsense, rubbish, garbage, malarkey [informal]
