check in
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (在旅馆)登记入住;(在门诊部)挂号 When you check in or check into a hotel or clinic, or if someone checks you in, you arrive and go through the necessary procedures before you stay there. (在旅馆)登记入住;(在门诊部)挂号 [Also V n P n]

  • I'll ring the hotel. I'll tell them we'll check in tomorrow. [VERB PREP.]

    我来给旅馆打电话，通知他们我们明天入住。

  • He has checked into an alcohol treatment centre. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他住进了戒酒治疗中心。

  • Check us in at the hotel and wait for my call. [VERB noun PREP.]

    去宾馆替我们办理好入住手续，然后等我的电话。

2 PHRASAL VERB (在机场)办理登机手续，检票登机 When you check in at an airport, you arrive and show your ticket before going on a flight. (在机场)办理登机手续，检票登机

  • He had checked in at Amsterdam's Schiphol airport for a flight to Manchester. [VERB PREP.]

    他已经在阿姆斯特丹的斯希普霍尔机场办好了登机手续，准备飞往曼彻斯特。
