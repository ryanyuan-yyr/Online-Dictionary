scaly
skeɪli ˈskeɪli
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 长鳞片的;浑身是鳞的;有鳞的;多鳞的 A scaly animal has small pieces of hard skin covering its body. 长鳞片的;浑身是鳞的;有鳞的;多鳞的

  • The brown rat has prominent ears and a long scaly tail.

    那只棕鼠两只耳朵向前支着，一条长尾巴布满鳞片。

SYN squamous, squamate, lamellose, lamelliform  
2 ADJ-GRADED (皮肤)干涩粗糙的，脱皮的 If someone's skin is scaly, it has dry areas and small pieces of it come off. (皮肤)干涩粗糙的，脱皮的

  • If your skin becomes red, sore or very scaly, consult your doctor.

    如果皮肤发红、瘙痒或脱皮，要向医生咨询。

SYN flaky, scabrous, scurfy, furfuraceous [medicine]
