collection
kəlekʃən kəˈlɛkʃən
Word forms: plural collections
1 N-COUNT (一批)收藏品，收集物 A collection of things is a group of similar things that you have deliberately acquired, usually over a period of time. (一批)收藏品，收集物

  • Robert's collection of prints and paintings has been bought over the years.

    这些年来，罗伯特逐渐收藏了一批版画和绘画作品。

  • The Art Gallery of Ontario has the world's largest collection of sculptures by Henry Moore. [+ of]

    安大略美术馆是世界上收藏亨利·摩尔雕塑作品最多的美术馆。

  • He made the mistake of leaving his valuable record collection with a former girlfriend.

    他犯了一个错误，把自己收藏的一批珍贵唱片留在了以前的一个女友那里。

SYN accumulation, set, store, mass  
2 N-COUNT (故事、诗歌或文章的)集子，作品集 A collection of stories, poems, or articles is a number of them published in one book. (故事、诗歌或文章的)集子，作品集

  • Two years ago he published a collection of short stories called 'Facing The Music'.

    两年前，他出版了短篇小说集《勇敢面对》。

  • The Brookings Institution has assembled a collection of essays from foreign affairs experts. [+ of]

    布鲁金斯研究所将一批外交专家撰写的文章结集出版。

SYN compilation, accumulation, anthology  
3 N-COUNT 一堆，一群(东西) A collection of things is a group of things. 一堆，一群(东西)

  • Wye Lea is a collection of farm buildings that have been converted into an attractive complex. [+ of]

    “怀河草地”是一处由农舍改造而成的赏心悦目的建筑群。

4 N-COUNT (为新季设计的)系列时装 A fashion designer's new collection consists of the new clothes they have designed for the next season. (为新季设计的)系列时装
5 N-UNCOUNT 收集;采集 Collection is the act of collecting something from a place or from people. 收集;采集

  • Money can be sent to any one of 22,000 agents worldwide for collection.

    可以把钱汇给分布在世界各地的22,000名筹款代理人中的任何一位。

  • ...computer systems to speed up collection of information. [+ of]

    加速信息收集的计算机系统

  • ...public services including mail delivery and garbage collection.

    包括邮件派送和垃圾清理在内的公共服务

SYN gathering, acquisition, accumulation  
6 N-COUNT 募捐 If you organize a collection for charity, you collect money from people to give to charity. 募捐

  • I asked my headmaster if he could arrange a collection for a refugee charity. [+ for]

    我问校长他是否能为救济难民的慈善机构组织一次募捐。

SYN contribution, donation, alms  
7 N-COUNT (教堂礼拜时的)捐款 A collection is money that is given by people in church during some Christian services. (教堂礼拜时的)捐款
