irreversible
ɪrɪvɜːʳsɪbəl ˌɪrɪˈvɜrsəbəl
ADJ 不可逆转的;不可挽回的;不可撤销的 If a change is irreversible, things cannot be changed back to the way they were before. 不可逆转的;不可挽回的;不可撤销的

  • She could suffer irreversible brain damage if she is not treated within seven days.

    如果她在 7 天之内没有得到治疗,她的大脑有可能遭受不可逆转的损伤。

  • The reforms are irreversible.

    改革没有回头路。

SYN irrevocable, incurable, irreparable, final  
irreversibly

  • Television has irreversibly changed our perception of the Royal Family.

    电视不可逆转地改变了我们对皇室的印象。
