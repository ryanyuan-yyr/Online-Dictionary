knead
niːd nid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense kneads, present participle kneading, past tense , past participle kneaded
1 VERB 揉，捏（面团或其他食物） When you knead dough or other food, you press and squeeze it with your hands so that it becomes smooth and ready to cook. 揉，捏（面团或其他食物）

  • Lightly knead the mixture on a floured surface. [VERB noun]

    在撒上面粉的台面上轻揉该混合物。

SYN squeeze, work, massage, manipulate  
kneading

  • Just how much kneading is required depends on the sort of flour.

    需要揉多久取决于用哪种面。

2 VERB 揉捏；按摩；推拿 If you knead a part of someone's body, you press or squeeze it with your fingers. 揉捏；按摩；推拿

  • She felt him knead the aching muscles. [VERB noun]

    她感到他在按摩她酸痛的肌肉。
