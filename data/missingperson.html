missing person
ˈmɪsɪŋ ˈpɜːsən
Word forms: plural missing persons
N-COUNT 失踪的人;下落不明的人 A missing person has suddenly left their home without telling their family where they are going, and it is not known whether they are alive or dead. 失踪的人;下落不明的人

  • She's tracked down over two hundred missing persons, in many cases after the police have given up.

    她已经追查到了200多名失踪人口，多数是警方已经不抱希望的案子。
