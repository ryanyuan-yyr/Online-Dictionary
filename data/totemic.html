totem
toʊtəm ˈtoʊtəm
Word forms: plural totems
1 N-COUNT 图腾 In some societies, a family's totem is the particular animal, plant, or natural object which they regard as a special symbol and which they believe has spiritual significance. 图腾
2 N-COUNT 标志物;象征物 Something that is a totem of another thing is a symbol of it. 标志物;象征物 [written]

  • This opera is one of the cultural totems of Western civilisation. [+ of]

    这部歌剧是西方文明的文化标志物之一。
