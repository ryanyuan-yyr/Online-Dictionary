scrimmage
skrɪmɪdʒ ˈskrɪmɪdʒ ˈskrɪmɪdʒ
Word forms: scrimmages
1 N-UNCOUNT (美式足球中的)混战In American football, scrimmage is the action during a single period of play. (美式足球中的)混战

  • Bloom scored two touchdowns Saturday in a scrimmage.

    布卢姆在周六的一场混战中两次达阵得分。

2 N-COUNT (如美式足球、曲棍球运动的)训练比赛In sports such as American football and hockey, a scrimmage is a session of practice that consists of an actual game. (如美式足球、曲棍球运动的)训练比赛

  • ...the first full scrimmage in Flyers training camp.

    ...飞行员集训营中首次完整的训练比赛。
