ending
endɪŋ ˈɛndɪŋ
Word forms: plural endings
1 N-COUNT （书、故事、戏剧等的）结尾，结局 You can refer to the last part of a book, story, play, or film as the ending, especially when you are considering the way that the story ends. （书、故事、戏剧等的）结尾，结局

  • The film has a Hollywood happy ending.

    那部电影有一个好莱坞式的美满结局。

SYN finish, end, close, resolution  
2 N-COUNT 词尾;字尾 The ending of a word is the last part of it. 词尾;字尾

  • ...common word endings, like 'ing' in walking.

    常见词尾，如walking一词中的ing
