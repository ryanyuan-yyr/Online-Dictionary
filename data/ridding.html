rid
rɪd rɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rids, present participle ridding
The form rid is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle of the verb. rid 的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
1 PHRASE 扔掉;丢弃 When you get rid of something that you do not want or do not like, you take action so that you no longer have it or suffer from it. 扔掉;丢弃

  • The owner needs to get rid of the car for financial reasons.

    因为经济原因，车主必须把汽车处理掉。

  • There's corruption, and we're going to get rid of it.

    存在贪污腐败现象，我们将予以清除。

  • She will have to get rid of the excess weight on her hips.

    她得减去臀部多余的脂肪。

2 PHRASE 摆脱;甩掉;赶走 If you get rid of someone who is causing problems for you or who you do not like, you do something to prevent them affecting you any more, for example by making them leave. 摆脱;甩掉;赶走

  • He believed that his manager wanted to get rid of him for personal reasons.

    他认为经理是出于个人原因想开除他。

  • You seem in rather a hurry to get rid of me.

    看起来你急于想摆脱我。

3 VERB 使…去掉;使…去除;使…摆脱 If you rid a place or person of something undesirable or unwanted, you succeed in removing it completely from that place or person. 使…去掉;使…去除;使…摆脱

  • The proposals are an attempt to rid the country of political corruption. [VERB noun + of]

    这些提议试图使这个国家摆脱政治腐败。

  • The new vaccine may rid the world of one of its most terrifying diseases. [V n of n]

    这种新疫苗可能会消除世界上最可怕的一种疾病。

SYN free, clear, deliver, relieve  
4 VERB 摆脱;去除;消除 If you rid yourself of something you do not want, you take action so that you no longer have it or are no longer affected by it. 摆脱;去除;消除

  • Why couldn't he ever rid himself of those thoughts, those worries? [V pron-refl + of]

    为什么他总是无法摆脱那些想法和担忧?

  • ...the country's efforts to rid itself of poverty and hunger. [V pron-refl of n]

    该国为了消除贫困和饥饿而作出的努力

5 ADJ 得到解脱的;摆脱…的 If you are rid of someone or something that you did not want or that caused problems for you, they are no longer with you or causing problems for you. 得到解脱的;摆脱…的

  • The family had sought a way to be rid of her and the problems she had caused them. [+ of]

    这家人已经找到一个方法摆脱她以及她给他们带来的问题。

6 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 幸亏摆脱了 If you say that someone is well rid of someone, you think it is good that the person has gone because you did not like them or you think they caused a lot of problems. 幸亏摆脱了

  • It seems to me your wife was a shallow woman and you're well rid of her.

    在我看来你妻子是一个浅薄的女人，幸好你离开了她。
