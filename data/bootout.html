boot out
PHRASAL VERB 解雇；开除；赶出 If someone boots you out of a job, organization, or place, you are forced to leave it. 解雇；开除；赶出 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Schools are booting out record numbers of unruly pupils. [VERB PREP. noun]

    学校开除了数量空前的难以管教的小学生。

SYN dismiss, sack [informal] , expel, throw out
