gourmet
gʊəʳmeɪ ɡurmɛ gʊrˈmeɪ gɔrmeɪ ˈgʊrmeɪ ˈgɔrmeɪ
Word forms: plural gourmets
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 珍馐的；美味佳肴的 Gourmet food is nicer or more unusual or sophisticated than ordinary food, and is often more expensive. 珍馐的；美味佳肴的

  • Flavored coffee is sold at gourmet food stores and coffee shops.

    花式咖啡可在美食店和咖啡店买到。

  • The couple share a love of gourmet cooking.

    这夫妇俩都有烹饪美食的嗜好。

  • ...a gourmet dinner.

    美味大餐

SYN gastronomic, epicurean  
2 N-COUNT 美食家；讲究饮食的人;老饕 A gourmet is someone who enjoys good food, and who knows a lot about food and wine. 美食家；讲究饮食的人;老饕
SYN connoisseur, foodie [informal] , bon vivant, epicure
