convoy
kɒnvɔɪ ˈkɑnˌvɔɪ kənˈvɔɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural convoys, present participle convoying, past tense , past participle convoyed
1 N-COUNT [oft in NOUN] 车队;舰队 A convoy is a group of vehicles or ships travelling together. 车队;舰队

  • ...a U.N. convoy carrying food and medical supplies.

    运送食品和医疗用品的联合国车队

  • ...humanitarian relief convoys.

    人道主义援助车队

  • They travel in convoy with armed guards.

    他们的车队在武装警卫的护送下行进。

SYN escort, entourage, guard, protection  
2 VERB 护送;护卫;为…护航 To convoy goods or people somewhere means to move them there in a convoy. 护送;护卫;为…护航 [Also VERB n]

  • He ordered the combined fleet to convoy troops to Naples. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    他命令联合舰队将军队护送到那不勒斯。

SYN escort, conduct, accompany, shepherd
