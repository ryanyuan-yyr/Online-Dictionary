inferno
ɪnfɜːʳnoʊ ɪnˈfɜrnoʊ
Word forms: plural infernos
N-COUNT [usually singular] 大火灾 If you refer to a fire as an inferno, you mean that it is burning fiercely and causing great destruction. 大火灾 [journalism]

  • Rescue workers fought to get to victims inside the inferno.

    救援人员奋力营救大火中的受害者。

SYN blaze, fire, flames, conflagration
