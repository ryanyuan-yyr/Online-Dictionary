broad-minded
ˈbrɔdˈmaɪndɪd ˈbrɔdˌmaɪndɪd
(also broadminded)
ADJ-GRADED 心胸开阔的；开明的；宽宏大量的 If you describe someone as broad-minded, you approve of them because they are willing to accept types of behaviour which other people consider immoral. 心胸开阔的；开明的；宽宏大量的 [approval]

  • ...a fair and broad-minded man.

    公正无私、心胸开阔的人

SYN tolerant, open-minded, flexible, liberal
