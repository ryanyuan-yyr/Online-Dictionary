underlie
ʌndəʳlaɪ ˌʌndərˈlaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense underlies, present participle underlying, past tense underlay, past participle underlain
1 VERB 为…的起因；构成…的基础 If something underlies a feeling or situation, it is the cause or basis of it. 为…的起因；构成…的基础

  • Try to figure out what feeling underlies your anger. [VERB noun]

    努力找出你的愤怒之下潜藏的情感。

  • ...the energy which seems to underlie all human success. [VERB noun]

    人类一切成功背后似乎都不可缺少的干劲
