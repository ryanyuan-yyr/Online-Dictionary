disagreeable dɪsəgriːəbəl ˌdɪsəgriəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 令人不快的;讨厌的;不合意的 Something that is disagreeable is rather unpleasant. 令人不快的;讨厌的;不合意的

  • ...a disagreeable odour.

    难闻的气味

  • ...a comfort kit designed to make flying an altogether less disagreeable experience.

    让飞行总体上变得不再那么难受的舒适套装

SYN nasty, offensive, disgusting, unpleasant  
disagreeably

  • The taste is bitter and disagreeably pungent.

    味道既苦又辣得呛鼻。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不友好的;脾气坏的;难相处的 Someone who is disagreeable is unfriendly or unhelpful. 不友好的;脾气坏的;难相处的

  • He's a shallow, disagreeable man.

    他是个肤浅、难相处的人。

SYN ill-natured, difficult, nasty, cross
