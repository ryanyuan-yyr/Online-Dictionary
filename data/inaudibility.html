inaudible
ɪnɔːdɪbəl ɪnˈɔdəbəl
ADJ (声音)听不见的 If a sound is inaudible, you are unable to hear it. (声音)听不见的

  • His voice was almost inaudible.

    他的话音低得几乎听不见。

  • Animals are able to hear high-pitched sounds that are inaudible to humans.

    动物可以听到人类所听不到的高频声音。

SYN indistinct, low, stifled, mumbling
