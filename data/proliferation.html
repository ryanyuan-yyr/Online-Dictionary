proliferate
prəlɪfəreɪt proʊˌlɪfəˌreɪt prəˌlɪfərˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense proliferates, present participle proliferating, past tense , past participle proliferated
VERB 激增；剧增 If things proliferate, they increase in number very quickly. 激增；剧增 [formal]

  • Computerized data bases are proliferating fast. [VERB]

    计算机化的数据库正在激增。

  • In recent years commercial, cultural, travel and other contacts have proliferated between Europe and China. [VERB]

    近年来，欧洲和中国之间的商业、文化、旅游及其他交往发展迅猛。

SYN increase, expand, breed, mushroom  
proliferation

  • ...the proliferation of nuclear weapons.

    核武器的扩散

  • Smoking triggers off cell proliferation.

    吸烟会引起细胞增生。
