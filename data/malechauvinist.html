male chauvinist
Word forms: plural male chauvinists
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （态度、言语等）大男子主义的 If you describe an attitude or remark as male chauvinist, you are critical of it because you think it is based on the belief that men are naturally superior to women. （态度、言语等）大男子主义的 [disapproval]

  • The male chauvinist attitude of some people in the company could get you down.

    公司里一些人的大男子主义作风会让人不快。

  • ...the male chauvinist pig who wants women in the home and in their place.

    希望女人都足不出户、安分守己的满脑子大男子主义的蠢货

A male chauvinist is a man who has male chauvinist views.大男子主义者

  • I'm not a male chauvinist.

    我不是个大男子主义者。
