alias
eɪliəs ˈeɪliəs ˈeɪljəs
Word forms: plural aliases
1 N-COUNT （尤指罪犯使用的）化名，别名 An alias is a false name, especially one used by a criminal. （尤指罪犯使用的）化名，别名

  • Using an alias, he had rented a house in Fleet, Hampshire.

    他用化名在汉普郡的舰队街租了间房子。

SYN pseudonym, pen name, assumed name, stage name  
2 PREP （尤指罪犯或演员的）别名，又名 You use alias when you are mentioning another name that someone, especially a criminal or an actor, is known by. （尤指罪犯或演员的）别名，又名

  • ...the defendant Pericles Pericleous, alias Peter Smith.

    被告佩里克莱斯·佩里克利奥斯，又名彼得·史密斯
