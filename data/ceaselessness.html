ceaseless
siːsləs ˈsislɪs
ADJ-GRADED (常指令人不快的事)不停歇的，无休止的，不断的 If something, often something unpleasant, is ceaseless, it continues for a long time without stopping or changing. (常指令人不快的事)不停歇的，无休止的，不断的 [formal]

  • There is a ceaseless struggle from noon to night.

    从中午到夜晚，争斗没有停歇。

SYN continual, constant, endless, continuous  
ceaselessly

  • The characters complain ceaselessly about food queues, prices and corruption.

    那些人不停地抱怨购买食品要排队、物价问题以及贪污腐败的问题。
