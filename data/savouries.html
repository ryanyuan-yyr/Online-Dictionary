savoury
seɪvəri
Word forms: plural savouries
in AM, use 美国英语用 savory
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 咸味的;辛辣的 Savoury food has a salty or spicy flavour rather than a sweet one. 咸味的;辛辣的

  • Italian cooking is best known for savoury dishes.

    意大利菜以辛辣菜系最为出名。

SYN spicy, rich, delicious, tasty  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 咸味小吃;辣味小吃;下酒菜 Savouries are small items of savoury food that are usually eaten as a snack, for example with alcoholic drinks at a party or before a meal. 咸味小吃;辣味小吃;下酒菜 [British]
