house-sit
Word forms: house-sits, house-sitting, house-sat
V-I (主人外出期间)代为照看房屋If someone house-sits for you, they stay at your house and look after it while you are away. (主人外出期间)代为照看房屋

  • Otherwise I might just have gone straight back to Connecticut, where Will was house-sitting for me.

    否则我可能直接回康涅狄格州了，威尔正帮我看房子呢。
