spillage
spɪlɪdʒ ˈspɪlɪdʒ
Word forms: plural spillages
N-VAR 溢出;溢出物 If there is a spillage, a substance such as oil escapes from its container. Spillage is also used to refer to the substance that escapes. 溢出;溢出物

  • ...an oil spillage off the coast of Texas.

    得克萨斯州沿海的溢油事件

  • ...an accident in the workplace involving blood spillage.

    有流血发生的劳动事故
