expunge
ɪkspʌndʒ ɪkˈspʌndʒ ɪkˈspʌŋkt ɛkˈspʌndʒ ɪkˈspʌndʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense expunges, present participle expunging, past tense , past participle expunged
VERB 删除;清除;抹掉 If you expunge something, you get rid of it completely, because it causes problems or bad feelings. 删除;清除;抹掉 [formal]

  • The revolutionaries expunged domestic opposition. [VERB noun]

    革命者肃清了国内敌对势力。

  • The experience was something he had tried to expunge from his memory. [VERB noun + from]

    他曾努力将那段经历从记忆中抹去。

  • His name was expunged from the record books. [be VERB-ed + from]

    他的名字被从档案簿中删去了。

SYN erase, remove, destroy, abolish
