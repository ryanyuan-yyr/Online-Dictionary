redefine
riːdɪfaɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redefines, present participle redefining, past tense , past participle redefined
VERB 重新定义;重新界定;重新思考 If you redefine something, you cause people to consider it in a new way. 重新定义;重新界定;重新思考

  • We will finally have to redefine our relationship with neighbouring states in north Africa. [VERB noun]

    我们最终将不得不重新界定我们和北非邻国的关系。

  • Feminists have redefined the role of women. [VERB noun]

    女权主义者重新界定了女性的角色。
