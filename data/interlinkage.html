interlink
ɪntəʳlɪŋk ˌɪntəˈlɪŋk ˈɪntərˈlɪŋk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense interlinks, present participle interlinking, past tense , past participle interlinked
V-RECIP-ERG (使)相互连接;(使)相互联结 Things that are interlinked or interlink are linked with each other in some way. (使)相互连接;(使)相互联结

  • Those two processes are very closely interlinked. [be VERB-ed]

    那两个过程紧密相连。

  • The question to be addressed is interlinked with the question of human rights. [be VERB-ed + with]

    将要谈到的问题与人权问题有关。

  • ...a more integrated transport network, with bus, rail, and ferry services all interlinking. [VERB]

    更协调、更加一体化的交通网络,公共汽车、铁路与轮渡全部相互连接

  • The play takes three Scottish tales and interlinks them. [V pl-n]

    剧本吸收了三个苏格兰民间故事并把它们相互衔接起来。

SYN interconnect, link, knit, mesh
