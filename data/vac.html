Word forms: plural vacs
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] （大学的）假期，寒暑假 A vac is a period of the year when universities and colleges are officially closed. Vac is an abbreviation for 'vacation'. （大学的）假期，寒暑假 [British, informal]

  • ...the summer vac.

    暑假

2 N-COUNT 真空吸尘器 A vac is an electric machine which sucks up dust and dirt from carpets. Vac is an abbreviation for 'vacuum cleaner'. 真空吸尘器 [informal]

  • We found the vac easy to push.

    我们发现这种真空吸尘器很容易推着走。
