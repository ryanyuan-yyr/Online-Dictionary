never-ending
ADJ 永无止境的;永无休止的;永恒的 If you describe something bad or unpleasant as never-ending, you are emphasizing that it seems to last a very long time. 永无止境的;永无休止的;永恒的 [emphasis]

  • ...a never-ending series of scandals.

    一系列无休止的丑闻

  • The spiral of terrorism becomes never-ending.

    恐怖主义活动没完没了，不断升级。

SYN endless, constant, continuous, persistent
