announcement
ənaʊnsmənt əˈnaʊnsmənt
Word forms: plural announcements
1 N-COUNT [NOUN that] 布告;声明;通告 An announcement is a statement made to the public or to the media which gives information about something that has happened or that will happen. 布告;声明;通告

  • Sir Robert made his announcement after talks with the President.

    在与总统会谈之后，罗伯特爵士发表了声明。

  • There has been no formal announcement by either government.

    双方政府都未作正式通告。

SYN statement, communication, broadcast, explanation  
2 N-SING 宣布;宣告;公布 The announcement of something that has happened is the act of telling people about it. 宣布;宣告;公布

  • ...the announcement of their engagement. [+ of]

    他们订婚消息的宣布

  • There has been no official announcement of the arrests.

    还没有正式发布逮捕这些人的消息。

SYN declaration, report, reporting, publication  
3 N-COUNT （在报纸、商店橱窗等处刊登的）启事，广告，通告 An announcement in a public place, such as a newspaper or the window of a shop, is a short piece of writing telling people about something or asking for something. （在报纸、商店橱窗等处刊登的）启事，广告，通告

  • He will place an announcement in the personal column of The Daily Telegraph.

    他将在《每日电讯报》的个人专栏上登一则启事。
