deservedly
dɪzɜːʳvɪdli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] [ADVERB adjective/adverb] 应得地;值得地;理所应当地 You use deservedly to indicate that someone deserved what happened to them, especially when it was something good. 应得地;值得地;理所应当地

  • He deservedly won the Player of the Year award.

    他理所应当地获得了年度最佳球员奖。

  • You will have to book well in advance for this deservedly popular hotel.

    这家备受欢迎的旅馆可谓名不虚传，你需要早早预订。

  • He was highly praised, and deservedly so, by the Asian Times.

    他受到了《亚洲时报》的高度赞扬，而他确实当之无愧。

SYN rightly, fittingly, fairly, appropriately
