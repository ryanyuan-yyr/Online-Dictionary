fleck
flek flɛk
Word forms: plural flecks
N-COUNT [usually plural] 斑点;(斑点状的)微粒 Flecks are small marks on a surface, or objects that look like small marks. 斑点;(斑点状的)微粒

  • He went to the men's room to wash flecks of blood from his shirt.

    他去男洗手间清洗衬衫上的斑斑血迹。

  • His hair is dark grey with flecks of ginger.

    他深灰色的头发夹杂着几缕姜黄色。

SYN mark, speck, streak, spot
