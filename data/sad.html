Word forms: comparative sadder, superlative saddest
1 ADJ-GRADED [oft ADJ that/to-inf] 悲哀的;悲伤的;伤心的 If you are sad, you feel unhappy, usually because something has happened that you do not like. 悲哀的;悲伤的;伤心的

  • The relationship had been important to me and its loss left me feeling sad and empty.

    这份感情对我很重要，失去它让我感到悲伤而又空虚。

  • I'm sad that Julie's marriage is on the verge of splitting up.

    朱莉的婚姻濒临破裂，我为此感到难过。

  • I'd grown fond of our little house and felt sad to leave it.

    我已经慢慢喜欢上了我们的小屋，要离开它让我很伤心。

  • I'm sad about my toys getting burned in the fire. [+ about]

    我的玩具在这场火中付之一炬，这令我很难过。

SYN unhappy, down, low, blue  
sadly

  • ...a gallant man who will be sadly missed by all his comrades.

    所有同志都会沉痛怀念的勇士

  • Judy said sadly, 'He has abandoned me.'

    朱迪伤心地说，“他抛弃了我。”

sadness

  • It is with a mixture of sadness and joy that I say farewell.

    我怀着悲喜交加的心情道别。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (故事、消息)凄惨的,可悲的,令人难过的 Sad stories and sad news make you feel sad. (故事、消息)凄惨的,可悲的,令人难过的

  • ...a desperately humorous, impossibly sad novel.

    极其幽默诙谐又令人无比感伤的小说

  • I received the sad news that he had been killed in a motorcycle accident.

    我得知了这个不幸的消息，说他在一场摩托车事故中丧生。

SYN tragic, moving, upsetting, dark  
3 ADJ-GRADED (事件、境况)不幸的,令人遗憾的 A sad event or situation is unfortunate or undesirable. (事件、境况)不幸的,令人遗憾的

  • It's a sad truth that children are the biggest victims of passive smoking.

    不幸的事实是，儿童是被动吸烟的最大受害者。

SYN deplorable, bad, sorry, terrible  
sadly

  • Sadly, bamboo plants die after flowering.

    可惜的是，竹子开花后便会枯死。

  • Things are sadly different in Britain.

    令人遗憾的是，英国却非如此。

4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 可悲的;荒唐的 If you describe someone as sad, you do not have any respect for them and think their behaviour or ideas are ridiculous. 可悲的;荒唐的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...sad old bikers and youngsters who think that Jim Morrison is God.

    把吉姆·莫里森奉作神明的一些荒唐可悲的摩托车老手和年轻人

5 PHRASE [PHRASE with cl] 可惜的是;很遗憾 You can use the expression sad to say when you are describing an situation which you find unfortunate. 可惜的是;很遗憾

  • How does a suffering alcoholic get into one of these hospitals? Sad to say, there are not very many of them around.

    一个受酒瘾折磨的酗酒者怎样才能进入这样一家医院呢？很遗憾，这种医院并不多。

  • He died five or six years ago I'm sad to say.

    很遗憾，他在五六年前去世了。
