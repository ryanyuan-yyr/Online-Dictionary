welter
weltəʳ ˈwɛltər
QUANT 杂乱的一堆 A welter of something is a large quantity of it which occurs suddenly or in a confusing way. 杂乱的一堆 [written]

  • ...patients with a welter of confusing symptoms. [+ of]

    有一堆疑难症状的病人

  • ...the welter of publicity that followed his engagement. [+ of]

    他上任后的一堆杂乱的宣传活动
