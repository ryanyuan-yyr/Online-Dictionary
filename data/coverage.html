coverage
kʌvərɪdʒ ˈkʌvərɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 新闻报道；采访 The coverage of something in the news is the reporting of it. 新闻报道；采访

  • Now a special TV network gives live coverage of most races. [+ of]

    现在有一个专门的电视网络对大部分比赛进行现场直播。

  • Most media coverage disapproves of the travellers' lifestyle and values.

    大多数媒体报道都不赞成那些旅行者的生活方式和价值观。

SYN reporting, treatment, analysis, description
