shock horror
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 令人震惊的;令人愤怒的;骇人听闻的 A shock horror story is presented in a way that is intended to cause great shock or anger. 令人震惊的;令人愤怒的;骇人听闻的 [informal]

  • The media is full of shock-horror headlines about under-age crime.

    媒体上充斥着各种关于未成年人犯罪的骇人听闻的新闻标题。

2 EXCLAM 真恐怖,太意外了(假装震惊或惊讶时说) You can say shock horror! in reaction to something that other people may find shocking or surprising, to indicate that you do not find it shocking or surprising at all. 真恐怖,太意外了(假装震惊或惊讶时说) [humorous, informal, feelings]

  • I felt intellectually superior despite — shock horror — my lack of qualifications.

    我觉得自己比别人聪明，尽管，天啊，我没有什么资历。
