initially
ɪnɪʃəli ɪˈnɪʃəli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 最初;开始 Initially means soon after the beginning of a process or situation, rather than in the middle or at the end of it. 最初;开始

  • Forecasters say the gales may not be as bad as they initially predicted.

    预报员说大风也许不像他们起初预报的那么猛烈。

  • Initially, they were wary of Simon.

    开始时他们对西蒙小心提防。

SYN at first, first, firstly, originally
