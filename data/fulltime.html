full-time
ˈfʊlˌtaɪm
(also full time)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 全职的;专职的;全日制的 Full-time work or study involves working or studying for the whole of each normal working week rather than for part of it. 全职的;专职的;全日制的

  • ...a full-time job.

    全职工作

  • ...full-time staff.

    专职员工

Full-time is also an adverb.

  • Deirdre works full-time.

    戴尔德丽从事全职工作。

2 PHRASE [usually verb-link PHRASE] 费时费力的活 If you describe a regular activity or task as a full-time job, you mean that it takes up so much of your time it is like doing a paid job. 费时费力的活

  • The way she did it, mothering was a full-time job.

    照顾孩子于她是一件不得空闲的苦差事。

3 N-UNCOUNT (足球等的)比赛结束，终场 In games such as football, full-time is the end of a match. (足球等的)比赛结束，终场 [British]

  • The score at full-time was Arsenal 1, Sampdoria 1.

    比赛结束时阿森纳队和桑普多利亚队 1 比 1 战平。
