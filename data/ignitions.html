ignition
ɪgnɪʃən ɪgˈnɪʃən
Word forms: plural ignitions
1 N-VAR 点火装置;点火器 In a car engine, the ignition is the part where the fuel is ignited. 点火装置;点火器

  • The device automatically disconnects the ignition.

    该装置使点火器自动断开。

2 N-SING (汽车里的)点火开关 Inside a car, the ignition is the part where you turn the key so that the engine starts. (汽车里的)点火开关
3 N-UNCOUNT 着火;起火 Ignition is the process of something starting to burn. 着火;起火

  • The ignition of methane gas killed eight men. [+ of]

    甲烷起火，致使 8 人丧生。
