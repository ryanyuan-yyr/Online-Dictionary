overpay
oʊvəʳpeɪ ˌoʊvərˈpeɪ ˈoʊvərˌpeɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overpays, present participle overpaying, past tense , past participle overpaid
1 VERB 给…过高报酬；多付 If you overpay someone, or if you overpay for something, you pay more than is necessary or reasonable. 给…过高报酬；多付

  • Management has to make sure it does not overpay its staff. [VERB noun]

    管理层必须确保没有向员工支付过高工资。

  • The council is said to have been overpaying for repairs made by its housing department. [VERB + for]

    据说市政委员会一直在向其房管部支付过高的维修费。

  • The scheme will overpay some lawyers and underpay others. [VERB noun]

    该方案将使一些律师获得过高酬劳，而其他律师则报酬过低。
