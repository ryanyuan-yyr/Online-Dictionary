bite-sized ˈbaɪtsaɪzd ˈbaɪtˌsaɪz
(also bite-size)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （食物）大小适合入口的 Bite-sized pieces of food are small enough to fit easily in your mouth. （食物）大小适合入口的

  • ...bite-sized pieces of cheese.

    大小适合入口的干酪块

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 很小的；小而简单的 If you describe something as bite-sized, you like it because it is small enough to be considered or dealt with easily. 很小的；小而简单的 [approval]

  • ...bite-size newspaper items.

    简短的报纸新闻
