fortitude
fɔːʳtɪtjuːd -tuːd ˈfɔrtəˌtud ˈfɔrtəˌtjud
1 N-UNCOUNT 刚毅;坚忍 If you say that someone has shown fortitude, you admire them for being brave, calm, and uncomplaining when they have experienced something unpleasant or painful. 刚毅;坚忍 [formal, approval]

  • He suffered a long series of illnesses with tremendous dignity and fortitude.

    他凭借超乎常人的保持生命尊严的信念和顽强意志力忍受着各种病痛的折磨。

SYN courage, strength, resolution, determination
