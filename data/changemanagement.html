change management
tʃeɪndʒ ˈmænɪdʒmənt
N-UNCOUNT (鼓励组织及个人有效应对变化的)变革管理 Change management is a style of management that aims to encourage organizations and individuals to deal effectively with the changes taking place in their work. (鼓励组织及个人有效应对变化的)变革管理 [business]
