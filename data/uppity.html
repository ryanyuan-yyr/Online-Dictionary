uppity
ʌpɪti ˈʌpəti
ADJ-GRADED 自大的；骄横的 If you say that someone is uppity, you mean that they are behaving as if they were very important and you do not think that they are important. 自大的；骄横的 [informal, disapproval]

  • If you just tried to show normal dignity, you were viewed as uppity.

    就算是只想表现出正常的尊严，也会被人认为是骄傲自大。

SYN conceited, cocky, swanky [informal] , self-important
