disequilibrium
dɪsiːkwɪlɪbriəm ˌdɪsˌikwɪˈlɪbriəm
N-UNCOUNT 不平衡;不稳定;失调 Disequilibrium is a state in which things are not stable or certain, but are likely to change suddenly. 不平衡;不稳定;失调 [formal]

  • There may be a period of disequilibrium as family members adjust to the new baby.

    宝宝的降生可能会使家庭有一段手忙脚乱的适应阶段。
