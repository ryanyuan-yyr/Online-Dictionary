static
stætɪk ˈstætɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 静止的;不变化的 Something that is static does not move or change. 静止的;不变化的

  • The number of young people obtaining qualifications has remained static or decreased.

    获得资格证的年轻人数量一直维持不变或出现下降。

  • Both your pictures are of static subjects.

    你的两张照片拍的都是静止物体。

SYN stationary, still, motionless, fixed  
2 N-UNCOUNT 静电 Static or static electricity is electricity which can be caused by things rubbing against each other and which collects on things such as your body or metal objects. 静电
3 N-UNCOUNT (广播、电视里的)静电噪声，静电干扰 If there is static on the radio or television, you hear a series of loud noises which spoils the sound. (广播、电视里的)静电噪声，静电干扰
