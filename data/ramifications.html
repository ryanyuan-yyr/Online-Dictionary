ramification
ræmɪfɪkeɪʃən ˌræməfɪˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural ramifications
N-COUNT [usually plural, oft with poss] （决定、计划、事件产生的）后果，影响 The ramifications of a decision, plan, or event are all its consequences and effects, especially ones which are not obvious at first. （决定、计划、事件产生的）后果，影响

  • The book analyses the social and political ramifications of AIDS for the gay community. [+ of]

    这本书分析了艾滋病给同性恋群体所带来的社会和政治影响。
