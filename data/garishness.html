garish
geərɪʃ ˈɡɛərɪʃ ˈgærɪʃ ˈgɛrɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 花哨的；炫目的；俗艳的 You describe something as garish when you dislike it because it is very bright in an unattractive, showy way. 花哨的；炫目的；俗艳的 [disapproval]

  • They climbed the garish purple-carpeted stairs.

    他们登上铺着俗艳的紫色地毯的楼梯。

  • ...the restaurant's garish, illuminated signs.

    餐馆花哨的烫金招牌

SYN gaudy, bright, glaring, vulgar  
garishly

  • ...a garishly patterned three-piece suite.

    图案艳丽的三件套家具
