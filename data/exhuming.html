exhume
ekshjuːm ɪgzuːm ɛksˈhjum ɪkˈsjum ɛgˈzjum ɪgˈzjum
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense exhumes, present participle exhuming, past tense , past participle exhumed
VERB [usually passive] （尤指为查明死因而）挖出，掘出（尸体） If a dead person's body is exhumed, it is taken out of the ground where it is buried, especially so that it can be examined in order to find out how the person died. （尤指为查明死因而）挖出，掘出（尸体） [formal]

  • His remains have been exhumed from a cemetery in Queens, New York City. [be VERB-ed]

    他的遗体被从纽约市皇后区的墓地里挖了出来。

SYN dig up, unearth, disinter, unbury  
exhumation

  • Detectives ordered the exhumation when his wife said she believed he had been killed.

    由于他妻子说她相信他是被杀害的，侦探们命令挖出尸体。
