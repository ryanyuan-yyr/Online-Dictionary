tarmac
tɑːʳmæk ˈtɑrˌmæk
1 N-UNCOUNT (铺路面用的)柏油碎石 Tarmac is a material used for making road surfaces, consisting of crushed stones mixed with tar. (铺路面用的)柏油碎石 [British, trademark]

  • ...a strip of tarmac.

    狭长的柏油碎石路面

  • ...tarmac paths.

    柏油碎石小道

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 blacktop
2 N-SING 柏油碎石地面;(尤指)停机坪 The tarmac is an area with a surface made of tarmac, especially the area from which planes take off at an airport. 柏油碎石地面;(尤指)停机坪

  • Standing on the tarmac were two American planes.

    停机坪上停着两架美国飞机。
