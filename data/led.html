led
led lɛd
Led is the past tense and past participle of lead.
-led -led
1 COMB in ADJ [usually ADJ n] （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“…领导的”，“…管理的”，“…控制的” -led combines with nouns to form adjectives which indicate that something is organized, directed, or controlled by a particular person or group. （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“…领导的”，“…管理的”，“…控制的”

  • ...the student-led democracy movement.

    由学生领导的民主运动

  • ...a German-led European consortium.

    由德国人主事的欧洲财团

2 COMB in ADJ-GRADED （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“…起主导作用（或影响）的” -led combines with nouns to form adjectives which indicate that something is mainly caused or influenced by a particular factor. （与名词连用构成形容词）表示“…起主导作用（或影响）的”

  • Their prosperity depends on export-led growth.

    他们的繁荣依靠的是出口带动的经济增长

  • ...a market-led economy.

    市场主导的经济
