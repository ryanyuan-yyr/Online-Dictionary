wash down
1 PHRASAL VERB (用水)送服 If you wash something, especially food, down with a drink, you drink the drink after eating the food, especially to make the food easier to swallow or digest. (用水)送服 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • He took two aspirin immediately and washed them down with three cups of water. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他立刻吃了两片阿司匹林，用3杯水送服下去。

  • ...a massive beef sandwich washed down by a bottle of beer. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    就着一瓶啤酒吃下的一个大号牛肉三明治

2 PHRASAL VERB (全部)冲洗;(彻底)冲刷 If you wash down an object, you wash it all, from top to bottom. (全部)冲洗;(彻底)冲刷 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The prisoner started to wash down the walls of his cell. [VERB PREP. noun]

    囚犯开始冲洗各自囚室的墙壁。
