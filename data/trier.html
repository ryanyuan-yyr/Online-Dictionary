trier
traɪəʳ ˈtraɪər
Word forms: plural triers
N-COUNT 干劲十足的人;工作勤奋的人 If you say that someone is a trier, you approve of them because they try very hard at things that they do, although they are not often successful. 干劲十足的人;工作勤奋的人 [British, approval]

  • He may not always achieve greatness but at least he's a trier.

    他也许并非总能成就大事，但至少他竭尽所能。
