mortify
mɔːʳtɪfaɪ ˈmɔrtəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mortifies, present participle mortifying, past tense , past participle mortified
1 VERB [no cont] 使丢面子;使窘迫;使羞愧 If you say that something mortifies you, you mean that it offends or embarrasses you a great deal. 使丢面子;使窘迫;使羞愧

    简离开丈夫的做法使她的家庭蒙羞。
