think-tank
Word forms: plural think-tanks
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (尤指政府的)智囊团，智库 A think-tank is a group of experts who are gathered together by an organization, especially by a government, in order to consider various problems and try and work out ways to solve them. (尤指政府的)智囊团，智库

  • ...Moscow's leading foreign policy think-tank.
