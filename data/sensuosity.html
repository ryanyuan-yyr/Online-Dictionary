sensuous
senʃuəs ˈsɛnʃuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 愉悦感官的;给人以美感的 Something that is sensuous gives pleasure to the mind or body through the senses. 愉悦感官的;给人以美感的

  • The film is ravishing to look at and boasts a sensuous musical score.

    这部电影画面迷人，配乐悦耳动听。

  • It is a sensuous but demanding car to drive.

    这车坐上去很舒服，但需要高超的驾驶技术。

SYN pleasurable, pleasing, sensory, gratifying  
sensuously

  • She lay in the deep bath for a long time, enjoying its sensuously perfumed water.

    她在一个深浴缸里泡了半天，享受着舒服而充满芳香的沐浴。

2 ADJ-GRADED 性感的;耽于肉欲的 Someone or something that is sensuous shows or suggests a great liking for sexual pleasure. 性感的;耽于肉欲的

  • ...his sensuous young mistress, Marie-Therese.

    他年轻性感的情妇玛丽－特蕾泽

  • ...wide sensuous lips.

    丰阔而性感的嘴唇

  • His voice was deep but gentle, almost sensuous.

    他的声音低沉而温柔，几乎可以说是性感。

SYN sexy, erotic, voluptuous, lush  
sensuously

  • The nose was straight, the mouth sensuously wide and full.

    鼻子直挺，嘴巴大而饱满，很性感。
