glue
gluː glu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense glues, present participle glueing, present participle gluing, past tense , past participle glued
1 N-MASS 胶；胶水 Glue is a sticky substance used for joining things together, often for repairing broken things. 胶；胶水

  • ...a tube of glue.

    一管胶水

  • ...high quality glues.

    优质胶水

SYN adhesive, cement, gum, paste  
2 VERB 用胶水粘 If you glue one object to another, you stick them together using glue. 用胶水粘

  • Glue the fabric around the window. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    用胶水把布料粘在窗户周围。

  • The material is cut and glued in place. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    材料被剪切下来并粘贴到合适位置。

  • They are glued together. [be V-ed together]

    它们被粘贴在一起。

SYN stick, fix, seal, cement  
3 V-PASSIVE 完全吸引；使专注于 If you say that someone is glued to something, you mean that they are giving it all their attention. 完全吸引；使专注于

  • They are all glued to the Olympic Games. [be VERB-ed + to]

    他们都被奥运会吸引住了。

If you say that someone keeps their eyes glued to something, you mean that they are watching it with all their attention.目不转睛地看

  • He kept his eyes glued to the clock.

    他目不转睛地盯着钟表。
