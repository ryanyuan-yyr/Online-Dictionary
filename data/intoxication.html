intoxication
ɪntɒksɪkeɪʃən ɪnˌtɑksɪˈkeɪʃən
1 N-UNCOUNT 喝醉;醉酒 Intoxication is the state of being drunk. 喝醉;醉酒 [formal]

  • Intoxication interferes with memory and thinking, speech and coordination.

    醉酒影响记忆、思考、语言与协调性。

SYN drunkenness, inebriation, tipsiness, inebriety  
2 N-UNCOUNT 极度兴奋;陶醉 You use intoxication to refer to a quality that something has that makes you feel very excited. 极度兴奋;陶醉 [literary]

  • ...the intoxication of greed and success. [+ of]

    对贪婪与成功的沉醉

SYN excitement, euphoria, elation, exhilaration
