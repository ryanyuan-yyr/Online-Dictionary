unluckily
ʌnlʌkɪli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 不幸地；不走运地 You use unluckily as a comment on something bad or unpleasant that happens to someone, in order to suggest sympathy for them or that it was not their fault. 不幸地；不走运地

  • Unluckily for him, the fraud officers were watching this flight too. [+ for]

    对他而言不幸的是，那些反诈骗官员也在密切注视着这个航班。

  • Some people unluckily achieve suicide when they only meant to attempt it.

    一些人只是想尝试一下自杀，但却不幸真的身亡了。
