variety
vəraɪɪti vəˈraɪɪtɪ vəˈraɪəti
Word forms: plural varieties
1 N-UNCOUNT 变化;多样化 If something has variety, it consists of things which are different from each other. 变化;多样化

  • Susan's idea of freedom was to have variety in her life style. [+ in]

    苏珊心目中的自由就是拥有丰富多彩的生活。

  • I know no store anywhere in the world that has such variety and display.

    我不知道世界上还有哪个商店的经营品种如此繁多，陈列如此美观。

  • The music itself has so much variety.

    音乐本身就种类繁多。

2 N-SING 各式各样;多种多样 A variety of things is a number of different kinds or examples of the same thing. 各式各样;多种多样

  • West Hampstead has a variety of good shops and supermarkets.

    西汉普斯特德有各种各样不错的店铺和超级市场。

  • The island offers such a wide variety of scenery and wildlife. [+ of]

    这个岛屿拥有丰富多样的景致及种类繁多的野生动植物。

  • People change their mind for a variety of reasons. [+ of]

    人们会由于种种原因改变主意。

3 N-COUNT 品种;种类 A variety of something is a type of it. 品种;种类

  • I'm always pleased to try out a new variety.

    我一直乐于尝试新产品。

  • She has 12 varieties of old-fashioned roses. [+ of]

    她有12种双花玫瑰。

SYN type, sort, kind, make  
4 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 综艺表演;综艺节目 Variety is a type of entertainment which includes many different kinds of acts in the same show. 综艺表演;综艺节目

  • ...a variety show of music, comedy, and magic.

    汇集了音乐、喜剧及魔术表演的一档综艺节目
