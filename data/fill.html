fill
fɪl fɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense fills, present participle filling, past tense , past participle filled
1 V-ERG (使)装满;(使)注满;(使)充满 If you fill a container or area, or if it fills, an amount of something enters it that is enough to make it full. (使)装满;(使)注满;(使)充满

  • Fill a saucepan with water and bring to a slow boil. [VERB noun + with]

    往平底锅里加满水,小火煮沸。

  • She made sandwiches, filled a flask and put sugar in. [VERB noun]

    她做了三明治，把保温瓶灌满，又放了糖进去。

  • The victims' lungs fill quickly with fluid. [VERB with noun]

    受害者的肺里很快充满了积液。

  • The boy's eyes filled with tears. [VERB + with]

    男孩眼泪汪汪。

  • While the bath was filling, he padded about in his underpants. [VERB]

    往浴缸里放水时，他穿着内裤走来走去。

SYN top up, fill up, make full, become full

5 VERB 使强烈地感到;(感情)充盈于 If something fills you with an emotion, or if an emotion fills you, you experience this emotion strongly. 使强烈地感到;(感情)充盈于
