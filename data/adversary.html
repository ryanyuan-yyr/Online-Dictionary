adversary
ædvəʳsəri -seri ˈædvərˌsɛri
Word forms: plural adversaries
N-COUNT 敌手；对手 Your adversary is someone you are competing with, or arguing or fighting against. 敌手；对手

  • Elliott crossed the finish line just half a second behind his adversary.

    埃利奥特跨过终点线时仅比对手落后半秒钟。

  • His political adversaries were creating a certain amount of trouble for him.

    他的政敌正在给他制造一些麻烦。

SYN opponent, rival, opposer, enemy
