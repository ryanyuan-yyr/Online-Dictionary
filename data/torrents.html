torrent
tɒrənt tɔːr- ˈtɔrənt ˈtɑrənt
Word forms: plural torrents
1 N-COUNT 急流;激流;洪流 A torrent is a lot of water falling or flowing rapidly or violently. 急流;激流;洪流

  • Torrents of water gushed into the reservoir. [+ of]

    急流涌入了水库。

  • The rain came down in torrents, and we could see nothing…

    大雨倾盆而下，我们看不见任何东西。

  • The trip involved crossing a raging torrent.

    旅途中要穿过一条水势凶猛的急流。

SYN downpour, flood, shower, deluge  
2 N-COUNT (谩骂、问题等的)连发，迸发 A torrent of abuse or questions is a lot of abuse or questions directed continuously at someone. (谩骂、问题等的)连发，迸发

  • He turned round and directed a torrent of abuse at me. [+ of]

    他转过身来冲我劈头盖脸地骂了一通。

  • …a £45,000 offer which prompted a torrent of criticism in the media.

    激起媒体一片指责的 4.5 万英镑的出价

SYN outburst, stream, barrage, hail
