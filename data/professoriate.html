professor
prəfesəʳ proʊˈfɛsər prəˈfɛsər
Word forms: plural professors
1 N-TITLE; N-COUNT; N-VOC （英国大学的）教授 A professor in a British university is the most senior teacher in a department. （英国大学的）教授

  • ...Professor Cameron.

    卡梅伦教授

  • In 1979, only 2% of British professors were female.

    1979年，英国只有2%的教授是女性。

2 N-COUNT; N-TITLE; N-VOC （美国或加拿大的）大学教师，学院教师 A professor in an American or Canadian university or college is a teacher of the highest rank. （美国或加拿大的）大学教师，学院教师

  • Robert Dunn is a professor of economics at George Washington University.

    罗伯特·达恩是乔治·华盛顿大学经济学的教师。

SYN don [British] , fellow [British] , prof [informal] , head of faculty
