highlighter
haɪlaɪtəʳ ˈhaɪˌlaɪtər
Word forms: plural highlighters

2 N-COUNT （用来标注文件的）荧光笔 A highlighter is a pen with brightly-coloured ink that is used to mark parts of a document. （用来标注文件的）荧光笔
