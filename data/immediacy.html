immediacy
ɪmiːdiəsi ɪˈmidiəsi
N-UNCOUNT 即时性;紧迫性 The immediacy of an event or situation is the quality that it has which makes it seem important or exciting because it is happening at the present time. 即时性;紧迫性

  • Do they understand the immediacy of the crisis?. [+ of]

    他们明白危机的紧迫性吗？

  • The books that result have intensity and a fascinating immediacy, far beyond that of ordinary narrative.

    结果写出来的书情节紧张，矛盾冲突强烈,令人欲罢不能,效果与使用普通的叙事手法大为不同。
