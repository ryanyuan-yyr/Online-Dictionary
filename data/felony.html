felony
feləni ˈfɛləni
Word forms: plural felonies
N-COUNT 重罪 In countries where the legal system distinguishes between very serious crimes and less serious ones, a felony is a very serious crime such as armed robbery. 重罪 [law]

  • He pleaded guilty to six felonies.

    他承认犯有 6 项重罪。

SYN crime, offence, misdemeanour, transgression
