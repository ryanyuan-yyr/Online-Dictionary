clove
kloʊv kloʊv
Word forms: plural cloves
1 N-VAR 丁香（用作香料） Cloves are small dried flower buds which are used as a spice. 丁香（用作香料）

  • ...chicken soup with cloves.

    丁香鸡汤

2 N-COUNT (大蒜的)一瓣 A clove of garlic is one of the sections of a garlic bulb. (大蒜的)一瓣
3 Clove is a past tense of cleave.
