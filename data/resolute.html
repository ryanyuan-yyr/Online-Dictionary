resolute
rezəluːt ˈrɛzəˌlut
ADJ-GRADED 坚决的;坚定的;果断的 If you describe someone as resolute, you approve of them because they are very determined not to change their mind or not to give up a course of action. 坚决的;坚定的;果断的 [formal]

  • Voters perceive him as a decisive and resolute international leader.

    投票者认为他是一位坚定果断的国际领导人。

  • He described the situation as very dangerous and called for resolute action.

    他将形势描述得非常危险，并号召采取果断行动。

SYN determined, set, firm, dogged  
resolutely

  • He resolutely refused to speak English unless forced to.

    除非迫不得已，否则他坚决不说英语。

  • The United States remains resolutely opposed to this.

    美国对此仍然坚决反对。
