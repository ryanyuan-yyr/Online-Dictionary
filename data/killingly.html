killing
kɪlɪŋ ˈkɪlɪŋ
Word forms: plural killings
1 N-COUNT 杀害;谋杀 A killing is an act of deliberately killing a person. 杀害;谋杀

  • This is a brutal killing.

    这是残忍的谋杀。

SYN murder, massacre, slaughter, execution  
2 PHRASE 发大财；获取暴利 If you make a killing, you make a large profit very quickly and easily. 发大财；获取暴利 [informal]

  • They have made a killing on the deal. [+ on]

    他们在这笔交易上赚了大钱。

SYN profit, gain, clean up [informal] , be lucky
