puffed up
pʌft ʌp
1 ADJ-GRADED 自满的；自负的 If you describe someone as puffed up, you disapprove of them because they are very proud of themselves and think that they are very important. 自满的；自负的 [disapproval]

  • He was too puffed up with his own importance, too blinded by vanity to accept their verdict on him. [+ with]

    他过于骄矜自大，被虚荣心冲昏了头脑，根本不能接受他们对他的意见。

SYN swollen-headed, proud, high and mighty [informal] , bigheaded [informal]
