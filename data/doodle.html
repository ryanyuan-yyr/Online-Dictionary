doodle
duːdəl ˈduːdəl ˈdudəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense doodles, present participle doodling, past tense , past participle doodled
1 N-COUNT (无聊或心不在焉时)涂鸦，胡写乱画 A doodle is a pattern or picture that you draw when you are bored or thinking about something else. (无聊或心不在焉时)涂鸦，胡写乱画
2 VERB 乱涂乱画;涂鸦 When someone doodles, they draw doodles. 乱涂乱画;涂鸦 [Also VERB n]

  • He looked across at Jackson, doodling on his notebook. [VERB]

    他一边朝杰克逊看过去，一边在自己的笔记本上乱涂乱画。
