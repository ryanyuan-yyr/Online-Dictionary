muso
mjuːzoʊ ˈmjuːzəʊ
Word forms: plural musos
1 N-COUNT 音乐家;乐师 A muso is a musician. 音乐家;乐师 [informal]

  • ...country muso Shania Twain.

    乡村流行音乐歌手仙妮亚·唐恩

2 ADJ 同 musical Muso means the same as musical . 同 musical [informal]

  • Bruce Springsteen has re-hired his muso collective, the E Street Band.

    布鲁斯·斯普林斯汀重新组建了他的音乐团队，E街乐队。
