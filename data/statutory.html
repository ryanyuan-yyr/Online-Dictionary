statutory
stætʃʊtəri -tɔːri ˈstætʃuˌtɔri
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 法令的;法规的 Statutory means relating to rules or laws which have been formally written down. 法令的;法规的 [formal]

  • We had a statutory duty to report to Parliament.

    我们有向议会报告的法定义务。

statutorily

  • Such tenants are statutorily protected.

    这样的租户是受到法律保护的。

  • Broadcasting has had to be regulated statutorily.

    广播业已经必须进行法律监管了。
