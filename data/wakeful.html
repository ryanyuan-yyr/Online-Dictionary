wakeful
weɪkfʊl ˈweɪkfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 难以入睡的;失眠的;睡眠轻的 Someone who is wakeful finds it difficult to get to sleep and wakes up very often when they should be sleeping. 难以入睡的;失眠的;睡眠轻的

  • Wakeful babies will often continue to need little sleep as they grow older.

    通常睡眠轻的婴儿长大后仍然只需要较少的睡眠。

SYN sleepless, restless, insomniac, unsleeping  
wakefulness

  • It is never a good idea to take sleeping tablets regularly for this kind of wakefulness.

    经常吃安眠药来对付这种失眠绝不是什么好办法。
