fin de siècle fæn də sieklə fɛ̃ də sjɛklə fɛ̃t ˈsjɛklə
(also fin-de-siècle)
N-COUNT [ADJ n] 十九世纪末式的Fin de siècle is used to describe something that is thought to be typical of the end of the nineteenth century, especially when it is considered stylish or exaggerated. 十九世纪末式的 [written]

  • ...fin de siècle decadence.

    ...典型的十九世纪末式颓废
