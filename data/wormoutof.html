worm out of
PHRASAL VERB 套问出;盘问出 If you worm information out of someone, you gradually find it out by constantly asking them about it. 套问出;盘问出

  • It took me weeks to worm the facts out of him. [VERB noun PREP. PREP. noun]

    我花了几个星期才从他那儿套出事情的真相。

  • It didn't take long before she'd wormed out of him confessions of his other infidelity. [V P P n n (not pron)]

    不久，在她的盘问之下，他承认还有其他婚外情。
