quicksand
kwɪksænd ˈkwɪkˌsænd
Word forms:
1 N-UNCOUNT 流沙 Quicksand is deep, wet sand that you sink into if you try to walk on it. 流沙

  • The sandbank was uncertain, like quicksand under his feet.

    那个沙坝并不稳固，他脚下如同踩着流沙一般。

2 N-UNCOUNT 难以摆脱的困境；不可靠的局面 You can refer to a situation as quicksand when you want to suggest that it is dangerous or difficult to escape from, or does not provide a strong basis for what you are doing. 难以摆脱的困境；不可靠的局面

  • I was about to sink into the quicksand of sin.

    我就要陷入罪孽的深渊了。

  • The research seemed founded on quicksand.

    这项研究犹如建在流沙之上，基础极不稳固。
