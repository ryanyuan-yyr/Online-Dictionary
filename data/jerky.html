jerky
dʒɜːʳki ˈdʒɜrki
Word forms: comparative jerkier, superlative jerkiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 突然的；急促的；颠簸的；不平稳的 Jerky movements are very sudden and quick, and do not flow smoothly. 突然的；急促的；颠簸的；不平稳的

  • Mr Griffin made a jerky gesture.

    格里芬先生急匆匆地做了个手势。

SYN bumpy, rough, jolting, jumpy  
jerkily

  • Using his stick heavily, he moved jerkily towards the car.

    他费力地拄着拐杖，一瘸一拐地着向汽车走去。

jerkiness

  • Avoid jerkiness by breathing easily throughout the exercise.

    练习过程中注意放松呼吸，以免动作不顺畅。
