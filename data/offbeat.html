offbeat
ɒfbiːt ɔːf- ˈɔfˌbit
(also off-beat)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不寻常的；非常规的；非正统的 If you describe something or someone as offbeat, you think that they are different from normal. 不寻常的；非常规的；非正统的

  • She adores old, offbeat antiques.

    她非常喜欢那些稀奇古怪的老古董。

  • ...a wickedly offbeat imagination.

    绝妙另类的想象力

SYN unusual, odd, strange, novel
