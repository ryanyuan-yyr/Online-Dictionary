rental
rentəl ˈrɛntəl
Word forms: plural rentals
1 N-UNCOUNT 租用;出租;租赁 The rental of something such as a car or piece of equipment is the activity or process of renting it. 租用;出租;租赁

  • We can organise car rental from Chicago O'Hare Airport.

    我们可以安排提供从芝加哥奥黑尔机场出发的租车服务。

  • ...Scotland's largest video rental company.

    苏格兰最大的音像制品租赁公司

2 N-COUNT 租金;租用费 The rental is the amount of money that you pay when you rent something such as a car, property, or piece of equipment. 租金;租用费

  • It has been let at an annual rental of 393,000.

    它以每年39.3万英镑的价格被租了出去。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 租用的;出租的 You use rental to describe things that are connected with the renting out of goods, properties, and services. 租用的;出租的

  • A friend drove her to Oxford, where she picked up a rental car.

    一个朋友开车把她送到牛津，在那里她租了一辆车。

  • Repairs and improvements can lead to higher rental rates.

    修缮后可以提高租价。
