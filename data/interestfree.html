interest-free
ˌɪntrəstˈfriː
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 免息的;无息的 An interest-free loan has no interest charged on it. 免息的;无息的

  • He was offered a £10,000 interest-free loan.

    他获得了 1 万英镑的无息贷款。

  • Many stores are offering interest-free credit.

    很多商店提供免息赊购。

Interest-free is also an adverb.

  • Customers allowed the banks to use their money interest-free.

    顾客允许银行无息使用他们的钱。
