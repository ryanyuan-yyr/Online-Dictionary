baron
bærən ˈbærən ˈbɛrən
Word forms: plural barons
1 N-COUNT; N-TITLE 男爵（贵族爵位中最低的一级） A baron is a man who is a member of the lowest rank of the nobility. 男爵（贵族爵位中最低的一级） [British]

  • ...their stepfather, Baron Michael Distemple.

    他们的继父，迈克尔·迪斯腾普尔男爵

2 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 巨头；大王；大亨 You can use baron to refer to someone who controls a large amount of a particular industry or activity and who is therefore extremely powerful. 巨头；大王；大亨

  • ...the battle against the drug barons.

    打击毒枭之战

  • ...the British press barons.

    英国新闻业巨子

SYN tycoon, leader, chief, fat cat [slang , mainly US]
