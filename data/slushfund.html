slush fund
Word forms: plural slush funds
N-COUNT (尤指用于非法政治或商业活动的)贿赂基金，行贿基金 A slush fund is a sum of money collected to pay for an illegal activity, especially in politics or business. (尤指用于非法政治或商业活动的)贿赂基金，行贿基金
