majesty
mædʒɪsti ˈmædʒɪsti
Word forms: plural majesties
1 N-VOC 陛下(对国王或王后的尊称) You use majesty in expressions such as Your Majesty or Her Majesty when you are addressing or referring to a King or Queen. 陛下(对国王或王后的尊称) [politeness]

  • I quite agree, Your Majesty.

    我十分赞成，陛下。

  • His Majesty requests your presence in the royal chambers.

    国王陛下要在皇宫召见你。

  • Their Majesties celebrated our arrival by giving us each a little silver spoon.

    国王和王后陛下赐给我们每人一只小银匙，以欢迎我们的到来。

2 N-UNCOUNT 壮观;庄严;雄伟 Majesty is the quality of being beautiful, dignified, and impressive. 壮观;庄严;雄伟

  • ...the majesty of the mainland mountains.

    大陆山脉的壮观

SYN grandeur, glory, splendour, magnificence
