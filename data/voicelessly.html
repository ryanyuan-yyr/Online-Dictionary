voiceless
vɔɪsləs ˈvɔɪslɪs
1 ADJ （发）清音的 A voiceless speech sound is one that is produced without vibration of the vocal cords. （发）清音的 [technical]

  • ...the voiceless 'th'.

    发清音的th

2 ADJ 失语的;不能说话的 Someone who is voiceless is unable to speak. 失语的;不能说话的 [written]

  • His voiceless lips formed the words 'Thank you'.

    他发不出声音来，翕动着嘴唇说了“谢谢你”这几个字。
