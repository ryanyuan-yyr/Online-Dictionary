rump
rʌmp rʌmp
Word forms: plural rumps
1 N-SING 残余分子;残存力量 The rump of a group, organization, or country consists of the members who remain in it after the rest have left. 残余分子;残存力量 [mainly British]

  • The rump of the party does in fact still have considerable assets. [+ of]

    事实上，这个党派的残余分子确实仍然拥有可观的资产。

  • He finds himself heading a rump party largely out of tune with his own views.

    他发现自己领导的这个残存的党派和自己的观点大多不符。

SYN remains, rest, those left, remaining part  
2 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] （动物的）尾部，臀部 An animal's rump is its rear end. （动物的）尾部，臀部

  • The cows' rumps were marked with a number.

    奶牛的臀部标着一个数字。

3 N-UNCOUNT 臀肉牛排 Rump or rump steak is meat cut from the rear end of a cow. 臀肉牛排
4 N-COUNT [usually singular] （人的）屁股，臀部 A person's rump is his or her bottom. （人的）屁股，臀部

  • ...jeans stretching across her rump.

    紧裹她臀部的仔裤

SYN buttocks, bottom [informal] , rear [informal] , backside [informal]
