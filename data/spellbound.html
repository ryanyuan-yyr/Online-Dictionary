spellbound
spelbaʊnd ˈspɛlˌbaʊnd
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] （对…）入迷的;（被…）吸引的 If you are spellbound by something or someone, you are so fascinated that you cannot think about anything else. （对…）入迷的;（被…）吸引的

  • His audience had listened like children, spellbound by his words. [+ by]

    他的观众像孩子般倾听着，被他的话深深吸引了。

  • He was in awe of her; she held him spellbound.

    他对她十分敬畏;她令他着迷。

SYN entranced, gripped, fascinated, transported
