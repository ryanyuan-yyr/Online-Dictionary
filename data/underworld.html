underworld
ʌndəʳwɜːʳld ˈʌndərˌwɜrld
1 N-SING [oft NOUN n, n NOUN] 黑帮；黑社会 The underworld in a city is the organized crime there and the people who are involved in it. 黑帮；黑社会

  • ...a Spanish Harlem underworld of gangs, drugs and violence.

    充斥着犯罪团伙、毒品和暴力的西班牙哈莱姆区黑社会

  • Some claim that she still has connections to the criminal underworld.

    有人称她仍与黑社会犯罪势力有来往。

  • ...a wealthy businessman with underworld connections.

    一位与黑社会有联系的富商

SYN criminals, gangsters, organized crime, gangland [informal]  
2 N-SING 地狱；阴间；冥府 In many ancient religions and legends, the underworld is a place under the earth's surface where people go after they die. 地狱；阴间；冥府

  • ...Persephone, goddess of the underworld.

    冥后珀耳塞福涅

SYN nether world, hell, Hades, the inferno
