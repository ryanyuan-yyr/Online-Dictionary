orifice
ɒrɪfɪs ɔːr- ˈɔrəfɪs ˈɑrəfɪs
Word forms: plural orifices
N-COUNT （尤指身体上的）口，孔，洞 An orifice is an opening or hole, especially one in your body such as your mouth. （尤指身体上的）口，孔，洞 [formal]

  • After a massive heart attack, he was strapped to a bed, with tubes in every orifice.

    经历了一次严重的心脏病发作之后，他被绑在床上，全身都插着导管。

SYN opening, space, hole, split
