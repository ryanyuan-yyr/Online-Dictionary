residential
rezɪdenʃəl ˌrɛzəˈdɛnʃəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 居住的;住宅区的;居民区的 A residential area contains houses rather than offices or factories. 居住的;住宅区的;居民区的

  • ...a smart residential area.

    整洁漂亮的住宅区

  • Fontbonne is a liberal arts college, located in a residential suburb of St. Louis.

    芳邦大学是一所文科大学，位于圣路易斯市郊的居民区。

SYN suburban, commuter, dormitory  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （机构）寄宿制的，需住宿的 A residential institution is one where people live while they are studying there or being cared for there. （机构）寄宿制的，需住宿的

  • Training involves a two-year residential course.

    培训包含一门为期两年的住校学习课程。

  • ...a residential home for children with disabilities.

    残疾儿童福利院
