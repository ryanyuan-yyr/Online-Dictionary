circumstantial
sɜːʳkəmstænʃəl ˌsɜrkəmˈstænʃəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (证据)间接的;佐证的 Circumstantial evidence is evidence that makes it seem likely that something happened, but does not prove it. (证据)间接的;佐证的 [formal]

  • Fast work by the police in Birmingham had started producing circumstantial evidence.

    快速开展工作的伯明翰警方已经开始发现间接证据。

2 ADJ 视情况而定的;与特定情况有关的 Something that is circumstantial is related to a particular circumstance. 视情况而定的;与特定情况有关的 [formal]

  • The reasons for the project collapsing were circumstantial.

    工程垮掉系环境所迫。

SYN detailed, particular, specific
