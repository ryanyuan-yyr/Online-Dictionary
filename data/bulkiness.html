bulky
bʌlki ˈbʌlki
Word forms: comparative bulkier, superlative bulkiest
ADJ-GRADED 庞大的；笨重的 Something that is bulky is large and heavy. Bulky things are often difficult to move or deal with. 庞大的；笨重的

  • ...bulky items like lawn mowers.

    割草机之类的庞大物件

  • ...a bulky man with balding hair.

    一个秃顶的大块头男人

SYN large, big, huge, heavy
