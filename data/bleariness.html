bleary
blɪəri ˈblɪri
ADJ （眼睛）模糊的，蒙眬的，惺忪的 If your eyes are bleary, they look dull or tired, as if you have not had enough sleep or have drunk too much alcohol. （眼睛）模糊的，蒙眬的，惺忪的

  • I arrived bleary-eyed and rumpled.

    我赶到时睡眼惺忪，头发蓬乱。

  • He stared at Leo with great bleary eyes.

    他用一双模糊的大眼睛盯着利奥。

SYN dim, blurred, fogged, murky
