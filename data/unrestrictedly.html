unrestricted
ʌnrɪstrɪktɪd ˌʌnrɪˈstrɪktɪd
1 ADJ 不受拘束的；没有限制的 If an activity is unrestricted, you are free to do it in the way that you want, without being limited by any rules. 不受拘束的；没有限制的

  • Freedom to pursue extracurricular activities is totally unrestricted.

    参加课外活动的自由完全不受限制。

  • The Commissioner has absolutely unrestricted access to all the files.

    特派员可以随意查阅所有文件。

SYN unlimited, open, free, absolute  
2 ADJ 完整的；清晰的 If you have an unrestricted view of something, you can see it fully and clearly, because there is nothing in the way. 完整的；清晰的

  • Nearly all seats have an unrestricted view.

    几乎在所有的座位上都可以看到完整清晰的画面。

SYN clear, open, unobstructed
