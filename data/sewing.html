sewing
soʊɪŋ ˈsoʊɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 缝纫;缝补 Sewing is the activity of making or mending clothes or other things using a needle and thread. 缝纫;缝补

  • Her mother had always done all the sewing.

    所有的针线活一直都是她母亲来做。

SYN needlework, stitching, needlecraft  
2 N-UNCOUNT 缝纫的衣物;针线活 Sewing is clothes or other things that are being sewn. 缝纫的衣物;针线活

  • We all got out our own sewing and sat in front of the log fire.

    我们都把自己的针线活拿出来，坐到了火堆前。
