boiling
bɔɪlɪŋ
1 ADJ 炎热的；酷热的 Something that is boiling or boiling hot is very hot. 炎热的；酷热的

  • 'It's boiling in here,' complained Miriam.

    “这里热死了，”米里亚姆抱怨道。

  • Often the food may be bubbling and boiling hot on the top, but the inside may still be cold.

    食物常常是表面滚烫，而里面可能还是凉的。

SYN very hot, hot, burning, baking  
2 ADJ 感觉极热的；热得难受的 If you say that you are boiling or boiling hot, you mean that you feel very hot, usually unpleasantly hot. →see usage note at: hot 感觉极热的；热得难受的

  • When everybody else is boiling hot, I'm freezing!

    当其他所有人都酷热难耐时，我却冻得够呛！
