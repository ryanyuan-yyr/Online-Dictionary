unloved
ʌnlʌvd ʌnˈlʌvd
ADJ-GRADED 未被爱的；缺少爱的 If someone feels unloved, they feel that nobody loves them. 未被爱的；缺少爱的

  • I think she feels desperately wounded and unloved at the moment.

    我想她此刻定会觉得受到了极大的伤害，无人关爱。

  • ...a lonely, unloved child.

    孤独的、没人疼爱的孩子

SYN uncared-for, neglected, unwanted, rejected
