occurrence
əkʌrəns -kɜːr- əˈkɜrəns
Word forms: plural occurrences
1 N-COUNT 发生的事情；事件 An occurrence is something that happens. 发生的事情；事件 [formal]

  • Complaints seemed to be an everyday occurrence.

    似乎每天都有投诉。

  • The food queues have become a daily occurrence across the country.

    排队购买食品的长龙已成了全国各地每日可见的景象。

2 N-COUNT 发生；出现 The occurrence of something is the fact that it happens or is present. 发生；出现

  • The greatest occurrence of coronary heart disease is in those over 65. [+ of]

    65岁以上者是冠心病的最高发人群。

SYN existence, instance, appearance, manifestation
