insane
ɪnseɪn ɪnˈseɪn
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 精神错乱的;精神失常的;疯狂的 Someone who is insane has a mind that does not work in a normal way, with the result that their behaviour is very strange. 精神错乱的;精神失常的;疯狂的

  • Some people simply can't take it and they just go insane.

    一些人完全承受不了，结果就精神失常了。

  • Agnes was a battered woman who had killed, in self-defense, while temporarily insane.

    阿格尼丝是个备受虐待的女人,她在一时精神错乱下出于自我防卫杀了人。

SYN mad, crazy, nuts [slang] , cracked [slang]  
The insane are people who are insane.精神病人;精神失常的人

  • ...the state hospital for the criminally insane.

    专门收治精神病犯人的州立医院

2 ADJ-GRADED 蠢极的;荒唐的 If you describe a decision or action as insane, you think it is very foolish or excessive. 蠢极的;荒唐的 [disapproval]

  • I said, 'Listen, this is completely insane.'

    我说：“听着,这简直荒唐之极。”

  • ...insane jealousy.

    不可理喻的嫉妒心

SYN stupid, foolish, daft [informal] , bizarre  
insanely

  • I would be insanely jealous if Bill left me for another woman.

    如果比尔为了别的女人离开我,我会妒忌得发疯的。
