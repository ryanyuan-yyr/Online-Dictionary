whizz-kid
(also whizzkid,whizz kid)Word forms: plural whizz-kids
N-COUNT （尤指在金融方面的）神童，奇才 If you refer to a young person as a whizz-kid, you mean that they have achieved success at a young age because they are very clever and very good at something, especially making money. （尤指在金融方面的）神童，奇才 [informal]

  • ...a financial whizz kid.

    金融神童

  • ...a whizz-kid physics student.

    物理神童
