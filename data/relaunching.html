relaunch
riːlɔːntʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense relaunches, present participle relaunching, past tense , past participle relaunched
VERB 重新开办（公司）;重新推出(产品)；重新实施（计划） To relaunch something such as a company, a product, or a scheme means to start it again or to produce it in a different way. 重新开办（公司）;重新推出(产品)；重新实施（计划）

  • He is hoping to relaunch his film career. [VERB noun]

    他希望重振他的电影事业。

Relaunch is also a noun.

  • Football kit relaunches are simply a way of boosting sales.

    成套足球用品的重新推出仅仅是一种促销的手段。
