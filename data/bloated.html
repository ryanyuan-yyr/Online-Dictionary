bloated
bloʊtɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED （身体或身体某部位）肿胀的，膨胀的，发胀的 If someone's body or a part of their body is bloated, it is much larger than normal, usually because it has a lot of liquid or gas inside it. （身体或身体某部位）肿胀的，膨胀的，发胀的

  • ...the bloated body of a dead bullock.

    一头死的小阉牛肿胀的尸体

  • His face was bloated.

    他脸部浮肿。

SYN puffed up, swollen, blown-up, enlarged  
2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 吃撑了的；肚子胀的 If you feel bloated after eating a large meal, you feel very full and uncomfortable. 吃撑了的；肚子胀的

  • Diners do not want to leave the table feeling bloated.

    食客们不想吃到肚子胀鼓鼓的才离开饭桌。

SYN too full, stuffed [informal] , blown up, swollen up  
3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （机构）臃肿的，人浮于事的 If you describe an organization as bloated, you mean that it is larger and less efficient than it should be. （机构）臃肿的，人浮于事的

  • ...its massive state apparatus and bloated bureaucracy.

    它那庞大的国家机关和臃肿的官僚机构
