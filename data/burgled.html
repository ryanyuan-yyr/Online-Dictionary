burgle
bɜːʳgəl ˈbɜrgəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense burgles, present participle burgling, past tense , past participle burgled
VERB 破门盗窃；入室盗窃 If a building is burgled, a thief enters it by force and steals things. 破门盗窃；入室盗窃 [British]

  • I found that my flat had been burgled. [be VERB-ed]

    我发现我的公寓被盗了。

  • I thought we had been burgled. [be VERB-ed]

    我想我们被盗了。

  • Two teenagers burgled the home of Mr Jones's mother. [VERB noun]

    两个少年闯进了琼斯先生的母亲家里行窃。

SYN rob, raid, loot, steal from
