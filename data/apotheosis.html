apotheosis
əpɒθioʊsɪs əˌpɑθiˈoʊsɪs ˌ æpəˈθiəsɪs
1 N-SING 典型;典范;榜样 If something is the apotheosis of something else, it is an ideal or typical example of it. 典型;典范;榜样 [formal]

  • The Oriental in Bangkok is the apotheosis of the grand hotel. [+ of]

    曼谷的东方饭店是豪华饭店的典范。

2 N-SING [with poss] （事业或人生的）巅峰，顶峰 If you describe an event or a time as someone's apotheosis, you mean that it was the high point in their career or their life. （事业或人生的）巅峰，顶峰 [formal]

  • That night was Richard's apotheosis.

    是夜，理查德登上了人生的顶峰。
