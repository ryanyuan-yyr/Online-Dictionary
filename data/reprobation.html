reprobate
reprəbeɪt ˈrɛprəʊˌbeɪt ˈrɛprəˌbeɪt ˈrɛprəbɪt
Word forms: plural reprobates
N-COUNT 堕落者;放荡者;行为不检的人 If you describe someone as a reprobate, you mean that they behave in a way that is not respectable or morally correct. 堕落者;放荡者;行为不检的人 [old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • ...a drunken reprobate.

    一个行为不检的醉鬼

SYN scoundrel [old-fashioned] , villain, degenerate, profligate
