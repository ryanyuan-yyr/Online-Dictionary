engraving
ɪngreɪvɪŋ ɛnˈgreɪvɪŋ ɪnˈgreɪvɪŋ
Word forms: plural engravings
1 N-COUNT 雕版；图版 An engraving is a picture or design that has been cut into a surface. 雕版；图版
2 N-COUNT 版画 An engraving is a picture that has been printed from a plate on which designs have been cut. 版画

  • ...a color engraving of oranges and lemons.

    橙子和柠檬的彩色版画
