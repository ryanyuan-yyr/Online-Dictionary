pilfer
pɪlfəʳ ˈpɪlfər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pilfers, present participle pilfering, past tense , past participle pilfered
VERB 小偷小摸；偷窃（不太贵重的东西） If someone pilfers, they steal things, usually small cheap things. 小偷小摸；偷窃（不太贵重的东西）

  • Staff were pilfering behind the bar. [VERB]

    店员在柜台后有些小偷小摸的行为。

  • When food stores close, they go to work, pilfering food for resale on the black market. [VERB noun]

    食品店关门后，他们就行动起来，偷食品拿到黑市上出售。

SYN steal, take, rob, lift [informal]  
pilfering

  • Precautions had to be taken to prevent pilfering.

    必须采取防范措施阻止偷盗行为。
