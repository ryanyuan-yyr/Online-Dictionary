reciprocity
resɪprɒsɪti ˌrɛsəˈprɑsəti
N-UNCOUNT 互惠；互换 Reciprocity is the exchange of something between people or groups of people when each person or group gives or allows something to the other. 互惠；互换 [formal]

  • They gave assurances they would press for reciprocity with Greece in the issuing of visas.

    他们保证将推进本国与希腊两国间签证的互相发放。
