slanderous
slɑːndərəs slæn- ˈslændərəs
ADJ-GRADED 诋毁性的;诽谤性的;中伤的 A spoken statement that is slanderous is untrue and intended to damage the reputation of the person that it refers to. 诋毁性的;诽谤性的;中伤的

  • Herr Kohler wanted an explanation for what he described as 'slanderous' remarks.

    赫尔·科勒希望有人能够为那些他称之为“造谣中伤”的言辞作出解释。

SYN defamatory, libellous, abusive, malicious
