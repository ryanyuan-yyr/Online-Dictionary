fish and chip shop fɪʃ ən tʃɪp ʃɒp
Word forms: plural fish and chip shops
N-COUNT 炸鱼薯条店 In Britain, a fish and chip shop is a shop which sells hot food such as fish and chips, fried chicken, sausages, and meat pies. The food is cooked in the shop and people take it away to eat at home or in the street. 炸鱼薯条店
