additional
ədɪʃənəl əˈdɪʃənəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 额外的;附加的;添加的 Additional things are extra things apart from the ones already present. 额外的;附加的;添加的

  • The US is sending additional troops to the region.

    美国正向该地区增兵。

  • The insurer will also have to pay the additional costs of the trial.

    保险公司将不得不支付审判的额外费用。

SYN extra, more, new, other
