self-proclaimed
ˈsɛlfproʊˈkleɪmd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 自称的;自命的;自封的 Self-proclaimed is used to show that someone has given themselves a particular title or status rather than being given it by other people. 自称的;自命的;自封的

  • ...a self-proclaimed expert.

    自封的专家

  • He is President of his own self-proclaimed republic.

    他是其自己宣布成立的共和国的总统。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 不吝承认的;无耻自称的 Self-proclaimed is used to show that someone says themselves that they are a type of person which most people would be embarrassed or ashamed to be. 不吝承认的;无耻自称的

  • One of the prisoners is a self-proclaimed racist who opened fire on a crowd four years ago.

    其中一名囚犯恬不知耻地自称是种族主义者，他曾在四年前向一群人开枪射击。

  • ...a self-proclaimed Anglophile.

    不吝承认的崇英者
