indigenous
ɪndɪdʒɪnəs ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs
ADJ 当地的;本土的;土生土长的 Indigenous people or things belong to the country in which they are found, rather than coming there or being brought there from another country. 当地的;本土的;土生土长的 [formal]

  • ...the country's indigenous population.

    该国的本土人口

  • ...animals that are indigenous to Asia.

    原产于亚洲的动物

SYN native, original, aboriginal, home-grown
