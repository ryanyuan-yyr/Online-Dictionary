fascination
fæsɪneɪʃən ˌfæsəˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural fascinations
1 N-UNCOUNT 入迷;着迷;酷爱 Fascination is the state of being greatly interested in or delighted by something. 入迷;着迷;酷爱

  • I've had a lifelong fascination with the sea and with small boats. [+ with/of/for]

    我一生都对大海和小船着迷。

SYN obsession, interest, complex [informal] , enthusiasm  
2 N-COUNT 魅力;令人着迷的事物 A fascination is something that fascinates people. 魅力;令人着迷的事物

  • ...a series focusing on the fascinations of the British Museum.

    主要讲述大英博物馆魅力的系列节目
