douse
daʊs daʊz daʊs
(also dowse)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense douses, present participle dousing, past tense , past participle doused
1 VERB 浇灭(火) If you douse a fire, you stop it burning by pouring a lot of water over it. 浇灭(火)

  • The pumps were started and the crew began to douse the fire with water. [VERB noun]

    水泵开动了，消防队员们开始用水灭火。

SYN put out, smother, blow out, extinguish  
2 VERB (把液体)浇（或洒、泼）在…上 If you douse someone or something with a liquid, you throw a lot of that liquid over them. (把液体)浇（或洒、泼）在…上

  • They hurled abuse at their victim as they doused him with petrol. [V n + with/in]

    他们一边大声辱骂这名受害者一边将汽油淋在他身上。

3 VERB 熄灭(灯) If you douse a light, you turn it off. 熄灭(灯)

  • I doused the headlights and got out. [VERB noun]

    我灭了车前灯后下了车。
