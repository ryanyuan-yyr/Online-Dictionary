filling
fɪlɪŋ ˈfɪlɪŋ
Word forms: plural fillings
1 N-COUNT (补牙用的)填料 A filling is a small amount of metal or plastic that a dentist puts in a hole in a tooth to prevent further decay. (补牙用的)填料

  • The longer your child can go without needing a filling, the better.

    孩子越晚需要补牙就越好。

2 N-MASS (糕点的)馅 The filling in something such as a cake, pie, or sandwich is a substance or mixture that is put inside it. (糕点的)馅

  • Spread some of the filling over each pancake.

    在每个薄煎饼上摊上一些馅。

3 N-MASS (软体家具或垫子的)芯，填充物 The filling in a piece of soft furniture or in a cushion is the soft substance inside it. (软体家具或垫子的)芯，填充物

  • ...second-hand sofas with old-style foam fillings.

    老式泡沫芯子的二手沙发

4 ADJ-GRADED (食物)令人感到饱足的 Food that is filling makes you feel full when you have eaten it. (食物)令人感到饱足的

  • Although it is tasty, crab is very filling.

    螃蟹虽然好吃，却容易让人感到饱足。

SYN satisfying, heavy, square, substantial
