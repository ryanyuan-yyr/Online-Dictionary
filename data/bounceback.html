bounce back
PHRASAL VERB 恢复；复原；重整旗鼓 If you bounce back after a bad experience, you return very quickly to your previous level of success, enthusiasm, or activity. 恢复；复原；重整旗鼓

  • We lost two or three early games in the World Cup, but we bounced back. [VERB PREP.]

    在世界杯比赛开始时我们有两三场比赛失利，但后来我们又恢复了状态。

  • He is young enough to bounce back from this disappointment. [VERB PREP. prep./adv.]

    他还年轻，会很快从失望沮丧中恢复过来的。

SYN recover, pick up, rally, take heart
