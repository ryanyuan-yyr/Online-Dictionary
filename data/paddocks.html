paddock
pædək ˈpædək
Word forms: plural paddocks
1 N-COUNT （养马的）小围场 A paddock is a small field where horses are kept. （养马的）小围场

  • The family kept horses in the paddock in front of the house.

    那户人家把马养在屋前的小围场里。

SYN field, meadow, pasture, pen  
2 N-COUNT （赛马的）集中场；（赛车的）装备区 In horse racing or motor racing, the paddock is the place where the horses or cars are kept just before each race. （赛马的）集中场；（赛车的）装备区
