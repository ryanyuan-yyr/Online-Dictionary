deprecating
deprɪkeɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED （尤指对与自身相关的事物）反对的，不以为然的，表示不赞同的 A deprecating attitude, gesture, or remark shows that you think that something is not very good, especially something associated with yourself. （尤指对与自身相关的事物）反对的，不以为然的，表示不赞同的 [written]

  • Erica made a little deprecating shrug.

    埃丽卡有点不以为然地耸耸肩。

deprecatingly

  • He speaks deprecatingly of his father as a lonely man.

    他语带机锋，说父亲是一个孤独的人。
