palace
pælɪs ˈpæləs
Word forms: plural palaces
1 N-COUNT [oft in names, NOUN n] 宫殿；王宫；皇宫 A palace is a very large impressive house, especially one which is the official home of a king, queen, or president. 宫殿；王宫；皇宫

  • ...Buckingham Palace.

    白金汉宫

  • They entered the palace courtyard.

    他们进入了宫殿庭院。

2 N-SING 王室 When the members of a royal palace make an announcement through an official spokesperson, they can be referred to as the Palace . 王室

  • The Palace will not comment on questions about the family's private life.

    王室不会就有关皇家私生活的问题发表任何评论。

3 N-COUNT 豪宅；宫殿般的建筑 You can refer to any large splendid house or other building as a palace . 豪宅；宫殿般的建筑

  • They'd bought a huge barn with some land and planned to turn it into a palace.

    他们买了一个带有一片土地的大谷仓，计划将其改建为一座豪宅。
