gallic ˈɡælɪk ˈgælɪk
ADJ-GRADED 高卢的；高卢人的；法国式的；法国人特有的 Gallic means the same as French. You sometimes use Gallic to describe ideas, feelings, or actions that you think are very typical of France and French people. 高卢的；高卢人的；法国式的；法国人特有的
