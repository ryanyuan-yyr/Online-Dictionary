agile
ædʒaɪl -dʒəl ˈædʒəl ˈædʒˌaɪl
1 ADJ-GRADED 敏捷的；灵敏的 Someone who is agile can move quickly and easily. 敏捷的；灵敏的

  • At 20 years old he was not as agile as he is now.

    20岁时他并不如现在这般矫健。

SYN nimble, active, quick, lively  
agility

  • She blinked in surprise at his agility.

    他敏捷的身手让她惊奇地直眨眼睛。

2 ADJ-GRADED 机敏的;机智的 If you have an agile mind, you think quickly and intelligently. 机敏的;机智的

  • She was quick-witted and had an extraordinarily agile mind.

    她机智聪慧，思维极为敏捷。

SYN acute, sharp, quick, bright [informal]  
agility

  • His intellect and mental agility have never been in doubt.

    他的才智和机敏从未受到怀疑。
