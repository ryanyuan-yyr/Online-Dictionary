encircle
ɪnsɜːʳkəl ɛnˈsɜrkəl ɪnˈsɜrkəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense encircles, present participle encircling, past tense , past participle encircled
VERB 包围;环绕 To encircle something or someone means to surround or enclose them, or to go round them. 包围;环绕

  • A forty-foot-high concrete wall encircles the jail. [VERB noun]

    监狱周围有一圈40英尺高的混凝土墙。

  • By 22nd November the Sixth Army was encircled. [VERB noun]

    到11月22日，第六军被包围了。

SYN surround, ring, circle, enclose
