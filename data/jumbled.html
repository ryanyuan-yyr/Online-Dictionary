jumbled dʒʌmbəld
ADJ-GRADED （东西）杂乱的；（想法）混乱的，纷乱的 If you describe things or ideas as jumbled, you mean that they are mixed up and not in order. （东西）杂乱的；（想法）混乱的，纷乱的

  • These jumbled priorities should be no cause for surprise.

    这些乱七八糟的需优先考虑的事项没有什么可惊讶的。

  • The Judaeo-Christian tradition is diverse, jumbled, contradictory, at every point inviting inquiry and debate.

    犹太-基督教传统呈现多样化，混乱复杂，相互矛盾，在各个方面都会引发质疑和争论。
