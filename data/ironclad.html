ironclad
aɪəʳnklæd ˈaɪərnˌklæd
(also iron-clad)
ADJ-GRADED 绝对可靠的;天衣无缝的;万无一失的 If you describe a guarantee or plan as ironclad, you are emphasizing that it has been carefully put together, and that you think it is absolutely certain to work or be successful. 绝对可靠的;天衣无缝的;万无一失的 [emphasis]

  • ...ironclad guarantees of safe passage.

    安全通行的绝对保证

  • The case for testing now is ironclad.

    正在接受检验的这一论据牢不可破。
