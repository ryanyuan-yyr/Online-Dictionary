agitator
ædʒɪteɪtəʳ ˈædʒɪˌteɪtər
Word forms: plural agitators
N-COUNT (政治上的)鼓动者，煽动者 If you describe someone involved in politics as an agitator, you disapprove of them because of the trouble they cause in organizing campaigns and protests. (政治上的)鼓动者，煽动者 [disapproval]

  • ...a famous actress who was accused of being a political agitator.

    被指控为政治煽动者的著名女演员

SYN troublemaker, revolutionary, inciter, firebrand
