racer
reɪsəʳ ˈreɪsər
Word forms: plural racers
1 N-COUNT 参赛者；参赛队员；参赛动物 A racer is a person or animal that takes part in races. 参赛者；参赛队员；参赛动物

  • Tim Powell is a former champion powerboat racer.

    蒂姆·鲍威尔曾是汽艇比赛冠军。

SYN competitor, runner, contender, sprinter  
2 N-COUNT 比赛用的车辆;赛车;赛艇 A racer is a vehicle such as a car or bicycle that is designed to be used in races and therefore travels fast. 比赛用的车辆;赛车;赛艇
