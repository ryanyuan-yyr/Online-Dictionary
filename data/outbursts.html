outburst
aʊtbɜːʳst ˈaʊtˌbɜrst
Word forms: plural outbursts
1 N-COUNT （尤指愤怒等感情的）爆发，迸发 An outburst of an emotion, especially anger, is a sudden strong expression of that emotion. （尤指愤怒等感情的）爆发，迸发

  • ...an outburst of anger.

    勃然大怒

  • ...a spontaneous outburst of cheers and applause. [+ of]

    从心底爆发的欢呼和掌声

  • There has been another angry outburst against the new local tax introduced today. [+ against]

    于今天开始实施的新地方税再次遭到人们愤怒的抗议。

SYN fit, storm, attack, gush  
2 N-COUNT （暴力活动的）突发 An outburst of violent activity is a sudden period of this activity. （暴力活动的）突发

  • Five people were reported killed today in a fresh outburst of violence. [+ of]

    据报道在今天最新发生的暴乱中有5人丧生。

  • ...this first great outburst of nationalist student protest. [+ of]

    首次大规模爆发的民族主义学生抗议活动
