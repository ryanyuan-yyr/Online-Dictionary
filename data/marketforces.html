market forces
N-PLURAL 市场力量;市场调节作用 When politicians and economists talk about market forces, they mean the economic factors that affect the availability of goods and the demand for them, without any help or control by governments. 市场力量;市场调节作用 [business]

  • ...opening the economy to market forces and increasing the role of private enterprise.

    放开经济由市场调节，增强私有企业的作用

  • ...saying that the prices of most commodities should be determined by market forces.

    说绝大部分商品的价格应由市场力量决定
