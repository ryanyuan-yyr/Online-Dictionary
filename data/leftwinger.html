left-winger
Word forms: plural left-wingers
N-COUNT （团体或政党内的）左翼成员（或人士） A left-winger is a person whose political beliefs are close to socialism, or closer to them than most of the other people in the same group or party. （团体或政党内的）左翼成员（或人士）

  • We were accused of being militant left-wingers.

    我们被人指责是好战的左翼分子。

SYN socialist, communist, red [informal] , pinko [derogatory , mainly US]
