succinct
səksɪŋkt səkˈsɪŋkt
ADJ-GRADED 简洁的;简明的;简要的 Something that is succinct expresses facts or ideas clearly and in few words. 简洁的;简明的;简要的 [approval]

  • The book gives an admirably succinct account of the technology and its history.

    这本书极其简明扼要地介绍了这项技术及其发展史。

  • If you have something to say make sure that it is accurate, succinct and to the point.

    在阐述观点时一定要准确、简洁、中肯。

SYN brief, to the point, concise, compact  
succinctly

  • He succinctly summed up his manifesto as 'Work hard, train hard and play hard'.

    他把自己的宣言简练地总结为“努力干，努力练，努力玩”。
