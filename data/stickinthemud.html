stick-in-the-mud ˈstɪkənðəˌmʌd
Word forms: plural stick-in-the-muds
N-COUNT 顽固守旧者;墨守成规者 If you describe someone as a stick-in-the-mud, you disapprove of them because they do not like doing anything that is new or fun. 顽固守旧者;墨守成规者 [informal, disapproval]
SYN (old) fogey, conservative, reactionary, stick [informal]
