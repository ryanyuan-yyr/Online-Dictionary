slick
slɪk slɪk
Word forms: comparative slicker, superlative slickest, plural , 3rd person singular present tense slicks, present participle slicking, past tense , past participle slicked
1 ADJ-GRADED 技法娴熟的;灵巧的;巧妙的 A slick performance, production, or advertisement is skilful and impressive. 技法娴熟的;灵巧的;巧妙的

  • There's a big difference between an amateur video and a slick Hollywood production.

    业余人士拍摄的视频片段和制作精良的好莱坞影片之间存在着巨大的差距。

  • His style is slick and visually exciting.

    他的演技娴熟，赏心悦目。

SYN efficient, professional, smart, smooth  
slickly

  • The products had been slickly marketed.

    那些产品已经被巧妙地推向了市场。

slickness

  • These actors and directors brought a new sophistication and slickness to modern theatre.

    这些演员和导演为现代戏剧带来了新的高超技巧。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 利索的;流畅的;不费力的 A slick action is done quickly and smoothly, and without any obvious effort. 利索的;流畅的;不费力的

  • They were outplayed by the Colombians' slick passing and decisive finishing.

    哥伦比亚队凭借流畅的传球和终场前的制胜进球击败了他们。

  • ...a slick gear change.

    轻松流畅的换挡

SYN skilful, deft, adroit, dextrous  
3 ADJ-GRADED (人)油嘴滑舌的，巧舌如簧的 A slick person speaks easily in a way that is likely to convince people, but is not sincere. (人)油嘴滑舌的，巧舌如簧的 [disapproval]

  • Don't be fooled by slick politicians.

    别被那些巧舌如簧的政客蒙骗了。

  • ...a slick, suit-wearing detective.

    油嘴滑舌、西装笔挺的侦探

SYN glib, smooth, sophisticated, plausible  
slickness

  • He had the slickness but not the sharpness.

    他虽巧舌如簧，但机智不足。

4 N-COUNT 同 oil slick A slick is the same as an oil slick . 同 oil slick

  • Experts are trying to devise ways to clean up the huge slick.

    专家们正想方设法清除这片面积巨大的浮油。

5 VERB (用水或油)使(头发)顺溜光滑 If someone slicks their hair back, they make it flat, smooth, and shiny by putting oil or water on it. (用水或油)使(头发)顺溜光滑

  • She had slicked her hair back. [V with back/down]

    她把头发往后梳得光溜溜的。

  • He slicked down his few remaining wisps of gray hair. [V with back/down]

    他捋平了自己仅剩的几绺白发。

  • His hair was slicked carefully into waves. [be VERB-ed prep.]

    他的头发精心地梳成了波浪状的卷发。

SYN smooth, oil, grease, sleek
