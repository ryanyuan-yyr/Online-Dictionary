half-price
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ n, ADJ after verb] 半价的 If something is half-price, it costs only half what it usually costs. 半价的

  • Main courses are half price from 12.30pm to 2pm.

    主菜在中午12点30分到下午两点之间是半价。

  • Mind you, a half-price suit still cost $400.

    要知道，一套打对价的西服也要400美元。

  • We can get in half-price.

    我们可以半价入场。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usu at/for N] 按半价 If something is sold at or for half-price, it is sold for only half of what it usually costs. 按半价

  • By yesterday she was selling off stock at half price.

    到昨天为止她一直在以半价抛售股票。

  • They normally charge three hundred pounds but we got it for half price.

    他们通常要价300英镑，但我们按半价买到了。
