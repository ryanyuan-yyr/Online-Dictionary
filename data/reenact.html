re-enact
(also reenact)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense re-enacts, present participle re-enacting, past tense , past participle re-enacted
VERB 再次展现;再现;重现 If you re-enact a scene or incident, you repeat the actions that occurred in the scene or incident. 再次展现;再现;重现

  • He re-enacted scenes from his TV series. [VERB noun]

    他再次表演了他在连续剧中的情节。
