flawless
flɔːləs
ADJ-GRADED 完美的;无瑕的;无可挑剔的 If you say that something or someone is flawless, you mean that they are extremely good and that there are no faults or problems with them. 完美的;无瑕的;无可挑剔的

  • She attributed her flawless complexion to the moisturiser she used.

    她将自己无瑕的肌肤归功于所使用的保湿霜。

  • Discovery's takeoff this morning from Cape Canaveral was flawless.

    “发现”号航天飞机今晨从卡纳维拉尔角顺利发射升空。
