splay
spleɪ spleɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense splays, present participle splaying, past tense , past participle splayed
V-ERG (使)(物体的多个末端)向外张开 If things splay or are splayed, their ends are spread out away from each other. (使)(物体的多个末端)向外张开

  • He splayed his fingers across his face. [VERB noun]

    他张开手指蒙住了脸。

  • His fingers splay out in a star shape. [VERB adv./prep.]

    他的手指呈星形张开。

  • He was on his stomach, his legs splayed apart. [VERB-ed]

    他趴着，双腿张开。
