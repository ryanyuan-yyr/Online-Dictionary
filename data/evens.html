evens
iːvənz ˈiːvənz
1 N-UNCOUNT （赛马、比赛等的）同额赌注 In a race or contest, if you bet on a horse or competitor that is quoted at evens, you will win a sum of money equal to your bet if that horse or competitor wins. （赛马、比赛等的）同额赌注 [British]

  • He won his first race by six lengths at evens.

    他在第一场赛马中以6个马位的优势赢得了同额赌金。

  • The Martell Cup Chase was won by the evens favourite Toby Tobias.

    最被同额赌注赌家看好的托比·托拜厄斯赢得了马特尔杯追逐赛。

2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （机会）均等的，各半的 If there is an evens chance that something will happen, it is equally likely that it will happen or will not happen. （机会）均等的，各半的 [British]

  • You've then got an evens chance of doubling your money at a stroke.

    然后你就有一半的机会可以把钱一下子翻一倍。
