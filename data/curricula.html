curriculum
kərɪkjʊləm kəˈrɪkjuləm kəˈrɪkjələm
Word forms: plural curriculums, plural curricula/kərɪkjʊlə/
1 N-COUNT (学校、学院或大学的)全部课程 A curriculum is all the different courses of study that are taught in a school, college, or university. (学校、学院或大学的)全部课程

  • There should be a broader curriculum in schools for post-16-year-old pupils.

    学校对16岁以上的学生开设的课程，范围应该更广一些。

  • Russian is the one compulsory foreign language on the school curriculum.

    俄语在学校课程设置中是一门必修的外语课。

2See also:National Curriculum 
3 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] (特定的)课程 A particular curriculum is one particular course of study that is taught in a school, college, or university. (特定的)课程

  • ...the history curriculum.

    历史课
