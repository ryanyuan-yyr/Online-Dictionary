sluice
sluːs slus
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense sluices, present participle sluicing, past tense , past participle sluiced
1 N-COUNT (设有可开启和关闭的水闸的)泄洪道，泄水道 A sluice is a passage that carries a current of water and has a barrier, called a sluice gate, which can be opened and closed to control the flow of water. (设有可开启和关闭的水闸的)泄洪道，泄水道
2 VERB 冲洗 If you sluice something or sluice it down or out, you wash it with a stream of water. 冲洗

  • He sluiced the bath and filled it. [VERB noun]

    他冲洗了浴缸，然后放满了一缸水。

  • Ten minutes later we were sluicing off dust at the fountain in the town centre. [VERB noun with adv.]

    10 分钟之后，我们就在市中心的喷泉里冲洗身上的灰尘了。

SYN drain, cleanse, flush, drench
