club
klʌb klʌb
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense clubs, present participle clubbing, past tense , past participle clubbed
1 N-COUNT 俱乐部;会;社团 A club is an organization of people interested in a particular activity or subject who usually meet on a regular basis. 俱乐部;会;社团

  • ...the Chorlton Conservative Club.

    升尔顿保守党俱乐部

  • ...a youth club.

    青年俱乐部

  • He was club secretary.

    他是俱乐部秘书。

2 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 俱乐部会所；俱乐部活动场所 A club is a place where the members of a club meet. 俱乐部会所；俱乐部活动场所

  • I stopped in at the club for a drink.

    我顺路到俱乐部喝了一杯。

3 N-COUNT 职业运动俱乐部 A club is a team which competes in sporting competitions. 职业运动俱乐部

  • ...the New York Yankees baseball club.

    纽约扬基职业棒球俱乐部

  • ...Liverpool football club.

    利物浦足球俱乐部

4 N-COUNT 同 nightclub A club is the same as a nightclub . 同 nightclub

  • It's a big dance hit in the clubs.

    这支舞曲在夜总会很火。

  • ...the London club scene.

    伦敦的夜总会场景

SYN nightclub, disco, nightspot  
5 N-COUNT (高尔夫球)球杆 A club is a long, thin, metal stick with a piece of wood or metal at one end that you use to hit the ball in golf. (高尔夫球)球杆

  • ...a six-iron club.

    6号铁杆

6 N-COUNT 大棒;棍棒 A club is a thick heavy stick that can be used as a weapon. 大棒;棍棒

  • Men armed with knives and clubs attacked his home.

    一群手持刀棒的人袭击了他家。

SYN stick, bat, bludgeon, truncheon [British]  
7 VERB (用大棒等)狠狠地打,猛击 To club a person or animal means to hit them hard with a thick heavy stick or a similar weapon. (用大棒等)狠狠地打,猛击

  • Two thugs clubbed him with baseball bats. [VERB noun]

    两个暴徒用棒球棒猛击他。

  • Clubbing baby seals to death for their pelts is wrong. [VERB noun + to]

    为了获得海豹皮而用棍棒打死幼海豹是不道德的。

SYN beat, strike, hammer [informal] , batter  
8 N-UNCOUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] (纸牌中的)梅花 Clubs is one of the four suits in a pack of playing cards. Each card in the suit is marked with one or more black symbols: ♣. (纸牌中的)梅花

  • ...the ace of clubs.

    梅花A

A club is a playing card of this suit.(一张)梅花(牌)

  • The next player discarded a club.

    下一个牌手打出一张梅花。

club together
