carnivore
kɑːʳnɪvɔːʳ ˈkɑrnəˌvɔr
Word forms: plural carnivores
1 N-COUNT 食肉动物 A carnivore is an animal that eats meat. 食肉动物 [technical]
2 N-COUNT 喜欢吃肉的人；食肉动物(诙谐说法) If you describe someone as a carnivore, you are saying, especially in a humorous way, that they eat meat. 喜欢吃肉的人；食肉动物(诙谐说法)

  • This is a vegetarian dish that carnivores love.

    这是一道肉食主义者爱吃的素菜。
