dam
dæm dæm dæm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dams, present participle damming, past tense , past participle dammed
1 N-COUNT 坝;堤 A dam is a wall that is built across a river in order to stop the water flowing and to make a lake. 坝;堤

  • ...plans to build a dam on the Danube River.

    在多瑙河上建坝的计划

  • ...the Aswan Dam.

    阿斯旺大坝

SYN barrier, wall, barrage, obstruction  
2 VERB 在（河）上筑坝 To dam a river means to build a dam across it. 在（河）上筑坝

  • ...plans to dam the nearby Delaware River. [VERB noun]

    在附近的特拉华河上筑坝的计划

  • This reservoir was formed by damming the River Blith. [VERB noun]

    这个水库是通过在布莱斯河上筑坝形成的。

SYN block up, block, hold in, restrict  
3 N-COUNT [usually with poss] 雌亲;母畜 In the breeding of farm animals, an animal's dam is its mother. 雌亲;母畜 [technical]
