stash
stæʃ stæʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense stashes, present participle stashing, past tense , past participle stashed
1 VERB 存放，储藏，藏匿(贵重物品) If you stash something valuable in a secret place, you store it there to keep it safe. 存放，储藏，藏匿(贵重物品) [informal] [Also VERB n]

  • We went for the bottle of whiskey that we had stashed behind the bookcase. [VERB noun prep.]

    我们去拿那瓶藏在书架后面的威士忌。

  • Andrews had stashed money away in secret offshore bank accounts. [VERB noun with adv.]

    安德鲁斯已经将钱存到了秘密的海外银行账户中。

SYN store, stockpile, save up, hoard  
2 N-COUNT 藏匿的物品 A stash of something valuable is a secret store of it. 藏匿的物品 [informal]

  • A large stash of drugs had been found aboard the yacht. [+ of]

    在游艇上找到了一大批藏匿的毒品。

SYN hoard, supply, store, stockpile
