wide-ranging
ˌwaɪdˈreɪndʒɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 范围广的;广泛的 If you describe something as wide-ranging, you mean it deals with or affects a great variety of different things. 范围广的;广泛的

  • ...a wide-ranging debate about the party's goals.

    关于该政党目标宗旨的广泛争论

  • ...a package of wide-ranging economic reforms.

    内容广泛的一揽子经济改革方案

  • The aims of the redesign are wide-ranging but simple.

    重新设计的目的是多方面的，但又是简单的。

SYN far-reaching, sweeping, broad, widespread
