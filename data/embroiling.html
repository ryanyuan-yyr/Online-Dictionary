embroil
ɪmbrɔɪl ɛmˈbrɔɪl ɪmˈbrɔɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense embroils, present participle embroiling, past tense , past participle embroiled
VERB 使卷入，使陷入（战斗、争论等） If someone embroils you in a fight or an argument, they get you deeply involved in it. 使卷入，使陷入（战斗、争论等） [Also VERB n]

  • Any hostilities could result in retaliation and further embroil U.N. troops in fighting. [VERB noun + in]

    任何敌对行为都可能招致报复，进而使联合国部队卷入战斗。

SYN involve, complicate, mix up, implicate
