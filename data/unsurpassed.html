unsurpassed
ʌnseʳpɑːst -pæst ˌʌnsɜːˈpɑːst
ADJ 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的；超凡绝伦的 If you describe something as unsurpassed, you are emphasizing that it is better or greater than anything else of its kind. 无与伦比的；无可匹敌的；超凡绝伦的 [emphasis]

  • The quality of Smallbone furniture is unsurpassed.

    斯莫尔本恩家具品质超凡。

  • ...the Hamburg weekly, surely unsurpassed in the world for its intellectual range and quality. [+ for]

    在汉堡出版的这份周刊视野广博，见解深刻，无疑卓绝于世

SYN supreme, unparalleled, unrivalled, exceptional
