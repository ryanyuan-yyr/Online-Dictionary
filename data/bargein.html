barge in
PHRASAL VERB （粗鲁地）打断（某人说话），打扰（某人做事） If you barge in or barge in on someone, you rudely interrupt what they are doing or saying. （粗鲁地）打断（某人说话），打扰（某人做事） [informal] [Also V P + on]

  • I'm sorry to barge in like this, but I have a problem I hope you can solve. [VERB PREP.]

    很抱歉这样打扰您，但是我有个问题希望您能解决。
