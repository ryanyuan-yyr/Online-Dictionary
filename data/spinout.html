spin out
1 PHRASAL VERB 拖长;拉长 If you spin something out, you make it last longer than it normally would. 拖长;拉长

  • My wife's solicitor was anxious to spin things out for as long as possible. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我妻子的律师很想尽可能拖延时间。

  • The Government will try to spin out the conference into next autumn. [VERB PREP. noun]

    政府会竭力将会议拖长到下个秋季。

SYN prolong, extend, lengthen, draw out  
2 See also: spin off
