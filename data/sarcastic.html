sarcastic
sɑːʳkæstɪk sɑrˈkæstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 讽刺的;嘲笑的;挖苦的 Someone who is sarcastic says or does the opposite of what they really mean in order to mock or insult someone. 讽刺的;嘲笑的;挖苦的

  • She poked fun at people's shortcomings with sarcastic remarks.

    她冷嘲热讽地拿别人的缺点开玩笑。

SYN ironical, cynical, satirical, cutting  
sarcastically

  • 'What a surprise!' Caroline murmured sarcastically.

    “太神奇了!”卡罗琳轻声挖苦道。
