traduce
trədjuːs -duːs trəˈdus trəˈdjus
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense traduces, present participle traducing, past tense , past participle traduced
VERB [usually passive] 诋毁;中伤;诽谤 If someone has been traduced, unpleasant and untrue things have deliberately been said about them. 诋毁;中伤;诽谤 [formal]

  • We have been traduced in the press as xenophobic bigots. [be VERB-ed]

    我们被新闻界诋毁为仇外的偏狭之徒。

SYN malign, abuse, knock [informal] , rubbish [informal]
