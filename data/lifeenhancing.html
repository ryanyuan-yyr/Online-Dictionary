life-enhancing
laɪf ɪnˈhɑːnsɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 使人更加快乐满足的 If you describe something as life-enhancing, you mean that it makes you feel happier and more content. 使人更加快乐满足的

  • ...a life-enhancing and exciting trip.

    一次充实而激动人心的旅行

  • His letters, like his poetry, are life-enhancing and a delight.

    他的信跟他的诗一样，读来令生活更添滋味，可谓一大乐事。
