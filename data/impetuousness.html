impetuous
ɪmpetʃuəs ɪmˈpɛtʃuəs
ADJ-GRADED 急躁的;冲动的 If you describe someone as impetuous, you mean that they are likely to act quickly and suddenly without thinking or being careful. 急躁的;冲动的

  • He was young and impetuous.

    他年轻,易于冲动。

  • He tended to react in a heated and impetuous way.

    他往往反应过火,急躁冲动。

SYN rash, hasty, impulsive, violent
