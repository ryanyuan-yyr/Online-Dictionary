composite
kɒmpəzɪt kəmpɑːzɪt kəmˈpɑzɪt
Word forms: plural composites
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 拼成的;混合成的;复合的 A composite object or item is made up of several different things, parts, or substances. 拼成的;混合成的;复合的

  • Galton devised a method of creating composite pictures in which the features of different faces were superimposed over one another.

    高尔顿发明了一种把不同人的五官相互叠加在一起的制作合成照片的方法。

Composite is also a noun.

  • Spain is a composite of diverse traditions and people.

    西班牙是一个汇集了多种传统和民族的国家。
