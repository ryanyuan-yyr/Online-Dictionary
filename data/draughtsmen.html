draughtsman
drɑːftsmən dræfts- ˈdræftsmən ˈdrɑːftsmən drɑftsmən
Word forms: plural draughtsmen
in AM, use 美国英语用 draftsman
1 N-COUNT 绘图员;制图员 A draughtsman is someone whose job is to prepare very detailed drawings of machinery, equipment, or buildings. 绘图员;制图员
2 N-COUNT [usually adjective NOUN] 擅长绘画之人;画家 If someone is a good draughtsman, they are very skilled at drawing. 擅长绘画之人;画家

  • This is a great drawing by one of the 19th century's finest draughtsmen.

    这幅恢宏画作出自一位19世纪最杰出的画家之手。
