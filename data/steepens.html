steepen
stiːpən ˈstiːpən ˈstipən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense steepens, present participle steepening, past tense , past participle steepened
VERB 变得陡峭;变得陡直 If a slope or an angle steepens, it becomes steeper. 变得陡峭;变得陡直 [literary]

  • The road steepened and then levelled out suddenly. [VERB]

    路变得陡直起来，然后又突然变平坦了。
