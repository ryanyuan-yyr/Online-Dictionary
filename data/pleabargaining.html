plea bargain
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense plea bargains, present participle plea bargaining, past tense , past participle plea bargained
1 N-COUNT （一些法律体系中被告承认有罪以减轻刑罚的）认罪辩诉协议 In some legal systems, a plea bargain is an agreement that, if an accused person says they are guilty, they will be charged with a less serious crime or will receive a less severe punishment. （一些法律体系中被告承认有罪以减轻刑罚的）认罪辩诉协议

  • A plea bargain was offered by the state assuring her that she would not go to prison.

    州方提出了一份认罪辩诉协议，保证她不会入狱。

2 VERB （被告）接受认罪辩诉协议 If an accused person plea bargains, they accept a plea bargain. （被告）接受认罪辩诉协议

  • More and more criminals will agree to plea-bargain. [VERB]

    越来越多的罪犯会同意接受认罪辩诉协议。
