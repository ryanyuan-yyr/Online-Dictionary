coming of age ˈkʌmɪŋ əv eɪdʒ
1 N-SING 成熟;发达 When something reaches an important stage of development and is accepted by a large number of people, you can refer to this as its coming of age . 成熟;发达

  • ...postwar Germany's final coming-of-age as an independent sovereign state.

    战后德国作为独立主权国家的最终成熟

2 N-SING [with poss] 成年 Someone's coming of age is the time when they become legally an adult. 成年

  • ...traditional coming-of-age ceremonies.

    传统的成年仪式
