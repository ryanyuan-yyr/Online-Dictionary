formulaic
fɔːʳmjʊleɪɪk ˌfɔrmjuˈleɪɪk ˌfɔrmjəˈleɪɪk
ADJ-GRADED 程式化的;俗套的;非独创的 If you describe a way of saying or doing something as formulaic, you are criticizing it because it is not original and has been used many times before in similar situations. 程式化的;俗套的;非独创的 [disapproval]

  • His paintings are contrived and formulaic.

    他的绘画作品做作而又俗套。
