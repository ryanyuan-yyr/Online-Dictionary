amalgamate
əmælgəmeɪt əˈmælgəˌmeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense amalgamates, present participle amalgamating, past tense , past participle amalgamated
V-RECIP-ERG （使）合并 When two or more things, especially organizations, amalgamate or are amalgamated, they become one large thing. （使）合并 [Also VERB n + into]

  • The firm has amalgamated with an American company. [VERB + with]

    该公司和一家美国公司合并了。

  • The chemical companies had amalgamated into a vast conglomerate. [VERB + into]

    那些化学品公司已经合并成一个庞大的企业集团。

  • The Visitors' Centre amalgamates the traditions of the Old World with the technology of the New. [VERB noun + with]

    来宾中心将欧洲的传统与美洲的技术结合在一起。

SYN combine, unite, ally, compound
