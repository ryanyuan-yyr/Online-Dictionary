secrecy
siːkrəsi ˈsiːkrɪsɪ ˈsikrəsi
N-UNCOUNT [oft preposition NOUN] 保密;秘密状态 Secrecy is the act of keeping something secret, or the state of being kept secret. 保密;秘密状态

  • The British government has thrown a blanket of secrecy over the details.

    英国政府对细节讳莫如深。

  • He shrouds his business dealings in secrecy.

    他秘密进行交易。

SYN mystery, stealth, concealment, furtiveness  
Quotations
If you would wish another to keep your secret, first keep it yourselfSeneca Hippolytus
