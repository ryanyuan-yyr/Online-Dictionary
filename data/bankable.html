bankable
bæŋkəbəl ˈbæŋkəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （娱乐行业的人或事物）有票房号召力的，能吸金的 In the entertainment industry, someone or something that is described as bankable is very popular and therefore likely to be very profitable. （娱乐行业的人或事物）有票房号召力的，能吸金的
