deregulate
diːregjʊleɪt diˈrɛgjəˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deregulates, present participle deregulating, past tense , past participle deregulated
VERB 撤销对…的管制规定;解除对…的控制 To deregulate something means to remove controls and regulations from it. 撤销对…的管制规定;解除对…的控制

  • ...the need to deregulate the US airline industry.

    撤销对美国航空业管制的需要

  • Once wholesale prices are deregulated, consumer prices will also rise. [VERB noun]

    一旦批发价格放开，零售价格也会随之上涨。
