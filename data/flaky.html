flaky
fleɪki ˈfleɪki
1 ADJ-GRADED 易碎成小薄片的;易剥落的 Something that is flaky breaks easily into small thin pieces or tends to come off in small thin pieces. 易碎成小薄片的;易剥落的

  • ...a small patch of red, flaky skin.

    一小块发红而且快要脱落的皮肤

2 ADJ-GRADED 古怪的;不可靠的 If you describe an idea, argument, or person as flaky, you mean that they are rather eccentric and unreliable. 古怪的;不可靠的 [informal, disapproval]

  • He wondered if the idea wasn't just a little too flaky, a little too outlandish.

    他怀疑这个想法不仅仅是有点太古怪、太离奇而已。
