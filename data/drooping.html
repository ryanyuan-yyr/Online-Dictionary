droop
druːp drup
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense droops, present participle drooping, past tense , past participle drooped
VERB 低垂;下垂;垂落 If something droops, it hangs or leans downwards with no strength or firmness. 低垂;下垂;垂落

  • Crook's eyelids drooped and he yawned. [VERB]

    克鲁克的眼皮耷拉下来,打了个哈欠。

  • Pale wilting roses drooped from a blue vase. [VERB prep.]

    泛白凋零的玫瑰从蓝色的花瓶里耷拉下来。

  • ...a young man with a drooping moustache. [VERB-ing]

    蓄着八字胡的年轻人

SYN sag, drop, hang (down), sink  
Droop is also a noun.

  • ...the droop of his shoulders.

    他肩膀耷拉着
