pennant
penənt ˈpɛnənt
Word forms: plural pennants
1 N-COUNT 三角旗 A pennant is a long, narrow, triangular flag. 三角旗

  • The second car was flying the Ghanaian pennant.

    第二辆车插着加纳的三角旗。

SYN flag, jack, banner, ensign  
2 N-COUNT （棒球联赛冠军队获得的）锦旗，冠军地位 In baseball, a pennant is a flag that is given each year to the top team in a league. The championship is also called the pennant. （棒球联赛冠军队获得的）锦旗，冠军地位 [US]

  • The Red Sox lost the pennant to Detroit by a single game.

    红袜队以一场之差将冠军输给了底特律队。
