proofread
pruːfriːd ˈprufˌrid
(also proof-read)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense proofreads, present participle proofreading
VERB 校对 When someone proofreads something such as a book or an article, they read it before it is published in order to find and mark mistakes that need to be corrected. 校对 [Also VERB]

  • I didn't even have the chance to proofread my own report. [VERB noun]
