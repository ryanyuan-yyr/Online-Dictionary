give over
PHRASAL VERB 停止；别再做；住手 If you tell someone to give over, you are telling them to stop doing something, usually because they are annoying you. 停止；别再做；住手 [informal]

  • Tell him to give over. [VERB PREP.]

    告诉他到此为止吧。

  • She gave over teasing and grinned at him. [V P -ing/n]

    她不再取笑他，只是朝他咧着嘴笑。
