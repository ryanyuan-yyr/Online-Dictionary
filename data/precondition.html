precondition
priːkəndɪʃən ˌpriːkənˈdɪʃən ˌprikənˈdɪʃən
Word forms: plural preconditions
N-COUNT 先决条件；前提 If one thing is a precondition for another, it must happen or be done before the second thing can happen or exist. 先决条件；前提 [formal]

  • They made multi-party democracy a precondition for giving aid. [+ for/of/to]

    他们把实施多党民主作为给予援助的前提。

SYN necessity, essential, requirement, prerequisite
