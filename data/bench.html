bench
bentʃ bɛntʃ
Word forms: plural benches
1 N-COUNT 长凳；长椅 A bench is a long seat of wood or metal that two or more people can sit on. 长凳；长椅

  • He sat down on a park bench.

    他在公园的长椅上坐了下来。

SYN seat, stall, pew  
2 N-COUNT （长方形）工作台，实验台 A bench is a long, narrow table in a factory or laboratory. （长方形）工作台，实验台

  • ...the laboratory bench.

    实验台

SYN worktable, stand, table, counter  
3 N-PLURAL 议员席；议席 In parliament, different groups sit on different benches. For example, the government sits on the government benches . 议员席；议席 [British]

  • ...the opposition benches.

    反对派议席

4See also:backbench  backbencher  backbenches  front bench 
5 N-SING-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 法官；地方执法官 In a court of law, the bench is the judge or magistrates. 法官；地方执法官

  • The chairman of the bench adjourned the case until October 27.

    主审法官将案件延期到10月27日审理。

6 N-SING [the NOUN] 法官职位；地方执法官职位 If someone serves on the bench, they work as a judge or magistrate. 法官职位；地方执法官职位

  • Allgood served on the bench for more than 50 years.

    奥尔古德当了五十多年的法官。

7 N-SING [the NOUN] （体育比赛的）替补席 If a member of a sports team is on the bench for a particular match, he or she waits at the side of the field until the manager tells them to play. （体育比赛的）替补席 [journalism]

  • Ian Ferguson was preferred to Stuart McCall, who was on the bench.

    伊恩·弗格森比坐在替补席上的斯图尔特·麦考尔更受器重。
