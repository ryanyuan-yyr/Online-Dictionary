ensconced ɪnskɒnst
ADJ 舒服地坐于…的;安坐的 If you are ensconced somewhere, you are settled there firmly or comfortably and have no intention of moving or leaving. 舒服地坐于…的;安坐的

  • Brian was ensconced behind the bar.

    布赖恩安坐在柜台后。

  • She looked at Miss Melville, snugly ensconced among her new friends.

    她看了看梅尔维尔小姐，她正舒适地安坐在新朋友中间。

SYN settled, established, installed, entrenched
