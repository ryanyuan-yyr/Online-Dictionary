organizer
ɔːʳgənaɪzəʳ ˈɔrgəˌnaɪzər
Word forms: plural organizers
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 organiser
1 N-COUNT 组织者；筹备者 The organizer of an event or activity is the person who makes sure that the necessary arrangements are made. 组织者；筹备者

  • The organisers of the demonstration concede that they hadn't sought permission for it.

    游行示威的组织者承认他们并未申请批准这次活动。

  • ...his campaign organisers who are canvassing for last minute support.

    为他作最后一刻游说以获取支持的竞选组织者

  • She was a good organiser.

    她是个出色的组织者。

SYN planner, manager, administrator, supervisor  
2See also:personal organizer
