supportive
səpɔːʳtɪv səˈpɔrtɪv
ADJ-GRADED 给予帮助的;热心援助的;支持的 If you are supportive, you are kind and helpful to someone at a difficult or unhappy time in their life. 给予帮助的;热心援助的;支持的

  • They were always supportive of each other. [+ of]

    他们一直互相帮助。

  • Her boss was very supportive and gave her time off work to see her mum.

    她的老板非常热心地帮助她，并准许她请假去看望母亲。

SYN helpful, caring, encouraging, understanding
