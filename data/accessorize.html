accessorize
æksesəraɪz ækˈsɛsəˌraɪz əkˈsɛsərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense accessorizes, present participle accessorizing, past tense , past participle accessorized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 accessorise
VERB （以配件、装饰品等）补充，附加；给…装配附件 To accessorize something such as a set of furniture or clothing means to add other things to it in order to make it look more attractive. （以配件、装饰品等）补充，附加；给…装配附件 [Also VERB n + with]

  • Use a belt to accessorise a plain dress. [VERB noun]

    用腰带来装点一件素裙。

SYN add to, decorate, supplement, complement
