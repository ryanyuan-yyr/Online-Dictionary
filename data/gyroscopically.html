gyroscope
dʒaɪrəskoʊp ˈdʒaɪrəˌstæt ˈdʒaɪroʊˌskoʊp ˈdʒaɪrəˌskoʊp
Word forms: plural gyroscopes
N-COUNT 陀螺仪；回转仪 A gyroscope is a device that contains a disc turning on an axis that can turn freely in any direction, so that the disc maintains the same position whatever the position or movement of the surrounding structure. 陀螺仪；回转仪
