retinue
retɪnjuː -nuː ˈrɛtənˌu ˈrɛtənˌju
Word forms: plural retinues
N-COUNT 随行人员;随从;随员 An important person's retinue is the group of servants, friends, or assistants who go with them and look after their needs. 随行人员;随从;随员 [Also + of]

  • Mind trainers are now part of a tennis star's retinue.

    现在，网球明星的随行人员中有些是心理培训师。

SYN attendants, entourage, escort, servants
