bomb disposal bɒm dɪˈspəʊzəl
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 未爆炸弹处理 Bomb disposal is the job of dealing with bombs which have not exploded, by taking out the fuse or by blowing them up in a controlled explosion. 未爆炸弹处理
