betrothed
bɪtroʊðd bɪˈtroʊðd bɪˈtrɔθt
ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 订了婚的；定了亲的 If you are betrothed to someone, you have agreed to marry them. 订了婚的；定了亲的 [old-fashioned]

  • She was betrothed to his brother.

    她和他哥哥订了婚。

SYN engaged, promised, pledged, plighted  
Your betrothed is the person you are betrothed to.订婚对象；未婚夫；未婚妻

  • She is here without her betrothed.

    她丢下未婚夫一个人来了。
