oxford
ɑksfərd ˈɑksfərd
(also Oxford)Word forms: oxfords
1 N-PLURAL 牛津浅帮鞋Oxfords are low shoes with laces at the front. 牛津浅帮鞋 [mainly US]

  • He had changed his boots for a pair of Oxfords.

    他已经将靴子换成了一双牛津浅帮鞋。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usu N n] (做男衬衫用的)牛津棉布Oxford or oxford cloth is a type of cotton fabric, used especially for men's shirts. (做男衬衫用的)牛津棉布 [mainly US]

  • This pure cotton Oxford shirt comes in three colours.

    这款牛津纯棉布衬衫有三种颜色。

  • I hugged him, smelling new oxford cloth and warm skin.

    我拥抱了他，闻到他那新牛津棉布衫和温暖的皮肤的味道。
