extraordinaire
ekstrɔːʳdɪneəʳ ɛksˌtrɔːdɪˈnɛə ɪkˌstrɔrdəˈnɛr
ADJ [n ADJ] 非凡的，卓越的，出色的（略显幽默的说法） If you describe someone as being, for example, a musician extraordinaire, you are saying in a slightly humorous way that you think they are an extremely good musician. 非凡的，卓越的，出色的（略显幽默的说法）
