infinity
ɪnfɪnɪti ɪnˈfɪnɪti
1 N-UNCOUNT 无穷大 Infinity is a number that is larger than any other number and can never be given an exact value. 无穷大

  • These permutations multiply towards infinity.

    这些排列可以增加到无穷大。

  • There is always an infinity of numbers between any two numbers.

    任何两个数字之间都存在无穷多的数字。

2 N-UNCOUNT 无限远的距离 Infinity is a point that is further away than any other point and can never be reached. 无限远的距离

  • ...the darkness of a starless night stretching to infinity.

    没有星星的夜晚，黑暗无边无际

SYN eternity, vastness, immensity, perpetuity
