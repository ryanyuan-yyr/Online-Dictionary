real estate
1 N-UNCOUNT 不动产;房地产 Real estate is property in the form of land and buildings, rather than personal possessions. 不动产;房地产 [mainly US]

  • By investing in real estate, he was one of the richest men in the United States.

    通过投资房地产，他成为美国最富有的人之一。

  • ...the most spectacular piece of real estate he had ever imagined.

    他能想象出来的最气派的房产

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 房地产（商）；房地产（代理人） Real estate businesses or real estate agents sell houses, buildings, and land. 房地产（商）；房地产（代理人） [US]

  • ...the real estate agent who sold you your house.

    卖给你房子的房地产经纪商

  • ...the real estate industry.

    房地产行业

  • He had a long career in real estate.

    他曾长期从事房地产业。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 estate agency, estate agents
