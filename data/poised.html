poised
pɔɪzd pɔɪzd
1 ADJ （身体部位）摆好姿势不动的 If a part of your body is poised, it is completely still but ready to move at any moment. （身体部位）摆好姿势不动的

  • He studied the keyboard carefully, one finger poised.

    他仔细研究了键盘，一个手指随时准备敲击。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ, usually ADJ to-infinitive] 作好一切准备的；随时准备行动的 If someone is poised to do something, they are ready to take action at any moment. 作好一切准备的；随时准备行动的

  • Britain was poised to fly medical staff to the country at short notice.

    英国随时准备把医护人员空运到该国。

  • US forces are poised for a massive air, land and sea assault.

    美国军队作好了发动大规模海、陆、空攻击的准备。

SYN ready, waiting, prepared, standing by  
3 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 镇定的；沉着的；泰然自若的 If you are poised, you are calm, dignified, and self-controlled. 镇定的；沉着的；泰然自若的

  • She was self-assured, poised, almost self-satisfied.

    她自信、沉着，几乎有些洋洋自得。

  • Rachel appeared poised and calm.

    蕾切尔看上去沉着冷静。

SYN composed, calm, together [informal] , collected
