henchman
hentʃmən ˈhɛntʃmən
Word forms: plural henchmen
N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 党徒;走狗;帮凶;打手 If you refer to someone as another person's henchman, you mean that they work for or support the other person, especially by doing unpleasant, violent, or dishonest things on their behalf. 党徒;走狗;帮凶;打手 [disapproval]
SYN attendant, supporter, heavy [slang] , associate
