aground
əgraʊnd əˈgraʊnd
ADV [ADVERB after verb] （船）搁浅 If a ship runs aground, it touches the ground in a shallow part of a river, lake, or the sea, and gets stuck. （船）搁浅

  • The ship ran aground where there should have been a depth of 35ft.

    船搁浅在水深本应为35英尺的地方。

SYN beached, grounded, stuck, shipwrecked
