condominium
kɒndəmɪniəm ˌkɒndəˈmɪnɪəm ˌkɑndəˈmɪniəm
Word forms: plural condominiums
1 N-COUNT (每一套公寓归居住者所有的)公寓楼 A condominium is an apartment building in which each apartment is owned by the person who lives there. (每一套公寓归居住者所有的)公寓楼 [US]
2 N-COUNT 一套公寓住房 A condominium is one of the privately owned apartments in a condominium. 一套公寓住房 [US]
