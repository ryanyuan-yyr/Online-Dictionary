smelly
smeli ˈsmɛli
Word forms: comparative smellier, superlative smelliest
ADJ-GRADED 气味难闻的;发臭的 Something that is smelly has an unpleasant smell. 气味难闻的;发臭的

  • He had extremely smelly feet.

    他的脚奇臭无比。

  • Pubs are dirty, smelly and unfriendly, according to the Good Pub Guide.

    据《好酒吧指南》所说，酒吧一般环境肮脏，气味难闻，而且气氛很不友好。

SYN stinking, reeking, fetid, foul-smelling
