ride out
PHRASAL VERB 安然度过，挺过(风暴或危机) If someone rides out a storm or a crisis, they manage to survive a difficult period without suffering serious harm. 安然度过，挺过(风暴或危机)

  • The ruling party think they can ride out the political storm. [VERB PREP. noun]

    执政党认为他们可以安然度过这场政治风暴。

  • He has to just ride this out and hope that it turns in his favor. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他必须经受住此次危机，并且希望结果会对他有利。

SYN survive, last, live through, hold out against
