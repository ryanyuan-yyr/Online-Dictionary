buccaneering
bʌkənɪərɪŋ ˌbʌkəˈnɪərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （尤指以赚钱为目的）热衷投机的 If you describe someone as buccaneering, you mean that they enjoy being involved in risky or even dishonest activities, especially in order to make money. （尤指以赚钱为目的）热衷投机的 [British]

  • ...a buccaneering British businessman.

    一个喜欢投机的英国商人
