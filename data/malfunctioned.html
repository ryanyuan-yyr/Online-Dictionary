malfunction
mælfʌŋkʃən mælˈfʌŋkʃən
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense malfunctions, present participle malfunctioning, past tense , past participle malfunctioned
1 VERB （机器）失灵，出故障；（人体器官）机能失常，出现功能障碍 If a machine or part of the body malfunctions, it fails to work properly. （机器）失灵，出故障；（人体器官）机能失常，出现功能障碍 [formal]

  • The radiation can damage microprocessors and computer memories, causing them to malfunction. [VERB]

    辐射会损坏微处理器和电脑内存，使之失灵。

  • Early physicians removed the malfunctioning organs. [VERB-ing]

    早年间的医生会把机能失常的器官摘除掉。

Malfunction is also a noun.

  • There must have been a computer malfunction.

    一定是出了电脑故障。
