-year-old -jɪər-oʊld
Word forms: plural -year-olds
COMB in ADJ [ADJ n] （与数字连用）表示“…岁的”，“…年的” -year-old combines with numbers to describe the age of people or things. （与数字连用）表示“…岁的”，“…年的”

  • She has a six-year-old daughter.

    她有一个6岁的女儿。

  • ...their 200-year-old farmhouse in Ohio.

    他们在俄亥俄州的有两百年历史的农舍

-year-old also combines to form nouns.（亦用于构成名词）

  • Snow Puppies is a ski school for 3 to 6-year-olds.

    “雪狗之家”是一所针对3至6岁儿童的滑雪学校。
