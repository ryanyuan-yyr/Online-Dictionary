intern
ɪntɜːʳn ɪntɜːʳn ˈɪnˌtɜrn ɪnˈtɜrn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense interns, present participle interning, past tense , past participle interned
The verb is pronounced /ɪn'tɜːn/. The noun is pronounced /'ɪntɜːn/. 动词读作 /ɪn'tɜːn/。名词读作 /'ɪntɜːn/。
1 VERB [usually passive] (因政治原因)拘留，扣押 If someone is interned, they are put in prison or in a prison camp for political reasons. (因政治原因)拘留，扣押

  • He was interned as an enemy alien at the outbreak of the Second World War. [be VERB-ed]

    第二次世界大战爆发时他被作为敌国侨民扣押了。

SYN imprison, hold, confine, detain  
2 N-COUNT 实习生;(尤指)实习医师 An intern is an advanced student or a recent graduate, especially in medicine, who is being given practical training under supervision. 实习生;(尤指)实习医师 [US]
SYN apprentice, student, pupil, novice
