paralytic
pærəlɪtɪk ˌpærəˈlɪtɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 麻痹的；瘫痪的 Paralytic means suffering from or related to paralysis. 麻痹的；瘫痪的

  • ...paralytic disease.

    致瘫疾病

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 烂醉如泥的 Someone who is paralytic is very drunk. 烂醉如泥的 [British, informal]

  • By the end of the evening they were all absolutely paralytic.

    晚上结束时，他们全都酩酊大醉。

SYN drunk, pissed [British , Australian , New Zealand , slang] , intoxicated, wasted [slang]
