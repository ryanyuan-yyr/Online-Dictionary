embodiment
ɪmbɒdimənt ɛmˈbɑdɪmənt ɛmˈbɑdimənt ɪmˈbɑdɪmənt ɪmˈbɑdimənt
N-SING （品质、思想等的）典型，化身 If you say that someone or something is the embodiment of a quality or idea, you mean that that is their most noticeable characteristic or the basis of all they do. （品质、思想等的）典型，化身 [formal]

  • A baby is the embodiment of vulnerability. [+ of]

    婴儿极为脆弱。

SYN personification, example, model, type
