grievous
griːvəs ˈgrivəs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 极其严重的；令人担忧的 If you describe something such as a loss as grievous, you mean that it is extremely serious or worrying in its effects. 极其严重的；令人担忧的

  • Their loss would be a grievous blow to our engineering industries.

    失去它们将会是对我们工程行业的一个沉重打击。

  • Mr Morris said the victims had suffered from a very grievous mistake.

    莫里斯先生说遇难者因为一个极其严重的失误而遭遇不幸。

SYN deplorable, shocking, appalling, dreadful  
grievously

  • Birds, sea-life and the coastline all suffered grievously.

    鸟类、海洋生物及沿海生物均受到严重影响。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 剧烈的；极痛苦的；疼痛难耐的 A grievous injury to your body is one that causes you great pain and suffering. 剧烈的；极痛苦的；疼痛难耐的

  • He survived in spite of suffering grievous injuries.

    尽管身受重伤，他还是活下来了。

SYN severe, damaging, heavy, wounding  
grievously

  • Nelson Piquet, three times world champion, was grievously injured.

    三度获得世界冠军的尼尔森·皮奎特受了重伤。
