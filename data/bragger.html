brag
bræg bræg
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense brags, present participle bragging, past tense , past participle bragged
VERB 吹嘘；夸耀 If you brag, you say in a very proud way that you have something or have done something. 吹嘘；夸耀 [disapproval]

  • He's always bragging about his prowess as a cricketer. [VERB + about]

    他总是吹嘘自己板球球技的高超。

  • He'll probably go around bragging to his friends. [VERB + to]

    他很可能会到处向他的朋友们吹嘘。

  • The chairman never tires of bragging that he and Mr. McCormack are old friends. [VERB that]

    主席总是不厌其烦地到处炫耀他和麦科马克先生是老朋友。

SYN boast, crow, swagger, vaunt
