plan
plæn plæn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense plans, present participle planning, past tense , past participle planned
1 N-COUNT [oft according to N] 计划；规划；方案 A plan is a method of achieving something that you have worked out in detail beforehand. 计划；规划；方案

  • The three leaders had worked out a peace plan.

    3位领导人已制订出了一个和平方案。

  • The project is part of a United Nations plan for refugees.

    该项目是联合国难民计划的一部分。

  • ...a detailed plan of action for restructuring the group. [+ of]

    改组该集团的详细行动计划

  • He maintains that everything is going according to plan.

    他坚称一切都在按计划进行。

2 VERB 计划；筹划；谋划 If you plan what you are going to do, you decide in detail what you are going to do, and you intend to do it. 计划；筹划；谋划

  • If you plan what you're going to eat, you reduce your chances of overeating. [VERB wh]

    如果计划一下要吃什么，就有可能不会吃得过多。

  • He planned to leave Baghdad on Monday. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他计划周一离开巴格达。

  • It would be difficult for schools to plan for the future. [VERB + for]

    学校很难筹划未来。

  • I had been planning a trip to the West Coast. [VERB noun]

    我一直计划去趟西海岸。

  • A planned demonstration has been called off by its organisers. [VERB-ed]

    原定的游行示威已被组织者取消。

SYN devise, arrange, prepare, scheme  
3 N-PLURAL [NOUN to-infinitive] （有做某一特定事的）计划，打算，意向 If you have plans, you are intending to do a particular thing. （有做某一特定事的）计划，打算，意向

  • 'I'm sorry,' she said. 'I have plans for tonight.' [+ for]

    “抱歉，”她说，“我今晚有安排了。”

  • The government is making plans to evacuate more than two hundred of its citizens from the troubled area.

    政府打算疏散200多名留在骚乱地区的公民。

4 VERB 设计；绘制…的图样（或图表） When you plan something that you are going to make, build, or create, you decide what the main parts of it will be and do a drawing of how it should be made. 设计；绘制…的图样（或图表）

  • We are planning a new kitchen. [VERB noun]

    我们在设计新厨房。

5 N-COUNT 平面图；详图；图表 A plan of something that is going to be built or made is a detailed diagram or drawing of it. 平面图；详图；图表

  • ...when you have drawn a plan of the garden. [+ of/for]

    当你画出花园的平面图后

SYN diagram, map, drawing, chart
