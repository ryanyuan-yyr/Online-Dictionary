halterneck
hɔːltəʳnek ˈhɔːltənɛk
Word forms: plural halternecks
N-COUNT （露背装的）绕颈系带 A piece of clothing with a halterneck has a strap that goes around the back of the neck, rather than a strap over each shoulder. （露背装的）绕颈系带
Halterneck is also an adjective.

  • ...a halterneck evening dress.

    绕颈系带的露背晚礼服
