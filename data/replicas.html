replica
replɪkə ˈrɛplɪkə
Word forms: plural replicas
1 N-COUNT 复制品;仿制品 A replica of something such as a statue, building, or weapon is an accurate copy of it. 复制品;仿制品

  • ...a human-sized replica of the Statue of Liberty. [+ of]

    真人大小的自由女神像复制品

  • Royce Hall was an exact replica of the basilica of Sant Ambrogio in Milan.

    罗伊斯大厅是位于米兰的圣安布洛乔大教堂的原样复制建筑。

  • It was a replica gun, for display only.

    那是一把仿真枪，仅供展示。

SYN reproduction, model, copy, imitation  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular, usually NOUN of n] 翻版;酷似的人 If you say that one person is a replica of another, you mean that the first person looks very like the second. 翻版;酷似的人

  • Tina as a child was a replica of her mother.

    蒂娜小的时候和她妈妈简直一模一样。

SYN duplicate, copy, clone, spitting image
