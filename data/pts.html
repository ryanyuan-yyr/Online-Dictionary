pt
(also pt.)Word forms: plural pts
The plural in meaning 1 is either pt or pts. 义项1的复数形式为pt或pts。
1 pt is a written abbreviation for (书面缩略=) pint .

  • ...1 pt single cream.

    一品脱稀奶油

2 pt is the written abbreviation for (书面缩略=) point .

  • Here's how it works — 3 pts for a correct result, 1 pt for the correct winning team.

    规则如下—每答对1题得3分，猜对获胜方得1分。
