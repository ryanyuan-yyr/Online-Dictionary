overwork
oʊvəʳwɜːʳk ˌoʊvərˈwɜrk ˈoʊvərˌwɜrk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overworks, present participle overworking, past tense , past participle overworked
V-ERG （使）工作过度；（使）过分劳累 If you overwork or if someone overworks you, you work too hard, and are likely to become very tired or ill. （使）工作过度；（使）过分劳累

  • He's overworking and has got a lot on his mind. [VERB]

    他工作太累，而且非常操心。

  • He overworks and underpays the poor clerk whom he employs. [VERB noun]

    他让那个可怜的职员超负荷工作，却只支付极低的薪水。

Overwork is also a noun.

  • He died of a heart attack brought on by overwork.

    他死于劳累过度引起的心脏病发作。
