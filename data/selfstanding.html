self-standing
ˌsɛlfˈstændɪŋ
1 ADJ (物体、结构)能自己立住的 An object or structure that is self-standing is not supported by other objects or structures. (物体、结构)能自己立住的

  • ...self-standing plastic cases.

    能自己立住的塑料盒子

2 ADJ (公司、组织)自立的，独立的 A company or organization that is self-standing is independent of other companies or organizations. (公司、组织)自立的，独立的 [business]

  • Five separate companies, all operating as self-standing units, are now one.

    5 个独立经营的不同公司如今已合并为一家公司。
