crackle
krækəl ˈkrækəl ˈkrækəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense crackles, present participle crackling, past tense , past participle crackled
VERB 发出爆裂声;噼啪作响 If something crackles, it makes a rapid series of short, harsh noises. 发出爆裂声;噼啪作响

  • The radio crackled again. [VERB]

    收音机又吱吱啦啦地响了起来。

  • ...a crackling fire. [VERB-ing]

    噼啪作响的炉火

Crackle is also a noun.

  • ...the crackle of flames and gunfire.

    火焰的噼啪声和炮火的轰鸣声
