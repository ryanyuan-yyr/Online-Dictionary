straighten out
1 PHRASAL VERB 理清，整顿(混乱的局面) If you straighten out a confused situation, you succeed in getting it organized and tidied up. 理清，整顿(混乱的局面)

  • He would make an appointment with him to straighten out a couple of things. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他将和他约个时间把几件事情理清楚。

  • My sister had come in with her calm common sense and straightened them out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我姐姐凭着自己的镇定和常识帮着把这些问题都解决了。

SYN sort out, resolve, put right, settle
