unfocused
ʌnfoʊkəst ʌnˈfəʊkəst ʌnˈfoʊkəst
(also unfocussed)
1 ADJ （目光）茫然的，呆滞的 If someone's eyes are unfocused, they are open, but not looking at anything. （目光）茫然的，呆滞的

  • Her eyes were unfocused, as if she were staring inside at her memories of the day.

    她目光呆滞，仿佛沉浸在那天的记忆当中。

  • ...his unfocused gaze.

    他茫然的凝视

2 ADJ-GRADED （情绪、计划等）漫无目的的，不着边际的 If you describe someone's feelings or plans as unfocused, you are criticizing them because they do not seem to be clearly formed or have any clear purpose. （情绪、计划等）漫无目的的，不着边际的 [disapproval]

  • But for now, she is in the grip of a blind, unfocused anger.

    而现在，她陷入了一种无名的愤怒之中。

  • It is not perhaps surprising that the administration now appears so indecisive and unfocused.

    政府现在优柔寡断、毫无章法，这或许并不奇怪。
