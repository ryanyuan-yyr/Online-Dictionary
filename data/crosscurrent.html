cross-current
Word forms: plural cross-currents
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 crosscurrent
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (穿过其他水流的)交叉水流，逆流 A cross-current is a current in a river or sea that flows across another current. (穿过其他水流的)交叉水流，逆流

  • Cross-currents can sweep the strongest swimmer helplessly away.

    即使最强壮的游泳者也会无助地被交叉水流冲走。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 不同的观点(或传统) You can refer to conflicting ideas or traditions as cross-currents . 不同的观点(或传统)

  • ...the cross-currents within the Conservative Party.

    保守党内观点迥异
