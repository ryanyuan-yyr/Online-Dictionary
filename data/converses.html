converse
kənvɜːʳs kɒnvɜːʳs ˈkɑnvɜrs kənˈvɜrs ˈkɑnˌvɜrs .
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense converses, present participle conversing, past tense , past participle conversed
The verb is pronounced /kən'vɜːs/. The noun is pronounced /'kɒnvɜːs/. 动词读作 /kən'vɜːs/，名词读作 /'kɒnvɜːs/。
1 V-RECIP (与…)交谈;(和…)说话 If you converse with someone, you talk to them. You can also say that two people converse . (与…)交谈;(和…)说话 [formal]

  • Luke sat directly behind the pilot and conversed with him. [VERB + with]

    卢克就坐在飞行员后面，并且和他说着话。

  • They were conversing in German, their only common language. [VERB]

    他们正用德语交谈，这是他们唯一的共同语言。

SYN talk, speak, chat, communicate  
2 N-SING 相反的声明;相反的话;相反的言论 The converse of a statement is its opposite or reverse. 相反的声明;相反的话;相反的言论 [formal]

  • What you do for a living is critical to where you settle and how you live — and the converse is also true.

    一个人的谋生手段对于其居住地的选择和生活方式来说有着至关重要的影响，反之亦然。

SYN opposite, reverse, contrary, other side of the coin
