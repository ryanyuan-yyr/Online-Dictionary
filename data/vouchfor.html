vouch for
1 PHRASAL VERB 为…担保(或保证) If you say that you can or will vouch for someone, you mean that you can guarantee their good behaviour. 为…担保(或保证)

  • Kim's mother agreed to vouch for Maria and get her a job. [VERB PREP. noun]

    金的母亲同意为玛丽亚担保，好让她找份工作。

2 PHRASAL VERB 证明;证实 If you say that you can vouch for something, you mean that you have evidence from your own personal experience that it is true or correct. 证明;证实

  • He cannot vouch for the accuracy of the story. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他不能证明这一说法的准确性。
