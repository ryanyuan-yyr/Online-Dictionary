hot-foot
(also hotfoot)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hot-foots, present participle hot-footing, past tense , past participle hot-footed
VERB 急匆匆地走;火速赶路 If you hot-foot it somewhere, you go there in a hurry. 急匆匆地走;火速赶路 [informal]

  • ...a group of actors hot-footing it for the bar.

    一群急匆匆赶往酒吧的演员
