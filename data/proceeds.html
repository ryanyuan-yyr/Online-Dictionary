proceed
prəsiːd proʊsiːdz prəˈsiːd proʊˈsid prəˈsid
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense proceeds, present participle proceeding, past tense , past participle proceeded
The verb is pronounced /prə'siːd/. The plural noun in meaning 5 is pronounced /'prəʊsiːdz/. 动词读作 /prə'siːd/，义项5的复数名词读作 /'prəʊsiːdz/。
1 VERB 接下来做；接着做 If you proceed to do something, you do it, often after doing something else first. 接下来做；接着做

  • He proceeded to tell me of my birth. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他接着给我讲我出生的事。

  • He asked for ice for his whiskey and proceeded to get contentedly drunk. [VERB to-infinitive]

    他要求在威士忌里加冰，接着心满意足地喝了个酩酊大醉。

2 VERB 继续进行；继续做 If you proceed with a course of action, you continue with it. 继续进行；继续做 [formal]

  • The group proceeded with a march they knew would lead to bloodshed. [VERB + with]

    这个团体继续示威游行，他们知道这将导致流血事件的发生。

  • The trial has been delayed until November because the defence is not ready to proceed. [VERB]

    因为被告方没有做好应诉的准备，审判已经被延期至11月份。

SYN continue, go on, progress, carry on  
3 VERB （活动、过程或事件）继续，持续，连续不断 If an activity, process, or event proceeds, it goes on and does not stop. （活动、过程或事件）继续，持续，连续不断

  • The ideas were not new. Their development had proceeded steadily since the war. [VERB]

    这些并不是什么新思想，自从战争开始就一直稳定地发展演变。

  • Efforts to reform the Interior Ministry have not yet proceeded very far. [VERB]

    对内政部进行改革的尝试还没有取得很大进展。

4 VERB （朝某个方向）前进，行进 If you proceed in a particular direction, you go in that direction. （朝某个方向）前进，行进 [formal]

  • She climbed the steps and proceeded along the upstairs hallway. [VERB prep./adv.]

    她爬上楼梯，顺着楼上的走廊走去。

  • The freighter was allowed to proceed after satisfying them that it was not breaking sanctions. [VERB]

    在确认没有违反制裁条例后，货船被放行了。

SYN go on, continue, advance, progress  
5 N-PLURAL （某个事件或活动的）收入，收益 The proceeds of an event or activity are the money that has been obtained from it. （某个事件或活动的）收入，收益

  • The proceeds of the arms sales were then funneled to Contra fighters in Central America.

    出售武器所得的收入随后用来支持了中美洲的反政府武装组织。

  • The proceeds from the concert will go towards famine relief.

    音乐会的收入将用于救助饥荒。
