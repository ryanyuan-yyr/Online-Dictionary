wrapped up
ADJ-GRADED 专心致志的；全神贯注的；完全沉浸的 If someone is wrapped up in a particular person or thing, they spend nearly all their time thinking about them, so that they forget about other things which may be important. 专心致志的；全神贯注的；完全沉浸的 [Also + with]

  • He's too serious and dedicated, wrapped up in his career. [+ in]

    他太严肃、太专注，一心扑在事业上。

  • New mothers can get very wrapped up in their baby without realising it.

    初为人母者很容易把心思全放在婴儿身上而浑然不觉。

SYN absorbed (in), lost (in), involved (in), preoccupied (with)
