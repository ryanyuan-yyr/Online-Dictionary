cost-plus
ˈkɔstˈplʌs
ADJ [ADJ n] 成本加利润的 A cost-plus basis for a contract about work to be done is one in which the buyer agrees to pay the seller or contractor all the cost plus a profit. 成本加利润的

  • All vessels were to be built on a cost-plus basis.

    所有的船只都将在成本加利润的基础上制造。

  • ...cost-plus contracts.

    成本加成合同
