printing
prɪntɪŋ ˈprɪntɪŋ
Word forms: plural printings
1 N-COUNT （书籍的）一次印刷 If copies of a book are printed and published on a number of different occasions, you can refer to each of these occasions as a printing. （书籍的）一次印刷

  • The American edition of 'Cloud Street' is already in its third printing.

    美国版的《云街》已经是第三次印刷了。
