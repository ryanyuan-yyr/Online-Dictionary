breeding
briːdɪŋ bridɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 教养 If someone says that a person has breeding, they mean that they think the person is from a good social background and has good manners. 教养

  • It's a sign of good breeding to know the names of all your staff.

    知道所有员工的名字是有良好教养的一种体现。

  • ...men of low birth and no breeding.

    出身低微、缺乏教养的男人们

SYN refinement, style, culture, taste
