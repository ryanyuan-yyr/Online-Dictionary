resentful
rɪzentfʊl rɪˈzɛntfəl
ADJ-GRADED 怨恨的;愤慨的;憎恨的 If you are resentful, you feel resentment. 怨恨的;愤慨的;憎恨的

  • At first I felt very resentful and angry about losing my job. [+ about]

    起初我对丢掉工作感到非常怨恨和恼怒。

  • He turned away in sullen, resentful silence.

    他闷闷不乐、心怀怨恨、一言不发地转过身去了。

SYN bitter, hurt, wounded, angry  
resentfully

  • For a moment she continued to look at him resentfully.

    她怨恨地看了他一会儿。
