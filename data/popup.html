pop up
See also: pop-up  突然出现；不意冒出 If someone or something pops up, they appear in a place or situation unexpectedly. 突然出现；不意冒出 [informal]

  • She was startled when Lisa popped up at the door all smiles. [VERB PREP.]

    莉萨突然笑容满面地出现在门口时，她吓了一跳。

  • You solved one problem and another would immediately pop up. [VERB PREP.]

    你解决完一个问题，另一个问题又会马上冒出来。

SYN appear, emerge, turn up, show up [informal]  
pop-up
ˈpɑpˌʌp
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （书、通常指童书打开时）有跳起立体图片的 A pop-up book, usually a child- ren's book, has pictures that stand up when you open the pages. （书、通常指童书打开时）有跳起立体图片的
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （烤面包片机）自动跳起式的 A pop-up toaster has a mechanism that pushes slices of bread up when they are toasted. （烤面包片机）自动跳起式的
3 ADJ [ADJ n] （显示屏上的菜单或广告）弹出式的 On a computer screen, a pop-up menu or advertisement is a small window containing a menu or advertisement that appears on the screen when you perform particular operations. （显示屏上的菜单或广告）弹出式的 [computing]

  • ...a program for stopping pop-up ads.

    阻止弹出式广告的程序
