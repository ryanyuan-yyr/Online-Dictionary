bijou
biːʒuː ˈbiˌʒu biˈʒu
ADJ [ADJ n] （房屋）小巧雅致的，精巧的 Small houses are sometimes described as bijou houses in order to make them sound attractive or fashionable. （房屋）小巧雅致的，精巧的

  • ...a bijou Mayfair flat.

    伦敦梅费尔区精巧的小公寓

  • ...bijou shops and boutiques.

    精品店和时装屋

SYN desirable, pleasing, elegant, fashionable
