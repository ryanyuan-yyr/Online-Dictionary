circumference
səʳkʌmfrəns sərˈkʌmfərəns sərˈkʌmfrəns
1 N-UNCOUNT (圆、地方、圆状物的)周长 The circumference of a circle, place, or round object is the distance around its edge. (圆、地方、圆状物的)周长

  • ...a scientist calculating the earth's circumference.

    计算地球周长的科学家

  • The island is 3.5 km in circumference.

    这个岛周长3.5千米。

2 N-UNCOUNT (圆、地方、圆状物的)边缘，周缘 The circumference of a circle, place, or round object is its edge. (圆、地方、圆状物的)边缘，周缘

  • Cut the salmon into long strips and wrap it round the circumference of the bread. [+ of]

    把鲑鱼切成长条，缠裹在面包上。
