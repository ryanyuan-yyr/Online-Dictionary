prosthesis
prɒsθiːsɪs ˈprɒsθɪsɪs ˈprɑsθəsɪs prɑsˈθisɪs
Word forms: plural prostheses
N-COUNT 假体 A prosthesis is an artificial body part that is used to replace a natural part. 假体 [medicine]

  • The woman whose leg had been amputated could get a prosthesis and learn to walk on it.

    那个腿部被截肢的女人可以装假肢并学着用它来行走。
