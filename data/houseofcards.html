house of cards
N-SING [usu a N] 可能失败的计划If you say that a system, organization, or plan is a house of cards, you mean that it is likely to fail or collapse. 可能失败的计划

  • The film is a house of cards. If one scene fails to hold, the entire credibility of the film comes crashing down.

    这部电影可能失败，如果一个场景失败了，那整个电影的可信度就崩溃了。
