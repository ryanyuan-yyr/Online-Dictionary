bloodsucker
blʌdsʌkəʳ ˈblʌdˌsʌkər
Word forms: plural bloodsuckers
1 N-COUNT 吸血动物；吸血虫 A bloodsucker is any creature that sucks blood from a wound that it has made in an animal or person. 吸血动物；吸血虫
2 N-COUNT 吸血鬼；剥削者 If you call someone a bloodsucker, you disapprove of them because you think they do not do anything worthwhile but live off the efforts of other people. 吸血鬼；剥削者 [disapproval]

  • At last he was free from the financial bloodsuckers.

    最后他终于摆脱了那些吸金者的经济剥削。
