indifference
ɪndɪfərəns ɪnˈdɪfərəns ɪnˈdɪfrəns
N-UNCOUNT 不感兴趣;不关心;冷淡 If you accuse someone of indifference to something, you mean that they have a complete lack of interest in it. 不感兴趣;不关心;冷淡

  • ...his callous indifference to the plight of his son. [+ to]

    他对儿子所处困境的无情漠视

  • ...the prejudice and indifference which surround the Aids epidemic.

    对艾滋病传播的偏见与漠视

SYN disregard, apathy, lack of interest, negligence
