soup
suːp sup
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural soups, present participle souping, past tense , past participle souped
1 N-MASS 汤;羹 Soup is liquid food made by boiling meat, fish, or vegetables in water. 汤;羹

  • ...home-made chicken soup.

    家常的鸡汤

SYN broth, bisque, chowder, consommé  
2 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb, verb-link PHRASE] 处于困境;处境尴尬 If you say that someone is in the soup, you mean they are in trouble. 处于困境;处境尴尬 [journalism, informal]

  • She has a knack of landing herself right in the soup.

    她老是让自己的处境很尴尬。

souped-up

  • ...a souped-up Peugeot 205.

    改装过的标致 205 型汽车

  • ...a souped-up version of the theme from 'The Generation Game'.

    主题取自《世代游戏》的加强版本
