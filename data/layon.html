lay on
PHRASAL VERB （尤指慷慨或盛情地）提供（食物、娱乐或服务） If you lay on something such as food, entertainment, or a service, you provide or supply it, especially in a generous or grand way. （尤指慷慨或盛情地）提供（食物、娱乐或服务） [mainly British] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • They laid on a superb evening. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们办了一场非常精彩的晚会。

  • Every facility was laid on to ease their homecoming. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    动用了一切设施以缓解他们的回乡之困。

SYN provide, prepare, supply, organize
