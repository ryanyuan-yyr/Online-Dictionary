hot spot
ˈhɒtˌspɒt
(also hotspot)Word forms: plural hot spots
1 N-COUNT 喧嚣之地;热闹的娱乐场所 You can refer to an exciting place where there is a lot of activity or entertainment as a hot spot . 喧嚣之地;热闹的娱乐场所 [informal]

  • ...a fancy Manhattan hot spot.

    曼哈顿一家热闹喧哗的高级娱乐场所

  • ...a popular and lively package tour hotspot.

    广受欢迎、意趣盎然的包价旅游热点

2 N-COUNT （发生战斗或政治动乱的）热点地区，多事之地 You can refer to an area where there is fighting or serious political trouble as a hot spot. （发生战斗或政治动乱的）热点地区，多事之地 [journalism]

  • There were many hot spots in the region, where fighting had been going on.

    该地区有很多处于交火状态的热点。
