overrate
oʊvəʳreɪt ˌoʊvərˈreɪt
(also over-rate)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overrates, present participle overrating, past tense , past participle overrated
VERB 对…评价过高；高估 If you say that something or someone is overrated, you mean that people have a higher opinion of them than they deserve. 对…评价过高；高估

  • More men are finding out that the joys of work have been overrated. [be VERB-ed]

    越来越多的男性认识到工作并没有预想的那样有乐趣。

  • If you consider him a miracle man, you're overrating him. [VERB noun]

    如果你认为他能创造奇迹，那你是高估他了。

SYN overestimate, glorify, overvalue, oversell  
overrated

    在他人眼中，成功常常被夸大为了不起的成就。
