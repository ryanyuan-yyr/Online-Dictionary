step back
PHRASAL VERB 退一步，置身事外(思考) If you step back and think about a situation, you think about it as if you were not involved in it. 退一步，置身事外(思考)

  • I stepped back and analysed the situation. [VERB PREP.]

    我抽身出来，从局外人的角度分析了形势。

  • It was necessary to step back from the project and look at it as a whole. [VERB PREP. + from]

    有必要退一步，全面地审视一下该项目。
