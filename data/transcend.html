transcend
trænsend trænˈsɛnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense transcends, present participle transcending, past tense , past participle transcended
VERB 超越;超出 Something that transcends normal limits or boundaries goes beyond them, because it is more significant than them. 超越;超出

  • ...issues like European union that transcend party loyalty. [VERB noun]

    诸如欧洲结盟这样超越党派忠诚的议题

SYN surpass, exceed, go beyond, rise above
