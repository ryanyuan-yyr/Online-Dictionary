antipathy
æntɪpəθi ænˈtɪpəθi
N-UNCOUNT 憎恶；反感 Antipathy is a strong feeling of dislike or hostility towards someone or something. 憎恶；反感 [formal]

  • She'd often spoken of her antipathy towards London.

    她常提及她对伦敦的反感。

SYN hostility, opposition, disgust, dislike
