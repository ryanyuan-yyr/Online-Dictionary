hinge on
PHRASAL VERB 有赖于;取决于 Something that hinges on one thing or event depends entirely on it. 有赖于;取决于

  • The plan hinges on a deal being struck with a new company. [V P n/v-ing/wh]

    该计划完全取决于正在和一家新公司洽谈着的一项交易。

  • A lot hinges on how we are viewed by overseas investors. [V P n/-ing/wh]

    很多事都取决于海外投资者对我们的看法。
