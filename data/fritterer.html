fritter
frɪtəʳ ˈfrɪtər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fritters, present participle frittering, past tense , past participle frittered
1 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 油炸馅饼 Fritters are round pieces of fruit, vegetables, or meat that are dipped in batter and fried. 油炸馅饼

  • ...apple fritters.

    苹果油炸馅饼

2 PHRASAL VERB 浪费;挥霍 If someone fritters away time or money, they waste it on unimportant or unnecessary things. 浪费;挥霍

  • The firm soon started frittering away the cash it was generating.

    这家公司不久就开始挥霍赚来的钱。

  • I seem to fritter my time away at coffee mornings.

    我似乎把时间全都浪费在咖啡早茶会上了。

Phrasal verbs:
fritter away
