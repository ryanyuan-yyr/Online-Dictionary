banter
bæntəʳ ˈbæntər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense banters, present participle bantering, past tense , past participle bantered
1 N-UNCOUNT （善意的）取笑，戏谑，打趣 Banter is teasing or joking talk that is amusing and friendly. （善意的）取笑，戏谑，打趣

  • She heard Tom exchanging good-natured banter with Jane.

    她听见汤姆和简互相打趣。

SYN joking, kidding [informal] , ribbing [informal] , teasing  
2 V-RECIP （善意地和某人）开玩笑，打趣 If you banter with someone, you tease them or joke with them in an amusing, friendly way. （善意地和某人）开玩笑，打趣

  • The soldiers bantered with him as though he was a kid brother. [VERB + with]

    士兵们把他当作弟弟一样和他开着玩笑。

  • We bantered a bit while I tried to get the car started. [VERB]

    我试图发动车子的时候我们互相打趣了几句。

  • All this was said in a bantering tone. [VERB-ing]

    这些都是当玩笑话说的。

SYN joke, kid [informal] , rib [informal] , tease
