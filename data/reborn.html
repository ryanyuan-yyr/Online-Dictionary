reborn
riːbɔːʳn riːˈbɔːn riˈbɔrn
V-PASSIVE 重生;复兴;新生 If you say that someone or something has been reborn, you mean that they have become active again after a period of being inactive. 重生;复兴;新生

  • Shilling has been reborn as an artist. [be V-ed as n]

    希林以艺术家的身份再次出现。

  • Russia was being reborn as a great power. [be VERB-ed + as]

    俄罗斯经过重生成为强国。
