mental
mentəl ˈmɛntəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 智力的;脑力的;思考的 Mental means relating to the process of thinking. 智力的;脑力的;思考的

  • ...the mental development of children.

    儿童的智力发展

  • ...intensive mental effort.

    高强度的脑力劳动

SYN intellectual, rational, theoretical, cognitive  
mentally

  • I think you are mentally tired.

    我想你的脑子累了。

  • I had never felt more mentally alive.

    我从未感到思维如此活跃。

  • Physically I might not have been overseas but mentally and spiritually I was with them.

    虽然我人不在国外，可是从精神和心灵上，我和他们是在一起的。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 心理上的;精神上的 Mental means relating to the state or the health of a person's mind. 心理上的;精神上的

  • The mental state that had created her psychosis was no longer present.

    导致她精神失常的那种心理状态已经消失了。

  • ...mental health problems.

    心理健康问题

SYN psychiatric, psychogenic  
mentally

  • ...an inmate who is mentally disturbed.

    精神失常的囚犯

  • ...the needs of the mentally ill and the mentally handicapped.

    精神病患者与智障人士的需要

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 内心的;在头脑中进行的 A mental act is one that involves only thinking and not physical action. 内心的;在头脑中进行的

  • Practise mental arithmetic when you go out shopping.

    外出购物的时候练习一下心算。

  • Graham made a quick mental calculation.

    格雷厄姆很快就心算出来了。

mentally

  • This technique will help people mentally organize information.

    这项技术将帮助人们在脑子里组织信息。

4 ADJ-GRADED 疯狂的;发疯的 If you say that someone is mental, you mean that you think they are mad. 疯狂的;发疯的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • I just said to him 'you must be mental'.

    我刚才对他说：“你一定是疯了。”

SYN insane, mad, disturbed, unstable  
5 PHRASE 记在脑子里;把…牢记在心 If you make a mental note of something, you make an effort to store it in your memory so that you will not forget it. 记在脑子里;把…牢记在心

  • She made a mental note to have his prescription refilled.

    她记着要拿他的处方再配一回药。

  • She made a mental note not to sit anywhere near him.

    她心里牢牢记着不坐在他的附近。
