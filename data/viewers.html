viewer
vjuːəʳ vjuər
Word forms: plural viewers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 电视（节目）观众 Viewers are people who watch television, or who are watching a particular programme on television. 电视（节目）观众

  • These programmes are each watched by around 19 million viewers every week.

    这些电视节目的观众各自都在每周1,900万左右。

SYN watcher, observer, spectator, onlooker  
2 N-COUNT 观看者;观察者 A viewer is someone who is looking carefully at a picture or other interesting object. 观看者;观察者

  • ... the relationship between the art object and the viewer.

    艺术品与其观赏者之间的关系

3 N-COUNT （看幻灯片的）观片器，看片器 A viewer is a device shaped like a box with a lens in one side. It is used for looking at transparent colour photographs. （看幻灯片的）观片器，看片器

  • This special viewer is simple to use and comes with 70 full-colour slides.

    这个特别的观片器操作简单，配有70张全彩幻灯片。
