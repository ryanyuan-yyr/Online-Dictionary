ADJ-GRADED 苍白的;有倦容的;疲惫的 If you describe someone as wan, you mean that they look pale and tired. 苍白的;有倦容的;疲惫的 [literary]

  • He looked wan and tired.

    他脸色苍白，面带倦容。

  • All she could manage was a thin, wan smile.

    她只能挤出一丝疲惫的笑容。

SYN pale, white, washed out, pasty  
wanly

  • Marcia smiled wanly and shook her head.

    马西娅苍白无力地一笑，摇了摇头。
