anatomize
ənætəmaɪz əˈnætəˌmaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense anatomizes, present participle anatomizing, past tense , past participle anatomized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 anatomise
VERB 详细分析；剖析 If you anatomise a subject or an issue, you examine it in great detail. 详细分析；剖析 [formal]

  • The magazine is devoted to anato-mizing the inadequacies of liberalism. [VERB noun]

    该杂志致力于剖析自由主义的不足之处。

SYN examine, study, separate, divide
