identity crisis
aɪˈdɛntɪtɪ ˈkraɪsɪs
Word forms: identity crises
N-COUNT [usu sing: an N] 身份危机If you say that someone or something is having an identity crisis, you mean that it is not clear what kind of person or thing they are, or what kind of person or thing they would like to be. 身份危机

  • After Terminator 2, however, Arnie suffered a mid-career identity crisis and got mixed up in comedy.

    在《终结者2》之后，阿尼遭遇到一次职业生涯中期的身份危机，与戏剧搅和到了一起。

  • ...another organization with an identity crisis; its very existence is a matter of dispute.

    ...另一个面临身份危机的组织；它的自身生存都是一件有争议的事情。
