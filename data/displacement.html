displacement
dɪspleɪsmənt dɪsˈpleɪsmənt ˈdɪsˈpleɪsmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT 取代;代替 Displacement is the removal of something from its usual place or position by something which then occupies that place or position. 取代;代替 [formal]

  • ...the displacement of all my energy into caring for the baby.

    我所有精力都放在了照顾宝宝上

  • ...too much resistance to the displacement of your reason by your emotions.

    有太多阻力使你无法用情感代替理智

SYN replacement, substitution, superseding, ousting  
2 N-UNCOUNT 迫使迁徙;迫使背井离乡 Displacement is the forcing of people away from the area or country where they live. 迫使迁徙;迫使背井离乡

  • ...the gradual displacement of the American Indian.

    美洲印第安人被迫逐渐迁徙

SYN dispersal, spread, scattering, distribution  
3 N-UNCOUNT (船的)排量,排水量 Displacement is the weight or volume of a liquid that is displaced by an object under the surface or floating on it, for example the weight of water displaced by a ship. (船的)排量,排水量 [technical]
