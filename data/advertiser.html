advertiser
ædvəʳtaɪzəʳ
Word forms: plural advertisers
1 N-COUNT 登广告者；广告客户 An advertiser is a person or company that pays for a product, event, or job to be advertised in a newspaper, on television, or on a poster. 登广告者；广告客户
2 N-IN-NAMES (用于某些地方报纸的名称中) Advertiser is used in the name of some local newspapers. (用于某些地方报纸的名称中)

  • ...the Croydon Advertiser.

    《克罗伊登广告报》
