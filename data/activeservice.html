active service
N-UNCOUNT [oft on NOUN] 现役；战时服役 Someone who is on active service is taking part in a war as a member of the armed forces. 现役；战时服役 [mainly British]

  • In April 1944 he was killed on active service.

    1944年4月他在战时服役中阵亡。

  • The colonel saw active service in Borneo and was awarded the General Service Medal.

    战争期间上校在婆罗洲服役并被授予一般服役奖章。
