fourth
fɔːʳθ fɔrθ foʊrθ
Word forms: plural fourths
1 ORD 第四 The fourth item in a series is the one that you count as number four. 第四

  • Last year's winner Greg Lemond of the United States is in fourth place.

    去年的冠军、来自美国的格雷格·莱蒙德现在排名第 4。

2 FRACTION 四分之一 A fourth is one of four equal parts of something. 四分之一 [US]

  • Three-fourths of the public say they favor a national referendum on the issue.

    四分之三的公众表示希望就这个问题进行全民公决。
