breach of the peace
Word forms: plural breaches of the peace
N-VAR 扰乱治安 A breach of the peace is noisy or violent behaviour in a public place which is illegal because it disturbs other people. 扰乱治安 [law]

  • He admitted causing a breach of the peace.

    他承认扰乱了治安。

  • Four men were found guilty of breach of the peace.

    四名男子被判扰乱治安罪名成立。
