wired
waɪəʳd waɪəd waɪrd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 紧张的;不安的;神经紧绷的 If someone is wired, they are tense, nervous, and unable to relax. 紧张的;不安的;神经紧绷的 [mainly US, informal]

  • Tonight he is manic, wired and uptight.

    今晚，他躁动不安，紧张兮兮的。

2 ADJ 联网的;有上网设备的 A computer, organization, or person that is wired has the equipment that is necessary to use the Internet. 联网的;有上网设备的 [informal]

  • Once more people are wired, the potential to change the mainstream media will be huge.

    一旦更多人能上网，网络改变主流媒体的潜力将十分巨大。

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (材料或衣服)内带金属丝的，夹丝的 Wired is used to describe material or clothing that has wires sewn into it in order to keep it stiff. (材料或衣服)内带金属丝的，夹丝的

  • ...a length of wired ribbon.

    一段夹丝的丝带
