civilian
sɪvɪliən sɪˈvɪljən səˈvɪljən
Word forms: plural civilians
1 N-COUNT 平民;老百姓 In a military situation, a civilian is anyone who is not a member of the armed forces. 平民;老百姓

  • The safety of civilians caught up in the fighting must be guaranteed.

    必须保证陷入战斗中的平民的人身安全。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 民用的;平民的;非军用的 In a military situation, civilian is used to describe people or things that are not military. 民用的;平民的;非军用的

  • ...the country's civilian population.

    该国的平民人口

  • ...civilian casualties.

    平民伤亡

  • ...a soldier in civilian clothes.

    穿便装的士兵
