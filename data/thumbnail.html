thumbnail
θʌmneɪl ˈθʌmˌneɪl
(also thumb-nail)Word forms: plural thumbnails
1 N-COUNT 拇指甲 Your thumbnail is the nail on your thumb. 拇指甲
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 简要的;概括的;简略的 A thumbnail sketch or account is a very short description of an event, idea, or plan which gives only the main details. 简要的;概括的;简略的
SYN brief, short, concise, quick
