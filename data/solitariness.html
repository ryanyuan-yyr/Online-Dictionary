solitary
sɒlɪtri -teri ˈsɑləˌtɛri
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人或动物)独处的，独居的 A person or animal that is solitary spends a lot of time alone. (人或动物)独处的，独居的

  • Paul was a shy, pleasant, solitary man.

    保罗是一个羞怯、友善、惯于独处的男人。

  • They often have a lonely and solitary life to lead.

    他们常常过一种独居的孤独生活。

SYN unsociable, retiring, reclusive, unsocial  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] (行动)单独的，独自的 A solitary activity is one that you do alone. (行动)单独的，独自的

  • His evenings were spent in solitary drinking.

    他晚上的时间都是靠独自喝闷酒打发。

SYN lone, alone  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (人或物)孤单的，孤零零的 A solitary person or object is alone, with no others near them. (人或物)孤单的，孤零零的

  • ...the occasional solitary figure making a study of wildflowers or grasses.

    偶尔出现的研究野花野草的孤单身影

4 N-UNCOUNT [usually in NOUN] 同 solitary confinement Solitary is the same as solitary confinement . 同 solitary confinement [informal]

  • Tom was in solitary across the way from me.

    汤姆在我对面被单独监禁。

SYN hermit, loner [informal] , recluse, introvert
