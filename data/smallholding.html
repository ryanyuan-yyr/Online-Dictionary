smallholding
smɔːlhoʊldɪŋ ˈsmɔlˌhoʊldɪŋ
Word forms: plural smallholdings
N-COUNT (比一般农场更小的)一块耕地;小块农田 A smallholding is a piece of land that is used for farming and is smaller than a normal farm. (比一般农场更小的)一块耕地;小块农田 [British]

  • A smallholding in the hills could not support a large family.

    山间的一小块耕地是不可能养活一大家人的。
