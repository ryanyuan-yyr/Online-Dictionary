stringy
strɪŋi ˈstrɪŋi
Word forms: comparative stringier, superlative stringiest
1 ADJ-GRADED (食物)多筋的，纤维多而不嫩的，柴的 Stringy food contains long, thin pieces that are difficult or unpleasant to eat. (食物)多筋的，纤维多而不嫩的，柴的

  • The meat was stringy.

    这肉嚼不烂。

  • Thankfully, it wasn't smothered in stringy cheese like some pizzas.

    幸好它不像有些比萨饼那样上面涂满了难嚼的奶酪。

SYN fibrous, tough, chewy, sinewy  
2 ADJ-GRADED (头发)干枯的，稀疏的 Stringy hair is thin and unattractive. (头发)干枯的，稀疏的

  • ...an enormously fat man with long, stringy gray hair.

    留着稀疏的灰白长发、身形臃肿的男子

SYN straggly, thin, straggling, lank
