illness
ɪlnəs ˈɪlnɪs
Word forms: plural illnesses
1 N-UNCOUNT 疾病;患病(期) Illness is the fact or experience of being ill. 疾病;患病(期)

  • If your child shows any signs of illness, take her to the doctor.

    如果您的孩子出现任何患病症状,带她去看医生。

  • Mental illness is still a taboo subject.

    精神疾病仍然是禁忌话题。

2 N-COUNT (特指某种)疾病 An illness is a particular disease such as measles or pneumonia. (特指某种)疾病

  • She returned to her family home to recover from an illness.

    她回家养病来了。

SYN sickness, ill health, malaise, attack
