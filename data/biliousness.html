bilious
bɪliəs ˈbɪljəs
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 令人不快的；让人恶心的 If someone describes the appearance of something as bilious, they mean that they think it looks unpleasant and rather disgusting. 令人不快的；让人恶心的 [written, disapproval]

  • ...a bilious shade of green.

    让人感觉不好的一种绿色

2 ADJ 感觉不舒服的；恶心头痛的 If you feel bilious, you feel sick and have a headache. 感觉不舒服的；恶心头痛的

  • She is suffering a bilious attack.

    她觉得恶心头痛。

SYN sick, nauseated, queasy, out of sorts  
3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 暴躁的；狂怒的；脾气坏的 Bilious is sometimes used to describe the feelings or behaviour of someone who is extremely angry or bad-tempered. 暴躁的；狂怒的；脾气坏的 [written]

  • His speech was a bilious, rancorous attack on young people.

    他的讲话是对年轻人满怀敌意的狂暴攻击。
