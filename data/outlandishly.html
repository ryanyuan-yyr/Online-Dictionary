outlandish
aʊtlændɪʃ ˌaʊtˈlændɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED 稀奇古怪的；异乎寻常的；不切实际的 If you describe something as outlandish, you disapprove of it because you think it is very unusual, strange, or unreasonable. 稀奇古怪的；异乎寻常的；不切实际的 [disapproval]

  • They appeared at parties in outlandish clothes.

    他们穿着奇装异服参加各种聚会。

  • This idea is not as outlandish as it sounds.

    这主意听上去荒诞不经，实际并非如此。

SYN strange, odd, extraordinary, wonderful
