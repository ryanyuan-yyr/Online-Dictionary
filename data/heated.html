heated
hiːtɪd ˈhitɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED （讨论或争吵）激烈的 A heated discussion or quarrel is one where the people involved are angry and excited. （讨论或争吵）激烈的

  • It was a very heated argument and they were shouting at each other.

    争论非常激烈，他们相互之间大喊大叫。

  • Our discussions were rather heated.

    我们的讨论相当激烈。

SYN impassioned, intense, spirited, excited  
2 ADJ-GRADED 发怒的;激动的 If someone gets heated about something, they get angry and excited about it. 发怒的;激动的

  • You will understand that people get a bit heated about issues such as these. [+ about/over]

    你要明白，对于这类问题人们不免会有些激动。

  • The baron became increasingly heated over the hypocrisy of it all.

    男爵对整件事的虚伪感到越来越生气。

SYN wound up, worked up, keyed up, het up [informal]  
heatedly

  • The crowd continued to argue heatedly about the best way to tackle the problem.

    这群人继续激烈地争论解决问题的最佳办法。
