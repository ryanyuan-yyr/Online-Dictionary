bare-faced
(also barefaced)
ADJ [ADJ n] 厚颜无耻的；公然的；露骨的 You use bare-faced to describe someone's behaviour when you want to emphasize that they do not care that they are behaving wrongly. 厚颜无耻的；公然的；露骨的 [emphasis]

  • What bare-faced cheek!

    真是厚颜无耻！

  • ...crooked politicians who tell bare-faced lies.

    满嘴无耻谎言的奸诈政客

SYN flagrant, open, obvious, naked
