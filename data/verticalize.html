vertical
vɜːʳtɪkəl ˈvɜrtɪkəl
Word forms: plural verticals
1 ADJ 垂直的;直立的 Something that is vertical stands or points straight up. 垂直的;直立的

  • The climber inched up a vertical wall of rock.

    攀登者慢慢爬上了陡直的石壁。

  • The gadget can be attached to any vertical or near vertical surface.

    该装置可以装在任何垂直或近似垂直的平面上。

SYN upright, sheer, perpendicular, straight (up and down)  
vertically

  • Cut each bulb in half vertically.

    将每个球茎竖切成两半。

2 N-SING 垂直方向 The vertical is the direction that points straight up, at an angle of 90 degrees to a flat surface. 垂直方向

  • Pluto seems to have suffered a major collision that tipped it 122 degrees from the vertical.

    冥王星似乎受到过严重撞击，倾斜了122度。

3 N-COUNT 垂直线;垂直面 A vertical is a line or structure that is vertical. 垂直线;垂直面

  • As long as the verticals align, the design will look regular.

    只要所有垂直面在一条线上，图案看起来就会很整齐。
