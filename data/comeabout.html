come about
PHRASAL VERB 产生;发生 When you say how or when something came about, you say how or when it happened. 产生;发生

  • Any possible solution to the Irish question can only come about through dialogue. [V P + through]

    任何解决爱尔兰问题的可行方案只能通过对话产生。

  • That came about when we went to Glastonbury last year. [VERB PREP.]

    那是我们去年去格拉斯顿伯里的时候发生的事。

  • Thus it came about that, after many years as an interior designer and antiques dealer, he combined both businesses. [VERB PREP. that]

    这样，在从事多年的室内设计和古董经营后，他把二者结合了起来。

SYN happen, result, occur, take place
