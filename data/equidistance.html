equidistant
iːkwɪdɪstənt ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstənt ˌikwɪˈdɪstənt
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 等距的 A place that is equidistant from two other places is the same distance away from each of these places. 等距的

  • Horsey is equidistant from Great Yarmouth and Mundesley. [+ from/between]

    从霍西到大雅茅斯和曼斯利的距离相等。
