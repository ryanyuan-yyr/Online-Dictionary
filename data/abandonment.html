abandonment
əbændənmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT 离弃；遗弃；抛弃 The abandonment of a place, thing, or person is the act of leaving it permanently or for a long time, especially when you should not do so. 离弃；遗弃；抛弃

  • ...memories of her father's complete abandonment of her. [+ of]

    对父亲完全弃她不顾的回忆

SYN desertion, leaving, forsaking, jilting  
2 N-UNCOUNT （工作、活动等的）中途放弃，中止 The abandonment of a piece of work or activity is the act of stopping doing it before it is finished. （工作、活动等的）中途放弃，中止

  • Constant rain forced the abandonment of the next day's competitions. [+ of]

    连绵阴雨致使翌日各项赛事被迫中止。

SYN stopping, cessation, discontinuation, desistance  
3 N-UNCOUNT （想法或思路的）放弃 The abandonment of an idea or way of thinking is the act of stopping having the idea or of stopping thinking in that way. （想法或思路的）放弃

  • ...the government's abandonment of the policy.

    政府对这项政策的放弃
