pirouette
pɪruet ˌpɪruˈɛt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense pirouettes, present participle pirouetting, past tense , past participle pirouetted
1 N-COUNT （芭蕾舞中的）单脚尖旋转 A pirouette is a movement in ballet dancing. The dancer stands on one foot and spins their body round fast. （芭蕾舞中的）单脚尖旋转

  • Virginia Zucci of the Russian Ballet was famous for her pirouettes.

    俄罗斯芭蕾舞团的维尔吉尼娅·祖奇以她的单脚尖旋转而著称。

SYN spin [informal] , turn, whirl, pivot  
2 VERB 作单脚尖旋转 If someone pirouettes, they perform one or more pirouettes. 作单脚尖旋转

  • She pirouetted in front of the glass. [VERB]

    她在镜前作单脚尖旋转。

SYN spin, turn, whirl, pivot
