waistline
weɪstlaɪn ˈweɪstˌlaɪn
Word forms: plural waistlines
1 N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 腰围 Your waistline is your waist measurement. 腰围

  • A passion for cooking does not necessarily have to be bad for your waistline.

    热衷于做菜并不一定会导致腰围增粗。

2 N-COUNT (衣服的)腰身 The waistline of a piece of clothing is the place where the upper and lower parts are sewn together, which is near to your waist when you wear it. (衣服的)腰身
