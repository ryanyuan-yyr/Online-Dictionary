in-patient
(also inpatient)Word forms: plural in-patients
N-COUNT 住院病人 An in-patient is someone who stays in hospital while they receive their treatment. 住院病人
In-patient is also an adjective.

  • ...in-patient treatment involving an open-heart operation.

    包含直视心外科手术的住院治疗

  • ...inpatient hospital care.

    住院护理
