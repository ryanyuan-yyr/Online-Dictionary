purgative
pɜːʳgətɪv ˈpɜrgətɪv
Word forms: plural purgatives
1 N-COUNT 泻药；泻剂；通便药物 A purgative is a medicine that causes you to get rid of unwanted waste from your body. 泻药；泻剂；通便药物 [formal]

  • The doctors attempted to reduce his high fever by inducing diarrhea with a purgative.

    医生们尝试用通便药物催泻以使他退烧。

SYN purge, laxative, cathartic, enema  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 催泻的；通便的 A purgative substance acts as a purgative. 催泻的；通便的 [formal]

  • ...purgative oils.

    通便油

  • ...a purgative tea.

    通便茶

SYN purging, cleansing, laxative, aperient [medicine]
