bribery
braɪbəri ˈbraɪbəri
N-UNCOUNT 贿赂行为；行贿 Bribery is the act of offering someone money or something valuable in order to persuade them to do something for you. 贿赂行为；行贿

  • He was jailed on charges of bribery.

    他因受到行贿指控而被捕入狱。

  • ...accusations of bribery and corruption.

    行贿和腐败的指控

SYN corruption, graft [informal] , inducement, buying off
