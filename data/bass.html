bass
beɪs bæs beɪs bæs beɪs
Word forms: plural basses
Pronounced /beɪs/ for meanings 1 to 4, and /bæs/ for meaning 5. The plural of the noun in meaning 5 is bass. 义项1到4读作 /beɪs/，义项 5 读作 /bæs/，义项 5 的复数为 bass。
1 N-COUNT 男低音歌手 A bass is a man with a very deep singing voice. 男低音歌手

  • ...the great Russian bass Chaliapin.

    伟大的俄罗斯男低音歌唱家夏利亚宾

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （鼓、吉他等乐器）低音的 A bass drum, guitar, or other musical instrument is one that produces a very deep sound. （鼓、吉他等乐器）低音的

  • ...bass guitarist Dee Murray.

    低音吉他手迪伊·默里

SYN deep, low, resonant, sonorous  
3 N-VAR 低音吉他；低音提琴 In popular music, a bass is a bass guitar or a double bass . 低音吉他；低音提琴

  • They had a bass and a piano and a sax and percussion.

    他们有一把低音吉他、一架钢琴、一管萨克斯和打击乐器。

  • ...Dave Ranson on bass and Kenneth Blevins on drums.

    戴夫·兰森弹低音吉他，肯尼思·布莱文斯打鼓

  • Kurt had started out playing bass in a rock band.

    库尔特起初在一个摇滚乐队弹低音吉他。

4 N-UNCOUNT （立体声音响或收音机等的）低音部，低音部旋钮 On a stereo system or radio, the bass is the ability to reproduce the lower musical notes. The bass is also the knob which controls this. （立体声音响或收音机等的）低音部，低音部旋钮

  • ...larger models which will then give more bass and a higher fidelity sound.

    能够听到更多低音和高保真音效的更大的型号

5 N-VAR 鲈鱼 Bass are edible fish that are found in rivers and the sea. There are several types of bass. 鲈鱼

  • They unloaded their catch of cod and bass.

    他们把捕到的鳕鱼和鲈鱼卸下来。

Bass is a piece of this fish eaten as food.（作为食物的）鲈鱼

  • ...a large fresh fillet of sea bass.

    大块的新鲜海产鲈鱼片
