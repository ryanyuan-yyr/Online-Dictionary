long-awaited
ˌlɒŋəˈweɪtɪd
ADJ [ADJ n] 翘首以待的；期待已久的 A long-awaited event or thing is one that someone has been waiting for for a long time. 翘首以待的；期待已久的

  • ...the long-awaited signing of a peace agreement.

    期待已久的和约的签订

  • ...his long awaited autobiography.

    令人期待已久的他的自传

SYN expected, wanted, promised, looked-for
