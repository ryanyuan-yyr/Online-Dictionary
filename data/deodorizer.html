deodorize
dioʊdəraɪz diˈoʊdərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense deodorizes, present participle deodorizing, past tense , past participle deodorized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 deodorise
VERB 除去…的臭味;脱去…的异味 If you deodorize something, you remove unpleasant smells from it. 除去…的臭味;脱去…的异味 [formal]

  • The machine uses minute quantities of ozone to sterilise and deodorise refrigerated food vehicles. [VERB noun]

    这种机器使用极少量的臭氧为食品冷藏车消毒杀菌和去除异味。

  • ...a deodorising foot spray. [VERB-ing]

    脚部除臭喷剂

SYN freshen, refresh, purify, ventilate
