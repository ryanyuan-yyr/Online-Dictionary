communicate
kəmjuːnɪkeɪt kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense communicates, present participle communicating, past tense , past participle communicated
1 V-RECIP 沟通;联系;交流 If you communicate with someone, you share or exchange information with them, for example by speaking, writing, or using equipment. You can also say that two people communicate . 沟通;联系;交流

  • My natural mother has never communicated with me. [VERB + with]

    我的亲生母亲从未和我联系过。

  • Officials of the CIA depend heavily on electronic mail to communicate with each other. [VERB + with]

    中情局官员之间的联系在很大程度上依赖电子邮件。

  • They communicated in sign language. [VERB]

    他们用手语交流。

SYN contact, talk, speak, phone  
communication

  • Lithuania hasn't had any direct communication with Moscow.

    立陶宛与莫斯科之间没有任何直接的联系。

  • ...use of the radio telephone for communication between controllers and pilots.

    无线电话在空中交通管理员与飞行员联络中的使用

  • We were in communication with each other.

    我们之间保持着联系。

2 VERB 传达;传递 If you communicate information, a feeling, or an idea to someone, you let them know about it. 传达;传递

  • They successfully communicate their knowledge to others. [VERB noun + to]

    他们成功地把知识传授给他人。

  • The results will be communicated to parents. [VERB noun to noun]

    结果会通知父母。

  • People must communicate their feelings. [VERB noun]

    人们需要表达自己的情感。

SYN make known, report, announce, reveal  
3 V-RECIP 沟通，交流(情感、想法等) If one person communicates with another, they successfully make each other aware of their feelings and ideas. You can also say that two people communicate . 沟通，交流(情感、想法等) [Also + with]

  • He was never good at communicating with the players. [VERB + with]

    他不善于和队员们进行沟通。

  • Family therapy showed us how to communicate with each other. [VERB + with]

    家庭疗法指导我们彼此之间的沟通交流。

  • ...considerate individuals who can communicate and work in a team. [VERB]

    善于沟通、富有团队精神、并且能为他人考虑的个人
