napkin
næpkɪn ˈnæpkɪn
Word forms: plural napkins
N-COUNT 餐巾;餐巾纸 A napkin is a square of cloth or paper that you use when you are eating to protect your clothes, or to wipe your mouth or hands. 餐巾;餐巾纸

  • She was taking tiny bites of a hot dog and daintily wiping her lips with a napkin.

    她小口地吃着热狗，用餐巾优雅地擦着嘴。

SYN serviette, cloth
