yoga
joʊgə ˈjoʊgə
1 N-UNCOUNT 瑜伽;瑜伽术 Yoga is a type of exercise in which you move your body into various positions in order to become more fit or flexible, to improve your breathing, and to relax your mind. 瑜伽;瑜伽术
2 N-UNCOUNT 瑜伽派（起源于印度、崇尚身体锻炼和冥想的哲学派别） Yoga is a philosophy which first developed in India, in which physical exercises and meditation are believed to help people to become calmer and united in spirit with God. 瑜伽派（起源于印度、崇尚身体锻炼和冥想的哲学派别）
