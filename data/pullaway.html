pull away
1 PHRASAL VERB （车辆）开始驶离；（驾车者）开走车辆 When a vehicle or driver pulls away, the vehicle starts moving forward. （车辆）开始驶离；（驾车者）开走车辆

  • I stood in the driveway and watched him back out and pull away. [VERB PREP.]

    我站在车道上，看着他把车倒出来然后开走。

2 PHRASAL VERB 远离；疏远 If you pull away from someone that you have had close links with, you deliberately become less close to them. 远离；疏远

  • Other daughters, faced with their mother's emotional hunger, pull away. [VERB PREP.]

    面对母亲的情感渴求，其他几个女儿都疏远了她。

  • He'd pulled away from her as if she had leprosy. [VERB PREP. + from]

    他离她远远的，好像她得了麻风病似的。
