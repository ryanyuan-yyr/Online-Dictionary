hex
hɛks hɛks hɛks
Word forms: hexes, hexing, hexed
1 N-COUNT [oft N on n] 魔法; 妖法If you say that there is a hex on someone or something, you mean that there seems to be a supernatural power causing unpleasant things to happen to them. 魔法; 妖法

  • It's almost as if there is a hex on my family.

    我家几乎像中了魔一样。

2 V-T (巫师)施魔法If a person such as a witch or a wizard hexes someone, they put a hex on them. (巫师)施魔法

  • ...shamans who could hex someone at a distance.

    ...巫师从远处就可给人施魔法。

3 N 十六进制；hexadecimal的缩写short for hexadecimal 十六进制；hexadecimal的缩写
4 N 十六进制的(as modifier) 十六进制的
