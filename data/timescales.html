timescale
taɪmskeɪl
(also time scale)Word forms: plural timescales
N-COUNT 时段;时标;时间表 The timescale of an event is the length of time during which it happens or develops. 时段;时标;时间表

  • The likelihood is that these companies now will show excellent profits on a two-year time scale…

    如今这些公司很可能会在两年内创造丰厚的利润。

  • He gave no timescale for these steps. [+ for]

    他没有给出这些步骤的时间表。
