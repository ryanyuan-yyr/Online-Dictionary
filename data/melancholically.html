melancholic
melənkɒlɪk
Word forms: plural melancholics
ADJ-GRADED 忧郁的;忧伤的 If you describe someone or something as melancholic, you mean that they are very sad. 忧郁的;忧伤的 [literary]

  • The night was as melancholic as his mood.

    夜晚和他的心情一样阴郁。

  • ...his gentle, melancholic songs.

    他温柔而伤感的歌曲

A melancholic is someone who is melancholic.忧郁的人

  • ...a self-made man, energetic and obsessively orderly, a histrionic exhibitionist and a melancholic.

    一个白手起家、精力充沛且强迫性讲求整齐的人，一个装腔作势、好出风头的人，一个忧郁伤感的人
