terrestrial
tɪrestriəl təˈrɛstrɪəl təˈrɛstriəl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (动植物)陆地的，陆生的，陆栖的 A terrestrial animal or plant lives on land or on the ground rather than in the sea, in trees, or in the air. (动植物)陆地的，陆生的，陆栖的 [technical]

  • Terrestrial and aquatic fauna may sometimes be found resting together under a loose stone.

    有时在松动的石头下可以同时看见栖息的陆生动物和水生动物。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 地球的;地球上的 Terrestrial means relating to the planet Earth rather than to some other part of the universe. 地球的;地球上的

  • ...terrestrial life forms.

    地球上的各种生命形态

SYN earthly, worldly, global, mundane  
3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (电视频道)地面的(有别于卫星转播的) Terrestrial television channels are transmitted using equipment situated at ground level, and not by satellite. (电视频道)地面的(有别于卫星转播的) [British]
