amnesiac
æmniːziæk
Word forms: plural amnesiacs
ADJ 记忆缺失的；患健忘症的 Someone who is amnesiac has lost their memory. 记忆缺失的；患健忘症的

  • She was taken to hospital, apparently amnesiac and shocked.

    她被送往医院，好像是受了惊吓出现了失忆。

An amnesiac is someone who is amnesiac.健忘症患者

  • Even profound amnesiacs can usually recall how to perform daily activities.

    即使病情严重的健忘症患者通常也能记起如何进行日常活动。
