befit
bɪfɪt biˈfɪt bɪˈfɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense befits, present participle befitting, past tense , past participle befitted
VERB 适合；适宜 If something befits a person or thing, it is suitable or appropriate for them. 适合；适宜 [formal]

  • They offered him a post befitting his seniority and experience. [VERB noun]

    他们给他提供了一个同他资历相当的职位。

  • He writes beautifully, as befits a poet. [VERB noun]

    他文笔优美，适合当诗人。

SYN be appropriate for, become, suit, be fitting for
