frosted
frɒstɪd frɔːst-
1 ADJ (玻璃)磨砂的，毛面的 Frosted glass is glass that you cannot see through clearly. (玻璃)磨砂的，毛面的

  • The top half of the door to his office was of frosted glass.

    他办公室门的上半部分是磨砂玻璃。

2 ADJ 结霜的;被霜覆盖的 Frosted means covered with frost. 结霜的;被霜覆盖的

  • ...the frosted trees.

    被霜覆盖的树木

3 ADJ 覆有霜状物的 Frosted means covered with something that looks like frost. 覆有霜状物的

  • ...frosted blue eye shadow.

    蓝色霜状眼影

4 ADJ 覆有糖霜的 Frosted means covered with icing. 覆有糖霜的 [US]

  • ...a plate of frosted cupcakes.

    一盘撒有糖霜的纸杯蛋糕
