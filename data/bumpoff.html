bump off
PHRASAL VERB 杀死；干掉 To bump someone off means to kill them. 杀死；干掉 [often humorous, informal]

  • They will probably bump you off anyway! [VERB noun PREP.]

    不管怎样他们很可能会干掉你！

  • ...the hit man he's hired to bump off his wife. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他雇来杀害妻子的职业杀手

SYN murder, kill, assassinate, remove
