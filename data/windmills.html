windmill
wɪndmɪl ˈwɪnˌmɪl ˈwɪndˌmɪl
Word forms: plural windmills
N-COUNT 风车磨坊；（风力发电的）风车 A windmill is a building with long pieces of wood on the outside which turn around as the wind blows and provide energy for a machine that crushes grain. A windmill is also a similar structure that is used to convert the power of the wind into electricity. 风车磨坊；（风力发电的）风车
