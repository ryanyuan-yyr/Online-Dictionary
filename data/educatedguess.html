educated guess
ˈɛdʒʊˌkeɪtɪd ɡɛs
Word forms: plural educated guesses
N-COUNT 有根据的猜测 An educated guess is a guess which is based on a certain amount of knowledge and is therefore likely to be correct. 有根据的猜测

  • Estimating the right cooking time will always be an educated guess.

    拿捏好烹饪的时间常常需要日积月累。
