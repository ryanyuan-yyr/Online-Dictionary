well-timed
ˈwɛlˈtaɪmd
(also well timed)
ADJ-GRADED 时机正好的；适时的;不早不晚的 A well-timed action or comment is done or made at the most appropriate or suitable time. 时机正好的；适时的;不早不晚的

  • He built the company through a string of well-timed acquisitions.

    通过一系列时机恰到好处的并购，他建立了这家公司。

  • One well-timed word from you will be all it needs.

    现在需要的只是你一句适时的话。

SYN timely, appropriate, suitable, judicious
