separately
sepərətli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 单独地;分别地 If people or things are dealt with separately or do something separately, they are dealt with or do something at different times or places, rather than together. 单独地;分别地 [Also + from]

  • Cook each vegetable separately until just tender.

    每样蔬菜都单独烹调，煮到稍稍变软就好。

  • Chris had insisted that we went separately to the club.

    克里斯坚持我们不要一块儿去俱乐部。

SYN alone, independently, apart, personally
