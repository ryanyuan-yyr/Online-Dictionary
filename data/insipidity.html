insipid
ɪnsɪpɪd ɪnˈsɪpɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED (食物或饮料)淡而无味的,没有味道的 If you describe food or drink as insipid, you dislike it because it has very little taste. (食物或饮料)淡而无味的,没有味道的 [disapproval]

  • It tasted indescribably bland and insipid, like warmed cardboard.

    这东西尝起来淡而无味至极,像加热过的纸板一样。

SYN tasteless, bland, flavourless, watered down  
2 ADJ-GRADED 枯燥乏味的;令人厌烦的 If you describe someone or something as insipid, you mean they are dull and boring. 枯燥乏味的;令人厌烦的 [disapproval]

  • On the surface she seemed meek, rather insipid.

    表面上她似乎温顺驯服,相当乏味无趣。

  • They gave an insipid opening performance in a nil-nil draw with Peru.

    他们与秘鲁队的揭幕战打得波澜不惊，最终以 0:0 战平。

SYN bland, boring, dull, flat
