self-styled
ˈsɛlfˈstaɪld
ADJ [ADJ n] 自诩的;自封的 If you describe someone as a self-styled leader or expert, you disapprove of them because they claim to be a leader or expert but they do not actually have the right to call themselves this. 自诩的;自封的 [disapproval]

  • Two of those arrested are said to be self-styled area commanders.

    据说在那些被捕的人员中有两人自称是地区指挥官。

  • He fiercely criticised self-styled educational experts for ignoring Shakespeare.

    他强烈批评那些无视莎士比亚的自诩为教育专家的人。

SYN so-called, would-be, professed, self-appointed
