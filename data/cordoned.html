cordon
kɔːʳdən ˈkɔrdən
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cordons, present participle cordoning, past tense , past participle cordoned
N-COUNT (由警察、士兵或车辆组成的)警戒线，封锁线;封锁圈 A cordon is a line or ring of police, soldiers, or vehicles preventing people from entering or leaving an area. (由警察、士兵或车辆组成的)警戒线，封锁线;封锁圈

  • Police formed a cordon between the two crowds.

    警察在两群人之间筑起了一道封锁线。

SYN chain, line, ring, barrier  
Phrasal verbs:
cordon off
