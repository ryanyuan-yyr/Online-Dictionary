boarding
bɔːʳdɪŋ ˈbɔrdɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 学校寄宿（制） Boarding is an arrangement by which children live at school during the school term. 学校寄宿（制）

  • ...the master in charge of boarding.

    主管学生住宿的男老师

  • Annual boarding fees are £10,350.

    每年的寄宿费为10,350英镑。

2 N-UNCOUNT （用来建造围墙、门、栅栏的）长条木板 Boarding is long, flat pieces of wood which can be used to make walls, doors, and fences. （用来建造围墙、门、栅栏的）长条木板

  • ...the white-painted boarding in the sitting room.

    起居室里粉刷成白色的门板
