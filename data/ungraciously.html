ungracious
ʌngreɪʃəs ʌnˈɡreɪʃəs ʌnˈgreɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 不礼貌的；不友好的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as ungracious, you mean that they are not polite or friendly in their speech or behaviour. 不礼貌的；不友好的 [formal]

  • ...his ungracious behaviour during the Queen's recent visit.

    女王最近到访期间他的不得体行为

  • I was often rude and ungracious in refusing help. [+ in]

    我拒绝接受帮助的时候时常显得粗鲁无礼。

SYN bad-mannered, rude, churlish, offhand
