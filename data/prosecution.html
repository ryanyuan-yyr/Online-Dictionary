prosecution
prɒsɪkjuːʃən ˌprɑsəˈkjuʃən
Word forms: plural prosecutions
1 N-VAR 起诉；检举；告发 Prosecution is the action of charging someone with a crime and putting them on trial. 起诉；检举；告发

  • Yesterday the head of government called for the prosecution of those responsible for the deaths. [+ of]

    昨天，政府首脑要求起诉那些该为这些人的死负责的人。

SYN trial, trying  
2 N-SING 公诉的律师；控方律师 The lawyers who try to prove that a person on trial is guilty are called the prosecution . 公诉的律师；控方律师

  • Colonel Pugh, for the prosecution, said that the offences occurred over a six-year period.

    皮尤上校为控方律师作证时说这些罪行是在6年的时间里犯下的。
