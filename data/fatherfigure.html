father figure
ˈfɑːðə ˈfɪɡə
(also father-figure)Word forms: plural father figures
N-COUNT 父亲般的人;(像父亲一样的)长者 If you describe someone as a father figure, you mean that you feel able to turn to that person for advice and support in the same way that you might turn to your father. 父亲般的人;(像父亲一样的)长者

  • She believed her daughter needed a father-figure.

    她认为女儿需要一个父亲一样的人。

  • He became a father figure to the whole company.

    他成了整个公司的长者。
