frightened
fraɪtənd ˈfraɪtənd
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ to-infinitive] 受惊的;害怕的 If you are frightened, you are anxious or afraid, often because of something that has just happened or that you think may happen. 受惊的;害怕的

  • She was frightened of flying. [+ of]

    她害怕坐飞机。

  • Miriam was too frightened to tell her family what had happened.

    米丽娅姆太害怕了，不敢告诉家人发生了什么事。

SYN afraid, alarmed, scared, terrified  
Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse frightened and frightening.

    注意不要混淆frightened 和 frightening。
