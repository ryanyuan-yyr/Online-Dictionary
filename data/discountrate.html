discount rate
ˈdɪskaʊnt reɪt
Word forms: discount rates
1 N-COUNT [usu sing] (央行的)贴现率The discount rate is the rate of interest that the central bank of a country charges on the loans that it makes to other banks. (央行的)贴现率 [mainly US]

  • The Federal Reserve has cut the discount rate five times in 12 months.

    美联储已在12个月间5次下调贴现率。

2 N-COUNT 折扣率A discount rate is an amount of interest deducted in advance in the purchase, sale, or loan of negotiable assets. 折扣率

  • There are plenty of good deals in fixed and discount rates that homebuyers should consider.

    房屋市场上有大量不错的固定率和折扣率交易供购房者考虑。
