muck out
PHRASAL VERB 清扫(马厩、畜栏) If you muck out a stable or a farm animal's home, you clean out all the faeces and old hay. 清扫(马厩、畜栏) [British] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He stamped off to muck out the pigsty. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    他去打扫猪圈了。

  • Here's how to muck out. [VERB PREP.]

    就这样清扫。
