hat-trick
(also hat trick)Word forms: plural hat-tricks
N-COUNT （尤指体育比赛中的）连中三元，帽子戏法 A hat-trick is a series of three achievements, especially in a sports event, for example three goals scored by the same person in a football game. （尤指体育比赛中的）连中三元，帽子戏法

  • I scored a hat-trick against Arsenal.

    我在和阿森纳队的比赛中上演了帽子戏法。
