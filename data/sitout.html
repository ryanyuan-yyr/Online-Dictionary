sit out
PHRASAL VERB (不采取行动)静等…结束，等到…结束 If you sit something out, you wait for it to finish, without taking any action. (不采取行动)静等…结束，等到…结束

  • The only thing I can do is keep quiet and sit this one out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我唯一能做的就是保持沉默，等待这件事结束。

  • He can afford to sit out the property slump. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他的经济实力可以撑过这次房地产市场萧条期。
