average out
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （将…）平均为 If a set of numbers average out to a particular figure or if you average them out to that figure, their average is calculated to be that figure. （将…）平均为 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • There are six glasses of wine in one bottle, which averages out to 50p a glass. [V P + to/at]

    一瓶酒可以分6杯，平均一杯50便士。

  • Averaging it out between us there's less than £10 a month each to live on. [VERB noun PREP.]

    咱俩把这笔钱平均分一下，每人每月的生活费还不到10英镑。
