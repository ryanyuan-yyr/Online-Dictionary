well-kept
(also well kept)
1 ADJ-GRADED (建筑、街道等)保持整洁的，悉心照料的 A well-kept building, street, garden, or other place is always neat and tidy because it is carefully looked after. (建筑、街道等)保持整洁的，悉心照料的

  • ...two idyllic thatched cottages with well-kept gardens.

    两间富有田园气息，带有漂亮、整洁的花园的茅舍

  • ...broad, well-kept streets.

    宽阔整洁的街道

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 保密的;严守的 A well-kept secret has not been told or made known to anyone, or has been told or made known to only a small number of people. 保密的;严守的

  • His resignation was such a well-kept secret that only Leavis knew about it in advance.

    他要辞职一事保守得很严密，事前只有利维斯知道。
