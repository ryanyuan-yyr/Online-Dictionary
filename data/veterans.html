veteran
vetərən ˈvɛtrən ˈvɛtərən ˈvɛtrən
Word forms: plural veterans
1 N-COUNT 退伍军人;老兵;老战士 A veteran is someone who has served in the armed forces of their country, especially during a war. 退伍军人;老兵;老战士

  • They approved a $1.1 billion package of pay increases for the veterans of the Persian Gulf War. [+ of]

    他们通过了对参加过波斯湾战争的老兵增发共计11亿美元的一揽子提案。

2 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] 有经验的人;老手 You use veteran to refer to someone who has been involved in a particular activity for a long time. 有经验的人;老手

  • ...Tony Benn, the veteran Labour MP and former Cabinet minister.

    托尼·本，一位经验丰富的工党下院议员和前内阁部长
