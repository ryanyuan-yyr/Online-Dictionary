edit out
PHRASAL VERB （常因可能具有冒犯性而）删除，删节 If you edit something out of a book or film, you remove it, often because it might be offensive to some people. （常因可能具有冒犯性而）删除，删节

  • His voice will be edited out of the final film. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    电影的最终版本会剪掉他的声音。

  • She edited that line out again. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她再次删掉了那句台词。

SYN remove, cut, exclude, omit
