hue
hjuː hju
Word forms: plural hues
1 N-COUNT 颜色;色调 A hue is a colour. 颜色;色调 [literary]

  • The same hue will look different in different light.

    同一颜色在不同的光线下看起来会有所不同。

  • ...a selection of tops in natural hues and fibres.

    一批自然色彩和纤维的上衣

SYN colour, tone, shade, dye  
2 PHRASE 大声抗议;强烈抗议 If people raise a hue and cry about something, they protest angrily about it. 大声抗议;强烈抗议 [written]

  • Just as the show ended, he heard a huge hue and cry outside.

    演出刚结束，他就听到外面愤怒的高声抗议。
