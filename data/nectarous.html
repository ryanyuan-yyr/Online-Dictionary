nectar
nektəʳ ˈnɛktər
Word forms: plural nectars
1 N-UNCOUNT 花蜜 Nectar is a sweet liquid produced by flowers, which bees and other insects collect. 花蜜
2 N-UNCOUNT 甘露;(尤指酒类)琼浆玉液 If you refer to a drink, especially an alcoholic drink, as nectar, you think it tastes very nice. 甘露;(尤指酒类)琼浆玉液 [literary, approval]

  • Tony sipped from his glass. 'Mmm. Ambrosia. Nectar of the gods. Divine. Wonderful.'

    托尼从杯中抿了一口感叹道：“哇，人间美味啊，神灵的琼浆玉液呀，天赐之物，太棒了。”
