draw out
1 PHRASAL VERB 拉长,拖长(声音、单词等) If you draw out a sound or a word, you make it last longer than usual. 拉长,拖长(声音、单词等) [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Liz drew the word out carefully. [VERB noun PREP.]

    莉兹小心翼翼地说出了那个词。

2 PHRASAL VERB 使畅所欲言;使愿意交谈 If you draw someone out, you make them feel less nervous and more willing to talk. 使畅所欲言;使愿意交谈

  • Her mother tried every approach to draw her out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她母亲想尽一切办法让她开口。

SYN persuade someone to talk, put someone at their ease
