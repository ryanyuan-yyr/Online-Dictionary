go without
PHRASAL VERB 缺少（所需之物）；没有（常有之物等） If you go without something that you need or usually have or do, you do not get it or do it. 缺少（所需之物）；没有（常有之物等）

  • I have known what it is like to go without food for days. [VERB PREP. noun/verb-ing]

    我知道几天没有东西吃是什么滋味了。

  • The embargo won't hurt us because we're used to going without. [VERB PREP.]

    禁运不会对我们有妨害，因为我们已经习惯了没有供应的日子。

SYN be deprived of, want, lack, be denied
