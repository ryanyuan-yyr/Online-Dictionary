self-catering
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 自理膳食的;自供伙食的 If you go on a self-catering holiday or you stay in self-catering accommodation, you stay in a place where you have to make your own meals. 自理膳食的;自供伙食的 [British]

  • ...a week's self-catering in Majorca for £239.

    膳食自理的马略卡岛一周游的费用为 239 英镑

  • The self-catering flats are usually reserved for postgraduate students.

    可以自己做饭的公寓一般留给研究生住。
