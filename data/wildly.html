wildly
waɪldli
1 ADV-GRADED [usually ADVERB adjective, oft ADVERB after verb] 非常;极其 You use wildly to emphasize the degree, amount, or intensity of something. 非常;极其 [emphasis]

  • Reports of his drinking have been wildly exaggerated.

    有关他酗酒的报道极尽夸张。

  • The island's hotels vary wildly.

    岛上的宾馆千差万别。
