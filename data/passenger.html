passenger
pæsɪndʒəʳ ˈpæsɪndʒə ˈpæsəndʒər
Word forms: plural passengers
1 N-COUNT 乘客；旅客 A passenger in a vehicle such as a bus, boat, or plane is a person who is travelling in it, but who is not driving it or working on it. →see usage note at: client 乘客；旅客

  • Mr Fullemann was a passenger in the car when it crashed.

    出车祸时，福勒曼先生正在那辆车上。

  • ...a flight from Milan with more than forty passengers on board.

    搭载着四十多名乘客的米兰来的航班

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 乘客的；旅客的 Passenger is used to describe something that is designed for passengers, rather than for drivers or goods. 乘客的；旅客的

  • I sat in the passenger seat.

    我坐在乘客座位上。

  • ...a passenger train.

    客运列车
