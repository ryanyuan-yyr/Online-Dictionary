notifiable
noʊtɪfaɪəbəl ˈnoʊtəˌfaɪəbəl
ADJ (疾病、罪行等)须报告当局的，应向官方汇报的 A notifiable disease or crime is one that must be reported to the authorities whenever it occurs, because it is considered to be dangerous to the community. (疾病、罪行等)须报告当局的，应向官方汇报的

  • Many doctors fail to report cases, even though food poisoning is a notifiable disease.

    很多医生没有报告病例，尽管食物中毒是应该通报的。
