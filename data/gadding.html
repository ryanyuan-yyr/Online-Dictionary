VERB 闲逛；游荡 If you gad about, you go to a lot of different places looking for amusement or entertainment. 闲逛；游荡 [informal]

  • Don't think you'll keep me here while you gad about. [V about]

    甭指望你去四处闲荡的时候我会呆在这儿。

SYN gallivant, wander, roam, run around
