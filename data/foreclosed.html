foreclose
fɔːʳkloʊz fɔrˈkloʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense forecloses, present participle foreclosing, past tense , past participle foreclosed
VERB (因未按时还款等)取消(抵押品)的赎取权，止赎 If the person or organization that lent someone money forecloses, they take possession of a property that was bought with the borrowed money, for example because regular repayments have not been made. (因未按时还款等)取消(抵押品)的赎取权，止赎 [business] [Also VERB]

  • The bank foreclosed on the mortgage for his previous home. [VERB + on]

    银行取消了他先前住房的抵押赎回权。
