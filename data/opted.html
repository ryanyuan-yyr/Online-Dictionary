opt
ɒpt ɑpt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense opts, present participle opting, past tense , past participle opted
1 VERB 选择；作出抉择 If you opt for something, or opt to do something, you choose it or decide to do it in preference to anything else. 选择；作出抉择

  • Depending on your circumstances you may wish to opt for one method or the other. [VERB + for]

    依据自身情况的不同，你可能希望选择这种或那种方法。

  • Our students can also opt to stay in residence. [VERB to-infinitive]

    我们的学生也可以选择住校。

SYN choose, decide, prefer, select
