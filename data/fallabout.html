fall about
PHRASAL VERB 捧腹大笑;笑得前仰后合 If you say that people are falling about, you mean that they are laughing a lot about something. 捧腹大笑;笑得前仰后合 [British, informal] [Also V P with n]

  • Dan fell about and slapped his thighs. [VERB PREP.]

    丹笑得前仰后合，直拍大腿。

  • The men at the table fell about laughing. [VERB PREP. verb-ing]

    一桌人都笑得直不起腰来。
