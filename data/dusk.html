dusk
dʌsk dʌsk
1 N-UNCOUNT 黄昏;傍晚;薄暮 Dusk is the time just before night when the daylight has almost gone but when it is not completely dark. 黄昏;傍晚;薄暮

  • We arrived home at dusk.

    我们傍晚到家。

SYN twilight, evening, evo [Australian , slang] , nightfall  
2 N-UNCOUNT [also the N] 暮色 The dusk is the dull, shadowy light there is at dusk. 暮色 [literary]

  • She turned and disappeared into the dusk.

    她转身消失在暮色中。

SYN shade, darkness, gloom, obscurity
