monogamy
mənɒgəmi məˈnɑgəmi
1 N-UNCOUNT 一夫一妻制;单配偶 Monogamy is used to refer to the state or custom of having a sexual relationship with only one partner. 一夫一妻制;单配偶

  • People still opt for monogamy and marriage.

    人们仍然选择一夫一妻制。

2 N-UNCOUNT 一夫一妻制 Monogamy is the state or custom of being married to only one person at a particular time. 一夫一妻制

  • Support for monogamy and marriage was strongest among the professional classes.

    专业人士阶层最支持一夫一妻制。
