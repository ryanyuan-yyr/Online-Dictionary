live down
lɪv
PHRASAL VERB 使人们忘记（错误、失败、恶名等） If you are unable to live down a mistake, failure, or bad reputation, you are unable to make people forget about it. 使人们忘记（错误、失败、恶名等）

  • Labour was also unable to live down its reputation as the party of high taxes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    工党也无法让人忘记其高税党的名声。

  • I thought I'd never live it down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我原以为大家永远都不会忘记这件事了。
