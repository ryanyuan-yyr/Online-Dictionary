fairyland
feərilænd ˈfɛərɪˌlænd ˈfɛərɪdəm ˈfɛriˌlænd
Word forms: plural fairylands
1 N-UNCOUNT 仙国;仙界 Fairyland is the imaginary place where fairies live. 仙国;仙界
2 N-VAR 仙境;美妙之地 If you describe a place as a fairyland, you mean that it has a delicate beauty. 仙境;美妙之地

  • If you came with me to one of my toy shops, you'd think you were stepping into a fairyland.

    如果你跟我到我的一个玩具店，你会以为踏进了仙境。
