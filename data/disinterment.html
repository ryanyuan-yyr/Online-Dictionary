disinter
dɪsɪntɜːʳ ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜː ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜr
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disinters, present participle disinterring, past tense , past participle disinterred
1 VERB [usually passive] 挖掘出(尸体) When a dead body is disinterred, it is dug up from out of the ground. 挖掘出(尸体)

  • The bones were disinterred and moved to a burial site. [be VERB-ed]

    尸骨被刨出，移到了墓地。

SYN dig up, unearth, exhume, disentomb  
2 VERB 使重现;使复活;再度使用(很久未用过的东西) If you disinter something, you start using it again after it has not been used for a long time. 使重现;使复活;再度使用(很久未用过的东西) [humorous]

  • ...the trend for disinterring sixties soul classics for TV commercials. [VERB noun]

    在电视广告中再度使用60年代经典灵歌的趋势
