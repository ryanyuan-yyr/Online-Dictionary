generosity
dʒenərɒsɪti ˌdʒɛnərˈɑsəti
N-UNCOUNT 慷慨；大方 If you refer to someone's generosity, you mean that they are generous, especially in doing or giving more than is usual or expected. 慷慨；大方

  • There are stories about his generosity, the massive amounts of money he gave to charities.

    有传闻说他向慈善机构慷慨解囊，捐献巨款。

  • ...a man of great generosity of spirit.

    宽容大度的人

SYN liberality, charity, bounty, munificence
