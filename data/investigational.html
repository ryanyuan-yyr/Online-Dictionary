investigate
ɪnvestɪgeɪt ɪnˈvɛstəˌgeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense investigates, present participle investigating, past tense , past participle investigated
1 VERB 调查;审查 If someone, especially an official, investigates an event, situation, or claim, they try to find out what happened or what is the truth. 调查;审查 [Also VERB]

  • Gas officials are investigating the cause of an explosion which badly damaged a house in Hampshire. [VERB noun]

    煤气系统的官员正在调查一起瓦斯爆炸的起因,这起事故严重毁坏了汉普郡的一所房屋。

  • The two officers were being investigated by the director of public prosecutions. [VERB noun]

    两名警官正在接受检察官的审查。

  • Police are still investigating how the accident happened. [VERB wh]

    警方仍然在调查事故的起因。

SYN examine, study, research, consider  
investigation

  • He ordered an investigation into the affair.

    他命令对此事件展开调查。

  • He has been notified by magistrates that he is under investigation for corruption.
