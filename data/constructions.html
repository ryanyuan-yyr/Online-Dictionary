construction
kənstrʌkʃən kənˈstrʌkʃən
Word forms: plural constructions
1 N-UNCOUNT (房屋、工厂、道路、桥梁等的)建筑，建造 Construction is the building of things such as houses, factories, roads, and bridges. (房屋、工厂、道路、桥梁等的)建筑，建造

  • He'd already started construction on a hunting lodge.

    他已经开始建造一间狩猎用的小屋。

  • ...the only nuclear power station under construction in Britain.

    英国唯一一家在建的核电站

  • ...the downturn in the construction industry.

    建筑业的衰败

  • Quincy wants a job in construction.

    昆西想在建筑行业找一份工作。

2 N-UNCOUNT (车辆、机器等的)制造，建造 The construction of something such as a vehicle or machine is the making of it. (车辆、机器等的)制造，建造

  • ...companies who have long experience in the construction of those types of equipment. [+ of]

    长期从事此类设备制造的公司

  • With the exception of teak, this is the finest wood for boat construction.

    这是除柚木以外最好的造船木材。

SYN building, assembly, creation, formation  
3 N-UNCOUNT [with poss] (体系等的)构建，创建 The construction of something such as a system is the creation of it. (体系等的)构建，创建

  • ...the construction of a just system of criminal justice. [+ of]

    一套公正的刑事审判制度的创建

4 N-COUNT 建造物;制造物 You can refer to an object that has been built or made as a construction . 建造物;制造物

  • The British pavilion is an impressive steel and glass construction the size of Westminster Abbey.

    这座英式亭阁是令人赞叹的钢和玻璃建筑，大小和威斯敏斯特教堂相当。

SYN structure, building, edifice, form  
5 N-UNCOUNT 构造；结构 You use construction to refer to the structure of something and the way it has been built or made. 构造；结构

  • The Shakers believed that furniture should be plain, simple, useful, practical and of sound construction.

    震颤派教徒认为家具应当朴素、简单、有用、实用，并且结构合理。

  • The chairs were light in construction yet extremely strong.

    这些椅子构造轻巧，但却极为结实。

6 N-COUNT [usually singular] (对某人言行的)解释，理解，阐释 The construction that you put on what someone says or does is your interpretation of what it means. (对某人言行的)解释，理解，阐释

  • The denial was limited to rejecting the construction put on his remarks.

    这种否认仅限于不接受对他所说话语的阐释。

  • He put the wrong construction on what he saw.

    他误解了所见到的事情。

SYN interpretation, meaning, reading, sense  
7 N-COUNT (语法)结构 A grammatical construction is a particular arrangement of words in a sentence, clause, or phrase. (语法)结构

  • Avoid complex verbal constructions.

    避免使用复杂的动词结构。

SYN composition, structure, arrangement
