agribusiness

N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 农业综合经营;（尤指）大农场经营 Agribusiness is the various businesses that produce, sell, and distribute farm products, especially on a large scale. 农业综合经营;（尤指）大农场经营 [business]
