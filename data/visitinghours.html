visiting hours
ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ aʊəz
N-PLURAL (医院或监狱的)探视时间In an institution such as a hospital or prison, visiting hours are the times during which people from outside the institution are officially allowed to visit people who are staying at the institution. (医院或监狱的)探视时间

  • Visiting hours were over.

    探视时间结束了。
