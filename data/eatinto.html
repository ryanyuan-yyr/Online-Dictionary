eat into
1 PHRASAL VERB 消耗，耗费（时间或资源） If something eats into your time or your resources, it uses them, when they should be used for other things. 消耗，耗费（时间或资源）

  • Responsibilities at home and work eat into his time. [VERB PREP. noun]

    家庭和工作的双重负担耗费了他的时间。

  • Wages were rising faster than productivity and this was eating into profits. [VERB PREP. noun]

    工资的涨幅高过了生产率增长的速度，从而消耗了利润所得。

2 PHRASAL VERB (酸、锈等)侵蚀，腐蚀 If a substance such as acid or rust eats into something, it destroys or damages its surface. (酸、锈等)侵蚀，腐蚀

  • Ulcers occur when the stomach's natural acids eat into the lining of the stomach. [VERB PREP. noun]

    胃酸侵蚀胃内壁引发溃疡。
