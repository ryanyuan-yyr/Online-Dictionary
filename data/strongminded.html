strong-minded ˈstrɔŋˌmaɪndɪd
ADJ-GRADED (尤指女性)意志坚强的，果断的，有主见的 If you describe someone, especially a woman, as strong-minded, you approve of them because they have their own firm attitudes and opinions, and are not easily influenced by other people. (尤指女性)意志坚强的，果断的，有主见的 [approval]

  • She is a strong-minded, independent woman.

    她是一个独立、有主见的女人。

SYN determined, resolute, strong-willed, firm
