turncoat
tɜːʳnkoʊt ˈtɜrnˌkoʊt
Word forms: plural turncoats
N-COUNT 叛徒;变节者 If you describe someone as a turncoat, you think they are disloyal or deceitful, because they have left their party or organization and joined an opposing one. 叛徒;变节者 [disapproval]

  • His one-time admirers now accuse him of being a turncoat.

    曾经一度仰慕他的人如今指责他是叛徒。

SYN traitor, renegade, defector, seceder
