equities
ekwɪtiz ˈɛkwɪtɪz
1 N-PLURAL （在优先股之后享受红利的）股票，普通股 Equities are shares in a company that are owned by people who have a right to vote at the company's meetings and to receive part of the company's profits after the holders of preference shares have been paid. （在优先股之后享受红利的）股票，普通股 [business]

  • Investors have poured money into US equities.

    投资者投入大量资金购买美国股票。
