ravings
reɪvɪŋz ˈreɪvɪŋz
N-PLURAL 疯话；胡言乱语 If you describe what someone says or writes as their ravings, you mean that it makes no sense because they are mad or very ill. 疯话；胡言乱语

  • Haig and Robertson saw it as the lunatic ravings of a mad politician.

    黑格和罗伯逊把它看作是一个疯癫政治家的胡言乱语。

SYN ranting, rambling, babbling, gibberish
