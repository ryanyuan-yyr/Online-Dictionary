open-and-shut ˈoʊpənənˈʃʌt
ADJ [usually ADJ n] （争论、案件等）容易裁决（或解决）的，一目了然的 If you describe a dispute or a legal case as open-and-shut, you mean that is easily decided or solved because the facts are very clear. （争论、案件等）容易裁决（或解决）的，一目了然的

  • It's an open and shut case. The hospital is at fault.

    这件事一目了然，责任在医院一方。

  • ...an open-and-shut murder charge.

    事实清楚的谋杀指控

SYN straightforward [British] , simple, obvious, routine
