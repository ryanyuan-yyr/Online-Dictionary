individuality
ɪndɪvɪdʒuælɪti ˌɪndəˌvɪdʒuˈæləti
N-UNCOUNT 独特性;个性;个人特征 The individuality of a person or thing consists of the qualities that make them different from other people or things. 独特性;个性;个人特征

  • People should be free to express their individuality.

    人们应该能自由表达他们的个性。

  • This will give your promotional material individuality and style.

    这会让你的促销品显得个性十足、格调高雅。

SYN character, personality, uniqueness, distinction
