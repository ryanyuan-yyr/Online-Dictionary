protected
prətektɪd prəˈtɛktɪd
ADJ 受保护的 Protected is used to describe animals, plants, and areas of land which the law does not allow to be destroyed, harmed, or damaged. 受保护的

  • In England, thrushes are a protected species so you will not find them on any menu.

    在英格兰，歌鸫是受保护物种，因此你在任何菜单上都找不到。

  • ...a protected zone of national forest.

    国家森林保护区
