fixture
fɪkstʃəʳ ˈfɪkstʃər
Word forms: plural fixtures
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (房间等建筑物内的)固定装置，固定设施 Fixtures are pieces of furniture or equipment, for example baths and sinks, which are fixed inside a house or other building and which stay there if you move. (房间等建筑物内的)固定装置，固定设施

  • ...a detailed list of what fixtures and fittings are included in the purchase price.

    房屋购买价中包括的固定装置及设备的详细清单

2 N-COUNT (定期举行的)体育活动，体育赛事 A fixture is a sports event which takes place on a particular date. (定期举行的)体育活动，体育赛事 [British]

  • City won this fixture 3-0 last season.

    上个赛季曼城队在该项赛事中以3比0获胜。

3 N-COUNT (…的)常客;(…的)固定事物 If you describe someone or something as a fixture in a particular place or occasion, you mean that they always seem to be there. (…的)常客;(…的)固定事物

  • She was a fixture in New York's nightclubs. [+ in]

    她是纽约夜总会的常客。

  • The cordless kettle may now be a fixture in most kitchens. [+ in]

    无绳电热水壶现在可能是多数厨房的固定设备。
