mug up
PHRASAL VERB 攻读;快速学习 If you mug up a subject or mug up on it, you study it quickly, so that you can remember the main facts about it. 攻读;快速学习 [British, informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • ...visitors who want to mug up their knowledge in the shortest possible time. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    想在尽可能短的时间内突击学习知识的参观者

  • It is advisable to mug up on your Spanish, too, as few locals speak English. [V P on n]

    你最好能突击提高一下你的西班牙语，因为当地没多少人讲英语。
