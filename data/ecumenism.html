N-UNCOUNT （主张基督教大联合的）普世教会主义（或运动） Ecumenism is the belief that the different Christian Churches should be as united as possible, and can also be used to refer to actions based on this belief. （主张基督教大联合的）普世教会主义（或运动） [formal]
