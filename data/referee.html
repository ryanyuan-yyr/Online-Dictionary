referee
refəriː ˌrɛfəˈri
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense referees, present participle refereeing, past tense , past participle refereed
1 N-COUNT （比赛的）裁判员 The referee is the official who controls a sports event such as a football game or a boxing match. （比赛的）裁判员
2 VERB 为…担任裁判；执法（比赛） When someone referees a sports event or contest, they act as referee. 为…担任裁判；执法（比赛）

  • The match will be refereed by Derek Bevan from Wales. [be VERB-ed]

    这场比赛将由来自威尔士的德里克·贝文执法。

  • Vautrot has refereed in two World Cups. [VERB]

    沃特罗特执法过两届世界杯。

SYN umpire, judge, mediate, adjudicate  
3 N-COUNT （求职等时的）推荐人，介绍人，证明人 A referee is a person who gives you a reference, for example when you are applying for a job. （求职等时的）推荐人，介绍人，证明人 [mainly British]
