overbearing
oʊvəʳbeərɪŋ ˌəʊvəˈbɛərɪŋ ˌoʊvərˈbɛrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 傲慢的；专横的；盛气凌人的；飞扬跋扈的 An overbearing person tries to make other people do what he or she wants in an unpleasant and forceful way. 傲慢的；专横的；盛气凌人的；飞扬跋扈的 [disapproval]

  • My husband can be quite overbearing with our son. [+ with]

    我丈夫有时对儿子很专横。

SYN domineering, lordly, superior, arrogant
