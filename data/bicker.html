bicker
bɪkəʳ ˈbɪkər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bickers, present participle bickering, past tense , past participle bickered
V-RECIP 斗嘴；争吵；发生口角 When people bicker, they argue or quarrel about unimportant things. 斗嘴；争吵；发生口角

  • I went into medicine to care for patients, not to waste time bickering over budgets. [V + over/about]

    我行医是为了治病救人，而不是浪费时间为预算问题争论不休。

  • The two women bickered constantly. [plural-noun VERB]

    这两个女人经常发生口角。

  • ...as states bicker over territory. [V + over/about]

    当国家为领土问题而争吵时

  • He is still bickering with the control tower over admissible approach routes. [VERB + with]

    他还在为可采用的进近航线与指挥塔台争执不下。

SYN quarrel, fight, argue, row [informal]  
bickering

  • The election will end months of political bickering.

    这次选举将会结束数月来的政治纷争。
