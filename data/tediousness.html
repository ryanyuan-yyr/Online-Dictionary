tedious
tiːdiəs ˈtidiəs ˈtidʒəs
ADJ-GRADED 枯燥的;乏味的;令人生厌的 If you describe something such as a job, task, or situation as tedious, you mean it is boring and rather frustrating. 枯燥的;乏味的;令人生厌的

  • Such lists are long and tedious to read.

    这种表单读起来冗长乏味。

  • ...the tedious business of line-by-line programming.

    枯燥乏味的逐行编程工作

SYN boring, dull, dreary, monotonous  
tediously

  • ...the most tediously boring aspects of international relations.

    国际关系中最枯燥乏味的方面
