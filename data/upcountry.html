upcountry
ʌpkʌntri ʌpˈkʌntrɪ ˈʌpˌkʌntri ʌpˈkʌntri
(also up-country)
ADJ [ADJ n] 内陆的；北方边远地区的 Upcountry places are towards the middle or north of a large country, usually in the countryside. 内陆的；北方边远地区的

  • ...a collection of upcountry hamlets.

    内地村落

Upcountry is also an adverb.

  • I run a cattle station some miles up-country.

    我在北边几英里开外的地方有一个养牛场。

  • We went up-country to Ballarat.

    我们向北往巴拉腊特走。
