bust-up
Word forms: plural bust-ups
1 N-COUNT （常常导致关系破裂的）激烈争吵 A bust-up is a serious quarrel, often resulting in the end of a relationship. （常常导致关系破裂的）激烈争吵 [informal]

  • She had had this bust-up with her family.

    她跟家人大吵过一场。

2 N-COUNT 斗殴；打斗 A bust-up is a fight. 斗殴；打斗 [British, informal]

  • ...a bust-up which she says left her seriously hurt.

    一场她声称使她受了重伤的斗殴
