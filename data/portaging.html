portage
pɔrtɪdʒ ˈpɔːtɪdʒ pɔrtaʒ ˈpɔrtɪdʒ pɔrˈtɑʒ
Word forms: portages, portaging, portaged
1 V-ERG 陆路搬运; 水陆联运If people in a boat such as a canoe portage somewhere, they carry the boat overland until they can return it to another stretch of water. 陆路搬运; 水陆联运 [US]

  • When we reached the river, we decided to portage to the campsite.

    当我们抵达该河时，我们决定进行水陆联运到营地。

  • There were too few men left to portage the boats.

    留下来对这些船进行陆路搬运的人太少了。

2 N-COUNT 陆上搬运; 水陆联运Portage is also a noun 陆上搬运; 水陆联运

  • The day began with a portage of two hundred yards.

    这一天一开始就搬运了200码。
