bearable
beərəbəl ˈbɛrəbəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 可忍受的；可容忍的；忍耐得住的 If something is bearable, you feel that you can accept it or deal with it. 可忍受的；可容忍的；忍耐得住的

  • A cool breeze made the heat pleasantly bearable.

    一阵凉风吹来，让炎热的天气变得稍微舒适了一些。

SYN tolerable, acceptable, sustainable, manageable
