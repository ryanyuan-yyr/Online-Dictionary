skulk
skʌlk skʌlk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense skulks, present participle skulking, past tense , past participle skulked
VERB 躲藏;潜伏;躲躲闪闪地走;鬼鬼祟祟地活动 If you skulk somewhere, you hide or move around quietly because you do not want to be seen. 躲藏;潜伏;躲躲闪闪地走;鬼鬼祟祟地活动

  • You, meanwhile, will be skulking in the safety of the car. [VERB prep./adv.]

    与此同时，你会安全地藏在汽车里。

  • Harry skulked off. [VERB prep./adv.]

    哈里偷偷地溜走了。

SYN lurk, hide, lie in wait, loiter
