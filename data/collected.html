collected
kəlektɪd kəˈlɛktɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (作品、信件)收成全集的 An author's collected works or letters are all their works or letters published in one book or in a set of books. (作品、信件)收成全集的

  • ...the collected works of Rudyard Kipling.

    拉迪亚德·吉卜林作品全集

  • His collected poems have just been published.

    他的诗歌集刚刚出版。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (尤指在困难或危急情境中)镇定的，泰然自若的 If you say that someone is collected, you mean that they are very calm and self-controlled, especially when they are in a difficult or serious situation. (尤指在困难或危急情境中)镇定的，泰然自若的

  • Police say she was cool and collected during her interrogation.

    警方说她在审问过程中表现得冷静自若。

SYN calm, together [slang] , cool, confident  
3 See also: collect
