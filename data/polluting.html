pollute
pəluːt pəˈlut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pollutes, present participle polluting, past tense , past participle polluted
VERB 污染；弄脏 To pollute water, air, or land means to make it dirty and dangerous to live in or to use, especially with poisonous chemicals or sewage. 污染；弄脏

  • Heavy industry pollutes our rivers with noxious chemicals. [VERB noun]

    重工业产生的有毒化学品会污染我们的河流。

  • A number of beaches in the region have been polluted by sewage pumped into the Irish Sea. [VERB noun]

    这一地区的一些海滩已被排放到爱尔兰海的污水污染了。

SYN contaminate, dirty, mar, poison  
polluted

  • The police have warned the city's inhabitants not to bathe in the polluted river.

    警方已警告该市居民，不要在这条遭污染的河里游泳。
