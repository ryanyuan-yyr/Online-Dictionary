behove
bɪhoʊv bɪˈhuːv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense behoves, past tense , past participle behoved
in AM, use 美国英语用 behoove
VERB 使（某人）应当（或必须、有必要做某事） If it behoves you to do something, it is right, necessary, or useful for you to do it. 使（某人）应当（或必须、有必要做某事） [formal]

    我们必须考虑到这些危险。

SYN be fitting, benefit, be necessary, be wise
