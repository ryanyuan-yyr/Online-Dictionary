bullshit
bʊlʃɪt ˈbʊlˌʃɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense bullshits, present participle bullshitting, past tense , past participle bullshitted
1 N-UNCOUNT 胡说；狗屁 If you say that something is bullshit, you are saying that it is nonsense or completely untrue. 胡说；狗屁 [informal, rude, disapproval]

  • All the rest I said, all that was bullshit.

    我说的其余那些全部都是废话。

2 VERB 对…胡说；哄骗 If you say that someone is bullshitting you, you mean that what they are telling you is nonsense or completely untrue. 对…胡说；哄骗 [informal, rude]

  • Don't bullshit me, Brian!. [VERB noun]

    别骗我，布赖恩！

  • He's basically bullshitting. [VERB]

    他基本上是在胡扯。
