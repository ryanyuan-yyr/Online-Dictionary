hush up
1 PHRASAL VERB 隐瞒;遮掩;不让…张扬 If someone hushes something up, they prevent other people from knowing about it. 隐瞒;遮掩;不让…张扬

  • The crime was so base that everyone wanted to hush it up.

    这一罪行如此卑鄙无耻，大家都不想声张出去。

  • The Ministry desperately tried to hush up the whole affair. [VERB PREP. noun]

    部里拼命想隐瞒整个事件。

SYN cover up, conceal, suppress, sit on [informal]  
2 PHRASAL VERB （当权者）堵住…的嘴，对…封口 If people in authority hush someone up, they try to stop that person revealing information which they want to keep secret. （当权者）堵住…的嘴，对…封口

  • The Conservative Government was only too quick to hush him up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    保守党政府很快就堵住了他的嘴。

SYN silence, gag [informal] , keep quiet, muzzle
