sweetheart
swiːthɑːʳt ˈswitˌhɑrt
Word forms: plural sweethearts
1 N-VOC 亲爱的;宝贝儿 You call someone sweetheart if you are very fond of them. 亲爱的;宝贝儿

  • Happy birthday, sweetheart.

    生日快乐，亲爱的。

SYN dearest, beloved, sweet, angel [informal]  
2 N-COUNT 恋人;情人 Your sweetheart is your boyfriend or your girlfriend. 恋人;情人 [journalism, old-fashioned]

  • I married Shurla, my childhood sweetheart, in Liverpool.

    我在利物浦和我青梅竹马的恋人舒拉结婚了。

SYN love, boyfriend or girlfriend, beloved, lover
