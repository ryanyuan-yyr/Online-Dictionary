ebony
ebəni ˈɛbəni
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 乌木；黑檀 Ebony is a very hard, heavy, dark-coloured wood. 乌木；黑檀

  • ...a small ebony cabinet.

    乌木小橱柜

2 ADJ 乌黑的;黝黑的 Something that is ebony is a very deep black colour. 乌黑的;黝黑的 [literary]

  • He had rich, soft ebony hair.

    他一头浓密、柔软的黑发。

SYN black, dark, jet, raven
