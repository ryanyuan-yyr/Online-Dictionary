go away
1 PHRASAL VERB 离开 If you go away, you leave a place or a person's company. 离开

  • I think we need to go away and think about this. [VERB PREP.]

    我认为我们有必要走开一下去考虑这件事。

2 PHRASAL VERB 外出（尤指度假） If you go away, you leave a place and spend a period of time somewhere else, especially as a holiday. 外出（尤指度假）

  • When you go away on holiday, you need to take extra security precautions. [VERB PREP.]

    外出度假时，需要格外注意安全。

  • Why don't you and I go away this weekend? [VERB PREP.]

    不如咱俩这个周末出去度假吧？
