entertainer
entəʳteɪnəʳ ˌɛntərˈteɪnər
Word forms: plural entertainers
N-COUNT （娱乐节目）表演者;艺人 An entertainer is a person whose job is to entertain audiences, for example by telling jokes, singing, or dancing. （娱乐节目）表演者;艺人

  • Some have called him the greatest entertainer of the twentieth century.

    有人曾称他为20世纪最伟大的艺人。
