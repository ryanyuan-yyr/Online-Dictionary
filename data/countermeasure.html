counter-measure
(also countermeasure)Word forms: plural counter-measures
N-COUNT 对策;对抗手段;反措施 A counter-measure is an action that you take in order to weaken the effect of another action or a situation, or to make it harmless. 对策;对抗手段;反措施

  • Because the threat never developed, we didn't need to take any real countermeasures.

    威胁从未加剧，因此我们没有必要真采取什么对策。
