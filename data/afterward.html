afterward
æftərwərd ˈæftərwərd
(also afterwards)
ADV [ADV with cl] (某事件、某时间) 之后If you do something or if something happens afterward, you do it or it happens after a particular event or time that has already been mentioned. (某事件、某时间) 之后

  • Shortly afterward, police arrested four suspects.

    之后不久，警方逮捕了４名嫌疑犯。
