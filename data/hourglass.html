hourglass
aʊəʳglɑːs ˈaʊrˌglæs
(also hour glass)Word forms: plural hourglasses
N-COUNT （计时用的）沙漏 An hourglass is a device that was used to measure the passing of an hour. It has two round glass sections linked by a narrow channel, and contains sand which takes an hour to flow from the top section into the lower one. （计时用的）沙漏
