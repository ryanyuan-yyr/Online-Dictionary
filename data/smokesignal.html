smoke signal
sməʊk ˈsɪɡnəl
Word forms: plural smoke signals
N-COUNT [usually plural] (政客、商人等暗示观点和意图的)信号，标记,征兆 If someone such as a politician or businessman sends out smoke signals, they give an indication of their views and intentions. This indication is often not clear and needs to be worked out. (政客、商人等暗示观点和意图的)信号，标记,征兆

  • The smoke signals from the central bank suggest further cuts are coming. [+ from]

    来自中央银行的信号表明，马上会有进一步的降息举动。
