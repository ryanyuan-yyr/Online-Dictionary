low life
(also low-life,lowlife)
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 罪犯；恶棍；社会渣滓；下层社会生活 People sometimes use low life to refer in a disapproving way to people who are involved in criminal, dishonest, or immoral activities, or to these activities. 罪犯；恶棍；社会渣滓；下层社会生活 [disapproval]
