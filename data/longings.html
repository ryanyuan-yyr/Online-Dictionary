longing
lɒŋɪŋ lɔːŋ- ˈlɔŋɪŋ
Word forms: plural longings
N-VAR [NOUN to-infinitive] 渴望；热望；憧憬 If you feel longing or a longing for something, you have a rather sad feeling because you want it very much. 渴望；热望；憧憬

  • He felt a longing for the familiar. [+ for]

    他渴望找到知交。

  • Imelda spoke of her longing to return home. [+ to-infinitive]

    伊美尔达说她期望能够回家。

  • I was overwhelmed with longing for those innocent days of early childhood.

    我内心充满着对纯真的童年时光的怀念。

SYN desire, hope, wish, burning
