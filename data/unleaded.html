unleaded
ʌnledɪd ʌnˈlɛdɪd
ADJ (燃料)无铅的 Unleaded fuel contains a smaller amount of lead than most fuels so that it produces fewer harmful substances when it is burned. (燃料)无铅的

  • The new Metro is designed to run on unleaded fuel.

    新的地铁系统设计为使用无铅燃料。

Unleaded is also a noun.

  • All its V8 engines will run happily on unleaded.

    其所有的V8引擎使用无铅汽油都将运转顺畅。
