waterproof
wɔːtəʳpruːf ˈwɔtərˌpruf
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense waterproofs, present participle waterproofing, past tense , past participle waterproofed
1 ADJ 防水的;不透水的 Something which is waterproof does not let water pass through it. 防水的;不透水的

  • Take waterproof clothing — Orkney weather is unpredictable.

    带上防水的衣服吧—奥克尼的天气可是变幻莫测的。

  • Designed to be completely waterproof, the lights are manufactured from heavy duty plastic.

    为了彻底防水，这些灯具均采用重型塑料制成。

SYN watertight, water-resistant, impermeable, water-repellent  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 防水服;雨衣 Waterproofs are items of clothing which do not let water in. 防水服;雨衣 [mainly British]

  • For staying dry you'll want nice lightweight waterproofs to wear over your leathers.

    为了不被淋湿，你需要在皮衣的外面套上一件轻便的防水服。

SYN raincoat, mac [British , informal] , anorak, mackintosh  
3 VERB [usually passive] 对…进行防水处理 If something is waterproofed, it is treated so that water cannot pass through it or damage it. 对…进行防水处理

  • The whole boat has been totally waterproofed. [be VERB-ed]

    整条船全部经过防水处理。

  • Waterproofed fabric pants are more expensive than plastic pants. [VERB-ed]

    防水纤维短裤比塑料短裤贵多了。
