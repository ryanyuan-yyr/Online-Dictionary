titter
tɪtəʳ ˈtɪtər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense titters, present participle tittering, past tense , past participle tittered
VERB (尤指窘迫时)干笑，傻笑，窃笑，哧哧地笑 If someone titters, they give a short nervous laugh, especially when they are embarrassed about something. (尤指窘迫时)干笑，傻笑，窃笑，哧哧地笑

  • Mention sex therapy and most people will titter in embarrassment. [VERB]

    提及性治疗，大多数人会尴尬地偷笑。

SYN snigger, laugh, giggle, chuckle  
Titter is also a noun.

  • Mollie gave an uneasy little titter.

    莫莉不自在地轻声干笑了一下。

tittering

  • There was nervous tittering in the studio audience.

    演播室观众席上传来紧张的低笑声。
