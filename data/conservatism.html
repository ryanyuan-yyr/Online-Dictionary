conservatism
kənsɜːʳvətɪzəm kənˈsɜrvəˌtɪzəm
The spelling Conservatism is also used for meaning 1. 义项1亦可拼作 Conservatism。
1 N-UNCOUNT (政治)保守主义;保守党的政治主张 Conservatism is a political philosophy which believes that if changes need to be made to society, they should be made gradually. You can also refer to the political beliefs of a conservative party in a particular country as Conservatism . (政治)保守主义;保守党的政治主张

  • ...the philosophy of modern Conservatism.

    现代保守主义哲学

2 N-UNCOUNT 保守;守旧 Conservatism is unwillingness to accept changes and new ideas. 保守;守旧

  • The conservatism of the literary establishment in this country is astounding. [+ of]

    这个国家文学界的因循守旧令人震惊。

  • He began his professional life as an accountant, the very model of respectability and conservatism.

    他开始了会计师的职业生涯，一份典型的体面而保守的工作。
