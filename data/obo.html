obo
价格可议 (在广告中标在价格后表示卖主愿意还价)In advertisements, obo is used after a price to indicate that the person who is selling something is willing to accept slightly less money than the sum they have mentioned. obo is a written abbreviation for "or best offer." 价格可议 (在广告中标在价格后表示卖主愿意还价)

  • Family boat. $6,000 obo.

    家用船。$6000，价格可议。
