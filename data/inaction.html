inaction
ɪnækʃən ɪnˈækʃən
N-UNCOUNT [oft with poss] 无为;无作为 If you refer to someone's inaction, you disapprove of the fact that they are doing nothing. 无为;无作为 [disapproval]

  • He is bitter about the inaction of the other political parties. [+ of]

    对其他政党的无作为他多有挖苦。

SYN inactivity, inertia, idleness, immobility  
Quotations
