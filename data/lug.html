lug
lʌg lʌg
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lugs, present participle lugging, past tense , past participle lugged
VERB （用力）拉，拖，提 If you lug a heavy or awkward object somewhere, you carry it there with difficulty. （用力）拉，拖，提 [informal] [Also VERB n]

  • Nobody wants to lug around huge suitcases full of clothes. [VERB noun with adv.]

    谁都不想拖着个装满衣服的大箱子到处走。

  • I hastily packed the hamper and lugged it to the car. [VERB noun prep.]

    我匆忙装好食品篮，吃力地把它拎上车子。

SYN drag, carry, pull, haul
