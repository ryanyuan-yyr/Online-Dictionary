shaving
ʃeɪvɪŋ ˈʃeɪvɪŋ
Word forms: plural shavings
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 刨花;削片;削屑 Shavings are small very thin pieces of wood or other material which have been cut from a larger piece. 刨花;削片;削屑

  • The floor was covered with shavings from his wood carvings.

    地上满是他刻木雕时落下的碎屑。

  • ...metal shavings.

    金属屑

SYN flake, strip, slice, wafer
