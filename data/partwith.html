part with
PHRASAL VERB 放弃，交出，卖掉（贵重物品或舍不得的东西） If you part with something that is valuable or that you would prefer to keep, you give it or sell it to someone else. 放弃，交出，卖掉（贵重物品或舍不得的东西）

  • Buyers might require further assurances before parting with their cash. [VERB PREP. noun]

    买方在付现之前可能要求提供进一步的担保。

  • He parted with much of his collection to pay his gardening bills. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他卖掉了大部分藏品来付园艺费。

SYN give up, abandon, yield, sacrifice
