joyful
dʒɔɪfʊl ˈdʒɔɪfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 令人愉快的；使人高兴的 Something that is joyful causes happiness and pleasure. 令人愉快的；使人高兴的 [formal]

  • Giving birth to a child is both painful and joyful.

    生小孩是件既痛苦又令人高兴的事。

  • A wedding is a joyful celebration of love.

    婚礼就是快乐的爱情庆典。

SYN pleasing, satisfying, engaging, charming  
2 ADJ-GRADED 高兴的；快乐的；欣喜的 Someone who is joyful is extremely happy. 高兴的；快乐的；欣喜的 [formal]

  • We're a very joyful people; we're very musical people and we love music.

    我们是非常快乐的民族；我们能歌善舞，热爱音乐。

SYN delighted, happy, satisfied, glad
