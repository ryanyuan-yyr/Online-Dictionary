incessant
ɪnsesənt ɪnˈsɛsənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不停的;连续的;持续不断的 An incessant process or activity is one that continues without stopping. 不停的;连续的;持续不断的

  • ...incessant rain.

    阴雨连绵

  • ...his incessant demands for affection.

    他对感情的不断索取

SYN constant, endless, continuous, persistent  
incessantly

  • Dee talked incessantly.

    迪伊一直说个不停。
