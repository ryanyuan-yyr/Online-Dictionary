panorama
pænərɑːmə -ræmə ˌpænəˈræmə ˌpænəˈrɑmə
Word forms: plural panoramas
1 N-COUNT 全景；远景 A panorama is a view in which you can see a long way over a wide area of land, usually because you are on high ground. 全景；远景

  • Horton looked out over a panorama of fertile valleys and gentle hills. [+ of]

    霍顿向外望去，肥沃的谷地和平缓的丘陵尽收眼底。

SYN view, prospect, scenery, vista  
2 N-COUNT 概述；概论 A panorama is a broad view of a state of affairs or of a constantly changing series of events. 概述；概论

  • ...a gripping panorama of medieval history.

    引人入胜的中世纪史概论

SYN survey, perspective, overview, overall picture
