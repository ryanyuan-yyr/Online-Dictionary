inventory
ɪnvəntri ˈɪnvəntərɪ -tɔːri ˈɪnvənˌtɔri
Word forms: plural inventories
1 N-COUNT 详细目录;清单 An inventory is a written list of all the objects in a particular place. 详细目录;清单

  • Before starting, he made an inventory of everything that was to stay. [+ of]

    开始之前,他把所有要留下的东西列了详细的清单。

SYN list, record, catalogue, listing  
2 N-VAR 储备;存货 An inventory is a supply or stock of something. 储备;存货 [US]

  • ...one inventory of twelve sails for each yacht. [+ of]

    每艘快艇 12 张帆的储备
