formative
fɔːʳmətɪv ˈfɔrmətɪv
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (时期或经历)决定性格的，影响发展的 A formative period of time or experience is one that has an important and lasting influence on a person's character and attitudes. (时期或经历)决定性格的，影响发展的

  • She was born in Barbados but spent her formative years in east London.

    她出生于巴巴多斯，但是其性格形成的关键期是在伦敦东区度过的。

SYN developmental, sensitive, susceptible, impressionable
