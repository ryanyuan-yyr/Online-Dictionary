radiator
reɪdieɪtəʳ ˈreɪdiˌeɪtər
Word forms: plural radiators
1 N-COUNT 散热器;暖气装置 A radiator is a hollow metal device, usually connected by pipes to a central heating system, that is used to heat a room. 散热器;暖气装置
2 N-COUNT （汽车等的）水箱，冷却器，散热器 The radiator in a car is the part of the engine which is filled with water in order to cool the engine. （汽车等的）水箱，冷却器，散热器
