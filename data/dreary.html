dreary
drɪəri ˈdrɪri
Word forms: comparative drearier, superlative dreariest
ADJ-GRADED 沉闷的;枯燥无味的;令人沮丧的 If you describe something as dreary, you mean that it is dull and depressing. 沉闷的;枯燥无味的;令人沮丧的

  • ...a dreary little town in the Midwest.

    位于中西部的沉闷的小镇

  • They live such dreary lives.

    他们的生活如此乏味！

SYN dull, boring, tedious, routine  
drearily

  • ...a drearily familiar scenario.

    俗套的沉闷剧情
