bulb
bʌlb bʌlb
Word forms: plural bulbs
1 N-COUNT 电灯泡 A bulb is the glass part of an electric lamp, which gives out light when electricity passes through it. 电灯泡

  • The stairwell was lit by a single bulb.

    楼梯间只有一盏灯照明。

SYN light bulb  
2 N-COUNT 球茎；鳞茎 A bulb is a root shaped like an onion that grows into a flower or plant. 球茎；鳞茎

  • ...tulip bulbs.

    郁金香球茎
