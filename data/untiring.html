untiring
ʌntaɪərɪŋ ʌnˈtaɪrɪŋ
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 孜孜不倦的；不懈的 If you describe a person or their efforts as untiring, you approve of them because they continue what they are doing without slowing down or stopping. 孜孜不倦的；不懈的 [approval]

  • ...an untiring fighter for justice, democracy and tolerance.

    为公正、民主和宽容奋斗不息的斗士

SYN tireless, constant, persistent, patient
