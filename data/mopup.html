mop up
1 PHRASAL VERB 揩干;抹干 If you mop up a liquid, you clean it with a cloth so that the liquid is absorbed. 揩干;抹干

  • A waiter mopped up the mess as best he could. [VERB PREP. noun]

    一名侍者尽力抹掉溅出的食物。

  • When the washing machine spurts out water at least we can mop it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    洗衣机喷出水时，我们至少能把水抹干。

  • Michael mopped up quickly with his napkin. [VERB PREP.]

    迈克尔迅速用餐巾将其揩干。

SYN clean up, wash, sponge, mop  
2 PHRASAL VERB 完成;结束;肃清 If you mop up something that you think is undesirable or dangerous, you remove it or deal with it so that it is no longer a problem. 完成;结束;肃清 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The infantry divisions mopped up remaining centres of resistance. [VERB PREP. noun]

    陆军各师肃清了残余的抵抗据点。

  • These reactive molecules are mopped up and made harmless by Vitamin E. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    这些活性分子已被维生素E灭活，没有害处了。

SYN finish off, clear, account for, eliminate
