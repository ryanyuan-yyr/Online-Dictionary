price war
Word forms: plural price wars
N-COUNT 价格战 If competing companies are involved in a price war, they each try to gain an advantage by lowering their prices as much as possible in order to sell more of their products and damage their competitors financially. 价格战 [business]

  • A vicious price war between manufacturers has cut margins to the bone. [+ between]

    制造商之间的恶性价格战已将利润削减到了最低。
