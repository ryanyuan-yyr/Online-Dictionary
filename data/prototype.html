prototype
proʊtətaɪp ˈprəʊtəˌtaɪp ˈproʊtəˌtaɪp
Word forms: plural prototypes
1 N-COUNT [NOUN n] 原型；样品；样本 A prototype is a new type of machine or device which is not yet ready to be made in large numbers and sold. 原型；样品；样本

  • Chris Retzler has built a prototype of a machine called the wave rotor. [+ of]

    克里斯·雷茨勒已制造出了一种叫波转子的机器的样机。

  • ...the first prototype aircraft.

    第一架飞机样机

SYN original, model, precedent, first  
2 N-COUNT 模范；典型 If you say that someone or something is a prototype of a type of person or thing, you mean that they are the first or most typical one of that type. 模范；典型

  • He was the prototype of the elder statesman. [+ of]

    他是老一辈政治家的典型。
