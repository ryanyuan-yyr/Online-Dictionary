smug
smʌg smʌg
ADJ-GRADED 沾沾自喜的;自鸣得意的;洋洋自得的 If you say that someone is smug, you are criticizing the fact they seem very pleased with how good, clever, or lucky they are. 沾沾自喜的;自鸣得意的;洋洋自得的 [disapproval]

  • Thomas and his wife looked at each other in smug satisfaction.

    托马斯和夫人得意地互相望了一眼。

SYN self-satisfied, superior, complacent, conceited  
smugly

  • The Major smiled smugly and sat down.

    少校洋洋得意地笑着坐了下来。

smugness

  • He had the smugness that many lawyers show.

    他有着许多律师身上都有的那种自命不凡。
