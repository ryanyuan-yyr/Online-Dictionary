check up
1 PHRASAL VERB 了解;查清 If you check up on something, you find out information about it. 了解;查清

  • It is certainly worth checking up on your benefit entitlements. [VERB PREP. + on]

    查清楚你可以享受哪些福利是绝对有必要的。

  • The Government employs tax inspectors to check up and make sure people pay all their tax. [VERB PREP.]

    政府雇用税务稽查员做检查工作，以确保人们足额纳税。

2See also:check-up 
3 PHRASAL VERB (常指秘密地)调查，侦查 If you check up on someone, you obtain information about them, usually secretly. (常指秘密地)调查，侦查

  • I'm sure he knew I was checking up on him. [VERB PREP. + on]

    我能肯定，他当时知道我在暗中调查他。

check-up
(also checkup)Word forms: plural check-ups

    这个病是在做常规体检时查出来的。

  • ...a regular check-up at the dentist.
