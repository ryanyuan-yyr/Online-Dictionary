sunder
sʌndəʳ ˈsʌndər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sunders, present participle sundering, past tense , past participle sundered
VERB [usually passive] 分开;使分离;割裂 If people or things are sundered, they are separated or split by something. 分开;使分离;割裂 [literary]

  • The city is being sundered by racial tension. [be VERB-ed]

    该城市因种族关系紧张正在形成分裂。

  • Police moved in to separate the two groups, already sundered by distrust. [VERB-ed]

    警方及时介入将互不信任、业已分化的两组人隔离开。
