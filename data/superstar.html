superstar
suːpəʳstɑːʳ ˈsupərˌstɑr
Word forms: plural superstars
N-COUNT 巨星;超级明星 A superstar is a very famous entertainer or sports player. 巨星;超级明星 [informal]

  • He was more than a footballing superstar, he was a celebrity.

    他不仅仅是足球场上的巨星，也是一位社会名流。

  • ...a Hollywood superstar.

    好莱坞超级巨星

SYN celebrity, star, big name, face [informal]
