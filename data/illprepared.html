ill-prepared
ˌɪlprɪˈpɛəd ɪlpriˈpærd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ, ADJ to-infinitive] 准备不足的;措手不及的 If you are ill-prepared for something, you have not made the correct preparations for it, for example because you are not expecting it to happen. 准备不足的;措手不及的

  • The government was ill-prepared for the problems it now faces. [+ for]

    对于当前面临的问题，政府准备不足。

  • It was ill-prepared to meet this challenge.

    应对这一挑战明显准备不足。
