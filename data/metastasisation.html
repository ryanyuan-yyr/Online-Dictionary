metastasize
mətæstəsaɪz mɪˈtæstəˌsaɪz məˈtæstəˌsaɪz
Word forms: metastasizes, metastasizing, metastasized
V-I (癌细胞)扩散If cancer cells metastasize, they spread to another part of the body. (癌细胞)扩散 [medicine]

  • A checkup revealed a small tumour on the left lower lobe of his lung, but it had not yet metastasized.

    一次检查显示，他的肺部左下叶有一小块肿瘤，但尚未扩散。

  • ...when diagnosis is delayed until cancer has metastasized to other parts of the body.

    ...当诊断被推迟到癌症已经扩散到身体其他部位的时候。
