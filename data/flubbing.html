flub
flʌb flʌb flʌb
Word forms: flubs, flubbing, flubbed
1 V-T 弄糟If you flub something that you are trying to do, you are unsuccessful or you do it badly. 弄糟 [US, informal]

  • If you try a sales technique and flub it, not making a sale, will you try it again?

    如果你尝试了一种销售技巧，但是没有成功，一份单子都没做成，你会再试一次吗？

2 N-COUNT 过失A flub is a mistake or an unsuccessful attempt to do something. 过失 [US, informal]

  • ...a flub that made listeners cringe.

    ...使所有听众都憎恶的一次过失。
