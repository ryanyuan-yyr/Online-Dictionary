secessionist
sɪseʃənɪst sɪˈsɛʃənɪst
Word forms: plural secessionists
N-COUNT [usually plural, NOUN n] 脱离论者;分离论者;独立主义者 Secessionists are people who want their region or group to become separate from the country or larger group to which it belongs. 脱离论者;分离论者;独立主义者

  • ...Lithuanian secessionists.

    立陶宛独立主义者

  • The government is trying to crush a secessionist movement.

    政府正试图镇压一场分离运动。
