knock-on
ADJ [ADJ n] 使产生连锁反应的 If there is a knock-on effect, one action or event causes several other events to happen one after the other. 使产生连锁反应的 [British]

  • The cut in new car prices has had a knock-on effect on the price of used cars.

    新车的降价使二手车价格产生了连锁反应。

  • ...a knock-on boost to the economy.

    连锁反应带来的经济增长
