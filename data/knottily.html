knotty
nɒti ˈnɑti
Word forms: comparative knottier, superlative knottiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 复杂的；难以解决的；棘手的 A knotty problem is complicated and difficult to solve. 复杂的；难以解决的；棘手的

  • The new management team faces some knotty problems.

    新的管理团队面临一些棘手的问题。

SYN puzzling, hard, difficult, complex  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 有节的；多节的 Knotty wood has a lot of small hard areas on it where branches once grew. 有节的；多节的

  • ...the knotty trunk of a hawthorn tree.

    多节的山楂树干

SYN knotted, rough, rugged, bumpy
