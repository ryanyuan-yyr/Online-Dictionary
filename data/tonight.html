tonight
tənaɪt təˈnaɪt
ADV [n ADVERB] 在今晚;在今夜 Tonight is used to refer to the evening of today or the night that follows today. 在今晚;在今夜

  • I'm at home tonight.

    我今晚在家。

  • Tonight, I think he proved to everybody what a great player he was.

    我认为今晚他向所有人证明了他是个多么出色的选手。

  • There they will stay until 11 o'clock tonight.

    他们今晚将一直在那里呆到 11 点钟。

Tonight is also a noun.

  • Tonight is the opening night of the opera.

    今晚是这部歌剧的首演之夜。

  • ...tonight's flight to London.

    今晚去伦敦的航班
