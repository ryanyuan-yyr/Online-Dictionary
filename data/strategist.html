strategist
strætədʒɪst ˈstrætədʒɪst
Word forms: plural strategists
N-COUNT (尤指军事)战略家，善于策划的人 A strategist is someone who is skilled in planning the best way to gain an advantage or to achieve success, especially in war. (尤指军事)战略家，善于策划的人

  • Military strategists had devised a plan that guaranteed a series of stunning victories.

    军事战略家们制订出的计划确保了一连串的辉煌胜利。

  • ...a clever political strategist.

    机智的政治战略家

SYN planner, tactician, schemer, intriguer
