behold
bɪhoʊld biˈhoʊld bɪˈhoʊld
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense beholds, present participle beholding, past tense , past participle beheld
1 VERB 看见；看到 If you behold someone or something, you see them. 看见；看到 [literary]

  • She looked into his eyes and beheld madness. [VERB noun]

    透过他的眼睛，她看到了疯狂。

  • He was a joy to behold. [VERB]

    他看着让人赏心悦目。

SYN look at, see, view, eye  
2 CONVENTION （用于引起注意）看，瞧 People used to say or write 'Behold' to draw people's attention to something. （用于引起注意）看，瞧 [humorous, literary, old-fashioned]

  • Fear Not. Behold The Saviour.

    别怕。看救世主…

  • Behold a series of thrilling photographs of Felix.

    看费利克斯拍的一系列惊心动魄的照片。

3 lo and behold→see: lo
