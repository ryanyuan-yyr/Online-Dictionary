turgid
tɜːʳdʒɪd ˈtɜrdʒɪd
ADJ-GRADED （文章、电影等）晦涩难懂的，索然无味的 If you describe something such as a piece of writing or a film as turgid, you think it is boring and difficult to understand. （文章、电影等）晦涩难懂的，索然无味的

  • He used to make extremely dull, turgid and frankly boring speeches.

    他过去常作些极其乏味、晦涩难懂而且令人非常厌烦的演讲。

  • The rest of the arts scene looks increasingly turgid by comparison.

    比较而言，艺术界的其他领域看起来越发索然无味。

SYN pompous, inflated, windy, high-flown
