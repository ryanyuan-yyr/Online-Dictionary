creditable
kredɪtəbəl ˈkrɛdɪtəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 值得赞扬的;应当认可的 A creditable performance or achievement is of a reasonably high standard. 值得赞扬的;应当认可的

  • They turned out a quite creditable performance.

    他们上演了一场相当精彩的表演。

  • Gazza finished a creditable third.

    加扎以优异的成绩获得了三级荣誉学位。

SYN praiseworthy, worthy, respectable, admirable  
creditably

  • British riders performed creditably.

    英国骑手表现得很出色。

  • She puts in a creditably spirited performance.

    她的表演精彩而且富有激情。

2 ADJ-GRADED 合乎道德的;高尚的 If you describe someone's actions or aims as creditable, you mean that they are morally good. 合乎道德的;高尚的

  • Not a very creditable attitude, I'm afraid.

    这种态度恐怕不怎么道德。
