mire
maɪəʳ maɪr
1 N-SING 泥潭;困境;艰难处境 You can refer to an unpleasant or difficult situation as a mire of some kind. 泥潭;困境;艰难处境 [literary]

  • ...a mire of poverty and ignorance. [+ of]

    贫穷与无知的泥潭

2 N-UNCOUNT 灰尘;烂泥;污泥 Mire is dirt or mud. 灰尘;烂泥;污泥 [literary]

  • ...the muck and mire of sewers and farm- yards.

    下水道和农家庭院的污垢和烂泥

SYN mud, dirt, muck, ooze
