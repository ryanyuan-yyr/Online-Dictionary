leech
liːtʃ litʃ
Word forms: plural leeches
1 N-COUNT 水蛭；蚂蟥 A leech is a small animal which looks like a worm and lives in water. Leeches feed by attaching themselves to other animals and sucking their blood. 水蛭；蚂蟥
2 N-COUNT 吸血鬼；寄生虫 If you describe someone as a leech, you disapprove of them because they deliberately depend on other people, often making money out of them. 吸血鬼；寄生虫 [disapproval]

  • They're just a bunch of leeches cadging off others!

    他们只不过是一帮伸手向人要这要那的寄生虫！

SYN parasite, hanger-on, sycophant, freeloader [slang]
