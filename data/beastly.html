beastly
biːstli ˈbistli
1 ADJ-GRADED （事物）令人不快的，令人厌恶的 If you describe something as beastly, you mean that it is very unpleasant. （事物）令人不快的，令人厌恶的 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • The weather was beastly.

    天气糟透了。

SYN unpleasant, mean, terrible [informal] , awful  
2 ADJ-GRADED （人）刻薄的，恶毒的 If you describe someone as beastly, you mean that they are behaving unkindly. （人）刻薄的，恶毒的 [informal, old-fashioned]

  • He must be wondering why everyone is being so beastly to him.

    他一定正在纳闷为什么大家都对他如此刻薄。

SYN cruel, mean [informal] , nasty, harsh
