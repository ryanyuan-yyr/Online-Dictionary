oddment
ɒdmənt ˈɑdmənt
Word forms: plural oddments
N-COUNT 边角料；残余物；零碎物品 Oddments are unimportant objects of any kind, usually ones that are old or left over from a larger group of things. 边角料；残余物；零碎物品

  • ...searching street markets for interesting jewellery and oddments.

    在街边小摊儿上搜寻有趣的首饰和零碎物件

SYN leftover, bit, scrap, leaving
