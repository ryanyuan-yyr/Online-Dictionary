home in
1 PHRASAL VERB 将注意力集中（于） If you home in on one particular aspect of something, you give all your attention to it. 将注意力集中（于） [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • The critics immediately homed in on the group's newly-elected members. [VERB PREP. + on]

    批评家马上将矛头对准该组织新选出的成员。

2 PHRASAL VERB （导弹等）自动寻（的），自动导向（目标） If something such as a missile homes in on something else, it is aimed at that thing and moves towards it. （导弹等）自动寻（的），自动导向（目标） [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • Two rockets homed in on it from behind without a sound. [VERB PREP. + on]

    两枚火箭从它的后面悄无声息地追了上来。

  • A bank of telephoto lenses homed in on him. [V P on n]

    一整排的远摄镜头都对准了他。
