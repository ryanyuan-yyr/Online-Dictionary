unscheduled
ʌnʃedjuːld ʌnˈʃɛdjuːld -sked-
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 计划外的；出乎意料的 An unscheduled event was not planned to happen, but happens unexpectedly or because someone changes their plans at a late stage. 计划外的；出乎意料的

  • ...an unscheduled meeting with Robin Cook.

    和罗宾·库克的不期而遇

  • The ship made an unscheduled stop at Hawaii.

    轮船临时停靠在夏威夷。
