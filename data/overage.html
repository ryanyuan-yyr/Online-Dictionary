over-age
1 ADJ 超过规定（或合格）年龄的 If you are over-age, you are officially too old to do something. 超过规定（或合格）年龄的

  • He was a couple of months over-age for the youth team.

    他年龄大了几个月，进不了青年队。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 过老的；扮嫩的 You use over-age to describe someone who is doing something that is usually done by much younger people, and which therefore seems inappropriate or silly. 过老的；扮嫩的 [disapproval]

  • ...an over-age nightclub singer.

    过老的夜总会歌手
