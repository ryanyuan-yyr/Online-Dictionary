teething problems
ˈtiːðɪŋ ˈprɒbləmz
N-PLURAL (项目、新产品的)初期问题，萌芽期的困难 If a project or new product has teething problems, it has problems in its early stages or when it first becomes available. (项目、新产品的)初期问题，萌芽期的困难 [British]

  • There are bound to be teething problems with something so new.

    如此新鲜的事物刚开始必然会有些问题。
