punishing
pʌnɪʃɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 繁重费力的；艰难的 A punishing schedule, activity, or experience requires a lot of physical effort and makes you very tired or weak. 繁重费力的；艰难的

  • He claimed his punishing work schedule had made him resort to taking the drug.

    他声称繁重的工作安排使他不得不依靠服药来维持精力。

  • Besides diets, he devised punishing exercise routines.

    除了节制饮食，他还设计了魔鬼健身计划。

SYN hard, taxing, demanding, grinding
