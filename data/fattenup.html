fatten up
1 PHRASAL VERB 喂肥;催肥;养胖 To fatten up an animal or person means to make them fatter, by forcing or encouraging them to eat more food. 喂肥;催肥;养胖

  • They fattened up ducks and geese. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们喂肥了鹅鸭。

  • You're too skinny — we'll have to fatten you up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    你太瘦了，我们要把你养胖些。
