untitled
ʌntaɪtəld ʌnˈtaɪtəld
1 ADJ 无标题的 If something such as a book, film, or song is untitled, it does not have a title. 无标题的

  • The full-length feature, as yet untitled, will include interviews plus footage of their live gigs.

    这部尚未定名的正规长度影片将包括对他们的采访和他们现场表演的片段。

2 ADJ 无头衔的；无爵位的 Someone who is untitled does not have a title such as 'Sir' or 'Lord'. 无头衔的；无爵位的
