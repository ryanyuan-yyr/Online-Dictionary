battering
bætərɪŋ
Word forms: plural batterings
N-COUNT 沉重打击；重创；强烈冲击 If something takes a battering, it suffers very badly as a result of a particular event or action. 沉重打击；重创；强烈冲击

  • Sterling took a battering yesterday as worries grew about the state of Britain's economy.

    由于人们对英国经济状况的担心与日俱增，英镑昨天受到重挫。
