linear
lɪniəʳ ˈlɪniər
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （过程或发展）线性的 A linear process or development is one in which something changes or progresses straight from one stage to another, and has a starting point and an ending point. （过程或发展）线性的

  • Her novel subverts the conventions of linear narrative. It has no neat chronology and no tidy denouement.

    她的小说颠覆了线性叙事的传统，没有清晰的时间线索，也没有简单明了的结局。

  • ...the linear view of time, with the idea that the past is moving into the present and the present into the future.

    线性的时间观，认为过去通向现在，现在通向未来。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 线构的；利用线条的 A linear shape or form consists of straight lines. 线构的；利用线条的

  • ...the sharp, linear designs of the Seventies and Eighties.

    七八十年代轮廓清晰的线条设计

3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （运动或力）成一直线的，沿直线的 Linear movement or force occurs in a straight line rather than in a curve. （运动或力）成一直线的，沿直线的
