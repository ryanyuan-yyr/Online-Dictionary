withering
wɪðərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （眼神、话语）尖刻的，令人难堪的，咄咄逼人的 A withering look or remark is very critical, and is intended to make someone feel ashamed or stupid. （眼神、话语）尖刻的，令人难堪的，咄咄逼人的

  • Deborah Jane's mother gave her a withering look.

    德博拉·简的妈妈狠狠地瞪了她一眼。

  • She launched a withering attack on the Press.

    她向该通讯社发起了一场声势逼人的讨伐。

SYN scornful, blasting, devastating, humiliating
