go off with
1 PHRASAL VERB 移情别恋于；跟（新情人）私奔 If someone goes off with another person, they leave their husband, wife, or lover and have a relationship with that person. 移情别恋于；跟（新情人）私奔

  • I suppose Carolyn went off with some man she'd fallen in love with. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我想卡罗琳与她爱上的那个男人私奔了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 私自拿走 If someone goes off with something that belongs to another person, they leave and take it with them. 私自拿走

  • He's gone off with my passport. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他拿走了我的护照。
