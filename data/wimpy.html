wimpy
wɪmpi
ADJ-GRADED 懦弱的;窝囊的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as wimpy, you disapprove of them because they are weak and seem to lack confidence or determination. 懦弱的;窝囊的 [informal, disapproval]

  • ...a wimpy unpopular schoolboy.

    不合群的懦弱小男生

  • This portrays her as wimpy, but she has a very strong character.

    这把她描绘得很懦弱，其实她性格十分坚强。
