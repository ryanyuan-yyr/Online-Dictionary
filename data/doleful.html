doleful
doʊlfʊl ˈdoʊlfəl
ADJ-GRADED (表情、声音等)悲伤的，忧郁的 A doleful expression, manner, or voice is depressing and miserable. (表情、声音等)悲伤的，忧郁的

  • He gave me a long, doleful look.

    他眼神悲伤地久久望着我。

SYN mournful, sad, gloomy, depressing  
dolefully

  • 'I don't know why they left,' he said dolefully.

    “我不知道他们为什么离开，”他哀愁地说道。
