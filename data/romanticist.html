romanticism
roʊmæntɪsɪzəm roʊˈmæntəˌsɪzəm
1 N-UNCOUNT 浪漫态度;浪漫精神;不切实际的理想（或感受） Romanticism is attitudes, ideals and feelings which are romantic rather than realistic. 浪漫态度;浪漫精神;不切实际的理想（或感受）

  • Her determined romanticism was worrying me.

    她坚定的浪漫主义情怀让我感到担忧。

  • His poetry tended towards a dreamy romanticism.

    他的诗歌带有一种梦幻般的浪漫色彩。

2 N-UNCOUNT 浪漫主义运动 Romanticism is the artistic movement of the eighteenth and nineteenth centuries which was concerned with the expression of the individual's feelings and emotions. 浪漫主义运动
