loaf
loʊf loʊf
Word forms: plural loaves, 3rd person singular present tense loafs, present participle loafing, past tense , past participle loafed
The plural form of the noun is loaves. Loafs is the third person singular present tense of the verb. 名词复数为loaves。loafs为动词的第三人称单数现在式。
1 N-COUNT 一条（面包） A loaf of bread is bread which has been shaped and baked in one piece. It is usually large enough for more than one person and can be cut into slices. 一条（面包）

  • ...a loaf of crusty bread. [+ of]

    一条硬皮面包

  • ...freshly baked loaves.

    新鲜出炉的面包

SYN lump, block, cake, cube  
2 VERB 游荡；闲逛；虚掷光阴 If you loaf, you stand or wait in a place, not doing anything interesting or useful. 游荡；闲逛；虚掷光阴

  • Soldiers loafed at street corners. [VERB]

    士兵们在街角逛荡。

Loaf around means the same as loaf .Loaf around同loaf

  • We had been at Cambridge together, she studying medicine and me loafing around.

    我们一起在剑桥呆过。她学医，我则无所事事。
