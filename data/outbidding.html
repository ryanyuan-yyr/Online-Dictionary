outbid
aʊtbɪd ˌaʊtˈbɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outbids, present participle outbidding
The form outbid is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. outbid的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
VERB 出价高于…；比…报出更高价 If you outbid someone, you offer more money than they do for something that you both want to buy. 出价高于…；比…报出更高价

  • The Museum has antagonised rivals by outbidding them for the world's greatest art treasures. [VERB noun]

    该博物馆为这些世界上最伟大的艺术瑰宝开出了更高价格，惹恼了竞争对手。

  • Last year, Cray failed to take over the software company, when it was outbid by Electronic Data Systems. [VERB noun]

    去年，由于电子数据系统公司竞价更高，克雷未能收购这家软件公司。
