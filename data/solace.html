solace
sɒlɪs ˈsɑlɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT 安慰;慰藉 Solace is a feeling of comfort that makes you feel less sad. 安慰;慰藉 [formal]

  • I found solace in writing when my father died three years ago.

    我父亲 3 年前去世的时候，我从写作中找到了安慰。

  • Henry was inclined to seek solace in drink.

    亨利往往借酒消愁。

SYN comfort, consolation, help, support  
2 N-SING 给人慰藉的事物 If something is a solace to you, it makes you feel less sad. 给人慰藉的事物 [formal]

  • She found the companionship of Marcia a solace.

    她觉得马西娅的陪伴是莫大的安慰。
