hourly
aʊəʳli ˈaʊrli
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 每小时一次的;每小时的 An hourly event happens once every hour. 每小时一次的;每小时的

  • He flipped on the radio to get the hourly news broadcast.

    他打开收音机收听整点新闻。

Hourly is also an adverb.

  • The hospital issued press releases hourly.

    医院每小时发布一次新闻稿。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （薪酬）按钟点计算的 Your hourly earnings are the money that you earn in one hour. （薪酬）按钟点计算的

  • They have little prospect of finding new jobs with the same hourly pay.

    他们不太可能找到时薪和原来一样的新工作。
