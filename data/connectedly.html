connect
kənekt kəˈnɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense connects, present participle connecting, past tense , past participle connected
1 V-RECIP-ERG (使)连接 If something or someone connects one thing to another, or if one thing connects to another, the two things are joined together. (使)连接 [Also VERB]

  • You can connect the machine to your hi-fi. [VERB noun + to]

    你可以把机器连在高保真音响上。

  • The traditional method is to enter the exchanges at night and connect the wires. [VERB noun]

    传统的办法是晚上进入电话局，把线连上。

  • Two cables connect to each corner of the plate. [VERB + to]

    两条电缆连到金属板的每个角上。

  • ...a television camera connected to the radio telescope. [VERB-ed]

    连接射电望远镜的电视摄影机

SYN link, join, couple, attach  
2 VERB 使连接，使接通(电源、水源等) If a piece of equipment or a place is connected to a source of power or water, it is joined to that source so that it has power or water. 使连接，使接通(电源、水源等) [Also VERB n + to]

  • These appliances should not be connected to power supplies. [be VERB-ed + to]

    这些家用电器不应该接通电源。

  • Ischia was now connected to the mainland water supply. [VERB-ed]

    伊斯基亚这时已经通上了大陆的水。

Connect up means the same as connect .connect up同 connect

  • The shower is easy to install — it needs only to be connected up to the hot and cold water supply.

    淋浴器很容易安装—只要接上冷热水就行。

  • They turned the barricade into a potential death trap by connecting it up to the mains.

    他们给路障接上电源，让它变成了一个潜在的死亡陷阱。

3 VERB （接线员）给…接通电话 If a telephone operator connects you, he or she enables you to speak to another person by telephone. （接线员）给…接通电话 [Also VERB n + to]

  • To call the police, an ambulance or the fire brigade dial 999 and the operator will connect you. [VERB noun]

    呼叫警察、救护车或消防队，请拨打999，接线员会为您接通电话。

  • He asked to be connected to the central switchboard. [be VERB-ed + to]

    他请求接到总机。

4 V-RECIP-ERG (使)相连;(使)相通 If two things or places connect or if something connects them, they are joined and people or things can pass between them. (使)相连;(使)相通 [Also VERB]

  • ...the long hallway that connects the rooms. [VERB noun]

    连接各个房间的长过道

  • The fallopian tubes connect the ovaries with the uterus. [VERB noun + with]

    输卵管连接着卵巢和子宫。

  • His workshop connected with a small building in the garden. [VERB + with]

    他的工作间连着花园里的一栋小楼。

  • The two rooms have connecting doors. [VERB-ing]

    这两间屋子有门相通。

5 V-RECIP (火车或飞机等)联运，衔接 If one train or plane, for example, connects with another, it arrives at a time which allows passengers to change to the other one in order to continue their journey. (火车或飞机等)联运，衔接 [Also VERB]

  • ...a train connecting with a ferry to Ireland. [VERB + with]

    与开往爱尔兰的渡船联运的火车

  • My connecting plane didn't depart for another six hours. [VERB-ing]

    我搭乘的联运航班又过了6个小时才起飞。

6 V-ERG (使)换乘(飞机或火车) If you connect to a particular plane or train, or if another plane or train connects you to it, you change to that plane or train from another one in order to continue your journey. (使)换乘(飞机或火车) [Also VERB n + to]

  • ...business travellers wanting to connect to a long-haul flight. [VERB + to]

    想换乘长途航班的商务旅行者

  • That will connect you with time to spare for the seven o'clock Concorde. [VERB noun]

    那样时间会有富余，你可以换乘7点的“协和”式客机。

7 VERB 把…联系起来 If you connect a person or thing with something, you realize that there is a link or relationship between them. 把…联系起来

  • I hoped he would not connect me with that now-embarrassing review I'd written seven years earlier. [V n + with/to]

    我希望他不会把我和我7年前写的那篇现在看来令人难堪的评论联系起来。

  • I wouldn't have connected the two things. [VERB noun]

    我就不会把这两件事联系起来。

8 VERB 使…有联系 Something that connects a person or thing with something else shows or provides a link or relationship between them. 使…有联系

  • A search of Brady's house revealed nothing that could connect him with the robberies. [V n + with/to]

    对布雷迪住所的搜查没有发现任何能证明他与这些抢劫案有关的东西。

  • What connects them? [VERB noun]

    他们之间有什么联系？

9 V-RECIP (与…)有同感，产生共鸣 If you connect with someone, you feel a sense of agreement and familiarity with them because you have the same kind of ideas. (与…)有同感，产生共鸣 [Also pl-n V]

  • If you stand on stage and share your view of the world, people will connect with you. [VERB with noun]

    如果你站在舞台上，分享自己对世界的看法，人们会与你产生共鸣的。
