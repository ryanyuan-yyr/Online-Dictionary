demarcate
diːmɑːʳkeɪt dɪmɑːrk- diˈmɑrkeɪt dɪˈmɑrkeɪt ˈdimɑrˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense demarcates, present participle demarcating, past tense , past participle demarcated
VERB 划定…的界线(或界限) If you demarcate something, you establish its boundaries or limits. 划定…的界线(或界限) [formal]

  • A special UN commission was formed to demarcate the border.

    联合国成立了一个特别委员会来划定边界。

SYN delimit, mark, separate, determine
