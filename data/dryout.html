dry out
1 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)变干;(使)干透 If something dries out or is dried out, it loses all the moisture that was in it and becomes hard. (使)变干;(使)干透 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • If the soil is allowed to dry out the tree could die. [VERB PREP.]

    如果任由土壤变干，这棵树可能会枯死。

  • The cold winds dry out your skin very quickly. [VERB PREP. noun]

    冷风很快会把你的皮肤吹干。

2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG (使)戒酒 If someone dries out or is dried out, they are cured of addiction to alcohol. (使)戒酒 [informal] [Also be V-ed P]

  • He checked into Cedars Sinai Hospital to dry out. [VERB PREP.]

    他住进雪松西奈医院去戒酒。

  • I approved the doctor's order to keep him in the room till he was dried out. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    我遵照医嘱,将他关在屋子里直到他戒酒成功。

SYN become sober, give up drinking, give up alcohol, go on the wagon
