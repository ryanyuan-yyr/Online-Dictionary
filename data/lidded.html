lidded
lɪdɪd ˈlɪdɪd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （容器）有盖的，带盖的 Lidded is used to describe a container that has a lid. （容器）有盖的，带盖的

  • ...a lidded saucepan.

    有盖炖锅

2 ADJ 眼皮半闭（或全闭）的 When someone has lidded eyes, their eyelids are partly or fully closed. 眼皮半闭（或全闭）的 [literary]

  • Julie squinted at her through lidded eyes.

    朱莉耷拉着眼皮斜眼看了看她。

  • My own eyes had felt heavy-lidded, but now I was wide awake.

    我自己刚才也觉得眼睛都快睁不开了，不过现在我清醒得很。
