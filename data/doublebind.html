double bind
Word forms: plural double binds
N-COUNT [usually singular] 两难境地;进退维谷 If you are in a double bind, you are in a very difficult situation, because whatever decision you make will have bad results. 两难境地;进退维谷 [Also + of]

  • Women are caught in a double bind, marginalised in the community if they are not wives and mothers, under excessive pressure to be perfect if they are.

    女性陷入了两难境地，如果她不为人妻为人母就会被社会边缘化，如果她为人妻为人母就会承受成为完美的妻子和母亲的巨大压力。
