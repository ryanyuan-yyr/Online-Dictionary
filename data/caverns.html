cavern
kævəʳn ˈkævərn
Word forms: plural caverns
1 N-COUNT 大地穴;大洞穴 A cavern is a large deep cave. 大地穴;大洞穴
2 N-COUNT 黑洞洞的大厦(或大房间) If you describe the inside of a building or a room as a cavern, you mean that it is very large and, usually, dark or without much furniture. 黑洞洞的大厦(或大房间)

  • The kitchen now is a dark cavern, with an antiquated black stove in a corner.

    厨房现在黑洞洞的，角落里有只破旧的黑色炉子。
