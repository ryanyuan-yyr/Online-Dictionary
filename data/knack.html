knack
næk næk
Word forms: plural knacks
N-COUNT [usually singular] 诀窍；本领 A knack is a particularly clever or skilful way of doing something successfully, especially something which most people find difficult. 诀窍；本领

  • He's got the knack of getting people to listen. [+ of/for]

    他有使人倾听的本领。

SYN skill, art, ability, facility
