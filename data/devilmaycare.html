devil-may-care ˌdɛvəlmeɪˈkɛr
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无所顾忌的;满不在乎的;逍遥自在的 If you say that someone has a devil-may-care attitude, you mean that they seem relaxed and do not seem worried about the consequences of their actions. 无所顾忌的;满不在乎的;逍遥自在的 [approval]

  • I love Italian food and wine and the devil-may-care attitude of the people.

    我喜欢意大利的食物、美酒和人们那种天塌当被盖的心态。

SYN happy-go-lucky, casual, careless, easy-going
