opacity
oʊpæsɪti oʊˈpæsəti
1 N-UNCOUNT 模糊；不透明 Opacity is the quality of being difficult to see through. 模糊；不透明 [formal]

  • Opacity of the eye lens can be induced by deficiency of certain vitamins. [+ of]

    眼球晶状体的混浊可由缺乏某些维生素造成。

  • He insisted that the mineral content of the water determined the opacity.

    他坚持认为水的清澈程度取决于其中矿物质的含量。

SYN opaqueness, cloudiness, density, obscurity  
2 N-UNCOUNT 费解；难懂；晦涩 If you refer to something's opacity, you mean that it is difficult to understand. 费解；难懂；晦涩 [formal]

  • Its dramatic nuances were often generalised to the point of opacity.

    它那戏剧性的微妙之处常被含糊处理到了晦涩的地步。
