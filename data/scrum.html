scrum
skrʌm skrʌm
Word forms: plural scrums
1 N-COUNT (英式橄榄球)并列争球，密集争球 In rugby, a scrum is a tight group formed by players from both sides pushing against each other with their heads down in an attempt to get the ball. (英式橄榄球)并列争球，密集争球
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 拥挤的人群 A scrum is a group of people who are close together and pushing against each other. 拥挤的人群 [British]

  • She pushed through the scrum of photographers. [+ of]

    她从挤作一团的摄影记者中挤了过去。

  • ...the scrum of shoppers. [+ of]

    熙熙攘攘的购物者

SYN crowd, group, mob, lot
