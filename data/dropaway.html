drop away
PHRASAL VERB (地势)走低,降低 If land or ground drops away, it slopes down so that it is at a lower level to where you are or from a particular point that has been mentioned. (地势)走低,降低

  • To the south the hills dropped away to farmland. [VERB PREP. prep.]

    山势在南边一路下倾延伸至农田。

  • From the house, the garden drops away, surrounded by a rural scene of woodland. [VERB PREP.]

    从房子的位置,花园开始变低,四周树木葱郁，一派田园风光。
