nadir
neɪdɪəʳ -dər ˈneɪdər ˈneɪˌdɪr
1 N-SING [usually with poss] 最低点;最不幸的时刻 The nadir of something such as someone's career or the history of an organization is its worst time. 最低点;最不幸的时刻 [literary]

  • 1945 to 1946 was the nadir of Truman's presidency. [+ of]

    1945至1946年的那段时间是杜鲁门总统执政最艰难的时期。

SYN bottom, depths, lowest point, rock bottom  
2 N-SING 天底 In astronomy, the nadir is the point at which the sun or moon is directly below you, on the other side of the earth. 天底 [technical]
