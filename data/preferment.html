preferment
prɪfɜːʳmənt priˈfɜrmənt prɪˈfɜrmənt
Word forms: plural preferments
N-VAR 晋升；晋职；提升 Preferment is the act of being given a better and more important job in an organization. 晋升；晋职；提升 [formal]

  • He was told by the governors that he could expect no further preferment.

    他被董事们告之晋升无望了。

SYN promotion, rise, upgrading, dignity
