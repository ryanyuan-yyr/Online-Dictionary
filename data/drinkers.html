drinker
drɪŋkəʳ ˈdrɪŋkər
Word forms: plural drinkers
1 N-COUNT 常饮…者;常喝…者 If someone is a tea drinker or a beer drinker, for example, they regularly drink tea or beer. 常饮…者;常喝…者

  • Are you a coffee drinker?

    你常喝咖啡吗？

2 N-COUNT 饮酒者;(尤指)酗酒者，酒徒 If you describe someone as a drinker, you mean that they drink alcohol, especially in large quantities. 饮酒者;(尤指)酗酒者，酒徒

  • You don't need to be a smoker or a drinker to risk heart disease.

    并不是只有抽烟喝酒的人才会犯心脏病。

  • I'm not a heavy drinker.

    我酒瘾不大。

SYN alcoholic, drunk, boozer [informal] , soak [slang]
