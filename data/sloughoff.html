slough off
slʌf
1 →see: slough 
2 PHRASAL VERB 放弃;抛弃 If you slough off something that you no longer want or need, you get rid of it. 放弃;抛弃 [written] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She tried hard to slough off her old personality. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    她努力告别从前的个性。

  • The nation states of Eastern Europe finally sloughed off their totalitarian regimes. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    东欧民族国家最终抛弃了极权统治。
