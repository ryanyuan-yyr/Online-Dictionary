unattended
ʌnətendɪd ˌʌnəˈtɛndɪd ˌʌnəˈtɛndɪd
ADJ [ADJ after verb, ADJ n, verb-link ADJ] 无人照料的；无人看管的 When people or things are left unattended, they are not being watched or looked after. 无人照料的；无人看管的

  • Never leave young children unattended near any pool or water tank.

    千万不能放任小孩呆在水池或水槽附近不管。

  • An unattended bag was spotted near the platform at Gatwick.

    在盖特威克机场站的站台附近发现了一个无主袋子。

  • The mob broke into the family house while it was unattended and started four fires.

    这群匪徒闯进了那所无人看管的民宅，在4个地方放了火。

SYN abandoned, left alone, unguarded, ignored
