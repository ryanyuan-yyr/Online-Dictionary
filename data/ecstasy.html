ecstasy
ekstəsi ˈɛkstəsi
Word forms: plural ecstasies
1 N-VAR 狂喜;极乐 Ecstasy is a feeling of very great happiness. 狂喜;极乐

  • ...a state of almost religious ecstasy.

    近乎宗教狂喜的状态

  • ...the agony and ecstasy of holiday romance.

    假日恋情的大喜大悲

SYN rapture, delight, joy, enthusiasm  
2 N-UNCOUNT 摇头丸;迷幻药 Ecstasy is an illegal drug which makes people feel happy and energetic. 摇头丸;迷幻药
3 PHRASE 欣喜若狂；处于狂热状态 If you are in ecstasy about something, you are very excited about it. If you go into ecstasies, you become very excited. 欣喜若狂；处于狂热状态

  • My father was in ecstasy when I won my scholarship.

    我获得奖学金时父亲欣喜若狂。

  • She went into ecstasies over actors.

    她对男演员们着了迷。
