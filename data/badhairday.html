bad hair day
Word forms: plural bad hair days
N-COUNT [usually singular] （尤指因头发乱糟糟而）不顺心的一天，紧张的一天 People sometimes say they are having a bad hair day when they do not feel very happy or relaxed, especially because their hair does not look good. （尤指因头发乱糟糟而）不顺心的一天，紧张的一天 [informal]

  • All this fuss is because Carol is having a bad hair day.

    就因为卡萝尔今天不太顺心，所以弄得大家鸡飞狗跳。
