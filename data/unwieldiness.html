unwieldy
ʌnwiːldi ʌnˈwildi
1 ADJ-GRADED 沉甸甸的；粗笨的；笨重的 If you describe an object as unwieldy, you mean that it is difficult to move or carry because it is so big or heavy. 沉甸甸的；粗笨的；笨重的

  • They came panting up to his door with their unwieldy baggage.

    他们提着笨重的行李气喘吁吁地来到他门口。

SYN bulky, massive, hefty, clumsy  
2 ADJ-GRADED 臃肿的；庞大的 If you describe a system as unwieldy, you mean that it does not work very well as a result of it being too large or badly organized. 臃肿的；庞大的

  • His firm must contend with the unwieldy Russian bureaucracy.

    他的公司必须与俄罗斯庞大的官僚机构周旋。

  • ...an unwieldy legal system.

    臃肿的法律体系

SYN awkward, cumbersome, inconvenient, burdensome
