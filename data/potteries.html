pottery
pɒtəri ˈpɑtəri
Word forms: plural potteries
1 N-UNCOUNT 陶器；陶瓷器皿 You can use pottery to refer to pots, dishes, and other objects which are made from clay and then baked in an oven until they are hard. 陶器；陶瓷器皿
2 N-UNCOUNT 陶土；黏土 You can use pottery to refer to the hard clay that some pots, dishes, and other objects are made of. 陶土；黏土

  • Some bowls were made of pottery and wood.

    一些碗是用陶土和木头制成的。

3 N-UNCOUNT 制陶手艺；陶艺；陶瓷制造 Pottery is the craft or activity of making objects out of clay. 制陶手艺；陶艺；陶瓷制造
4 N-COUNT 陶器工厂；陶器作坊；陶窑 A pottery is a factory or other place where pottery is made. 陶器工厂；陶器作坊；陶窑
