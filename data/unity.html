unity
juːnɪti ˈjunəti
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft adjective NOUN] 联合（状态）；统一（状态） Unity is the state of different areas or groups being joined together to form a single country or organization. 联合（状态）；统一（状态）

  • Senior politicians met today to discuss the future of European economic unity.

    高级政要今天会晤，探讨欧洲经济一体化的未来。

  • ...German unity.

    德国的统一

SYN union, unification, coalition, federation  
2 N-UNCOUNT 团结；一致 When there is unity, people are in agreement and act together for a particular purpose. 团结；一致

  • ...a renewed unity of purpose. [+ of]

    目标的重新确认一致

  • Speakers at the rally mouthed sentiments of unity.

    集会上的发言人空泛地呼吁大家要团结一致。

  • The choice was meant to create an impression of party unity.

    这个选择意在给人一种党内团结的印象。

SYN agreement, accord, consensus, peace
