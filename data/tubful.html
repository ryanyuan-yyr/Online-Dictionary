tub
tʌb tʌb
Word forms: plural tubs
1 N-COUNT （大小不一的）桶,盆,缸,盆 A tub is a deep container of any size. （大小不一的）桶,盆,缸,盆

  • He peeled the paper top off a little white tub and poured the cream into his coffee.

    他把小白盒上的纸盖撕去，然后把奶油倒进他的咖啡里。

  • Shrubs can be grown in tubs or large containers.

    灌木可以种植在盆里或大型容器中。

A tub of something is the amount of it contained in a tub.一桶(之物);一桶之量

  • She would eat four tubs of ice cream in one sitting.

    她以前常常一次就吃4桶冰激凌。

2 N-COUNT 同 bathtub。 A tub is the same as a bathtub . 同 bathtub。 [US]

  • She lay back in the tub.

    她向后靠着躺在浴缸里。

3 See also: hot tub
