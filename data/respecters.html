respecter
rɪspektəʳ rɪˈspɛktə rɪˈspɛktər
Word forms: plural respecters
1 N-COUNT （信仰的）拥护者;重视(某种观念）的人 If you say that someone is a respecter of something such as a belief or idea, you mean that they behave in a way which shows that they have a high opinion of it. （信仰的）拥护者;重视(某种观念）的人

  • Ford was a respecter of proprieties and liked to see things done properly. [+ of]

    福特非常重视礼仪，喜欢把事情办得妥帖得体。

2 PHRASE （对规则、传统的）无视，不顾及 If you say that someone or something is no respecter of a rule or tradition, you mean that the rule or tradition is not important to them. （对规则、传统的）无视，不顾及

  • The British Sports Council is no respecter of privacy or careers as it tries to stamp out the cheats.

    为了杜绝舞弊行为，英国体育委员会丝毫不会顾及个人的隐私或职业。

  • Accidents and sudden illnesses are no respecters of age.

    不管什么年龄都有可能遭遇意外事故和突发疾病。
