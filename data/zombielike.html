zombie
zɒmbi ˈzɑmbi
Word forms: plural zombies
1 N-COUNT 麻木迟钝的人;毫无生气的人;无精打采的人 You can describe someone as a zombie if their face or behaviour shows no feeling, understanding, or interest in what is going on around them. 麻木迟钝的人;毫无生气的人;无精打采的人

  • I didn't want to do anything, I just sat at home and vegetated. I became a total zombie.

    我什么也不想做，就是呆坐在家里，过着百无聊赖的生活。我完全变成了行尸走肉。

  • Without sleep you will become a zombie at work.

    不睡觉的话，工作起来会变得反应迟钝。

2 N-COUNT （恐怖故事或某些宗教中的）僵尸，还魂尸 In horror stories and some religions, a zombie is a dead person who has been brought back to life. （恐怖故事或某些宗教中的）僵尸，还魂尸
