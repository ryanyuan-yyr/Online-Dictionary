jump in
PHRASAL VERB 匆忙行动；不假思索地做 If you jump in, you act quickly, often without thinking much about what you are doing. 匆忙行动；不假思索地做

  • The Government had to jump in and purchase millions of dollars worth of supplies. [VERB PREP.]

    政府不得不匆忙购进价值数百万美元的补给品。

SYN dive in, leap in, plunge in, swoop in
