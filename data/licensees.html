licensee
laɪsənsiː ˌlaɪsənˈsi
Word forms: plural licensees
1 N-COUNT 执照领有人；许可证接受者 A licensee is a person or organization that has been given a licence. 执照领有人；许可证接受者 [formal]
2 N-COUNT 售酒执照领有人 A licensee is someone who has been given a licence to sell alcoholic drinks, for example in a pub. 售酒执照领有人 [British]
