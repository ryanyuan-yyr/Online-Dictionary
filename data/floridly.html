florid
flɒrɪd flɔːr- ˈflɔrɪd ˈflɑrɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 过分花哨的;过分修饰的 If you describe something as florid, you disapprove of the fact that it is complicated and extravagant rather than plain and simple. 过分花哨的;过分修饰的 [disapproval]

  • ...florid language.

    过于华丽的词藻

2 ADJ-GRADED (脸色)发红的，红润的 Someone who is florid always has a red face. (脸色)发红的，红润的

  • Jacobs was a stout, florid man.

    雅各布斯身材结实，脸色红润。

SYN flushed, ruddy, rubicund, high-coloured
