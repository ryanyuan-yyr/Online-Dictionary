cattle market
Word forms: plural cattle markets
1 N-COUNT 牛市场 A cattle market is a market where cattle are bought and sold. 牛市场
2 N-COUNT 以貌取人的场合(如迪斯科舞厅、选美比赛等) If you refer to an event such as a disco or a beauty contest as a cattle market, you disapprove of it because women are considered there only in terms of their sexual attractiveness. 以貌取人的场合(如迪斯科舞厅、选美比赛等) [disapproval]
