retool
riːtuːl riːˈtuːl riˈtul
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense retools, present participle retooling, past tense , past participle retooled
VERB 重新装备，更换(机器设备等) If the machines in a factory or the items of equipment used by a firm are retooled, they are replaced or changed so that they can do new tasks. 重新装备，更换(机器设备等)

  • Each time the product changes, the machines have to be retooled. [be VERB-ed]

    每次更换产品，就得重新装备机器。

retooling

  • Retooling, or recasting new toy moulds, is a slow and expensive process.

    更换机器或者重新铸造新的玩具模具都是一个耗费时间和财力的过程。
