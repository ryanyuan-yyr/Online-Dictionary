unfathomable
ʌnfæðəməbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （事物）高深莫测的，深奥的 If you describe something as unfathomable, you mean that it cannot be understood or explained, usually because it is very strange or complicated. （事物）高深莫测的，深奥的

  • For some unfathomable reason, there are no stairs where there should be.

    不知什么原因，本该有楼梯的地方却没有。

  • How odd life was, how unfathomable, how profoundly unjust.

    生活多么离奇，多么莫测，多么不公啊！

2 ADJ （人或表情）难以捉摸的 If you use unfathomable to describe a person or the expression on their face, you mean that you cannot tell what they are thinking or what they intend to do. （人或表情）难以捉摸的 [literary]

  • ...a strange, unfathomable and unpredictable individual.

    一个古怪、难以捉摸、反复无常的人

  • ...the dark eyes that right now seemed opaque and unfathomable.

    此刻显得浑浊莫测的黑眼睛
