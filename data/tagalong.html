tag along
PHRASAL VERB （尤指未经要求）跟随，尾随 If someone goes somewhere and you tag along, you go with them, especially when they have not asked you to. （尤指未经要求）跟随，尾随

  • I let him tag along because he had not been too well recently. [VERB PREP.]

    我让他跟着我，因为最近他身体不舒服。

  • She seems quite happy to tag along with them. [VERB PREP. + with]

    她好像很乐意跟着他们。
