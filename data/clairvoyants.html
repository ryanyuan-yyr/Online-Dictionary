clairvoyant
kleəʳvɔɪənt klɛəˈvɔɪənt klɛrˈvɔɪənt
Word forms: plural clairvoyants
1 ADJ 未卜先知的;通灵的 Someone who is believed to be clairvoyant is believed to know about future events or to be able to communicate with dead people. 未卜先知的;通灵的

  • ...clairvoyant powers.

    未卜先知的禀赋

SYN psychic, visionary, prophetic, prescient  
clairvoyance

  • ...his well-attested powers of telepathy and clairvoyance.

    他那被证实灵验的心灵感应与预知未来的能力

2 N-COUNT 未卜先知的人;通灵大师 A clairvoyant is someone who claims to be clairvoyant. 未卜先知的人;通灵大师

  • You did not have to be a clairvoyant to see that the war would go on.

    就算没有未卜先知的能力也能料到战争会持续下去。

SYN psychic, diviner, prophet, visionary  
Quotations
