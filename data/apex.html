apex
eɪpeks ˈeɪˌpɛks
Word forms: plural apexes
1 N-SING （机构、体系的）最高地位，最高职位 The apex of an organization or system is the highest and most important position in it. （机构、体系的）最高地位，最高职位

  • At the apex of the party was its central committee.

    该党的最高权力机构是中央委员会。

SYN culmination, top, crown, height  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 尖端；顶点 The apex of something is its pointed top or end. 尖端；顶点

  • ...the apex of the pyramid.

    金字塔的顶端

SYN highest point, point, top, tip
