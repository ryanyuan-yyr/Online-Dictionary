stipulate
stɪpjʊleɪt ˈstɪpjʊlɪt ˈstɪpjʊlɪt ˈstɪpjəˌleɪt ˈstɪpjʊˌleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stipulates, present participle stipulating, past tense , past participle stipulated
VERB 规定;明确要求 If you stipulate a condition or stipulate that something must be done, you say clearly that it must be done. 规定;明确要求

  • She could have stipulated that she would pay when she collected the computer. [V that/wh]

    她本可以要求她来取电脑时付钱。

  • International rules stipulate the number of foreign entrants. [VERB noun]

    国际规则规定了外国参赛者的人数。

SYN specify, state, agree, require  
stipulation

  • Clifford's only stipulation is that his clients obey his advice.

    克利福德唯一的要求就是客户必须听从他的建议。
