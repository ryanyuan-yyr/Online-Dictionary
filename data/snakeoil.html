snake oil
sneɪk ɔɪl
N-UNCOUNT 蛇油; 狗皮膏药; 号称灵药但并无药效的江湖药Snake oil is used to refer to any substance that is claimed to be a medical remedy but which in fact has no healing properties. 蛇油; 狗皮膏药; 号称灵药但并无药效的江湖药 [disapproval]

  • Traditional and folk remedies are often labelled as snake oil and witches' potions.

    传统和民间治疗方法常被贬为万金油和巫师药。

  • ...these modern-day snake oil salesmen.

    ...这些新时代的狗皮膏药叫卖者。
