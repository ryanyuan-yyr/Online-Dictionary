national debt
Word forms: national debts
N-COUNT [usu sing] 国债A country's national debt is all the money that the government of the country has borrowed and still owes. 国债

  • He talked about the importance of reducing the national debt.

    他谈了减少国债的重要性。

  • No wonder then that the national debts of all the civilized countries in the world are reaching astronomical proportions.

    难怪世界上所有文明国家的国债都近乎天文数字。
