sleight of hand slaɪt əv hænd
Word forms: plural sleights of hand
N-VAR (偷东西或变戏法时)敏捷而熟练的手法;空空妙手 Sleight of hand is the deceiving of someone in a skilful way. (偷东西或变戏法时)敏捷而熟练的手法;空空妙手

  • He accused Mr MacGregor of 'sleight of hand'.

    他指控麦格雷戈先生“使用障眼法”。

  • ...a financial sleight of hand.

    在财务上巧做手脚

SYN trickery, fraud, deception, cunning
