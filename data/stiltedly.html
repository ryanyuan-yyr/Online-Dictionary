stilted
stɪltɪd ˈstɪltɪd
ADJ-GRADED (言谈)呆板的，生硬的，不自然的 If someone speaks in a stilted way, they speak in a formal or unnatural way, for example because they are not relaxed. (言谈)呆板的，生硬的，不自然的

  • We made polite, stilted conversation.

    我们客套了一番。

  • His delivery was stilted and occasionally stumbling.

    他的发言很生硬，有时还打结巴。

SYN stiff, forced, wooden, laboured
