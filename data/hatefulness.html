hateful
heɪtfʊl ˈheɪtfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 极坏的;可恶的;令人讨厌的 Someone or something that is hateful is extremely bad or unpleasant. 极坏的;可恶的;令人讨厌的 [old-fashioned]

  • I'm sorry. That was a hateful thing to say.

    对不起，我不该说那么难听的话。

2 ADJ-GRADED 心怀仇恨的;充满憎恨的 Someone who is hateful hates someone else. 心怀仇恨的;充满憎恨的

  • These are not necessarily hateful, malicious people.

    这些人不一定心怀仇恨，恶意伤人。

  • ...a lying, hateful and racist campaign.

    充满谎言、仇恨和种族主义情绪的运动

SYN malicious, malevolent, spiteful, hating
