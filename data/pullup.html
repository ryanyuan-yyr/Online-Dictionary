pull up
1 PHRASAL VERB （车或驾车者）减速停下 When a vehicle or driver pulls up, the vehicle slows down and stops. （车或驾车者）减速停下

  • The cab pulled up and the driver jumped out. [VERB PREP.]

    出租车减速停下，司机跳了出来。

SYN stop, park, halt, arrive  
2 PHRASAL VERB 把（椅子）拉到近处坐下 If you pull up a chair, you move it closer to something or someone and sit on it. 把（椅子）拉到近处坐下 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He pulled up a chair behind her and put his chin on her shoulder. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他拉过一把椅子坐在她后面，将下巴抵在她的肩膀上。

3 PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）突然停下；（使）猛地停住 If you pull up or if something pulls you up, you suddenly stop what you are doing. （使）突然停下；（使）猛地停住

  • Suddenly, he pulled up sharply in his stride, and fell to the floor. [VERB PREP.]

    突然，原本大步流星的他猛地停住了脚步，倒在了地上。

  • She recognized at the same instant Rachel's presence. It pulled her up short. [VERB noun PREP.]

    雷切尔一出现她就认出她来了，随即停下了手中的活儿。

4 PHRASAL VERB 提高；改进 If someone pulls you up or if you pull yourself up, you improve your situation or your skill at something. 提高；改进

  • We had a very good mathematics mistress who pulled me up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我们有个很出色的女数学老师，她帮我提高了水平。

  • He was made redundant and now he's trying to pull himself up again. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    他之前作为冗员被裁，现在他正试图再一次奋发图强。
