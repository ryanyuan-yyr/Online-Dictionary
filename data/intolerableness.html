intolerable
ɪntɒlərəbəl ɪnˈtɑlərəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 不能忍受的;无法容忍的 If you describe something as intolerable, you mean that it is so bad or extreme that no one can bear it or tolerate it. 不能忍受的;无法容忍的

  • They felt this would put intolerable pressure on them.

    他们觉得这会给他们带来无法承受的压力。

  • Human rights abuses by any party are intolerable.

    任何党派践踏人权的行为都不能容忍。

SYN unbearable, insufferable, unendurable, impossible  
intolerably

  • ...intolerably cramped conditions.

    拥挤不堪的条件

  • Her leg ached intolerably.

    她的腿疼痛难忍。
