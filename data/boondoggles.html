boondoggle
buːndɒgl ˈbunˌdɑgəl ˈbunˌdɔgəl
Word forms: plural boondoggles
N-COUNT 浪费时间金钱的无用机构；毫无意义的活动 People sometimes refer to an official organization or activity as a boondoggle when they think it wastes a lot of time and money and does not achieve much. 浪费时间金钱的无用机构；毫无意义的活动 [US, informal, disapproval]

  • The new runway is a billion-dollar boondoggle.

    新的机场跑道耗资10亿美元，却没什么大用处。
