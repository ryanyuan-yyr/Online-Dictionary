deafening
defənɪŋ ˈdɛfənɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 震耳欲聋的 A deafening noise is a very loud noise. 震耳欲聋的

  • ...the deafening roar of fighter jets taking off.

    喷气式战斗机起飞时震耳欲聋的轰鸣声

SYN ear-splitting, intense, piercing, ringing  
2 ADJ (沉默）没有任何回应的，死一般的 If you say there was a deafening silence, you are emphasizing that there was no reaction or response to something that was said or done. (沉默）没有任何回应的，死一般的 [emphasis]

  • When we ask people for suggestions we get a deafening silence.

    当我们向大家寻求建议时，得到的只是一片死寂的沉默。
