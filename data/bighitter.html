big hitter
(also big-hitter)Word forms: plural big hitters
1 N-COUNT （高尔夫球、网球等运动中的）大力球手 A big hitter is a sportsperson such as a golfer or tennis player who hits the ball with a lot of force. （高尔夫球、网球等运动中的）大力球手

  • The Uruguayan-born big-hitter smashed 28 aces.

    这位乌拉圭出生的重炮手轰出了28记爱司球。

2 N-COUNT （尤指商业或政治领域的）大人物，风云人物 A big hitter is a powerful or influential person, especially in business or politics. （尤指商业或政治领域的）大人物，风云人物 [informal]

  • ...if Tony Blair fails to persuade a Cabinet big hitter to take on the job.

    如果托尼·布莱尔不能说服一位重磅内阁成员承担此项工作…
