paunch
pɔːntʃ pɔntʃ
Word forms: plural paunches
N-COUNT 大肚子；将军肚 If a man has a paunch, he has a fat stomach. 大肚子；将军肚

  • He finished his dessert and patted his paunch.

    他吃完甜点，拍了拍他的大肚子。

  • His once lean figure was developing a paunch.

    他不像以前那么瘦削，开始长啤酒肚了。

SYN belly, beer-belly [informal] , spread [informal] , corporation [informal]
