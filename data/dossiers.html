dossier
dɒsieɪ -iəʳ ˈdɒsɪˌeɪ dosje ˈdɑsiˌeɪ ˈdɔsiˌeɪ ˈdɑsˌjeɪ dɔsˌjeɪ
Word forms: plural dossiers
N-COUNT 档案;卷宗;资料汇编 A dossier is a collection of papers containing information on a particular event, or on a person such as a criminal or a spy. 档案;卷宗;资料汇编

  • The company is compiling a dossier of evidence to back its allegations. [+ of/on]

    公司正汇编一份证据材料来支持指控。

  • The government kept dossiers on thousands of its citizens.

    政府保存着成千上万个公民的档案。

SYN report, record, file, detail
