venture capital
N-UNCOUNT 风险资本;投机资本 Venture capital is capital that is invested in projects that have a high risk of failure, but that will bring large profits if they are successful. 风险资本;投机资本 [business]

  • Successful venture capital investment is a lot harder than it sometimes looks.

    成功的风险投资有时要比它表面看起来困难得多。
