paranormal
pærənɔːʳməl ˌpærəˈnɔrməl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 不符合科学法则的；超自然的；超常的 A paranormal event or power, for example the appearance of a ghost, cannot be explained by scientific laws and is thought to involve strange, unknown forces. 不符合科学法则的；超自然的；超常的

  • Science may be able to provide some explanations of paranormal phenomena.

    科学也许能够解释某些超自然现象。

You can refer to paranormal events and matters as the paranormal.超自然事件；超自然现象；超常事物

  • We have been looking at the shadowy world of the paranormal.

    我们一直在关注玄奇事物这一神秘世界。
