relocation expenses
N-PLURAL 安置费;安家费 Relocation expenses are a sum of money that a company pays to someone who moves to a new area in order to work for the company. The money is to help them pay for moving house. 安置费;安家费 [business]

  • Relocation expenses were paid to encourage senior staff to move to the region.

    公司发放了安置费来鼓励高级员工搬到这个地区。
