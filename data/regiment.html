regiment
redʒɪmənt ˈrɛdʒəmənt ˈrɛdʒəˌmɛnt
Word forms: plural regiments
1 N-COUNT （军队的）团 A regiment is a large group of soldiers that is commanded by a colonel. （军队的）团
2 N-COUNT 大量;一(大)批 A regiment of people is a large number of them. 大量;一(大)批
