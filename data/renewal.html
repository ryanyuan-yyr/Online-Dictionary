renewal
rɪnjuːəl -nuː- rɪˈnuəl rɪˈnjuəl
Word forms: plural renewals
1 N-SING (活动或状态的)恢复，重新开始，复兴 If there is a renewal of an activity or a situation, it starts again. (活动或状态的)恢复，重新开始，复兴

  • They will discuss the possible renewal of diplomatic relations. [+ of]

    他们将讨论恢复外交关系的可能性。

  • Is he really considering a renewal of hostilities at this stage?

    他真的在考虑在这个阶段恢复敌对状态吗？

SYN resumption, continuation, re-establishment, recommencement  
2 N-VAR (执照、合同等的)续签，延期 The renewal of a document such as a licence or a contract is an official increase in the period of time for which it remains valid. (执照、合同等的)续签，延期

  • His contract came up for renewal.

    他的合同该续签了。

  • The premium should be received in this office within fourteen days of the renewal date.

    保险费用需在14天的续约期内交到此办公室。

SYN extension  
3 N-UNCOUNT 恢复;更新;再生;重建 Renewal of something lost, dead, or destroyed is the process of it growing again or being replaced. 恢复;更新;再生;重建

  • ...urban renewal and regeneration.

    城市改造和重建

  • Now it is spring, a time of renewal.

    现在是春天，万物重生的季节。

SYN restoration, revival, regeneration, rejuvenation
