red carpet
Word forms: plural red carpets
N-COUNT [usually singular] （迎接贵宾时的）红地毯（礼遇） The red carpet is special treatment given to an important or honoured guest, for example the laying of a strip of red carpet for them to walk on. （迎接贵宾时的）红地毯（礼遇）

  • We'll give her some VIP treatment and roll out the red carpet.

    我们将给予她贵宾级礼遇，并且为她铺设红地毯。

  • He is embarrassed by the red carpet treatment.

    走红地毯的礼遇让他感到很局促。
