masterly
mɑːstəʳli mæs- ˈmæstərli
ADJ-GRADED 巧妙的;精湛的;出色的;技艺高超的 If you describe something as masterly, you admire it because it has been done extremely well or shows the highest level of ability and skill. 巧妙的;精湛的;出色的;技艺高超的 [approval]

  • Malcolm Hebden gives a masterly performance.

    马尔科姆·赫布登上演了一场精湛的表演。

  • Attlee was extremely intelligent and his grasp of the situation was masterly.

    阿特利聪明过人，对局势的把握颇为高明。

SYN skilful, skilled, expert, finished
