conclusion
kənkluːʒən kənˈkluʒən
Word forms: plural conclusions
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN that] 结论;推论 When you come to a conclusion, you decide that something is true after you have thought about it carefully and have considered all the relevant facts. 结论;推论

  • Over the years I've come to the conclusion that she's a very great musician.

    这些年来，我得出的结论是她是一位非常伟大的音乐家。

  • I have tried to give some idea of how I feel — other people will no doubt draw their own conclusions.

    我试着说了一些我的感受，其他人无疑会有他们自己的结论。

SYN decision, agreement, opinion, settlement  
2 N-SING 结束;末尾 The conclusion of something is its ending. 结束;末尾

  • At the conclusion of the programme, I asked the children if they had any questions they wanted to ask me. [+ of]

    节目结束的时候，我问孩子们有没有问题想问我。

SYN end, ending, close, finish  
3 N-SING 缔结;签订 The conclusion of a treaty or a business deal is the act of arranging it or agreeing it. 缔结;签订

  • ...the expected conclusion of a free-trade agreement between Mexico and the United States. [+ of]

    墨西哥与美国之间自由贸易协定的如期签订

4 PHRASE 必然会发生的事;事先料定的结局 You can refer to something that seems certain to happen as a foregone conclusion . 必然会发生的事;事先料定的结局

  • It was a foregone conclusion that I would end up in the same business as him.

    早已注定我最终会和他干同一行。

  • The championship result was almost a foregone conclusion.

    锦标赛的结果差不多在意料之中。

5 PHRASE 最后;总而言之 You say 'in conclusion' to indicate that what you are about to say is the last thing that you want to say. 最后;总而言之

  • In conclusion, walking is a cheap, safe, enjoyable and readily available form of exercise.

    总而言之，散步是一种廉价、安全、愉快的锻炼方式，而且随时随地都可以进行。

SYN finally, lastly, in closing, to sum up  
6 PHRASE 匆忙地下判断;草率地下结论 If you say that someone jumps to a conclusion, you are critical of them because they decide too quickly that something is true, when they do not know all the facts. 匆忙地下判断;草率地下结论 [disapproval]

  • I didn't want her to jump to the conclusion that the divorce was in any way her fault. [PHRASE that]

    我不想让她草率地断定离婚完全是她的错。

  • Forgive me. I shouldn't be jumping to conclusions.

    原谅我，我不该草率地下结论。
