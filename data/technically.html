technically
teknɪkli
1 ADV [ADVERB adjective] 严格说来;准确来说 If something is technically the case, it is the case according to a strict interpretation of facts, laws, or rules, but may not be important or relevant in a particular situation. 严格说来;准确来说

  • Nude bathing is technically illegal but there are plenty of unspoilt beaches where no one would ever know.

    严格说来，裸泳是违法的，但在许多人迹罕至的海滩，没人会知道。

  • Technically, the two sides have been in a state of war ever since 1949.

    准确来说，双方自 1949 年起就一直处于交战状态。
