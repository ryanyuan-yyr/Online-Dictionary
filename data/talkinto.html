talk into
1 PHRASAL VERB 说服，劝服(尤指做错事或蠢事) If you talk a person into doing something they do not want to do, especially something wrong or stupid, you persuade them to do it. 说服，劝服(尤指做错事或蠢事) [Also V n P n]

  • He talked me into marrying him. He also talked me into having a baby. [V n P v-ing]

    他花言巧语让我嫁给了他，又说服我生了个孩子。

  • I was a fool to have let her talk me into it. [V n P -ing/n]

    我真笨，竟然听从了她的劝说。

SYN persuade, convince, win someone over, sway  
2 PHRASAL VERB (通过谈话)取得，进入 If you talk yourself into a particular situation or state, you get yourself into it by talking. (通过谈话)取得，进入

  • He has talked himself into a position where he will have no option but to go. [V pron-refl P n]

    他通过耍嘴皮子得到了一个职位，现在他只能硬着头皮去了。
