inconclusive
ɪnkənkluːsɪv ˌɪnkənˈklusɪv
1 ADJ-GRADED 无最后结果的;无结论的 If research or evidence is inconclusive, it has not proved anything. 无最后结果的;无结论的

  • Research has so far proved inconclusive.

    目前研究尚无定论。

  • I find the evidence inconclusive.

    我发现证据无说服力。

SYN uncertain, vague, ambiguous, open  
2 ADJ-GRADED (竞赛、冲突)胜负不明的,无最后结果的 If a contest or conflict is inconclusive, it is not clear who has won or who is winning. (竞赛、冲突)胜负不明的,无最后结果的

  • The past two elections were inconclusive.

    之前的两次选举都没有结果。

  • ...eight years of bloody and inconclusive war.

    8年没完没了的血腥战争
