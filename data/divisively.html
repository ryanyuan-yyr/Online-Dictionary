divisive
dɪvaɪsɪv dəˈvaɪsɪv dəˈvaɪzɪv dəˈvɪsɪv
ADJ-GRADED 造成不和的;引起分歧的;制造争端的 Something that is divisive causes unfriendliness and argument between people. 造成不和的;引起分歧的;制造争端的

  • Abortion has always been a divisive issue.

    人工流产一直是个引发争议的问题。

  • A referendum would be divisive.

    公投将产生分歧。

SYN disruptive, unsettling, alienating, troublesome  
divisiveness
