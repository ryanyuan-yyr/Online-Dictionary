pilgrimage
pɪlgrɪmɪdʒ ˈpɪlgrəmɪdʒ
Word forms: plural pilgrimages
1 N-COUNT 朝圣；朝觐 If you make a pilgrimage to a holy place, you go there for a religious reason. 朝圣；朝觐

  • ...the pilgrimage to Mecca. [+ to]

    去麦加的朝圣

2 N-COUNT 拜谒；重要旅程 A pilgrimage is a journey that someone makes to a place that is very important to them. 拜谒；重要旅程

  • ...a private pilgrimage to family graves. [+ to]

    私下前往家族墓地的拜谒

  • His father took him on a sentimental pilgrimage to Ireland.

    他爸爸带他踏上了去爱尔兰的情感之旅。
