histrionics
hɪstriɒnɪks ˌhɪstrɪˈɒnɪks ˌhɪstriˈɑnɪks
N-PLURAL 装腔作势;矫揉造作 If you disapprove of someone's dramatic and exaggerated behaviour, you can describe it as histrionics . 装腔作势;矫揉造作 [disapproval]

  • When I explained everything to my mum and dad, there were no histrionics.

    我一五一十地把一切都向父母解释清楚了。
