flavour
fleɪvəʳ ˈfleɪvər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense flavours, present participle flavouring, past tense , past participle flavoured
in AM, use 美国英语用 flavor
1 N-VAR 味;滋味;味道 The flavour of a food or drink is its taste. 味;滋味;味道

  • This cheese has a crumbly texture with a strong flavour.

    这种奶酪口感松脆，味道浓郁。

  • I always add some paprika for extra flavour.

    我总是加一些辣椒粉来提味儿。

  • ...drinks of rich colours and strong flavours.

    颜色鲜艳、味道浓郁的饮料

SYN taste, seasoning, flavouring, savour  
2 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] (食物等的)口味，味道 If something is orange flavour or beef flavour, it is made to taste of orange or beef. (食物等的)口味，味道

  • ...salt and vinegar flavour crisps.

    咸酸口味的炸薯片

  • ...now available in three new flavours.

    现有3种新口味

3 VERB 给…调味;加味于 If you flavour food or drink, you add something to it to give it a particular taste. 给…调味;加味于

  • Flavour your favourite dishes with exotic herbs and spices. [VERB noun + with]

    把异国风味的芳草和香料加到你最喜爱的菜肴里调味。

  • Lime preserved in salt is a north African speciality which is used to flavour chicken dishes. [VERB noun]

    盐渍的酸橙是北非的一种特产，在烹制鸡肉时用来调味。

SYN season, spice, add flavour to, enrich  
4 N-COUNT [usually with supplement] 特色;风味;风格 You can refer to a special quality that something has as its flavour. For example, if something has an Italian flavour it reminds you of Italian things. 特色;风味;风格

  • ...a car with a flavour that is distinctly Italian.

    带有明显意大利风格的轿车

  • ...clothes with a nostalgic Forties flavour.

    带有 40 年代怀旧风格的服装

SYN quality, feeling, feel, style  
5 N-SING [oft a N of n] 大体介绍;概述;概念 If something gives you a flavour of a subject, situation, or event, it gives you a general idea of what it is like. 大体介绍;概述;概念

  • The book gives you a flavour of what alternative therapy is about.

    这本书让你大致了解一下什么是替代疗法。

6 PHRASE [usually verb-link PHRASE] 红极一时的人物;风靡一时的事物 If you think that something or someone is very popular at a particular time, you can say that they are flavour of the month . 红极一时的人物;风靡一时的事物

  • Hats were very much flavour of the month.

    帽子在当时是时尚热宠。

  • Middlesex cricketers Gatting and Emburey are hardly flavour of the month.

    米德尔塞克斯郡板球选手盖廷和恩伯里很难说是当红人物。

SYN the latest thing, fashionable, in vogue, all the rage
