capsize
kæpsaɪz kæpsaɪz ˈkæpˌsaɪz kæpˈsaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense capsizes, present participle capsizing, past tense , past participle capsized
V-ERG (使)(船)倾覆；（使）（船）翻沉 If you capsize a boat or if it capsizes, it turns upside down in the water. (使)(船)倾覆；（使）（船）翻沉

  • The sea got very rough and the boat capsized. [VERB]

    海上波涛汹涌,小船翻了。

  • I didn't count on his capsizing the raft. [VERB noun]

    我没有想到他会把筏子弄翻。

SYN overturn, turn over, invert, tip over
