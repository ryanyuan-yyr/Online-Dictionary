infatuated
ɪnfætʃueɪtɪd ɪnˌfætʃuˌeɪtɪd
ADJ-GRADED 热恋的;着迷的;迷恋的 If you are infatuated with a person or thing, you have strong feelings of love or passion for them which make you unable to think clearly or sensibly about them. 热恋的;着迷的;迷恋的

  • He was utterly infatuated with her. [+ with]

    他完全被她迷住了。

  • I became infatuated with the case.

    我对这个案例着迷了。

SYN obsessed, fascinated, captivated, possessed
