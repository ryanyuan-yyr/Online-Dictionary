icky
ɪki ˈɪki
1 ADJ-GRADED 过于煽情的;过分伤感的 If you describe something as icky, you mean that it is too emotional or sentimental. 过于煽情的;过分伤感的 [mainly US, informal, disapproval]

  • They've even got one of those icky photos of themselves on the bedside table.

    他们甚至在床头桌上摆了一张他们自己的煽情照片。

2 ADJ-GRADED 黏糊糊的;令人厌恶的 If you describe a substance as icky, you mean that it is disgustingly sticky. 黏糊糊的;令人厌恶的 [mainly US, informal, disapproval]

  • She could feel something icky on her fingers.

    她感到指尖有什么黏糊糊的恶心东西。
