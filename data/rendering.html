rendering
rendərɪŋ ˈrɛndərɪŋ
Word forms: plural renderings
1 N-COUNT (剧本、诗歌或音乐作品的)演绎，表演 A rendering of a play, poem, or piece of music is a performance of it. (剧本、诗歌或音乐作品的)演绎，表演

  • ...a rendering of Verdi's Requiem by the BBC Symphony Orchestra. [+ of]

    由BBC交响乐团演奏的威尔第的《安魂曲》

SYN recitation, performance, interpretation, recital  
2 N-COUNT 翻译 A rendering of an expression or piece of writing or speech is a translation of it. 翻译

  • This phrase may well have been a rendering of a popular Arabic expression. [+ of]

    这个短语可能翻译自一个阿拉伯语常用表达。

3 N-COUNT (涂抹墙壁的)灰泥，水泥 The rendering on a wall is a layer of plaster or cement which covers and protects it. (涂抹墙壁的)灰泥，水泥 [mainly British]
