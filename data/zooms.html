zoom
zuːm zum
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , 3rd person singular present tense zooms, present participle zooming, past tense , past participle zoomed
1 VERB 疾行;快速移动 If you zoom somewhere, you go there very quickly. 疾行;快速移动 [informal]

  • We zoomed through the gallery. [VERB prep./adv.]

    我们飞快地穿过画廊。

  • A police car zoomed by very close to them. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一辆警车从他们身边擦身疾驰而过。

SYN speed, shoot, fly, tear  
2 VERB （价格或销量）飙升，急剧增长，猛涨 If prices or sales zoom, they increase greatly in a very short time. （价格或销量）飙升，急剧增长，猛涨

  • The economy shrank and inflation zoomed. [VERB]

    经济萎缩，通货膨胀加剧。

  • Profits zoomed from nil in 1981 to about 16 million last year. [VERB]

    利润从1981年的零收益飙升至去年的 1,600万。

SYN rise, rocket, soar, escalate  
3 N-COUNT 同zoom lens A zoom is the same as a zoom lens . 同zoom lens
