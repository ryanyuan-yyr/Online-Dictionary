powder
paʊdəʳ ˈpaʊdər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense powders, present participle powdering, past tense , past participle powdered
1 N-MASS 粉末；细面 Powder consists of many tiny particles of a solid substance. 粉末；细面

  • Put a small amount of the powder into a container and mix with water.

    把少量粉末放入容器，加水混合。

  • The wood turns to powder in his fingers.

    木头在他指间会变成粉末。

  • ...a fine white powder.

    白色细粉

  • ...cocoa powder.

    可可粉

SYN dust, pounce [rare] , talc, fine grains  
2 N-MASS 同 face powder Powder is the same as face powder . 同 face powder [old-fashioned]
3 VERB 往…上搽粉；往…上抹粉 If a woman powders her face or some other part of her body, she puts face powder or talcum powder on it. 往…上搽粉；往…上抹粉

  • She powdered her face and applied her lipstick and rouge. [VERB noun]

    她往脸上搽了粉，抹了口红和胭脂。

  • ...the old woman's powdered face. [VERB-ed]

    老妇人那张搽了粉的脸

4 N-UNCOUNT 同 gunpowder Powder is the same as gunpowder . 同 gunpowder [old-fashioned]

  • The smell of powder was in the air.

    空气里有股火药味儿。

5 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 粉状雪；干细雪 Powder is very fine snow. 粉状雪；干细雪

  • ...a day's powder skiing.

    一天的滑雪时光
