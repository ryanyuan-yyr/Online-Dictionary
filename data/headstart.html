head start
(also head-start)Word forms: plural head starts
N-COUNT [usually singular] 领先;占先 If you have a head start on other people, you have an advantage over them in something such as a competition or race. 领先;占先

  • Hungarian businessmen have had a head start over most of their Eastern European neighbors.

    匈牙利商人已经领先于东欧邻国的大多数同行了。

  • A good education gives your child a head start in life.

    良好的教育会让你的孩子在人生的起跑线上比别人领先一步。

SYN start, advantage, help, edge
