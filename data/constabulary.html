constabulary
kənstæbjʊləri -leri kənˈstæbjəˌlɛri
Word forms: plural constabularies
1 N-COUNT (英国等国家某一地区的)警察部队 In Britain and some other countries, a constabulary is the police force of a particular area. (英国等国家某一地区的)警察部队

  • ...the Chief Constable of the Nottinghamshire Constabulary.

    诺丁汉郡警察局局长

2 N-COUNT (美国某一地区的)巡官队伍，巡官队伍辖区 In the United States, a constabulary is the constables in a particular area, or the area that they are responsible for. (美国某一地区的)巡官队伍，巡官队伍辖区
