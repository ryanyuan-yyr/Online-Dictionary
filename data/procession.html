procession
prəseʃən proʊˈsɛʃən prəˈsɛʃən
Word forms: plural processions
N-COUNT （走路、骑马、开车等的）队伍，行列 A procession is a group of people who are walking, riding, or driving in a line as part of a public event. （走路、骑马、开车等的）队伍，行列

  • ...a funeral procession.

    出殡的队伍

  • ...religious processions.

    宗教游行的队伍

SYN parade, train, march, file
