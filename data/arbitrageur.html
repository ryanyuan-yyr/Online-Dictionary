arbitrager
ɑːʳbɪtrɑːʒɜːʳ ˈɑːbɪˌtrɑːʒə ˌɑːbɪtræˈʒɜː
(also arbitrageur)Word forms: plural arbitragers
N-COUNT 套利者;套购商;套汇人 In finance, an arbitrager is someone who buys currencies, securities, or commodities on one country's market in order to make money by immediately selling them at a profit on another country's market. 套利者;套购商;套汇人 [business]
