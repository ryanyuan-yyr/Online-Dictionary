oddball
ɒdbɔːl ˈɑdˌbɔl
Word forms: plural oddballs
N-COUNT 行为古怪的人；怪人 If you refer to someone as an oddball, you think they behave in a strange way. 行为古怪的人；怪人 [informal]

  • His mother and father thought Jim was a bit of an oddball too.

    吉姆的父母也认为他有些古怪。

Oddball is also an adjective.

  • I came from a family that was decidedly oddball you know.

    你要知道，我的家人个个古怪得紧。
