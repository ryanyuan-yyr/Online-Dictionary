unnamed
ʌnneɪmd ʌnˈneɪmd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 未披露姓名的；不知姓名的 Unnamed people or things are talked about but their names are not mentioned. 未披露姓名的；不知姓名的

  • An unnamed man collapsed and died while he was walking near Dundonald.

    一个不知姓名的人在邓唐纳德附近走着走着突然倒地而亡。

  • The cash comes from an unnamed source.

    这笔现金来源不详。

SYN anonymous, unknown, unidentified, unrevealed  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 没有名字的；未被命名的 Unnamed things have not been given a name. 没有名字的；未被命名的

  • ...unnamed comets and asteroids.

    未命名的彗星和小行星

SYN nameless, obscure, untitled, undesignated
