banded bændɪd
ADJ 有条纹的 If something is banded, it has one or more bands on it, often of a different colour which contrasts with the main colour. 有条纹的

  • ...a stark tower, banded in dark and light stone. [+ in/with]

    有深色和浅色石头条纹的荒凉塔楼

  • ...banded stripes of dyed wool.

    印染毛织品上的带纹

-banded -bændɪd
COMB in ADJ （用于表示颜色的名词后）表示“带…条纹的”，“镶…边的” -banded combines with colours to indicate that something has bands of a particular colour. （用于表示颜色的名词后）表示“带…条纹的”，“镶…边的”

  • Tables are set with white china and gold-banded silver cutlery.

    桌上摆放着白色瓷器和镶着金边的银制餐具。
