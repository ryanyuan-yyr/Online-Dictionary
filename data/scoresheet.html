scoresheet
skɔːʳʃiːt ˈskɔːʃiːt
(also score sheet)
PHRASE (比赛中)得分，进球 In football, rugby, and some other sports, if a player gets on the scoresheet, he or she scores one or more goals, tries, or points. (比赛中)得分，进球 [British, journalism]

  • Although Stewart did not get on the scoresheet, he was directly involved in both goals.

    尽管斯图尔特没有进球，但两个进球他都直接参与了助攻。
