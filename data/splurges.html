splurge
splɜːʳdʒ splɜrdʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense splurges, present participle splurging, past tense , past participle splurged
VERB 挥霍 If you splurge on something, you spend a lot of money, usually on things that you do not need. 挥霍

  • We splurged on Bohemian glass for gifts, and for ourselves. [VERB + on]

    我们花了很多钱买波希米亚玻璃制品送人和自用。

Splurge is also a noun.

  • I'm confident that there's enough in the bank for a splurge on a great pair of shoes.

    我相信，银行里存有足够的钱能让我破费一回买双好鞋。
