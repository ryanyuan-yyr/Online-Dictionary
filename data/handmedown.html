hand-me-down ˈhændmiˌdaʊn
Word forms: plural hand-me-downs
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 别人用过的东西;（尤指）旧衣物 Hand-me-downs are things, especially clothes, which have been used by someone else before you and which have been given to you for your use. 别人用过的东西;（尤指）旧衣物

  • Edward wore Andrew's hand-me-downs.

    爱德华穿安德鲁穿过的旧衣服。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] [usually plural] （尤指衣物）别人用过的 Hand-me-down is used to describe things, especially clothes, which have been used by someone else before you and which have been given to you for your use. （尤指衣物）别人用过的

  • Most of the boys wore hand-me-down military shirts from their fathers.

    多数男孩子穿着自己父亲穿过的旧军装衬衫。

  • ...hand-me-down tennis rackets.

    别人给的旧网球拍

SYN cast-off, used, second-hand, worn
