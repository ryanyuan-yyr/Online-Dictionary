fever
fiːvəʳ ˈfivər
Word forms: plural fevers
1 N-VAR 发热;发烧 If you have a fever when you are ill, your body temperature is higher than usual and your heart beats faster. 发热;发烧

  • My Uncle Jim had a high fever.

    我叔叔吉姆发了高烧。

  • Symptoms of the disease include fever and weight loss.

    这种疾病的症状包括发烧和体重减轻。

SYN ague, high temperature, feverishness, pyrexia [medicine]  
2See also:hay fever  rheumatic fever  scarlet fever 
3 N-COUNT 亢奋;极度兴奋 A fever is extreme excitement or nervousness about something. 亢奋;极度兴奋

  • Angie waited in a fever of excitement.

    安吉在极度兴奋中等待着。

SYN excitement, heat, passion, intensity
