teaspoon
tiːspuːn ˈtiˌspun
Word forms: plural teaspoons
1 N-COUNT 茶匙 A teaspoon is a small spoon that you use to put sugar into tea or coffee. 茶匙

  • Drop the dough onto a baking sheet with a teaspoon.

    用茶匙把生面团舀到烤盘上。

2 N-COUNT 一茶匙的量 You can refer to an amount of food resting on a teaspoon as a teaspoon of food. 一茶匙的量

  • He wants three teaspoons of sugar in his coffee. [+ of]

    他要在咖啡里放 3 茶匙的糖。
