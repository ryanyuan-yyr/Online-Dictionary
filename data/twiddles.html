twiddle
twɪdəl ˈtwɪdəl ˈtwɪdəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense twiddles, present participle twiddling, past tense , past participle twiddled
1 VERB 旋弄;捻弄;摆弄 If you twiddle something, you twist it or turn it quickly with your fingers. 旋弄;捻弄;摆弄

  • He twiddled a knob on the dashboard. [VERB noun]

    他转动着仪表板上的一个旋钮。

  • She had sat there twiddling nervously with the clasp of her handbag. [VERB + with]

    她坐在那里紧张地摆弄着手提包的扣钩。

SYN fiddle with, adjust, finger, play with  
2 to twiddle your thumbs→see: thumb
