avenue
ævɪnjuː ˈævɪˌnjuː -nuː ˈævəˌnu ˈævəˌnju
Word forms: plural avenues
1 N-IN-NAMES （有时用于街名）大街，街道（书面缩略形式为Ave.） Avenue is sometimes used in the names of streets. The written abbreviation Ave. is also used. （有时用于街名）大街，街道（书面缩略形式为Ave.）

  • ...the most expensive stores on Park Avenue.

    帕克大街上最贵的商店

2 N-COUNT 大道；（尤指）林阴大道 An avenue is a wide, straight road, especially one with trees on either side. 大道；（尤指）林阴大道
3 N-COUNT 方法;途径 An avenue is a way of getting something done. 方法;途径

  • Talbot was presented with 80 potential avenues of investigation. [+ of]

    给塔尔博特提出了80种进行调查的可能途径。

  • There is another avenue to pursue — it involves further negotiations.

    还有一个办法可用，但需要进一步的磋商。

SYN method, line, approach, direction
