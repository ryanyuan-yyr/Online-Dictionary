media circus
ˈmiːdɪə ˈsɜːkəs
Word forms: plural media circuses
N-COUNT 媒体关注的焦点;媒体云集的盛会 If an event is described as a media circus, a large group of people from the media is there to report on it and take photographs. 媒体关注的焦点;媒体云集的盛会 [disapproval]

  • The couple married in the Caribbean to avoid a media circus.

    为了摆脱媒体的关注，这对新人在加勒比喜结连理。

  • ...the media circus surrounding international movie stars.

    聚焦国际影星的媒体盛会
