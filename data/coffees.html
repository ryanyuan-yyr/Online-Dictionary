coffee
kɒfi kɔːfi ˈkɔfi ˈkɑfi
Word forms: plural coffees
1 N-UNCOUNT 咖啡 Coffee is a hot drink made with water and ground or powdered coffee beans. 咖啡

  • Would you like some coffee?.

    要来点咖啡吗？

  • Newman poured more black coffee and lit a cigarette.

    纽曼又倒了些黑咖啡，接着点了支烟。

A coffee is a cup of coffee.一杯咖啡

  • I made a coffee.

    我冲了杯咖啡。

2 N-MASS 咖啡豆;咖啡粉 Coffee is the roasted beans or powder from which the drink is made. 咖啡豆;咖啡粉

  • Brazil harvested 28m bags of coffee in 1991, the biggest crop for four years.

    1991年，巴西的咖啡豆产量达2,800万袋，为4年来最高。

  • ...superior quality coffee.

    优质咖啡

Look here, Steward, if this is coffee, I want tea; but if this is tea, then I wish for coffee Punch
The morning cup of coffee has an exhilaration about it which the cheering influence of the afternoon or evening cup of tea cannot be expected to reproduceOliver Wendell Holmes Sr. Over the Teacups
