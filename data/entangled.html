entangled
ɪntæŋgəld ɪnˈtæŋɡəld
1 ADJ-GRADED 被缠住的;纠结在一起的 If something is entangled in something such as a rope, wire, or net, it is caught in it very firmly. 被缠住的;纠结在一起的

  • ...a whale that became entangled in crab nets. [+ in/with]

    被捕蟹网缠住的鲸鱼

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 陷入的;卷入的 If you become entangled in problems or difficulties, you become involved in problems or difficulties from which it is hard to escape. 陷入的;卷入的

  • This case was bound to get entangled in international politics. [+ in/with]

    这件事肯定会在国际政坛上纠缠不清。
