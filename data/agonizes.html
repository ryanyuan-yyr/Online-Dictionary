agonize
ægənaɪz ˈægəˌnaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense agonizes, present participle agonizing, past tense , past participle agonized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 agonise
1 VERB 感到极度焦虑；苦苦思索 If you agonize over something, you feel very anxious about it and spend a long time thinking about it. 感到极度焦虑；苦苦思索 [Also VERB]

  • Perhaps he was agonizing over the moral issues involved. [V + over/about]

    也许他正在为其中牵扯的道德问题而伤脑筋。

  • She only made the decision to apply for training after years of agonizing. [VERB-ing]

    多年的苦苦思索之后，她才作出了申请培训的决定。

SYN suffer, labour, worry, struggle
