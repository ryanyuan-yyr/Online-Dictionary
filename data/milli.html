milli- mɪlɪ- ˈmɪlɪ ˈmɪlə ˈmɪli
PREFIX (用于名词之前构成度量单位)表示“毫”，“千分之一” Milli- is added to some nouns that refer to units of measurement in order to form other nouns referring to units a thousand times smaller. (用于名词之前构成度量单位)表示“毫”，“千分之一”

  • ...a small current, around 5 milliamps.

    5毫安左右的弱电流
