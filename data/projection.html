projection
prədʒekʃən proʊˈdʒɛkʃən prəˈdʒɛkʃən
Word forms: plural projections
1 N-COUNT 预计；预测；估计 A projection is an estimate of a future amount. 预计；预测；估计

  • ...the company's projection of 11 million visitors for the first year. [+ of]

    公司对第一年将有1,100万游客的预测

  • ...sales projections.

    销售预测

SYN forecast, estimate, reckoning, prediction  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 投射；放映 The projection of a film or picture is the act of projecting it onto a screen or wall. 投射；放映

  • They took me into a projection room to see a picture.

    他们把我带到放映室去看一张图。
