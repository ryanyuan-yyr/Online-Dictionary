reimburse
riːɪmbɜːʳs ˌriɪmˈbɜrs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reimburses, present participle reimbursing, past tense , past participle reimbursed
VERB 补偿;赔偿;向…偿付 If you reimburse someone for something, you pay them back the money that they have spent or lost because of it. 补偿;赔偿;向…偿付 [formal]

  • I'll be happy to reimburse you for any expenses you might have incurred. [VERB noun + for]

    我将很乐意赔偿您所发生的任何费用。

  • The funds are supposed to reimburse policyholders in the event of insurer failure. [VERB noun]

    这项基金将在保险公司不能偿付的情况下对投保人进行赔付。

SYN pay back, refund, repay, recompense
