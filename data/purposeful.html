purposeful
pɜːʳpəsfʊl ˈpɜrpəsfəl
ADJ-GRADED 有明确目标的；坚决的 If someone is purposeful, they show that they have a definite aim and a strong desire to achieve it. 有明确目标的；坚决的

  • She had a purposeful air, and it became evident that this was not a casual visit.

    她一副无事不登三宝殿的神情，很明显，这不是一次随意造访。

SYN determined, resolved, resolute, decided  
purposefully

  • He strode purposefully towards the barn.

    他毅然大步朝粮仓走去。
