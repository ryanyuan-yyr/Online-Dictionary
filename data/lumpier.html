lumpy
lʌmpi ˈlʌmpi
Word forms: comparative lumpier, superlative lumpiest
ADJ-GRADED 多结块的；起疙瘩的 Something that is lumpy contains lumps or is covered with lumps. 多结块的；起疙瘩的

  • When the rice isn't cooked properly it goes lumpy and gooey.

    米饭没煮好会结块，而且软黏黏的。

  • Many women, especially as they get older, develop lumpy veins in their legs.

    许多妇女，尤其是年纪大了以后，腿上会现出疙疙瘩瘩的青筋。

SYN bumpy, clotted, uneven, knobbly
