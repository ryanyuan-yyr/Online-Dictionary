rough and ready
(also rough-and-ready)
1 ADJ-GRADED (解决方案、方法)简单的，马马虎虎的，临时对付的 A rough and ready solution or method is one that is rather simple and not very exact because it has been thought of or done in a hurry. (解决方案、方法)简单的，马马虎虎的，临时对付的

  • Here is a rough and ready measurement.

    这里有一次简单的测量。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不礼貌的;粗鲁的 A rough and ready person is not very polite or gentle. 不礼貌的;粗鲁的

  • ...rough and ready soldiers.

    粗鲁的士兵
