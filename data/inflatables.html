inflatable
ɪnfleɪtəbəl ɪnˈfleɪtəbəl ɪnˈfleɪtəbəl
Word forms: plural inflatables
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 可吹胀的;可充气的;可膨胀的 An inflatable object is one that you fill with air when you want to use it. 可吹胀的;可充气的;可膨胀的

  • ...an inflatable dinghy.

    一艘充气式橡皮筏

  • The children were playing on the inflatable castle.

    孩子们在充气的城堡上嬉戏。

2 N-COUNT 可充气物品(尤指小船) An inflatable is an inflatable object, especially a small boat. 可充气物品(尤指小船)
