anaemia
əniːmiə əˈnimiə əˈniːmɪə
Word forms: plural anaemias
in AM, use 美国英语用 anemia
N-UNCOUNT 贫血（症） Anaemia is a medical condition in which there are too few red cells in your blood, causing you to feel tired and look pale. 贫血（症）

  • She suffered from anaemia and even required blood transfusions.

    她患有贫血症，甚至需要输血。
