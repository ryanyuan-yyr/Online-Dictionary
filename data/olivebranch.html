olive branch
(also olive-branch)Word forms: plural olive branches
N-COUNT [usually singular] （象征和平的）橄榄枝 If you offer an olive branch to someone, you say or do something in order to show that you want to end a disagreement or quarrel. （象征和平的）橄榄枝

  • Clarke also offered an olive branch to critics in his party.

    克拉克还向党内的批评人士伸出了橄榄枝。
