cement
sɪment sɪˈmɛnt səˈmɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cements, present participle cementing, past tense , past participle cemented
1 N-UNCOUNT 水泥 Cement is a grey powder which is mixed with sand and water in order to make concrete. 水泥

  • ...a mixture of wet sand and cement.

    湿沙子和水泥的混合料

2 N-UNCOUNT 同 concrete Cement is the same as concrete . 同 concrete

  • ...the hard cold cement floor.

    又硬又冷的混凝土地面

3 N-UNCOUNT [usually n NOUN] 胶接剂;接合剂 Glue that is made for sticking particular substances together is sometimes called cement . 胶接剂;接合剂

  • Stick the pieces on with tile cement.

    用瓷砖胶把它们一块块贴上去。

SYN sealant, glue, gum, adhesive  
4 VERB 巩固;加强 Something that cements a relationship or agreement makes it stronger. 巩固;加强

  • Nothing cements a friendship between countries so much as trade. [VERB noun]

    贸易最能增强国家间的友谊。

5 N-UNCOUNT [usually with supplement] 起巩固作用的因素;加强剂；纽带 The cement of a relationship or agreement is something that makes it stronger and more long-lasting. 起巩固作用的因素;加强剂；纽带 [literary]

  • ...the power of the party, once the cement that held the Soviet Union together.

    党的力量，曾经是保持苏联团结的黏合剂

  • In the old days, television was the cement of society.

    过去，电视曾是团结社会的力量。

6 VERB [usually passive] 把…黏合;使黏结 If things are cemented together, they are stuck or fastened together. 把…黏合;使黏结

  • Most artificial joints are cemented into place. [be VERB-ed prep./adv.]

    多数人工关节是用骨水泥固定到位的。

SYN stick, join, bond, attach
