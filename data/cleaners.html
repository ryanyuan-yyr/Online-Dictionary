cleaner
kliːnəʳ ˈklinər
Word forms: plural cleaners
1 N-COUNT （大楼的）保洁员，清洁工 A cleaner is someone who is employed to clean the rooms and furniture inside a building. （大楼的）保洁员，清洁工
2 N-COUNT [n NOUN] (专业的)清洁工 A cleaner is someone whose job is to clean a particular type of thing. (专业的)清洁工

  • He was a window cleaner.

    他是个洗窗工。

3 N-MASS [usually n NOUN] 清洗剂;去污剂 A cleaner is a substance used for cleaning things. 清洗剂;去污剂

  • ...oven cleaner.

    烤箱清洁剂

  • ...abrasive cleaners.

    擦洗剂

4 N-COUNT [usually n NOUN] 清洁器;吸尘器 A cleaner is a device used for cleaning things. 清洁器;吸尘器

  • ...an air cleaner.

    空气净化器

5See also:pipe cleaner  vacuum cleaner 
6 N-COUNT 干洗店 A cleaner or a cleaner's is a shop where things such as clothes are dry-cleaned. 干洗店
7 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 骗走，骗光(钱财) If someone takes you to the cleaners, they unfairly take most of your money, for example in a business deal or in gambling. 骗走，骗光(钱财) [informal]
