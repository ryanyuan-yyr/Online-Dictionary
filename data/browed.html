brow
braʊ braʊ
Word forms: plural brows
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 额；额头 Your brow is your forehead. 额；额头

  • He wiped his brow with the back of his hand.

    他用手背擦了擦前额。

  • She wrinkled her brow inquisitively.

    她好奇地皱起了眉头。

SYN forehead, temple  
2 to knit your brow→see: knit 
3 N-COUNT [usually plural] 眉；眉毛 Your brows are your eyebrows. 眉；眉毛

  • He had thick brown hair and shaggy brows.

    他一头浓密的棕色头发，眉毛乱糟糟的。

4 N-COUNT 山脊；坡顶 The brow of a hill is the top part of it. 山脊；坡顶

  • He was on the look-out just below the brow of the hill. [+ of]

    他就在山脊下负责警戒。

SYN top, summit, peak, edge
