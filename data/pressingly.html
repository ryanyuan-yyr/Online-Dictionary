pressing
presɪŋ ˈprɛsɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 紧迫的；紧急的；急迫的 A pressing problem, need, or issue has to be dealt with immediately. 紧迫的；紧急的；急迫的

  • It is one of the most pressing problems facing this country.

    这是该国面临的最紧迫的问题之一。

  • There is a pressing need for more funds.

    迫切需要更多的资金。

SYN urgent, serious, burning, vital  
2 See also: press
