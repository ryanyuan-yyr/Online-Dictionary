encyclopedic
ɪnsaɪkləpiːdɪk ɛnˌsaɪkloʊˈpidɪk ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpidɪk ɪnˌsaɪkloʊˈpidɪk ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpidɪk
(also encyclopaedic)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 百科全书般的;知识丰富的 If you describe something as encyclopedic, you mean that it is very full, complete, and thorough in the amount of knowledge or information that it has. 百科全书般的;知识丰富的

  • He had an encyclopaedic knowledge of drugs.

    他在药品方面学识广博。

  • ...an almost overwhelmingly encyclopaedic volume.

    几乎包罗万象的卷册

SYN comprehensive, full, complete, vast
