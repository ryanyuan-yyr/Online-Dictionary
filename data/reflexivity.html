reflexive
rɪfleksɪv rɪˈflɛksɪv
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 应激反应的;本能反应的;条件反射的 A reflexive reaction or movement occurs immediately in response to something that happens. 应激反应的;本能反应的;条件反射的 [formal]

  • ...that reflexive urge for concealment.

    想要躲藏起来的本能冲动
