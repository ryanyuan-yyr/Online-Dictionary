touch off
PHRASAL VERB 引发;触发;引起 If something touches off a situation or series of events, it causes it to start happening. 引发;触发;引起 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Is the massacre likely to touch off a new round of violence? [VERB PREP. noun]

    大屠杀有可能触发新一轮的暴行吗？

SYN trigger (off), start, begin, cause
