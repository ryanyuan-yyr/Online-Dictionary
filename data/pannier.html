pannier
pæniəʳ ˈpænjər ˈpæniər
Word forms: plural panniers
1 N-COUNT （自行车或摩托车后轮两侧的）挂包，挂篮，挂篓 A pannier is one of two bags or boxes for carrying things in, which are fixed on each side of the back wheel of a bicycle or motorbike. （自行车或摩托车后轮两侧的）挂包，挂篮，挂篓
2 N-COUNT （通常挂在驮兽两侧的）驮篮，驮篓，驮包 A pannier is a large basket or bag, usually one of two that are put over an animal and used for carrying loads. （通常挂在驮兽两侧的）驮篮，驮篓，驮包
