steeplechase
stiːpəltʃeɪs ˈstipəlˌtʃeɪs
Word forms: plural steeplechases
1 N-COUNT 越野障碍赛马 A steeplechase is a long horse race in which the horses have to jump over obstacles such as hedges and water jumps. 越野障碍赛马
2 N-COUNT (3000 米)障碍赛跑 A steeplechase is a 3000 metres race around a track, during which people jump over obstacles and water jumps. (3000 米)障碍赛跑
