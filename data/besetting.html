beset
bɪset bɪˈsɛt biˈsɛt bɪˈsɛt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense besets, present participle besetting
The form beset is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. beset的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
VERB 困扰；烦扰；使苦恼 If someone or something is beset by problems or fears, they have many problems or fears which affect them severely. 困扰；烦扰；使苦恼

  • The country is beset by severe economic problems. [be V-ed + by/with]

    这个国家被严重的经济问题所困扰。

  • The discussions were beset with difficulties. [be V-ed by/with n]

    讨论困难重重。

  • ...the problems now besetting the country. [VERB noun]

    现在正困扰该国的问题

SYN plague, trouble, embarrass, torture
