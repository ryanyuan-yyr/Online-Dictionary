student body
Word forms: student bodies
N-COUNT [usu sing] (某所大学的)全体学生A student body is all the students of a particular college or university, considered as a group. (某所大学的)全体学生

  • Today, the student body is roughly 60 percent Black and Hispanic, 25 percent Asian, and 15 percent white.

    今天，全体学生中大概有60%是黑人和西班牙人，25%是亚洲人，另外15%是白人。

  • City College of New York has one of the most diversified student bodies in the nation.

    纽约城市大学有全美最多元化的学生群体之一。
