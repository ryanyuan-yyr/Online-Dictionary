mentor
mentɔːʳ ˈmɛntər ˈmɛnˌtɔr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense mentors, present participle mentoring, past tense , past participle mentored
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] (尤指工作方面的)导师，指导者 A person's mentor is someone who gives them help and advice over a period of time, especially help and advice related to their job. (尤指工作方面的)导师，指导者
SYN guide, teacher, coach, adviser  
2 VERB 为…出谋划策;指导;辅导 To mentor someone means to give them help and advice over a period of time, especially help and advice related to their job. 为…出谋划策;指导;辅导

  • He had mentored scores of younger doctors. [VERB noun]

    他指导过许多更年轻的医生。
