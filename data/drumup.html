drum up
PHRASAL VERB 竭力争取(支持);兜揽(生意) If you drum up support or business, you try to get it. 竭力争取(支持);兜揽(生意)

  • The UN secretary-general arrived in Tokyo to drum up support for international peacekeeping.

    联合国秘书长抵达东京竭力为国际维和争取支持。

  • It is to be hoped that he is merely drumming up business. [VERB PREP. noun]

    但愿他只是在招揽生意。

SYN seek, attract, request, ask for
