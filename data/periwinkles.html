periwinkle
periwɪŋkəl ˈpɛrəˌwɪŋkəl
Word forms: plural periwinkles
1 N-VAR 蔓长春花 Periwinkle is a plant that grows along the ground and has blue flowers. 蔓长春花
2 N-COUNT 滨螺；玉黍螺 Periwinkles are small sea snails that can be eaten. 滨螺；玉黍螺
