astound
əstaʊnd əˈstaʊnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense astounds, present participle astounding, past tense , past participle astounded
VERB 使大吃一惊;使惊骇;使震惊 If something astounds you, you are very surprised by it. 使大吃一惊;使惊骇;使震惊 [Also VERB]

  • He used to astound his friends with feats of physical endurance. [VERB noun]

    过去，他表现出来的惊人耐力常让朋友们大吃一惊。

SYN amaze, surprise, overwhelm, astonish
