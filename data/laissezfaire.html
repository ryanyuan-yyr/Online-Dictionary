laissez-faire
leɪseɪ feəʳ les-
N-UNCOUNT 自由放任的政策Laissez-faire is the policy which is based on the idea that governments and the law should not interfere with business, finance, or the conditions of people's working lives. 自由放任的政策 [business]

  • ...the doctrine of laissez-faire and unbridled individualism.

    …自由放任和肆无忌惮的个人主义信条。
