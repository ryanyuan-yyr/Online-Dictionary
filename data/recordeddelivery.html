recorded delivery
N-UNCOUNT 挂号邮寄 If you send a letter or parcel recorded delivery, you send it using a Post Office service which gives you an official record of the fact that it has been posted and delivered. 挂号邮寄 [British]

  • Use recorded delivery for large cheques or money orders.
