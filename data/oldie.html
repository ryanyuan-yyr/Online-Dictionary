oldie
oʊldi ˈoʊldi
Word forms: plural oldies
1 N-COUNT （尤指美好的）旧事物 You can refer to something such as an old song or film as an oldie, especially when you think it is still good. （尤指美好的）旧事物 [informal]

  • Radio Aire only plays Top 40 stuff and oldies.

    空中电台仅播放排行榜上前40位的歌曲和怀旧金曲。

Oldie is also an adjective.

  • During the festival, we'll be showing 13 classic oldie films.

    我们将在电影节期间展映13部经典老片。

2 N-COUNT 老人 You can use oldies to refer to fairly old people. 老人 [British, humorous, informal]

  • ...a lush English fairy tale that many oldies will remember from their youth.

    很多老年人会记得的年轻时代的王子公主式英国童话
