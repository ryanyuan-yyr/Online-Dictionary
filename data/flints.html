flint
flɪnt flɪnt
Word forms: plural flints
1 N-UNCOUNT 燧石;火石 Flint is a very hard greyish-black stone that was used in former times for making tools. 燧石;火石

  • ...a flint arrowhead.

    燧石箭头

  • ...eyes the colour of flint.

    燧石色的眼睛

2 N-COUNT 打火石 A flint is a small piece of flint which can be struck with a piece of steel to produce sparks. 打火石
