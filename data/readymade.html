ready-made
ˈrɛdiˈmeɪd
1 ADJ 现成的;预制的 If something that you buy is ready-made, you can use it immediately, because the work you would normally have to do has already been done. 现成的;预制的

  • We rely quite a bit on ready-made meals — they are so convenient.

    我们很是依赖现成食物—它们真的很方便。

  • You can buy it ready-made at Chinese groceries.

    在中国杂货店里你可以买到现成的。

  • The ready-made bedcovers cost from £200.

    现成的床罩定价200英镑起。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 方便的;实用的;有用的 Ready-made means extremely convenient or useful for a particular purpose. 方便的;实用的;有用的

  • Those wishing to study urban development have a ready-made example on their doorstep.

    那些想要研究城市发展的人眼下就有一个十分现成的范例。

  • It provides perfect strangers with a ready-made and infinitely adaptable topic of conversation.

    它给完全陌生的人提供了一个方便灵活的聊天话题。
