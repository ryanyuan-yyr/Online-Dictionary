gussy up ˈɡʌsɪ
PHRASAL VERB 把…打扮得漂漂亮亮；把…装饰得更吸引人 If someone is gussied up, they are dressed very smartly. If something is gussied up, it is made more interesting or attractive. 把…打扮得漂漂亮亮；把…装饰得更吸引人 [mainly US, informal] [Also V pron P]

  • They all got gussied up. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    他们都打扮得漂漂亮亮。

  • ...plans to gussy up the venues, offering better food and games arcades. [VERB PREP. noun]

    将会场装饰一新、提供更加美味的食物和更好的电子游戏厅的计划
