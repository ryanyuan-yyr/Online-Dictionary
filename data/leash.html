leash
liːʃ liʃ
Word forms: plural leashes
N-COUNT （牵狗的）皮带，链条 A dog's leash is a long thin piece of leather or a chain, which you attach to the dog's collar so that you can keep the dog under control. （牵狗的）皮带，链条

  • All dogs in public places should be on a leash.

    在公共场所所有的狗都应该用皮带牵住。

SYN lead, line, restraint, cord
