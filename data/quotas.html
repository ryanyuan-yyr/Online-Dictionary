quota
kwoʊtə ˈkwoʊtə
Word forms: plural quotas
1 N-COUNT 定量;定额;配额 A quota is the limited number or quantity of something which is officially allowed. 定量;定额;配额

  • The quota of four tickets per person had been reduced to two. [+ of]

    每人可购买的票的限额已经由四张降至两张。

SYN share, allowance, ration, allocation  
2 N-COUNT [NOUN n] (给予某团体的)限额，配额 A quota is a fixed maximum or minimum proportion of people from a particular group who are allowed to do something, such as come and live in a country or work for the government. (给予某团体的)限额，配额 [Also + of]

  • The bill would force employers to adopt a quota system when recruiting workers.

    该法案将迫使雇主在招聘工人时采取限额制度。

3 N-COUNT 配额;份额;指标 Someone's quota of something is their expected or deserved share of it. 配额;份额;指标

  • They have the usual quota of human weaknesses, no doubt. [+ of]

    毫无疑问，他们身上也有人所共有的弱点。
