morrow
mɒroʊ mɔːr- ˈmɑroʊ ˈmɔroʊ
1 N-SING [oft on the N] 翌日;次日 The morrow means the next day or tomorrow. 翌日;次日 [literary, or old-fashioned]

  • We do depart for Wales on the morrow.

    我们明天确实要前往威尔士。

2 CONVENTION good morrow 同 good morning Good morrow means the same as 'good morning'. good morrow 同 good morning [literary, or old-fashioned]

  • Good morrow to you, my lord.

    早上好，主人。
