preoccupied
priɒkjʊpaɪd priˈɑkjuˌpaɪd priˈɑkjəˌpaɪd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 全神贯注的；入神的 If you are preoccupied, you are thinking a lot about something or someone, and so you hardly notice other things. 全神贯注的；入神的

  • Tom Banbury was preoccupied with the missing Shepherd child and did not want to devote time to the new murder. [+ with/by]

    汤姆·班伯里一心寻找谢泼德家失踪的孩子，不想花时间处理这起新的谋杀案。

  • She looked very preoccupied.

    她看起来心事重重。
