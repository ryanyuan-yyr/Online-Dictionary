out-and-out ˈaʊtənˈaʊt
ADJ [ADJ n] 十足的；完全的；彻头彻尾的 You use out-and-out to emphasize that someone or something has all the characteristics of a particular type of person or thing. 十足的；完全的；彻头彻尾的 [emphasis]

  • The Olympic theme tune 'Amigos para Siempre' (Friends for Life) proved an out-and-out success.

    结果证明奥运会主题歌《永远的朋友》极为成功。

  • Much of what has been written about us is out-and-out lies.

    关于我们的报道大都是彻头彻尾的胡编乱造。

SYN absolute, complete, total, perfect
