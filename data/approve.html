approve
əpruːv əˈpruv
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense approves, present participle approving, past tense , past participle approved
1 VERB 赞成；同意 If you approve of an action, event, or suggestion, you like it or are pleased about it. 赞成；同意

  • Not everyone approves of the festival. [VERB + of]

    不是所有人都赞成庆祝该节日。

  • I approved of the proposal. [VERB of noun]

    我赞成该提案。

2 VERB 赞许;嘉许;喜欢 If you approve of someone or something, you like and admire them. 赞许;嘉许;喜欢

  • You've never approved of Henry, have you?. [VERB + of]

    你从来没有喜欢过亨利，对吗？

  • I didn't approve of his manner. [VERB of noun]

    我不喜欢他的态度。

3 VERB 批准；认可；通过 If someone in a position of authority approves a plan or idea, they formally agree to it and say that it can happen. 批准；认可；通过

  • The Russian Parliament has approved a program of radical economic reforms. [VERB noun]

    俄罗斯议会已经通过一项重大经济改革方案。

  • MPs approved the Bill by a majority of 97. [VERB noun]

    议员们以97票的多数票通过了该议案。

SYN agree to, second, allow, pass  
4 VERB 批准；核准 If a product or person is approved by an official organization, they are declared to be of a good enough standard to be used or employed. 批准；核准 [Also VERB n]

  • We have three suppliers in all who are approved by the Organic Farm Food Association. [be VERB-ed]

    我们总共有3家获有机农产品协会认可的供货商。

5 See also: approved  approving
