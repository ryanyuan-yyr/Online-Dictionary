omnipotent
ɒmnɪpətənt ɑmˈnɪpətənt
1 ADJ 全能的；有无限权力的；无所不能的 Someone or something that is omnipotent has complete power over things or people. 全能的；有无限权力的；无所不能的 [formal]

  • Doug lived in the shadow of his seemingly omnipotent father.

    道格生活在他那看似权威至高无上的父亲的阴影里。

  • ...the omnipotent power of an author.

    作家的全能力量

SYN almighty, supreme, invincible, all-powerful
