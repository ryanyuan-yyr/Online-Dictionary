redeemable
rɪdiːməbəl rɪˈdɛmptəbəl
ADJ 可交换的;可兑换的 If something is redeemable, it can be exchanged for a particular sum of money or for goods worth a particular sum. 可交换的;可兑换的 [Also + for]

  • Their full catalogue costs $5, redeemable against a first order. [+ against]

    他们的完整目录售价5美元，第一次订购时可返还这5美元。
