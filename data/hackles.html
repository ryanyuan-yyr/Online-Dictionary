hackles
hækəlz ˈhækəlz
PHRASE 使…发怒 If something raises your hackles or makes your hackles rise, it makes you feel angry and hostile. 使…发怒

  • Oh boy, this record's going to raise a few hackles.

    哎，这张唱片会激怒一些人的。

  • You could see her hackles rising as she heard him outline his plan.

    看得出来，她在听他大致介绍他的计划时很是恼火。
