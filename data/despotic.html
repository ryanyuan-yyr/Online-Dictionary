despotic
dɪspɒtɪk dɛsˈpɑtɪk
ADJ-GRADED 专制的;残暴的;暴虐的 If you say that someone is despotic, you are emphasizing that they use their power over other people in a very unfair or cruel way. 专制的;残暴的;暴虐的 [emphasis]

  • The country was ruled by a despotic tyrant.

    该国处在一个专制暴君的统治之下。

SYN tyrannical, authoritarian, dictatorial, absolute
