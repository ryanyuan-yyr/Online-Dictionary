bored
bɔːʳd
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 感到厌倦的；感到厌烦的 If you are bored, you feel tired and impatient because you have lost interest in something or because you have nothing to do. 感到厌倦的；感到厌烦的

  • I am getting very bored with this entire business. [+ with]

    我开始对这整件事情感到非常厌倦。

SYN fed up, tired, hacked (off) [US , slang] , wearied  
Usage Note :

  • Take care not to confuse bored and boring.

    注意不要混淆bored和boring。
