flagship
flægʃɪp ˈflægˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural flagships
1 N-COUNT 旗舰;(尤指指挥官所在的)主舰 A flagship is the most important ship in a fleet of ships, especially the one on which the commander of the fleet is sailing. 旗舰;(尤指指挥官所在的)主舰
2 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 王牌;主打产品;旗舰产品 The flagship of a group of things that are owned or produced by a particular organization is the most important one. 王牌;主打产品;旗舰产品

  • The hospital has been the government's flagship, leading the health service reforms.

    这家医院一直是政府的旗舰机构，引领着医疗服务的改革。

  • The company plans to open a flagship store in New York this month.

    公司计划本月在纽约开一家旗舰店。
