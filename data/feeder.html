feeder
fiːdəʳ ˈfidər
Word forms: plural feeders
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 汇入主干道的;汇入干流的;(铁路)支线的 A feeder road, railway, or river is a smaller one that leads to a more important one. 汇入主干道的;汇入干流的;(铁路)支线的

  • ...the feeder road leading to the airport.

    通往机场的岔路

2 N-COUNT [usually NOUN n] (航空或铁路)支线 Feeder airline and railway services connect major routes and local destinations. (航空或铁路)支线

  • ...a feeder to British Airways's transatlantic destinations.

    英国航空公司飞往大西洋彼岸的航班的支线航线

3 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] (为重点学校或球队输送学生或球员的)生源学校，直属学校，直属球队 A feeder school or team provides students or players for a larger or more important one. (为重点学校或球队输送学生或球员的)生源学校，直属学校，直属球队

  • ...children coming up from two infant feeder schools.

    从两个直属幼儿学校来的孩子

4 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 鸟食罐;喂食器 A feeder is a container that you fill with food for birds or animals. 鸟食罐;喂食器
