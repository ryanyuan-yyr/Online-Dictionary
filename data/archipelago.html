archipelago
ɑːʳkipeləgoʊ ˌɑrkəˈpɛləˌgoʊ
Word forms: plural archipelagos, plural archipelagoes
N-COUNT 群岛；列岛 An archipelago is a group of islands, especially small islands. 群岛；列岛

  • ...the Azores, a rugged, volcanic archipelago of nine islands.

    亚速尔群岛，由9个小岛组成的多岩石的火山群岛
