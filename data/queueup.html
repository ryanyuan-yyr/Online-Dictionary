queue up
1 PHRASAL VERB [usually cont] (为得到某机会而)排队，排队等候 If you say that people are queuing up to do or have something, you mean that a lot of them want the opportunity to do it or have it. (为得到某机会而)排队，排队等候 [mainly British]

  • People are queueing up to work for me!. [VERB PREP. to-infinitive]

    想为我效力的人可是排着长队呢！

  • There are a growing number of countries queueing up for membership. [VERB PREP. + for]

    越来越多的国家争相成为其中的一员。

in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 line up
