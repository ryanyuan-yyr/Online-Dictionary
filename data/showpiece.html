showpiece
ʃoʊpiːs ˈʃoʊˌpis
(also show-piece)Word forms: plural showpieces
N-COUNT (尤指令人惊叹的)样品，典范 A showpiece is something that is admired because it is the best thing of its type, especially something that is intended to be impressive. (尤指令人惊叹的)样品，典范

  • The factory was to be a showpiece of Western investment in the East. [+ of]

    这家工厂将会成为西方在东方投资的样板。

  • Wembley is the showpiece stadium in this country.

    温布利球场是该国最具代表性的体育场。

SYN display, model, ideal, showcase
