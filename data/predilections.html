predilection
priːdɪlekʃən pred- ˌprɛdəˈlɛkʃən ˌ pridəˈlɛkʃən
Word forms: plural predilections
N-COUNT 爱好；偏爱；偏好 If you have a predilection for something, you have a strong liking for it. 爱好；偏爱；偏好 [formal]

  • ...his predilection for fast cars and fast horses. [+ for]

    他对跑车和骏马的偏爱

SYN liking, love, taste, weakness
