head off
1 PHRASAL VERB 拦截;拦住 If you head off a person, animal, or vehicle, you move to a place in front of them in order to capture them or make them change the direction they are moving in. 拦截;拦住 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • He changed direction swiftly, turned into the hallway and headed her off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他迅速改变方向，拐进走廊截住了她。

SYN intercept, divert, deflect, cut someone off  
2 PHRASAL VERB 阻止，防止（尤指不愉快的事）发生 If you head something off, especially something unpleasant, you take action before it is expected to happen in order to prevent it from happening. 阻止，防止（尤指不愉快的事）发生

  • He would ask Congress to intervene and head off a strike. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他将要求国会进行干预，防止发生罢工。

  • You have to be good at spotting trouble on the way and heading it off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    你必须善于发现潜在的麻烦并加以化解。

SYN prevent, stop, avert, parry
