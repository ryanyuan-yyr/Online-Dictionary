mollycoddle
mɒlikɒdəl ˈmɑliˌkɑdəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mollycoddles, present participle mollycoddling, past tense , past participle mollycoddled
VERB 溺爱;娇惯；宠爱 If you accuse someone of mollycoddling someone else, you are critical of them for doing too many things for the other person and protecting them too much from unpleasant experiences. 溺爱;娇惯；宠爱 [disapproval]

  • Christopher accused me of mollycoddling Andrew. [VERB noun]

    克里斯托弗指责我太宠着安德鲁。

SYN pamper, baby, ruin, pet
