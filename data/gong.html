gong
gɒŋ gɔːŋ gɔŋ gɑŋ
Word forms: plural gongs
1 N-COUNT 锣 A gong is a large, flat, circular piece of metal that you hit with a hammer to make a sound like a loud bell. Gongs are sometimes used as musical instruments, or to give a signal that it is time to do something. 锣

  • On the stroke of seven, a gong summons guests into the dining-room.

    7点整时一阵锣声将客人召集到餐厅。

2 N-COUNT 奖章；勋章 People sometimes refer to a medal or an award as a gong . 奖章；勋章 [British, informal]

  • Spike Milligan has finally got the gong he had been promised.

    斯派克·米利根最终拿到了早就承诺给他的那块奖章。

  • ...peerages, knighthoods and other assorted gongs.

    贵族头衔、爵士称号以及其他各种勋章
