disrepute
dɪsrɪpjuːt ˌdɪsrɪˈpjuːt ˌdɪsrɪˈpjut ˈdɪsrɪˌpjut
PHRASE 丧失名誉;落得坏名声 If something is brought into disrepute or falls into disrepute, it loses its good reputation, because it is connected with activities that people do not approve of. 丧失名誉;落得坏名声

  • Such people bring our profession into disrepute.

    这种人令我们这一行业蒙羞。
