disseminate
dɪsemɪneɪt dɪˈsɛməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense disseminates, present participle disseminating, past tense , past participle disseminated
VERB 散布;传播 To disseminate information or knowledge means to distribute it so that it reaches many people or organizations. 散布;传播 [formal]

  • It took years to disseminate information about Aids in Africa. [VERB noun]

    在非洲传播关于艾滋病方面的知识耗时数年。

  • They disseminated anti-French propaganda. [VERB noun]

    他们散布反对法国的宣传资料。

SYN spread, publish, broadcast, distribute  
dissemination

  • He promoted the dissemination of scientific ideas.

    他推动了科学观念的传播。
