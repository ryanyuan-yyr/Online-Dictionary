quarrelsome
kwɒrəlsəm kwɔːr- ˈkwɔrəlsəm ˈkwɑrəlsəm
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 好吵架的;喜欢争吵的；好争论的 A quarrelsome person often gets involved in arguments. 好吵架的;喜欢争吵的；好争论的

  • Benedict had been a wild boy and a quarrelsome young man.

    贝内迪克特小时候野性难驯，年轻时又爱与人争吵。

SYN argumentative, belligerent, pugnacious, cross
