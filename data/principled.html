principled
prɪnsɪpəld ˈprɪnsəpəld
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 原则性强的；坚持操守的 If you describe someone as principled, you approve of them because they have strong moral principles. 原则性强的；坚持操守的 [approval]

  • She was a strong, principled woman.

    她是一个坚强而有原则的女人。

  • You have to take a principled stand.

    你得讲原则。

SYN moral, ethical, upright, honourable
