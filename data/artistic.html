artistic
ɑːʳtɪstɪk ɑrˈtɪstɪk
1 ADJ-GRADED 有艺术天赋的;有艺术鉴赏力的 Someone who is artistic is good at drawing or painting, or arranging things in a beautiful way. 有艺术天赋的;有艺术鉴赏力的

  • They encourage boys to be sensitive and artistic.

    他们鼓励培养男孩子的敏感和艺术气质。

  • Mary's got it all so nice — you remember how artistic she always was with colors.

    玛丽把一切弄得都很漂亮—你还记得她以前总是对色彩很有艺术品位吧。

SYN creative, cultured, original, sensitive  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 艺术的;艺术家的 Artistic means relating to art or artists. 艺术的;艺术家的

  • ...the campaign for artistic freedom.

    艺术自由运动

  • ...their 1,300 year old artistic traditions.

    他们1,300年的艺术传统

artistically

  • ...artistically gifted children.

    有艺术天分的孩子

  • Artistically, the photographs are stunning.

    从艺术角度来说，这些照片精美绝伦。

3 ADJ-GRADED 精美的；富有艺术性的 An artistic design or arrangement is beautiful. 精美的；富有艺术性的

  • ...an artistic arrangement of stone paving.

    石子路的精美图案

artistically

  • ...artistically carved garden ornaments.

    精雕细琢的庭园装饰物

  • ...vegetarian dishes which can be presented artistically.

    可以做得很精美的素菜
