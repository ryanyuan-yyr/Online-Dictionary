put through
1 PHRASAL VERB 为…接通电话 When someone puts through someone who is making a telephone call, they make the connection that allows the telephone call to take place. 为…接通电话 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • The operator will put you through. [VERB noun PREP.]

    接线员会为你接通电话。

  • He asked to be put through to Charley Lunn. [be V-ed P + to]

    他要求跟查利·伦恩通话。

2 PHRASAL VERB 使遭受 If someone puts you through an unpleasant experience, they make you experience it. 使遭受

  • She wouldn't want to put them through the ordeal of a huge ceremony. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    她不想让他们参加大型仪式受罪。

  • Those two husbands put me through hell. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    那两任丈夫使我受尽地狱般的煎熬。
