get on to
1 PHRASAL VERB 开始讨论 If you get on to a topic when you are speaking, you start talking about it. 开始讨论

  • We got on to the subject of relationships. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我们开始讨论情爱关系这个话题。

2 PHRASAL VERB （同…）联系，接触 If you get on to someone, you contact them in order to ask them to do something or to give them some information. （同…）联系，接触 [mainly British]

  • I got on to him and explained some of the things I had been thinking of. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我同他取得了联系，并向他解释了我一直在思考的一些事情。
