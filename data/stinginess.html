stingy
stɪndʒi ˈstɪŋɪ ˈstɪŋi ˈstɪndʒi
Word forms: comparative stingier, superlative stingiest
ADJ-GRADED 吝啬的;小气的 If you describe someone as stingy, you are criticizing them for being unwilling to spend money. 吝啬的;小气的 [informal, disapproval]

  • The West is stingy with aid.

    西方在援助上有点吝啬。

  • Winston was not a stingy man.

    温斯顿不是个小气之人。

SYN mean, penny-pinching [informal] , miserly, near [informal]
