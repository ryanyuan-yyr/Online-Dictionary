flannel
flænəl ˈflænəl
Word forms: plural flannels
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 法兰绒 Flannel is a soft cloth, usually made of cotton or wool, that is used for making clothes. 法兰绒

  • He wore a faded red flannel shirt.

    他身穿一件褪了色的红色法兰绒衬衫。

2 N-PLURAL [also a pair of N] 法兰绒男裤 Flannels are men's trousers made of flannel. 法兰绒男裤 [old-fashioned]

  • ...respectable young chaps, dressed in flannels and blazers.

    穿着法兰绒裤子和夹克的体面小伙子们

3 N-COUNT (洗澡用的)毛巾 A flannel is a small cloth that you use for washing yourself. (洗澡用的)毛巾 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 washcloth
4 N-UNCOUNT 废话;敷衍的话;兜圈子的话 If you describe what someone has said as flannel, you disapprove because they have said a lot but they have avoided telling you what you wanted to know. 废话;敷衍的话;兜圈子的话 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • No amount of flannel could disguise that this was, at best, a minimalist solution.

    说多少废话都无法掩饰这最多只是一个极简主义的解决方案。
