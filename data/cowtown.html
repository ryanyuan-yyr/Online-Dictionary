cow town
Word forms: cow towns
1 N-COUNT (乏味老式的)小镇If someone describes a town as a cow town, they mean that it is small, dull, and old-fashioned. (乏味老式的)小镇 [US, disapproval]

  • People thought we were a little cow town.

    大家以为我们是一个养牛小镇。

2 N-COUNT (养牛场附近的)小镇A cow town is a town or city that is near to cattle ranches. (养牛场附近的)小镇 [US]

  • At its peak, Dodge City was the biggest cow town in the West.

    道奇市在巅峰时期是西部最大的牛镇。
