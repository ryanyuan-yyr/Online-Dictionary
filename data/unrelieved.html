unrelieved
ʌnrɪliːvd ˌʌnrɪˈliːvd
ADJ 未减轻的；未得缓解的 If you describe something unpleasant as unrelieved, you mean that it is very severe and is not replaced by anything better, even for a short time. 未减轻的；未得缓解的

  • ...unrelieved misery.

    难以释怀的悲痛

  • The sun baked down on the concrete, unrelieved by any breeze. [+ by]

    太阳炙烤着水泥，热度没有因为任何微风而有所减轻。
