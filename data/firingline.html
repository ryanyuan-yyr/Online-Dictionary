firing line
(also firing-line)Word forms: plural firing lines
1 N-COUNT 射击线 If you are in the firing line in a conflict, you are in a position where someone is aiming their gun at you. 射击线

  • Any hostages in the firing line would have been sacrificed.

    任何射击线上的人质就都会成为牺牲品。

  • He was sure he would pin down the enemy between two firing lines.

    他确信自己能够把敌人压制在两条火力线间。

2 N-SING [usu in/out of N] 批评;责难;攻击 If you say that someone is in the firing line, you mean that they are being criticized, blamed, or attacked for something. 批评;责难;攻击

  • Foreign banks are in the firing line too.

    外国银行也在遭批之列。

  • Young players found themselves too much in the firing line.

    年轻球员总是发现自己备受责难。
