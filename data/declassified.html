declassify
diːklæsɪfaɪ diˈklæsəˌfaɪ ˈdiˈklæsəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense declassifies, present participle declassifying, past tense , past participle declassified
VERB [usually passive] 撤销(秘密文件或档案)的密级;对…解密 If secret documents or records are declassified, it is officially stated that they are no longer secret. 撤销(秘密文件或档案)的密级;对…解密

  • These reports were only declassified last year. [be VERB-ed]

    这些报告去年才被撤销了密级。
