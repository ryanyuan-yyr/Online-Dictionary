desire
dɪzaɪəʳ dɪˈzaɪr
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense desires, present participle desiring, past tense , past participle desired
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN to-infinitive] 渴望;热望;欲望 A desire is a strong wish to do or have something. 渴望;热望;欲望

  • I had a strong desire to help and care for people.

    我非常渴望能够帮助和照顾他人。

  • They seem to have lost their desire for life. [+ for]

    他们似乎已丧失了对生活的热望。

SYN wish, want, longing, need  
2 VERB [no cont] 渴望;想望;想要 If you desire something, you want it. 渴望;想望;想要 [formal]

  • She had remarried and desired a child with her new husband. [VERB noun]

    她又结婚了，并且渴望和新任丈夫生个小孩。

  • But Fred was bored and desired to go home. [VERB to-infinitive]

    但弗雷德觉得很无聊，想要回家。

  • He desired me to inform her that he had made his peace with God. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    他想让我告诉她他已接受了上天的安排，获得了心灵的安宁。

desired

3 N-UNCOUNT 情欲;肉欲;性欲 Desire for someone is a strong feeling of wanting to have sex with them. 情欲;肉欲;性欲

  • Teenage sex, for instance, may come not out of genuine desire but from a need to get love.

    例如，青少年的性行为可能并不是源于真正的情欲，而是出于一种对爱的需要。

SYN lust, passion, libido, appetite  
4 VERB [no cont] 想与…发生性关系 If you desire someone, you want to have sex with them. 想与…发生性关系

  • It never occurred to him that she might not desire him. [VERB noun]

    他从没有想过她可能不想跟他上床。

5 PHRASE （用于说明性的书面语中）如果需要的话,如果愿意的话 If desired is used in instructions in written English to indicate that someone can choose to take the course of action mentioned. （用于说明性的书面语中）如果需要的话,如果愿意的话

  • Additional courses may be taken if desired.

    如有需要可选修额外的课程。

  • Transfer this sauce to a separate saucepan, if desired.

    如果愿意的话，可以把这些调味汁倒入一只单独的长柄锅里。

6 PHRASE [Ns inflect, usu poss PHR] 心里想望之人;心中渴望之物 If you say that someone or something is your heart's desire, you mean that you want that person or thing very much. 心里想望之人;心中渴望之物 [literary]

  • He was extremely devious in his efforts to achieve his heart's desire.

    他在设法得到自己的中意之物时会不择手段。

7 PHRASE 尚有（许多）需要改进之处;有待（大幅）提高 If you say that something leaves a lot to be desired, you mean that it is not as good as it should be. 尚有（许多）需要改进之处;有待（大幅）提高 [disapproval]

  • The selection of TV programmes, especially at the weekend, leaves a lot to be desired.

    可供选择的电视节目，尤其是在周末的时候，还有大幅提升的空间。

  • Food seems to have been available, even if the quality left much to be desired.

    食品似乎能够买到了，虽然质量还远不尽如人意。

  • It is just possible that the accuracy of the information provided might leave something to be desired.

    很有可能所提供信息的准确性还不够高。
