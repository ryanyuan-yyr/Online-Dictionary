potentiality
pətenʃiælɪti poʊˌtɛnʃiˈæləti pəˌtɛnʃiˈæləti
Word forms: plural potentialities
N-VAR 潜在性；可能性；潜力 If something has potentialities or potentiality, it is capable of being used or developed in particular ways. 潜在性；可能性；潜力 [formal]

  • The breathtaking potentialities of mechanization set the minds of manufacturers and merchants on fire. [+ of]

    机械化的惊人潜力使制造厂和商人们激动不已。

  • All of these are quite useful breeds whose potentiality has not been realised.

    所有这些都是十分有用的品种，其潜力还没有被完全发挥。

SYN capacity, promise, ability, potential
