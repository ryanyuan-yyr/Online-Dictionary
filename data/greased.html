grease
griːs gris griz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense greases, present participle greasing, past tense , past participle greased
1 N-UNCOUNT 润滑油 Grease is a thick, oily substance which is put on the moving parts of cars and other machines in order to make them work smoothly. 润滑油

  • ...grease-stained hands.

    沾满油渍的手

2 VERB 给…加润滑油；为…涂（或抹）油 If you grease a part of a car, machine, or device, you put grease on it in order to make it work smoothly. 给…加润滑油；为…涂（或抹）油

  • I greased front and rear hubs and adjusted the brakes. [VERB noun]

    我在前后轮毂上都抹了润滑油，还调校了刹车。

3 N-UNCOUNT （皮肤分泌的）油脂 Grease is an oily substance that is produced by your skin. （皮肤分泌的）油脂

  • His hair is thick with grease.

    他的头发油乎乎的。

4 N-UNCOUNT 动物油脂 Grease is animal fat that is produced by cooking meat. You can use grease for cooking. 动物油脂

  • He could smell the bacon grease.

    他闻到一股腌肉的油脂味儿。

5 VERB 给…涂上油（以防粘锅） If you grease a dish, you put a small amount of fat or oil around the inside of it in order to prevent food sticking to it during cooking. 给…涂上油（以防粘锅）

  • Grease two sturdy baking sheets and heat the oven to 400 degrees. [VERB noun]

    在两块结实的烤板上抹些油，把烤炉加热到400度。

  • Place the frozen rolls on a greased baking tray. [VERB-ed]

    将冷冻的肉卷放到抹了油的烤盘上。
