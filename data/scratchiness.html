scratchy
skrætʃi ˈskrætʃi
1 ADJ-GRADED 沙哑的;发出沙沙声的 Scratchy sounds are thin and harsh. 沙哑的;发出沙沙声的

  • Listening to the scratchy recording, I recognized Walt Whitman immediately.

    听到沙哑的录音，我立刻知道那是沃尔特·惠特曼。

2 ADJ-GRADED (衣服、织物)粗糙扎人的，使人发痒的 Scratchy clothes or fabrics are rough and uncomfortable to wear next to your skin. (衣服、织物)粗糙扎人的，使人发痒的

  • Wool is so scratchy that it irritates the skin.

    毛料衣服太扎人，令皮肤很不舒服。
