stick at
PHRASAL VERB 继续做;坚持做 If you stick at a task or activity, you continue doing it, even if it is difficult. 继续做;坚持做

  • You will find it hard at first, but stick at it. [VERB PREP. noun]

    你会发现刚开始时有点难，但要坚持下去。

  • He became more and more irritated by her inability to stick at anything. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他对她做任何事都会半途而废感到越来越恼火。

SYN keep at, continue, persist, see through
