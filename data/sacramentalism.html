sacramental
sækrəmentəl ˌsækrəˈmɛntəl
1 ADJ 圣事的;圣礼的 Something that is sacramental is connected with a Christian religious ceremony. 圣事的;圣礼的

  • ...the sacramental wine.

    圣餐酒

2 ADJ 神圣的;宗教的 Sacramental is used to describe something that is considered holy or religious. 神圣的;宗教的

  • ...her view that music is a sacramental art.

    她将音乐视为神圣艺术的观点
