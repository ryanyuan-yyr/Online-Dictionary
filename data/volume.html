volume
vɒljuːm ˈvɑljum ˈvɑljəm
Word forms: plural volumes
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 量;数额 The volume of something is the amount of it that there is. 量;数额

  • Senior officials will be discussing how the volume of sales might be reduced. [+ of]

    高层领导将要讨论销售量如何削减。

  • ...the sheer volume of traffic and accidents. [+ of]

    交通流量之大、事故之多

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 体积;容积;容量 The volume of an object is the amount of space that it contains or occupies. 体积;容积;容量

  • It is 2,300 metres above sea level, so a given volume of air contains only about one-third as much oxygen as it would at sea level.

    这是在海拔2,300米的高空，因此一定体积空气的含氧量只有海平面处的约1/3。

  • When egg whites are beaten they can rise to seven or eight times their original volume.

    蛋清打散后，体积可增至原来的七八倍。

3 N-COUNT 书;书籍 A volume is a book. 书;书籍 [formal]

  • ...a 125-page volume.

    一本125页的书

4 N-COUNT （书籍的）卷，册 A volume is one book in a series of books. （书籍的）卷，册

  • ...the first volume of his autobiography. [+ of]

    他自传的第一卷

5 N-COUNT （杂志的）合集，合订本 A volume is a collection of several issues of a magazine, for example all the issues for one year. （杂志的）合集，合订本

  • ...bound volumes of the magazine. [+ of]

    杂志的合订本

6 N-UNCOUNT （广播、电视或音响系统的）音量，声量,响度 The volume of a radio, television, or sound system is the loudness of the sound it produces. （广播、电视或音响系统的）音量，声量,响度

  • He turned down the volume.

    他调低了音量。

  • He came to complain about the volume of the music. [+ of]

    他过来抱怨音乐的音量太高了。

SYN loudness, sound, amplification  
7 PHRASE 清楚表明;详尽说明;充分说明 If something such as an action speaks volumes about a person or thing, it gives you a lot of information about them. 清楚表明;详尽说明;充分说明

  • What you wear speaks volumes about you. [+ about]

    你的衣着能传递很多关于你的信息。

  • Their absence spoke volumes.

    他们的缺席已经充分说明了问题。
