ultraviolet
ʌltrəvaɪələt ˌʌltrəˈvaɪəlɪt ˌʌltrəˈvaɪəlɪt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 紫外的；紫外线的 Ultraviolet light or radiation is what causes your skin to become darker in colour after you have been in sunlight. In large amounts ultraviolet light is harmful. 紫外的；紫外线的

  • The sun's ultraviolet rays are responsible for both tanning and burning.

    阳光中的紫外线是皮肤晒黑和灼伤的根源。
