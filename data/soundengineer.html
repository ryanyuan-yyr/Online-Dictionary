sound engineer
saʊnd ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪə
Word forms: plural sound engineers
N-COUNT 音响师 A sound engineer is a person who works in a recording studio or for a radio or television company, and whose job it is to alter and balance the levels of different sounds as they are recorded. 音响师 [mainly British]
