furnished
fɜːʳnɪʃt ˈfɜːnɪʃt
1 ADJ 带家具的;配有家具的 A furnished room or house is available to be rented together with the furniture in it. 带家具的;配有家具的

  • Eleanor moved into a small furnished apartment.

    埃莉诺搬进了一间配有家具的小公寓里。

2 ADJ [adverb ADJ] (房间或房屋)配有…家具的 When you say that a room or house is furnished in a particular way, you are describing the kind or amount of furniture that it has in it. (房间或房屋)配有…家具的

  • We took tea by lamplight in his sparsely furnished house.

    在他那所少有陈设的房子里，我们坐在灯下喝着茶。

  • All 300 elegantly furnished rooms have private bath.

    300 个布置讲究的房间全都配有独立浴室。
