demented
dɪmentɪd diˈmɛntɪd dɪˈmɛntɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 精神错乱的;（尤指）患早老性痴呆的 Someone who is demented has a severe mental illness, especially Alzheimer's disease. 精神错乱的;（尤指）患早老性痴呆的 [medicine, old-fashioned]

  • At what point does it become necessary to place a demented person in a nursing home?

    到了什么时候算是有必要把早老性痴呆病人送到养老院去？

2 ADJ-GRADED 古怪的;愚蠢的;疯狂的 If you describe someone as demented, you think that their actions are strange, foolish, or uncontrolled. 古怪的;愚蠢的;疯狂的 [informal, disapproval]

  • He had been granted his own TV show by some demented executive.

    某个愚蠢的主管已经批准他自己来做一个电视节目。

  • Sid broke into demented laughter.

    锡德狂笑起来。
