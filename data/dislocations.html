dislocation
dɪsləkeɪʃən ˌdɪsləˈkeɪʃən ˌdɪsloʊˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural dislocations
N-VAR (制度、进程、生活方式等的)混乱，紊乱 Dislocation is a situation in which something such as a system, process, or way of life is greatly disturbed or prevented from continuing as normal. (制度、进程、生活方式等的)混乱，紊乱

  • Millions of refugees have suffered a total dislocation of their lives. [+ of]

    无数的难民饱受生活颠沛流离之苦。

SYN disruption, disorder, disturbance, disarray
