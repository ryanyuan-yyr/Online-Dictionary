blade
bleɪd bleɪd
Word forms: plural blades
1 N-COUNT 刃；锋 The blade of a knife, axe, or saw is the edge, which is used for cutting. 刃；锋

  • Many of these tools have sharp blades, so be careful.

    这些工具中很多都有锋利的刀片，务必小心。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （螺旋桨的）叶片 The blades of a propeller are the long, flat parts that turn round. （螺旋桨的）叶片
3 N-COUNT 桨叶 The blade of an oar is the thin flat part that you put into the water. 桨叶
4 N-COUNT （草的）叶片 A blade of grass is a single piece of grass. （草的）叶片
