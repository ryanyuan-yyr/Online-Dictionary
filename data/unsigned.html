unsigned
ʌnsaɪnd ʌnˈsaɪnd
1 ADJ 没签名的 An unsigned document does not have anyone's signature on it. 没签名的

  • The envelope contained a typed, unsigned letter demanding £75,000 in cash.

    信封里装着一封打印的、没签名的信，索要75,000英镑。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 没签约的 An unsigned band has not signed a contract with a company to produce CDs. 没签约的

  • Fugazi are America's biggest unsigned alternative band.

    弗格齐是美国最大的未签约的另类乐队。
