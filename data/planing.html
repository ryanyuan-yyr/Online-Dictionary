plane
pleɪn pleɪn
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense planes, present participle planing, past tense , past participle planed
1 N-COUNT 飞机 A plane is a vehicle with wings and one or more engines, which can fly through the air. 飞机

  • He had plenty of time to catch his plane.

    他有足够的时间赶飞机。

  • Her mother was killed in a plane crash.

    她母亲在一次坠机中遇难。

  • ...fighter planes.

    战斗机

SYN aeroplane, aircraft, jet, airliner  
2 N-COUNT （可能带有斜度的）平面 A plane is a flat, level surface which may be sloping at a particular angle. （可能带有斜度的）平面

  • ...a building with angled planes.

    带有坡面的建筑物

SYN flat surface, the flat, horizontal, level surface  
3 N-SING （想象中的）平面 If a number of points are in the same plane, one line or one flat surface could pass through them all. （想象中的）平面

  • All the planets orbit the Sun in roughly the same plane, round its equator.

    所有的行星大致在同一平面上绕太阳赤道运转。

4 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] （精神的）水平，水准，层次 If you say that something is on a higher plane, you mean that it is more spiritual or less concerned with ordinary things. （精神的）水平，水准，层次 [literary]

  • ...life on a higher plane of existence.

    更高境界的生活

  • We felt we were living life on several different planes.

    我们感到大家生活的精神层次各不相同。

SYN level, position, stage, footing  
5 N-COUNT 木工刨；刨子 A plane is a tool that has a flat bottom with a sharp blade in it. You move the plane over a piece of wood in order to remove thin pieces of its surface. 木工刨；刨子
6 VERB 刨；刨平；刨光 If you plane a piece of wood, you make it smaller or smoother by using a plane. 刨；刨平；刨光 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She watches him plane the surface of a walnut board. [VERB noun]

    她看着他刨平一块胡桃木板。

  • Again I planed the surface flush. [VERB noun adj.ive]

    我又一次把表面刨平。

Plane down means the same as plane.plane down 同 plane

  • The piece was reduced in size by planing down the four corners.

    这块木头的4个角刨过后就变小了。

7 VERB （轻触水面）疾驶，滑行 If something such as a boat planes across water, it moves quickly across the water, just touching the surface. （轻触水面）疾驶，滑行 [Also VERB]

  • All four of the boats planed across the Solent with the greatest of ease. [V across n]

    4艘船都极其自如地掠过索伦特海峡。

SYN skim, sail, skate, glide  
8 N-COUNT 悬铃木 A plane or a plane tree is a large tree with broad leaves which often grows in towns. 悬铃木
Phrasal verbs:
plane down
