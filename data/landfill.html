landfill
lændfɪl ˈlændˌfɪl
Word forms: plural landfills
1 N-UNCOUNT 垃圾填埋；垃圾堆填 Landfill is a method of getting rid of very large amounts of rubbish by burying it in a large deep hole. 垃圾填埋；垃圾堆填

  • ...the environmental costs of landfill.

    垃圾填埋的环境代价

2 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 垃圾填埋场；垃圾堆填区 A landfill is a large deep hole in which very large amounts of rubbish are buried. 垃圾填埋场；垃圾堆填区

  • The rubbish in modern landfills does not rot.

    现代垃圾填埋场里的垃圾不会腐烂。

  • ...the cost of disposing of refuse in landfill sites.

    垃圾填埋场处理废弃物的成本
