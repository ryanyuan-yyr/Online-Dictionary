sadistic
sədɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 施虐狂的;虐待狂的 A sadistic person obtains pleasure from hurting other people and making them suffer physically or mentally. 施虐狂的;虐待狂的

  • The prisoners rioted against mistreatment by sadistic guards.

    囚犯因不堪忍受狱警施虐而发动了暴乱。

  • There was a sadistic streak in him.

    他有虐待狂的倾向。

SYN cruel, savage, brutal, beastly [informal]  
sadistically

  • Many were killed, often most sadistically.

    许多人被杀害，而且常常是惨遭虐杀。
