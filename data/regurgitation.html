regurgitate
rɪgɜːʳdʒɪteɪt rɪˈgɜrdʒəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense regurgitates, present participle regurgitating, past tense , past participle regurgitated
1 VERB 机械重复 If you say that someone is regurgitating ideas or facts, you mean that they are repeating them without understanding them properly. 机械重复 [disapproval]

  • You can get sick to death of a friend regurgitating her partner's opinions. [VERB noun]

    对于一个只会机械地重复其搭档观点的朋友，你可能会无比腻烦。

2 VERB 反刍;使(食物)回涌 If a person or animal regurgitates food, they bring it back up from their stomach before it has been digested. 反刍;使(食物)回涌 [formal]

  • Sometimes he regurgitates the food we give him because he cannot swallow. [VERB noun]

    有时他会倒嚼我们喂给他吃的食物，因为他无法吞咽。
