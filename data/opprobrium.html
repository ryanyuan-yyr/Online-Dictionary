opprobrium
əproʊbriəm əˈproʊbriəm
N-UNCOUNT （公开的）谴责，责难，抨击 Opprobrium is open criticism or disapproval of something that someone has done. （公开的）谴责，责难，抨击 [formal]

  • His political opinions have attracted the opprobrium of the Left. [+ of]

    他的政治观点遭到左派的抨击。

  • ...public opprobrium.

    公众的责难

SYN censure, criticism, condemnation, discredit
