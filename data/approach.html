approach
əproʊtʃ əˈproʊtʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense approaches, present participle approaching, past tense , past participle approached
1 VERB 靠近;接近;走近 When you approach something, you get closer to it. 靠近;接近;走近

  • He didn't approach the front door at once. [VERB noun]

    他没有马上走向前门。

  • When I approached, they grew silent. [VERB]

    当我走近时，他们就不说话了。

  • We turned to see the approaching car slow down. [VERB-ing]

    我们转身看见驶近的车慢慢停下。

SYN move towards, come to, reach, near  
Approach is also a noun.

  • At their approach the little boy ran away and hid.

    他们一走近，小男孩就逃走藏了起来。

  • ...the approach of a low-flying helicopter.

    一架低空飞行的直升机的逼近

2 N-COUNT 路径;途径;道路 An approach to a place is a road, path, or other route that leads to it. 路径;途径;道路

  • The path serves as an approach to the boat house. [+ to]

    这条小路通往船屋。

SYN access, way, drive, road  
3 VERB [no cont] 与…接洽；找…商量 If you approach someone about something, you speak to them about it for the first time, often making an offer or request. 与…接洽；找…商量 [Also + to]

  • When Chappel approached me about the job, my first reaction was of disbelief. [VERB noun prep.]

    当查普尔为这份工作找我商量时，我的第一反应是不相信。

  • He approached me to create and design the restaurant. [VERB noun to-infinitive]

    他请我建造并设计该饭店。

  • Anna approached several builders and was fortunate to come across Eddie. [VERB noun]

    安娜与几个建筑商有过接洽，并且很幸运地碰到了埃迪。

SYN make a proposal to, speak to, apply to, appeal to  
Approach is also a noun.

  • There had already been approaches from buyers interested in the whole of the group.

    已有对整组感兴趣的买家来进行洽谈。

4 VERB 探讨；处理；对待 When you approach a task, problem, or situation in a particular way, you deal with it or think about it in that way. 探讨；处理；对待

  • The Bank has approached the issue in a practical way. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    银行已经实际解决了这个问题。

  • Employers are interested in how you approach problems. [VERB noun]

    雇主们对你如何处理问题感兴趣。

SYN set about, tackle, undertake, embark on  
5 N-COUNT 方法;态度;手段 Your approach to a task, problem, or situation is the way you deal with it or think about it. 方法;态度;手段

  • We will be exploring different approaches to gathering information. [+ to]

    我们将探索收集信息的不同方法。

  • ...the adversarial approach of the British legal system.

    英国法律制度采用的辩论式审判模式

SYN way, means, course, style  
6 VERB 临近；来临 As a future time or event approaches, it gradually gets nearer as time passes. 临近；来临

  • As autumn approached, the plants and colours in the garden changed. [VERB]

    秋天临近，花园中的草木和颜色也发生了变化。

  • ...the approaching crisis. [VERB-ing]

    步步逼近的危机

Approach is also a noun.

  • ...the festive spirit that permeated the house with the approach of Christmas.

    随着圣诞节临近房子里洋溢着的节日气氛

7 VERB 接近（未来时间或事件） As you approach a future time or event, time passes so that you get gradually nearer to it. 接近（未来时间或事件）

  • We approach the end of the year with the economy slowing and little sign of cheer. [VERB noun]

    接近年末时，我们面临经济迟滞、处处萧条的局面。

8 VERB 近似；接近于 If something approaches a particular level or state, it almost reaches that level or state. 近似；接近于

  • Oil prices have approached their highest level for almost ten years. [VERB noun]

    石油价格已接近近10年中的最高水平。

  • Mansell will race at average speeds approaching 200mph. [VERB noun]

    曼塞尔将以接近每小时200英里的平均车速参赛。

SYN approximate, touch [informal] , be like, compare with
