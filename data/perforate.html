perforate
pɜːʳfəreɪt ˈpɜrfəˌreɪt ˈpɜrfərɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense perforates, present participle perforating, past tense , past participle perforated
VERB 在…上钻孔；在…上打眼 To perforate something means to make a hole or holes in it. 在…上钻孔；在…上打眼

  • I refused to wear headphones because they can perforate your eardrums. [VERB noun]

    我不愿戴耳机，因为它可能会使鼓膜穿孔。

SYN pierce, hole, bore, punch  
perforated

  • Keep good apples in perforated polythene bags.

    把好苹果放在有孔的聚乙烯袋中保存。
