duffer
dʌfəʳ ˈdʌfər
Word forms: plural duffers
N-COUNT 笨蛋;傻瓜;无用之人 If you describe someone as a duffer, you mean that they are very bad at doing something. 笨蛋;傻瓜;无用之人 [British, informal, old-fashioned, disapproval]

  • Waugh was a duffer at cricket.

    沃的板球打得很臭。

SYN clot, blunderer [British , informal] , booby, clod
