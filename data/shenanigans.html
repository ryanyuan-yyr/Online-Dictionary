shenanigans
ʃɪnænɪgənz ʃɪˈnænɪɡənz
N-PLURAL (尤指可笑、有趣的)恶作剧，诡计，鬼把戏 You can use shenanigans to refer to rather dishonest or immoral behaviour, especially when you think it is amusing or interesting. (尤指可笑、有趣的)恶作剧，诡计，鬼把戏 [informal]
