withered
wɪðəʳd
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (人或身体部位)瘦弱的，衰老的，枯槁的 If you describe a person or a part of their body as withered, you mean that they are thin and their skin looks old. (人或身体部位)瘦弱的，衰老的，枯槁的

  • ...her withered hands.

    她干瘪的双手

  • ...the bartender's withered face.

    酒保憔悴的面容

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (四肢因疾病或受伤)瘦弱的，萎缩的 Withered is used to describe someone's leg or arm when it is thin and weak because of disease or injury. (四肢因疾病或受伤)瘦弱的，萎缩的

  • She has one slightly withered leg, noticeably thinner than the other.

    她有一条腿有点儿萎缩，看上去明显比另一条腿瘦。
