1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 曲棍球 Hockey is an outdoor game played between two teams of 11 players who use long curved sticks to hit a small ball and try to score goals. 曲棍球 [British]

  • She played hockey for the national side.

    她在国家队打曲棍球。

  • ...the British hockey team.

    英国曲棍球队
