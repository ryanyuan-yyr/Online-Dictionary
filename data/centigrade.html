centigrade
sentɪgreɪd ˈsɛntəˌgreɪd
1 ADJ ℃. 摄氏度的 Centigrade is a scale for measuring temperature, in which water freezes at 0 degrees and boils at 100 degrees. It is represented by the symbol →see usage note at: temperature ℃. 摄氏度的

  • ...daytime temperatures of up to forty degrees centigrade.

    高达40摄氏度的日间温度

Centigrade is also a noun.

  • The number at the bottom is the recommended water temperature in Centigrade.

    底部的数字就是推荐的摄氏水温。
