solidarity
sɒlɪdærɪti ˌsɑləˈdærəti
N-UNCOUNT (尤指政治或国际事务中的)团结一致，相互支持 If a group of people show solidarity, they show support for each other or for another group, especially in political or international affairs. (尤指政治或国际事务中的)团结一致，相互支持

  • Supporters want to march tomorrow to show solidarity with their leaders. [+ with]

    为了显示和领袖的同心同德，支持者们希望明天举行游行。

SYN unity, harmony, unification, accord
