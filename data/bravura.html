bravura
brəvjʊərə brəˈvjʊərə -vʊrə brəˈvjʊrə bʀɑˈvuʀɑ
1 N-UNCOUNT 卖弄性的技巧；精湛技艺 If you say that someone is doing something with bravura, you mean that they are using unnecessary extra actions that emphasize their skill or importance. 卖弄性的技巧；精湛技艺 [literary]

  • The film is directed with a technical bravura and visual splendour.

    这部电影的导演过分卖弄摄影技巧，追求华丽炫目的视觉效果。

SYN brilliance, energy, spirit, display  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （表演或作品）精彩的，华丽的 A bravura performance or piece of work is done with bravura. （表演或作品）精彩的，华丽的 [literary]

  • ...a bravura performance from Durham's scorer, Brian Hunt.

    达勒姆队的得分手布赖恩·亨特的精彩表现

SYN brilliant, outstanding, superb, magnificent
