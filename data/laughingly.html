laughingly
lɑːfɪŋli læf-
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 戏弄地；开玩笑地；可笑地 If you laughingly refer to something with a particular name or description, the description is not appropriate and you think that this is either amusing or annoying. 戏弄地；开玩笑地；可笑地

  • I spent much of what I laughingly call 'the holidays' working through 621 pages of typescript.

    我把我戏称为“假日”的大部分时间都花在那621页的打字稿上了。

  • What I laughingly refer to as my garden is a corner lot by our postage-stamp size house.

    我戏称为花园的地方，是我们家那豆腐块大小的屋子旁边的一个犄角旮旯。
