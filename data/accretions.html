accretion
əkriːʃən əˈkriʃən
Word forms: plural accretions
1 N-COUNT 增加物;添加物 An accretion is an addition to something, usually one that has been added over a period of time. 增加物;添加物 [formal]

  • The script has been gathering editorial accretions for years.

    多年来该剧本一直在修改。

2 N-UNCOUNT 添加；累积 Accretion is the process of new layers or parts being added to something so that it increases in size. 添加；累积 [formal]

  • A coral reef is built by the accretion of tiny, identical organisms. [+ of]

    珊瑚礁是由许多相同的微生物不断堆积而成。
