banana
bənɑːnə -næn- bəˈnænə
Word forms: plural bananas
1 N-VAR 香蕉 Bananas are long curved fruit with yellow skins. 香蕉

  • ...a bunch of bananas.

    一串香蕉

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 愚蠢的；发狂的；疯狂的 If someone is behaving in a silly or crazy way, or if they become extremely angry, you can say that they are going bananas . 愚蠢的；发狂的；疯狂的 [informal]

  • People went bananas with boredom.

    人们闷得发狂。

  • Adamson's going to go bananas on this one.

    亚当森会被这个气疯的。
