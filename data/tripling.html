triple
trɪpəl ˈtrɪpəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense triples, present participle tripling, past tense , past participle tripled
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 三个的;三方的;三部分的 Triple means consisting of three things or parts. 三个的;三方的;三部分的

  • ...a triple somersault.

    空翻三周

  • In 1882 Germany, Austria, and Italy formed the Triple Alliance.

    1882 年，德国、奥地利和意大利结成了三国同盟。

SYN three-way, threefold, tripartite  
2 V-ERG (使)变成三倍;(使)增至三倍 If something triples or if you triple it, it becomes three times as large in size or number. (使)变成三倍;(使)增至三倍

  • I got a fantastic new job and my salary tripled. [VERB]

    我得到了一份非常好的新工作，薪水涨到了原来的 3 倍。

  • The Exhibition has tripled in size from last year. [VERB + in]

    展览会规模自去年已经扩至3倍。

  • The merger puts the firm in a position to triple its earnings. [VERB noun]

    合并可使公司利润增长两倍。

SYN treble, triplicate, increase threefold  
3 PREDET 是…的三倍 If something is triple the amount or size of another thing, it is three times as large. 是…的三倍

  • The mine reportedly had an accident rate triple the national average.

    据报道,该矿事故发生率是全国平均水平的 3 倍。

  • The kitchen is triple the size it once was.

    厨房面积扩为原先的 3 倍。
