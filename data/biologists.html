biology
baɪɒlədʒi baɪˈɑlədʒi
Word forms: plural biologists
1 N-UNCOUNT 生物学 Biology is the science which is concerned with the study of living things. 生物学
biologist

  • ...biologists studying the fruit fly.

    研究果蝇的生物学家

2 N-UNCOUNT （生物的）生命机理 The biology of a living thing is the way in which its body or cells behave. （生物的）生命机理

  • The biology of these diseases is terribly complicated.

    这些疾病的机理极其复杂。

  • ...human biology.

    人体生命机理
