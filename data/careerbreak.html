career break kəˈrɪə breɪk
Word forms: plural career breaks
N-COUNT 事业中断;离职 If someone takes a career break, they stop working in their particular profession for a period of time, with the intention of returning to it later. 事业中断;离职 [business]

  • Many women still take career breaks to bring up children.

    仍然有许多女性为了抚养孩子而离职。
