blab
blæb blæb
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense blabs, present participle blabbing, past tense , past participle blabbed
VERB 泄露（秘密） If someone blabs about something secret, they tell people about it. 泄露（秘密） [informal] [Also VERB]

  • Her mistake was to blab about their affair. [VERB + about]

    她错在说出了他们的婚外情。

  • No blabbing to your mates!. [VERB + to]

    不要告诉你的同伴！

  • She'll blab it all over the school. [VERB noun prep.]

    她会满学校乱说。

SYN tell, reveal, disclose, divulge
