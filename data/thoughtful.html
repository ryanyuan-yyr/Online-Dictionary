thoughtful
θɔːtfʊl ˈθɔtfəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 沉思的;默想的;认真思考的 If you are thoughtful, you are quiet and serious because you are thinking about something. 沉思的;默想的;认真思考的

  • Nancy, who had been thoughtful for some time, suddenly spoke.

    南希沉思了一会儿，突然开口说话了。

  • He was looking very thoughtful.

    他似乎在静静思考着什么。

  • She had a thoughtful expression on her face.

    她一脸若有所思的表情。

SYN reflective, pensive, contemplative, meditative  
thoughtfully

  • Daniel nodded thoughtfully.

    丹尼尔若有所思地点了点头。

2 ADJ-GRADED 体贴入微的;考虑周到的 If you describe someone as thoughtful, you approve of them because they remember what other people want, need, or feel, and try not to upset them. 体贴入微的;考虑周到的 [approval]

  • ...a thoughtful and caring man.

    体贴入微、关心他人的男人

  • Thank you. That's very thoughtful of you. [+ of]

    谢谢你，你想的真周到。

  • It was a very kind and thoughtful gesture.

    这真是十分友好体贴的举动。

SYN considerate, kind, caring, kindly  
thoughtfully

  • ...the bottle of wine he had thoughtfully purchased for the celebrations.

    他特地为庆祝活动买的那瓶葡萄酒

thoughtfulness

  • I can't tell you how much I appreciate your thoughtfulness.

    非常感谢您的体贴入微，我真的无以言表。

3 ADJ-GRADED (书籍、电影、讲话等)严肃的，思想性强的 If you describe something such as a book, film, or speech as thoughtful, you mean that it is serious and well thought out. (书籍、电影、讲话等)严肃的，思想性强的

  • ...a thoughtful and scholarly book.

    一本兼具思想性和学术性的书

SYN profound, serious, deep, intelligent  
thoughtfully

  • ...these thoughtfully designed machines.

    这些精心设计的机器
