rummage
rʌmɪdʒ ˈrʌmɪdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rummages, present participle rummaging, past tense , past participle rummaged
1 VERB 搜寻;翻找;乱翻 If you rummage through something, you search for something you want by moving things around in a careless or hurried way. 搜寻;翻找;乱翻 [Also VERB]

  • They rummage through piles of second-hand clothes for something that fits. [VERB prep.]

    他们在一堆堆的二手衣服里翻找着合身的衣服。

  • Marianne went to rummage in the refrigerator. [VERB prep.]

    玛丽安娜在冰箱里翻了翻。

SYN search, hunt, root, explore  
Rummage is also a noun.

  • A brief rummage will provide several pairs of gloves.

    稍微翻翻，就能找到几双手套。

  • ...a rummage through his wardrobe for some tennis whites.

    在他的衣柜里翻找白色网球服

Rummage about and rummage around mean the same as rummage .rummage about 和 rummage around 同 rummage

  • I opened the fridge and rummaged about.

    我打开冰箱，到处乱翻。

  • He rummaged around the post room and found the document.

    他翻遍了收发室，找到了那份文件。

2 N-UNCOUNT 捐赠品;捐赠物 Rummage is old or unwanted things that people give away to charities. 捐赠品;捐赠物 [US]

  • ...loads of pitiful rummage.

    大量慈善捐赠品
