fashion
fæʃən ˈfæʃən
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense fashions, present participle fashioning, past tense , past participle fashioned
1 N-UNCOUNT 时装领域;时尚界 Fashion is the area of activity that involves styles of clothing and appearance. 时装领域;时尚界

  • There are 20 full-colour pages of fashion for men.

    书中有 20 张有关男性时尚的彩色插页。

  • The fashion world does not mind what the real world thinks.

    时尚界并不在意现实世界的想法。

2 N-COUNT 时装;时尚 A fashion is a style of clothing or a way of behaving that is popular at a particular time. 时装;时尚

  • In the early seventies I wore false eyelashes, as was the fashion.

    70 年代初我戴假睫毛，这在当时是时尚。

  • Queen Mary started the fashion for blue and white china in England. [+ for]

    玛丽女王开启了青花瓷在英格兰的流行风尚。

  • He stayed at the top through all changes and fashions in pop music. [+ in]

    在流行音乐的各种变化和各式潮流中他一直处于前沿。

SYN style, look, trend, rage  
3 N-SING 以…方式;以…方法 If you do something in a particular fashion or after a particular fashion, you do it in that way. 以…方式;以…方法

  • There is another drug called DHE that works in a similar fashion.

    另外还有一种叫 DHE 的药物，药效也差不多。

  • It is happening in this fashion because of the obstinacy of one woman.

    事情会如此发展是因为一个女人的固执己见。

4See also:parrot-fashion 
5 VERB 制作;制成 If you fashion an object or a work of art, you make it. 制作;制成 [formal]

  • Stone Age settlers fashioned necklaces from sheep's teeth. [VERB noun]

    石器时代的移居者用羊的牙做成项链。

6 See also: old-fashioned 
7 PHRASE 差强人意;马马虎虎 If you say that something was done after a fashion, you mean that it was done, but not very well. 差强人意;马马虎虎

  • She was educated — after a fashion — at home.

    她算得上是受过教育—家庭教育。

  • He knew the way, after a fashion.

    他勉强认路。

SYN to some extent, somehow, in a way, moderately  
8 PHRASE 时兴／过时 If something is in fashion, it is popular and approved of at a particular time. If it is out of fashion, it is not popular or approved of. 时兴／过时

  • That sort of house is back in fashion.

    那种房子又时兴起来了。

  • Marriage seems to be going out of fashion.

    婚姻似乎快要过时了。
