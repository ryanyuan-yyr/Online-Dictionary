bioengineering
baɪoʊendʒɪnɪərɪŋ ˌbaɪəʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ ˌbaɪoʊˌɛndʒəˈnɪrɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 遗传工程；基因工程 People sometimes use bioengineering to talk about genetic engineering. 遗传工程；基因工程
2 N-UNCOUNT 生物工程 Bioengineering is the use of engineering techniques to solve medical problems, for example to design and make artificial arms and legs. 生物工程
