staccato
stəkɑːtoʊ stəˈkɑtoʊ
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (噪音)断断续续的，短促不连贯的 A staccato noise consists of a series of short, sharp, separate sounds. (噪音)断断续续的，短促不连贯的

  • He spoke in Arabic, a short staccato burst.

    他突然用阿拉伯语叽里呱啦说了一串话。

  • ...the staccato chattering of several machine-guns.

    几架机关枪嗒嗒嗒的射击声

2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB after verb] (乐谱上)以断音，以断奏方式 The instruction staccato on a piece of music means that the notes should be played or sung very briefly with gaps between them. (乐谱上)以断音，以断奏方式
Staccato is also an adjective.

  • ...a rapid staccato passage.

    快速断奏乐段
