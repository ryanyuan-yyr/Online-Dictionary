unimpeded
ʌnɪmpiːdɪd ˌʌnɪmˈpiːdɪd
ADJ [ADJ after verb, ADJ n, verb-link ADJ] 未受阻止的；未受妨碍的 If something moves or happens unimpeded, it continues without being stopped or interrupted by anything. 未受阻止的；未受妨碍的 [formal]

  • We drove, unimpeded by anyone, to Arras.

    我们一路畅行至阿拉斯。

  • He promised to allow justice to run its course unimpeded.

    他承诺要让司法正义不受阻碍地得到伸张。

  • U.N. aid convoys have unimpeded access to the city.

    联合国救援车队可以畅通无阻地进入该市。

SYN unhindered, open, free, unchecked
