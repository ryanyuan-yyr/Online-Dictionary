awestruck
ɔːstrʌk ˈɔːˌstrʌk
(also awe-struck)
ADJ-GRADED 敬畏的；肃然起敬的 If someone is awestruck, they are very impressed and amazed by something. 敬畏的；肃然起敬的 [written]

  • I stood and gazed at him, awestruck that anyone could be so beautiful.

    我站在那里凝望着他，惊叹竟有这么美的人。

SYN impressed, shocked, amazed, stunned
