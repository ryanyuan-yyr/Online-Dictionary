bequest
bɪkwest bɪˈkwɛst
Word forms: plural bequests
N-COUNT 遗产 A bequest is money or property which you legally leave to someone when you die. 遗产

  • The church here was left a bequest to hire doctors who would work amongst the poor.

    这个教堂接收到一笔遗产，可以用来聘请医生为穷人治病。

SYN legacy, gift, settlement, heritage
