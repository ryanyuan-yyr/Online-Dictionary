valley
væli ˈvæli
Word forms: plural valleys
N-COUNT [oft in names] 山谷;（尤指）溪谷 A valley is a low stretch of land between hills, especially one that has a river flowing through it. 山谷;（尤指）溪谷

  • ...a wooded valley set against the backdrop of Monte Rosa.

    罗莎峰背后树木繁茂的山谷

  • ...the Loire valley.

    卢瓦尔山谷

SYN hollow, dale, glen, vale
