infidel
ɪnfɪdəl ˈɪnfəˌdɛl ˈɪnfədəl
Word forms: plural infidels
N-COUNT 无宗教信仰者;异教徒 If one person refers to another as an infidel, the first person is hostile towards the second person because that person has a different religion or has no religion. 无宗教信仰者;异教徒 [literary, disapproval]

  • ...a holy war, to drive the infidels and the non-believers out of this holy land.

    一场圣战，为的是把异教徒和不信仰上帝的人赶出这片圣地

SYN unbeliever, sceptic, atheist, heretic  
Infidel is also an adjective.

  • He promised to continue the fight against infidel forces.

    他许诺继续对抗异教势力。
