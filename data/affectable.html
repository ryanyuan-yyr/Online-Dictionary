affect
əfekt æˈfɛkt əˈfɛkt ˈæfˌɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense affects, present participle affecting, past tense , past participle affected
1 VERB 影响;使改变 If something affects a person or thing, it influences them or causes them to change in some way. 影响;使改变

  • Nicotine adversely affects the functioning of the heart and arteries. [VERB noun]

    尼古丁会对心脏及动脉功能造成损害。

  • More than seven million people have been affected by drought. [VERB noun]

    已有超过700万的民众受到旱灾的影响。

  • ...the worst-affected areas of Somalia. [VERB-ed]

    索马里受影响最为严重的地区

SYN influence, involve, concern, impact  
2 VERB （疾病）侵袭，感染 If a disease affects someone, it causes them to become ill. （疾病）侵袭，感染

  • Arthritis is a crippling disease which affects people all over the world. [VERB noun]

    关节炎是一种会危害全人类健康的致残性疾病。

3 VERB （感情上）打动，震动，使悲伤（或怜悯） If something or someone affects you, they make you feel a strong emotion, especially sadness or pity. （感情上）打动，震动，使悲伤（或怜悯）

  • The divorce affected Jim deeply. [VERB noun]

    离婚对吉姆打击很大。

  • Gregor loved his sister, and her loss clearly still affects him. [VERB noun]

    格雷戈尔爱他的姐姐，她的去世显然仍令他悲伤不已。

SYN emotionally move, touch, upset, overcome  
4 VERB 假装；佯装；装出…的样子 If you affect a particular characteristic or way of behaving, you pretend that it is genuine, or natural for you. 假装；佯装；装出…的样子 [literary] [Also VERB to-infinitive]

  • He listened to them, affecting an amused interest. [VERB noun]

    他装作饶有兴致地听他们说话。

  • Ms. Redgrave affects a heavy Italian accent. [VERB noun]

    雷德格雷夫女士假装带有浓重的意大利口音。

SYN put on, assume, adopt, pretend  
Usage Note :

  • Note that the noun that comes from affect is effect. You can say that something affects you, or that is has an effect on you. ...the effect that noise has on people in factories. You can also talk about the effect of something. ...the effect of the anaesthetic. Effect can also be a verb. If you effect something such as a change or a repair, you make it happen or do it. This is a fairly formal word. She had effected a few hasty repairs.

    注意，与affect相对应的名词是effect。可以说something affects you，也可以说it has an effect on you。例如：the effect that noise has on people in factories (噪音对工厂里的人们产生的影响）。谈论某事物的效果也可用 effect: the effect of the anaesthetic（麻醉药的作用）。effect也可用作动词，后接 change, repair 等词, 意为使发生或进行，该用法较为正式。例如：She had effected a few hasty repairs（她已经做了一些匆忙的修补）。
