stowaway
stoʊəweɪ ˈstoʊəˌweɪ
Word forms: plural stowaways
N-COUNT 无票偷乘者;偷渡者 A stowaway is a person who hides in a ship, aeroplane, or other vehicle in order to make a journey secretly or without paying. 无票偷乘者;偷渡者

  • The crew discovered the stowaway about two days into their voyage.

    船员在开船约两天后发现了那名偷乘者。
