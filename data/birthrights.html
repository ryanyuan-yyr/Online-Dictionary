birthright
bɜːʳθraɪt ˈbɜrθˌraɪt
Word forms: plural birthrights
N-COUNT [usually singular] 与生俱来的权利 Something that is your birthright is something that you feel you have a basic right to have, simply because you are a human being. 与生俱来的权利

  • Freedom is the natural birthright of every human.

    自由是每一个人与生俱来的权利。

SYN right, due, heritage, privilege
