bank manager
Word forms: plural bank managers
N-COUNT 银行经理；银行分行经理 A bank manager is someone who is in charge of a bank, or a particular branch of a bank, and who is involved in making decisions about whether or not to lend money to businesses and individuals. 银行经理；银行分行经理 [business]
