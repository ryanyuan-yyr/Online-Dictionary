lukewarm
luːkwɔːm ˌluːkˈwɔːm ˈlukˌwɔrm
1 ADJ-GRADED （尤指液体）微温的，微热的，不冷不热的 Something, especially a liquid, that is lukewarm is only slightly warm. （尤指液体）微温的，微热的，不冷不热的

  • Wash your face with lukewarm water.

    用温水洗脸。

  • The coffee was weak and lukewarm.

    咖啡寡淡无味，温乎乎的。

SYN tepid, warm, blood-warm  
2 ADJ-GRADED 缺乏兴趣的；不热情的；冷淡的 If you describe a person or their attitude as lukewarm, you mean that they are not showing much enthusiasm or interest. 缺乏兴趣的；不热情的；冷淡的

  • Economists have never been more than lukewarm towards him.

    经济学家对他向来态度冷淡。

  • The study received a lukewarm response from the Home Secretary.

    内政大臣对这项研究反应不是很热烈。

SYN half-hearted, cold, cool, indifferent
