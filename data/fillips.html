fillip
fɪlɪp ˈfɪlɪp
Word forms: plural fillips
N-COUNT [usually singular] (突然的)刺激,激励 If someone or something gives a fillip to an activity or person, they suddenly encourage or improve them. (突然的)刺激,激励 [written]

  • The news gave a fillip to the telecommunications sector. [+ to/for]

    这个消息给电信部门注入了一针强心剂。

  • This will prove a much-needed fillip to racecourses and the racing industry in general.

    这将会证明是赛马场和整个赛马业迫切需要的激励。

SYN boost, push, spur, spice
