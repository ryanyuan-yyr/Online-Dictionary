entrant
entrənt ˈɛntrənt
Word forms: plural entrants
1 N-COUNT 新加入者；新成员；新生 An entrant is a person who has recently become a member of an institution such as a university. 新加入者；新成员；新生

  • ...a young school entrant.

    年轻的新生

2 N-COUNT 参赛者 An entrant is a person who is taking part in a competition. 参赛者

  • All items entered for the competition must be the entrant's own work.

    所有参赛作品必须是参赛者本人的作品。

SYN competitor, player, candidate, entry
