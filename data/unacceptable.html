unacceptable
ʌnəkseptəbəl ˌʌnəkˈsɛptəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 难以接受的；不能容忍的 If you describe something as unacceptable, you strongly disapprove of it or object to it and feel that it should not be allowed to continue. 难以接受的；不能容忍的

  • It is totally unacceptable for children to swear. [+ for]

    孩子说脏话是完全不能容忍的。

  • Joanna left her husband because of his unacceptable behaviour.

    乔安娜因其丈夫令人难以容忍的行为而离开了他。

SYN intolerable, unsatisfactory, unreasonable, off [British , informal]  
unacceptably

  • The reform program has brought unacceptably high unemployment and falling wages.

    改革计划造成了令人无法接受的高失业率和工资水平下降。
