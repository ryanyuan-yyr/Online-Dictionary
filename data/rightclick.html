right-click
ˌraɪtˈklɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense right-clicks, present participle right-clicking, past tense , past participle right-clicked
VERB 右击;点击鼠标右键 To right-click or to right-click on something means to press the right-hand button on a computer mouse. 右击;点击鼠标右键 [computing]

  • All you have to do is right-click on the desktop and select New Folder. [VERB + on]

    你只要在桌面上点击鼠标右键，选择“新建文件夹”就行了。
