disinterest
dɪsɪntrəst dɪsˈɪntrɪst dɪsˈɪntrəst dɪsˈɪntərəst
N-UNCOUNT 无兴趣;不关心;冷漠 If there is disinterest in something, people are not interested in it. 无兴趣;不关心;冷漠

  • The fact Liberia has no oil seems to explain foreign disinterest in its internal affairs. [+ in]

    利比里亚没有石油，这一事实似乎解释了为什么外部势力无意过问其内政。

  • We have had to contend with the disinterest of much of the medical profession about this topic.

    我们必须全力对付很多医疗界人士对这一话题的冷漠。
