undercurrent
ʌndəʳkʌrənt -kɜːr- ˈʌndərˌkɜrənt
Word forms: plural undercurrents
1 N-COUNT (情感的)潜流，暗流 If there is an undercurrent of a feeling, you are hardly aware of the feeling, but it influences the way you think or behave. (情感的)潜流，暗流

  • ...a deep undercurrent of racism in British society. [+ of]

    英国社会中强劲的种族主义暗流

SYN undertone, feeling, atmosphere, sense  
2 N-COUNT (水流的)潜流；暗流 An undercurrent is a strong current of water that is moving below the surface current and in a different direction to it. (水流的)潜流；暗流

  • Colin tried to swim after him but the strong undercurrent swept them apart.

    科林想跟在他后面游，但强大的暗流把他们分开了。

SYN undertow, tideway, riptide, rip
