exterior
ɪkstɪəriəʳ ɪkˈstɪərɪə ɛkˈstɪriər ɪkˈstɪriər
Word forms: plural exteriors
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 外观;外部 The exterior of something is its outside surface. 外观;外部

  • In one ad the viewer scarcely sees the car's exterior.

    在一则广告中，观众几乎看不到汽车的外观。

  • The exterior of the building was elegant and graceful. [+ of]

    这座大厦外观雅致而优美。

SYN outside, face, surface, covering  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] [oft poss NOUN] （尤指与实际性格相差甚远的）外表，外貌，外在 You can refer to someone's usual appearance or behaviour as their exterior, especially when it is very different from their real character. （尤指与实际性格相差甚远的）外表，外貌，外在

  • According to Mandy, Pat's tough exterior hides a shy and sensitive soul.

    据曼迪说，帕特彪悍的外表下隐藏着一颗害羞且敏感的心。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 外面的;外部的 You use exterior to refer to the outside parts of something or things that are outside something. 外面的;外部的

  • The exterior walls were made of pre-formed concrete.

    外墙用的是预制混凝土。

  • ...the oven's exterior surfaces.

    烤箱的外表面

SYN outer, outside, external, surface
