tear apart
1 PHRASAL VERB 使起内讧;使分裂;离间 If something tears people apart, it causes them to quarrel or to leave each other. 使起内讧;使分裂;离间

  • The quarrel tore the party apart. [VERB noun PREP.]

    这次争吵导致该党出现分裂。

  • War and revolution have torn families apart. [VERB noun PREP.]

    战争和革命导致许多家庭妻离子散。

2 PHRASAL VERB 使痛苦;使担忧;折磨 If something tears you apart, it makes you feel very upset, worried, and unhappy. 使痛苦;使担忧;折磨

  • Don't think it hasn't torn me apart to be away from you. [VERB noun PREP.]

    不骗你，离开你让我痛苦万分。
