planning
plænɪŋ ˈplænɪŋ
1 N-UNCOUNT 计划；规划；设计 Planning is the process of deciding in detail how to do something before you actually start to do it. 计划；规划；设计

  • The trip needs careful planning.

    这趟行程需要周密计划。

  • The new system is still in the planning stages.

    新体系仍处于规划阶段。

SYN arrangement, organization, setting up, working out  
2See also:family planning 
3 N-UNCOUNT 城镇规划 Planning is control by the local government of the way that land is used in an area and of what new buildings are built there. 城镇规划

  • ...a masterpiece of 18th-century town planning.

    18世纪城镇规划的杰作

  • He supported the planning authority in refusing the application because of pressures on the Green Belt.

    他支持城镇规划部门拒绝该项申请，因为这会给绿化带带来损害。
