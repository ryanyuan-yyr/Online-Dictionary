anytime
enitaɪm ˌɛnɪˈtaɪm ˈɛniˌtaɪm
ADV-INDEF [ADVERB with verb] 在任何时候 You use anytime to mean a point in time which is not fixed or set. 在任何时候

  • The college admits students anytime during the year.

    该学院一年中随时招生。

  • He can leave anytime he wants.

    他想走的话随时可以走。

  • He can call me anytime.

    他随时可以给我打电话。
