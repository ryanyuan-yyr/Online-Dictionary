effrontery
ɪfrʌntəri ɛˈfrʌntəri ɪˈfrʌntəri
N-UNCOUNT 厚颜无耻;放肆;粗鲁 Effrontery is behaviour that is bold, rude, or disrespectful. 厚颜无耻;放肆;粗鲁 [formal, disapproval]

  • One could only gasp at the sheer effrontery of the man. [+ of]

    那人十足的厚颜无耻让人们吃惊得无话可说。

SYN insolence, nerve [informal] , arrogance, presumption
