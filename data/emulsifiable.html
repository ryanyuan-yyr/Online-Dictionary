emulsify
ɪmʌlsɪfaɪ iˈmʌlsəˌfaɪ ɪˈmʌlsəˌfaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense emulsifies, present participle emulsifying, past tense , past participle emulsified
V-ERG （使）乳化 When two liquids of different thicknesses emulsify or when they are emulsified, they combine. （使）乳化 [technical]

  • It is the pressure which releases the coffee oils; these emulsify and give the coffee its rich, velvety texture. [VERB]

    压力将咖啡的油脂挤出，这些油脂经乳化带给咖啡一种浓郁、柔滑的口感。

  • Whisk the cream into the mixture to emulsify it. [VERB noun]

    将奶油搅打到混合物中，使其乳化。

  • Beeswax acts as an emulsifying agent. [VERB-ing]

    蜂蜡起到乳化剂的作用。

  • ...emulsified oil. [VERB-ed]

    乳化油
