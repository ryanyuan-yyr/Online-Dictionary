ill-treat
ˈɪlˈtrit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ill-treats, present participle ill-treating, past tense , past participle ill-treated
VERB 虐待;折磨 If someone ill-treats you, they treat you badly or cruelly. 虐待;折磨

  • They thought Mr Smith had been ill-treating his wife. [VERB noun]

    他们认为史密斯先生一直在虐待他的妻子。

  • They said they had not been ill-treated but that their time in captivity had been miserable. [VERB noun]

    他们说他们没有遭受虐待,但是被囚禁的日子令人痛苦。

SYN abuse, injure, harm, wrong
