containment
kənteɪnmənt kənˈteɪnmənt
1 N-UNCOUNT (对他国力量的)遏制 Containment is the action or policy of keeping another country's power or area of control within acceptable limits or boundaries. (对他国力量的)遏制
2 N-UNCOUNT 控制;抑制 The containment of something dangerous or unpleasant is the act or process of keeping it under control within a particular area or place. 控制;抑制

  • Fire crews are hoping they can achieve full containment of the fire before the winds pick up. [+ of]

    消防队员希望趁着风还没有起来将火势完全控制住。

  • ...containment of the disease.

    对于该病的控制

  • ...a national health board to oversee cost containment and health-care reform.

    监督费用控制与医疗改革的国家医疗委员会
