communion
kəmjuːnjən kəˈmjuːnjən kəˈmjunjən
Word forms: plural communions
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] (思想或情感的)交流，交融 Communion with nature or with a person is the feeling that you are sharing thoughts or feelings with them. (思想或情感的)交流，交融

  • ...communion with nature. [+ with]

    与大自然的交融

  • ...a communion of souls.

    心灵之交

2 N-UNCOUNT (基督教的)圣餐仪式 Communion is the Christian ceremony in which people eat bread and drink wine in memory of Christ's death. (基督教的)圣餐仪式

  • Most villagers took communion only at Easter.

    大部分村民只在复活节的时候领受圣餐。

  • ...the Communion service.

    圣餐仪式
