paradoxical
pærədɒksɪkəl ˌpærəˈdɑksɪkəl
ADJ-GRADED 自相矛盾的；似矛盾而（可能） 正确的 If something is paradoxical, it involves two facts or qualities which seem to contradict each other. 自相矛盾的；似矛盾而（可能） 正确的

  • Some sedatives produce the paradoxical effect of making the person more anxious.

    一些镇静剂适得其反，加重了患者的焦虑。

  • We were a team of individuals — as paradoxical as that sounds.

    我们是一个保持了个体的团队—尽管这听起来自相矛盾。

SYN contradictory, inconsistent, incongruous, ironic  
paradoxically

  • Paradoxically, the less you have to do the more you may resent the work that does come your way.

    矛盾的是，你手头的事越少，一旦有工作落在头上，你就越会愤愤不平。

  • ...a growing up that paradoxically involves remaining a child, too.

    一方面在成长、一方面还是个孩子的矛盾
