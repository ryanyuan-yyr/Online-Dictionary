mane
meɪn meɪn
Word forms: plural manes
1 N-COUNT (马或狮子的)鬃毛 The mane on a horse or lion is the long thick hair that grows from its neck. (马或狮子的)鬃毛
2 N-COUNT 浓密的头发 If you refer to a person's hair as their mane, you mean that they have a lot of hair. 浓密的头发 [literary]

  • He had a great mane of white hair.

    他满头浓密的白发。
