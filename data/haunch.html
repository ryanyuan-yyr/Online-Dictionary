haunch
hɔːntʃ hɔntʃ hɑntʃ
Word forms: plural haunches
1 PHRASE 以蹲姿 If you get down on your haunches, you lower yourself towards the ground so that your legs are bent under you and you are balancing on your feet. 以蹲姿

  • Edgar squatted on his haunches.

    埃德加蹲着。

  • Ferris was crouched down on his haunches.

    费里斯蹲伏着。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] （动物的）腰腿;（人的）臀胯部 The haunches of an animal or person are the area of the body which includes the bottom, the hips, and the tops of the legs. （动物的）腰腿;（人的）臀胯部
