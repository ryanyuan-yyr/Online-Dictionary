messenger
mesɪndʒəʳ ˈmɛsəndʒər
Word forms: plural messengers
N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 送信者;通信员;信使 A messenger takes a message to someone, or takes messages regularly as their job. 送信者;通信员;信使

  • There will be a messenger at the Airport to collect the photographs from our courier.

    机场会有一名信使从我们的递送员手中取走照片。

  • He gave the instruction for the document to be sent by messenger.

    他下令由通信员送出文件。

SYN courier, agent, runner, carrier
