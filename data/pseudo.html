pseudo- sjuːdoʊ- suːdoʊ- ˈsudoʊ ˈsudə ˈsjudoʊ
PREFIX （用于构成形容词和名词）表示“假”，“伪”，“冒牌” Pseudo- is used to form adjectives and nouns that indicate that something is not the thing it is claimed to be. For example, if you describe a country as a pseudo-democracy, you mean that it is not really a democracy, although its government claims that it is. （用于构成形容词和名词）表示“假”，“伪”，“冒牌”

  • ...pseudo-intellectual images.

    伪知识分子的形象
