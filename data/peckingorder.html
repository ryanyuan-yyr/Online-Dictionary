pecking order
Word forms: plural pecking orders
N-COUNT [usually singular] （群体中的）尊卑秩序，权势等级 The pecking order of a group is the way that the positions people have are arranged according to their status or power within the group. （群体中的）尊卑秩序，权势等级

  • He knew his place in the pecking order.

    他知道自己排老几。

  • They both came from families fairly far down the social pecking order.

    他们两个都来自社会最底层的家庭。
