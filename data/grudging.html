grudging
grʌdʒɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 勉强的；不情愿的 A grudging feeling or action is felt or done very unwillingly. 勉强的；不情愿的

  • He even earned his opponents' grudging respect.

    他甚至令对手也不得不尊重他。

  • There seems to be a grudging acceptance of the situation.

    这一局面看来已经被勉强接受。

SYN reluctant, unwilling, hesitant, unenthusiastic  
grudgingly

  • The film studio grudgingly agreed to allow him to continue working.

    这家电影公司勉强同意让他继续工作。
