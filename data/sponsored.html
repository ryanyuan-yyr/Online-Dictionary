sponsored
spɒnsəʳd ˈspɒnsəd
ADJ [ADJ n] (活动或比赛)为慈善筹款而举办的 In Britain, a sponsored event is an event in which people try to do something such as walk or run a particular distance in order to raise money for charity. (活动或比赛)为慈善筹款而举办的

  • The sponsored walk will raise money for AIDS care.

    本次慈善步行活动将为艾滋病人筹款。
