well-balanced ˈwɛlˈbælənst
(also well balanced)
1 ADJ-GRADED 通情达理的;头脑清醒的;情绪稳定的 If you describe someone as well-balanced, you mean that they are sensible and do not have many emotional problems. 通情达理的;头脑清醒的;情绪稳定的

  • ...a fun-loving, well-balanced individual.

    喜欢玩乐、通情达理的人

SYN sensible, rational, level-headed, well-adjusted  
2 ADJ-GRADED 均衡的;均匀的 If you describe something that is made up of several parts as well-balanced, you mean that the way that the different parts are put together is good, because there is not too much or too little of any one part. 均衡的;均匀的

  • ...a well balanced diet.

    均衡的膳食

SYN healthy, balanced, mixed, varied
