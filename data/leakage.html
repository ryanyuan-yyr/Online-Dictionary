leakage
liːkɪdʒ ˈlikɪdʒ
Word forms: plural leakages
N-VAR 漏出物（或量）；渗漏物（或量） A leakage is an amount of liquid or gas that is escaping from a pipe or container by means of a crack, hole, or other fault. 漏出物（或量）；渗漏物（或量）

  • A leakage of kerosene has polluted water supplies.

    泄漏的煤油污染了水源。

  • It should be possible to reduce leakage from pipes. [+ from]

    应该可以减少管道的渗漏量。
