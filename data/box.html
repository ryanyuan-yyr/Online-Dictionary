box
bɒks bɑks
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense boxes, present participle boxing, past tense , past participle boxed
1 N-COUNT 盒子；箱子；匣子 A box is a square or rectangular container with hard or stiff sides. Boxes often have lids. 盒子；箱子；匣子

  • He reached into the cardboard box beside him.

    他把手伸进身边的纸板盒里。

  • They sat on wooden boxes.

    他们坐在木箱子上。

  • ...the box of tissues on her desk.

    她桌子上的纸巾盒

SYN container, case, chest, trunk  
A box of something is an amount of it contained in a box.一盒之量；一箱之量；一匣之量

  • She ate two boxes of liqueurs.

    她吃了两盒酒心巧克力。

2 N-COUNT （在纸张、道路等上印制或画出的）方格，方框 A box is a square or rectangle that is printed or drawn on a piece of paper, a road, or on some other surface. （在纸张、道路等上印制或画出的）方格，方框
3 N-SING （足球场的）罚球区 In football, the box is the penalty area of the field. （足球场的）罚球区

  • He scored from the penalty spot after being brought down in the box.

    在罚球区内被违规撞倒后，他罚点球破门得分。

4 N-COUNT （剧院或运动场的）包厢 A box is a small separate area in a theatre or at a sports ground or stadium, where a small number of people can sit to watch the performance or game. （剧院或运动场的）包厢
5 N-SING 电视机 Television is sometimes referred to as the box . 电视机 [British, informal]

  • Do you watch it live at all or do you watch it on the box?

    你是在现场看还是在电视上看？

6 N-COUNT （用在数字前表示邮政地址）邮政信箱，邮箱 Box is used before a number as a postal address by organizations that receive a lot of mail. （用在数字前表示邮政地址）邮政信箱，邮箱

  • ...Country Crafts, Box 111, Landisville.

    兰蒂斯维尔111号信箱，乡村工艺社

7 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 黄杨（小型长绿树，常用作篱笆） Box is a small evergreen tree with dark leaves which is often used to form hedges. 黄杨（小型长绿树，常用作篱笆）

  • ...box hedges.

    黄杨篱笆

8 VERB （按照拳击运动的规则）击打 To box means to fight someone according to the rules of boxing. （按照拳击运动的规则）击打

  • At school I boxed and played rugby. [VERB]

    在学校时我既打拳击又玩橄榄球。

  • The two fighters had previously boxed a 12-round match. [VERB noun]

    这两位拳击手之前已经打了一场12回合的比赛。

SYN fight, spar, exchange blows, prizefight
