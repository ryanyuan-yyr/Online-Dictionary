powder keg
(also powder-keg)Word forms: plural powder kegs
N-COUNT 火药桶（指形势危急的地区或情况） If you describe a situation or a place as a powder keg, you mean that it could easily become very dangerous. 火药桶（指形势危急的地区或情况）
