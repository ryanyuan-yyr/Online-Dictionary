commando
kəmɑːndoʊ -mænd- kəˈmændoʊ kəˈmɑndoʊ
Word forms: plural commandos, plural commandoes
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 突击队 A commando is a group of soldiers who have been specially trained to carry out surprise attacks. 突击队

  • ...a small commando of marines. [+ of]

    海军陆战队突击小组

  • The hostages were freed in the commando raid.

    在突击队的突袭行动中，人质获释。

2 N-COUNT 突击队员 A commando is a soldier who is a member of a commando. 突击队员

  • Captain David Clement and 150 commandos stormed the port this morning.

    戴维·克莱门特上尉和150名突击队员今天早上对港口展开突袭。
