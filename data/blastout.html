blast out
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （音乐或噪音）震天响，轰响 If music or noise is blasting out, loud music or noise is being produced. （音乐或噪音）震天响，轰响

  • ...loudspeakers blasting out essential tourist facts in every language known to man. [VERB PREP. noun]

    喇叭里用人们知道的每一种语言大声播放着基本旅游信息

  • Pop music can be heard 10 miles away blasting out from the huge tented shanty-town. [VERB PREP.]

    响亮的流行音乐从10英里之外巨大的搭满帐篷的棚户区传来。

SYN blast, scream, boom, roar
