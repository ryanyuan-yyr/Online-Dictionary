predestination
priːdestɪneɪʃən priːdest- priˌdɛstəˈneɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 宿命论；命定说 If you believe in predestination, you believe that people have no control over events because everything has already been decided by a power such as God or fate. 宿命论；命定说
SYN fate, destiny, predetermination, election [theology]
