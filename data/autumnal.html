autumnal
ɔːtʌmnəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 秋天的 Autumnal means having features that are characteristic of autumn. 秋天的 [literary]

  • ...the autumnal colours of the trees.

    秋天树木的颜色

  • We used shades of gold and green to give the room a fresh, autumnal look.

    我们用金色和绿色的色调为房间平添了一种清爽的秋日感觉。

2 ADJ 在秋天的；秋季里的 Autumnal means happening in autumn. 在秋天的；秋季里的

  • ...the autumnal equinox.

    秋分

  • ...autumnal gales.

    秋风
