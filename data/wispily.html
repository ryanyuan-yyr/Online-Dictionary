wispy
wɪspi
1 ADJ-GRADED (头发)稀疏的，稀稀拉拉的 If someone has wispy hair, their hair does not grow thickly on their head. (头发)稀疏的，稀稀拉拉的

  • Grey wispy hair straggled down to her shoulders.

    稀疏的灰白头发披散在她肩头。

SYN straggly, fine, thin, frail  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (云)一缕缕的，虚无缥缈的 A wispy cloud is thin or faint. (云)一缕缕的，虚无缥缈的

  • The half moon is hidden behind some wispy clouds.

    半轮月亮躲在淡淡的云彩之后。
