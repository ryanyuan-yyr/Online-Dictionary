soul-searching ˈsɑtˌsɜrtʃɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT 自我剖析；深刻反省 Soul-searching is a long and careful examination of your thoughts and feelings, especially when you are trying to make a difficult moral decision or thinking about something that has gone wrong. 自我剖析；深刻反省

  • My year was really spent doing a lot of soul-searching and trying to find out what had gone wrong in my life.

    这一年来，我真的进行了许多深刻的反思，努力地找出自己的生活中到底哪里出了问题。
