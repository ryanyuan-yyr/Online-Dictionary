easterly
iːstəʳli ˈistərli
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 东的;向东的 An easterly point, area, or direction is to the east or towards the east. 东的;向东的

  • He progressed slowly along the coast in an easterly direction.

    他沿着海岸慢慢向东前行。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （风）从东方吹来的；东风的 An easterly wind is a wind that blows from the east. （风）从东方吹来的；东风的

  • ...the cold easterly winds from Scandinavia.

    来自斯堪的纳维亚的寒冷东风
