lash out
1 PHRASAL VERB （用武器或手脚）迅猛攻击，殴打 If you lash out, you attempt to hit someone quickly and violently with a weapon or with your hands or feet. （用武器或手脚）迅猛攻击，殴打

  • Riot police fired in the air and lashed out with clubs to disperse hundreds of demonstrators. [VERB PREP.]

    防暴警察朝天开枪并挥舞警棍驱散数百名示威者。

  • Her husband has a terrible temper and lashes out at her when he's angry. [VERB PREP. + at]

    她丈夫脾气极差，一生气就殴打她。

2 PHRASAL VERB 猛烈抨击；批评 If you lash out at someone or something, you speak to them or about them very angrily or critically. 猛烈抨击；批评 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • As a politician Jefferson frequently lashed out at the press. [V P + at/against]

    作为一个政治人物，杰斐逊常常抨击新闻界。

  • The Cuban leader lashed out against the policy of the US President.

    古巴领导人猛烈抨击美国总统的政策。
