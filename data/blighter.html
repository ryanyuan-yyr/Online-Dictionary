blighter
blaɪtəʳ ˈblaɪtər
Word forms: plural blighters
1 N-COUNT 可恶的家伙；讨厌鬼 You can refer to someone you do not like as a blighter . 可恶的家伙；讨厌鬼 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • He was a nasty little blighter.

    他是一个让人讨厌的小东西。

2 N-COUNT 家伙 You can use blighter as an informal way of referring to someone. 家伙 [British]

  • Lucky blighter, thought King.

    幸运的家伙，金想。
