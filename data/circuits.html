circuit
sɜːʳkɪt ˈsɜrkɪt
Word forms: plural circuits
1 N-COUNT 电路;(电流的)回路 An electrical circuit is a complete route which an electric current can flow around. 电路;(电流的)回路

  • Any attempts to cut through the cabling will break the electrical circuit.

    只要一割断电缆，电路就中断。

2See also:closed-circuit  short-circuit 
3 N-COUNT （尤指工作时的）巡回线路，巡回，巡游 A circuit is a series of places that are visited regularly by a person or group, especially as a part of their job. （尤指工作时的）巡回线路，巡回，巡游

  • He joined the professional circuit.

    他参加了职业巡回赛。

  • It's a common problem, the one I'm asked about most when I'm on the lecture circuit.

    这问题很常见，我在巡回讲演时被问得最多的就是这个问题。

SYN course, round, tour, track  
4 N-COUNT 赛车道 A racing circuit is a track on which cars, motorbikes, or cycles race. 赛车道 [mainly British]
5 N-COUNT (绕着某地的)环行，环游 A circuit of a place or area is a journey all the way round it. (绕着某地的)环行，环游 [formal]

  • She made a slow circuit of the room. [+ of]

    她绕着房间缓缓地走了一圈。

SYN lap, round, tour, revolution
