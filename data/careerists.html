careerist
kərɪərɪst kəˈrɪrɪst
Word forms: plural careerists
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 事业心极强的；有抱负的 Careerist people are ambitious and think that their career is more important than anything else. 事业心极强的；有抱负的

  • ...careerist politicians.

    野心勃勃的政客

Careerist is also a noun.

  • ...a single-minded careerist with few friends.

    一心只想着事业、朋友寥寥无几的工作狂
