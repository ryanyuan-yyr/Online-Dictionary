go down
1 PHRASAL VERB （价格、水平或数量）下降，下跌 If a price, level, or amount goes down, it becomes lower or less than it was. （价格、水平或数量）下降，下跌

  • Income from sales tax went down. [VERB PREP.]

    来自销售税的收入下降了。

  • Crime has gone down 70 percent. [V P amount]

    犯罪率下降了70%。

  • Average life expectancy went down from about 70 to 67. [V P + from/to/by]

    平均预期寿命从70岁左右降到了67岁。

SYN fall, drop, decline, slump  
2 PHRASAL VERB 跪下；趴下 If you go down on your knees or on all fours, you lower your body until it is supported by your knees, or by your hands and knees. 跪下；趴下

  • I went down on my knees and prayed for guidance. [VERB PREP. + on]

    我跪了下来，祈求得到指引。

3 PHRASAL VERB （体育比赛中）被打败 In sport, if a person or team goes down, they are defeated in a match or contest. （体育比赛中）被打败 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • They went down 2-1 to Australia. [V P num]

    他们以1比2输给了澳大利亚队。

SYN lose, be beaten, go under, suffer defeat  
4 PHRASAL VERB （运动队）降级 In sport, if a team goes down, they move to a lower division in a league. （运动队）降级 [British]

  • Only two go down at the end of this season. [VERB PREP.]

    本赛季末只有两支队伍降级。

5 PHRASAL VERB 引起…反响；得到…回应 If you say that a remark, idea, or type of behaviour goes down in a particular way, you mean that it gets a particular kind of reaction from a person or group of people. 引起…反响；得到…回应

  • Solicitors advised their clients that a tidy look went down well with the magistrates. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    律师告诫当事人穿戴整洁会赢得地方法官的好感。

6 PHRASAL VERB （太阳）落下 When the sun goes down, it goes below the horizon. （太阳）落下

  • ...the glow left in the sky after the sun has gone down. [VERB PREP.]

    天空中落日的余晖

SYN set, sink  
7 PHRASAL VERB （船只）沉没； （飞机）坠落 If a ship goes down, it sinks. If a plane goes down, it crashes out of the sky. （船只）沉没； （飞机）坠落

  • Their aircraft went down during a training exercise. [VERB PREP.]

    他们的飞机在一次训练中坠毁。

SYN sink, founder, go under, be submerged  
8 PHRASAL VERB （计算机）出故障 If a computer goes down, it stops functioning temporarily. （计算机）出故障

  • The main computers went down for 30 minutes. [VERB PREP.]

    这几台主要的电脑有30分钟没能正常运行。

9 PHRASAL VERB 发生 To go down means to happen. 发生 [informal]

  • 'What's going down? Any ideas?' [VERB PREP.]

    “发生什么事了？你知道吗？”
