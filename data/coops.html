coop
kuːp ˈkəʊˌɒp kup
Word forms: plural coops
1 N-COUNT (鸡、兔等小动物的)笼子;禽舍 A coop is a cage where you keep small animals or birds such as chickens and rabbits. (鸡、兔等小动物的)笼子;禽舍
SYN pen, pound, box, cage  
2 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 挣脱束缚;逃离 If you say that someone has flown the coop, you mean that they have left a place or situation that limits their freedom. 挣脱束缚;逃离

  • ...a family whose children have grown up and flown the coop.

    孩子们已经长大离家开始自己生活的家庭

  • She graduates to senior stylist and then flies the coop to set up in a salon of her own.

    她晋升为高级发型师，然后又冲破种种限制，开起了自己的发廊。
