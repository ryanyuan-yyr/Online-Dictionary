masochistic
mæsəkɪstɪk
1 ADJ 性受虐狂的 Masochistic behaviour involves a person getting sexual pleasure from their own pain or suffering. 性受虐狂的

  • ...his masochistic tendencies.

    他的性受虐狂的倾向

2 ADJ-GRADED 受虐狂的;自虐的;自讨苦吃的 If you describe someone's behaviour as masochistic, you mean that they seem to be trying to get into a situation which causes them suffering or great difficulty. 受虐狂的;自虐的;自讨苦吃的

  • It seems masochistic, somehow.

    这有点受虐狂的味道。

3 See also: sado-masochistic
