ingratiate
ɪngreɪʃieɪt ɪnˈgreɪʃiˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ingratiates, present participle ingratiating, past tense , past participle ingratiated
VERB 使得到…的欢心;讨好 If someone tries to ingratiate themselves with you, they do things to try and make you like them. 使得到…的欢心;讨好 [disapproval] [Also VERB pron-reflexive]

  • Many politicians are trying to ingratiate themselves with her. [VERB pronoun-reflexive + with]

    许多政客都设法讨她的欢心。
