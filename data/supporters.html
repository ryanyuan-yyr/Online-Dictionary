supporter
səpɔːʳtəʳ səˈpɔrtər
Word forms: plural supporters
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 支持者;拥护者 Supporters are people who support someone or something, for example a political leader or a sports team. 支持者;拥护者

  • The fourth night of violence in the German city of Rostock was triggered by football supporters.

    发生在德国城市罗斯托克的第四起夜间暴乱是由足球球迷们挑起的。

  • Bradley was a major supporter of the 1986 tax reform plan.

    布拉德利是 1986 年税收改革计划的主要支持者。
