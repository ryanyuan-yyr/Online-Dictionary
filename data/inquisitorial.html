inquisitorial
ɪnkwɪzɪtɔːriəl ɪnˌkwɪzəˈtɔriəl
ADJ 审判(官)似的;寻根究底的;爱查问的 If you describe something or someone as inquisitorial, you mean they resemble things or people in an inquisition. 审判(官)似的;寻根究底的;爱查问的

  • The next hearings will be structured differently in order to minimize the inquisitorial atmosphere.

    下次听证会将以不同的方式进行,以尽可能淡化审判似的气氛。
