gem
dʒem dʒɛm
Word forms: plural gems
1 N-COUNT 宝石 A gem is a jewel or stone that is used in jewellery. 宝石

  • ...a gold mask inset with emeralds and other gems.

    一张镶有翡翠和其他宝石的金面具

SYN precious stone, jewel, stone, semiprecious stone  
2 N-COUNT 难能可贵的人（或物）；令人非常惬意的人（或事物）；精品 If you describe something or someone as a gem, you mean that they are especially pleasing, good, or helpful. 难能可贵的人（或物）；令人非常惬意的人（或事物）；精品 [informal]

  • ...a gem of a hotel, Castel Clara. [+ of]

    卡斯特尔·克拉拉酒店，酒店中的明珠

  • Miss Famous, as she was called, was a gem.

    “知名小姐”人如其名，是个尤物。

SYN treasure, pick, prize, jewel
