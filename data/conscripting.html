conscript
kɒnskrɪpt kənskrɪpt kənˈskrɪpt ˈkɑnˌskrɪpt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense conscripts, present participle conscripting, past tense , past participle conscripted
The noun is pronounced /'kɒnskrɪpt/. The verb is pronounced /kən'skrɪpt/. 名词读作 /'kɒnskrɪpt/，动词读作 /kən'skrɪpt/。
1 N-COUNT 应征入伍者 A conscript is a person who has been made to join the armed forces of a country. 应征入伍者
2 VERB [usually passive] 征召;招募 If someone is conscripted, they are officially made to join the armed forces of a country. 征召;招募

  • He was conscripted into the German army. [be VERB-ed + into]

    他应征加入德国军队。

  • Peter was conscripted like every other young man. [be VERB-ed]

    和所有其他年轻人一样，彼得应征入伍。
