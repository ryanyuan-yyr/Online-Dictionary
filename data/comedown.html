comedown
kʌmdaʊn ˈkʌmˌdaʊn
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 come-down
N-SING 败落;失势;落魄 If you say that something is a comedown, you think that it is not as good as something else that you have just done or had. 败落;失势;落魄

  • The prospect of relegation is a comedown for a club that finished second two seasons ago.

    对于两个赛季前排名第二的俱乐部来说，竟然有降级之虞真是太落魄了。
