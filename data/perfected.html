perfect
pɜːʳfɪkt pəʳfekt ˈpɜrfɪkt pərˈfɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense perfects, present participle perfecting, past tense , past participle perfected
The adjective is pronounced /'pɜːfɪkt/. The verb is pronounced /pə'fekt/. 形容词读作/'pɜːfɪkt/，动词读作/pə'fekt/。
1 ADJ-GRADED 完美的；最佳的 Something that is perfect is as good as it could possibly be. practice makes perfect→see: practice  完美的；最佳的

  • He spoke perfect English.

    他英语说得棒极了。

  • Hiring a nanny has turned out to be the perfect solution.

    雇用保姆结果成了解决问题的最佳方法。

  • It's a perfect example of a house reflecting the person who lives there.

    这是房子反映主人个性的最佳例子。

  • Nobody is perfect.

    人无完人。

2 ADJ-GRADED 绝配的；十分合适的 If you say that something is perfect for a particular person, thing, or activity, you are emphasizing that it is very suitable for them or for that activity. 绝配的；十分合适的 [emphasis]

  • Carpet tiles are perfect for kitchens because they're easy to take up and wash. [+ for]

    方块地毯非常适用于厨房，因为其便于揭起来清洗。

  • So this could be the perfect time to buy a home.

    所以这可能是买房子的绝佳时机。

SYN excellent, ideal, supreme, superb  
3 ADJ 无瑕的；完好的；完美无缺的 If an object or surface is perfect, it does not have any marks on it, or does not have any lumps, hollows, or cracks in it. 无瑕的；完好的；完美无缺的

  • Use only clean, Grade A, perfect eggs.

    只用表面干净、无磕碰的A级鸡蛋。

  • ...their perfect white teeth.

    他们洁白无瑕的牙齿

4 ADJ [ADJ n] 十足的；完全的 You can use perfect to give emphasis to the noun following it. 十足的；完全的 [emphasis]

  • She was a perfect fool.

    她愚蠢至极。

  • Some people are always coming up to perfect strangers and asking them what they do.

    有些人总爱和完全不认识的人搭讪，询问人家的职业。

  • What he had said to her made perfect sense.

    他对她说的话非常合乎情理。

SYN complete, absolute, sheer, utter  
5 VERB 使完美；完善 If you perfect something, you improve it so that it becomes as good as it can possibly be. 使完美；完善

  • We perfected a hand-signal system so that he could keep me informed of hazards. [VERB noun]

    我们完善了一套手势信号，这样他就能随时告知我什么时候有危险了。

  • I removed the fibroid tumours, using the techniques that I have perfected. [VERB noun]

    我用完善后的技术摘掉了纤维瘤。

  • ...girls who needed to perfect their English. [VERB noun]

    需要提高英语水平的女孩们

6 ADJ [ADJ n] （动词）完成时的 The perfect tenses of a verb are the ones used to talk about things that happened or began before a particular time, as in 'He's already left' and 'They had always liked her'. The present perfect tense is sometimes called the perfect tense. （动词）完成时的
