polemicist
pəlemɪsɪst poʊˈlɛməsɪst pəˈlɛməsɪst
Word forms: plural polemicists
N-COUNT 善辩者；辩论家 A polemicist is someone who is skilled at arguing very strongly for or against a belief or opinion. 善辩者；辩论家 [formal]

  • The greatest polemicist of the 20th century must be Leon Trotsky.

    20世纪最了不起的辩论家非列昂·托洛茨基莫属。
