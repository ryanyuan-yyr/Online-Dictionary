exuberance
ɪgzjuːbərəns -zuːb- ɛgˈzubərəns ɛgˈzjubərəns ɪgˈzubərəns ɪgˈzjubərəns
1 N-UNCOUNT 热情洋溢；兴高采烈;喜气洋洋 Exuberance is behaviour which is energetic, excited, and cheerful. 热情洋溢；兴高采烈;喜气洋洋

  • Her burst of exuberance and her brightness overwhelmed me.

    她勃发的热情和阳光的性格征服了我。

SYN high spirits, energy, enthusiasm, vitality  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN of n] 勃勃生机 If you talk about the exuberance of something, you like it because it is lively, exciting, and full of energy and life. 勃勃生机

  • The sheer exuberance of the sculpture was exhilarating.

    那尊雕塑表现出的勃勃生机让人振奋。
