lanky
læŋki ˈlæŋki
Word forms: comparative lankier, superlative lankiest
ADJ-GRADED 瘦高挑的；瘦长而行动笨拙的 If you describe someone as lanky, you mean that they are tall and thin and move rather awkwardly. 瘦高挑的；瘦长而行动笨拙的

  • He was six feet four, all lanky and leggy.

    他身高6英尺4英寸，瘦高个儿，大长腿。

SYN gangling, thin, tall, spare
