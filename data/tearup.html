tear up
1 PHRASAL VERB 摧毁，拆毁，毁坏(公路、铁路、地面等) If something such as a road, railway, or area of land is torn up, it is completely removed or destroyed. 摧毁，拆毁，毁坏(公路、铁路、地面等)

  • Dozens of miles of railway track have been torn up. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    好几十英里的铁轨被毁坏了。

  • The company came under furious attack from environmentalists for tearing up the forests. [VERB PREP. noun]

    由于破坏了森林，这家公司受到环境保护主义者的猛烈抨击。
