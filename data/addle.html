addle
ædəl ˈædəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense addles, present participle addling, past tense , past participle addled
VERB 使糊涂;使迷惑 If something addles someone's mind or brain, they become confused and unable to think properly. 使糊涂;使迷惑

  • I suppose the shock had addled his poor old brain. [VERB noun]

    我估摸这个打击已经把他那可怜的脑袋瓜搞糊涂了。

SYN confuse, bewilder, mix up, muddle
