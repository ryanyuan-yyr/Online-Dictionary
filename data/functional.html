functional
fʌŋkʃənəl ˈfʌŋkʃənəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 实用的;为实用而设计的 Functional things are useful rather than decorative. 实用的;为实用而设计的

  • ...modern, functional furniture.

    实用的现代家具

  • The decor is functional.

    装潢风格追求实用。

SYN practical, utility, utilitarian, serviceable  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 工作(或运转)方式的;功能性的 Functional means relating to the way in which something works or operates, or relating to how useful it is. 工作(或运转)方式的;功能性的

  • ...rules defining the territorial boundaries and functional limits of the local state.

    划定本州地界并明确政府职权的章程

functionally

  • The Indian cavalry under Haig became a functionally efficient unit.

    黑格领导下的印度骑兵成为了一支高效执行任务的队伍。

  • It satisfies the user's requirements both functionally and emotionally.

    它同时满足了用户在功能和情感上的需求。

3 ADJ 在工作的;在起作用的;运转正常的 Functional equipment works or operates in the way that it is supposed to. 在工作的;在起作用的;运转正常的

  • We have fully functional smoke alarms on all staircases.

    我们在各层楼梯都安装有完全可用的烟雾警报器。

SYN working, operative, operational, in working order
