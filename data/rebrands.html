rebrand
riːbrænd riːˈbrænd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rebrands, present participle rebranding, past tense , past participle rebranded
VERB 给（产品或组织）重新命名（或包装），重塑…的形象 To rebrand a product or organization means to present it to the public in a new way, for example by changing its name or appearance. 给（产品或组织）重新命名（或包装），重塑…的形象 [business]

  • There are plans to rebrand many Texas stores. [VERB noun]

    有计划要重塑得克萨斯州诸多门店的品牌形象。
