barber
bɑːʳbəʳ ˈbɑrbər
Word forms: plural barbers
1 N-COUNT （为男性理发的）男理发师 A barber is a man whose job is cutting men's hair. （为男性理发的）男理发师

  • ...a barber's shop in south London.

    伦敦南部的理发店

2 N-SING 理发店 A barber's is a shop where a barber works. 理发店 [British]

  • My Mum took me to the barber's.

    我妈带我理发去了。
