telegram
telɪgræm ˈtɛləˌgræm
Word forms: plural telegrams
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] 电报 A telegram is a message that is sent by telegraph and then printed and delivered to someone's home or office. 电报

  • Scores of congratulatory telegrams and letters greeted Franklin on his return.

    富兰克林一回来，就收到了好几十封贺电和贺信。

  • The President received a briefing by telegram.

    总统收到用电报发来的一份简报。
