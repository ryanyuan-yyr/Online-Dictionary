freestyle
friːstaɪl ˈfriˌstaɪl
ADJ [ADJ n] (尤指游泳、摔跤、滑雪等体育竞赛)自由式的 Freestyle is used to describe sports competitions, especially in swimming, wrestling, and skiing, in which competitors can use any style or method that they like when they take part. (尤指游泳、摔跤、滑雪等体育竞赛)自由式的

  • ...the 100m freestyle swimming event.

    100 米自由泳比赛

  • ...the reigning European Freestyle Champion.

    本届欧洲自由式冠军

  • ...freestyle skiing.

    自由式滑雪

Freestyle is also a noun.

  • She won the 800 metres freestyle.

    她获得了 800 米自由泳冠军。
