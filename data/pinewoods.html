pinewood
paɪnwʊd ˈpaɪnˌwʊd
Word forms: plural pinewoods
The spelling pine wood is also used for meaning 1. 拼写pine wood亦用于义项1。
1 N-COUNT 松林 A pinewood is a wood which consists mainly of pine trees. 松林

  • ...the hilly pinewoods of northeast Georgia.

    佐治亚州东北部的丘陵松林

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 松木 Pinewood is wood that has come from a pine tree. 松木

  • ...Italian pinewood furniture.

    意大利松木家具
