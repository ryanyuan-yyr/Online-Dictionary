hypothesize
haɪpɒθɪsaɪz haɪˈpɑθəˌsaɪz hɪpɑθəsaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense hypothesizes, present participle hypothesizing, past tense , past participle hypothesized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 hypothesise
VERB 假设;假定 If you hypothesize that something will happen, you say that you think that thing will happen because of various facts you have considered. 假设;假定 [formal] [Also VERB]

  • To explain this, they hypothesise that galaxies must contain a great deal of missing matter which cannot be detected. [VERB that]

    为了解释这一点，他们假定各星系一定包含了大量无法探测到的不明物质。

  • I have long hypothesized a connection between these factors. [VERB noun]

    我一直以来就认为这些因素之间存在着某种关联。
