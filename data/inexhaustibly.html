inexhaustible
ɪnɪgzɔːstəbəl ˌɪnɛgˈzɔstəbəl ˌɪnɪgˈzɔstəbəl
ADJ 用不完的;无穷无尽的 If there is an inexhaustible supply of something, there is so much of it that it cannot all be used up. 用不完的;无穷无尽的

  • She has an inexhaustible supply of enthusiasm.

    她有用不完的热情。

  • His energy was unbounded and his patience inexhaustible.

    他有无穷的精力和无限的耐心。
