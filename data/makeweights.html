makeweight
meɪkweɪt ˈmeɪkˌweɪt
Word forms: plural makeweights
N-COUNT 凑数的人（或物）；无关紧要的人（或物） If you describe someone or something as a makeweight, you think that they are not good or valuable and that they have been included in an activity in order to fill up a gap. 凑数的人（或物）；无关紧要的人（或物） [disapproval]

  • He has not been signed to the club as a makeweight to fill out the numbers.

    他并不是作为凑数的成员签给俱乐部的。
