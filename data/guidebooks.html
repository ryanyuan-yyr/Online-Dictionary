guidebook
gaɪdbʊk ˈgaɪdˌbʊk
(also guide book)Word forms: plural guidebooks
1 N-COUNT 旅行指南 A guidebook is a book that gives tourists information about a town, area, or country. 旅行指南
2 N-COUNT 指南；手册 A guidebook is a book that gives you information or instructions to help you do or understand something. 指南；手册

  • In 1987 Congressional Quarterly published a series of guidebooks to American politics.

    1987年《国会季刊》出版了一系列美国政治指南读物。

SYN handbook, guide, manual, instructions
