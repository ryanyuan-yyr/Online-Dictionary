graceless
greɪsləs ˈgreɪslɪs
1 ADJ-GRADED 不优美的；难看的；不吸引人的 Something that is graceless is unattractive and not at all interesting or charming. 不优美的；难看的；不吸引人的

  • It was a massive, graceless house.

    这是一间非常难看的大房子。

2 ADJ-GRADED （行动）笨拙的,难看的，不稳的 A graceless movement is clumsy and uncontrolled. （行动）笨拙的,难看的，不稳的

  • ...a graceless pirouette.

    笨拙的单脚尖旋转

SYN inelegant, forced, awkward, clumsy  
gracelessly

  • He dropped gracelessly into a chair opposite her.

    他一屁股坐在她对面的椅子上，姿势非常不雅。

3 ADJ-GRADED 不懂礼貌的；粗鲁无礼的 If you describe someone as graceless, you mean that their behaviour is impolite. 不懂礼貌的；粗鲁无礼的

  • She couldn't stand his blunt, graceless manner.

    她无法忍受他的唐突和粗鲁。

SYN ill-mannered, crude, rude, coarse  
gracelessly

  • The task fell to Mr Harris to deliver this bad news. It was gracelessly done.

    宣布这一坏消息的任务落到了哈里斯先生身上，结果他处理得很不妥当。
