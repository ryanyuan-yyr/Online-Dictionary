unspecified
ʌnspesɪfaɪd ʌnˈspɛsɪˌfaɪd
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 未说明的；不明确的 You say that something is unspecified when you are not told exactly what it is. 未说明的；不明确的

  • The government said an unspecified number of bandits were killed.

    政府称已歼灭若干路匪。

  • He was arrested on unspecified charges.

    他遭到逮捕，但未透露具体罪名。

SYN unnamed, unknown, unidentified, uncertain
