exclusion
ɪkskluːʒən ɪkˈskluːʒən ɛksˈkluʒən ɪksˈkluʒən
Word forms: plural exclusions
1 N-VAR 排除;不予考虑 The exclusion of something is the act of deliberately not using, allowing, or considering it. 排除;不予考虑

  • It calls for the exclusion of all commercial lending institutions from the college loan program. [+ of]

    它提倡将所有商贷机构排除在高校贷款计划之外。

  • Certain exclusions and limitations apply.

    一些排除因素和限制条件比较适用。

2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] 排斥;拒绝 Exclusion is the act of preventing someone from entering a place or taking part in an activity. 排斥;拒绝

  • ...women's exclusion from political power. [+ from]

    在政治权力上对女性的排斥

  • A spokeswoman for Mr Prescott yesterday made it clear that he had been unhappy at his exclusion.

    普雷斯科特先生的发言人昨天表明他对于自己被拒绝十分不满。

3 PHRASE 把…排除在外；排除… If you do one thing to the exclusion of something else, you only do the first thing and do not do the second thing at all. 把…排除在外；排除…

  • Diane had dedicated her life to caring for him to the exclusion of all else.

    黛安娜一生都在照顾他，对其他一切都无暇顾及。
