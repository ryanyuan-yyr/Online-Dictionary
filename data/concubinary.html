concubine
kɒŋkjʊbaɪn ˈkɑnkjuˌbaɪn ˈkɑŋkjuˌbaɪn
Word forms: plural concubines
N-COUNT (旧时的)姘妇，情妇，妾 In former times, a concubine was a woman who lived with and had a sexual relationship with a man of higher social rank without being married to him. (旧时的)姘妇，情妇，妾
SYN mistress, courtesan, kept woman, paramour [old-fashioned]
