ice-skating ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪŋ
(also ice skating)
VERB [only cont] 溜冰;滑冰 If you go ice-skating, you move about on ice wearing ice-skates. This activity is also a sport. 溜冰;滑冰

  • They took me ice-skating on a frozen lake. [VERB]

    他们带我去一个结冰的湖面上溜冰了。

Ice-skating is also a noun.

  • I love watching ice-skating on television.

    我喜欢看电视上的溜冰表演。

  • ...British ice-skating champion Joanne Conway.

    英国滑冰冠军乔安妮·康威
