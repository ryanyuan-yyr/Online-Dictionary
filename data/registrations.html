registration
redʒɪstreɪʃən ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən
Word forms: plural registrations
N-UNCOUNT 注册；登记 The registration of something such as a person's name or the details of an event is the recording of it in an official list. 注册；登记

  • They have campaigned strongly for compulsory registration of dogs. [+ of]

    他们强烈呼吁实行犬类强制登记。

  • With the high voter registration, many will be voting for the first time.

    登记选民数量的上升意味着许多人将第一次参加投票。
