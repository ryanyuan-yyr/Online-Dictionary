bawl out
1 PHRASAL VERB 痛骂；大声责备 If someone bawls you out, they shout at you angrily because you have done something wrong. 痛骂；大声责备 [informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Do you think I'm just going to bawl you out and that'll be an end of it? [VERB noun PREP.]

    你觉得我把你痛骂一顿就完事了？
