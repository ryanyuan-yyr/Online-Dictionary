pratfall
prætfɔːl ˈprætˌfɔːl ˈprætˌfɔl
Word forms: plural pratfalls
1 N-COUNT 出丑；洋相 If someone takes a pratfall, they make an embarrassing mistake. 出丑；洋相 [mainly US]

  • They're waiting for the poor little rich girl to take a pratfall.

    他们等着瞧那个可怜的富家小姐出丑。

2 N-COUNT 屁股蹲儿；坐跌 A pratfall is a fall onto your bottom. 屁股蹲儿；坐跌 [mainly US]
