indescribable
ɪndɪskraɪbəbəl ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbəl
ADJ 难以形容的;无法描述的 You use indescribable to emphasize that a quality or condition is very intense or extreme, and therefore cannot be properly described. 难以形容的;无法描述的 [emphasis]

  • ...her indescribable joy when it was confirmed her son was alive.

    儿子活着的消息得到确认后她难以形容的喜悦

  • The stench from the sewer is indescribable.

    下水道发出一股说不出的恶臭。

SYN unutterable, indefinable, beyond words, ineffable  
indescribably

  • ...indescribably filthy conditions.

    极其肮脏的环境
