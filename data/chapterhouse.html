chapter house
Word forms: plural chapter houses
1 N-COUNT 座堂会议厅；牧师会礼堂 A chapter house is the building or set of rooms in the grounds of a cathedral where the members of the clergy hold their meetings. 座堂会议厅；牧师会礼堂
2 N-COUNT (高校的)学生联谊会住所(或会所) In a university or college, a chapter house is the place where a fraternity or sorority lives or meets. (高校的)学生联谊会住所(或会所) [US]
