siding
saɪdɪŋ ˈsaɪdɪŋ
Word forms: plural sidings
1 N-COUNT (铁路线的)侧线，岔道 A siding is a short railway track beside the main tracks, where engines and carriages are left when they are not being used. (铁路线的)侧线，岔道
2 N-UNCOUNT (建筑外墙的)墙板，壁板 Siding is a wooden or metal covering on the outside walls of a building. (建筑外墙的)墙板，壁板 [US]
