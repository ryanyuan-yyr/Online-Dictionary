limpid
lɪmpɪd ˈlɪmpɪd
1 ADJ-GRADED 清澈的；透明的 If you say that something is limpid, you mean that it is very clear and transparent. 清澈的；透明的 [literary]

  • ...limpid blue eyes.

    清澈的蓝眼睛

  • ...limpid rock-pools.

    清澄的岩石区潮水潭

SYN clear, bright, pure, transparent  
2 ADJ-GRADED 清晰的；畅达的 If you describe speech, writing, or music as limpid, you like it because it is clear, simple and flowing. 清晰的；畅达的 [literary, approval]

  • He thought the speech a model of its kind, limpid and unaffected.

    他认为这一演讲堪称典范，简洁流畅，清新自然。

SYN understandable, clear, lucid, unambiguous
