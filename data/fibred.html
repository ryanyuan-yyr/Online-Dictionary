fibre
faɪbəʳ ˈfaɪbər ˈfaɪbə
Word forms: plural fibres
in AM, use 美国英语用 fiber
1 N-COUNT (尤指制织物或绳索的天然或人造)纤维，丝 A fibre is a thin thread of a natural or artificial substance, especially one that is used to make cloth or rope. (尤指制织物或绳索的天然或人造)纤维，丝

  • If you look at the paper under a microscope, you will see the fibres.

    如果在显微镜下观察纸张，就会看见纤维。

  • ...a variety of coloured fibres.

    各式彩色丝线

SYN thread, strand, filament, tendril  
2 N-VAR 织物；纤维制品 A particular fibre is a type of cloth or other material that is made from or consists of threads. 织物；纤维制品

  • The ball is made of rattan — a natural fibre.

    这个球是由一种天然纤维—省藤制成的。

3 N-UNCOUNT (植物或种子中的)纤维,食物纤维 Fibre consists of the parts of plants or seeds that your body cannot digest. Fibre is useful because it makes food pass quickly through your body. (植物或种子中的)纤维,食物纤维

  • Most vegetables contain fibre.

    大多数蔬菜都含有纤维。

4 N-COUNT (神经或肌肉)纤维 A fibre is a thin piece of flesh like a thread which connects nerve cells in your body or which muscles are made of. (神经或肌肉)纤维

  • ...the nerve fibres.

    神经纤维

5 PHRASE 深切地;全身心地 If you say that you feel something with every fibre of your being, you mean that you feel it very deeply. 深切地;全身心地 [literary]

  • I wanted to be an actress with every fibre of my being.

    我一心一意想去做演员。
