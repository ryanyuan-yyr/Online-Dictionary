controversy
kɒntrəvɜːʳsi kəntrɒvəʳsi ˈkɒntrəˌvɜːsɪ kənˈtrɒvəsɪ ˈkɑntrəˌvɜrsi
Word forms: plural controversies
1 N-VAR 争论;争议;辩论 Controversy is a lot of discussion and argument about something, often involving strong feelings of anger or disapproval. 争论;争议;辩论 [Also + over/about]

  • The proposed cuts have caused considerable controversy.

    削减开支的提议引起了诸多争议。

  • ...a fierce political controversy over human rights abuses.

    关于侵犯人权问题的激烈的政治辩论

SYN argument, debate, row, discussion
