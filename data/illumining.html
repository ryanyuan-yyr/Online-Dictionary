illumine
ɪluːmɪn ɪˈlumən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense illumines, present participle illumining, past tense , past participle illumined
VERB 同 illuminate To illumine something means the same as to illuminate it. 同 illuminate [literary]

  • The interchange of ideas illumines the debate. [VERB noun]

    思想的交锋使辩论熠熠生辉。

  • By night, the perimeter wire was illumined by lights. [be VERB-ed]

    到了夜里,周遭的铁丝网都被灯光照亮。
