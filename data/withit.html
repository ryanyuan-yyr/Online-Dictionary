with it
wɪð ɪt
(also with-it)
1 ADJ-GRADED (尤指文化上)时髦的，新派的 If you say that someone is with it, you mean that they are fashionable or know about new things, especially in culture. (尤指文化上)时髦的，新派的 [informal, old-fashioned]
2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 警觉的；敏锐的；机敏的 If someone is not with it, they do not feel alert and therefore fail to understand things. 警觉的；敏锐的；机敏的 [informal]

  • She wasn't really with it. She hadn't taken in the practical consequences.

    她并不怎么机敏。她还没有意识到实际的后果。
