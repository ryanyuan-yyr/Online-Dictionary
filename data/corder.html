cord
kɔːʳd kɔrd
Word forms: plural cords
1 N-VAR 粗线;细绳 Cord is strong, thick string. 粗线;细绳

  • The door had been tied shut with a length of nylon cord.

    门用一段尼龙绳拴住了。

  • ...gilded cords and tassels.

    镀金的绳子和流苏

SYN rope, line, string, twine  
2 N-VAR 电源线 Cord is wire covered in rubber or plastic which connects electrical equipment to an electricity supply. 电源线

  • ...electrical cord.

    电源线

  • We used so many lights that we needed four extension cords.

    我们用的灯很多，因此需要4个接线板。

3 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] 灯芯绒裤子 Cords are trousers made of corduroy . 灯芯绒裤子

  • He had bare feet, a T-shirt and cords on.

    他光着脚，穿着T恤衫和灯芯绒裤子。

4 ADJ [ADJ n] 灯芯绒的 Cord means made of corduroy . 灯芯绒的

  • ...a pair of cord trousers.

    一条灯芯绒裤子
