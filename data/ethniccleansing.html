ethnic cleansing
N-UNCOUNT 种族清洗 Ethnic cleansing is the process of using violent methods to force certain groups of people out of a particular area or country. 种族清洗 [disapproval]

  • In late May, government forces began the 'ethnic cleansing' of the area around the town.

    5月底，政府军对该镇周边地区开始实施“种族清洗”。

  • ...the obnoxious policy of ethnic cleansing.

    可憎的种族清洗政策
