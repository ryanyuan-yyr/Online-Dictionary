look on
(also look upon)
PHRASAL VERB （以某种方式）看待 If you look on or look upon someone or something in a particular way, you think of them in that way. （以某种方式）看待

  • A lot of people looked on him as a healer. [V P n + as]

    很多人视他为信仰疗法术士。

  • A lot of people look on it like that. [V P n prep/adv]

    这事情很多人都这样看。

  • Employers look favourably on applicants who have work experience. [VERB PREP. noun]

    雇主往往青睐有工作经验的求职者。
