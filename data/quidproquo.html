quid pro quo ˌkwɪd prəʊ ˈkwəʊ ˌkwɪd proʊ ˈkwoʊ
Word forms: plural quid pro quos
N-COUNT 回报;交换物；报酬 A quid pro quo is a gift or advantage that is given to someone in return for something that they have done. 回报;交换物；报酬 [formal]

  • The statement is emphatic in stating that there must be a quid pro quo.

    这项声明强调必须要有所补偿。

  • They share a great deal of information on a quid pro quo basis.

    他们互通有无，共享大量信息。

SYN exchange, interchange, tit for tat, equivalent
