formula
fɔːʳmjʊlə ˈfɔrmjulə ˈfɔrmjələ
Word forms: plural formulae/fɔːʳmjʊliː/, plural formulas
1 N-COUNT 原则;规划;方案 A formula is a plan that is invented in order to deal with a particular problem. 原则;规划;方案

  • It is difficult to imagine how the North and South could ever agree on a formula to unify the divided peninsula.

    很难想象南北双方在统一半岛的方案上究竟怎样才能达成一致。

  • ...a peace formula.

    和平方案

2 N-SING 方案;方法 A formula for a particular situation, usually a good one, is a course of action or a combination of actions that is certain or likely to result in that situation. 方案;方法

  • After he was officially pronounced the world's oldest man, he offered this simple formula for a long and happy life.

    在他被正式宣布为世界上最年长的人后，他给出了这样一个简单的快乐长寿的秘诀。

  • Clever exploitation of the latest technology would be a sure formula for success. [+ for]

    对最新技术的灵活利用必将带来成功。

  • This socialism does not after all offer a magic formula for prosperity and human dignity.

    这种社会主义说到底并不能提供实现富足和人格尊严的神奇方案。

3 N-COUNT 公式;方程式 A formula is a group of letters, numbers, or other symbols which represents a scientific or mathematical rule. 公式;方程式

  • He developed a mathematical formula describing the distances of the planets from the Sun.

    他研究出了一个用于计算行星与太阳之间距离的数学公式。

4 N-COUNT 分子式;结构式 In science, the formula for a substance is a list of the amounts of various substances which make up that substance, or an indication of the atoms that it is composed of. 分子式;结构式
5 N-UNCOUNT [NOUN num] (赛车的)级，方程式 Formula is used followed by a number to indicate a particular type of racing car or something relating to that type of car. (赛车的)级，方程式

  • ...Formula 1 racing cars.

    一级方程式赛车

  • ...Formula 3000 racing.

    方程式 3000 赛车

6 N-UNCOUNT 配方奶;代乳品 Formula is a powder which you mix with water to make artificial milk for babies. 配方奶;代乳品

  • ...bottles of formula.

    几瓶配方奶
