blank cheque
Word forms: plural blank cheques
in AM, use 美国英语用 blank check
1 N-COUNT 空白支票 If someone is given a blank cheque, they are given the authority to spend as much money as they need or want. 空白支票 [journalism]

  • We are not prepared to write a blank cheque for companies that have run into trouble.

    我们不打算给那些陷入困境的公司开空白支票。

2 N-COUNT 自行处理权；全权 If someone is given a blank cheque, they are given the authority to do what they think is best in a particular situation. 自行处理权；全权 [journalism]

  • He was, in a sense, given a blank cheque to negotiate the new South Africa.

    从某种意义上来说，他已经全权负责建立新南非的协商工作。
