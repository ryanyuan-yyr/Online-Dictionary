lefty
lefti ˈlɛfti
(also leftie)Word forms: plural lefties
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 左派分子 If you refer to someone as a lefty, you mean that they have socialist beliefs. 左派分子 [mainly British, informal, disapproval]

  • ...a large group of students and trendy lefties.

    一大群学生和赶时髦的左派分子

2 N-COUNT 左撇子；（尤指）惯用左手的运动员 A lefty is someone, especially a sports player, who is left-handed. 左撇子；（尤指）惯用左手的运动员 [mainly US, informal]

  • The fact that Goran is a lefty just makes his serve that little bit better.

    戈兰是个左撇子，他的发球由此质量要更高一些。
