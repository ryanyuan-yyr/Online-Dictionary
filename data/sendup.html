send up
1 PHRASAL VERB (通过滑稽模仿)取笑，嘲弄 If you send someone or something up, you imitate them in an amusing way that makes them appear foolish. (通过滑稽模仿)取笑，嘲弄 [British, informal]

  • You sense he's sending himself up as well as everything else. [VERB noun PREP.]

    你意识到他在嘲弄自己以及周围的一切。

  • ...a spoof that sends up the macho world of fighter pilots. [VERB PREP. noun]

    嘲讽战斗机飞行员个个硬派男子作风的滑稽模仿

2See also:send-up 
3 PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 判…入狱;对…判刑 If someone who is on trial is sent up, they are found guilty and sent to prison. 判…入狱;对…判刑 [US]

  • If I'm going to be sent up for killing one guy, then I might as well kill three more. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    如果我会因杀一个人而坐牢，那我还不如再杀3个呢。

in BRIT, use 英国英语用 send down
