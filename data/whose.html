whose
huːz huːz huz
Usually pronounced /'huːz/ for meanings 2 and 3. 义项2和3通常在句中作重读。
1 PRON-REL (用于关系从句的句首，表示与前句所提到的人或事物有所属关系或相关联） You use whose at the beginning of a relative clause where you mention something that belongs to or is associated with the person or thing mentioned in the previous clause. (用于关系从句的句首，表示与前句所提到的人或事物有所属关系或相关联）

  • I saw a man shouting at a driver whose car was blocking the street.

    我看见一个男人正朝一个司机叫嚷，他的车把街道堵塞了。

  • ...a speedboat, whose fifteen-strong crew claimed to belong to the Italian navy.

    一艘快艇，其上船员15人，声称自己属于意大利海军

  • ...tourists whose vacations included an unexpected adventure.

    休假旅行中经历了意外冒险的游客们

2 QUEST (用于疑问句中，询问某物所属或其与何人何事有关)谁的 You use whose in questions to ask about the person or thing that something belongs to or is associated with. (用于疑问句中，询问某物所属或其与何人何事有关)谁的

  • Whose was the better performance?.

    谁表演得更好?

  • 'Whose is this?' — 'It's mine.'.

    “这是谁的？”—“是我的。”

  • 'It wasn't your fault, John.' — 'Whose, then?'.

    “那不是你的错，约翰。”—“那么，是谁的错呢？”

  • Whose car were they in?.

    他们坐的谁的车？

  • Whose daughter is she?

    她是谁的女儿？

3 DET-POSS (尤用在动词或形容词等后面，引导谈论某物所属或与何人何事相关的从句)谁的 You use whose after certain words, especially verbs and adjectives, to introduce a clause where you talk about the person or thing that something belongs to or is associated with. (尤用在动词或形容词等后面，引导谈论某物所属或与何人何事相关的从句)谁的

  • I'm wondering whose mother she is then.

    我很纳闷，那么她是谁的母亲呢？

  • I can't remember whose idea it was for us to meet again.

    我记不清我们再次会面是谁的主意了。

Whose is also a conjunction.

  • I wondered whose the coat was.

    我不知道那是谁的外套。

  • That kind of person likes to spend money, it doesn't matter whose it is.

    那种人就是喜欢花钱，钱是谁的不重要。

+−Usage
Usagewhose
Whose and who's are often confused. Whose expresses possession: Are you the one whose mobile phone kept ringing during class today? Who's means who is or who has: Who's calling you at this hour? Who's been calling you all night?
