gild
gɪld gɪld
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gilds, present participle gilding, past tense , past participle gilded
1 VERB 给…镀金；涂金于… If you gild a surface, you cover it in a thin layer of gold or gold paint. 给…镀金；涂金于…

  • Carve the names and gild them. [VERB noun]

    把姓名刻上去，再镀上金。

  • ...gilded statues. [VERB-ed]

    镀金雕像

SYN embellish, coat, grace, enhance  
2 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 画蛇添足；多此一举 If you say that someone is gilding the lily, you mean that they are spoiling something that is already beautiful or perfect by trying to improve it or by praising it too highly. 画蛇添足；多此一举

  • Gilding the lily of what is basically a romantic comedy, the director falls a little short of his best mark.

    这基本上是一出浪漫喜剧，这位导演却画蛇添足，没有发挥出他的最佳水平。
