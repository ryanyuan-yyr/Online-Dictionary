cocooned
kəkuːnd kəˈkuːnd
1 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 被(毯子或衣服)包裹的 If someone is cocooned in blankets or clothes, they are completely wrapped in them. 被(毯子或衣服)包裹的

  • She is comfortably cocooned in pillows. [+ in]

    她舒适地窝在枕头里。

  • ...my snugly cocooned baby sleeping in his pram.

    我的小宝贝包裹得舒舒服服的，正睡在婴儿车里

2 ADJ-GRADED 与世隔绝的;隐逸的 If you say that someone is cocooned, you mean that they are isolated and protected from everyday life and problems. 与世隔绝的;隐逸的

  • She was cocooned in a private world of privilege. [+ in]

    她生活在一个与世隔绝的特权世界里。

  • They were cocooned from the experience of poverty. [+ from]

    他们没受过穷。
