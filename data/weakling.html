weakling
wiːklɪŋ wiklɪŋ
Word forms: plural weaklings
1 N-COUNT 孱弱的人;弱小的动物 If you describe a person or an animal as a weakling, you mean that they are physically weak. 孱弱的人;弱小的动物 [disapproval]
2 N-COUNT 性格软弱的人 If you describe someone as a weakling, you mean that they have a weak character and are not at all brave. 性格软弱的人 [disapproval]

  • ...a moral weakling.

    道德感不强的人
