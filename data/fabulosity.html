fabulous
fæbjʊləs ˈfæbjuləs ˈfæbbjələs
1 ADJ-GRADED 极好的;绝妙的 If you describe something as fabulous, you are emphasizing that you like it a lot or think that it is very good. 极好的;绝妙的 [informal, emphasis]

  • This is a fabulous album. It's fresh, varied, fun.

    这张专辑真棒，新颖、有趣且富于变化。

  • The scenery and weather were fabulous.

    景色和天气都好极了。

SYN wonderful, excellent, brilliant, superb  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (成功)极大的;(美貌)惊人的 If you talk about someone's fabulous beauty, for example, or their fabulous success, you are emphasizing that they are extremely beautiful or extremely successful. (成功)极大的;(美貌)惊人的 [emphasis]

  • Despite his fabulous wealth, he is very much a man of the people.

    他虽然腰缠万贯，却非常亲民。

SYN legendary, imaginary, mythical, fictitious  
fabulously

    他们极其富有的父母
