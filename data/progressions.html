progression
prəgreʃən proʊˈgrɛʃən prəˈgrɛʃən
Word forms: plural progressions
1 N-COUNT [usually singular] 进展；发展；演变 A progression is a gradual development from one state to another. 进展；发展；演变

  • Both drugs slow the progression of HIV, but neither cures the disease. [+ of]

    这两种药物都可以延缓艾滋病病情发展，但是都不能治愈这种疾病。

  • I think they saw it as a natural progression for me.

    我想他们认为这对我来说是顺理成章的。

SYN progress, advance, advancement, gain  
2 N-COUNT （事件的）一连串，一系列，连续 A progression of things is a number of things which come one after the other. （事件的）一连串，一系列，连续 [formal]

  • ...a progression of habitats from dry meadows through marshes to open water. [+ of]

    从干燥的草地到沼泽地再到开阔的水面的一系列栖息地
