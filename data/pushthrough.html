push through
PHRASAL VERB 使被接受；使被通过 If someone pushes through a law, they succeed in getting it accepted although some people oppose it. 使被接受；使被通过 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The vote will enable the Prime Minister to push through tough policies. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这次投票将使首相能够力行强硬政策。

  • He tried to push the amendment through Parliament. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    他力促议会通过该修正案。
