dye
daɪ daɪ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense dyes, present participle dyeing, past tense , past participle dyed
1 VERB 给…染色 If you dye something such as hair or cloth, you change its colour by soaking it in a special liquid. 给…染色

  • The women prepared, spun and dyed the wool. [VERB noun]

    女人们处理羊毛,纺羊毛并给羊毛染色。

  • She had dyed black hair. [VERB-ed]

    她将她的黑头发染了色。

SYN colour, stain, tint, shade  
2 N-MASS 染料;染液 Dye is a substance made from plants or chemicals which is mixed into a liquid and used to change the colour of something such as cloth or hair. 染料;染液

  • ...bottles of hair dye.

    一瓶瓶染发剂

SYN colouring, colour, pigment, wash
