count against
PHRASAL VERB 对…不利（致使其被拒、受罚等） If something counts against you, it may cause you to be rejected or punished, or cause people to have a lower opinion of you. 对…不利（致使其被拒、受罚等）

  • He is highly regarded, but his youth might count against him. [VERB PREP. noun]

    人们对他的评价很高，但他年纪轻轻这一点也许会对他不利。
