mess around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 mess about
1 PHRASAL VERB 无所事事;混日子;闲荡 If you mess around or mess about, you spend time doing things without any particular purpose or without achieving anything. 无所事事;混日子;闲荡

  • We were just messing around playing with paint. [VERB PREP.]

    我们只是玩玩油彩，打发时间而已。

  • Stop messing about and go and buy one. [VERB PREP.]

    别再磨蹭了，快去买一个。

2 PHRASAL VERB 乱弄;瞎摆弄 If you say that someone is messing around with or messing about with something, you mean that they are interfering with it in a harmful way. 乱弄;瞎摆弄

  • Boys and girls will enjoy messing about with any kind of machine. [VERB PREP. + with]

    男孩和女孩会喜欢摆弄各种机械装置。

  • I'd like to know who's been messing about with the pram. [VERB PREP. + with]

    我想知道是谁在瞎摆弄这辆婴儿车。

3 PHRASAL VERB 胡闹;瞎闹 If someone is messing around or messing about, they are behaving in a joking or silly way. 胡闹;瞎闹

  • I thought she was messing about. [VERB PREP.]

    我认为她是在胡闹。

4 PHRASAL VERB 恶劣地对待;玩弄 If you mess someone around or mess them about, you treat them badly, for example by not being honest with them, or by continually changing plans which affect them. 恶劣地对待;玩弄 [mainly British]

  • Davison had pulled out of a move to his old club because they had 'messed him about'. [VERB noun PREP.]

    戴维森已经打消了返回原来的俱乐部的念头，因为他们“糊弄他”。

  • I think they've been messed around far too much. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我认为他们被耍得太过头了。
