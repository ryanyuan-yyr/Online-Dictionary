remunerate
rɪmjuːnəreɪt rɪˈmjunəˌreɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense remunerates, present participle remunerating, past tense , past participle remunerated
VERB [usually passive] 给…报酬 If you are remunerated for work that you do, you are paid for it. 给…报酬 [formal]

  • You will be remunerated and so will your staff. [be VERB-ed]

    你将得到酬劳，你的职员也如此。

  • ...an adequately remunerated job. [VERB-ed]

    报酬丰厚的工作

SYN pay, reward, compensate, repay
