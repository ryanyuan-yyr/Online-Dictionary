commentator
kɒmənteɪtəʳ ˈkɑmənˌteɪtər
Word forms: plural commentators
1 N-COUNT 解说员;实况播音员 A commentator is a broadcaster who gives a radio or television commentary on an event. 解说员;实况播音员

  • ...a sports commentator.

    体育解说员

SYN reporter, special correspondent, sportscaster, commenter  
2 N-COUNT 评论员 A commentator is someone who often writes or broadcasts about a particular subject. 评论员

  • ...a political commentator.

    政治评论员

  • A. M. Babu is a commentator on African affairs. [+ on]

    A. M. 巴布是一位非洲事务评论员。

SYN critic, interpreter, expositor, annotator
