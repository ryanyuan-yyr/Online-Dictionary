hybrid
haɪbrɪd ˈhaɪbrɪd
Word forms: plural hybrids
1 N-COUNT （动物或植物的）杂（交）种，混（合）种 A hybrid is an animal or plant that has been bred from two different species of animal or plant. （动物或植物的）杂（交）种，混（合）种 [technical]

  • All these brightly coloured hybrids are so lovely in the garden.

    花园里所有这些色彩鲜艳的杂交花真美丽。

  • ...a hybrid between watermint and spearmint.

    水生薄荷和留兰香的杂交种

SYN crossbreed, cross, mixture, compound  
Hybrid is also an adjective.

  • ...the hybrid maize seed.

    杂交玉米种子

2 N-COUNT （尤指两种不同物质的）混合物，合成物 You can use hybrid to refer to anything that is a mixture of other things, especially two other things. （尤指两种不同物质的）混合物，合成物

  • ...a hybrid of solid and liquid fuel. [+ of]

    固体和液体燃料的混合物

SYN mixture, compound, composite, amalgam  
Hybrid is also an adjective.

  • ...a hybrid system.

    合成系统

3 混合动力车；油电混合车 A hybrid or a hybrid car is a car that has both an electric motor and an ordinary car engine. It uses the ordinary engine when it needs extra power. 混合动力车；油电混合车

  • Hybrids, unlike pure electric cars, never need to be plugged in.

    与纯电动汽车不同，混合动力车永远不需要接电源。

  • Hybrid cars can go almost 600 miles between refueling.

    混合动力车加一次油可以跑几乎600英里。
