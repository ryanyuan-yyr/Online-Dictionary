protégé prɒtɪʒeɪ proʊt- ˈproʊtəˌʒeɪ ˌ proʊtəˈʒeɪ
Word forms: plural protégés
LANGUAGE NOTE:

  • The spelling protégée is often used when referring to a woman.

    拼写protégée通常用于指代女性。

N-COUNT 被保护者; 门徒 The protégé of an older and more experienced person is a young person who is helped and guided by them over a period of time. 被保护者; 门徒

  • ...Kelley, a former lawyer and protégé of Steven Bochco.

    ...曾是一名律师的凯利，史蒂文•博克寇的门徒。

SYN charge, student, pupil, ward
