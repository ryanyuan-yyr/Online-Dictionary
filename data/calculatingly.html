calculating
kælkjʊleɪtɪŋ ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 有心计的;诡计多端的;能算计的 If you describe someone as calculating, you disapprove of the fact that they deliberately plan to get what they want, often by hurting or harming other people. 有心计的;诡计多端的;能算计的 [disapproval]

  • Northbridge is a cool, calculating and clever criminal who could strike again.

    诺思布里奇是个头脑冷静、诡计多端、阴险狡诈的罪犯,他可能还会犯案。

SYN scheming, designing, sharp, shrewd
