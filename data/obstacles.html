obstacle
ɒbstəkəl ˈɑbstəkəl
Word forms: plural obstacles
1 N-COUNT 障碍物 An obstacle is an object that makes it difficult for you to go where you want to go, because it is in your way. 障碍物

  • Most competition cars will only roll over if they hit an obstacle.

    大多数赛车只有在撞上障碍物时才会翻车。

  • He left her to navigate her own way round the trolleys and other obstacles.

    他扔下她一个人在一堆手推车和其他障碍物之间绕来绕去。

SYN obstruction, block, barrier, hurdle  
2 N-COUNT 障碍；困难；阻碍 You can refer to anything that makes it difficult for you to do something as an obstacle . 障碍；困难；阻碍

  • Overcrowding remains a large obstacle to improving conditions. [+ to]

    过分拥挤仍然是改善环境的一大障碍。

  • To succeed, you must learn to overcome obstacles.

    必须学会克服困难才能成功。

SYN hindrance, check, bar, block
