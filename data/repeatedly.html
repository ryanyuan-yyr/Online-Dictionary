repeatedly
rɪpiːtɪdli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 重复地;反复地;再三地 If you do something repeatedly, you do it many times. 重复地;反复地;再三地

  • Both men have repeatedly denied the allegations.

    两个人都一再否认指控。

  • The rebel soldiers tried repeatedly to storm the building.

    叛军屡次尝试强攻这座建筑。

SYN over and over, often, frequently, many times
