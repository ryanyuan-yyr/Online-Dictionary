lonesome
loʊnsəm ˈləʊnsəm ˈloʊnsəm
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 孤独的；寂寞的；孤单的 Someone who is lonesome is unhappy because they do not have any friends or do not have anyone to talk to. 孤独的；寂寞的；孤单的 [mainly US]

  • I've grown so lonesome, thinking of you.

    想起你，我觉得非常寂寞。

SYN lonely, deserted, isolated, lone  
2 ADJ-GRADED 荒僻的；荒凉的；人迹罕至的 A lonesome place is one which very few people come to and which is a long way from places where people live. 荒僻的；荒凉的；人迹罕至的 [US]

  • He was finding the river lonesome.

    他发现这条河很偏僻。
