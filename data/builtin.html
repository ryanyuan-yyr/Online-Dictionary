built-in ˈbɪltˈɪn
ADJ [ADJ n] 内置的；嵌入式的 Built-in devices or features are included in something as a part of it, rather than being separate. 内置的；嵌入式的

  • ...modern cameras with built-in flash units.

    有内置闪光灯的新式相机

  • We're going to have built-in cupboards in the bedrooms.

    我们将在卧室安装嵌入式衣橱。

SYN integral, fitted, incorporated, integrated
