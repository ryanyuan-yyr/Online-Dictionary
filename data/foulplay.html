foul play
1 N-UNCOUNT (致人死亡的)暴行;谋杀 Foul play is criminal violence or activity that results in a person's death. (致人死亡的)暴行;谋杀

  • The report says it suspects foul play was involved in the deaths of two journalists.

    该报道称怀疑两名记者死于谋杀。

2 N-UNCOUNT (尤指体育比赛中的)不公平行为，犯规动作 Foul play is unfair or dishonest behaviour, especially during a sports game. (尤指体育比赛中的)不公平行为，犯规动作

  • Players were warned twice for foul play.

    运动员们因为犯规动作被警告了两次。
