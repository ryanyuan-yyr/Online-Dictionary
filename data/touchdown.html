touchdown
tʌtʃdaʊn ˈtʌtʃˌdaʊn
Word forms: plural touchdowns
1 N-VAR (飞机或宇宙飞船的)降落，着陆 Touchdown is the landing of an aircraft or spacecraft. (飞机或宇宙飞船的)降落，着陆

  • ...a perfect touchdown.

    完美着陆

  • The astronauts are preparing for touchdown tomorrow morning.

    宇航员正在为明天早上的着陆作准备。

2 N-COUNT (橄榄球中的)攻方持球越线得分，达阵 In rugby and American football, a touchdown is when a team scores points by taking the ball over the opposition's goal line. (橄榄球中的)攻方持球越线得分，达阵
