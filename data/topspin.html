topspin
tɒpspɪn ˈtɒpˌspɪn ˈtɑpˌspɪn
N-UNCOUNT (网球)上旋In sports such as tennis, topspin is the way that a ball spins rapidly when you hit it in a certain way. (网球)上旋

  • Sabatini used her exaggerated topspin to good effect.

    萨巴蒂尼将她过大的上旋发挥出了威力。

  • He put a lot more topspin on his backhand.

    他在反手打出了更多上旋。
