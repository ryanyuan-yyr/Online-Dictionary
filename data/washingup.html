washing-up
1 N-UNCOUNT 洗餐具 To do the washing-up means to wash the plates, cups, cutlery, and pans which have been used for cooking and eating a meal. 洗餐具 [British]

  • Martha volunteered to do the washing-up.

    玛莎主动提出去洗餐具。

in AM, use 美国英语用 wash the dishes
2 N-UNCOUNT 待洗的餐具 Washing-up is the plates, cups, cutlery, and pans which you have to wash after a meal. 待洗的餐具 [British]

  • ...a brimming bowl of washing-up.

    一满盆待洗的餐具

in AM, use 美国英语用 dirty dishes, the dishes
