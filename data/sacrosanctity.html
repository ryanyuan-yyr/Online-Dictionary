sacrosanct
sækroʊsæŋkt ˈsækroʊˌsæŋkt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 神圣不可侵犯的;不容指责的;不可变更的 If you describe something as sacrosanct, you consider it to be special and are unwilling to see it criticized or changed. 神圣不可侵犯的;不容指责的;不可变更的

  • Freedom of the press is sacrosanct.

    新闻自由是神圣不可侵犯的。

  • ...weekend rest days were considered sacrosanct.

    占用周末休息日被认为没得商量。

SYN inviolable, sacred, inviolate, untouchable
