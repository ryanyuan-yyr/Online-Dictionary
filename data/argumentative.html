argumentative
ɑːʳgjʊmentətɪv ˈɑrgjəˈmɛntətɪv
ADJ-GRADED 好辩的;好争论的;好争吵的 Someone who is argumentative is always ready to disagree or start quarrelling with other people. 好辩的;好争论的;好争吵的 [disapproval]

  • Great chess players have a reputation for being both eccentric and argumentative.

    国际象棋大师们以性格怪异和好与人争辩而闻名。

  • You're in an argumentative mood today!

    你今天就是找人吵架的。

SYN quarrelsome, contrary, contentious, belligerent
