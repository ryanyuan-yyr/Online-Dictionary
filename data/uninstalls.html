uninstall
ʌnɪnstɔːl ˈʌnɪnˌstɔːl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense uninstalls, present participle uninstalling, past tense , past participle uninstalled
VERB 卸载（程序） If you uninstall a computer program, you remove it permanently from your computer. 卸载（程序） [computing]

  • If you don't like the program, just uninstall it and forget it. [VERB noun]
