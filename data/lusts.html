lust
lʌst lʌst
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lusts, present participle lusting, past tense , past participle lusted
1 N-UNCOUNT （强烈的）肉欲，淫欲，色欲 Lust is a feeling of strong sexual desire for someone. （强烈的）肉欲，淫欲，色欲

  • His relationship with Angie was the first which combined lust with friendship.

    他和安吉的关系属第一种，即肉欲加友谊。

  • His lust for her grew until it was overpowering. [+ for]

    他企图在肉体上占有她的欲望变得一发不可收拾。

SYN lechery, sensuality, licentiousness, carnality  
2 N-UNCOUNT （强烈的）欲望，渴望 A lust for something is a very strong and eager desire to have it. （强烈的）欲望，渴望

  • It was Fred's lust for glitz and glamour that was driving them apart. [+ for]

    是弗雷德对浮华、奢靡生活的强烈欲望使他们越走越远。

SYN desire, longing, passion, appetite  
Phrasal verbs:
lust after
