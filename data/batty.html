batty
bæti ˈbæti
Word forms: comparative battier, superlative battiest
ADJ-GRADED 古怪的；疯疯癫癫的 If you say that someone is batty, you mean that they are rather eccentric or slightly crazy. 古怪的；疯疯癫癫的 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • Laura's going a bit batty.

    劳拉变得有些古怪。

  • ...some batty uncle of theirs.

    他们那个有点疯癫的叔叔

SYN crazy, odd, mad, eccentric
