comradeship
kɒmreɪdʃɪp -ræd-
N-UNCOUNT 同志情谊 Comradeship is friendship between a number of people who are doing the same work or who share the same difficulties or dangers. 同志情谊

  • ...the comradeship of his fellow soldiers. [+ of]

    他的战友们之间的同志情谊

SYN fellowship, solidarity, fraternity, brotherhood
