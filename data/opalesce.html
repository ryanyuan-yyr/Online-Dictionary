opalescent
oʊpəlesənt ˌoʊpəˈlɛsənt
ADJ-GRADED 蛋白石般无色的；乳白色的；发乳光的 Opalescent means colourless or white like an opal, or changing colour like an opal. 蛋白石般无色的；乳白色的；发乳光的 [literary]

  • Elaine turned her opalescent eyes on him.

    伊莱恩一双乳白色的眼睛转向他。

  • ...a sky which was still faintly opalescent.

    仍然泛白的天空

SYN iridescent, pearly, shimmering, lustrous  
opalescence

  • The sunset was making great splashes of fiery opalescence across the sky.

    夕阳将大片天空染成色彩变幻的火红色。
