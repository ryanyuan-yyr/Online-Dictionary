tag on
PHRASAL VERB 增加;添加 If you tag something on, you add it. 增加;添加 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • It is also worth tagging on an extra day or two to see the capital. [VERB PREP. noun]

    为了看看首都，再多个一两天也是值的。

  • Johnson tagged on a happy ending and changed the title to 'Life Begins at 8:30'. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    约翰逊为其添加了个圆满的结尾，并将标题改作《生活从 8:30 开始》。

SYN add, tack on, append [formal] , adjoin
