bomb out
1 PHR-V-PASSIVE （建筑物、地区、家园）被炸毁；被炸得无家可归 If a building or area is bombed out, it is destroyed by bombs. If people are bombed out, their houses are destroyed by bombs. （建筑物、地区、家园）被炸毁；被炸得无家可归

  • London had been bombed out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    伦敦已经被炸得面目全非。

  • On 27 October they were bombed out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    在10月27号他们的家园被炸毁了。

SYN blow to bits, destroy, devastate, flatten
