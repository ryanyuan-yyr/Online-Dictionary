favouritism
feɪvərɪtɪzəm
in AM, use 美国英语用 favoritism
N-UNCOUNT 偏心;偏袒;偏爱 If you accuse someone of favouritism, you disapprove of them because they unfairly help or favour one person or group much more than another. 偏心;偏袒;偏爱 [disapproval]

  • Maria loved both the children. There was never a hint of favouritism.

    玛丽亚对两个孩子都疼爱有加，向来一视同仁。

SYN bias, preference, nepotism, preferential treatment
