zing
zɪŋ zɪŋ
N-UNCOUNT [oft a NOUN] 活力;精神;趣味 If you refer to the zing in someone or something, you mean the quality that makes them lively or interesting. 活力;精神;趣味 [informal]

  • He just lacked that extra zing.

    他就是缺乏那多一点的活力。

  • There's nothing like fresh basil to put a zing into a tomato sauce.

    在番茄沙司中无论加入什么都不如加一点儿新鲜的紫苏更能提味。

SYN vitality, go [informal] , life, energy
