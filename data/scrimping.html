scrimp
skrɪmp skrɪmp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense scrimps, present participle scrimping, past tense , past participle scrimped
VERB 节省;精打细算;省吃俭用 If you scrimp on things, you live cheaply and spend as little money as possible. 节省;精打细算;省吃俭用

  • Scrimping on safety measures can be a false economy. [VERB + on]

    在安全措施上抠手抠脚可能会打错算盘。

  • He has had to give up luxuries as he scrimps and saves while looking for a job. [VERB]

    找工作期间，他只好放弃奢侈的生活，精打细算、省吃俭用。

SYN economize, save, scrape, limit
