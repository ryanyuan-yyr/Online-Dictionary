fence off
PHRASAL VERB (用围栏、篱笆等)将(一块地)围起来 If you fence off an area of land, you build a fence round it. (用围栏、篱笆等)将(一块地)围起来

  • When she and Luther got their house, they would fence off a lawn and raise roses from cuttings. [VERB PREP. noun]

    等她和卢瑟有了自己的房子，他们就会围起一块草坪扦插玫瑰。

  • They had to evict some gypsies today and I had to go and fence the area off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他们今天必须驱逐一些吉普赛人，我得去把那个区域圈起来。
