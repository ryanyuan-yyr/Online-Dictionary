virtuous circle
ˈvɜːtʃʊəs ˈsɜːkəl
N-SING 良性循环 If you describe a situation as a virtuous circle, you mean that once one good thing starts happening, other good things happen, which cause the first thing to continue happening. 良性循环

  • Exercise creates its own virtuous circle. Once you start a programme and do it regularly, you'll feel so good you'll want to continue.

    锻炼身体能够形成一种良性循环。一旦你开始一项锻炼计划并持之以恒，你会感觉良好并坚持下去。

  • ...a virtuous circle of investment and growth. [+ of]

    投资和增长的良性循环
