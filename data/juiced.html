juiced dʒust dʒust
(also juiced up)
ADJ 兴奋的Someone who is juiced or juiced up is excited. 兴奋的 [informal]

  • Script editors are all juiced up over the humorous potential.

    剧本编辑们全都因剧本潜在的幽默性而兴奋起来了。

  • Even small eclipses get him juiced and should be fascinating to any amateur astronomer.

    甚至是小型的月食都会令他兴奋，而且应该会令任何一位业余的天文爱好者着迷。
