enhancement
ɪnhɑːnsmənt -hæns-
Word forms: plural enhancements
N-VAR 提高;增加;增强 The enhancement of something is the improvement of it in relation to its value, quality, or attractiveness. 提高;增加;增强 [formal]

  • Music is merely an enhancement to the power of her words.

    音乐只是增强了她话语的力度。

  • He was concerned with the enhancement of the human condition.

    他关心人类生存环境的改善。

SYN improvement, strengthening, heightening, enrichment
