outnumber
aʊtnʌmbəʳ ˌaʊtˈnʌmbər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outnumbers, present participle outnumbering, past tense , past participle outnumbered
VERB （在数量上）压倒，比…多 If one group of people or things outnumbers another, the first group has more people or things in it than the second group. （在数量上）压倒，比…多

  • ...a town where men outnumber women four to one. [VERB noun]

    男性比女性多3倍的城镇

  • Donkeys outnumber cars in this landscape of tiny stonewalled fields.

    在这片以石墙隔出巴掌大的农田的乡村，驴子比汽车还多。
