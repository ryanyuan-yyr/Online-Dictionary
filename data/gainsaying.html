gainsay
geɪnseɪ ˌgeɪnˈseɪ ˈgeɪnˌseɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gainsays, present participle gainsaying, past tense , past participle gainsaid
VERB 反驳；反对 If there is no gainsaying something, it is true or obvious and everyone would agree with it. 反驳；反对 [formal]

  • However much people have criticised her style and some of her policies no one will gainsay her courage. [VERB noun]

    无论人们如何强烈地批评她的处事风格和某些政策，她的勇气却无人能够否认。

  • There is no gainsaying the fact that they have been responsible for a truly great building. [VERB noun]

    不容否认的事实是，他们一手建起了一座真正伟大的建筑。

SYN deny, dispute, disagree with, contradict
