wall-to-wall ˈwɔltəˈwɔl
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (地毯)铺满整个地板的 A wall-to-wall carpet covers the floor of a room completely. (地毯)铺满整个地板的
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 无处不在的;处处可见的 You can use wall-to-wall to describe something that fills or seems to fill all the available space. 无处不在的;处处可见的

  • ...television's wall-to-wall soccer coverage.

    电视上铺天盖地的足球报道

  • There were wall-to-wall people, all invited guests and celebrities.

    到处都是人，全是邀请来的贵客名流。
