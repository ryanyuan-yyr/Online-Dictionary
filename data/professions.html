profession
prəfeʃən proʊˈfɛʃən prəˈfɛʃən
Word forms: plural professions
1 N-COUNT [oft by NOUN] （需要高等教育或训练的）职业 A profession is a type of job that requires advanced education or training. （需要高等教育或训练的）职业

  • Harper was a teacher by profession.

    哈珀的职业是教师。

  • Only 20 per cent of jobs in the professions are held by women.

    在这些职业中，只有20%的岗位是由女性担任的。

SYN occupation, calling, business, career  
2 N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] （某一专业工作的）全体人员，业界人士，专业界 You can use profession to refer to all the people who have the same profession. （某一专业工作的）全体人员，业界人士，专业界

  • The attitude of the medical profession is very much more liberal now.

    现在，医务人员的态度开明多了。
