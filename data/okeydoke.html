okey doke oʊkeɪ doʊk ˈəʊkɪ dəʊk
(also okey dokey)
CONVENTION （表示同意或希望转换话题或做别的事情）好的，好吧，好了 Okey doke is used in the same way as 'OK' to show that you agree to something, or that you want to start talking about something else or doing something else. （表示同意或希望转换话题或做别的事情）好的，好吧，好了 [informal, spoken]

  • Okey doke. I'll give you a ring.

    好的。我会给你打电话。
