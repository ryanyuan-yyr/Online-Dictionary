sui generis
suːi dʒenərɪs ˌsuːaɪ ˈdʒɛnərɪs ˈsui ˈdʒɛnərɪs ˈsuaɪ
ADJ 独特的;特有的 If you describe a person or thing as sui generis, you mean that there is no-one else or nothing else of the same kind and so you cannot make judgments about them based on other things. 独特的;特有的 [formal]

  • Japanese politics are sui generis.

    日本的政治非常特殊。
