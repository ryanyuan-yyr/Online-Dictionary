hand-wringing ˈhændˌrɪŋɪŋ
(also handwringing)
N-UNCOUNT 绝望If you accuse someone of hand-wringing, you mean that they are expressing sorrow about a bad situation but are saying that they are unable to change it. 绝望 [disapproval]

  • ...despite all the public hand-wringing about the importance of voting.

    ...尽管公众对选举的重要性产生了绝望。
