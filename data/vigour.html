vigour
vɪgəʳ
in AM, use 美国英语用 vigor
N-UNCOUNT 体力;精力;活力 Vigour is physical or mental energy and enthusiasm. 体力;精力;活力

  • His body lacks the bounce and vigour of a normal two-year-old.

    他缺乏一个正常两岁儿童应具备的弹跳力与活力。

  • He blew his nose with great vigour.

    他使劲擤了擤鼻子。

SYN energy, might, force, vitality
