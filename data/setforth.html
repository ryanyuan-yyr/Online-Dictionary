set forth
1 PHRASAL VERB (清晰而有条理地)阐述，陈述，说明 If you set forth a number of facts, beliefs, or arguments, you explain them in writing or speech in a clear, organized way. (清晰而有条理地)阐述，陈述，说明 [formal]

  • Dr. Mesibov set forth the basis of his approach to teaching students. [VERB PREP. noun]

    梅西伯夫博士阐述了他的教学方法的基础。

SYN present, describe, explain, detail  
2 PHRASAL VERB 出发;动身;启程 If you set forth, you start a journey. 出发;动身;启程 [literary]

  • It was during the reign of Queen Isabella that Christopher Columbus set forth on his epic voyage of discovery. [VERB PREP.]

    正是在伊莎贝拉女王统治时期克里斯托弗·哥伦布开始了他伟大的发现之旅。

SYN embark, set off, start out, sally forth
