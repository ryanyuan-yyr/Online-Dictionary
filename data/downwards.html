downwards
daʊnwəʳdz
(also downward)
1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向下;往下 If you move or look downwards, you move or look towards the ground or a lower level. 向下;往下

  • Benedict pointed downwards again with his stick.

    贝内迪克特用他的拐棍又往下指了指。

  • She gazed downwards.

    她朝下望去。

  • The child lay face downwards.

    那孩子趴着。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] (数量、比率等)呈下降趋势地 If an amount or rate moves downwards, it decreases. (数量、比率等)呈下降趋势地

  • Inflation is moving firmly downwards.

    通货膨胀率正稳步下降。

3 ADV [from n ADV] （自…）以下;往下 If you want to emphasize that a statement applies to everyone in an organization, you can say that it applies from its leader downwards. （自…）以下;往下 [emphasis]

  • ...from the Prime Minister downwards.

    自首相以下的每个人
