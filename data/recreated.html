recreate
riːkrieɪt ˈrɛkrɪˌeɪt ˈrɛkriˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense recreates, present participle recreating, past tense , past participle recreated
VERB 重现;重建;再创造 If you recreate something, you succeed in making it exist or seem to exist in a different time or place to its original time or place. 重现;重建;再创造

  • The rooms are furnished and recreate the atmosphere of the castle's medieval heyday. [VERB noun]

    房间都进行了布置，重新营造出该城堡中世纪全盛时期的氛围。

  • I am trying to recreate family life far from home. [VERB noun]

    我正试图在远离家园的地方重建家庭生活。
