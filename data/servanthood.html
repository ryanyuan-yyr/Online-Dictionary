servant
sɜːʳvənt ˈsɜrvənt
Word forms: plural servants
1 N-COUNT 仆人;用人 A servant is someone who is employed to work at another person's home, for example as a cleaner or a gardener. 仆人;用人
2 N-COUNT 提供服务的人(或事物);可以利用的人(或事物) You can use servant to refer to someone or something that provides a service for people or can be used by them. 提供服务的人(或事物);可以利用的人(或事物)

  • Like any other public servants, police must respond to public demand.

    和其他所有公务员一样，警察必须服务于公众的需求。

  • The question is whether technology is going to be our servant or our master.

    问题是科技将为我们所用还是要驾驭我们。
