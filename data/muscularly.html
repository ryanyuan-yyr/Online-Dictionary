muscular
mʌskjʊləʳ ˈmʌskjʊləs ˈmʌskjulər ˈmʌskjələr
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 肌肉的;影响肌肉的 Muscular means involving or affecting your muscles. 肌肉的;影响肌肉的

  • As a general rule, all muscular effort is enhanced by breathing in as the effort is made.

    通常，肌肉的力量能通过使劲儿吸气增强。

  • Early symptoms include anorexia, muscular weakness and fatigue.

    早期症状有厌食、肌无力和疲劳。

2 ADJ-GRADED 肌肉结实的;强壮的 If a person or their body is muscular, they are very fit and strong, and have firm muscles which are not covered with a lot of fat. 肌肉结实的;强壮的

  • Like most female athletes, she was lean and muscular.

    像大多女运动员一样，她清瘦但肌肉发达。

  • ...his tanned muscular legs.

    他那肌肉发达、肤色黝黑的双腿
