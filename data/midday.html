midday
mɪddeɪ ˈmɪdˌdeɪ
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft preposition NOUN] 中午;正午 Midday is twelve o'clock in the middle of the day. 中午;正午

  • At midday everyone would go down to Reg's Cafe.

    中午，大家都会下楼去雷吉餐馆。

  • It's eight minutes after midday.

    现在的时间是正午过8分钟。

SYN noon, twelve o'clock, noonday, noontime  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 中午时分(指中午12点钟前后的一段时间) Midday is the middle part of the day, from late morning to early afternoon. 中午时分(指中午12点钟前后的一段时间)

  • People were beginning to tire in the midday heat.

    在正午时分的热浪之中，人们开始感到疲倦。
