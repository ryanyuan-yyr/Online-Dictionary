view
vjuː vju
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense views, present participle viewing, past tense , past participle viewed
1 N-COUNT [NOUN that] 观点;看法;见解;态度 Your views on something are the beliefs or opinions that you have about it, for example whether you think it is good, bad, right, or wrong. 观点;看法;见解;态度

  • Washington and Moscow are believed to have similar views on Kashmir. [+ on]

    据信华盛顿和莫斯科在克什米尔问题上的看法相似。

  • I take the view that she should be stopped as soon as possible.

    我认为应该尽快阻止她。

  • My own view is absolutely clear. What I did was right.

    我自己的观点非常清楚。我做的事是正确的。

  • You should also make your views known to your local MP.

    你也应该让本地议员了解你的观点。

SYN opinion, thought, idea, belief  
2 N-SING （对某一问题的）看法，考虑，思量 Your view of a particular subject is the way that you understand and think about it. （对某一问题的）看法，考虑，思量

  • The drama takes an idealistic, even a naive view of the subject. [+ of]

    这部戏在对待这一主题上有些理想化，甚至是天真。

  • The whole point was to get away from a Christian-centred view of religion. [+ of]

    其主旨就是要抛开以基督教为中心的宗教观。

  • In the old animistic world view, people believed that nature was organised by invisible souls.

    过去信仰万物有灵论的人们认为自然是由看不见的灵魂安排的。

3 VERB 考虑;看待 If you view something in a particular way, you think of it in that way. 考虑;看待 [Also V n + in]

  • First-generation Americans view the United States as a land of golden opportunity. [VERB noun + as]

    第一代美国人认为美国是一个充满了机遇的国度。

  • Abigail's mother Linda views her daughter's talent with a mixture of pride and worry. [VERB noun + with]

    阿比盖尔的母亲琳达带着一种又骄傲又担忧的复杂情绪看待女儿的才华。

  • Sectors in the economy can be viewed in a variety of ways. [V n with/in n]

    可以以多种方式看待经济的构成。

  • We would view favourably any sensible suggestion for maintaining the business. [VERB noun with adv.]

    我们欢迎为维持业务稳定而提出的所有合理建议。

SYN regard, see, consider, judge  
4 N-COUNT 景观;景色;风景;(尤指）美景 The view from a window or high place is everything which can be seen from that place, especially when it is considered to be beautiful. 景观;景色;风景;(尤指）美景

  • The view from our window was one of beautiful green countryside. [+ from]

    从我们的窗口能看见一片美丽的绿色田园风光。

  • Each of the rooms has a superb view of Pissouri Bay.

    每个房间都能将皮苏里湾的美景尽收眼底。

SYN scene, picture, sight, prospect  
5 N-SING 视线;视域;视野 If you have a view of something, you can see it. 视线;视域;视野

  • He stood up to get a better view of the blackboard. [+ of]

    为了更清楚地看到黑板上的内容，他站了起来。

  • He stopped in the doorway, blocking her view.

    他站在门口，挡住了她的视线。

6 N-UNCOUNT 在能看得见的地方/在（所有人）视线内 You use view in expressions to do with being able to see something. For example, if something is in view, you can see it. If something is in full view of everyone, everyone can see it. 在能看得见的地方/在（所有人）视线内

  • She was lying there in full view of anyone who walked by.

    她躺在那里，过往的人都能看见她。

  • A group of riders came into view on the dirt road.

    一群骑车的人出现在那条土路上。

  • On South Main Street, a huge brick building looms into view.

    在南大街上，一座巨大的砖砌建筑赫然出现在人们的视线中。

SYN vision, sight, visibility, perspective  
7 VERB 查看;察看 If you view something, you look at it for a particular purpose. 查看;察看 [formal]

  • They came back to view the house again. [VERB noun]

    他们又回来查看了一遍房子。

  • Twenty-five thousand mourners passed to view the body. [VERB noun]

    2.5万名送葬者前来瞻仰死者遗容。

SYN look at, see, inspect, gaze at  
8 VERB 看;收看 If you view a television programme, video, or film, you watch it. 看;收看 [formal]

  • We have viewed the video recording of the incident. [VERB noun]

    我们看过了该事件的录像。

  • 'Elizabeth R', a TV portrait of the Queen, had record viewing figures. [VERB-ing]

    关于女王的电视专题片《伊丽莎白女王》创下了收视纪录。

9 N-UNCOUNT （文本或图表在计算机屏幕上的）显示方式 View refers to the way in which a piece of text or graphics is displayed on a computer screen. （文本或图表在计算机屏幕上的）显示方式 [computing]

  • To see the current document in full-page view, click the Page Zoom Full button.

    要完整显示当前文件，请按“全屏显示”按钮。PRHASES

10 PHRASE 对…评价不高;对…持否定看法 If you take a dim view or a poor view of someone or something, you disapprove of them or have a low opinion of them. 对…评价不高;对…持否定看法

  • They took a dim view of local trade unionists.

    他们对当地的工会会员们评价很低。

  • The judge took a dim view and I spent six years in prison.

    法官对此并不赞成，我因此蹲了6年的监狱。

11 PHRASE 在我看来;按我的看法 You use in my view when you want to indicate that you are stating a personal opinion, which other people might not agree with. 在我看来;按我的看法

  • In my view things won't change.

    依我看，事情不会改变。

  • There is, in my view, a simple explanation.

    在我看来，有一个简单的解释。

  • It's not cheating in my view.

    按我的看法，这不是欺骗。

12 PREP-PHRASE 鉴于;由于;考虑到 You use in view of when you are taking into consideration facts that have just been mentioned or are just about to be mentioned. 鉴于;由于;考虑到

  • In view of the fact that Hobson was not a trained economist his achievements were remarkable.

    考虑到霍布森不是科班出身的经济学家，他可谓是成就非凡。

  • In view of this, the decision may not be easy.

    考虑到这点，可能很难作出决定。

SYN considering, taking into account, in the light of, bearing in mind  
13 PHRASE 将…作为目标 If you have something in view, you are aware of it and your actions are aimed towards it. 将…作为目标

  • They have very clear career aims in view.

    他们有非常明确的职业目标。

  • Ackroyd worked out this whole plot with one objective in view.

    阿克罗伊德制订出这一整套秘密计划就是为了达到一个目的。

14 PHRASE 从长远来看;作长远打算 If you take the long view, you consider what is likely to happen in the future over a long period, rather than thinking only about things that are going to happen soon. 从长远来看;作长远打算 [Also + of]

  • Some investors are taking the long view.

    一些投资者是从长远角度考虑的。

  • Taking a long view of the project, I began to think in terms of the rehearsal schedules required.

    为了对这个项目作长远打算，我开始根据要求的排练计划考虑问题。

15 PHRASE 在展出;陈列着 If something such as a work of art is on view, it is shown in public for people to look at. 在展出;陈列着

  • A significant exhibition of contemporary sculpture will be on view at the Portland Gallery.

    一场关于当代雕塑的重要展览即将在波特兰美术馆开展。

SYN on show, showing, displayed, on display  
16 PHRASE 为了;以便 If you do something with a view to doing something else, you do it because you hope it will result in that other thing being done. 为了;以便

  • He has called a meeting of all parties tomorrow, with a view to forming a national reconciliation government.

    为了组建一个协调各方利益的全国性联合政府，他已召集所有党派参加明天的会议。

SYN with the aim or intention of, in order to, so as to, in the hope of
