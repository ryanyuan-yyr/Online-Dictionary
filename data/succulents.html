succulent
sʌkjʊlənt ˈsʌkjulənt
Word forms: plural succulents
1 ADJ-GRADED (尤指蔬菜及肉类)汁多味美的 Succulent food, especially meat or vegetables, is juicy and good to eat. (尤指蔬菜及肉类)汁多味美的 [approval]

  • Cook pieces of succulent chicken with ample garlic and a little sherry.

    烹饪鲜美滑嫩的鸡肉时多加些大蒜，并滴入少许雪利酒。

  • ...succulent early vegetables.

    新鲜多汁的早熟蔬菜

SYN juicy, moist, luscious, rich  
2 N-COUNT 肉质植物(叶子肥厚的植物) Succulents or succulent plants are types of plants which have thick, fleshy leaves. 肉质植物(叶子肥厚的植物)

  • His potted succulents were looking parched.

    他盆栽的肉质植物看起来已经干枯了。
