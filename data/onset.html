onset
ɒnset ˈɑnˌsɛt
N-SING （尤指不快之事的）开始，开端，肇始 The onset of something is the beginning of it, used especially to refer to something unpleasant. （尤指不快之事的）开始，开端，肇始

  • Most of the passes have been closed with the onset of winter. [+ of]

    冬天来临，大部分关口均已关闭。

  • With the onset of war, oil prices climbed past $30 a barrel.

    战争一爆发，油价就攀过了每桶30美元。
