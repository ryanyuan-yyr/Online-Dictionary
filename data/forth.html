forth
fɔːʳθ fɔrθ foʊrθ

  • In addition to the uses shown below, forth is also used in the phrasal verbs ‘put forth’ and ‘set forth’.

    除下列用法外，forth 还可用于短语动词 put forth 和 set forth 中。

1 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 向外，往外(走) When someone goes forth from a place, they leave it. 向外，往外(走) [literary]

  • Go forth into the desert.

    向前走进沙漠。

  • I came forth to take the air.

    我出来透透气。

SYN forward, out, away, ahead  
2 ADV [ADVERB after verb] 产生;得出 If one thing brings forth another, the first thing produces the second. 产生;得出 [literary]

  • Nature herself brings forth new forms of life.

    自然本身会创造出新的生命形态。

  • My reflections brought forth no conclusion.

    我的思考没有得出结论。

3 ADV [ADVERB after verb] (带)来;(拿)出;(现)出 When someone or something is brought forth, they are brought to a place or moved into a position where people can see them. (带)来;(拿)出;(现)出 [literary]

  • Pilate ordered Jesus to be brought forth.

    彼拉多命令把耶稣带上来。

  • He brought forth a small gold amulet from beneath his robe.

    他从袍子下面拿出一个不大的金护身符。

SYN out, into the open, out of concealment
