undernourished
ʌndəʳnʌrɪʃt ˌʌndəˈnʌrɪʃt -nɜːr-
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 营养不足的；营养不良的 If someone is undernourished, they are weak and unhealthy because they have not been eating enough food or the right kind of food. 营养不足的；营养不良的

  • People who are undernourished also lack reserves of energy when faced with physical or mental crises.

    营养不良的人在体力或脑力上出现紧急情况时还缺乏足够的能量储备。

  • ...undernourished children.

    营养不良的孩子们

SYN malnourished, starving, underfed
