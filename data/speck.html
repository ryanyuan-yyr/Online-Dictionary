speck
spek spɛk
Word forms: plural specks
1 N-COUNT 污点;痕迹;斑点;小块 A speck is a very small stain, mark, or shape. 污点;痕迹;斑点;小块

  • ...a speck of blood. [+ of]

    一点血迹

2 N-COUNT (粉末状物质的)微粒 A speck is a very small piece of a powdery substance. (粉末状物质的)微粒

  • Billy leaned forward and brushed a speck of dust off his shoes. [+ of]

    比利俯身擦掉了鞋子上的一丝灰尘。

SYN particle, bit, grain, dot
