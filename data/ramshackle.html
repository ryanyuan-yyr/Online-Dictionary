ramshackle
ræmʃækəl ˈræmˌʃækəl
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （建筑）破烂不堪的，摇摇欲坠的 A ramshackle building is badly made or in bad condition, and looks as if it is likely to fall down. （建筑）破烂不堪的，摇摇欲坠的

  • They entered the shop, which was a curious ramshackle building.

    他们进了商店，那是幢年久失修的奇特建筑。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （系统、联盟、收集物等）草率组合成的，拼凑的 A ramshackle system, union, or collection of things has been put together without much thought and is not likely to work very well. （系统、联盟、收集物等）草率组合成的，拼凑的

  • They joined with a ramshackle alliance of other rebels.

    他们和一个由其他反叛者草草拼凑成的同盟联起手来。

  • ...the present tax system, which the opposition says is ramshackle and complicated.

    被反对派批评为草率且复杂的现行税收体系
