roll call
(also roll-call)Word forms: plural roll calls
1 N-VAR 点名 If you take a roll call, you check which of the members of a group are present by reading their names out. 点名

  • We had to stand in the snow every morning for roll call.

    我们每天早上都得站在雪地里等着点名。

2 N-SING 名单;名册 A roll call of a particular type of people or things is a list of them. 名单;名册 [journalism]

  • Her list of pupils read like a roll-call of the great and good. [+ of]

    她的学生名单看起来像一本大人物名册。
