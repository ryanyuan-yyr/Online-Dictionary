turf war
(also turf battle)Word forms: plural turf wars
1 N-COUNT 黑帮火拼;帮派争夺地盘的争斗 A turf war is a struggle between criminals or gangs over who controls a particular area. 黑帮火拼;帮派争夺地盘的争斗 [mainly journalism] [Also + over]

  • The estate is at the centre of a bitter turf war between rival drug gangs. [+ between]

    该地产正处于两伙敌对的毒品犯罪团伙激烈火拼的中心地带。

2 N-COUNT 争权夺势;争夺控制权 A turf war is a struggle between people over who controls a particular activity. 争权夺势;争夺控制权 [mainly journalism] [Also + over]

  • Both sides say this is more than just a turf war between big and small banks. [+ between]

    双方都称这不仅仅是大小银行争地盘这么简单的问题。
