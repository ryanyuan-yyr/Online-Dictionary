preset
priːset prɪˈsɛt ˈpriˌsɛt priˈsɛt
(also pre-set)Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense presets, present participle presetting
The form preset is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle. preset的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
VERB [usually passive] 预先设置，预调（设备） If a piece of equipment is preset, its controls have been set in advance of the time you want it to work. 预先设置，预调（设备）

  • ...a computerised timer that can be preset to a variety of programs. [be VERB-ed]

    可以预设各种程序的电脑定时器

  • Bake the cake in a preset oven. [VERB-ed]

    在预先设置好的烤箱中烤蛋糕
