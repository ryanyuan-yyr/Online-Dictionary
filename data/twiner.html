twine
twaɪn twaɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense twines, present participle twining, past tense , past participle twined
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤用于园艺和农事的)麻线，合股线 Twine is strong string used especially in gardening and farming. (尤用于园艺和农事的)麻线，合股线
2 V-ERG (使)缠绕;(使)盘绕;(使)环绕 If you twine one thing around another, or if one thing twines around another, the first thing is twisted or wound around the second. (使)缠绕;(使)盘绕;(使)环绕

  • He had twined his chubby arms around Vincent's neck. [VERB noun prep.]

    他胖乎乎的双臂搂住文森特的脖子。

  • He twined his fingers into hers… [VERB noun prep.]

    他和她十指相扣。

  • These strands of molecules twine around each other to form cable-like structures. [VERB prep.]

    这些分子链彼此缠绕，形成缆线状的结构。

SYN twist together, weave, knit, braid
