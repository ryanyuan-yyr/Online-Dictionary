backbench
bækbentʃ ˈbækbɛntʃ
ADJ [ADJ n] 后座（议员）的;普通（议员）的 A backbench MP is a Member of Parliament who is not a minister and who does not hold an official position in his or her political party. 后座（议员）的;普通（议员）的 [British, Australian]

  • ...the Conservative backbench MP Sir Teddy Taylor.

    保守党下院普通议员泰迪·泰勒爵士

  • Such a motion is considered a test of backbench opinion.

    这样的动议被视为对后座议员立场的考验。
