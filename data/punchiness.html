punchy
pʌntʃi ˈpʌntʃi
Word forms: comparative punchier, superlative punchiest
ADJ-GRADED 简洁有力的；言简意赅的 If you describe something as punchy, you mean that it expresses its meaning in a forceful or effective way. 简洁有力的；言简意赅的

  • A good way to sound confident is to use short punchy sentences.

    想要听起来很自信，使用简短有力的句子不失为一个好方法。

SYN effective, spirited, dynamic, lively
