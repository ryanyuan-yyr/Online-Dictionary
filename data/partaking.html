partake
pɑːʳteɪk pɑrˈteɪk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense partakes, present participle partaking, past tense partook, past participle partaken
1 VERB 吃；喝；享用 If you partake of food or drink, you eat or drink some of it. 吃；喝；享用 [formal]

  • They were happy to partake of our feast, but not to share our company. [VERB + of]

    他们喜欢享用我们准备的丰盛大餐，但是不喜欢和我们呆在一起。

2 VERB 参加；参与 If you partake in an activity, you take part in it. 参加；参与 [formal]

  • You will probably be asked about whether you partake in very vigorous sports. [VERB + in]

    你可能会被问到是否参加非常剧烈的体育运动。

3 VERB 在某种程度上具有（某种特性） If something partakes of a particular quality, it has that quality to some extent. 在某种程度上具有（某种特性） [formal]

  • Miracles are not generally regarded as magical. Nevertheless, they do partake of the same nature. [VERB of noun]

    奇迹一般不会被认为是魔法使然，不过二者在本质上有点相似。
