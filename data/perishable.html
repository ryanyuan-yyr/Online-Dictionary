perishable
perɪʃəbəl ˈpɛrɪʃəbəl
ADJ （食物)易腐烂的，易变质的 Goods such as food that are perishable go bad after quite a short length of time. （食物)易腐烂的，易变质的

  • ...perishable food like fruit, vegetables and meat.

    水果、蔬菜、肉类等易变质的食品

  • Raw eggs are highly perishable and must be chilled before and after cooking.

    生鸡蛋非常容易变质，烹制前后必须冷藏。

SYN short-lived, biodegradable, easily spoilt, decomposable
