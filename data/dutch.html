dutch
dʌtʃ
1 ADJ 荷兰的;荷兰语的;荷兰人的 Dutch means belonging or relating to the Netherlands, or to its people, language, or culture. 荷兰的;荷兰语的;荷兰人的

  • ...the Dutch prime minister.

    荷兰首相

2 N-PLURAL 荷兰人 The Dutch are the people of the Netherlands. 荷兰人

  • The Dutch developed a custom by which children put out shoes which Saint Nicholas would fill with gifts when he came visiting.

    荷兰人有一套自己的风俗，孩子们会将鞋子放在外面，圣诞老人到来时便会在里面装满礼品。

3 N-UNCOUNT 荷兰语 Dutch is the language that is spoken by the people who live in the Netherlands. 荷兰语
4 PHRASE 各付各的账;平摊费用;AA 制 If two or more people go Dutch, each of them pays their own bill, for example in a restaurant. 各付各的账;平摊费用;AA 制

  • We went Dutch on a meal in the new restaurant in Shaftesbury Avenue.

    我们在沙夫茨伯里大道一家新开的饭馆里AA制吃的饭。
