dash off
1 PHRASAL VERB 迅速去做If you dash off to a place, you go there very quickly. 迅速去做

  • He dashed off to lunch at the Hard Rock Cafe. [VERB PREP. + to]

    他急匆匆地到硬石餐馆去吃午饭。

2 PHRASAL VERB 匆匆地写If you dash off a piece of writing, you write or compose it very quickly, without thinking about it very much. 匆匆地写

  • He dashed off a couple of novels. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他匆匆地完成了几本小说。
