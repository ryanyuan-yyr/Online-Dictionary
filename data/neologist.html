neologism
niːələdʒɪzəm niɒl- niˈɑləˌdʒɪzəm
Word forms: plural neologisms
N-COUNT 新词;新义 A neologism is a new word or expression in a language, or a new meaning for an existing word or expression. 新词;新义 [technical]

  • The newspaper used the neologism 'dinks', Double Income No Kids.

    报纸使用了新词“丁克族”，即“双收入无子女”。

SYN new word, buzz word [informal] , coinage, new phrase
