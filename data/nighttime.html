night-time
(also night time)
N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 夜间；夜里 Night-time is the period of time between when it gets dark and when the sun rises. 夜间；夜里

  • They wanted someone responsible to look after the place at night-time.

    他们想要个有责任心的人夜间照管这个地方。

  • A twelve hour night time curfew is in force.

    正在执行12小时的宵禁。
