widowed
wɪdoʊd ˈwɪdəʊd
V-PASSIVE 使丧偶 If someone is widowed, their husband or wife dies. 使丧偶

  • More and more young men are widowed by cancer. [be VERB-ed]

    越来越多的青年男子被癌症夺去了妻子。

  • She was widowed in 1967. [be VERB-ed]

    1967年她成了寡妇。

  • Imogen stayed with her widowed sister. [VERB-ed]

    伊莫金和她守寡的姐姐住在一起。
