baptize
bæptaɪz bæpˈtaɪz ˈbæptaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense baptizes, present participle baptizing, past tense , past participle baptized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 baptise
VERB [usually passive] 给…施行洗礼（或浸礼） When someone is baptized, water is put on their heads or they are covered with water as a sign that their sins have been forgiven and that they have become a member of the Christian Church. 给…施行洗礼（或浸礼）

  • At this time she decided to become a Christian and was baptised. [be VERB-ed]

    她此时决定成为基督教徒并接受了浸礼。

  • I think your mother was baptized a Catholic.

    我想你母亲受洗成了天主教徒。

SYN christen, cleanse, immerse, purify
