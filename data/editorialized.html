editorialize
edɪtɔːriəlaɪz ˌɛdɪˈtɔriˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense editorializes, present participle editorializing, past tense , past participle editorialized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 editorialise
VERB （主要指记者和报纸）发表社论，作出主观评论 If someone editorializes, they express their opinion about something rather than just stating facts; mainly used in contexts where you are talking about journalists and newspapers. （主要指记者和报纸）发表社论，作出主观评论

  • Other papers have editorialized, criticizing the Czech government for rushing to judgment on this individual. [VERB]

    其他报纸已经发表了社论，谴责捷克政府对这一独立的个体草率下定论。
