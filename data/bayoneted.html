bayonet
beɪənət ˌbeɪəˈnɛt ˈbeɪənət
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bayonets, present participle bayoneting, past tense , past participle bayoneted
1 N-COUNT 刺刀 A bayonet is a long, sharp blade that can be fixed to the end of a rifle and used as a weapon. 刺刀
SYN sword, knife, blade, lance  
2 VERB 用刺刀刺 To bayonet someone means to push a bayonet into them. 用刺刀刺

  • The soldiers were ordered by their inhuman officers to bayonet every man they could find. [VERB noun]

    毫无人性的军官们命令士兵见人就刺。

SYN stab, cut, wound, knife
