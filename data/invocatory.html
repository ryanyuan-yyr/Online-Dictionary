invocation
ɪnvəkeɪʃən ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃən ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃən
Word forms: plural invocations
1 N-VAR (向神灵)求助,祈求宽恕;乞灵 An invocation is a request for help or forgiveness made to a god. (向神灵)求助,祈求宽恕;乞灵 [formal]

  • ...an invocation for divine guidance. [+ for]

    祈求神的指引

SYN appeal, request, petition, beseeching  
2 N-COUNT (公共集会的)祈祷，祷告 An invocation is a prayer at a public meeting, usually at the beginning. (公共集会的)祈祷，祷告 [US]

  • Please stand and pledge the flag, after which Dr. Jerome Taylor will give the invocation.

    请起立对旗宣誓,之后杰尔姆·泰勒博士将做祈祷。
