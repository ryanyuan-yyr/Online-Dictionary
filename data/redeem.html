redeem
rɪdiːm rɪˈdim
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redeems, present participle redeeming, past tense , past participle redeemed
1 VERB 挽回（声誉）；补偿，弥补（过错） If you redeem yourself or your reputation, you do something that makes people have a good opinion of you again after you have behaved or performed badly. 挽回（声誉）；补偿，弥补（过错）

  • He had realized the mistake he had made and wanted to redeem himself. [VERB noun]

    他已经认识到了自己的错误，想要进行弥补。

  • The sole redeeming feature of your behaviour is that you're not denying it. [VERB-ing]

    你的行为中唯一可取之处就是你没有否认错误。

SYN reinstate, vindicate, absolve, free from blame  
2 VERB 挽回;弥补;补救 When something redeems an unpleasant thing or situation, it prevents it from being completely bad. 挽回;弥补;补救

  • Work is the way that people seek to redeem their lives from futility. [VERB noun]

    工作可以避免人们虚度生命。

  • ...a long face with too prominent features that were redeemed by a fine pair of brown eyes. [VERB noun]

    脸部较长，五官很突出，还好一双棕色的眼睛长得恰到好处

  • Does this institution have any redeeming features? [VERB-ing]

    这个机构有什么可取之处吗？

SYN make up for, offset, make good, compensate for  
3 VERB 偿还;兑现;付清 If you redeem a debt or money that you have promised to someone, you pay money that you owe or that you promised to pay. 偿还;兑现;付清 [formal]

  • The amount required to redeem the mortgage was £358,587. [VERB noun]

    需要偿还的抵押贷款总计358,587英镑。

  • Take the voucher to your local branch of Woolworths and it will be redeemed for one toy. [VERB noun]

    持兑换券到当地沃尔沃斯的分店可以兑换一个玩具。

SYN pay off, clear, square, honour  
4 VERB 赎回 If you redeem an object that belongs to you, you get it back from someone by repaying them money that you borrowed from them, after using the object as a guarantee. 赎回

  • Make sure you know exactly what you will be paying back when you plan to redeem the item. [VERB noun]

    务必弄清楚将来赎回物品时需要支付多少钱。

SYN buy back, recover, regain, retrieve  
5 VERB （基督教的）拯救，救赎 In religions such as Christianity, to redeem someone means to save them by freeing them from sin and evil. （基督教的）拯救，救赎

  • ...a new female spiritual force to redeem the world. [VERB noun]

    全新的拯救世界的女性精神力量

SYN save, free, deliver, rescue
