clever
klevəʳ ˈklɛvər
Word forms: comparative cleverer, superlative cleverest
1 ADJ-GRADED 聪明的;聪颖的;智商高的 Someone who is clever is intelligent and able to understand things easily or plan things well. 聪明的;聪颖的;智商高的

  • He's a very clever man.

    他非常聪明。

  • My sister was always a lot cleverer than I was.

    我妹妹向来比我聪明许多。

  • Her mother was clever at many things.

    她妈妈对许多事情都很精通。

cleverly

  • She would cleverly pick up on what I said.

    她会非常聪慧地领悟我所说的话。

cleverness

  • Her cleverness seems to get in the way of her emotions.

    她好像聪明有余，激情不足。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (观点、书、发明)巧妙的，绝妙的 A clever idea, book, or invention is extremely effective and shows the skill of the people involved. (观点、书、发明)巧妙的，绝妙的

  • ...a clever and gripping novel.

    构思巧妙、扣人心弦的小说

  • A colleague of mine in Milan devised the following very clever little experiment.

    我在米兰的一个同事设计了下面这个非常巧妙的小实验。

  • ...this clever new gadget.

    这个构思巧妙的新奇小玩意儿

cleverly

  • ...a cleverly designed swimsuit.

    设计新颖独特的女式泳衣

3 PHRASE 聪明过头的;过分聪明的 If someone is too clever by half, they are very clever and they show their cleverness in a way that annoys other people. 聪明过头的;过分聪明的 [British, disapproval]

  • His many admirers describe him as clever: his enemies as too clever by half.

    崇拜他的人很多说他聪明;恨他的人则说他聪明过了头。
