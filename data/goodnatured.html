good-natured
ˈgʊdˈneɪtʃərd
ADJ-GRADED 性情温和的；友善的；温驯的 A good-natured person or animal is naturally friendly and does not get angry easily. 性情温和的；友善的；温驯的

  • Bates looks like a good-natured lad.

    贝茨看起来像个性情温和的小伙子。

  • He was good natured about it, he didn't fuss.

    他在这件事情上态度友善，没有大吵大闹。

SYN amiable, kind, kindly, friendly
