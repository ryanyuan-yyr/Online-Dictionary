martyred
mɑːʳtəʳd ˈmɑːtəd
ADJ [ADJ n] (为博取同情或赞赏而)假装可怜的，满脸苦相的 If you describe a person or their behaviour as martyred, you mean that they often exaggerate their suffering in order to gain sympathy or praise. (为博取同情或赞赏而)假装可怜的，满脸苦相的 [literary, disapproval]

  • 'As usual,' muttered his martyred wife.

    “老样子，”他那故作可怜的妻子嘟囔道。

  • You put on your martyred expression, sigh and say, 'If you really want to...'.

    你一脸可怜状，叹息道：“要是你真打算……”

  • ...with a lot of sighs, moans and a martyred air.

    不断叹息、呻吟，一副受苦受难的神情
