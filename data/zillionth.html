zillion
zɪljən ˈzɪljən
Word forms: plural zillions
NUM 极大的数目;庞大的数量 If you talk about a zillion people or things, you are emphasizing that there is an extremely large number of them. 极大的数目;庞大的数量 [informal, emphasis]

  • It's been a zillion years since I've seen her.

    我已经有很多年没见过她了。

You can also use zillions as a quantifier.

  • There are zillions of things to look at or try out.

    有无数的事情需要考虑，或进行尝试。
