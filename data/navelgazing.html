navel-gazing
N-UNCOUNT 自我耽溺;冥思苦想;钻牛角尖 If you refer to an activity as navel-gazing, you are critical of it because people are thinking about something for a long time but take no action on it. 自我耽溺;冥思苦想;钻牛角尖 [disapproval]

  • She dismisses the reform process as an exercise in collective navel gazing.

    她对这场改革嗤之以鼻，认为只是无实际行动的集体空想而已。
