go down as
PHRASAL VERB 被认为；被记作；被记录为 If you say that an event or action will go down as a particular thing, you mean that it will be regarded, remembered, or recorded as that thing. 被认为；被记作；被记录为

  • It will go down as one of the highlights of my career. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    这将成为我事业的一个巅峰。

  • The riots of the early '80s must go down as a significant event in British history. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    80年代早期的骚乱势必成为英国历史上的重大事件。
