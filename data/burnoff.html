burn off
1 PHRASAL VERB 消耗；耗费 If someone burns off energy, they use it. 消耗；耗费 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • This will improve your performance and help you burn off calories. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这将会改善你的表现，并帮你消耗卡路里。

2 PHRASAL VERB 烧掉；烧除 To burn off something unwanted means to get rid of it by burning it. 烧掉；烧除 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The bushfire actually helped to burn off a lot of dead undergrowth. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    林区大火实际上有助于清除很多枯死的矮灌木。
