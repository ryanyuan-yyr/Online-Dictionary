snooty
snuːti ˈsnuti
Word forms: comparative snootier, superlative snootiest
ADJ-GRADED 傲慢自大的;自以为高人一等的 If you say that someone is snooty, you disapprove of them because they behave as if they are superior to other people. 傲慢自大的;自以为高人一等的 [disapproval]

  • ...snooty intellectuals.

    妄自尊大的知识分子

  • Everyone thought Annabel was being snooty.

    人人都认为安娜贝尔太目中无人。

SYN snobbish, superior, aloof, pretentious
