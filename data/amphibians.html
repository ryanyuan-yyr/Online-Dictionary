amphibian
æmfɪbiən æmˈfɪbiən
Word forms: plural amphibians
1 N-COUNT 两栖动物 Amphibians are animals such as frogs and toads that can live both on land and in water. 两栖动物
2 N-COUNT 水陆两用车 An amphibian is a vehicle which is able to move on both land and water, or an aeroplane which can land on both land and water. 水陆两用车
