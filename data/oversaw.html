oversee
oʊvəʳsiː ˌoʊvərˈsi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense oversees, present participle overseeing, past tense oversaw, past participle overseen
VERB 监督；监视；监管 If someone in authority oversees a job or an activity, they make sure that it is done properly. 监督；监视；监管

  • Use a surveyor or architect to oversee and inspect the different stages of the work. [VERB noun]

    请一位房产检视员或建筑师来监督并检查不同阶段的工作。

  • ...the agreement to set up a commission to oversee the peace process. [VERB noun]

    设立委员会以监督和平进程的协议

SYN supervise, run, control, manage
