inertia
ɪnɜːʳʃə ɪnˈɜːʃə ɪnˈɜrʃə ɪnˈɜrʃiə
1 N-UNCOUNT 惰性;迟钝;怠惰 If you have a feeling of inertia, you feel very lazy and unwilling to move or be active. 惰性;迟钝;怠惰

  • ...her inertia, her lack of energy.

    她的怠惰和缺乏活力

  • This might help you overcome inertia.

    这可能有助于你克服惰性。

2 N-UNCOUNT 惯性 Inertia is the tendency of a physical object to remain still or to continue moving, unless a force is applied to it. 惯性 [technical]
