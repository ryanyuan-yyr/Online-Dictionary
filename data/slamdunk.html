slam dunk
(also slam-dunk)Word forms: slam dunks
1 N-COUNT [usu sing: v-link a N] 轻而易举的事If you say that something is a slam dunk, you mean that a success or victory will be easily achieved. 轻而易举的事 [informal]

  • So it's an easy decision. It's a slam dunk.

    所以说做这个决定很容易。这是件轻而易举的事情。

2 N-COUNT 扣篮In basketball, a slam dunk is a shot in which a player jumps up and forces the ball through the basket. 扣篮

  • ...a series of spectacular slam dunks.

    ...接二连三的漂亮扣篮。
