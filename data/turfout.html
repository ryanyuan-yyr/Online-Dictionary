turf out
PHRASAL VERB 撵走;逐出;轰走 If someone is turfed out of a place or position, they are forced to leave. 撵走;逐出;轰走 [British, informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • We hear stories of people being turfed out and ending up on the streets. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    我们听说了人们被赶出去流落街头的故事。

  • The party was turfed out of office after 15 years. [be V-ed P + of]

    该党在 15 年后被赶下了台。

  • ...the right wing landslide which has turfed out the Socialist government. [VERB PREP. noun]

    右翼取得了把社会党政府赶下台的压倒性胜利

SYN throw out, evict, cast out, kick out [informal]
