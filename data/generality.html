generality
dʒenərælɪti ˌdʒɛnərˈæləti
Word forms: plural generalities
1 N-COUNT 概括；概述；总述 A generality is a general statement that covers a range of things, rather than being concerned with specific instances. 概括；概述；总述 [formal]

  • I'll start with some generalities and then examine a few specific examples.

    我首先进行概述，然后会分析几个实例。

  • He avoided this tricky question and talked in generalities.

    他回避了这个非常微妙的问题，只做了个笼统的表述。

SYN generalization, abstraction, sweeping statement, vague notion  
2 N-UNCOUNT 一般性；普遍性；笼统 The generality of a statement or description is the fact that it is a general one, rather than a specific, detailed one. 一般性；普遍性；笼统

  • That there are problems with this kind of definition is hardly surprising, given its level of generality.

    由于其笼统性，不难料想这种定义会存在问题。

SYN impreciseness, vagueness, looseness, lack of detail  
3 QUANT [QUANT of n] 主体；大多数 If someone refers to the generality of a group of people, they mean the majority of that group. 主体；大多数 [formal]

  • When the generality of the electorate is doing badly, the mood will grow pessimistic.

    当多数选民都不予以支持时，情绪就会变得悲观起来。
