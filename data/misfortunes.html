misfortune
mɪsfɔːʳtʃuːn mɪsˈfɔrtʃən
Word forms: plural misfortunes
N-VAR 不幸;灾祸 A misfortune is something unpleasant or unlucky that happens to someone. 不幸;灾祸

  • She seemed to enjoy the misfortunes of others. [+ of]

    她似乎喜欢幸灾乐祸。

  • He had his full share of misfortune.

    他吃尽了苦头。

SYN bad luck, adversity, hard luck, ill luck
