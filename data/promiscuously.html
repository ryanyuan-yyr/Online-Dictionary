promiscuous
prəmɪskjuəs proʊˈmɪskjuəs prəˈmɪskjuəs
1 ADJ-GRADED 与多人发生性关系的；淫乱的；滥交的 Someone who is promiscuous has sex with many different people. 与多人发生性关系的；淫乱的；滥交的 [disapproval]

  • She is perceived as vain, spoilt and promiscuous.

    她被认为是个被人惯坏了的、既虚荣又淫乱的女人。

  • You know the risks of promiscuous sex.

    你知道滥交的危险。

SYN licentious, wanton, profligate, debauched  
promiscuity

  • He has recently urged more tolerance of sexual promiscuity.

    最近他呼吁给予淫乱行为更多的宽容。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 混杂的 Promiscuous means including a wide range of different things. 混杂的 [formal]

  • ...the dazzling, promiscuous display of new styles.

    各种新的风格混杂在一起，令人眼花缭乱

  • ...fifty years of promiscuous reading in pursuit of pleasure.

    50年来博览群书以图一乐

SYN mixed, confused, disordered, diverse
