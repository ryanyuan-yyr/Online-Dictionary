unsettled
ʌnsetəld ʌnˈsɛtəld
1 ADJ-GRADED 多变的；不安定的；不平稳的；动荡不安的 In an unsettled situation, there is a lot of uncertainty about what will happen. 多变的；不安定的；不平稳的；动荡不安的

  • Britain's unsettled political scene also worries some investors.

    英国动荡不安的政治局面也让一些投资者担心不已。

  • The junk market has been unsettled for the past seven months.

    过去7个月中废旧货市场一直起伏不定。

SYN unstable, shaky, insecure, disorderly  
2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 不能集中精力的 If you are unsettled, you cannot concentrate on anything because you are worried. 不能集中精力的

  • A lot of people wake up every day with a sense of being unsettled and disturbed.

    很多人每天起床的时候都感到无法集中精力、心烦意乱。

  • To tell the truth, I'm a bit unsettled tonight.

    实话告诉你，我今晚有点儿心神不宁。

SYN restless, tense, uneasy, troubled  
3 ADJ （争论等）未解决的，悬而未决的 An unsettled argument or dispute has not yet been resolved. （争论等）未解决的，悬而未决的

  • They were in the process of resolving all the unsettled issues.

    他们正在着手解决所有悬而未决的问题。

  • Can one even talk of stability in the Middle East as long as the conflict is still unsettled?

    冲突尚未解决，又何谈中东的稳定呢？

SYN unresolved, undecided, undetermined, open  
4 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 渺无人烟的；荒芜的 Unsettled places are places where no people have yet lived. 渺无人烟的；荒芜的

  • Until very recently Texas was an unsettled frontier.

    就在没多久之前，得克萨斯州还是一个荒无人烟的边远地区。

SYN uninhabited, unoccupied, unpopulated, unpeopled  
5 ADJ-GRADED 不可预测的；易变的 Unsettled weather is unpredictable and changes a lot. 不可预测的；易变的

  • Despite the unsettled weather, we had a marvellous weekend.

    尽管天气一会儿好一会儿差，我们仍然度过了一个美好的周末。

SYN inconstant, changing, unpredictable, variable
