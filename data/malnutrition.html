malnutrition
mælnjuːtrɪʃən ˌmælnjuːˈtrɪʃən -nuːt- ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 营养不良；营养失调 If someone is suffering from malnutrition, they are physically weak and extremely thin because they have not eaten enough food. 营养不良；营养失调

  • Infections are more likely in those suffering from malnutrition.

    营养不良的人更有可能被传染。

SYN undernourishment, hunger, famine, starvation
