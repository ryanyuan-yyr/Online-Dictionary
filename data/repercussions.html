repercussion
riːpəʳkʌʃən ˌripərˈkʌʃən ˌ rɛpərˈkʌʃən
Word forms: plural repercussions
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] (不良)反响，反应，影响 If an action or event has repercussions, it causes unpleasant things to happen some time after the original action or event. (不良)反响，反应，影响 [formal]

  • It was an effort which was to have painful repercussions.

    这么做会带来极坏的负面影响。

  • Members of congress were warned of possible repercussions if their vote went through.

    国会议员被警告如果他们的投票通过可能会带来不良影响。

SYN consequences, result, side effects, backlash
