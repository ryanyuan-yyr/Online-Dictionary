reissue
riːɪʃuː ˌriːˈɪʃjuː riˈɪʃu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense reissues, present participle reissuing, past tense , past participle reissued
1 N-COUNT (书的)重印，再版;(唱片的)重新发行;(电影的)重映 A reissue is a book, CD, or film that has not been available for some time but is now published or produced again. (书的)重印，再版;(唱片的)重新发行;(电影的)重映

  • ...this welcome reissue of a 1955 Ingmar Bergman classic.

    英马尔·贝里曼1955年经典作品这次可喜的重映

SYN rerelease, relaunch  
2 VERB [usually passive] 重印;重新发行;重映 If something such as a book, CD, or film is reissued after it has not been available for some time, it is published or produced again. 重印;重新发行;重映

  • Her novels have just been reissued with eye-catching new covers. [be VERB-ed]

    她的小说刚进行了重印，新封面非常抢眼。

SYN rerelease
