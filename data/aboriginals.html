aboriginal
æbərɪdʒɪnəl ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənəl
Word forms: plural aboriginals
1 N-COUNT 澳大利亚土著居民 An Aboriginal is an Australian Aborigine. 澳大利亚土著居民

  • He remained fascinated by the Aboriginals' tales.

    他一直对澳大利亚土著居民的传说极感兴趣。

SYN original inhabitant, native, aborigine, indigene  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 澳大利亚土著居民的 Aboriginal means belonging or relating to the Australian Aborigines. 澳大利亚土著居民的

  • ...Aboriginal art.

    澳大利亚土著艺术

  • ...a protest over Aboriginal land rights.

    为争取澳大利亚土著居民土地权的抗议

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 土著的;本土原有的 The aboriginal people or animals of a place are ones that have been there from the earliest known times or that were there before people or animals from other countries arrived. 土著的;本土原有的

  • Most Canadians acknowledge that the aboriginal people have had a rotten deal.

    大多数加拿大人承认土著居民遭受了不公平的待遇。

SYN indigenous, first, earliest, original
