backhand
bækhænd ˈbækˌhænd
Word forms: plural backhands
N-VAR （网球、壁球等运动中的）反手击球 A backhand is a shot in tennis or squash, which you make with your arm across your body. （网球、壁球等运动中的）反手击球

  • Agassi hit a backhand.

    阿加西反手一击。

  • She practised her backhand.

    她练习反手击球。
