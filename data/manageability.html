manageable
mænɪdʒəbəl ˈmænɪdʒəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 容易管理的;容易对付的 Something that is manageable is of a size, quantity, or level of difficulty that people are able to deal with. 容易管理的;容易对付的

  • He will now try to cut down the task to a manageable size.

    他现在要努力把任务的规模降至可控的范围。

  • The present flow of refugees was manageable.

    目前难民的流动尚可控制。

  • Keep your spending on luxuries down to manageable proportions.

    将你在奢侈品上的花费降至可控范围。
