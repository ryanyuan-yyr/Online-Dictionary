found
faʊnd faʊnd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense founds, present participle founding, past tense , past participle founded
1 Found is the past tense and past participle of find .
2 VERB 建立;成立;创立 When an institution, company, or organization is founded by someone or by a group of people, they get it started, often by providing the necessary money. 建立;成立;创立

  • The Independent Labour Party was founded in Bradford on January 13, 1893. [be VERB-ed]

    独立工党1893年1月13日在布拉德福德成立。

  • He founded the Centre for Journalism Studies at University College Cardiff. [VERB noun]

    他在加的夫大学学院成立了新闻学研究中心。

  • The business, founded by Dawn and Nigel, suffered financial setbacks. [VERB-ed]

    唐和奈杰尔创办的企业在资金上遇到了一些问题。

SYN establish, start, set up, begin  
foundation

  • ...the 150th anniversary of the foundation of Kew Gardens.

    基尤花园建成 150 周年

founding

  • I have been a member of The Sunday Times Wine Club since its founding in 1973.

    自从 1973 年《星期日泰晤士报》葡萄酒俱乐部成立之日起，我就是其会员了。

3 VERB [usually passive] 建成;兴建 When a town, important building, or other place is founded by someone or by a group of people, they cause it to be built. 建成;兴建

  • The town was founded in 1610. [be VERB-ed]

    这个镇建于 1610 年。

SYN erect, build, construct, raise
