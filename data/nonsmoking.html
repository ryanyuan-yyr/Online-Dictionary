non-smoking
(also nonsmoking)
1 ADJ (公共场所)禁止吸烟的 A non-smoking area in a public place is an area in which people are not allowed to smoke. (公共场所)禁止吸烟的

  • More and more restaurants are providing non-smoking areas.

    越来越多的餐厅开始辟出无烟区。

2 ADJ (人)不吸烟的 A non-smoking person is a person who does not smoke. (人)不吸烟的

  • The fertility of women who smoke is half that of non-smoking women.

    吸烟女士的生育能力是不吸烟女士的一半。
