anaesthetic
ænɪsθetɪk ˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk
(also anesthetic)Word forms: plural anaesthetics
N-MASS [oft under N] 麻醉药；麻醉剂 Anaesthetic is a substance that doctors use to stop you feeling pain during an operation, either in the whole of your body when you are unconscious, or in a part of your body when you are awake. 麻醉药；麻醉剂

  • The operation is carried out under a general anaesthetic.

    该手术是在全身麻醉情况下实施的。

SYN painkiller, narcotic, sedative, opiate
