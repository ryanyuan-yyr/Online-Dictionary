unsettling
ʌnsetəlɪŋ ʌnˈsɛtəlɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 令人担忧的；令人不安的 If you describe something as unsettling, you mean that it makes you feel rather worried or uncertain. 令人担忧的；令人不安的

  • The prospect of change of this kind has an unsettling effect on any organisation.

    这种变化的前景对任何组织都有不确定的影响。

  • His sense of humour was really unsettling.

    他的幽默感确实令人不安。

SYN disturbing, worrying, troubling, upsetting  
unsettlingly

  • It was unsettlingly quiet.

    静得令人不安。
