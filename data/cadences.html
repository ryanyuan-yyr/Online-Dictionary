cadence
keɪdəns ˈkeɪdəns
Word forms: plural cadences
1 N-COUNT 抑扬顿挫 The cadence of someone's voice is the way their voice gets higher and lower as they speak. 抑扬顿挫 [formal]

  • He recognized the Polish cadences in her voice.

    他从她的口音中听出了波兰腔。

  • He is not attempting necessarily to reproduce the cadence of speech.

    他不想刻意重复那种讲话的语调。

SYN intonation, accent, inflection, modulation  
2 N-COUNT (音乐的)终止式,结尾和弦 A cadence is the phrase that ends a section of music or a complete piece of music. (音乐的)终止式,结尾和弦
