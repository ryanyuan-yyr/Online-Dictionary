peeve
piv piːv piv
Word forms: peeves
N-COUNT 怨恨; 触怒If something is your peeve or your pet peeve, it makes you particularly irritated or angry. 怨恨; 触怒 [US]

  • The unsolicited ads appear prominently on the screen and are a pet peeve for many users.

    不请自来的广告显眼地出现在屏幕上，对于许多使用者来说是个恼人的事。

  • We all have our irrational peeves.

    我们都有非理性的怨恨。
