egomaniac
iːgoʊmeɪniæk eg-
Word forms: plural egomaniacs
1 N-COUNT 利己主义者;自我主义者 An egomaniac is someone who thinks only of themselves and does not care if they harm other people in order to get what they want. 利己主义者;自我主义者 [disapproval]

  • Adam is clever enough, but he's also something of an egomaniac.

    亚当足够聪明，但是他也有点自私自利。

SYN egotist, boaster, swaggerer, self-seeker
