answer back
PHRASAL VERB （尤指小孩）回嘴，顶嘴 If someone, especially a child, answers back, they speak rudely to you when you speak to them. （尤指小孩）回嘴，顶嘴

  • She was punished by teachers for answering back. [VERB PREP.]

    她由于顶嘴而受到了老师的惩罚。

  • I always answered him back when I thought he was wrong. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我觉得他说得不对时总是要顶他几句。
