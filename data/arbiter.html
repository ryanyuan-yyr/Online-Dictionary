arbiter
ɑːʳbɪtəʳ ˈɑrbətər
Word forms: plural arbiters
1 N-COUNT 仲裁人;公断人 An arbiter is a person or institution that judges and settles a quarrel between two other people or groups. 仲裁人;公断人 [formal]

  • He was the ultimate arbiter on both theological and political matters.

    他在神学和政治事务上都是最终的仲裁人。

  • ...the court's role as arbiter in the law-making process.

    法庭在制定法律过程中担任裁决机构的角色

SYN judge, referee, umpire, umpie [Australian , slang]  
2 N-COUNT （时尚或风格的）权威，决定者 An arbiter of taste or style is someone who has a lot of influence in deciding what is fashionable or socially desirable. （时尚或风格的）权威，决定者 [formal]

  • Sequins have often aroused the scorn of arbiters of taste.

    闪光装饰片经常引起时尚引领者的鄙视。

SYN authority, expert, master, governor
