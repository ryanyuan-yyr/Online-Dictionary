comrade-in-arms ˌkɒmreɪdɪnˈɑːmz
(also comrade in arms)Word forms: plural comrades-in-arms
N-COUNT [oft poss NOUN] 共患难的同志；战友 A comrade-in-arms is someone who has worked for the same cause or purpose as you and has shared the same difficulties and dangers. 共患难的同志；战友

  • Mr Meridor's father was a comrade-in-arms of the party's founder.

    梅里多先生的父亲是该党创始人的战友。
