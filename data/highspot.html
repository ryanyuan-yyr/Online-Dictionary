high spot
Word forms: plural high spots
N-COUNT 最精彩部分 The high spot of an event or activity is the most exciting or enjoyable part of it. 最精彩部分

  • Rough weather would have denied us a landing on the island, for me the high spot of the entire cruise. [+ of]

    暴风雨天气差点使我们无法在岛上登陆，而上岛对我来说正是整个航游过程中最精彩的部分。
