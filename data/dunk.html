dunk
dʌŋk dʌŋk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dunks, present participle dunking, past tense , past participle dunked
1 VERB (将饼干、面包等)浸一浸,泡一泡 If you dunk something such as a biscuit or a piece of bread in a drink or in soup, you dip it into the drink or soup before eating it. (将饼干、面包等)浸一浸,泡一泡

  • Many people dunk their foods in coffee, tea or milk. [V n in n]

    很多人在咖啡、茶或牛奶里蘸东西吃。

2 VERB 浸;泡 If you dunk something in a liquid, you put it in the liquid, especially for a particular purpose and for a short time. 浸;泡

  • Dunk new plants in a bucket of water for an hour or so before planting. [VERB noun + in]

    栽种新植物之前将它们在水桶中浸泡一小时左右。
