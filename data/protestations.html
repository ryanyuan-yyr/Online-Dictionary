N-COUNT 申明；断言 A protestation is a strong declaration that something is true or not true. 申明；断言 [formal]

  • Despite his constant protestations of devotion and love, her doubts persisted. [+ of]

    尽管他一直宣称对她的爱忠贞不渝，她仍然心存怀疑。

SYN declaration, pledge, vow, oath
