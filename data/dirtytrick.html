dirty trick
Word forms: plural dirty tricks
N-COUNT [usually plural] 政客伎俩;卑鄙手段 You describe the actions of an organization or political group as dirty tricks when you think they are using illegal methods to harm the reputation or effectiveness of their rivals. 政客伎俩;卑鄙手段

  • He claimed he was the victim of a dirty tricks campaign..

    他声称他是一场卑鄙的竞选运动的牺牲品。
