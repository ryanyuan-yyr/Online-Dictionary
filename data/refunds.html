refund
riːfʌnd rɪfʌnd riˈfʌnd rɪˈfʌnd ˈriˌfʌnd
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense refunds, present participle refunding, past tense , past participle refunded
The noun is pronounced /'riːfʌnd/. The verb is pronounced /rɪ'fʌnd/. 名词读作/'riːfʌnd/。动词读作/rɪ'fʌnd/。
1 N-COUNT 退款;返还金额 A refund is a sum of money which is returned to you, for example because you have paid too much or because you have returned goods to a shop. 退款;返还金额
SYN repayment, compensation, rebate, reparation  
2 VERB 退还（钱款） If someone refunds your money, they return it to you, for example because you have paid too much or because you have returned goods to a shop. 退还（钱款）

  • We guarantee to refund your money if you're not delighted with your purchase. [VERB noun]

    我们承诺如果您对所买物品不满意可以退款。

  • Take the goods back to your retailer who will refund you the purchase price. [VERB noun noun]

    把商品退还给你的零售商，他们会按原价退款的。

SYN repay, return, restore, make good
