materialistic
mətɪəriəlɪstɪk
ADJ-GRADED 实利主义的;物质至上主义的 If you describe a person or society as materialistic, you are critical of them because they attach too much importance to money and material possessions. 实利主义的;物质至上主义的 [disapproval]

  • During the 1980s Britain became a very materialistic society.

    20世纪80年代期间，英国变成一个物质至上的社会。

SYN consumerist, worldly, grasping, greedy
