move around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 move about
PHRASAL VERB 持续换工作;经常搬迁 If you move around or move about, you keep changing your job or keep changing the place where you live. 持续换工作;经常搬迁

  • I was born in Fort Worth but we moved around a lot and I was reared in east Texas. [VERB PREP.]

    我出生在沃思堡，但由于常搬家，所以我是在得克萨斯州的东部长大的。

  • He moved around the country working in orange groves. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他在乡村不断换工作，为不同的柑橘园干活。
