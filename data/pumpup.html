pump up
1 PHRASAL VERB 用泵（或气筒）给…打气 If you pump up something such as a tyre, you fill it with air using a pump. 用泵（或气筒）给…打气 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • I tried to pump up my back tyre. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我试图给后胎打气。

SYN inflate, blow up, fill up, dilate  
2 PHRASAL VERB （尤指通过自我心理暗示）给自己打气，自我鼓励 If you pump yourself up for something, you prepare yourself for it mentally, especially by telling yourself that you can succeed. （尤指通过自我心理暗示）给自己打气，自我鼓励 [informal]

  • They find it difficult to pump themselves up for the games. [VERB pronoun-reflexive PREP.]

    他们感到要为自己加油打气，自信满满地去打比赛并不容易。
