play around
1 PHRASAL VERB 逗乐；玩闹 If you play around, you behave in a silly way to amuse yourself or other people. 逗乐；玩闹 [informal]

  • Stop playing around and eat!. [VERB PREP.]

    别逗了，快吃吧！

  • There was no doubt he was serious, it wasn't just playing around. [VERB PREP.]

    毫无疑问他很认真，那可不是闹着玩的。

  • Had he taken the keys and played around with her car? [VERB PREP. + with]

    他是不是拿过车钥匙，开着她的车子玩儿？

2 PHRASAL VERB 反复琢磨；自由调换…的排列顺序 If you play around with a problem or an arrangement of objects, you try different ways of organizing it in order to find the best solution or arrangement. 反复琢磨；自由调换…的排列顺序 [informal]

  • I can play around with the pictures to make them more eye-catching. [VERB PREP. + with]

    我可以尝试用不同的方式排列这些图片以使它们更加吸引眼球。

3 PHRASAL VERB 鬼混；搞不正当性关系 If someone plays around, they have sex with people other than the person they are married to or having a serious relationship with. 鬼混；搞不正当性关系 [informal]

  • Up to 75 per cent of married men may be playing around. [VERB PREP.]

    多达75%的已婚男士可能在外拈花惹草。

  • Robert was playing around with another woman. [VERB PREP. with noun]

    罗伯特和另一个女人乱搞。

SYN philander, have an affair, carry on [informal] , fool around
