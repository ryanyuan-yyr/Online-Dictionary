trying
traɪɪŋ ˈtraɪɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 难对付的;令人厌烦的;令人恼火的 If you describe something or someone as trying, you mean that they are difficult to deal with and make you feel impatient or annoyed. 难对付的;令人厌烦的;令人恼火的

  • Support from those closest to you is vital in these trying times.

    在这些困难的时刻，最亲近的人给予你的支持至关重要。

  • The whole business has been very trying.

    整个事情一直令人非常头疼。

SYN annoying, hard, taxing, difficult
