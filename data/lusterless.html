lustre
lʌstəʳ ˈlʌstər
in AM, use 美国英语用 luster
1 N-UNCOUNT 光泽；光彩 Lustre is gentle shining light that is reflected from a surface, for example from polished metal. 光泽；光彩

  • Gold retains its lustre for far longer than other metals.

    黄金保持其原有光泽的时间较其他金属要长得多。

  • It is softer than cotton and nylon and has a similar lustre to silk.

    它较棉布和尼龙柔软，光泽则与丝绸相仿。

SYN sparkle, shine, glow, glitter  
2 N-UNCOUNT 情致；意趣；荣光 Lustre is the qualities that something has that make it interesting and exciting. 情致；意趣；荣光

  • What do you do if your relationship is beginning to lose its lustre?

    如果你们的关系开始失去情趣，你会怎么办？

SYN excitement, kick [informal] , pleasure, thrill
