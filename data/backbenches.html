backbenches
bækbentʃɪz ˌbækˈbɛntʃɪz
N-PLURAL （英国下院的）后座（或普通）议员席；（亦总称）后座（或普通）议员 The backbenches are the seats in the British House of Commons where backbenchers sit. The Members of Parliament who sit on the backbenches are also referred to as the backbenches. （英国下院的）后座（或普通）议员席；（亦总称）后座（或普通）议员 [British]

  • This issue is creating unrest on the backbenches.

    这一问题引发了后座议员的不满。
