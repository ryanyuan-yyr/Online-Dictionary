revoke
rɪvoʊk rɪˈvoʊk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense revokes, present participle revoking, past tense , past participle revoked
VERB 取消，废除，撤销（许可、法律、协议等） When people in authority revoke something such as a licence, a law, or an agreement, they cancel it. 取消，废除，撤销（许可、法律、协议等） [formal]

  • The government revoked her husband's license to operate migrant labor crews. [VERB noun]

    政府撤销了她丈夫管理外来打工人群的许可证。

SYN cancel, recall, withdraw, reverse  
revocation

  • The Montserrat government announced its revocation of 311 banking licences.

    蒙特塞拉特岛政府宣布吊销311家银行机构的从业许可证。
