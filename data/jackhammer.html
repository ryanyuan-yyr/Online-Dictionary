jackhammer
dʒækhæmər ˈdʒækˌhæmə ˈdʒækˌhæmər
Word forms: jackhammers
N-COUNT 手提钻A jackhammer is a large hammer, powered by compressed air, that is used for breaking up rocks and other hard material. 手提钻

  • Outside a jackhammer was pounding away at a particularly stubborn piece of concrete.
