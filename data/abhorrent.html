abhorrent
æbhɒrənt -hɔːr- æbˈhɔrənt æbˈhɑrənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 令人憎恶的；使人厌恶的 If something is abhorrent to you, you hate it very much or consider it completely unacceptable. 令人憎恶的；使人厌恶的 [formal]

  • Racial discrimination is abhorrent to my council and our staff. [+ to]

    我的顾问班子和全体工作人员都对种族歧视深恶痛绝。

  • There are many people who still find the act of abortion abhorrent.

    仍有很多人对堕胎行为表示厌恶。

SYN hateful, hated, offensive, disgusting
