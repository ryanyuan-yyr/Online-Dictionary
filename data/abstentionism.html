abstention
æbstenʃən æbˈstɛnʃən əbˈstɛnʃən
Word forms: plural abstentions
1 N-VAR （投票时之）弃权 Abstention is a formal act of not voting either for or against a proposal. （投票时之）弃权

  • ...a vote of sixteen in favor, three against, and one abstention.

    16票赞成、3票反对、1票弃权

  • The number of abstentions is likely to be crucial.

    弃权票数很可能起决定性作用。

  • Abstention is traditionally high in Colombia.

    在哥伦比亚,弃权率历来较高。

SYN abstaining, non-voting, refusal to vote  
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft N from n] 同 abstinence Abstention is the same as abstinence. 同 abstinence [formal]

  • The goal is complete abstention from all mind-altering substances.

    目标是彻底戒绝所有致幻物质。

SYN abstinence, refraining, avoidance, forbearance
