commotion
kəmoʊʃən kəˈmoʊʃən
Word forms: plural commotions
N-VAR 喧闹;混乱;骚动 A commotion is a lot of noise, confusion, and excitement. 喧闹;混乱;骚动

  • He heard a commotion outside.

    他听见外面一阵骚动。

  • Sounds of voices and commotion could be heard downstairs now.

    现在能听见楼下人声鼎沸。

SYN disturbance, to-do, riot, disorder
