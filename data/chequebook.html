cheque book
Word forms: plural cheque books
The spellings chequebook, and in American English checkbook, are also used. 亦拼作 chequebook 和 checkbook (美国英语)。
N-COUNT 支票簿 A cheque book is a book of cheques which your bank gives you so that you can pay for things by cheque. 支票簿

  • Leave your cheque book and credit cards at home unless you know you will need them.

    除非确实需要用到支票簿和信用卡，否则别带在身上。
