eminently
emɪnəntli
ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective/-ed] （置于褒义词前表强调）非常，极其 You use eminently in front of an adjective describing a positive quality in order to emphasize the quality expressed by that adjective. （置于褒义词前表强调）非常，极其 [emphasis]

  • His books on diplomatic history were eminently readable.

    他写的有关外交史的书可读性非常强。

  • His family was eminently respectable.

    他的家族非常有名望。

SYN extremely, very, highly, greatly
