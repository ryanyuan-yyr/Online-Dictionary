ye
jiː ðiː ji ðə ðɪ ði
1 PRON-PLURAL 你们，汝等，尔辈（you的过时用法，诗歌或宗教用语） Ye is an old-fashioned, poetic, or religious word for you when you are talking to more than one person. 你们，汝等，尔辈（you的过时用法，诗歌或宗教用语）

  • Abandon hope all ye who enter here.

    汝等进入此地，须弃绝希望。

2 DET （模仿the的旧时书面形式） Ye is sometimes used in imitation of an old written form of the word 'the'. （模仿the的旧时书面形式）

  • ...Ye Olde Tea Shoppe.

    老茶庄
