roil
rɔɪl rɔɪl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense roils, present participle roiling, past tense , past participle roiled
1 VERB (水)激荡，翻滚，翻腾 If water roils, it is rough and disturbed. (水)激荡，翻滚，翻腾 [mainly US]

  • The water roiled to his left as he climbed carefully at the edge of the waterfall. [VERB]

    当他在瀑布边缘小心攀爬时，水就在他左边奔腾。

2 VERB 扰乱;搅乱;使混乱 Something that roils a state or situation makes it disturbed and confused. 扰乱;搅乱;使混乱

  • Times of national turmoil generally roil a country's financial markets. [VERB noun]

    在国家动荡不安的时代，该国的金融市场一般都会出现混乱。
