dawn on
(also dawn upon)
PHRASAL VERB 被领悟;被理解;被想到 If a fact or idea dawns on you, you realize it. 被领悟;被理解;被想到

  • It gradually dawned upon me that I still had talent and ought to run again. [V P n that]

    我渐渐明白了自己还有才能，应该再次参加竞选。

  • Then the chilling truth dawned on Captain Gary Snavely. [VERB PREP. noun]

    接着加里·斯内夫利船长意识到了这个令人不寒而栗的事实。
