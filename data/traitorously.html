traitorous
treɪtərəs ˈtreɪtərəs
ADJ-GRADED 谋叛的;背叛的;卖国的 A traitorous action will betray or bring danger to a country or to the group of people that someone belongs to. 谋叛的;背叛的;卖国的

  • ...the monstrous betrayal of men by their most traitorous companions.

    最为背信弃义的同伴对他们令人发指的出卖

  • ...the movement could be labeled as divisive, even traitorous.

    该运动可以被称作是分裂性的，甚至是背叛性的。

SYN treacherous, unfaithful, disloyal, false
