spare part
Word forms: plural spare parts
N-COUNT [usually plural] 备用零件;零配件 Spare parts are parts that you can buy separately to replace old or broken parts in a piece of equipment. They are usually parts that are designed to be easily removed or fitted. 备用零件;零配件

  • In the future the machines will need spare parts and maintenance.

    这些机器将来会需要备件和维修保养。
