bear out
PHRASAL VERB 支持，证实（某人的说法） If someone or something bears a person out or bears out what that person is saying, they support what that person is saying. 支持，证实（某人的说法） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Recent studies have borne out claims that certain perfumes can bring about profound psychological changes. [VERB PREP. noun]

    最近的研究证实了有些香水可以引起深刻心理变化的说法。

SYN support, prove, confirm, justify
