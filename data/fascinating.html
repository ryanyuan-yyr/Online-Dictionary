fascinating
fæsɪneɪtɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED 迷人的;让人着迷的;极有吸引力的 If you describe something as fascinating, you find it very interesting and attractive, and your thoughts tend to concentrate on it. 迷人的;让人着迷的;极有吸引力的

  • Madagascar is the most fascinating place I have ever been to.

    马达加斯加是我去过的最迷人的地方。

  • Her perceptions and intuitions about human nature were fascinating.

    她对人性的理解和感知引人入胜。

SYN captivating, engaging, gripping, compelling
