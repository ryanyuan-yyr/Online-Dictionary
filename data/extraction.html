extraction
ɪkstrækʃən ɪkˈstrækʃən ɛkˈstrækʃən ɪkˈstrækʃən
N-UNCOUNT 血统;家世 If you say, for example, that someone is of French extraction, you mean that they or their family originally came from France. 血统;家世 [formal]

  • Her real father was of Italian extraction.

    她的亲生父亲是意大利裔。

  • He married a young lady of Indian extraction.

    他娶了一位年轻的印度裔女子。

SYN origin, family, ancestry, descent
