slew
sluː slu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense slews, present participle slewing, past tense , past participle slewed
1 Slew is the past tense of slay .
2 V-ERG (使)(车辆失控)滑行,(使)侧滑 If a vehicle slews or is slewed across a road, it slides across it. (使)(车辆失控)滑行,(使)侧滑

  • The bus slewed sideways. [VERB adv./prep.]

    公共汽车滑到了一边。

  • A seven-ton lorry slewed across their path. [VERB adv./prep.]

    一辆 7 吨的货车侧滑到了一侧。

  • He slewed the car against the side of the building. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    他的车滑到了大楼的一侧，抵住了。

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 许多;大量 A slew of things is a large number of them. 许多;大量 [mainly US]

  • There have been a whole slew of shooting incidents. [+ of]

    发生过大量枪击案。

  • They dealt with a slew of other issues.

    他们处理了大量的其他问题。
