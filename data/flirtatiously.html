flirtatious
flɜːʳteɪʃəs flərˈteɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 调情的;卖弄风情的 Someone who is flirtatious behaves towards someone else as if they are sexually attracted to them, usually not in a very serious way. 调情的;卖弄风情的

  • He was dashing, self-confident and flirtatious.

    他风度翩翩，自信满满，而且很会挑逗女人。

SYN teasing, flirty, coquettish, amorous
