sliver
slɪvəʳ ˈslɪvər
Word forms: plural slivers
N-COUNT 小片;一点儿 A sliver of something is a small thin piece or amount of it. 小片;一点儿

  • Not a sliver of glass remains where the windows were. [+ of]

    窗户上连一点儿玻璃碎片都没有留下。

  • There was only one sliver of light in the darkness.

    黑暗中只有一点零星的光亮。

SYN shred, fragment, splinter, slip
