state of affairs steɪt əv əˈfɛəz
N-SING 一般状态;事态 If you refer to a particular state of affairs, you mean the general situation and circumstances connected with someone or something. 一般状态;事态

  • This state of affairs cannot continue for too long, if parliament is to recover.

    如果议会想要恢复常态的话，这种状态就不会持续太久。

  • The nation had a chance to move towards a more democratic, and modern, state of affairs.

    该国有可能变得更加民主、更加现代化。

SYN situation, state, circumstances, scenario
