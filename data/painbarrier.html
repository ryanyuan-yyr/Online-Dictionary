pain barrier
peɪn ˈbærɪə
N-SING （运动员的）伤痛，伤病 If you say that a sports player has gone through the pain barrier, you mean that he or she is continuing to make a great effort in spite of being injured or exhausted. （运动员的）伤痛，伤病 [British, journalism]

  • England's World Cup hero is determined to play through the pain barrier.

    英格兰的世界杯英雄决定带伤上阵。
