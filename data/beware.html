beware
bɪweəʳ bɪˈwɛə biˈwɛr bɪˈwɛr
1 VERB [only imper and inf] 小心；提防；当心；注意 If you tell someone to beware of a person or thing, you are warning them that the person or thing may harm them or be dangerous. 小心；提防；当心；注意

  • Beware of being too impatient with others. [VERB + of]

    注意不要对他人太没有耐心。

  • Motorists were warned to beware of slippery conditions. [V of n/-ing]

    驾驶员接到提醒当心道路湿滑。

  • Beware, this recipe is not for slimmers. [VERB]

    注意，本食谱不适用于减肥者。

SYN be careful, look out, watch out, be wary
