mitt
mɪt mɪt
Word forms: plural mitts
1 N-COUNT 同 mittens Mitts are the same as mittens . 同 mittens [informal]
2 N-COUNT 手 You can refer to a person's hands as their mitts . 手 [informal]

  • I pressed a dime into his grubby mitt.

    我把一个十分硬币塞进他脏兮兮的手里。

3 N-COUNT 棒球手套 A baseball mitt is a large glove worn by a player whose job involves catching the ball. 棒球手套
