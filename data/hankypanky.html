hanky-panky
hæŋki pæŋki ˈhæŋkiˈpæŋki
1 N-UNCOUNT （尤指不得体或不认真的）性行为;调情 Hanky-panky is used to refer to sexual activity between two people, especially when this is regarded as improper or not serious. （尤指不得体或不认真的）性行为;调情 [humorous, informal]

  • Does this mean no hanky-panky after lights out?

    这是否意味着熄灯以后不许有性行为？

SYN sex [informal] , sexual activity, lovemaking, nookie [slang]  
2 N-UNCOUNT 花招;诡计;欺骗行为 If you describe behaviour as hanky-panky, you disapprove of it because it is dishonest or breaks rules. 花招;诡计;欺骗行为 [disapproval]

  • The government has been offering tax credits, accelerated depreciation, and other economic hanky-panky.

    政府一直在搞税额抵免、加速折旧及其他一些经济上的花招。

SYN mischief, trickery, shenanigans [informal] , machinations
