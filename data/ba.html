ba
bɑ
1 N-COUNT 文学士学位 A BA is a first degree in an arts or social science subject. BA is an abbreviation for 'Bachelor of Arts'. 文学士学位

  • I did a BA in film making.

    我获得了电影摄制的文学士学位。

2 （置于人名后）文学士 BA is written after someone's name to indicate that they have a BA. （置于人名后）文学士

  • ...Helen Rich, BA (Hons).

    …海伦·里奇（荣誉）文学士
