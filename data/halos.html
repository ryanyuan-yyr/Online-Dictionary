halo
heɪloʊ ˈheɪloʊ
Word forms: plural haloes, plural halos
1 N-COUNT （绘画中圣人、天使头上的）光环，光轮 A halo is a circle of light that is shown in pictures round the head of a holy figure such as a saint or angel. （绘画中圣人、天使头上的）光环，光轮
2 N-COUNT 晕;晕状物 A halo is a circle of light round a person or thing, or something that looks like a circle of light. 晕;晕状物 [Also + of]

  • The sun had a faint halo round it.

    环绕太阳有圈淡淡的光晕。

  • She angrily slung a scarf around her head, flattening her halo of hair.

    她气鼓鼓地把头巾裹在头上，发髻都被压扁了。

SYN ring of light, aura, corona, radiance
