condolence
kəndoʊləns kənˈdəʊlmənt kənˈdoʊləns
Word forms: plural condolences
1 N-UNCOUNT 慰唁 A message of condolence is a message in which you express your sympathy for someone because one of their friends or relatives has died recently. 慰唁

  • Neil sent him a letter of condolence.

    尼尔给他发了一封唁函。

SYN sympathy, pity, compassion, consolation  
2 N-PLURAL 慰唁 When you offer or express your condolences to someone, you express your sympathy for them because one of their friends or relatives has died recently. 慰唁

  • He expressed his condolences to the families of the people who died in the incident.

    他向这次事件中的死者家属表示了慰唁。
