vex
veks vɛks
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vexes, present participle vexing, past tense , past participle vexed
VERB 使恼火;使困惑;使伤脑筋 If someone or something vexes you, they make you feel annoyed, puzzled, and frustrated. 使恼火;使困惑;使伤脑筋

  • It vexed me to think of others gossiping behind my back. [VERB noun]

    一想到别人在背后说我闲话，我就很恼火。

  • Everything about her vexed him. [VERB noun]

    有关她的一切都令他困惑。
