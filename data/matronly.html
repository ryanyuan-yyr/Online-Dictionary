matronly
meɪtrənli ˈmeɪtrənli
ADJ-GRADED (中年妇女)稍胖的，发福的 You can use matronly to describe a woman who is fairly fat and looks middle-aged, especially if you think the clothes she is wearing are not fashionable or attractive. (中年妇女)稍胖的，发福的

  • ...a matronly woman with an air of authority.
