implode
ɪmploʊd ɪmˈploʊd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense implodes, present participle imploding, past tense , past participle imploded
1 VERB 内爆;向心聚爆;向心压挤 If something implodes, it collapses into itself in a sudden and violent way. 内爆;向心聚爆;向心压挤

  • The engine imploded. [VERB]

    发动机内爆了。

  • He has nightmares about the tanks imploding. [VERB]

    他老是做油箱爆炸的噩梦。

2 VERB 崩溃;瓦解 If something such as an organization or a system implodes, it suddenly ends completely because it cannot deal with the problems it is experiencing. 崩溃;瓦解

  • ...the possibility that the party may implode in opposition. [VERB]

    该党派于在野期间瓦解的可能性
