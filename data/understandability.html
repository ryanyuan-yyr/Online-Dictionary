understandable
ʌndəʳstændəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （行为、情绪等）可以理解的，正常的，合情理的 If you describe someone's behaviour or feelings as understandable, you think that they have reacted to a situation in a natural way or in the way you would expect. （行为、情绪等）可以理解的，正常的，合情理的

  • His unhappiness was understandable.

    他不高兴是可以理解的。

SYN reasonable, natural, normal, justified  
understandably

  • The duke is understandably proud of Lady Helen and her achievements.

    公爵为海伦女士和她的成就感到自豪，这很容易理解。

  • Most organizations are, quite understandably, suspicious of new ideas.

    大多数机构对新想法持怀疑态度，这不难理解。

2 ADJ-GRADED （论述、理论等）容易理解的 If you say that something such as a statement or theory is understandable, you mean that people can easily understand it. （论述、理论等）容易理解的

  • Roger Neuberg writes in a simple and understandable way.

    罗杰·纽伯格的文章简明易懂。

SYN comprehensible, clear, straightforward, coherent
