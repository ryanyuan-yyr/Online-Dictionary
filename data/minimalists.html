minimalist
mɪnɪməlɪst
Word forms: plural minimalists
1 N-COUNT 极简派设计师；极简派艺术家 A minimalist is an artist or designer who uses minimalism. 极简派设计师；极简派艺术家

  • He was influenced by the minimalists in the 1970s.

    他受到20世纪70年代极简派艺术家的影响。

2 ADJ-GRADED 极简派风格的;受极简主义影响的 Minimalist is used to describe ideas, artists, or designers that are influenced by minimalism. 极简派风格的;受极简主义影响的

  • The two designers settled upon a minimalist approach.

    两位设计师决定采用极简派风格。

SYN sparse, simple, basic, plain
