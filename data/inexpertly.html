inexpert
ɪnekspɜːʳt ɪnˈɛkspərt ˌ ɪnɛkˈspɜrt ˌɪnɪkˈspɜrt
ADJ-GRADED 不熟练的;不灵巧的 If you describe someone or something as inexpert, you mean that they show a lack of skill. 不熟练的;不灵巧的

  • He was too inexperienced and too inexpert to succeed.

    他太缺乏经验,太不熟练,难以成功。

  • ...inexpert needlework.

    蹩脚的针线活

SYN amateurish, awkward, bungling, clumsy
