flapper
flæpər ˈflæpə ˈflæpər
Word forms: flappers
N-COUNT (20世纪20年代着装举止异常的)年轻女郎A flapper was a young woman in the 1920s who dressed or behaved unconventionally. (20世纪20年代着装举止异常的)年轻女郎

  • She'd had to wear her short, auburn hair flat like a twenties flapper for the play.

    为了该话剧，她需要像二十年代年轻女郎一样将短褐色头发弄直。
