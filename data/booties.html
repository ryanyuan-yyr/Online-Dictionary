bootee
buːtiː ˈbuːtiː buˈti ˈbuti
Word forms: plural bootees, plural booties
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] （婴儿当鞋穿的）毛绒袜，毛绒鞋 Bootees are short woollen socks that babies wear instead of shoes. （婴儿当鞋穿的）毛绒袜，毛绒鞋
2 N-COUNT 短筒靴；（尤指）短筒女靴 Bootees are short boots which come to just above the ankle. They are worn especially by women and girls. 短筒靴；（尤指）短筒女靴
