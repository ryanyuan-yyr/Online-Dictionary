uncoordinated
ʌnkoʊɔːʳdɪneɪtɪd ˌʌnkəʊˈɔːdɪˌneɪtɪd
(also unco-ordinated)
1 ADJ-GRADED （人）动作不协调的，笨拙的 If you describe someone as uncoordinated you mean that their movements are not smooth or controlled. （人）动作不协调的，笨拙的

  • They were unsteady on their feet and rather uncoordinated.

    他们站立不稳，协调性较差。

  • ...an uncoordinated toddler.

    蹒跚学步的小孩

SYN clumsy, awkward, ungainly, bungling  
2 ADJ-GRADED （行动、计划）组织不力的，未协调好的 If you describe actions or plans as uncoordinated, you mean they are not well-organized. （行动、计划）组织不力的，未协调好的

  • Government action has been half-hearted and uncoordinated.

    政府行动半心半意，组织不力。

  • ...late, uncoordinated and piecemeal enemy responses.

    敌方拖沓迟缓、组织乏力、全无章法的反应

SYN disorganized, confused, chaotic, disordered
