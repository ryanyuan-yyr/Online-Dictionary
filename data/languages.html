language
læŋgwɪdʒ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ
Word forms: plural languages
1 N-COUNT 语言；语言文字 A language is a system of communication which consists of a set of sounds and written symbols which are used by the people of a particular country or region for talking or writing. 语言；语言文字

  • ...the English language.

    英语

  • Students are expected to master a second language.

    学生们要掌握一门外语。

  • Holidays are for seeing the sights, hearing the language and savouring the smells.

    度假是为了赏风景之美，听语言之妙，品百味之香。

SYN tongue, speech, vocabulary, dialect  
2 N-UNCOUNT 言语；语言表达 Language is the use of a system of communication which consists of a set of sounds or written symbols. 言语；语言表达

  • Students examined how children acquire language.

    学生们仔细研究儿童如何习得语言。

  • Language is not art but both are forms of human behavior.

    言语不是艺术，但两者都是人类的行为方式。

SYN speech, communication, expression, speaking  
3 N-UNCOUNT （某一领域的）全部词汇，术语 You can refer to the words used in connection with a particular subject as the language of that subject. （某一领域的）全部词汇，术语

  • ...the language of business. [+ of]

    商业用语

SYN vocabulary, tongue, jargon, terminology  
4 N-UNCOUNT [adjective NOUN] 粗话；脏话 You can refer to someone's use of rude words or swearing as bad language when you find it offensive. 粗话；脏话

  • Television companies tend to censor bad language in feature films.

    电视公司往往会在审查故事片时删去其中的粗话。

  • There's a girl gonna be in the club, so you guys watch your language.

    俱乐部里会有一位女孩，你们男的说话注意点。

5 N-UNCOUNT 语言风格；措辞 The language of a piece of writing or speech is the style in which it is written or spoken. 语言风格；措辞

  • ...a booklet summarising it in plain language.

    以平实语言对其作出概述的小册子

  • The tone of his language was diplomatic and polite.

    他说话圆通得体、彬彬有礼。

  • Mr Harris has not been afraid to use language that many in his party despise.

    哈里斯先生素来不怕使用为许多党内人士所不齿的言辞。

SYN style, wording, expression, phrasing  
6 N-VAR 交流方式，交际方式（包括可识别符号、非语言声音、动作等） You can use language to refer to various means of communication involving recognizable symbols, non-verbal sounds, or actions. 交流方式，交际方式（包括可识别符号、非语言声音、动作等）

  • Some sign languages are very sophisticated means of communication.

    有些手语可以表达非常复杂的内容。

  • ...the digital language of computers. [+ of]

    计算机的数字语言
