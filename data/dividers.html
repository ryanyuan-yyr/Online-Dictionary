divider
dɪvaɪdəʳ dəˈvaɪdər
Word forms: plural dividers
1 N-COUNT 分隔物;分开物;隔板 A divider is something which forms a barrier between two areas or sets of things. 分隔物;分开物;隔板

  • A curtain acted as a divider between this class and another.

    帘子作为分隔物将这间教室和另一间分开。

  • ...room dividers.

    房间隔板

2 N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] 分线规;两脚规 Dividers are an instrument used for measuring lines and for marking points along them. Dividers consist of two pointed arms joined with a hinge. 分线规;两脚规
