plug into
1 PHRASAL VERB 接入（计算机系统） If you plug into a computer system, you are able to use it or see the information stored on it. 接入（计算机系统）

  • It is possible to plug into remote databases to pick up information. [VERB PREP. noun]

    接入远程数据库来获取信息是可以办到的。

2 PHRASAL VERB 探究；设法了解 If you plug into a group of people or their ideas, you find out about them and try to understand them. 探究；设法了解 [informal]

  • The Centre for European Policy Studies is plugged into the thinking of the people who matter. [VERB PREP. noun]

    欧洲政策研究中心在了解重要人士的想法。

3 See also: plug-in
