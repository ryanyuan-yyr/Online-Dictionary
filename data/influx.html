influx
ɪnflʌks ˈɪnˌflʌks
Word forms: plural influxes
N-COUNT [usually singular] 流入;涌进 An influx of people or things into a place is their arrival there in large numbers. 流入;涌进

  • ...problems caused by the influx of refugees. [+ of]

    大批难民涌入产生的问题

  • ...the influx of American popcorn into the British market.

    美国爆米花大量涌入英国市场

SYN arrival, flow, rush, invasion
