sparse
spɑːʳs spɑrs
Word forms: comparative sparser, superlative sparsest
ADJ-GRADED 稀少的;稀疏的 Something that is sparse is small in number or amount and spread out over an area. 稀少的;稀疏的

  • Many slopes are rock fields with sparse vegetation.

    许多山坡都是石头地，植被稀疏。

  • He was a tubby little man in his fifties, with sparse hair.

    他50来岁，头发稀疏，身材矮胖。

  • Traffic was sparse on the highway.

    公路上车流稀少。

SYN scattered, scarce, meagre, sporadic  
sparsely

  • ...the sparsely populated interior region, where there are few roads.

    人口分散、公路稀少的内地
