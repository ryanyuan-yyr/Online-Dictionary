no-show
ˈnoʊˌʃoʊ
N-SING 未能如期出现的人;爽约者 If someone who is expected to go somewhere fails to go there, you can say that they are a no-show . 未能如期出现的人;爽约者

  • But Ivanisevic's no-show will be a blow for Wimbledon chiefs.

    但伊万尼塞维奇不能参赛对温布尔登主办方将是一个重大打击。

  • John Henry Williams was a no-show at last week's game in Milwaukee.

    上周在密尔沃基进行的比赛约翰·亨利·威廉斯没有出场。
