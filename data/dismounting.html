dismount
dɪsmaʊnt dɪsˈmaʊnt ˈdɪsˌmaʊnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dismounts, present participle dismounting, past tense , past participle dismounted
VERB 下马;下车 If you dismount from a horse or a bicycle, you get down from it. 下马;下车 [formal]

  • Emma dismounted and took her horse's bridle. [VERB]

    埃玛下了马，拉着马笼头。

SYN get off, descend, get down, alight
