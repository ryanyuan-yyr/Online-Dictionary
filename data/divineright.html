divine right
Word forms: plural divine rights
N-COUNT [usually singular] 神授之权;应有的权力 If someone thinks they have a divine right to something, they think that it is their right to have it, without making any effort. 神授之权;应有的权力

  • A degree does not give you a divine right to wealth.

    拥有学位并不能保证你就能获得财富。
