countryman
kʌntrimən ˈkʌntrimən
Word forms: plural countrymen
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] 同胞;同国人;同乡 Your countrymen are people from your own country. 同胞;同国人;同乡

  • He lost last year's final to fellow countryman Michael Stich.

    他在去年的决赛中输给了自己的同胞迈克尔·施蒂希。

2 N-COUNT 乡下人;农村人 A countryman is a person who lives in the country rather than in a city or a town. 乡下人;农村人

  • He had the red face of a countryman.

    他有着乡下人的红脸庞。

SYN yokel, farmer, peasant, provincial
