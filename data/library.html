library
laɪbrəri -breri ˈlaɪˌbrɛri laɪbrəri ˈlaɪbəri
Word forms: plural libraries
1 N-COUNT （公共）图书馆 A public library is a building where things such as books, newspapers, videos, and music are kept for people to read, use, or borrow. （公共）图书馆

  • ...the local library.

    当地图书馆

  • She issued them library cards.

    她给他们发了借书证。

2 N-COUNT （私人）收藏的图书（或唱片等） A private library is a collection of things such as books or music, that is normally only used with the permission of the owner. （私人）收藏的图书（或唱片等）

  • My thanks go to the British School of Osteopathy, for the use of their library.

    感谢不列颠骨疗法学校允许我使用他们的藏书。

3 N-COUNT 书房；书斋 In some large houses the library is the room where most of the books are kept. 书房；书斋

  • Guests were rarely entertained in the library.

    很少会在书房招待客人。
