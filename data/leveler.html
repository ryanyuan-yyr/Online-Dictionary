leveller
levələʳ
Word forms: plural levellers
in AM, use 美国英语用 leveler
N-COUNT [usually singular, oft adjective NOUN] 使人平等的事物 If you describe something as a leveller, you mean that it makes all people seem the same, in spite of their differences in, for example, age or social status. 使人平等的事物

  • The computer is a leveller, making information available to everyone.

    计算机是公平之物，所有人物都能从中获得信息。

  • Violence is a dangerous leveller.

    以暴力实现平等是危险的。
