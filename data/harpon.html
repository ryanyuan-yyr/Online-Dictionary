harp on
PHRASAL VERB 唠唠叨叨地说个不停;喋喋不休 If you say that someone harps on a subject, or harps on about it, you mean that they keep on talking about it in a way that other people find annoying. 唠唠叨叨地说个不停;喋喋不休 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • Jones harps on this theme more than on any other. [VERB PREP. noun]

    这个话题最能让琼斯唠叨个没完。

  • She concentrated on the good parts of her trip instead of harping on about the bad. [V P + about]

    她着重说了自己旅途中遇到的开心事，并没有唠叨那些不愉快的事。
