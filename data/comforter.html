comforter
kʌmfəʳtəʳ ˈkʌmfərtər
Word forms: plural comforters
1 N-COUNT 安慰者;给人安慰的事物 A comforter is a person or thing that comforts you. 安慰者;给人安慰的事物

  • He became Vivien Leigh's devoted friend and comforter.

    他成了费雯丽忠诚的朋友和给她安慰的人。

2 N-COUNT 羽绒被 A comforter is a large cover filled with feathers or similar material which you put over yourself in bed instead of a sheet and blankets. 羽绒被 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 duvet, quilt
