bump up
PHRASAL VERB 猛增；（大幅）提高 If you bump up an amount, you increase it suddenly, usually by a lot. 猛增；（大幅）提高 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The extra cost will bump up the price. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    额外的成本会使价格猛涨。

  • Add pasta to your salads to bump up your fibre intake. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    在色拉中加入意大利面条，增加你的纤维摄入量。

SYN increase, raise, boost, expand
