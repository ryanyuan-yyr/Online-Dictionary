middlebrow
mɪdəlbraʊ ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊ ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊ
(also middle-brow)
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (书、电影等)娱乐的,品位一般的，通俗的 If you describe a piece of entertainment such as a book or film as middlebrow, you mean that although it may be interesting and enjoyable, it does not require much thought. (书、电影等)娱乐的,品位一般的，通俗的

  • ...such middlebrow fare as Poirot, Sherlock Holmes and Jeeves and Wooster.

    诸如《大侦探波洛》、《夏洛克·福尔摩斯探案集》、《万能管家》之类的轻松娱乐剧
