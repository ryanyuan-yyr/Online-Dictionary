tented
tentɪd ˈtɛntɪd
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (田野、营地)搭了帐篷的 A tented field or a tented camp is an area where a number of people are living in tents. (田野、营地)搭了帐篷的

  • He said the refugees would be moved to a tented camp.

    他说，难民们将被转移到一个搭有帐篷的营地去。

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （房屋）帐篷形的 A tented room has long pieces of material hanging down from the centre of the ceiling to the walls, so that the room has the appearance of the inside of a large tent. （房屋）帐篷形的

  • ...a tented dining area.

    帐篷形的就餐区

  • The tented ceiling hides a maze of water pipes.

    形似帐篷的天花板上隐藏着迷宫似的水管。
