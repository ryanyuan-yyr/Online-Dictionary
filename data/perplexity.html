perplexity
pəʳpleksɪti pərˈplɛksəti
Word forms: plural perplexities
1 N-UNCOUNT 困惑；迷惘 Perplexity is a feeling of being confused and frustrated because you do not understand something. 困惑；迷惘

  • He began counting them and then, with growing perplexity, counted them a second time.

    他开始数，然后更加困惑，于是又数了一遍。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] 难以理解的事物；疑团 The perplexities of something are those things about it which are difficult to understand because they are complicated. 难以理解的事物；疑团

  • ...the perplexities of quantum mechanics. [+ of]

    量子力学难题

SYN complexity, difficulty, mystery, involvement
