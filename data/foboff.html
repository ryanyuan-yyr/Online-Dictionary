fob off
PHRASAL VERB 搪塞If someone fobs you off, they tell you something just to stop you from asking questions. 搪塞 [disapproval]

  • I've asked her about it but she fobs me off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我问过她这件事，但她一味搪塞。

  • Don't be fobbed off with excuses. [be VERB-ed PREP. + with]

    不要以各种借口来搪塞。

SYN put off, deceive, appease, flannel [British , informal]
