stencil
stensəl ˈstɛnsəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense stencils, present participle stencilling, past tense , past participle stencilled
in AM, use 美国英语用 stenciling, stenciled
1 N-COUNT (镂有图案的)模版，蜡纸 A stencil is a piece of paper, plastic, or metal which has a design cut out of it. You place the stencil on a surface and paint it so that paint goes through the holes and leaves a design on the surface. (镂有图案的)模版，蜡纸
2 VERB (用模版)印制(图案) If you stencil a design or if you stencil a surface with a design, you put a design on a surface using a stencil. (用模版)印制(图案) [Also VERB n]

  • He then stencilled the ceiling with a moon and stars motif. [VERB noun + with]

    他随后用模版在天花板上印上了月亮和繁星图案。

  • ...a stencilled design. [VERB-ed]

    用模版印刷出来的图案
