seeker
siːkəʳ
Word forms: plural seekers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural, usually n NOUN] 寻找者;寻求者;追求者;谋求者 A seeker is someone who is looking for or trying to get something. 寻找者;寻求者;追求者;谋求者

  • I am a seeker after truth.

    我追求真理。

  • The beaches draw sun-seekers from all over Europe.

    这些海滩吸引了欧洲各地喜欢晒太阳的人。
