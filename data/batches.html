batch
bætʃ bætʃ
Word forms: plural batches
N-COUNT 一批；一组；一群 A batch of things or people is a group of things or people of the same kind, especially a group that is dealt with at the same time or is sent to a particular place at the same time. 一批；一组；一群

  • ...the current batch of trainee priests. [+ of]

    目前的一批牧师学员

  • She brought a large batch of newspaper cuttings. [+ of]

    她带来了一大堆剪报。

  • We're still waiting for the first batch to arrive.

    我们还在等着第一批货的到来。

SYN group, set, lot, crowd
