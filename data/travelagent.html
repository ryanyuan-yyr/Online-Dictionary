travel agent
Word forms: plural travel agents
1 N-COUNT 旅行社 A travel agent or travel agent's is a shop or office where you can go to arrange a holiday or journey. 旅行社

  • He worked in a travel agent's.

    他在一家旅行社工作。

2 N-COUNT 旅行代办人;旅行代理商 A travel agent is a person or business that arranges people's holidays and journeys. 旅行代办人;旅行代理商
