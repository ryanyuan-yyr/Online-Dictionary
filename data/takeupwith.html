take up with
1 PHRASAL VERB [no passive] 和…混在一起;和…好上了;和…关系暧昧 You say that someone has taken up with someone unsuitable when they have begun a sexual or friendly relationship with them, and you disapprove of this. 和…混在一起;和…好上了;和…关系暧昧 [disapproval]

  • Sandy took up with a widow 21 years his junior. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    桑迪和一个比他小 21 岁的寡妇搞上了。

  • He took up with smugglers. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他和走私犯混到一块儿去了。

SYN become friendly with, get involved with, start seeing, fall in with  
2 PHR-V-PASSIVE 忙于 If you are taken up with something, it keeps you busy or fully occupied. 忙于

  • His mind was wholly taken up with the question. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    他满脑子都在想这个问题。
