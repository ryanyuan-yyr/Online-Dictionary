nigh
naɪ naɪ
1 ADV [be ADVERB] 即将发生;接近地 If an event is nigh, it will happen very soon. 即将发生;接近地 [old-fashioned]

  • The end of the world may be nigh, but do we really care?.

    世界末日可能不远了，但我们真的在意吗？

  • The storm must still be nigh, she thought.

    她想暴风雨必定仍会到来。

SYN almost, about, nearly, close to  
2See also:well-nigh 
3 PHRASE (数量、数字或年龄)将近，接近 Nigh on an amount, number, or age means almost that amount, number, or age. (数量、数字或年龄)将近，接近 [old-fashioned]

  • I've been one of your number one fans for nigh on three years now.

    我一直是你的铁杆粉丝之一，到现在快3年了。

  • I had to pay nigh on forty pounds for it.

    我不得不为它花了将近40英镑。
