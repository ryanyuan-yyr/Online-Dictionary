raucous
rɔːkəs ˈrɔkəs ˈrɑkəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 喧闹的;嘶哑的;刺耳的 A raucous sound is loud, harsh, and rather unpleasant. 喧闹的;嘶哑的;刺耳的

  • They heard a bottle being smashed, then more raucous laughter.

    他们听见酒瓶摔碎的声音，然后是一阵更喧闹的笑声。

  • ...the raucous cries of the seabirds.

    海鸟喧闹的叫声

  • ...a raucous crowd of 25,000 delirious fans.

    25,000名疯狂粉丝组成的喧闹人群

SYN harsh, rough, loud, noisy  
raucously

  • They laughed together raucously.

    他们一起放声大笑，声音很刺耳。
