analogy
ənælədʒi əˈnælədʒi
Word forms: plural analogies
1 N-COUNT 相似；类似；类推；类比 If you make or draw an analogy between two things, you show that they are similar in some way. 相似；类似；类推；类比

  • It is probably easier to make an analogy between the courses of the planets, and two trains travelling in the same direction. [+ between/with]

    将行星的运行比作两列火车朝同一方向行驶可能更容易些。

SYN similarity, relation, comparison, parallel
