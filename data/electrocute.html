electrocute
ɪlektrəkjuːt ɪˈlɛktrəˌkjuːt iˈlɛktrəˌkjut ɪˈlɛktrəˌkjut
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense electrocutes, present participle electrocuting, past tense , past participle electrocuted
1 VERB 使触电身亡;用电击伤 If someone is electrocuted, they are accidentally killed or badly injured when they touch something connected to a source of electricity. 使触电身亡;用电击伤

  • Three people were electrocuted by falling power-lines. [be VERB-ed]

    3个人碰到落下的电缆，触电身亡。

  • He accidentally electrocuted himself. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    他意外触电身亡。

2 VERB [usually passive] 用电刑处死（罪犯） If a criminal is electrocuted, he or she is executed using electricity. 用电刑处死（罪犯）

  • He was electrocuted for a murder committed when he was 17. [be VERB-ed]

    他因17岁时犯下的谋杀罪而被处以电刑。
