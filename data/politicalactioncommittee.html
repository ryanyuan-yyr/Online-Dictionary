political action committee pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈækʃən kəˈmɪtɪ
Word forms: political action committees
N-COUNT 政治行动委员会A political action committee is an organization which campaigns for particular political policies, and that gives money to political parties or candidates who support those policies. The abbreviation is also used. 政治行动委员会 [US]

  • ...a political action committee that supports gun control.

    ...一个支持枪支管制的政治行动委员会。
