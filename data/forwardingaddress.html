forwarding address
ˈfɔːwədɪŋ əˈdrɛs
Word forms: plural forwarding addresses
N-COUNT 转递地址;转寄地址 A forwarding address is an address that you give to someone when you go and live somewhere else so that they can send your mail on to you. 转递地址;转寄地址

  • The former owner had not left any forwarding address.

    以前的房主没有留下任何转递地址。
