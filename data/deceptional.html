deception
dɪsepʃən diˈsɛpʃən dɪˈsɛpʃən
Word forms: plural deceptions
N-VAR 欺骗;受骗 Deception is the act of deceiving someone or the state of being deceived by someone. 欺骗;受骗

  • He admitted conspiring to obtain property by deception.

    他承认曾与人合谋骗取财产。

  • You've been the victim of a rather cruel deception.

    你是一场相当残忍的骗局的受害者。

SYN trickery, fraud, deceit, hypocrisy
