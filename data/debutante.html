debutante
debjʊtɑːnt ˈdɛbjʊˌtɑːnt ˈdɛbjuˌtɑnt ˈdɛbjuˌtænt ˌ dɛbjuˈtɑnt ˌdɛbjuˈtænt
Word forms: plural debutantes
N-COUNT (上层社会）首次进入社交界的年轻女子 A debutante is a young woman from the upper classes who has started going to social events with other young people. (上层社会）首次进入社交界的年轻女子 [old-fashioned]

  • She dazzled London society as the most beautiful debutante of her generation.

    她首次出现在伦敦社交界便艳惊四座，被视为同龄人里最美丽的年轻女子。
