stagnant
stægnənt ˈstægnənt
1 ADJ-GRADED 停滞不前的;不发展的;无变化的 If something such as a business or society is stagnant, there is little activity or change. 停滞不前的;不发展的;无变化的 [disapproval]

  • He is seeking advice on how to revive the stagnant economy.

    他正在就如何振兴低迷的经济征求意见。

  • Mass movements are often a factor in the awakening and renovation of stagnant societies.

    群众性运动经常是能唤醒和振兴停滞不前的社会的一个因素。

SYN inactive, declining, stagnating, flat  
2 ADJ (水)不流动而污浊的 Stagnant water is not flowing, and therefore often smells unpleasant and is dirty. (水)不流动而污浊的
