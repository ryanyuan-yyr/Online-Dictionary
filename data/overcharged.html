overcharge
oʊvəʳtʃɑːʳdʒ ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑrdʒ ˈoʊvərˌtʃɑrdʒ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense overcharges, present participle overcharging, past tense , past participle overcharged
VERB 对…要价（或收费）过高 If someone overcharges you, they charge you too much for their goods or services. 对…要价（或收费）过高

  • If you feel a taxi driver has overcharged you, say so. [VERB noun]

    如果你觉得出租车司机敲了你的竹杠，就说出来。

  • She claims she was overcharged by £1,000. [VERB noun]

    她称自己被多收了1,000英镑。
