unattainable
ʌnəteɪnəbəl
ADJ-GRADED 无法实现的；难以得到的 If you say that something is unattainable, you mean that it cannot be achieved or is not available. 无法实现的；难以得到的

  • There are those who argue that true independent advice is unattainable.

    有一些人认为真正独立的意见是不存在的。

  • ...an unattainable dream.

    无法实现的梦想
