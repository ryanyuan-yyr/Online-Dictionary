capital account
Word forms: plural capital accounts
1 N-COUNT 资本账户 A country's capital account is the part of its balance of payments that is concerned with the movement of capital. 资本账户

  • ...restrictions that affect the capital account of a country's balance of payments.

    影响一个国家国际收支平衡的资本账户的限制措施

2 N-COUNT 资本账;资本净值 A capital account is a financial statement showing the capital value of a company on a particular date. 资本账;资本净值 [business]

  • No business can survive without a capital account.

    企业没有资产净值就无法生存。
