poseur
poʊzɜːʳ poʊˈzɜr
Word forms: plural poseurs
N-COUNT 装腔作势的人；装模作样的人 You can describe someone as a poseur when you think that they behave in an insincere or exaggerated way because they want to make a particular impression on other people. 装腔作势的人；装模作样的人 [disapproval]

  • I am sometimes accused of being an inveterate poseur.

    有时有人说我惯于装模作样。
