revenue stream
ˈrɛvənjuː striːm
Word forms: plural revenue streams
N-COUNT 收入来源;收益流 A company's revenue stream is the amount of money that it receives from selling a particular product or service. 收入来源;收益流 [business]

  • The events business, she said, was crucial to the group in that it provides a constant revenue stream.

    她说举办活动的业务对于集团至关重要，因为它是一个稳定的收入来源。
