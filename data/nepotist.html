nepotism
nepətɪzəm ˈnɛpəˌtɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 裙带关系;任人唯亲 Nepotism is the unfair use of power in order to get jobs or other benefits for your family or friends. 裙带关系;任人唯亲 [disapproval]

  • Many will regard his appointment as the kind of nepotism British banking ought to avoid.

    很多人会把他的任命看作是英国银行业应该避免的一种裙带关系。

SYN favouritism, bias, patronage, preferential treatment
