recrimination
rɪkrɪmɪneɪʃən
Word forms: plural recriminations
N-UNCOUNT 反诉;相互指责;反唇相讥 Recriminations are accusations that two people or groups make about each other. 反诉;相互指责;反唇相讥

  • The bitter rows and recriminations have finally ended the relationship.

    激烈的争吵和相互指责最终为他们的感情画上了句号。

  • The war sweeps up everyone in hatred and recrimination.

    战争让大家充满仇恨并相互指责。

SYN bickering, retaliation, counterattack, mutual accusation
