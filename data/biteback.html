bite back
1 PHRASAL VERB 强忍住 If you bite back a feeling or something that you were going to say, you stop yourself from expressing it. 强忍住 [literary]

  • Susan bit back the words she would like to have said. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    苏珊话到嘴边又咽了回去。

  • A scream rose to her lips again. She had to bite it back. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她想尖叫，又不得不忍了回去。

2 PHRASAL VERB 反击；回嘴；反唇相讥 If a person or a group of people who have been defeated, criticized, or insulted bite back, they respond strongly or angrily. 反击；回嘴；反唇相讥 [journalism]

  • He is quickly on the attack, biting back at the questioner until the original question is lost. [VERB PREP.]

    他迅速发难，反击提问者，直到完全偏离了最初的问题。
