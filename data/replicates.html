replicate
replɪkeɪt ˈrɛplɪkɪt ˈrɛplɪˌkeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense replicates, present participle replicating, past tense , past participle replicated
1 VERB 复制;重做;使复现 If you replicate someone's experiment, work, or research, you do it yourself in exactly the same way. 复制;重做;使复现 [formal]

  • He invited her to his laboratory to see if she could replicate the experiment. [VERB noun]

    他邀请她到他的实验室看她能否复制该实验。

2 VERB (分子)复制 If a molecule replicates, it divides into smaller molecules which are exact copies of itself. (分子)复制 [technical] [Also VERB pron-reflexive]

  • Cells can reproduce but only molecules can replicate. [VERB]

    细胞可以再生，但是只有分子可以复制。

replication

  • The process of replication is very quick and efficient.

    复制的过程快捷、高效。
