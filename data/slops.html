slop
slɒp slɑp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense slops, present participle slopping, past tense , past participle slopped
1 V-ERG (使)溢出;(使)溅出;(使)泼出 If liquid slops from a container or if you slop liquid somewhere, it comes out over the edge of the container, usually accidentally. (使)溢出;(使)溅出;(使)泼出

  • A little cognac slopped over the edge of the glass. [VERB adv./prep.]

    有一点白兰地溢出了玻璃杯。

  • Refilling his cup, she slopped some tea into the saucer. [VERB noun adv./prep.]

    给他续茶的时候，她不小心溅了一些在茶碟上。

SYN spill, splash, overflow, splatter  
2 N-UNCOUNT 残羹剩饭;泔脚 You can use slop or slops to refer to liquid waste containing the remains of food. 残羹剩饭;泔脚

  • Breakfast plates were collected and the slops emptied.

    早餐的盘子已经收走，剩饭也清理干净了。
