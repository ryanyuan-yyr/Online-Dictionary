stopgap
stɒpgæp ˈstɑpˌgæp
Word forms: plural stopgaps
N-COUNT 权宜之计;临时替代物 A stopgap is something that serves a purpose for a short time, but is replaced as soon as possible. 权宜之计;临时替代物

  • Gone are the days when work was just a stopgap between leaving school and getting married.

    工作曾经只是毕业和结婚之间的临时过渡，但这样的日子已经一去不复返了。

  • Even if the bill were approved, it would be no more than a stopgap measure.

    即使议案获得批准，它也只不过是个临时措施。

SYN makeshift, improvisation, temporary expedient, shift
