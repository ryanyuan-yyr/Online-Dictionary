beehive
biːhaɪv ˈbiˌhaɪv
Word forms: plural beehives
1 N-COUNT 蜂箱；蜂窝 A beehive is a structure in which bees are kept, which is designed so that the beekeeper can collect the honey that they produce. 蜂箱；蜂窝
2 N-COUNT 蜂窝式发型 A beehive is a hairstyle for women in which the hair is piled up onto the top of the head into a high rounded shape. 蜂窝式发型

  • She wore butterfly glasses and had a beehive hairdo.

    她戴着一副蝶形眼镜，顶着一头蜂窝式发型。
