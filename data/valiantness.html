valiant
væliənt ˈvæljənt ˈvæljənt
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 勇敢的;果决的;无畏的 A valiant action is very brave and determined, though it may lead to failure or defeat. 勇敢的;果决的;无畏的

  • Despite valiant efforts by the finance minister, inflation rose to 36%.

    尽管财政部部长采取了一系列果决措施，通货膨胀率还是涨到了36%。

  • ...a valiant attempt to keep the business going.

    为把生意维持下去而作的勇敢尝试

SYN brave, heroic, courageous, bold  
valiantly

  • He suffered further heart attacks and strokes, all of which he fought valiantly.

    他还曾遭受心脏病和中风的折磨，但他都顽强地挺了过来。
