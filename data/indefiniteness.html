indefinite
ɪndefɪnɪt ɪnˈdɛfənɪt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 期限不定的;无限期的 If you describe a situation or period as indefinite, you mean that people have not decided when it will end. 期限不定的;无限期的

  • The trial was adjourned for an indefinite period.

    审判被无限期推迟。

  • ...an indefinite strike by government workers.

    公务人员的无限期罢工

SYN uncertain, general, vague, unclear  
2 ADJ 不明确的;不清楚的 Something that is indefinite is not exact or clear. 不明确的;不清楚的

  • ...at some indefinite time in the future.

    在未来的某个时间

  • ...a handsome woman of indefinite age.

    年龄不明的端庄女子

SYN unclear, unknown, uncertain, obscure
