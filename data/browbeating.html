browbeat
braʊbiːt ˈbraʊˌbit
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense browbeats, present participle browbeating, past participle browbeaten
The form browbeat is used in the present tense and is also the past tense. browbeat的过去式和原形相同。
VERB 威逼；恫吓 If someone tries to browbeat you, they try to force you to do what they want. 威逼；恫吓

  • ...attempts to deceive, con, or browbeat the voters. [VERB noun]

    蒙蔽、欺骗或恫吓选民的企图

  • When I backed out of the 100 metres, an older kid tried to browbeat me into it. [VERB noun + into]

    我要退出100米比赛的时候，一个年纪大些的孩子试图吓唬我就范。

SYN bully, threaten, cow, intimidate  
browbeaten

  • ...the browbeaten employees.

    受到威逼的员工
