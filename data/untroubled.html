untroubled
ʌntrʌbəld
ADJ-GRADED 不为所动的；不心烦意乱的；不忧心的 If you are untroubled by something, you are not affected or worried by it. 不为所动的；不心烦意乱的；不忧心的

  • She is untroubled by the fact that she didn't win. [+ by]

    她并没有为失败而烦心。

  • ...an untroubled night's sleep.

    一夜安枕

SYN undisturbed, unconcerned, unperturbed, cool
