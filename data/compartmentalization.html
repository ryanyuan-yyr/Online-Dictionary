compartmentalize
kɒmpɑːʳtmentəlaɪz ˌkɑmpɑrtˈmɛntəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense compartmentalizes, present participle compartmentalizing, past tense , past participle compartmentalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 compartmentalise
VERB 分隔;划分 To compartmentalize something means to divide it into separate sections. 分隔;划分 [Also VERB n + into]

  • Traditionally men have compartmentalized their lives, never letting their personal lives encroach upon their professional lives. [VERB noun]

    传统上男人把自己的生活划分清楚，他们不会让私生活影响其工作。

SYN categorize or categorise, classify, pigeonhole, sectionalize or sectionalise  
compartmentalized

  • ...the compartmentalised world of Japanese finance.

    被割裂开来的日本金融界
