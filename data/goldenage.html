golden age
Word forms: plural golden ages
N-COUNT （尤指艺术、文学等的）黄金时代，鼎盛时期 A golden age is a period of time during which a very high level of achievement is reached in a particular field of activity, especially in art or literature. （尤指艺术、文学等的）黄金时代，鼎盛时期
