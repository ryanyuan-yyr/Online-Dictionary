unforgiving
ʌnfəʳgɪvɪŋ ˌʌnfəˈɡɪvɪŋ ˌʌnfərˈgɪvɪŋ
1 ADJ 不宽容的；不原谅人的 If you describe someone as unforgiving, you mean that they are unwilling to forgive other people. 不宽容的；不原谅人的 [formal]

  • He was an unforgiving man who never forgot a slight.

    他斤斤计较，很不大度。

  • He finds human foibles endearing, but is unforgiving of pretension. [+ of]

    他觉得人性的弱点很可爱，但不能容忍惺惺作态。

2 ADJ-GRADED （形势或活动）无情的，难以应付的，不利的 If you describe a situation or activity as unforgiving, you mean that it causes a lot of people to experience great difficulty or failure, even people who deserve to succeed. （形势或活动）无情的，难以应付的，不利的

  • Business is a competitive activity. It is very fierce and very unforgiving.

    商场如战场，竞争激烈，不近人情。
