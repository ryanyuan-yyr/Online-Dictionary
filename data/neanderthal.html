neanderthal
niændəʳtɑːl -θɔːl
Word forms: plural neanderthals
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (生活在3.5万年至7万年之前的欧洲的)尼安德特人Neanderthal people lived in Europe between 35,000 and 70,000 years ago. (生活在3.5万年至7万年之前的欧洲的)尼安德特人

  • Neanderthal man was able to kill woolly mammoths and bears.

    尼安德特人能够捕杀长毛猛犸象和熊。

2 N-COUNT 尼安德特人You can refer to people from the Neanderthal period as Neanderthals. 尼安德特人
3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (尤指男性)极端守旧的; 粗鲁的If you describe people's, especially men's, ideas or ways of behaving as Neanderthal, you disapprove of them because they are very old-fashioned and uncivilized. (尤指男性)极端守旧的; 粗鲁的 [disapproval]

  • Let us deal with the question of his notoriously Neanderthal attitude to women.

    我们来看看他臭名昭著的、对女性态度粗鲁的问题。

4 N-COUNT (指男性)粗鲁的人If you call a man a neanderthal, you disapprove of him because you think he behaves in a very uncivilized way. (指男性)粗鲁的人 [disapproval]

  • ...drunken neanderthals.

    ...醉醺醺的粗汉。
