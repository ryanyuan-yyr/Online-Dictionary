half-assed ˈhæfˌæst
ADJ [usu ADJ n] 不周的; 欠妥的Half-assed remarks, ideas, or actions are poorly planned or poorly performed. 不周的; 欠妥的 [mainly US, vulgar, disapproval]

  • ...a half-assed comment I'd made on television.

    ...我在电视上发表的不妥评论。

  • The National Park Service in America – let's be candid here – does a pretty half-assed job of running many of the national parks.

    咱们还是直说吧，美国国家公园管理局在管理众多国家公园方面做得相当不妥。
