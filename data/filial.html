filial
fɪliəl ˈfɪljəl ˈfɪliəl ˈfɪljəl
ADJ [ADJ n] 子女的;孝顺的 You can use filial to describe the duties, feelings, or relationships which exist between a son or daughter and his or her parents. 子女的;孝顺的 [formal]

  • His father would accuse him of neglecting his filial duties.

    他的父亲会指责他不尽孝道。

  • Right now I'm in need of a little filial affection.

    此刻我需要的是一份孝心。

SYN devoted, familial, dutiful, respectful
