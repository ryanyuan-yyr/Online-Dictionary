sup
sʌp sʌp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense sups, present participle supping, past tense , past participle supped
1 VERB (尤指小口地)喝,呷,啜 If you sup something, you drink it, especially by taking small amounts. (尤指小口地)喝,呷,啜 [literary, or old-fashioned]

  • We supped mulled wine. [VERB noun]

    我们小口地喝着热葡萄酒。

2 VERB 吃晚饭;进晚餐 If you sup, you eat dinner in the evening. 吃晚饭;进晚餐 [literary, or old-fashioned]

  • He had been invited to sup with a colleague and his wife. [VERB]

    他已经受邀和一位同事及其夫人共进晚餐。
