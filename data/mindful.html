mindful
maɪndfʊl ˈmaɪndfəl
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 留心的;注意的 If you are mindful of something, you think about it and consider it when taking action. 留心的;注意的 [formal]

  • We must be mindful of the consequences of selfishness. [+ of]

    我们必须考虑自私自利的后果。

  • Mindful of the needs of its students, Cardiff has invested heavily in providing new and improved residences.

    考虑到学生的需要，加的夫大学投入了巨资兴建和改善学生宿舍。

SYN aware, careful, conscious, alert
