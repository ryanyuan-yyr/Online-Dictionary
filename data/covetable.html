covet
kʌvɪt ˈkʌvɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense covets, present participle coveting, past tense , past participle coveted
VERB 渴望;贪求;觊觎 If you covet something, you strongly want to have it for yourself. 渴望;贪求;觊觎 [formal]

  • She coveted his job so openly that conversations between them were tense. [VERB noun]

    她毫不隐讳地表示想得到他的工作，以至于他们之间说话都带着火药味。

SYN long for, desire, fancy [informal] , envy
