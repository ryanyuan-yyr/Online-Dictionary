outshine
aʊtʃaɪn ˌaʊtˈʃaɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outshines, present participle outshining, past tense , past participle outshone
VERB 比…更优秀；比…更出色；胜过 If you outshine someone at a particular activity, you are much better at it than they are. 比…更优秀；比…更出色；胜过

  • Jesse has begun to outshine me in sports. [VERB noun]

    杰西在体育方面开始超过我。

SYN outclass, beat, eclipse, overshadow
