bizarre
bɪzɑːʳ bɪˈzɑr
ADJ-GRADED 奇异的；古怪的 Something that is bizarre is very odd and strange. 奇异的；古怪的

  • The game was also notable for the bizarre behaviour of the team's manager.

    这场比赛也因球队主教练怪异的举动而尽人皆知。

  • You know, that book you lent me is really bizarre.

    你知道吗，你借给我的那本书真的很怪诞。

SYN strange, odd, unusual, out there [slang]  
bizarrely

  • She dressed bizarrely.

    她穿着奇装异服。

  • Bizarrely, death is not a disqualification for voting although non-attendance at party meetings is.

    奇怪的是，死亡不会让人失去投票资格，但是不参加政党会议却会让人失去投票资格。
