topsy-turvy
tɒpsi tɜːʳvi ˈtɒpsɪˈtɜːvɪ ˈtɑpsiˈtɜrvi
ADJ 混乱的;乱七八糟的;颠三倒四的 Something that is topsy-turvy is in a confused or disorganized state. 混乱的;乱七八糟的;颠三倒四的 [informal]

  • The world has turned topsy-turvy in my lifetime.

    在我的一生中世界已经变得一团糟。

  • …the moss-covered, topsy-turvy gravestones.

    长满苔藓、东倒西歪的墓碑

SYN confused, upside-down, disorderly, chaotic
