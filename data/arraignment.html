arraignment
əreɪnmənt
Word forms: plural arraignments
N-VAR 传讯;提审;提讯 Arraignment is when someone is brought before a court of law to answer a particular charge. 传讯;提审;提讯 [law]

  • They are scheduled for arraignment October 5th.

    他们定于10月5号接受提审。

  • Crowds appeared at the arraignments, clashing with security forces.

    审讯时，涌入的人群和安全部队发生冲突。

SYN accusation, charge, complaint, prosecution
