repeal
rɪpiːl rɪˈpil
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense repeals, present participle repealing, past tense , past participle repealed
VERB 废止;废除;撤销 If the government repeals a law, it officially ends it, so that it is no longer valid. 废止;废除;撤销

  • The government has just repealed the law segregating public facilities. [VERB noun]

    政府刚刚废止了不同种族分开使用公共设施的法令。

SYN abolish, reverse, revoke, annul  
Repeal is also a noun.

  • Next year will be the 60th anniversary of the repeal of Prohibition.

    明年将是禁酒令废除60周年。
