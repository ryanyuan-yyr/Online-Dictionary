snowy
snoʊi ˈsnoʊi
Word forms: comparative snowier, superlative snowiest
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 积雪覆盖的;大雪纷飞的 A snowy place is covered in snow. A snowy day is a day when a lot of snow has fallen. 积雪覆盖的;大雪纷飞的

  • ...the snowy peaks of the Bighorn Mountains.

    比格霍恩山白雪皑皑的群峰

  • ...a cold, snowy day in mid-February.

    2 月中旬一个寒冷的雪天

SYN snow-covered, frosty, wintry, ice-capped
