perfumed
pɜːʳfjuːmd pəʳfjuːmd ˈpɜːfjuːmd pəˈfjuːmd
1 ADJ-GRADED （水果、酒等）芬芳的，芳香的 Something such as fruit or wine that is perfumed has a sweet pleasant smell. （水果、酒等）芬芳的，芳香的

  • Champenois wines can be particularly fragrant and perfumed.

    香槟酒的香味会特别浓郁。

  • ...sweetly perfumed, creamy yellow flowers.

    散发芬芳的乳黄色花朵

SYN scented, smelling, fragranced, fragrant  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 芳香的；有香味的 Perfumed things have a sweet pleasant smell, either naturally or because perfume has been added to them. 芳香的；有香味的

  • She opened the perfumed envelope.

    她打开了带有香味的信封。

  • ...perfumed roses.

    芬芳的玫瑰
