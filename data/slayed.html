slay
sleɪ sleɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense slays, present participle slaying, past tense slew, past tense , past participle slayed, past participle slain
1 VERB 残杀;屠杀;杀戮 If someone slays an animal, they kill it in a violent way. 残杀;屠杀;杀戮 [formal]

  • ...the hill where St George slew the dragon. [VERB noun]

    圣乔治屠龙的那座山

SYN kill [informal] , destroy, slaughter, eliminate  
slaying

  • The festival commemorates the slaying of the demon buffalo.

    那个节日是为了纪念杀死魔牛。

slayer

  • ...the story of the Monster Slayer.

    “魔鬼杀手”的故事

2 V-PASSIVE 谋杀 If someone has been slain, they have been murdered. 谋杀 [mainly US]

  • Two Australian tourists were slain. [be VERB-ed]

    两名澳大利亚游客被杀。

SYN murder, kill, assassinate, do in [slang]
