peacekeeper
piːskiːpəʳ
(also peace-keeper)Word forms: plural peacekeepers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 维和士兵 Peacekeepers are soldiers who are members of a peacekeeping force. 维和士兵

  • There's been much fear that the United Nations peacekeepers would be under attack in a situation like that.

    人们非常担心联合国维和士兵在这种局势下会遭到攻击。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 和平维护国；和平维护组织 If you describe a country or an organization as a peacekeeper, you mean that it often uses its influence or armed forces to try to prevent wars or violent conflicts in the world. 和平维护国；和平维护组织

  • They want the United Nations to play a bigger role as the world's peacekeeper.

    他们希望联合国发挥更大的世界和平维护者的作用。

  • Russia's record as a peace-keeper is mixed.

    俄罗斯作为维和国家的记录有好有坏。
