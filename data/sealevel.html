sea level
(also sea-level)
N-UNCOUNT 海平面 Sea level is the average level of the sea with respect to the land. The height of mountains or other areas is calculated in relation to sea level . 海平面

  • The stadium was 2275 metres above sea level.

    这个体育馆海拔 2,275 米。

  • The whole place is at sea level.

    整个地区与海平面齐平。

  • The melting ice caused a rise in sea level.

    冰川消融造成海平面上升。
