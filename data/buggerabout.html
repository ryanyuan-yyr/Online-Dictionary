bugger about
(also bugger around)
1 PHRASAL VERB 无所事事；不务正业 If someone buggers about or buggers around, they waste time doing unnecessary things. 无所事事；不务正业 [British, informal, rude, disapproval]
2 PHRASAL VERB [VERB n PREPOSITION] 找…的麻烦；骚扰… If someone buggers you about or buggers you around, they cause you problems. 找…的麻烦；骚扰… [British, informal, rude, disapproval]
