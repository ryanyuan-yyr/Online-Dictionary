inestimable
ɪnestɪməbəl ɪnˈɛstəməbəl
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 难以计算的;无法估计的 If you describe the value, benefit, or importance of something as inestimable, you mean that it is extremely great and cannot be calculated. 难以计算的;无法估计的 [formal]

  • Human life is of inestimable value.

    人的生命无价。

  • This gives the professional an inestimable advantage.

    这给了专业人士一个无法估量的优势。

SYN incalculable, invaluable, priceless, precious
