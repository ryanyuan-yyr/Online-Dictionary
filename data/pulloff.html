pull off
1 PHRASAL VERB 成功完成，做成（困难的事情） If you pull off something very difficult, you succeed in achieving it. 成功完成，做成（困难的事情）

  • The National League for Democracy pulled off a landslide victory. [VERB PREP. noun]

    全国民主联盟获得了压倒性的胜利。

  • It will be a very, very fine piece of mountaineering if they pull it off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    如果他们能成功，那将是一次非常了不起的登山壮举。

2 PHRASAL VERB （车或驾车者）停靠（路边） If a vehicle or driver pulls off the road, the vehicle stops by the side of the road. （车或驾车者）停靠（路边）

  • I pulled off the road at a small village pub. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我将车停在公路旁的一家乡村小酒馆前。

  • One evening, crossing a small creek, he pulled the car off the road. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    一天傍晚，他驶过一条小溪后把车停在了路边。
