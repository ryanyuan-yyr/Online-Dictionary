conspiracy theory
Word forms: plural conspiracy theories
N-COUNT 阴谋论 A conspiracy theory is a belief that a group of people are secretly trying to harm someone or achieve something. You usually use this term to suggest that you think this is unlikely. 阴谋论

  • Did you ever swallow the conspiracy theory about Kennedy?

    你有没有轻信过有关肯尼迪总统遇刺的阴谋论？
