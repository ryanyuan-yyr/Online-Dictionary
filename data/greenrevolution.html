green revolution
(also Green Revolution)
N-SING 绿色革命（尤指发展中国家为增加粮食产量而采用新品种和新耕种方法的举措） The green revolution is the increase in agricultural production that has been made possible by the use of new types of crops and new farming methods, especially in developing countries. 绿色革命（尤指发展中国家为增加粮食产量而采用新品种和新耕种方法的举措）
