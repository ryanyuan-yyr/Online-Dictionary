intercede
ɪntəʳsiːd ˌɪntərˈsid
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense intercedes, present participle interceding, past tense , past participle interceded
VERB 向…说情;调解;斡旋 If you intercede with someone, you try to persuade them to forgive someone or end their disagreement with them. 向…说情;调解;斡旋 [formal]

  • They asked my father to intercede with the king on their behalf. [VERB + with]

    他们请我父亲为他们向国王求情。

  • He had occasionally tried to intercede for me. [VERB for noun]

    他有时候也极力为我说情。

  • It has also asked Britain and the United States to intercede. [VERB]

    它也请了英国与美国进行斡旋。

SYN mediate, speak, plead, intervene
