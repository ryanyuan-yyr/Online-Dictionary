inspect
ɪnspekt ɪnˈspɛkt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense inspects, present participle inspecting, past tense , past participle inspected
1 VERB 检查;审视 If you inspect something, you look at every part of it carefully in order to find out about it or check that it is all right. 检查;审视

  • Elaine went outside to inspect the playing field. [VERB noun]

    伊莱恩走到外面查看操场。

  • Cut the fruit in half and inspect the pips: if they are turning slightly brown it is ready for harvesting. [VERB noun]

    把果实切成两半，检查一下其中的籽：如果有点成棕色，那就该收获了。

SYN examine, check, look at, view  
inspection

  • He had completed his inspection of the doors.

    他已经检查完所有的门。

  • From a distance, the birds appear mainly green in colour. However, closer inspection reveals that their chests are banded with yellow.

    从远处看，这些鸟主要是绿色的。但是,近距离仔细看，就会发现它们的胸部带有黄色条纹。

2 VERB 视察;检阅 When an official inspects a place or a group of people, they visit it and check it carefully, for example in order to find out whether regulations are being obeyed. 视察;检阅

  • The Public Utilities Commission inspects us once a year. [VERB noun]

    公共事业委员会每年到我们这里视察一次。

  • Each hotel is inspected and, if it fulfils certain criteria, is recommended. [VERB noun]

    会对每家旅馆都进行检查,如果它达到了一定的标准,就获得推荐。
