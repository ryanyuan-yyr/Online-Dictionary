parley
pɑːʳli ˈpɑrli
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense parleys, present participle parleying, past tense , past participle parleyed
1 N-VAR （对立双方的）和谈，会谈，谈判 A parley is a discussion between two opposing people or groups in which both sides try to come to an agreement. （对立双方的）和谈，会谈，谈判 [old-fashioned]
SYN discussion, conference, meeting, talk(s)  
2 V-RECIP （对立双方）和谈，会谈，谈判 When two opposing people or groups parley, they meet to discuss something in order to come to an agreement. （对立双方）和谈，会谈，谈判 [humorous, or informal]

  • ...a place where we meet and parley. [VERB]

    我们见面协商的地方

  • I don't think you've ever tried parleying with Gleed, have you? [VERB + with]

    我想你没有尝试和格利德谈和过，是吗？

SYN discuss, talk, speak, negotiate
