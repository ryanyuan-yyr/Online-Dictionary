violet
vaɪəlɪt ˈvaɪəlɪt ˈvaɪlɪt
Word forms: plural violets
1 N-COUNT 紫罗兰 A violet is a small plant that has purple or white flowers in the spring. 紫罗兰
2 COLOUR 紫罗兰色;紫色 Something that is violet is a bluish-purple colour. 紫罗兰色;紫色

  • The light was beginning to drain from a violet sky.

    光线开始从紫罗兰色的天空渐渐消退。

3 PHRASE 羞怯的人;腼腆的人 If you say that someone is no shrinking violet, you mean that they are not at all shy. 羞怯的人;腼腆的人

  • When it comes to expressing himself he is no shrinking violet.

    他在表达自己的想法时毫不羞怯。

  • None of the women he paints, however, could be described as shrinking violets.

    然而，他画过的所有女人中没有一个是羞涩腼腆的。
