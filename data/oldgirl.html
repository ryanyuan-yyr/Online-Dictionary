old girl
Word forms: plural old girls
1 N-COUNT 女校友 You can refer to a woman who used to be a pupil at a particular school or university as an old girl . 女校友 [British]

  • ...the St Mary's Ascot Old Girls' Reunion Lunch.

    圣玛丽阿斯科特女子寄宿学校校友团聚午餐

2 N-COUNT 老太婆；中年老女人 If you refer to an old or middle-aged woman as an old girl, you are referring to her in a disrespectful way. 老太婆；中年老女人 [British, informal, disapproval]

  • The old girl in there runs the saloon bar at the pub in the evenings.

    那边的那个老女人晚上负责照看酒馆里的雅座酒吧。
