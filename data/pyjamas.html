pyjamas
pɪdʒɑːməz pəˈdʒɑməz pəˈdʒɑːməz pəˈdʒæməz
The spelling pajamas is used in American English. The forms pyjama and pajama are used as modifiers. 拼写pajamas用于美国英语。pyjama 和 pajama 用作修饰语。
N-PLURAL [oft a pair of NOUN] （一套）睡衣裤 A pair of pyjamas consists of loose trousers and a loose jacket that people wear in bed. （一套）睡衣裤
