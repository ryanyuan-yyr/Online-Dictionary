carcass

  • A cluster of vultures crouched on the carcass of a dead buffalo. [+ of]

    一群秃鹫蹲伏在一具野牛的尸体上。

  • Prepare the chicken stock using the carcases, the diced vegetables and herbs.

    做鸡汤的用料有鸡肉、切成块的蔬菜和香料。

SYN body, remains, corpse, skeleton

  • ...the carcass of a rusted tractor.

    一辆生锈的拖拉机残骸
