amethyst
æməθɪst ˈæmɪθɪst
Word forms: plural amethysts
1 N-VAR 紫（水）晶，水碧（有时用于制作珠宝） Amethysts are clear purple stones, sometimes used to make jewellery. 紫（水）晶，水碧（有时用于制作珠宝）

  • The necklace consisted of amethysts set in gold.

    这是一条金镶紫水晶项链。

  • ...rows of amethyst beads.

    一排排紫水晶珠子

  • Amethyst was believed by the Greeks to protect a person from drunkenness.

    希腊人认为紫水晶可以防止人喝醉。

2 COLOUR 淡紫色（的） Amethyst is used to describe things that are pale purple in colour. 淡紫色（的）

  • ...as the colours changed from green to amethyst.

    当颜色从绿色转变成淡紫色

  • ...amethyst glass.

    淡紫色的杯子
