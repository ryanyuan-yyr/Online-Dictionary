plum
plʌm plʌm
Word forms: plural plums
1 N-COUNT 李子；梅子 A plum is a small, sweet fruit with a smooth red or yellow skin and a stone in the middle. 李子；梅子
SYN prize, find, pick, cream  
2 COLOUR 紫红色；青紫色 Something that is plum or plum-coloured is a dark reddish-purple colour. 紫红色；青紫色

  • ...plum-coloured silk.

    紫红色的丝绸

3 ADJ [ADJ n] （工作、合同、角色等）令人垂涎的，吸引人的 A plum job, contract, or role is a very good one that a lot of people would like. （工作、合同、角色等）令人垂涎的，吸引人的 [journalism]

  • Laura landed a plum job with a smart art gallery.

    劳拉在一家时尚画廊找到了份美差。
