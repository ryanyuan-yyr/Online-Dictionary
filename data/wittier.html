witty
wɪti ˈwɪti
Word forms: comparative wittier, superlative wittiest
ADJ-GRADED 机智幽默的;妙趣横生的;诙谐的 Someone or something that is witty is amusing in a clever way. 机智幽默的;妙趣横生的;诙谐的

  • His plays were very good, very witty.

    他的剧作非常精彩，妙趣横生。

  • He is a very witty speaker.

    他说话妙语连珠。

SYN humorous, gay, original, brilliant  
wittily

  • The play deals wittily and intelligently with the pain of betrayal.

    该剧讲的是遭到背叛的痛苦，语言诙谐，充满智慧。

  • 'Count Dracula, I presume,' I said wittily.

    “我想是德拉库拉伯爵吧。”我打趣地说。
