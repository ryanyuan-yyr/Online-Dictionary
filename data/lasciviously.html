lascivious
ləsɪviəs ləˈsɪviəs
ADJ-GRADED 好色的；淫欲的 If you describe someone as lascivious, you disapprove of them because they show a very strong interest in sex. 好色的；淫欲的 [disapproval]

  • The man was lascivious, sexually perverted and insatiable.

    这个人是一个欲壑难填的变态色情狂。

  • ...their lewd and lascivious talk.

    他们之间下流猥亵的对话

SYN lustful, sensual, immoral, randy [informal , mainly British]
