chew up
1 PHRASAL VERB 细细咀嚼;嚼得稀烂 If you chew food up, you chew it until it is completely crushed or soft. 细细咀嚼;嚼得稀烂 [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • I took one of the pills and chewed it up. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我拿起一粒药丸，把它嚼碎。

2 PHRASAL VERB 毁坏;破坏 If something is chewed up, it has been destroyed or damaged in some way. 毁坏;破坏 [informal]

  • Every spring the ozone is chewed up, and the hole appears. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    每年春天，臭氧遭到破坏，空洞就出现了。

  • ...rebels who are now chewing up Government-held territory. [VERB PREP. noun]

    正在逐渐蚕食政府控制的领地的叛乱分子

  • This town is notorious for chewing people up and spitting them out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    这个城市恶名昭彰，它把人作践了，然后抛弃掉。
