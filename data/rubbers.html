rubber
rʌbəʳ ˈrʌbər
Word forms: plural rubbers
1 N-UNCOUNT 橡胶;合成橡胶 Rubber is a strong, waterproof, elastic substance made from the juice of a tropical tree or produced chemically. It is used for making tyres, boots, and other products. 橡胶;合成橡胶

  • ...the smell of burning rubber.

    橡胶燃烧的气味

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 橡胶做的;橡胶制成的 Rubber things are made of rubber. 橡胶做的;橡胶制成的

  • ...rubber gloves.

    橡胶手套

  • ...a rubber ball.

    橡胶球

3 N-COUNT 橡皮擦 A rubber is a small piece of rubber or other material that is used to remove mistakes that you have made while writing, drawing, or typing. 橡皮擦 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 eraser
4 N-COUNT 避孕套 A rubber is a condom. 避孕套 [US, informal]
5 N-COUNT (桥牌或惠斯特牌的)一盘，一局 In some card games, for example bridge or whist, a rubber is a match of three games. (桥牌或惠斯特牌的)一盘，一局

  • Let's have a few rubbers of bridge.

    我们玩几局桥牌吧。
