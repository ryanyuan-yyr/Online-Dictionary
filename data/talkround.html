talk round
in AM, usually use 美国英语通常用 talk around
PHRASAL VERB 说服;劝说…回心转意 If you talk someone round, you persuade them to change their mind so that they agree with you, or agree to do what you want them to do 说服;劝说…回心转意

  • He went to the house to try to talk her round. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他到她家里，试图劝她回心转意。

  • It advises salesmen to talk round reluctant customers over a cup of tea. [VERB PREP. noun]

    它建议销售人员和犹豫不决、不愿购买的顾客一边喝茶，一边说服他们。
