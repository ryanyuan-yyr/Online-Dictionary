dispatcher
dɪspætʃər dɪˈspætʃər ˈdɪsˌpætʃər
Word forms: dispatchers
N-COUNT (警察局或消防队的)调度员A dispatcher is someone who works for an organization such as the police or the fire department and whose job is to send members of the organization to the places where they are needed. (警察局或消防队的)调度员 [US]

    警察局的调度员在早上十点半左右接到电话。
