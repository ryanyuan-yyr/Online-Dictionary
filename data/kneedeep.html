knee-deep
ˈniˈdip
1 ADJ 齐膝深的 Something that is knee-deep is as high as your knees. 齐膝深的

  • The water was only knee-deep.

    水只有齐膝深。

  • ...knee-deep snow.

    齐膝深的雪

2 ADJ [ADJ after verb] 没膝的 If a person or a place is knee-deep in something such as water, the level of the water comes up to a person's knees. 没膝的

  • They spent much of their time knee-deep in mud. [+ in]

    他们在齐膝深的泥地里费了好长时间。

  • They sometimes sank knee-deep into the marsh.

    他们不时陷入没膝的湿地。
