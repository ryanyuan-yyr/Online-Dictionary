uninformed
ʌnɪnfɔːʳmd ˌʌnɪnˈfɔːmd
ADJ-GRADED 未被告知（或通知）的；知识（或信息）贫乏的 If you describe someone as uninformed, you mean that they have very little knowledge or information about a particular situation or subject. 未被告知（或通知）的；知识（或信息）贫乏的

  • He could not complain that he was uninformed about the true nature of the regime. [+ about]

    他不能抱怨说没人告诉他该政权的实质。

  • Cases of child abuse often go unreported or ignored by uninformed citizens.

    虐待儿童案件常常没人举报，或者为缺乏相关知识的公民所忽略。
