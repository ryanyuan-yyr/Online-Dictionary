continuing education
kənˌtɪnjuːɪŋˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT 继续教育;成人教育 Continuing education is education for adults in a variety of subjects, most of which are practical, not academic. 继续教育;成人教育

  • But those lacking a family tradition of continuing education still face difficulties.

    但是那些并无接受继续教育这种家庭传统的人仍然面临着困难。
