hideously
hɪdiəsli
1 ADV [usually ADVERB adjective/-ed, oft ADVERB after verb] 可怕地;丑陋地 You use hideously to emphasize that something is very ugly or unattractive. 可怕地;丑陋地 [emphasis]

  • Everything is hideously ugly.

    一切都非常丑陋。

  • He has been left hideously disfigured by plastic surgery.

    整形外科手术导致他完全毁容了。

2 ADV-GRADED [ADVERB adjective/-ed] 令人无法接受地;极其恶劣地 You can use hideously to emphasize that something is very unpleasant or unacceptable. 令人无法接受地;极其恶劣地 [emphasis]

  • ...a hideously complex program.

    极其复杂的程序

  • ...a simple but undoubtedly hideously expensive black suit.

    款式简单但无疑又贵得离谱的黑色套装
