incompetence
ɪnkɒmpɪtəns
N-UNCOUNT 无能力;不称职;不合格 If you refer to someone's incompetence, you are criticizing them because they are unable to do their job or a task properly. 无能力;不称职;不合格 [disapproval]

  • The incompetence of government officials is appalling. [+ of]

    政府官员的无能令人震惊。

  • ...his incompetence in failing to conduct full inquiries.

    他未能实施全面调查

SYN ineptitude, inability, inadequacy, incapacity
