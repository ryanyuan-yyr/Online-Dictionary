coma
koʊmə ˈkoʊmə
Word forms: plural comas
N-COUNT [usu in/into N] 昏迷 Someone who is in a coma is in a state of deep unconsciousness. 昏迷

  • She was in a coma for seven weeks.

    她已经昏迷7个星期了。

  • She had slipped into a coma by the time she reached hospital.

    到医院时，她已陷入昏迷。

SYN unconsciousness, trance, oblivion, lethargy
