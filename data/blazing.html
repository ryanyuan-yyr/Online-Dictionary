blazing
bleɪzɪŋ ˈbleɪzɪŋ
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 炽热的；炎热的 Blazing sun or blazing hot weather is very hot. 炽热的；炎热的

  • Quite a few people were eating outside in the blazing sun.

    不少人在外面的炎炎烈日下吃饭。

  • ...freezing cold winters and blazing hot summers.

    严冬酷暑

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （争吵）激烈的 When people have a blazing row, they quarrel in a very noisy and excited way. （争吵）激烈的 [British]

  • My husband has just had a blazing row with his boss.

    我丈夫刚和他老板大吵了一架。

SYN furious, excited, angry, raging
