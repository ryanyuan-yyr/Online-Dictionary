stringency
strɪndʒənsi ˈstrɪndʒənsi
N-UNCOUNT 银根紧缩;财政紧缩;手头紧 Financial stringency is a situation in which a government or person does not have much money or is trying not to spend much. 银根紧缩;财政紧缩;手头紧 [formal]

  • In times of financial stringency it is clear that public expenditure has to be closely scrutinized.

    财政紧缩时期，无疑要对公共支出进行严格审查。
