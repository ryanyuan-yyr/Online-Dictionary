deficit financing
1 N-UNCOUNT 赤字财政经济政策Deficit financing is an economic policy in which a government spends more money raised by borrowing than it receives in revenue. 赤字财政经济政策

  • ...detailed plans to end deficit financing by early next year.

    ... 到明年年初结束赤字财政经济政策的详细计划。

2 N-UNCOUNT 赤字财政Deficit financing is the financing of government spending through borrowing rather than revenue. 赤字财政 [US]

  • The overriding problem is the continuing deficit financing of the federal government.

    最重要的问题是联邦政府的持续赤字财政。
