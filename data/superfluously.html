superfluous
suːpɜːʳfluəs suːˈpɜːflʊəs səˈpɜrfluəs suˈpɜrfluəs
ADJ-GRADED 多余的;过剩的;不必要的 Something that is superfluous is unnecessary or is no longer needed. 多余的;过剩的;不必要的

  • My presence at the afternoon's proceedings was superfluous.

    我去参加下午的活动是多此一举。

  • I rid myself of many superfluous belongings and habits that bothered me.

    我把很多无用的物品都丢掉了，并且改掉了一些让我烦心的习惯。

SYN excess, surplus, redundant, remaining
