wonderland
wʌndəʳlænd ˈwʌndərˌlænd
Word forms: plural wonderlands
1 N-UNCOUNT (童话中的)仙境，奇境 Wonderland is an imaginary world that exists in fairy stories. (童话中的)仙境，奇境
2 N-COUNT [usually singular, usually with supplement, oft NOUN of n] 非常奇妙的地方;极美丽的地方 You can refer to a place as a wonderland when it is strange and very beautiful or exciting. 非常奇妙的地方;极美丽的地方

  • Children find Lake George Village a wonderland of amusement parks.

    孩子们觉得乔治湖村是个绝妙的游乐园。
