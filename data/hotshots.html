hotshot
hɒtʃɒt ˈhɑtˌʃɑt
Word forms: plural hotshots
N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 高手;能人;前途无量的人 If you refer to someone as a hotshot, you mean they are very good at a particular job and are going to be very successful. 高手;能人;前途无量的人

  • ...a bunch of corporate hotshots.

    一群公司精英

  • She's a hotshot broker on Wall Street.

    她是华尔街一名业绩斐然的经纪人。
