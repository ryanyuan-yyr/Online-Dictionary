derogatory
dɪrɒgətri -tɔːri dɪˈrɑgəˌtɔri
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 贬低的;贬损的 If you make a derogatory remark or comment about someone or something, you express your low opinion of them. 贬低的;贬损的

  • He refused to withdraw derogatory remarks made about his boss.

    他拒绝收回那些贬损老板的话。

SYN disparaging, damaging, offensive, slighting
