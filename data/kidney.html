kidney
kɪdni ˈkɪdni
Word forms: plural kidneys
1 N-COUNT 肾；肾脏 Your kidneys are the organs in your body that take waste matter from your blood and send it out of your body as urine. 肾；肾脏
2 N-VAR （供食用的）腰子 Kidneys are the kidneys of an animal, for example a lamb, calf, or pig, that are eaten as meat. （供食用的）腰子

  • ...lambs' kidneys.

    羊腰子

  • ...steak and kidney pie.

    牛排和腰花馅饼
