N-COUNT 含义甚广的术语;笼统的类别 A catch-all is a term or category which includes many different things. 含义甚广的术语;笼统的类别

  • Globalisation is a catch-all to describe increased international trade.

    “全球化”是个泛称，用以描述增进了的国际贸易。

  • Indigestion is a catch-all term for any kind of stomach distress.

    “消化不良”是个笼统的术语，可以指各种胃部不适。
