impoverishment
ɪmpɒvərɪʃmənt
N-UNCOUNT 穷困化状况(或过程) Impoverishment is the state or process of being impoverished. 穷困化状况(或过程)

  • ...a meeting of experts on how the impoverishment of Africa can be reversed.

    研讨如何逆转非洲穷困化状况的专家会议

  • National isolation can only cause economic and cultural impoverishment.

    锁国只会导致经济和文化上的困厄。
