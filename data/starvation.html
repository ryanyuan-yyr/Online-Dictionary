starvation
stɑːʳveɪʃən stɑrˈveɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [usu of/from N] 极度饥饿;饿死 Starvation is extreme suffering or death, caused by lack of food. 极度饥饿;饿死

  • Over three hundred people have died of starvation since the beginning of the year.

    从年初起，已有 300 多人饿死。

SYN lack of food, famine, malnourishment, food deprivation
