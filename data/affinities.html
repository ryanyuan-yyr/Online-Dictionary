affinity
əfɪnɪti əˈfɪnɪti æˈfɪnɪti
Word forms: plural affinities
1 N-SING 亲近；熟稔 If you have an affinity with someone or something, you feel that you are similar to them or that you know and understand them very well. 亲近；熟稔

  • He has a close affinity with the landscape he knew when he was growing up. [+ with]

    他对这片从小就了解的土地有着一种归属感。

  • There is a natural affinity between British and Asian women.

    英国女性和亚洲女性之间有一种自然的亲近感。

SYN attraction, liking, leaning, sympathy  
2 N-COUNT 类似；近似 If people or things have an affinity with each other, they are similar in some ways. 类似；近似 [formal]

  • The two plots share certain obvious affinities.

    这两个情节有某种明显的相似。

SYN similarity, relationship, relation, connection
