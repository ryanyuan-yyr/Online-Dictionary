invent
ɪnvent ɪnˈvɛnt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense invents, present participle inventing, past tense , past participle invented
1 VERB 发明;创造;首创 If you invent something such as a machine or process, you are the first person to think of it or make it. 发明;创造;首创

  • He invented the first electric clock. [VERB noun]

    他发明了第一个电动机械钟。

  • Writing had not been invented as yet. [VERB noun]

    书写体系当时还没有创造出来。

SYN create, make, produce, develop  
2 VERB 编造;捏造;虚构 If you invent a story or excuse, you try to make other people believe that it is true when in fact it is not. 编造;捏造;虚构

  • I stood still, trying to invent a plausible excuse. [VERB noun]

    我站着不动,试图编个说得过去的借口。

SYN make up, devise, concoct, forge
