potency
poʊtənsi ˈpoʊtənsi
1 N-UNCOUNT （人、行动或思想的）影响力，支配力 Potency is the power and influence that a person, action, or idea has to affect or change people's lives, feelings, or beliefs. （人、行动或思想的）影响力，支配力

  • They testify to the extraordinary potency of his personality. [+ of]

    这些事情证明了他非凡的人格魅力。

  • All their songs have a lingering potency.

    他们所有的歌曲都有耐人寻味的魅力。

SYN influence, might, force, control  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually with poss] （药物等的）效力；药力；药效 The potency of a drug, poison, or other chemical is its strength. （药物等的）效力；药力；药效

  • Sunscreen can lose its potency if left over winter in the bathroom cabinet.

    如果把防晒霜在盥洗室的壁橱里放一个冬天，就有可能失效。

3 N-UNCOUNT （男子的）性交能力，性机能 Potency is the ability of a man to have sex. （男子的）性交能力，性机能

  • Alcohol abuse in men can cause loss of sex drive and reduced potency.

    男子酗酒可能导致性欲丧失和性功能下降。

SYN vigour, puissance
