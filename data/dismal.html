dismal
dɪzməl ˈdɪzməl
1 ADJ-GRADED 忧郁的;凄凉的;令人沮丧的 Something that is dismal is bad in a sad or depressing way. 忧郁的;凄凉的;令人沮丧的

  • ...Israel's dismal record in the Olympics.

    以色列在奥运会上让人沮丧的记录

  • My prospects of returning to a suitable job are dismal.

    我重新找到一个合适的工作岗位的希望很渺茫。

  • It was a dismal failure.

    这是个让人沮丧的失败。

SYN bad, awful, dreadful, rotten [informal]  
dismally

  • He failed dismally in his opening match.

    他在揭幕战中惨败。

2 ADJ-GRADED (尤指外表)阴暗的，阴沉的，沉闷的 Something that is dismal is sad and depressing, especially in appearance. (尤指外表)阴暗的，阴沉的，沉闷的

  • The main part of the hospital is pretty dismal but the children's ward is really lively.

    医院的主体部分死气沉沉，但是儿科病房却充满生气。

  • ...a dark dismal day with rain falling steadily.

    天空昏暗、阴雨绵绵的一天

  • You can't occupy yourself with dismal thoughts all the time.

    不要终日陷在阴郁的思绪中。

SYN sad, gloomy, melancholy, black
