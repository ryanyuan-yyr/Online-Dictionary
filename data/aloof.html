aloof
əluːf əˈluf
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 冷漠的；离群的 Someone who is aloof is not very friendly and does not like to spend time with other people. 冷漠的；离群的 [disapproval]

  • He seemed aloof and detached.

    他显得冷漠离群。

SYN distant, cold, reserved, cool  
aloofness

  • He had an air of aloofness about him.

    他给人一种冷冰冰的感觉。

2 ADJ-GRADED 置身事外的 If someone stays aloof from something, they do not become involved with it. 置身事外的 [formal]

  • The Government is keeping aloof from the controversy. [+ from]

    政府对争论保持置身事外的态度。

  • I will hold myself aloof from wrong and corruption.

    我会让自己远离错误和腐败。
