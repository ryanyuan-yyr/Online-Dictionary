has-been ˈhæzˌbɪn
Word forms: plural has-beens
N-COUNT 过气的人物;风光不再的人 If you describe someone as a has-been, you are indicating in an unkind way that they were important or respected in the past, but they are not now. 过气的人物;风光不再的人 [disapproval]

  • ...the so-called experts and various has-beens who foist opinions on us.

    将观点强加给我们的所谓专家和各式过气人物
