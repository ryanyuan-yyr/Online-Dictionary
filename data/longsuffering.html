long-suffering
ˈlɔŋˈsʌfərɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 长期忍受的；饱受磨难的 Someone who is long-suffering patiently puts up with a lot of trouble or unhappiness, especially when it is caused by someone else. 长期忍受的；饱受磨难的

  • He went back to Yorkshire to join his loyal, long-suffering wife.

    他回到约克郡，与对他忠贞不贰、饱受磨难的妻子团聚。

SYN uncomplaining, patient, resigned, forgiving
