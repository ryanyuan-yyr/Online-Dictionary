clink
klɪŋk klɪŋk
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense clinks, present participle clinking, past tense , past participle clinked
V-RECIP-ERG (使)发出叮当声;(使)叮当作响 If objects made of glass, pottery, or metal clink or if you clink them, they touch each other and make a short, light sound. (使)发出叮当声;(使)叮当作响

  • She clinked her glass against his. [V n + against/with]

    她和他碰了一下杯。

  • They clinked glasses. [VERB noun]

    他们碰了杯。

  • The empty whisky bottle clinked against the seat. [VERB + against]

    空威士忌酒瓶碰到座位上，叮当作响。

  • Their glasses clinked, their eyes met. [VERB]

    他们举杯相碰，目光交会。

Clink is also a noun.

  • ...the clink of a spoon in a cup.

    杯勺相碰的叮当声
