high street
Word forms: plural high streets
1 N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES （市镇的）主要街道，大街 The high street of a town is the main street where most of the shops and banks are. （市镇的）主要街道，大街 [mainly British]

  • Vegetarian restaurants and health food shops are springing up in every high street.

    每条大街上都冒出很多素食餐馆和保健食品店。

  • ...West Bromwich high street.

    西布罗米奇的主要街道

in AM, use 美国英语用 Main Street
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （大型银行、大型商业企业）开在商业区大街的，设在城市主要商业区的 High street banks and businesses are companies which have branches in the main shopping areas of most towns. （大型银行、大型商业企业）开在商业区大街的，设在城市主要商业区的 [mainly British]

  • The scanners are available from high street stores.

    扫描仪在大型商店有售。
