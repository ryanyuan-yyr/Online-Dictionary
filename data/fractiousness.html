fractious
frækʃəs ˈfrækʃəs
ADJ-GRADED 易怒的;烦躁的;脾气不好的 If you describe someone as fractious, you disapprove of them because they become upset or angry very quickly about small unimportant things. 易怒的;烦躁的;脾气不好的 [disapproval]

  • ...fractious national movements.

    难以控制的全国运动

  • Nancy was in a fractious mood.

    南希情绪烦躁。

  • The children were predictably fractious.

    孩子们肯定会耍脾气。

SYN irritable, cross, awkward, unruly
