unabated
ʌnəbeɪtɪd ˌʌnəˈbeɪtɪd
ADJ [usu ADJ after v, oft ADJ n, verb-link ADJ] 未减弱的；未减少的；未衰退的 If something continues unabated, it continues without any reduction in intensity or amount. 未减弱的；未减少的；未衰退的

  • The fighting has continued unabated for over 24 hours. [+ for]

    激战已经持续了24小时以上。

  • ...his unabated enthusiasm for cinema.

    他对电影始终不减的热情
