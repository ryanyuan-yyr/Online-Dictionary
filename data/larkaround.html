lark around
(also lark about)
PHRASAL VERB 胡闹；嬉戏 If you lark around or lark about, you behave in a playful, childish, and silly way, often in order to make people laugh. 胡闹；嬉戏 [British, informal]

  • The other actors complained about me larking about when they were trying to concentrate. [VERB PREP.]

    其他演员埋怨我在他们想专心的时候在一旁胡闹。
