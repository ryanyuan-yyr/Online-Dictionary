injury
ɪndʒəri ˈɪndʒəri
Word forms: plural injuries
1 N-VAR 伤害;损伤 An injury is damage done to a person's or an animal's body. →see usage note at: wound 伤害;损伤

  • Four police officers sustained serious injuries in the explosion.

    4名警官在爆炸中受重伤。

  • The two other passengers escaped serious injury.

    另外两名乘客逃过一劫，未受重伤。

2 N-VAR (感情、名誉上的)伤害,损害 If someone suffers injury to their feelings, they are badly upset by something. If they suffer injury to their reputation, their reputation is seriously harmed. (感情、名誉上的)伤害,损害 [law]

  • She was awarded £3,500 for injury to her feelings. [+ to]

    她判得 3,500 英镑精神损失赔偿。

SYN wrong, abuse, offence, insult  
3 to add insult to injury→see: insult
