scuff
skʌf skʌf
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense scuffs, present participle scuffing, past tense , past participle scuffed
1 V-ERG (使)磨损;(使)磨坏 If you scuff something or if it scuffs, you mark the surface by scraping it against other things or by scraping other things against it. (使)磨损;(使)磨坏

  • Constant wheelchair use will scuff almost any floor surface. [VERB noun]

    任何地板上经常有轮椅走动几乎都会有所磨损。

  • Molded plastic is almost indestructible, but scuffs easily. [VERB adv.]

    注塑几乎弄不坏，但是容易磨损。

scuffed

  • ...scuffed brown shoes.

    几双磨坏的棕色鞋

2 VERB 拖着脚走 If you scuff your feet, you pull them along the ground as you walk. 拖着脚走

  • Polly, bewildered and embarrassed, dropped her head and scuffed her feet. [VERB noun]

    波莉既困惑又尴尬，低下头拖着脚走开了。
