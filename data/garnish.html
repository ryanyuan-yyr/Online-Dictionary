garnish
gɑːʳnɪʃ ˈgɑrnɪʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense garnishes, present participle garnishing, past tense garnished
1 N-VAR （菜肴的)装饰菜，配菜 A garnish is a small amount of salad, herbs, or other food that is used to decorate cooked or prepared food. （菜肴的)装饰菜，配菜

  • ...a garnish of chopped raw onion, tomato and fresh coriander.

    用生洋葱末、西红柿和新鲜香菜切好后搭配的装饰菜

  • Reserve some watercress for garnish.

    留点豆瓣菜作配菜。

SYN decoration, ornament, embellishment, adornment  
2 VERB 给（菜肴）加装饰菜 If you garnish cooked or prepared food, you decorate it with a garnish. 给（菜肴）加装饰菜

  • She had finished the vegetables and was garnishing the roast. [VERB noun]

    她已经做好了蔬菜，正在给烤肉添加饰菜。

SYN decorate, adorn, ornament, embellish
