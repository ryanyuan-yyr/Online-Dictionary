creak
kriːk kriːk krik
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense creaks, present participle creaking, past tense , past participle creaked
VERB 嘎吱作响 If something creaks, it makes a short, high-pitched sound when it moves. 嘎吱作响

  • The bed-springs creaked. [VERB]

    床垫的弹簧嘎吱作响。

  • The steps creaked beneath his feet. [VERB]

    楼梯在他脚下嘎吱作响。

  • The door creaked open. [VERB adj.ive]

    门嘎吱一声开了。

  • ...the creaking stairs. [VERB-ing]

    嘎吱作响的楼梯

SYN squeak, grind, scrape, groan  
Creak is also a noun.

  • The door was pulled open with a creak.

    门嘎吱一声被拉开了。
