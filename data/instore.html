in-store
(also instore)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 商场(或超市、商店)内的 In-store facilities are facilities that are available within a department store, supermarket or other large shop. 商场(或超市、商店)内的

  • ...in-store banking.

    设在商场内的银行业务

  • ...an instore bakery.

    设在超市内的面包店

In-store is also an adverb.

  • Ask in-store for details.

    详情请进店咨询。
