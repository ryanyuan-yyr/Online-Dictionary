fairy tale
(also fairytale)Word forms: plural fairy tales
1 N-COUNT 神话;童话 A fairy tale is a story for children involving magical events and imaginary creatures. 神话;童话

  • She was like a princess in a fairy tale.

    她就像童话里的公主。

SYN folk tale, romance, traditional story  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 童话般的;难以置信的;神奇的 A fairy tale place or situation is so wonderful that you can hardly believe that it is real. 童话般的;难以置信的;神奇的

  • ...an excursion to the fairy-tale castle of Neuschwanstein.

    到童话般的新天鹅堡的短途旅行

  • She loved him so much: it was a fairytale romance.

    她深深爱着他：这是个童话般的浪漫故事。
