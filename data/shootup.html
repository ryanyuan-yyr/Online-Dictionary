shoot up
1 PHRASAL VERB 迅速成长;迅速增加 If something shoots up, it grows or increases very quickly. 迅速成长;迅速增加 [Also V P + to]

  • Sales shot up by 9% last month. [V P + by]

    销售量上个月猛增了 9％。

  • The fair market value of the property shot up. [VERB PREP.]

    这处房产的公平市价增长迅速。

SYN increase, grow, expand, go up  
2 PHRASAL VERB 注射毒品 If a drug addict shoots up, they inject a quantity of drugs into their body. 注射毒品 [informal]

  • Drug addicts shoot up in the back alleys. [VERB PREP.]

    瘾君子们在后街小巷里注射毒品。

  • We shot up heroin in the playground. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我们在操场上注射海洛因。
