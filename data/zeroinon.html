zero in on
1 PHRASAL VERB 瞄准（目标）；向…移动 To zero in on a target means to aim at it or move towards it. 瞄准（目标）；向…移动

  • He raised the binoculars again and zeroed in on an eleventh-floor room. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    他再一次举起双筒望远镜，瞄准了11层的一个房间。

SYN zoom in on, focus on, aim at, train on  
2 PHRASAL VERB 全神贯注于（问题或主题） If you zero in on a problem or subject, you give it your full attention. 全神贯注于（问题或主题）

  • Many of the other major daily newspapers have not really zeroed in on the problem. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    其他许多重要日报并未将注意力集中在这个问题上。

  • Critics have zeroed in on his plan to raise gasoline taxes 10 cents a gallon. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    批评者将矛头对准他打算将汽油税每加仑提高10美分的计划上。

SYN focus on, concentrate on, home in on, pinpoint
