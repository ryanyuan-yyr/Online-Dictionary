chisel
tʃɪzəl ˈtʃɪzəl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense chisels, present participle chiselling, past tense , past participle chiselled
in AM, use 美国英语用 chiseling, chiseled
1 N-COUNT 凿子;錾子 A chisel is a tool that has a long metal blade with a sharp edge at the end. It is used for cutting and shaping wood and stone. 凿子;錾子

  • ...a hammer and chisel.

    锤子和凿子

2 VERB 凿，雕，刻(木头、石头) If you chisel wood or stone, you cut and shape it using a chisel. 凿，雕，刻(木头、石头)

  • They sit and chisel the stone to size. [VERB noun]

    他们坐下来把石头凿成合适的大小。
