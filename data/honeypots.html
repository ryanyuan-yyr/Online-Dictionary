honeypot
hʌnipɒt
Word forms: plural honeypots
1 N-COUNT 极具吸引力的事物;极受欢迎的事物 If you describe something as a honeypot, you mean that it is very desirable or very popular. 极具吸引力的事物;极受欢迎的事物

  • ...traditional tourist honeypots such as London, Bath, Edinburgh, and York.

    诸如伦敦、巴斯、爱丁堡、约克等传统的旅游胜地

2 PHRASE 蜂拥而至地;成群地;众多地 If something attracts people like bees to a honeypot or like bees round a honeypot, it attracts people in large numbers. 蜂拥而至地;成群地;众多地

  • This is the show that attracts computer industry people like bees to a honeypot.

    这就是那个吸引计算机业界人士蜂拥而至的展览。
