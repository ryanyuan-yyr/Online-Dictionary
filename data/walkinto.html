walk into
1 PHRASAL VERB (不小心)遇上;(不慎)陷入 If you walk into an unpleasant situation, you become involved in it without expecting to, especially because you have been careless. (不小心)遇上;(不慎)陷入

  • He's walking into a situation that he absolutely can't control. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他正陷入自己完全无法控制的局面。

2 PHRASAL VERB 轻易获得(工作) If you walk into a job, you manage to get it very easily. 轻易获得(工作) [informal]

  • When I left school, I could walk into any job. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我毕业后可以不费吹灰之力地找到任何工作。
