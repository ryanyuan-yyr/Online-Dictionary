degeneracy
dɪdʒenərəsi dɪˈdʒɛnərəsi
N-UNCOUNT 堕落;腐化 If you refer to the behaviour of a group of people as degeneracy, you mean that you think it is shocking, immoral, or disgusting. 堕落;腐化 [disapproval]

  • ...the moral degeneracy of society.

    社会的道德堕落
