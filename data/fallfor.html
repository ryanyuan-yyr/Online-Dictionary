fall for
1 PHRASAL VERB 爱上If you fall for someone, you are strongly attracted to them and start loving them. 爱上

  • He was fantastically handsome – I just fell for him right away. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他帅极了–我对他简直就是一见钟情。

SYN fall in love with, become infatuated with, be smitten by, be swept off your feet by  
2 PHRASAL VERB 被骗If you fall for a lie or trick, you believe it or are deceived by it. 被骗

  • It was just a line to get you out here, and you fell for it! [VERB PREP. noun]

    那只是一个让你到这儿来的谎言，你被骗了！

SYN be fooled by, be deceived by, be taken in by, be duped by
