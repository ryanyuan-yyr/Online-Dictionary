spanking
spæŋkɪŋ ˈspæŋkɪŋ
Word forms: plural spankings
1 N-COUNT (作为惩罚的)打屁股 If someone gives a child a spanking, they punish them by hitting them on the bottom several times with their hand. (作为惩罚的)打屁股

  • Andrea gave her son a sound spanking.

    安德烈娅给了儿子结结实实一顿痛打。

SYN smacking, hiding [informal] , whacking, slapping  
2 ADV [ADVERB adjective] 非常；极其 If you describe something as spanking new, spanking clean, or spanking white, you mean that it is very new, very clean, or very white. 非常；极其 [informal, emphasis]

  • ...a spanking new Mercedes.

    崭新的梅赛德斯车

  • The upstairs bedrooms were spanking clean.

    楼上的房间打扫得一尘不染。

3 ADJ [ADJ n] 飞快的;疾行的 If something moves at a spanking pace, it moves quickly. 飞快的;疾行的 [old-fashioned]

  • The film moves along at a spanking pace.

    影片节奏紧凑。

  • We rode at a spanking trot.

    我们骑着马疾驰而去。

SYN fast, quick, brisk, lively
