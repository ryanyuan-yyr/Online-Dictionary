string along
PHRASAL VERB 哄骗;愚弄 If you string someone along, you deceive them by letting them believe you have the same desires, beliefs, or hopes as them. 哄骗;愚弄 [informal]

  • She took advantage of him, stringing him along even after they were divorced. [VERB noun PREP.]

    她利用了他，甚至离婚后还在欺骗他。

SYN deceive, fool, take (someone) for a ride [informal] , kid [informal]
