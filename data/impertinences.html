impertinence
ɪmpɜːʳtɪnəns ɪmˈpɜrtənəns
Word forms: plural impertinences
N-VAR 不礼貌;傲慢;莽撞 If someone talks or behaves in a rather impolite and disrespectful way, you can call this behaviour impertinence or an impertinence . 不礼貌;傲慢;莽撞

  • He was punished for his impertinence.

    他因为冒失无礼受到惩罚。

SYN rudeness, nerve [informal] , cheek [informal] , face [informal]
