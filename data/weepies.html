weepy
wiːpi ˈwipi
Word forms: plural weepies
1 ADJ-GRADED 要哭的;眼泪汪汪的 Someone who is weepy is sad and likely to cry easily. 要哭的;眼泪汪汪的

  • I suddenly felt very weepy.

    我突然很想哭。

  • ...weepy moods.

    欲哭的情绪

SYN tearful, crying, weeping, sobbing  
2 N-COUNT 赚人眼泪的电影(或故事) A weepy is a film or a story which is sentimental and makes you cry. 赚人眼泪的电影(或故事) [informal]
SYN tear-jerker [informal]
