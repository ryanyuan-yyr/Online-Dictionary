let out
1 PHRASAL VERB 放出，释放（水、空气等）；呼（气） If something or someone lets water, air, or breath out, they allow it to flow out or escape. 放出，释放（水、空气等）；呼（气）

  • It lets sunlight in but doesn't let heat out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    它吸收阳光，但并不释放热量。

  • Meer let out his breath in a long sigh. [VERB PREP. noun]

    米尔长叹了一口气。

SYN release, discharge  
2 PHRASAL VERB 发出（声音） If you let out a particular sound, you make that sound. 发出（声音） [written] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • When she saw him, she let out a cry of horror. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她看见他时吓得大叫一声。

SYN emit, make, produce, give vent to  
3 See also: let
