baseless
beɪsləs ˈbeɪslɪs
1 ADJ 毫无根据的；无缘无故的 If you describe an accusation, rumour, or report as baseless, you mean that it is not true and is not based on facts. 毫无根据的；无缘无故的

  • The charges against her are baseless.

    对她的指控毫无根据。

  • ...baseless allegations of drug taking.

    毫无根据的吸毒指控

SYN unfounded, false, fabricated, unconfirmed
