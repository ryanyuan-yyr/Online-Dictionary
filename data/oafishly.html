oafish
oʊfɪʃ
ADJ-GRADED (尤指男性)无礼的，鲁钝的，笨手笨脚的 If you describe someone, especially a man or a boy, as oafish, you disapprove of their behaviour because you think that it is impolite, clumsy, or aggressive. (尤指男性)无礼的，鲁钝的，笨手笨脚的 [disapproval]

  • The bodyguards, as usual, were brave but oafish.

    这些保镖照旧勇气可嘉但鲁钝无礼。

  • ...oafish humour.

    粗野的幽默

SYN loutish, stupid, gross, thick
