call out
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤指在紧急情况下)命令…出动,请求…出动 If you call someone out, you order or request that they come to help, especially in an emergency. (尤指在紧急情况下)命令…出动,请求…出动

  • Colombia has called out the army and imposed emergency measures. [VERB PREP. noun]

    哥伦比亚出动了军队,并采取了紧急措施。

  • I called the doctor out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我叫了医生。

  • The fire brigade should always be called out to a house fire. [be V-ed P + to]

    一旦房屋失火,就应该马上叫消防队来。
