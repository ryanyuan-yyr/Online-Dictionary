hurt
hɜːʳt hwɜːt hɜrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hurts, present participle hurting, past tense , past participle hurt
1 VERB 使（自己或身体某部位）受伤 If you hurt yourself or hurt a part of your body, you feel pain because you have injured yourself. 使（自己或身体某部位）受伤

  • Yasin had seriously hurt himself while trying to escape from the police. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    亚辛试图从警察手中逃脱时受了重伤。

  • He had hurt his back in an accident. [VERB noun]

    他在一次事故中伤了后背。

SYN injure, damage, wound, cut  
2 VERB （身体某部位）感到疼痛 If a part of your body hurts, you feel pain there. （身体某部位）感到疼痛

  • His collar bone only hurt when he lifted his arm. [VERB]

    他的锁骨只有在抬臂时才感觉到痛。

SYN ache, be sore, be painful, burn  
3 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 受伤的 If you are hurt, you have been injured. 受伤的

  • His comrades asked him if he was hurt.

    战友问他是否受伤了。

  • They were dazed but did not seem to be badly hurt.

    他们有些神志不清，但看起来没有受重伤。

SYN injured, wounded, damaged, harmed  
4 VERB 弄伤，弄痛（他人） If you hurt someone, you cause them to feel pain. 弄伤，弄痛（他人）

  • I didn't mean to hurt her, only to keep her still. [VERB noun]

    我不是故意要弄痛她，只是想让她别乱动。

  • You're hurting my arm. [VERB noun]

    你弄痛我的胳膊了。

  • Ouch. That hurt. [VERB]

    哎哟，好痛。

5 VERB 伤害（感情）;使伤心 If someone hurts you, they say or do something that makes you unhappy. 伤害（感情）;使伤心

  • He is afraid of hurting Bessy's feelings. [VERB noun]

    他怕伤害了贝茜的感情。

  • She's afraid she's going to be hurt and that she'll never fall in love again. [VERB noun]

    她害怕自己会受到伤害，从而永远不会再恋爱了。

  • What hurts most is the betrayal, the waste. [VERB]

    最痛彻心扉的是背叛和感情的浪费。

6 ADJ-GRADED （感情上）受到伤害的，伤心的 If you are hurt, you are upset because of something that someone has said or done. （感情上）受到伤害的，伤心的

  • Yes, I was hurt, jealous.

    是的，我感到伤心、嫉妒。

  • He gave me a slightly hurt look.

    他有点受伤地看了我一眼。

SYN upset, pained, injured, wounded  
7 VERB [only cont] 伤心;感到痛苦 If you say that you are hurting, you mean that you are experiencing emotional pain. 伤心;感到痛苦

  • I am lonely and I am hurting. [VERB]

    我很孤单，很难过。

8 VERB 危害;损害;妨碍;对…产生不良影响 To hurt someone or something means to have a bad effect on them or prevent them from succeeding. 危害;损害;妨碍;对…产生不良影响

  • The combination of hot weather and decreased water supplies is hurting many industries. [VERB noun]

    炎热的天气和供水的减少正危及很多行业。

  • They may fear hurting their husbands' careers. [VERB noun]

    她们可能担心对丈夫的事业造成损害。

9 N-VAR 伤心;痛苦;委屈 A feeling of hurt is a feeling that you have when you think that you have been treated badly or judged unfairly. 伤心;痛苦;委屈

  • ...feelings of hurt and anger, fear and despair.

    伤心、愤怒、恐惧、绝望的复杂情感

  • I was full of jealousy and hurt.

    我满怀嫉妒，伤心不已。

  • There would be a hurt in her heart for a while, but in the end she would get over it.

    她可能会伤心难过一阵子，但最终她会好起来。

SYN distress, suffering, pain, grief  
10 PHRASE （做…）不会有坏处 If you say 'It won't hurt to do something' or 'It never hurts to do something', you are recommending an action which you think is helpful or useful. （做…）不会有坏处 [informal]

  • It never hurts to ask.

    多问无妨。

  • It wouldn't hurt you to be a bit more serious.

    认真一点对你没有坏处。
