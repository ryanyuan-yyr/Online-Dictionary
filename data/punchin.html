punch in
PHRASAL VERB 将（信息）输入计算机 If you punch in a number on a machine or punch numbers into it, you push the machine's buttons or keys in order to give it a command to do something. 将（信息）输入计算机

  • You can bank by phone in the USA, punching in account numbers on the phone.

    在美国你可以通过电话输入账号办理银行业务。

  • Punch your credit card number into the keypad. [VERB noun PREP.]

    在小键盘上输入你的信用卡号。
