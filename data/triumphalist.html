triumphalist
traɪʌmfəlɪst
ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] (政客或组织的行为)炫耀胜利(或成功)的，洋洋得意的，耀武扬威的 Triumphalist behaviour is behaviour in which politicians or organizations celebrate a victory or a great success, especially when this is intended to upset the people they have defeated. (政客或组织的行为)炫耀胜利(或成功)的，洋洋得意的，耀武扬威的 [mainly British, journalism]

  • ...a triumphalist celebration of their supremacy.

    对他们霸主地位的耀武扬威的庆祝
