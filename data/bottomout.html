bottom out
PHRASAL VERB （价格等）达到最低点，停止恶化 If a trend such as a fall in prices bottoms out, it stops getting worse or decreasing, and remains at a particular level or amount. （价格等）达到最低点，停止恶化 [journalism]

  • He expects the recession to bottom out. [VERB PREP.]

    他预期经济萧条将见底。

  • House prices have bottomed out. [VERB PREP.]

    房产价格已经止跌企稳。
