hinge
hɪndʒ hɪndʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense hinges, present participle hinging, past tense , past participle hinged
N-COUNT （门等的）铰链，合页 A hinge is a piece of metal, wood, or plastic that is used to join a door to its frame or to join two things together so that one of them can swing freely. （门等的）铰链，合页

  • The top swung open on well-oiled hinges.

    用润滑良好的铰链固定的盖子一旋转就打开了。

Phrasal verbs:
hinge on
