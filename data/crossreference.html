cross-reference
ˈkrɔsˈrɛfərəns
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense cross-references, present participle cross-referencing, past tense , past participle cross-referenced
1 N-COUNT (书中的)参见项，互见条，相互参照 A cross-reference is a note in a book which tells you that there is relevant or more detailed information in another part of the book. (书中的)参见项，互见条，相互参照
2 VERB [usually passive] (书等)加注使相互参照 If something such as a book is cross-referenced, cross-references are put in it. (书等)加注使相互参照

  • Nearly 2,300 plant lists have been checked and cross-referenced. [be VERB-ed]

    已经对约2,300份植物列表进行核对，并注明了相互参照项。

  • ...an index of products and services which is cross-referenced to the supplying companies. [VERB-ed]

    注明供货商参照项的产品和服务索引
