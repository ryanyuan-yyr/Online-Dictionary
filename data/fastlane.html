fast lane
Word forms: plural fast lanes
1 N-COUNT (高速公路上的)快车道 On a motorway, the fast lane is the part of the road where the vehicles that are travelling fastest go. (高速公路上的)快车道 [mainly British]
2 N-SING 快节奏生活;忙碌刺激的生活 If someone is living in the fast lane, they have a very busy, exciting life, although they sometimes seem to take a lot of risks. 快节奏生活;忙碌刺激的生活

  • ..a tale of life in the fast lane.

    快节奏生活的故事

  • ...the fast lane of world cricket.

    世界板球的快节奏
