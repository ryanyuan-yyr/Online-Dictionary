south-westerly
(also southwesterly)
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 在西南方的;西南的;向西南部的 A south-westerly point, area, or direction is to the south-west or towards the south-west. 在西南方的;西南的;向西南部的

  • ...the most south-westerly tip of Scotland.

    苏格兰最西南部

2 ADJ (风)从西南方吹来的 A south-westerly wind is a wind that blows from the south-west. (风)从西南方吹来的
