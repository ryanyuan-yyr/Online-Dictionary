product placement
Word forms: plural product placements
N-VAR 植入式广告 Product placement is a form of advertising in which a company has its product placed where it can be clearly seen during a film or television programme. 植入式广告 [business]

  • It was the first movie to feature onscreen product placement for its own merchandise.

    这是第一部在银幕上为自己的产品做植入式广告的电影。
