carrel
kærəl ˈkærəl ˈkærəl
Word forms: carrels
N-COUNT 小阅读室A carrel is a desk with low walls on three sides, at which a student can work in private, especially in a library. 小阅读室

  • Merced was pondering this problem one day in his student carrel at the Bradbury library.

    麦塞德整天都在布拉德布瑞图书馆的学生私人单间里思考这个问题。
