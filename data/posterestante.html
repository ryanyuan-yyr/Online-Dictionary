poste restante

N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] （邮局的）邮件待领业务 Poste restante is a service operated by post offices by which letters and packages that are sent to you are kept at a particular post office until you collect them. （邮局的）邮件待领业务 [mainly British]
