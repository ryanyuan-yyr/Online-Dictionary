interstate
ɪntəʳsteɪt ˈɪntəˌsteɪt ˈɪntərˌsteɪt
Word forms: plural interstates
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (尤指美国)州际的，州与州之间的 Interstate means between states, especially the states of the United States. (尤指美国)州际的，州与州之间的

  • ...interstate highways.

    州际公路

  • ...federal barriers to interstate banking.

    联邦政府对州际银行业设置的障碍

2 N-COUNT (美国)州际公路 In the United States, an interstate is a major road linking states. (美国)州际公路

  • We were driving along Interstate 280, toward my home in Woodside.

    我们正驾车沿280号州际公路往我在伍德赛德的家行驶。

  • ...the southbound lane of Interstate 75.

    75 号州际公路的南向车道
