-pounder -paʊndəʳ ˈpaʊndər
Word forms: plural -pounders
1 COMB in N-COUNT （与数字连用构成名词）表示“重…磅的动物（或鱼）” -pounder can be added to numbers to form nouns that refer to animals or fish that weigh a particular number of pounds. （与数字连用构成名词）表示“重…磅的动物（或鱼）”

  • My fish average 2 lb 8 oz and I've had two eight-pounders.

    我的鱼平均每条重2磅8盎司，还有过两条重8磅的。

2 COMB in N-COUNT （与数字连用构成名词）表示“发射…磅重炮弹的大炮” -pounder can be added to numbers to form nouns that refer to guns that fire shells weighing a particular number of pounds. （与数字连用构成名词）表示“发射…磅重炮弹的大炮”

  • The guns were twelve-pounders.

    这些大炮发射12磅重的炮弹。
