medic
medɪk ˈmɛdɪk
Word forms: plural medics
1 N-COUNT 医生;医科学生 A medic is a doctor or medical student. 医生;医科学生 [informal]
2 N-COUNT (随军)卫生员 A medic is a doctor who works with the armed forces, as part of a medical corps. (随军)卫生员 [US]

  • A Navy medic was wounded by sniper fire.

    一名海军卫生员被狙击手射伤了。

SYN doctor, physician, medical practitioner
