snappy
snæpi ˈsnæpi
Word forms: comparative snappier, superlative snappiest
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (说话)生气勃勃的，活泼的，明快的 If someone has a snappy style of speaking, they speak in a quick, clever, brief, and often funny way. (说话)生气勃勃的，活泼的，明快的

  • Each film gets a snappy two-line summary.

    每部影片都配有简洁明快的双行内容提要。

  • ...snappy American film dialogue.

    简洁明快的美国电影对白

SYN succinct, brief, concise, to the point  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] (穿着)入时的,时髦的 If someone is a snappy dresser or if they wear snappy clothes, they wear smart, stylish clothes. (穿着)入时的,时髦的

  • She has already made a name for herself as a snappy dresser.

    她已是一个小有名气的时尚达人。

  • ...snappy sports jackets.

    时髦的运动夹克

SYN smart, fashionable, stylish, trendy [British , informal]  
snappily

  • ...his usual band of snappily dressed friends.

    他平常那群衣着入时的朋友

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (说话态度)没好气的，恶声恶气的 If someone is snappy, they speak to people in a sharp, unfriendly manner. (说话态度)没好气的，恶声恶气的

  • He wasn't irritable or snappy or anything, just slightly perplexed.

    他并不是爱发火或爱起急什么的，他只是有点困惑。

SYN irritable, cross, bad-tempered, pissed [US , slang]  
4 PHRASE (催促别人做事)快一点 If you tell someone to make it snappy, you tell them to do something quickly. (催促别人做事)快一点 [informal]

  • Look at the pamphlets, and make it snappy.

    看看手册，动作要快。

SYN hurry (up), be quick, get a move on [informal] , buck up [informal]
