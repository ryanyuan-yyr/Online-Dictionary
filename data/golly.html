golly
gɒli ˈgɑli
1 EXCLAM （表示惊奇）天哪，啊 Some people say golly to indicate that they are very surprised by something. （表示惊奇）天哪，啊 [informal, old-fashioned, feelings]

  • 'Golly,' he says, 'Isn't it exciting!'

    “天哪！”他说，“这真让人激动！”

2 EXCLAM （强调某事确实发生或应该发生）确实，的确 Some people say by golly to emphasize that something did happen or should happen. （强调某事确实发生或应该发生）确实，的确 [informal, old-fashioned, emphasis]

  • By golly we can do something about it this time.

    这次我们确实可以采取行动了。
