citizen
sɪtɪzən ˈsɪtɪzən ˈsɪtəzən ˈsɪtəsən
Word forms: plural citizens
1 N-COUNT 公民;国民 Someone who is a citizen of a particular country is legally accepted as belonging to that country. 公民;国民

  • ...American citizens.

    美国公民

  • The life of ordinary citizens began to change.

    老百姓的生活开始发生变化。

2 N-COUNT 城镇居民;市民 The citizens of a town or city are the people who live there. 城镇居民;市民

  • ...the citizens of Buenos Aires. [+ of]

    布宜诺斯艾利斯的市民

3 See also: senior citizen
