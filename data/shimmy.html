shimmy
ʃɪmi ˈʃɪmi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense shimmies, present participle shimmying, past tense , past participle shimmied
VERB (抖动着肩膀和臀部)跳希米舞;一扭一摆地走 If you shimmy, you dance or move in a way that involves shaking your hips and shoulders from side to side. (抖动着肩膀和臀部)跳希米舞;一扭一摆地走

  • Dancers shimmied in the streets of New Orleans. [VERB]

    舞者们在新奥尔良的大街上跳着希米舞。

  • Tina Turner shimmied across the stage in an incredibly sexy dress. [VERB prep./adv.]

    蒂娜·特纳穿着极其性感的裙子一边跳着希米舞一边穿过舞台。
