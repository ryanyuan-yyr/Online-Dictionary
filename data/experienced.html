experienced
ɪkspɪəriənst ɪkˈspɪərɪənst ɪkˈspɪriənst
ADJ-GRADED 有经验的;老练的 If you describe someone as experienced, you mean that they have been doing a particular job or activity for a long time, and therefore know a lot about it or are very skilful at it. 有经验的;老练的

  • ...lawyers who are experienced in these matters. [+ in]

    在这些问题上颇有经验的律师们

  • It's a team packed with experienced and mature professionals.

    那个小组里都是些有经验的成熟专业人士。

  • Perhaps I'm a bit more experienced about life than my contemporaries.

    可能我比同龄人更有生活经验。

SYN worldly-wise, knowing, worldly, wise
