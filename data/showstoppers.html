showstopper
ʃoʊstɒpəʳ ˈʃəʊstɒpə ˈʃoʊˌstɑpər
(also show-stopper)Word forms: plural showstoppers
N-COUNT 令人赞叹的东西;令人叹服的事物 If something is a showstopper, it is very impressive. 令人赞叹的东西;令人叹服的事物 [informal, approval]

  • Her natural creativity and artistic talent make her home a real showstopper.

    她天生的创造力和艺术天赋让她的家不同凡响，令人叹服。
