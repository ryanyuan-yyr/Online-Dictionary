credentials
krɪdenʃəlz krɪˈdɛnʃəlz
1 N-PLURAL 资格;资历 Someone's credentials are their previous achievements, training, and general background, which indicate that they are qualified to do something. 资格;资历

  • ...her credentials as a Bach specialist. [+ as]

    她作为巴赫研究专家的资历

  • I can testify to the credentials of the clientele. [+ of]

    我可以证明委托人的资质。

2 N-PLURAL [usually poss NOUN] 资格证书;证明书;证件 Someone's credentials are a letter or certificate that proves their identity or qualifications. 资格证书;证明书;证件

  • Britain's new ambassador to Lebanon has presented his credentials to the President.

    英国驻黎巴嫩的新任大使已将国书递交给总统。
