uncompromising
ʌnkɒmprəmaɪzɪŋ ʌnˈkɑmprəˌmaɪzɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED 不妥协的；不让步的；坚定的 If you describe someone as uncompromising, you mean that they are determined not to change their opinions or aims in any way. 不妥协的；不让步的；坚定的

  • Mrs Thatcher was a tough and uncompromising politician.

    撒切尔夫人是一位态度强硬、不轻易妥协的政治家。

SYN inflexible, strict, rigid, decided  
uncompromisingly

  • The company had once been uncompromisingly socialist.

    该公司曾一度坚定地走社会主义道路。

  • He states uncompromisingly that he is opposed to any practices which oppress animals.

    他坚决声明反对任何虐待动物的行为。

2 ADJ-GRADED 不折不扣的；十足的 If you describe something as uncompromising, you mean that it does not attempt to make something that is shocking or unpleasant any more acceptable to people. 不折不扣的；十足的

  • ...a film of uncompromising brutality.

    极度血腥的影片

SYN unrelenting, ruthless, relentless, tough  
uncompromisingly

  • ...the uncompromisingly modern decor.

    现代气息浓厚的装饰
