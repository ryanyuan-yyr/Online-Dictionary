unknowingly
ʌnnoʊɪŋli
ADV [ADVERB with verb] 未意识到地；不知不觉地；无意中 If someone does something unknowingly, they do it without being aware of it. 未意识到地；不知不觉地；无意中

  • ...if people unknowingly move into more contaminated areas of the river.

    如果人们在不知情的情况下搬到了河流污染更严重的地区

  • ...the extent to which the workforce colludes knowingly or unknowingly with such criminal activity.

    在何种程度上职工有意或无意地参与到这样的犯罪活动中

  • Art often imitates nature unknowingly.

    艺术经常会在不经意中模仿自然。
