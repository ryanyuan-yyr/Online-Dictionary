date from
PHRASAL VERB 追溯到;开始于;始造（或建）于 If something dates from a particular time, it started or was made at that time. 追溯到;开始于;始造（或建）于

  • All the cupboards and appliances dated from the 1950s. [VERB PREP. noun]

    所有这些橱柜和家用电器都是 20世纪 50 年代的物品。

  • The present controversy dates from 1986. [VERB PREP. noun]

    现在的这一争议始于 1986 年。
