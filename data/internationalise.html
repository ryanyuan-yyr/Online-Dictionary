internationalize
ɪntəʳnæʃənəlaɪz ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense internationalizes, present participle internationalizing, past tense , past participle internationalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 internationalise
VERB 使(问题、危机)国际化 If an issue or a crisis is internationalized, it becomes the concern of many nations throughout the world. 使(问题、危机)国际化 [journalism]

  • A very real danger exists of the conflict becoming internationalised. [be VERB-ed]

    这一冲突实际上很有可能演变成国际化问题。

  • They have been trying to internationalise the Kashmir problem. [VERB noun]

    他们一直想使克什米尔问题国际化。
