fait accompli
feɪt əkɒmpli fɛt akɔ̃pli - ækɔːmpliː fɛtakɔ̃̃ˈpli ˌfeɪtəˌkɑmˈpli
Word forms: plural faits accomplis
N-COUNT [usually singular] 既成事实;木已成舟之事 If something is a fait accompli, it has already been decided or done and cannot be changed. 既成事实;木已成舟之事 [formal]

  • They became increasingly annoyed that they were being presented with a fait accompli.

    他们得知时已是木已成舟，无法变更了，他们为此越来越恼火。
