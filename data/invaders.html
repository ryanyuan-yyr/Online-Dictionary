invader
ɪnveɪdəʳ
Word forms: plural invaders
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 入侵者;侵略者 Invaders are soldiers who are invading a country. 入侵者;侵略者

  • The invaders were only finally crushed when troops overcame them at Glenshiel in June 1719.

    1719 年 6 月，部队在格兰舍一役击败了入侵者，直至此时他们才最终覆灭。

SYN attacker, raider, plunderer, aggressor  
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 侵略国;侵略军 You can refer to a country or army that has invaded or is about to invade another country as an invader . 侵略国;侵略军

  • ...action against a foreign invader.

    抵抗外国侵略军的战斗
