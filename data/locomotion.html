locomotion
loʊkəmoʊʃən ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən
N-UNCOUNT 运动（力）；移动（力） Locomotion is the ability to move and the act of moving from one place to another. 运动（力）；移动（力） [formal]

  • Flight is the form of locomotion that puts the greatest demands on muscles.

    飞行是对肌肉要求最高的运动形式。

  • He specialises in the mechanics of locomotion.

    他专攻运动力学。

SYN movement, travel, travelling, moving
