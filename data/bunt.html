bunt
bʌnt bʌnt bʌnt
Word forms: bunts, bunting, bunted
1 V-ERG (棒球)短打In baseball, if you bunt or if you bunt the ball, you deliberately hit the ball softly, in order to gain an advantage. (棒球)短打 [US]

  • Davis estimated he had been called on to bunt once in six years.

    戴维斯估计六年来就要求他进行了一次短打。

  • Rich Becker bunted a ball on the third-base side of the mound.

    里奇.贝克尔把球打到了三垒旁的投球区土墩。

2 N-COUNT (棒球)一记短打In baseball, a bunt is the act of bunting a ball or a hit made by bunting the ball. (棒球)一记短打 [US]

  • Then came a bunt from Russ Davis.

    接着鲁斯.戴维斯打出了一记短打。
