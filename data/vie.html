vie
vaɪ vaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense vies, present participle vying, past tense , past participle vied
V-RECIP 竞争;争夺 If one person or thing is vying with another for something, the people or things are competing for it. 竞争;争夺 [formal]

  • California is vying with other states to capture a piece of the growing communications market. [VERB + with]

    为了在日渐扩大的通讯市场分得一杯羹，加利福尼亚正在和其他州展开竞争。

  • Four rescue plans are vying to save the zoo. [VERB to-infinitive]

    4个拯救动物园的方案正争得不可开交。

  • In hospitals, business plans vie with patients for doctors' attention. [VERB + for]

    在医院，经营方针和病人都是医生所要关心的。

  • The two are vying for the support of New York voters. [VERB + for]

    两人在争夺纽约选民的支持。

SYN compete, struggle, contend, contest
