go along with
1 PHRASAL VERB 服从；遵守 If you go along with a rule, decision, or policy, you accept it and obey it. 服从；遵守

  • Whatever the majority decided I was prepared to go along with. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    只要是多数人的决定我都愿意服从。

SYN agree, follow, cooperate, concur  
2 PHRASAL VERB 赞同；支持 If you go along with a person or an idea, you agree with them. 赞同；支持

  • 'I don't think a government has properly done it for about the past twenty-five years.' — 'I'd go along with that.' [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    “我认为在过去25年左右的时间里，政府一直未能妥善处理此事。”—“我同意你的观点。”
