ogre
oʊgəʳ ˈoʊgər
Word forms: plural ogres
1 N-COUNT （幽默说法）凶残的人，可怕的人 If you refer to someone as an ogre, you are saying in a humorous way that they are very frightening. （幽默说法）凶残的人，可怕的人

  • Bank managers—like tax inspectors—do not really like being thought of as ogres.

    银行经理像税务稽查员一样，不喜欢被人们认为是妖魔化身。

2 N-COUNT （传说、童话中的）食人恶魔 In legends and fairy stories, an ogre is a large, cruel, frightening man who often eats people. （传说、童话中的）食人恶魔
