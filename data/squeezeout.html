squeeze out
PHRASAL VERB [usually passive] 把…排挤出去;将…排除在外 If a person or thing is squeezed out, they are no longer included in something that they were previously involved in. 把…排挤出去;将…排除在外

  • Other directors appear happy that Lord Hollick has been squeezed out. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    其他董事都因为霍利克勋爵被挤了出去而显得很高兴。

  • Latin and Greek will be squeezed out of school timetables. [be V-ed P + of]

    拉丁语和希腊语将退出学校的课程表。

SYN leave out, exclude, eliminate, omit
