groom
gruːm grum grʊm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense grooms, present participle grooming, past tense , past participle groomed
1 N-COUNT 同bridegroom A groom is the same as a bridegroom . 同bridegroom

  • ...the bride and groom.

    新娘与新郎

SYN newly-wed, husband, bridegroom, marriage partner  
2 N-COUNT 马夫；马倌 A groom is someone whose job is to look after the horses in a stable and to keep them clean. 马夫；马倌
3 VERB 洗刷（动物）；给（动物）梳毛 If you groom an animal, you clean its fur, usually by brushing it. 洗刷（动物）；给（动物）梳毛

  • The horses were exercised and groomed with special care. [VERB noun]

    这些马接受专门训练，鬃毛也得到精心梳理。

SYN brush, clean, tend, rub down  
4 VERB [usually passive] 训练；培养 If you are groomed for a special job, someone prepares you for it by teaching you the skills you will need. 训练；培养

  • George was already being groomed for the top job. [be VERB-ed + for]

    为承担这份高级工作，乔治已在接受专门的培训。

  • Marshall was groomed to run the family companies. [be VERB-ed to-infinitive]

    马歇尔在接手家族企业之前接受了培养。

SYN train, prime, prepare, coach
