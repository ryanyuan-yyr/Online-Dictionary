squirt
skwɜːʳt skwɜrt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense squirts, present participle squirting, past tense , past participle squirted
1 V-ERG (使)(液体)喷射 If you squirt a liquid somewhere or if it squirts somewhere, the liquid comes out of a narrow opening in a thin fast stream. (使)(液体)喷射

  • Norman cut open his pie and squirted tomato sauce into it. [VERB noun prep./adv.]

    诺尔曼切开自己的派，往里面挤了点番茄酱。

  • The water squirted from its throat. [VERB prep./adv.]

    水从它的喉部喷出来。

  • The liquid is squirted out in powerful jets. [be VERB-ed adv./prep.]

    液体一股股猛地喷射出来。

SYN spurt, shoot, gush, burst  
Squirt is also a noun.

  • It just needs a little squirt of oil.

    它只需要喷一点油。

2 VERB 朝…喷… If you squirt something with a liquid, you squirt the liquid at it. 朝…喷…

  • I squirted him with water. [VERB noun + with]

    我朝他喷水。

  • Its linings were simply squirted with oil. [VERB noun with noun]

    它的内衬只是简单地喷了些油。

SYN spray, wet, sprinkle, shower
