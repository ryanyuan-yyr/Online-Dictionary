irreconcilable
ɪrekənsaɪləbəl ɪˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl
1 ADJ 相对立的;不兼容的 If two things such as opinions or proposals are irreconcilable, they are so different from each other that it is not possible to believe or have both of them. 相对立的;不兼容的 [formal]

  • ...incompatibility between two irreconcilable positions.

    两种对立立场的不可调和性

  • These old concepts are irreconcilable with modern life. [+ with]

    这些陈旧的观念与现代生活格格不入。

2 ADJ 不可调和的;无法消除的 An irreconcilable disagreement or conflict is so serious that it cannot be settled. 不可调和的;无法消除的 [formal]

  • ...an irreconcilable clash of personalities.

    不可调和的个性冲突

SYN implacable, uncompromising, inflexible, inexorable
