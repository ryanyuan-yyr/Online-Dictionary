foal
foʊl foʊl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense foals, present participle foaling, past tense , past participle foaled
1 N-COUNT 马驹 A foal is a very young horse. 马驹
2 VERB 下驹 When a female horse foals, it gives birth. 下驹

  • The mare is due to foal today. [VERB]

    母马今天要下小马驹了。
