physician's assistant
Word forms: physician's assistants
N-COUNT 医生助理 A physician's assistant is a person who is trained to do some of the same work that a doctor does but who is not a doctor. 医生助理 [US]

  • In a few Minnesota communities, physician's assistants are being used in place of doctors.

    在明尼苏达州的一些社区，医生助理正被用来代替医生。
