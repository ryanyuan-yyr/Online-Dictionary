banana skin
Word forms: plural banana skins
1 N-COUNT 香蕉皮 The thick yellow or green covering of a banana is called a banana skin . 香蕉皮 [British]
in AM, use 美国英语用 banana peel
2 N-COUNT 引起麻烦（或使人出丑）的事物 If an important or famous person slips on a banana skin, they say or do something that makes them look stupid and causes them problems. 引起麻烦（或使人出丑）的事物 [mainly British, journalism]

  • ...waiting for the government to slip on this week's banana skin.

    等着政府这周的洋相

in AM, use 美国英语用 banana peel
