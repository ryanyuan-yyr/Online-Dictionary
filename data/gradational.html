gradation
grədeɪʃən greɪd- greɪˈdeɪʃən grədeɪʃən
Word forms: plural gradations
N-COUNT [usually plural] 逐渐的变化；渐变 Gradations are small differences or changes in things. 逐渐的变化；渐变 [formal]

  • But TV images require subtle gradations of light and shade.

    但是，电视影像要求有十分细微的光影变化。

SYN degree, level, grade, sequence
