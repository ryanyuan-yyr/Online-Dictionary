triumphal
traɪʌmfəl traɪˈʌmfəl
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 庆祝胜利(或成功)的;以示凯旋的 Triumphal is used to describe things that are done or made to celebrate a victory or great success. 庆祝胜利(或成功)的;以示凯旋的

  • He made a triumphal entry into the city.

    他以胜利者之姿进入了那座城市。

  • The triumphal arch commemorates Caesar's victory over Pompey.

    凯旋门用于纪念恺撒击败庞培，得胜而归。
