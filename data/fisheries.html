fishery
fɪʃəri ˈfɪʃəri
Word forms: plural fisheries
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 渔场 Fisheries are areas of the sea where fish are caught in large quantities for commercial purposes. 渔场

  • ...the fisheries off Newfoundland.

    纽芬兰附近的渔场

2 N-COUNT 养鱼场 A fishery is a place where fish are bred and reared. 养鱼场
