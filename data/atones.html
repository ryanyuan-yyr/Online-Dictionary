atone
ətoʊn əˈtoʊn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense atones, present participle atoning, past tense , past participle atoned
VERB 弥补;赔罪;补偿 If you atone for something that you have done, you do something to show that you are sorry you did it. 弥补;赔罪;补偿 [formal]

  • He felt he had atoned for what he had done to his son. [VERB + for]

    他觉得他对儿子已无亏欠。

  • He atoned by apologizing. [V by -ing]

    他道歉赔罪。

  • I'll never live long enough to atone. [VERB]

    我一生都无法弥补。

SYN make amends, pay, do penance, make reparation
