working party
Word forms: plural working parties
N-COUNT-COLL [with singular or plural verb] 专题调查委员会;特别工作组 A working party is a committee which is formed to investigate a particular situation or problem and to produce a report containing its opinions and suggestions. 专题调查委员会;特别工作组 [mainly British]

  • They set up a working party to look into the issue.

    他们设立了一个特别工作组来调查这个问题。

  • ...a finance working party.

    财政调查委员会
