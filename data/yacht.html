yacht
jɒt jɑt
Word forms: plural yachts
N-COUNT 帆船;游艇;快艇 A yacht is a large boat with sails or a motor, used for racing or pleasure trips. 帆船;游艇;快艇

  • His 36ft yacht sank suddenly last summer.

    他那36英尺长的游艇去年夏天突然沉没了。

  • ...a round-the-world yacht race.

    环球帆船比赛

SYN boat, ship, cutter, sloop
