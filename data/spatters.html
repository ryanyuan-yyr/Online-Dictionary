spatter
spætəʳ ˈspætər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense spatters, present participle spattering, past tense , past participle spattered
V-ERG 溅;洒 If a liquid spatters a surface or you spatter a liquid over a surface, drops of the liquid fall on an area of the surface. 溅;洒

  • He stared at the rain spattering on the glass. [VERB prep.]

    他凝视着雨点溅落在玻璃上。

  • Gently turn the fish, being careful not to spatter any hot butter on yourself. [VERB noun prep.]

    轻轻地把鱼翻过来，小心别把滚烫的黄油溅到自己身上。

  • Blood spattered the dark concrete. [VERB noun]

    鲜血溅落在黑色的水泥地上。

  • I always spatter my blouse with gravy when I eat. [VERB noun with noun]

    吃饭的时候，我总是把肉汁溅到衬衣上。

  • Her dress was spattered with mud. [VERB-ed]

    她的裙子溅上了泥浆。

SYN splash, spray, sprinkle, soil
