carob
kærəb ˈkærəb
Word forms: plural carobs
1 N-COUNT 角豆树（果实味似巧克力） A carob or carob tree is a Mediterranean tree that stays green all year round. It has dark brown fruit that tastes similar to chocolate. 角豆树（果实味似巧克力）
2 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 角豆(常制成粉以替代巧克力) The dark brown fruit of the carob tree can be referred to as carob. It is often made into powder and used instead of chocolate. 角豆(常制成粉以替代巧克力)

  • If you do yearn for chocolate, try a carob bar instead.

    你要是非常想吃巧克力,那就嚼根角豆条解馋吧。
