cursed
kɜːʳst kɜːʳsɪd ˈkɜːsɪd kɜːst ˈkɜrsɪd kɜrst
The pronunciation /'kɜːsɪd/ is used for meaning 3. 义项3读作 /'kɜːsɪd/
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ with n] 因…而遭殃的;受…折磨的 If you are cursed with something, you are very unlucky in having it. 因…而遭殃的;受…折磨的

  • Bulman was cursed with a poor memory for names. [+ with]

    布尔曼总是记不住人名。

2 ADJ [usually verb-link ADJ] 遭受诅咒的 Someone or something that is cursed is suffering as the result of a curse. 遭受诅咒的

  • The whole family seemed cursed.

    全家人仿佛都受到了诅咒。

  • The ground was cursed because of him.

    大地因为他而遭受诅咒。

SYN under a curse, damned, doomed, unholy  
3 ADJ [ADJ n] (尤用于生气时加强语气)该死的，可恶的 Cursed is used by some people to emphasize what they are saying, especially when they are angry. (尤用于生气时加强语气)该死的，可恶的 [old-fashioned, rude, emphasis]

  • I wish this cursed place was burned.

    我希望这该死的地方被烧毁。
