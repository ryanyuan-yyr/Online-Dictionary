onrushing
ɒnrʌʃɪŋ ˈɒnˌrʌʃɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 猛冲的；汹涌的 Onrushing describes something such as a vehicle that is moving forward so quickly or forcefully that it would be very difficult to stop. 猛冲的；汹涌的

  • He was killed by an onrushing locomotive.

    他被飞驰而来的火车头撞死了。

  • ...the roar of the onrushing water.

    河水汹涌奔流的咆哮声
