hole up
PHRASAL VERB （为免受打扰等而）躲藏,隔绝 If you hole up somewhere, you hide or shut yourself there, usually so that people cannot find you or disturb you. （为免受打扰等而）躲藏,隔绝 [informal]

  • His creative process involves holing up in his Paris flat with the phone off the hook. [VERB PREP.]

    他在创作的过程中躲在自己巴黎的公寓里，连电话都挂起。

SYN hide, shelter, take refuge, go into hiding
