ridiculous
rɪdɪkjʊləs rɪˈdɪkjələs
ADJ-GRADED 愚蠢的;荒谬的;可笑的 If you say that something or someone is ridiculous, you mean that they are very foolish. 愚蠢的;荒谬的;可笑的

  • It is ridiculous to suggest we are having a romance.

    暗示我们正在谈恋爱的说法真是太荒谬了。

  • It was an absolutely ridiculous decision.

    这是一个绝对荒谬的决定。

SYN laughable, stupid, incredible, silly  
Quotations
It is only one step from the sublime to the ridiculousNapoleon Bonaparte
The sublime and the ridiculous are often so nearly related, that it is difficult to class them separately. One step above the sublime, makes the ridiculous; and one step above the ridiculous, makes the sublime againThomas Paine The Age of Reason
