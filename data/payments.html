payment
peɪmənt ˈpeɪmənt
Word forms: plural payments
1 N-COUNT [oft n NOUN] 付款额；支付的款项；支付；付款 A payment is an amount of money that is paid to someone, or the act of paying this money. 付款额；支付的款项；支付；付款 [Also + to/on]

  • Thousands of its customers are in arrears with loans and mortgage payments.

    有数千客户拖欠其贷款和抵押贷款还款。

  • The fund will make payments of just over £1 billion next year. [+ of]

    该基金明年的支付额刚好过10亿英镑。

2 N-UNCOUNT 支付；付款 Payment is the act of paying money to someone or of being paid. 支付；付款 [Also + for]

  • He had sought to obtain payment of a sum which he had claimed was owed to him. [+ of]

    他声称别人欠他一笔钱，他在想方设法讨要回来。

SYN settlement, paying, discharge, outlay
