unshakable
ʌnʃeɪkəbəl ʌnˈʃeɪkəbəl
(also unshakeable)
ADJ [usu ADJ n] (信念)不可动摇的If you describe someone's beliefs as unshakable, you are emphasizing that they are so strong that they cannot be destroyed or altered. (信念)不可动摇的 [emphasis]

  • She had an unshakable faith in human goodness and natural honesty.

    她对人性的善良和天性的诚实有着不可动摇的信心。
