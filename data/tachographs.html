tachograph
tækəgrɑːf -græf
Word forms: plural tachographs
N-COUNT (卡车、长途汽车等的)速度里程仪 A tachograph is a device that is put in vehicles such as lorries and coaches in order to record information such as how fast the vehicle goes, how far it travels, and the number of breaks the driver takes. (卡车、长途汽车等的)速度里程仪 [British]
