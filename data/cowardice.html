cowardice
kaʊəʳdɪs ˈkaʊərˌdɪs
N-UNCOUNT 惧怕;胆小;懦弱 Cowardice is cowardly behaviour. 惧怕;胆小;懦弱

  • He openly accused his opponents of cowardice.

    他公然指责对手胆小懦弱。

SYN faint-heartedness, weakness, softness, fearfulness
