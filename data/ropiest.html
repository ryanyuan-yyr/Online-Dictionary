ropey
roʊpi
Word forms: comparative ropier, superlative ropiest
ADJ-GRADED 差劲的;劣质的;差强人意的 If you say that something is ropey, you mean that its quality is poor or unsatisfactory. 差劲的;劣质的;差强人意的 [British, informal]

  • Your spelling's a bit ropey.

    你的拼写有点难看。

  • Their health-care system suffers from queues, shortages and ropey equipment.

    他们的医疗体系有很多不足：要排队等候，资源短缺，设备陈旧。

SYN inferior, poor, inadequate, mediocre
