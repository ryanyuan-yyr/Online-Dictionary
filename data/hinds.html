hind
haɪnd haɪnd
Word forms: plural hinds
1 ADJ [ADJ n] （动物的腿）后面的，后部的 An animal's hind legs are at the back of its body. （动物的腿）后面的，后部的

  • Suddenly the cow kicked up its hind legs.

    奶牛突然尥起了蹶子。

SYN back, after, rear, hinder  
2 N-COUNT 雌鹿；（尤指）雌赤鹿 A hind is a female deer, especially one of the red deer family. 雌鹿；（尤指）雌赤鹿
