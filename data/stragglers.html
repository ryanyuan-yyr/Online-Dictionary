straggler
strægələʳ
Word forms: plural stragglers
1 N-COUNT [usually plural] 掉队者;落伍者;拖后腿的人 The stragglers are the people in a group who are moving more slowly or making less progress than the others. 掉队者;落伍者;拖后腿的人

  • There were two stragglers twenty yards back.

    两名掉队者落后了 20 码。

  • Any straggler that fell behind or got lost in the darkness was easy prey for the enemy.

    任何在黑暗中落伍或走失的掉队者都很容易成为敌人的攻击目标。

2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (某项活动中)留到最后的人 The stragglers at an event such as a party are the people who are still there after most of the other people have left. (某项活动中)留到最后的人

  • ...round about two o'clock in the morning when there were only a few stragglers left.

    大约在凌晨两点钟只剩下几个人时
