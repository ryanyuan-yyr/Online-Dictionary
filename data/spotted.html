spotted
spɒtɪd ˈspɑtɪd
1 ADJ 带圆点的；有斑点的 Something that is spotted has a pattern of spots on it. 带圆点的；有斑点的

  • ...hand-painted spotted cups and saucers in green and blue.

    带有手绘圆点的绿色和蓝色茶杯和茶托

  • His cheeks were spotted with blackheads. [+ with]

    他的双颊上长了一些黑头。

SYN speckled, dotted, flecked, pied
