infallible
ɪnfælɪbəl ɪnˈfæləbəl
ADJ 不可能错的;不会犯错误的;永无过失的 If a person or thing is infallible, they are never wrong. 不可能错的;不会犯错误的;永无过失的

  • Although he was experienced, he was not infallible.

    尽管他经验丰富,却并非不会出错。

  • She had an infallible eye for style.

    在时尚方面她的眼光错不了。

SYN perfect, impeccable, faultless, unerring  
infallibility

  • ...exaggerated views of the infallibility of science.

    关于科学永无过失的言过其实的观点
