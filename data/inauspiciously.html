inauspicious
ɪnɔːspɪʃəs ˌɪnɔːˈspɪʃəs ˌɪnɔˈspɪʃəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 不祥的;凶兆的;不吉利的 An inauspicious event is one that gives signs that success is unlikely. 不祥的;凶兆的;不吉利的 [formal]

  • The meeting got off to an inauspicious start when he was late.

    他迟到了,会议一开始就不顺利。

SYN unpromising, bad, unfortunate, discouraging
