set piece
(also set-piece)Word forms: plural set pieces
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] (战争中)严密策划的行动;(足球赛中)精心设定的攻防套路 A set piece is an occasion such as a battle or a move in a game of football that is planned and carried out in an ordered way. (战争中)严密策划的行动;(足球赛中)精心设定的攻防套路

  • Guerrillas avoid fighting set-piece battles.

    游击队避免打部署严密的战斗。

  • The first three Oldham goals came from set-pieces.

    奥尔德姆队的头三个进球都是按着既定套路踢进的。

2 N-COUNT (电影、小说或音乐作品中的)固定套路 A set piece is a part of a film, novel, or piece of music which has a strong dramatic effect and which is often not an essential part of the main story. (电影、小说或音乐作品中的)固定套路

  • ...the film's martial arts set pieces.

    电影中精彩的武打套路
