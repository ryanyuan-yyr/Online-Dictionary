boil away
PHRASAL VERB-ERG （使）煮干 When you boil away a liquid, or when it boils away, it is boiled until all of it changes into steam or vapour. （使）煮干

  • Remove the lid and boil away all the liquid. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    拿掉盖子并把所有液体都烧干。

  • Check every 20 minutes that the water has not boiled away. [VERB PREP.]

    每过20分钟检查一下以防水烧干。
