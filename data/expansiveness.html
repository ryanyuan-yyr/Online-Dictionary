expansive
ɪkspænsɪv ɪkˈspænsɪv ɛkˈspænsɪv
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 广袤的;辽阔的;全面的 If something is expansive, it covers or includes a large area or many things. 广袤的;辽阔的;全面的 [formal]

  • ...an expansive grassy play area.

    广阔的游乐草地

  • They have played an expansive style of rugby.

    他们采用向外展开打法打了一场橄榄球。

SYN wide, broad, extensive, spacious  
2 ADJ-GRADED 豪爽的;开朗的;健谈的 If you are expansive, you talk a lot, or are friendly or generous, because you are feeling happy and relaxed. 豪爽的;开朗的;健谈的

  • He was becoming more expansive as he relaxed.

    他放松的时候变得更加开朗。

  • The premier was in expansive mood.

    总理心情开朗。

SYN talkative, open, friendly, outgoing  
expansively

  • 'I'm here to make them feel good,' he says expansively.

    “我来就是让他们开心的，”他豪爽地说。

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 扩张的;发展的 If you describe something such as a period of time or an economy as expansive, you mean that it is associated with growth or expansion. 扩张的;发展的

  • An active and expansive market economy is a necessary condition for progress.

    积极扩张的市场经济是发展的必要条件。
