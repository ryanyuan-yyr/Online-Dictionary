go towards
PHRASAL VERB 用于支付…的部分款项；作为对…的部分付款 If an amount of money goes towards something, it is used to pay part of the cost of that thing. 用于支付…的部分款项；作为对…的部分付款

  • One per cent of total public spending should eventually go towards the arts. [VERB PREP. noun/verb-ing]

    公共事业总支出的1%最终应当花在艺术事业上。

  • Under the new approach more money will go towards improving the standard of training. [VERB PREP. noun/verb-ing]

    根据新方案，将投入更多的资金提高训练水平。
