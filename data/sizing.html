size
saɪz saɪz
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense sizes, present participle sizing, past tense , past participle sized
1 N-VAR 大小;数量;规模 The size of something is how big or small it is. Something's size is determined by comparing it to other things, counting it, or measuring it. 大小;数量;规模

  • Scientists have found the bones of a hoofed grazing animal about the size of a small horse. [+ of]

    科学家们已经发现了一种约有马驹般大小的有蹄草食性动物的骨骼。

  • In 1970 the average size of a French farm was 19 hectares. [+ of]

    1970 年，法国农场的平均规模为 19 公顷。

  • Iraq itself has oil reserves second in size only to Saudi Arabia's.

    伊拉克本身的石油储量在世界上居第二位，仅次于沙特阿拉伯。

  • ...shelves containing books of various sizes.

    摆放了各种开本书籍的书架

2 N-UNCOUNT 巨大;庞大 The size of something is the fact that it is very large. 巨大;庞大

  • He knows the size of the task. [+ of]

    他明白这个任务有多艰巨。

  • Jack walked around the hotel and was mesmerized by its sheer size.

    杰克在宾馆里四处转了转，为其宏伟气势所倾倒。

3 N-COUNT (尤指衣服、鞋子等的)尺寸，尺码，号 A size is one of a series of graded measurements, especially for things such as clothes or shoes. (尤指衣服、鞋子等的)尺寸，尺码，号

  • My sister is the same height but only a size 12.

    我姐姐是一样的身高，不过只穿 12 号的。

  • I tried them on and they were the right size.

    我试穿了一下，大小正合适。

4 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 使有自知之明;灭…的嚣张气焰 If someone is cut down to size, something happens to make them realize that they are not as important as they think they are. 使有自知之明;灭…的嚣张气焰 [informal]

  • The once-powerful post unions have been cut down to size.

    一度很强势的邮政联盟已经威风不再。

5 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 把…修改到合适的尺寸 If an object is cut to size, its size is altered to make it suitable for a particular purpose. 把…修改到合适的尺寸

  • Every piece of timber was planed, cut to size and stained with cedar preservative.

    每根木材都被刨平了，切割成一定尺寸，并涂上了雪松防腐剂。

6 PHRASE [PHRASE after verb] (试试)大小;(试试)是否合适 If you try something on for size, you try it to see if it is suitable for you. (试试)大小;(试试)是否合适

  • She was trying on an $8,000 jacket for size.

    她正在试穿一件价值 8,000 美元的短外套。

  • He suggests that Nietzsche was trying each style on for size.

    他的言下之意是尼采当时正在尝试每一种风格。

-size -saɪz saɪz
(also -sized)
1 COMB in ADJ (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“像…大小的”，“与…一样大的” You can use -size or -sized in combination with nouns to form adjectives which indicate that something is the same size as something else. (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“像…大小的”，“与…一样大的”

  • ...golfball-sized lumps of coarse black rock.

    跟高尔夫球一般大小的粗糙的黑色石块

2 COMB in ADJ (与形容词连用构成形容词)表示“…大小的”，“…规模的” You can use -size or -sized in combination with adjectives to form adjectives which describe the size of something. (与形容词连用构成形容词)表示“…大小的”，“…规模的”

  • ...full-size gymnasiums.

    大型健身房

  • ...a medium-sized college.

    一所中等规模的学院

3 COMB in ADJ (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“合适…的”，“合…规格的” You can use -size or -sized in combination with nouns to form adjectives which indicate that something is big enough or small enough to be suitable for a particular job or purpose. (与名词连用构成形容词)表示“合适…的”，“合…规格的”

  • ...a small passport-size photograph.

    大小适合护照用的小照片

  • ...a child-sized knife.

    适合儿童用的刀
