unadulterated
ʌnədʌltəreɪtɪd ˌʌnəˈdʌltəreɪtɪd
1 ADJ 未掺杂的;纯的 Something that is unadulterated is completely pure and has had nothing added to it. 未掺杂的;纯的

  • Organic food is unadulterated food produced without artificial chemicals or pesticides.

    有机食品是未使用人工化学制剂和杀虫剂的纯净食品。

SYN uncontaminated, pure, unprocessed  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （常用于强调不好的品质）十足的，纯粹的，地道的 You can also use unadulterated to emphasize a particular quality, often a bad quality. （常用于强调不好的品质）十足的，纯粹的，地道的 [emphasis]

  • It was pure, unadulterated hell.

    那里简直就是个不折不扣的地狱。

SYN sheer, complete, total, pure
