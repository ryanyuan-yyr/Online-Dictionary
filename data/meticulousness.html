meticulous
mətɪkjʊləs məˈtɪkjuləs məˈtɪkjələs
ADJ-GRADED 小心谨慎的;一丝不苟的;注意细节的 If you describe someone as meticulous, you mean that they do things very carefully and with great attention to detail. 小心谨慎的;一丝不苟的;注意细节的

  • He was so meticulous about everything.

    他对所有事情都一丝不苟。

  • The painting had been executed with meticulous attention to detail.

    画这幅画的时候，画家非常注意细节。

SYN thorough, detailed, particular, strict  
meticulously

  • The flat had been meticulously cleaned.

    公寓被仔仔细细地打扫过了。
