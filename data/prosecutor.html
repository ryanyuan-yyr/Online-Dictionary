prosecutor
prɒsɪkjuːtəʳ ˈprɒsɪˌkjuːtə ˈprɑsəˌkjutər
Word forms: plural prosecutors
N-COUNT 检察官；公诉人 In some countries, a prosecutor is a lawyer or official who brings charges against someone or tries to prove in a trial that they are guilty. 检察官；公诉人
SYN lawyer, attorney, counsel, procurator fiscal [Scottish]
