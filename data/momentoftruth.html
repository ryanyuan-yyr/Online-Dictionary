moment of truth
Word forms: plural moments of truth
N-COUNT 紧要关头;决定性时刻 If you refer to a time or event as the moment of truth, you mean that it is an important time when you must make a decision quickly, and whatever you decide will have important consequences in the future. 紧要关头;决定性时刻

  • Both men knew the moment of truth had arrived.

    两人都知道关键时刻到了。
