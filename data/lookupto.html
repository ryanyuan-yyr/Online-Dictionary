look up to
PHRASAL VERB （尤指对年长者）敬仰，钦佩，仰慕 If you look up to someone, especially someone older than you, you respect and admire them. （尤指对年长者）敬仰，钦佩，仰慕

  • You're a popular girl, Grace, and a lot of the younger ones look up to you. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    你很受大家欢迎，格雷丝，很多比你小的女孩子都很崇拜你。

SYN respect, honour, admire, esteem
