carnival
kɑːʳnɪvəl ˈkɑrnəvəl
Word forms: plural carnivals
1 N-COUNT 狂欢节;嘉年华会 A carnival is a public festival during which people play music and sometimes dance in the streets. 狂欢节;嘉年华会
2 N-COUNT 流动游乐场;嘉年华 A carnival is a travelling show which is held in a park or field and at which there are machines to ride on, entertainments, and games. 流动游乐场;嘉年华 [US]
in BRIT, use 英国英语用 funfair
3 N-COUNT [NOUN of n] 激动人心的事物的组合;五彩斑斓;八音齐奏 A carnival of something such as colours or sounds is a bright or exciting mixture of them. 激动人心的事物的组合;五彩斑斓;八音齐奏 [literary]

  • The avenues lined with jacaranda trees burst into a carnival of purple.

    大街的两旁种着蓝花楹，绽放的花朵汇成一片紫色的海洋。
