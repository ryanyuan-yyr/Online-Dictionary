overhang
oʊvəʳhæŋ oʊvəʳhæŋ ˌoʊvərˈhæŋ ˈoʊvərˌhæŋ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense overhangs, present participle overhanging, past tense , past participle overhung
The verb is pronounced /ˌəʊvə'hæŋ/. The noun is pronounced /'əʊvəhæŋ/. 动词读作 /ˌəʊvə'hæŋ/。名词读作 /'əʊvəhæŋ/。
1 VERB 悬于…之上；凸出于…之上 If one thing overhangs another, it sticks out over and above it. 悬于…之上；凸出于…之上

  • Part of the rock wall overhung the path at one point. [VERB noun]

    石墙的一部分在某处悬于小路的上方。

  • These restored houses overhang a system of quiet canals. [VERB noun]

    这些修葺一新的房屋俯视着静静流淌的运河河网。

  • ...the low, overhanging branches of a giant pine tree. [VERB-ing]

    一棵巨大松树低悬的树枝

SYN project (over), extend (over), loom (over), stand out (over)  
2 N-COUNT 垂悬物；凸出物 An overhang is the part of something that sticks out over and above something else. 垂悬物；凸出物

  • A sharp overhang of rock gave them cover. [+ of]

    一块凸出来的尖利岩石成了他们的避身之所。

  • There is a wide veranda under the overhang of the roof.

    悬吊的屋顶下是一条宽阔的游廊。

3 VERB 搭挂在…之上 If one thing overhangs another, it is supported by it and hangs down its sides. 搭挂在…之上

  • Let the pastry overhang the rim and brush between the layers with the melted butter. [VERB noun]

    用油酥面皮盖住边缘，每层之间抹些熔化的黄油。
