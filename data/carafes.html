carafe
kəræf kəˈræf kəˈrɑf
Word forms: plural carafes
N-COUNT (盛水或酒的)玻璃瓶,玻璃壶 A carafe is a glass container in which you serve water or wine. (盛水或酒的)玻璃瓶,玻璃壶

  • He ordered a carafe of wine. [+ of]

    他要了一瓶葡萄酒。

SYN jug, pitcher, flask, decanter  
A carafe of something is the amount of it contained in a carafe.一瓶的量;一壶的量

  • At dinner, share a half carafe of agreeable wine with an agreeable companion.

    晚饭时,和知己分享半壶美酒。
