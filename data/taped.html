tape
teɪp teɪp
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense tapes, present participle taping, past tense , past participle taped
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft on NOUN] 磁带;录音带;录像带 Tape is a narrow plastic strip covered with a magnetic substance. It is used to record sounds, pictures, and computer information. 磁带;录音带;录像带

  • Tape is expensive and loses sound quality every time it is copied.

    磁带成本高，而且每录制一次音质就会变差一些。

  • Many students declined to be interviewed on tape.

    许多学生谢绝录音采访。

2 N-COUNT (盒式或卷盘式)录音带，录像带 A tape is a cassette or spool with magnetic tape wound round it. (盒式或卷盘式)录音带，录像带

  • ...a new cassette tape.

    一盒新带子

  • She still listens to the tapes I made her.

    她还在听我为她录制的带子。

SYN recording, cassette, tape recording  
3 VERB (用录音机、录像机)录制(音乐、声音或电视画面) If you tape music, sounds, or television pictures, you record them using a tape recorder or a video recorder. (用录音机、录像机)录制(音乐、声音或电视画面)

  • She has just taped an interview. [VERB noun]

    她刚录制完一次采访。

  • He shouldn't be taping without the singer's permission. [VERB]

    他不该在没有征得演唱者同意的情况下录音。

  • ...taped evidence from prisoners. [VERB-ed]

    犯人的证词录音

SYN record, video, tape-record, make a recording of  
taping

  • ...an unauthorized taping.

    未经授权的录音

4 N-VAR (捆扎东西或注明衣物主人的)布条，布带 A tape is a strip of cloth used to tie things together or to identify who a piece of clothing belongs to. (捆扎东西或注明衣物主人的)布条，布带

  • The books were all tied up with tape.

    所有书籍都用布条捆扎了起来。

SYN binding, strip, band, string  
5 N-COUNT (赛道上的)终点线 A tape is a ribbon that is stretched across the finishing line of a race. (赛道上的)终点线

  • ...the finishing tape.

    终点线

6 N-UNCOUNT 胶带;胶条 Tape is a sticky strip of plastic used for sticking things together. 胶带;胶条

  • ...strong adhesive tape.

    强力胶带

7 VERB (用胶带)粘贴 If you tape one thing to another, you attach it using sticky tape. (用胶带)粘贴

  • I taped the base of the feather onto the velvet. [V n + onto/to]

    我用胶带把羽毛的根部粘在了天鹅绒上。

  • There are notes from years ago taped to the walls. [V n onto/to n]

    墙上有多年前用胶带粘上去的字条。

  • The envelope has been tampered with and then taped shut again. [be VERB-ed adj.ive]

    信封被人动过手脚，然后又用胶带封上了。

SYN bind, secure, stick, seal
