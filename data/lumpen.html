lumpen
lʌmpən ˈlʊmpən ˈlʌmpən
1 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 笨重的；大块的 A lumpen object is large, heavy, and lumpy. 笨重的；大块的 [mainly British, literary]

  • She was kneading a lumpen mass of dough.

    她正揉着一大块面团。

  • Lumpen shapes began to appear out of the shadows.

    阴影里闪出几个大大的黑影。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 呆头呆脑的；蠢笨乏味的 If you describe people as lumpen, you think they are dull and clumsy. 呆头呆脑的；蠢笨乏味的 [mainly British, literary, disapproval]

  • The people seemed lumpen and boring.

    这些人看起来蠢笨无趣得紧。
