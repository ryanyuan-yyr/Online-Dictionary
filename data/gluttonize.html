glutton
glʌtən ˈglʌtən
Word forms: plural gluttons
1 N-COUNT 贪吃的人；食量大的人；饕餮 If you think that someone eats too much and is greedy, you can say they are a glutton . 贪吃的人；食量大的人；饕餮 [disapproval]

  • I can't control my eating. It's hard when people don't understand and call you a glutton.

    我无法克制自己的食欲，当人们不理解而叫我贪吃鬼的时候心里别提有多难受了。

2 N-COUNT 酷爱…的人；急需…的人 If you say that someone is a glutton for something, you mean that they enjoy or need it very much. 酷爱…的人；急需…的人

  • He was a glutton for hard work. [+ for]

    他是个吃苦耐劳的人。

  • Ivy must be a glutton for punishment. [+ for]

    艾维一定是个不怕吃苦的家伙。
