settle in
PHRASAL VERB 习惯(新居);适应(新工作、新学校等) If you settle in, you become used to living in a new place, doing a new job, or going to a new school. 习惯(新居);适应(新工作、新学校等)

  • I enjoyed King Edward's School enormously once I'd settled in. [VERB PREP.]

    适应了爱德华国王学校的生活后，我就深深地爱上了这里。

SYN get used to something, adapt, accustom yourself, become acquainted
