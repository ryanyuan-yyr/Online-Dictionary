warring
wɔːrɪŋ ˈwɔːrɪŋ
ADJ [ADJ n] 交战的;敌对的;冲突的 Warring is used to describe groups of people who are involved in a conflict or quarrel with each other. 交战的;敌对的;冲突的

  • The warring factions have not yet turned in all their heavy weapons.

    敌对双方尚未将其重型武器悉数上缴。

  • ...warring husbands and wives.

    打打闹闹的夫妻

SYN hostile, fighting, conflicting, opposed
