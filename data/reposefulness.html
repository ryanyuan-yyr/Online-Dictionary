repose
rɪpoʊz rɪˈpoʊz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reposes, present participle reposing, past tense , past participle reposed
1 N-UNCOUNT 休息;休憩;闲适 Repose is a state in which you are resting and feeling calm. 休息;休憩;闲适 [literary]

  • He had a still, almost blank face in repose.

    他休息时面部平静，几乎毫无表情。

  • Its atmosphere is one of repose rather than excitement.

    那里气氛安宁而不热闹。

SYN rest, relaxation, inactivity, restfulness  
2 VERB 位于;安置于;坐落于 If something reposes somewhere, it is there. 位于;安置于;坐落于 [formal]

  • Exquisite china soup dishes reposed on silver plates. [VERB prep./adv.]

    银盘子上面摆放着精制的陶瓷汤盘。

SYN rest, lie, be set, be placed
