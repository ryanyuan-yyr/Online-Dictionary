boy racer
Word forms: plural boy racers
N-COUNT （尤指驾驶昂贵的高性能汽车的）飙车小子，飙车手 British journalists sometimes refer to young men who drive very fast, especially in expensive and powerful cars, as boy racers. （尤指驾驶昂贵的高性能汽车的）飙车小子，飙车手 [disapproval]

  • Bad driving is not just the preserve of boy racers.

    不良驾驶不仅仅是飙车小子们才会做的事。

  • Car manufacturers must stop pandering to the boy racers' fantasies about power and speed.

    汽车制造商们必须停止迎合飙车手对汽车动力和速度的狂热迷恋。
