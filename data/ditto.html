ditto
dɪtoʊ ˈdɪtoʊ
同上，同前(列清单时可用符号SDSq表示) In informal English, you can use ditto to represent a word or phrase that you have just used in order to avoid repeating it. In written lists, ditto can be represented by ditto marks — the symbol " — underneath the word that you want to repeat. 同上，同前(列清单时可用符号SDSq表示)
