unemployable
ʌnɪmplɔɪəbəl ˌʌnɛmˈplɔɪəbəl
ADJ （因缺乏相应的技术或能力而）不能受雇的 Someone who is unemployable does not have a job and is unlikely to get a job, because they do not have the skills or abilities that an employer might want. （因缺乏相应的技术或能力而）不能受雇的

  • He freely admits he is unemployable and will probably never find a job.

    他坦率地承认自己没什么技能，可能永远也找不到一份工作。
