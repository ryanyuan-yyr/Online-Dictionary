materialism
mətɪəriəlɪzəm məˈtɪriəlˌɪzəm
Word forms: plural materialists
1 N-UNCOUNT 实利主义;物质主义 Materialism is the attitude of someone who attaches a lot of importance to money and wants to possess a lot of material things. 实利主义;物质主义

  • ...the rising consumer materialism in society at large.

    全社会范围内日渐蔓延的消费至上意识

materialist

  • Leo is a materialist, living for life's little luxuries.

    利奥是个物质享乐主义者，生活的乐趣就在一些小享受上。

2 N-UNCOUNT 唯物主义;唯物论 Materialism is the belief that only physical matter exists, and that there is no spiritual world. 唯物主义;唯物论
