missus
mɪsɪz ˈmɪsəz
(also missis)
1 N-SING 妻子;太太 Some people refer to a man's wife as his missus . 妻子;太太

  • That's what bugs my missus more than anything.

    那种事最容易激怒我太太。

  • I do a bit of shopping for the missus.

    我帮老婆买了一点东西。

2 N-VOC 女士，夫人(在英国某些地区用于称呼不认识的妇女) In some parts of Britain, people use missus as a very informal way of addressing a woman who they do not know. 女士，夫人(在英国某些地区用于称呼不认识的妇女)

  • Thanks, missus.

    谢谢，夫人。
