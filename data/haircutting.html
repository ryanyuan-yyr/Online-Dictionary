haircut
heəʳkʌt ˈhɛəˌkʌt ˈhɛrˌkʌt
Word forms: plural haircuts
1 N-COUNT 理发 If you have a haircut, someone cuts your hair for you. 理发

  • Your hair is all right; it's just that you need a haircut.

    你的头发还好;不过你该理发了。

2 N-COUNT 发式;发型 A haircut is the style in which your hair has been cut. 发式;发型

  • Who's that guy with the funny haircut?

    那个发型怪怪的家伙是谁？
