second-degree
ˌsɛkənddɪˈɡriː ˈsɛkənddɪˈgri
1 ADJ [ADJ n] (犯罪)二等的，情节较轻的 In the United States, second-degree is used to describe crimes that are considered to be less serious than first-degree crimes. (犯罪)二等的，情节较轻的

  • The judge reduced the charge to second-degree murder.

    法官把罪名降为二级谋杀罪。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] (烧伤)二度的，中度的 A second-degree burn is more severe than a first-degree burn but less severe than a third-degree burn. (烧伤)二度的，中度的

  • James Bell suffered second-degree burns in an explosion.

    詹姆斯·贝尔在一次爆炸中被二度烧伤。
