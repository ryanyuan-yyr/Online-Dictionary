bash out
PHRASAL VERB 大量炮制；粗制滥造 If you say that someone bashes something out, you mean that they produce it quickly or in large quantities, but without much care or thought. 大量炮制；粗制滥造 [informal]

  • Up to then, they'd merrily bashed out albums in between tours. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那时，他们已经利用巡演的间隙轻松炮制了多张专辑。

  • Their ambitions are to bash out good grub with minimal fuss. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他们的目标就是尽可能省劲地大量供应可口饭菜。
