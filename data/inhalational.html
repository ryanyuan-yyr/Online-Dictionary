inhalation ɪnhəleɪʃən ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃən
Word forms: plural inhalations
1 N-VAR (空气等的)吸入 Inhalation is the process or act of breathing in, taking air and sometimes other substances into your lungs. (空气等的)吸入 [formal]

  • ...a complete cycle of inhalation and exhalation.

    吸入与呼出的完整循环

  • They were taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation.

    他们因吸入大量烟尘被送往医院。

  • Accidental inhalation of the powder can be harmful.

    意外的粉尘吸入可能有害。

  • Take several deep inhalations.

    猛吸几大口。

SYN breathing, breath, inspiration, inhaling  
2 N-COUNT 吸入药;吸入剂;吸入物 An inhalation is a treatment for colds and other illnesses in which you dissolve substances in hot water and breathe in the vapour. 吸入药;吸入剂;吸入物

  • Inhalations can soothe and control the cough.

    吸入药剂可以缓解并抑制咳嗽。

  • Inhalations of menthol may do the trick.

    吸入薄荷醇或许可以起到作用。
