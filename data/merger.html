merger
mɜːʳdʒəʳ ˈmɜrdʒər
Word forms: plural mergers
N-COUNT (公司、组织等的)合并，归并 A merger is the joining together of two separate companies or organizations so that they become one. (公司、组织等的)合并，归并 [business]

  • ...a merger between two of Britain's biggest trades unions.

    英国两个最大的工会的合并

  • ...the proposed merger of two Japanese banks.

    合并两家日本银行的建议

SYN union, fusion, consolidation, amalgamation
