insurgency
ɪnsɜːʳdʒənsi ɪnˈsɜrdʒənsi
Word forms: plural insurgencies
N-VAR 起义;暴动;叛乱 An insurgency is a violent attempt to oppose a country's government carried out by citizens of that country. 起义;暴动;叛乱 [formal]

  • He has led a violent armed insurgency for 15 years.

    他领导武装暴动已达15年之久。

SYN rebellion, rising, revolution, resistance
