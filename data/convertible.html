convertible
kənvɜːʳtɪbəl kənˈvɜrtəbəl
Word forms: plural convertibles
1 N-COUNT 敞篷车;折篷汽车 A convertible is a car with a soft roof that can be folded down or removed. 敞篷车;折篷汽车

  • Her own car is a convertible Golf.

    她自己有一辆敞篷高尔夫。

2 ADJ (投资工具、货币等)可兑换的，可转换的 In finance, convertible investments or money can be easily exchanged for other forms of investments or money. (投资工具、货币等)可兑换的，可转换的 [business]

  • ...the introduction of a convertible currency.

    可兑换货币的采用

SYN changeable, interchangeable, exchangeable, adjustable  
convertibility

  • ...the convertibility of the rouble.

    卢布的可兑换性

  • ...rapid export growth based on currency convertibility.

    基于货币自由兑换基础上的快速出口增长
