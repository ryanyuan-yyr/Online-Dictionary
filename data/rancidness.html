rancid
rænsɪd ˈrænsɪd
ADJ-GRADED （脂肪食物）变坏的，发臭的 If butter, bacon, or other oily foods are rancid, they have gone bad and taste old and unpleasant. （脂肪食物）变坏的，发臭的

  • Butter is perishable and can go rancid.

    黄油很容易变质，而且会发臭。

  • ...the odour of rancid milk.

    腐败牛奶发出的臭味

SYN rotten, sour, foul, bad
