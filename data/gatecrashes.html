gatecrash geɪtkræʃ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense gatecrashes, present participle gatecrashing, past tense , past participle gatecrashed
VERB （未经邀请）擅自参加（或出席） If someone gatecrashes a party or other social event, they go to it, even though they have not been invited. （未经邀请）擅自参加（或出席）

  • Scores of people tried desperately to gatecrash the party. [VERB noun]

    很多不速之客拼命想要挤进这个聚会。

  • He had gatecrashed but he was with other people we knew and there was no problem. [VERB]

    他不请自来，但他是跟我们认识的人同行，因此也没有什么关系。
