free-to-air
ADJ (电视节目和频道)供免费接收的 Free-to-air television programmes and channels are broadcast to all televisions and do not require a subscription or payment. (电视节目和频道)供免费接收的
Free to air is also an adverb.

  • For a change, the fight will be televised free to air on the Fox Network.

    与以往不同的是，这场拳击赛将在福克斯电视网免费播出。
