serial number
Word forms: plural serial numbers
1 N-COUNT [oft with poss] 序列号;编号 The serial number of an object is a number on that object which identifies it. 序列号;编号

  • ...the gun's serial number.

    枪支的序列号

  • ...your bike's serial number.

    你自行车的编号

  • All the bills had the same serial number. The cash was counterfeit.

    所有钞票的序列号都是一样的。这些钱是伪钞。

2 N-COUNT (美国的)部队编号，部队番号 The serial number of a member of the United States military forces is a number which identifies them. (美国的)部队编号，部队番号

  • He could never ever give any responses to his captor other than name, rank, serial number and date of birth.

    除姓名、军衔、部队编号和出生年月外，他再也不肯回答俘获他的人的任何问题。
