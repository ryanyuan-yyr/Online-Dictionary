bleat on about
PHRASAL VERB 喋喋不休；唠唠叨叨 If you say that someone is bleating on about something, you mean that they are talking about it a great deal in a way which makes them sound weak and irritating. 喋喋不休；唠唠叨叨 [mainly British, disapproval]

  • It's no good bleating on about it, you ought to do something about it. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    光喋喋不休没用，你应该采取行动。
