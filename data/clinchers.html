clincher
klɪntʃəʳ ˈklɪntʃər
Word forms: plural clinchers
N-COUNT 起决定性作用的事实(或论点) A clincher is a fact or argument that finally proves something, settles a dispute, or helps someone achieve a victory. 起决定性作用的事实(或论点) [informal]

  • DNA fingerprinting has proved the clincher in this investigation.

    在这次调查中,DNA指纹鉴定起了决定性的作用。

  • The clincher was City's second goal, scored minutes from the end.

    离比赛结束还剩几分钟的时候，曼城队的第二粒进球锁定了胜局。
