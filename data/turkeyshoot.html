turkey shoot
ˈtɜːkɪ ʃuːt
Word forms: turkey shoots
N-COUNT [usu sing: a N] 土耳其射击; 指在战争等冲突中的一方比另一方实力强很多，以致于另一方没有获胜的可能。If someone refers to a battle or other conflict as a turkey shoot, they mean that one side is so much stronger or better armed than the other one that the weaker side has no chance of winning at all. 土耳其射击; 指在战争等冲突中的一方比另一方实力强很多，以致于另一方没有获胜的可能。 [US, informal]

  • Some American pilots described the experience as a turkey shoot.

    一些美国飞行员将这次经历描述为一方占有绝对优势的情形。
