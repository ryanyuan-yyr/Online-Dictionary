deal with
1 PHRASAL VERB 处理;应付;与…打交道 When you deal with something or someone that needs attention, you give your attention to them, and often solve a problem or make a decision concerning them. 处理;应付;与…打交道

  • ...the way that building societies deal with complaints. [VERB PREP. noun]

    建屋互助会处理投诉的方式

  • In dealing with suicidal youngsters, our aims should be clear. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在对待有自杀倾向的青少年时，我们的目标应当很明确。

  • The President said the agreement would allow other vital problems to be dealt with. [VERB PREP. noun]

    总统说这项协议将能使其他关键问题得到解决。

2 PHRASAL VERB 克制(不快情感);冷静面对(感情上困难的局面) If you deal with an unpleasant emotion or an emotionally difficult situation, you recognize it, and remain calm and in control of yourself in spite of it. 克制(不快情感);冷静面对(感情上困难的局面)

  • She saw a psychiatrist who used hypnotism to help her deal with her fear. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她去看了一位用催眠术帮她克服恐惧感的精神科医生。

  • He was able to deal with his captivity by maintaining a high degree of anger about the unfairness of his capture. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他对自己不公正的被俘极为怨愤，正是这股怨愤使他得以熬过囚禁生涯。

3 PHRASAL VERB 论述;讨论;涉及 If a book, speech, or film deals with a particular thing, it has that thing as its subject or is concerned with it. 论述;讨论;涉及

  • ...the parts of his book which deal with contemporary Paris. [VERB PREP. noun]

    他的书中涉及当代巴黎的那些部分

SYN be concerned with, involve, concern, touch  
4 PHRASAL VERB 与…做买卖;和…有生意往来 If you deal with a particular person or organization, you have business relations with them. 与…做买卖;和…有生意往来

  • When I worked in Florida I dealt with British people all the time. [VERB PREP. noun]

    在佛罗里达工作时，我总与英国人有生意往来。

  • He's a hard man to deal with. [VERB PREP. noun]

    要跟他这个人做生意很难。
