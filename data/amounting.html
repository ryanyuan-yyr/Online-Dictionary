amount
əmaʊnt əˈmaʊnt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense amounts, present participle amounting, past tense , past participle amounted
1 N-VAR 总数；总额 The amount of something is how much there is, or how much you have, need, or get. 总数；总额

  • He needs that amount of money to survive. [+ of]

    他需要那样一笔钱以生存下去。

  • I still do a certain amount of work for them. [+ of]

    我仍然为他们做一些工作。

  • Postal money orders are available in amounts up to $700.

    邮政汇票的金额最高可达700美元。

SYN quantity, lot, measure, size  
2 VERB 合计；共计 If something amounts to a particular total, all the parts of it add up to that total. 合计；共计

  • Consumer spending on sports-related items amounted to £9.75 billion. [VERB + to]

    消费者在体育相关产品上的花费共计97.5亿英镑。

3 PHRASE 大量 If you say that there is any amount of something, there is a lot of it. If you say that there are any amount of people or things, there are many people or things. 大量

  • I'm able to lay my hands on any amount of cash at a minute's notice.

    多少钱我都可以马上弄到。

  • There are any amount of clubs you could join.

    有无数个俱乐部你可以加入。

Usage Note :

  • You should avoid using a plural noun after amount of  instead you should use number of with a plural noun. ...the number of people out of work.

    amount of后不用复数名词，而number of后要用复数名词：the number of people out of work（失业人数）。

Phrasal verbs:
amount to
+−Usage
Usageamount and number
Number is used to talk about how many there are of something: Madhu was surprised at the large number of students in the class. Amount is used to talk about how much there is of something: There is only a small amount of water in the glass.
