talk out
PHRASAL VERB 详细商谈;透彻讨论 If you talk out something such as a problem, you discuss it thoroughly in order to settle it. 详细商谈;透彻讨论

  • Talking things out with someone else can be helpful. [VERB noun PREP.]

    跟别人讨论讨论把问题彻底谈开了可能会有用。

  • Talk out your problems. Do not keep them bottled up. [VERB PREP. noun]

    把问题说出来，别都憋在心里。
