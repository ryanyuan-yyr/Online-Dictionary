moisture
mɔɪstʃəʳ ˈmɔɪstʃər
N-UNCOUNT 潮气;湿气;水分 Moisture is tiny drops of water in the air, on a surface, or in the ground. 潮气;湿气;水分

  • When the soil is dry, more moisture is lost from the plant.

    土壤干燥时，植物会流失更多的水分。

  • Rainfall affects the moisture content of the atmosphere.

    降雨会影响空气湿度。

SYN damp, water, liquid, sweat
