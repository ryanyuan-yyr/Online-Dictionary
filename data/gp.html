gp
N-COUNT 全科医生；普通医生 A GP is a doctor who does not specialize in any particular area of medicine, but who has a medical practice in which he or she treats all types of illness. GP is an abbreviation for 'general practitioner'. 全科医生；普通医生

  • Her husband called their local GP.

    她的丈夫打电话叫来了当地的全科医生。
