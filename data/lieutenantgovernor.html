lieutenant governor
Word forms: plural lieutenant governors
1 N-COUNT （美国）副州长 A lieutenant governor is an elected official who acts as the deputy of a state governor in the United States. （美国）副州长 [US]
2 N-COUNT （加拿大）副总督 A lieutenant governor is an official elected by the Canadian government to act as a representative of the British king or queen in a province of Canada. （加拿大）副总督
