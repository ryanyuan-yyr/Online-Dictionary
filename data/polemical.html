polemical
pəlemɪkəl pəˈlɛmɪkəl pəˈlɛmɪk
ADJ-GRADED 争辩的；辩论的；论战的 Polemical means arguing very strongly for or against a belief or opinion. 争辩的；辩论的；论战的

  • Daniels is at his best when he's cool and direct, rather than combative and polemical.

    丹尼尔斯冷静、坦率时状态最佳；而好斗、爱辩时则不然。

  • ...Kramer's biting polemical novel.

    克莱默尖锐的辩论小说

SYN controversial, cutting, biting, critical
