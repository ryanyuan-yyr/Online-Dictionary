hack off
PHRASAL VERB 砍下;砍去;劈下;劈掉 If you hack something off, you cut it off with strong, rough strokes using a sharp tool such as an axe or knife. 砍下;砍去;劈下;劈掉

  • Kim even hacked off her long hair. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    金甚至剪掉了她的长发。

  • Surgeons saved a man's arm after it was hacked off at the elbow. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    一位男子的手臂从肘部被砍下，经外科医生实施手术后得以保住了手臂。

SYN cut off, chop off, lop off
