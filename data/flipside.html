flip side
(also flipside)
1 N-SING (没有主打歌曲的)唱片反面 The flip side of a record is the side that does not have the main song on it. (没有主打歌曲的)唱片反面

  • 'What's on the flip side?'

    “唱片反面是什么歌？”

2 N-SING (情况的)不明显的一面，反面，负面 The flip side of a situation consists of the less obvious or less pleasant aspects of it. (情况的)不明显的一面，反面，负面

  • The trade deficit is the flip side of a rapidly expanding economy.

    贸易赤字是经济快速发展带来的负面产物。
