gratuity
grətjuːɪti -tuː- grəˈtuɪti grɛˈtjuɪti
Word forms: plural gratuities
1 N-COUNT 小费；赏钱 A gratuity is a gift of money to someone who has done something for you. 小费；赏钱 [formal]

  • The porter expects a gratuity.

    行李员想要小费。

SYN tip, present, gift, reward  
2 N-COUNT 退休金；遣散费；（尤指军人的）退役金 A gratuity is a large gift of money that is given to someone when they leave their job, especially when they leave the armed forces. 退休金；遣散费；（尤指军人的）退役金 [British, formal]

  • He is taking a gratuity from the Navy.

    他将领到海军补助的退役金。
