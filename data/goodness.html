goodness
gʊdnəs ˈgʊdnɪs
1 EXCLAM （表示惊讶）天哪，啊呀 People sometimes say 'goodness' or 'my goodness' to express surprise. for goodness sake→see: sake  thank goodness→see: thank  （表示惊讶）天哪，啊呀

  • Goodness, I wonder if he knows.

    天哪，不知道他是否晓得。

  • My goodness, he's earned millions in his career.

    我的天哪，他干那一行已挣了好几百万了。

2 N-UNCOUNT 善良；美德 Goodness is the quality of being kind, helpful, and honest. 善良；美德

  • He retains a faith in human goodness.

    他依然相信人是善良的。

SYN virtue, honour, merit, integrity
