signature
sɪgnətʃəʳ ˈsɪɡnɪtʃə ˈsɪgnətʃər
Word forms: plural signatures
1 N-COUNT 签名;署名;签字 Your signature is your name, written in your own characteristic way, often at the end of a document to indicate that you wrote the document or that you agree with what it says. 签名;署名;签字

  • I was writing my signature at the bottom of the page.

    我正往那页纸的底部签名。

  • ...a petition containing 170 signatures.

    有 170 人签名的请愿书

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 为(某人)所特有的;体现个体特征的 A signature item is typical of or associated with a particular person. 为(某人)所特有的;体现个体特征的 [mainly journalism]

  • Rabbit stew is one of chef Giancarlo Moeri's signature dishes.

    炖野兔是厨师詹卡洛·莫里的招牌菜之一。

  • The dress reflects our signature style of understated elegance with a strong feeling of individuality.

    这条裙子体现了我们所追求的低调优雅又有强烈个性感的独特风格。

3 PHRASE [VERB and NOUN inflect, PHRASE n] 在…上签字;签署 If you put your signature to a document, you sign it as a way of officially showing that you agree with what is written. 在…上签字;签署

  • The two sides met to put their signatures to a formal agreement.

    双方聚首签署一份正式协议。
