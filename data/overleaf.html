overleaf
oʊvəʳliːf ˈoʊvərˌlif
ADV [n ADVERB] [oft ADVERB after verb] 在（书页、杂志页等的）背面；在页后 Overleaf is used in books and magazines to say that something is on the other side of the page you are reading. 在（书页、杂志页等的）背面；在页后

  • Answer the questionnaire overleaf.

    请回答背面的问卷。

  • There are two types of contraceptive pill available and these are described overleaf.

    现有两种避孕药物，说明请见背面。
