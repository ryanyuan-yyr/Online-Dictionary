switch on
PHRASAL VERB 打开,开启(电灯等) If you switch on a light or other electrical device, you make it start working by operating a switch. 打开,开启(电灯等) [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • She emptied both their mugs and switched on the electric kettle. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她把他们俩的杯子倒空，烧上了电水壶。

  • He pointed the light at his feet and tried to switch it on. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他把灯对准自己的脚，试图打开它。

SYN turn on, put on, set off, activate
