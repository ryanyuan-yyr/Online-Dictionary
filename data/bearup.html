bear up
PHRASAL VERB 保持振作；支撑下去；不气馁 If you bear up when experiencing problems, you remain cheerful and show courage in spite of them. 保持振作；支撑下去；不气馁

  • How's Mary bearing up?. [VERB PREP.]

    玛丽现在怎么样？

  • She was frightened that she would be unable to bear up under the pain of childbirth. [V P under n]

    她害怕自己无法承受生孩子的痛苦。

SYN cope, suffer, manage, survive
