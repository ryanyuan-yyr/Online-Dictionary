outplay
aʊtpleɪ ˌaʊtˈpleɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense outplays, present participle outplaying, past tense , past participle outplayed
VERB （在体育比赛中）打得比…好，战胜，击败 In sports, if one person or team outplays an opposing person or team, they play much better than their opponents. （在体育比赛中）打得比…好，战胜，击败

  • We outplayed the Australians. [VERB noun]

    我们战胜了澳大利亚队。

  • He was outplayed by the Swedish 21-year-old. [VERB noun]

    他输给了21岁的瑞典小将。
