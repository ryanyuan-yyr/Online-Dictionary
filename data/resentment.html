resentment
rɪzentmənt rɪˈzɛntmənt
Word forms: plural resentments
N-UNCOUNT 怨恨;愤慨;憎恨 Resentment is bitterness and anger that someone feels about something. 怨恨;愤慨;憎恨

  • She expressed resentment at being interviewed by a social worker. [+ at]

    她表达了对被一位社工采访的愤恨。

  • But the problems of inflation and unemployment still cause a lot of resentment.

    但是通货膨胀和失业问题仍然激起了很多人的怨恨。

SYN bitterness, indignation, ill feeling, ill will  
Quotations
