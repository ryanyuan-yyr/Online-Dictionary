N-COUNT 股份证明;股票凭证 A scrip is a certificate which shows that an investor owns part of a share or stock. 股份证明;股票凭证 [business]

  • The cash or scrip would be offered as part of a pro rata return of capital to shareholders.

    作为按比例资本收益的一部分，股票持有者将获得现金或股息凭证。
