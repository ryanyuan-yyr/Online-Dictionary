nationalize
næʃənəlaɪz ˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz ˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense nationalizes, present participle nationalizing, past tense , past participle nationalized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 nationalise
VERB 把…收归国有;使国有化 If a government nationalizes a private company or industry, that company or industry becomes owned by the state and controlled by the government. 把…收归国有;使国有化 [business]

  • In 1987, Garcia introduced legislation to nationalize Peru's banking and financial systems. [VERB noun]

    1987年，加西亚立法将秘鲁银行业和金融系统收归国有。

  • The coffee industry was nationalised at the time of independence. [VERB noun]

    国家取得独立时，咖啡行业被收归国有。

nationalization

  • ...the campaign for the nationalization of the coal mines.

    争取将煤矿收归国有的运动

  • The steel workers were relatively indifferent to the issue of nationalization.

    相对而言，钢铁工人对国有化的议题并不太关心。
