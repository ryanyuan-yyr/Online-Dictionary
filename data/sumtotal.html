sum total
sʌm ˈtəʊtəl
N-SING (通常用来表示总量非常小)总共，一切，全部 The sum total of a number of things is all the things added or considered together. You often use this expression to indicate that you are disappointed because the total amount is rather small. (通常用来表示总量非常小)总共，一切，全部

  • That small room contained the sum total of the family's possessions. [+ of]

    那个小房间放着这家人的全部家当。

  • We have already seen the sum total of his attributes.

    我们已经见识了他所有的那点儿品质。
