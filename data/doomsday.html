doomsday
duːmzdeɪ ˈdumzˌdeɪ
1 N-UNCOUNT 末日;灾难日 Doomsday is a day or time when you expect something terrible or unpleasant is going to happen. 末日;灾难日

  • ...the doomsday scenario of civil war between the two factions.

    两个派系之间的内战可能会带来世界末日般的灾难

2 N-PROPER (基督教) 世界末日，最后审判日 In the Christian religion, Doomsday is the last day of the world, on which God will judge everyone. (基督教) 世界末日，最后审判日
