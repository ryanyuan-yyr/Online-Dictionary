unobserved
ʌnəbzɜːʳvd ˌʌnəbˈzɜːvd
ADJ [verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb, ADJ n] 不被注意的；未被看到的 If you do something unobserved, you do it without being seen by other people. 不被注意的；未被看到的

  • Looking round to make sure he was unobserved, he slipped through the door.

    他看了看周围，确定没有被人注意，就从那扇门溜走了。

  • John had been sitting, unobserved, in the darkness.

    约翰一直坐在黑暗中，没有被人察觉。
