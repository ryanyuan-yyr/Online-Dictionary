pack off
PHRASAL VERB 把…打发到 If you pack someone off somewhere, you send them there to stay for a period of time. 把…打发到 [informal] [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • He packed off his wife and children to stay in a caravan in Wales. [V P n to-inf]

    他把妻儿打发到在威尔士的一辆旅行拖车上去住。

  • I finally succeeded in packing her off to bed. [VERB noun PREP. + to]

    最后我总算是把她打发上床睡觉了。

SYN send away, dismiss, send packing [informal] , bundle out
