uprising
ʌpraɪzɪŋ ˈʌpˌraɪzɪŋ
Word forms: plural uprisings
N-COUNT [usually singular] 起义;暴动 When there is an uprising, a group of people start fighting against the people who are in power in their country, because they want to bring about a political change. 起义;暴动

  • ...a popular uprising against the authoritarian government. [+ against]

    反对独裁政府的群众暴动

  • Isolated attacks in the north-east of the country have now turned into a full-scale uprising.

    该国东北部的零星袭击现在已经演变成为全面暴动。

SYN rebellion, rising, revolution, outbreak
