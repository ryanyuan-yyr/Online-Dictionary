neckline
neklaɪn ˈnɛkˌlaɪn
Word forms: plural necklines
N-COUNT (连衣裙、女衬衫等的)领口，开领 The neckline of a dress, blouse, or other piece of clothing is the edge that goes around your neck, especially the front part of it. (连衣裙、女衬衫等的)领口，开领

  • ...a short brown dress with a plunging neckline.

    棕色深开领短裙

  • ...a dress with pale pink roses around the neckline.

    领口周围饰有淡粉色玫瑰花的裙装
