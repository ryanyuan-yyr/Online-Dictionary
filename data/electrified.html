electrified
ɪlektrɪfaɪd ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪd
ADJ [ADJ n] 通电的;带电的 An electrified fence or other barrier has been connected to a supply of electricity, so that a person or animal that touches it will get an electric shock. 通电的;带电的

  • The house was set amid dense trees and surrounded by an electrified fence.

    那座房子坐落于茂密的树林中，四周布有带电围栏。
