equal opportunity employer ˈiːkwəl ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪtɪ ɪmˈplɔɪə

N-COUNT 提供均等机会的雇主An equal opportunity employer is an employer who gives people the same opportunities for employment, pay, and promotion, without discrimination against anyone. 提供均等机会的雇主 [business]
