mecca
mekə
Word forms: plural meccas
1 N-PROPER 麦加; 沙特城市，伊斯兰教圣城，先知穆罕默德的诞生地，所有穆斯林祈祷朝拜的方向Mecca is a city in Saudi Arabia, which is the holiest city in Islam because the Prophet Mohammed was born there. All Muslims face toward Mecca when they pray. 麦加; 沙特城市，伊斯兰教圣城，先知穆罕默德的诞生地，所有穆斯林祈祷朝拜的方向
2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 众人向往的地方; 胜地If you describe a place as a mecca or Mecca for a particular thing or activity, you mean that many people who are interested in it go there. 众人向往的地方; 胜地

  • Thailand has become the tourist mecca of Asia.

    泰国已经成为亚洲旅游胜地。
