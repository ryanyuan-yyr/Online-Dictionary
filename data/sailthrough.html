sail through
PHRASAL VERB 顺利地通过;轻易地做成 If someone or something sails through a difficult situation or experience, they deal with it easily and successfully. 顺利地通过;轻易地做成 [Also VERB PREPOSITION]

  • While she sailed through her maths exams, he struggled. [VERB PREP. noun]

    她顺利地通过数学考试，而他却异常地吃力。

  • The agreement sailed through the French national assembly by 495 votes to 61. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该协议以 495 票对 61 票在法国国民议会顺利通过。

SYN cruise through, walk through, romp through, pass easily
