mass market mæs ˈmɑːkɪt
Word forms: plural mass markets
1 N-COUNT (有意购买某产品的)大众市场 Mass market is used to refer to the large numbers of people who want to buy a particular product. (有意购买某产品的)大众市场 [business]

  • They now have access to the mass markets of Japan and the UK.

    他们现在进入了日本和英国的大众市场。

  • ...a new computer aimed at the mass market.

    以大众市场为目标客户的新型电脑

2 ADJ [ADJ n] （产品）面向大众的 Mass-market products are designed and produced for selling to large numbers of people. （产品）面向大众的 [business]

  • ...mass-market paperbacks.

    面向大众的平装书籍
