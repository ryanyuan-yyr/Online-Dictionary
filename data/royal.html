royal
rɔɪəl ˈrɔɪəl
Word forms: plural royals
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 国王的;女王的;王室的;皇室的 Royal is used to indicate that something is connected with a king, queen, or emperor, or their family. A royal person is a king, queen, or emperor, or a member of their family. 国王的;女王的;王室的;皇室的

  • ...an invitation to a royal garden party.

    参加皇家游园会的邀请

  • ...the Japanese royal couple, Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko.

    明仁天皇和美智子皇后这对日本皇室夫妇

SYN regal, kingly or queenly, princely, imperial  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] （用于机构或组织名称）皇家的 Royal is used in the names of institutions or organizations that are officially appointed or supported by a member of a royal family. （用于机构或组织名称）皇家的

  • ...the Royal Academy of Music.

    皇家音乐学院

  • ...several pilots of the Royal Navy's 846 Squadron.

    皇家海军846中队的几个领航员

3 N-COUNT [usually plural] 王室成员;皇族 Members of the royal family are sometimes referred to as royals . 王室成员;皇族 [informal]

  • The royals have always been patrons of charities pulling in large donations.

    王室成员一向热心慈善事业，捐资数目巨大。
