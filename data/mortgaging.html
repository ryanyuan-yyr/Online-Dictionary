mortgage
mɔːʳgɪdʒ ˈmɔrgɪdʒ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense mortgages, present participle mortgaging, past tense , past participle mortgaged
1 N-COUNT [oft NOUN n] 房屋抵押贷款 A mortgage is a loan of money which you get from a bank or building society in order to buy a house. 房屋抵押贷款

  • ...an increase in mortgage rates.

    房屋抵押贷款利率的提高

2 VERB 抵押(房屋、土地)借款 If you mortgage your house or land, you use it as a guarantee to a company in order to borrow money from them. 抵押(房屋、土地)借款

  • They had to mortgage their home to pay the bills. [VERB noun]

    他们不得不抵押房屋借贷来支付这些款项。

  • ...mortgaged homes. [VERB-ed]

    按揭房
