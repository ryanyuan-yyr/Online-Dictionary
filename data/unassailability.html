unassailable
ʌnəseɪləbəl ˌʌnəˈseɪləbəl
ADJ-GRADED 坚不可摧的；无懈可击的 If you describe something or someone as unassailable, you mean that nothing can alter, destroy, or challenge them. 坚不可摧的；无懈可击的

  • That was enough to give Mansell an unassailable lead.

    那足以让曼塞尔获得别人无法撼动的领先地位。

  • His legal position is unassailable.

    他的合法地位是不容置疑的。

  • Liverpool football club are still looking unassailable.

    利物浦足球俱乐部显得依然坚如磐石。

SYN invincible, impregnable, invulnerable, secure
