three-quarters
(also three quarters)
QUANT 四分之三 Three-quarters is an amount that is three out of four equal parts of something. 四分之三

  • Three-quarters of the country's workers took part in the strike. [+ of]

    该国有 3/4 的工人参加了这次罢工。

  • It took him about three-quarters of an hour. [+ of]

    那花了他大概 45 分钟。

Three-quarters is also a pronoun.

  • Road deaths have increased by three-quarters.

    交通事故死亡人数增加了 3/4。

  • I'm 29 and three-quarters.

    我 29 岁零 9 个月。

Three-quarters is also an adverb.

  • We were left with an open bottle of champagne three-quarters full.

    给我们留下一瓶打开的香槟酒，里面的酒还有3/4。

  • Remove the vegetables from the steamer when they are three-quarters cooked.

    将蔬菜蒸至大半熟后从蒸锅里捞出。
