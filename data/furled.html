furl
fɜːʳl fɜrl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense furls, present participle furling, past tense , past participle furled
VERB 收拢(伞等);卷起，收卷(帆、旗等) When you furl something made of fabric such as an umbrella, sail, or flag, you roll or fold it up because it is not going to be used. 收拢(伞等);卷起，收卷(帆、旗等)

  • An attempt was made to furl the headsail. [VERB noun]

    人们试图收起艏斜帆。

  • ...a furled umbrella. [VERB-ed]

    收拢的雨伞
