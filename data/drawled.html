drawl
drɔːl drɔl
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense drawls, present participle drawling, past tense , past participle drawled
VERB 拉长调子说;慢吞吞地说 If someone drawls, they speak slowly and not very clearly, with long vowel sounds. 拉长调子说;慢吞吞地说

  • 'I guess you guys don't mind if I smoke?' he drawled. [VERB with quote]

    “伙计们，我想你们不介意我抽烟吧？”他拉长音调说。

  • He has a deep voice and he drawls slightly. [VERB]

    他嗓音低沉，说话略带拖腔。

Drawl is also a noun.

  • ...Jack's southern drawl.

    杰克南方味的拖腔
