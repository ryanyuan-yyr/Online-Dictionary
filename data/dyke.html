dyke
daɪk daɪk
Word forms: plural dykes
The spelling dike is also used, especially for meaning 1. 亦拼作 dike，尤用于义项1。
1 N-COUNT 堤;堰;坝 A dyke is a thick wall that is built to stop water flooding onto very low-lying land from a river or from the sea. 堤;堰;坝
SYN wall, barrier, dam, enclosure  
2 N-COUNT 女子同性恋者 A dyke is a lesbian. 女子同性恋者 [informal, offensive]
