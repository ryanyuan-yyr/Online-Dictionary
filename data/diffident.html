diffident
dɪfɪdənt ˈdɪfədənt
ADJ-GRADED 羞怯的;胆怯的;缺乏自信的 Someone who is diffident is rather shy and does not enjoy talking about themselves or being noticed by other people. 羞怯的;胆怯的;缺乏自信的

  • Helen was diffident and reserved.

    海伦比较害羞内向。

SYN shy, reserved, withdrawn, reluctant  
diffidence

  • He entered the room with a certain diffidence.

    他怯生生地走进房间。

diffidently

  • 'Would you,' he asked diffidently, 'like to talk to me about it?'

    “你想跟我说说这件事吗？”他怯怯地问道。
