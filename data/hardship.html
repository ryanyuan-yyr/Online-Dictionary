hardship
hɑːʳdʃɪp ˈhɑrdˌʃɪp
Word forms: plural hardships
N-VAR 艰难;艰苦;贫困 Hardship is a situation in which your life is difficult or unpleasant, often because you do not have enough money. 艰难;艰苦;贫困

  • Many people are suffering economic hardship.

    很多人正处于经济困难之中。

  • One of the worst hardships is having so little time to spend with one's family.

    最大的痛苦之一是几乎没有时间和家人在一起。

SYN suffering, want, need, trouble
