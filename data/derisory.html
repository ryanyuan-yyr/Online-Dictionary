derisory
dɪraɪzəri
1 ADJ-GRADED 微不足道的;少得可怜的 If you describe something such as an amount of money as derisory, you are emphasizing that it is so small or inadequate that it seems silly or not worth considering. 微不足道的;少得可怜的 [disapproval]

  • She was being paid what I considered a derisory amount of money.

    在我看来，她得到的报酬真是少得可怜。

  • Were the contracts that were offered to the players as derisory as we have been led to believe?

    合同里给球员开出的薪水真如想要我们相信的那样低得可笑吗？

SYN ridiculous, insulting, outrageous, ludicrous  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 同 derisive Derisory means the same as derisive . 同 derisive

  • ...derisory remarks about the police.

    嘲弄警察的话
