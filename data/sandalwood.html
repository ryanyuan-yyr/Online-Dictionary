sandalwood
sændəlwʊd ˈsændəlˌwʊd
1 N-UNCOUNT 檀香木;檀香树 Sandalwood is the sweet-smelling wood of a tree that is found in South Asia and Australia. It is also the name of the tree itself. 檀香木;檀香树
2 N-UNCOUNT 檀香(木)油 Sandalwood is the oil extracted from the wood of the tree. It is used to make perfume. 檀香(木)油
