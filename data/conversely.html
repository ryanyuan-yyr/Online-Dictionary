conversely
kɒnvɜːʳsli kənvɜːʳsli ˈkɒnvɜːslɪ
ADV 相反地;反过来说 You say conversely to indicate that the situation you are about to describe is the opposite or reverse of the one you have just described. 相反地;反过来说 [formal]

  • In real life, nobody was all bad, nor, conversely, all good.

    在现实生活中，没有彻头彻尾的坏人，相反地，也没有完完全全的好人。
