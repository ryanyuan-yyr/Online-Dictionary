connivance
kənaɪvəns kəˈnaɪvəns
N-UNCOUNT [oft with the N of n] 纵容;默许;共谋 Connivance is a willingness to allow or assist something to happen even though you know it is wrong. 纵容;默许;共谋 [disapproval]

  • The deficit had grown with the connivance of the banks. [+ of]

    在银行的放纵之下，赤字增加了。

  • The goods were exported with official connivance.

    这些商品是在官方的默许下出口的。

SYN collusion, intrigue, conspiring, complicity
