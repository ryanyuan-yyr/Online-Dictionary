high-impact
ˈhaɪˈɪmˌpækt
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （锻炼）高强度的 High-impact exercise puts a lot of stress on your body. （锻炼）高强度的

  • ...high-impact aerobics.

    高强度有氧健身操

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （材料）高强度的，承载重压的 High-impact materials are very strong. （材料）高强度的，承载重压的

  • The durable high-impact plastic case is water resistant to 100 feet.

    这种结实的高强度塑料盒子在水下100英尺以内是防水的。
