butt out
PHRASAL VERB 走开；别管闲事 If someone tells you to butt out, they are telling you rudely to go away or not to interfere with what they are doing. 走开；别管闲事 [mainly US, informal]

  • She would have liked to tell him to butt out. [VERB PREP.]

    她本想告诉他叫他别插手的。

  • The time has come for parents to butt out of the adolescent's daily life. [V P of n]

    到了让父母别再干预未成年子女日常生活的时候了。
