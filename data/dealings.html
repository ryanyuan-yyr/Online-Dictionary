dealings
diːlɪŋz
N-PLURAL 来往;交道;交易;生意 Someone's dealings with a person or organization are the relations that they have with them or the business that they do with them. 来往;交道;交易;生意

  • He has learnt little in his dealings with the international community. [+ with]

    他在与国际社会打交道的过程中几乎什么都没学到。

  • ...her family's business dealings.

    她家族的生意来往
