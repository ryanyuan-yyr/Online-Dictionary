homespun
hoʊmspʌn ˈhoʊmˌspʌn
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] （观点、想法等）简单的，朴实的 You use homespun to describe opinions or ideas that are simple and not based on special knowledge. （观点、想法等）简单的，朴实的

  • The book is simple homespun philosophy.

    这本书阐述的是简单朴实的哲学。

SYN unsophisticated, homely, plain, rough  
2 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] （衣物）用家织土布做的 Homespun clothes are made from cloth that has been made at home, rather than in a factory. （衣物）用家织土布做的

  • Most of them still wore the homespun clothes in which they had left their farms.

    他们大多仍穿着当初离开农场时身上穿的那些土布衣服。
