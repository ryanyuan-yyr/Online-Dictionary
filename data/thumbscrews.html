thumbscrew
θʌmskruː ˈθʌmˌskru
(also thumb screw)Word forms: plural thumbscrews
1 N-COUNT 拇指夹(旧时的一种刑具) A thumbscrew is an object that was used in the past to torture people by crushing their thumbs. 拇指夹(旧时的一种刑具)
2 N-COUNT 极大的压力 If someone puts the thumbscrews on you, they start to put you under extreme pressure in order to force you to do something. 极大的压力
