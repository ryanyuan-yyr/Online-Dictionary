windsock
wɪndsɒk ˈwɪndˌsɒk ˈwɪndˌsɑk
(also wind sock)Word forms: windsocks
N-COUNT 风标A windsock is a device, consisting of a tube of cloth mounted on a pole, that is used at airports and airfields to indicate the direction and force of the wind. 风标

  • The hangar had a windsock on the roof.

    飞机库房顶上有一只风标。
