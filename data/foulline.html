foul line
Word forms: foul lines
1 N-COUNT (篮球比赛中的)罚球线In basketball, the foul line is the line from which a player tries to throw the ball through the basket after they have been fouled. (篮球比赛中的)罚球线
2 N-COUNT (体育场上划分比赛区域的)边界In other sports, the foul lines are the lines that form the boundaries of the playing area. (体育场上划分比赛区域的)边界
