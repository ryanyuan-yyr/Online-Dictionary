dragnet
drægnet ˈdrægˌnɛt
N-SING [oft n NOUN] (警方)拉网式搜捕 A dragnet is a method used by police to catch suspected criminals. A large number of police officers search a specific area, in the hope that they will eventually find the person they are looking for. (警方)拉网式搜捕

  • ...a massive police dragnet for two suspected terrorists.

    警方为抓捕两名恐怖嫌疑人而展开的大规模拉网式搜查
