anchorage
æŋkərɪdʒ ˈæŋkərɪdʒ
Word forms: plural anchorages
N-VAR 锚地；泊地 An anchorage is a place where a boat can anchor safely. 锚地；泊地

  • The nearest safe anchorage was in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

    最近的安全泊地在新斯科舍省的哈利法克斯。

  • The vessel yesterday reached anchorage off Dubai.

    船昨天到达了迪拜附近的泊地。

SYN berth, haven, port, harbour
