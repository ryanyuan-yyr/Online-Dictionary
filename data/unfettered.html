unfettered
ʌnfetəʳd ʌnˈfɛtəd
ADJ [ADJ n, verb-link ADJ, ADJ after verb] 自由的；不受拘束的 If you describe something as unfettered, you mean that it is not controlled or limited by anyone or anything. 自由的；不受拘束的 [formal]

  • ...unfettered free trade.

    不受限制的自由贸易

  • Unfettered by the bounds of reality, my imagination flourished. [+ by]

    跳出现实的束缚，我的想象力天马行空。

  • ...city slums, where drug traffickers reign virtually unfettered.

    毒贩横行无忌的城市贫民窟

SYN uncontrolled, free, unlimited, unchecked
