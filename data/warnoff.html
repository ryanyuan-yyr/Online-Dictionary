warn off
PHRASAL VERB 警告(…)离开;告诫(…)停止(做…) If you warn someone off, you tell them to go away or to stop doing something because of possible danger or punishment. 警告(…)离开;告诫(…)停止(做…) [Also VERB PREPOSITION n]

  • The police warned the intruder off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    警察警告闯入者离开。

  • He pressed for a full investigation, but was warned off. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他迫切要求展开全面调查，但是被叫停。

  • He spends his spare time visiting schools to warn pupils off drugs. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    他用自己的业余时间访问学校，告诫小学生远离毒品。
