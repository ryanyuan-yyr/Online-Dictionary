punctuate
pʌŋktʃueɪt ˈpʌŋktʃuˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense punctuates, present participle punctuating, past tense , past participle punctuated
VERB [usually passive] 不时打断 If an activity or situation is punctuated by particular things, it is interrupted by them at intervals. 不时打断 [written]

  • The silence of the night was punctuated by the distant rumble of traffic. [be V-ed + by/with]

    夜晚的宁静不时被远处车辆的隆隆声打断。

SYN interrupt, break, pepper, sprinkle
