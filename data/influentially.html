influential
ɪnfluenʃəl ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl
ADJ-GRADED 有影响力的;有权势的 Someone or something that is influential has a lot of influence over people or events. 有影响力的;有权势的

  • It helps to have influential friends.

    有几个位高权重的朋友很有好处。

  • ...the influential position of president of the chamber.

    很有影响力的议院议长之位

  • He had been influential in shaping economic policy. [+ in]

    他在制定经济政策方面起到了影响。

  • ...one of the most influential books ever written.

    最有影响的书之一

SYN instrumental, important, significant, controlling
