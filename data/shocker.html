shocker
ʃɒkəʳ ˈʃɑkər
Word forms: plural shockers
N-COUNT 耸人听闻的故事(或新闻);恐怖电影 A shocker is something such as a story, a piece of news, or a film that shocks people or that is intended to shock them. 耸人听闻的故事(或新闻);恐怖电影 [informal]

  • Marsha Hunt's second novel, 'Free', is a shocker.

    玛莎·亨特的第二部小说《自由》是一部恐怖小说。
