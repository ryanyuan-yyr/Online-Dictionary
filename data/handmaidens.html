handmaiden
hændmeɪdən ˈhændˌmeɪdən
Word forms: plural handmaidens
1 N-COUNT 侍女;女仆 A handmaiden is a female servant. 侍女;女仆 [literary, old-fashioned]
2 N-COUNT 起服务作用的东西;辅助;支持 If one thing is the handmaiden of another, the first thing helps the second or makes it possible. 起服务作用的东西;辅助;支持 [formal]

  • The fear is that science could become the handmaiden of industry. [+ of/to]

    人们担心的是科学会成为工业的仆人。
