harbinger
hɑːʳbɪndʒəʳ ˈhɑrbɪndʒər
Word forms: plural harbingers
N-COUNT （尤指不祥的）先兆，预兆 Something that is a harbinger of something else, especially something bad, is a sign that it is going to happen. （尤指不祥的）先兆，预兆 [literary]

  • The November air stung my cheeks, a harbinger of winter. [+ of]

    11月的空气刺痛了我的脸颊，预示着冬天就要来临。

SYN sign, indication, herald [literary] , messenger
