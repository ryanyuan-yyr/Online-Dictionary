cantilever
kæntɪliːvəʳ ˈkæntəlˌivər ˈkæntəlˌɛvər
Word forms: plural cantilevers
N-COUNT 悬臂梁;肱梁;托臂 A cantilever is a long piece of metal or wood used in a structure such as a bridge. One end is fastened to something and the other end is used to support part of the structure. 悬臂梁;肱梁;托臂

  • ...the old steel cantilever bridge.
