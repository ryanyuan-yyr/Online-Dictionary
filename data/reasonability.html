reasonable
riːzənəbəl ˈrizənəbəl
1 ADJ-GRADED （人）公平的，明智的，理智的，有判断力的 If you think that someone is fair and sensible you can say that they are reasonable. （人）公平的，明智的，理智的，有判断力的

  • He's a reasonable sort of chap.

    他是个理智的家伙。

  • Oh, come on, be reasonable.

    噢，好了，理智一点。

SYN sensible, reasoned, sound, practical  
reasonably

  • 'I'm sorry, Andrew,' she said reasonably.

    “我很抱歉，安德鲁，”她很懂事地说道。

reasonableness

  • 'I can understand how you feel,' Desmond said with great reasonableness.

    “我能理解你的感受，”德斯蒙德很通情达理地说。

2 ADJ-GRADED （决定或行动）公平的，合理的 If you say that a decision or action is reasonable, you mean that it is fair and sensible. （决定或行动）公平的，合理的

  • ...a perfectly reasonable decision.

    完全合理的决定

  • At the time, what he'd done had seemed reasonable.

    那时他的所作所为似乎很合理。

SYN fair, just, right, acceptable  
3 ADJ-GRADED （期望或解释）合理的，有道理的 If you say that an expectation or explanation is reasonable, you mean that there are good reasons why it may be correct. （期望或解释）合理的，有道理的

  • It seems reasonable to expect rapid urban growth.

    似乎有理由期望城市快速发展。

SYN within reason, fitting, fit, appropriate  
reasonably

  • You can reasonably expect your goods to arrive within six to eight weeks.

    你有理由预期自己的货物会在6到8周内到达。

4 ADJ-GRADED （价钱）合理的，公道的，不太贵的 If you say that the price of something is reasonable, you mean that it is fair and not too high. （价钱）合理的，公道的，不太贵的

  • You get an interesting meal for a reasonable price.

    你花不多的钱就可以吃一顿很特别的饭。

  • His fees were quite reasonable.

    他的收费还挺合理。

SYN low, cheap, competitive, moderate  
reasonably

  • ...reasonably priced accommodation.

    价钱公道的住宿

5 ADJ-GRADED 还算好的;不错的 You can use reasonable to describe something that is fairly good, but not very good. 还算好的;不错的

  • The boy answered him in reasonable French.

    这个男孩用还算流利的法语回答他。

  • He had never been able to make a reasonable living from his writing.

    他一直没能靠写作过上舒适的生活。

SYN average, fair, moderate, modest  
reasonably

  • I can dance reasonably well.

    我跳舞还可以。

6 ADJ-GRADED 较多的；相当大的 A reasonable amount of something is a fairly large amount of it. 较多的；相当大的

  • They will need a reasonable amount of desk area and good light.

    他们将需要一个面积较大的摆放办公桌的地方和充足的照明。

reasonably

  • From now on events moved reasonably quickly.

    从此事情进展得相当快。
