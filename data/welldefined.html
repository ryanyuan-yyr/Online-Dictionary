well-defined
ˈwɛldiˈfaɪnd
(also well defined)
ADJ-GRADED 易于辨认理解的;明确的;定义清楚的 Something that is well-defined is clear and precise and therefore easy to recognize or understand. 易于辨认理解的;明确的;定义清楚的

  • Can you look back and see a well-defined path, or simply an aimless series of job moves with no coherent structure?.

    回顾过去时能看到一条清晰的轨迹，还是只能看到一连串毫无目的、前后不连贯的工作经历？

  • Today's pawnbrokers operate within well-defined financial regulations.

    当今的典当商按照明晰的金融规章运作。
