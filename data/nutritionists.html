nutritionist
njuːtrɪʃənɪst njuːˈtrɪʃənɪst nuː- nuˈtrɪʃənɪst
Word forms: plural nutritionists
N-COUNT 营养学家 A nutritionist is a person whose job is to give advice on what you should eat to remain healthy. 营养学家

  • Nutritionists say only 33% of our calorie intake should be from fat.

    营养学家说我们所摄入的卡路里中只应有33％来自脂肪。
