curdle
kɜːʳdəl ˈkɜrdəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense curdles, present participle curdling, past tense , past participle curdled
V-ERG (使)(牛奶或鸡蛋)凝结；（使）结成凝乳 If milk or eggs curdle or if you curdle them, they separate into different bits. (使)(牛奶或鸡蛋)凝结；（使）结成凝乳

  • The sauce should not boil or the egg yolk will curdle. [VERB]

    调味汁不能煮沸，不然蛋黄会凝结的。

  • The herb has been used for centuries to curdle milk. [VERB noun]

    那种香草几个世纪以来都被用来制作酸乳。

SYN congeal, clot, thicken, condense
