quantum
kwɒntəm ˈkwɑntəm
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 量子的 In physics, quantum theory and quantum mechanics are concerned with the behaviour of atomic particles. 量子的

  • Both quantum mechanics and chaos theory suggest a world constantly in flux.

    量子力学和混沌理论都表明世界永远处于不断变化中。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 巨变;猛增;飞跃；突飞猛进 A quantum leap or quantum jump in something is a very great and sudden increase in its size, amount, or quality. 巨变;猛增;飞跃；突飞猛进

  • The vaccine represents a quantum leap in healthcare.

    这种疫苗的问世是医疗保健领域的重大突破。

  • The scale of migration took a quantum leap in the early 1970s.

    20世纪70年代初，移民的规模骤然扩大。
