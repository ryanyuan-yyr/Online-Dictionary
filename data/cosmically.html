cosmic
kɒzmɪk ˈkɑzmɪk
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 外太空的 Cosmic means occurring in, or coming from, the part of space that lies outside Earth and its atmosphere. 外太空的

  • ...cosmic radiation.

    外太空的辐射

  • ...cosmic debris.

    外太空垃圾

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 宇宙的 Cosmic means belonging or relating to the universe. 宇宙的

  • ...the cosmic laws governing our world.

    支配我们世界的宇宙规律

  • ...humanity's place in the cosmic order of things.

    在宇宙万物的秩序中人类的位置

SYN universal, general, omnipresent, all-embracing
