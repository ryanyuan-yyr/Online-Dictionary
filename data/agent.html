agent
eɪdʒənt ˈeɪdʒənt
Word forms: plural agents
1 N-COUNT 代理人;代理商 An agent is a person who looks after someone else's business affairs or does business on their behalf. 代理人;代理商 [business]

  • You are buying direct, rather than through an agent.

    你这是直接购买，而不是通过代理人。

  • ...a written declaration by someone, authorizing another person to act as his agent.

    一份某人授权他人为其代理人的书面申明

SYN representative, deputy, substitute, advocate  
2See also:estate agent  press agent  travel agent 
3 N-COUNT （艺术领域的）代理人，经纪人 An agent in the arts world is a person who gets work for an actor or musician, or who sells the work of a writer to publishers. （艺术领域的）代理人，经纪人
4 N-COUNT 间谍；特务 An agent is a person who works for a country's secret service. 间谍；特务

  • All these years he's been an agent for the East. [+ for]

    这些年来他一直是为东方国家效力的一位间谍。

SYN spy, operative [US , Canadian] , mole, secret agent  
5 N-COUNT [NOUN of n] 动力;媒介;因素;工具 If you refer to someone or something as the agent of a particular effect, you mean that they cause this effect. 动力;媒介;因素;工具

  • In many societies young men regard themselves as highly active, the agents of change, shapers of the world.

    在许多社会中，年轻人将自己看成是高度活跃的分子，是变革的推动者和世界的塑造者。

SYN author, officer, worker, actor  
6 N-COUNT （化学）剂;作用剂 A chemical that has a particular effect or is used for a particular purpose can be referred to as a particular kind of agent. （化学）剂;作用剂

  • ...the bleaching agent in white flour.

    白面粉中的漂白剂

  • ...fibrinogen, a blood clotting agent.

    血纤维蛋白原，一种凝血剂

SYN force, means, power, cause
