leave off
1 PHRASAL VERB 剔除，排除（在名单之外） If someone or something is left off a list, they are not included on that list. 剔除，排除（在名单之外） [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • She has been deliberately left off the guest list. [be VERB-ed PREP. noun]

    她被故意排除在嘉宾名单之外。

  • The judge left Walsh's name off the list of those he wanted arrested. [VERB noun PREP. noun]

    法官把沃尔什的名字从逮捕名单中拿掉了。

2 PHRASAL VERB 停止，中断（做某事） If someone leaves off doing something, they stop doing it. 停止，中断（做某事）

  • We all left off eating and stood about with bowed heads. [VERB PREP. verb-ing]

    我们都停下不吃了，低着头闲站着。

  • Some of the patients left off treatment. [VERB PREP. noun]

    有些病人停止了治疗。

SYN stop, end, finish, give up
