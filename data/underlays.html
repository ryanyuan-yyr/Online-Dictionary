underlay
ʌndəʳleɪ ʌndəʳleɪ ˌʌndərˈleɪ ˈʌndərˌleɪ
Word forms: plural underlays
The noun is pronounced /'ʌndəleɪ/. The verb is pronounced /ˌʌndə'leɪ/. 名词读作/'ʌndəleɪ/。动词读作/ˌʌndə'leɪ/。
1 N-MASS 地毯衬垫 Underlay is a layer of thick material that you place between a carpet and the floor to protect the carpet and make it feel warmer and softer. 地毯衬垫 [British]
2 Underlay is the past tense of underlie.
