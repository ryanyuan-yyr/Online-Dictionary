bibliography
bɪbliɒgrəfi ˌbɪbliˈɑgrəfi
Word forms: plural bibliographies
1 N-COUNT （有关某一专题的）书目 A bibliography is a list of books on a particular subject. （有关某一专题的）书目

  • At the end of this chapter there is a select bibliography of useful books.

    本章末尾列有一份有用的精选书目。

SYN book list, record, catalogue, list of books  
2 N-COUNT （参考或引证的）文献目录，参考书目 A bibliography is a list of the books and articles that are referred to in a particular book. （参考或引证的）文献目录，参考书目
