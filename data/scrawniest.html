scrawny
skrɔːni ˈskrɔni
Word forms: comparative scrawnier, superlative scrawniest
ADJ-GRADED 骨瘦如柴的;皮包骨头的 If you describe a person or animal as scrawny, you mean that they look unattractive because they are so thin. 骨瘦如柴的;皮包骨头的 [disapproval]

  • ...a scrawny woman with dyed black hair.

    染了黑发、骨瘦如柴的女人

  • The vulture extended his scrawny neck.

    兀鹫伸出细长的脖子。

SYN thin, lean, skinny, angular
