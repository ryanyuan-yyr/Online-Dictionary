de rigueur
də rɪgɜːʳ də riɡœr də rɪˈɡɜː ˌdeɪrɪˈgɜr dərɪˈgɜr
ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 按照时尚所要求的 If you say that a possession or habit is de rigueur, you mean that it is fashionable and therefore necessary for anyone who wants to avoid being considered unfashionable. 按照时尚所要求的

  • T-shirts now seem almost de rigueur in the West End.

    T恤衫现在几乎成了伦敦西区时尚人士的必备之物。

SYN necessary, right, required, fitting
