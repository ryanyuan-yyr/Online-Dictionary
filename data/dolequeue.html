dole queue
dəʊl kjuː
Word forms: plural dole queues
N-COUNT 失业队伍;失业状况;(尤指)失业人数 When people talk about the dole queue, they are talking about the state of being unemployed, especially when saying how many people are unemployed. 失业队伍;失业状况;(尤指)失业人数 [British]

  • Another 29,100 people have joined the dole queue.

    又有29,100人加入了失业大军。

  • ...a backdrop of lengthening dole queues.

    失业人数增加的背景
