sick building syndrome
N-UNCOUNT 病态建筑综合征，大楼病综合征(指因办公楼空气不好而引起的头痛、眼睛疼痛、疲劳等症状) Sick building syndrome is a group of conditions, including headaches, sore eyes, and tiredness, which people who work in offices may experience because the air there is not healthy to breathe. 病态建筑综合征，大楼病综合征(指因办公楼空气不好而引起的头痛、眼睛疼痛、疲劳等症状)

  • Built-up static contributes to sick building syndrome.

    高楼林立中的静电干扰是引发大楼病综合征的原因之一。
