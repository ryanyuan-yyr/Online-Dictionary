antioxidant
æntiɒksɪdənt ˌæntɪˈɒksɪdənt ˌæntaɪˈɑksɪdənt ˌ æntiˈɑksɪdənt
(also anti-oxidant)Word forms: plural antioxidants
N-COUNT 抗氧（化）剂；阻氧化剂；防老（化）剂 An antioxidant is a substance which slows down the damage that can be caused to other substances by the effects of oxygen. Foods which contain antioxidants are thought to be very good for you. 抗氧（化）剂；阻氧化剂；防老（化）剂
