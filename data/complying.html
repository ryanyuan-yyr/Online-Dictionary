comply
kəmplaɪ kəmˈplaɪ
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense complies, present participle complying, past tense , past participle complied
VERB 服从;遵守 If someone or something complies with an order or set of rules, they are in accordance with what is required or expected. 服从;遵守 [Also V to n]

  • The commander said that the army would comply with the ceasefire. [VERB + with]

    指挥官说军队会遵守停火协议。

  • Some beaches had failed to comply with European directives on bathing water. [VERB with noun]

    有些海滩未能遵守欧洲有关浴场的指令。

  • There are calls for his resignation, but there is no sign yet that he will comply. [VERB]

    有人要求他辞职，不过还没有迹象表明他会屈从。

SYN obey, follow, respect, agree to
