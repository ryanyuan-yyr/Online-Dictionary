bad cheque
Word forms: plural bad cheques
in AM, use 美国英语用 bad check
N-COUNT （支票的）废票，错票；空头支票 A bad cheque is a bank cheque that will not be paid because there is a mistake on it, or because there is not enough money in the account of the person who wrote the cheque. （支票的）废票，错票；空头支票

  • She wrote another bad check.

    她又开了一张空头支票。
