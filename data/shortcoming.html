shortcoming
ʃɔːʳtkʌmɪŋ ˈʃɔːtˌkʌmɪŋ ʃɔrtˌkʌmɪŋ
Word forms: plural shortcomings
N-COUNT [usually plural, oft with poss] 缺点;短处 Someone's or something's shortcomings are the faults or weaknesses which they have. 缺点;短处

  • Marriages usually break down as a result of the shortcomings of both partners. [+ of]

    婚姻常因为配偶双方的缺点而破裂。

  • His book has its shortcomings.

    他的书有其自身的缺点。

SYN failing, fault, weakness, defect
