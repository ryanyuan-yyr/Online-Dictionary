intriguing
ɪntriːgɪŋ ɪnˈtrigɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有趣的;引人入胜的;奇怪的 If you describe something as intriguing, you mean that it is interesting or strange. 有趣的;引人入胜的;奇怪的

  • This intriguing book is both thoughtful and informative.

    这本引人入胜的书既有思想性又富知识性。

SYN interesting, fascinating, absorbing, exciting  
intriguingly

  • ...the intriguingly-named newspaper Le Canard Enchainé (The Chained Duck).

    名字新奇的报纸 Le Canard Enchainé (《被链条锁住的鸭子》)
