dine
daɪn daɪn
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense dines, present participle dining, past tense , past participle dined
VERB 进餐;用餐;吃饭 When you dine, you have dinner. to wine and dine→see: wine  进餐;用餐;吃饭

  • He dines alone most nights. [VERB adv./prep.]

    大多数晚上他都是一个人用餐。

  • That night the two men dined at Wilson's club. [VERB adv./prep.]

    那天晚上这两个男人在威尔逊俱乐部进了餐。

  • They used to enjoy going out to dine. [VERB]

    他们以前很喜欢出去吃饭。
