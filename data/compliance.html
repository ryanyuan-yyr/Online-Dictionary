compliance
kəmplaɪəns kəmˈplaɪəns
N-UNCOUNT 遵守;服从;听从 Compliance with something, for example a law, treaty, or agreement means doing what you are required or expected to do. 遵守;服从;听从 [formal]

  • Inspectors were sent to visit nuclear sites and verify compliance with the treaty.

    监督员被派去视察核武器基地并核实条约的遵守情况。

  • The company says it is in full compliance with US labor laws.

    这家公司说自己严格遵守美国的各项劳工法。

  • The Security Council aim to ensure compliance by all sides, once an agreement is signed.

    一旦签署协议，安理会的目标就是保证各方都要遵守。

SYN conformity, agreement, obedience, assent
