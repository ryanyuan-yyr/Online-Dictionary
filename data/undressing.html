undress
ʌndres ʌnˈdrɛs ˈʌnˌdrɛs
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense undresses, present participle undressing, past tense , past participle undressed
1 VERB 脱衣服；脱去…的衣服 When you undress or undress someone, you take off your clothes or someone else's clothes. →see usage note at: wear 脱衣服；脱去…的衣服

  • She went out, leaving Rachel to undress and have her shower.

    她出去了，留下雷切尔一个人除衣淋浴。

  • She undressed the child before putting her in the tin bath.

    她给孩子脱下衣服，然后把她放进锡铁澡盆里。

2 PHRASE 一丝不挂；赤身裸体 If someone is in a state of undress, they do not have all their clothes on. 一丝不挂；赤身裸体

  • Every cover showed a woman in a state of undress.

    每个封面上都有一名裸体女子。
