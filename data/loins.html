loin
lɔɪn lɔɪn
Word forms: plural loins
1 N-PLURAL 耻骨区；私处；（尤指）性器官 Someone's loins are the front part of their body between their waist and legs, especially their sexual parts. 耻骨区；私处；（尤指）性器官 [literary, old-fashioned]
2 N-VAR （动物的）腰肉，脊背肉 Loin or a loin is a piece of meat which comes from the back or sides of an animal, quite near the tail end. （动物的）腰肉，脊背肉

  • Heat the honey and brush it on to the outside of the loin.

    将蜂蜜加热后涂抹在腰子的表面。

  • ...roast loin of venison. [+ of]

    烤鹿腰肉

3 PHRASE [VERB inflects] 准备采取（困难或危险的）行动 If you gird your loins, you prepare to do something difficult or dangerous. 准备采取（困难或危险的）行动 [literary]
