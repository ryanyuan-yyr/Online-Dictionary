hospice
hɒspɪs ˈhɑspɪs
Word forms: plural hospices
N-COUNT; N-IN-NAMES 临终关怀医院;临终安养院 A hospice is a special hospital for people who are dying, where their practical and emotional needs are dealt with as well as their medical needs. 临终关怀医院;临终安养院

  • ...a hospice for cancer patients.

    癌症病人临终安养院

  • He died in March at St Julia's Hospice.

    他于3月在圣朱莉娅临终关怀医院去世。

SYN nursing home, hospital, sanatorium, convalescent home
