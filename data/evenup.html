even up
PHRASAL VERB 使平衡;使势均力敌 To even up a contest or game means to make it more equally balanced than it was. 使平衡;使势均力敌 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • The nation's electronics industry made important strides this year to even up its balance of trade. [VERB PREP. noun]

    该国的电子产业今年取得了重大进步，平衡了贸易差额。

  • I would like to see the championship evened up a little bit more. [VERB-ed PREP.]

    我希望冠军赛更加势均力敌一点。

SYN equalize, match, balance, equal
