recondition
riːkəndɪʃən ˌriːkənˈdɪʃən ˌrikənˈdɪʃən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense reconditions, present participle reconditioning, past tense , past participle reconditioned
VERB 修理;修复;翻新 To recondition a machine or piece of equipment means to repair or replace all the parts that are damaged or broken. 修理;修复;翻新

  • He made contact with someone with an idea for reconditioning laser copiers. [VERB noun]

    他联系了一个会修理激光复印机的人。

  • They sell used and reconditioned motorcycle parts. [VERB-ed]

    他们出售二手的和翻新的摩托车零件。

SYN restore, repair, renew, overhaul
