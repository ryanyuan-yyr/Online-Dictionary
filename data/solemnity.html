solemn
sɒləm ˈsɑləm
1 ADJ-GRADED 严肃的;庄严的 Someone or something that is solemn is very serious rather than cheerful or humorous. 严肃的;庄严的

  • His solemn little face broke into smiles.

    他严肃的小脸蛋上突然绽放出笑容。

  • He looked solemn.

    他看上去很严肃。

SYN serious, earnest, grave, sober  
solemnly

  • Her listeners nodded solemnly.

    听她说话的人神情严肃地点了点头。

solemnity

  • The setting for this morning's signing ceremony matched the solemnity of the occasion.

    今天上午签字仪式的布置与该场合的严肃性非常协调。

2 ADJ-GRADED (承诺、协定等)庄严的，郑重的 A solemn promise or agreement is one that you make in a very formal, sincere way. (承诺、协定等)庄严的，郑重的

  • ...a solemn pledge that he would never remarry.

    他永远不再结婚的郑重承诺

solemnly

  • ... he had undergone an emergency operation for a stomach condition which—the doctors solemnly assured him—was potentially fatal.

    他接受了一次紧急手术，因为他的胃病非常严重，有可能致命—这是医生郑重告知他的。
