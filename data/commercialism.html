commercialism
kəmɜːʳʃəlɪzəm kəˈmɜrʃəlˌɪzəm
N-UNCOUNT 商业主义;营利主义 Commercialism is the practice of making a lot of money from things without caring about their quality. 商业主义;营利主义 [disapproval]

  • Koons has engrossed himself in a world of commercialism that most modern artists disdain.

    昆斯投身于商界，那是一个大部分现代艺术家所不齿的圈子。
