weave
wiːv wiːv wiv
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense weaves, present participle weaving, past tense wove, past participle woven
The form weaved is used for the past tense and past participle for meaning 4. 义项4的过去式和过去分词为 weaved。
1 VERB 织(布);编织 If you weave cloth or a carpet, you make it by crossing threads over and under each other using a frame or machine called a loom. 织(布);编织

  • They would spin and weave cloth, cook and attend to the domestic side of life. [VERB noun]

    她们纺纱、织布、煮饭以及料理家务。

  • In one room, young mothers weave while babies doze in their laps. [VERB]

    在一间屋子里，年轻的母亲们在织布，而她们的孩子就在她们膝上打盹。

woven

  • ...woven cotton fabrics.

    棉织物

weaving

  • When I studied weaving, I became intrigued with natural dyes.

    学习纺织激起了我对天然染料的兴趣。

2 N-COUNT 织法;编织的式样 A particular weave is the way in which the threads are arranged in a cloth or carpet. 织法;编织的式样

  • Fabrics with a close weave are ideal for painting.

    编织紧密的布最适合作画。

3 VERB 编,编制(篮子等) If you weave something such as a basket, you make it by crossing long plant stems or fibres over and under each other. 编,编制(篮子等)

  • Jenny weaves baskets from willow she grows herself. [VERB noun]

    珍妮用她自己种的柳树的枝条编篮子。

woven

  • The floors are covered with woven straw mats.

    地板上铺着编织的草垫。

4 VERB 迂回行进;穿行 If you weave your way somewhere, you move between and around things as you go there. 迂回行进;穿行

  • The cars then weaved in and out of traffic at top speed. [VERB prep.]

    这些轿车就在车流中全速穿行。

  • He weaved around the tables to where she sat with Bob. [VERB prep.]

    他绕过一张张桌子，走到她和鲍勃坐着的地方。

  • He weaves his way through a crowd. [V n + through]

    他在人群中迂回前行。

SYN zigzag, wind, move in and out, crisscross  
5 VERB 编造（故事） If you weave a story, you invent a complicated story. 编造（故事） [written]

  • Jan Roberts weaves a compelling tale which traps a young woman in a world run by the Mafia. [VERB noun]

    简·罗伯茨编了一个引人入胜的故事，诱骗一位年轻女性进入了黑手党的地盘。

SYN create, tell, recount, narrate  
6 VERB 将(素材等)编入（故事或设计中） If you weave details into a story or design, you include them, so that they are closely linked together or become an important part of the story or design. 将(素材等)编入（故事或设计中） [written]

  • She weaves imaginative elements into her poems. [VERB noun + into]

    她在诗中编织着奇思妙想。

  • Bragg weaves together the histories of his main characters. [VERB noun with together]

    布拉格把他主要人物的身世都串在一起。

SYN incorporate, work, include, mix
