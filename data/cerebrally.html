cerebral
serɪbrəl səriːbrəl ˈsɛrəbrəl səˈriːbrəl səˈribrəl
1 ADJ-GRADED 运用智力的;理智的;冷静的 If you describe someone or something as cerebral, you mean that they are intellectual rather than emotional. 运用智力的;理智的;冷静的 [formal]

  • Washington struck me as a precarious place from which to publish such a cerebral newspaper.

    我的感觉是，在华盛顿出版这样一份理性的报纸不是太靠谱。

2 ADJ [ADJ n] 脑的;大脑的 Cerebral means relating to the brain. 脑的;大脑的 [medicine]

  • ...a cerebral haemorrhage.

    脑溢血
