communal
kɒmjʊnəl kəmjuːnəl kəˈmjunəl ˈkɑmjənəl
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 群体的;社区的;团体的 Communal means relating to particular groups in a country or society. 群体的;社区的;团体的

  • Communal violence broke out in different parts of the country.

    在该国不同的地区发生了群体暴力事件。

  • ...inter-communal relations.

    不同群体之间的关系

SYN community, neighbourhood  
2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 公共的;公用的 You use communal to describe something that is shared by a group of people. 公共的;公用的

  • The inmates ate in a communal dining room.

    囚犯们在公共餐厅吃饭。

  • ...communal ownership.

    公共所有

SYN public, shared, general, joint  
communally

  • Meals are taken communally in the dining room.

    大家在餐厅集体用餐。
