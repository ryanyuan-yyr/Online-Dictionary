giver
gɪvəʳ ˈgɪvər
Word forms: plural givers
N-COUNT 给予者；赠与者；提供者 You can refer to a person or organization that gives or supplies a particular thing as a giver of that thing. 给予者；赠与者；提供者

  • Germany is the largest giver of aid among the wealthy countries of the West.

    德国是西方富裕国家中给予援助最多的国家。

  • ...a complex social ritual that says a lot about both the giver and the receiver.

    关于捐赠者与受援者的复杂的社会礼仪

SYN donor, provider, contributor, benefactor  
Giver is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...if the money-givers do not have specific projects in view.

    如果捐款者心中没有具体的方案
