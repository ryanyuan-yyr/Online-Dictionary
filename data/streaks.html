streak
striːk strik
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense streaks, present participle streaking, past tense , past participle streaked
1 N-COUNT 条纹;条痕 A streak is a long stripe or mark on a surface which contrasts with the surface because it is a different colour. 条纹;条痕

  • There are these dark streaks on the surface of the moon.

    月球表面有这样一些黑色的条痕。

  • The flames begin as a few streaks of red against the pale brown of the walls.

    火焰燃起时，犹如几条红色条纹映衬在淡褐色的墙上。

SYN band, line, strip, stroke  
2 VERB 在…上留下条纹(或条痕);使布满条纹(或条痕) If something streaks a surface, it makes long stripes or marks on the surface. 在…上留下条纹(或条痕);使布满条纹(或条痕)

  • Rain had begun to streak the windowpanes.

    雨水已经开始在窗玻璃上冲出道道条痕。

  • Fine shades of grey streaked his dark hair. [VERB noun]

    他的黑发间有几丝白发。

  • His face was pale and streaked with dirt. [be VERB-ed + with]

    他脸色苍白，脸上有一道道的污痕。

SYN fleck, smear, daub, band  
-streaked

  • Her bare feet were dirt-streaked and cracked with cold.

    她赤裸的双脚上满是一道道的泥污，都冻得开裂了。

  • ...soot-streaked silver trains.

    车身上满是道道煤烟灰痕的银色火车

3 N-COUNT [usually singular] 性格特征;特点 If someone has a streak of a particular type of behaviour, they sometimes behave in that way. 性格特征;特点

  • We're both alike—there is a streak of madness in us both.

    我们俩很像—都有点疯。

  • He's still got a mean streak.

    他这人还是有点损。

SYN trace, touch, element, strain  
4 VERB 飞奔;疾驰 If something or someone streaks somewhere, they move there very quickly. 飞奔;疾驰

  • A meteorite streaked across the sky. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一颗流星划过天空。

  • He got a near perfect start, streaking away from the pack. [VERB prep./adv.]

    他的起跑近乎完美，迅速从那群人中蹿了出去。

SYN speed, fly, tear, sweep  
5 N-COUNT [adjective NOUN] (赌博或体育比赛等中的)运气，手气 A winning streak or a lucky streak is a continuous series of successes, for example in gambling or sport. A losing streak or an unlucky streak is a series of failures or losses. (赌博或体育比赛等中的)运气，手气

  • The casinos had better watch out since I'm obviously on a lucky streak!

    很明显我现在手气正旺，赌场最好当心了！

  • ...a losing streak that had extended back to June 1.

    从 6 月 1 日开始就频频失利
