sit around
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 sit about
PHRASAL VERB 闲坐着;无所事事 If you sit around or sit about, you spend time doing nothing useful or interesting. 闲坐着;无所事事 [informal]

  • Eve isn't the type to sit around doing nothing. [VERB PREP.]

    伊芙不是那种喜欢坐在那里无所事事的人。

  • We sat about in the gloomy airport lounge. [VERB PREP.]

    我们百无聊赖地坐在阴暗的候机大厅里。
