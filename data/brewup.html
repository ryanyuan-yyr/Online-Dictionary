brew up
1 PHRASAL VERB 泡茶；沏茶 If someone brews up or if they brew up some tea, they make tea by pouring hot water over tea leaves. 泡茶；沏茶 [British, informal] [Also VERB PREPOSITION n (not pron)]

  • Brew up, Curly. We could all do with a cup of tea. [VERB PREP.]

    沏茶，柯利，我们每人都要喝一杯。

2 PHRASAL VERB-ERG [usually cont] 策划；酝酿 If someone is brewing up an unpleasant situation or if an unpleasant situation is brewing up, it is starting to develop. 策划；酝酿

  • I realized the extent of the trouble that Mary Morse was brewing up. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    我意识到了玛丽·莫尔斯正在制造的麻烦有多大。

  • There's another security scandal brewing up. [VERB PREP.]

    另一桩安全丑闻已渐露端倪。
