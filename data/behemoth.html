behemoth
bɪhiːmɒθ -məθ bəˈhiməθ ˈbiəməθ
Word forms: plural behemoths
N-COUNT 庞然大物；巨兽 If you refer to something as a behemoth, you mean that it is extremely large, and often that it is unpleasant, inefficient, or difficult to manage. 庞然大物；巨兽 [journalism, literary, disapproval]

  • The city is a sprawling behemoth with no heart.

    这座城市无计划地向外扩展着，成为一个中心缺失的庞然大物。

  • ...his behemoth 1,047 page book.

    他厚达1,047页的皇皇巨著

SYN giant, monster, mammoth, titan
