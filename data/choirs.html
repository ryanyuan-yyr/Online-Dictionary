choir
kwaɪəʳ kwaɪə kwaɪr
Word forms: plural choirs
1 N-COUNT (教堂的)唱诗班;(学校的)合唱队，合唱团 A choir is a group of people who sing together, for example in a church or school. (教堂的)唱诗班;(学校的)合唱队，合唱团

  • He has been singing in his church choir since he was six.

    他打6岁起一直在教堂唱诗班里唱歌。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] (教堂里的)唱诗班席 In a church building, the choir is the area in front of the altar where the choir sits. (教堂里的)唱诗班席
