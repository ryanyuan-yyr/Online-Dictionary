bloodthirsty
blʌdθɜːʳsti ˈblʌdˌθɜrsti
ADJ-GRADED 嗜杀成性的；耽于暴力的；残忍的；血腥的 Bloodthirsty people are eager to use violence or display a strong interest in violent things. You can also use bloodthirsty to refer to very violent situations. 嗜杀成性的；耽于暴力的；残忍的；血腥的

  • They were savage and bloodthirsty.

    他们野蛮残暴。

  • ...some of the most tragic scenes witnessed even in this bloodthirsty war.

    在这场血腥的战争中一些最令人惨不忍睹的场景

SYN cruel, savage, brutal, vicious
