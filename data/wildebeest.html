wildebeest
wɪldɪbiːst vɪl- ˈwɪldɪˌbiːst ˈwɪldəˌbist ˈvɪldəˌbist
Word forms: plural wildebeest
N-COUNT 牛羚，角马(一种大型非洲羚羊) A wildebeest is a large African antelope which has a hairy tail, short curved horns, and long hair under its neck. Wildebeest usually live in large groups. 牛羚，角马(一种大型非洲羚羊)
