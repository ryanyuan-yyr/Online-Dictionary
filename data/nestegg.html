nest egg
(also nest-egg)Word forms: plural nest eggs
N-COUNT [usually singular] 储备金;存款 A nest egg is a sum of money that you are saving for a particular purpose. 储备金;存款 [informal]

  • They have a little nest egg tucked away somewhere for a rainy day.

    他们把一小笔钱存在了什么地方，以备不时之需。

SYN savings, fund(s), store, reserve
