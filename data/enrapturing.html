enrapture
ɪnræptʃəʳ ɛnˈræptʃər ɪnˈræptʃər
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense enraptures, present participle enrapturing, past tense , past participle enraptured
VERB 使着迷;使陶醉 If something or someone enraptures you, you think they are wonderful or fascinating. 使着迷;使陶醉 [literary]

  • The place at once enraptured me. [VERB noun]

    那个地方一下子把我迷住了。

  • The 20,000-strong audience listened, enraptured. [VERB-ed]

    两万多名观众听得如痴如醉。

  • ...an enraptured audience. [VERB-ed]

    陶醉的观众
