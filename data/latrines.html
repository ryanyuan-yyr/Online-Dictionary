latrine
lətriːn ləˈtrin
Word forms: plural latrines
N-COUNT （营地等处的）茅厕，便坑 A latrine is a structure, usually consisting of a hole in the ground, that is used as a toilet, for example in a military camp. （营地等处的）茅厕，便坑

  • There were no proper toilets, but only an outdoor latrine.

    没有正规的厕所，只有一个位于室外的茅坑。

  • They made me clean out the latrines.

    他们逼我打扫茅厕。
