establishment
ɪstæblɪʃmənt ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt ɛˈstæblɪʃmənt ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt
Word forms: plural establishments
1 N-SING 成立;建立;创立 The establishment of an organization or system is the act of creating it or beginning it. 成立;建立;创立 [formal]

  • His ideas influenced the establishment of National Portrait Galleries in London and Edinburgh. [+ of]

    他的观念影响了伦敦和爱丁堡两所国家肖像艺术馆的建立。

  • ...discussions to explore the establishment of diplomatic relations.

    旨在探究如何建立外交关系的讨论

SYN creation, founding, setting up, foundation  
2 N-COUNT 机构;团体;企业 An establishment is a shop, business, or organization occupying a particular building or place. 机构;团体;企业 [formal]

  • ...a scientific research establishment.

    科研机构

  • ...shops and other commercial establishments.

    商店和其他商业单位

SYN office, house, building, plant  
3 N-SING 权威；当权者；权势集团 You refer to the people who have power and influence in the running of a country, society, or organization as the establishment . 权威；当权者；权势集团

  • Shopkeepers would once have been pillars of the Tory establishment.

    商店店主一度本该成为保守党统治集团的重要支柱。

  • What do you expect? This is the Establishment we're taking on.

    你还期望什么？我们要应对的是“权威”。

  • ...a hit-list of prominent British establishment figures.

    英国显要权贵的暗杀名单
