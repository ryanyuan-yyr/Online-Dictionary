itchy
ɪtʃi ˈɪtʃi
1 ADJ-GRADED 发痒的;令人感到瘙痒的 If a part of your body or something you are wearing is itchy, you have an unpleasant feeling on your skin that makes you want to scratch. 发痒的;令人感到瘙痒的 [informal]

  • ...itchy, sore eyes.

    又痒又痛的眼睛

  • Wigs are itchy and uncomfortable.

    假发让人感觉发痒,很不舒服。

SYN impatient, eager, restless, unsettled  
2 PHRASE 漫游的渴望;旅行的渴望 If you have itchy feet, you have a strong desire to leave a place and to travel. 漫游的渴望;旅行的渴望 [informal]

  • The trip gave me itchy feet and I wanted to travel more.

    这次旅行让我渴望出游,想去更多的地方。
