necessitate
nɪsesɪteɪt nəˈsɛsəˌteɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense necessitates, present participle necessitating, past tense , past participle necessitated
VERB 使成为必需;需要 If something necessitates an event, action, or situation, it makes it necessary. 使成为必需;需要 [formal]

  • A prolonged drought had necessitated the introduction of water rationing. [VERB noun/verb-ing]

    由于持续干旱，用水需要实行配给了。

  • Frank was carrying out fuel-system tests which necessitated turning the booster pumps off. [VERB noun/-ing]

    弗兰克在做燃料系统测试，这一测试需要关掉升压泵。

SYN compel, force, involve, demand
