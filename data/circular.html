circular
sɜːʳkjʊləʳ ˈsɜrkjələr
Word forms: plural circulars
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 圆的;圆形的 Something that is circular is shaped like a circle. 圆的;圆形的

  • ...a circular hole twelve feet wide and two feet deep.

    12英尺宽、2英尺深的圆坑

  • Place your hands on your shoulders and move your elbows up, back, and down, in a circular motion.

    双手置于肩上，肘部向上、向后、向下做圆圈运动。

2See also:semi-circular 
3 ADJ [usually ADJ n] (旅程或路线)环行的，巡回的 A circular journey or route is one in which you go to a place and return by a different route. (旅程或路线)环行的，巡回的

  • Both sides of the river can be explored on this circular walk.

    沿着这条环行线路走一圈，河的两边都可以看到。

SYN circuitous, cyclical, orbital  
4 ADJ-GRADED (论证或理论)循环论证的 A circular argument or theory is not valid because it uses a statement to prove something which is then used to prove the statement. (论证或理论)循环论证的
5 N-COUNT (同时发送给很多人的)广告印刷品，通告 A circular is an official letter or advertisement that is sent to a large number of people at the same time. (同时发送给很多人的)广告印刷品，通告

  • The proposal has been widely publicised in BBC-TV press information circulars sent to 1,800 newspapers.

    在英国广播公司电视台发送给1,800家报社的新闻通报中，这项提议得到了广泛的宣传。

SYN advertisement, notice, ad [informal] , announcement
