admiration
ædmɪreɪʃən ˌædməˈreɪʃən
N-UNCOUNT [in NOUN] 钦佩；羡慕；赞赏 Admiration is a feeling of great liking and respect for a person or thing. 钦佩；羡慕；赞赏 [Also + of]

  • I have always had the greatest admiration for him. [+ for]

    我始终对他最为钦佩。

  • Meg's eyes widened in admiration.

    梅格羡慕地睁大了双眼。

SYN regard, surprise, wonder, respect
