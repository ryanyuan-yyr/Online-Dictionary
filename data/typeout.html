type out
PHRASAL VERB (用打字机或文字处理器)把…完全打出来 If you type something out, you write it in full using a typewriter or word processor. (用打字机或文字处理器)把…完全打出来

  • The two of us stood by while two typists typed out the whole document again. [VERB PREP. noun]

    当两个打字员重新打出文件全文的时候，我们俩就站在旁边。

  • I read it down the phone to a man called Dave, who typed it out. [VERB noun PREP.]

    我在电话里把它从头到尾地读给一个叫戴夫的人，他则把它全部打出来。
