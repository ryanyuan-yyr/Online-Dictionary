cremate
krɪmeɪt kriːmeɪt ˈkriˌmeɪt krɪˈmeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense cremates, present participle cremating, past tense , past participle cremated
VERB [usually passive] 焚烧，火化(尸体) When someone is cremated, their dead body is burned, usually as part of a funeral service. 焚烧，火化(尸体)

  • She wants Chris to be cremated. [be VERB-ed]

    她想把克里斯的尸体火化。
