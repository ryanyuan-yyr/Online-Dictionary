conqueror
kɒŋkərəʳ
Word forms: plural conquerors
N-COUNT [usually plural] 征服者The conquerors of a country or group of people are the people who have taken complete control of that country or group's land. 征服者

  • The people of an oppressed country obey their conquerors because they want to go on living.

    一个被压迫国家的人民顺从他们的征服者，是因为他们想活下去。
