panache
pənæʃ pəˈnæʃ pəˈnɑʃ
N-UNCOUNT 潇洒；派头；神气十足 If you do something with panache, you do it in a confident, stylish, and elegant way. 潇洒；派头；神气十足

  • The BBC Symphony Orchestra played with great panache.

    英国广播公司交响乐团演奏起来挥洒自如。

  • Her panache at dealing with the world's media is quite astonishing.

    她应付世界媒体的派头非常令人吃惊。

SYN style, spirit, dash, flair [informal]
