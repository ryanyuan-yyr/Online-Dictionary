drainage
dreɪnɪdʒ ˈdreɪnɪdʒ
N-UNCOUNT 排水系统;排水;放水 Drainage is the system or process by which water or other liquids are drained from a place. 排水系统;排水;放水

  • Line the pots with pebbles to ensure good drainage.

    在罐子里铺一层鹅卵石，以确保排水良好。

  • The drainage system has collapsed because of too much rain.

    由于降雨过多，排水系统已经彻底瘫痪了。

SYN sewerage, waste, sewage
