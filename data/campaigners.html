campaigner
kæmpeɪnəʳ
Word forms: plural campaigners
N-COUNT (为争取社会、政治变革而四处奔走的)活动家，运动参加者 A campaigner is a person who campaigns for social or political change. (为争取社会、政治变革而四处奔走的)活动家，运动参加者 [Also + for/against]

  • ...anti-hunting campaigners.

    反狩猎的活动家们

  • ...campaigners for multi-party democracy.

    主张实行多党制的活动家们

SYN demonstrator, champion, advocate, activist
