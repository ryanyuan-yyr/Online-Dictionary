nitwit
nɪtwɪt ˈnɪtˌwɪt
Word forms: plural nitwits
N-COUNT 笨蛋;蠢人 If you refer to someone as a nitwit, you think they are stupid or silly. 笨蛋;蠢人 [informal, disapproval]

  • You great nitwit!

    你这大笨蛋！

  • Hollywood was full of 'nitwits'.

    好莱坞到处都是“蠢人”。

SYN fool, dummy [slang] , plank [British , slang] , geek [slang]
