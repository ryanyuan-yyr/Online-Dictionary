prime time
(also primetime)
N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 黄金时段；收视（或收听）高峰时间 Prime time television or radio programmes are broadcast when the greatest number of people are watching television or listening to the radio, usually in the evenings. 黄金时段；收视（或收听）高峰时间

    黄金时段的电视节目

  • ...prime time viewing in mid-evening.

    晚间高峰时段的收视率
