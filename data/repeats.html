repeat
rɪpiːt rɪˈpit ˈriˌpit
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense repeats, present participle repeating, past tense , past participle repeated
1 VERB 重复;重写 If you repeat something, you say or write it again. You can say I repeat to show that you feel strongly about what you are repeating. 重复;重写

  • He repeated that he had been misquoted.

    他反复说他的话被错误引用了。

  • I repeat that medicine is on the brink of a revolution. [VERB that]

    我要再次强调，医学面临着变革。

  • The Libyan leader Colonel Gadaffi repeated his call for the release of hostages. [VERB noun]

    利比亚领导人卡扎菲上校反复呼吁释放人质。

  • 'You fool,' she kept repeating. [VERB with quote]

    “你这个傻瓜，”她不断地重复着。

SYN reiterate [formal] , restate, say again, recapitulate  
2 VERB 复述;转述 If you repeat something that someone else has said or written, you say or write the same thing, or tell it to another person. 复述;转述

  • She had an irritating habit of repeating everything I said to her. [VERB noun]

    她有个惹人讨厌的习惯，我跟她说的每一件事她都告诉别人。

  • I trust you not to repeat that to anyone else. [VERB noun + to]

    我相信你不会把我的话告诉别人。

  • Now, brother, repeat after me, 'All praise to Allah, Lord of All the Worlds'. [V + after]

    现在，我的兄弟，跟着我说，“至上伟大的真主，全世界的主宰。”

3 VERB 反复说;说话反反复复 If you repeat yourself, you say something which you have said before, usually by mistake. 反复说;说话反反复复

  • Then he started rambling and repeating himself. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    他接下来就开始不着边际、说话来回重复了。

4 CONVENTION 再说一次;重申一遍 People sometimes say repeat before saying again a word they have just said, in order to emphasize it or to make sure that people hear it. 再说一次;重申一遍 [formal, emphasis]

  • We are not, I repeat, not actually in the negotiating process.

    我们不是，我重申一下，实际上不是在进行谈判。

  • Find and destroy, repeat destroy, these units.

    找到并且销毁，重复一下，销毁这些部件。

5 VERB 重复做(动作) If you repeat an action, you do it again. 重复做(动作)

  • The next day I repeated the procedure. [VERB noun]

    第二天我重复了这个程序。

  • He said Japan would never repeat its mistakes. [VERB noun]

    他说日本永远不会再犯同样的错误。

  • Hold this position for 30 seconds, release and repeat on the other side. [VERB]

    保持这个姿势30秒钟，放松，然后换另一侧重复。

6 VERB 重复发生;再次发生 If an event or series of events repeats itself, it happens again. 重复发生;再次发生 [Also VERB]

  • The UN will have to work hard to stop history repeating itself.

    联合国必须努力避免历史重演。

  • The cycle then repeats itself. [VERB pronoun-reflexive]

    这个循环周而复始。

7 N-COUNT [usually singular] (通常指令人不快的事件的)再次发生 If there is a repeat of an event, usually an undesirable event, it happens again. (通常指令人不快的事件的)再次发生

  • There were fears that there might be a repeat of last year's campaign of strikes. [+ of]

    有人担心去年的罢工运动可能再次重演。

  • ...in order to prevent a repeat tragedy.

    为了防止悲剧的再次发生

8 ADJ [ADJ n] 回头客的;再次光顾的 If a company gets repeat business or repeat customers, people who have bought their goods or services before buy them again. 回头客的;再次光顾的 [business]

  • Nearly 60% of our bookings come from repeat business and personal recommendation.

    我们近60%的订单来自于回头客和熟人介绍。

9 N-COUNT (电视或广播节目的)重播 A repeat is a television or radio programme that has been broadcast before. (电视或广播节目的)重播 [British]

  • There's nothing except sport and repeats on TV.

    除了体育节目和重播节目外，电视上没什么新鲜内容。

SYN rerun, replay, reproduction, reshowing  
in AM, use 美国英语用 re-run
10 PHRASE （通常指令人不快的事件的）再次出现，再次发生 If there is a repeat performance of something, usually something undesirable, it happens again. （通常指令人不快的事件的）再次出现，再次发生

  • This year can only see a repeat performance of the decline.

    今年还会出现下降。
