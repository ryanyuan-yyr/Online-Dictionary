parrot-fashion
(also parrot fashion)
ADV [ADVERB after verb] 鹦鹉学舌般地；亦步亦趋地 If you learn or repeat something parrot-fashion, you do it accurately but without really understanding what it means. 鹦鹉学舌般地；亦步亦趋地 [British]

  • Under the old system pupils often had to stand to attention and repeat lessons parrot fashion.

    在旧体制下，学生常常得立正站好，机械地重复课文。

SYN by rote, automatically, without thinking, mechanically
