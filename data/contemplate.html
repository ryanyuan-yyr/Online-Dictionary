contemplate
kɒntəmpleɪt ˈkɑntəmˌpleɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense contemplates, present participle contemplating, past tense , past participle contemplated
1 VERB 考虑;思量;思忖 If you contemplate an action, you think about whether to do it or not. 考虑;思量;思忖

  • For a time he contemplated a career as an army medical doctor. [VERB noun]

    有段时间，他考虑去当军医。

  • She contemplates leaving for the sake of the kids. [VERB verb-ing]

    她考虑为了孩子而离开。

SYN consider, plan, think of, propose  
2 VERB 深思熟虑;沉思;苦思冥想 If you contemplate an idea or subject, you think about it carefully for a long time. 深思熟虑;沉思;苦思冥想

  • As he lay in his hospital bed that night, he cried as he contemplated his future. [VERB noun]

    那天晚上，他躺在医院的病床上，想到未来，他哭了起来。

  • That makes it difficult to contemplate the idea that the present policy may not be sustainable. [VERB noun]

    那让人难以认真思考这个可能，即目前的政策也许并不能持续下去。

SYN think about, consider, ponder, mull over  
contemplation

  • It is a place of quiet contemplation.

    这是个静心冥想的好地方。

3 VERB 凝视;注视 If you contemplate something or someone, you look at them for a long time. 凝视;注视

  • He contemplated his hands, still frowning. [VERB noun]

    他盯着自己的双手，眉头紧锁。

SYN look at, examine, observe, check out [informal]  
contemplation

  • He was lost in the contemplation of the landscape for a while.

    有一会儿，他怔怔地注视着周围的景物。
