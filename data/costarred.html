co-star
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense co-stars, present participle co-starring, past tense , past participle co-starred
in AM, also use 美国英语亦用 costar
1 N-COUNT [usually poss NOUN] (电影中的)联袂主演明星 An actor's or actress's co-stars are the other actors or actresses who also have one of the main parts in a particular film. (电影中的)联袂主演明星

  • During the filming, Curtis fell in love with his co-star, Christine Kaufmann.

    在电影摄制过程中，柯蒂斯爱上了同他联袂主演的明星克里斯汀·考夫曼。

2 V-RECIP 联袂主演 If an actor or actress co-stars with another actor or actress, the two of them have the main parts in a particular film. 联袂主演

  • This fall she co-stars in a film with the acclaimed British actor Kenneth Branagh. [VERB + with]

    今秋她和广受欢迎的英国演员肯尼思·布拉纳联袂主演了一部电影。

  • Wright and Penn met when they co-starred in the movie 'State of Grace'. [VERB + in]

    赖特和佩恩在联袂主演电影《魔鬼警长地狱镇》的时候相识。

  • Cosby had originally selected her to co-star in his movie 'Leonard Part 6'. [VERB + in]

    科斯比最初选中了她来联袂主演他的电影《天才老爹拯救地球》。

3 VERB 由…联袂主演 If a film co-stars particular actors, they have the main parts in it. 由…联袂主演

  • Produced by Oliver Stone, 'Wild Palms' co-stars Dana Delaney, Jim Belushi and Angie Dickinson. [VERB noun]

    《野棕榈》由奥利弗·斯通制片，达娜·德莱尼、吉姆·贝鲁西和安吉·迪金森联袂主演。
