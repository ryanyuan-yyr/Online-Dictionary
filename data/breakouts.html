breakout
breɪkaʊt ˈbreɪkˌaʊt ˈbreɪkˌaʊt
(also break-out)Word forms: plural breakouts
N-COUNT 越狱；逃亡 If there has been a break-out, someone has escaped from prison. 越狱；逃亡

  • A prisoner escaped one day after he was recaptured following a previous break-out.

    一名囚犯在前次越狱被抓回一天后再次逃脱。

  • High Point prison had the highest number of breakouts of any jail in Britain.

    海波因特监狱在英国各监狱中越狱事件最多。
