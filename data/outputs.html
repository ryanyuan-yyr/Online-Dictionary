output
aʊtpʊt ˈaʊtˌpʊt
Word forms: plural outputs
1 N-VAR 产量；输出量；排出量 Output is used to refer to the amount of something that a person or thing produces. 产量；输出量；排出量

  • Manual workers need a good breakfast for high-energy output.

    体力劳动者身体消耗巨大，早餐要丰盛一些。

  • Government statistics show the largest drop in industrial output for ten years.

    政府统计数据显示这是10年来工业产量最大幅度的滑坡。

SYN production, manufacture, manufacturing, yield  
2 N-VAR （计算机、文字处理器等的）输出信息，输出文件 The output of a computer or word processor is the information that it displays on a screen or prints on paper as a result of a particular program. （计算机、文字处理器等的）输出信息，输出文件

  • You run the software, you look at the output, you make modifications.

    运行该软件，查看输出结果并进行修正。
