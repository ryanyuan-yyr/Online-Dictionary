mesh
meʃ mɛʃ
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense meshes, present participle meshing, past tense , past participle meshed
1 N-VAR 网眼织物;网状物 Mesh is material like a net made from wire, thread, or plastic. 网眼织物;网状物

  • The ground-floor windows are obscured by wire mesh.

    一楼的窗户装有防盗网。

  • ...a mesh small enough to exclude tiny insects.

    网眼小得足以挡住小昆虫的网

SYN net, netting, network, web  
2 V-RECIP-ERG (使)相匹配;(使)相互协调;(使)吻合 If two things or ideas mesh or are meshed, they go together well or fit together closely. (使)相匹配;(使)相互协调;(使)吻合 [Also VERB n + with]

  • Their senses of humor meshed perfectly. [VERB]

    他们的幽默感配合得天衣无缝。

  • This of course meshes with the economic philosophy of those on the right. [VERB + with]

    这自然与右派的经济哲学相吻合。

  • Meshing the research and marketing operations will be Mr. Furlaud's job. [V n-pl]

    把研究与市场运作紧密结合起来将成为弗劳德先生的工作任务。

SYN engage, combine, connect, knit
