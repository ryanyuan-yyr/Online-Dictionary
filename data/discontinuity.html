discontinuity
dɪskɒntɪnjuːɪti -nuː- ˌdɪskɑntəˈnuəti dɪskɑntəˈnjuəti
Word forms: plural discontinuities
N-VAR 不连贯;间断性;不连续性 Discontinuity in a process is a lack of smooth or continuous development. 不连贯;间断性;不连续性 [formal]

  • The text is good in parts, but suffers from discontinuity.

    文章分开看还好，但是问题在于不连贯。

  • There may appear to be discontinuities between broadcasts.

    广播节目中可能会出现断音。

SYN lack of unity, disconnection, incoherence, disunion
