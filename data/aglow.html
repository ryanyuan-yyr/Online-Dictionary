aglow
əgloʊ əˈgloʊ
1 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] 发柔光的;光照融融的 If something is aglow, it is shining and bright with a soft, warm light. 发柔光的;光照融融的 [literary]

  • The night skies will be aglow with fireworks. [+ with]

    焰火将点亮夜空。

2 ADJ [verb-link ADJ] （因兴奋而）红光满面的，面色发红的 If someone is aglow or if their face is aglow, they look excited. （因兴奋而）红光满面的，面色发红的 [literary] [Also + with]

  • 'It was incredible,' Kurt says, suddenly aglow.

    “简直难以置信，”库尔特突然满面红光地说。
