lollop
lɒləp ˈlɑləp
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense lollops, present participle lolloping, past tense , past participle lolloped
VERB 缓慢而蹒跚地跑 When an animal or a person lollops along, they run along awkwardly and not very fast. 缓慢而蹒跚地跑 [mainly British, literary]

  • A herd of elephants lolloped across the plains towards a watering hole. [VERB prep./adv.]

    一群大象缓慢而笨拙地穿过平原，朝水潭走去。
