gabardine
gæbəʳdiːn ˈɡæbəˌdiːn -diːn ˈgæbərˌdin ˌ gæbərˈdin
Word forms: plural gabardines
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 gaberdine
1 N-UNCOUNT [oft NOUN n] 华达呢，轧别丁（用于制作外套等的厚布料） Gabardine is a fairly thick cloth which is used for making coats, suits, and other clothes. 华达呢，轧别丁（用于制作外套等的厚布料）

  • ...a demure white blouse and long, straight gabardine skirt.

    庄重的白色衬衣和笔挺的华达呢直筒长裙

2 N-COUNT 华达呢外套；华达呢大衣 A gabardine is a coat made from gabardine. 华达呢外套；华达呢大衣
