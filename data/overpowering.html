overpowering
oʊvəʳpaʊərɪŋ
1 ADJ-GRADED （情感等）强烈得令人难以承受的，不可抗拒的 An overpowering feeling is so strong that you cannot resist it. （情感等）强烈得令人难以承受的，不可抗拒的

  • ...hard, cold, overpowering anger.

    冷酷无情、难以遏制的愤怒

  • The desire for revenge can be overpowering.

    报复的欲望有时会压倒一切。

SYN overwhelming, powerful, extreme, compelling  
2 ADJ-GRADED （气味、声音等）盖过一切的，过于强烈的 An overpowering smell or sound is so strong that you cannot smell or hear anything else. （气味、声音等）盖过一切的，过于强烈的

  • There was an overpowering smell of alcohol.

    有一股刺鼻的酒味。

  • The noise was overpowering.

    噪音吵得人什么都听不清。

SYN strong, marked, powerful, distinct  
3 ADJ-GRADED （人）个性极强的，专横的 An overpowering person makes other people feel uncomfortable because they have such a strong personality. （人）个性极强的，专横的

  • Mrs Winter was large and somewhat overpowering.

    温特夫人块头很大，个性也较强悍。

  • ...an overpowering manner.

    态度专横
