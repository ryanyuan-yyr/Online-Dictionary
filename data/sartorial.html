sartorial
sɑːʳtɔːriəl sɑːˈtɔːrɪən sɑrˈtɔriəl
ADJ [ADJ n] 服装的;裁缝的;缝纫的;衣着的 Sartorial means relating to clothes and to the way they are made or worn. 服装的;裁缝的;缝纫的;衣着的 [formal]

  • ...Sebastian's sartorial elegance.

    塞巴斯蒂安精致考究的穿着

  • James gave him some sartorial advice.

    詹姆斯给了他一些穿着方面的建议。

sartorially

  • He was sartorially impeccable.

    他的着装无可挑剔。
