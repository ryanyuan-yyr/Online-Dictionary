putt
pʌt pʌt
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense putts, present participle putting, past tense , past participle putted
1 N-COUNT （高尔夫球中的）轻击，转击入穴，推球入洞 A putt is a stroke in golf that you make when the ball has reached the green in an attempt to get the ball in the hole. （高尔夫球中的）轻击，转击入穴，推球入洞

  • ...a 5-foot putt.

    五英尺远的轻击

2 VERB （高尔夫球中）轻击（球），转击（球）入穴，推（球）入洞 In golf, when you putt the ball, you hit a putt. （高尔夫球中）轻击（球），转击（球）入穴，推（球）入洞

  • Turner, however, putted superbly, twice holing from 40 feet. [VERB]

    然而特纳两次从40英尺处漂亮地将球轻击入洞。
