pittance
pɪtəns ˈpɪtəns
Word forms: plural pittances
N-COUNT [usually singular] 少量报酬；微薄工资 If you say that you receive a pittance, you are emphasizing that you get only a very small amount of money, probably not as much as you think you deserve. 少量报酬；微薄工资 [emphasis]

  • Her secretaries work tirelessly for a pittance.

    她的秘书们为一点微薄的工资不知疲倦地工作。

SYN peanuts [slang] , trifle, modicum, drop
