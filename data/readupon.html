read up on
PHRASAL VERB 熟知;专攻;对…了如指掌 If you read up on a subject, you read a lot about it so that you become informed about it. 熟知;专攻;对…了如指掌

  • I've read up on the dangers of all these drugs. [VERB PREP. PREP. noun]

    我对所有这些药物的危险性了如指掌。

SYN study, learn, bone up on [informal] , swot up on [British , informal]
