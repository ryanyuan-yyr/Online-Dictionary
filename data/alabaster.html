alabaster
æləbɑːstəʳ -bæs- ˈæləˌbæstər
1 N-UNCOUNT [usually NOUN n] 雪花石膏（用于雕塑及装饰） Alabaster is a white stone that is used for making statues, vases, and ornaments. 雪花石膏（用于雕塑及装饰）

  • ...four carved alabaster figures of maidens.

    四座雪花石膏雕刻的少女像

2 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 白净光滑的 If you say that someone has alabaster skin, you mean that their skin is very beautiful because it is so white and smooth. 白净光滑的 [literary]

  • She wore a fine chain about her alabaster neck.

    她细腻光滑、美丽白皙的脖子上戴着一条精致的链子。
