pathway
pɑːθweɪ pæθ- ˈpæθˌweɪ
Word forms: plural pathways
1 N-COUNT 小路；路径 A pathway is a path which you can walk along or a route which you can take. 小路；路径

  • Richard was coming up the pathway.

    理查德正沿着小路走过来。

  • ...a pathway leading towards the nearby river.

    通往附近河流的小路

2 N-COUNT 途径；行动路线 A pathway is a particular course of action or a way of achieving something. 途径；行动路线

  • Diplomacy will smooth your pathway to success. [+ to]

    为人圆通会让你成功的道路通畅无阻。

  • ... the call to arms that opened the pathway to freedom for the Brazilian people.

    为巴西人民开启了自由之路的武装起来的号召
