mispronounce
mɪsprənaʊns ˌmɪsproʊˈnaʊns ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense mispronounces, present participle mispronouncing, past tense , past participle mispronounced
VERB 读错…的音；把…发错音 If you mispronounce a word, you pronounce it wrongly. 读错…的音；把…发错音

  • He repeatedly mispronounced words and slurred his speech. [VERB noun]

    他一再读错单词，而且说话含含糊糊的。
