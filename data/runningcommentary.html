running commentary
Word forms: plural running commentaries
N-COUNT 现场转播;实况报道 If someone provides a running commentary on an event, they give a continuous description of it while it is taking place. 现场转播;实况报道

  • John gave the police control room a running commentary on the driver's antics as he followed him at 90mph. [+ on]

    约翰一边以每小时90英里的速度跟踪这个司机，一边向警方监控室描述该司机的异常行为。
