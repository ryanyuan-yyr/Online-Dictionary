abomination
əbɒmɪneɪʃən əˌbɑməˈneɪʃən
Word forms: plural abominations
N-COUNT 令人完全无法接受的事物；令人厌恶的事物 If you say that something is an abomination, you think that it is completely unacceptable. 令人完全无法接受的事物；令人厌恶的事物 [formal, disapproval]

  • What is happening is an abomination.

    正在发生的事令人非常反感。

SYN outrage, bête noire, horror, evil
