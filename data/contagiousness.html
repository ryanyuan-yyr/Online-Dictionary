contagious
kənteɪdʒəs kənˈteɪdʒəs
1 ADJ-GRADED （病）接触传染的 A disease that is contagious can be caught by touching people or things that are infected with it. （病）接触传染的

  • ...a highly contagious disease of the lungs.

    极具传染性的肺病

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] (感情、态度等)感染性的，会迅速蔓延开的 A feeling or attitude that is contagious spreads quickly among a group of people. (感情、态度等)感染性的，会迅速蔓延开的

  • Laughing is contagious.

    笑是有感染力的。

  • Antonio has a contagious enthusiasm for the beautiful aspect of food.

    安东尼奥对食品赏心悦目这一方面的热情非常有感染性。
