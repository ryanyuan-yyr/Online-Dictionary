downer
daʊnəʳ ˈdaʊnər
Word forms: plural downers
1 N-COUNT 镇静剂;安眠药 Downers are drugs that make you feel sleepy or very calm. 镇静剂;安眠药 [informal]

  • She was taking uppers and downers at the time.

    她那时在服用兴奋剂和抑制剂。

2 N-COUNT [usually singular] 令人悲伤(或沮丧)的情景 If you describe a situation as a downer, you think that it is very depressing. 令人悲伤(或沮丧)的情景 [informal]

  • For divorced people, Christmas can be a downer.

    对离异的人来说，圣诞节令人感伤。

If you are on a downer, you are feeling depressed and without hope.

  • We've been on a bit of a downer since the Liverpool game.

    自从利物浦比赛以来，我们一直有点沮丧。
