profundity
prəfʌndɪti proʊˈfʌndəti prəˈfʌndəti
Word forms: plural profundities
1 N-UNCOUNT 深奥；深刻 Profundity is great intellectual depth and understanding. 深奥；深刻

  • The profundity of this book is achieved with breathtaking lightness. [+ of]

    这本书极为深入浅出。

2 N-UNCOUNT （情感、经历、变化等的）深厚，深刻，深切 If you refer to the profundity of a feeling, experience, or change, you mean that it is deep, powerful, or serious. （情感、经历、变化等的）深厚，深刻，深切

  • ...the profundity of the structural problems besetting the country. [+ of]

    困扰这个国家的严重的结构性问题

SYN intensity, strength, depth, seriousness  
3 N-COUNT 深刻的话语；深奥的话语 A profundity is a remark that shows great intellectual depth and understanding. 深刻的话语；深奥的话语

  • His work is full of profundities and asides concerning the human condition.

    他的作品充满了有关人类境况的深邃见解和话外音。
