bondage
bɒndɪdʒ ˈbɑndɪdʒ
1 N-UNCOUNT 奴役 Bondage is the condition of being someone's property and having to work for them. 奴役

  • Masters sometimes allowed their slaves to buy their way out of bondage.

    奴隶主们有时允许奴隶为自己赎身。

2 N-UNCOUNT 束缚；约束 Bondage is the condition of not being free because you are strongly influenced by something or someone. 束缚；约束 [formal]

  • All people, she said, lived their lives in bondage to hunger, pain and lust. [+ to]

    她说，所有人的生活都受缚于饥饿、苦痛和欲望。

3 N-UNCOUNT （通过捆绑以寻求性快感的）性虐待 Bondage is the practice of being tied up or tying your partner up in order to gain sexual pleasure. （通过捆绑以寻求性快感的）性虐待
