shorn
ʃɔːʳn ʃɔrn
1 ADJ 剪短的;修剪过的 If grass or hair is shorn, it has been cut very short. 剪短的;修剪过的 [literary]

  • ...his shorn hair.

    他剪短了的头发

2 ADJ 被剥夺了…的;被剥去…的 If a person or thing is shorn of something that was an important part of them, it has been removed from them. 被剥夺了…的;被剥去…的 [literary]

  • She looks terrible, shorn of all her beauty and dignity. [+ of]

    失去了美貌和尊严，她看上去糟透了。

  • ...an age increasingly shorn of religious and political faith.

    宗教和政治信仰逐渐丧失的时代

3 Shorn is the past participle of shear.
