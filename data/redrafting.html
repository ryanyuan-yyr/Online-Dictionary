redraft
riːdrɑːft -dræft ˈriˌdræft ˈriˌdrɑft riˈdræft ˈriˈdrɑft
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense redrafts, present participle redrafting, past tense , past participle redrafted
VERB 改写;重新起草 If you redraft something you have written, you write it again in order to improve it or change it. 改写;重新起草

  • Parliament plans to redraft the law on privatisation. [VERB noun]

    国会计划重新起草私有化法律。

  • The speech had already been redrafted 22 times. [be VERB-ed]

    演讲稿已经改写过22遍了。
