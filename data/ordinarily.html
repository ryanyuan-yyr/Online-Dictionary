ordinarily
ɔːʳdɪnərəli -nerɪli ˌɔrdənˈɛrəli
ADV [oft ADVERB adjective] [ADVERB before verb] 通常；正常情况下 If you say what is ordinarily the case, you are saying what is normally the case. 通常；正常情况下

  • The streets would ordinarily have been full of people. There was no one.

    街上通常应该人满为患，当时却空无一人。

  • Similar arrangements apply to students who are ordinarily resident in Scotland.

    类似的安排适用于长期居住在苏格兰的学生。

  • ...places where the patient does not ordinarily go.

    病人通常不会去的地方

SYN usually, generally, normally, commonly
