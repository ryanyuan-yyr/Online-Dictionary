wrapper
ræpəʳ ˈræpər
Word forms: plural wrappers
N-COUNT (尤指用于食品的)包装纸,包装塑料,包装用金属薄纸 A wrapper is a piece of paper, plastic, or thin metal which covers and protects something that you buy, especially food. (尤指用于食品的)包装纸,包装塑料,包装用金属薄纸

  • I emptied the sweet wrappers from the ashtray.

    我把烟灰缸里的糖纸倒掉。

  • ...an unsmoked cigar in its cellophane wrapper.

    包着塑料膜、没有吸过的雪茄

SYN cover, case, paper, packaging
