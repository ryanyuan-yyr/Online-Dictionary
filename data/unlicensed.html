unlicensed
ʌnlaɪsənst ʌnˈlaɪsənst
1 ADJ (人)无执照的If you are unlicensed, you do not have official permission from the government or from the authorities to do something. (人)无执照的

  • ...unscrupulous, and usually unlicensed investment managers who regularly target wealthy widows.

    ...不择手段的无执照的投资经理常把有钱的寡妇作为他们的目标。

2 ADJ (物品)无许可的If something that you own or use is unlicensed, you do not have official permission to own it or use it. (物品)无许可的

  • He went out with an unlicensed shotgun to look for the intruders.

    他带着一把没有执照的霰弹枪出去寻找闯入者。

3 ADJ (饭店、酒吧)无售酒执照的If a place such as a bar or restaurant is unlicensed, it does not have a license to sell alcoholic drinks. (饭店、酒吧)无售酒执照的
4 ADJ (活动、产品)无许可的If something such as an activity or product is unlicensed, it takes place or is produced without official permission. (活动、产品)无许可的

  • Unlicensed steroids are being disguised as herbal medicines.

    无许可的类固醇被伪装成为草药。
