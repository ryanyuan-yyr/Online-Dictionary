endearing
ɪndɪərɪŋ ɛnˈdɪrɪŋ ɪnˈdɪrɪŋ
ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 惹人喜爱的;讨人喜欢的;可爱的 If you describe someone's behaviour as endearing, you mean that it causes you to feel very fond of them. 惹人喜爱的;讨人喜欢的;可爱的

  • She has such an endearing personality.

    她的个性非常讨人喜欢。

  • Henry's lisp is so endearing.

    亨利的咬舌音很可爱。

SYN attractive, winning, pleasing, appealing  
endearingly

  • He admits endearingly to doubts and hesitations.

    他坦率地承认有疑虑和顾忌。

  • She is endearingly free of pretensions.

    她丝毫不做作，很讨人喜爱。
