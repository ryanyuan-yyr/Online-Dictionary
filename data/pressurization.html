pressurize
preʃəraɪz ˈprɛʃərˌaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense pressurizes, present participle pressurizing, past tense , past participle pressurized
in BRIT, also use 英国英语亦用 pressurise
1 VERB 强迫；向…施加压力 If you are pressurized into doing something, you are forcefully persuaded to do it. 强迫；向…施加压力 [Also VERB n to-infinitive]

  • Do not be pressurized into making your decision immediately. [be VERB-ed + into]

    不要迫于压力马上做出决定。

  • He thought she was trying to pressurize him. [VERB noun]

    他觉得她正试图对他施压。

SYN force, drive, compel, intimidate  
2See also:pressurized
