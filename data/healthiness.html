healthy
helθi ˈhɛlθi
Word forms: comparative healthier, superlative healthiest
1 ADJ-GRADED 健康的;健壮的 Someone who is healthy is well and is not suffering from any illness. 健康的;健壮的

  • Most of us need to lead more balanced lives to be healthy and happy.

    我们中大多数人需要过更为平衡的生活，以保持健康和快乐。

  • She had a normal pregnancy and delivered a healthy child.

    她正常妊娠，生了个健康的孩子。

SYN well, sound, fit, strong  
healthily

  • What I really want is to live healthily for as long as possible.

    我真正想要的是健康长寿。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 显示健康的;反映健康的 If a feature or quality that you have is healthy, it makes you look well or shows that you are well. 显示健康的;反映健康的

  • ...the glow of healthy skin.

    健康的肤色

  • ...young adults with healthy appetites.

    食欲旺盛的年轻人

3 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 有益于健康的;促进健康的 Something that is healthy is good for your health. 有益于健康的;促进健康的

  • ...a great healthy outdoor pursuit.

    对健康大有裨益的室外活动

  • ...a healthy diet.

    有益于健康的饮食

SYN wholesome, beneficial, nourishing, good for you  
4 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （机构或体系）兴旺的，运作良好的 A healthy organization or system is successful. （机构或体系）兴旺的，运作良好的

  • ...an economically healthy socialist state.

    经济繁荣的社会主义国家

5 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 大量的;可观的 A healthy amount of something is a large amount that shows success. 大量的;可观的

  • He predicts a continuation of healthy profits in the current financial year.

    他预计本财年能继续保持可观的利润。

  • ...a healthy bank account.

    巨额账户

6 ADJ-GRADED 明智的;聪明的;合理的 If you have a healthy attitude about something, you show good sense. 明智的;聪明的;合理的

  • She has a refreshingly healthy attitude to work.

    她对工作持一种明智态度，令人耳目一新。

  • It's very healthy to be afraid when there's something to be afraid of.

    不逞匹夫之勇是很明智的。

healthily

  • I had never seen bombing on such a scale, and I was healthily apprehensive.

    我从未见过如此大规模的轰炸，所以我的恐惧是理所当然的。
