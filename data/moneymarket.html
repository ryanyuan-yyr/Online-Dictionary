money market
Word forms: plural money markets
N-COUNT 金融市场;货币市场 A country's money market consists of all the banks and other organizations that deal with short-term loans, capital, and foreign exchange. 金融市场;货币市场 [business]

  • On the money markets the dollar was weaker against European currencies.

    在货币市场上，美元不如欧洲货币坚挺。
