prompting
prɒmptɪŋ ˈprɒmptɪŋ
Word forms: plural promptings
N-UNCOUNT 鼓励；敦促；驱使；怂恿 If you respond to prompting, you do what someone encourages or reminds you to do. 鼓励；敦促；驱使；怂恿

  • New York needed little prompting from their coach Bill Parcells.

    纽约队不太需要他们的教练比尔·帕索斯的鼓动。

  • ...the promptings of your subconscious.

    潜意识的驱使

SYN urging, pressing, pressure, pushing
