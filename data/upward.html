upward
ʌpwəʳd ˈʌpwərd
1 ADJ [ADJ n] 向上的 An upward movement or look is directed towards a higher place or a higher level. 向上的

  • She started once again on the steep upward climb.

    她又开始沿着陡峭的山路往上爬。

  • She gave him a quick, upward look, then lowered her eyes.

    她抬眼迅速瞥了他一下，然后眼睛又垂了下去。

SYN uphill, rising, ascending, climbing  
2 ADJ [ADJ n] 上升的；升高的 If you refer to an upward trend or an upward spiral, you mean that something is increasing in quantity or price. 上升的；升高的

  • ...the Army's concern that the upward trend in the numbers avoiding military service may continue.

    部队对逃避服兵役人数可能持续增多的担忧

  • Oil prices continued an upward swing in New York this morning.

    今天上午纽约油价继续上扬。

  • ...if prices continue their inexorably upward spiral.

    如果物价继续疯狂攀升的话
