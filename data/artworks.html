artwork
ɑːʳtwɜːʳk ˈɑrtˌwɜrk
Word forms: plural artworks
1 N-UNCOUNT （书、广告中的）插图，插画 Artwork is drawings and photographs that are prepared in order to be included in something such as a book or advertisement. （书、广告中的）插图，插画

  • The artwork for the LP was done by Bill Hofstadter.

    林肯公园的广告插图出自比尔·霍夫斯塔特之手。

SYN art, design, cover art  
2 N-VAR （高品质的）艺术作品 Artworks are paintings or sculptures which are of high quality. （高品质的）艺术作品

  • The museum contains 6,000 contemporary and modern artworks.

    博物馆收藏了6,000件近现代艺术作品。

  • ...a magnificent collection of priceless artwork.

    令人叹为观止的价值连城的艺术收藏品
