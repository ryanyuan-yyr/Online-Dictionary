trouper
truːpəʳ ˈtrupɛr
Word forms: plural troupers
N-COUNT （尤指经验老到的）演员，表演者 You can refer to an actor or other performer as a trouper, especially when you want to suggest that they have a lot of experience and can deal with difficult situations in a professional way. （尤指经验老到的）演员，表演者

  • Like the old trouper he is, he timed his entry to perfection.

    他是个老演员，出场时间把握得恰到好处。

SYN performer, player, actor, theatrical
