embittered
ɪmbɪtəʳd
ADJ-GRADED 愤恨的;痛苦的;怨恨的 If someone is embittered, they feel angry and unhappy because of harsh, unpleasant, and unfair things that have happened to them. 愤恨的;痛苦的;怨恨的

  • He had turned into an embittered, hardened adult.

    他已经变成了一个愤世嫉俗、冷酷无情的成年人。

  • Gerald turned sour and embittered when he felt people were not dealing honestly with him.

    当杰拉尔德感到人们没有真诚待他时，就变得乖张易怒，怨恨不平。

SYN resentful, angry, acid, bitter
