malcontent
mælkəntent ˈmælkənˌtɛnt ˈmælkənˌtɛnt
Word forms: plural malcontents
N-COUNT [usually plural] 不满现状者 You can describe people as malcontents when you disapprove of the fact that they are dissatisfied with a situation and want it to change. 不满现状者 [formal, disapproval]

  • Five years ago a band of malcontents, mainly half-educated radicals, seized power.

    5年前一群主要由文化程度不高的激进分子构成的不满现状者夺取了政权。

SYN troublemaker, rebel, complainer, grumbler
