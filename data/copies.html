copy
kɒpi ˈkɑpi
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense copies, present participle copying, past tense , past participle copied
1 N-COUNT 复制品;复印件 If you make a copy of something, you produce something that looks like the original thing. 复制品;复印件

  • The reporter apparently obtained a copy of Steve's resignation letter. [+ of]

    那记者显然弄到了史蒂夫辞职信的复印件。

  • Always keep a copy of everything in your own files.

    要时刻牢记把所有文件都备份。

SYN reproduction, duplicate, photocopy, carbon copy  
2 VERB 复制;复印;仿造 If you copy something, you produce something that looks like the original thing. 复制;复印;仿造

  • She never participated in obtaining or copying any classified documents for anyone. [VERB noun]

    她从未参与过为任何人获取或复制机密文件之事。

  • ...lawsuits against companies who have unlawfully copied computer programs. [VERB noun]

    对非法复制计算机程序的公司提起的诉讼

  • ...top designers, whose work has been widely copied. [be VERB-ed]

    其作品被很多人仿制的顶级设计师

  • He copied the chart from a book. [VERB noun + from]

    他从一本书上复印了那个图表。

SYN reproduce, replicate, duplicate, photocopy  
3 VERB 抄写;誊写 If you copy a piece of writing, you write it again exactly. 抄写;誊写

  • He would allow John slyly to copy his answers to impossibly difficult algebra questions. [VERB noun]

    他会让约翰偷偷抄下自己对一些极难的代数题的解答。

  • He copied the data into a notebook. [VERB noun + into]

    他将数据抄在一个笔记本上。

  • We're copying from textbooks because we don't have enough to go round. [VERB + from]

    我们在抄课本，因为课本不够。

Copy out means the same as copy .copy out 同 copy

  • He wrote the title on the blackboard, then copied out the text sentence by sentence.

    他将题目写在黑板上，然后逐句抄下课文。

  • 'Did he leave a phone number?' — 'Oh, yes.' She copied it out for him.

    “他留电话号码了吗？”—“嗯，留了。”她将号码给他抄了下来。

4 VERB 模仿;效法;仿效 If you copy a person or what they do, you try to do what they do or try to be like them, usually because you admire them or what they have done. 模仿;效法;仿效

  • Children can be seen to copy the behaviour of others whom they admire or identify with. [VERB noun]

    人们发现儿童会模仿他们所崇拜或有共鸣感的人。

  • He can claim to have been defeated by opponents copying his own tactics. [VERB noun]

    他可以说是被仿效自己策略的对手们给打败了。

  • ...the coquettish gestures she had copied from actresses in soap operas. [VERB noun + from]

    她从肥皂剧中的那些女演员那里学来的一些搔首弄姿的动作

copying

  • Children learn by copying.

    儿童通过模仿来学习。

5 N-COUNT (书、报纸或CD的)一本，一册，一份 A copy of a book, newspaper, or CD is one of many that are exactly the same. (书、报纸或CD的)一本，一册，一份

  • I bought a copy of 'USA Today' from a street-corner machine.

    我从街角的自动售卖机买了一份《今日美国》。

  • You can obtain a copy for $2 from New York Central Art Supply.

    花两美元就能从纽约艺术用品中心买到一册。

6 N-UNCOUNT (准备刊印或广播的)原稿，稿件 In journalism, copy is written material that is ready to be printed or read in a broadcast. (准备刊印或广播的)原稿，稿件 [technical]

  • ...his ability to write the most lyrical copy in the history of sports television.

    他能够撰写体育节目史上最富热情和激情的稿件

  • ...advertising copy.

    广告文案

7 N-UNCOUNT 有报道价值的消息;新闻素材 In journalism, copy is news or information that can be used in an article in a newspaper. 有报道价值的消息;新闻素材 [technical]

  • ...journalists looking for good copy.

    搜罗好题材的记者
