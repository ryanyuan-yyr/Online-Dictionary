glockenspiel
glɒkənʃpiːl ˈglɑkənˌspil ˈglɑkənˌʃpil
Word forms: plural glockenspiels
N-COUNT （用木槌敲击发声的）钟琴 A glockenspiel is a musical instrument which consists of metal bars of different lengths arranged like the keyboard of a piano. You play the glockenspiel by hitting the bars with wooden hammers. （用木槌敲击发声的）钟琴
