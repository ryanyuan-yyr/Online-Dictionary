shampoo
ʃæmpuː ʃæmˈpu
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense shampoos, present participle shampooing, past tense , past participle shampooed
1 N-MASS 洗发液;香波 Shampoo is a soapy liquid that you use for washing your hair. 洗发液;香波

  • ...a bottle of shampoo.

    一瓶洗发液

  • ...bubble baths, soaps and shampoos.

    泡泡浴、肥皂和香波

2 VERB 用洗发液洗(头发) When you shampoo your hair, you wash it using shampoo. 用洗发液洗(头发)

  • Shampoo your hair and dry it. [VERB noun]

    用洗发液洗洗头发然后擦干。
