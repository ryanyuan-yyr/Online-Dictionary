timber
tɪmbəʳ ˈtɪmbər
Word forms: plural timbers
1 N-UNCOUNT 木材;原木;树木；林木 Timber is wood that is used for building houses and making furniture. You can also refer to trees that are grown for this purpose as timber . 木材;原木;树木；林木

  • These Severn Valley woods have been exploited for timber since Saxon times.

    从撒克逊时代起就开始采伐塞文山谷的这些树林获取木料。

  • ...a single-story timber building.

    木质平房

SYN wood, logs  
2 N-COUNT [usually plural] (造船或建屋用的)栋木，大梁 The timbers of a ship or house are the large pieces of wood that have been used to build it. (造船或建屋用的)栋木，大梁

  • ...a bird nestling in the timbers of the roof.

    栖息于屋顶横梁上的鸟

SYN beams, boards, planks
