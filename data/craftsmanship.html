craftsmanship
krɑːftsmənʃɪp kræft-
1 N-UNCOUNT 手艺;技艺 Craftsmanship is the skill that someone uses when they make beautiful things with their hands. 手艺;技艺

  • It is easy to appreciate the craftsmanship of Armani. [+ of]

    阿玛尼的精致工艺很容易博得人们的赞誉。

2 N-UNCOUNT 精工细作 Craftsmanship is the quality that something has when it is beautiful and has been very carefully made. 精工细作

  • His canoes are known for their style, fine detail and craftsmanship.

    他的独木舟以其风格独特、注重细节和做工精细著称。

SYN workmanship, technique, expertise, mastery
