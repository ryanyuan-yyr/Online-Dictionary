brinkmanship
brɪŋkmənʃɪp ˈbrɪŋkmənˌʃɪp
N-UNCOUNT （尤指政治上故意将危险局势推至极限的）边缘政策，冒险政策 Brinkmanship is a method of behaviour, especially in politics, in which you deliberately get into dangerous situations which could result in disaster but which could also bring success. （尤指政治上故意将危险局势推至极限的）边缘政策，冒险政策 [journalism]

  • There is a lot of political brinkmanship involved in this latest development.

    最新的进展涉及许多政治冒险政策的推行。
