honorarium
ɒnəreəriəm ˌɒnəˈrɛərɪəm ˌɑnəˈrɛriəm
Word forms: plural honoraria/ɒnəreəriə/, plural honorariums
N-COUNT （本职工作之外获得的）酬金，谢礼 An honorarium is a fee that someone receives for doing something which is not a normal part of their job, for example giving a talk. （本职工作之外获得的）酬金，谢礼

  • All sorts of people I found were getting honoraria for various extra duties that they had assigned to them.

    我看到各种各样的人都因为承担他们所指派的各种额外工作而获得酬金。
