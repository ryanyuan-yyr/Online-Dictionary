stymie
staɪmi ˈstaɪmi
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense stymies, present participle stymieing, past tense , past participle stymied
VERB [usually passive] 妨碍;阻挠;阻碍 If you are stymied by something, you find it very difficult to take action or to continue what you are doing. 妨碍;阻挠;阻碍 [informal]

  • Companies have been stymied by the length of time it takes to reach an agreement. [be VERB-ed]

    由于耗时过长，各公司一直未能达成协议。

  • Relief efforts have been stymied in recent weeks by armed gunmen. [be VERB-ed]

    最近几周的救援工作一直受到武装分子的阻挠。

SYN frustrate, defeat, foil, thwart
