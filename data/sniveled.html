snivel
snɪvəl ˈsnɪvəl
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense snivels, present participle snivelling, past tense , past participle snivelled
in AM, use 美国英语用 sniveling, sniveled
VERB (令人讨厌地)啜泣，抽抽噎噎地哭，抽鼻子 If someone is snivelling, they are crying or sniffing in a way that irritates you. (令人讨厌地)啜泣，抽抽噎噎地哭，抽鼻子

  • Billy started to snivel. His mother smacked his hand. [VERB]

    比利抽抽搭搭地哭了起来。妈妈在他的手上打了一下。

  • ...a journalist snivelling with the flu. [VERB]

    得了流感、不停抽鼻子的记者

Snivel is also a noun.

  • Carol managed a few proper snivels for the sake of appearance.

    为了做做样子，卡萝尔适时地抽了几下鼻子。
