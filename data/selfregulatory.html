self-regulatory
ˌsɛlfrɛɡjʊˈleɪtərɪ
(also self-regulating)
ADJ [usually ADJ n] 自我管理的;自我调节的 Self-regulatory systems, organizations, or activities are controlled by the people involved in them, rather than by outside organizations or rules. 自我管理的;自我调节的

  • For a self-regulatory system to work, the consent of all those involved is required.

    一个自我管理的体制要运作起来，必须得到所有相关人员的一致认可。
