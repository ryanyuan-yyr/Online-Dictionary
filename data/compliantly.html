compliant
kəmplaɪənt kəmplaɪənt
ADJ-GRADED 服从的;顺从的;俯首帖耳的 If you say that someone is compliant, you mean they willingly do what they are asked to do. 服从的;顺从的;俯首帖耳的 [formal]

    她哥哥很听话，她却调皮得多。

    驯顺的工人们

SYN obedient, willing, accepting, yielding
