entirety
ɪntaɪərɪti ɪnˈtaɪərɪtɪ ɛnˈtaɪrti ɪnˈtaɪrti
PHRASE 整个地;全面地 If something is used or affected in its entirety, the whole of it is used or affected. 整个地;全面地

  • The peace plan has not been accepted in its entirety by all parties.

    和平计划并没有被各方全盘接受。

  • ...a number of cities were now to be bombed in their entirety.

    一些城市将全面遭到轰炸。
