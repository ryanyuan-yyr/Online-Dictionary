finery
faɪnəri ˈfaɪnəri
N-UNCOUNT 盛装;华丽的服饰 If someone is dressed in their finery, they are wearing the elegant and impressive clothes and jewellery that they wear on special occasions. 盛装;华丽的服饰 [literary]

  • ...the guests in all their finery.

    身着盛装的客人们

SYN splendour, trappings, frippery, glad rags [informal]
