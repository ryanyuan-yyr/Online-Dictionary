perilous
perɪləs ˈpɛrələs
ADJ-GRADED 危险的；艰险的 Something that is perilous is very dangerous. 危险的；艰险的 [literary]

  • ...a perilous journey across the war-zone.

    横穿交战区的危险之旅

  • The road grew even steeper and more perilous.

    道路变得越来越陡峭，越来越凶险。

  • ...perilous cliffs.

    险峻的悬崖

SYN dangerous, threatening, exposed, vulnerable  
perilously

  • The track snaked perilously upwards.

    这条小道蜿蜒而上，很危险。

  • ...a perilously narrow bridge.

    危险的窄桥
