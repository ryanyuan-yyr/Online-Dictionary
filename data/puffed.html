puffed
pʌft pʌft
1 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 肿的；肿胀的 If a part of your body is puffed or puffed up, it is swollen because of an injury or because you are unwell. 肿的；肿胀的

  • His face was a little puffed.

    他的脸有些肿胀。

  • His mouth was all puffed up where he had taken a rifle butt.

    他的嘴上挨了枪托的地方都肿了起来。

2 ADJ-GRADED [verb-link ADJ] 气喘吁吁的；上气不接下气的 If you are puffed or puffed out, you are breathing with difficulty because you have been using a lot of energy. 气喘吁吁的；上气不接下气的 [informal]

  • Do you get puffed out running up and down the stairs?

    你要是在楼梯上跑上跑下的会不会喘不过气来？

SYN out of breath, winded, exhausted, spent
