copyright
kɒpiraɪt ˈkɑpiˌraɪt
Word forms: plural copyrights
N-VAR 版权;著作权 If someone has copyright on a piece of writing or music, it is illegal to reproduce or perform it without their permission. 版权;著作权

  • To order a book one first had to get permission from the monastery that held the copyright.

    要想订购书，必须先得到持有该书版权的修道院的许可。

  • She threatened legal action against the Sun for breach of copyright.

    她威胁说要对《太阳报》侵犯其著作权的行为采取法律措施。
