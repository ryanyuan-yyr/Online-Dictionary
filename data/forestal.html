forest
fɒrɪst fɔːr- ˈfɔrɪst ˈfɑrɪst
Word forms: plural forests
1 N-VAR 森林;树林 A forest is a large area where trees grow close together. 森林;树林

  • Parts of the forest are still dense and inaccessible.

    森林的一些部分仍然很茂密，人迹罕至。

  • ...25 million hectares of forest.

    2,500 万公顷的森林

SYN wood, trees, woods, grove  
2 N-COUNT 林立;大量耸立 A forest of tall or narrow objects is a group of them standing or sticking upright. 林立;大量耸立 [literary]

  • They descended from the plane into a forest of microphones and cameras. [+ of]

    他们从飞机上走下来，迎接他们的是数不清的麦克风和照相机。
