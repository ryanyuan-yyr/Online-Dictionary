butchery
bʊtʃəri ˈbʊtʃəri
1 N-UNCOUNT 大屠杀；残杀 You can refer to the cruel killing of a lot of people as butchery when you want to express your horror and disgust at this. 大屠杀；残杀 [disapproval]

  • In her view, war is simply a legalised form of butchery.

    在她看来，战争只不过是一种合法化的屠杀。

SYN slaughter, killing, murder, massacre  
2 N-UNCOUNT 屠宰工作 Butchery is the work of cutting up meat and preparing it for sale. 屠宰工作

  • ...a carcass hung up for butchery.

    悬挂起来待宰的畜体

SYN carving, cutting up, dressing, cleaning
