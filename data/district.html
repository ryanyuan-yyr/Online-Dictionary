district
dɪstrɪkt ˈdɪstrɪkt
Word forms: plural districts
1 N-COUNT 地区;区域 A district is a particular area of a town or country. 地区;区域

  • I drove around the business district.

    我开车在商业区转了转。

  • ...Nashville's shopping district.

    纳什维尔的购物区

  • ...a summer holiday hike in the Lake District.

    夏日假期在湖区的远足

  • Varieties of these crops have been collected from all around the district.

    这些不同的作物已从该地区各处收集起来。

SYN area, community, region, sector  
2 N-COUNT 行政区;辖区;管区 A district is an area of a town or country which has been given official boundaries for the purpose of administration. 行政区;辖区;管区

  • ...the home of the governor of the district.

    该区行政长官的家

  • ...the continuing support of Glasgow District Council.

    格拉斯哥行政区政务委员会一直以来给予的支持

  • ...the district health authority.

    区卫生局
