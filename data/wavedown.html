wave down
PHRASAL VERB 挥手示意（车辆）停下；招手拦（车） If someone waves down a vehicle, they wave their hand as a signal to the driver to stop the vehicle. 挥手示意（车辆）停下；招手拦（车）

  • He was frustrated by his inability to wave down a taxi. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    连一辆出租车都打不到，他很沮丧。

  • He turned the corner a little too fast, narrowly missing the boy who ran into the road to wave him down. [VERB noun PREP.]

    他转弯有点急，差一点就撞倒那个冲上马路拦他车的男孩。
