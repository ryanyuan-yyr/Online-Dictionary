ravenous
rævənəs ˈrævənəs
ADJ 非常饥饿的;饥肠辘辘的 If you are ravenous, you are extremely hungry. 非常饥饿的;饥肠辘辘的

  • Amy realized that she had eaten nothing since leaving Bruton Street, and she was ravenous.

    埃米意识到自己从离开布鲁顿大街之后什么东西都没吃，她饿极了。

  • ...a pack of ravenous animals.

    一群饿极了的动物

SYN starving, starved, very hungry, famished  
ravenously

  • She began to eat ravenously.

    她开始狼吞虎咽起来。

  • She emerged looking ravenously hungry.

    她出现时看起来饿坏了。
