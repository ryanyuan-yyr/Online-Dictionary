sturdy
stɜːʳdi ˈstɜrdi
Word forms: comparative sturdier, superlative sturdiest
ADJ-GRADED 坚固的;结实的;强壮的 Someone or something that is sturdy looks strong and is unlikely to be easily injured or damaged. 坚固的;结实的;强壮的

  • She was a short, sturdy woman in her early sixties.

    她是个 60 岁出头、身材矮小结实的女人。

  • The camera was mounted on a sturdy tripod.

    相机固定在一个坚固的三脚架上。

SYN robust, hardy, vigorous, powerful  
sturdily

  • It was a good table too, sturdily constructed of elm.

    这也是张很不错的桌子，榆木的，很结实。
