stair
steəʳ stɛə stɛr
Word forms: plural stairs
1 N-PLURAL 楼梯 Stairs are a set of steps inside a building which go from one floor to another. 楼梯

  • Nancy began to climb the stairs.

    南希开始爬楼梯。

  • We walked up a flight of stairs.

    我们上了一段楼梯。

  • He learned to walk safely up and down stairs.

    他学会了安全地上下楼梯。

  • He stopped at the top of the stairs.

    他在楼梯顶端停了下来。

  • ...a stair carpet.

    楼梯地毯

2 N-SING 一层楼梯;一段楼梯 A stair is a flight of stairs. 一层楼梯;一段楼梯 [literary]

  • I followed her down the stair.

    我跟着她下了楼梯。

3 N-COUNT 一级楼梯 A stair is one of the steps in a flight of stairs. 一级楼梯

  • Terry was sitting on the bottom stair.

    特里正坐在最下面的一级楼梯上。
