withdrawal
wɪðdrɔːəl wɪðˈdrɔːəl wɪðˈdrɔəl wɪθˈdrɔəl
Word forms: plural withdrawals
1 N-VAR 收回;撤回;停止 The withdrawal of something is the act or process of removing it, or ending it. 收回;撤回;停止 [formal]

  • ...withdrawal of friendship.

    绝交

  • If you experience any unusual symptoms after withdrawal of the treatment then contact your doctor. [+ of]

    如果停止治疗后你感觉有任何异常症状，要即刻就医。

  • ...allied troop withdrawal from the north of the country.

    盟军从该国北部撤出

2 N-UNCOUNT （从活动或组织中）退出 Someone's withdrawal from an activity or an organization is their decision to stop taking part in it. （从活动或组织中）退出

  • ...his withdrawal from government in 1946. [+ from]

    他1946年退出政府

SYN departure, retirement, exit, secession  
3 N-COUNT 取款;提款 A withdrawal is an amount of money that you take from your bank account. 取款;提款
4 N-SING [NOUN of n] （对所说的话或声明的）撤回，收回 The withdrawal of a remark or statement that you have made is the act of saying that you want people to ignore it. （对所说的话或声明的）撤回，收回

  • The charity says it wants a withdrawal of the comments.

    该慈善机构称希望收回所作评论。

SYN retraction, recall, disclaimer, repudiation  
5 N-UNCOUNT 戒毒期;脱瘾过程 Withdrawal is the period during which someone feels ill after they have stopped taking a drug which they were addicted to. 戒毒期;脱瘾过程

  • Withdrawal from heroin is actually like a severe attack of gastric flu. [+ from]

    戒断海洛因的过程实际上就像患了一场严重的胃肠型流感。

6 N-UNCOUNT 孤僻；自闭 Withdrawal is behaviour in which someone prefers to be alone and does not want to talk to other people. 孤僻；自闭

  • ...an inability to cope with emotional problems except by retreating into withdrawal.

    无法应对情感问题，只能消极避世
