bellyache
belieɪk ˈbɛliˌeɪk
(also belly-ache)Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense bellyaches, present participle bellyaching, past tense , past participle bellyached
1 N-VAR 肚子痛；腹痛 Bellyache is a pain inside your abdomen, especially in your stomach. 肚子痛；腹痛 [informal]

  • They may complain of diarrhea or bellyache.

    他们可能会主诉腹泻或腹痛。

2 VERB [usually cont] 无病呻吟；反复大声发牢骚 If you say that someone is bellyaching, you mean they complain loudly and frequently about something and you think this is unreasonable or unjustified. 无病呻吟；反复大声发牢骚 [informal] [Also VERB]

  • ...bellyaching about recession.

    无端就经济衰退叫苦不迭
