luxuriate
lʌgʒʊərieɪt lʌgˈʒʊriˌeɪt lʌkˈʃʊriˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense luxuriates, present participle luxuriating, past tense , past participle luxuriated
VERB （尤指因舒适和豪华而）尽情享受，沉湎于，陶醉于 If you luxuriate in something, you relax in it and enjoy it very much, especially because you find it comfortable and luxurious. （尤指因舒适和豪华而）尽情享受，沉湎于，陶醉于

  • Lie back and luxuriate in the scented oil. [VERB + in]

    躺下来尽情享受精油的滋润。

  • Ralph was luxuriating in the first real holiday he'd had in years. [VERB + in]

    拉尔夫正沉浸于假期之中，这是他这么多年来第一个真正的假期。

SYN enjoy, delight, indulge, relish
