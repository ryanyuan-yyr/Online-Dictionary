providence prɒvɪdəns ˈprɑvədəns
N-UNCOUNT 上天；天意；天命；天佑 Providence is God, or a force which is believed by some people to arrange the things that happen to us. 上天；天意；天命；天佑 [literary]

  • The first Slav pope, John Paul, said that providence had chosen him to proclaim the spiritual unity of Europe.

    第一位斯拉夫教皇约翰·保罗声称上天选择了他来昭示欧洲的精神统一。

  • These women regard his death as an act of providence.

    这些女人认为他的死乃是天意。

SYN fate, fortune, destiny, God's will
