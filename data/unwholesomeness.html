unwholesome
ʌnhoʊlsəm ʌnˈhəʊlsəm ʌnˈhoʊlsəm
1 ADJ-GRADED 不健康的；不卫生的 Unwholesome food or drink is not healthy or good for you. 不健康的；不卫生的

  • The fish were unwholesome and old.

    这些鱼既不卫生又不新鲜。

2 ADJ-GRADED 讨人厌的；不自然的 If you describe someone's feelings or behaviour as unwholesome, you are critical of them because they are unpleasant or unnatural. 讨人厌的；不自然的 [disapproval]

  • My desire to be rich was an insane, unwholesome, oppressive desire.

    我对财富的渴望疯狂、病态，犹如心头的一块重石。

SYN wicked, bad, evil, corrupting
