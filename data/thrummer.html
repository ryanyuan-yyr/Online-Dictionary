thrum
θrʌm θrʌm
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense thrums, present participle thrumming, past tense , past participle thrummed
VERB (机器、引擎)嗡嗡响，发出沉闷声 When something such as a machine or engine thrums, it makes a low beating sound. (机器、引擎)嗡嗡响，发出沉闷声

  • The air-conditioner thrummed. [VERB]

    空调嗡嗡作响。

Thrum is also a noun.

  • ...the thrum of refrigeration motors.

    制冷机低沉的嗡嗡声

  • My head was going thrum thrum thrum.

    我的脑袋嗡嗡作响。
