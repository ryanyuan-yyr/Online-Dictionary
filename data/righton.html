right on
1 ADJ 好极了 If you describe something as right on, you mean that it is extremely good. 好极了

  • The film's comic moments are well-timed, and as the party animal Stuart, Baldwin is right on.

    这部电影的喜剧部分很合时宜，饰演派对爱好者斯图尔特的鲍德温出色极了。

2 CONVENTION 说得对; 没错 You say right on to encourage someone or to emphasize that you agree with them. 说得对; 没错
