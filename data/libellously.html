libellous
laɪbələs
in AM, use 美国英语用 libelous
ADJ-GRADED （书报、杂志的言论）诽谤（性）的 If a statement in a book, newspaper, or magazine is libellous, it wrongly accuses someone of something, and is therefore against the law. （书报、杂志的言论）诽谤（性）的

  • He claimed the articles were libellous and damaging to the interests of the team.

    他声称那些文章造谣诽谤，对全队的利益造成了损害。

SYN defamatory, false, untrue, malicious
