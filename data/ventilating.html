ventilate
ventɪleɪt ˈvɛntɪˌleɪt ˈvɛntəlˌeɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense ventilates, present participle ventilating, past tense , past participle ventilated
1 VERB 使（房间或建筑）通风;使空气流通 If you ventilate a room or building, you allow fresh air to get into it. 使（房间或建筑）通风;使空气流通

  • Ventilate the room properly when paint stripping. [VERB noun]

    当油漆开始剥落时，要给房间适当通风。

  • The pit is ventilated by a steel fan. [VERB noun]

    矿井中用钢制通风机来保持空气流通。

  • ...badly ventilated rooms. [VERB-ed]

    通风条件很差的房间

SYN aerate, fan, cool, refresh  
ventilation

  • The only ventilation comes from tiny sliding windows.

    只能通过小推拉窗来通风。

2 VERB 发表（看法）；表达（感情） If you ventilate your ideas or feelings, you talk about them or express them freely in front of other people. 发表（看法）；表达（感情） [formal]

  • He did not think it the job of officials to ventilate their doubts or daydreams. [VERB noun]

    他认为公开提出自己的疑问或不切实际的想法并非官员们的分内之事。
