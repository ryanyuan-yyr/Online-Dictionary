astern
əstɜːʳn əˈstɜrn
1 ADV [be ADVERB] 在船尾;在船后 Something that is astern is at the back of a ship or behind the back part. 在船尾;在船后 [technical]

  • The captain was astern, pretending he was sleeping.

    船长在船尾，假装睡着了。

2 ADV [ADVERB after verb, from ADVERB] （船的行驶）向后 A ship that is moving astern is moving backwards. （船的行驶）向后 [technical]

  • At the crucial moment, the barge would not go astern.

    在关键时刻，驳船不能向后驶。
