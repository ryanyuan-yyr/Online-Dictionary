patriot
pætriət peɪt- ˈpeɪtrɪət ˈpætriˌɑt ˈpeɪtriət ˈpeɪtriˌɑt
Word forms: plural patriots
N-COUNT 爱国者 Someone who is a patriot loves their country and feels very loyal towards it. 爱国者

  • They were staunch British patriots and had portraits of the Queen in their flat.

    他们是英国坚定的爱国者，并在公寓里挂着女王的肖像。

SYN nationalist, loyalist, chauvinist, jingo
