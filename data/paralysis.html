paralysis
pəræləsɪs pəˈrælɪsɪs pəˈræləsɪs
1 N-UNCOUNT 麻痹；瘫痪 Paralysis is the loss of the ability to move and feel in all or part of your body. 麻痹；瘫痪

  • ...paralysis of the leg. [+ of]

    腿部麻痹

2 N-UNCOUNT 瘫痪状态 Paralysis is the state of being unable to act or function properly. 瘫痪状态

  • The paralysis of the leadership leaves the army without its supreme command. [+ of]

    领导层瘫痪导致军队没有最高指挥。

  • ...a kind of mental paralysis.

    一种精神瘫痪状态
