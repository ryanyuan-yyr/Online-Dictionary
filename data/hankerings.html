hankering
hæŋkərɪŋ ˈhæŋkərɪŋ
Word forms: plural hankerings
N-COUNT [NOUN to-infinitive] 渴望;热望 A hankering for something is a desire or longing for it. 渴望;热望

  • From time to time we all get a hankering for something a little different. [+ for/after]

    我们每个人偶尔都会渴望有点变化。

  • Have you always had a hankering to be an actress?

    你是否一直渴望成为一名演员？

SYN desire, longing, wish, hope
