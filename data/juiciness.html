juicy
dʒuːsi ˈdʒusi
Word forms: comparative juicier, superlative juiciest
1 ADJ-GRADED 多汁的；汁液丰富的 If food is juicy, it has a lot of juice in it and is very enjoyable to eat. 多汁的；汁液丰富的

  • ...a thick, juicy steak.

    厚实而多汁的牛排

SYN moist, lush, watery, succulent  
2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] （流言或故事）生动的，绘声绘色的，（尤指）涉及隐私的 Juicy gossip or stories contain details about people's lives, especially details which are normally kept private. （流言或故事）生动的，绘声绘色的，（尤指）涉及隐私的 [informal]

  • It provided some juicy gossip for a few days.

    这制造了一些非常八卦的流言蜚语，足够说上几天的。

  • ...the juicy details.

    绘声绘色的细节

SYN interesting, colourful, sensational, vivid
