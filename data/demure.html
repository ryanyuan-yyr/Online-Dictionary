demure
dɪmjʊəʳ dɪˈmjʊr
1 ADJ-GRADED (通常指年轻女子)羞涩的，娴静的 If you describe someone, usually a young woman, as demure, you mean they are quiet and rather shy, usually in a way that you like and find appealing, and behave very correctly. (通常指年轻女子)羞涩的，娴静的 [approval]

  • She's very demure and sweet.

    她非常娴静可爱。

  • The luscious Miss Wharton gave me a demure but knowing smile.

    性感迷人的沃顿小姐对我羞涩地会心一笑。

SYN shy, reserved, modest, retiring  
demurely

  • She smiled demurely.

    她羞涩地笑了笑。

2 ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] (衣着等)端庄的，庄重的 Demure clothes do not reveal your body and they give the impression that you are shy and behave correctly. (衣着等)端庄的，庄重的 [written]

  • ...a demure high-necked white blouse.

    端庄的高领白衬衫

demurely

  • She was demurely dressed in a black woollen suit.

    她穿了一身黑色羊毛套装，显得很庄重。
