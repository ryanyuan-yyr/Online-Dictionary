traumatic
trɔːmætɪk traʊ-
ADJ-GRADED 造成创伤的;惊骇的;痛苦的 A traumatic experience is very shocking and upsetting, and may cause psychological damage. 造成创伤的;惊骇的;痛苦的

  • I suffered a nervous breakdown. It was a traumatic experience.

    我曾经神经崩溃过。那是一次痛苦的经历。

  • For a child the death of a pet can be traumatic.

    对孩子来说，一只宠物的死亡可能会造成心理创伤。

SYN shocking, upsetting, alarming, awful
