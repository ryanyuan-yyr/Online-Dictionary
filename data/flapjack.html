flapjack
flæpdʒæk ˈflæpˌdʒæk
Word forms: plural flapjacks
1 N-VAR 燕麦甜饼 Flapjacks are thick biscuits made from oats, butter, and syrup. 燕麦甜饼 [British]
2 N-COUNT 煎饼;烤饼 Flapjacks are thin, flat, circular pieces of cooked batter made of milk, flour, and eggs. Flapjacks are usually rolled up or folded and eaten hot with a sweet or savoury filling. 煎饼;烤饼 [US]
