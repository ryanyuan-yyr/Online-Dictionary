printer
prɪntəʳ ˈprɪntər
Word forms: plural printers
1 N-COUNT 打印机 A printer is a machine that can be connected to a computer in order to make copies on paper of documents or other information held by the computer. 打印机
2See also:laser printer 
3 N-COUNT 印刷工人；印刷厂；印刷公司 A printer is a person or company whose job is printing things such as books. 印刷工人；印刷厂；印刷公司

  • The manuscript had already been sent off to the printers.

    原稿已经寄给印刷厂了。
