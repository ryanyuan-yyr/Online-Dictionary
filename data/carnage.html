carnage
kɑːʳnɪdʒ ˈkɑrnɪdʒ
1 N-UNCOUNT (尤指战争中的)屠杀,残杀 Carnage is the violent killing of large numbers of people, especially in a war. (尤指战争中的)屠杀,残杀 [literary]

  • ...his strategy for stopping the carnage in Kosovo.

    他提出的制止科索沃大屠杀的策略

  • ...the carnage of motorway accidents.

    高速公路车祸导致的大量人员死亡

SYN slaughter, murder, massacre, holocaust
