look through
1 PHRASAL VERB 逐一查看… If you look through a group of things, you examine each one so that you can find or choose the one that you want. 逐一查看…

  • Peter starts looking through the mail as soon as the door shuts. [VERB PREP. noun]

    彼得一关上门就开始逐一查看起邮件来。

2 PHRASAL VERB 浏览;翻阅 If you look through something that has been written or printed, you read it. 浏览;翻阅

  • He happened to be looking through the medical book 'Gray's Anatomy' at the time. [VERB PREP. noun]

    那时，他正好在看医书《格雷氏解剖学》。

3 PHRASAL VERB （因生气、沉思等）对…视而不见 If you say that someone looks through another person, you mean that they look at that person without seeming to see them or recognize them, for example because they are angry with them or are thinking deeply about something else. （因生气、沉思等）对…视而不见

  • As for doctors, when you go to see them they just look right through you. [VERB PREP. noun]

    说到医生，你去看病时，他们直把你当透明的。
