clime
klaɪm klaɪm
Word forms: plural climes
N-COUNT [usually plural, usually adjective NOUN] 气候带;气候区;(具有某种气候的)地区 You use clime in expressions such as warmer climes and foreign climes to refer to a place that has a particular kind of climate. 气候带;气候区;(具有某种气候的)地区 [literary]

  • He left Britain for the sunnier climes of Southern France. [+ of]

    他离开英国，去了阳光更为明媚的法国南部。

  • We always take our holidays in foreign climes.

    我们总是去国外一些气候宜人的地方度假。
