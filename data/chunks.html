chunk
tʃʌŋk tʃʌŋk
Word forms: plural chunks
1 N-COUNT 厚块;大块 Chunks of something are thick solid pieces of it. 厚块;大块

  • They had to be careful of floating chunks of ice.

    他们必须当心大块浮冰。

  • ...a chunk of meat. [+ of]

    一大块肉

  • Cut the melon into chunks.

    把瓜切块。

SYN piece, block, mass, portion  
2 N-COUNT 大量;大部分 A chunk of something is a large amount or large part of it. 大量;大部分 [informal]

  • The company owns a chunk of farmland near Gatwick Airport. [+ of]

    该公司拥有盖特威克机场周边的大片农田。
