garbed gɑːʳbd
ADJ 身着…衣服的 If someone is garbed in particular clothes, they are wearing those clothes. 身着…衣服的 [literary]

  • He was garbed in sweater, tweed jacket, and flying boots. [+ in]

    他穿着套头毛衣和粗花呢夹克，脚蹬一双飞行靴。

Garbed is also a combining form.（亦可用于构词）

  • ...the small blue-garbed woman with a brown wrinkled face.

    肤色黝黑、满脸皱纹、身穿蓝色衣服的小个子女人
