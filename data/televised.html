televise
telɪvaɪz ˈtɛləˌvaɪz
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense televises, present participle televising, past tense , past participle televised
VERB [usually passive] 通过电视播放 If an event or programme is televised, it is broadcast so that it can be seen on television. 通过电视播放

  • The Grand Prix will be televised by the BBC. [be VERB-ed]

    汽车大奖赛将由英国广播公司进行电视转播。

SYN broadcast, show, transmit, air
