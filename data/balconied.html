balcony
bælkəni ˈbælkəni
Word forms: plural balconies
1 N-COUNT 阳台 A balcony is a platform on the outside of a building, above ground level, with a wall or railing around it. 阳台
2 N-SING （剧院或电影院的）楼座，楼厅 The balcony in a theatre or cinema is an area of seats above the main seating area. （剧院或电影院的）楼座，楼厅
