bedlam
bedləm ˈbɛdləm
N-UNCOUNT 混乱；喧闹；嘈杂 Bedlam means a great deal of noise and disorder. People often say 'It was bedlam' to mean 'There was bedlam'. 混乱；喧闹；嘈杂

  • The crowd went absolutely mad. It was bedlam.

    人们彻底疯了。乱成了一团。

  • He is causing bedlam at the hotel.

    他正搅得旅馆鸡犬不宁。

SYN pandemonium, noise, confusion, chaos
