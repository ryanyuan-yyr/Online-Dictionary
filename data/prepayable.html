pre-pay
(also prepay)Word forms: pre-pays, pre-paying, pre-paid
V-ERG 预付; 预先付款If you pre-pay something or pre-pay for it, you pay for it before you receive it or use it. 预付; 预先付款

  • Also, pre-paying funeral expenses can avoid the effects of inflation.

    另外，预先支付丧葬费可以避免通货膨胀的影响。

  • ...electricity customers who pre-pay for their energy.

    ...那些预付能源费的电力用户。
