mellifluous
mɪlɪfluəs mɪˈlɪflʊəs məˈlɪfluəs
ADJ-GRADED [usually ADJ n] 悦耳的;流畅的;动听的 A mellifluous voice or piece of music is smooth and gentle and very pleasant to listen to. 悦耳的;流畅的;动听的 [formal]

  • I grew up around people who had wonderful, mellifluous voices.

    从小到大，生活在我周围的人都有着悦耳动听的嗓音。

  • Soon the room is filled with Bates' mellifluous tones.

    很快，房间里便充满了贝茨动听的声音。

SYN sweet, soft, smooth, honeyed
