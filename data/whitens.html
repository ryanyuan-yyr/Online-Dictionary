whiten
hwaɪtən ˈhwaɪtən ˈwaɪtən
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense whitens, present participle whitening, past tense , past participle whitened
V-ERG (使)变白 When something whitens or when you whiten it, it becomes whiter or paler in colour. (使)变白

  • Her knuckles whiten as she clenches her hands harder. [VERB]

    她的手握得越来越紧，指节都变白了。

  • ...toothpastes that whiten teeth. [VERB noun]

    增白牙膏

SYN bleach, lighten
