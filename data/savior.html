saviour
seɪvjəʳ
Word forms: plural saviours
in AM, use 美国英语用 savior
1 N-COUNT 救助者;拯救者;救星 A saviour is a person who saves someone or something from danger, ruin, or defeat. 救助者;拯救者;救星

  • ...the saviour of his country. [+ of]

    他的国家的救星

  • ...the saviour of English football.

    英国足球的拯救者

  • She regarded him as her saviour.

    她将他当成自己的救星。

SYN rescuer, deliverer, defender, guardian  
2 N-PROPER [the NOUN] 救主，救世主(基督教中对耶稣基督的称谓) In the Christian religion, the Saviour is Jesus Christ. 救主，救世主(基督教中对耶稣基督的称谓)
