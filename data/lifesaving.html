life-saving
1 ADJ [usually ADJ n] 救命的；救生的 A life-saving drug, operation, or action is one that saves someone's life or is likely to save their life. 救命的；救生的

  • ...life-saving drugs such as antibiotics.

    抗生素之类的救生药物

  • She decided her child should go to America for life-saving treatment.

    她决定把孩子送到美国去接受可能挽救生命的治疗。

2 N-UNCOUNT （尤指救助溺水者的）救生术 You use life-saving to refer to the skills and activities connected with rescuing people, especially people who are drowning. （尤指救助溺水者的）救生术

  • She teaches swimming, lifesaving and water aerobics.

    她教授游泳、救生术和水中有氧运动。
