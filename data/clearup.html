clear up
1 PHRASAL VERB 整理;收拾 When you clear up or clear a place up, you tidy things and put them away. 整理;收拾 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • After breakfast they played while I cleared up. [VERB PREP.]

    早饭后，他们玩耍，我收拾餐具。

  • I cleared up my room. [VERB PREP. noun]

    我把我的房间整理了一下。

2 PHRASAL VERB 解决(问题);消除(误会);揭开(谜团) To clear up a problem, misunderstanding, or mystery means to settle it or find a satisfactory explanation for it. 解决(问题);消除(误会);揭开(谜团) [Also V n P, V P n]

  • There should be someone to whom you can turn for any advice or to clear up any problems. [VERB PREP. noun (not pronoun)]

    应该有个你可以事事请教或帮助你解决一切难题的人。

  • During dinner the confusion was cleared up: they had mistaken me for Kenny. [be VERB-ed PREP.]

    吃晚饭时疑惑消除了：原来他们把我当成肯尼了。

SYN solve, explain, resolve, clarify  
3See also:clear-up 
4 PHRASAL VERB-ERG 解决(医学难题);消除，清除(感染);治愈(疾病);痊愈 To clear up a medical problem, infection, or disease means to cure it or get rid of it. If a medical problem clears up, it goes away. 解决(医学难题);消除，清除(感染);治愈(疾病);痊愈 [Also VERB n PREPOSITION]

  • Antibiotics should be used to clear up the infection. [VERB PREP. noun]

    应该用抗生素消除感染。

  • Acne often clears up after the first three months of pregnancy. [VERB PREP.]

    粉刺在怀孕3个月后往往会自动消失。

5 PHRASAL VERB (天气)放晴，变晴朗 When the weather clears up, it stops raining or being cloudy. (天气)放晴，变晴朗

  • It all depends on the weather clearing up. [VERB PREP.]

    这完全取决于天气是否会放晴。

clear-up
ADJ [ADJ n] 破案的；案件了结的; The clear-up rate for a crime or in an area is the percentage of criminals caught by the police, compared to the total number of crimes reported. 破案的；案件了结的; [British]

  • The clear-up rate for murders remains high.

    谋杀案的破案率一直很高。

  • The Metropolitan Police say clear-up figures were improved.

    伦敦警方称破案率在过去有所提高。
