way-out ˈweɪˈaʊt
ADJ-GRADED 极不寻常的;十分古怪的 If you describe someone or something as way-out, you are critical of them because they are very unusual, often in a way that is very modern or fashionable. 极不寻常的;十分古怪的 [informal, disapproval]

  • They will not allow your more way-out ideas to pass unchallenged.

    他们不会放过你这些古怪的念头的。

  • Didn't you find her a little way-out for you, a bit too arty?

    你不觉得她对你来说有些另类，有些太做作吗？
