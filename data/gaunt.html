gaunt
gɔːnt gɔnt gɑnt
1 ADJ-GRADED （因生病、焦虑等）瘦削的，憔悴的 If someone looks gaunt, they look very thin, usually because they have been very ill or worried. （因生病、焦虑等）瘦削的，憔悴的

  • Looking gaunt and tired, he denied there was anything to worry about.

    他看起来消瘦而疲惫，却说没什么可担心的。

SYN thin, lean, skinny, skeletal  
2 ADJ-GRADED [ADJ n] （建筑物）寒碜的，非常不起眼的，难看的 If you describe a building as gaunt, you mean it is very plain and unattractive. （建筑物）寒碜的，非常不起眼的，难看的 [literary]

  • Above on the hillside was a large, gaunt, grey house.

    山坡上有幢难看的灰色大房子。

SYN bleak, bare, harsh, forbidding
