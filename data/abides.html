abide
əbaɪd əˈbaɪd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense abides, present participle abiding, past tense , past participle abided
1 PHRASE 难以忍受；无法容忍 If you can't abide someone or something, you dislike them very much. 难以忍受；无法容忍

  • I can't abide people who can't make up their minds.

    我受不了优柔寡断的人。

  • She couldn't abide his success.

    她见不得他成功。
