behead
bɪhed biˈhɛd bɪˈhɛd
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense beheads, present participle beheading, past tense , past participle beheaded
VERB [usually passive] 砍…的头；将…斩首 If someone is beheaded, their head is cut off, usually because they have been found guilty of a crime. 砍…的头；将…斩首

  • Charles I was beheaded by the Cromwellians. [be VERB-ed]

    查理一世被克伦威尔派斩首。

SYN decapitate, execute, guillotine, truncate
