border
bɔːʳdəʳ ˈbɔrdər
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense borders, present participle bordering, past tense , past participle bordered
1 N-COUNT 边界；边疆；边界地区 The border between two countries or regions is the dividing line between them. Sometimes the border also refers to the land close to this line. 边界；边疆；边界地区

  • They fled across the border.

    他们穿越边界逃走了。

  • ...the isolated jungle area near the Panamanian border.

    靠近巴拿马边境的偏僻丛林地区

  • Clifford is enjoying life north of the border.

    克利福德正在边境北部自在地生活。

  • ...the Mexican border town of Tijuana.

    墨西哥边境的提华纳镇

  • Soldiers had temporarily closed the border between the two countries.

    士兵们临时封锁了两国之间的边境线。

SYN frontier, line, marches, limit  
2 VERB 和…毗邻；与…接壤 A country that borders another country, a sea, or a river is next to it. 和…毗邻；与…接壤

  • ...the European and Arab countries bordering the Mediterranean. [VERB noun]

    地中海沿岸的欧洲和阿拉伯国家

SYN adjoin, be situated alongside, join, touch  
Border on means the same as border .border on同border

  • Both republics border on the Black Sea.

    两个共和国都临近黑海。

3 N-COUNT 镶边；包边 A border is a strip or band around the edge of something. 镶边；包边

  • ...pillowcases trimmed with a hand-crocheted border.

    饰有手工编织花边的枕套

SYN edge, lip, margin, skirt  
4 N-COUNT （花园边上的）狭长花坛，花带 In a garden, a border is a long strip of ground along the edge planted with flowers. （花园边上的）狭长花坛，花带

  • ...a lawn flanked by wide herbaceous borders.

    两侧栽种大片草本花的草坪

  • ...border plants.

    花带里的植物

SYN flower bed, area, garden, bed  
5 VERB 形成…的边界；环绕；毗连 If something is bordered by another thing, the other thing forms a line along the edge of it. 形成…的边界；环绕；毗连

  • ... the mile of white sand beach bordered by palm trees and tropical flowers [VERB-ed]

    四周种有棕榈树和热带花卉的长达1英里的白色沙滩

  • Caesar marched north into the forests that border the Danube River. [VERB noun]

    恺撒挥师北上，深入到多瑙河沿岸的森林地带。

SYN edge, bound, decorate, trim  
Phrasal verbs:
border on
