rethink
riːθɪŋk riˈθɪŋk
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense rethinks, present participle rethinking, past tense , past participle rethought
1 VERB 重新考虑;反思 If you rethink something such as a problem, a plan, or a policy, you think about it again and change it. 重新考虑;反思 [Also VERB]

  • Both major political parties are having to rethink their policies. [VERB noun]

    两个主要政党都必须反思其政策。

  • I think all of us need to rethink our attitudes toward health and sickness. [VERB noun]

    我想我们都需要反思自己对待健康和疾病的态度。

rethinking

  • ...some fundamental rethinking of the way in which pilots are trained.

    对飞行员的训练方法的彻底反思

2 N-SING 重新考虑;反思 If you have a rethink of a problem, a plan, or a policy, you think about it again and change it. 重新考虑;反思 [Also VERB] [journalism]

  • There must be a rethink of government policy towards this vulnerable group. [+ of/on]

    必须重新考虑政府对待这一弱势群体的政策。
