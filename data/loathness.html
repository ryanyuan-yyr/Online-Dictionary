loath
loʊθ loʊθ loʊð
(also loth)
ADJ-GRADED 不乐意的；不情愿的；勉强的 If you are loath to do something, you do not want to do it. 不乐意的；不情愿的；勉强的

  • She is loath to give up her hard-earned liberty.

    她不愿放弃好不容易得来的自由。

  • The new finance minister seems loth to cut income tax. [+ to-infinitive]

    新任财政部长似乎不愿削减所得税。

SYN unwilling, against, opposed, counter
