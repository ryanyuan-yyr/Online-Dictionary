pint
paɪnt paɪnt
Word forms: plural pints
1 N-COUNT 品脱（液量单位，英制中等于0.568升或1/8英国加仑，美制中等于0.473升或1/8美国加仑） A pint is a unit of measurement for liquids. In Britain, it is equal to 568 cubic centimetres or one eighth of an imperial gallon. In America, it is equal to 473 cubic centimetres or one eighth of an American gallon. 品脱（液量单位，英制中等于0.568升或1/8英国加仑，美制中等于0.473升或1/8美国加仑）

  • ...a pint of milk. [+ of]

    一品脱牛奶

  • The military requested 6,000 pints of blood from the American Red Cross. [+ of]

    军方请求美国红十字会支援6,000品脱的血液。

  • ...glasses which can hold a full pint.

    一品脱容量的玻璃杯

2 N-COUNT 一品脱啤酒 If you go for a pint, you go to the pub to drink a pint of beer or more. 一品脱啤酒 [British]

  • He sits down and reads the paper, then goes out for a pint.

    他会坐下读报，然后出去喝杯啤酒。
