driver's seat
ˈdraɪvəz siːt
1 N-SING 驾驶员座位;司机座位 In a vehicle such as a car or a bus, the driver's seat is the seat where the person who is driving sits. 驾驶员座位;司机座位
2 PHRASE 处于控制地位;负责;主管 If you say that someone is in the driver's seat, you mean that they are in control in a situation. 处于控制地位;负责;主管

  • Now he knows he's in the driver's seat and can wait for a better deal.

    现在他明白他处于支配地位，可以等到更好的待遇。
