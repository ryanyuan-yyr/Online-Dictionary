incongruous
ɪnkɒŋgruəs ɪnˈkɑŋgruəs ɪnˈkɑngruəs
ADJ-GRADED 不合适的;不和谐的;不相称的 Someone or something that is incongruous seems strange when considered together with other aspects of a situation. 不合适的;不和谐的;不相称的 [formal]

  • She was small and fragile and looked incongruous in an army uniform.

    她又小又弱,穿一身军装看起来很不协调。

  • The Indian temple is an incongruous sight in the Welsh border country.

    坐落在威尔士边界乡村里的印度庙宇看上去相当突兀。

SYN inappropriate, absurd, out of place, conflicting  
incongruously

  • ...buildings perched incongruously in a high green valley.

    楼房突兀地耸立在高高的绿色山谷里
