broadcast
brɔːdkɑːst -kæst ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˈbrɔdˌkɑst
Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense broadcasts, present participle broadcasting
The form broadcast is used in the present tense and is the past tense and past participle of the verb. broadcast的过去式和过去分词与原形相同。
1 N-COUNT （无线电或电视）广播；广播节目；电视节目 A broadcast is a programme, performance, or speech on the radio or on television. （无线电或电视）广播；广播节目；电视节目

  • In a broadcast on state radio the government also announced that it was willing to resume peace negotiations. [+ on]

    在国家电台的广播中，政府也声明愿意重新开始和平谈判。

SYN transmission, show, programme, telecast  
2 VERB （电台或电视台）广播，播出（节目） To broadcast a programme means to send it out by radio waves, so that it can be heard on the radio or seen on television. （电台或电视台）广播，播出（节目） [Also VERB n]

  • The concert will be broadcast live on television and radio. [be VERB-ed adv./prep.]

    音乐会将通过电视和广播现场直播。

  • CNN also broadcasts in Europe. [VERB]

    美国有线新闻网也在欧洲播出节目。

SYN transmit, show, send, air
