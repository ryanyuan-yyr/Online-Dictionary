fearsome
fɪəʳsəm ˈfɪrsəm
ADJ-GRADED 可怕的;骇人的;令人生畏的 Fearsome is used to describe things that are frightening, for example because of their large size or extreme nature. 可怕的;骇人的;令人生畏的

  • He had developed a fearsome reputation for intimidating people.

    他曾因恐吓他人而令人闻风丧胆。

  • ...a fearsome array of weapons.

    一大批令人生畏的武器

SYN formidable, alarming, frightening, awful [obsolete]
