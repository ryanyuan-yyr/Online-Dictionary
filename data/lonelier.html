lonely
loʊnli ˈloʊnli
Word forms: comparative lonelier, superlative loneliest
1 ADJ-GRADED 孤独的；寂寞的；孤单的 Someone who is lonely is unhappy because they are alone or do not have anyone they can talk to. 孤独的；寂寞的；孤单的

  • ...lonely people who just want to talk.

    只想找人倾诉的孤独的人

  • I feel lonelier in the middle of London than I do on my boat in the middle of nowhere.

    我身处伦敦市中心比驾船航行于茫茫大海更感到孤单。

SYN solitary, alone, isolated, abandoned  
The lonely are people who are lonely.孤独的人；寂寞的人

  • He looks for the lonely, the lost, the unloved.

    他找的是孤独的人、失落的人和缺少爱的人。

2 ADJ-GRADED （指状态或时期）孤独的，寂寞的，孤零零的 A lonely situation or period of time is one in which you feel unhappy because you are alone or do not have anyone to talk to. （指状态或时期）孤独的，寂寞的，孤零零的

  • I desperately needed something to occupy me during those long, lonely nights.

    在那一个个漫长、孤单的夜晚，我极需找点事做。

  • ...her lonely childhood.

    她寂寞的童年

3 ADJ-GRADED 偏僻的；荒凉的；人迹罕至的 A lonely place is one where very few people come. 偏僻的；荒凉的；人迹罕至的

  • It felt like the loneliest place in the world.

    感觉这就像是天底下最荒凉的地方。

  • ...dark, lonely streets.

    昏暗偏僻的街道

SYN desolate, deserted, remote, isolated
