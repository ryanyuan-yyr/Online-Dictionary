glum
glʌm glʌm
Word forms: comparative glummer, superlative glummest
ADJ-GRADED [usually verb-link ADJ] 闷闷不乐的；忧郁的；愁闷的 Someone who is glum is sad and quiet because they are disappointed or unhappy about something. 闷闷不乐的；忧郁的；愁闷的

  • She was very glum and was obviously missing her children.

    她郁郁寡欢，显然在惦念自己的孩子们。

SYN gloomy, miserable, dismal, down  
glumly

  • When Eleanor returned, I was still sitting glumly on the settee.

    埃莉诺回来的时候，我还闷闷不乐地坐在长沙发上。
