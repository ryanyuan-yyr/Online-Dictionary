climate
klaɪmət ˈklaɪmət
Word forms: plural climates
1 N-VAR 气候 The climate of a place is the general weather conditions that are typical of it. 气候

  • ...the hot and humid climate of Cyprus. [+ of]

    塞浦路斯炎热潮湿的气候

SYN weather, country, region, temperature  
2 N-COUNT 氛围；形势;局势;状况 You can use climate to refer to the general atmosphere or situation somewhere. 氛围；形势;局势;状况

  • The economic climate remains uncertain.

    经济形势依然不确定。

  • ...the existing climate of violence and intimidation. [+ of]

    目前暴力和恐吓的氛围

  • A major change of political climate is not in prospect.

    暂时还看不出政治面貌有可能发生巨变。

SYN atmosphere, environment, spirit, surroundings
