exterminate
ɪkstɜːʳmɪneɪt ɪkˈstɜːmɪˌneɪt ɪkˈstɜːmɪn ɛkˈstɜrməˌneɪt ɪkˈstɜrməˌneɪt
Word forms: 3rd person singular present tense exterminates, present participle exterminating, past tense , past participle exterminated
VERB 消灭;灭绝;根除 To exterminate a group of people or animals means to kill all of them. 消灭;灭绝;根除

  • A huge effort was made to exterminate the rats. [VERB noun]

    灭鼠花了大力气。

  • They have a real fear that they'll be exterminated in the ongoing civil war. [VERB noun]

    他们十分害怕自己会在持续的内战中被彻底消灭。

  • Man is exterminating too many species for zoos to be much help. [VERB noun]

    人类正在导致过多物种灭绝，动物园也无济于事。
