come along
1 PHRASAL VERB (尤用于鼓励别人参加某活动)来吧，一起来 You tell someone to come along to encourage them in a friendly way to do something, especially to attend something. (尤用于鼓励别人参加某活动)来吧，一起来

  • There's a big press launch today and you're most welcome to come along. [VERB PREP.]

    今天有一个大型的媒体推介会，非常希望您能光临。

2 CONVENTION 加把劲儿;赶快 You say 'come along' to someone to encourage them to hurry up, usually when you are rather annoyed with them. 加把劲儿;赶快

  • Come along, Osmond. No sense in your standing around. [VERB PREP.]

    快点儿，奥斯蒙德，你闲站着干什么。

SYN hurry, let's go, hurry up, get moving  
3 PHRASAL VERB (偶然地)发生，来到 When something or someone comes along, they occur or arrive by chance. (偶然地)发生，来到

  • I waited a long time until a script came along that I thought was genuinely funny. [VERB PREP.]

    我等了很长时间，直到出现了一个我认为真正逗趣的剧本。

  • It was lucky you came along. [VERB PREP.]

    真幸运你来了。

4 PHRASAL VERB 进展;进步 If something is coming along, it is developing or making progress. 进展;进步

  • Pentagon spokesman Williams says those talks are coming along quite well. [VERB PREP. adv.]

    五角大楼发言人威廉斯说那些会谈进展得相当顺利。

  • How's Ferguson coming along? [VERB PREP.]

    弗格森现在怎么样啊？

SYN progress, develop, get on, come on
